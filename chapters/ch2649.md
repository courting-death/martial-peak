## Chapter 2649 — Forming the Array

"Yun'er, Xiu'er, Yao'er!" Bing Yun called out softly, her voice sounding somewhat weary.

"Disciple present!" An Ruoyun, Sun Yunxiu, and Ji Yao all stepped forward in unison. These three were the first disciples Bing Yun had ever accepted, and they were also the only three in Ice Heart Valley to have reached the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm.

"Let the disciples leave through the Space Array. I will stay behind to delay them for a while."

The three disciples looked at each other, and in the end, it was An Ruoyun who replied, "Master, it is not that we do not wish to obey, but... I fear no one is willing to leave."

Of the seven thousand disciples remaining in Ice Heart Valley, more than half were orphans, brought back to the Valley from a young age and raised with care. To them, Ice Heart Valley was their home, their very roots. Now that their home was in danger, who would be willing to leave?

It could be said that, throughout the entire Northern Territory—and even across the whole Star Realm—there were few Sects whose unity and cohesion could compare to that of Ice Heart Valley.

Some days ago, An Ruoyun and the others had made arrangements to send some disciples out of Ice Heart Valley in advance, but after asking around, not a single disciple was willing to survive by fleeing. All insisted on living and dying with the Sect.

"Willing or not, you must leave. Save as many as you can." Bing Yun ordered sternly, "Go now, take the disciples you trust most, and get far away from the Northern Territory."

The three women stood motionless, their faces expressionless.

Nanmen Dajun, watching from the side, was moved as well. He secretly regretted making things difficult for these women for the sake of some wealth. Now that the Grand Guardian Array had been almost broken, he bore the greatest responsibility.

"Why are you still standing there? Go!" Bing Yun, seeing their lack of response, couldn't help but scold them.

Seeing their master angry, the three women instinctively flinched.

At that moment, a crisp cracking sound suddenly echoed in all directions. The barrier of the grand array covering Ice Heart Valley shattered inch by inch like broken porcelain.

The final, fragile layer of protection tore apart, and Ice Heart Valley was completely exposed.

"The array is broken!" Nanmen Dajun's expression changed.

Bing Yun and the others' faces also turned cold.

"Bing Yun, Feng is here!"

Suddenly, a shout came from outside, followed by the sound of howling wind from all directions. Waves of powerful Divine Sense swept over the treasured land of Ice Heart Valley, and soon, the sounds of battle reached everyone's ears.

It was the disciples of Ice Heart Valley clashing with the invading enemies.

Swish, swish, swish...

The sound of figures breaking through the air rang out. In the blink of an eye, more than twenty people surrounded Bing Yun and the others, each exuding the aura of the Emperor Realm.

At the head were Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo, both possessing the formidable cultivation of the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm.

"So this is all the Sect Defending Grand Array of Ice Heart Valley amounts to!" Feng Xuan stood proudly in the air, looking down on Bing Yun and the others, a smug and disdainful smile on his lips.

Bing Yun's face was as calm as still water. Her beautiful eyes swept over each of the Emperor Realm cultivators. Though many met her gaze calmly, even more had shifty eyes, looking as if they had done something shameful.

Ice Heart Valley had stood in the Northern Territory for many years and had never made enemies with any Sect. On the contrary, many female disciples had married out. Although Bing Yun had set a rule that those who married out would have no further ties to Ice Heart Valley, rules were rules, and such entanglements could not be completely severed.

In other words, over the years, Bing Yun and the various Sects of the Northern Territory had all formed some connections. In every generation, some outstanding disciples married into these Sects.

Yet now, the experts of these Sects had followed Feng Xuan in breaking through the Sect Defending Grand Array and invading the heart of Ice Heart Valley, even surrounding Bing Yun and the others. Such actions were truly chilling.

Now, facing Bing Yun's burning gaze, these people couldn't help but feel guilty.

"Valley Lord Chen, Ice Heart Valley has never had any enmity with your Cang Le Valley. In fact, a hundred years ago, when you were afflicted by fire poison, it was I who helped cure you. Yet today, Valley Lord Chen, you come to make things difficult for Ice Heart Valley as well?" An Ruoyun's beautiful eyes turned cold as she gazed at the middle-aged man.

The middle-aged man's face flushed, his eyes dodging, and he said nothing.

An Ruoyun then looked at another woman dressed as a matron and said, "Elder Yu, when you used to visit Ice Heart Valley, we called each other sisters so affectionately. Today, you come uninvited, which truly surprises me."

The woman called Elder Yu forced a smile and said, "Sister An, don't blame me. I have my own difficulties and was forced into this. Besides, Sect Master Feng doesn't really want to make things hard for your Ice Heart Valley. He just wants to inquire about someone's background. If you tell him, it will all be over. Sister An, why not tell Sect Master Feng what he wants to know? I'll speak up for you, and you won't be troubled later."

An Ruoyun sneered, "Elder Yu, I appreciate your kindness."

Elder Yu frowned and said, "Sister An, why make things so difficult? You..."

"Enough! In the past, I was blind to have befriended you, never seeing you for the ungrateful wretch you are!"

These words were merciless, making Elder Yu's face flush red as she shouted angrily, "Truly ungrateful! Taking good intentions for ill. Sister An, if you remain stubborn, you will not have a good end today!"

After being spoken to like that by An Ruoyun, the woman became both embarrassed and angry.

She said sternly, "Today, Sect Master Feng comes with supreme might. Sister An, you'd best recognize the situation!"

"Well said!" Feng Xuan laughed heartily from the side, clapping his hands and looking down with a cold smile. "Bing Yun, when you sheltered the one who killed my son, did you ever think this day would come? But it's too late for regrets now. Since I am here, none of you will have a good end."

He seemed certain of victory, so he was in no hurry to attack Bing Yun and the others, instead basking in the pleasure of controlling the situation.

"Sect Master Feng..." Nanmen Dajun called out with a frown, cupping his fists. Before he could say anything more, Feng Xuan interrupted him, glancing at him indifferently and saying, "So Grandmaster Nanmen is here too."

Nanmen Dajun thought to himself, isn't that obvious? He had been captured right in front of Feng Xuan the other day—where else would he be?

Feng Xuan said, "Being able to break the array today, Grandmaster Nanmen deserves the most credit. Grandmaster, please stand back. Otherwise, if you get hurt later, I might feel bad. After I destroy Ice Heart Valley, there will still be much I need your help with."

Nanmen Dajun frowned, sighed, and said nothing more.

He knew his words carried little weight. Though he was a formation grandmaster, when people needed him, they would beg and plead, but the world was full of those who would discard you once you were no longer useful.

He didn't think he had enough influence to make Feng Xuan listen to his advice.

So he wisely shut his mouth, lest he really bring disaster upon himself.

"Brother Yao, join me! Let's kill Bing Yun first, then destroy Ice Heart Valley!" Feng Xuan, having enjoyed the despair of the Ice Heart Valley crowd, finally shouted, killing intent surging.

Yao Zhuo, who had been silent on the side, stepped forward at these words. The powerful aura of the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm erupted from him.

Although his aura was not as deep and pure as that of an old hand like Feng Xuan, there was no doubt he was truly at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. After all, he had only recently broken through.

Suddenly, a long saber appeared in his hand. The blade flashed, seeming to split the sky, and he slashed down at Bing Yun. At the same time, Feng Xuan also summoned the relic of the Great Emperor, the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear.

The spear rang with the sound of wind and thunder, transforming into a bolt of lightning as it shot toward Bing Yun.

With two Third Stage Emperor Realm experts joining forces, Bing Yun's expression became extremely solemn.

She had fought these two many times recently. In single combat, she feared neither, even confident she could kill Yao Zhuo within the time it took for an incense stick to burn. But against both at once, she was powerless. She tapped her toes lightly and floated back.

"Form the array!" An Ruoyun shouted at just the right moment.

In the next instant, six Emperor Realm cultivators from Ice Heart Valley flew to Bing Yun's side, spreading out in a mysterious formation.

As Emperor Yuan was activated, the power of the seven surged in harmony. For a moment, sand and stones flew, the sun and moon lost their light, and vaguely, a colossal beast seemed to be born, raising its head and roaring in fury.

Clang, clang...

After two resounding clashes, Yao Zhuo staggered back more than ten steps, his chest surging with Qi and blood. Feng Xuan also stumbled back several steps, the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear he had thrown blocked by a mysterious force.

"What is going on?"

"What happened?"

"Is Bing Yun really this powerful?"

The surrounding Emperor Realm cultivators were all dumbfounded, unable to believe it. Though Bing Yun's strength was well known, for her to block the combined assault of Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo, even forcing them back, was something no one present would have believed.

But the facts were right before their eyes, making it hard to doubt.

"No, it's not Bing Yun's power!"

"A formation—what kind of formation is this?"

"A Black Tortoise phantom—this formation... is incredible."

As the light faded, the scene on the ground became clear. Everyone immediately understood how Bing Yun had managed to repel the two Third Stage Emperor Realm experts.

At this moment, Bing Yun and the other six disciples had formed a mysterious formation, their Emperor Yuan tightly linked. The energy they released condensed into a massive Black Tortoise phantom visible to the naked eye.

Bing Yun hid within the head of the Black Tortoise phantom, with An Ruoyun and Sun Yunxiu at the shoulders, Ji Yao and another Emperor Realm elder at the feet, one on the back, and one at the tail.

The Black Tortoise phantom stood proudly, exuding the aura of a lone hero holding back ten thousand foes.

Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation!

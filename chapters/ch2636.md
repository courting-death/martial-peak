## Chapter 2636 — My Name is Xiao Baiyi

In front of the Hall of Recruitment, a rather ordinary-looking young man strolled over. Though his appearance was plain, he was dressed quite properly, wearing a brocade robe and holding a folding fan. Despite the bitter cold of the Northern Territory, he walked along fanning himself, striking a suave and debonair pose.

The martial artists walking along the street who saw him all shook their heads and spat in disdain, finding him utterly contemptible.

Naturally, this young man was Yang Kai. After leaving Ice Heart Valley, he had made his way here with great caution, not alerting anyone, heading straight for Binglun City. After inquiring a bit about where talents were being recruited, he arrived at the Hall of Recruitment.

The folding fan in his hand was also a Secret Treasure, of Dao Origin-Grade Upper-Level quality. Who knew who had left it behind after dying? In any case, Yang Kai had killed many powerful enemies over the years and seized plenty of spoils. Now, this fan was coming in handy.

Outside the Hall of Recruitment, two disciples of Wenqing Sect stood guard. Their strength wasn’t high, only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, but in a place like this, no one would dare cause trouble for Wenqing Sect, so their cultivation didn’t matter. Their main role was simply to watch the gate and report arrivals.

Seeing Yang Kai swaggering over, the two guards exchanged glances, both revealing looks of disdain.

Although their cultivation wasn’t that high, they were still disciples of Wenqing Sect, and their status was clear. Yang Kai’s manner made it obvious he was some Young Master from a family, strutting around in such flashy attire as if he really was someone important.

If he were truly handsome and dashing, this getup might have worked, but with such an utterly ordinary face, the brocade robe and fine fan didn’t suit him at all, naturally drawing people’s scorn.

“Stop. What business do you have here?” One of the guards stretched out a hand to block Yang Kai’s path, asking coldly.

Yang Kai glanced up at the plaque, closed his folding fan with a snap, slapped it against his palm, and said, “This is the Hall of Recruitment. What do you think I’m here for?”

The guard frowned and asked, “You’re here to receive the Recruitment Order?”

"That's right," Yang Kai replied with a slight smile. With a crisp snap, he flicked open his folding fan and leisurely fanned himself a few times, looking every bit the picture of a promising young man.

Since he was here to receive the Recruitment Order and serve Wenqing Sect, the two guards had no right to stop him. The one who spoke before nodded lightly and said, “Go straight in, then turn left. Look for one of the Deacons.”

Yang Kai nodded slightly and strode inside.

Following the guard’s directions, he walked straight ahead, then turned left, entering a grand hall.

Unexpectedly, there were quite a few people inside, all waiting in line. Behind each desk, Wenqing Sect’s Deacons were questioning the martial artists who had come to join, asking their names and backgrounds. If nothing seemed amiss, they would register them and issue a Recruitment Order.

With a Recruitment Order in hand, it meant one temporarily belonged to Wenqing Sect. Once Ice Heart Valley’s Grand Guardian Array was broken and the sect destroyed, rewards would be distributed according to merit.

Those with clear backgrounds who received the Recruitment Order would be led away by Wenqing Sect disciples and assigned tasks.

Each desk had a long line in front of it, which made Yang Kai frown. So many people coming to receive Wenqing Sect’s Recruitment Order was not good news for Ice Heart Valley.

He stood in place and observed for a while, quickly understanding the process. Without further hesitation, he picked a desk and walked straight over. As he walked, he rudely waved his fan left and right, shouting, “Out of the way, out of the way, out of the way! Good dogs don’t block the road!”

He acted utterly arrogant and lawless. Anyone who didn’t know better would think he was some big shot from Wenqing Sect.

The martial artists waiting in line cried out in pain as Yang Kai’s folding fan struck them. In an instant, the orderly queue fell into chaos. Everyone glared angrily at Yang Kai, but uncertain of his strength, they only dared to seethe in silence, not one bold enough to speak out.

Yang Kai let out a mocking laugh, not sparing these people a second thought. They were here to join Wenqing Sect and stand against Ice Heart Valley—once battle broke out, they would be enemies. Naturally, Yang Kai saw no reason to show them any courtesy. The force behind his fan was subtle but sharp, enough to leave those struck by it aching for ten days to half a month.

Behind the desk, a Wenqing Sect Deacon saw Yang Kai’s arrogance and utter disregard for Wenqing Sect, and immediately grew angry, snorting coldly, “Brat, stop making a racket!”

“Exactly, exactly! This man is too rude. Deacon, please teach him a lesson!”

“It hurts so much! This brat actually dared to strike us! Deacon, you must uphold justice!”

“Don’t let him off!”

The martial artists who had been struck all began shouting at once.

“Noisy!” Yang Kai’s face turned cold as he struck out with his palm at the crowd. In an instant, Emperor Yuan surged, Laws descended, and the entire hall buzzed.

“Emperor Realm!”

“Heavens, he’s in the Emperor Realm!”

The martial artists who had been shouting were instantly terrified, and even the Wenqing Sect Deacons present turned pale. Though they were Deacons, they were only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Against an Emperor Realm master, they had no power to resist.

Boom—

With a single palm, dozens of people were sent flying, all coughing up blood in midair and landing in a sorry state. Still, everyone was relieved that, despite the power of the blow, no one was killed. But those injuries would take at least ten days to half a month to heal.

“If you keep making noise, I’ll take your worthless lives next!” Yang Kai snorted coldly, his arrogance boundless, acting as if he alone reigned supreme under the heavens.

No one dared make a sound. The hall was so quiet you could hear a pin drop.

Swish—

Suddenly, a figure shot out from the inner hall, landing in front of the desks.

As soon as this person appeared, the Wenqing Sect Deacons, who had been on edge and barely dared to breathe, looked as if they’d seen a savior. Their eyes lit up and they hurriedly cupped their fists, “Greetings, Elder Sun!”

“Elder Sun! It’s Elder Sun Ping!”

“It really is Elder Sun Ping. That brat is in trouble now. Elder Sun Ping is at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm—he’s definitely not a match.”

“Daring to cause trouble in Wenqing Sect’s territory, that brat should take a good look at himself.”

Hearing the crowd’s chatter, Yang Kai finally glanced at the newly arrived expert.

The newcomer was an old man, hair and beard white, his aura as deep as the sea, exuding immense pressure. His face was thin, but his eyes shone like blazing suns, piercing to the soul.

“Elder Sun?” Yang Kai raised an eyebrow.

He wasn’t familiar with Wenqing Sect’s Emperor Realm masters, only knowing Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo. This Elder Sun was a first, but his cultivation was indeed at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, so his status in Wenqing Sect must be high.

“Young man, you have quite a temper.” To everyone’s surprise, after appearing, Sun Ping didn’t cause trouble for Yang Kai, but instead smiled amiably.

Yang Kai sneered, “They were too noisy. I was just teaching them a lesson.” Then, changing his tone, he cupped his fists and grinned, “Didn’t mean to trouble Elder Sun. My apologies, my apologies.”

Acting like a lord before the weak, but playing the humble junior before the strong—this was exactly Yang Kai’s current behavior.

Everyone in the hall looked down on him, but with his Emperor Realm cultivation, no one dared show it. It was suffocating.

“No harm, no harm!” Sun Ping smiled. “Since you’ve come to our Hall of Recruitment, you’re here for the Recruitment Order?”

Yang Kai nodded, “Of course. I heard Wenqing Sect is recruiting talents and is eager for capable people, so I came. Elder Sun, you wouldn’t turn me away, would you?”

“Not at all!” Sun Ping stroked his beard and smiled. “Our sect welcomes outstanding young talents like you.”

“That’s good.” Yang Kai chuckled.

“However…” Sun Ping’s tone shifted, his eyes narrowing. “Let me be clear: to receive the Recruitment Order, your background must be clean. Wenqing Sect doesn’t accept people of unknown origin, especially those with your level of cultivation. So, I’ll need you to answer a few questions.”

If it were below the Emperor Realm, a vague background wouldn’t matter, but for someone in the Emperor Realm, their origins had to be clear and traceable. Otherwise, they might be a spy planted by Ice Heart Valley.

That was the rule.

“I understand.” Yang Kai smiled, showing not the slightest hint of panic. “What would Elder Sun like to ask?”

Sun Ping said, “Where are you from, what is your name, and who is your master?”

Yang Kai replied calmly, “My name is Xiao Baiyi. I come from Qingyang Shrine in the Southern Territory, disciple of Elder Gao Xueting!”

Though he said this aloud, Yang Kai was thinking, Xiao Bai, I’m just borrowing your identity for a bit—don’t hold it against me later. He’d long since planned this, so he answered without hesitation.

“Qingyang Shrine!” Sun Ping was shocked at the answer.

Although the Southern Territory was far from the Northern Territory, Sun Ping had heard of Qingyang Shrine. Its status in the Southern Territory was equal to Wenqing Sect’s in the Northern Territory—both were top sects.

For a disciple of Qingyang Shrine to appear in Binglun City in the Northern Territory was truly hard to understand.

Sun Ping frowned, “I’ve long heard of your Shrine’s great name, but… why are you here?”

Yang Kai smiled, “I only recently broke through to the Emperor Realm. My master told me to travel and broaden my horizons, so I’ve been wandering the Star Realm. Passing through here, I heard about your sect’s situation with Ice Heart Valley and thought it interesting, so I came to take a look.”

“So that’s how it is!” Sun Ping had no doubts.

For a young talent like Yang Kai, traveling the Star Realm after achieving success in cultivation was normal. Sun Ping himself had done the same, though he’d only traveled the Northern and Eastern Territories, never the Southern. So this explanation had no flaws.

After pondering for a while, Sun Ping looked up again, “With your age and cultivation, you must be a core disciple of Qingyang Shrine, right? Words alone aren’t enough—do you have anything to prove your identity?”

“Proof of identity…” Yang Kai thought for a moment, then suddenly flipped his hand, producing a token and tossing it to Sun Ping. “This should be enough to prove who I am.”

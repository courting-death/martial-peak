## Chapter 2948 — This Was a Misunderstanding

Supporting Zhu Qing, Yang Kai let her lean against him, then placed two fingers on her wrist and channeled his Emperor Yuan into her body to examine her condition.

After a moment, Yang Kai’s brows furrowed deeply. The aura within Zhu Qing’s body was in utter chaos. This disorder didn’t seem to be the result of a serious injury, but rather resembled Qi Deviation. He was completely baffled, unable to understand what Zhu Qing had encountered in Frozen Earth to put her at risk of Qi Deviation—she was a member of the Dragon Clan, after all.

Besides that, Zhu Qing’s physical body was in dire straits. She must have stayed in Frozen Earth for far too long, and the life-extinguishing cold had already invaded her, leaving most of her flesh and blood devoid of vitality. If she wasn’t treated soon, her body would likely be ruined as well.

Yang Kai dared not delay. He released his Divine Sense, quickly found a nearby cave, scooped Zhu Qing up, and rushed inside.

This cave was probably left behind by some wild beast. The air inside was thick with a pungent, fishy stench, but it had clearly been abandoned for a long time—no living creature had entered in ages.

To avoid any unnecessary interference during treatment, Yang Kai directly summoned the Xuan Realm Pearl and brought Zhu Qing into the Small Xuan Realm with him.

In a land of birdsong and fragrant flowers, Yang Kai laid Zhu Qing flat on the ground, then took out a large handful of Spiritual Pills from his Spatial Ring. Without hesitation, he pried open her mouth and stuffed them in.

Zhu Qing’s situation was rather tricky. The cold from Frozen Earth had severely damaged her body, and her Dragon Yuan was in complete disarray for some unknown reason. To save her, he would have to drive out the cold that had invaded her flesh from the outside in—only then could she regain control over her Dragon Yuan.

Most of the pills in Yang Kai’s hand were top-quality Spiritual Pills. With just this handful, at least a million Source Crystals were gone.

But the effect was remarkable. With Yang Kai’s assistance, the medicinal power of the Spiritual Pills quickly spread throughout Zhu Qing’s body. As it circulated through her limbs and bones, waves of bone-chilling cold began to radiate from her, identical to the pervasive cold of Frozen Earth. As it seeped out of her, the surrounding flowers and grass withered and died almost instantly.

Gradually, her icy body gained a trace of warmth, and her snow-white face showed a hint of life.

Yang Kai kept calling her name, even using his Divine Sense to break into her Sea of Consciousness, trying to wake her up.

Yet even so, Zhu Qing showed no reaction at all. She seemed completely unaware of the outside world, her eyes tightly shut, as if she were already dead.

Yang Kai could only keep channeling Emperor Yuan to ease her crisis.

No one knew how much time passed, but the situation barely improved. After the medicinal power of the Emperor Pill was spent, the cold in Zhu Qing’s body surged up again. It was as if the cold had a life of its own, able to split and multiply within her. As long as even a trace remained, it would rise again.

Yang Kai withdrew his hand with a sigh, gazing at her delicate face. After a long while, as if making a decision, he reached out and grabbed at the air.

Suddenly, a cluster of emerald-green light appeared in his palm, so vibrant it seemed to drip. Though it was just a ball of light, it contained an unimaginable, surging vitality.

The essence of the Everlasting Tree!

This time, Yang Kai didn’t pluck a leaf from the Everlasting Tree. There weren’t many leaves left, and if he kept picking them, the tree would soon be bare. Extracting the tree’s essence like this would certainly harm it, but with a life at stake, Yang Kai couldn’t worry about that now.

“You’ll have to rely on your own luck. If even this can’t save you, then your fate truly ends here.” Muttering to himself, Yang Kai pried open Zhu Qing’s mouth with one hand and stuffed the ball of green light in with the other.

The green light turned into a drop of tree sap, which slid down Zhu Qing’s throat and into her belly.

In an instant, dazzling light burst forth. Zhu Qing’s skin was suddenly suffused with a green glow, as if covered in moss. At the same time, her faint vitality erupted, as if a bucket of oil had been poured onto a blazing fire.

Hiss, hiss, hiss, hiss—

Threads of cold seeped from Zhu Qing’s pores like tiny worms, merging with the green light on her skin and producing a dense, crackling sound.

Yang Kai watched closely, his Divine Sense constantly probing Zhu Qing’s condition.

As time passed, less and less cold seeped from Zhu Qing’s body, and the green light on her skin grew fainter, as if both the cold and the Everlasting Tree’s essence were running out of energy.

Meanwhile, Zhu Qing’s body gradually regained vitality, and the exposed skin slowly turned rosy.

Yang Kai let out a quiet breath, feeling that his efforts had not been in vain.

At a certain moment, when the last trace of cold was expelled from Zhu Qing’s body, the green light enveloping her also faded away. The Everlasting Tree’s essence was not exhausted, but would remain dormant in Zhu Qing’s flesh, becoming the foundation for her future strength.

She lay there on the ground, as if she could sleep until the end of time.

Arms crossed, Yang Kai stood over her, looking down from above. His gaze swept from her head to her toes, utterly unrestrained, savoring the feeling of holding her earlier, and he couldn’t help but feel a little excited.

“As expected of the Dragon Clan… this chest, these hips, these thighs…” Yang Kai commented, as if admiring a work of art. Suddenly, a wicked thought crept into his mind—after suffering such a loss, shouldn’t he collect a little interest?

Anyway, this girl had already been loose with him before. Once is a shock, twice is a habit, and the third time is second nature. If it weren't for her, he wouldn't have risked venturing into Frozen Earth in the first place, nor would he have been inexplicably swallowed by the cold tide, leaving him confused about where this place even was.

Maybe he’d be stuck here for the rest of his life, living with her, raising a brood of little dragon children.

In his mind, an excited voice clamored: collect interest, collect interest, you must collect some interest!

As soon as this thought arose, Yang Kai was startled, realizing that his suppressed desires were acting up again. He hurried to restrain himself, but suddenly felt a soft, irresistible sensation in his hands.

Looking down, Yang Kai was dumbfounded.

Somehow, both his hands had ended up on Zhu Qing’s ample chest, those majestic curves transmitting an astonishing elasticity that made him unconsciously squeeze twice.

His breathing grew heavy, and Yang Kai felt his blood boiling. The voice in his mind grew even louder, and his gaze locked onto the pale, slender neck below Zhu Qing’s face. He gulped, as if a traveler lost in the desert for months had finally found a basin of clear water, desperate to dive in.

Suddenly, the woman before him opened her eyes and stared at Yang Kai with a blank expression.

Their eyes met, and Yang Kai felt as if a bucket of cold water had been poured over him from head to toe. He instantly sobered up, forced a smile, and said awkwardly, “If I told you this was a misunderstanding, would you believe me?”

Zhu Qing gave no response, her clear eyes still fixed coldly on him.

Yang Kai stood up, frowned in thought for a moment, then struck a shameless pose. “Fine, I touched you. So what? It’s not like you’ll lose a piece of flesh if I squeeze you twice. Besides, I saved your life. If I hadn’t gone through all that trouble to find you in Frozen Earth and rescue you from danger, you’d probably be dead by now. So let’s just call what happened my reward, and we’re even. I think that’s fair—what do you say?”

Zhu Qing still didn’t react.

Yang Kai grew annoyed. “Can’t you at least give me some reaction?!”

As if in response to Yang Kai’s endless chatter, Zhu Qing finally reacted—but not in the way he expected. She opened her mouth and let out a long, agonized moan, as if she were dying, then her whole body curled up, and at the same time, an extremely cold aura erupted from her.

Yang Kai was shocked. “Still not working?”

He thought the Everlasting Tree’s essence hadn’t expelled all the cold from her body, but soon realized something was wrong. This chill wasn’t the same as Frozen Earth’s cold. Though it was just as frigid, it was of a different nature, not from the same source.

While Yang Kai was still confused, a high-pitched dragon roar rang out, and a giant dragon’s phantom shot out from Zhu Qing’s body.

“Hm?” Yang Kai stared blankly at the dragon phantom, wondering if he was seeing things.

Zhu Qing was a Red Dragon of the Dragon Clan, so her Origin Power should be the same as Zhu Lie’s. If she revealed her true form, it would be a crimson dragon. But the phantom before Yang Kai was a pure, crystalline white dragon.

At first glance, the giant dragon looked like a lifelike ice sculpture, exquisitely crafted, flawless. But it had nothing in common with a Red Dragon—this was an Ice Dragon. The cold now radiating from Zhu Qing’s body came from this Ice Dragon.

Yang Kai couldn’t help but wonder if he’d mistaken her for someone else—did Zhu Qing perhaps have a twin sister? There were people in this world who looked alike, but as a member of the Dragon Clan, there was no way he could be wrong about her Origin Power.

Just then, another high-pitched dragon roar rang out.

Yang Kai looked closely and saw a crimson dragon phantom appear, also emerging from Zhu Qing’s body.

Yang Kai’s expression shifted as countless thoughts flashed through his mind. In an instant, he understood a great deal.

“Two kinds of Origin?”

Zhu Qing actually had two kinds of Origin within her, and they were completely opposite in nature! The red dragon that appeared second was clearly Zhu Qing’s own Origin Power, while the Ice Dragon that appeared first was an invader. The clash between these two Origin Powers was what had thrown her Dragon Yuan into chaos and put her at risk of Qi Deviation.

Although Yang Kai had examined her before, he hadn’t noticed this. It wasn’t that he hadn’t checked carefully enough, but that Origin Power was hidden too deeply, and with Zhu Qing’s body so badly damaged, he’d simply overlooked it.

No wonder her physical body posed no major issues; it turned out that Zhu Qing still wouldn't wake because the true problem didn't lie in her flesh, but rather in the clash between the two Origins within her.

So the question remained: where had this Ice Dragon's Origin come from?

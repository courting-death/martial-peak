## Chapter 2967 — A Heaven Beyond Heaven, A Man Above Men

"Yes." The Human Sovereign answered meekly. How could he still have any of his previous lofty bearing? The Sacred Tree's support had been stripped away from him, and his own strength was lacking, so he could only tuck his tail between his legs and play the obedient grandson.

However, doubt gnawed at his heart. Who exactly was this Qing? Could it be referring to the Sacred Tree itself? But if that were the case, how could he not have known of such a thing?

"Go out," Yang Kai called out somewhat dispiritedly. He reached out and patted Qing's towering trunk, then turned and walked outside.

The Human Sovereign followed close behind, keeping pace step by step.

After walking out of the mist and returning to the palace, the sounds of fierce battle immediately rang beside their ears, mixed now and then with waves of miserable screams and furious roars.

"They are fighting quite fiercely." Yang Kai swept his eyes around and found people lying all over the ground in disorder. Of the twenty-plus princes and princesses, ninety percent were sprawled out, most with broken arms or legs. A few appeared to be heavily injured, with large pools of blood beside them, and it was impossible to tell whether they were dead or alive.

As for the guards who had been watching over the Sacred Tree all along, roughly half had also been beaten to the ground. At this moment, in the sky, a red dragon thirty zhang long coiled through heaven and earth. Mouthfuls of scorching dragon breath sprayed out, and exquisite Dragon Clan Secret Techniques bloomed one after another, beating those surrounding humans, who were like ants, until they fled in panic with their heads in their hands.

The Human Sovereign was greatly shocked when he saw this, and the astonishment in his eyes was hard to conceal.

Although he knew the Dragon Clan was not easy to provoke, he had never imagined they would be this powerful.

This place had basically gathered all the experts he could mobilize, yet they still could do nothing to one Dragon Clan member. Instead, they had suffered heavy casualties. Moreover, looking at the battle situation in the sky, things were clearly not very optimistic. Although the red dragon's body was enormous and an obvious target, every attack carried terrifying might. If any of the experts still able to move took one hit, they would be either dead or wounded.

In the blink of an eye, two more Emperor Realm masters were struck by dragon breath and fell from the sky with their whole bodies on fire.

"Stop! All of you, stop!" the Human Sovereign hurriedly shouted.

Hearing this, the princes, princesses, and guards who could still move all retreated one after another, pulling away from Zhu Qing. When they saw Yang Kai and the Human Sovereign, they were stunned again.

Because at this moment, Yang Kai was actually standing in front of the Human Sovereign, while the lofty Human Sovereign was standing three steps behind him like an attendant, his expression more anxious than ever before.

"Royal Father!" the Second Princess cried out softly. She had the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, making her the strongest among the princes and princesses. Moreover, she had been entangled with the little black dog the whole time and had not directly faced Zhu Qing, so her injuries were not too severe. She only looked somewhat battered, her clothes tattered and torn, her fair skin exposed. She stared at the Human Sovereign in surprise and wanted to ask what exactly was going on, but no matter what, she could not get the words out.

"Bastards!" The Human Sovereign flew into a rage. Without any warning, the moment he opened his mouth, everyone was stunned. "These two are both honored guests of this Sovereign. Instead of entertaining them properly, you actually came into conflict with our guests. What is the meaning of this? You simply have no regard for this Sovereign!"

The entire place fell silent, and the Second Princess felt even dizzier.

This... Had they heard wrong? The reason they had acted was entirely because they had received the Human Sovereign's instructions and wanted to join everyone's strength to take down that Dragon Clan member. Yet in the blink of an eye, her Royal Father had actually given a completely different explanation.

Was this still the Human Sovereign?

The Third Prince reacted quickly and hurriedly knelt down, saying, "Royal Father, please calm your anger. We only felt a momentary itch to test our skills and exchanged a move or two with our guests. We had no intention of neglecting them."

"Test your skills?" The Human Sovereign's face darkened. He secretly glanced at Yang Kai, and after seeing that this fellow's expression was indifferent, he raised his hand and struck toward the Third Prince.

A fierce palm landed on the Third Prince's chest, sending him flying as he coughed up blood endlessly in midair.

The Human Sovereign shouted angrily, "Is this how you test your skills? You actually bullied the few with the many. You have utterly disgraced my Imperial Family!"

Ignoring his heavy injuries, the Third Prince got up again and knelt, trembling as he said, "Royal Father, please calm your anger. We know our mistake."

"We know our mistake!"

Those princes, princesses, and guards all hurriedly knelt down, even the two Third-Stage Emperors doing the same. Such a sight would be absolutely impossible to see outside. Third-Stage Emperors stood high above others. Outside, which one of them was not a mighty figure who could summon wind and rain? How could they possibly kneel to someone whose cultivation was weaker than their own?

But in Human Sovereign City, this was a common sight.

"You neglected our guests and even came to blows. Do you think one sentence of knowing your mistake is enough? Do not be too naive." The Human Sovereign's face was cold, his expression impartial and stern. "It seems you have lived too long in peace and do not know that there is always a heaven beyond heaven, and a man beyond man."

The Second Princess opened her mouth as if to say something, but a single glare from the Human Sovereign sent the words back down her throat, leaving her heart brimming with grievance.

In this Human Sovereign City, the Imperial Family was heaven. What heaven beyond heaven? What man above men?

"These blind bastards actually dared to attack your esteemed wife. I wonder how Your Excellency wishes to deal with them? Whether they are to be killed or carved up, it is entirely up to you. This old man will absolutely not interfere." The Human Sovereign turned his head and looked sincerely at Yang Kai.

When everyone heard this, all of them froze like wooden chickens. Even the Third Prince, who had made some guesses, was dumbfounded. He had never imagined the Human Sovereign would be so courteous to this outsider, even to the point of swallowing humiliation and begging for compromise.

Just what had happened inside earlier? Why had the Human Sovereign's attitude changed so greatly? He had even called himself this old man...

Although he could not guess, the Third Prince vaguely felt that something extraordinary must have happened just now. Otherwise, the Human Sovereign could not possibly be so servile and submissive.

Yang Kai sneered, raised his head to look at the graceful figure in the sky, and called out, "Qing'er, were you injured?"

Zhu Qing had already restored her human form. Hearing this, she slowly shook her head.

If she had not been a member of the Dragon Clan, facing so many powerful enemies, she absolutely would not have been able to retreat unscathed. But after transforming into a giant dragon, her defenses were astonishing. Even fighting many opponents alone, she had not fallen into any disadvantage. Although she had suffered quite a few attacks, she had not been injured.

Yang Kai smiled, turned to look at the Human Sovereign, and said, "Then there is nothing else. It was only a test of skill."

The Human Sovereign was incomparably moved. "Your Excellency is magnanimous. This old man is endlessly grateful."

He had truly been afraid Yang Kai would cling to this matter and not let go. He secretly rejoiced that the Dragon Clan member's cultivation was profound; otherwise, there would have been absolutely no way to settle this peacefully.

"Why are you still here? Get out, all of you! A bunch of worthless fools!" The Human Sovereign turned his head and roared furiously at the crowd.

The princes, princesses, and guards felt as though they had been granted amnesty. They hastily retreated, and before leaving, they made sure to gather the wounded from the ground and carry them away.

The little black dog appeared and disappeared like a ghost. No one knew where it had darted out from, but taking advantage of the Second Princess's distracted state, it bit down on her chest.

The Second Princess paled in shock and raised her hand to strike.

"Hm?" the Human Sovereign snorted coldly.

The palm the Second Princess had struck out immediately froze in midair. The little black dog became even braver and directly tore a large piece from the clothing in front of her chest, exposing the pink undergarment inside. Then it ran happily to the side, its tail raised high, as if it had avenged some deep hatred, looking extremely proud of itself.

The Second Princess, however, turned pale with fright. She hurriedly covered her exposed chest with both hands, her face flushing red as she stomped her foot hatefully and flashed away in retreat.

"I have let Your Excellency see something laughable." The Human Sovereign was unbearably embarrassed. In the past, he had thought these princes and princesses of his were still acceptable, but looking at them today, each one seemed more detestable than the last.

Yang Kai said indifferently, "My wife and I will be staying in the palace for a period of time."

The Human Sovereign was unwilling in his heart, but he had no way to refuse, so he could only say, "Your Excellency may stay as long as you wish."

"Rest assured, I will not stay long. Once I am prepared, I will leave."

The Human Sovereign did not dare respond. At a time like this, the more one said, the more mistakes one would make.

"In addition, I need all information regarding the Revolving World and Yin Wind Mountain. Send it to me as soon as possible."

"Yes!"

"There is one more matter where I must request Your Excellency's assistance. Accompanying my wife and me to the Revolving World are a member of the Dragon Clan and a human. I hope the Human Sovereign will keep watch for them. Should you discover any news of them, please inform me at once."

"Please rest assured, Your Excellency. Once the World Principles change, this old man will immediately dispatch people to search."

"Excellent."

...

One hour later, inside an extremely luxurious palace, Yang Kai lightly held Zhu Qing in his arms as he sat on a spacious chair. Zhu Qing sat on his lap, while the little black dog lay to the side, dozing.

"In that case, this Revolving World really is quite strange. If we want to leave this place, we can only pass through Yin Wind Mountain?" Zhu Qing asked softly.

Yang Kai held her delicate hand and gently toyed with it. Zhu Qing's hands were very beautiful. He had never paid attention before, but now, after their intimacy, he discovered that this pair of small hands was snow-white and flawless, soft as if boneless, with slender fingers like peeled scallions.

It was difficult to imagine how such a small hand could erupt with such terrifying power.

Zhu Qing felt uncomfortable all over from his playing and wanted to pull her hand back, but she did not have much strength. She could only glare at him, giving off a charm of another kind.

Yang Kai pretended not to see it and continued to play with her hand, saying absentmindedly, "Judging from the information we have obtained now, Yin Wind Mountain is the only way out, so we can only go to that place."

Zhu Qing said worriedly, "But that cold is not easy to resist. We did not go too deep into the Frozen Earth before, so no one knows what the cold inside the Frozen Earth is like. If the cold there comes from the deepest part of the Frozen Earth..."

It was very possible that even a Third-Stage Emperor would find it difficult to move an inch and would be frozen into an ice block with the slightest contact.

"No matter what, we have to go take a look, but first we must find Zhu Lie."

"Actually, this place... is also quite good. With that Sacred Tree's protection, Human Sovereign City has nothing to worry about and need not fear any disturbance. If we could live here..."

"You want to stay?" Yang Kai was slightly startled.

Zhu Qing was just about to speak when she suddenly seemed to notice something. Her face reddened, and she hurriedly tried to stand up from Yang Kai's lap.

Yang Kai pulled her back again.

"Someone is coming," Zhu Qing said somewhat shyly. After all, she had only recently experienced intimacy, and was easily embarrassed.

Seeing her insist on this, Yang Kai did not force her and let her go.

A moment later, a cold voice rang out from outside the hall. "Are the two Lords present? Fu Yu seeks an audience."

The little black dog, which had been lying on the ground all along, suddenly raised its head and bared its teeth fiercely. In the previous battle, it had formed a grudge with Fu Yu, so as soon as it heard this woman's voice, it became hostile.

"Come in."

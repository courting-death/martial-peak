## Chapter 2867 — The Second Demon King

As soon as Yang Kai spoke, the expressions of both Demon Kings changed.

Shaya exclaimed in astonishment, "My lord, you want to take him in?"

She had assumed Yang Kai had come to kill Bao Qi, so she had been exceptionally diligent in her efforts. Succubi were a vindictive race, especially the females—without exaggeration, they could hold a grudge over even the smallest slight until the end of time itself. Bao Qi had merely humiliated her once before, yet she had never forgotten it. When Yang Kai had asked where a Demon King could be found, she had immediately sold Bao Qi out without hesitation.

She originally thought that, having been brought to this world by Yang Kai, Bao Qi was doomed to die. She had been secretly delighted, only to discover that Yang Kai had other plans.

This development was not good.

If Bao Qi really was subdued by Lord Shaman Niu, wouldn't she have to work with him in the future? Shaya found the very thought unacceptable.

"You want to subdue me?" Bao Qi looked at Yang Kai with a mocking smile, unable to hold back a laugh. He sneered, "Arrogant outsider, I don't know how you made that bitch submit, but if you think you can subdue me, you'll be disappointed. This king is not the same as that wench!"

Shaya’s face turned cold as she said, “Mind your tongue—have you gone mad?”

Bao Qi snorted, glared at her, but didn't argue further, only watched Yang Kai warily.

He was determined never to bow his head to this outsider, but this world was indeed strange. He had to be extra cautious to avoid suffering a setback. For now, the priority was to figure out what this place was, then plan an escape.

"Shaya, is he stubborn?" Yang Kai suddenly turned and asked.

Shaya immediately replied, "Extremely. He even dares to defy the Demon Saint."

Bao Qi wore a proud expression, as if it was a great achievement.

Shaya continued, "So, my lord, don't waste words on him. Just kill him and be done with it."

She made the suggestion with ill intent, having her own plans. Not only did she not want to work with Bao Qi, but if Yang Kai subdued another Demon King, her special status would be gone, and her position in Yang Kai's eyes would surely be affected.

So she wished for Bao Qi's death as soon as possible.

"Then there's no other way." Yang Kai nodded slightly, deciding to cut the matter short. As he spoke, he raised a hand and gently pressed it toward Bao Qi.

Shaya watched with gleaming eyes, inwardly cursing him, hoping to see Bao Qi torn to pieces.

Bao Qi also instantly unleashed his demonic power, pouncing at Yang Kai the moment he made his move.

Yang Kai's raised hand clenched slightly, and Bao Qi was struck with terror. He suddenly felt an unimaginably vast force binding him tightly. As a dignified Demon King, he was suddenly like a newborn infant, completely unable to resist, pinned in place.

His eyes trembled violently, his whole body went cold, and the Demonic Qi he had summoned was suppressed inside him by an invisible force, leaving him unable to move.

In all his years of cultivation, Bao Qi had never encountered such a thing. He couldn't help but feel dazed.

Shaya burst out laughing, "Here, my lord is the master. Even the Demon Saint is no match, let alone you!"

Three months ago, when Yang Kai subdued her, he had said something similar. At the time, Shaya didn't care, thinking Yang Kai was just boasting, but facts proved that in this place, he truly had the power to rule everything.

And now, as she spoke those same words to Bao Qi, there was inevitably a trace of gloating in her tone.

Bao Qi's face turned red as he struggled desperately, but before the might of this grand world, a Demon King's resistance was like an ant trying to shake a tree—utterly futile.

Yang Kai's left eye changed color again, golden light flickering.

Staring into Bao Qi's eyes, he unleashed his Divine Soul power, destroying Bao Qi's Divine Soul defenses without hesitation.

Bao Qi screamed miserably, his face twisted in pain. Having his Divine Soul defenses forcibly destroyed caused him significant harm.

Seeing Bao Qi's wretched state, Shaya laughed even more viciously, her face full of satisfaction.

As Yang Kai's Divine Soul rampaged through Bao Qi's Sea of Consciousness, Bao Qi's eyes rolled back, his whole body trembling violently, his tower-like frame instantly drenched in sweat.

Not long after, the golden light in Yang Kai's eyes faded.

Bao Qi collapsed limply to the ground, his aura weak. If one didn't check carefully, they might think he was already dead.

Shaya frowned, clearly displeased.

She realized that although Bao Qi was in a sorry state, Yang Kai hadn't actually killed him. He had just planted a Soul Brand in his mind, just as he had done to her before. The difference was, she had willingly opened her Sea of Consciousness, while Bao Qi had been forced to accept it, so the treatment was different.

Regardless, with things as they were, she would really have to work with Bao Qi in the future. Shaya couldn't help but purse her lips.

After waiting a long time, Bao Qi finally recovered a bit, slowly stood up, and looked at Yang Kai with a complicated gaze. As a Demon King, he had actually been subdued by a Demon Commander, with a brand imprinted in his mind. Bao Qi could feel that the brand could control his life and death.

He had just mocked that bitch Shaya, but now he had become just like her.

Yang Kai gazed at him and said lightly, "If you're unwilling, you can always take your own life."

Bao Qi's eyes flickered, but he had no urge to die. It hadn't been easy to reach the Demon King realm—how could he want to die? There were people in the world who would rather die than submit, but Bao Qi was definitely not one of them.

"Very good!" Yang Kai's lips curled into a barely noticeable smile. "From now on, you are mine."

Shaya pursed her lips even more.

"My lord... what are your orders?" Bao Qi asked with some difficulty. It was the first time he had called someone weaker than himself 'my lord', and he couldn't help but feel awkward. He tried to stand as straight as possible to show his remaining pride.

"Eat this first." Yang Kai tossed him a jade bottle.

Shaya's beautiful eyes suddenly lit up.

Bao Qi caught it, hesitated, and asked, "What is this?"

Shaya snorted, "If my lord tells you to eat it, just eat it. Why so much nonsense? If you don't want it, give it to me."

The last time she had taken a Spiritual Pill from Yang Kai, its taste had left her unable to forget it—it was something she had never imagined could exist. Yet that fool Bao Qi failed to recognize its value, remaining so wary, even thinking it was some kind of poison.

He should have realized that, with a brand in his mind and his life and death in Yang Kai's hands, there was no need for poison.

Hearing this, Bao Qi immediately understood it must be something good. He sneered, opened the bottle, and, without even looking, threw the Spiritual Pill into his mouth. The next moment, his expression changed, and he sat down cross-legged involuntarily. The pain from his injured Divine Soul eased greatly, replaced by a look of immense pleasure.

Shaya looked disappointed, turning to Yang Kai with a face full of hope.

Another jade bottle flew toward her. Shaya caught it in surprise and beamed, "Thank you for your reward, my lord! Shaya will work even harder."

Half an hour later, Bao Qi opened his eyes, still a bit shocked. He found that the thing he had just taken had powerful healing effects, greatly nourishing his injured Divine Soul. Though he was not fully healed, he would recover in a month or two, with no lasting damage to hinder his future cultivation.

What was that? Why had he never heard of outsiders having such things?

Lost in thought, he suddenly heard Yang Kai say, "My name is Shaman Niu. Call me whatever you like."

"Yes, Lord Shaman Niu!" Bao Qi replied respectfully.

“Now tell me how your forces are deployed.”

Bao Qi immediately guessed what Shaman Niu was planning. He hesitated, sighed, and then disclosed everything about his forces without holding anything back.

He knew that hiding anything from this great shaman was pointless.

After listening, Yang Kai frowned. "You have quite a lot of people."

Bao Qi actually had six or seven thousand subordinates, more than Shaman Niu's tribe, and more than double what Shaya had controlled.

After three months of battles and recruitment, Shaman Niu's tribe only had about five thousand people.

With such a huge number, if they clashed head-on, even with Shamanic Art support, Shaman Niu's tribe would inevitably suffer casualties. In a war of over ten thousand, deaths were unavoidable.

After pondering for a while, Yang Kai said, "Here's what you'll do when you go out..."

After giving detailed instructions, Yang Kai sent Bao Qi and Shaya out of the Small Xuan Realm together.

In the room, Shaya looked at Bao Qi with a cold smile. "Don't mess this up, or you'll regret it."

As the first Demon King to submit to Yang Kai, Shaya, though not as strong as Bao Qi, still behaved assertively to secure her unique position in Yang Kai's heart.

In the past, if Shaya had said such things, Bao Qi would have argued or even fought with her. But now, in a bad mood, he had no energy to quarrel and just nodded lightly. "Don't worry, my lord."

"Good, then I'll wait for your good news," Yang Kai said, giving Shaya a look. The two of them flew out of the palace one after the other, turning into black mist and leaving the city.

After they left, several Demon Commanders rushed in. One square-faced Demon Commander looked at Bao Qi and asked, "My lord, what did that bitch want?"

Bao Qi's expression was cold as he replied, "Nothing, she just came to ask for reinforcements."

"Reinforcements?" The Demon Commanders were all surprised.

Bao Qi said, "A group of outsiders is after her, so she came to me for help." As he spoke, he pointed at the square-faced Demon Commander. "Take two thousand men and go check it out."

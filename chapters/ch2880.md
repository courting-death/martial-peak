## Chapter 2880 — Curse

Mo Duo had not been on this land for long, but he was already thoroughly familiar with the methods of these outsiders. Suddenly, he noticed that one of them was using a completely different kind of power, and this power was actually able to easily kill a formidable Demon King. Naturally, Mo Duo was very interested.

So he took action personally, wanting to capture this outsider alive and study just what kind of power this was.

When he suddenly appeared in front of Yang Kai, he looked down at the outsider before him without the slightest restraint, his powerful Divine Sense sweeping over him unscrupulously, as if to examine him inside and out.

Yet the result left Mo Duo somewhat shocked, because even he could not penetrate the depths of this outsider’s Sea of Consciousness. It was as if there was an invisible force protecting it, so strong that even a Demon Saint was helpless before it.

What a strange fellow. Mo Duo’s interest grew even stronger, and he asked, “What is your name?”

Maintaining his Dragon Transformation, Yang Kai stared warily at the young man before him. Although it was their first meeting, Yang Kai still knew his identity, because those eyes felt so familiar—he had always felt their gaze upon him.

He nimbly leapt out of the giant pit, stood ten zhang away from Mo Duo, clapped his hands, and grinned, “Your grandpa!”

As soon as he finished speaking, Yang Kai attacked. With a clap and a pull of his hands, a massive Moon Blade took shape and slashed thunderously toward Mo Duo.

“Oh…” Mo Duo frowned, showing a look of surprise, but even more of delight. He did not dodge, but instead reached out to grab the pitch-black Moon Blade, as if wanting to experience it firsthand.

The Moon Blade sliced through his large hand, banishing half his arm into the void. Blood gushed from the wound.

Mo Duo’s expression grew even more excited, as if he had discovered something novel and amusing. Not only did he not retreat from the pain, he even took a step forward.

Yang Kai’s attacks, as fierce as a storm, arrived right on cue. After unleashing the Moon Blade, he lunged at Mo Duo, meeting the Demon Saint’s advancing body head-on.

Two dragon claws transformed into a flurry of claw shadows, instantly enveloping Mo Duo.

The sound of tearing rang out incessantly as blood spurted wildly from Mo Duo’s body, which was quickly torn to pieces.

In just three breaths, Demon Saint Mo Duo met a violent death, reduced to a pile of rotten flesh.

The Demon Kings watching from afar were dumbfounded, staring blankly at the scene, wishing they could pluck out their own eyes and throw them over for a closer look.

Demon Saint Mo Duo… was dead?

How could this be? And the one who killed him wasn’t even a King or Saint among the outsiders, but just some obscure nobody.

Yang Kai was also a bit dazed. When this Mo Duo appeared before him, he had sensed the man’s strength—this was definitely someone on par with a Great Emperor! That kind of aura could only be exuded by a Great Emperor.

Yet such a figure had actually been killed by him in just a few moves?

There was no joy at all. Instead, he felt uneasy.

He quickly realized the source of his unease: the supposedly dead Mo Duo had inexplicably appeared behind him, as if everything he had just experienced was nothing but an illusion.

But Yang Kai knew it wasn’t an illusion, because the feeling of contact and the spray of blood had been so real. If it were an illusion, there was no way he wouldn’t have noticed.

The Mo Duo standing behind him spread his arms, demonic qi swirling around his fingertips, rapidly closing in to form a cage, as if to capture Yang Kai alive.

How could Yang Kai just sit and wait for death? Facing such a powerful enemy, one on par with a Great Emperor, he immediately used his trump card.

With a light ringing sound, a plain little bell appeared in his palm. The bell’s surface was engraved with images of mountains, rivers, birds, fish, and beasts, and it exuded an aura of the Primordial Era.

The Mountain and River Bell!

Though wielding such an Imperial Treasure was still taxing with Yang Kai’s current strength, it was nevertheless his strongest weapon.

As the bell rang, Mo Duo frowned, and the pitch-black demonic qi between his ten fingers shrank back, causing the encirclement to instantly reveal a gap. Yang Kai reacted swiftly, leaping through the opening and, turning around, hurled the Mountain and River Bell downward.

The little bell spun rapidly, growing larger as it descended, and came crashing down toward Mo Duo.

Mo Duo looked up, wanting to escape, but was shocked to find that within the bell’s range, everything was sealed off. Even with his strength, it would take some effort to break free.

Before he could do anything, the Mountain and River Bell crashed down with a thunderous boom, suppressing him.

Yang Kai’s expression was cold and stern as he rushed forward and struck the Mountain and River Bell.

Clang…

A deafening sound erupted, and visible sound waves spread out from the Mountain and River Bell, sweeping in all directions. The Demon Kings who had been preparing to help were struck by the waves, falling to the ground like birds with broken wings, their faces full of shock and panic. The weaker ones even spat blood, as if struck by lightning.

As for the demons still besieging the Southern Barbarian Tribe in the giant pit, they could not withstand the terrifying might of the Mountain and River Bell at all. Whether ordinary demons or Demon Commanders and Demon Generals, they were all reduced to dust by the sweeping sound waves.

With a single ring of the bell, over ten thousand enemies were annihilated.

Yet Yang Kai felt no joy, because Mo Duo, who had been suppressed under the Mountain and River Bell, exploded into a cloud of blood mist, and the unease in his heart only grew stronger.

“Interesting. Who exactly are you? You’re not a member of the Barbarian Tribe!”

Mo Duo’s voice eerily sounded from behind, full of vigor, as if he hadn’t been injured at all. He seemed to possess an immortal body, able to recover even after exploding into blood mist.

“Told you, your grandpa!” Yang Kai retrieved the Mountain and River Bell, turned to look at Mo Duo, his gaze scrutinizing.

Mo Duo didn’t care, or perhaps simply disdained to argue. Instead, he studied Yang Kai seriously, and after a moment, his face lit up with sudden understanding. With great interest, he asked, “Which world are you from?”

The methods Yang Kai used and the treasures he wielded were clearly not something the Barbarian Tribe could possess. Mo Duo naturally assumed Yang Kai, like the demons, came from another world.

But Yang Kai ignored the question and smiled. “So you’re just tough on the outside but weak within.”

After two rounds of fighting, he finally saw through a bit. This Demon Saint Mo Duo was indeed a Great Emperor-level existence, but right now, he couldn’t display the full power of a Great Emperor. Otherwise, even at his peak, Yang Kai wouldn’t have been able to stir up any trouble before him.

Bao Qi was right—Mo Duo must have paid some price to enter here from the Demon Domain, so his strength had been diminished, and the Black Sun world-destroying move he used earlier had definitely cost him dearly.

At this moment, Yang Kai was facing someone who had the foundation of a Great Emperor but couldn’t unleash all his power.

This was somewhat similar to his own current situation.

“Answer my question first.” Mo Duo looked at Yang Kai seriously, as if they were merely chatting rather than confronting each other as enemies.

Yang Kai said, “I am Shaman Niu, Shaman Niu of the Southern Barbarian Tribe!”

Mo Duo shook his head and reached out a hand to Yang Kai. “Come to my side. Whatever the Barbarian Tribe can give you, I can give you too. I can even take you to see more worlds—something the Barbarian Tribe cannot offer.”

Yang Kai sneered disdainfully, pointing a dragon claw at him. “Take your people and get out of this land, or you will be destroyed!”

Mo Duo sighed, withdrew his hand, and looked toward the giant pit. “Those who are still struggling there are your people. They are already on the verge of extinction. Your threats are meaningless.”

Yang Kai snarled, “I will avenge them! Today, three hundred thousand of the Southern Barbarian Tribe have fallen. One day, the demons will pay the same price!”

Mo Duo smiled. “But you won’t get that chance. As long as I stand here, you cannot escape.”

“If I want to leave, you can’t stop me!” Yang Kai grinned, revealing a mouthful of gleaming fangs.

Mo Duo said nothing more. The Demonic Qi within him began to surge, as he clearly had lost patience. To him, Yang Kai’s appearance was merely a novelty, and he had invited him to join the demons only so he could study his power. Since Yang Kai refused, Mo Duo could only destroy him.

He raised his finger slightly, and a pitch-black energy orb appeared at his fingertip, spinning rapidly. As Mo Duo poured in his power, the orb spun faster and faster. Though its size did not increase, its might grew ever more terrifying, exuding a truly shocking aura.

He lightly pointed in Yang Kai’s direction, and the energy orb shot toward him.

Yang Kai made no move to dodge, simply maintaining his smile.

Mo Duo frowned imperceptibly.

Just as the energy orb was about to hit Yang Kai, a strange fluctuation of power surged forth, and Yang Kai’s figure suddenly vanished.

“Teleportation!” Mo Duo’s expression changed slightly. He suddenly looked in a certain direction and struck at the void there. The blow seemed ordinary, but it shattered the barriers of space and struck that mysterious area.

Faintly, Mo Duo heard a muffled grunt, followed by Shaman Niu’s voice echoing across the world.

“The Demon Race will pay the price for today’s scheme! All demons will live in fear and unrest!”

The voice faded, and Mo Duo could no longer sense Shaman Niu’s presence. Only his words lingered in the air, like a curse.

Mo Duo’s face darkened, and after a long while, he gave a soft snort.

If he were at his peak, even if the other had used teleportation, he could have dragged him back from the void. But as Yang Kai had guessed, though he still had the foundation of a Demon Saint, he could not display the power of one. Against an enemy who could use space teleportation, he was utterly helpless.

Everyone heard Yang Kai’s parting words, including the more than one hundred thousand demons and the few hundred surviving members of the Barbarian Tribe.

Shaman Dang, covered in blood, fought several Demon Kings alone. There was hardly an uninjured spot on his body, and he was missing an arm and a leg.

At his side, only a handful of Barbarian Tribe members still stood, but their destruction was only a matter of time.

Hearing Yang Kai’s voice, Shaman Dang managed a faint smile and, in his final moments, ignited the fire of life and performed the Sacrifice of Life.

## Chapter 2796 — The Five Elements United, A World Is Born

Just as the four searched for the cause, the scene that had occurred earlier unexpectedly played out once more.

By now, it had already happened more than a dozen times.

For such a situation to arise on the Fourth Layer, it was undoubtedly because several Emperor Realm juniors were encountering something during their tempering within.

Among those juniors, not counting Yang Kai, the remaining Lan Xun, Xiao Chen, and Lei Ting were all descendants of the Star God Palace’s upper echelon—none of them could be allowed to meet with mishap.

The matter was of great importance, so even Great Elder Lei Hong dared not delay any longer. He could only send word to the Great Emperor, reporting the situation here and requesting the Great Emperor’s decision on how to proceed.

“Has the Great Emperor not given instructions yet?” Xue Zhengmao paced back and forth impatiently, then suddenly stopped and looked at Lei Hong to ask.

Although none of the disciples tempering themselves on the Fourth Layer were his own descendants, as one of the Four Elders of Star God Palace, his life was tied to the Palace, and he cared deeply about everything concerning it.

If not for this, he would not have relied on his strength to bully the weak and deal with Yang Kai that day.

Lei Hong had just shaken his head when, all of a sudden, a streak of white light shot over from the distance. His spirits lifted instantly as he reached out to catch it. After a brief inspection, however, his expression turned rather strange.

“What did the Great Emperor say?” Xiao Yuyang asked in a deep voice.

Lei Hong glanced at the other three Elders and replied, “Wait and see what happens..."

“Wait and see? How could it just be ‘wait and see’...” Xue Zhengmao muttered, clearly not expecting such instructions from the Great Emperor.

Xiao Yuyang pondered for a moment before speaking, “Since the Great Emperor said so, he must have his reasons. We cannot act rashly and interfere.”

No one dared to defy the Great Emperor’s orders. Since the Great Emperor had given clear instructions, everyone could only obey. Secretly, they guessed that perhaps those juniors were not in any real danger within the Fourth Layer, which was why the Great Emperor was unconcerned.

……

Half a month later, in a shattered space, Yang Kai frowned deeply.

Over these half a month, he had shuttled through various broken spaces, urging the Xuan Realm Pearl to devour the Law Power of countless shattered spaces, reaping immense gains.

Not only did he gain some personal insight into the power of these worlds, but the sheer amount of Law Power devoured and absorbed by the Xuan Realm Pearl was unimaginable. Each time a broken world was completely annihilated, it meant the Xuan Realm Pearl had consumed every last trace of its Laws.

Yet each time, Yang Kai had to face the rejection and hostility of those worlds. Some worlds were large, some small; the hostility varied in strength.

Thunder rumbled and lightning struck—this was the most common occurrence. Some broken worlds even harbored hidden ancient remnants, each one powerful. Driven by the world’s will, these ancient remnants would come to kill Yang Kai in a desperate battle, all to expel the outsider. No matter how well Yang Kai hid, he could not escape this fate.

Fortunately, this was only the Fourth Layer. Even if some ancient remnants lingered, their strength was not too terrifying. With Yang Kai’s current cultivation, dealing with them was not difficult.

If it were the highest, Fifth Layer, things might not be so simple.

However, the Fifth Layer could not be entered directly from the buffer zone; one could only search for an entrance from certain broken spaces within the Fourth Layer.

These days, Yang Kai had destroyed dozens of broken spaces and slain countless ancient remnants. Yet he had never found an entrance to the Fifth Layer. He did not know if his luck was simply too poor, or if the entrances were just too few.

Half a month’s hard work had naturally yielded great rewards.

But now, Yang Kai faced a difficult problem.

He could clearly feel the Xuan Realm Pearl had devoured an immense amount of Law Power—so much it seemed saturated. Yet it still had not transformed from quantity to quality; something was missing, preventing that final step.

This left him somewhat conflicted.

“The Dao gives birth to One, One gives birth to Two, Two gives birth to Three, Three gives birth to all things. Yin and Yang transform into the Five Elements. When the Five Elements are united, a world is born... Five Elements... Five Elements...”

Muttering to himself, Yang Kai’s eyes suddenly lit up, as if he had grasped something.

Moments later, he abruptly straightened, tore open space with a wave of his hand, and returned to the buffer zone. His eyes shone sharply as he searched through the endless clouds and mist.

After a short while, his eyes flashed brilliantly. “Found it!”

His figure flickered as he charged straight toward a mass of dazzling golden mist.

In the next instant, he appeared in a world twisted by golden Laws—the very first broken world Yang Kai had entered half a month ago.

Lacking the Five Elements? Then let’s complete the Five Elements.

In this Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, these things were anything but scarce.

He summoned the Xuan Realm Pearl, opened its boundary, and began devouring the Laws and Metal Spiritual Qi of this place with all his might.

Yang Kai devoted part of his mind to staying alert to his surroundings.

Having done this many times, Yang Kai had gained experience. No matter what, this world would never just stand by and watch as he seized its Law Power—it would certainly interfere and resist.

Only, depending on the world and its Laws, the methods of interference and rejection varied.

There was no sign of clouds gathering in the sky, which allowed Yang Kai to breathe a little easier. He had truly been traumatized by lightning strikes lately; just hearing thunder made him flinch. Fortunately, in this place, the golden Laws twisted everything, leaving no room for other Laws, so there would be no lightning here.

However, the world’s hostility and rejection were fiercer than ever. If not for Yang Kai’s solid foundation and formidable strength, he would have been expelled long ago.

“What will it be...” Far from nervous, Yang Kai actually looked a bit excited.

Not everyone could experience standing against an entire world. Each time he did, he grew stronger.

All the worlds Yang Kai had sought out before were those with mixed Laws. This was his first time in a world of a single Law, so he could not help but feel some anticipation.

Zheng...

Suddenly, a clear ringing echoed through the air, like a treasured sword being drawn from its sheath.

Yang Kai looked toward the sound, his brows immediately rising.

Not far away, a blade of Spiritual Qi radiating golden light suddenly took shape, hovering in midair. The long sword spun gently, its tip pointing straight at Yang Kai.

“A Sword of Metal, huh…” Yang Kai grinned, “This could be interesting…”

Zheng zheng zheng zheng...

The clear ringing sounded again and again, interrupting Yang Kai’s muttering. In the next moment, hundreds of Swords of Metal suddenly appeared from all directions. Each one was identical, without the slightest difference, and every sword radiated an extremely sharp aura, as if they could split mountains and cut seas.

Gulp...

Yang Kai could not help but swallow hard, his expression turning a bit strained.

So many Swords of Metal, each one razor-sharp and brimming with hostility—there was no way Yang Kai could feel any joy at the sight.

Worse still, even more Swords of Metal were condensing. In the blink of an eye, above and below, front and back, left and right, Yang Kai seemed to be surrounded by a sea of Swords of Metal. In such a sea, he was like a lone boat, in danger of capsizing at any moment.

After all those Swords of Metal appeared, they did not immediately attack Yang Kai. Instead, they all rang out together, issuing a warning with surprising intelligence. The overwhelming sword intent seemed as if it could shatter the heavens and overturn the earth.

Yang Kai remained unmoved, his expression solemn as he stood ready.

After three breaths, the Swords of Metal began to tremble, then spun rapidly, leaving afterimages in place. As if controlled by an invisible hand, they broke through the barriers of space and, in an instant, struck Yang Kai.

The Myriad Sword had long since been summoned, its supreme sword intent erupting as Yang Kai wielded it, defending himself as solidly as a fortress.

But such a defense lasted only a single breath before it was broken.

With a series of light clinks and a shower of sparks, Yang Kai’s defenses were breached, leaving him wide open. More Swords of Metal surged in, as if to tear him to pieces.

Startled, Yang Kai realized that even though he had not underestimated the power of these Swords of Metal, he had still misjudged them. In his haste, he quickly urged the Undying Five Elements Sword to surround himself with sword qi, but even that was shattered in an instant.

Sss sss sss sss...

With a series of soft sounds, Yang Kai’s clothes were soon in tatters, and he looked utterly wretched. Even his cheek was slashed open, revealing white bone and streaming blood—a truly ghastly sight.

After that volley, the Swords of Metal calmed down considerably and did not attack again.

The Way of Heaven takes from the surplus to make up for the deficiency. To the will of this world, there was no need to go to extremes—just to make Yang Kai retreat in the face of difficulty.

Yang Kai had experienced such scenes countless times and was naturally familiar with them.

So, though he had suffered a minor setback, he had no intention of giving up. Instead, he urged the Xuan Realm Pearl even harder to devour the Laws of this place.

The world was enraged.

Countless Swords of Metal radiated even more terrifying sword intent, spinning ever more fiercely.

Swish...

With a soft sound, the Swords of Metal attacked again.

Yang Kai was ready. He shouted in a deep voice, “Dragon Transformation!”

The Origin Power of the Golden Sacred Dragon suddenly manifested. A massive golden dragon head flashed behind Yang Kai and then merged into his body. In the next moment, Yang Kai’s figure swelled, his already tall frame shooting up to over three zhang. Scales appeared on his exposed skin, and bulges rose on either side of his head, as if something was about to break out.

His hands became dragon claws, incomparably sharp. A dragon tail grew from his back, and the Feihong Black Dragon Armor covered him, exuding an awe-inspiring might.

During this period, whenever he had spare time, Yang Kai had been cultivating this true Dragon Transformation Secret Technique. With the help of the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power, his progress was twice as fast for half the effort. By now, his Dragon Transformation was even stronger than last time.

The last time he transformed in front of Hua Qingsi, Yang Kai remembered his height was only two zhang. Now, he was over three zhang! If he kept cultivating, he would surely grow even larger.

If not for the Feihong Black Dragon Armor, Yang Kai would probably be stark naked again by now.

The Dragon Transformation took but an instant, and the countless Swords of Metal were already upon him.

With a sweep of his dragon claw, Yang Kai grabbed more than a dozen Swords of Metal. With a slight squeeze, there was a crisp crack, and all those swords turned to motes of light and vanished.

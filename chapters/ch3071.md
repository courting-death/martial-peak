## Chapter 3071 — Wu Kuang Is Here

"Is doing this worth it?"

A soft question rang out. Fu Xuan raised her head to look up at Mo Huang, whose thick stubble pricked her forehead, leaving it itchy and numb. Yet she cherished the sensation immensely, gently rubbing her head against it as though trying to etch the feeling into the depths of her Soul.

A rare tenderness filled Mo Huang’s rugged face as he reached out to caress her cheek. Smiling faintly, he said, “Though the realm may be as beautiful as a painting, how could it compare to the vermilion mark between your brows?”

Fu Xuan's beautiful eyes shone brilliantly.

Mo Huang grinned again. "That brat Li Wuyi said this before. I merely borrowed it."

Fu Xuan giggled. "So those were Wuyi's words."

Mo Huang sighed. "You have suffered all these years."

Fu Xuan shook her head. "I expected this long ago. It was no great suffering. Haven't we met again today?"

Mo Huang declared solemnly, "This time, I will never let you leave my side again!"

"That would mean becoming enemies with all of Dragon Isle." Fu Xuan rested her head against Mo Huang's broad chest and listened to his heartbeat, yet it seemed to her like the most beautiful immortal music in the world. Her face was filled with a blissful smile.

Mo Huang snorted. "From the day we met, I was already prepared to become Dragon Isle's enemy."

When they met back then, they had both been in the prime of their youth, full of vigor and ambition. At a glance, he recognized that proud woman as a member of the Dragon Clan. As the Beast Great Emperor, he commanded all the world's myriad beasts, but he had never experienced taming a Dragon. His heart immediately stirred, and once the idea arose, it became impossible to contain. He schemed time and again to approach her, even resorting to force, and finally managed to subdue her. Unexpectedly, however, he had stepped into a land of tenderness from which he could never extricate himself, and had also formed a grudge with the Dragon Clan that no outsider knew about.

Recalling those years, a warm smile appeared on Mo Huang's face. It was the wisest decision he had ever made.

"How is Xiaoqi?" Fu Xuan suddenly asked.

"She has already grown up, but she has been spoiled a little. You should come back to Spirit Beast Island with me and discipline her."

Fu Xuan asked, "She doesn't know her own identity, does she?"

Mo Huang shook his head. "I haven't told her yet. Mm, that girl has been rather troubling lately."

"What happened?" Fu Xuan was startled. Xiaoqi was her own flesh and blood, after all. Although she had never seen her daughter again after giving birth to her, mother and daughter were bound by heart, so how could she not be concerned?

“She can’t stop thinking about some brat,” Mo Huang said, his face darkening.

Fu Xuan was taken aback, then began to laugh.

"What are you laughing at?"

Fu Xuan stopped laughing and said, "Considering her age, Xiaoqi has about reached that time. It isn't strange for one or two men to catch her eye."

Mo Huang shook his head like a rattle-drum. "No, no. She is still too young. It isn't time for her to consider such things."

Fu Xuan sighed softly. “Though I gave birth to her, I never had the chance to raise her. You’ll have to look after her affairs yourself—but whatever you do, don’t let her come to resent you.”

Mo Huang was shocked. "She would hate me for that little bastard?"

Fu Xuan pursed her lips in a smile. “There’s no telling. Once daughters grow up, their hearts tend to turn toward others.”

Hearing this, Mo Huang's expression grew even darker, and he suddenly began worrying over gains and losses.

"All right, we should go over and take a look as well."

Mo Huang frowned. "Let's not. We can stay here and talk, then wait until those two old fellows finish handling the Dragon Clan's affairs."

Fu Xuan looked up at him. "Take me to have a look. They are my clansmen, after all."

Mo Huang was least able to resist her delicate, dependent manner, so he nodded repeatedly. "Good, good, good. Since you say so, we will go take a look." After a pause, he smiled. "If we're lucky, we might even see that little bastard."

By the time he finished speaking, he had already flown off with Fu Xuan.

Fu Xuan was stunned for a moment before belatedly realizing, "You mean the man Xiaoqi has taken a liking to is also on Dragon Isle?"

"Yes. I was able to sneak in this time without anyone noticing partly thanks to him." Mo Huang nodded. If Yang Kai had not caused a huge disturbance on Dragon Isle and drawn Zhu Yan's and Fu Zhun's attention, he could not have secretly slipped inside.

Under ordinary circumstances, if he set foot on Dragon Isle, Zhu Yan and Fu Zhun would certainly notice him immediately.

"Is he a member of the Dragon Clan?" Fu Xuan frowned. She was a member of the Dragon Clan herself, so she naturally knew just how debauched the private lives of the clan's male Dragons were. If her daughter had taken a liking to one of them, this matter would truly be difficult to handle.

"He isn't from the Dragon Clan, merely a human," Mo Huang replied with a shake of his head.

"A human..." Fu Xuan found this even stranger. "How could a human enter Dragon Isle? And what did he come here for?"

The corner of Mo Huang’s mouth twitched. “He came to steal the bride.”

Fu Xuan's face filled with astonishment.

Only after she questioned him incessantly did she learn of the earth-shattering things Yang Kai had done on Dragon Isle. She was repeatedly amazed, feeling that this fellow was far too bold. Then, after thinking about it, she realized just how similar the circumstances of this man named Yang Kai were to Mo Huang's.

Both were humans, both had married a Dragon woman, both had met with Dragon Isle's fierce opposition, and both had been forced to become Dragon Isle's enemies.

Fu Xuan suddenly felt some sympathy for Yang Kai.

Mo Huang was one of the Ten Great Emperors, yet even he had been unable to take her away from Dragon Isle back then. Yang Kai would certainly fail this time as well. This love between a Human and a Dragon was destined to end in tragedy; the Dragon Clan's stubbornness had already been proven.

......

A figure suddenly shot out of the sea, drawing the attention of Yang Kai and the others.

Turning to look, they saw that the Incarnation had already returned to the height of an ordinary person. Steam rose from its entire body as it flew out of the sea.

Zhu Qing and Fu Ling both examined it in surprise, wondering where this fellow had suddenly come from. However, the Divine Spirit aura emanating from this stone man was extremely distinct, allowing them to recognize its identity as Shi Huo at a glance.

What surprised them even more was that this Shi Huo actually carried Yang Kai's aura. If they had investigated it with Divine Sense instead of looking with their eyes, they might have mistaken it for Yang Kai himself.

Zhu Qing looked suspiciously at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai said softly, "One of us."

The Incarnation had been formed by combining his Soul Incarnation with a Stone Puppet. Even though it had integrated Shi Huo's Origin and become the Divine Spirit Shi Huo, its foundation would not change.

It had previously fought a great battle with a member of the Dragon Clan. Since it had now returned alone, its opponent had clearly fled, though no one knew how the battle had ultimately ended.

Yang Kai was just about to ask when a single sentence from the Incarnation rooted him to the spot.

"Wu Kuang is here!"

Yang Kai exclaimed, "How do you know?"

Zhu Qing also frowned. "Wu Kuang? Which Wu Kuang?"

Fu Ling laughed. "Why is that name the same as the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor's?"

The Heaven Devourer Great Emperor was a legend of his generation, an existence no one had surpassed in more than a hundred thousand years. Thus, even the proud Dragon Clan remembered this name deeply and feared it greatly.

The Incarnation said, "I should not have awakened today. A certain force woke me."

"What force?"

"The power of the Secret Art I cultivate!"

After it said this, Yang Kai immediately felt the matter was almost certainly as they suspected. The Secret Art cultivated by the Incarnation was the Heaven Devouring Battle Art, a Divine Ability created by Wu Kuang. Back then, it was precisely by relying on this Secret Art that the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor roamed the universe without equal. Several Great Emperors had died at his hands during the Great Emperors' War.

If there was anyone in this world who could sense Wu Kuang's presence, it could only be the Incarnation.

No wonder the Incarnation had awakened early. It turned out that his previous deduction had not been wrong; rather, a special situation had arisen.

"How could he be on Dragon Isle? If he is here, doesn't that mean Senior Duan is also on Dragon Isle?"

After the battle in the Shattered Star Sea, Wu Kuang and Duan Hongchen had shared one body with two Souls. Later, inside Qingyang Shrine's Divine Roaming Mirror, Wu Kuang seized control of the body, escaped from Qingyang Shrine, and vanished without a trace.

Yang Kai had never imagined that Wu Kuang would actually appear on Dragon Isle.

As he thought about it, Yang Kai's heart suddenly clenched. The Incarnation could sense Wu Kuang's presence only because Wu Kuang had used the Heaven Devouring Battle Art, and Wu Kuang would not use it for no reason. Recalling the earlier sight of a fallen Dragon transforming into rain, a horrifying thought surfaced in Yang Kai's mind.

"Not good!" Yang Kai turned to look in a certain direction and shouted in a low voice, "All of you, follow me!"

By the time he finished speaking, he had already shot away. As he flew, a series of crackling explosions came from all over his body, and he returned to his original appearance.

Only after they left did the members of the Dragon Clan who had been defeated earlier exchange glances. Then they all followed.

Wu Kuang had appeared on Dragon Isle and used the Heaven Devouring Battle Art, while a member of the Dragon Clan had now fallen. It was highly likely that he had already devoured a Dragon. Possessing the heritage of a Great Emperor and the Heaven Devouring Battle Art, no one knew how much harm Wu Kuang could cause if he was not stopped.

Yang Kai did not care about Dragon Isle's safety, but Liu Yan, Jiu Feng, and the others were all over there. If they encountered Wu Kuang, they would likely be unable to resist him.

In a moment, Yang Kai had already returned to the original battlefield.

Looking around, he could not help breathing a sigh of relief.

Liu Yan and Jiu Feng were both safe and sound. The members of the Dragon Clan who had been fighting them had already stopped, gathering together with ugly expressions. The death of a Dragon had clearly dealt them an unimaginable blow, so who would still be in the mood to continue fighting at a time like this?

Moreover, none of the Dragon Clan members here were missing, so Wu Kuang had clearly not made his move in this place.

Seeing Yang Kai arrive, Liu Yan hurriedly flew over.

Yang Kai reached out and stroked her head, asking with concern, "Are you hurt?"

Liu Yan shook her head.

She had benefited greatly from today's battle. After all, the experience of fighting the Dragon Clan was exceedingly rare. She believed her strength would improve again after this battle.

"Boy Yang, was that your doing?" Jiu Feng looked at Yang Kai and asked with a frown.

If Yang Kai really had killed that Dragon, then a great disaster would surely befall him, so Jiu Feng could not help asking.

Yang Kai shook his head. "It wasn't me."

Jiu Feng immediately asked in surprise, "Then who was it? Could Zhu Yan or Fu Zhun really have died? Uh... forget it."

She turned to look in that direction, where two streaks of light—one azure and one white—shot toward them like lightning. In the blink of an eye, they landed before the crowd, revealing the figures of Zhu Yan and Fu Zhun.

## Chapter 2896 — Are You an Idiot?

The Demon Race, just like the Barbarian Tribe, placed great emphasis on tempering the body. Especially someone like Mo Duo, a Demon Saint—though his flesh could not be called immortal or indestructible, he had long since cultivated his body to its utmost limit.

If Yang Kai were to compete with him in terms of divine abilities or secret techniques, perhaps Mo Duo would have to put in more effort. Yet Yang Kai actually charged straight at him, clearly intending to engage in close combat. This made Mo Duo feel both amused and enraged.

He was amused by how foolish his enemy was, and enraged that, for once, someone dared to underestimate him.

Mo Duo struck with all his might.

When their fists collided, Mo Duo’s body merely swayed, while Yang Kai staggered back several steps.

Even after using the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, Yang Kai was still no match for a Demon Saint wielding only seventy percent of his strength in terms of raw physical power! The might of a Demon Saint was evident.

The tremendous backlash surged like a raging river, flooding into his meridians and flesh. Yang Kai felt pain unlike anything he had experienced before, making him grit his teeth in agony.

Before he could steady himself, Mo Duo’s counterattack had already arrived. That fist, brimming with destructive power, slammed heavily into Yang Kai’s abdomen. As Demonic Qi surged, Yang Kai’s eyes nearly bulged out, and for a moment, he lost the ability to breathe.

It wasn’t over yet—Mo Duo’s next punch smashed into Yang Kai’s face, sending him flying, tumbling through the air and coughing up blood all the while.

If not for the massive boost to his physique from the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, just those two punches would have been enough to claim the life of a Third Stage Emperor. Even so, Yang Kai was in terrible shape—his stomach churned, and the Demonic Qi that had invaded his body rampaged through his meridians and flesh, leaving him dizzy and unsettled.

Crashing to the ground in a sorry state, Yang Kai scrambled to his feet. Before he could regain his balance, Mo Duo appeared before him like a ghost and threw another heavy punch.

Just then, a sly glint flashed across Yang Kai’s seemingly dazed face. As his eyes sparkled, the Mountain and River Bell was summoned, instantly enveloping the ground beneath his feet.

Mo Duo’s eyes widened. He wanted to pull back his fist, but it was too late. Yang Kai had timed the summoning of the Mountain and River Bell perfectly—right at the peak of Mo Duo’s attack.

Clang…

The destructive punch landed on the Mountain and River Bell, sending visible ripples across the ancient bell. The aura of the Primordial Era instantly spread out.

Mo Duo’s fist, which struck the Mountain and River Bell, was instantly mangled and bloody. Even as a Demon Saint, the backlash from his own full-force blow was hard to bear. Worse still, as the bell rang, Mo Duo suddenly felt as if he were being suppressed.

It was as if countless mountains, each ten thousand zhang tall, had descended above his head, making it hard for him to straighten his back.

The bell’s toll could suppress mountains and rivers, and its Imperial Aura could reverse the heavens. Throughout the ages, this was how the strongest experts had evaluated the Mountain and River Bell. The greatest power of this Primordial Era artifact was suppression—suppression of all things under heaven.

Mo Duo’s momentum plummeted.

Suddenly, the Mountain and River Bell vanished. Yang Kai lunged forward with a savage expression, thrusting his dragon claws—sharper than any weapon—straight toward Mo Duo’s chest.

At the brink of life and death, Mo Duo let out a furious snort and forcefully circulated his Demon Sovereign Technique, dispelling all the discomfort brought by the Mountain and River Bell. His hand shot out like lightning, grabbing toward Yang Kai’s Dragon Claw, clearly intending to stop his attack.

Yet at that very moment, the Mountain and River Bell, which had seemingly vanished, suddenly reappeared. This time, however, it did not envelop Yang Kai. Instead, it shrank to the size of a bronze bell and rested upon his other dragon claw.

With a flick of his fingers, the Mountain and River Bell rang out again. The sound waves condensed into a formless attack that surged toward Mo Duo.

Mo Duo’s body hunched over, and he instantly lost the strength he had just recovered.

With a tearing sound, Yang Kai’s sharp claws pierced through Mo Duo’s chest, emerging from his back. In his dragon claw, a violently beating demon heart gleamed blood-red.

“You…” Mo Duo glared at Yang Kai, who was now right in front of him, furious as he opened his mouth to roar.

An unimaginable shockwave burst from his mouth, gathering into a visible force that slammed into Yang Kai’s chest. Bones cracked as Yang Kai was sent flying like a paper kite.

Mo Duo staggered, blood gushing from his chest. A massive hole ran straight through his torso, exposing his organs, and his chest was now empty.

He glanced down, his face twisted and terrifying.

Yang Kai climbed to his feet not far away, spat out a mouthful of blood, and squeezed the demon heart in his palm. Grinning at Mo Duo, he said, “I’ve heard that experts like you have more than one demon heart. As long as the demon heart isn’t destroyed, a demon cannot die! So how many demon hearts do you have?”

Mo Duo narrowed his eyes, a cold light flashing through them. As Demonic Qi surged, the flesh around the hole in his chest squirmed and quickly healed, as if he had never been injured.

“Give it back!” he said in a low voice.

Though he had more than one demon heart and losing one wouldn’t threaten his life, its destruction still affected his strength. Yang Kai had already shown he could fight Mo Duo head-on; losing a demon heart meant losing a measure of advantage.

Yang Kai grinned and said, “What did you say? Did I mishear, or did you misspeak? Did you just ask me to give this thing back to you?”

“You didn’t mishear. Give it back!” Mo Duo replied coldly.

“Haha!” Yang Kai burst into laughter. “You really do love to joke. We’re locked in a life-and-death struggle, each using every method to decide victory or defeat. I seized your Demon Heart, and you’re actually telling me to return it? Let me ask you one thing… are you an idiot?”

“Give it back, and I’ll grant you a quick death. Otherwise, you’ll taste things far worse than death.”

Yang Kai’s expression turned serious as he said in a deep voice, “Your demon heart is in my hand. If you don’t beg me, that’s one thing, but you actually want me to give it back and promise me a quick death?” His expression turned mocking. “You really are an idiot!”

Mo Duo fell silent, his gaze fixed on the demon heart.

In the next instant, Yang Kai’s heart suddenly skipped a beat. Sensing something, his face changed dramatically as he clenched his hand tight.

With a thunderous boom, the demon heart exploded. No blood or flesh splattered out; instead, the Demonic Qi within surged forth, transforming into a black mist that enveloped Yang Kai. The mist seemed alive, swarming toward every pore and orifice, flooding into his body.

Yang Kai’s face turned grim. Though he wanted to resist, he was powerless—no matter how he tried to muster his strength, he couldn’t stop the Demonic Qi’s invasion.

Moreover, the demon heart hadn’t exploded because he crushed it, but because Mo Duo had used some mysterious technique to make it self-destruct.

As the demon heart self-destructed, Mo Duo grunted, his aura weakening as if he’d suffered a heavy blow. Still, he said, “I know you have ways to resist ordinary Demonic Qi, but you’ll never withstand mine. You’ll lose yourself and become my slave. I’ll keep my promise and let you taste every horror worse than death. Shaman Niu, kneel and beg—perhaps I’ll show some mercy.”

As if to prove his words, Yang Kai’s face twisted in agony, his eyes bulging as if they might burst, his whole body tinged with an unnatural blackness.

Mo Duo stood by, watching coldly, occasionally glancing at the distant Divine Tree, as if considering when to destroy the strange tree.

The Demonic Qi outside had vanished, all of it having flooded into Yang Kai’s body and seemingly merged with him.

Mo Duo lost patience and reached toward Yang Kai. Though losing a demon heart to such an enemy was hard to accept, and he wanted to torment Yang Kai to vent his anger, he had more important matters to attend to now.

The giant tree already reached the sky, not far from the passage between the two worlds. If he delayed any longer, there might be trouble.

Mo Duo decided to kill Yang Kai first, then deal with the tree.

But just as he was about to act, a shocking scene unfolded.

Yang Kai, his face twisted in pain, suddenly let out a long groan. With it, a dense, pitch-black Demonic Qi spewed from his mouth, pouring out endlessly as if inexhaustible.

Mo Duo was stunned.

This Demonic Qi was clearly the same as that contained in his demon heart. By rights, it should have invaded Shaman Niu’s body and fused with him—so why was Yang Kai spitting it out?

More and more Demonic Qi poured out, and it seemed to panic, as if something inside Yang Kai frightened it.

Mo Duo could hardly believe what he was seeing. He stood there, momentarily dazed.

After a long while, Yang Kai finally spat out all the Demonic Qi. Patting his stomach with a look of distaste, he muttered, “What the hell is this? It tastes awful.”

“How could you…” Mo Duo wanted to ask, but stopped halfway, realizing Yang Kai would never answer. This Shaman Niu had secrets, so he left the question unfinished.

Yang Kai had no intention of answering. After expelling all the Demonic Qi, he raised his hand, sending the Mountain and River Bell spinning toward Mo Duo. As it spun, the bell rapidly grew larger, descending to suppress Mo Duo in one fell swoop.

How could Mo Duo just stand there and wait for death? He knew how powerful the little bell was, so as soon as he saw Yang Kai’s move, he dodged to the side.

But Yang Kai was already a step ahead, catching up to the Mountain and River Bell and slamming both hands onto its surface.

Clang…

The visible sound waves gathered into an attack. Under Yang Kai’s control, they sliced through the void like blades, slashing toward Mo Duo.

Mo Duo couldn’t dodge in time. As the sound waves struck, his body froze, and a look of intense pain appeared on his face.

The Mountain and River Bell had already been able to suppress his movements. Now, having lost a demon heart, Mo Duo found it even harder to resist its power.

With that pause, Yang Kai was already upon him, grinning and baring a mouthful of gleaming fangs.

Mo Duo instinctively leapt back, but Yang Kai grabbed his arm. The dragon claw, like an iron hoop, squeezed with terrifying force, making Mo Duo’s arm crack and creak.

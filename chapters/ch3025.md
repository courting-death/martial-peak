## Chapter 3025 — The Dragon Clan Arrives

Hearing Yang Kai ask this, Li Jiao also became suspicious and frowned. "That's right. Why are you here? And where is the bastard who harmed you?"

Lu Sanniang lowered her head and fell silent for a moment before softly saying, "After I was brought back to Dragon Isle, Sanniang was imprisoned in the Dragon Palace and lived a life worse than death." Her voice choked with emotion, and her body trembled without cease. "Until fourteen years ago..."

She turned to look at her daughter and smiled desolately. "I was only driven out when Qin'er was born."

Li Jiao froze, not quite understanding at first, but once he grasped her meaning, his face immediately turned livid. Slamming the table, he roared, "Bastard!"

Yang Kai's expression was also ugly. He felt that the Dragon Clan truly was a wretched lot.

It was bad enough that they had abducted a woman like her to Dragon Isle and ravaged her for so many years, but when her child was born, they had actually driven both mother and child out. Did they have no humanity at all? Yang Kai had not held much prejudice against the Dragon Clan before. He had merely thought them too arrogant; in this regard, Zhu Qing and Zhu Lie seemed to have been carved from the same mold. Still, that was no great flaw. If the Dragon Clan were not arrogant, they would not be the Dragon Clan.

But he had never imagined that the Dragon Clan, who proclaimed themselves the leaders of all living creatures and foremost among the Divine Spirits, would harbor such vile practices within their own ranks.

"This girl is..." Yang Kai asked, looking at the young girl.

Lu Sanniang said nothing, but her meaning was already obvious.

"Since she is a descendant of the Dragon Clan, why was she driven out of the Dragon Palace?" Yang Kai asked with a frown. Even a vicious tiger would not eat its own cubs. No matter what, Lu Sanniang's daughter carried the Dragon Clan's bloodline, so why had she been driven out together with her mother as soon as she was born?

Lu Sanniang whispered, "Those are the rules of the Dragon Palace. Nothing happens if a woman bears no children, but once she gives birth, anyone without a Dragon Bloodline is expelled from the Dragon Palace. Qin'er... has no Dragon Bloodline."

"What kind of dogshit rule is that?" Li Jiao flew into a rage.

Whether the child had a Dragon Bloodline or not, she was still their own flesh and blood. How could they sweep her out like rubbish? The Dragon Clan was truly too heartless.

"That really is going too far!" Yang Kai instantly lost any favorable impression he might have had of the Dragon Palace.

Lu Sanniang smiled sorrowfully and shook her head. "Such things are already commonplace on Dragon Isle." She turned to look outside and pointed at the pedestrians hurrying to and fro. "Where do you think all the people in Half-Dragon City came from?"

"Where did they come from?" Li Jiao asked.

Lu Sanniang replied, "Many were driven out of the Dragon Palace. Even if they were not, their ancestors were."

"What?" Yang Kai and Li Jiao stared at her in unison.

"The Dragon Clan is small in number, and producing offspring is extremely difficult. Often, ten thousand years pass without a new clansman being born. On top of that, the ratio of men to women within the Dragon Clan is unbalanced, so the adult male Dragons often leave Dragon Isle in search of exceptional women, whom they capture and bring back as tools to sate their lust. Over time, many of those women become pregnant. When their children are born, those who possess a Dragon Clan bloodline are allowed to remain in the Dragon Palace for further observation, while those with no Dragon Clan bloodline, or whose bloodline is too low in rank, are expelled. Over countless years, Dragon Isle came to have many Humans, Yao, and Dragon Descendants with impure bloodlines."

"Later, as the number of these Humans, Yao, and Dragon Descendants increased, everyone established Half-Dragon City here. That was the earliest form of Half-Dragon City. Its residents paired off and had children of their own, and as the years passed, the population grew until the city gradually developed to its present scale."

After hearing Lu Sanniang's explanation, Yang Kai and Li Jiao were too shocked to speak for a long time.

So that was how Half-Dragon City had come into being?

The living beings in Half-Dragon City, whether Human, Yao, or Dragon Descendant, were actually all descendants of Dragons?

This... This was simply too unbelievable.

Lu Sanniang had explained it clearly. Some of the people in Half-Dragon City had been driven out of the Dragon Palace. They were all women captured by the Dragon Clan and the children those women had borne, just like Lu Sanniang herself. This group should be quite small, while the vast majority were descendants born and raised here over successive generations.

No wonder this place was called Half-Dragon City. So there was another reason behind its name.

Every resident of Half-Dragon City had some connection to the Dragon Clan, more or less. What else could it be called but Half-Dragon City?

Li Jiao had originally felt as though he had been betrayed, leaving him with a belly full of anger and nowhere to vent it. He had even felt some resentment toward Lu Sanniang herself, and had only asked so many questions because he wanted to understand what had happened.

After hearing all this, however, his heart filled with sympathy.

Lu Sanniang's capture had not been her fault. After all, she had only been at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm at the time. Faced with a member of the Dragon Clan, how could she have had any power to resist?

Her present circumstances were so miserable that Li Jiao no longer had any intention of blaming her.

He let out a long sigh, feeling that he and Sanniang were truly fated to meet but not destined to be together in this life.

"What is the child's name?" Li Jiao asked, looking at the young girl.

Lu Sanniang replied, "Yuqin. Lu Yuqin!"

Li Jiao nodded, then asked, "Who was the Dragon Clan member who harmed you?"

Lu Sanniang looked up at him nervously. "What are you going to do?"

Li Jiao replied, "This Lord suffered such humiliation entirely because of that bastard. Am I not even qualified to know his name?"

Lu Sanniang shook her head and remained silent. Clearly, she was unwilling to reveal the Dragon Clan member's name for fear of implicating Li Jiao. She knew Li Jiao's circumstances, but that man was a member of the Dragon Clan, and this was Dragon Isle. How could he be offended so lightly?

She did not want to see Li Jiao meet a tragic end.

Seeing this, Li Jiao became furious. Just as he was about to say more, he suddenly frowned and turned to look in a certain direction.

At the same time, Yang Kai also sensed something and focused his gaze in that direction.

"The Dragon Clan!" Li Jiao growled, his expression turning ugly.

He and Yang Kai had searched for a long time without finding any trace of the Dragon Clan, yet who could have known that one would take the initiative to appear at this precise moment? Before the Dragon had even drawn near, an overwhelming pressure swept over them from afar. The suppression stemming from his bloodline caused Li Jiao's flesh and blood to writhe without cease.

Many of the Dragon Descendants in Half-Dragon City had clearly sensed it as well and stopped to watch.

A short while later, a resounding Dragon Roar came from the distance and rapidly drew closer. Immediately afterward, the figure of a giant Dragon entered everyone's view. It was a green Dragon more than ten zhang long, twisting and rolling among the clouds. It soon arrived above Half-Dragon City, where its enormous body coiled in the sky and its head rose high as it looked down below.

The whole of Half-Dragon City seemed to tremble, and all its residents were filled with fear and unease.

On Dragon Isle, the Dragon Clan were the absolute rulers. They also rarely visited Half-Dragon City, so the sudden appearance of one naturally filled everyone with dread. No one knew why this green Dragon had come.

The green Dragon flaunted his might in midair for quite some time, taking in the whole of Half-Dragon City, before leisurely transforming into Human form.

Light flashed, and a young man who appeared to be in his twenties or thirties emerged in his place. Dressed in gaudy clothes, he looked like a butterfly parading through the streets.

A figure suddenly flew up from within Half-Dragon City.

Yang Kai and Li Jiao looked closely, and both showed expressions of surprise. The reason was simple: the person flying up to greet the green Dragon was actually the same man Li Jiao had beaten earlier.

At this moment, the sinister man wore a cautious, ingratiating smile as he respectfully stopped before the green Dragon, positioning himself a full body's height lower. His earlier injuries had not healed; his cheeks were badly swollen, and several of his teeth were missing. Coupled with his current obsequious posture, he looked rather comical.

The green Dragon glanced at him and frowned slightly, the contempt and disgust in his eyes plain to see.

"Greetings, Sir Fu Qi. May I ask what instructions Sir has upon arriving here?" He did not immediately complain to the green Dragon named Fu Qi, because he knew that in the eyes of the Dragon Clan, everyone in Half-Dragon City, himself included, was no more than an ant. Although he had some connections with those on Dragon Isle, the Dragon Clan saw him as no different from any other ant. If he complained now, Fu Qi would simply ignore him, and he would only invite the other's disgust.

Fu Qi did not look at him and merely said indifferently, "The Dragon Blood Flowers need watering."

Hearing this, the sinister man hurriedly nodded. "Understood, understood. This lowly one will handle it immediately. Will two hundred do, as before?"

Fu Qi nodded lightly. "Mm."

"Sir, please go on ahead. This lowly one will bring the people to the Dragon Palace at once."

Fu Qi said, "I also need five hundred mortals!"

The sinister man was taken aback. "Five hundred mortals?"

His face was filled with confusion, and he did not understand why Fu Qi had suddenly made such a request. In the past, whenever the Dragon Clan came to Half-Dragon City, it was basically always because of the Dragon Blood Flowers, so he only needed to bring two hundred Dragon Descendants. Never before had they asked him to bring mortals.

In the eyes of the Dragon Clan, no matter how high one's cultivation was, anyone without the Dragon Clan's bloodline was a mortal.

What did they intend to do? The sinister man could not understand.

"Are you questioning me?" Fu Qi turned his head and looked at him indifferently.

The sinister man's expression changed in alarm, and he realized he should not have asked. Whatever the Dragon Clan intended to do, he only needed to obey their orders. He had no right to inquire further. He immediately lowered his head and said, "This lowly one would not dare. Please calm your anger, Sir Fu Qi. This lowly one misspoke."

"Scram!" Fu Qi spat out a single word, then closed his eyes and stood in the void.

The sinister man felt as though he had received a great pardon and hurriedly turned to fly back toward Half-Dragon City.

Inside the teahouse, Lu Sanniang's beautiful face had gone pale with fright. She cried out urgently, "Big Brother Li, you must leave at once! If you do not go now, it will be too late."

Li Jiao frowned and looked at the sinister man flying straight toward him, then said calmly, "It is already too late to leave."

Li Jiao had beaten the sinister man earlier, and the other party had clearly taken the grudge to heart. Li Jiao had not heard what he had said to the Dragon Clan member, but one look at the malicious expression on the man's face told him that he might escape for a while, but he could not hide forever.

Did he regret it? No.

Lu Sanniang had been so humiliated right before his eyes. If he had remained indifferent, he would not have been worthy of calling himself a man.

He simply had not expected trouble to arrive so quickly.

Lu Sanniang had said earlier that the sinister man had connections with those on the other side. Now it seemed that this so-called other side referred to the Dragon Palace and the Dragon Clan.

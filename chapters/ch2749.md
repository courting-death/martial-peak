## Chapter 2749 — Xia Sheng

Advancing to the Emperor Realm was by no means without danger. A single misstep could result in being overwhelmed by Heaven-and-Earth Energy and dying. Many at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm not only failed to advance, but instead perished, their Dao dissipating.

For example, Wu Dao died at the threshold of advancing to the Dao Origin Realm. He was recognized as the strongest in the Hengluo Star Domain, yet even he died at the gate of the Dao Origin Realm. The dangers of advancing to the Emperor Realm were even greater.

“Hehe, Junior Nephew Yang has been out of touch for a long time. I didn’t expect you to come to Linghu Palace today.” A voice suddenly came from the side. Yang Kai looked up and saw a man of about fifty, a middle-aged elder with a ruddy complexion, white hair, and youthful features. His Qi and blood were vigorous, and his entire body radiated a robust aura.

First Stage of the Emperor Realm! Yang Kai saw it at a glance.

However, this First Stage Emperor felt stronger than most cultivators of the same level. Not only did he possess deep foundations, but even his aura alone was far beyond the likes of Le Dongzheng.

Yang Kai estimated that if this elder and Le Dongzheng fought one-on-one, the outcome would be decided in less than the time it takes for a stick of incense to burn.

“Senior, you are...?” Yang Kai stood and cupped his fists.

The middle-aged elder stroked his beard and smiled slightly. “This old man is Yao Li of Tianwu Sacred Land!”

Yang Kai didn’t know many people from Tianwu Sacred Land, aside from Chen Wenhao and Wuchang. He didn’t recognize the others, but since Yao Li possessed the cultivation of the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, he must be an elder in Tianwu Sacred Land.

Elders are to be respected, so Yang Kai naturally dared not be negligent.

“So it’s Elder Yao. Yang Kai greets Elder Yao!”

“Junior Nephew, no need to be so polite. Sit!” Yao Li smiled and gestured with his hand.

Immediately, a delicate young maiden brought over tea.

Yao Li looked at Yang Kai with a smile, seemingly quite interested in him. After all, Yang Kai had refined the Supreme Mystic Pill and was likely an Imperial-Grade Pill Master. Unfortunately, he wasn’t from Tianwu Sacred Land. Suddenly, Yao Li’s gaze shifted to Bian Yuqing and he asked, “And this is...?”

“My elder cousin!” Yang Kai replied casually.

Bian Yuqing stood and said, “I greet Elder Yao!”

Although Yang Kai and she hadn’t discussed it beforehand, Bian Yuqing handled herself flawlessly. She had wandered outside for many years and was no naive girl.

“So you’re Junior Nephew Yang’s relative. Please, sit.” Yao Li was quite amiable.

This left Bian Yuqing somewhat flattered. Before today, people like Yao Li were lofty figures she could only look up to, and even advancing to the Emperor Realm couldn’t change that. Yet now, just because of Yang Kai, she could sit here and drink tea with him.

Deep inside, a sense of absurdity welled up. It seemed that after meeting Yang Kai again, her fate had taken a dramatic turn.

“I heard from the disciples that Junior Nephew Yang came to requisition a secluded cultivation site?” Yao Li didn’t waste words and got straight to the point. Yang Kai might be impressive, but as an elder of Tianwu Sacred Land, Yao Li had no need for flattery.

“That’s right.” Yang Kai nodded. “May I ask if there are any vacant secluded cultivation sites among those not open to outsiders?”

Yao Li frowned slightly, pondered, and said, “Junior Nephew Yang, are you requesting the site for yourself, or...?” He glanced at Bian Yuqing.

Experienced and shrewd, Yao Li guessed Yang Kai’s intention in bringing Bian Yuqing along, even though Yang Kai hadn’t said anything.

Yang Kai answered honestly, “My elder cousin has reached the peak of the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. If there’s a suitable place to retreat, she might attempt a breakthrough.”

Yao Li nodded, “So that’s it.”

After a pause, he said, “Junior Nephew Yang, to be frank, those secluded sites not open to outsiders are reserved for core disciples like you. But you’ve come at an unfortunate time—they’re all full. If you needed one for yourself, I’d arrange it immediately, but since it’s for someone else, I’m in a difficult position.”

“Full?” Yang Kai was stunned.

He thought something was off. Although the Spiritual Qi here at Linghu Palace was rich and the reserved cultivation sites were excellent, compared to the top three sects, they were about the same. Why would disciples from the three top sects come to Linghu Palace instead of cultivating at their own sects?

“They’re indeed full.” Yao Li nodded seriously.

Yang Kai was suddenly embarrassed.

He had assumed there would be vacant sites, so he’d sold Bian Yuqing’s Qing Jade Command Token. Now, with no available spots, wouldn’t Bian Yuqing end up empty-handed?

Yao Li said, “Junior Nephew Yang, how about this? Although the top sites are full, I can arrange a Heaven-Grade one for you. The environment is also good, and many Emperor Realm cultivators are waiting in line. If your elder cousin retreats there, she’ll surely benefit.”

At this point, Yang Kai had no better option. He couldn’t really ask Yao Li to vacate a top site. Those were reserved for disciples of the three top sects, all of equal status. Forcing the issue might not only be refused by Yao Li, but could also cause conflict between sects.

Besides, Yao Li, as an elder of Tianwu Sacred Land, was sincere in his attitude, so Yang Kai felt embarrassed to press further.

“How long will the wait be?” Yang Kai asked.

Yao Li smiled, “You can enter today.”

This was essentially cutting the line, and no red Command Token was needed—he arranged it directly. Just from this, Yang Kai’s Qingyang Gold Token was quite useful.

“Elder cousin, what do you think?” Yang Kai turned to ask Bian Yuqing.

Bian Yuqing replied, “You decide.”

She originally planned to wait two months to enter an Earth-Grade site, but now suddenly had a Heaven-Grade one without waiting, so she was naturally delighted.

Seeing Bian Yuqing had no objections, Yang Kai thought this was the only way. Just as he was about to agree, a voice suddenly sounded from outside: “No vacant sites? That can’t be. I remember there’s one.”

Hearing this, Yang Kai was slightly stunned. Looking up, a familiar figure appeared, wearing a carefree smile and standing boldly.

“Senior Brother Xia!” Yang Kai quickly stood, surprised to see Xia Sheng. “Why are you here?”

Xia Sheng, as the chief disciple of Qingyang Shrine, advanced to the Emperor Realm in the Shattered Star Sea. Now, he was a prominent figure in the Southern Territory. Given time, reaching the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm was certain, and even the Third Stage was within reach.

Yang Kai never expected to see him here, and was pleasantly surprised.

Yao Li, on the other hand, suddenly looked a bit awkward and sipped his tea.

“Senior Brother, I’m one of the three chief stewards of Linghu Palace, stationed here year-round!” Xia Sheng walked up with a smile.

“Three chief stewards!” Yang Kai was stunned.

Thinking it over, he immediately understood. Linghu Palace was jointly managed by the three top sects, so each sent a steward to oversee things, ensuring their disciples weren’t treated unfairly and monitoring income.

When the Tianwu Sacred Land disciple brought Yang Kai in earlier, he’d simply said they were looking for the steward, and then Yao Li appeared. Yang Kai hadn’t thought much of it, assuming Linghu Palace was managed solely by Yao Li. But now, hearing Xia Sheng’s words, he realized that wasn’t the case.

“The Hall Master actually let you out.” Yang Kai smiled.

Xia Sheng replied, “I only recently advanced to the Emperor Realm. The Hall Master said I needed to experience the mundane world and stabilize my cultivation, so he sent me here.”

As he spoke, his face looked bitter, as if this was a tough assignment.

“How are the Hall Master and Elder Gao these days?” Yang Kai asked.

“They’re well, they’re well. But you, you’ve been gone from the sect for years.”

Yang Kai replied awkwardly, “Worldly affairs kept me busy. I planned to return in a few days.”

Xia Sheng suddenly lowered his voice, “When you go back, avoid Elder Gao. She said if she sees you again, she’ll teach you a lesson.”

“It can’t be that bad...” Yang Kai broke out in a cold sweat.

Xia Sheng gloated, “That’s what Elder Gao said. Her temper...”—he shivered—“Oh, and bad news: Elder Gao advanced to the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm two years ago. So even though you’re an Emperor, you definitely can’t beat her.”

“Elder Gao broke through?” Yang Kai was shocked, then happy for Gao Xueting. The last time he saw her was in the Eastern Territory, when she was trapped in a secret medicine garden. At that time, she was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. He hadn’t expected her to break through so soon.

“Good luck to you.” Xia Sheng patted Yang Kai’s shoulder, looking sympathetic.

“Senior Brother, what did you mean about the cultivation site not being full?” Yang Kai frowned. Yao Li said it was full, but Xia Sheng gave a different answer, making Yang Kai suspicious.

Xia Sheng smiled and looked at Yao Li. “There are nine top cultivation sites, three for each of the three sects. Qingyang Shrine has only used two. When was the third used? Steward Yao, may I ask which disciple from my shrine is in retreat there?”

Yang Kai’s frown deepened.

He had known Linghu Palace had special cultivation sites reserved for disciples of the three top sects, but hadn’t realized there were such details.

Judging by Xia Sheng and Yao Li’s conversation, there seemed to be some tension between them.

Yao Li slapped his forehead and smiled, “Right, right, Steward Xia, if you hadn’t mentioned it, I would’ve forgotten. Qingyang Shrine’s three sites have only used two. I’m getting old and forgetful.”

Yang Kai’s face suddenly darkened. His previous goodwill toward Yao Li vanished instantly.

## Chapter 2894 — Incarnation

Beneath the Boundary Passage, beside the Divine Tree, the Demon Saint Mo Duo suddenly appeared, causing the sliver of hope that had just emerged for the Barbarian Tribe to collapse in an instant. In the face of a Saint, unless the Divine Tree personally intervened, no one could resist—not even Yang Kai.

But Qing now had no strength left to fight a Demon Saint. He had devoted all his power to growing, sealing off all perception of the outside world. At this moment, Qing was burning away the life essence he had accumulated over tens of thousands of years, seeking to block the passage between the two worlds with his own strength alone.

"I am but a Shaman King. Must Lord Mo Duo really bully the weak?"

"Heh heh..."

Mo Duo chuckled softly, his smile harmless and gentle. Anyone unaware of his identity would surely find him amiable and radiant, completely unlike a member of the Demonic Qi–shrouded, sinister Demon Race. "You are not weak at all."

Yang Kai curled his lip. "Still, I am no match for you. You have your battlefield, I have mine. Why don't we each mind our own business?"

Mo Duo showed a troubled expression. "I'm afraid that's not possible. This big tree of yours is rather peculiar. If we let it keep growing, we might not be able to communicate with the Demon Domain anymore. So I have to destroy it. My apologies."

Yang Kai remained expressionless on the surface, but inside he was secretly delighted.

The information Mo Duo had inadvertently revealed confirmed that Qing truly did have the ability to block the Boundary Passage—so long as Yang Kai could create enough opportunities for Qing to continue growing.

Yang Kai let out a long sigh, his face full of helplessness. "Lord, I understand what you mean."

Mo Duo still smiled, nodding. "Are you ready to die?" After a pause, he added, "Actually, you could surrender to me. Then we wouldn't have to fight."

Yang Kai replied solemnly, "Your favor is appreciated, Lord, but Shaman Niu must decline. I, Shaman Niu, was born a man of the Barbarian Tribe, and will become a ghost of the Barbarian Tribe. I could never surrender to the Demon Race."

These words made the members of Shaman Niu's tribe below brim with pride, their respect for Yang Kai growing even deeper.

Mo Duo shrugged. "Then there's nothing to be done. Enough talk. I'm going to kill you now."

His tone was as calm as a butcher facing a lamb.

As soon as he finished speaking, he reached out toward Yang Kai.

This strike seemed utterly ordinary, yet as his hand reached out, the laws of heaven and earth trembled. Yang Kai suddenly felt as if the space around him was sealed, as though he had been plunged into a mire—the more he struggled, the deeper he sank.

Demon Saints and Shaman Saints were existences on par with Great Emperors. At this level, every movement was in harmony with the ultimate principles of heaven and earth, so the more unremarkable the attack, the deeper the mysteries it contained.

Around Yang Kai, the power of the Laws of Space surged. After a momentary pause, he suddenly vanished from where he stood.

A faint sound came from where he had been, as if something had been torn open. After the sound, everyone could clearly see that the void there had collapsed.

Mo Duo tilted his head, his eyes flashing with interest. "Space Divine Ability, truly impressive. I am even more reluctant to kill you now. If the Great Demon God could have your help, breaking open the Boundary Passages of other great worlds would be much easier. He might even be able to descend in full."

"If you don't want to kill me, then don't." Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and forced a smile. "Isn't it better for us to just stand here and chat, and leave the fighting to others?"

Mo Duo shook his head. "My task is to kill or capture you, so I'm afraid I can't keep chatting with you."

As he spoke, he flicked his finger, and a jet-black energy shot toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai countered with a Moon Blade. The two attacks collided in midair and vanished without a trace.

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. "Lord, your strength seems a bit off. Are you holding back against me?"

"Heh heh..." Mo Duo smiled, his hands never stopping. As Demonic Qi surged, a giant blade of Demonic Qi suddenly slashed toward Yang Kai without warning. At the same time, he said, "This is all my power right now. I'm not holding back."

Yang Kai's brow furrowed slightly. By instinct, he was reluctant to believe it, yet he couldn't shake the feeling that Mo Duo wasn't lying. The power Mo Duo was displaying now was formidable, but to Yang Kai it didn't seem to have reached the point where it was completely beyond his ability to handle.

Right now, Mo Duo's strength was about the same as when he had first descended.

Two years ago, when Mo Duo first arrived in this world, he had paid a price to pass through the Boundary Passage, greatly weakening him and allowing Yang Kai to escape using his Space Divine Ability.

In two years, Mo Duo's strength should have fully recovered, but in fact, he was displaying much the same level of power as before. The only difference was that he no longer seemed weak.

The power of a Demon Saint could not be so limited. Yang Kai kept this in mind.

He used teleportation again to dodge Mo Duo's attack.

But just then, Mo Duo's eyes suddenly flashed, and he stared into the endless void.

He immediately caught Yang Kai's trace. Space Divine Ability was not omnipotent; though it could exile oneself into the void, there were still patterns that experts could discern.

Mo Duo was just such an expert. He raised his hand and pointed at a spot in the void.

With a tearing sound, the void seemed to be ripped open, and Yang Kai stumbled out.

At the same time, a pitch-black net suddenly enveloped that part of the space, clearly intending to trap Yang Kai as well.

Yang Kai's expression changed. He raised his hand and struck out with a Years Like a Shuttle Seal, unleashing all his strength.

With a rumble, the black net was blocked for a moment. Yang Kai took the opportunity to slip away, avoiding the net and steadying himself, cold sweat pouring down his back.

"Sigh." Mo Duo had attacked twice without success and sighed helplessly. "Fifty percent of my power really isn't enough."

"Fifty percent?" Yang Kai glared at him, exasperated. "Didn't you just say this was all your power? Now you say fifty percent? Which is it?"

Mo Duo replied blandly, "Both are true."

He raised his hand and beckoned in a certain direction.

Yang Kai thought he was about to use some secret technique and was on guard, but after Mo Duo beckoned, he sensed no attack. Instead, a jet-black light shot over and merged into Mo Duo's body.

Mo Duo was already formidable, but now his aura suddenly surged. The increase in power seemed to change his demeanor as well; his once gentle face now showed a trace of ferocity. He smiled slightly. "This is seventy percent!"

Yang Kai gritted his teeth. "Are you an incarnation?"

Mo Duo said, "I am myself. There is no distinction between main and secondary. That one over there is also me!"

Over there, naturally, referred to the battlefield of the Saints.

Only now did Yang Kai realize that it wasn't that the Shaman Saints of the Barbarian Tribe had carelessly let Mo Duo slip by—Mo Duo was still fighting the Shaman Saints over there. He simply had an incarnation here as well.

This incarnation was clearly a contingency left by the Demon Race, and it had proven highly effective. Without Mo Duo's interference, nothing would have disturbed the Divine Tree, but his sudden appearance forced Yang Kai to be cautious at every step.

What troubled Yang Kai even more was that Mo Duo's power seemed able to shift freely between his main body and incarnation.

With fifty percent of his power, he couldn't stop Yang Kai's teleportation, so he was forced to use seventy percent. The more power here, the less on the other side.

"I know what you're planning," Mo Duo said casually. "You're just trying to stall for time so your big tree can block the passage between the two worlds. But now, you no longer have that chance, because you are no longer... my match!"

As the last word fell, Mo Duo suddenly appeared in front of Yang Kai, thrusting a hand straight at his chest.

Yang Kai's expression changed drastically. Though Mo Duo's figure still lingered in his vision, the attack was already upon him.

In his panic, he instinctively tried to use his Space Divine Ability, only to find the space around him as solid as iron—he couldn't move at all.

A cruel smile appeared at the corner of Mo Duo's mouth as his Demonic Qi–shrouded hand pierced through Yang Kai's chest.

There was no spray of blood as expected, nor any sensation of flesh. Mo Duo frowned, only then realizing that Yang Kai's body had somehow become a dim shadow, and his hand had passed straight through it without any resistance.

Yang Kai's face twisted in pain, as if enduring immense agony. His body, nearly fading into the air, suddenly leapt back, escaping Mo Duo's grasp.

As his body reformed, Yang Kai was clearly gravely injured, blood gushing from his mouth.

He took out a Pill and stuffed it into his mouth, chewing hastily as cold sweat poured down like a waterfall.

"Was that also a Space Divine Ability?" Mo Duo frowned slightly. His sure-kill strike had been neutralized by Shaman Niu in a way he couldn't comprehend. Though the other party looked badly hurt, it was far from the result he had expected.

This was not what Mo Duo wanted to see.

"Space Divine Abilities are not just teleportation," Yang Kai said, swallowing the Pill. He was happy to chat longer to buy time to recover. "That move just now—I call it Nothingness."

"Exiling yourself into the void to avoid powerful attacks?" Mo Duo's eyes lit up in admiration. "An impressive Divine Ability."

Yang Kai said, "But your power is too strong. Though most of it didn't affect me, some still broke through the void and struck me. You nearly killed me."

"So that's how it is!" Mo Duo showed a look of realization, then a thoughtful expression. "It seems killing you really isn't easy."

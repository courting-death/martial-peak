## Chapter 2932 — Mirror Soul

It was as if he had traversed an endless tunnel of time and space. By the time Yang Kai regained his senses, he was already in a brand new world.

In front of him, an elderly man with white hair and beard was smiling at him. Beside the old man, someone was sitting cross-legged in meditation. This person had a dignified appearance and an imposing aura, dressed in purple robes—who else could it be but Wen Zishan?

“Senior Tian Yan!” Yang Kai hurriedly cupped his fists in salute to the old man. After so many years, Tian Yan was still the same as ever, unchanged in the slightest.

That said, Tian Yan himself was a Great Emperor-level expert. With no physical body, he existed solely as a Divine Soul within the Divine Roaming world, so naturally there could be no significant changes to his appearance.

“You’ve arrived.” Tian Yan stroked his beard, as if he had long anticipated Yang Kai’s arrival.

Yang Kai asked, “How did we happen to appear here? Was it thanks to Senior’s help?”

This place was a mountain peak, towering into the clouds. No one knew where in the Divine Roaming Mirror world it was located. To appear here directly was clearly no coincidence—it must have been with someone’s assistance.

Tian Yan shook his head. “It wasn’t me. He’s the one who brought you here.” As he spoke, Tian Yan gestured to the side.

Following his gaze, Yang Kai finally noticed a young boy not far away. The boy was chubby and adorable, looking only seven or eight years old, chasing a dancing butterfly and playing with great joy.

“He…” Yang Kai was stunned, not knowing what background this boy had to possess such power.

Gao Xueting had also arrived here, but all her attention was on Wen Zishan. After carefully examining him and confirming that Wen Zishan was unharmed—only slightly injured—she was finally at ease. Turning to Tian Yan, she asked curiously, “Junior Brother, this Senior is…?”

Although she had entered the Divine Roaming Mirror many times, she had never met Tian Yan. She had only once heard Wen Zishan mention that there seemed to be an existence in the Divine Roaming world comparable to a Great Emperor.

Yang Kai said, “This is Senior Tian Yan.”

Gao Xueting hurriedly saluted.

Tian Yan smiled, “I remember you, little girl. The first time you came in, you were only sixteen, right?”

Gao Xueting's face flushed slightly as she spoke softly, "Senior, you have quite the memory."

It had been so long that she herself barely remembered, yet Tian Yan recalled it clearly.

“Senior, what exactly happened here?” Gao Xueting had no mind for idle chatter. She only wanted to quickly understand the changes here—why Wu Kuang had seized Mortal Great Emperor’s body and escaped.

Tian Yan said, “Old friend Hongchen seemed to have encountered some trouble earlier, so he came to seek my help. After many days of research, we barely came up with a way to separate the two souls. If successful, we could imprison Brother Wu’s Divine Soul in this Divine Roaming world, and Hongchen would regain his freedom. Although things hadn’t been going smoothly, we managed to get by. But just now, something suddenly barged in and disrupted the stability of the Divine Roaming world. Brother Wu took the opportunity to strike back, injuring old friend Hongchen and this young friend Wen, and then escaped.”

Yang Kai was shocked. “So, Senior Hongchen isn’t dead?”

Tian Yan glanced at him with a smile. “Brother Wu may be powerful, but killing Hongchen is beyond him. Did he tell you old friend Hongchen was dead?”

Yang Kai said, “That’s what he claimed. No wonder he was so murderous yet let us go—it wasn’t out of kindness, but because Senior Hongchen was interfering.” Only now did Yang Kai realize why Wu Kuang hadn’t truly killed them before.

Tian Yan said, “He escaped, but with old friend Hongchen restraining him, he can’t act as he pleases. Still, it’s going to be hard on old friend Hongchen—his body seized, his Divine Soul suppressed… Enough about him. For now, we must resolve the crisis in the Divine Roaming world.”

Yang Kai’s expression grew solemn. He looked into the distance, where the sky was shrouded in darkness. Thunder and lightning flashed within the black clouds, creating a terrifying scene.

Yet, for some reason, those dark clouds, though constantly surging, could never break past a certain range, as if some powerful restriction was binding them.

Within the clouds surged a dense demonic aura—clearly, Demonic Intent was at work.

Tian Yan said, “The thing that broke in from the outside world is highly corrosive. The living beings of the Divine Roaming world cannot resist it. As you can see, in just a short while, such a vast area has been corrupted. If this continues, the entire world may fall.”

Yang Kai nodded gravely. Even without Tian Yan’s explanation, he could sense it. He asked, “How can we resolve this? Senior, do you have no way?”

“There is a way, but I’ll need your help!” Tian Yan turned to look at him.

“Me?” Yang Kai was stunned. He hadn’t expected the task of saving the world to fall on his shoulders.

Tian Yan smiled, “I was worried I wouldn’t find you in time, but you came in so quickly yourself. Actually, dealing with those things is both difficult and simple—just use your Soul-Devouring Worms.”

Yang Kai’s eyes lit up. “So that’s how it is.”

The Heaven Devouring Battle Art could devour anything, and the Soul-Devouring Worms were the same. The Divine Roaming world was being corroded by Demonic Intent, giving rise to vast Demonic Qi—a calamity for the world’s inhabitants, but a delicious feast for the Soul-Devouring Worms.

Tian Yan knew Yang Kai had Soul-Devouring Worms because, years ago, Yang Kai had taken a batch from this place. They had once been the source of chaos in the Divine Roaming world, and Tian Yan had guarded them for countless years before entrusting them to Yang Kai.

“The Soul-Devouring Worms aren’t with me. I’ll need to go out and bring them in,” Yang Kai said.

Tian Yan replied, “Go quickly and return. Young friend Wen and the little girl should go out as well. The rest is no longer your concern.”

Gao Xueting nodded gently. She had come here only to check on Wen Zishan. Now that she knew he was fine, there was no need to stay.

With a gentle wave of his hand, Tian Yan enveloped the three of them in a soft power. In the next instant, their vision shifted, and Yang Kai returned to his body.

He immediately summoned the Slave-Insect Bracelet, connected with it through his mind, and drew the Soul-Devouring Worms into his Sea of Consciousness, settling them atop the Seven-Colored Treasure Isle. The Divine Roaming world could only be entered with the Divine Soul; even though the Slave-Insect Bracelet was an Imperial Treasure, it couldn’t be brought in. That was why Yang Kai couldn’t release the Soul-Devouring Worms before. But placing these strange insects on the Seven-Colored Treasure Isle in his Sea of Consciousness was different.

After being raised for so long, the Soul-Devouring Worms had become extremely fierce, especially after more than two years of battles in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. In the later stages, they had performed many feats. For them, devouring Demonic Qi was nothing new, so resolving the crisis in the Divine Roaming world wouldn’t be difficult.

After greeting Granny You, he had her once again send his Divine Soul into the Divine Roaming world. Soon, Yang Kai reappeared on the mountain peak.

Seeing him return, Tian Yan nodded slightly, then turned to the boy chasing butterflies. “Boy, stop playing and lend him a hand.”

Yang Kai had already been curious about this boy, not knowing what kind of being he was. Now, hearing Tian Yan ask him to help, Yang Kai was even more surprised.

“Senior, who is he…?”

Tian Yan smiled faintly. “He is the Mirror Soul of the Divine Roaming Mirror. The reason those things are confined and can’t expand is thanks to him.”

“Mirror Soul!” Yang Kai’s eyes lit up as he stared at the boy.

The boy was clearly uncomfortable under his gaze. He shuffled over, pretending to be fierce. “I’m warning you, don’t get any ideas. I won’t form any connection with you.”

Yang Kai chuckled. “I don’t want to form any connection with you. No need to be so nervous.”

“Who… who’s nervous?” The boy was obviously wary of Yang Kai, dragging his feet as he walked over. Then he looked up and asked, “Where is it?”

“Where is what?” Yang Kai was puzzled.

“The Soul-Devouring Worms!” The boy sighed. “You’re such a grown-up, how can you still be so dense?”

“I have them!” Yang Kai quickly opened his Sea of Consciousness and released all the Soul-Devouring Worms from the Seven-Colored Treasure Isle. Instantly, a buzzing filled the air as a massive cloud of insects gathered before him.

The boy shivered, waving his hand in disgust and annoyance.

The insect cloud suddenly vanished, but Yang Kai keenly sensed they had been transported thousands of li away, rushing into the demonic clouds.

Focusing carefully, Yang Kai soon sensed the Soul-Devouring Worms devouring the demonic clouds that shrouded the world. With the Mirror Soul binding and confining the clouds, they couldn’t expand. As long as this continued, the demonic clouds would eventually be devoured, and the Divine Roaming world would return to normal.

Rolling his eyes, Yang Kai glanced at the boy. “Junior Brother, how should I address you?”

The boy rolled his eyes. “Zhang San, Li Si, Wang Erma—call me whatever you like.”

Yang Kai coughed dryly. “Do you want to see the outside world?”

Tian Yan suddenly laughed. “Young friend Yang, there’s no need to waste your efforts. He cannot leave this place.” Clearly, he had seen through Yang Kai’s intentions.

Caught, Yang Kai felt a bit embarrassed, but then turned serious. “By the way, Senior, there’s news about the Life Origin Pill.”

Tian Yan raised his brows. “Oh?”

“It should be almost refined. If Senior wishes, you can create a physical body at any time.”

Tian Yan said, “Young friend Yang, you truly are a man of your word. This old man thanks you here.”

Yang Kai laughed. “No need to be polite, Senior. I only obtained the Life Origin Fruit by chance—I didn’t seek it out deliberately.”

The boy frowned and interjected, “You’re leaving?”

He looked up at Tian Yan, seeming a bit agitated.

Tian Yan was silent for a while, then said, “I still want to see the outside world.”

The boy sneered, “Go, go—best if you die out there.” As he spoke, he glared resentfully at Yang Kai.

The corner of Yang Kai’s mouth twitched. He immediately understood he’d have no chance of subduing this Mirror Soul.

## Chapter 2669 — Flowing Shadow City

Hearing that the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect was actually going to publicly auction off Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppets, Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were both furious beyond words. The sect was destroyed because their strength was lacking, so there was no one to blame, but for the sect’s greatest treasures to be put up for auction—this was simply humiliation.

It was like digging up a corpse to flog it after the person had died.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian both wished they could sprout wings and fly to Flowing Shadow City, disrupt the auction, and snatch back those Heavenly Puppets.

But after the anger faded, they were left dejected. Their ambitions were lofty, but their strength was lacking. Even if they really went to Flowing Shadow City, it would be like throwing a meat bun to a dog—never to return.

The reason they hid here was to gather strength, waiting for the day they could take revenge.

Seeing their concerns, Yang Kai said, “No matter what you decide, I’m going to Flowing Shadow City.”

Du Xian was startled and said worriedly, “But Brother Yang, Flowing Shadow City is the foundation of the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect. Since they dare to hold an auction, they must be fully prepared. If you go like this, aren’t you walking right into their trap?”

Yang Kai grinned and said, "Then we'll just have to see whose net is bigger."

“Brother Yang, you…” Du Xian was stunned, admiring his boldness, his eyes brightening. “Could it be you’ve become an Emperor?”

Ye Qinghan’s expression also lit up upon hearing this.

The two had never paid much attention to Yang Kai’s cultivation, since when they first met him, his cultivation was about the same as theirs. But seeing Yang Kai so confident now, they couldn’t help but release their Divine Sense to probe him.

Their Divine Sense sank like a stone into the sea, without a ripple. Yet a faint Emperor Intent lingered around Yang Kai.

He really had become an Emperor.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were both shocked, amazed at Yang Kai’s speed of cultivation and growth. But thinking further, wasn’t this world full of geniuses who left others in the dust, climbing the Martial Dao at incredible speed?

It was precisely because of these many geniuses that this world was so vibrant and exciting.

Du Xian said solemnly, “But Brother Yang, even if you are an Emperor, Flowing Shadow City… is still a dragon’s lair and tiger’s den.”

The implication was that it was impossible to break in.

Not to mention that the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect already had an Emperor Realm Sect Master. Just the mastermind behind Thousand Leaves Sect’s destruction was not someone an ordinary Emperor could provoke. If this matter really involved Star God Palace, then Thousand Leaves Sect would have to swallow the loss.

Yang Kai waved his hand and said, “If it weren’t a dragon’s lair and tiger’s den, breaking in wouldn’t be any fun.”

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian both gazed at Yang Kai, not knowing where he got such confidence and courage. But remembering their sect’s destruction and the countless disciples who died tragically, their blood boiled, and they nodded together, “Alright. We’ll go too. Even if it means dying, we must snatch back the Heavenly Puppets.”

Though their momentum was impressive enough, the two of them knew all too well that with their cultivation, such a feat was impossible. Once they revealed their identities in Flowing Shadow City, they would surely face a siege from all directions.

Since the enmity between Flowing Shadow Sword Sect and Thousand Leaves Sect was established, they would surely cut the grass and pull up the roots.

Yang Kai smiled and said, “That’s how it should be.”

He wanted to bring Ye Qinghan and Du Xian along, not because he expected them to contribute much. Two Dao Origin Realm cultivators wouldn’t amount to much in this whirlpool of conflict. But since they were Thousand Leaves Sect people—one the Sect Master’s daughter, the other the leading disciple of the younger generation—Yang Kai bringing them to Flowing Shadow City to stir up trouble was justified. Otherwise, as an outsider meddling in Thousand Leaves Sect’s affairs, at best it would be called helping the oppressed, at worst, sticking his nose where it didn’t belong.

He didn’t want to reveal his connection with Ai Ou and the others just yet.

With the decision made, Ye Qinghan and Du Xian no longer hesitated. They summoned some trusted disciples and carefully instructed them to stay in Emperor Heaven Valley and cultivate well. If they didn’t return, the task of reviving Thousand Leaves Sect would fall on their shoulders.

Naturally, the Thousand Leaves Sect disciples pleaded bitterly, but the two had already made up their minds. No amount of persuasion could change their resolve.

Half a day later, Yang Kai took the two and left Emperor Heaven Valley, using a Space Array to return to Thousand Leaves Sect’s nameless valley.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were both natives of the Southern Territory and were familiar with Flowing Shadow Sword Sect and Flowing Shadow City. They had been to Flowing Shadow City a few times, so they pointed out the direction to Yang Kai, and the group sped off.

In just three days, the four arrived at Flowing Shadow City.

From afar, Flowing Shadow City covered a vast area, grand and imposing. The city walls were over ten zhang tall, and a giant sword hung above the city gate. The sword seemed to be cast from pure copper, extremely imposing and solid, suspended above the gate as if it could fall at any moment and split the earth in two. The martial artists coming and going were used to the sight.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian followed behind Yang Kai, landing outside the city gate with uneasy expressions.

In their view, since they were infiltrating the enemy’s stronghold, they should at least change their appearance or disguise themselves to avoid attracting unnecessary trouble, then act in secret.

But Yang Kai didn’t let them change their appearance, instead letting them show their true faces.

The two were on edge the whole way, and now outside Flowing Shadow City, they were even more tense and uneasy.

Though they were mentally prepared to die, they wanted their deaths to be meaningful. If they died without reason, they’d rather stay in Emperor Heaven Valley, enduring hardship and cultivating for hundreds of years.

Yet Yang Kai seemed completely confident, so the two didn’t dare ask him.

At the city gate, many martial artists were queuing to enter the city. Every moderately prosperous metropolis was like this, and Flowing Shadow City was no exception. Now that the auction was about to commence, countless cultivators from across the Southern Territory had heard of the Heavenly Puppets from Thousand Leaves Sect and came flocking here. There was even a Star Lord who possessed a complete Stellar Origin, naturally drawing martial artists from all directions in an endless stream.

Nowadays, Flowing Shadow City was full of Dao Origin Realm cultivators, Emperors everywhere, and experts like clouds.

In the City Lord’s mansion, the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect’s Sect Master, Li Qingyun, was personally overseeing things.

Li Qingyun, as a First Stage Emperor, specialized in the sword path of slaughter. In the Southern Territory, he was a remarkable expert. With his status, he shouldn’t have needed to be here, but as the auction approached, more and more Emperors gathered in Flowing Shadow City. Many top sects sent experts to participate, and the other members of Flowing Shadow Sword Sect couldn’t hold the fort, so as Sect Master, he had to handle things himself.

During this period, Li Qingyun had visited countless experts. Each one was not someone Flowing Shadow Sword Sect could afford to provoke. Now that they were in Flowing Shadow City, his territory, as host he had to pay his respects.

Normally, Flowing Shadow Sword Sect wouldn’t have the chance to befriend so many experts.

But after so many visits, Li Qingyun was exhausted, with no time to cultivate. And as the auction approached, he felt a sense of foreboding.

Though his cultivation wasn’t the highest, because he specialized in the sword path of slaughter, his sense of danger was extremely keen.

He kept feeling that something big would happen at this auction. If things went wrong, Flowing Shadow Sword Sect might be wiped out.

But the auction had to be held. Facing the command of that great figure, Li Qingyun couldn’t muster any resistance.

With a long sigh, the Imperial Treasure Yuxu Sword before him hummed softly, as if sensing its master’s mood.

“Sect Master!” came a voice from outside the door.

“What is it?” Li Qingyun looked up, a trace of fatigue on his face. If he had a choice, he’d rather be secluded in the sect, practicing swordsmanship. The sword path was deep and long; even if he spent his whole life, he might not comprehend it. Who had time to stir up trouble? But in that great figure’s eyes, he and the entire Flowing Shadow Sword Sect were just chess pieces, placed on the board, unable to act freely.

“Reporting to Sect Master. We’ve found two Thousand Leaves Sect remnants. They seem to be the Sect Master’s daughter and the leading disciple of this generation.”

“Where?” Li Qingyun’s eyes lowered.

“At the city gate, about to enter.”

Li Qingyun’s gaze narrowed, the sense of unease growing stronger, as if an invisible maw was about to bite him. The intense sense of crisis made his skin crawl.

He hadn’t felt this way in years. The Yuxu Sword hummed incessantly, confirming his intuition.

The person outside waited quietly, but after a long time, the Sect Master didn’t respond and he could only wait bitterly.

Meanwhile, at the city gate, the crowd was restless, people running in all directions.

A group of Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples surrounded Yang Kai and the other four, both in the air and on the ground. Bystanders retreated, watching from afar.

Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had destroyed Thousand Leaves Sect. Though they themselves felt it was sudden, since a deadly feud was formed, they had to investigate the enemy’s background, especially those who escaped.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian held high status in Thousand Leaves Sect, and their portraits had long circulated outside. Moreover, Yang Kai brought them straight in, not even bothering to queue, not caring about the city gate guards at all.

So as soon as they entered, Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were recognized by the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples guarding the gate.

The enemies had come knocking, and so brazenly provoking—how could this stand?

Without needing any orders, dozens of Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples immediately set up an inescapable net, their eyes gleaming with predatory intent.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian exchanged bitter smiles.

They never expected Yang Kai to be so bold. Not only did he refuse to let them hide their faces, now he was actively provoking. What was Yang Kai planning? Was he really so confident he could come and go freely in Flowing Shadow City?

But at this point, regret was useless. Remembering what these Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples did in Thousand Leaves Sect a year ago, their eyes gradually reddened, killing intent slowly spreading.

Even if they had to die, they would drag a few of these bastards down with them. Only then would they live up to the name of Thousand Leaves Sect disciples.

“You two are bold, daring to break into Flowing Shadow City. Looks like Thousand Leaves Sect people really don’t know what death means.”

The leader, a Dao Origin Realm cultivator, looked at Ye Qinghan and Du Xian, sneering. But after glancing at Yang Kai and Yingfei, he showed a puzzled expression, not knowing who these two were, daring to mix with Thousand Leaves Sect people.

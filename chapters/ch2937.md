## Chapter 2937 — A Dragon Scale

When he came to his senses, Zhu Lie had already retreated a hundred zhang away. Yang Kai laughed maniacally, “Where do you think you’re going?!”

As the Laws of Space surged, he caught up to Zhu Lie in an instant, his claws seizing the dragon’s tail and yanking it fiercely.

Zhu Lie, who had been rushing toward the sky, was restrained by this tremendous force. His massive dragon body involuntarily plummeted downward, a deafening roar erupting from his maw. He suddenly twisted his head around, opening his blood-red jaws wide as he snapped viciously at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai sneered, his figure weaving back and forth with ease. Not only did he dodge Zhu Lie’s attack, he even managed to tie the dragon’s tail into a bow.

With a breakthrough in the Dragon Transformation Art, the Origin Power of the Golden Sacred Dragon within Yang Kai was drawn out even further. Suddenly, he felt that Zhu Lie was no match for him at all, and he was certain this feeling was correct.

It wasn’t that his own strength had increased, but that the bloodline of his half-dragon form had become even purer, now able to suppress Zhu Lie’s lineage.

“Roar!” Zhu Lie howled, his voice full of grief and indignation, as if he had suffered a great humiliation. As a member of the Dragon Clan, the leader of all living beings, to have his tail tied into a bow by someone else was simply intolerable. He thrashed about, trying to break free from Yang Kai’s grip, but under the suppression of bloodlines, he could only exert seventy percent of his strength. How could he possibly get his way?

Yang Kai clung tightly to Zhu Lie’s bow-tied tail, shouting as the muscles on his arms bulged, spinning him around with all his might.

Hooch—

Hooch—

The twenty-zhang-long dragon in Yang Kai’s hands was like a plaything, spun up and down like a windmill.

The Emperor Realm cultivators from Star God Palace and Qingyang Shrine below watched with twitching eyelids, even their soles cramping up.

At first, the rotation wasn't too fast, but gradually the speed intensified. Zhu Lie's twenty-zhang body had transformed into a perfect circle, seemingly splitting into countless afterimages as dozens of shadowy forms radiated outward from Yang Kai as their center.

It was not some kind of clone technique, merely the afterimages left behind by the sheer speed of the rotation.

Dizzy and disoriented, Zhu Lie couldn’t gather even a trace of his dragon force. Feeling the gazes of the ants below, an indescribable sense of shame surged in his heart, making him wish he could faint on the spot.

With a whoosh, Yang Kai let go, and Zhu Lie shot toward the ground like a cannonball.

The earth trembled, dust billowed, and a massive pit appeared on the ground. The twenty-zhang-long dragon body shook its head desperately in the pit, trying to get up but stumbling around like a drunk.

Yang Kai descended from the sky, landing squarely on Zhu Lie’s head. He grabbed one of the dragon horns with one hand, clenched his other fist, and smashed it down onto Zhu Lie’s forehead.

Boom, boom, boom—

It was as if a giant hammer was pounding their hearts. With every blow Yang Kai landed, Wen Zishan and the others felt their chests tighten.

Zhu Lie still struggled, but under the double pressure of bloodline suppression and dizziness, his resistance was laughably childish.

“Come on, tell me—how high is the sky, how deep is the earth?” Yang Kai taunted as he beat him.

Zhu Lie had no time to answer, only able to roar and howl. Hearing Yang Kai’s mocking words, he felt even more miserable, his qi and blood churning so fiercely he nearly coughed up blood.

After a hundred punches, Zhu Lie’s head was battered and bloody, several dragon scales nearly shattered. He seemed exhausted, sprawled on the ground, tongue lolling, panting heavily, but his huge dragon eyes still burned with fury.

If not for the suppression of his bloodline, he would have leapt up and swallowed Yang Kai whole by now.

“So, do I still need to go to Dragon Isle?” Yang Kai, still sitting on his head, slapped him as he spoke, his own hand stinging from the blow.

“Whether you want to or not, you must go. The Dragon Clan will not—”

Before he could finish, Yang Kai stomped on his head, the immense force slamming Zhu Lie’s skull into the ground with a bang. He nearly bit his own tongue and quickly shut up.

“I’ll ask you again—do I still need to go to Dragon Isle?” Yang Kai looked down at him from above.

Having suffered once, Zhu Lie ignored him completely.

“Think I can’t handle you?” Yang Kai grinned wickedly, his gaze searching Zhu Lie’s body. He had noticed something during the fight, but hadn’t been sure. Now was the time to check.

In no time, Yang Kai’s eyes lit up, staring intently at a dragon scale on Zhu Lie’s neck.

At first glance, that scale looked no different from the others, but on closer inspection, it was a bit darker and its aura was distinct.

Yang Kai extended his dragon claw and grabbed the basin-sized scale.

“What are you doing?” Zhu Lie was startled, roaring as he turned his head. Seeing Yang Kai’s action, he struggled with newfound strength, trying to soar into the sky.

Yang Kai ignored him, locking both claws onto the scale. With a shout, he used all his strength to rip it from Zhu Lie’s body.

With a tearing sound, dragon blood spurted as Zhu Lie’s anguished howl echoed through the heavens.

In that instant, the giant dragon’s body shrank rapidly. In the blink of an eye, Zhu Lie transformed into a young man with fiery red hair, looking just over twenty, with sharp brows and starry eyes, strikingly handsome—a face sure to attract countless women wherever he went.

But after Yang Kai’s earlier beating, Zhu Lie now looked rather miserable. His flaming hair was a tangled mess, as if it hadn’t been combed in over a decade. Bruises covered his body, his clothes were in disarray, and a thin wound on his neck was still bleeding.

Without that dragon scale, he could no longer resist the suppression of the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power within Yang Kai, not even able to maintain his dragon form.

He leapt up from the ground, lunging madly at the dragon scale in Yang Kai’s hand, shouting, “Give it back! That’s mine!”

But now, before the four-zhang-tall half-dragon Yang Kai, he looked as ridiculous as a child.

Yang Kai slapped him down as if swatting a mosquito. Zhu Lie was pinned to the ground, Yang Kai’s foot on his head, unable to move. Yang Kai carefully examined the dragon scale in his hand and asked, “Is this what you were relying on to come after me?”

Zhu Lie gnashed his teeth, refusing to answer.

“Without this, how are you going to take me back to Dragon Isle?” Yang Kai grinned, looking down at Zhu Lie and snorting, “Looks like that girl Zhu Qing told you quite a bit about me.”

He felt a surge of annoyance. Zhu Lie was clearly prepared this time. If he hadn’t made a breakthrough in the Dragon Transformation Art at the critical moment, the outcome of this battle would have been uncertain.

He felt a surge of annoyance—this information must have been leaked by that wretched girl Zhu Qing. It left a bitter taste in his mouth.

But thinking it over, Zhu Qing was, after all, from Dragon Isle. She had invited him several times and been refused, so it was only natural she’d reveal some things.

“Tell me—how does this scale counteract bloodline suppression?” Yang Kai asked in a deep voice.

He had previously thought this dragon scale didn’t belong to Zhu Lie, that it was from a higher-ranked dragon on Dragon Isle. But now, on closer inspection, he realized he was wrong. It was indeed Zhu Lie’s own scale. Its deeper color and different aura were probably due to some secret art.

Yang Kai was wary of this secret art.

Two members of the Dragon Clan had come to him in succession. He might have to deal with more in the future. He couldn’t always rely on breakthroughs in the Dragon Transformation Art and the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power. Figuring out this secret art would help him prepare.

“Just kill me!” Zhu Lie stiffened his neck, putting on a look of facing death unflinchingly, then sneered, “Do you have the guts to slaughter a member of the Dragon Clan?”

Yang Kai’s face turned cold. “I can’t stand being provoked. When I’m provoked, I get impulsive, and when I’m impulsive, who knows what I might do. So, young man, don’t get clever. Your life or death is up to me.”

Zhu Lie gritted his teeth and fell silent.

Suddenly, Yang Kai crouched down, peering closely at the side of Zhu Lie’s face, frowning. “What’s your relationship with Zhu Qing?”

“No comment!” Zhu Lie snorted.

“Fine, don’t say.” Yang Kai curled his lip. He just thought Zhu Lie and Zhu Qing looked a bit alike, and since both were red dragons, they were probably related, so he asked casually. But since the other party didn’t want to answer, he couldn’t be bothered to dig further.

Wen Zishan and Lei Hong flew over side by side, coming to a halt ten zhang away from Yang Kai. Both of their expressions were somewhat complicated.

No one had expected things to escalate to this point.

At first, when Yang Kai and Zhu Lie started fighting, they thought Yang Kai would suffer. They planned to intervene after the Dragon’s anger subsided. Who would have thought Yang Kai would be so mighty, even beating a member of the Dragon Clan and pinning him underfoot?

Now things were troublesome!

“Great Elder Lei, you’re just in time!” Yang Kai’s eyes lit up as he spotted Lei Hong, as if seeing a savior.

Lei Hong’s body stiffened, a sense of foreboding welling up. He suddenly regretted not leaving earlier and foolishly following Wen Zishan over. Wasn’t this asking for trouble?

Yang Kai said, “I’ve caught a member of the Dragon Clan. This guy was too arrogant, so I’ll hand him over to you to bring back to Star God Palace for the Great Emperor to deal with. Whether he’s killed or skinned is up to the Great Emperor—I have no opinion!”

Lei Hong’s mouth twitched. He refused to take another step forward, acting as if he hadn’t heard Yang Kai at all. He turned to Wen Zishan and cupped his fists, “Hall Master Wen, now that this matter is settled, I’ll take my leave. The matter of eliminating demons will require your continued efforts. All of Star God Palace will do its utmost to assist. If there’s any news, please let us know as soon as possible.”

## Chapter 3089 — I Want to Gamble

"Sir wishes to go to the Ancestral Domain?" He Yunxiang asked.

"Mm. Do either of you have any leads?" Yang Kai looked at them expectantly.

Lu Huaishuang gave a bitter laugh. "Although my Lu Family is considered fairly strong on Morning Sun Star, across the entire Star Domain, we are nothing. I know nothing about matters concerning the Ancestral Domain either." She felt somewhat vexed. If she could help this Sir with this matter, the Lu Family would surely obtain an enormous return, yet the opportunity was right before her and she could not grasp it.

He Yunxiang's eyes turned, and she said, "Although I do not know how to go to the Ancestral Domain either, I think there should be someone who does."

"Who?" Yang Kai's eyes lit up.

Lu Huaishuang also reacted and turned to look at He Yunxiang. "First Chief is referring to the Yan Family?"

He Yunxiang pursed her lips in a smile and said, "Five hundred years ago, one of the Yan Family's ancestors left Taiyi Star and headed for the Ancestral Domain. At the time, this caused quite an uproar. Although no one knows whether that Yan Family ancestor successfully entered the Ancestral Domain, if one were to ask who in Wuji Star Domain understands this matter best, it would be none other than the Yan Family. Since Sir wishes to go to the Ancestral Domain, starting with the Yan Family would be best."

"The Yan Family..." A trace of wariness flashed through Lu Huaishuang's eyes. "They are not easy to provoke."

"What is the background of this Yan Family?" Yang Kai asked.

Knowing that he was not from Wuji Star Domain and did not understand how the forces within the Star Domain were distributed, the two women immediately explained carefully.

In Wuji Star Domain, the Yan Family was both the surname of a clan and a byword for a gathering of experts. This one Yan Family not only possessed a Cultivation Star like Taiyi Star, but also controlled four or five other Cultivation Stars, any one of which was no worse than Morning Sun Star, while Taiyi Star itself was far superior to Morning Sun Star.

If Cultivation Stars were also divided into grades, then Morning Sun Star could only be considered middle level, while Taiyi Star belonged to the very highest tier. Whether in terms of its Spiritual Qi of Heaven and Earth or the resources it produced, Morning Sun Star could not compare.

One could imagine just how terrifying the foundation of the Yan Family, which occupied so many Cultivation Stars, must be.

In Wuji Star Domain, the Yan Family could be called an overlord among overlords. It had as many as four or five Third Stage Void King Realm experts alone, and among them, the Yan Family's current Family Head, Yan Luo, enjoyed the reputation of being the number one person in Wuji Star Domain.

A Star Pirate group like Gale committed every evil, burning, killing, looting, and plundering, but if they encountered a Yan Family Warship in the Star Domain, they would never dare make a move.

After listening to their explanation, Yang Kai gained some understanding of this Yan Family and felt rather satisfied. If that was truly the case, perhaps he could start with the Yan Family and inquire about the method and route to the Ancestral Domain.

When the two women saw Yang Kai's calm expression and how the Yan Family's enormous power did not move him in the slightest, they increasingly felt that this man was unfathomable.

He Yunxiang was even more delighted, feeling that abandoning her pride and face to cling to this thick thigh was indeed no losing bargain. If she could go with him to the Ancestral Domain, what harm was there in willingly attaching herself to one leg hair?

Her expression shifted, and she pursed her lips in a smile. "Speaking of which, this is rather coincidental. Sister Shuang, is the destination of your trip not precisely Taiyi Star?"

Yang Kai said, "You were heading there?"

Lu Huaishuang nodded. "We were originally going to Taiyi Star to exchange some resources. If Sir does not mind, how about traveling together?" Morning Sun Star was very far from Taiyi Star. Even a Void King-Grade Warship would need a full three months to make the journey. The products of the two naturally differed in many ways. Every three to five years, the Lu Family would bring a batch of Morning Sun Star's specialties to other Cultivation Stars to exchange for those stars' specialties. The destination of this trip was precisely Taiyi Star.

"That is fine." Yang Kai naturally had no reason to refuse. Although he was in the Emperor Realm, after coming to this Lower Plane Star Domain, many things were inconvenient. He always felt as if all his strength was suppressed inside his body and could not be displayed, and even using a little strength would invite the rejection of the Heavenly Way.

Since it was on the way, he was naturally happy to hitch a ride.

With the destination decided, Yang Kai asked some more questions about Wuji Star Domain. The two women naturally explained with extreme enthusiasm. He Yunxiang's intention to attach herself to him was obvious and not concealed in the slightest.

Two hours later, Yang Kai said he was tired.

Lu Huaishuang tactfully rose to take her leave. Before leaving, she hesitated, then asked Yang Kai how he wished to deal with the He Family's Void King-Grade Warship.

Yang Kai waved his hand grandly. "You can have it."

How could a single Void King-Grade Warship still catch his eye?

He Yunxiang's dark brows twitched, and she sighed inwardly that this Sir truly was generous, giving away a Void King-Grade Warship just like that. But after thinking carefully, standing at his height, the way he viewed things was already completely different from hers. A treasure in her eyes might only be a pile of rubbish before him.

Lu Huaishuang thanked him profusely and stated that she would exchange Crystal Stones of equal value with Yang Kai for this Warship. Only after Yang Kai refused did she finally leave.

It was not that Yang Kai was trying to act generous. Rather, the Crystal Stones in this Lower Plane Star Domain were of no use to him at all. In his Spatial Ring, he still had piles upon piles of Upper-Level Source Crystals. If he took out even one, it might stir up a storm of blood in this Star Domain.

Lu Huaishuang bid farewell and left. Before leaving, she gave He Yunxiang a meaningful glance. The latter acted as if she had not seen it and shamelessly stayed behind.

The room door closed, and inside the wing room, a man and woman were suddenly alone together.

"Does Sir have any further instructions? If you have any need, just call on me." The wing room was not hot, yet He Yunxiang's cheeks were flushed. Her peach blossom eyes shimmered with watery light, and when she spoke, she gently pursed her red lips. If any other man saw her like this, he would probably lose his soul to lust in an instant and be so bewitched that his Divine Soul was turned upside down. The implication in her words was obvious.

Facing the youth before her, the only thing she could rely on was her own beauty, and coincidentally, she was very confident in it.

Yang Kai was a man, and moreover a hot-blooded man in the prime of life. In such an environment, with such a beauty and such an invitation, it was naturally inevitable that he would feel some heat rising. But after cultivating to this day, he did not lack the self-control he ought to have. Having wandered for many years, his horizons were broad, and he had seen who knew how many beauties of every kind, so how could he be seduced by a mere He Yunxiang?

He looked up at her and smiled. "Is it worth it?"

"What?"

"Your Primordial Yin is still intact. You are still a virgin, right?"

He Yunxiang's face instantly turned bright red. If the blush on her cheeks just now had carried some hint of deliberation, then at this moment it was a true expression of genuine feeling. She lowered her head and bit her red lips, then when she raised her head again, she said firmly, "As long as Sir can lead me onto a higher Martial Dao, then it is worth it."

Yang Kai shook his head. "I do not use this kind of thing as a transaction."

Since the topic had already been made clear, He Yunxiang no longer hid anything and said leisurely, "In a woman's life, she must always find a man she can entrust herself to. Looking across the entire Wuji Star Domain, the man worthy of entering my, He Yunxiang's, eyes has not yet been born." The youth's figure was reflected in her watery eyes. He had more than entered her eyes; it was practically impossible to pull him out.

It was not love at first sight, and there was not even the slightest bit of love, but she knew that if she truly wanted to find a man to entrust her life to, then the person before her was the best choice.

The opportunity was before her eyes. If she did not seize it with all her strength, it would soon slip through her fingers. She did not want to wait until her lifespan was about to end and, when looking back on the past, regret today's hesitation.

"You are so certain I can give you a broader world?"

He Yunxiang smoothed the fine hair by her ear and smiled faintly. "Although I do not know exactly what kind of power Sir possesses, a woman's sixth sense is never wrong."

"You are too confident. This trip of mine is to the Yan Family."

"I know." He Yunxiang looked at him curiously, not knowing why he had brought this up.

"I am going to the Yan Family to inquire about the method of entering the Ancestral Domain. How do you think the Yan Family will treat me?"

He Yunxiang was startled. She had not thought about this point, but as she followed Yang Kai's question and pondered it more deeply, she immediately connected many things.

The method of entering the Ancestral Domain had always been a secret. Although rumors circulated in Wuji Star Domain that many experts from different eras had successfully entered the Ancestral Domain, as for exactly where they entered from and how they entered, no one knew. This method had always been held only by those few most powerful forces.

Since it was a secret, they naturally would not be willing to reveal it to outsiders.

Yang Kai's trip would certainly not be smooth sailing. In all likelihood, some conflict would arise with the Yan Family.

Taiyi Star was the Yan Family's headquarters, the place where the high-level strength of the entire Yan Family was gathered most densely. If one clashed with the Yan Family there, anyone would likely suffer a loss.

The youth before her had heaven-reaching hands and eyes. Even if he was not a match for them, retreating in one piece would definitely be no problem. But if she, a mere Second Stage Void King Realm cultivator, had too close a relationship with him, how could she not be targeted by the Yan Family? At that time, she feared she would not be able to escape even if she grew wings.

She was also meticulous by nature. With a turn of her thoughts, she understood many things.

The resolve on her face vanished, replaced by hesitation.

Previously, she had thought that leaning on Yang Kai, this great tree, would make it easy to enjoy the shade, but now it seemed she had considered things too simply.

"Go out," Yang Kai waved his hand.

He Yunxiang did not move. Suddenly gritting her teeth, she knelt on one knee as she had when they first met and said in a deep voice, "Sir, please allow me to follow by your side and serve you like a dog or horse."

Yang Kai said in surprise, "I thought I had explained it clearly to you." The trace of hesitation on her face just now had been absolutely real, so why had she changed her mind in the blink of an eye? Did a woman's heart change too quickly, or was he simply too straightforward?

He Yunxiang lowered her head. The front of her dress was wide open, but not deliberately so; this posture simply made exposure hard to avoid. She said firmly, "In life, opportunities always exist, but only those one can grasp are truly opportunities."

"You might also grasp a poisoned needle."

"Sir must have gambled many times in this life as well, so I ask Sir to give me a chance to gamble once."

"You will regret it."

“The path is one’s own to choose. Once a decision has been made, there is no room for regret.”

Yang Kai gazed deeply at her, and He Yunxiang remained motionless.

Just as she said, for those on the Martial Dao, before one opportunity after another, every decision they made carried the nature of a gamble. Some people gambled and won, rising to the heavens in a single step. Some people gambled and lost, falling into the abyss.

She had also gambled her way here step by step. If one did not dare gamble, how could one talk about cultivation? Although she was a woman, she had mixed for years among a group of coarse men, with less of a woman's dithering and more of a man's decisiveness.

She wanted to gamble! To gamble that she could borrow this momentum and soar into the sky in a single flight!

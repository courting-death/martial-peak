## Chapter 2936 — Four Zhang Dragon Body

A resounding dragon roar echoed out. As golden light burst forth, a massive dragon head flashed behind Yang Kai and then vanished, plunging into his body and disappearing from sight.

Yang Kai threw a punch, smashing it fiercely into the giant dragon’s nose bridge.

With a thunderous boom, Zhu Lie’s head jerked to the side. The wild force left him dizzy and dazed, making him feel as if his nose had been broken.

“Hm?” Yang Kai was a bit surprised, because this strike did not achieve the effect he had expected.

After spending some time with Zhu Qing, he knew that for the Dragon Clan, the suppressive power of bloodlines was incomparably strong. The one with the higher bloodline could absolutely suppress the one with the lower bloodline, regardless of their actual strength.

The Dragon Clan’s bloodlines were divided into tiers. It was said that the Great Elder Zhu Yan was a Tenth-Tier Azure Dragon. No matter how strong a Dragon Clan member below the Tenth-Tier was, they could never be his match.

For example, Zhu Qing herself possessed strength comparable to the Third-Stage Emperor Realm. If Yang Kai were to fight her one-on-one, even if he used all his means, he might not be able to guarantee victory. But if he relied on the suppression of bloodline power, Zhu Qing would be completely unable to resist and could not display her strength at all.

In Yang Kai’s view, this Zhu Lie was indeed formidable, but his bloodline power could not possibly surpass Zhu Qing’s by much.

He could use the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power to suppress Zhu Qing, so naturally, dealing with Zhu Lie should not be a problem either.

Yet, after landing this punch, he realized he was wrong. The expected bloodline suppression did not appear. Instead, the sturdy dragon scales left his fist a bloody mess.

Zhu Lie was completely enraged by this punch and growled, “Since I dared to come for you, I naturally have means to counter you. Did you really think you could suppress me?”

As Zhu Lie roared with his maw wide open, a torrent of scorching flame erupted forth. In an instant, Yang Kai became a man of fire, his body rapidly melting away within the inferno.

The crowd below cried out in shock.

Zhu Lie was also a bit dumbfounded, his eyes going blank.

He had come under orders to bring Yang Kai back to Dragon Isle, but he had never intended to kill him. Faced with Yang Kai’s resistance and provocation, he could not restrain his anger and only wanted to teach him a lesson. Who would have thought that a single breath of dragon fire would burn the man to death?

What should he do now?

If the Great Elder found out, he would definitely be punished.

Just as he was at a loss, it suddenly felt as if a great mountain pressed down from above, making his head ache terribly. He could not help but let out a furious roar, and the figure that had been burned away before his eyes finally became clear.

It was actually just a remnant afterimage, left behind by moving too quickly.

Zhu Lie was both excited and furious. Excited that he had not accidentally killed his target, but furious that this little insect had managed to deceive him. He was utterly enraged. As his body swayed to dodge the attack from above, he reached out to grab at Yang Kai.

The massive dragon claw seemed to seal off a vast expanse of space. Qi gathered within the claw, its power terrifying. When this force was compressed to the limit, it suddenly exploded violently.

Fierce, unmatched power surged from the dragon claw, blasting toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai clapped his hands together, and a massive Moon Blade formed, slicing through space to meet the attack head-on.

With a deafening boom, the two forces collided, unleashing a mighty storm that tore countless rifts in the void.

Zhu Lie’s enormous form remained unmoved, while Yang Kai was blasted back over a hundred zhang before he could steady himself.

After this exchange, Yang Kai was at a complete disadvantage, clearly no match for Zhu Lie.

“What rare treasure did you bring?” Yang Kai’s gaze swept over Zhu Lie. He had seen clearly just now that whenever he attacked, a glimmer of light would flash on Zhu Lie’s body. It was this light that neutralized the suppressive effect of his Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power, allowing Zhu Lie to exert his full strength.

Zhu Lie snorted coldly, “Surrender quietly and come back to the Dragon Isle with me, and nothing will happen. If you dare resist again, bear the consequences yourself.”

“Can you force a cow to drink water?” Yang Kai laughed loudly, his laughter turning cold as he said grimly, “I want to see just how much protection your rare treasure can give you!”

As he spoke, he gritted his teeth and let out a low shout. His heroic body suddenly swelled, pure dragon might spreading out in threads, faint golden light seeping from every pore. Both hands transformed into dragon claws, two strange little bumps bulged on his head, and all the exposed skin was covered in dragon scales.

In the blink of an eye, Yang Kai had transformed into a monster about two or three zhang tall. He bore some traces of the Dragon Clan, but the imitation was so poor it was almost laughable—like a tiger drawn by a child that ended up looking more like a dog.

“This is…” Lei Hong’s eyes widened in shock as he stared at the sky.

Wen Zishan and the others were also dumbfounded. Gao Xueting’s beautiful eyes sparkled, and over a hundred Emperor Realm experts stood in stunned silence, each more shocked than the last.

No one had ever seen such a secret technique before, one that could bring about such a drastic transformation in a person.

Zhu Lie, however, reacted like a cat whose tail had been stepped on, gritting his teeth and roaring, “Dragon Transformation Art, Dragon Transformation Art! That damned girl actually taught you the Dragon Transformation Art—unforgivable, simply unforgivable!”

It was as if Yang Kai had committed an unpardonable crime. Zhu Lie’s voice was filled with extreme fury.

“Now you have no choice but to go to the Dragon Isle. The Dragon Transformation Art is a secret that must not be passed on. From now on, prepare to spend the rest of your life on the Dragon Isle!” As Zhu Lie shouted, he lunged forward, his twenty-zhang-long body flashing through the sky and instantly appearing before Yang Kai, spewing dragon breath from his mouth.

Yang Kai frowned inwardly, countering each move as he resisted Zhu Lie’s fierce assault.

He had not expected Zhu Lie to react so violently after he used the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique. Judging by his words, this Dragon Transformation Art was a forbidden secret of the Dragon Isle.

Yet when Zhu Qing taught him this secret technique, she had never mentioned it.

At that time, Zhu Qing had been in a hurry to head to Frozen Earth to search for the remains of a previous generation Dragon. Before leaving, on a sudden impulse, she had passed this pure Dragon Transformation Secret Technique to Yang Kai.

Only now did Yang Kai realize that Zhu Qing had taken a great risk in doing so. With the secret of cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art exposed, Dragon Isle would never let things rest. Yang Kai would be in trouble, and Zhu Qing likely would not fare well either.

The secret arts of any sect cannot be casually transmitted to outsiders, let alone a secret technique of Dragon Isle itself. Only now did Yang Kai realize just how great a risk Zhu Qing had taken by doing so. With the secret of the Dragon Transformation Art exposed, Dragon Isle would absolutely not let the matter rest. Yang Kai would face severe consequences, and Zhu Qing would likely suffer greatly as well.

Yang Kai could not understand why Zhu Qing had done this, but there was no time to ponder. The twenty-zhang-long dragon that Zhu Lie had become was unwieldy, but every attack carried earth-shattering power, forcing Yang Kai to respond with all his might and utmost caution.

Moreover, he could feel that after using the Dragon Transformation Art, he had gained a slight ability to suppress Zhu Lie’s Dragon Clan bloodline. So, although it was difficult, he could actually fight him to a standstill.

The Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power was a tremendous fortune for Yang Kai, but for a long time, he had not known how to convert it into his own strength.

The Dragon Transformation Art could solve this problem. The deeper he cultivated it, the more fully he could wield the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power. If he reached the pinnacle, he could even transform into a true dragon and roam the universe.

Unfortunately, Yang Kai had not cultivated it for long, so he could only transform into a half-dragon body about two or three zhang tall. Even so, he could exert some suppression on Zhu Lie, who possessed a rare treasure.

The two figures spun and darted through the air, each unleashing their own unique moves, locked in a fierce battle.

Streams of dragon breath rained down from the sky. Wen Zishan had to activate the power of the Grand Guardian Array to block the aftermath, or else the entire Qingyang Shrine would be in danger.

Gradually, the battlefield shifted higher and higher as Yang Kai and Zhu Lie fought their way into the clouds. Even Emperor Realm experts could barely glimpse their movements, only able to judge the intensity of the battle from the occasional dragon roars that echoed from the clouds.

Everyone was on edge, unsure how today’s events would end.

With the Dragon Clan making a move, Yang Kai would not come out ahead no matter the outcome, and Qingyang Shrine would likely suffer as well.

Realizing this, many Emperor Realm experts no longer dared to stay and watch, each finding an excuse to bid Wen Zishan farewell, lest they be caught up in the disaster. Wen Zishan, naturally in no mood to keep them, exchanged a few words and let them leave.

Over a hundred people quickly departed, leaving only Lei Hong and a group from Star God Palace behind.

“Brother Xiao said this kid was a troublemaker. I didn’t quite believe it at first, but now…” Lei Hong shook his head with a wry smile, straining his eyes to look up, but could only make out faint traces.

Wen Zishan said solemnly, “Yang Kai is not at fault today. The Dragon Clan is too arrogant. If it were me, I wouldn’t go with them either.”

Lei Hong said, “The problem now is, how will this end?”

Wen Zishan replied, “The shrine has suffered repeated upheavals and is stretched thin. Today’s matter will likely require the Great Emperor’s intervention.”

Lei Hong pondered for a moment, then nodded, “I will report this to the Great Emperor. The Dragon Clan may be difficult to deal with, but the Southern Territory is not to be trifled with either.”

“Thank you, Brother Lei!”

Lei Hong waved his hand, “You’re too polite, Hall Master Wen. Let’s join forces and… eh?”

Before he could finish, Lei Hong seemed to notice something new, his eyes widening as he looked upward.

Wen Zishan clearly noticed as well, his face full of astonishment.

Above the clouds, Yang Kai, as if aided by the gods, suddenly grew much taller from his original two or three zhang, abruptly reaching over four zhang. Such a body was already a giant among men, but still could not compare to a true dragon.

As his body swelled, the two little bumps on his forehead seemed to grow larger as well, and an indescribable aura spread out at once.

“You…” Zhu Lie, who was locked in a fierce struggle with Yang Kai, was shocked. If he were in human form, his face would surely be filled with terror. As he spoke, he hurriedly retreated, as if he had seen something truly horrifying.

Yang Kai was also momentarily stunned. Just now, as he fought desperately with Zhu Lie, intent on suppressing the other’s Dragon Clan bloodline, he had unknowingly broken through the shackles of the Dragon Transformation Art, taking another step forward in this secret technique. This allowed the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power within him to be drawn out even more, resulting in the change in his body.

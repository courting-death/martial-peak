## Chapter 2655 — Master Yang!

This Formation Talisman was nothing but a dispensable trinket to Yang Kai, and even to Nanmen Dajun, it was something he had casually refined, not particularly precious.

But for Old Ban and Ling'er, it was a different story.

Old Ban was only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. To suddenly see a Formation Talisman capable of trapping even a cultivator at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, how could he not be shocked? In other words, such a talisman could easily kill him a thousand times over.

What’s more, this Formation Talisman was crafted by Nanmen Dajun himself. It was truly the finest of the fine, enough to protect Ling'er for a very long time on her path of growth.

Old Ban’s heart was filled with unease. Such a precious item—how could he accept it without feeling ashamed? Especially since Yang Kai and Grandmaster Nanmen didn’t get along well, he felt all the more that he shouldn’t take it.

But before he could refuse, Yang Kai set down the jade pendant and said, “Since it’s for Ling'er, just keep it.”

Nanmen Dajun was delighted to hear this, knowing his roundabout strategy had succeeded. He couldn’t help but chuckle.

Old Ban’s expression was complicated as he cupped his fists and said, “This old man thanks the master on Ling'er’s behalf for your great kindness.”

“No need to be so polite, Elder. If you ever need my help in the future, just ask.” Nanmen Dajun was in a good mood and made a generous promise.

After all, Old Ban was clearly an honest man. Whether he would ever have the nerve to ask for help was another matter. Even if that day did come, anything concerning the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm would be a trivial matter for Nanmen Dajun, so he simply said this to leave a good impression on Yang Kai.

As it turned out, his approach was indeed effective.

Yang Kai’s previous indifference eased a little. He nodded slightly and said, “Grandmaster Nanmen, you are thoughtful.”

Nanmen Dajun laughed heartily. “Young Master Yang, you flatter me.”

He let out a small sigh of relief, but also knew it wasn’t the right time to press further. So, he didn’t mention the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation or the Wild Ancient Land, and simply stood aside to wait quietly. There would be plenty of opportunities in the future.

Looking left and right, Nanmen Dajun’s gaze was suddenly drawn to a certain array.

This was the small island on the Ice Lake. The Space Array Yang Kai had set up before was located here.

When Nanmen Dajun first arrived, he had been focused only on Yang Kai’s attitude and hadn’t noticed anything else. Now that he was relaxed, he immediately saw the extraordinary nature of this array.

He let out a soft exclamation, then, full of interest, walked over to the Space Array, circling around it to examine it closely.

“Ah!” A moment later, as if he had discovered something incredible, Nanmen Dajun cried out in surprise. His eyes shone with a startling light as he lunged at the array, touching here and there. After a while, he exclaimed in shock, “A cross-territory Space Array!”

No one knew how he could tell, but he immediately determined that this was a cross-territory Space Array.

In an instant, Nanmen Dajun’s face flushed red with excitement. The light in his eyes grew even brighter as he immersed himself in studying the array, his focus absolute.

At this moment, for Nanmen Dajun, the entire world consisted only of this cross-territory Space Array. Nothing else could distract him. As time passed, he would sometimes furrow his brow as if encountering a problem, sometimes smile, and sometimes even let out a mad cackle.

A cross-territory Space Array—there were only a handful in the entire Star Realm, all of them treasures left from ancient times. Every one of these arrays was controlled by Sects founded by Great Emperors.

In fact, there wasn’t a single cross-territory Space Array in the entire Northern Territory.

It could be said that cross-territory Space Arrays were extremely rare in the Star Realm. Even with his reputation, Nanmen Dajun had never had the chance to study one.

Nanmen Dajun never imagined he would encounter one here in Ice Heart Valley. It was his luck, his destiny. If he could unravel the secrets of this cross-territory Space Array, with his skills, he might even be able to replicate it. At that point, it would be a blessing for the entire Star Realm, and his name would surely resound throughout it.

Fame didn’t matter to him. What mattered was that if he could make another breakthrough with the help of this ancient marvel, he would have no regrets in life.

Every field has its specialists. For someone like Nanmen Dajun, the allure of a cross-territory Space Array was stronger than anything else.

“Marvelous, marvelous!” After a long while, Nanmen Dajun finally stood up, full of admiration. No one knew what he had discovered, but the look of respect on his face was obvious.

Looking up, he asked Yang Kai, “Young Master Yang, since you’re familiar with Ice Heart Valley, do you know who set this up?”

“What’s wrong?” Yang Kai asked curiously.

Nanmen Dajun hesitated for a moment, then explained, “I can confirm this is a cross-territory Space Array. The design is extremely ingenious and unparalleled, truly rare in the world. However…”

“However what?” Yang Kai frowned.

“It’s just that the technique feels a bit unpolished. But that can’t be right—someone capable of setting up such a brilliant array must have profound mastery, far above mine. Yet, for some reason, it feels a little unrefined to me.” Nanmen Dajun didn’t know if he was explaining to Yang Kai or just talking to himself. He muttered for a while, shaking his head. “I can’t figure it out, I really can’t!”

No wonder he couldn’t figure it out.

Although his eye was sharp enough to recognize a cross-territory Space Array, he could never have guessed that Yang Kai himself had set it up.

He wasn’t wrong—the design of this array was indeed ingenious and unparalleled, because the method and principles came from Yang Yan, but the one who set it up was Yang Kai. Yang Kai wasn’t proficient in the Dao of Arrays; he only knew how to set up this one, so some of the finer details were a bit rough.

The reason was simple: when Yang Kai set up the array, he knew how but not why. He was just following Yang Yan’s instructions step by step, without his own understanding of the Dao of Arrays.

“What puzzles me most is… this array doesn’t seem like an ancient relic, but rather something set up recently.” Nanmen Dajun frowned deeply, then gave a self-deprecating laugh. “Maybe I’m just seeing things.”

Even he didn’t quite believe his own judgment. Everyone knew that the method for setting up cross-territory Space Arrays had long been lost. Who could possibly set up such an array now?

“Grandmaster Nanmen, you truly have a discerning eye!”

Just then, a voice called out.

Everyone turned to look and saw a group of women, each with a different bearing and beauty, approaching. They were the Emperor Realm disciples of Ice Heart Valley.

Leading them, of course, was Bing Yun.

Yang Kai, Nanmen Dajun, and the others quickly saluted.

Although Old Ban had never met Bing Yun, after hearing Yang Kai’s greeting, he immediately understood that this peerlessly beautiful woman was the Valley Lord of Ice Heart Valley. He hurriedly pulled Ling'er along to pay their respects.

A trace of surprise flashed in Bing Yun’s eyes. She didn’t know why Yang Kai had brought an elder along, but she didn’t ask. She simply raised her hand, helping Old Ban up, and said, “No need for such formalities.”

Glancing at Ling'er, Bing Yun smiled. “What a clever little girl. Yang Kai, where did you pick her up?”

Yang Kai smiled and explained the origins of Old Ban and Ling'er, then said, “Senior, I’d like Ling'er to join Ice Heart Valley to cultivate. Would that be possible?”

Bing Yun nodded. “There’s no problem at all. There are plenty of disciples in the valley around her age. She can train with them from now on.”

Old Ban was overjoyed and said excitedly, “Quick, Ling'er, hurry and pay your respects to the Valley Lord!”

As he spoke, he gave the little girl a gentle push.

The little girl was clever indeed. She immediately knelt down and said in a crisp voice, “Ling'er greets the Valley Lord and all the seniors.”

“She really is a clever little girl.” A trace of surprise flashed in Bing Yun’s eyes as she helped her up, then turned and said, “Ying’er, make arrangements for her.”

Changsun Ying stepped forward, came to Ling'er, and spoke to her softly. Ling'er kept nodding in response.

After a while, Ling'er turned back and said, “Grandpa, take care of yourself. When I have time, I’ll come visit you.”

Old Ban’s eyes grew moist. He forced himself to hold back his reluctance and waved. “Go on, go on. Grandpa will be living in Binglun City from now on. Train well and don’t trouble the seniors.”

Only then did Ling'er, led by Changsun Ying, walk away, looking back at every step as she gradually disappeared from sight.

“Ling'er being able to join Ice Heart Valley is something Old Ban should be happy about.” Yang Kai comforted him from the side.

“Happy! This old man is just too happy.” Old Ban lifted his sleeve and wiped the corners of his eyes.

Although he had thought that with Yang Kai’s recommendation, it wouldn’t be difficult for the little girl to join Ice Heart Valley, now that his wish had come true so easily, he was still moved to tears.

“Senior Bing Yun, what did you mean by what you said when you arrived just now?”

Nanmen Dajun, who had been holding back for a while, finally found a chance to ask, “Do you mean this cross-territory Space Array was really set up recently?”

“You’ll have to ask Yang Kai about that.” Bing Yun smiled meaningfully and glanced to the side.

“What?” Nanmen Dajun was greatly surprised. He looked at Bing Yun, then at Yang Kai, and asked suspiciously, “Senior, are you saying that Young Master Yang set up this Space Array? Senior Bing Yun, you’re not joking, are you?”

Bing Yun said, “In any case, Ice Heart Valley didn’t have this before. A little over a month ago, Yang Kai asked me for a lot of Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade, and then this appeared.”

The meaning was clear. With Bing Yun’s status, she wouldn’t joke about such things. Nanmen Dajun’s eyes widened as he stared at Yang Kai, dazed for a long time before exclaiming excitedly, “Young Master Yang… no, Master Yang! Please accept my bow!”

As he spoke, he really bowed to the ground, his attitude sincere.

If before, Nanmen Dajun had tried to curry favor with Yang Kai out of personal motives, now he was truly convinced. To be able to set up a cross-territory Space Array, Yang Kai’s attainments in the Dao of Arrays were far beyond his own.

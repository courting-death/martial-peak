## Chapter 3141 — Dragged Down to Death

"Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor is just ahead." Biluo stood shoulder to shoulder with Liu Yan on a high mountain, pointing toward the brightly lit grounds below.

That place had originally been the Lord’s Mansion of the Mad Lion Domain, but ever since Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor invaded more than ten years ago, the Lord of the Mad Lion Domain, one of the Ten Great Lords, had been the first to betray his own side and surrender. From then on, the invaders had used this place as their main base for gaining a foothold on Di Chen Star.

It was not very far from Red Moon Domain. With Biluo's cultivation, she had reached it after only an hour of flying.

A cold wind blew, and Biluo was suddenly filled with regret.

What had possessed her to bring Liu Yan here? This was not a safe place. If the people from Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor discovered them, how could they possibly survive? But she truly could not bear watching the little girl beside her imitate all of her Lady's poses in front of her, and in a fit of embarrassment and anger, she had brought her here.

Liu Yan stood beside her, her eyes fixed on the largest building below. With a single sweep of her Divine Sense, she saw the situation clearly.

"Have you seen enough?" Biluo asked, lowering her head. "If you have, come back with me. If anything happens to you, I will have no way to explain it to the Lords."

She shook her head and laughed despite herself. This little girl was clever and strange; Biluo had no idea what she had come to Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor to see.

Biluo turned and flew away, only for her expression to change sharply a moment later. When she looked back, Liu Yan had not followed at all. Instead, her tiny body had leapt down from the peak and was flying straight toward Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor.

"What are you doing!" Biluo cried under her breath, alarmed, then turned and chased after her.

Yet she could not catch up. This already counted as the enemy's heartland, so Biluo did not dare use too much strength for fear of being noticed. The little girl did not look as if she was flying very fast, yet somehow she kept leaving Biluo behind to eat her dust.

"Get back here! Are you trying to get yourself killed?" Biluo cursed through clenched teeth, but Liu Yan ignored her.

That attitude, as if Biluo did not exist at all, made her temper flare. Without realizing it, she pushed a little more Saint Yuan through her body, and her speed suddenly rose by quite a bit. The distance between them rapidly shrank.

A short while later, she grabbed Liu Yan by the arm and dragged her to a stop, then scolded her fiercely, "Why are you so disobedient, little girl? Do I have to smack your bottom before you behave?"

Liu Yan lifted her head and looked at her quietly.

"Still glaring at me? We are leaving!"

Liu Yan slowly shook her head. "Too late."

Biluo's face suddenly turned pale as well, for powerful Divine Senses surged out from that brightly lit place one after another like a tide, sweeping over her. Her heart sank. In chasing this little girl just now, she must have accidentally exposed her aura. This time, she feared she really would be killed after being dragged into this.

"More hindrance than help!" Liu Yan curled her lip.

She had planned to go straight in and launch a sneak attack, wiping out all of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor in one stroke. Now that the grass had been beaten and the snake startled, it would likely take a bit more work. Still, it was not much of a problem.

"Who are you talking about!" Biluo could hardly believe her ears. Even at a time like this, you actually dare lecture me? Do you not know I was dragged into this by you?

"Hahaha, since guests have come calling, why not come inside for a chat?" A loud voice suddenly rang out, rolling like thunder and carrying a deep, profound aura. When Biluo heard it, her mind shook despite herself, and even her body staggered slightly. The speaker's cultivation was definitely far above hers. It was very likely the Manor Master of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor.

She did not notice that Liu Yan was entirely unmoved by the voice. To her, it was no more than a light breeze across her face, and she remained perfectly composed.

After gritting her teeth and struggling for a moment, Biluo said bitterly, "This time, you really have gotten me killed."

More than a dozen powerful Divine Senses had locked onto her, held in reserve but ready to strike. She knew that if she showed the slightest sign of fleeing, she would be severely injured in the next instant. For now, she could only take things one step at a time. She had to think of a way out quickly, or she feared she would not leave this place alive today.

Her thoughts raced. At the same time, she took a deep breath, suppressed the fear in her heart and on her face, smiled faintly, and forced herself to say calmly, "Then I shall intrude."

She reached out and took Liu Yan's small hand, then walked toward the source of the voice. Quietly, she transmitted, "Once we are inside, do not say anything. Take your cue from me in everything." After a pause, she added, "If a fight really breaks out, find a chance to run for your life." Then she shook her head and said gloomily, "Actually, you should just kill yourself."

Although telling such a little girl to kill herself was far too cruel, if she truly fell into the hands of those people from Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, her end would likely be even harder to accept than death. It would be better to die and have done with it.

Liu Yan said nothing and let Biluo pull her along.

Inside Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, many disciples had long since been alerted. Seeing Biluo and Liu Yan arrive together, they all looked at them with mocking smiles, their gazes roaming brazenly over Biluo's lovely body. Some of the bolder ones even whistled.

Liu Yan naturally drew no small amount of attention as well. Such an exquisite little girl could always stir the covetous hearts of those with certain twisted tastes.

Biluo wore a faint smile and walked with leisurely ease, entering this dragon pool and tiger den as if she were strolling through her own rear garden. At this point, she could only put on this air of mystery to improve her bargaining position and increase her chances of survival. If she showed even the slightest fear, she would likely come to no good end.

The path was only a few dozen zhang, yet to Biluo, it felt as if she walked it for a year. If she had not deliberately restrained herself, her clothes would likely have been soaked through with sweat.

Suddenly, she felt someone pinch her palm. Looking down, she saw Liu Yan wink at her, and Liu Yan's clear, childish voice sounded beside her ear. "Do not be afraid."

Biluo laughed despite herself and felt more than ever that this child was strange. Compared with Liu Yan's calm, she was simply a paper tiger. Even so, her mood seemed to be somewhat affected by Liu Yan, and she was no longer as nervous as before.

In front of the main hall, more than a dozen Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor disciples blocked the way.

Each disciple had a Yao Beast at his side. Wolves, bears, tigers, leopards, all sorts of beasts were present, each one a pet under their control. Their faces were also covered in many strange markings, giving them a strong exotic air.

Biluo knew those patterns were beast marks. The disciples of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor based their cultivation on beast taming. Every time they successfully controlled a Yao Beast, another beast mark appeared on their bodies. These marks were their most obvious feature and also the link between themselves and their Yao Beasts.

The more beast marks a person had, the stronger he was, and the more Yao Beasts he could control.

More than a dozen Origin Returning Realm cultivators formed a human wall, with no intention of making way. They only stared at Biluo provocatively, while the dozen or so Yao Beasts growled low in their throats. As if sensing their masters' intentions, they crouched ready to pounce.

Biluo stopped, swept a smiling gaze over the dozen or so faces before her, then her expression chilled and she shouted, "Move!"

Her fair hand swept out, and a fierce wind struck their faces. The dozen or so people blocking the path were blown staggering in all directions, while the many Yao Beasts retreated to either side! Shock filled their faces. They had never expected this woman, who had charged into Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor alone in the dead of night, to still dare act so wildly. For a moment, they were cowed. At last, they remembered that this woman was, in any case, a Void King Realm master, not someone they could oppose.

Biluo strode straight in, and no one dared stop her again. It was as if she had entered an empty place. Though she was a delicate, charming woman, at this moment her heroic bearing was magnificent.

As soon as she entered the main hall, the sound of strings and flutes reached her ears. Inside, everything was brightly lit. In the spacious hall, a group of young women sang and danced, clad in thin gauze that half hid and half revealed their charms, stirring endless imagination.

Cultivators sat in rows on both sides, some male and some female, some tall and some short, some fat and some thin. In front of each person was a small table piled with delicacies and fine wines, and every one of those figures radiated the aura of the Void King Realm.

Tonight, it seemed Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor was holding a grand banquet!

Biluo was stunned, and her heart sank. This time, it was truly over. The experts of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor had all gathered here. If she could not come up with a good explanation, she definitely would not be able to leave.

Pairs of eyes turned toward her, and Biluo immediately found it hard to breathe, as if a great mountain were pressing on her chest. She hurriedly pushed her Saint Yuan to resist, and only then did she feel a little better.

She kept her eyes forward, looking only at the burly man seated in the place of honor. He had a bear's waist and a tiger's back, and was built like an iron tower. He wore a beast-skin shirt that was not fastened shut, leaving his chest bare, making him look bold and unrestrained to the extreme.

He reclined on an unknown beast hide, a warm, delicate beauty nestled in his arms. One hand propped up his head, while the other lightly tapped the beat on his thigh along with the string and flute music. He did not open his eyes when Biluo walked in, and when she came to a stop below, he still seemed not to notice. He was like a sleeping lion, his entire body giving off an awe-inspiring aura that brooked no offense.

Biluo naturally recognized him. He was Huang Tudao, the Manor Master of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, and the chief culprit behind the disaster on Di Chen. Shan Qingluo had fought him several times without either side gaining the upper hand. This was a Third Stage Void King Realm expert!

The dancing girls continued their graceful dance, and the string and flute music went on without pause. Tonight's grand banquet had not been interrupted by Biluo's arrival. Only the Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor experts seated around the hall continued to study Biluo with amused gazes.

As for Liu Yan... no one cared about her. Many were merely curious why Biluo had brought a little girl along.

In the main hall, Biluo pressed her red lips together and said nothing.

Huang Tudao suddenly opened his eyes like a sleeping lion awakening. A sharp light seemed to flash through them as he shouted with stern majesty, “That dancing girl below, why are you not dancing? Are you courting death?”

As soon as he said this, everyone around them burst into laughter and began jeering. "Come dance for us. Dance well, and you will be heavily rewarded." "Hurry up and dance, hurry. Do not worry; even if you dance poorly, there will be no punishment. Our Manor Master cherishes beauties the most." "She probably has not eaten enough and has no strength to dance. Come, come, this fine piece of meat is your reward!" A piece of beast meat, perfect in color, aroma, and flavor, was suddenly tossed at Biluo's feet.

Biluo paid it no mind. She only smiled faintly, dipped into a graceful bow, and said, "This envoy of Red Moon Domain greets Manor Master Huang."

Huang Tudao raised a brow. "Envoy of Red Moon Domain?" He pretended to look her over carefully, as though only now seeing Biluo's face clearly. "You are that maidservant beside Shan Qingluo."

"Biluo."

"So it is you!" Huang Tudao grinned, his expression ferocious, then suddenly shouted, "You have quite some nerve, daring to barge into my Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor. Could it be that you have grown tired of living?"

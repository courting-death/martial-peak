## Chapter 2755 — Big Business

"A...all of them?" Kang Siran was shocked. That was thirty million Middle-Level Source Crystals, not thirty million Lower-Level Source Crystals. Even if exchanged for Upper-Level Source Crystals, it would be worth three hundred thousand pieces.

"All of them!" Yang Kai nodded seriously.

Kang Siran immediately realized this was likely the biggest deal he'd ever negotiated. With this deal as a foundation, even if he was transferred, he wouldn't be sent to some godforsaken place.

Before the joy could show on his face, Yang Kai said, "Additionally, I want to ask Brother Kang to keep helping me purchase Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade. However much there is, I want it all."

"Hiss..." Kang Siran drew in a cold breath.

Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade were mostly used to refine Spatial Rings. But in the Star Realm, Spatial Rings weren't considered valuable. Many cultivators carried a dozen or so, simply because it's convenient to toss a Spatial Ring over during a transaction.

So the demand for these two materials wasn’t very high. Yet Yang Kai was buying up all thirty million Middle-Level Source Crystals’ worth of materials and wanted more. Just how many Spatial Rings was he planning to refine?

Steadying his mind, Kang Siran said, "Our branch only has thirty million Middle-Level Source Crystals' worth on hand. The main guild should have more, and we can transfer from other branches as well. Brother Yang, are you sure you want as much as we can get? That's no small sum."

A single branch had thirty million Middle-Level Source Crystals’ worth of goods; ten branches would be three hundred million, and a hundred branches would be three billion. The Ziyuan Commerce Guild had over a hundred branches in the Southern Territory alone. If Yang Kai truly wanted to buy everything available, it would easily require tens of millions of Upper-Level Source Crystals’ worth of materials.

Tens of millions, and they're Upper-Level Source Crystals! This is absolutely a massive deal.

If this deal went through, Kang Siran’s performance would be enough to earn him a promotion to branch president! The only thing holding him back was his cultivation—he was still a step short, and without reaching the Emperor Realm, he wouldn’t be able to convince the others. After all, the Commerce Guild’s rules were clear: only those in the Emperor Realm could serve as branch presidents.

If it had been anyone else coming to Kang Siran with such a deal, he wouldn’t have hesitated for a moment—he’d have agreed on the spot without a second thought. But since it was Yang Kai, Kang Siran couldn’t help but consider things more carefully for his sake.

Afraid Yang Kai might not grasp the full weight of the matter, Kang Siran even took the trouble to calculate the numbers for him.

“I'm certain.” Yang Kai smiled faintly. “Or is it that Brother Kang doesn’t have the authority to decide?”

Kang Siran straightened his expression and said solemnly, "This is such a huge transaction—I'm just a minor steward, I truly can't make the final decision."

Yang Kai was just joking, but didn't expect it to be true. He was a bit stunned.

Kang Siran said, "If you really want to do this deal, I'll go invite the president to talk with you personally."

Yang Kai said, "Alright!"

"Wait a moment!" Kang Siran said, then immediately opened the door and walked out.

Outside, Gu Hong saw Kang Siran come out and smiled, "Steward Kang, how's the negotiation? Need my help?"

Kang Siran snorted, "There's no need for Steward Gu."

Gu Hong gave a cold snort and flicked his sleeve. "Some people just don't know what's good for them." In front of Yang Kai, he had to maintain the Commerce Guild's image, so he couldn't show any displeasure. But with Kang Siran, there was no need to hold back.

Kang Siran headed straight upstairs. Gu Hong frowned.

Judging by this, it seemed he was going to find the president. Was the deal so big that even a steward couldn't make the call? Realizing this, Gu Hong was shocked. If that's the case, Kang Siran's achievement would be huge. He might secure his position in the branch.

Anxiety appeared on his face.

Before long, someone with powerful backing would inevitably be sent here to take up a steward’s post. But since the number of stewards in each branch was fixed, whenever a new arrival came, one of the current stewards would have to give up their position.

Based on both performance and cultivation, the one who should have to give up his position was definitely Kang Siran. But if Kang Siran managed to secure his place, then it would probably be Gu Hong’s turn to leave.

In any case, someone would have to leave.

Fenglin City was a great place. Lots of cultivators, lots of transactions. Gu Hong couldn't bear to leave. Working here for a year was worth two or three years in another branch. That's why he always made things difficult for Kang Siran, stealing his deals, hoping to cut off Kang Siran's hope of staying and force him to leave.

Now it might all be for nothing! Gu Hong was both shocked and angry.

Lost in his tangled thoughts, Gu Hong suddenly saw the president descending the stairs with Kang Siran. His expression changed at once—this deal had actually alarmed the president himself! Just how enormous was this transaction?

He felt indignant. How could Kang Siran have such luck?

In front of the private room, Kang Siran gently knocked. After Yang Kai responded, he opened the door and respectfully said, "President, please!"

A short, middle-aged elder stepped in, his gaze sharp as lightning as he swept it over Yang Kai.

Yang Kai raised his brows, stood up, and cupped his fists, "Greetings, President Lou!"

The branch president in Fenglin City was actually Lou Chi, the vice president of the entire Ziyuan Commerce Guild! This showed how much Ziyuan Commerce Guild valued Fenglin City.

Yang Kai had met him years ago, so he naturally remembered.

"You recognize this old man?" Lou Chi was surprised. Though he was the vice president of Ziyuan Commerce Guild and president of the Fenglin City branch, he rarely appeared in public. Few had seen him, so being recognized by Yang Kai was surprising. Looking closely, he felt Yang Kai looked familiar, as if he'd seen him somewhere before.

Lou Chi released his Divine Sense and was shocked to find that the young man before him had cultivation not inferior to his own—both were at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

When Kang Siran reported that a friend wanted to discuss a big deal, Lou Chi hadn't paid much attention. What kind of friend could someone of Kang Siran's cultivation have?

Now he realized he was wrong. This man was in the Emperor Realm!

He immediately put away any trace of contempt and smiled, "Seems I've seen you somewhere before, young friend!"

Yang Kai smiled, "When the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda of Star God Palace opened in Fenglin City, I was there. When Four Seasons Land opened, I was there too."

Lou Chi thought for a moment, then his eyes lit up, "You are..."

"Yang Kai!"

Lou Chi's face darkened and he shouted, "You have some nerve, showing up so openly!"

His expression changed rapidly. Kang Siran was startled, not knowing what Yang Kai had done to offend Lou Chi.

Yang Kai was momentarily confused, but quickly understood the attitude was related to Star God Palace. Some things Kang Siran couldn't access, but Lou Chi could.

Smiling, he said, "My conscience is clear. Why wouldn't I show myself?"

Lou Chi stared at him, "You are indeed bold. But who knows what fate awaits you."

Yang Kai said, "President Lou, are we going to discuss business, or not? If not, I can go to Qiyao Commerce Guild. I'm sure they'd be interested."

"We'll talk, why not!" Lou Chi snorted. Yang Kai deliberately brought up Ziyuan Commerce Guild's old rival, clearly playing hard to get. But business is business, and Lou Chi wouldn't let it slip by.

Yang Kai gestured, and Lou Chi immediately sat down, "Young friend, please sit as well!"

Yang Kai glanced at Kang Siran standing beside Lou Chi.

Lou Chi said, "Steward Kang, you sit too." He wondered why Yang Kai valued Kang Siran so much. Their relationship seemed quite good.

Kang Siran was a bit nervous, but since Lou Chi had spoken, he could only sit, looking a bit restrained.

Lou Chi got straight to the point, "Steward Kang said the deal is worth tens of millions of Upper-Level Source Crystals?"

"Exactly! I wonder if President Lou can handle it." Yang Kai nodded.

Lou Chi laughed, "There's no deal Ziyuan Commerce Guild can't handle. But I'm curious, why do you need so many Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade?"

Yang Kai smiled, "That's not something President Lou needs to worry about, is it?"

Lou Chi was stunned, then smiled, "I suppose I was being nosy." He pondered, "The amount you need is enormous. It's not that I don't trust you, but shouldn't you show some sincerity?"

As soon as he finished, Yang Kai tossed over a Spatial Ring, "Here's the deposit. See if you're satisfied."

Lou Chi took it, swept it with his Divine Sense, and immediately saw a mountain of Upper-Level Source Crystals—at least ten million.

His expression became serious. He knew Yang Kai was qualified to do business with the Commerce Guild. He nodded, "Very good. You're an honest man, so I'll be straightforward. Within two months, I can transfer goods worth forty million Upper-Level Source Crystals for you. I hope you'll bring enough Source Crystals to the Commerce Guild then, and we'll settle the deal."

"Agreed!" Yang Kai nodded. "But I have one condition."

Lou Chi said, "Please speak, young friend!"

Yang Kai looked at Kang Siran, "I will only handle this matter with Steward Kang. No one else."

Lou Chi frowned, "Why? Do you not trust Ziyuan Commerce Guild?"

Yang Kai shook his head, "The Commerce Guild's reputation is well known. Of course I trust it. But Steward Kang and I are close friends, so I feel more comfortable working with him."

Lou Chi looked troubled. In a month or two, Kang Siran would definitely be transferred. When he's no longer in Fenglin City, how could he handle the deal with Yang Kai? But this business would bring huge profits to the Commerce Guild, so Lou Chi had to consider carefully.

He asked, "Are you trying to help Steward Kang?"

He could more or less guess Yang Kai’s intention—Yang Kai simply wanted to use this deal to keep Kang Siran in prosperous Fenglin City. Shaking his head, Lou Chi said, “Young friend, your efforts are in vain. At most, this will let Steward Kang stay in Fenglin City for another two months, but once the transaction is complete, he’ll still be transferred as scheduled.”

Yang Kai frowned, "President Lou, is there no way?"

Lou Chi said, "That's a decision from the main guild. I can't do anything about it."

Kang Siran looked grateful and was about to speak, but Yang Kai stopped him and said seriously, "What if I said there's more business to do with Steward Kang? And it's long-term!"

Lou Chi narrowed his eyes, "That depends on what kind of business."

Yang Kai casually swept his hand across the table, and a bright, round, beast inner core the size of a dragon's eye appeared. Intense yao qi surged forth.

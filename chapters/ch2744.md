## Chapter 2744 — Kicked the Iron Plate

It was well known that both Fenglin City and Linghu City were places where hidden dragons and crouching tigers lurked. Anyone you pulled out at random might have powerful connections behind them, and if you weren’t careful, you could easily kick the iron plate.

For someone like Bian Yuqing, even if she had brought reinforcements, their strength would not be very high. And with Yang Kai’s age laid bare before them, it was difficult not to look down on him.

"Junior Brothers, kill this brat, then come help your Senior Brother. If we seize the command token today, ascending to the Emperor Realm will be within reach!" The middle-aged man pointed at Yang Kai and ordered the other two.

The two Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivators nodded slightly, their eyes flashing with ferocity. Their figures flickered, and they flanked Yang Kai from left and right.

"Yang Kai, be careful!" Bian Yuqing warned.

Hearing this, the middle-aged man couldn’t help but sneer: “Still worried about your little lover? You’d better worry about yourself first.”

As soon as he finished, his sword flashed and he lunged at Bian Yuqing, his long sword raised, bringing forth a dazzling sword light.

Just then, two miserable screams suddenly rang out at the same time.

The middle-aged man’s expression changed. He turned toward the source of the sound, his eyes widening in shock.

He saw that the two Junior Brothers who had just charged at Yang Kai now had gaping holes in their chests. Through those holes, you could see the scenery on the other side of their bodies, and their writhing organs were clearly visible.

"What happened!" The middle-aged man’s mind went blank. Just a moment ago, his two Junior Brothers were fine, but in the blink of an eye, they ended up like this. The terrifying wounds were right at their hearts, and even their entire hearts had vanished. In other words, his two Junior Brothers had absolutely no chance of survival—they were definitely dead.

They were Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivators, their strength not much less than his own. How could they be killed so quickly?

Who did this?

The middle-aged man’s first reaction was that a master was hiding nearby. As soon as this thought arose, his attack on Bian Yuqing halted, and the dazzling sword light on his blade instantly dissipated.

Bian Yuqing was also stunned. She had planned to risk dying here today to kill this middle-aged man and avenge Kou Wu. As long as Yang Kai could hold off the other two for a while, she was confident she could do it.

Although she had never managed to ascend to the Emperor Realm, she had spent many years at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. At this level, she was definitely a top expert.

But now, the two opponents Yang Kai was supposed to be holding off had been gravely wounded and were clearly doomed.

Everything happened in a flash, so fast that everyone’s thoughts struggled to keep up.

A shocking aura suddenly erupted from Yang Kai’s body. He reached out and squeezed his hand. The two Junior Brothers exploded into a mist of blood, leaving no trace behind.

Ding! Two Spatial Rings fell to the ground. The sound was light, but it struck everyone’s ears like a morning bell, snapping them out of their shock.

"Emperor Realm!" The middle-aged man’s face changed dramatically, as he nearly bit his tongue.

This sudden helper turned out to be an Emperor Realm cultivator!

And not just any Emperor. Even a First Stage Emperor couldn’t have killed his two Junior Brothers in an instant. This young man was definitely a Second Stage, maybe even Third Stage Emperor—an utterly terrifying existence!

Realizing this, the middle-aged man was seized with terror and despair.

He really had kicked the iron plate.

He couldn’t help but feel like crying. He had carefully investigated Bian Yuqing’s background—she came from a sect called Biyu Sect. After the Sect Master disappeared, the sect fell apart and soon disbanded. Bian Yuqing didn’t seem to know any Emperor Realm cultivators. How could such a terrifying helper suddenly appear?

Fear rising in his heart, he no longer dared attack Bian Yuqing. All he could think about was how to save his own life. That young man could kill his two Junior Brothers with a flick of his wrist; killing him would be as easy as blowing on him.

"Yang Kai, you—" Bian Yuqing was completely stunned.

She never could have imagined it. In just a few short years, Yang Kai had actually grown to the Emperor Realm—a realm she had pursued for so long but could never grasp, yet Yang Kai had reached it with ease. Was this really the same boy she once looked after when he first joined Biyu Sect? Cultivators from a lower Star Domain, could they truly be so terrifying?

For a moment, Bian Yuqing felt as though Yang Kai was a complete stranger.

"Protector Bian, aren’t you going to avenge Kou Wu?" Yang Kai looked at Bian Yuqing solemnly and urged, “If you don’t act now, when will you?”

Yang Kai’s words were like a wake-up call, snapping Bian Yuqing out of her daze. Her expression hardened, and she channeled her Origin Qi into the bracelet she’d been gripping tightly. Suddenly, the bracelet burst forth with dazzling light and expanded, growing as thick as a person’s waist. With a swift hand seal, she tossed the enlarged jade bracelet toward the middle-aged man, sending it hurtling through the air with a thunderous roar.

The middle-aged man was still flustered. Bian Yuqing’s sudden attack caught him off guard. By the time he activated his Origin Qi and raised his sword to block, the enlarged bracelet was already upon him.

A loud crash rang out.

The bracelet seemed to carry the force of ten thousand jin. It struck the Dao Origin-Grade sword, bending it, and the residual force blasted through the sword and smashed his chest, shattering his protective Origin Qi.

The middle-aged man’s expression changed. He hadn’t expected Bian Yuqing’s attack to be so fierce. He and his two Junior Brothers had fought her before, but with numbers on their side, he hadn’t thought much of her. Now, fighting one-on-one, he realized how terrifying this beautiful woman was.

What’s more, an Emperor was watching him like a tiger. Distracted, he couldn’t use his full strength.

He was no match!

Before he could finish the thought, the bracelet bounced away, gathered strength, and came whistling back again.

Having suffered from this Secret Treasure before, the middle-aged man was terrified. He quickly swung his sword, using a set of sword techniques to guard himself tightly.

But once he fell behind, he kept falling behind. When Bian Yuqing attacked earlier, he was a beat too slow to react, and now he couldn’t keep up with her relentless assault. Again and again, his sword clashed with the Secret Treasure, numbing his grip so badly that several times he nearly lost hold of his blade.

His impressive sword techniques were full of flaws.

Bian Yuqing seized the opportunity, lunged forward, and her palms gathered stormy power, delivering a devastating blow to the middle-aged man.

Caught off guard, the middle-aged man had to guard against Yang Kai’s potential strike while also fending off the jade bracelet’s fierce assault. Split between the two, he was left unable to cope. He barely managed to sweep out a streak of sword light toward Bian Yuqing, but its power was lacking and she dispelled it with a casual motion.

Her palm struck his chest, causing him to spit blood.

At the critical moment, the middle-aged man twisted his body, avoiding a fatal spot. Otherwise, that single blow would have sent him into an abyss with no return.

Even so, he was far from safe. As he retreated, the bracelet Secret Treasure relentlessly pursued him, smashing down again and again. One careless move, and he would lose his life on the spot.

Terrified, he cried out, “Sir, I was blind and foolish! Please, have mercy and spare my life. The entire Duanyue Sect will reward you handsomely!”

He was clever, knowing his life depended on Yang Kai’s whim. Although Yang Kai hadn’t interfered in his fight with Bian Yuqing, if he could persuade Yang Kai to spare him, Bian Yuqing wouldn’t be able to kill him.

He deliberately mentioned his background, hoping Yang Kai would hesitate. After all, Duanyue Sect had an Emperor Realm expert.

He didn’t know that Yang Kai had no idea what Duanyue Sect was.

The Southern Territory was vast, with countless sects. How could Yang Kai know them all? Even if he did, he wouldn’t care. He dealt with the top sects of every territory, and even their Sect Masters didn’t dare speak loudly in front of him. He had tricked Li Jiao and Mi Tian out of two and a half billion Upper-Level Source Crystals. What was a mere Duanyue Sect to him?

Yang Kai grinned: “I’m not the one who wants to kill you. What’s the point of begging me?”

Hearing this, the middle-aged man’s face turned hopeless. He knew Yang Kai wouldn’t spare him, so he turned to Bian Yuqing: “Sister, you lost a disciple, I lost two Junior Brothers. Why don’t we call it quits? I’ll apologize to you.”

“Dream on!” Bian Yuqing snarled, her beautiful eyes red, almost crazed. Her Origin Qi surged like a tide, determined to finish him off.

The middle-aged man was furious: “Then I’ll fight you to the death!”

As he spoke, he desperately activated a layer of protective Origin Qi, raised his sword, and smeared it with his fingers. He opened his mouth and spat a large mouthful of Essence-Blood onto the blade.

The sword suddenly began to hum, its blade trembling as if it might soar skyward at any moment, radiating an incredibly sharp and fierce sword intent.

“Protector Bian, be careful,” Yang Kai warned, his eyes narrowing. He had no intention of intervening. He could easily kill the middle-aged man, but revenge was something that had to be taken personally.

In fact, Yang Kai didn’t need to remind her. Bian Yuqing knew the middle-aged man was going all out. Her expression grew serious, she clapped her hands together, and shouted, “Split!”

As soon as she spoke, the bracelet Secret Treasure suddenly split into two, then four, then eight.

In the blink of an eye, the sky was filled with enlarged bracelets, attacking the middle-aged man from all directions. Each one seemed real, radiating a powerful aura.

But Yang Kai could tell that only one was real; the rest were illusions.

The Secret Treasure was impressive, but only at the Dao Origin-Grade level.

The middle-aged man was startled by the change, his eyes confused. But he quickly steadied himself and snarled, “Even if I die, you won’t have it easy!”

Sword light flashed, man and sword became one, transforming into a stream of light that pierced through the crowd of bracelets, attacking Bian Yuqing.

## Chapter 2976 — Having Eyes Yet Failing to See

As she spoke, the Succubus actually turned around and began asking on Yang Kai's behalf. However, every Demon King she approached shook their head vigorously, looking at Yang Kai as though he were already a dead man, their expressions filled with ridicule and mockery.

The Succubus turned back and looked at Yang Kai with regret. "Little Brother, it isn't that Big Sister is unwilling to help, but they don't seem willing to let you pass. What do you think we should do?"

Yang Kai grinned. "No matter what, I still have to thank Big Sister. Mm, you should step aside for now."

"What are you going to do?" The Succubus was startled.

Yang Kai said seriously, "Since they aren't willing to move aside on their own, I can only use force. Big Sister, your skin is delicate and your flesh tender, and you look too frail to withstand even a breeze, so you should stand farther away to avoid being accidentally injured."

The Succubus was dumbfounded. She found Yang Kai's words utterly absurd. A mere fellow equivalent to a Lower-Level Demon King actually dared boast so wildly here. Had he grown tired of living? Yet his expression was extremely sincere, so she cautiously asked for confirmation, "You mean... you're going to fight your way through?"

"That's right!" Yang Kai nodded and immediately attacked.

With a raise of his hand, the ancient and unadorned Mountain and River Bell appeared. An exceedingly ancient and desolate aura burst forth as the intricate patterns on the bell lit up. A force capable of suppressing all things descended from the sky like an invisible sledgehammer.

Sensing this suppressive force, the many Demon Race experts standing in midair changed color. Under its pressure, they felt as though a ten-thousand-zhang mountain was bearing down on their heads. Their Qi and blood churned, their breathing became labored, and their bodies involuntarily sank toward the ground.

Some of the weaker Demon Commanders even coughed up blood. They flailed about in midair, unable to steady themselves no matter what they did, and plummeted one after another.

Even the Demon Kings with profound cultivations found it difficult to move freely and had to use their Secret Techniques to break free from the suppressive force.

The Mountain and River Bell rapidly expanded before descending directly from high above. Its enormous shadow blotted out the light before several Demon Kings, swallowing them like the maw of a giant beast amid their panicked cries.

With a thunderous crash, the Mountain and River Bell struck the ground, making the whole of Demon Fury City shake three times. Many less sturdy buildings collapsed at that very instant.

The entire area fell silent. Every member of the Demon Race stared in horror at the enormous bell. Several Demon Kings with considerable cultivations should now be pinned beneath it, including one whose strength was enough to rank among the top ten in all of Demon Fury City.

Yet even such a powerful Demon King had not had time to react to this terrifying blow. No one knew whether he was dead or alive.

The answer was soon revealed.

Yang Kai formed a hand seal, and the Mountain and River Bell spun as it shrank before flying back into his hand. Several mangled corpses were left on the ground, each as flat as a pancake, as though someone had used a rolling pin to flatten them into that state.

Sharp gasps rang out as the many members of the Demon Race changed color. The Demon Kings who had wantonly mocked Yang Kai earlier could no longer utter a single word, their hearts filled with mounting terror. Every Demon involuntarily stepped back, wanting to put more distance between themselves and Yang Kai.

A casual blow possessed such might. Who in Demon Fury City could withstand him head-on? Even the strongest Demon King could not accomplish such a feat. If they truly provoked this man into carrying out a massacre here, the whole of Demon Fury City might be destroyed.

"Heh heh..." The Succubus who had spoken earlier turned deathly pale. Swallowing hard, she said, "Little Brother, you're truly awful. You actually pretended to be a pig to eat a tiger just to frighten me. Big Sister was nearly scared to death by you." She forced a smile onto her face, but it looked uglier than crying. Her voice trembled, clearly showing that she was terrified.

Although she was a Demon King, her strength ranked near the bottom in Demon Fury City. If that blow had been directed at her, she certainly would not have met a good end.

Only now did she realize that this seemingly harmless human possessed devastating power.

Holding the Mountain and River Bell on his palm, Yang Kai shook his head at her words. "Big Sister, what you said isn't right."

"What isn't right?" The Succubus's heart thumped. She thought she must have said something wrong and was about to suffer a terrible calamity. She glanced fearfully at the little bell, unable to stop her body from trembling.

Yang Kai said solemnly, "I never said I wasn't a tiger, so how could I have been pretending?"

"Right, right, that's exactly how it is," the Succubus hurriedly agreed. "It was they who had eyes but failed to see, looking down on others with their dog eyes. They deserved to die."

Yang Kai smiled. "Big Sister is truly good at conversation. I can't help wanting to chat with you a while longer. Aiya, what should I do? I still have urgent business."

"Heh heh..." The Succubus's expression stiffened as she moved aside. "Since you have urgent business, you should hurry along. Don't delay matters."

"Very well!" Yang Kai nodded. "Then this Young Master will take his leave first. No one wants to stop me now, right?"

The Succubus shook her head. "Naturally, no one is so blind."

The many Demon Kings wore solemn expressions, but none dared speak carelessly. They were afraid that a loose tongue might provoke the human into striking again and killing several more of them.

"Good." Yang Kai winked at the Succubus. "Big Sister need not be so sad and worried. This Young Master may return one day. When that time comes, I'll be sure to have a candlelit night-long conversation with Big Sister and let you experience Little Brother's charm."

When was I ever sad or worried? The Succubus cursed endlessly in her heart but did not dare show the slightest sign of it. She could not have felt more aggrieved.

The group of Demon Kings also wore ugly expressions. Yang Kai's words made their hearts pound with fear. They only wanted this fellow to hurry up and leave, preferably to die in Yin Wind Mountain. How could they possibly hope that he would turn around and come charging back?

Fortunately, Yang Kai said nothing more, or these Demon Kings might truly have gone mad. The Flowing Cloud Shuttle once again pierced through the air, heading straight for Yin Wind Mountain several hundred li away.

The Succubus let out a long, heavy breath. She felt as though her whole body was soaked through, and when the Yin Wind blew over her, she became icy cold from head to toe.

One pair of eyes after another turned toward her. Gritting her teeth, the Succubus said, "Why are you all looking at me? I wasn't the one who killed them."

"You shouldn't have said so much to him," a Demon King with a powerful aura coldly snorted. Demon Fury City had never suffered such a great humiliation. Not only had someone barged into the city, but he had also wantonly killed several Demon Kings, yet no one had been able to restrain the human's brutality.

The Demon Race had utterly lost face, so naturally, someone had to take the blame.

The Succubus, who was the only one to have interacted with Yang Kai, was both the best and only choice.

"How can you blame me for this?" the Succubus shrieked, feeling more wronged than she could express.

"If you had spoken to him properly, how could Demon Fury City have suffered this undeserved calamity?"

The Succubus stared blankly at the Demon King, never imagining this could become a reason to reprimand her.

"Hm? Someone else is coming!" The Demon King turned his head and saw a handsome, red-haired young man approaching at an unhurried pace. His expression was gloomy and his brow deeply furrowed, clearly troubled by something.

He barged into Demon Fury City without the slightest restraint, treating the many Demon Race experts as though they did not exist, as if he were entering his own back garden.

The Demon King flew into a rage.

Demon Fury City had stood in the Revolving World for more than a hundred thousand years. When had anyone ever looked down on it like this? They could overlook the human who had forced his way through earlier. Although his cultivation was not particularly high, that artifact of his possessed astonishing power. But who did this fellow think he was?

He signaled to those around him with a glance, and the many Demon Race experts immediately flew up and blocked the red-haired young man's path.

Zhu Lie stopped and swept a cold gaze over those ahead of him. The fury in his heart was like a volcano on the verge of erupting and could explode at any moment.

The matter between Zhu Qing and Yang Kai had left him indescribably irritated. Worse still, what was done could not be undone, and he had no idea how the matter could be resolved in the future. His bellyful of rage had nowhere to go, yet someone had actually delivered themselves to his doorstep.

He took a deep breath and tried his best to calm himself before asking indifferently, "Who among you is the strongest?"

The question was rather baffling, but many members of the Demon Race still subconsciously glanced in a certain direction. Under everyone's gaze, a broad-shouldered, thick-waisted fellow shrouded in towering Demonic Qi stepped forward. His thunderous voice boomed, "This King is Hao Fei. Who are you?"

He was the same man who had reprimanded the Succubus earlier. Among all the Demon Kings, he was publicly acknowledged as the strongest. He was only one step away from breaking through his shackles and reaching the Demon Saint Realm.

He was confident he could break through within a hundred years. Once he became a Demon Saint, he could launch a large-scale attack on Human Sovereign City, overturn the unchanging balance that had endured in the Revolving World for over a hundred thousand years, enslave those humans, and turn this world into the Demon Race's back garden.

He possessed both such lofty ambitions and the potential to realize them.

When Yang Kai had used the Mountain and River Bell to unleash that terrifying blow, Hao Fei had weighed his own chances and estimated that he could not withstand such an attack, so he had swallowed his anger and remained silent. At this moment, however, he could no longer cower.

The Demon Race was warlike and worshipped the strong. If he did not answer, his clansmen would inevitably look down on him, and it would also be detrimental to his cultivation.

He secretly resolved to thoroughly humiliate this red-haired young man and show him the strength of Demon Fury City's number one master.

He looked contemptuously ahead, his gaze empty as though nothing in the world was worthy of his notice.

Zhu Lie softly spat out a single word. "Die!"

As soon as the word fell, his entire body transformed into a streak of fire and shot toward the Demon King named Hao Fei like a meteor, moving as swiftly as lightning.

Hao Fei's expression changed. The instant Zhu Lie attacked, he instinctively sensed the other man's strength and hurriedly circulated all his Demonic Qi to resist.

"Roar!"

A deafening Dragon Roar suddenly rang out, making everyone's eardrums ache.

The Dragon Roar seemed to contain an extremely peculiar power. When it entered Hao Fei's ears, the Demonic Qi in his body abruptly stagnated and nearly showed signs of collapsing.

His face changed drastically, and he hurriedly steadied his mind.

Just then, his vision suddenly turned fiery red, as though the entire world were burning. An incomparably enormous Dragon Head abruptly appeared, blotting out Heaven and Earth. Its majestic Dragon Eyes looked down upon him from high above, leaving Hao Fei's body rigid and his hands and feet ice-cold.

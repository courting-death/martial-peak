## Chapter 2874 — Want to Die or Live?

"What a joke!"

"Delusional!"

"Are you dreaming?"

A group of Demon Kings immediately began shouting. Although this fellow called Shaman Niu looked invincible, it was impossible for them to willingly submit. Most Demon Kings were the type who would rather break than bend; someone like Shaya was merely an exception.

Bao Qi stood beside Yang Kai, watching coldly, sneering in his heart.

Back then, he had been just like these Demon Kings, unwilling to submit to Lord Shaman Niu. But in the end, what happened? Lord Shaman Niu didn’t need his cooperation at all—he simply broke through his Sea of Consciousness by force and planted a brand within it.

The lesson from the past was a warning for the future. The fate of this group below would likely be the same. In this place, no one could resist him. From the moment these former companions stepped into this world, everything had already been decided.

"You want us to be your slaves, like Bao Qi?" Fei Li’s jaw opened and closed as he sneered, "You're dreaming!"

"If you have the guts, kill us all!" another Demon King shouted. It was the voice in the hearts of all the Demon Kings—except for Shaya, who harbored other thoughts, every Demon King showed unwavering resolve.

Yang Kai looked over at him. That Demon King met his gaze coldly, showing no weakness.

Yang Kai suddenly nodded lightly and said, "Since you want to die, I’ll grant your wish."

As he spoke, he suddenly raised his hand. The expression of the Demon King who had spoken changed abruptly as his body floated uncontrollably into the air, as if gripped tightly by an invisible giant hand. No matter how he struggled, he could not resist.

The other Demon Kings naturally wouldn’t just stand by. Their eyes met, and they all surged their demonic qi, preparing to attack together.

But before they could act, Yang Kai’s other hand suddenly pressed downward. At the same time, an overwhelming force descended from the heavens and earth. The power was so strong that it felt like the will of the entire world, instantly pinning all fifteen Demon Kings in place. Even moving a finger became nearly impossible.

The faces of the demons changed dramatically. Although Shaman Niu had previously displayed unimaginable power, everyone thought that if they joined forces, they might still have a chance. With so many Demon Kings acting together, even a Demon Saint would likely be hard-pressed to cope.

Reality, however, left them utterly terrified!

With a mere gesture, he suppressed fifteen Demon Kings. And it seemed he hadn’t even used much strength. Such power—perhaps only a true god would be qualified to possess it?

Each Demon King’s eyes trembled violently as they struggled to look up.

"Ku Ke!" Fei Li gritted his teeth and growled, "Never bow your head!"

The panic and confusion in the Demon King called Ku Ke’s eyes vanished, replaced by unwavering determination. He nodded and said in a muffled voice, "I will never bow my head!"

He struggled to lift his head, staring straight into Yang Kai’s eyes.

Yang Kai’s hand, which was clenched in the air, suddenly tightened. With a soft popping sound, as if something had been crushed, Ku Ke, who had been suspended in midair, suddenly shattered into pieces. His flesh and blood turned into chunks like stone, scattering from the sky and hitting the ground with a series of thuds.

Ku Ke belonged to the Stone Demon clan. His flesh and blood were as hard as the toughest stone in the world. His defense was the strongest among all the Demon Kings—even the Shadow Demon Mo Ke Tuo wasn’t confident she could break through it in an instant and deliver a fatal blow.

Yet such a Demon King was crushed by that Shaman Niu fellow with a mere squeeze—and from a distance, no less.

The falling stones were Ku Ke’s flesh and blood, mixed with three heart-like objects.

Those were Ku Ke’s demon hearts!

As long as a demon heart remained, a demon would not die! As long as any of the three demon hearts survived, Ku Ke would have a chance to come back to life.

Thud, thud, thud…

As they fell, the three demon hearts burst one after another, releasing a surge of pure, terrifying demonic qi that turned the whole world dark.

The Demon Kings felt as if they had fallen into an ice cellar, their bodies turning cold.

Ku Ke was dead! He perished just like that, utterly powerless. All three of his demon hearts were destroyed. Even the Great Demon God himself could not resurrect him.

Every Demon King knew this was Shaman Niu’s way of making an example, but they couldn’t help feeling horrified. He actually killed Ku Ke. They had thought that since he wanted them as slaves, he would at least value their lives—after all, dead men can’t be slaves.

But he killed Ku Ke without hesitation, as if killing a few more wouldn’t matter at all.

"I forgot to mention," Yang Kai said, hands behind his back, sweeping his gaze over the Demon Kings, speaking unhurriedly, "My ability is limited. I can only take ten slaves. After all, your cultivation is higher than mine, so ten is my limit. Any more and I can’t handle it. But there are fifteen of you now, so…" Yang Kai smiled. "Some of you must die!"

That smile imprinted itself in the Demon Kings’ eyes, and it felt even more terrifying than the wrath of the Demon Saints.

Bao Qi stood silently beside Yang Kai, saying nothing. He didn’t know whether Yang Kai’s claim about the ten was true, but from what he knew, it likely was. After all, planting Soul Brands in others’ minds required splitting off portions of one’s own soul—if too many were planted, even he wouldn’t be able to bear the strain.

Lord Shaman Niu’s true realm wasn’t that high. Being able to enslave ten Demon Kings was already shocking enough.

Counting himself and Shaya, there were only eight spots left. Excluding Shaya, there were still fourteen Demon Kings present, which meant six more had to die.

Bao Qi could easily calculate this, and so could the other Demon Kings. For a moment, gloom and heaviness filled the air.

Killing Ku Ke was not just to make an example, but also a necessary measure—there were simply too many people, and he had no use for the extras! Realizing this, some Demon Kings’ attitudes began to change.

If Yang Kai hadn’t said this, everyone might have united, living and dying together. But now that some would die and some would live, selfishness began to sprout in the hearts of the Demon Kings, breaking the atmosphere of shared resolve in an instant.

Yang Kai continued, "Don’t be afraid—even if you refuse to submit, I have ways to make you my slaves. But I prefer those who are willing. Forced melon is never sweet!"

Fei Li shouted, "Don’t listen to his nonsense! He wants to break our will. As long as we hold firm, he can only kill us! As Demon Kings, we’d rather die than submit!"

The shouting did stir up the blood of many Demon Kings.

Becoming a slave to an outsider was a disgrace. If that outsider’s cultivation was lower than theirs, it was an even greater shame.

Yang Kai smiled faintly, not stopping Fei Li’s incitement. After he finished, Yang Kai looked down at a Demon King with blood-red eyes. "You have ten breaths to decide—do you want to die, or live?"

That Demon King's arrogant expression turned cold as he sneered repeatedly at Yang Kai's question. With his power locked away deep within his body and unable to be released, he could only open his mouth and spit toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai said, "Don’t act like I’m bullying you. You invaded this land for no reason, slaughtering and pillaging the Barbarian Tribe. You’re the invaders!"

The Demon King laughed loudly, "Then kill me! I’ve killed plenty of outsiders myself. Dying isn’t a loss!"

"As you wish!" Yang Kai reached out and tapped his forehead. With a soft squelch, a hole appeared in the Demon King’s head. Several explosions sounded from within his body as his demon heart shattered, and he collapsed limply, the light fading from his eyes.

"I’ll keep asking until enough have died. The rest of you—congratulations. You’ll have the honor of becoming my slaves!" Yang Kai’s tone was indifferent, his power over life and death in stark contrast to the helpless Demon Kings. It was as if he were the true Demon King, and those before him were powerless weaklings. "So you’d better pray that everyone I ask refuses me. That way, the rest of you will have a chance to live."

He walked forward, soon arriving before Shaya, and asked calmly, "Die, or live?"

Shaya’s face was pale. The deaths of the previous two Demon Kings had clearly shocked her, leaving her flustered. She couldn’t answer Yang Kai’s question right away.

Fei Li shouted, "Shaya, we’ll be joining you soon. Death is just returning to the embrace of the Great Demon God. There’s nothing to fear."

Shaya mumbled, "Easy for you to say, Fei Li. You’re not the one being asked. Dying isn’t much, but dying so miserably would disgrace our demon race…"

"You…" Fei Li was shocked, his gaze dark as he looked at Shaya. "Do you really want to…"

"You have three breaths left!" Yang Kai slowly raised a finger, pointing at Shaya.

Shaya immediately half-knelt, head bowed. "Shaya is willing to serve Lord Shaman Niu."

All the Demon Kings’ faces changed. Most looked at Shaya with contempt and anger, feeling it was a disgrace to share the title of Demon King with her! Only a few showed understanding in their expressions.

Every race has those who face death unflinchingly, and those who fear death. Demon Kings were strong, but even they couldn’t escape this rule.

"Very good!" Yang Kai revealed a slight smile, placing his hand on Shaya’s head. After he made a show of performing the ritual, Shaya looked up with delight. "Thank you, my lord!"

With that, she stood up and walked over to Bao Qi.

Bao Qi glanced at her, giving her an approving look.

To be honest, if he hadn’t already known Shaya’s true nature, he would have been completely fooled by her.

"I really should have killed you before!" Fei Li gritted his teeth, thinking that dying by his own hand would have been better than being enslaved by an outsider. That was true disgrace for the demon race.

Yang Kai turned around, his expression turning serious as he let out a cold shout: "I'll ask one final time—do you wish to die, or do you wish to live?"

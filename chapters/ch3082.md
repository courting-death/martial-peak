## Chapter 3082 — Struck by Heaven's Lightning

The three chiefs of Gale each had their own unique skills. The First Chief, Sheng Yao, naturally went without saying. With his powerful cultivation at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm, he was someone who ranked highly even across the entire Star Domain.

The Second Chief, Ma Chaoqun, was a plump, fair-skinned fatty with an endearingly simple look, but that body of fat was not merely the result of overeating. It was also the capital he relied on to survive. His defense was astonishing, making him something like a human shield. With that body of fat, he could exchange a few moves with an expert one level stronger than himself without being put at a disadvantage.

Of course, it was only a few moves. The gap in cultivation was not something some fat could make up for.

As for the Third Chief, Ge Ming, he was a small, wiry man who was best at hiding and assassination. His movements were mysterious and unpredictable, coming without a shadow and leaving without a trace.

An enemy hidden in the dark was the most terrifying. This was precisely what people meant when they said an open spear was easy to dodge, but a hidden arrow was hard to guard against.

The one who had suddenly attacked Lu Huaishuang was none other than Gale's Third Chief, Ge Ming.

Lu Huaishuang's earlier guess had not been wrong. When Gale moved, they had indeed come out in full force. Ma Chaoqun's claim that their boss and Third Brother had important matters to attend to was nothing but a blatant lie.

Relying on his superior Concealment Divine Ability, Ge Ming had silently crept to Lu Huaishuang's side. Just as Lu Huaishuang's attention was drawn away by Ma Chaoqun and He Wuzui, his spike struck at leisure, erupting into a sudden assault.

He also had a cultivation at the First Stage of the Void King Realm, and he held the advantage of a sneak attack. With this strike, he was confident that even if he could not take Lu Huaishuang's life, he could at least heavily wound her!

The Lu Family was led by Lu Huaishuang. As long as this woman was heavily wounded, the remaining small fry were not worth worrying about.

Everything was going according to plan.

Then, the sudden accident nearly scared Ge Ming's soul out of his body.

In the time it took for sparks to fly, a raggedly dressed young man had appeared in front of Lu Huaishuang, calmly gazing at him. Though those eyes held not the slightest color, Ge Ming involuntarily felt a sense of inferiority. The young man before him was like a mountain ten thousand zhang tall. He looked up and could not see the scenery at the summit; even halfway up the mountain, clouds and mist roiled, hazy and indistinct.

Who was this person?

When he had secretly crept over earlier, he had noticed this person too. He had been lying at Lu Huaishuang's feet with no vitality at all, clearly already dead.

Then why could he stand up again? And why had he erupted with such powerful vitality in an instant?

How was this a dead man? He was clearly alive and well.

The panic lasted only an instant. Since Ge Ming could cultivate to the level of the Void King Realm, both his temperament and his wits were first-rate. After briefly weighing retreat against advance, dazzling cold light burst from his spike, and he thrust forward even faster.

Whoever this man was, at such close range, even a Third Stage Void King Realm master standing in his way would be pierced straight through.

"Move!" Lu Huaishuang finally came back to her senses. Seeing Ge Ming advance instead of retreat, his eyes filled with fierce light while the young man in front of her had no reaction at all, she immediately cried out and placed a hand on his shoulder, trying to pull him back.

What horrified her beyond measure was that the other party did not react at all. His feet seemed to have taken root in the earth. With that pull, not only did she fail to move him, she dragged herself into a stagger instead and nearly crashed into his back.

A crisp sound rang out.

The vicious expression on Ge Ming's face stiffened, replaced by extreme terror. He looked up at the young man, and even his eyeballs trembled violently.

In that instant, he had imagined many possibilities.

The most likely outcome was that this reckless fellow would be killed by him. It was also possible that he would only injure him, or that the other party would counterattack and he would adapt as needed.

But no matter what, he had never imagined the current situation.

The other party had not moved at all, simply letting his spike stab him in the chest.

His flesh was not pierced, and even his skin did not suffer the slightest damage. Instead, light flashed across Ge Ming's spike, its color dimmed slightly, and the web between his thumb and forefinger jolted, nearly sending the spike flying from his hand.

The spike was a Void King-Grade Secret Treasure, incomparably sharp. Forget a body of flesh and blood, even metal could be pierced through at will.

Ge Ming had not even sensed any fluctuation of power from the other party. In other words, this strange young man had truly taken his blow with a body of flesh and blood, and nothing had happened to him.

Was this bastard still human?

Ge Ming was proficient in concealment and assassination, skilled at killing in a single strike, and his sense for danger was extremely sharp. So when he discovered that his full-powered blow had no effect at all, he immediately made a decisive choice. His figure flickered, and his small body turned hazy, as though he was about to merge into the air.

He had no choice but to flee.

The existence of this young man before him had exceeded his understanding. A voice deep in his heart was shouting: Run, or die!

The young man slowly raised one hand. His movements were clearly not fast and even seemed a little stiff, but with one casual reach, he seized Ge Ming, who was already about to merge into the air, directly by the neck. His half-transparent body instantly solidified as well.

His Concealment Divine Ability was broken just like that.

In an instant, Ge Ming was scared out of his wits. He felt an immense force clamp down on his throat, as though his neck could be crushed at any moment. He struggled desperately, but it was all in vain.

The young man raised his hand and lifted him straight up.

Such a scene made people's hair stand on end. If they did not know Ge Ming's identity and strength, that would be one thing, but Ge Ming was a Void King Realm cultivator. How could he be captured so easily, like a powerless chick with no way to resist?

“Third Brother!” The smile finally vanished from Ma Chaoqun’s face, and his plump face turned grim.

He Wuzui's expression was also extremely grave. He silently summoned his own Secret Treasure, his gaze drifting as though searching for a path of retreat.

Under everyone's eyes, the young man looked at Ge Ming and examined him from head to toe. A moment later, he frowned and curled his lip. "Weak. Simply too weak!"

How many years had it been since he had fought someone in the Void King Realm? In these years, what enemy he had encountered had not been a heaven-reaching cultivator? Suddenly experiencing this again, he was actually somewhat unaccustomed to it, and also a little nostalgic. His mood was inexplicably complicated.

Back then, he had once walked this very same path himself.

"May I ask what great figure Your Excellency is!" Ma Chaoqun shouted in a deep voice. The faces and figures of several overlords of the Star Domain flashed through his mind, but none of them matched the person before him.

He had never heard of a young man whose strength was this astonishing.

"A pack of trash like you wants to know this Lord's name?" the young man coldly snorted.

His tone was wildly arrogant, yet no one felt it was out of place. Since he had such a heaven-high tone, he also had the strength to match. If someone else spoke like this, they would likely only invite laughter.

Ma Chaoqun did not even dare to get angry. He swallowed and cupped his fists. "Please calm your anger, Your Excellency. We were blind and ignorant. I ask Your Excellency to be magnanimous and spare my Third Brother. We will leave at once and absolutely never offend you again."

He kept a cautious, placating look on his face, while in his heart he cursed He Wuzui bloody.

That idiot Fourth Brother. He had not even known when the Lu Family had latched onto such a powerful backer, and they had foolishly run over here to seek humiliation. This time, they had kicked an iron plate, and he did not even know whether they would live to return. If there was a chance to go back, he would definitely sever all ties with Fourth Brother!

If he continued staying with Fourth Brother, sooner or later that idiot would get him killed.

"You want to come as you please and leave as you please? Where in this world is there such a cheap deal?" the young man sneered.

Although he had previously been in a special state, it was not as though he knew nothing about his surroundings. He had heard everyone's conversation, so naturally he knew exactly what these people were here for.

Though he was no sword-wielding chivalrous hero, dealing with trouble delivered right to his door took no effort.

Ma Chaoqun groaned bitterly in his heart, but he could only lower his posture again and again. "Please state your terms, Your Excellency. We of Gale admit defeat."

He could not even see the depth of the other party's cultivation, so he truly did not have the confidence to make an enemy of him. Otherwise, why would he demean himself like this? He was a man with some face, after all!

The young man frowned, looking down on the plump man with deep disdain. The fellow had no backbone whatsoever; he could not even stir the young man’s desire to kill him.

Rumble...

A muffled thunder-like rumble suddenly came from the sky. At the same time, the aura of the entire world abruptly became extremely dangerous, and arcs of lightning actually began to wander through the air.

Everyone's expression changed, and they looked up at the sky. At some unknown point, a pitch-black vortex had appeared among the clouds, and within that vortex, electric dragons seemed to be swimming, transmitting an aura that could destroy Heaven and Earth.

"Are you sick or something? I hid myself so well!" The young man was also looking up, and he suddenly burst into curses.

Lu Huaishuang's dark brows furrowed, and she stared at the young man's back with an odd expression.

Was he cursing the Heavens?

She suddenly felt a trace of sympathy for the young man. His cultivation seemed extraordinarily profound, his bearing imposing, and from the brief glimpse she had caught earlier, his appearance was quite handsome as well. So why did there seem to be something wrong with his head?

If not, why would he curse the Heavens?

Did the Heavens envy heroic talent? This thought surfaced in Lu Huaishuang's mind.

Before the thought finished turning, a great crack rang out, shaking everyone's eardrums. As if responding to the young man's curses, a bolt of lightning as thick as a water bucket crashed down from the vortex in the clouds, connecting Heaven and Earth like a divine stroke that split the world apart.

Everyone's hair stood on end. Lu Huaishuang, who was closest to the young man, turned even paler with fright.

For one simple reason: the bolt of lightning was hurtling straight toward the young man, its target unmistakable and its aim unerring.

Struck by Heaven's lightning!

How many wicked things had this young man done to make even Heaven and Earth refuse to tolerate him!

"Go!" The young man suddenly turned around, grabbed Lu Huaishuang by the collar, and casually tossed her away.

Before Lu Huaishuang could react to what had happened, her whole body flew out like an arrow loosed from a bowstring.

Then she saw the lightning strike the young man. His black hair instantly stood on end, and with him as the center, everything within a radius of a hundred zhang became an ocean of lightning!

Several cultivators who failed to dodge in time did not even have time to scream before they were turned into nothingness. At the same time, a scorched smell spread out, as if someone were roasting meat. The stench entering the nose made people want to vomit.

The lightning strike continued for a full ten breaths. During those ten breaths, the thunder and lightning in the sky poured down like moonlight, constantly flooding downward. The young man was struck until his whole body twitched as though he had caught chicken plague. It hurt just to look at him.

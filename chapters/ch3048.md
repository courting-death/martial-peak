## Chapter 3048 — Five-Zhang Dragon Body

Dragon Blood Flowers were also divided into grades: Lower-Level, Middle-Level, Upper-Level, and, at the very top, Supreme-Level. Even on Dragon Isle, Supreme-Level Dragon Blood Flowers were treasures that could only be encountered by chance. They could not be produced through age alone, as an element of natural fortune was also involved.

Of Fu Ling's three Dragon Blood Flowers, two were Lower-Level and one was Middle-Level.

By this grading system, the Dragon Blood Flower Li Jiao had possessed back then could probably only be considered an inferior specimen, not even comparable to a Lower-Level one.

Ji Ying had refined that Dragon Blood Flower into Dragon Blood Pills. After taking them and cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art, Yang Kai had developed a Half-Dragon body and gained considerable benefits.

Now that he had three higher-grade Dragon Blood Flowers, Yang Kai naturally could not let them go to waste. He intended to refine them into Dragon Blood Pills to stimulate his Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power and fuse it more thoroughly with himself.

The best way to deal with the Dragon Clan was through bloodline suppression. Otherwise, relying on strength alone, Yang Kai had no chance of winning.

Yang Kai had never refined Dragon Blood Pills before, but his many discussions with Ji Ying had made him extremely familiar with the process, and he knew the pill recipe by heart.

His attainments in the Alchemic Dao were profound, so there should not be any major problems.

Once everything was ready, Yang Kai began changing the seals in his hands, and raging flames suddenly sprang to life inside the Purple Void Cauldron.

Adding the herbs...

Inscribing the Spirit Array...

Controlling the heat...

The medicinal liquids were extracted from each ingredient in turn, and a faint fragrance gradually drifted from the Purple Void Cauldron, refreshing anyone who breathed it in.

As his strength had increased, alchemy had also become easier and easier for him. Although he had not practiced it in a long time, his movements remained smooth and fluid, without the slightest awkwardness. Back on Shuiyue Star, the Great Dao Pill Tone had allowed him to receive instruction in alchemy from the Heavenly Dao, so once he entered the proper state, refining pills was almost instinctive.

When Yang Kai placed a Lower-Level Dragon Blood Flower into the pill furnace, the medicinal fragrance grew even richer.

Yang Kai's movements were meticulous and his expression solemn, as though he were not refining pills but performing the most sacred task in the world.

Half a day later, Yang Kai performed the Nine Heavens Profound Pill Technique.

This pill-collecting technique had also been imparted to him by the Great Dao Pill Tone. It formed a system of its own, yet subtly accorded with the Laws of the Heavenly Dao, making it profoundly mysterious.

Countless afterimages flashed, and a series of crisp, melodious chimes rang out from inside the pill furnace.

The furnace flames went out, but Yang Kai's expression remained perfectly calm.

As he lifted the furnace lid, he struck the furnace with his palm. Two blood-red Spiritual Pills flew out, and Yang Kai, already prepared, swept them directly into a jade bottle.

After examining them carefully, Yang Kai looked somewhat dissatisfied.

Although he had successfully refined the Dragon Blood Pills, their quality did not seem very good. They were only slightly better than the ones Ji Ying had refined back then.

The Dragon Blood Flower he had used today was far superior to Ji Ying's, yet the resulting pills had failed to meet his expectations. Viewed that way, his refinement this time had actually been inferior to Ji Ying's.

The number of pills was also less than ideal, a mere two. As for Pill Patterns or Pill Clouds, there was no sign of either.

Had any other Imperial-Grade Pill Master achieved this, they would likely have been beside themselves with pride.

Although Imperial-Grade Pill Masters could refine Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills, they could not guarantee success every time, much less when refining something like a Dragon Blood Pill, which ranked among the most difficult of all Imperial Pills.

The reason was simple: the main ingredient was a Dragon Blood Flower. Aside from Ji Ying, no other Imperial-Grade Pill Master had ever been fortunate enough to refine Dragon Blood Pills.

This was Yang Kai's first time refining pills with a Dragon Blood Flower, yet he had already produced two. For any Imperial-Grade Pill Master, such a result would be an exceptional performance and naturally cause for celebration.

Yang Kai, however, could not bring himself to rejoice. These Dragon Blood Pills concerned the momentous affair one month from now, so naturally, he wanted their quality to be as high as possible and their number as great as possible.

After composing himself, Yang Kai did not immediately begin another refinement. Instead, he took out several Spiritual Pills and swallowed them to restore his strength.

An hour later, Yang Kai started the furnace again.

This time, he once again used a Lower-Level Dragon Blood Flower.

Another half a day passed. Amid a series of clear clinks and chimes, Yang Kai raised his brow and struck the pill furnace with his palm.

Even without examining it, Yang Kai knew from the sound that this batch contained more than two pills.

Sure enough, four blood-red Spiritual Pills shot out of the pill furnace and were once again swept into a jade bottle.

The second batch of Dragon Blood Pills was clearly better. Their quality was much higher and their color much richer than those of the first batch, while their medicinal efficacy should also be considerably stronger.

Alchemy truly was a craft in which practice made perfect.

This time, he did not rest again and instead immediately began refining the third batch of Dragon Blood Pills.

After another half a day, the third batch of Dragon Blood Pills successfully emerged from the furnace.

The number remained unchanged at four Spiritual Pills, but their quality had risen another level, and the improvement was extremely obvious.

Yang Kai's growing familiarity naturally played a part, but the more important reason was the sole Middle-Level Dragon Blood Flower. In alchemy, while the Pill Master's skill was certainly important, the ingredients were also crucial in determining the final quality of the pills. With the same Pill Master, better ingredients would naturally produce higher-quality Spiritual Pills.

Three batches had produced a total of ten Spiritual Pills.

This result could only be called outstanding. No other Imperial-Grade Pill Master would likely dare guarantee that they could accomplish the same, not even Ji Ying. Perhaps only Great Emperor Miaodan could ensure a one-hundred-percent success rate.

With all his preparations complete, Yang Kai began cultivating without delay.

This was the Small Xuan Realm, his own territory, so he had no need for restraint. No matter how great a disturbance he caused, the outside world could not possibly notice.

"Dragon Transformation!"

With a low shout, Yang Kai's body rapidly expanded amid a series of crackling sounds. Dragon Scales covered him, two fleshy bumps swelled slightly on his forehead, his enormous hands transformed into sharp Dragon Claws, and even his feet changed shape.

His four-zhang Half-Dragon body radiated Dragon Pressure. Even sitting cross-legged on the ground, he appeared exceptionally tall and mighty. His eyes were as wide as copper bells, emitting a terrifying, savage light.

The tiny jade bottle in his palm was as insignificant as a speck of dust, almost impossible to see.

Yang Kai opened his mouth and inhaled. As his chest rose, a Dragon Blood Pill flew into his mouth and was swallowed into his belly.

A burning pain immediately erupted in his lower abdomen. It was as though he had swallowed not a Dragon Blood Pill, but a stream of deadly molten lava.

Hot air poured from Yang Kai's nostrils. He closed his eyes and circulated the Dragon Transformation Art with all his strength.

The flesh throughout his body began to squirm, and every pore seemed to open wide.

The Dragon Blood Pill instantly dissolved in his abdomen, its medicinal efficacy flowing through his limbs and bones. The Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power that had lain hidden in his flesh for so many years seemed to be stimulated by some force and swiftly transformed into a golden light, flowing through his body along with the surging Emperor Yuan.

His meridians expanded like parched travelers, greedily devouring every trace of Dragon Clan Origin Power that passed, like plucking a feather from every passing goose.

Kacha kacha...

His bones seemed to split apart, and intense pain came from every part of his body. Combined with the fire burning in his lower abdomen, it made Yang Kai feel in that instant that death would be preferable.

He let out a muffled grunt and firmly guarded his mind, remaining unmoved by the pain as he ceaselessly circulated the Dragon Transformation Art.

Kacha kacha...

A dense succession of sounds rang out as though every bone in his body were being shattered and then reconstructed.

Every advancement in the Dragon Transformation Art was a process of shedding one's old body and being reborn. Only by enduring pain beyond an ordinary person's imagination could one gain a new lease on life.

Although persevering was difficult, Yang Kai was overjoyed.

This was because his Dragon Transformation had made no progress for a very long time, ever since Zhu Qing's Pure Yin Qi had helped increase his strength.

Or rather, its progress had been so slight as to be almost imperceptible, and his four-zhang Dragon body had barely changed.

After all, he himself was only human and had obtained the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power through a fortunate coincidence. Having coexisted with it for so many years, he now had to pay the corresponding price if he wished to fuse this Dragon Clan Origin into his own bloodline, and that process was bound to be extremely long.

Without external assistance, it would likely take hundreds or even thousands of years.

The medicinal efficacy of the Dragon Blood Pills, however, spared him that enormous expenditure of time, allowing him to fuse more thoroughly with the Dragon Clan Origin Power in a short period.

Crackling sounds continued to ring out, and the Dragon Scales covering Yang Kai's skin gradually began to change color. He could even feel an itching, tingling sensation on both sides of his head, as though something were about to break free from its shell.

Those tingling spots were precisely where his Dragon Horns were located.

The medicinal efficacy of the Dragon Blood Pill lasted a full hour.

Only after an hour did the continuous crackling gradually subside, and Yang Kai's grimacing expression slowly relaxed. He did not open his eyes until the medicinal efficacy had completely disappeared. His entire body was drenched in sweat as though he had just been pulled from the water, but his expression was filled with joy.

Although he had not examined himself carefully, he knew he had definitely undergone some changes.

After recovering briefly, he inhaled sharply once more, and a second Dragon Blood Pill shot from the jade bottle into his mouth.

...

Inside the Small Xuan Realm, Yang Kai did not pause for even a moment. He continuously consumed the Dragon Blood Pills he had refined and cultivated the Dragon Transformation Art with the aid of these Imperial Pills.

Although Dragon Blood Pills could improve a Dragon Clan bloodline to some extent, their greatest function was stimulation: stimulating the Dragon Clan's own Origin Power and making it purer and stronger.

Yang Kai's situation differed from that of ordinary members of the Dragon Clan, whose bloodlines and Origin Power gradually grew over time. His did not. His Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power was already fully formed and had no room to grow, but this also made it easier to stimulate and fuse with.

Yang Kai was like a man sitting atop a mountain of treasure but unable to make use of everything it held. He could only scrape away its riches little by little, gradually filling his own pockets.

Two days later, the last Dragon Blood Pill had been completely refined.

His eyes suddenly snapped open, brilliant light surging within them as unmatched Dragon Pressure erupted from his body and swept outward in every direction.

He let out a long howl, but the sound that emerged was like a dragon roar, its might shaking heaven and earth.

His mountain-like body slowly rose to its feet, and as he exhaled a breath of hot air, the entire world seemed to tremble.

Sweeping his Divine Sense over himself, Yang Kai smiled.

The ten Dragon Blood Pills had indeed produced some results.

His four-zhang Dragon body had already reached five zhang. The improvement was exceptionally significant. After all, when he had received the aid of Zhu Qing's Pure Yin Qi, he had only grown from three and a half zhang to four zhang, yet these ten Dragon Blood Pills had made him grow by another full zhang. Yang Kai was extremely satisfied with the result.

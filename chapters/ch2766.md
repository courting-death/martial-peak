## Chapter 2766 — Refining

At the Qingyang Shrine, every Elder had their own Spirit Peak, and this Purple Bamboo Peak belonged to Gao Xueting.

If Yang Kai were to be appointed as an Elder of the Qingyang Shrine, he would enjoy the same privilege. However, the appointment ceremony would be held in a few days, so for now, Yang Kai could only stay at Purple Bamboo Peak for a short while.

The peak was quiet, not even a single maid in sight. Gao Xueting arranged for Yang Kai to stay in a guest hall on one side of the mountain, instructed him not to wander about, and then turned to leave.

“Elder Gao!” Yang Kai suddenly called out to her.

Gao Xueting looked back.

“This disciple has one more matter to ask Elder Gao for help with,” Yang Kai said solemnly.

“What is it?”

“I want to enter the Divine Roaming Mirror!”

“Enter the Divine Roaming Mirror?” Gao Xueting frowned. “Why?”

The Divine Roaming Mirror was the greatest secret of the Qingyang Shrine. Back then, Yang Kai had been able to enter the Divine Roaming Mirror’s world only because he had brought back several Star Seals for the Shrine, rendering great merit. Although Yang Kai was now considered a Guest Elder of the Qingyang Shrine and qualified to enter the Divine Roaming Mirror, this was not a trivial matter, so Gao Xueting naturally had to ask for details.

Yang Kai pondered for a moment, then, without hiding anything, explained the agreement he had made with Tian Yan.

Gao Xueting exclaimed in surprise, “Last time in the Divine Roaming Mirror, you actually met that senior? And even made such an agreement with him?”

“Yes.” Yang Kai nodded. “At the time, it was just something I said in passing, but so many years have passed, it’s time to give him an answer.”

Gao Xueting was momentarily lost in thought. “I’ve long heard the Hall Master say that there’s an extraordinary expert in the Divine Roaming Mirror’s world. I’ve entered and exited the Divine Roaming Mirror many times, but have never had the fortune to meet him. I didn’t expect you to have such luck… Since there’s an agreement, it should indeed be fulfilled. But that Life Origin Pill is not easy to obtain.”

“The Life Origin Pill is already accounted for,” Yang Kai smiled faintly.

Gao Xueting’s expression brightened. “Truly?”

Yang Kai smiled and said, “I’ve gathered all the materials. Soon, the Imperial-Grade Pill Master of my Lingxiao Palace will be able to refine the Life Origin Pill.”

“How confident are you?” Gao Xueting asked nervously. Although it was her first time hearing of the Life Origin Pill, she knew it was definitely not easy to refine. Even if an Imperial-Grade Pill Master personally took action, there was no guarantee of a one hundred percent success rate.

Yang Kai said, “I forgot to mention, the Imperial-Grade Pill Master of my Lingxiao Palace is the fifth disciple under Great Emperor Miaodan.”

Gao Xueting was dumbfounded. After a while, she exclaimed, “You actually lured Great Emperor Miaodan’s personal disciple to your Sect as a Pill Master?”

That was far too bold! Originally, it was already shocking that Yang Kai’s Lingxiao Palace had an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, but to think that this Pill Master’s background was so great—being the fifth disciple under Great Emperor Miaodan. Even those at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm would have to show utmost respect.

What Gao Xueting couldn’t understand most was how Great Emperor Miaodan could possibly agree to such a thing.

However, if it was Great Emperor Miaodan’s disciple making the pill, there should be no chance of failure in refining the Life Origin Pill.

“Normally, if you wanted to enter the Divine Roaming Mirror, it wouldn’t be a difficult matter. But right now…” Gao Xueting suddenly frowned again.

“What about right now?” Yang Kai asked in surprise.

Gao Xueting said, “You’re not an outsider, so there’s no harm in telling you. At present, someone is using the Divine Roaming Mirror, and it must not be disturbed lightly. If you want to enter now, I’m afraid it will be difficult.”

Yang Kai looked astonished.

Among the people he had seen earlier at Ten Thousand Saints Peak, almost all the high-level members of the Qingyang Shrine were present. Who else could be using the Divine Roaming Mirror? And to think that it must not be disturbed—clearly, the identity of the person using it was extraordinary.

No matter how much Yang Kai thought about it, he couldn’t figure out who in the Qingyang Shrine could fit that description.

“I’ll speak with the Hall Master about this and see if there’s a way to satisfy both sides. If it really can’t be done… you’ll just have to wait a while. After all, so many years have passed since your agreement with that senior, and you don’t have a Life Origin Pill ready at hand.”

“Alright.” Yang Kai nodded. At this point, there was nothing else to be done.

He then asked about Xiao Baiyi and Murong Xiaoxiao. Upon learning that the two had been in seclusion cultivating ever since returning from the Shattered Star Sea and had yet to emerge, Yang Kai gave up on the idea of catching up with them.

The two of them were also Core Disciples of the Qingyang Shrine, pillars of its future. However, compared to Xia Sheng, they were a bit lacking. Xia Sheng had broken through his shackles and advanced to the Emperor Realm in the Shattered Star Sea, while Xiao Baiyi and Murong Xiaoxiao hadn’t had such fortune. Still, they had gained much in the Shattered Star Sea, and this time, they were determined not to leave seclusion until they broke through to the Emperor Realm.

After Gao Xueting left, Yang Kai sat cross-legged, waved his hand, and a large chest that looked as if it were cast from refined iron appeared before him.

Feihong Black Dragon Armor!

This Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor, which Yang Kai had won at the Ziyuan Commerce Guild’s auction, had never been properly examined by him until now.

He reached out and gently stroked the armor in the chest. In an instant, a furious roar suddenly erupted from within. Black mist surged out from the armor plates, gathering into a mass that transformed into the shape of an Ink-Scaled Jiao, coiling atop the chest, its eyes as large as copper gongs glaring at Yang Kai.

“Heh…” Yang Kai looked up at the Ink-Scaled Jiao’s phantom and grinned.

According to the Ziyuan Commerce Guild, this Feihong Black Dragon Armor was refined from the hide of an Ink-Scaled Jiao. Ink-Scaled Jiaos were extremely rare and renowned for their formidable defenses. During the refining process, its Inner Core was likely fused in as well, which was why the Divine Soul of the Ink-Scaled Jiao appeared at this moment.

In other words, this Feihong Black Dragon Armor seemed to possess an Artifact Spirit.

Secret Treasures refined from the bodies of powerful demonic beasts could perfectly merge the Divine Soul within their Inner Core.

The Ziyuan Commerce Guild hadn’t mentioned this point, likely fearing it would affect the armor’s value in the eyes of bidders. Although Secret Treasures with Artifact Spirits were much more valuable, it also depended on what kind of Artifact Spirit it was.

The Artifact Spirit of the Feihong Black Dragon Armor was the Divine Soul of the Ink-Scaled Jiao. Although an Ink-Scaled Jiao was a jiao, it possessed Dragon Clan blood. Such an Artifact Spirit was not something ordinary people could subdue.

Just as Yang Kai triggered the Feihong Black Dragon Armor, the Ink-Scaled Jiao’s Divine Soul Artifact Spirit suddenly appeared. If the Artifact Spirit wasn’t refined and made to recognize its master, it would be impossible to draw out the armor’s true power.

The massive Ink-Scaled Jiao Artifact Spirit was almost tangible, and in its eyes, Yang Kai’s reflection appeared as insignificant as an ant.

But in the next instant, a deep yet piercing dragon roar suddenly erupted from within that insignificant “ant.” Brilliant golden light burst forth as the Origin Power of the Golden Sacred Dragon unfurled, vast and majestic. A colossal golden dragon, far more imposing than the Ink-Scaled Jiao Artifact Spirit, appeared behind Yang Kai’s head, its presence dominating the space.

The Ink-Scaled Jiao Artifact Spirit, which should have been a dead thing without consciousness, actually revealed a trace of fear. Its raised head drooped lower.

As the Golden Sacred Dragon’s aura grew stronger, the Ink-Scaled Jiao involuntarily shrank back into the chest.

The bloodline suppression of the Dragon Clan was a strange and wondrous phenomenon, unimaginable to any other race.

Just like how Li Jiao, an Emperor at the Third Stage, could not muster the slightest resistance when facing Zhu Qing. Any being with Dragon Clan blood, while gaining great power, also had to bear this indelible shackle.

If it were any other Emperor at the Third Stage, Zhu Qing might not win so easily, but if she faced Li Jiao, she could take his life with ease.

“Scared now?” Yang Kai sneered triumphantly, staring at the Ink-Scaled Jiao Artifact Spirit with a sinister look. “If you’re afraid, get back in there and serve me. Otherwise, I’ll destroy your Divine Soul!”

Whether the Ink-Scaled Jiao Artifact Spirit understood or not, as soon as Yang Kai finished speaking, it really did shrink back, the dense black mist dissipating and merging once more into the armor.

Meanwhile, atop Ten Thousand Saints Peak, Wen Zishan led the high-level members of the Qingyang Shrine, all waiting solemnly.

Two streaks of light flew over at high speed, landing before the crowd and revealing the figures of Xiao Yuyang and his son.

“Xiao Chen greets Hall Master Wen and all the seniors!” Xiao Chen cupped his fists in salute. Though he was proud and arrogant among his peers, he had been well taught and would not lose his manners on such occasions.

Wen Zishan smiled and nodded. “Young Master Xiao is thoughtful.” He then cupped his fists toward Xiao Yuyang. “Brother Xiao, it’s been a long time.”

“Hall Master Wen!” Xiao Yuyang replied solemnly.

Although he was from the Star God Palace and also an Emperor at the Third Stage, facing Wen Zishan, the master of the Shrine, he dared not be careless in the slightest.

Before Xiao Yuyang could speak again, Wen Zishan interjected, “Brother Xiao, you’ve come at just the right time. You’ve arrived for a joyous occasion at my Shrine. You must stay as my guest for a few days.”

“A joyous occasion?” Xiao Yuyang frowned. “What occasion?”

Wen Zishan said, “It’s like this. Our Shrine will be appointing a new Elder in five days. If you don’t mind, Brother Xiao, please stay and have a cup of celebratory wine.”

Upon hearing this, Xiao Yuyang’s eyes narrowed. He said lightly, “Hall Master Wen, as far as I know, among your Sect’s Core Disciples, Xia Sheng has long since advanced to the Emperor Realm and already holds the position of Elder. The others are all in seclusion and won’t be coming out any time soon. The person you’re appointing… could it be the one I’m thinking of?”

Wen Zishan asked in confusion, “Oh? Who does Brother Xiao have in mind?”

Xiao Yuyang enunciated each word: “Yang… Kai!”

Wen Zishan was greatly surprised. “Brother Xiao, your insight is truly remarkable! You even guessed this—impressive, impressive!”

Xiao Yuyang shouted angrily, “Hall Master Wen!”

Seeing him feigning ignorance, Xiao Yuyang grew even more furious.

Wen Zishan chuckled and said, “Brother Xiao, could it be that you already guessed this and came to offer your congratulations in advance? How thoughtful.”

The veins on Xiao Yuyang’s forehead bulged as he said in a deep voice, “Brother Wen, we’ve known each other for many years. Let me give you a word of advice—don’t bring disaster upon yourself.”

Wen Zishan narrowed his eyes. “Brother Xiao, I don’t understand what you mean. Our Shrine is merely appointing an Elder. How could that bring disaster?”

Xiao Yuyang stared at Wen Zishan and said, “That brat killed Tan Junhao and Wu Ming. Don’t tell me you didn’t know about this, Hall Master Wen.”

Wen Zishan was greatly shocked. “Impossible! Tan Junhao was an Emperor at the Third Stage, and Wu Ming was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. Yang Kai is only at the First Stage. How could he possibly kill them? That’s absolutely impossible. Could there be some mistake?”

Xiao Yuyang sneered, “More than twenty Emperor Realm experts from the major Sects of the Southern Territory all pointed to him. Could this be false?”

## Chapter 3075 — Masterful Planning

Wu Kuang did not have three heads and six arms. If they had not already known his identity and had simply encountered him by chance, no one would have associated him with the title of Heaven Devourer Great Emperor.

Not only was there nothing particularly remarkable about him, but even the figure standing before the Dragon Tomb at this moment seemed neither illusory nor real, like a phantom yet seemingly true.

Zhu Yan came to a halt, his expression turning ugly.

"This is bad—a Void Soul Wandering Shadow!" Mo Huang growled, his expression solemn.

The figure ahead was indeed Wu Kuang, but it was not his true body. It was an illusory projection created by one of Wu Kuang's Secret Arts, one that had replicated his aura and appearance almost perfectly.

"We've been tricked!" Fu Zhun gritted her teeth.

Both the Secret Art she had used to track Wu Kuang and the guidance of Mo Huang's little beast had pointed toward the Dragon Tomb. Coupled with Yang Kai's extremely plausible deduction, everyone had assumed that Wu Kuang's goal in coming to Dragon Isle was the Dragon Tomb and the many Dragon Clan Origin Powers within it.

Only after truly arriving here did they discover that everything had been wrong.

With nothing more than a Void Soul Wandering Shadow, Wu Kuang had successfully drawn everyone's attention to this place, while his true body was carrying out some unknown sinister scheme elsewhere.

This was truly luring the tiger away from the mountain!

In this world, only another Great Emperor could deceive a Great Emperor. Although Wu Kuang had yet to recover his strength, he had hidden in the shadows and toyed with everyone in the palm of his hand with just a few casual moves.

He was daring, but supremely confident!

While everyone was still unsettled, Wu Kuang's phantom smiled faintly. "Everyone, I trust you've been well!"

No one paid him any attention. It was only a Void Soul Wandering Shadow created by a Secret Art, so speaking with it would serve no purpose. They might even inadvertently fall into one of Wu Kuang's schemes.

Since the Dragon Tomb was not Wu Kuang's target, what exactly did he intend to do?

Yang Kai and Li Wuyi simultaneously turned toward a certain direction, then exchanged a glance and saw the surprise in each other's eyes. "What intense fluctuations of Space Force!"

Of everyone present, only the two of them were proficient in the Laws of Space, so when those energy fluctuations emerged, they were the first to sense them.

Immediately afterward, Zhu Yan, Fu Zhun, and even Mo Huang noticed them as well.

Fu Zhun stamped her foot and transformed into a streak of white light that shot in that direction. Zhu Yan followed close behind, moving as swiftly as a meteor.

Mo Huang's expression turned livid as he growled, "It's the Dragon Temple!"

At these words, realization dawned on everyone.

Wu Kuang's true target was actually the Dragon Temple. He had used the Void Soul Wandering Shadow to draw the attention of everyone present while his true body had long since secretly infiltrated the Dragon Temple, though no one knew what he intended to do there.

"Not good—he wants to escape!" Mo Huang growled. Seeming to have guessed something, he hurried toward the Dragon Temple. For the first time, anxiety appeared on his face, making it obvious that this was a grave matter.

As everyone raced onward, a dazzling burst of light suddenly erupted in the distance. Immediately afterward, a pillar of light soared into the sky, piercing through the clouds and shooting into the firmament as though it had punched a hole through the Heavens.

Meanwhile, the fluctuations of Space Force grew increasingly distinct and intense.

Yang Kai held Zhu Qing's hand and flew beside her, his expression equally solemn.

As someone proficient in the Laws of Space, he knew exactly what such fluctuations meant. What he could not understand was how Wu Kuang had done it. Although Wu Kuang was the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor, Yang Kai had never heard of him possessing any attainments in Space Force.

Sighing inwardly, he realized that every expert on Dragon Isle had been led around in circles by Wu Kuang this time. Even if they rushed over now, it would probably be useless.

Wu Kuang had gone to such great lengths to stage all of this on Dragon Isle, so he must have made flawless preparations and plans.

If Wu Kuang escaped, Yang Kai and Mo Huang would have to face the Dragon Clan's towering rage. Mo Huang was the Beast Great Emperor and probably did not fear Dragon Isle, but Yang Kai was different. Before a colossus like Dragon Isle, he could not help feeling apprehensive.

After pondering for a moment, Yang Kai called Li Jiao, Liu Yan, and the Incarnation over. While no one was paying attention, he put all three of them into the Small Xuan Realm.

He needed to prepare for his escape as soon as possible.

The pillar of light soaring from the Dragon Temple grew brighter and brighter until it nearly illuminated all of Dragon Isle. Only after a short while did its radiance begin to weaken, then slowly recede.

By the time the group hurried to the Dragon Temple, the light had already faded away. Only an exceptionally distinct fluctuation of Space Force continued to spread from deep within the Dragon Temple.

The Dragon Temple was lofty and majestic, its aura ancient and everlasting. Having weathered hundreds of thousands of years, it was one of the most important foundations of the entire Dragon Clan.

Only those who possessed the Dragon Clan's bloodline were qualified to set foot in the Dragon Temple, and that bloodline had to be exceedingly pure. A Dragon Descendant like Li Jiao was not even qualified to approach it. If he insisted on trespassing, the Dragon Pressure within the Dragon Temple would immediately crush him into dust.

Yang Kai arrived last. Li Wuyi and Jiu Feng were waiting outside the Dragon Temple, but everyone else had disappeared, clearly having entered.

When Li Wuyi saw Yang Kai, he smiled faintly at him, while Jiu Feng let out a surprised sound.

She had noticed that Liu Yan was gone, along with everyone else associated with Yang Kai. She immediately gave him a meaningful glance but did not expose what he had done.

"I'll go inside and take a look," Yang Kai said before walking in with Zhu Qing.

He was not merely joining the excitement; he only wanted to know Duan Hongchen's current condition.

The Dragon Temple was deep and secluded, with enormous Dragon pillars towering all around. From the outside, the entire temple did not look particularly remarkable, but inside was a completely different world.

Powerful waves of Dragon Pressure surged through the temple, creating an extremely chaotic sensation. The moment Zhu Qing stepped inside, she frowned.

She had entered the Dragon Temple before, but it had never been anything like this. From the chaotic Dragon Pressure alone, she could infer that something had gone wrong with the Dragon Temple.

They walked inward for a long time, with only the thud of footsteps echoing around them.

A short while later, a glimmer of light entered their view. Following it, they soon saw the many experts gathered on Dragon Isle today.

The great battle they had imagined was nowhere to be seen. Everyone stood gathered around the light, completely motionless.

Observing their expressions, Yang Kai's heart sank as a bad feeling arose within him.

When he moved closer and looked, his eyes widened with astonishment.

The source of the light was actually a Void Passage. At its far end, countless stars twinkled brilliantly, as though someone had torn a piece from the Starry Sky at night, shrunk it, and placed it here.

Zhu Yan’s face was ashen, while Fu Zhun looked no better. Her beautiful face was so dark that it seemed a storm could break from it at any moment.

Mo Huang kept shaking his head and sighed. "We're too late."

Now that Wu Kuang had escaped Dragon Isle today, there was no telling how many lives would be plunged into misery or how many Star Domains would be destroyed in the future. The Heaven Devourer Great Emperor had done such things before. The world was now boundlessly vast, and no one knew where Wu Kuang had escaped to, so tracking him down would probably be impossible.

Yang Kai glanced around, but ultimately could not suppress his curiosity. Moving closer to Mo Huang, he asked in a low voice, "Sir, did Wu Kuang escape?"

Mo Huang nodded lightly.

Yang Kai asked, "But how did he shatter space? And this Void Passage feels... anything but ordinary."

Even the cross-domain Space Arrays he had arranged did not produce such intense fluctuations of Space Force when activated. He vaguely sensed that this Void Passage led somewhere extremely distant.

Mo Huang did not answer, but Fu Xuan said, "He borrowed the power of the Dragon Temple."

Yang Kai raised his brow. "He borrowed the power of the Dragon Temple? He actually has that ability?"

The Dragon Temple was a foundation of the Dragon Clan, so how could an outsider casually borrow its power? Moreover, from the moment Yang Kai stepped inside, he sensed several extremely powerful auras hidden within the Dragon Temple, many of which were no weaker than the three Great Emperors present.

Yang Kai guessed that they were the auras of the Dragon Clan's Ancestral Spirits.

Fu Xuan patiently explained, "He killed Fu Chi and seized his Dragon Clan Origin so that he could enter the Dragon Temple without obstruction. As for how he used the Dragon Temple's power to shatter space after entering, I am not too sure." She turned to Mo Huang and softly asked, “Do you know how he did it?”

She was essentially asking on Yang Kai's behalf, though she was also somewhat curious herself.

Mo Huang said, "Wu Kuang is older than any of us. It is said—and this is only hearsay—that when Wu Kuang first became a Great Emperor, he had a good relationship with several previous generations of Dragon Clan Elders."

If someone else had said this, it would have possessed no credibility and could basically be dismissed as groundless rumor. Since it came from Mo Huang, however, it meant there was more to it than mere hearsay.

Wu Kuang had most likely truly shared a good relationship with several previous generations of Dragon Clan Elders.

If that were the case, it was not strange that he knew some Dragon Clan Secret Arts or profound Secret Techniques. He had used Fu Chi's Dragon Clan Origin to communicate with the Dragon Temple, then borrowed the Dragon Temple's power to shatter the Void. This was probably his entire plan.

Yet from one step to the next, not a single mistake had occurred. Everything had gone smoothly, with a few scares but no danger.

Despite his extremely poor impression of Wu Kuang, even Yang Kai had to praise his masterful planning. The wisdom and methods of a Great Emperor were indeed extraordinary.

"The Void Passage still exists, so why aren't we chasing him through it?" Yang Kai asked again.

"Chase him?" Mo Huang glanced at him with a sneer.

Yang Kai frowned. "What's wrong with that?"

"Then do you know where this Void Passage leads?"

"Please enlighten me, Sir," Yang Kai humbly asked.

Although he could guess that the Void Passage led somewhere extremely distant, he truly could not determine the exact location.

"A Lower Plane Star Domain!" Mo Huang's words were as shocking as a thunderclap.

Yang Kai froze for a moment before reacting and exclaiming, "A Lower Plane Star Domain?"

He had shouted rather loudly, causing everyone to turn toward him. Fu Zhun's gaze was especially icy.

Yang Kai rubbed his nose, looking slightly embarrassed, but he quickly became anxious. "How could it lead to a Lower Plane Star Domain? Isn't there a force called the Star Court guarding the entrances between the Star Realm and the Lower Plane Star Domains? How could an entrance be opened here?"

Wu Kuang had actually escaped to a Lower Plane Star Domain, so how could Yang Kai not be anxious? Although there were many Lower Plane Star Domains, who knew whether Wu Kuang would enter Hengluo Star Domain? If, by some unfortunate coincidence, Wu Kuang entered Hengluo Star Domain, the entire Star Domain would probably be finished.

That was where Yang Kai's roots lay.

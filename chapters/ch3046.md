## Chapter 3046 — Brother-in-Law, You Must Be Gentle

Fu Ling twirled a lock of her hair around her finger and said casually, "This Young Lady is a Purple Dragon, born with the ability to perform myriad transformations. What's so remarkable about turning into a mere rock?"

"Myriad transformations!" Yang Kai clicked his tongue in wonder. "That's quite the Divine Ability!"

Had he not inadvertently circulated his Emperor Yuan earlier and detected something unusual, she probably would have fooled him completely.

Even after noticing the anomaly, Yang Kai had not been entirely certain, which was why he pretended to leave while secretly hiding nearby to observe.

He had not expected to actually catch someone spying on them, much less Fu Ling.

Because of Fu Chi, he had no favorable impression of the entire Fu branch of the Dragon Clan. Fu Ling's promiscuity in particular disgusted him. Now that she had overheard his secret discussion with Zhu Lie, she would become a serious problem unless he dealt with her properly.

Fu Ling finally sensed that something was amiss. Her beautiful brows knitted together as she looked around warily.

"If you're waiting for reinforcements, I'm afraid you're going to be disappointed."

Fu Ling's expression changed, and she shouted in a low voice, "This isn't an Illusion Array! It's a Small World?"

When the scenery suddenly changed earlier, taking her from a Spirit Island in the middle of the sea to this inexplicable place, she had assumed Yang Kai had trapped her in some kind of Illusion Array. Only now did she belatedly realize that this was no Illusion Array at all.

This was a Small World!

The moment she understood this, Fu Ling's composure crumbled. At last, she understood why none of her clansmen had arrived after all this time. A Small World was an independent space. Even though she had used a Secret Technique to send out a distress signal, how could it possibly be transmitted outside?

With no clansmen coming to her aid, she would have to face Yang Kai's attacks alone.

"Damn it!" Fu Ling cried out in alarm and fury, then turned and fled. Her figure transformed back into a streak of purple light, her earlier calm nowhere to be seen.

She had no idea what sort of strange place this Small World was or how Yang Kai had brought her here, but she knew the odds were now heavily against her, so she had no choice but to flee.

She had barely flown any distance when Fu Ling let out a startled cry. An unimaginable crushing force suddenly closed in from all directions and pinned her in place. It was as though she had crashed into an invisible wall and become stuck there, unable to break free no matter how she struggled.

A force came from ahead and pushed her body backward against her will, drawing her closer and closer to Yang Kai.

She shrieked in alarm. In all her many years of life, this was the first time she had encountered anything so bizarre.

World Force! This was the power of the Small World itself.

Fu Ling's beautiful eyes overflowed with terror and shock. The fact that Yang Kai could control this Small World filled her with despair; she knew she had absolutely no way to contend with him.

In just three breaths, all her efforts came to nothing, and she found herself back in front of Yang Kai.

Gazing up at his four-zhang Dragon body, Fu Ling trembled in terror and shouted, "You can't kill me!"

Yang Kai remained unmoved. His enormous palm slapped downward, his expression cold. He intended to vent all the fury he had bottled up throughout the night on Fu Ling.

Fu Ling cried out again, "I'm Zhu Lie's woman!"

The Dragon palm stopped just three cun above Fu Ling's head. The fierce wind it stirred up made her clothes flap loudly. Having narrowly escaped death, Fu Ling collapsed onto the ground, her face deathly pale.

Yang Kai looked down at her and slowly raised a finger. His sharp Dragon Claw touched Fu Ling's forehead, and a bead of crimson blood immediately welled up. "Say that again!"

"I'm not lying to you. I really am Zhu Lie's woman." Fu Ling's delicate body trembled violently. She had never before experienced the feeling of death bearing down on her, and it nearly broke her. The Dragon Clan's pride had long since been cast beyond the highest heavens. She raised her face with a fawning smile, but her fear made it look even uglier than crying.

“You certainly say that with confidence.” Yang Kai chuckled, but because he was in his Half-Dragon form, even his laughter sounded somewhat frightening.

Fu Ling hurriedly said, "It's true. This was the Elders' decision. Of course... it was also at my request."

Yang Kai frowned. She did not seem to be lying. "What do you mean?"

"Zhu Qing was betrothed to Fu Chi, so as compensation, one of us from the Fu branch will marry into the Zhu branch. I am the one they chose, and Zhu Lie is the man I am to marry."

The corner of Yang Kai's mouth twitched.

"We're all one family..." Fu Ling gazed pitifully up at Yang Kai with watery eyes, as though looking at the person she admired most, and said sweetly, "Brother-in-Law, I'm telling the truth."

She addressed him this way without the slightest reluctance, her expression perfectly natural, as though she and Yang Kai truly had become family.

Yang Kai asked with a strange expression, "Zhu Lie... agreed to this?"

He found the thought amusing. That brat Zhu Lie had truly appalling taste, didn't he? Yang Kai did not believe Zhu Lie was unaware of what kind of woman Fu Ling was. He even dared pick up a worn-out shoe like her? Was he not afraid she would make a fool of him in the future?

Fu Ling forced a smile. "He has no choice but to agree. In the Elders' eyes, continuing the Dragon Clan's bloodline is the most important matter of all."

"How miserable." Yang Kai sighed, beginning to sympathize with Zhu Lie's plight. His little brother-in-law had truly suffered a calamity through no fault of his own. If Yang Kai were in his position, he would have died of rage.

"Brother-in-Law." The more Fu Ling called him that, the more naturally it came. "I truly never intended to reveal your secret. I only wanted to use what I heard to threaten Zhu Lie and make him submit obediently. As for Fu Chi's affairs, I can't even be bothered with them. Besides, Zhu Qing possesses a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline. Only someone like you, Brother-in-Law, is worthy of her. What does Fu Chi amount to? He actually dares to steal your woman? He simply doesn't know the immensity of Heaven and Earth."

She had probably never flattered anyone before, because every word sounded insincere and false. When Yang Kai remained silent, Fu Ling grew extremely uneasy, but for the sake of survival, she could only summon her courage, reach out, and grab his ankle. Shaking it coquettishly, she pleaded, "Believe me, Brother-in-Law. I'm not lying to you."

"What difference does it make whether I believe you?" Yang Kai looked down at her, his expression indifferent.

Fu Ling's face went pale again. Pressing her red lips together, she asked, "Do you really intend to kill me?"

Before Yang Kai could answer, she continued, "Brother-in-Law, we're all family. If you truly want to kill me, I have nothing to say. I only have myself to blame for hearing what I shouldn't have heard and seeing what I shouldn't have seen. But you need to think this through. If I die, the whole of Dragon Isle will certainly be alarmed. When the Great Elder, Second Elder, and the others investigate, they will definitely trace it back to you. Then you can forget about rescuing Zhu Qing."

Hearing this, Yang Kai frowned.

Of everything Fu Ling had said, this last point finally struck the heart of the matter.

In truth, he was also hesitating over whether to kill Fu Ling to silence her. If he truly wanted to kill her inside the Small Xuan Realm, it would take little effort. He was the absolute ruler here, to say nothing of the bloodline suppression he exerted over her.

But what would happen after he killed her?

The Dragon Clan was already small in number, and the death of any one of its members would shake Dragon Isle. If Fu Ling died, Yang Kai would find it difficult to keep his whereabouts hidden.

Hesitation appeared on his face. Seeing this, Fu Ling rejoiced inwardly, knowing that Yang Kai indeed had misgivings. She had made the right move.

Her expression grew even more charming as she said softly, "As long as Brother-in-Law doesn't kill me, Ling'er will obey your every command."

She knelt there with the front of her robes hanging wide open, exposing the snow-white cleavage between her breasts.

Her words carried a deeper meaning, and Yang Kai's gaze was immediately drawn to those snow-white curves.

Fu Ling smiled faintly and tugged at her clothes, revealing even more. She secretly felt smug. He was merely a human, while she was a noble member of the Dragon Clan. With even a slight display of her charms, how could he possibly resist?

Moreover, this human controlled the Ancestral Dragon Origin.

Even Zhu Qing, with her Eighth-Tier Dragon Bloodline, had received enormous benefits from him and advanced to the Ninth Tier.

Fu Ling possessed only a Sixth-Tier bloodline. If she could do as Zhu Qing had and receive the same benefits, wouldn't her gains be even greater? Deep down, not only did she not mind Yang Kai's aggressive gaze, she was delighted by it, even eager for more. Restless desire stirred within her, her body grew hot, and she pressed her legs tightly together.

With a series of crackling sounds, Yang Kai suddenly returned to his human form. He reached out, seized Fu Ling, and wrapped one hand around her slender neck.

"Brother-in-Law..." Fu Ling's cheeks flushed, making her look alluringly radiant. She struck a pose and feigned a shy expression.

"Open your Sea of Consciousness!" Yang Kai barked.

Fu Ling's coyness instantly vanished. She stared at Yang Kai in shock, suddenly realizing what he intended to do, and her face went deathly pale.

"I won't say the same thing twice!" Yang Kai's voice was icy. "If you want to die, I'll grant your wish right now."

Fu Ling struggled and hesitated for a moment, but ultimately resigned herself to her fate. With a bitter smile, she said, "Brother-in-Law, you must be gentle. This is my first time."

The instant she opened her Sea of Consciousness, she sensed an extraordinarily pure surge of Divine Soul energy pouring in.

A wave of discomfort swept through her mind. This was the first time anyone had invaded her Sea of Consciousness, and she had even voluntarily abandoned all resistance. For a moment, Fu Ling wished she were dead. Had she known this would happen, she would never have waded into these muddy waters today. Deep down, she had already cursed Zhu Lie to hell and back.

Manifesting her Spirit Body, Fu Ling spotted Yang Kai immediately. He too had manifested his Spirit Body and now stood above the surging Sea of Consciousness with a solemn expression, his hands continuously forming seals.

Fu Ling knew what he was doing, but she was powerless to stop him. She could only stand to one side and watch, feeling unbearably aggrieved.

A short while later, a seal blossomed from Yang Kai's hands and blasted into Fu Ling's Sea of Consciousness.

In an instant, the Sea of Consciousness began churning violently. Fu Ling moaned, and her Spirit Body fluctuated and distorted as though it might collapse at any moment.

Only after quite some time did she gradually recover. When she looked up, Yang Kai was already gone. He had evidently withdrawn from her Sea of Consciousness at some point without her noticing.

Fu Ling examined the Soul Brand and felt a bitter taste in her mouth. She had wondered whether she might have a chance to dissolve it, but after seeing how complex and robust the brand was, she immediately abandoned the idea.

When her Divine Soul returned to her body, Fu Ling looked at Yang Kai uneasily and asked softly, "Brother-in-Law, what are your orders?"

Now that a Soul Brand had been planted in her, her life and death rested on a single thought from Yang Kai. She had no choice but to submit. Her former brazenness was gone, replaced by a cautious demeanor.

"Behave yourself. I'll leave in one month, and when I do, you'll regain your freedom."

"Understood." Fu Ling lowered her eyes submissively.

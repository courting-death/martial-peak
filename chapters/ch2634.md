## Chapter 2634 — Offering a Plan

“I've been busy dealing with the external threat these past few days and haven't had a chance to visit you. Are you still getting used to living in the valley?” Bing Yun asked.

“I'm used to it, very much so…” Yang Kai nodded repeatedly and said, “Ice Heart Valley is a place of outstanding people and beautiful scenery. How could I not be comfortable here?”

“That's good.” Bing Yun smiled slightly, then suddenly grew solemn. “But you won't be able to stay here for many more days. In a couple of days, I'll personally send you away.”

Yang Kai was taken aback and said, “Senior, are you planning to fight Wenqing Sect to the death?”

Bing Yun nodded. “They've already come to our doorstep. My Ice Heart Valley can only accept the challenge.”

“Is there any chance of victory?” Yang Kai asked in a deep voice.

Bing Yun shook her head. “None. But it's not so easy for them to destroy my Ice Heart Valley either. Yang Kai, since we're on this topic, I have a favor to ask.”

Yang Kai replied solemnly, “Senior, please speak freely.”

Bing Yun let out a long sigh. “Ice Heart Valley was founded by my own hand. Though it now stands on the brink of destruction, I still hope to leave behind some roots. When the time comes for me to send you away, I want you to take a few disciples with you—lead them out of the Northern Territory. No matter where you settle them, make sure they understand: they must not throw themselves against the impossible, and until their wings have fully grown, they are not to return to the Northern Territory. Even if they long for vengeance, they must only act when they are absolutely certain of success. If they have no such resolve… then let them live out their days in peace.”

Yang Kai felt a heaviness in his heart, knowing Bing Yun was making arrangements for the aftermath. It seemed she was determined to live and die with Ice Heart Valley.

“At that time, Zi Yu will lead the disciples. Yang Kai, you and Zi Yu are acquainted. I'll entrust them to you.” Bing Yun looked at Yang Kai with a serious expression.

Yang Kai frowned. “Senior, the situation… hasn't become that dire yet, has it?”

Bing Yun shook her head. “Seven thousand against a hundred thousand. My Ice Heart Valley has no chance of victory.”

Standing behind Bing Yun, Ji Yao suddenly spoke. “Senior Brother Yang, can you ask those beings from the Eastern Territory for help? If you could, we wouldn't need many—just two or three would suffice…”

Yang Kai shook his head at her words. “Distant water can't put out a nearby fire!”

Yang Kai naturally knew exactly who Ji Yao was referring to.

It was nothing more than those great beings in the Wild Ancient Land. There was no need for Luan Feng and the others to appear; just sending a few Yao Kings would be enough to intimidate the enemy. But those fellows were all in the Ancient Land of the Eastern Territory, far from here.

If Yang Kai went to the Wild Ancient Land to bring back reinforcements, Ice Heart Valley would likely have already fallen by the time he returned.

Moreover, they were yao and Divine Spirits. If they rashly intervened in human Sect affairs, they might anger the Great Emperors. Last time, when Luan Feng accompanied Yang Kai to Netherworld Sect, she didn't dare act lightly either. It was only when the Family Head provoked her that she killed Zhou Yong.

Though the beings of the Wild Ancient Land were powerful, once they left their domain, there were still many taboos that bound their actions.

Ji Yao clearly understood this as well. She asked only out of a sliver of hope. Hearing Yang Kai's answer, she couldn't help but sigh.

Yang Kai pondered, “Senior, I've been thinking these past two days. The reason Ice Heart Valley is in such danger is simply because they've invited a formation-breaking master.”

Bing Yun nodded. “That's right. That Nanmen Dajun does have some skill. Our Ice Heart Valley's Grand Guardian Array has already started to show signs of loosening. If he gets another ten days or half a month, he might really be able to break the formation.”

Yang Kai grinned. “But what if he doesn't get those ten days or half a month?”

Bing Yun was startled. “What do you mean by that?”

Yang Kai said, “Here's what I'm thinking. Didn't Wenqing Sect just issue a recruitment order? Maybe I can pretend to defect to Wenqing Sect, infiltrate them, and then look for a chance to kill Nanmen Dajun.”

“How could that work!” Bing Yun exclaimed. “I know you're skilled in the Power of Space, but there are so many enemies outside Ice Heart Valley now—just the Emperors alone are countless. If you make a single mistake, you might not even be able to escape. Besides, don't forget, the upper echelons of Wenqing Sect definitely know what you look like. If you rashly try to join them, you'll just be walking into a trap. No, no, I can't let you take such a risk for Ice Heart Valley.”

Yang Kai smiled slightly. “Senior, you don't need to worry about my appearance. As long as I don't run into Feng Xuan or Yao Zhuo, I believe no one else will recognize me.”

Bing Yun frowned. “Have you cultivated some kind of body transformation secret art?”

Yang Kai shook his head. “No.”

Ji Yao suddenly said, “Senior Brother means to ask Junior Nephew Tianhe for help?”

Yang Kai looked at her with a smile. “Exactly.”

Ji Yao thought for a moment, then nodded. “If that's your plan, Senior Brother, it could work.”

Bing Yun looked confused. “Tianhe? The disciple who returned with you a few days ago? She only has the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm cultivation. Even if she helps, what can she do?”

Ji Yao smiled. “Master, although Junior Nephew Tianhe's cultivation is lacking, she does have a special skill.”

“What skill?”

Ji Yao replied, “Her disguise technique is extremely advanced. Last time in Taiping City, she stood right in front of me and I didn't notice a thing. If Senior Brother Yang hadn't reminded me, I would have been fooled by her.”

Bing Yun was surprised. “She has such an ability?”

Even Ji Yao hadn’t noticed any flaws—let alone other Second Stage Emperors, who would likely be just as oblivious. Such mastery of disguise was truly remarkable.

Yang Kai smiled. “With Junior Nephew Tianhe's help, even if I go to Wenqing Sect, it's unlikely I'll be recognized. As long as I can get inside and find a chance to kill or abduct Nanmen Dajun, no one will be able to break the formation.”

Bing Yun's eyes lit up at this, but she quickly shook her head and smiled bitterly. “It still won't work. Even if you kill Nanmen Dajun, the result will be the same.”

“Why?” This time, both Yang Kai and Ji Yao were stunned.

Bing Yun sighed. “Although our Ice Heart Valley's Grand Guardian Array is formidable, it consumes too much energy when activated. Even without Nanmen Dajun, we can't hold out for more than two months. After that, the array will collapse on its own, and the situation won't change.”

Yang Kai was relieved. “So that's the reason. Senior, don't worry. I have some Source Crystals on hand—enough for Ice Heart Valley to use for a long time. And… two months is enough for me to make a trip to the Eastern Territory to find help.”

As long as he could buy that much time, he could go to the Eastern Territory and bring back a few Yao Kings.

By then, even if Wenqing Sect had a hundred thousand, or even two or three hundred thousand, they would still be nothing but a disorderly mob.

“Who are you going to find in the Eastern Territory?” Bing Yun asked curiously.

“Junior Sister Yao knows.” Yang Kai smiled faintly.

Ji Yao's eyes turned, and she quietly sent Bing Yun a message.

Bing Yun's expression changed dramatically after hearing it. “Yang Kai, you can actually ask them for help?”

“It's just a small matter. They should be willing to help.” Yang Kai replied confidently. Without waiting for Bing Yun to object, he said, “Then it's settled. There's no time to lose—I'll go find Junior Nephew Tianhe now!”

The mess at Ice Heart Valley was, after all, caused by him, so he couldn't just stand by. These past few days, he'd been thinking about how to help resolve this disaster, and now he finally had a plan.

“Wait, Senior Brother Yang, count me in on this.” Ji Yao called out hurriedly.

“You?” Yang Kai turned in surprise, then waved his hand. “No, no, I can handle this alone. One more person will just make things more complicated.”

Ji Yao said, “This is a matter for my Ice Heart Valley. How can I let someone else take all the risk? I must go with you.”

After saying this, before Yang Kai could refuse again, she suddenly sent a message: “Senior Brother Yang, if you say 'no' one more time, I'll tell Master you took liberties with me.”

Yang Kai's face turned black, and cold sweat broke out instantly. The words on his lips were swallowed, and he quickly changed his tune. “With Junior Sister Yao coming along, that's naturally for the best, haha!”

Bing Yun could tell he was being insincere, but she didn't know what Ji Yao had said to make him change his attitude so drastically.

But things had come to this point, and she couldn't stop them any longer. As Yang Kai said, if everything went smoothly, Ice Heart Valley could avoid destruction. She had to consider the seven thousand disciples of the Sect.

“Since you two insist, then call Tianhe over. Let me see her disguise technique before I decide.”

Ji Yao nodded. “I'll have Senior Sister bring her over.”

As she spoke, she took out a Transmission Compass and poured her Divine Sense into it.

Yang Kai also sat back down in his chair, feeling a bit out of sorts. Even the tea in his mouth tasted bitter. He glanced at Ji Yao a few times, but she acted as if nothing had happened, as if she hadn't just said those words.

Not long after, An Ruoyun's voice requesting an audience sounded from outside the hall. After receiving Bing Yun's summons, she entered with Shi Tianhe.

After paying respects, An Ruoyun said, “Master, is there something you need from me?”

Bing Yun said, “It's nothing to do with you. I just need Tianhe to do something.”

Shi Tianhe quickly replied, “Ancestor, please instruct me. I will do my utmost.”

Bing Yun smiled. “Don't be nervous. I just heard your disguise technique is quite impressive and wanted to see if it's really that miraculous.”

Shi Tianhe replied respectfully, “It's just a minor skill, probably not worthy of Ancestor's attention.”

An Ruoyun glared at her. “Since Ancestor wants to see, just show her.”

“Yes!” Shi Tianhe answered obediently. “Then I'll do my best.”

Yang Kai called to her, “Tianhe, disguise me.”

Shi Tianhe looked to Bing Yun for approval. After Bing Yun nodded, she walked over to Yang Kai and said softly, “Senior Uncle Yang, forgive me.”

As she spoke, she waved her hand and took out a bunch of odd-looking things, placing them on the table beside Yang Kai. The others looked closely and saw they were just some stuff that looked like rouge or clay, nothing special, and no energy fluctuations.

Shi Tianhe first carefully studied Yang Kai's face, her expression thoughtful. After a long while, she suddenly took a deep breath, and her hands moved swiftly, picking up the materials and applying them to various parts of Yang Kai's face with exquisite technique.

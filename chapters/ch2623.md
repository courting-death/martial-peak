## Chapter 2623 — Severed Kindness, Broken Righteousness

"Perhaps that Madam Dong found a new lover and wanted to leave the Dong Family, but how could the Dong Family Head agree? So he brought people to catch her and bring her back."

"Brother, you make a good point..."

For a moment, everyone looked at the Dong Family Head with sympathy, as if he had suffered the greatest humiliation. The Dong Family Head's face grew even darker, and he roared, "Wretched woman, look at the mess you've made!"

Madam Dong stood suspended in midair, gazing at the Dong Family Head with a desolate expression. She cried sorrowfully, "Dong Hai, do you truly feel nothing at all for our years as husband and wife?"

Dong Hai's face twitched slightly, a trace of struggle flashing in his eyes. He sighed, "Tianhe, if you still care for our marriage even a little, then come back to the Dong Family with me and never leave Taiping City again. The two of us can spend the rest of our lives together."

Tears streamed down Madam Dong's face as she shook her head. "Dong Hai, you know why I had to leave, and you know what I am going to do. Please, don't make things harder for me."

Dong Hai snapped harshly, "Are you trying to drive the Dong Family to ruin?"

"I'm sorry, I'm sorry..." Madam Dong's tears fell in large drops down her cheeks, her face full of guilt and self-blame, as if she had truly committed some heinous crime. The onlookers were left completely confused, having no idea what was really going on.

"Come back with me. I won't pursue what happened today, and neither will the Dong Family. You are still the mistress of the Dong Family!" Dong Hai reached a hand up toward the sky, gesturing to Madam Dong.

Madam Dong wept like rain, only shaking her head again and again.

The tenderness on Dong Hai's face gradually faded, replaced by cold severity. At last, he lowered his outstretched hand and said calmly, "Foolish woman Shi Tianhe, since you married into the Dong Family, you have been childless for a hundred years, which is a grave offense. You are vain and competitive, corrupting the Dong Family's reputation. Worse, you have no sense of shame, stealing the Dong Family's ancestral treasure. Today, I, Dong Hai, as the Family Head of the Dong Family, hereby declare: Shi Tianhe is expelled from the Dong Family. From this day forth, our marriage is severed, and we have nothing more to do with each other!"

With those words, the whole place erupted in an uproar.

Especially the local martial artists of Taiping City. Every one of them stared wide-eyed in disbelief.

Although they hadn't seen much of Madam Dong, there were plenty of rumors in the city that she was a good person, kind and compassionate, often helping the poor. Yet from the Family Head's mouth, she had become a shameless, vain woman.

But rumors are just rumors. If even her own husband says so, how could it be false?

And she actually stole the Dong Family's ancestral treasure! That was beyond poor character—it was utter moral depravity.

For a while, everyone sighed, unable to understand why the Dong Family's mistress would do such a thing. No wonder all the Dong Family Elders had come out in force.

Any man would find it hard to tolerate such a woman as his wife, wouldn't he?

"Puh..." With a soft sound, Shi Tianhe, standing in midair, suddenly spat out a mouthful of bright red blood, her face flushed. The blood soaked her chest, glaring and shocking.

Her body staggered, clutching her chest as if enduring the agony of ten thousand ants gnawing at her heart. Looking down at Dong Hai, her brows were filled with despair. She had never imagined Dong Hai could be so heartless.

Even a single night as husband and wife should count for something… and she and Dong Hai had shared a full hundred years together!

Back then, she had even risked offending her own Master and was expelled from her sect, swearing to be with Dong Hai. In the end, she got her wish, but she hadn't returned to her sect in a hundred years, nor seen her Senior Sisters she missed so much.

She had always stayed in the Dong Family, by Dong Hai's side, thinking she had found her ideal husband for life. Who would have thought, in the end, he would stab her in the heart.

The man she regarded as her sky, her earth, her only one in this life, had stabbed her so cruelly.

"Master, this matter doesn't seem so simple!" Ji Yao observed for a while, then softly said to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai curled his lips. "Why bother with other people's business?"

Though he said that, he couldn't help but sigh inwardly. Shi Tianhe's eyes, filled with despair, were like thick clouds blocking out all light—she was clearly in utter agony.

After a pause, Yang Kai said, "Yao'er, should we help her?"

Ji Yao replied, "I'll listen to Master."

"Ah!" Yang Kai couldn't help but sigh. Though he wasn't keen on meddling in today's affairs, the Dong Family had clearly gone too far—especially Dong Hai. Husband and wife, after living together for a hundred years, surely know each other inside and out. Even if there were some conflicts, couldn't they just sit down and talk things through? Was it really necessary to expel her from the Dong Family and sever their marriage in front of everyone like this?

How was she supposed to face people after this?

This man had no sense of responsibility. Shi Tianhe must have been blind to follow such a man.

"Hahahahaha..." Suddenly, Shi Tianhe burst into wild laughter in the sky, blood still trickling from the corner of her mouth, making for a shocking sight.

That laughter was full of bitterness, making everyone's heart tremble.

“Dong Hai!” Shi Tianhe suddenly cried out, her voice trembling with both grief and anger. “For the sake of the Dong Family, you would go this far. As the Family Head… you truly live up to your title!”

"Enough talk!" Dong Hai didn't want to drag this out any longer. With so many martial artists watching, the more this went on, the more it would disgrace the Dong Family and himself. He waved his hand lightly. "Elders, do it!"

The Elders' faces darkened at his words. "Forgive us!"

As soon as they spoke, they surged their Origin Qi and lunged at Shi Tianhe.

Shing—

With a resonant sound, no one knew when, but an icy longsword suddenly appeared in Shi Tianhe’s hand. The moment the blade emerged, the air around her plummeted in temperature, and a chilling, profound intent swept outward, enveloping the entire scene.

As the sword light flashed, snowflakes suddenly fell from the sky, enveloping the Dong Family Elders. Each snowflake was like a deadly blade, making the Elders' faces change dramatically—they dared not face its edge.

"It's snowing?"

"No, that's a secret technique—an ice-type secret technique!"

"This Madam Dong is actually this powerful?"

A group of people cried out in surprise.

On the other side, Dong Hai's face darkened when he saw Shi Tianhe attack. He shouted in panic, "Foolish woman, do you still want to resist?"

Meanwhile, Commander Yu frowned and said, "Dong Family Head, your wife... hmm, this technique she's using feels a lot like a secret art from the Ice Heart Valley."

Dong Hai forced a smile. "Commander Yu, you must be joking. This foolish woman has practiced ice-type techniques since childhood and has mastered some powerful secret arts, but it has nothing to do with Ice Heart Valley."

From his tone, it was clear that any connection to Ice Heart Valley would spell disaster.

Commander Yu didn't doubt him and nodded slightly. "That's for the best. You know what kind of situation Ice Heart Valley is in now. If your wife really is a disciple of the Ice Heart Valley, your Dong Family is finished."

Cold sweat broke out on Dong Hai's forehead. He laughed dryly, "She is herself. She has nothing to do with the Dong Family anymore."

"Snow Like Clear Sky!"

Ji Yao suddenly cried out, her brows tightly knit, staring intently at Shi Tianhe, who was fighting in the sky.

As Bing Yun's third disciple, Ji Yao was extremely familiar with the secret arts of the Ice Heart Valley. The moment Shi Tianhe used her sword move, Ji Yao recognized it as Snow Like Clear Sky, a secret technique only core disciples of the Ice Heart Valley could practice.

In the sky, Shi Tianhe forced back the Dong Family Elders with one move and tried to escape, but the Elders clung to her like leeches, forcing her to turn her sword and unleash a flurry of sword flowers.

The sword flowers were like pear blossoms, blooming beautifully in midair, yet filled with deadly intent. Shi Tianhe weaved through the blossoms, her figure flickering as she searched for a chance to escape.

"Ice Snow Pear Blossom Sword!"

Ji Yao cried out again.

If she had only suspected Shi Tianhe was an Ice Heart Valley disciple when she recognized the Snow Like Clear Sky technique, now she was completely certain.

If she wasn't a disciple of the Ice Heart Valley, how could she know these two secret arts?

Ji Yao was greatly surprised. She hadn't expected that, having just returned to the Northern Territory and not even reached her sect, she would encounter an Ice Heart Valley disciple here in Taiping City.

Just then, a Dong Family Elder broke through Shi Tianhe's sword moves, drawing her attention. Another Elder slipped behind her and struck at her back.

Although Shi Tianhe was strong, she couldn't fight so many at once. Within a few moves, she was overwhelmed. Even though she sensed the attack from behind, she was powerless to resist and grew anxious.

"Die!"

At that moment, a sudden cry rang out. Everyone saw a flash, and a stunningly beautiful, cold-faced woman appeared behind Shi Tianhe. With a casual palm strike, she blasted a Dong Family Elder at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm into a cloud of blood.

She was Bing Yun's Third Disciple, of extremely high status in her sect. Now, seeing someone bullying an Ice Heart Valley disciple right in front of her, and with numbers on their side, how could Ji Yao show mercy? With a single palm, she killed the Dong Family Elder.

She reached out and swept Shi Tianhe over, shielding her behind. Her delicate jade-like palm struck forward again and again, sending out a flurry of attacks.

Boom, boom, boom...

Explosions rang out as several clouds of blood burst in the sky. All the Dong Family Elders attacking Shi Tianhe were blasted into blood mist, leaving no bones behind, unable to fight back at all.

"Emperor Realm!"

A cry of shock rang out, and the crowd erupted.

Shi Tianhe had been in mortal danger, and the onlookers all thought she was doomed this time. But to their shock, a breathtakingly beautiful woman with an icy demeanor—an Emperor Realm master—suddenly appeared, and with one palm after another, wiped out the entire group of Dong Family Elders. In an instant, the situation was completely reversed.

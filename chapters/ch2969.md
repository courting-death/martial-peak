## Chapter 2969 - Eating People

"I really don't know..." Zhu Qing replied softly.

"Is it a secret of the Dragon Clan?"

Zhu Qing shook her head.

"Then tell me." Yang Kai reached out and carefully scooped Zhu Qing into his palm, as though he were holding the finest porcelain, then threatened, "If you know and don't report it, beware the family punishment."

"What family punishment..." Zhu Qing's eyes widened, her face blank with confusion, but she quickly understood what Yang Kai meant. Embarrassment heated her entire body. She looked up at Yang Kai and found his eyes fixed straight ahead, his breathing beginning to grow heavy. With a sigh in her heart, she knew that what would come could not be avoided.

At once, she lifted the book to cover half her face and said softly, "All things bear yin and embrace yang; when yin and yang mingle, Sources converge, and the rushing... qi becomes full and harmonious."

Yang Kai was stunned at first, then his expression turned strange. With a smile that was not quite a smile, he said, "You mean..."

Zhu Qing's entire face was hidden behind the book, so no one could see her expression, but the pretty face behind it was surely burning like fire. She hurriedly said, "I just said that casually. Don't take it seriously."

"The Dragon Clan also cares about dual cultivation?" Yang Kai smiled even more happily, his huge mouth splitting open in an especially fierce grin, as though he could swallow Zhu Qing in one bite.

If that was really the case, then the days ahead would be far too blissful.

"That's not it, it's just..."

"Just what?" Yang Kai stretched out one claw and gently pushed aside the book blocking Zhu Qing, bringing his face much closer with an overbearing air.

Zhu Qing had nowhere to hide. With a troubled expression, she said, "It's just that even for the women of your Human Race, their Primordial... Yin Qi is greatly beneficial to a man's cultivation, and the higher her cultivation, the more obvious the effect. All the more so for us of the Dragon Clan."

Yang Kai said, "You mean the reason I became like this is because of your... first time?"

Zhu Qing nodded gently. Hearing Yang Kai say such a thing to her face left her so ashamed that she wished she could find a hole and crawl into it.

"It won't have any effect in the future?"

"No." Zhu Qing shook her head.

"I don't believe it!" As Yang Kai spoke, he grabbed Zhu Qing and darted to the side. While striding forward, he withdrew the Dragon Transformation Art, and his enormous figure rapidly shrank.

"What are you doing?" Zhu Qing asked in alarm.

"Whether it has an effect or not, we'll know after trying." Yang Kai had already returned to his original appearance and lifted Zhu Qing by the waist.

Pressed against Yang Kai's chest, feeling that scorching heat, Zhu Qing immediately panicked and hurriedly said, "It really has no effect anymore. I'm not lying to you."

Yang Kai gave a low, wicked laugh. "I already said we're going to try. Stop struggling. It's useless."

As his words fell, he threw Zhu Qing heavily onto the great bed. Zhu Qing cried out in surprise, and Yang Kai had already pounced up like a hungry dog lunging at food. Although she had experienced this before, Zhu Qing was still not quite used to it. Her heart beat violently in her chest, as though it might leap from her mouth at any moment.

The man above her was extremely aggressive, his unstoppable momentum crashing straight through and blasting her not particularly sturdy defenses to pieces.

Her clothes began to fall into disarray. Zhu Qing tilted her head, her beautiful eyes tightly shut, both hands clutching the bed sheets, looking as if she were about to be marched to the execution grounds, unbearably nervous.

Yang Kai suddenly seemed to remember something. He turned his head to the side, pointed with one hand, and said word by word, "Get... out!"

Off to the side, the little black dog had somehow been startled awake. These past few days it had been quietly dozing, neither noisy nor troublesome, extremely well behaved. At this moment, however, it had raised its head and was staring toward the great bed with its eyes wide open, motionless, even holding its breath as it watched intently.

Those canine eyes were filled with curiosity and bewilderment.

Hearing Yang Kai's words, it whimpered and stood up somewhat unwillingly, then tucked its tail and slowly walked toward the door.

When it reached the door, it even looked back reluctantly, its eyes full of pleading, as if begging Yang Kai to let it stay.

Yang Kai snorted, and it could only dejectedly push open the door with its head, slip out through the crack, and even remember to close the door behind it before leaving.

Outside the palace, several women of different apparent ages stood straight as spears, and a layer of frost could practically be scraped from each of their faces.

The Princesses had simply had enough. A few days ago, Yang Kai had told them to wait outside the palace, and they could only obey.

Who would have thought this wait would last several days? During these days, they had not heard any summons, nor had Yang Kai given the slightest instruction, yet they could only obediently remain outside, planted there like wooden stakes.

Having lived in comfort and privilege all year round, when had they ever suffered such grievance? Each of them cursed Yang Kai bloody in her heart, wishing he would die sooner and be reborn sooner.

Only the little princess was innocent and naive, completely unaware of what had happened. She only knew that Royal Father had ordered them to come here, and that they had to obey the man inside and satisfy all his demands.

She was still young, her purity not yet tainted by the complexity of the royal family, so she did not feel particularly wronged. It was just that after standing there blankly for several days, she was sleepy and tired. At this moment, she was leaning on one of her elder sisters' shoulders and dozing off, her head bobbing again and again, almost pitching to the ground several times.

When the little black dog walked out, the princesses all saw it. It immediately drew the women's attention, and they all looked toward it.

Second Princess Fu Yu's face was full of resentment. She still remembered how this little beast had made her lose all face and expose her body a few days ago, so naturally she bore a deep grudge against it. In her heart, she calculated how she might avenge herself, wondering whether she could seize the chance to catch this little beast and boil a pot of dog meat for a treat.

The little black dog clearly sensed something. It turned its head and looked at Fu Yu, baring its teeth as a threatening sound came from its throat, as if it were about to pounce.

"Little beast, you still dare act arrogant. One day, I'll kill you and eat your meat!" Fu Yu thought viciously in her heart. She seemed to have already foreseen that scene, and an inexplicable pleasure swept through her whole body.

Just then, a cry of surprise suddenly came from inside the palace.

The princesses were all startled. Not knowing what change had occurred inside the palace, they looked at one another, faces blank.

The dozing little princess was also awakened. Her eyes widened, and she looked more energetic than anyone as she hurriedly asked, "What happened? What happened?"

No one paid attention to her. The princesses were all tilting their ears to listen.

Another startled cry came. This time, the women heard it more clearly, but this sound... seemed a little strange. It did not sound like someone had met with an accident. It was very suppressed and low, more like...

The faces of the princesses flushed red at once. They had clearly realized something, so how could they still have any thoughts of eavesdropping? Instead, they wished they could block their ears to avoid letting that sound dirty them.

The suppressed sounds grew louder and louder, intermittent, sometimes high, sometimes low, as though someone were about to die.

The little princess jumped in fright, her face paling as she said, "What's happening? The sounds inside are so strange. Did something happen to someone? Should we go in and take a look?"

This proposal left the women torn between laughter and tears.

The Sixth Princess standing beside the little princess hurriedly said, "Absolutely do not go in."

"Why? Why can't we go in?" the little princess pressed.

The Sixth Princess stamped her foot and said, "Children shouldn't ask so many questions."

"Oh." The little princess obediently nodded. Although she did not understand, since Sixth Sister had said so, there must be a reason. Then she suddenly looked curiously at several of her sisters and asked worriedly, "Second Sister, Sixth Sister, Eleventh Sister, why are your faces so red? Are you sick?"

The Eleventh Princess glared at her and said irritably, "Sick? When have we ever been sick?"

"But..." The little princess worriedly stretched out her small hand and touched the Sixth Princess's cheek, then drew her hand back in fright. "Sixth Sister, your face is so hot. This is terrible. Hurry and call Royal Father to come take a look."

The Eleventh Princess glanced at the Sixth Princess, covered her mouth, and laughed sweetly. "It's useless to call Royal Father to look at Sixth Sister's problem."

"Then who should look?"

The Eleventh Princess puckered her lips toward the palace and said, "Just call the man inside to come take a look." After speaking, she burst into laughter, laughing so hard her waist bent, her body trembling like flowers in the wind for quite a while.

"Then I'll go call him over." The little princess believed her and immediately took a step as if to rush into the palace, but after only two steps, she timidly retreated again and said fearfully, "Is he killing someone... I don't dare go in."

Eleventh Princess laughed even more happily, her peach-blossom eyes glistening as she said suggestively, “He isn’t exactly killing anyone. He’s just... eating someone.”

"Eat... eating someone!" The little princess turned pale with fright, her face going deathly white.

The Eleventh Princess frightened her further. "The man inside is an evildoer who commits every crime imaginable. He likes eating tender little girls like you the most. If he catches you, he'll eat your ten fingers first, then eat your nose, then bite off your ears..."

As she spoke, she even chewed in an extremely vivid manner, as if she really were eating something with great relish.

"Ah!" The little princess was terrified and leapt into the Sixth Princess's embrace, trembling all over as she said in a shaking voice, "Sixth Sister, save me. I don't want to be eaten. I don't want him to eat me."

"Why are you scaring her!" The Sixth Princess glared fiercely at the Eleventh Princess. "Little Sister is already simple-minded and believes whatever others say. Why scare her like that?"

The Eleventh Princess shrugged her delicate shoulders. "We're bored anyway, so I found a little amusement. Ah, this erotic drama can only be heard but not joined. How dull."

Fu Yu said coldly, "If you want to, you can go in. The question is whether he wants you."

The Eleventh Princess said, "Forget it. That guy is not easy to provoke. As for me, I'm afraid I'd be eaten until not even my bones were left."

Hearing these words, the little princess believed her even more. She clung tightly to the Sixth Princess and cried, "Sixth Sister, take me away. I don't want to stay here anymore. Royal Father, save me! Royal Father, save me! Wuwuwu!"

Fu Yu glanced at her and shouted in a low voice, "Cover her ears."

The Sixth Princess hurriedly stretched out her hands and covered the little princess's ears tightly.

The sounds from inside the palace continued to drift out. At first, they were still somewhat restrained, but by the end they had actually become completely unbridled, making the princesses feel uncomfortable all over as they listened. Fortunately, after being frightened and crying for a while, the little princess seemed to have grown tired and fell soundly asleep in her Sixth Sister's arms, tear tracks still on her face, making the Sixth Princess both distressed and furious. She felt that man truly was no good.

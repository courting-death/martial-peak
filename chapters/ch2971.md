## Chapter 2971 — Hello, Little Brother-in-Law!

Yang Kai and Zhu Qing intended to leave through the Space Array in the city, and the Human Sovereign personally came to see them off.

A group of Princes and Princesses followed close behind, along with everyone of any standing in Human Sovereign City. The large, straggling procession left those unaware of the truth puzzled, wondering what exactly was happening.

Along the way, the Human Sovereign was remarkably talkative and considerate, repeatedly reminding Yang Kai of things to watch out for outside.

Yang Kai responded from time to time.

After finally seeing them onto the Space Array, the Human Sovereign secretly breathed a sigh of relief. As long as this fellow left, Human Sovereign City would remain his domain, and the rule of the Imperial Family would not be shaken.

He personally bestowed the Human Sovereign Seal upon Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, then ordered someone to activate the array.

When the light of transmission lit up, the Human Sovereign's suspended heart finally settled, and a relieved smile appeared on his face.

But a single sentence from Yang Kai completely shattered his good mood.

"This Young Master may return here someday, so I hope Sir will take good care of the Sacred Tree and refrain from drawing upon its power at will."

The light flared, and Yang Kai and Zhu Qing disappeared.

The Human Sovereign felt as though he had fallen into an ice cellar, the smile on his face turning stiff.

The many Princes, Princesses, and experts of the city all sensed the Human Sovereign's foul mood and fell silent as cicadas in winter. Only after a long while did an immensely powerful aura erupt from the Human Sovereign's body, transforming into a wave that spread in every direction. As the wave dispersed, he seemed to have vented the fury in his heart, and his entire person appeared much older.

...

Tens of thousands of li from Human Sovereign City, deep within an underground cave in an extremely secluded jungle, Yang Kai and Zhu Qing calmly appeared.

The cave was pitch-black, so dark that one could not see one's hand before one's face. Only a sloping passage led to the surface. Inside stood a crude Space Array connected to the one in the city. There were more than a dozen such places throughout the Revolving World, covering nearly half of it.

The cultivators of Human Sovereign City traveled back and forth through these Space Arrays.

Every Space Array had been built in an extremely concealed location out of fear that the Demon Race would discover and destroy it.

Even if the Demon Race did discover these Space Arrays, however, they could only destroy them. Without the blessing of the Human Sovereign Seal, they could not use the arrays to enter Human Sovereign City. In a sense, the Human Sovereign Seal was a barrier protecting Human Sovereign City, somewhat similar to the Teleportation Tokens Yang Kai made.

The Space Arrays he arranged could only be activated and used by someone carrying a special Teleportation Token.

The two left the underground cave and came to the surface.

At that moment, there was nothing unusual about the world's laws outside, clearly indicating that the Revolving World was currently leaning toward the Star Realm.

"Yang Kai, stand guard for me." Zhu Qing did not delay. After giving Yang Kai a brief instruction, she prepared to use a Dragon Clan Secret Technique to search for Zhu Lie.

"Hm? What did you call me?"

Zhu Qing whispered, "Husband..."

She had never addressed a man this way before. Her heart pounded fiercely, and that simple form of address seemed to bind the two of them tightly together, sharing life and death, glory and disgrace.

Only then did Yang Kai reveal a satisfied expression.

Zhu Qing reached out and cut open her palm. A drop of fiery red blood rolled down, blazing like a flame and radiating intense heat. After falling to the ground, it merged into the earth and disappeared.

Obscure and profound incantations rang out. Zhu Qing's expression became sacred and solemn, as though she were devoutly praying to some unknown deity.

Yang Kai had witnessed a similar scene once before, when Zhu Lie used a Secret Technique in the Frozen Earth. He therefore knew that the technique Zhu Qing was using should be the same one Zhu Lie had used back then.

It was a Secret Technique specifically used to find people.

A moment later, the chanting became loud and intense, shaking the world like rolling thunder or a Dragon's roar resounding through the Nine Heavens.

A point of crimson bloomed from the ground. Immediately afterward, something resembling blood coral grew from the earth, stopping only when it reached one foot in height.

Zhu Qing ceased her chanting and drew the blood coral into her hand. After examining the direction in which the hazy light upon it drifted, she pointed and said, "He's over there."

Yang Kai nodded, soared into the air, and flew in that direction together with Zhu Qing.

Half a cup of tea later, the two arrived before a cliff.

From a distance, the cliff resembled a perched eagle, with the cliff face forming the eagle's beak.

This was Eagle Beak Cliff, the place the Human Sovereign had mentioned before. When the two arrived, they indeed found the remains of Demon Race corpses. Blood, severed limbs, and mangled flesh littered the ground. Judging from the traces left behind, although the battle had been fierce, it had lasted only a short time, as these demons seemed to have been killed before they could even exert much strength.

This could only mean one thing: the enemy these demons encountered was extremely powerful, powerful enough to crush them with ease.

Yang Kai looked at Zhu Qing. She nodded gently and said, "There is a Dragon Clan aura here. It was Zhu Lie."

"Can you tell which way they went?" Yang Kai asked.

Zhu Qing examined the blood coral, then pointed to the left. "This way."

Another half an hour later, Yang Kai, who was flying at great speed, suddenly exclaimed softly and looked into the distance, clearly having discovered something.

Zhu Qing sensed it as well and focused her vision on the area ahead.

The world's Spiritual Qi was roiling over there, and the faint sounds of fighting could be heard. It seemed that a conflict had broken out.

The two exchanged a glance and immediately accelerated.

A short while later, the scene ahead entered their view.

An army of over a hundred demons had completely surrounded a certain area, their gathered Demonic Qi soaring into the sky. Trapped within were two figures: Zhu Lie and Li Jiao, who had become separated from Yang Kai after entering the Revolving World.

It was unclear whether the two had remained together all along or had reunited later.

Their current situation was somewhat grim. Although they were among the strongest experts in the world, several Demon Kings seemed to be overseeing the demons surrounding them. Relying on their advantage in numbers, the demons continuously harried the pair and wore down their strength.

Several dozen demons already lay dead on the ground around them, all clearly slain by Zhu Lie and Li Jiao.

The Demon Race had a hundred clans, each possessing its own unique innate divine ability. Shadow Demons excelled at concealment, Succubi at beguilement, Might Demons possessed boundless strength, and Blood Demons were sinister and unpredictable. Anyone unfamiliar with the traits of these clans could easily suffer a loss.

Had two ordinary Third-Stage Emperors come here, they would likely have been defeated long ago.

It was only because Zhu Lie was a member of the Dragon Clan that he could remain standing under such a heavy encirclement. But Zhu Lie was Zhu Lie, and Li Jiao was Li Jiao.

Zhu Lie clearly paid little attention to whether Li Jiao lived or died.

When Yang Kai and Zhu Qing arrived, Li Jiao was clearly in an abnormal state. His emotions were running extremely high, and wisps of black Qi were actually seeping from his body, making it appear that he had been corroded by Demonic Qi.

Moreover, his eyes were tinged red, his movements were stiff when he attacked, and the circulation of his Emperor Qi was intermittent.

Amid the Demon Race army, an enchanting and seductive Succubus was casting a spell on him. Her invisible voice of temptation slipped soundlessly through Li Jiao's mental defenses and echoed in his mind, seeking to destroy his true self and reduce him to a Demon slave.

This was the method Succubi used most often. Compared to the other Demon Race clans, Succubi were generally delicate and physically weak, but their Divine Sense was extraordinarily powerful. They excelled most at such underhanded, insidious methods.

Several Demon Commanders launched fearless attacks at Zhu Lie, only for him to point at them one after another and make their bodies explode, sending blood raining from the sky.

Just then, the blood filling the sky suddenly contracted. As though possessing a mind of its own, it transformed into blood snakes that twisted and lunged at Zhu Lie with snapping jaws.

Zhu Lie coldly snorted and breathed out a scorching Dragon Breath, incinerating the countless blood snakes. Before he could relax, however, a blood snake unexpectedly lunged at his face from within the Dragon Breath. Zhu Lie had not even noticed how it had broken through.

At first glance, this blood snake looked no different from the others, but closer inspection revealed its power and uniqueness.

It was already too late to defend, so Zhu Lie could only circulate his Dragon Qi and prepare to withstand the blow directly.

A strange, cackling laugh suddenly rang out from within the blood snake, filled with triumph, as though some scheme had succeeded.

Zhu Lie frowned, instinctively sensing danger. Choosing to endure this attack head-on no longer seemed wise.

Just as the blood snake was about to strike Zhu Lie, a pitch-black Moon Blade suddenly slashed down from the sky. It skimmed over Zhu Lie's head and sliced the blood snake in two. The blood snake turned to ash as though it had been burned, while an agonized scream rang out nearby at the same time.

A Blood Demon suddenly clutched his head and fell to one knee, his expression twisted with unbearable pain.

"Who goes there?" Zhu Lie shouted in a low voice. When he looked up, he saw a familiar figure in the sky.

Yang Kai grinned and greeted him with overflowing enthusiasm. "Hello, Little Brother-in-Law!"

Zhu Lie frowned in confusion. "What?"

He had not clearly heard what Yang Kai had just called him.

As soon as he spoke, Li Jiao, who had been standing behind him and fending off the enemy, suddenly turned around and thrust a palm at him without warning. Emperor Qi surged in his palm as the full cultivation of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm master erupted at that moment.

Li Jiao's eyes were unfocused, making it obvious that he was not acting of his own volition, but even Zhu Lie could not ignore the power of this palm.

A red light flashed, and Zhu Qing appeared strangely between Zhu Lie and Li Jiao. She swung her delicate fist, moving after Li Jiao yet striking first as she slammed it into his chest.

The sound of bones breaking rang out. Li Jiao screamed, and his unfocused eyes began moving again, clearly bewildered by his circumstances. The next instant, an agonizing pain erupted from his chest. The tremendous force pressed against his heart, nearly stopping it from beating. A burst of energy exploded from his back and tore open his clothes, sending him flying backward as he spat out a mouthful of blood in midair.

Zhu Lie turned his head and saw Zhu Qing. He was delighted at first, but his expression quickly turned indifferent. "You're still alive?"

"Is that any way to speak when you see your Elder Sister?" Zhu Qing glared at him.

"Hmph!" Zhu Lie coldly snorted. He seemed about to say something else when his eyes suddenly widened. He stared at Zhu Qing in disbelief and exclaimed, "You... you..."

"What about me?" Zhu Qing pursed her lips, her cheeks flushing. Guessing why Zhu Lie was so shocked, she interrupted him. "Whatever it is, we can talk about it later."

## Chapter 2659 — Delivering Charcoal in the Snow

Half a day later, a Space Array had already taken shape.

Yang Kai called out to Ji Yao, "Junior Sister Yao, please make a trip to Ice Heart Valley and inform Nanmen Dajun. Tell him to come over after he finishes repairing Ice Heart Valley's Grand Guardian Array."

As he spoke, he handed Ji Yao a specially crafted token.

Ji Yao took it, asking suspiciously, "What is this token for?"

With a single glance, she could tell the token was newly refined, and it was made by Yang Kai himself, as it still carried traces of his aura.

Yang Kai smiled, "Any Space Array I set up cannot be used by others unless they have this token or I personally activate it."

This was why Luan Feng failed to activate the Space Array in Fengluo Palace that day—she didn't have Yang Kai's restriction token, so she mistakenly thought the array was defective. She had hoped to see a joke, but ended up disappointed.

Hearing this, Ji Yao's eyes flickered, but she said nothing more and stepped directly onto the Space Array.

While embedding Source Crystals, Yang Kai said, "There's no rush for this. Everything should wait until he finishes repairing Ice Heart Valley's grand array."

"Understood!" Ji Yao nodded slightly, and in the next moment, her figure was enveloped in white light and vanished.

After Ji Yao left, Yang Kai glanced around, then his figure flickered and disappeared as well.

Since he planned to take root here, Yang Kai naturally wanted to thoroughly investigate the foundation that originally belonged to Wenqing Sect. Although he had wandered around before, he was always searching for the hidden Treasure Vault and hadn't examined things closely.

Now that he had free time, Yang Kai, as the new master, needed to inspect this land.

With a change in mindset, everything he saw looked completely different.

Within Wenqing Sect, the Spiritual Qi was dense and abundant. Picturesque peaks soared into the clouds, rare and wondrous beasts roamed the wilds, and spiritual springs and wells dotted the land—a sacred place that martial artists dreamed of cultivating in.

Such a vast foundation could easily accommodate a hundred thousand disciples without feeling crowded. His friends and family on Dark Star didn't even number that many.

After several days of wandering, Yang Kai finally examined every corner of this land, leaving nothing unchecked. He had some vague plans in mind.

He returned to the mountain gate where he had entered Wenqing Sect.

Previously, a plaque stood here inscribed with "Wenqing Sect," passed down for tens of thousands of years, but Yang Kai had smashed it.

Returning now, Yang Kai naturally had plans, but when he swept his Divine Sense, he found many people’s auras nearby.

These people varied in strength; some hid stealthily in the shadows, others stood openly nearby, all watching Wenqing Sect and whispering among themselves. There were thousands, and more were gathering.

After the battle between Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley, Wenqing Sect was utterly defeated. The helpers they recruited suffered heavy casualties. News of this spread quickly throughout the Northern Territory.

These people suddenly appeared outside Wenqing Sect’s mountain gate simply because they heard the news and wanted to see if they could profit from the situation.

When Wenqing Sect was at its peak, they could only look up in awe, never daring to approach. But times had changed; Wenqing Sect was doomed, and a top sect with tens of thousands of years of history surely hid great wealth.

Since ancient times, wealth has moved people’s hearts. Thinking of the vast riches hidden within Wenqing Sect, many couldn’t sit still and wanted to come see if they could pick up some leftovers.

A behemoth like Wenqing Sect—just a little of its leftovers would be enough to keep them satisfied for a lifetime.

But no one knew the situation inside Wenqing Sect, so no one dared to be the first to enter. They could only watch from outside the gate.

This crowd had gathered over the past few days, from various sects and family forces.

After hesitating for days, they finally thought to elect a few leaders to plan together, but before they could agree, they saw Yang Kai stroll out of Wenqing Sect.

Instantly, thousands of eyes fixed on Yang Kai, and the chatter stopped.

The next moment, the crowd erupted.

"Someone came out from inside. Could it be a Wenqing Sect expert?"

Wenqing Sect’s reputation in the Northern Territory was immense. Seeing someone emerge, many instinctively thought it was a survivor of Wenqing Sect, and were fearful of being blamed.

"Nonsense! All Wenqing Sect’s high-level members below the Sect Master are gone. Even if someone escaped, how could they return? That’s just walking into a trap."

"Exactly! Didn’t you see Wenqing Sect’s ten-thousand-year-old sign smashed? Who would dare stay here?"

"I think this person is here to profit, just arrived before us, and surely has gained something!"

"Damn it, damn it! What right does he have to trespass Wenqing Sect’s foundation? Outrageous!"

Hearing this, many grew restless, their gazes at Yang Kai filled with envy and resentment, as if Wenqing Sect belonged to them and Yang Kai’s entry was an affront.

They had lingered outside for days, never daring to rush in, but now it seemed someone had already taken the lead.

Since that was the case, why were they still hesitating?

In that instant, whether lurking in the shadows or standing openly, all the cultivators revealed themselves. Some who fancied themselves strong even soared forward, rushing straight toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai was alone and looked young, so people underestimated him. With their numbers, they felt no fear.

A dozen people landed thirty zhang away from Yang Kai, led by a middle-aged man who looked down at him and asked, "Kid, did you just come out of Wenqing Sect?"

Yang Kai glanced at him, sneering inwardly, already knowing their intentions.

At first, he was surprised to see so many gathered outside, but after a moment’s thought, he understood their motives.

It was undeniable that their thinking was not wrong. Now that Wenqing Sect had fallen, there were surely many treasures left behind in its foundation. When a wall collapses, everyone piles on; when a drum breaks, all come to beat it—such has always been the way of the world.

Unfortunately, Wenqing Sect was conquered by Yang Kai, and he had no intention of handing over its benefits.

Facing the middle-aged man’s question, Yang Kai didn’t even bother to respond, instead rummaging through his Spatial Ring.

His aloof attitude made the man’s face darken.

He was a sect master, and though his sect wasn’t as grand as Wenqing Sect, it was still a notable force in the Northern Territory. Being ignored after personally questioning someone was humiliating.

Sensing his anger, a young man beside him glared and said, "Kid, are you deaf? My Sect Master is asking you a question!"

Yang Kai glanced at the youth, replying indifferently, "My ears work fine. I just don’t bother with pointless questions."

He had clearly just come out of Wenqing Sect, yet the man asked such a thing—what was he thinking?

The youth grew angry and was about to scold him, but the middle-aged man waved him off, stopping his outburst. He looked Yang Kai up and down, then smiled, "This Lord is Li Qingyuan, Sect Master of Feiyuan Pavilion. May I ask your name, Young Brother?"

He changed his arrogant demeanor to a friendly one, making the youth beside him frown, unsure of the Sect Master’s intentions.

Yang Kai waved his hand dismissively. "We’re just strangers crossing paths—no need to ask further."

Li Qingyuan’s brow furrowed, his face displeased. As a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert and Sect Master of Feiyuan Pavilion, he had lowered himself to ask a junior’s name, only to be rebuffed. It was embarrassing.

Still, he had some composure. Though unhappy, he kept a calm face and said, "Young Brother, this Lord and many heroes of the Northern Territory came here, saw Wenqing Sect’s ruined gate, and felt sorrow. We wanted to investigate but feared causing misunderstandings. Since you came out, you must know Wenqing Sect’s situation well. Could you tell us about it?"

The others’ eyes lit up, nodding in agreement and urging Yang Kai to explain Wenqing Sect’s situation.

Yang Kai sneered, "You lot dare call yourselves heroes of the Northern Territory?"

He glanced around indifferently, then nodded, "A dozen Emperor Realm cultivators of the First and Second Stage—well, I suppose that counts."

His shameless words made the Emperor Realm cultivators around Li Qingyuan unhappy. They were Emperor Realm experts, not nobodies, each famous in the Northern Territory, yet Yang Kai dismissed them as barely counting.

This kid was truly arrogant.

Yang Kai’s smile faded as he looked at the crowd, "You’re here to take advantage, correct? Just unsure if there are any Wenqing Sect experts left inside, so you don’t dare go in."

Li Qingyuan and the others blushed.

Though it was true, being exposed so bluntly was embarrassing.

Li Qingyuan forced a smile, "Young Brother, you’re mistaken. We’re sects and family forces with ties to Wenqing Sect. Hearing of its disaster, we mainly wanted to see if there’s any way we could help, not to take advantage. Don’t think so badly of people."

"Exactly!"

"That's right, we're all here to deliver charcoal in the snow. Boy, don't judge others by your own standards or assume the worst of people's hearts."

## Chapter 2693 — Come Bite Me, Then

In an instant, Yingfei's face turned extremely dark.

He had always believed that with his own protection, even facing an Elder of Star God Palace was nothing to fear. After all, he was a Yao King, comparable to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm—how could he be afraid of a Star God Palace Elder? But now, this so-called Eight Directions Division Array had separated everyone. He didn't even know where Yang Kai was, so how could he protect him? On the contrary, the one who set up the array could use its power to toy with Yang Kai at will.

Regret welled up in his heart. If only he had insisted earlier that Yang Kai bring Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei as well. Back then, Yang Kai refused, and he couldn't say much more.

But thinking it over, since the other side had this Eight Directions Division Array that could separate everyone, bringing more Yao Kings would have been useless.

A few days ago, he had already exposed his identity as a Yao King. Since the enemy dared to act, they must have been fully prepared. Now it was clear—the arrangements were meticulous. Two arrays were running, not only trapping Yang Kai in this world but also forcing him to fight alone.

"Patriarch Gong, can you break the array?" Yingfei asked in a deep voice.

Gong Tai sighed leisurely, "It can be done, but it will take time."

"How long?" Yingfei felt uneasy.

"Ten days!"

Yingfei fell silent. Ten days—by then, even the flowers would have withered. Given Yang Kai’s current predicament, how could he possibly afford to wait that long?

Ai Ou spoke from the side, "Yang boy has always been blessed with great fortune. Though the enemy's methods are impressive this time, it doesn't mean they'll succeed. Patriarch Gong, focus on breaking the array. We'll also try to find that boy and see if we can lend him a hand."

There was no better plan. Everyone knew he was right. They immediately split up, searching for any flaws in the array.

……

In the pitch-black world, Yang Kai seemed to be standing in the void, yet the surroundings were completely different from true emptiness. He sent out his Divine Sense, but an invisible barrier blocked him on all sides, preventing him from sensing anyone else.

He stood quietly in place, the corners of his mouth slightly raised, calm and unafraid, patiently waiting.

In the darkness, a pair of eyes quietly observed his every reaction. Seeing that he showed not the slightest hint of panic or urgency, it became clear that this youth possessed an extraordinary temperament—he was not someone who could be dealt with by ordinary means. Realizing this, the observer no longer bothered to hide and simply revealed himself without reservation.

Suddenly, light appeared in the darkness, and a figure emerged ahead.

Yang Kai looked up and immediately saw an old man, as if he had always been standing there, appearing without a trace. Behind him were two more figures: one was Wu Ming, who had disappeared earlier, and the other was Chi Yue, whom he hadn't seen in a long time.

Chi Yue's cultivation seemed to have been sealed. Her beautiful face was a bit pale, and at this moment, she was being restrained by Wu Ming, her hands pinned behind her back so she couldn't move.

At the sight of Yang Kai, Chi Yue's eyes flashed with surprise and joy. She clearly hadn't expected him to appear here, but her face quickly darkened. She slowly shook her head at Yang Kai, signaling him to escape.

Wu Ming saw all this and sneered coldly. He tightened his grip, making Chi Yue groan in pain, her face growing even paler.

"Young friend Yang!" The old man looked like an immortal recluse at first glance—three long whiskers, dressed in a blue robe, exuding the air of a worldly master. He smiled as he greeted Yang Kai.

"Old dog, you finally decided to show yourself," Yang Kai grinned, baring his teeth.

The old man shook his head with a wry smile. "Young friend Yang, please mind your words. This Old Man is Tan Junhao!"

"So Old dog Tan!" Yang Kai raised his eyebrows.

Wu Ming's face twisted in anger. "Watch your filthy mouth, you little brat! Say that one more time if you dare!"

Yang Kai tilted his head back and rattled off at lightning speed, "Old dog Tan, Old dog Tan, Old dog Tan, Old dog Tan! Little Dog Wu, Little Dog Wu, Little Dog Wu, Little Dog Wu! I said it—what are you going to do, bite me?"

"You brat!" Wu Ming was livid. He was a Second Stage Emperor, and no one had ever insulted him to his face like this. Rage boiled in his chest, ready to erupt like a volcano.

Tan Junhao took a deep breath and waved his hand, stopping Wu Ming's outburst. He squinted at Yang Kai and said, "Young friend Yang, you seem to hold quite a grudge against this old man."

Yang Kai replied coldly, "What do you think?"

Tan Junhao smiled and nodded. "You have every right to be angry. But this old man had no choice. I hope you can understand."

"I'll forgive you when you're dead."

Tan Junhao was stunned, then burst out laughing. "You jest, young friend. I am an Elder of Star God Palace, blessed by the Great Emperor. Who in this world would dare kill me?"

"So that's what you're relying on!" Yang Kai nodded lightly, then suddenly raised his eyes, his voice chilling. "I'm curious—what would happen if I killed an Elder of a Great Emperor's Sect?"

Tan Junhao's eye twitched. He realized Yang Kai wasn't joking—he truly meant to kill. But he didn't take it to heart. Having the intent was one thing; having the ability was another. He smiled, "Then this old man will wait and see. But before that, shouldn't you return something to its rightful owner? That item originally belonged to me, but you obtained it by chance years ago."

"You mean this?" Yang Kai reached out and grabbed at the air. Instantly, a token appeared in his palm, carved with a lifelike dragon and a large 'Dragon' character on the front. The token looked ancient, its material unknown, clearly very old.

"Exactly!" Tan Junhao's eyes lit up at the sight of the token, and he nodded slightly.

This Dragon Isle Token was an extremely precious treasure. Though it couldn't be used for refining weapons or pills, it was the only pass for ordinary people to enter Dragon Isle. With it, one could go to Dragon Isle and make a request that the Dragon Isle could fulfill.

Tan Junhao was already at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. Beyond that was the realm of the Great Emperor. He knew he could never reach that level on his own, but perhaps there was something on the mysterious Dragon Isle that could help him break through.

To him, the Dragon Isle Token was a tremendous opportunity.

At the very least, he could ask the Dragon Clan for a few drops of true dragon blood—there would be no problem with that.

That was why he had spent years tracking down the Dragon Isle Token.

Now, seeing the token in Yang Kai's hand, he was naturally delighted. But as an old monster who had lived for who knows how many years, he kept his composure, showing no emotion on his face.

Wu Ming, on the other hand, couldn't help but look pleased at the sight of the Dragon Isle Token.

He was a Second Stage Emperor. With his talent and effort, who knew how long it would take to reach the Third Stage? If he could get the Dragon Isle Token and visit Dragon Isle, he might break through immediately.

But the token belonged to Tan Junhao. Even if he wanted it, he didn't dare make a move.

Yang Kai tossed the Dragon Isle Token a few times, then said after a moment's thought, "I've got what you want, and you've got who I want. How about we make a trade?"

Tan Junhao smiled. "A trade requires both sides to be equal. In this situation... do you think you have the right to bargain with me?"

Yang Kai's face darkened. "That's not much of a conversation. Do you really think you can just take the Dragon Isle Token for free?"

Tan Junhao chuckled. "Not at all. But please return the Dragon Isle Token first, and we can discuss the rest."

Yang Kai said, "Let her go first, and the token is yours. This damned thing is useless to me anyway."

"Give me the Dragon Isle Token first!" Tan Junhao remained unmoved, a faint smile on his face, clearly confident he had Yang Kai cornered.

"Let her go first!" Yang Kai snarled.

The negotiation broke down in a few words. Tan Junhao stopped wasting time and turned to Wu Ming. "Disciple, show Young friend Yang our bargaining chip, so he doesn't think we're not sincere."

Wu Ming grinned wickedly. "Yes!"

He turned to Yang Kai and said, "Little bastard, I hear this demoness is your mother-in-law?"

Yang Kai's eye twitched. He thought, Sister Hua, why did you have to tell them everything? It seemed the secrets of his Xuan Realm Pearl and Everlasting Tree had probably been revealed too.

But he couldn't blame her. In the face of absolute power, Hua Qingsi couldn't resist at all. She probably didn't even remember what she told Tan Junhao.

It must have been an extremely sinister secret technique that forced Hua Qingsi to spill her secrets.

"If you so much as harm a hair on her head, you're dead," Yang Kai said coldly.

Wu Ming chuckled. "Relax, how could this Lord bear to hurt such a beauty? I like her very much. If not for my master stopping me, I would have taken her as a concubine long ago!"

Chi Yue was a rare beauty, with a mature, seductive charm. Though not as enchanting as Shan Qingluo, she was even more alluring in her own way. That was why, back then, Luo Jin, the City Lord of Tianhe City, fell for her at first sight and tried to force her into marriage.

As Wu Ming spoke, he reached out and gently stroked Chi Yue's cheek.

Chi Yue's body trembled violently, her eyes full of disgust. But with her cultivation sealed and her body restrained, she couldn't resist—she could only grit her teeth and close her eyes.

"How dare you!" Yang Kai roared. Right in front of him, this bastard was toying with his mother-in-law—how could he tolerate that? He stomped his foot and charged straight at Wu Ming.

Wu Ming's face changed dramatically. He had suffered at Yang Kai's hands before and knew how dangerous he was. He hurriedly retreated with Chi Yue.

Tan Junhao blocked the way, his wide sleeves sweeping out as multicolored blades of light formed and slashed at Yang Kai.

Boom, boom, boom...

A series of explosions erupted as Spiritual Qi went wild. Yang Kai was forced back step by step, but all the multicolored blades shattered.

Tan Junhao narrowed his eyes and couldn't help but praise, "Young friend Yang, you are truly remarkable. I underestimated you."

He had thought that a mere First Stage Emperor could be subdued by the Grand Square Five Elements Array. He never expected Yang Kai to fight the array to a standstill. Such strength shouldn't exist at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

If he ever reached the Third Stage, who but the Great Emperors could be his match in this world?

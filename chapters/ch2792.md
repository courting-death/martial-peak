## Chapter 2792 — Thunder

The Southern Dipper Peak was a secondary peak of Star God Palace’s Star-Moon Mountain, but its cultivation environment was also extremely favorable. The concentration of Spiritual Qi there was in no way inferior to that of a major peak of Lingxiao Palace.

Star God Palace stood at the heart of the Southern Territory, where the Origin Qi of countless cultivators converged. Its fate was intimately tied to that of the entire Southern Territory, and it even suppressed the region’s earth veins. With such abundant Spiritual Qi, it was only natural that cultivation here would be exceptionally favorable.

After arranging for Yang Kai to settle in, Lan Xun left.

For a time, Southern Dipper Peak became tranquil and serene. Yang Kai’s state of mind seemed to be affected as well, becoming incomparably calm. With no one to disturb him, he naturally closed his eyes and sat in meditation, cultivating the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique Zhu Qing had taught him before she left.

In a flash, night arrived.

While cultivating, Yang Kai suddenly sensed something, abruptly opened his eyes, and with a flicker, exited his room to stand atop the Southern Dipper Peak.

What he saw made him involuntarily gape in astonishment.

The crescent moon hung in the sky, stars scattered everywhere. Over the entire Star-Moon Mountain, it was as if a magnificent painting had been unfurled. Every star radiated dazzling light, seemingly within reach.

Most astonishing to Yang Kai was that from each star a beam of brilliant light descended, linking heaven and earth. Those beams carried a mysterious power that made the Spiritual Qi of Star-Moon Mountain even more vibrant. The mountain took on a charm not seen by day, elusive yet subtly resonant with the principles of the Great Dao.

At this moment, the entire Star-Moon Mountain looked like an immortal realm, making one reluctant to leave.

Yang Kai gazed in a daze, even forgetting to breathe, his mind unprecedentedly calm.

“This is the Star Tide. It comes once a month. The Great Emperor said that the might of the Star Tide contains the principles of the Great Dao. If one can glimpse even a little, the benefits are endless. In fact, many disciples of my Star God Palace have gained much from the Star Tide—some break through cultivation bottlenecks, some are struck by inspiration and comprehend divine abilities. Especially for those who cultivate the Xingyue Divine Art, the wonders are even greater.”

A voice suddenly sounded not far away.

“So that’s how it is.” Yang Kai didn’t seem surprised, immersed in the starlight. However, perhaps because he was not a disciple of Star God Palace, although he found the Star Tide quite wondrous and sensed its mysteries, he did not gain any particular benefit.

After a moment, Yang Kai turned his head and looked at a sturdy young man standing with his hands behind his back. “How should I address you?”

The young man turned his head, grinned, and revealed a mouthful of white teeth. “Lei Ting!”

Yang Kai frowned in thought. “I’ve long heard that among the four Great Elders of Star God Palace, the Great Elder Lei Hong is the most outstanding, far surpassing ordinary Third Stage Emperors. What is your relationship with Great Elder Lei?”

Lei Ting curled his lips, seemingly displeased at hearing Lei Hong’s name, but still answered truthfully, “Uncle and nephew!”

Yang Kai let out an ‘oh’. “So you’re Great Elder Lei’s nephew.”

Lei Ting’s gaze sharpened, cold and piercing. “I would rather hear you say that he is my uncle.”

Yang Kai shrugged. “Sorry, I’ve only ever heard of Great Elder Lei’s reputation.”

Lei Ting let out a cold snort, faint arcs of pale blue lightning crackling through his hair. “I’ve spent years in secluded cultivation here at Star God Palace, rarely venturing outside. It’s no wonder you haven’t heard of me. But that’s fine—starting today, you’ll be hearing my name quite often.”

“Then, Brother Lei…” Yang Kai smiled faintly, unimpressed by his boasting. “Visiting so late at night, what business do you have?”

Lei Ting’s eyes drooped, a cold, piercing light flashing from beneath his lashes. “I heard that when you arrived today, Her Highness the Princess personally came out to greet you?”

“That did happen!” Yang Kai nodded.

Lei Ting’s aura suddenly surged as he gritted his teeth. “So, you and Her Highness the Princess are very close, then?”

Yang Kai rubbed his chin, looking a bit troubled. “I wouldn’t say that. We’ve only met a few times, that’s all.” He sighed, then added, “But I think I understand why you’ve come looking for me, Brother Lei.”

“As long as you understand! Come fight me at once. If you lose, get out of Star God Palace immediately and never see Her Highness the Princess again!” Lei Ting roared in anger, his aura of the First Stage of the Emperor Realm boiling over. It was as if Yang Kai standing before him was not a first-time acquaintance, but an enemy with a blood feud.

Yang Kai stared wide-eyed. “Aren’t you picking the wrong opponent? Shouldn’t your enemy be Xiao Chen? He’s the biggest obstacle between you and Her Highness.”

Lei Ting sneered. “Xiao Chen is nothing! If he dares to act up, I’ll beat him so badly even his father won’t recognize him!”

Yang Kai was stunned. Xiao Chen was, after all, a rising star of the Southern Territory and had now advanced to the Emperor Realm. Lei Ting did seem a bit stronger, but he was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. How could he boast so shamelessly?

Still, Yang Kai had truly never heard of this Lei Ting before. He had always thought that this generation of Star God Palace was led by Xiao Chen and Lan Xun, but now it seemed there was another contender.

Thinking back on what Lei Ting had said, Yang Kai quickly understood. If he had spent years in closed-door cultivation at Star God Palace and rarely went out, it was no wonder his reputation was not widespread.

After pondering for a moment, Yang Kai said seriously, “Brother Lei, I think you might have misunderstood me a bit…”

Lei Ting’s confrontational attitude upon arrival did make Yang Kai somewhat displeased, but as a guest, if he really clashed with Lei Ting, it might offend the upper echelons of Star God Palace—especially since Lei Ting’s uncle was Great Elder Lei Hong.

If that ruined his chance to cultivate in the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, it would be a real shame.

Yang Kai had only spoken half a sentence when Lei Ting suddenly barked, “Enough nonsense, come fight!”

As soon as he finished speaking, he raised his hand. A deep purple bolt of lightning crackled and exploded, instantly condensing into a whip of lightning in his hand, which he lashed toward Yang Kai with tremendous force, as if splitting heaven and earth.

The rest of Yang Kai’s words were stuck in his throat, and his expression turned serious.

From Lei Ting’s first move, Yang Kai could tell that this guy was indeed stronger than Xiao Chen, and even stronger than any First Stage Emperor he had seen before. In fact, he was not at all inferior to an ordinary Second Stage Emperor.

And this was when Lei Ting wasn’t even going all out. If he did, even Second Stage Emperors might not be his match.

He truly was something else—no wonder he dared to come looking for trouble.

As the lightning whip struck, Yang Kai summoned the Myriad Sword, holding it horizontally in front of him.

With a clang, sparks flew everywhere. Although the whip’s blow was blocked, the terrifying current traveled along the Myriad Sword to Yang Kai’s hand. In an instant, electric snakes danced across his skin, and every hair on his body stood on end.

With a jolt of Emperor Yuan, he expelled the invading electricity from his body.

Before he could recover, the lightning whip struck again. In midair, its tip twisted and transformed, suddenly turning into a giant lightning python. Its bloody maw opened wide, lightning crackling within, and it bit down at Yang Kai’s head with terrifying momentum.

Yang Kai formed a hand seal with one hand and flicked his finger, sending out a Moon Blade.

The Moon Blade sliced through the python’s head, splitting it cleanly in two.

A surprised exclamation rang out. Lei Ting was clearly shocked. He had cultivated this lightning secret art for years and knew its power well. Ordinary First Stage Emperors could never break it so easily, yet Yang Kai had split it in two with a casual strike.

Moreover, that divine ability gave off a strange feeling, as if it contained spatial power.

As the lightning python dissipated, Lei Ting had already taken advantage of its cover to close in. In a flash of lightning, he was within three feet of Yang Kai, raising a palm as large as a fan. Electricity surged in his palm, crackling as he brought it down on Yang Kai’s head.

“That’s enough!” Yang Kai shouted angrily, pointing directly at Lei Ting’s palm.

If that finger landed, it would definitely pierce Lei Ting’s palm. Yang Kai might get hurt as well, but not seriously.

Just as their finger and palm were about to collide, Lei Ting suddenly shuddered, a hint of panic flashing across his face as he turned to look in another direction.

The descending palm froze in midair.

He didn’t move, and Yang Kai also stopped, his fingertip pressing against Lei Ting’s palm, power gathered but not released.

“Damn Xiao Chen, ruining my plans!” Lei Ting suddenly cursed, withdrew his divine ability, and turned to leave.

After only a few steps, he turned back, staring intently at Yang Kai. “I was never here tonight. You and I never met, and we definitely didn’t fight! Got it?”

Yang Kai looked up at the sky and said leisurely, “The night is quite enchanting.”

“You…” Lei Ting wanted to say more, but it was clearly too late. He glared at Yang Kai, then his figure flickered and he vanished in a flash of lightning.

Less than three breaths after he left, a rainbow light landed on Southern Dipper Peak. Lan Xun looked at Yang Kai and asked, “Senior Brother Yang, are you alright?”

“I’m fine, what could possibly happen?” Yang Kai smiled. “The night scenery at Star God Palace is quite nice. I just came out for some fresh air.”

“I heard Lei Ting came by. He didn’t give you any trouble, did he?”

“Who’s Lei Ting?” Yang Kai looked at Lan Xun in confusion, feigning ignorance, but his hair standing on end betrayed him.

Lan Xun glanced at him and smiled.

Yang Kai casually smoothed his hair and said lightly, “The night wind messed up my hairstyle.”

Lan Xun said, “That Lei Ting has cultivated himself silly. He’s a bit unreasonable. Senior Brother Yang, if you see him next time, let me know right away. I’ll deal with him.”

Yang Kai paused for a moment, then nodded. “Alright.”

“Then, Senior Brother Yang, you go ahead and enjoy the night breeze. I’ll take my leave now.” Lan Xun covered her mouth with a delicate laugh, turned, and gracefully departed.

“Lei Ting…” Yang Kai stroked his chin, pondered for a while, then suddenly smiled.

Not far from the side of Southern Dipper Peak, Lei Ting grabbed Xiao Chen by the collar and growled, “It was you, wasn’t it? It had to be you! If you hadn’t told Sister Xun, how could she have shown up at just the right time? You bastard, if you’d given me a little more time, I could have driven that guy out of Star God Palace!”

“Let go of me if you want to talk! What kind of behavior is this, grabbing and pulling at people?” Xiao Chen lowered his eyes, staring at Lei Ting’s large hand with a look of utter disdain.

## Chapter 2653 — Don’t Come Begging Me Later

The Wind and Thunder Divine Spear was a relic left behind by Great Emperor Wenqing in the past, so it was naturally extraordinary.

However, Yang Kai now possessed countless rare treasures, so he didn’t think much of it. He put it away, planning to see if there would be a chance to gift it to someone in the future.

“Go help Ice Heart Valley clean up the aftermath.” Yang Kai turned his head and instructed the three Yao Kings.

The three Yao Kings said nothing and flickered away.

Within Ice Heart Valley, waves of battle of varying intensity continued to erupt, occasionally accompanied by miserable screams. Judging by the sounds, they were mostly men, likely the invading enemies being killed.

There were more than thirty corpses of Emperor Realm experts on the ground. No one would have thought that just an incense stick ago, these thirty-some people were still alive and kicking, but in such a short time, they had all perished.

Yang Kai collected each of their Spatial Rings.

“Young Master Yang, Young Lord!”

At that moment, a shout came from the side.

Yang Kai turned to look and said indifferently, “What is it, Grandmaster Nanmen? You enjoyed quite the show and still haven’t left. Don’t tell me you want to stay in Ice Heart Valley as a guest? Ice Heart Valley has never welcomed men. Grandmaster Nanmen, you’d best take your leave soon.”

Nanmen Dajun had been watching from the sidelines. Neither Ice Heart Valley nor Wenqing Sect had any intention of making things difficult for him, so he remained safe and sound until now.

Hearing the warning in Yang Kai’s words, he just chuckled and said, “Aren’t you a man too, Young Lord?”

Yang Kai sneered, “How could you compare to me?”

Such shameless boasting didn’t bother Nanmen Dajun at all. He showed none of the arrogance one would expect from a legendary formation grandmaster. After all, from Ice Heart Valley’s perspective, he truly couldn’t compare to Yang Kai. The only reason Ice Heart Valley survived was thanks to Yang Kai. For a Sect, that was a life-saving grace. Rubbing his hands, he said, “Young Lord, I’d like to ask you something…”

“No need!” Yang Kai snorted coldly, not interested in talking to him at all.

Although Nanmen Dajun was a formation grandmaster, Yang Kai’s own skills were not lacking—he was an Imperial-Grade Alchemist, after all. There was no need to lower himself to give face to others, especially since much of Ice Heart Valley’s crisis this time was due to this man. Not killing him was already merciful, yet he still wanted to pry for information.

“Young Lord, don’t be so heartless.” Nanmen Dajun gave a dry laugh, showing a stubborn will. “It’s just a few questions. If you can tell me, I’ll be sure to reward you handsomely.”

Without waiting for Yang Kai to refuse, he suddenly lowered his voice and said, “Young Lord, those three Yao Kings… they’re from that place in the Eastern Territory, aren’t they?”

As soon as he said this, Yang Kai’s gaze turned cold, and he stared sharply at Nanmen Dajun. “For someone your age, you sure speak recklessly. Watch your mouth, or disaster may follow.”

Xi Lei and the other two Yao Kings were brought out from the Wild Ancient Land by him. Although there weren’t many of them, if word got out that the three Yao Kings had left the mountains, it would likely arouse the displeasure of human experts, especially since they came from a place like the Wild Ancient Land.

The suspicious might even wonder if the yao of the Wild Ancient Land couldn’t bear their loneliness and had come out to wreak havoc on human Sects. If that happened, it would be a real headache for Yang Kai.

“So they really are from that place?” Nanmen Dajun ignored Yang Kai’s threat and instead looked delighted, cupping his fists solemnly. “Rest assured, Young Lord, only the heavens, the earth, you, and I know of this. I absolutely won’t spread it.”

“Hmph!” Yang Kai looked at him with clear displeasure, even considering whether he should silence him.

Nanmen Dajun might seem reckless, but he was actually quite perceptive. The moment Yang Kai’s killing intent surfaced, he sensed it and immediately grew nervous, hastily saying, “Young Lord, please calm down. I’m just interested in the many ancient restrictions and natural formations hidden in that place. I’ve gone in and out a few times to study them, but my strength is lacking, so I never got far. It’s a real regret! Your relationship with those three lords seems… rather close. Could you perhaps…”

“I have no idea what you’re talking about. Don’t bring this up again!” Yang Kai interrupted him bluntly.

After hearing Nanmen Dajun out, Yang Kai understood his intent—he just wanted to use Yang Kai to get close to Xi Lei and the others, so he could enter the Wild Ancient Land in the future to study those ancient restrictions and formations.

He was a formation grandmaster, uninterested in anything else, but anything related to formations drew him like a moth to a flame.

Nanmen Dajun opened his mouth, as if wanting to say more, but in the end, he just sighed heavily, comforting himself that there would be opportunities in the future.

Changing the subject, he said, “Since you don’t want to talk about that, I’ll drop it. But there’s something else I’d like your advice on—it’s about the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation.”

“Oh?” Yang Kai was surprised. “You even know about the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation?”

Bing Yun and the others had previously set up the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation here, so Nanmen Dajun had seen it clearly. Yang Kai hadn’t expected his eyesight to be so sharp—he could even recognize a secret technique of the Black Tortoise Sect lost for tens of thousands of years. It seemed his reputation as a formation grandmaster was well-deserved, not just empty fame.

Though if he were just a fame-seeker with no real skill, there’s no way he could have broken through Ice Heart Valley’s Grand Guardian Array.

“You flatter me, Young Lord. This is my field, after all.” Nanmen Dajun chuckled. “With such a famous formation appearing before my eyes, if I couldn’t recognize it, I might as well be blind.”

“What about the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation? Do you want the method to set it up?” Yang Kai looked at him with interest.

Nanmen Dajun rubbed his hands, embarrassed. “If there’s a chance, of course I’d like to study it…”

“Hmph!” Yang Kai sneered. “Don’t even think about it.”

Nanmen Dajun was taken aback. “Why not?”

“No reason. Just forget it.” Yang Kai waved his hand.

Nanmen Dajun said angrily, “Don’t be so absolute, Young Lord. Senior Bing Yun is kind-hearted. I believe that as long as I show enough sincerity, she might not refuse me.”

He had spoken politely to Yang Kai before because he felt guilty and had other requests. But now, he just wanted to study the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation, yet Yang Kai refused him outright.

This was simply too much! Why should Yang Kai refuse him so decisively? The Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation was set up by Ice Heart Valley’s people. He only wanted Yang Kai to put in a good word. If he didn’t help, fine, but why crush his hopes entirely?

A peerless formation lost for tens of thousands of years had suddenly appeared. How could he just give up? If he couldn’t get his hands on the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation, how could he focus on studying formations in the future?

“Oh, throwing a tantrum?” Yang Kai looked at him with a smile.

“Hmph, I won’t talk to you anymore. I’ll go find Senior Bing Yun.” Nanmen Dajun flung his sleeve, looking displeased as he turned to leave.

Yang Kai sneered, “Go ahead, but don’t come begging me later.”

Nanmen Dajun really wanted to retort that he’d never beg Yang Kai, but thinking of the Wild Ancient Land, he held back. He was still obsessed with the restrictions and formations there. If Yang Kai could put in a good word, he wouldn’t have to fear the yao of the Ancient Land causing him trouble and could study to his heart’s content. Maybe he could even make a breakthrough in the Dao of Formations.

But this young man was hard to talk to, which left Nanmen Dajun both troubled and conflicted.

After Nanmen Dajun left, Yang Kai finally released his Divine Sense to scan the situation within Ice Heart Valley.

The outcome was just as he expected. The more than thirty Emperor Realm experts from Wenqing Sect had been completely wiped out. Earlier, Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei had joined forces to slaughter over ten thousand people. Although many enemies remained, they were now leaderless and scattered.

Soon after, the high-level members of Ice Heart Valley arrived, and the battle quickly turned one-sided.

At this moment, Ice Heart Valley was already cleaning up the aftermath. It wouldn’t be long before everything was settled.

With a flicker, Yang Kai flew toward the forbidden area of the ice lake. Along the way, he casually killed quite a few people, leaving corpses in his wake.

Moments later, he appeared in the original forbidden area of the ice lake within Ice Heart Valley.

He looked around and softly whistled.

From behind a nearby hill, an old man and a young girl cautiously poked their heads out. When they saw it was Yang Kai, their faces lit up with joy and they hurried over to greet him.

This old and young pair were, of course, Old Ban and his granddaughter, who had come to Ice Heart Valley with Yang Kai.

Buzzzzz…

A strange sound rang out, and following the two figures, a swarm of about a hundred pitch-black insects flew toward Yang Kai.

Soul-Devouring Demonic Worms!

Yang Kai took out the Slave-Insect Bracelet and, with a flick of his wrist, collected the Soul-Devouring Demonic Worms back into it. Only then did he turn to Old Ban and Ling’er with a gentle smile. “No one found this place, did they?”

Old Ban shook his head. “No, thanks to Young Master Yang’s careful arrangements.”

As he spoke, he glanced at the Slave-Insect Bracelet in Yang Kai’s hand, a faint look of fear on his face.

Although he didn’t know what those pitch-black insects were, each one gave him a sense of dread. Old Ban also knew that since Yang Kai had left those insects to guard them, he must have absolute confidence in their strength.

With the Soul-Devouring Demonic Worms guarding them, Yang Kai naturally had no worries. Over a hundred of these insects together could even fight a First Stage Emperor Realm expert. Now that all the Emperor Realm experts of Wenqing Sect were dead, even if someone stumbled upon Old Ban and his granddaughter and meant them harm, they would only end up killed by the Soul-Devouring Demonic Worms.

“Young Master Yang, things are chaotic outside. Don’t you need to help? Ling’er and I can just hide in here, there’s no need to worry too much.” Old Ban glanced worriedly toward Ice Heart Valley. Although he had been hiding in the hill, he could still hear the commotion and shouts of battle, so he knew a great battle was taking place.

Yang Kai smiled and shook his head. “No need, it’s almost over. Just wait a little longer and it’ll all be settled.”

He lowered his head and gently patted Ling’er’s head, smiling. “Do you like this place? From now on, this will be your Sect.”

Ling’er blinked and said crisply, “I like it, but it’s a little cold.”

Yang Kai laughed, “This is Ice Heart Valley. It’s supposed to be cold. You’ll get used to it.”

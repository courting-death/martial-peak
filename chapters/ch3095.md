## Chapter 3095 — Failing to Steal the Chicken, Losing the Rice Instead

With a boom, violent force struck Yang Kai's chest.

Yan Qing stared straight ahead, not even sparing him a glance. Although the strike just now had only been casual, his foundation at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm was there. If someone with not the slightest trace of Spiritual Qi anywhere on his body took such a hit, how could he possibly come to a good end?

Being smashed to pieces would be lucky; being reduced to dust would be normal.

His whole body suddenly trembled, and he turned his head in disbelief, only to see the young man over there casually raise a hand and brush off the spot on his chest where he had been struck. Then he grinned and strolled toward him.

What the hell?

Yan Qing almost thought his eyes were playing tricks on him. How could he be unharmed?

In disbelief, he released his Divine Sense and examined Yang Kai again, confirming without doubt that there was not the slightest trace of cultivation on him. This fellow was practically no different from ordinary people.

Frowning, Yan Qing swept out another palm.

This palm already carried thirty percent of his strength, with some intent to test him.

The young man walking toward him neither dodged nor avoided, allowing that fierce attack to strike his chest. He did not so much as sway, as if a fresh breeze had brushed his face, looking utterly at ease.

Yan Qing was greatly alarmed. How could he not realize he had misjudged him? To withstand two of his palms without using any power at all, could this person be...

"You are a body refiner!"

In this world, there was an exceedingly rare group who did not cultivate the power of their Meridians, only their flesh bodies. Rumour had it that they too could reach the peak of the Martial Dao, ascending by tempering their fleshly bodies. But no one could verify this claim, because compared with ordinary cultivation, this path was as narrow as a sheep-gut trail. For countless years, no one had specialized in cultivating the flesh body; only in the distant ancient era had such records existed.

Was this person He Yunxiang's backing? The thought surfaced unbidden in Yan Qing's mind.

If this person truly was a body refiner, he would absolutely not be easy to deal with. Body refiners cultivated only the flesh body, possessed extremely tenacious vitality, and were very hard to kill. Moreover, though this path was covered in thorns and extremely arduous to walk, once one achieved something on it, the strength one could display was terrifying to the extreme. Among those of the same level, almost no one could match them.

When Yan Qing struck out with his third palm, he was already holding nothing back.

He wanted to see just how far this body refiner had grown.

A howling wind rang out, and the power in his palm gathered into a vortex. A horrifying aura surged out as his palm descended.

Boom...

Energy churned, and the inside of the great hall seemed to turn upside down. A cyan palm print, visible to the naked eye, blasted against Yang Kai's chest.

Yan Qing's eyes widened until he wished he could pluck them out and set them on that young man to look more clearly.

The result left him both shocked and terrified. Even a full-powered strike still could not shake that young man in the slightest. It had not even left a crease in his clothes.

He was absolutely no match!

In that moment of distraction, another person had already appeared before his eyes.

The young man who had been more than ten zhang away suddenly appeared before him, as if he could shift positions in an instant.

"Hah!" Yan Qing's reaction was also extremely swift. As he opened his mouth and shouted, a burst of cyan light suddenly shot out, flying straight toward Yang Kai's completely undefended eyes. It was a three-inch-long needle, an Upper-Level Void King-Grade Secret Treasure, shadowless and formless, impossible to guard against.

Long-needle Secret Treasures were not common and were difficult to refine, but they often possessed tremendous power. It was also one of Yan Qing's strongest killing moves.

Yet this strongest killing move did not bring Yan Qing the slightest sense of safety. After one strike, he immediately tried to retreat, wanting to open up the distance between himself and that young man.

His body suddenly stiffened. A large hand clamped over his neck and squeezed until he could not breathe. The instant he was seized, Yan Qing went limp like a dead dog, and the Saint Yuan in his body no longer obeyed his commands.

With a clang, the long-needle Secret Treasure fell to the ground.

Yan Qing was horrified beyond measure. The young man before him had merely blown out a breath and broken his killing move.

How was this possible? How could this be possible? He must be dreaming, right? Ha ha ha ha!

With a crashing clatter, all the doors and windows of the great hall shattered, and a dozen or so people filed inside.

The commotion inside naturally could not be hidden from the perception of the experts outside, so the moment they sensed traces of a fight, they immediately rushed in, each one exuding the aura of the Void King Realm.

The one in the lead was an elderly man in his eighties or nineties, with grizzled hair but hale spirits. He was also at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and his aura was even a notch stronger than Yan Qing's.

The Yan Family's Great Elder, Yan An!

The long sword in his hand spun out blossoms of sword light. The sword tip wavered, then suddenly snapped perfectly straight. Countless sword rays gathered into a single point of light and stabbed straight toward Yang Kai's back.

Killing intent surged as if there were no one else around! His cultivation in the Sword Dao was displayed to the fullest at this moment, making those who watched gasp in admiration and become absorbed in it, as if drawn into an endless world of sword cultivators.

Chi...

The long sword stabbed in through the back, encountering no obstruction at all, and pierced straight out through the front of the chest.

He had succeeded!

The sensation transmitted through the long sword in his hand told Yan An that his strike had not missed. It was the unique feeling of piercing flesh and blood. The scent of blood spread, and the many Yan Family Void King Realm cultivators who followed in afterward all showed joyful expressions.

"Ah!" He Yunxiang cried out in alarm.

She never would have imagined that Yang Kai, who in her eyes was an invincible existence, would actually fail to withstand even this sword from the Yan Family's Great Elder. Earlier, when Yan An had broken through the door and entered, she had seen it too, but because she had complete confidence in Yang Kai, she had not moved to block him at all. She had merely stood to the side, smiling as she watched the show.

Then she saw a scene she could not believe.

What was going on? Impossible. When dealing with Second Elder Yan Qing, Yang Kai had displayed absolutely overwhelming strength and directly crushed him. How could Great Elder Yan An run him through with one sword? Even with his back turned, he should not have made such a low-level mistake.

"Overestimating yourself!" Yan An coldly snorted, and the long sword in his hand suddenly released an extremely icy aura, freezing Yang Kai's back. A layer of frost rapidly spread outward from the wound.

“Great Elder, you… Pu…” Yan Qing’s panicked voice rang out, filled with grief and indignation. As he opened his mouth to speak, blood sprayed from his mouth.

Yan An raised his head and looked at Yan Qing, only to see the latter wearing an eerie grin.

Yan An frowned. What was Second Elder doing? Why was he smiling at him like that? Just as suspicion rose in his heart, that familiar face before him actually twisted and changed, transforming into the appearance of an unfamiliar young man.

"What?" Yan An was greatly alarmed.

If the one facing him was that young man, then who was the person he had run through with his sword?

He no longer dared think any further. Cold sweat drenched his forehead. When he fixed his eyes and looked, his soul nearly fled his body. A familiar figure, familiar clothes, familiar height and hair bun...

Who else could the one he had run through be but Second Elder?

What was going on! What exactly was going on? He had clearly attacked that young man and had already succeeded, so why had the result become like this? Even though the Great Elder's cultivation was not weak and his temperament was steady, at this moment he could not help losing his composure.

"Illusion!" He Yunxiang's beautiful eyes lit up, and her heart immediately settled. She knew it; how could this Sir be so easy to deal with?

So it turned out that in that instant just now, everyone inside the great hall had fallen into a profound illusion technique and mistaken Yang Kai and Second Elder Yan Qing for each other. Without leaving any trace at all, it could make so many Void King Realm cultivators sink into an illusory reality and be unable to extricate themselves. Such an ability could only be called terrifying.

At Yang Kai's left eye, a point of golden light withdrew, and the surging Divine Soul power also calmed. Clicking his tongue, he said, "Great Elder really can bring himself to strike. I truly do not know what grudge or hatred there is between Great Elder and Second Elder. Could it be that he cuckolded you, or perhaps killed your old mother? Great Elder actually used this opportunity to avenge a private grudge under public cover and struck so ruthlessly. This Young Master has no choice but to say to you: well done! Men of our generation should settle gratitude and grudges as we please, cleanly and decisively. Great Elder truly is a model for us all."

"You..." Yan An was furious and reached out to pull the long sword free.

"Pu..." Yan Qing spat another mouthful of blood, which Yang Kai easily avoided. His face was deathly pale and utterly bloodless. If he did not know the Great Elder's character so well, he might really have thought the man had something against him and had taken the chance to retaliate.

Stabbing me once was one thing, but why did you suddenly pull it out without a word? The pain tore at his heart and lungs, and there was a bone-deep chill that almost froze him stiff. Yan Qing knew that was Yan An's Mysterious Yin Cold Qi, extremely tyrannical and vicious.

"I will kill you!" Rage attacked Yan An's heart. His hair and beard flew wildly as he raised his sword and stabbed toward Yang Kai.

Because of this young man before him, his lifetime of illustrious fame had been destroyed in one stroke. The crime of murdering a clansman and brother was not light. After today, his reputation would certainly plummet. What face or qualification would he still have to serve as Great Elder? He would probably have to cast off his armor and return to the fields, retiring into seclusion in his old age.

Facing this furious strike from Yan An, a veteran Third Stage Void King Realm master, Yang Kai merely smiled faintly and reached out to pinch.

Zheng...

The lingering note was clear and pleasant to the ear.

Yan An's belly full of rage seemed to be doused by a basin of cold water, extinguished in an instant. All that remained was fear and horror.

When he fixed his eyes on it, he saw that the young man was holding Yan Qing's neck with one hand while casually raising his other hand to pinch. His Secret Treasure long sword had actually been caught steadily between the young man's fingers. What he had pinched seemed not to be a Void King-Grade Secret Treasure long sword that had killed countless enemies, but a light, fluttering fallen leaf.

Yan An's heart shook wildly, and he could not conceal the shock in his eyes. He hurriedly poured all his cultivation into the long sword, advanced instead of retreating, and stabbed down again.

Yang Kai's eyes were full of ridicule. He bent a finger and gently flicked the long sword. It immediately let out a clear sword cry and bloomed with specks of cold light.

The long sword nearly flew from his hand. Yan An had to exert a great deal of strength before he managed to keep his grip on the hilt, but the long sword's momentum was already beyond his control and swept straight off to the side.

Chi...

With a soft sound, the long sword pierced Yan Qing's chest from the front, stabbing into his chest and running through his heart!

"Uh..." Yan Qing's eyes instantly bulged round, and a weak breath escaped his throat. With great difficulty, he moved his eyes, fixed his gaze on Yan An, opened his mouth, and used all the strength in his body to spit out a few words: "...fuck your mother!"

His head lolled to the side, and his aura vanished completely.

Everyone was dumbfounded. Yan An had nearly lost the ability to think, his mind completely blank, and his lips trembled fiercely several times.

## Chapter 2780 — Small But Complete

Although the effects of setting up a Space Array would not be apparent in the short term, in the long run, it would have a tremendous impact on both sects.

This was also one of the reasons why Yang Kai had agreed to become an Elder of the Qingyang Shrine in the first place. As long as he became an Elder, he would have his own Spirit Peak, making it much easier to set things up.

But now, after being tricked by Gao Xueting like this, Yang Kai had no choice but to reconsider.

If Gao Xueting were to find out about this, Ji Ying might very well become the Shrine’s exclusive Alchemist in the future, and Lingxiao Palace would have no chance to get involved at all.

For now, it was best to proceed quietly. When the time was right, he could reveal everything.

Half a day later, a brand new Space Array was completed. With the Elder Jade Token mobilizing the power of Spirit Sword Peak’s Earth Vein to conceal it, unless Yang Kai himself wished it, even if Wen Zishan came here, he would not be able to discover it.

After embedding Source Crystals into the array and channeling the power of the Laws of Space, a flash of light appeared, and Yang Kai vanished.

The next moment, Yang Kai’s figure appeared atop the Space Array in Lingxiao Palace.

A short while later, on the main peak of Medicine Pill Peak in Lingxiao Palace, Yang Kai found Ji Ying.

Upon meeting again, Ji Ying was extremely surprised. “Brother Yang, you’ve returned so quickly?”

Yang Kai sighed helplessly, accepting the tea offered by the senior disciple. He sat down, looking dispirited. “Let’s not talk about it.”

Ji Ying smiled. Although he didn’t know what Yang Kai had encountered, he could tell his mood was poor. Casually, he took out a jade bottle and tossed it over.

Yang Kai caught it, asking suspiciously, “What is this?”

“Dragon Blood Pill,” Ji Ying explained. “It was refined using that Dragon Blood Flower from last time as the main ingredient. It should be of some use to Brother Yang.”

“Oh? This is the Dragon Blood Pill?” Yang Kai was extremely surprised. He opened the jade bottle and poured out three blood-red Spiritual Pills, each about the size of a fingernail. They emitted a bloody scent, but it was not pungent. Instead, it made one’s blood boil.

Judging by their appearance, these Dragon Blood Pills should be Imperial-Grade Lower-Level Spiritual Pills.

“It was my first time refining this kind of Spiritual Pill, so I was inevitably a bit unpracticed. In the end, only three pills were formed. I hope Brother Yang won’t mind,” Ji Ying said apologetically.

Yang Kai laughed loudly. “Brother Ji, you’re too modest. Three pills is already an impressive result.”

Ji Ying was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master and a direct disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, but to be able to produce three pills on his first attempt at refining this kind of Spiritual Pill was enough to show his profound alchemy skills. An ordinary Imperial-Grade Pill Master could never have done as well as Ji Ying.

Ji Ying waved his hand repeatedly, smiling bitterly. “Brother Yang flatters me.”

In front of Yang Kai, who had once defeated him in a fair alchemy contest, Ji Ying truly could not act proud.

After a pause, Ji Ying said, “But back to the point. Now that I’ve come to Lingxiao Palace, what exactly does Brother Yang want me to do? A few days ago, I went to see Chief Steward Hua, but she only told me to rest for a while and didn’t assign me any specific tasks. Now that I’m the Chief Alchemist of Lingxiao Palace, I can’t just idle away here, can I? I have no other skills but alchemy.”

Yang Kai smiled. “What I want is precisely your alchemy skills, Brother Ji. Take a look at this.”

As he spoke, Yang Kai tossed over the ring that Gao Xueting had given him.

Ji Ying caught it. Sweeping it with his Divine Sense, he was slightly stunned. “So many Imperial-Grade Spiritual Medicines.”

Yang Kai said, “Refine them as you see fit, Brother Ji. There’s no rush—just go with the flow.”

“Alright, I won’t let you down.”

“There’s one more thing I’d like to ask, Brother Ji.”

“Please, go ahead.”

Yang Kai’s expression grew solemn. “I wonder how much Brother Ji knows about the Life Origin Pill?”

“Life Origin Pill?” Ji Ying’s expression became serious, and his breathing quickened slightly. “Could it be… Brother Yang, do you have a Life Origin Fruit?”

Yang Kai thought to himself that he possessed far more than just a Life Origin Fruit—he even had an entire Life Origin Fruit Tree. However, the ripe fruits from that tree had already been plucked, and the remaining ones would likely need a long time yet before they could mature.

He didn’t hide it. He simply reached out and took out a Life Origin Fruit.

Ji Ying’s eyes lit up. He quickly stood, carefully took the Life Origin Fruit, and examined it closely with his Divine Sense. The more he looked, the more delighted he became, nodding repeatedly. “It really is a Life Origin Fruit, exactly as described in the ancient texts. Wasn’t this thing supposed to be extinct? How could it appear now?”

As he observed, he scratched his head, clearly unable to put the Life Origin Fruit down.

“So-called extinct things are simply those that haven’t been discovered. I obtained this by chance.”

Yang Kai had more than just a single so-called extinct item like the Life Origin Fruit, so he understood this well.

Ji Ying suddenly looked up. “Brother Yang, do you want to refine a Life Origin Pill?”

“Exactly.” Yang Kai nodded, looking a bit troubled. “I originally planned to refine it myself. I’ve gathered most of the materials, but I still feel uneasy. If the refinement fails, there won’t be a second Life Origin Fruit in this world. So I wanted to ask Brother Ji—do you have confidence in succeeding?”

Ji Ying frowned in thought, carefully considering for a long while before saying, “Less than thirty percent.”

Thirty percent—such slim odds. Even Ji Ying himself only had a thirty percent chance of success; Yang Kai might fare a bit better, but not by much. Thinking about it this way, it was indeed wise not to have rashly attempted refining the Life Origin Fruit before.

“Could we ask the Great Emperor to help?” Yang Kai asked solemnly.

“My Master?” Ji Ying looked up, thought for a moment, then suddenly smiled. “My Master hasn’t refined pills for three thousand years, but if it’s for a Life Origin Fruit… he might make an exception. I’ll need to return to Medicine Pill Valley and report this to my Master. As for whether he’ll agree to help, that will depend on Heaven’s Will.”

“Then I’ll trouble you, Brother Ji. There’s no rush—go back whenever you have time.”

“I’ll go now.” Ji Ying stood up. It seemed that the appearance of the Life Origin Fruit was quite tempting even for a Great Emperor, or else Ji Ying wouldn’t be in such a hurry.

Yang Kai naturally didn’t try to stop him.

After sending Ji Ying off, Yang Kai found Hua Qingsi and discussed the matter of the Mountain Wave Diagram with her.

As expected, Hua Qingsi knew about the Mountain Wave Diagram, but although she knew of it, she didn’t know how to set it up. After all, she had only advanced to the Emperor Realm in recent years, and many things were simply out of reach before reaching that level.

“Nanmen Dajun should know, right?” Hua Qingsi suddenly thought of someone. The Mountain Wave Diagram should be a miniature formation, and as an Imperial-Grade Formation Master, Nanmen Dajun would surely understand it.

“Summon him here.”

Hua Qingsi immediately took out a Transmission Compass and sent a message to Nanmen Dajun.

In less than the time it takes to drink a cup of tea, Nanmen Dajun appeared before them.

“Palace Master, Chief Steward, you were looking for me?” Nanmen Dajun seemed to be doing quite well these days. His face was glowing, and he looked nothing like his former dejected self.

Previously, he had been lured by Yang Kai’s knowledge of formations and had no choice but to agree to join Lingxiao Palace for ten years. In truth, Nanmen Dajun had been quite unwilling, but as time passed, he seemed to have accepted his fate.

Especially after Yang Kai entrusted him with full authority over all the formations in Lingxiao Palace, Nanmen Dajun gradually adapted to his role as Chief Imperial-Grade Formation Master. He had been inspecting, repairing, and perfecting the Palace’s formations everywhere these days, spending countless resources. This extravagant feeling made him inexplicably happy.

In the past, he had always been alone. While that meant freedom, his finances were limited. Many profound and exquisite formations were simply impossible for him to set up, no matter how much he wanted to. But now, with the entire Lingxiao Palace backing him, those grand formations that had only existed in theory were coming to life one by one under his hands.

Nothing could make Nanmen Dajun happier than this.

He suddenly realized that joining Lingxiao Palace might not have been such a bad choice after all.

Of course, the most important thing was freedom!

Although he was now the Chief Formation Master of Lingxiao Palace, basically no one managed him. He was free to do as he pleased. These days, he had been living a carefree life, and he even had a grand blueprint in his heart. He just needed time to set up a world-shaking grand formation in Lingxiao Palace. By then, perhaps even a Great Emperor’s attack could be defended against.

As for attacks from the Emperors, as long as the grand formation was activated, they would all be left helpless outside.

This dream was not impossible to realize, as long as Lingxiao Palace had enough financial resources to support his actions.

Did Lingxiao Palace have such resources? Nanmen Dajun thought it did. That cross-domain Space Array was a huge source of wealth.

So as soon as he heard Yang Kai summon him, he immediately dropped what he was doing and rushed over without delay.

Hua Qingsi said, “The Palace Master wants to set up a Mountain Wave Diagram in the Palace. Can you do it?”

“Chief Steward, are you looking down on me?” Nanmen Dajun glanced sideways at Hua Qingsi, snorting hot air from his nostrils. “If I couldn’t even handle something like the Mountain Wave Diagram, how could I call myself a Formation Master?”

Yang Kai nodded in satisfaction. “Very good. Then I’ll leave this matter to you and Chief Steward Hua.”

Nanmen Dajun suddenly scratched his face. “But the Mountain Wave Diagram isn’t something I can do alone.”

“Why not?” Yang Kai frowned. Just now, this guy had acted all high and mighty, but now he was backing out. What was the meaning of this?

Nanmen Dajun said, “It’s like this, Palace Master. The Mountain Wave Diagram is simple and complicated at the same time. It doesn’t just require a Formation Master, but also an artificer. Only by working together can it be set up.”

“An artificer?”

“That’s right. Strictly speaking, the Mountain Wave Diagram is a kind of Secret Treasure, so it naturally requires an artificer. And the requirements for the artificer are very high. An ordinary, unskilled artificer simply couldn’t make one. At the very least, they’d need to be Dao Origin-Grade Upper-Level.”

“That’s quite a high bar.” Yang Kai stroked his chin in thought, feeling a bit troubled.

The thing was, although Lingxiao Palace was small, it had everything: alchemists, formation masters, even puppet masters. The only thing it lacked was an artificer.

Nanmen Dajun’s words made Yang Kai realize a problem he could not ignore—should Lingxiao Palace recruit or train its own artificers?

For a great sect, all kinds of talents were indispensable.

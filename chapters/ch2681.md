## Chapter 2681 — Let's Be Friends

Now that she had taken the initiative to admit it, even though Yang Kai had been somewhat prepared, he was still utterly shocked.

The Dragon Clan—lords of all living things. Although that title might be a bit self-proclaimed, it was not far from the truth.

In ancient times, countless Divine Spirits roamed the world, yet even among them, the Dragon and Phoenix reigned supreme. Even now, though most Divine Spirits have long since vanished, the Dragon Clan still commands an island in the Eastern Sea, thriving and multiplying.

One could say that this sacred and powerful race had witnessed the changes of heaven and earth, had seen the passage of endless years. The Dragon Clan was not just a name, but a symbol of inheritance and might!

Yang Kai possessed the Origin Power of the Golden Sacred Dragon within his body, so when facing Zhu Qing, a living Dragon Clan member, he did not feel much restraint or fear. Instead, he felt a strange sense of closeness.

That sense of closeness quickly turned into greed and desire. He began to calculate: if he could truly subdue this dragon girl and make her his exclusive possession... tsk tsk, what more could a man ask for in life?

"How did you know?" Zhu Qing asked again.

Yang Kai smiled faintly and pinched his fingers together as if performing a divination. "I am well-versed in the arts of fortune-telling and spirit communication. With just a simple calculation..."

Zhu Qing glared at him in annoyance, thinking, Only a fool would believe you. Still, she had revealed quite a bit during her previous entanglement with Yang Kai, so it was not surprising that he could deduce her Dragon Clan identity.

"It's not a big deal if you know, but I must ask you to keep it a secret. The identity of the Dragon Clan is simply... far too shocking for the world to bear."

Although there were rumors that in the Eastern Sea of the Eastern Territory, there was a Dragon Isle where many Dragon Clan members lived, that was just hearsay—no one knew the details. If a living dragon girl were exposed to the world, it would cause unimaginable upheaval.

"No problem," Yang Kai agreed readily. "I'm not the kind of person who likes to gossip."

Zhu Qing looked grateful, thinking that when this man spoke properly, he was actually not so bad. Maybe she had been too prejudiced, so no matter how she looked at him, she found his face annoying. Perhaps it was time to change her opinion of him.

"But what benefits can you give me?" Yang Kai suddenly changed the subject, his predatory gaze once again sweeping over Zhu Qing's sensitive areas, as if a giant hand were stripping her bare.

Zhu Qing took a deep breath, slowly closed her eyes, and her body trembled lightly.

How naive of me to think this man was decent!

"What... do... you... want... in... return!" Zhu Qing opened her eyes and asked through gritted teeth, finding his face more and more detestable.

"That depends on what you can offer," Yang Kai propped his face on his hand and looked at her leisurely.

He instinctively felt something was off. Ever since he met Zhu Qing today, the suppressed and long-forgotten desires in his heart kept surging up. After they parted, the feeling disappeared, but now, alone together again, those thoughts returned. Even though he warned himself that this dragon girl was not to be trifled with, the thrill of playing with fire was hard to resist.

Zhu Qing stared at him deeply for a long while before softly parting her red lips: "Friends!"

"What?" Yang Kai looked at her in astonishment.

Zhu Qing took a gentle breath and said, "I said, I can be your friend."

Her tone was calm, but there was an unmistakable pride between her brows, as if this was some kind of favor.

Yang Kai was dumbfounded, staring at her in a daze.

Zhu Qing met his gaze without flinching. After a long while with no response from Yang Kai, she frowned and said, "Did you hear what I said..."

"Hahahaha!" Yang Kai burst out laughing, slapping his thigh so hard it made a loud sound.

Zhu Qing snapped, "What are you laughing at?"

Yang Kai stopped laughing and looked at Zhu Qing mockingly. "The pride of the Dragon Clan truly lives up to its reputation."

Zhu Qing sneered back, "Still better than the treachery of you humans."

Yang Kai pondered for a moment, then nodded. "You're right. Humans are indeed very treacherous—no one can deny that. But you say you want to be my friend. Do you even know... what a friend is?"

Zhu Qing said proudly, "I don't need to know."

Yang Kai slowly shook his head and looked at her. "You don't have any friends, do you?"

What need does the mighty Dragon Clan have for friends? Zhu Qing sneered inwardly, her face full of disdain. But then she suddenly noticed a trace of pity and sympathy on Yang Kai's face, which made her furious. How dare a lowly human look at her like that! Her slender fingers twitched as she suppressed the urge to gouge out his eyes.

Because she was no match for him. Acting rashly would only bring humiliation upon herself.

Elder, oh Elder, the task you gave me is truly difficult. Qing'er really doesn't know how to accomplish it. Maybe this is a test from the clan?

"Why do you want to be my friend?" Yang Kai asked with a frown.

Of course, it was because of the Elder's order and the Ancestral Dragon's Origin, Zhu Qing thought to herself, but replied, "Does there need to be a reason?"

"Doesn't there?" Yang Kai was surprised.

Zhu Qing grew impatient and gritted her teeth. "Just say whether you agree or not."

For a dignified Dragon Clan member to want to be his friend—he should be thanking his ancestors, yet he dared to hesitate. Truly, he did not know the immensity of heaven and earth.

"What if I agree? What if I don't?" Yang Kai smiled at her, then his expression turned serious. "Friendship isn't something you can just declare with words—it's not that simple."

He had never met anyone like this before—someone who wanted to be his friend while acting as if she was doing him a favor. Yang Kai didn't know whether Zhu Qing was clueless about human relationships or just too arrogant.

"Then what should I do?" Zhu Qing frowned and thought for a moment, actually showing a rare willingness to ask for advice.

Yang Kai glanced at her, waved his hand, and said solemnly, "To be a friend, one must be willing to go through fire and water, share worries and difficulties. To be a friend, one must be honest and open, loyal and true. To be a friend..."

"Be specific," Zhu Qing interrupted impatiently. "What do you want me to do to be your friend?"

Yang Kai looked at her with a sly smile, patted the bed beside him, and said, "I'm short of someone to warm my bed these days. If you take off your clothes and keep me company for a night, I might consider it."

"Get lost!" Zhu Qing spat out the word. What a shameless lecher—he couldn't go three sentences without showing his true colors. Besides, that's not something friends do. Even if Zhu Qing had no friends, she wasn't ignorant of common sense.

Yang Kai shrugged, looking helpless.

Zhu Qing turned her head away and said reluctantly, "Pick something else."

"Then there's nothing," Yang Kai spread his hands.

"What do you want, exactly?" Zhu Qing's face turned red with anger as she glared at him.

Yang Kai slammed the table in front of him to pieces and shouted angrily, "I should be asking you—what do you want, exactly?"

It was truly baffling. She came over and said she wanted to be friends. In all his years, Yang Kai had never encountered such a thing. Friendship couldn't be forced.

Zhu Qing pursed her red lips and said, "I just want to be your friend."

Yang Kai looked her up and down, confirming that this woman was sane and not acting crazy, then said loudly, "Fine! I heard there's a Wine Sword Manor in the Western Territory, and their Sword Wine is the best in the world—even the Great Emperors praise it. If you can get some..."

Zhu Qing's eyes lit up. "You'll be my friend if I do?"

Yang Kai stroked his chin. "We'll see when the time comes."

"You..." Zhu Qing was furious. "You're going too far!"

Yang Kai sneered, "That's just how I am. If you can't stand it, get lost!"

"Fine! No one has ever dared treat me like this. You'll regret it." Zhu Qing glared at him hatefully, spat out a harsh line, and turned to leave.

Yang Kai turned and grinned. "It's late, Qing'er. Aren't you staying the night?"

Zhu Qing had just reached the door and was about to leave when she heard this. She suddenly punched forward.

In an instant, the door was smashed open, wood splinters flying everywhere. Zhu Qing glanced back at Yang Kai, then dashed into the darkness.

Outside the door, Yingfei was drenched in cold sweat. Only after Zhu Qing had gone far did he peek in, saw that Yang Kai was unharmed, and finally breathed a sigh of relief. "Young Master Yang, what happened to her?"

"Who knows?" Yang Kai laughed heartily, feeling much more at ease.

After this, Zhu Qing probably wouldn't come to bother him again, right?

Although being pursued by a dragon girl who insisted on being friends was a pretty good thing, whenever he was with her, Yang Kai couldn't suppress his desires. That was a big problem.

He was afraid that one day he really wouldn't be able to hold back and would force himself on her. That would mean offending the entire Dragon Clan.

In short, although Zhu Qing didn't seem malicious, her existence was far too dangerous for him.

Yang Kai couldn't figure it out. Zhu Qing hadn't cultivated any charm techniques, nor was her beauty so overwhelming that he couldn't control himself. So why did her scent make him so restless?

"Th-this... this... this..." Suddenly, a middle-aged man dressed in a gaudy golden robe—looking every bit the wealthy landlord—came rushing over. The moment he saw Yang Kai's shattered door, his face changed dramatically and he cried out in shock, unable to stop exclaiming.

Every room here was reinforced with restriction arrays—even a First Stage Emperor couldn't break them. Yet now there was a huge hole in the door. Who could have done this? They must be incredibly bold.

"Who are you?" Yingfei glanced at him and asked.

The middle-aged man seemed to recognize Yingfei and replied respectfully, "Sir, I am the shopkeeper of this inn. I don't know what just happened here..."

"Don't ask what you shouldn't," Yingfei said coldly, tossing him some Source Crystals from his Spatial Ring. "Take this as compensation. Get my Young Master a new room."

"Yes, yes!" The shopkeeper nodded repeatedly and hurried to make arrangements. If anyone else had caused trouble in this inn, he would never let it go. Although he was just a shopkeeper, he had the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect backing him, and in Flowing Shadow City, he had support.

But who was Yingfei? He was a mighty Yao King—compared to him, the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect was nothing but dust in the wind.

In no time, Yang Kai was given a new room, and the shopkeeper left respectfully.

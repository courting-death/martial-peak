## Chapter 2958 — Shamanic Arts Reappear

"Say it, say it!" Fu Shu urged hurriedly.

He did not have many experts under his command, and now all of them were already lying down. He could only pin his hopes on the goateed old man.

The goateed old man said, "With the Sacred Tree's protection, the Eldest Prince will definitely not be harmed. Since you cannot be harmed, what are you afraid of? You are a prince, after all, and can wield the Sacred Tree's power. Even if you can't defeat him, can't you just run? As long as you return to your residence, what can this man do to you?"

Fu Shu's eyes lit up at these words, cursing himself for being so frightened that he lost his wits. Indeed, as this old goat said, with the Sacred Tree's protection, ordinary people simply could not hurt him. Since he would not be harmed, what was there to fear?

He hated himself for being so scared just now that he lost all composure, not even thinking of such a thing. He hated Yang Kai even more for making him lose face like this. Glancing at Zhu Qing standing over there, his courage grew, and he slowly stood up from the corner.

Though his body was still trembling a little, it was much better than his previous desperate begging and fleeing.

The earlier conflict had also drawn the attention of many Human Sovereign City residents. Now, the teahouse was surrounded on all sides by onlookers, and several patrol squads arrived after hearing the commotion. When they saw the mess inside the teahouse, they were all shocked.

Upon seeing Fu Shu, they all stepped forward to salute and inquire about the situation.

"You came just in time." The appearance of these city guards made Fu Shu even more confident. Pointing at Yang Kai, he said, "This man is vicious and violent, he injured my guards and insulted this prince. Quickly, seize him with me!"

The guards all responded in unison.

A cold sneer appeared on Fu Shu's face, and suddenly, an incantation began to echo from his mouth. The words were obscure and hard to understand, leaving others completely baffled, but Yang Kai was shocked.

He was all too familiar with this incantation. As soon as it sounded, a grand scene of war involuntarily surfaced in his mind. Back then, under his command, a hundred thousand troops coordinated their branches and fearlessly charged into the demon ranks. At those times, the shamans would chant such incantations.

The chanting of the incantation made one's blood boil, filling the chest with killing intent, making one want to destroy everything before their eyes.

Fu Shu's incantation was brief, with some changes, but its essence remained the same.

Suddenly, a beam of light burst forth, enveloping the guards.

In the next instant, the qi and blood of the many guards surged violently, their bodies swelling a size larger, as if they were balloons being inflated. Every patch of exposed skin glowed crimson, and even their eyes turned blood-red.

All timidity and fear vanished from them, replaced by resolve and ferocity.

"Bloodlust Art!" Yang Kai's eyes nearly popped out. He never expected that after more than a hundred thousand years, he would witness the Bloodlust Art being cast by someone else.

Although this Bloodlust Art had changed somewhat, Yang Kai could never mistake it.

Previously, in that cave, when Steward Liao cast his spell, there had been traces of Shamanic Arts, but they were not obvious, so Yang Kai had not paid much attention. But now, it seemed that Shamanic Arts still existed in the Revolving World. Who knew how it had been passed down for so long?

Zhu Qing clearly had not expected such a scene either. Her beautiful eyes curiously observed the transformed warriors, somewhat puzzled.

As Fu Shu's incantation sounded again, Yang Kai already knew what kind of Shamanic Art this was. This Shamanic Art had once been the greatest headache for the demons, an art they could not counter.

It was precisely because of this Shamanic Art's protection that the Barbarian Tribe could contend with the demons.

Yang Kai knew all too well how terrifying this Shamanic Art was. How could he let Fu Shu finish casting it?

He stepped forward, the Myriad Sword vanished without a trace, and as his hands clapped together, a massive Moon Blade slashed toward Fu Shu with a thunderous roar.

The attack appeared extremely suddenly, and its power was unfathomable. Even the goateed old man was secretly startled, and Fu Shu was naturally shocked as well. But, remembering the old man's words, though terrified, he did not dodge or defend, and his incantation grew even louder.

The enormous Moon Blade struck Fu Shu as expected, but was blocked by a green light that appeared on his body. This time, the green light was especially thick, almost dripping with verdancy. This green light was a kind of protection, growing stronger when faced with strength, which in turn highlighted the Moon Blade's terrifying power.

Silent and invisible, the divine ability that could devour space failed to harm Fu Shu. The green light once again blocked the Moon Blade's attack, but at the point of contact, a tiny crack appeared, almost imperceptible.

This protection was not invincible, nor was it unbreakable. Yang Kai suddenly realized this. The reason his previous attacks had little effect was simply because his strength was not enough.

In that brief moment, Fu Shu had already completed his Shamanic Art.

Another beam of light burst forth, enveloping the guards.

Vaguely, something seemed to link these people together, making their lives and deaths one. The power of everyone's qi and blood converged into a single point, soaring skyward.

Zhu Qing's expression changed.

"Chain of Life!" Yang Kai gritted his teeth and growled. He never imagined that one day he would face both the Bloodlust Art and Chain of Life, the two great war Shamanic Arts, head-on. In the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, the Barbarian Tribe army he led had swept all before them thanks to these two Shamanic Arts. Because of this, Yang Kai knew their power better than anyone.

The goateed old man looked surprised. "You actually know the Sacred Art?"

"What is the Sacred Art?" Yang Kai took a few steps back, standing shoulder to shoulder with Zhu Qing, his gaze coldly fixed ahead.

The group of guards who had been subjected to the Bloodlust Art and Chain of Life numbered more than thirty. None of them had cultivation above the Emperor Realm; the strongest was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, the weakest at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. Such a group, if fighting one-on-one, Yang Kai could blow them away with a breath and easily teach them a lesson.

But now, they had the capital to fight to the death with an Emperor Realm master.

The reputation of the Shamanic Arts was not exaggerated.

The goateed old man said, "Only royals with a Six-Leaf Body or higher can borrow the Sacred Tree's power to perform this secret art. Since you don't even know the Sacred Art itself, how could you possibly name it?"

Yang Kai ignored him and said quietly to Zhu Qing, "This is a bit troublesome."

Zhu Qing said, "I'll handle these people. That so-called prince is yours."

Yang Kai shook his head. "They're not easy to deal with. Let me do it."

Zhu Qing said languidly, "Don't underestimate me, all right? Besides, I don't want to deal with that annoying guy."

"Since you put it that way, I guess that's how it has to be." Yang Kai shrugged.

Zhu Qing snorted, "If you can't handle him, don't even think about touching me in the future."

As she finished speaking, she darted forward.

Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and muttered, "Does she have to be so ruthless?"

The goateed old man poured cold water from the side. "It's useless. Now that the Sacred Art has been unleashed, you have no chance of winning unless you can reverse it. But for that, you'd need the Sacred Tree's protection first."

Fu Shu roared in anger, "Old man, whose side are you on?"

The goateed old man replied blandly, "This old man is just here to watch the fun. I'm not on any side. Eldest Prince, focus on your opponent and don't disgrace the royal family."

Fu Shu was infuriated by his words, his ambition soaring. "Then open your dog eyes and watch carefully!"

As they spoke, Zhu Qing was already fighting the thirty-plus guards. As violent energy surged, the small teahouse collapsed in an instant. The teahouse owner ran into the street, weeping and cursing loudly.

Zhu Qing quickly discovered the troublesome aspect of this group. Their movements were not very agile—after all, there were more than thirty of them, and their fighting was uncoordinated and chaotic, often interfering with each other. They looked clumsy and awkward.

Yet, every one of these thirty-plus people could withstand her attacks head-on.

Every time she struck, any random person could block it. This was simply inconceivable. She was of the Dragon Clan, her strength overwhelming. Even an ordinary Third Stage Emperor would not dare to take her blow head-on and remain unscathed, yet this ragtag group managed it.

After a few punches, Zhu Qing quickly discovered the secret behind their ability to withstand her attacks.

She had no idea what was going on, but no matter who blocked her, her attacks were all dispersed and borne by the thirty-plus people together.

A single chopstick breaks with ease, yet two become far harder to snap. What of thirty-plus people blessed by the Sacred Art? Once their qi and blood surged forth, these warriors felt neither pain nor peril—their eyes blazing crimson. They could now withstand blows many times greater than before.

Zhu Qing's expression turned cold, and her attacks grew fiercer. For her, she had only been testing them just now.

With a rumble, the battlefield shifted constantly, soon engulfing half the street.

At the ruins of the teahouse, Yang Kai stared at Fu Shu and said calmly, "You heard what my wife said. For the sake of my future happiness, Eldest Prince, please... lie down."

As he finished speaking, he suddenly appeared in front of Fu Shu, almost close enough to touch him.

The goateed old man's eyes narrowed, his pupils shrinking to the size of a needlepoint.

In that instant, he had not even seen what happened.

Fu Shu cried out in alarm, nearly falling again, but quickly steadied himself and gritted his teeth. "Do you think this prince is afraid of you?"

Yang Kai raised his fist and smashed it fiercely toward Fu Shu's face.

Fu Shu had no time to react before the fist was already in front of him. Fortunately, the green light appeared and blocked the blow, sparing him from harm. Otherwise, he would likely have died on the spot.

His face turned pale, but he gritted his teeth and began chanting the incantation again.

He wanted to cast a Bloodlust Art on himself so he would no longer feel fear.

Yang Kai suddenly stared at his chest and said, "Eldest Prince, you only have an Eight-Leaf Body? Tsk tsk, the Third Prince has a Nine-Leaf Body. Why, as the eldest brother, do you have one less leaf than him? Is it because the Human Sovereign favors him, or are you just unpopular?"

Fu Shu's incantation stopped abruptly. He shouted in anger, "Stop spouting nonsense! How could Father favor the Third? I am the Eldest Prince!"

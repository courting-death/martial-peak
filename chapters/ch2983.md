## Chapter 2983 — Killing the Chicken for Its Eggs

The Demon Kings discussed matters among themselves, while Zhu Qing and her younger brother had no intention of paying them any attention. Li Jiao stood timidly off to the side, utterly devoid of the bearing befitting a Palace Master. He wanted to strike up a conversation with the two members of the Dragon Clan and build some rapport, but lacked the courage to do so, leaving him looking worried and miserable.

An hour later, a figure flashed above the square as Yang Kai suddenly appeared. Although he looked rather battered, his brow was filled with joy.

The moment Zhu Qing and her brother saw him, they knew his earlier conjecture had probably been correct. Otherwise, he would never look so delighted. Both of them secretly grew excited.

The Demon Kings, on the other hand, all wore solemn expressions and inwardly groaned.

The reason was simple: the young Guixu Yang Kai was carrying was once again covered in a layer of frost, clearly because it had swallowed a great deal of Yin Wind on Yin Wind Mountain. With the previous example to follow, resolving this problem would be easy, but it was certainly not what the Demon Kings hoped for.

Yang Kai nodded slightly to Zhu Qing, then turned his gaze toward the Demon Kings like a butcher eyeing a flock of lambs. Without saying a word, Zhu Lie and Zhu Qing both flew into the air, surrounding the Demon Kings in a triangular formation with Yang Kai.

After thinking for a moment, Li Jiao hurriedly joined them. He pushed his Imperial Might, contorted his face into a fierce expression, and assumed a posture as though ready to bite someone at any moment.

"Please wait, Sirs." Whether she stepped forward willingly or under duress, no one knew, but the Succubus hurriedly emerged, her forehead covered in cold sweat. Raising her voice, she said, "We discussed the matter just now and are willing to cooperate with Sirs to strengthen Guixu, helping you leave this place as soon as possible."

"Oh?" Yang Kai raised his brow and asked with great interest, "It is rare for you to be so considerate. I would feel embarrassed if I did not grant your wish. So, may I ask whom you have chosen to die first?"

The Succubus hurriedly wiped the sweat from her forehead and forced out a smile. "Guixu favors things of the Demon Race and can use the Demon Race's power to grow stronger, but that does not mean it has to devour our clansmen, Sir."

"I know." Yang Kai nodded and said as though it were only natural, "But isn't that the most direct and efficient method?"

The Succubus replied, "It is certainly the most direct, but not necessarily the most efficient."

Yang Kai gave her a half-smile. "How is it not efficient?"

Summoning her courage, the Succubus said, "There are only so many Demon King-level existences in Demon Fury City. May I ask what Sir plans to do if Guixu devours all of us and still fails to gain enough strength to leave this place?"

Yang Kai frowned. "You may be the only Demon Kings, but there are plenty of Demon Race clansmen in Demon Fury City. One day, it will devour enough."

The Succubus said, "How could those clansmen compare with us? Sir is also an expert, so you should know that strength cannot be measured by numbers."

Yang Kai smiled slightly. "What you say makes some sense. Good, I will give you a chance to persuade me."

The Succubus's spirits rose, and she immediately said, "This humble one has heard a saying: killing the chicken for its eggs. Sir's present actions perfectly embody those words. If you want eggs, you do not necessarily have to kill the chicken that lays them."

"You are willing to be the chickens that lay the eggs?"

The Succubus smiled bitterly. "It is still better than dying. We are willing to cooperate and allow Guixu to devour our Demon Yuan. Although it will not grow as quickly this way as it would by devouring us directly, the difference should not be too great. Moreover, the source of its strength will not be severed all at once. As long as we remain alive, we can continue providing Guixu with the power to grow. Sir is an intelligent man and should understand the gains and losses involved."

Yang Kai snorted lightly and said indifferently, "What if I insist on killing the chickens for their eggs?"

The Succubus pursed her lips and turned to glance at the other Demon Kings before saying bitterly, "If Sir insists on doing so, we will have no choice but to risk everything in one last struggle. We know there is little hope, but the worst that awaits us is death. Moreover... if we die, Guixu will gain nothing. Sir may not know this, but Guixu does not eat the dead; it only eats living creatures."

After speaking, she stared nervously at Yang Kai. The many Demon Kings also remained silent, and the atmosphere suddenly became tense and solemn.

Under everyone's watchful gaze, Yang Kai wore a stern expression and pondered for a long time before finally showing a look of realization. "No wonder it refused to eat those members of the Demon Race last time. It turns out it is such a picky eater!"

The Succubus could not help staring blankly. Seeing Yang Kai thinking so seriously just now, she had assumed he was considering her proposal. Who could have known he was thinking about something completely unrelated?

Just as she was about to speak, Yang Kai said, "If things are as you say, there is no harm in trying. Killing a few people is one thing, but slaughtering an entire city goes against the harmony of Heaven. Unless there is no other choice, this Young Master is unwilling to attempt such a thing. Whether you live or die will depend on your own performance."

As soon as he finished speaking, Yang Kai tossed the little black dog over.

The Demon Kings paled in fright and instinctively dodged aside. They had all clearly witnessed the little black dog swallow the Blood Demon in a single bite just now, and that cautionary example was still fresh in their minds.

Fortunately, the Succubus was fairly dependable. Summoning her courage, she caught the little black dog.

The little black dog's enormous mouth opened, instantly blotting out all light before the Succubus's eyes. Her entire body went limp with fear as she watched helplessly while that great maw bit down toward her, unable to muster even the strength to flee.

A smack rang out as Yang Kai slapped the dog's head. The little black dog whimpered and closed its mouth, finally allowing the Succubus to escape disaster.

"You cannot eat them!" Yang Kai stared at it solemnly. "Do not play dumb with me. When I say you cannot eat them, you cannot eat them."

The little black dog's ears immediately drooped.

"It is fine now." Yang Kai looked at the Succubus and smiled.

The Succubus remained frozen in place. Only after a long while did she say awkwardly, "My legs are a little weak. Which of you will go first?"

The Demon Kings looked from one to another. In the end, a Bone Demon stepped forward and said in a hoarse voice, "I will go first."

As he spoke, he strode over to the little black dog and urged his Demon Yuan. In an instant, Demonic Qi roiled around him and surged fiercely toward the little black dog.

The little black dog immediately became excited and opened its mouth once more. A powerful suction force emerged from that enormous maw, drawing the Bone Demon's power into its mouth and swallowing it into its belly.

A black line seemed to become visible between the little black dog and the Bone Demon. An endless stream of power flowed from the Bone Demon into the little black dog's body, and the frost covering it rapidly melted away.

This Bone Demon was quite powerful. The aura radiating from him lay somewhere between the Second-Stage and Third-Stage Emperor Realm, making him unquestionably one of the ten strongest among the Demon Kings.

Yet even such a Demon King could not last for the time it took an incense stick to burn before the little black dog.

No matter how he urged his strength, the little black dog devoured every last drop. Its tiny belly seemed like a bottomless pit that could never be filled.

After an incense stick's worth of time, the Bone Demon unsteadily withdrew his Demonic Qi. Even the ghostly flames flickering in his eye sockets had dimmed considerably, an obvious sign that he had expended too much strength.

He walked to the side and immediately sat down cross-legged to recover.

With such a good start, the other Demon Kings came before the little black dog one after another and followed the Bone Demon's example.

One Demon King after another flashed past the little black dog in rapid succession, each taking the stage as the last departed, and every one of them was nearly drained dry.

Even the strongest Demon King could not endure this torture for two incense sticks before his strength was exhausted.

In no more than six hours, all the Demon Kings were seated on the ground in several rows.

Yang Kai's expression darkened. Although he knew the little black dog had undoubtedly grown much stronger, he was also certain it was still not strong enough to take them away from this place.

Observing his expression, the Succubus forced herself to remain alert and said, "This humble one can summon all the Demon Commanders in the city. Although their strength is somewhat lower, they make up for it with their numbers."

Yang Kai nodded lightly, and only then did the Succubus cast a spell to summon them.

Before long, figures with Demon Commander-level cultivations flew in from every direction across the city.

Just as the Succubus had said, although the Demon Commanders' cultivations were somewhat lower, there were more than enough of them. They could certainly provide the little black dog with some capital to grow stronger.

When the many Demon Commanders arrived and saw the Demon Kings' miserable state, they were all startled. Their hearts filled with anxiety, none of them knowing what sort of fate awaited them.

The Succubus called several of the strongest Demon Commanders to her side and quietly said a few words to them. Their faces went pale as they listened, and they occasionally looked up at the little black dog with profound dread.

The Demon Commanders soon learned why they had been summoned.

With the many Demon Kings serving as examples, they knew they could not resist. They lined up and came before the little black dog one at a time, urging their Demon Yuan.

The Demon Commanders were far inferior to the Demon Kings. A Demon Commander, an existence comparable to a Dao Origin Realm cultivator, could generally last only a few dozen breaths. The suction force from the little black dog's mouth seemed capable of wildly drawing the surging power out of their bodies, making it impossible for them to endure for long.

One Demon Commander after another was left utterly exhausted.

By the following day, all the Demon Commanders had taken a turn. Everywhere one looked, members of the Demon Race sat meditating to recover, creating an extraordinarily spectacular scene.

A single day was nowhere near enough for the Demon Kings to recover completely.

The little black dog barked impatiently several times. Since its birth, it had never feasted so freely. Although this was not as delicious as directly devouring a Demon King, this method had the advantage of lasting much longer, and it was so delighted that it never wanted the feast to end.

The Bone Demon who had been the first to rest had no choice but to stand again and repeat what he had done the previous day.

Yang Kai stood to the side and watched coldly, knowing this would only lead to a vicious cycle. The members of the Demon Race could not get sufficient rest, so the strength they could provide the little black dog would only diminish until almost nothing remained.

After thinking for a while, Yang Kai took out several bottles of restorative Spirit Pills, woke the Succubus, and told her to distribute them.

The Succubus looked hesitant. She wanted to ask what kind of Spirit Pills they were, but lacked the courage to do so.

Yang Kai snorted coldly and offered an explanation on his own initiative, finally putting her somewhat at ease.

Before long, all the Spirit Pills had been distributed. The Demon Kings who took them looked delighted, clearly feeling that the speed at which their strength recovered had increased considerably.

The Succubus returned and, seeming to understand Yang Kai's thoughts, whispered, "Sir need not worry too much. The World Laws currently favor the Star Realm's side. Once the Laws shift, our recovery speed will become much faster, and there should be no further problems."

"I hope so." Yang Kai declined to comment further.

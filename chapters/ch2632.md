## Chapter 2632 — Senior Brother Becomes Senior Uncle

"Vice Sect Master of the Wenqing Sect?" Yang Kai was taken aback. A figure quickly surfaced in his mind, and he said in a deep voice, "Yao Zhuo?"

Bing Yun nodded and said, "Yes, that's him!"

Ji Yao, who had been silent all along, asked curiously, "You know Yao Zhuo?"

Yang Kai nodded, "The last time I came to Ice Heart Valley, I had quite a few dealings with him."

The last time he came to Ice Heart Valley, he happened to catch the marriage alliance between the Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley. The Young Sect Master of Wenqing Sect, Feng Xi, had taken a fancy to Zi Yu and tried every means to obtain her. Ice Heart Valley, under pressure, was forced to agree to the marriage.

At that time, it was Yao Zhuo who brought Feng Xi to Ice Heart Valley to propose.

Back then, Yao Zhuo was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, while Yang Kai was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. The gap in strength between them was enormous.

Yet, Yao Zhuo was still injured by the Emperor Extinction Pill that Bing Yun had given Yang Kai. Sealed within that pill was Ice Heart Valley's secret technique, the Ice Snow Pear Blossom Sword.

Later, Yang Kai even snatched Yao Zhuo's Spatial Ring, which contained the betrothal gifts prepared by the Wenqing Sect. That windfall truly made Yang Kai a fortune at the time.

This was only a few years ago, so Yang Kai remembered it vividly. Now, hearing Bing Yun mention the Vice Sect Master of the Wenqing Sect, he immediately recalled it.

On the other side, Changsun Ying's lips moved as she quietly sent Ji Yao a voice transmission, explaining what had happened a few years ago.

After listening, Ji Yao was left speechless. She suddenly realized that Yang Kai was simply a born bandit. Just a few years ago, when he was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, he robbed the Spatial Ring of someone at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. And not long ago in the Wild Ancient Land, he had extorted a huge fortune from the Four Great Saint Venerables.

This man was truly born to be a bandit.

"How did Yao Zhuo suddenly break through to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm?" Yang Kai frowned slightly in disbelief.

A few years ago, Yao Zhuo was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, but breaking through to the next stage was not so easy, especially at the Emperor Realm, where it could take hundreds or even thousands of years without progress.

It hadn't been long, so by rights, Yao Zhuo's cultivation shouldn't have improved so quickly.

Bing Yun shook her head, "I don't know either. Maybe he had his own opportunity." She sighed and continued, "Originally, my Ice Heart Valley and the Wenqing Sect were evenly matched, but ever since Yao Zhuo broke through to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, that balance was broken. I can't match Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo together, so Ice Heart Valley is naturally suppressed. Plus, with all the lackeys the Wenqing Sect has drawn in, Ice Heart Valley is now rather isolated."

Yang Kai nodded, "So what do you plan to do, Senior? Are you just going to hide in the valley?"

Bing Yun shook her head, "There's no good solution for now. We can only rely on the Grand Guardian Array to hold out for a while."

"I'm afraid the Grand Guardian Array won't last much longer," Yang Kai said in a low voice.

"What do you mean by that?" Bing Yun was startled.

Yang Kai said, "Earlier, Junior Sister Ji Yao and I ran into this person outside. He said the Wenqing Sect had hired some Grandmaster Nanmen, who is currently studying how to break the array. He seems to be a great formation expert. Senior, have you ever heard of him?"

"Grandmaster Nanmen..." Bing Yun's brows furrowed.

An Ruoyun exclaimed, "Nanmen Dajun?"

Sun Yunxiu and Changsun Ying both turned pale, as if they knew who this Grandmaster Nanmen was.

Bing Yun looked at An Ruoyun for answers. An Ruoyun quickly said, "Master, this Nanmen Dajun is indeed a formation grandmaster, famous throughout the Northern Territory. His attainments in formations are extraordinary."

Bing Yun's expression also became serious as she asked, "What's this person's background? What is his relationship with the Wenqing Sect?"

She had been away from the Northern Territory for too long and had never heard of Nanmen Dajun before.

An Ruoyun said, "He shouldn't have any connection with the Wenqing Sect. As far as I know, Nanmen Dajun came from humble origins and supposedly obtained a rare formation manual in his youth. He is self-taught and has no sect background. Because he suffered hardships and bullying when he was young, he only cares about money, not people. As long as the price is right, anyone can hire him."

Sun Yunxiu also nodded, "But this man is always elusive. I didn't expect the Wenqing Sect to actually find him. They must have paid a hefty price."

Bing Yun's face grew solemn as she asked, "Yang Kai, is this information reliable?"

Yang Kai said, "That's just what he told me. Whether it's true or not, Senior can ask him yourself."

As he spoke, he kicked Fang Minghui, who had been lying on the ground, and snorted coldly, "Get up! If you keep playing dead, I'll make you really die!"

Fang Minghui, who had seemed unconscious, shuddered at the words, hurriedly got up, and, face pale, cupped his fists, "Greetings, Senior Bing Yun, greetings to all the honored seniors. I am Fang Minghui of the Fang Family from Shuangyu City. I have nothing to do with the Wenqing Sect. Please judge fairly, seniors."

No one knew when he had woken up, but he had clearly overheard much of the previous conversation, which was why he knew the person sitting above was Bing Yun.

"Did I tell you to say that? Did I?" Yang Kai went up and gave him a beating, scolding as he hit, "If you dare spout nonsense again, I'll tear you to pieces!"

Unable to dodge, Fang Minghui took a few solid hits and wailed, "I'll say whatever you want me to, my lord!"

Yang Kai snorted, "Senior Bing Yun has some questions for you. Answer honestly, or you'll be sorry."

"Yes, yes!" Fang Minghui nodded repeatedly.

Although Fang Minghui had already anticipated the fate of his fellow Fang Family disciples, at this moment, he couldn’t even muster the slightest thought of revenge. After all, his strength was lacking, and it was they who had willingly involved themselves in the conflict between these two great sects.

Bing Yun looked at Fang Minghui and asked, "What's the story with this Nanmen Dajun?"

Fang Minghui said bitterly, "Senior, I don't know much about this either. I only heard others say that Sect Master Feng Xuan... hmm, that old scoundrel, hired a Grandmaster Nanmen to break the array. In as little as half a month, or as long as a month, the array will be broken. By then, all one hundred thousand disciples outside will swarm in and flatten Ice Heart Valley."

"That's all you know?" Bing Yun frowned.

Fang Minghui said, "Please judge fairly, Senior. The few of us from the Fang Family aren't strong. Though we foolishly sided with the Wenqing Sect for a while, we were only responsible for patrolling the outskirts. We really don't know any secrets, nor have we done anything to wrong Ice Heart Valley."

Yang Kai said, "He also said earlier that the Wenqing Sect issued a recruitment order, promising many benefits to attract some cultivators to serve them."

"I already know about this," Bing Yun nodded. "Otherwise, how could the Wenqing Sect have one hundred thousand disciples?"

Ice Heart Valley had fewer than ten thousand disciples to begin with, and after years of battles both large and small, their numbers had dwindled even further. Now, fewer than seven thousand remained. Facing a force of one hundred thousand, the sheer difference in numbers alone was enough to make one despair—let alone the fact that the Wenqing Sect had recruited many powerful experts, and even boasted two Third Stage Emperor Realm masters.

If the Grand Guardian Array was truly broken, Ice Heart Valley would be finished. By then, there would likely be only a handful of disciples who could escape with their lives.

The situation was dire, and everyone in the hall wore gloomy expressions.

They had thought that with the Grand Guardian Array, the Wenqing Sect could only glare helplessly. Now they realized that was just wishful thinking.

Yang Kai thought for a moment and said, "The root cause of Ice Heart Valley's disaster lies with me. If I step forward..."

The reason Feng Xuan didn't hesitate to start a war between the two sects and disregard the situation in the Northern Territory was simply to avenge his son Feng Xi. The one who killed Feng Xi was Yang Kai. Yang Kai felt that if he showed himself, he could probably lure the Wenqing Sect's people away, and then Ice Heart Valley's crisis would be resolved.

"No!" Before he could finish, Bing Yun shook her head.

An Ruoyun, Sun Yunxiu, Changsun Ying, and the others all spoke up to dissuade him.

Bing Yun said, "Although it started because of you, the Wenqing Sect has had its eyes on Ice Heart Valley for a long time. You also know what the Supreme Sentiment Art of the Wenqing Sect is all about. Many disciples in the Wenqing Sect practice this technique, and most of my Ice Heart Valley's female disciples are beautiful and talented, making them the perfect cauldrons for their cultivation. So even if you do show yourself, the Wenqing Sect won't give up easily. Now that things have developed to this point, they are determined to destroy Ice Heart Valley. Your appearance won't change anything."

What she said made sense, and Yang Kai couldn't help but sigh softly.

Bing Yun stood up and said, "Although my Ice Heart Valley is isolated, it's not so easy for the Wenqing Sect to destroy us. Yang Kai, you don't need to worry. Since you're here, stay for a few days. Zi Yu, take your Senior Uncle Yang to rest."

"Yes!" Zi Yu responded crisply, then turned to Yang Kai and said with a gentle smile, "Senior Uncle Yang, please come with me."

Yang Kai nodded, knowing that Bing Yun and the others still had things to discuss, and as an outsider, it wasn't appropriate for him to stay. So he followed Zi Yu out.

As they walked along, the scenery inside Ice Heart Valley was beautiful and enchanting, a feast for the eyes.

After a while, Zi Yu suddenly smiled and said, "A few years ago you were Senior Brother, but now you've become Senior Uncle."

Yang Kai was taken aback and said, "Miss Zi Yu, if you don't mind, let's just address each other as before."

A few years ago, when they first met, Yang Kai did indeed call Zi Yu Junior Sister. Now, with just a word from Bing Yun, his seniority had risen by a level.

But this wasn't strange. Yang Kai and Ji Yao were peers, and Zi Yu was Ji Yao's junior, so naturally she had to call Yang Kai Senior Uncle.

Zi Yu shook her head, "That won't do. If the Ancestor finds out, she'll scold me for sure." She paused, then said, "By the way, I still have to thank Senior Uncle Yang for helping me out in the Shattered Star Sea. If you hadn't lured those bad people away, I wouldn't have been able to break through so smoothly."

"It was nothing, don't worry about it," Yang Kai smiled.

As they chatted, they arrived at a small courtyard. The courtyard was exquisite and elegant, with red plum trees standing proudly in the snow and frost, and a neatly paved stone path.

"Senior Uncle Yang, now that you're in Ice Heart Valley, make yourself at home. If you need anything, just let me know," Zi Yu said as she led Yang Kai into the courtyard.

"Alright, you go ahead. I'll be fine on my own," Yang Kai nodded.

Zi Yu excused herself and left.

Yang Kai looked around, then pushed open the door, sat cross-legged on a cushion, and began to meditate and cultivate.

## Chapter 3040 — The Dragon Clan Is Here

Yuan Wu lay unconscious, while Madam Hua's face was livid.

The beach fell utterly silent.

Only after a long while did Madam Hua finally shout at Yuan Wu, "What are you playing at? Hurry up and get up for this Madam!"

How could a Dragon Descendant at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm be knocked unconscious by a single palm strike from a human at the First Stage? She was certain Yuan Wu was merely pretending. Although she did not know why, he was the only person she could command right now, so she had no choice but to keep ordering him around.

Yuan Wu did not move, as though he truly had fainted.

Madam Hua strode over to him and kicked him viciously, but Yuan Wu still did not respond.

"How dare you defy me!" Madam Hua flew into a rage. Just as she was about to berate him further, the light before her suddenly dimmed. She looked up, and her beautiful eyes could not help narrowing.

Yang Kai was already standing before her, staring at her coldly.

Madam Hua's heart tightened. She involuntarily retreated several steps and asked in alarm, "What are you trying to do?"

The moment the words left her mouth, her Emperor Yuan suddenly surged, and she thrust a palm toward Yang Kai's chest. She had clearly planned this attack in advance, for she struck without the slightest warning. Combined with her frightened expression and retreat just now, no one could have expected her to suddenly launch a sneak attack.

As her palm shot out, a triumphant smile appeared on Madam Hua's face.

She was certain Yang Kai had not guarded against her. Since their cultivations were at the same Stage, she was confident she could severely injure him in a single blow.

"How treacherous! Consider me enlightened!" Yang Kai snorted and struck out with a palm of his own. Though he attacked later, his palm arrived first and slammed directly into Madam Hua's shoulder.

With a startled cry, Madam Hua was sent flying backward. A metallic taste surged up her throat and instantly filled her mouth. At the same time, excruciating pain tore through her shoulder, as though the bones had been shattered. A violent force rushed into her meridians and spread through her internal organs, throwing all her Emperor Yuan into turmoil.

How could he be so strong? Madam Hua's beautiful eyes widened.

Although they were both at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, Madam Hua discovered that her opponent's strength was simply beyond comparison with her own. A single exchange was enough to make her realize the enormous gap between them.

Her shapely body crashed heavily to the ground. Before she could get up, a figure blocked the light above her. She looked up and saw Yang Kai standing over her, gazing down with a mocking sneer.

"You... dare hit me?" Shaken to her core, Madam Hua forgot the pain in her shoulder and stared at Yang Kai in disbelief.

"Tell me, why wouldn't I dare hit you?" Yang Kai slowly raised one foot and planted it on her chest, immediately deforming her full breasts.

Madam Hua winced in pain, her fine brows knitting tightly together. Yang Kai's retort left her somewhat dumbfounded.

Why wouldn't he dare hit me? This Madam is one of Fu Chi's most favored women. On this Spirit Island, who would dare lay a hand on me?

"You are courting death!" Madam Hua flew into a complete rage and frantically gathered her Emperor Yuan in an attempt to resist. Yet under the pressure of Yang Kai's foot, even rising was nearly impossible, much less fighting back. She thrashed and clawed wildly, and before long her hair was disheveled and her hairpins askew. She looked like a shrew who had suffered some terrible grievance, without the slightest trace of beauty remaining. Her eyes were filled with venom, and even her face had become horribly twisted.

"Even with death staring you in the face, you still dare speak so arrogantly." Yang Kai snorted and exerted a little more force with his foot. Madam Hua let out a low cry, and her face went pale.

Lu Sanniang finally returned to her senses.

Ever since Yang Kai had crushed the Golden-Backed Red Carp to death, one sudden upheaval after another had unfolded in the blink of an eye, leaving her so shaken that she stood there in a daze. Only now, when she sensed Yang Kai's murderous intent, did she abruptly awaken. She hurried forward, grabbed his arm, and cried softly, "Brother Yang, don't! You cannot kill her!"

"There is no one in this world who cannot be killed." Yang Kai turned and glared at her, looking fierce and menacing.

This was the first time Lu Sanniang had seen Yang Kai like this. During their previous encounters, he had always been amiable and approachable. His sudden change in demeanor startled her, and she instinctively felt somewhat afraid.

But today's trouble had begun because of her and her daughter. If Yang Kai truly killed Madam Hua, they would probably have no chance of leaving this place alive.

Even the present situation would be difficult to resolve. Lu Sanniang knew Madam Hua's temperament well. After suffering such utter humiliation today, she would likely never let the matter rest. They would have to pay some price before Madam Hua's anger could be appeased.

"You cannot kill her. Brother Yang, hurry and let her go!" Lu Sanniang pleaded repeatedly. She knew Yang Kai was standing up for her and her daughter, and she was endlessly grateful, but now that matters had reached this point, she had no idea what to do.

No matter what, Madam Hua could not be killed. They could only carefully consider what to do next.

Lu Sanniang's show of weakness gave Madam Hua hope, and her arrogance immediately flared again. She snarled viciously, "Wretched woman, this Madam will report every detail of today's events to Sir. You and your daughter can wait for death!"

Lu Sanniang's face paled as she pleaded, "Elder Sister, Sanniang was in the wrong, but this has nothing to do with Qin'er. Please show mercy and do not mention Qin'er before Sir."

Madam Hua grinned malevolently. "Now you know to beg for mercy? Unfortunately, it is already too late. All of you will die today!"

Her venomous gaze swept over Lu Sanniang and her daughter. She savored their fear with great satisfaction, then fixed her eyes on Yang Kai again, only to freeze when she saw him.

She could not detect the slightest trace of fear on Yang Kai's face. Instead, he remained perfectly calm and unconcerned, as though he had done nothing of consequence.

What exactly was going on with this man?

"Elder Sister Lu, you saw it yourself. You are trying to spare her out of kindness, yet she refuses to let the matter rest. You might as well let me kill her and end this once and for all." Yang Kai stared into Madam Hua's eyes, but his words were addressed to Lu Sanniang, his tone perfectly calm.

Lu Sanniang fell silent, anxious and uneasy. She truly had no idea how to handle the situation before her.

Release Madam Hua? That would be like releasing a tiger back into the mountains. But if they did not release her, who could withstand Fu Chi's wrath?

Neither choice would end well, and Lu Sanniang felt both furious and aggrieved. The entire incident had begun merely because the red carp had lost two scales. It was no great matter at all, yet Madam Hua had come with such fanfare to call them to account. Moreover, she and her daughter had already apologized, but Madam Hua had still refused to relent and insisted on repaying injury for injury.

"You... want to kill me?" Madam Hua finally understood the meaning in Yang Kai's eyes. Terror welled up from the depths of her heart, and her earlier arrogance immediately withered. Her entire body turned ice-cold, as though even her blood had frozen.

She had never imagined there would truly be someone on this Spirit Island who wanted to kill her.

Yang Kai sneered. "Do you think I'm joking with you?"

If this were anywhere else, it would not matter much whether Madam Hua lived or died, but this was Dragon Isle. Since he had already come into such serious conflict with her, he could not allow her to leave.

As for what consequences her death might bring, he could only deal with them as they came.

Feeling Yang Kai's murderous intent envelop her like a tide, Madam Hua finally panicked. Her face turned deathly pale, and she regretted her actions bitterly.

She had never expected matters to develop this way. Had she known this man was so recklessly bold, she would have stopped while she was ahead. Then again, she had never imagined that even Yuan Wu would be unable to help her at all.

"Don't kill me! Please, don't kill me! I won't pursue the earlier matter any further. I truly won't. Please spare my life." Madam Hua trembled as she begged. With the aura of death bearing down on her, all she wanted now was to survive. How could she still care about her dignity? Tears were already streaming down her cheeks from terror.

Seeing Yang Kai remain unmoved, she hurriedly called out to Lu Sanniang, "Sanniang, you and I have been sisters for several hundred years. Your elder sister never treated you poorly in the past. Today was Elder Sister's fault, so please be magnanimous and forgive me."

Lu Sanniang was softhearted, and a look of pity appeared on her face when she heard this.

Lu Yuqin, however, was still fuming. A moment ago, this wretched woman had insulted her mother and acted insufferably arrogant, yet in the blink of an eye she had become so meek and submissive. Lu Yuqin found it immensely satisfying, yet at the same time felt deeply uncomfortable.

She had never witnessed a killing before. Only now did she realize just how fragile a person's pride was when their life was threatened.

"Sanniang..." Madam Hua continued to plead.

Lu Sanniang looked dazed, unsure whether she should speak up for Madam Hua again.

She had always been gentle by nature. Although Madam Hua had bullied her many times on this Spirit Island in the past, Lu Sanniang still could not bear to watch her be killed before her very eyes.

In the end, her nature prevailed. She opened her mouth and was just about to plead for mercy when her brow suddenly furrowed and she turned toward the sea.

The next moment, her expression changed drastically and she cried out, "The Dragon Clan!"

A pure Dragon aura was rapidly approaching from that direction, and judging from its intensity, it belonged to a high-ranking member of the Dragon Clan, one far stronger than Fu Qi at the very least. Lu Sanniang had lived on Dragon Isle for so many years that her senses in this regard were naturally keen.

She immediately looked at Yang Kai and urged anxiously, "Brother Yang, stop at once!"

The Dragon Clan had arrived. No matter which member of the Dragon Clan it was, they could not be allowed to witness this scene, or Yang Kai would undoubtedly die.

Yang Kai had clearly noticed the approaching presence long ago, but unlike Lu Sanniang's panic, an extremely peculiar expression appeared on his face. His gaze remained fixed on the direction from which the Dragon Clan member was approaching.

"Hahaha!" Madam Hua suddenly burst into laughter. Her earlier servility vanished without a trace, and venom once more crept across her face. She looked at Yang Kai smugly and sneered, "You want to kill me? Go on, let this Madam see whether you dare! Since all of you dared bully this Madam so badly, I will settle this debt with you properly in a moment. This Madam will make every one of you taste all the tortures this world has to offer!"

With the Dragon Clan's arrival, she immediately became fearless. She glared viciously at Yang Kai, as though she meant to engrave his hateful face into the depths of her mind so she would remember it forever. Then she turned toward Lu Sanniang and her daughter, her icy gaze like a sharp sword that sought to pierce straight through them.

Lu Sanniang's beautiful face lost all color as she cried softly, "Brother Yang, hurry and release her! A Dragon is coming!"

Madam Hua was indeed nothing more than a plaything, but she was still Fu Chi's woman. No matter which member of the Dragon Clan had come, they would not stand by after seeing this scene. Most likely, they would kill Yang Kai.

Lu Sanniang was beside herself with anxiety.

## Chapter 2646 — Why Have You Come Again

"Young Master, this is...?"

Only after Yingfei had flown off did Old Ban cautiously open his mouth to ask.

"Luan Feng's palace, Fengluo Palace!" Yang Kai replied.

"Luan Feng..."

Old Ban's eyes bulged. He realized that what he had experienced today was more shocking than anything in his entire life. Just riding on the back of a Yao King was already enough to make one's hair stand on end, but now he had actually arrived at the palace of a Divine Spirit.

If Yang Kai hadn't brought him here, he probably wouldn't have come in eight lifetimes. But Yang Kai not only made that Yao King Yingfei willingly act as a mount, he even seemed completely at ease coming to this Divine Spirit's palace...

Old Ban couldn't quite figure out what kind of identity or background Yang Kai really had.

As they were speaking, the palace doors suddenly opened, and a beautiful, graceful woman in palace attire walked out, coming straight to stand before Yang Kai. She gave a respectful salute and said, "This servant greets Young Master Yang!"

Yang Kai glanced at her and smiled, "Tianlong, it's been a while."

This woman was the maid Luan Feng had arranged to serve him the last time he visited Fengluo Palace. Although Yang Kai hadn't interacted with her much, they were at least acquainted, so seeing her again now didn't feel unfamiliar.

"It hasn't been that long..." Tianlong forced a smile, then looked up and said, "I wonder what important matter brings Young Master Yang here this time?"

Yang Kai said, "I do have something I need Lady Feng's help with. Where is she?"

Tianlong replied, "Young Master has come at a bad time. A few days ago, Lady Feng happened to enter seclusion."

Hearing this, Yang Kai couldn't help but glance at her and snort, "Lady Feng is a Divine Spirit, her cultivation is a gift of heaven and earth. What kind of seclusion could she possibly need? Is she just unwilling to see me?"

Tianlong hurriedly said, "That's not it at all, Lady really is in seclusion."

Yang Kai ignored her and walked straight into Fengluo Palace.

Seeing this, Tianlong quickly followed with small, hurried steps.

Entering Fengluo Palace, Yang Kai charged straight ahead, calling out as he went, "Lady Feng, Lady Feng, Yang Kai has come to see you!"

Tianlong was at a loss whether to laugh or cry, and could only follow along, constantly reminding, "Young Master, please don't shout, if you disturb Lady's cultivation, she'll blame this servant."

"Lady Feng, Lady Feng!" Yang Kai still ignored her, continuing to call out on his own.

After quite a while, a figure suddenly appeared before Yang Kai. Dignified and elegant, with a deep and far-reaching aura—who else could it be but Luan Feng?

However, at this moment, Luan Feng looked at Yang Kai with a rather resentful expression, as if he had abandoned her after having his way. Her eyes were filled with an accusation of blood and tears.

"Lady Feng!" Yang Kai grinned when he saw her appear, stepped forward and cupped his fists, "Yang Kai greets Lady Feng."

Tianlong, who had followed behind Yang Kai, also came up, looking helplessly at Luan Feng, "My Lady, this servant..."

"You may go." Luan Feng waved her hand at her.

Receiving the order, Tianlong respectfully withdrew.

Luan Feng gave Yang Kai a disdainful look, then turned and walked ahead.

Yang Kai followed along with a cheerful grin, calling out to Little Ling’er and Old Ban to come as well.

When they reached a grand hall, Luan Feng turned around and sat at the head seat. She didn't even bother to greet Yang Kai and the others, simply picked up a steaming cup of tea and sipped it on her own.

"Sit, sit!" Yang Kai gestured to Old Ban.

How would Old Ban dare sit? Just being able to stand here was already more than enough for him. He quickly waved his hands, "This old man will just stand, standing is fine."

The woman before him was none other than the Divine Spirit Luan Feng. Even though she wasn't releasing any overwhelming aura, Old Ban still didn't dare show the slightest disrespect in front of her. He didn't even dare to smile, afraid that the wrong expression would displease the great Divine Spirit.

As for Little Ling'er, he held her tightly by the hand, lest the child accidentally offend Luan Feng.

Since Old Ban wouldn't sit, Yang Kai didn't force him. He just turned to Luan Feng with a grin, "Lady doesn't seem to be in a good mood."

Luan Feng glanced at him and said irritably, "I was in a good mood, but now I'm not. Why have you come again?"

Yang Kai said, "It's been many days since we last met, so I came to see Lady Feng."

Luan Feng nodded slightly, "Well, now you've seen me. Please leave, Young Master."

"Come on now." Yang Kai gave an awkward smile, "I've just arrived, and Lady is already chasing me away. That's not how you treat a guest, is it?"

"If Young Master wishes to stay a few days, I can only welcome you. You can stay where you did last time. If you need anything, just ask Tianlong." As she spoke, Luan Feng stood up, clearly not wanting to spend more time with Yang Kai.

"Wait, wait, wait!" Seeing her about to leave, Yang Kai quickly stopped her.

Luan Feng snorted, "Brat, what exactly did you come here for this time? Speak quickly, I have no mood to waste words with you."

Yang Kai gave a sheepish smile, "Lady's insight is as sharp as ever. I do have a small matter I need your help with."

"Oh..." Luan Feng suddenly laughed with a strange tone, "What could possibly trouble someone like Young Master Yang so much that you need my help? Last time you stirred up the Ancient Land, showing off in front of the Thirty-Two Yao Kings and the Eight Saint Envoys, I didn't see you being so humble then."

Yang Kai said awkwardly, "That was then, this is now. Wasn't that all just a misunderstanding?"

Luan Feng snorted, "Even a Divine Spirit was slain, and you call that a misunderstanding?"

Yang Kai said seriously, "That wasn't my doing, it was Ruoxi. If you want to blame someone, blame her."

Luan Feng frowned, "I have no desire to seek death. Besides, I don't owe you anything, so why should I help you again and again?"

Last time, when Yang Kai brought her to the Netherworld Sect, it ended with their Great Elder being killed. Although it was Luan Feng who struck the fatal blow, if not for Yang Kai, how would she have gone to someone else's sect to stir up trouble in the first place?

It hadn’t been long since Yang Kai last came knocking, and now here he was again. Luan Feng felt a hundred kinds of unease toward him—who knew what trouble this fellow was bringing this time?

"Why be so distant, Lady? It's just a small favor for you." Yang Kai chuckled.

Luan Feng let out a deep sigh, "Go on, what is it?"

Since Yang Kai had come knocking, Luan Feng couldn’t very well refuse him outright. If not for his own sake, then at least for Zhang Ruoxi’s—given how close Yang Kai was to her, and considering that Zhang Ruoxi was a descendant of the Heaven’s Punisher, Luan Feng couldn’t help but feel a deep sense of wariness toward her.

Yang Kai said seriously, "I want to ask Lady Feng to deal with two Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivators!"

"Who?" Luan Feng frowned.

"The Sect Master and Vice Sect Master of Wenqing Sect in the Northern Territory!"

"Wenqing Sect in the Northern Territory?" Luan Feng's brows knitted, "That's a major sect, and I have no enmity with them. Why should I make a move against them?"

"I don't care! Lady Feng, you have to help me with this, no matter what," Yang Kai said stubbornly.

"You..." Luan Feng was so angry she laughed, "Why are you acting shameless in front of me? They're the ones who offended you, not me."

Yang Kai grinned, "Just lend a hand, Lady Feng. Two Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivators, you could crush them with a flick."

Luan Feng snorted, "Easy for you to say. The Northern Territory may not have a sect founded by a Great Emperor, but Great Emperor Miaodan is there. If I attack a human sect for no reason, how will I get away with it?"

Yang Kai said, "Great Emperor Miaodan may be in the Northern Territory, but he's obsessed with alchemy. He won't care about these grudges."

Luan Feng shook her head, "He might not care about other matters, but if I make a move, that's another story. I can't help with this."

"Ah, since Lady Feng says so, I won't force it." Yang Kai sighed, looking helpless.

Luan Feng was stunned, looking at Yang Kai with a strange expression. She hadn't expected him to give up so easily, but soon frowned, feeling that Yang Kai surely wouldn't let things go so simply—he must have another trick up his sleeve.

Sure enough, after getting up, Yang Kai walked out while muttering, "When Ruoxi comes out, and finds her sect destroyed, she'll take revenge anyway. No rush."

"Wait!" Luan Feng quickly called out.

Yang Kai turned around, "Does Lady Feng have further instructions?"

Luan Feng gritted her teeth, "What did you just say? Say it again."

Yang Kai sighed, "Since Lady Feng heard it, why ask again?"

Luan Feng said in a deep voice, "You said that lady's sect was destroyed? What do you mean?"

"What else could it mean? Wenqing Sect wants to destroy Ice Heart Valley. Ruoxi is from Ice Heart Valley. I'm powerless alone, so I came to ask Lady Feng for help. But since you have your concerns, I won't insist."

Luan Feng was surprised, "That lady is from Ice Heart Valley?"

Ice Heart Valley and Wenqing Sect were both top sects in the Northern Territory. Now, from Yang Kai's words, it seemed the two were at war, and Ice Heart Valley was in a bad situation.

"Yes, didn't I say so before?" Yang Kai asked in return.

Luan Feng grew solemn, "She's really from Ice Heart Valley? You're not lying to me?"

Yang Kai replied earnestly, "How could it be false? That crazy woman last time was Ruoxi's Senior Aunt Ji Yao. She also made a move. You should have noticed her cultivation technique, right? That's an authentic ice technique."

Luan Feng thought back carefully and realized it was just as Yang Kai said. Although Ji Yao hadn't done much, as a Divine Spirit, she could naturally sense the ice-element Emperor Yuan in Ji Yao's body.

As for Zhang Ruoxi, she hadn't shown any signs of cultivating ice techniques, but she had come together with Ji Yao, and they seemed close.

Luan Feng didn't know whether to believe Yang Kai's words or not.

While she was lost in thought, Yang Kai cupped his fists, "Lady Feng, I'll take my leave. When Ruoxi comes out of the Blood Gate, please tell her that her sect has been destroyed, and ask her to return to the Northern Territory to avenge them!"

Luan Feng was livid the moment she heard this.

If Zhang Ruoxi really was from Ice Heart Valley, there was no way she could tell her that. If Ruoxi asked how her sect was destroyed, how could Luan Feng answer?

Could she say she knew about it but stood by and did nothing?

"Stop right there!" Seeing Yang Kai really about to leave, Luan Feng hurriedly shouted.

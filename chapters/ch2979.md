## Chapter 2979 — They Really Came Back

Zhu Lie snorted, "A wave that crashes against a boulder will only shatter. It will accomplish nothing else."

Yang Kai raised his head. "Then we'll have to see whether Dragon Isle is hard enough to smash me to pieces."

Zhu Lie stared at him in astonishment, seemingly trying to find some hint that he was joking. To his disappointment, although Yang Kai's expression could not be called solemn, he was definitely not joking either.

Slowly shaking his head, Zhu Lie said with a complicated expression, "You're truly a madman."

Yang Kai laughed heartily. "I'll take that as a compliment."

Zhu Lie had no desire to continue discussing the matter with him, because Yang Kai's attitude had already made everything clear. He would never give up on Zhu Qing, nor would he submit to Dragon Isle's rules.

Since that was the case, there was no point wasting any more words. He suddenly turned his head and asked, "What did you come here for?"

While the two were talking, they had unknowingly arrived at Demon Fury City. Only a little more than half a day had passed since they came through earlier, and the aftermath of the disturbance had yet to subside. The corpses of several Demon Kings still lay where they had fallen, with no one daring to collect them, and the stench of blood filled the air.

When the many Demons of Demon Fury City suddenly saw these two terrifying fellows actually come charging back, this time side by side and brimming with aggression as though ready to wreak havoc, their faces went pale and their hearts filled with despair. They felt that there was no escaping this calamity.

Even so, not a single Demon abandoned the city and fled for their life. The Demon Race had occupied this place for more than a hundred thousand years, facing Human Sovereign City from afar, and the conflict between the two races had never ceased. If they were timid and afraid of trouble, they would not be worthy of calling themselves Demons.

Demons emerged from every corner of the city and gradually gathered near Yang Kai and Zhu Lie. Their cultivations varied greatly, but their numbers were enormous.

The Demon Kings also assembled in one place, staring coldly at the two humans flying through the air from the direction of Yin Wind Mountain as the fighting intent within them steadily rose.

Yang Kai and Zhu Lie stopped a hundred zhang away from the group of Demon Kings. Although there were only two of them, it seemed as though a mighty army had descended, their momentum utterly overwhelming the Demon Race.

"What are you planning to do here?" Zhu Lie frowned. "Don't tell me you intend to slaughter the city."

Humans and Demons were irreconcilable enemies. Even within the tiny Revolving World, the enmity between the two races had persisted for more than a hundred thousand years, so it would not have been strange if Yang Kai had truly come to massacre the city.

"Why would I slaughter the city? All that killing and fighting is the dullest thing imaginable." Yang Kai curled his lips and swept his gaze over the Demon Kings opposite him. Then, wearing an innocent and harmless smile, he called to a figure hiding at the back of the crowd, "That pretty Big Sister over there, would you come out for a chat?"

The Demon Kings all turned their heads and looked in the direction of his gaze.

Behind the crowd, a graceful figure was furtively hiding as though afraid to show her true face, constantly keeping herself concealed within the shadows of others.

She had already hidden with the utmost care, yet Yang Kai had still picked her out of the crowd at a glance. Her heart immediately filled with misery. She secretly cursed Yang Kai's sharp eyes and regretted provoking him for no reason earlier. Now she had attracted the attention of this killing god and could not shake him off, with no idea what fate awaited her.

Under everyone's gaze, the Succubus had nowhere left to hide and could only walk out bashfully. Forcing a strained smile onto her face, she knowingly asked, "Sir... were you speaking to me? Surely you weren't speaking to me, were you? Heh heh heh..."

No one joined in, and even she found her laughter terribly awkward, so she immediately fell silent. Lowering her eyes meekly, she looked so delicate and pitiful that anyone would feel sorry for her.

"Yes, you." Yang Kai nodded to her with a warm smile.

Although she had known this would be the answer, her face still paled when Yang Kai confirmed it. She asked in a trembling voice, "May I ask what Sir... wishes to discuss with me?"

Yang Kai chuckled. "You were calling me Little Brother just now, but now you're addressing me as Sir. Big Sister is truly strange."

The Succubus’ face turned even paler, tears welling up in her eyes, making those peach blossom eyes of hers appear all the more alluring. Lightly biting her thin lip, she said, “This humble one was blind and offended you, Sir. Fortunately, Sir is magnanimous and did not stoop to my level. This humble one will never dare do so again. I beg Sir to spare my life.”

She humbled herself and begged for mercy, but the Demon Kings beside her wore ugly expressions. Every one of them felt that this wretched woman had disgraced the Demon Race and caused the despicable Human Race to look down on them. They wished they could rush forward and tear her mouth apart.

Yet they could only entertain the thought. Although the Demon Kings looked down on the Succubus's attitude, they also knew this was no time to provoke a conflict. If a fight truly broke out, these two young men were more than capable of washing the whole of Demon Fury City in blood.

"There's no need to be so nervous. You're acting as though we're going to eat you." Yang Kai smiled lightly at the Succubus. "This Young Master only came here to ask a few questions. Mm, is there somewhere convenient to talk?"

Hearing this, the Succubus did not know whether she should believe him, but after a moment's thought, she realized it made no difference even if she did not. His arm was thicker than her thigh.

"There is, there is. Would that place suit Sir?" As she spoke, the Succubus pointed in a nearby direction.

Yang Kai turned his head and saw a spacious square. Stone tables and chairs stood along its edges, while numerous statues occupied the square itself. Every statue was tall and powerfully built with a fierce visage. They had clearly been carved by an expert, each one so lifelike that it almost seemed real.

There were both men and women among the statues, and they belonged to many different races. Even with Yang Kai's considerable knowledge of the Demon Race, he could not identify them all.

"That will do!" Yang Kai nodded and flew over first.

Zhu Lie frowned, having no idea what Yang Kai intended to do, but he could only follow.

The Succubus, however, stood rooted to the spot with a troubled expression. She truly lacked the courage to face Yang Kai, but if she did not go, she feared she would anger him.

She turned to the other Demon Kings as though seeking someone to rely on.

Every Demon King her gaze swept across looked away. Some raised their heads to study the sky, while others whispered with their companions as though discussing some grave matter, their expressions exceedingly solemn. Most outrageous of all, one Demon King had actually started counting the hairs on his own head!

The Succubus felt as though she had been completely isolated, leaving her utterly lost and helpless.

"All Demon Kings, get over here! Anyone who doesn't come will die!"

Yang Kai's voice came from afar. It was not loud, yet it made the expressions of the many Demon Kings change.

The Succubus's face immediately lit up with joy, and she nearly burst into laughter. However, when she saw the ashen faces of her clansmen, she somehow managed to hold it in.

She snorted lightly, then twisted her slender waist as she hurried after Yang Kai, every movement brimming with allure.

With her leading the way, the other Demon Kings followed one after another. Naturally, a few proud and unyielding ones remained standing stubbornly in place, unwilling to obey Yang Kai's command, but several of their companions dragged them away by force.

They had no desire to anger those two terrifying fellows again.

In the square, Yang Kai and Zhu Lie each took a seat. Sweeping his eyes over the statues, Yang Kai turned and asked, "What are all these?"

The Succubus was the first to arrive. Standing less than three zhang from Yang Kai, she trembled lightly, her mouth dry and her tongue parched, without the slightest sense of security. She was anxiously wondering what Yang Kai wanted to ask and how she should answer without angering him when she heard his question. Her heart immediately relaxed, and she hurriedly replied, "These are statues of Demon Fury City's successive generations of Demon Kings."

Yang Kai raised his brow. "Demon Fury City has stood in this world for more than a hundred thousand years. Surely these aren't the only Demon Kings to have left their names behind?"

Although there were many statues in the square, they numbered only one or two thousand, fewer than ten thousand. It was impossible for Demon Fury City to have produced so few Demon Kings over more than a hundred thousand years.

The Succubus hurriedly explained, "Of course not. However, only the First Demon King is qualified to leave their name here." After speaking, she added, "The First Demon King is the strongest expert in Demon Fury City."

"So that's how it is." Yang Kai nodded lightly, then asked with great interest, "Was the First Demon King among those who died earlier?"

The corner of the Succubus's mouth twitched. After carefully considering Yang Kai's intentions, she finally nodded.

"Who did it?" Yang Kai was somewhat surprised, feeling that the First Demon King had truly suffered terrible luck.

The Succubus cautiously glanced at Zhu Lie and forced a smile. "We must thank this Sir for giving the First Demon King an early opportunity to leave his name behind... After all, according to the rules, his statue could only be placed here after his death."

Zhu Lie coldly snorted. "This Dragon wasn't singling anyone out. All of you Demon Kings are nothing but weaklings!"

The Demon Kings of Demon Fury City happened to be arriving in scattered groups at that moment. Hearing Zhu Lie's words, they felt as though their chests were blocked and found it difficult to breathe. Yet they dared to be angry but not speak, their expressions turning extremely ugly.

The Succubus did not know how to respond either. Although she intended to curry favor with Yang Kai and Zhu Lie by humbling herself, she was not foolish enough to agree with him in front of so many Demon Kings.

Still, this red-haired young man was truly terrifying. It was not that the First Demon King had been weak. In fact, no one who could cultivate to the Demon King Realm was weak, and the one who claimed the title of First Demon King was the strongest expert among all the Demon Kings.

Even so, the red-haired young man had killed him in a single move.

The Succubus now wanted only to know who this red-haired young man was and what cultivation he possessed.

"What's the story behind that statue in the very center? It towers over all the others, but why can't I see its face?" Yang Kai turned his gaze toward the center of the square.

An enormous statue stood there. Compared with it, the other statues were like grandfathers beside a grandson, differing in size by at least tenfold. Strangely, while all the other statues possessed complete features and appeared lifelike, the face of this one was perfectly smooth, without the slightest trace of carving.

Hearing Yang Kai ask about this statue, the Succubus immediately became solemn. "This is the divine statue of the Great Demon God of our Demon Domain! However, because no one has ever seen the Great Demon God, it..."

"Great Demon God!" Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and instantly recalled his precious experiences within the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. In the passage between the two worlds, the Great Demon God had launched a strike across the void that nearly scattered Qing's Soul. Even now, the memory of that terrifying might remained vivid in his mind.

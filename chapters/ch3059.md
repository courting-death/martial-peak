## Chapter 3059 — Something Major Has Happened on Dragon Isle

Dragon Punishment was the most powerful means of destroying a Dragon Clan member's Origin, and a forbidden technique throughout the entire Dragon Clan.

If Yang Kai's earlier threat to perish together with Fu Chi was something the Second Elder could just barely accept in her fury, then this was a sight she absolutely could not bear to see. A Dragon could die, but their Origin could not be destroyed!

"Stop!" Zhu Yan thundered, his expression as dark as water.

Sure enough, Yang Kai stopped. The stream of syllables spilling from his mouth ceased, and the Thunder Dragon being stripped from above Fu Chi's head was no longer in such agony.

"What further instruction does Great Elder have for me?" Yang Kai asked in a low voice, secretly feeling a lingering fear. Although he had acted as if he had nothing to lose, who would willingly seek death unless forced into a corner? Fortunately, Great Elder had given him some face and stepped in at the critical moment; otherwise, he truly would not have known how to continue.

With matters having reached this point, he had no path of retreat. He could either negotiate terms with the Dragon Clan, pay a certain price, and take Zhu Qing away, or cast aside all pretenses and fight them with everything he had. The latter was not what he wanted, while for the former, he was already mentally prepared to pay a sufficient price.

Only after seeing Yang Kai halt the Dragon Punishment Secret Technique did Zhu Yan breathe a sigh of relief. Since this young man had obtained the Dragon Clan's inheritance, been acknowledged by the Ancestral Dragon Origin, and fused with it, he could no longer be regarded as an ordinary human. Continuing to put on the Dragon Clan's arrogant airs would accomplish nothing.

He pondered for a moment and was just about to speak when an unexpected change suddenly occurred.

A tremendous hum abruptly rolled in from the distant horizon, and the whole world seemed to tremble. Immediately afterward, a pillar of light erupted far in the distance, while the surrounding Spiritual Qi churned and shook, filling the air with a bleak unease.

The Dragon Clan's three Elders abruptly turned their heads and looked toward the source of the disturbance.

In the next instant, all three Dragons' expressions changed.

The Second Elder, Fu Zhun, even exclaimed in a low voice, "Not good!"

As soon as the words fell, her delicate figure flickered and she sped off in that direction like lightning. She vanished in the blink of an eye, no longer able to spare any attention for the matter here.

This sudden turn left the several hundred mortals dumbfounded. Even Yang Kai's eyes shone with surprise as they darted about.

No one knew what had sent the Second Elder hurrying away.

Logically speaking, what was happening here concerned the Dragon Clan's honour and dignity. After all, snatching away a bride on the day of a Dragon Clan wedding was an outrage. The Second Elder had also maintained an utterly unyielding stance and even declared that the Dragon Clan and Yang Kai would fight to the death.

This could almost be called irreconcilable enmity.

What could possibly make an expert like Fu Zhun lose colour and rush off to deal with it, without even bothering about Yang Kai stealing the bride?

Something had happened on Dragon Isle, and it was something major!

This unbelievable thought arose in everyone's mind. Watching the Elders' expressions, they saw that Great Elder Zhu Yan and Fourth Elder Zhu Kong both looked grave, as though facing a formidable enemy.

They had not shown such expressions even when Yang Kai tore off both of Fu Chi's arms.

The other Dragons also looked alarmed and uncertain, as though they had some idea of what had happened.

"Qing'er, what place lies in that direction? What happened?" Yang Kai secretly sent Zhu Qing a transmission. Excitement stirred within him as he keenly sensed that this might be an opportunity.

Of the Dragon Clan's three Elders, Second Elder Fu Zhun was the most difficult to deal with. Had she not been so unreasonable, Yang Kai would not have gone so far earlier. Now that she had actually left of her own accord, he was naturally delighted.

Without the Second Elder as such a tremendous obstacle, perhaps with some effort everyone could sit down and have an amicable discussion with Great Elder Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong. After all, both Elders belonged to the Zhu branch of the Dragon Clan and were somewhat partial to Zhu Qing, as could be seen from their earlier attitudes.

Hearing Yang Kai's question, Zhu Qing replied, "The Dragon Tomb. That is where the Dragon Tomb lies. Something seems to have happened there."

"The Dragon Tomb?" Yang Kai raised his brow.

He naturally knew what the Dragon Tomb was. It was the place every Dragon Clan member would enter before death and was forbidden ground to the entire clan. Zhu Qing's primary purpose in going to the Frozen Earth to search for the lost Dragon Clan Origin had been to bring it back to the Dragon Tomb.

Since the Dragon Tomb was the Dragon Clan's burial ground, what could possibly happen there? Yet the Second Elder had run off without even a word. This whole affair was deeply strange.

Zhu Qing could not explain it either. She only knew that something must have happened to the Dragon Tomb.

While Yang Kai and Zhu Qing were communicating, the Great Elder and Fourth Elder were also communicating in secret. No one knew what the Great Elder said to the Fourth Elder, but Zhu Kong's expression suddenly changed and became exceedingly grave.

Yang Kai's eyes shifted about before he suddenly looked up and said, "Great Elder, Fourth Elder, this Junior admits that some of his actions today were improper, but I was forced by circumstances. Everything I did was for Qing'er's happiness, and only then did I steel my heart and act so ruthlessly. I hope the two Elders can forgive me."

His conflict with the Dragon Clan was largely centred on Second Elder Fu Zhun. Now that Fu Zhun had left, if he could take this opportunity to persuade the Great Elder and Fourth Elder, he might be able to depart safely.

He simply did not know whether the Dragon Clan was willing to let him off. Uneasy over what he might gain or lose, he could not help feeling somewhat apprehensive.

Hearing this, Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong looked at Yang Kai, both exasperated and amused.

When confronting the Second Elder earlier, this brat had displayed an unyielding stance and refused to show the slightest weakness, seemingly eager to fight Fu Zhun to the death on the spot to prove his love for Zhu Qing. Who could have expected him to start speaking softly in the very next moment? He truly knew when to yield and when to stand firm.

With a solemn expression, Zhu Yan said, "You injured one of my clansmen and humiliated the Dragon Clan's honour. That cannot be explained away with a simple claim that you were forced by circumstances."

Yang Kai raised his brow and said in a booming voice, "Then what does Great Elder intend to do? You saw Qing'er's attitude for yourself. A melon forcibly plucked is not sweet, so why must the Dragon Clan continue deceiving itself? Fu Chi is a member of the Dragon Clan, but is Zhu Qing not one as well? You should give more consideration to her wishes."

Zhu Yan shook his head. "That may be so, but today's affair is destined not to end peacefully."

Yang Kai's face sank. "Since Great Elder has said so, this Junior can only accept whatever moves you make. Please forgive me if I cause offence." His attitude toward the Great Elder was completely different from his attitude toward the Second Elder.

He had already offended Fu Zhun beyond all hope of reconciliation, so if there was still any room to turn matters around, he did not wish to truly offend the Great Elder as well.

"Today's bloodshed should ordinarily be repaid in blood," Zhu Yan said in a deep voice, before abruptly changing tack. "However, this old man does not wish to bully the young with his strength and tarnish the Dragon Clan's reputation."

Yang Kai's eyes lit up. "What does Great Elder mean?"

"Release Fu Chi, and this old man will not act against you."

"Truly?" Yang Kai was delighted. On all of Dragon Isle, the ones he feared most were none other than the Great Elder and Second Elder. These two were existences comparable to Great Emperors. Now that the Second Elder had inexplicably left ahead of time, if the Great Elder also refrained from acting against him, then he could come and go across Dragon Isle as he pleased. The Fourth Elder alone would likely have difficulty keeping him here.

In an instant, Yang Kai felt that the Great Elder had become warm and amiable, and even his stern face seemed much gentler. As expected of a Dragon from the Zhu branch. Deep down, he was still somewhat partial to Zhu Qing and was taking a roundabout way to make things easier for them.

“The Great Elder of the Dragon Clan is a man of his word. This Junior naturally trusts you," Yang Kai said, nodding repeatedly with a joyous expression, causing the several hundred mortals to roll their eyes.

A moment ago, he had still looked doubtful, but in the blink of an eye, he was already showering the Great Elder with praise.

"Then release him," Zhu Yan said, looking at him calmly.

Yang Kai wasted no words and directly hurled away Fu Chi, whom he had been clutching in his hand.

Fu Chi had lost both arms and was already deathly pale from the pain. He had thought he would surely die here today, but who could have expected him to find a chance of survival in the depths of despair? Once Yang Kai tossed him away, he immediately grabbed the two severed arms Yang Kai had discarded and sped off in one direction.

He did not dare remain here and only wanted to find a place to properly treat his injuries. Although Yang Kai had torn off his arms, the Dragon Clan possessed tenacious vitality. So long as he recuperated for a time, he would certainly be able to restore them.

Before leaving, he glared viciously at Yang Kai, plainly determined to return and avenge this hatred.

He was an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon. Had Yang Kai not caught him off guard with his feint to the east and strike to the west, Zhu Qing could never have subdued him outright. Now that he had suffered such a tremendous loss, how could he possibly swallow this humiliation?

Before so many people, he had been wounded and Zhu Qing had been snatched away. If he could endure such pent-up rage, then he would not be Fu Chi.

Among the crowd, Li Jiao stared at the fleeing Fu Chi with a gloomy expression, conflict written all over his face.

Earlier, when Yang Kai taught Fu Chi a lesson and tore off both his arms, Li Jiao had felt immensely gratified.

If not for this beast, how could Sanniang have suffered for two or three hundred years? He had wished Yang Kai would simply kill Fu Chi.

Yet now that Yang Kai had reached an agreement with the Dragon Clan's Great Elder and released Fu Chi, Li Jiao felt a trace of disappointment as well as a trace of relief.

He was disappointed that Fu Chi had escaped this calamity, but relieved that Fu Chi had not died at Yang Kai's hands.

Only by taking vengeance with his own hands could he vent his hatred! Fu Chi's escape from death had therefore given Li Jiao a chance to avenge himself.

But Fu Chi was still an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon. Even though he was severely wounded, Li Jiao knew he was no match for him. His bloodline was merely Third-Tier Lower-Level, so how could he have any chance of victory against an Eighth-Tier Dragon? Unless he could raise his own bloodline.

Thus, he hesitated and struggled, wondering what he had to do to kill Fu Chi!

He knew full well what consequences would follow from killing a Dragon Clan member, but there were things a man should do and things he should not. Everyone carried a scale within their heart by which to judge such matters.

While he was still hesitating, Fu Chi had already fled without a trace.

Li Jiao was not the only one in the crowd watching Fu Chi. Another pair of eyes stared greedily in the direction he had fled, the expression within them shifting incessantly.

"I have released him. Great Elder had better keep his word." Yang Kai turned to look at Zhu Yan.

Zhu Yan nodded. "This old man naturally will not go back on his word."

After speaking, he glanced at Zhu Kong, then turned and left.

Judging by the direction he was heading, he was also going to the Dragon Tomb, and he was no slower than the Second Elder. After only a few flickers, he had disappeared from everyone's sight.

This discovery sent an uproar through the crowd.

## Chapter 2739 — You Dare Steal from the Dragon Clan

“Dragon Blood Pill…” Yang Kai murmured, his face lighting up with excitement.

According to Ji Ying, this Dragon Blood Flower could be refined into Dragon Blood Pills, which not only tempered the bloodline of the Dragon Clan, but also significantly enhanced physical strength, not to mention other effects.

His own physique was already extremely formidable. If he could refine this Dragon Blood Flower into Dragon Blood Pills and take them, it would be like adding wings to a tiger.

Yang Kai’s heart stirred. He looked at Ji Ying and asked, “Brother Ji, do you have the recipe for Dragon Blood Pills?”

Ji Ying scratched his cheek and coughed dryly. “I do, but…” He glanced at Zhu Qing.

There was a Dragon Girl here, so he didn’t dare discuss the refinement of Dragon Blood Pills with Yang Kai. After all, the Dragon Blood Flower was of immense importance to Dragon Isle. If he angered the Dragon Girl, even his status as a disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan might not help.

“You want Dragon Blood Flowers?” Zhu Qing looked at Yang Kai.

“You have them?” Yang Kai’s eyes brightened. It made sense—Zhu Qing was a Dragon girl, so she probably had quite a few Dragon Blood Flowers. If she could bring them out, he could refine more Dragon Blood Pills for himself. That would be wonderful.

“I do.” Zhu Qing nodded.

Yang Kai was overjoyed, but before he could speak, Zhu Qing said, “But they’re all left on Dragon Isle. There’s a clan prohibition—Dragon Blood Flowers can’t be taken off Dragon Isle.”

His excitement was instantly doused. Yang Kai complained, “Then why mention it at all?”

Thinking about it, he realized he’d already checked Zhu Qing’s Spatial Ring before. If she really had Dragon Blood Flowers, he would have found them long ago.

Ji Ying broke out in a cold sweat again. Yang Kai’s attitude toward the Dragon girl was so casual, even rude, that it made Ji Ying nervous.

Zhu Qing smiled slightly, a sly glint in her beautiful eyes. “If you come with me to Dragon Isle, I’ll give you Dragon Blood Flowers. I have about ten of them!”

Ji Ying’s eyes widened in shock.

This Dragon Girl… was actually inviting Yang Kai to Dragon Isle? Was he hearing things?

Dragon Isle was the most mysterious place in the Star Realm. Ordinary people couldn’t even figure out where it was, let alone visit. The Dragon Clan considered themselves the leaders of all living beings and never invited outsiders to Dragon Isle. Only the Great Emperors had the right to set foot there, but even they didn’t visit often, and certainly never received Dragon Blood Flowers from the Dragon Clan.

But now, this Dragon Girl not only invited Yang Kai to Dragon Isle, she even said she’d give him ten Dragon Blood Flowers…

Ji Ying was stunned. Even though it had nothing to do with him, he hurriedly shot Yang Kai a look.

This was a rare opportunity—what was there to hesitate about? He should agree immediately. If it were him, he’d have gone without a second thought.

“If you keep pushing, I’ll get angry.” Yang Kai said, glaring at Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing pouted and reached out to Yang Kai. “Then give me back the box.”

With a flick of his hand, the box vanished. He turned away. “What box? I didn’t see anything!”

Zhu Qing said, “The Dragon Blood Flower belongs to Dragon Isle. Aren’t you afraid it’ll burn your hand?”

Yang Kai replied, “What Dragon Blood Flower? I don’t know what you’re talking about!”

Zhu Qing was both angry and amused. Yang Kai was clearly refusing to return what he’d taken. If anyone else had stolen the Dragon Blood Flower, Zhu Qing would have acted immediately. But although Yang Kai wasn’t a member of the Dragon Clan, he possessed the Ancestral Dragon Origin, so he was qualified to use the Dragon Blood Flower.

Frowning, Zhu Qing didn’t argue further.

Ji Ying was dumbfounded. Was there really someone who dared steal from the Dragon Clan?

He felt that he’d never been so shocked in his life. His mind was struggling to keep up.

“Qing’er, the weather outside is nice. Go get some sun—it’s not good for your skin to stay indoors.” Yang Kai suddenly smiled at Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing snorted, knowing he was trying to send her away. She stomped her foot and left.

Yang Kai then shot a look at Hua Qingsi and the three Yao Kings. The group all took their leave.

In an instant, only Yang Kai and Ji Ying remained in the hall, along with Ji Ying’s eldest disciple, who was still meditating.

Yang Kai casually tossed the wooden box back to Ji Ying. “Brother Ji, please refine a batch of Dragon Blood Pills for me.”

“Huh?” Ji Ying was stunned, woodenly catching the box.

Yang Kai laughed. “Brother Ji, don’t forget—you’re now the Chief Alchemist of Lingxiao Palace!”

Ji Ying paused, then smiled bitterly. “I lost the bet, so I won’t go back on my word. But I need to return to Medicine Pill Valley and report to my master first…”

Yang Kai nodded. “No problem, Brother Ji. Take care of your own affairs. Come to Lingxiao Palace when you’re free.”

“Thank you, Brother Yang.” Ji Ying thanked him, feeling deeply moved. Before coming to Lingxiao Palace, he’d been free and unrestrained, but after this visit, he’d become their Chief Alchemist.

This wasn’t the outcome he’d hoped for.

But thinking further, Yang Kai’s own alchemy skills were exceptional, even faintly touching the essence of the Dao. If he had the chance to exchange pointers with Yang Kai often, it would be greatly beneficial.

With that thought, he felt less resistant to becoming Lingxiao Palace’s Chief Alchemist, and even started to look forward to it.

“Brother Yang, there’s something I’d like to confirm with you.” Ji Ying’s expression suddenly turned serious.

Yang Kai smiled, already knowing what he meant. “Is it about my cauldron?”

Ji Ying trembled, his face full of surprise. “Could it be…?”

Yang Kai nodded. “Brother Ji, your guess is correct. My cauldron is called the Purple Void Cauldron!”

“It really is the Purple Void Cauldron!” Ji Ying’s expression changed. When he first saw Yang Kai’s cauldron, he’d felt it looked familiar, but he’d never seen it before.

Later, when they refined Emperor Yuan Pills together, their process was almost identical, which made Ji Ying even more puzzled.

After thinking it over, there was only one possibility…

“The Purple Void Cauldron was bestowed by my master to our Third Senior Brother. I joined later and never met him, but I’ve heard of him. Now that the cauldron is in Brother Yang’s hands, may I ask where my Third Senior Brother, Gongsun Mu, is?”

Gongsun Mu had committed a grave mistake years ago and was expelled by their master. Many years had passed, and there was no news of Gongsun Mu in the Star Realm. Now, seeing the Purple Void Cauldron, Ji Ying, as a Junior Brother, naturally wanted to ask about Gongsun Mu.

He knew that although their master had expelled Gongsun Mu from Medicine Pill Valley, he still cared about him. After all, there was a bond between master and disciple. The Third Senior Brother was said to be a genius in alchemy, with talent unmatched even by the First and Second Senior Brothers. Unfortunately, he went astray. If not, his achievements would likely surpass all his peers.

Yang Kai sighed. “Senior Gongsun Mu passed away long ago.”

Ji Ying was shaken, then smiled bitterly. “So it’s true.”

It seemed he’d suspected as much, but hearing Yang Kai confirm it still made him sad—a prodigy in alchemy had fallen before his time.

Yang Kai said, “I found the Purple Void Cauldron in a cave left behind by Senior Gongsun Mu, along with some of his legacy.”

Ji Ying smiled bitterly, understanding now why their process of refining Emperor Yuan Pills had been so similar. Yang Kai had inherited Gongsun Mu’s legacy, including his pill recipes, which were originally passed down from Great Emperor Miaodan, the same as Ji Ying’s.

As they spoke, Yang Kai took out a jade slip and handed it to Ji Ying. “This was left by Senior Gongsun Mu. I’ll entrust it to you to take back to Medicine Pill Valley. But I’d like to keep the Purple Void Cauldron, so please ask the Great Emperor if that’s allowed.”

Ji Ying took it and nodded. “Brother Yang, your generosity is admirable. I’ll report everything to my master. I believe he won’t take back the Purple Void Cauldron. Someone inheriting our Third Senior Brother’s legacy should make him happy.”

Yang Kai said, “That would be best.”

After a pause, he asked curiously, “Senior Gongsun Mu’s last words said he was expelled for studying the Human Pill technique. Brother Ji, what exactly is this Human Pill technique?”

Ji Ying’s expression changed, becoming serious. “Brother Yang, are you interested in the Human Pill technique?”

Yang Kai waved his hand. “I was just asking. Since it offended the Great Emperor, it must be something bad. Why would I be interested?”

Ji Ying’s expression eased. After thinking for a moment, he said, “Actually, there’s no harm in telling you. I trust your character—you wouldn’t pursue the Human Pill technique.”

Yang Kai looked at him curiously.

Ji Ying explained, “Our Third Senior Brother’s Human Pill technique used people as the catalyst to refine Spiritual Pills. The higher the cultivator’s strength, the better the pill produced.”

“What?” Yang Kai’s face changed. “Using people as the catalyst?”

Ji Ying sighed. “Exactly. That’s why our master was furious. If not for the First Senior Brother pleading for him, the Third Senior Brother would have been killed on the spot. After I joined, the First Senior Brother told me about it as a warning.”

Yang Kai said, “Senior Gongsun Mu went astray, but he regretted it in his later years. Unfortunately, he was too ashamed to face the Great Emperor and ended up passing away in his cave.”

Ji Ying smiled bitterly. “Everyone makes mistakes.”

As they spoke, Ji Ying’s disciple suddenly opened his eyes, exhaling deeply, his face full of excitement.

“Did you gain anything?” Ji Ying asked with a smile.

The eldest disciple nodded quickly, stood up, and bowed respectfully to Yang Kai. “Thank you, Master Yang.”

Ji Ying laughed. “If you ever become an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, it’ll be thanks to your Uncle Yang’s efforts. Don’t forget his kindness.”

The disciple said, “I would never dare forget.”

Yang Kai smiled. “It’s his own opportunity—no need to thank me!” His eyes shifted. “Now that your master is the Chief Alchemist of Lingxiao Palace, why not stay here as well?”

“Uh…” The disciple hesitated. He couldn’t make that decision himself.

Ji Ying laughed and scolded, “Brother Yang, you’re getting greedy. Should I bring over the First, Second, and Fourth Senior Brothers too?”

“That would be perfect.”

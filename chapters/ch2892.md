## Chapter 2892 — Guardian

Perhaps because he had heard Yang Kai’s call, an aged face appeared on the trunk before Yang Kai, smiling faintly as he said, “Shaman Niu, we meet again.”

“Yes, we meet again. I didn’t expect to see you here.” Yang Kai wiped the blood from his face.

The elderly face spoke kindly, “I truly wasn’t mistaken about you. You’ve grown quickly. Perhaps, in the future, the world will be yours.”

Yang Kai shook his head, not commenting on this, and asked curiously, “Senior, weren’t you in Frost Snow City? Is this your avatar?”

“Avatar? No, no, this isn’t an avatar. This is truly me.”

Yang Kai's expression grew solemn as he immediately grasped the implication: "Senior, are you planning to sacrifice yourself?"

Qing burst out in hearty laughter. “The Boundary Passage is far too mysterious. If one wishes to seal it, this is the only way. I’ve lived long enough and witnessed two invasions by the demons. Last time, I was powerless. This time, I want to give it my all. Over all these years, I’ve received much care from the Barbarian Tribe.”

“Maybe there’s another way.” Yang Kai frowned.

The face on the trunk shook, as if shaking its head. “Once I left Frost Snow City, I could never return.”

In that moment, Yang Kai understood many things. The Divine Tree had guarded Frost Snow City for tens of thousands of years, never once leaving. It wasn’t that he didn’t want to, but that he couldn’t. There must have been a deep connection between him and that land.

Yet this time, to seal the Boundary Passage, he left the land that had nurtured him for tens of thousands of years, condensed all his essence into a single point, and hid within that seven-colored mud, letting Yang Kai bring him beneath the Boundary Passage.

This was an irreversible process. Qing’s current second growth was nothing more than a final flare before the end.

Yang Kai stood in solemn respect, placing his hand over his chest. “The Barbarian Tribe will witness your sacrifice, Senior. Future generations will remember your deeds.”

Qing laughed loudly. “No need to comfort me. I’m already prepared.” He paused, then became much more serious. “But before that, I still need your help.”

“Rest assured, Senior. My Shaman Niu’s tribe will guard this place.”

“Good.” The aged face nodded, then faded away.

Yang Kai turned to look at Die. “Why aren’t you saying anything?”

Die replied, “What is there to say?”

“Did you already know?”

Die said, “That’s right.”

Yang Kai looked at her strangely, trying to discern something from her face. But Die’s expression was indifferent, as if she and Qing were mere strangers. She was very different from the Die he had first met two years ago; her personality had changed greatly. Yang Kai even wondered if she was still the same Die as before.

He shook his head, dismissing the thought.

Qing’s rapid growth had not stopped. While he was speaking with Yang Kai, the massive Divine Tree had already grown from fifty zhang to a hundred zhang tall. Looking up, one could hardly see where the crown ended.

Everyone on the battlefield noticed this strange phenomenon.

The Barbarians cheered. Many recognized that this was the guardian deity of Frost Snow City. With excited shouts, more people learned the news, and morale soared. Each person seemed to be reborn with new vigor.

Although Qing was only the guardian deity of the Frost Snow Tribe, his fame had long spread throughout the various Barbarian tribes. Though no one had ever seen Qing display his power, everyone said he was an expert on par with a Shaman Saint.

Because he was not of the Barbarian Tribe, the tribe only had four Shaman Saints. Yet he was another Saint beyond those four.

At this moment, such a powerful ally suddenly appeared in the midst of the chaotic battlefield, deep within the demon army’s ranks. Naturally, this lifted the spirits of the tribesmen.

It was as if they could already see the demon army collapsing and fleeing for thousands of li. Qing’s appearance caused the Barbarians’ morale to soar, and the once-stalemated battle now seemed to tilt in their favor.

In contrast, the demons looked as if they were mourning the loss of their parents. Though they didn’t know what the giant tree was, the unknown was the most terrifying. Especially since the tree was so massive and imposing, it created a powerful visual impact. As it grew taller and taller, it seemed as if it would pierce the heavens. The emerald light surrounding the tree also seemed to have a miraculous purifying effect, cleansing all the Demonic Qi that drifted near it.

Many demons who didn’t know the truth tried to rush over and destroy the tree’s growth, but were all slain by the great shamans of Shaman Niu’s tribe guarding nearby.

The sudden appearance of the Divine Tree caused chaos among the demons.

But soon, the situation stabilized. It seemed someone had given an order, and the demons began to form ranks and launch an organized assault on the Divine Tree.

The focus of the battlefield instantly shifted to beneath the Boundary Passage.

Shaman Niu’s tribe endured unimaginable pressure as demons surged in from all directions like a tidal wave. They fought fearlessly, and not even the Soul-Devouring Worms could stop their charge.

The cavalry of Shaman Niu’s tribe, led by Ah Hu, circled the giant tree like a steel whip, sweeping through everything in their path. Their weapons were chipped and dulled, and every one of them was covered in thick blood.

The four surviving Demon Kings broke out of the encirclement, each greatly exhausted and pale.

Shaya was not dead, but she had lost an arm. Bao Qi was nowhere to be seen. In Yang Kai’s perception, his flame of life had already been extinguished. He had clearly been killed by his former comrades.

Fewer than thirty thousand remained in Shaman Niu’s tribe: one Shaman King, six Demon Kings, and three Great Shaman Masters. This represented virtually all the strength Shaman Niu’s tribe had left, yet they faced hundreds of thousands, even millions, of demons, with as many as twenty or thirty Demon Kings.

This was a war of extremely unequal forces, but also one that could decide the final outcome.

Yang Kai didn’t need to rally them; every Barbarian knew the heavy responsibility on their shoulders. They fought bravely, disregarding life and death, killing every enemy they could.

More than twenty thousand demons had died by Yang Kai’s hand, including five or six Demon Kings. But in such a massive battle, no matter how strong an individual was, their strength was insignificant—unless they possessed the power of a Shaman Saint.

Shaman Niu’s tribe gradually became unable to withstand the demons’ siege, and their defensive circle shrank.

Thirty thousand became twenty thousand, then fifteen thousand.

Ah Hu’s cavalry of several thousand was reduced to fewer than a thousand. Each was drenched in blood, both their own and their enemies’, and their weapons had been replaced several times.

Yang Kai continuously took out Spirit Pills and wondrous medicines, giving them to the shamans of Shaman Niu’s tribe. Even the six Demon Kings received many supplements.

It was thanks to these pills that everyone had managed to hold on until now.

The crown of the Divine Tree had already pierced the clouds, completely out of sight. As long as it was given enough time, it would eventually reach the Boundary Passage.

Time—time was now the most critical factor.

The demons clearly would not give Shaman Niu’s tribe this chance.

The attacks grew fiercer. Though Shaman Niu’s tribe fought desperately, they could not hold out. The overwhelming numerical disadvantage left them with no options.

Suddenly, a commotion erupted. Yang Kai turned to see that the tribesmen of Shaman Niu’s tribe were suffering heavy casualties. The already precarious defensive circle was finally breached, and a flood of demons poured in, slaughtering at will.

Yang Kai darted over, Myriad Sword’s light flashing, single-handedly blocking the breach and giving his tribesmen a chance to reinforce the gap.

But the next moment, another breach opened on the other side.

Yang Kai shouted, and Fei Li immediately rushed over. The pitch-black skeleton burned with emerald flames, as if it would incinerate everything in the world, halting the demons’ advance.

A third breach appeared…

The defensive circle of Shaman Niu’s tribe was like a dilapidated house leaking everywhere in a rainy night. Countless demons broke through, some staying behind to tie down the tribesmen, while others charged toward the Divine Tree.

Some gathered at the roots, hacking desperately with their weapons as if to fell the Divine Tree. Many more demons climbed onto the trunk, wantonly destroying it.

Yang Kai’s eyes were bloodshot, wishing he could split himself into a million bodies.

At this critical moment, chaos suddenly erupted on the outskirts. The ground trembled, and the clash of countless weapons rang out, interspersed with screams.

A battered Barbarian squad suddenly appeared in Yang Kai’s sight, led by a Shaman King. He looked familiar to Yang Kai—he seemed to be a Shaman King from the Frost Snow Tribe.

Over the past two years, Yang Kai had dealt with many Shaman Kings. Even if they weren’t close, they had at least met each other a few times.

He couldn’t be mistaken—this Shaman King was definitely from the Frost Snow Tribe.

The Shaman King wielded an ice dragon, weaving through the battlefield and greatly relieving Shaman Niu’s tribe’s crisis. Then he shouted, “Kill!”

The Frost Snow Tribe’s people responded with a shout. The morale of Shaman Niu’s tribe surged, and with the two forces attacking from within and without, they quickly brought the newcomers into the defensive circle.

There weren’t many from the Frost Snow Tribe—only five thousand—but they had clearly paid a heavy price.

Shaman Niu’s tribe had managed to break through to this point thanks to the strength of ten Demon Kings and Yang Kai, but the Frost Snow Tribe clearly didn’t have so many Shaman Kings to spare. When they set out, they might have numbered one or two hundred thousand, but by the time they arrived, only five thousand remained.

The unimaginable price they paid finally brought them to the Divine Tree.

The Shaman King nodded to Yang Kai in greeting and said, “Now it’s our turn to guard the Divine Tree.”

The Divine Tree had protected the Frost Snow Tribe for tens of thousands of years, shielding them from disasters and allowing them to thrive. Now, after all those years, the Frost Snow Tribe had come to repay that protection, ready to use their flesh and blood to form an iron wall and protect the Divine Tree from harm.

“You arrived just in time!” Yang Kai grinned.

The Shaman King nodded slightly. With a wave of his hand, the five thousand behind him spread out to both sides. The strong warriors joined the defensive ranks, but a thousand remained behind.

Yang Kai’s gaze swept over those thousand, and he was shocked. An expression of disbelief flashed across his face.

He discovered that all one thousand of them were shamans!

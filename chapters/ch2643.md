## Chapter 2643 — Return to Desolate City

The method for arranging a Space Array was something Yang Kai had long since mastered, having been personally taught by Yang Yan. For him, setting up such a formation was already second nature.

If it were any other array master, even someone like Nanmen Dajun, it would probably take three to five months and a lot of trouble to set it up, because it involved the laws and mysteries of space. The person arranging it would have to carefully deduce everything before starting.

But Yang Kai was different. He was deeply versed in the Laws of Space, possessing an innate mastery over spatial power. For him, arranging such a formation was as natural as breathing—he held an unrivaled advantage that others could only envy.

Under Ji Yao’s attentive gaze, it took Yang Kai less than half a day to form the prototype of the array. Piece by piece, he refined and set Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade into the formation, steadily perfecting the framework until it neared completion.

After a day, Yang Kai made the final adjustments and only then let out a gentle breath.

“Is it done?” Ji Yao asked in a calm tone, but her beautiful eyes could not hide her shock.

Even if you’ve never eaten pork, you’ve at least seen a pig run.

The Space Array was one of the most difficult arrays to arrange. It was said that setting one up required a huge amount of manpower, resources, and energy, and nowadays, in the entire Star Realm, only a handful of people could do it.

But for Yang Kai, it only took a single day for a Space Array to stand tall in the forbidden grounds of the Ice Lake, appearing before Ji Yao’s eyes.

“It’s done.” Yang Kai nodded affirmatively, circling the Space Array and examining his handiwork with great satisfaction.

Although it had been many years since he last arranged such a thing, picking it up again now felt effortless and natural. It seemed that once some skills were learned, they were never truly forgotten.

The Space Array before him covered an area of about ten zhang, only a medium-small array, able to transfer at most ten people at a time. The Formation Foundation was deeply buried underground, and the Formation Plate was engraved with countless intricate runes, mysterious and unparalleled. The array gate was as white as jade, as if it led to the deepest void.

On that Formation Foundation, there were more than a hundred grooves, used for embedding Source Crystals. Only by embedding Source Crystals could this Space Array be activated.

“Where does it lead?” Ji Yao asked curiously.

Yang Kai shook his head and smiled, “It doesn’t lead anywhere. Right now, it’s an independent Space Array.”

“Independent…” Ji Yao was stunned. “Then what’s the point?”

Space Arrays have always needed to be connected to each other to function. If martial artists want to travel from one place to another via Space Array, both places must have one, connected by spatial nodes, to achieve teleportation.

An independent Space Array was completely useless.

“It’s useless for now, but give it some time—it’ll come in handy,” Yang Kai replied with a wide grin.

Ji Yao didn’t know what Yang Kai was up to, but seeing his mysterious look, she could only hold back her questions.

“Take me to your Ice Heart Valley’s Space Array and send me to the place closest to the Eastern Territory. I need to make a trip to the Ancient Land.” Yang Kai turned to glance at Ji Yao.

Ji Yao’s beautiful eyes flashed as she nodded lightly, “Follow me.”

Ji Yao naturally knew why Yang Kai was going to the Ancient Land. He was going to seek reinforcements. Now, Wenqing Sect was strong, with thirty or forty Emperor Realm experts, and two at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. With only Ice Heart Valley’s current strength, they couldn’t possibly resist. If a fight broke out, few would survive.

But if Yang Kai could bring reinforcements from the Ancient Land, it would be a different story. Ji Yao had seen the power of those Yao Kings and Saint Envoys with her own eyes—any one of them was no weaker than Feng Xuan.

On the way, Yang Kai spoke, “There’s no need for me to bid farewell to Senior Bing Yun—please let her know on my behalf. Whether it takes a month or two, I will certainly return. Your Ice Heart Valley’s Grand Guardian Array should be able to hold until then.”

“Alright.” Ji Yao nodded gently.

“Also…” Yang Kai said, taking out a jade slip. His Divine Sense surged as he inscribed a message inside, then tossed it to Ji Yao. “Take this and teach it to the disciples of Ice Heart Valley. Have them practice it well during this time.”

Ji Yao took it curiously, glanced at it, and her delicate body trembled. “What kind of array is this?”

The jade slip didn’t contain any powerful secret techniques, but a set of incredibly profound formations—a joint attack formation. Ji Yao herself was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, with broad experience, so she immediately saw the power of this formation.

With this formation, the strength of Ice Heart Valley’s disciples could increase several times over.

“The Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation!” Yang Kai replied. “It was once the core formation of the Black Tortoise Sect in the Eastern Territory.”

This Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation was something Qin Chaoyang of Fenglin City had taught him back then. At that time, Fenglin City was besieged by Demonic Qi, and it was only after much effort that they gathered seven Dao Origin Realm cultivators to form the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation and venture out, but they nearly perished to the last.

That outcome wasn’t because the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation was lacking, but because the participants’ cultivation was too low and their hearts weren’t united.

This Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation was once the core defensive array of the Black Tortoise Sect in the Eastern Territory. When seven cultivators joined forces to form the array, it could be used for both offense and defense, but its true strength lay in its unparalleled defensive power, which would soar to new heights.

Unfortunately, the Black Tortoise Sect somehow offended the Divine Spirit Shi Huo back then, and Shi Huo came to destroy them. Even with the full strength of the sect, they couldn’t resist and were wiped out.

The ancestors of the Qin Family of Fenglin City were experts from the Black Tortoise Sect. After the sect was destroyed, they drifted to the Southern Territory and settled in Fenglin City. Sadly, the Qin Family declined with each generation, and now even a Dao Origin Realm cultivator was rare. The Imperial Treasure Myriad Sword had no one to wield it, which was truly lamentable.

“If it really comes to all-out war with Wenqing Sect, with this great array, the disciples of Ice Heart Valley can at least reduce their casualties.” Yang Kai sighed softly.

Wenqing Sect had a force of a hundred thousand. Though their origins were complex and not all were of one mind, the numbers alone were daunting. With only seven thousand disciples, Ice Heart Valley was like an egg striking a stone.

For the disciples of Ice Heart Valley, this Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation was absolutely a life-saving formation.

“On behalf of all the sisters of Ice Heart Valley, thank you, Senior Brother,” Ji Yao suddenly said solemnly.

Ji Yao had seen all that Yang Kai had done for Ice Heart Valley during this time, and now he was even giving them this unparalleled formation. It was truly a great favor.

There are many in this world who add flowers to a brocade, but how many will send charcoal in the snow?

Yang Kai chuckled, “I have deep ties with Senior Bing Yun. This is nothing. As long as Junior Sister Yao doesn’t tease me anymore, that’s enough.”

Ji Yao’s face flushed slightly. She naturally knew what Yang Kai was referring to.

Thinking back to when she lost her senses, kneeling naked before him and letting him see her entire body, then even massaging his shoulders from behind, Ji Yao felt utterly embarrassed.

She didn’t know how she could have been so muddleheaded at the time. Yang Kai was clearly a man, so how did she mistake him for her master?

This was a stain on her life, one that could never be erased…

The two said nothing more along the way and soon arrived at the Space Array of Ice Heart Valley.

This Space Array was a one-way array—in other words, it could only send people out, not bring them in.

Normally, arrays within sects are one-way, to prevent enemies from teleporting inside. But now that they were at odds with Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley was in danger, Bing Yun had even shut down this one-way array just in case.

With Ji Yao present, opening the array was naturally no problem.

Soon, the Space Array was reopened. After enough Source Crystals were set in place, Yang Kai stepped onto it. Light flashed, and his figure disappeared.

Ji Yao watched silently for a while, then her delicate body flickered as she hurried off to find Bing Yun.

She needed to hand over the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation to Bing Yun, who would then teach it to all the disciples.

In a city near the border of the Northern Territory, Yang Kai appeared leisurely. Without stopping, he left the main hall, asked someone for directions, and immediately soared into the sky, heading toward the Eastern Territory.

Although this was the border of the Northern Territory, it still took Yang Kai five full days to completely leave it behind.

Traveling without rest, twenty days later, Yang Kai finally arrived at Desolate City beside the Wild Ancient Land.

At this moment, he couldn’t help but feel a bit annoyed. If only he’d exchanged message beads or a Transmission Compass with Luan Feng and the others last time, he could have had them come out to meet him. Now, to enter the Ancient Land again, he’d have to go through a lot of trouble.

Familiar with the way, he pushed open the door to a small courtyard. Sweeping it with his Divine Sense, he sensed a familiar aura inside and relaxed. He had been worried the owner wouldn’t be home, which would have complicated things.

“Old Ban!” Yang Kai called into the house.

Soon, Old Ban appeared before Yang Kai. Seeing it was him, Old Ban smiled, “So it’s Young Master Yang. Didn’t you leave Desolate City? Why have you come back?”

Yang Kai replied solemnly, “I need to enter the Ancient Land again. I hope Old Ban can help me. Here’s your payment!”

As he spoke, he flicked out a Spatial Ring.

Old Ban waved it away with a smile, “If Young Master Yang wants to enter the Ancient Land, this old man will do his utmost. There’s no need for payment.”

Last time, he’d been captured by people from the Netherworld Sect and was only saved thanks to Yang Kai. Naturally, he couldn’t accept any payment now.

Yang Kai didn’t insist, only saying, “Then I’ll thank you, Old Ban.”

Old Ban said, “Young Master Yang, please wait a moment while I settle Ling’er.”

As he spoke, a little girl of seven or eight ran out of the house. This was Old Ban’s granddaughter, Ling’er. Last time Yang Kai saw her, she was still sick, but now she seemed to have recovered, though she was still thin and pale, clearly having lost some vitality.

“Grandpa is leaving again?” Although Ling’er was young, she was very sensible. She glanced at Yang Kai, then turned to Old Ban and asked.

Old Ban gently stroked Ling’er’s head, “Grandpa is going out for a bit and will be back soon. Ling’er, be good at home and don’t run around.”

“Ling’er knows. Grandpa, be careful on your journey.” The little girl earnestly reminded him.

Old Ban looked gratified and called out, “Young Master Yang, let’s go.”

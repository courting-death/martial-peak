## Chapter 2928 — Different Paths

Spirit Sword Peak was situated in a remote corner of the Qingyang Shrine, a location Yang Kai had deliberately chosen, but he never expected it would become a loophole for Gong Yue to exploit.

Midway there, streaks of light flew in from all directions of Qingyang Shrine, merging into the group.

Except for those who were out or in seclusion, all the other Emperor Realm Elders had arrived. The Deputy Hall Master, Qiu Ran, also came. After greeting Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming, he looked at Gao Xueting and asked, "What happened?"

Observing their expressions, he vaguely sensed that something major had occurred. Otherwise, Gao Xueting would never have barged into the Shrine with two Elders from other sects. Moreover, as far as he knew, these people should have been in the Southern Marsh.

Last time, Yang Kai had sent a message back, roughly explaining what had happened in the Southern Marsh, so Qiu Ran knew a bit about the situation.

"Gong Yue is on Spirit Sword Peak!" Gao Xueting replied.

"What?" Qiu Ran was shocked. "Gong Yue? Which Gong Yue?" He could hardly believe it, suspecting Gao Xueting had made a mistake.

"The same Gong Yue Junior Brother Yang mentioned before, the old Family Head of the Gong family from Tianhe Valley!"

"How could Gong Yue be on Spirit Sword Peak?" Qiu Ran turned to look at You Kun, who was responsible for the sect's defenses and array maintenance. If Gong Yue had entered the Shrine and made it to Spirit Sword Peak, it would have been his negligence.

You Kun broke out in a cold sweat. "I have no idea. I never received any suspicious reports."

Yang Kai said, "Gong Yue is well-versed in the Dao of Arrays. If he truly wanted to sneak into a sect, he would always find a way. The Shrine has always been peaceful, and the Grand Guardian Array hasn't been fully activated. There were vulnerabilities in the defenses, so it's normal he managed to slip in. What we need to figure out now is why he came to the Shrine, and why he's on my Spirit Sword Peak!"

"That's right. Gong Yue's actions are extremely suspicious, and we don't know his current state. So everyone must be extra careful," Chen Wenhao nodded in agreement.

Qiu Ran also nodded slightly, stroking his long beard as he pondered.

You Kun glanced at Yang Kai with gratitude, hatred for Gong Yue surging in his heart.

"There's one thing you all must know: Demonic Intent is insidious and hard to guard against, so you must protect your minds and never give it an opening," Gao Xueting warned sternly. The Elders of Qingyang Shrine all nodded, secretly activating Divine Soul arts to reinforce the defenses of their Sea of Consciousness.

After the time it takes to drink half a cup of tea, the group finally arrived at Spirit Sword Peak.

The peak looked no different from when Yang Kai had left, except the grand array was fully open. There was only one anomaly—a figure sat cross-legged on the mountaintop clearing, motionless, as if dead.

Beside him, a pitch-black little beast lay sprawled out, content and at ease. Its short tail flicked occasionally. When the group approached, it merely glanced at them and did nothing more.

Swish, swish, swish…

Nearly ten Emperor Realm cultivators spread out, sealing off Spirit Sword Peak so tightly that not even a breeze could slip through.

Gong Yue slowly opened his eyes. His gaze shifted and locked onto Yang Kai. Those pitch-black pupils made the hearts of those seeing them for the first time tremble, as if they were bottomless pits capable of devouring everything. They hurriedly circulated their power to resist.

"You've come!" Gong Yue spoke softly, as if he had little strength, his voice barely above a whisper.

"Should this old man call you Gong Yue, or by some other name?" Feng Ming sneered. Judging by Gong Yue's current state, he couldn't tell if the man had lost himself or been possessed by Demonic Intent.

He didn't even know if Demonic Intent could possess others.

Gong Yue was certainly excellent research material, but there was no way to study him now.

"This old man is Gong Yue, Gong Yue of the Gong family," Gong Yue replied.

Feng Ming laughed loudly. "You still have the nerve to mention the Gong family? After this, the Gong family will be utterly disgraced. You'll be their eternal sinner."

Gong Yue said, "Is the pursuit of power a crime? If so, then everyone here is a criminal."

Feng Ming snorted coldly, “There are three thousand Great Daos, each with its own path, yet you’ve strayed into the wilds. What kind of power are you even pursuing?”

Gong Yue said indifferently, “If one path is mastered, all paths can be understood. The Great Dao is a road—so why can’t the wilderness be one as well? In the end, all roads lead to the same destination.”

"Nonsense!" Feng Ming's beard bristled with anger.

Gong Yue declared proudly, "If there is no road in the wild, then this old man will carve one out!"

"I doubt you have the ability."

Gong Yue chuckled, "I alone cannot, but more will follow me."

Chen Wenhao sighed, "Brother Gong, you've truly entered the Demonic State!"

He and Gong Yue had some friendship. Before coming, he had hoped to help Gong Yue if possible, but seeing him now, he knew it was impossible. Gong Yue hadn't been possessed, or perhaps Demonic Intent couldn't possess others.

But Gong Yue had entered the Demonic State. His thoughts were paranoid and wild, influenced by the Demonic Intent invading his body, making him a completely different person. Such a man was dangerous—no one knew what he might do.

Chen Wenhao silently gave up on helping him.

"Entered the Demonic State?" Gong Yue smiled. "I've merely found a suitable path."

"That isn't your own path!" Yang Kai said solemnly.

Gong Yue looked at him. "Are you all walking your own paths? The roads you walk were trampled out by those before you. Who can prove that path is correct? In my eyes, you're just parroting others."

Yang Kai replied seriously, "Perhaps we are walking the paths of our predecessors, but we strive to reach new heights. Old Family Head Gong, you are different. Your path has deviated from the Dao. You'll never reach the end. You have submitted to Demonic Intent and will become its slave."

Who knew which words provoked Gong Yue, but his expression suddenly turned sinister, his eyes flashing coldly. He said in a chilling voice, "Absurd! This old man feels just fine. Who could possibly enslave me?"

Yang Kai pondered for a moment, deciding not to argue further. He asked, "May I ask, Old Family Head Gong, why did you risk so much to come to Qingyang Shrine? And why Spirit Sword Peak? Is there something here that attracts you?"

Gong Yue smiled mysteriously, saying nothing.

Yang Kai said, "It seems there really is something here that attracts you. Is there something related to the Demon Race? Could there be some ancient demon relic?"

"Oh?" Gong Yue looked at Yang Kai in surprise. "You actually know about the ancient demons."

Yang Kai grinned, "I might know more than you."

"No wonder!" Gong Yue sized up Yang Kai with interest, as if discovering something. "No wonder you seem different. So you are the same as this old man."

Yang Kai shook his head and said, "You're mistaken. We are not of the same kind."

"Words can deceive me, but your body cannot. I can feel it—it's inside you," Gong Yue's expression suddenly became excited, and he couldn't help but laugh. "How interesting! But why are you still so stubborn?"

Yang Kai shook his head, refusing to argue further. Gong Yue had been corrupted by Demonic Intent; his thoughts and ideas were no longer normal. There was no point in debating with him.

Chen Wenhao and the others looked at Yang Kai suspiciously, unsure what Gong Yue meant, especially that line about something inside Yang Kai, which made them very uneasy.

Gong Yue laughed, "One day, you'll see that the path this old man chose is the right one. I hope that day comes soon."

"Whether that day comes or not, you won't live to see it," Feng Ming sneered. He reached out and grasped the air, and the Yanlei Spear gleamed coldly. With a flick, he thrust it at Gong Yue, shouting, "Let this old man show you what the right path is!"

The spear was like a Jiao, its momentum overwhelming and unstoppable.

This strike unleashed all of Feng Ming's power, dazzling the First Stage Emperor Realm Elders of Qingyang Shrine. Under this spear, it seemed as if all things in heaven and earth would be reduced to dust.

Facing Feng Ming's earth-shattering blow, Gong Yue neither dodged nor flinched. He merely smiled and said, "Though this old man knows his days are numbered, he won't surrender so easily. If you want my life, you'll have to show some real skill."

As he spoke, Gong Yue grabbed the little black dog beside him and hurled it at Feng Ming.

The little black dog was clearly stunned by the sudden turn of events. It barked a few times in fright, its round eyes full of terror, limbs flailing helplessly in midair, utterly terrified.

Feng Ming frowned, not understanding Gong Yue's intentions. He sensed no threat from the little black dog—it was just an ordinary yao beast, weak and unremarkable. Someone like Feng Ming could kill it with a breath.

So Feng Ming didn't dodge. His only target was Gong Yue. As for the little black dog, it would probably be blown to pieces by the spear's force.

Gong Yue formed a series of complex hand seals and pointed in the direction of the little black dog.

Suddenly, a change occurred. It was as if the little black dog's hidden ferocity had been awakened. The panic in its eyes vanished, replaced by an indescribable savagery. Its gaze was now filled with a destructive aura, as if it wanted to annihilate the entire world.

It no longer barked meaninglessly, but opened its mouth wide at Feng Ming.

It wasn't large—at most a foot long—and its mouth could barely fit a fist.

But that mouth suddenly split open, growing larger than a house, completely engulfing the little black dog's tiny body, as if only a giant mouth remained.

It was an extremely bizarre sight.

Everyone had focused their vigilance on Gong Yue. Although they had seen the little black dog when they arrived, no one had paid it much attention.

Only now did they realize the little black dog was not as harmless as it appeared.

Everyone was startled.

All those on guard simultaneously unleashed their divine abilities, attacking the giant mouth together.

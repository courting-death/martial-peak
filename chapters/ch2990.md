## Chapter 2990 — Did I See Wrong?

As soon as this man finished speaking, another said, “Who told that little bitch to kill Wind and Cloud Pavilion’s Young Pavilion Master? After provoking Wind and Cloud Pavilion in this territory, how could she possibly survive? Sooner or later, she’ll die.”

The first man said, “That woman is really something. Of all the people she could have killed, she just had to kill their Young Pavilion Master. Although Wind and Cloud Pavilion’s old Pavilion Master has countless wives and concubines, he never had an heir. It was only in his old age that he finally had a son, whom he doted on beyond measure. Now that the boy has been killed, how could he possibly let the matter rest? If not for that, the reward could never have been so generous.”

“Speaking of which, that Young Pavilion Master was no good either. Relying on his status and the old Pavilion Master’s indulgence, he bullied men and violated women, committing every kind of outrage. Although his cultivation wasn’t high, his background was formidable, so there was nothing anyone could do about him. Who knows how many women he ruined? I heard he was killed this time because he tried to molest that little bitch.”

Hearing this, the burly man surnamed Xiong’s eyes lit up. “In that case, the woman must be quite a beauty?”

The first man laughed loudly. “Naturally. Think about it: that Young Pavilion Master toyed with countless women. What kind of woman hadn’t he seen? How could an ordinary beauty catch his eye? To tell you both the truth, I once saw that woman from a distance. She was truly a peerless beauty, like a goddess descended to the mortal world. If a man could marry a woman like that and bring her home, he probably wouldn’t have much interest in cultivating afterward. How could a profligate Young Master like the Young Pavilion Master possibly restrain himself after seeing her? Unfortunately...”

“Unfortunately what?”

“Unfortunately, that woman’s cultivation is also extraordinary. I wasn’t certain I could defeat her, so I could only watch helplessly as the opportunity slipped through my fingers.”

“This Lord also likes beautiful women.” The man surnamed Xiong picked up the wine jar on the table and took a drink. Wine trickled from the corner of his mouth, lending him an exceedingly bold and unrestrained air. An obscene light gleamed in his eyes as he said, “This Lord would like to see just how stunning that woman is. She’s merely a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivator. If this Lord finds her, she won’t escape even if she grows wings.”

“With Lord Xiong taking action, that woman certainly won’t escape,” one man flattered him.

The burly man surnamed Xiong laughed loudly. “Once this Lord captures her, I’ll have my fill of her before handing her over to Wind and Cloud Pavilion for the reward.”

“Excellent. If Lord Xiong truly captures her, please allow this Junior Brother to share in the pleasure.”

The burly man surnamed Xiong chuckled. “Brother, your tastes are rather heavy. You even want what this Lord has finished with?”

“It’s only for a little fun. I’m not taking her home as my wife, so what does it matter?”

“Fine, then it’s settled. I just wonder whether the information is accurate. Will that little bitch really pass through here?”

“The information is unquestionably accurate. That woman is remarkably bold. After provoking Wind and Cloud Pavilion, not only did she make no effort to conceal her whereabouts, but she actually fought her way toward Wind and Cloud Pavilion. Quite a few people seem to have died at her hands. Point Star City lies on the only route to Wind and Cloud Pavilion, so if she passes through, she will definitely reveal herself. We only need to wait here. Haven’t you two noticed that there are many more Emperor Realm Masters in Point Star City? They’ve all come for that woman.”

“Hmph! They want to compete with this Lord? They truly overestimate themselves. If they stay out of this Lord’s way when the time comes, all will be well, but if they interfere, don’t blame this Lord for showing no mercy.”

“Lord Xiong is right. How could that trash compare with you?”

As they spoke, the cultivator surnamed Xiong suddenly raised his head and glanced toward Yang Kai.

At that moment, Yang Kai was sampling the middle-grade Sword Wine the waiter had brought him. After taking just one sip, he frowned, because he discovered that this Sword Wine was somewhat different from what he had drunk before.

They were not even remotely on the same level!

Although the wine contained Sword Intent, it was extremely shallow. When it rushed into his meridians and flesh, he easily dissolved it, and it had no effect on him whatsoever.

The Sword Wine Zhu Qing had given him before seemed to have been exceptionally high-grade.

Holding his wine cup, he frowned and immediately lost all interest.

Just then, the opened wine jar on his table was suddenly pulled aside by an attractive force. The burly man surnamed Xiong laughed loudly and called out to Yang Kai, “Brat, Sword Wine isn’t something you can drink. This Lord will reluctantly dispose of it for you. There’s no need to thank me.”

The Sword Wine at his table had already been finished, so when he saw that Yang Kai had ordered a jar, he seized it without so much as a word.

As he spoke, he lifted the wine jar and gulped it down. His Emperor Yuan surged, seemingly stimulated by the Sword Intent in the wine.

Yang Kai’s expression immediately sank.

This burly man surnamed Xiong had humiliated him time and again, but Yang Kai had been too lazy to argue with him. He had not expected the man to go so far as to snatch his belongings. Yang Kai might have looked down on this jar of middle-grade Sword Wine and had no intention of drinking any more, but having it stolen from him was an entirely different matter.

“Good wine!” After finishing the Sword Wine, the burly man surnamed Xiong’s blood and vitality churned under the Sword Intent’s stimulation. His face flushed and his neck swelled as he wiped his mouth and shouted in delight. The other two men at his table looked at Yang Kai with mocking expressions, their eyes filled with ridicule.

Yang Kai’s face turned icy as he said in a chilling voice, “The kind are bullied, and the gentle are ridden. I had no intention of stooping to your level, but trash like you just had to provoke this Young Master. It seems you’re courting death.”

Hearing this, the burly man surnamed Xiong froze for a moment before laughing. “Brat, what did you say? This Lord didn’t hear you clearly. Say it again.”

As he spoke, he deliberately leaned over and turned his ear toward Yang Kai, as though he truly intended to hear every word clearly.

Yang Kai sighed leisurely. “Some people sit at the bottom of a well and think the sky is only as large as its mouth, yet still believe themselves to be remarkable.”

The burly man surnamed Xiong’s expression turned cold, and he abruptly stood up. “Brat, who are you calling a frog at the bottom of a well?”

The other two men at his table also shot to their feet, their Imperial Might surging. One of them snorted. “Brat, don’t spout nonsense here. This isn’t your impoverished backwater. It doesn’t matter if saying the wrong thing merely embarrasses you, but losing your life over it would be unfortunate.”

“The ones who will lose their lives are you!” Yang Kai raised his hand slightly, preparing to teach these three a profound and unforgettable lesson.

Just then, an unexpected change occurred.

A tremendous crash suddenly came from the distance, followed by several screams that pierced the sky. It sounded as though people had exchanged blows. Emperor Yuan surged, the World Energy trembled, and Laws blossomed.

In the next instant, a pink figure suddenly sped past nearby.

Yang Kai was seated beside the window, and as the figure swept past, a faint, delicate fragrance drifted over. From the corner of his eye, he caught sight of a graceful figure with long hair streaming behind her and pink robes fluttering as she shot far into the distance in the blink of an eye.

Streaks of light sped past in close pursuit of the woman.

“Wretched slut, don’t you dare run!”

“Since you dared show yourself in Point Star City, you won’t escape this time even if you grow wings!”

“Hurry and chase her! Don’t let her get away! The reward is twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals!”

With a great commotion, more than a hundred people rushed out in pursuit. There were Emperor Realm Masters among them, but there were also quite a few Dao Origin Realm cultivators, who had clearly gone merely to join the excitement and try their luck.

Yang Kai froze in place, his mind going blank. He stared foolishly at the figure already disappearing into the distance and rubbed his eyes hard.

“It’s that little bitch!” an exclamation came from nearby.

“Let’s go!” another man shouted before hurriedly leaping outside.

When the burly man surnamed Xiong heard this, he lost all interest in entangling with Yang Kai. After glaring viciously at him, he stomped heavily on the floor, smashed straight through the roof, and flew off in pursuit.

The tavern was instantly left in complete disarray. When the waiter heard the commotion and hurried over, he was nearly reduced to tears.

Yang Kai also came back to his senses at this moment. He hurriedly tossed down some Source Crystals, then vanished from the spot with a flicker.

When he reappeared, he was already outside the city.

A crowd surged out of the city and paid little attention to his sudden appearance. Everyone’s gaze was fixed on the beautiful figure fleeing ahead, and they chased after her with feverish excitement.

To these people, that woman was equivalent to ten or twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals.

Without a word, Yang Kai joined the pursuing crowd and did not attract much attention. There was only one question deep in his heart: Had he seen wrong?

When the woman flew past him just now, she had been moving too quickly, and his attention had still been focused on the men at the neighbouring table, so he had not seen her appearance clearly. He had only caught a glimpse from the corner of his eye.

Yet that fleeting glimpse had shaken him deeply.

He had actually found the woman somewhat familiar, and she resembled someone he had not seen in many years. If he had not mistaken her, it would be simply inconceivable.

He could not be certain, so he planned to blend into the crowd and observe her carefully before deciding what to do. Otherwise, it would be embarrassing if he rushed forward only to discover he had mistaken her for someone else. Moreover, the woman was currently in a precarious situation, and she might misunderstand his intentions.

Outside Point Star City, the woman moved with astonishing speed, transforming into a streak of pink light that raced through the sky.

A group of cultivators pursued her relentlessly, each regarding the woman as a priceless prize.

Before long, the differences in cultivation became apparent. The Dao Origin Realm cultivators who had joined the pursuit gradually fell behind, and only the Emperor Realm Masters could barely keep pace with the woman.

There were not many of them, only about ten or so, and most were First-Stage Emperor Realm Masters. Even so, this was already a force that could not be underestimated, especially by the woman.

Although he was not particularly close, Yang Kai could still sense that the woman was only a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivator.

It was precisely because her cultivation was at this level that Yang Kai could not be sure whether he had mistaken her for someone else. After all, even he had only just reached the First-Stage Emperor Realm. If this woman truly was the person he thought she was, how could she possibly possess such a cultivation?

A First-Stage Emperor Realm Master facing so many pursuers clearly had little chance of victory.

However, Yang Kai soon noticed that something was amiss.

The fleeing woman ahead did not seem particularly flustered. Her aura was deep and profound, and she was clearly not using her full strength. Instead, it seemed as though she were using herself as bait. When this thought surfaced, Yang Kai could not help laughing, wondering whether he was overthinking things.

During the pursuit, the group quickly travelled far from Point Star City.

In a wilderness five thousand li from Point Star City, the woman who had been fleeing suddenly stopped. She stood quietly in the void, leaving the crowd with a lovely view of her back. A breeze swept past, sending her long hair dancing and leaving all who saw her dazzled and entranced.

The dozen or so Emperor Realm Masters pursuing her also stopped one after another. By this point, everyone had finally noticed that something was wrong. The woman did not appear to be fleeing at all; rather, it seemed as though she had lured them into some kind of trap.

Realizing this, everyone hurriedly investigated their surroundings, afraid that some kind of barrier or Spirit Array might suddenly appear, or that the woman might have a powerful helper lying in ambush.

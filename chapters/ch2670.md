## Chapter 2670 — Collecting Some Interest

Although the presence of Yang Kai and Yingfei aroused this man's suspicion, he didn't pay it much mind. The Thousand Leaves Sect in the Southern Territory was nothing more than a minor, unranked sect, far inferior to their Flowing Shadow Sword Sect. So what if they had some connections? This was Flowing Shadow City, the foundation of the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect—who could possibly act with impunity here?

His gaze lingered on Yang Kai for a moment before he snorted coldly, "Who are you? If you know what's good for you, leave at once. Don't bring trouble upon yourself!"

Yang Kai ignored him completely, simply looking at Du Xian and Ye Qinghan. "Shall we collect a little interest first?"

Yang Kai had no interest in dealing with this group of small fry, but for Ye Qinghan and Du Xian, who both harbored deep hatred, they were the perfect targets to vent their anger.

Upon hearing these words, Du Xian's expression trembled slightly as he cried out loudly, "Very well! Then I must trouble Brother Yang to keep watch for us!"

Yang Kai grunted in response, standing quietly in place.

"Insolence!" The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple was furious to see Du Xian so arrogant. With a wave of his hand, he shouted, "Seize them!"

Clang, clang...

Swords rang out, sword light surged, and more than a dozen Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples moved as one, covering each other as they charged forward. Sword Qi was chilling, and sword intent soared to the heavens.

"Senior Brother!" Ye Qinghan shouted, her voice crisp. Du Xian understood instantly, turning to stand back-to-back with Ye Qinghan. Their hands danced in unison, and one after another, round objects shot out.

Crack, crack, crack...

After a series of crisp sounds, those round objects suddenly unfolded, transforming before everyone's eyes. In the blink of an eye, they became lifelike demonic beasts.

Spiritual Qi surged as four puppet beasts abruptly appeared in everyone's sight.

A python. A lizard. A wolf. A bird.

Each one looked just like a living creature. If not for the complete lack of vitality, anyone would have mistaken them for real demonic beasts.

Yang Kai's eyes lit up at the sight.

Although he had visited the Thousand Leaves Sect before and interacted with them, at that time, many of their secret techniques had been lost. It was only thanks to him opening Emperor Heaven Valley that they were able to recover their long-lost manuals and secret arts.

Back then, he had never seen the true methods of the Thousand Leaves Sect.

Now, with these four puppet beasts revealed, Yang Kai could tell that the Thousand Leaves Sect had made great progress in recent years. Each of these puppets possessed strength comparable to the peak of the Dao Origin Realm—they must be Earth-Grade puppets of the Thousand Leaves Sect.

With the manuals and secret arts Yang Kai had retrieved from Emperor Heaven Valley, the disciples of the Thousand Leaves Sect had become far superior in both crafting and controlling puppets compared to before.

He had thought that Ye Qinghan and Du Xian would be no match for so many opponents, but now it seemed he had underestimated them.

Sss, sss, sss, sss...

Sword light flashed brilliantly as blades descended upon Ye Qinghan and Du Xian from all directions. Each sword beam seemed solid—had they been struck, the two would have been in dire straits. Yet remarkably, neither showed the slightest hint of activating their protective Origin Qi, their full attention devoted entirely to controlling the four puppet beasts.

The giant python puppet's scales gleamed as its body suddenly coiled, wrapping Ye Qinghan and Du Xian inside. All the incoming sword Qi was blocked by the python's body, sending up dazzling sparks.

Boom, boom, boom...

At the same time, the lizard puppet's body flashed with light. The ground instantly softened, and it leapt into the air before burrowing into the earth, vanishing from sight.

A wolf's howl rang out as the puppet wolf, its body glowing with green light, lunged at the two nearest Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples, baring its fangs. The disciples, terrified, hurriedly raised their swords to block.

Just then, the nimblest puppet—the bird—swept in a graceful arc, like a bolt of lightning, piercing straight through a Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple's chest.

The protective Origin Qi was as fragile as paper, utterly useless.

"Pfft..." The disciple spat out a mouthful of blood, feeling emptiness in his chest. Looking down, he saw a bloody hole in his chest, with writhing organs faintly visible.

Despite outnumbering their opponents, the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect lost a man in the very first exchange.

The distant onlookers were all shocked, deeply impressed by the strength of the Thousand Leaves Sect's puppet beasts.

But anyone with sharp eyes could see this was not a mere accident, but a display of overwhelming strength. Though the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had the advantage in numbers, each of the four puppet beasts was as strong as the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, while the disciples' strengths varied greatly—they simply couldn't withstand such power.

"Retreat!" The leading disciple was also shocked, his face full of disbelief. The Thousand Leaves Sect had been destroyed, and the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had seized many spoils, with puppets being the most numerous.

Now, many disciples of the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect possessed puppets from the Thousand Leaves Sect, but none could compare to these four.

The art of puppetry required not only powerful puppets, but also the right techniques and secret arts to control them. In other words, the same puppet in the hands of others and in the hands of a Thousand Leaves Sect disciple would display vastly different power.

Even though the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had obtained many puppets as spoils and studied them for some time, they could never unleash their full potential, leading them to believe these puppets were nothing special.

Only now did they realize how gravely mistaken they had been.

The disciples were already terrified. Now, hearing the order, they quickly used their sword techniques to guard themselves and retreated at full speed.

But at that moment, the puppet python protecting Ye Qinghan and Du Xian suddenly lashed its tail like a steel whip, stirring up a murderous wind.

The tail was as thick as a bucket—no one knew what it was made of. With the force of a collapsing mountain, it struck several Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples who failed to dodge in time. Their abdomens caved in instantly, blood and entrails spraying from their mouths as their lives were snuffed out on the spot.

Those lucky enough to escape had no time to catch their breath before a huge hole suddenly opened beneath their feet. The previously vanished puppet lizard burst from the ground, jaws wide, and bit down on a disciple, dragging him into the earth.

A blood-curdling scream echoed from underground, chilling everyone to the bone.

With this as a warning, the remaining disciples all leapt into the air, no longer daring to stand on the ground.

But the sky was no safer. The puppet wolf kept attacking, and the puppet bird was as swift as lightning. Each strike brought a spray of blood—someone died every time.

"Sword formation!" the leading Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple shouted, his face pale.

He had thought that dealing with two Thousand Leaves Sect disciples would be easy, but now he realized who was the wolf and who was the sheep. In just a short time, six or seven of his own had died—if this continued, wouldn't they all be wiped out?

At his command, the remaining disciples immediately split into groups, forming sword formations to resist the terrifying puppet beasts together.

Protected by the puppet python, Du Xian and Ye Qinghan killed six or seven people without a scratch. Their hearts surged with joy, a year of pent-up frustration and anger finally released, and their control of the puppets became even more skillful.

Du Xian laughed loudly, "Flowing Shadow Sword Sect, is this all you've got?"

Though his tone was arrogant, he was fully focused, controlling the puppets in a fierce battle with the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples.

“Your sect has already been wiped out, and you still dare act so brazenly!” a disciple of Flowing Shadow Sword Sect roared. Six or seven of their own had been slain right before their very city gates, and they were being openly provoked. If they failed to recover face here, Flowing Shadow Sword Sect would have no standing left in the Northern Territory.

After the initial panic, the disciples gradually steadied themselves, hearts full of hatred, thinking only of tearing Du Xian and Ye Qinghan to pieces to avenge their shame.

"Wiped out?" Du Xian's expression turned fierce as he gritted his teeth. "If not for outside help, what ability did your Flowing Shadow Sword Sect have to destroy my Thousand Leaves Sect? Today, I, Du Xian, have come to seek justice for all the disciples of my Thousand Leaves Sect who died!"

"That depends on whether you have the ability. Kill!"

"Kill!" The remaining Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples shouted in unison. Sword intent pierced the clouds, and under the sword formation, sword light slashed down like a white rainbow, as if to shatter the void itself.

"Senior Brother!" Ye Qinghan's face was grave as she formed a hand seal. The puppet python suddenly contracted, wrapping her and Du Xian tightly inside.

Sword light rained down, sending sparks flying from the puppet python. Its body trembled, but Ye Qinghan and Du Xian remained completely unharmed.

"If you're going to be a turtle hiding in its shell, why come to Flowing Shadow City and make a fool of yourself?" a Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple sneered, waving his hand. "Attack again!"

Sss, sss, sss, sss...

One after another, terrifying sword lights slashed down, pinning the puppet python in place. No matter how sturdy it was, it couldn't withstand such attacks for long, and soon countless cracks appeared.

The onlookers discussed in hushed tones, thinking the two from the Thousand Leaves Sect had overestimated themselves. Having escaped once, why come back to throw their lives away? Their prospects now looked grim.

If they were truly wise, they would have found a place to hide and cultivate, waiting for the day to rise again and avenge their sect.

"Hahahaha!" The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple laughed as Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were beaten into helplessness. "Let's see how long you can last!"

Boom...

Suddenly, the ground split open. The puppet lizard, which had vanished, reappeared, shaking its head and tail as it charged the sword formation. Atop the puppet lizard's head stood a man, head held high, riding the wind—who else could it be but Du Xian?

"When did he—?" The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples were shocked. Though they didn't know what Du Xian was planning, it was clearly nothing good, so they immediately unleashed their power at him.

Du Xian didn't dodge at all, only sneering coldly.

"The Thousand Leaves Sect's puppet arts... are truly exquisite!" Suddenly, a voice rang out from the void, full of admiration, as if someone had seen through the mystery.

He was not the only one. At this moment, many hidden eyes were fixed on Du Xian, standing atop the puppet lizard, all with looks of disbelief and shock in their hearts.

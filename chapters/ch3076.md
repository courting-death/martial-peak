## Chapter 3076 — At the Slightest Disagreement

There was not merely one Lower Plane Star Domain, but countless such places. Some were completely sealed off, existing alone and isolated from the world, while others were connected to the Star Realm. The cultivators in these Star Domains were undoubtedly fortunate, for once their cultivation reached a certain realm, they could travel to the Star Realm, climb to greater heights, and experience more of the world's wonders.

Many years ago, the Star Realm was connected to numerous Lower Plane Star Domains. Not only could those below ascend once their cultivation was high enough, but experts from the Star Realm could also descend through certain means.

After the time of the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor, however, the entrances from the Star Realm to the Lower Plane Star Domains were sealed, because experts from the Star Realm were simply unstoppable to the cultivators in those Star Domains. The Star Domains destroyed by the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor alone were too numerous to count, and no one could guarantee that another Heaven Devourer Great Emperor would not appear in the future.

The existence of the many Star Domains was extremely important to the Star Realm, so these seals were also a form of protection.

Thus, the Star Court came into being! This force was exceedingly mysterious and unknown to ordinary people. It had been founded by the Great Emperors of that era and was responsible for guarding the entrances to the Lower Plane Star Domains, preventing people from the Star Realm from descending at will.

If Yang Kai remembered correctly, the affairs of the current Star Court were overseen by the Iron Blood Great Emperor.

He had always believed that anyone wishing to travel to a Lower Plane Star Domain had to pass through the Star Court. He had also made plans to find a way back to Hengluo Star Domain once he had some free time and bring over his friends and family.

He had even speculated that Yang Yan, from whom he had heard no news all this time, might be in the Star Court.

Never had he imagined that before he could put any of those plans into action, he would discover a Void Passage leading to a Lower Plane Star Domain inside Dragon Isle's Dragon Temple, much less one opened by the infamous Heaven Devourer Great Emperor.

Although the chance that Wu Kuang had gone to Hengluo Star Domain was less than one in a hundred, how could Yang Kai rest easy? In some matters, one did not fear ten thousand possibilities, only the one chance among them.

Thus, the moment he heard Mo Huang say that Wu Kuang had gone to a Lower Plane Star Domain, he immediately grew anxious.

After pondering briefly, he called out, "Sirs, Wu Kuang has yet to regain his full strength. If we do not seize this opportunity to pursue and kill him, he will surely become a great scourge in the future. What are you still hesitating for?"

Zhu Yan raised his head and glanced at him, his already displeased expression darkening slightly. "If we could pinpoint Wu Kuang's location, this old man would pursue him without needing you to say so, but who knows which Star Domain he has fled into?"

"And because of that, you're simply going to let the matter go unchecked?" Yang Kai stared back at him without the slightest weakness.

Mo Huang frowned. "Things aren't as simple as you think."

Yang Kai stubbornly craned his neck. "Even if we don't know exactly which Star Domain Wu Kuang fled into, with so many people here, if everyone goes through after him, there will at least be a glimmer of hope of finding him. But if we don't even try, we'll only let him roam free. When Wu Kuang returns at his peak one day, who in the Star Realm will be able to stop him? Surely none of you wants another War of the Great Emperors."

"You've got a lot of nerve and a temper to match." Mo Huang sneered.

Yang Kai said, "I'm merely discussing the matter on its merits."

Fu Xuan said, "This matter has far-reaching implications, so calm yourself for now." Compared to the others, Fu Xuan still treated him fairly well. She could not understand why Yang Kai had suddenly become so agitated, as though he desperately wanted to pursue Wu Kuang.

Yang Kai shook his head. "I simply don't understand."

"Shut your mouth! How do you still have the face to speak?" Fu Zhun suddenly raised her head and cast Yang Kai an icy glance.

Yang Kai met her gaze, his temper flaring as well, and replied evenly, "What instruction does Second Elder have for me?"

Fu Zhun gritted her teeth. "If not for you, how could my Dragon Clan have suffered so many upheavals today? If you hadn't wounded Fu Chi, how could he have fallen so easily? Without Fu Chi's Dragon Clan Origin, how could Wu Kuang have penetrated deep into the Dragon Temple?"

The force of her accusations was oppressive.

Yang Kai sneered repeatedly. "Why does Second Elder make it sound as though I'm the chief culprit? This boy's cultivation is still shallow, so I cannot shoulder such a responsibility. Second Elder truly thinks too highly of me."

He had no favorable impression of Fu Zhun whatsoever. She wanted to ruin the relationship between Mo Huang and Fu Xuan, and she also insisted on interfering in the matter between him and Zhu Qing. It was as though she had been born with a love for breaking up devoted couples and could not bear to see anyone enjoy the slightest happiness. Even if she was a peerless beauty, aloof and cold as frost, she had dared to betroth Zhu Qing to someone else, so in his eyes, her face was hateful. He could not defeat her now, so he would endure her and yield to her for the time being, but when the day came that he could deal with her, he would make certain she learned what it felt like to be forced by someone else.

After speaking, Yang Kai gave Fu Zhun no chance to respond. He cupped his fists toward Zhu Yan and said, "Great Elder, this incident occurred on Dragon Isle, and Wu Kuang also escaped from Dragon Isle. No matter what, the Dragon Clan bears an inescapable responsibility. The most urgent task now is to pursue Wu Kuang and nip this danger in the bud; otherwise, he will surely become a great calamity in the future."

"Boy, do not push your luck!" Fu Zhun shouted in a low voice.

"Great Elder!" Yang Kai continued to ignore her and merely stared stubbornly at Zhu Yan.

Zhu Yan frowned. "This matter has nothing to do with you. Stand aside."

He was also growing annoyed with Yang Kai. Although Fu Zhun's accusations just now had been somewhat extreme, Yang Kai did bear a large part of the responsibility for today's events on Dragon Isle. He was the one who had wounded Fu Chi, and he was also the one who had tied down so many members of the Dragon Clan. Otherwise, Wu Kuang's scheme could not have proceeded so smoothly.

Zhu Yan knew this, and Yang Kai himself understood it as well. He simply did not like hearing Fu Zhun place all the blame on him.

Yang Kai's expression sank, but he did not retreat. He calmly said, "It seems Dragon Isle doesn't intend to concern itself with this matter. Fine. If you won't handle it, I will!"

As soon as he said this, everyone looked at him with strange expressions.

Fu Xuan even secretly sent him a Divine Sense transmission. "Don't say such foolish things. Listen to them and step aside for now. There may still be room to turn things around between you and Qing'er."

Yang Kai looked at her gratefully, but slowly shook his head. "I ask only one thing: do not obstruct me. Simply let me leave through here. If I catch Wu Kuang, I will deal with him myself. If I fail to catch him, none of you will suffer any loss."

As he spoke, he pointed toward the Void Passage.

While everyone had been speaking, the Void Passage had slowly begun to close. The World Laws themselves possessed the divine might to repair themselves, after all. Wu Kuang had forcibly opened this passage using the power of the Dragon Temple, which amounted to damaging the world itself. Under the power of the Laws, such damage would soon disappear.

If Yang Kai wanted to leave through this passage, he had to do so quickly. Otherwise, once it closed, he would be unable to leave even if he wanted to.

"In your dreams!" Fu Zhun shouted.

Zhu Yan also shook his head. "We cannot agree to that."

Allowing Wu Kuang to travel from Dragon Isle to a Lower Plane Star Domain was already a dereliction of duty on Dragon Isle's part. Although they did not need to answer to anyone, it would still be shameful if word spread. If they allowed Yang Kai to go through as well, would that not be slapping themselves in the face?

Since both Elders of the Dragon Clan opposed it, there was no longer any room for negotiation. If Yang Kai knew what was good for him, he would naturally avoid confronting them, for if he angered the Dragon Clan, how could he possibly come to a good end?

Yet to everyone's surprise, Yang Kai not only refused to retreat, but took another step forward instead. With a resolute expression, he declared, "If you cannot agree, then this boy can only force his way through."

"You refuse a toast only to drink a forfeit!" Fu Zhun snorted. "Seize him!"

Her command was clearly directed at the members of the Dragon Clan.

Although she greatly disliked Yang Kai, given her status and cultivation, she could not very well use her strength to bully the weak.

At her command, more than a dozen members of the Dragon Clan sprang into action.

Although many of them had previously suffered a great loss at Yang Kai's hands when he broke their protective technique, they had not sustained serious injuries. Now, with several Elders presiding over this place, how could they possibly fear a single Yang Kai?

In the blink of an eye, Yang Kai and Zhu Qing were completely surrounded.

"I'd like to see who dares!" Yang Kai suddenly roared. He stamped one foot hard against the ground, and the entire Dragon Temple abruptly trembled.

As a resounding Dragon Roar rang out, the enormous phantom of a Golden Sacred Dragon suddenly appeared behind his head. It shone with dazzling golden light and radiated an overwhelming momentum, its head held high as it gazed haughtily in all directions.

The moment the aura of the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin appeared, every member of the Dragon Clan became dazed, and even the Dragon Clan's several Elders were no exception.

The Ancestral Dragon Origin!

This was the true Ancestral Dragon Origin!

Although they had long known that the Ancestral Dragon Origin was inside Yang Kai, this was the first time any member of the Dragon Clan had seen it with their own eyes.

That pure Dragon aura was something even the Great Elder and Second Elder could not compare to. To every member of the Dragon Clan, this Ancestral Dragon Origin seemed to be the source of all things and the embrace of a mother, involuntarily filling them with an incomparably intimate feeling.

Their overwhelming hostility vanished like smoke in an instant.

The Dragon Clan's several Elders all grew solemn and suppressed their minds, refusing to let themselves be influenced by the power of the Ancestral Dragon Origin.

"Second Elder, I'm afraid you've forgotten what place this is and what power I possess!" Once the Ancestral Dragon Origin appeared, Yang Kai seemed to suddenly grow taller. Although there was no trace of Dragon Transformation on him, the gaze he directed at Fu Zhun was lofty and carried the air of one looking down from above.

His hands suddenly formed a strange seal, and two entirely different voices emerged from his mouth, as though two people were speaking through him at the same time.

One was the exceedingly strange Dragon Language, profound and complex.

The other was his normal voice. "Water without a source does not flow, and wood without roots does not grow. The Ancestral Origin is here, so why have you not come forth to pay your respects?"

The two voices rose and fell over one another, yet complemented each other perfectly. Far from sounding jarring, they instead possessed a unique charm.

The many ordinary members of the Dragon Clan did not react much upon hearing this, but the expressions of the Dragon Clan's several Elders all changed at once.

A strange light also flashed through Fu Xuan's eyes, and she let out a soft cry.

Dragon Roars rang out once more, but this time they came from every direction within the Dragon Temple, rising and falling without end.

It was as though the sleeping Great Dragons had heard a summons and all awakened together.

Light flared through the dark hall and rapidly spread in every direction like a prairie fire. In the blink of an eye, the enormous Dragon Temple became as bright as day.

Great Dragons came weaving through the air in scarlet, orange, red, green, azure, blue, and violet, turning the hall into a riot of brilliant colors in an instant.

The first Great Dragon circled several times above Yang Kai's head before settling into place. Facing the Golden Sacred Dragon, it lowered its noble head.

Then came the second...

The third...

...

Everyone stared in utter disbelief.

## Chapter 2949 — Be Gentle With Me

A normal member of the Dragon Clan could not possibly harbor two types of Origin Power, much less two fundamentally opposing Origin Qi. Ice and fire simply could not coexist. If Zhu Qing had possessed such conflicting origins in her body from the beginning, she would never have survived to this day.

In a flash, a bold guess surfaced in Yang Kai’s mind.

That Ice Dragon Origin was likely the power left behind by the great dragon that perished in the Frozen Earth.

The reason Zhu Qing came to the Frozen Earth was not to search for her clansman’s remains, because Li Jiao had made it clear back then that the dragon’s bones had long since turned to dust, leaving not a trace behind. Zhu Qing insisted on coming only to retrieve the Origin Power left by her kin.

Now it seemed she had found it, only to be harmed by this Ice Dragon Origin.

Origin Power had no thought or reason; it was merely a force. With two such forces coexisting in Zhu Qing’s body, a tremendous instinctive rejection and struggle erupted, her flesh serving as the battlefield for their endless conflict.

Yang Kai could not see this internal struggle, unless the two forces manifested as directly as they did now.

Just as he was shocked, the two giant dragon shadows roared in unison and then plunged back into Zhu Qing’s body, vanishing from sight.

Zhu Qing howled in pain. At this moment, she was completely unconscious, her mind lost. Even her earlier opening of her eyes had not been a conscious act, but a reflex of her body.

Visibly, a thin layer of frost appeared on Zhu Qing’s skin, wisps of cold seeping from her flesh, as if to freeze her solid.

But soon, a scorching force surged forth, shattering the cold into pieces. Zhu Qing’s entire body became as if set ablaze—her skin flushed red, her whole form burning hot, even the ground beneath her was scorched black.

The two forces cycled through Zhu Qing in endless alternation, never ceasing, and her moans grew ever more pained.

Yang Kai was at a loss, completely unsure what to do.

If it were only an injury, no matter how severe, he could always think of a way to help. But in truth, after being healed by the Everlasting Tree’s essence, Zhu Qing no longer had any serious wounds—only the two origins battling uncontrollably within her.

As the battlefield, her body was bound to suffer devastating damage.

Origin, origin... Yang Kai’s eyes lit up. He rushed forward, scooping Zhu Qing into his arms again, letting her rest against his chest. Then, he silently activated his own Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power.

A golden dragon shadow shimmered behind him, flashing once before plunging into his body and disappearing.

The temperature on Zhu Qing’s skin changed rapidly—one moment burning hot as fire, the next as if plucked from a world of ice, chilling to the bone.

But as the aura of the Golden Sacred Dragon origin spread, the alternation of hot and cold suddenly slowed.

There was hope! Yang Kai rejoiced inwardly. He had only been trying to see if his Golden Sacred Dragon origin could exert some suppressive effect, and now it seemed it truly worked.

The most urgent thing now was to wake Zhu Qing. Only if she regained consciousness could she fully mobilize her own origin’s power, suppress the Ice Dragon Origin, and drive it out or destroy it.

Yang Kai steadied his mind and mobilized the power of his Divine Soul. Just as he was about to cast his technique, Zhu Qing suddenly reached back and wrapped him in a tight embrace. It was as if she had tasted something sweet—his aura triggered an instinctive, almost reflexive response from her.

Though Zhu Qing’s figure was petite and delicate, she was, after all, a member of the Dragon Clan. Her strength was in no way inferior to Yang Kai’s. He had experienced her wild power the very first time they met.

With this squeeze, Yang Kai felt as if his body was about to be crushed, his face turning a little blue.

Yet the soft sensation pressing against his chest sent his mind reeling.

And that wasn’t all—Zhu Qing actually began to nuzzle about in his arms like a little puppy, sniffing him incessantly, making strange noises in her throat, as if she had found some delicious treat and was eager to feast.

Yang Kai was struck as if by lightning, his body going rigid. Only when a sharp pain shot from his chest did he snap out of it, angrily pushing Zhu Qing’s head away, his palm on her face as he rasped, “Are you a dog? Why are you biting me?”

Zhu Qing’s eyes were full of confusion, shining with a strange light. Her breathing grew inexplicably rapid. Yang Kai could clearly feel the heat of her breath, her exquisite face flushed and alluring, radiating a charm that made one want to throw themselves into the fire.

Yang Kai felt as if tens of thousands of ants were crawling over his heart, the itch nearly unbearable.

Zhu Qing opened her mouth again, her white teeth biting down hard on the base of Yang Kai’s thumb, leaving a mark that actually drew golden blood from the wound.

The blood was like the most delicious food. Zhu Qing gulped it down, her small, warm tongue ceaselessly lapping at Yang Kai’s palm, sending tingles through him.

Yang Kai wanted to pull his hand back, but with her doing this, he found himself reluctant to let go.

Soon, Yang Kai noticed something strange. After swallowing his blood, Zhu Qing’s condition seemed to improve greatly. The alternation of hot and cold slowed even more, and the cold was quickly suppressed by the heat, vanishing almost instantly.

His blood seemed to be of immense help to her current state!

After a moment’s thought, Yang Kai vaguely understood.

Though he was born human, after cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art, he had begun to truly merge with the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power. In other words, his blood was no longer purely human, but now contained some of the Dragon Clan’s bloodline—and a very high-grade one at that.

Among the Dragon Clan, the strong exerted great suppression over the weak through their bloodlines.

If the aura of the Golden Sacred Dragon origin could help Zhu Qing alleviate her crisis, then his half-dragon blood would be even more effective. No wonder Zhu Qing bit him—she must have sensed this herself, which was why she acted so.

Of course, Yang Kai could have broken free, but he simply stood there, letting Zhu Qing drink his blood.

Zhu Qing was still muddle-headed, her mind not yet restored. Her hazy eyes brimmed with allure as she gulped down Yang Kai’s blood, her nimble tongue circling endlessly in his palm.

The evil thoughts in Yang Kai’s body nearly erupted. He had to struggle desperately to keep his mind steady, turning his gaze away from Zhu Qing’s eyes and ignoring the tingling in his palm, afraid something else might happen.

Who knew how much time passed before he suddenly felt Zhu Qing release his hand.

He opened his eyes and immediately saw Zhu Qing, her face flushed, looking at him. Delighted, he asked, “Are you alright now?”

He could tell that Zhu Qing had likely awakened and regained her senses, for the look in her eyes was now different from before.

Instead of answering, Zhu Qing opened her mouth and unleashed a Dragon Clan secret art. A spear of blood shot forth—her own blood, gleaming crimson like flickering flames. Mid-air, the dragon blood transformed into an intricately woven seal that struck Yang Kai squarely on the forehead.

Yang Kai looked blank, then collapsed straight to the ground.

This Dragon Clan secret art seemed to have a powerful binding force, leaving Yang Kai unable to move a finger. Alarmed, he instinctively tried to summon his Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power.

Though Zhu Qing’s secret art was strong, Yang Kai felt that with the Golden Sacred Dragon origin’s suppression, he should be able to break free. He had simply been caught off guard, never expecting her to attack him, and so he fell for it.

But he did not act immediately, for a fragrant scent suddenly wafted over. Zhu Qing was now kneeling between his legs, her hands on his chest, looking down at him. Her fiery red hair, like the finest silk, draped over her smooth back, covering her shapely hips and adding a bewitching allure.

Her breathing was rapid to the extreme, as if she might suffocate at any moment. Her ample chest heaved up and down, drawing breathtaking curves.

That intoxicating fragrance grew even stronger, as if every pore of her body exuded it, filling the entire world.

Her long lashes trembled—she was clearly nervous, but her eyes were like a stirred pool, revealing intense unrest and struggle.

“You... what are you doing?” Yang Kai blurted out, feeling his question was rather foolish.

He recognized that look—it was the gaze of someone starving, searching for food. Back then, Shan Qingluo would often show him such eyes.

His blood seemed to ignite all at once, boiling in his meridians and flesh, setting him ablaze. The evil thoughts he had long suppressed now found an outlet, overwhelming his reason.

The two small hands on his chest suddenly exerted force, tearing his clothes apart.

Yang Kai took a deep breath, opened his mouth, but no words came out.

Their clothes quickly became disheveled, the fabric wrapping them soon reduced to shreds, fluttering everywhere. Zhu Qing kept making strange noises in her throat, her burning body straddling Yang Kai, writhing restlessly.

She knew what she wanted, but had never experienced this before. It took her a long time to gradually figure out how.

Her fair body slowly lowered, pearly teeth biting her red lips, her brows tightly knit.

Yang Kai closed his eyes, spread his hands, gripping the grass at his sides, and murmured, “It’s my first time, be gentle with me...”

Zhu Qing’s body suddenly sank, a flush spreading across her snowy skin. She threw her head back as if suffocating, her hair cascading down, unmoving for a long while. Only after a long time did she scream, a heart-wrenching cry.

Yang Kai howled as well, feeling as if he had fallen into magma, unimaginable heat engulfing him from all sides, as if to melt him away.

He had never experienced such a feeling before—it was an indescribable thrill.

Within the Small Xuan Realm, heaven became their blanket and earth their bed. Hair tangled in disarray, fingers intertwined, they asked not for glory and riches in this life, nor for companionship in the next. When passion reaches its zenith, who can blame whom for surrendering to it?

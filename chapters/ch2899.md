## Chapter 2899 — Entering Demonic State

Within the void, Yang Kai’s figure reappeared, his face pale as paper, clearly at the end of his strength. The Mountain and River Bell had also transformed into a massive entity, floating before him as it continuously emitted ancient, desolate tolls. Wave after wave of sound rippled down from the bell’s body, sweeping across and shaking countless demons clinging to the Divine Tree loose. The weaker demons burst into clouds of blood before even reaching the ground, leaving no corpses behind, while only the stronger ones managed to resist.

Yet even those Demon Commanders were bleeding from all seven orifices, their minds reeling as they suffered grievous injuries. Only Demon King-level experts could barely hold on, but even they kept falling, increasing the gap between themselves and the Mountain and River Bell.

After more than a dozen bell tolls, the demons on the Divine Tree were completely cleared away.

Only Mo Duo’s massive body continued to rise rapidly. The waves of sound from the Mountain and River Bell struck his flesh, sending out ripples, but could not halt his advance.

“Wretch, did you come out here to die?” Mo Duo roared, climbing even faster. Through layers of leaves, his enormous eyes locked onto Yang Kai’s position.

At last, he overcame all obstacles and saw that insignificant barbarian. Mo Duo grinned ferociously and reached out to grab him. Yang Kai did not evade—or rather, could not evade. A Demon Saint’s casual strike embodied the profound truths of the Great Dao. It seemed slow, but it was the essence of his life’s cultivation, not something one could dodge at will.

The giant hand seized Yang Kai as if grasping an inconspicuous insect. Mo Duo brought him before his face, applying a bit of force.

The sound of bones cracking rang out. Even the body sustained by the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique could not withstand the brute strength of a Demon Saint, especially one at his peak.

Yet Yang Kai’s face showed no pain at all; instead, he was utterly calm. This calmness made Mo Duo extremely displeased.

“If you have any tricks left, use them. Let’s see if you can stop me.” Mo Duo snorted coldly, the breath blasting from his nostrils forming violent gusts of wind.

“I do have one more move…” Yang Kai gave him a slight smile.

Mo Duo was momentarily stunned, not expecting this insignificant barbarian to still have something up his sleeve at this point. But his expression quickly turned indifferent. No matter what trump card the other party had, it could not possibly work against him now that he had regained his peak strength—unless Yang Kai possessed power equal to his own.

Mo Duo said no more. His grip tightened abruptly, intending to crush this barbarian to death.

The sound of bones breaking grew denser, but Yang Kai seemed oblivious to the pain. Suddenly, he pressed a finger to his forehead, his expression more solemn than ever, and intoned in a low voice, “Enter Demonic State!”

Crack—

A crisp sound echoed, as if something had shattered.

Mo Duo instinctively felt a deep sense of foreboding, an intense discomfort as if something momentous was about to happen. Demon Saints were especially sensitive to danger, so he trusted his intuition.

His grip tightened sharply, but at that moment, Mo Duo’s expression changed. His already huge eyes widened even further, as if witnessing something utterly inconceivable.

An immense, irresistible force of rejection suddenly welled up in his hand. This force felt akin to his own Demonic Qi, but somehow even more ancient, as if from a far earlier era. This astonished Mo Duo greatly.

Pitch-black energy oozed like paste from between his fingers, gradually gathering in one place and emanating a heart-palpitating aura.

The right eye of the insignificant barbarian in his grasp suddenly turned pitch-black, like a black hole capable of devouring everything in existence.

When Mo Duo met the gaze of that pitch-black eye, he was dazed for a moment, feeling his mind become unsettled and his Divine Soul tremble.

In the next instant, his expression changed dramatically as he growled, “Why do you have the aura of the Ancient Demons?”

He finally understood the origin of the aura emerging from this barbarian’s body—it was the purest ancient demonic aura! The Ancient Demons were beings even older than himself, the ancestors of the demon race, the first living beings born when the Demon Domain came into existence. It was these Ancient Demons who carved out territory in the Demon Domain, creating space for the demon race to survive. After millions of years, the later generations of demons came to be.

The Ancient Demons had not perished. Even now, there were still Ancient Demons living in the Demon Domain. Each Ancient Demon was an extremely powerful existence, one that even Demon Saints had to show respect to.

However, though the Ancient Demons were strong, their intelligence was pitifully low. They were like a group of untamed beasts, acting purely on instinct. Not even the Great Demon God could command them.

In the Demon Domain, Ancient Demons were extremely rare. Ordinary demons, and even Demon Saint-level experts, might never see one in their entire lives. They lived in the harshest, most hidden environments and never revealed themselves lightly.

Mo Duo had once been fortunate enough to encounter them. He had accidentally trespassed into a place where several Ancient Demons lived and was hunted for hundreds of thousands of li as an intruder, barely escaping with his life. The memory of their aura was still vivid in his mind.

Now, seeing ancient demonic aura in this world, how could Mo Duo not be shocked?

Only then did he finally understand why the Demonic Qi formed from the shattering of his Demon Heart had no effect on this Barbarian. The other party already possessed Ancient Demon Qi within his body, so it was only natural he could resist the erosion of his own Demonic Qi.

But… why? Why would the aura of the Ancient Demons appear in the body of an outsider?

Yang Kai did not answer—whether he did not want to or simply did not hear was unclear. At this moment, he only felt his Divine Soul in turmoil. After unlocking the two-colored seal at his Dantian, he immediately sensed a power far beyond his imagination surging out, flooding his entire body. Under the influence of this power, he felt as if he could do anything, as if he could pluck the stars and seize the moon with a wave of his hand.

While gaining immense power, all the evil hidden deep within his heart was awakened and magnified. His consciousness gradually became lost. His intact left eye twitched violently, darting up and down, left and right, as if out of his control. Yet the right eye, now pitch-black, remained a void, forming a stark contrast with the left and appearing even more bizarre.

In his Sea of Consciousness, a strange black aura arose, seemingly possessing immense infectious power. Wherever it passed, everything dimmed. Each memory orb was stained black, losing its former colorful brilliance.

Yang Kai hid his Divine Soul on the island formed by the Warm Spirit Lotus, using this supreme treasure to barely maintain his last shred of clarity.

This was not his first time entering the Demonic State, yet this time was somewhat different from before. He found that the power he gained was far greater, as if he had gradually adapted to the unsealed Demonic Qi within his body and could now exert more of its strength. But correspondingly, the price he had to pay was also greater.

This was his final trump card, one he dared not use unless absolutely necessary, for every use risked truly falling into the demonic path, perhaps losing himself and becoming a demon unable to recognize even his own kin.

But faced with the relentless pressure of a Demon Saint, Yang Kai had no other choice.

His body, which had originally been only two zhang tall, grew even taller after entering the Demonic State. The immense power directly forced Mo Duo’s palm open as his body expanded until it reached three zhang in height before coming to a stop. Even the short horns on his forehead had grown longer.

Then Yang Kai discovered something that delighted him.

The Dragon Transformation Secret Technique seemed to have a restraining effect on the Demonic State. Although his Sea of Consciousness was in chaos, he could still maintain his sanity. This was not only due to the Warm Spirit Lotus, but also the enhancement from his dragon-transformed body.

He did not understand why, but vaguely guessed it was related to the Origin Power of the Golden Sacred Dragon.

His Dragon Transformation was a secret technique built upon the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power. Even now, the strength he could bring forth was likely less than one percent of it. If one day he could fully unleash the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power, he would truly transform into a dragon, roaming freely across the myriad worlds of the universe, rather than merely taking on this three-zhang-tall draconic humanoid form as he did now.

The Origin Power of the Golden Sacred Dragon was divine, while the ancient demonic aura was demonic. The two were fundamentally opposed, yet now coexisted in Yang Kai’s body and were being used simultaneously, producing an utterly inexplicable phenomenon.

Yang Kai craved power, so the ancient demonic aura took the upper hand, allowing him to enter the Demonic State. But he did not wish to lose his mind, so his dragon-transformed body and the Warm Spirit Lotus guarded the last shred of clarity in his Sea of Consciousness, preventing him from completely losing himself.

Agonizing pain spread from every part of his body. Yang Kai felt as if he was being shattered and then reassembled—not just flesh and bone, but even his internal organs.

Such pain was unbearable for anyone.

Yet Yang Kai, who managed to maintain a sliver of clarity, felt the pain ten or twenty times more intensely, far beyond his own limits.

He threw his head back and roared, his body trembling violently. His left eye twitched even more fiercely.

Mo Duo’s expression changed again and again. He found that no matter how hard he tried, he could no longer close his hand or crush this once insignificant insect.

This foreigner had, in an extremely short time, gained the strength to contend with him.

Mo Duo could not accept such an outcome.

He planted his feet firmly, then reached out with his other hand to grab Yang Kai’s head, his face twisted and ferocious.

Yang Kai’s roar abruptly ceased, stopping without warning. The cracking sounds from within his body also fell silent.

He was now completely shrouded in black qi, his figure obscured. Yet, bizarrely, the pitch-blackness of his right eye was clearly visible, standing out starkly—an extremely unusual phenomenon.

“Ancient Demon’s Eye!” Mo Duo finally realized what was special about that eye, his heart filled with shock.

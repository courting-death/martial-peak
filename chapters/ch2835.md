## Chapter 2835 — Taking the Initiative

As the light faded, both Shaman Chi and the six skulls vanished, replaced by a monstrous figure about five zhang tall. The creature had a green face and fangs, its hair wild and disheveled, its features twisted and ferocious. At first glance, it looked just like the soul-reaping fiends spoken of in legends.

On the fiend’s body, countless translucent human faces floated and sank, struggling and writhing, each showing a different expression of misery. The wails that came from their mouths sent chills down the spine.

Yang Kai stood with sword in hand, his expression icy cold. In a voice as chilling as winter frost, he spat, “You truly deserve to die.”

The faces struggling on the fiend’s body were clearly souls! Where had these souls come from? Obviously, they were the people Shaman Chi had killed and devoured. Those slain by Shaman Chi not only suffered immense terror and torment before death and had their bodies eaten, but even their souls could not escape. He devoured and refined them, trapping them within his body, never to reincarnate, doomed to eternal damnation.

Such a Shaman King, in later generations, would be a true evil cultivator, a target for all to destroy.

Yet in this era, with the Bone-Eater Tribe protected by a Shaman Saint, he could do as he pleased.

Transformed into a fiend, Shaman Chi’s already formidable aura surged to new heights. The terrifying pressure he now exuded was enough to rival that of an Upper-Level Shaman King.

He let out a sinister cackle, the breath from his mouth seeming to bring death to the world itself. The surrounding grass and trees withered instantly. He spread his massive hand, turning it into a curtain of light that blotted out the sky, and grabbed at Yang Kai.

The space around them was sealed. Yang Kai tried to teleport, but it was completely ineffective. His expression hardened, but instead of retreating, he advanced, Myriad Sword in hand unleashing a ring of light. Wrapped in sword radiance, he charged straight at the giant hand, the power of a Great Shaman Master surging wildly around him.

The giant hand closed, and a muffled grunt came from within. But in the next instant, a hole appeared on the back of the hand, and Yang Kai, drenched in blood, burst out, fearlessly descending atop Shaman Chi’s head and slashing down with all his might.

Shaman Chi showed no fear or panic. With his other palm, he swept through the air, as if brushing away a fly with ease.

Before Yang Kai’s attack could land, that palm had already swatted him away. The shield of Shamanic Art protecting him was instantly shattered, offering no resistance at all.

Boom boom boom…

Yang Kai tumbled through the air, crashing into a small mountain before he could steady himself. When he got up, he was in a sorry state, blood streaming from his mouth and nose.

“This is the end!” Shaman Chi’s cold, harsh voice rang in his ears. As the words fell, he reappeared before Yang Kai, reaching out to grab him again.

Yang Kai sneered coldly, “Boasting so shamelessly—aren’t you afraid the wind will cut your tongue?” As he spoke, his hands moved in a blur, forming seals at lightning speed. In an instant, a mysterious power surged forth, rippling through the air.

Shaman Chi was momentarily stunned, inexplicably feeling as if time had stopped. At the same moment, an overwhelming sense of danger welled up in his heart, filling him with alarm. He had transformed into a fiend—his strongest trump card. With this, he could contend with an Upper-Level Shaman King. His opponent was just a Great Shaman Master. Even if he struggled on the brink of death, what trouble could he possibly cause?

Yet the panic and crisis were real, impossible to ignore.

Realizing this, he quickly withdrew eighty percent of his strength to guard himself, taking a fully defensive posture.

In the next moment, time seemed to flow again. The Great Shaman Master before him calmly struck out with a palm. That palm looked utterly powerless, yet it gave the sense of destroying all things, of sending all things through the cycle of reincarnation.

“Years Like a Shuttle!”

Shaman Chi’s pupils shrank sharply. Though he couldn’t see what mystery lay within that palm, his instincts screamed that he absolutely could not take it head-on. Even if it did not kill him, it would leave him grievously wounded.

He opened his mouth wide and let out a howl.

In an instant, countless ferocious Yin-Souls flew from his mouth, charging at the palm imprint.

Boom boom boom…

A violent, surging power erupted. Shaman Chi used this moment to retreat over a hundred zhang, while every Yin-Soul struck by the palm imprint was utterly annihilated without exception.

Before dissipating, these Yin-Souls showed no pain. Instead, each looked as if they’d been released, their expressions peaceful. A few even glanced at Yang Kai with gratitude before vanishing like smoke.

What kind of Shamanic Art was this? To possess such power! Shaman Chi’s expression was full of shock and doubt. The methods this Great Shaman Master displayed had already surpassed his understanding.

After that palm, Yang Kai was gasping for breath, feeling as if all his strength had been drained. He cursed under his breath.

The Years Like a Shuttle imprint was a Great Emperor’s divine ability. Though he’d had the chance to cultivate it, it wasn’t something he could freely use at his current level. For a Lower-Level Great Shaman Master to wield such a divine ability was simply too much of a stretch.

So after unleashing it, Yang Kai immediately felt utterly drained…

But since Shaman Chi had already used his trump card, if Yang Kai didn’t risk everything, he’d have no chance to resist.

Not daring to show weakness, Yang Kai wiped the blood from his mouth and nose, using the motion to slip an Imperial-Grade Spirit Pill into his mouth. Swallowing it, he pointed Myriad Sword at Shaman Chi and sneered, “Scared?”

Shaman Chi snorted, “Your tricks are certainly unexpected, but… they’re far from enough!”

As he spoke, he grasped at the air, and a pitch-black scythe suddenly appeared in his hand. With a casual wave, he slashed it toward where Yang Kai stood.

Yang Kai’s face changed dramatically. In that instant, he felt his Divine Soul suddenly lurch, as if it wanted to leave his body. He immediately realized that the scythe wasn’t some shamanic weapon, but a strange and mysterious Shamanic Art with the power to reap souls. If his Divine Soul hadn’t far surpassed what a Great Shaman Master should possess, he’d have been in real trouble.

Yang Kai grunted, focusing his mind and locking down his Sea of Consciousness. He felt a powerful force pulling at his Divine Soul, trying to wrench his Spirit Body from his Sea of Consciousness.

Vaguely, it seemed there was an invisible link connecting his Sea of Consciousness to Shaman Chi’s black scythe. As Shaman Chi swung the scythe faster, the pulling force grew stronger, leaving Yang Kai no room for error.

“It’s hard, isn’t it? If it’s so hard, why not stop resisting? Just surrender yourself to me, and I’ll show you the rest of the wonders.” Shaman Chi’s sinister voice rang out, thick with temptation, trying to break Yang Kai’s will.

“You want my Divine Soul?” Yang Kai growled through gritted teeth.

“You can’t resist. Why deceive yourself?” Shaman Chi sneered.

“Fine!” Yang Kai shouted, “If you want it, I’ll give it to you!”

Shaman Chi was stunned. He’d thought the other was joking—after all, once a person’s Divine Soul was taken, they became a walking corpse, with no hope of survival. Who would willingly hand over their Divine Soul?

But soon, Shaman Chi was dumbfounded.

Because the other really wasn’t joking. He truly gave up his defenses, allowing Shaman Chi’s Shamanic Art to succeed and draw out his Divine Soul.

“This feeling…” Shaman Chi was shocked, then overjoyed. “Wonderful, absolutely wonderful! I can’t wait to taste it!”

He never expected this Great Shaman Master’s Divine Soul to be so powerful and pure. Such a Divine Soul shouldn’t belong to a Great Shaman Master, but to an Upper-Level Shaman King.

No, no, he’d killed Upper-Level Shaman Kings before, but even their Divine Souls weren’t this exquisite.

In an instant, Shaman Chi was like a cat smelling fish, his eyes shining, even licking his lips in anticipation.

“I’ll take it, then. Don’t worry, since you’re so cooperative, I won’t make you suffer!” Shaman Chi laughed, and with a flick of his black scythe, a dazzling ball of light was pulled from Yang Kai’s mind.

That radiance was utterly pure and flawless, without the slightest impurity. Not only was it unimaginably refined, but it also brimmed with a boundless, majestic power.

Shaman Chi’s eyes lit up. He’d only smelled the Divine Soul’s aura before, but now, seeing it with his own eyes, he knew he hadn’t been mistaken. This was a feast he could not refuse.

After his Divine Soul was pulled out, Yang Kai dropped from the air like a wooden stake.

Without a Divine Soul, one naturally couldn’t control their body.

Meanwhile, the extracted Divine Soul shot rapidly toward Shaman Chi. Suddenly, Shaman Chi’s expression changed, his joy and anticipation vanishing, replaced by fury. “You dare trick me!”

Yang Kai burst out laughing. “Idiot, when have I ever lied to you? Didn’t I come to you myself? But… let’s see if you have the ability to swallow it!”

As he spoke, a blade of light burst from the Spirit Body. The entire Spirit Body transformed into a long blade, slashing straight at Shaman Chi.

Shaman Chi retreated sharply. He didn’t know what technique the other was using, but all of this Great Shaman Master’s methods had been too bizarre—he couldn’t afford to be careless.

The black scythe in his hand turned into a pure Divine Soul attack, slashing forward.

In the next instant, Shaman Chi’s face changed again. His Shamanic Art had no effect at all. The opponent’s Divine Soul, wrapped in blade light, was unstoppable, breaking through everything in its path and closing in on him in a flash.

Shaman Chi was terrified. He surged his Divine Sense, defending with all his might and not daring to let the other approach.

But the result left him aghast.

His Shaman King-level Divine Sense blockade couldn’t stop the other’s blade. As his mind throbbed with pain, the opponent’s Divine Soul, along with the blade light, broke through his defenses and charged into his Sea of Consciousness.

Shaman Chi grunted, but despite the sudden change, he didn’t panic. His body plummeted, eyes closing as he manifested his own Spirit Body within his Sea of Consciousness, quietly hiding to the side.

In the hellish Sea of Consciousness, fiends ran rampant, ghostly shadows everywhere, wails and howls filling the air. If he hadn’t seen it with his own eyes, Yang Kai would never have believed anyone’s Sea of Consciousness could look like this.

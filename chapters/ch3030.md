## Chapter 3030 — On What Grounds

The surroundings remained noisy. Yuan Wu endured it for a while, but when he could bear it no longer, he roared, "All of you, shut up!"

The scene instantly fell silent, and everyone turned to look at him. Yuan Wu swept a cold gaze over the crowd before gritting his teeth and saying, "I know a little about the general situation, but I'm not entirely clear on the details. Let me examine things first."

Saying so, he picked up the Spatial Ring Fu Qi had tossed him earlier and carefully examined it.

Everyone stared closely at his reaction.

They saw Yuan Wu’s expression first fill with shock, then joy, before it immediately sank again. After shifting several times, it finally returned to that same bitter look.

With a shake of his hand, he suddenly took something resembling a sheet of beast hide from the Spatial Ring and carefully studied it. The instant he saw what was on it, he let out a low cry of surprise.

The Emperor Realm Masters gathered around him could no longer restrain themselves and crowded closer. Once they saw what was drawn on the beast-hide sheet, their expressions also changed.

"What is this?"

"It looks like a palace."

"And a huge one at that."

"Huh? It seems even the construction materials have specific requirements."

"What does this mean? Are we supposed to build this palace?"

"Surely not? Why would the Dragon Clan want to build a palace? I've never heard of the Dragon Clan having any need for such a thing."

"Incredible, truly incredible. If this palace is completed, I'm afraid there won't be a second one like it in the entire Star Realm."

"Qing Gold Purple Copper, Ice Soul Snow Jade, Heavenly Crystal Meteor Sand... Hiss, these are all materials used to refine Imperial Treasures. A-Are they really going to use them to construct a palace? Isn't this far too extravagant?"

Once the group of Emperor Realm Masters clearly saw what was on the beast-hide blueprint, they immediately lost their composure, their expressions changing one after another.

Those gathered on the outskirts who could not see the blueprint also widened their eyes in disbelief, their faces twitching repeatedly.

Qing Gold Purple Copper? Ice Soul Snow Jade? Heavenly Crystal Meteor Sand?

One astonishing name after another thundered in their ears, leaving them too stunned to recover.

Although they had always lived on Dragon Isle and never interacted with the outside world, that did not mean they could not recognize valuable materials. Half-Dragon City had no shortage of Alchemists or Artificers, nor did Dragon Isle lack cultivation resources. So long as they did not enter the Dragon Palace's territory, they were free to move about this Small World.

Thus, they all knew exactly what these materials represented.

These were all exceedingly rare Artifact Refining materials. Each one was worth a fortune and extremely difficult to find. If an Emperor Realm Master obtained even a little, it would greatly help improve their Imperial Treasure.

Yet from what they were hearing now, these materials, so precious that they were nearly impossible to find, were actually only going to be used to build a palace?

Could that really be true? Everyone was filled with doubt, unable to believe what they had heard.

In an instant, all eyes focused on Yuan Wu, wanting him to give them an answer.

Yang Kai was also somewhat surprised. If this was really true, the scale of this undertaking was simply too great. Even a Great Emperor Sect could not manage such extravagance. Could Dragon Isle truly do it?

Yuan Wu continued studying the beast-hide blueprint. Only after quite some time did he put it away and sweep his gaze over the crowd. His gloomy eyes paused briefly on Yang Kai before he announced, "The Dragon Clan has given us an extremely arduous task!"

The moment those words fell, the crowd erupted.

"Surely not?"

"We really have to build a palace?"

"And with those materials?"

"Building a palace itself is no problem. There are so many of us, so no matter how large it is, we can certainly finish it within the one-month deadline. But the key issue is those materials..."

"Exactly. Where are we supposed to find them? How can we build the palace without the materials? The Dragon Clan is trying to work us to death!"

"Shh! What nonsense are you spouting? This is the Dragon Palace. Be careful, the walls have ears."

The crowd began discussing the matter noisily once more.

Yuan Wu's expression was as ugly as could be. After waiting a long while, he continued, "You are correct. The Dragon Clan wants us to construct a palace, and an incomparably grand and enormous one at that. This Lord took a look just now. Once completed, the palace will cover more than a hundred mu."

A chorus of sharp breaths rang out.

More than a hundred mu?

How large was this island? A hundred mu would occupy almost a tenth of it. They had originally thought they had more than enough manpower, but the moment they heard the palace would cover over a hundred mu, every one of them was dumbfounded.

Was this thing really a palace? Wasn't it a city?

Yuan Wu raised a hand and pointed toward the very center of the island, declaring, "The palace will be built over there. The Dragon Clan has already planned out most of it, so we need only follow the plan."

Seeing signs that the crowd was about to grow restless again, Yuan Wu raised a hand to quiet them and shouted in a low voice, “This Lord knows what you are all worried about. Dragon Isle has prepared materials.” He lifted the ring in his hand high. “They are all inside here.”

At these words, everyone stared blankly for a moment before a chorus of relieved sighs rang out.

"So the Dragon Clan made preparations long ago. That nearly scared me to death."

"Exactly. If we had to find those materials ourselves, we might as well all commit suicide."

"Now that's more like it. I've long heard that the Dragon Clan is wealthy, but only now do I know the rumors are true."

"Indeed. Using so many materials just to build a palace is simply too extravagant and wasteful. If only I could have a little..."

"Dream on. Who would dare skim anything from that ring? If you really did such a thing, you wouldn't even know how you died."

"That's true, heh heh. I was just saying."

Just as everyone relaxed, Yuan Wu sneered. "This Lord does not mean to dampen everyone's spirits, but the materials in the ring are insufficient for the palace. A large portion is still missing, and we will have to mine it ourselves."

"What?" Everyone was instantly shocked. Their moods had risen and fallen far too many times in the span of a few sentences, leaving them somewhat unable to bear it.

The materials in the ring were insufficient?

They had to mine the rest themselves?

Where were they supposed to mine so many precious materials? Every one of them was something that could only be found by chance.

Yuan Wu said, "There is no need to worry. Several Spirit Islands within the Dragon Palace's territory produce these materials, so we only need to mine them and bring them back."

Everyone's taut nerves relaxed once more. If that was the case, then it was not too bad. What they feared was the Dragon Clan making them find the materials themselves. Even the tiniest amount of such precious materials was scarce in the outside world, so where could they possibly find them?

Now that they heard several nearby Spirit Islands produced them, they were no longer so worried.

They could not help secretly grumbling about Yuan Wu, feeling that this fellow was rather unkind for only revealing half the story at a time and repeatedly startling them.

"Sir Yuan Wu, may I ask why the Dragon Clan wants us to come here and build such a palace?"

Someone suddenly asked.

This was also the question in everyone's heart. They had coexisted with the Dragon Clan in this Small World for countless years, yet there had never been any precedent for something like this. Although members of the Dragon Clan had occasionally visited Half-Dragon City in the past, they mostly went there to find Dragon Descendants to cultivate Dragon Blood Flowers.

It was naturally strange that they had suddenly summoned people to construct a palace. Since when had the Dragon Clan become so particular about such things? Moreover, they were paying such an enormous price.

Judging solely by the palace's size and the materials required, it was definitely not intended for an ordinary member of the Dragon Clan. It might even be a palace for one of the Dragon Clan's Great Elders.

This speculation inevitably made everyone uneasy. They knew that if they botched this task, none of the five hundred people here would survive.

"This Lord would like to know that too." Yuan Wu snapped irritably. His day had been utterly miserable. First, he had been beaten and humiliated, then he had been saddled with such a miserable assignment. A bellyful of rage had been building inside him, and now that he had found an excuse, he vented it all at once.

The person who had asked was actually a human cultivator at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, but after being snapped at by Yuan Wu, he merely laughed awkwardly and did not dare say anything more.

There was no helping it. In this Small World, even though his cultivation was higher than Yuan Wu's, he still did not dare make an enemy of him.

Humans and Yao aside, all the Dragon Descendants took their cues from Yuan Wu. Now that he was wielding the Dragon Clan’s authority as his warrant, provoking him would bring no benefit at all.

Yuan Wu's gloomy gaze swept around him. Taking a deep breath, he said, "The situation is grave, so this Lord will not say much more. All of you are likely well aware that if the five hundred of us are to complete such a palace within a month, we must give it everything we have, even... risk our lives."

His words were no exaggeration.

If it were merely a palace covering a hundred mu, it would not pose any difficulty for the five hundred people present. Emperor Realm Masters were everywhere among them, numbering more than a hundred. How difficult could constructing a palace be?

The key was that the Dragon Clan had specified the materials.

These were all precious Imperial-Grade materials, each incomparably sturdy. Mining them and shaping them to the required specifications would consume a great deal of manpower and resources.

A single palm-sized piece of Qing Gold Purple Copper alone would likely take more than half a day to melt.

Everyone recognized the difficulty of this task and fell silent, their expressions solemn.

Only Yang Kai remained calm and composed, glancing around without taking the matter to heart at all.

He had come to Dragon Isle to find Zhu Qing, not to serve as manual labor. As long as he could find an opportunity to meet Zhu Qing, he could not be bothered with anything else.

A gaze suddenly fell on him from the side. Yang Kai looked over and saw the sleazy-looking man from before smiling at him.

When their eyes met, the man nodded lightly.

Yang Kai had a good impression of him. When Yang Kai had clashed with Yuan Wu earlier, this man had voluntarily stepped forward to smooth things over, showing that he was a chivalrous and righteous person. Regardless of whether Yang Kai had needed his help, he still had to acknowledge the favor.

Thus, when the man greeted him, Yang Kai smiled back, returning the gesture of goodwill.

Not far away, Yuan Wu inadvertently noticed Yang Kai's little exchange and immediately flew into a rage.

Here he was, shrouded in gloom and worrying himself sick over what would happen a month from now, yet Yang Kai looked utterly carefree and at ease. On what grounds?

On what grounds?!

## Chapter 2722 — Arrogant Before, Submissive After

Yang Kai had just told Zhu Qing that this young man was a disciple of Medicine Pill Valley, under the tutelage of Great Emperor Miaodan.

Now that she had slapped him, Zhu Qing knew she would surely bring trouble to Lingxiao Palace. If things went badly, Yang Kai might even resent her for it, and then her mission would be impossible to complete...

Zhu Qing was anxious, but she simply couldn’t hold back in that situation.

“I nearly got killed by this wretch’s slap, can’t you see?” the boy screamed hysterically, his face full of grief as if he’d suffered a great injustice.

“I really didn’t see it,” Yang Kai replied, looking at him in surprise and beckoning, “Come here, let me check where she hit you.”

The boy jumped three feet high, rushed to Yang Kai, and shoved his face forward. “My face! She actually hit my face! Look at what she’s done. If you don’t give me a satisfactory explanation, I won’t let this go!”

Yang Kai examined the injury on his face, nodded slightly, then suddenly raised his hand and slapped the other, uninjured side of the boy’s face.

Smack...

With a crisp sound, the boy spun through the air, tumbling over a dozen times before crashing heavily to the ground. In midair, a mouthful of teeth flew out, most of them knocked loose.

After landing hard, stars danced before his eyes. He lay there dazed, unable to recover for quite a while.

The girls were all shocked, their eyes wide open.

“Palace Master, you…” Hua Qingsi was utterly terrified. If Zhu Qing’s slap was the boy’s own fault, then Yang Kai, as the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace, slapping him made the situation completely unmanageable.

Yang Kai now represented Lingxiao Palace, and every action carried great weight. This slap could mean a complete break with Medicine Pill Valley.

Just thinking about it was frightening. As the Chief Steward of Lingxiao Palace, Hua Qingsi suddenly felt exhausted! But soon she relaxed, seeing the boy called Li Xuan sent flying, feeling immense satisfaction.

This brat was just too annoying.

“Next time you run into something like this, hit harder!” Yang Kai glanced at Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing nodded dumbly.

As a Dragon girl, her overwhelming strength was nearly unstoppable. Yang Kai had almost suffered greatly at her hands before. If she hadn’t held back, that slap could have killed Li Xuan a dozen times. Only because of Yang Kai’s earlier instructions did she restrain herself, merely giving him a light punishment. Otherwise, Li Xuan wouldn’t have survived.

“You… you dare…” Li Xuan finally came to his senses, trembling as he pointed at Yang Kai, his words slurred by the wind whistling through his mouth, “You dare hit me!”

“So what if I hit you!” Yang Kai made a move as if to strike again. Li Xuan immediately scrambled on the ground, clutching his head and crying out in fear.

After a long while with nothing happening, Li Xuan peeked at Yang Kai, realizing he’d been tricked. His face flushed red with anger. “I’m a Medicine Pill Valley disciple! How dare you hit me? You’re all dead!”

“You remember the beating but not the pain, huh!” Yang Kai’s expression turned fierce as he walked over, grabbed Li Xuan, and lifted him up.

Li Xuan struggled desperately, but with his strength, he couldn’t escape Yang Kai’s grip. He shouted in terror, “What are you doing!”

“Teaching you a lesson!” Yang Kai snorted coldly and slapped him again.

Smack, smack, smack, smack...

The continuous sound echoed. Even without using Emperor Yuan, the sheer force made Li Xuan wish he were dead. After several slaps, he was crying and wailing, completely losing his dignity.

“Young as you are, you’ve already learned such bad habits.” Yang Kai shot him a sidelong glance, raising his palm before Li Xuan’s face, his expression fierce and intimidating. “Well? Can you speak properly now?”

“Yes… yes, yes…” Li Xuan nodded repeatedly, feeling his cheeks go numb, and saw in the corner of his eye that his face was swelling up.

“Go apologize!” Yang Kai tossed Li Xuan aside, landing him right at Zhu Qing’s feet.

Zhu Qing looked at Yang Kai in surprise, her heartstrings inexplicably stirred.

Was he venting for her? With Yang Kai’s current status, it would be best to minimize trouble and keep things peaceful, but he didn’t hesitate to beat up the boy and even made him apologize.

Didn’t he dislike her and avoid her all the time? What was he doing?

For a moment, Zhu Qing’s heart was in turmoil.

Li Xuan staggered to his feet, stood obediently before Zhu Qing, bowed deeply, and said sincerely, “Sister, I’m sorry. Junior Brother was rude just now. Please be generous and don’t hold it against me. I’m just a kid and don’t know any better. Please don’t be angry.”

After being beaten, Li Xuan seemed like a completely different person, suddenly well-behaved and sensible.

Zhu Qing frowned and said, “It’s nothing.”

It really was nothing. The boy had tried to touch her, but she dodged every time and hadn’t suffered at all. Instead, he was beaten by Yang Kai until he was barely recognizable.

“Thank you, Sister, for your generosity!” Li Xuan said gratefully, then turned to Yang Kai with a smile. “Eldest Brother, Junior Brother has already apologized, and Sister has forgiven me. So…”

Yang Kai squinted and said, “Hmm, not bad. You’re teachable.”

Li Xuan grinned, “Eldest Brother flatters me. If there’s nothing else, Junior Brother will take his leave!”

“You’re not taking the Spiritual Medicine?” Yang Kai looked at him, his expression meaningful.

Li Xuan slapped his forehead and laughed awkwardly, “Haha, almost forgot. This is the task my master gave me!”

Yang Kai smiled and tossed him the prepared Spatial Ring. “All the Spiritual Medicine on the list is inside. Check it yourself.”

“No need, no need. I trust Eldest Brother’s character.” Li Xuan said, quickly putting away the Spatial Ring, then took out another one and handed it to Yang Kai. “This is the Source Crystals for buying the Spiritual Medicine. Eldest Brother, please check it!”

Yang Kai responded, took the Spatial Ring, swept it with his Divine Sense, then looked at Li Xuan and said, “This amount is a bit short. Are you sure your master only gave you this many Source Crystals?”

The Spiritual Medicines listed on that prescription were numerous and of no low grade. By Yang Kai’s estimation, they would cost at least twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals!

But the Spatial Ring only had ten million, and all were Lower-Level Source Crystal!

That was a huge difference.

Li Xuan was stunned, then laughed awkwardly, “Wrong ring, wrong ring. This is the one my master gave me.”

As he spoke, he quickly swapped the ring for Yang Kai.

This time, the Source Crystal amount was correct, even a bit more—about twenty-five million Upper-Level Source Crystal. It seemed Ji Yao was right: Medicine Pill Valley had the Northern Territory’s major sects collect Spiritual Medicine, but they wouldn’t let them suffer losses. All the herbs would be bought at fair prices, sometimes even a bit extra.

After all, this concerned the reputation of Medicine Pill Valley and Great Emperor Miaodan. The disciples wouldn’t dare mess around.

But Yang Kai knew very well that if the Wenqing Sect still existed, this boy Li Xuan would only need to pay the amount in that first ring to buy Spiritual Medicine from them.

Li Xuan clearly wanted to take advantage. Twenty-five million Upper-Level Source Crystal was no small sum.

That’s why he prepared two sets of Source Crystals, two rings.

But after Yang Kai’s harsh lesson, he didn’t dare try anything here.

With the transaction complete, Li Xuan immediately said his farewells. Yang Kai naturally didn’t try to stop him.

The boy summoned a Multi-deck Ship, soared into the air, and looked quite dashing. If not for his swollen face, he’d be handsome enough.

“Goodbye, Eldest Brothers and Sisters!” Li Xuan stood on the deck, waving enthusiastically.

“Safe travels!” Yang Kai replied with a smile.

Li Xuan piloted the Multi-deck Ship away quickly. As he turned, his smile vanished, replaced by a cold expression.

…

“What are you doing?” Ji Yao suddenly stepped in front of Yang Kai, blocking his path. Her cold gaze pierced his heart, seeing straight into his soul.

The sudden move stunned Hua Qingsi and Zhu Qing. They had no idea what Ji Yao meant.

“Just bored, going out for a walk!” Yang Kai looked up at the sky, avoiding Ji Yao’s eyes.

“I’ll go with you,” Ji Yao said.

“I’m just wandering around. Why do you want to come?” Yang Kai glared at her.

“I want to walk too.”

Yang Kai gritted his teeth, “I miss women. I’m going to a brothel for some fun. Are you coming too?”

“Miss women?” Ji Yao sneered, “Fine, come back to Ice Heart Valley with me. If you call out, a hundred disciples will swarm and drown you!”

Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead, “Isn’t that a bit much? Aren’t Ice Heart Valley disciples all about purity and restraint?”

“Men and women both have desires. There's nothing to hide. I can’t speak for others, but I have a few Junior Nieces who admire you. If you want, I’ll arrange it. They’ll be happy to oblige.”

This Senior Aunt really was thoughtful and thorough…

Yang Kai couldn’t help swallowing hard, nearly blurting out his agreement on the spot. But suddenly, he felt two pairs of burning eyes fixed on him from the side, and the words stuck in his throat. He snapped irritably, “What’s so great about proper young ladies? This Young Master prefers the fallen flowers and withered willows of the brothels—only with them is there any real fun to be had.”

“You’ve gone too far!” Ji Yao snapped, her anger making her tremble. Even though Yang Kai was only talking nonsense and hadn’t actually done anything, she still found it hard to accept. And from the way he spoke, it sounded as if he was a regular at brothels…

“You’re the one who’s gone too far! What are you making such a fuss for? I already have one little tagalong—now you want to join in too? How am I supposed to live like this?” Yang Kai shot her an exasperated glare.

Zhu Qing frowned, displeased, “Who are you talking about? Make yourself clear.”

“I mean you! Any objections?” Yang Kai looked at her.

Zhu Qing turned away, muttering softly, “You think I want to?”

“Don’t try to muddy the waters!” Ji Yao sneered, “You’re not really going to a brothel. You want to kill and silence someone, right?”

“Kill and silence?” Hua Qingsi was shocked, “Who’s being killed? Who’s being silenced?”

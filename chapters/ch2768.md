## Chapter 2768 — Xue Zhengmao

It was only after a full half an hour that Wen Zishan finally finished reading aloud. Retracting his hand, he looked at Yang Kai and said, “Did you remember what this Lord just said?”

Yang Kai replied solemnly, “I remember.”

“Very good.” Wen Zishan nodded in satisfaction, then gestured behind him. Chen Qian, who stood where Wen Zishan indicated, immediately stepped forward holding an object and handed it to Wen Zishan.

“Take this Elder Jade Token. From this day forth, Yang Kai, you are an Elder of my Qingyang Shrine. You will advance and retreat with the Shrine, sharing its fate!”

“I respectfully obey the Hall Master’s command!” Yang Kai raised both hands high, receiving the Elder Jade Token, then promptly returned the previous Qingyang Gold Token to Wen Zishan.

From this moment on, Yang Kai was an Elder of Qingyang Shrine. The jade token was not large and quite light, but as Yang Kai held it in his palm, it felt incomparably heavy—the weight of the care and protection bestowed upon him by the seniors of Qingyang Shrine.

“Offer sacrifice to the heavens!” Wen Zishan waved his hand grandly and shouted in a deep voice.

A thunderous beastly roar split the sky. Following the sound, all eyes turned to see Qiu Ran soaring through the air, a prison cage suspended in his grasp. Within the cage was a fearsome Yao Beast—a great ape, its back gleaming with dazzling golden light, as if cast from molten gold and iron. Even confined and suppressed by powerful restrictions, the beast radiated a wild, murderous aura. Again and again, it hurled its massive body against the bars, roaring in fury, but no matter how it struggled, it could not break free.

“Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape!” someone exclaimed in shock.

On the plaza, many Emperor Realm cultivators’ expressions changed.

The offering to the heavens was the final ceremony in the conferral of an Elder. It was also a bridge for the newly appointed Elder to display a few of their divine abilities before the crowd, allowing everyone to witness their strength.

Generally, the Yao Beast chosen for this segment was neither too strong nor too weak. If it was too strong and the Elder failed, it would be a laughingstock; if too weak, it would not showcase the Elder’s abilities.

Yet this time, Qingyang Shrine had chosen the infamous Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape as the Yao Beast, which was truly unexpected.

Although the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape was a Twelfth-Tier Lower-Level Yao Beast, equivalent to a First Stage of the Emperor Realm, if one truly encountered such a beast, most First Stage Emperor Realm cultivators would only be able to flee. Its defense was astonishing, its recovery formidable. An ordinary First Stage Emperor Realm would have to risk their life to kill it, and might not even succeed.

No matter how one looked at it, Yang Kai was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Could he really complete this offering to the heavens against the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape?

Many people began to worry, fearing that if Yang Kai failed, Qingyang Shrine would become a laughingstock, and the situation would spiral out of control.

However, some secretly guessed that the Yao Beast’s strength must have been restricted to make it easier for Yang Kai to kill.

While the crowd was full of doubts, Wen Zishan nodded slightly to Qiu Ran. Qiu Ran formed several seals with his hands, instantly removing the cage imprisoning the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape. With a flash of light, even the restrictive force was dispelled.

“Roar…” An even more terrifying killing intent erupted. Freed at last, the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape was like a tiger returning to the mountains or a dragon diving into the sea. Its aura surged, its fists pounded its chest with a booming sound, and its blood-red eyes swept the surroundings.

Many people’s faces changed in shock, their hearts filled with dread.

Now that the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape was completely unrestrained and enraged, it would likely perform beyond its limits. At this point, a First Stage Emperor Realm was absolutely no match; only a Second Stage Emperor Realm could suppress it.

What was Qingyang Shrine thinking? Wen Zishan was always shrewd—how could he be so muddle-headed now? If something went wrong during the offering, wouldn’t they become a laughingstock to the world?

In a flash of sword light, Yang Kai had already risen into the air.

Previously, on Purple Bamboo Peak, Gao Xueting had explained every step to him in detail. He knew exactly what to do in this offering segment. A mere Twelfth-Tier Lower-Level Yao Beast was nothing to him.

The Myriad Sword was summoned, pointing at the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape from afar, locking killing intent onto the beast.

Sensing the killing intent, the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape immediately turned to look at Yang Kai. Its Yao Qi surged, and with a furious roar, it charged at Yang Kai, intent on tearing him to pieces.

Just then, a sudden change occurred—a streak of light shot over from the distance like lightning, piercing through the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape’s body and landing directly in front of Yang Kai.

“Who goes there?” Wen Zishan shouted, wanting to intervene, but it was already too late.

With a crisp clang, the Myriad Sword in Yang Kai’s hand flashed with light. His figure was blasted backward, retreating thirty zhang before he could steady himself. His face paled, and he spat out a mouthful of fresh blood.

Only then did Wen Zishan move to block the streak of light. Gao Xueting also instantly appeared at Yang Kai’s side, shielding him, her beautiful eyes warily fixed on the newcomer.

Bang…

With a muffled sound, the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape, which had been so fierce a moment ago, suddenly exploded into a cloud of blood mist, raining down in midair, leaving no corpse behind.

Everyone was stunned, rising to their feet. No one had expected someone to interfere at the final offering ceremony and kill the sacrificial beast in an instant, even injuring the star of today’s grand event.

This was a public slap in the face to Qingyang Shrine—how could that be tolerated?

Yet to be able to obliterate a Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast like the Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape in one blow was a display of power that shocked everyone present. Among so many Emperor Realm experts, there were definitely no more than three who could do such a thing.

Who could be so brazen?

Looking closely, everyone was momentarily stunned, then revealed looks of realization, faintly sensing that today’s conferral ceremony would not end peacefully.

“Elder Xue!” Wen Zishan’s eyes narrowed as he fixed his gaze on the newcomer, his tone low and his expression turning grim.

The newcomer was actually Xue Zhengmao, an Elder of Star God Palace. This was unexpected—after all, Xiao Yuyang was already here. With both being Elders of Star God Palace, one should have sufficed. Why had Xue Zhengmao come as well? And to cause trouble at such an occasion, giving Qingyang Shrine no face at all.

As he spoke, Wen Zishan glanced at Xiao Yuyang, only to see Xiao Yuyang frowning slightly, seemingly unaware of what was happening.

“Hall Master Wen!” Xue Zhengmao replied indifferently, his gaze passing over Wen Zishan to land on Yang Kai. He said, “So you are Yang Kai? To take a blow from this old man and remain unscathed is impressive. However… you’ll have to come with this old man now.”

As he spoke, he reached out directly to grab Yang Kai.

Wen Zishan’s expression turned angry. He raised his hand and struck at Xue Zhengmao with a palm.

“Impudent!” Xue Zhengmao shouted sternly, changing his claw to a palm and clashing with Wen Zishan. The shockwave swept out, space collapsed, and both retreated three steps, evenly matched.

“Elder Xue, this Lord respects you as a guest, but you come here and try to seize my Shrine’s Elder the moment you arrive. Isn’t that unreasonable?” Wen Zishan snorted coldly.

Xue Zhengmao said, “Your Shrine’s Elder? Wen Zishan, you’re getting more foolish with age. Do you not know what trouble this brat has caused? Yet you still dare to shelter him. Are you trying to jeopardize Qingyang Shrine’s foundation?”

“I don’t know what trouble Elder Yang has caused to make Elder Xue so angry. This Lord would like to hear the details.” Wen Zishan refused to back down.

“I won’t argue with you. Today, I must take Yang Kai away.” Xue Zhengmao snorted coldly, his attitude unyielding. He turned to Xiao Yuyang and said, “Elder Xiao, what do you say?”

At these words, Wen Zishan’s expression changed slightly.

Though he was a Third Stage Emperor, he could only handle one opponent of the same level. If Xiao Yuyang shared Xue Zhengmao’s intentions, Yang Kai would be doomed today. Even if all the Emperor Realm experts of Qingyang Shrine acted together, they would not be able to keep him.

Xiao Yuyang frowned and said, “Five days ago, I already reported this matter to the Great Emperor. We are awaiting the Great Emperor’s decision!”

Wen Zishan immediately breathed a sigh of relief. Xiao Yuyang not interfering was good for him. With only Xue Zhengmao to deal with, he was not afraid.

“Hmph!” Xue Zhengmao, dissatisfied with the answer, seemed displeased with Xiao Yuyang as well. But since both were Elders of Star God Palace, neither was superior. Though annoyed, he could not say more. He turned to Wen Zishan and said, “Hall Master Wen, the conferral ceremony isn’t finished yet. Yang Kai hasn’t officially become your Shrine’s Elder. Hand him over to me—there’s only benefit for your Shrine, no harm.”

Wen Zishan shook his head. “The ceremony may not be complete, but Yang Kai has already received the Elder Jade Token. Who dares say he isn’t a Shrine Elder?”

“You’re so stubborn. Must you wait until the Great Emperor is angered before you yield?” Xue Zhengmao shouted angrily.

Wen Zishan chuckled lightly. “The Great Emperor is wise and mighty. How could he be angered by such a matter? Perhaps he’ll even reward Elder Yang for cleansing the ranks.”

“You’re being insolent!” Xue Zhengmao’s face darkened.

Wen Zishan said coldly, “If you want to take Elder Yang, you’ll have to get past this Lord first.”

“Do you think I really won’t act against you?” Xue Zhengmao growled through gritted teeth.

“Didn’t you already try just now?” Wen Zishan retorted.

These two Third Stage Emperors went back and forth, neither yielding an inch. Their anger grew, and it seemed they might come to blows at any moment, leaving the guests on edge and drenched in sweat.

If these two fought, it would certainly be lively, but who knew how many bystanders would be caught up in the chaos?

No one wanted to see such a thing happen.

“Please, both of you, calm down. You are both pillars of the Southern Territory and see each other often. Why be like this?” Feng Ming of Wuhua Hall stepped forward to mediate.

“Indeed, please don’t get angry. Sit down and have some tea first.” Zeng Yuan, Vice President of Qiyao Commerce Guild, also spoke up.

Chen Wenhao of Tianwu Sacred Land said in a deep voice, “Didn’t Elder Xiao say the matter has already been reported to the Great Emperor? Five days have passed; surely the Great Emperor’s decision will come soon. Why rush, Elder Xue?”

Xue Zhengmao frowned, pondered for a moment, then said, “Fine. This Lord will wait here for the Great Emperor’s decree. I want to see how you, Wen Zishan, plan to protect that brat when the time comes.”

As he spoke, he glared at Xiao Yuyang. Today’s events had left him furious. He thought that with his intervention, Yang Kai would surely surrender, but Wen Zishan had refused to give him any face. More importantly, he had no idea Xiao Yuyang had already reported the matter to the Great Emperor. If he’d known, he wouldn’t have made such a scene. In any case, the Great Emperor’s decree would arrive soon. With the Great Emperor’s order, how could Wen Zishan dare defy it?

## Chapter 2761 — The Frog in the Well

Ziyuan Commerce Guild was one of the two top Commerce Guilds in the Southern Territory, conducting business with many Sects in the region. If one were to offend them, it would bring no benefit to their own Sect.

“This old man bids twenty-one million. Boy, if you have the guts, follow me.” The old man in the private room snorted coldly, clearly convinced of Yang Kai’s identity as a shill.

“Thirty million!”

“Pfft!” The old man nearly spat out a mouthful of blood, his face turning extremely ugly.

He had thought Yang Kai was just someone arranged by the auction house to drive up the price. If that were the case, then adding a million would basically fulfill the task. Though he was gnashing his teeth in hatred, if he could secure it for twenty-one million, it would still be a decent gain.

He never expected that the boy in Room Three would raise the bid straight to thirty million in one breath.

Not a shill! Definitely not a shill. There couldn’t possibly be such an arrogant or irrational shill in this world. Such a crazy increase would likely scare off most bidders.

In other words, this boy truly wanted to bid for the Feihong Black Dragon Armor.

Not only did the old man notice this, but most of the Emperor Realm cultivators present saw it as well.

Room Three was definitely occupied by someone with deep pockets.

“Thirty-one million!” Ten breaths later, someone finally managed to offer a new bid.

“Thirty-five million!” Yang Kai immediately followed up.

A sigh echoed from one of the private rooms. Soon, more sighs followed, but no one raised the bid again.

An ordinary Imperial Treasure could usually fetch fifteen to twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals. If it was a defensive Imperial Treasure, the price would be even higher, reaching twenty to thirty million. The Feihong Black Dragon Armor, though an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level defensive Secret Treasure, had some flaws, so its value lay between an Imperial-Grade Lower-Level and Imperial-Grade Middle-Level defensive treasure.

Thirty-five million was about the limit.

Besides, such a huge sum of Source Crystals wasn’t something just anyone could produce.

At the auction in Flowing Shadow City a few months ago, most Emperor Realm cultivators present had assets of only ten to twenty million, and rarely more than thirty million Upper-Level Source Crystals.

So Yang Kai could almost be certain that thirty-five million was enough to seal the deal.

And indeed, that was the case.

The beautiful female auctioneer observed for a moment, saw that no one would bid further, and immediately announced loudly, “Feihong Black Dragon Armor, thirty-five million Upper-Level Source Crystals! Is there any higher bid? If not, this armor belongs to the friend in Room Three.”

She counted three times, then smiled at Yang Kai’s direction and said, “Congratulations to the friend in Room Three.”

A strange feeling arose in her heart. This finale treasure should have been fiercely contested by the Emperor Realm cultivators, with many rounds of bidding before it was settled. Yet, the person in Room Three had cut through the chaos and won it in just a few moves.

Most of those who intended to participate didn’t even get a chance to bid, only watching the excitement.

Having hosted so many auctions, this was the first time she’d encountered such a situation. The person in Room Three was truly a major client. If there was a chance, it might be worth getting to know him. Perhaps there could be future cooperation.

With the final armor settled, the auction was officially over. The martial artists in the hall rose and headed out, still feeling a bit dazed. The Emperor Realm cultivators in the private rooms were led out by maids, exiting through secret passages.

In Room Three, Yang Kai waited quietly for a moment. A knock sounded at the door.

Yang Kai responded, and the door was immediately pushed open. A maid entered with two strong men, carrying the iron chest. Soon, the transaction was completed, and someone else led Yang Kai toward the secret passage.

This was a method the auction house used to protect its clients, preventing them from being targeted by enemies after winning valuable items. The Ziyuan Commerce Guild auction house had hundreds of secret passages leading in all directions outside Fenglin City. Without prior knowledge, it was impossible to track someone.

Yang Kai wasn’t afraid of trouble, but he didn’t refuse the arrangement either.

Soon, guided by the maid, he entered an underground corridor.

In Room Nine, Gu Hong knocked and entered.

Le Dongzheng, who had been resting with his eyes closed, suddenly opened them, a sharp gleam flashing across his gaze. His lips moved slightly as he sent a message with his Divine Sense.

Gu Hong couldn’t hide his extreme excitement and replied quickly.

Le Dongzheng stood up, flung his wide sleeves, and left gracefully.

Gu Hong thought for a moment, then furtively followed. He knew Le Dongzheng’s temperament well. He hadn’t expected Yang Kai to be so wealthy. If Le Dongzheng succeeded, he doubted the man would honor his promise to share half the profits.

So it was better to keep an eye on things himself. If Le Dongzheng succeeded, he could catch him in the act, and the man wouldn’t be able to deny it.

This was a matter involving several tens of millions of Upper-Level Source Crystals. Gu Hong couldn’t afford not to care. If he pulled off this deal, he wouldn’t have to compete with Kang Siran anymore and could live carefree anywhere.

About a quarter of an hour later, Yang Kai and Qin Yu emerged from the belly of a small mountain fifty li from Fenglin City.

In the sky the moon was bright and the stars sparse.

After checking the direction, Yang Kai urged his Emperor Yuan, wrapped Qin Yu, and sped off.

In no time, the two arrived above a hill. As they looked down, they saw many people gathered below. Sensing someone approaching, those people looked up, and seeing a streak of light shooting over, they all became alert.

“Grandfather!” Qin Yu called out loudly.

Qin Chaoyang heard her voice, laughed, and said, “No need to worry, it’s Brother Yang and Yu’er.”

The streak of light landed before the crowd, revealing Yang Kai and Qin Yu.

Yang Kai glanced around and said, “Everyone’s here, right?”

Before going to the auction, he had sent a message to the Zhang Family, asking them to meet the Qin Family here. Now, it seemed no one was missing.

The old man from the Zhang Family said, “All the Zhang Family members are here.”

The Zhang Family didn’t have many people, and preparations had been made over the past few days. With Yang Kai’s message, they could set out immediately. The Qin Family was the same; even the land deed Qin Chaoyang had managed to sell, gathering everyone together.

The two small families combined had about one hundred fifty people, with varying strength. The strongest was Qin Yu, at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. The others ranged from the Void King Realm, Origin Returning Realm, Saint King Realm, Saint Realm, to Mortal Realms.

“Brother-in-law, are we really going to the Northern Territory?” Zhang Ruoyu jumped out, her face both nervous and excited.

She had never traveled far before, and now she was going to the distant Northern Territory. As a native of the Southern Territory, she was very unfamiliar with the North.

“Brother-in-law…” Qin Yu was stunned, suddenly feeling flustered and dejected.

“Who’s your brother-in-law, little girl? Don’t call me that!” Yang Kai’s face darkened.

Ning Suwan also scolded, “Ruoyu, step back.”

“Oh.” Zhang Ruoyu pouted, stood aside, and looked unhappy. Glancing up, she met Qin Yu’s eyes, flashed a small tiger tooth smile, and said, “Sister Qin.”

Qin Yu forced a smile, “Sister Ruoyu.”

“Everyone’s here, let’s go.” Yang Kai called out. If he were alone, he could reach the Thousand Leaves Sect in two days, but with such a large group of varying strength, it would take at least half a month.

As soon as he finished speaking, Yang Kai suddenly frowned, sensing something, and looked back. He saw a streak of light speeding over from the horizon, seemingly heading straight for them.

Qin Chaoyang and Ning Suwan noticed it too, immediately becoming nervous, especially Qin Chaoyang. Just two days ago, Yang Kai had offended disciples of the Star God Palace at the Qin Family. If a strong expert from Star God Palace came, the Qin Family would be doomed.

“You all go ahead, I’ll follow soon.” Yang Kai swept out his Divine Sense and recognized who it was. After instructing the Qin and Zhang families, he turned to wait.

“Which way?” Qin Chaoyang asked.

Yang Kai pointed in a direction.

“Brother Yang, be careful.” Qin Chaoyang reminded, nodded to Ning Suwan, and the two immediately led their families forward.

Staying behind now would only drag Yang Kai down, so they made a quick decision.

In the blink of an eye, over a hundred people gradually moved away.

Moments later, the streak of light crashed down, its glow fading to reveal a middle-aged old man.

“Sect Master Le, what’s with the rush?” Yang Kai smiled, looking at the pursuing Le Dongzheng.

Le Dongzheng’s eyes flashed coldly, and he snorted, “You know perfectly well.”

After glancing at the two families moving away, Le Dongzheng asked, “Are those all your people?”

“That’s right!” Yang Kai nodded.

Le Dongzheng grinned fiercely, “Good. None of them will escape. This old man will slaughter them all!”

Yang Kai smiled calmly. “That depends on whether you live long enough to do it.”

Le Dongzheng squinted, “Boy, you’ve got quite the mouth. Do you think you can beat this old man?”

Even though Yang Kai’s cultivation was similar to his, Le Dongzheng didn’t take him seriously. Both were at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, but he had spent a hundred years in this realm. How could he be compared to ordinary riffraff?

“Frog in a well—your sky is only so wide!” Yang Kai sneered.

“Insolence!” Le Dongzheng was furious.

As soon as he finished speaking, Yang Kai, who should have been dozens of zhang away, suddenly appeared right in front of him, their faces almost touching, separated by only three inches!

Le Dongzheng gasped, his whole body’s hair standing on end in shock.

He hadn’t seen Yang Kai make any move, yet the man was suddenly right before his eyes.

The pair of eyes so close brimmed with disdain and mockery, making Le Dongzheng’s heart sink as if falling into the depths of the abyss.

Instinctively, he activated his Emperor Yuan and shifted his steps to get away from Yang Kai, but to his horror, no matter how he dodged, Yang Kai stuck to him like a shadow.

A palm landed on Le Dongzheng’s chest. Yang Kai’s mocking gaze turned cold and sharp: “Die!”

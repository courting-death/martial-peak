## Chapter 2908 — A Difficult Road Ahead

In the wilderness, as soon as Yang Kai greeted them, four unfamiliar gazes immediately converged on him, each carrying a scrutinizing intent.

Among the four, there were three men and one woman—two at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and two at the Second Stage. They stood in pairs, clearly divided. Before Yang Kai arrived, only Hua Yulu was alone.

Although the Secret Treasure could conceal the pallor on Yang Kai’s face, it could not hide the weakness in his aura no matter what. Moreover, everyone present was at the Emperor Realm, so with just a brief probe, they could discern the depth of his cultivation.

First Stage of the Emperor Realm! This made all four of them inexplicably breathe a sigh of relief.

One of the short, stocky men snorted coldly, his expression displeased as he said, “Palace Master Hua, is this your friend? He sure puts on airs, making us wait so long.”

He made no effort to hide his true thoughts, clearly annoyed after waiting here too long, and reproached Yang Kai directly. Besides, Yang Kai had indeed failed to arrive at the agreed time, having been delayed for several days on the road. If he were at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, perhaps the man would have held his tongue, but since Yang Kai was only at the First Stage, he felt no need to restrain himself.

Facing the man’s reproach, Hua Yulu forced a smile and said, “Brother Wu, please calm your anger. My Senior Brother Yang had some matters to attend to a few days ago, so he was delayed on the road. I offer you my apologies here.”

She did not know exactly what had delayed Yang Kai for several days, causing him to miss the appointed time, but she believed Yang Kai was not someone who would break a promise on purpose. Otherwise, Xia Sheng would not have asked him for help. As the inviter, Hua Yulu naturally had to speak up for Yang Kai.

The man snorted coldly, "You are a woman of the Hundred Flowers Palace—where did this 'Senior Brother Yang' come from? I've never heard of any man being part of the Hundred Flowers Palace. Besides, this Brother Yang hides his head and tail, lacking even the courage to reveal his true face. Or does this Brother Yang harbor some ulterior motive?"

He clearly did not like Yang Kai. Upon noticing Yang Kai wore a Secret Treasure for disguise, he immediately became confrontational.

By rights, this was only a temporary treasure-hunting party, so there was no need to delve into each other’s backgrounds or origins. Everyone harbored their own motives—if they could cooperate during the search, they would; if not, they would simply part ways. Should any treasure truly be found, it was even possible they would turn on each other and fight. Everyone understood this perfectly well. Hua Yulu had never asked who the helpers the other two had brought were or where they came from, and they all tacitly maintained this understanding.

But at this moment, the short, stocky man was clearly targeting Yang Kai on purpose, and his last words were almost an accusation.

The Star Realm was vast, home to billions upon billions of cultivators, each with their own temperament and disposition. It was hardly unusual for a martial artist to traverse the Star Realm while wearing a Secret Treasure for disguise. Yet this man surnamed Wu seemed intent on magnifying the issue, as if he would not rest until Yang Kai removed the Secret Treasure and revealed his true face.

Hua Yulu frowned, clearly a bit displeased as well. She was the Palace Master of the Hundred Flowers Palace, and had already shown sincerity by lowering herself to apologize. The other party’s endless nagging naturally annoyed her. But the current situation was not in her favor—she and Yang Kai were both at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, while each of the other two pairs included one cultivator at the First Stage and one at the Second Stage. If a conflict broke out, it would be hard to resolve.

She had been fortunate enough to witness Yang Kai’s abilities at the Qingyang Shrine’s conferment ceremony, where he had fought an expert at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. To withstand such an attack showed that Yang Kai could not be judged by common sense. However, Hua Yulu sensed that Yang Kai’s aura was somewhat weak at this moment, and secretly guessed he might be injured, which was why he concealed his face.

She did not wish to start a conflict with the other side.

Just as she was thinking of what to say, Yang Kai spoke up, “How should I address this friend?”

The man surnamed Wu snorted, “Wu Kuangyi!”

“So it’s Brother Wu!” Yang Kai nodded lightly and smiled. “There’s no special reason for the disguise, it’s just… my appearance is a bit unusual. If I showed my true face, I’m afraid I’d frighten… that young lady!”

As he spoke, Yang Kai glanced at the other woman present besides Hua Yulu.

This woman was not with Wu Kuangyi, but stood with an old man. She was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, clearly invited by the old man as his helper. Though not stunningly beautiful, her temperament was exceptionally refined, her figure graceful, and her pale yellow palace dress outlined her exquisite form perfectly.

Since Yang Kai’s arrival, this woman had silently observed him like the others. When the conversation suddenly turned to her, she was taken aback, then smiled and said, “I’m not so easily frightened.”

Her smile had a peculiar charm, as if it could influence others’ moods, yet bore no trace of enchantment techniques. It was entirely natural, clearly an innate gift. This made Yang Kai look at her a moment longer, while secretly growing wary.

“And how should I address you, young brother?” the woman asked.

“Yang Jiu!” Yang Kai replied.

He ranked ninth in the Yang Family, so using the name Yang Jiu, with “Jiu” signifying nine, wasn’t entirely arbitrary.

Seeing this woman chatting so easily with Yang Kai, Wu Kuangyi’s frown deepened. Just as he was about to speak again, the old man beside the woman stepped forward, smiling kindly, “Alright, alright, it’s only a matter of a few days. For cultivators at our level, a few days pass in the blink of an eye. Brother Wu, there’s no need to mind—just treat it as a bit of meditation and rest.”

His smile was so amiable, like a kindly old neighbor, that one could not help but feel goodwill toward him.

With the old man saying so, Wu Kuangyi could not press the issue further. After all, it was not a big deal, and continuing would only make him seem petty and lacking in grace. Besides, Hua Yulu had already apologized, so he took the opportunity to let it go, though he still kept a stern face, as if to show he was not pleased.

After waiting a while, Wu Kuangyi finally said, “Now that everyone’s here, let’s set out. We can discuss the details of our cooperation on the way.”

Hua Yulu and Yang Kai naturally had no objections and nodded in agreement. To be honest, it was only now that Hua Yulu felt truly relieved. She and Yang Kai were not particularly familiar with each other, and during these past few days without his arrival, she had worried he might break his word. Now that Yang Kai had finally appeared, she could set aside her concerns.

“Wait a moment!” the kindly old man raised his hand.

Everyone looked at him, not knowing what he meant.

The old man said, “There’s still one more person to come!”

“Someone else?” Wu Kuangyi’s face darkened, his suppressed anger flaring up again as he said coldly, “Yang Tai, what do you mean? Half a year ago, we agreed that each of us could only bring one helper, and they had to be below the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. You’ve already brought one—are you bringing a second?”

Even Hua Yulu’s face turned grim at this.

Compared to Yang Kai being a few days late, if this old man Yang Tai really did as Wu Kuangyi said, that would be a true breach of their agreement, something no one could tolerate.

Yang Kai glanced at the woman standing beside Yang Tai and saw that her expression remained unchanged, still smiling. Clearly, she already knew about this and was not surprised.

Yang Tai hurriedly said, “Brother Wu, calm down, calm down. I haven’t broken our agreement. I only brought Madam Shen as my helper. The one who’s coming doesn’t count as my helper.”

He wore an innocent expression, as if Wu Kuangyi had wronged him.

Wu Kuangyi said angrily, “Then please explain yourself, Brother Yang. If you can’t satisfy us, even if I agree, Palace Master Hua and Brother Yang Jiu may not.”

The existence of that ancient cave residence had been discovered by him, Yang Tai, and Hua Yulu together, making it their shared find. Wu Kuangyi and Hua Yulu had both kept their word and brought only one helper each, but Yang Tai wanted to wait for another person, which made Wu Kuangyi furious.

Yang Tai smiled bitterly, “When you see that person, Brother Wu, you’ll understand. Whether to bring him along or not, you and Palace Master Hua can decide. I won’t object.”

Wu Kuangyi sneered, “Sounds like Brother Yang thinks that person must come along? Or will he be of some great use to us? I’d like to know just who this person is.”

At this point, Yang Tai said no more, only smiling mysteriously, appearing extremely inscrutable.

Things had come to this, and though Wu Kuangyi was angry, he could not really start a conflict with Yang Tai, so he could only suppress his temper and wait.

Yang Tai sounded so confident that, truth be told, Wu Kuangyi was also quite curious about who they were waiting for.

Yang Kai and Hua Yulu exchanged a glance, both feeling that this treasure-seeking trip would be fraught with difficulties. They had only just gathered, and already there were all sorts of conflicts. Six people, three small groups—it was clear they could not cooperate wholeheartedly. If they did not end up fighting, that would already be fortunate.

But this was normal. Yang Kai had long been mentally prepared for it, while Hua Yulu showed a trace of worry on her face.

While waiting for the newcomer to arrive, Yang Kai and Hua Yulu quietly exchanged information, inquiring about the names and backgrounds of the others present.

He already knew Wu Kuangyi and Yang Tai, both at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Half a year ago, they had explored that ancient cave residence with Hua Yulu. Wu Kuangyi’s cultivation had soared there; although he was at the same stage as the others, he had been the weakest of the three at the time. Now, however, his aura faintly rivaled the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, clearly having reached the peak of the First Stage and becoming the strongest among them.

Wu Kuangyi's helper was a taciturn man. From the moment Yang Kai arrived until now, he had not heard this person speak a single word. He seemed to have been born with a cold expression, looking as though he despised life itself and wished to die on the spot. According to Wu Kuangyi's earlier introduction, this man was called Fang Zhuo.

The woman standing with Yang Tai was named Shen Bingru. Hua Yulu did not know the backgrounds of Fang Zhuo and Shen Bingru, but she was very clear about Wu Kuangyi and Yang Tai.

In their quiet exchange, Hua Yulu shared all this information with Yang Kai.

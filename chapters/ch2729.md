## Chapter 2729 — The Shadow Mirror

"What do you want to do!" Ji Ying’s face darkened as he looked at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai said, "Nothing much. Since Grandmaster Ji is willing to believe his disciple’s words, there’s no point in arguing. I can’t be bothered to waste my breath. I just want to show you something."

"What is it?" Ji Ying frowned, not knowing what Yang Kai was up to.

Yang Kai clapped his hands and looked outside the hall. "Come in!"

Everyone looked toward the entrance. A man strolled in, stood in the center of the hall, cupped his fists, and said, "Greetings, Palace Master!"

Yang Kai nodded lightly.

"You..."

"Grandmaster Nanmen! Why are you here?"

Mi Qi and Li Jiao’s eyes widened as they stared at the entering Nanmen Dajun, hardly able to believe their eyes.

As Sect Masters of top Sects in the Northern Territory, both had dealings with Nanmen Dajun before. They knew his attainments in the Dao of Formations were extremely high, and he was rather proud and aloof. Over the years, they had tried many times to recruit him into their own Sects, but never succeeded.

To suddenly see Nanmen Dajun appear here, and show such respect to Yang Kai, left Mi Qi and Li Jiao feeling as if it were all unreal.

Was this really the same Nanmen Dajun? Even he would bow his head to someone else?

"I am now the Chief Formation Master of Lingxiao Palace. Why shouldn’t I be here?" Nanmen Dajun glanced at the two of them.

"Chief Formation Master!"

"Grandmaster Nanmen, you... ah!" 

Li Jiao and Mi Qi were both shocked and disappointed. They had tried countless times to win over Nanmen Dajun, only to be refused every time. Yet Lingxiao Palace, which had only just been established, managed to recruit him so quickly. What method did this Yang Kai use?

Yang Kai pursed his lips and said, "Grandmaster Nanmen, take it out."

"Yes!" Nanmen Dajun responded, then suddenly took several formation flags from his Spatial Ring and began setting them up around the hall.

In no time at all, a formation was set up, but everyone was left confused, unable to understand what he was trying to show them at this moment.

Nanmen Dajun then took out an object from his Spatial Ring. It looked like a mirror, but strangely, its surface reflected nothing, making one’s scalp tingle just looking at it.

Holding the mirror-like object high, Nanmen Dajun said, "I obtained this in my early years. It’s called the Shadow Mirror, and it can record images. I don’t know how it was made. I’ve shown it to several artifact refiners, but none could identify the material. It’s usually of little use, so today I’ll just make a fool of myself."

"The Shadow Mirror!"

"It records images!"

Hearing this, everyone vaguely understood Nanmen Dajun’s intention.

Li Xuan’s face turned pale as he stared dumbly at the Shadow Mirror, apparently never having imagined such a wondrous thing existed in this world.

Ji Ying frowned, glancing thoughtfully at Li Xuan, whose gaze immediately dodged his. This only made Ji Ying’s expression grow uglier.

Nanmen Dajun tossed the Shadow Mirror into the air, waved a formation flag, and urged his Emperor Yuan. Instantly, mist swirled in the center of the hall, and a beam of light shot from the Shadow Mirror, casting images all around.

Suddenly, a fairly clear scene appeared in the center of the hall.

Everyone focused their attention and instantly recognized the scene as the mountain gate of Lingxiao Palace, for the plaque inscribed with the three characters "Lingxiao Sect" was right in the center of the image.

At this moment, there were four figures in the scene.

One of them was Li Xuan. Besides him, there were three women, two of whom had left earlier. The one called Qing’er was among them.

No sound came out. The Shadow Mirror seemed only able to record images.

As Nanmen Dajun continued to channel his Emperor Yuan, the scene kept changing, as if replaying past events, vivid and lifelike.

Before everyone’s eyes, Li Xuan sidled up to Zhu Qing, grinning foolishly and saying something. Zhu Qing ignored him with a cold face.

Several times, Li Xuan even tried to take liberties with Zhu Qing. Zhu Qing grew angry, but only dodged him.

After a while, seeing Li Xuan grow more and more brazen, Zhu Qing finally slapped him, sending him flying.

A moment later, Yang Kai suddenly appeared, asked a few questions, then gave Li Xuan a fierce slap, knocking him to the ground and sending his teeth flying.

……

The scene shifted, replaying everything that happened at the mountain gate that day, until Li Xuan slunk away in disgrace, at which point the image froze.

Nanmen Dajun put away the formation flags and the Shadow Mirror, cupped his fists to Yang Kai, and left the hall.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi exchanged strange looks as they gazed at Li Xuan, their expressions a mix of anger and amusement.

They had already experienced Zhu Qing’s strength—she was someone even they couldn’t defeat. For Li Xuan to try to take liberties with her, being slapped was a stroke of luck. If not for the protection of being a Medicine Pill Valley disciple, he might have died on the spot.

What was truly hateful was that this fellow had the nerve to spout lies, slandering Yang Kai for not only hitting him but also stealing his Source Crystals, which led the two of them to get involved. Now, it would be hard to extricate themselves.

Ji Ying’s eyes blazed as he turned to glare at Li Xuan, his face full of disappointment and pain.

"Master, I was wrong! Disciple truly knows his mistake. Please punish me!" Li Xuan dropped to his knees with a thud.

With the images recorded by the Shadow Mirror, he knew that no matter how he tried to defend himself, it was useless. Now, his only hope was to admit his mistake quickly and hope his master would remember their bond and not expel him.

"I never imagined you were this kind of person." Ji Ying was heartbroken and furious. In his anger, he raised his hand to slap Li Xuan. In the past, Li Xuan had always behaved obediently in front of him. He truly hadn’t expected his disciple to act like this, and was utterly disappointed.

"Master!" Li Xuan cried out in fright, crouching on the ground and covering his head with both hands.

But the slap never fell.

After a long while, Ji Ying sighed deeply, lowered his hand, and said calmly, "Go."

Li Xuan looked up, staring at Ji Ying in shock.

"From today on, you are no longer my disciple, nor a disciple of Medicine Pill Valley. If you dare act in the name of a Medicine Pill Valley disciple again, I will not spare you!" Ji Ying said with a flick of his sleeve.

"Master..." Only now did Li Xuan panic, realizing Ji Ying truly meant to expel him. Being a Medicine Pill Valley disciple brought many benefits and a bright future—how could he bear to leave? If he became an abandoned disciple, there would be nowhere for him in the vast Northern Territory.

"Say one more word, and I’ll take your life!" Ji Ying didn’t look at him, his expression resolute, clearly determined to drive him out of Medicine Pill Valley.

Such a petty, deceitful person who turned black into white—he hated himself for not seeing it sooner. If he stayed in Medicine Pill Valley, who knew when he might ruin its reputation? If he truly dragged his master’s name through the mud, not even death would atone for it!

Li Xuan had wanted to beg for mercy, but after hearing this, he dared not say another word.

A Senior Brother nearby shot him a look, and only then did Li Xuan, with tears in his eyes, get up and rush out of the hall.

The hall fell silent, with only the sound of Yang Kai sipping tea.

After a long while, Ji Ying finally recovered, giving a self-deprecating smile. "It seems Palace Master Yang was right. My judgment in accepting disciples really is lacking."

Yang Kai said blandly, "If not for Grandmaster Nanmen’s Shadow Mirror today, I would have had no way to clear my name."

If he and Li Xuan had to confront each other, it would have been nothing but a war of words, with no evidence. At that point, Ji Ying would surely have chosen to believe Li Xuan over him. If the slander of disrespecting Medicine Pill Valley and bullying its disciples stuck, how could Lingxiao Palace stand in the Northern Territory?

Knowing he still harbored resentment, Ji Ying cupped his fists and said, "Today’s matter was my fault. I was too hasty and trusted a petty man’s words. I hope Palace Master Yang will not hold it against me. I offer my apologies."

With that, he stood up and bowed to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai remained seated, replying calmly, "Grandmaster Ji is too polite. Now that the misunderstanding is resolved, it’s nothing serious."

Ji Ying said, "Palace Master Yang is magnanimous. I am ashamed." After a pause, he added, "But there is one thing I must say."

Yang Kai looked at him. "Please speak."

"Today, since I came here, I can take what you said earlier as words spoken in anger and won’t hold it against you. But if you meet my Senior Brothers in the future, you mustn’t say such things again."

"Say what?" Yang Kai frowned.

"Words belittling my Medicine Pill Valley!" Ji Ying said sternly.

Yang Kai suddenly remembered that he had said something like "Medicine Pill Valley isn’t worth a damn..."

He gave a wry smile. "I’ll remember!" He had indeed spoken in anger at the time.

Ji Ying said seriously, "We alchemists may not deliberately pursue the Martial Dao, but what we cultivate is also one of the great Daos of the world. To reach the Great Emperor’s position through alchemy, throughout history, only my master has done so. The Medicine Pill Valley he founded is not only the Sacred Land in the hearts of Northern Territory cultivators, but also the Sacred Land in the hearts of all alchemists. If Palace Master Yang’s words were to spread, you might offend every alchemist in the world. How would Lingxiao Palace ever find someone to refine pills for you in the future?"

Mi Qi and Li Jiao’s eyes lit up as they listened, secretly wondering if they should spread Yang Kai’s earlier words and make him the enemy of all alchemists.

Yang Kai laughed loudly. "There’s no need to ask anyone else. I’ll just refine the pills myself."

Ji Ying was stunned, looking at Yang Kai in surprise. "Palace Master Yang, are you also an alchemist?"

Even Mi Qi and Li Jiao stared at Yang Kai in disbelief, as if they had just heard something shocking.

Yang Kai nodded. "I know a thing or two."

Ji Ying grew interested. "May I ask what grade you are, and what kind of pills you can refine?"

Yang Kai stroked his chin. "What grade is it if I can refine Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills?"

"What?" Ji Ying exclaimed in shock. To be able to refine Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills—that was the mark of an Imperial-Grade Pill Master. Across the entire Star Realm, how many could claim such a title? Though he and his Senior Brothers had all reached this level, it was only because Great Emperor Miaodan had exceptionally high standards for accepting disciples, and had personally guided them for so many years. If they hadn’t achieved something by now, it would only mean Great Emperor Miaodan’s judgment was lacking.

They are who they are, and others are others. In the entire Star Realm, aside from them, the total number of Imperial-Grade Pill Masters can be counted on two hands—no more than ten in all.

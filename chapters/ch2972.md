## Chapter 2972 — Ah Wang's Outburst

The arrival of Yang Kai and Zhu Qing startled the many members of the Demon Race. In particular, what had happened to the Blood Demon and Succubus who had secretly attacked left them trembling with fear. Whether it was the wailing Blood Demon or the pale-faced Succubus, both were Demon King-level existences.

The two Demon Kings had attacked without leaving the slightest trace, yet the newcomers had actually neutralized their methods one after another and even injured one of them in return. It was simply inconceivable.

While they were still distracted, a burst of barking suddenly reached their ears. Immediately afterward, an exceedingly homely little black dog charged out from the side, its four short legs pumping like wheels as it excitedly rushed toward a Demon Commander. It was as though a dog in heat had spotted an alluring bitch prancing before it, making for an extremely comical sight.

That Demon Commander appeared to be from the Might Demon clan. He was tall and burly, and wielded a huge axe whose gleaming, razor-sharp blade radiated dense black Qi. It was clearly a Demonic Artifact.

Seeing the little black dog charge toward him, the Demon Commander's expression turned fierce. He shook his head, raised the huge axe in his hand, and brought it crashing down at the little black dog.

His movements were extremely casual, for he could sense that the little black dog was not very strong. He could kill such a little beast with a wave of his hand.

The attention of the other members of the Demon Race had also been drawn to Yang Kai and Zhu Qing. Aside from a handful who glanced at the little black dog, no one paid it any further attention.

The huge axe came down, but the little black dog nimbly dodged aside with an agility that seemed almost absurd. The Demon Commander's strike missed, leaving him briefly stunned and somewhat enraged from embarrassment. Before he could use another move, the little black dog suddenly opened its mouth and bit at him.

Its modest-sized head abruptly swelled, instantly becoming as large as a door before engulfing the Demon Commander in one bite.

Everyone present was stunned.

The many members of the Demon Race turned their heads one after another and stared at the little black dog in horror, their eyes nearly popping out of their sockets.

Zhu Qing and Zhu Lie were clearly taken aback as well. They had never imagined that such an unremarkable little creature could possess such terrifying might, swallowing a Demon Commander in a single bite. A Demon Commander might not be particularly powerful, but it was still equivalent to a cultivator in the Void King Realm. Moreover, Demon Commanders of the Might Demon Clan, thanks to their exceptional physical strength, were generally more than a match for ordinary Dao Origin Realm cultivators.

Zhu Qing was especially astonished. Although she had discovered that the little black dog was somewhat unusual during the days she had spent with it, she had never imagined it possessed such an ability.

Only Yang Kai remained calm in the face of this unexpected development, stroking his chin thoughtfully.

According to Gong Yue, the little black dog possessed the bloodline of Guixu, an exotic beast from the Demon Domain. This trace of bloodline had been hidden extremely deeply, but Gong Yue had activated it with a Demon Race Secret Technique in Qingyang Shrine. At the time, the little black dog had truly been like one man holding a pass against ten thousand enemies. Relying on its divine ability, Gong Yue had managed to contend with ten Emperor Realm Masters for a long time.

It had been able to swallow even someone like Feng Ming, a Third-Stage Emperor Realm master, in a single bite. A mere Demon Commander was nothing by comparison.

However, the little black dog's performance had been rather inconsistent recently. Aside from swallowing Steward Liao, it had not displayed this devouring divine ability again. A few days ago, it had fought Fu Yu in the imperial palace for so long, yet it had shown no sign of swallowing her.

Now, however, it seemed to have been given a powerful stimulant. It had remained silent until now, only to amaze everyone the moment it acted.

Yang Kai guessed that it had something to do with these members of the Demon Race. Guixu was an exotic beast from the Demon Domain, after all, so the aura of the Demon Race delighted the little black dog immensely. To it, these demons might well be some kind of delicious food.

While Yang Kai was pondering, the Demon Commander trapped in the little black dog's mouth began struggling desperately. Bulges visibly rose from the little black dog's great maw from time to time. Its enormous mouth was completely out of proportion with its short body, and the Demon Commander's strength sent it staggering from side to side.

Suddenly, it raised its head and swallowed.

Under everyone's watchful eyes, a small lump appeared in its throat, rolled all the way down into its belly, and vanished.

At the same time, its door-sized mouth returned to normal, yet there was not the slightest change in its belly. No one knew where it had swallowed a living Demon Commander to, and even the Demonic Artifact axe had disappeared without a trace.

A chorus of sharp gasps rang out as the members of the Demon Race felt their blood run cold.

The little black dog merely licked its lips, looking as though it had not had nearly enough. Its bright, sparkling eyes turned toward the gathered demons. Every one of them who met its gaze, regardless of the strength or level of their cultivation, involuntarily broke out in goosebumps. It was as though they had encountered their natural enemy, and their hearts trembled with fear.

No command was needed. The instant danger enveloped them, the many members of the Demon Race used their Secret Techniques one after another. Streams of pitch-black energy transformed into attacks that rained down upon the little black dog from every direction.

Yang Kai's expression shifted. He wanted to observe the situation, so he did not intervene.

The little black dog began whining, but not from fear. Its cries were filled with excitement. It leapt three feet into the air and opened its mouth once more.

This time, its mouth opened even wider than before. The gaping maw seemed to lead to an unknown world, and an immensely powerful suction force came from within.

Every attack aimed at it was devoured without a single trace remaining, failing to have even the slightest effect. The little black dog grew even more valiant. Braving the attacks of the many demons, it pounced forward as its mouth expanded to the size of a house, then bit down.

Space shattered where its jaws closed, and a huge section of the void itself vanished. More than a dozen demons within that area disappeared in an instant, while the survivors fled for their lives, swiftly putting distance between themselves and the little black dog.

Gulp...

The little black dog swallowed once more, but it was still not satisfied and turned its gaze toward the other demons.

"The Domain-Destroying Beast... Guixu!" A low cry rang out. Among the members of the Demon Race, an existence whose entire body was pitch-black, as though wrapped in smoke, shouted in terror.

He had recognized the little black dog's origins, and his voice was filled with fear.

This demon was a Demon King-level existence, and his aura was not weak, roughly equivalent to a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master. Furthermore, as a Shadow Demon, he could easily assassinate even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master if he caught them unprepared.

He was the strongest Master among this group of demons and was likely their leader as well.

Yet after recognizing the little black dog's origins, even such a powerful Master cried out in alarm. His figure transformed into a cloud of smoke that burst into nine separate streams, each fleeing in a different direction.

Guixu was infamous throughout the Demon Domain as the Domain-Destroying Beast. Although it originated from the Demon Domain, it could almost be called the Demon Domain's enemy. It existed to devour, and Demonic Qi and the Demon Race were its favorite foods.

Rumor had it that in the true Demon Domain, a Guixu had once brought calamity in every direction, and even the Demon Saints had been unable to contend with it. In an era without a Great Demon God, that Guixu had practically been the overlord of the Demon Domain. Countless members of the Demon Race had been swallowed by it, and innumerable Demon Race cities and towns had been destroyed.

Guixu represented destruction, leaving only ruins wherever it went, which was how it had earned its name.

In the end, a powerful Demon Saint led the entire Demon Race in a decisive battle against Guixu. That battle lasted for several decades. The Demon Race suffered catastrophic casualties, losing more than half its population, while the devastated lands covered two-thirds of the entire Demon Domain.

The Demon Race ultimately emerged victorious and killed that Guixu. The Demon Saint who had led them also glimpsed the pinnacle of power through that battle and advanced to become a Great Demon God, the ruler of the entire Demon Domain. That very battle had made the living conditions throughout the Demon Domain extremely harsh, forcing its many Masters to expand into new territories and search for Great Worlds more suitable for survival.

Every member of the Demon Race knew this piece of history by heart, and those in the Revolving World were no exception.

Thus, after seeing the little black dog display its devouring divine ability, this Demon King had immediately recalled the fearsome reputation of the Domain-Destroying Beast.

Although he could not be certain that it truly was Guixu, the feeling of mortal peril he experienced could not be false. Once swallowed by Guixu, there was essentially no hope of survival.

His courage had completely deserted him, and he hurriedly used a life-preserving divine ability in an attempt to escape.

Only one of the nine streams of smoke concealed his true body; the other eight were merely decoys. Faced with such a divine ability, even an enemy somewhat stronger than him could not hope to stop him.

Shadow Demons excelled at escape, and this was the perfect demonstration of that fact.

This time, however, the Shadow Demon Demon King had miscalculated. Before the scattered streams of smoke could travel even a hundred zhang, they suddenly struck an invisible barrier. Eight of them burst apart, while the remaining stream contracted and expanded, revealing the Shadow Demon's figure once more. His face livid, he looked up at the human in the sky.

Yang Kai gazed at him with a broad smile as the power of the Laws of Space surged around him. With Yang Kai at the center, the space within a radius of a thousand zhang turned into a quagmire, suddenly becoming incomparably viscous, while its outer boundary became harder than steel, forming a cage.

Every member of the Demon Race sagged as though an invisible mountain were pressing down on their chests, making it difficult to breathe.

Zhu Qing and Zhu Lie also felt slightly uncomfortable, but Yang Kai was not deliberately targeting them. Moreover, the two members of the Dragon Clan were both quite powerful, so they fared much better than the demons.

Li Jiao groaned, seemingly having regained consciousness. However, Zhu Qing's palm strike to his chest had injured him severely, so he could only lie on the ground and circulate his Emperor Qi, healing his wounds while resisting the corrosion of the Demonic Qi.

"Ah Wang, if you don't act now, then when?" Yang Kai said lightly.

The little black dog immediately shot forward and pounced at the Blood Demon who had used a Secret Technique to ambush Zhu Lie earlier. It could clearly sense that this demon smelled more delicious than the others.

The Blood Demon had failed to ambush Zhu Lie earlier, and Yang Kai's Moon Blade had severed his Secret Technique, injuring his Divine Sense and causing his strength to plummet. Seeing the little black dog pounce toward him, his expression changed drastically. He spun around and transformed into a mass of blood-red light, charging forward instead of retreating as he moved to envelop the little black dog.

The name Guixu resounded like thunder, so he had naturally heard of it. However, even if this little black dog before him truly was Guixu, it was only a Guixu cub. As a Demon King, he naturally had the confidence to contend with it.

The blood-red light spread through the air, growing larger than a house. Like a vast crimson net, it moved to envelop the little black dog.

The little black dog tilted its head and stared, looking thoroughly puzzled. It clearly could not understand how this fellow had become even larger than its mouth.

It opened its mouth with all its might, but no matter what it did, it could not make it larger than the blood net. Growing annoyed, it suddenly took a deep breath.

An incomparably powerful devouring force erupted. A cry of alarm came from within the blood net as it folded in on itself like a thin pancake and was slowly dragged toward the little black dog's enormous mouth.

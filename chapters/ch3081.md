## Chapter 3081 — A Dead Man Revived?

The young woman had displayed extraordinary talent and aptitude for cultivation from a very young age.

She was of noble birth and came from a wealthy family, so she had never lacked cultivation resources.

With her outstanding aptitude and her family's unconditional support, she naturally rose as a new star of cultivation, reaching the Void King Realm step by step in scarcely a hundred years.

She had none of the strange temperaments common to young ladies of other great families and treated even servants with great kindness, earning immense popularity on Morning Sun Star.

Had the old Family Head's old injuries not suddenly flared up and left him in poor health, she would likely still be cultivating in retreat. She did not find cultivation dull; on the contrary, she rather enjoyed it. Feeling the power within her grow little by little brought her an indescribable joy.

She had never wanted to be called upon in a time of crisis to lead the Lu Family.

Her peaceful days of cultivation had been shattered, replaced by an endless tangle of trivial affairs. Every morning when she opened her eyes, she seemed to hear countless mouths crying out to be fed. For the past thirty years, she had poured all her effort into the Lu Family, yet her own strength had not improved in the slightest.

No matter how she turned it over in her mind, she could not understand how anyone in the Lu Family could have betrayed her.

Why? What did they stand to gain?

Was this betrayal truly all she had received in return for thirty years of devotion? Her hand tightened around her sword, then slowly relaxed. Her gaze swept past that clan uncle and settled on the fat man surnamed Ma. "Since Second Chief is here, First Chief and Third Chief must be nearby as well. Why not show yourselves and meet me?"

Her beautiful eyes swept the surroundings, her red lips slightly parted.

Although the fat man surnamed Ma was formidable, she did not fear him. Their brief clash had given her some measure of his strength, which was likely on par with her own. Both were First Stage Void King Realm cultivators, and neither held much advantage over the other.

Her advantage lay in her abundant Saint Yuan, while Ma Chaoqun's lay in his wealth of combat experience. He was a Star Pirate, after all, an expert who had fought his way through seas of blood and mountains of corpses.

If Ma Chaoqun were the only one here, the strength of the Lu Family's people would be enough to carve out a bloody path of escape.

But whenever Gale mobilized, it always came out in full force.

Gale had three Chiefs. First Chief Sheng Yao had reached the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. Second Chief was this fair, fat man, Ma Chaoqun, while Third Chief Ge Ming was also a First Stage Void King Realm cultivator.

Gale had survived the encirclement and suppression of numerous forces until now through more than mere luck. It also possessed formidable strength of its own.

Ma Chaoqun chuckled. "Many thanks for Young Lady Lu's concern, but I'm afraid you'll be disappointed. This Ma came alone today. Big Brother and Third Brother have other important matters to attend to. If Young Lady Lu wishes to see them, why not return to Gale's territory with me? You'll naturally meet them there."

Lu Huaishuang narrowed her beautiful eyes. "Is Second Chief inviting me?"

Ma Chaoqun's smile did not change. "If Young Lady Lu cooperates, then it is an invitation."

"And if I don't?"

"Heh heh..." Ma Chaoqun smiled without answering, his intentions obvious.

If she did not cooperate, then it was a threat.

Lu Huaishuang said calmly, "I have long heard of Gale's great reputation. The Star Domain is vast, so meeting here by chance can be considered fate. We came unprepared, but my Lu Family is willing to offer a modest gift so Gale's brothers may enjoy some wine and tea. I ask that Second Chief clear a path for us."

Most Star Pirates sought only wealth, and Lu Huaishuang preferred to avoid conflict with them if possible. More importantly, however, she had a bad feeling.

Ma Chaoqun was definitely not the only one here.

First Chief Sheng Yao and Third Chief Ge Ming might be lying in ambush somewhere, waiting for an opportunity to strike. If a fight broke out, the Lu Family members would likely have no chance of survival, and even she might not escape.

If they could sacrifice some wealth to avert disaster, so much the better.

Ma Chaoqun's eyes gleamed as he praised, "I have long heard that the Lu Family's Eldest Young Lady is no less capable than any man. Now that I've seen you for myself, your reputation is indeed well deserved."

Women had been called difficult to deal with since ancient times, yet this Lu Family Young Lady possessed a breadth of mind and boldness ordinary women lacked. No wonder she could lead the Lu Family and command half the holdings on Morning Sun Star. Had she been born a man, the Star Domain would likely have gained another overlord in the near future.

He shook his enormous head. "What a pity, what a pity. This Ma would like to let Young Lady Lu pass, but I'm afraid certain people won't agree."

"Who?" Lu Huaishuang asked in a deep voice.

A pitch-black Warship suddenly broke through the dense mist and descended from the clouds. Its massive hull was every bit the equal of the Lu Family's Warship, as though the two had been cast from the same mold.

A conspicuous character reading "He" was clearly visible on the ship even from a great distance.

The crowd burst into an uproar.

"It's the He Family's Warship."

"How could it be them?"

"This is a trap, damn it!"

"The He Family actually colluded with Gale! How shameless!"

The He Family was the other top family on Morning Sun Star. Together with the Lu Family, it occupied the star, with each ruling its own territory. As the saying went, one mountain could not accommodate two tigers. With two top families opposed to each other on a single Cultivation Star, friction was inevitable. One attacked today, the other struck back tomorrow. Minor clashes escalated, while major ones became blood feuds...

The two great families of Morning Sun Star were practically irreconcilable.

Yet Lu Huaishuang had never imagined the He Family had ties to Gale.

Gale was the most notorious band of Star Pirates in the entire Star Domain, bar none! Anyone with even the slightest connection to them could never wash away the stain.

Was the He Family not afraid word would leak out and cause the experts of the entire Star Domain to rise against them? Even if they swallowed the Lu Family's holdings, they would likely never have the chance to enjoy them.

Lu Huaishuang's expression soon sank, however.

Since the He Family's Warship dared to appear so openly, they clearly had no intention of leaving anyone alive.

The dead could never reveal any secrets.

Her eyes turned cold as she looked back at that clan uncle. His mouth hung open, his face filled with shock and his eyes brimming with horror. This turn of events was clearly beyond his expectations as well.

The Warship landed nearby, and as its hatch opened, an elderly man strode out. He appeared to be around sixty years old, with hair already half gray. He carried the natural authority of one long accustomed to a position of power, his mere presence commanding respect.

He made no visible display of skill, yet crossed the distance in only a few steps.

Several hundred He Family elites followed close behind him, eyeing the Lu Family members like tigers watching their prey.

The atmosphere instantly grew tense. The Lu Family members could be heard swallowing, every one of them nervous and uneasy.

Ma Chaoqun still wore his beaming smile, as though he had not even noticed He Wuzui's arrival.

He Wuzui stared at Lu Huaishuang and let out three cold laughs before speaking. "Little girl, so this day has finally come for you!"

His was the brazen face of a petty man intoxicated by success.

Lu Huaishuang exhaled softly, knowing the odds were heavily against her today. Yet in this moment of crisis, she only grew calmer. "Old Master He, the grievances on Morning Sun Star are a matter between our two families. Involving outsiders seems rather inappropriate, does it not?"

He Wuzui coldly snorted. "Gale's three Chiefs are this He's sworn brothers. How could they be considered outsiders?"

The revelation was earth-shattering!

Lu Huaishuang was deeply astonished. She had never heard of such a thing, and neither had anyone else in the entire Star Domain. Yet with matters having reached this point, she doubted He Wuzui would joke about it. Ma Chaoqun also showed no sign of denying the claim. Clearly, He Wuzui had spoken the truth: he and Gale's three Chiefs were indeed sworn brothers.

He Wuzui savored Lu Huaishuang's stunned expression, his heart filling with delight.

After years of patient endurance, his efforts had finally borne fruit. From this day forward, Morning Sun Star would belong to the He Family, and those surnamed Lu would no longer have any say in its affairs.

"Fourth Brother, never mind the others, but do you want the Lu Family's Eldest Young Lady dead or alive?" Ma Chaoqun suddenly asked, turning to He Wuzui.

The other Lu Family trash could simply be killed, but Lu Huaishuang stirred his desire. Beautiful women were not rare, and with Ma Chaoqun's strength and status, he had no shortage of beauties at his side.

Beauties whose cultivation had reached the Void King Realm, however, were as rare as phoenix feathers and qilin horns. He had never tasted one before.

Moreover, he had long heard that the Lu Family's Eldest Young Lady had spent all her time cultivating arduously in retreat and had likely never even tasted a man. It would be quite a pity for her to die just like that.

The moment he heard this, He Wuzui knew what wicked idea Ma Chaoqun was entertaining. He said solemnly, "Second Brother, a rose may be fragrant, but it is covered in thorns. Be careful they don't wound you."

Ma Chaoqun gave the matter serious thought, then nodded. "Very well."

He knew how difficult it would be to capture a First Stage Void King Realm cultivator alive, so he dismissed the idea. Otherwise, the slightest hesitation during the fight might result in his own death.

"Attack!" He Wuzui suddenly barked, clearly intent on ending the battle quickly and giving the Lu Family no chance to resist.

As he spoke, Ma Chaoqun brought his hands together, and two gleaming golden spheres suddenly appeared in his palms. They radiated astonishing Spiritual Qi and were clearly an extraordinary Secret Treasure.

He Wuzui's body also shuddered as his Saint Yuan surged thunderously through him.

Lu Huaishuang's expression grew solemn. She swept two fingers across her azure sword, and sword intent soared into the sky.

A great battle was about to erupt.

Without warning, a ghostlike figure darted to her side, thrusting a spike at her in the blink of an eye.

Lu Huaishuang paled in shock.

All her attention had been focused on Ma Chaoqun and He Wuzui, so the instant they moved, she naturally guarded against them.

Who could have known those two were merely a diversion? The true killing move had already stolen up beside her, and by the time she reacted, it was too late.

He Wuzui and Ma Chaoqun both smiled, ready to enjoy the show.

A cold gleam flashed across the spike, which radiated a deadly aura as though blood would bloom in the very next instant.

Yet at that very instant, the spike wielder's hair stood on end. The chilling sensation of plunging into an ice cellar arose unbidden, and all the blood in the wielder's body seemed about to freeze.

Lu Huaishuang's beautiful eyes narrowed as astonishment crossed her face.

The young man who had lain quietly at her feet, long since dead, actually moved at that moment.

And he did so in an extremely bizarre manner.

First, his feet planted themselves on the ground. His lower legs abruptly rose upright, then his thighs straightened and his body sprang up.

When Lu Huaishuang looked down, she found herself staring into a pair of clear, profound black eyes. They were like a bottomless abyss. After just one glance, Lu Huaishuang felt as though her entire being were sinking into them.

Greatly alarmed, she hurriedly circulated her Divine Sense and steadied her mind.

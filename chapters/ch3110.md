## Chapter 3110 — I Am Her Husband

Yunxia Mountain was lush with green forests and beautiful scenery. Mountain ranges rose and fell in an unbroken chain, Spirit Beasts and flying birds moving among them, giving the place an extraordinary air.

Outside the mountain gate, the Star Shuttle descended. The disciples guarding the mountain immediately came forward, staring at Xiang Fei in surprise. "Senior Brother Xiang, why are you back? And these people are..."

Xiang Fei might have no real backing in Yunxia Sect, and he might not be favored by the elders, but his cultivation at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm was there for all to see. How could a few mountain-guarding disciples dare show him disrespect?

Xiang Fei mumbled for a while, somewhat at a loss as to how to explain. Returning to the Sect without being summoned was already a violation of Sect rules. If he wanted to enter, he still had to explain the reason to the men in front of him and have them report it on his behalf.

The mountain-guarding disciples looked at Xiang Fei suspiciously, not knowing what he was hesitating over.

"I'm looking for someone!" Yang Kai was in a hurry to see Su Yan; how could he have time to waste here? With a sweep of his hand, he pushed the disciples aside and strode inward.

Seeing this, Xiang Fei's heart lurched, and he inwardly cried that this was bad, very bad. He had known things were going to go wrong, but he had not expected this fellow to be so overbearing. If they had politely submitted a visiting card, it was not as if entering would have been impossible, but what was the meaning of charging straight in like this?

With him making such a scene, Xiang Fei feared he would be saddled with the charge of secretly colluding with an outside enemy.

Run? Or not run? If he did not run, he would certainly be punished by the Sect, but if he did run, where could he possibly go?

His thoughts raced, but in the end he could only grit his teeth and follow, pleading bitterly, "Senior Brother, please wait..."

He Yunxiang stepped sideways and blocked him, smiling faintly. "There is nothing more for you to do here. Consider this bottle of Spiritual Pills our thanks." Leaving behind a bottle of Spiritual Pills, she also chased after Yang Kai.

Only after standing stunned for a while did the mountain-guarding disciples snap back to their senses as if waking from a dream. One of them immediately took out a Transmission Compass and poured his Divine Sense into it.

A short while later, warning bells rang loudly through Yunxia Sect, echoing into the clouds.

Xiang Fei's face went pale, and he slumped onto the ground. The bottle of Spiritual Pills in his hand suddenly felt scalding hot.

Figures flickered everywhere as streaks of light rose from within Yunxia Sect and rushed toward the mountain gate from all directions.

Yang Kai very quickly encountered the first layer of obstruction: the Guardian Mountain Array!

Every Sect had its own Guardian Mountain Array. On ordinary days, it would not be fully activated, but it still provided some protection and warning. Without a special method, one could not pass through the Array. In wartime, if the Guardian Mountain Array operated at full strength, it would also become a powerful defense.

An invisible wall blocked the path ahead, forcing Yang Kai to stop.

Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh. Figure after figure appeared all around, each one looking at him with hostility. Although Yunxia Sect was no top force, it was not a soft persimmon anyone could squeeze at will. This fellow before them had tried to force his way up the mountain without reporting his arrival, clearly placing Yunxia Sect's rules beneath his notice. To any Sect, this was a major taboo, an act of hostility.

"Halt, newcomer. State your name!" A red-faced man stood before the crowd and shouted in a deep voice. This man had a First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation, so within Yunxia Sect he was clearly a rare expert as well. He held some important post and was probably an Elder, a Hall Master, or the like.

Yang Kai's brow was tightly furrowed, but not because the Guardian Mountain Array had blocked his path, nor because Yunxia Sect had the advantage in numbers.

He had actually failed to sense her!

Inside Yunxia Sect, there was actually no trace of Su Yan's aura!

Back then, he and Su Yan had experienced a fortuitous encounter together, obtaining the Dual Cultivation Technique and the inheritances of the Dragon Emperor and Phoenix Empress. They had jointly comprehended the mysteries of yin-yang creation and were linked heart to heart. As long as they were within a certain distance of one another, each could sense the other's presence.

Yunxia Mountain stretched only a few hundred li. If Su Yan was truly here, there was no reason he would not be able to sense her.

Since he could not sense her at all, that meant Su Yan was not inside Yunxia Sect.

Had he misunderstood? Could it really be only the same name, and the Su Yan inside Yunxia Sect was not his Su Yan? But the person Xiang Fei had described was clearly the very one Yang Kai longed for day and night.

Or perhaps Su Yan was not in Yunxia Sect at this moment? Mm, that was somewhat possible.

The red-faced man was indeed an Elder of Yunxia Sect. Under the attention of so many disciples, his question had unexpectedly gone ignored, and he could not help growing angry out of embarrassment. Sweeping his Divine Sense over the three people below, he discovered that one was at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, while the other two actually showed no trace of cultivation at all. What surprised him even more was that there was also a little girl of seven or eight.

Was this a family of three who had gone out to play and accidentally lost their way?

"Are you deaf? Did you not hear this Elder asking you a question?" the red-faced man shouted angrily again.

Only then did Yang Kai raise his head and look toward him. When he lifted his hand, an illusory figure suddenly stood upright in the void, so lifelike it seemed to be a living person. Black hair fluttered, her brows and eyes were picturesque, her features exquisite, and a faint smile rested on her face, leaving all who saw her dazzled and entranced.

Yang Kai looked at the red-faced man and said, "Is this person in your noble Sect?"

Before the red-faced man could answer, some of Yunxia Sect's disciples burst into an uproar.

"This is..."

"Is this Senior Sister Su?"

"It looks a little like Senior Sister Su. No, this is Senior Sister Su!"

"My heavens, Senior Sister Su is actually this beautiful when she smiles."

The image of Su Yan that Yang Kai had condensed with his Yuan was how she looked in his memories, but how could this group of Yunxia Sect disciples ever have seen Su Yan smile? Her title as an ice mountain beauty was not for nothing, so for a moment they hardly dared recognize her.

Who was this man? Why had he been able to see Senior Sister Su Yan's smile? For a moment, many of them were filled with righteous indignation and felt this fellow's face was hateful. Why should such a good thing have fallen to him?

The whispers from all around reached his ears, and the answer was already obvious. Yang Kai let out a soft breath and asked, "Where is she?"

"Who exactly are you?" Having been ignored by Yang Kai again and again, the red-faced man was thoroughly enraged.

"Where. Is. She!" Yang Kai looked at him and growled each word.

The red-faced man laughed in fury. "Why should this Lord tell you?"

With a great boom, the ground shook and the mountains trembled. The Guardian Mountain Array blocking Yang Kai suddenly collapsed, turning into specks of fluorescent light that vanished.

Yang Kai withdrew his fist and said calmly, "My apologies. My temper is somewhat violent today. If I have offended you in any way, I ask everyone to forgive me." As he spoke, he took a few steps and had already arrived before the red-faced man.

Before the red-faced man could recover from that intense shock, a fierce aura abruptly bore down on him. Looking up, he saw Yang Kai's face close at hand and could not help stumbling back several steps, asking warily, "What are you trying to do?"

To shatter Yunxia Sect's Guardian Mountain Array with one punch, even if the Array had not been operating at full power, was not something an ordinary person could do. Moreover, from beginning to end, there had not been the slightest energy fluctuation. What kind of brute force was this?

Yang Kai reached out and seized his collar. "Are you deaf or stupid? I have asked several times already. Where is she?"

Spittle flew all over the red-faced man's face, making him fly into a rage. "Junior, do not be so arrogant! This is my Yunxia Sect. If you know what's good for you, withdraw at once, or you will regret it for the rest of your life!"

Although Yang Kai had displayed extraordinary brute force, as an Elder of Yunxia Sect, how could he show weakness? There were more than a thousand disciples in the Sect, and over a dozen Dao Origin Realm cultivators. The Sect Master even had a Second Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation. If this boy truly dared act wantonly here, they would make sure he walked in upright and was carried out lying down.

Besides, after witnessing Yang Kai's brute force, he had already sent messages to many Elders and the Sect Master. By now, they should already be arriving, right?

A streak of light came flying over, bringing a biting chill. When it drew near, it suddenly revealed the shape of a long saber and chopped down at Yang Kai's head.

Yang Kai flicked a finger, striking the spine of the blade dead on. As light flashed, the saber flew back upside down and was caught by an old man, whose face could not help twisting. In that instant just now, he had actually sensed that the spirituality of his Secret Treasure had been somewhat damaged.

Who was this fellow?

Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh.

One powerful aura after another revealed itself, and seven or eight people suddenly appeared. When the many Yunxia Sect disciples saw them, they all became solemn and respectful. These people were all Dao Origin Realm Elders of the Sect. Normally, even seeing one was difficult, yet so many had appeared at this moment. From the looks of it, aside from those few who were out on business, all the Elders in the Sect had already gathered here.

A black-clothed man came walking through the air, his broad sleeves billowing in the wind, his expression dignified, his aura a notch stronger than anyone present.

"Sect Master!" Those on all sides bowed, and the black-clothed man nodded reservedly.

He was Yunxia Sect's Sect Master, Han Zhengyuan.

With the Guardian Mountain Array broken, even without the red-faced man's notification, he would have come to investigate what had happened.

Looking over, he saw that one of his own Elders was actually being held by the collar by a young man, and his face immediately sank.

"Let go!" the red-faced man said with a cold laugh, his gaze sharp as he glared at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai's mood had calmed somewhat as well. He did bear some responsibility for things having escalated to this point; mainly, because the matter involved Su Yan, he had been somewhat impulsive. Hearing this, he let go.

In the eyes of everyone from Yunxia Sect, however, this action meant he was feeling guilty. The red-faced man thought, At least you know how to read the situation, then proudly cupped his fist and said, "Greetings, Sect Master." At least he had not lost face for Yunxia Sect, so at this moment he was naturally full of confidence.

Han Zhengyuan nodded, looked at Yang Kai, and asked, “How should I address you? What business brings you to my Yunxia Sect?”

Yang Kai said, "My name is unimportant. I have come to your noble Sect only to find someone."

"Who?"

"Su Yan!"

Han Zhengyuan could not help frowning and asked, "What are you to her?"

Yang Kai said, "I am her husband!"

As soon as these words came out, the whole scene burst into an uproar. The Yunxia Sect disciples were all dumbstruck, looking at Yang Kai as if he were a fool. Su Yan had entered the Sect more than twenty years ago, yet they had never heard of her having any husband. On ordinary days, even seeing her speak with men was rare, so how could such an intimate person suddenly appear? Their first reaction was disbelief, followed immediately by anger.

Su Yan was extremely popular throughout Yunxia Sect and was treated by many Senior Brothers and Junior Brothers as the woman of their dreams. When someone suddenly appeared and claimed to be her husband, they were naturally furious. They felt this fellow had profaned the beauty in their hearts and wished they could rush forward and cut him into ten thousand pieces before the hatred in their hearts could be relieved.

"Bullshit!" Before Han Zhengyuan could answer, a young man suddenly stepped out and cursed.

Yang Kai turned his head to look at him and saw that this fellow had a pretty, powdered face, fair skin, and a thin frame. Although he had Void King Realm cultivation, his aura rose and fell unstably, clearly showing his foundation was not firm. This kind of cultivation had definitely been piled up with medicines.

Looking at his face again, Yang Kai saw that he bore a faint resemblance to Han Zhengyuan.

Yang Kai immediately understood who he was.

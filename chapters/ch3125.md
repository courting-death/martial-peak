## Chapter 3125 — Not Returning Until Drunk

A Star Lord and a Cultivation Star did not share a simple master-servant relationship. They rose and fell together. When the Star Lord's cultivation was high, the Cultivation Star benefited as well, and the reverse was also true.

Tongxuan Continent was one such example. Its Spiritual Qi had suddenly begun to dry up, and if Xia Ningshang had not risked refining Tongxuan Continent's Stellar Origin, then after these past several dozen years, the once-flourishing Tongxuan Continent would likely have become a land of decline and desolation.

Yang Kai had been away from Dark Star for so many years that the bond between him and Dark Star's Stellar Origin had weakened. Add to that the campaigns of the past dozen-plus years, and the entire star had been sliding downhill.

It was only because too little time had passed, and because the changes had been subtle and gradual, that the cultivators on Dark Star had not noticed.

But Yang Kai sensed it the moment he returned.

Compared to when he had left, Dark Star was undoubtedly more desolate.

Now, by a fortunate coincidence, the Star Lord was nourishing it, drawing power from the universe beyond the heavens to nourish all of Dark Star. The scarred and battered land received an immense replenishment and became fertile once more. Every living thing would benefit.

After this, the mortal worlds would rarely suffer from droughts or floods again. Cultivators would also find cultivation easier, and when they met bottlenecks and shackles, they would be able to break through more quickly. It was a deed that benefited the present age, profited generations to come, and bestowed grace upon all living beings.

Yang Kai knew none of this. At the moment, he was in an exceptionally clear and empty state, countless sounds pouring into his ears. An ordinary person would have gone mad if they encountered such a thing, but he remained calm and solemn, as though he had been born for this. Those chaotic sounds did not feel noisy to him at all. Instead, they gave him a strange, wonderful sense of closeness.

With his eyes closed, he could sense every blade of grass, every tree, every flower, and every plant on Dark Star. The entire world became vivid and brilliantly colored.

Time flowed slowly by.

In Lingxiao Sect, on the square before the main hall, people moved in dense crowds, and the hall itself was packed full. They were not only members of Lingxiao Sect, but also cultivators from Dark Star's other great forces.

At this moment, almost all of Dark Star's strongest figures had gathered here.

One month ago, an enormous image of Yang Kai lying asleep across the sky had suddenly appeared, and everyone had seen it clearly. Such an image could not have appeared out of nowhere, so those with sharp minds naturally formed their own guesses and sent messages to Lingxiao Sect to inquire.

What they received in return was news that sent them into raptures: Yang Kai had returned!

Only then did everyone understand. No wonder the Warships from Great Wilderness Star Domain had suddenly been destroyed, and no wonder the cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain had been killed for no apparent reason. So all of it had been Yang Kai's work. Even as they rejoiced in secret, they were deeply shaken. The power of a Star Lord was truly terrifying.

One after another, they felt as if they had found their backbone, and the hearts that had been hanging in suspense finally settled. With Yang Kai holding the fort, who would dare invade Dark Star again? This was probably the best news they had heard in over a dozen years.

Ever since Yang Kai's enormous image appeared a month ago, it had remained in that soundly sleeping posture. He would only occasionally turn over, kick a leg, or shift his sleeping position. With every breath he took, countless rays of starlight from beyond the heavens were drawn in, adorning all of Dark Star until it shone bright and dazzling.

For an entire month, there had been only daylight.

Even after all this time, the sight still left them awestruck whenever they looked at it.

And over the course of that month, the effects of the Star Lord's nourishing had already become clear.

In every region, every place, every Sect and school, disciples were breaking through in great waves like bamboo shoots after a spring rain. The lower one's cultivation, the faster the breakthrough. The most shocking case was a disciple from a small Sect. He had originally been only in the Separation and Union Realm, yet now he had reached the Sixth Stage of the Divine Roaming Realm. In a single short month, he had nearly crossed two great realms.

In ordinary times, such a thing would have been impossible. Even the most outstanding genius could not have done it in one month.

But under the influence of the Star Lord's nourishing, that impossibility had become reality.

What excited the cultivators of Dark Star most was that many cultivators who had been stuck at the Third Stage of the Origin Returning Realm finally advanced to the Void King Realm one after another. The number was neither too high nor too low. Across Dark Star, there were at least twenty newly advanced Void King Realm cultivators, and Lingxiao Sect alone accounted for four of them.

More than twenty Void King Realm cultivators would be an extremely terrifying number on any Cultivation Star. If these twenty Void King Realm cultivators had appeared over a dozen years earlier, Dark Star's cultivators would have driven away the people from Great Wilderness Star Domain long ago. How could they have had to fight bitterly until now?

The saying that one should look at someone with new eyes after three days apart had originally been only that, a saying. But on today's Dark Star, if one went three days without seeing someone, one might truly have to look at them anew, because they might already have broken through a realm.

At the front of the great hall, a dozen or so people stood shoulder to shoulder, looking up into the void. They were the leaders of the dozen or so strongest Sects on Dark Star, with Ye Xijun in the center and the others spread out to her left and right.

"He still hasn't woken up?" Qian Tong, Hall Master of Yingyue Hall, looked at the enormous image in the sky and thought back to when Yang Kai had first arrived. It seemed like yesterday, and he could not help feeling wistful. Back then, how could he ever have imagined that green young man would one day grow to a height where he could stand shoulder to shoulder with Heaven and Earth? The past was like smoke. Thinking of it now, it almost felt unreal.

"He's been asleep for a month already. Nothing could have gone wrong, could it?" Gu Zhen, Sect Master of Qiantian Sect, asked, worry showing on his face.

Gong Aofu said, "With the Star Lord's nourishing, the longer it lasts, the better. How could anything go wrong? We should be hoping he sleeps a little longer."

"He's awake," Ye Xijun suddenly said, looking up.

Hearing this, everyone turned their attention toward the edge of the sky.

Sure enough, the enormous image that had been sleeping there without moving all this time turned over, then slowly sat up, covered its mouth, and yawned, its face blank with drowsy confusion. Although it was only an enormous image, it seemed to project Yang Kai's current expression and movements with lifelike precision, without the slightest difference.

"That stinking brat!" Dong Suzhu cursed through gritted teeth, her eyes a little red. "This old lady came all this way to find him, and he actually refused to show his face for a whole month. He definitely isn't my son."

Fourth Master Yang was shocked. "Then who gave birth to him... hiss..." The words had only just left his mouth when Dong Suzhu kicked him, making him bare his teeth in pain.

"Make sure you give him a proper lesson later!" Dong Suzhu instructed Su Yan with a murderous look.

"Mother, I'll remember," Su Yan said, nodding obediently.

"Little Su Yan is still the obedient one. Aih, how wonderful it would have been if I had given birth to a daughter. Giving birth to a son was the same as not giving birth at all." After complaining for a while, she suddenly glared at Fourth Master Yang.

"What!" Fourth Master Yang's heart jumped, and he thought to himself, I am standing here perfectly well, so how have I provoked you this time? You kicked me and I didn't even make a sound, yet you still want more?

"This is all because of the sin you committed!" Dong Suzhu said through gritted teeth.

Fourth Master Yang protested, feeling wronged, "What does this have to do with me?"

Dong Suzhu said, "If it weren't for you... if it weren't for you... how could I have given birth to a son like this? He has worried this old lady half to death."

Fourth Master Yang forced out a faint smile, but it looked worse than crying, and he suddenly felt as if life was no longer worth living.

At the side, Su Yan covered her mouth and laughed softly. Among all the married couples in the world, there were probably not many who could still be like this after so many years together.

"Just looking at you makes me angry!" Dong Suzhu seemed to have fire pent up in her heart with nowhere else to vent it, so she could only take it out on Fourth Master Yang.

Fourth Master Yang's eyes shifted, and an idea suddenly came to him. He leaned forward, his Divine Sense surging, and softly whispered something by Dong Suzhu's ear.

Dong Suzhu's face immediately reddened. Pinching his waist, she said, "What are you saying? You're old and improper."

"Come on, come on." Fourth Master Yang tugged her away.

"I'm not going. Aiya, stop pulling me. There are so many people here. You may not care about face, but I still do."

The two of them bickered noisily all the way and soon disappeared.

Su Yan's cheeks were slightly red. Fourth Master Yang had thought no one would know what he had said, but how could he have expected that Su Yan's current cultivation was actually at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm? Even Divine Sense sound transmission could not block her perception.

Not that she had done it on purpose. It had only been an accident.

Giving Yang Kai a little sister... that was actually rather worth looking forward to.

Up in the sky, Yang Kai finally came back to his senses. One could tell at a glance that he had just woken up dazed and was slowly gathering his thoughts. He turned his head and looked around, then suddenly stared down from high above.

His gaze seemed able to pierce through the barrier of countless thousands of li, taking in everything in Lingxiao Sect. Then he smiled faintly.

Everyone felt he was smiling at them. No matter where they were, and no matter what angle they watched him from, the feeling was the same.

Only Su Yan saw her own reflection in those eyes.

Crash...

The enormous image suddenly broke apart, transforming into rich, pure Spiritual Qi that filled Heaven and Earth.

High above Lingxiao Sect's square, a figure appeared, standing in the air, black hair and robes flying. In an instant, every eye was drawn to him.

Immediately afterward, voices rang out together like a collapsing mountain, like rolling thunder.

"Greetings, Sect Master Yang!"

Facing this Star Lord who could stand shoulder to shoulder with Heaven and Earth, who had brought earth-shaking changes to all of Dark Star in the short span of a month, everyone offered their highest respect.

Yang Kai swept out a hand. A clear breeze passed through, and everyone involuntarily straightened.

Yang Kai looked around, his gaze passing over familiar and unfamiliar faces alike, then smiled and said, "Today, the heroes of my Dark Star have gathered from every road. This is a blessing as great as the heavens, and such a chance is rare. I shall drink three hundred cups with all of you!" With a wave of his hand, he declared, "Let the banquet last three days. We won't go home until we're drunk!"

"Many thanks, Sect Master Yang!"

Even without Yang Kai's order, Lingxiao Sect had long since made preparations. After all, Yang Kai's return was an event as great as the heavens, and he had also swept away the foreign enemies who had invaded Dark Star, freeing everyone from the suffering of more than a dozen years of war. This was precisely the time to celebrate and reward them.

Lingxiao Sect was Yang Kai's Sect, and on Dark Star, its position was that of an overlord. No one could shake it.

Naturally, this reward banquet could only be held in Lingxiao Sect. No one objected, no one dared object, and that was even more true now that Yang Kai himself had spoken.

The enormous square was quickly filled with tables, chairs, and benches. The venue, large enough to accommodate several thousand people, was packed to the brim. Among them were not only Lingxiao Sect disciples, but also disciples from other Sects who had come with their elders.

As for the leaders of the major Sects and the cultivators with profound cultivation, they were arranged inside the great hall, separate from the square outside.

An endless stream of rare delicacies and fine dishes was soon served, filling the tables. Jar after jar of good wine was opened, and the fragrance spread freely, just like the joy of Dark Star's cultivators at that moment, as if it meant to drift beyond the Starry Sky.

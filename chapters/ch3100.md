## Chapter 3100 — The Day a Salted Fish Turns Over

That glance shocked everyone, because the young man at the head of the three was actually studying them with great interest.

Hiss...

A chorus of sharp inhalations rang out. How was this possible?

Coming from a Star Domain to the Ancestral Domain meant traveling through the Void. Because this kind of traversal crossed an extremely vast distance, it always came with aftereffects, the most obvious being an unsettled Divine Soul, dizziness, and blurred vision.

Depending on one's strength, these aftereffects lasted for different lengths of time. In mild cases they would settle within a few breaths; in severe cases, they might take a dozen breaths or more.

Yet from the look of that young man, he showed no aftereffects whatsoever!

What was going on? Everyone was stunned.

When they swept him again with Divine Sense, their expressions turned peculiar.

This young man actually did not have the slightest fluctuation of Spiritual Qi on him. How could any cultivator lack Spiritual Qi fluctuations? Either the gap in cultivation was so great that the weaker side could not see through him, or he possessed some Secret Treasure that concealed his cultivation.

But what kind of good Secret Treasure could a cultivator from a Star Domain possibly have? With so many people present, how could they fail to see through it?

He really was a strange fellow.

A moment later, He Yunxiang and Yan Luo gradually recovered as well. The instant He Yunxiang saw their surroundings, her beautiful face paled, and the Saint Yuan throughout her body surged as she warily looked around.

She had not expected to face such a situation the moment she stepped into the Ancestral Domain. Moreover, judging from the cultivation of the surrounding cultivators, not one of them was below the Void King Realm, and the auras emanating from quite a few of them made her tremble with fear!

Above the Void King Realm! This was the aura of someone above the Void King Realm. It had to be the Dao Origin Realm Sir had mentioned!

Had they reached the Ancestral Domain? If they had not, how could so many cultivators above the Void King Realm be gathered here? But why were they blocking this place? Could they be here to rob travelers? What could cultivators from a Star Domain possibly have that would catch their eye? The two sides were simply not on the same level.

Was it fun for adults to rob children of their things?

While she was on guard, Yan Luo suddenly moved and strode off to the side.

He Yunxiang was stunned, while Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and watched Yan Luo's back.

Over there, three people stood on a small mound, two men and one woman. The one in the lead was a young man who rode the wind in midair, handsome and carefree, his aura deep and profound. Beside him was a woman under thirty with a faint smile, while the last was a very old man with crane-white hair, a youthful face, and a ruddy complexion.

Yan Luo looked excited. When he reached the mound, he dropped to one knee and said in a trembling voice, "Yan Luo greets Old Ancestor!"

As soon as these words came out, He Yunxiang's expression changed drastically. She stared blankly at the aged old man, and a flash of insight crossed her mind. She immediately understood exactly who he was.

It was rumored that five hundred years ago, the Yan Family's Old Ancestor had gone to the Ancestral Domain, and no one knew whether he lived or died. Since Yan Luo had addressed this old man that way the moment he arrived, the old man's identity was obvious.

He was none other than the Yan Family Old Ancestor who had left the Wuji Star Domain five hundred years ago, Yan Renhao!

A bad feeling immediately welled up in He Yunxiang's heart. Five hundred years ago, this Yan Renhao had already been the strongest expert in the Wuji Star Domain, at the peak of the Third Stage of the Void King Realm. Now that five hundred years had passed, and he had spent them cultivating in the Ancestral Domain, he had likely long since advanced beyond the Void King Realm.

This was simply a trap!

No wonder Yan Luo had agreed so readily to bring Yang Kai to the Ancestral Domain. No wonder he had taken the initiative to come along. So this was what had been waiting here all along.

She did not know what method the Yan Family had used to contact Yan Renhao, but Yan Renhao could not possibly be waiting here without reason. Clearly, after their group left Taiyi Star, someone from the Yan Family had sent word to this side of the Ancestral Domain.

Yan Renhao had precisely waited here to welcome Yan Luo's arrival.

This was bad! Sir's cultivation was indeed extraordinary, and he did not fear the Yan Family on Taiyi Star, but could he defeat Yan Renhao? What was more, Yan Renhao seemed to have two helpers beside him. The woman was one thing; she was only at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and He Yunxiang could probably handle her. But the young man felt even stronger than Yan Renhao. Could Sir really be his opponent?

In that instant, countless thoughts flashed through He Yunxiang's mind. Her face grim, she glanced at Yang Kai, only to find him perfectly calm and free of the slightest anxiety. She could not help being startled.

"Good, good, good!"

Over there, Yan Renhao laughed heartily, stepped forward, and reached out to help Yan Luo up. After looking him over from head to toe, he patted Yan Luo's shoulder with an approving look. "Not bad, not bad!"

Since a descendant of the Yan Family had come here, he was naturally happy. Before this, no one from the Yan Family had ever successfully reached the Ancestral Domain. In this world, although his strength had greatly increased, his life was far less carefree and unrestrained than it had been in the Wuji Star Domain. Yan Luo's arrival was a pleasant surprise. If the two of them joined hands and worked hard in the future, it might not be impossible to extend the Yan Family's prestige into the Ancestral Domain.

"Seeing Old Ancestor safe and well puts Yan Luo at ease," Yan Luo said excitedly. "Old Ancestor, your cultivation..."

Yan Renhao chuckled and waved his hand. "Let us not discuss cultivation for now. Come, I will introduce you to a Senior." He took Yan Luo by the arm, brought him before the young man, and introduced him, "This is my Senior Brother from Sea Hall, Duanmu Qi. You may address him as Uncle Duanmu."

"Sea Hall..." Hearing this, a gleam flashed through Yan Luo's eyes, and he seemed thoughtful.

Although the Yan Family could contact their Old Ancestor, they had no way to communicate for very long, and had only contacted him once in the past five hundred years. Therefore, the Yan Family knew nothing at all about how Yan Renhao was doing in the Ancestral Domain.

Suddenly hearing Old Ancestor introduce him this way, Yan Luo immediately understood that his Old Ancestor must have joined this force called Sea Hall in the Ancestral Domain. Moreover, judging from Old Ancestor's attitude toward Duanmu Qi, he seemed somewhat respectful as well.

Yan Luo was no young man, and he certainly looked far older than Duanmu Qi, but among cultivators under Heaven, those who had attained mastery came first. Since his cultivation was inferior, he could only lower his head.

Not daring to be negligent, he hurriedly bowed and saluted. "Yan Luo of the Yan Family greets Uncle Duanmu."

Duanmu Qi smiled reservedly and nodded. "Junior Nephew, there is no need to be polite. From now on, we are one family."

Yan Renhao said, "Your Uncle Duanmu's strength ranks in the top ten of all Sea Hall. Back then, it was also thanks to his recommendation that I was able to join Sea Hall. In the future, you must seek his guidance often."

"Yes!" Yan Luo answered solemnly.

"This is your Aunt Shen Cuiling." Yan Renhao pointed at the woman.

Yan Luo thought to himself that the other party was merely at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, equal to his own cultivation. If they truly fought, it was not certain who would win. How had she somehow become a whole generation above him? In the Wuji Star Domain, he was an overlord-like existence standing at the very peak. When had he ever been beneath anyone? However, seeing how intimate she was with Duanmu Qi, he immediately understood the reason and saluted solemnly. "Greetings, Aunt Shen."

Shen Cuiling pursed her lips and smiled. "Junior Nephew, there is no need to be polite. You are Senior Brother Yan's descendant. Once you enter Sea Hall in the future, we will naturally look after you. I did not prepare much for our first meeting, so I will give you this bottle of Pills. I imagine it will be of some help to your future cultivation."

"This..." Although Yan Luo was tempted, he was also somewhat embarrassed to accept something the first time they met, so he could only look to his Old Ancestor for guidance.

Yan Renhao said, "Your Aunt Shen is an Alchemist. These Spiritual Pills were all refined by her personally. Since she is giving them to you, take them."

"Thank you, Aunt Shen!" Yan Luo hurriedly thanked her and received the bottle with both hands, greatly excited. Coming to the Ancestral Domain had indeed been the correct choice. He had only just arrived and had already obtained a bottle of Pills. Although he did not know what grade they were or what effects they had, since they came from the Ancestral Domain, how could they be poor?

As these few people exchanged a few words, the surrounding cultivators heard enough to understand their relationship. They sighed inwardly, knowing there was likely no chance to snatch anyone from Sea Hall's hands.

Since their Old Ancestor was in Sea Hall, they would certainly join Sea Hall. How could they possibly choose another force?

As for the two people who had come with Yan Luo, they should also be from the same Star Domain. With that connection, they definitely would not have any other choice either, so everyone abandoned the thought of recruiting them.

Under ordinary circumstances, cultivators from the same Star Domain would band together for mutual support, unless there were grudges between them.

But if there were grudges, how could they have come to the Ancestral Domain together?

After the few people from Sea Hall finished exchanging greetings, Yan Renhao and Yan Luo exchanged a glance, and Divine Sense seemed to stir between them.

In the next instant, Yan Renhao looked toward Yang Kai and He Yunxiang with a beaming smile and said, "Since you two came here together with Yan Luo, come with this old man."

This place was crowded with too many eyes watching. Even if he intended to deal with Yang Kai, he could not possibly make a move here, lest other forces seize upon some pretext and denounce him. But once they left this place and returned to the Sea Hall, would Yang Kai not be his to flatten or knead however he pleased?

You actually dared kill my Yan Family Elder and destroy my Yan Family's foundation. How could I possibly let you off lightly? Do you truly think a little strength lets you act however you please? If I call you a frog at the bottom of a well, then that is exactly what you are. Do not dream of jumping out of that well mouth in this lifetime.

First, however, he had to think of a way to get them to Sea Hall.

Yan Luo also smiled and said, "Brother Yang, please? Yan Luo was able to come to the Ancestral Domain thanks to Brother Yang's care along the way, so please be sure to give this Yan a chance to repay you."

Gone were his previous servility and caution. His own Old Ancestor stood behind him, and Uncle Duanmu was here to hold the line. Why would he need to fear Yang Kai? In his view, no matter how strong Yang Kai was, he could not possibly be stronger than his own Old Ancestor and Duanmu Qi.

Previously, at the Yan Family, Yang Kai had bullied others by relying on his power. Now the wheel of fortune had turned, and Yan Luo would let him taste what it was like to be bullied.

He Yunxiang's pretty face darkened as she stared at Yan Luo in anger.

Although the matter of the Yan Family had all been within Yang Kai's calculations, in the end, the Yan Family had only brought it upon themselves. If the Yan Family had agreed to her initial proposal, she believed Yang Kai definitely would not have treated the Yan Family unfairly and would certainly have given them sufficiently generous compensation. Unfortunately, the Yan Family had been arrogant and ignorant, courting death on their own. How could they blame others?

What was more, Yang Kai clearly had the power to sweep the Yan Family flat, yet he had not used his strength to bully the weak. In the end, however, he had still fallen into the Yan Family's schemes. Now that Yan Luo had gained the upper hand, he immediately revealed such a petty, vile face, truly making He Yunxiang feel contempt for him.

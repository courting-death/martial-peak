## Chapter 2711 — Why Should I Save You?

“You all must have heard it as well.” The man swept his hand, and the many Emperor Realm cultivators all nodded.

Each of them had sharp ears and keen eyes. Although Yang Kai hadn’t spoken loudly, how could they not have heard him?

“Sir Yang, you mustn’t play favorites. If you truly have a way to expel it, please, you must help us as well.” The man pleaded.

Anyone with a Gu inside them would find it impossible to feel at ease. Even though Tan Junhao was already dead, who could say what dangers the Gu left behind might pose? If it ever acted up, they might not even know how they died.

Now that they heard Yang Kai could expel it, they all clung to him like drowning men grasping at a lifeline, refusing to let go no matter what.

“Sigh...” Yang Kai let out a heavy sigh, looking helpless. “Fine, I did say that just now. I do have a way to expel the Heart-Controlling Gu!”

“Truly?” Everyone was overjoyed, immediately turning back and landing in front of Yang Kai, gazing at him eagerly.

Just moments ago, they had all been desperate to leave. Now, even if Yang Kai tried to drive them away, he probably couldn’t.

“The Heart-Controlling Gu ranks high on the Earth List of the Strange Insects Manual. It’s vicious and well-hidden—even Emperor Realm cultivators can’t easily expel it. If left alone for too long, it will devour your heart meridian and consume your Essence-Blood. Tsk tsk, by then, not even the gods could save you.”

Who knew if Yang Kai was exaggerating, but everyone’s faces turned pale as they listened, their hearts pounding with anxiety.

During this time, everyone had tried using Emperor Yuan to expel the Heart-Controlling Gu, but it was better not to touch it at all. The moment they did, the Gu would burrow toward their heart meridian. Now, not only could they not expel it, they even had to be extremely careful when circulating Emperor Yuan, for fear of disturbing the Gu.

“As it happens... I just so happen to know a method to expel this thing.” Yang Kai smiled faintly.

“Please, Young Master Yang, save us!”

Everyone hurriedly bowed in supplication.

Yang Kai’s face darkened as he said coldly, “Why should I save you? Give me a reason!”

Everyone was stunned, left speechless. Just now, Yang Kai’s long speech had made it seem like he was willing to help them with the Heart-Controlling Gu, but in the blink of an eye, he’d changed completely, leaving everyone at a loss.

But thinking about it, it wasn’t unreasonable. Yang Kai had no ties to any of them and didn’t seem like the type to go out of his way for others. Why should he save them?

Yang Kai said, “Earlier, you all sided with Tan Junhao against me. If I didn’t have some skill, I’d have been killed by you long ago. But I’ve always been magnanimous and know you were forced into it, so I can’t be bothered to hold it against you. I even used an Imperial Treasure to protect you, sparing you from Tan Junhao’s control. I don’t need you to repay me for saving your lives—that’s already more than enough. Now you want me to go even further and expend my own effort to help you expel the Gu? Isn’t that going too far?”

The crowd was left speechless, faces flushed with shame.

Though Yang Kai’s words were sharp, every one of them was true. They had no way to refute him.

These people had indeed been his enemies before, and he had saved their lives. Now they wanted him to help them expel the Gu as well. Wasn’t that just pushing their luck and making unreasonable demands?

For a moment, everyone looked at each other awkwardly.

Hua Yulu’s beautiful eyes sparkled. She smiled gently and said, “Young Master Yang, all these fellow cultivators are Sect Masters, Sect Leaders, and Family Heads of the major sects in the Southern Territory. If the Heart-Controlling Gu isn’t expelled and it acts up in the future, their lives will be in danger. At that point, the sects will be left leaderless, and chaos will erupt in the Southern Territory. Young Master Yang, you are compassionate—please, you must help them.”

She had seen through Yang Kai’s intentions—he was playing hard to get.

If he truly didn’t want to help expel the Gu, he wouldn’t have stayed behind or said what he did. He clearly wanted everyone to know he had this ability, practically writing “come beg me” on his face.

Now, he was just waiting for the right offer before acting.

Though Hua Yulu was a woman, her mind was sharp and perceptive. How could she not see through this? That’s why she secretly fanned the flames and played along.

“Palace Master Hua is absolutely right. Sir Yang, please, you must help us!” Whether or not they saw through Yang Kai’s intentions, everyone earnestly pleaded, and many felt grateful to Hua Yulu.

Of the two or three dozen Emperor Realm cultivators present, only Hua Yulu had any real connection with Yang Kai. Her willingness to speak up naturally earned their gratitude.

Yang Kai put on a troubled expression, but in his heart he gave Hua Yulu a hundred and twenty silent praises. What a clever and perceptive woman, he thought. He couldn’t help but wonder what exactly her relationship with Xia Sheng was—if he took advantage of her later, he’d better make sure Xia Sheng never found out.

Observing Yang Kai’s less resolute attitude, everyone sensed hope and pressed their pleas even harder. For a moment, it sounded like hundreds of ducks quacking at once.

“Alright, alright!” Yang Kai raised his hand to cut off their clamor, frowning. “I can indeed expel the Gu, but it will cost me dearly. With so many people needing help, I’ll have to use my own Essence-Blood. Afterward, I might not be able to fight anyone for three to five years.”

That was clearly an exaggeration, but no one dared to argue. Whatever he said now was the law—they just let it go in one ear and out the other.

Yang Kai glanced sidelong at the crowd. “After all this effort, surely you don’t expect me to work for nothing?”

In the end, that was the real point. Hua Yulu covered her mouth and laughed quietly.

Everyone wiped the sweat from their brows. One person cautiously asked, “Then, Sir Yang, what do you want?”

“What do you have?” Yang Kai shot back.

Hua Yulu immediately stepped forward, producing a Spatial Ring and offering it with both hands. “Young Master Yang, I brought twelve million Upper-Level Source Crystals for the auction. If you don’t mind, please accept them as payment for expelling the Heart-Controlling Gu for me.”

“This...” Yang Kai hesitated. “Sister Hua, you misunderstand me. I’m not trying to profit from your gratitude!”

Everyone’s faces darkened at his words, silently cursing him in their hearts. Wasn’t this just taking advantage and then pretending otherwise? He’d just asked what they could offer, and now he dared claim he wasn’t seeking repayment—how shameless could he be?

Hua Yulu smiled gently. “Young Master Yang, I know you are righteous. But I have nothing else to offer. With your strength, you wouldn’t care for any treasures I have. If you won’t even accept these Source Crystals, then... I can only offer myself.”

As she spoke, two blushes appeared on her cheeks, and she looked bashful, her beautiful eyes almost brimming with tears.

Yang Kai’s face darkened with exasperation, but he reached out and took Hua Yulu’s ring, saying with a laugh, “Since Sister Hua insists, it would be rude of me to refuse. Haha…”

Hua Yulu immediately shot him a reproachful look, her expression full of grievance.

Yang Kai’s expression turned serious. “The Gu must be expelled without interruption.  Sister Hua, come with me.”

With that, he activated his Emperor Yuan and flew toward a nearby room that was still intact. Hua Yulu followed closely behind.

“Sir Yang, what about us?” someone called from behind.

“Wait!”

Once inside, Yang Kai found a spot to sit cross-legged and pointed in front of him, saying to Hua Yulu, “Sit.”

Hua Yulu nodded slightly, her graceful figure settling in front of Yang Kai.

Yang Kai picked up the ring and placed it before Hua Yulu, smiling. “I really must thank you, Sister Hua.”

Without her cooperation, things wouldn’t have gone so smoothly. Those Emperor Realm cultivators had been forced into it, but they had still been his enemies. Yang Kai wouldn’t kill them, but he wasn’t about to let them off cheaply either. He’d planned to extort them anyway—after all, they’d all come for the auction, and who didn’t have tens of millions of Upper-Level Source Crystals?

Unexpectedly, Hua Yulu was so tactful.

Accepting her ring earlier was just for show. Yang Kai had no intention of actually taking her Source Crystals.

Glancing at the Spatial Ring but not taking it, Hua Yulu smiled faintly. If Young Master Yang doesn’t want the Source Crystals, do you really want me instead?" she sighed. "I’m nothing special—just afraid you’d find me unworthy..."

As she spoke, her mood turned gloomy.

Yang Kai felt overwhelmed. This woman was a natural actress—if she kept going, he might really lose control. He quickly wiped the sweat from his brow and forced a laugh. “Sister Hua, you misunderstand. You’ve helped me several times—how could I think ill of you? I only feel grateful.”

Hua Yulu looked as if she was about to cry. “So you do look down on me. Forget it, forget it. With your charm, you must be surrounded by beauties—how could you ever notice someone like me...?”

“Enough.” Yang Kai sighed heavily, looking helpless.

Only then did Hua Yulu giggle, saying, “Alright, I’ll stop teasing you.”

She found it all quite amusing. In the past few days, Yang Kai had been like a fierce beast, awe-inspiring and impossible to look at directly. Only today did she realize that, beneath that ferocious exterior, this young man was actually quite interesting.

Putting away the Spatial Ring, Hua Yulu said, “Then I must thank you, Young Master Yang.”

Twelve million Upper-Level Source Crystals was a huge fortune for her sect. If Yang Kai really took it, her disciples’ cultivation would surely suffer.

“What should I do?” Hua Yulu asked seriously, no longer joking around.

“Just empty your mind and relax,” Yang Kai said. “There may be some awkwardness in a moment, so please don’t blame me.”

Hua Yulu glanced at him curiously but didn’t ask further. She obediently closed her eyes and relaxed as instructed.

Yang Kai immediately began his work.

Not long after, Hua Yulu suddenly spat out a mouthful of blood.

When she opened her eyes again, her gaze was like spring water, her cheeks flushed, and even her fair neck was tinged with red.

She hadn’t expected Yang Kai’s so-called awkwardness to involve tapping her sensitive chest area. The pressure and heat from his fingertips made her feel unbearably shy. If not for his earlier words, she would have thought he was just a lecher.

Still, since Yang Kai hadn’t shown any improper intentions before, it was clear he wasn’t taking advantage of her.

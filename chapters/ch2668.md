## Chapter 2668 — Suspicion

"Brother Yang!" Although Du Xian was not as emotional as Ye Qinghan, he was also visibly moved as he cupped his fists.

As long as Yang Kai was here, they had a chance to leave this place, rather than being trapped in Emperor Heaven Valley for the rest of their lives. Although Thousand Leaves Sect had been wiped out for no reason and their enemy was powerful, as the Senior Brother of the younger generation, he had both the responsibility and the duty to avenge his Sect.

To seek revenge, the first step was to increase his strength, and the second was to leave this place.

Improving his strength was his own matter, but to leave here, he could only rely on Yang Kai.

"Miss Ye, Brother Du..." Yang Kai nodded slightly and sighed, "Please accept my condolences."

He didn't know what else to say. Anyone who suffered the calamity of their Sect being exterminated would surely be in a foul mood. He also understood Ye Qinghan's loss of composure.

After a few exchanges between the three, the disciples of Thousand Leaves Sect who were unaware of the truth began to sense something. Soon, murmurs arose, and everyone guessed Yang Kai's identity.

After hiding here, Du Xian had told them not to lose hope or give up easily. They still had a chance to leave, a chance to avenge their Sect, as long as they waited for someone named Yang Kai to arrive.

Now that this Yang Kai had arrived, the disciples of Thousand Leaves Sect were all invigorated, their gazes toward Yang Kai burning with excitement.

"Is everyone here?" Yang Kai swept his gaze around but did not see the people he wanted to meet. Although it was within his expectations, he couldn't help but feel a little disappointed.

Not many from Thousand Leaves Sect had escaped this time—at most, five hundred people. Now, all had gathered in this valley, but their cultivation levels varied greatly, with fewer than ten Dao Origin Realm cultivators among them.

"Young Master Yang, please come inside to talk!" Ye Qinghan, having regained some composure, wiped the corners of her eyes and gestured invitingly.

Yang Kai nodded and walked toward the cave, with Yingfei following closely behind, his aura concealed, making it impossible to gauge his strength.

The cave had been tidied up. For over a year, Ye Qinghan, Du Xian, and the others had lived here, harboring thoughts of revenge and cultivating diligently, hoping that one day they could personally slay their enemies.

There was little furniture in the cave, and everyone sat on the ground. Besides Yang Kai and Yingfei, only Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were present. It seemed that among the surviving disciples of Thousand Leaves Sect, only these two held the highest cultivation and status.

"What exactly happened? I only heard that Thousand Leaves Sect was destroyed by Flowing Shadow Sword Sect. Is this true?" Yang Kai asked.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian exchanged glances. The former nodded lightly, and the latter spoke, "Brother Yang, what you heard is correct. The one who destroyed our Thousand Leaves Sect was indeed Flowing Shadow Sword Sect."

"There's a rumor outside that Flowing Shadow Sword Sect coveted the existence of the Heavenly Puppets. Is that true as well?"

Du Xian said angrily, "Flowing Shadow Sword Sect focuses solely on the sword path. Even if our Thousand Leaves Sect had Heavenly Puppets, how could that tempt them? There is a mastermind behind this!"

Yang Kai raised his brow and said, "What do you mean by that?"

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian exchanged another look, seeming hesitant to speak.

Yang Kai said, "It's nothing. Since I'm here, I naturally want to get to the bottom of this. I have friends in Thousand Leaves Sect as well."

Upon hearing this, Du Xian finally spoke, "Brother Yang... do you still remember Hua Qingsi?"

Yang Kai frowned, "Sister Hua?"

How could he forget Hua Qingsi? But from Du Xian's words, it seemed that the destruction of Thousand Leaves Sect was somehow related to her. This made him suspicious. Moreover, after parting ways with Hua Qingsi in Shattered Star Sea, they had never met again.

Yang Kai also didn't know where Hua Qingsi had gone.

Sister Hua knew many of his secrets, especially about the Small Xuan Realm. However, he trusted Hua Qingsi, which was why he had given her freedom and even gifted her a precious Supreme Mystic Pill.

Du Xian said, "After the end of Shattered Star Sea, Hua Qingsi suddenly came to Thousand Leaves Sect. It was said that she had an agreement with Brother Yang to wait for you at Thousand Leaves Sect."

Yang Kai nodded, "That's right. That did happen."

Back in Shattered Star Sea, when he parted ways with Hua Qingsi, Yang Kai had told her that if she couldn't find him in the future, she should wait for him at Thousand Leaves Sect, since Chi Yue and the others were still there, and he would definitely return.

It now seemed that Sister Hua had indeed kept her promise. After leaving Shattered Star Sea, she had come to Thousand Leaves Sect.

Du Xian's expression turned odd as he asked, "Brother Yang, do you know Lady Hua's true background?"

Yang Kai replied, "A disciple of Star God Palace."

This was no secret. He had known for a long time. Hua Qingsi had been ordered by her master to investigate the death of Han Leng, a traitor of Star God Palace, in Fenglin City. She had encountered Yang Kai, who then captured her into the Small Xuan Realm and made her his soul slave.

Du Xian was surprised, "Brother Yang, you actually knew?"

He had thought Yang Kai knew nothing about this. He had believed Hua Qingsi had used Yang Kai, which was why he hesitated to speak. But now, it seemed things were not as he thought.

Now, he didn't know what to think.

Yang Kai frowned, "Brother Du, just speak plainly. I trust Sister Hua's character. She shouldn't be involved in Thousand Leaves Sect's destruction."

If he didn't trust her, he wouldn't have set her free. With her soul imprint in his hand, he could control her life and death at any time.

After a moment's thought, Du Xian finally said, "Brother Yang, when Lady Hua returned to Thousand Leaves Sect, she was already an Emperor. But she said she had an agreement with you to wait at Thousand Leaves Sect, so Master let her stay. During that time, she became very close with Chi Yue and several Honored Guests."

Chi Yue and the others' relationship with Yang Kai was naturally known to Hua Qingsi, so it was only natural for them to be close. Moreover, after the trip to Shattered Star Sea, Hua Qingsi had indeed advanced to the Emperor Realm, not wasting the Supreme Mystic Pill Yang Kai had given her.

"Everything was peaceful at first. Our Thousand Leaves Sect gained many lost secrets, and with Lady Hua, an Emperor Realm cultivator, as a guest, the Sect flourished." Du Xian's face showed nostalgia, as if recalling those days. "But fate is cruel. One day, a powerful expert came to Thousand Leaves Sect, looking for Lady Hua. We couldn't see through his cultivation, but he was definitely an Emperor."

"No one knows what was said between him and Lady Hua, but it was then that we learned she was a disciple of Star God Palace, and even a disciple under one of its Elders."

"After that man left, the Sect Master went to see Lady Hua, but she had disappeared. No one knew where she went. But since she was from Star God Palace and an Emperor, no one thought she would be in danger, so we didn't worry. But a month later, Flowing Shadow Sword Sect suddenly attacked and destroyed our Thousand Leaves Sect."

Yang Kai listened for a long time before saying with a grim face, "So you suspect this is related to Sister Hua? How does that connect?"

Du Xian said, "It's not just suspicion. On the day our Sect fell, that expert who had come for Lady Hua also took action. I saw him from afar. Though I couldn't see clearly, I couldn't mistake his figure. He was definitely at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. If not for that, even with all our Heavenly Puppets, Thousand Leaves Sect wouldn't have been so easily destroyed."

Ye Qinghan nodded from the side, "That's right. I saw that man too. Most of our Heavenly Puppets were subdued by him, which is why we couldn't resist Flowing Shadow Sword Sect!"

Hearing this, Yang Kai's face darkened. No wonder Thousand Leaves Sect couldn't resist even with their Heavenly Puppets—there was a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert involved. But... what was really going on?

If that Second Stage Emperor Realm expert was from Star God Palace, he only needed to find Hua Qingsi. She was a disciple of Star God Palace, not a traitor, just someone who failed to complete her master's task. There was no reason to destroy Thousand Leaves Sect out of anger.

Vaguely, Yang Kai sensed a conspiracy at work.

Looking up at Du Xian, Yang Kai said, "Brother Du, the mastermind you mentioned earlier, could it be..."

He pointed to the sky.

Du Xian smiled bitterly and shook his head, "I don't know. But Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had no reason to trouble Thousand Leaves Sect. Someone must have instigated them."

He had previously suspected Hua Qingsi and Star God Palace, but thinking it over, if Star God Palace really wanted to destroy Thousand Leaves Sect, it would have been easy. Star God Palace is the overlord of the Southern Territory, a Great Emperor's Sect. Crushing Thousand Leaves Sect would be effortless.

For a while, everyone fell silent.

"Young Master Yang, since you came from outside, have you heard any news of my father?" Ye Qinghan asked anxiously, looking at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai shook his head, "I've only just returned to the Southern Territory and only know the general situation. I don't know the specifics. Sect Master Ye isn't dead?"

Ye Qinghan, on the verge of tears, said, "I don't know. That day, Father sent us into Emperor Heaven Valley and destroyed the Space Array outside. We don't know what happened afterward."

Du Xian said, "Master wanted to send Chi Yue and the other Honored Guests in as well, but they refused and instead fought to defend the Sect with the other disciples. Now... their fate is unknown."

Yang Kai nodded slightly. Each of those people—Chi Yue, Ghost Ancestor, and Gu Cangyun—had been overlords in the Hengluo Star Domain. Though they couldn't fully display their strength in the Star Realm, they were well cared for in Thousand Leaves Sect. With the Sect in danger, it was only natural for them to fight.

"Don't worry about them. Since they made that choice, whether they live or die is up to them." Yang Kai sighed solemnly. For now, only Ai Ou was confirmed alive. The fates of Chi Yue, Ghost Ancestor, and Gu Cangyun were unknown, which troubled him.

Standing up, he said, "A month from now, Flowing Shadow Sword Sect will hold an auction in Flowing Shadow City. The items for sale are your Thousand Leaves Sect's Heavenly Puppets. Do you want to go and see?"

"What?" Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were both shocked.

Yang Kai sneered, "This auction is the key. As long as we go, the truth will come to light."

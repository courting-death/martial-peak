## Chapter 3091 — Slow

For the moment, He Yunxiang had no time to examine the cultivation method, but she believed that since it had come from the hands of the Sir before her, it certainly would not be poor. At the very least, it would be better than what she had originally cultivated.

As for the quality of the colored-ribbon Secret Treasure before her, that was clear at a glance.

She reached out and stroked the colored ribbon, sensing the aura fluctuations spreading from within it. Excitement appeared on her face, and she said with endless yearning, "Sir truly is above the Void King Realm!"

The cultivators of the Lower Plane Star Domains were certain there were higher levels above the Void King Realm, but they had no idea what mysteries that level held, what sort of height it represented, or even what that realm was called.

Above the Void King Realm was what they called it.

This colored-ribbon Secret Treasure in her hand was definitely beyond the Void King-Grade level. Someone who could casually give away such a Secret Treasure was certainly also above the Void King Realm.

"That is called the Dao Origin Realm," Yang Kai said with a faint smile.

"Dao Origin Realm?" He Yunxiang raised her head and looked at him excitedly. For the first time, she learned the name of the realm above the Void King Realm, as if a new door had opened before her and revealed an entirely new world.

“Only after you transform all the Saint Yuan in your body into Origin Qi will you be qualified to attempt a breakthrough to the Dao Origin Realm. Work hard. The days ahead are still long, and for now, what you need to do is stabilize your own cultivation.”

He Yunxiang suppressed the excitement and longing in her heart and said respectfully, "Yes."

"Go. When we are about to reach Taiyi Star, come notify me. Do not disturb me at other times."

He Yunxiang bowed and withdrew, clutching the colored ribbon before her chest as if she were holding the most important thing in her life.

After leaving the room, she did not look for a secluded chamber to enter retreat. Instead, she sat down cross-legged right outside the door, assuming the posture of a guard down to the last detail.

Before long, Lu Huaishuang came looking for her.

She had previously gone to handle matters involving the He Family Warship, splitting off a portion of the Lu Family's people and having them take the He Family's Warship back to Morning Sun Star. The He Family Head was dead, and several hundred elites had all been lost. This news had to be sent back as quickly as possible so the Lu Family could prepare early. That way, when she returned to Morning Sun Star again, she could move directly against the He Family.

With one side weakening while the other strengthened, she believed the He Family would never be able to stir up any waves again.

Seeing He Yunxiang outside the wing room once more, Lu Huaishuang glanced over casually, only to stare blankly at her again.

They had been apart for less than four hours, yet she felt that this Gale's First Chief had changed quite a bit, as if she had become even more charming and alluring. As a fellow woman, even Lu Huaishuang could not help feeling her heart stir when she looked at her.

Immediately afterward, she was greatly shocked. "You... how are you at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm?"

Having only just advanced and broken through, He Yunxiang could not conceal the aura around her at all. Her cultivation at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm was plain for anyone to see.

Four hours ago, she had only been at the Second Stage!

He Yunxiang smiled without speaking. It was not that she was treating her broom as a treasure and unwilling to reveal secrets; it was just that Yang Kai's methods were truly somewhat inconceivable. She could not guarantee whether Lu Huaishuang would have improper thoughts after hearing about them. If this woman also wanted to attach herself to Sir, would that not mean she would have another competitor in the future?

How could Gale's First Chief go looking for trouble like that?

But although she did not say it, how could Lu Huaishuang fail to guess? Shocked, she said, "It was him?"

He Yunxiang smiled and said, "Sir is in retreat, and I need to meditate as well. If Little Sister has no business here, please return. Sir instructed that when we are about to reach Taiyi Star, you should remember to come notify him."

Lu Huaishuang's expression shifted in a complicated way for a while before she finally nodded lightly. "I understand. I should congratulate you."

Seeing He Yunxiang again, the enormous change in the other woman gave her an unimaginable shock.

Attaching oneself to an expert could bring such enormous benefits? Then what had all these years of her own persistence been for? The Lu Family's burden was too heavy, pressing down on her shoulders until it left her somewhat unable to breathe. Should she also go find an expert to attach herself to?

The person inside the room was the best choice. Since he could accept He Yunxiang, it did not necessarily mean he could not accept her. In terms of appearance, Lu Huaishuang was confident she did not lose to anyone.

This was a woman's natural advantage.

No! Other people walked their own paths. Only the path beneath her feet was hers.

Vines could climb higher only by clinging to great trees, but a sapling that received sunlight and the nourishment of rain and dew could likewise grow into a towering tree.

A shackle that had troubled her for many years loosened slightly, causing the aura of her whole person to undergo a subtle change. Her wavering beautiful eyes grew firm. Deep in her heart, she let go of something, smiled faintly, and said, "I understand. When the time comes, I will come notify you."

We are not people of the same path. You have your way of surviving, and I have my method of becoming stronger. The broad sunny road and the single-plank bridge may differ in difficulty, but in the end, all roads lead to the same destination. When we meet again in years to come, I hope you will not be surprised.

...

The pitch-black Warship started up again, its speed shifting from slow to fast as it gradually left the range of the Dead Star and sailed toward the endless, vast Star Domain.

For the first time in all these years, Yang Kai slowed down.

On the road of the Martial Dao, he had always been running hard, not daring to be the slightest bit dispirited or lazy. What spurred him on were threats from every direction and his own thirst for strength. Only after suddenly stopping did he realize that the road of life was not limited to the one beneath his feet, and that the scenery around him was also quite extraordinary.

Hiding inside the wing room, he would enter the Small Xuan Realm from time to time and chat freely for a while with Liu Yan, his Incarnation, and the others. At other times, he would brew a pot of clear tea in the room, take up a book of records, and read for several days at a stretch, leisurely and at ease.

On the path of cultivation, there had to be a proper balance of slack and tension. An outstanding archer unstrung his bow when not using it in order to preserve the lifespan of a strong bow.

The saying that books held beauties like jade and mansions of gold was nothing more than the self-delusion and consolation of poor, pedantic scholars.

But there was no denying that reading could indeed grant a person a certain spiritual sublimation.

He suddenly put down the book in his hand and thought seriously for a long time before coming to a sudden realization. "So I was too impatient!"

His cultivation had always advanced at an extremely astonishing pace. Every advancement had been enough to shatter ordinary people's imaginations, but ever since he broke through to the Emperor Realm in the Shattered Star Sea, he had been stuck at this level and unable to move forward.

Previously, he had thought that his opportunity had not yet arrived, so he could not advance.

Only now did he understand that this so-called opportunity not yet arriving was nothing more than a problem with his own state of mind. All along, he had been too impatient, urgently thirsting for power and wanting to break through the First Stage of the Emperor Realm to explore the mysteries of the Second Stage.

Desire without attainment.

That overly impatient state of mind had caused an extra barrier and shackle to appear at the peak of his First Stage Emperor Realm.

Even though he had gained some comprehension before and had intended to let nature take its course, in an environment like the Star Realm, he still consciously or unconsciously wanted his strength to increase. How could that be called letting nature take its course? Naturally, he had been unable to achieve a breakthrough.

But after coming here, not only did he not dare casually use his strength, he had also sealed away most of his cultivation and could not cultivate either. With his mind opened wide, he had only now abruptly realized where the problem lay.

This situation was somewhat like darkness under the lamp. The problem had been right before his eyes all along, but he had failed to notice it because his gaze had never been focused beneath his own feet.

Having made this trip, regardless of whether he could return to Hengluo Star Domain, this layer of understanding alone meant the journey had not been in vain.

He believed that after he returned to the Star Realm, perhaps there would be a different surprise.

He picked up the book again and began reading seriously, leisurely as if he were an old man peacefully passing his twilight years.

Knock knock knock...

A knock came from the door.

"Come in!" Yang Kai did not even raise his head.

He Yunxiang pushed the door open and entered, seeming to stir up a faint, fragrant breeze that filled the entire wing room with an enchanting scent.

"Sir, we are about to reach Taiyi Star." She had kept Yang Kai's previous instructions firmly in mind. When Lu Huaishuang sent someone over to notify her, she immediately reported the news to Yang Kai.

"Mm." Yang Kai answered casually, his gaze still fixed on the book he held in his hands.

What secret manual or treasured scripture was this? He Yunxiang could not help being a little curious. Since this Sir before her could casually take out an extremely profound cultivation method and give it to her, his foundation was clearly deep. How could something even he read with such relish possibly be poor?

Peeping at someone else was not good, especially peeping at such an expert. It was extremely likely to provoke his dislike. If that truly happened, then all her previous effort and investment would be wasted.

But a woman's curiosity was just like this: the more it was suppressed, the more fiercely it rebounded. At first, He Yunxiang could still keep her head lowered. Sir did not guard against her, but that did not mean she could casually pry into his secrets.

But after waiting for a moment, she really could not hold back and raised her eyes to glance over the book.

Three large characters leaped into view, and the corner of He Yunxiang's mouth could not help twitching.

Green Mountain Chronicles!

How could this count as any damned treasured manual or secret scripture?

Pa...

Yang Kai closed the Green Mountain Chronicles, then stood up. In a few steps, he brushed past He Yunxiang and called, "Let's go."

He Yunxiang followed closely behind him.

"Only when your aura is concealed and does not leak out can your realm be considered stable. You still need to work much harder." His voice came from ahead, and He Yunxiang nodded like a chick pecking rice, saying obediently, "Sir is right. The time was a little too short."

Only half a month had passed, which was indeed not enough for her to stabilize her realm. Although she was somewhat better now than she had been half a month ago, the aura of the Third Stage of the Void King Realm on her body was still extremely obvious. Anyone with the slightest bit of eyesight could see it.

"How is the refining of the Secret Treasure?"

"Although there has been some progress, I still cannot operate it freely."

That colored ribbon was a Dao Origin-Grade Lower-Level Secret Treasure. Yang Kai did not know where he had obtained it from. Too many cultivators had died by his hand over the years, and their belongings had naturally all become Yang Kai's spoils of war. With He Yunxiang's cultivation, having only just advanced to the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, refining a Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure was somewhat strenuous even though it was Lower-Level.

"And the cultivation method?"

"I am studying it."

"If there is anything you do not understand, ask me."

"Thank you, Sir. There are indeed many obscure parts." He Yunxiang did not stand on ceremony either. If she wanted to stay by Yang Kai's side, she had to ensure that she had useful value, and that value depended on her strength.

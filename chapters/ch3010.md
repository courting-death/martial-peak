## Chapter 3010 — Lingxiao Palace's Foundation

The Iron Blood Great Emperor. Little Yun'er's Master was actually the Iron Blood Great Emperor!

Yang Kai stood in place and pondered for a long while before letting out a carefree laugh. Although it was somewhat regrettable that he had failed to bring little Yun'er back with him, there would be plenty of time in the future and they would always have another chance to meet. Little Yun'er now had a good Master, so the road ahead of her would certainly not be as thorny and difficult as his own had been.

Hua Qingsi flew toward him from up ahead and called out with a smile from afar, "Palace Master, you've returned."

She had rushed over as soon as she received the news, with Bian Yuqing accompanying her.

When they landed nearby, Bian Yuqing appeared somewhat reserved and cupped her fists. "Greetings, Palace Master!"

If Yang Kai had not offered her his generous assistance at Linghu Palace that day, she might never have had the opportunity to advance to the Emperor Realm. Times had changed. The inexperienced young man who once stood before her had now become her direct superior and had even provided her with protection and a place to cultivate, leaving Bian Yuqing rather emotional.

However, she had adjusted her state of mind well and knew how to be grateful. She had integrated smoothly into Lingxiao Palace over these past few days and had even come to address Hua Qingsi as a sister.

Yang Kai nodded lightly, then looked at Hua Qingsi and said, "It's been a long time, but Elder Sister Hua has grown even more beautiful."

Hua Qingsi shot him a reproachful glance, though the joy in her eyes could not be concealed. "You are the Master of a Palace, so don't speak so frivolously. If someone who didn't know better heard you, who knows what they might think?"

"I'm only telling the truth." Turning to Bian Yuqing, he asked, "Are you accustomed to living in the palace?"

Bian Yuqing hurriedly replied, “Many thanks for the Palace Master’s concern. That I have found a place to settle down and build a life for myself is all thanks to the Palace Master’s kindness.”

Yang Kai waved his hand. "Once you enter Lingxiao Palace, you are one of its people. There's no need to be so polite. This will be your home from now on."

Bian Yuqing pondered for a moment before breaking into a smile.

Hua Qingsi said, "Palace Master, you've been travelling outside all this time, so Younger Sister Qing has been helping with many of the palace's affairs. When it comes to managing a Sect, she has far more experience than I do. I never had a chance to tell you, but I've already appointed her Vice Chief Steward of Lingxiao Palace."

"That's fine." Yang Kai nodded. "From now on, Protector Bian will be our Vice Chief Steward."

His word settled the matter. With Yang Kai's approval, Bian Yuqing's position as Vice Chief Steward was now official.

Although Bian Yuqing came from a small Sect and her foundation was not as solid as Hua Qingsi's, she had still been a Protector. When Biyu Sect's Sect Master, Wu Mengchuan, was imprisoned, she and the other three Protectors had managed Biyu Sect, so she had naturally accumulated a great deal of experience.

Hua Qingsi had indeed learned much from her during the time they had spent together.

Although Lingxiao Palace did not have many people at present, and most of them were disciples of Thousand Leaves Sect, its foundation was immense, and sooner or later it would become a top Sect. By that time, it might have tens or even hundreds of thousands of disciples, so people would inevitably be needed to manage them.

It was best to become familiar with such matters now so that they would not be overwhelmed in the future.

Bian Yuqing said in alarm, “Palace Master, I have done nothing to deserve such favor. Being allowed to join Lingxiao Palace is the greatest blessing of my life. In life, I belong to Lingxiao Palace; in death, I will be a ghost of Lingxiao Palace. Should the Palace ever have need of me, I would not hesitate to brave boiling water or raging flames. But as for the position of Vice Chief Steward, I truly am unworthy of it.”

This was not modesty, but the honest truth.

When she first arrived at Lingxiao Palace, she had not thought much of it. She merely felt that living here in the future would be a good choice. Setting aside the debt of gratitude she owed Yang Kai, this blessed land alone was far beyond anything the former Biyu Sect could compare with. Cultivating and living here was something any cultivator would yearn for.

However, as time passed and she learned more about Lingxiao Palace's foundation, she discovered that she had underestimated this still-incomplete Sect.

Three Yao Kings stood guard here, each comparable to a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master.

Great Emperor Miaodan's fifth disciple was Lingxiao Palace's Chief Alchemist, and it also had an Imperial-Grade Formation Master, an Imperial-Grade Artifact Refiner...

There was also the Heavenly Tone Treasure Hall and the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape...

Lingxiao Palace shared an intimate relationship with Ice Heart Valley, a top Sect in the Northern Territory, and the two were practically one family. The other two top Sects, Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect, also owed Lingxiao Palace enormous debts.

A cross-territory Space Array connected it to the Southern Territory, where it enjoyed an extremely close partnership with Ziyuan Commerce Guild.

Its annual income was several times greater than that of other top Sects.

She had even heard that Yang Kai had an ambiguous relationship with a member of the Dragon Clan...

It was difficult to imagine that a Sect established only recently could possess such a foundation. Moreover, Yang Kai had built all of it single-handedly.

The more she learned, the more shocked she became.

What virtue or ability did she possess to serve as Vice Chief Steward of such a Sect? Forget that she had only recently advanced to the Emperor Realm; even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master would not be qualified.

Therefore, when she heard Yang Kai truly intended to confirm her position as Vice Chief Steward, she hurriedly tried to refuse. This was not modesty; she simply lacked the confidence to accept.

Hua Qingsi knew what she was worried about and smiled. "In our Sect, status isn't determined by cultivation, but by whether one can be of help. Our Palace Master is a hands-off Shopkeeper. Elder Sister Hua knows what you're capable of, so you really can't turn down the position of Vice Chief Steward."

Bian Yuqing still wanted to say something, but Yang Kai had already waved his hand. "This matter is settled."

Turning to Hua Qingsi, he asked, "Has Zhu Qing returned?"

Hua Qingsi shook her head. "No. Didn't she go to the Frozen Earth?"

Although Yang Kai had returned once before, he had immediately left again in a great hurry, so Hua Qingsi had no idea that he had also gone to the Frozen Earth and experienced so many things there.

Hearing this, Yang Kai frowned.

He had been delayed for quite some time in the Western Territory. After all, fighting his way up to Wind and Cloud Pavilion with Lin Yun'er had taken several days. By his calculations, Zhu Qing should have returned already, yet Hua Qingsi had not seen her.

Could she have been delayed along the way?

After thinking about it, Yang Kai felt this was possible and did not ask any further. In his opinion, if Zhu Qing had left the Western Territory, she should have returned to Lingxiao Palace first. No matter what, the two of them had already formed an intimate bond that could not be severed, so at the very least, she should have come back to inquire about him.

He decided to wait a little longer. Perhaps Zhu Qing would arrive after a few days.

"Palace Master, did something happen?" Hua Qingsi keenly noticed the change in Yang Kai's expression and asked.

"It's nothing." Yang Kai shook his head.

"Then how long will Palace Master be staying this time?"

"I can't say for certain."

"Should I gather everyone so they can meet with you?"

"All right." Yang Kai nodded. He also felt that he had gone too far in acting as a hands-off Shopkeeper. Since he had finally returned, he naturally ought to spend some time with everyone.

A short while later, figures began appearing one after another inside Lingxiao Palace's main discussion hall.

Yang Kai sat at the head of the hall. The first seat on the left below him was occupied by Imperial-Grade Pill Master Ji Ying. Although his cultivation was not the highest among those present, his status was special. As the fifth disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, he naturally stood far above the others, so no one objected to him sitting there.

The first seat on the right was occupied by Yao King Xi Lei. The three Yao Kings' cultivations were roughly equal, but Xi Lei's strength was slightly greater. Among the Thirty-Two Yao Kings of the Wild Ancient Land, he ranked near the top.

Seated below them were Yingfei, Xie Wuwei, Chief Imperial-Grade Formation Master Nanmen Dajun, and Imperial-Grade Artifact Refiner Hou Yu.

In contrast, Chief Steward Hua Qingsi and Vice Chief Steward Bian Yuqing sat near the bottom.

There were a full nine Emperor Realm Masters in the hall, three of whom were Third-Stage Emperors.

Such a lineup could only be described as magnificent. Lingxiao Palace might have had slightly fewer Emperor Realm Masters than the other top Sects, but in terms of quality, no top Sect could contend with it.

Chi Yue and the others were absent. According to Hua Qingsi, they had spent all this time tempering themselves inside the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. Its unique and irreplaceable distortion of space and time allowed them to grow extremely quickly within it.

Everyone had recognized the power of the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. It would undoubtedly become the cornerstone upon which Lingxiao Palace stood in the future.

There was no particular matter that required everyone to be summoned. Although Lingxiao Palace was enormous, it did not have many people at present, so naturally there were few affairs for Yang Kai to concern himself with.

Ji Ying had spent this entire period refining pills. Yang Kai had given him countless exotic flowers and rare herbs, most of which came from the Wild Ancient Land in the Eastern Territory. Even Ji Ying had only ever heard of many of these Spirit Herbs.

They had already accumulated a great many Imperial-Grade Spirit Pills, enough to use freely for quite some time.

Some time ago, Nanmen Dajun had also finished arranging all of Lingxiao Palace's Spirit Arrays and had even completed the Mountain Wave Diagram of the entire palace. Once the Mountain Wave Diagram was brought out, everything throughout Lingxiao Palace could be seen at a glance, so he had recently had almost nothing to do.

Only Hou Yu remained busy every day. She was not one to behave herself and tried to escape every few days. Unfortunately, the three Yao Kings took turns guarding her, so she had no hope of escaping at all, causing her to harbour deep resentment toward them.

After chatting for a while, everyone dispersed half a day later.

Yang Kai returned to Lingxiao Peak and spent a long while calming his heart and clearing his mind before finally raising his hand.

Countless Upper-Level Source Crystals filled the room. Lingxiao Peak was already the finest Spirit Peak in all of Lingxiao Palace, and its Spiritual Qi was extremely rich. When so many Upper-Level Source Crystals appeared, the density of the Spiritual Qi rose another level.

The Spiritual Qi in the room had almost begun to turn into mist and was on the verge of condensing into a tangible substance.

Enveloped by such rich Spiritual Qi, Yang Kai felt incomparably comfortable, and every pore on his body seemed about to open.

He intended to cultivate properly for a while. Ever since breaking through to the Emperor Realm in the Shattered Star Sea, his cultivation had continued to increase, but he had never shown any sign of breaking through again. A long time had passed since then.

Moreover, he had always felt that a breakthrough was imminent, yet whenever he reached the critical moment, he remained just a hair short. He had no idea what the problem was.

Strictly speaking, he had not spent much time in secluded cultivation. Since he had finally found a rare period of leisure, he naturally wanted to raise his cultivation by another Stage as quickly as possible.

He was now in the First-Stage Emperor Realm. If he used all his methods, he could contend with a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master and might even win if he was lucky, but this was clearly still not enough. The stronger he became, the higher the level of people he came into contact with, and the more Yang Kai recognized his own shortcomings.

The Emperor Realm was not the peak. Above the Emperor Realm stood the Great Emperors!

Yang Kai's goal was precisely to become one of those supreme Great Emperors!

Since his journey to the Shattered Star Sea, Yang Kai's experiences had been rich and varied, especially the years he had spent in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. That was an opportunity ordinary people could never encounter. Logically speaking, such experiences should have been enough to support his breakthrough to the Second-Stage Emperor Realm, yet he had still failed to advance.

He felt that he needed to exert himself a little more and charge to new heights in one vigorous push.

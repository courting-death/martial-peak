## Chapter 2714 — How Could That Be Difficult

Over five hundred people were quickly teleported away. Yang Kai was the last to step onto the Space Array. As soon as he steadied himself, a fragrant scent wafted over, and Zhu Qing landed lightly beside him.

Yang Kai rubbed his temples and groaned, “How long are you planning to follow me? Why don’t we just part ways here—each of us goes home, finds our own family, and lives our own life?”

Zhu Qing looked at him seriously and asked, “Are you going to Dragon Isle or not?”

“Get lost!” Yang Kai was instantly irritated at the mention of Dragon Isle.

“Is that how you talk to your friends?” Zhu Qing said angrily.

“If you mention those two words again, we won’t even be friends!” Yang Kai snorted, his tone sinister. “You’d better be careful. When we teleport, I might exile you into a void crevice, and you’ll never find your way out.”

Zhu Qing’s expression changed. She immediately grabbed Yang Kai’s arm tightly, her full chest pressing against his arm without her even noticing.

“You’re so close to me, I can’t control myself.” Yang Kai turned to look at her, putting on a serious face. “I don’t know why, but whenever I’m near you, I always get restless.”

“Then don’t do anything reckless!” Zhu Qing said a bit awkwardly, moving a little farther from Yang Kai.

“Got it!” Yang Kai replied irritably, activated the Space Array, and in a flash of light, disappeared from the spot.

“Young Master Yang!”

“Young Master Yang!”

As soon as his vision cleared, two familiar voices called out by his ear. Looking up, he saw the two Yao Kings who had stayed behind—Xie Wuwei and Xi Lei. After not seeing Yang Kai for several months, both Yao Kings looked excited, exchanging glances with a hint of rivalry.

Yang Kai nodded lightly. “Thank you both for your hard work.”

“It’s nothing, really. Just house-sitting. As long as Young Master Yang gives the word, we can watch the place as long as needed,” Xi Lei grinned.

Xie Wuwei immediately patted his shoulder and said, “Since you like house-sitting so much, I’ll leave this important task to you. Next time Young Master Yang goes out, I’ll go with him.”

Xi Lei’s face changed and he shouted angrily, “Nonsense! It’s my turn this time, no matter what!”

Xie Wuwei curled his lip. “You lost the drinking contest last time. Are you trying to go back on your word?”

Xi Lei burst out laughing. “How can drunken words be taken seriously?”

“As a Yao King, how can you go back on your word?” Xie Wuwei snorted coldly.

“If you’re not convinced, let’s fight!” Xi Lei puffed out his chest, showing off his solid muscles.

“Sigh!” Yang Kai sighed, stepped down from the Space Array, bypassed the two bickering men, and walked up to Ye Hen, smiling as he asked, “Sect Master Ye, how is this place?”

At this moment, Ye Hen wore a shocked expression, taking deep breaths. Feeling the rich Spiritual Qi in the air, he could hardly believe he had arrived at such a blessed land. What stunned him even more was that he had come here via a cross-domain Space Array—one moment he was in the Southern Territory, and in the blink of an eye, he was in the Northern Territory.

“Good, excellent—there’s no place better than this.” Ye Hen praised sincerely.

No wonder this is the foundation of a top Sect. Even ordinary places have such dense Spiritual Qi. What must the forbidden cultivation grounds and spirit peaks be like? Compared to this, the foundation of Thousand Leaves Sect is simply not in the same league.

No wonder disciples of great Sects grow so quickly. With such a good cultivation environment, of course their progress is fast.

The five hundred disciples of Thousand Leaves Sect were all beaming with joy. If not for the need to save face, they would probably be running around cheering, but the happiness on their faces was impossible to hide.

Seeing this, Ye Hen sighed inwardly. As the saying goes, it’s easy to go from frugality to extravagance, but hard to go back. If his disciples cultivated here for years, who knows how many would be willing to return with him to rebuild Thousand Leaves Sect one day.

But he quickly composed himself and shook off the thought. Now was not the time to worry about that. What he needed to focus on was improving his cultivation and advancing to the Emperor Realm!

Only by becoming an Emperor would he have a chance to rebuild Thousand Leaves Sect. Otherwise, it would all be empty talk.

“There are many spirit peaks. Sect Master Ye, feel free to choose any to reside in, and arrange your disciples as you see fit. Aside from a few forbidden areas protected by arrays, the rest of the Sect is open.” Yang Kai instructed.

“Many thanks, Lord Yang.” Ye Hen bowed deeply.

Yang Kai nodded, then walked over to Chi Yue and the others, smiling as he looked at them. Spreading his arms, he called out, “Everyone, from now on, this is your home in the Star Realm!”

Chi Yue and the others were different from the Thousand Leaves Sect group—they all came from the same Star Domain and had deep ties. Yang Kai had established this Lingxiao Palace for the sake of his people from his homeland, so naturally Chi Yue and the others were included.

“Sect Master has real skill!” Ghost Ancestor chuckled darkly.

Gu Cangyun said, “Now he should be called Palace Master.”

Ghost Ancestor immediately nodded in agreement. Lingxiao Sect and Lingxiao Palace may differ by only one word, but they are completely different entities. The latter’s scale is far beyond the former.

Chi Yue looked around and sighed, “If only Qingluo could come here to cultivate…”

Yang Kai said solemnly, “I’m still looking into how to return. For now, I have no leads, but if I can ever go back to the Star Domain, I’ll definitely bring everyone over.”

Ai Ou patted Yang Kai’s shoulder and said gravely, “You’ve really had it tough.”

He had ventured into the Star Realm alone, and in just a few years, had built such a vast foundation. Though others only saw his success, Ai Ou knew there must have been countless hardships and untold bitterness behind it. What was even more admirable was that, despite his changed strength and status, Yang Kai had never forgotten his roots.

Ai Ou was extremely satisfied with this son-in-law.

“No need to just stand around. Go find a place to settle in,” Yang Kai said with a smile.

“Come, let’s see how big this home is!” Ghost Ancestor called out, instantly turning into a mass of ghostly energy and flying ahead. The others followed excitedly.

Although they had been living at Thousand Leaves Sect, and Ye Hen had treated them well, it was still someone else’s place. Now it was different—this Lingxiao Palace truly belonged to them, giving them a strong sense of belonging.

Ye Hen cupped his fists and said, “Lord Yang, I’ll take these youngsters to have a look around.”

“As you wish, Sect Master Ye,” Yang Kai replied with a smile.

In a lively rush, the several hundred people scattered in all directions, vanishing in an instant—each one brimming with excitement, as if they’d been injected with new life.

Wenqing Sect was enormous. Choosing a place to live would probably take at least several days.

Yang Kai turned to the side and said, “Sister Hua, aren’t you going?”

Hua Qingsi smiled, “No need. I’ll just find a place to stay later.”

She was from Star God Palace, so she was used to such cultivation environments. Naturally, she wasn’t as amazed as Ghost Ancestor or Ye Hen and the others.

Yang Kai’s eyes flickered. “What are you worried about? Star God Palace? Or are you blaming me for killing Tan Junhao?”

Hua Qingsi glanced at him and shook her head. “Master… that man brought it on himself. I don’t blame you. Even if Star God Palace investigates, I doubt they’ll blame you. It’s just…”

“Just what?”

“I’m still a disciple of Star God Palace. Now that I’ve come with you to the Northern Territory, I suppose I’ve betrayed my Sect.” Hua Qingsi gave a self-mocking smile, her face bitter.

Yang Kai said in surprise, “You haven’t done anything to let Star God Palace down, so how is that betrayal?”

Hua Qingsi reached out and tapped his forehead, smiling gently. “Alright, alright, let’s not talk about this anymore. From now on, I’m a member of your Lingxiao Palace. If Star God Palace really comes knocking one day…”

“I’ll beat them back for you. No one can take my people—not even Star God Palace!” Yang Kai said fiercely.

Hua Qingsi nodded in satisfaction and smiled. “Well, I’ve boarded your pirate ship now. I’ll go find a place to stay.”

With that, she flickered away into the distance.

Yang Kai watched her back for a while, then shook his head and turned to Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei. “Has anything happened lately?”

Xi Lei scratched his cheek and looked at Xie Wuwei.

The latter cupped his fists and said, “Reporting to Young Master Yang, nothing major has happened. But every day, many cultivators come to the Sect wanting to join Lingxiao Palace. We ignored them. Other than that, Miss Ji Yao came twice. When she saw you weren’t here, she left. She told me to let you know that when you return, you should visit Ice Heart Valley—she seems to have something to discuss.”

“Got it.” Yang Kai nodded. “I’ll go take a look.”

With that, he stepped onto the Space Array.

Zhu Qing stood silently beside Yang Kai.

Yang Kai glanced at her sideways. “You’re following me for this too? Are you my shadow?”

Zhu Qing replied calmly, “Either you agree to come with me, or I’ll keep following you.”

Yang Kai looked at her in distress. “What grudge do I have with you? Why are you tormenting me like this? I’m warning you, don’t push your luck, or I won’t be polite.”

As soon as Xi Lei heard this, his eyes widened and he cracked his knuckles. “Young Master Yang, you go ahead. Leave this girl to me—I guarantee I’ll beat her so badly even her mother won’t recognize her!”

“Good, good!” Yang Kai was overjoyed, giving Xi Lei a thumbs up. “Old bull, if you can handle her, I’ll reward you handsomely—riches and glory await!”

Xi Lei laughed loudly. “How could that be difficult?”

As he spoke, he unleashed his Yao Qi, and his huge palm swung straight at Zhu Qing, showing no mercy at all.

Zhu Qing’s face turned cold, her beautiful eyes glaring at Xi Lei.

She was a supreme member of the Dragon Clan. She tolerated Yang Kai because he bore the Ancestral Dragon Origin, but for a Yao King to spout off in front of her—her pent-up grievances exploded all at once.

Her delicate fist shot out, lightly meeting Xi Lei’s palm.

Xi Lei frowned. Though he said he’d beat Zhu Qing until her mother wouldn’t recognize her, he could tell she and Yang Kai weren’t enemies, so he knew he couldn’t really go all out. If he accidentally killed her, who knew how Young Master Yang would punish him.

He was a Yao King, with a body of copper and bones of iron, and immense strength. Even ordinary Third-Stage Emperors wouldn’t dare face him head-on. The girl was delicate and petite—she didn’t look like she had any strength at all.

Seeing her about to clash with him head-on, Xi Lei didn’t dare use his full strength. He quickly held back a bit and sneered, “Little girl, you’re still wet behind the ears, yet you dare act so arrogant? Let this King show you…”

Bang…

Fist and palm collided.

Before Xi Lei could finish his sentence, he turned into a bolt of lightning and shot straight out, crashing into a mountain several hundred zhang away, leaving a huge hole in it.

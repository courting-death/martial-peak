## Chapter 3061 — Jiu Feng, Li Wuyi

When Yang Kai appeared to snatch the bride, the gathered Dragons regarded him with contempt and disdain.

When Yang Kai assumed an eight-zhang Dragon form, the many members of the Dragon Clan marvelled at the sight.

When Yang Kai ruthlessly tore off both of Fu Chi's arms, the gathered Dragons were furious. A member of their clan had suffered such torment and humiliation, so they naturally could not turn a blind eye.

But when this man and woman appeared, the expressions of every member of the Dragon Clan changed drastically, as though they were facing formidable enemies.

Even Fourth Elder was no exception. With a grave expression, he gritted his teeth and growled, "Jiu Feng, Li Wuyi!"

The two experts who had abruptly shattered the void and arrived through the Void Passage were none other than Jiu Feng and Li Wuyi of Spirit Beast Island. Setting Jiu Feng aside for the moment, Li Wuyi was proficient in the Laws of Space and renowned as the strongest person in the Star Realm beneath the Great Emperors. Bringing several people through the shattered void was no difficult task for him.

Judging by the expressions and attitudes of the many Dragons, however, there seemed to be some grievance between them and these two. Every one of them gnashed their teeth as though they wanted nothing more than to rush forward and bite chunks of flesh from them.

Yang Kai also asked in surprise, "Seniors, why are you here?"

Li Wuyi glanced at him with a broad smile. "When drinking water, one should not forget who dug the well. Have you forgotten this Lord's favour so quickly?"

Yang Kai was somewhat confused, unable to understand what he meant, but his eyes soon lit up as though he had recalled something. He asked in astonishment, "Senior, was that strand of Divine Sense left by you?"

Li Wuyi smiled faintly. "Indeed."

"So that was it." Realization dawned on Yang Kai, and he cupped his fists. "Many thanks for showing me the way, Senior."

When he and Li Jiao had previously trespassed onto Dragon Isle, they had become trapped in the Void Maze and could not find a way out. In the end, they encountered a phantom formed from a strand of Divine Sense that guided them through the maze. Without that Divine Sense phantom, he and Li Jiao would probably still be wandering around inside in a daze.

He had been unable to figure out who had left that Divine Sense phantom behind, but after hearing Li Wuyi's words, he finally learned that it had been Li Wuyi's Divine Sense.

No wonder it had seemed somewhat familiar at the time.

Li Wuyi waved his hand lightly. "It was a trivial matter. Besides, this Lord should be thanking you as well."

"Thank me?" Yang Kai looked bewildered.

Li Wuyi did not explain and merely looked at Zhu Kong. "We have not seen each other in over ten years, yet Fourth Elder remains as distinguished as ever."

Zhu Kong coldly snorted. He had shown little change in expression when Yang Kai snatched the bride and injured a member of the Dragon Clan, but when facing Li Wuyi, a trace of apprehension and vigilance clearly entered his gaze. Ignoring the greeting, he asked, "Since the two of you are here, does that not mean that person has come as well?"

Li Wuyi chuckled. "Why else does Fourth Elder think the Great Elder and Second Elder went over there together?"

Although he had already suspected as much, Fourth Elder's expression still changed when it was confirmed.

"That person?" Yang Kai raised his brow. The exchange between Li Wuyi and Zhu Kong sounded simple, but it contained an enormous amount of information. Recalling how the Great Elder and Second Elder had hurried away earlier, an unbelievable thought suddenly leapt into his mind, impossible to suppress.

His eyes widened as he looked at the little girl before him.

Liu Yan's Divine Sense surged as she secretly transmitted, "The Beast Great Emperor is here!"

Yang Kai trembled, his face lighting up with wild delight. He finally understood why Great Elder Zhu Yan and Second Elder Fu Zhun had been unable to spare any attention for his affairs. The Beast Great Emperor had personally come to Dragon Isle! Faced with a Great Emperor who had long been famous, neither the Great Elder nor the Second Elder would dare claim they could easily defeat him alone. Only by joining forces could they suppress him to some extent.

Yang Kai asked in confusion, "Why did the Beast Great Emperor come to Dragon Isle? And why did he go to the Dragon Tomb?"

Liu Yan replied, "I do not know either. I only know that this matter is extremely important to him."

Although Liu Yan had been living on Spirit Beast Island for the past two years, she had spent most of that time merging with the True Phoenix Fire. After breaking free of her shell and being reborn, she had also devoted herself to cultivating the Phoenix Clan's Secret Arts, so she knew little about the affairs of Spirit Beast Island.

This time, she had also received the news without warning and hurried to Dragon Isle with several experts.

"The Beast Great Emperor, the Dragon Tomb, more than ten years ago..." One thought after another flashed through Yang Kai's mind, and he increasingly felt that the situation was becoming complicated.

Regardless, the change in circumstances meant the situation was no longer so disadvantageous to him.

Originally, there had been only four people on his side, leaving them somewhat outmatched against the entire Dragon Clan. With the powerful reinforcements from Spirit Beast Island, however, the scales had tipped considerably in his favour.

Li Wuyi and the others had already made their position clear: they stood opposed to Dragon Isle. The enemy of one's enemy was naturally a friend.

Jiu Feng had not spoken since appearing. Her beautiful eyes roamed about as she surveyed her surroundings with a smile, and when her gaze met Yang Kai's, she even winked at him.

Though she appeared calm on the surface, inwardly she could not help sighing with emotion.

She still remembered the first time she saw Yang Kai. Back then, this brat had only just entered the Dao Origin Realm, and she had stopped him on his way to Qingyang Shrine. At the time, he had been nothing more than a Junior with some fortunate opportunities in her eyes. Like Li Wuyi, he cultivated Space Divine Abilities, but she could have killed him with a flick of her finger.

But how many years had passed since then? Facing him now, Jiu Feng could no longer look down on him from high above. His enormous body contained unimaginable power, power that compelled her to treat him as an equal.

His growth had been far too swift. For the first time, Jiu Feng saw the shadow of a future Great Emperor in someone at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

"Why must Fourth Elder look so troubled? Neither you nor I can interfere in the matter over there, so why not use this opportunity to continue the battle we left unfinished more than ten years ago? What does Fourth Elder think?" In contrast to Fourth Elder's grave expression, Li Wuyi remained all smiles, looking perfectly calm and relaxed.

Zhu Kong clearly understood this principle as well. Unless one reached the Tenth Tier, one could not compare to a Great Emperor. Although he was also an Elder on Dragon Isle, his true strength fell far short of the Great Elder and Second Elder.

He truly could not interfere in the battle over there, and neither could Li Wuyi, Jiu Feng, and the others. Otherwise, they would not have come here at all; staying there to lend even a little help would have been better.

It was precisely because Li Wuyi and Jiu Feng understood their own limits that they had not lingered there.

As they spoke, another tremendous hum suddenly came from the direction of the Dragon Tomb. Violent fluctuations of power raced toward them like an arrow loosed from a bow. Waves churned across the sea, and the entire island seemed on the verge of collapse.

The battle over there had already begun!

Yang Kai looked in that direction excitedly, his heart itching as though a cat were clawing at it.

A battle between Great Emperors! This was a genuine clash between Great Emperors, completely unlike the battle between Duan Hongchen and Wu Kuang that he had witnessed in the Shattered Star Sea. Although Duan Hongchen and Wu Kuang had both borne the title of Great Emperor back then, their strength had been greatly diminished. Their battle certainly could not compare with the earth-shaking conflict taking place today.

If he could personally observe such a battle, it would undoubtedly be worth years of painstaking cultivation. He wished he could sprout wings and fly over to take a look. Even if it was extraordinarily dangerous, who would willingly miss a battle like this?

"My Lord truly is impatient." Li Wuyi glanced in that direction and slowly shook his head. Turning back, he urged, "If Fourth Elder would rather everyone sit down and chat, this Li has no objection. As it happens, I brought several jars of fine wine and would like to invite Fourth Elder to sample them."

Zhu Kong gave him a deep look. "You have always had so much nonsense to say."

Jiu Feng also curled her lip. "I told you to speak less and watch more, speak less and watch more, but you never listen. It is not as though you have never suffered for it before. A dog truly cannot change its ways!"

Li Wuyi awkwardly rubbed his nose and muttered, "If a fight can be avoided, it is best to avoid it. Fourth Elder is no easy target, after all."

Zhu Kong lightly snorted. "You want to bully me? Let us see whether you have the ability!"

The two exchanged a glance, then attacked simultaneously without the slightest warning.

Li Wuyi raised his hand and thrust his palm toward Zhu Kong. The Laws of Space surged at once, transforming into a void vortex that enveloped Fourth Elder.

Fourth Elder remained unmoved, his expression solemn. The phantom of a huge Dragon abruptly emerged around his body, raised its head, and roared as it charged upward to meet the attack.

A transparent barrier seemed to appear in the void and halted the huge Dragon. Li Wuyi's hand also stopped in midair, unable to descend, while the Laws of Space around him churned violently.

Both men wore extremely serious expressions. Most people could not understand what was happening, but Yang Kai's eyes shone brightly as he carefully sensed Li Wuyi's Laws of Space and compared them against his own. To his surprise, he gained a great deal in only a short time.

Ancient and abstruse syllables rang out as Zhu Kong swiftly formed seals with his hands. He paid no heed to Li Wuyi's attack, while the syllables issuing from his mouth came faster and faster until they merged into an unbroken stream.

"The Protection Technique!" Yang Kai frowned, instantly understanding what Secret Art Fourth Elder was using.

Not daring to hesitate, he likewise chanted in the Dragon Language and formed seals with both hands.

Zhu Kong gave him a deep look, a trace of astonishment flashing in the depths of his eyes.

He had already known that Yang Kai could use the Dragon Clan's Secret Arts. Since Yang Kai could use them, it meant he had already obtained the Ancestral Dragon Origin's acknowledgement and inheritance.

What Zhu Kong had not expected was that Yang Kai could even communicate with the Dragon Temple.

There were two great forbidden grounds on Dragon Isle.

One was the Dragon Tomb, and the other was the Dragon Temple.

The Dragon Tomb was the burial ground of the Dragon Clan, while the Dragon Temple was where the heroic spirits of generations of Dragons bestowed their protection. Both were foundations of the Dragon Clan, and the clan could not do without either one.

To communicate with the Dragon Temple, one needed not only an extremely high-grade and pure bloodline, but also years of prayer and an important ritual.

On all of Dragon Isle, only a few Elders could communicate with the Dragon Temple.

The greatest benefit of communicating with the Dragon Temple was the ability to swiftly use the Dragon Clan's Secret Arts without performing certain cumbersome procedures.

Beyond that, the Dragon Temple's greatest purpose was to provide a kind of protection.

It protected the Dragon Clan against bloodline suppression.

Zhu Lie had previously told Yang Kai that the Dragon Temple's protection could largely offset the effects of bloodline suppression. Thus, on Dragon Isle, if the Dragon Clan had time to prepare, the suppressive effect of Yang Kai's Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power on them would not be so pronounced.

Fu Chi had been captured so easily by Zhu Qing for two reasons. First, she had caught him completely by surprise; Fu Chi could never have imagined that Zhu Qing would regain her strength and attack him. Second, Fu Chi had not received the Dragon Temple's protection beforehand.

But now, the Secret Art Fourth Elder was using was clearly the Protection Technique.

Zhu Kong understood better than anyone that the Dragon Clan's advantage lay in their numbers, while their disadvantage lay in their bloodlines.

Yang Kai alone was enough to suppress the bloodlines of every member of the Dragon Clan, preventing them from displaying their full strength.

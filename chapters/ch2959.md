## Chapter 2959 — I Am the Shaman King

"Is that so? But the Third Prince really does have one more leaf than you. If I'm not mistaken, the more leaves you have, the higher your status, right? So, it seems you really aren't as good as the Third Prince."

"That's... that's because this Prince's cultivation is lacking, yes, that's it. This Prince simply doesn't like to cultivate, so I only have eight leaves. If one day this Prince has the same cultivation as Third Brother, getting nine leaves would be a piece of cake."

"Tsk tsk tsk..." Yang Kai looked at him with utter disdain. "I don't think so. You can tell a lot from the little things. With your character, if I were the Human Sovereign, I wouldn't like you either. If the day comes to pass on the throne, I'd definitely consider the Third Prince. The Third Prince is a dragon among men, with solid cultivation and a generous heart. How could you compare?"

"You... shut up!" Fu Shu was so furious at Yang Kai that he was practically fuming, baring his teeth and claws as he lunged, relying on the Sacred Tree's protection and acting as if he feared nothing.

Although Yang Kai was talking, his hands never stopped, launching fierce attacks at Fu Shu's body, but he could never break through that green light's protection. Meanwhile, the Eldest Prince's attacks were no more than a tickle to him and could be completely ignored.

For a while, the two of them brawled like street thugs, but neither could knock the other down. Every attack vanished without a trace.

The goateed man sighed, unable to keep watching, and said, "Eldest Prince, why care about his words? Just use the Sacred Art. Your cultivation and strength are both inferior to his; only the Sacred Art can help you redeem your honor."

Yang Kai turned his head and snorted coldly, "Old thing, keep nagging and I'll knock your teeth out."

The goateed man said earnestly, "Young people should know to respect their elders and care for the young."

“That depends on the person. I beat dozens of showoffs like you to death every day.”

As they spoke, Fu Shu's incantation rang out again. With the goateed man's reminder, he no longer tangled with Yang Kai and instead cast a Bloodlust Art on himself. The fear and timidity in his eyes quickly vanished, replaced by anger and excitement. He laughed wildly, as if no one else existed, and snarled, "Brat, you dare offend this Prince? I want you dead! After I kill you, I'll make sure to properly enjoy that beauty."

The Bloodlust Art could affect a person's reason, making them lose some judgment, but it was undeniable that in certain situations, it was extremely effective.

Fu Shu's fear and timidity were suppressed by the Bloodlust Art. His blood-red eyes locked onto Yang Kai, and as if he had become a different person, he shouted, "Bind!"

An invisible force suddenly surged from the void, like a wave, binding Yang Kai's raised fist.

Yang Kai simply couldn't bring his fist down. No matter how he struggled, his fist was fixed in midair.

He felt as if his fist was being pulled by an invisible force. The more he tried to attack, the stronger the pull became. Yang Kai's expression grew grave. The Eldest Prince Fu Shu was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, yet he could achieve this with the so-called Sacred Art. It was beyond Yang Kai's expectations and made him even more wary of this altered Shamanic Art.

Ordinary Shamanic Arts could never have such an effect or accomplish something so unbelievable. He had always been the one to fight above his level, killing enemies and avenging grudges. This was the first time someone weaker than him had gotten the upper hand.

He drifted back, no longer hindered, and easily widened the distance between himself and Fu Shu.

Having succeeded with one move, Fu Shu's confidence soared. He laughed arrogantly, "Run? Let's see where you run! Sword, come!"

He reached out, and suddenly a mass of emerald light burst from the void. The color was identical to the energy protecting Fu Shu. The light quickly gathered in midair, forming a heaven-shaking longsword.

Fu Shu pointed, and the longsword slashed down at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai's pupils shrank. Instinctively sensing the power of this strike, he summoned several Moon Blades with the Laws of Space to intercept it.

With a thunderous roar, the emerald longsword swept through, destroying all the Moon Blades in its path. But after intercepting the last Moon Blade, it suddenly shattered apart.

Fu Shu's body trembled, and two streams of blood flowed from his nostrils. He didn't notice at all, instead raising his hands and shouting, "Again!"

Once more, a burst of emerald light bloomed, and another massive green longsword appeared.

Yang Kai glanced thoughtfully in a certain direction.

After several tries, he finally caught a subtle trace. Every time Fu Shu used the Sacred Art, he seemed to resonate with something in that direction. The resonance was faint, and if Yang Kai's Divine Sense wasn't so strong, he might not have noticed it at all.

With only the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, Fu Shu shouldn't have the strength to contend with him. The only reason he could do so was because he was borrowing power.

He was borrowing the power of the Sacred Tree!

And the direction of that resonance should be where the Sacred Tree was located.

He was curious to see what exactly this so-called Sacred Tree was, to possess such miraculous power.

His Divine Sense surged like a tide, causing the void to hum. The goateed man's face changed again.

In an instant, everything in Human Sovereign City was imprinted in Yang Kai's mind, as if the finest artist had painted a picture in his head, every detail clear. As his Divine Sense pushed forward, his vision reached farther and farther.

Suddenly, a barrier blocked his way. Caught off guard, Yang Kai's Divine Sense crashed into it, making him dizzy.

At that moment, the emerald longsword slashed down, cutting into Yang Kai's shoulder and sending blood gushing out.

Fu Shu laughed wildly, "That's what you get for being arrogant and humiliating this Prince! This is your punishment!"

He formed a seal with both hands, pressing the emerald longsword down, as if to split Yang Kai in two.

Yang Kai snorted coldly. The Myriad Sword appeared in his hand, and with a single slash, a fierce sword aura erupted, severing the emerald longsword.

Fu Shu froze, a bit surprised.

Yang Kai said, "Is that all you've got, Eldest Prince? It's laughable. Use whatever tricks you have left. Let's see what you can really do to me."

Fu Shu was furious and shouted, "Brat, don't get cocky!"

He suddenly began chanting again, but this time Yang Kai couldn't tell which Shamanic Art it was. As the chant rang out, Fu Shu also took up a bow-drawing stance.

A green arrow mysteriously appeared before him. He gently pinched it and set it on an invisible bow.

"Chase the Stars, Pursue the Moon! Die!"

With Fu Shu's furious shout, the green arrow spun rapidly, breaking through the barriers of space, leaving a visible vacuum in its wake, and instantly shot toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai tilted his head, and the arrow grazed his temple, slicing off a few strands of black hair that drifted away on the wind.

"Still dare to dodge!" Fu Shu erupted like a volcano, firing arrow after arrow at Yang Kai like shooting stars. Blood from his nostrils gushed like a flood, soaking his clothes and the ground, but he seemed not to notice, completely lost in a frenzy.

After only three or five arrows, blood had begun flowing from all seven of his facial orifices.

Using the Sacred Art seemed to put an enormous strain on him.

There is no power in this world that comes without a price. Even though Fu Shu was the Eldest Prince, born with the Sacred Tree's blessing and able to use the powerful Sacred Art, for him to display strength on par with Yang Kai at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, he had to pay a huge price.

His reckless attacks made it even easier for Yang Kai to probe the source of the Sacred Art's power.

At a certain moment, Yang Kai suddenly frowned, as if he remembered something. He took a deep breath and closed his eyes.

Seeing this, Fu Shu was overjoyed and said naively, "Now you've seen this Prince's strength, haven't you? It's too late to beg for mercy now. I'll make sure you taste every torture under the sun."

As he spoke, he raised his hand again, and another green arrow howled toward Yang Kai.

Just then, Yang Kai suddenly opened his eyes, raised his hand, and caught the green arrow. The arrow, filled with powerful energy, instantly froze in front of him, unable to move.

"How is this possible?"

Yang Kai and Fu Shu spoke at the same time, one bewildered, the other utterly shocked.

"Your aura..." The goateed man's eyes suddenly widened, staring at Yang Kai like a newborn calf, full of confusion.

In the instant Yang Kai closed and opened his eyes, his aura underwent a complete transformation.

It didn't become stronger, but now it felt incredibly ancient, as if he had stepped out of a distant era. His entire being exuded an indescribable, archaic charm.

The green arrow in Yang Kai's hand shattered, and an incantation sounded from his mouth.

At this moment, he was not an Emperor Realm cultivator, but a Shaman King, a Shaman King from the ancient Southern Barbarian Tribe!

His incantation was far more cryptic and primordial than the Eldest Prince's, barely audible yet remarkably arduous to articulate.

"You..." The Eldest Prince was dumbfounded, forgetting to continue his attack, pointing at Yang Kai as if witnessing the impossible.

The goateed man also trembled violently, completely stunned.

A small fire serpent suddenly materialized before Yang Kai, lifelike and vivid, as if it were a true living creature, writhing and tumbling endlessly through the air.

"Sacred Art!" the goateed man cried out, nearly biting his own tongue, but quickly denied his own judgment.

This wasn't the Sacred Art, because the fire snake lacked the Sacred Art's unique green glow. However, the entire casting process was almost identical to the Sacred Art.

"So this is the Sacred Art..." Yang Kai's lips curled up. He could clearly sense that he had established a connection with something in Human Sovereign City, and this connection only formed after he transformed into a Shaman King. After casting the Shamanic Art, the connection became much stronger.

## Chapter 3054 — Snatching a Bride from the Dragon Clan

The bridal sedan and the woman inside were the true centerpieces of the day. The four giant Dragons that had appeared earlier were merely the bearers pulling the sedan.

Two Dragon women stood beside the bridal sedan. One had flowing purple hair and was none other than Fu Ling. She had originally been guarding the Dragon Blood Flowers, but today was a joyous occasion for the Dragon Clan, so naturally she had been summoned back. The other Dragon woman did not appear particularly remarkable. Aside from her outstanding beauty, she looked no different from an ordinary human.

The two Dragon women each held a flower basket and stood on either side of the bridal sedan. As they drifted through the air, petals flew from their baskets, trailing a delightful rain of flowers across the sky. It was an exceptionally beautiful sight.

This was no ordinary shower of flowers. Petals of every color burst apart in the sky, releasing a multicolored radiance that dazzled everyone who saw it.

This sight alone held everyone's gaze. They secretly marveled that the Dragon Clan truly lived up to its reputation. Even a wedding could be turned into such an elaborate spectacle. Ordinary people could never hope to match such extravagance.

Before long, the bridal sedan arrived nearby. The four Dragons halted and hovered several dozen zhang from the palace.

Every pair of eyes turned toward the bridal sedan, while a heartfelt smile appeared on Fu Chi's face.

Once today was over, the Dragon woman inside the sedan would be his. For Fu Chi, this was naturally an occasion of immense joy.

Shua, shua, shua...

Three figures abruptly appeared.

An elderly man appeared, thin and gaunt, with a head of red hair. His expression was naturally imposing, and although no aura leaked from him, anyone could feel an immense pressure coming from his body.

That indescribable pressure was something even the four giant Dragons before them, all of whom had revealed their true forms, could not compare with.

Beside the red-haired man stood a silver-haired woman in palace robes, her face as cold and indifferent as ice. The aura radiating from her sent chills through everyone present. No one dared look at her for more than a fleeting moment, as though staring too long would freeze both body and soul.

The last was a young man. A faint, amiable smile hung on his face as he stood there with composed bearing.

As soon as these three appeared, Fu Chi and the others all grew solemn and bowed in unison. "Greetings, three Elders!"

The Dragon Clan's arrogance was reserved for creatures of other races. Before stronger members of their own clan, even the proudest head would sometimes bow.

Hearing Fu Chi and the others greet them, the expressions of the three hundred cultivators changed slightly. They hurriedly lowered their eyes and assumed deferential postures, each inwardly exclaiming, "So these are the Dragon Clan's three great Elders!"

Great Elder Zhu Yan, Second Elder Fu Zhun, and Fourth Elder Zhu Kong!

These three were towering figures throughout Dragon Isle, especially the Great Elder and Second Elder. Both possessed Tenth-Tier Dragon Bloodlines and were unmatched on Dragon Isle. Although Zhu Kong did not possess a Tenth-Tier Dragon Bloodline, he was still Ninth-Tier, a height ordinary members of the Dragon Clan could scarcely hope to reach.

It was said that Dragon Isle had once had four Elders, and that the Third Elder also possessed a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline. For some unknown reason, however, the Third Elder had not appeared this time. Such secrets were not something ordinary people could learn. The three hundred cultivators had lived in Half-Dragon City for many years, so naturally they knew nothing about Dragon Isle's internal affairs.

More than a dozen members of the Dragon Clan had gathered before the palace.

This was almost all the power Dragon Isle had to offer. Apart from a Dragon Clan wedding, an event that might not occur even once in ten thousand years, it was difficult to find an occasion that could gather so many members of the Dragon Clan in one place.

Great Elder Zhu Yan's expression remained as tranquil as an ancient well, while Second Elder Fu Zhun was cold-faced by nature. Neither of them said anything more.

Only the smiling Fourth Elder Zhu Kong nodded to Fu Chi and said, "Congratulations."

Fu Chi replied respectfully, "Fu Chi is deeply grateful for the Elders' favor."

Zhu Kong said, "After the wedding, I hope you and your wife will always treat each other with respect and remain loving for the rest of your lives."

Fu Chi hurriedly replied, "Fourth Elder may rest assured. Fu Chi will never mistreat Qing'er. We will also produce a Dragon heir for Dragon Isle as soon as possible and carry on the bloodline."

Zhu Kong smiled faintly but did not comment.

Fu Zhun frowned. "Enough nonsense. Help Zhu Qing down."

At her command, none of the Dragons dared disobey. The four Dragons before the bridal sedan moved apart of their own accord, while Fu Ling and the other Dragon woman reached out to lift the gauze curtain. "Elder Sister Qing, it is time to come down," they said softly.

Zhu Qing did not move, remaining as steady as a mountain.

Fu Ling timidly glanced at the Second Elder. Seeing her expression grow even colder, she softly urged Zhu Qing several more times, but to no effect.

Fu Ling grew so anxious that sweat began to bead on her forehead.

Of course she knew that Zhu Qing was unhappy with this marriage. Everyone on Dragon Isle knew. But with the Elders all present, what could Zhu Qing do even if she was unwilling? If this matter was not handled properly today, it would certainly become a laughingstock.

The smile on Fu Chi's face gradually faded as well. The situation was becoming rather embarrassing for him.

Fu Zhun's expression also turned icy as she glanced toward Zhu Yan.

Zhu Yan kept his gaze lowered, utterly unmoved.

Fu Zhun could not help snorting coldly to herself. Just as she was about to personally drag Zhu Qing from the bridal sedan, a figure suddenly flashed past.

A young man appeared leisurely before the bridal sedan. Most people did not even notice how he had arrived. Only the Dragon Clan's Elders narrowed their eyes and turned to look at him.

Fu Zhun knitted her brows and barked, "Drive him away."

What was this man doing? He had actually run out on an occasion like this and blocked the bridal sedan's path. Was he trying to get himself killed?

Under ordinary circumstances, Fu Zhun would have struck him dead already. Today, however, was the Dragon Clan's grand wedding, and it was best to avoid bloodshed if possible, so she restrained herself.

A piercing Dragon roar rang out. The twenty-zhang-long red Dragon suddenly coiled its body around the bridal sedan, enveloping it completely. It raised its head high, widened its Dragon eyes, and glared down at the figure ahead in a protective stance, immediately shutting the other three Dragons outside.

"Zhu Lie, what are you doing?" Fu Qi frowned at the red Dragon that had been pulling the sedan.

The Dragon's jaws opened and closed as Zhu Lie replied in a thunderous voice, "Nothing. I merely ask everyone to wait a moment!"

Zhu Yan, who had remained perfectly composed all this time, finally raised his eyes. When he saw the back of the figure standing before the bridal sedan, his brow rose involuntarily.

Zhu Kong also looked astonished. Despite his formidable cultivation, even he was somewhat bewildered at this moment.

Under everyone's watchful gaze, Yang Kai gently drew a breath. Looking at the woman inside the bridal sedan with a bright red veil over her head, he held out a hand and smiled tenderly. "I've come to take you away."

His words sent a shock through everyone present.

The three hundred cultivators stared at Yang Kai's back in utter disbelief, wailing inwardly.

What was he doing?

To act so outrageously before more than a dozen members of the Dragon Clan and say such a thing to a Dragon woman who was about to be married... had he gone mad?

If something similar happened in human territory, there would be no question that this was an attempt to snatch the bride.

But what place was this? This was Dragon Isle! Today was the wedding of two members of the Dragon Clan. Had this man lost his mind? He had actually come to snatch a bride from the Dragon Clan?

The members of the Dragon Clan wore strange expressions, clearly not yet able to process what was happening.

Fu Chi was no exception. He had not yet realized what the scene before him truly meant.

In the next instant, however, his face abruptly turned livid.

After the human spoke those words, Zhu Qing, who had been sitting motionless inside the bridal sedan, actually trembled. She tore the red veil from her head and stared ahead in utter shock, crying out, "Why are you here?"

She could scarcely believe her eyes.

Yang Kai had actually appeared before her.

As she gazed at the face she had yearned to see day and night, Zhu Qing thought for an instant that she must be dreaming. Yet the man before her was so real. His familiar aura and scent told her this was no dream.

Yang Kai had truly come.

He had appeared openly and without the slightest restraint before every member of the Dragon Clan, and on this day of all days, when she had fallen into despair. The man before her was like a blazing sun, his brilliant light sweeping away all the gloom in her heart.

"I've come to take you away, of course." Yang Kai winked at her.

Zhu Qing's eyes immediately reddened. She shook her head and said, "You shouldn't have come."

Her heart was filled with joy, but even more than that, she was worried for Yang Kai. If he had not come today, she alone would have suffered. But now that he was here, how could he possibly escape?

The Dragon Clan could not tolerate humiliation. After today, Yang Kai would likely become the enemy of every member of the Dragon Clan, with no hope whatsoever of leaving this place alive.

"I see..." Yang Kai lowered his head and thought for a moment, then withdrew his hand and turned around. "In that case, I'll be going."

"Don't you dare!" Zhu Qing cried anxiously. She hurriedly stood up and threw herself out of the bridal sedan, grabbing his arm as she glared at him. "You came all this way and still think you can leave?"

Yang Kai grinned. "It seems you don't want to get married. Now I can rest easy."

Zhu Qing held his arm, her expression suddenly dimming. "We're going to die."

"Then we will share the same bed in life and the same grave in death. That would be a beautiful thing in its own way."

Zhu Qing's eyes grew moist as she leaned against him. In that brief instant, she had also come to terms with everything. Yang Kai was already here, so asking him to leave now was no longer realistic. The only thing she could do was stand beside him and face the Dragon Clan's raging storm together.

She slowly turned her head and looked toward her clansmen, only to see pair after pair of incredulous eyes.

There were few things in this world capable of shocking the Dragon Clan, but the scene before them certainly counted as one.

Someone had actually come openly to snatch the bride at a Dragon Clan wedding!

And the man trying to snatch her was actually a human!

Zhu Qing was actually behaving so intimately with that human!

Every member of the Dragon Clan was dumbfounded.

If even the Dragon Clan reacted this way, there was no need to mention the humans and Yao, all of whom stood slack-jawed. Among the three hundred cultivators who had remained to observe the ceremony, many had previously found Yang Kai displeasing. After all, they had worked themselves to exhaustion over the past month while Yang Kai had not needed to do anything at all. The comparison had naturally left them feeling resentful.

Only now did they understand how bold this man truly was! No one knew what had given him such incredible nerve, but he actually dared to snatch a bride from the Dragon Clan. Compared with this, what did his slacking off over the previous month even matter?

The world was full of strange and wondrous events, but what happened today had truly opened everyone's eyes.

Fu Chi's expression was especially ugly.

The woman he was supposed to marry now had her arm linked with another man's, looking every bit like a loving couple. They had even spoken of sharing the same bed in life and the same grave in death. Where did that leave him? Now that this had happened, no matter how the matter was ultimately resolved, Fu Chi's reputation would be utterly ruined.

Fu Ling had urged Zhu Qing several times earlier, yet Zhu Qing had remained completely motionless inside the bridal sedan, leaving Fu Chi thoroughly embarrassed. But after this human said only a few words, Zhu Qing had actually rushed out of her own accord...

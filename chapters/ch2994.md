## Chapter 2994 — Escaped

"Master said that if you cut the grass without removing the roots, it will grow again when the spring breeze blows," Lin Yun'er explained.

Cold sweat covered Yang Kai's forehead. Just who was this Master of hers to be so brutal and ruthless? Back then, after Yang Yan brought Lin Yun'er to the Star Realm, who had she entrusted the little girl to?

Lin Yun'er had already taken the initiative to grab Yang Kai's large hand and sped off in a certain direction, chattering away in delight.

As they travelled onward, Yang Kai spent most of the time listening. He wanted to ask how she had been all these years, but could not bear to dampen her enthusiasm.

Yang Kai soon discovered something peculiar.

Lin Yun'er was an absolute glutton!

Those three spirit fruits of his were Imperial-Grade, after all. Even an Emperor Realm Master would need some time to refine them before fully digesting them, yet in this girl's hands, the three spirit fruits were no different from snacks.

After finishing the spirit fruits, she actually pulled a large quantity of dried meat from her Spatial Ring. At a glance, it was obvious that the meat had been made from the carcasses of extremely high-rank Yao Beasts and contained an extremely abundant amount of energy.

"Is it all right for you to eat so much?" Yang Kai could not help asking.

Lin Yun'er replied as though it were only natural, "Master said that only when my belly is full will I have the strength to cultivate and fight."

What kind of theory was that? Yang Kai grew even more curious about her Master.

One day later, the two arrived at another city. It was in no way inferior to Point Star City, and the streets were packed shoulder to shoulder with people, as numerous as carp crossing a river.

In light of Lin Yun'er's behaviour along the way, Yang Kai took her directly into the largest restaurant he could find, asked for a private room, called over a waiter, and ordered a whole table full of sumptuous dishes.

These delicacies were naturally no ordinary food. Every dish contained Spiritual Herbs and Yao Beast flesh and blood, and even experts would benefit greatly in their cultivation from eating them.

Lin Yun'er's eyes shone as she feasted heartily, sweeping through the dishes like a whirlwind and astonishing the serving staff so badly that their eyes nearly popped out.

It was hard to imagine that such a beautiful young woman, as lovely as a celestial maiden, could eat so much.

Only after she had eaten and drunk her fill did Yang Kai ask, "Little Yun'er, where is your Aunt Yang Yan?"

Lin Yun'er reclined in her chair with one hand rubbing her belly. Despite having eaten so much, her stomach had not bulged at all and remained perfectly flat. Her face had been filled with comfort and satisfaction, but upon hearing Yang Kai's question, she immediately pouted and replied aggrievedly, "I don't know."

"You don't know?" Yang Kai was stunned. "Wasn't she the one who took you away back then?"

Lin Yun'er replied, "She was, but after bringing me here, she handed me over to Master. I never saw her again after that, and she has never once come to visit me." The little girl looked on the verge of tears as she spoke, clearly heartbroken. At the same time, she grabbed a beast leg from the table and began nibbling it, seemingly intent on turning her grief and indignation into appetite...

Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and continued, "What about your Master? Who is your Master?"

Without raising her head, Lin Yun'er replied, "Master is Master."

Yang Kai said, "He must have some form of address, right?"

Lin Yun'er raised her head and stared blankly at Yang Kai. Only after a long while did she give him a silly smile and say, "I don't know either. I've always just called him Master."

It was hopeless. Not only was this girl carefree, she was also rather foolish. Yang Kai could not help worrying about her future. How could someone with such a personality gain a foothold in the Star Realm? No wonder she had provoked that Wind and Cloud Pavilion and ended up being hunted all across the world.

He had no idea how she had managed to grow up all these years.

Then again, since Yang Yan had entrusted Lin Yun'er to that person, he must have been extremely reliable. This much was clear from how much Lin Yun'er had grown. Her Master had to be an expert, and very likely a great figure of world-shaking ability.

Unfortunately, Lin Yun'er knew nothing about that person's identity, so no matter how much Yang Kai wanted to investigate, he could not uncover any clues.

Yang Kai remembered that Lin Yun'er possessed a somewhat special physique. Back then, Yang Yan had called it the Force Tyrant Body. The reason she had taken Lin Yun'er away was that a friend of hers practised a cultivation technique highly suited to the little girl.

Looking at things now, Yang Yan's decision had been correct. Although it had separated the little girl from her family for many years, her growth was indeed remarkable. If Lin Yun'er had remained on Dark Star back then, she could never have achieved what she had today.

"Can you take me to find your Master?" Yang Kai asked solemnly.

"Why do you want to find him?" Lin Yun'er was greatly alarmed. She even stopped eating and stared at Yang Kai with wide eyes, panic written all over her face.

"I want to ask him about your Aunt Yang Yan's whereabouts."

"Oh." The little girl nodded lightly, then said, "If that's the reason, there's no need to find him."

"Why?" Yang Kai asked with a frown.

"Because he won't tell you. I've asked him many times over the years, but he never tells me."

Yang Kai smiled. "He may have his reasons for not telling you, but it will be different if I ask. So, if possible, I hope you can take me to find your Master."

Lin Yun'er's expression immediately became somewhat unnatural. She let out two dry chuckles, her eyes darting about as she ate absent-mindedly.

Yang Kai asked curiously, "Is there something making this inconvenient for you?"

The little girl hurriedly waved her hands. "No, no, no. There's nothing inconvenient..." As she spoke, however, her head drooped in dejection, and she frankly confessed, "Uncle Yang, I ran away."

Yang Kai paled in shock. "What do you mean, you ran away? Where did you escape from? Was someone imprisoning you?"

Lin Yun'er lowered her head. "No one was imprisoning me. I ran away from Master's place."

Yang Kai was utterly stunned. "Your Master doesn't treat you well? He even restricts your freedom?"

"That's not it." The little girl hurriedly denied it. "Master treats me extremely well. He says I'm the inheritor of his legacy, so he gives me every good thing he has and feeds me delicious food every day."

Sweat covered Yang Kai's forehead. "If he treats you so well, why did you run away?"

Lin Yun'er fidgeted and replied, "I just wanted to come out and look around. I've always stayed in one place and never gone outside before. I... I missed Aunt Yang Yan too, so I wanted to come out and look for her."

Hearing her explanation, Yang Kai could not help laughing, but he also relaxed. Although he did not know who the little girl's Master was, since she said so, that person clearly treated her quite well.

Lin Yun'er giggled again. "It's a good thing I ran away, otherwise I wouldn't have met you, Uncle Yang."

"Girl, let's discuss something."

"Mm, mm!"

"Look, I'm not old, so stop calling me Uncle."

Lin Yun'er tilted her head and thought for a moment, then smiled without a care in the world. "But you're Little Yun'er's Uncle Yang."

"Fine." Yang Kai looked helpless. Suddenly, he frowned and asked thoughtfully, "Your Master must be very powerful, right?"

Lin Yun'er beamed with pride. "Of course! Master is the most powerful. There is nothing under the Heavens that he can't do."

Yang Kai asked, "If he's so powerful, how did you manage to escape from him?"

"I just ran away while he wasn't paying attention. What was so difficult about that?"

This answer left Yang Kai completely speechless. Just as he was about to ask something else, his expression suddenly changed and he shouted, "Yun'er, stop eating! It's poisoned!"

He had suddenly noticed an abnormal colour appearing on Lin Yun'er's face, which was clearly a sign of poisoning.

A poison capable of affecting a First-Stage Emperor Realm Master was obviously extraordinarily potent.

Yang Kai blamed himself bitterly. Although he had briefly examined the food when it was served, he had found nothing unusual. He had not expected the poison to be so well concealed that it completely deceived his perception.

"So it was poisoned. No wonder it tasted a little strange." As Lin Yun'er spoke, she burped and glanced at the food in her hand...

Then continued chewing!

"You..." Yang Kai was completely dumbfounded. She had clearly been poisoned, and he had even warned her, yet she did not take it seriously at all.

Had something gone wrong with the little girl's head?

Just as Yang Kai was about to snatch away the food in her hand, he noticed another strange phenomenon. A wisp of emerald-green mist had begun to spread from the surface of Lin Yun'er's body, and it contained an extremely fierce toxicity.

This actually worked?

Yang Kai nearly bit his tongue.

The poisonous mist had clearly been forced out by Little Yun'er herself. In other words, the potent poison in the food had no effect on her whatsoever. Lin Yun'er was obviously aware of this, which was why she continued eating without the slightest fear.

Was the Force Tyrant Body really so formidable? Or was the little girl's Master's inheritance simply that extraordinary?

Yang Kai could not understand the reason for the moment, but he relaxed and instructed, "Don't wander off. I'll be right back!"

As soon as he finished speaking, his figure flickered and disappeared from the spot.

In the next instant, Yang Kai arrived in a place resembling a kitchen. Waves of heat rolled through the room, where numerous people dressed as cooks were preparing all kinds of delicacies.

Yang Kai swept his gaze around and immediately fixed his eyes on one person. This person's cultivation was not low, as he had also reached the First-Stage Emperor Realm. His entire body was wrapped in a black robe, concealing his face, and he gave off an extremely dangerous aura.

"You..." The man was clearly startled by Yang Kai's sudden appearance. With a raise of his hand, he sent a brightly colored venomous snake flying toward Yang Kai. Its head was hideous, and as its jaws opened, several fangs gleamed with a strange light as it lunged straight at him.

"Courting death!" Yang Kai raised his hand and sent out a Moon Blade. It easily sliced through the venomous snake's body, cutting it in half and sending both pieces falling.

The black-robed man remained calm. Forming a hand seal, he caused the two halves of the snake's body to suddenly explode, transforming into a cloud of blood-red poisonous mist that spread in every direction.

Taking advantage of this opportunity, the black-robed man drifted backward and smashed straight through the wall in an attempt to flee.

With a thunderous crash, the kitchen collapsed. By the time dust and smoke rose into the air, the black-robed man had already fled more than a hundred zhang away.

"Do you think you can escape?" Yang Kai's voice suddenly came from ahead.

The black-robed man was horrified. Looking up, he discovered that Yang Kai had somehow already blocked his path.

He shook his sleeves, and poisonous creatures of all shapes and sizes surged forward. Among them were snakes, frogs, and spiders, each brightly colored and clearly highly venomous. There were over a hundred of them, and they swept toward Yang Kai in an overwhelming tide.

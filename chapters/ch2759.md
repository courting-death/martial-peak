## Chapter 2759 — It's Yours

"One hundred seventy million!" Yang Kai called out calmly, his expression unchanged. Qin Yu watched him in astonishment, unable to hide her surprise.

"One hundred eighty million!" the old man surnamed Zhou growled.

"Two million!"

As soon as these words left his mouth, everyone was stunned. The old man surnamed Zhou burst out laughing, "Boy, are you confused? Two million..."

"Upper-Level Source Crystals!" Yang Kai added.

The old man surnamed Zhou's laughter was instantly cut off.

Two million Upper-Level Source Crystals, when converted, were worth two hundred million Middle-Level Source Crystals. However, at an auction like this, two hundred million Middle-Level Source Crystals couldn't be exchanged for two million Upper-Level Source Crystals. In other words, although two million Upper-Level Source Crystals were theoretically the same as two hundred million Middle-Level Source Crystals, their value was much higher.

If the old man surnamed Zhou wanted to raise the bid, he would have to offer Upper-Level Source Crystals.

He immediately fell silent.

On the high platform, the beautiful auctioneer smiled sweetly, gazing at the private room where Yang Kai was, and said, "The friend in Heaven Room Three has bid two million Upper-Level Source Crystals. Is there any higher offer?"

Naturally, no one raised the bid.

To put things in perspective, when Yang Kai sold a Twelfth-Tier Lower-Level Inner Core to the Ziyuan Commerce Guild, he only received two million Upper-Level Source Crystals. The value of this Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin was nearly equal to that of a Twelfth-Tier Lower-Level Inner Core.

But not everyone could get their hands on such a treasure.

"Congratulations to the friend in Heaven Room Three!" The beautiful woman smiled slightly, though she was puzzled in her heart. Judging by Yang Kai's voice, he was a stranger to her, and she didn't know when the Commerce Guild had gained such a major client. Not just anyone could enter a Heaven Room, let alone occupy the highly ranked Room Three. Clearly, the person inside was an extremely important guest to the Commerce Guild.

Meanwhile, in another Heaven Room, Le Dongzheng's gaze turned cold as Yang Kai placed his bid.

Having dealt with Yang Kai before, he naturally recognized his voice. With a hostile glare toward Room Three, he felt a surge of anger. He was also a major client of the Ziyuan Commerce Guild, but even with his status, he could only get Room Nine. What right did that young man have to occupy Heaven Room Three?

After pondering for a moment, he turned to the maid kneeling before him and said, "Go call Steward Gu over."

"Yes!" The maid stood up and left the room.

Not long after, Gu Hong knocked and entered, cupping his fists. "Master Le, did you need something from me?"

Le Dongzheng said, "The person in Heaven Room Three... what's his background?"

Gu Hong was taken aback and replied awkwardly, "Master Le, why are you asking about this? You know the rules of the Commerce Guild. Although I'm a steward, the guests' information is absolutely confidential."

Le Dongzheng smiled faintly, "There are always exceptions, aren't there?"

Gu Hong said, "Master Le, you're making things difficult for me."

Le Dongzheng frowned. After dealing with Gu Hong for so many years, they both knew each other's temperament. Gu Hong was not someone who played strictly by the rules. With a light sigh, he said, "To be honest, I have a grudge with that boy."

Gu Hong asked in surprise, "Did he offend Master Le in some way?"

Le Dongzheng snorted coldly, "If it were just an offense, it would be nothing. Three of my disciples at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm died at his hands, and one of them was even my own descendant!"

"What!" Gu Hong was shocked. No wonder Le Dongzheng was seeking information on that person—there was such a deep grudge.

Le Dongzheng said, "Steward Gu, you saw it too. That boy bid without hesitation and is clearly wealthy. If I can take him down, I'll surely gain a hefty profit. How about I give you thirty percent when the time comes?"

Gu Hong put on a bitter face, "Master Le, it's not that I don't want to help you..."

"Fifty percent!"

"This, this..." Gu Hong pretended to hesitate for a while, then slapped his thigh and said, "Master Le, I don't really know much about his background. He suddenly appeared in the Commerce Guild yesterday, and I hadn't seen him before..."

"Looks like Steward Gu doesn't want to do me this small favor. Fine, in the future, I won't do business with the Ziyuan Commerce Guild anymore." Le Dongzheng snorted coldly.

Gu Hong smiled bitterly, "Master Le, it's not that I don't want to help, but I really don't know his background. However, after the auction, I can let you know where he's headed."

Le Dongzheng's eyes flashed with a sharp light, and he nodded, "Then I'll trouble Steward Gu."

Gu Hong rubbed his hands, "About that fifty percent you mentioned earlier..."

"You won't be shortchanged." Le Dongzheng waved his hand.

Gu Hong cupped his fists, "Thank you, Master Le. I wish you great fortune and success!"

Gu Hong stepped outside, a cold gleam flickering in his eyes. “Don’t blame me, boy,” he thought grimly. “You're just in my way.”

After Yang Kai appeared yesterday, he clearly sensed that Vice President Lou Chi's attitude toward him had cooled, while he was much warmer to Kang Siran. Thinking about what happened before and after Yang Kai came to the Commerce Guild, Gu Hong realized that Kang Siran must have used him to strike a big deal—one that was likely beyond his imagination.

Only in this way could Kang Siran turn his fortunes around. If Kang Siran did, then Gu Hong would be the unlucky one.

The branch would definitely have to transfer someone out. If Kang Siran stayed, then Gu Hong would be the one to go.

But if Yang Kai died and Kang Siran lost his support, he would no longer be a threat.

In Heaven Room Three, the maid delivered the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin. Yang Kai paid two million Upper-Level Source Crystals before taking the hairpin and playing with it for a while. Although he hadn't refined it and couldn't sense its true power, he could tell that the jade hairpin was crafted with great care and was a rare treasure.

Up close, the monkey-head insignia on the side was even more exquisite—crafted with lifelike detail, every feature vivid and true.

After admiring it for a while, Yang Kai casually tossed the jade hairpin to Qin Yu.

Qin Yu, still in a daze, fumbled to catch it, staring at Yang Kai in astonishment.

"It's yours," Yang Kai said, then closed his eyes to rest again.

"For me..." Qin Yu held the jade hairpin in her hands, her heart pounding and her cheeks flushing.

Just a moment ago, she had been wondering which woman held such an important place in his heart that he would spend two million Upper-Level Source Crystals to buy this item. Yet in the blink of an eye, the hairpin was in her hands.

Did Eldest Brother buy this just to give it to her?

Did she really hold such an important place in his heart?

Qin Yu couldn't help but let her thoughts run wild. She looked up timidly at Yang Kai, but the heat in her heart was instantly doused with cold water. Yang Kai had already closed his eyes, as if what he had given away was not a flying Secret Treasure worth two million Upper-Level Source Crystals, but some trinket from a street stall.

"Eldest Brother, this is too valuable," Qin Yu steadied herself and hurriedly refused.

Two million Upper-Level Source Crystals was an astronomical sum for the entire Qin Family. She and Yang Kai were not related by blood, so how could she accept such a generous gift?

As she spoke, she tried to return the jade hairpin.

Yang Kai opened his eyes and asked, "You really don't want it?"

Qin Yu bit her lip and shook her head. It was simply too valuable; she couldn't accept it.

Yang Kai smiled. “If you won’t take it, it’s no use to me either.”

Qin Yu lowered her head and whispered, "You could give it to the woman you like, Eldest Brother. No matter who it is, she would definitely be happy."

"But I've already given it to you," Yang Kai said.

"But I'm not..." Qin Yu's voice grew even softer.

Yang Kai said, "Your Qin Family's Myriad Sword has been in my hands for so long and has helped me many times. Just treat this hairpin as my thank-you gift to the Qin Family. Don't feel any burden."

"It's not the same," Qin Yu shook her head. They weren't that close, so what did it mean to give her such an expensive piece of jewelry?

"Then I'll just return the Myriad Sword to your Qin Family," Yang Kai said seriously.

"No!" Qin Yu quickly shook her head. The Myriad Sword belonged to the Qin Family, but it was an Imperial Treasure. The Qin Family simply couldn't protect it now. In fact, it was thanks to Qin Chaoyang back then that Yang Kai took the Myriad Sword away. If the Imperial Treasure had stayed with the Qin Family, they might have been implicated long ago.

"Then keep it. Two million Upper-Level Source Crystals is nothing to your Eldest Brother," Yang Kai said with a slight smile.

Qin Yu was stunned for a moment, but then felt inexplicably relieved.

What was an astronomical sum for the Qin Family was nothing to him. In other words, what he gave her wasn't a particularly expensive gift, but a simple token of thanks.

Thinking this way, Qin Yu felt much more at ease.

"Then... thank you, Eldest Brother," Qin Yu said softly, holding the jade hairpin in her palms.

To Yang Kai, it was merely a simple token of gratitude. But to Qin Yu, it was the most precious gift she had ever received in her life—its true value lay far beyond its price.

The auction continued. After the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin was sold for a sky-high price, the atmosphere became even more heated. It seemed everyone's competitive spirit had been ignited, and the price of each item kept climbing.

An hour later, the auction was nearing its end.

After a short break, the beautiful auctioneer returned to the stage, her eyes shining. "Next up is the grand finale of this auction... the Feihong Black Dragon Armor!"

As her words fell, she gestured with her hand, and two burly men immediately stepped forward from the rear, carrying a sturdy iron chest. Step by step, they made their way onto the auction platform, clearly struggling under its weight.

In an instant, over a thousand pairs of eyes focused on the high platform. Secretly, many people's breathing grew rapid.

The Feihong Black Dragon Armor had been advertised by the Ziyuan Commerce Guild three months ago, so many Emperor Realm experts had come specifically for it. It could be said that the value of this armor surpassed that of all the previous auction items combined.

Boom...

When the two burly men set the chest down, the platform let out a dull thud. The Feihong Black Dragon Armor was clearly extremely heavy, making many people shudder. But everyone knew that as long as it could be refined, no matter how heavy the armor was, it would become as light as air. Otherwise, just this one flaw would greatly diminish its value.

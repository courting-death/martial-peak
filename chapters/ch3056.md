## Chapter 3056 — Overbearing

The appearance of the Dragon Isle Token caused an uproar. The several hundred cultivators had all lived in Half-Dragon City for many years, so they were naturally familiar with tales of the Dragon Isle Token. However, they had only ever heard of it; this was their first time seeing the real thing. For a moment, hundreds of pairs of eyes turned toward the token and stared at it without blinking.

Fu Chi sneered. "You casually take out some token and claim it is a Dragon Isle Token? Do you truly think my Dragon Clan is so easily fooled?"

He simply could not contain his anger toward Yang Kai, so he could not resist making a mocking remark.

Yang Kai looked at him as though he were an idiot. “Great Elder already said this is the Dragon Isle Token. Are your eyes sharper than Great Elder’s? Or are you saying you do not believe him?”

Fu Chi froze, then said with an ugly expression, "Boy, do not make baseless accusations!"

Even as an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon, he could not bear being branded as someone who distrusted the Great Elder. He hurriedly looked toward Zhu Yan and said, "Great Elder, that was not what I meant."

Zhu Yan waved his hand slightly, then looked at Yang Kai. "Although this old man also senses that the aura of this Dragon Isle Token is pure, whether it is genuine still requires careful examination. If it is convenient, could you allow this old man to take a look?"

Yang Kai lowered his head and looked at Zhu Qing. Only after she nodded did he smile and say, "Then I will have to trouble Great Elder."

With a flick of his hand, he tossed the Dragon Isle Token toward the Great Elder.

The Great Elder caught it, formed a hand seal, and tapped one finger against the token.

In an instant, the dragon carved upon the token began to swim, and a clear, resonant Dragon roar rang out.

Fourth Elder Zhu Kong nodded. "Indeed. It is undoubtedly a genuine Dragon Isle Token."

In truth, the Elders had already been eighty percent certain when they first saw the token. However, this was a matter of great importance, so verifying it was naturally the safest course.

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "I heard that whoever holds a Dragon Isle Token may make one request of Dragon Isle..." At this point, his expression suddenly changed, and he shouted in shock and fury, "What are you doing?!"

While he was speaking, the Second Elder had suddenly reached for the Dragon Isle Token in the Great Elder's hand. Her movements were astonishingly fast, her outstretched fair hand leaving a trail of afterimages as it swept violently toward the token.

The Great Elder had clearly not expected Fu Zhun to act without warning. He turned his wrist to evade her, but the Second Elder had come prepared and followed his movement like a shadow.

The Dragon Clan's two senior Elders remained perfectly still, but their hands transformed into countless afterimages as they clashed in silence.

After a sharp smack, everything returned to calm.

Zhu Yan's brow furrowed, while Fu Zhun's expression remained cold and indifferent. Zhu Kong looked at Zhu Yan, then at Fu Zhun, and let out a long sigh.

Before everyone's eyes, the Dragon Isle Token in the Great Elder's hand suddenly crumbled into fine powder. It spilled through his fingers and vanished without a trace in the blink of an eye.

"What is the meaning of this, Second Elder?!" Yang Kai's face darkened as he glared at Fu Zhun through gritted teeth. Never had he imagined that this woman would openly destroy his Dragon Isle Token. This was simply shameless! He found it difficult to believe that an expert comparable to a Great Emperor could do such a thing in front of so many people.

He had already been somewhat distrustful of this woman, which was why he had only handed the Dragon Isle Token to the Great Elder after seeking Zhu Qing's opinion. Yet it seemed he had still miscalculated.

Zhu Yan also turned to look at Fu Zhun, seemingly waiting for an explanation.

Fu Zhun said indifferently, “I do not need to explain my actions to you. Since Great Elder and Fourth Elder have both determined that it is the Dragon Isle Token, I naturally will not deny it. For now, I will treat it as the Dragon Isle Token.”

“For now?” The veins on Yang Kai’s forehead throbbed wildly. Was this not twisting the truth right in front of him? Could she cast doubt on the Dragon Isle Token’s authenticity with just those two words?

Fu Zhun did not continue arguing with him over the matter and merely said coldly, "You trespassed on Dragon Isle without the Dragon Clan's permission. That is your first crime. You caused chaos on the day of the Dragon Clan's grand wedding. That is your second crime. You sullied the reputation of a Dragon woman of my clan. That is your third crime. Each offense is punishable by death. However, I will not kill you today. Consider that Dragon Isle Token exchanged for your life. Step aside at once!"

Yang Kai said defiantly, "When did I say I wanted to exchange that Dragon Isle Token for my life? Is Second Elder not being far too arbitrary?"

He was genuinely furious. He had taken out the Dragon Isle Token without even stating his request, yet this woman had destroyed it and then declared as though it were only natural that it would be exchanged for his life.

Even a forced transaction could not be this outrageous.

"If you will not exchange it for your life, do you wish to die?"

Yang Kai straightened his neck and shouted, "I want Dragon Isle to release her! Let Qing'er and me leave this place!"

"Wishful thinking!" Fu Zhun sneered contemptuously.

Zhu Qing said in anguish, "Second Elder, the Dragon Clan's ancestral rules state that whoever holds a Dragon Isle Token may make one reasonable request of the clan. Though you are the Second Elder, even you cannot disregard the teachings of our ancestors!"

Yang Kai turned toward Zhu Yan, his gaze sharp as a blade. "Great Elder, I need an explanation."

There was no reasoning with a woman like Fu Zhun. That bitch was simply a block of stubborn ice that would never thaw; saying anything more would be useless and only make him angrier. Yang Kai could only shift his attention to Zhu Yan.

The Dragon Isle Token had been destroyed while in the Great Elder's hand, so no matter what, he could not shirk responsibility.

Zhu Yan nodded upon hearing this. "Indeed, the Dragon Clan does have such a rule."

Yang Kai's spirits rose. Cupping his fists, he said, "In that case, this Junior asks for nothing else. I only ask Great Elder to show leniency and allow Qing'er and me to leave safely."

Zhu Yan fell silent for a moment, then shook his head. "I am afraid that is impossible."

"Great Elder..." The hope in Zhu Qing's eyes faded. If the Great Elder would not intervene on their behalf today, she and Yang Kai had no hope of leaving this place.

Yang Kai sneered. "The Dragon Clan truly is awe-inspiring. This Young Master has certainly broadened his horizons today."

The Great Elder shook his head. "It is not that this old man wishes to make things difficult for you. It is simply that your request... violates the Dragon Clan's laws, so this old man cannot agree. The Second Elder was correct. Since you possessed a Dragon Isle Token, we should grant you one request, but that request must be reasonable! You wish to take Zhu Qing away... That is absolutely impossible. You should leave on your own."

The Great Elder's answer was within Yang Kai's expectations, so he was not particularly disappointed.

He had previously asked Zhu Lie whether the Dragon Isle Token could be exchanged for Zhu Qing's freedom, but Zhu Lie had rejected the idea at the time. Bringing out the Dragon Isle Token today had merely been an attempt born of faint hope.

It now appeared that Zhu Lie had been right. Even with a Dragon Isle Token, the Dragon Clan could never agree to let him take a Dragon woman away so easily.

Zhu Kong also said, "Indeed. You should leave. My Dragon Clan will not make things difficult for you today."

Given the Dragon Clan's temperament, anyone else who dared cause such trouble today would have died without a burial place long ago. However, Yang Kai truly had possessed a Dragon Isle Token. No matter how discourteous he had been today, the token was still enough to preserve his life.

Of course, that was only if Yang Kai knew when to yield.

"Leave?" The Second Elder suddenly snorted. "When did I say he could leave?"

Hearing this, the corner of Yang Kai's mouth curled upward, and he mocked, "Does Second Elder intend to go back on her word and kill me after all? Good! Then let our strength decide the outcome. If this Young Master is going to suffer, your Dragon Clan can forget about enjoying any peace!"

"What an arrogant boast!" Fu Chi snorted.

The many members of the Dragon Clan also wore dark expressions. Yang Kai's words had undoubtedly provoked them all. They had long since found him offensive for daring to snatch a bride at a Dragon Clan wedding, yet even with death at hand, he still dared speak so arrogantly. He truly did not know the immensity of Heaven and Earth.

"Whether it is an empty boast depends on whether I have the strength to back it up." Yang Kai glanced sideways at Fu Chi and silently resolved that if a fight truly broke out, Fu Chi would be his first target.

An idiot like him had actually dared to steal Yang Kai's woman. Yang Kai had suppressed his rage for an entire month, and it had to be vented somewhere.

"Second Elder..." Zhu Yan frowned at Fu Zhun. "The Dragon Clan keeps its word. What is the meaning of this?"

The Dragon Isle Token had already been destroyed, so what harm was there in letting Yang Kai go? He could not understand why Fu Zhun would go back on her word.

Fu Zhun gazed at Yang Kai with calm indifference. "I have always kept my word. I said I would not kill him, and I shall not. But when did I ever say I would let him leave? He cannot depart Dragon Isle without leaving behind the Dragon Clan's Ancestral Origin!"

As soon as she said this, Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong's expressions grew solemn. They immediately understood what Fu Zhun was planning.

Yang Kai possessed the Ancestral Dragon Origin. Since he had delivered himself to their doorstep, they naturally could not let him go so easily. The Ancestral Dragon Origin was of tremendous importance. If they could obtain it, the strength of the entire Dragon Clan might rise dramatically.

Zhu Yan had previously wanted Zhu Qing to invite Yang Kai to Dragon Isle for precisely the same reason: he also had designs on the Ancestral Dragon Origin.

Thus, once he realized the Second Elder's intentions, Zhu Yan found it difficult to object.

As far as he knew, Yang Kai was proficient in the Laws of Space. If they truly allowed him to leave today, there was no telling whether they would ever have another chance to find him. Zhu Yan sighed softly and immediately fell silent.

"Second Elder, do not go too far!" Zhu Qing trembled all over with rage.

"You dare speak to me that way?" Fu Zhun's gaze turned sharp.

"What are you shouting about?" Yang Kai's gaze was even fiercer than Fu Zhun's. "Qing'er spoke to you that way. What are you going to do about it?"

"Boy, you keep pressing your luck and know neither advance nor retreat. You are courting death!" When had Fu Zhun ever suffered such an affront? Her anger finally overwhelmed her restraint, and she raised a hand and struck her palm toward Yang Kai.

Her palm instantly turned to crystalline ice, as though sculpted from ice and carved from jade. Peerless Ice Laws formed a swirling vortex that engulfed Yang Kai.

Her palm rapidly expanded before Yang Kai's eyes, seemingly capable of blotting out both Heaven and Earth.

Yang Kai shouted in a low voice, “Is this Young Master the one pressing his luck, or is it your Dragon Clan that is bullying others too far?” As he spoke, he pushed Zhu Qing aside. Then, under everyone’s stunned gaze, instead of retreating, he advanced, charging straight toward the Second Elder.

"Roar!"

As the furious roar rang out, Yang Kai's body rapidly swelled, transforming in an instant into an enormous eight-zhang-tall figure. Short horns sprouted from his head, and scales covered his entire body. The flesh across his body writhed rapidly as a series of explosive cracks rang out from his bones, while his hands abruptly transformed into sharp Dragon Claws.

Peerless Dragon Pressure spread outward as two streams of scorching air burst from his nostrils.

"The Dragon Transformation Art!"

Cries of alarm rang out, and the many members of the Dragon Clan stared in astonishment.

The Dragon Transformation Art was a secret technique of the Dragon Clan, so they were naturally extremely familiar with it. However, this was their first time seeing anyone display such astonishing might with it.

Although his eight-zhang body had only a Half-Dragon form, it still gave off an incredibly powerful sense of oppression, especially since his Dragon aura was incomparably pure.

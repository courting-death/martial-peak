## Chapter 2724 — Dragon Blood Flower

The scent of tea filled the pavilion as the three of them sat around the table, brewing tea and discussing the Dao, enjoying each other's company.

The subtle flattery and praise from Li Jiao and Mi Qi made Ji Ying feel even more delighted, causing him to wave his hand repeatedly and burst into hearty laughter.

No one knew how much time had passed when a strange sound suddenly came from somewhere in the medicine garden. Though the sound was faint, all three were Emperor Realm experts—how could they not notice?

Ji Ying sprang to his feet, his gaze fixed in a certain direction within the medicine garden, his face full of excitement. "The spirit flower is about to bloom!"

Mi Qi and Li Jiao exchanged a glance, each seeing the excitement in the other's eyes.

Li Jiao said, "Grandmaster Ji truly has sharp eyes. If not for you, I might not have known this spirit flower would bloom so soon."

Mi Qi laughed, "Grandmaster Ji is an Imperial-Grade Pill Master and a personal disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan. He knows all the spirit flowers and rare herbs in the world like the back of his hand. How could a rough man like you compare? If you were left to tend this spirit flower, you might miss its best blooming period and waste a heavenly treasure for nothing."

"Yes, yes, yes," Li Jiao nodded repeatedly. "Brother Mi Qi is right. If not for Grandmaster Ji's reminder, such a precious spiritual medicine might have been wasted. Since the spirit flower is about to bloom, why don't we go take a look?"

"Good, good!" Ji Ying nodded excitedly and took the lead, striding in that direction.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao exchanged another glance, their eyes meeting in silent understanding.

Soon, the three arrived at a certain spot in the medicine garden. Only a single spiritual medicine was cultivated here, about a foot tall, with tangled roots. What was astonishing was that this spiritual medicine exuded a peculiar aura. If Zhu Qing or Yang Kai were present, they would instantly recognize it as dragon aura.

However, this dragon aura was rather faint, not as pure as when the two of them activated their Dragon Clan bloodline.

Even so, it was still an extraordinary thing. After all, this was just a spiritual medicine.

Strangely enough, not only did this spiritual medicine emit a faint dragon aura, but its appearance also resembled a miniature coiled dragon. The tiny dragon scales on its roots were lifelike, complete with claws and tail. Atop the dragon's head, a blood-red flower bud gently unfurled, ready to bloom, like a lively butterfly about to take flight.

Ji Ying stood three zhang away from the spiritual medicine, holding his breath and focusing all his attention, watching nervously.

From the way Ji Ying stood there, it seemed less like he was waiting for a spirit flower to bloom, and more like he was anxiously awaiting the birth of his own child...

Li Jiao gazed at the soon-to-bloom spirit flower with a proud expression, secretly signaling Mi Qi with his eyes.

Mi Qi immediately spoke up, "Grandmaster Ji, is this Dragon Blood Flower really so precious?"

"What do you know?" Ji Ying was fully focused on watching the flower, and reflexively scolded when he heard Mi Qi speak. But he quickly realized the speaker was not his usual disciple, but the Sect Master of a great sect.

His tone softened a bit as he explained, "You are not alchemists, so naturally you don't know how rare the Dragon Blood Flower is. But Palace Master Li has been nurturing it with his essence-blood, so he must know something about it."

Li Jiao hurriedly said, "I'm rather muddleheaded about it myself. My understanding is quite shallow—I'll have to trouble Grandmaster Ji to enlighten me."

After tending it carefully for hundreds of years, how could he not know? But at this moment, he naturally had to pretend ignorance to satisfy the other's vanity.

Ji Ying nodded, "The Dragon Blood Flower is a specialty of Dragon Isle. Nowhere else in the world has it. Tell me, is it precious or not!"

Mi Qi exclaimed in shock, "Is that so?" He turned to Li Jiao, "Then Brother Li, where did you get this Dragon Blood Flower?"

Li Jiao smiled, "Many years ago, I found it during a trip and thought it was unusual, so I brought it back. After transplanting it, I found it was withering, so I tried feeding it with essence-blood. To my surprise, it revived, so I've been nurturing it with essence-blood ever since."

"That was quite a lucky accident," Mi Qi nodded slightly.

Ji Ying nodded, "Palace Master Li did the right thing. The reason the Dragon Blood Flower is unique to Dragon Isle is because it must feed on dragon blood to grow! Nowhere else has Dragons, so even if a Dragon Blood Flower appears elsewhere, it can't survive. Palace Master Li has Dragon Clan blood in his veins, so feeding it with essence-blood was exactly what it needed."

Li Jiao was not a pure human, but a half-human, half-yao. It was said he was the offspring of a human and a Jiao. Not many in the Northern Territory knew this secret, but Ji Ying and Mi Qi were among those who did.

The Dragon Blood Flower needed dragon blood to survive. Although Li Jiao's Dragon Clan bloodline was thin, it still existed. After years of careful care, the Dragon Blood Flower had finally grown to the point of blooming.

"I suspect that when Palace Master Li found this Dragon Blood Flower, there must have been a fallen Dragon Clan member there. Otherwise, it would have been impossible for a Dragon Blood Flower to appear," Ji Ying said solemnly.

Li Jiao admired him, "Grandmaster Ji, your insight is truly sharp. When I found the Dragon Blood Flower, I did indeed discover a Dragon skeleton there. I don't know how many years it had been dead, but it was already decayed and ruined."

"Dragon bones are indestructible and eternal. How could they decay so easily? It must be that the Dragon Blood Flower absorbed the nutrients from the bones."

"So that's how it is!" Li Jiao nodded like a clueless child.

Ji Ying said excitedly, "With the Dragon Blood Flower, you can refine Dragon Blood Pills. These will be of great use to you, Palace Master Li."

"What do you mean by that?" Li Jiao asked, barely containing his excitement and humbly seeking guidance.

Ji Ying smiled, "On Dragon Isle, Dragon Clan disciples devour Dragon Blood Flowers mainly to temper and purify their bloodline. You should have heard that Dragon Clan bloodlines are divided into different grades, and higher-grade bloodlines have overwhelming dominance over lower ones. So even if two Dragon Clan members have similar strength, if their bloodlines differ too much, the one with the lower bloodline can't resist the other at all."

Mi Qi and Li Jiao both nodded lightly.

They naturally knew about this.

"Dragon Clan disciples are born strong and can easily cultivate to the Emperor Realm, even without much effort. Their strength can rival that of a Third Stage Emperor. Their daily cultivation focuses on purifying their bloodline. The purer the bloodline, the higher the grade, and the stronger the power! The current Great Elder of the Dragon Clan is said to be an Eighth-Tier Azure Dragon, with strength comparable to a Great Emperor. Above that, there are even legendary Ninth-Tier and Tenth-Tier bloodlines! The Dragon Blood Flower is their most important treasure."

Li Jiao said excitedly, "Grandmaster Ji, do you mean that I can also…"

"Exactly!" Ji Ying nodded. "Although your Dragon Clan bloodline is thin, you can still enhance it by consuming the Dragon Blood Flower. Of course, if it can be refined into a Dragon Blood Pill, the effect will be even better."

Li Jiao's breathing suddenly quickened. Although he had secretly investigated this long ago, hearing it confirmed by Ji Ying made him feel that his centuries of effort had not been in vain.

He had obtained this Dragon Blood Flower centuries ago and had been nurturing it with his own essence-blood ever since, which caused his strength to stagnate for so many years. Although both he and Mi Qi were Third-Stage Emperors, if they really fought, he would definitely not be Mi Qi's match.

But now things were different. If he could consume and refine this Dragon Blood Flower, his strength would surely surpass Mi Qi's in one leap.

"It's just a pity…" Ji Ying suddenly changed the subject, frowning slightly.

"What is it?" Li Jiao's heart skipped a beat, afraid to hear bad news from Ji Ying.

Ji Ying smiled, "Palace Master Li, don't worry. I just mean that Dragon Clan bloodlines are divided into grades, and so are Dragon Blood Flowers. Even if this one blooms, it's probably the lowest grade."

Such a Dragon Blood Flower would probably not even catch the eye of a Dragon Clan member on Dragon Isle, but in this medicine garden, it was already the most precious thing.

Simply because it was so rare.

It was precisely because of this Dragon Blood Flower that Ji Ying stayed at Li Dragon Palace. Otherwise, he would have gone to Ice Heart Valley and Lingxiao Palace to collect various spiritual medicines himself. He wanted to witness the blooming of the Dragon Blood Flower—an opportunity no alchemist could pass up.

Although Ji Ying had studied alchemy under Great Emperor Miaodan for many years, this was his first time seeing a Dragon Blood Flower. As an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, seeing this spirit flower for the first time was naturally thrilling.

Mi Qi asked from the side, "Grandmaster Ji, besides being useful to Brother Li, do Dragon Blood Pills have any effect on human cultivators?"

Ji Ying smiled, "How could they not? They can increase strength, purify cultivation, and strengthen the body. Dragon Blood Pills have countless uses."

After a pause, he said, "Palace Master Li, if you trust me, once this Dragon Blood Flower blooms, could you let me refine it?"

Li Jiao was overjoyed and cupped his fists, "That is exactly what I hoped for. If Grandmaster Ji can help, nothing could be better."

He was overjoyed. Although he was the Palace Master of Li Dragon Palace, even with his status, it was never easy to ask Ji Ying to refine pills. He had gone to Medicine Pill Valley several times to request pills, and each time it cost him dearly and took a long time.

But this time, Ji Ying had taken the initiative and didn't ask for any reward.

It seemed the Dragon Blood Flower was just as attractive to Ji Ying.

"Grandmaster Ji, if you refine it, how many pills can you make?" Li Jiao asked cautiously.

Ji Ying replied solemnly, "I can't say for sure, but three pills is absolutely no problem."

In the past, when he refined pills, not only did he charge a fee, but he would also keep a third of the pills. That was his rule.

That was why Li Jiao asked how many pills Ji Ying could make. Now, hearing that three pills could be made, he was delighted. At the very least, he would get two Dragon Blood Pills, which would greatly help his strength!

"Congratulations, Brother Li!" Mi Qi cupped his fists in congratulations, though he felt a bit sour inside.

## Chapter 2743 — The World Is Truly Small

Yang Kai was unaware of the changes in Linghu Palace and Fenglin City. At this moment, he gazed curiously at the twin cities ahead, standing opposite each other in the distance. His eyes were filled with surprise, for he saw that both cities were teeming with people, bustling crowds shoulder to shoulder wherever he looked.

Even though he was still over a thousand li away, Yang Kai could sense the prosperity of these two cities.

It seemed Fenglin City truly was different now. He wondered how the Qin Family was doing these days. Back then, the Qin Family was considered a major clan in Fenglin City, with a Dao Origin Realm cultivator among them, but in the current situation, Dao Origin Realm was clearly nothing special anymore.

His Myriad Sword was a Qin Family artifact, and he had long ago made an agreement with Qin Chaoyang: once Qin Yu advanced to the Emperor Realm, he would return the Myriad Sword.

The Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation he taught to the disciples of Ice Heart Valley was also a secret technique of the Qin Family.

One could say that, although he hadn’t received much direct help from the Qin Family along his journey, he owed them many favors. On this trip back to Fenglin City, he naturally had to pay the Qin Family a visit.

He also remembered that he should visit Ruoxi’s family. Since he had returned to the Southern Territory, it was only right to pay a visit to Qingyang Shrine as well. After all, he was a registered disciple of Qingyang Shrine and even held a core disciple token.

He still remembered the matter of Senior Tian Yan. Now that he had found the Life Origin Fruit, he just needed to gather a few auxiliary herbs to refine the Life Origin Pill. Then he could release Tian Yan from the Divine Roaming Mirror and help him forge a physical body.

Tian Yan was a Great Emperor-level existence. Once he had a physical body and could walk the Star Realm, Lingxiao Palace would have a powerful backing in the future.

As Yang Kai was planning his itinerary, a streak of light suddenly shot toward him at incredible speed.

Closely following that streak of light was another ball of radiance, within which several figures could be vaguely seen.

Judging by the scene, it looked like a chase. The streak of light in front was a lone figure, while the group behind was numerous, all exuding Dao Origin Realm auras. Yang Kai didn’t pay them much mind.

In the Star Realm, such incidents were common—sometimes for wealth, sometimes for grudges. Countless people were killed every day. Even Yang Kai himself had been hunted down many times during his growth, always escaping death by a hair’s breadth.

The fleeing streak of light seemed to be in a panic, heading straight toward Yang Kai’s position.

Yang Kai frowned slightly. He wondered if the person had noticed him and wanted to draw trouble his way, but he couldn’t be bothered to intervene. He didn’t change direction, instead heading straight toward the streak of light.

A few Dao Origin Realm cultivators were nothing he needed to avoid.

In the blink of an eye, Yang Kai was only a hundred zhang away from the streak of light ahead.

Taking a closer look, Yang Kai discovered that the figure hidden within the light was curvaceous—a woman. He examined her face and was immediately surprised, stopping abruptly.

The woman ahead clearly noticed Yang Kai as well. Her beautiful eyes first showed confusion, then delight. “Yang Kai!”

Yang Kai smiled faintly. “The world is truly small—Protector Bian, it has been a long time. I trust you’ve been well.”

This woman was an old acquaintance, Protector Bian Yuqing of Biyu Sect.

Back when he and Liu Qianyun first arrived in the Star Realm, they were captured by Kou Wu of Biyu Sect and later took refuge under Bian Yuqing. They stayed in Biyu Sect for a while, until Wu Mengchuan, descendant of the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor, broke free and went on a killing spree, forcing them to flee in panic.

Bian Yuqing had been away on business at the time, escaping Wu Mengchuan’s slaughter. Later, she served under Wu Mengchuan.

Afterwards, the two met again in Four Seasons Land.

Thinking about it, Wu Mengchuan was able to enter the Shattered Star Sea because Bian Yuqing obtained a Star Seal in Four Seasons Land. That time, she brought Kou Wu to Four Seasons Land, likely under Wu Mengchuan’s orders to seek the Star Seal.

Unfortunately, Wu Mengchuan failed to wreak havoc in the Shattered Star Sea and was killed by Yang Kai, allowing the Incarnation to cultivate the Heaven Devouring Battle Art to completion and greatly increase his strength.

All these causes and effects seemed destined from the day Yang Kai first entered Biyu Sect.

These thoughts flashed through Yang Kai’s mind as Bian Yuqing rushed to his side, gritting her teeth. “Why is it you!”

Yang Kai grinned. “Protector Bian, you seem quite disappointed to see me.”

Bian Yuqing said, “I was trying to draw trouble away, but you… This isn’t the place to talk, we need to leave quickly.”

She admitted frankly that she flew this way to divert danger, which surprised Yang Kai. As she spoke, she brushed past him.

But after flying a short distance and seeing Yang Kai unmoving, she stopped anxiously. “Run! What are you doing?”

Yang Kai stood firm, staring at Bian Yuqing’s abdomen where a sword wound was visible. He frowned. “What grudge do you have with those people? Why are they chasing you?”

“Are you out of your mind? You still have time to ask me that?” Bian Yuqing was desperate. She was already at a disadvantage, and now Yang Kai stood still, which to her seemed suicidal.

She knew Yang Kai was a martial artist from the lower Star Domain, exceptionally talented and quick to cultivate, but it had only been a few years. She didn’t believe he could be stronger than her. She herself couldn’t defeat those people, so how could Yang Kai?

As they spoke, the ball of radiance behind them was now within three li. A furious shout came from within: “Wretched woman, stop running! Hand over the command token and we’ll spare your life!”

“Command token? What command token? Did you steal something from them?” Yang Kai frowned. If that was the case, Bian Yuqing had brought trouble upon herself.

“Who stole from them? It’s them who are trying to rob me!” She spoke quickly, “Are you leaving or not? If you stay, I won’t care what happens to you—they’ll definitely kill you to silence any witnesses.”

Though her tone was harsh, she was still thinking of Yang Kai, which made him view her in a new light.

Speaking of which, he and Bian Yuqing had no real enmity. Back in Biyu Sect, he caused some trouble, but Bian Yuqing spoke up for him, and he was only punished with seclusion at Ice Cliff to reflect.

“Go if you want.” Yang Kai smiled slightly.

“I won’t bother with you anymore!” Bian Yuqing snapped, whipping her head around and shooting forward at full speed.

But after flying a short distance, she suddenly turned back and landed beside Yang Kai again.

“Not leaving?” Yang Kai looked at her curiously.

Bian Yuqing’s face was sullen as she snapped, “Now you’ve gotten me killed. I really don’t know what you’re thinking.”

Yang Kai said with a grin, “Protector Bian, are you planning to share life and death with me? Fine, let’s be a pair of lovers in the Yellow Springs!”

“Get lost!” Bian Yuqing shot him a cold glare, her expression suddenly frosty. “In a moment, help me hold them off—I must kill that man myself.”

“What grudge do you have with them?” Yang Kai raised an eyebrow, curious.

“Kou Wu died at his hands!” Bian Yuqing’s voice was icy, her fists clenched tightly.

“Kou Wu is dead?” Yang Kai was shocked, his face darkening. Though Kou Wu had once captured him and Liu Qianyun for Biyu Sect, he later treated them well as a Senior Brother.

He recalled how, back in Four Seasons Land, Kou Wu had accompanied Bian Yuqing. Now, to hear he was gone made Yang Kai sigh with sorrow. The path of Martial Dao was truly perilous—one misstep, and all that remained was dust and ashes.

“He bought me a little time.” Bian Yuqing took a deep breath and said solemnly, “If you still care about our shared sect, help me this once.”

“Alright!” Yang Kai nodded.

Bian Yuqing added, “Those people are all Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. You can’t fight them head-on. If things go wrong… leave first, don’t worry about me.”

Yang Kai looked at her, truly seeing her for the first time.

Bian Yuqing smiled slightly. “Don’t look at me like that. Biyu Sect is gone. Kou Wu and I depended on each other. He’s dead, so I must avenge him.”

“You’ll get your wish.” Yang Kai nodded gently.

“Let’s hope so.” Bian Yuqing exhaled softly, closed her beautiful eyes, then opened them again, cold light flashing. A secret treasure in the form of a bracelet appeared in her palm.

As they spoke, the ball of radiance had already arrived, revealing three figures as the light faded.

All three possessed Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm cultivation. Judging by their attire, they seemed to belong to the same sect, but Yang Kai couldn’t tell which sect they were from.

The leader was a pale-faced, beardless man around forty, holding a sharp sword stained with fresh blood.

Bian Yuqing’s eyes instantly reddened at the sight of the blood.

She knew it was definitely Kou Wu’s blood.

“Wretched woman, run again if you dare!” The middle-aged man pointed his sword, shouting fiercely, his eyes full of murderous intent.

Bian Yuqing gritted her teeth, saying nothing.

The middle-aged man said, “Hand over the command token and we’ll spare your life!”

“Was it you who killed my disciple?” Bian Yuqing asked coldly.

The middle-aged man sneered, “That’s right, I killed him. Ran him through with a sword—he died swiftly and cleanly. He was fiercely loyal to you, you know. Even at the end, he sacrificed himself just to buy you a chance to escape.”

Bian Yuqing’s face was cold and gloomy, suppressing her anger. Her lips were already bleeding, her body trembling. “Soon, I’ll give you a quick death too!”

At her words, the three men burst out laughing.

The middle-aged man shot Yang Kai a sidelong glance and sneered, “Who knows where you picked up this helper, yet you dare speak so arrogantly. Boy, if I were you, I’d run as far as I could. A beauty may be tempting, but you need to be alive to enjoy her.”

He clearly thought Yang Kai was lured by Bian Yuqing’s beauty. To be fair, Bian Yuqing was indeed quite attractive, especially her mature charm, far beyond what young girls could offer.

Many men Yang Kai’s age preferred just that.

Yang Kai smiled lightly. “I’m afraid I can’t oblige.”

The man’s face darkened, snorting coldly. “Since you’re courting death, don’t blame us Senior Brothers.”

Bian Yuqing’s background and identity had long been investigated. She was just a person without sect or backing. If not for that, they wouldn’t dare target her for the command token.

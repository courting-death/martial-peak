## Chapter 2642 — Escape Unscathed

"Space Divine Ability, that's a Space Divine Ability!" Suddenly, someone called out in a low voice. Though not loud, the words quickly spread across the wilderness.

All the Emperor Realm cultivators changed color.

Space Divine Abilities were obscure and unpredictable. Across the entire Star Realm, those who could dabble in this path were few and far between. According to rumors, the one most proficient in the Power of Space was that person from Spirit Beast Island in the Eastern Territory.

But that person possessed the powerful cultivation of the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm.

Yet now, there was actually a First Stage Emperor who has also mastered the Space Divine Ability, and to such a degree—it was simply miraculous, making others envious beyond words.

"That bell must be the Mountain and River Bell!" Someone else recognized the origin of the bell.

"Mountain and River Bell? The relic of the Origin Cauldron Great Emperor?"

"That's right, it really is the Mountain and River Bell. I've long heard disciples say that in the Shattered Star Sea, the Mountain and River Bell appeared, but no one knew where it ended up. Now it seems someone has subdued the Mountain and River Bell and even brought it out of the Shattered Star Sea."

"Hiss... Proficient in the Power of Space and able to subdue the Mountain and River Bell—what incredible fortune."

"Who exactly is that person!"

Just as everyone was filled with suspicion, Feng Xuan, his face ashen, suddenly raised his head and roared, "Yang Kai, you little brat, this Lord will kill you!"

"Yang Kai!"

"Isn't that the one Wenqing Sect has been searching for all this time?"

"I heard he has close ties with Ice Heart Valley. That's why Wenqing Sect launched an attack on Ice Heart Valley—to force Bing Yun to reveal Yang Kai's origins."

"So that brat is Yang Kai!"

......

No matter how famous Yang Kai was before, after today, his name would surely resound throughout the land. Right in front of Wenqing Sect’s Sect Master Feng Xuan, he killed one of their Elders, abducted Nanmen Dajun, and escaped unscathed. Such skill was not something just anyone could possess.

And the explosive news that Yang Kai possessed Space Divine Abilities and the Mountain and River Bell naturally spread as well.

It likely won't be long before Yang Kai's name spreads throughout the entire Northern Territory.

Comprehending Space Divine Abilities is one thing—such insight depends on personal talent, and even if Yang Kai were captured, others might never grasp it. But the Mountain and River Bell is another matter entirely. That is the relic of the Origin Cauldron Great Emperor, a legendary artifact said to have been brought out from the Wild Ancient Land in the Primordial Era. Countless people covet it with envy.

Back in the battle of the Great Emperors, even someone like the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor had to risk injury to kill Origin Cauldron first, all because he feared the Mountain and River Bell in his hand.

This was a treasure even the number one in the Star Realm feared. If one could obtain it, wouldn't they soar to the heavens in a single leap?

Back at Ice Heart Valley, Yang Kai and Ji Yao flickered into view, returning in a flash.

The moment she sensed the grand array fluctuate, Changsun Ying immediately appeared. She glanced nervously at Ji Yao and Yang Kai, then saw blood at the corner of Yang Kai's mouth and exclaimed, "Senior Brother Yang, you're hurt!"

Yang Kai waved his hand and said, "It's nothing!"

Changsun Ying said, "You two are finally back. Master has been worried about you these past few days. If not for fear of alerting the enemy, Master would have acted personally long ago."

As she spoke, Changsun Ying curiously glanced at Nanmen Dajun, whom Yang Kai was carrying, and asked, "Who is this person?"

Ji Yao replied, "Nanmen Dajun!"

Changsun Ying was startled at first, but then her face lit up with joy. "You succeeded?"

A few days ago, she didn't know what Yang Kai had gone out to do, but after Ji Yao left as well, she realized Yang Kai was targeting Nanmen Dajun. Now that Nanmen Dajun had been captured and brought back, it was clear they had succeeded.

Yang Kai nodded, pushed Nanmen Dajun forward, and said, "I'll leave him to you. Whether to kill or spare him, it's up to Senior Bing Yun. I'm going to heal my wounds."

After speaking, he turned to Ji Yao and said, "Junior Sister Yao, come find me tomorrow. I have something I need your help with!"

Ji Yao didn't ask what it was, only nodded lightly.

Yang Kai's figure flickered and he disappeared from sight.

As for the captured Nanmen Dajun, though his cultivation was sealed, he showed no sign of panic. With the bearing of a true master, he looked around and said, "Ice Heart Valley. This Lord has long intended to visit, but I didn't expect it would be as a prisoner."

Changsun Ying and Ji Yao exchanged a glance. The former gestured, "Grandmaster Nanmen, please, I'll take you to see Master!"

Nanmen Dajun smiled slightly and said, "Thank you!"

His calm demeanor made many of Ice Heart Valley's female disciples see him in a new light. Although he was the cause of Ice Heart Valley's recent crisis, in truth, Nanmen Dajun bore no real grudge against them—he simply took money to resolve others' troubles.

Now that he had been captured, the crisis at Ice Heart Valley was resolved, so there was no need to make things difficult for him.

In a small courtyard room, Yang Kai sat cross-legged, swallowed a few Spiritual Pills, and quietly circulated his cultivation technique.

After a night's effort, Yang Kai was full of vigor, showing no trace of injury.

The next morning, Yang Kai pushed open the door and walked out.

Ji Yao was already waiting outside.

Yang Kai glanced at her and said, "I want to see Senior Bing Yun."

"Come with me!" Ji Yao responded, leading the way while taking out a Transmission Compass to send a message to Bing Yun.

Before long, the two arrived at Bing Yun's residence.

Upon meeting, Bing Yun naturally expressed her gratitude. If Yang Kai hadn't acted personally this time, even she might not have been able to capture Nanmen Dajun so easily.

Though her cultivation was impressive, some things can't be accomplished by strength alone.

"How does Senior plan to deal with Nanmen Dajun?" Yang Kai asked.

Bing Yun replied, "We've already agreed—Ice Heart Valley won't make things difficult for him, but he must never again become our enemy. For now, he's a guest. Once the war is over, we'll let him go."

Yang Kai nodded slightly. "Senior, you are truly kind-hearted."

If it were him, he'd definitely make Nanmen Dajun suffer a bit. Even if he didn't kill him, he'd have to give him a good beating to vent his anger.

Bing Yun smiled. "He is, after all, a formation grandmaster. In the future, Ice Heart Valley might need his help. It's best to form good relations if possible. Besides, he wasn't really targeting us."

Yang Kai no longer mentioned Nanmen Dajun and instead asked, "Senior, is what I asked you to prepare ready?"

Upon hearing this, Bing Yun immediately tossed a Spatial Ring to Yang Kai and said, "Everything is inside. See if it's enough."

Yang Kai swept it with his Divine Sense and nodded. "It's enough."

Bing Yun asked curiously, "Why do you need so many Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade? As far as I know, these are mostly used to refine Spatial Rings. Don't tell me you can refine artifacts?"

Yang Kai smiled. "I can't refine artifacts, but besides making Spatial Rings, these materials have another major use."

Bing Yun thought for a moment, then said, "Space Array?"

"Exactly!" Yang Kai nodded. "I want to set up a Space Array in Ice Heart Valley. Senior, do you know of a suitable place? Preferably somewhere well-guarded and hidden."

Bing Yun asked in surprise, "Where are you planning to go? Ice Heart Valley already has a Space Array that can reach most places in the Northern Territory. It's been closed recently, but it can still be opened."

Yang Kai shook his head. "I need to set one up myself."

Seeing his insistence, Bing Yun pondered for a moment and said, "How about the Ice Lake Forbidden Area?"

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. "The place where Zi Yu was once imprisoned? That's a good spot."

Bing Yun said, "Then let's choose there."

Yang Kai frowned. "But isn't that a forbidden area of Ice Heart Valley?"

Bing Yun smiled. "It used to be, but ever since you took that pearl from there, it's no longer forbidden."

Yang Kai was taken aback, then gave an embarrassed smile.

A few years ago, when he came to Ice Heart Valley, he took the Winter Pearl from that forbidden area. Because of this opportunity, he gathered all four pearls of spring, summer, autumn, and winter, and was able to comprehend the Divine Ability of the Great Emperor of Time in the Shattered Star Sea, gaining the Years Like a Shuttle Seal.

Ice Heart Valley had made that place forbidden only because the Winter Pearl was kept there. Now that the Winter Pearl was gone, it was no longer forbidden.

"Setting up a Space Array isn't easy. Yang Kai, are you also skilled in formations?" Bing Yun asked curiously.

Yang Kai shook his head. "I only know how to set up this one array. It was a stroke of fortune years ago—a master taught me."

As he spoke, a woman dressed in black appeared in Yang Kai's mind.

Yang Yan...

He hadn't seen her in years. In all his time wandering the Star Realm, he hadn't heard of any great force with a powerful expert named Yang Yan, nor any news of Lin Yun'er, whom she had taken away.

Collecting his thoughts, Yang Kai stood up and said, "No time to lose, I'll go set it up now. Junior Sister Yao, come with me."

Bing Yun instructed, "Yao'er, go with Yang Kai. Fulfill any requests he has."

"Yes!" Ji Yao agreed.

The two immediately left the main hall and headed for the former forbidden area of Ice Heart Valley, quickly arriving at their destination.

It was a small island in the center of a lake. The last time Yang Kai came, the place was bitterly cold because of the Winter Pearl, but now it was no different from the rest of Ice Heart Valley.

The altar at the center of the island still remained. The Winter Pearl had originally been placed atop it.

Yang Kai swept the island with his Divine Sense, carefully examining the surrounding terrain, and said with satisfaction, "This place is good. Let's do it here."

As he spoke, he waved his hand, sending countless materials flying out, including a large amount of Ethereal Crystal and Ethereal Jade.

Yang Kai had set up Space Arrays many times before. Back in the Hengluo Star Domain, the Space Arrays he arranged connected the entire domain, making travel between the various Cultivation Stars extremely convenient for cultivators.

Before Yang Kai set up those Space Arrays, if a cultivator in the Hengluo Star Domain wished to travel from one Cultivation Star to another, they might have to drift through the vastness of the Star Domain for several months, or even a year or more.

But with Yang Kai's methods, one could instantly reach their destination through the Space Array, greatly saving time and avoiding dangers along the way.

It could be said that Yang Kai's contribution to the Hengluo Star Domain was unprecedented. Before him, no one had ever achieved such a feat.

Having not set up a Space Array in years, Yang Kai was a bit rusty, but his foundation remained. After a moment of preparation, he began to work.

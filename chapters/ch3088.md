## Chapter 3088 — Ancestral Domain

"Why are you standing here?" Since she already knew this woman was Gale's First Chief, Lu Huaishuang put away her azure longsword and withdrew her hostility.

"Sir is bathing. What business would a woman like me have staying inside?"

"Oh." Lu Huaishuang nodded and did not dare knock on the door either, obediently standing off to the side.

For a time, there was no sound. He Yunxiang was still fussing with her makeup when irritation suddenly crossed her face. She flung the rouge box in her hand far away, then angrily reached out and clenched her fingers, condensing a clear, pure ball of water in her palm.

She used this water ball to wash the makeup from her face, revealing her natural appearance.

Letting out a breath, she said, "This will have to do." As if she were about to go to the execution ground, she put on an air of resignation.

Lu Huaishuang said, "Natural is better."

"You think so too, right?" He Yunxiang suddenly found this Young Lady of the Lu Family much more agreeable. She carefully sized Lu Huaishuang up and secretly compared the two of them. En, in looks, each had her own merits, and it was hard to say who was prettier, but their temperaments were completely different. She just did not know whether the Sir inside preferred women who were mature and enchanting or elegant and natural. As for figure, though...

He Yunxiang glanced at Lu Huaishuang's chest, and pride welled up in her heart.

"What are you looking at?" Lu Huaishuang clearly noticed her gaze, and her expression immediately turned somewhat unnatural. Inwardly, she could not help resenting her a little. Who knew what this woman had eaten to become so large?

The room door suddenly opened without a sound.

The two women exchanged a glance, and both their expressions suddenly turned serious.

He Yunxiang reached out to cup her own chest, then squeezed hard toward the middle, making that ravine even deeper and more alluring. Only then did she sway her waist and walk in.

Lu Huaishuang was speechless. She examined herself and, finding nothing improper, followed in as well.

"Sir!" He Yunxiang came before Yang Kai and bowed respectfully, intentionally or otherwise revealing the spring scenery at her chest.

Yang Kai nodded lightly and did not even look at her. Clearly, he had long since known she had restored her true body, so he was not surprised.

"Sir!" Lu Huaishuang also saluted, though hers was much more proper.

"Sit. There are some matters on which I would like your guidance." Yang Kai gestured with his hand, then sat in the main seat.

He Yunxiang covered her mouth and smiled. “I would not dare offer guidance, Sir. Whatever Sir wishes to know, I will certainly tell all I know and hold nothing back.”

Watching her, Lu Huaishuang truly recognized for the first time what it meant to curry favor with the powerful.

“Sir, would you like me to tie up your hair?” He Yunxiang volunteered, glancing at the long hair draped over Yang Kai’s shoulders. “I’m quite skilled at it.”

"Very well. Then I'll trouble you." Yang Kai smiled faintly at her.

“You are too polite, Sir.” He Yunxiang immediately stepped behind him, and a comb appeared from thin air in her hand. She gently began combing Yang Kai’s hair, looking every bit the devoted newlywed wife.

Lu Huaishuang took this scene in, and with a sudden movement of her hand, a complete tea set appeared on the table before her.

She took out the stored spirit spring water and began brewing tea with meticulous care.

She could not do what He Yunxiang was doing, so she could only begin with the tea ceremony. Fortunately, she had been proficient in this Dao since childhood. As she selected the tea and brewed it, her technique was practiced and her movements graceful, giving those who watched a pleasure all its own.

Very soon, an enticing tea fragrance spread through the room.

Without stopping her hands, He Yunxiang said, "Is this Cloud Mountain Spirit Mist Tea?"

Lu Huaishuang said, "First Chief has good eyesight."

He Yunxiang smiled. "It is only that this tea is too famous. What eyesight do I have? Today, it is thanks to Sir that I can taste one of the top three teas of the Star Domain."

Yang Kai smiled faintly but did not speak, which inevitably discouraged Lu Huaishuang. The Lu Family had paid a great price to obtain this tea, and the amount was less than three liang. On ordinary days, they would never take it out to drink, but bringing it out today had not produced the effect it should have.

But on second thought, if this Sir truly came from that place, then one of the top three teas of the Wuji Star Domain probably really would not catch his eye.

She immediately restrained her thoughts and brewed the tea seriously.

A short while later, the tea was ready. Lu Huaishuang ladled out a cup and respectfully pushed it before Yang Kai. "Sir, please have some."

Yang Kai nodded and did not stand on ceremony. He picked up the teacup and took a sip, savored it for a moment, then drained it in one gulp. "Not bad!"

For such spirit tea to exist in a Star Domain was indeed not bad.

Having received praise, Lu Huaishuang finally revealed a smile.

"You sit too." Yang Kai turned his head and spoke to He Yunxiang. His hair had already been combed, but He Yunxiang had not sat down immediately and was still standing to the side.

"Yes." He Yunxiang smiled and took a seat, poised and at ease, without much restraint.

After three rounds of tea, Yang Kai suddenly said, "There are some things I want to ask you two."

Both women put down their teacups at the same time and sat upright.

"Does your Wuji Star Domain have any connection or contact with other Star Domains?"

Hearing this, both women shook their heads.

Yang Kai's heart immediately sank. Unwilling to give up, he asked, "Truly?"

He believed these two women could not possibly be deceiving him, but the answer he obtained disappointed him beyond measure.

Lu Huaishuang said, "As far as I know, there truly is no such thing."

He Yunxiang, however, picked up on something else and asked carefully, "Does Sir wish to go to another Star Domain?"

Yang Kai said, "Correct."

He Yunxiang asked curiously, "Then why did Sir not go from the Ancestral Domain to the Star Domain you wanted to reach, but instead come to our place?"

Yang Kai stared at her in astonishment. "What is the Ancestral Domain?"

He Yunxiang blinked. "Sir, are you not from the Ancestral Domain?"

"I have never heard of it."

Lu Huaishuang's small mouth also fell open. She exchanged a glance with He Yunxiang, and both saw the shock in each other's eyes.

How was that possible? If he did not come from the Ancestral Domain, how could he possess such powerful cultivation and strength? They had always thought Yang Kai came from the Ancestral Domain. Who would have thought this guess was actually wrong?

Both women felt somewhat muddled. If he did not come from the Ancestral Domain, why would he be rejected by the Heavenly Way, and why could he kill Void King Realm experts with a casual gesture?

"If I may ask presumptuously, where does Sir come from?" He Yunxiang stared dazedly at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai hesitated for a moment, but still said, "The Star Realm."

"The Star Realm?" The two women were at a loss again.

This time, it was Yang Kai's turn to feel surprised. "You have never heard of it?"

He Yunxiang and Lu Huaishuang shook their heads together, and suddenly both found it somewhat funny. Everything each side mentioned was completely unknown to the other; it rather felt as if an ox head had been matched to a horse mouth.

"Mm, I suppose your side has neither an entrance leading to the Star Realm nor any precedent for it." Yang Kai thought of a possibility.

Many Star Domains were completely sealed off. Not every Star Domain's cultivators could enter the Star Realm. The Wuji Star Domain was probably one of them.

"We truly have never heard of the Star Realm, but people from our Wuji Star Domain can enter the Ancestral Domain," Lu Huaishuang explained.

"What exactly is the Ancestral Domain?" Hearing them mention it again and again, Yang Kai also became curious.

"The Ancestral Domain is the ancestral land of all Star Domains!"

How could He Yunxiang let such a chance to gain favor with Yang Kai slip by? Without waiting for Lu Huaishuang to speak, she took the initiative to explain. "In the boundless world, there are countless planes and innumerable Star Domains, but has Sir ever wondered how these Star Domains were born?"

"I have not."

He Yunxiang pursed her lips in a smile and waved her hand. Spiritual Qi surged before them, and a round ball the size of a washbasin suddenly appeared. Pointing at that Spiritual Qi sphere, she said, "This is the Ancestral Domain! It is the place where Heaven and Earth were first born."

Yang Kai nodded, indicating for her to continue explaining.

He Yunxiang waved her delicate hand, and some Spiritual Qi suddenly separated from that sphere, slowly stretching outward. As she acted, she explained, "No one knows exactly what happened over those ancient years, but in that place where Heaven and Earth began, by some fortuitous coincidence, another plane was derived. And that plane was a Star Domain." As her delicate hand moved, the separated Spiritual Qi gradually transformed into a Spiritual Qi sphere the size of a fist, attaching itself beside the original Spiritual Qi sphere.

Her hand moved again, and another Spiritual Qi sphere was derived. Then a second, a third...

In the blink of an eye, densely packed Spiritual Qi spheres floated in the air, varying in size and brightness, some near the original Spiritual Qi sphere and some far away.

From time to time, some Spiritual Qi spheres were extinguished, while at the same time, others were born.

But no matter which Spiritual Qi sphere it was, none could compare with the original one.

Yang Kai suddenly felt as if his eyes had been opened, and he said in surprise, "There is actually such a wondrous theory?"

If this were truly so, then how had the Star Realm been born? And in this wondrous theory, what sort of position did the Star Realm occupy?

He Yunxiang said, “Though the various Star Domains are not connected to one another, every Star Domain is linked to the Ancestral Domain in countless ways. I have heard that some Star Domains maintain especially close ties with the Ancestral Domain. In those places, once a cultivator’s strength reaches a certain level, they can travel to the Ancestral Domain in pursuit of a higher Martial Dao.”

Lu Huaishuang continued, "That is why we both thought Sir came from the Ancestral Domain."

Yang Kai shook his head and said, "I have never been there, and I had never heard the term Ancestral Domain before." After pausing, he said, "What about the Wuji Star Domain? Can it lead to the Ancestral Domain?"

If so, then he might still have a chance to return to Hengluo Star Domain. He only needed to use the Ancestral Domain as a stepping stone. According to what He Yunxiang had said, the Ancestral Domain had countless ties with every Star Domain, so naturally it would have such ties with Hengluo Star Domain as well.

"It can, yes," He Yunxiang said with a bitter laugh, "but we do not know where the entrance is."

"In all these years, has no expert from your Wuji Star Domain ever entered the Ancestral Domain?"

"There have been!" Lu Huaishuang nodded solemnly. Those who could enter the Ancestral Domain were all the very top experts, and only such people were qualified to survive there. Although the two of them were in the Void King Realm, neither was among the very top, nor did they have the means.

"The experts who entered the Ancestral Domain have never returned either. So most people speculate that perhaps it is impossible to return to a Star Domain from the Ancestral Domain."

Perhaps that was possible, but Yang Kai felt the greater possibility was the Heavenly Way's rejection, or else that after experiencing even greater power, those people's sights were no longer set on a Lower Plane Star Domain.

Just like him. If possible, he would not casually go to a Star Domain either. With that time, it would be better to remain in the Star Realm and improve his cultivation.

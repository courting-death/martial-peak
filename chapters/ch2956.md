## Chapter 2956 — Since They’re Here, I’ll Deal With Them

That sedan bed was the height of luxury, draped in a sheer pink veil that gave off an air of hazy decadence.

There were people inside the sedan, one man and two women, all reclining lazily. Fine wine and fresh fruit were laid out on the couch. The man held a woman in each arm, sipping wine and tasting spirit fruit, his face full of satisfaction. The two women wore very little, large swathes of snowy skin exposed as they nestled in his embrace, wriggling and cooing, their coquettish voices constantly vying for his attention in a subtle contest of jealousy.

The man acted as if no one else existed, his large hands roaming wantonly over the women’s delicate bodies, drawing out breathy moans from them again and again.

The people along the road all knelt, not daring to raise their heads, and had no idea of the endless spring scenery within the sedan. Only the women’s seductive voices drifted out, making the listeners’ lower bellies burn as if set aflame.

In the blink of an eye, the sedan bed arrived in front of the teahouse. Before it had even landed, the sedan bearers, all Dao Origin Realm cultivators, straightened up and stood at attention.

All around fell silent, and the wanton sounds from within the sedan became even more jarring.

Yang Kai stared wide-eyed, watching with great interest.

Zhu Qing’s cheeks flushed slightly. She snorted coldly, and when she saw Yang Kai’s soul practically leaving his body with lust, she couldn’t help but kick him under the table, glaring, “What are you looking at? If you keep staring, your eyeballs will fall out.”

Yang Kai grinned, baring his teeth. “Qing’er, you look so cute when you’re jealous.”

“Nonsense.” Zhu Qing’s face reddened even more. “Who… who’s jealous?”

The two of them flirted openly, drawing cold looks from the surrounding cultivators. A bald, burly man strode into the teahouse, looking like a vanguard. His cultivation was quite impressive, about the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, his aura deep and long.

He glared coldly at Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, barking, “The Eldest Prince is here! Why aren’t you kneeling?”

“So he really is a prince.” Yang Kai and Zhu Qing exchanged a glance. He’d just said it offhandedly before, but hadn’t expected the newcomer to actually be a prince.

He and Zhu Qing had only just arrived in Human Sovereign City, not even the time it takes for an incense stick to burn. They’d only had a minor conflict with the Third Prince’s people at the Space Array, and now, in just a short while, another prince had appeared.

Although the Third Prince had taught him a small lesson, he’d then sent people over, clearly wanting to recruit him as a subordinate.

Who knew what this Eldest Prince was plotting by coming here? But seeing the prince with a woman in each arm, Yang Kai had his suspicions and felt a bit annoyed, his tone turning unfriendly: “Sorry, but I don’t have a habit of kneeling to anyone. If you have something to say, say it. If not, get lost.”

The bald man was furious at these words, shouting, “Insolence!”

As soon as he finished, he raised his hand to strike, but a soft, languid voice came from the sedan: “Stop right there! Always so quick to fight and kill—what if you hurt a beauty?”

At these words, the bald man immediately stopped, but shot Yang Kai a fierce glare.

The voice from the sedan said, “Beauty, let’s get acquainted. I am Fu Shu, the Eldest Prince of Human Sovereign City. Are you interested in coming to play with this prince?”

Zhu Qing didn't answer, but the two women nestled in Fu Shu's arms grew displeased. One of them crawled onto Fu Shu's lap and brought her lips close to his, passing him a sip of wine from her mouth as she pouted coquettishly, "The Eldest Prince is so bad! Isn't it enough to have us sisters serving you? How could you want to find someone else? I hate it!"

The other woman chimed in sweetly, “That’s right, Eldest Prince. What’s so good about that cold-faced woman? How can she compare to us sisters, who are so attentive and considerate? Don’t bother with her. Who knows where that country bumpkin came from? Let’s just go back to the palace. My sister and I have come up with a great idea lately—we’re sure you’ll be satisfied.”

The Eldest Prince swallowed the wine, feeling light all over. He squeezed the two women’s soft bodies, drawing out coquettish cries, and laughed, “You two are great, but she’s not bad either. I’ll just take her in as your new sister.”

“No, no, we already have enough sisters, Eldest Prince. You can’t take in any more.”

“Exactly! I can barely count all our sisters as it is. Besides, you promised me just a few days ago that you wouldn’t fool around anymore. How can you go back on your word so soon? I won’t allow it!”

The Eldest Prince gave a dry laugh. “Yes, yes, I did say that, but that was before I met this beauty. I’ll just take in one more—just one more, and then I’ll stop for good.”

“You’re lying, Eldest Prince. I don’t believe you.”

“Don’t, Eldest Prince. There’s nothing special about that woman. Oh… she does have red hair. If you like, we sisters can dye our hair red and sway it in front of you every day until you’ve had your fill.”

“That’s not the same.” Fu Shu shook his head repeatedly. “You don’t understand. This beauty may not look special, but I hear she’s from the outside world. I’ve seen countless women, but this is the first time I’ve met one from outside. I can’t let her go so easily.”

“She’s from the outside?”

“What’s so special about that? Women are all the same. Eldest Prince, you…”

“Enough, all of you shut up.” Fu Shu’s tone suddenly turned cold. The two women, who had been acting spoiled, immediately fell silent. They knew their place and their status in the prince’s eyes—they were just playthings. It was fine to act cute while favored, but if they tried to stop the prince from having his fun, they’d lose his favor in an instant.

There were countless beauties in the prince’s palace; he didn’t need just these two to serve him.

So, sensing the prince’s change in tone, the two women didn’t dare disobey. One quickly changed her tune: “If the Eldest Prince likes her so much, then take her in. It’s just one more sister, as long as you’re happy.”

“My sister’s right. That woman’s looks aren’t bad either. She’s barely qualified to serve the Eldest Prince.”

Fu Shu beamed, kissing each woman on the cheek. “You two are the most understanding. I haven’t spoiled you for nothing.”

The two women squirmed in his arms. The first one said, “But it seems that beauty already has someone in her heart, Eldest Prince. What will you do?”

Fu Shu laughed, “That’s just because she doesn’t know how great I am. Once she’s tasted my skills, she won’t think of any other man. Watch closely—this prince will conquer her right now!” As he spoke, he broke free from the two women, straightened his disheveled clothes, stepped out of the sedan, and entered the teahouse, looking straight at Zhu Qing.

At a glance, his heart soared with joy.

He hadn’t seen clearly through the veil before, but now he saw that the red-haired woman was stunningly beautiful, almost unreal. Her expression was cold, but that only stoked a man’s desire to conquer.

As he’d said, he’d never tried a beauty from outside before—not for lack of power, but because it had been many years since anyone from outside had entered Human Sovereign City.

He was the Eldest Prince, second only to one in Human Sovereign City, and could have any beauty he wanted with a single word. No woman dared resist; all did their utmost to please him.

Having grown accustomed to delicacies and fine fare, it was a rare joy to occasionally sample something different.

So the colder Zhu Qing’s expression, the more he liked her. His heart surged with desire, and he wished he could take this red-haired beauty back to the palace right now and dote on her.

His burning gaze clung to Zhu Qing’s face like a leech, roaming up and down without restraint, about to scrutinize her from head to toe.

Yang Kai’s figure flickered, and he stood in front of Zhu Qing.

The Eldest Prince’s face changed instantly, as if he’d seen a pile of stinking filth, and he said with disgust, “What’s your name?”

Before Yang Kai could answer, Fu Shu said, “It doesn’t matter what your name is. If you want to live, get out of the way. Since you’ve offered up a beauty, I can grant you the status of a Leaf.”

At these words, the surrounding cultivators’ eyes all burned with envy.

The status of a Leaf! Though it was the lowest rank among the nobility, it was still nobility, granting privileges ordinary people could never enjoy in Human Sovereign City. Instantly, it set one apart from the commoners. These people had followed the Eldest Prince for years, but none had ever received such a reward. Yet this outsider, just for bringing a beauty, was granted such an honor. The prince’s many followers couldn’t help but feel jealous, sighing at the man’s luck.

The bald man stepped forward, shouting at Yang Kai, “Boy, thank the prince and step aside! Do you want to die?”

Yang Kai sighed dejectedly, looking earnestly at the prince. “Your Highness, my wife and I are deeply in love and don’t wish to be separated. I have no intention of offering her up. Your Highness has power and countless beauties at your side—those two in the sedan seem quite good. Why fixate on a married woman? If word got out, it would harm your reputation. Why don’t we just part ways here and meet again if fate allows?”

Fu Shu’s face darkened. “Are you insulting my intelligence, or your own?”

Yang Kai’s mouth twitched. He realized his suggestion was indeed childish.

Fu Shu said, “I don’t want to bully others with my status either. How about this: since you like the two in the sedan, I’ll give them both to you. The one behind you, I’ll take.”

At these words, the two women in the sedan went stiff as if struck by lightning. They dared not say a word, their faces full of misery and tears falling in silence.

Yang Kai exclaimed, “Wouldn’t Your Highness be at a loss then?”

Fu Shu snorted, “This prince is magnanimous. What’s the harm in letting you take advantage?”

Yang Kai said, "But I have no interest in taking what belongs to another. As long as I can be with my wife, that's all I need."

Fu Shu waved impatiently, “Then take it as a reward. No need to thank me.”

Yang Kai opened his mouth, not knowing what to say.

Behind him, Zhu Qing said, “He’s got his eyes glued to me. What are you going to do?”

Yang Kai grinned, flashing a bright smile. “What else can I do? Since they’re here, I’ll deal with them.”

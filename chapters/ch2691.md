## Chapter 2691 — Almost There

Fortunately, no one knew what Wu Ming’s master was busy with, for after Yang Kai brought back the Gong Family Head, there was not the slightest reaction from him.

About a quarter of an hour later, Hua Qingsi once again stepped onto the high platform.

The crowd's spirits lifted, knowing the second half of the auction was about to begin. Everyone looked up at the stage.

On the high platform, Hua Qingsi called out sweetly, "Thank you all for waiting. Next up for auction... is a cultivator at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm!"

As soon as she spoke, the crowd erupted, all eyes fixed on the stage.

After all, the auction had long advertised that a Star Lord from a lower Star Domain would appear. Hearing Hua Qingsi's words, everyone instinctively thought this Second Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator was that Star Lord.

Everyone was curious about the Star Lord. After all, in the vast Star Realm, there was no such thing as a Star Lord.

There had been many tens of thousands of years ago, but ever since the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor ravaged the Star Domains, all the passages between the Star Realm and the major Star Domains had been sealed. The experts of the Star Realm could no longer easily travel to those lower Star Domains to refine Stellar Origins.

Now, the sudden appearance of a Star Lord was an extremely rare occurrence.

Every Star Lord possessed a complete Stellar Origin. If it could be extracted, one could inherit the position of a Star Lord and possess a complete Stellar Origin. The path of Martial Dao ahead would be smooth sailing.

Under everyone's gaze, an old man with a sinister aura, gaunt and skeletal like a ghost, suddenly appeared from backstage. He must have been placed under some restriction, for his aura was completely suppressed. Even so, he gave off a chilling feeling, especially those sunken eyes—like two ghostly flames flickering, truly terrifying.

"Is this the Star Lord?"

"Doesn't seem all that special."

"He possesses a complete Stellar Origin, yet only reached the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Looks like his aptitude isn't much."

"What do you know? It's said that the cultivators who come from lower Star Domains all have top-tier aptitude and steady temperaments. They dominate their own Star Domains. If not, how could they endure so much hardship to reach the Star Realm? Every cultivator from a lower Star Domain is on par with the core disciples of the great sects in terms of talent."

The hall buzzed with discussion, people whispering and pointing at the frail old man on the stage.

Even if you've never eaten pork, you've seen pigs run.

Although it's not easy for cultivators from lower realms to reach the Star Realm and their numbers are few, many know that those Star Domains lack the excellent cultivation environment of the Star Realm. For them to reach the peak in their own Star Domains is already a rare achievement.

For this reason, when they first arrived in the Star Realm, Liu Qianyun told Yang Kai that cultivators like them were highly welcomed by many great sects. As long as they revealed they were from a lower realm, they would surely be accepted and nurtured by some major sects.

Because cultivators like them had a much better chance of advancing to the Emperor Realm than most in the Star Realm.

"Ghost Ancestor..." In Room One, Yang Kai looked at the frail old man on the stage with a complicated gaze, recognizing him at a glance.

Ghost Ancestor was always shrouded in ghostly energy, so ordinary people could never see his true face. But now, with his cultivation sealed, he could no longer summon that ghostly aura.

His skeletal appearance wasn't due to torture, but simply his natural state. He cultivated the Ghost Dao, and his flesh and blood had long since dried up, transformed into ghostly energy.

On the high platform, Hua Qingsi continued, "He is not a Star Lord, but he is a friend of that Star Lord and came from the lower Star Domain as well, so there's no need to worry about his aptitude."

"Not a Star Lord..."

"What is this?"

"Auctioning someone who's not even a Star Lord? Are they kidding us?"

The people below shouted in protest, none of them buying it.

"Jie jie jie jie..." Ghost Ancestor let out a cold laugh, like a wailing specter, making all the cultivators in the hall turn pale.

"Enough, Sister Hua," Yang Kai suddenly called out. "Your master should be ready by now. Send the person over."

Ghost Ancestor's laughter stopped abruptly. He looked in astonishment toward Room One, and the ghostly flames in his eyes flared with strange light.

Hua Qingsi gave a wry smile. "Alright."

She hadn't wanted to do this either, but her master had instructed her, so she had no choice. Now that Yang Kai had spoken, she would naturally obey, especially since her master had not objected. Clearly, Yang Kai was right—her master was indeed ready, and there was no need to delay any longer.

As she spoke, Hua Qingsi gestured, and a maid immediately led Ghost Ancestor toward Room One.

The door opened, and Ghost Ancestor entered. Upon seeing Yang Kai, he excitedly cupped his fists and said, "Sect Master!"

"Elder, you've suffered," Yang Kai raised his hand and helped him up.

Ghost Ancestor said with shame, "It is this old man who lacked skill."

Back in the Hengluo Star Domain, he might not have been invincible, but he was a powerful figure with Third Stage Void King Realm cultivation. There was hardly anyone in the entire Star Domain who could surpass him. But after coming to this Star Domain, he was constantly suppressed. Now, he had even become a prisoner, put on display as an auction item.

It was truly a great humiliation.

Ghost Ancestor knew the enemy was powerful, far beyond what he could handle. If Yang Kai hadn't suddenly appeared, he would have been bought by someone and spent his life as a slave.

The thought alone was chilling.

Ye Hen, Ye Qinghan, and the others hurried forward to greet him as well.

Ghost Ancestor and the others had spent quite some time in the Thousand Leaves Sect, so they were already familiar with each other. Now that he was free, they were naturally happy for him.

"Let me remove your restriction," Yang Kai placed his hand on Ghost Ancestor's shoulder, sending out his Divine Sense to investigate while asking, "How are Chi Yue and the others?"

Ghost Ancestor replied, "Their lives aren't in danger, but their cultivation is sealed. It seems they'll all be brought out for auction soon."

Yang Kai nodded, "That's good."

With that, his Emperor Yuan surged and poured into Ghost Ancestor's body.

A flush of red appeared on Ghost Ancestor's aged face, and he spat out a mouthful of black blood. Yet, he suddenly felt his whole body lighten, and the sealed Origin Qi within him began to flow again.

Ghostly energy surged out, enveloping him. Ghost Ancestor said in disbelief, "Sect Master, your cultivation..."

He didn't fully understand how powerful the one who sealed his strength was, but he had some idea.

That was a legendary Third Stage Emperor Realm expert.

He hadn't expected Sect Master to break the seal in just a few breaths. Did that mean Sect Master's cultivation was on par with that old monster? No wonder Ghost Ancestor was so shocked—if that was the case, it was simply unbelievable.

After all, they had all come to the Star Realm together. Now, the rest were only at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, while Yang Kai had far surpassed them. He couldn't imagine how Yang Kai had cultivated all these years.

"Just entered the Emperor Realm," Yang Kai patted Ghost Ancestor's shoulder. "We'll catch up later."

Ghost Ancestor nodded, still deeply shaken. Although Yang Kai's cultivation wasn't as high as he'd imagined, it was still astonishing to reach the Emperor Realm in such a short time.

Especially since, with just First Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, he could easily break a seal set by a Third Stage expert. That was no ordinary feat.

Back in the Hengluo Star Domain, Yang Kai was already known for creating miracles—defeating the strong while being the underdog. It seemed that even after coming to the Star Realm, this had not changed; he was still the same, always turning the impossible into reality.

Realizing this, Ghost Ancestor felt a surge of excitement.

They had no one to rely on in the Star Realm, and now even the Thousand Leaves Sect, where they had taken refuge, had been destroyed. With Yang Kai suddenly displaying such strength, he could become their pillar.

On the high platform, another person emerged from backstage. Yang Kai looked over and saw it was Gu Cangyun, the former Alliance Master of the Sword Alliance.

Hua Qingsi didn't even bother to introduce him, simply signaling the maid, who led Gu Cangyun toward Room One.

In no time, he was brought into the room.

Upon seeing Yang Kai, Gu Cangyun was both surprised and delighted. The last time they had been in trouble in Tianhe City, Yang Kai had saved them. Now, a few years later, something similar was happening again.

This made Gu Cangyun sigh inwardly—when it comes down to it, you can only count on your own people.

Next to be brought out was Ai Ou.

The auction hall fell into a strange silence. They thought the first half of the auction had been odd enough, with only Yang Kai and Hua Qingsi interacting and no one else getting a word in.

But the second half was even stranger.

The auctioneer wasn't conducting a proper auction, and Room One wasn't placing bids, and yet one cultivator after another was being led out and sent into Room One.

What was even stranger was that the Emperor Realm experts in the private rooms remained silent—no one questioned or objected.

No one had ever attended such an auction. The whole thing seemed like a farce, leaving many people deeply dissatisfied.

"Almost there!" After settling Ai Ou, Yang Kai suddenly narrowed his eyes and spoke.

"Almost what?" Ai Ou looked at him suspiciously, the shock still not gone from his face.

When he learned Yang Kai had advanced to the Emperor Realm, he hadn't dared believe it. Only after Yang Kai broke his seal did he accept it as fact.

As Yang Kai's prospective father-in-law, Ai Ou didn't know whether to envy or rejoice. He could only sigh inwardly—Xue Yue really found herself a good man. He just wondered if, during all these years in the Star Realm, Yang Kai had attracted any other women.

There were plenty of good women in the Star Realm. With Yang Kai's abilities and the brilliance he displayed, it was only natural for him to have a few women by his side.

If, one day, Xue Yue could make her way from her home Star Domain to this Star Realm, she might not be able to compete with the women here. As a father, Ai Ou couldn't help but worry. Sometimes, having a son-in-law who is too outstanding isn't necessarily a good thing—especially when it comes to matters of the heart.

## Chapter 2929 — Guixu

Nearly ten Emperor Realm experts struck out together, and among them were even those at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. What a spectacular scene it was! Rays of light from all sorts of secret techniques and divine abilities blossomed, the might of Imperial Treasures rampaged freely, and the Spiritual Qi of heaven and earth was thrown into utter chaos.

Feng Ming also reacted in time, and a burst of light erupted from the Yanlei Spear, exploding directly ahead.

All the attacks surged into that giant mouth, but it was as if they had sunk into the sea—there was not the slightest response.

The giant mouth snapped shut, swallowing Feng Ming whole in a single bite.

“Hiss…”

The sound of cold breaths being drawn rang out. Everyone felt their scalps tingle, staring in terror at the one-zhang-long little beast, their eyes nearly popping out of their sockets.

Yang Kai was also somewhat dazed.

He had encountered this little black dog twice before. The first time was after the Qingyang Shrine Elders had all gotten drunk together, and the second was when he was arranging a Space Array. Other than his Dantian’s Demonic Qi stirring a bit the second time, there was nothing particularly noteworthy about it.

Thinking back now, it seemed he had misjudged it.

This little black dog most likely inherited the bloodline of some exotic beast. Otherwise, it could not possibly possess such a bizarre ability. For even a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert like Feng Ming to be swallowed in one bite—regardless of Feng Ming’s carelessness—such a feat was enough to leave one in awe.

After swallowing Feng Ming, the giant mouth vanished, and the little black dog returned to its original size. However, it still appeared extremely violent and aggressive.

With Feng Ming’s fate as a warning, who else dared to approach recklessly? Each displayed their own divine abilities, unleashing them all at once toward the front.

The giant mouth reappeared, swallowing all the Emperor Realm attacks. It was as if the mouth was connected to a bottomless pit—no matter how much was sent in, it was devoured cleanly.

Gong Yue hid behind the little black dog, his hands constantly shifting through seals, controlling the beast to battle the many experts. Even while outnumbered, he did not fall behind in the slightest.

Several times, Yang Kai wanted to teleport over and capture the ringleader, but each time Gong Yue saw through him in advance and blocked his attempts.

Divine Sense surged among the Emperor Realm experts. After a round of exchanges, not a single person recognized what exotic beast’s bloodline the little black dog had inherited. None had ever heard of such a heaven-swallowing, earth-devouring ability. With no clues, no one dared to act rashly for fear of ending up like Feng Ming.

In ancient times, there were indeed many powerful exotic beasts between heaven and earth. The Divine Spirits were the outstanding ones among them, each possessing the power to move mountains and overturn seas. Yet, there was never any record of a creature as peculiar as this little black dog.

Amidst the struggle, a flash of inspiration struck Yang Kai’s mind, and he shouted in a low voice, “Demonic Beast?”

Seated behind the little black dog, using it as a shield, Gong Yue smiled faintly. “You truly are different from the rest. You know quite a lot. This beast has a trace of Guixu bloodline. Though not pure or thick, it is still extraordinary. If you all wish to kill this old man, you’ll have to get past it first.”

“Guixu?”

The Emperor Realm experts present were all shocked at these words. No one had ever heard this name before, not even Elder Chen Qian, who was most well-versed in history.

Yet, it was undeniable that this little black dog with a trace of Guixu bloodline was absurdly powerful—almost heaven-defying. Under Gong Yue’s command, with a single bite, a hole would appear in the void. The entire world before it seemed like a delicious cake, and each bite left a mark that would not heal for a long time.

After several such instances, the area around Spirit Sword Peak was riddled with void holes. Looking around, it was as if a beautiful landscape painting had been splattered with ink. Within those holes was pure nothingness, containing immense lethality, forcing everyone to avoid them carefully.

Only Yang Kai moved through them like a fish in water, darting about freely. He leapt and dashed through the void holes, seeking opportunities to bypass Guixu and ambush Gong Yue. Several times he nearly succeeded, but each time Guixu doubled back and forced him to retreat.

“We can’t drag this out any longer. Brother Feng may be in danger.” Chen Wenhao shouted in a low voice, his expression grave. “When the moon is full, it wanes; when water is full, it overflows! Everyone, lend me your strength!”

As his words fell, the Waterflow Sword in his hand suddenly radiated a misty light. Under the spread of Imperial Might, he seemed to become an indestructible sword, his sword intent soaring to the heavens.

The Spiritual Qi of heaven and earth within a radius of dozens of li was stirred, all converging toward the Waterflow Sword.

Everyone’s eyes lit up at his words, and each began casting their own spells.

Gong Yue’s brow furrowed imperceptibly, but his hands did not stop moving.

Nearly ten Emperor Realm experts unleashed their full power. The entire Qingyang Shrine seemed to tremble. Centered on Spirit Sword Peak, it was as if a massive vortex had appeared, drawing in all the Spiritual Qi within a thousand li. All sorts of fierce and surging divine abilities were brewing.

“Sword Dao Without Bounds!” Chen Wenhao was the first to strike. As the Waterflow Sword slashed forward, a visible tidal wave of Spiritual Qi surged forth. Within that giant wave, countless sword lights flickered, seemingly able to destroy anything in their path.

“Life and Death Seal, one seal to discern life and death!”

“Nine Suns Shine Together!”

“Reverse the Heavens!”

“The years wither and flourish, like a shuttle, like a dream!”

…

Countless divine abilities blossomed together, shattering the sky and turning the universe upside down. With a thunderous rumble, they pressed forward. With so many Emperor Realm experts joining forces, even a Third Stage Emperor Realm would be torn to pieces if they tried to block the way.

Gong Yue’s expression grew incomparably solemn, and his hand seals changed even faster.

A beastly roar shook all directions. Guixu’s body remained unchanged, but its mouth suddenly opened wider than ever before, as if it could swallow half a mountain. Inside the giant mouth was utter darkness, as if it connected to another world.

One after another, mighty attacks fell into its mouth, raising visible ripples before vanishing without a trace.

In the span of just three breaths, Guixu had swallowed all the attacks. It snapped its mouth shut, and its one-zhang-long body swayed in midair as if drunk, its body flickering with lights of varying intensity.

The hearts of nearly ten Emperor Realm experts sank to rock bottom. Each wore an ashen expression, and even Chen Wenhao’s confidence was greatly shaken.

He had called for everyone to attack together in hopes of bursting Guixu from within. As he had said before, when the moon is full, it wanes; when water is full, it overflows. Though Guixu’s heaven-swallowing, earth-devouring ability was bizarre, it could not be limitless. As long as it had a limit, they could defeat it.

Yet, even after joining forces, they had not managed to do anything to it. The result was far from what they had expected.

“Again! It can’t hold on much longer!” Yang Kai’s explosive shout suddenly rang in everyone’s ears.

In an instant, the group seemed to find their backbone and once more gathered their strength to unleash their divine abilities.

Moments later, accompanied by a thunderous roar, Guixu’s mouth opened wide again. This time, however, it was different—Guixu revealed an expression of extreme pain. This discovery made everyone’s spirits soar, feeling that victory was within reach.

Sure enough, this time, after Guixu opened its mouth, it failed to swallow all the attacks. Halfway through, it abruptly shut its mouth, and the remaining attacks swept past, the violent power tearing its flesh and leaving it battered and bloody.

Its physical body did not seem particularly strong; it was only that special divine ability that left people at a loss.

It swayed unsteadily, its mouth tightly shut, its belly constantly churning as if something was brewing inside.

“To not return the favor would be impolite. Everyone, take a move from this old man as well!” Gong Yue smiled profoundly, raising his right hand before his chest, thumb and ring finger joined, the other three fingers pointing upward. As he finished speaking, he pointed forward.

Everyone’s hearts were suddenly filled with a sense of foreboding, an inexplicable aura of death enveloping them. Many felt chills all over, their hands and feet turning cold.

Guixu suddenly opened its mouth, spewing out beams of dazzling light, each carrying a familiar aura.

“This is…” Chen Wenhao exclaimed in shock, his face full of astonishment and horror.

What Guixu spat out was actually the divine abilities they had unleashed earlier. This time, however, the targets were not the enemy, but the very ones who had cast them.

No one had expected Guixu to possess such a strange ability. Not only could it swallow others’ attacks, it could even spit them back out unchanged.

In an instant, everyone suffered a terrible misfortune.

Just now, in order to end the battle quickly, everyone had used their most powerful divine abilities without holding back. Now, those very attacks had become weapons in the enemy’s hands. The reversal was so sudden that no one could adapt.

The attacks came too abruptly for anyone to react. All they could do was hastily gather their Emperor Yuan for protection.

At the critical moment, Yang Kai stepped forward, instantly appearing above everyone’s heads. The Laws of Space surged wildly around him as he slammed his palm downward.

Without any warning, as his palm descended, he and the other Emperor Realm experts suddenly vanished.

Beams of mighty divine abilities struck empty air, carving visible marks into the void before crashing into distant peaks. The entire Qingyang Shrine shook violently, chickens and dogs scattering in chaos. Countless disciples stared in panic at the collapsing mountains, not knowing what had happened.

Since the founding of the Shrine, they had never experienced anything like this.

Several hundred zhang away, figures flickered in the void as Yang Kai reappeared with the others. Each wore an expression of lingering fear, and Yang Kai’s face was as pale as paper, his whole body trembling violently.

Forcing a spatial divine ability on others without their cooperation was an enormous drain on him—especially this time, when he had brought so many, all of them powerful. He was completely spent in an instant.

At this moment, even standing in place took all his strength.

“Teleportation!” Gong Yue’s gaze darkened as he looked off into the distance, his face twitching, eyes full of venomous hatred.

Clearly, he blamed Yang Kai for ruining his plans. If not for Yang Kai’s interference, that sudden attack just now would have been enough to kill or injure more than half of the Emperor Realm experts present.

But it was precisely because of Yang Kai’s presence that everyone managed to escape unscathed.

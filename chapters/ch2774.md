## Chapter 2774 — Heavenly Sound Treasure Box

When the melody began to play, the Emperors were mostly unaffected, but the younger disciples brought by their elders reacted in various ways. The lower their cultivation, the stronger their response. One martial artist at the Void King Realm even let out a moan, his face filled with pleasure, and a glint of comprehension flashed in his eyes as if he had gained some insight.

The clear, tinkling sound continued to ring out as the box rose layer by layer. Only now did everyone see that it was not some ordinary cosmetic case, but rather a treasure shaped like a pagoda. As each layer lifted, the pagoda gradually took form, standing about three feet tall, and at a glance, it astonishingly had nine distinct tiers.

Each tier spun—some to the left, some to the right, some fast, some slow. As the pagoda rotated layer by layer, the melody gradually changed, yet remained just as melodious and pleasant to the ear.

When the pagoda was fully formed, a hazy mist began to emerge. The mist was a deep blue and quickly filled the great hall.

Looking up, everyone saw that above the great hall was a vast, star-studded sky, countless stars twinkling brilliantly.

It seemed as if they were no longer in the hall, but in the boundless starry sky itself. It felt as though the stars were within arm's reach, giving everyone a truly wondrous sensation.

The faces of the Emperor Realm experts grew solemn, faintly realizing the marvelous use of this treasure box. Each began to calculate in their hearts what price they would have to pay to obtain it.

Crash...

With a soft sound, the myriad stars shattered and vanished, and the breathtaking scene disappeared without a trace. Accompanied by the melodious music, the treasure box slowly retracted, returning to the appearance of a cosmetic case, with nothing remarkable about its exterior.

The Dao Origin Realm and Void King Realm martial artists instantly snapped out of their daze, each showing a look of lingering fascination, their eyes burning as they stared at the treasure box.

Holding the treasure box, the old man smiled and said, "This is the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box, the heavenly music it produces and the illusions it creates are greatly beneficial for the cultivation of martial artists below the Dao Origin Realm. With its assistance, cultivation will yield twice the result with half the effort, and there are absolutely no negative side effects. I obtained this treasure box by chance in my youth. Since I entered the Saint Realm, it has accompanied me for a thousand years. My achievements today are largely thanks to it."

Hearing the old man's explanation, everyone immediately understood that their earlier guesses were correct. Although the Emperors present could not feel the wondrous effects of the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box, the reactions of the younger disciples made it clear that this treasure was indeed of great help to those with lower cultivation. With the strange and marvelous melody and the beautiful illusions, it greatly enhanced their cultivation.

This was not something that could be matched by taking Spiritual Pills. Every Spiritual Pill contained a trace of toxicity. Taking a few was fine, but too many would hinder a martial artist's growth. The Heavenly Sound Treasure Box was different—everything about it was completely natural.

Everyone grew even more curious about who could have crafted such a treasure.

Judging by the old man's words, the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box had accompanied him for a thousand years. That meant it was definitely not something that could be made by artificers of today. It was likely an artifact from a distant era, perhaps even dating back to ancient times.

The old man continued, "To be honest, I am reluctant to exchange such a treasure. However... I am alone in this world, with neither descendants nor disciples. If the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box remains with me, it will only gather dust. I hope it can continue to serve its purpose, as a way to thank it for all the help it has given me over the years."

The old man's words were tinged with loneliness, but they struck a chord in everyone's hearts.

Generally speaking, martial artists like the old man, who have no Sect or backing, face a path full of thorns and hardships, rarely achieving great success. Advancing to the Emperor Realm is even more difficult.

Yet now he was an Emperor Realm expert, most likely thanks to the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box.

"However, there is one thing you should all know. As miraculous as the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box is, it has no effect on those in the Emperor Realm, and even for the Dao Origin Realm, its effects are greatly diminished. Only martial artists below the Dao Origin Realm can truly benefit from it."

The old man seemed to be an honest person, so he did not hide anything and revealed the shortcomings of the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box as well.

But even so, what did it matter?

The Emperors present were not like the old man, alone in the world. They either had countless disciples or their own family members, and those below the Dao Origin Realm were innumerable.

Take the Qingyang Shrine for example. Such a large Sect had martial artists of all levels, and those below the Dao Origin Realm were everywhere. The Heavenly Sound Treasure Box would be of great use to them.

If the Qingyang Shrine could obtain the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box, their disciples below the Dao Origin Realm would have another option for cultivation in the future.

This item was of immense appeal to Sects and families.

So as soon as the old man finished speaking, someone in the audience could not help but ask, "Elder Lu, may I ask what you want in exchange for this Heavenly Sound Treasure Box?"

"That's right, Elder Lu. Name your price so we can prepare. We certainly won't let you down."

Some Sect Masters even secretly sent voice transmissions to Elder Lu, offering him positions as Elders or even Vice Sect Master, hoping he would join their Sect. Unfortunately, the result was destined to disappoint them. There were countless people like Elder Lu in the Star Realm—some lacked opportunities or talent and could not join Sects, while others were free spirits unwilling to be bound by Sect rules.

Elder Lu was undoubtedly the latter. Otherwise, with his Emperor Realm cultivation, any Sect would welcome him, even Qingyang Shrine would have a place for him.

Elder Lu politely declined all those secret invitations.

After a while, Elder Lu finally spoke, "A few years ago, I began cultivating a secret art that requires the Inner Core of Demonic Beasts. So, I hope to exchange the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box for Demonic Beast Inner Cores."

"Demonic Beast Inner Cores!" Upon hearing this, some were delighted while others were troubled, their expressions varied.

"The Inner Cores I need must be from Twelfth-Tier Demonic Beasts, and I want two complete sets of Five Elements Inner Cores!"

"Five Elements Inner Cores?"

"And two sets?"

A wave of exclamations rang out as many Emperor Realm experts frowned, feeling that Elder Lu was asking for too much.

Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores—even the Lower-Level ones—were worth two or three million Upper-Level Source Crystals each. A set of Five Elements Inner Cores—gold, wood, water, fire, earth—would cost at least ten to fifteen million, and since it was a complete set, the price would be even higher, likely approaching twenty million.

One set of Five Elements Inner Cores was twenty million; two sets would be forty million!

And that was calculated using Twelfth-Tier Lower-Level Inner Cores.

The Heavenly Sound Treasure Box was extraordinary, but it was only useful for martial artists below the Dao Origin Realm. Who would spend tens of millions of Upper-Level Source Crystals on it? Even if they had them, it would not be worth it.

So as soon as Elder Lu named his price, the Emperors who had been interested fell silent.

Many people turned their eyes to Qiu Ran and the elders of the Qingyang Shrine.

Two sets of Five Elements Inner Cores—none of the Emperors present could produce them. Only the host of this gathering, the Qingyang Shrine, could possibly have them.

Everyone immediately understood that Elder Lu had brought out the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box at this occasion specifically to target the Qingyang Shrine for this transaction. If even the Qingyang Shrine could not meet his demand, the exchange would be called off.

Sure enough, the Divine Soul power of several elders from the Qingyang Shrine fluctuated as they clearly communicated via voice transmission.

After a moment, Qiu Ran slowly shook his head.

Elder Lu, standing on the high platform, immediately looked disappointed.

"The Shrine doesn't want this treasure box?" Yang Kai sent a voice transmission to Gao Xueting.

He was a Guest Elder of the Shrine and had no authority to decide matters concerning the Shrine's interests, so Qiu Ran and the others had not consulted him during their discussion.

"The price is too high, and the Shrine doesn't have complete sets of Five Elements Inner Cores," Gao Xueting replied.

Twelfth-Tier Demonic Beast Inner Cores were rare, and complete sets even rarer. Although the Qingyang Shrine's treasury had more than a dozen Twelfth-Tier Demonic Beast Inner Cores, all of good quality, they were missing the earth attribute, so they could not meet Elder Lu's demand.

"If the Shrine doesn't want it, then I'll make an offer," Yang Kai said with a grin.

Gao Xueting looked at him in surprise. "You have two sets of Five Elements Inner Cores?"

Yang Kai chuckled and said, “Why not discuss it with him and see if there’s a way?” Then, raising his voice toward the high platform, he called out, “Elder Lu, would you take a look and see if this might meet your requirements?”

As he spoke, he tossed out a Spatial Ring.

Elder Lu caught it and swept it with his Divine Sense. At first he was pleased, but then he frowned and said, "Elder Yang, this seems to be a bit lacking."

Inside the ring was a set of Twelfth-Tier Five Elements Inner Cores, but only one set. The other two sets were Eleventh-Tier Upper-Level ones.

Yang Kai shook his head. "Twelfth-Tier Demonic Beast Inner Cores, especially complete sets, are not so easy to obtain. Even these were acquired by chance. Elder Lu, see if you can accept this. If not, then forget it."

Everyone immediately understood that what Yang Kai offered probably did not meet Elder Lu's requirements, but it was still something he needed, which was why Elder Lu hesitated.

Elder Lu fell silent and did not give an immediate answer.

Yang Kai suddenly sent a voice transmission: "But I do have some information I can offer Elder Lu, regarding Twelfth-Tier Demonic Beast Inner Cores."

"What is it?"

"At Fenglin City’s Ziyuan Commerce Guild, every six months, two Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores appear, even Upper-Level ones. Elder Lu, keep an eye out and you might be able to gather another set."

"How do you know this?" Elder Lu asked in surprise and delight.

Those Inner Cores were all provided by me—how could I not know?

Yang Kai found it amusing and replied, "I am close friends with a manager at the Ziyuan Commerce Guild. He told me this himself."

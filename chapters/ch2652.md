## Chapter 2652 — Wenqing Sect's Fall

Seeing Bing Yun and the others about to leave, the Valley Lord of Cang Le Valley, Chen, opened his mouth as if to speak, but hesitated. His expression was strained, seemingly wanting to admit defeat, but recalling his earlier actions of aiding the wicked, he swallowed his words again.

He knew that, at this point, nothing he said would make a difference.

Regret gnawed at his heart. If only he had known Ice Heart Valley could summon such powerful allies, he would never have gotten involved with Wenqing Sect. Now, not only was Wenqing Sect facing annihilation, but even Cang Le Valley might not survive.

He was too ashamed to speak, but someone else broke the silence.

"Sister An, please wait! I beg you, for the sake of our past sisterhood, spare me this time. I truly made a mistake, please forgive me."

It was the Elder Yu, dressed as a woman, who cried out in panic.

Earlier, when the many experts of Wenqing Sect arrived in force, she had quarreled with An Ruoyun, accusing her of being ungrateful and saying that if she remained stubborn, she would surely meet a bad end. She had acted as a loyal servant to Wenqing Sect.

But who could have guessed that, in less than the time it takes to burn an incense stick, the tables would turn so completely?

Elder Yu’s face was thick enough, but her words drew contemptuous looks from many.

Still, losing face was better than losing one’s life. If a few words could save her, what harm was there in swallowing her pride?

Unfortunately, her opportunistic nature ensured that An Ruoyun would pay her no heed. In years past, Elder Yu had frequently visited Ice Heart Valley as a guest, and she and An Ruoyun had maintained a respectable friendship. But now that she had arrived together with Wenqing Sect, she was nothing less than a sworn enemy.

So, despite hearing her plea, An Ruoyun had no intention of responding, not even bothering to say a word, and quickly charged into the valley under Bing Yun’s lead.

"Sister An..." Elder Yu’s face turned ashen, her desperate cry echoing in vain.

Seeing that begging An Ruoyun was useless, Elder Yu suddenly turned her gaze to Yang Kai, forcing a smile. "Young Brother, I am not a member of Wenqing Sect. I was confused this time and shouldn’t have opposed Ice Heart Valley, but I now understand and regret my actions. Please, Young Brother, be magnanimous and spare me. I am willing to pay any price."

As she spoke, her beautiful eyes shimmered like water, her red lips pressed together, presenting a pitiful appearance.

She had realized that the three Yao Kings who had suddenly appeared seemed to follow Yang Kai’s orders. If she could persuade Yang Kai, she might survive.

Though her true age was certainly not young, she maintained her looks well. From her appearance, she seemed about thirty, full of charm. Now, deliberately acting weak and delicate, she exuded a seductive allure.

Yang Kai sneered, "You wretch, still dreaming of seducing the young. Disgusting! Kill her, so she doesn’t taint my eyes!"

Elder Yu’s pretty face changed dramatically. She hadn’t expected Yang Kai to be so ruthless and merciless. With a scream, she unleashed all her power and retreated, trying to escape.

She was fast, but Yingfei’s was faster. A flash of green light, and Yingfei appeared before her, his fingers like blades, stabbing fiercely at her chest.

The Emperor Yuan protecting her body shattered like bubbles under Yingfei’s strike. His sharp fingers pierced her chest like swords, and with a fierce tug, he pulled out a blood-red heart.

Yingfei struck again, sending Elder Yu crashing to the ground, dead on the spot.

"Kill!" There was no need for Yang Kai to give further orders. Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei, the two Yao Kings, roared and charged into Wenqing Sect’s ranks. Yingfei returned, moving as a streak of green light.

The three mighty Yao Kings tore through Wenqing Sect’s ranks like tigers among sheep. In an instant, chaos reigned—no one could withstand their onslaught, nor could anyone hope to escape. Especially before Yingfei, famed for his speed, the very thought of fleeing was nothing but a fool’s dream.

Mangled corpses rained from the sky, blood blooming like crimson flowers—beautiful, yet laced with a chilling murderous intent.

In less than ten breaths, fourteen or fifteen Emperor Realm cultivators were wiped out.

Whether by accident or design, everyone else died, but Feng Xuan remained unharmed. The three Yao Kings didn’t attack him at all. While they slaughtered, Feng Xuan simply stood in midair, coldly watching.

Only after everyone else had perished did Feng Xuan let out a soft sigh, his face seeming to age several years in an instant.

Wenqing Sect has fallen!

After standing in the Northern Territory for tens of thousands of years, passed down from the hands of the Great Emperor, Wenqing Sect has fallen—not at the hands of Ice Heart Valley, but at the hands of a young man in the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

Feng Xuan still couldn’t understand who this young man was, or why he seemed to be Wenqing Sect’s nemesis!

When he appeared in Ice Heart Valley years ago, the marriage alliance between Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley fell through. Later, Feng Xuan’s own son was killed by him. Now, even the entire Sect had capsized in this gutter.

"The last one—who will do it?" Xi Lei stared at Feng Xuan and asked calmly.

As Yao Kings of the Wild Ancient Land, Xi Lei and the others had their own pride. Earlier, the three had joined forces because their enemies were many. Now, with only one left, there was no need to cooperate.

Deciding who would kill this man was a troublesome matter.

All three Yao Kings knew this was a chance to shine in front of Yang Kai, so none wanted to let the opportunity slip by.

"I’ll do it!" Xie Wuwei replied immediately. As he activated his Yao Yuan, a giant scorpion shadow appeared behind him, its tail swinging and glowing green, clearly poisonous.

"Why you?" Yingfei looked at him with displeasure.

"Do you want to go instead?" Xie Wuwei snorted coldly.

"Why not?" Yingfei raised his brows and stepped forward, ready to act.

"Let this King handle it." Xi Lei chuckled, his fox tail showing.

"Nonsense!" Yingfei and Xie Wuwei glared at him together.

At that moment, Feng Xuan, who had been silent, suddenly widened his eyes, releasing a shocking light. His powerful Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivation erupted, pouring into the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear in his hand. The relic of the Great Emperor trembled violently, transforming into a purple thunderbolt.

"Die, you little beast!" Feng Xuan roared, his figure flickering as he charged at Yang Kai, wrapped in purple lightning.

Xi Lei and the others stopped arguing. As Feng Xuan suddenly attacked, all three moved. Yingfei was fastest, rushing at Feng Xuan, his hands turning into eagle claws, grabbing at him with countless shadows, seemingly sealing the heavens.

Xie Wuwei and Xi Lei, wrapped in fierce Yao Qi, attacked Feng Xuan from left and right, unleashing their full strength.

Boom boom boom...

Thunderous explosions rang out. Emperor Yuan and Yao Qi surged, the purple lightning carrying a will to destroy everything, tearing space as it collided with the three Yao Kings.

A muffled groan sounded. Yingfei was sent flying, landing unsteadily, his hands covered in blood, even his fingers twisted and scorched as if struck by lightning.

Xie Wuwei also spat out a mouthful of blood, the giant scorpion shadow behind him shattering, his face pale, clearly badly injured.

The strongest, Xi Lei, was thrown over a hundred zhang before finally stabilizing, his face full of shock.

The three Yao Kings had joined forces, yet Feng Xuan alone had injured them all.

Sss sss sss sss...

Like the final radiance before death, the purple lightning flickered a few more times before suddenly collapsing into nothingness.

Feng Xuan stood less than ten zhang from Yang Kai, gripping the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear, glaring at him with gritted teeth.

But those ten zhang now seemed like an unbridgeable chasm, blocking Feng Xuan’s path.

Yang Kai stood motionless, his gaze cold.

"You little beast! Even as a ghost, I won’t let you go!" Feng Xuan’s lips bled, his smile twisted and fierce.

As he spoke, his Third Stage Emperor Realm aura rapidly weakened, and his black hair turned white in the blink of an eye.

Though he had injured the three Yao Kings alone, Xi Lei and the others weren’t weak. By risking injury, they not only blocked Feng Xuan’s desperate attack but also inflicted fatal wounds on him.

"We’ll see if you even get the chance to become a ghost!" Yang Kai sneered, waving his hand. A Black Banner appeared in his palm, exuding chilling yin energy and the sounds of wailing ghosts.

Netherworld Infernal Banner!

The relic of Hua Feichen, Elder of Netherworld Sect, this was an Imperial Treasure-level Netherworld Infernal Banner, capable of devouring ten thousand souls, its power unfathomable.

Holding the Netherworld Infernal Banner, Yang Kai sneered, "Once you’re dead, I’ll collect your soul into this banner. Tsk tsk, the soul of a Third Stage Emperor should make for a fine main soul."

"Pfft..."

Hearing this, Feng Xuan couldn’t help but spit out a mouthful of blood, mixed with fragments of his organs.

With such an evil artifact in Yang Kai’s possession, Feng Xuan feared that even in death he wouldn’t become a ghost, but would be trapped in the banner as a puppet.

It was like the last straw. After spitting that blood, Feng Xuan’s eyes dimmed, his vitality quickly fading, and he tumbled from midair.

Yang Kai watched coldly, but didn’t actually collect his soul in the Netherworld Infernal Banner. After all, he hadn’t refined it and didn’t know how to collect souls.

Before Feng Xuan’s body hit the ground, Yang Kai struck, Emperor Yuan erupting and blasting him into a cloud of blood.

Yang Kai waved his hand again, retrieving the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear.

"That guy was really strong!" At this moment, Yingfei and the others gathered, looking down with lingering fear.

"It wasn’t him, it was this thing!" Yang Kai played with the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear, then casually put it into his Spatial Ring.

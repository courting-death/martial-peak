## Chapter 3124 — The Star Lord’s Nourishing

Above the clouds and beneath the sun, Yang Kai stood with his eyes closed and his hands spread open. Golden light shrouded him, lending him a faintly sacred air.

Screams rang out one after another. In less than half a cup of tea's time, several hundred cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain were slaughtered to the last man.

Tu Feng and the others returned, and all of them grew excited when they saw Yang Kai.

Qiu Yimeng raised a finger to her lips and softly shushed them.

Only after a moment did Yang Kai suddenly open his eyes and look at everyone with a smile.

"Family Head!"

"Sect Master!"

Everyone saluted in unison.

Yang Kai's eyes swept over one familiar face after another. Tu Feng, Tang Yuxian, Ying Jiu, and the other Yang Family Blood Servants who had followed him into the Yang Family Succession War had all grown immensely. The disciples of the Eight Great Families had shed their youthful immaturity and become adults as well. There were also disciples from the various Sects of Zhongdu's world. Though several dozen years had passed since they last met, each face still matched a familiar name.

Yang Kai had not forgotten them. They were companions he had come to know when he was at his weakest.

Every scene from those days remained vivid in his mind.

"Your elder sister has returned," Yang Kai said, looking at Su Mu.

Su Mu froze, then burst into wild joy. "Elder Sister, she..."

"She had another opportunity."

Tears poured from Su Mu's eyes. He leapt three chi into the air, excited as a monkey. It took him great effort to calm down, and only then did he look at Yang Kai and ask, "Where is she?"

"Lingxiao Sect."

"I want to go back." Unable to wait another moment, Su Mu turned to leave.

Su Yan had been missing for twenty years, her life or death unknown, and he had been frantic with worry. Now that he had heard she had returned safe and sound, of course he was excited.

Yet he could not move, as if someone had bound him in place. Su Mu turned back to Yang Kai and said awkwardly, "Brother-in-law, I admit I've cursed you plenty in my heart over the years, but since Elder Sister is back, just treat all that as me talking nonsense. I'll apologize to you another day, so let me go now."

"You cursed me?" Yang Kai was stunned.

Su Mu scratched his head and gave a dry chuckle.

Of course he had cursed him, and not just a little. He had always felt that Su Yan had gone to investigate that Void Passage only because she wanted to search for Yang Kai. Otherwise, she would never have gone missing there. Naturally, the chief culprit behind everything was Yang Kai.

"I'll have your elder sister settle accounts with you later," Yang Kai snorted.

"She can punish me however she likes, as long as the two of you are safe." Su Mu had the air of someone past caring; none of the frivolous arrogance of his youth remained. "Can you let me go now?"

"I'll transport you." Yang Kai waved his hand, and Su Mu vanished, leaving only a ripple of Space Force behind.

Yang Kai turned to Qiu Yimeng and said, "Once matters here are settled, all of you should return to the Sect as well. No foreign enemy will invade Dark Star again."

"Yes!" everyone answered with a resounding shout.

"Where are you going?" Qiu Yimeng asked urgently.

"There are some matters I need to handle. I'll be back soon." Yang Kai smiled faintly at her. His figure flickered, and he too vanished from the spot.

Qiu Yimeng reached out, but caught only empty air, her face clouding with loss.

The group looked at her with complicated expressions. Everyone knew her feelings, but unfortunately, her affection was one-sided. In such matters, no one could say much.

Tu Feng grinned and said, "At last, we can relax for a while. Elder Qiu, shall we head back?" He could not bear to see her looking so lost and wanted to change the subject.

Qiu Yimeng nodded, her eyes turning toward Lingxiao Sect. "It really is time to go back."

Yang Kai's figure flickered as he plunged deep underground.

Before long, he arrived at the heart of the world.

This was where he had refined Dark Star's Stellar Origin back then, and also the place where Dark Star's Origin had been born.

After several dozen years away, he had suddenly returned and sensed something unusual from Dark Star's Stellar Origin, especially during the few times earlier when he had communed with the World Laws.

As soon as Yang Kai entered the cavern at the heart of the earth, a feeling of intimacy rose in him, like a wanderer coming home. That feeling was something even his return to Lingxiao Sect could not compare to, as if this place alone was the destination of all things, the beginning and end of everything.

The Dark Star Origin Power inside him also grew much more active, pulsing again and again without rest.

Yang Kai stepped onto a stone bed, sat down cross-legged, emptied his mind, and let the Origin Power within him gradually resonate with Dark Star.

A moment later, he lay down on the stone bed. Another moment passed, and he shifted from lying flat to lying on his side, his expression peaceful, like an unborn infant nurtured in its mother's womb.

Some sound seemed to drift into his ears. At first he could not hear it clearly, but little by little, it grew distinct.

The sounds were chaotic and countless, weaving an indescribable movement that trembled through his mind.

This was...

Yang Kai was astonished and listened carefully.

He soon understood. These sounds were coming from every corner of Dark Star. He had not used any power, had not communed with the World Laws, and had not even used any of the advantages he possessed as Star Lord. Simply lying here on his side, he could hear everything on Dark Star.

The speech of insects and the calls of birds, sounds whose meaning he had never understood before, were now perfectly clear.

Somewhere in the wilderness, two dung beetles were fighting over a round ball, their antennae touching as they argued without end. "This dung is mine, get lost! It's mine, you get lost! If you don't get lost, I'll beat you! Try it, then. Fine, I'll try!" The quarrel became a scramble, and the scramble turned into a fight. Neither was willing to back down, and they actually fought to a stalemate.

In a spiderweb, a moth struggled. An eight-legged spider let out a delighted laugh, swiftly climbed to the center of the web, injected its poison into the moth, and began to feast with great satisfaction. "Delicious, delicious, truly delicious. If only I could eat this every day."

A pair of ants chanted in perfect rhythm, marching in formation as they tirelessly carried food gathered from several li away back to their nest.

More pleas entered Yang Kai’s ears. Following the sound, he discovered that a certain place had suffered a great drought for several years. The people could no longer survive, starving corpses littered the ground, and in their desperation, families had reached the point of exchanging children for food. Some mortals were inviting an expert to set up an altar and perform a ritual, praying for the Heavens to send rain and relieve the poverty and suffering of that land.

Yang Kai, seemingly fast asleep, waved his hand in irritation.

Thunder cracked several times in the sky. The long cloudless heavens suddenly turned dark and overcast. Black clouds gathered overhead, covering more than ten thousand li, and amid lightning and thunder, a torrential rain poured down.

Those mortals raised their heads to the firmament, dropped to their knees in worship, kowtowed again and again, and shouted that a deity had revealed himself.

At Liuli Sect, Daiyuan had transformed into a Colored Glass body, indestructible as diamond, and was locked in battle with enemies several times her number. Just as she was about to lose, the Spiritual Qi of Heaven and Earth suddenly rippled. Several blades of force swept across those enemies, slicing them in two. Blood splashed down, and innards spilled across the ground.

Daiyuan stared in shock, unable to understand what had happened.

The enemies who had been cut in half were just as horrified. They had no idea who had ambushed them, much less how the attacker could be so ghostly and unpredictable.

Chi chi chi chi.

Blades of force crisscrossed without end, and one enemy after another fell. All the disciples of Liuli Sect stared dumbly as those inexplicable powers cut down every enemy before them without harming a single disciple of Liuli Sect.

"Junior Sister, what is going on?" Yin Sudie flew over and stood shoulder to shoulder with Daiyuan, asking in confusion.

Daiyuan shook her head. "I don't know."

Together, the two women turned to look at Gong Aofu.

Gong Aofu looked just as astonished. Only after all the invading enemies had died did she cup her fists and call out in a clear, gentle voice, "May the High Master who aided us please show yourself. All of Liuli Sect is endlessly grateful!"

She called three times in a row, but no one answered, as if the High Master who had secretly helped them did not exist at all.

Gong Aofu sighed softly. Since the other party was unwilling to reveal himself, she knew she likely had no way to find him. She could only keep this kindness in her heart, and if one day she learned her benefactor's name, she would certainly repay him.

The same scene played out across Dark Star's great Sects and in every corner of the star.

After the Warships from Great Wilderness Star Domain were destroyed, the cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain naturally wanted to flee. But how could Dark Star let them leave so easily? Its cultivators seized the chance to pursue and entangle them, and a great battle broke out.

Then, for no apparent reason, one powerful enemy after another was killed. In less than an hour, the whole of Dark Star was swept clean, and not a single battle remained.

Countless people were utterly bewildered, with no idea what had happened or who that hidden High Master might be.

At a certain moment, the Spiritual Qi of Heaven and Earth suddenly stirred. Far away in the distance, a blurry shadow appeared. When it first emerged, it was indistinct, and no one could tell what it was. They only knew it was vast beyond measure, like a black curtain covering half the sky.

Every living being on Dark Star, no matter where they were, could see that shadow simply by raising their head.

An indescribable feeling grew in everyone's heart. That shadow stirred in them an uncontrollable urge to prostrate themselves in worship.

As they stared blankly, the shadow gradually changed. It had a head, hands, feet...

It had astonishingly transformed into a human shape, a figure that seemed able to stand shoulder to shoulder with Heaven and Earth. The sight shook every heart and left everyone unable to recover.

After a while longer, the figure's features gradually became clear.

Eyes went blank, then filled with excitement and joy, while astonishment appeared on face after face.

Many people recognized that face.

Yang Kai!

It was Yang Kai, who had been gone for several dozen years. It was the master of Dark Star!

The giant shadow had appeared, but where was his real body?

What baffled them even more was that the giant shadow stretching across the void and covering the sky was lying on its side in the heavens, eyes closed, seemingly fast asleep.

As his chest rose and fell, the tides of the world's Spiritual Qi surged without end.

Specks of starlight suddenly descended from the Starry Sky, falling toward every part of Dark Star from all directions. At first glance, they looked like falling meteors, making people tremble in fear.

Yet when the starlight fell, it caused no destruction at all. Instead, it seemed to pour a mysterious power into Dark Star, nourishing this Cultivation Star that had nurtured hundreds of millions of living beings.

With each breath of Yang Kai's giant shadow, more and more starlight fell, dense beyond counting.

Across all of Dark Star, the Spiritual Qi of Heaven and Earth grew richer and purer at a clearly visible pace. Even the elusive World Laws became a little clearer.

It was the Star Lord’s Nourishing!

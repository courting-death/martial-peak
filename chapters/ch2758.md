## Chapter 2758 — Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin

The auction hall was bustling with excitement. The auction held by the Ziyuan Commerce Guild was of a high standard, with quite a few Emperor Realm experts participating. Every auction item fetched an excellent price, causing the beautiful woman standing on the auction stage to smile continuously.

Spiritual Pills, wondrous medicines, rare treasures—one after another, they were brought out like flowing water, and the sounds of bidding from all directions rose and fell in succession.

“Next up for auction is the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin.” The beautiful woman on stage suddenly raised her voice, her bright eyes sweeping over the crowd below as she gently parted her red lips and said, “Friends who have brought a female companion or are preparing a gift for one, please pay attention. I dare guarantee this is absolutely the best gift.”

No matter how this beautiful woman dressed, as a qualified auctioneer, she naturally knew how to hype up the price. So as soon as she spoke, many people showed interest, since just from the name, the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin was clearly a piece of jewelry.

Ordinary jewelry would never make it to an auction. Only valuable Secret Treasures were qualified to appear. Everyone was curious about what kind of jewelry the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin was and what kind of power it possessed.

Under everyone’s gaze, a maid walked in from the back carrying a tray. She stopped beside the beautiful auctioneer, and on the tray, a red cloth covered the item, preventing anyone from seeing the true appearance of the auction lot.

The beautiful woman kept everyone in suspense, then finally smiled slightly, reached out, and lifted the red cloth. In an instant, a stream of deep blue light suddenly danced into the air, trailing a string of blue radiance that illuminated every detail of the auction stage, dazzling and magnificent.

A chorus of exclamations rang out, and many women widened their eyes, gazing up at the stage. Such beautiful things always attracted the attention of women.

Once they saw clearly, those women exclaimed again, for the blue light that had flown up was none other than a butterfly fluttering gracefully. Each beat of its wings brought forth beautiful glimmers, dazzling the eyes and lifting the spirits.

A moment later, the butterfly suddenly descended, alighting gracefully atop a jade-green hairpin. Its wings continued to flutter gently, so lifelike it seemed as if it might take flight again at any moment.

But everyone knew that this butterfly had no life; it existed as a special ornament on the hairpin.

The crowd was amazed. Now, not only did the women’s eyes reveal fondness, but even some men were interested.

If one were to buy such jewelry and give it to a woman, it would surely win her favor—perhaps even lead to something good.

“This Secret Treasure isn’t just for decoration, is it? May I ask what powers it has?” A magnetic voice came from a private room.

This question struck a chord with everyone. Although the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin looked extraordinary and beautiful, if it was only an ornament, its price would surely drop significantly.

Pretty but useless things are only liked by some women, but it’s the men who pay the bill.

The beautiful woman on stage smiled slightly and said, “A Secret Treasure refined by Master Hou—how could it be just for decoration?”

“What?”

“This hairpin is a Secret Treasure refined by Master Hou?”

“Is that true?”

The beautiful woman’s words on the stage caused an uproar below. Exclamations rang out as if Master Hou was some extraordinary figure.

Yang Kai also opened his eyes and asked curiously, “Who is this Master Hou?”

He asked but received no reply. Turning his head, he saw Qin Yu gently biting her red lips, her beautiful eyes fixed on the stage, not moving for a moment, her face full of excitement—clearly, she was also very interested in the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin.

Yang Kai asked again, and only then did Qin Yu snap out of it. Blushing, she said, “Master Hou is a newly risen artifact refining master of the past ten years. Every Secret Treasure forged is a masterpiece. It’s just that…”

“Just what?”

“Master Hou is extremely reserved—no one has ever seen their true face, and not even their gender is known.”

“So mysterious?” Yang Kai raised his eyebrows.

Qin Yu smiled and said, “That’s right. In the past ten years, quite a few excellent Secret Treasures have appeared in the Southern Territory, all refined by Master Hou. They seem to be proficient in refining every kind of Secret Treasure, whether defensive, offensive, or auxiliary. In any case, Master Hou is a very formidable Artifact Refiner.”

Yang Kai said, “Since no one has seen Master Hou, how can anyone be sure who refined these Secret Treasures?”

Qin Yu replied, “Every Secret Treasure refined by Master Hou has a mark. On each one, there’s a hidden monkey head symbol.”

At this moment, the beautiful woman on the stage said, “If you don’t believe me, you can check the jade hairpin in my hand to see if it’s from Master Hou.”

As soon as she finished speaking, countless Divine Senses spread from all directions, focusing on the jade hairpin. Yang Kai’s Divine Sense was among them. He carefully scanned it and found, just as Qin Yu had said, that on the side of the jade slip, there was a lifelike monkey head. At first glance, it even seemed to be scratching its ears, vivid and lively.

“It really is Master Hou’s work!”

“No doubt about it, and it’s even a Dao Origin-Grade Upper-Level Secret Treasure.”

“Not bad, not bad. This old man wants it.”

“Old Zhou, you’re already so old. What do you want this for? Leave the opportunity to the young people.”

“I have just taken a new concubine and want to give her this Secret Treasure. Do you have a problem with that?”

……

On the high platform, the beautiful auctioneer paid no mind to the commotion below and said, “The number of Secret Treasures forged by Master Hou is not great, but each one is a masterpiece among masterpieces. Even the Emperor-Grade Artifact Refiners of our Commerce Guild hold Master Hou in the highest regard, believing their skill already reaches the Emperor-Grade level! I’m sure everyone is eager to know what type of Secret Treasure this Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin truly is!”

There were only a few types of Secret Treasures, but jewelry Secret Treasures for women were mostly defensive, though there were some offensive ones, but far fewer than the former.

The Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin looked extraordinary. If it was defensive, the price would naturally be even higher.

The beautiful woman didn’t keep them guessing and smiled, “This jade hairpin is neither offensive nor defensive, but rather…”

As she spoke, she urged her Origin Qi, and the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin in her hand suddenly grew, instantly becoming three zhang long. At first glance, it looked like an enlarged butterfly, its huge wings gently flapping, stirring up a misty radiance.

The beautiful woman formed a seal with both hands, and with a push of Origin Qi, the giant butterfly transformed into a stream of blue light, circling the auction hall a few times before returning to the stage.

“A flying Secret Treasure!”

The crowd exclaimed in surprise.

Previously, everyone had been guessing what type of Secret Treasure the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin was, but no one expected it to be a flying Secret Treasure. If that was true, its value would be immense, and judging by the speed just displayed, it seemed extremely impressive.

The beautiful woman said, “Since it hasn’t been refined, I can only bring out half of the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin’s speed. But according to the authoritative experts of my Commerce Guild, if fully activated, its flying speed would be almost on par with an Emperor Realm expert—definitely a superb tool for escape. Of course, the blue radiance of this Secret Treasure can also be concealed, so there’s no need to worry about exposing your whereabouts.”

With all her praise and hype, the beautiful auctioneer was simply trying to convey one message: this is truly a treasure!

It really was a great treasure. Whether in appearance or practicality, it was enough to make many cultivators covet it.

Seeing that her promotion was almost done, the beautiful woman called out, “Dao Origin-Grade Upper-Level flying Secret Treasure, Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin, starting bid: fifty million Middle-Level Source Crystals. Each increase must be no less than one million. Please, everyone!”

As soon as she finished speaking, a wave of bids rang out.

In the blink of an eye, the item that started at fifty million Middle-Level Source Crystals was pushed up to sixty million, and it still wasn’t stopping.

After another ten breaths, the price had already climbed to seventy million, and after twenty breaths, eighty million…

Qin Yu’s expression changed constantly. The earlier joy was swept away, replaced by utter dejection.

She knew full well that, with her own resources, she could never compete for such a treasure. Yet hearing such a staggering bid left her feeling utterly powerless.

Eighty million Middle-Level Source Crystals—just that could buy four Qing Jade Command Tokens at Linghu Palace.

But compared to the value of the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin, eighty million was far from the bottom line. The appearance of this jade hairpin instantly set off a new bidding frenzy at the auction. After eighty million, the cultivators sitting in the main hall could no longer participate; only the guests in the private rooms continued to compete.

Especially that old man who had just taken a new concubine—he bid extremely quickly, determined to win.

When the price climbed to a terrifying one hundred and thirty million, only two people remained bidding, and each increase seemed to require careful consideration, unlike the earlier brisk pace.

“One hundred and forty-one million! Old Zhou, if you can top this, the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin is yours.” A voice came from a private room.

Old Zhou laughed and said, “Since Brother Wang is so inclined, this old man will oblige—one hundred and forty-two million!”

“You win.” The Emperor Realm expert sighed softly.

One hundred and forty-two million Middle-Level Source Crystals—converted, that’s one million four hundred and twenty thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals. Emperor Realm experts certainly had that kind of wealth, but it depended on what they were buying. For just a piece of jewelry for a woman, it wasn’t worth spending so much. The auction was only halfway through, and there were still good things to come. If they spent too much now, how would they bid later?

“Thank you for letting me win!” Old Zhou laughed heartily, urging from below the stage, “Go ahead and start the countdown—no one else is bidding.”

The beautiful woman’s smile didn’t fade as she looked around and said, “Anyone else want to bid? If not…”

“One hundred and fifty million!” Yang Kai’s voice suddenly rang out.

Inside the private room, Qin Yu stared at Yang Kai in astonishment. She hadn’t expected him, who had been silent all this time, to suddenly speak up. Was he planning to buy it—and for whom? Such an item surely wouldn’t be for himself. Who could possibly hold such an important place in his heart?

Old Zhou’s laughter came to an abrupt halt, and he became somewhat exasperated: “One hundred and sixty million!”

Originally, he could have gotten it for one hundred and forty-two million, but Yang Kai’s sudden intervention made it even more expensive, so of course he wasn’t happy.

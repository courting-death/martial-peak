## Chapter 3131 — Returning to Cuiwei

Across the whole Hengluo Star Domain, Cuiwei Star did not count as a top-tier Cultivation Star, nor was it the main star of any of the strongest forces, yet it was still widely famous throughout the Star Domain.

There were two reasons for its fame. The first was that this place contained a forbidden land called the Blood Prison. It opened once every hundred years, and Origin Returning Realm cultivators could enter to take part in the Blood Prison Trial, gaining many benefits and opportunities while glimpsing the mysteries of breaking through to the Void King Realm.

Yang Kai had taken part in the Blood Prison Trial back then and obtained many Domain Stones inside. Domain Stones could help Origin Returning Realm cultivators condense their Domains, and it was by relying on those Domain Stones that Yang Kai had been able to advance swiftly to the Void King Realm.

No one could verify how long the Blood Prison had existed, but it had been at least ten thousand years. As for how it was born, rumor had it that many Void King Realm experts once fought a chaotic battle here and all died in this place. The essence and comprehension of their lifelong Martial Dao scattered outward and seeped into the earth, forming a unique environment through a chance convergence of circumstances, and Domain Stones were born from it.

Cuiwei Star's second reason for fame, however, was something that had happened only in the last few dozen years.

After the previous Blood Prison Trial ended, Cuiwei Star's Star Lord, Luo Hai, inexplicably fell, shaking the entire Star Domain and leaving countless people in disbelief.

Although Luo Hai had only been at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm, he was also the only expert in the entire Star Domain to become a Star Lord with Second Stage cultivation. His aptitude was extraordinary, and with the aid of the Stellar Origin, given enough time, he would certainly have advanced to the Third Stage, stood at the peak of the Star Domain, and become a hegemon of the region.

Yet such a brilliant figure had suddenly fallen. No one knew what he had encountered after that Blood Prison Trial. At the time, people guessed that he might have offended some reclusive old monsters and been ruthlessly killed.

After all, he was quite strong in his own right, and a Star Lord besides. Apart from those few people, who could have killed him?

But over the next dozen or so years, the truth gradually came to light.

The one who killed him was no reclusive old monster, but Dark Star's Star Lord, Yang Kai!

The news came from Ice Heart Valley on Chi Lan Star. No one could verify who spread it, but the claim was made with absolute certainty, because Luo Hai had fallen on Chi Lan Star, and many disciples of Ice Heart Valley had seen it with their own eyes.

Yang Kai had already left the Star Domain at that time and gone to the Star Realm, so after this news spread, it did not cause much of a disturbance. Although Luo Hai had many friends, no one actually attacked Dark Star to avenge him. Besides, Yang Kai was no longer in the Star Domain, so even those who wanted revenge had no way to pursue it. The matter was left unsettled and gradually calmed with the passage of time.

But how shocking was the fall of a Star Lord? It could be said that in the entire Hengluo Star Domain, among Star Lords killed in conflicts with others, Luo Hai was the first. Even if one could not say there would be no one after him, it was absolutely unprecedented.

As Yang Kai looked at this familiar star, long-sealed memories stirred.

This was not his first time coming to Cuiwei Star. Counting this visit, it was already his third.

The previous time, he had come here to take part in the Blood Prison Trial, only to be hunted afterward by that Star Lord, Luo Hai. He had fled through the Star Domain for more than half a year, the pursuit stretching all the way to Chi Lan Star. If he had not repeatedly hidden inside the Xuan Realm Pearl, he likely would have met with disaster long ago.

That had been one of the rare moments in his life when he had hovered on the edge of death, so he had never forgotten it.

However, he could no longer quite remember why Luo Hai had wanted to kill him at the time, only that the man had clung to him like a maggot on the bone, impossible to shake off.

In the end, he still had to thank the dead Luo Hai a little. If not for Luo Hai's relentless pursuit, he would never have unintentionally barged into Chi Lan Star, and he certainly would not have found Su Yan in Ice Heart Valley.

The past was like smoke!

Yang Kai smiled faintly, gathered his thoughts, transformed into a streak of light, and descended toward Cuiwei Star.

He had originally intended to go to Yao Star Di Chen. Di Chen Star was the only Cultivation Star in the Star Domain primarily inhabited by yao, and humans and other races had very little room to survive there. Shan Qingluo was there at this very moment, and it was also the closest to Dark Star.

If the Space Array he had arranged back then had not been destroyed, he could have transmitted directly to Di Chen Star and saved himself the travel time. But the Space Array on Di Chen seemed to have been ruined and could no longer be used for direct transmission, so Yang Kai could only go by a roundabout route.

The reason he had come to Cuiwei Star was simply that there was a hidden passage in the Blood Prison that led directly to Di Chen. During the Blood Prison Trial back then, Shan Qingluo and several young talents of the yao race had traveled back and forth through that hidden passage. Although Yang Kai did not know its exact location, with his current cultivation, finding a hidden passage would be simple as long as he entered the Blood Prison.

As he flew down, Yang Kai frowned slightly.

This Cuiwei Star felt entirely different from what he had seen back then. The whole star was permeated with a thick aura of death, and even with the naked eye, one could see a faint yellow halo shrouding it.

The fall of the Star Lord was certainly part of the reason, but it was absolutely not the root cause. Cuiwei Star's Star Lord, Luo Hai, had died several dozen years ago, and Cuiwei Star's Stellar Origin had already returned. Even if it had suffered some damage at the time, it should not have caused such an earth-shaking change to Cuiwei Star.

Thinking of what he had heard in Lingxiao Sect these days about the actions of the Great Wilderness Star Domain, Yang Kai faintly felt that Cuiwei Star's transformation could not be unrelated to the Great Wilderness Star Domain.

This Cultivation Star had probably already fallen.

Without a Star Lord overseeing it, and without any particularly powerful cultivators either, Cuiwei Star simply could not resist the invasion of the Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators.

Above a city, Yang Kai descended at an easy pace like a falling meteor. His figure suddenly stopped several dozen zhang above the city. He lowered his head to look, and his brow furrowed even more deeply.

He had originally wanted to find someone and ask about the location of the Blood Prison, but when he looked around, there were hardly any living people in the city. Instead, the stench of blood soared to the sky, nauseating when breathed in. Broken walls and ruins were everywhere in the city, birds swept through the sky, and the earth seemed to groan.

Judging from the city's scale, it could accommodate at least a million people, yet at this moment there were only sparse traces of human presence. Dense corpse Qi had gathered until it was almost tangible, spreading over the entire city and shrouding it like black clouds.

Sweeping his eyes across the city, he saw a huge blood pool at its very center. The pool was full, its water dark red. Severed limbs floated in it, along with face after face like ghostly visages, rising and sinking in the pool while giving off heart-shaking wails.

Corpses lay scattered throughout the city as well, visible everywhere. Who knew how long they had been dead, but all of them gave off a rotten smell.

Beside that blood pool, teams of cultivators were being escorted here by another group of people, then ruthlessly killed and pushed into the blood pool.

Pleading, weeping, and curses rang out without end.

It was simply hell on earth, and the sight made Yang Kai's expression turn grim.

It was not as if he had never killed anyone. Plenty of people had died at his hands, but this was the first time he had seen such a large-scale tragedy, and it was also the first time he had truly witnessed the brutality of the Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators.

This was nothing less than the slaughter of a city!

What exactly did the cultivators of the Great Wilderness Star Domain want to do? Had they invaded the Hengluo Star Domain just to display their strength and kill people for sport?

Yang Kai studied the blood pool. Array runes flickered all around it, and there were also tall platforms standing nearby. Taken as a whole, it seemed to be a strange altar.

Realization dawned in his heart. He understood that these people were probably refining something, and the lives being pushed into the blood pool one after another were the materials for that refinement.

"Who goes there!" Although the cultivators of the Great Wilderness Star Domain were slow to react, they were not so dull that they would fail to notice Yang Kai after he had watched for so long, especially since Yang Kai had not hidden his aura. Someone immediately shouted and looked over warily.

Yang Kai swept his eyes over them. His figure flickered, and he suddenly appeared beside the blood pool. He lightly patted the head of the person who had shouted, and the man immediately collapsed limply, falling into the blood pool.

Ghostly cries and wolfish howls erupted from within the blood pool. One ghostly hand after another reached out, dragging the man into the pool water, and he vanished in the blink of an eye.

The remaining cultivators were greatly shocked. They had not expected the newcomer to kill without saying a word, and they rushed toward Yang Kai one after another.

"Careful!" someone shouted. It was one of the cultivators who had been escorted here after having their cultivation sealed. These people should all have been locals of Cuiwei Star who had slipped up and been captured here. They had thought they could not escape calamity, but now that a divine warrior had descended from the heavens, the desire to live had been kindled in all of them. How could they bear to watch Yang Kai die an unnatural death? They loudly warned him.

But they saw Yang Kai's figure suddenly sway, conjuring countless afterimages as he circled the huge blood pool once.

Three breaths later, he stood still again, as if he had never moved at all.

The Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators who had rushed toward him, along with those who had shown hostility, all froze in place, motionless.

Immediately afterward, corpses began hitting the ground.

The group of Cuiwei Star cultivators were dumbstruck, all of them staring stupidly.

Only after a long while did people begin sucking in cold breaths.

These Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators were no weaklings. Among them were more than a dozen Origin Returning Realm cultivators, and the rest included at least a hundred Saint Realm cultivators. Yet they had all been slaughtered in just three breaths.

If they had not seen it with their own eyes, who would dare believe it? They looked at Yang Kai as though they were looking at a god.

The joy of surviving disaster surged into their hearts, and quite a few people actually began to cry.

Yang Kai swept his gaze over them and fixed it on a red-faced man. This man had the highest cultivation here, at the Third Stage of the Origin Returning Realm, but someone had planted a restriction in him, sealing all his Saint Yuan in his meridians so it could not move.

The red-faced man had clearly also realized that Yang Kai was an extraordinary expert, and whether they could survive this ordeal depended entirely on him. He hurriedly cupped his fists and said, "Peng Shizong greets Sir!"

Yang Kai raised a hand. "Take them aside for now. I will speak with you shortly."

His gaze pierced through the air and looked toward the very center of the city.

From that direction, two Void King Realm auras suddenly surged over like overturning rivers and seas. They had clearly been alarmed by what Yang Kai had done here. One was at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm, and the other was at the First Stage of the Void King Realm.

When those auras appeared, Peng Shizong and the others all changed expressions and retreated one after another. There were many of them, at least three hundred, but their cultivation levels were uneven, and their clothing differed as well, clearly indicating that they all came from different backgrounds. At this moment, however, they were of one mind, all of them staring at Yang Kai with worry.

"You dare ruin this old man's good business? Junior, don't even think of leaving!" A voice as sharp as a jackal rang out. Immediately afterward, two figures appeared at the center of the city and flew this way as swiftly as wind and lightning.

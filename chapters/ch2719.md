## Chapter 2719 — Visitors from Medicine Pill Valley

Source Crystals and pills are, after all, just external things. Martial arts techniques are the true foundation of a cultivator. Ghost Ancestor, having obtained the secret techniques of the Netherworld Sect, was naturally overjoyed—this was a truly grand gift!

Yang Kai turned to look at Chi Yue.

Chi Yue pursed her lips and smiled. "There are so few cultivation techniques for the yao. Don’t tell me you’ve prepared some for me too."

"Not exactly, but I do have this." Yang Kai spread his hand, and suddenly a small mountain appeared before him. Billowing yao qi instantly filled the great hall, leaving everyone present visibly moved.

"Inner Cores!" Chi Yue exclaimed. She casually picked one up and sensed it, her expression changing again. "A Twelfth-Tier Inner Core!"

Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores could only be formed within Twelfth-Tier demonic beasts, which were equivalent to the Emperor Realm among humans.

Each one was worth a fortune, rarely seen by ordinary people.

"This one is also a Twelfth-Tier Inner Core." Ai Ou was holding one as well, his face full of shock.

"This is Tenth-Tier, this is Eleventh-Tier..." Gu Cangyun, watching from the side, was wide-eyed in amazement.

The small mountain of Inner Cores before them numbered over a thousand, all Tenth-Tier and above. At least a tenth were Twelfth-Tier—about a hundred in total. Such a number was truly terrifying.

"I know your yao cultivate differently from humans, but with these Inner Cores to assist you, Senior, your cultivation should progress twice as fast with half the effort," Yang Kai said to Chi Yue.

Chi Yue felt dizzy as she stared at the Inner Cores, still unable to believe it. "Where did you get so many Inner Cores?"

"Picked them up!" Yang Kai chuckled.

Chi Yue couldn’t help but give him a reproachful look.

How could things like these be picked up so casually? She thought Yang Kai was just making things up.

But in truth, Yang Kai really had just picked them up—from the beast graveyard in the Wild Ancient Land. He’d collected over a million in total, so a mere thousand or so was nothing. If Chi Yue knew the real number of Inner Cores Yang Kai possessed, she’d probably go mad.

In this world, only the beast graveyard in the Ancient Land could accumulate such a vast number of Inner Cores.

Chi Yue didn’t ask further. Excited, she put the Inner Cores away. As Yang Kai had said, the yao cultivated differently from humans. Most simply absorbed the spiritual qi of heaven and earth, the essence of sun and moon, and could even devour the Inner Cores of other yao.

With so many Inner Cores to aid her, Chi Yue’s future cultivation speed would surely soar.

"Senior Gu, here are a few sword techniques. They’re not too profound, but you can cultivate them for now. If I find better ones in the future, I’ll bring them to you." Yang Kai took out several jade slips and handed them to Gu Cangyun.

In the Star Domain, Gu Cangyun was the Alliance Master of the Sword Alliance and specialized in the sword. These sword techniques were undoubtedly the most suitable for him.

Yang Kai had killed so many Emperor Realm experts and obtained so many spoils of war that finding a few decent sword techniques was no trouble at all.

Gu Cangyun took them and, after a glance, his eyes lit up. "These are far superior to what I cultivated before."

The sword techniques he’d encountered before were, at best, Dao Origin-Grade. But what Yang Kai had brought out were all relics of Emperor Realm experts, naturally far superior to what he’d previously cultivated.

Finally, it was Ai Ou’s turn. Yang Kai also gave him several matching secret techniques.

Everyone was overjoyed. It felt as though they had leapt to an entirely different level in an instant.

And that wasn’t all. When Yang Kai took out a cluster of Stellar Origins, everyone was utterly stunned.

One had to know that Stellar Origins could only be born from a Cultivation Star. A complete Stellar Origin was the very foundation of a Cultivation Star. Having spent years in the Star Realm, they all knew just how rare Stellar Origins were here.

Chi Yue and the others had always regretted, in secret, not having steeled themselves to refine a Stellar Origin back in the Star Domain, leaving them with no chance after arriving in the Star Realm.

At the time, refining a Stellar Origin was simply too dangerous—no one dared to risk it. Only Ai Ou had succeeded.

But now, Yang Kai had produced a cluster for each of them, and even passed down the Star Refining Technique.

Ai Ou was already a Star Lord, so he didn’t need the Stellar Origin.

After a flurry of activity, Yang Kai helped Chi Yue, Ghost Ancestor, and Gu Cangyun refine the Stellar Origin as their protector.

Three days later, the three opened their eyes, faces full of joy. With Stellar Origin, it would be much easier to comprehend the Martial Dao and Law Power, greatly aiding their future growth and advancement.

With what Yang Kai had done today, their cultivation efficiency would increase tenfold, a hundredfold—saving them an enormous amount of time.

"Well then, does anyone have any doubts or difficulties in their cultivation? If so, feel free to bring them up so we can discuss and learn from each other." After bestowing his generous gifts, Yang Kai looked around at everyone.

The others understood that this so-called discussion was just Yang Kai being modest—he simply wanted to answer their cultivation questions.

Without hesitation, everyone began to speak up.

In the great hall, questions and answers flowed among the group as time slowly passed.

"Are all the people inside dead? Get out here!"

No one knew how much time had passed when a voice suddenly exploded outside Lingxiao Palace. The speaker seemed to have infused their words with power, so the sound carried for dozens of li, echoing among the mountains, lingering in the air.

Yang Kai frowned, turning to look toward the mountain gate. A wisp of Divine Sense quietly spread out, and he quickly saw what was happening outside.

Outside the gate, in front of the Grand Guardian Array, a finely crafted multi-deck ship hovered in midair. The ship was exquisitely ornate—though not an Imperial Treasure, it was still of Upper-Level Dao Origin-Grade. Hanging from the mast was a large banner with the character for "medicine" written on it.

At this moment, a youth who looked only fifteen or sixteen stood on the deck, glaring angrily at the plaque of Lingxiao Palace.

Though young, the youth possessed the cultivation of the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, which truly amazed Yang Kai.

It was obvious that the earlier shout had come from him, though who knew what secret technique or secret treasure he’d used to make his voice carry so far.

Where did this brat come from, daring to act so wildly in front of Lingxiao Palace with just First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm cultivation! Yang Kai’s expression turned odd.

Just then, Yang Kai sensed something, took out his Transmission Compass, and checked it. He saw a message from Hua Qingsi: "Palace Master, it seems someone is here to cause trouble."

She had clearly heard the youth’s voice as well.

Yang Kai sent back a message with his Divine Sense: "Just a little brat. You handle it."

Now that Hua Qingsi was the Chief Steward of Lingxiao Palace, Yang Kai naturally had no intention of stepping in. Besides, the other party was only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm—whatever backing he had, Hua Qingsi was the perfect one to deal with it.

Seeing him take out the Transmission Compass and send messages, Chi Yue and the others asked, "What’s going on?"

"Nothing important, don’t worry about it. Let’s continue!" Yang Kai said, putting the Transmission Compass away.

Seeing this, the others didn’t ask further and continued discussing cultivation with Yang Kai.

After a while, Yang Kai suddenly frowned, took out the Transmission Compass again, and probed it with his Divine Sense, immediately showing a look of surprise.

"If something’s up, Palace Master, go handle it. Lingxiao Palace is newly established—it’s best not to make enemies lightly," Ai Ou said.

"Alright, I’ll go take a look." Yang Kai nodded, stood up, and in a flash, left the great hall.

With a few steps, he arrived outside the mountain gate.

Hua Qingsi was there, facing the youth across the great array. What surprised Yang Kai was that, despite being only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, the youth showed not the slightest fear in front of Hua Qingsi, an Emperor Realm expert. Instead, he looked utterly defiant.

Seeing Yang Kai appear, the youth looked him up and down, then said carelessly, "So this is your Lingxiao Palace’s Palace Master?"

"That’s right," Hua Qingsi nodded. "This is our Palace Master, Yang Kai. If you have business, speak to him."

"Not much to look at. I thought he’d have three heads and six arms." The youth curled his lip in disdain.

This strange attitude made Yang Kai inexplicably angry, but he couldn’t be bothered to stoop to the youth’s level. He just chuckled and said, "You say you’re from Medicine Pill Valley? What proof do you have?"

It was because Hua Qingsi had messaged him, saying the youth claimed to be a disciple of Medicine Pill Valley, that Yang Kai had come over.

The youth didn’t answer. He just pointed at the big banner behind him and snorted, "Are you blind? Can’t you see the character?"

A vein throbbed on Yang Kai’s forehead—he had the urge to beat the boy up. But thinking of Medicine Pill Valley, he held back and said in a deep voice, "What can a single character prove? Are you joking with me?"

The youth’s eyes widened in surprise as he looked at Yang Kai. "Where did this country bumpkin come from, not even recognizing the symbol of my Medicine Pill Valley? And you have the nerve to found a sect!"

He actually called him a country bumpkin!

Yang Kai felt a surge of anger, like a volcano about to erupt. He took a deep breath to calm himself.

The youth, oblivious, leapt onto the mast, unfurled the big banner, and let it wave in the wind. He shouted, "Open your eyes and look carefully! There’s not a soul in the entire Northern Territory who doesn’t recognize this banner!"

"Heh..." Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and nodded slightly. "This Palace Master recognizes it now."

He sent a message to Hua Qingsi: "I really want to beat this kid up."

Hua Qingsi was startled and quickly replied, "Palace Master, don’t be impulsive! If he really is from Medicine Pill Valley, we absolutely can’t offend him. We still have to make a living in the Northern Territory!"

"If not for that, do you think I’d be putting up with him?" Yang Kai replied angrily.

A youth, a banner, and a single character weren’t enough to make Yang Kai wary.

But the three words "Medicine Pill Valley" truly carried weight.

After all, this was the place where Great Emperor Miaodan secluded himself for cultivation—the sacred land in the hearts of countless cultivators in the Northern Territory!

Although Great Emperor Miaodan didn’t concern himself with worldly affairs, nor had he established a sect like other Great Emperors, choosing instead to live in seclusion in Medicine Pill Valley, that didn’t diminish the reverence and admiration Northern Territory cultivators felt for him.

In the Northern Territory, offending Great Emperor Miaodan or Medicine Pill Valley meant you wouldn’t be able to take a single step. Not to mention the Great Emperor’s own supreme cultivation—even the cultivators of the entire Northern Territory wouldn’t tolerate it.

The youth leapt down from the mast and stood at the fore of the deck, hands planted on his hips as he spoke in an imposing tone, “Good, it’s enough that you recognize it. You’d best remember it well—who knows, we may be dealing with one another often in the future!”

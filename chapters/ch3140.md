## Chapter 3140 — Old Beyond Her Years

In another wing room, strange stones decorated the room and the light was soft. Biluo sat with her cheeks propped in both hands, looking utterly heartsick.

He was back! That man had actually come back. And the moment he returned, he made her Lady cry. It was maddening. With him back, she was afraid she would have no chance at all from now on. But... why did she not feel jealous in the slightest? Instead, she was happy for her Lady, and even... felt the tiniest bit of anticipation herself.

She shook her head, threw off those wild thoughts, and only then came back to her senses, remembering that she had brought a little guest back with her.

When she turned to look, she found the little girl sitting on the other side of the table, motionless and quiet to an almost unnatural degree. Her black-and-white eyes were clear as spring water, though who knew what she was looking at; her gaze had no focus at all.

What an exquisite child. Who had that man made her with? Whoever it was, they certainly knew how to make a beautiful child.

Embarrassed at having neglected her, Biluo forced out a small smile, rummaged through her Spatial Ring for a while, and took out some berry-like fruits to place in front of her. "Try them. They are very sweet."

Liu Yan lowered her head to look at the spirit fruits, then looked up at her and said solemnly, "I am not a child."

Biluo burst out laughing and reached over to pat her head. "A child is a child. Why refuse to admit it?"

Liu Yan shook off her hand in annoyance and snapped, "Do that again, and I will not be polite." She tried hard to look angry, but given her age, her tiny figure, and that jade-carved, powder-soft face, it only made her more irresistible.

Biluo said, "So young, yet such a temper. All right, all right, I will not touch you."

Liu Yan shifted back in the chair until almost her whole body sank into it.

Casting about for something to say, Biluo asked, "Is your father good to you?"

"He is not my father."

"Eh, didn't you just call him father? Why deny it now? Oh, I understand. You must be standing up for your mother. You really are old beyond your years. But that will not help. Your father has several women, and my Lady is only one of them." She abruptly realized that discussing this sort of thing with a little girl was far too absurd and hurriedly changed the subject. "How old are you this year? What is your name? Where is your mother? How did your parents meet?" A woman's appetite for gossip blazed fiercely. Biluo consoled herself that she was only scouting the enemy for her Lady, all so they could know themselves and know their enemy.

Liu Yan slowly turned her head and stared at her in silence.

Biluo asked, "Is there something on my face?"

"You are really annoying. Can you shut up?"

Biluo blinked.

She had actually... been scolded by a little girl! She could hardly believe her ears, and her face flushed at once.

"Your mind is wandering. Do you want to know what they are doing?" Liu Yan asked.

"Of course not!" Biluo's face reddened even more, as if Liu Yan had struck right at her thoughts.

The tip of Liu Yan's ear twitched. She tilted her head to listen, then said, "Oh, it is quite intense. Your Lady really does know how to let herself go. This position is rather interesting."

"You... you..." Biluo shot up from her chair and stared at Liu Yan as if she had seen a ghost, her beautiful eyes round and wide.

Was that any way for a child to talk? She could guess what those two were doing in the next room with her toes, yet this girl had actually pretended to eavesdrop, and now she was talking about positions?

Even an old soul in a child's body should not be old to this extent. Biluo felt as if the moral limits she had held for so many years had taken a massive blow and were on the verge of collapse.

Liu Yan reached out with both hands and made grabbing motions in the air.

"What are you doing now?" Biluo asked suspiciously.

Liu Yan said, "This is how he is grabbing your Lady, and now your Lady is like this..." As she spoke, her little head began to sway, her fine hair flying.

Biluo's mouth fell open. She froze where she stood, as if someone had cast a body-binding spell on her.

Did she have to describe it so vividly, as though she had truly seen it with her own eyes? After hearing that, Biluo could almost picture the scene next door. Worse, Liu Yan described it with a perfectly solemn face, which only made the whole thing stranger. Despite herself, Biluo felt a chill crawl over her.

This child... was far too strange. Did Yang Kai know? Or was there some Primordial Era giant beast hiding inside that tiny body of hers? What child in this world was like this?

Biluo suddenly snapped back to herself and smiled with pursed lips. "What nonsense are you spouting?"

She had nearly been fooled. Her Lady's wing room was layered with restrictions. Even she, a First Stage Void King Realm cultivator, could not hope to spy inside, so how could a little girl possibly manage it?

"Believe it or not, suit yourself." Liu Yan lost interest in the description as well and reclined in the chair with an air of ancient composure. Her two little hands rested folded over her stomach, two fingers idly circling each other out of boredom.

The room fell silent for a while. After what had just happened, Biluo did not dare casually chat with this child anymore, afraid she would suddenly say something shocking and scare her half to death.

"Hey..." Liu Yan suddenly said.

"Are you calling me?" Biluo turned to look at her.

"Is there anyone else in the room?" Liu Yan looked astonished, as if asking, Are you an idiot?

Biluo took a deep breath and nearly failed to suppress the urge to rush over and strangle her. Such a pretty little girl, yet every word out of her mouth was infuriating. One could not judge a person by appearance, true, but this contrast was too much to accept. Forcing out a smile, she tried to keep her voice calm. "My name is Biluo. You may call me Aunt Biluo, or Big Sister Biluo. Either is fine."

"I know what your name is. You do not need to tell me."

Endure it. Endure it! This was Yang Kai's daughter, after all. If she really strangled her to death, explaining it to Yang Kai and her Lady would be troublesome.

"Are you sleepy? You can sleep here."

Liu Yan raised a finger and wagged it. "Do not presume to guess other people's thoughts."

"What exactly do you want!" All Biluo's earlier fondness for this little girl vanished. There was definitely a demon hidden inside that pretty, spotless shell. Like father, like daughter indeed. Since she was Yang Kai's daughter, how good could she possibly be?

"Tell me about the situation on your side."

"The situation?" Biluo looked at her in surprise. "What situation?"

Liu Yan said, "Your arrays are fully activated and your defenses are tight. You are clearly guarding against some enemy. Tell me what you know about them."

"Why are you asking about that?" Biluo was truly helpless. This girl concerned herself with far too much.

"If I tell you to speak, then speak. Why all the nonsense?" Liu Yan glared at her.

"Fine." Biluo raised a hand to her forehead, helpless. The long night stretched on, and she had no heart to sleep, so she might as well treat it as idle talk to pass the time.

She proceeded to explain the situation on Di Chen Star in detail. At first she was only speaking casually, but to her surprise, Liu Yan listened with great seriousness. Biluo laughed to herself. I am saying all this, but can you even understand it? And you still look so grave.

An hour later, Biluo poured herself a cup of water to moisten her throat. After talking for so long, her mouth had gone dry.

Liu Yan tapped her little hand against the chair back and said, "In that case, the situation on Di Chen is not promising. The yao forces have basically been wiped out, and only your Red Moon Domain is left?"

"There are still some hiding in the shadows, but that is more or less the situation." Biluo sighed. In truth, Di Chen Star had no shortage of experts. Each of the ten great lords was a Void King Realm cultivator and an extremely powerful Yao Beast in their own right. Several even had the bloodlines of ancient variant beasts and were not inferior to Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor. But the yao were not united, and on top of that, Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor's cultivation methods and Secret Techniques were said to restrain the yao, which was why things had turned so miserable.

There was another reason as well: the humans' resistance!

For so many years, the humans on Di Chen Star had been oppressed too severely. When Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor suddenly arrived, the human race naturally celebrated as one. Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor hardly had to lift a finger before more than half the humans on Di Chen Star had gone over to them.

Even the humans in Red Moon Domain were no exception, let alone those in the other lords' territories.

Although these humans who defected had no particularly powerful experts, they were numerous, and they had lived on Di Chen Star for generations, so they possessed a great deal of intelligence that Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor urgently needed. With both strength and intelligence on their side, how could Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor fail to win battle after battle? Seven of the ten great lords had been removed, either killed or subdued, and in all of Di Chen, only Red Moon Domain remained as one last pure land.

As soon as Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor finished gathering its strength, it would launch a full invasion.

Biluo said indignantly, "That old lion from Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor is shameless. He sent a message before, saying that if Lady lowered herself and submitted to him, he would spare Red Moon Domain and even let Lady continue managing it. Wishful thinking!"

"Old lion?"

Biluo said, "The Manor Master of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor. He seems to have the bloodline of some lion-type Yao Beast, or perhaps he refined some Yao Beast Origin. His strength is not low."

Shan Qingluo had fought him before, but she had been unable to do anything to him. The main reason was that the old bastard had beast pets assisting him in battle. Shan Qingluo had to face several opponents alone, so even with her extraordinary methods, she could not deal with him.

"Since the situation here is so dangerous, why do you not leave? Why stay here like fools?"

Biluo said crossly, "What do you understand? This is the territory Lady's adoptive mother passed down to her. How could Lady abandon it before the final moment?"

"By the time the final moment comes, it will probably be too late!"

Biluo said, "Lady naturally has a way to escape." Shan Qingluo was already at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm. Even if she truly could not defeat Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, escaping should still be no problem.

Liu Yan fell silent for a while after hearing this, then suddenly hopped down from the chair and said, "Come with me on a trip."

"Where?"

"Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor!"

Biluo cried in alarm, "Are you crazy?"

What was this little girl up to? She actually wanted to go to Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor. That place was now a dragon pool and tiger den. How could one simply barge in?

"Are you going or not!" Liu Yan stared at her.

"No!" Biluo said decisively.

Liu Yan turned around, braced both hands on the chair, and stuck out her little bottom with great effort, swaying her head until her black hair flew.

"What are you doing now?"

Liu Yan turned her head and put on a sultry, lingering gaze. "Your Lady is doing this right now!"

## Chapter 2905 — Moonlight Cascades

Lan Xun said, “It seems there’s a problem with the Fourth Stage of the Pagoda. Many small worlds have shattered, which has affected the structure of the Pagoda. Uncle Xiao and the others must stabilize it and wait for everyone to come out. Otherwise, if the void collapses, those inside might get trapped.”

A bead of cold sweat quietly slid down Yang Kai’s forehead as he said awkwardly, “How could a supreme treasure like the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda have any problems?”

Lan Xun looked toward the Pagoda and shook her head, “I’m not sure either. Nothing like this has ever happened before.”

Yang Kai gave a dry laugh, “Then my luck really isn’t great. It’s my first time here, and I’ve already run into this.”

He didn’t dare say much. Fortunately, his current state was already poor, so even if his expression was a bit odd, it didn’t attract much attention.

The Fourth Stage of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda had a problem, and many small worlds were shattered. Yang Kai didn’t need to think to know it was related to him. Earlier, he had used the Xuan Realm Pearl in the Fourth Stage to devour a large amount of the worlds’ laws, causing those small worlds to break apart. But he hadn’t expected such consequences.

Fortunately, the consequences didn’t seem irreparable, nor were they too severe.

As the two were speaking, two figures suddenly appeared at the entrance of the Pagoda—it was Xiao Chen and Lei Ting, walking out together. They had also entered the secret realm of the Fifth Stage of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, but each had their own fortunes. Judging by their expressions, it was easy to guess how things had gone for them.

Xiao Chen wore a smile, his face full of confidence, clearly having gained some benefits in the Fifth Stage.

Lei Ting’s face was gloomy, his aura unstable. He looked even more injured than Yang Kai, likely having suffered some setbacks.

But for martial artists, both setbacks and gains are necessary steps on the path of growth. Only those with weak resolve are defeated by setbacks and stop moving forward. Those with high fighting spirit often turn setbacks into motivation, pressing ever onward.

Xiao Chen and Lei Ting were the last to come out. After they appeared, Xiao Yuyang gave a low shout, “Everyone, leave this place.”

The disciples of Star God Palace received the order, bowed toward the four Elders, and withdrew.

Yang Kai, Lan Xun, and the others also took their leave.

Before they had gone far, Yang Kai suddenly felt a powerful energy fluctuation surge from the void. Turning his head, he saw a beam of moonlight shoot from somewhere in Star God Palace, striking the body of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda.

This beam of light was not an attack, but had a mysterious effect. As the light hit, Xiao Yuyang and the others’ expressions brightened, their hand seals changing, and the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda clearly began to stabilize.

Yang Kai’s expression turned solemn. He knew that beam of moonlight was the work of the Great Emperor.

Speaking of which, he had met Great Emperor-level experts in the Thousand Illusions Dreamscape, and even fought against them.

The Demon Saint Mo Duo was an existence comparable to a Great Emperor. This could be considered Yang Kai's first true clash with an expert at that level. His previous experience in the Shattered Star Sea, when the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor and the Mortal Great Emperor had clashed, didn't count—he had merely stumbled upon the scene and played no real role, let alone deeply participated. Besides, at that time, the two Great Emperors hadn't even unleashed their full power.

But the experience in the illusion was different. Even though it was an illusion, everything was based on reality. In other words, the power Mo Duo displayed was truly at the level of a Great Emperor.

Yang Kai used all his means without holding back, yet was still no match. If not for Mo Duo’s overconfidence at first, giving Yang Kai the chance to use many secret techniques, and later focusing on stopping the Divine Tree, his chances of victory would have been less than thirty percent.

Even so, Yang Kai couldn’t do anything to him in the end, and could only rely on a shameless method to drag him along to withstand the Great Demon God’s distant strike.

This was an extremely precious memory and a rare experience. As long as he could absorb and digest the lessons from that battle, Yang Kai believed he would grow even further.

At this moment, the Great Emperor’s strike was unfathomably profound. Yang Kai couldn’t compare it to Mo Duo’s, but he could feel that the Radiant Moon Great Emperor was at least as strong as, if not stronger than, Mo Duo.

Lan Xun also stopped, turned to look at the beam of moonlight, and suddenly pouted, her face full of grievance. She hadn’t seen her father in a long time.

“Junior Sister,” Yang Kai suddenly said, “Now that things here are settled, I should take my leave.”

He had destroyed many small worlds in the Fourth Stage of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda. Xiao Yuyang and the others surely suspected him. When Yang Kai walked out earlier, those Third Stage Emperor Elders had been watching him. But as long as he didn’t admit it, they couldn’t accuse him, so they let him go.

But Yang Kai still held the Butterfly key in his hand—the key to the Thousand Illusions Dreamscape, which should have belonged to the Star God Palace. To put it nicely, it was his fortune; to put it bluntly, it was stolen.

Having taken something from them, Yang Kai naturally didn’t want to linger, lest Star God Palace discover anything. Besides, he had other matters to attend to.

Lan Xun looked at Yang Kai in surprise, “Senior Brother, you’re leaving already?”

“Yes, I have an appointment with someone.”

“But Senior Brother, you seem injured. Why not rest here for a couple of days before leaving?” Lan Xun asked with concern.

Yang Kai smiled and shook his head, “No need. My injuries aren’t serious, I just need time to recuperate.”

His body wasn’t badly hurt, and the wounds to his Divine Soul couldn’t be healed by rest alone. He had the Warm Spirit Lotus; all he needed was time.

“I see…” Lan Xun, seeing he was determined to leave, didn’t try to persuade him further. She smiled, “In that case, Junior Sister won’t keep Senior Brother. Take care, Senior Brother Yang.”

Yang Kai said, “About the Elders…”

Lan Xun smiled slightly, “When they’re done, I’ll let them know.”

“Thank you.” Yang Kai cupped his fists in salute, then turned and flew out of Star God Palace.

Lan Xun stood in place, watching his figure disappear from sight before leaving herself. Not far away, Xiao Chen, who had been silently observing, let out a quiet sigh of relief.

After leaving Star God Palace, Yang Kai got his bearings and flew toward the Thousand Leaves Sect.

His Divine Soul hadn’t recovered, so he didn’t fly fast. As he traveled, he recalled the scenes of his battle with Mo Duo, replaying its many developments in his mind. This trip to the Thousand Illusions Dreamscape had brought him great gains, and the fight with Mo Duo was just one part. Most of it he hadn’t had time to digest, and he needed to spend some time sorting through his memories.

In a daze, Yang Kai suddenly sensed something and abruptly stopped, squinting ahead.

In the void ahead, a man stood in midair. He looked to be about twenty, with red lips and white teeth, handsome and elegant. One hand was behind his back, the other naturally placed on his abdomen. This calm and composed posture was completely at odds with his youthful appearance.

Yang Kai’s expression grew extremely serious, because he found that this person’s aura was unfathomable—even with his eyesight, he couldn’t gauge the other’s depth. Even though his Divine Soul was injured, the foundation of his powerful Divine Sense was still there. Ordinary Emperor Realm cultivators couldn’t hide from him.

Yet just now, he hadn’t sensed anything, as if this person had suddenly appeared before him, making him alert.

In this situation, either the other’s cultivation was far above his own, or he possessed a particularly powerful concealment-type Secret Treasure.

Yang Kai looked at the other, and the other looked back, a faint, inscrutable smile on his face.

That smile made Yang Kai uneasy. He frowned and asked, “Do you need something, friend?”

The man spoke, uttering a word that made Yang Kai’s soul nearly flee: “Bind!”

With a single word, myriad laws followed.

Yang Kai's body suddenly tensed, and inexplicably, the space around him became sealed. It was as if thick ropes had bound him tightly, leaving his body rigid and stiff. Even moving a single finger became an arduous task.

Yang Kai was shocked and appalled. He hadn’t sensed the other using any secret technique or divine ability. The other had simply spoken a word, yet it had such an effect.

Even a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert couldn’t do this. Only one kind of person in the world could achieve such a feat.

And since this was near the Star God Palace, the identity of the person before him was obvious.

“You…” Yang Kai was both shocked and furious. He never expected this person to intercept him here, and even make a move against him. Why? If he wanted to act, there had been plenty of chances back at the Star God Palace. Why wait until outside?

Moreover, with his status, why was he acting so covertly? Yang Kai couldn’t figure it out, nor did he have time to think.

As soon as he spoke, his vision blurred, and the young man, who had been a hundred zhang away, suddenly appeared before him like a ghost, startling Yang Kai.

At last, he experienced what it felt like to be one of those enemies left helpless by his own Space Divine Abilities.

The two were now face to face. The man didn’t make a move, still wearing a calm, gentle smile as he quietly watched Yang Kai.

As their eyes met, the man’s eyes seemed to turn into two full moons, radiating astonishing light. Under this light, everything about Yang Kai was laid bare, all his secrets seemingly exposed to that gaze.

Yang Kai was furious. Even knowing who the other was, he couldn’t allow anyone to pry into his secrets. Though his body couldn’t move and his thoughts weren’t as quick as usual, he still launched a powerful counterattack.

He frantically mobilized the power of his Divine Soul. In his Sea of Consciousness, the island formed by the Warm Spirit Lotus spun violently, like a whipped top, faster and faster, more and more fiercely.

Multicolored radiance erupted, filling the entire Sea of Consciousness.

Above the Sea of Consciousness, moonlight cascaded down. Originally, wherever the moonlight passed, everything was revealed, but now, as the multicolored radiance swept through, it scattered the pure, clear moonlight.

The man let out a light “huh,” clearly not expecting Yang Kai to still have the strength to fight back despite his methods.

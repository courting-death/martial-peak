## Chapter 3070 — Beast Great Emperor

Rain fell from a clear sky over Dragon Isle. At that instant, the entire island seemed to fall silent, and everyone turned their gaze in a certain direction.

Fu Ling swallowed and said in a strained voice, "When a member of the Dragon Clan falls, they transform into spirit rain. This is the rain of a fallen Dragon!"

"Are you certain?" Yang Kai stared at Fu Ling with a grave expression.

Fu Ling said, "There can be no mistake."

Zhu Qing also nodded. "A member of the Dragon Clan really has died."

Cold sweat poured down Yang Kai's brow. "It can't be, right? Who did it? Senior Jiu Feng? Or Li Wuyi? Could it have been that brat Zhu Lie?"

Hearing Zhu Qing confirm that a member of the Dragon Clan had fallen, he was shocked as well.

Although he had fallen out with the Dragon Clan today and even injured several of its members, Yang Kai had never once intended to kill any of them. Falling out with the Dragon Clan was no great matter. At worst, they would simply never associate with one another again. He had never liked their lofty attitude in the first place, so he naturally had no interest in dealing with them. But if a member of the Dragon Clan died by his hand, that would be entirely different. Not only would it put Zhu Qing in a difficult position, but it would also make him a mortal enemy of all Dragon Isle.

With Dragon Isle's strength, he was still unable to contend with it. Once Dragon Isle made a move against him, vast though the world was, he feared there would be nowhere for him to hide.

Therefore, despite attacking in a rage today, Yang Kai had not actually killed any member of the Dragon Clan. None of those he injured had suffered damage to their foundations. Once he broke the protective technique granted to them by the Dragon Temple, he left them alone.

If he wanted to take Zhu Qing away, he had to display his capital and strength, but he had also been careful not to cross a certain line, one that allowed him to vent his anger while remaining within what Dragon Isle could accept.

But now, that line had been crossed. A member of the Dragon Clan had been killed.

Who was so outrageously bold that they dared kill a member of the Dragon Clan on Dragon Isle? Did they have a death wish?

Yang Kai's expression instantly became solemn. Thoughts churned through his mind as he considered who the Dragon slayer might be.

It could not be Zhu Lie. He was also a member of the Dragon Clan, so he could not truly bring himself to kill one of his own, and he probably did not have the ability either. Jiu Feng likely could not have done it, either. Yang Kai remembered that she was fighting two opponents alone. It was already impressive that she could hold her ground, so how could she possibly have killed a member of the Dragon Clan instead?

Li Wuyi? The Fourth Elder was a Ninth-Tier Great Dragon, so how could he have been killed so easily?

Could it have been Liu Yan?

Yang Kai's heart lurched. If it was Liu Yan, then today's situation had truly taken a turn for the worse.

While Yang Kai's thoughts ran wild, the battlefield around Zhu Lie and the others also fell silent. Everyone who had been fighting withdrew from the fray and formed separate camps, their expressions shifting endlessly.

Jiu Feng reached out and caught a few drops of spirit rain, her face filled with glee at their misfortune. "Which one of your clansmen died?"

She clearly knew that a fallen Dragon transformed into rain. Her beautiful eyes slid toward the two members of the Dragon Clan who had just been fighting her as she teased, "It couldn't be that either Zhu Yan or Fu Zhun died, could it?"

Although she said this, she knew it was impossible. Great Elder Zhu Yan and Second Elder Fu Zhun of the Dragon Clan were both existences comparable to Great Emperors. Even the Beast Great Emperor did not possess the ability to kill them.

Who was it? Someone had actually slain a Dragon.

She felt the same as Yang Kai, secretly shocked beyond measure. One possible candidate after another flashed through her mind, but she rejected them all.

The fighting had ceased here, and the same was true elsewhere.

Four figures stood quietly before the Dragon Clan's Dragon Tomb.

One was a tall, burly man with broad shoulders and a thick waist, dressed in a dark red cloak. His bearing inspired awe, and the aura radiating from him was enough to make the world tremble and the space distort.

In his arms, he held a woman who looked extremely weak. Her face was pale as she rested quietly against his chest, her breath as fragrant as orchids and her long eyelashes trembling slightly. Her expression was serene, as though so long as she could remain in this man's embrace, it would not matter even if the sky fell. Yet upon closer inspection, a deep sense of guilt could still be seen hidden beneath that tranquil expression.

Nearby, Great Elder Zhu Yan and Second Elder Fu Zhun stood to either side of the man and woman, hemming them in.

A blue-green light spread across the Great Elder’s body, while the Second Elder pushed her Ice Laws to their limit, both of them looking as if they were facing a great enemy.

The man had rugged features, thick eyebrows, and large eyes. He panted lightly, looking greatly exhausted. After the spirit rain began to fall, he suddenly grinned. "You two need no reminder from this Lord about what it means when a fallen Dragon transforms into rain, do you? If you continue tangling with this Lord, I fear all the Dragon descendants on Dragon Isle may fall. When that happens, you two will become eternal sinners of the Dragon Clan. I doubt either of you can bear that responsibility."

The Great Elder's and Second Elder's expressions remained unchanged. With their cultivation and strength, it was already difficult for outside matters to disturb them. Although the death of a member of the Dragon Clan was an earth-shattering event, neither of them dared allow themselves the slightest distraction.

This was for no other reason than that the man before them was Mo Huang, the Beast Great Emperor, one of the Ten Great Emperors. In terms of skill and strength, he was no weaker than either of them.

The slightest distraction or negligence might give him an opening to exploit.

The death of a member of the Dragon Clan was indeed an extraordinary matter, but Mo Huang breaking into the Dragon Tomb and bringing out the exiled Dragon woman was equally serious.

"Tsk." Seeing the two Dragon Clan Elders remain unmoved, Mo Huang could not help curling his lip and saying in annoyance, "How exactly are we going to settle today's matter? You two may as well name your terms. This Lord will accept your challenge."

The Second Elder said indifferently, "Release Fu Xuan and never set foot on Dragon Isle again. In return, I will allow everyone from your Spirit Beast Island to leave safely."

Mo Huang coldly snorted. "Xuan'er and I are husband and wife. Our feelings are mutual, and we love one another. Why does the Second Elder make it sound as though this Lord is holding her hostage? Your Dragon Clan is devoid of compassion, rigidly adhering to dogma and rules, and you exiled Xuan'er to the Dragon Tomb. This Lord will not settle that debt with you. Let me take Xuan'er away, and you two may deal with the affairs of Dragon Isle. This Lord will not obstruct you."

Fu Zhun shook her head. "Fu Xuan is the Fourth Elder of my Dragon Clan. She was born on Dragon Isle and will die in the Dragon Tomb. You will not take her away today!"

Mo Huang sneered. "This Lord is determined to take her away today. Just try to stop me."

Fu Zhun said, "Then we can only end this quickly." As she spoke, her aura suddenly became extremely dangerous.

Mo Huang did not waste words with her. He turned to Zhu Yan and asked, "What does the Great Elder say? If we truly must fight, this Lord would be happy to experience your profound skills. I only fear that your Dragon Clan is facing a great calamity today and may even be in danger of annihilation."

Zhu Yan wore a grave expression and sighed inwardly.

What troubled times. These truly were troubled times.

The upheaval on Dragon Isle today was simply dizzying. Even after living for so many years, he had never encountered such a situation.

First, Yang Kai, who bore the Ancestral Dragon Origin, had come to snatch the bride. Immediately afterward, the Beast Great Emperor led the people of Spirit Beast Island in an invasion of Dragon Isle, even venturing deep into the Dragon Tomb to bring out Fu Xuan. Before the matter here could be resolved, a member of the Dragon Clan had been killed.

Even though the Great Elder's cultivation reached the Heavens, he still found today's affairs extraordinarily difficult to handle and felt mentally and physically exhausted. It was as if all the troubles that had not arisen in tens of thousands of years had converged at once.

The death of a member of the Dragon Clan could not be ignored. The clan's population had always been extremely small, and nothing like this had happened for many years. Most importantly, Zhu Yan did not know whether the killer would continue, or even whether they had the ability to kill other members of the Dragon Clan.

If the Dragon slayer still had the ability to kill other members of the Dragon Clan, then he had to hurry over and deal with the situation; otherwise, the consequences would be unimaginable.

Yet he could not treat the matter here lightly either.

Zhu Yan truly found it difficult to choose.

"Does the Great Elder also insist on making things difficult for this Lord?" Mo Huang demanded in a low voice, already growing impatient.

Zhu Yan looked up. "Mo Huang, this old man has a proposal."

"Let's hear it." Mo Huang raised a brow.

"We will set aside the grievances between Dragon Isle and you for now."

Fu Zhun's fine brow furrowed when she heard this, but she did not rush to say anything.

Mo Huang laughed loudly. "A wise decision, Great Elder."

Zhu Yan continued, "On the condition that you come with us."

Mo Huang's laughter ceased. He stared intently at Zhu Yan, seemingly trying to determine whether he was joking. A moment later, he sneered. "Great Elder, have you gone senile? With turmoil breaking out on Dragon Isle, this Lord should naturally seize the opportunity to leave. Otherwise, once you two are free to deal with me, I fear this Lord will be unable to leave even if he wishes to. Great Elder, please do not insult my intelligence."

Zhu Yan shook his head. "If you do not agree, then this old man can only join forces with the Second Elder and capture you first."

Mo Huang shouted in a low voice, "The Great Elder's confidence is truly breathtaking."

Zhu Yan sighed. "If you were alone, this old man could not stop you even by joining forces with the Second Elder, but you also have Fu Xuan with you. How can you fight with all your strength? You do not want her to be injured because of this, do you?"

Mo Huang's face sank. He looked down at the woman in his arms, and his fierce expression softened somewhat.

Zhu Yan continued, "In return, this old man is willing to give you an opportunity. If you agree to this old man's proposal, then after today, this old man can commune with the Dragon Temple and plead with the Ancestors to forgive Fu Xuan, to see whether she might have a chance to leave the Dragon Tomb."

Mo Huang's eyes lit up when he heard this. "Do you truly mean that?"

Zhu Yan said, "This old man can only promise to do his utmost. Whether it succeeds will depend on the Ancestors' attitude."

Mo Huang nodded. "I trust you."

"Then we have an agreement?" Zhu Yan looked at him inquiringly.

Mo Huang turned to Fu Zhun. "That will depend on the Second Elder's attitude."

Without saying a word, Fu Zhun suddenly transformed into a streak of white light and sped into the distance.

Her actions had already made her attitude clear. She neither agreed nor objected, evidently giving her tacit consent. After all, this was the Great Elder's proposal. Even if she had objections, it would be inappropriate for her to openly oppose him. Moreover, this proposal was the best way to resolve their immediate predicament.

Seeing this, Zhu Yan nodded to Mo Huang, then also transformed into a streak of light and sped off in that direction.

The two Dragon Clan Elders simply left just like that.

They both knew that since Mo Huang had agreed to the proposal, he could not go back on his word, given his status.

Moreover, what if he did go back on his word? He could seize this opportunity to escape with Fu Xuan, but while he could flee, could Spirit Beast Island do the same? If he truly broke faith, then in a few days, the entire Dragon Clan of Dragon Isle might pay Spirit Beast Island a visit. That was why the two Elders felt at ease leaving.

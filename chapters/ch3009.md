## Chapter 3009 — Her Master Is the Iron Blood Great Emperor?

Lin Yun'er stuck out her tongue, showing no awareness whatsoever that she had been caught red-handed after running away from home. Pouting, she said, "Master, you've been following me all along."

The man replied, "The world is a dangerous place, and you are simple-minded. Naturally, this Master had to watch over you and keep you safe."

Lin Yun'er snorted angrily. "No wonder I captured that big fellow so easily yesterday. So you secretly helped me."

Though she was simple-minded, she was no fool. Yesterday, she had found it strange that her abilities had suddenly improved so much, but now she immediately understood. It was not that she had become more capable, but that her Master had secretly helped her. Otherwise, there was no way she could have captured that Divine Spirit descendant in a single exchange.

Seeing that she had exposed him, the man merely smiled and did not deny it.

"Where did Uncle Yang go?" Lin Yun'er asked.

The man replied, "He is already hundreds of millions of li away." As he spoke, he glanced at the cross-territory Space Array nearby and was secretly astonished. He had seen Yang Kai’s cultivation with his own eyes; not only was his mastery of the Dao of Space extraordinary, but he could even construct such an array. No matter how critical his judgment might be, even he had to admit that the young man’s future achievements would be boundless.

But so what? The brat had actually dared try to abduct his disciple. It was only out of consideration for Lin Yun'er that he had not broken both his legs.

"Ah! I have to go find Uncle Yang!" Lin Yun'er cried anxiously.

"Nonsense!" The man's face sank. "You have been out for quite some time. It is time you returned with this Master. Do not neglect your cultivation."

"I don't want to! I want to find Uncle Yang!" As she spoke, she stepped onto the Space Array on her own and urged her Emperor Yuan. Her face turned red as she tried to activate the array, but there was not the slightest response.

The man's heart twisted painfully at the sight. This Master has painstakingly taught you all these years, raising you from infancy and cleaning up your shit and piss, allowing you to reach the Emperor Realm, yet I still can't compare to some inexplicable Uncle Yang?

They said daughters always sided with outsiders, but wasn't this going too far? He had not had a good impression of Yang Kai to begin with, and now it became even worse. He wished he could drag the brat back from the other end of the Space Array and torment him thoroughly to vent the anger in his heart.

Taking a deep breath, the man suppressed the bitterness in his heart and smiled. "Disciple, let us discuss something."

"What?" Lin Yun'er glared at him, her cheeks puffed up in anger.

"Do you like that Yang brat very much?"

"Uncle Yang is family to little Yun'er."

"You want to go find him?"

"Mm, mm! Master, hurry and help me. I don't know why this thing stopped working."

Of course it had stopped working. This Master had severed its connection through the void with a hidden burst of force. It would be a wonder if it still worked.

Naturally, he could not say that aloud. Otherwise, he could forget about living peacefully in the future.

"This Master also heard what your Uncle Yang said earlier. He has a Sect called Lingxiao Palace in the Northern Territory, correct?" After a pause, he continued, "How about this? Disciple, once you reach the Third-Stage Emperor Realm, this Master will take you to Lingxiao Palace in the Northern Territory to find your Uncle Yang. When the time comes, you may stay there as long as you like."

Lin Yun'er's beautiful eyes lit up. "Really?"

His heart hurt even more! Nevertheless, the man forced out a smile and nodded gently. "When has this Master ever lied to you?"

Without even thinking, Lin Yun'er immediately sprang down from the Space Array, grabbed his arm, and flew forward with him in tow.

"What are you doing?" the man asked in astonishment.

"Going back! I have to hurry and cultivate. Don't worry, Master, little Yun'er will definitely reach the Third-Stage Emperor Realm very soon."

“Good, good. It’s rare to see you so motivated. Your Master is truly pleased. That’s the disciple I raised,” the man said with a beaming smile. Yet inwardly, he felt as though he had swallowed bitter gall. Damn it, why did it feel like he’d somehow lost?

Why was this happening?!

Brat, your name is Yang Kai, right? Just you wait for this Lord!

...

"Greetings, Palace Master!"

"The Palace Master has returned!"

"Quickly, notify the Chief Steward!"

Beside Lingxiao Palace's Space Array, the disciples guarding it were overjoyed when they saw Yang Kai and began shouting one after another.

They had originally been disciples of Thousand Leaves Sect. After Thousand Leaves Sect was destroyed, Yang Kai brought them to Lingxiao Palace. Although they still bore the insignia of Thousand Leaves Sect, as time passed, their sense of belonging to and identification with Lingxiao Palace grew ever stronger.

By now, not a single Thousand Leaves Sect disciple felt that they were not a member of Lingxiao Palace. Cultivating here was simply too comfortable. Not only was the ambient Spiritual Qi many times denser than it had been at Thousand Leaves Sect, but they also never had to worry about cultivation resources. The Chief Steward did not treat them differently simply because they had once been Thousand Leaves Sect disciples.

In such a short time, every former Thousand Leaves Sect disciple had made great progress in cultivation. Even Ye Hen, the former Sect Master of Thousand Leaves Sect, had entered retreat to attempt a breakthrough to the Emperor Realm. This was something they would never have dared imagine before.

But after coming to Lingxiao Palace, everything changed.

Now, if anyone claimed they were not Lingxiao Palace disciples, they would surely fight that person to the death.

Yang Kai was Thousand Leaves Sect's benefactor and the Master of Lingxiao Palace.

However, their Palace Master was as elusive as a Divine Dragon, rarely remaining in Lingxiao Palace. He spent most of his time travelling outside, and even when he occasionally returned, he would leave again before long. Chief Steward Hua Qingsi had long ago issued instructions that she was to be informed immediately whenever Yang Kai returned.

That was why the disciples guarding the array were so excited.

"Hm?" Li Jiao recovered from the aftereffects of cross-territory teleportation and looked around in surprise. "Where is that young lady?"

She had been standing right beside him moments ago, so how had she vanished in the blink of an eye?

Yang Kai replied, "Her Master kept her behind, so she couldn't come with us."

"Her Master!" Li Jiao's expression changed drastically, and he asked in horror, "That Senior... has revealed himself?"

Yang Kai shot him a glance and asked curiously, “From the way you said that, it sounds like you know who Little Yun’er’s Master is?”

Li Jiao's expression shifted before he answered honestly, "I have a guess."

"Oh?" Yang Kai's interest was immediately piqued. "Just who is that person? Tell me."

Although he could guess that Lin Yun'er's Master was no ordinary person and was very likely a Great Emperor, he did not know exactly who it was. He had only met a handful of Great Emperors, and none of them were likely to be connected to little Yun'er, so Li Jiao having a guess was truly beyond his expectations.

Li Jiao pondered for a moment before asking, "Palace Master Yang, how much do you know about that young lady's iron pot?"

"That was my first time seeing it. I had never heard of it before."

"Then that explains it." Li Jiao nodded. "Since Palace Master Yang does not know the iron pot's origins, it is only natural that you could not guess."

"What is so special about that iron pot?"

Li Jiao replied solemnly, "That pot is called the Unity Cauldron. Palace Master Yang must have sensed it as well; that pot is a Primordial Era Treasure. Once brought out, it can collect all treasures under Heaven."

Yang Kai nodded lightly. He had witnessed the Unity Cauldron's power. Within the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation, the Secret Treasures of more than a hundred people had all been collected by Lin Yun'er through its mysterious might.

It could even be said that capturing the Divine Spirit descendant had not been due to little Yun'er's own ability. Most of the credit belonged to the Primordial Era Treasure known as the Unity Cauldron.

"As far as this Li knows, the previous owner of this pot was the renowned... Iron Blood Great Emperor!"

"Iron Blood Great Emperor!" Yang Kai's expression sharpened.

"Indeed, it was none other than Sir Iron Blood. No one knows how Sir Iron Blood obtained this Primordial Era Treasure. People only know that it was in his hands, though that was already a thousand years ago. With Lord Iron Blood’s unmatched might, he ranked among the foremost of all the Great Emperors, and there was scarcely anyone in the world who could stand against him. Why would he ever need to use such a treasure? Thus, for the past thousand years, the Unity Cauldron had never appeared. However, the older generation of Emperor Realm Masters would not be unfamiliar with this Primordial Era Treasure. I can say with certainty that the iron pot in that young lady’s hands is none other than the Unity Cauldron!”

A strange light flashed through Yang Kai's eyes. "In other words, little Yun'er's Master is Sir Iron Blood?"

"There is a ninety percent chance!" Li Jiao nodded. "Sir Iron Blood is elusive as a Divine Dragon, and very few people know him, but the path he cultivates is quite similar to that young lady's. Both place great emphasis on physical strength."

"Mm." Yang Kai pondered for a while, then said, "What you say makes a great deal of sense."

Little Yun'er's Master being the Iron Blood Great Emperor was both within and beyond his expectations. It seemed that when Yang Yan had taken little Yun'er away all those years ago, her purpose had been to entrust the little girl to the Iron Blood Great Emperor.

With a Great Emperor personally teaching her, there was now a reasonable explanation for why little Yun'er's cultivation had advanced so quickly. The Iron Blood Great Emperor had held nothing back in teaching her, and coupled with little Yun'er's Force Tyrant Body, it would have been strange if her cultivation had not advanced rapidly.

Yang Kai suddenly recalled something else.

Back in the Shattered Star Sea, before the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor fought Duan Hongchen, the Mortal Great Emperor had casually remarked that Iron Blood had accepted a fine disciple. Looking at it now, that fine disciple was clearly Lin Yun'er.

Yang Kai was happy for her. The little girl had found herself a good Master. With a Great Emperor watching over her, who under Heaven would dare provoke her?

Judging from the words the Iron Blood Great Emperor had transmitted to him earlier, the Great Emperor himself also valued Lin Yun'er extremely highly. It could even be said that he doted on her, which had somehow caused him to develop a prejudice against Yang Kai.

Rubbing his nose, Yang Kai felt as though he had suffered an entirely undeserved calamity.

Beside him, Li Jiao was drenched in cold sweat. Swallowing hard, he asked fearfully, "Sir Iron Blood... was following us all along?"

"Probably," Yang Kai replied casually.

Li Jiao began sweating even more fiercely, his conscience terribly guilty. Earlier, when Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er had been trapped in the Array at Wind and Cloud Pavilion, he had shirked his duties and held back for quite some time, only going all out after Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er broke the Array. He might have deceived the disciples of Wind and Cloud Pavilion, but he definitely could not deceive a Great Emperor.

Fortunately, the Great Emperor had not lowered himself to Li Jiao's level. Otherwise, how could he have returned alive?

A terrifying feeling of having taken a trip to the gates of the underworld arose in him for no reason, and his whole body felt unwell.

"Palace Master Yang, now that matters here are settled, this Li will take his leave. I have been away for quite a few days, and I do not know whether anything has happened at my Palace. This Li's heart is already flying home, so..."

"Go," Yang Kai said, waving his hand.

"Then this Li bids farewell!" Li Jiao felt as though he had received a great pardon and hurriedly flew away. Inwardly, he firmly resolved to guard against fire, thieves, and Yang Kai. No matter what, he would never go out with Palace Master Yang again. It was simply far too thrilling and dangerous.

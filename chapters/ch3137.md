## Chapter 3137 — Di Chen

Two days later, in the pavilion, Liu Yan sat with her chin propped in one hand, bored out of her mind. Li Jiao and the others were there as well. None of them had taken the previous battle to heart; to them, it had been no more than casually cutting down a few stray cats and dogs.

In front of the pavilion, a group of white-robed cultivators looked on in deep awe, their expressions stiff and restrained. These people were the foundation of the White Robe Sect, and there were even several Void King Realm cultivators among them, but before these divine soldiers descended from the heavens, they had no confidence at all. The gap between their cultivations was too great. They did not even have the courage to exchange a few polite words.

Their vision blurred, and a young man appeared.

The White Robe Sect members immediately brightened at the sight of him. The leader hurriedly cupped his fists and said, "Sir, may I ask about Sect Master..."

Yang Kai smiled. "Brother Xu is refining Cuiwei Star's Stellar Origin. You need not worry."

"What?"

"Refining Cuiwei Star's Stellar Origin?"

Everyone found it hard to believe, but Yang Kai had spoken with such certainty that they did not dare doubt him.

Refining Cuiwei Star's Origin. What an earth-shaking matter that was. It was not as though the Star Domain had no Star Lords, but in the past, Star Lords had basically all been Third Stage Void King Realm masters. Only with that level of cultivation would one have a chance of successfully refining an Origin. Otherwise, the Origin would backfire, killing the cultivator on the spot. Such cases had been common throughout the ages.

Years ago, Luo Hai had shaken the entire Star Domain by becoming a Star Lord with Second Stage Void King Realm cultivation. Everyone felt that he had been blessed with extraordinary fortune, and they envied him endlessly. If Yang Kai had not later broken that record, Luo Hai's achievement would likely have stood alone for all time.

But Yang Kai was a special case after all, and now Xu Binbai was actually attempting to refine a Stellar Origin with Second Stage Void King Realm cultivation as well. The White Robe Sect members' first reaction was not joy, but alarm.

After the battle at Yanghe City, the Netherworld Sect's strength on Cuiwei Star had been badly damaged. This was precisely when the White Robe Sect needed to step forward and take charge of the situation. Yet at such a critical moment, Xu Binbai, their Sect Master, had actually cast life and death aside and gone to refine the Stellar Origin. This was simply reckless.

If he was lucky enough to succeed, then well and good, but if he failed and perished, body and Dao alike, then all their previous efforts would likely go down the drain. Not only would Cuiwei Star be plunged into chaos once more, but internal strife might even erupt within the White Robe Sect.

Yang Kai took in their expressions but offered no further explanation. Time would naturally reveal everything. He called to Li Jiao and the others, "We are setting out!"

Light flashed, and the group sped into the distance.

Half a day later, before the Blood Prison.

A blood-colored restriction enveloped the area, turning a vast stretch of land into a forbidden zone and blocking anyone from peering inside. Only when the appointed time arrived would this layer of blood-colored restriction open, allowing cultivators below the Void King Realm to enter.

This restriction had been arranged by Cuiwei Star's previous Star Lord using the Stellar Origin. Even Luo Hai had been unable to alter it at will back then and could only maintain the custom of the Blood Prison Trial. Although the Netherworld Sect's cultivators had poisoned all of Cuiwei Star, they had no way to deal with the Blood Prison restriction.

When Yang Kai arrived, the place was no different from how it had been back then.

He reached out and pressed his hand against the blood-colored restriction. The light curtain immediately rippled with a blood-colored halo that seemed intensely corrosive, making Yang Kai's palm hiss.

Yang Kai paid it no mind. If he truly were only a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator, he could only sigh helplessly before this restriction. But his true cultivation had already reached the Emperor Realm, and his body was that of a half-dragon.

What could a mere Blood Prison restriction do to him?

Strange vibrations pulsed outward as Yang Kai's palm slowly sank into the blood-colored restriction, followed by his entire arm, then half his body.

With one step, he entered the Blood Prison.

Heaven and Earth were drenched in blood-red, and even the clouds in the sky seemed dyed crimson. Any ordinary person who came here would surely be scared out of their wits, but Yang Kai felt a deep sense of familiarity. This was, after all, a place where he had once tempered himself, and it bore the mark of his growth.

Liu Yan followed him in as well, standing prettily at his side. Her fitted dress made her small figure look even more delicate and exquisite, her long hair reached her waist, and she was tender and adorable.

This time, no matter what he said, she refused to enter the Small Xuan Realm again. She only wanted to stay beside Yang Kai.

Helpless, Yang Kai could only let her do as she wished. Li Jiao and the others, however, had all been sent into the Xuan Realm Pearl.

"Wait here a moment." Yang Kai rubbed her head.

Liu Yan angrily shook off his hand, apparently still sulking because he had kept her from coming out for so long.

Yang Kai smiled faintly, spread his arms, closed his eyes, and silently began to sense his surroundings.

His Divine Sense surged, but he did not dare release too much of it. This was not Dark Star. If he displayed too much power, he would be rejected by Heaven and Earth.

Strands of Divine Sense, like swimming fish, carried Space Force with them as they shuttled and leapt through the Void. A vast image of the place immediately flowed back into Yang Kai's mind through these leaping strands, allowing him to observe the Blood Prison's scenery as if seeing it with his own eyes.

His Divine Sense radiated in every direction, seemingly without end.

A quarter hour later, Yang Kai suddenly opened his eyes and looked in one direction.

Found it!

There was a hidden passage in the Blood Prison connected to Di Chen. Back then, Shan Qingluo and a group of yao disciples had used this hidden passage to enter the Blood Prison directly and take part in the trial. Since it was a passage connecting two Cultivation Stars, it had to be a Void Passage, and fluctuations of Space Force would naturally seep from it.

Such fluctuations might be extremely obscure and difficult for others to find, but to Yang Kai, they were as bright and obvious as a lighthouse in the dark.

He reached out and took Liu Yan's small hand, and the two walked forward side by side. Their steps were unhurried, yet the scenery around them rushed backward.

Before long, Yang Kai arrived at an extremely hidden place inside the belly of a mountain. Within it stood something like an altar. Looking at the many patterns and designs carved across the altar, Yang Kai knew what to do and stepped onto it together with Liu Yan.

...

Di Chen Star was the only star in the Star Domain ruled by the yao. It could be called the yao's paradise, as well as the land where the yao had risen. On the yao star Di Chen, humans and the other races had very little room to survive, and their status was extremely low. They lived under the yao's oppression year-round. Nor did the human race have any true masters. The strongest human cultivators were only at the Origin Returning Realm, because any human who showed the potential to advance to the Void King Realm would be killed by the yao in advance. Here, the human race existed merely as a foil and vassal to the yao.

Di Chen Star had ten great yao lords, each ruling over a territory. Every one of them possessed Void King Realm cultivation, and their true forms were all Tenth-Tier Yao Beasts. Only after advancing to the Third Stage of the Void King Realm would one be qualified to be called a Yao King!

Yao Kings were exceedingly rare. Often, not one would appear for several hundred or even a thousand years.

Several dozen years ago, a Yao King had indeed appeared on Di Chen Star: Lady Chi Yue, one of the ten great lords. However, she left Di Chen not long afterward and handed her territory over to her adopted daughter to manage. From then on, Yao Kings became the stuff of legend.

Di Chen's ten great lords had different temperaments, and their attitudes toward the human race differed as well. In one lord's territory, humans had no place to stand at all, because he had killed every last one of them.

Of the ten great lords, the one with the most harmonious relationship with the human race was Chi Yue. In the Red Moon Domain, humans did not suffer much oppression. They could even live freely, to the point that many human Sect forces had taken root there.

Chi Yue had great foresight. The yao did not understand artifact refining or pill refining, but humans did. Back then, she had provided those long-oppressed humans with a place to live and multiply, protected their safety, and gathered large numbers of Artificers and Alchemists to refine artifacts and pills for her, strengthening the Red Moon Domain.

After she left, Shan Qingluo took over the Red Moon Domain, and this policy continued. With the support of Dark Star's Lingxiao Sect behind Shan Qingluo, the Red Moon Domain's strength grew like a rolling snowball. In barely more than ten years, it surpassed the other nine lords and showed a clear momentum toward unifying all of Di Chen Star.

If no foreign enemies had invaded, ruling Di Chen Star would not have been difficult for Shan Qingluo with her current cultivation and the power behind her.

But the invasion of Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators disrupted her plans.

Di Chen Star had also been targeted by people from Great Wilderness Star Domain, and the Sect that set its sights on this star was a first-class force even in Great Wilderness Star Domain. Though it was not equal to the Netherworld Sect, it was still quite formidable.

Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor!

This Sect was founded on beast control. Every disciple was proficient in enslaving and commanding beasts, and as their cultivation increased, so did the number of Yao Beasts they could command. When disciples of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor fought others, they had Yao Beasts assisting them, giving them an almost overwhelming advantage.

Once they heard that Hengluo Star Domain had a star ruled by the yao, one with countless yao as well as many rare bloodlines and exotic beasts, how could they possibly restrain themselves? They immediately came to Di Chen with the full strength of their Sect and brought the flames of war here.

Over a dozen years, only three of Di Chen Star's ten great yao lords remained. Of the other seven, some refused to be tamed and died by Self-Destruction of their Inner Cores, while others read the situation and submitted to Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, becoming lackeys under its command.

The title of lord now existed in name only.

Now, only the Red Moon Domain was still bitterly holding on.

The Red Moon Domain's ability to persist until now in its struggle against Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor owed no small part to the current Lord of the Red Moon Domain, Shan Qingluo. Shan Qingluo already possessed Third Stage Void King Realm cultivation, and she also bore the Origin Power of the Divine Spirit Heavenly Moon Demon Spider. Not just anyone could overcome someone with such abilities.

On top of that, the Red Moon Domain had received support from Lingxiao Sect in earlier years and developed vigorously, with masters as numerous as clouds. If Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor truly insisted on going to war, both sides would only suffer grievous losses.

Therefore, after Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor had eroded most of Di Chen Star's land and subdued ninety percent of the yao, it actually entered a strange period of peace with the Red Moon Domain.

Beneath that peace, undercurrents surged, and a storm was approaching.

Everyone knew this peace could not last long. Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor was gathering strength, and its disciples needed time to subdue new beast pets. Once Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor was fully prepared, the Red Moon Domain would surely face a storm-like assault.

When that time came, the Red Moon Domain would definitely be unable to hold out.

On Half Moon Mountain, inside the bedchamber, Shan Qingluo sat by the window and gazed out at the mountain scenery. Her bright red dress was like sun and flame, outlining every curve of her graceful figure. Her black hair draped over her shoulders, and her face held a charm that could enchant all living beings.

## Chapter 2757 — Ziyuan Auction

“Would an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor suit your needs, Brother Yang?” Kang Siran smiled faintly.

“Imperial-Grade Middle-Level!” Yang Kai’s expression brightened. “There’s an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor at the auction?”

Kang Siran nodded, “That’s right. The highlight of this auction is precisely an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor.”

“Is that true!” Yang Kai was overjoyed. This time, he had come to the Ziyuan Commerce Guild for two reasons: first, to purchase Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade to prepare for setting up Space Arrays in the future; second, of course, to look for a suit of armor.

Yang Kai’s requirements weren’t high. As an Emperor Realm cultivator, he simply hoped to find an Imperial-Grade armor. Yet, to his surprise, the auction featured an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level suit—far better than he’d expected.

He didn’t want to end up ragged or even stark naked again after using the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique.

“Is a private transaction possible?” Yang Kai asked.

Most of the items at the auction house were consigned by cultivators, but many were also sourced by the auction house itself. Either way, with Yang Kai’s current long-term partnership with the Ziyuan Commerce Guild, he was qualified to arrange a private deal.

Kang Siran said apologetically, “I’m afraid not. If it were any other item, perhaps, but this Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor is the highlight of the auction and was advertised three months ago. President Lou would never agree to a private sale.”

“I see…” Yang Kai wasn’t particularly disappointed. Since it had long been promoted as the highlight, it certainly wouldn’t be sold privately, as that would greatly damage the Ziyuan Commerce Guild’s reputation. “No matter, I’ll just bid for it then.”

Kang Siran said, “Brother Yang, please wait a moment. I’ll go prepare an invitation for you!”

With that, he walked out.

After a short wait, Kang Siran returned with a gilded invitation and handed it to Yang Kai with a smile. “The auction begins tomorrow at seven in the evening, Brother Yang. Come a little early—you might find something to your liking.”

“Alright.” Yang Kai took the invitation and slipped it into his Spatial Ring.

After the time it takes for an incense stick to burn, Yang Kai left the private room and walked straight out of the Ziyuan Commerce Guild branch.

He strolled around Fenglin City, bought some supplies, and spent Source Crystals freely. Only then did Yang Kai return to the Qin Family.

The Qin Family was extremely busy. Everyone was packing up. Though it was a small clan, they had lived and thrived here for thousands of years. Now, having to leave so suddenly, the members of the Qin Family couldn’t help but feel reluctant.

But under Qin Chaoyang’s orders, even if they were unwilling, they could only obey. Everything that could be taken had to be taken.

Yang Kai walked around the Qin Family estate but didn’t see any sign of Qin Chaoyang.

Instead, Qin Yu had already finished her seclusion. As soon as she saw Yang Kai, she ran over with a face full of joy. “Eldest Brother Yang!”

Yang Kai smiled and nodded. “It’s been years, and your cultivation has improved a lot.”

He had checked before—Qin Yu now had the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. This speed was already extremely fast, perhaps related to her Heaven and Earth Severed Body. With such talent, as long as she had enough time, she would surely grow strong.

“It’s all thanks to you, Eldest Brother Yang.” Qin Yu intertwined her fingers, standing tall and graceful before Yang Kai, and said gratefully, “If not for you, I might have died at eighteen. Eldest Brother Yang, you are my greatest benefactor.”

“This is your fate. No need to thank me.” Yang Kai smiled. “Where’s your grandfather?”

Qin Yu tucked a strand of hair behind her ear. “We’re about to leave, so Grandpa wants to sell the Qin Family’s land deed. He should have gone to the City Lord’s mansion.” She paused, then asked, “Eldest Brother Yang, are we really going to the Northern Territory?”

“That’s right.”

“I heard it’s very cold in the Northern Territory, with snow everywhere.”

Yang Kai smiled. “That’s a misconception. While many places in the Northern Territory are indeed snowy, there are also many where birds sing and flowers bloom. The place I’m taking you to is the latter. Once there, your Qin Family will have even better prospects.”

Qin Yu’s beautiful eyes lit up, and she couldn’t help but look forward to it.

“You go ahead. I’ll rest for a while.” Yang Kai said, then walked toward the back hall.

Qin Yu watched his departing figure, her lips parting as if to speak, but in the end, she could only let out a soft sigh.

The day passed quickly. When night fell the next day, Yang Kai finally opened his door and came out.

In front of the door, Qin Yu was pacing back and forth, looking troubled. Hearing movement, she looked up and, seeing Yang Kai, quickly said, “Eldest Brother Yang, we’re all ready.”

Qin Chaoyang also walked over from not far away and said solemnly, “Brother Yang, the Qin Family is fully prepared. When do we set out?”

Yang Kai said, “We’ll set out at nine in the evening. Wait five hundred li southeast of the city—there’s another group who’ll be joining you!”

“Who?” Qin Chaoyang was surprised.

“The Zhang Family.” Yang Kai replied.

Qin Chaoyang nodded. “So it’s them.”

The Zhang Family also seemed to have a good relationship with Yang Kai. Qin Chaoyang knew this. After Yang Kai left back then, the Qin Family had looked after the Zhang Family, but in recent years, they could barely take care of themselves, let alone the Zhangs. Now, hearing that the Zhang Family would be leaving with them, Qin Chaoyang couldn’t help but feel a sense of shared hardship.

“Eldest Brother Yang, where are you going?” Qin Yu suddenly asked.

“To the Ziyuan Commerce Guild’s auction. There’s something I want to buy.” Yang Kai replied, then took a step and flew out. Suddenly, he stopped a dozen zhang away, turned back to look at Qin Yu, and said, “Do you want to come along?”

The anticipation on Qin Yu’s face instantly turned to joy. Her delicate figure flickered as she chased after him.

“Let’s go.” Yang Kai called out, Emperor Yuan surging as he wrapped Qin Yu up, turning into a stream of light that disappeared above the Qin Family estate.

At the entrance of the Ziyuan Commerce Guild’s auction house, lanterns were hung everywhere, and a long red carpet was rolled out. It was as lively as a festival.

Kang Siran and Gu Hong, the two chief stewards, stood to the left and right, greeting guests with constant smiles and clasped fists.

“Sect Master Le, it’s been a while. I hope you’ve been well!”

An elderly man strode forward with his head held high, and Gu Hong immediately went up to greet him.

Le Dongzheng smiled slightly and returned the greeting. “Steward Gu, you’re too polite. I heard there are many good things at this auction, so I came to join the fun.”

Gu Hong said, “Sect Master Le, you must be joking. If you’re just here for fun, I’m afraid no one else will buy anything.”

Le Dongzheng said, “You go ahead. This old man will look around on his own.”

“Forgive us if our hospitality is lacking, Sect Master Le.” Gu Hong smiled apologetically and gestured. Immediately, a beautiful maid came forward to lead Le Dongzheng into the auction hall.

Anyone with status or position who came here would be led by a maid, showing their difference from others.

“Brother Yang.” On the other side, Kang Siran also clasped his fists toward Yang Kai. His gaze shifted to Qin Yu, and he smiled, “Miss Qin is here as well.”

Of course, Kang Siran recognized Qin Yu. After all, he had managed affairs in Fenglin City for half his life.

Qin Yu smiled in return. “Steward Kang!”

“No need to see me in. I’ll go in myself.” Yang Kai smiled.

As soon as he finished speaking, he suddenly felt a hostile gaze from ahead. Yang Kai looked over and saw Le Dongzheng staring at him coldly, his eyes full of malice, as if Yang Kai had killed his father or stolen his wife.

Yang Kai smiled faintly, not taking him to heart at all.

Le Dongzheng snorted coldly and turned away.

Though the exchange of glances between the two was brief, Kang Siran and Gu Hong both caught it clearly. Gu Hong’s eyes flickered with some unknown thought, and he let out a low, sly chuckle.

Kang Siran, however, looked worried and whispered, “Brother Yang, do you have some grudge with Sect Master Le?”

“A bit.” Yang Kai rubbed his nose. He’d killed three of Le Dongzheng’s disciples, all at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. That was no small grudge.

Kang Siran said seriously, “You’d best be careful. Le Dongzheng is the Sect Master of Duanyue Sect and became an Emperor a hundred years ago. It’s said he could break through to the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm at any time. Don’t underestimate him.”

Yang Kai gave a reassuring smile. “It’s fine. I’ll go in first.”

Kang Siran nodded and signaled to a nearby maid, who immediately came over, took Yang Kai’s invitation, and led him inside the auction hall.

Passing through a wide, bright corridor, he soon arrived at a private room.

As he opened the door, a spacious box appeared before him, luxuriously decorated and easily able to accommodate twenty people without feeling crowded. At the front was a huge transparent Crystal Stone window, through which everything outside was clearly visible.

Only very distinguished guests were qualified for such a private room. The maid clearly understood this and was extremely humble and respectful.

“If you need anything, just tell me.” After introducing some auction rules, the maid spoke gently.

“We don’t need anything for now. You may leave us,” Qin Yu suddenly spoke up, waving the maid away as if shooing off a fly.

The maid’s smile didn’t falter. Clearly well-trained, she bowed and said, “Then I’ll step out and wait at the door.”

With that, she left and gently closed the door.

Qin Yu glanced at Yang Kai and found him smiling at her. She couldn’t help but blush and looked away.

“Sit.” Yang Kai called out, then found a chair and sat facing the auction stage. “If you see anything you like, just bid.”

“I’m just here to watch the excitement…” Qin Yu muttered, but saw that Yang Kai had already closed his eyes. She pouted and found a chair for herself.

The room fell silent, making Qin Yu a bit uncomfortable. She’d wanted to ask Yang Kai where he’d been and what he’d done these past years, but seeing he wasn’t in the mood to talk, she didn’t dare disturb him.

Fortunately, they didn’t have to wait long before the auction began.

A beautiful woman walked onto the auction stage, dressed revealingly, with most of her chest exposed and her long legs faintly visible beneath sheer gauze. Her peach blossom eyes were full of allure, and she smiled before speaking, instantly livening up the atmosphere. Qin Yu blushed at the sight and secretly cursed her.

She sneaked a glance at Yang Kai and saw that he still had his eyes closed, which made her feel much better.

## Chapter 2989 — Western Territory, Point Star City

Ten days later, Yang Kai leisurely appeared in a city.

Over the past several days, he had also figured out where he was.

The Western Territory!

In the Star Realm, the Western Territory was relatively the most barren and desolate.

Yang Kai had been in the Star Realm for so many years, yet he had never set foot in the Western Territory. He had not expected to arrive here by accident while escaping from the Revolving World this time, and he could not help wondering whether Heaven's Will had guided him here.

After leaving the oasis ten days ago, Yang Kai had gone to kill a great snake Yao Beast. That Yao Beast had reached the Twelfth-Tier, making it comparable to an ordinary Second-Stage Emperor Realm cultivator. It seemed to have been keeping the people of the oasis as livestock, devouring one or two of them from time to time. Even if the oasis's inhabitants wanted to flee, they were powerless to do so, as anyone who dared venture more than ten li from the oasis would die without a burial place.

A Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast was a terrifying nightmare to those people, but it was nothing to Yang Kai.

He had casually eliminated it as repayment for their help. Yang Kai did not return to the oasis afterward and instead departed directly. He believed the people there would discover before long that the great snake plaguing them had been slain.

He had followed Zhu Qing and Zhu Lie's route all the way here, but had found no trace of them.

Presumably, the two of them had already returned to Dragon Isle or gone to his Lingxiao Palace. Once this thought occurred to him, Yang Kai no longer hurried on his journey. Along the way, he crossed countless mountains and rivers while taking in the Western Territory's distinctive customs and scenery, feeling quite carefree and at ease.

Since it was rare for him to visit the Western Territory, Yang Kai also wanted to find a secluded place here to arrange a cross-territory Space Array. That way, it would be much more convenient for him to return to the Western Territory in the future.

However, after searching for several days, he had yet to find a suitable place, leaving him rather helpless.

Although the Western Territory was barren and impoverished compared with the other three territories, that was only a relative assessment. It still possessed many famous mountains, great rivers, and cultivation Sacred Lands rich in Spiritual Qi.

The renowned Shadow Slaughter Palace was located in the Western Territory. Shadow Slaughter Palace was the Western Territory's hegemonic Sect as well as a Great Emperor Sect, and its founder was none other than the fearsome Night Shadow Great Emperor.

Compared with the other Great Emperor Sects, Shadow Slaughter Palace was undoubtedly the most mysterious. Even the Western Territory's native cultivators did not know exactly where this Great Emperor Sect was located or how many people it contained, but that did nothing to diminish Shadow Slaughter Palace's supreme status in their hearts.

If Star God Palace was the Sacred Land in the hearts of the Southern Territory's cultivators, then Shadow Slaughter Palace held the same weight in the hearts of the Western Territory's cultivators.

Great Emperors were already the strongest among experts, and a Great Emperor proficient in concealment and assassination was even more terrifying. A rumour circulated throughout the Western Territory that there was no clear difference in strength among the Great Emperors, but if they were asked which opponent they dreaded most, their answer could only be Night Shadow Great Emperor.

Such an honour was not something an ordinary person could receive.

The city was not small. Yang Kai found a tavern, went inside, and sat at a table by the window. A waiter immediately came over and greeted him enthusiastically, "Honoured Guest, what would you like?"

"Do you have Sword Wine?" Yang Kai asked.

The waiter grinned. "Honoured Guest has truly come to the right place. There are over a thousand taverns of all sizes in Point Star City, but fewer than ten sell Sword Wine. This humble establishment happens to be one of them."

Yang Kai waved his hand grandly. "Bring me a hundred jars to wet my throat first!"

Shadow Slaughter Palace and Night Shadow Great Emperor were not the only things that made the Western Territory famous throughout the world. There was also the Sword Wine brewed by Wine Sword Manor. The process of brewing this Sword Wine was extremely complicated and time-consuming, and it even required the assistance of an expert proficient in the Sword Dao, who would infuse their own insights into the Sword Dao into the wine. Every jar was incomparably precious.

However, Sword Wine offered enormous benefits to cultivators. Drinking it over a long period could improve one's cultivation, and it was especially helpful to cultivators who practised the Sword Dao, as it could aid them in comprehending it.

It was precisely because Sword Wine existed that the Western Territory had far more experts who used swords than the other three territories.

When Yang Kai and Zhu Qing had first met, she had constantly pestered him, annoying him to no end. To rid himself of her harassment, Yang Kai had told her to go to the Western Territory and bring back some Sword Wine for him to taste. He had only meant to make things difficult for her, but unexpectedly, Zhu Qing truly obtained some.

Recalling their initial estrangement and mutual rejection, then comparing it with how they had now truly become husband and wife, Yang Kai could not help sighing with emotion. The Heavenly Dao was impermanent, yet Heaven's course followed its laws; all causes and effects moved in cycles, while fate worked in its own mysterious ways.

Zhu Qing probably had not expected the two of them to end up like this either.

Now that he had personally arrived in the Western Territory, the first thing Yang Kai thought of was Sword Wine. If he could bring it back in large quantities, it would also help Lingxiao Palace's future development. The strength of his friends and family on Dark Star was probably still quite low. Once he brought them to the Star Realm, cultivating would certainly be much easier with Sword Wine's assistance.

Perhaps he could visit Wine Sword Manor and find a way to establish a partnership with them. He was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, after all. As long as he could satisfy their requirements, he believed Wine Sword Manor would not reject his proposal.

As all kinds of thoughts flashed through his mind, a sneer suddenly came from a nearby table. "What backwater did this bumpkin crawl out of? Sword Wine is such a fine brew, yet he actually wants a hundred jars. He truly doesn't know how high the Heavens are or how deep the Earth is."

Yang Kai turned his head and saw a burly man with his chest bared looking at him mockingly. This man had a bulbous nose and a toad-like mouth, making him extraordinarily ugly. A necklace of skulls hung around his neck. No one knew what kind of beast bones these skulls came from, but each was the size of a fist and exuded a cold, sinister aura that made him look even more ferocious.

Although his appearance left much to be desired, what surprised Yang Kai was that this burly man actually possessed a Second-Stage Emperor Realm cultivation.

Two other people sat at the same table as the burly man, and both were First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivators.

This discovery made Yang Kai frown. From what he had observed, this Point Star City did not seem particularly large, so there was no reason for so many Emperor Realm Masters to be gathered here.

In fact, besides the three Emperor Realm Masters at that table, there seemed to be two more in the hall. Their Divine Senses quietly surged as they vigilantly observed their surroundings.

Yang Kai clearly sensed something unusual. These Emperor Realm Masters seemed to be waiting for something here.

When their gazes met, the burly man slapped the table and snapped fiercely, "What are you looking at? You're the bumpkin I'm talking about!"

What a foul temper! Yang Kai secretly laughed and could not be bothered to argue with him. However, the two Emperor Realm Masters at the same table hurriedly said, "Please calm your anger, Lord Xiong. Why lower yourself to the level of this country bumpkin? Discussing serious business is more important."

"Indeed, indeed," the other person hurriedly agreed.

The burly man surnamed Xiong coldly snorted. Yang Kai's apparent show of weakness seemed to greatly satisfy his vanity. He cast Yang Kai a disdainful glance, raised his wine cup, and drained it in one gulp, his face filled with smugness.

Seeing the disturbance end just as quickly as it had begun, the waiter beside them broke out in a cold sweat. He cautiously looked at Yang Kai and asked, "Has Honoured Guest come from afar?"

"You've got a good eye, lad!" Yang Kai smiled lightly and did not deny it.

Generally speaking, servants who worked in such bustling places developed extremely discerning eyes and could roughly determine a person's identity and status with a single glance. Moreover, Yang Kai's clothing differed subtly from that of the Western Territory's cultivators, and he had the travel-worn air of someone who had journeyed a great distance. If the waiter could not even notice these things, he would be woefully unqualified for his job.

The waiter smiled. "Then Honoured Guest cannot be blamed. Allow me to explain: only Wine Sword Manor can produce Sword Wine in this entire world, and its annual output is limited. Although this humble establishment sells Sword Wine, our supply is not large, so as for a hundred jars... there is absolutely no way we could provide that many."

Yang Kai understood and asked, "How many jars can I buy?"

The waiter replied awkwardly, "One jar."

"Only one jar!" Although Yang Kai had been somewhat prepared, he was still taken aback. It seemed that even in the Western Territory, Sword Wine was not something one could casually drink. He truly did not know where Zhu Qing had obtained hers back then. Not paying it much mind, he nodded. "One jar it is, then. Bring me your best."

As soon as he said this, the burly man nearby sneered again. "You think you're worthy of drinking the finest Sword Wine? Why don't you piss on the ground and look at your reflection? Truly, a horse never knows how long its face is."

Another person at that table also sneered. "When the forest is large, it contains every kind of bird. Why should Lord Xiong concern himself with someone like him?"

The burly man shook his head and declared, "This Lord despises embroidered pillows like him the most, people who think themselves elegant and refined but possess nothing except good looks. Whenever this Lord sees one, I'll beat one; whenever I see two, I'll beat both!"

Yang Kai acted as though he had heard nothing and simply looked at the waiter. "What's the catch?"

The waiter grew even more embarrassed and explained, "Sword Wine is divided into three grades: low, middle, and high. This humble establishment normally sells only low- and middle-grade Sword Wine to its guests. We offer one jar of high-grade Sword Wine each day, but one must participate in an auction for a chance to obtain it."

"So it has to be auctioned!" Yang Kai showed a look of understanding, knowing this was a method the tavern used to attract customers by making a rare commodity even more desirable. He immediately asked, "Today's auction..."

"It was already held this morning."

Yang Kai nodded and pondered for a moment before saying, "Then bring me a jar of middle-grade Sword Wine."

"Right away, Honoured Guest. Please wait a moment!" Seeing that Yang Kai was so easy to deal with, the waiter also breathed a sigh of relief. After acknowledging the order, he turned and left.

Yang Kai turned to look out the window at the ceaseless stream of people passing below, and his desire to visit Wine Sword Manor grew even stronger.

“One thing’s for sure, Wind and Cloud Pavilion is really bleeding this time. They’ve already issued a bounty: whoever kills that little bitch will receive ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals. And if someone captures her alive, the reward is even more incredible—a full twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals.”

The voice came from the neighbouring table. All three people there were Emperor Realm Masters and believed they were speaking quietly enough that no one else could hear them. They did not know that Yang Kai's Divine Sense was immensely powerful, so no matter how softly they spoke, he could hear them with perfect clarity.

Rewards of ten or twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals were enough to tempt even these Emperor Realm Masters. After all, a top Northern Territory Sect like Li Dragon Palace earned only fifty million Upper-Level Source Crystals a year, and that was the income of the entire Sect. The amount that reached any individual was far smaller.

By comparison, ten or twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals was an astonishing sum.

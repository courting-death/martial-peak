## Chapter 3058 — Matching Ruthlessness

A fight to the death!

The moment the Second Elder said this, the atmosphere changed abruptly.

Had anyone else spoken these words, they might have been nothing more than a threat, but since they had come from the Second Elder, she truly intended to fight him to the death! She had been thoroughly enraged, and only Yang Kai's blood could wash away the Dragon Clan's humiliation.

Zhu Qing's face went pale.

She had thought that capturing Fu Chi would give them room to negotiate with the Elders, but who could have known it would instead drive the Second Elder to make such a declaration? Panic filled her heart, and she no longer knew what to do. All she could do was look toward Yang Kai's towering back.

"You still dare threaten me?" Yang Kai's expression turned vicious. Without another word, he grabbed one of Fu Chi's arms. Given the enormous difference in their size, it looked as though he were pinching a toothpick between two fingers.

"What are you doing?!" Fu Chi cried out in alarm.

Before the words had even left his mouth, Yang Kai suddenly exerted his strength.

Chi...

Blood sprayed through the air.

Fu Chi stared blankly for an instant, then began screaming without end.

Yang Kai had actually torn off his arm by brute force. The ragged wound gushed blood, and drops of pure Dragon Blood spurted out like a fountain. Every drop was crystalline and perfectly rounded, and as they struck the earth, they instantly stained the ground red.

Dragon Blood was an extremely precious substance. Every drop was worth a fortune and could be used for Alchemy or consumed directly. For Dragon Descendants like Li Jiao in particular, Dragon Blood was even more beneficial than Dragon Blood Flowers.

Yet at this moment, this incomparably precious Dragon Blood poured out as though it cost nothing. The blood-red sight battered everyone's nerves and filled them with dread.

"Hiss..."

The sound of people drawing sharp breaths rang out.

Everyone stared at Yang Kai in a daze, their eyes brimming with shock and horror.

Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong each stepped forward, their expressions abruptly turning solemn as boundless Dragon Pressure descended upon Yang Kai. They had not reacted before because Yang Kai had yet to do anything that truly harmed Dragon Isle's interests, and out of consideration for Zhu Qing, they had been unwilling to bully a junior with their superior strength.

Now, however, the situation was different. He had torn off the arm of an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon. As Dragon Isle's Great Elder and Fourth Elder, they naturally could not stand by and ignore it.

"Don't you dare!" Yang Kai turned his head and glared at Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong as he seized Fu Chi's other arm. His savage expression resembled a ferocious beast ready to devour someone at any moment, and a terrifying light gleamed in his eyes.

Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong stopped in their tracks. The Great Elder said in a deep voice, "You have gone too far."

Yang Kai shook his head. "It is your Dragon Isle that has gone too far!"

"Right and wrong are clear to all. Release Fu Chi, and we can still discuss this."

Yang Kai replied without hesitation, "There is nothing to discuss with you. Let Qing'er and me leave, and I will spare his life! Otherwise, we all die together!"

If those words had been nothing more than a feeble threat before, they now carried tremendous weight. Yang Kai had torn off Fu Chi's arm at the slightest disagreement, clearly showing that he was prepared to perish together with him.

Both the Great Elder and Second Elder of the Dragon Clan possessed the strength to take Yang Kai's life, but the moment they killed him, Fu Chi would certainly be buried alongside him. The Dragon Clan had too few members and could not afford such a loss.

"Release him!" the Second Elder growled through gritted teeth.

Yang Kai offered no reply. He merely sneered and exerted his strength once more.

Chi...

Fu Chi's other arm was torn off as well. Yang Kai casually tossed the severed limb aside, his eyes fixed unwaveringly on Fu Zhun in open provocation.

You threaten me, and I tear off one of Fu Chi's arms. You shout at me, and I tear off the other! Once his arms are gone, he still has his legs. The Dragon Clan possesses tenacious vitality anyway, so injuries like these are not enough to kill him. Did you not want to bestow Zhu Qing in marriage upon Fu Chi? I will kill Fu Chi first and see how you marry her to him then.

Before the Dragon Clan, he could not show the slightest weakness. If they were ruthless, then he had to be even more ruthless.

Fu Zhun's face turned livid with rage.

Not even what had happened more than ten years ago had provoked such fury from her, but Yang Kai's actions this time had undoubtedly driven her rage to unprecedented heights.

With both arms torn off by brute force, Fu Chi was pale from the pain and trembling all over. He was a member of the Dragon Clan, and his body should have been incomparably powerful. Under ordinary circumstances, no one could possibly have torn off his arms like this.

But Yang Kai had transformed into an eight-zhang Dragon body and possessed boundless strength. Forget Fu Chi; even if the Great Elder or Second Elder fell into his grasp, he could tear one of them apart for the world to see.

Fu Chi lowered his head, his eyes filled with venomous hatred.

"I will give you ten breaths to consider. Decide for yourselves what you intend to do!" Yang Kai swept his gaze across the Dragon Clan's three Elders and immediately began counting.

Although he was clearly the weaker party, his bearing was exceedingly lofty, as though everything was already within his grasp and he alone was master of the situation. The sight left everyone feeling deeply disconcerted.

"Ten..."

"Nine..."

"Eight..."

His voice was steady, without the slightest trace of nervousness, and his face was filled with resolve.

Zhu Qing stepped out from the side and stood beside Yang Kai. She drew a soft breath, her expression equally resolute.

Husband and wife acting as one, sharing joy and hardship, living and dying together. Zhu Qing did not say a word, yet she had already made her position clear.

"One!" Yang Kai finished counting to ten, but the three Dragon Clan Elders still showed no response.

It was not that they had no concern for Fu Chi's life. Rather, the Dragon Clan could not yield today, or the reputation of the entire clan would suffer once word spread. Yang Kai's forceful stance had left them no choice but to abandon Fu Chi.

Zhu Yan sighed inwardly. He had never expected matters to reach this point. Thinking about it now, the greatest reason was that Yang Kai's methods had been far too extreme. Yet anyone placed in the same situation would likely have been unable to find a solution that satisfied both sides. This was destined to become a tragedy for the Dragon Clan and the end of Yang Kai's road.

Yang Kai lowered his head and grinned at Zhu Qing. A smile appeared on his face, but paired with his Half-Dragon features, it looked somewhat savage. "Qing'er, it seems we are destined to become a pair of ill-fated lovers who die together today."

Zhu Qing's beautiful hair fluttered as she smiled faintly. "With someone beside me on the Yellow Springs Road, neither of us will be lonely."

"Good!" Yang Kai roared, his expression abruptly turning solemn. Extremely intricate and profound syllables suddenly spilled from his mouth. They were so strange that the several hundred mortals found them merely wondrous, but when the sound entered the Dragons' ears, their hearts pounded wildly and all the Dragon Blood in their bodies began to boil.

"A Dragon Clan Secret Technique!" Fu Zhun's expression changed as she growled through gritted teeth, "You actually learned a Dragon Clan Secret Technique?"

Yang Kai stared at her without replying. His voice gradually rose in pitch until it resembled a true Dragon's roar, echoing through the nine heavens.

"The inheritance of the Ancestral Dragon Origin!" Zhu Yan exclaimed in realization, his face filled with shock.

The inheritances of Divine Spirits were not passed down orally from generation to generation, but hidden within their own bloodlines. Once a bloodline awakened to a certain degree, its inheritance would awaken along with it.

As the foremost of all Divine Spirits, the Dragon Clan was naturally no exception to this rule.

As the bloodlines of pure-blooded Dragons grew stronger, they would gradually master certain Secret Techniques that only the Dragon Clan could use.

What Yang Kai was displaying now was a Dragon Clan Secret Technique, and an extremely advanced one at that. From the looks of it, he had clearly obtained part of the Dragon Clan's inheritance, and the source of that inheritance could only be the Ancestral Dragon Origin within him.

This was precisely what shocked Zhu Yan. If Yang Kai had obtained the Dragon Clan's inheritance, did that not mean the Ancestral Dragon Origin had already acknowledged him?

No wonder. No wonder he had been able to undo Zhu Qing's seal without anyone noticing.

Zhu Qing's seal had been placed by the Second Elder using a Dragon Clan Secret Technique. Judging by Yang Kai's current display, he truly did possess the ability to undo it.

If that was the case, then he could no longer be considered human. He could be said to be a member of the Dragon Clan, and an extremely important one at that.

Zhu Yan had understood this, so how could Fu Zhun fail to realize it as well? For a moment, she could scarcely believe it.

She had previously planned to detain Yang Kai and then find an opportunity to extract the Ancestral Dragon Origin from his body. Now, however, that plan was clearly impossible.

Extraction required the Origin to be intact.

The Ancestral Dragon Origin had now acknowledged this human and had even fused with him to a great extent. How could it be extracted? Even if they somehow succeeded, the Origin would no longer be complete, and that was a loss the entire Dragon Clan could not accept.

Only a member of the Dragon Clan could use a Dragon Clan Secret Technique. This was an ironclad rule that could not be broken.

The syllables spilling from Yang Kai's mouth were words of the exceedingly ancient Dragon Language. Others might not understand them, but how could the Dragon Clan fail to?

In an instant, every member of the Dragon Clan was shocked, and the three Elders stared in a daze.

Before they could react, Fu Chi, who had remained clutched in Yang Kai's hand, suddenly screamed. His whole body trembled violently, and sweat soaked his red robes. His eyes, which had originally been filled with venomous hatred, suddenly went blank, as though he had fallen under some evil spell.

Immediately afterward, arcs of lightning began to dance, and amid a burst of crackling, a Dragon-shaped figure abruptly appeared above Fu Chi's head. The Thunder Dragon opened its mouth and roared, seemingly unwilling to leave Fu Chi's body, but under Yang Kai's unbroken stream of Dragon Language, it was forced upward inch by inch. Its Dragon eyes were filled with pain and terror, and in a brief instant, its entire head had been pulled free.

This was clearly Fu Chi's Dragon Clan Origin. Once his Origin left his body, he would lose his status as a member of the Dragon Clan, a fate more agonizing than death.

"Dragon Punishment!" The faces of the gathered Dragons changed drastically, and their bodies went ice-cold, as though all the Dragon Blood within them had frozen.

When a Dragon died, their Origin would not disappear. Instead, it would return to the Dragon Tomb and become Origin Power in a chaotic state. When a female Dragon became pregnant, the Dragon Clan would hold a ceremony to commune with the Dragon Tomb, allowing the Origin Power within to be transferred to the unborn child.

In other words, although the death of a Dragon brought some loss, it did not sever the clan's foundation. If an Origin was destroyed, however, everything would truly be reduced to ashes.

This was also why the Dragon Clan had always remained small in number yet never gone extinct. Every Dragon who died left behind their Origin, making one final contribution to the continuation of the clan.

Losing even one Origin might mean that the Dragon Clan would forever have one fewer member from that day onward.

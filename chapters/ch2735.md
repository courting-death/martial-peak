## Chapter 2735 — You’re Cheating

“Master Ji truly is remarkable!” Yang Kai praised as he gazed at the Spiritual Pill adorned with Pill Patterns. An Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pill bearing Pill Patterns was indeed impressive. Even when he himself refined Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills, it was rare to see Pill Patterns appear.

Just this alone proved Ji Ying was worthy of the title Imperial-Grade Pill Master.

“I’m ashamed, truly ashamed. The Pill Patterns this time are thanks to Brother Yang’s help!” Ji Ying waved his hand modestly.

“What does this have to do with him?” Mi Qi retorted from the side. “The Pill Patterns are a testament to Master Ji’s superb pill refining skills. What’s it got to do with him?”

“Exactly. If he hadn’t deliberately interfered, there might have been more than one Spiritual Pill with Pill Patterns by now,” Li Jiao chimed in.

Ji Ying shook his head, not bothering to explain. He believed Yang Kai didn’t need him to clarify.

This time, being able to refine an Emperor Yuan Pill with Pill Patterns was thanks to the immense pressure Yang Kai gave him. Though he was distracted twice during the process, under that tremendous pressure—especially that fleeting glance—he gained some insight, allowing him to produce such a perfect result.

Otherwise, with his usual ability, even after refining a hundred batches of Emperor Yuan Pills, he might not get a single Pill Pattern. Now, one batch produced one—it was nothing short of a miracle.

But if he tried to refine another batch of Emperor Yuan Pills, he probably wouldn’t be so lucky again.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao saw Ji Ying remain silent and assumed he was just being polite to Yang Kai. They couldn’t imagine the twists and turns behind the scenes. Mi Qi spoke up, “Palace Master Yang, we’ve already checked Master Ji’s Spiritual Pills. What about the ones you refined?”

“Heh, Palace Master Yang’s pill refining skills are extraordinary. Surely he produced Pill Patterns as well,” Li Jiao teased, deliberately elevating Yang Kai with malicious intent.

Yang Kai smiled faintly.

“Since we all made a bet, let’s take a look. Palace Master Yang, you’re not avoiding showing your Spiritual Pills because you think you can’t beat Master Ji, are you?” Mi Qi pressed, his face taut as if he’d already won.

Ji Ying quickly interjected, “No need for this, you two. Brother Yang is also an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, and the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace. We’ll all be living in the Northern Territory from now on, seeing each other often. Why not let today’s matter end here? Let’s drink together, let bygones be bygones, and make it a story worth spreading.”

He had agreed to compete with Yang Kai in pill refining out of anger, but now he was worried for Yang Kai.

He was lucky to refine an Emperor Yuan Pill with Pill Patterns, but Yang Kai might not be so fortunate. If Yang Kai lost, that would mean fifty billion Upper-Level Source Crystals. Both he and his disciple had benefited from Yang Kai, especially his disciple, who was still immersed in enlightenment. If Yang Kai lost fifty billion because of him, he’d feel terrible.

That was why Ji Ying wanted to play the peacemaker and smooth things over. He deliberately emphasized Yang Kai’s status as an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, hoping Mi Qi and Li Jiao would take the hint and let the matter rest. If they could drop the bet today, they’d surely win Yang Kai’s goodwill. In the future, wouldn’t there be plenty of benefits? At the very least, if they ever needed a Spiritual Pill refined, Yang Kai would never refuse—and Ji Ying himself could lend a hand as well.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi exchanged glances, understanding Ji Ying’s intentions, but both looked troubled.

If it were an ordinary matter, they’d gladly give Ji Ying face, but this involved fifty billion Upper-Level Source Crystals—even they hesitated.

After a while, Mi Qi frowned and said, “Master Ji, a man’s word is his bond. Palace Master Yang made a bet with us, so he must honor it. If Li Jiao and I let it go, Palace Master Yang might feel slighted.”

Li Jiao nodded, “Mi Qi is right. Please forgive us, Master Ji. It’s not that we don’t want to, but a bet is a bet.”

Ji Ying’s face darkened. He hadn’t expected that even after personally pleading, these two wouldn’t give him face. Clearly, they were determined to fleece Yang Kai.

Just as he was about to say more, Yang Kai suddenly raised his hand, “No need to say more, Brother Ji. I accept the bet and its outcome.”

Li Jiao and Mi Qi were delighted, praising, “Palace Master Yang is a man of his word. If Lingxiao Palace needs help in the Northern Territory, just let us know. Our two sects won’t refuse.”

Anyone can say nice things. When the time comes, they can decide whether to help. Besides, they didn’t think Yang Kai would ask for help. After this, Lingxiao Palace and their two sects wouldn’t be friendly, even if not outright enemies.

Yang Kai smiled lightly and said, “I only said I’d accept the outcome of the bet, but I haven’t lost, have I? Are you two so confident you can take those five billion?”

Li Jiao and Mi Qi were stunned. Li Jiao said, “Let’s see then. Let’s see how Palace Master Yang’s Spiritual Pills turned out.”

“Yes, yes, let’s take a look,” Li Jiao nodded repeatedly.

Since he was bound to lose, let him lose clearly.

“Ah!” Ji Ying sighed. Things had reached this point, and he couldn’t change the outcome. Five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals were enough for Li Jiao and Mi Qi to risk offending him.

“Brother Ji, help me take a look.” Yang Kai tossed the jade bottle in his hand to Ji Ying.

Ji Ying caught it and nodded, “Alright.”

As he spoke, he uncorked the jade bottle and poured out the Emperor Yuan Pills inside.

All eyes focused intently, and everyone’s breathing seemed to quicken.

The three Yao Kings exchanged glances, their eyes flashing with ferocity. No words were needed—they’d already decided that if Yang Kai lost, they’d immediately seize Mi Qi and Li Jiao.

Trying to seize fifty billion Upper-Level Source Crystals? That’s pure fantasy!

“Nine… nine pills?” Li Jiao’s eyes, full of anticipation, suddenly widened as he stared at the nine Spiritual Pills in Ji Ying’s left hand, his face transformed by shock.

Yang Kai had refined nine Emperor Yuan Pills, matching Ji Ying exactly. Did this mean Yang Kai’s pill refining skills were equal to Ji Ying’s?

Not every Imperial-Grade Pill Master could refine nine Spiritual Pills in one batch.

“Pill Patterns?” Mi Qi gasped, staring dumbly at one of the nine Spiritual Pills.

That pill matched the one Ji Ying had refined earlier, covered in intricate patterns, as if containing profound mysteries.

“Ah?” Ji Ying was also taken aback.

Yang Kai refining nine Emperor Yuan Pills was expected, but for one to have Pill Patterns was truly surprising.

He managed it by luck, but Yang Kai was just as lucky.

“This… this can’t be!” Mi Qi rubbed his eyes hard, afraid he was hallucinating. But no matter how he looked, one of the nine pills indeed had Pill Patterns.

“You’re cheating!” Li Jiao’s face darkened as he glared at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai snorted, “Palace Master Li, mind your words. You can eat whatever you want, but you can’t say whatever you want! When did I cheat?”

Li Jiao snorted, “You suggested refining Emperor Yuan Pills. That pill with Pill Patterns must have been prepared beforehand, and now you’re passing it off as part of the batch!”

Mi Qi immediately felt this made sense. When Yang Kai finished, he quickly put the pills into the jade bottle, then had Ji Ying take his out first. The bottle stayed in Yang Kai’s hand the whole time—he had plenty of opportunity to swap pills.

Yes, yes, that’s the only explanation.

The kid’s cunning! Absolutely scheming and despicable.

“Palace Master Yang, this isn’t right.” Mi Qi’s face was grim. The five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals were almost in hand, but now, thanks to Yang Kai’s trickery, it was uncertain. He was naturally unhappy, and the worst part was, he and Li Jiao had no proof.

“If you want to know whether it’s a fake, just ask Brother Ji.” Yang Kai sneered, unwilling to argue.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi both turned to Ji Ying.

Ji Ying nodded, “This pill was just made, not prepared in advance.”

“What!”

“Impossible, Master Ji, look again.”

“You doubt my word?” Ji Ying’s face darkened, clearly displeased. He wanted to befriend Yang Kai, but wouldn’t lie about this. For Mi Qi and Li Jiao to question him was an insult to his character and reputation.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao were startled, realizing they’d angered Ji Ying.

Mi Qi hurriedly said, “Master Ji, we wouldn’t dare doubt you. But we don’t know Palace Master Yang’s character, so we have to be cautious. Please check again—did he do anything?”

Ji Ying replied calmly, “Each batch of Spiritual Pills has its own unique marks. Only those deeply immersed in this field can tell. In my opinion, these nine pills are from the same batch.”

Mi Qi and Li Jiao were stunned.

“Of course, the simplest way to tell is that the pill is still warm. If you don’t believe me, feel it yourself.” Ji Ying said, tossing the Emperor Yuan Pill with Pill Patterns to Mi Qi.

Mi Qi took it and carefully felt it, discovering it was indeed as Ji Ying said—still warm, clearly just refined.

His heart sank, and he handed it to Li Jiao to check as well.

Yang Kai sneered, “So, do you still want to accuse me of cheating?”

Mi Qi forced a laugh, “Just joking, just joking. It was only a joke, Palace Master Yang, don’t take it seriously.”

“That joke wasn’t funny at all.”

Li Jiao coughed, “Both of you refined a pill with Pill Patterns, and both got nine pills. So… it’s a tie?”

“A tie, a tie. No winner or loser,” Mi Qi quickly agreed, though his disappointment was obvious.

Yang Kai chuckled, “Same quantity, same Pill Patterns. Then let’s compare quality. There’s always a difference in quality!”

Ji Ying nodded, “Indeed, quality can distinguish the winner.”

## Chapter 2777 — The Three Great Taboos

Half a day later, a group of people huddled together in a corner, each one pale-faced. Chen Qian and another female Elder were trembling uncontrollably.

Yang Kai stared in horror at the beautiful figure dancing like a sprite in the great hall, wielding an Imperial Treasure longsword. He swallowed hard and said, “What is going on here?”

That figure moved with wild abandon, swaying as if about to fall, holding a wine jar in one hand, her eyes bloodshot. Every so often, she would take a swig, praise the fine wine, and when the mood struck, she could not help herself—her longsword blossomed with sword flowers, cold light flashing everywhere.

Di Rong said hoarsely, “Junior Brother Yang, you joined the Shrine later, so you probably don’t know about our Shrine’s three great taboos. I didn’t expect you’d witness one on your very first day as an Elder. Truly… congratulations.”

Yang Kai was stunned. “Three great taboos? What three great taboos?”

Chen Qian whispered, “The Hall Master’s birthday, Senior Brother Qiu losing his temper, and… Senior Sister Gao drinking!”

“How is that a taboo?” Yang Kai exclaimed in surprise. Looking again at the woman ahead, he felt the noble and pure image he had of her completely collapse, vanishing without a trace. Now, she was nothing but a madwoman. Wiping the cold sweat from his forehead, he muttered, “Why does Senior Sister Gao become a different person after drinking? Was she possessed by some wicked cultivator?”

“It’s not that serious…” You Kun hurriedly waved his hand, his expression odd. “It’s just that Junior Sister Gao… was born unable to drink.”

“That can’t be, right? Senior Sister Gao is at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. No matter how strong the spirit wine, she could easily dissolve it with Emperor Yuan. How did it end up like this?”

Chen Qian sighed. “It’s all because of our Hall Master.”

“What does the Hall Master have to do with it?” Yang Kai was utterly astonished. Everything he saw today had overturned his worldview.

Chen Qian said, “Senior Sister Gao was raised by the Hall Master himself. You should know that, right?”

“Mm, I’ve vaguely heard about it,” Yang Kai nodded.

Chen Qian’s face darkened with sorrow as she continued, “When Senior Sister Gao was little, the Hall Master would force her to drink. She got drunk every time. Once, she drank too much and went mad for days, unable to recover. Ever since then, whenever Senior Sister Gao touches alcohol, she becomes like this.”

In just a few words, she painted a tragic picture of Gao Xueting’s childhood—enough to make anyone who heard it feel sorrowful and shed tears.

“Absolutely… inhuman! Outrageous!” Yang Kai cursed angrily, drawing a chorus of agreement from the other Elders. They all nodded, their eyes filled with undisguised contempt for the Hall Master.

After a moment, Yang Kai said, “Since she can’t drink, she should know better herself. Where did she get so much spirit wine…” As he spoke, Yang Kai sniffed the aroma and praised, “This is spirit wine aged for several hundred years. Tsk tsk, what a waste.”

“Who knows where she got it?” Chen Qian was on the verge of tears. “Maybe she’s in a good mood these days? Last night, she even came to my Xiaoquan Peak and chatted with me for half the night before going back.”

Another female Elder chimed in, “She came to find me in the second half of the night…”

Yang Kai sighed, “What should we do now? How long will this madness last?”

Chen Qian said, “Once she finishes the wine, it’ll be over.”

Yang Kai glanced around and groaned, “There are still a hundred jars left. How long will that take? At this rate, it’ll be days and nights before she’s done.”

“There’s no other way,” Chen Qian shook her head.

Yang Kai pondered for a moment, determination flashing across his face as he stood up.

“What are you doing, Junior Brother Yang?” You Kun exclaimed in alarm.

“I’ll drink with her. The sooner we finish the wine, the sooner this ends.” With that, he strode forward, undaunted by the odds!

The Elders instantly looked at him with admiration, stunned by his resolve.

“Ah, youth…” Di Rong shook his head and sighed.

“Enough talk, let’s go while Junior Brother Yang keeps her busy.” You Kun signaled to the others, glancing at Gao Xueting, who had suddenly grabbed Yang Kai by the neck and was pouring wine down his throat. He quickly looked away, unable to bear the sight.

Junior Brother, we’ll remember your kindness. One day, we’ll repay you handsomely!

Everyone understood, holding their breath and tiptoeing toward the exit.

Just as they reached the hall’s entrance, a streak of sword light suddenly slashed past, grazing You Kun’s cheek and slicing off a few strands of hair, which floated in the air. The ground was left with a deep gash. If not for the protective array, that strike might have brought the whole hall down.

“Trying to leave?” Gao Xueting’s face was icy cold, like a frigid wind from the depths of the underworld. Even the air seemed to freeze. Her chilling voice echoed in everyone’s ears, making them feel as if they’d fallen into an ice cellar. “Take one more step and see what happens.”

Chen Qian looked pitifully at Gao Xueting and whispered, “Senior Sister, you’re drunk…”

Gao Xueting tossed Yang Kai aside, her figure flickering as she appeared at the door, blocking the way. She slashed her longsword down, glaring at Chen Qian. “How am I drunk?”

“You… I…” Chen Qian was at a loss, the dignified Emperor Realm expert now as timid as a mouse before a cat, unable to utter a complete sentence.

“Enough talk, drink!” Gao Xueting grabbed Chen Qian, locking her neck in her arm, and raised the wine jar to pour it into her mouth.

After just a few gulps, Chen Qian couldn’t help but spit it out. The wine trickled down her slender neck, soaking her clothes, and her gentle, refined image was utterly ruined…

When the jar was empty, Gao Xueting finally let go of Chen Qian, tossing her aside like a rag. Chen Qian collapsed to the ground, pounding her chest and coughing, her face flushed bright red.

“Hahahahaha!” Gao Xueting laughed heartily, clearly in a wonderful mood. Her beautiful eyes turned to fix on You Kun.

You Kun immediately jumped up, snatched a wine jar, and said quickly, “No need for you to do it, Junior Sister. Senior Brother will drink on his own.”

With that, he raised his head and drank, bold and unrestrained.

Only then did Gao Xueting show a satisfied expression, her eyes turning again.

Every Elder she set her sights on looked as if they were attending a funeral. Not daring to resist, each hurriedly grabbed a jar of fine wine and gulped it down in large mouthfuls.

Gao Xueting giggled, completely losing her senses, and once again began sword dancing in the hall. Sword energy flew everywhere, forcing Yang Kai and the others to flee in disarray.

“Junior Brother Yang…” Chen Qian finally recovered, wiped her mouth, and kindly warned, “Whatever you do, don’t use your cultivation to dispel the drunkenness, or else…”

“Or else what?” Yang Kai asked in horror.

He was a bit late with his question. Gao Xueting suddenly appeared before him like a ghost, grabbed his collar, and lifted him like a chick. In a chilling voice, she said, “You brat, not playing fair, are you trying to cheat?”

As she spoke, her longsword trembled, as if it could slip from her hand at any moment.

Yang Kai’s face turned pale. He squeezed his legs together and cried out, “Senior Sister Gao, have mercy!”

“Hmph, you want mercy?” Gao Xueting snorted, looking Yang Kai up and down before nodding. “Fine.”

With a flick of her sword, a jar of spirit wine appeared on the tip and was steadily delivered to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai’s face was full of misery, but under Gao Xueting’s forceful gaze, he could only grit his teeth and pick up the jar.

He drank heartily!

“Good, good, good!” Gao Xueting clapped and jumped, her face full of joy like a little girl with a new toy. As soon as Yang Kai finished, another jar was handed to him.

Yang Kai looked at her with deep resentment.

“Are you drinking or not?” Gao Xueting’s face suddenly turned cold.

“Drinking, drinking…” Yang Kai stared at the sword across his lap, nodding like a pecking chick.

After downing thirteen jars of spirit wine aged for centuries, Yang Kai burped, his body swaying. Under the sympathetic gazes of the Elders, he managed to say, “Senior Brothers and Sisters… your Junior Brother will go on ahead!”

With that, he fell flat on his back.

“Useless!” Gao Xueting mercilessly kicked Yang Kai, sneered, then leaned against him, using him as a cushion. She hugged a wine jar and started drinking again.

The clear, fiery wine turned into a rain of arrows, vanishing down her throat in just a few breaths.

With Yang Kai as a warning, the other Elders dared not hesitate. Each grabbed a jar and started drinking. Soon, the whole hall was shrouded in gloom, as if Qingyang Shrine was about to be wiped out.

The spirit wine was potent, and since they couldn’t use their cultivation to dispel the drunkenness, even Emperors couldn’t handle drinking like this.

Before long, Chen Qian was the first to succumb. She felt the world spinning, her eyelids weighed down as if by mountains, and she collapsed to the ground, falling asleep.

Soon after, the other female Elder followed suit.

You Kun and Di Rong were also knocked out one after another.

By midday, the hall was filled with snores, bodies sprawled everywhere. All the Elders of Qingyang Shrine lay on the ground. If anyone else saw this, they would be shocked.

Exalted Emperors, behaving so disgracefully—and not just one or two, but a whole group.

Only Gao Xueting remained, stubbornly battling the wine.

Only after finishing the last jar did she smack her lips, then collapse.

……

“In broad daylight, you all gathered to drink—what kind of example is this!” On Lingyu Peak, Deputy Hall Master Qiu Ran slammed the table, his beard bristling. The Elders of Qingyang Shrine before him all hung their heads in embarrassment.

“You are all Elders of the Shrine, each holding important positions, the pillars of our Shrine. If a powerful enemy had attacked yesterday, what would have become of our Shrine’s foundation built over thousands of years?” Qiu Ran fumed, spittle flying.

“Senior Brother, we know we were wrong,” Gao Xueting admitted readily, head bowed.

“You know you were wrong?” Qiu Ran grew even angrier, glaring at Gao Xueting. “You think just admitting it is enough?”

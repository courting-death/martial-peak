## Chapter 2895 — Hooked Shadow

Yang Kai let out a chuckle. “Why don’t you withdraw all the power from your Incarnation? Facing a genuine Saint, I probably wouldn’t have much strength to fight back.”

“No need!” Mo Duo snorted coldly. To deal with someone who was only equivalent to a Demon King, using seventy percent of his strength was the bottom line he could accept. If he were truly forced to use his full power, that would be a disgrace. He was a dignified Demon Saint, after all, and had his own pride.

As he spoke, a pitch-black iron hook suddenly appeared in his hand. Dense, pure Demonic Qi swirled around the hook, making anyone who glanced at it feel uncomfortable, as if the evil hidden deep within their hearts was being awakened.

Yang Kai’s gaze was fixed on that iron hook, his heart stirring slightly.

The feeling this iron hook gave him was somewhat similar to the Demonic Warhammer that his Incarnation now possessed. Although their forms were completely different, they indeed shared the same origin.

A Saint Artifact! This must be the Saint Artifact that Bao Qi and the others had mentioned before. In the Demon Domain, only experts at the Demon Saint level could wield such treasures. In other words, this iron hook and the Demonic Warhammer were of the same grade.

Yang Kai’s expression grew solemn as he inwardly raised his guard.

For Mo Duo to suddenly take out such a demonic Saint Artifact clearly meant he was about to make a big move. Yang Kai had no choice but to be on full alert.

To Yang Kai’s surprise, after taking out the Saint Artifact, Mo Duo didn’t immediately attack him. Instead, he raised the iron hook and slashed it through the void—east, west, left, right—seemingly without any pattern, acting entirely on a whim.

His demeanor and movements were extremely casual, yet filled with arrogance and self-assurance.

But it was precisely these casual movements that made Yang Kai’s face darken, because he suddenly felt the surrounding space inexplicably become isolated, as if a vast expanse of heaven and earth had been separated from the great world by Mo Duo’s actions, forming an independent space.

He could still see all the scenery outside, but within this isolated world, he could no longer teleport at will.

Space manipulation was an extremely troublesome ability even for a Demon Saint, so Mo Duo had no choice but to neutralize this technique first. His casual slashing through the void had been precisely his method to do so.

After finishing all this, Mo Duo finally turned around. He looked at Yang Kai with a cold expression, slightly raised the iron hook in his hand, and pulled it toward the void.

A massive hooked shadow suddenly appeared, like a soul-reaping weapon, slashing down toward where Yang Kai stood.

The entire world changed at that moment, a chilling aura spreading everywhere.

Yang Kai hurriedly retreated, his expression more solemn than ever. Not daring to be careless, he clapped his hands together. As the Laws of Space surged, a gigantic Moon Blade quietly formed and slashed down at the hooked shadow.

But that wasn’t all. He began to chant, and the Laws of Time started to flow. His hands quickly formed seals, and Years Like a Shuttle followed the Moon Blade, blasting forward.

Space and time—two of the most profound Law Powers—surged one after another, making the already turbulent world even more unpredictable and mysterious.

Mo Duo's expression shifted, a flicker of astonishment crossing his face. He had clearly sensed something amiss, his eyes widening in disbelief. For one person to cultivate both the Laws of Space and the Laws of Time simultaneously—this was something he had never even heard of.

Since ancient times, the Great Dao of Heaven and Earth had three thousand paths and countless laws, but whether it was the Laws of Space or the Laws of Time, only a rare few lucky ones could touch upon them. As for those who could grasp both domains at once, they were almost unheard of. This was not something that could be explained by luck alone—only those favored by destiny could possess such unmatched fortune. Throughout history, such people were always peerless experts.

Boom…

A thunderous roar rang out as the Moon Blade and Years Like a Shuttle collided with the massive hooked shadow, dispersing in the void. Violent energy swept in all directions, causing the isolated world to tremble as if the end of days had arrived.

The shockwaves spread for hundreds of li, knocking both the Barbarian Tribe and the demons off their feet. Yang Kai, under the force of the backlash, was sent flying dozens of zhang. Despite his battered appearance, he still stubbornly shouted, “Mo Duo, seventy percent of your strength isn’t enough! Hurry up and show me your full power—stop holding back!”

As he spoke, he frantically swallowed Pills, looking utterly disheveled.

Mo Duo’s usual elegance and warmth had long since vanished. Provoked by Yang Kai’s taunts, he finally showed anger. Glancing at the Divine Tree over there, he saw that while he had been entangled with Yang Kai, the strange tree had grown over a hundred zhang taller—truly incomprehensible.

“Let’s see how many times you can block this!” Mo Duo roared in anger, once again slashing toward Yang Kai.

The massive hooked shadow appeared a second time.

Yang Kai’s eye twitched as he cursed under his breath, but his movements did not slow.

That brief clash with Mo Duo had made him keenly aware of the gap between them. Even though Mo Duo had only used seventy percent of his strength, Yang Kai was no match for him.

Blocking it once was merely luck; there was no way he could block a second or third time.

“Is this the only move you know? Can’t you do something new?” Yang Kai taunted with a sneer.

Mo Duo sneered back. “A move doesn’t have to be new—so long as it works!”

He had clearly seen his advantage and was preparing to crush Yang Kai with his own divine ability.

The hooked shadow swept in, tearing the void apart. Sun, moon, and stars all seemed to lose their brilliance under that slash.

Yang Kai grunted, and a high-pitched dragon roar rang out. As golden light burst forth, a massive golden dragon head appeared behind him, its majestic eyes overlooking the world, as if all things under heaven were insignificant.

The Golden Sacred Dragon’s phantom appeared for only a moment before merging back into Yang Kai’s body and vanishing.

Yang Kai’s previously weakened aura suddenly surged, climbing to new heights—even stronger than at his previous peak.

“What is this…?”

Mo Duo was greatly shocked, staring intently at the space behind Yang Kai as if trying to see more clearly, but the phantom had already disappeared. His face showed disbelief. “Divine Spirit? How do you have a Divine Spirit Origin?”

Even in ancient times, Divine Spirits were synonymous with power—especially the Dragon Clan, whom even Demon Saints could not ignore. There were also dragons in the Demon Domain—demonic dragons—each one comparable to a Demon Saint, each possessing world-destroying might.

“That’s none of your business.” As the golden light faded, Yang Kai’s figure reappeared.

At this moment, he was no longer that seemingly weak human. He now stood two zhang tall, his entire body covered in scales, short horns sprouting from his head, and his hands transformed into sharp claws. His face was covered in scales, though faint traces of his former features could still be seen.

As the hooked shadow attacked, Yang Kai raised the Myriad Sword high and slashed down with all his might.

After Dragon Transformation, Yang Kai’s strength had clearly increased. With a single swing of the Myriad Sword, the seemingly unstoppable hooked shadow suddenly paused, as if blocked by an impenetrable barrier.

Yang Kai staggered backward, pouring all his strength into the Myriad Sword’s sword light as it clashed with the hooked shadow, producing a thunderous rumble.

Mo Duo sneered, silently flicking the iron hook in his hand, aiming straight at Yang Kai.

In the next instant, Yang Kai’s eyes widened, his face going blank as if he had lost his soul. Even his movements to block the hooked shadow became weak and powerless, allowing the shadow to surge forward once more.

A moment later, Yang Kai shuddered and shouted angrily, “Soul-hooking? Mo Duo, you bully the weak and still resort to such despicable means—how dare you call yourself a Demon Saint!”

Mo Duo gnashed his teeth, visibly flustered and exasperated. “What on earth are you?!”

Just now, he had indeed used a soul-hooking technique. The iron hook in his hand could not only unleash powerful attacks, but also directly target an enemy’s Divine Soul. That silent hook had already turned into a chain in the void, hooking onto Yang Kai’s Divine Soul.

That was why Yang Kai had been dazed for a moment, nearly losing consciousness.

If Mo Duo could pull his Divine Soul out of his body, Yang Kai would inevitably become a walking corpse.

The second massive hooked shadow was just a feint to distract Yang Kai, but what Mo Duo hadn’t expected was that all his painstaking efforts had failed to achieve the desired effect.

For someone only at the Demon King level to barely withstand seventy percent of his strength in a head-on clash was already astonishing. Even though he hadn’t used his full power, from an outsider’s perspective, Yang Kai had already far exceeded all expectations.

What Mo Duo truly couldn’t accept was that the soul-hooking technique hadn’t worked. The man before him was definitely not just someone on par with a Demon King. Even as a Demon Saint, Mo Duo had never encountered such a monster.

Yang Kai snorted coldly, ignoring him, though he was still filled with lingering fear.

Although the moment had been brief, it was the most dangerous moment in his fight with Mo Duo so far. If not for the Warm Spirit Lotus, a supreme treasure of heaven and earth, his Divine Soul would likely have been hooked by Mo Duo.

His Divine Soul was indeed powerful, but not strong enough to withstand Mo Duo’s secret technique. At the critical moment, it was the Warm Spirit Lotus that pulled his Divine Soul back, allowing him to recover.

His own weakness made Yang Kai furious. The sword light from the Myriad Sword soared, suppressing Mo Duo’s hooked shadow and, while Mo Duo was distracted, directly shattering it.

In the dazzling light, all things in the world lost their color.

Mo Duo still seemed dazed, which was extremely unusual for a Demon Saint. Yang Kai’s performance had simply been too shocking.

At that moment, Yang Kai, his face twisted with ferocity, charged at Mo Duo. A dragon claw clenched into a fist and smashed down hard.

Wherever the fist passed, the void rolled and the universe shattered, like a hot iron branding tender skin, leaving a mark that lingered for a long time.

Mo Duo looked up, his emotionless gaze fixed on Yang Kai. He said indifferently, “You will pay the price for your recklessness!”

As he finished speaking, he also raised his fist to meet the attack.

## Chapter 2991 — Beaten to Death with Two Punches

After sweeping the area with their Divine Senses, everyone relaxed. There were no hidden traps or Spirit Arrays here, nor were there any signs of experts lying in ambush.

On the contrary, the woman's posture as she stood with her back to the crowd greatly irritated them. Each of them felt slighted and was extremely indignant.

An old man with a wine-red nose sighed. "Little girl, why did you have to provoke Wind and Cloud Pavilion and bring disaster upon yourself for no reason? Although this Old Master is unwilling to bully the young, the reward is so generous that it is truly impossible not to be tempted. Take this Old Master's advice and surrender obediently. Otherwise, if we really start fighting later and you get hurt, that would be most unfortunate."

His tone was earnest and well-meaning, but his eyes darted about as he spoke, clearly plotting something underhanded.

The others remained silent, but each of them secretly circulated their Emperor Yuan, ready to act at any moment.

The old man continued, "Little girl, you have no chance of winning. Stubborn resistance will only cause you to suffer more. It is truly rare for someone so young to possess such cultivation, but unfortunately, you show too much of your edge and do not know how to keep a low profile! This Old Master is willing to act as a mediator and accompany you to Wind and Cloud Pavilion to apologize to the old Pavilion Master and resolve this grievance. What do you think?"

As soon as the old man finished speaking, the woman chuckled. "If you weren't secretly casting a technique on me, I might really have thought you were a good person. Unfortunately, your methods are far too crude. Master was right after all. The people outside are all treacherous and cunning. They're truly detestable."

At this point, the woman suddenly raised a finger and slashed through the air. With a soft tearing sound, as though something had been cut apart, a burst of light exploded in midair. The wine-red-nosed old man shuddered, and his face instantly turned deathly pale. His Secret Technique had clearly been broken, causing him to suffer a backlash and sustain an internal injury.

He was both shocked and horrified. He had never expected this delicate little girl to break his Secret Technique with a casual gesture. He instantly gained a clear understanding of her strength and became incomparably wary of her.

At the same time, a figure suddenly materialized eerily three zhang away from the woman.

No one knew when this person had stealthily concealed himself beside the woman, as he had not revealed the slightest trace of his presence before now. Taking advantage of the moment when the woman broke the wine-red-nosed old man's Secret Technique, he suddenly launched an attack. This was precisely when her guard was at its lowest, and his timing was flawless. His Emperor Yuan surged as he raised a hand, sending a net-shaped Secret Treasure toward the woman. The great net flashed with five-colored light and gleamed brilliantly. At a glance, it was clearly a fine Imperial Treasure, and one specifically designed to capture people.

As the great net spread open, it seemed capable of enveloping a whole region of the world, sealing off the space around the woman completely.

The attacker laughed loudly. "Haha! Let's see where you can run now!"

Although the woman possessed considerable strength, she clearly had little combat experience. When this sudden change occurred, her brow furrowed and she drifted backward.

Unexpectedly, the great net clung to her like a maggot to bone. No matter what she did, she could not shake it off, and its opening rapidly contracted, intent on enveloping her.

If this Imperial Treasure truly caught her, then no matter how strong she was, she could forget about escaping. After all, quite a few Emperor Realm Masters had gathered here. Once they swarmed her together, they would certainly be able to capture her.

The instant the woman turned around, Yang Kai's expression went blank. He stared fixedly at that face, which seemed both familiar and strange, unable to connect her with the person he knew no matter how hard he tried.

Was it really her? Even Yang Kai could no longer be certain.

Just then, the Emperor Realm Master who had attacked formed several hand seals and shouted, "Close!"

The great net abruptly contracted and completely enveloped the woman. A trace of panic appeared on her face. She wanted to escape through the opening, but the surrounding space had already been sealed. A resolute light flashed through her beautiful eyes. At this point, her only option was to use her trump card to break this Imperial Treasure.

Just then, however, a strange force suddenly wrapped around her. Inexplicably, when the great net closed, she discovered that she had somehow escaped.

The owner of the great net was clearly surprised as well. His face was filled with confusion, and he had no idea what had happened.

"Master said that if someone hits me once, I should hit them twice. Take two punches from me, and if you don't die, I'll let you go." After being ambushed just now, the woman was clearly somewhat angry. Her peerlessly beautiful face wore an angry expression, but a beauty was still a beauty; even when angry, she was exceedingly lovely.

As she spoke, her delicate figure flickered and she charged toward the man. A faint radiance blossomed around her pair of dainty fists, and while still more than ten zhang away, she brought them smashing down toward him.

The man was still wondering why his Imperial Treasure had failed. Hearing her words, he flew into a rage. "Little girl, such wild boasting! You truly look down on everyone. This Lord doesn't know who your Master is, but since he taught a disciple like you, your Master obviously isn't anyone formidable either. Forget two punches, even a hundred... Tss..."

Before he could finish speaking, he suddenly noticed that something was wrong.

When the woman opposite him punched, the entire world suddenly seemed to solidify. The man himself felt as though a ten-thousand-zhang mountain were pressing down on him. His chest tightened, his breathing grew difficult, and even the blood throughout his body stopped flowing.

Boom...

A muffled bang rang out, accompanied by the sound of snapping bones. The Emperor Realm cultivator was smashed down like a meteor, vomiting blood without pause as he fell, his face turning deathly pale in an instant.

Bang...

His impact blasted an enormous pit into the ground. Cracks spread outward from the pit in every direction, as though the Heavens were collapsing and the Earth was splitting apart.

A chorus of sharp gasps rang out. Everyone stared wide-eyed at the seemingly frail young woman, unable to imagine that such a plain and unremarkable strike could possess such divine might. The visual impact felt so unreal that they could not help wondering whether their eyes had deceived them.

Yang Kai was also dumbfounded and felt even more strongly that this young woman was not the person he knew. Although she possessed only a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, the strength she could display was terrifying beyond belief. If she truly were the person he knew, she should not have been capable of this.

"Second punch!" The young woman flew down and arrived above the pit. As she spoke, she threw another punch toward the ground below.

This time, everyone was mentally prepared and saw it even more clearly. It was still a simple, unadorned strike without the slightest trace of a Secret Technique, yet this single punch expanded the pit by more than tenfold. The earth shook, and the First-Stage Emperor Realm Master who had been smashed into the pit earlier let out only a muffled grunt before falling silent.

Numerous Divine Senses swept over the pit, and everyone's face filled with shock.

Dead!

That man was actually dead.

He had been beaten to death with two punches by a delicate, seemingly frail young woman!

At the bottom of the pit, the man's corpse was too ghastly to behold. It could almost be said that nothing remained of his body or bones.

"Who... Who exactly is your Master?" the wine-red-nosed old man asked in alarm. At this point, even the greatest fool could tell that something was amiss. The young woman's strength was astonishing, and she clearly was not someone an ordinary person could have trained. If the disciple was already this formidable, how could her Master possibly be weak? There was very likely some heaven-defying expert behind her. As this thought occurred to them and they looked at the young woman again, everyone seemed to see an enormous figure standing behind her, blotting out the sky and covering the earth.

For a time, everyone felt uneasy, realizing that they might have provoked an existence they should never have offended. Sure enough, there was a reason she dared to kill Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Young Pavilion Master.

Although Wind and Cloud Pavilion was quite formidable, several forces in the Western Territory could contend with it, to say nothing of the elusive Shadow Slaughter Palace that towered above them all.

"What does it matter to you who my Master is? You plotted against me just now, so I'm going to punch you twice as well!" After dealing with her previous opponent, the young woman turned her gaze toward the wine-red-nosed old man and raised her dainty fist.

The old man was horrified. Although his strength was not bad, he did not dare claim to be stronger than the man who had just died. That man had failed to withstand two of the young woman's punches, so he certainly could not either. The raised fist was fair with a rosy tint and undoubtedly beautiful, but in the old man's eyes, it was a deadly weapon that would cost him his old life.

Panicking, he shouted fiercely, "This little brat is formidable! Everyone, attack together!"

"What a pretty little bitch! This Lord will take good care of you!" With an angry roar, a burly figure pounced toward the young woman.

Everyone froze, thinking there really was someone unafraid of death. With the previous man's fate serving as a warning, they had all been preparing to withdraw. After all, they were not of one mind. If everyone could truly work together, they might have had a strong chance of capturing the young woman, but they all knew that each person harboured their own schemes. If they swarmed her together, they would only give her the opportunity to defeat them one by one, inevitably resulting in disastrous casualties.

Someone charging out at this moment undoubtedly changed the situation. Everyone looked closely, and when they clearly saw his appearance and cultivation, their hearts shook.

"Xiong Kaishan!"

"It's actually that bastard!"

"Good! With him leading the charge, we may actually be able to pull this off."

For a time, the Emperor Realm Masters who had been preparing to retreat all stayed behind and quietly watched.

Xiong Kaishan had already charged in front of the young woman. His Emperor Yuan surged, and he rapidly swung fists larger than serving bowls. Every punch possessed the might to shatter mountains and overturn seas, and in an instant, fist shadows filled the sky. His figure was burly, and his attacks followed a broad, forceful style, making him appear extraordinarily fierce.

Faced with this incomparably violent assault, the young woman pursed her red lips and silently countered each move as it came.

Amid a series of thunderous booms, the two exchanged several hundred punches in just a few breaths.

Xiong Kaishan suddenly pulled back and retreated, his face filled with shock while his arms trembled uncontrollably.

The crowd stared in astonishment. Xiong Kaishan's fists were actually reduced to a bloody mess, while the young woman remained completely unharmed. When her opponent retreated, she did not pursue him and merely glared at him with puffed cheeks.

"Such great strength?" Xiong Kaishan lowered his head and looked at his fists in disbelief, but the truth was right before his eyes, so he had no choice but to believe it.

He, a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master, had actually lost to a young woman in a contest of fists and feet, leaving Xiong Kaishan unable to save face. With a roar, he formed a hand seal, and the necklace of skulls that had been hanging around his neck suddenly broke apart. The bone-white skulls flew out and transformed into enormous skulls. Eerie ghost flames flickered in their eye sockets as they converged on the young woman from all sides.

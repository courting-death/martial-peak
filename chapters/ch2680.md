## Chapter 2680 — Courage

Since that unknown enemy had already counted him as a threat, their arrangements must be extremely thorough. However, no matter how well the enemy planned, they could never have anticipated the existence of Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei.

All Yang Kai needed to do was set up a Space Array, and he could bring those two Yao Kings over from the Northern Territory. When faced with absolute power, any scheme or plot would be nothing but an illusion.

And he truly had this ability. There was still some time before the auction began, so as long as Yang Kai set up a Space Array somewhere, he could quietly bring the two Yao Kings over without anyone noticing.

After thinking it over carefully, Yang Kai slowly shook his head. "No need."

For one, he didn’t have many Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade left and didn’t want to waste them. For another, if he really did bring the two Yao Kings over, it might alert the enemy. After all, the four of them had entered Flowing Shadow City together—if two more suddenly appeared, anyone would become suspicious.

He could, of course, hide Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei inside the Small Xuan Realm, but the fewer people who knew about the Xuan Realm Pearl, the better.

Seeing Yang Kai like this, Yingfei couldn’t persuade him any further and could only secretly resolve to protect him no matter what.

“Let’s go, come take a walk with me!” Yang Kai said, tossing a few Source Crystals onto the table to pay for their food and drink. With that, he and Yingfei left the inn together.

Before coming to Flowing Shadow City, he had vaguely felt that this auction was a trap, as if it had been deliberately set up to lure someone in.

And after arriving in Flowing Shadow City and meeting the Sect Master of Flowing Shadow Sword Sect, this suspicion only grew stronger.

Now, having suddenly received an invitation to the auction, he couldn’t help but wonder if this scheme had been aimed at him from the very beginning.

He couldn’t figure it out, but he wasn’t willing to act according to someone else’s arrangements.

Since the auction was being held in Flowing Shadow City, the items for sale would naturally appear here as well. He wanted to see if Ai Ou and the others were in the city. If they were, he could simply snatch them away.

As he wandered through the city, Yang Kai’s powerful Divine Sense swept out like a tide, extremely well concealed.

He was very familiar with the auras of Ai Ou and the others. If they were really in Flowing Shadow City, he would definitely notice.

But after half a day, Yang Kai could only sigh. The enemy really wasn’t that foolish. He hadn’t found anything at all.

With Yingfei, he headed back and entered the Yuelai Inn.

The inn’s attendant came up to him, stopped in front of Yang Kai, and said, "Sir, there’s a guest waiting for you."

"Who?" Yang Kai asked in surprise.

He didn’t really know anyone in Flowing Shadow City.

"It’s a woman," the attendant replied with a meaningful smile. "She’s waiting for you outside your room."

Yang Kai frowned and swept his Divine Sense over, immediately realizing who was waiting for him. He couldn’t help but mutter, "Why is it her?"

Yingfei had clearly noticed as well and whispered, "Young Master Yang, be careful. This woman is not to be trifled with."

"I know," Yang Kai nodded. If he hadn’t known her background, it would be one thing, but now that he did, he didn’t dare underestimate her.

Casually tossing the attendant some Source Crystals, Yang Kai strode upstairs.

Outside the room, a petite and delicate woman was pacing back and forth in front of an upper room, muttering to herself with a rather impatient expression. She seemed distracted, not even noticing when Yang Kai and Yingfei’s Divine Senses swept over her.

It wasn’t until footsteps sounded nearby that the woman suddenly stopped and looked up.

The moment she looked up, the first thing she saw was that infuriating face she longed to smash with a single punch.

"Qing’er, you came to see me?" Yang Kai smiled at Zhu Qing, walking toward her step by step.

Zhu Qing’s face darkened. She snapped, "What did you just call me?"

Outrageous! A mere human actually dared to call her by her given name!

Yingfei’s face darkened as well, secretly marveling at Yang Kai’s audacity. Even knowing she was a member of the Dragon Clan, he still dared to provoke her like this—he truly had nerves of steel. Quietly, Yingfei circulated his Yao Yuan, ready to act at a moment’s notice. He had no desire to make an enemy of the Dragon Clan, but right now, Yang Kai’s safety was his top priority.

"Why so cold? I still prefer how you used to be," Yang Kai said, stopping in front of her, his eyes sweeping over her ample chest with a grin.

The lecherous gleam in his eyes was almost tangible. Zhu Qing shivered, the feeling of being taken advantage of washing over her again. Ashamed and angry, she suddenly punched at Yang Kai’s face.

The moment she struck, she regretted it. The Elder had ordered her to get along with Yang Kai and invite him to the island as a guest. Wasn’t that the whole reason she’d come here—to make peace? How could she lose control so easily?

She immediately held back most of her strength.

Even so, her fist was caught by a large hand, which gently stroked it.

A strange sensation swept through her, making her whole body tingle. Zhu Qing’s face flushed red. "Let go!"

She shouldn’t have held back. That punch should have smashed his hateful face to pieces. As she spoke, she raised her other small fist, looking ready to go all out with Yang Kai.

"Alright, alright, I’ll let go. Don’t be so fierce." Yang Kai reluctantly gave her hand a few more squeezes before finally releasing it.

Zhu Qing immediately jumped back, putting distance between them. Her chest heaved as she shook the hand he’d touched, as if trying to shake off something dirty.

Yingfei was already sweating coldly on the side. When Zhu Qing attacked, he’d almost joined in, but then noticed she’d held back. That only confused him more.

What exactly is this Dragon Clan member up to?

Could it be she really fell for Young Master Yang at first sight? Otherwise, why would she hold back her strength in a fit of rage? But judging by her expression, she didn’t look like she was in love—more like she wanted to kill him.

Yingfei was completely baffled.

Yang Kai just grinned, his smile lewd and shameless. He took out his room token, opened the barrier, pushed open the door, and leaned against the frame. "If you have something to say, come in."

He didn’t know why Zhu Qing had come to see him, but since she was here, there must be a reason. Yang Kai wasn’t afraid she’d back out—she was a proud Dragon Clan member, after all.

Her beautiful eyes glared at Yang Kai, as if she wanted to cut him into a thousand pieces, but her feet wouldn’t move.

Yang Kai kept smiling, not rushing her.

That mocking smile was a challenge to Zhu Qing’s pride. Gritting her teeth, she slipped into the room.

"You’ve got guts!" Yang Kai grinned, thinking, Since you’re throwing yourself at me, don’t blame me for taking full advantage this time.

He turned to close the door.

Yingfei stepped forward, clearly intending to follow them in. He stared wide-eyed at Yang Kai and called out, "Young Master Yang, I—"

"Get... out!" Yang Kai jerked his chin, and the door slammed shut, nearly hitting Yingfei in the nose.

"Young Master Yang, you..." Yingfei was anxious, calling out, "Lust leads to disaster, Young Master Yang, don’t fall for a beauty’s trap!"

There was no response from inside. Yingfei was as anxious as an ant on a hot pan. Du Xian poked his head out from the next room and asked, "What’s going on?"

"Nothing!" Yingfei snapped, feeling irritable but not daring to barge in.

But then he thought about it—last time, Yang Kai had managed to subdue Zhu Qing, so there probably wasn’t any real danger this time either. Besides, from beginning to end, Zhu Qing hadn’t shown any murderous intent; if anything, he was just being overly cautious.

Inside the room, Yang Kai immediately wrapped his arms around Zhu Qing’s waist, resting his head on her fragrant shoulder, breathing in her intoxicating scent, and gazing at her fair neck. He whispered, "Qing’er, you missed me so soon?"

Zhu Qing froze. She hadn’t expected Yang Kai to be so bold, touching her as soon as he got the chance. By the time she reacted, it was too late. Furious, she shouted, "Take your filthy hands off me!"

Yang Kai ignored her and licked Zhu Qing’s earlobe.

The delicate body in his arms shuddered, and her head snapped back, hair flying.

With a bang, Yang Kai saw stars as Zhu Qing’s head smashed into his face, sending two streams of golden blood from his nostrils.

Taking advantage of the moment, Zhu Qing jumped away, glaring at him from the corner, gritting her teeth. "Show some respect!"

Yang Kai wiped his face, the pain helping to calm his excitement. He glanced at Zhu Qing and sneered, "I never thought a dignified Dragon Clan member would actually practice seduction techniques!"

Zhu Qing frowned. "What nonsense are you spouting? When did I ever practice seduction techniques?"

Yang Kai snorted, "If you haven’t, then why can’t I control myself whenever I see you?"

Zhu Qing huffed, aggrieved, "You were born a lecher—how is that my fault?" It was like blaming the dung for being too fragrant when a dog eats it. No, wait, how could she think such a thing? Wasn’t that just belittling herself?

"Bullshit!" Yang Kai retorted. "I may not be a gentleman, but I can keep my composure. When have I ever been lecherous?"

Zhu Qing sneered, "With your shameless and wanton behavior, what else could you be?"

Yang Kai glared at her for a while before waving his hand. "Forget it, a real man doesn’t argue with women."

He took a deep breath, circulated his cultivation to suppress his restless urges, and looked up. "So, what did you come to see me for? Don’t tell me you’ve really fallen for me. I’m a responsible man, but if you’re unwilling, I won’t force you."

Zhu Qing sneered, "You always think so highly of yourself?"

Yang Kai replied offhandedly, “I wonder who it was that clung to me so tightly. Honestly, I still have marks on my back. As expected of the Dragon Clan—such impressive strength…”

"Shut up!" Zhu Qing stomped her foot in shame and anger, nearly collapsing the inn with her brute force.

What happened before was the greatest humiliation of her life, and Yang Kai just had to bring it up again.

Taking a deep breath, Zhu Qing silently reminded herself not to get angry, that the clan’s affairs were more important, and her own sacrifice was just a small matter...

After a while, she finally calmed down and looked at Yang Kai. "You know I’m from the Dragon Clan?"

Yang Kai smiled at her, but inside he was in turmoil. When he’d talked with Yingfei earlier, he’d only guessed that Zhu Qing was a Dragon Clan member, but it had just been speculation.

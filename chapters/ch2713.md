## Chapter 2713 — A Better Place

Ye Hen straightened his expression and said solemnly, “Sir Yang, you once saved my life, and your kindness to my Thousand Leaves Sect is as weighty as a mountain. If you have anything to say, please speak freely—I am all ears.”

Yang Kai smiled and said, "Emperor Heaven Valley is indeed a good place. It's safe here, and the Spiritual Qi is quite decent, making it very suitable for cultivation. However, as good as Emperor Heaven Valley is, it's ultimately too small and inconvenient to enter or leave. Sect Master Ye, you have the ambition to reach the Emperor Realm and a resolute heart. Spending three to five years here is nothing for you, but what about your disciples?"

Those five hundred disciples have varying strengths. If they were really trapped here for three to five years, they would likely grow restless and anxious, which could give rise to inner demons.

Ye Hen sighed, "How could I not know this? But where can we go now?"

Yang Kai said, "What if I could offer Sect Master Ye a better place?"

"A better place?" Ye Hen was stunned.

Yang Kai spread his arms and said, "That place is a thousand times larger than here, and the Spiritual Qi is far richer. Forget five hundred people—even fifty thousand could enter without feeling crowded. There are countless empty spirit peaks, all unclaimed. If Sect Master Ye goes there, you might not need three to five years; perhaps just one or two years would be enough to break through to Emperor."

Ye Hen's eyes lit up as he listened. With Yang Kai's description, it was as if a grand and magnificent landscape slowly unfolded before his eyes.

But soon, Ye Hen gave a bitter laugh and said, “If such a wondrous land truly existed, it would have long since been claimed by the great sects. How could it ever fall to the Thousand Leaves Sect’s turn?”

Yang Kai chuckled, "Sect Master Ye is right. That treasure land was indeed occupied by a great sect before, but now, that sect has been wiped out."

"Oh?" Ye Hen was shocked. "May I ask, Sir Yang, where exactly is this place?"

"Northern Territory, Wenqing Sect!"

Ye Hen frowned in thought, but soon his expression changed dramatically as he exclaimed, "Wenqing Sect!"

Judging by his reaction, he clearly knew the name Wenqing Sect. After all, Wenqing Sect was one of the top sects in the Northern Territory. There were even Third Stage Emperor experts in the sect. In terms of status, it was on par with the great sects of the Southern Territory, like Qingyang Shrine and Tianwu Sacred Land. It was simply not something the Thousand Leaves Sect could compare to.

"That's right!"

"Wenqing Sect was wiped out. Who did it?" Ye Hen was horrified. Such a colossal force had actually been exterminated—he could hardly imagine it. By comparison, although the Thousand Leaves Sect had also suffered a calamity, at least five hundred disciples and Emperor Heaven Valley remained. But from Yang Kai's tone, it seemed Wenqing Sect had been utterly destroyed; otherwise, why would even the sect's foundation be abandoned?

As soon as he spoke, Ye Hen seemed to recall something and said in shock, "Could it be you, Sir Yang...?"

Although Wenqing Sect had Third Stage Emperor experts sitting in command, Yang Kai was someone who could even kill Star God Palace Elders, and he had Yao Kings at his side. Destroying Wenqing Sect... it didn't seem impossible.

This thought startled him. He never imagined that the gentle and amiable young man before him could be so ruthless, wiping out an entire sect at a whim.

Yang Kai grinned, neither confirming nor denying it, and said, "I established Lingxiao Palace on the former site of Wenqing Sect. That place is now mine. If Sect Master Ye is interested, you can temporarily settle there. There's no need to worry about safety—I've invited a grand array master to rebuild the Grand Guardian Array. It's as solid as a fortress."

Ye Hen's emotions surged as he listened, filled with admiration.

Yang Kai was still so young, yet he had already taken over the site of a top sect in the Northern Territory and founded his own sect.

In contrast, he himself had failed to protect the legacy passed down from his ancestors. The comparison made him feel utterly ashamed.

Yang Kai said, "Although I established Lingxiao Palace, there are very few people in the sect. The treasure land is basically empty. Since it's just sitting there, if Sect Master Ye thinks it's suitable, you can bring your disciples there."

Ye Hen asked curiously, "May I ask, Sir Yang, how many people does your sect have now?"

Yang Kai gave a dry laugh and held up three fingers.

"Three thousand? That's not many," Ye Hen said, suddenly understanding. For such a vast foundation as Wenqing Sect's, three thousand people would hardly fill it. There must be many empty spirit peaks. Even the Thousand Leaves Sect once had three thousand disciples.

"Not three thousand, not three thousand!" Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead.

"Three hundred?" Ye Hen was surprised, but after thinking about it, it made sense. Even if Yang Kai had really established Lingxiao Palace, it would be impossible to recruit so many disciples in such a short time. A new sect must be selective in accepting people. Even if there were only three hundred, they would surely be the elite of the elite.

Yang Kai's face darkened as he said with difficulty, "Not even three hundred."

"Uh..." Ye Hen was stunned and hesitated, "Could it be..."

"For now, there are only three people," Yang Kai laughed heartily.

Ye Hen could hardly believe his ears. Such a vast foundation, yet only three disciples? How blessed those three must be! They could absorb the rich Spiritual Qi of the mountains and rivers at will and freely enter all the cultivation grounds. Their cultivation would surely soar.

"To be precise, they're just Honored Elders. There aren't even any disciples yet."

"Three Honored Elders? What are their cultivations?" Ye Hen asked in surprise.

Yang Kai glanced to the side and pointed, "Yao King Yingfei is one, and the other two... are just like him. But I had them stay behind to watch the place this time, so they didn't come with me!"

"Hiss..." Ye Hen drew a sharp breath and hesitated, "Are the other two also Yao Kings?"

"Yes," Yang Kai nodded.

Ye Hen felt as if he had been struck by a giant hammer, completely dazed.

He had thought that having a Yao King-level expert at Yang Kai's side was already a stroke of fortune, but he never expected there to be three Yao Kings! A sect that had just been established, without even a single disciple, already had three Yao Kings as Honored Elders. What a profound foundation! Even the top sects couldn't compare.

It was easy to imagine that, given time, Lingxiao Palace would surely become the number one sect in the entire Star Realm, second only to the Great Emperor sects.

No wonder Yang Kai said there was no need to worry about safety. With the Grand Guardian Array and two Yao Kings guarding the place, who would dare provoke them?

It had to be said, Ye Hen was truly tempted. Compared to Emperor Heaven Valley, Lingxiao Palace was undoubtedly more suitable for them. The superior cultivation environment alone was far beyond what Emperor Heaven Valley could offer.

As good as Emperor Heaven Valley was, it was ultimately too small. With five hundred disciples cultivating here, efficiency would surely be low, and he still needed to break through to the Emperor Realm...

But as a sect master, Ye Hen had many things to consider. So, even though he was tempted, he didn't immediately agree.

After a long hesitation, Ye Hen finally sighed and said, "The Thousand Leaves Sect has been passed down for tens of thousands of years, but in my hands, it has been suppressed to this point. I do not wish to become a sinner for the ages, nor can I let the Thousand Leaves Sect's legacy end with me. I..."

Yang Kai smiled and said, "I understand what Sect Master Ye is worried about. Letting you go to Lingxiao Palace isn't about absorbing your sect. Rest assured, the Thousand Leaves Sect will remain the Thousand Leaves Sect. Once you're there, you can focus on recuperating. When you feel ready to rebuild the Thousand Leaves Sect, I will never stand in your way."

Ye Hen's hesitation was probably because he feared Yang Kai wanted to swallow up the Thousand Leaves Sect. Although Yang Kai had done them a great favor, if they were truly absorbed, the Thousand Leaves Sect would be finished, never to rise again.

As sect master, Ye Hen saw it as his duty to revive the Thousand Leaves Sect. How could he accept such an outcome?

"This..." Ye Hen was taken aback, then said with shame, "I was being petty."

Thinking about it, if Yang Kai really wanted to absorb the Thousand Leaves Sect, he could have just used force. Whether Ye Hen agreed or not, he would have had no choice. There was no need for so many words.

"Sect Master Ye has his own concerns. I understand," Yang Kai said with a gentle smile.

"Since Sir Yang is so generous, I and the Thousand Leaves Sect will gratefully accept. We will remember this great kindness forever." Ye Hen bowed deeply and said, "But... the Northern Territory is far away. How should we get there?"

"I have my own arrangements."

With everything settled, Yang Kai immediately began setting up a Space Array in the center of the valley.

Moving the people of the Thousand Leaves Sect to Lingxiao Palace was partly to fill the empty place with some life, and partly because Yang Kai was interested in their puppet techniques. As the saying goes, one who eats another's food must speak for them. With so many people from the Thousand Leaves Sect at Lingxiao Palace, over time, their puppet arts would become part of Lingxiao Palace as well. When the people from Dark Star arrived in the future, they would have another means of self-defense. The people from Dark Star weren't very strong, so puppet techniques would suit them well.

Thirdly, Yang Kai wanted to use Emperor Heaven Valley as a transfer station for the Space Array.

Emperor Heaven Valley was already a small, independent world. After the outside Space Array was destroyed, only Yang Kai could open it. Placing a cross-territory Space Array inside Emperor Heaven Valley was the safest option—there was no need to worry about it being destroyed.

Yang Kai still had some Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade left, just enough to set up a cross-territory Space Array.

After a busy half day, a brand new array stood tall in the valley.

Zhu Qing had been watching from the side, shocked by what she saw. Only now did she realize how accomplished Yang Kai was in the Dao of Space.

Ye Hen also took the opportunity to inform his five hundred disciples of his decision. Naturally, no one objected. In fact, many were excited to hear they would be going to the Northern Territory. For them, it would have been impossible to reach the Northern Territory on their own. Now, hearing they would travel billions of li away, they were full of curiosity, wondering what the Northern Territory was like.

Once everything was ready, Yang Kai had Yingfei go ahead as the vanguard, then arranged for the five hundred disciples of the Thousand Leaves Sect to take turns stepping onto the Space Array.

Each activation of the array consumed a huge amount of Source Crystals, and at most, only twenty or thirty people could be sent at a time.

Just the teleportation alone cost Yang Kai nearly one hundred thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals, making Ye Hen's heart ache to watch.

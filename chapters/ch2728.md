## Chapter 2728 — Dragon’s Lair, Tiger’s Den

In the guest hall of Lingxiao Palace, host and guests took their seats.

Ji Ying’s face was a bit unsightly, all because Yang Kai had taken the seat of honor, leaving him in the secondary position.

This had never happened before. Wherever he traveled in the Northern Territory, no matter which Sect he visited, he was always treated as the most distinguished guest, and the seat of honor was always reserved for him. Even a few days ago at Li Dragon Palace, it had been the same.

Normally, Ji Ying wouldn’t have cared about such things. After all, a guest should follow the host’s arrangements. No matter how noble his status, he was still an outsider, and the difference between the seats was just a matter of position, nothing worth fussing over.

But with his preconceived notions about Lingxiao Palace and Yang Kai, he now felt even more that Yang Kai didn’t respect him at all.

Yang Kai, on the other hand, hadn’t considered any of this. As the host, it was only natural for him to sit at the head and entertain his guests.

Glancing around below, he chuckled, “It’s quite the coincidence that Master Ji could come together with the two Sect Masters.”

Ji Ying replied in a deep voice, “It’s no coincidence. I was only able to come thanks to Palace Master Li and Sect Master Mi escorting me along the way.”

Yang Kai asked in surprise, “Master Ji, you are highly respected, and Medicine Pill Valley’s reputation is renowned throughout the Northern Territory. Could there really be anyone foolish enough to cause you trouble? I’ve heard the Northern Territory is quite peaceful.”

Li Jiao snorted coldly, “It used to be peaceful, but lately... things haven’t been so great.”

There was a hidden meaning in his words, and Yang Kai could hardly miss it. He was clearly implying that the rise of Lingxiao Palace had disturbed the peace of the Northern Territory.

Not bothering to respond, Yang Kai turned to look at Li Xuan behind Ji Ying and said with a smile, “Young Brother, we meet again so soon.”

Li Xuan’s face turned pale as he cried out, “You... what are you trying to do? I’m warning you, don’t try anything!”

He was half pretending, half genuinely frightened. After suffering a huge loss at Yang Kai’s hands last time, he still hadn’t recovered from the shock.

Yang Kai sneered, “Young Brother, you really know how to joke. It seems I didn’t treat you well enough last time, leaving you with complaints. If you have time, why not stay as a guest for a while? I guarantee you’ll feel right at home!”

Li Xuan retorted angrily, “Like hell I want to be your guest.”

With his master present, his courage had grown, and he no longer needed to grovel as he had last time.

Zhu Qing and Hua Qingsi came forward with tea, serving each person in turn.

Hua Qingsi was fine, but Zhu Qing slammed her teacup down on the table, splashing tea everywhere. Yang Kai was furious and slammed the table, shouting, “What are you doing? Have you no manners? I’m talking to you! Get back here!”

But Zhu Qing was already gone. After serving the tea, she had immediately left.

After shouting for a while, Yang Kai could only sigh helplessly, “Master Ji, please don’t mind her. That girl is a bit wild. I’ll be sure to teach her a lesson.”

Ji Ying replied blandly, “It’s a trivial matter, no need to worry.”

Yang Kai smiled slightly, “Master Ji is truly magnanimous. May I ask what brings you to my Lingxiao Palace this time?”

Mi Qi slammed the table and sneered, “Palace Master Yang, why pretend you don’t know? Don’t you remember what you did a few days ago?”

Yang Kai glanced at him and said, “I’m not sure what I did to make Sect Master Mi so agitated. This should be our first meeting, and I’ve never had any dealings with Mitian Sect.”

Mi Qi snorted, “This Lord and Palace Master Li came today only to seek justice for this Junior Nephew. Palace Master Yang, you should know that those who do evil will bring disaster upon themselves. I advise you to watch yourself, or you’ll bring ruin to your Lingxiao Palace.”

Li Jiao nodded in agreement, “Mi Brother is right.”

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes, “So, the two of you are here to make things difficult for my Lingxiao Palace? I’d like to know what offense we’ve given you.”

Mi Qi said, “It’s not me or Li Brother you’ve offended, but Medicine Pill Valley! Medicine Pill Valley is the Sacred Land of the Northern Territory. To offend them is to offend the entire Northern Territory. As people of the Northern Territory, we can’t just stand by!”

Yang Kai asked in surprise, “When did I offend Medicine Pill Valley?”

Ji Ying’s face darkened, “My useless disciple is still injured, and Palace Master Yang dares to feign ignorance?”

Yang Kai turned to look at him, then glanced at Li Xuan’s swollen cheek. As if just realizing, he said, “So that’s what this is about.”

Ji Ying stared at him expressionlessly, “Palace Master Yang, don’t tell me my disciple wasn’t injured by you!”

“That’s right, I did hit him,” Yang Kai admitted with a nod.

Ji Ying flew into a rage, grinding his teeth, “Palace Master Yang, you’re an Emperor Realm cultivator. Why would you bully someone weaker and insult my disciple? Do you really think nothing of Medicine Pill Valley?”

“Yang Kai, you’ve gone too far!”

“Master Ji, this matter cannot be let go. If word gets out, what face will Medicine Pill Valley have left?”

Li Jiao and Mi Qi both shouted angrily, as if they themselves had been beaten instead of Li Xuan, eager to push Ji Ying to teach Yang Kai a lesson.

“What do you have to say for yourself?” Ji Ying asked, staring coldly at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai raised an eyebrow, “If a child does something wrong, shouldn’t he be disciplined?”

“Did something wrong!” Ji Ying’s face flashed with anger. “My disciple has always been well-behaved. What could he possibly have done to deserve such a beating from you?”

Yang Kai glanced at Li Xuan with a smile, then said thoughtfully, “I’d like to know what he told you.”

Ji Ying snorted and ordered, “Xuan’er, tell me in detail what happened that day! Don’t be afraid, your master will stand up for you.”

“Uh...” Li Xuan hesitated. After all, everything he’d told Ji Ying before was made up, and now that Yang Kai was here, he didn’t dare to confront him directly.

But if he didn’t, he’d look too guilty, and his lies would be exposed. With his master’s temper, he’d never be forgiven. But if his master was willing to believe him, then it didn’t matter what he said.

With that thought, a trace of malice flashed in Li Xuan’s eyes as he made up his mind.

“Here’s what happened...”

Although young, Li Xuan was quite articulate. According to his account, he had come to Lingxiao Palace on Ji Ying’s orders to buy medicine, but was stopped at the mountain gate. He waited for days before anyone came to ask his purpose, and after learning why he was there, they beat him up, injured him, and even robbed him of the Source Crystals he’d brought to buy Spiritual Medicine.

As he spoke, it was as if he was reliving that day. Li Xuan’s face turned pale, his body trembled, and he kept wiping away tears, making everyone who heard him feel sorry for him. By the end, his words grew clearer and louder, his back straightened, as if he had truly suffered all that.

Ji Ying’s face grew darker and darker, while Mi Qi and Li Jiao were secretly delighted. The more miserable Li Xuan’s story, the more trouble Lingxiao Palace and Yang Kai would be in.

“Look at what you’ve done!”

As soon as Li Xuan finished, Mi Qi jumped up and pointed at Yang Kai, shouting, “Bullying the weak! It’s bad enough for an Emperor Realm cultivator to pick on a Dao Origin Realm, but to rob him as well? You’ve disgraced all Northern Territory cultivators!”

Yang Kai ignored him, instead smiling at Li Xuan, “Are you sure you have nothing to add?”

Li Xuan shuddered and quickly hid behind Ji Ying.

“Palace Master Yang, are you threatening my disciple?” Ji Ying glared coldly at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai shrugged, “Not a threat, just a question.”

“So you admit it, then?”

Yang Kai frowned, staring at Ji Ying, “Does Master Ji really believe his disciple’s story?”

Ji Ying snorted, “He’s been my disciple for ten years. I know his character well—he would never make up such things.”

Yang Kai spread his hands, “If you say so, then nothing I say will matter.”

Ji Ying snorted, “Do you have anything else to say?”

“No, no... I just think Master Ji’s eye for choosing disciples isn’t very good.”

Ji Ying narrowed his eyes, “If you have anything to defend yourself with, I’ll listen. But if not... then Lingxiao Palace has no reason to exist, and you’ll come with me to Medicine Pill Valley to answer for your crimes!”

With a few words, he decided the fate of a top Sect. Only a personal disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan would have such authority.

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes, “And what crime have I committed?”

Ji Ying replied, “You show no respect for my master, Great Emperor Miaodan, and you scorn all alchemists. That is your greatest crime!”

Yang Kai shouted, “If all of Great Emperor Miaodan’s disciples are as stubborn and rotten as you, and as cunning and deceitful as Li Xuan, then I truly don’t respect him. And what of Medicine Pill Valley? What kind of Northern Territory Sacred Land is that? Utter nonsense!”

Ji Ying’s face changed dramatically, “Insolence!”

Yang Kai looked at him disdainfully. “So what if I’m being insolent? Come and bite me.”

“You...” Ji Ying pointed at Yang Kai, wishing he could really bite him. But though he was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, he was just an alchemist, with little experience in fighting. He couldn’t really risk himself.

His gaze swept to the side. Mi Qi and Li Jiao understood instantly, and both surged their Emperor Yuan, ready to attack.

Suddenly, three terrifying waves of Yao Qi erupted. Three figures shot in from outside the hall, standing in front of Mi Qi and Li Jiao.

“Want to fight? Just try it!” Xi Lei cracked his knuckles, glaring menacingly ahead.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao were both startled, freezing in place, nerves taut.

They knew there were powerful yao experts in Lingxiao Palace, and that Wenqing Sect had been destroyed because of yao interference, but they never expected there would be three Yao Kings!

Two against three, they stood no chance. Not to mention that woman named Qing’er in Lingxiao Palace.

That woman had sent Mi Qi flying with a single punch. Her strength was likely even greater than these three Yao Kings.

A dragon’s lair, a tiger’s den!

How did Lingxiao Palace have so many experts? With such a foundation, they were almost on par with a Great Emperor’s Sect—only lacking a Great Emperor himself.

Ji Ying also felt a chill run through him. He hadn’t faced such danger in years. The overwhelming Yao Qi made it hard to breathe, but as a disciple of a Great Emperor, his mind and cultivation were strong. Though his expression was grave, he didn’t show any panic.

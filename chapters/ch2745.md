## Chapter 2745 — Qing Jade Command Token

Facing this thunderous strike, Bian Yuqing did not dodge or evade, but met it head-on. Driven by her will, the countless bracelet shadows all smashed toward the middle-aged man.

One after another, the bracelet shadows struck down, but the middle-aged man was completely unharmed. Instead, the shadows shattered—after all, they were only illusions and had no real killing power.

The distance between the two suddenly closed, and they were about to clash directly.

The middle-aged man's expression brightened, and he thrust his longsword forward.

With a sharp sound, Bian Yuqing's body trembled as the longsword pierced straight through her chest.

Enduring the piercing pain, Bian Yuqing's face twisted fiercely as she shouted, "Die!"

As her words fell, a bracelet smashed onto the middle-aged man's head. With a splattering sound, like a watermelon bursting open, red and white sprayed into the air.

The headless corpse swayed twice, then suddenly fell from midair.

Bian Yuqing also staggered, clutching the wound in her chest, and after a few shaky steps, blood gushed from her mouth.

A gentle breeze stirred as Yang Kai suddenly appeared at her side, supporting her with one arm. Frowning, he said, "You could have worn him down—why take such a risk?"

In that situation, Bian Yuqing absolutely held the advantage, and with Yang Kai nearby to assist, she could have killed her opponent with minimal cost, perhaps even unscathed. Yet, to Yang Kai’s surprise, she chose a path where both sides suffered, risking everything for a swift victory.

The middle-aged man was killed, but her chest was also pierced by a sword. The injury was perilous; a slight misstep and she would have died first.

Bian Yuqing wiped her mouth and gave a self-deprecating smile. "It's not just you men who have guts!"

Yang Kai was silent.

Bian Yuqing probably felt guilty toward Kou Wu, so she would rather risk injury to quickly slay her opponent. That was true courage.

"Do you feel better now?" Yang Kai asked.

Bian Yuqing shook her head. She felt no satisfaction from revenge, only deep loneliness.

Biyu Sect was gone. She had always relied on Kou Wu, but now even Kou Wu was dead. She was left all alone, suddenly lost about the future.

"Healing comes first," Yang Kai said, glancing at the sword that had pierced her chest, his brows furrowing slightly.

If she hadn't shifted her body at the critical moment, that sword would have truly taken her life. It wasn't that Yang Kai couldn't save her, but he knew the sword wouldn't kill her, so he stood by.

If this injury wasn't treated in time, it might leave hidden dangers.

Bian Yuqing shook her head again. "First, let's gather Kou Wu's remains. There are many wild beasts outside."

Yang Kai sighed. "Where?"

"Over there!" Bian Yuqing pointed in a direction.

Yang Kai wrapped his arm around her, ready to move, when Bian Yuqing suddenly said, "Their rings."

Yang Kai beckoned, and several streaks of light flew up from below. Three Spatial Rings, along with the other two's Secret Treasures, were brought over.

The possessions of these Dao Origin Realm cultivators meant little to Yang Kai, but to Bian Yuqing, they were a fortune not to be missed.

He casually tossed them to Bian Yuqing, then set off.

In no time, they arrived. On the ground lay a corpse, face up—it was none other than Kou Wu. Just as the middle-aged man had said, Kou Wu died swiftly. A fatal sword to the chest; he likely felt no pain.

Bian Yuqing's eyes reddened. She sniffed, holding back bitter tears, left Yang Kai's embrace, and flew down.

Landing softly on the ground, Bian Yuqing stood in silence for a moment. She murmured, “Following me never brought you wealth or glory. In your next life, may you find a better place.”

Tears finally slid down her cheeks. She bent over, carefully gathered Kou Wu's corpse, and placed it in her Spatial Ring.

Yang Kai watched quietly, saying nothing.

When Bian Yuqing finished, she flew up. He then led her toward a nearby mountain peak.

Sweeping with Divine Sense, he found a cave and entered. Yang Kai took out several Spiritual Pills and said, "Open your mouth!"

Bian Yuqing obediently opened her mouth, and Yang Kai flicked the pills into it.

Before she could react, Yang Kai suddenly grabbed the sword hilt in her chest and, with lightning speed, pulled it out.

Bian Yuqing's body stiffened, and the next moment she let out a heart-wrenching scream.

Yang Kai pressed his fingers like a sword, tapping her body several times to stop the bleeding. He stood and said, "Refine the medicine yourself. You'll be fine."

He turned and left the cave.

Those healing Spiritual Pills were personally refined by him, and one was even Imperial-Grade. Bian Yuqing's injury wasn't severe, and with her Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation and Yang Kai's pills, she would recover soon.

Half a day later, Yang Kai stood at the cave entrance with his hands behind his back. Hearing footsteps, he looked up to see Bian Yuqing emerge in clean clothes.

Their eyes met. Bian Yuqing's gaze was slightly complicated as she softly said, "Thank you."

The boy she once looked down on had grown to a level she now had to look up to. This change left Bian Yuqing at a loss, making her more reserved than before.

"It's nothing," Yang Kai shook his head. "Maybe I bear some responsibility for how things turned out."

Bian Yuqing asked, puzzled, "What does it have to do with you?"

Yang Kai looked at her intently. "Wu Mengchuan was killed by me."

"What?" Bian Yuqing trembled, almost unable to believe her ears. But she knew, given Yang Kai's current status and strength, he had no reason to lie to her.

Since he said so, Wu Mengchuan really died by his hand.

Biyu Sect's decline was closely tied to Wu Mengchuan's disappearance. The Sect Master was gone, and the original Protectors were killed by Wu Mengchuan, leaving only Bian Yuqing. She couldn't hold the sect together, so it soon disbanded and the disciples scattered.

Others didn't know why Wu Mengchuan disappeared, but Bian Yuqing knew it happened after he went to the Shattered Star Sea. The Star Seal for that journey was obtained by her and Kou Wu from Four Seasons Land.

But Wu Mengchuan died at Yang Kai's hands. He was a descendant of the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor, and as far as Bian Yuqing knew, Wu Mengchuan possessed an Imperial Treasure passed down from the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor.

When Yang Kai entered the Shattered Star Sea, he was also Dao Origin Realm, yet he managed to kill Wu Mengchuan.

Yang Kai observed her expression and smiled faintly. "It seems you have no intention of avenging him."

After Yang Kai revealed he killed Wu Mengchuan, Bian Yuqing was shocked but showed no other emotion.

Bian Yuqing let out a cold laugh. "Why would I avenge him? If he hadn’t needed someone to manage Biyu Sect after escaping, he would’ve killed me long ago. You killed him—I have no objections."

"No objections is good," Yang Kai nodded. "By the way, when those three chased you, they mentioned a command token. What is it?"

Bian Yuqing paused, then took a palm-sized green jade pendant from her Spatial Ring and handed it to Yang Kai. "It's this."

Yang Kai took it and saw it was carved from green jade, nothing special.

Qing jade was a type of jade mineral—not particularly rare, found in many places. It was sometimes used to forge low-grade Secret Treasures, but nothing remarkable. Why, then, would such a token carved from it spark a deadly struggle among Dao Origin Realm cultivators?

Turning it over, he saw several numbers carved on the back: 3912!

"What's it for?" Yang Kai asked, puzzled.

"It's a Linghu Palace command token. With it, you can cultivate in Linghu Palace's secret area," Bian Yuqing explained. "You've been away for years, so you don't know how lively Linghu Palace is now. The three tiers of cultivation secret areas—Heaven, Earth, and Human—draw countless people every year. I spent all my assets to exchange for this token, but I still have to wait two months before I can enter."

Yang Kai's face darkened in surprise. "Two months? There are so many people in line?"

Bian Yuqing said, "Do you know how long I've had this token?"

"How long?"

"Half a year," Bian Yuqing replied. "I got it six months ago, but had to wait eight months to enter. Others wait a year or two—countless people."

"Goodness!" Yang Kai exclaimed. "Linghu Palace is that popular?"

Bian Yuqing laughed. "Linghu Palace's Spiritual Qi is dense. Even the lowest Human-grade secret area is far better than what small sects can offer. People like us want breakthroughs, so we flock to Linghu Palace. Without connections, you can only wait in line."

Yang Kai asked, "Is that why those three wanted your token?"

Bian Yuqing replied, "Of course. Don't underestimate its value. Linghu Palace only recognizes the token, not the person. Whoever holds it can enter the secret area in two months. I spent all my Source Crystals to get it, but if I sold it on the black market, I'd double my money. Top-ranked tokens often appear at auctions."

"Because of the timing?" Yang Kai raised his brows.

"Yes, the sooner the entry, the more valuable the token. The black market in Fenglin City is crazy for these—so many people want one."

Yang Kai nodded. The token's value wasn't just in itself, but in the chance to enter the secret area in two months. Bian Yuqing must have accidentally exposed it, which led to the Duanyue Sect trio hunting her.

After years away, he hadn't expected such changes here.

"What tier secret area can your token access?" Yang Kai asked.

"White jade tokens grant access to Human-grade, green jade to Earth-grade, and the top red jade tokens to Heaven-grade!" Bian Yuqing explained. "Heaven-grade isn't for people like me."

Even many Emperor Realm masters covet entry to that place. With her resources, there was no way she could compete.

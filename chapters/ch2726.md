## Chapter 2726 — Lingxiao Palace, a Festering Tumor

"If you want to be boyfriend and girlfriend, I don't mind. I'm just worried you..." Yang Kai took Zhu Qing's soft hand and gently rubbed it. "...might feel forced."

Tsk tsk, this little hand is so smooth and white, touching it feels like the finest silk. It's hard to let go.

Zhu Qing frowned, glancing at Yang Kai's bold actions. She twisted away, pulled her hand back, and turned her head. "Don't be like that, it's weird."

Yang Kai put on a serious face. "Weren't you the one who said you wanted to be a couple?"

Zhu Qing asked curiously, "Is this what couples are supposed to do?"

"Of course." Yang Kai snorted hot air from his nose. "That's just the basics."

Zhu Qing hesitated, her expression uncertain. "What else do couples do?"

Yang Kai stroked his chin, grinning slyly. "A lot, a whole lot..."

"Tell me, let me see... if I can manage." Zhu Qing's pink neck flushed, as if she realized something.

The sight made Yang Kai's heart race. He quickly took a deep breath to calm his surging Qi and blood, then lay down on Zhu Qing's firm, rounded thigh, resting his head on her belly. He sighed contentedly, "Let's try a massage first."

Zhu Qing's face was bright red. Though it wasn't the first time she'd been so close to Yang Kai—there had been even bolder moments before—her heart still raced, tangled with nervousness.

She reached out with both hands, gently pressing her thumbs on Yang Kai's temples, softly rotating them.

Yang Kai's whole body relaxed, feeling as if he was floating among the clouds, rising and falling, so comfortable he nearly lost control. He thought, this girl is really cooperative. Maybe he should let go of everything and get to know her deeply, step by step.

Suddenly, a wild force surged from his temples, as if two giant hammers struck at once. Yang Kai's face twisted, his eyes widened.

"Did you really think I know nothing and would let you do whatever you want?" Zhu Qing gritted her teeth, unleashing a strength that could move mountains and seas. Her thumbs drilled fiercely into Yang Kai's temples.

"I'm dying, I'm dying!" Yang Kai's head was locked in place, his whole body unable to move. He slapped the ground desperately.

"It's my first time leaving Dragon Isle, but I'm no fool. Don't bully me again!" Zhu Qing snapped, her hands never stopping.

Yang Kai felt both heavenly and miserable at once, his body arching as he suddenly bounced off the ground. He kicked at Zhu Qing's head.

Zhu Qing blocked with a wave of her hand, easily deflecting the attack. She stood up, putting distance between them, glaring angrily.

Yang Kai staggered a few steps, still feeling dizzy and lightheaded, nearly losing his balance. He protested indignantly, "So you were just pretending to be innocent with me? That's no fun at all."

It was as if he'd been deceived, a deep wound opening in his heart.

"I've had enough of you!" Zhu Qing shouted, gritting her teeth.

"It's not like I wanted you to stay. You're the one who won't leave, so how is that my fault?"

Zhu Qing's chest heaved, as if a volcano was about to erupt inside her. She stomped her foot. "Fine! From now on, I won't bother you again, and you don't come looking for me!"

"Ridiculous!" Yang Kai put his hands on his hips, spitting as he spoke. "Even if every woman in the world died, I wouldn't come looking for you."

Zhu Qing’s face suddenly turned icy cold. A mist welled up in her beautiful eyes, but her voice remained calm: “Remember what you said.”

She turned and left.

Yang Kai sneered, waving his hand. "Go on, I'm not seeing you off!"

Zhu Qing was already gone.

In a flash, Hua Qingsi appeared atop the mountain, glanced at Yang Kai, then looked in the direction Zhu Qing had left. "Had a fight?"

"There's no point in talking when we can't see eye to eye," Yang Kai snorted coldly.

"You can still catch up, she's not far." Hua Qingsi gazed into the distance. "Women are easy to coax. If she's angry, it means she cares about you."

"She has other motives." Yang Kai pouted, turning to Hua Qingsi. "What do you want?"

Hua Qingsi said seriously, "They're here."

Yang Kai's expression hardened, sneering. "So they've come. Let's go, follow me to see what the Great Emperor's disciples are like."

Outside Lingxiao Palace's mountain gate, beyond the Grand Guardian Array, a massive multi-deck ship hovered in midair, like a dark cloud pressing down, blocking most of the sky.

Five figures stood on the deck.

Ji Ying stood at the front, his face gloomy as he waited. Li Jiao, Palace Master of Li Dragon Palace, and Mi Qi, Sect Master of Mitian Sect, stood like guards at his sides. Behind them were two more figures—Ji Ying’s disciples, with Li Xuan among them, his presence especially conspicuous.

Li Xuan had lost most of his teeth, and his cheeks were still swollen today.

It wasn't that his injuries were severe—he was an alchemist from Medicine Pill Valley, and naturally had excellent healing pills. Such wounds could be healed easily, but he deliberately kept them, hoping Ji Ying would see and understand the humiliation he'd suffered at Lingxiao Palace.

It worked perfectly.

When Ji Ying saw his beloved disciple beaten like a pig, with most of his teeth gone, he exploded with anger.

Li Xuan was only a second-generation disciple of Medicine Pill Valley, not personally taught by Great Emperor Miaodan, but he was still from Medicine Pill Valley.

To be so humiliated in the Northern Territory was unforgivable.

And now, Ji Ying had come to Lingxiao Palace himself, only to be stopped outside the gate, receiving no respect or proper treatment. This made him dislike Lingxiao Palace even more.

"This is outrageous!" Li Jiao fumed. "Lingxiao Palace is far too arrogant. Master Ji's visit should be an honor for them, yet no one came to greet him. Such a sect has no manners at all."

Mi Qi chimed in, "That's right. It seems Lingxiao Palace doesn't respect Master Ji or Medicine Pill Valley."

The two were eager to stir up trouble, hoping Lingxiao Palace would offend Ji Ying even more. They wouldn't miss the chance to fan the flames.

Ji Ying's already unpleasant expression grew even darker.

He was a disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, and an Imperial-Grade Pill Master. In the Northern Territory, every sect treated him as an honored guest. Wherever he went, people welcomed him warmly. But at Lingxiao Palace, he was shut out!

Earlier, some Chief Steward had come out, asked a few questions, then disappeared. There was no sign of her since.

Is Lingxiao Palace really so arrogant? Hearing Mi Qi and Li Jiao badmouth Lingxiao Palace before, Ji Ying knew they were wary of the place and deliberately slandering it. But now, his own experience told him Lingxiao Palace really wasn't impressive.

"Master, you must stand up for your disciple." Li Xuan jumped out at the right moment, sobbing and sniffling. "The Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace not only beat me, but also took the Source Crystals I was going to use to buy spiritual medicine. I told him your name and our ancestor's, but he didn't care. Master, you can't let him off so easily."

"What!" Ji Ying's face changed, snarling. "He dared disrespect our ancestor?"

Though his own humiliation and his disciple’s suffering had already left him seething, Ji Ying had not yet lost all reason. But now, hearing Li Xuan say that the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace actually dared show no respect for Great Emperor Miaodan, Ji Ying finally exploded in fury.

Li Xuan watched for reactions, then said, "He also said... he also said..."

"What did he say?" Ji Ying asked, his face grim.

Li Xuan hesitated, "I don't dare say."

"Say it!" Ji Ying glared at him.

A senior brother urged, "Junior Brother, just tell Master what he said. Don't be afraid!"

Encouraged, Li Xuan said, "He said all alchemists are worthless, that just because we can refine pills, we think we're better than others, but in his eyes we're nothing..."

"Outrageous!" Ji Ying shouted.

Li Xuan dropped to his knees. "Master, those weren't my words, that's what he said."

"Of course I know it wasn't you." Ji Ying took a deep breath to calm himself, raised his hand. "Get up!"

Li Xuan stood shakily, wiping his eyes with his sleeve. No one noticed the sinister smile at the corner of his mouth.

As Ji Ying's disciple, he knew Ji Ying's greatest weaknesses.

Great Emperor Miaodan was Ji Ying's mentor, and pill refining was Ji Ying's pursuit. Both meant everything to him. Anyone who touched these two nerves would never be forgiven.

Now, thanks to Li Xuan's story, Yang Kai had crossed both lines. There was no way he'd get off easy.

Let's see how you die now!

"Master Ji, it seems Lingxiao Palace has no reason to exist. If it stays in the Northern Territory, it's a disgrace to us all!" Li Jiao said coldly. "Please gather the Northern Territory's allies and destroy Lingxiao Palace!"

Mi Qi added, "Mitian Sect is willing to use all its strength to help the Northern Territory remove this tumor."

Ji Ying frowned, thinking for a while before saying, "Let me meet the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace first."

He wasn't someone ruled by emotion. Though furious, he'd only heard his disciple's side of the story.

He didn't think Li Xuan would lie to him, but to be safe, he wanted to meet the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace and see what he was really about. If things were as Li Xuan said, then it wouldn't be too late to act against Lingxiao Palace.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao exchanged glances, knowing it wasn't wise to rush. They stayed silent.

Li Xuan, however, couldn’t help but show a flicker of panic. He had exaggerated and even fabricated much of what he told Ji Ying, and he truly feared being confronted face-to-face with the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace. But he had always acted obedient and sensible before his master. Even if they met in person, there would be no evidence against him. As long as he stuck to his story, would his master really trust an outsider over his own disciple?

Thinking this way, Li Xuan relaxed a little.

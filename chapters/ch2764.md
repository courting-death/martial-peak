## Chapter 2764 — Try Refusing If You Dare

As an expert at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, Xiao Yuyang’s insight was far beyond that of someone like Xiao Chen, who had grown up sheltered. With just a glance, he could tell that Xiao Chen was by no means a match for Yang Kai.

A wave of shock rippled through Xiao Chen’s heart. Both he and Yang Kai had broken through to the Emperor Realm thanks to their adventures in the Shattered Star Sea, so in terms of timing, there was little difference between them. Yet, their strength was worlds apart.

“Chen’er, fall back!” Xiao Yuyang shouted loudly.

Unfortunately, it was already too late. From within the curtain of swords, a palm suddenly shot out, crushing everything in its path. The sword curtain withered in an instant, and even Xiao Chen’s sword intent became utterly chaotic.

Xiao Chen’s face changed drastically. He hurriedly retreated, but the palm that had destroyed his sword curtain followed him like a shadow, about to land squarely on his chest.

At the critical moment, a fierce gust of wind swept in from the side—Xiao Yuyang had made his move to save him.

Yang Kai tapped his foot lightly, and his figure, which had been moving at extreme speed, suddenly came to a halt. The fierce gust of wind brushed past his fingertips, missing him by a hair’s breadth.

Xiao Yuyang stepped forward, placing himself between Yang Kai and Xiao Chen. With a grave expression, he said, “Yang Kai, I have no wish to bully the young with my seniority, nor do I desire to fight you. Would you be willing to cooperate with me?”

Yang Kai grinned. “I won’t!”

Xiao Yuyang sighed lightly. “If that’s the case, then forgive my offense.”

As soon as he finished speaking, he reached out to grab Yang Kai. With that grab, it was as if the entire world was sealed, and even the void began to hum.

Facing Xiao Yuyang’s grab, Yang Kai did not dodge. He replied nonchalantly, “Sir Xiao, it’s not that I look down on you, but I’m afraid you won’t be able to keep me here like this. The world is vast, and we’ll meet again.”

The Laws of Space surged forth. His figure suddenly turned into nothingness, leaving only a remnant image behind.

Xiao Yuyang’s grab shattered the remnant image. His expression changed slightly, and he shouted, “Space Divine Ability!”

Turning his head, Xiao Yuyang found that Yang Kai had vanished without a trace—he had no idea where he’d gone. Even when he released his Divine Sense, he couldn’t sense Yang Kai’s presence at all. Xiao Yuyang’s expression instantly darkened.

He had acted personally, yet he still failed to stop Yang Kai. If word got out, no one would believe it.

Originally, he thought Tan Junhao and Wu Ming’s deaths were the work of that Yao King, but now it seemed there was a real possibility Yang Kai had done it. Possessing space divine abilities was already a miraculous means, not to mention, intelligence reported that this brat also had the Mountain and River Bell of the Origin Cauldron Great Emperor.

That was a primordial era treasure—no one knew just how powerful the Mountain and River Bell truly was.

Turning his head, Xiao Yuyang saw Xiao Chen standing there, utterly lost and devastated. His hand, clutching the longsword, trembled uncontrollably, and his face was ashen, drained of all color.

Being defeated by Yang Kai in a single move was a huge blow to Xiao Chen. Before this, he always believed that even though he had only recently broken through to the Emperor Realm, he was far above the rabble. He was a core disciple of the Star God Palace, and the son of Xiao Yuyang, raised with great care since childhood—a true elite. Among those of the same level, he was considered invincible.

But now, the sense of superiority he’d had since childhood was shattered by a single palm from Yang Kai. He could hardly accept it.

“Victory and defeat are common among warriors. Rather than dwelling on this loss, you should think about how to improve your strength,” Xiao Yuyang said calmly.

Xiao Chen’s whole body trembled. He finally came to his senses and said in a deep voice, “Father, your lesson is right.”

“Let’s go,” Xiao Yuyang beckoned.

“Where to?” Xiao Chen asked.

“Qingyang Shrine,” Xiao Yuyang replied, taking the lead and flying off.

He had been lying in wait here and encountered Yang Kai, so Yang Kai’s intentions were obvious—he was heading to Qingyang Shrine. Since he couldn’t stop him here, he would just go to Qingyang Shrine to find him.

No matter what, this matter had to be settled.

“Who goes there? Stop at once!”

After flying forward for a short while, two figures rushed over to block Yang Kai’s path. The leader shouted loudly.

Yang Kai had entered and exited Qingyang Shrine many times, so he naturally knew the rules. He immediately took out the Qingyang Gold Token and tossed it to the two mountain-guarding disciples.

The leader took the token, and his expression changed. “Qingyang Gold Token?”

This gold token was something only a few core disciples within the shrine were qualified to possess. Although they were also disciples of Qingyang Shrine, they were far from being core disciples.

As for Yang Kai, his face was completely unfamiliar—none of the mountain-guarding disciples recognized him at all. He was clearly not one of the shrine’s core disciples.

The leading disciple immediately shouted angrily, “What little thief dares to impersonate a disciple of our shrine? Where did you get this gold token?”

As he spoke, the two of them immediately summoned their Secret Treasures, locking their auras onto Yang Kai, ready to attack at any moment. Yang Kai was a registered disciple of Qingyang Shrine, and the gold token had been given to him by Elder Gao Xueting back in Fenglin City. It hadn’t been reported to the entire Shrine, so many disciples had no idea who Yang Kai was. Now, seeing a stranger holding a core disciple’s token, they were naturally on high alert.

Yang Kai said, “The token was given to me by Elder Gao. You can ask Elder Gao or any of the other elders. Just tell them Yang Kai is here—they’ll explain everything.”

“Yang Kai!” The leading disciple frowned, gave a look to his junior brother, and nodded. “Wait here.”

As he spoke, he took out a Transmission Compass, presumably to report to the higher-ups.

After a moment, the Transmission Compass vibrated. The leader infused his Divine Sense into it, and his face immediately took on a strange expression.

The elder’s message confirmed that the token was real, and that this Yang Kai was indeed a disciple of the Shrine.

“Forgive us, Senior Brother, please!” With the elder’s order, the mountain-guarding disciples no longer dared to block him. They immediately stepped aside. “Elder Gao said you should go to Ten Thousand Saints Peak to find her.”

Yang Kai nodded, retrieved the gold token, and as he passed the two disciples, he suddenly reminded them, “By the way, there will be two more people coming soon. Try to hold them up for a while.”

“Yes!” The two disciples, not knowing the truth, immediately agreed.

Ten Thousand Saints Peak was one of the main peaks of Qingyang Shrine. Yang Kai, familiar with the way, flew straight to the top. From afar, he saw a pure white figure standing before the main hall, gazing in his direction.

That figure was like a white lotus, flawless and pure. Her fitted dress outlined her beautiful curves perfectly, and beside her stood another petite figure.

From a distance, Yang Kai smiled and walked over.

Before he got close, the white figure suddenly raised her hand. A round, sun-like mirror rose behind her head, blazing like the scorching sun, and the air temperature soared.

Imperial Treasure—Scorching Sun Mirror!

Several scorching beams of light shot out from the mirror, forming graceful arcs as they blasted toward Yang Kai.

Startled, Yang Kai summoned the Myriad Sword. With a single move, he unleashed Sword Intent, forming a curtain of swords to shield himself.

Sss sss sss sss…

The blazing beams were blocked by the sword curtain, unable to advance an inch, but Yang Kai was forced to retreat repeatedly.

After neutralizing the scorching beams, a figure suddenly flickered before Yang Kai—Gao Xueting appeared out of nowhere. Her slender jade hand formed a loose fist, and with a graceful bend of her finger, she tapped Yang Kai smartly on the head.

Yang Kai didn’t dare dodge and took the hit head-on. His head made a dull thud, and he shrank down a bit.

“Elder Gao…” Yang Kai rubbed the spot where he’d been hit, looking aggrieved.

Gao Xueting snorted through her delicate nose and glanced sideways at Yang Kai. “Your wings have hardened? You dare fight back against me?”

“How could I?” Yang Kai looked wronged. “It was just a reflex…”

“Do you know why I hit you?” Gao Xueting asked.

Yang Kai grinned sheepishly. “I haven’t come back in a long time, so you were worried about me.”

Gao Xueting snorted, “It’s not just about coming back or not. You caused such a huge mess and faced such danger—why didn’t you ask the Shrine for help? Do you think the Shrine is made up of outsiders, or are you the outsider?”

Yang Kai immediately understood she was referring to the Star God Palace incident. His heart warmed, and he chuckled.

“Don’t give me that cheeky grin. Get down here!” Gao Xueting reached out, grabbed Yang Kai by the ear, and dragged him down to Ten Thousand Saints Peak.

“I’m dead, I’m dead, I’m dead…” Yang Kai tilted his head, holding the Myriad Sword, being dragged along by Gao Xueting. Even when facing Great Emperors and Divine Spirits, he could stand tall, but now he didn’t dare resist at all. He felt like his ear was about to be torn off and begged loudly, completely losing his dignity.

Only after flying down to Ten Thousand Saints Peak did Gao Xueting let go, giving Yang Kai a slap on the back of the head.

Yang Kai staggered, not daring to say a word.

Spotting Wen Zishan, he hurried forward and greeted him respectfully, “Greetings, Hall Master Wen! May fortune smile upon you!”

Wen Zishan’s mouth twitched. He looked up at the sky, pretending not to hear, and kindly sent Yang Kai a voice transmission: “Little Xueting is very angry. You’d better let her calm down first, or this Hall Master won’t dare talk to you.”

Yang Kai’s face darkened, and he shot him a look of disdain.

Looking at the others nearby—whether it was Deputy Hall Master Qiu Ran, Elder Chen Qian, Di Rong, or You Kun—they all averted their eyes, pretending not to see him.

Only Gao Xueting stared at him coldly, her gaze as sharp as a sword, as if she wanted to stab Yang Kai full of holes.

Yang Kai obediently walked up to Gao Xueting, lowered his head, and said, “Elder Gao, this disciple was wrong. Please punish me.”

Gao Xueting said calmly, “What did you do wrong? Why should I punish you? Actually, I should thank you. If you hadn’t saved me last time, I might not have escaped safely.”

As she spoke, she actually bowed to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai was startled and quickly stepped aside.

Gao Xueting adjusted her direction and bowed again.

Yang Kai pulled a long face. “Elder Gao, what do you want? I’ll agree to anything!”

Gao Xueting immediately looked up, her eyes blazing as she stared at Yang Kai. “Since you’ve said that, I won’t make things difficult for you. Just promise me one thing, and I’ll let you off this time.”

“Please say it,” Yang Kai’s eyes lit up.

“Become an Elder of the shrine.”

Hearing this, Yang Kai’s expression turned solemn. “Elder Gao, this matter…”

“You dare refuse?” Gao Xueting’s hand flashed with cold light, and a sword was instantly pressed against Yang Kai’s neck. She gritted her teeth and said, “This Elder is asking you personally. Try refusing if you dare!”

## Chapter 2682 — Wu Ming

The next day, while Yang Kai was still meditating, a sudden knock sounded at the door.

Sweeping out with his Divine Sense, he discovered it was actually Ye Qinghan and Du Xian.

Yang Kai opened the door and looked at the two of them. "What is it?" he asked.

Ye Qinghan’s voice trembled as she said, "Young Master Yang, the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect has brought my father over. He’s right outside the inn."

"Sect Master Ye?" In his mind, Yang Kai recalled Ye Hen’s appearance and nodded slightly. "It seems that Li Qingyun knows what’s good for him. Since he’s brought him here, let’s just receive him."

Du Xian said with shame, "It was Li Qingyun himself who brought him over."

Li Qingyun was, after all, a First Stage Emperor. Both he and Ye Qinghan were only at the Dao Origin Realm, so they really had no right to speak as equals. They also didn’t know what the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect intended with this move, so they could only ask Yang Kai to handle it.

Yang Kai nodded at their words. "I’ll go with you."

"Thank you, Brother Yang!" Du Xian said gratefully.

"No need to be so polite." Yang Kai smiled faintly, but sighed inwardly. If his previous guess was correct, then the calamity that befell the Thousand Leaves Sect this time was probably because of him.

The three of them stepped outside. Out of nowhere, Yingfei appeared and followed closely behind Yang Kai.

Once outside the inn, they indeed saw Li Qingyun standing there, dressed in white, a longsword on his back, exuding the air of a swordsman.

In front of him stood Ye Hen. Though his expression was somewhat dispirited, there were no signs of torture. It was likely just the blow of his sect being destroyed and the deaths of countless disciples that weighed on his heart.

"Father!"

"Master!" Ye Qinghan and Du Xian called out in unison, then glared angrily at Li Qingyun.

Ye Hen glanced at the two of them and gave a bitter smile.

The Thousand Leaves Sect had endured for tens of thousands of years, but now, in his generation, it had been wiped out. He truly felt he had let down his ancestors. After a year apart, seeing his daughter and disciple again, Ye Hen’s feelings were complicated.

Li Qingyun said nothing, only cupped his fists to Yang Kai before turning and leaving without looking back.

It seemed his sole purpose in coming was to deliver Ye Hen.

Just as he’d said, the mastermind had already gotten what they wanted. Whether Ye Hen lived or died no longer mattered. That was why, after being captured by the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect, Ye Hen was able to keep his life—otherwise, he would have been killed long ago.

"Father!" Ye Qinghan rushed over, carefully checking Ye Hen’s body. Only after confirming he hadn’t lost any limbs did she finally breathe a sigh of relief.

"Sect Master Ye!" Yang Kai stepped forward and cupped his fists.

"Young Master Yang!" Ye Hen and Yang Kai were old acquaintances. Seeing him together with his daughter and chief disciple, Ye Hen naturally understood that their rescue was thanks to Yang Kai, and he was moved to tears.

"Let’s talk inside," Yang Kai gestured.

Ye Hen nodded. Supported by Ye Qinghan and Du Xian, he walked step by step into the inn.

Once in a private room, Yang Kai had the waiter bring wine and food. The group sat cross-legged, drinking away their sorrows.

Ye Qinghan fussed over Ye Hen, and upon learning that he hadn’t been tortured in the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect but merely placed under house arrest, her hatred for the sect lessened somewhat.

However, after this blow, Ye Hen seemed to have aged considerably, appearing old and frail. Cup after cup of wine went down, and his expression grew more desolate.

He had long since reached the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Back when Yang Kai brought back the long-lost secret techniques and manuals to the Thousand Leaves Sect from Emperor Heaven Valley, Ye Hen had been confident he could break through to the Emperor Realm in his lifetime. But before he could realize that ambition, his sect was destroyed by powerful enemies.

Truly, the ways of Heaven are unpredictable. Fate moves as it will.

Now, with his will shaken, he didn’t know if he would ever have the chance to reach the Emperor Realm.

Yang Kai raised his cup. "Sect Master Ye, what happened to the Thousand Leaves Sect this time may have something to do with me."

Ye Hen looked at him in surprise, as if misunderstanding. He gave a bitter smile. "It was because of that Lady Hua, not you, Young Master Yang."

Yang Kai shook his head. "Sister Hua was just a pretext. Chi Yue and Ai Ou were the real targets."

Ye Hen asked in surprise, "What do you mean by that?"

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian also looked at Yang Kai in confusion.

Yang Kai pondered for a moment. "I don’t really know how to explain it. It’s just a feeling. Maybe this is all a scheme targeting me, and the Thousand Leaves Sect was just caught up in it. Everything was to lure me to Flowing Shadow City."

Ye Hen was stunned. If that was true, then the Thousand Leaves Sect was truly unlucky.

"I’m sorry!" Yang Kai looked at Ye Hen and said solemnly.

Ye Hen gave a bitter smile. "Young Master Yang, there’s no need for that. This was a calamity my Thousand Leaves Sect was destined to face. You have done us a great kindness, and I have never dared forget it. Even if this happened because of you, I have no complaints—only regret that my sect was too weak to resist our enemies."

Yang Kai said, "Rest assured, Sect Master Ye. No matter who is behind this, I will avenge the Thousand Leaves Sect."

Ye Hen smiled. "With your words, I am at ease. But... the enemy is in the shadows, and we are in the light. Are you confident?"

Ye Qinghan glanced at Yingfei, then leaned in to whisper something in Ye Hen’s ear.

Ye Hen’s body trembled at her words. He looked up at Yingfei in shock. "So you are a Yao King, sir. Please forgive my discourtesy."

His heart settled. Yang Kai was already formidable, and now he had a Yao King at his side. There was nothing to worry about.

Yingfei smiled and waved his hand.

Yang Kai said, "When the auction begins, the hidden dagger will finally be revealed. No matter what the mastermind’s true goal is, they’ll have to bare their fangs. When that moment comes, I will cut them down."

Ye Hen stood up and said solemnly, "Please accept my bow, Young Master Yang."

The Thousand Leaves Sect no longer had the strength to seek revenge. If Yang Kai could avenge them, he would truly be their benefactor.

Yang Kai quickly helped Ye Hen up and shook his head. "Sect Master Ye, you’re being too serious. I can no longer stay uninvolved. Let’s see who is stronger when the time comes."

After drinking for a while longer, Yang Kai finally took his leave and returned to his room.

The next half-month passed peacefully.

As expected, Zhu Qing seemed to have vanished from the world and didn’t come to bother him again, much to Yang Kai’s satisfaction.

Although this woman was stunning and pleasing to the eye, and being with her was a unique experience, she was, after all, a dragon girl. Yang Kai really didn’t want to provoke her.

During this time, many Emperors arrived in Flowing Shadow City, all likely drawn by the Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppets. After all, each Heavenly Puppet was equivalent to an Emperor, making them perfect for guarding a sect. Perhaps Ai Ou’s Stellar Origin was also a great temptation.

Yang Kai searched Flowing Shadow City many times, but never found any trace of Chi Yue, Ai Ou, or the others. He had no choice but to give up.

But the enemy probably wasn’t foolish enough to reveal their cards before the auction began.

One day, the long-quiet Flowing Shadow City suddenly became lively. The entrance to the Qiyao Commerce Guild was packed with carriages and people, bustling with activity.

As one of the two top Commerce Guilds in the Southern Territory, the Qiyao Commerce Guild naturally had a branch in Flowing Shadow City. This auction was to be held at their branch.

All the Emperor Realm experts who received invitations arrived as scheduled.

In front of the Commerce Guild, two rows of beautiful young women waited respectfully. Whenever a distinguished guest arrived, one would step forward with a smile to lead them inside. Each of these girls was a beauty, graceful and alluring, dressed in thin veils that revealed just enough to captivate the eye.

They were clearly well-trained. Every smile made guests feel at home, and their soft voices left a favorable impression.

The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect’s Sect Master, Li Qingyun, also stood outside the Commerce Guild, holding the Yuxu Sword, eyes closed, standing like a statue. His sword intent was restrained, and he looked like an ordinary person from the outside. But those who knew him knew he was not to be trifled with. At this moment, he was like a sheathed sword—once drawn, he would be a powerful sword cultivator, his killing intent surging.

Suddenly, Li Qingyun’s eyes snapped open and fixed ahead. A deep wariness—and even a trace of helplessness—flashed across those sword-like eyes.

Ahead, a group of five strolled over, moving as if no one else existed. The surrounding guests all glared at them, but when they saw who it was, their expressions changed and they quickly stepped aside, as if avoiding a fierce tiger.

The group of five was led by a young man, followed by a sinister-looking man with a hooked nose, a pair of young man and woman, and a middle-aged elder.

It was Yang Kai and his companions.

Walking straight to the Commerce Guild, Yang Kai glanced at Li Qingyun and grinned. "Sect Master Li, are you here to greet guests too? What a grand gesture!"

The helplessness on Li Qingyun’s face deepened. The corners of his mouth twitched, revealing an indescribable expression.

"Hahaha, this must be Young Master Yang, right? It’s truly an honor to have you here. Please, come inside!"

A hearty laugh came from the side, full of enthusiasm.

Yang Kai turned to look and saw an unremarkable middle-aged man walking over with a beaming smile. That smile made Yang Kai frown.

"Who are you?" Yang Kai asked, squinting.

"It’s you!" Ye Qinghan and Du Xian exclaimed, looking at the man with fear.

Ye Hen’s body trembled, his eyes blazing with anger.

"You know him?" Yang Kai asked in surprise.

Gritting her teeth, Ye Qinghan said, "It was he who subdued our Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppet! He’s also the one who came for Lady Hua!"

"I am Wu Ming," the middle-aged man said with a slight smile, staring intently at Yang Kai, completely ignoring the three from the Thousand Leaves Sect. To him, they were nothing but ants, not worth his attention.

Yang Kai’s gaze turned cold. "So you’re the one behind all this."

Wu Ming smiled. "Young Master Yang, you flatter me. How could I possibly... What are you doing?"

Before he could finish, Wu Ming was shocked—Yang Kai had suddenly slapped at him. Emperor Yuan surged, Spiritual Qi roiled. Although there was a small gap in their Stages, Wu Ming was horrified to find that he could hardly dodge this blow.

He had never expected Yang Kai to be so bold as to attack him right away, catching him completely off guard. By the time he reacted, it was already too late.

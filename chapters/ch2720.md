## Chapter 2720 — Is That Really True

“Deal with one another? What dealings could there possibly be?” Yang Kai’s expression darkened slightly.

He also held Medicine Pill Valley in high regard. Not to mention that he had inherited the legacy of Gongsun Mu, the Third Disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, and thus had some ties to Medicine Pill Valley, but just having a Great Emperor was enough for Yang Kai to take them seriously.

But that was before.

If everyone from Medicine Pill Valley was as arrogant and unruly as this youth, looking down on everyone, then Yang Kai didn't see much reason to care about them.

"You'll know when the time comes," the youth replied impatiently.

Yang Kai frowned, "If you're from Medicine Pill Valley, what are you doing at my Lingxiao Palace?"

Lingxiao Palace had only just been established and had never had any dealings with Medicine Pill Valley. For someone to suddenly show up like this naturally made Yang Kai suspicious.

"You think I wanted to come here?" The youth sneered, cupping his fists, then turned to the void and called out loudly, "By my master's order, I have come here to buy medicine!"

"Buy medicine!"

Yang Kai and Hua Qingsi exchanged glances, both looking completely baffled.

"Is this kid right in the head? Coming to my Lingxiao Palace to buy medicine!" Yang Kai secretly sent a message.

Hua Qingsi wasn't sure either. "He doesn't seem to have any problems, though he is a bit annoying."

"Maybe he really does have something wrong with him. Tsk, kind of pitiful."

"If he really is sick, maybe his identity is fake too."

"Hmm, that makes sense!"

"Hey, you two... what are you whispering about?" The youth, seeing that Yang Kai and Hua Qingsi hadn't responded for a long time, swept his gaze over and noticed their Divine Senses surging as they secretly communicated, their looks toward him growing strange. He immediately became annoyed.

"Heh, it's nothing." Yang Kai grinned, "So, Young Brother, what medicine have you come to buy at my Lingxiao Palace?"

The youth flicked his hand, and a jade slip shot straight over.

Yang Kai moved his mind slightly, opened the Grand Guardian Array just a bit to let the jade slip through, and caught it in his palm.

Immersing his Divine Sense into it for a look, his expression immediately became rather odd.

The jade slip really did contain a long list of spiritual medicines, dazzling in variety—over a thousand kinds, all of different grades, with quite a few even being Imperial-Grade spiritual medicines, each worth a fortune.

The youth said, "Ten years ago, my master gave a copy of this medicine list to Wenqing Sect and asked them to collect these for us. Now the deadline is up, so my master sent me to purchase them. Does that make things clear?"

Yang Kai suddenly understood and asked, "Is that really the case?"

This youth wasn't crazy—he really had come to buy medicine. But what he wanted were the spiritual medicines that Wenqing Sect had been asked to collect on behalf of Medicine Pill Valley. Although the list was long and the value high, Wenqing Sect was a massive sect. Ten years should have been enough to gather everything.

Unfortunately, Wenqing Sect had been destroyed by Yang Kai. Naturally, the youth could only come to Yang Kai now.

The youth continued, "It's not just Wenqing Sect. All the top sects in the Northern Territory cooperate with my Medicine Pill Valley. Every ten years, we update the medicine list, and when the time comes, someone from Medicine Pill Valley comes to collect it."

"All of them cooperate?" Yang Kai was startled.

But thinking about it, it made sense. Medicine Pill Valley's approach really did save time and effort, and with Great Emperor Miaodan's reputation, all the major sects in the Northern Territory would be happy to comply. They might even use it as a way to build a good relationship with Medicine Pill Valley, so that if they ever needed pills refined, they'd have an advantage.

The youth said, "That's right. Now that you've destroyed Wenqing Sect, all the spiritual medicines they've collected over the past ten years must be in your hands. Hand them over—I need to bring them back and report to my master!"

He spoke so earnestly that it didn't seem like he was lying. Yang Kai was almost convinced.

Still, to be safe, he said, "Wait here!"

Now that he was the master of a Palace, and the other party was just a snot-nosed youth with no proof of identity, he couldn't just take his word for it. If he got tricked, it would be too embarrassing.

So Yang Kai decided it was best to verify things first.

As soon as he finished speaking, he disappeared.

"Where are you going... Bastard!" The youth was furious. He'd thought that revealing his identity as a disciple of Medicine Pill Valley would have them immediately invite him in with utmost respect, offering food and drink. Who would have thought he'd be left standing outside in the wind, not even allowed through the gate, with the Grand Guardian Array still up as if guarding against a thief.

Unable to hold back, he shouted, "Fine, fine! So this is how Lingxiao Palace treats its guests, looking down on my Medicine Pill Valley! I'll be sure to report this to my master when I return! In the future, don't even think about getting a single pill from us!"

Hua Qingsi hurried to smooth things over, "Young Master, please don't be angry. Our Palace Master is always this brash—he really didn't mean to slight you."

Coming from a great sect herself, she knew well that it's easy to meet the King of Hell, but hard to deal with his little devils. This youth might only be at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm and still young, but if he went back to Medicine Pill Valley and said a few bad things about Lingxiao Palace, it could cause trouble.

As the Chief Steward of Lingxiao Palace, she naturally wanted to turn big problems into small ones, and small ones into nothing.

"Not slighting me?" The youth pointed in the direction Yang Kai had left, still roaring, "Look at his attitude! How can someone like that be the master of a Palace? I don't think Lingxiao Palace will be able to stay in the Northern Territory for long."

He was young, but his arrogance knew no bounds—he spoke as if a few words from him could drive Lingxiao Palace out of the Northern Territory entirely.

Hua Qingsi was helpless. She couldn't argue back, lest she make things worse, so she could only watch the youth throw his tantrum.

After ranting for a while, the youth's gaze shifted and he looked Hua Qingsi up and down. Suddenly, he put on a smile and said, "Sister, what's your name?"

Hua Qingsi forced a smile and gave her name.

The youth clapped his hands, "Qingsi, what a good name! Truly a good name! If you ask me, this Lingxiao Palace won't last long. Sister, you don't need to suffer here—why not come back to Medicine Pill Valley with me?"

Hua Qingsi couldn't help but laugh, "I can't refine pills, so what would I do at Medicine Pill Valley?"

The youth chuckled, "You don't need to know how to refine pills—it's enough that I do. As a woman, you must know how to make beds and fold quilts, right? Just follow me, and I guarantee no one will drive you out of Medicine Pill Valley. I'm a Dao Origin-Grade Lower-Level Alchemist. Given time, I'll definitely become an Imperial Alchemist. Tsk tsk, Sister, you're really beautiful."

Make beds and fold quilts...

Hua Qingsi bit her silver teeth, her smile suddenly turning cold. She was an Emperor Realm cultivator, yet this First Stage Dao Origin Realm brat actually wanted her to follow him around making beds and folding quilts? And he was brazenly ogling her with a lecherous look.

If it were anyone else, Hua Qingsi would have already gouged out his eyes. But since he might be from Medicine Pill Valley, she could only endure her anger.

Still, the fact that he was a Dao Origin-Grade Lower-Level Alchemist truly shocked Hua Qingsi. She couldn't help but sigh—worthy of being from Medicine Pill Valley. So young, yet already so accomplished.

"Sister, you should really think it over. Opportunities like this don't come often. My standards are very high—it's not just any woman who can catch my eye." The youth was completely oblivious, still full of himself.

Hua Qingsi replied coolly, "Young Brother flatters me, but I'm afraid I can't accept your kindness."

A brat who hadn't even grown all his hair yet dared to make a move on her? If she were to find a man, it would have to be someone like Yang Kai—how could she possibly be interested in him?

The youth couldn't help but snort coldly, "Don't know what's good for you!"

He'd been so sincere, yet this woman still refused. Truly unreasonable—choosing to wallow in mediocrity.

"Where did that Yang Kai go? Why isn't he back yet?" After being rebuffed by Hua Qingsi, the youth grew impatient.

Hua Qingsi turned her head away, "Who knows."

...

"Eh, what are you doing here!" By the Space Array, Yang Kai had just appeared when he saw a familiar figure.

Zhu Qing glared at Yang Kai, her chest heaving like mountains, and gritted her teeth, "You're avoiding me!"

"Nonsense!" Yang Kai denied guiltily. "Why would I avoid you?"

"You are avoiding me!"

For months, Zhu Qing hadn't been able to find Yang Kai. If she hadn't waited by the Space Array, she would have thought he'd already teleported away. Today, her patience finally paid off—she'd caught him at last.

"I told you I'm not! Why are you so naggy?" Yang Kai waved impatiently. "Move aside, I need to go out for a bit."

Zhu Qing said nothing, blocking his way and watching him warily, "Where are you going? I'm coming with you!"

"I'm going to Ice Heart Valley. I'll be right back," Yang Kai glared at her. "Why are you following me?"

"Didn't you just go to Ice Heart Valley last time? Why are you going again?"

"Someone came saying they want to buy medicine. I'm going to Ice Heart Valley to ask if it's true... Damn, why am I explaining this to you? It's too much trouble."

As he spoke, the Space Array in front of him suddenly rippled with light, and a blurry figure appeared on the array.

Someone was teleporting over.

Yang Kai and Zhu Qing looked over together.

The figure quickly became clear, revealing Ji Yao's face.

"Yao'er, you came at just the right time! I was just about to go to Ice Heart Valley to ask something," Yang Kai said happily.

"Hold on, I have something to tell you too," Ji Yao stepped off the Space Array and looked at Yang Kai.

"What is it?" Yang Kai was puzzled.

"The ten-year deadline for Medicine Pill Valley to collect medicine is up. There may be disciples from Medicine Pill Valley coming here soon. My master sent me to let you know—if anyone comes, treat them with respect and give them whatever medicine they ask for. Medicine Pill Valley won't let you suffer a loss."

"Uh... so it's really true!" Yang Kai scratched his face.

Earlier, when the youth said Medicine Pill Valley cooperated with all the major sects in the Northern Territory, Yang Kai immediately thought to go ask Ice Heart Valley about it. If it was true, they'd definitely know, and he could confirm the youth's identity.

Who would have thought that before he even got there, Ji Yao would teleport over.

"Of course it's true. In fact, a disciple from Medicine Pill Valley is at Ice Heart Valley right now. Why... why do you look like that?"

"It's nothing. Someone just came here too," Yang Kai laughed awkwardly.

"Where are they?" Ji Yao asked curiously.

"Still outside the mountain gate."

Ji Yao glared, "And you haven't invited them in yet?"

"I'll go right now..." Yang Kai turned and headed back.

"Wait!" Zhu Qing called out anxiously, quickly following after him.

Ji Yao frowned, then turned into a stream of light and hurried after them.

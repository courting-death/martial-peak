## Chapter 2926 — Spirit Peak and a Dog

Gao Xueting acted with the force of wind, rain, and lightning, about to destroy that half-formed Demonic Intent as she spoke, but Yang Kai had already flashed in front of it, reached into the light of the Scorching Sun Mirror, and seized the Demonic Intent in one grab.

"Junior Brother, you..." Gao Xueting's face paled.

Yang Kai grinned at her. "Don't worry, nothing will happen. I'll trouble you all to act as Protectors for me!"

While others avoided the Demonic Intent like the plague, Yang Kai showed no fear. His years of experience in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape had taught him that a mere strand of Demonic Intent could do nothing to him, for his body also sealed extremely pure ancient demonic energy, and even an ancient demon's black pupil. As the saying goes, the barefooted do not fear those with shoes—this was probably the same logic.

But this was not something he could explain clearly, so he could only act first and talk later.

At this point, Gao Xueting could no longer stop him. She solemnly warned, "Be careful. If anything seems wrong, let go immediately!"

"I've got it," Yang Kai nodded.

He sat cross-legged in the void, closed his eyes, and used his Divine Sense to communicate with that half strand of Demonic Intent, hoping to probe Gong Yue's whereabouts.

Chen Wenhao, Gao Xueting, and Feng Ming formed a triangle around him, guarding him closely. Gao Xueting's beautiful eyes were fixed on Yang Kai's figure, not moving for an instant. If anything seemed off, the light of the Scorching Sun Mirror would immediately envelop him.

Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming exchanged a glance, both seeing a trace of admiration in each other's eyes. It was simple: faced with such a strange Demonic Intent, neither of them had the courage to do what Yang Kai was doing. Yet, the weakest among them, Yang Kai, dared to take the risk. This was worthy of respect.

At this moment, both felt a sense that each generation brings forth new talents. They had cultivated to the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, with outstanding aptitude and comprehension, and had been leaders among the young in their day—just like Yang Kai now.

Times change, but the endless emergence of geniuses remains the same.

All around was silent. The dozens of martial artists from that small sect still dared not move. They were scattered across the mountain, having witnessed their own Sect Master die in a sudden explosion. Fear had seeped into their bones, and as they looked up at the four in the sky, it was as if they were gazing at gods. They didn't even dare think of avenging their Sect Master.

They did not know that their Sect Master's death had nothing to do with Yang Kai and the others. They thought it was that fellow called Feng Ming who killed him, and only felt that these experts from the great sects were all more and more detestable, lacking all humanity.

Darkness. Yang Kai seemed to be walking in the land of nothingness, surrounded by utter blackness, unable to see any light, hear any sound, or feel anything at all. He was using his mind to communicate with that half strand of Demonic Intent, tracing it back to search for the other half. If he could find the other half, he could find Gong Yue.

Progress was not smooth. This was Yang Kai's first time doing such a thing, and he had no experience—he had to rely entirely on his own exploration.

He searched in this boundless darkness for a long time before changing his strategy, splitting off part of his mind to sense the outside world.

This finally had an effect. Vaguely, Yang Kai sensed that this half strand of Demonic Intent was connected to something by a faint, unbreakable thread. He followed this weak connection to explore further.

The darkness slowly changed. Something seemed to appear ahead in his vision, but it was too blurry to see clearly.

Yang Kai pushed his Divine Soul power even more fiercely, even drawing that half strand of Demonic Intent into his Sea of Consciousness, using the power of his Divine Soul to scour it, washing away all its disguises.

Gradually, the thing in his vision became clearer.

It seemed to be a mountain peak, a solitary one, with beautiful scenery and abundant Spiritual Qi.

Yang Kai suddenly felt that this mountain looked familiar, as if he had seen it somewhere before. But because everything in his vision was still unclear, he couldn't confirm where it was for the moment.

All of this was perceived through the pervasive connection between the half strands of Demonic Intent, revealing Gong Yue's location. So as long as he could confirm the position of this mountain peak, he could determine where Gong Yue was.

He pushed his Divine Soul power even harder.

Now he saw a bit more. There was a figure on the mountain, somewhat resembling Gong Yue, standing motionless somewhere on the peak, as if dead.

Yang Kai's situation was strange, as if he were inside a thick water sphere. He could vaguely see the outlines of things outside, but no matter what, he couldn't see them clearly. This made him quite anxious.

Suddenly, Yang Kai seemed to hear a sound. Like the image, it was vague and intermittent, impossible to grasp.

The sound came again, and this time Yang Kai heard it more clearly—it seemed to be the barking of a dog, incessant and sharp. As his vision shifted, he indeed saw something resembling a small dog, standing not far from the blurred figure and barking furiously.

After barking for a while, the little thing actually pounced, biting at the figure.

The figure still did not move, as if completely unaware.

At that moment, the figure seemed to sense something, suddenly raising its head and looking in Yang Kai's direction. That sharp gaze seemed able to pierce all obstacles, colliding with Yang Kai's vision across endless void.

The blurry scene changed at that instant, becoming much clearer.

Yang Kai shuddered all over, suddenly opening his eyes, dumbfounded.

"Are you alright?" Gao Xueting's palm was slick with sweat. She hadn't been this nervous in all her years since advancing to the Emperor Realm, but now her voice trembled, afraid Yang Kai had been corrupted by the Demonic Intent.

Yang Kai shook his head, his expression a bit dazed.

"Did you find him?" Chen Wenhao asked in a deep voice.

"Spirit Sword Peak!" Yang Kai fixed his gaze on Gao Xueting and replied softly.

Gao Xueting was stunned. "What?"

"Spirit Sword Peak. Gong Yue is at Qingyang Shrine, on my Spirit Sword Peak!" Even as Yang Kai said this, he felt it was absurd, as if what he had just seen was an illusion, but he couldn't find any reason to doubt it.

"Gong Yue is at Qingyang Shrine?" Feng Ming's eyes widened, clearly skeptical.

"That's what I saw," Yang Kai stood up, forced the half strand of Demonic Intent out of his Sea of Consciousness, and crushed it in his hand. Now that he had tracked down Gong Yue, the Demonic Intent was no longer useful.

"Why would he go to Qingyang Shrine?" Feng Ming asked.

"I don't know!" Yang Kai shook his head. Gong Yue had not only gone to Qingyang Shrine, but also to his own Spirit Sword Peak!

Spirit Sword Peak was the spirit peak Yang Kai received after becoming a Shrine Elder. The whole peak was in a rather remote part of the Shrine, but both its scenery and Spiritual Qi were excellent, and it was protected by formations.

Yang Kai had found the mountain familiar at first because of this. Although he hadn't spent much time on Spirit Sword Peak, it was still his territory—how could he not recognize it?

Gong Yue was the former Family Head of the Gong family, with profound attainments in formations. The formation restrictions on Spirit Sword Peak could block others, but might not be able to stop Gong Yue.

If that were all, Yang Kai would have thought everything he saw was an illusion, a trick by Gong Yue to mislead them.

But that last dog's bark convinced him that Gong Yue had really gone to Qingyang Shrine, and was really on his Spirit Sword Peak.

Because there really was a small beast on Spirit Sword Peak, pitch black and somewhat like a little dog. Though a bit odd, it wasn't strong. Yang Kai had only seen it twice and hadn't paid much attention.

"Why would Gong Yue go to Qingyang Shrine? Is he seeking death?" Chen Wenhao frowned. Gong Yue's strength was what it was—even if he ran into all four of them, he had no chance of survival. Logically, he should be hiding, but instead he ran straight into Qingyang Shrine.

The Shrine was full of experts. What chance did Gong Yue have there?

"I can't reach them. Junior Brother, you try." Gao Xueting, who had been silent, took out her Elder Jade Token after learning Gong Yue might have gone to Qingyang Shrine, trying to contact her fellow disciples inside.

To her surprise, the Elder Jade Token didn't work at this time.

Hearing this, Yang Kai quickly took out his own Elder Jade Token and poured his Divine Sense into it.

After waiting a moment, he looked at Gao Xueting and slowly shook his head. "No response!"

Although this place was some distance from Qingyang Shrine, it wasn't so far as to make contact impossible. If any Elder inside had received the message, they would have replied. But in fact, both of them tried in turn, and there was no reply at all.

"Something's happened at the Shrine!" Gao Xueting's face changed.

"Let's go!" Feng Ming barked, realizing the seriousness of the situation.

Yang Kai immediately summoned his Flowing Cloud Shuttle. The four entered in single file, and the Flowing Cloud Shuttle turned into a streak of light and vanished.

After the four left, the disciples of the small sect finally relaxed, collapsing to the ground, backs drenched in cold sweat.

After waiting a while, making sure the four experts were truly gone and wouldn't return, the remaining dozens began to form factions to fight for the Sect Master position, blades drawn, and a fierce battle broke out.

Inside the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, both Yang Kai and Gao Xueting looked grim.

Chen Wenhao consoled them, "Though I don't know what Gong Yue is doing at the Shrine, he's basically walking into a trap. With Hall Master Wen presiding over the Shrine, Gong Yue can't stir up any trouble. Don't worry, perhaps by the time we arrive, Gong Yue will already have been executed."

Gao Xueting’s worry did not lessen; she only replied perfunctorily, “Let’s hope so.”

Observing their expressions, Yang Kai sensed something was wrong and quietly sent a voice transmission to Gao Xueting: "Did something happen at the Shrine? Is there something I don't know?"

Gao Xueting glanced at him and replied, "The Hall Master is currently preoccupied. If Gong Yue is really causing trouble inside, the Hall Master can't intervene."

## Chapter 2740 — The True Dragon Transformation Secret Technique

"I'm going to make a trip to Frozen Earth."

It had been half a month since Yang Kai's alchemy contest with Ji Ying. During these days, Yang Kai had been refining the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome. Suddenly, Zhu Qing came to find him and said this.

Yang Kai exclaimed, "That place is forbidden. Even as a member of the Dragon Clan, you might not make it out unscathed. Why do you want to go there?"

"You know why."

Yang Kai frowned, understanding Zhu Qing's intentions.

The reason she wanted to go to Frozen Earth was simply to retrieve her clansmen's remains. When Li Jiao revealed the origin of the Dragon Blood Flower, Zhu Qing's expression was a bit off. The Dragon that had fallen in Frozen Earth might have something to do with her.

"Didn't Li Jiao say the remains have long since turned to dust? There's no point in going." Yang Kai tried to persuade her. Although he'd been annoyed by this girl lately and wished she'd stay far away, he was still worried about her heading to a place like Frozen Earth.

A Dragon Clan member had already perished there. If Zhu Qing went, she might follow in her predecessor's footsteps.

"There's something else there," Zhu Qing said.

"What is it?"

Zhu Qing pondered for a moment, then answered honestly, "Origin power. The origin power of my Dragon Clan. I need to go there to find my clansman's origin power!"

Yang Kai understood. The Dragon Clan's origin is the origin of a Divine Spirit, and it couldn't possibly disappear so easily. Even if the remains had turned to dust, the origin must still exist.

He nodded, "Then go, but be careful. Frozen Earth isn't a place you can wander into recklessly."

Zhu Qing gazed at him quietly. "Will you come?"

Yang Kai immediately rolled his eyes. "I'm not going."

"Can't you accompany me?" Disappointment flashed in Zhu Qing's eyes.

Yang Kai snorted, "My strength is so low, going to Frozen Earth is a death sentence. If I go with you, I might just drag you down."

The key point was, there wasn't much benefit for him. Why would he go to Frozen Earth?

Zhu Qing thought seriously for a moment, then nodded, "You're right. I was forcing you. In that case, I'll go alone." She paused, "But you must stay here and not run around."

Yang Kai sneered, "Why should I stay here? As soon as you leave, I'll get far away from the Northern Territory. From then on, the sea is wide for fish to leap, the sky high for birds to fly—you'll never find me again."

Zhu Qing's face darkened instantly. "Then I'm not going."

Compared to her clansman's origin power, Yang Kai's affairs were far more important.

Yang Kai's mouth twitched. He wished he could slap himself. He quickly straightened up, "Just kidding. Don't worry, I'll wait for you in Lingxiao Palace."

"You're not lying?" Zhu Qing stared into Yang Kai's eyes, as if trying to see into his soul.

"What good would lying do? There's plenty to handle here in the Sect. As Palace Lord, I'm busy every day. I don't have time to run around." Yang Kai replied seriously.

"That's good." Zhu Qing nodded, finally at ease. Suddenly, she said, "By the way, I have a secret technique here. Try cultivating it—it's much better than the one you used before."

"Secret technique? What kind?" Yang Kai frowned.

Zhu Qing took out a jade slip and tossed it to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai caught it. He probed it with his Divine Sense, and his eyes instantly widened. "Dragon Transformation!"

Zhu Qing said, "I saw you use a similar Dragon Transformation secret technique before, but that one seemed very basic. It's not as good as this. If you can cultivate this technique, you can at least boost your combat power by twenty percent."

Yang Kai's eyes lit up with joy. "Really?"

Zhu Qing smiled, "Do you think I'm like you, always lying?"

Yang Kai blushed and changed the subject, "How does Dragon Isle have such a secret technique?"

The Dragon Clan only needed to activate their origin power to transform directly into a true dragon. What use did they have for a Dragon Transformation Secret Technique? Yang Kai was puzzled.

"Dragon Isle isn't only inhabited by the Dragon Clan. There are other beings as well," Zhu Qing said softly. "You'll understand when you get to Dragon Isle one day."

"We'll talk about it then." Yang Kai waved his hand, happily studying the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique in the jade slip. If it was as Zhu Qing said, once he mastered it, his combat power would soar.

"Take your time cultivating. I'm leaving," Zhu Qing said, turning to walk out.

"Don't die out there," Yang Kai suddenly called, looking at Zhu Qing's departing figure with solemn concern.

Zhu Qing smiled slightly, opened the door, and left. She felt quite pleased. With this Dragon Transformation Secret Technique for him to cultivate, she could delay him for a month or two. By then, she would surely have returned from Frozen Earth.

Outside the mountain gate, Zhu Qing transformed into a stream of light, flying toward the far north. Suddenly, she paused and looked back, vaguely sensing someone watching her.

Her gaze swept over the area, but no one was there. She shook her head and left even faster, disappearing in the blink of an eye.

"Sigh!" In the side room, Yang Kai sighed, feeling a bit guilty.

Zhu Qing wanted him to accompany her to Frozen Earth, but he refused. First, because Frozen Earth was too dangerous; second, he hoped she'd give up the idea. Who knew she'd still go?

"Stubborn to the end—maybe it's best if she just dies there," Yang Kai snorted, feeling rather gloomy.

But soon, that gloom vanished. He focused intently on the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique in his hands.

The Dragon Transformation Secret Technique he had before was taught to him years ago in the Hengluo Star Domain, on Yao Star Di Chen, by Chi Yue. When his strength was lower, the technique was quite effective—once used, his combat power soared. But now, as his strength had grown, it was often useless.

Looking over the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique Zhu Qing had given him, Yang Kai found it truly profound. Its essence was deep and mysterious, able to fully unleash the potential of the Dragon Clan's origin power and the secret treasures he'd refined within his body.

The previous Dragon Transformation was indeed much more basic.

The more Yang Kai studied, the more delighted he became, throwing himself wholeheartedly into it.

He even forgot his original plan to flee once Zhu Qing left.

Time flew by—another half month passed.

In the side room, Yang Kai suddenly opened his eyes and shouted, "Dragon Transformation!"

A high, resonant dragon roar sounded. The huge shadow of a Golden Sacred Dragon flashed behind him and merged into his body. The Secret Treasures he'd refined within also resonated instantly.

Crackling sounds echoed as his bones shifted. Yang Kai's face twisted in pain, and his sitting figure suddenly grew over three feet taller.

Crack, crack...

His hands transformed into massive dragon claws, far more realistic than before. The claws were sharp, like the finest blades, gleaming coldly. Scales covered his chest, spreading down to his waist.

These scales weren't formed from energy, but condensed from the Dragon Clan's origin power. They were as real as true dragon scales, indistinguishable to the naked eye.

There was a strange sensation on his head and tailbone.

Yang Kai touched his head and his eyes widened. He found two small lumps on either side, as if something was about to emerge.

Dragon horns? Yang Kai recalled the scene when he first met Zhu Qing, when she activated her Dragon Clan origin power, and suddenly understood.

The lumps on either side of his head should be dragon horns, but his cultivation of the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique wasn't complete, so they looked rather odd.

He touched his back, and his expression grew stranger.

To his surprise, a tiny dragon tail had appeared behind him...

How could he show himself like this?

After collecting himself, Yang Kai slowly stood up, only to find his height had more than doubled. He was never short to begin with, but now he towered so tall that only those with extraordinary gifts could compare. Anyone else standing before him would have no choice but to look up in awe.

He gently clenched his fists, feeling power surging through his hands, as if it could explode at any moment.

The sensation of power flooding his body filled Yang Kai with immense delight.

He waved his hand, and the air rippled, forming a mirror. Yang Kai examined his reflection and couldn't help but gasp.

His appearance was nothing short of terrifying—far beyond what could be described as fierce or menacing. Not only had his entire form changed dramatically, but he now radiated an aura of savage power from head to toe. Anyone timid enough to catch sight of him would surely flee in panic.

Especially his eyes, which had turned golden, just like when he used the World-Ending Demon Eye. They were filled with majesty. If he glared, even those familiar with him would be startled.

Yang Kai couldn't help but laugh heartily, very satisfied with his new look.

And this was still an incomplete Dragon Transformation Secret Technique. If he fully mastered the technique Zhu Qing gave him, the physical changes would be even more dramatic—he'd become even taller.

This secret technique was truly incomparable to the previous Dragon Transformation Secret Technique.

"Palace Lord, Palace Lord!" Hua Qingsi's voice suddenly came from outside.

Yang Kai's eyes turned, and he strode outside. His heavy steps thudded on the floor like war drums.

As he opened the door, Hua Qingsi's expression changed drastically. Her face paled. "What kind of monster are you!"

Before she finished speaking, she summoned her five-colored spear. Emperor Yuan surged, and the Imperial Treasure transformed into five-colored light, attacking Yang Kai.

Instinctively, Hua Qingsi felt this monster was dangerous, so she used her strongest killing move right away.

Yang Kai grinned wickedly and reached out with his dragon claw.

With one swipe, the five-colored light shattered, and the five-colored spear was caught in his hand, unable to move.

With her Imperial Treasure restrained, Hua Qingsi was terrified. She desperately tried to regain control.

The buzzing sound continued as the five-colored spear trembled in Yang Kai's hand, but it couldn't break free from his grasp.

Yang Kai let out a sinister laugh. “Heh heh heh… Your Palace Master has already been slain by this Lord! I will wipe out Lingxiao Palace—none of you will have a grave!”

The terror on Hua Qingsi's face suddenly vanished. She looked Yang Kai up and down and frowned, "Palace Lord, what are you doing? You really scared me."

## Chapter 3018 — Void Maze

Dragon Clan Secret Techniques were immensely powerful, but only those who possessed the Dragon Clan's bloodline could use them. These phantoms were clearly Secret Techniques left behind by Dragon Clan experts.

Such Secret Techniques could not have appeared in the Void Passage for no reason, nor did Yang Kai believe the Dragon Clan's defenses would be so stringent. After all, Dragon Isle lay at the other end of the Void Passage. Who would be suicidal enough to sneak inside?

Looking again at the Dragon Clan Secret Techniques left here, every one of them was clearly intended to take lives. If he had not possessed his Dragon Transformation form, he certainly would not still be standing here.

This was a trap!

It was definitely a trap aimed at someone, and one powerful enough to instantly kill an ordinary Third-Stage Emperor.

Was it aimed at him? The thought flashed through Yang Kai's mind, but he could not be certain.

While he was distracted, obscure words in the Dragon Language suddenly rang out from the direction of the dragon phantoms. The Dragon Language was the foundation supporting the Dragon Clan's Secret Techniques. At first, it sounded somewhat similar to the incantations of the Ancient World's Shamanic Arts, but it was even more awkward and abstruse, making it extremely difficult to understand.

The Dragon Language echoed continuously, and the dragon phantoms suddenly broke apart, but the voice did not stop.

Li Jiao trembled uncontrollably, instinctively terrified. He was already overcome with regret; if he had known this would happen, he would have turned back earlier.

The scattered dragon phantoms reassembled and abruptly transformed into a huge ancient character, descending over Yang Kai and Li Jiao like a vast net that blotted out the sky.

The two swiftly retreated, only to discover in horror that no matter how far they withdrew, they could not escape the ancient character's reach. The character was profoundly mysterious, resembling four characters meaning "upright" superimposed upon one another to form a perfectly square, regular shape.

In the blink of an eye, the ancient character enveloped them, and a strange Law Power began to fluctuate.

Li Jiao's face went pale, and his heart filled with despair.

Yang Kai, however, exclaimed softly in surprise, a strange light flashing through his eyes.

In the next instant, their vision blurred, and the two suddenly appeared in a world of nothingness. There was no light and no sound here, making it even more unsettling and frightening than the depths of the sea.

Yang Kai spread out his Divine Sense and detected Li Jiao nearby, so he reached out and pulled him over.

"Palace Master Yang... what is this place?" Li Jiao asked, swallowing hard.

"A Void Gap!" Yang Kai replied. He released the Dragon Transformation Art and returned to his original appearance.

The dragon phantoms from before had vanished. After the ancient character appeared, it had actually cast the two of them directly into a Void Gap.

"Ah!" Li Jiao paled in alarm.

He was naturally no stranger to Void Gaps. Even for an expert like him, they were extremely dangerous places. The slightest mishap could cause him to lose his way inside and never find an exit.

Yang Kai took out a fresh set of clothes from his Spatial Ring and put them on while carefully surveying his surroundings.

He had entered and left Void Gaps countless times, but this place gave him an extremely strange feeling, as though mysteries were hidden everywhere.

He tried circulating the Laws of Space and was relieved to find that they were not restricted. He felt that if he exerted his full strength, he should be able to break out of this Void Gap and return to the outside world, but there was no telling where he would emerge.

Therefore, he did not act hastily, because he needed to enter Dragon Isle.

This Void Gap could not have appeared without reason. Considering what they had just experienced, Yang Kai vaguely felt that this place should exist within the Void Passage. In other words, as long as he could find the exit from the Void Gap itself, he would be able to enter Dragon Isle smoothly.

That Dragon Clan Secret Technique was what had created this Void Gap.

This really was a trap! Yang Kai vaguely felt it had been aimed at him, yet that did not seem quite right.

Anyone who passed through the Void Passage would trigger this trap and be banished here, so perhaps he had simply encountered it by chance. After all, Dragon Isle was already so well hidden that its defenses should not have needed to be this stringent, much less require a Dragon Clan Secret Technique to be left in the Void Passage to deal with intruders.

If that was really the case, then he had even more reason not to tear space open recklessly. If he did, he would have no way to determine where he emerged and could not guarantee that he would enter Dragon Isle.

With this thought, Yang Kai formed a plan and sent Li Jiao a Divine Sense transmission. "Stay close to me. Undercurrents surge throughout this gap. If one sweeps you away, you will instantly be transported elsewhere, and even I may not be able to find you. There are also Void Rifts everywhere. If you touch one, you will be severely injured even if you don't die."

The Void Gap and Void Rifts were not the same thing. The vast expanse the two of them now occupied was a Void Gap, while Void Rifts were far smaller, some so subtle they were nearly impossible to detect. They were like razor-sharp blades suspended throughout the void. Brush against one, and if fortune favored you, you would merely be torn open; if not, you could be sliced clean in two.

Li Jiao clearly understood his predicament. In this damned place, only Yang Kai, who was proficient in the Power of Space, could keep him safe. He nodded heavily. "Understood."

Yang Kai immediately took the lead. The Laws of Space fluctuated around him as he probed their surroundings, carefully avoiding the various turbulent currents and Void Rifts. Li Jiao followed close behind, always remaining one body-length behind Yang Kai, his nerves stretched to the limit.

Yang Kai's direction of travel was erratic, sometimes veering left and sometimes right. Although there were no points of reference here and Li Jiao could not distinguish north, south, east, or west, Yang Kai's changes in direction were still obvious.

Sometimes, while they were walking, Yang Kai would even suddenly turn around and head back the way they had come.

Li Jiao remained silent throughout, not daring to disturb him.

After an unknown amount of time, Yang Kai suddenly stopped, an utterly baffled expression on his face.

"Palace Master Yang... did you find the exit?" Li Jiao asked cautiously.

"No." Yang Kai shook his head.

"Then..." Li Jiao looked at him questioningly.

"We're lost," Yang Kai replied.

"What?" Li Jiao was startled. "L-Lost?"

He almost thought he had misheard. The surrounding space was nothing but chaotic nothingness, without any road to speak of, so how could they be lost? However, since Yang Kai said so, he must have his reasons. In a place like this, Yang Kai was the authority.

"We were here earlier." Yang Kai let out a soft breath.

Li Jiao scratched his head and spread out his Divine Sense to inspect both sides. He thought this damned place looked the same everywhere, without the slightest difference as far as he could tell. The fact that Yang Kai could actually recognize it was almost unbelievable.

"This is a maze." A strange light flashed in Yang Kai's eyes. Had he not seen it for himself, he would never have believed that such a profound maze could exist within a Void Gap.

The entire maze was enormous, using the Void Undercurrents as paths and the many Void Rifts as barriers, dividing a vast region into a maze from which no exit could be found.

What shocked Yang Kai even more was that this maze was not fixed, but constantly changing. The surging undercurrents and flowing cracks meant that any single change could alter the entire maze.

Who had arranged it? Who possessed such astonishing ability?

Dragon Isle? If it was Dragon Isle, why had they arranged such a maze here, and whom were they trying to trap?

Suddenly, something Li Jiao had said earlier surfaced in Yang Kai's mind.

More than twenty years ago, the Beast Great Emperor had once caused an enormous disturbance on Dragon Isle!

Cold sweat broke out across Yang Kai's brow. This maze... might have been designed to deal with the Beast Great Emperor. Only an arrangement of such immense scale could trap a Great Emperor, and only a Great Emperor would warrant Dragon Isle constructing such a maze.

As for him, he had merely been caught up in an undeserved calamity.

Upon realizing this, Yang Kai's spirits suddenly rose again.

It seemed his previous guess had been correct. As long as he could unravel this maze, he should be able to enter Dragon Isle, because this Void Maze existed with the Void Passage as its foundation.

The maze's exit should coincide with the passage's exit.

Yang Kai set off again, carefully observing the changes around him as a blueprint gradually took shape in his mind, a blueprint of the entire maze.

He believed that once he completed this blueprint, he should be able to find a route out of the maze.

Li Jiao did not know what Yang Kai had thought of. He only knew that Yang Kai's pace suddenly quickened considerably, so he could only grit his teeth and keep up.

Time passed swiftly. Although there was no sun, moon, or stars here, making it impossible to determine precisely how much time had elapsed, Li Jiao felt that at least a month had gone by.

Throughout that month, Yang Kai wandered aimlessly through this place, occasionally stopping to observe the surroundings, mutter something under his breath, and then set off again.

Although there had not been a single battle during this month, Li Jiao had narrowly escaped death several times.

The Void Undercurrents appeared and vanished unpredictably. Once, one swept him up, almost transporting him elsewhere. Fortunately, Yang Kai reacted quickly and pulled him back. Otherwise, he might have remained trapped here for the rest of his life.

He certainly had no confidence that he could find his way out.

Over the course of that month, Yang Kai's expression also grew increasingly solemn.

He discovered that he had still underestimated this Void Maze. The blueprint in his mind had been overturned and redrawn time and again, yet he had made little progress.

Given his attainments in the Power of Space, leaving this place would not be difficult. As long as he could tear open the space here, he would be able to return to the Star Realm.

But doing so would mean giving up on entering Dragon Isle, and that was not what he intended.

On this day, Yang Kai and Li Jiao were still walking through the Void Maze in search of the exit.

Li Jiao had already fallen into utter despair, believing he had no hope of setting foot on Dragon Isle in this lifetime. He merely followed Yang Kai mechanically, stepping only where Yang Kai had stepped.

Suddenly, Yang Kai's figure halted, and his Emperor Yuan began surging.

Li Jiao was startled and immediately circulated his Emperor Yuan as well.

He thought another Void Undercurrent was surging toward them.

However, Yang Kai did not move quickly as he usually did. Instead, he stood there, staring blankly ahead.

Li Jiao frowned and peered in the direction Yang Kai was looking, then his mouth fell open.

Not far away in the void, a figure sat cross-legged, wondrous Laws surging around his body and preventing any of the surrounding undercurrents from approaching.

Someone was here?

There was actually someone in this damned place?

Li Jiao could hardly believe his eyes.

However, he soon noticed something unusual. This person was not truly alive, but instead resembled a lingering image, because his entire body emitted a faint glow and he possessed no body of flesh and blood.

## Chapter 3112 — The Dagger Revealed

Others might not have caught the hidden meaning in those words, but as Su Yan's Master in Yunxia Sect, how could Ruan Biting fail to understand? When Su Yan came over from the Star Domain, she had brought with her a Void King-Grade Secret Treasure, a longsword named the Profound Frost Divine Sword. One of its sword techniques was called Snow Like Clear Sky.

That single sentence was enough to confirm Yang Kai's identity. If their relationship were ordinary, how could he have named all of this so readily? Ruan Biting had also heard of Yang Kai from Su Yan. After all, the two were Master and Disciple and had spoken openly with each other, though only today had she finally seen him in person.

"Boy, come with me!" Once Ruan Biting confirmed Yang Kai's identity, she reached out and grabbed at him.

She had only just received a quiet message from a disciple saying that Senior Sister Su Yan's husband had come from Hengluo Star Domain to look for her, only for Elder Chen to take him to the side hall to wait and drink tea. Ruan Biting had known at once that something was wrong and had rushed over in a panic, but in the end she still had not managed to stop the tragedy from happening.

She was also rather annoyed. She had already warned him to put down that teacup, and had even tried to snatch it away, yet this boy had ignored her completely and instead drained the tea right in front of her.

Su Yan, ah, Su Yan, what kind of dog-shit husband did you find? With a brain like that, how had he survived until now? Had no adult ever taught him not to eat and drink random things while away from home?

Her slender jade hand clamped around Yang Kai's wrist and pulled hard, but to her surprise, she failed to move him. Instead, she nearly stumbled.

Ruan Biting's beautiful eyes narrowed, her shock deepening. She was a Second Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator. Even if she had not used her full strength, surely she should not be unable to pull him, right?

Yang Kai smiled at her. He had originally lost every last shred of goodwill toward Yunxia Sect and had only been waiting to find out Su Yan's exact situation before going on a killing spree. But now it seemed that in this place, there was still one person who truly cared about Su Yan.

That was enough, was it not? The world was not entirely wicked after all. There was always a little warmth, like a light in the darkness, shining all around.

He gently patted Ruan Biting's hand, signaling for her to let go.

Ruan Biting frowned. "What are you doing?" Her Divine Sense surged as she transmitted, "This place is dangerous. Why are you still not leaving?"

Seeing that she was genuinely concerned for him, Yang Kai felt a warmth in his heart as well. He and this woman were complete strangers, yet because of his relationship with Su Yan, she cared for him this much. Clearly, she truly treated Su Yan well.

"Elder Ruan, what are you doing?" The red-faced Elder Chen looked at Ruan Biting with a cold smile, showing none of the respect a weaker cultivator ought to show an expert. Although they were both Elders of Yunxia Sect, she was merely an idle recluse with no authority or influence in the Sect. If not for the old Sect Master's instructions back then, she could never have lived in Yunxia Sect for so many years while still carrying the empty title of Elder.

Very few people knew the true relationship between Ruan Biting and Yunxia Sect. There were only rumors that she had once done the old Sect Master a kindness, which was why she had remained in Yunxia Sect all this time. However, ever since the old Sect Master passed away and the current Sect Master took power, Ruan Biting had been pushed completely to the margins.

"This has nothing to do with you!" Ruan Biting snapped, glaring at Elder Chen.

Elder Chen smiled pleasantly. "That is hardly appropriate. I am receiving this Young Brother by the Sect Master's order. Elder Ruan, acting like this is rude to our guest. Please withdraw at once."

Ruan Biting ignored him and looked at Yang Kai. "Are you leaving or not?"

"How can I leave before this matter is settled?" Yang Kai smiled faintly, then looked at Elder Chen. "Elder Chen seems quite pleased. May I ask what you are laughing about?"

"Naturally, I am laughing at something worth laughing at." Elder Chen's face filled with mockery. "How does my Yunxia Sect's Yunxia Tea taste?"

"The tea is decent, but the flavor is impure." Only then did Yang Kai put down the teacup. He toyed with it a little and said, "You put something in it, didn't you?"

"It is too late to realize that now!" Elder Chen's face suddenly turned cold, all his earlier false courtesy vanishing. Once struck by that poison, Yang Kai would soon become weak and powerless. Even a Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator could not easily resolve it. At this moment, in Elder Chen's eyes, Yang Kai was already fish on the chopping block.

"Elder Chen seems very confident in that thing." Yang Kai lowered his head, his hanging hair veiling his face and inexplicably giving him a sinister air.

"You... knew there was poison in the tea all along?" Ruan Biting suddenly realized something and cried out in shock, while inwardly cursing without end. Did this fellow have a hole in his head?

"Slurp..."

In the tense, drawn-sword atmosphere, a strange sound suddenly rang out. The three of them turned and saw Liu Yan holding another teacup, draining its tea in one gulp.

Elder Chen and Ruan Biting were both dumbstruck. Was this what they meant by keeping good company makes one good, and keeping bad company makes one bad? Yang Kai was sick in the head, and this little girl seemed no better.

"We already said the tea was poisoned, yet you still drank it." Yang Kai rubbed Liu Yan's head.

"I wanted a taste," Liu Yan replied casually. "It does not taste good. Here, you can have it back!"

Her small mouth opened, and a stream of tea shot straight toward Elder Chen's face like a sharp arrow. While still in midair, the tea suddenly turned crimson, like a fiery snake.

The temperature inside the hall abruptly soared.

Elder Chen's face changed drastically. Instinctively, he felt a sense of crisis envelop him, and without thinking, he summoned a Secret Treasure to guard his face. With a clang, the tea arrow struck the shield-like Secret Treasure squarely, making its light flash wildly before it dimmed all at once. Its spirituality had actually been destroyed. The fiery-red tea arrow continued on without losing momentum and directly melted a hole through the Secret Treasure.

"Ah!" Elder Chen screamed and staggered to the ground. When he looked down, he saw a pitch-black hole in his shoulder, fresh blood gurgling out. At the same time, an extremely terrifying Fire Attribute force surged wildly from the wound, and everywhere it passed, his meridians, flesh, and blood were burned away.

His eyes suddenly bulged.

How could this be? How could this be?

Had they not been poisoned? How could they still use their strength? And just what in the world was this little girl? A casual tea arrow from her mouth actually possessed such power.

Ruan Biting was stunned as well, staring blankly at Liu Yan. If she had not seen it with her own eyes, she would have found it very hard to believe such a little girl could casually... no, with one casual spit, heavily injure Elder Chen! Looking again at Yang Kai, she saw that he had already risen to his full height, his expression as normal as ever. Where was there the slightest trace of poisoning?

"You... are fine?" Ruan Biting had nearly lost the ability to think.

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "Mere trash. What can it do to me?"

Tra... trash! That was Yunxia Sect's strongest poison, yet he had actually called it trash. Had he really come from Hengluo Star Domain?

What she did not know was that Yang Kai had cultivated the Dragon Transformation Art, and after fusing with the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power, he already possessed the Dragon Clan bloodline. The Dragon Clan was publicly acknowledged to have powerful resistance against all kinds of negative energy, and poison was one of them.

With Yang Kai's current body, it could not be said that he was immune to ten thousand poisons, but any poison capable of affecting him definitely could not be found in the Ancestral Domain. He had drunk the tea calmly not because he was stupid, nor because there was something wrong with his head, but because he had nothing to fear.

"Elder, with you holding on like this, I will have no way to explain myself to Su Yan later. If she saw this, she would think I was flirting with women outside again." Yang Kai turned to Ruan Biting and winked.

Only then did Ruan Biting realize that she had been holding his wrist all this time without letting go. She could not help giving him an annoyed look, her face somewhat red, and hurriedly released him.

In today's matter, she really had lost her composure somewhat.

Shua, shua, shua, shua...

Figure after figure abruptly appeared. The many Elders of Yunxia Sect, who had long been lying in ambush outside and waiting, had heard Elder Chen's miserable cry and come to investigate. With one look, they were all dumbstruck.

Yang Kai was unharmed. Instead, Elder Chen seemed quite badly injured.

Han Zhengyuan said coldly, "Elder Ruan, do you need to give me an explanation?"

He had also seen Ruan Biting rush in just now, but had not had time to stop her. Now, seeing Elder Chen injured, he thought Ruan Biting had attacked him. This woman... he had endured her long enough. Today, no matter what, he would sweep her out the door!

Ruan Biting said, "Han Zhengyuan, don't go too far!"

Han Zhengyuan said, "Who exactly is going too far? As an Elder of Yunxia Sect, you actually side with outsiders. How can you face the members of Yunxia Sect, and how can you face the old Sect Master's instructions?"

"We can talk about your matters later. I only want to know this: I merely came to find Su Yan, so why target me like this?" Yang Kai swept his cold gaze around, then stopped on Han Zhengyuan's face.

This could not possibly have been Elder Chen acting on his own initiative. Clearly, he had received Han Zhengyuan's instructions.

For the master of a Sect to make an enemy for himself for no reason, such narrow-mindedness was truly laughable.

"Because Su Yan is not in Yunxia Sect at all. Everything he said earlier was meant to deceive you," Ruan Biting explained.

Yang Kai asked, "Where is she?"

"She was sent out to guard the Fire Cloud Mine and has not returned to the Sect for more than ten years. It is a mine under Yunxia Sect's jurisdiction and one of the Sect's foundations."

"Guard the Fire Cloud Mine!" Yang Kai's brow rose, and the worry that had just seized him eased again. If that was all, then Su Yan should not be in any danger. But very quickly, he understood the key point and coldly snorted. "Why?"

The Fire Cloud Mine was surely filled with abundant Fire Qi, making it extremely suitable for those who cultivated Fire Attribute Secret Arts and techniques. If someone who cultivated that kind of art received such a duty, they would certainly be delighted.

But Su Yan cultivated an Ice Attribute Secret Art! Moreover, she possessed the Ice-Crystal Yu Body. Staying year-round in a place filled with abundant Fire Qi, forget cultivating; she would be fortunate if she could keep her cultivation from falling.

Yang Kai did not believe Yunxia Sect was unaware of Su Yan's attribute. Since they knew, yet had still sent her to guard the Fire Cloud Mine, it was clearly intentional.

Damn it! Ten years was not long, but it was absolutely not short either! In other words, Su Yan had wasted ten years for nothing! Yang Kai was furious.

"Why?" Ruan Biting sneered, her heart turning cold. "Naturally, because they wanted to force her to yield. You may not know this, but someone's son is quite devoted to Su Yan." After a pause, she said, "And... Su Yan grew too quickly. Certain people were afraid her cultivation would surpass their own and threaten their position!"

"Nonsense!" Han Zhengyuan coldly snorted. "Going to guard the Fire Cloud Mine was something you Master and Disciple both agreed to. Why are you now coming here to slander this Sect Master?"

Ruan Biting shook her head, disappointment written across her face. “Han Zhengyuan, you are the Sect Master, yet you cannot tolerate anyone better than yourself. In that regard, you are far inferior to the old Sect Master.” If that had not been the case, she would not have been pushed aside all these years.

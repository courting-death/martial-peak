## Chapter 3129 — Ambush

A man spent several dozen years away from home, surrounded by a dazzling world and beautiful women. How could he never make a mistake? Dong Suzhu knew what sort of person Yang Kai was. The only reason she had made such a fuss was to ask on Su Yan's behalf, and in truth, she had long since guessed what Su Yan's attitude would be.

It was not that she was scheming. It was simply that, as Yang Kai's mother, she had to make a show of standing up for Su Yan.

Su Yan smiled faintly. "Although my three sisters and I do not mind, Junior Brother, you should also know that not just any woman can enter the Yang Family. She must have a clean background and a kind nature. Otherwise, once you bring her home, what you enjoy will not be the blessings of a harmonious household, but trouble you invited upon yourself. Junior Brother, isn't that right?"

She suddenly took on the air of a proper eldest sister, leaving Yang Kai rather unused to it. He quickly nodded. "Senior Sister is right."

Su Yan said, "Father and Mother happen to be here, so tell us about the sisters in the Star Realm. Father and Mother can help you judge them."

Dong Suzhu said solemnly, "Exactly. If there is anything you ought to say, say it now. Do not even think of hiding it, or when the time comes, I will make sure you die an ugly death."

Fourth Master Yang glared at him from the side as well.

It looked like a three-court interrogation.

Yang Kai felt pressure as heavy as a mountain. He wiped the sweat from his forehead, raised one finger, and said, "Just one. It isn't as many as you're imagining."

"Only one?" All three of them were stunned.

This answer was far beyond their expectations. They had thought that after so many years outside, Yang Kai must have provoked quite a few women no matter what. Who would have thought there was actually only one? It seemed he had exercised considerable restraint. For a man away from home, that was already quite rare.

Dong Suzhu said, "What sort of woman is she? Tell us."

Yang Kai said, "She isn't human."

"Not human?" Fourth Master Yang's eyes lit up. "Could she be a yao?"

Tsk, tsk, tsk. The boy had rather strong tastes. Then again, women of the yao race did have a different sort of charm from human women. How enviable! This rotten boy truly had ridiculous luck with women.

"She isn't yao either..."

Dong Suzhu frowned. "Neither human nor yao. Could she be some plant that gained sentience?" She slapped the chair. Damn it, how desperate could he be? Even if a sentient plant did have the chance to take human form, how could he join with one? He might as well pick a yao woman at random.

"She's a dragon!" Yang Kai said, steeling himself.

The room fell silent for several breaths. Dong Suzhu stared at him in shock. "What dragon?"

"A dragon. Literally." Yang Kai explained, and seeing that they still did not seem to understand, he continued, "There is a Dragon Clan in the Star Realm, the foremost of the Ancient Divine Spirits from our ancient legends. Mm, I had a little accident with one of their dragon women, and then..."

Dong Suzhu and Fourth Master Yang both sucked in a cold breath. At last, they understood what Yang Kai meant.

A dragon! That was a truly legendary existence. Among the Ancient Divine Spirits, dragons and phoenixes reigned supreme, but in the present Star Realm, such powerful races could not be seen at all. Their majestic bearing could only occasionally be glimpsed in ancient records.

Their own son had actually won over a dragon woman? It was almost impossible to believe.

Dong Suzhu swallowed. "That dragon woman... young lady. Does she have family?"

Yang Kai said, "The Dragon Clan of the Star Realm has roughly twenty clansmen, all living in a place called Dragon Isle."

"Twenty Dragon Clan members!" Dong Suzhu was stunned. One giant dragon was already hard to imagine. What sort of scene would more than twenty Dragon Clan members gathered together make? Her mind could hardly keep up.

"You actually came back alive." Fourth Master Yang broke out in cold sweat as he sized Yang Kai up in amazement.

Although he did not know what sort of existence the Dragon Clan was in the Star Realm, or what status they held, they were definitely not low. They might even be the most powerful race there. Yang Kai, a mere human, had subdued a dragon woman and somehow had not been beaten to death. That was simply outrageous good fortune.

Yang Kai grinned. "I still have some say on Dragon Isle." Strength was the right to speak.

Dong Suzhu fell silent for a while, then suddenly said, "No matter what your dragon woman is like, if she wants to enter my Yang Family, she must acknowledge Su Yan as the Mistress of the household."

Su Yan said, "Mother, among sisters, there is no higher or lower rank. We are all one family."

Dong Suzhu shook her head. "It is different. Different. She is a dragon woman. This must be made clear to her in advance." She looked at Yang Kai. "Do you understand?"

Yang Kai nodded again and again. "I understand, I understand."

"What if she refuses?"

Yang Kai raised his fist. "Then I'll beat her until she agrees!"

Dong Suzhu slapped him. "So now you hit women too!"

Ten days later, Yang Kai stood before Lingxiao Sect's Space Array, both eyes closed as he silently sensed his surroundings.

For these ten days, he had stayed on Jade Tree Peak with Su Yan, accompanying his parents. He had spent so many years running about outside and had not fulfilled his filial duties, so now that he had returned, he naturally had to make up for it. If Dong Suzhu had not urged him to leave, he likely would have stayed even longer.

It was not that Dong Suzhu did not want to keep Yang Kai longer, but the situation in the Star Domain was strange and treacherous now, with war raging everywhere. Leaving others aside, their lives and deaths had nothing to do with Yang Kai, but Shan Qingluo and the others were still on their respective stars, so how could Yang Kai ignore them?

By today, Dong Suzhu had driven Yang Kai away, telling him to set out and look in on his three wives. Yang Kai gladly agreed. To be honest, he missed Xia Ningshang and the other two terribly as well.

The situation on Dark Star had already been settled. The invading enemies had been wiped out, and not one had escaped. After this battle, the Great Wilderness Star Domain would presumably not dare invade again before thoroughly investigating Dark Star's circumstances.

Moreover, Su Yan remained here to hold the fort. Even if more foreign enemies came, they would certainly be made to come and never return.

When Yang Kai left the Star Domain back then, he had laid down no fewer than a hundred Space Arrays throughout it, nearly linking the whole Star Domain together. But over the dozen-plus years of spreading war, many Space Arrays had been destroyed, and some had been seized by cultivators from the Great Wilderness Star Domain. As a result, the great forces of the Star Domain could not support one another quickly and effectively, and could only fight alone. Otherwise, they would not have been so passive.

Lingxiao Sect's Space Array was the starting point of the entire Star Domain's Space Array network, so it had been targeted the most. The transmission channels connecting Lingxiao Sect to the outside world had almost all been destroyed or occupied, nearly severing Dark Star's contact with the outside world.

Yang Kai had arranged these Space Arrays himself, so naturally he had absolute authority over them.

His Divine Sense surged, the Laws of Space rippled, and Yang Kai released his strength.

Although this was a Lower Plane Star Domain, he was Dark Star's Star Lord. On Dark Star, he could use a bit of his power without worrying about being rejected by the World Laws.

A moment later, he opened his eyes. He had sensed a spatial corridor that could be used for transmission.

A group of people stood not far away, watching him.

Yang Kai said to Su Yan, "Senior Sister, I will leave things here to you."

Su Yan nodded. "Rest assured!" She now had the foundation of the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Although she had also suppressed her cultivation, a cultivator at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm was absolutely no match for her. With her holding the fort on Dark Star, there was nothing to worry about.

"Come back soon!" Dong Suzhu urged, reluctant to part.

Yang Kai nodded and drew a deep breath. As the Laws surged, light flashed over the array, and his figure vanished.

On a Dead Star hundreds of millions of li away from Dark Star, deep underground in a mine tunnel, a Space Array suddenly gave off a faint glow.

Several pairs of eyes opened in the silent darkness, all turning toward the array.

These people were all Void King Realm experts from the Great Wilderness Star Domain. They had been lying in wait here precisely to guard the stump and wait for rabbits, hunting down cultivators who transmitted over from Dark Star. They had succeeded several times before. Since Dark Star was under siege, it naturally had to send people out with messages.

But all of those messengers had been cut down here, and several Teleportation Tokens Yang Kai had specially made back then had fallen into their hands.

The Space Arrays Yang Kai arranged could not be used by just anyone. To transmit, one had to hold a Teleportation Token he had made. Otherwise, one would simply be exiled into the endless Void.

With those several Teleportation Tokens, it had become much more convenient for the cultivators of the Great Wilderness Star Domain to move about. They could even transmit directly into Lingxiao Sect. But there were too few Teleportation Tokens, and they did not have the nerve to do that, so they merely left those Teleportation Tokens and some Space Arrays for passing intelligence. Even that was extremely convenient.

However, no one had come to this place for several years. Clearly, Dark Star knew its messengers had been killed and no longer made futile attempts, lest they hand the enemy more advantages.

Yet today, someone actually dared to repeat the same mistake.

In the darkness, those pairs of eyes gleamed with bloodthirsty light. They quietly restrained their auras and swiftly surrounded the Space Array from every direction.

A figure suddenly appeared atop the Space Array, a handsome, spirited young man.

His figure had only just taken shape when five black shadows pounced toward him like hunting leopards. Each one brimmed with killing intent and displayed extraordinary methods. In an instant, the pitch-black mine tunnel lit up as bright as day, and chaotic energy surged everywhere.

Some of the five men were calm, while others grinned savagely. The way they looked at Yang Kai was like looking at a dead man.

It was not because they thought themselves invincible. All five were only at the First Stage of the Void King Realm, and not one of them had even reached the Second Stage. With such a lineup, even joining hands against a Second Stage cultivator would be difficult, let alone a Third Stage.

But how far was Dark Star from this place? After such an extremely long-distance transmission, anyone would suffer a brief daze while adjusting. Even someone at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm could not avoid it.

That adjustment might last only a few breaths, but in a life-or-death battle, a few breaths were enough for them to cut the newcomer down a thousand times over.

They struck without the slightest mercy, determined to kill the newcomer on the spot.

This was how they had always done it, and they had never once failed.

They did not care whether Yang Kai lived or died. What they cared about was the Teleportation Token he carried. Anyone who could use this kind of array would certainly have a Teleportation Token on them, and the Great Wilderness Star Domain was currently collecting such tokens. Once they gathered ten, they would assemble ten peak experts and transmit directly into Dark Star, or into the headquarters of the top forces on other Cultivation Stars, and strike straight at the heart.

## Chapter 3126 — Feeding the Tiger with One's Own Body

Cups passed from hand to hand, goblets crossed in endless toasts. Jar after jar of fine wine was drained, body after body toppled over, and snores thundered across the square.

Under ordinary circumstances, cultivators naturally would not get drunk from wine. A few casual circulations of their Secret Arts would be enough to dissolve the intoxication. But this was a celebration. The wine did not make men drunk; men let themselves be drunk. Who would do something so mood-killing at a time like this?

They drank when they woke and slept when they were drunk. Several thousand cultivators hurried about clinking cups, as if they had pushed all the troubles of the world to the backs of their minds. Today, in this moment, their hearts were open and unburdened; only drinking to their fill would do.

Inside the great hall, Yang Kai sat upright in the seat of honor as one person after another came forward to toast him.

Yang Kai turned no one away. When wine was offered, he drank, giving every guest ample face and driving the atmosphere of the victory banquet and welcome-home feast ever higher.

First came the older generation of cultivators, followed by the younger generation.

He was not especially familiar with the older generation, but among the younger cultivators present, many had crossed paths with Yang Kai before.

Wei Guchang and his wife Xuan'er of Yingyue Hall, Daiyuan and Yin Sudie of Liuli Sect, and Shen Shitao and Lu Ying of Qiantian Sect had all shared life-and-death experiences with him, and their relationships had once been close.

Yet when they met again after several dozen years, an extra layer of distance stood between them, and they no longer had the easy familiarity of the past.

That layer of distance was called Realm!

The enormous gulf in cultivation and status made it impossible for them to connect the Yang Kai before them with the man from several dozen years ago. Without anyone meaning it to happen, a certain restraint had settled between them.

On the Great Dao, there were friends who travelled beside you, but when you suddenly looked back, many familiar figures had already vanished from sight.

Even so, this was a precious memory, one that could be buried deep in the heart, taken out now and then for remembrance, and met with a proud smile. Look, that great man who can order Heaven and Earth once ran beside me on the road of the Martial Dao. It was only a pity that I could not keep up with his stride and was soon left far, far behind. But no matter what, that experience would never be forgotten. It was destined to become a memory shared between them.

"Sect Master, let me toast you. Back then, if not for Sect Master, I fear I would have died long ago. Everything I have today is thanks to Sect Master's support along the way. I am endlessly grateful."

Looking at the woman before him, Yang Kai smiled faintly, raised his cup, and drank. Then he said, "Yun'er is doing very well now."

Huang Juan trembled and stared blankly at Yang Kai.

"I only saw her a few days ago. She has grown up, become an adult." Yang Kai's voice was not loud, but he had been the focus of the entire hall all along. The moment he spoke, the great hall fell silent, everyone listening either openly or in secret.

"Grown up..." Yearning rose in Huang Juan's eyes, as if she were imagining what that little girl, the one who had been so reluctant to leave her back then, looked like now as an adult. Lin Yun'er was not her biological daughter, but the daughter of a friend. That friend and her husband had suffered a sudden calamity and died unnatural deaths, so Huang Juan had taken Lin Yun'er in and raised her as her own. Later, after meeting Yang Kai, they had been brought into Lingxiao Sect; later still, Lin Yun'er had been taken away by Yang Yan.

Huang Juan covered her mouth. "As long as she's safe." Tears, however, poured down her face. She wiped them away hard, but one stream followed another. She was alone in the world, and the person she worried about most was Lin Yun'er. It was just that, in the current atmosphere, it truly was not appropriate to ask Yang Kai about her.

She had not expected Yang Kai to bring it up on his own.

"Her current strength is no lower than mine!" Yang Kai smiled faintly. "She took a Great Emperor as her Master. In that world, there are very few people who would dare provoke her."

Great Emperor!

Everyone pricked up their ears, not knowing what that title meant.

Back then, there had indeed been a Starry Sky Emperor in the Star Domain, and many of those present had even seen her face. She was an extraordinary woman who roamed the universe unmatched. Could it be... that Lin Yun'er's Master was her?

Yang Kai lifted his wine jar, slowly stood, swept his gaze around, and said in a clear voice, "All of you may be curious about my current cultivation realm."

Of course they were curious. When Yang Kai left the Star Domain back then, he had already been at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. After so many years wandering outside, he could not possibly have advanced only to the Third Stage of the Void King Realm. There were quite a few Void King Realm cultivators present, yet none of them could sense Yang Kai's depths. This was not merely because he was the Star Lord. There had to be another reason, a more fundamental reason: Yang Kai's cultivation surpassed theirs by far, far too much.

It was just that no one dared ask him about it. Even Ye Xijun had never asked.

Was the answer about to be revealed now?

Everyone looked toward him without blinking, holding their breath. In the great hall, the sound of heartbeats could actually be heard, wave after wave.

"Above the Void King Realm is the Dao Origin Realm, and above Dao Origin is Emperor. I am now at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm!"

Dao Origin Realm, Emperor Realm! These two brand-new terms struck everyone's minds once more, like two great hammers shattering their shackles, opening a new door, and revealing a new world.

"The place I went to these years is called the Star Realm! It is a higher plane than the Star Domain. In that place, the Void King Realm amounts to nothing. Only those in the Dao Origin Realm barely qualify to establish a foothold, and only those in the Emperor Realm have the ability to roam the world unhindered." Yang Kai's tone shifted. "Of course, if you're too arrogant, you'll still get killed."

Many people laughed, and the tense, solemn atmosphere eased considerably. Yet Yang Kai's few words had raised stormy waves in every heart. This was especially true of the Void King Realm cultivators. They had thought that after climbing all this way, they had reached the summit, able to look down on all the smaller mountains and overlook the countless living beings below.

But when they raised their heads, they discovered two even higher peaks not far away, their shadows falling over them. The sight left them powerless, yet at the same time incomparably stirred.

Climb them, ascend to the summit, behold that broader world, and gaze upon even more beautiful scenery.

Their throats went dry.

"Emperor is not the strongest. In the Star Realm, there are ten Great Emperors above Emperor Realm. With a turn of the hand they can summon clouds and rain, and their power is so great that even I cannot imagine it."

Everyone held their breath again.

Emperor Realm was already a distant, unreachable dream. What kind of bearing, then, must those ten Great Emperors above the Emperor Realm possess?

They could not imagine it. They did not dare imagine it.

Yang Kai turned to Huang Juan and said, "Yun'er's Master is Iron Blood Great Emperor!"

Everyone stiffly turned their heads and looked at Huang Juan with boundless envy.

Earlier, when they heard Yang Kai say that Lin Yun’er had taken a Great Emperor as her Master, they had not had any clear sense of what that meant. After all, none of them knew what a Great Emperor was. But after his explanation, everyone understood that Great Emperors were clearly that small group of people standing at the very peak.

That woman called Lin Yun'er, what virtue or ability did she possess to be able to take a Great Emperor as her Master?

And with such a heaven-reaching backer, who in the Star Realm would dare provoke her? Who could compare with such an opportunity? Lin Yun'er could truly be said to have ascended to the heavens in a single step. Even in the Star Realm, she could walk wherever she pleased.

Taking in everyone's shocked expressions, Yang Kai smiled faintly. "The Star Realm is not beyond reach."

Everyone's vision blurred, and Yang Kai suddenly vanished.

Only after a moment of silence did everyone come back to themselves.

What had Yang Kai meant by that last sentence? Why did it stir so many thoughts? Could it be that they, too, had a chance to set foot in the Star Realm?

Right. He had gone to the Star Realm several dozen years ago, and since he could return now, he could certainly go there again. Perhaps he could even bring some people with him.

Once that thought arose, no one could remain calm. They all turned to look at Ye Xijun, only to be shocked by what they saw. Others had already thought of this, and at that moment Ye Xijun was surrounded three layers deep inside and out by people asking about the method of entering the Star Realm.

Ye Xijun raised a hand to her forehead, feeling nothing but a constant buzzing by her ears, as if countless flies were clamoring around her. When something could no longer be endured, there was no need to endure it: "I know nothing whatsoever. If you lot dare make any more noise, do not blame me for turning hostile!"

The scene cleared at once.

Ye Xijun snorted, grabbed a wine jar, tipped back her head, and drank. The watching Void King Realm cultivators were dumbfounded. After so many years of knowing her, they had never seen Ye Xijun drink wine, yet now she was so bold and free that they almost wanted to clap and cheer.

...

In Lingxiao Sect, atop Jade Tree Peak, Yang Kai leisurely appeared before a house and raised his hand to knock on the door.

After a long while, the door opened, and Fourth Master Yang appeared before Yang Kai, his clothes disheveled and his hair a mess.

"Father..." The smile on Yang Kai's face froze. He stared for a moment at the red marks on Fourth Master Yang's face and neck, then looked around with extreme vigilance. "Father, are you keeping a mistress hidden away? Does Mother know? How could you be so muddleheaded? If Mother discovers this, I'm afraid both you and I will be in mortal danger! Who is the woman inside?"

"It's your old mother!" came a sharp, feminine shout.

Yang Kai tucked in his neck.

Fourth Master Yang glared at him. His mouth moved, but no sound came out. Judging from the shape of his lips, however, he was saying, "Boy, you're dead for sure." Then he moved to shut the door.

Cold sweat streamed down Yang Kai's forehead. He shifted his foot, blocking the door before it could close, grabbed his old father's arm with one hand, and looked at him with utter sincerity. "Save me!"

Fourth Master Yang glared.

Yang Kai said, "Father, you can't just watch me die. I'm your only son. Can you really bear to see me suffer?"

Since returning this time, he had not gone to visit his parents at all. Instead, he had disappeared again for an entire month. No wonder his old mother was so furious. It was just that Yang Kai had not expected to be delayed for so long over at the Earth's Core. He had thought he would casually deal with a few invading enemies and return very quickly, but who could have known that plans could not keep up with changes?

Fourth Master Yang patted him on the shoulder and said with deep sincerity, "Pray for yourself."

Yang Kai immediately felt powerless. Gone was the calm, carefree bearing he had shown earlier in front of those Void King Realm cultivators. Wearing a fawning smile, he called out loudly, "Mother, your son has come to pay his respects. May you be well and auspicious."

His old mother's voice came from inside the room: "Whose son? Which family's son are you? Don't disturb us husband and wife in our pleasure. Hurry up and get lost."

Yang Kai turned pale with shock. "Mother, you don't recognize me anymore? I'm your son, Yang Kai."

Dong Suzhu gave a soft cold laugh. "Do I know you?"

Yang Kai was about to say more, but Fourth Master Yang sent him a Divine Sense Message, “Leave for now. Your mother is still angry. This old man will go comfort her for a while and let her cool down. When it comes to women, once they are satisfied, everything becomes easy to discuss.”

Hearing this, Yang Kai also felt it made sense. He reached into his Spatial Ring, took out a jade bottle, and quietly stuffed it into his old father's hand.

"This is..." Fourth Master Yang looked down at the bottle, then up at him.

"Dragon Tendon Tiger Bone Pill." Yang Kai raised his brows, wearing an expression all men understood.

Fourth Master Yang took a deep breath and slipped the jade bottle into his sleeve.

What a good son! Very well, today this old man would offer his body to feed the tigress and quell her wrath. He turned around, shut the door, and strode forward.

Though tens of millions stand against me, I shall go!

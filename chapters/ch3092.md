## Chapter 3092 — Taiyi Star

"Miss Lu, I will take my leave here."

The Warship stopped in the Starry Sky about half a day's journey from Taiyi Star. When the hatch opened, Yang Kai cupped his fists to Lu Huaishuang.

"May Sir have a smooth journey. Respectfully sending Sir off!" Lu Huaishuang gave a graceful salute. Behind her, the Lu Family elites also cupped their fists in unison to see him off. There was no attempt to make him stay, nor any promise to meet again, because Lu Huaishuang knew Yang Kai had chosen to part ways here because he did not want to implicate the Lu Family.

Taiyi Star was the Yan Family's headquarters. Their influence on Taiyi Star was deeply entrenched, with countless eyes and ears everywhere. On this trip, Yang Kai would definitely have some kind of conflict with the Yan Family. If the matter were investigated afterward, it would certainly come to light that he had arrived together with the Lu Family, and the Lu Family would inevitably be dragged in.

Yang Kai knew this, and Lu Huaishuang knew it as well. She could not help sighing inwardly. If not for concern over her family, she would have been quite happy to send Yang Kai another stretch of the way. After all, it was only a ride on a ship headed the same way.

But with such a large family behind her, Lu Huaishuang had no choice but to consider many things. She felt somewhat grateful. This Sir was thoughtful and perceptive, and knew how to consider others. If he had insisted on traveling with the Lu Family, Lu Huaishuang definitely would not have been able to refuse.

Instead, he chose to leave the Warship while they were still some distance from Taiyi Star, severing any connection between himself and the Lu Family.

After this parting, who knew whether they would ever have the chance to meet again?

She hoped they would. When that time came, she would stand before him with stronger cultivation and a higher realm.

Yang Kai turned his head and gestured to He Yunxiang.

He Yunxiang immediately summoned her Star Shuttle. Standing on it shoulder to shoulder with Yang Kai, she turned into a streak of light and shot toward Taiyi Star.

A Star Shuttle was a very common flying Secret Treasure in the Star Domain. Yang Kai had once owned one himself back in Hengluo Star Domain, but after entering the Star Realm, it had been replaced and discarded.

He Yunxiang's Star Shuttle was a Void King-Grade Secret Treasure and moved quickly, but even so, it still took almost seven hours before they barely reached the outskirts of Taiyi Star.

The enormous azure sphere was like the eye of the boundless Star Domain, radiating a captivating brilliance. Around the entire sphere lingered a faint, hazy aura, as though it were covered in a layer of mist.

Yang Kai, however, knew that this was Taiyi Star's Spiritual Qi.

It truly deserved to be the Yan Family's headquarters. Among all the Cultivation Stars Yang Kai had seen, Taiyi Star ranked among the very finest. Across the whole Hengluo Star Domain, perhaps only Hengluo Commerce Guild's main star, Shuiyue Star, could compare with it.

"Sir, are we going directly to the Yan Family?" He Yunxiang asked while steering her Star Shuttle.

Yang Kai stood behind her. He clearly had not used the slightest bit of power, yet he remained as steady as a mountain. No matter how fast He Yunxiang moved, he did not sway in the least, as if roots had grown beneath his feet.

"Straight to the heart!"

"Good." He Yunxiang nodded, infected by the overwhelming confidence in Yang Kai's words.

Someone above the Void King Realm was enough to move unhindered through the entire Star Domain. How could he possibly fear a mere Yan Family? If the Yan Family knew what was good for them on this trip, obediently cooperated, and offered up the method of entering the Ancestral Domain, then all would be well. If they did not, He Yunxiang could already imagine what kind of blow this overlord of Wuji Star Domain would suffer.

Although they had not spent much time together, and she did not yet understand Yang Kai's temperament in detail, He Yunxiang could sense that the Sir she had attached herself to was relatively easy to talk to. He was not the sort who liked to bully others by relying on power or oppress the weak with strength.

This was the bearing an expert ought to have. Those who liked to crush others with strength and force others to submit were often the ones who lacked confidence in themselves.

When dealing with someone like Sir, sincerity was the best approach. Perhaps there might even be some reward.

Hopefully, the Yan Family would know what was good for them.

He Yunxiang had come to Taiyi Star a few times before. After all, it was the best Cultivation Star in the entire Star Domain. Although she was Gale's First Chief, notorious and despised by all, no one knew her true appearance.

She only needed to resume her original appearance, and naturally she could enter Taiyi Star safely.

So this trip was familiar territory.

She circled Taiyi Star in the opposite direction for a while before finally speeding diagonally downward.

Once they rushed through the clouds, the scenery below immediately came into view.

"This location seems a little remote." He Yunxiang observed the surrounding area, looking somewhat embarrassed.

"No matter."

After flying high in the sky for another two hours, a huge city finally came into view.

"That is where the Yan Family's foundation lies."

When they reached the front of the city, they descended to the ground and continued on foot.

Any city of a certain scale would forbid cultivators from flying inside, since it made management inconvenient. Only certain special people had such qualifications.

Yang Kai had long been used to this, so he did not find it strange.

The city walls towered more than thirty zhang high. Below them, the city gate stood wide open. A hideous and terrifying head, like a malicious ghost, hung above the gate. Scarlet light shone from its eyes, and its wide-open maw formed the entrance. One cultivator after another entered the city, as if swallowed into the malicious ghost's belly, vanishing from sight.

Whenever cultivators walked past, the malicious ghost's scarlet eyes would flicker, as if investigating something.

Quite a few cultivators were lined up before the gate waiting to enter the city. He Yunxiang asked Yang Kai for his opinion, then waited with him at the very back of the line.

After the time it took to drink a cup of tea, they entered the city gate. Yang Kai suddenly felt a wave of energy sweep over his body. It was somewhat similar to a sweep of Divine Sense, yet completely different. He immediately understood that this energy came from the Secret Technique in the malicious ghost statue's eyes.

It carried no malice and was only making a record.

By now, the night was deep, but the entire city was bustling and clamorous, like a living thing. Ten thousand homes were lit within the city, and even the narrowest alleys were illuminated from all sides until every detail could be seen.

Cultivators streamed past without end, shoulder to shoulder.

Streets spread out like stars on a chessboard, crisscrossing in every direction. Shops lined both sides, flourishing to the utmost. The shops came in all shapes, some filled with a dense exotic air that dazzled the eyes, and there were also tall buildings dozens of stories high, standing in this prosperous city like pillars propping up the heavens.

At the very center of the city stood a pair of twin peaks, their cliffs rising like ten thousand blades and piercing the clouds. The left and right sides were perfectly symmetrical, as if carved from a single mold. Yang Kai raised his head and looked up, only to see many buildings constructed from top to bottom across the twin peaks like terraced fields. The lights there were especially bright, and there were broad stretches of palace complexes as well, grand, ostentatious, and imposing.

"That side is where the Yan Family's main clan lives. Aside from those assigned elsewhere, the Yan Family's experts basically all live over there." He Yunxiang pointed at the twin peaks and explained, then asked, "Sir, do we go over now, or..."

“We are only going to ask for directions. Let us go over now,” Yang Kai replied.

"Sir, please follow me." With that, He Yunxiang led the way.

The two went straight toward the twin peaks.

Although the city was large, He Yunxiang was, after all, at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, so she was naturally quick on her feet. Although Yang Kai did not use any power, he also moved like the wind.

An hour later, they arrived below the twin peaks.

"Outsiders, halt. This is the Yan Family's residential area."

At the foot of the peaks, guards blocked their path. The guard's cultivation was not low, astonishingly at the level of the Third Stage of the Origin Returning Realm, only one step from the Void King Realm. Such cultivation already counted as an expert in the Star Domain, yet in the Yan Family's headquarters, he was merely a gatekeeping guard. From this alone, the strength of the Yan Family's foundation could be seen.

Although his words were still fairly polite, his manner was extremely arrogant. He swept his eyes lightly over Yang Kai, revealing some disdain.

He did not sense any power fluctuations from Yang Kai and thought this person was merely an ordinary man.

But He Yunxiang's aura at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm made him pause. Although he could not judge He Yunxiang's true cultivation, he knew she was in the Void King Realm. Added to that, He Yunxiang's appearance was extraordinary and her charm mature, so his thoughts immediately began to wander.

A Void King Realm expert born with such stunning beauty was not common.

If this were outside, a cultivator like him would never dare casually ogle a Void King Realm cultivator. But what place was this? It was the Yan Family, and he was also a member of the Yan Family. So even though his cultivation was inferior, his eyes still roamed wantonly over He Yunxiang's lovely figure for a while. He praised her inwardly, secretly thinking that if he could tumble in bed with this woman once, it would be worth dying for.

Several other Yan Family guards stood nearby, and at this moment they all looked over as well, each one staring in wonder.

He Yunxiang's expression darkened. She was now at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, standing at the very peak of the Star Domain. So what if these were Yan Family people? The weak should maintain the proper respect toward experts.

If she had come alone, she probably could only have endured this, but now she had already been marked as Yang Kai's person, and her every action represented Yang Kai's face. How could she tolerate a few servants being so impudent?

The aura of the Third Stage of the Void King Realm burst out. The Yan Family disciple who had been sizing her up all along went pale and retreated more than a dozen steps with thumping footsteps. He almost sat down on the ground, clutching his chest, his face full of lingering fear.

In that instant, he had actually felt as if he were about to die.

This was the pressure of the Void King Realm! Moreover, it was not an ordinary Void King Realm. At the very least, she was at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm.

His expression shifted back and forth, but he could only grit his teeth and swallow the grievance.

Although he was indeed a Yan Family disciple, the other party's cultivation was not low. The Yan Family could not possibly offend a Void King Realm expert over such a minor matter.

Pulling back all his arrogance, he cupped his fists with a grave expression. "May I ask Madam's honored name, and what business brings you here?"

He was completely different from his arrogant self of moments ago.

He Yunxiang said, "I request an audience with Old Family Head Yan. I hope you will pass the word along."

"Request an audience with the Family Head?" The man was startled and shook his head. "I am afraid that may be somewhat difficult."

The Yan Family's master, the true overlord of the Star Domain, was not someone just anyone could see at will. It was true that the woman before him was in the Void King Realm, but the Void King Realm was also divided into levels.

"I only request to meet the Yan Family Head once. Please make things convenient."

The man shook his head again. "It is not a matter of convenience, only..." He paused for a moment and said, "Does Madam know the Family Head?"

If she was an old acquaintance, then there would definitely be no problem. He would also be happy to pass on the word. Perhaps this charming woman was even the Family Head's old lover from outside.

"I have long admired his great name, but have never had the fortune to meet him."

"This..."

"Help me out, won't you?" As He Yunxiang spoke, a Spatial Ring had already been flicked over without a sound.

## Chapter 3101 — Something Wrong With His Head

"Brother Yang, come with me to Sea Hall. With my Yan Family's Old Ancestor looking after you, you will surely achieve great things in the future," Yan Luo invited him warmly.

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "I appreciate Family Head Yan's kind intentions, but I have no intention of joining Sea Hall or anything of the sort. We part here, never to meet again."

Duanmu Qi, who had been speaking quietly with Shen Cuiling, heard this and his eyes darkened. He had not taken Yang Kai seriously at first. He had only come here with Yan Luo because they had some friendship and he wanted to lend a little support. As for the dirty grudge between the Yan Family and this young man, he could not be bothered to care.

But he had not expected this young man to actually refuse to join Sea Hall, and with no respect in his words at all. His voice immediately sank. "Boy, my Sea Hall can be considered a decent force even in the Ancestral Domain. Ordinary cultivators who want to join may not even have a way in. Since you have been given this opportunity, you had best cherish it. If you miss it, you may regret it in the future."

Yang Kai said nothing more and merely shook his head slowly.

Duanmu Qi's face darkened further. He snorted. "You refuse a toast only to drink a forfeit!" A bumpkin from a Lower Plane Star Domain actually dared act so proud. Urging his Dao Origin Realm aura, he was about to put some pressure on Yang Kai.

Yang Kai almost laughed out loud.

This Duanmu Qi was only a First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator, yet who knew where he got such a sense of superiority from, daring to act so overbearing before an Emperor like him. If Yang Kai truly unsealed his cultivation, would he not scare everyone here to death?

"Duanmu Qi, what are you doing?" Another Dao Origin Realm aura suddenly spread out, silently colliding with Duanmu Qi's and dissolving it into nothing. It came from a large man whose whole body bulged with muscle, his rough appearance carrying a frank and heroic air. His cultivation was equal to Duanmu Qi's, so he did not fear him. "Do you not even understand the rules anymore? Are you trying to snatch him?"

"Exactly. This friend has already said he is unwilling to join Sea Hall, so why are you still pestering him? Have some face, will you? Do not let others look down on us people of the Ancestral Domain."

"Come, come, young man, do not be afraid. Not joining Sea Hall is the wise choice. What good can Sea Hall have? You would be better off joining our Sacred Mountain Peak. Your future prospects will be bright, and the benefits will be plentiful."

"Come to my Nine Stars Hall. As long as your aptitude is sufficient, I guarantee you will advance to the Dao Origin Realm within ten years!"

"Hee hee, little brother, would you like to consider Yin-Yang Sect? Once you enter the Sect, you can take home a Senior Sister or Junior Sister, whichever you like. And the young lady behind you has a face like peach blossoms. If I am not mistaken, it is precisely a peach-blossom beauty's face, very suited to cultivating my Yin-Yang Sect's secret art that is not passed to outsiders. Why not consider it?"

...

He Yunxiang stood there dumbstruck, staring blankly as a group of cultivators above the Void King Realm enthusiastically invited Yang Kai, or both Yang Kai and herself.

These were all cultivators above the Void King Realm?

Where was your reserve? Where was your bearing?

He Yunxiang felt like a tycoon clutching a fortune who had accidentally wandered into a vegetable market, drawing warm welcomes from countless people.

What was going on? Why was it like this? There had to be something wrong somewhere.

In this short while, nearly all the cultivators above the Void King Realm present had spoken up and extended invitations. By a rough count, there were no fewer than a dozen forces.

An old man smoking a dry pipe stepped forward. He did not greet Yang Kai, but instead went straight to He Yunxiang's side and solemnly stuffed something into her hand, lowering his voice. "Enter my Longevity Pavilion. As long as you make contributions, there will be no shortage of these." The old fellow seemed to want to take a roundabout route. As long as he won over the woman beside Yang Kai, would he still need to worry about winning Yang Kai over?

He Yunxiang lowered her head to look at her palm, and a somewhat familiar Spiritual Pill immediately came into view.

Was this not an Origin Condensing Pill? She had taken many Origin Condensing Pills recently and was very familiar with them, so she recognized it at once. However... compared with the Origin Condensing Pills Yang Kai had given her, this one seemed far inferior. Its pill fragrance was not as rich, its size was lacking, and its color was somewhat dim, as if it were malnourished.

She immediately understood that among pills of the same grade, the Origin Condensing Pills Yang Kai had given her were probably the very best. How could the one in her hand compare?

He Yunxiang did not know whether to laugh or cry, but her confidence in Yang Kai grew even stronger. Leaving everything else aside, judging from pills alone, the foundation of these people before her could not compare with Yang Kai's!

On the small earthen slope, Sea Hall's Duanmu Qi and the two Yan Family members looked displeased. Yan Luo in particular was incomparably anxious. He had originally counted on relying on the Old Ancestor after reaching the Ancestral Domain to teach Yang Kai a lesson and let him know the consequences of offending the Yan Family, but he had not expected such a change to occur.

He seemed to understand somewhat what his own Old Ancestor was wary of. He could not attack Yang Kai under the eyes of everyone present.

If Yang Kai entered another Sect, it would probably be very difficult to find a chance for revenge in the future. Turning to look at the Old Ancestor, Yan Luo saw Yan Renhao merely shake his head slowly, indicating that he too was powerless.

Yan Luo clenched his fist hatefully, the anger in his chest with nowhere to vent.

"I appreciate everyone's kind intentions." Yang Kai casually cupped his fists and smiled. "It is just that the two of us have no intention of joining any force for the time being. I am truly sorry."

The clamor came to an abrupt halt. Pair after pair of eyes turned to Yang Kai as if they were looking at a fool.

The enchanting woman from Yin-Yang Sect said in a low voice, "Little brother, although I do not know what grudge you have with that person surnamed Yan, if you do not seek some protection at this time, I fear you will find it hard to move even a single step in the Ancestral Domain."

The dry-pipe old man also said quietly, "A wise man submits to circumstances. As a man, one should be able to bend and stretch. A moment of forbearance is nothing."

Yang Kai shook his head. "Many thanks, everyone."

He Yunxiang handed the Spiritual Pill in her hand back to the dry-pipe old man. Since she would not be joining the other party's Sect, naturally she could not keep taking someone else's benefits.

The dry-pipe old man waved his hand. "Meeting is fate. It is yours." It was only an Origin Condensing Pill, so he did not take it too seriously. If it could make this man and woman change their minds, it would be a decent investment.

He believed that as long as He Yunxiang took that Origin Condensing Pill, she would definitely change her mind.

"Until we meet again." After Yang Kai said this, he left with He Yunxiang. He Yunxiang summoned her flying Secret Treasure with practiced ease, and she and Yang Kai stepped onto it together, disappearing from sight in the blink of an eye. They left behind a group of people sighing in vain.

Yan Luo almost wanted to throw his head back and laugh. Truly, when there was a road to heaven he refused to take it, and when hell had no gate he insisted on barging in. He clearly could have casually chosen a force to join and left this place safely, yet he insisted on walking into a dead end.

Was this not plainly letting Yan Luo get exactly what he wanted? How could he let such a fine opportunity slip by?

Exchanging a glance with the Yan Family's Old Ancestor, how could they fail to see the thoughts in each other's hearts?

Yan Renhao said, "Senior Brother Duanmu, now that matters here are finished, we should also return to Sea Hall."

"Very well." Duanmu Qi nodded in a pretentious manner, then casually tossed out a small boat. The small boat swelled in the wind, quickly growing large enough to carry five or six people.

The four people from Sea Hall boarded the small boat, rose on the wind, and chased straight in the direction Yang Kai had left, without trying to hide it in the slightest.

"That boy..."

"He really is seeking his own death."

"I think there may be something wrong with his head."

"Not so, not so. Of the fellows who come from Lower Plane Star Domains, which one does not have eyes higher than his head? Which one was not a tyrant in his own Star Domain? Now that he is suddenly told to join a force and live beneath others, he is probably somewhat unwilling. Since he has cultivated to this point, there is nothing wrong with his head. He simply thinks too highly of himself. I fear he will have to suffer greatly before he can recognize reality."

These words could not help but draw a wave of agreement. Many of these people had come through the same experience, so how could they not know the importance of changing one's mindset? Being a tyrant in a Star Domain was nothing. Being able to stand firm in the Ancestral Domain was true ability.

From the look of things, that boy probably had no chance to live to see tomorrow's sun.

"It is just a pity about that woman..." The woman from Yin-Yang Sect sighed softly. Earlier, she had not been exaggerating. He Yunxiang's features were charming, and she truly was very suited to cultivating their Yin-Yang Sect's Secret Technique. It was just that before she could promise any benefits, and before she could show He Yunxiang the strength and foundation of Yin-Yang Sect, the matter had come to an end. In her heart, she also had quite a few complaints about that young man.

But toward a person who was about to die, she had nothing much to fuss over.

...

The scenery around them rapidly changed. Yang Kai and He Yunxiang flew along while taking in the sights of the Ancestral Domain.

The Spiritual Qi here was indeed somewhat better than in the Star Domain, but compared with the Star Realm, it was much worse, sitting at a level between the two. Moreover, Yang Kai felt that the World Laws here were not as powerful as those of the Star Realm.

In other words, in this Ancestral Domain, the Dao Origin Realm was probably the peak. For an Emperor to be born here would likely be more difficult than ascending to heaven.

If not for the previous matter, He Yunxiang would certainly have been quite willing to admire this dazzling scenery. But after that incident, how could she be in any mood to enjoy it? There was clearly an aura trailing behind them all this time, clinging to them like a maggot on the bone, impossible to shake off.

There was no need to think about it. It had to be the people from Sea Hall and the Yan Family.

Several times she wanted to ask Yang Kai what he planned to do, but each time the words reached her lips, she swallowed them back down. In her heart, she comforted herself that there was nothing to fear. Since Yang Kai was this confident, he definitely had some method to deal with those people.

"Cultivators who have fought their way here from a Star Domain are all people of outstanding talent, existences found only once among tens of thousands. Their futures are extremely bright, so those people in the Ancestral Domain stand guard there and do their utmost to invite us to join them." Yang Kai suddenly said something that seemed to come out of nowhere.

He Yunxiang was surprised. "So that is how it is?"

"Otherwise, why do you think they would sit around there with nothing better to do? If they had the time, would it not be better to meditate and cultivate?"

He had experienced something similar as well. When he first entered the Star Realm, Bian Yuqing had taken notice of him.

It was just that the Ancestral Domain and the Star Realm were somewhat different. When entering the Star Realm, there was no fixed arrival point. After passing through the Starlight Corridor, one’s landing place was uncertain and ever-shifting. But judging from what had just happened, the entry point into the Ancestral Domain was fixed.

That seemed to be a passage, a road connecting the Ancestral Domain and the Star Domains, and this gave those people the opportunity to lie in wait.

Yang Kai even felt that he could open the passage in reverse and return to the Wuji Star Domain again.

This discovery invigorated him, because if he could find the passage connected to the Hengluo Star Domain, he would be able to return to his homeland.

## Chapter 2715 — Why Are You Acting Strange

Zhu Qing stood motionless on the Space Array, her expression calm and unruffled, as if the one she had just sent flying was not a Yao King, but merely a minor yao foot soldier.

Her beautiful eyes shifted as she fixed her gaze on Xie Wuwei and asked, "Do you want to teach me a lesson too?"

Cold sweat streamed down Xie Wuwei's forehead as he shook his head so vigorously it looked like a rattle-drum.

Yingfei beamed and bowed deeply, saying, "Safe travels to you both, my lords!"

Yang Kai rubbed his forehead helplessly, shot Zhu Qing a glance, and then activated the Space Array to teleport away.

After a long while, Xie Wuwei, still shaken, nudged Yingfei and cautiously asked, "That girl... who is she?"

She went head-to-head with Xi Lei and sent him flying with a single punch. Not to mention humans—even ordinary Yao Kings couldn't manage that. Could she be another Divine Spirit?

Yingfei's lips moved, and he softly uttered two words.

Xie Wuwei was left dumbstruck.

......

A biting chill filled the air, snow blanketed the ground.

As soon as Yang Kai appeared atop the Space Array in Ice Heart Valley, he saw someone bent over in front of him, rear in the air, sprawled across the Formation Foundation, completely absorbed in his research, muttering to himself, oblivious to the two people who had just arrived behind him.

Wasn't this Nanmen Dajun? The entire Ice Heart Valley was full of women, yet here was a man, and his back was so familiar that Yang Kai couldn't possibly mistake him.

Yang Kai listened for a while, but couldn't make sense of what he was muttering. Unable to hold back, he asked, "What are you doing?"

"Don't bother me!" Nanmen Dajun snapped impatiently, not even turning his head, maintaining his posture.

But soon, as if remembering something, he suddenly stood up and turned to look at Yang Kai.

Their eyes met, and Yang Kai was startled to see Nanmen Dajun's hair in disarray, his eyes bloodshot, looking almost possessed.

Instead, Nanmen Dajun's face lit up with joy. He rushed over and called out, "Master Yang, you're finally back! I've been waiting for you for so long."

"Waiting for me?" Yang Kai was surprised. "What for?"

Nanmen Dajun replied enthusiastically, "To discuss the Dao of Formations with you, of course! Come, let's find a place to have a good chat!"

"What could we possibly talk about?" Yang Kai rolled his eyes.

Nanmen Dajun straightened up and said earnestly, "Master Yang, you mustn’t say that. The Dao of Formations is vast and profound, with a long and storied history. As the saying goes, 'stones from other mountains can polish jade.' If we exchange insights and learn from each other, it will benefit us both."

"But I don't want to."

Nanmen Dajun was left speechless.

Yang Kai glanced at the Space Array on the ground and said blandly, "You can't figure out this Space Array, can you?"

Nanmen Dajun blushed and shook his head. "How could that be? This array is impressive, but I've already worked out its structure and operating principles. It won't be long before I can crack it and set up a new one."

"Alright, then keep at it!" Yang Kai patted his shoulder and turned to leave.

"Wait, wait..." Nanmen Dajun grabbed Yang Kai's clothes.

"Let go!" Yang Kai glared at him.

Nanmen Dajun looked miserable, lowering his head. "Master Yang, your insight is unmatched. This Space Array... I really can't make much sense of it. Please, enlighten me!"

"Let go first!" Yang Kai shot a look at the hand clutching his clothes.

Only then did Nanmen Dajun let go, grinning obsequiously, but still poised to grab Yang Kai again at any moment, as if afraid he'd run off.

Yang Kai brushed the wrinkles from his clothes, gave him a sidelong glance, and said, "You want to learn?"

Nanmen Dajun nodded repeatedly, like a pecking chick, eyes shining with thirst for knowledge.

"I can teach you," Yang Kai smiled faintly.

Nanmen Dajun immediately straightened his robes and bowed deeply. "Please accept my respects, Master Yang!"

"But what can you offer me?" Yang Kai countered.

Nanmen Dajun gave a wry smile. "What do you want? I have plenty of Source Crystals..."

"I have more than enough Source Crystals," Yang Kai scoffed.

"Then... I'll trade you other formation techniques. I have some ancient arrays, their power is boundless and ever-changing..."

"Not interested!"

Nanmen Dajun looked crestfallen. "Then what do you want?"

Yang Kai stroked his chin and mused, "My Lingxiao Palace is just getting started, and we're short a chief Formation Master..."

Nanmen Dajun's face changed at those words, and he shook his head repeatedly. "No, no, I’ve always been a free spirit. If I joined a Sect, I’d only bring trouble to you and your people, Master Yang..."

"Then forget it." Before he could finish, Yang Kai turned and walked away.

"Master Yang, Master Yang, let's talk this over!" Nanmen Dajun tried to grab Yang Kai's clothes again.

"Think it over yourself. Let things take their course—I won't force you." Yang Kai's figure flickered and he quickly ran off. Though both were at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, Nanmen Dajun specialized in formations and was no match for him.

Watching Yang Kai's retreating back, Nanmen Dajun couldn't help but stomp his foot in frustration.

Within Ice Heart Valley, the air was filled with the fragrance of wintersweet and the lively presence of countless graceful maidens. Though the valley had recently endured a great calamity, it had emerged from the flames stronger and more united than ever. Everywhere Yang Kai passed, he saw female disciples diligently cultivating and sparring, striving to improve their strength.

Upon seeing Yang Kai, they all greeted him respectfully, calling out, "Greetings, Lord Yang."

It seemed all of them recognized Yang Kai. After all, last time he had turned the tide and saved Ice Heart Valley from disaster. Many disciples had seen his true face, and even those who hadn't had seen his image.

Now, seeing that he was so young, handsome, and imposing, they couldn't help but stare at him.

Some bolder ones even cast him flirtatious glances, making Yang Kai feel overwhelmed.

Though Ice Heart Valley was a fine place, it was all women, with yin flourishing and yang waning. Many of the female disciples had lived in the valley since childhood and had never even seen a man before.

Once Lingxiao Palace grew stronger, perhaps they could form an alliance with Ice Heart Valley. With so many women here and no men to care for them, it was rather pitiful.

That idea wasn't bad. Bing Yun probably wouldn't refuse, since Ice Heart Valley didn't forbid marriage. It's just that once married, a disciple was no longer part of the valley.

As he walked along the main hall corridor, a graceful fragrance wafted by and a cold-faced woman suddenly appeared before Yang Kai, nodding lightly. "When did you return?"

"Just now. I heard you were looking for me?" Yang Kai looked at Ji Yao.

"Master wants to see you," Ji Yao replied, glancing suspiciously at the tagalong behind Yang Kai. "And this is...?"

She noticed Zhu Qing was petite yet curvaceous and stunningly beautiful, and couldn't help but compare herself.

Yang Kai had only been gone a few months, yet he brought back a breathtaking woman, and the two seemed quite close. Otherwise, why would he bring her to Ice Heart Valley?

For some reason, she felt a little annoyed.

"Don't mind her!" Yang Kai couldn't be bothered to introduce Zhu Qing—her identity as a dragon girl was simply too shocking.

Ji Yao glanced at Yang Kai, her previous warmth gone, replaced by a hint of coldness. She said casually, "Come with me."

He couldn't even be bothered to introduce her? After just a few months, he'd become so distant? Or was it because he had a new flame?

She turned and led the way.

Yang Kai hurried to catch up. "How have you been lately?"

Ji Yao replied coolly, "Thank you for your concern. All is well."

Sensing her coldness, Yang Kai scratched his head in confusion and decided to keep quiet to avoid making things worse.

When they reached a palace, Yang Kai stopped and turned to Zhu Qing. "Wait here. I have some matters to discuss with Senior Bing Yun."

Zhu Qing eyed him warily. "You’re not going to run off, are you?"

Yang Kai snorted. "I haven’t done anything wrong. Why would I run?"

Only then did Zhu Qing nod slightly and stand quietly in place.

Ji Yao glanced at the two of them, her lips curling slightly.

Entering the hall, Yang Kai saw Bing Yun, with several disciples attending her, apparently discussing something.

Yang Kai stepped forward and paid his respects.

Bing Yun smiled. "We're all friends here, no need for formality. Sit!"

Yang Kai took a seat and inquired about Liu Qianyun, learning that she had been in seclusion, preparing to break through to the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, which put his mind at ease.

Although Liu Qianyun was from the Great Wilderness Star Domain, she and Yang Kai had shared hardships and life-and-death experiences. She was able to join Ice Heart Valley thanks to his recommendation, so he naturally cared about her.

That said, Liu Qianyun herself was a martial artist from a lower Star Domain, with outstanding talent. As long as she had enough resources, advancing to the Emperor Realm was almost guaranteed. Whether for Yang Kai's sake or her own talent, Ice Heart Valley would spare no effort to nurture her.

"Have you returned to the Southern Territory recently?" Bing Yun asked, recalling that Yang Kai had said he was heading there last time.

"Yes, I made a trip back."

Ji Yao brought over tea, set it before Yang Kai, and said coolly, "You even brought back a woman. Senior Brother, aren’t you going to introduce her to Master? She might be visiting often in the future."

Bing Yun looked at Ji Yao in surprise, as did several Senior Sisters. They all felt Ji Yao was radiating a strange hostility today.

Many Divine Senses swept outside, and sure enough, they sensed a woman standing outside the hall.

Bing Yun said, "A guest is a guest. Yang Kai, why are you letting her wait outside?"

"No need to worry about her, Senior. She’s a handful," Yang Kai replied, unable to explain.

Ji Yao said, "You must have wronged her somehow, that’s why you’re so afraid of her."

"Afraid of her?" Yang Kai bristled like a cat whose tail had been stepped on. "What a joke."

Ji Yao stared at him. "So you admit you really did wrong her?"

"Nonsense, don’t talk rubbish." Yang Kai blushed, hurriedly taking a sip of tea to hide his embarrassment.

An Ruoyun and the others exchanged glances, all nodding slightly, as if they’d figured something out.

Ji Yao sneered. "So it’s true. Senior Brother, if you ever abandon a woman, I’ll never speak to you again."

Yang Kai spat out his tea, protesting, "That’s not true at all! Yao’er, why are you acting so strange today?"

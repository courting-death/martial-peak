## Chapter 3134 — White Robe Sect

After being cut off by Yang Kai, Peng Shizong did not dare show the slightest dissatisfaction. Instead, he grew even more respectful. "May I ask if Sir is from the White Robe Sect?"

"What is that? Never heard of it."

Peng Shizong said, "It is the strongest force on Cuiwei Star resisting the Great Wilderness Star Domain. I thought Sir was from the White Robe Sect." On second thought, that made sense. Although the White Robe Sect had risen to fame over the past dozen years, it did not seem to have any especially powerful experts. Even the Master of the White Robe Sect was only at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. Someone with such Divine Abilities naturally would never serve beneath another.

"Enough. This is as far as I can help you. From here on, you'll have to look after yourselves." Yang Kai reached out and patted Peng Shizong on the shoulder. "One more question. Which direction is the Blood Prison?"

Peng Shizong hurriedly pointed out a direction for him. He wanted to ask what Yang Kai was going to the Blood Prison for, but Yang Kai had already leapt up and sped off into the distance.

"May I ask Sir's honored name!" Peng Shizong called after him.

Two words came from far away.

Peng Shizong heard them clearly and frowned slightly. The name sounded strangely familiar, as if he had heard it somewhere before, but he could not remember where.

"Yang Kai!" someone suddenly cried out in alarm.

Peng Shizong turned to look at him, silently asking for an explanation.

"Brother Peng, don't tell me you've forgotten that name." The man gave Peng Shizong a complicated look. "Several dozen years ago, when Sir Luo Hai, the Star Lord, died..."

Peng Shizong suddenly understood. "It was actually him?"

He finally remembered where he had heard that name. Back then, Star Lord Luo Hai's death had shaken the entire Star Domain, but those most affected had still been the cultivators of Cuiwei Star. Once the Star Lord died, the Stellar Origin returned, but it had inevitably suffered some damage. That led to subtle changes in the world's Spiritual Qi across Cuiwei Star and made its billions of cultivators hate Yang Kai to the bone.

He had never imagined that the one who saved him today was actually the person who had killed Luo Hai back then.

"If it's him... then this makes sense." Peng Shizong thought to himself. He had already been able to kill a Star Lord several dozen years ago, so it was no wonder he could display such world-shaking methods now.

"Wasn't it said that this Sir left the Star Domain several dozen years ago and went to a higher-level world? Could the rumors have been false?"

"Maybe he came back. It's possible."

"Back then, the Star Domain had the Starry Sky Emperor, who roamed the universe unmatched. In my view, even if this Sir Yang is not equal to that Starry Sky Emperor, he cannot be far off."

"If another Starry Sky Emperor could appear, it would be a blessing for the billions of cultivators in our Star Domain."

Although Yang Kai's killing of Luo Hai had left the cultivators of Cuiwei Star with a poor impression of him, that matter was far too distant from them. No one here was thinking of avenging Luo Hai or anything of the sort. Today, they had received his life-saving grace, so how could they not be moved to grateful tears?

Everyone talked for a while longer before finally letting the topic drop. One after another, they stepped forward to congratulate Peng Shizong on his advancement.

Peng Shizong forced out a smile. In the past, advancing to the Void King Realm would have been a joyous occasion beyond measure, but now the entire city had been slaughtered, and nearly all of his friends and family were dead. Whatever joy his advancement brought had faded greatly.

"Brother Peng, what should we do now?" someone asked.

Peng Shizong's cultivation had already been the highest among them, and now he was their only Void King Realm master. If they wanted to live a little longer, they could only gather around him and follow his lead.

Peng Shizong pondered for a moment, then looked up and said, "This Peng wants to join the White Robe Sect."

On Cuiwei Star, many forces were resisting the oppression of the Great Wilderness Star Domain, but the largest was the White Robe Sect. It had also risen only recently and was at the very stage where it needed manpower. Peng Shizong believed that if a Void King Realm master like him went to join them, the White Robe Sect would certainly welcome him.

The great swings of emotion he had experienced today had brought him many insights. With the help of a noble person, he had not only escaped death but also advanced to the Void King Realm. Naturally, he should now throw himself into the campaign to save Cuiwei Star. Even if he died on the battlefield, he would have no regrets.

This proposal won the approval of many people and soon passed unanimously.

Peng Shizong said, "Find the others and bring them back. If they are willing to come with me, we will go join them together. If they are unwilling, we will not force them." Everyone immediately set out to search for those who had fled earlier.

...

Smoke rose everywhere, and scorched earth stretched as far as the eye could see.

Yang Kai's expression was extremely dark.

He had thought what he saw in the city where he met Peng Shizong was an isolated case. He had not expected it to be the norm on Cuiwei Star.

Along the way, most of the cities he encountered were already ruined beyond recognition, with not a trace of human habitation left. Within them, remnants of blood pools and altars still remained, clearly the handiwork of Netherworld Sect cultivators.

As for the residents, there was no need to wonder. They had already met a vicious end, taken by the Netherworld Sect cultivators to refine those Blood Sea Banners and Ten Thousand Souls Banners.

The Netherworld Sect's cruelty was enough to make one's hair stand on end. On the current Cuiwei Star, nine out of ten cities were empty, and Death Qi pervaded everything.

Along the way, he had also saved some people and killed some Netherworld Sect cultivators and other cultivators from the Great Wilderness Star Domain, but it was nothing more than a drop in the bucket.

In yet another city, Yang Kai slaughtered every invading enemy and finally could endure no more. He left the last man alive, seized him by the throat, and growled, "Where is the strongest expert from your Netherworld Sect right now?"

Yang Kai's Heaven-shaking methods had long since shattered the man's courage. Hearing the question, he answered in terror, "The Sixth Elder is currently overseeing Yanghe City."

"Take me there!" Yang Kai ordered.

"Yes, yes, yes!" the man answered again and again, hurriedly pointing out a direction. In the next instant, the scenery around them flew backward at a terrifying speed, frightening him so badly that he squeezed his eyes shut.

Although Yang Kai did not have time to remain here and restore peace to Cuiwei Star, he could still strike straight at the heart and cut down the enemy's leaders.

Yanghe City was one of the top great cities on Cuiwei Star. It covered a vast area and had once been extremely prosperous, with cultivators coming and going in an endless stream. After the Netherworld Sect invaded, however, they occupied it, and now Yanghe City had become their main camp on Cuiwei Star.

At least several hundred thousand people were imprisoned in the city, all of them materials the Netherworld Sect cultivators intended to use to refine Blood Sea Banners and Ten Thousand Souls Banners. As for the people killed over the past dozen years, they were beyond counting.

Such an important place was naturally also the Netherworld Sect's most heavily guarded location. Although they held overwhelming strength over Cuiwei Star, they still could not completely enslave every cultivator on the entire star. They had never intended to do so either. They simply treated the people here as materials for refining Secret Treasures.

Yanghe City was extraordinarily lively today.

When Yang Kai arrived under the man's guidance and prepared to display his Divine Might, he was astonished to discover that a fierce battle was already raging here.

Clusters of light bloomed, and figure after figure fell. In the air and on the ground, countless people were locked together in a fierce, chaotic battle.

"White Robe Sect!" the man in Yang Kai's hand cried out.

"Those are the people of the White Robe Sect?" Yang Kai swept his gaze over the battlefield and immediately became interested.

He had only heard from Peng Shizong yesterday that the White Robe Sect existed, yet today he already had the chance to see them for himself. Peng Shizong had said that this White Robe Sect was the strongest force on Cuiwei Star resisting the Great Wilderness Star Domain. Looking at them now, their reputation was well deserved. They would never have dared attack Yanghe City without some real strength.

"Yes, they are from the White Robe Sect," the man replied. Then he suddenly came back to his senses and shouted, "Sir, I have already brought you to Yanghe City! Spare my life..."

His voice stopped abruptly. Yang Kai threw down his corpse and curled his lip.

Looking ahead, the battle was fairly easy to read. Although the White Robe Sect had plenty of people and no lack of courage, they did not appear to be the Netherworld Sect's match. They simply had too few masters. Across the various battlefields, the White Robe Sect won few exchanges and lost many. At this rate, they would likely be forced to flee in miserable defeat within half a day.

That was assuming they could flee at all.

Right now, everyone was locked in bitter battle within the city, while Yang Kai keenly sensed that the Grand Array surrounding all of Yanghe City was slowly closing. Although he did not know what had happened earlier, he could more or less guess that the White Robe Sect had fallen into a trap and walked right into the enemy's snare. Once the gates shut behind them, their fate would be grim.

How could the people of the White Robe Sect have failed to notice? Many of them wanted to break out, but the enemy kept them tightly pinned down. As one Ten Thousand Souls Banner and Blood Sea Banner after another unfurled, Blood Qi soared toward Heaven, and Chimeras and Demons howled through the city, turning all of Yanghe City into a ghost realm.

He had come at just the right time!

Yang Kai strolled forward and waved his hand. Several figures appeared beside him. At a time like this, there was strength in numbers. He would not have much trouble charging in alone, but a few extra helpers could save a few more people.

Before he could speak, Liu Yan rushed over and kicked him.

"What are you doing!" Yang Kai glared at her.

Liu Yan pouted, her eyes a little red, looking deeply aggrieved, as if blaming Yang Kai for ignoring her all these days. The reprimand on Yang Kai's tongue died before he could say it. He reached out, picked her up, and set her on his shoulder. "Is this better?"

Liu Yan sat astride Yang Kai's neck, wrapped both arms around his head, and beamed with joy.

Li Jiao cupped his fists. "Brother Yang."

He Yunxiang also said, "Sir."

The Incarnation, Lu Sanniang, and Ruan Biting said nothing, but all of them looked at him curiously.

"Have all of you suppressed your cultivation properly? Will there be any problem if you fight now?" Yang Kai asked.

"No problem." Li Jiao nodded. This was the first time he had been released since Yang Kai took him into the Small Xuan Realm, so he naturally wanted to perform well.

He Yunxiang glanced at Yanghe City and said, "Sir intends to intervene in that fight?"

"That's right!" Yang Kai pointed ahead and said, "See those fellows wreathed in ghost Qi, the ones holding those great banner Secret Treasures? Once we go in, kill them all."

"Yes!" He Yunxiang nodded.

Ruan Biting looked as if she wanted to say something, then stopped herself. Yang Kai said, "Su Yan is somewhere else." This time, he had actually forgotten to leave her behind to keep Su Yan company, but since he had brought her out anyway, she would serve well enough as a fighter.

As they spoke, the group had already arrived before Yanghe City.

At this moment, the city’s Grand Array was just about to close. Countless White Robe Sect cultivators glared in furious desperation, but though they wanted to break through, they were powerless and could only watch helplessly.

When this group of uninvited guests suddenly appeared, everyone looked over in curiosity.

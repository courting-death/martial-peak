## Chapter 2797 — Fifth Layer Entrance

Two days later, a dragon’s roar echoed through the heavens. Yang Kai’s eyes were wide open, his entire body drenched in blood, as if he had survived hundreds of life-and-death battles. The Dragon Transformation Secret Technique was still active, and aside from the areas covered by the Feihong Black Dragon Armor, the rest of his body was riddled with wounds—hundreds, even thousands of cuts, large and small, terrifying to behold. Even the dragon horn on his forehead had snapped off.

Though his aura was faint, his fighting spirit still burned as fiercely as fire.

Before him, a golden giant blade over a hundred zhang long hovered in the air, confronting him from afar. Its terrifying sword intent seemed capable of slicing space itself.

For two days, Yang Kai had fought desperately against this metal-element world, unleashing every technique he had and consuming countless Spiritual Pills and elixirs. At last, he had managed to hold out until now.

If not for his deep foundation and extraordinary physique, this world would have long since rejected or killed him.

To stand against the world itself—what exhilaration.

But Yang Kai was at the end of his strength, and this world was in no better shape.

For two days, the Xuan Realm Pearl had been devouring the laws and Spiritual Qi of this place, wearing down the world’s spirit. If not for Yang Kai’s own exhaustion after such a long battle, this gradual shift in power would have been enough for him to conquer this world.

But now, it was about time to bring things to an end.

“Come! One last strike—let’s see who lives and who dies!” Yang Kai stared at the golden giant blade and laughed heartily.

As if answering his provocation, the golden blade let out a hum and slashed down at Yang Kai. This was the world’s final, all-out attack—its last show of hostility.

Such a terrifying blow could overturn rivers and seas, could shatter the very void.

A massive, pitch-black scar appeared in the sky, like a wound that could never heal.

Yang Kai roared, advancing instead of retreating. With a flick of his dragon tail, he shot forward like a cannonball.

A high, resonant dragon cry rang out. The image of a golden dragon appeared, wrapping around Yang Kai’s entire body. The dragon’s form, also over a hundred zhang long, was vivid and lifelike. As it shook its head and tail, thunder and lightning crackled.

Boom…

A deafening sound erupted, and wild waves of power swept in all directions. The space within a hundred li shattered inch by inch.

Two dazzling golden lights exploded in midair. The entire world was flooded with a golden sea.

Crack…

As the crisp sound rang out, a huge crack appeared first on the golden blade. Very soon, countless fine fissures spread from that crack like a spiderweb, densely covering the blade.

Crash—

Like a mirror shattering, the golden blade suddenly broke apart, turning into countless motes of light that filled the world.

Yang Kai left as quickly as he had arrived, falling like a meteor from the sky and crashing straight into the ground. Even the giant dragon phantom covering his body shattered in an instant.

He hit the ground hard, bounced up, then landed again. Yang Kai spat out a mouthful of blood, his aura so weak he was like a dying lamp, lying motionless on the ground.

Only the Xuan Realm Pearl continued to ceaselessly devour the laws and Spiritual Qi of this place.

After a long while, Yang Kai finally let out a groan. When he moved even a little, pain shot through his whole body—it felt as if every bone had been broken.

He hurriedly took out Spiritual Pills from his Spatial Ring and swallowed them, forcing himself to sit cross-legged and circulate his cultivation art.

Half a day later, Yang Kai slowly got up. His face was still pale—the previous battle had drained him so much that he would need time to recover. But he could not stay here any longer; he could clearly sense that this world was on the verge of collapse, and the surrounding space had already become unstable.

Retrieving the Xuan Realm Pearl, Yang Kai gave this world one last look before returning to the buffer zone.

Another day later, Yang Kai, now full of vigor, charged into a mass of dazzling green mist, entering a world twisted by wood-element laws.

In this world, Yang Kai encountered an old acquaintance—Mu Xiao!

Not just one or two, but in swarms—thousands upon thousands…

Mu Xiao’s vitality was incredibly strong, and their seeds could even parasitize others. Every Mu Xiao was extremely difficult to kill.

Unfortunately for them, they had run into Yang Kai.

Mu Xiao’s greatest fear was fire-based techniques and secret arts. Although Yang Kai was now renowned for his mastery of space divine abilities, his first steps on the Martial Dao had actually been with fire-element techniques, and he also cultivated Divine Sense Fire. 

For Mu Xiao, he was a natural enemy.

In this world, Yang Kai’s situation was far better than in the metal-element realm. Standing his ground, he unleashed his Divine Sense Fire, forming a barrier that the Mu Xiao could not even approach.

After another two days, the Xuan Realm Pearl had devoured all the laws of this world.

Returning to the buffer zone, Yang Kai did not waste a single moment, purposefully seeking out the five-element laws.

The towering waves and oceanic prisons of the water-element world, the stone puppets of the earth-element world—each one possessed the power to destroy nations, yet Yang Kai cut them all down one by one.

Twenty-five days after entering the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, in the fire-element world, Yang Kai’s expression was solemn as he swung the Myriad Sword, pouring all his Emperor Yuan into it. The sword light he unleashed split open the ancient Flame Demon before him.

The Laws of Space surged, hidden within the sword light. The shattered space turned into vortices, forming black holes that sucked in the Flame Demon’s remains one by one.

The Flame Demon let out a mournful howl, unwilling to accept its fate, but it could not escape the devouring of space and finally vanished.

Yang Kai let out a gentle breath. Compared to the razor-sharp assaults of the metal-element world, the ancient Flame Demon he faced in the fire world was not as fierce in attack, nor was its defense as unbreakable as the stone puppets of the earth world. It lacked the ever-shifting unpredictability of the water world, and it certainly did not possess the overwhelming numbers of Mu Xiao in the wood world.

Its most troublesome trait was its nearly immortal body.

No matter how severe its injuries, as long as it absorbed some fire-element Spiritual Qi, it could heal itself.

Fortunately, Yang Kai’s space divine abilities could banish it to the endless void. Otherwise, he truly would not have known how to kill it.

With the ancient Flame Demon banished, the greatest threat of the fire world was gone. Yang Kai relaxed, focusing on urging the Xuan Realm Pearl to devour the fire-element laws here.

According to his earlier guess, the reason the Xuan Realm Pearl had not taken the final step was because the five elements were incomplete. Since that was the case, he would simply complete them.

And so he put his plan into action.

With the laws of metal, wood, water, fire, and earth all devoured, the Xuan Realm Pearl was now filled with five-colored radiance, stretching from sky to earth in a magnificent display. The five-element powers generated and restrained each other in an endless cycle, as if undergoing some wondrous transformation, making Yang Kai even more confident in his theory.

A day later, the fire-element laws of this world were completely devoured, and the world was about to collapse.

Yang Kai put away the Xuan Realm Pearl. He had no time to check what changes it might undergo. Unlike before, he did not return to the buffer zone, but instead sped off in a certain direction.

There was a Void Passage there.

It should have been the entrance to the Fifth Layer.

Over these twenty-some days, Yang Kai had traversed dozens of shattered spaces. It had been hard to find an entrance, so of course he could not let this one slip by.

He was eager to know what awaited in the Fifth Layer of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda.

Staying on guard, Yang Kai dove headfirst into the Void Passage. Instantly, he felt a sense of weightlessness.

But soon his feet were on solid ground again. Before Yang Kai could even look around, he suddenly sensed a pair of sharp eyes staring at him.

Yang Kai’s expression tightened. Emperor Yuan surged as he looked up, only to be taken aback. “Brother Lei?”

The one looking at him was actually Lei Ting.

Running into Lei Ting here was truly unexpected for Yang Kai.

“You really did come to the Fifth Layer.” Lei Ting, however, was not surprised at all. He grinned, as if he had expected this. “It wasn’t a waste for me to wait here for you for over ten days.”

Yang Kai frowned and asked, “Brother Lei, you were waiting for me? Why?”

Lei Ting grew angry. “Don’t play dumb! Our fight isn’t over yet. Don’t tell me you plan to hide like a turtle.”

Yang Kai was startled, then couldn’t help but laugh. “I thought we’d already finished our fight.”

Lei Ting snorted, “How can it be over if we haven’t decided the winner?”

“Why must there be a winner?” Yang Kai retorted.

Lei Ting replied, “Of course it’s because…” He suddenly waved his hand. “What’s the point of talking? Let’s finish the fight first.”

As soon as he finished speaking, he threw a punch at Yang Kai without warning. Lightning snaked around his fist, crackling with explosive power.

Yang Kai calmly took a step forward, meeting him head-on without changing expression.

Lei Ting’s fist stopped right in front of him, the stray arcs of electricity making Yang Kai’s hair stand on end.

“What are you doing?” Lei Ting glared at Yang Kai, his voice tinged with anger. “Do you realize if I hadn’t managed to pull back my fist in time, you could have died?”

Just now, when he threw his punch, Yang Kai had not only failed to retreat, he hadn’t even gathered any power to defend himself, simply letting Lei Ting attack as he pleased, as if he had no intention of resisting.

This left Lei Ting feeling as if he had punched a ball of cotton. Though he wanted to give Yang Kai a good beating, if the other party didn’t fight back, what was the point? He wanted a real battle—a proper victory.

Yang Kai said blandly, “Brother Lei, your strength is profound. I admit defeat!”

A vein throbbed on Lei Ting’s forehead. He felt as if a volcano was brewing in his chest, ready to erupt at any moment.

Yang Kai ignored him, glancing ahead and then back. “What is this place? Why is there a corridor?”

Only now did he realize he was in a corridor, with a space array at his feet. The corridor’s end was pitch-black, like the maw of a beast.

Lei Ting grunted, “This is the place between the Fourth and Fifth Layers. Cross this corridor and you’ll reach the Fifth Layer. As for what’s over there, that depends on your luck.”

Yang Kai stroked his chin, thought for a moment, and muttered to himself, “It’s a bit like the Fourth Layer.”

There was also a buffer zone in the Fourth Layer, but not everyone could enter it. Only someone like Yang Kai, who cultivated space divine abilities and could resist the pulling force, was able to remain there.

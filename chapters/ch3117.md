## Chapter 3117 — Sweet Rain After a Long Drought

Sweet rain after a long drought; ten thousand trees revived, and a hundred flowers bloomed.

The white dress came off and went back on, went back on and came off, as if caught in an endless cycle.

Even the coldest nature could not withstand Yang Kai's invasion, fierce as flame. That raging fire seemed capable of melting even her Ice-Crystal Yu Body completely. She lay limp on the ground, without a trace of strength anywhere, feeling as if every bone in her body had come apart. Her snow-white skin was covered in marks left by the passion of clouds and rain.

This went on for a full day and night.

Naked before each other, Yang Kai rested his head on Su Yan's full chest and gathered his strength.

The dazed look in Su Yan's eyes gradually cleared a little. She patted his head. "Is it enough?"

She could no longer bear it. The man in her arms seemed determined to pour several dozen years of longing into her body, sending her tumbling endlessly among the clouds. Her state of mind, still as an ancient well for decades, had been shattered to pieces over this single day and night.

"..." Yang Kai mumbled something, though she could not tell what, as if he were talking in his sleep.

Su Yan smiled faintly. Her slender jade hand patted his back, gentle as if coaxing a child back to sleep after a nightmare. Then she suddenly said, "These years must have been hard on you."

When he left back then, he had only been at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. Now she could no longer see what Realm he had reached, but she could sense that it was absolutely stronger than the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.

Growth always came at a price.

Although she had not stayed by his side and weathered those storms with him, she knew he must have suffered greatly and endured many hardships over the years, and her heart ached faintly for him. When he was injured, who had been there to care for him and serve him?

"It wasn't hard." Yang Kai was no longer sleepy at all. Propping himself up on one hand, he gazed at the face so close before him, while his other hand covered that full roundness and fondled it gently. A trace of self-reproach rose on his face. "I came back too late."

Su Yan caressed Yang Kai's cheek. "Fool!"

Yang Kai was stunned. "I'm a fool?"

Su Yan pursed her lips and smiled. "The biggest fool."

"Why would you say that about me?" Yang Kai looked thoroughly wronged.

Su Yan only smiled without answering.

Yang Kai feigned anger, “Good, so you actually dared to speak ill of your husband!” He reached out to tickle her, making Su Yan’s brows knit tightly as she desperately held back her laughter. His movements stopped, and Yang Kai tilted his head. "If I'm a fool, then all of you are foolish wives." Lowering his head, he kissed that point of vivid red as if savoring the finest delicacy in the world.

Su Yan’s body went taut, her pearly teeth lightly biting her red lips, while her two slender legs unconsciously tangled together. She reached out to cup Yang Kai’s cheeks and lifted his head.

"What? Have you gotten your strength back?"

"Come!" Su Yan tilted her head, not daring to look Yang Kai in the eye, and spoke softly.

A terrifying light burst from Yang Kai's eyes. With that command, how could he hesitate at all? With a low roar, he leaned down and pressed himself against her.

...

"Let me put my clothes on." A faint trace of pain showed on Su Yan's face as she looked at Yang Kai and pleaded.

This would not do. They could not keep going like this. If she did not put on her clothes soon, she really would die here. Besides, she had let herself go too far, actually entangling herself with him here for two days and two nights. Master was probably already waiting anxiously. At the thought that she would still have to face Ruan Biting later, Su Yan's cheeks flushed.

Yang Kai grinned. "What's the hurry? We're not finished yet."

Su Yan reached out and poked his forehead. "Behave."

Yang Kai nodded repeatedly. "I'm listening."

Su Yan said, "We have plenty of time ahead of us. Why rush now?"

"But we still haven't dual cultivated!"

The two days of indulgence had only been an outpouring of longing. They had not used the Dual Cultivation Technique at all.

"Can we do it later?" Su Yan pleaded.

"No." Yang Kai shook his head.

Su Yan said sternly, "Senior Sister is angry."

Yang Kai could not help feeling a little apprehensive. His eyes turned, and he said, "Then what if I tell you about my experiences over the years? Doesn't Senior Sister want to hear them?"

Su Yan's fine brows furrowed slightly, and hesitation crossed her face. How could she not want to hear? She had wanted to ask from the very beginning, but Yang Kai had never given her the chance. After hesitating for a while, she said, "Then... make it short?"

"Good!" Yang Kai reached out and lifted her up, then sat down cross-legged and grinned. "We'll talk while we dual cultivate."

Su Yan had no choice. She nodded gently, reached up to smooth her disheveled hair, casually coiled it into a bun behind her head, and slowly sat on Yang Kai's lap.

How could several dozen years of experiences be covered in a short account?

Beside the medicine garden, dragon roars and phoenix cries rang out, two instruments singing in harmony as energy surged.

Time slipped by little by little as Yang Kai spoke. Too many things had happened after he went to the Star Realm, so Yang Kai chose some to tell her and avoided the dangers he had faced. Su Yan listened quietly, interrupting with a few questions from time to time.

By the time he finished, who knew how much time had passed?

"What about you, Senior Sister? How did you end up in the Ancestral Domain?"

Su Yan said, "By chance."

Speaking of it, it was strange. Hengluo Star Domain had originally been an extremely closed Star Domain. Even the passage to the Star Realm had only been opened through the Star Emperor Token Yang Yan left behind. Yet less than five years after Yang Kai left, abnormalities suddenly appeared in a certain stretch of space in the Star Domain, as though it was about to collapse.

That place was not too far from Dark Star. After Su Yan received the news, she brought people over to investigate, but they found nothing. Somehow, in all that confusion, she passed from there into the Ancestral Domain, then later joined Yunxia Sect.

What happened after she entered Yunxia Sect was much simpler. Ruan Biting valued her, accepted her as a disciple, and carefully nurtured her. Later, because she refused to yield to the pressure of Han Qiancheng's obscene demands, she was sent here to guard the Fire Cloud Mine, and that stay lasted ten years.

After hearing this, Yang Kai coldly snorted, somewhat regretting that he had killed Han Qiancheng too quickly. He should have spared his life and properly tortured him instead.

...

"Elder Ruan, Yunxia Sect is finished. What should we do?"

Inside the Fire Cloud Ore mine, a group of disciples responsible for mining the ore here looked at Ruan Biting and asked.

News had come from the Sect. From the Sect Master down to the Elders, almost no one in the Sect had been left alive, and the one who had done it was, astonishingly, only a seven- or eight-year-old little girl. All of this seemed to have something to do with Senior Sister Su Yan.

After the Sect Master and the others died, Yunxia Sect collapsed overnight, and its disciples scattered. They fled in panic, afraid of being implicated. By now, Yunxia Sect was probably only an empty shell.

These disciples had been sent here to mine Fire Cloud Ore, so they learned the news somewhat late. Now they had lost all direction and could only come to Ruan Biting for answers.

Ruan Biting sighed softly. So it really was as she had expected.

After all, this was the Sect she had sheltered for a hundred years. Now that it had been destroyed so suddenly, she still felt reluctant to part with it. But when she thought about it carefully, who could be blamed? If Han Zhengyuan had possessed even the slightest tolerance for others, Yunxia Sect would not have reached this point. If they had treated Su Yan well, they could naturally have received an enormous return today.

That young man called Yang Kai possessed unfathomable strength. Ruan Biting could not even see the depth of his cultivation, but she knew that as long as Yang Kai casually let a little something slip through his fingers, it would probably be enough for Yunxia Sect to benefit endlessly.

Unfortunately... there were no ifs in this world. Presumably, if Han Zhengyuan and his son knew this in the underworld, they would be filled with regret.

"Each of you should seek your own path." Ruan Biting waved her hand dispiritedly.

"Elder Ruan, only you can preside over Yunxia Sect now." The leading disciple clearly did not want to leave the Sect and wanted to persuade her.

Ruan Biting shook her head and said, "There is no banquet under heaven that does not end, nor are there eternal Sects or families. Tides rise and fall, flowers bloom and wither. Let nature take its course. Go."

Although she felt some regret, what she felt even more was freedom. She drew a deep breath, as if the shackles that had always bound her had shattered with a crash, and her mood became inexplicably light.

"But..."

Ruan Biting's expression suddenly tightened. She raised her head and looked in a certain direction, saying faintly, "That person has come out. If you don't want to be implicated, hurry and leave. I don't know whether he will start a massacre."

When the many disciples heard this, their faces changed drastically. How could they dare remain where they were? They scattered in every direction, and in the blink of an eye, not a single one remained.

A moment later, Yang Kai and Su Yan arrived side by side.

"Master!" Su Yan curtsied, her cheeks slightly flushed.

Ruan Biting was not some naive young girl, so how could she fail to guess what the two of them had been doing these past days? Leaving aside Su Yan's special charm after being nourished by rain and dew, the strange smell that came along with the two of them was enough for Ruan Biting to understand the truth.

Ten days! A full ten days. This boy really knew how to carry on.

"Su Yan, you... hm? Your cultivation!" Before Ruan Biting could finish, she suddenly stared at Su Yan in utter shock, almost thinking she was dreaming.

After ten days apart, Su Yan's cultivation had actually advanced from the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm to the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Moreover, her aura was vigorous and her foundation stable, without the slightest sign that anything was wrong.

This... how was this possible?

Ruan Biting did not doubt Su Yan's aptitude or talent. Otherwise, back then, Su Yan would not have taken only five years to advance from the Third Stage of the Void King Realm to the Dao Origin Realm. One had to know that Ruan Biting herself had taken ten years to make that step.

But... this was inside the Fire Cloud Mine. Moreover, Su Yan had been suppressed for a full ten years. Logically speaking, her cultivation should not have grown at all.

The facts had indeed borne that out. When Ruan Biting saw her ten days ago, Su Yan's cultivation had not been much different from when she had first come here ten years before.

Yet now, she was already at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, on par with Ruan Biting herself!

How had she done it? How could it possibly be done?

Once her shock passed, Ruan Biting immediately looked toward Yang Kai.

Su Yan could not possibly have such an ability. This had nothing to do with aptitude or talent, just as this world could not possibly produce an expert above the Third Stage Dao Origin Realm.

All of this had probably been brought about by this young man.

Just what kind of Spiritual Pill or miraculous medicine had he given Su Yan to produce such a wondrous effect?

Su Yan’s face grew even rosier. The reason she had been able to break through was naturally because of dual cultivation. The Dual Cultivation Technique was originally meant for cultivating through Yin and Yang union, and after so many years apart, both of their foundations had continued to accumulate. Through this union, those many years of accumulation were immediately transformed into strength.

What was more, Yang Kai was now in the Emperor Realm. With him guiding her upward, it would have been difficult for Su Yan not to advance.

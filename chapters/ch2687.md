## Chapter 2687 — Won't Shed Tears Until He Sees the Coffin

The martial artists in the hall erupted in an uproar, their eyes wide as they stared up at the high platform. Even those Emperor Realm experts hiding in the private rooms couldn't help but crane their necks, gazing in awe at the breathtaking scene atop the stage.

"So many Source Crystals! I've never seen this many Source Crystals in my entire life!"

"They're all Upper-Level Source Crystals—truly Upper-Level Source Crystals!"

"That pile is enormous. There must be over a hundred million at least."

"My lord, let me bear your children! Look at me, please, look at me..."

The martial artists sitting in the hall had all gone mad. Their cultivations weren't high, and their access to cultivation resources was limited. When had they ever seen so many Source Crystals, and all of them Upper-Level Source Crystals at that? Such a vast fortune was enough to shake their very souls.

If such wealth were to fall into their own hands, not only would they never have to worry about cultivation resources for the rest of their lives, but even their descendants for a hundred generations would have nothing to fear.

Gulp...

The sound of swallowing echoed from the various private rooms. The Emperor Realm experts, though filled with disbelief, were also brimming with greed and desire. It wasn't just the martial artists in the hall who had never seen so many Source Crystals—even they hadn't! Each one felt their horizons had been broadened.

"There really are this many Upper-Level Source Crystals..." The old man in Room Seven twitched at the corner of his eye, shock and a trace of dread flickering across his face. Earlier, he'd spoken in anger and boasted recklessly, but now he suddenly felt a chill at his neck and subconsciously rubbed it.

But soon, he let out a cold laugh.

So what if this brat really did take out a hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals? He himself was a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator, and had been for many years. Could a mere youngster possibly be his match? If the boy knew what was good for him, this matter could be settled quietly. If he dared to keep pestering, he might just have to teach him a lesson.

Besides, this brat had already angered the crowd, and now he didn't even understand the principle of not flaunting one's wealth. If he really made a move here, it wouldn't just be him who couldn't tolerate the boy—at the very least, the friend in Room Thirteen would definitely lend a hand as well.

With that thought, the old man felt completely at ease.

Meanwhile, atop the high platform, Yang Kai casually waved his hand, and another pile of Upper-Level Source Crystals appeared—no less than the previous one, perhaps even more.

"Hiss..."

A chorus of sharp intakes of breath rang out. Everyone stood frozen like statues, staring dumbly at the Source Crystals on the stage. The dazzling light from the Upper-Level Source Crystals made their eyes swim and their hearts race with desire.

And that wasn't all—soon there was a third pile, then a fourth...

In just a short while, the entire high platform was nearly buried under Upper-Level Source Crystals. The Spiritual Qi wafting out was so dense it almost became tangible, making every martial artist at the auction feel refreshed in both body and mind, as if they'd become immortals.

"Ah, my bottleneck... my bottleneck loosened! I'm about to break through!"

Someone suddenly shouted, their face full of joy.

Before the words had even faded, a surge of energy exploded and spread out—someone had broken through before him.

Boom, boom, boom...

One surge of energy after another erupted in the auction hall, some strong, some weak. No fewer than a hundred martial artists broke through their shackles in this rich Spiritual Qi, reaching a new realm.

Those who didn't break through were still overjoyed, quickly circulating their cultivation arts to absorb the Spiritual Qi drifting all around them.

These people didn't have high cultivation or good resources. The places they usually chose for closed-door cultivation were nothing special. Now, with Yang Kai taking out several hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals, the cultivation environment he created was practically on par with the forbidden grounds of the great Sects.

It was only natural that they could make progress before their bottlenecks.

"What a good man..." someone cried, tears streaming down his face. He'd been stuck at the Second Stage of the Saint King Realm for thirty years, thinking he'd never advance again. Who would have thought that just by attending an auction, he'd encounter such a stroke of luck?

"Benefactor, please accept my bow!" Another person stood up and bowed deeply to Yang Kai on the stage, his attitude devout.

More people stood up to express their thanks.

Yang Kai watched with a beaming smile, making no move to stop them from absorbing the Spiritual Qi from his Source Crystals.

He'd struck it rich in the Wild Ancient Land and raided the Treasure Vault of Wenqing Sect. His wealth was immeasurable—just Upper-Level Source Crystals alone numbered in the billions. A little Spiritual Qi leaking out was nothing to him.

To put it bluntly, with his current status, even a single hair from his leg would be enough for these martial artists to strive for several lifetimes.

Backstage, Wu Ming stared intently at those Upper-Level Source Crystals, swallowing hard as a single thought kept echoing in his mind: Mine, they're all mine!

He hadn't expected Yang Kai to be so wealthy. He could casually take out hundreds of millions of Upper-Level Source Crystals, which meant there must be even more treasures in his Spatial Ring. As long as he killed Yang Kai, wouldn't all that wealth become his?

No, no, it would belong to his Master. But if Master got the meat, surely he could drink some of the broth, right? At this thought, Wu Ming was thrilled to his core. This was truly a windfall from the heavens—he was so excited he nearly burst out laughing.

"You lot of trash, see clearly now? Was I just making random bids or not?" Yang Kai swept his gaze around, his tone icy.

No one spoke.

Hearing is one thing, seeing is another. Yang Kai had just casually produced so many Source Crystals, proving he had the wealth to back up his bids. How could anyone slander him for bidding recklessly?

As he spoke, Yang Kai casually put away all those Upper-Level Source Crystals.

The dense Spiritual Qi vanished in an instant, leaving the martial artists in the hall looking resentful. But the Source Crystals belonged to someone else, and that person was an Emperor Realm expert. The fact that Yang Kai had let them see such a spectacle was already a great favor—they couldn't expect him to leave the Source Crystals out for them to cultivate with.

For a moment, many sighs sounded.

"No objections? Good." Yang Kai grinned, then turned to Room Seven and called out, "Old thing, it's time for you to fulfill your promise."

"What promise? Nonsense!" the old man in Room Seven shouted angrily.

"Dare to go back on your word?" Yang Kai's face darkened. "Who was it just now that said if I could take out a hundred million Source Crystals, he'd twist off his own head and let me use it as a ball? You dare go back on your word in front of everyone? Have you no shame?"

The old man snorted, "You'd take a joke seriously?"

Yang Kai said coldly, "Old thing, you really are shameless. I'll count to three. If you don't twist off your head, don't blame me for doing it myself."

"You dare, brat?" the old man roared, not expecting Yang Kai to be so unyielding. After all, they were both First Stage Emperor Realm cultivators. Though he'd spoken rashly before, anyone could tell he hadn't meant it. Did Yang Kai really expect him to go through with it?

"One!" Yang Kai ignored him and began counting.

"Boy, don't go too far! I am the Sect Master of Cang Kong Sect—do you want to make an enemy of Cang Kong Sect?" Seeing Yang Kai's relentless attitude, the old man quickly revealed his identity, hoping Yang Kai would hesitate.

"Two!" Yang Kai remained unmoved, spitting out the word coldly.

Cang Kong Sect? What nonsense. He'd already destroyed Wenqing Sect—why would he fear a Cang Kong Sect?

The old man's face paled. He hadn't expected Yang Kai to be so serious. Was the boy insane? Their cultivations were about the same—was there really a need to make such a scene without a deep grudge?

What he didn't know was that a mere First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator, a mere Cang Kong Sect, was nothing to Yang Kai.

Only if he were a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert, Yang Kai might have to think twice.

The old man gritted his teeth, then suddenly looked toward Room Thirteen, locking his Divine Sense onto the Emperor Realm expert there and sent a message: "Friend, this boy is crazy. Why don't we join forces and take him down? Otherwise, with his temperament, after he's done with me, he won't let you off either. Better to strike first—those hundreds of millions of Source Crystals, we can split them fifty-fifty!"

The Emperor Realm expert in Room Thirteen wore a gloomy expression. Yang Kai's overwhelming presence had left him feeling pressured, and he knew the old man was right. Earlier, his "eat three catties of shit" remark was just to mess with Yang Kai, and he'd deliberately hidden his location. Who knew Yang Kai would find him so easily?

He was just worrying that Yang Kai would make him fulfill his promise when the old man's message arrived.

Stimulated by the thought of those hundreds of millions of Source Crystals, he nodded without hesitation and replied, "Alright!"

The old man was overjoyed. Brat, you brought this on yourself—don't blame me.

"Three!" Yang Kai finished counting, then stared coldly at Room Seven and nodded. "Very well. Looks like you really won't shed tears until you see the coffin. In that case, I'll grant your wish!"

As he spoke, he charged straight at Room Seven. In midair, he threw a fierce punch at the private room.

Boom! With a thunderous crash, a barrier of light suddenly appeared around Room Seven. The light flickered wildly, blocking Yang Kai's attack.

Every private room at the auction was protected by a barrier formation, mainly to safeguard the guests' privacy and safety. In the top auction houses, the barriers and formations in the private rooms could withstand the probing and attacks of even Third Stage Emperor Realm experts.

Flowing Shadow City wasn't a small city, but it wasn't a major one either. The barrier formation of this Qiyao Commerce Guild branch could withstand attacks from ordinary First or even Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivators.

If a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert attacked, there was no way it could hold.

But there weren't many experts of that level. Under normal circumstances, they were rarely seen, and even if they appeared, they were people of high status who wouldn't act so rudely.

"Hahaha!" The Sect Master of Cang Kong Sect in Room Seven saw Yang Kai suffer a setback and burst out laughing. "Boy, do you really think you can trouble me? Go home and train some more—ah!"

Halfway through his words, he suddenly cried out, as if he'd seen something shocking.

Everyone looked over, their eyelids twitching, faces full of disbelief.

They saw that the barrier of light in front of Room Seven had suddenly developed a tiny crack.

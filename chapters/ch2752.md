## Chapter 2752 — Enduring Humiliation for Survival

“Brother-in-law, take care! Come back soon to fetch us!”

A tender call from Zhang Ruoyu rang out behind him, and Yang Kai, who was flying toward Fenglin City, nearly tumbled from the sky.

The Zhang Family really seemed convinced that something was going on between him and Ruoxi.

Yang Kai couldn’t be bothered to explain. After discussing matters with the Zhang Family’s old madam, he told them to pack their things and be ready to leave at any time. However, Yang Kai still had some unfinished business, so he couldn’t depart immediately. He instructed them to wait at the Zhang Family, and once he finished his affairs, he would bring them back to the Sect.

Before leaving, Yang Kai left seven or eight Blood Beasts at the Zhang Family, just in case.

These Blood Beasts were ones he had refined when his strength was lower. Now that his power had grown, they were mostly useless, but they still possessed Dao Origin Realm strength, making them a great help to the Zhang Family.

As long as no Emperor Realm experts came to trouble the Zhang Family, these Blood Beasts could hold their own. Of course, Yang Kai also left them a way to contact him. If anything unexpected happened, a single message would let Yang Kai know.

Fenglin City—after years away, setting foot here again felt truly different.

Just like in Linghu City, Dao Origin Realm cultivators were common among the passing martial artists, and Emperor Realm experts soared through the skies. Even the law enforcement team could only stare helplessly, not daring to interfere.

Yang Kai made his way toward the Qin Family with practiced ease.

At the gate, a young servant wearing a round cap saw Yang Kai striding over, his bearing extraordinary, and hurriedly asked with respect, "Sir, who are you looking for?"

"Qin Chaoyang, the head of the Qin Family. Please inform him for me," Yang Kai replied.

"Looking for the Family Head?" The servant was momentarily stunned, then cautiously asked, "May I ask how I should address you?"

"Just tell him an old acquaintance surnamed Yang has come to visit."

The servant nodded, apologized, and turned to enter the Qin Family.

Yang Kai stood outside with his hands behind his back. After a long time with no movement, he frowned and sent his Divine Sense into the Qin Family to investigate.

Wherever his Divine Sense passed, everything appeared in detail. It was much the same as when he left years ago. The Qin Family hadn’t benefited much from Fenglin City’s rise, which was no surprise. The Qin Family was just a small clan; even if Fenglin City prospered, it didn’t mean they could share in the bounty.

Still, overall, the Qin Family’s martial artists had improved their cultivation. Living here for years, the Spiritual Qi in Fenglin City was much richer than before.

Qin Yu!

Yang Kai suddenly discovered Qin Yu’s figure in a secret chamber. After years apart, this girl now possessed Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm cultivation. Her former frailty was gone, her body had grown stronger, her cheeks were rosy, and she was cultivating in seclusion.

Truly worthy of the Heaven and Earth Severed Body. Though it brought great disaster, once resolved, it brought great fortune.

As Qin Yu cultivated in seclusion, she seemed to sense something and suddenly opened her eyes. Her beautiful gaze pierced through layers of barriers, looking directly toward Yang Kai. In her eyes, a mysterious light flashed, as if it could pierce illusions and see the truth.

Fortune Heavenly Eye!

Yang Kai was startled and quickly used his Divine Sense to shield himself, making his whole person turn into Nothingness. He stood there awkwardly, rubbing his nose.

If Qin Yu, a Second Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator, discovered he was peeking, he’d lose a lot of face.

Still, Qin Yu’s transformation shocked Yang Kai. Her rapid cultivation aside, this Fortune Heavenly Eye was extremely mysterious. He was an Emperor Realm expert with a powerful Divine Soul, yet even while secretly investigating, Qin Yu noticed him. If she kept growing… her future was promising.

Shifting his Divine Sense, Yang Kai continued to probe other parts of the Qin Family.

In the secret chamber, Qin Yu’s brows furrowed slightly, a hint of doubt on her face.

She had clearly sensed a probing gaze just now, but when she tracked it, she found nothing. There were only two possibilities: either she was mistaken, or the person’s cultivation far exceeded her imagination.

Qin Yu guessed it was the latter. Strong experts often appeared in Fenglin City, and it was normal for them to probe occasionally. Most importantly, Qin Yu felt no malice, so it didn’t matter.

"Lord Luo, I really have no other way. Can you give me a few more days?"

As Yang Kai’s Divine Sense probed a side room, he suddenly found Qin Chaoyang. At this moment, he wore a bitter expression, pleading with a young man in brocade robes.

Two maids stood nearby, faces bloodless, trembling with fear.

"No other way?" The young man surnamed Luo sneered at Qin Chaoyang. "If you have no way, then find one. Steal, rob—do I need to teach you?"

Qin Chaoyang’s face changed, calling out, "How could I do that!"

"Can’t?" The young man snorted heavily, suddenly pointing at the two maids and shouting fiercely, "Open your dog eyes and see clearly! The two wretches you brought today—are they fit to serve this Young Master?"

The two maids, whose cultivation was not high, turned pale as he pointed at them, immediately kneeling and trembling, terrified of this man.

Qin Chaoyang’s face was full of bitterness. As the head of the family, he was being scolded by someone young enough to be his grandson, spat on and humiliated, yet he dared not vent his anger. Truthfully, the two maids he brought today weren’t unattractive—they had delicate features and gentle beauty—but the young man surnamed Luo was impossibly picky, demanding only the most stunning women and insisting on changing them every single day.

All the slightly attractive maids in the Qin Family had been ruined by him. Qin Chaoyang had even bought some maids from ordinary families, but still couldn’t satisfy the young man’s demands.

"Looks like your Qin Family doesn’t need this Young Master’s protection. Fine, I won’t bother anymore. But you know what happened to the Du Family, right?" The young man said as he prepared to leave.

Qin Chaoyang’s face changed, hurriedly trying to stop him: "Lord Luo, please wait!"

The young man snorted coldly, "What else do you have to say?"

Qin Chaoyang spoke with difficulty, "Please, Lord Luo, give me two more days… no, just one. I’ll find a way to satisfy you."

The young man sighed, "Qin Family Head, I’ve been at your Qin Family for quite some time. With me here, has anyone caused you trouble?"

"No one has!" Qin Chaoyang replied quickly. Though Lord Luo was hard to serve, he was merely lustful. The Qin Family wasn’t a major clan, but they could find beautiful maids. The problem was how quickly he changed women—two beauties sent in would be replaced in days. He was like a breeding pig, indulging day and night without pause.

Still, as he said, with him at the Qin Family, no one had caused trouble. The Du Family, on the other hand, had offended an expert recently and was wiped out overnight—over a hundred people, servants included, all killed.

Fenglin City wasn’t like Linghu City, where rules were strict. Though the City Lord’s mansion maintained order, who could they really intimidate? Many experts didn’t care about the City Lord’s mansion. They might obey the law openly, but acted ruthlessly in secret.

Because of this, every major family in Fenglin City had someone powerful and influential stationed to protect them. The price was steep. The Qin Family was lucky—they only had to provide beautiful maids to serve the young man. Some families had to hand over seventy percent of their yearly profits, which was a nightmare.

"Qin Family Head, you’re a sensible man." The young man smiled, calmly patting Qin Chaoyang’s shoulder. "I have a proposal. Would you be willing to accept it?"

Qin Chaoyang’s heart skipped a beat, feeling uneasy, but forced himself to ask, "Please, tell me."

The young martial artist surnamed Luo chuckled, whispering, "Doesn’t the Qin Family Head have a granddaughter? As long as…"

"Absolutely not!" Qin Chaoyang was shocked, immediately refusing. He’d seen how lustful this young man was, so he had Qin Yu cultivate in seclusion, never coming out unless necessary. He hadn’t expected her to be targeted anyway.

Ever since Yang Kai returned from Four Seasons Land with the Calamity Fruit and resolved Qin Yu’s Heaven and Earth Severed Body, Qin Yu had shown extraordinary talent. In just a few years, she’d reached the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Given time, she might even advance to the Emperor Realm.

The Qin Family’s future rested entirely on Qin Yu. Even if she weren’t his granddaughter, Qin Chaoyang would never use her as a bargaining chip.

As for the maids bought to serve the young man, Qin Chaoyang had always made it clear—they were only brought to the Qin Family if they agreed themselves.

Qin Yu would never agree to such a thing, and Qin Chaoyang wouldn’t even consider it.

"Why not?" The young martial artist’s face darkened, as if his authority had been challenged.

"No, absolutely not…" Qin Chaoyang waved his hands repeatedly.

His attitude instantly enraged the young man surnamed Luo, who sneered, “You say it’s impossible, but I say it’s perfectly possible. Don’t worry—if your granddaughter is willing to serve me, anything concerning the Qin Family can be negotiated! Enough talk, I’ll go find her myself. You don’t need to interfere.”

With that, he strode out, as if he already knew where Qin Yu was.

Qin Chaoyang was alarmed, trying to stop him, but the young martial artist slipped past like an eel, leaving him grasping at nothing.

Opening the door, the young martial artist was stunned to find someone standing outside, blocking his way.

"Get out of the way!" The young martial artist was eager to find Qin Yu and had no patience to linger. He struck out with his palm.

The person didn’t move, simply reached out and pointed, his fingertip touching the young martial artist’s palm.

A piercing pain shot through him. The young martial artist shuddered, jumped back in shock and fear, and shouted, "Who are you!"

Qin Chaoyang was also stunned by the sudden turn of events. Only when a bloody scent lingered at his nose did he snap out of it, looking up in a daze: "Young Brother Yang!"

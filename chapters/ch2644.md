## Chapter 2644 — Repaying Kindness

Yang Kai did not move. He looked at Ling'er for a moment before speaking, "Old Ban, is it safe for such a little girl to stay here alone?"

Desolate City was a chaotic place, with no one maintaining order. Fights and brawls broke out frequently. Ling'er was just a seven- or eight-year-old girl. If she were to get caught up in any danger, she would have no way to protect herself.

Old Ban did not answer, but Ling'er piped up crisply, "I won't cause Grandpa any trouble."

Old Ban smiled, "Ling'er is very sensible. If I'm not around, she won't go outside."

Yang Kai shook his head. This wasn't a matter of going out or not. If someone truly wanted to harm Ling'er, hiding at home would be useless. He wondered how Ling'er had managed to grow up safely these past few years.

After pondering for a moment, Yang Kai said, "Old Ban, have you never thought of sending her to a Sect to cultivate?"

Old Ban gave a bitter smile at those words. "I've thought about it, but this old man has no connections. Where could I send her? If it's just some small Sect, she wouldn't have much of a future. It's better to keep her by my side and teach her myself. As for those famous great Sects, it's not so easy to join them."

"How about I send her to a Sect?" Yang Kai turned and said, "Take it as payment for guiding me this time."

Old Ban was stunned, then overjoyed. "Young Master, do you mean it?"

Yang Kai smiled, "Aren't you going to ask which Sect I'll send her to?"

Old Ban replied, "Since Young Master is willing to help, the Sect can't be a bad one."

Yang Kai was an Emperor Realm cultivator. Old Ban had seen his strength with his own eyes. Since he had made this offer, he definitely wouldn't send Ling'er to some unknown small Sect.

For him, this was a long-cherished wish. For Ling'er, it was also a great opportunity.

"Ling'er, kneel and thank Young Master," Old Ban said joyfully to Ling'er.

Ling'er did not kneel. She just looked curiously at Yang Kai, her big eyes blinking. Her long eyelashes fluttered as she asked crisply, "Ling'er wants to ask something, is that okay?"

Yang Kai was amused by her, surprised that such a young girl could be so opinionated. He crouched down to meet her at eye level and nodded, "What would you like to ask?"

Ling'er tilted her head and thought for a while. "Will I be separated from Grandpa?"

Yang Kai nodded, "Yes. The place I'm sending you only allows girls. Men aren't allowed to stay inside."

As soon as she heard that, Ling'er shook her head like a rattle-drum. "Then I won't go. I want to stay with Grandpa."

Old Ban grew anxious and stomped his foot. "You silly girl, what nonsense are you saying? Young Master wants to send you to cultivate, that's your fortune. Why are you making excuses?"

Ling'er said firmly, "If I have to be separated from Grandpa, I won't go."

Old Ban grew both anxious and flustered, but his heart was full of warmth. He looked pleadingly at Yang Kai, afraid that Yang Kai might withdraw his generous offer because of Ling'er's stubbornness.

"So pure and sensible—Ling'er is a good child." Yang Kai smiled and nodded, his tone gentle and encouraging. "Ling'er, do you still remember what happened last time, when your Grandpa was taken away?"

At those words, Ling'er's face changed. She clearly remembered what happened last time, and fear appeared in her big eyes. She said, "If I don't agree, will you take Grandpa away again? If that's the case, then I'll go with you. Just don't take Grandpa."

Yang Kai laughed, "I won't take your Grandpa, but if he was taken once, there could be a second or third time... Do you want to protect Grandpa?"

Ling'er looked up at Old Ban and nodded hard. "Yes, I want to drive away those bad people and not let them bully Grandpa!"

Yang Kai said, "That's good. If you want to drive away bad people, you need to be strong. You need to cultivate, to join a powerful Sect. Come with me, and I'll give you the ability to protect Grandpa!"

Ling'er pursed her lips, her face full of hesitation.

Yang Kai added, "As for your Grandpa, you don't need to worry. He'll come with us. Although he can't always be by your side like before, whenever you miss him, you can go see him."

"Really?" Ling'er's eyes lit up in surprise.

"Pinky swear?" Yang Kai smiled and held out his little finger to her.

Ling'er glanced at him and said crisply, "Pinky swears are for little kids. I won't do that with you."

Yang Kai was left speechless, thinking this girl was too mature for her age. Maybe it was true that children from poor families grew up fast.

But Ling'er said, "But I believe you."

As she spoke, she suddenly knelt before Yang Kai and kowtowed.

Yang Kai reached out and lifted her up. "No need to bow to me. I'm just leading you into a Sect. When you get there, you can bow to your Master."

Turning his head, Yang Kai said to Old Ban, "Old Ban, pack up. We won't be coming back to Desolate City."

"Alright, alright. Young Master, please wait a moment." Old Ban was overjoyed. Although he had always stayed in Desolate City, making a living as a guide, if Ling'er truly had a bright future, he naturally wouldn't remain in this chaotic place.

He was old now, with only First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm cultivation. If he didn't guide people here for Source Crystals, he really had no other way to make a living.

Old Ban went inside to pack. Ling'er looked up at Yang Kai and said, "Grandpa calls you Young Master. Can I call you Brother Yang?"

"You can call me whatever you like," Yang Kai said, patting her little head.

"Brother Yang, what kind of place am I going to?"

"Ice Heart Valley!" Yang Kai replied. "Don't worry, there are many beautiful older sisters there, all gentle and kind. Ling'er will be well taken care of."

"Are there any children like me?"

"There should be." Yang Kai wasn't sure, but Ice Heart Valley often took in orphans as disciples, so there should be young disciples there.

Bringing Ling'er into Ice Heart Valley was a spur-of-the-moment decision for Yang Kai. Twice, he had relied on Old Ban's help to enter the Ancient Land. This time, Old Ban hadn't even asked for payment. Giving Ling'er a future was a way to repay kindness with kindness.

Before long, Old Ban finished packing and came out, cupping his fists. "Young Master, we're ready."

"Then let's go." Yang Kai called out, then picked up Ling'er and set her on his shoulders.

Ling'er was clearly startled, quickly grabbing Yang Kai's head with both hands. But as a child, she soon recovered and started giggling.

"How can this be?" Old Ban was flustered. "Young Master, I can look after Ling'er. She can't ride on your shoulders, that's too disrespectful."

Yang Kai waved his hand. "It's fine. Besides, the Ancient Land passage is extremely dangerous. With me protecting her, nothing will happen."

Hearing this, Old Ban gave up trying to persuade him. Yang Kai was right—the Ancient Land passage was dangerous. With an Emperor Realm cultivator protecting her, it was much better than him doing it. He could only gratefully cup his fists. "Thank you, Young Master."

On the way, Yang Kai briefly introduced Ice Heart Valley and his plans for Ling'er to Old Ban. Old Ban naturally had no objections.

The name of Ice Heart Valley was well-known, even in the Eastern Territory. He knew it was one of the top Sects in the Northern Territory. For Ling'er to join Ice Heart Valley in this lifetime was a tremendous fortune.

In the past, he wouldn't have even dared to dream of such a thing, but today it was coming true.

He didn't know what relationship Yang Kai had with Ice Heart Valley, but since Yang Kai had made such a promise, it was surely trustworthy.

For a moment, Old Ban couldn't help but shed tears of joy. He hadn't expected happiness to come so suddenly.

"Ling'er will join Ice Heart Valley. In the future, Old Ban, you can find some work in Binglun City. If the girl misses you, she can come visit."

"I'll leave everything to Young Master's arrangements." Old Ban wiped the corners of his eyes with his sleeve.

The Ancient Land passage was still shrouded in strange mists, with fierce winds and thunder raging, and Yin-Souls roaming within.

Fortunately, with Old Ban leading the way like an experienced guide, they encountered no real danger along the route.

After about ten days, the three of them finally passed through the Ancient Land passage and officially entered the Wild Ancient Land.

The Primordial Power of the surroundings surged, as if it could erode any living being of flesh and blood. Yang Kai carried Ling'er and told Old Ban not to stray too far, flying straight toward the depths of the Ancient Land.

Old Ban was nervous the whole way. Although he had passed through the Ancient Land passage countless times, this was his first time truly entering the Wild Ancient Land. After all, the Ancient Land was too dangerous. With his cultivation, entering meant certain death.

But Yang Kai was completely at ease, as if entering his own backyard. Old Ban was full of admiration.

He was puzzled, because Yang Kai had clearly said he would take Ling'er to join Ice Heart Valley, which was in the Northern Territory. Yet Yang Kai had entered the Ancient Land, which was in the opposite direction. He didn't know what Yang Kai was planning.

They sped along. For the first time, Ling'er experienced the joy of flying, giggling from time to time. Old Ban kept warning her not to laugh so loudly, afraid of attracting the yao of the Ancient Land.

But what you fear is what comes. Less than half a day after entering the Ancient Land, a surge of Yao Qi suddenly shot up ahead. Immediately, a group of fierce-looking yao blocked their path, the leader exuding an aura comparable to the Emperor Realm.

"Yao Commander!" Old Ban's face turned pale, his clothes instantly soaked with sweat.

He hadn't expected that the first yao they would encounter in the Wild Ancient Land would be a Yao Commander, and he had brought many Yao Generals with him.

This was the end!

Although Yang Kai was an Emperor Realm cultivator, these yao were also strong. If a fight broke out, Yang Kai might not fare well.

"Brazen human, how dare you run wild in my Wild Ancient Land! State your name at once, and perhaps I'll grant you a quick death!" The leading Yao Commander shouted fiercely, sending his Yao Generals and soldiers into a frenzy. In an instant, the group of yao looked like a horde of demons, the scene terrifying.

Old Ban trembled all over, nearly fainting. Ling'er also nervously grabbed Yang Kai's hair, her face turning pale.

But Yang Kai stood as steady as a mountain, only staring coldly at the Yao Commander.

Seeing his subordinates so excited, the Yao Commander couldn't help but show off, raising his voice, "Little human, are you scared silly? You... hmm?"

He hadn't finished speaking when he suddenly shuddered, his eyes widening as he stared at Yang Kai's face. After a moment, his expression changed dramatically, as if he had seen a ghost in broad daylight. He hurriedly waved at his subordinates, shouting, "Shut up, scatter, scatter, quickly scatter!"

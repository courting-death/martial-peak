## Chapter 2966 — The Secret of the Revolving World

Countless thoughts flashed through the Human Sovereign’s mind. He had already prepared himself to bargain with Yang Kai and secretly resolved that, even if the fellow made some excessive demands, he could feign agreement if necessary. So long as Yang Kai did not try to seize the position of Human Sovereign, everything else was open to negotiation. First, he would placate him; afterward, he could slowly devise a way to deal with him.

"Tell me about the history of this Human Sovereign City and the Revolving World."

"Hm? What?" The Human Sovereign was somewhat stunned, wondering if he had misheard.

Now that he was the fish on the chopping block, this outsider before him could do whatever he wished, yet unexpectedly, he had made such a request. The Human Sovereign had already prepared himself to have a piece of flesh carved off, but the other party had merely plucked a hair... No, this did not even count as plucking a hair.

"You really have grown old. Are your eyes dim and your ears deaf?" Yang Kai looked at him coldly, somewhat dissatisfied with his reaction.

Only then did the Human Sovereign confirm that the other party truly had made only this simple request. While secretly guessing Yang Kai's purpose in doing so, he organized his thoughts and said, "Since Your Excellency wishes to know, then this Sovereign..."

"Hm?"

"Then this old man will just speak casually." The Human Sovereign's expression turned somewhat awkward. It had always been others who acted according to his expression. He had never imagined that one day he would have to act according to someone else's.

"What this old man knows is also limited. If there are any unclear details, I ask Your Excellency not to take offense."

Yang Kai waved his hand and said, "Stop blabbering."

The Human Sovereign coughed dryly, then said, "If we truly speak of it, this Revolving World can actually be considered part of the Star Realm. Of course, at that time, the Star Realm was not yet called the Star Realm. I am not very clear on what the ancestral land was called. The ancestors called themselves the Barbarian Tribe. Shamanic Arts flourished, and the clansmen gathered together, living and multiplying by tribe. Although there was internal strife, the human race also prospered."

As he spoke, he quietly observed Yang Kai's expression and found that Yang Kai's face had become somewhat strange, as if he were reminiscing about something. He did not know which of his words had touched a nerve, but he did not dare stop and continued, "Then, one day, Heaven collapsed and Earth split apart. An enormous hole appeared in the sky, and experts walked out from that hole and invaded the Barbarian Tribe. Those experts who had come from an unknown land called themselves demons. The demon experts were numerous, bloodthirsty by nature, and many tribes suffered heavy casualties. Faced with such powerful enemies, the ancestors were naturally unwilling to sit and wait for death, so they exhausted the strength of the entire Barbarian Tribe to resist."

"The war between the two races was incomparably fierce. That battle seemed to have lasted many years, and both the Barbarian Tribe and the demons suffered disastrous casualties. The battlefield back then was precisely today's Revolving World."

Yang Kai asked in surprise, "The Revolving World was the battlefield? Then, according to what you said, why did this Revolving World separate from the Star Realm?"

The Human Sovereign said, "There is something Your Excellency does not know. The reason the ancestors were able to obtain victory in that battle seems to have been related to the Sacred Tree. The ancient records state that the Sacred Tree sacrificed itself for righteousness and sealed the passage through which the demons invaded. Unfortunately, in the end, no one knows what kind of accident occurred. The laws of the Star Realm and that Demon Domain clashed and surged violently, exiling the entire battlefield into the Void. Thus, the Revolving World was born. The humans and demons who still survived on the battlefield continued living here. At first, the two races still fought and slaughtered each other, but the laws of this place were extremely strange and caused my human race to suffer many losses. The laws of the Revolving World change. For one period of time, they are not much different from those of the Star Realm, and during that time, my human race can walk about freely, unaffected by the laws. But for another period of time, they are connected to that Demon Domain, and the world's Spiritual Qi is transformed into Demonic Qi, making it difficult for the human race to take even a single step. In the end, it was still by relying on the Sacred Tree that Human Sovereign City was gradually established, giving the human race a place to stand. In the blink of an eye, more than a hundred thousand years have passed."

Yang Kai nodded slightly, gazing at that towering ancient tree with complicated eyes.

Qing was still alive. Although he was not the wise old man from back then, he was indeed still alive, preserving the final thread of his will and still using his own body to shelter the surviving human race. Without him, there would be no place in this Revolving World where humans could exist.

When the Laws of Heaven and Earth were suited for the demon race's survival, Demonic Qi flourished abundantly, and the human race simply could not endure such conditions.

When it was the other way around, however, the demons were not particularly restricted. They had lived in that harsh environment for a long time, so naturally, they were unaffected in a better environment.

The information the Human Sovereign revealed corroborated much of what Yang Kai had experienced in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, showing that the Human Sovereign had not lied, nor had Die lied. It was just that reality and illusion were not completely the same. At the very least, in the illusion Yang Kai experienced, the Revolving World had not been born, and Qing had completely perished in that battle.

He did not know whether Die's records had been mistaken.

"In that case, the Revolving World is actually located in a spatial crack?" Yang Kai asked with a frown.

The Human Sovereign nodded and said, "Correct. As for the location of the Revolving World, the ancestors had many guesses, but basically all of them believed that this world is located in a crack, and this crack lies between the Star Realm and the Demon Domain. That is why the laws of this world change unpredictably, sometimes influenced by the laws of the Star Realm, sometimes filled by the laws of the Demon Domain, creating two extreme divisions."

Yang Kai nodded lightly. Many of his doubts had been explained.

Previously, when he had been swallowed by that Cold Tide in the Frozen Earth, Yang Kai had sensed fluctuations of the Laws of Space. Thinking about it now, that Cold Tide was actually nothing more than an entrance to a space. Passing through that entrance allowed one to enter the Revolving World. Similar to transmission through a Space Array, there would naturally be fluctuations of the Laws of Space.

It was just that this transmission was not between cities, nor was it across territories, but across worlds.

"Have you never thought of leaving the Revolving World and returning to the Star Realm?" Yang Kai asked.

The Human Sovereign gave an awkward chuckle and said, "The ancestors naturally harbored such thoughts, but the spatial barriers are so formidable. Even if we could locate them, who among us would possess the power to break through?" As he spoke, the Human Sovereign's expression suddenly shifted slightly. He hesitated for a moment before gazing at Yang Kai and asking carefully, "Your Excellency seems quite versed in the Power of Space?"

Earlier, he had asked Yang Kai why he could enter Human Sovereign City even without a Human Sovereign Seal.

Yang Kai had displayed the power of the Laws of Space before him. Although the Human Sovereign was old, his eyesight was not poor, so he had naturally seen through it at a glance. If it were this person... perhaps he really could break a spatial barrier.

Thinking of this, the Human Sovereign's mood suddenly became somewhat restless.

Yang Kai looked at him thoughtfully and said, "You seem not to want to leave this place."

A trace of embarrassment appeared on the Human Sovereign's face, and he said solemnly, "Leaving this world and returning to the ancestral land is the ancestors' last wish. This old man naturally also wishes to leave this place."

Yang Kai sneered, "What nonsense are you spouting? The Human Sovereign stands high above all others, and his words are law. In Human Sovereign City, whether strong or weak, none dare disobey your orders. If I were you, I would not want to leave this place either. How carefree and comfortable it is here."

The Human Sovereign laughed awkwardly several times. Clearly, Yang Kai had struck upon his true thoughts. His strength was not all that great, and he also knew his own limitations. If he truly left the Revolving World and returned to the Star Realm, his lofty station would be stripped in an instant, reducing him to his true form.

Yang Kai suddenly changed the subject and said, "Is there truly no way to leave this world?" Without waiting for him to answer, Yang Kai said, "You had best tell the truth. Although I do not particularly want to kill an old man, if I am forced to, I will not show mercy."

The Human Sovereign's expression changed slightly, and he hurriedly said, "Yin Wind Mountain!"

"Where is Yin Wind Mountain? Can one leave the Revolving World from Yin Wind Mountain?"

The Human Sovereign said, “There is only one possibility, though I cannot guarantee it. Of course, that possibility applies only to an expert like you. Without the protection of the Power of Space, that place is nothing more than a land of certain death. In their efforts to leave this world, our ancestors exhausted every possible means and tried countless methods, yet all ended in failure. In the end, they concluded that Yin Wind Mountain might contain a passage leading out of the Revolving World. I say might, because no one could be certain. However, that place is extraordinarily dangerous. The Yin Winds that blow from it are infused with endless cold and Demonic Qi. Even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm master cannot venture very far within.”

"Cold?" Yang Kai raised his brow, keenly grasping a crucial point. "What kind of cold?"

The Human Sovereign said, "Cold that extinguishes all vitality."

Yang Kai's eyes lit up. Was that not the cold in the Frozen Earth? From this, it seemed the old fellow had not lied. That Yin Wind Mountain might really be able to lead out of the Revolving World.

The cold from the Frozen Earth could not have appeared in the Revolving World without reason—they were two separate realms after all. Yet the fact that cold existed on Yin Wind Mountain meant that the cold from the Frozen Earth had entered the Revolving World through some entrance. If he could trace this entrance back to its source, he would surely be able to find a path to return to the Star Realm.

However, Demonic Qi was also mixed into the Yin Wind, which meant that the entrance did not only connect to the Star Realm, but also connected to the Demon Domain. The Demonic Qi in the Demon Domain could likewise pour in through it.

Therefore, a certain location on Yin Wind Mountain should be the spatial intersection point of the Revolving World, the Star Realm, and the Demon Domain.

The cold alone was terrifyingly powerful, and with Demonic Qi stirring up trouble within it, it could indeed force even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm master to halt.

No wonder no one from the human race had been able to leave the Revolving World for so many years.

The Human Sovereign stood at the side, observing Yang Kai's expression. Seeing the rich changes on Yang Kai's face, how could he not guess what he was thinking? He was delighted inwardly, but his expression did not shift in the slightest. Instead, he put on the appearance of earnestly advising him. "Does Your Excellency wish to take this risk? Forgive me for speaking frankly, but Yin Wind Mountain is not a good place to go. Even if Your Excellency really finds a path to leave, you may not be able to return to the Star Realm. It is possible you will mistakenly enter the Demon Domain!  And if that happened, your chances of survival would be no better than one in ten thousand.”

Yang Kai patted the Human Sovereign on the shoulder, driving him down by half a head, then grinned and said, “Aren’t you hoping I die as soon as possible? Whatever’s on your mind, just say it outright. Hiding your true thoughts while speaking the opposite—it’s sickening to watch.”

The Human Sovereign's expression was awkward, and he cursed endlessly in his heart, no longer daring to say anything.

Yang Kai, however, had made up his mind. He clapped his hands and said, "To Yin Wind Mountain!"

The Human Sovereign jumped in fright. "I... I am going too?"

"You want to go?" Yang Kai glanced at him sideways.

The Human Sovereign shook his head hard.

"If you do not want to go, why ask such a needless question?" Yang Kai sneered, then changed the subject and said, "Do you know why I am not killing you?"

The Human Sovereign wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and said in a trembling voice, "Please enlighten me."

Yang Kai turned his head and glanced at Qing, then said, "I am sparing your life so you can properly manage Human Sovereign City. Do not disgrace the old ancestor again. You people were preserved by Qing at the cost of his life."

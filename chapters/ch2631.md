## Chapter 2631 — An Ruoyun's Fury

"Taiping City?" An Ruoyun's expression shifted. "What happened?"

The name Taiping City might have been unfamiliar to others, but for An Ruoyun, it was like a thorn in her heart. Back then, Shi Tianhe had defied her just to marry a man from Taiping City.

Hearing her question, Shi Tianhe's delicate body trembled. She timidly looked up, only to find her master didn't even spare her a glance. Shi Tianhe's heart was filled with bitterness, knowing her Master had never truly forgiven her. Otherwise, since she had already seen her, why hadn't she summoned her to speak?

Yang Kai smiled slightly and said, "This matter, in fact, has something to do with Junior Sister An."

Upon hearing this, An Ruoyun glanced thoughtfully at the pale-faced Shi Tianhe, her expression calm and unreadable.

"How does the matter in Taiping City have anything to do with Senior Sister? Senior Brother Yang, tell us in detail," Changsun Ying asked curiously.

Yang Kai said, "A few days ago, Junior Sister Ji Yao and I stayed overnight in Taiping City. The next morning, as we were preparing to leave, we encountered someone in disguise trying to sneak out of the city, but their intentions were seen through and they were stopped..."

He then recounted everything that had happened at the city gate of Taiping City, watching An Ruoyun the entire time, as if speaking just for her.

When she heard that Dong Hai, disregarding a century of marriage, not only slandered Shi Tianhe for stealing the Dong Family's ancestral treasure but also expelled her from the Dong Family in front of everyone, An Ruoyun crushed the armrest with a single grip.

Although Shi Tianhe was her abandoned disciple, she had raised her from childhood. When had it ever been the Dong Family's place to bully her so?

Moreover, An Ruoyun knew Shi Tianhe's character better than anyone. How could she possibly do something like stealing?

"Later, Junior Sister Ji Yao and I learned that the so-called theft of the ancestral treasure was just a trumped-up charge. The real reason was that Tianhe, upon hearing her sect was in trouble, wanted to return to the valley to do what little she could. But now, with the Northern Territory in turmoil, anyone even slightly connected to Ice Heart Valley would meet a bad end. The Dong Family didn't dare let Tianhe reveal her identity, so they pinned a random crime on her, expelled her from the family, and severed ties to avoid being implicated."

Yang Kai continued, "It was only after meeting Tianhe that Junior Sister Ji Yao and I learned of Ice Heart Valley's situation, so we rushed here without delay."

After finishing, he picked up the tea at his side and took a gentle sip.

"Intolerable!" An Ruoyun could no longer hold back. With a slap, she shattered the other armrest as well.

In the great hall, the women of Ice Heart Valley all wore faces of anger as they listened.

In the past, every man in the Northern Territory had considered it an honor to marry a disciple of Ice Heart Valley. But now, with the dragon stranded in shallow waters and the tiger fallen from the mountain, even a mere Dong Family from Taiping City dared to bully them.

A disciple who had married into the Dong Family for a hundred years, honored as the matron, was now expelled from the clan in public and branded a thief.

This made the group of women furious beyond words.

"Tianhe, come here!" An Ruoyun lifted her gaze, looking at Shi Tianhe, who had been standing behind Yang Kai the whole time.

At her words, Shi Tianhe's body trembled violently. Her steps faltered, unable to move forward, until Yu Dan gently pushed her and gave her an encouraging look. Only then did Shi Tianhe walk up to An Ruoyun and kneel with a thud.

She said nothing, but tears streamed down her face.

An Ruoyun raised her palm high, her face full of disappointment. She seemed about to strike, but after several attempts, she couldn't bring herself to do it. In the end, she stomped her foot and withdrew her hand.

Taking a deep breath, An Ruoyun said in a low voice, "Rebellious disciple, do you know your mistake?"

With tears streaming down her face, Shi Tianhe nodded and said, "Disciple knows her mistake!"

An Ruoyun said angrily, "A hundred years ago, I warned you not to marry into the Dong Family just to repay a debt of gratitude. There are many ways to repay kindness; you didn't need to sacrifice your entire life's happiness. You refused to listen, and now look at the outcome. Why did you have to degrade yourself like this!"

Shi Tianhe kowtowed, unable to rise, and sobbed, "Please punish me, Master!"

An Ruoyun said coldly, "Don't call me Master. A hundred years ago, you were no longer my disciple. Our master-disciple relationship was severed long ago. Today's consequences are the result of your own actions. Whether it's bitter or sour, you must bear it yourself. It has nothing to do with me or Ice Heart Valley."

"Please punish me, Master!" Shi Tianhe pleaded in grief.

An Ruoyun shouted angrily, "You still dare call me Master!"

Changsun Ying couldn't bear to watch any longer. She stood up and said, "Senior Sister, don't be angry. Although Tianhe didn't listen to you back then and defied you, she's suffered enough now. I believe she won't make the same mistake again. Besides... this time, she risked her life to return to Ice Heart Valley because she heard the sect was in trouble. With such resolve and loyalty, can't you forgive her?"

The other sisters all nodded, chiming in to persuade her.

An Ruoyun listened in silence, saying nothing.

Seeing this, Yu Dan hurried forward, knelt before An Ruoyun, and said, "Master, please forgive Junior Sister Tianhe this once."

Zi Yu also stepped forward, knelt before An Ruoyun, and said, "Master, Junior Sister Tianhe already knows her mistake. Please forgive her."

Like Shi Tianhe, both were disciples under An Ruoyun and thus qualified to plead for her.

An Ruoyun was so furious she could hardly speak. Pointing at Yu Dan and Zi Yu, her slender finger trembling with anger, she ground out, "Well, well! It seems your wings have grown strong—you no longer listen, yet you still have the nerve to call me Master!"

Yu Dan and Zi Yu trembled and quickly said, "Please calm your anger, Master. We wouldn't dare."

"If you don't dare, then hurry and stand up," An Ruoyun said angrily.

Yu Dan and Zi Yu exchanged a glance and said in unison, "Then, Master, please forgive Junior Sister Tianhe."

"Are you still bargaining with me?" An Ruoyun was beside herself with rage.

"Enough!" Bing Yun suddenly spoke up.

As soon as she spoke, everyone fell silent.

Bing Yun glanced around before fixing her gaze on Shi Tianhe, who was still kneeling with her head to the ground. "Your name is Shi Tianhe?"

Shi Tianhe quickly turned, suppressing her sobs, and replied, "Disciple Shi Tianhe greets Ancestor!"

"Mm." Bing Yun nodded gently, then said, "A hundred years ago, you defied your Master and committed a grave mistake, resulting in your expulsion from the sect. By rights, you are no longer a member of Ice Heart Valley. Whether you live or die has nothing to do with us..."

Hearing this, Shi Tianhe trembled, despair filling her heart.

Bing Yun's tone shifted. "However, considering your devotion to the sect and your willingness to risk your life to return, I can give you a chance!"

Shi Tianhe hurriedly said, "Please instruct me, Ancestor!"

Bing Yun smiled. "I will personally readmit you to Ice Heart Valley. However... whether you can once again become your Master's disciple will depend on your future conduct."

Shi Tianhe looked up in surprise and gratitude at Bing Yun, but soon turned anxiously to An Ruoyun and said in a trembling voice, "If Master does not agree, I dare not return to Ice Heart Valley."

Bing Yun smiled. "Your Master is my disciple. Since I have spoken, how could she dare disagree? What do you say, Ruoyun?"

Under Bing Yun's gaze, An Ruoyun immediately bowed and said, "Disciple will obey Master's command!"

After speaking, she looked at Shi Tianhe and said, "Why are you still kneeling? Hurry and thank Ancestor!"

Shi Tianhe shuddered, quickly kowtowed, and said, "Thank you, Ancestor, for your mercy. Thank you, Master, for your mercy."

After giving thanks, she finally stood up, unable to hide her joy, though her face was still streaked with tears.

An Ruoyun scolded, "What a sight you are."

As she spoke, she tossed a brocade handkerchief toward Shi Tianhe.

Shi Tianhe caught it but didn't wipe her tears. She just clutched it tightly, using her sleeve to dry her face. Zi Yu and Yu Dan, who had also stood up, exchanged glances with her and couldn't help but smile.

"From now on, you will cultivate at Zi Yu's side. You need not worry about the Dong Family. When this matter is settled, I will seek justice for you myself!" An Ruoyun said coldly.

Hearing this, Shi Tianhe knew her Master still cared for her. Otherwise, she wouldn't have said she would seek justice for her.

She shook her head and said, "Master, the Dong Family is already gone..."

"Gone!" A cold light flashed in An Ruoyun's beautiful eyes as she snorted. "That spares them, then. Since they're gone, just stay in the valley and cultivate. If you make another grave mistake, I will not forgive you lightly."

"Yes!" Shi Tianhe replied respectfully.

"Congratulations to Senior Bing Yun and Junior Sister An on being reunited with your disciples," Yang Kai, who had been sipping tea on the side, finally said with a smile now that everything was settled.

Bing Yun smiled. "It's all thanks to you. In fact... several major events in Ice Heart Valley have been connected to you. It seems there really is a bond between us."

The reason she herself had been able to escape from the Jixu Secret Realm was largely thanks to Yang Kai. Now, both Ji Yao and Shi Tianhe had returned because of him as well. Three times now—if that isn't fate, what is?

"Just a coincidence," Yang Kai smiled, then grew serious. "Senior, earlier, Junior Sister Ji Yao and I only heard a few rumors. We still don't know the true situation in Ice Heart Valley. Could you tell us what's really going on?"

At Yang Kai's question, everyone's expressions turned solemn. Bing Yun said, "What do you want to know?"

Yang Kai asked, "Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley should be about equal in strength. How did Wenqing Sect manage to force Ice Heart Valley into such dire straits?"

Bing Yun sighed. "The two sects were indeed evenly matched, but more than half a year ago, Wenqing Sect's Vice Sect Master suddenly broke through to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. With him and Feng Xuan joining forces, I was no match. Moreover, Ice Heart Valley has always kept to itself and had little contact with other sects, while Wenqing Sect has drawn in many allies. We could not withstand them and had to retreat into the valley and activate the Grand Guardian Array."

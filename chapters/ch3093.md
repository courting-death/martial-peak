## Chapter 3093 — The Energy of an Origin Condensing Pill

Money could make even ghosts turn a millstone; that was a principle that worked anywhere. As Gale's First Chief, He Yunxiang naturally did not lack for wealth.

The guard took the Spatial Ring and swept it with his Divine Sense, then immediately raised a brow. "Well, since Madam is so insistent, I will go up and pass on the message. As for whether he sees you or not..."

He Yunxiang smiled. "Passing on the message is enough." There was no reason to make things difficult for a servant.

The man turned and left, sweeping upward at great speed.

"Madam, please sit." Perhaps because they knew they would have a share of the good things in that ring, the remaining guards' attitudes toward He Yunxiang improved quite a bit. One of them even took out a chair and set it before her.

He Yunxiang thanked him for the kindness, but smiled and shook her head in refusal.

In front of Yang Kai, how could she dare take a seat so presumptuously?

They did not wait long before the guard who had gone up the mountain hurried back down. Coming before He Yunxiang, he said, "Madam, the Family Head is currently in retreat, so I am afraid you will be disappointed." His tone shifted, and he added with a beaming smile, "However, Second Elder, who is on duty, is willing to meet you."

"Second Elder? Elder Yan Qing?" He Yunxiang was clearly familiar with the Yan Family's several Elders.

"Precisely."

Although He Yunxiang's visit was somewhat presumptuous, she was, after all, a Void King Realm cultivator, and definitely an expert above the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. Moreover, she was an exceptional beauty. After Yan Qing heard the report, he could not very well refuse, and out of curiosity, he wanted to meet her.

The Yan Family could dominate several Cultivation Stars not by relying solely on the strength of their own clan, but also on the many outside experts they had recruited. If they could take this beautiful woman under their command, it would naturally add another capable general to the Yan Family.

"Please follow me." The guard gestured with his hand.

He Yunxiang nodded in thanks. Since matters had reached this point, there was no way to force anything. She would go meet that Yan Qing first, then make plans.

"This..." Seeing Yang Kai also step forward to follow, the guard could not help frowning. The Yan Family's Twin Peaks were not somewhere just anyone could go. Although this young man had come with the beautiful woman, no matter how the guard looked at him, he seemed like an ordinary person who had never cultivated, far from qualified to go up the Twin Peaks.

"He came with me, so please make a small exception," He Yunxiang said with a gentle smile.

The guard frowned, but did not continue making things difficult. He nodded and led the way ahead. Rules were dead, but people were alive. Having eaten another’s food, one’s mouth was short; having taken another’s gifts, one’s hands were soft. Since he had received benefits, it would not be proper to keep haggling over such a small matter.

The party of three wound their way up the mountain.

Many cultivators also came and went on the mountain, each with decent cultivation. When they saw He Yunxiang, many of the men could not help taking a few extra looks, examining her without the slightest restraint.

He Yunxiang seemed very unaccustomed to such gazes. As they walked along, her dark brows knitted, and an inexplicable fire of anger burned in her chest with nowhere to vent. In the past, when she had disguised herself as a man, she had killed and set fires, settling grudges as she pleased. When had she ever been stared at like this?

Fortunately, the farther up they went, the fewer people there were.

After the time it took to drink a cup of tea, they arrived before a great hall.

The guard said, "Madam, please enter. Second Elder is waiting inside."

As he spoke, he glanced at Yang Kai in some shock. He himself was at the Third Stage of the Origin Returning Realm, while that Madam was in the Void King Realm, so the two of them naturally moved quickly. He had not expected this young man to actually be able to keep up.

From beginning to end, he had seen no sign of him using even the slightest bit of power.

Truly hellish!

He Yunxiang thanked him again, then finally stepped forward and walked inside.

The great hall was magnificent, with carved beams and painted rafters. An elderly man sat upright in one corner of the hall, holding a cup of fragrant tea and savoring it. Seeing someone enter, he raised his eyes and looked over, and his gaze could not help but brighten as he secretly praised what a charming woman she was.

After sensing the aura on He Yunxiang's body, he could not help being startled.

She was actually at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm?

Just now, when the clan guard had come to report, he had only said that a beautiful woman requested an audience with the Family Head, and that her cultivation was not low, at least at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. But that guard's eyesight was poor, and he had actually failed to see that she was a Third Stage expert.

He slowly rose and greeted her with a smile.

The Second Stage of the Void King Realm was not qualified to make him do this, but the Third Stage was. He himself was only at the Third Stage!

He Yunxiang walked forward, gathered her robes, and saluted. "This humble one greets Elder Yan Qing!"

Yan Qing returned the salute and smiled kindly. "Madam is too polite. May I ask how Madam is addressed?"

He Yunxiang gave her name.

Yan Qing's expression did not change, but a trace of suspicion flashed through his eyes. Among the experts of some standing in his mind, there was no one named He Yunxiang, especially since the other party was a woman. In the entire Star Domain, how many women at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm could there be? Where had this He Yunxiang popped out from?

Although he was puzzled, he did not rudely probe into another person's background. Since she had come to his door, she must have something to ask. Would he need to worry about not learning her origins?

However, from the look of things, this woman seemed to have only just advanced to the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and could not quite restrain the aura on her body.

He invited He Yunxiang to sit with considerable warmth.

Yang Kai also sat down leisurely, which made Yan Qing frown involuntarily, secretly thinking that this was truly a boy who did not know etiquette. However, before he understood the relationship between He Yunxiang and Yang Kai, he could not very well flare up, so he simply pretended not to see it.

He clapped his hands, and servants naturally came to present fragrant tea.

After a spell of idle chat, Yan Qing probed into He Yunxiang's background, intentionally or otherwise, but she cleverly steered the topic aside each time. This left Yan Qing somewhat displeased. After a cup of tea, he came straight to the point and said, "Madam He, may I ask what important business brings you to my Yan Family?"

The smiling expression on He Yunxiang's face withdrew, and she said solemnly, "This humble one has come uninvited because there is indeed a matter for which I seek help. I hope Elder Yan Qing will forgive the offense."

Yan Qing chuckled. "Madam He, you may as well speak of it. My Yan Family has some influence in the Star Domain, more or less. If it is something we can help with, we naturally will not refuse." Anyone could say polite words. If it really was a small favor, he could casually help, and having the other party owe the Yan Family a favor would not be bad. Perhaps there might even be a chance to recruit her.

"Elder Yan Qing is too modest. Looking across the Star Domain, I am afraid only the Yan Family can help this humble one with this matter."

"Madam He thinks too highly of my Yan Family." Yan Qing laughed loudly, but inwardly he grew vigilant. From her tone, why did it sound as if what this woman sought was no small thing? If that was truly the case, then he would have to carefully weigh it.

"This humble one wishes to make a trip to the Ancestral Domain," He Yunxiang said.

Yan Qing's laughter stopped abruptly, as if someone had pinched his throat. The smile on his face also slowly faded, and he looked at He Yunxiang. "Does Madam know what she is saying?"

He Yunxiang smiled bitterly and said, "If the matter were not difficult, I would not have specially come to seek your noble clan's help. This humble one knows that if anyone in the entire Star Domain understands the method to enter the Ancestral Domain, I am afraid it can only be the Yan Family. I ask Elder Yan Qing to grant this humble one's wish."

Yan Qing said with a cold face, "I do not know where Madam heard such groundless rumors, but no one in my Yan Family knows how to enter the Ancestral Domain."

He was exceedingly annoyed in his heart. She simply did not know the height of the heavens or the depth of the earth. She had not the slightest friendship with the Yan Family, and this was even their first meeting, yet she had actually set her sights on this. Had this woman lost her mind?

The method of entering the Ancestral Domain was a secret within the entire Yan Family. Only a few people at the very peak of strength knew a little about it.

"Five hundred years ago, your noble clan's Old Ancestor once..."

Before she could finish speaking, Yan Qing flicked his long sleeve, rose, and said, "Madam, please leave. This old man will not see you out."

There was nothing worth saying to her. If this woman had asked for some other matter, even if it were difficult, they could still sit down and discuss it properly, but the method of entering the Ancestral Domain absolutely could not be leaked. It was only on account of He Yunxiang being at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm that Yan Qing held back; otherwise, he would have already acted to teach her a lesson.

He Yunxiang's expression did not change. Clearly, she had long anticipated this situation. After hesitating for a moment, she said, "Since this humble one has come to ask for something, there naturally will be recompense."

Yan Qing smiled proudly. "What does my Yan Family not have? What can Madam give my Yan Family as recom... pense... What is this?"

His eyes bulged as he stared blankly at the Spiritual Pill pinched between He Yunxiang's fingers.

That Spiritual Pill was the size of a dragon's eye, completely crystal clear, white and flawless. It looked like an extremely high-quality pearl, but the pill fragrance it gave off was something Yan Qing had never smelled before. When he breathed it in, the Saint Yuan inside his body actually stirred because of it.

His heart filled with shock. In any case, he was also at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and he was even the Yan Family's Second Elder. What Spiritual Pill had he not seen before? What Spiritual Pill had he not eaten before? But the Spiritual Pill before his eyes was something he had never seen or heard of.

He could tell at a glance that this Spiritual Pill's grade was extremely high. In his understanding, there was no Spiritual Pill in the world that could compare with it.

He Yunxiang pursed her lips and smiled. "If this humble one uses this as recompense, I wonder whether Elder Yan Qing will be satisfied?"

Yan Qing's expression shifted. Instinctively, he felt that the Spiritual Pill in He Yunxiang's hand would be of enormous help to him. Even with his fairly good temperament, he could not help swallowing a mouthful of saliva. His eyes locked tightly on that Spiritual Pill, as if he were looking at a naked peerless beauty putting on seductive airs, and greed surged in his heart.

There were not many things in this world that could stir his greed. Whatever he wanted, the Yan Family had it. But at this moment, he urgently wanted to know what kind of change would happen to him if he took this Spiritual Pill.

"What Spiritual Pill is this, exactly?" Yan Qing asked again. The proud look on his face had long since vanished. This thing... the Yan Family really did not have it.

"This pill is called an Origin Condensing Pill."

Yan Qing thought carefully for a while, then shook his head. "I have never heard of it."

He Yunxiang smiled faintly, “In this vast world, there are all kinds of strange things. Although Elder Yan Qing is experienced and knowledgeable, there are still some things you have never seen before.”

The thing came from Yang Kai's hands, so how could Yan Qing possibly have seen it before?

He Yunxiang was now at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and the next step was to advance to the Dao Origin Realm. The Origin Condensing Pill was an indispensable Spiritual Pill, one that could assist her in transforming the Saint Yuan in her body into Origin Qi. Yang Kai had casually given her several bottles along the way for backup.

Taking one out at this moment had indeed achieved the intended effect.

"What effect does it have?" Yan Qing asked curiously.

He Yunxiang showed a troubled expression, looking as if she did not know whether she should say it or not.

Seeing this, Yan Qing grew even more anxious and said in a deep voice, "Since that is the case, Madam He, please leave."

He was certain He Yunxiang could not possibly leave so easily.

Sure enough, when He Yunxiang heard him say this, the hesitation on her face vanished at once. She opened her mouth and said: "Above the Void King Realm!"

Yan Qing's whole body shook violently, and a terrifying light burst from his eyes.

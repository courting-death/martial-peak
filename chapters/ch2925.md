## Chapter 2925 — Cicada Shedding Its Shell

The middle-aged man’s cultivation was not high, only at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm. It was clear his aptitude was poor, and reaching this level was already his limit. In his lifetime, it was impossible for him to make any further progress.

At this moment, atop the collapsed mountain, aside from him, there was no trace of anyone else.

Yet the aura of the mark everyone sensed was actually coming from him.

Feng Ming’s brow furrowed as he instantly grasped the truth, shouting in a low voice, “Cicada shedding its shell!”

Yang Kai also sighed, “We were careless. It seems Gong Yue is in good condition.”

The mark Yang Kai had placed on Gong Yue had been transferred to this middle-aged man, misleading the four of them. The mark could not have moved for no reason; it could only have been Gong Yue’s doing.

Yet the fact that Gong Yue could accomplish this without drawing Yang Kai's attention at all made it clear he was no longer in that muddled state of consciousness. Either he had regained his wits, or the Demonic Intent had seized his body and achieved perfect fusion with it—neither outcome was what the group hoped to see.

Each of them put away their Imperial Treasures.

The middle-aged man standing amidst the ruins was scared out of his wits. At this moment, he cried out in grief, “This old man greets… the honored sirs, please… spare… spare my life!”

He had no idea what Yang Kai and the others had come here for, but he knew these four were not people he could resist, especially the old man who had acted, who called himself Elder Feng Ming of Wuhua Hall.

He naturally knew what Wuhua Hall was. It was one of the top sects in the Southern Territory. His own small sect was not even qualified to look up to them. For an elder of Wuhua Hall to come in person and even make a move against him—if the other party hadn’t withdrawn his killing blow at the last moment, he would already be dead and buried.

He simply couldn’t understand how he had provoked Wuhua Hall and drawn out such powerful figures. Fear and terror left him unable to think, so he could only beg for mercy.

“Elder, don’t panic.” Yang Kai gave the old man a gentle smile, his Divine Sense surging as he quietly used a bit of it to soothe the man’s frightened emotions. “We are not here to trouble you, only to track down a dangerous criminal. There’s been a misunderstanding.”

“A dangerous criminal…” The old man, comforted, indeed calmed down a lot, his emotions no longer as panicked as before.

“Exactly.” Yang Kai’s expression grew solemn. He pointed in front of him, and as Emperor Yuan surged, an image appeared—Gong Yue’s face. “This is the man. Elder, have you seen him recently?”

Since the mark had been transferred to this middle-aged man, it meant Gong Yue had at least been here. Now that the mark was gone, tracking Gong Yue had become a problem, and this man might be the only clue left. Naturally, Yang Kai couldn’t let him go easily.

“It’s him?” The old man was shocked when he saw Gong Yue’s appearance.

Yang Kai said, “You’ve seen him!”

The old man replied, “I thought I was dreaming. Yesterday, while I was meditating, I seemed to see this man, but when I looked carefully, he was gone. I thought… I thought it was… just an illusion.”

Feng Ming said in a deep voice, “I want to see your memories!”

As soon as he finished speaking, he reached out and grabbed the middle-aged man, who cried out in alarm as his body was pulled uncontrollably in front of Feng Ming.

He was terrified. Feng Ming had just attacked him, and his attitude was not friendly. He thought something terrible was about to happen. In a panic, he tried to resist, but before an Emperor Realm master, what could a Void King do? He was subdued in an instant, unable to move a finger.

Feng Ming pointed at his forehead, clearly intending to peer into his memories from yesterday.

At that moment, Yang Kai suddenly sensed danger and shouted, “Elder Feng, be careful!”

Feng Ming was caught off guard. The middle-aged man he had pulled in front of him suddenly began to swell, his internal power turning chaotic and violent. The man himself had no idea what was happening, flailing helplessly, his face full of confusion.

With a bang, the middle-aged man exploded on the spot.

Amidst the blood and flesh, a streak of black light shot toward the nearby Feng Ming.

Although Feng Ming was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, he was caught off guard by this sudden turn of events. He had just been about to check the man’s memories, but in the next instant, the man self-destructed right before his eyes, and something inexplicable flew out from within.

The black light was small, but Feng Ming’s vision was completely filled by it. He could see nothing else, and at the same time, a faint whisper echoed in his ears, shaking his mind and leaving his Sea of Consciousness wide open.

“Demon Suppression!”

Yang Kai let out a low shout as a Shamanic Art burst forth, narrowly intercepting Feng Ming. The black light struck the technique and was violently deflected, veering away and shooting toward Gao Xueting.

The Scorching Sun Mirror floated high behind Gao Xueting, radiating dazzling light like a blazing sun.

Gao Xueting’s expression was solemn as her hands formed seals.

A beam of light shot down from the Scorching Sun Mirror, directly trapping the black light.

The beam seemed to have a powerful binding force. The black light struggled left and right but could not break free for the moment. Under the might of the Scorching Sun Mirror, it even showed signs of melting, emitting wisps of black smoke.

“Three Thousand Sword Dao—Silken Bind!” Chen Wenhao pointed with his Waterflow Sword, sending out strands of sword light as fine as hair. They shot into the beam, forming layer upon layer of restraints around the struggling black light.

With the combined strength of Chen Wenhao and Gao Xueting, they finally subdued the black light.

“This… is Demonic Intent?” Feng Ming wiped the cold sweat from his forehead, still feeling lingering fear.

If Yang Kai hadn’t reacted quickly and used a secret art to block it, he would have been invaded by Demonic Intent. He didn’t know if he could resist it, but with Gong Yue’s example before him, if Demonic Intent was truly that powerful, he might not have been able to escape either.

The four of them had come to hunt down Gong Yue, but they had been played for fools by a small trick and nearly failed before even starting. This left Feng Ming’s face extremely unsightly.

“This isn’t a complete Demonic Intent!” Yang Kai frowned, carefully observing the restrained black light and judging, “It was split off by Gong Yue. He knew we were after him, so he deliberately set a trap here.”

“Demonic Intent can be split?” Feng Ming was startled.

Chen Wenhao and Gao Xueting’s expressions also grew grave. If that was true, the problem was even more serious than they thought. They had believed that killing Gong Yue would be enough, but if Demonic Intent could be split, then even killing Gong Yue might not solve anything.

Because no one knew how many pieces he had split Demonic Intent into or where he had hidden them.

Of those present, only Yang Kai had directly confronted Demonic Intent, so everyone turned to him to see what he thought.

“Based on what we know, Demonic Intent can indeed be split.”

As soon as he said this, the other three’s hearts sank.

“However…” Yang Kai changed his tone, “it shouldn’t be without limits. This is half of that Demonic Intent, so it can probably only be split into two, and there should be a price to pay.”

“Even so, it’s still quite troublesome.” Chen Wenhao frowned deeply.

“Let’s destroy this Demonic Intent first.” Having suffered once, Feng Ming was more wary of this thing than anyone.

Hearing this, Gao Xueting prepared to increase the Scorching Sun Mirror’s power. No matter how strange Demonic Intent was, it could still be destroyed. The current situation had already proven that. Gao Xueting felt that with just a bit more effort, she could completely melt the restrained Demonic Intent.

“Wait.” Yang Kai raised his hand to stop her. “Gong Yue has already disappeared. If we want to track him again, our only clue is this half of Demonic Intent. If we destroy it, we’ll lose the trail.”

Feng Ming nodded, “You’re right. Gong Yue hoped to catch us off guard with this move, maybe even cause one of us to fall to the demonic path, but he didn’t expect to leave a flaw. Truly, the clever outsmart themselves.”

Chen Wenhao said, “That may be so, but how can we use this Demonic Intent to track Gong Yue? Who would dare touch it?”

Demonic Intent was too strange. None of the Emperor Realm masters present dared to make direct contact with it, but if they didn’t, they couldn’t track Gong Yue’s whereabouts.

Yang Kai said softly, “If you all trust me, leave it to me.”

Gao Xueting exclaimed, “You? What are you planning?”

Yang Kai smiled, “Senior Sister Gao, don’t worry. Since I could resist the Demon Script’s corruption in that cave residence, I can naturally withstand the temptation of this mere half of Demonic Intent.”

“Are you… sure?”

Gao Xueting looked worried. It wasn’t that she doubted Yang Kai’s strength, but no one could guarantee this. If Yang Kai was corrupted by Demonic Intent, it would be exactly what Gong Yue wanted.

“Are you confident?” Chen Wenhao also looked at Yang Kai seriously.

Feng Ming didn’t say anything, but it was clear he felt the same.

Yang Kai said, “I’m seventy percent sure. Besides… aren’t the three of you here to protect me?”

Chen Wenhao nodded, “If you’re seventy percent sure, this method is feasible!”

Gao Xueting glared at him, “If you think it’s so doable, why don’t you try it?”

Chen Wenhao coughed, “I’m not confident. I can’t.”

To use this half of Demonic Intent to track Gong Yue, one had to make deep contact with it, even probe its depths. That meant opening oneself completely to Demonic Intent, which was no joke.

“Destroy it!” Gao Xueting said, preparing to act. She didn’t want to see Yang Kai take the risk. Even if he said he was seventy percent sure, what about the other thirty percent?

Rather than let Yang Kai take the risk, it was better to cut the knot and destroy the Demonic Intent. Although Gong Yue’s trail would be lost, that didn’t mean he couldn’t be found. At worst, they could mobilize all the cultivators in the Southern Territory. No matter how well Gong Yue hid, he would have nowhere to run.

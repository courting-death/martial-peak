## Chapter 3132 — Blood Sea Banner, Ten Thousand Souls Banner

In the blink of an eye, they arrived before Yang Kai, their figures resolving into a man and a woman.

Both were elderly. One was a white-haired, white-bearded old man, the other an old woman with a face full of wrinkles, and both gave off a thick corpse Qi from head to toe. At this moment, the old man was glaring at Yang Kai with such venom that he seemed to want to skin him alive and tear out his tendons.

The old woman looked around the blood pool, then instantly grew hysterical as well. Glaring at Yang Kai, she gnashed her teeth and said, "Boy, you dare kill my Netherworld Sect's people? This old woman will make sure you can neither live nor die!"

Netherworld Sect disciples had always done whatever they pleased on Cuiwei Star. Even when they met resistance, it was quickly suppressed. Never had they imagined that someone would dare slaughter their disciples, let alone kill so many in one go.

As the Netherworld Sect's people in charge here, the two of them naturally had to bear some responsibility. More than that, many of the dead disciples had been like children to them. How could they tolerate such a blood debt?

"You are from the Netherworld Sect?" Yang Kai raised his brow.

Great Wilderness Star Domain, Netherworld Sect... Truly, enemies met on a narrow road.

“Boy, it is already too late to beg for mercy now. Your Soul belongs to me, and your fleshly body belongs to her. Do not even dream of entering reincarnation again for all eternity.” The decrepit old man’s jackal-like voice rang out, making anyone who heard it feel as if a hundred claws were scratching at their heart, unbearably uncomfortable.

Yang Kai looked at them disdainfully. "Do I look like I was going to beg for mercy? Old thing, are your eyes failing you?"

"Impudent!" Talked back to by Yang Kai, the old man flew into a rage. His withered hand shot out, grabbing toward Yang Kai. The hand was so thin it was almost nothing but skin over bone, its nails pitch-black, as horrifying as a ghost claw.

As that withered hand reached out, it stirred up a Yin wind that seeped into the marrow and made one shudder.

Yang Kai laughed aloud. "Brains are a fine thing, but it looks like not everyone has them. I was going to ask you a few questions, but since you are in such a hurry to die, I will grant your wish." He punched forward.

The old man's expression changed drastically. He had not sensed any trace of Yang Kai using strength, only a simple punch coming at him, yet that punch filled him with an intense unease, as though he could not block it.

Though his cultivation was only at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm, an old man who had survived this long was bound to be cunning, and his instincts in matters of life and death were profoundly sharp.

Before his grab could land, he hurriedly pulled his hand back.

He was still a step too late. The violent fist force swept across his five fingers, carrying an irresistible destructive power that blew them apart into a bloody mess.

"What!" The old woman's expression changed as well. What in the world was this boy? How could such a casual punch hold such terrifying might?

Only then did the howl of the wind arrive, blowing hard enough to make both of them sway.

The two exchanged a glance and saw the horror in each other's eyes. Neither dared hesitate any longer, and both brought out their trump cards.

At the same time, they reached toward the blood pool and beckoned.

Two great banners flew out of the blood pool and landed in their hands. One was blood-red, as if cast from blood itself, and gave off a dense, bloody aura. The other was black as night, without the slightest glimmer of light, and countless human faces could vaguely be seen floating, struggling, and roaring within it.

“Blood Sea Banner, Ten Thousand Souls Banner!” Peng Shizong, who was watching from afar, cried out softly. Looking at those two Secret Treasures forged from countless lives, his eyes filled with deep hatred and disgust, and everyone else wore similar expressions.

Countless people on Cuiwei Star had died because of these two Secret Treasures. How could they not know the names of the Blood Sea Banner and Ten Thousand Souls Banner?

With their life-bound Secret Treasures in hand, the old man and the old woman immediately felt reassured, and both fixed sinister stares on Yang Kai.

Yang Kai said in a deep voice, "So this was why you slaughtered the city?"

He could sense that the so-called Blood Sea Banner and Ten Thousand Souls Banner were both extremely evil Secret Treasures. They had clearly been soaking in the blood pool so the blood and lonely ghosts within could nourish their spirituality and power.

The moment the two Secret Treasures appeared, the entire blood pool quieted down, and even the water's color faded considerably.

"For all this trash to enter my banner is their honour," the old man snorted.

The old woman smiled maliciously. "No need to be impatient. You will be going in soon enough."

Yang Kai chuckled coldly. "Then please hurry up and take me in."

The old woman snorted. "As you wish."

The Blood Sea Banner in her hand unfurled, descending over Yang Kai from above, and the whole sky turned a deep crimson.

Yang Kai raised his head and glanced at it, completely unconcerned. The old woman had been guarding against resistance from him, but even after she swept him into the Blood Sea Banner, he did not move at all. She immediately sneered, "You truly know neither the height of Heaven nor the depth of Earth. You actually dared enter this old woman's Blood Sea Banner. Now let us see how you die."

The old man said, "There is something unusual about this person. Do not be careless. Let me inside, and the two of us will deal with him together."

The old woman said, "You are far too cautious. Once inside the Blood Sea Banner, even a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator would have to lose a layer of skin." Despite her words, she still took hold of the old man's arm with one hand, and with a sway of her figure, brought him into the Blood Sea Banner with her.

Peng Shizong and the others watched from afar, anxious in their hearts. They thought to themselves that this young man looked extraordinary, so how could he be so foolish? The Blood Sea Banner was an extremely powerful Secret Treasure, and with the old woman's First Stage Void King Realm cultivation driving it, how could he possibly survive once he entered? Not only would he fail to kill the old woman, he would even add to the Blood Sea Banner's power for nothing.

They had thought a saviour had arrived, only for him to turn out to be a hotheaded fool.

"Brother Peng, what should we do?" someone asked, looking at Peng Shizong. At this moment, only their own people remained near the blood pool. The Netherworld Sect's disciples had been slaughtered clean, and the two Void King Realm masters had entered the Blood Sea Banner. It could be said that they were the only people left in the entire city.

"Hurry up and run!" Before Peng Shizong could answer, someone shouted.

Peng Shizong smiled bitterly. "Run? Where could we run?"

If their cultivation had not been sealed, perhaps they might still have had a slim chance of survival. But now that everyone's cultivation was locked inside their bodies, they could only flee on foot. Even if they ran for an entire day, they would not cover a hundred li.

And what did a hundred li amount to for a Void King Realm master? They could catch up with ease. They had already reached a dead end, unless another miracle appeared.

"Then are we supposed to just wait here to die?"

Peng Shizong said, "Perhaps... that Sir can fight his way out of the Blood Sea Banner."

Everyone immediately turned to look at him, all of them staring as if he were a fool.

Peng Shizong rubbed his nose. "It is still a glimmer of hope."

It was just that this hope was far too faint, almost impossible.

"Brother Peng, if you will not go, then I will. We cultivators do not fear life and death, but having our fleshly bodies and Souls refined into a Secret Treasure and being unable to enter reincarnation is far too miserable. I would rather find a place to cut my own throat than wait for those two old things to come out."

These words stirred agreement among the crowd. Sometimes death itself was not frightening, but the thought of their Souls and fleshly bodies still being enslaved after death was hard to accept.

Peng Shizong said, "In that case, everyone, safe travels."

Seeing that he seemed determined to stay, the others stopped trying to persuade him. Time was precious now, and no one knew when the two Netherworld Sect people would come killing their way out, so fleeing for their lives was naturally the priority.

In an instant, more than half the crowd left. Only several dozen remained, most of them wearing lost expressions and the look of those resigned to death. They probably also felt that escape was useless, so they might as well save their strength and stay here. Perhaps some miracle really would happen.

The blood sea churned, and all in sight was red. Its stench was overpowering, and within the blood sea, countless blood corpses surged and rolled, their forms terrifying, each like a malicious ghost.

Yang Kai stood above the blood sea, coldly watching this hellish scene.

The old woman and the old man suddenly appeared together.

The old woman sneered, "Boy, now that you are inside my Blood Sea Banner, obediently accept death." The old man also laughed strangely. "Truly stupid beyond saving."

Yang Kai said, "These corpses are all cultivators from Cuiwei Star?"

The corpses rolling through this blood sea numbered far more than thousands or tens of thousands. There were probably several hundred thousand of them.

The old woman said, "Death is staring you in the face, yet you still have the leisure to pry into other people's affairs. Boy, you really are calm."

"Even if I die, I should die knowing the answer, shouldn't I?" Yang Kai turned to look at the old woman.

The old woman let out a crow-like laugh. "At least you know how to read the situation. Rest assured, when this old woman acts, you will not feel any pain. It will be over in an instant."

“You still have not answered my question. Has age made you not only blind, but deaf as well?”

The old woman's face darkened. "Correct! Most of these people were cultivators from this place, and thanks to their contribution, this old woman was able to advance to the Void King Realm."

Yang Kai said, "Your Netherworld Sect invaded this Star Domain for this?"

The old woman said, "The Purgatory Blood Sea Banner and Ten Thousand Souls Banner require vast amounts of material. In the Great Wilderness Star Domain, my Netherworld Sect has to show some restraint, but here there is no need. Speaking of which, there really are a great many cultivators in this place. I wonder just how far this old woman's Blood Sea Banner can be raised."

Yang Kai nodded. "This is its limit. You no longer have any chance to raise it further."

The old woman sneered. "Even now, you still think you can escape?"

"Why would I escape?" Yang Kai looked at her strangely.

The old woman froze, then said incredulously, "Could it be you still plan to kill us?"

"Yes."

As if they had heard the greatest joke under the Heavens, the old woman and the old man both burst into loud laughter.

"Do you two know Yin Lesheng?" Yang Kai suddenly asked.

Their laughter stopped abruptly, and they stared at Yang Kai in disbelief. The old man's voice sank even deeper. "How do you know that name?"

"Looks like there is no mistake, then. Yin Lesheng was your Netherworld Sect's previous Sect Master, was he not?"

The old man hesitated. "You know the former Sect Master?"

Impossible. The former Sect Master had left the Great Wilderness Star Domain several dozen years ago for that Star Realm, and he had never come to this Star Domain. How could anyone here know his name?

"I know him, of course I know him!" Yang Kai nodded, his voice suddenly turning savage. "He died by my hand!"

As soon as the words fell, the old man's body suddenly floated up against his will. A great force tightened around his neck; Yang Kai had somehow seized him by the throat, yet he was still staring at Yang Kai, who stood more than a dozen zhang away.

"I will send you to meet him. No need to thank me." As Yang Kai spoke, he squeezed, and a head flew off.

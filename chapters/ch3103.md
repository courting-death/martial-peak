## Chapter 3103 — Star Path Map

Yang Kai did not pursue, but not because he was magnanimous enough to overlook a petty man's offense.

Rather, it was because...

A spiritual net suddenly flew out of the forest below and shot straight toward Yan Luo. Caught off guard, he was wrapped up tight in an instant. Yan Luo was greatly alarmed and frantically urged his Saint Yuan to resist, but what made him despair was that the more he struggled, the tighter the spiritual net became. In just a few breaths, he had been bundled up like a zongzi. The fine strands bit into his flesh and blood, seeming to carry a sealing power that made the Saint Yuan in his body sluggish and hard to circulate.

At the other end of the spiritual net, an old man with an earthy yellow complexion leisurely appeared, a dry pipe in his hand, and looked toward Yang Kai with a complicated expression.

After hesitating for a moment, he held the pipe in one hand and the netted Yan Luo in the other, then reached Yang Kai in a few quick steps. Looking him up and down, he asked, "What cultivation do you have?"

Although Yang Kai had refused him earlier, he had still been unwilling to give up. He felt it would be a pity for someone who had come over from a Star Domain to be killed by Sea Hall, so he had quietly followed along. His original intention had been to look for a chance to rescue Yang Kai from danger, sell him a favor, and conveniently draw him into Longevity Pavilion.

Who could have known that the favor would go unsold, and that he would instead witness a thrilling great show?

Two First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivators had actually been killed so easily by this young man. Did that not mean that if the young man wanted to kill him, it would take no great effort either? What horrified him even more was that he had failed to see the depth of this young man's cultivation.

Even if one had some Secret Treasure that concealed cultivation, when Spiritual Energy surged during a fight, it could not be hidden. Yet from beginning to end, this young man's abilities had remained a mystery.

“How should I address you, Old Sir?” Yang Kai asked, smiling at him.

The old man said, "Xu Houtu."

Yang Kai said, "This Yang Kai must thank Elder Xu for lending a hand."

Xu Houtu shook his head helplessly. Since Yang Kai could casually kill two Dao Origin Realm cultivators, how could a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator stir up any waves? Even if he had not acted, this fellow called Yan Luo could not possibly have escaped alive. Yang Kai was clearly changing the subject because he did not want to answer his question.

After pondering for a moment, he said, "Sea Hall has nearly ten Dao Origin Realm cultivators, and its Hall Master has the powerful cultivation of the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Since you killed two of them this time, I fear they will not let the matter rest."

Yang Kai said, "Although I have no particular interest in wiping out a Sect, if someone insists on rushing over to seek death, I won't be softhearted."

Xu Houtu’s heart shook. Although his words just now had given Yang Kai some information about the Sea Hall, they had mostly been a probe. Now that he saw Yang Kai did not take even a Second Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator seriously, he immediately knew he had underestimated him.

He also gave up on drawing him into Longevity Pavilion. For such an expert, even if he really were brought into Longevity Pavilion, he would likely place an inexplicable pressure and unease on everyone there.

Only... how was this possible?

He had personally seen Yang Kai come over from a Star Domain, so how could Yang Kai possess such powerful cultivation? What Star Domain could nurture such a talent? It was simply unheard of, unseen.

"May I ask why Young Friend Yang has come to the Ancestral Domain?"

Since he could not draw him in, it was still possible to build a little friendship. Perhaps it would prove useful in the future.

Yang Kai pondered for a moment, then said, "I only want to use the Ancestral Domain to reach another Star Domain, that is all."

This Xu Houtu was not a bad person. That much could be seen from how he had followed them all the way here. Moreover, Yang Kai knew nothing at all about the Ancestral Domain, and He Yunxiang could not be counted on either. If a local could give him a bit of guidance, things would certainly be more efficient.

"I was not born in that Wuji Star Domain. I only ended up stranded there by chance. I want to return to my homeland."

Xu Houtu understood after hearing this. "That is indeed a good method."

The Ancestral Domain was connected to many Star Domains. Using the Ancestral Domain as a transfer point to reach another Star Domain was something many experts had examined before, and the conclusion was that it was possible. It only required paying a certain price, and of course, it also required extremely powerful methods.

"It is just that I do not know how to return. I wonder if Elder Xu has any good suggestions?" Yang Kai asked.

Xu Houtu said, "Then this old man must first ask which Star Domain Young Brother Yang wishes to go to. It is not that this old man is boasting, but I was born in the Ancestral Domain and have dealt with quite a few cultivators from other Star Domains. Perhaps I know someone from yours."

Yang Kai's eyes lit up. "Has Elder Xu heard of Hengluo Star Domain?"

Xu Houtu thought seriously for a while, then shook his head under Yang Kai's disappointed gaze. "That one, I have never heard of."

He comforted him again, "However, the Ancestral Domain is boundless and vast. My Longevity Pavilion guards only three to five Star Path entrances, and those three to five entrances should not be connected to Hengluo Star Domain. Otherwise, this old man could not possibly know nothing of it."

Yang Kai asked in surprise, "Elder Xu means that there are many entrances like the one from before in the Ancestral Domain?"

"Heh heh, at least several hundred."

Yang Kai was shocked.

Xu Houtu said, "You might as well go to the other Star Path entrances and inquire a little. If anyone from your homeland has come to the Ancestral Domain, you will definitely be able to find out something from those entrances."

"Where are those entrances located?"

Xu Houtu thought for a moment, then reached out and grabbed. Two Spatial Rings were drawn over to him. They were precisely the Spatial Rings owned by Yan Renhao and Duanmu Qi, who had been killed earlier. He forcibly erased the marks on the two Spatial Rings and searched inside them.

Suddenly, he took out something shaped like a scroll and tossed it to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai caught it and looked at him suspiciously.

Xu Houtu said, "Pour your Divine Sense into it and take a look."

Yang Kai unfurled the scroll and poured his Divine Sense into it. In the next instant, specks of light suddenly lit up from within the scroll. Yang Kai's heart stirred, and his Divine Sense sank a little deeper. Those lights were abruptly drawn much closer.

Most of the lights felt very far away, but one of them was quite close to him.

Xu Houtu said, "This is a Star Path Map. All the Star Paths discovered in the Ancestral Domain are recorded in it. The one nearest to you is the place where you entered the Ancestral Domain."

Yang Kai withdrew his Divine Sense, put the scroll away, and said in great delight, "This is a fine thing."

Xu Houtu smiled and said, "This thing does not have much value in the Ancestral Domain." Almost any Sect or individual cultivator with a little foundation had the ability to buy such a Star Path Map.

Yang Kai cupped his fists solemnly. "Many thanks to Elder Xu for guiding me through this confusion!"

With this Star Path Map, he could go to each Star Path entrance to inquire for news, and his future plans were settled all at once.

"It was only a small effort. No need for thanks. This old man wishes young friend an early fulfillment of your wish."

"Then I'll borrow your auspicious words."

"Since young friend has matters to attend to, please go as you like. The rest can be left to this old man."

Yang Kai nodded. "Then I will trouble Elder Xu. Farewell."

After calling to He Yunxiang, the two of them rode the Star Shuttle swiftly away.

A burst of force suddenly cut through the air. Xu Houtu casually grabbed, and a round object immediately landed in his hand. Lowering his head to look, he trembled all over.

It was a Spiritual Pill, one he had never seen before, but Xu Houtu could feel medicinal efficacy inside it so vast it was difficult to imagine. He could not help growing excited and expectant, wondering what sort of change would happen if he swallowed this Spiritual Pill.

He had intentionally planted a peach tree, and very quickly harvested a basket of plums. This return had come a little too fast.

He raised his head and glanced at Shen Cuiling, who was weeping like pear blossoms in the rain, and at Yan Luo, whom he had bound up like a dead pig. Then he took out some dried smoking leaf, lit it, and took a deep breath. Smoke billowed out and soon covered a vast area. By the time the smoke dispersed, there was no one there anymore.

...

"Sir, where are we going now?" On the Star Shuttle, He Yunxiang asked.

Having personally seen Yang Kai casually kill two cultivators above the Void King Realm, He Yunxiang had grown even more respectful toward him.

Yang Kai tossed the Star Path Map to her. "Follow the records on it and visit them all."

He did not know where the entrance connecting Hengluo Star Domain and the Ancestral Domain was, and he did not even know whether such an entrance existed. Since he knew nothing, he could only inquire one by one.

The method was a little clumsy and would certainly take time, but for now there was no better way.

He Yunxiang nodded, took the Star Path Map, and immersed her Divine Sense into it. Very quickly, she determined the direction and drove the Star Shuttle toward one of the points of light.

Five days later, in a swampy land where human tracks were rarely seen, there was a huge pool somewhere in the mire where five-colored light swirled and undulated. Looking down from high above, the place where this pool lay seemed extremely mysterious.

The five-colored radiance flowed, dazzling and resplendent. The place where this pool lay was impressively a Star Path entrance!

Around the pool, quite a few cultivators were waiting in scattered groups. Some of them emitted Dao Origin Realm auras, and each had found a place to sit cross-legged and regulate their breathing.

Although they were standing guard here waiting for newcomers, were newcomers so easy to wait for? Therefore, most of the time they spent here was used for cultivation. In this way, they did not delay the improvement of their own cultivation either. Of course, the environment here was a little harsh.

A streak of brilliant light sped over from afar, drawing the attention of many people. The cultivators sitting in meditation opened their eyes in displeasure, wanting to see exactly who was being so unrestrained and disturbing their quiet cultivation.

The light faded, and the Star Shuttle appeared. A woman with an enchanting, alluring figure entered their sight, making everyone's eyes brighten. She stood at the front of the Star Shuttle, while the rear half was occupied by a young man. What displeased them was that this young man was actually lying across the Star Shuttle with his head pillowed on both hands, one leg crossed over the other, looking utterly carefree.

What kind of person was this, putting on such airs?

Divine Senses swept over one after another, and expressions turned stunned. They were actually unable to spy out what cultivation he had.

He Yunxiang stood atop the Star Shuttle with a light smile, her voice warm as jade. "Pardon the disturbance, everyone. There is one matter I would like to ask about."

Beauties always had some natural advantages, and what was more, her words were proper, making it truly difficult for others to feel any ill will.

"What do you want to ask?"

"May I ask everyone, have you heard the name Hengluo Star Domain?"

Someone immediately replied, "I have heard of it."

He Yunxiang's eyes lit up. How could she have expected to find information at the very first stop? She looked at that person with delight and said, "Could you explain it to me in detail?"

That person was a burly great man, the muscles all over his body bulging high. Hearing this, he smiled and said, "Naturally I can. Young lady, why don't you find a secluded place with me? Whatever you want to know, I will tell you." He waggled his brows meaningfully.

He Yunxiang's face abruptly darkened. At the rear of the Star Shuttle, Yang Kai's opened eyes closed again as he pretended to nap.

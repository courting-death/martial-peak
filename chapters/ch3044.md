## Chapter 3044 — Towering Fury

Immediately afterward, Yang Kai realized something else. If even the Dragon Clan's Second Elder was comparable to a Great Emperor, then what about the Great Elder?

The Great Elder was probably even stronger than the Second Elder, and certainly no weaker!

In other words, Dragon Isle had the equivalent of two Great Emperor-level Masters overseeing it. This was simply astonishing.

In the past, Yang Kai had only heard others speak of how formidable Dragon Isle was, so he had never had a clear idea of its strength. Now he did. Two Great Emperors, and that was without even counting the rest of the Dragon Clan. Who beneath the Heavens could compare to such a foundation?

"What is your Great Elder's stance?" Yang Kai asked with a frown. Zhu Qing was being held on the Second Elder's own island, so the Second Elder certainly would not let her leave. If Yang Kai wanted to bring Zhu Qing out, his only option was to seek help from the Great Elder.

Zhu Lie said, "The Great Elder is named Zhu Yan, while the Second Elder is named Fu Zhun. Do you understand now?"

Yang Kai raised his brow. "The Second Elder belongs to the Fu branch of the Dragon Clan."

"Although the Zhu and Fu branches of the Dragon Clan share the same origin and both belong to the Dragon Clan, relations between them are not actually very harmonious."

Yang Kai immediately snapped, "Since the Great Elder belongs to your Zhu branch, why hasn't he protected Zhu Qing?"

Although he had never met Zhu Yan, Yang Kai could not help feeling some resentment toward the Great Elder now. He felt the old fellow was far too unreliable. If he could not even protect one of his own, what right did he have to remain Great Elder? He might as well retire early and find some island where he could live out his final years.

Zhu Lie said, "Zhu Qing violated the clan's rules and disgraced the Dragon Clan's bloodline. The Great Elder could not protect her even if he wanted to." He glared at Yang Kai furiously, feeling that all of this was this man's fault. If not for him, how could Zhu Qing have been imprisoned and ended up in such a plight?

After all, there had been a precedent. What happened more than ten years ago was remarkably similar to the present situation. The only difference was that the Dragon involved had not belonged to the Zhu branch, but the Fu branch.

She too had fallen in love with a human and had even given birth to a child. When word reached Dragon Isle, Second Elder Fu Zhun flew into a rage and personally captured that Fu branch Dragon and brought her back. Ignoring the persuasion and pleas of the other Dragons, she threw her straight into the Dragon Tomb and confined her there.

The Dragon Tomb was a place members of the Dragon Clan entered only when they were near death. It was forbidden ground to the Dragon Clan, and entering it was tantamount to receiving a death sentence. Anyone sent there could forget about ever emerging again.

The Dragon who had violated the clan's rules had been the clan member Fu Zhun valued most back then. It could almost be said that Fu Zhun had raised her with her own hands and regarded her as a daughter. Yet before the clan's rules, so-called familial affection counted for nothing. The Second Elder still mercilessly exiled her to the Dragon Tomb and left her to live or die on her own.

Zhu Lie still remembered that even the Great Elder had stepped forward to plead for her back then, believing a light punishment would suffice. However, the stern and impartial Second Elder had remained unmoved and insisted on having her way.

Although more than ten years had passed, Zhu Lie still remembered the incident vividly.

With that precedent before them, Zhu Qing was already fortunate not to have been thrown directly into the Dragon Tomb. How could anyone hope for more? It was precisely because of what had happened more than ten years ago that the Great Elder could not easily plead for Zhu Qing. If the Second Elder had exiled even someone she regarded as a daughter to the Dragon Tomb, why would she spare a member of the Zhu branch? Insisting on protecting Zhu Qing would only backfire.

From the looks of things, Zhu Qing had only escaped exile to the Dragon Tomb because she had not given birth to a child. Otherwise, her fate would certainly have been the same as that Fu branch Dragon's.

Although the Dragon Clan was hardly restrained in its private affairs and was even wildly promiscuous, with many male Dragons coupling with members of the Human and Monster Races and fathering children, it was an entirely different matter when a female Dragon did the same. This was truly a grave taboo among the Dragon Clan.

The main reason was the imbalance between the sexes within the Dragon Clan. Female Dragons had always been few in number, and every one of them was a treasure who carried the hope of continuing the Dragon Clan's bloodline. How could they be allowed to have illicit relations with Humans?

Yang Kai pondered with a gloomy expression for a while, then asked eagerly, "In your opinion, could I suppress your Second Elder's bloodline?"

Zhu Lie curled his lip. "Don't even think about it!"

"I can suppress an Eighth-Tier bloodline. I haven't tried it against a Ninth-Tier one, but the Second Elder's bloodline is Tenth-Tier at most. The difference isn't that great!"

Zhu Lie shook his head. "I know what you're planning, but I advise you to abandon the idea now. The Second Elder's cultivation is unfathomable. With your current ability, you have no hope of suppressing her bloodline. Forget the Second Elder; on Dragon Isle, if I had time to prepare, you wouldn't even be able to suppress me."

"What do you mean?" Yang Kai frowned.

Zhu Lie said, "The power of the Dragon Clan's ancestors throughout the generations is gathered within the Dragon Temple. By obtaining the protection of a Secret Art from the Dragon Temple, we can offset bloodline suppression to a certain degree. You should have experienced it last time."

Yang Kai froze, suddenly recalling the first time he met Zhu Lie. Back then, he had indeed failed to suppress Zhu Lie through his bloodline. Only after making a breakthrough in the middle of their battle and advancing his Dragon Transformation Art had he torn a Dragon Scale from Zhu Lie's body and completely suppressed him.

That torn Dragon Scale had borne traces of a Dragon Clan Secret Art.

Only now did Yang Kai understand that it must have been the protection Zhu Lie had received from the Dragon Temple. No wonder he had been unable to suppress Zhu Lie at first; this had been the reason.

"If you want to suppress a Master like the Second Elder, you would first have to completely merge with the Dragon Clan Origin in your body. Even then, you would only have a slim chance. Don't forget that aside from being a member of the Dragon Clan, the Second Elder is also an existence comparable to a Great Emperor."

"Completely merge with it... That's impossible!" Yang Kai shook his head vigorously. He had cultivated the Dragon Transformation Art for quite some time and had merged with a good deal of the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power, but it was still only a small portion of the whole. Without a hundred years of painstaking cultivation, completely merging with it would be impossible.

"Then stop thinking about going after the Second Elder."

"Can the Great Elder truly do nothing to help?" A sharp light flickered in Yang Kai's eyes. On Dragon Isle, the Great Elder was the only one capable of contending with the Second Elder. If Yang Kai could persuade him to act, there might be hope of rescuing Zhu Qing.

"You can't count on the Great Elder. He is the head of the Dragon Clan, so he cannot possibly violate the clan's rules."

Yang Kai immediately lost his temper. "This won't work, and neither will that. Are we supposed to just stand by and watch?"

As he spoke, something suddenly occurred to him, and he exclaimed excitedly, "Right, I have a Dragon Isle Token! I'll trade the Dragon Isle Token for your Elder Sister." As he spoke, he took it out.

Zhu Lie's eyes lit up, but they quickly dimmed again. He sighed. "If it were anything else, Dragon Isle would grant your request, but I'm afraid it won't work in this case."

Yang Kai snapped, "Does the Dragon Clan not keep its word? Wasn't it said that anyone holding a Dragon Isle Token could have the Dragon Clan grant them one wish? My wish is to take your Elder Sister away."

"That is true, but only on the condition that the request does not violate the Dragon Clan's principles or harm its interests! Taking Zhu Qing away would violate those principles."

Yang Kai fell silent for a moment, then let loose a furious curse.

Since advancing to the Emperor Realm, it had been a long time since he had experienced such powerlessness. The feeling left him deeply frustrated and agitated, as though every road had been blocked and he had no way to put his abilities to use.

Zhu Lie's gaze flickered before he suddenly said, "One month from now may present an opportunity."

Yang Kai's spirits rose, and he looked up at him.

Zhu Lie met his gaze and explained, "One month from now, Zhu Qing will leave the Second Elder's Spirit Island. Only then will you have a chance to take her away."

"How do you know what will happen a month from now?" Yang Kai frowned, sensing there was more to Zhu Lie's words.

Zhu Lie looked at him with a complicated expression and answered with a question of his own. "Do you know why the Dragon Clan has suddenly begun such a large construction project, building a palace on Fu Chi's Spirit Island?"

After thinking for a moment, Yang Kai asked, "Isn't Fu Chi getting married?"

Madam Hua had mentioned this to him during the day, but he had not paid much attention to it. Although a Dragon Clan wedding was a major event, it had no bearing on him. Right now, he only wanted to take Zhu Qing away.

"To be precise, Fu Chi and Zhu Qing are getting married!"

The air suddenly froze. The surrounding temperature plummeted as a bitter chill spread unchecked.

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes to slits. His Emperor Yuan surged uncontrollably as he enunciated each word. "Say that again. I don't think I heard you clearly."

Zhu Lie sighed. "As a compromise for not exiling her to the Dragon Tomb, she must marry Fu Chi and continue the Dragon Clan's bloodline."

Boom...

Boundless baleful aura erupted from every pore of Yang Kai's body.

Yang Kai clenched his fists, his bones cracking loudly. It had been a long time since he had experienced such utter fury. Rage engulfed him like a tide, throwing his mind into turmoil. All he wanted now was to kill someone, to return to that Spirit Island, find Fu Chi, and tear him into ten thousand pieces. Otherwise, there would be no venting the fury in his chest.

That damned bastard actually dared to steal my woman? Yang Kai had already disliked Fu Chi when he saw him during the day, but now his impression of him was even worse. He immediately regarded Fu Chi as a mortal enemy!

He had never imagined that the Dragon who had ruined Lu Sanniang would actually dare to steal his woman!

"Although Zhu Qing resisted, this was the decision of several Elders. Whether she agrees or not makes no difference. If not for this, why would they have suppressed her Dragon Bloodline and sealed her cultivation?"

Hearing this, Yang Kai's mood grew even darker, and his heart ached for Zhu Qing. He could almost imagine how powerless she had felt at the time.

A wave of self-reproach swept through him. If his woman was being bullied, it meant he had failed to protect her.

The thought of Fu Chi filled him with rage. What the hell was that bastard? He should piss into a puddle and take a good look at himself! Yang Kai ground his teeth audibly, and his face twisted into a terrifying snarl as though he wanted to devour someone.

Zhu Lie looked at him and said, "The wedding will be held one month from now. At that time, she will go to Fu Chi's Spirit Island. If you want to save her, that will be your only opportunity. Apart from then, you have no chance of even seeing her."

Yang Kai snarled, "In other words, I have to endure a month on that island?"

"You could also charge into the Second Elder's residence right now and see just how quickly you can get yourself killed."

Yang Kai glared at him viciously, drawing in deep breaths and slowly exhaling. Even the air blasting from his nostrils was scorching hot, as though flames were burning within him. Only after a long while did he gradually calm down and nod slightly. "I understand."

"There's still time for you to leave now." Zhu Lie looked at him. "With your abilities, since you managed to get in, you can surely get out. But if you insist on staying, then one month from now, you will face almost certain death!"

Yang Kai's anger came quickly and faded just as fast; it had not burned away his reason. Knowing Zhu Lie was right, he clenched his fist and declared, "I look forward to that day."

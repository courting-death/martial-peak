## Chapter 3015 — Incompatible as Fire and Water

Li Jiao did not answer, as though he had not heard her. His expression shifted repeatedly, and his gaze wavered with inner conflict.

Madam Hong glanced at him and stepped forward, gently straightening his robes before speaking softly, "I remember something you said to me many years ago, Palace Master."

“What?” Li Jiao asked instinctively.

"Palace Master said that your greatest wish in this life was to set foot on Dragon Isle and see the place where your bloodline originated. Palace Master said that if you could see it just once, you would die without regret."

"Hong'er, you..." Li Jiao turned to look at her.

He had not expected her to remember so clearly something he had casually said while drunk all those years ago.

"Go. Hong'er will wait for you at home." Madam Hong looked at him. Though her beautiful eyes were filled with reluctance to part, she wore a smile.

The struggle in Li Jiao's eyes grew even more pronounced.

Suddenly, he reached out and swept Madam Hong into his arms, one hand around her slender waist and the other supporting her back, then lowered his head and kissed those red lips.

"Mmm..." Madam Hong let out a soft moan and slowly closed her beautiful eyes, her long eyelashes trembling gently.

Li Jiao kissed her with great devotion and held her with all his strength, as though he wanted to meld Madam Hong's enchanting body into his own so that they would never again be parted.

A moment later, their lips parted. Li Jiao turned away and said in a low voice, "If I don't come back, then you should... remarry sooner rather than later."

With that, he shot straight into the clouds and raced after Yang Kai, his momentum fierce and resolute, as though he would press forward even if ten million people stood in his way!

Madam Hong stared tearfully in the direction he had gone. Cupping her hands around her mouth, she shouted, "Hong'er will wait for you to return! If you don't come back, Hong'er will go to the Netherworld to find you!"

Li Jiao's departing figure paused. The next moment, he accelerated and vanished from Madam Hong's sight.

...

Yang Kai set out alone, his mind occupied entirely with how he should enter Dragon Isle.

Dragon Isle was an extremely mysterious existence. Although its fame resounded far and wide and countless rumors claimed that the Dragon Clan lived there, almost no one knew where it was. All that was known was that Dragon Isle lay somewhere in the Eastern Sea of the Eastern Territory.

Naturally, Yang Kai did not know either.

He had only come to Li Dragon Palace to ask Li Jiao in the hope of getting lucky. Li Jiao was a descendant of the Dragon Clan, after all, and possessed cultivation at the Third-Stage Emperor Realm. Perhaps he knew something.

But after asking, Yang Kai was sorely disappointed.

If Li Jiao did not know, then he could only think of another way.

Fortunately, he had another plan, so he was not entirely unprepared.

Just as he was thinking this, his heart suddenly stirred and he looked back.

A figure was rapidly pursuing him from that direction. Before the person even arrived, Yang Kai's Divine Sense had already detected the familiar aura.

Yang Kai frowned and revealed a strange expression, remaining where he was to wait.

A short while later, Li Jiao appeared before Yang Kai with a complicated expression.

Yang Kai scoffed. "Brother Li, why did you chase after me?"

"Do you truly intend to go to Dragon Isle?" Li Jiao asked with a frown, ignoring the question.

"Mm." Yang Kai nodded lightly.

"For Young Lady Zhu?"

"Yes." Yang Kai nodded.

Li Jiao smiled bitterly and cupped his fists. "Palace Master Yang is truly bold because of his great skill. This Li admires you."

"Enough nonsense. Why did you follow me?" Yang Kai looked at him curiously.

Li Jiao scratched his cheek and said somewhat awkwardly, "This Li came only because I wished to lend Palace Master Yang a hand."

"Oh?" Yang Kai raised his brow.

Li Jiao said, "I know the approximate location of Dragon Isle..."

"Hm?" Yang Kai lowered his eyes.

Reading his expression, Li Jiao knew the situation was bad. He had flatly denied knowing anything earlier, only to deliver himself into Yang Kai's hands now. He might have angered this man, so he hurriedly explained, "I truly only know its approximate location. This Li doesn't know exactly where it is, which is why I didn't dare say anything earlier. I was afraid of delaying Palace Master Yang's important business."

"Is that so?" Yang Kai walked toward him with a broad smile.

Li Jiao's face paled slightly. He thought this man really was angry. He should never have chased after him. Would it not have been better to remain in Li Dragon Palace and live peacefully with Hong'er? Why had he come looking for trouble? Now he was riding a tiger and could not dismount, and he did not even know what he hoped to gain from this.

Watching Yang Kai approach and raise a hand, Li Jiao's heart leapt into his throat. He definitely could not beat Yang Kai, and even if he resisted, he would only be crushed.

He simply closed his eyes and resigned himself to whatever came. He believed Yang Kai would not actually kill him. At worst, he would only beat him up to vent his anger.

Pa...

Li Jiao shuddered, but the attack he had imagined never came. Instead, an arm settled across his shoulders.

Opening his eyes, he saw Yang Kai with an arm around his shoulders, laughing heartily. "Hahaha! I knew Brother Li had a way. It seems I really did ask the right person. Fine, fine! We brothers will go challenge Dragon Isle right now and see just what kind of dragon's pool and tiger's den that place is!"

Li Jiao wiped the cold sweat from his forehead. "I'm only going to take a look."

"Don't worry. With this Young Master there, I definitely won't let you suffer any losses."

The corner of Li Jiao's mouth twitched. "Then I'll have to rely on Palace Master Yang's protection."

He did not believe those words at all. Yang Kai was indeed formidable, but once he entered that damned Dragon Isle, even he would have to coil up like a dragon or crouch like a tiger. What waves could Li Jiao possibly expect him to stir up?

"Leave it to me. There's no time to lose, so let's set out now." As he spoke, Yang Kai summoned the Flowing Cloud Shuttle. The two entered one after the other, and the shuttle transformed into a streak of light before departing at even greater speed.

Inside the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, Li Jiao hesitated for a long time before cautiously saying, "Palace Master Yang, you see, this Li has helped you again this time, so about that debt..."

"A debt is a debt, and a favor is a favor. They're two separate matters, so how could they be mixed together?"

Li Jiao immediately sighed. He did not even know whether he would return alive from this trip, so why was he worrying about anything else? As long as he returned alive, he would already have profited greatly.

"Palace Master Yang, if this Li hadn't come, how were you planning to enter Dragon Isle? Did Young Lady Zhu tell you where Dragon Isle was before she left?"

"No." Yang Kai shook his head as he piloted the Flowing Cloud Shuttle. "I was originally planning to visit Spirit Beast Island and ask the Beast Great Emperor to point me in the right direction. Dragon Isle's location may be mysterious, but the Beast Great Emperor must know where it is."

"Spirit Beast Island!" Li Jiao jumped in fright and asked in alarm, "Palace Master Yang, are you acquainted with the Beast Great Emperor?"

"No!"

Li Jiao stared at him dumbfounded. "If you don't know the Beast Great Emperor, how could you get him to point you in the right direction?"

"I have my ways," Yang Kai casually replied. He truly did not know the Beast Great Emperor, but he knew the Beast Great Emperor's daughter. He had not seen Mo Xiaoqi for a long time since they parted in the Shattered Star Sea, and he wondered how that girl was doing now.

Although Spirit Beast Island was also difficult to find, at the very least, it was not as mysterious as Dragon Isle.

His original plan had been to visit Spirit Beast Island first, then head from there to Dragon Isle. Both great Spirit Islands were in the Eastern Sea, so even if some distance separated them, they certainly could not be too far apart.

As long as Mo Xiaoqi pointed him in the right direction, would finding Dragon Isle not be effortless?

His casual answer, however, frightened Li Jiao into breaking out in a cold sweat.

Just who was this Palace Master Yang? Although Li Jiao had spent quite some time with Yang Kai, the more contact he had with him, the more terrifying he found him. It was one thing for him to have unclear ties with Dragon Isle, but some time ago, he had also been extremely close to the Iron Blood Great Emperor's disciple. Now he was even connected to Spirit Beast Island.

Li Jiao's thoughts were in disarray. He found himself increasingly unable to see through Yang Kai.

After pondering for a moment, Li Jiao said, "Palace Master Yang, if... Mm, I mean if, if we truly manage to enter Dragon Isle, then no matter what you intend to do there, you absolutely must not mention your connection to Spirit Beast Island."

"What do you mean?" Yang Kai looked at him in astonishment.

Li Jiao said, "Dragon Isle and Spirit Beast Island... are as incompatible as fire and water."

Yang Kai's mouth fell open in surprise. "Where did this hearsay come from?"

He truly had never heard of this. His first reaction upon hearing Li Jiao's words was disbelief. The two great Spirit Islands stood among the most powerful forces in the entire Star Realm. If there were truly a conflict between them, everyone would have heard about it long ago, so how could he possibly know nothing?

Li Jiao wiped his sweat and said, "This isn't hearsay. Palace Master Yang doesn't know only because you're too young and only recently advanced to the Emperor Realm. There are too many secrets in the Star Realm that you know nothing about. Once you've lived a few more years, you should naturally learn of them."

Yang Kai frowned. "Incompatible as fire and water? Surely it isn't that serious."

Li Jiao said solemnly, "It's not far off."

"Why?" Yang Kai's interest was piqued. Such a secret was not something an ordinary person could hear about. Li Jiao probably knew of it because he possessed the Dragon Clan's bloodline.

Li Jiao said, "Among the ten Great Emperors, the Beast Great Emperor's foundation lies in beast taming. Spirit Beast Island is home to countless exotic beasts, and I hear there are even one or two Divine Spirits there. Since beast taming is his foundation, the Beast Great Emperor naturally wishes to tame the strongest Yao Beast under the Heavens. Think about it. What is the strongest Yao Beast under the Heavens?"

Yang Kai stared at him. "The Dragon Clan!"

Li Jiao slapped his thigh. "Exactly! Although the Dragon Clan claims to be the leader of all living creatures and foremost among the Divine Spirits, when all is said and done, they can still be considered Yao. The Beast Great Emperor's greatest dream in this life is to tame a Dragon, so how could his relationship with Dragon Isle possibly be good? One could even say that Spirit Beast Island is also in the Eastern Territory because the Beast Great Emperor wanted to take advantage of its proximity to Dragon Isle."

"Is this really true?"

"Absolutely." Li Jiao's expression was solemn. "Although not many people know about this, there is no wall in this world that can keep out every breath of wind."

Yang Kai's expression went blank. "This news is quite shocking."

The Beast Great Emperor wanted to tame Dragons, but how could the proud Dragon Clan possibly allow it? In that case, it made sense that their relationship was so poor.

Li Jiao continued, "The relationship between the Beast Great Emperor and Dragon Isle has deteriorated sharply in recent years. I heard that the Great Emperor even caused a huge disturbance on Dragon Isle more than twenty years ago." After saying this, he added, "Of course, I don't know whether that rumor is true or false, but the one thing that can be confirmed is that the Beast Great Emperor and Dragon Isle really are on bad terms."

Yang Kai nodded. "I understand. If we really do enter Dragon Isle, I definitely won't mention the Beast Great Emperor."

"That would be for the best." Li Jiao relaxed.

"By the way, what made you decide to follow me?" Yang Kai tilted his head and looked at him with a broad smile. "I didn't force you this time."

Li Jiao smiled bitterly and said, "Everyone has an obsession in their heart. Dragon Isle... is this Li's obsession."

His words sounded somewhat bitter, but Yang Kai understood what he meant. Reaching out, he patted Li Jiao's shoulder and said, "Don't worry, we'll definitely get in. If you're lucky, perhaps you can even elevate your bloodline."

Li Jiao laughed. "I'm not hoping for that. It will be enough for this Li to see it just once."

## Chapter 3004 — Now That You've Come Out, You Still Want to Go Back?

Inside Wind and Cloud Pavilion, Heaven and Earth shook and Spiritual Qi surged.

As the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation circulated, all the Spiritual Qi throughout Wind and Cloud Pavilion seemed to be drawn into it to sustain its operation. Sword Intent churned within the Formation, shrouding everything in a haze that made it impossible to see what was happening inside.

Countless Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples rushed over at the commotion, all drawing sharp breaths.

Just what kind of powerful enemy had invaded the Pavilion for even the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation to be deployed? Though they wished to help, the Formation had already been activated, and even as disciples of Wind and Cloud Pavilion, they could not force their way inside. If they overestimated themselves, the boundless Sword Intent would only grind them to dust.

Unable to see what was happening inside the Sword Formation, they could only turn their attention to the other battlefield.

It was a battle between two Third-Stage Emperor Realm Masters. Every exchange seemed capable of shattering mountains and splitting the earth, of dimming the sun and moon. The watching Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples were dazzled and enthralled, their hearts filled with shock.

"Li Jiao, those two juniors have already entered the Sword Formation. They will undoubtedly die without a grave, so why continue risking your life for them?" Hua Xing said while exchanging moves with Li Jiao, trying to undermine his resolve.

His cultivation was equal to Li Jiao's, but Li Jiao was not human. He was a Jiao, a descendant of the Dragon Clan, and Hua Xing knew he was no match for him in a true fight.

The only reason he had lasted this long was because he held the advantages of time and place. After all, he was the Pavilion Master of Wind and Cloud Pavilion and was tied to the destiny of this land, allowing him to exert slightly more strength than usual while inside the Pavilion.

Even so, he could only barely fight Li Jiao to a draw.

A Dragon Descendant truly lived up to the name. Li Jiao had inherited only the faintest trace of that bloodline, yet he could still display such formidable strength. Had they fought like this elsewhere, Hua Xing estimated that he would have lost long ago.

He hated Li Jiao for his double-dealing, but he also knew that continuing this battle would bring no benefit to either himself or Wind and Cloud Pavilion. The destructive force of two Third-Stage Emperor Realm Masters fighting at full strength was immense, and everything being destroyed was part of Wind and Cloud Pavilion's foundation.

Therefore, he wanted to cease hostilities and make peace with Li Jiao.

There were no eternal enemies in this world, only eternal interests. In any case, those two juniors had no chance of surviving, so there was no point in continuing to fight Li Jiao. Even if they determined a victor, what would it accomplish?

Li Jiao frowned at his words. Though he said nothing, Hua Xing clearly felt him withdraw some of his strength.

Hua Xing was delighted at this sight, realizing that Li Jiao was not as resolute as he appeared. Perhaps he had been forced by circumstances to become his enemy. Striking while the iron was hot, Hua Xing said, "Brother Li, we have at least a hundred years of friendship between us. If word spread that we fell out like this over two juniors, the whole world would only laugh at us. Why don't we stop here and wait to see the outcome of the battle over there before deciding what to do next? What do you think?"

By the time he finished speaking, Li Jiao had withdrawn even more of his strength.

Hua Xing knew his guess was correct. Casually exchanging moves with him, he said, "Brother Li, Brother Li, why put yourself through this?"

Li Jiao remained silent, but he was no longer as aggressive as before.

Hua Xing knew that Li Jiao was finding it difficult to save face. After all, changing his stance again and again made him seem rather fickle. The only reason he had not stopped now was because he still had misgivings, fearing those two juniors might fight their way out of the Sword Formation and catch him shirking his duty.

Sneering inwardly, Hua Xing no longer tried to persuade him. As long as Li Jiao stopped destroying Wind and Cloud Pavilion's foundation, Hua Xing was content to play along. Once those two juniors were killed and Great Elder and the others were free, he would make Li Jiao understand the consequences of offending Wind and Cloud Pavilion!

The battle here had become little more than a game. Neither man was using his full strength, but on the surface, it remained as fierce as ever, keeping the gathered Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples thoroughly engrossed.

Inside the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation, however, Yang Kai was not having nearly as easy a time.

This Sword Formation had been formed by the combined strength of a hundred people. Even after using the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, Yang Kai could not break it, and faced with the incoming sword lights, he could only defend himself passively.

Great Elder, however, grew more alarmed the longer they fought. Even after repeatedly launching fierce attacks with the power of the Formation, he had failed to achieve anything. Was this guy even human?

Though the enormous creature that young man had transformed into was in an extremely sorry state and covered in blood, his towering momentum had not diminished in the slightest. Instead, he only fought more fiercely as the battle continued, and every casual move he made unleashed tremendous power that caused the Sword Formation to tremble.

Although there was no need to worry about him breaking the Formation, Great Elder and the others still had to expend their strength to neutralize his attacks.

If this continued, he might truly manage to escape.

At this thought, a ruthless light flashed across Great Elder's eyes, and he sent out a Divine Sense transmission.

Before long, a figure appeared in front of him. It was Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Third Elder, one of those who had formed the Formation with him earlier.

"Take charge of the Formation!" Great Elder ordered.

Third Elder was startled and asked in horror, "Great Elder, are you going to use that move?"

Great Elder replied solemnly, "There is no other way."

Third Elder pondered for a moment before nodding. "Then be careful, Great Elder. There is something unusual about this brat."

"Mm!" Great Elder replied. He handed over the Formation Core for Third Elder to control in his place, then took a step through the air. Stretching out his hand, he summoned a stream of light from nearby and caught it in his palm.

It was his Imperial Treasure longsword.

Forming the Sword Formation required not only people, but also swords as its foundation, and all the cultivators who formed it cultivated the Sword Dao. Great Elder's Imperial Treasure had previously merged with the Formation, but now that he had recalled it, he was clearly preparing to use some immensely powerful Secret Technique.

Holding the longsword upright before him, Great Elder formed several seals with one hand before suddenly pressing two fingers against the blade and drawing them along it.

Blood flowed from his fingers, but not a single drop fell. Instead, the longsword absorbed it completely, and in the blink of an eye, the entire sword turned blood-red.

The surrounding Spiritual Qi surged over like cats catching the scent of fish, pouring into the longsword.

Great Elder's aura as a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master rose steadily, his robes snapping around him. In an instant, he erupted with the might of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, yet his aura still showed no sign of stopping.

Only when Great Elder's entire face had flushed crimson, as though he might burst apart at any moment, did his rising momentum finally stop.

Great Elder's might was now terrifying, surpassing even Hua Xing and Li Jiao. This was not because his foundation was so profound, but because he had borrowed the power of the Formation and drawn the full might of the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation into himself.

Great Elder's blood-red eyes locked onto Yang Kai. Raising his aura to its limit, he suddenly roared, "Die!"

As soon as he spoke, man and sword became one, transforming into a stream of light that slashed toward Yang Kai.

At the same moment, Yang Kai's skin tightened inside the Sword Formation. The aura of death enveloped him, causing the Emperor Yuan within his body to churn restlessly.

His Divine Sense burst out, instantly detecting the source of the danger.

A dazzling light shot through the air like a bolt of lightning. The sword light was incomparably brilliant, its Sword Intent condensed almost into substance, as though it could break through every barrier in existence and sever Heaven, Earth, and the universe itself.

Yang Kai's eyes narrowed sharply.

Although this terrifying strike could not compare to the Great Demon God's blow from across the Void, it was still far beyond anything a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master could unleash.

Even in his half-Dragon form, Yang Kai felt that the attack was impossible to block. Before the sword light even arrived, his body already seemed to be tearing apart.

Treating the threat with utmost seriousness, his hands rapidly formed a series of seals as he shouted, "Years Like a Shuttle!"

He thrust his palm forward just as the sword light tore through the air.

The Years Like a Shuttle Seal unexpectedly shattered on contact, while the sword light continued slashing down without losing momentum.

Yang Kai was taken aback.

This was the first time anyone had ever broken his Years Like a Shuttle head-on. Even if his mastery of the Years Like a Shuttle Seal was still lacking, this was still a Great Emperor's Divine Ability. How could it be broken just like that?

How could it be so fierce?

Just as he was considering hiding inside the Xuan Realm Pearl to avoid the attack, a clear shout suddenly rang out beside his ear, "Uncle Yang!"

Lin Yun'er had finished preparing, and her voice brimmed with confidence.

Yang Kai's heart stirred. The Mountain and River Bell, which had been protecting Lin Yun'er all this time, transformed into a stream of light and descended from above, enveloping him and causing him to vanish without a trace.

"Fuck!" Even with Great Elder's considerable mental fortitude, he could not help cursing.

He had already experienced just how formidable the defenses of this exotic treasure from the Primordial Era were.

Although he could borrow the power of the entire Array to launch a strike far beyond his cultivation, he still had no confidence he could break through the protection of this exotic treasure from the Primordial Era.

In other words, this move had been wasted. Encountering an opponent like this was simply eight lifetimes of bad luck.

If this had been his own sword strike, he could have changed targets and taken the opportunity to kill Lin Yun'er, but this power had already exceeded the limits of his control. Once unleashed, it could not be recalled.

He could only watch helplessly as the Mountain and River Bell blocked his path before crashing headlong into it.

Hong...

With a deafening explosion and a wild burst of light, the Mountain and River Bell shuddered violently, and Great Elder's figure was revealed. He no longer possessed his previous bearing. Although the collision had not killed him, it left blood pouring from all seven orifices and his head spinning. All his strength seemed to have scattered, and he could not summon even the slightest bit of Emperor Yuan.

"Collect!" Lin Yun'er did not take the opportunity to attack and instead let out a clear shout.

In the next instant, the dazed Great Elder's expression changed drastically. He suddenly felt the Imperial Treasure longsword in his hand being pulled by a tremendous force, and it flew from his grasp beyond his control.

Horror filled his face as he hurriedly urged his Divine Sense, trying to summon his Imperial Treasure back.

To someone who cultivated the Sword Dao, the sword in their hand was their very lifeblood. Moreover, this was an Imperial Treasure that Great Elder had cultivated with his own life. If this sword fell into someone else's hands, it would be equivalent to handing half his life over to them.

To Great Elder's utter horror, however, no matter what Secret Technique he used, the Imperial Treasure longsword that had accompanied him for over a thousand years did not respond in the slightest. It was as though the bond they had forged over that millennium had been severed outright by some mysterious force.

His eyes widened as he looked toward Lin Yun'er, and the sight left him dumbfounded.

Before he could react, Yang Kai's enormous body suddenly appeared in front of him, blocking out the light like a small mountain.

Not good! Great Elder went cold all over. His figure flickered as he tried to hide within the Sword Formation, but Yang Kai had already stretched out a hand and seized him in his palm. Grinning viciously, Yang Kai asked, "Now that you've come out, you still want to go back?"

Great Elder's soul nearly fled his body.

Cries of alarm rang out through the air, clearly coming from the Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples maintaining the Formation.

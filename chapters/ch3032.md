## Chapter 3032 — Begging for Mercy

Yuan Wu did not see how Yang Kai had lunged at him at all. He was simply too fast!

The pupils of the experts who had been watching Yang Kai all this time also contracted, their faces filled with shock. Their attention had remained fixed on Yang Kai ever since his calm and effortless palm strike just now, so they had naturally seen things more clearly than the others.

Even so, they had still failed to fully grasp Yang Kai's movements. It seemed as though an afterimage merely flashed past, and Yang Kai was already standing somewhere else.

Yuan Wu came back to his senses at this moment. A vicious look appeared on his face as he raised a clenched fist and punched straight at Yang Kai's face, acting almost entirely on instinct.

Yet while his fist was still in midair, he suddenly cried out in alarm, his expression becoming extremely ugly.

Because in that instant, he suddenly discovered that the Young Man’s left eye had actually turned into a golden vertical pupil. That golden vertical pupil carried not the slightest trace of emotion, only majesty, as if a god were gazing down from on high.

When their eyes met, his mind became dazed, and he vaguely saw a budding lotus flash before plunging into his mind.

Intense pain spread from his Sea of Consciousness throughout his entire body, causing him to scream and tremble. He could no longer bring his ferocious punch down.

Still, he was an expert after all. Faced with this sudden change, he immediately realized that Yang Kai had caught him with an underhanded move.

The young man opposite him did not have a high cultivation, only the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, yet his Divine Soul power left Yuan Wu trembling with fear. The Divine Soul Secret Technique he had used made Yuan Wu feel as though his Sea of Consciousness was about to be torn apart.

He bit the tip of his tongue. The pain cleared his mind for an instant, and he abruptly clenched both fists. Two bursts of lightning flared, transforming into thunderbolts that shot toward Yang Kai.

The two were extremely close, and Yuan Wu held nothing back in this strike. Its might was immense, and the dazzling lightning illuminated nearly half the sky.

Yet an unbelievable scene appeared before him. The lightning pierced through Yang Kai's body without harming him at all, instead passing straight out through his back and striking empty space.

"Hiss..." Yuan Wu sucked in a sharp breath, scarcely able to believe his eyes.

He could dodge such a sudden attack at such close range?

No, he had not dodged it. It was more like Yuan Wu's attack had simply failed to take effect.

But how was that possible? The lightning had clearly struck him. Yuan Wu had seen it with his own eyes.

Yuan Wu was not the only one who could not understand what had happened. No one else understood either, and every one of them stared in stupefaction.

While everyone was still distracted, Yang Kai suddenly reached out and seized Yuan Wu by the neck.

His movement was not fast. To an outside observer, it was as slow as a snail. Yuan Wu should clearly have been able to evade it with ease, yet for some reason he failed to do so and was caught just like that.

Yuan Wu's eyes bulged. He felt an immense force clamp down around his neck, as though it were about to crush it. Not only that, but the faint Dragon Pressure he had summoned was also suppressed in an instant, as though it had met its natural nemesis. The most obvious change was that the few dragon scales on his neck faded in color once more.

Bloodline suppression? Yuan Wu's heart clenched.

He had experienced something similar when Li Jiao thrashed him earlier that day. The suppression of his bloodline had left him able to display only eighty percent of his strength, which was why Li Jiao had beaten him so thoroughly that he could not fight back. Otherwise, with his cultivation at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, even if he was no match for Li Jiao, he should at least have been able to exchange a few moves with him. How could the battle have been so one-sided?

But what was going on with this human?

Yuan Wu could not sense even the slightest trace of the Dragon Clan's aura from him, nor was he a Dragon Descendant, so how could he suppress Yuan Wu through his bloodline?

What terrified him even more was that this suppression was far more dreadful than what he had experienced during the day!

The Dragon Descendant from earlier had possessed only a Third-Tier Middle-Level Dragon Bloodline. Although it exerted some suppression, it had not been too severe, and Yuan Wu had still been able to display eighty percent of his strength.

Yet now, with this young man gripping his neck, more than half of his strength had actually been suppressed. His Emperor Yuan flowed sluggishly through his meridians, leaving him unable to exert any power. It was as though he had fallen gravely ill, and his entire body had gone limp.

What the hell was going on?!

The large hand around his neck held him as tightly as an iron clamp. Yuan Wu could barely breathe, and his face and neck instantly flushed crimson.

The surrounding cultivators' eyes nearly fell out of their sockets.

What exactly was happening? Everyone felt completely baffled.

Was Yuan Wu not at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm? Was he not a Dragon Descendant? How had a human at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm seized him so easily? What in the world was going on? Even if he had underestimated his opponent, it should not have been this bad.

Moreover, judging from Yuan Wu's horrified expression, this was clearly not merely the result of underestimating his opponent.

It seemed he had kicked an iron plate.

Was this young man really so powerful? Many people could not understand it.

The strongest experts, however, exchanged glances and each saw the shock in the others' eyes.

Lu Sanniang did not understand the situation. When she saw that Yang Kai had actually dared to attack Yuan Wu and had even seized him by the neck, her expression changed drastically. "Brother Yang, don't! Hurry and let him go!"

She was not pleading for Yuan Wu, but merely felt that Yang Kai had acted too rashly. Yuan Wu now held great authority, so how could anything good come of truly offending him? Mining Ice Soul Snow Jade was certainly dangerous, but as long as Yang Kai was careful, it should not cost him his life. Now, however, he had completely offended Yuan Wu. How were they supposed to get through the coming month?

It was not that she lacked discernment. Yang Kai simply had his back to her and happened to be blocking Yuan Wu from view, so she truly could not see much of what was happening.

As soon as she spoke, many people turned to look at her with strange expressions.

Lu Sanniang was startled, unable to understand what they meant by this.

"Do you want to live or die?" Yang Kai looked at Yuan Wu and asked indifferently.

Although Yuan Wu was shocked by Yang Kai's strength, he was unwilling to show weakness here. Moreover, Fu Qi had personally appointed him to oversee the palace's construction, so he believed Yang Kai could not possibly kill him no matter what.

He had already lost face, so when he heard Yang Kai ask this, Yuan Wu immediately replied coldly, "Release this Lord at once, or I'll make sure you die without a burial place!"

Yang Kai immediately smiled and looked at him. "It seems you want to die. Since that is the case, I'll grant your wish!"

As he spoke, his expression abruptly turned cold. He suddenly exerted more force with his hand as killing intent rolled out like a tide.

With a soft crack, Yuan Wu felt the bones in his neck snap and immediately panicked. He had never imagined Yang Kai would truly dare to kill him, and judging from the situation, Yang Kai was not pretending.

Was this guy really going to kill him? Where the hell had he come from? He actually dared act so outrageously on Dragon Isle. It was simply unreasonable! Was he not afraid the Dragon Clan would come after him?

Regardless of whether the Dragon Clan would trouble Yang Kai afterward, if Yuan Wu refused to yield now, he would certainly die without a burial place.

With his life hanging by a thread, Yuan Wu no longer dared hesitate and forced out with difficulty, "Spare... my life!"

He begged for mercy?

The several hundred people gathered around them erupted into a commotion, all of them dumbfounded.

It was already inconceivable that someone at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm had been seized by the neck by someone at the First Stage and rendered completely incapable of resisting.

What was more, it was a Dragon Descendant against a human.

Now Yuan Wu had actually opened his mouth and begged for mercy, making it even harder for them to accept.

After all, in Half-Dragon City, the status of a Dragon Descendant was far beyond that of any human or Yao. Regardless of cultivation, all Dragon Descendants held much higher status.

This was the first time any of them had seen a Dragon Descendant lower his head to a human, and it was a Dragon Descendant at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm with a Second-Tier Upper-Level bloodline, no less.

Had they not witnessed it with their own eyes, they would have thought they were dreaming.

Of the five hundred people present, Yuan Wu was the only Dragon Descendant. Everyone else was either human, Yao, or Half-Yao. At the sight of this, their spirits soared, and they felt that much of the resentment pent up in their hearts over the years had finally been vented.

They truly did not want to offend the Dragon Descendants, and some had even bowed and fawned over Yuan Wu earlier, but that did not mean they had any fondness for them.

Admittedly, some Dragon Descendants were quite reasonable, but most of them in Half-Dragon City considered themselves superior and arrogantly ordered others about, never regarding them as worthy of notice. Yuan Wu was the best example.

Seeing Yuan Wu bow his head and beg for mercy now, they could not help feeling proud as though they shared in Yang Kai's glory.

"Wouldn't it have been better if you'd said this sooner?" Yang Kai raised his other hand and lightly patted Yuan Wu's face several times. Though the pats neither hurt nor bothered him, the crisp slapping sounds made Yuan Wu wish he could crawl into a hole.

He had his pride too.

What Yang Kai was doing was the same as slapping him in the face before everyone. How was he supposed to maintain his standing here after this? His heart seethed with resentment, but he did not dare show the slightest trace of it.

"Don't provoke me again. There won't be a next time." Yang Kai gave his arm a fierce swing and hurled Yuan Wu away.

Yuan Wu landed in a sorry state, clutching his throat as he coughed violently. The sound was utterly miserable, as though he were about to cough up his liver and gallbladder.

The surroundings were deathly silent. Everyone stared blankly at Yang Kai, Lu Sanniang included.

By this point, how could she still fail to understand what had happened?

This Young Brother named Yang Kai had actually humiliated Yuan Wu? And had completely crushed him with his strength?

She would not have found it strange if Big Brother Li had accomplished such a feat, as she knew Li Jiao's abilities inside and out.

But what was going on with this Brother Yang? Where had Big Brother Li met such a monster? This was simply too unbelievable!

Under everyone's watchful eyes, Yang Kai walked off to the side. Arriving at the edge of the sea, he gazed into the distance with his brow deeply furrowed.

He paid no mind to his minor conflict with Yuan Wu. What he needed to consider now was how to obtain news of Zhu Qing and confirm her location.

However, after Yang Kai caused such a scene, the atmosphere became somewhat awkward.

Yuan Wu had originally wielded his authority arrogantly, and everyone had been forced to act according to his mood. Yet now that Yang Kai had slapped him in the face before everyone and swept away all his dignity, how could he still have the face to order others around?

Rage churned in his heart, but since his skills were inferior, he could only swallow his broken teeth along with his blood. The bitterness in his mouth was almost unbearable.

"Sir Yuan, Sir Yuan, are you all right?" Yet at this moment, someone actually went over to him.

Everyone looked over and immediately saw a sharp-faced, monkey-cheeked man approach Yuan Wu and carefully help him to his feet.

Yang Kai also frowned when he heard the voice and looked back, only to discover that the man was actually the middle-aged man who had helped smooth things over for him earlier.

## Chapter 2901 — A Strike Across the Void

The innate divine ability of the Black-Pupil Purgatory was broken by Mo Duo, causing Yang Kai to suffer backlash. His qi and blood surged, his head throbbed as if it would split open, and he howled in agony without end.

Taking advantage of this opportunity, Mo Duo finally broke free from Yang Kai’s entanglement. He leapt up from the ground, soaring upward, and in just a few breaths, he had ascended over a thousand zhang, about to reach the treetop.

But at that moment, Yang Kai suddenly flickered strangely and blocked his path, one hand gripping the Demonic Warhammer, his entire body surging with Demonic Qi as he swung it fiercely at Mo Duo.

Mo Duo hurriedly jumped onto a nearby branch, narrowly avoiding Yang Kai’s strike.

The two stared at each other across the void. Mo Duo’s expression was furious, while Yang Kai, shrouded in Demonic Qi, revealed no emotion, but his eyes were even clearer and more spirited than before. He rested the Demonic Warhammer on his shoulder, raised his head, and said coldly, “I really must thank you. You helped me regain my sanity. Otherwise, I might never have escaped that state.”

Mo Duo’s face changed slightly as he replied coldly, “Without the Black-Pupil’s innate divine ability, what can you use to stop me?”

He could see it too—after the Black-Pupil’s divine ability was broken, Yang Kai’s aura had weakened greatly. Though his mind was clear again, he was definitely no longer a match for Mo Duo.

Yang Kai smiled faintly, raised his hand to the sky, and said, “Maybe I can’t stop you forever, but… as long as I delay you for a while, that’s enough.”

Hearing this, Mo Duo’s face turned as dark as water. Yang Kai spoke the truth—even if he was no longer Mo Duo’s opponent, he could still cause trouble and slow him down. As long as he bought the Divine Tree a little more time, it would have a chance to seal the Boundary Passage.

At this moment, the treetop was already directly beneath the Boundary Passage, completely covering its location. If it continued to grow, it would soon extend into the passage itself.

“Try it and see!” Mo Duo roared, stomping fiercely on the branch beneath his feet. His whole body shot upward like a cannonball, and while in midair, he suddenly punched out to the left.

Strangely enough, Yang Kai actually appeared right in front of his fist, as if he had run into it himself.

Yang Kai’s face showed astonishment, caught completely off guard and struck by the punch, sent flying. But his reaction was swift—at the moment he was knocked away, he used a space divine ability to teleport, vanishing and then reappearing above Mo Duo’s head.

The Demonic Warhammer in his hand surged with Demonic Qi, writhing and rolling, carrying world-destroying power as it smashed down toward Mo Duo.

“Damn it!” Mo Duo shouted, raising his hand to block.

A thunderous crash rang out. Mo Duo’s massive body dropped more than a dozen zhang before he stabilized himself, his eyes, as large as windows, nearly spitting fire.

After the Black-Pupil Purgatory was broken, Yang Kai’s strength had indeed dropped sharply. It was clear from the force of this hammer—if it had been the previous Yang Kai, this blow would have sent Mo Duo crashing to the ground. But now, Mo Duo only fell a dozen or so zhang.

Even so, Yang Kai still became the most formidable obstacle in his path. Without breaking through this barrier, Mo Duo felt he could not advance. But now, he had no time to tangle with this outsider.

He had underestimated his opponent, as had all the Demon Saints. Had they known this outsider was so powerful, they would have sent two Demon Saints to deal with him together.

Mo Duo let out a long howl, intending to summon reinforcements.

But there was no response.

The other four Demon Saints were currently fighting the four Shaman Saints of the Barbarian Tribe. The numbers were even, and no one could easily break away. Everything depended on him.

Crack…

A strange sound came. Mo Duo’s eyelid twitched as he looked up. At the mouth of the Boundary Passage, the massive treetop had already surged inside. Squeezed by the power of the passage, the treetop began to twist and deform, but it gradually filled the passage between the two worlds.

Too late. It was already too late. Mo Duo’s heart was ashen, his whole body icy cold.

Yang Kai burst into wild laughter. Though he was battered and exhausted, his laughter was utterly unrestrained. To Mo Duo, it sounded like the ultimate mockery and ridicule, igniting the volcano of rage suppressed in his chest.

But Yang Kai’s laughter cut off abruptly, as if someone had seized his throat.

Mo Duo’s expression also turned grim—he had clearly sensed something.

Both men looked up at the same time, their gazes piercing the void, reaching deep into the Boundary Passage.

Inside the passage, above the treetop, a black cloud suddenly appeared, drifting down slowly. Though it seemed slow, it carried an overwhelming pressure, as if nothing in the world could stop its descent. As it fell, the black cloud twisted and transformed, turning into the shape of a giant hand that pressed down toward the treetop.

“Lord Demon God!” Mo Duo cried out in shock.

“Great Demon God!” Yang Kai roared hoarsely.

In the Demon Domain, the strongest being was the Great Demon God, an existence even more powerful than the Demon Saints. It was he who tore open the world’s barrier, connecting the Demon Domain to this world, bringing destruction and disaster.

The Demon Saints were already powerful enough, but above them, the Great Demon God possessed unimaginable strength. Yang Kai had pondered this before, but could never conceive how powerful such an existence might be—just as, back when he was in the Origin Returning Realm, he could not imagine the terror of a Dao Origin Realm expert. Without reaching a certain level, no matter how rich one’s imagination, it was not enough.

The Great Demon God stood on another level entirely, beyond Yang Kai’s ability to fathom.

Yet now, he could easily sense the terror of the Great Demon God. Just a hand formed from black clouds seemed capable of destroying a world. It was a power that even Demon Saints and Shaman Saints would tremble before.

As the giant hand pressed down, the Divine Tree’s treetop creaked and could no longer grow. Instead, it seemed about to be forced down.

Watching this, Mo Duo’s eyes showed no joy, only terror. Beads of sweat as large as beans rolled down his cheeks, and his thirty-zhang-tall body actually trembled.

As a Demon Saint sent to this world by the Great Demon God, he had failed in his duty. He had not stopped the foreigners from sealing the passage in time, forcing the Great Demon God to act personally. Even if the demons won a great victory, he and the other four Demon Saints would not escape punishment.

And the Great Demon God’s punishment was something even a Demon Saint might not be able to bear.

Whoosh…

Another strange sound came. Mo Duo turned his head and his face changed dramatically. “You dare!”

In that instant of distraction, Shaman Niu, with a solemn expression, flew up toward the Boundary Passage, seemingly intent on stopping the Great Demon God’s technique alone.

This shocked Mo Duo greatly.

It wasn’t that he feared Shaman Niu could really do it. Since the Great Demon God had acted, no matter how strong Shaman Niu was, he was just an ant struggling. But Mo Duo could not allow even the slightest interference with the Great Demon God’s actions.

He rushed after him, trying to intercept, but his massive body was too unwieldy. He could only watch helplessly as Shaman Niu disappeared into the Boundary Passage.

In a panic, Mo Duo’s Demonic Qi surged wildly as he scrambled upward. The treetop he could not climb before, he now reached in less than ten breaths.

With no intention of lingering, Mo Duo also charged into the Boundary Passage, intent on capturing Yang Kai.

Inside the passage was a void of nothingness and chaos, making it impossible to tell one direction from another and filling one with unease. Even a Demon Saint could not easily dispel this feeling, for this was endless void. No matter how powerful, if lost here, one could be trapped forever.

An unimaginable pressure came from above. Mo Duo looked up and saw the Great Demon God’s giant hand pressing down, but Shaman Niu was nowhere to be seen.

That hand was the Great Demon God’s strike across the void. Even separated by a world, it felt utterly irresistible.

He dared not release his Divine Sense to search for Yang Kai, for the chaotic void currents would devour all his Divine Sense.

As he searched for Yang Kai, the latter suddenly appeared at his side, crashing into him like a cannonball. The terrifying force sent Mo Duo flying.

Yang Kai clung to his waist, the power of the Laws of Space surging around him. Chaotic void currents swept them both toward the giant hand.

Mo Duo’s face turned pale as he struggled desperately, but in this void where the Laws of Space ruled, he found he could not break free from Yang Kai’s push. They drew ever closer to the Great Demon God’s palm print.

“What are you doing!” Mo Duo shouted in shock and anger, a terrifying thought rising in his heart.

“Borrowing you for a bit!” Yang Kai grinned at him, though Mo Duo clearly didn’t see it.

These words made Mo Duo’s face turn green. His massive body trembled as he resisted with all his might, nearly breaking free several times.

Growing impatient, Yang Kai waved his hand, and countless chaotic void currents surged in, transforming into ropes that bound Mo Duo tightly.

Now, Mo Duo became much more obedient.

“Do you know what you’re doing?” Mo Duo, unable to break free, could only try to persuade Yang Kai. “If you do this, you’ll die too!”

Yang Kai said nothing, pushing Mo Duo with all his strength toward the giant hand.

“Madman, you’re a madman!” Mo Duo cursed. “I won’t be used by you. Dying under the Great Demon God’s hand is my honor. What you’re doing is meaningless.”

“You have Ancient Demon Qi and Black-Pupil eyes. Though you’re from the Barbarian Tribe, you’re half a demon now. Besides, you’ve already proven your strength. Give up— the Great Demon God will forgive you. You’ll get anything you want on the demon side.”

“Still not letting go? If you don’t, it’ll be too late!”

Mo Duo kept roaring, his panic growing, but Yang Kai ignored him.

Finally, as the Great Demon God’s giant hand descended, Mo Duo fell silent, knowing it was too late. Even if Yang Kai let him go, he could not escape the Great Demon God’s attack.

## Chapter 2770 — The Matter Ends Here

She was the daughter of a Great Emperor. Her beauty alone could make any man’s heart race, but even if she were plain, winning her favor would be a fortune that could save a man centuries of struggle.

In this situation, Xiao Chen’s face suddenly darkened. He gave a light snort and took the initiative to approach Lan Xun.

When he got close, he revealed a gentle smile and softly said, “Princess, what brings you here?”

Lan Xun gave him a slight nod as a greeting, then her expression turned solemn and her red lips parted: “By order of the Great Emperor, I bring the Great Emperor’s decree!”

Upon hearing this, everyone’s expression became serious.

Although there were ten Great Emperors in this world, the one Lan Xun referred to was undoubtedly the master of the Star God Palace, Radiant Moon Great Emperor, the pillar of the Southern Territory. Just as Great Emperor Miaodan was revered in the Northern Territory, Radiant Moon Great Emperor was an object of universal respect in the Southern Territory.

“We respectfully welcome the Great Emperor’s decree!” Wen Zishan called out solemnly.

The many guests also straightened up and listened intently.

Lan Xun said, “This is not the place to speak. Uncle Wen, could you please prepare a room?”

This was clearly because there were too many people here, but none of the guests dared show the slightest dissatisfaction. At this moment, Lan Xun represented Radiant Moon Great Emperor—who would dare show disrespect?

“This way, please!” Wen Zishan turned to gesture behind him.

Lan Xun nodded slightly and led the way toward the palace. As she passed by Yang Kai, she paused and turned her head: “Senior Brother Yang, come with me.”

Yang Kai hesitated for a moment, then followed. Lan Xun’s appearance here was definitely related to him, so as the person involved, he naturally had to go along.

Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao exchanged glances and also followed behind Lan Xun. Xiao Chen, as her protector, of course did not fall behind.

In no time, the group entered the grand hall. Wen Zishan waved his hand, and the doors immediately closed.

Lan Xun walked up to the high platform, then turned to look down. The three Third Stage Emperor cultivators stood in a row, while Yang Kai and Xiao Chen, the two juniors, stood clearly apart on either side.

Smiling, Lan Xun looked at Yang Kai and said, “First, congratulations, Senior Brother Yang. After today, you will be an Elder of the Qingyang Shrine!”

At these words, Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao both frowned. The information revealed made them vaguely sense something.

Yang Kai said, “Your Highness flatters me.”

Lan Xun pouted, “Why so distant, Senior Brother Yang? I’m as close as sisters with Xiaoqi, and you treat Xiaoqi like a younger sister. Just call me Lan Xun.”

“He wouldn’t dare!” Xiao Chen’s eyes widened instantly. Such an intimate form of address was not something just any man could use. He and Lan Xun had grown up together, and even he only called her that in private. In front of outsiders, they always maintained proper decorum.

"Since Princess Lan Xun has said so, I won’t stand on ceremony. Congratulations to you as well on your ascension to the Emperor Realm." As Yang Kai spoke, he glanced at Xiao Chen—nearly making him explode with anger.

The three Third Stage Emperors present were all surprised—first at Lan Xun’s attitude toward Yang Kai, and second at her words. For Lan Xun to say she was as close as sisters with someone, that person was certainly no ordinary figure.

“Xiaoqi?” Xiao Yuyang murmured. “Could it be the girl from Spirit Beast Island?”

Xue Zhengmao and Wen Zishan suddenly came to their senses and looked at Yang Kai in astonishment. They hadn’t expected him to have a connection with the princess of Spirit Beast Island, and from Lan Xun’s words, it was no ordinary relationship.

“Exactly, the girl from Spirit Beast Island.” Lan Xun covered her mouth and smiled. “You may not know, but Xiaoqi and Senior Brother Yang are very close. Last time I went to Spirit Beast Island with my father, she even asked me about Senior Brother Yang. But Senior Brother Yang is as elusive as a dragon, so I couldn’t tell her anything, leaving her quite disappointed.”

Given the relationship between Mo Xiaoqi and Lan Xun, even if she knew Yang Kai’s whereabouts, she probably wouldn’t tell her.

Lan Xun’s words were calm, but in the hearts of the Third Stage Emperors, they stirred up a storm.

Mo Xiaoqi herself was nothing special, but the one above her was not someone to be ignored. That person could stand shoulder to shoulder with Radiant Moon Great Emperor. With such a connection to Spirit Beast Island, anyone wanting to move against Yang Kai would have to think twice.

Radiant Moon Great Emperor doted on Lan Xun, and Beast Great Emperor doted on Mo Xiaoqi just as much. If their daughters whispered in their ears, even a Great Emperor’s resolve might soften.

At this moment, Xue Zhengmao couldn’t help but feel relieved. Fortunately, he hadn’t done anything to Yang Kai before, or things might have ended badly. As for Tan Junhao, only a Great Emperor could deal with that matter.

With a thought, Xue Zhengmao asked, “Princess, what are the Great Emperor’s instructions?”

Although he had some guesses, he still had to ask.

Wen Zishan and Xiao Yuyang also looked up at Lan Xun.

Lan Xun’s expression turned solemn. “Tan Junhao, as an Elder of the Star God Palace, not only failed to set an example for the countless cultivators of the Southern Territory, but acted against the norm, harming Emperor-level cultivators of the Southern Territory. Wu Ming aided and abetted him. The Great Emperor has investigated and found the truth. These two deserved to die. The Great Emperor decrees that the matter of Tan Junhao and Wu Ming ends here. No one is to stir up further trouble.”

As soon as she finished, Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao both cupped their fists and bowed. “We will strictly obey the Great Emperor’s decree!”

Wen Zishan grinned and cupped his fists. “The Great Emperor is wise and mighty!”

Yang Kai also said, “Many thanks to the Great Emperor!”

He finally breathed a sigh of relief. As expected, only someone with the heart of a Great Emperor could act so magnanimously. With this decree, Yang Kai no longer had anything to worry about.

By rights, since Yang Kai killed Tan Junhao and Wu Ming, the Star God Palace should be grateful to him for cleaning house. But these were minor details. The Star God Palace was the overlord of the Southern Territory. Their Elder and Deacon were killed; though there were reasons, not pursuing the matter was already generous. How could they be expected to show gratitude?

“There is one more matter. My father asked me to discuss something with Senior Brother Yang.” Lan Xun’s gaze shifted to Yang Kai.

Everyone keenly noticed the change in how she referred to the Great Emperor. Since Lan Xun’s arrival, she had always called him the Great Emperor, but now she said ‘my father’. In other words, what followed was not in the capacity of a Great Emperor.

“May I ask what it is?” Yang Kai inquired.

Lan Xun said, “My father wishes to know, where is Tan Junhao’s Golden Armored Heavenly Tome now?”

At these words, everyone in the hall turned their eyes to Yang Kai. Clearly, the Third Stage Emperors present all knew the reputation of the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome and its status as an Imperial Treasure.

In the Star Realm, there were many Imperial Treasures, but only a few were truly famous. For example, the Mountain and River Bell in Yang Kai’s possession was a Primordial Era treasure coveted even by Great Emperors.

Tan Junhao’s Golden Armored Heavenly Tome might not be as renowned as the Mountain and River Bell, but it was still a top-tier Imperial Treasure.

Yang Kai frowned, feeling a bit uneasy, but finally said, “The Golden Armored Heavenly Tome is with me.”

As he spoke, he took out the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome and held it in his palm.

Its golden brilliance immediately drew everyone’s gaze.

Lan Xun nodded and said gently, “Here’s the situation, Senior Brother Yang. My father hopes you can return the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome to me to take back…”

At these words, Yang Kai’s face darkened. He realized his premonition had come true.

He knew all too well how powerful the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome was. When fighting Tan Junhao, if not for his carelessness, had he unleashed its full power, Yang Kai would have paid a much higher price to kill him—perhaps even failed despite going all out.

Such a fine treasure, he naturally didn’t want to hand over. He had even refined it for a while and could use some of its secret techniques. He was quite fond of it.

Now, with just a single sentence from Lan Xun, he was being asked to hand it over—he truly found it hard to accept. Of course, refusing would bring him no benefit, but he also understood that since this was a negotiation, the Star God Palace would never try to take it by force.

He looked up at Lan Xun, wanting to hear what she would say next.

“The Golden Armored Heavenly Tome is your spoil of war, Senior Brother Yang. By rights, my Star God Palace has no authority to demand it. But you should know, the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome is not only an Imperial Treasure, but also the key to opening a secret realm of my Star God Palace.”

“The key to a secret realm?” Yang Kai was surprised.

“That’s right.” Lan Xun nodded slightly. “My Star God Palace has a secret realm called the Grand Illusionary Realm. It’s an independent world, and only the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome can open it. Elder Xiao and Elder Xue can confirm this.”

Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao both nodded in agreement, confirming Lan Xun’s words.

“Yes, the Grand Illusionary Realm. I know of this secret realm,” Wen Zishan chimed in. “I just didn’t expect it could only be opened with the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome.”

“In the past, this secret realm was managed by Tan Junhao, so the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome was in his hands. If the key is lost, the secret realm can never be opened again. That would be a huge loss for my Star God Palace. Many disciples train there, and right now, dozens are trapped inside and can’t get out.” Lan Xun looked sincerely at Yang Kai. “So, no matter what, I hope you can return the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome. Before I came, my father instructed me that if you agree, you may choose one of three conditions, and the Star God Palace will fulfill it.”

Yang Kai’s eyes lit up. “What are the three conditions?”

If the Star God Palace truly insisted on reclaiming the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, Yang Kai knew he would have no choice but to accept—there was no way he could go against such a mighty force. Yet now, with Lan Xun offering him three conditions to choose from, he felt much more at ease.

“First, the Star God Palace will compensate you with two hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals!” Lan Xun announced.

Wen Zishan’s expression changed at these words.

Two hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals was no small sum.

Most Imperial Treasures were worth only ten or twenty million, the better ones thirty or forty million. Two hundred million was a very real number. The Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, as an Imperial Treasure, wasn’t worth that much, but as the key to the secret realm, its value was much higher.

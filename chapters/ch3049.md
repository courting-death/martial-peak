## Chapter 3049 — I Am the Guard

Time flew by, and three days later, Yang Kai opened his eyes from meditation, his expression gloomy.

He had already consumed all the Dragon Blood Pills, allowing his four-zhang Dragon body to grow to five zhang. Yet despite cultivating without pause for the past three days, he had made no further progress at all. He knew his cultivation had reached a bottleneck and that advancing to the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm would be impossible without some special opportunity. However, with Zhu Qing's wedding close at hand, he desperately wanted to increase his strength and could not resist trying.

In the end, he had wasted three whole days.

His cultivation could not be improved, and all the Dragon Blood Pills had been used up. Was there truly nothing else he could do?

The Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power within him was an enormous treasure. If he had more Dragon Blood Pills, he would certainly be able to unlock more of the Dragon Clan Origin's potential. He did not need too many. As long as his Dragon body could grow to ten zhang, Yang Kai was highly confident he could take Zhu Qing away from Dragon Isle.

Ten zhang, ten zhang... Easier said than done.

Since he began cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art, he had received the aid of Zhu Qing's Pure Yin Qi and consumed more than ten Dragon Blood Pills, yet his Dragon body was still only a mere five zhang. He was only halfway to his goal of ten zhang. If ten Dragon Blood Pills were needed for every zhang of growth, another five zhang would require fifty pills, which meant at least a dozen Dragon Blood Flowers. That did not even account for the increasing difficulty as his Dragon body grew. Taking that into consideration, he would need at least twenty Dragon Blood Flowers.

Where was he supposed to find twenty Dragon Blood Flowers?

As he continued pondering, Yang Kai suddenly froze, slapped his forehead, and inwardly cursed himself for being an idiot.

Dragon Blood Flowers were indeed rare, but what place was this?

This was Dragon Isle!

It was the only place under Heaven that produced Dragon Blood Flowers. As long as he found the place where Dragon Isle cultivated them, why would he need to worry about obtaining twenty?

It was truly a case of overlooking what was right beneath his nose. He had been incredibly foolish. If he had realized this earlier, he would not have wasted three days.

The trial was close at hand, and every day was exceedingly precious. Those three wasted days could very well prove crucial.

Having thought this through, he no longer hesitated and immediately left the Small Xuan Realm.

The Xuan Realm Pearl had already sunk to the seabed. The moment Yang Kai appeared, he felt the immense pressure around him crushing inward, as though it would grind him to dust.

He circulated his Emperor Yuan and barely managed to form a protective layer around his body, offsetting the terrifying pressure of the sea. After putting away the Xuan Realm Pearl, he rushed straight toward the surface.

Half a day later, a streak of purple light sped toward the island where Yang Kai had previously met Zhu Lie under cover of night. It landed on the island and revealed a graceful figure. It was Fu Ling.

She cautiously surveyed her surroundings but found nothing suspicious, so she called out softly, "Brother-in-law, Brother-in-law..."

She acted furtively, as though she were doing something shameful that she could not let anyone see.

"I need Dragon Blood Flowers!" Yang Kai's voice suddenly rang out behind her, startling Fu Ling. She spun around and discovered that Yang Kai had appeared at some point without her noticing.

Fu Ling's face fell when she heard this. "I don't have any left. I gave you all my Dragon Blood Flowers last time. You can search me if you don't believe me."

She had felt somewhat uneasy when she received Yang Kai's summons today, but who could have known that he would make such an outrageous demand the moment they met? She already deeply resented him for robbing her of her last three Dragon Blood Flowers, yet now he was actually demanding even more.

Unfortunately, her life was in his hands, so no matter how dissatisfied Fu Ling felt, she did not dare show it.

As soon as the words left her mouth, Fu Ling was startled again. "What happened to those three Dragon Blood Flowers? Don't tell me you used them all."

Yang Kai replied, "You think three is a lot? They weren't even enough to fill the gaps between my teeth."

Fu Ling drew a sharp breath, her eyes widening in disbelief. "Fill the gaps between your teeth?"

With her Sixth-Tier Dragon Bloodline, even a single Lower-Level Dragon Blood Flower would take her around ten days to fully refine, while a Middle-Level one would take at least twenty days. In other words, if she had used those three Dragon Blood Flowers herself, they would have lasted her more than a month.

Yet only a few days had passed, and Yang Kai had actually used them all up.

Which one of us is the Dragon Clan member here, me or you? Does possessing the Ancestral Dragon Origin really make you this heaven-defying?

She could not help clicking her tongue in amazement as she looked Yang Kai up and down as though seeing him for the first time.

Yang Kai said with displeasure, "I need Dragon Blood Flowers. More Dragon Blood Flowers."

Fu Ling spread her hands. "I really don't have any left. Oh, right, you could ask Zhu Lie for some. He should still have a few. Do you want me to contact him for you?"

“What use are a few?” Yang Kai curled his lips and said, “I need the Dragon Blood Flowers on Dragon Isle.”

Fu Ling stared blankly for a moment, not quite understanding him, but her expression soon changed drastically as she cried out in a low voice, "You... What do you mean?"

"You should know exactly what I mean. There is no time to lose, so hurry up and lead the way," Yang Kai snorted coldly.

Fu Ling retreated several steps, shaking her head repeatedly. "Impossible! If the Elders learn of this, I'll definitely be banished to the Dragon Tomb."

This man's courage was simply outrageous. He actually dared set his sights on Dragon Isle's Dragon Blood Flowers, and he even wanted her to lead the way? Was he not pushing her straight into a fire pit? How could anyone act like this?

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes at her. "If you're banished to the Dragon Tomb, you might still have a chance to survive, but you should know what will happen if you dare refuse me."

Fu Ling's expression changed. She looked pitifully at Yang Kai and said, "Brother-in-law, we can discuss anything, but this matter... Ah!"

Before she could finish speaking, an excruciating pain suddenly pierced her mind. She instinctively clutched her head and let out a shrill scream, her delicate body trembling violently.

Yang Kai's expression was cold and merciless as he formed a hand seal, his Divine Sense surging.

Fu Ling's screams grew even more piercing. She collapsed to her knees, her waist curled like a shrimp, and cried out between screams, "Brother-in-law... spare... me!"

Yang Kai turned a deaf ear to her pleas and changed his hand seal again.

"Ah!"

Fu Ling threw back her head and roared. Purple light erupted as she abruptly transformed into a Purple Dragon more than ten zhang long. Thrashing her head and tail, she charged about like a headless fly before plunging into the sea.

In the next instant, she burst out of the water again, sending crystalline spray flying.

Her anguished Dragon Roar shook the Heavens.

Her Dragon body suddenly transformed back into human form, then changed into Dragon form once more.

The unimaginable pain and torment left Fu Ling unable to maintain either her human or Dragon form, forcing her to shift constantly between the two. Within her Sea of Consciousness, her Divine Soul seemed to be gripped by an invisible hand that squeezed with brutal force, as though trying to crush it to pieces.

Fu Ling could no longer even cry out for mercy. It was as though she were suffering the cruelest torture in the world, sinking ever deeper into pain and despair. Every instant was an ordeal worse than death.

Only after a full incense stick of time did Yang Kai release his hand seal and look down with a cold expression.

Fu Ling lay sprawled at his feet, her delicate body drenched in sweat. Her clothes clung tightly to her, outlining her beautiful figure. Her body trembled with spasms, her eyes were vacant, saliva continued to trickle from the corner of her mouth, and her beautiful hair was as disheveled as a bird's nest.

Even a member of the Dragon Clan like her could not endure torment inflicted directly upon the Divine Soul. Countless times, she had felt herself teetering on the brink of death. This experience would become a nightmare that haunted her for the rest of her life.

"Consider this a lesson. Next time, you won't be so lucky."

"I... I under... stand." Fu Ling gulped down air, her full chest heaving violently as she lay there limp as mud.

After being tormented by Yang Kai like this, she finally recognized reality. What she was about to do might betray the interests of Dragon Isle as a whole, and if the Elders truly found out, the only fate awaiting her would be banishment to the Dragon Tomb.

But if she refused to do it, what awaited her was death!

This supposed brother-in-law of hers had no sense of tenderness toward women whatsoever, nor did he regard her as family. He had shown no mercy at all while tormenting her. Comparing the two outcomes, she naturally knew which choice to make.

The Dragon Clan's restorative abilities truly were formidable. Although Fu Ling looked half-dead, she recovered in only the time it took to drink a cup of tea.

She straightened her clothes, wiped the drool from her mouth, and looked timidly at Yang Kai. "Are we really going?"

After what had just happened, she now felt an instinctive fear of Yang Kai that had never existed before. Although she had previously known that her life and death were under his control, it had ultimately been only a vague notion. Now, however, she knew that Yang Kai truly could kill her in an instant, or even make her suffer something worse than death.

"Hm?" Yang Kai turned and glared at her with an unfriendly expression, looking fierce and menacing.

"Uh..." Fu Ling did not dare say anything more. She immediately flew off. "I'll lead the way, Brother-in-law, so keep up. If we encounter any other members of the Dragon Clan along the way, make sure you hide before they spot you."

Yang Kai said nothing and made no visible movement. His figure merely flickered, and he was suddenly flying alongside Fu Ling.

What had happened earlier seemed to have left Fu Ling resentful. She remained silent throughout the journey, without any of the excitement or ardor she had shown a few days earlier. The occasional glances she cast at Yang Kai were instead filled with apprehension.

"Where are Dragon Isle's Dragon Blood Flowers planted?" Yang Kai asked.

Although Fu Ling was full of resentment, she had no choice but to answer. "They're also on an island. That island is specifically used to cultivate Dragon Blood Flowers and is one of the most important places belonging to my Dragon Clan."

"How many members of the Dragon Clan guard it?"

"One..." Fu Ling replied evasively.

"What Tier is their bloodline?"

"Sixth-Tier..."

A Sixth-Tier Dragon Bloodline was no different from Fu Ling's, so Yang Kai had nothing to fear. After pondering for a moment, he said, "When we arrive, you draw the guard's attention while I deal with him. We absolutely cannot give him any time to react."

Fu Ling said awkwardly, "Brother-in-law, there's no need to go to so much trouble."

"What do you mean?"

Fu Ling pointed at herself. "I am the guard..."

Yang Kai stared at her in astonishment, the corner of his eye twitching. Then he could not help laughing.

He had worn out iron shoes searching in vain, only to find what he sought without the slightest effort. It seemed all his previous plans were now completely unnecessary. To think he would encounter such a fortunate coincidence. It appeared Dragon Isle was destined to be plundered by him.

Seeing him laugh, Fu Ling smiled along with him, though her expression looked exceedingly awkward.

"Your Dragon Clan is far too arrogant." Yang Kai chuckled. This unexpected gain had put him in an excellent mood, so he could not be bothered to argue with Fu Ling any further. "Such an important place has only one member of the Dragon Clan guarding it? And judging by your behavior, you don't seem to take your duty very seriously."

## Chapter 2853 — What Comes Below

Only when a chill enveloped his neck did Yang Kai suddenly become alert, instinctively channeling his shaman power to defend himself.

With a hiss, sparks flew as a sharp dagger swept across Yang Kai’s throat, leaving a thin line of blood.

The attacker who struck from behind was momentarily stunned, for although his target was wounded, it was only a superficial injury. In other words, his surprise attack had failed to achieve its intended result.

As the assailant hesitated, Yang Kai spun around and punched at him.

A muffled groan sounded, and from the black mist, a figure about three feet tall suddenly appeared, eyes bulging, chest nearly collapsed, slamming into the stone wall and spraying blood everywhere.

After landing, the small-statured demon clearly showed a hint of fear. Seeing Yang Kai’s fist coming again, his face changed dramatically, and his body flickered, transforming into a wisp of black smoke, causing Yang Kai’s punch to miss.

Yang Kai frowned slightly. Although he had fought the Sand Demon a few days ago, this was his first encounter with such a small demon. Its troublesome ability left him no time to find a way to counter it.

The black mist retreated rapidly.

At that same moment, a piercing shriek erupted from behind, as if a cuckoo were crying blood.

As the sound entered his ears, Yang Kai’s Divine Soul wavered, his mind grew unsettled, and a chaotic vision of demons dancing appeared before his eyes.

Divine Soul attack! Yang Kai shook his head forcefully, steadied his mind, and channeled his Divine Soul power to dispel the influence of the scream.

Hissing sounds came from behind, accompanied by the roar of wind and thunder.

Yang Kai suddenly raised his hand to block his side.

With a crisp snap, he intercepted a soft whip made of unknown material. But that wasn’t all—the whip, though blocked, magically spun half a circle around his palm, its tip shooting straight for his face.

In a flash, the whip’s tip opened a wide mouth, filled with fangs that glowed emerald green, hissing as it lunged at Yang Kai.

This was no whip—it was clearly a venomous snake!

Yang Kai swiftly reached out with his other hand, precisely grabbing the snake’s head. With a squeeze, he crushed it.

A surprised cry came from behind, as if shocked by Yang Kai’s quick reaction and his ability to easily neutralize such a deadly move. The next moment, a fragrant breeze rushed to his side, transforming into a white light, with countless palm shadows enveloping him.

Yang Kai took a deep breath, steady as a mountain, and his hands also became a blur.

Pa pa pa...

The rapid, dense sounds echoed. When the white light withdrew, the two had exchanged over a hundred palms, the surging force shaking the surrounding sand and stones, making the stone cave seem on the verge of collapse.

All of this happened in an instant—from the small demon’s sneak attack in the darkness to Yang Kai’s clash with the white shadow, it was all over in the blink of an eye.

Only then did Yang Kai slowly turn, the corner of his mouth lifting in a mocking smile as he looked ahead.

Less than five zhang away, a completely naked woman stood quietly. Her frame was delicate and graceful, yet her curves were full and perfectly proportioned. Her abdomen was flat, her legs long and slender, and her skin was so fair and flawless it seemed to rival frost and snow—as if it would break at the slightest touch.

Her skin seemed to have just been moistened by rain and dew. A faint blush colored her delicate features, and her watery eyes held a hint of satisfied intoxication.

This demon woman was precisely the one Yang Kai had secretly observed earlier.

She had naturally noticed the brief skirmish with the nearby shadow.

At this moment, she stood completely unclothed, boldly displaying her most beautiful features, yet showed no discomfort.

Beside her, a mass of black shadow wavered unsteadily, a weak aura emanating from it—clearly the demon who had failed to ambush Yang Kai and had been injured in return.

“It’s you!” The demon woman glanced at Yang Kai, her eyes brightening, head tilted, looking innocent. “You actually found your way here, that’s impressive.”

Yang Kai grinned, “Do we know each other?”

The demon woman giggled, “Now we do, don’t we?”

Yang Kai nodded, “How should I address you?”

The demon woman laughed, her snowy chest trembling, eyes teasing, “Demon King Shaya.”

“Demon King?” Yang Kai raised his brows. “If you’re a Demon King, then above you is a Demon Saint?”

Shaya smiled at Yang Kai, “Junior Brother, are you trying to get information from me? That’s not a very clever trick.”

Yang Kai shrugged, “It's my first time dealing with your kind, just chatting. If you don’t want to talk, that’s fine.”

“You’re quite interesting.” Shaya looked him up and down, her brows furrowing with a hint of regret. “Just a bit too weak physically.”

Yang Kai scoffed, "Small stature doesn't mean weak. You've seen plenty of battles yourself—surely you've encountered those who look impressive but are worthless?"

Shaya nodded in agreement, “You’re right. Above a Demon King is a Demon Saint. Don’t you have Saints on your side? They fought recently, don’t tell me you didn’t know.”

“Of course I know. I heard it was a draw, pity I didn’t see it myself.”

Shaya looked at him oddly, “You want to see it yourself? Careful, you might lose your life.”

Yang Kai laughed, “Everything about me is hard—especially when it comes to taking my life.”

Shaya said meaningfully, “Then I’ll have to see for myself.”

“If the one above you is a Demon Saint, then what comes below you?”

“Can’t you see for yourself?” Shaya’s face suddenly flushed with shyness, her tone softening as she subtly displayed her charms, changing the atmosphere.

Yang Kai gulped, glancing downward, “I see, I see...”

The small demon who had been standing beside Shaya suddenly groaned and fled at high speed.

Shaya used her charm technique, and he dared not linger, lest he become her captive, a mindless puppet.

"Never mind him; he's just a useless little thing," Shaya said with a soft laugh, a faint moan escaping her throat as her delicate body flushed red. She bit her lower lip gently and continued, "I'm feeling a bit uncomfortable... could you help me?"

“Alright!” Yang Kai’s eyes grew hazy, and as he responded, he walked forward, breathing heavily, his gaze locked on Shaya’s white body. This aggressive look seemed to please Shaya, filling her with unimaginable delight. She laughed, her voice laced with strange magic, opening her arms to embrace Yang Kai.

As she entered his arms, Yang Kai’s eyes suddenly flashed, grabbing Shaya’s hair and yanking her body backward.

“That hurts!” Shaya cried, a strange look in her eyes as she frowned, “What are you doing?”

Yang Kai grinned fiercely, “What do you think I’m doing?”

Shaya was stunned, “How is this possible...?”

Meeting his mocking gaze, Shaya realized he hadn’t been charmed at all. How could this be? She was a renowned succubus among the hundred demon clans, a Demon King of the succubus clan. His aura was clearly much weaker than hers—she could easily charm such opponents. Yet her charm technique had no effect.

The earlier haze was just an act. Seeing the ridicule and coldness in his eyes, there was no sign of being charmed.

Yang Kai laughed, “You dare show off your charm technique in front of a shaman? Truly ignorant!”

As he spoke, he punched Shaya’s full chest.

Shaya had never experienced this before. Her charm failed, and she was caught off guard. Only when Yang Kai struck did her expression change and she screamed.

As she screamed, it was as if an invisible blade pierced Yang Kai’s head, making him dizzy, but his fist still smashed into Shaya’s chest.

With a bang, shaman power erupted.

Shaya’s snowy, full chest burst open, turning into a mass of blood.

Using the force of the impact, and taking advantage of the moment when Yang Kai’s Divine Soul was affected, Shaya whipped her head, tearing her hair at the roots, and slipped away like an eel.

After steadying herself, Shaya's complexion turned ashen as she glanced down at her chest, her entire body trembling.

The two snowy mounds she once prided herself on were now missing one, blood streaming down her belly and legs.

“I’ll kill you!” Shaya screamed, nearly mad.

Shaya was a succubus, and her stunning figure was one of her greatest assets. Now that Yang Kai had ravaged her so brutally, she found it nearly unbearable. While such injuries were not beyond recovery, healing them would require a considerable price to be paid.

In a moment of carelessness, she had suffered a significant loss at the hands of a barbarian several tiers beneath her.

Yang Kai said nothing, his body flickering as he lunged at Shaya again. Though he had come to gather intelligence, now that he was exposed, he might as well go all out. If he could kill this Demon King here, it would be worth the trip.

Seeing the barbarian charging like an enraged lion, Shaya instinctively felt a sense of danger, as if she would die if she didn’t escape.

The thought shocked her.

After a brief hesitation, Shaya decided to trust her instincts.

A ring of light suddenly burst from her body, releasing a massive repulsive force. Yang Kai, who had just rushed up, was pushed back several steps by the light before he could strike.

## Chapter 2866 — Blood Demon Bao Qi

Above the crude and wild Barbarian Tribe city, a stream of pure black qi drifted forth, swift as lightning. In the blink of an eye, it broke through layers of defensive formations and surged into the city.

The demons within the city were all alarmed and hurried over from every direction, appearing nearby one after another and watching the two figures emerging from that mass of shadow with wary and apprehensive eyes.

One of them had a graceful figure and a powerful aura—clearly a Demon King-level existence.

This made the surrounding demons too afraid to approach rashly.

Shaya glanced around and let out a string of silvery laughter, laced with an irresistible charm.

Many demons retreated again and again, putting even more distance between themselves and her. They were clearly all aware of how terrifying a Succubus could be. Only those Demon Commanders with considerable strength dared to remain, though they too silently resisted her allure.

Yang Kai stood behind Shaya, silent and inconspicuous.

“Bitch, you actually dare to come to this King’s territory!” A thunderous voice suddenly rang out from the palace ahead. As the words sounded, a flash of blood-red light shot out from within, instantly appearing before Shaya and transforming into a figure as tall as an iron tower.

He reached out, grabbed Shaya by the neck, and lifted her high, his face twisted in a savage grin, murderous intent overflowing.

Dangling in his grasp, Shaya did not struggle. She simply looked at him calmly, not the least bit flustered, as if she had long anticipated this encounter.

“Do you want to die?” Bao Qi stared coldly at Shaya.

"You wouldn't dare kill a Demon King outright," Shaya said with difficulty, her face growing slightly pale. "The Demon Saints would never allow it." As a Demon King herself, she seemed unable to even muster the thought of resistance in Bao Qi's presence.

Bao Qi snorted, “Everyone here is mine. If I kill you, who would know? The Demon Saints are all healing their wounds—none of them care about your business.”

Shaya replied, “Then go ahead and kill me.”

Bao Qi’s gaze grew dangerous, but he still held back. With a vicious swing, he flung Shaya away.

When she landed, Shaya coughed violently for a while before slowly recovering. She looked up and said, “Bao Qi, I didn’t come here to fight you. I have something to discuss.”

“I have nothing to say to you. Get out now, or don’t blame me for really killing you.” Bao Qi snorted coldly.

Shaya smiled faintly, her red lips moving. She said something, but no sound came out.

Bao Qi was taken aback, looking at Shaya with a strange expression. He sneered, “You really are a slut!”

Shaya didn’t mind, replying softly, “I’m just a Succubus. The outsiders are not easy to deal with. If I want to survive, I have to join hands with you. For that, I’m willing to pay any price.”

Bao Qi sneered, “That’s exactly why I look down on you.”

Shaya said, “Plenty of people look down on me. I only care if you’ll accept my proposal.”

Bao Qi pondered for a moment, then nodded, “If you’re that determined, what do I have to fear?” As he spoke, he licked his lips, a lecherous glint in his eyes. “A Demon King-level Succubus—I’ve never played with one before.”

Shaya giggled, swaying her hips as she walked forward. “Then tonight, your wish will come true.”

Bao Qi burst out laughing, wrapping his arm around Shaya’s waist and giving her shapely rear a hard slap.

Shaya feigned shyness, “Not here. There are so many people.”

Bao Qi scoffed, “You’re afraid of people?” Even so, he flickered and, taking Shaya with him, flew toward the palace.

Just moments ago, these two had seemed utterly irreconcilable, but in the blink of an eye, sparks were flying between them. It was simply incomprehensible.

Yang Kai followed silently after them.

Inside the palace, Bao Qi released Shaya, then glanced back at Yang Kai and snorted, “Where did you catch this slave?”

With a single glance, he could tell Yang Kai was not a true demon, but had been transformed by Demonic Qi. Though Demonic Qi coiled around his body, that foreign aura could not be concealed.

Shaya chuckled, “This guy overestimated himself and brought people to attack me, so I taught him a lesson. Ignore him.”

Bao Qi sneered, “You were so timid with people around—now that someone’s watching, you’re fine?”

“He’s just my slave.”

Bao Qi said no more. Instead, he reached out with a crude and violent motion, tearing away Shaya's clothes in an instant. Her snow-white, delicate body was suddenly exposed to the air, with hints of alluring beauty visible beneath the tattered fabric.

Shaya let out a soft hum of complaint, as if reproaching Bao Qi for his brutishness, yet her beautiful eyes sparkled with seductive allure that could bewitch the Divine Soul.

She coiled around Bao Qi’s burly body like a nimble serpent, her full chest pressing against his torso, compressing slightly.

Bao Qi let out a cold laugh, one arm wrapping around Shaya’s soft, supple body and pulling her in with force, as if he wished to fuse her into himself, while his other hand began to roam wantonly.

At that moment, Yang Kai suddenly stepped forward.

This unexpected movement made Bao Qi alert. He looked up at Yang Kai.

What he saw was a golden light at the corner of that tribesman’s left eye—a vertical pupil radiating majesty, as if it could seize souls. Bao Qi’s Divine Soul trembled, and he was dazed for a moment.

“Bitch!” Bao Qi cursed furiously.

He had never truly believed Shaya's words about joining forces with him. He simply relied on his superior strength, and the fact that this was his own territory. With the mindset of "why waste good food when it's right in front of you," he harbored intentions of toying with her for a while.

He was certain Shaya couldn’t stir up any trouble while in his grasp. The Succubus’ charms were indeed hard to guard against, but as a powerful Demon King, he had a high resistance to such arts.

The first thing he did was grab Shaya, cutting off any chance for her to plot against him. If she dared try anything, she would face his storm-like assault.

He had never expected the attack to come not from Shaya, but from the slave she had brought!

He could roughly sense the slave’s strength—about that of a Demon Commander—so he hadn’t paid him any mind.

But this Demon Commander had moved so swiftly that Bao Qi found himself caught off guard. As he was consumed by rage, the Demonic Qi within him surged violently outward as he sought to strike Shaya down on the spot. In that moment, he no longer cared about the rules of the Demon Saints or the punishment that might follow.

Shaya was clearly stunned as well.

Bao Qi hadn’t expected Yang Kai to act, and neither had Shaya. According to her plan, the best time to strike would have been when she and Bao Qi were in the throes of passion, when a man’s guard was at its lowest.

Only at that moment would a man’s vigilance drop drastically.

Yang Kai’s sudden move was so unexpected that she had no time to react. Sensing the violent Demonic Qi within Bao Qi, Shaya let out a sharp cry. Her pure Divine Soul Secret Art struck Bao Qi, leaving him dazed once more.

The next instant, he suddenly saw a budding lotus blossom rapidly before his eyes, accompanied by intense pain in his mind.

The Demonic Qi he had gathered instantly collapsed. Bao Qi didn’t even have time to roar before he felt a sudden weightlessness. When he regained control of his body, he was no longer in the familiar palace, but in a strange place.

His arms were empty. Shaya had taken advantage of his daze to escape, her face now pale and full of lingering fear.

Yang Kai stood not far away, hands behind his back, quietly watching Bao Qi.

“Sir, why did you…” Shaya looked at Yang Kai with a hint of complaint, but quickly remembered her status and softened her tone. “If you’re going to act, at least give me a heads up.”

"Why would I need to inform you about dealing with a mere Middle-Level Demon King?" Yang Kai cast a faint glance at Shaya.

Shaya dared not make a sound. She thought, if I hadn’t run fast enough just now, I’d probably already be dead at Bao Qi’s hands.

Even so, she couldn’t help but be shocked. Last time Yang Kai dragged her into this world, it was because her demon heart had been severely wounded, leaving her unable to resist.

This world was strange, but she guessed Yang Kai couldn’t just pull people in at will—there must be some condition or price to pay. Otherwise, he’d be invincible and wouldn’t need to cultivate at all.

Bao Qi’s strength far surpassed hers, so Shaya couldn’t understand how Yang Kai had managed to drag him in as well.

The current situation made Shaya breathe a sigh of relief. Since Bao Qi was now in this world, his life and death were no longer his to decide. Thinking of this, Shaya revealed a genuine smile.

“Bitch, you dare plot against me?” Bao Qi remained calm, quietly sizing up this world while glaring coldly at Shaya.

Shaya laughed, “Who else can you blame for your own carelessness?” She turned to Yang Kai, “Sir, let me kill him.”

“Sir?” Bao Qi narrowed his eyes, looking at Shaya with disdain. “You actually call a Demon Commander ‘Sir’? What makes him worthy to stand above us Demon Kings? Shaya, you really have no self-respect.”

Shaya smiled, “My lord’s strength is beyond your imagination.”

Bao Qi shook his head, “You’re not worthy of the title Demon King.”

Though his face remained calm, Bao Qi was inwardly surprised. He had thought Shaya and this tribesman were master and servant, but it turned out to be the opposite. What strange ability did this demonized Demon Commander have to make a Demon King bow her head?

Then again, his abilities were indeed mysterious. Just now, he’d been hit by two consecutive Divine Soul secret arts and couldn’t defend against either, ending up in this damned place before he knew it.

“Put some clothes on before you speak.” Yang Kai glanced at Shaya.

“Yes!” Shaya obediently complied, quickly taking out a fresh set of clothes and dressing herself. Though still revealing, it was much better than being naked.

Yang Kai turned to Bao Qi and said, “You can probably guess the situation. There are only two paths before you.” As he spoke, Yang Kai raised one finger, pointing at Shaya. “Be like her and serve me.” Then he raised another finger, pointing at Bao Qi. “Or I’ll kill you.”

## Chapter 2942 — Life Is Hard

A little over half a month ago, when Nanmen Dajun returned to Lingxiao Palace from Qingyang Shrine, Yang Kai entrusted him with bringing back the Thousand Illusion Dream Butterfly. Yang Kai valued the Thousand Illusion Dream Butterfly greatly, for he himself had gained much from the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape.

The Thousand Illusion Dreamscape would surely become the foundation of Lingxiao Palace. Compared to it, even the Heavenly Sound Hall, arranged with the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box at its core, seemed far less significant.

However, the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape was not unchanging. Every cultivator who entered might encounter a different illusion. Those illusions could easily pass for reality, yet were born from the real world, making it impossible to distinguish truth from falsehood. Still, the benefits gained within the illusions could bring considerable improvement.

Especially since the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape had the miraculous effect of stretching time, which was extremely precious. Chi Yue and the others were Yang Kai’s friends and family, so Hua Qingsi chose them as the first group to enter the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. As it turned out, each of them gained something from the experience.

The Thousand Illusion Dreamscape was a vast treasure trove. It was impossible to mine it all at once; it could only be explored gradually, step by step.

After exchanging a few words with Hua Qingsi, Yang Kai set out with Zhu Lie.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle was extremely fast. One man and one dragon sped through the sky inside the shuttle.

Zhu Lie was puzzled by Yang Kai’s actions.

“Zhu Qing is trapped in the Frozen Earth. Why are you going to Li Dragon Palace?”

While controlling the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, Yang Kai glanced at him and asked, “Have you ever been to the Frozen Earth?”

Zhu Lie shook his head. “Since I was born, I have rarely left Dragon Isle. The Dragon Clan is not allowed to show themselves outside often. I’ve only heard of the Frozen Earth by name, never visited.”

“I haven’t been there either.” Yang Kai chuckled, then asked, “So do you know where to look for your sister?”

Zhu Lie shook his head again. “I don’t. I’ve tried to contact her these days, but I’ve never gotten a response. I’m afraid she really might be…”

Yang Kai said, “If she’s alive, we must see her in person; if she’s dead, we must see her corpse. Don’t jump to conclusions before you see her.”

“That may be so, but…”

“No buts!” Yang Kai shot him a sharp look. “Neither of us has been to the Frozen Earth, nor do we know where to find your sister. But there is someone who can help.”

Zhu Lie thought for a moment and said, “That person is in Li Dragon Palace?”

“Not bad!” Yang Kai grinned. “To do a good job, one must first sharpen their tools. Although going to Li Dragon Palace to find that person will take some time, it’s better than blindly searching the Frozen Earth. However, that person’s status is rather special. We may need your help.”

Zhu Lie sneered, “You can’t beat him?”

Yang Kai shook his head. “It’s not a matter of strength, but of courage. If I ask him to accompany me to the Frozen Earth, he definitely won’t be willing. If I try to force him, we’ll fall out, which won’t help us find your sister. But if you step in, things might go smoothly.”

“Oh?” Zhu Lie was curious, not knowing who Yang Kai meant, but he didn’t ask further. He’d find out soon enough.

Li Dragon Palace had stood for over ten thousand years. In the Northern Territory, it was once counted among the four top sects alongside Ice Heart Valley, Wenqing Sect, and Mitian Sect, because each of these sects had an Emperor Realm expert overseeing them.

The Palace Master of Li Dragon Palace, Li Jiao, was a half-yao expert with a trace of Dragon Clan blood. At its peak, Li Dragon Palace once overshadowed the other three sects, enjoying unmatched prestige.

But ever since Lingxiao Palace rose to prominence in the Northern Territory, life at Li Dragon Palace had become difficult.

No one knew how their Palace Master had offended Lingxiao Palace, but since last year, eighty percent of the sect’s annual income had to be handed over to Lingxiao Palace, leaving only twenty percent for themselves.

Li Dragon Palace was a large sect with tens of thousands of disciples. Yet, that twenty percent amounted to only ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals—a sum far from enough. From Li Jiao down to the ordinary disciples, everyone’s benefits and welfare had plummeted, and all had to tighten their belts.

Because of this, many elders in Li Dragon Palace were extremely dissatisfied with Lingxiao Palace. They often whispered in Li Jiao’s ear, urging him to lead them to Lingxiao Palace to seek revenge and restore their honor.

But Li Jiao didn’t have the guts. The elders and disciples below didn’t know Lingxiao Palace’s true strength, but he knew all too well. Last time, he’d only gone along with Ji Ying for the excitement, but nearly lost everything. Now, just hearing the words “Lingxiao Palace” made his heart pound—he wouldn’t dare visit them again.

Fortunately, Li Dragon Palace wasn’t the only unlucky one. Mitian Sect was in a similar situation. The two top sects were now like brothers in hardship, clinging together for warmth.

On this day, in the main hall of Li Dragon Palace, many elders were gathered. As usual, the hall was noisy and chaotic. The Emperor Realm experts had none of the composure or calm befitting their status. The whole hall was like a bustling marketplace, filled with smoke and noise.

This hadn’t been common before, but since last year, it happened frequently. The elders were fighting over just one thing—profit!

Li Dragon Palace’s annual income was about fifty million Upper-Level Source Crystals. Such a large sum was more than enough for the entire sect, meeting every elder’s needs and leaving a surplus to expand the sect’s reserves for emergencies.

But since last year, the fifty million had dropped to ten million, and problems emerged. Every elder wanted more for their own peaks and halls. Alchemy needed Source Crystals, artifact refining needed Source Crystals, cultivation needed Source Crystals—who would be willing to lose out?

But there were only ten million Source Crystals to last the whole year, and the amount left for each was even less.

If there were still reserves in the treasury, it would be fine—they could just draw from the sect’s stores to ease the crisis. But the treasury of Li Dragon Palace was nearly empty. Years of accumulation had already been sent to Lingxiao Palace to pay off debts, and there would be no improvement for the next fifty years.

Given all this, the elders gathered in the hall were even less willing to give an inch. Each argued fiercely for the greatest share.

Li Jiao sat at the head, listening to the uproar below with a gloomy face, saying nothing and making no move to stop it.

Now, he only regretted going to Lingxiao Palace with Ji Ying back then. It would have been one thing to go, but he even made a bet with Yang Kai—and wagered so much of the sect’s fortune.

What madness! Li Jiao sighed and slowly closed his eyes.

At some point, the noise below ceased. An old man with white hair and beard cupped his fists and said, “Palace Master, the disciples can’t cultivate without pills. The operation of my Alchemy Room is vital to the whole sect. I ask the Sect Master to allocate three million Upper-Level Source Crystals for this year’s alchemy expenses.”

“Three million? Elder Xu, you’re asking for too much! The palace only has ten million Source Crystals a year now, and you want three million in one go. What are the rest of us supposed to do—drink the northwest wind?”

“Indeed, three million is too much. I think five hundred thousand is enough. Even without pills, we can still cultivate—just a bit slower. We just have to get through these fifty years.”

“Fifty years? In fifty years, the younger generation will be ruined. Without enough pills, how can their potential be developed? Li Dragon Palace’s younger generation will have a huge gap, and when the sect declines, who will take responsibility?”

“Elder Xu, don’t exaggerate. Fifty years is nothing. Which of us hasn’t cultivated for over a thousand years? Did you have plenty of pills when you were young? Yet you still reached the Emperor Realm.”

“That’s why this old man is only at the First Stage. If I’d had enough pills when I was young, I’d be much stronger now.”

After a brief calm, another round of arguments broke out.

At last, Li Jiao could bear it no longer. He slammed his palm down in anger, and the chair beneath him instantly turned to dust.

The noisy hall fell silent. All eyes turned to Li Jiao.

With the Palace Master angry, no one dared act out anymore.

“Enough!” Li Jiao swept his gaze over them, biting his teeth. “Every day, I hear you all bickering here, but you never come up with a solution. You are all elders of Li Dragon Palace, all Emperor Realm experts. Is this how you should behave? If the disciples below saw this, where would our dignity be?”

Chastised, everyone lowered their heads in embarrassment.

Elder Xu of the Alchemy Room pondered for a moment and said, “Palace Master, the Source Crystal issue must be resolved quickly, or else…”

“You think I don’t know?” Li Jiao glared at him. “This Lord was careless that day and fell into a little thief’s trap, dragging all of Li Dragon Palace down.”

Elder Xu said, “Palace Master, don’t blame yourself too much. Even Sect Master Mi didn’t see through it that day. It’s just that brat from Lingxiao Palace was too cunning. As long as you remain, Li Dragon Palace will surely rise again.”

The other elders all nodded in agreement.

Li Jiao said, “In any case, things are as they are. This Lord has a proposal and hopes all the elders will cooperate. If we can carry it out, Li Dragon Palace will surely get through this crisis.”

Elder Xu asked, “What brilliant plan does the Palace Master have?”

Li Jiao looked around, coughed lightly, and said, “You are all Emperor Realm experts and have served as elders of Li Dragon Palace for many years. I’m sure you all have some savings. My idea is… everyone first brings out their savings for public use. Consider it a loan to the palace. After fifty years, you’ll be repaid with interest. You won’t lose out. What do you all think?”

No one responded. Wherever Li Jiao’s gaze fell, everyone lowered their heads.

Li Jiao snorted coldly. “None of you have any savings? Is everyone in Li Dragon Palace a pauper?”

## Chapter 2651 — The Arrival of the Three Yao Kings

Last time, when he brought Feng Xi to Ice Heart Valley to propose marriage, he suffered a crushing defeat at Yang Kai's hands. Not only was he ruthlessly beaten with the Emperor Extinction Pill, but even the Spatial Ring containing the betrothal gifts was snatched away.

Back then, Yang Kai was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.

This was a humiliation beyond compare—a disgrace that could never be washed away. Now, meeting his enemy face to face, Yao Zhuo’s eyes blazed with hatred, his desire for vengeance burning fiercer than ever.

Yao Zhuo's strength was already formidable, and now, having advanced to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, his attacks were even more overwhelming, as if he could tear the heavens asunder. An ordinary First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator would stand no chance; attempting to block his long blade would likely mean instant death.

"Yang Kai, be careful!"

"Senior Brother Yang, be careful!"

Bing Yun, Ji Yao, and the others all cried out in alarm, deeply worried.

In contrast, Yang Kai stood motionless, as if he hadn't reacted at all, not even attempting to dodge. This sight made Bing Yun and the others even more anxious.

Yao Zhuo was overjoyed, pouring even more power into his hand. The dazzling blade light fell like a meteor, blinding to the eye.

Just then, everyone's vision blurred. A mysterious figure brushed past Yao Zhuo like a wisp of green smoke and appeared behind him.

At the same time, Yao Zhuo, who had been charging at Yang Kai with great momentum, suddenly froze in place as if struck by a binding spell. The light of the Imperial Treasure long blade in his hand shattered, and the aura of his Third Stage Emperor Realm deflated rapidly, like a punctured balloon.

"Pfft..." For no apparent reason, Yao Zhuo spat out a mouthful of blood mist. His face turned as pale as paper, and he swayed unsteadily.

"What?" The people of Wenqing Sect were shocked, their eyes filled with horror as they stared at the wisp of green smoke behind Yao Zhuo.

That wisp of green smoke slowly unfurled, revealing a figure. At this moment, the figure, who had appeared out of nowhere, stood behind Yao Zhuo, looking calm and at ease, holding something in his hand.

Thump, thump... thump, thump...

The sound of beating echoed from that object, making everyone's scalp tingle and their hearts tremble in fear.

They saw clearly that what the man held was nothing else but a still-beating, warm heart, about the size of a fist, with thick blood vessels clearly visible. With every beat, fresh blood flowed from it.

Gulp...

The sound of swallowing echoed as everyone's gaze instinctively shifted to Yao Zhuo's back. With just one look, their faces turned ashen and their bodies trembled.

At some unknown moment, a hole had appeared in Yao Zhuo's back, piercing straight through his body so one could see the scenery in front of him through it. And in his chest cavity, where his heart should have been, there was nothing at all!

The truth was now plain for all to see—this middle-aged man in green robes had reached out and ripped Yao Zhuo’s heart straight from his chest. What kind of terrifying skill was this? After all, Yao Zhuo was a Third Stage Emperor Realm master!

"Who is this person?"

"Is there such a person in the Northern Territory?"

One question after another arose in everyone's hearts, but no answers could be found.

"Yao King!" Feng Xuan suddenly shouted in a deep voice, as if recognizing something.

At the same time, Yao Zhuo struggled to lower his head and glance at the hole in his chest. His face instantly drained of color, and he turned back, trembling, to plead, "Sect Master, save me..."

Fortunately, his strength was great; otherwise, having his heart ripped out would have meant instant death. If Feng Xuan could help him retrieve his heart and supplement it with some healing elixirs, he might yet survive. So Yao Zhuo desperately used his cultivation to seal his blood flow and cried out for help.

“Your Sect Master can barely save himself—how could he possibly come to rescue you?” Yang Kai sneered. With a flick of his finger, a Moon Blade shot toward Yao Zhuo.

Normally, even if Yao Zhuo couldn't dodge such an attack, he would have ways to defend against it. But now, most of his strength was being used to suppress his injuries, leaving him no energy to evade or resist.

With a hiss, the Moon Blade swept past, slicing Yao Zhuo cleanly in two. His remains fell to the ground, turning into a pile of flesh.

A Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivator was killed so easily!

The people of Wenqing Sect were all struck dumb with terror, their eyes filled with fear.

Pfft...

Yingfei crushed the heart in his hand, his eagle-like gaze sweeping over the Wenqing Sect crowd as he asked, "Young Master Yang, are these the people?"

Yang Kai nodded, "Yes."

Yingfei's lips curled slightly as he said calmly, "How should we deal with them? Just say the word, Young Master Yang."

Yang Kai snorted, "How else? Kill them all!"

Yingfei nodded. In the next instant, his figure turned into a streak of green light and shot into the ranks of the more than twenty Emperor Realm cultivators of Wenqing Sect, weaving back and forth as if no one could stop him, his aura overwhelming.

The crowd was thrown into chaos, each person unleashing their own techniques to dodge or defend.

Feng Xuan was especially furious, his Wind and Thunder Divine Spear transforming into wind and thunder as he attacked Yingfei.

Among those present, only his cultivation matched Yingfei's, so he might be able to put up some resistance. But Yingfei's true form was a giant eagle, naturally gifted with speed. He seemed intent on sowing panic, never clashing head-on with Feng Xuan, instead darting through the crowd a few times before suddenly retreating.

"Pfft, pfft, pfft..."

Several muffled sounds rang out as a few Emperor Realm cultivators spewed blood skyward. When the others looked over, they saw holes torn open in these people's chests, and where their hearts should have been, there was nothing.

This scene was exactly the same as what had happened to Yao Zhuo earlier.

Looking at Yingfei's large hand, sure enough, he was clutching several still-beating hearts of various sizes, all thumping away.

He squeezed, crushing the hearts to pulp.

The wounded saw this, and their faces turned ashen. Their bodies swayed, and they fell from the sky. Though their cultivation allowed them to hold on for a moment, with their hearts gouged out, they were only prolonging the inevitable. Death would come soon.

In the blink of an eye, four or five Emperor Realm cultivators had fallen victim. Panic instantly swept through the Wenqing Sect ranks. The remaining Emperor Realm cultivators looked at Yingfei as if he were a demon incarnate, with no will to fight left, only wishing to escape this place as soon as possible.

Meanwhile, the people of Ice Heart Valley were greatly encouraged. The worry and resolve in the beautiful eyes of the women vanished, replaced by a dazzling brilliance.

"A mere Yao King dares to act so arrogantly before this Lord!" Feng Xuan roared in fury. Sensing the wavering morale among his ranks, he naturally wanted to turn the tide.

Things had reached a point of no return—between Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley, it was now a battle to the death, with no other path left to take. What was supposed to be a triumphant siege had suddenly turned chaotic with the unexpected appearance of a Yao King. Feng Xuan’s hatred ran deep; to steady his shaken forces, he was determined to kill this foe first and restore morale.

"One Yao King isn't enough? How about two?" A thunderous voice rang out, and a bolt of lightning struck, transforming into a burly figure who stood shoulder to shoulder with Yingfei.

Xi Lei!

"If two aren't enough, then how about three!"

Another figure strolled over and stood beside Yingfei and Xi Lei.

The three great Yao Kings gathered, no longer hiding themselves. Their Yao Yuan surged, as if about to break through the heavens.

Sensing the strength of these three, the remaining Emperor Realm cultivators of Wenqing Sect began to tremble, and even Feng Xuan's face turned pale with disbelief.

After all, while a Yao King's cultivation was about the same as a Third Stage Emperor Realm, due to their sensitive status as yao, they rarely appeared in the human world, usually hiding deep in the mountains and forests, ruling their own domains.

Of course, some powerful yao did join human sects, becoming elders or protectors.

But those were few and far between.

Feng Xuan had never imagined that three Yao Kings would gather in one place, all targeting his Wenqing Sect.

In that instant, Feng Xuan tasted a bitterness deeper than swallowing gall. If it were just a single Yao King, he might still have the will to fight. But with three appearing at once, even if Yao Zhuo miraculously returned from the dead, Wenqing Sect would stand no chance against such overwhelming power.

"Three Yao Kings!"

"Where did Senior Brother Yang find them?"

"He disappeared for a while—was he out seeking reinforcements? I thought he ran off alone when Ice Heart Valley was in danger. I'm so ashamed!"

"We're saved, we're saved!"

...

The Emperor Realm cultivators of Bing Yun's Valley who didn't know the truth were overjoyed, looking at Yang Kai with gratitude.

Now they finally understood how Yang Kai had managed to kill ten Emperor Realm cultivators at once. With three Yao Kings at his side, what were ten Emperor Realm cultivators?

"How did things go?" Yang Kai asked, feeling reassured as Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei approached.

Xie Wuwei replied, "Working with Brother Xi Lei, we killed over ten thousand. There are still some stragglers, but their morale is broken—they're no longer a threat."

"Over ten thousand..."

Instantly, the people of Wenqing Sect staggered.

They didn't doubt Xie Wuwei's words. Two Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivators working together could easily slaughter over ten thousand, since none of those people were Emperors and couldn't resist at all.

With over ten thousand dead, every sect would suffer heavy losses. And that didn't even count the Emperor Realm cultivators who had died. After this battle, the Northern Territory's strength would be greatly diminished.

But what concerned them most was their own fate. They had charged into someone else's sect, thinking victory was assured, but in the blink of an eye, the tables had turned. Now, they were the ones at the mercy of others.

"Senior Bing Yun, leave things here to us. Please take the Junior Sisters and check inside the valley," Yang Kai said, turning to Bing Yun.

Bing Yun hesitated for a moment, then nodded, "Alright!"

Although the outcome here was already decided and Ice Heart Valley was destined to win, an army of a hundred thousand flooding into Ice Heart Valley was no joke. With only seven thousand disciples, they would be hard-pressed to hold out. Fortunately, two Yao Kings had already acted, scattering the enemy and minimizing Ice Heart Valley's losses.

Now, they just needed to clean up the aftermath and preserve as much of Ice Heart Valley's strength as possible.

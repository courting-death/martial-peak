## Chapter 3142 — Hundred Birds Pay Court to the Phoenix

Huang Tudao's explosive shout made Biluo jolt, but fortunately, she had used the walk over to prepare an excuse. She showed no panic, merely smoothing the hair by her ear with a smile. "Manor Master Huang, please calm your anger. I came today only to pass along a message from my Lady."

If she wanted to survive tonight, she could only borrow Shan Qingluo's name. Just as Shan Qingluo dreaded Huang Tudao, Huang Tudao was deeply wary of Shan Qingluo as well. After all, she was an opponent whose cultivation was no weaker than his.

Huang Tudao's eyes narrowed. "A message from Shan Qingluo? I wonder what it is. Could she have agreed to give herself to this Lord? Hahahaha!"

Biluo pursed her lips in a smile.

"She said all of you will die tonight!"

Huang Tudao's booming laughter cut off at once. Every cultivator in the hall was startled, and all of them slapped their tables and rose, glaring furiously at Biluo.

Biluo's smile froze. She lowered her head and looked down, because those words had not come from her at all. Liu Yan had spoken them.

Huang Tudao looked at Liu Yan, then at Biluo, and said mildly, "I cannot pretend I did not hear that."

Cold sweat slid down Biluo's forehead, and she hurriedly said, "Children speak without thinking. She was only talking nonsense." At the same time, she shot Liu Yan a vicious glare, warning her not to speak again.

"Children speak without thinking?" Huang Tudao snorted. "It seems to me you came here tonight to pick a fight. Very good. I was willing to let things pass because you are only a woman, but since this is how you want it, do not blame this Lord for having no pity for the fairer sex."

"Manor Master Huang, calm your anger!" Bitterness filled Biluo. She truly did not know what sin she had committed to deserve such torment. She quickly gave Liu Yan a push. "Be good and apologize to Manor Master Huang."

Liu Yan said, "He is going to die anyway. Why apologize?"

Huang Tudao laughed from sheer anger. He sat up straight, braced both hands on the table, leaned forward slightly, and fixed Liu Yan with a hard stare. "Interesting little girl. Then this Lord would like to see exactly how I die tonight." Then came another explosive shout. "If I do not die tonight, the ones who die will be you!"

Whether or not it was childish nonsense, this little girl had offended him again and again, so she naturally deserved a lesson. He was no kindhearted soul, and killing a child would not weigh heavily on his conscience.

"You will not see it." Liu Yan answered calmly. Her small hand suddenly rose, and a wisp of fire burned to life at her fingertip. It was no more than a tiny flame, wavering as if any casual breath could put it out.

The sight drew a burst of loud laughter from the surrounding crowd.

Huang Tudao did not laugh. Instead, his expression turned utterly grave, because the instant that tiny flame appeared, a chill had run through his bones.

Liu Yan flicked her finger, and the little flame flew out, shooting straight toward Huang Tudao.

Huang Tudao shot to his feet as if facing a mortal enemy. With a low roar, more than a dozen thick beast marks suddenly surfaced on his body. At the same time, one of the marks flashed, and an ape-like giant beast appeared before him. The Yao Beast was full of ferocity and carried the aura of a Tenth-Tier beast. The moment it appeared, it beat its chest with both hands, bared its teeth, and roared in fury.

The roar cut off abruptly. A pitch-black bloody hole appeared in the giant beast's chest, and faint flames clung to the edges of the wound, spreading swiftly outward until its whole body caught fire.

After piercing through the giant beast, the flame kept coming. It rushed right up to Huang Tudao and easily burned through his protective Saint Yuan and a defensive Secret Treasure.

Like dry firewood meeting a roaring blaze, Huang Tudao's entire body caught fire, and his screams shot straight into the clouds.

With a clattering commotion, everyone from Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor who had been laughing so loudly moments ago shot to their feet in horror. Wine and delicacies spilled all over the floor from the tables before them, and the dancing girls in the hall screamed and fled in every direction.

Biluo stood rooted to the spot, stiff all over. Firelight reflected in her eyes, and for a moment she almost thought she was dreaming.

The screams did not last long. After only three breaths, Huang Tudao crashed to the ground and turned into a pile of black ash, without even his bones remaining. The giant beast he had summoned met the same fate.

In the blink of an eye, Huang Tudao had fallen!

The entire hall fell silent. Not even breathing could be heard. Everyone was dumbstruck. No one could have imagined that the Manor Master of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, who had been so majestic and overbearing moments ago, would be killed just like that, and at the hands of a little girl of seven or eight.

A single speck of flame had burned a Third Stage Void King Realm master to ash. Who was this little girl, and what kind of fire was that?

After a brief silence, robes snapped through the air as figure after figure fled in every direction. Amid a rumbling series of crashes, more than a dozen holes were smashed through the hall.

They had no choice but to run. Although they did not know exactly what cultivation this little girl had or what methods she possessed, if she could kill Huang Tudao with the slightest motion, taking everyone else's lives would probably be as easy as turning over her hand.

If they wanted to live, they could only flee as far as possible.

"Thirteen..." Liu Yan seemed not to notice them at all. She merely murmured calmly, then said in an indifferent voice, "Hundred Birds, Pay Court to the Phoenix!"

Liu Yan's small frame trembled, and clusters of fire suddenly shot out in every direction. Each cluster transformed into a small bird, every one strange and unique, without the slightest repetition. The fiery little birds chirped, traced beautiful arcs through the air, then rushed out of the hall and vanished.

Screams rang out one after another, all of them short and sharp, as powerful auras were snuffed out one by one.

Biluo trembled, not from joy, but from fear.

The little girl she had thought harmless had suddenly revealed power far beyond her imagination, frightening her to the core. Acting on instinct, she flickered out of the hall, looked around, and froze where she stood.

She saw fire birds flying and weaving through the vast city. They were no longer small and exquisite; each had become a colossal creature trailing a dazzling tail of flame. Wherever they passed, everything was burned to nothing.

In only this short while, within a hundred zhang of the hall, there was no longer a single living thing besides her and Liu Yan. Everyone had been killed cleanly. Those people who had strutted through the hall earlier and spoken so rudely had long since turned to ash somewhere unknown; now not even their shadows remained. And the fire birds were still spreading their might outward.

A chill ran through her bones!

Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor was finished. This time, it had truly been uprooted.

Though she did not know exactly what Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor had been celebrating tonight, gathering so many masters together had clearly been an immense mistake. It had given Liu Yan the chance to wipe them all out at once.

Only now did Biluo belatedly understand.

No wonder this little girl had insisted Biluo bring her to Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor. No wonder she had not been afraid at all, instead speaking wildly again and again in the hall. With such terrifying strength, what was there to fear?

A cold wind blew over, and Biluo's chaotic thoughts cleared slightly. She returned to the hall in a daze, only to find Liu Yan sitting quietly in Huang Tudao's former seat, yawning carelessly and looking bored.

The corner of Biluo's eye twitched hard.

Three days later, Yang Kai painstakingly pulled his arm out from beneath Shan Qingluo, only for her to wrap herself around him again like an octopus.

He reached out and slapped her backside, leaving a bright red handprint at once. Yang Kai said, "Still not full?"

Shan Qingluo narrowed her hazy eyes and said lazily, "Am I that hard to satisfy?"

Yang Kai asked, amused, "Are you not?"

The past three days had been utterly wanton. Shan Qingluo seemed determined to make up for all the decades she had lost, taking the initiative again and again, as if she would not stop until she squeezed Yang Kai into dried meat. The result, naturally, was that her skills were inferior, and she lost every bout.

Yang Kai's current cultivation was two great realms higher than hers, and he also possessed a half-dragon body. How could she endure him once he went on the offensive? Yet the more she lost, the more determined she became, allowing Yang Kai to enjoy himself thoroughly for several days.

She and Su Yan were simply two extremes.

"What nonsense." Shan Qingluo shot him an annoyed look and pinched him hard.

Yang Kai smiled faintly, his large hand settling over her flat lower belly. "If you pinch me to death, the child in your belly will have no father."

Shan Qingluo's eyes flew open. "Child..." she murmured, reaching out to touch her belly. That was right. Perhaps there was already a child in her belly now. If so, then even when he was no longer by her side, she would still have some comfort.

She flipped over and straddled him, her beautiful hair falling down to cover the boundless spring scenery before her chest. Shan Qingluo pursed her lips and said, "Give me a little more. This time, I must get pregnant."

Yang Kai did not know whether to laugh or cry. "I only said that casually, yet you took it seriously."

"You said it, and you cannot take it back. Do not even think of shirking responsibility!" Shan Qingluo reached out and grabbed Yang Kai by the neck, her face fierce.

Yang Kai said, "How would I dare? It is just that someone has come looking for you!" He reached out and pointed outside the door.

"Who?" Shan Qingluo frowned. "I will not see them!"

"Yu Xiong!" Yang Kai smiled faintly.

Chi Yue had taken in an adopted son and an adopted daughter back then. The adopted daughter was naturally Shan Qingluo, while the adopted son was Yu Xiong. He and Shan Qingluo addressed each other as brother and sister.

When Yang Kai had come to Di Chen Star back then, he had dealt with Yu Xiong before. He was an extremely straightforward man, so Yang Kai was quite familiar with him.

Hearing that Yu Xiong had come looking for her, Shan Qingluo naturally could not refuse to see him, and could only rise and dress.

A moment later, the door opened, and Yu Xiong's loud shouting was immediately heard. "Biluo, move aside. I have an important matter to tell Little Sister. You dare block me?"

Biluo said anxiously, "Sir, Lady cannot receive guests right now. Please wait a moment. I will go report to her."

Yu Xiong said angrily, "What is inconvenient about receiving guests..." As he was still arguing with her, the door opened. Yu Xiong immediately cast Biluo aside and went forward to meet Shan Qingluo. Seeing Shan Qingluo radiant and glowing, he could not help being stunned, and the words on the tip of his tongue were swallowed back down.

When he raised his head again, he saw Yang Kai walking out from behind Shan Qingluo.

"It is you, you little bastard!" Yu Xiong's brow jumped, and he threw a punch straight at Yang Kai, shouting, "Boy, hand over your life!"

Shan Qingluo flashed aside and watched with a beaming smile.

She was not worried that Yang Kai would suffer a loss, nor that Yang Kai would injure Yu Xiong, so she had no intention of interfering.

## Chapter 2706 — This Is Mine

While Yang Kai was examining himself, Zhu Qing stood to the side, watching him with a hint of curiosity, but she didn’t rush him.

A few days ago, although she was the first to find Yang Kai, she hadn’t known exactly what had happened. She only knew he’d been through a fierce battle. It was only afterward that she learned that his opponents in that fight had actually included Second Stage and even Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivators.

Many of them were far stronger than him, and one was even an Elder of Star God Palace.

Yet in the end, he was the one who won, and all his enemies were wiped out.

Zhu Qing couldn’t help but be moved.

As a member of the Dragon Clan, she prided herself as the leader of all living beings, bearing the mightiest Divine Spirit bloodline. Her standards were always high, and she never regarded other races as equals. Even among the various Divine Spirits, few could compare to the Dragon Clan.

Yet Zhu Qing admitted to herself that even if she had acted personally, when she was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, she could never have accomplished such a feat.

Was he really human? Just for this alone, Zhu Qing truly admired him, but his character was extremely vile. The first time he took liberties with her could be explained as him losing control, drawn by her bloodline, but the second time was definitely on purpose.

If not for the Elder’s instructions to maintain good relations with him, Zhu Qing would have torn him to pieces long ago. How could she have let him live until now?

“You keep staring at me like that—aren’t you afraid I’ll get embarrassed?”

Yang Kai suddenly opened his eyes, looking at Zhu Qing with a teasing smile.

Zhu Qing quickly averted her gaze and turned her head away.

“I’m curious—why didn’t you kill me?” Yang Kai asked, frowning.

“Why would I kill you?” Zhu Qing replied, looking out the window without turning back.

Yang Kai asked in surprise, “You didn’t come to kill me, so why did you come back?”

He hadn’t seen her for nearly a month. For her to suddenly appear when he was at his weakest, it was no wonder Yang Kai misunderstood. He’d thought she was here for revenge, but things turned out differently than he expected.

Zhu Qing turned, glaring at Yang Kai. “What else? Of course, I came to be your friend!”

“Friend...” Yang Kai was stunned, thinking that sounded oddly familiar.

Zhu Qing tossed something toward Yang Kai and said, “This is what you wanted. It took me a lot of effort to get it.”

Yang Kai caught it, giving her a puzzled look, only to realize it was a wine jug.

Could it be...

Yang Kai’s heart skipped a beat. He opened the jug and took a sniff, immediately catching the rich aroma of wine.

“Sword Wine from Wine Sword Manor in the Western Territory. Only ninety-nine jugs are brewed each year. The one in your hand is one of them.”

Cold sweat broke out on Yang Kai’s forehead. He looked at her in surprise and said, “You went to the Western Territory?”

A month ago, he’d just made an offhand remark, half to tease her and half to make things difficult, never expecting she’d actually go and get him a jug of Sword Wine. So, the reason he hadn’t seen her lately wasn’t because she’d given up, but because she’d been running around trying to fulfill his request?

Just a casual remark had sent her running around for a whole month. Yang Kai couldn’t help wondering whether the girl was truly that foolish.

Still, he felt a little guilty inside.

But then he realized something was off. Even if she was a member of the Dragon Clan, how could a month be enough time to travel to and from the Western Territory?

“What does it matter where I went? The point is, I got you what you wanted.” Zhu Qing gritted her teeth in secret—she’d paid quite a price for that jug of Sword Wine. Fixing Yang Kai with a piercing gaze, she demanded, “So, does this make us friends now?”

Yang Kai looked up at her, saying nothing.

Zhu Qing snapped, “Are you going back on your word?”

She steeled herself. If this bastard dared go back on his word, she’d beat him up, Elder’s orders or not, then drag him back to Dragon Isle unconscious. Judging by his current state, she should be able to win. She clenched her fist, sizing up Yang Kai and calculating where to strike to knock him out in one blow. Otherwise, if he activated his Ancestral Dragon Origin, she’d be suppressed again.

Yang Kai stared at her for a long time before finally nodding slightly. “Alright, we’re friends now.”

Being forced to be friends with a Dragon Clan member felt strange, to say the least.

As he spoke, he put the jug of Sword Wine into his Spatial Ring.

Zhu Qing’s face lit up with joy. “Really?”

“Really!” Yang Kai nodded solemnly.

Zhu Qing’s expression changed again. Fiercely, she demanded, “Then why aren’t you drinking the wine? Are you just saying yes, but not really treating me as a friend?”

Yang Kai smiled. “No one’s ever gone to such trouble for me before. I want to keep it as a memento. Whenever I see this jug, I’ll always remember you.”

Who knew what might be in that wine? Although he agreed to be friends with Zhu Qing, this Dragon Clan friend had come out of nowhere, and she definitely had other motives. Yang Kai couldn’t fully trust her.

Still, one thing was certain—she meant him no harm. Otherwise, during the time he was unconscious, he’d have died a hundred times over.

Besides, as he’d told Zhu Qing before, being friends wasn’t as simple as just saying the words.

Zhu Qing hadn’t expected Yang Kai to say something like that. Her face flushed, and her heart fluttered. “Don’t talk nonsense,” she muttered shyly.

Her bashful look made Yang Kai’s eyes light up. Remembering her sweet tongue and warm lips, he couldn’t help but feel his mind wander.

Zhu Qing’s heart was in turmoil too. Although Yang Kai had agreed to be friends, which was a first step, how to invite him to Dragon Isle was still a problem. She couldn’t just blurt it out now—that would make her intentions too obvious. For the moment, she was at a loss.

The atmosphere grew a bit strange. Yang Kai felt his blood racing, as if something would happen if he didn’t suppress it.

He couldn’t understand it. Every time he was with Zhu Qing, he lost control. She was beautiful and had a stunning figure, but there were plenty of beautiful women out there. Why did she alone make his desire surge?

He wanted to ask her to leave, but he’d just agreed to be friends. Chasing her out right after would be too ungracious.

So, he simply grabbed a few Spatial Rings from his bedside and began inspecting them one by one.

These Spatial Rings had belonged to the Emperors he’d killed—seven or eight in total.

Although he’d been unconscious before, all these rings had been collected by Yingfei, who’d arrived later, and placed by his side.

“Let’s see what treasures are inside.” Yang Kai sat cross-legged on the bed, rubbing his hands together in excitement.

Zhu Qing glanced over but didn’t pay much attention. To her, the items in those Spatial Rings were plentiful, but not particularly impressive.

“Tsk tsk, so many Source Crystals.” Yang Kai had just opened the first Spatial Ring and saw it was packed with Source Crystals.

There were nearly ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals alone, thirty or forty million Middle-Level ones, and hundreds of millions of Lower-Level ones.

There were also all kinds of Spiritual Pills, secret manuals, rare minerals, Secret Treasures, and even Imperial Treasures—a dazzling array.

By the time one reaches the Emperor Realm, they’ve already lived for centuries, if not longer. Over such a long span, it’s only natural that they’ve accumulated vast fortunes.

Although Yang Kai’s own wealth was considerable, now that he had established Lingxiao Palace, he naturally had to plan for the future. On Dark Star alone, at least ten thousand people—perhaps even more—would need to relocate here. When that time came, the cultivation resources required would be like a bottomless pit; the more good things he could gather, the better.

Source Crystals would be needed for Lingxiao Palace’s disciples to cultivate. Secret manuals and techniques could provide them with broader paths, and those Secret and Imperial Treasures could be given out as rewards for merit...

He transferred everything from that Spatial Ring into his own, feeling overjoyed.

Then he opened another Spatial Ring and found it even richer.

Yang Kai couldn’t stop grinning, making Zhu Qing frown deeply.

Each of the seven or eight Spatial Rings contained at least ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals, with even more Middle- and Lower-Level ones.

It made sense. These Emperors had come to attend the Flowing Shadow City auction, all aiming for the Heavenly Puppets. How could they not bring enough Source Crystals?

These Upper-Level Source Crystals were probably the entire reserves of their Sects.

In the end, it all ended up in Yang Kai’s hands.

“Wow...” Yang Kai exclaimed exaggeratedly. The Spatial Ring in his hand had even more Source Crystals—fifty or sixty million Upper-Level ones, four or five times more than the others.

This Spatial Ring must have belonged to Tan Junhao, because inside, Yang Kai found the Dragon Isle Token he’d handed over before.

He casually took out the Dragon Isle Token and tossed it into the Small Xuan Realm. Yang Kai always stored his most valuable items in the Small Xuan Realm, leaving only less important things in his Spatial Ring.

“Wait!” Zhu Qing, who had been silent, suddenly called out.

“What is it?” Yang Kai looked up at her.

“That thing just now...” Zhu Qing stepped forward, her tone a bit agitated. “That token—can I see it?”

“You mean this?” Yang Kai took out the Dragon Isle Token again and waved it in front of Zhu Qing.

“Yes, that’s it!” Zhu Qing nodded repeatedly, her beautiful eyes shining with excitement.

“What do you want with it?” Yang Kai guarded the Dragon Isle Token against his chest, turning sideways and declaring seriously, “This is mine!”

Zhu Qing stomped her foot. “I know it’s yours, I just want to look at it.”

What’s with this guy? He’s acting like I’m some thief, guarding that token as if I’d actually steal it from him. Does he really think a proud Dragon Clan member like me would stoop so low?

“No need to look. This is a Dragon Isle Token!” Yang Kai waved the token in front of her. Since the Dragon Isle Token came from Dragon Isle, Zhu Qing, as a Dragon Clan member, would naturally recognize it, so he didn’t bother hiding it.

“It really is a Dragon Isle Token!” Zhu Qing was astonished.

She’d left Dragon Isle this time to track down the last missing Dragon Isle Token. When she passed through Flowing Shadow City, she sensed Yang Kai’s dragon aura, which was why she’d stayed.

She never expected, by a twist of fate, to actually find the Dragon Isle Token.

With this Dragon Isle Token in hand, she could finally return to Dragon Isle and report her success. More than that, she would be able to enter the forbidden grounds to advance her bloodline and gain even greater power!

This Dragon Isle Token meant a great deal to her. How could Zhu Qing not be excited?

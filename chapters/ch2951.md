## Chapter 2951 — The Revolving World

Zhu Qing sensed something was wrong and hurriedly changed the subject, asking, “Where is he?”

Yang Kai shook his head and said, “I don’t know where he went. When the Cold Tide struck, we were all swept up and brought into a small world. He and I got separated. But he shouldn’t be in any danger, so don’t worry.”

“A small world!” Zhu Qing was startled, recalling the hazy sensations she had experienced earlier, and only then did she understand that the overwhelming sense of danger had come from being drawn into a small world. She turned to look around and said, “So this is that small world? It actually looks quite nice.”

In the Small Xuan Realm, birds sang and flowers bloomed, Spiritual Qi was abundant, and the environment was indeed pleasant. Especially after Yang Kai entered the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, the Small Xuan Realm had undergone a fundamental transformation. It was now a world of its own, with complete laws of heaven and earth—a true blessed land.

“Heh heh.”

Yang Kai shook his head with a smile. “This isn’t that small world. This is another one—one that belongs to me... hmm?”

Halfway through his words, Yang Kai suddenly turned his head in a certain direction, as if he had noticed something unusual.

Zhu Qing asked, “What is it?”

Yang Kai pondered for a moment and said, “Let me show you something interesting.”

As he spoke, he suddenly reached out in a certain direction. Zhu Qing widened her eyes in curiosity, and then saw a pitch-black, dog-like creature appear out of thin air before her.

The little black dog appeared in a flash, letting out a few barks as if greatly startled. However, judging by the way its tail wagged and the vigor in its voice, all traces of its former weakness had vanished without a trace.

“Where did this little beast come from? It’s so ugly.” Zhu Qing stared at the little black dog, glanced at Yang Kai, and teased, “Just like you.”

Yang Kai slowly shook his head. “It’s not an ordinary little beast.”

“What’s so special about it?” Zhu Qing frowned slightly.

“You’re of the Dragon Clan. Take a closer look.” As Yang Kai spoke, he lifted the little black dog in front of himself.

Upon hearing Yang Kai’s voice, the little black dog seemed to calm down. Its beady black eyes stared at him, panting with a fawning expression, drool dripping from its big tongue, looking utterly disgusting.

Zhu Qing finally noticed something unusual and hesitated, “It seems to have a very powerful bloodline, but it’s not pure.”

The members of the Dragon Clan were bound by their bloodlines and possessed profound knowledge of them. After Gong Yue had used a secret technique to activate the Guixu bloodline within the little black dog, some traces inevitably remained. It was through this that Zhu Qing was able to discern what was unusual about it.

“Demonic Beast Guixu, ever heard of it?” Yang Kai asked.

Zhu Qing shook her head blankly.

Yang Kai said, “It was my first time hearing about it too, but I’ve seen its power.” As he spoke, he shook the little black dog vigorously. “Come on, show us that move of yours—the one where your mouth splits open. If you refuse, I’ll stew you and eat you.”

“Wang wang!”

“Don’t play dumb. I mean the move you used before, the one where your mouth splits wide open. If you refuse, I’ll cook you.”

“Wang wang!”

Zhu Qing pressed a hand to her forehead and said, “It’s not fully sentient yet—it only understands a little. Talking to it like that is useless.”

Yang Kai sneered, “Whether it understands or not, it knows in its heart. But... forget it. If you say it doesn’t, then it doesn’t. Consider yourself lucky to have survived.”

As he spoke, he let go. The little black dog fell to the ground, looked at Yang Kai, then at Zhu Qing, and finally crawled to Yang Kai’s feet, dawdling as it lay down.

Zhu Qing covered her mouth and giggled. “It seems to like you a lot.”

“Just putting on an act!” Yang Kai nudged the little black dog with his foot, but it remained unmoved.

Ignoring it, Yang Kai looked up at Zhu Qing and said, “Let’s head out first. We need to figure out where we are, and I don’t know if we can return to the Star Realm.”

“It’s not that serious, is it? Aren’t you proficient in the Laws of Space?” Zhu Qing thought Yang Kai was exaggerating, but she felt no sense of crisis. As long as she could be with this man, she didn’t care where they were.

Yang Kai smiled, and with a thought, took Zhu Qing with him as they left the Small Xuan Realm.

Returning to the previous cave, Yang Kai looked at Zhu Qing with a grin. “Want me to carry you? We don’t know where this place is, so we should look around and see if anyone’s here to ask for information.”

Zhu Qing blushed slightly. “I’m not that weak.”

With that, she took the lead and walked out of the cave. Though her steps were light, she seemed to frown with every step.

Yang Kai chuckled to himself, but then suddenly put on a serious expression and said, "You're being annoying."

Zhu Qing’s body trembled, her face instantly drained of color, and she stammered, “You’re already tired of me?”

Yang Kai quickly said, “I’m not talking about you, I mean this guy.”

As he finished speaking, a dog’s bark rang out. The little black dog was thrown out of the Small Xuan Realm. After landing, it barked furiously at Yang Kai, as if complaining about not being brought along.

“Alright, alright, stop barking. If you keep it up, I’ll throw you back in.” Yang Kai said impatiently.

The little black dog immediately fell silent and dashed outside, rushing past Zhu Qing to be the first out of the cave—free to roam the world.

Zhu Qing couldn’t help but breathe a sigh of relief. Yang Kai stepped forward and put his arm around her. She struggled a little but didn’t resist further.

Exiting the cave, they were greeted by a world of white. At first glance, this place looked no different from the Frozen Earth, but both Yang Kai and Zhu Qing knew this was not the Frozen Earth.

Because here, there was none of that unbearable cold.

“Woof woof!” The little black dog’s barking grew closer, sounding panicked, as if it had encountered something terrifying.

Yang Kai released his Divine Sense and soon looked delighted. Zhu Qing also noticed, but, unaccustomed to such intimacy in public, subtly put some distance between herself and Yang Kai.

A black shadow flashed by—the little black dog had returned.

Hot on its heels were two agile figures, a man and a woman, both radiating powerful energy.

When the man and woman saw Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, they were clearly startled. They had carefully searched this area before and found no sign of life, so they were shocked to see two people appear out of nowhere.

If these two were enemies... the pair broke out in a cold sweat.

The little black dog quickly hid behind Yang Kai, concealing itself thoroughly.

Yang Kai stepped forward, cupped his fists, and said, “Greetings, friends.”

The man and woman, though strong, were only about at the Dao Origin Realm level, so Yang Kai wasn’t too concerned. Still, it was rare to meet living people here, so he naturally wanted to gather information.

The pair exchanged glances. The man cupped his fists in return and frowned, “Is that little beast yours?”

He was a Dao Origin Realm cultivator, yet facing Yang Kai, an Emperor Realm master, he showed no fear and even sounded a bit accusatory. Yang Kai found this quite curious. Such things were rare in the Star Realm unless the other party had a significant background, but judging by their attire, they seemed like ordinary martial artists.

New to this place, Yang Kai could only go along with local customs. He smiled and nodded, “That’s right. Ah Wang’s intelligence hasn’t awakened and he’s quite mischievous. If he’s caused any offense, please forgive him.”

The man shook his head and sent his Divine Sense past Yang Kai to carefully examine the little black dog. After a while, he said, “Perhaps I was mistaken. It hasn’t offended us. It’s just a misunderstanding. What’s your name?”

“Yang Kai!” In this foreign land, there was no need to hide his name.

Just then, the woman beside the man quietly sent him a message. The man was momentarily stunned, then looked at Yang Kai in surprise. “You two came from outside?”

If not for his wife’s reminder, he might not have noticed. The clothing of these two was slightly different from their own.

Yang Kai smiled bitterly. “Yes, we came from outside.”

The man nodded. “It’s been a long time since any outsiders came here. Looks like your luck isn’t too good.”

“May I ask what this place is called?” Yang Kai asked solemnly.

The man replied, “This place is called the Revolving World.”

“The Revolving World?” Yang Kai frowned, wanting to ask more, but the man interrupted, “If you want to survive, follow me quickly. There’s not much time.”

“What do you mean, not much time?” Yang Kai was puzzled.

The man didn’t answer. After speaking, he turned and flew off in a certain direction, clearly in a hurry.

This was the first time Yang Kai had met such a headstrong Dao Origin Realm cultivator—one who spoke so boldly before an Emperor Realm master and even acted superior. Still, it was a lead, and he couldn’t just let it go. After exchanging a glance with Zhu Qing, he immediately followed.

The two of them were strong, so they weren’t worried about a mere Dao Origin Realm cultivator playing tricks.

With a flicker, they caught up. The little black dog bit onto Yang Kai’s clothes, stubbornly following and refusing to let go, even as the wind filled its mouth.

Zhu Qing, feeling sorry for it, reached out and scooped it up.

The four of them sped along and soon arrived at a cliff. At first glance, the cliff looked ordinary, but on closer inspection, traces of secret techniques could be seen.

The leading man took out a mirror and sent a mysterious light from it onto the cliff.

Immediately, a cave large enough for two people appeared.

The four entered in single file, passing into a dry tunnel. Only then did the man and woman breathe a sigh of relief, looking as if a great weight had been lifted.

Yang Kai asked curiously, “Why are we in such a hurry?”

The man explained as he led the way, “Soon, the laws of heaven and earth will change. When that happens, demonic creatures will swarm out, and the outside will become extremely dangerous. No one can survive in such an environment.”

“The laws of heaven and earth will change?” Yang Kai was shocked, wondering if he had misheard.

## Chapter 3099 — Ancestral Domain

Inside the ship's hold, the lights of the various Spirit Arrays flashed wildly, a sign that the arrays had been pushed to their limits. Cracking sounds rang out from every direction without end.

"Family Head, we cannot go any deeper. If we go any deeper, I'm afraid..." A sturdy man walked over, panic written across his face. Only now did he understand that this trip was no stroke of fortune at all. They were clearly throwing themselves to their deaths.

Yan Luo said lightly, "Increase speed!"

The sturdy man's expression changed.

Yan Luo raised his hand and brought his palm down. The sturdy man did not make a sound. The crown of his head caved in, and he collapsed limply to the floor, brain matter spilling out. The surrounding Yan Family disciples fell silent as cicadas in winter.

"Increase speed!" Yan Luo shouted again.

How could there still be any hesitation? The many Yan Family disciples hurried into motion, madly pouring their strength into the various Spirit Arrays. After the Warship gave a slight pause, its speed suddenly rose by quite a bit.

There was no way out ahead, only endless brilliant colors. The pressure from the Void was destroying the Warship's structure at every moment. Even though this Warship was a Middle-Level Void King-Grade vessel, it still let out wave after wave of strained groans.

A short while later, a Spirit Array inside the ship's hold suddenly dimmed, having completely collapsed. At the same time, one part of the Warship also split apart. As if some kind of chain reaction had been triggered, the damage rapidly spread from that broken section. Piece after piece of its structure broke away, collapsing bit by bit.

One Spirit Array after another stopped operating.

The many Yan Family disciples were pale as paper. They all raised their heads to look at Yan Luo, only to see his indifferent expression and unruffled composure, as though he did not take this crisis seriously in the slightest. Hope immediately surged in their hearts.

The Family Head would certainly lead them out of this predicament. They were all people carefully selected to follow the Family Head and campaign through the Ancestral Domain. How could they possibly die here for no reason?

After the time it took for a stick of incense to burn, that hope was shattered.

With a tremendous crash, the Warship broke apart.

The power of the Void surged in from every direction. Without the Warship's protection, how could those cultivators whose strength had not yet reached the Void King Realm survive?

Pu pu pu... As if bubbles were being popped one after another, clouds of blood mist burst open in the Void, blooming in vivid crimson.

"Family Head, save us!"

Roaring cries for help came from everywhere, but Yan Luo turned a deaf ear. He moved, and a layer of pitch-black treasure armor covered his body, wrapping him from head to foot, making him look like a general galloping across an ancient battlefield and charging bravely into enemy lines, majestic and awe-inspiring.

At the same time, He Yunxiang also summoned that ribbon Secret Treasure.

After this period of careful refining, although this Lower-Level Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure still could not be controlled as freely as her own arm, it could at least play some role. This ribbon Secret Treasure combined offense and defense, making it extremely rare, and since its grade was not too high, it suited He Yunxiang's current cultivation perfectly.

The ribbon grew larger and wider, wrapping around her body as if she had put on a colorful new garment out of thin air. The pressure on her immediately eased, and she followed closely beside Yang Kai, not daring to stray from him in the slightest.

Yan Luo also ran over and stood shoulder to shoulder with He Yunxiang. He glanced at the ribbon in surprise, and his eyes could not help narrowing.

His eyesight was extraordinary, so he naturally saw that this ribbon was no ordinary item. It was definitely not something the Secret Treasures he had seen before could compare with. He coveted it greatly, but not the slightest trace showed on his face!

I will let you keep it for now. In a little while, this treasure will bear the Yan surname!

By now, the cries for help all around had already died down. From the moment the Warship was damaged until now, only about ten breaths had passed, yet not one of the several hundred Yan Family disciples who had followed along to operate the Warship survived. From beginning to end, Yan Luo had not reacted in the slightest, as if the ones who had died were not members of his Yan Family, but strangers he had never known.

He Yunxiang felt a chill in her heart and thought more and more that this man was ruthless and vicious. She herself had done plenty of killing, yet she absolutely could not be so heartless and devoid of feeling as he was.

In the endless Void, there was no telling left from right or south from north. Everywhere they looked, flowing light spilled with color. Yan Luo and He Yunxiang were both experiencing such a scene for the first time, and they quickly cast aside what had happened before, beginning to take in the wonders around them.

Yang Kai walked at the very front, still without the slightest fluctuation of Spiritual Qi coming from his body. That terrifying pressure bore directly down on his flesh body, yet it could not make him furrow his brow even once.

The entire world was extremely quiet. Each of their heartbeats could be heard clearly.

Yan Luo kept feeling that something was wrong, because the entire journey had been extremely peaceful. They had not encountered any danger or sudden mishaps at all.

Five hundred years ago, the Old Ancestor had set out for the Ancestral Domain. The people of the world did not know whether he had succeeded, but Yan Luo did, because three months after the Old Ancestor departed, the Yan Family used a Secret Technique the Old Ancestor had left behind to contact him. At that time, the Old Ancestor described his experience entering the Ancestral Domain in great detail.

Especially when passing through this multi-colored Void, according to the Old Ancestor, the place was extraordinarily dangerous. The slightest carelessness would end with one's body being smashed to pieces. In this world that looked so beautiful, countless dangers ordinary people could not discover were hidden.

Yan Luo had made every preparation and kept alert at every moment, afraid that some danger would suddenly descend.

But from beginning to end, everything had been calm and tranquil, as safe as strolling through his own back garden. Where was the slightest danger?

The Old Ancestor could not have deceived him.

Then the only explanations were either that the three of them had heaven-defying luck, or that the person leading the way had avoided many dangers in advance, which was why they had not encountered any.

Yan Luo hoped it was the former, because if it were the latter, then this man was far too terrifying. Perhaps... the Yan Family's arrangements had not been the right choice. He suddenly grew somewhat uneasy.

There were no reference points around them either, so no one knew how far they had gone. Yan Luo's treasure armor had already gone dim, clearly having lost much of its spirituality. But he had long since made preparations and immediately changed into another set. He Yunxiang had also consumed a great deal. The ribbon Secret Treasure required Saint Yuan to maintain its defenses, and with her cultivation at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, operating a Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure was strenuous to begin with, so naturally she could not keep it up for long.

With some heartache, she took a Spiritual Pill from her Spatial Ring and swallowed it. Her tender body immediately trembled as she felt a hot current spreading from her lower abdomen and flowing into her limbs and bones. Her nearly exhausted Saint Yuan was greatly replenished in that instant. Moreover, the medicinal efficacy continued without end, and she could not refine and absorb it quickly enough, so much of it was simply wasted.

The things Sir took out really were all treasures! It was absolutely impossible for such outstanding Spiritual Pills to exist in the Star Domain. She did not even know what these Spiritual Pills were called, only that they were used to replenish strength and that Yang Kai had given them to her a few days ago together with those Origin Condensing Pills.

Time passed. No matter how beautiful the scenery was, there would come a time when one grew tired of it. The three were like travelers walking through a lonely desert, with no idea where the road ahead lay. Yan Luo's expectant expression had already turned dispirited, and He Yunxiang was also somewhat anxious. Only Yang Kai's expression remained unchanged, and even his pace did not alter in the slightest.

At a certain moment, Yang Kai suddenly raised his brow, turned to He Yunxiang, and said, "We have arrived!"

He Yunxiang was startled, then immediately overjoyed.

Yan Luo also abruptly raised his head and looked forward. Far in the distance, there seemed to be scenery that was different from the rest, and delight immediately showed on his face. His earlier treasure armor was already gone, and the set he wore now was his last. If they still could not reach the Ancestral Domain, he could only thicken his face and request Yang Kai's protection.

Fortunately, their destination was right before their eyes. This period of persistence had not been in vain after all.

The three of them quickened their steps at the same time, as if by tacit agreement.

Only after a full day did a rotating five-colored vortex appear before the three of them. The vortex had fallen into an extremely strange state of stillness. Standing before it, the three of them were as tiny and insignificant as specks of dust.

Yan Luo's lips trembled with excitement. He fixed his gaze on the vortex, as if he could pierce through the barrier of space and see the scenery on the other side.

A strange light also rose in He Yunxiang's eyes. Three months ago, she would never have dared imagine that such a day would come for her. The Ancestral Domain seemed as if it could be reached with just a single step. This was the dream countless people chased for a lifetime, yet now it was within easy reach.

Both of them turned to look at Yang Kai and were stunned to discover that he had actually closed his eyes and was standing there quietly.

No one dared disturb him.

After waiting for quite a while, Yang Kai opened his eyes and said, "Let's go."

He took a single step and directly entered the still five-colored vortex.

Not to be outdone, Yan Luo and He Yunxiang followed close behind. A sense of weightlessness instantly enveloped them.

Dizzy and dazed, their minds shaken, they suddenly heard waves of noisy clamor beside their ears. He Yunxiang and Yan Luo tried hard to listen, but their ears buzzed, and they could not make out what those voices were saying.

Only Yang Kai looked around with a speechless expression.

The Ancestral Domain was a mysterious place. On the way here, he had imagined many things, wondering just what kind of place it was. But after truly arriving, he discovered that it was somewhat different from what he had pictured.

It was not exactly disappointment, but why did the scene before his eyes feel... somehow wrong?

"They're here, they're here! More people have come."

"Oh? Three came at once this time. Which Star Domain are they from?"

“Of these three, my Tower Beyond Heavens wants one.”

“Liu, why don’t you go fuck yourself? You think you can take one just because you say so? First ask whether this Brother agrees!”

"What if you agree, and what if you do not? Do you want to fight me?"

"Come on then, who's afraid of whom!"

"No matter what you lot think, my Star Luo Mountain wants one too."

"Then my Sacred Mountain Peak will take the one left over."

"Are you joking?"

"I am perfectly serious."

"Fuck your mother!"

"Why are you cursing people?"

It seemed that because Yang Kai and the other two had appeared, the situation had suddenly spun somewhat out of control, leaving Yang Kai dumbstruck.

"All of you, shut up. What kind of conduct is this, shouting and arguing? Do not let them look down on us. Do you not know the rules we have always followed? First ask clearly where they come from, then talk." An elderly man with a sallow face suddenly spoke. This old man seemed to have some prestige. His figure was thin and dry, but his spirit was hale, and he held a long-stemmed smoking pipe in his hand, a few sparks glowing in its bowl. As soon as he spoke, the scene quieted down at once, and everyone looked eagerly toward Yang Kai and the others.

## Chapter 2984 — Li Jiao Enters a Demonic State

The World Laws of the Revolving World shifted in a constant cycle, sometimes leaning toward the Star Realm and sometimes toward the Demon Domain, without the slightest discernible pattern. The reason was simply that the Revolving World lay in the gap between these two Great Worlds. As it drifted through the Void Crack, it would occasionally intersect briefly with one of them, and whichever Great World it intersected with would influence its World Laws.

Generally speaking, however, extremely obvious celestial phenomena would appear before the World Laws changed. The Human Race in Human Sovereign City relied on these signs to avoid danger.

With the Spirit Pills Yang Kai distributed, the many Demon Kings did indeed recover much faster, but it was still somewhat insufficient to satisfy the little black dog's appetite.

Yang Kai could only pin his hopes on the Revolving World's next transformation.

This wait lasted half a month.

During that half month, nearly every Demon King and Demon Commander in Demon Fury City took almost ten turns feeding the little black dog, endlessly meditating to recover before urging their Demon Yuan for it to devour.

No expert could withstand such torment, not even Yang Kai. After half a month, all the Demon Kings and Demon Commanders looked lifeless and ashen, as though their lifespans were nearly exhausted and they might suddenly drop dead at any moment. A few Demon Kings and Demon Commanders had even suffered significant regressions in their cultivation, and there was no telling how many years it would take them to recover.

This was simply no way to live. They would have fled long ago if they could, but with the exotic beast Guixu here, two members of the Dragon Clan standing guard, and Yang Kai watching them like a tiger eyeing its prey, what chance did they have to escape?

They, too, desperately longed for the Demon Domain's Laws to descend.

Half a month later, the world finally began to change. A series of muffled, thunderous rumbles rolled across the sky, bringing tears of excitement to the eyes of the Demon Race clansmen who had waited bitterly for so many days. They immediately began cheering.

After the rumbling, the surrounding Spiritual Qi seemed to undergo a subtle transformation.

Li Jiao came to Yang Kai with a worried expression and pleaded miserably, "Palace Master Yang, save me!"

Since arriving here, he had asked Yang Kai to save him more than once.

"What is it this time?"

Li Jiao said, "This Li has already been corroded by Demonic Qi, and Demonic Intent has constantly plagued me these past few days. I have barely managed to endure until now. The World Laws are about to change, so if I do not find somewhere to hide, I fear I will truly enter a demonic state this time."

The last time he and Zhu Lie were stranded outside, they had failed to find shelter when the World Laws changed, allowing Demonic Qi to invade his body. Ever since then, he had relied on his powerful cultivation to suppress the Demonic Intent within him. Now that the World Laws were about to change again, the entire world would likely be flooded with Demonic Qi once more.

In his current state, he had no hope of enduring it. When the time came, he would certainly be completely consumed by his Demonic Intent and reduced to a Demon.

"Then find somewhere to hide. Why are you asking me?" Yang Kai asked with a chuckle.

Li Jiao said bitterly, "But I do not know where to go. Palace Master Yang mentioned before that there was a place called Human Sovereign City here. Perhaps..."

"Forget about Human Sovereign City. Although it isn't too far from here, you no longer have enough time to reach it."

Hearing this, Li Jiao immediately panicked. "Then what should I do?"

Yang Kai shrugged and spread his hands. "Pray for good fortune."

Li Jiao's face darkened, and he nearly burst into tears. "Palace Master Yang, you cannot just stand by and watch me die! This Li only came here because of you."

Yang Kai replied, "Naturally, I would save you if I could, but there is nothing I can do right now."

Li Jiao's expression became increasingly ugly. Among everyone present, the two members of the Dragon Clan should not need to fear being corroded by Demonic Qi. They were Divine Spirits, after all, and their Dragon Blood possessed a divine nature that gave them an extremely strong resistance to Demonic Qi. Forget a change in the World Laws; even if they were truly thrown into the Demon Domain, they were unlikely to be demonized.

As for Yang Kai, he could not be judged by ordinary standards. Although Li Jiao did not know what gave him the confidence to guarantee his own safety, Yang Kai's relaxed demeanor made it obvious that he had a way to protect himself.

Li Jiao grew somewhat angry, feeling that Yang Kai was selfishly hoarding his secret method and refusing to lend him a hand.

"Didn't I already point out a path for you? What can I do if you refuse to take it?"

Li Jiao said in a low voice, "Palace Master Yang, please stop joking. Only you could think of asking the Dragon Clan for their blood. If this Li truly made such a request, I fear my end would be miserable."

"You're going to enter a demonic state anyway. Could anything be worse than that?"

"Palace Master Yang, do you truly intend to watch me die?" A baleful aura emerged between Li Jiao's brows as black Qi roiled around his body. Anger and evil thoughts welled up in his heart, causing his demonic nature to suddenly intensify. In particular, the surrounding Spiritual Qi had already begun transforming into Demonic Qi, further affecting his temperament.

Yang Kai stared at him with a sneer. "Why don't you bite me?"

"Damn you!" Li Jiao finally lost control. His face twisted savagely as he roared through gritted teeth, "If not for you, how could this Li have fallen into such a state? Since you are so cold-blooded and heartless, do not blame this Li for showing no mercy! I'll fight you to the death!"

As soon as he finished speaking, black Qi churned around his body and his eyes turned scarlet. He shook his head violently, which abruptly transformed into an enormous, ferocious Jiao head that snapped down toward Yang Kai.

He had already been corroded by Demonic Qi, suffered endless frustration over these past few days, and was now being provoked by Yang Kai. At last, he could no longer maintain his true nature. The Demonic Intent he had suppressed erupted in full, causing him to enter a demonic state in an instant.

"A mere little Jiao dares act presumptuously before me? Li Jiao, you have quite the nerve."

Facing Li Jiao’s furious attack, Yang Kai merely chuckled. Activating the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power within his body, he raised a hand and pointed toward Li Jiao’s forehead.

A speck of golden light bloomed at his fingertip. Although it was not particularly dazzling, Li Jiao reacted as though facing a mortal enemy. With a startled cry, his enormous Jiao head shrank back to its original size, while the Emperor Yuan in his body suddenly became sluggish, nearly causing him to choke on his own breath.

Yang Kai's fingertip touched Li Jiao's forehead, causing his whole body to tremble as a trace of clarity returned to his eyes.

After coming to his senses, Li Jiao paled in shock. He had never imagined he would do such a thing and actually attack Yang Kai. He knew his actions had been controlled by his demonic nature and were not his true intentions, but he feared Yang Kai would not understand and would instead kill him without mercy.

His cultivation was indeed two Stages higher than Yang Kai's, but under the suppression of bloodline power, that difference gave him no advantage whatsoever. If Yang Kai wanted to kill him, it would be as easy as crushing a snail between his fingers; Li Jiao would have no chance to resist.

This was the tragedy of possessing a Dragon Clan descendant's bloodline. His Dragon Clan bloodline had indeed allowed him to travel farther and stand higher than others, as proven by his current cultivation at the Third-Stage Emperor Realm. Without the Dragon Clan's bloodline, his aptitude could never have brought him to such heights.

However, when facing an opponent who also possessed a Dragon Clan bloodline, cultivation often became secondary, while the power of one's bloodline was the key.

Cold sweat broke out across his back, and he hurriedly begged for mercy. "Palace Master Yang, I was wrong. Please show mercy!"

Yang Kai continued to chuckle, completely ignoring him as though he truly intended to kill him without mercy.

Li Jiao's face turned ashen as he thought that he was truly finished this time. What a pity for his Li Dragon Palace and all the beautiful concubines in his chambers. There was no telling who would enjoy them in the future.

These distracting thoughts flashed through his mind, yet death never came. Yang Kai's finger remained pressed against his forehead without the slightest murderous intent, and Yang Kai merely stared at him teasingly.

Suddenly, Li Jiao sensed an enormous change in the power within Yang Kai's body. The aura spreading from him carried an ancient and timeworn quality, as though it had crossed the ages from antiquity.

A deep, solemn incantation rang out as a beam of light blossomed from Yang Kai's fingertip and enveloped Li Jiao.

In the next instant, Li Jiao's body shook and his bones crackled. His already tall frame abruptly swelled by another measure, while fierce, surging Blood Qi poured from his body and assailed the Demonic Qi surrounding him.

Li Jiao stood rooted to the spot. Although he did not know what method Yang Kai had used on him, he could clearly feel that his strength had increased considerably. An inexplicable bloodthirsty and warlike impulse also rose within him. Though this impulse made him somewhat uncomfortable, it diluted the influence of his Demonic Intent, allowing him to maintain a strange state of lucidity.

From a distance, Li Jiao now seemed filled with two colors: the pitch-black Demonic Qi and the crimson Blood Qi erupting from his body. Red and black intertwined, churning and writhing in an extremely bizarre sight.

"This is..." Li Jiao asked in surprise. By now, he knew Yang Kai bore him no murderous intent and was most likely helping him. He simply could not understand why Yang Kai had not acted sooner if he possessed such an ability. Why had he toyed with him instead?

"The Ancient Bloodlust Art!" Yang Kai smiled slightly. "It is highly effective at restraining Demonic Qi that has invaded a human's body. The Great Shamans of ancient times used this method to save their clansmen. However, today's World Laws are somewhat different from those of that era. It will not be difficult to expel the Demonic Qi from your body, but eradicating your demonic nature will require more effort. It was best to make you release all of it first so the problem could be resolved once and for all. Otherwise, hidden dangers might remain. You would not want to suffer Qi Deviation for no apparent reason while cultivating in the future, would you?"

Li Jiao looked at Yang Kai with deep gratitude. "So that is why Palace Master Yang has ignored me all these days? You deliberately kept me in suspense and provoked me?"

Otherwise, his demonic nature would not have erupted so quickly.

Yang Kai said, "Since we came in together, naturally we must leave together. Besides, your Li Dragon Palace still owes me a great many Source Crystals, so I certainly cannot let you die so easily."

"Palace Master Yang's great kindness will remain engraved in this Li's heart!" Li Jiao was beside himself with excitement. What he had believed to be certain death had unexpectedly taken a turn for the better. Not only did he now have hope of surviving, but he would also be spared any future worries.

"Do not celebrate too soon. As I said, today's World Laws are different from those of ancient times, and the people living under these different Laws are not the same either. This method worked on ancient humans, but I do not know how effective it will be on you. You must be especially careful when cultivating in the future."

"Yes, yes, this Li will certainly be careful!" Li Jiao nodded repeatedly.

Yang Kai's expression suddenly became solemn again, and the incantation he chanted changed. He raised his hand and struck his palm toward Li Jiao, shouting in a low voice, "Demon Suppression!"

Under this palm strike, the Demonic Qi churning around Li Jiao seemed to be suppressed by some unseen force and madly withdrew into his body.

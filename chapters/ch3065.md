## Chapter 3065 — Hidden Pawn

Although Yang Kai continued to resist and evade, two fists could not contend with four hands. He looked utterly wretched, as though any one of the seven Dragons might land a fatal blow at any moment and kill him on the spot.

Zhu Qing roared, spewing out a sea of raging flames that swept toward the seven Dragons.

The Dragons were shocked and turned to look along with Yang Kai. The same vexed thought sprang into all their minds at once: Why had Zhu Qing come here?

That blast of flame relieved Yang Kai's immediate crisis. Zhu Qing instantly flew toward him, clearly intending to fight by his side and help shoulder the pressure.

The seven Dragons merely exchanged glances before arriving at a plan.

Two Dragons broke away from the group and flew out, twisting their heads and tails as they blocked Zhu Qing's path. The three Dragons immediately entered a standoff, their Dragon Pressure surging forward and colliding, making the void tremble and the world itself shatter.

"Get out of my way!" Zhu Qing growled.

The two Dragons confronting her naturally could not back down. One of them said solemnly, "Zhu Qing, do not bring ruin upon yourself! You now possess a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline, and barring any unforeseen circumstances, you will become the Fifth Elder of our Dragon Clan. Please consider the greater good and put the Dragon Clan first!"

Zhu Qing replied coldly, "I am only a woman. I know nothing of the greater good. All I know is that you are bullying my man!"

That Dragon flew into a rage. "Mind your words! He is nothing but a despicable human! Do not bring shame upon my Dragon Clan!"

The other Dragon also said gravely, "Zhu Qing, have you forgotten what happened more than ten years ago? That woman was no worse than you, and you know how she ended up. Do you wish to follow in her footsteps?"

Zhu Qing said quietly, "More than ten years ago, I could not understand why she was so stubborn, but today I do. If I could make that choice again, I would have helped her back then instead of standing against her."

"You are beyond reason!"

"You will be exiled to the Dragon Tomb! Zhu Qing, stop now."

"You are the ones who should stop!"

As they spoke, Fu Ling finally arrived. The Purple Dragon, more than ten zhang long, had a graceful form and an impressive bearing, but the instant she appeared, two voices berated her at once: "Useless!"

One naturally came from the Dragon Clan. The seven Dragons could not understand how Fu Ling and the Blue Dragon had failed to stop Zhu Qing despite working together. Judging by the current situation, the Blue Dragon even seemed to have been incapacitated; otherwise, Fu Ling would not have been the only one to pursue Zhu Qing.

Logically speaking, with the Dragon Temple's Protection Technique bolstering them, Fu Ling and the Blue Dragon should have been able to hold Zhu Qing back. In reality, however, they had delayed her for only several dozen breaths, forcing the others to divert two Dragons to confront her.

The other rebuke naturally came from Yang Kai.

He had not expected Fu Ling to fail at such a simple task. If she had clearly conveyed his plan and gotten Zhu Qing to help the others defeat their opponents one by one first, Yang Kai's side would have held an overwhelming advantage today.

Yet Zhu Qing had come straight here, clearly showing that Fu Ling had failed to complete her task.

This left him quite displeased with Fu Ling.

Fu Ling also looked somewhat embarrassed. She stopped in midair, seemingly at a loss for what to do.

"Why aren't you helping yet?" one of the Dragons confronting Zhu Qing shouted at her.

Hearing this, Fu Ling could only head toward Yang Kai. The five remaining Dragons snorted when they saw her approach. They had originally held an overwhelming advantage with seven against one. Although Zhu Qing's sudden appearance had forced them to divert two of their number, they had gained Fu Ling in return, so their overall strength had not changed by much.

By now, they had essentially figured out the full extent of Yang Kai's abilities and knew he had no hope of victory today. They only needed to draw out the fight, gradually wearing down his ferocity and strength until he could no longer use that elusive Space Divine Ability. Then they could capture or even kill him.

At this thought, the Dragons' gazes toward Yang Kai filled with mockery and scorn.

Who knew what madness had possessed this man to make him come to Dragon Isle and cause trouble?

What kind of place was Dragon Isle? How could anyone who dared cause trouble here possibly meet a good end?

"Choose one."

Just as the Dragons were looking down on him, Yang Kai suddenly turned toward Fu Ling and uttered those baffling words.

The Dragons were bewildered and wary. Choose one? Choose what?

A trace of struggle and hesitation flashed through Fu Ling's eyes. Yang Kai immediately snorted and said ominously, "Do not think I won't take your life!"

The Purple Dragon's body, more than ten zhang long, trembled slightly. She hesitated no longer, opening her mouth to spit out a beam of purple light that blasted toward one of the Dragon Clan members.

That Dragon was roughly the same size as Fu Ling in her True Dragon form, and his strength and bloodline rank should also have been similar to hers. Both were among the weakest members of the Dragon Clan. His attention had been fixed on Yang Kai, so he never expected Fu Ling to attack him without warning. Caught completely off guard, he was struck head-on by the purple light and sent tumbling through the air as furious roars burst from his mouth.

"Fu Ling, have you lost your mind?" one of the Dragons roared.

The scene before them was far too sudden. Fu Ling was a member of the Dragon Clan, yet she had actually helped Yang Kai attack another member of the Dragon Clan. It was utterly incomprehensible. Zhu Qing and her younger brother Zhu Lie were one thing, but why had Fu Ling gone mad along with them?

Fu Ling knew she was in the wrong and had no way to explain herself. Her enormous body had already transformed into a streak of purple light and lunged toward her chosen target.

Her opponent finally recovered from his shock and fury. Turning around, he charged to meet Fu Ling. The two Dragons instantly became locked in battle. Dragon roars shook the heavens and Secret Arts blossomed as their tumbling bodies plunged into the sea, sending monstrous waves soaring into the sky.

"What did you do to Fu Ling?" Not all of them were fools. One Dragon's eyes flickered a few times as he seemingly realized something, then he turned and glared viciously at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai chuckled and wiped the blood from his face, revealing a savage expression. "Guess!"

That Dragon's face immediately sank. He could feel the situation slipping further and further out of control.

Originally, with seven Dragons against Yang Kai alone, no matter how capable he was, there was no chance he could turn the situation around. Victory had only been a matter of time. But then Zhu Qing arrived and drew away two of their clansmen, while Fu Ling's betrayal occupied another.

In an instant, seven Dragons had become four, nearly halving their number and filling them with unease. They had experienced Yang Kai's elusive Space Divine Ability firsthand. Earlier, they had relied on their overwhelming advantage in numbers to surround and intercept him, and only then had they managed to avoid any serious mishaps. With only four Dragons left, their encirclement could not possibly remain as flawless as before.

If Yang Kai truly found an opportunity to break the Dragon Temple's Protection Technique, the Dragon Clan might suffer a crushing defeat in today's battle.

Such humiliation was unacceptable to every member of the Dragon Clan.

Now wary and cautious, the four Dragons no longer dared attack rashly. Their gazes met, clearly communicating something among themselves.

Yang Kai gave them no such opportunity. His eight-zhang Dragon body charged straight at them with an unrivalled, domineering momentum beneath the four Dragons' apprehensive gazes.

The great battle erupted anew.

On an uninhabited island, Fu Chi sat cross-legged in a place rich with Spiritual Qi. His face was deathly pale as he silently circulated a profound Secret Art.

The arms Yang Kai had torn off had already been reattached, but even with the Dragon Clan's tremendous vitality and recuperative powers, recovering from such injuries would not be easy. It would take six months to a year at minimum for him to return to his peak.

His face was twisted, whether from pain or something else. As he tended his wounds, he gritted his teeth and cursed, "Bitch! Bitch, bitch!"

The Dragon Clan had indeed been humiliated by today's events, but the humiliation Fu Chi had personally suffered was thousands, even tens of thousands of times greater than that of the entire clan.

His bride had been stolen on his wedding day, and the woman he was supposed to marry had even subdued him herself. That despicable human had then torn off both his arms. Had the Great Elder not stepped forward to protect him at the final moment, Fu Chi likely would have died today.

Given that man's decisiveness and ruthlessness, he was absolutely capable of it.

This humiliation weighed upon his chest like a mountain, leaving him barely able to breathe.

He could easily imagine that after today, his reputation would be utterly ruined. He would likely be too ashamed to face his clansmen again. Whenever Fu Chi imagined the expressions they might wear when they saw him, an oppressive tightness filled his chest.

As his Qi and blood churned, he suddenly opened his mouth and spat out a mouthful of Dragon Blood, staining the ground before him red.

The figures of a man and a woman repeatedly flashed through his mind. Fu Chi hated Yang Kai and Zhu Qing to the bone and secretly swore that one day, he would repay today's vengeance a hundredfold; otherwise, the rage in his heart could never be quelled. He hated Zhu Qing in particular, even more than he hated Yang Kai.

Just as he was thinking this, Fu Chi suddenly seemed to notice something. He opened his eyes and looked ahead, his gaze immediately turning fierce.

A figure was standing quietly in the void not far ahead. No, it was not a human, but a Dragon Descendant!

Fu Chi could sense the chaotic aura of impure Dragon Blood emanating from him. He was all too familiar with it; every mongrel in Half-Dragon City carried this same nauseating aura.

Every member of the Dragon Clan detested this aura, their rejection of it almost instinctive, and Fu Chi was naturally no exception.

"Scram!" Fu Chi shouted, his expression cold and severe.

He had already suffered immense humiliation and fled to this island to tend his wounds alone, yet now a Dragon Descendant had actually followed him here and stood nearby watching him.

A despicable human had humiliated him, and now even this mongrel intended to humiliate him as well?

Ordinarily, Fu Chi would have attacked and disposed of him long ago, but he was currently in the middle of healing and had no desire to waste words on this mongrel.

He had assumed that one furious roar would scare the mongrel before him shitless and send him fleeing in disgrace. In truth, no mongrel from Half-Dragon City could summon the will to resist when facing a pure-blooded member of the Dragon Clan. It had nothing to do with strength, but stemmed from an inferiority rooted deep within their souls.

Dragon Bloodlines were divided by strict ranks, and the difference between a pure Dragon Bloodline and a half-Dragon bloodline was like the gulf between Heaven and Earth.

To Fu Chi's immense surprise, the mongrel opposite him showed no reaction whatsoever to his roar. He continued to gaze at Fu Chi in silence, his expression as tranquil as an ancient well.

Fu Chi flew into a complete rage. The fury pent up in his chest had finally found an outlet, and he said in a sinister voice, "You are courting death!"

As he spoke, his Dragon Pressure spread out and bore down upon Li Jiao from above.

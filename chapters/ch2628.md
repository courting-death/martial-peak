## Chapter 2628 — The Recruitment Order

“Yang Kai?” Shi Tianhe was momentarily stunned, then exclaimed in shock, “Senior, you are that...?”

Yang Kai smiled and said, “That’s right, I am the one Wenqing Sect has been searching for.”

Hearing Yang Kai admit it himself, Shi Tianhe could only give a bitter smile.

When she had heard those rumors before, she had secretly criticized Yang Kai for being unkind. After all, the reason Ice Heart Valley suffered such a calamity was fundamentally because of Yang Kai—because he killed Feng Xi in the Shattered Star Sea, which enraged Feng Xuan, leading to a series of conflicts and the current chaos in the Northern Territory.

Shi Tianhe had never imagined that one day, the very culprit who threw the Northern Territory into turmoil would appear alive before her eyes.

As she thought about it, Shi Tianhe was startled again.

She suddenly remembered that Feng Xi was killed in the Shattered Star Sea several years ago, which meant that Yang Kai was also in the Shattered Star Sea back then. But only cultivators below the Emperor Realm could enter the Shattered Star Sea.

Didn’t that mean Yang Kai was only in the Dao Origin Realm a few years ago? Even now, at most, he should only be at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

Yet, even though he was at the same First Stage of the Emperor Realm, the elders of Thunderclap Pavilion and even the City Lord of Taiping City were no match for him at all.

The cultivation Yang Kai had displayed earlier was indeed his true strength; he hadn’t concealed it in the slightest!

If she hadn’t witnessed it herself, Shi Tianhe would never have believed it. Since when did First Stage Emperors become so easy to kill?

She had once fantasized that if she ever found this Yang Kai, she would capture him and hand him over to Wenqing Sect to resolve her sect’s crisis. But now, how could she even dare to think of such a thing?

“Bai Yu is finished!” At that moment, Yang Kai suddenly spoke.

Shi Tianhe quickly looked up and saw a blinding light burst forth in the distance. An icy chill swept across the world, and as a sword light flashed, the battle there finally came to an end.

As the light faded, Ji Yao stood motionless, gently flicking her long sword, her expression calm and unruffled.

In front of her, at some point, a block of ice had appeared. Inside that block, the Pavilion Master of Thunderclap Pavilion, Bai Yu, was frozen solid.

Trapped within the ice, Bai Yu’s face was filled with terror and disbelief, as if he still couldn’t accept that he had lost so utterly, without the slightest ability to resist.

Swoosh—

The block of ice plummeted to the ground and shattered with a thunderous crash.

Bai Yu, who had been sealed inside, was torn to pieces, not a single part of him left intact.

Just as Ji Yao had said before, she truly had cut him to pieces.

With a flash, Ji Yao appeared at Yang Kai’s side. She glanced around, not bothering to ask where the other four enemies had gone—the blood and flesh scattered everywhere was answer enough. She nodded to Yang Kai and said, “Master, let’s hurry back to the Valley!”

Before, she hadn’t known the situation in Ice Heart Valley, but now, after hearing from Shi Tianhe that the Valley was under siege, Ji Yao was anxious to return, longing to share hardship and joy with her sisters.

“Mm!” Yang Kai nodded and gave Shi Tianhe a meaningful look as he spoke.

Shi Tianhe was quick-witted. She immediately turned and flew off, retrieving Bai Yu’s Spatial Ring from his remains.

Ji Yao then summoned her Multi-deck Ship. The three of them boarded in turn, and as Ji Yao formed a seal with her hands, the ship shot forward like the wind.

“Senior Yang, this is for you.” Shi Tianhe handed over the Spatial Ring, still encased in ice and attached to a severed finger.

Yang Kai took it, urged his Emperor Yuan to melt the ice, tossed away the finger, and then handed the ring back to Shi Tianhe.

“Senior Yang, what are you...?” Shi Tianhe looked at Yang Kai in confusion.

“This is your Third Senior Aunt’s spoils of war. Since she doesn’t care for it, you take it. When you return to Ice Heart Valley, cultivate well and don’t let your master down again.” Yang Kai smiled.

Shi Tianhe said gratefully, “Thank you, Senior Yang. Thank you, Third Senior Aunt...”

“What do you mean, Senior Yang!” Ji Yao glared at her. “I told you, this is your Ancestor!”

“Yes, thank you, Ancestor.” Shi Tianhe’s expression changed as she quickly corrected herself, not knowing how to explain to Ji Yao. She snuck a glance at Yang Kai and saw that he looked just as helpless.

...

Two days later, three thousand li from Ice Heart Valley, atop a snowy mountain.

Snowflakes drifted down, the world was bitterly cold, and Yang Kai and the others stood at the summit, gazing into the distance.

Ji Yao trembled with excitement, unable to suppress her emotions. Looking at the familiar scenery, breathing the familiar air, and feeling the heavy snow melt on her body, she felt as if she had been reborn. The joy in her heart was impossible to contain.

Perhaps because she was so close to home, Ji Yao’s eyes also held a trace of timidity and worry.

“There are a lot of people.” Yang Kai exhaled, white mist billowing before him.

From here, they could clearly see martial artists patrolling outside Ice Heart Valley. These were obviously not disciples of the Valley. Groups of three, five, or even a dozen or more gathered at the main entrances, guarding the way in and out.

The entire Ice Heart Valley seemed to be surrounded as tightly as an iron barrel.

“How does Wenqing Sect have so many people?” Yang Kai frowned.

From what he had learned from Shi Tianhe, not only was Ice Heart Valley under siege, but even the nearby Binglun City had been seized.

Binglun City was a crucial hub for Ice Heart Valley’s external communications. With it taken, their connection to the outside world was cut off.

Wenqing Sect needed to besiege Ice Heart Valley and occupy Binglun City—how could they have so many people?

Shi Tianhe said, “There are many sects and families in the Northern Territory that are subordinate to Wenqing Sect. With such a big commotion, those subordinate forces are sure to help.”

“Even so, there still shouldn’t be this many people.” Yang Kai slowly shook his head, then said, “Both Ice Heart Valley and Wenqing Sect are major powers. Wenqing Sect has subordinate families and sects, but doesn’t Ice Heart Valley as well? As far as I know, many female disciples from Ice Heart Valley have married out. With those connections, now that the Valley is in crisis, shouldn’t those forces come to help?”

Shi Tianhe could only give a bitter smile, not knowing how to answer.

Ji Yao said, “Master, didn’t you set a rule when you founded Ice Heart Valley? The Valley doesn’t forbid marriage, but once a disciple marries out, she has no further ties to the Valley. You were worried that people would think Ice Heart Valley was expanding its power through marriage alliances and would attract trouble.”

Yang Kai was surprised. “There was such a rule?”

Ji Yao pouted, “You set the rule yourself...”

Yang Kai gave a dry laugh. “Your master was muddle-headed.”

“Who’s sneaking around here?!”

As the three of them were observing, a sudden shout rang out.

Immediately after, the sound of wind splitting the air approached, and in an instant, a group of five appeared before Yang Kai and the others.

Of the five, the leader was a middle-aged man, while the other four looked to be under forty. There were no women, only men.

With a sweep of his Divine Sense, Yang Kai saw that all five were in the Dao Origin Realm. The leader was at the Second Stage, while the other four were only at the First Stage.

“And who are you?” Yang Kai asked with a sidelong glance, releasing a bit of pressure as he spoke, enveloping the five.

Their faces changed instantly. The leader, especially, was terrified and cupped his fists, “I am Fang Minghui. Greetings, my lord. If we have offended, please forgive us.”

Realizing Yang Kai was in the Emperor Realm, Fang Minghui dared not be disrespectful. He immediately lowered his posture, afraid of angering Yang Kai.

“What are you doing here?” Yang Kai asked coldly.

Fang Minghui quickly replied, “We are on patrol by order.” As he spoke, he gave Yang Kai a fawning smile.

“On patrol? Are you disciples of Wenqing Sect?” Yang Kai frowned.

Fang Minghui shook his head. “No, we are from the Fang Family of Shuangyu City. These behind me are all Fang Family disciples.”

“Shuangyu City’s Fang Family...” Yang Kai said in realization. “A subordinate force, huh.”

“You flatter us, my lord,” Fang Minghui said awkwardly. “Our Fang Family is just a minor clan. We could never hope to be attached to a great tree like Wenqing Sect. Even all of Shuangyu City is beneath their notice.”

Yang Kai asked curiously, “If you’re not a subordinate of Wenqing Sect, why are you here? Whose orders are you following to patrol?”

This place was so close to Ice Heart Valley, and with Wenqing Sect and the Valley at odds, a battle could break out at any moment. Anyone unrelated would surely have left by now.

Fang Minghui was taken aback. “Of course, we’re acting on Wenqing Sect’s orders.”

Yang Kai snorted, “You’re talking nonsense. This Lord is confused by your words. If you don’t make yourself clear, I’ll rip out your tongue!”

Fang Minghui was terrified. “Please, Senior, calm your anger! I truly am acting on Wenqing Sect’s orders. And my Fang Family really has no connection to them. I’m not lying!”

The four behind him turned pale as well, thinking to themselves that Emperor Realm cultivators were truly not to be provoked. If he was in a bad mood, he’d rip out someone’s tongue. If only they could reach the Emperor Realm, they could do the same for fun...

“If you have no connection, why do you obey their orders? Do you take this Lord for a fool?” Yang Kai looked at Fang Minghui with a cold expression.

Fang Minghui wiped the cold sweat from his forehead. “My lord, there are many like my Fang Family near Ice Heart Valley now...”

As he spoke, he suddenly seemed to realize something and looked at Yang Kai timidly. “My lord, haven’t you heard of Wenqing Sect’s Recruitment Order?”

“What Recruitment Order?” Yang Kai asked with a frown.

Fang Minghui let out a breath. “So my lord really hasn’t heard.”

“Explain in detail. If you do well, I won’t rip out your tongue.”

“Yes, yes...” Fang Minghui nodded repeatedly and immediately explained, “A few years ago, Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley started fighting. Both are top sects in the Northern Territory, and their strength is evenly matched, so both sides suffered losses. Later, to avoid further losses, Wenqing Sect issued a Recruitment Order, informing all cultivators in the Northern Territory that anyone who temporarily joined them and followed orders to help deal with Ice Heart Valley would be rewarded with Source Crystals, Secret Treasures, and cultivation techniques. Depending on their contributions, they could even become a subordinate force of Wenqing Sect in the future. What’s more, after Ice Heart Valley is destroyed, they could divide up the female disciples of the Valley.”

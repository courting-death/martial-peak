## Chapter 2656 — Gone Without a Trace

Among the countless Great Daos, attainment determines seniority.

Nanmen Dajun mistakenly believed Yang Kai to be some extraordinary hidden expert, and he truly admired him from the bottom of his heart.

“Master Yang, you hid your abilities so well. I was quite disrespectful before, I hope you can forgive me!” Nanmen Dajun gave a bitter smile, utterly convinced.

Yang Kai’s face reddened slightly, but he couldn’t be bothered to explain much. He simply said, “This Space Array was indeed arranged by me, but it’s not as you imagine.”

“Master Yang, you’re too modest. It was I who made a fool of myself earlier.”

Yang Kai shook his head, ignored him, and turned to Bing Yun, “Senior, what are the casualties?”

Bing Yun sighed softly, “Over a thousand disciples died, more than half are injured. The losses are not small, but it’s already a fortunate outcome. It’s all thanks to you and those three Yao Kings.”

Although Yang Kai hadn’t participated much in the fighting, the Soul-Devouring Demonic Worms he released made great contributions. Without them, the casualties in Ice Heart Valley would have been several times higher. After all, the enemy’s numbers were simply too great. Relying on Ice Heart Valley’s Emperors and the three Yao Kings alone, they simply couldn’t kill them all.

But with the Soul-Devouring Demonic Worms unleashed, wherever the insect cloud passed, people from Wenqing Sect fell in droves. It was truly a weapon of mass slaughter.

“What about Wenqing Sect?” Yang Kai asked again.

“Out of one hundred thousand, half died, twenty or thirty thousand escaped, and a few surrendered.”

Yang Kai nodded at these words; this result was within his expectations.

From this day forward, Wenqing Sect—once a mighty force—had truly fallen. From the Sect Master down, nearly all their Emperor Realm experts had perished. Even if a few strong cultivators remained behind, they could no longer amount to anything.

“Senior, what are your plans next?”

Bing Yun said, “First, settle the wounded disciples, and the Grand Guardian Array needs repairs. As for those who surrendered... that’s a bit troublesome.”

There were twenty or thirty thousand surrendered cultivators. Killing them all would cause trouble, but letting them go would make Ice Heart Valley seem easy to bully. That’s why Bing Yun was so hesitant.

After all, only a small portion of those people were from Wenqing Sect; the rest had been recruited recently and had no deep grudge against Ice Heart Valley.

Yang Kai snorted coldly, “They can be spared from death, but not from punishment. Letting them go is fine, but they must suffer a bit first.”

Bing Yun nodded, “That’s what I was thinking too.”

As the two spoke, Nanmen Dajun suddenly said, “Senior Bing Yun, do you want to repair the Grand Guardian Array? I can lend a hand.”

Bing Yun smiled, “That’s exactly what I hoped for, but I was afraid Grandmaster Nanmen wouldn’t be willing.”

“Willing, willing! How could I not be!” Nanmen Dajun’s face lit up with joy. As he spoke, he glanced at the Space Array over there.

Yang Kai naturally knew what he was thinking and said, “As long as you can repair Ice Heart Valley’s Grand Guardian Array, you can study this cross-territory Space Array however you like.”

After all, this thing was arranged with his own space abilities. No matter how thoroughly Nanmen Dajun studied it, he couldn’t replicate it. Yang Kai wasn’t afraid of him learning the secret.

Nanmen Dajun’s eyes lit up at this, and he quickly cupped his fists, “Many thanks, Master Yang!”

He truly regarded Yang Kai as an even greater array master than himself.

“Senior, I need to make a trip to Wenqing Sect!” Yang Kai’s expression suddenly turned stern, a faint killing intent swirling around him.

Bing Yun understood at once and nodded, “Then let Yao’er accompany you.”

Yang Kai glanced at Ji Yao, who stood behind Bing Yun, and nodded in agreement.

Without delay, he took Ji Yao and flew out, leaving the Ice Lake isle. He called over Xi Lei and the other two Yao Kings, and together they left Ice Heart Valley.

“Young Master Yang, where are we going?” Xi Lei, being straightforward, sensed the killing intent around Yang Kai and couldn’t help but ask.

“Wenqing Sect!” Yang Kai replied.

Xi Lei and the others understood and said no more, simply following behind Ji Yao.

This incident at Ice Heart Valley was, in some ways, related to Yang Kai. In fact, it all stemmed from the trouble he caused. If he hadn’t killed Feng Xi in the Shattered Star Sea back then, Ice Heart Valley wouldn’t have been besieged.

Now that Wenqing Sect’s upper echelon was wiped out, they had suffered, but Yang Kai clearly didn’t intend to let things end here.

Since the enmity was already formed, it was only natural to eradicate the roots.

What’s more, Wenqing Sect had stood in the Northern Territory for tens of thousands of years. There must be many treasures stored within. How could Yang Kai let such an opportunity slip by?

Before long, the group arrived at Binglun City.

Previously, Binglun City had been seized by Wenqing Sect, but after today’s battle and their crushing defeat, the city changed hands in an instant. The streets, once bustling, were now deserted.

They went straight to the City Lord’s mansion, found the Space Array, and after placing Source Crystals to activate it, the group vanished from sight.

The Wenqing Mountain Range was where Wenqing Sect’s foundation stood.

It was said that in the past, the Great Emperor Wenqing started from nothing here, founded the vast Sect, and comprehended the Dao, achieving the realm of a Great Emperor.

A Sect with a legacy of tens of thousands of years naturally had extraordinary heritage.

The mountains rose and fell, rocks jutted out, the scenery was beautiful, and the land was blessed with talent and spirit.

Within the Wenqing Mountain Range, there were countless peaks and the Spiritual Qi was extremely dense. Unlike Ice Heart Valley, which was built in a land of ice and snow, the climate here was pleasant, with birds singing and flowers blooming everywhere.

A day later, when Yang Kai arrived with the Yao Kings and Ji Yao, they found Wenqing Sect’s mountain gate wide open. Not only was the array inactive, but there wasn’t even a single disciple guarding the gate.

Spreading out his Divine Sense, Yang Kai found the entire Wenqing Sect was like a ghost land, not a single living person in sight.

“They sure ran fast.” Xi Lei also noticed this and couldn’t help but mutter.

Such a large Sect— even if Feng Xuan took many disciples and high-level members away, some should have stayed behind. But now, the entire Wenqing Sect was empty. Clearly, the fleeing cultivators from Ice Heart Valley had spread the news, causing those left behind to abandon their ancestral home and escape disaster.

In other words, since yesterday, Wenqing Sect had been completely wiped out.

Looking up at the mountain gate, the three characters for Wenqing Sect on the white jade plaque shone brightly, as if someone polished them regularly, keeping them fresh and clean.

Yang Kai raised his hand and struck, shattering the plaque to pieces.

He called to the others and walked straight inside.

Along the way, as expected, there was no sign of life. The pavilions and towers were in disarray, likely because the disciples took anything valuable when they fled.

In any case, as they walked, not only did they see no one, but even the valuables were all gone.

Only this vast foundation remained.

“This place is pretty good. The Spiritual Qi is dense, there must be an excellent Earth Vein here.” Xi Lei looked around, nodding and swaying his head.

Yang Kai smiled slightly, “This is a Sect with the inheritance of a Great Emperor, founded by the Great Emperor himself. If the place wasn’t good, he wouldn’t have chosen it. I’d wager there’s nowhere better in the entire Northern Territory.”

“What a pity, what a pity. They provoked someone they shouldn’t have, and ten thousand years of foundation was destroyed in an instant.” Yingfei sighed.

Xie Wuwei’s eyes turned, and he suddenly smiled, “Young Master Yang, why not take over this place, establish your own Sect? Now that it’s ownerless, it’d be a shame to let it go to waste.”

At these words, Xi Lei and Yingfei were both stunned. It wasn’t that Yang Kai lacked the qualifications to found a Sect—he was an Emperor, after all, and the place was ready-made. With such a rich environment, there was nowhere more suitable.

The two Yao Kings just didn’t know what Xie Wuwei was plotting, or why he suddenly made such a suggestion.

But neither was stupid. After a moment’s thought, their eyes lit up, and they understood Xie Wuwei’s plan.

Yingfei grinned, “Brother Xie’s suggestion is good. If Young Master Yang establishes a Sect here, remember to give us some Elder or Protector positions.”

Xi Lei also grinned, “Count me in!”

Hearing this, Yang Kai glanced at them and secretly laughed. He understood that the three of them didn’t really want to return to the Ancient Land, but wanted to stay by his side.

He didn’t have the face to recruit three Yao Kings to his side, but Zhang Ruoxi certainly did—this was a case of the real intention lying elsewhere.

Understanding this, Yang Kai didn’t expose them and just said, “Aren’t you afraid the Saint Venerables will come after me? I don’t have the guts to poach their people.”

“How could that be?” Xie Wuwei chuckled, “The Saint Venerables aren’t petty. If you talk to them, they’ll surely agree.”

“That’s right, that’s right. The Saint Venerables are very reasonable,” Yingfei chimed in.

If it were anyone else trying to poach their people, who knows whether the Saint Venerables would be reasonable or not—but if Yang Kai were to ask, there’d definitely be no problem.

“We’ll see.” Yang Kai pondered for a moment, not giving a definite answer.

The three Yao Kings’ eyes lit up at the suggestion, secretly feeling this was a golden opportunity. Excitement stirred in their hearts, and they grew eager at the prospect.

Xie Wuwei’s sudden suggestion had truly tempted Yang Kai.

As they said, this place was excellent and ready-made. Even if Yang Kai didn’t want it, it wouldn’t be long before another force took over.

After all, this was a place Yang Kai had conquered. Though he hadn’t seen a single living person here, the fact that it was empty was entirely his doing.

Since he’d taken this territory, there was no reason to hand it over to someone else.

What’s more, Yang Kai felt that after so many years in the Star Realm, always wandering, he’d never had a stable place to stay.

It was fine when he was alone, but if he brought his friends and family from Dark Star here in the future, where would they go? And what about Chi Yue, Ghost Ancestor, and the others? He couldn’t let them stay in Thousand Leaves Sect forever.

It was better to prepare a place for them to settle and cultivate as soon as possible.

So, Xie Wuwei’s casual remark truly moved Yang Kai’s heart.

But he also knew that founding a Sect wasn’t so simple. Back on Dark Star, he’d only managed to establish Lingxiao Sect by a stroke of luck. Now, to gain a foothold in the Star Realm, he’d have to plan carefully.

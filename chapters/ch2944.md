## Chapter 2944 — Fend for Yourself

A group of Emperor Realm cultivators stared at him in shock. Even the black-faced burly man clearly hadn’t expected his own Palace Master to give such a reply. For a moment, his mouth hung open, his expression dazed.

Li Jiao said, “Who knows when this kind of trash snuck in, daring to impersonate an Elder of my Li Dragon Palace. Palace Master Yang is magnanimous and won’t stoop to his level, so let’s not bother with him.”

Yang Kai said coldly, “If he’s trash, then he should be cleaned up.”

Li Jiao hurriedly said, “Palace Master Yang is absolutely right.”

As soon as he finished speaking, he raised his hand toward the black-faced burly man. Instantly, the great hall was filled with swirling winds and surging clouds.

When the massive palm imprint crashed down, the black-faced burly man still hadn’t reacted. But even if he had, with cultivation only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, he could never be a match for Li Jiao.

With a miserable scream, the black-faced burly man was slammed to the ground, countless bones shattering, blood gushing from his mouth and nose as he fainted on the spot.

The hall was so silent that a pin drop could be heard. All the Elders of Li Dragon Palace were stunned, never expecting the Palace Master to strike one of their own. Their hearts chilled, bodies turning cold.

Li Jiao was not a capricious man. Every Emperor Realm Elder present had followed him for at least a hundred years and contributed greatly to Li Dragon Palace. Yet, to curry favor with an outsider, Li Jiao had ruthlessly struck his own subordinate. Such actions inevitably left people disheartened.

After suffering such a blow, even if the black-faced burly man didn’t die, his injuries were severe. At the very least, he would need a month or two of rest to recover.

Under the gaze of everyone present, Li Jiao raised his hand again and flung the dark-faced man straight out of the hall. His eyes turned cold as he swept his gaze across the surroundings and snorted, “Being blind at home is merely embarrassing; being blind outside will cost you your life. I trust you will all remember that.”

These words, laced with warning, entered everyone’s ears, making them shudder involuntarily, their expressions varied.

Li Jiao turned his head and looked at Yang Kai with a dry laugh. “I wonder if Palace Master Yang is satisfied?”

Yang Kai chuckled, “Brother Li, you’re handling your own affairs. What does my satisfaction matter?”

Only then did Li Jiao breathe a sigh of relief and exhale. “May I ask what brings Palace Master Yang here today?”

Yang Kai smiled, “To collect payment, of course. Didn’t Brother Li just guess as much? Why ask when you already know?”

Li Jiao was startled, wiping the cold sweat from his forehead. “Palace Master Yang, please don’t joke, please don’t joke…”

“Do I look like I’m joking?” Yang Kai looked at him blandly.

Now, Li Jiao wasn’t sure what Yang Kai’s real purpose was. By rights, a debt of forty million Upper-Level Source Crystals a year wasn’t worth Yang Kai making such a fuss and coming in person. But his expression didn’t look like he was joking, making Li Jiao’s heart pound.

Owing someone such a huge sum always made one feel inferior, lacking confidence even when speaking. If Lingxiao Palace didn’t have such deep foundations, Li Dragon Palace might have resisted. But knowing Lingxiao Palace’s true strength, Li Jiao didn’t even dare to think of resisting.

After all, they were connected to Grandmaster Ji Ying, had three Yao Kings sitting in residence, and even a member of the Dragon Clan… With such a lineup, how could Li Dragon Palace compare? If they really angered them, Wenqing Sect’s fate would be Li Dragon Palace’s tomorrow.

For a moment, Li Jiao could only accompany his smile, not knowing what to do.

Yang Kai suddenly said, “Brother Li, can’t you pay up?”

Li Jiao replied awkwardly, “I must ask Palace Master Yang to grant a few more days of grace!”

“How long do you need?”

“Half a year… No, no, three months. Three months will do.” Li Jiao hurriedly said.

“Three months? Isn’t that a bit too little, Palace Master Li?” Yang Kai smiled faintly. “How about I give you three years?”

“What?” Li Jiao was stunned, not quite understanding Yang Kai’s meaning.

Yang Kai said, “I’ll give you three years. For these three years, your Li Dragon Palace doesn’t need to pay tribute to my Lingxiao Palace. After three years, everything returns to normal. With this buffer, your Li Dragon Palace should have a much easier time!”

Instead of feeling joy, Li Jiao looked even more alarmed and hesitated. “Palace Master Yang… what do you want?”

There’s no such thing as a free lunch. This Lingxiao Palace Master couldn’t possibly be so kind. Li Jiao had lived long enough to know at least that much.

Yang Kai smiled lightly. “I need Brother Li to accompany me somewhere.”

Li Jiao immediately became wary, his expression shifting. “Where?”

Yang Kai quietly sent a voice transmission, uttering two words.

Li Jiao’s face changed dramatically, as if he’d heard some shocking bad news. He shook his head like a rattle-drum. “Palace Master Yang, it’s not that I’m unwilling to help, but…”

“Then pay up now!” Yang Kai interrupted, extending his hand.

Li Jiao’s mouth twitched uncontrollably, and he said angrily, “Palace Master Yang, I’ve treated you sincerely, but don’t push me too far. I, Li Jiao, am not someone to be frightened so easily.”

Yang Kai smiled lightly, "Brother Li, why be so unreasonable? I'm only asking you to accompany me to that place, not asking for your life."

Li Jiao shook his head. “Anywhere else is fine, but that place…” He seemed to recall something, shivering involuntarily. “Palace Master Yang, ask for something else. If I can do it, I will.”

“Truly not possible?” Yang Kai’s face darkened slightly.

Li Jiao remained silent.

Yang Kai sighed softly. “Forget it, a forced melon is never sweet. Since Brother Li is unwilling, I won’t insist. I was going to help you resolve a crisis, but since you won’t cooperate, you’ll have to fend for yourself.”

Li Jiao’s heart skipped a beat. “A crisis? What crisis?”

Yang Kai shrugged.

All this time, Zhu Lie, who had been standing silently to the side, said, “Does this guy know where Zhu Qing is?”

“No one knows better than him,” Yang Kai replied.

Back then, the reason Zhu Qing went to Frozen Earth was ultimately because of Li Jiao. Years ago, Li Jiao had obtained a Dragon Blood Flower from Frozen Earth by chance and brought it back to Li Dragon Palace, nurturing it with his own blood. According to him, the reason a Dragon Blood Flower could appear in Frozen Earth was because a giant dragon had fallen there, and the flower absorbed the dragon’s essence to survive.

Zhu Qing had previously asked Li Jiao about the location and went to Frozen Earth, intending to retrieve the Dragon Clan origin left behind by that giant dragon.

So, if anyone in this world knew exactly where Zhu Qing was in Frozen Earth, it was Li Jiao. Having him lead the way was the fastest method.

“That’s good!” Zhu Lie nodded and strode toward Li Jiao.

Li Jiao’s heart tightened, an inexplicable sense of crisis welling up. He had already noticed this red-haired young man and sensed his strength, which was why he had tolerated Yang Kai’s demands. Now, with just a word, Li Jiao became even more wary of him.

He asked in a deep voice, “May I ask your name, sir?”

Zhu Lie let out a cold laugh. "A mere insect has no right to know my name."

“In… insect!” Li Jiao was furious. He feared Yang Kai because of the many powerful experts behind him, especially that female dragon, who was utterly beyond his reach. This red-haired young man was indeed strong, but not to the point of inspiring awe.

After all, he was a Third-Stage Emperor Realm master. There weren’t many in this world who could surpass him.

“I can’t stand despicable bloodlines like yours. They should have been wiped out long ago!” Zhu Lie’s eyes were full of contempt.

These words completely enraged Li Jiao. His impure bloodline was a sore spot, and anyone who mentioned it would suffer.

With a furious roar, Li Jiao prepared to teach this red-haired young man a lesson. No matter how strong the other party was, as long as he wasn’t a Great Emperor, in Li Dragon Palace, he couldn’t possibly be his match.

He had the advantage of time, place, and people, able to unleash power far beyond his own strength.

But his roar was cut short. The Emperor Yuan surging within him suddenly stilled. Standing atop the dais, he looked down, his body trembling as if he’d been struck by lightning, nearly collapsing as he cried out, “Dra… Dragon Clan!”

Below, as Zhu Lie approached, a massive, crimson dragon shadow suddenly appeared behind him, coiling above his head. The enormous dragon eyes stared at Li Jiao without a hint of emotion. Under that gaze, Li Jiao felt all his strength drain away, his blood nearly freezing, and even his Emperor Yuan wouldn’t circulate.

At this point, even a Dao Origin Realm cultivator could probably kill him with ease.

Only now did the high, resounding dragon roar echo out, deafening and making the entire hall tremble.

All the Elders of Li Dragon Palace turned ashen, flustered and at a loss, their eyes fixed on the giant dragon shadow behind Zhu Lie, an involuntary sense of insignificance welling up in their hearts.

Only now did they realize just how wise and heroic their Palace Master truly was.

When Li Jiao knocked out the black-faced burly man and threw him out, they’d thought their Palace Master was cold and heartless, their hearts chilled. But now they understood—it wasn’t cowardice, but foresight.

Never mind the strength of the Lingxiao Palace Master watching the show—even a single Dragon Clan member could sweep everyone present aside. If a conflict really broke out, only Li Dragon Palace would suffer.

A Dragon—a living, breathing Dragon Clan member! They had always existed only in legends and ancient records, yet now one had appeared before their very eyes, making everything feel utterly unreal.

Only by witnessing it firsthand could they realize how feeble the records were. The Dragon Clan’s might was far beyond what words could describe.

If a Dragon grew angry, who could withstand their wrath? To clash with a Dragon was to oppose the entire Dragon Isle. Li Dragon Palace neither had the courage nor the ability.

In a flash, Zhu Lie had already reached the dais, grabbing Li Jiao by the throat and lifting him up.

Despite possessing Third Stage Emperor cultivation, Li Jiao was like a baby before Zhu Lie, utterly unable to resist. Though he struggled, he couldn’t break free. His face quickly turned red, his breathing labored.

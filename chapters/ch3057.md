## Chapter 3057 — Feinting East, Striking West

Some of the Sixth-Tier and Seventh-Tier Dragons secretly compared themselves to him and reckoned that even without bloodline suppression, they likely would not gain any advantage against such a foe. Their expressions grew much more solemn, and their earlier contempt and arrogance vanished.

The moment Yang Kai assumed his Half-Dragon form, he unleashed his Dragon Pressure toward Fu Zhun.

Fu Zhun's expression remained unchanged, while the fair hand she thrust forward became even more crystalline and translucent.

In the blink of an eye, the two had already clashed.

Before everyone's eyes, Fu Zhun's hand effortlessly broke through all of Yang Kai's defenses and pressed lightly against his chest.

Without the slightest sound, Yang Kai's broad chest suddenly caved inward as though struck by a massive boulder, while his back bulged out at the same instant. A shockwave visible to the naked eye erupted behind him, making for a terrifying sight.

Kacha...

Only then did any sound emerge: the noise of countless bones breaking, enough to make anyone who heard it shudder.

After his towering Half-Dragon body, over eight zhang tall, paused for an instant, it was sent hurtling away with thunderous speed, swift as lightning. In the blink of an eye, Yang Kai flew several thousand zhang, shedding Golden Blood along the way and tracing a straight golden line through the air.

"He overestimated himself!" The same thought surfaced in the minds of the many Dragons as they watched Yang Kai being sent flying.

Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong exchanged a glance and secretly shook their heads.

Although Yang Kai possessed the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power and was not weak himself, the Second Elder was still an expert comparable to a Great Emperor and possessed a Tenth-Tier Dragon Bloodline. The Dragon Pressure he had unleashed at the outset had failed to exert any bloodline suppression upon her. Without that advantage, how could Yang Kai possibly withstand the power of the Second Elder's palm?

That seemingly gentle palm immediately showed Yang Kai the gulf between himself and a Great Emperor.

He had not even managed to harm a single hair on Fu Zhun's head.

The scene fell silent, with only a bone-chilling aura enveloping the surroundings. Fu Zhun stared coldly at Yang Kai as he barely managed to steady himself, her expression indifferent.

Suddenly, her eyes flickered sharply. She slowly turned her head to look to one side, narrowing her eyes as she calmly asked, "Zhu Qing, do you know what you are doing?"

At her words, everyone abruptly came to their senses and turned to look in that direction.

What they saw left them dumbfounded.

At some point, Zhu Qing had appeared beside Fu Chi. Her Dragon Pressure spread out and enveloped him, while one of her fair hands had transformed into a Dragon Claw and now gripped his skull. She stared vigilantly at Fu Zhun as though facing a great enemy.

Zhu Qing showed little reaction to Yang Kai's injury, not because she did not care whether he lived or died, but because she did not dare divide her attention. Before an expert comparable to a Great Emperor, the slightest lapse could cause Yang Kai's entire plan to fail. She did not even allow herself the thought of turning to check his injuries. Her sharp Dragon Claw had already pierced Fu Chi's flesh, seemingly ready to kill him on the spot with the slightest exertion of force.

Fu Chi clearly had not expected anything like this to happen. A moment ago, he had still been mocking Yang Kai for trying to shake a tree like an ant and secretly rejoicing at the sight of him suffering at the Second Elder's hands. He had even wished that her palm would take Yang Kai's life outright.

Before the thought had even finished crossing his mind, a figure flashed before his eyes and Zhu Qing appeared behind him. Immediately afterward, he felt the suppression of her Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline and the pain coming from his head.

Fu Chi's face instantly turned livid, his expression as ugly as could be.

A Ninth-Tier bloodline did not hold an overwhelming advantage over an Eighth-Tier one. If they truly fought head-on, Fu Chi could have held out for some time. Zhu Qing, however, had prepared in advance and seized the initiative, so she now held complete control over his life and death.

Everyone's expressions became wonderfully varied in an instant.

The scene before them was bizarre in the extreme. Today was supposed to be Fu Chi and Zhu Qing's wedding day. Both were dressed in bright red robes and should have made a perfect couple, yet now the bride looked ready to kill the groom. No matter how one viewed it, the sight was exceedingly strange.

Zhu Qing did not answer, keeping all her attention fixed on Fu Zhun.

"Your seal has actually been broken..." Zhu Kong frowned in disbelief. "When did this happen?"

Zhu Qing's Dragon Bloodline had been suppressed and her cultivation sealed all this time, yet she showed no sign of either now. She had clearly recovered completely, which was how she had managed to catch everyone off guard.

"So that is how it is!" A look of realization flashed through Zhu Yan's eyes. Quietly astonished, he gazed at Yang Kai, who was clutching his chest and shaking his head as he flew back.

"So this is your doing!" Fu Zhun glared furiously at Yang Kai and shouted.

Zhu Qing's seal could not possibly have undone itself for no reason, and Yang Kai was the only person who had come into contact with her from beginning to end. If anyone could have broken her seal without anyone noticing, it could only have been Yang Kai.

But how could he possess such an ability? She had personally placed that seal!

"You are allowed to make your move, but this Young Master is not allowed to return the favor? What kind of logic is that?" Yang Kai's expression was somewhat fierce, and traces of Golden Blood remained at the corner of his mouth. His chest had not fully recovered and was still slightly caved in. Fu Zhun's palm had been no small matter; although it had not taken his life, it had sent his Qi and blood roiling and broken several of his bones. His eight-zhang-tall body strode through the air and came to a halt before Zhu Qing like a small mountain, directly facing the pressure of the Dragon Clan's three Elders.

Seeing that his voice was full of vigor and that he was in no serious danger, Zhu Qing, who had been worried sick, finally breathed a sigh of relief and relaxed considerably.

"Well done, Qing'er," Yang Kai praised without looking back. They were husband and wife, after all, and understood each other without words. He had not had time to explain his plan to Zhu Qing in detail, but at the crucial moment, she had still made the correct choice.

"Feinting east to strike west!" Zhu Kong's brow rose.

He, too, now understood what had happened in that brief instant.

Yang Kai had activated the Dragon Transformation Art, assumed an eight-zhang Half-Dragon body, and exchanged blows with the Second Elder. It had looked recklessly arrogant, as though he had no idea of the immensity of Heaven and Earth, but in truth, he had only been drawing everyone's attention.

While every eye was fixed on him, Zhu Qing, concealed behind his enormous body, had been free to act.

The facts proved that this move was extremely effective.

Yang Kai had originally been nothing more than a fish on the chopping block. On Dragon Isle, the Dragon Clan could knead him flat or round as they pleased, and he had no strength to resist. Now, however, the situation was somewhat different.

Zhu Qing's seal had been broken and her strength restored, while Fu Chi was in her hands. She looked ready to drag him down with her at the slightest disagreement.

At the cost of taking a thunderous blow from the Second Elder, Yang Kai had seized a measure of initiative.

What deep cunning, and what a daring plan! Zhu Yan and Zhu Kong were both slightly moved. They secretly felt that if today's affair could not be resolved perfectly, Dragon Isle was very likely to make a powerful enemy.

"Zhu Qing, are you rebelling? Release Fu Chi at once! What sort of disgraceful behavior is this?!" Fu Zhun's face was icy, and her voice was just as cold and biting.

Zhu Qing's expression did not change. She merely replied, "I listen to Yang Kai. If he tells me to release him, I will."

Fu Zhun flew into a rage. "Do not forget that you are a member of the Dragon Clan, a member of Dragon Isle! You should know the punishment for harming one of your own clansmen!"

Seeing that she was truly enraged, the many Dragons trembled with fear.

They had not seen the Second Elder become thoroughly enraged for a very long time. The last time had been more than ten years ago, and the result of her fury had been that the one she cherished most was sent into the Dragon Tomb. At the time, that person had been the Dragon Clan's Third Elder!

Zhu Qing now possessed a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline and was, in principle, qualified to become the Dragon Clan's Fifth Elder. But so what? Even the Third Elder had been personally sent into the Dragon Tomb by the Second Elder, so how could Zhu Qing possibly be spared?

"Harming your own clansmen?" Yang Kai snorted coldly. Reaching out, he seized Fu Chi in his enormous palm and pulled him over, then directed the full force of his Dragon Pressure at him. Fu Chi cried out in alarm as fear welled up from the depths of his bones.

He possessed only an Eighth-Tier bloodline. Even Zhu Qing's Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline could suppress him, to say nothing of Yang Kai's Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power. Had this been Yang Kai a month ago, Fu Chi would not have been so terrified even if Yang Kai had used the Dragon Transformation Art. Over the past month, however, Yang Kai had made astonishing progress in the Dragon Transformation Art, and the degree to which the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power had fused with his body was incomparable to before.

Fu Chi felt the Dragon Yuan within him suddenly stagnate until he could barely circulate it.

Yet that was not the most unbearable part. What humiliated him most was that he, a dignified Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon, was being held in Yang Kai's hand like a toy. Under the gazes of several hundred people, he was immediately overwhelmed by shame and fury.

"You vile woman! You sealed Qing'er's cultivation and suppressed her Dragon Bloodline. You knew perfectly well that her heart belonged to someone else, yet you still forced her to marry a Dragon she did not love. Does that not count as harming one of your own clansmen? As I see it, you are the one who deserves punishment most!"

Sweat suddenly beaded on the foreheads of the gathered Dragons, while even the Great Elder's and Fourth Elder's brows twitched.

This fellow was far too bold. How could he dare speak to the Second Elder like that?

Fu Zhun's face twitched as she gritted her teeth and asked, "What did you call me?"

"Vile woman! Vile woman, vile woman, vile woman, vile woman!" Yang Kai opened his mouth and immediately hurled the insult at her again and again. His cavernous maw gaped wide and spittle flew everywhere, nearly spraying Fu Zhun in the face.

Fu Zhun's face went from livid to deathly pale, her delicate body trembling as her terrifying murderous intent nearly condensed into tangible form.

"I advise you not to think about killing me." Yang Kai looked down at her with a mocking smile. "That is right. If you truly wanted to kill me, I likely would have no chance to escape. But I am rather timid and cannot bear being frightened. If I lose control of my strength and accidentally crush this little worm, things will become rather difficult."

As though to prove that his words were no empty threat, he exerted a little force with his hand.

Fu Chi immediately screamed. He felt as though every bone in his body had been dislocated. Gritting his teeth, he endured as beads of sweat the size of beans rolled down his forehead, his gaze overflowing with venomous hatred. When had he ever suffered such humiliation? Even all the seawater of Dragon Isle could not wash away his hatred for Yang Kai. Yet now that he had fallen into Yang Kai's hands, he could not summon even the strength to resist.

"I have heard that the Dragon Clan is small in number and that every Dragon is an extremely precious clansman. I wonder how much weight an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon carries in the Second Elder's heart?"

Fu Zhun gritted her teeth and growled one word at a time, "From this day onward, my Dragon Clan will fight you to the death!"

Zhu Qing's beautiful face instantly lost all color.

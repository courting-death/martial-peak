## Chapter 3008 — Why Are You Here?

Once Yang Kai understood this, his resistance faded considerably. After thinking for a moment, he sat down beside Lin Yun'er, listening to the bubbling sounds from the iron pot while breathing in its aroma.

Now they only had to wait for the feast!

A short while later...

"Delicious! So delicious!" Lin Yun'er held a large thighbone in her hands, gnawing on it while showering the food with praise.

“Slow down!” Yang Kai reached out and wiped the grease from her face. “No one’s going to steal it from you!”

Despite saying so, he ate no slower himself. Bones lay scattered all around him, while the pile beside Lin Yun'er was even larger, nearly forming a small hill.

As expected of a Divine Spirit descendant, its flesh contained an extremely rich essence of energy. Every bite made their mouths water and their lower abdomens burn hot.

Although there was no seasoning whatsoever, this was definitely the most delicious food Yang Kai had ever eaten. The iron pot seemed to possess the power to transform the mundane into the miraculous; even casually boiling something in it produced a delicacy so delicious one might swallow one's tongue.

After eating a few bites of meat, Yang Kai picked up a large bowl and took a sip of soup. Glancing at Li Jiao, who stood nearby, he asked, "Brother Li, care to try some?"

"No... thank you," Li Jiao politely declined.

"Then why are you swallowing your drool? Enough nonsense."

Li Jiao gave an embarrassed smile. "Then this Li won't stand on ceremony."

Though he still felt somewhat resistant, since they were both Divine Spirit descendants and one had suddenly ended up cooking in a pot, it was only natural that he would have misgivings about actually eating it no matter how good it smelled. However, with Yang Kai inviting him like this, it would be awkward to refuse. Besides, he truly wanted a bite to see what it tasted like.

Walking over to the pot, Li Jiao reached in and fished out a rib. After excusing himself, he took a small bite. His eyes immediately widened, and he began eating much faster, devouring everything before him like a whirlwind sweeping away clouds.

"Tasty, isn't it?" Yang Kai asked with a broad smile.

Li Jiao nodded repeatedly. "Not bad, not bad. Truly not bad. Following Palace Master Yang really does bring good fortune to one's palate."

Lin Yun'er remained silent on the other side. While Yang Kai and Li Jiao were talking, she had already spent some time wolfing down her food, and several more bones swiftly appeared beside her.

The little black dog refused to be outdone. Lying beside the pot, it bit down and dragged out a large chunk of meat, then ate with great relish.

An hour later, little Yun'er lay on the ground, rubbing her belly.

Such an enormous Divine Spirit descendant had actually been eaten clean by three people and a dog, without even a mouthful of soup left over.

Lin Yun'er wore a thoroughly satisfied expression. She had finally eaten her fill. Ever since secretly running away, there had not been a single day when she had eaten enough. When she was with her Master, she never had to worry about food or drink, as her Master handled everything for her. Only after leaving her Master did she learn how hard life was, when even eating a full meal proved so difficult.

Thinking of this, she began to miss her Master a little.

"Little Yun'er, if you had such a trump card, why didn't you use it sooner?" Yang Kai asked while picking his teeth with a piece of grass root he had found somewhere.

He had no idea what sort of origins the Unity Cauldron had, but if little Yun'er had summoned it from the start, that damned Sword Formation would have been broken long ago, and none of the later trouble would have happened.

Lin Yun'er narrowed her eyes and replied, "Master said a trump card can't be used unless there's no other choice."

Her Master again. The little girl seemed to truly regard her Master's words as sacred law.

"I'll take you to eat something even better another day," Yang Kai casually said.

Lin Yun'er exclaimed in delight, "Really?"

"Mm. There's a Wild Ancient Land in the Eastern Territory with plenty of delicious things inside."

He was even setting his sights on the Wild Ancient Land! Cold sweat poured down Li Jiao's forehead as he listened. He had heard that Divine Spirits presided over that place. Palace Master Yang was far too bold.

"Thank you, Uncle Yang." Little Yun'er was overjoyed.

After lying there for a while longer, Yang Kai finally asked, "Where do you plan to go next?"

Wind and Cloud Pavilion was effectively destroyed, and the little girl's goal had been achieved. She had vented her anger.

"I don't know." Lin Yun'er shook her head. She had run away from home on a whim because she felt bored and had no plans whatsoever. Even meeting Yang Kai had been a coincidence.

"I see..." Yang Kai thought for a moment before saying, "Why don't you come with me to the Northern Territory? I established the Lingxiao Palace there."

"Lingxiao Palace?" Lin Yun'er immediately became interested when she heard this. After all, she had once spent some time at Lingxiao Sect on Dark Star, so the name made her feel especially close to it. She nodded. "Good, good!"

"All right, then we'll return tomorrow." Yang Kai immediately made his decision.

The disturbance at Wind and Cloud Pavilion had grown rather large. A top Sect had been destroyed just like that. It would have been one thing if he and Lin Yun'er were cultivators from the Western Territory, but neither of them was. If they continued to remain here, they might draw the attention of a Great Emperor's Sect.

The Night Shadow Great Emperor and Shadow Slaughter Palace were not so easy to provoke.

Li Jiao's spirits also rose when he heard this. It had not been easy following Yang Kai on this journey, but at last he saw hope of returning home.

Nothing happened overnight.

Early the next morning, Yang Kai began arranging a Space Array.

He had originally planned to find a concealed location in the Western Territory to arrange it. That way, it would be convenient for him to travel back and forth in the future, and if he wanted to return to the Western Territory, he could simply come through the Space Array. However, he was unfamiliar with the Western Territory, so finding such a place would not be easy and might take one or two months, perhaps even longer.

After considering it carefully, he abandoned his original plan.

It was better to return first. If he truly needed to visit the Western Territory one day, making another arduous journey here would be no great hardship.

He began arranging the array directly atop this mountain peak in Wind and Cloud Pavilion.

Li Jiao meditated quietly nearby, but in truth, he was secretly watching Yang Kai's movements. The more he watched, the more shocked and horrified he became.

He was also a Palace Master and possessed keen eyesight, so he could naturally see how extraordinary this cross-territory Space Array was.

With nothing to do, Lin Yun'er played with the little black dog nearby.

Half a day later, the Space Array was complete.

At Yang Kai's call, Lin Yun'er immediately ran over carrying the little black dog, while Li Jiao also stepped onto the Formation Foundation.

The Laws of Space fluctuated and enveloped the three people and one dog. Yang Kai's expression grew solemn, and as his Emperor Yuan surged, the array lit up.

"Activate!" he shouted in a low voice. Dazzling light erupted, and a sense of weightlessness enveloped everyone before they vanished in an instant.

Just then, Yang Kai suddenly felt a strange force assail them. The force was not particularly powerful, but it was extremely ingenious and actually interfered with his teleportation for a moment.

Yang Kai paled in shock. Before he could determine whether something had gone wrong, a person's voice suddenly rang out in his Sea of Consciousness.

"You've got some nerve, brat, daring to abduct this Lord's beloved disciple. Since you bore no ill intent, this Lord will let you off this time. Do it again, and this Lord will kill you!"

The voice had sounded directly in his Sea of Consciousness, clearly meaning its defenses had been breached. What shocked Yang Kai, however, was that he had not sensed the slightest disturbance.

This was simply inconceivable. His Divine Sense was extraordinarily powerful, comparable to that of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master. Who could silently breach the defenses of his Sea of Consciousness and send a voice directly into his mind?

Before he could finish the thought, his vision blurred, and he found himself back at Lingxiao Palace in the Northern Territory.

Turning his head, he saw Li Jiao standing beside him with tightly furrowed brows, clearly not yet recovered from the aftereffects of cross-territory teleportation. The little black dog stood by his feet with a vacant expression.

Only Lin Yun'er was nowhere to be seen!

Yang Kai's expression changed, and he hurriedly prepared to teleport back and see what had happened. However, when he poured his Divine Sense into the Space Array beneath his feet, he discovered that its connection with the array in the Western Territory had actually been severed.

Impossible. He and Li Jiao had only just teleported here; the connection could not have suddenly broken like this.

The Space Arrays he arranged could all connect to one another. So long as they were properly adjusted, he could freely travel between them. After teleporting so many times, Yang Kai was certain there was nothing wrong with the array he had arranged. It was definitely not something that would easily malfunction.

Recalling the words that had just sounded in his mind, Yang Kai's expression shifted.

Little Yun'er must have been kept behind by her Master.

Had little Yun'er's Master been following her all along? If not, how could this person have kept little Yun'er behind at precisely the right moment? The connection between the arrays had clearly been severed because someone had destroyed the other one.

Needless to say, the person who destroyed it was little Yun'er's Master.

Yang Kai swore furiously to himself. This was already the second time something like this had happened to him. The last time was in the Wild Ancient Land, when Luan Feng destroyed the array he had left there. Now it had happened again.

Just who was little Yun'er's Master? This person had followed behind them for so many days, yet he had not noticed a thing. At this thought, Yang Kai could not help becoming drenched in cold sweat.

No, that wasn't right! Yang Kai suddenly remembered the hostility he had inexplicably sensed in that city some time ago. It had come and gone so quickly that he had thought it was merely his imagination, but now that he considered it, it had not been an illusion at all.

That hostility had clearly come from little Yun'er's Master.

Why was this person hostile toward him? The words transmitted into his mind just now had clearly carried some prejudice as well, and the tone had been quite unfriendly. Yang Kai found it baffling. It was not as though he had done anything to little Yun'er.

However, after figuring this out, Yang Kai relaxed. As long as little Yun'er's failure to come through was not caused by a problem with the teleportation, there was nothing to worry about. Her Master was certainly an expert and likely had reasons for keeping her behind. Judging from those words, this expert cared deeply for Lin Yun'er.

Atop a mountain peak in Wind and Cloud Pavilion.

"Huh?" Lin Yun'er stood all alone on the array, her face blank with confusion. She looked around, but not a single person remained. She immediately called out anxiously, "Uncle Yang! Uncle Yang!"

No one answered.

She called out several more times, but her surroundings remained silent.

"Stop shouting." A person's voice suddenly rang out, and as the words fell, a middle-aged man appeared atop the mountain peak.

Lin Yun'er turned toward the voice and immediately exclaimed in delight, "Master, why are you here?"

She ran over in a few quick steps and grinned at the middle-aged man.

The middle-aged man sighed. "If I hadn't come, you would have been abducted. How could I not come?"

He had not originally planned to reveal himself so soon. It had not been easy to find a successor to inherit his mantle, so he cared deeply for Lin Yun'er. Over the years they had spent together, Lin Yun'er had lived up to his expectations, advancing extremely quickly in her cultivation. Thus, he doted on this little girl in every way.

As for her running away from home and secretly escaping, that was nothing more than Lin Yun'er's wishful thinking.

Right under his nose, how could Lin Yun'er possibly have escaped if he had not deliberately indulged her?

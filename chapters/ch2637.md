## Chapter 2637 — The Heart That Loves Beauty

This was a token, a golden, radiant token whose extraordinary origin was obvious at a glance.

Sun Ping took it, rubbed it for a while, and smiled, "I've heard that your temple's disciple tokens come in four grades: iron, bronze, silver, and gold. Only the most core disciples possess the golden token. It seems this is the Qingyang Gold Token."

The token's material was special and clearly not a fake—this much, Sun Ping could tell.

Yang Kai's gold token was, of course, genuine. It was something Gao Xueting had personally given him back then. Although Qingyang Shrine had never strictly demanded Yang Kai join, gifting him a Qingyang Gold Token was enough to make him a registered disciple of the Shrine.

The reason he chose Xiao Baiyi as his impersonated identity on this trip was precisely because he had the Qingyang Gold Token in hand.

"That's not right." Yang Kai smiled slightly and corrected, "Our Shrine's tokens aren't four grades, but five."

"Five grades?" Sun Ping raised his eyebrows.

Yang Kai said, "The Elders hold jade tokens!"

"So that's how it is." Sun Ping nodded slightly, though it was unclear if he truly believed it. After all, he didn't know much about Qingyang Shrine, but seeing how confidently Yang Kai spoke, it didn't seem like he was making things up.

For a moment, he hesitated.

An Emperor Realm cultivator coming to join Wenqing Sect was definitely good news—he might be a great help in the coming war. The key issue was that Sun Ping couldn't be sure of Yang Kai's background.

But Sun Ping was reluctant to let such an opportunity slip by—and it would only make the world mock Wenqing Sect for being timid.

After thinking for a while, Sun Ping suddenly turned his head and sent a sound transmission to a young disciple.

The young disciple immediately stepped forward upon hearing this, cupped his fists toward Yang Kai, and said, "Cao Xu greets Senior Brother."

Yang Kai glanced at him sideways but didn't respond, unsure what orders Sun Ping had given this man.

Cao Xu continued, "A few years ago, when the Shattered Star Sea opened, I was fortunate enough to enter and saw several outstanding young talents from the Southern Territory!"

Hearing this, Yang Kai's heart skipped a beat—this was bad.

He had chosen to impersonate Xiao Baiyi mainly because no one recognized him. With the Qingyang Gold Token in hand, the mix of truth and falsehood made it a foolproof plan.

But now, hearing that this Cao Xu had also been to the Shattered Star Sea and seen some Southern Territory cultivators...

If he recognized Xiao Baiyi, Yang Kai's disguise would be exposed instantly.

"Oh?" Yang Kai felt uneasy inside, but his face remained calm as he looked at him and said, "I, Xiao, also went to the Shattered Star Sea, but I never saw you there."

Cao Xu gave a bitter smile, "My cultivation is low. The places I entered probably weren't worthy of Senior Brother Xiao's attention, so naturally we never met."

Never met! Yang Kai's confidence surged. "Is that so? But now that you've stepped forward, what advice do you have?"

Cao Xu hurriedly said, "I wouldn't dare. Though I haven't met friends from Qingyang Shrine, I have seen several others from the Southern Territory—especially one named Wuchang, who left a deep impression. Since Senior Brother Xiao is from the Southern Territory, you must know him."

"Wuchang..." Yang Kai nodded slightly. Wuchang of Tianwu Sacred Land, born with the Yin-Yang Twin Body, fierce and unmatched—Yang Kai certainly remembered him well.

Ignoring Cao Xu, Yang Kai instead turned to Sun Ping, who had been watching him, and pointed in front of himself. Instantly, an image of a person appeared.

Yang Kai said, "Elder Sun, you had Cao Xu ask these questions just to confirm my identity. Now you can ask him—does this image look like Wuchang or not?"

Sun Ping immediately looked at Cao Xu, who nodded lightly in confirmation.

Sun Ping burst out laughing, "It seems Young Master Xiao truly is an elite disciple of Qingyang Shrine."

To be able to conjure the image of a Southern Territory cultivator and possess the Qingyang Shrine Gold Token—there was really nothing to doubt.

Yang Kai smiled, "So, does that mean I can stay?"

Sun Ping's smile faded, and he said solemnly, "There's one last question."

Yang Kai grew annoyed, "Aren't you all a bit much? I'm here to help Wenqing Sect, not to be interrogated. Elder Sun, you've made things difficult for me again and again. What's your intention? Wenqing Sect may be impressive, but Qingyang Shrine isn't afraid either."

He borrowed the Shrine's prestige and erupted on the spot, leaving the surrounding cultivators dumbfounded. They thought, as expected of a disciple from a great sect—he showed no fear even on someone else's turf. None of them would have such courage.

Sun Ping, however, smiled amiably, "Young Master Xiao, please calm down, don't get angry. Let's talk things over."

Yang Kai bared his teeth and snapped, "Old man, this is the last question! If you keep nagging, I'll turn around and leave right now."

"Absolutely the last one," Sun Ping said seriously.

Yang Kai looked displeased, "Ask."

Sun Ping said, "Young Master Xiao, since you come from Qingyang Shrine, you shouldn't lack cultivation resources. Why did you accept our Wenqing Sect's recruitment order?"

Yang Kai smiled proudly, fanning himself slowly, "I certainly don't lack cultivation resources. To be blunt, the many benefits your sect offers don't really interest me. However..." At this, Yang Kai suddenly grinned slyly, looking like a fox that had stolen a chicken.

"However, what?" Sun Ping pressed.

Yang Kai glanced around, then lowered his voice to Sun Ping, "Elder Sun, I'm quite interested in the beautiful women of Ice Heart Valley. After this battle, could your sect let me take a few Ice Heart Valley disciples back as maids?"

Sun Ping smiled knowingly and nodded, "So that's Young Master Xiao's plan."

Hearing Yang Kai's request for Ice Heart Valley disciples, Sun Ping felt much more at ease. After all, for an Emperor Realm cultivator to come for the recruitment order without any needs would be too strange.

This Young Master Xiao's fondness for beauty was understandable.

"The heart that loves beauty is common to all," Yang Kai said with a grin.

Sun Ping stroked his beard and nodded, "That's no problem. If Ice Heart Valley is broken, with your strength, you'll naturally get a share of their disciples. I'm sure the Sect Master won't refuse. I'll agree to your request on his behalf."

"How about ten?" Yang Kai raised his eyebrows.

Sun Ping chuckled, "Young Master Xiao, your appetite is too big. Ice Heart Valley has only seven thousand disciples, but our Wenqing Sect has an army of a hundred thousand. Even after dividing them up, you couldn't possibly get ten."

"Eight," Yang Kai bargained.

"Two at most," Sun Ping replied honestly.

Yang Kai jumped, "Your sect is too stingy!"

Sun Ping shook his head, "It's not that we're stingy—there are just too many wolves and too little meat."

Yang Kai let out a long sigh and said, “Elder Sun, I won’t hide it from you—my life is truly bitter. Back at the Shrine, I was already engaged, but my fiancée is not only overbearing and arrogant, she’s also hideously ugly. That’s why I fled all the way from the Southern Territory to the Northern Territory to temper myself. With such a fierce wife at home, surely it’s not too much to ask for a few pleasing maids to soothe my eyes? Elder Sun, for the sake of my miserable days, can’t you spare me a few more?”

Laughter immediately rang out in the hall, everyone gloating. Even Sun Ping couldn't help but laugh.

But he had no interest in Yang Kai's troubles. He simply waved, "Cao Xu, take Young Master Xiao to Elder Feng Chi and have him assign a task!"

"Yes!" Cao Xu responded, gesturing, "This way, Young Master Xiao!"

Yang Kai didn't move, still shouting, "Elder Sun, let's negotiate the terms some more!"

Sun Ping ignored him, his figure flashing as he disappeared.

Yang Kai sighed, looking disgruntled as he walked out. Wherever he went, everyone avoided him.

After leaving the Recruitment Hall, Yang Kai still looked sullen.

Cao Xu didn't provoke his bad mood, simply leading the way in silence. Soon, they left Binglun City.

"Where are we going?" After a while, Yang Kai suddenly asked.

Cao Xu replied, "To find Elder Feng Chi. He'll assign you a task."

"Elder Feng Chi..." Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. "I heard your Sect Master has the same surname. Is Elder Feng Chi..."

Cao Xu smiled, "Elder Feng Chi is the Sect Master's cousin. So, although his cultivation is only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, his status in the sect is second only to the Sect Master and Vice Sect Master. He's in charge of the siege on Ice Heart Valley."

"I see!" Yang Kai nodded, then asked, "Where are your Sect Master and Vice Sect Master? Since I'm new here, I should pay my respects."

A trace of hidden disdain flashed in Cao Xu's eyes—how could the Sect Master or Vice Sect Master be someone you could just meet? But he quickly concealed it and replied, "The Sect Master is stationed in Binglun City, and the Vice Sect Master is there as well."

"I see. Next time, then. There'll be a chance to meet." Yang Kai smiled freely, not pressing the issue.

Soon, the two arrived at a valley less than three hundred li from Ice Heart Valley. Many wooden houses had been built there, with numerous cultivators coming and going. Many were flying overhead, making the place quite lively.

Yang Kai swept his gaze over the area, his heart sinking.

With so many cultivators gathered here, if Ice Heart Valley's protective array was broken, its seven thousand disciples would stand no chance.

Cao Xu led Yang Kai to a relatively tall wooden house and respectfully said, "Elder Feng, disciple Cao Xu requests an audience."

"Come in!" came a voice from inside.

Cao Xu nodded to Yang Kai and led him in.

As soon as they entered the room, Yang Kai saw a massive Formation Plate laid out in the center of the hall. The Formation Plate resembled a projection, shimmering with points of light, and upon its surface was a clearly outlined map of the surrounding terrain.

Yang Kai glanced at it and was shocked to recognize the outline as Ice Heart Valley. He had no idea what kind of strange artifact this Formation Plate was to have such miraculous effects.

At this moment, a middle-aged elder was quietly giving instructions to another person, who quickly turned and left in a hurry.

After that person left, the elder stared at the Formation Plate for a while, then suddenly let out a cold laugh.

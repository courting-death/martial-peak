## Chapter 2730 — Dare to Gamble

Upon hearing Yang Kai claim he could refine Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills, Ji Ying was truly taken aback.

His expression quickly darkened, and he replied with some displeasure, "Palace Master Yang, are you joking?"

Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills were not so easily refined. Only Imperial-Grade Pill Masters could produce them! If just anyone could become an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, then this title wouldn’t hold such high status in the Star Realm.

Yang Kai was clearly young. To have advanced his cultivation to the Emperor Realm already made him a rare talent—where would he find time to study alchemy? So Ji Ying concluded Yang Kai was just spouting nonsense.

This was simply an insult to alchemists everywhere, a blatant disregard for the Dao of Alchemy!

This man truly didn’t respect alchemists. That rebellious disciple Li Xuan may have twisted the truth and fabricated things, but on this point, he wasn’t wrong.

Ji Ying was furious, wishing he could just leave. But having just misunderstood Yang Kai earlier, he felt awkward about storming off.

Still, his face clearly grew darker.

"Master Ji doesn’t believe me?" Yang Kai looked at Ji Ying.

Ji Ying snorted.

If he didn’t believe, so be it! Yang Kai chuckled. He was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, and there was no need to prove it to anyone. But after a moment’s thought, he raised his brows and said, "Since Master Ji doesn’t believe me, do you dare to make a bet?"

"A bet?" Ji Ying frowned. "What are we betting on?"

"Let’s bet on whether I am truly an Imperial-Grade Pill Master!" Yang Kai smiled slightly.

Ji Ying’s frown deepened. Yang Kai’s confident demeanor made him start to doubt—could this guy really be an Imperial-Grade Pill Master? If so, that would be extraordinary.

Anyone who could become an Imperial-Grade Pill Master deserved respect. This piqued Ji Ying’s curiosity, and he wanted to verify the truth.

"What’s the wager?"

"Let me think…" Yang Kai scratched his head theatrically, glanced around, then clapped his hands. "Got it! My Lingxiao Palace is just starting out and needs all kinds of talent. Now that we have a Chief Formation Master, we’re missing a Chief Alchemist. If Master Ji loses, how about becoming Lingxiao Palace’s Chief Alchemist?"

Mi Qi and Li Jiao were both shocked by this and hurried to dissuade him, "Master Ji, don’t believe him."

"You mustn’t gamble with him."

Lingxiao Palace was already troublesome enough. If Ji Ying became their Chief Alchemist, tying Medicine Pill Valley to them, then in the future, no one in the Northern Territory would be able to challenge Lingxiao Palace. Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace would have no choice but to survive under Lingxiao Palace’s shadow.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao certainly didn’t want to see that happen.

Xi Lei glared, "You two, shut up!"

Though Mi Qi and Li Jiao were unhappy, they didn’t dare say another word.

Ji Ying was momentarily stunned, then laughed, "So that’s Palace Master Yang’s real intention."

Yang Kai chuckled, "Master Ji, do you dare to bet?"

Ji Ying said, "If my wager is to become your Lingxiao Palace’s Chief Alchemist, may I ask what Palace Master Yang’s wager is?"

Yang Kai replied cheerfully, "If I’m not an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, Master Ji can do whatever he wants with me! I won’t complain."

Ji Ying’s expression grew serious. Yang Kai’s words made him realize the other party wasn’t just bluffing—he must have some real confidence, likely his identity as an Imperial-Grade Pill Master.

Was he really an Imperial-Grade Pill Master?

Ji Ying was starting to believe it, though he was still somewhat shocked.

At such a young age, not only had he reached the Emperor Realm, but he’d also mastered alchemy? Ji Ying himself was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master and knew how thorny and difficult this path was, so he could appreciate just how rare Imperial-Grade Pill Masters were.

After thinking it over, Ji Ying said, "No, no, Palace Master Yang, this wager isn’t fair—it’s too advantageous for me."

Only Yang Kai knew if he was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master. If Ji Ying really made such a bet, it would be foolish. Winning brought little benefit, but losing would be a trap. Ji Ying wasn’t that stupid.

"Master Ji, are you afraid?" Yang Kai smiled.

Ji Ying shook his head, "Don’t try to provoke me. I won’t fall for it."

The more Yang Kai pressed, the more Ji Ying felt he was being set up. If he lost, he’d have to become Lingxiao Palace’s Chief Alchemist—how would he ever have a peaceful life?

Though alchemy was as easy as eating or drinking for him, he was Great Emperor Miaodan’s personal disciple. How could he become another sect’s alchemist? Besides, with such a large sect, refining pills would wear him out.

Yang Kai scratched his chin, "Alright… let’s change the terms of the bet."

Ji Ying frowned, "What are you up to now, Palace Master Yang?" He instinctively felt uneasy.

Yang Kai said, "The wager stays the same, but let’s compete in alchemy. We’ll both refine the same pill and see who produces more Spiritual Pills of higher quality!"

Ji Ying was shocked, eyes wide, "You… you want to bet on alchemy with me?"

Had he heard right? As Great Emperor Miaodan’s disciple, someone actually wanted to challenge him in alchemy?

Mi Qi and Li Jiao both wore strange expressions, seriously sizing up Yang Kai, wondering if he was crazy.

Of all things, he wanted to compete with Ji Ying in alchemy—wasn’t that asking for trouble?

"That’s right, let’s compete in alchemy!" Yang Kai nodded, provoking, "Master Ji, are you afraid of this too?"

Ji Ying’s face darkened, "Palace Master Yang, my rebellious disciple twisted the truth earlier, and I’ve already apologized and admitted fault. You’ve founded your own sect—are you so petty as to keep coming after me, trying to humiliate me again and again?"

If Yang Kai’s earlier bet about being an Imperial-Grade Pill Master was reasonable, this new challenge was just foolish. Ji Ying felt Yang Kai was deliberately picking a fight.

Yang Kai shook his head, "Not at all. I genuinely want to compete in alchemy with Master Ji, and I sincerely hope you’ll become Lingxiao Palace’s Chief Alchemist!"

Ji Ying was furious, "You think you can beat me?"

His initial displeasure had now turned to outright fury. To Ji Ying, Yang Kai’s words were no longer just troublemaking—they were a complete humiliation. What gave Yang Kai the right to think he could become Lingxiao Palace’s Chief Alchemist? Everything depended on him winning, yet Ji Ying found the very idea insulting.

Ji Ying had studied alchemy under his master for years, everything he knew was taught by his master. Insulting him was insulting his master! Ji Ying couldn’t tolerate it. He’d felt guilty toward Yang Kai because of Li Xuan, but now that guilt was gone, replaced by burning rage.

Yang Kai slowly stood up, took a step forward, and smiled, "Master Ji, do you dare or not?"

Ji Ying’s eyes blazed with fury, so angry he didn’t know what to say.

Yang Kai ignored him and said, "If Master Ji doesn’t dare, then forget it. Just pretend I never said anything."

"Why wouldn’t I dare!" Ji Ying slammed the table. Being so openly provoked, how could he endure it? If Yang Kai wanted to compete in martial arts, Ji Ying would let it go, since he rarely fought. But now it was about alchemy—whether he liked it or not, he had to accept the challenge, to defend his own name, Medicine Pill Valley’s name, and Great Emperor Miaodan’s name! Anyone who underestimated him would pay the price.

"Good!" Yang Kai clapped his hands.

"But the wager must be changed!" Ji Ying snorted coldly.

"How will it change?" Yang Kai raised his brows.

Ji Ying pointed at Yang Kai, "If I win, I’ll take Palace Master Yang’s head."

If you dare challenge me in alchemy, I’ll play big—let’s see if you’re afraid.

Life and death were at stake. Ji Ying thought Yang Kai would panic after hearing this, but Yang Kai just thought for a moment and nodded, "Alright, as Master Ji says."

"You…" Ji Ying was stunned, his anger surging even higher.

It had been years since he’d been this angry. He didn’t really want Yang Kai dead—he just wanted to scare him off. But Yang Kai accepted the wager without batting an eye.

Did he really have confidence in winning? How could that be? Even among Imperial-Grade Pill Masters, there were differences in skill. In all the world, only a few of his senior brothers could surpass him in alchemy.

"Damn it!" Ji Ying had never met anyone more infuriating than Yang Kai. Even contempt should have its limits, you bastard!

Face full of rage, Ji Ying said, "Palace Master Yang, do you want to change the wager? If you want my life, that’s fine too."

Yang Kai laughed, "I only want Master Ji to become Lingxiao Palace’s Chief Alchemist. Why would I want your life?"

"Then let’s see if you have what it takes." Ji Ying angrily flicked his sleeves.

"Now that the wager is set, let’s discuss which Spiritual Pill to refine." Yang Kai looked at Ji Ying.

"You decide, Palace Master Yang. I don’t mind." His words carried strong confidence, as if it didn’t matter what Spiritual Pill Yang Kai chose.

He truly had the right to be confident. After so many years of alchemy, what Spiritual Pill hadn’t he refined? The only one he hadn’t was the Dragon Blood Pill, simply because he’d never obtained a Dragon Blood Flower.

"Then… let’s do the Emperor Yuan Pill," Yang Kai decided after a moment.

Ji Ying looked at Yang Kai, solemnly, "Palace Master Yang, are you sure you want to refine Emperor Yuan Pills? Though they’re the easiest Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills to refine, they’re also the most demanding in terms of alchemy skill. Why not choose a Dao Origin-Grade Spiritual Pill instead?"

This guy really was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master!

As soon as Yang Kai suggested refining Emperor Yuan Pills, Ji Ying was certain. Though Emperor Yuan Pills were the most common and easiest Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills to refine, only Imperial-Grade Pill Masters could make them, and they required exceptional skill.

It’s just like how the simplest dishes test a chef’s skill the most. The same principle applies in alchemy. With the same ingredients, an average Imperial-Grade Pill Master might only produce three or four Emperor Yuan Pills, but in Ji Ying’s hands, he could definitely make nine!

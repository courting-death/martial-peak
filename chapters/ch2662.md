## Chapter 2662 — On What Grounds

Li Qingyuan and the others felt thoroughly uncomfortable. Three Yao Kings had joined Lingxiao Palace, seeking only the reward of a Deacon position. Their own strength was naturally no match for the three Yao Kings, so if they stayed in Lingxiao Palace, would they only be comparable to ordinary disciples?

Of course, it wasn’t as if they could just abandon their own sects to join Lingxiao Palace either. The waters here ran far too deep—this was not a place where they could simply blend in or make a name for themselves.

Yet, with the addition of these three Yao Kings, Lingxiao Palace would likely become a first-class sect in the Northern Territory. Once it developed, it would surely overshadow the former glory of Wenqing Sect.

Li Qingyuan and the others seemed to see the shadow of a giant about to rise, their hearts filled with shock.

Such good fortune falling to Yang Kai naturally made a group of people jealous to the point of madness. Any one of these three Yao Kings joining their sect would bring endless benefits.

But what shocked everyone was that, faced with the earnest requests of the three Yao Kings, Yang Kai actually looked troubled and said, "Let’s discuss this later!"

Li Qingyuan and the others instantly widened their eyes, cursing Yang Kai for not knowing what was good for him. Others begged for such an opportunity, yet he wanted to discuss it further. Was there something wrong with his head?

What’s there to discuss? He should have agreed immediately.

Xi Lei and the others exchanged glances, understanding what Yang Kai was concerned about. Although, given his current status among the three Saint Venerables, asking the Wild Ancient Land for three Yao Kings to hold the fort was not a big deal, he clearly didn’t want to dig up others’ walls so recklessly—especially the walls of the three Saint Venerables.

At the very least, he should first inform Luan Feng and the others about this matter.

Realizing this, Xi Lei and the other three Yao Kings no longer pressed the issue.

After pondering for a while, Xi Lei said, "Young Master Yang, these people... should we kill them?"

He asked casually, as if he were talking about slaughtering a few thousand chickens or ducks, making everyone’s faces turn pale as they cast pleading looks at Yang Kai.

At this point, they knew their lives depended entirely on Yang Kai’s whim. If he wanted them to live, they would live; if he wanted them to die, it would be hard not to.

The dozen or so Emperor Realm leaders forced a flattering yet anxious smile at Yang Kai, fearing he might utter the word "kill."

"Kill? Why kill? There’s no grudge or enmity." Yang Kai waved his hand, smiling, "Didn’t you hear them say they came to congratulate me on founding my sect? Since they’re here to congratulate, they’re guests of Lingxiao Palace."

"Right, right! Young Master Yang is wise!" Li Qingyuan shouted repeatedly, a look of relief appearing on his face.

He knew that since Yang Kai had said this, their lives were safe, and he was deeply grateful for Yang Kai’s generosity.

Xi Lei nodded, "This old bull was too reckless." Then, turning sharply, he shouted at Li Qingyuan and the others, "You bunch of trash, since you’re here to congratulate, where are your gifts? Or are you just saying it?"

Li Qingyuan and the others were startled and hurriedly replied, "How could we dare? Of course, we have gifts!"

As they spoke, the dozen Emperor Realm leaders quickly searched their spatial rings, sometimes showing determination, sometimes hesitation.

In no time, a dozen spatial rings were gathered in Li Qingyuan’s hands. He nervously approached Yang Kai, forcing a smile, "Young Master Yang, these humble gifts are a token of respect. Please accept them."

Yang Kai took them casually, not bothering to check what was inside.

He was already wealthy and didn’t lack a dozen spatial rings like these.

He nodded slightly, "Sect Master Li, you’re thoughtful."

A hidden look of pain flashed across Li Qingyuan’s face. He had just handed over most of his years of savings, fearing Yang Kai would be dissatisfied.

The other Emperor Realm leaders were likely the same, otherwise they wouldn’t all look so distressed.

Who would have thought that once the gifts were handed over, they weren’t even looked at before being put away? Had he known, he would have just stuffed anything inside.

"Of course, of course, Young Master Yang... If there are no further instructions, I, Li Qingyuan, will take my leave." Having lost so much wealth, Li Qingyuan felt stifled and just wanted to get away from here as quickly as possible.

"Speaking of instructions, I do have one thing I need your help with." Yang Kai said kindly.

"Ah..." Li Qingyuan was startled, wiping sweat from his forehead and nervously asked, "What does Young Master Yang need?"

Yang Kai smiled, "Sect Master Li, don’t be so nervous. What I need you to do is simple. After leaving here, just help me spread the word in the Northern Territory: I’ve claimed this land and established Lingxiao Palace!"

Li Qingyuan relaxed, letting out a long breath, "So that’s all!"

Yang Kai looked at him meaningfully, "What did Sect Master Li think it was?"

Li Qingyuan replied awkwardly, "Nothing, nothing. It’s a small matter, leave it to me. Though my strength is lacking, I know some friends and will spread the news as quickly as possible, so no petty thieves come to bother your palace."

"That’s good." Yang Kai smiled and nodded, then looked at the other Emperor Realm leaders, "I hope you all can help as well."

The Emperor Realm leaders dared not refuse, nodding and promising, each patting their chest in assurance.

Yang Kai smiled and cupped his fists, "Since you are all heroes of the Northern Territory, when Lingxiao Palace officially opens its gates, I’ll send invitations. I hope you’ll come for a cup of celebratory wine."

Everyone felt bitter, knowing they’d have to give another big gift then, but outwardly they chatted with Yang Kai, promising to attend.

After a few more words, the group hurriedly escaped.

"A bunch of trash. Young Master Yang, why bother giving them face?" Xi Lei snorted coldly. Although he hadn’t seen the conflict between Yang Kai and the Emperor Realm leaders, he guessed some of the truth. With his temperament, he’d have just killed them all to make Lingxiao Palace’s name.

Yang Kai shook his head with a smile, "More friends, more paths. They acted out of greed, not enmity. No need to wipe them out. If Lingxiao Palace wants to stand in the Northern Territory, it’s not wise to make enemies everywhere. Remember, snakes have their path, mice have theirs. Someday, they might be useful."

Xi Lei immediately flattered, "Young Master Yang is wise. Old Bull didn’t think it through."

Yang Kai ignored the flattery, pretending not to hear, and raised his hand, "Follow me."

The three Yao Kings exchanged glances. Though they didn’t know what Yang Kai wanted, they obediently followed.

Soon, everyone arrived at the space array Yang Kai had just set up.

Yang Kai glanced at the three Yao Kings and said, “I must thank you all for your help this time. Without your intervention, Ice Heart Valley would not have escaped so easily. On behalf of Senior Bing Yun and the thousands of disciples of Ice Heart Valley, I offer you my gratitude.”

Xi Lei and the others were startled, "Young Master Yang, you’re exaggerating. It was nothing."

Yang Kai smiled, "It may be nothing to you, but to Ice Heart Valley, it was a lifesaving favor. It’s not the same." After a pause, he continued, "Now that things are settled here, you three should return to the Wild Ancient Land."

"Ah..." Yingfei looked disappointed, gazing at Yang Kai, "Young Master Yang, you’re sending us back already? We’ve only been out a few days."

"Yes, yes!" Xie Wuwei nodded vigorously, "Young Master Yang, if you have anything else for us, just say the word. The three of us will do it without hesitation."

Xi Lei eagerly suggested, "Young Master Yang, why not take us to sweep through the Northern Territory and unify it?"

Yingfei and Xie Wuwei cheered loudly, their yao yuan surging, as if Yang Kai’s command would send them to wreak havoc on the other sects of the Northern Territory.

Yang Kai chuckled, “Though you three are powerful, there’s still a difference between humans and yao. If you really stirred up a storm in the Northern Territory, it would only bring trouble. I know what you’re thinking, but there’s no need to rush. Who knows when the girl will come out of seclusion? Just wait patiently for news in the Ancient Land.”

Having their intentions exposed, the three Yao Kings looked embarrassed.

After all, it was because of Yang Kai’s relationship with Zhang Ruoxi that they were so loyal. Without Zhang Ruoxi, Yang Kai wouldn’t have such influence.

They knew Yang Kai spoke the truth. Though they wanted to stay close to him, the Blood Gate had vanished and remained hidden, and no one knew when Zhang Ruoxi would emerge. Following Yang Kai was pointless.

Still, going back felt unsatisfying. Who knew when Yang Kai would remember them again?

Xie Wuwei’s eyes rolled, "Let them go back. I’ll stay with you, Young Master Yang, to protect you. Heaven and earth are ruthless, treating all things as straw dogs. Traveling the Star Realm, one must be careful."

"Why should we leave and you stay?" Yingfei and Xi Lei asked in unison, clearly unhappy.

This guy wanted to hog the benefits—unforgivable.

Xie Wuwei snorted, "No reason."

Xi Lei shouted, "Come on, let’s fight! Whoever’s strongest stays. Let fists decide, not words. If we’re to protect Young Master Yang, only the strongest should stay!"

Among the thirty-two Yao Kings and eight Saint Envoys, his strength ranked in the top three. In a fight, Yingfei and Xie Wuwei were no match for him.

Xie Wuwei sneered, "This Lord disdains it."

Yingfei interjected, “Brother Xie, that’s not right. Even if someone must stay behind to protect Young Master Yang, it shouldn’t be you. In terms of strength, Brother Xi Lei is a cut above you. As for agility and speed, if I claim second place, no one dares claim first. So, this important task of being a bodyguard naturally falls to me.”

Xie Wuwei’s mouth twitched, snorting coldly, "Because I am Young Master Yang’s soul slave."

As he spoke, he suddenly activated his soul power. A faint golden light appeared in his mind, clearly a Divine Soul restriction branded in Xie Wuwei’s Sea of Consciousness, and from that restriction, Yang Kai’s aura was unmistakably present.

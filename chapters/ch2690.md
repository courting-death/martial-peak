## Chapter 2690 — Do You Dare Have Any Objections

The Gong family head glanced around and said, "Moreover, it seems there’s not just one Grand Square Five Elements Array here—there’s another formation linked within it."

"A formation within a formation!" The speaker’s face darkened. Even without being skilled in arrays, everyone knew what that meant. It was several times harder than simply setting up two separate formations.

"Even the Gong family head can’t see through it?" asked a middle-aged man with a pale, beardless face. He had thought that with the Gong family head present, breaking the formation would be a trivial matter. Unexpectedly, the Gong family head also looked troubled, which made everyone even more anxious.

The Gong family head shook his head. "It’s not that I can’t see through it, it’s just hidden too deeply."

"Then can the Gong family head break this formation?"

The Gong family head replied, "I can, but I’m afraid there isn’t enough time."

With his skills, if given two or three days, he could break such a formation. But in two or three days, the auction would already be over, and the formation would naturally be lifted—so why bother breaking it himself?

"Everyone, please remain calm."

Just then, a voice suddenly came from behind. Startled, everyone turned to see Wu Ming standing not far away, smiling at them.

"What exactly is your Qiyao Commerce Guild trying to do? Don’t you owe us an explanation?" The pale, beardless middle-aged man jumped out, glaring angrily at Wu Ming.

He still thought Wu Ming was the person in charge of the Qiyao Commerce Guild, since he had been the one welcoming guests at the door earlier.

The others also glared at Wu Ming with anger in their eyes.

Wu Ming smiled slightly. "You are all honored guests. What could I possibly do to you? It’s just that my master has instructed that no one may leave before the auction ends. Please, return to your rooms for now. Once the auction is over, my master will naturally remove the formation."

"You think you can force this Lord to stay if he doesn’t want to? I come and go as I please! Remove the formation at once and let me leave!"

Wu Ming looked at the speaker with a faint smile, saying nothing. He didn’t move, but suddenly, the Spiritual Qi in the surroundings surged. A five-colored blade appeared out of nowhere and shot toward the speaker like an arrow loosed from a bow.

Everyone was shocked and scrambled to dodge.

The Emperor Realm cultivator who was targeted turned pale and tried to defend himself with his Emperor Yuan. But under the five-colored blade, all his defenses were as fragile as paper. The blade pierced through his Emperor Yuan barrier and left a gaping hole in his chest.

Blood splattered everywhere.

The crowd fell silent, each person staring wide-eyed at the victim.

The man wasn’t dead yet. He looked down at his chest in disbelief, then looked up at Wu Ming, trembling. "You..."

He only managed to utter a single word before his body went limp and he collapsed to the ground.

Over a dozen Emperor Realm cultivators felt their scalps go numb. They couldn’t believe the Qiyao Commerce Guild would dare kill someone in their own branch. No one knew what that five-colored blade was, except the Gong family head. It wasn’t a human attack, but the power of a formation. The Grand Square Five Elements Array could not only guard the area, but also kill enemies.

Wu Ming was still smiling, but to everyone present, that smile was more terrifying than anything else in the world.

Earlier, Yang Kai had killed an old man from Room Seven in the auction hall, but at least he had thrown a punch and used a sword. Wu Ming, on the other hand, killed without even lifting a finger. Was this something a human could do?

Suddenly, they remembered he had mentioned his master before.

Wu Ming himself was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. For someone to be his master, wouldn’t they have to be at the Third Stage?

A disciple at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, a master at the Third Stage, and with the help of a grand formation—if a fight broke out, they could sweep everyone here. Only now did the crowd realize how dangerous the situation was.

"Does anyone else want to leave?" Wu Ming asked with a smile.

No one answered.

Wu Ming nodded. "Then please, go ahead."

With that, he stepped aside and gestured politely.

The crowd looked at each other, but no one dared to act rashly. The corpse was still warm—a clear warning. Though their faces were full of anger, they could only swallow it and return to their rooms.

"Gong family head," Wu Ming suddenly called out.

The Gong family head paused and turned. "Do you have instructions?"

Wu Ming smiled. "You’re too polite, Gong family head. Who would have thought a mere auction would bring you all this way? My master was quite surprised and would like to meet you."

"Your master is...?" The Gong family head asked with a frown.

Wu Ming smiled. "You’ll know when you meet him."

The Gong family head gave a bitter smile. "Can this old man refuse?"

He knew nothing good would come of it. He regretted speaking at such length about the formation earlier. He didn’t know what the purpose of the formation was, but as a formation expert, his presence clearly made the one who set it up wary.

Wu Ming’s eyes narrowed. "My master’s invitation is sincere. You’d best not decline."

Though his tone was calm, the threat was obvious to all.

The Gong family head sighed, knowing he couldn’t resist, and said, "Lead the way."

He was also curious who was behind all this and what their goal was.

"Please!" Wu Ming turned, but before he could take a step, his body suddenly stiffened and he stared ahead with wide eyes.

Not far ahead, Yang Kai had appeared at some point, smiling at him.

Wu Ming, who had just been beaten up by this young man at the auction entrance, was both fearful and wary. His Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivation was nothing in Yang Kai’s eyes.

Seeing Yang Kai blocking his path, Wu Ming instinctively jumped several zhang back and shouted, "Yang Kai, what are you doing!"

Yang Kai glanced at him, then around the area, and nodded. "A Grand Square Five Elements Array, guarding the heaven and earth. Looks like you’ve made plenty of preparations to deal with me."

Wu Ming’s face darkened. He realized that the Gong family head’s earlier words had been overheard by Yang Kai.

Indeed, his master had set up the Grand Square Five Elements Array specifically to deal with Yang Kai. After all, Yang Kai was skilled in the Power of Space. Without sealing the area, he could teleport away at any moment. Only by turning this place into a cage could they restrict his movements.

Exposed by Yang Kai, Wu Ming didn’t bother to hide it. He just snorted coldly. "I warn you, you’d better behave, or you’ll regret it."

Yang Kai sneered. "Whether I regret it or not is none of your business. But if you act up again, I guarantee you won’t live to see that day."

Wu Ming’s face turned cold, but he really didn’t dare act up again. He had seen Yang Kai’s strength—he was no match in a one-on-one fight. He could only glare at Yang Kai with hatred.

"Gong family head? I’ve heard much about you." Yang Kai cupped his fists toward the old man surnamed Gong.

"You’re Yang Kai?" The Gong family head looked him up and down in amazement.

Yang Kai frowned. "You’ve heard of me?"

The Gong family head replied, "I heard about you from Wenshan. Back then, you refined the Supreme Mystic Pill in the Four Seasons Land, which caused quite a stir in the Southern Territory."

"Gong Wenshan!" A vague figure flashed through Yang Kai’s mind. He didn’t have much of a relationship with Gong Wenshan—just a brief encounter in the Four Seasons Land.

Back then, the reason Ye Qinghan had gone to Fenglin City to seek Yang Kai’s help in opening Emperor Heaven Valley was precisely because Gong Wenshan had recommended him.

"Young Master Yang, you’re truly outstanding. You’ve already advanced to the Emperor Realm, while Wenshan is still struggling in the Dao Origin Realm. The younger generation really is impressive." The Gong family head sighed, thinking Yang Kai’s rapid advancement was probably related to the Supreme Mystic Pill.

Back then, he had refined several Supreme Mystic Pills and later sold them. It was said that Wuchang from Tianwu Sacred Land got one, and a disciple from the Eight Directions Sect got another. Now, Wuchang from Tianwu Sacred Land had already advanced to the Emperor Realm. The disciple from the Eight Directions Sect hadn’t yet, but was favored by many top sects. Several sects had extended invitations, and as soon as she agreed, she’d become a Core Disciple.

All of this was thanks to the Supreme Mystic Pill.

"You flatter me, Gong family head. I was just lucky." Yang Kai smiled modestly. "I’d like to invite you to my room for a chat. What do you think?"

The Gong family head looked grateful, knowing Yang Kai was helping him out of a difficult situation. Still, he hesitated and glanced at Wu Ming.

Yang Kai’s face darkened. "If the Gong family head comes to my place, do you dare have any objections?"

Wu Ming was furious but didn’t dare answer.

"Looks like you have no objections!" Yang Kai curled his lip and said to the Gong family head, "Please!"

The Gong family head relaxed and gestured, "After you, Young Master Yang!"

The two of them headed toward the auction hall. Before leaving, Yang Kai turned to Wu Ming and said, "Go tell your master that I’ll smash his head in. He’d better be ready."

Hearing this, the Gong family head nearly stumbled, thinking, Young people really are bold. A First Stage Emperor dares to challenge a Third Stage like this.

Watching the two walk away, Wu Ming’s face twisted with venomous resentment. Let him be arrogant for now—soon enough, he'll have him on his knees, begging for mercy.

Bringing the Gong family head back to Room One, Yang Kai introduced him to Yingfei, Ye Hen, and the others. After some polite conversation, they all exchanged greetings.

Still, the Gong family head was deeply grateful to Yang Kai for taking him away.

Yang Kai waved his hand. "No need to thank me, Gong family head. I don’t know what Wu Ming wanted you for, but it was definitely to use you against me. Bringing you here was just a way to protect myself."

Yang Kai knew better than to underestimate a grandmaster of arrays. If the Gong family head truly joined in controlling the formation, its power would increase by at least thirty percent. That was precisely why Yang Kai had acted so decisively—after all, Chi Yue, Ai Ou, and the others were still in Wu Ming’s hands. If he angered the master behind Wu Ming, nothing good would come of it.

## Chapter 2697 — All Under Control

Anyone who could cultivate to the Emperor Realm was no fool; they naturally knew how to assess the situation. None of them wanted to get involved in the grudge between Yang Kai and Tan Junhao.

Someone immediately cupped his fists and said, "Elder Tan, I have urgent matters to attend to. Please excuse me."

With that, he turned and left.

The others exchanged glances, then also stepped forward to take their leave. In the blink of an eye, more than half had scattered, leaving only a few behind, their brows furrowed in hesitation.

It seemed they had realized this trip was more misfortune than blessing. Since Tan Junhao had summoned them here for no reason and set up an array in advance, it was clear he had no intention of letting them leave easily. If they tried to leave now, they would likely offend him.

For a moment, they were filled with regret, thinking they should never have come to Flowing Shadow City for the auction. Now, inexplicably caught up in this mess, even hoping to withdraw was a luxury.

Sure enough, as the Emperor Realm cultivators scattered, Tan Junhao snorted and called out, "Do you all really think you can just walk away?"

The Emperor Realm cultivators stopped in their tracks and turned to look at Tan Junhao. The one who had spoken earlier frowned and asked, "Elder Tan, what do you mean by this?"

There was a hint of questioning in his tone. Even though Tan Junhao was an Elder of Star God Palace, with high status and great strength, he had no reason to restrict the freedom of so many Emperor Realm cultivators. His grudge was with Yang Kai, not with them. But from Tan Junhao's words, something seemed off.

Tan Junhao replied coolly, "It's nothing much. It's just that this place is now sealed by a grand array I set up. Without my permission, no one is leaving."

At these words, everyone's faces changed dramatically.

The man who had spoken earlier frowned and said, "Elder Tan, we respect you as an Elder of Star God Palace, and Star God Palace is the overlord of the Southern Territory, sheltering countless cultivators. Please, give us some guidance—what must we do to leave here?"

If anyone else had done this, the Emperor Realm cultivators present would have turned hostile immediately. To have their freedom restricted for no reason—who could tolerate that? But Tan Junhao was different. Not only was he a Third Stage Emperor, but also an Elder of Star God Palace. No one dared act rashly in front of him. So, though this man was angry, he had to suppress his fury and ask for clarification.

"Indeed, Elder Tan, please advise us," someone quickly echoed, cupping his fists.

Tan Junhao smiled faintly. "If you want to leave, it's simple." He pointed at Yang Kai. "Help me kill him, and I'll let you all go."

Everyone's eyes widened in shock, as if they'd just swallowed a hundred catties of bitter herbs.

They had all witnessed Yang Kai's earlier rampages and knew this young man was exceptionally powerful and not to be trifled with. None of the Emperor Realm cultivators present had any confidence they could survive a fight with him.

Now Tan Junhao wanted them to deal with Yang Kai? Wasn't that just pushing them into the fire?

Was this guy really an Elder of Star God Palace? In that instant, the lofty image of Star God Palace collapsed in their hearts. They couldn't be more disappointed.

Star God Palace was the overlord sect of the Southern Territory. If all its Elders acted so unreasonably, using their power to oppress others, who could still respect them?

"Elder Tan... surely you're joking? We have no enmity with this young brother..."

"Yes, Elder Tan, please show mercy and let us go."

For a moment, the crowd was in an uproar. But facing Tan Junhao, they still restrained their anger, not daring to be too rude.

Tan Junhao remained unmoved. He declared loudly, "This brat has shown me the utmost disrespect—not only did he insult me, he injured my disciple and even stole a precious treasure from me. If you can subdue him for me, I will reward you handsomely."

"So that's how it is. Then he really is in the wrong."

"Indeed, Elder Tan is an Elder of Star God Palace. To insult him and steal his treasure is truly outrageous."

"He must apologize. He absolutely must apologize."

The crowd made a noisy show of agreement, but in truth, none of them meant it. No one was foolish enough to risk their life for Tan Junhao's empty promise by going after Yang Kai.

Yang Kai grinned and called out, “Tan Junhao, you’re an Elder of Star God Palace, your cultivation is two Stages higher than mine, yet you still need to rally others to deal with me? Have you no shame? If you have any guts, don’t drag innocent people into this—come fight me one-on-one!”

At these words, the crowd's opinion of Yang Kai soared. They felt he was absolutely right.

Why should their grudge drag in innocent bystanders? This Elder Tan was truly unreasonable.

Tan Junhao's face darkened. He glared hatefully at Yang Kai, then swept his gaze over the crowd. "So, none of you are willing to help me?"

The crowd looked awkward, their eyes shifting, but no one responded.

The man who had spoken first said indignantly, "Elder Tan, please don't make things difficult for us. In this world, reason comes before all else. Your grudge with this young brother should be settled between the two of you. With your cultivation, taking him down should be easy—why do you need our help?"

This man, with a full beard, looked bold and forthright, clearly not the scheming type. Tan Junhao's actions had thoroughly disgusted him, and his words were laced with barbs.

Tan Junhao shot him a cold look and suddenly formed a hand seal with one hand.

The bearded man let out a miserable scream, as if suffering a terrible injury. His whole body trembled violently, and a bulge appeared on his neck, quickly moving beneath his skin.

"What's happening!" The crowd was shocked, jumping away from the bearded man for fear of being caught up in the disaster.

"Brother Liu! Brother Liu!" Some cultivators who knew the bearded man well shouted loudly.

But this Liu fellow seemed not to hear, only screaming in agony. Soon, his face turned pale and his body was drenched in sweat.

Rip—

Suddenly, he tore open his shirt, his bloodshot eyes wide as he looked down at his chest. He saw the bulge under his skin rapidly burrowing toward his heart.

"What... what is this?"

Gritting his teeth, Liu glared at Tan Junhao. "You... what did you do to me?"

"Heart-Controlling Gu!" Yang Kai's expression changed as he shouted in a low voice.

Tan Junhao's hand seal didn't change. He said coldly, "This is what happens to those who defy me!" He paused, then added, "Oh, and don't even think about circulating your Emperor Yuan, or you'll only die faster."

But it was too late. Sensing something was wrong, Liu had already tried to circulate his Emperor Yuan. As Tan Junhao said, the more he did, the faster the Heart-Controlling Gu moved, and in a flash, it burrowed into his chest.

Agonizing pain shot through his entire body!

With a furious roar, Liu brought his fingers together and stabbed them into his chest. Blood spurted everywhere—he seemed to be trying to dig the foreign object out of his body.

But before he could dig it out, there was a faint crack—his heart vein snapped. His eyes bulged wide, and he collapsed stiffly to the ground, dead.

"Hiss..." The crowd was horrified, gasping as their hands and feet turned cold.

None of them had seen Tan Junhao make any obvious move—just a hand seal, and Liu was already under the Heart-Controlling Gu's control. Clearly, the Gu had been planted in him long ago.

If Liu could be killed so easily, what about themselves?

The answer came quickly. Tan Junhao changed his hand seal again, his gaze sweeping the room.

More than twenty Emperor Realm cultivators all groaned at once. A small bulge appeared under each person's skin, rapidly moving toward their chests.

"Elder Tan, you..."

"When did this happen?"

None of the Emperor Realm cultivators could believe it—they had no idea when they'd been infected by the Heart-Controlling Gu. Thinking back to every detail since entering the auction, someone suddenly realized, "The tea..."

Tan Junhao nodded slightly. "That's right. I tampered with the tea."

Yang Kai was startled as well, but at the same time felt a surge of relief. Thankfully, he had been cautious before coming here—when he was in the private room, it was Ye Qinghan herself who brought out her own tea set and leaves to brew their tea. Otherwise, he would likely have fallen victim to Tan Junhao’s Heart-Controlling Gu just like the others, becoming a pawn at his mercy.

The Heart-Controlling Gu was so insidious that even Emperor Realm cultivators couldn't detect it. It seemed Tan Junhao had been cultivating these Gu for years, or they wouldn't be so powerful or so numerous.

The old bastard was truly cunning. Even with his Third Stage Emperor cultivation, he still resorted to such tricks to deal with Yang Kai.

"Elder Tan, you're insane!" someone shouted, eyes red with rage.

There were more than twenty Emperor Realm cultivators present, hailing from every corner of the Southern Territory. If word of today’s events were to spread, Star God Palace would be the first to hunt Tan Junhao down to preserve their reputation. To use such sinister means against so many Emperor Realm experts of the Southern Territory—how could Radiant Moon Great Emperor possibly tolerate him tarnishing the name of Star God Palace?

But then again, with the Heart-Controlling Gu inside them, who would dare spread the news?

After today, they were destined to become Tan Junhao's slaves, forced to obey his every command.

At this thought, everyone felt a deep sorrow. Their hearts were full of unwillingness and anger, but there was nowhere to vent it.

Little did they know, Tan Junhao would stop at nothing to get what Yang Kai had. As long as he could obtain the Everlasting Tree, he could leave Star God Palace and find a place to refine it. Once he achieved immortality, what could Radiant Moon Great Emperor do to him?

By then, even if everything came to light, he wouldn't care in the slightest.

With this plan in mind, Tan Junhao had no hesitation in using the Heart-Controlling Gu, even killing the bearded man as a warning to the others.

"Whoever helps me kill him will be greatly rewarded!" Tan Junhao pointed at Yang Kai, having dropped all pretense. He no longer cared about appearances and began ordering the Emperor Realm cultivators.

The crowd looked at each other in misery.

## Chapter 2793 — Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda

Lei Ting gave a soft snort but still released his grip. He said coolly, "I heard you fought that guy before, but didn’t come out ahead?"

Xiao Chen asked gravely in return, "You just fought him yourself. Can’t you tell how strong he is?"

Lei Ting’s expression grew solemn. After thinking seriously for a moment, he nodded, "That man is a formidable opponent!"

Xiao Chen said, "What’s even more infuriating is that Xun’er seems to look at him differently. So, Lei Ting, let’s put our issues aside for now. The right thing is to join forces and drive that man away from Xun’er!"

Lei Ting sneered, "Join forces? Why would I need to join forces with you? What a joke."

Xiao Chen said, "Do you even dare speak to Xun’er? Do you dare look her in the eyes?"

Lei Ting’s entire aura suddenly collapsed. After hesitating for a while, he muttered resentfully, "Who doesn’t have a weakness?"

Xiao Chen sneered, "You don’t even have the courage to look at Xun’er or speak to her. If she sides with him, what will you do then?"

"Then what do you suggest?" Lei Ting asked, thoroughly annoyed.

Xiao Chen smiled slightly, a look of confidence on his face. "In a few days, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda will open. Both of us are definitely going in. It’ll last a month—are you afraid we won’t find a chance? Once we do, we’ll give him a lesson he’ll never forget. He’ll lose all face then."

"Teaming up to beat him up?" Lei Ting hesitated a bit. "Isn’t that improper? Ganging up on someone isn’t what people like us should do."

Xiao Chen said, "Or you can challenge him one-on-one. I’ll keep Xun’er occupied so she won’t interfere."

Hearing this, Lei Ting thought it over seriously, then nodded, "Mm, that’s much better." He gave Xiao Chen’s shoulder a heavy pat. "Then I’ll leave Xun’er to you, and I’ll handle that guy!"

"So you’re agreeing to work with me?" Xiao Chen revealed a faint, hidden smile.

"Alright, we'll work together. Since you, Junior Brother, have already said so, as Senior Brother I naturally have to cooperate." Lei Ting laughed heartily, clearly in high spirits.

Xiao Chen’s face darkened. “I can’t be bothered to argue with you about who’s Senior Brother and who’s Junior Brother…”

As long as I can stay by Her Highness’s side, there will always be a chance to get close. As for you, idiot, just focus on driving out the outsider—leave the rest to me.

After settling their plans, the two parted ways to rest and recover.

…

Ever since that night when Lei Ting came looking for trouble, no one else had come to South Dipper Peak.

The entire Star God Palace seemed busy preparing to open the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda. Even Lan Xun hadn’t come to find Yang Kai.

With a few rare days of peace, Yang Kai secluded himself on South Dipper Peak to cultivate, and took the opportunity to refine the three Dragon Blood Pills Ji Ying had made for him.

The Dragon Blood Pills were truly extraordinary. After refining all three, Yang Kai clearly felt his physical body improve a bit. Although it wasn’t much, at his level, every bit of progress was precious.

If he could get more Dragon Blood Pills, perhaps his body could reach an even higher level.

Unfortunately, there were only three.

As for the effect Ji Ying mentioned about the Dragon Blood Pills purifying Dragon Clan bloodlines, Yang Kai didn’t feel anything. After thinking it over, he realized he wasn’t truly of the Dragon Clan—he’d only obtained the Golden Sacred Dragon’s Origin Power. Naturally, the effect of purifying Dragon Clan bloodlines had nothing to do with him.

Five days later, just as Yang Kai finished refining the third Dragon Blood Pill and was still immersed in the joy of his improved physique, he sensed someone flying over outside.

A moment later, Lan Xun’s voice reached his ears: "Senior Brother Yang!"

Yang Kai got up and walked out of the hall.

Lan Xun said, "The Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda opens today. Are you ready, Senior Brother Yang?"

"There’s nothing to prepare!"

"Then come with me." Lan Xun said, taking the lead.

The Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was one of the most important training grounds in the Star God Palace—a vast secret realm. From the Void King Realm to the Emperor Realm, cultivators of all levels could train there and gain unimaginable benefits.

It was said that the spaces sealed within the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda were fragments of shattered worlds. Thus, cultivators inside could easily comprehend the laws of heaven and earth. Even though these were incomplete laws, they were easier to grasp and had miraculous effects on cultivation.

The existence of the Divine Roaming Mirror allowed the experts of Qingyang Shrine to surpass their peers in Divine Soul power, and likewise, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda enabled the Star God Palace’s disciples to far outstrip others in their understanding of the laws of heaven and earth.

The deeper one’s comprehension of the laws of heaven and earth, the purer one’s cultivation, and the easier it was to progress.

In short, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was the foundation of the Star God Palace.

However, even in the Star God Palace, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda couldn’t be opened casually. Each opening required enormous manpower and resources—not only did several Great Elders have to act together, but the palace also consumed countless heaven-and-earth treasures.

Normally, the Star God Palace would only open the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda once every three years, carefully selecting disciples to enter each time.

The number of entrants was also limited. Disciples of all levels in the palace had to compete in a grand martial contest held half a year in advance, and only those who placed high enough qualified to enter the pagoda.

This time, the opening of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was originally scheduled for half a year later.

But because of Yang Kai, it had to be brought forward.

Of course, if the Star God Palace insisted on waiting half a year, Yang Kai would have had no choice but to wait. But as the anchor of the Southern Territory, the palace always kept its word and would never do anything to damage its reputation.

This early opening meant the Star God Palace had to spend even more manpower and resources.

Yang Kai had entered the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda once before. Back when he first arrived at Fenglin City, Xiao Yuyang had remotely opened the entrance, allowing all the Void King Realm cultivators in Fenglin City to enter and train.

Yang Kai had gained a lot from that experience, and the memory of that wondrous secret realm was still vivid.

The reason he chose to train in the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda for a month instead of asking the Great Emperor three questions was precisely because of that previous experience.

The pagoda was filled with countless broken laws of heaven and earth, which greatly aided his cultivation and also helped perfect the Xuan Realm Pearl.

Although the Xuan Realm Pearl was a world of its own, its laws of heaven and earth had never been complete. Yang Kai had possessed it for years but never had a chance to repair it. Last time in the pagoda, the Xuan Realm Pearl absorbed many broken laws, making the Small Xuan Realm’s world much more complete.

Later, in the Shattered Star Sea, the Xuan Realm Pearl absorbed a large amount of broken origin power, becoming even closer to perfection.

If this time he could absorb enough broken laws in the pagoda—or even complete laws—the Xuan Realm Pearl might finally become truly perfect.

A perfect Xuan Realm Pearl would be a true world, no different from the Star Realm itself, and might bring unimaginable benefits!

Compared to asking the Great Emperor three questions, Yang Kai preferred to give the Xuan Realm Pearl this opportunity.

This would be of unimaginable significance for his future comprehension of the Great Might of Heaven and Earth and his quest to reach the Great Emperor Realm.

Last time in Fenglin City, Xiao Yuyang and several Emperor Realm masters had only opened a remote entrance to the pagoda. Yang Kai hadn’t seen the pagoda’s true form.

But this time, he was fortunate enough to witness it.

The Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was truly a tower—soaring into the clouds, radiating five-colored light, and exuding an incredibly mysterious aura.

When Lan Xun led Yang Kai to the site, they found that more than a thousand people had already gathered near the pagoda.

Among these thousand people were cultivators from the Origin Returning Realm to the Emperor Realm. Regardless of their cultivation, each one’s aura was steady—they were clearly the future pillars of the Star God Palace.

These thousand people were the disciples chosen to enter the pagoda for training this time.

When the two arrived, over a thousand pairs of eyes turned their way. Every Star God Palace disciple who looked at Lan Xun, male or female, showed infatuation. But when they looked at Yang Kai, their gazes were filled with envy, jealousy, and resentment—a stark contrast.

Yang Kai didn’t care about the stares from these thousand people, but there were a few sharp, piercing gazes that he had to take seriously.

In front of the pagoda, four people stood side by side. Their auras were as deep as the sea, like four towering pillars.

These four were the current Four Elders of the Star God Palace.

Among them, Yang Kai recognized Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao. The other two were both elderly men—one with a ruddy face and a burly build, the other with white hair and a spry figure.

Xiao Yuyang nodded slightly to Yang Kai, while Xue Zhengmao, who had suffered at Yang Kai’s hands before, just snorted coldly.

"Is this Yang Kai?" The ruddy-faced, burly elder looked at Yang Kai and asked.

Xiao Yuyang said, "Yes, that’s Yang Kai!"

"He’s certainly a dragon among men, but with only First Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, how could he have injured Junior Brother Xue?" The burly elder frowned.

Xue Zhengmao looked a bit awkward and said, "That brat is skilled in space divine abilities. I was caught off guard and he managed to land a sneak attack."

After all, he was a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert. Being injured by a junior like Yang Kai was a blow to his pride.

"Even if it was a sneak attack, it is still impressive," the burly elder nodded slightly.

"Great Elder, you’re giving him too much credit," Xue Zhengmao said indignantly. "If we really fought, I could take his life within three moves."

The burly elder was none other than Lei Hong, the Great Elder of the Star God Palace, known as the number one Emperor in the Southern Territory.

Lei Hong chuckled, "It’s not that I overestimate him, but have you forgotten how Tan Junhao died?"

Xue Zhengmao snorted, "Didn’t the reports say that brat had a Yao King at his side? Tan Junhao must have died at the hands of that Yao King—what does it have to do with him!"

Lei Hong shook his head and said nothing more, knowing Xue Zhengmao had a grudge against Yang Kai. Any further talk might just make him lose his temper. They were all fellow elders of the Palace—no need to provoke him further.

Still, it had been a long time since he’d seen a young man capable of injuring a Third Stage Emperor Realm master.

Xiao Yuyang glanced at Yang Kai, then at Xiao Chen, and sighed deeply.

Although he felt his own son was quite outstanding, compared to Yang Kai, he seemed to fall a bit short.

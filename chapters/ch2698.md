## Chapter 2698 — How to Kill?

Their side certainly had the advantage in numbers, but if it really came down to a life-and-death struggle with Yang Kai, it was questionable whether even half of them would survive.

No one was willing to risk their own life just to give someone else a chance at survival.

But the outcome of disobeying Tan Junhao’s orders was undoubtedly the same as that Liu fellow—death.

For a moment, everyone was caught in a dilemma, unable to advance or retreat.

Tan Junhao naturally saw through their hesitation. He snorted coldly, and his hand seals changed once more.

Again, muffled groans rang out. Every Emperor Realm expert wore a pained expression, cold sweat pouring down their foreheads as the Heart-Controlling Gu burrowed rapidly toward their hearts. A few couldn’t bear the pain and let out miserable, blood-curdling screams that sent chills down everyone’s spine.

“Once the Gu reach your heart, you’ll die without a doubt!” Tan Junhao said indifferently. “Whoever helps this old man kill that brat, I’ll expel the Heart-Controlling Gu from their body and grant them freedom.”

The carrot-and-stick approach had always been the best way to enslave people’s hearts. As an elder of the Star God Palace, Tan Junhao was naturally well-versed in this method.

As soon as he finished speaking, many people’s eyes lit up. This Heart-Controlling Gu was simply too torturous—if there was a chance to get rid of it, it was worth a shot. Most importantly, resisting Tan Junhao meant certain death, but facing Yang Kai at least offered a sliver of hope.

Such a simple choice, yet it left them with no choice at all.

Unable to endure the pain and tempted by Tan Junhao’s promise, someone shouted, “Elder Tan, you must keep your word!”

Tan Junhao smiled faintly. “Of course this old man keeps his word.”

“Good! Then today, I, Xia, will lend Elder Tan a hand. Who else is willing to stand with me?”

As he spoke, he looked around. The many Emperor Realm experts all nodded—at this point, the arm couldn’t twist the thigh. What else could they do but agree?

With the support of the crowd, Xia’s confidence soared. He gathered his momentum, surging his Emperor Yuan, revealing himself as a formidable Second Stage Emperor Realm expert.

He let out a great shout, seemingly to bolster his own courage, and charged straight at Yang Kai.

The others followed close behind.

In an instant, twenty or thirty figures turned into streaks of light, all attacking Yang Kai. In midair, secret techniques blossomed with radiance, and one Imperial Treasure after another was summoned, transforming into multicolored beams that slashed toward Yang Kai.

Some, as they attacked, muttered, “Young Brother, don’t blame us—we have no choice.”

Suddenly facing so many Emperor Realm experts, Yang Kai felt the pressure surge, as if a mountain pressed down on his chest, making it hard to breathe. Yet he threw his head back and roared, “Hesitating and wavering—what kind of Emperors are you? If you lack the courage to stake your life, then don’t fight at all!”

“Arrogant brat, die!”

Not everyone felt guilty toward Yang Kai. His actions at the auction had already angered many, and now, because of him, they were under Tan Junhao’s control. With the Heart-Controlling Gu in their bodies, they dared not resent Tan Junhao, so all their fury was vented on Yang Kai. They believed that as long as they killed him, everything would be resolved.

They attacked without the slightest mercy.

Boom, boom, boom…

In an instant, twenty or thirty ferocious attacks enveloped Yang Kai’s position. Each strike was a powerful Emperor Realm technique, countless divine abilities erupting at once, plunging the scene into utter chaos.

Tan Junhao’s eyes narrowed. Even with his own cultivation, he knew that facing such a barrage would leave him gravely injured, if not dead on the spot. But this Yang brat was skilled in the Power of Space—no matter how fierce the assault, it might not be enough to truly threaten him.

Although he had sealed this area with the Grand Square Five Elements Array, it only prevented Yang Kai from teleporting outside the formation. Within it, Yang Kai could still move freely.

If not for that, he wouldn’t have needed to borrow the strength of these Emperor Realm experts. He’d have had the confidence to take Yang Kai down himself.

Soon, the dust settled. At the center of all those attacks, there was no one to be seen.

“Is he dead?” someone exclaimed.

“With so many of us attacking, even if that brat had three heads and six arms, he couldn’t have survived. What a pity—a true prodigy, gone.”

“Hahaha! Daring to oppose Elder Tan—he overestimated himself. He deserved to die!” someone laughed.

Since Yang Kai was dead, now was the time to flatter Tan Junhao. Maybe if he was in a good mood, he’d remove the Heart-Controlling Gu and grant them freedom.

Many also felt regret. If they’d known Yang Kai would be so easy to deal with, they would have gone all out earlier—maybe they could have earned a bit of Tan Junhao’s favor.

Pfft…

A strange sound rang out—faint, but every Emperor Realm expert present had sharp ears and heard it clearly. They all turned toward the source, and when they saw what had happened, their faces changed dramatically.

The last person who had spoken now had a broad sword tip protruding from his chest. A ghostly figure stood behind him, revealing a cold, sharp profile.

“Uh…” The man seemed to feel the chill in his chest. He looked down, his eyes going wide.

“If you want to be my enemy, you’d best be ready to die.” Yang Kai’s calm voice came from behind, but to that man, it sounded like a thunderclap, shaking his very soul.

Before he could react, Yang Kai twisted his wrist. With a sickening sound, he split the man in two—blood and viscera raining down from the sky, making everyone’s heart pound with fear.

A First Stage Emperor Realm expert had been killed in an instant by a sneak attack. What was even more terrifying was that no one had noticed how Yang Kai had escaped or struck.

Was their opponent really someone so ghost-like and unpredictable? Everyone felt a chill in their hearts.

“Did you all forget? That brat is skilled in the Power of Space!” Tan Junhao’s voice rang out from the side.

“What? Power of Space?”

“Was that just now a teleport?”

“I’ve heard that those who master the Power of Space can come and go without a trace, excelling at both escape and pursuit. How are we supposed to kill someone like that?”

…

Hearing Tan Junhao say that Yang Kai was skilled in the Power of Space, everyone’s expressions changed again. In their hearts, they cursed Tan Junhao—why hadn’t he mentioned such an important detail earlier? Why wait until after things turned ugly?

Yang Kai was already strong enough. If not for the Heart-Controlling Gu, none of them would have obeyed Tan Junhao’s orders and become his enemies. Now, learning that Yang Kai could even teleport, their faces turned ashen, and they felt utterly hopeless about killing him.

“Don’t worry. This old man has made thorough preparations and will lend you all a hand,” Tan Junhao said leisurely.

Clearly, everyone was helping him, but from his mouth, it sounded like he was doing them a favor.

Yang Kai hefted the Myriad Sword, pointed it at Tan Junhao with a mocking smile, then swept his gaze over the Emperor Realm experts and shouted, “Don’t take it personally—every last one of you here is trash! I held back just now out of consideration for your predicament, but if you keep pushing your luck, it won’t be as simple as killing just one of you next time.”

His arrogance infuriated many, but they knew he was right—he really had held back. With his ghostly skills, he could have killed three or five people in an instant.

It seemed he truly didn’t want to start a massacre and invite more trouble.

“Get out of my way. Once I deal with that old dog Tan, you’ll all regain your freedom,” Yang Kai shouted.

The crowd was moved.

Tan Junhao said in a deep voice, “Arrogant brat, do you really think you can kill this old man at will… hmm?”

He hadn’t finished speaking when his face suddenly darkened. With a sweep of his sleeve, he sent a wave of light forward.

Yang Kai suddenly appeared in front of him, slashing down with his sword. The blade struck the light, shattering it, but Yang Kai himself was knocked back by a powerful force.

“What are you all waiting for? Attack!” Tan Junhao roared.

Even Tan Junhao was wary of Yang Kai’s skills—if he hadn’t been on guard, he might have been killed just now. Seeing the Emperor Realm experts hesitating, he couldn’t help but urge them on.

Hearing this, the crowd, though reluctant, could only grit their teeth and charge.

But after the previous lesson, this time everyone kept seventy percent of their strength in reserve to guard themselves, lest Yang Kai catch them off guard again.

With a rumble, secret techniques and Imperial Treasures once again erupted in dazzling light, bombarding Yang Kai.

Yang Kai’s figure flickered as he tried to leave his original spot.

But just then, Tan Junhao let out a sinister laugh. A formation flag suddenly appeared in his hand, and he waved it fiercely at Yang Kai.

In the void, a shocking force erupted, instantly forcing Yang Kai’s vanishing figure back into view.

Before he could steady himself, all those attacks arrived at once.

Yang Kai was shocked. The Undying Five Elements Sword Qi surged forth.

Even though the Emperor Realm experts weren’t attacking with full force, there were still twenty or thirty strikes. If he took them all head-on, it would be disastrous. He hadn’t expected Tan Junhao’s formation to counter his space abilities so effectively—he’d suffered a huge loss. Now, it was too late to dodge, so he could only defend and evade as best he could.

The attacks came as expected, almost simultaneously, battering his Undying Five Elements Sword Qi until it churned and dimmed. Waves of force struck his body, shaking his organs and making his Qi and blood surge.

Seeing that the Undying Five Elements Sword Qi was about to collapse, Yang Kai gritted his teeth and growled, “Dragon Transformation!”

A flash of golden light appeared behind him, forming the image of a giant dragon. With a series of cracking sounds, dragon scales emerged on his skin, and his hands transformed into dragon claws, greatly increasing his physical defense.

Boom, boom, boom…

The remaining attacks kept coming, sending Yang Kai staggering left and right, battered and miserable.

Pfft…

A mouthful of golden blood sprayed from Yang Kai’s lips. Even with all his strength poured into defense, he couldn’t withstand so many attacks—his body had already suffered injuries.

“We… we hit him.” Someone muttered, staring blankly at the scene.

Most people couldn’t believe it. They hadn’t expected their attacks to actually land on Yang Kai. His ghostly movements had left them with no confidence, but this time, not only had they hit him, they’d even wounded him.

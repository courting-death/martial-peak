## Chapter 2829 — Saving Lives

In an instant, the three Great Shaman Masters were locked in fierce combat, their battlefield rapidly ascending until it reached the clouds, vanishing from sight.

Rumble...

The three hundred warriors of the Raging Flame Tribe and Gale Tribe charged in together, their iron-shod feet trampling the stone fortress’s defenses into chaos. Agile archers vaulted atop the stone walls, drawing their bows and loosing arrows in rapid succession. The sharp whistling of arrows filled the air as one Bone-Eater Tribe warrior after another was struck down.

A coordinated surprise attack from inside and out caught the Bone-Eater Tribe completely off guard. The Bone-Eater Tribe members, just startled awake from their feast and sleep, couldn’t even muster their usual fighting strength, and in an instant, dozens were killed or wounded.

However, after a brief moment of shock, the bloodthirsty and savage Bone-Eater Tribe finally regained their senses. Nearly twenty shamans scattered among the tribe began chanting incantations, bestowing various empowering Shamanic Arts upon their tribesmen.

Two groups of barbarians, each numbering in the hundreds, clashed fiercely. One side, protected by their own archers, fought fearlessly, while the other, driven by the scent of blood, roared and collided with them.

Even the earth was wailing.

The battlefield instantly became a massive meat grinder, blood splattering and flesh torn apart, as one barbarian after another fell.

Yang Kai withdrew his gaze from the clouds, glanced at the chaotic and savage battle ahead, frowned slightly, and in a flash appeared above the gathering Bone-Eater Tribe members.

Myriad Sword hummed loudly, a sword light shooting out from its tip.

Without a sound, as the light flashed, a long blank strip appeared among the clustered Bone-Eater Tribe members. Those originally in that position were instantly reduced to dust and vanished.

The noisy battlefield suddenly fell silent for a moment, everyone raising their heads to look up.

The Bone-Eater Tribe members’ eyes were filled with terror, while the Raging Flame Tribe and Gale Tribe were filled with delight. No one expected that this seemingly frail figure would display such divine might with a single move.

With a whoosh, a streak of blood shot up from below, transforming into a blood snake that lunged at Yang Kai.

This was a Shamanic Art of the Bone-Eater Tribe—sinister and difficult to guard against. As the blood snake’s jaws opened wide, a foul, nauseating stench poured forth, clearly laced with deadly poison. If one were truly bitten by it, the consequences would be dire.

Yang Kai didn’t even look at it. Amid the shocked gazes of the Raging Flame Tribe and Gale Tribe, he casually raised his hand, grasped the blood snake in midair, and squeezed hard. The blood snake splattered with a sound, turning into a pool of thick blood that rained down from above.

At the same time, a Shaman Master among the Bone-Eater Tribe groaned, blood trickling from his seven orifices.

That blood snake was his trump card, cultivated from his own Essence Blood. When Yang Kai destroyed it, he suffered a severe backlash.

His combat experience was undoubtedly rich. Seeing his blood snake easily broken by Yang Kai, he knew he was no match, so he tried to hide among the crowd.

But Yang Kai, with a flick of his wrist, sent the Myriad Sword flying. The blade transformed into a streak of light, weaving through the crowd before piercing straight through the Bone-Eater Tribe Shaman Master’s chest and emerging from his back. After circling once more, the sword returned to Yang Kai’s hand.

With a flurry, more than ten Bone-Eater Tribe members fell.

The targeted Shaman Master fared no better. The Shamanic Art shield he conjured, though of Shaman Master level, was as fragile as paper before the Myriad Sword—utterly useless as a defense. In the blink of an eye, he too met a tragic end.

The Bone-Eater Tribe members were truly terrified now, their eyes overflowing with fear as they retreated several steps.

Even their own Shaman Master was instantly killed by him. Who could stop this man now? Only Lord You might have a chance, but Lord You was entangled above the clouds by the enemy and couldn’t spare any attention.

“Roar!”

The warriors of the Raging Flame Tribe roared, seizing the moment as the Bone-Eater Tribe retreated in fear. Though they were numerous, the Bone-Eater Tribe, now terrified, retreated step by step.

Yang Kai nodded to Ya in the crowd, shifted his steps, and left the battlefield.

Although his two sword strikes had given the Raging Flame Tribe and Gale Tribe a huge advantage, suppressing the courage and confidence of the Bone-Eater Tribe, their numbers were still half as many, and prolonged fighting might not maintain the advantage.

He had a more important task.

So he quickly arrived at the stone fortress he had previously investigated. The enemy’s attack had drawn all the Bone-Eater Tribe members out, so when he rushed in, no one stopped him. Even if someone tried, they couldn’t have stopped him anyway.

After sweeping with his Divine Sense, Yang Kai soon found an entrance leading underground in a certain spot in the stone fortress. He opened the stone door and headed down.

A pungent, unpleasant smell lingered at his nose, and faint firelight could be seen deep underground.

He soon entered a place resembling a dungeon. His footsteps startled the Bone-Eater Tribe members guarding the cages, and a muffled voice came from ahead: “What’s happening upstairs?”

Yang Kai didn’t answer, advancing straight ahead.

“I asked you what’s happening upstairs!” The man seemed to have a bad temper and couldn’t help but shout.

His answer was a cold gleam in the darkness.

As Yang Kai passed by, the man’s head flew off, his burly body staggered and fell on its back, blood spurting from his neck and staining the ground red.

This sudden change shocked the other Bone-Eater Tribe members guarding the dungeon, but before they could react, Myriad Sword flew out, harvesting their lives like cutting straw.

Retrieving Myriad Sword, Yang Kai swept his gaze around. He saw that the underground prison was divided into more than ten cells, each packed full, with pairs of eyes staring at him in shock and hope.

“Ah Niu?”

A familiar call came.

Yang Kai turned to look and saw Ah Hu’s face.

“Brother Ah Niu?”

“Brother Ah Niu is here!”

“Ah Niu has come to save us!”

In the cells, other villagers from Cangnan Village shouted excitedly, their voices full of joy.

Yang Kai stepped forward, placed his hand on the cell door, surged his shamanic power, and with a forceful shake, the sturdy door shattered instantly.

The villagers of Cangnan Village swarmed out. Without needing instructions, they scattered to rescue the other prisoners.

Yang Kai looked at them and found that aside from being weak, they had no other issues. Soon, he frowned and asked, “Where’s the village chief?”

He hadn’t seen the kind old man here.

Upon hearing this, the villagers of Cangnan Village gathered around Yang Kai, their expressions dimmed.

Yang Kai looked at Ah Hu, his gaze sharp.

Ah Hu lowered his head and said, “When those bastards brought us here, the village chief couldn’t endure the torment and died on the way.”

Yang Kai said nothing, but his aura became icy cold.

Ah Hu raised his head, his eyes bloodshot, and said, “Ah Niu, I want revenge!”

“Revenge for the village chief!”

The villagers of Cangnan Village gritted their teeth and whispered.

Yang Kai looked up and saw the burning flames of vengeance in the villagers’ eyes, nodding gently.

At that moment, the other prisoners were also rescued. Among the Raging Flame Tribe and Gale Tribe, a leader from each stepped forward. Before they could speak, Yang Kai turned and said, “Lord Chi and Lady Zhu have brought people over. Your tribes are fighting the Bone-Eater Tribe above, and they need your strength.”

“Lord Chi is here?”

“Lady Zhu is here too?”

Hearing this, the two tribes were overjoyed. Both were Great Shaman Master level figures, and with them present, the tribes’ confidence soared.

“Ah Hu, lead everyone to fight alongside them. Don’t let a single Bone-Eater Tribe member escape!” Yang Kai instructed.

“Alright! Today, we’ll wipe out the Bone-Eater Tribe!”

“Wipe them out!” everyone shouted. United in hatred, hundreds seemed to become one, their gathered momentum soaring skyward.

Yang Kai began chanting incantations, one exquisite Shamanic Art after another appearing in his hands, transforming into mysterious power and enhancing the strength of the three tribes.

Hundreds were utterly shocked.

Especially Ah Hu and the others, each found it incredible. Although Ah Niu was a shaman, to empower hundreds with multiple Shamanic Arts was unheard of. Moreover, they felt that the Shamanic Arts Ah Niu cast were far superior and more effective than those the village chief had once bestowed.

At the very least, the Bloodlust Art felt different. Their previously weak bodies now surged with strength, as if they could tear apart a tiger or leopard bare-handed.

What level of power did Ah Niu have now?

With the battle imminent, there was no time to ask. Yang Kai waved his hand and said, “Go now.”

Ah Hu nodded to Yang Kai, and together with the leaders of the other two tribes, led hundreds out.

Yang Kai stood still, gently closed his eyes, and his Divine Sense surged like a tide, instantly locking onto Shaman You, who was fighting two opponents above the clouds.

The next moment, he raised his sword to the sky, spun like a top, and wherever the sword tip passed, the ground above cracked open, revealing a deep tunnel.

With a crash, Yang Kai burst out of the earth, his body wrapped in sword light, shooting straight into the clouds.

Meanwhile, Shaman You, fighting two opponents without falling behind and even gaining a slight advantage, suddenly felt a strong sense of danger, a deathly aura enveloping him and chilling him to the bone.

Seeing him distracted, Chi quickly jumped away, casting several layers of Shamanic Art shields on himself, but still sweating coldly.

That moment was extremely dangerous. He had thought that, with Zhu’s help, even if they couldn’t kill Shaman You, they could at least hold him off. As long as Shaman Niu released the captured tribesmen, their disadvantage in numbers on the battlefield would be resolved, and they could retreat safely.

The main goal this time was to save people, not to fight the Bone-Eater Tribe to the death.

But after actually fighting, Chi realized his judgment was wrong.

## Chapter 2712 — A Long and Arduous Road

"Is that all?" Hua Yulu asked.

"Of course!" Yang Kai nodded.

Hua Yulu was astonished. She had thought, from Yang Kai’s earlier words about the toll of expelling the Gu, that it would be an arduous and exhausting process. Yet in reality, it took only a few dozen breaths, and Yang Kai hadn’t even used much strength at all.

"Young Master Yang, this business of yours is truly a windfall." Hua Yulu was full of admiration. Thinking about the two or three dozen Emperor Realm cultivators still waiting, Yang Kai would earn two or three hundred million, maybe even more, Upper-Level Source Crystals from this one session alone.

This was basically daylight robbery.

"It’s just a little side hustle, earning some hard-earned money." Yang Kai chuckled, raising his hand in a gesture. "Sister Hua, please call in the next person. And make sure they keep order—if anyone tries to rush in, don’t blame this Young Master for showing no mercy."

"Understood." Hua Yulu rose gracefully, thanked him, and turned to leave. After a few steps, she looked back and said, "Young Master Yang, if you ever have free time, you must come visit my Hundred Flowers Palace. I’ll be sure to welcome you personally!"

The Hundred Flowers Palace! This was Yang Kai’s first time hearing of it, but with so many sects in the Southern Territory, it was only natural that some would be unfamiliar to him. However, he had seen plenty of Hundred Flowers Pavilions—almost every city in the Southern Territory seemed to have one. And those pavilions... tsk tsk...

After sending Hua Yulu off, Yang Kai sat in place, pondering for a while. Suddenly, he raised his hand and struck his own chest hard.

Pfft...

A mouthful of fresh blood spurted out. Yang Kai’s expression instantly became listless, as if he had just fought a fierce battle.

Someone pushed the door open and entered, grinning obsequiously. He was about to speak, but when he saw Yang Kai’s state, he was shocked. "Sir Yang, what happened to you?"

Yang Kai replied weakly, "It’s nothing. Sit down, I’ll help you expel the Gu now."

The man was delighted, but still worried. "Would you like to rest a bit? I can wait."

Yang Kai had been perfectly fine before, but now he was coughing up blood and looked exhausted. It was obvious that expelling the Gu had taken a heavy toll.

"No need. If I tell you to sit, then sit." Yang Kai waved his hand. "With the Gu inside you, none of you can be at ease. It’s best to get rid of it as soon as possible."

Hearing this, the man was overwhelmed with gratitude. He obediently handed over a Spatial Ring containing tens of millions of Upper-Level Source Crystals. Yang Kai accepted it and immediately began the process again.

......

Half a day later, Yang Kai’s figure flickered and he appeared in the same private room as before, looking refreshed and rosy-cheeked, with no trace of his earlier exhaustion.

Everyone was waiting.

When they saw him appear, they all gathered around.

Yang Kai glanced around, then looked at Ye Hen and said, "Sect Master Ye, what are your plans now?"

Ye Hen sighed and replied, "Let’s return to Thousand Leaves Sect first and decide from there."

"That’s fine," Yang Kai nodded.

Although Thousand Leaves Sect had been destroyed, it was still home for Ye Hen and the others. After being imprisoned for over a year, regaining their freedom meant they naturally wanted to return. Besides, there were still several hundred surviving disciples in Emperor Heaven Valley who needed to be settled.

"The Yao King and I will escort you for a while. I also have something to discuss with Sect Master Ye."

"Then we’ll trouble Sir Yang and the Yao King." Ye Hen was deeply grateful, thinking Yang Kai was going out of his way to escort them. With these two as protection, there was no need to worry about danger on the road.

The group set out immediately, leaving Flowing Shadow City and flying south.

"Yang Kai, I have something to say to you." Partway there, Zhu Qing suddenly sent a voice transmission.

This dragon girl had been following him all along, and Yang Kai had no idea what she was up to. Still, since she meant him no harm, he didn’t bother to drive her away.

"What is it?" Yang Kai turned to look at her.

Zhu Qing stopped in her tracks.

Yang Kai frowned, motioning for Yingfei to take the others ahead, then stayed behind himself.

Once everyone else had disappeared from sight, Yang Kai asked, "What’s so secretive?"

Zhu Qing said, "You killed an Elder of Star God Palace. Aren’t you worried at all?"

Yang Kai smiled, "Are you worried about me?"

Zhu Qing curled her lip. "We’re not related. Whether you live or die has nothing to do with me."

"Then why ask?" Yang Kai gazed at her with interest.

Zhu Qing looked a bit uneasy, turning her gaze away. "No matter what that Star God Palace Elder did wrong, his status is what it is. Now that you’ve killed him, Star God Palace won’t let this go. And this incident involves a lot of people—you can’t hide it, even if you try. Star God Palace will definitely trace it back to you."

"I never planned to hide it," Yang Kai said with a slight smile.

The main reason he hadn’t killed those two or three dozen Emperor Realm cultivators was so they could testify, making it easier for Star God Palace’s experts to investigate. If everyone had died, whatever he said would be one-sided and unconvincing.

But now, with all those Emperor Realm cultivators giving the same account, even if Star God Palace investigates, they can’t blame Yang Kai.

Of course, if they insist on protecting their own, there’s nothing he can do.

"You’re really not worried at all?" Zhu Qing seemed unconvinced, turning to study Yang Kai’s face for any sign of insincerity.

Yang Kai burst out laughing. “We who walk this path live by settling gratitude and grudges as they come. If we spend our lives worrying about this and that, what’s the point of living at all?”

Zhu Qing stared at him in astonishment, at a loss for words.

After a moment, she said, "I suggest you find a place to hide for a while."

Yang Kai snorted. "Someone else already told me that."

Hua Yulu had previously advised him to hide in the Northern Territory’s Frozen Earth, and now Zhu Qing was saying the same thing.

"Star God Palace is, after all, the sect of a Great Emperor. If they really want to make trouble for me, then no matter how vast the Star Realm is, I doubt there’ll be any place for me to hide. So what’s the point of running or hiding?" Yang Kai waved his hand dismissively.

"Why wouldn’t there be a place to hide?" Zhu Qing smiled slyly. "I know of a place where even the Radiant Moon Great Emperor himself couldn’t find you."

"Don’t tell me you mean Dragon Isle?" Yang Kai gave her a sidelong glance.

Zhu Qing nodded. "Exactly, Dragon Isle."

"Tsk tsk..." Yang Kai clicked his tongue, rubbing his chin as he suddenly circled around Zhu Qing.

"What are you doing?" Zhu Qing felt extremely uncomfortable under his gaze and instinctively tightened her clothes.

"I’m just curious..." Yang Kai stopped in front of her, grinning. "You keep trying to get me to go to Dragon Isle. What’s your real motive?"

Last time, she had also suggested he go to Dragon Isle, though not as obviously. He hadn’t paid much attention then, but now that she brought it up again, he sensed something was off.

Could it be this little girl thinks she can’t beat me, so she wants to trick me into going to Dragon Isle and have her brothers and sisters gang up on me?

Truly, the most dangerous thing is a woman’s heart!

"What motive could I have!" Zhu Qing’s eyes flickered as she avoided Yang Kai’s gaze, making her look even more suspicious.

"You never know..." Yang Kai shook his head solemnly. "Maybe you’ve taken a liking to me and want to drag me back to do whatever you want!"

"You... you’re full of nonsense!" Zhu Qing fumed, utterly exasperated—she had never met anyone so shameless.

Yang Kai laughed loudly and turned to catch up with Yingfei and the others.

"Wait!" Zhu Qing called out anxiously, feeling frustrated. The Elder’s advice was useless! She’d thought Yang Kai would be grateful and follow her to Dragon Isle to hide, but he didn’t care at all about killing the Star God Palace Elder.

This mission of hers was going to be a long and arduous one. Who knew when she’d finally complete it?

......

Thousand Leaves Sect was a field of ruins, broken walls and collapsed buildings everywhere.

Ye Hen and the others flew overhead, each one looking sorrowful at the sight.

Soon, everyone arrived at Emperor Heaven Valley. Yang Kai activated the Space Array and let everyone enter.

Reuniting with the several hundred surviving disciples, Ye Hen wept with grief, feeling he had failed his ancestors and was full of guilt. Such a grand foundation, and now only Emperor Heaven Valley remained intact. Even with the nine Heavenly Puppets, Ye Hen had no idea how to rebuild the sect.

Yang Kai wandered all around Emperor Heaven Valley, with Zhu Qing following him like a shadow, impossible to shake off. She kept urging him to go to Dragon Isle, which was driving Yang Kai crazy.

If she truly meant him harm, Yang Kai would have knocked her out long ago and done this and that!

"What are you looking for, wandering around like this?" Zhu Qing had followed Yang Kai for half a day, nearly circling the entire valley, and finally couldn’t help but ask.

“When a man acts, a woman keeps silent!” Yang Kai ignored her, his gaze finally settling on the valley ahead as he gave a slight nod.

Zhu Qing was so angry her face turned white. She stomped her foot and flew off, disappearing in the blink of an eye.

"Such a temper," Yang Kai snorted, glancing at her retreating figure.

Sweeping out with his Divine Sense, he located Ye Hen. With the Laws of Space swirling, Yang Kai instantly appeared at Ye Hen’s side.

"Sir Yang!" Ye Hen hurriedly stood up.

Yang Kai waved for him to sit and then asked, "Sect Master Ye, have you decided what to do next?"

Ye Hen sighed deeply. "There are countless things to do—I don’t even know where to start. But after all this, I’ve come to understand one thing."

"I’d like to hear it," Yang Kai said, gazing at him.

"A man without sin is envied for his treasures," Ye Hen said gloomily. "Though my Thousand Leaves Sect has the nine Heavenly Puppets, there are no Emperor Realm experts in the sect, so we can’t unleash their full power. Even if I want to restore the sect, with our current manpower and situation, we’ll only invite disaster."

Yang Kai nodded. "So Sect Master Ye plans to focus on improving your strength first?"

Ye Hen replied, "That’s exactly it. Fortunately, the Spiritual Qi in Emperor Heaven Valley is dense. If I have three to five years, I should be able to break through to the Emperor Realm. Then, when I emerge, I’ll at least have some ability to protect myself."

Yang Kai smiled. "Sect Master Ye has thought things through." After a pause, he added, "But I have another suggestion. I wonder if you’d be willing to hear it."

## Chapter 3143 — Who Exactly Is She?

Yang Kai stopped the massive fist with a single finger. Yu Xiong's eyes bulged in disbelief, and he threw another punch.

Yang Kai bent his finger and flicked Yu Xiong's fist. Yu Xiong's powerful arm instantly went limp, unable to muster the slightest strength, and he staggered back several steps. He looked Yang Kai up and down. "You..."

Though Yu Xiong looked rough and coarse, that did not mean he was simple-minded. He had not used his full strength in those two punches, but they were still not blows just anyone could block. Yang Kai's effortless response naturally gave him a great deal to think about.

Yang Kai stood there smiling, looking utterly unfathomable.

Yu Xiong shook his head and stopped tangling with him. Instead, he called out to Shan Qingluo, "Little Sister, something serious has happened."

Shan Qingluo started. "What happened?"

Yu Xiong was now a Void King Realm cultivator. Anything that could rattle him this badly had to be no small matter, and how many so-called major events could there be on Di Chen Star right now? The few that existed would certainly involve Red Moon Domain.

"Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor has been wiped out."

Shan Qingluo froze, then turned to Yang Kai as if asking whether he had done it. Red Moon Domain had fought Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor for more than ten years and had always been suppressed. She herself had exchanged blows with Huang Tudao more than once, so she knew exactly how formidable he was. Red Moon Domain did not have the strength to destroy Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, and neither did anyone else on Di Chen.

The only person who could have done such a thing silently was the man beside her. But... for the past three days, she had never left his side. How could he have done it? Or had he gone to Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor before coming to see her?

Yu Xiong said excitedly, "When I first heard the news, I could hardly believe it, so I went to investigate for myself. Guess what I found? Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor had already been razed flat, without a single living soul inside. I don't know who made the move, but whoever it was was definitely skilled in Fire Attribute power and Divine Abilities. Also, according to what I learned earlier, on the night Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor was destroyed, Old Dog Huang was hosting a banquet. The guests included not only the Void King Realm Elders of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor, but also Qian Yan and Kuang Shi, those two traitors. Good, good that they died! Hahaha! So good and evil do receive their due after all." He clenched his fist, a frightening gleam flashing in his eyes. "Little Sister, it is time to act!"

Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor had been destroyed at its roots, and all its masters had been slain. Red Moon Domain no longer had anything to fear. It was time to take back all of Di Chen Star.

"It was not me." Seeing Shan Qingluo keep staring at him, Yang Kai shrugged, smiled faintly, and turned to Liu Yan.

The moment he heard that Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor had been destroyed by fire attribute Divine Abilities, he knew who had acted in secret. Liu Yan and Biluo had gone out that night; they could hide it from Shan Qingluo, but not from his perception. Most likely, that was when they had made their move.

Liu Yan happened to be looking at him too, her eyes narrowed, her whole face saying, "Hurry up and praise me."

Yang Kai gave her a thumbs-up, and Liu Yan smiled sweetly.

"You did it?" Shan Qingluo read their expressions and stared at Liu Yan in astonishment. She had thought this little girl was Yang Kai's illegitimate daughter with some woman outside, but now it seemed she had been overthinking things.

"Her?" Yu Xiong's rambling was interrupted. He stared at Liu Yan in disbelief and curled his lip. "What kind of joke is that?"

It was not that he could not accept shocking things. This was simply impossible to accept.

Biluo said weakly from the side, "Sir Yu Xiong, Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor really was destroyed by her."

Yu Xiong glanced at Biluo and burst out laughing. "That is not funny at all."

Biluo looked at him solemnly.

Yu Xiong's laughter gradually died away. "Are you serious?"

Biluo said, "I was just about to report this to Lady. Three nights ago, she and I..." She immediately recounted everything that had happened that night. She described it plainly, without the slightest exaggeration, but as she spoke, the events of that night still seemed to unfold before their eyes, leaving both Shan Qingluo and Yu Xiong shaken.

When they heard that Huang Tudao had been burned to death by a tiny wisp of Liu Yan's flame, they broke out in a cold sweat.

He was a Third Stage Void King Realm master. What kind of fire could burn him to death in three breaths?

Yu Xiong's face went a little pale, and he looked at Liu Yan with awe and lingering fear. After all, when he arrived just now, he had seen how cute she was and casually patted her on the head.

Thinking back on it now, he had truly been lucky to survive. If he had angered this little girl just now, would he not have followed in Huang Tudao's footsteps? Since that fire could kill Huang Tudao and leave him powerless to resist, it would have no trouble killing him.

He swallowed hard and quietly edged a little farther away from Liu Yan, as if that could give him some sense of safety.

"Who exactly is she?" Shan Qingluo turned to Yang Kai. "Isn't she your daughter?"

Yang Kai spread his hands. "I told you from the start she was not, but you would not listen. It's Liu Yan."

"Liu Yan!" Shan Qingluo was shocked. Naturally, she was no stranger to Liu Yan, but no matter what, she could not connect that Artifact Spirit with the little girl before her. The person in front of her was a living being of flesh and blood.

She immediately understood that Liu Yan must have obtained some extraordinary opportunity while following Yang Kai all these years, one that had allowed her to form her own body.

After the shock passed, light shone in Shan Qingluo's beautiful eyes. This was a once-in-a-thousand-years opportunity. Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor's masters had all been wiped out, and it no longer had the strength to fight Red Moon Domain. If they struck now, who on Di Chen Star could stop them?

She gave Yang Kai an apologetic smile. "Husband, I am afraid I cannot keep you company any longer."

Yang Kai nodded. "Go tend to your affairs. The days ahead are long; there is no rush." This time, he had returned to take everyone to the Star Realm, and he would never again disappear for several dozen years without a word. There would be plenty of time for romance in the future.

Besides, after three days of campaigning in bed, both he and Shan Qingluo needed some time to recover.

Shan Qingluo and Yu Xiong soon left, setting out to recover Di Chen Star, while Biluo stayed behind to attend to Yang Kai.

Biluo looked at Yang Kai with a complicated expression. "Sir, what are your instructions?"

How could she ever have imagined that the boy Lady Queen had once trapped in Piaoxiang City would one day grow to such heights that even Lady Queen would benefit from his protection?

Yang Kai pondered for a moment. "I remember Red Moon Domain has a Space Array here. Take me to it."

Dark Star had now been thoroughly cleared, and Di Chen Star would likely be the same before long, but they still had to guard against experts from Great Wilderness Star Domain making a comeback. With Su Yan overseeing Dark Star, Yang Kai was not worried about that side. Di Chen Star, however, still needed its strength reinforced. The best method was to connect the two Cultivation Stars so they could watch over and support each other. When the time came, if any blind fool dared set their sights on Di Chen Star, he would make sure they came and never returned!

The three of them immediately headed deep into Red Moon Domain.

According to Biluo, Shan Qingluo had personally destroyed Red Moon Domain's Space Array more than ten years ago, solely to prevent anyone from using it to teleport into the heart of Red Moon Domain.

At the time, they had already lost contact with Dark Star. They had been forced into that desperate measure.

Along the way, they encountered many yao of Red Moon Domain, all of whom saluted Biluo respectfully. Although she was only Shan Qingluo's maidservant, she was still a Void King Realm cultivator, so she naturally received the respect due to an expert. Besides, among the yao of Red Moon Domain, who did not know that Lady had never truly treated her as a maidservant? She and Lady were as close as sisters.

Their gazes toward Yang Kai and Liu Yan, however, were full of curiosity.

Since Biluo said nothing, they naturally did not dare ask.

Before long, they arrived at the site of the Space Array.

Yang Kai had arranged this Space Array himself back then, but looking at it now, he found many parts of it quite crude. This was partly because his cultivation had risen and his horizons had broadened, and partly because his attainments in the Laws of Space had grown much deeper.

"Sir, do you need any materials?" Biluo asked. She vaguely remembered that arranging this Space Array consumed quite a few precious materials.

Yang Kai waved his hand. "No need. Go handle your own affairs." In his early years, when he arranged Space Arrays in the Star Domain, the main material he had used was Crystal Stone, but that material was not very high grade. He had brought countless Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade from the Star Realm, and using them to arrange a Space Array would produce much better results.

Biluo smiled faintly. "Serving Sir well is what I should be handling." Shan Qingluo had personally given her this instruction, so she naturally took it to heart, but the moment the words left her mouth, she realized something was wrong. They seemed to carry a certain ambiguity.

Right then, Yang Kai looked over, and Biluo clearly felt his gaze pause for an instant on her red lips. She hurriedly lowered her head, her long eyelashes casting a shadow that hid her expression.

When she looked up again, Yang Kai was already focused on the Space Array before him.

She felt someone watching her from the side. Turning her head, she saw Liu Yan staring straight at her, both hands spread wide as she drew a huge circle.

"What?" Biluo was truly afraid of her now, especially when she gestured like this. Her movements always made one's imagination run wild.

"So many women. So very many," Liu Yan said.

Biluo thought it over for a moment, then understood and blushed. "Why are you telling me?"

"Heh heh..." Liu Yan gave a smile that was not a smile.

Biluo really wanted to pry her mouth open.

"Liu Yan, come help!" Yang Kai called.

Liu Yan answered and skipped over to Yang Kai's side. Facing the pile of Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade he had taken out, she blew a breath, and a wisp of flame immediately flew out and wrapped around the materials.

With Liu Yan's help, arranging the Space Array became even easier.

In only half a day, a brand-new Space Array rose again.

After Yang Kai adjusted and positioned it, this Space Array could connect directly to Dark Star without any transit point in the middle, making it much more convenient.

Yang Kai instructed Liu Yan to stay behind and watch over it, then stepped onto the Space Array and activated the Power of Space. Light flashed, and he vanished.

Biluo and Liu Yan waited only half a day before light flared over the array again. Vaguely, they could see numerous figures within it, each one radiating a powerful aura.

When the light faded, Biluo was astonished to discover many familiar faces. These people were all experts from Lingxiao Sect and Dark Star. She and Shan Qingluo had lived on Dark Star for a time back then, so naturally she was familiar with them.

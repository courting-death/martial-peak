## Chapter 2873 — Utterly Powerless

Although Bao Qi had not uttered a word, the Demon Kings could all sense that something was amiss. They each raised their eyes to the sky. Everyone had noticed Bao Qi’s odd behavior earlier, but with the Sacred Artifact at stake, no one had stopped to consider it more deeply.

Now that they had stopped fighting, the contrast between Bao Qi’s composure and their own loss of control became starkly apparent.

“Bao Qi, you owe us an explanation!” Fei Li’s voice was icy cold. “Why did you incite the Demon Kings to fight? What do you hope to gain from this?”

Although the appearance of the Sacred Artifact was baffling, Fei Li instinctively sensed it was a trap—a ploy by Bao Qi to make the Demon Kings turn on each other.

He simply couldn’t fathom why Bao Qi would do this, or what benefit he could possibly gain.

The other Demon Kings frowned slightly, gradually regaining their senses. Though they still longed for the Sacred Artifact, they forcibly suppressed their restless urges and turned their gazes to the ever-calm Bao Qi, clearly intent on forcing a reasonable explanation from him.

“Ha!” Bao Qi couldn’t help but sneer, his expression mocking, as if shedding a layer of disguise to reveal his true face. With his eyes lowered, he looked at the Demon Kings below and taunted, “I thought you’d fight until the end of days. I didn’t expect there to be a clever one among you.”

At these words, the Demon Kings’ expressions all changed.

If there had only been suspicion before, Bao Qi’s words now confirmed it for them.

This was truly a trap! No one knew what schemes Bao Qi was plotting, but he had used a Sacred Artifact as bait to make them slaughter each other.

As this thought flashed through their minds, all the Demon Kings’ hearts blazed with fury.

Fei Li said darkly, “Bao Qi, you’re courting death!”

Bao Qi let out a soft chuckle. "Do you have what it takes?"

“You’ll know soon enough!” As Fei Li’s words fell, his entire skeleton suddenly ignited with green flames. Without any visible movement, he stepped forward and instantly appeared before Bao Qi, reaching out to grab him.

Although Bao Qi’s strength was a bit higher than Shaya’s, he was still a stage below Fei Li. According to the shaman hierarchy, Shaya was a Lower-Level Shaman King, Bao Qi was a Middle-Level Shaman King, and Fei Li was an Upper-Level Shaman King.

With such an overwhelming advantage, Fei Li felt there was no way he could fail.

His movements were casual, as if he were about to crush a fly.

Bao Qi smiled faintly. Facing this ruthless blow from a Demon King close to the Demon Saint level, he remained unflinching, the mockery in his eyes growing even stronger.

Fei Li instinctively sensed something was wrong, but couldn’t pinpoint what. Now that the arrow was nocked, he had no choice but to let it fly. He steeled himself, determined to take Bao Qi’s life and show him the consequences of his actions.

In the next instant, the ghostly fire in Fei Li’s eye sockets suddenly flickered.

His hand had stopped, entirely against his will, three feet from Bao Qi’s face. No matter how hard he tried, he couldn’t move it an inch closer.

In the next instant, a figure appeared ahead like a ghost. It was a young man—a young man eroded by Demonic Qi—yet he had not lost his sanity, for his eyes shone with keen intelligence.

His expression was indifferent, and though the aura he exuded was unremarkable, Fei Li felt a deep unease. He hadn’t felt this way in countless years—the last time was a millennium ago, when he was little more than a fragile skeleton, tottering as if his bones might fall apart at any moment.

Back then, he had encountered the nemesis of the Bone Demon Clan—the Bone-Crushing Beast. He had hidden in the grass, not daring to move, terrified the beast would devour him.

Reliving that feeling from a thousand years ago brought Fei Li no joy. Though he had no flesh, he still felt a chill from the depths of his soul.

He had no idea how this young man had appeared before him; it was as if he had always been standing there.

The other Demon Kings’ expressions changed several times as well. Over a dozen pairs of eyes focused on Yang Kai. The Demon Kings who had just been fighting to the death now joined their auras together. Even Mo Ke Tuo, the Shadow Demon who had seized the Sacred Artifact, was quietly edging closer to the others.

“My lord!” Bao Qi bowed deeply, his actions making it clear that Yang Kai’s status was far above theirs.

“My lord?” The ghostly fire in Fei Li’s eyes flickered again, surprised by Bao Qi’s form of address.

As Demon Kings, they only addressed Demon Saints as ‘my lord’. Yet this young man had no Demon Saint aura—he seemed more like a demonized being. What right did he have to be called ‘my lord’ by Bao Qi?

Among the demons, demonized beings held the lowest status, even beneath demonic beasts. Often, their only value was to serve as food for the beasts.

“Who are you?” Fei Li asked in a low voice.

Yang Kai gave him a slight smile, leaving Fei Li baffled. A terrifying force suddenly erupted from Yang Kai—a power so devastating that even a being on the cusp of becoming a Demon Saint found himself utterly powerless to resist. He was sent flying backward in an instant, his skeletal frame clattering violently, nearly shattered to pieces.

Fei Li let out a strange cry and crashed back among the other Demon Kings.

The Demon Kings were all stunned, staring at Fei Li in shock, then glancing suspiciously at Yang Kai, almost thinking they were hallucinating.

“This guy is strange!” Fei Li shouted the moment he landed.

He truly was strange. His aura wasn’t particularly strong, yet he could send Fei Li flying with ease. If he had wanted to kill Fei Li just now, could Fei Li have resisted?

“Bao Qi, what’s the meaning of this?” Shaya jumped out, shouting indignantly. “What exactly are you trying to do?”

It had to be said, succubi were unmatched in their acting skills. Her expression and tone radiated anger, leaving the other Demon Kings with no doubts. She voiced the question everyone wanted answered.

Bao Qi smiled slightly. “Shaya, you’re asking the wrong person. It’s not about what I want, but what my lord wants.”

Shaya sneered, “My lord? Someone on the level of a Demon Commander dares call himself ‘my lord’ before us?”

Bao Qi’s face darkened. “My lord’s methods are beyond your limited imagination.”

Shaya had said something similar to him months ago. Now, as he repeated those words, an unusual feeling washed over him—as if he had become the Shaya of months past, and the gathered Demon Kings below were his former self.

As if to deliberately display unparalleled power, Yang Kai casually lifted a hand. At once, the Shadow Demon Mo Ke Tuo let out a strange cry and was forcibly dragged toward Yang Kai, unable to resist. While still suspended midair, the black mist that had shrouded the demon's body suddenly shattered, revealing a delicate and well-proportioned figure.

In the mysterious Demon Domain, no one had ever seen the true form of the Shadow Demon King. To everyone’s shock, she was a female demon! Unlike most demons, who were tall and imposing, her figure was extremely petite.

The Demon Kings were nearly dumbfounded.

Among all the Demon Kings, Mo Ke Tuo ranked near the top. None dared face her alone. She was always shrouded in black mist, and aside from the Demon Saints, no one knew her true appearance.

Until now!

So everyone was surprised. No one had expected the Shadow Demon King’s true form to be so ordinary. Without the mysterious black mist, she looked just like any other demon, with nothing particularly remarkable about her appearance.

Mo Ke Tuo herself was equally shocked.

The dispersal of the black mist was not her doing—it was clearly the work of the young man. What terrified her was that she had been utterly unable to resist.

How could this be? Even against a Demon Saint, she, a master of stealth and assassination, would not be so helpless. In this moment, she felt as if her fate was entirely in someone else’s hands.

Until now, she had always been the one controlling others’ fates!

Under the complex gazes of all the Demon Kings, Mo Ke Tuo was dragged before Yang Kai. Naturally, she was unwilling to submit and instinctively tried to resist, but she couldn’t muster the slightest strength.

Her face turned pale, and she stared at the young man before her in terror.

The young man smiled at her, but she felt a chill run down her spine, as if she were facing a primordial beast capable of devouring her whole.

“This one is mine.” Yang Kai reached out and tapped her lightly.

Mo Ke Tuo shuddered as if struck by lightning, let out a muffled groan, and plummeted like a meteor. Blood spurted from her mouth midair—she was clearly injured. This scene made the other Demon Kings even more fearful.

From the moment this young man appeared, only a few breaths had passed, yet the power he displayed was already beyond the Demon Kings’ comprehension. He seemed like a god of this world, holding the power of life and death over all.

The Sacred Artifact that Mo Ke Tuo had seized now floated quietly in midair. Yang Kai reached out and grabbed it, then casually tossed it behind him. The Demonic Warhammer turned into a streak of light and vanished.

No one knew where the young man had thrown the Sacred Artifact.

At this point, no one cared about the artifact’s whereabouts. They were all wondering if, by joining forces, they could stand against this young man. One-on-one was hopeless—if even Fei Li and Mo Ke Tuo couldn’t withstand him, what chance did the rest have?

Nothing more needed to be said. The Demon Kings exchanged glances and reached a silent consensus.

"I am Shaman Niu!" Yang Kai floated in midair, looking down upon them all with an overwhelming presence that made his figure seem even more towering and majestic. "As you can see, Bao Qi is one of my people!"

The Demon Kings said nothing—this much was already obvious.

“What do you want?” Shaya asked at just the right moment.

Yang Kai smiled slightly. “I want you all, like Bao Qi, to become my people as well!”

## Chapter 3145 — Corpse Refining

A burst of clinking and clanging rang out, and several figures suddenly appeared beside the thin, tall man. Some were tall and imposing, others short and compact, but all of them radiated a thick corpse aura. Their faces were green, their fangs bared, their hair disheveled, and their expressions hideous and terrifying.

"Refined corpses!" Yang Kai raised a brow, rather surprised. He had not expected the people of Netherworld Sect to be proficient in corpse-refining arts as well. None of the Netherworld Sect cultivators he had encountered before had known such methods. From the looks of it, Netherworld Sect's Secret Techniques were divided into several schools, but no matter the school, none of them could be separated from the dead.

"Hm?" His eyes sharpened again. He fixed his gaze on one of the refined corpses, studied it closely for a few moments, and his face immediately darkened.

"Boy, this Lord will capture you, refine you with care, and make you a great general under my command!" the thin, tall man shouted darkly, staring at Yang Kai with a vicious look. The young man before him had just displayed extraordinary combat power, but he was no pushover either. With these refined corpses in hand, even a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator could do nothing to him; one careless move, and such a cultivator might even be taken down by him instead. So he was not especially panicked. On the contrary, Yang Kai's earlier performance had only made him more interested.

The strength of a refined corpse depended on the strength the corpse had possessed in life. Naturally, the stronger the cultivator had been, the greater the power they could display after being refined.

Yang Kai was not only quite powerful, his physique was also extremely tough. He was precisely the ideal corpse-refining material the man had spent years searching for in vain.

The man could barely keep from laughing wildly. Truly, one could wear out iron shoes searching without success, only to find what one sought without any effort. If he could refine this boy, his status in Netherworld Sect would rise immediately.

The copper ring in his hand moved, and the refined corpses he had summoned roared ferociously. Dense corpse aura spread across each of their bodies as they pounced toward Yang Kai with fangs bared and claws brandished. Their sharp nails were like long spears, incomparably keen, and they gleamed a dark green, clearly containing a fierce poison.

Firelight flared, and a small Fire Bird flew out, rushing toward the refined corpses. It was nimble and graceful, yet noble and dignified.

The first refined corpse to touch it burst into flames in an instant. The Fire Bird did not slow at all and flew straight toward the second refined corpse, burning several of them in the blink of an eye.

"Leave this one!" Yang Kai shouted. He suddenly reached out, seized one of the refined corpses by the neck, and hauled it over. The refined corpse was still struggling, its sharp nails whistling through the air as they stabbed at Yang Kai's chest, but they struck as if against metal and stone, producing a ringing clang and failing completely to pierce him.

Liu Yan tilted her head. Although she did not know what he intended, she did not ask. She simply controlled the flames and burned the remaining refined corpses clean in an instant.

"Puh..."

The thin, tall man spat out a mouthful of blood. His eyes were filled with horror as he stared fearfully at Liu Yan. "You... you..." His eyes were shot through with blood as well.

Each of those refined corpses could display Void King Realm strength, especially the strongest one, which had been refined from the corpse of a Third Stage Void King Realm expert. With such a lineup, he had been confident no one in Hengluo Star Domain could stop him. Yet now they had almost been burned clean by a little girl of seven or eight, while the strongest refined corpse had been seized directly by that young man.

His vision blurred, and the little girl drifted before him like a ghost, raising her head to look up at him. His terrified face was reflected in her clear black-and-white eyes.

He watched helplessly as she lifted a small, tender hand and pressed it against his stomach, without leaving him any time to react.

"Leave him alive too," Yang Kai instructed, turning his head.

Liu Yan released the slightest thread of force from her hand, and the thin, tall man immediately seemed to collapse like a punctured balloon. His aura rapidly withered. He staggered a few steps, dropped heavily onto the ground, and vomited blood in great mouthfuls, nearly throwing up his internal organs.

His Dantian had been destroyed, his cultivation completely crippled! To a cultivator, nothing in the world was more painful than this. On top of that, the destruction of his refined corpses had wounded his mind and spirit, so more than half his life was already gone.

The enemy had nearly taken his life with a casual move. If they truly became serious, who in this world could stand against them? He feared even the Sect Master could not.

Where had these two lunatics come from?

Yang Kai held the strongest refined corpse by the neck with one hand, letting its sharp nails scrape across his body. A complex look flashed through his eyes.

The refined corpse, still under control, kept growling and struggling like a mad thing. Cracking sounds came from its neck, clearly showing its bones had already broken, but it no longer felt pain and had no thoughts of its own. It knew only to destroy everything before it, so even with broken bones and torn tendons, it did not stop moving.

Yang Kai drew a soft breath, quietly urged his Divine Sense, then gritted his teeth and shouted, "Long Tianshang! Wake up!"

Pure Divine Soul power wrapped around his voice and rushed straight into the refined corpse's mind. Somewhere unseen, there seemed to come the sound of something shattering. The refined corpse, which had been restless all this time, suddenly became much more docile. Although it was still baring its fangs and brandishing its claws, its earlier ferocity was gone.

Its eyes shifted slightly a few times. Its once-scattered gaze gradually showed signs of focusing, but only signs.

Seeing this, Yang Kai immediately understood that its spirituality had not been extinguished. Perhaps it was because it had not been a refined corpse for long, or perhaps because its own strength had been considerable. In any case, there was hope of awakening him. Yang Kai immediately urged his Divine Soul power again, pouring it into the corpse's mind and carefully bringing its chaotic thoughts back into order.

Only after a full cup of tea's worth of time did the refined corpse before him suddenly regain a trace of clarity in its eyes. Its hands, which had been struggling and flailing all this time, also stopped, and a strange expression appeared on its hideous face. "You... you are..."

Its voice was dry and hoarse, as if it had not spoken in a very long time, or perhaps because its body itself had been damaged. Either way, it was extremely unpleasant to hear.

"Senior Long." Yang Kai's expression was complicated.

Long Tianshang. Yang Kai was not very familiar with him; he had only met him once when he went to Shuiyue Star years ago, and they had not spoken much. This man was the Grand Elder of Hengluo Commerce Guild. Strictly speaking, he could be considered Ai Ou's senior, which naturally made him Yang Kai's senior as well.

After Ai Ou left the Star Domain with Yang Kai and went to the Star Realm, Hengluo Commerce Guild's enormous foundation had been left for Xue Yue to manage, while Long Tianshang remained behind the scenes to hold the overall situation steady. In truth, with his strength, he could also have left the Star Domain with Yang Kai back then. But he had devoted his entire life to Hengluo Commerce Guild and truly could not cut his ties with it, so he simply stayed behind to serve as Hengluo Commerce Guild's protector.

Who could have imagined that when they met again several dozen years later, he would have been refined into a Heavenly Corpse, stripped of his freedom and enslaved by others?

Only because he had run into Yang Kai could such heaven-reaching methods be used to awaken him for a short while. No one else could possibly have done it.

"You are... Little Yang!" Long Tianshang's thoughts gradually grew clear as well. He recalled fragments from his memories and naturally had a deep impression of Yang Kai, the rising star of those days and Xue Yue's husband.

Yang Kai said solemnly, "Senior Long, we do not have much time. I want to know what happened to Hengluo Commerce Guild. Where is Xue Yue?"

The last question was what he cared about most. If Hengluo Commerce Guild was gone, then so be it. Ai Ou was already in the Star Realm now, and Yang Kai suspected he would not care much about a mere Hengluo Commerce Guild anymore. The key was Xue Yue. Nothing could have happened to her. Yet since even someone with Long Tianshang's cultivation had been refined into a Heavenly Corpse by Netherworld Sect, how could Yang Kai not worry about Xue Yue's safety?

"Xue Yue..." Long Tianshang murmured hoarsely. More memories surfaced. He lowered his head and looked at his own hands, then said bleakly, "Ah, so that is how it is..."

At last, he remembered his own situation as well. After organizing his thoughts and choosing his words, he said, "Hengluo Commerce Guild is gone. Xue Yue... when Shuiyue Star fell, I covered their escape. As for where they are now, I do not know."

All of his memories stopped at that earth-shaking battle. By himself, he had held back Netherworld Sect and a dozen or so Warships from Great Wilderness Star Domain, but in the end his strength ran dry and he was captured, then refined into his current state.

Yang Kai raised a brow. "Xue Yue escaped?"

That, at least, was a blessing amid misfortune. Although he still did not know whether Xue Yue was safe, at least there was hope. The breath trapped in Yang Kai's chest finally eased a little.

"Have you seen this woman?" Liu Yan crouched in front of the thin, tall man from Netherworld Sect. A cluster of flame rose and fell in her palm, then transformed into the image of a heroic, valiant woman. Although it was formed from fire, it was lifelike, showing Liu Yan's extraordinary talent for controlling flames.

She had refined the Fire Phoenix Origin, so controlling flames was naturally her specialty.

Her tone was calm and her voice soft and pleasant, but to the thin, tall man, it sounded like a call from the underworld. His whole body shivered, and he shook his head repeatedly. "No, I haven't seen her."

"Hehe..." Liu Yan smiled without warmth. Another cluster of flame flew out and turned into a fire snake, coiling around the thin, tall man. It opened its great mouth and hovered above his head, hissing and flicking its tongue as if it might swallow him at any moment. This flame could clearly burn several powerful refined corpses with ease, yet at this moment it seemed to have no temperature at all and did not harm the thin, tall man in the slightest, making it seem extraordinarily wondrous. "You said you haven't seen her without even looking. I may be small, but that does not mean you can lie to me."

The thin, tall man lifted his eyes and glanced at the figure formed from flame.

"You had best answer honestly. There are many things in this world more painful than death," Liu Yan said lightly.

"I have seen her, I have seen her!"

"Where did she go?"

The thin, tall man said, "She fled to a Cultivation Star with some others. That Cultivation Star has a Star Lord overseeing it, and we have been locked in a stalemate there for the past few years."

"Which direction did she flee?"

The thin, tall man raised his head, identified the direction, and pointed.

Liu Yan turned to look at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai sank his mind into his Sea of Consciousness and looked up at the Star Chart above it. Then his heart stirred.

Back then, Xue Yue and the others had fled to a Cultivation Star with a Star Lord presiding over it, and the direction also matched. If he was not mistaken, they should have gone to that place.

It was a wise decision as well. Although that place was some distance from Shuiyue Star, comparatively speaking, it had been the most suitable choice.

## Chapter 2965 — You Lost

A sharp emerald radiance condensed like an awl, piercing straight toward Yang Kai's chest.

Yang Kai stood in place and stared at him coldly. A layer of emerald radiance suddenly appeared over his body, transforming into a strong and powerful protection.

The emerald light struck that protection and scattered at the same time.

"How can this be?" The Human Sovereign was shocked. He naturally knew Yang Kai could display Sacred Arts and mobilize the Sacred Tree's power. After all, he was also a Shaman, and as long as one was a Shaman, one could display Sacred Arts.

But without receiving the Baptism of a certain ceremony, why would the Sacred Tree protect him of its own accord? The reason the princes and princesses could obtain protection and turn danger into safety at critical moments was precisely because they had undergone an extremely complicated Baptism ceremony.

At this moment, the Sacred Tree gave off a very strange feeling. On one hand, it did not reject the Human Sovereign's command; on the other, it obstructed his attempt to kill his enemy, like a disobedient child acting willfully and doing whatever made it happy.

"You really do want to kill me!" Yang Kai sneered, clearly having expected this long ago.

The Human Sovereign said nothing, his expression growing grave. His chanting shifted, and he raised his hand as a thunderous surge erupted toward Yang Kai. The lightning's might was terrifying, coiling like a flood dragon thrashing its tail. In a blink, it bore down upon Yang Kai, erupting in dazzling radiance that engulfed him completely.

The Human Sovereign's expression changed again.

When the light faded, Yang Kai was still standing there perfectly unharmed. Even his clothes showed no trace of a wrinkle.

"Why?" the Human Sovereign roared, but not at Yang Kai. Instead, he turned toward the Sacred Tree, like a stern father scolding a playful child, his fury towering.

The Sacred Tree had ruined his plans twice, making it somewhat difficult for him to endure.

Yang Kai said coldly, "Do you want to know why?"

The Human Sovereign shouted in a low voice, "What tricks did you use?"

Yang Kai snorted coldly, "Do you not already know whether I used any tricks or not? Why deceive yourself like this?"

The Human Sovereign gnashed his teeth and said, "I am the Human Sovereign. How dare you defy me!" It was unclear whether these words were directed at Yang Kai or the Sacred Tree.

He shouted loudly, and an incantation once more rang from his mouth.

When his incantation sounded, the Sacred Tree suddenly shivered, as if some strange change had occurred. Then great surges of green energy flowed out from within the Sacred Tree and poured into the Human Sovereign's body, causing his aura to climb steadily, while his not especially tall body also suddenly became imposing.

"Killing the chicken for its eggs! How dare you be so reckless!" Yang Kai's expression darkened with fury upon witnessing this. He too began to chant an incantation, though the sounds that emerged were far more ancient and obscure than the Human Sovereign's, imbued with a profundity that was difficult to fathom.

Although he did not know what Shamanic Art the Human Sovereign was displaying, he could sense some things invisible to the naked eye through his perception of the Sacred Tree. The Human Sovereign's action damaged the Sacred Tree's foundation, no different from killing the chicken for its eggs.

It seemed that in order to deal with Yang Kai, he had completely stopped caring about anything else.

If the Sacred Tree's foundation were shaken, Human Sovereign City would probably suffer disaster as well.

The green energy flowing toward the Human Sovereign seemed to meet some kind of resistance, and its speed began to slow.

Seeing this, the Human Sovereign's anger grew even more intense. His incantation became louder and louder as he tried to wrest the Sacred Tree's power away from Yang Kai.

Yang Kai's gaze remained cold and piercing as he stared at him, his tone unwavering, his heart steadfast and resolute.

The two of them stood face to face like this. There was no flash of swords and blades, nor any bloody storm, but rather a battle of methods unfolding in an extremely special way. The winner would take everything, while the loser would be trampled underfoot.

And the key to all of this lay in their resonance with the Sacred Tree. The deeper one's resonance with the Sacred Tree, the stronger the power one could command.

Yang Kai and the Human Sovereign both understood this, so neither of them attacked the other. They only focused on doing their own part. The Sacred Tree possessed no clear thoughts; it was only a clump of remnant will, firmly guarding its desire to shelter Shamans. No matter which Shaman sought power from it, it would cooperate.

The green light on the Human Sovereign's body flared and dimmed by turns. When it flared, it represented that he had gained a slight advantage in the battle of methods; when it dimmed, the opposite was true.

He suddenly discovered that Yang Kai was an extremely troublesome opponent, far less easy to deal with than he had previously thought.

This fellow's resonance with the Sacred Tree was actually not inferior in the slightest to his own, even though he, as the Human Sovereign, had lived beside the Sacred Tree from childhood to old age. If not for that, he would have dealt with the other party long ago, instead of fighting to a draw as they were now.

The Sacred Tree seemed to feel a particularly special intimacy toward this outsider, favoring him at every turn and standing opposed to himself.

He suddenly realized that allowing Yang Kai to enter this place and observe the Sacred Tree up close had been a mistake. If he had killed him ruthlessly back in the Human Sovereign Palace, perhaps things would not have become so troublesome.

I am the Human Sovereign. I am the king of the human race! the Human Sovereign roared in his heart, hoping this thought could be transmitted to the Sacred Tree. He had not expected it to have the opposite effect. He clearly felt that the Sacred Tree actually seemed to reject him somewhat, like a troubled child scolded by its father giving rise to a rebellious heart.

This phenomenon caused his expression to change wildly.

Though Yang Kai was troublesome, there were still ways to deal with him. Yet if he lost the Sacred Tree's support, he would be nothing more than an old man at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. In Human Sovereign City, those who surpassed him in strength were countless beyond measure.

The Human Sovereign no longer dared let his thoughts run wild and could only start with Yang Kai himself.

While chanting his incantation, he suddenly transmitted his voice. "Rather than spending your time here competing with this Sovereign, why not spare a moment to care about your woman? If this Sovereign has not guessed wrongly, she should be close to death by now, yes?"

So what if she was from the Dragon Clan? So what if she was the head of all living beings? Experts emerged one after another in Human Sovereign City, and even the Dragons would have to suffer here.

Yang Kai remained unmoved and said faintly, "My woman does not need my concern. You should worry about yourself instead. Tsk, tsk. Sir, you are no longer young, are you? It looks like you do not have many years left. Have you thought about when to pass down your position? I think a few princes are rather good, especially that Third Prince. His cultivation is not weak, and his temperament is tenacious. He is very suited to inherit your position as Human Sovereign. He does come across as a bit scheming, but no one is perfect. The Second Princess isn’t bad either. I wonder whether Human Sovereign City has ever had a woman ascend the throne. If there is such a precedent, you could pass it on to the Second Princess. It seems to me that Her Highness is quite ambitious…”

"Shut up!" the Human Sovereign shouted angrily.

Yang Kai chuckled. “Neither of them? Then it seems, Sir, that you intend to pass the throne to the First Prince. But the First Prince is hopelessly addicted to wine and women; he’s hardly an ideal choice. If Human Sovereign City were to fall into his hands, I fear nothing good would come of it. When the brothers turn against one another and calamity erupts from within the royal house, even in death you would find no peace. No, that can’t be right. The First Prince is obviously little more than mud that cannot be plastered onto a wall, yet he possesses the lofty status of an Eight-Leaf Body. Looking at it that way, you truly do seem especially fond of the First Prince. Could it be that his birth mother was your beloved, and so you cherish the son for the mother’s sake?”

The Human Sovereign's expression changed somewhat. Although it was faint, how could it escape Yang Kai's eyes?

Yang Kai continued adding fuel to the fire. "Looks like I was right. And judging from Sir's appearance, the one you loved probably died long ago. Who would have thought Sir was actually such a devoted lover? I imagine that when you were young, you must have had some fine times with that beauty. It is only that the dead cannot return to life, so please restrain your grief and accept the inevitable, Sir. Memories are also a kind of beauty. Occasionally waking from dreams at midnight with tears soaking your lapels is also a rather nice experience."

The Human Sovereign's face twitched, as if some sore spot had been stabbed. No longer able to endure, he madly drew upon the Sacred Tree's power.

Yang Kai snorted coldly, "You lost!"

As his words fell, the Human Sovereign let out a muffled groan and retreated several steps. The only faint green light left on his body also suddenly vanished like smoke.

The resonance between him and the Sacred Tree had actually been severed all at once. He intended to reconnect with the Sacred Tree, but it was like throwing a stone into the sea; the Sacred Tree gave no response at all. He knew his state of mind had fluctuated too greatly just now, and because he had been too eager for quick success, he had instead suffered a crushing defeat in this confrontation.

"So what if I no longer have the Sacred Tree's protection? This Sovereign's cultivation is still higher than yours." The Human Sovereign was unwilling to admit defeat. His aged body suddenly straightened, and the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm erupted fully. Raising his hand, he slapped toward Yang Kai, and in an instant, winds and clouds surged, his might unfathomable.

Yang Kai sneered and punched forward. Emperor Yuan surged over that fist, and a violent power poured out.

When fist and palm met, the Human Sovereign seemed to suffer a heavy blow. He opened his mouth and spat blood, flying backward like a paper kite before crashing heavily to the ground, where he lay motionless for quite some time.

The sound of footsteps came, and Yang Kai slowly walked over to him, lowering his head to look down at him.

The Human Sovereign swallowed the hot blood in his throat and stood up shakily, growling in a low voice, "I am the Human Sovereign. I am the king of the human race. How can you treat me like this?"

"Do you know why you lost?" Yang Kai asked faintly.

The Human Sovereign froze for a moment. He very much wanted to know the answer, but could not quite bring himself to ask. Only after hesitating for quite some time did he say stiffly, "Why?"

"Those who possess the Dao receive much help, while those who lose the Dao receive little! You treated the Sacred Tree as a source of power you could draw from at will, as capital that allowed you to stand above all living beings. You were wrong."

The Human Sovereign said, "The Sacred Tree is precisely such an existence!"

Yang Kai snorted. “You are truly obstinate. If one errs out of ignorance, there is still room for forgiveness. But to know what is right and still choose what is wrong—that deserves death and nothing less. The Sacred Tree does not protect your imperial family, nor does it protect the Shamans; it protects the entire Human Race. Yet you treat it as your own private possession. How ridiculous!”

The Human Sovereign said, "Utter nonsense!"

Yang Kai sneered. "I speak utter nonsense? The barrier arrays throughout all Human Sovereign City are powered by the Sacred Tree. It protects all of Human Sovereign City from the interference of the changing external laws, and you say I speak utter nonsense? I think your old eyes are dim."

After resonating with the Sacred Tree, Yang Kai had clearly sensed that the Sacred Tree's power filled the whole of Human Sovereign City. It was precisely by relying on it that Human Sovereign City could remain unchanged through the ages and endure forever within this Revolving World.

The Human Sovereign said dispiritedly, "What meaning is there in saying these things now? This Sovereign has already been defeated. Whether you kill or flay me is up to you."

Yang Kai glanced at him sidelong and said, "When did I say I wanted to kill or flay you?"

The Human Sovereign was startled and said with some surprise, "You are willing to let me go?"

"That will depend on your performance."

The Human Sovereign hesitated for a moment, then said, "May I ask... what requirements does Your Excellency have?"

In this situation, even if the person before him wanted to seize the position of Human Sovereign, he would be powerless to resist. Since ancient times, this was how things had been in Human Sovereign City: whoever controlled the Sacred Tree was the Human Sovereign, and now the one controlling it was an outsider.

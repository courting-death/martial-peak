## Chapter 2945 — Three Finger Mountain

"Save... save me!" Li Jiao struggled in vain to break free from Zhu Lie's grip, feeling as though his neck was about to be crushed. The icy killing intent in Zhu Lie's eyes made his heart tremble with terror. Unable to do anything else, he turned his head and called out to Yang Kai for help.

Since the two of them had come here together, Yang Kai's words naturally carried some weight.

But Yang Kai just shrugged and said, "Sorry, Brother Li, I really can't help you with this."

Li Jiao could only make hoarse noises from his throat, unable to utter a word. The elders of Li Dragon Palace were even more anxious, watching helplessly as their Palace Master was humiliated. They dared not step forward, nor could they retreat, feeling extremely awkward.

"Oh, I forgot to mention," Yang Kai sighed leisurely, "this Dragon Clan member is Zhu Qing's younger brother, also from Dragon Isle. Do you remember Zhu Qing? The female dragon who went to that place? Speaking of which, Brother Li, you bear a great deal of responsibility for that. Zhu Qing went to that place and hasn't returned since, apparently encountering some danger. Her brother left Dragon Isle to rescue his sister—such deep sibling affection, truly moving. But he learned that you were the cause of all this, so his impression of you isn't very good..."

Li Jiao's eyes nearly rolled back, leaving only the whites visible.

"Oh, right," Yang Kai continued, fanning the flames, "this brother here seems to have no tolerance for those with impure bloodlines—he kills every one he sees. I did mention your situation to him before, but I don't know if he listened. In any case, this is a matter between Dragon Isle and your Li Dragon Palace. As an outsider, it's really not my place to interfere."

Only now did Li Jiao understand what crisis Yang Kai had been referring to.

This was indeed a major crisis. No matter how hard he racked his brains, he couldn't understand why members of the rare Dragon Clan kept appearing before him, and all of them were connected to the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace.

He increasingly felt that behind Lingxiao Palace stood the shadow of Dragon Isle. In this situation, even if this member of the Dragon Clan were to kill him, he would have no one to appeal to, much less expect his subordinates to avenge him.

He wanted to speak, but no matter how hard he tried, he couldn't utter a word. Left with no choice, he raised a hand and held up three fingers, shaking them repeatedly.

"Brother Li, what do you mean by that? I can't understand your gesture," Yang Kai said, looking utterly confused.

Li Jiao kept shaking his hand.

Yang Kai ignored him.

The three fingers became two, then finally just one.

Only then did Yang Kai say, "I think I understand. Since Brother Li is so sincere, perhaps we can negotiate."

He turned to Zhu Lie and said, "Let him go for now. If you really kill him, don't expect to find your sister."

Zhu Lie snorted coldly and flung Li Jiao away like a rag doll.

With a loud thud, Li Jiao crashed heavily to the side, rolling several times before finally steadying himself. He hurriedly got up, coughing violently.

After finally regaining his composure, he didn't even have the courage to look at Zhu Lie. His gaze darted about in fear. If it were any other Third Stage Emperor here, he wouldn't have acted so pathetically, but in the face of a pure-blooded Dragon Clan member, he simply couldn't muster any resistance or resentment. He only felt utterly inferior, as if he deserved this abuse.

"Brother Li, there's no time to lose. We should set out now," Yang Kai said, looking at Li Jiao.

Li Jiao nodded weakly.

Yang Kai chuckled, "Don't give me that aggrieved look. I'm not asking for your life, just for you to show us the way. Besides, you've gained something from this too—I, as Palace Master, can extend your repayment deadline by one year."

Li Jiao's mouth twitched uncontrollably, regretting it so much that his guts turned green. If he'd known Yang Kai was bringing a Dragon Clan member, he would have agreed to anything. He wouldn't have tried to bargain. Now, the original three-year deadline had become one year, and it was his own doing.

Li Jiao turned around and instructed the many Emperor Realm experts, "Please look after the Palace. I'll be back soon."

The elders nodded repeatedly, looking at Li Jiao with sympathy. They knew his situation well and understood he wasn't cowardly—he'd just met his nemesis and had no choice.

Not wanting to linger, Li Jiao urged Yang Kai to get on the road quickly.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle was summoned, and the three entered it, transforming into a stream of light that shot northward.

Among the Four Great Territories of the Star Realm, each territory had its own dangerous places and forbidden areas.

The Southern Territory had the Southern Marsh, the Eastern Territory had its Wild Ancient Land, the Western Territory had the Western Desert, and the Northern Territory had the Frozen Earth. These were just the most famous places; there were countless other dangerous spots of all sizes.

But in terms of danger and mortality rate, if the Frozen Earth of the Northern Territory was second, nowhere else could claim first.

The Frozen Earth of the Northern Territory had earned its name as the most treacherous place throughout the entire Star Realm—a forbidden zone where even Great Emperors dared not venture deep. Other dangerous places, such as the Southern Marsh or the Wild Ancient Land, still harbored traces of life hidden within their perils and held countless opportunities for those brave enough to seek them. Yet the Frozen Earth contained only death and endless ice, nothing more.

No one knew what the inside of the Frozen Earth was like, because everyone who entered died.

Li Jiao had managed to escape from the Frozen Earth by sheer luck, and only because he hadn't ventured too deep. The place he went to back then was just the edge of the Frozen Earth.

That was several hundred years ago. That year, Li Jiao had just advanced to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, feeling high-spirited and bold, thinking he could go anywhere in the world.

On a whim, he made a trip to the Frozen Earth.

That trip was a near-death experience. Afterward, Li Jiao swore never to set foot in the Frozen Earth again for the rest of his life. It was then that he brought the Dragon Blood Flower back to Li Dragon Palace.

He never expected that, after several centuries, he would have to go again.

Even after so long, Li Jiao remembered that experience vividly. Along the way, he shared quite a bit of information about the Frozen Earth with Yang Kai and Zhu Lie. Though it wasn't much, it was still somewhat useful.

According to Li Jiao, the greatest feature of the Frozen Earth was its cold. It wasn't ordinary cold, but a chill that extinguished all life—a cold that was indescribable and impossible to resist.

That cursed place seemed to naturally suppress life. Nothing living could survive there for long.

This information was quickly confirmed.

The farther north the Flowing Cloud Shuttle flew, the colder the outside world became. All they could see was a vast expanse of white, making it impossible to tell direction.

Large snowflakes drifted endlessly from the sky, as if the whole world had been frozen. Apart from white, there was no other color to be seen.

Despite being sheltered within the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, the three could still sense wisps of cold seeping through, faint though they were. This was extremely unusual.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle was a flying Secret Treasure, and an Imperial Treasure at that, crafted by Hou Yu herself and equipped with many defensive functions. Even such an Imperial Treasure couldn't withstand the invasion of the cold, which showed just how harsh the environment outside was.

Li Jiao's face grew uglier and uglier. If not for Zhu Lie's intimidating presence, he would have already turned back and fled.

Ten days after leaving Li Dragon Palace, the three finally reached the edge of the Frozen Earth.

Li Jiao remembered clearly where he had found the Dragon Blood Flower back then, so he kept guiding the way.

Two days later, Li Jiao suddenly exclaimed excitedly, "Over there, do you see that Three Finger Mountain? That's the place! That's where I found the Dragon Blood Flower back then."

Yang Kai glanced at Zhu Lie, who frowned slightly and shook his head.

He and Zhu Qing were siblings, both red dragons, and shared a certain connection. If Zhu Qing were still there, he would definitely sense her presence.

But at this moment, he couldn't feel any trace of Zhu Qing's aura.

Regardless, Yang Kai still steered the Flowing Cloud Shuttle toward Three Finger Mountain. There was a valley between the peaks, and the shuttle slowly descended. The three stepped out one after another.

As soon as they left the protection of the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, a cold that penetrated to the bone and soul assaulted them from all directions. All three couldn't help but shiver and quickly circulated their Emperor Yuan to resist it.

This was only the edge of the Frozen Earth, yet it was already so cold. Ordinary First Stage Emperor Realm cultivators probably couldn't last long here. Who could imagine what it was like deeper inside?

Zhu Lie's dragon breath seemed to have some restraining effect on the cold. Seeing this, Li Jiao instinctively moved closer to him, only to be met with a cold glare from Zhu Lie. Embarrassed, he laughed and stepped back two paces.

Yang Kai released his Divine Sense, covering a hundred li around them. After a while, he withdrew it, shivering involuntarily.

In that short time, he felt his Divine Sense was nearly frozen. If he hadn't withdrawn it, it might have been damaged.

"Are you sure this is the place you told Zhu Qing about?" Yang Kai asked, turning to Li Jiao.

Li Jiao quickly replied, "Absolutely sure. I remember this place. Unless there's another spot in the Frozen Earth exactly like this."

Three Finger Mountain was a perfect landmark. Even after several centuries, Li Jiao's memory wouldn't fail him.

As he spoke, he beckoned, "I remember the Dragon Blood Flower was found in this direction."

Yang Kai and Zhu Lie followed. Soon, Li Jiao stopped at a spot, looked up at the position of Three Finger Mountain, and after silently calculating for a while, nodded solemnly, "This is the place. No mistake."

Zhu Lie said, "I sense a faint trace of dragon aura."

As he spoke, he raised his hand, and a flame burst from his palm, transforming into a lifelike fire dragon that struck the snow-covered ground.

The scorching flames melted the snow, quickly revealing a trench more than ten zhang deep, exposing the earth beneath.

The soil was frozen hard, even tougher than steel, but on top of it was a very distinct mark—a huge claw print.

Li Jiao exclaimed excitedly, "See, I told you! This is where the giant dragon fell."

That enormous claw print was clearly left by a giant dragon. Even after hundreds of years, it remained perfectly preserved beneath the snow.

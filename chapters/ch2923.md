## Chapter 2923 — Pursuit

The first time Yang Kai met these people was still back in Fenglin City. At that time, Gao Xueting and the others were only at the First Stage, yet to him, they were still lofty and out of reach.

Over the years, Gao Xueting was able to advance from the First Stage of the Emperor Realm to the Second Stage. Naturally, Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming did not fall far behind, each striving to catch up and making further progress in recent years.

Besides them, there was also a dignified-looking middle-aged man, dressed in a jade-belted brocade robe, who appeared extraordinary, as if he were a leader of some faction, holding an unusual status. This man possessed the cultivation of the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

Behind this middle-aged man stood a dozen or twenty martial artists, all uniformly dressed, most of whom were between the Emperor Realm and Dao Origin Realm. With just a glance, Yang Kai vaguely understood where these people came from, because among them, he saw a familiar face.

Gong Wenshan!

Yang Kai did not have much interaction with him, only having dealt with him in the Four Seasons Land. He remembered this guy was rather bold, even daring to provoke Wuchang of Tianwu Sacred Land at the time, and despite being hunted by Wuchang for days, he remained unscathed.

Since Gong Wenshan was here, it was obvious where the largest group of people came from.

The Gong family of Tianhe Valley! The middle-aged man at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm leading them should be the current Gong Family Head, Gong Zhen.

He was the foremost formation master in the entire Southern Territory, his reputation in no way inferior to Nanmen Dajun in the Northern Territory, and in some respects, even surpassed him. After all, Gong Zhen was the head of a family, while Nanmen Dajun was merely a solitary figure.

Xiao Yuyang held a spindle-shaped Secret Treasure in his hand, its brilliance flowing and quality extraordinary. It seemed he had used this Secret Treasure to forcefully bring everyone in. Yang Kai had already experienced the power of the poisonous barrier here, so a Secret Treasure capable of breaking through it could not possibly be ordinary.

Yang Kai cupped his fists in greeting to Elder Xiao, Elder Chen, Elder Feng, and the others in turn. A sense of time's passage washed over him—years like a shuttle. Back then, he had stood before these powerhouses with deference, addressing them as seniors. Now, he stood as their equal.

He greeted Gao Xueting last, and she nodded lightly at him. They were all from the same side, so there was no need for excessive formality.

“You really are… wherever you go, you cause trouble.” Xiao Yuyang wore a helpless, wry smile, clearly having a headache at seeing Yang Kai again.

After all, Yang Kai had only just left the Star God Palace not long ago. Even in the Star God Palace, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda had encountered problems. Several elders, including Xiao Yuyang, suspected it was related to Yang Kai, but since even the Great Emperor said nothing, they could not pursue it further. Fortunately, the issue with the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was not too serious, so the matter was left unsettled.

“I’m innocent.” Yang Kai replied expressionlessly. “I was merely entrusted by someone, and just happened to get involved.”

“No one is blaming you.” Xiao Yuyang waved his hand, his expression turning stern. “We’ve already heard the general course of events from Hua Yulu and the others, and we know this matter has nothing to do with you. In fact, thanks to you, the Southern Territory was spared a disaster.”

Yang Kai replied solemnly, “I am also a martial artist of the Southern Territory. It is my duty to help resolve calamities for the Southern Territory.”

“Tch…” Gao Xueting sneered from the side, clearly thinking of Yang Kai’s identity as the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace in the Northern Territory. She still held a grudge about this, always feeling that Yang Kai should not have established a sect in the Northern Territory and that it would have been proper for him to remain as an elder in the Shrine.

Yang Kai pretended not to hear, looking at Xiao Yuyang and asking, “Elder Xiao, since you’ve led the team here, what do you plan to do?”

Xiao Yuyang replied, “Just as you thought, we’ll reinforce the restriction and seal, and also designate this place as a forbidden area. No one will be allowed to enter or leave.”

Yang Kai stroked his chin and said, “That’s good, but there are always some reckless and overly curious people in the world. The more forbidden a place is, the more they want to break in, as if they’re afraid of living too long…”

Xiao Yuyang smiled slightly. “That’s why we need to rely on the power of the Gong Family Head.” He pointed to the middle-aged man beside him. “Once the restriction over there is reinforced, this entire landmass will be covered by a formation. Even if people are curious, they won’t be able to find a way in.”

“That’s for the best.” Yang Kai turned to the middle-aged man and cupped his fists. “Greetings, Gong Family Head.”

Gong Zhen’s expression was complicated as he returned the gesture. “Elder Yang, you are too polite. I have long heard of your great name, and today I finally have the fortune to meet you. You truly are a dragon among men.”

Yang Kai smiled in response. “Gong Family Head, you flatter me.”

Gong Zhen said, “The clan elder has caused trouble for everyone. On behalf of the Gong family, I apologize to Elder Yang.”

Yang Kai shook his head. “There’s no need to apologize. As long as the Gong Family Head reinforces the seal, that will suffice.”

Gong Zhen nodded slightly and said, “Although I’ve already heard the story from Hua Yulu and Palace Master Hua, I would still like to hear Elder Yang recount it once more, if possible.”

Since it concerned the reputation of the former Gong Family Head, he had to be cautious and verify everything.

“Of course.” Yang Kai gestured, “Let’s talk as we walk. The restriction is still deep underground.”

The group followed Yang Kai downward. As they walked, Yang Kai recounted everything that had happened that day in great detail. With his cultivation, recalling even the smallest matters was no problem. He spoke extremely quickly, but with great detail, including every restriction encountered along the way and everyone’s reactions.

Everyone simply listened, without asking any questions.

However, the moment Yang Kai brought up the Demon Script, the expressions of the several Emperor Realm cultivators visibly shifted.

They obviously also felt that this method was far too bizarre. Just a single character containing some kind of true meaning could cause someone to develop demonic tendencies, even making it their mission to break the restriction on the blood-red gate. But Yang Tai’s previous actions had already clearly proven this point.

Before long, the group arrived at the stone cave below. Yang Kai waved his hand, casting a Shamanic Art to illuminate the cave, and pointed ahead. “This is the gate sealing the Demonic Intent.”

Everyone’s expressions became solemn as they looked forward.

Yang Tai and Wu Kuangyi had died in front of the gate. The blood and flesh had not been cleaned up—not because Yang Kai was lazy, but because he had deliberately left it that way. Now, all that remained were dried corpses, with not a trace of blood to be seen.

All the blood had already been absorbed by the gate.

“There’s something else I must tell you all. While I was guarding and meditating here, the Demonic Intent inside the gate once tried to brand the true meaning of a Demon Script character into my mind. Fortunately, I was alert and withdrew in time, or I might have ended up like Yang Tai.”

Everyone was shocked upon hearing this.

Gao Xueting hurriedly asked, “Are you sure you’re all right?” Her beautiful eyes were filled with worry and tension, her concern overflowing.

“Absolutely sure,” Yang Kai replied solemnly.

Xiao Yuyang’s expression grew serious as he said, “If that’s the case, then your earlier speculation was correct. There’s not just one Demonic Intent behind this gate—there may be even more.”

Yang Kai shrugged. “Whether there are or not, this place must be completely sealed.”

“That’s exactly why we’re here.” Xiao Yuyang turned to Gong Zhen. “The next part will depend on you, Gong Family Head.”

Gong Zhen cupped his fists. “The Gong family will not shirk its duty.”

As he spoke, he drifted forward to stand before the gate, his Divine Sense surging as he began to examine the restriction.

Xiao Yuyang and the others focused their attention, ready to respond to any sudden danger.

After a moment, Gong Zhen said, “The situation is still manageable. Although the restriction is ancient, the one who set up this ancient gate must have anticipated this, so there’s a self-repairing formation in place. As long as it’s reinforced carefully, there won’t be any major problems.”

Hearing this, everyone finally relaxed.

Gong Zhen continued, “I’ll need a few days to come up with a plan. During this time, I’ll have to trouble everyone to stand guard.”

Xiao Yuyang said, “Don’t worry, Gong Family Head. I’ll stay here the whole time.”

Gong Zhen nodded lightly, found a clean spot to sit cross-legged, and then took out many strange items from his Spatial Ring, apparently preparing to deduce something.

Xiao Yuyang turned to Yang Kai. “Gong Yue has been corrupted by Demonic Intent and entered a demonic state to escape. This is a huge problem that must be resolved completely, or there will be endless trouble. Do you have any good ideas?”

Yang Kai grinned. “I can track his location.”

Xiao Yuyang’s eyes lit up. “It was indeed right to come to you first. Looks like you were prepared.”

Yang Kai said, “I just hope he hasn’t noticed.”

Back when Gong Yue entered a demonic state and left, Yang Kai had already made plans, secretly leaving a hidden mark on him. At that time, Gong Yue’s mind was in chaos, so he couldn’t possibly have noticed, making it easy for Yang Kai to succeed.

When Yang Kai told Hua Yulu and the others that there were two matters to handle, the second was to hunt down Gong Yue!

It was just that those three didn’t have the ability, so Yang Kai didn’t mention it and could only wait here for experts to arrive.

“I’ll guard this place. The matter of Gong Yue is up to you all.” Xiao Yuyang glanced at the several Second Stage Emperor Realm experts.

Chen Wenhao said, “Don’t worry, Elder Xiao. We will not fail you.”

The few of them had come together, first to examine the restriction on the blood-red gate, and second to hunt down Gong Yue. They had hoped to get some useful clues from Yang Kai, but did not expect that he could actually track Gong Yue’s location. That made things much easier.

With three Second Stage Emperor Realm experts working together, even if Gong Yue had the same cultivation, he could never be their match. What’s more, Yang Kai had now recovered to his peak, and he alone was enough to deal with Gong Yue.

“There’s no time to lose. Let’s set out now,” Xiao Yuyang ordered.

More than a month had already passed, and no one knew where Gong Yue had gone. If they delayed too long, the mark Yang Kai left might lose its effect or be discovered.

The group said no more and turned to leave.

Just then, Gong Zhen, who was deducing nearby, suddenly spoke. “If possible, I hope you can bring back the old Family Head’s body.”

He did not ask them to spare Gong Yue’s life, because he knew that once corrupted by Demonic Intent, even if spared, Gong Yue would no longer be himself. The old Family Head had been wise and illustrious all his life, but in the end, his reputation was ruined, even implicating the Gong family. Gong Zhen could not help but sigh with regret.

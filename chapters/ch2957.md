## Chapter 2957 — Sacred Tree Protection, Royal Clan Unharmed

"A mere Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator dares to act up before me?" Yang Kai's cold laugh accompanied a casual raise of his hand as he struck down toward Fu Shu without the slightest mercy.

He had seen it clearly by now. Human Sovereign City was indeed safe and free from the shifting interference of outside laws, but beneath that surface it was a muddy pool. If he and Zhu Qing, two outsiders, wanted to gain a foothold here, they had to display extraordinary martial strength and let others know they were not easy to bully.

The law of the jungle held true everywhere; if it ever changed, it only meant one was not strong enough.

Fu Shu’s strength was not high, only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, but Yang Kai’s eyesight was razor-sharp. He had long seen that this man’s cultivation was flashy and hollow, likely forcefully raised with some Spiritual Pills, miraculous medicines, or rare Imperial Treasures, so he could not possibly display the power he should have had. On top of that, the Eldest Prince looked drowned in wine and women, and his body had probably long since been emptied out.

Yang Kai could casually eliminate dozens or even hundreds of such men.

Yet Fu Shu did not possess even the slightest self-awareness. At that moment, he was only three zhang from Yang Kai. Once Yang Kai moved, how could he have any time to react?

Emperor Yuan surged, Spiritual Qi churned, and a gigantic palm imprint visible to the naked eye smashed straight down toward Fu Shu’s head.

The bald burly man’s face drastically changed, and he shouted, “Stop!”

"Shut your damned mouth!" Yang Kai cursed, his movements not ceasing but growing even more savage and relentless.

Fu Shu truly had not reacted in time. He had still been craning his neck, trying to peek at the beauty behind Yang Kai, and only when the palm imprint reached him did he suddenly cry out. His whole body stiffened as he trembled, completely at a loss for what to do.

Just as that palm had been about to smash Fu Shu into powder, a hazy green light suddenly surfaced from his body and instantly formed a protective barrier that wrapped him entirely.

Boom...

The protective barrier shattered, Fu Shu's hair whipped about wildly, and his figure became disheveled, yet he remained completely unharmed.

“What the hell was that?” Yang Kai was dumbstruck. In that instant just now, he had seen no trace of Fu Shu casting anything, yet that protective shield had appeared in such a bizarre way.

Yang Kai examined Fu Shu from head to toe, but found no signs of him wearing any Secret Treasure. Instead, his body was adorned with numerous useless ornaments and trinkets, serving only decorative purposes.

His first move failed, and the bald burly man had already charged in from the side. A flash of light appeared in his hand as a massive hammer suddenly materialized, nearly half the size of a table, its surface gleaming with a dazzling radiance that exuded an overwhelming sense of danger.

The bald burly man angrily shouted, “You dared show disrespect to the Eldest Prince, brat, go die!”

Muscles bulged all over his body. With one hand gripping the giant hammer and all his strength poured into it, he smashed down at Yang Kai’s head. In an instant, wind and thunder roared into the ears, terrifying all who heard it.

"Protect the prince! Protect the prince!" Yang Kai shouted loudly.

Zhu Qing glared at him. As her red hair flew, her petite body flashed and stood at Yang Kai’s side, then she raised one fair, delicate little fist and met the giant hammer head-on.

The bald burly man’s expression changed again, and he sternly shouted, “Get lost!”

He didn’t know why this red-haired woman was seeking her own death, but he knew the power behind his strike. Though her cultivation seemed somewhat extraordinary, she was still a delicate woman—if that blow truly landed, she would be severely injured if not killed outright.

How could he dare strike down at this woman whom the Eldest Prince had taken a fancy to?

Yet he had just gone all out, so even if he wanted to withdraw the blow now, it had already been too late. Seeing Zhu Qing blocking the front, he could only forcibly twist his body and shift the hammer’s path slightly, hoping she could escape disaster.

Zhu Qing's fist collided with the giant hammer, her delicate fist and the massive weapon creating an overwhelmingly striking visual contrast. Anyone watching would have had no doubt that Zhu Qing's fate would be tragically grim.

The result, however, left everyone flabbergasted.

A deafening clang rang out as Zhu Qing remained unmoving. The gale whipped her hair into a wild frenzy, while the bald burly man gripping the hammer widened his eyes as if struck by lightning. After a brief pause, he was blasted away like a bolt of lightning.

Rumble...

The walls of the teahouse and the neighboring buildings were smashed through in a line, leaving a series of gaping holes. In the distance, dust billowed as the bald burly man lay motionless amidst the ruins.

The crowd was left dumbstruck, especially the guards who had come with the Eldest Prince. They all stared at Zhu Qing, unable to believe that such a soft, seemingly boneless and petite body could unleash such heaven-shattering power.

This was even more unbelievable than seeing a demon running about freely in Human Sovereign City.

Zhu Qing shook her hand and wore an expression of disdain.

Outside the teahouse, the two seductive women on the sedan let out terrified screams.

How many years had Human Sovereign City known peace? No one had ever dared cause trouble in the city, much less clash with a prince. Upon suddenly witnessing such a huge scene, the two women had naturally been badly frightened. Their faces turned pale and their delicate bodies trembled.

Those screams awakened Fu Shu from his daze.

He stood stunned for quite a while before suddenly coming to his senses, then he gritted his teeth and shouted at Yang Kai, “You hit me?” His tone then turned much harsher, almost hysterical. “You actually dared hit me? This Prince...”

“Yes, I hit you, stop your yapping!” Yang Kai reached into the air and grabbed the Myriad Sword, then chopped down at Fu Shu’s head. Sword light blazed coldly, and a stern, chilling sword intent surged forth.

Fu Shu shrieked in terror and fled with his head in his arms.

The Myriad Sword pursued relentlessly like a maggot burrowing into bone, streaks of sword light slashing down upon Fu Shu's body, yet dealing him no injury whatsoever. Each time the sword light approached, that hazy green glow surfaced upon his body, blocking the attack.

When Yang Kai was strong, it became strong. When Yang Kai was weak, it became weak. As if it possessed spirit, it insisted on protecting Eldest Prince Fu Shu’s safety. Even someone as strong as Yang Kai could not break through that green protection.

He clicked his tongue in amazement. The world is truly vast and full of wonders. If he had not accidentally come to this Revolving World and entered Human Sovereign City, he probably would never have known such a thing existed.

“Help! Help!” the Eldest Prince screamed while fleeing in panic, with none of a prince’s dignity left. At that moment, he had only been an ordinary mortal running for his life, so rattled he had even forgotten he had once cultivated.

His guards finally reacted and rushed toward Yang Kai one after another to protect their master.

Though these guards were not weak, how could they be Yang Kai’s match? With the Myriad Sword, he cut them down one after another as easily as slicing melons and vegetables. However, he did not strike to kill—though blood filled the teahouse and wails rang out without end, none of them were in mortal danger.

But Yang Kai’s savage appearance had already scared the Eldest Prince out of his wits. He had no strength left even to run and was cornered in the teahouse. Trembling all over, he stared in horror at Yang Kai, who walked toward him step by step, and shouted, “Don’t come over, don’t come over. I’m the Eldest Prince. I order you to stop.”

Yang Kai wore a ferocious grin. Blood dripped from the Myriad Sword, making him look like an evil ghost that had climbed out of hell.

The Eldest Prince had wished he could simply faint and end it all, but fear fermented in his heart and made his mind even clearer than usual.

After only a few steps, Yang Kai stood before the Eldest Prince, tilted his head to glance at him, and slowly thrust the Myriad Sword toward him.

The Eldest Prince sucked in a cold breath, held it, fixed his eyes on the tip of the Myriad Sword, and felt terror beyond measure.

Just as the Myriad Sword came within an inch of Fu Shu, that hazy green glow surfaced once more, wrapping around the blade with remarkable resilience and blocking its advance. When Yang Kai's strength surged, it grew stronger; when his power weakened, it weakened as well.

“What is this?” Yang Kai used the Myriad Sword to tap that green protection and looked down at Fu Shu from above.

Fu Shu’s teeth chattered, and he had already become incapable of answering Yang Kai’s question.

A voice came from the side, “Under the Sacred Tree’s protection, the royal bloodline cannot be harmed.”

Yang Kai turned his head to look, only to see the goateed old man who had left earlier suddenly reappear in the teahouse at some point. He sat in the same spot as before, as if he had never moved at all, his gaze falling lightly upon Yang Kai.

Yang Kai raised his brow and said, “Sacred Tree protection? What Sacred Tree?”

The goateed old man replied, "It has nothing to do with you, nor does it concern me."

"You always speak in half-sentences—I hate people like you the most. What do you want?" Yang Kai said impatiently, fixing the goateed old man with an icy stare.

The goateed old man said, "You offended the Eldest Prince, injured so many of his guards, and even frightened him. Human Sovereign City already had no place left for you to stand."

"I'm so scared!" Yang Kai let out a cold laugh. "Next, you're going to tell me that if I want to live, I should hurry and grovel before the Third Prince? That only he can ensure my safety?"

The goateed old man smiled faintly. "A clever person needs but a hint to understand."

Yang Kai snorted. "Looks like everything that happened here was within your expectations. You were also the one who called over the Eldest Prince, right?"

The goateed old man shook his head. "Do not wrong me. The Eldest Prince's lust for beauty is known to everyone. When you and this young lady appeared in Human Sovereign City, someone went to inform him. This old man only foresaw the scene before us and came to watch the excitement." After pausing, he looked at Yang Kai and said, "Well? You still refuse?"

Before Yang Kai could reply, Fu Shu—who had been trembling in fear—suddenly looked at the goateed old man as if seeing his savior. His eyes lit up and he blurted out, “I remember now! You’re that… that… that fellow who always follows Third Brother around! Yes, that’s you! Hurry up and kill this man for me, and I’ll reward you handsomely!”

The goateed old man looked at the Eldest Prince with disdain. “This old man serves the Third Prince, so how could this old man help you? Eldest Prince, you are confused.”

Fu Shu’s face turned pale as he said, “Then what would make you willing to act? How about I give you those two women outside?”

The goateed old man glanced between his legs and sighed, “This old man is already advanced in years. It’s hard enough just to preserve a bit of my yang essence. Eldest Prince, I appreciate your kind intentions, but alas, I truly lack the strength to enjoy such blessings.”

“You... how could you be like this!” Fu Shu wore a deeply disappointed expression.

The goateed old man turned his head back toward Yang Kai. “Your answer still remains unchanged?”

Yang Kai hoisted the broad Myriad Sword onto his shoulder and let out a scornful laugh.

The goateed old man understood at once and said to Fu Shu, “Although this old man would not act, this old man could still point out a bright road for Eldest Prince.”

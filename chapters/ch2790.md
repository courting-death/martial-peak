## Chapter 2790 — Agreement

"This Soul Capturing Drum can shake the mind. Its power isn’t very great, but it excels in concealment, making it impossible to guard against." Hua Yulu said, then suddenly reached out and gently tapped the small flower drum.

Strangely enough, not a single sound was produced.

Yet a silent, invisible force rippled out, like a sharp blade slicing toward Yang Kai’s Sea of Consciousness.

With no warning, Yang Kai’s mind stirred.

But just as Hua Yulu said, the Soul Capturing Drum’s power wasn’t strong, so it failed to break through his Sea of Consciousness defenses. Of course, this was also because Hua Yulu hadn’t fully refined it and hadn’t used her full strength.

Even so, it was still quite impressive.

Imagine, two people with equal strength locked in a life-or-death struggle, and suddenly Hua Yulu activates the Soul Capturing Drum’s power—her opponent would surely suffer. Used well, this drum was absolutely a weapon for turning defeat into victory and killing without a trace, far more versatile than most Imperial Treasures.

"Good stuff!" Yang Kai tried the Soul Capturing Drum himself and praised it without reservation.

Hua Yulu replied, "Elder Yang flatters me."

Yang Kai asked, "The three of you explored together. You got the Soul Capturing Drum—what did the other two get?"

Hua Yulu answered, "One of them cultivated a secret technique in that cave mansion. I don’t know exactly what it is, since he never showed it, but judging by his expression at the time, it should be quite powerful. The other’s cultivation soared there. Originally, among the three of us, his strength was the lowest—he had only just advanced to the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. But when we left, his aura was the strongest of all."

"It can even make someone’s cultivation skyrocket!" Yang Kai exclaimed in shock.

Opportunities that can make an Emperor Realm cultivator’s strength surge are rare. This proved that the ancient cave mansion was truly extraordinary. The means of ancient masters were indeed remarkable—after so many years, there were still so many opportunities hidden inside.

If it could be fully explored, who knew how many benefits could be gained.

If Yang Kai had previously agreed to accompany Hua Yulu to explore the ancient cave mansion partly for Xia Sheng’s sake, now he was genuinely interested.

Cultivators don’t just shut themselves away to train. Going out to temper oneself is the most important path. The occasional opportunity often surpasses years of closed-door cultivation.

"Since Elder Yang is interested, let’s meet in two months at the Southern Marsh. How about it?" Hua Qingsi looked at Yang Kai and asked.

"Alright." Yang Kai nodded, naturally having no objections. It was just the right time to visit Star God Palace and cultivate in the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda.

The two immediately exchanged contact methods, each carving a Soul Brand into the other’s compass. This way, as long as the distance wasn’t too great, they could communicate with each other.

"Then it’s settled. When the time comes, Junior Brother, I’ll be counting on you to take care of Hua." Xia Sheng smiled slightly.

Yang Kai replied openly, "Senior Brother, rest assured. I’ll make sure to bring Palace Master Hua back safe and sound for you."

Xia Sheng smiled, neither agreeing nor disagreeing. Meanwhile, Hua Yulu’s cheeks flushed, and she secretly glanced at Xia Sheng, then showed a hint of disappointment.

Yang Kai took in the pair’s expressions, vaguely understanding their relationship.

"I’ll be staying in Linghu City for a few days. If Bian Yuqing comes out of seclusion, please let me know, Senior Brother."

"Don’t worry!" Xia Sheng replied seriously. "I’ll check on her often."

Only then did Yang Kai take his leave. As he turned to go, he suddenly looked back and said, "By the way, Palace Master Hua, do you remember anything about the barrier that blocked your path?"

Hua Yulu was taken aback, then nodded. "I remember. Do you need it, Elder Yang? I can draw it for you."

"That would be best."

Hua Yulu immediately took out a blank jade slip, infused it with her Divine Sense, imprinting what she saw that day, and handed it to Yang Kai. "This is the barrier. It should be an ancient formation. I don’t understand formations, so I just memorized it. But it should be accurate."

At her level, having a photographic memory was basic skill.

"I don’t understand either, but I can ask someone else." Yang Kai smiled.

Hua Yulu’s eyes brightened. "Elder Yang knows someone skilled in formations? That’s perfect. I was planning to visit the Gong family in Tianhe Valley, but now it seems unnecessary."

The Gong family in Tianhe Valley was recognized throughout the Southern Territory as formation experts. Hua Yulu wasn’t ignorant—she wouldn’t fully trust the other two Emperors. To break the formation and enter deeper into the ancient cave mansion, she naturally wanted to investigate it first.

And the Gong family in Tianhe Valley was the best choice.

But now, with Yang Kai’s promise, she could save herself some trouble. After all, if she went to Tianhe Valley and the Gong family asked about the formation’s origin, she’d have to lie.

"Leave it to me, Palace Master Hua. Rest and recover these two months, and we’ll meet again in the Southern Marsh."

"Thank you, Elder Yang." Hua Yulu nodded in gratitude.

As soon as Yang Kai left, Xia Sheng stood up and said, "Hua, do as you please. I’m heading out."

"Where are you going?" Hua Yulu glared at him angrily. "Leaving so quickly—are you afraid I’ll eat you?"

Xia Sheng’s mouth twitched. "What’s there to be afraid of? If I don’t work harder at cultivation, I won’t be able to keep my position as Senior Brother."

With his hands behind his back, he shook his head and walked out, disappearing in the blink of an eye.

"Stupid man!" Hua Yulu puffed out her cheeks in anger, her face full of indignation. With her host gone, there was no point in staying as a guest, so she left as well, leaving only a lingering fragrance behind.

Yang Kai had been staying at an inn in Linghu City for less than three days when Bian Yuqing, her aura overflowing, came to find him.

Inside the room, their eyes met. Yang Kai gave a slight nod and said, “Not bad at all.”

Bian Yuqing tucked a strand of hair behind her ear and smiled bitterly. "Thanks to your help, otherwise I wouldn’t be here today."

She was shocked. She had thought that after advancing to the Emperor Realm, she wouldn’t feel any pressure facing Yang Kai. But after meeting him this time, she was astonished to find she couldn’t gauge his depth at all.

If she hadn’t known he was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, she might have suspected he was a Third Stage expert.

How could the gap still be so wide?

“You can release your aura, but you can’t fully restrain it yet—your cultivation still isn’t completely stabilized,” Yang Kai commented. “Only when you can hide your presence at will will you have truly consolidated your realm.”

"I understand. Thank you for the guidance, Palace Master." Bian Yuqing replied earnestly, feeling a bit strange. Ten years ago, she was high above this young man, but now, the roles had completely reversed.

Her survival and advancement to the Emperor Realm were all thanks to him. Thinking back, it was truly moving.

Yang Kai smiled. "Looks like you’re ready to join my Lingxiao Palace."

Bian Yuqing laughed. "Does Palace Master not want me?"

Yang Kai laughed and waved her off. "How could that be? Come, let’s go."

Leaving Linghu City, they flew onward. Bian Yuqing continued to stabilize her newly advanced cultivation along the way.

After two days of travel like this, the results were clear—Bian Yuqing’s Emperor Realm aura had become much more restrained.

"Palace Master, where are we going now?" Bian Yuqing couldn’t help but ask, seeing Yang Kai so relaxed.

"Sending you back to Lingxiao Palace!" Yang Kai replied.

"Back to the Palace…" Bian Yuqing was stunned. "But isn’t Lingxiao Palace in the Northern Territory?"

The Northern Territory and Southern Territory were separated by billions of li. Flying all the way would take years, and any delays could easily add another decade.

She wanted to suggest speeding up or using a Space Array to teleport a few times to save time, but seeing Yang Kai had no such intention, Bian Yuqing kept quiet.

Anyway, she had already joined Lingxiao Palace. Whatever Yang Kai said was fine—she had nothing else to worry about, and even her life was saved by him.

Three days later, they arrived at a desolate, ruined place.

"If I’m not mistaken, this should be Thousand Leaves Sect?" Bian Yuqing landed with Yang Kai, looked around, and spoke.

"It is Thousand Leaves Sect!"

Bian Yuqing sighed. "Sects rise and fall like the tides. Without strength, they can’t stand."

She seemed moved, seeing the destroyed Thousand Leaves Sect, and couldn’t help but think of Biyu Sect. The two sects had similar strength, and their fates were much the same.

"Come in!" While she was lost in thought, Yang Kai was already standing on the Space Array in the valley, calling out.

Though puzzled, Bian Yuqing obediently stepped onto it.

The next moment, the two appeared in a small world. Yang Kai didn’t say much, just led Bian Yuqing straight toward Emperor Heaven Valley.

When Bian Yuqing saw the cross-territory Space Array, she was utterly shocked. "This… this, could it be…"

Yang Kai said, "Through this, you’ll return to Lingxiao Palace. I have three jade slips for you to take back."

Bian Yuqing collected herself, took the three jade slips, and asked, "Who should I give them to?"

Yang Kai said, “Give this one to Hua Qingsi—she’s the Chief Steward of my Lingxiao Palace and oversees all internal affairs. Hand her this jade slip, and she’ll make proper arrangements for you. As for the other two, have Hua Qingsi pass them on to Nanmen Dajun. Got it?”

Bian Yuqing nodded seriously. "Got it."

"Go on!" Yang Kai waved his hand.

Bian Yuqing stepped onto the array.

With a flick of Yang Kai’s hand, numerous Upper-Level Source Crystals were accurately embedded in the grooves. When the Laws of Space were activated, the array hummed, light flashed, and Bian Yuqing vanished.

Just before Bian Yuqing disappeared, her lips moved as if to say thanks.

Yang Kai smiled and left Emperor Heaven Valley.

His goal: Star God Palace!

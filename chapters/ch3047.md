## Chapter 3047 — A Plan

A pale streak of dawn appeared in the east.

As the sky began to brighten, Yang Kai returned to Fu Chi's Spirit Island.

The night had truly been full of twists and turns. He had learned what had been arranged for Zhu Qing one month from now, captured Fu Ling, and planted a Soul Brand in her mind, placing her life and death under his control. Yang Kai suppressed the fury in his heart, knowing that anger would accomplish nothing now. The only proper course was to find a way to rescue Zhu Qing in one month.

At the grand wedding of two Dragon Clan members, all the Dragons would likely attend, and the Elders would certainly be present as well. The Great Elder and Second Elder were both existences comparable to Great Emperors. With his current strength, could he rescue someone right beneath their eyes?

His chances of success were negligible unless he could somehow bring the Great Elder over to his side and have him deal with the Second Elder. Only then would there be a glimmer of hope.

This was no idle fantasy; it was quite feasible. From what Zhu Lie had said, the Great Elder was relatively mild-tempered, while the Second Elder was the rigidly impartial one. The Great Elder likely did not approve of this marriage either, but the clan's rules prevented him from openly opposing it, so he had tacitly accepted the decision.

But what could Yang Kai do to convince the Great Elder to help him?

The Dragon Isle Token might be a good choice, but it was hardly a sure thing.

Yang Kai could only hope that Fu Ling would uncover some useful information for him. Otherwise, one month from now, he might truly face nine chances of death to one of survival.

Subduing Fu Ling had been a good decision. At the very least, he now had a chess piece planted among the Fu branch of the Dragon Clan. If this piece proved useless, so be it, but if it proved useful, it might produce an unexpected result.

Lost in thought along the way, Yang Kai returned to the Spirit Island and found Lu Sanniang and her daughter still waiting on the beach.

Seeing Yang Kai land, Lu Sanniang hurried forward to greet him. "Brother Yang, you're back?" Her beautiful eyes were filled with concern. She did not know why Yang Kai had gone out, but she could tell that something serious must have happened.

Seeing his gloomy expression, she did not dare say anything more.

Lu Yuqin was still young, and after personally witnessing yesterday's events, especially several people dying tragically before her eyes, she did not seem to have recovered from the shock. Her little face was pale as she clung tightly to her mother's embrace.

Yang Kai stood in place and thought for a long while before finally untangling his chaotic thoughts.

What he needed to do now was ensure that he could see Zhu Qing in one month. If he could not even meet her, how was he supposed to take her away?

He took a deep breath, a plan already forming in his mind. Turning to Lu Sanniang, he made his tone as calm as possible and asked, "Where is Yuan Wu's Spatial Ring?"

"Right here." Lu Sanniang hurriedly handed over several rings. "Madam Hua's ring and the others' are all here too."

When she and Lu Yuqin buried Yuan Wu and the others yesterday, she had collected their Spatial Rings. She had never considered secretly keeping them, so when Yang Kai asked about them, she immediately took them out.

There were five Spatial Rings in total. Four had belonged to Yuan Wu and the others, while the remaining one had been given to Yuan Wu by Fu Qi several days ago and contained the precious materials needed to build the palace.

Yang Kai swept them with his Divine Sense, took the Spatial Ring belonging to Dragon Isle, and returned the remaining four to Lu Sanniang.

"This..." Lu Sanniang hesitantly accepted them.

"Keep them," Yang Kai said, then leapt into the air and flew toward the interior of the island.

The palace construction site was bustling with activity.

In just a few short days, the palace had already begun to take shape. Although only the foundations had been completed, this was already impressive progress for a construction project of such enormous scale.

Some two hundred people were busy at work, and no one dared slack off. After all, this was a task assigned by the Dragon Clan, and an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon lived on the island. Who could guarantee that their idleness would go unnoticed?

Thus, everyone worked with utmost diligence, toiling day and night without rest. Only when they became truly exhausted would they take a few Spiritual Pills and recover for a short while.

"Well, what a rare guest! What brings Brother Yang here?"

A warm voice rang out. The sharp-featured, monkey-faced Wu Chen greeted Yang Kai with a cheerful smile.

Hearing this, the surrounding cultivators all raised their heads to glance at Yang Kai, displeasure appearing on their faces.

The reason was simple. Every cultivator here had been assigned a different task by Yuan Wu, and no one had been left idle. Even Lu Sanniang and her daughter had previously gone into the sea to search for Luminous Pearls. Yang Kai alone seemed to have nothing to do and was completely at leisure.

Such treatment naturally aroused everyone's dissatisfaction, but Yang Kai's methods were extremely mysterious, and he had easily crushed Yuan Wu before. Even if some people were displeased, they were unwilling to provoke a conflict with him lightly.

Thus, after glancing at him, they resumed their work.

"Brother Wu!" Yang Kai nodded.

Wu Chen frowned and walked over to Yang Kai. "By the way, Brother Yang, have you seen Sir Yuan? I heard he went to your place earlier, so why hasn't he returned?"

"Yuan Wu is dead!"

Wu Chen froze and stared blankly at Yang Kai.

The surrounding cultivators also turned to look at Yang Kai again, disbelief written across every face.

The corner of Wu Chen's mouth twitched as he asked hoarsely, "Dead? How did he die?"

"He offended the Dragon Clan, so they killed him."

Hiss...

A chorus of sharp breaths rang out, and everyone's expression changed.

When Fu Qi brought them here, he had directly entrusted everything to Yuan Wu, so they had assumed the Dragon Clan held him in high regard. Yet only a few days later, he was dead, and at the hands of the Dragon Clan no less.

The Dragon Clan's capricious nature and disregard for human life filled them with unease. They silently warned themselves not to make any mistakes on Dragon Isle, or they might die without even knowing why.

Wu Chen fell silent for a moment, looking somewhat mournful at the fate of one of his own kind, then asked, "What should we do now? Without Sir Yuan here to take charge, this palace..."

"You can take charge," Yang Kai said, tossing the Spatial Ring to him.

"Me?" Wu Chen froze and fumbled to catch the ring before smiling bitterly. "Brother Yang, please don't joke. What virtue or ability does this Wu possess to..."

"Someone has to take charge, so Brother Wu need not refuse. I am merely conveying the Dragon Clan's wishes. If anyone dares disobey your orders, just tell me, and the Dragon Clan will naturally deal with them." As Yang Kai spoke, his gaze swept over the cultivators watching them. His cold expression immediately silenced everyone.

The implications of his words were considerable, forcing them to reassess Yang Kai.

Wu Chen had clearly grasped something as well and asked in a low voice, "Brother Yang, are you in contact with the Dragon Clan?"

"Just take charge with confidence. This palace must be completed by the deadline, or everyone will die!" Yang Kai did not answer him directly, instead giving an ambiguous response.

Strictly speaking, his words were not false. He was in contact with Zhu Lie, and now he had also subdued Fu Ling. If anyone truly refused to obey, he could summon Fu Ling to keep them in line.

Wu Chen's expression became solemn, and he nodded. "I understand, Brother Yang. Nothing will go wrong here."

"Good." Yang Kai reached out and patted him on the shoulder before turning to leave.

If Yang Kai wanted to see Zhu Qing, he had to ensure this palace was completed on schedule. If there was no palace on the day of the wedding, how could the ceremony be held? He might not even get the chance to see Zhu Qing then.

He had originally intended to oversee the palace's construction himself, but the thought was too galling. What man under Heaven would concern himself with preparations for his own woman's wedding to someone else?

If he truly took charge himself, he might lose his temper one day and simply destroy the palace.

Thus, the moment he saw Wu Chen, he decisively pushed the responsibility onto him.

Out of sight, out of mind.

He could use this time to cultivate properly and strive for a breakthrough in both strength and realm. That would give him a better chance of taking Zhu Qing away when the time came.

With a plan in mind, Yang Kai returned to the beach, gave Lu Sanniang a brief explanation, and then headed straight out to sea.

An hour later, far out at sea, the ocean stretched endlessly in every direction. As far as the eye could see, there was only the blue sea meeting the sky, with no other scenery in sight.

Yang Kai released his Divine Sense to investigate his surroundings. Only after confirming that no one was nearby did he plunge headfirst beneath the waves.

After diving several hundred zhang beneath the sea, Yang Kai suddenly summoned the Xuan Realm Pearl. With a flicker, he hid inside the Small Xuan Realm, while the Xuan Realm Pearl sank straight toward the seabed.

Beside the medicine garden in the Small Xuan Realm, Yang Kai sat down cross-legged, cleared his heart and mind, and breathed in the surrounding Spiritual Qi.

It took him two full hours to calm himself completely and cast aside the many distracting thoughts in his mind.

He summoned the Purple Void Cauldron, then took out one spiritual flower and exotic herb after another, arranging them beside him in order.

Finally, he carefully took out three gorgeous spiritual flowers, fresh and delicate, their color as crimson as blood.

Dragon Blood Flowers!

These wondrous flowers were produced nowhere in the world except Dragon Isle.

They looked similar to the Dragon Blood Flower Li Jiao had possessed, but whether in quality or size, the two could not be mentioned in the same breath.

These Dragon Blood Flowers had been cultivated on Dragon Isle, so naturally, they were far beyond anything a half-baked cultivator like Li Jiao could produce. Placed side by side, Li Jiao's Dragon Blood Flower would look like nothing more than a weed growing beside a field.

There were not many Dragon Blood Flowers, only three in total.

He had obtained them from Fu Ling, though it would be no exaggeration to say he had robbed her.

When Fu Ling handed over these three Dragon Blood Flowers, her face had been filled with resentment and heartache. However, under the influence of the Soul Brand, she had been completely unable to resist Yang Kai's demand.

Although every member of the Dragon Clan received a fixed allotment of Dragon Blood Flowers each year, the number was not large. A Dragon with a Sixth-Tier Dragon Bloodline like Fu Ling would receive twenty at most, and even that depended on how many Dragon Blood Flowers matured. In an unlucky year, she might receive only ten.

The Dragon Clan did not enjoy cultivating. Their lifespans were long, and as they aged, their bloodlines would gradually become purer and stronger until they finally reached their limits and ceased improving.

Aside from that, they could consume Dragon Blood Flowers, which offered them a shortcut to growth.

The time to distribute this year's Dragon Blood Flowers had not yet arrived. Fu Ling had received more than ten last year, but she had already used almost all of them, leaving only these three, and now they had all fallen into Yang Kai's hands.

She regretted it bitterly. Had she known this would happen, she would never have saved them. Even swallowing them whole would have been better than handing them over to Yang Kai.

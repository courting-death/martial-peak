## Chapter 3114 — Heartless and Unjust?

The Young Sect Master had actually come. Now this was a rare visitor. He had not been here in years. Ten years ago, he had come every few days, always arriving in high spirits and leaving in disappointment. Later, he had vanished altogether. Why had he come today?

And... two Elders had actually come with him.

The steward disciple looked puzzled, but he did not dare show any neglect and quickly saluted the two Elders as well.

"There is nothing for you here. Get out," Han Qiancheng said, waving him off impatiently.

The steward disciple lowered his head and hurried away.

Han Qiancheng walked forward a few steps and stopped a dozen or so steps from the white-robed woman. His eyes burned like newly mined Fire Cloud Ore as he stared at that graceful, delicate figure. After so many years apart, she was still as intoxicating as ever.

A flicker of regret rose in him. He should have come to see her more often. Perhaps enough sincerity could have worn even stone down. But he quickly cast the thought aside. He was the dignified Young Sect Master of Yunxia Sect. What woman could he not have? Yet this slut alone treated him so coldly. If not for Yunxia Sect, how could you have advanced to the Dao Origin Realm? Most likely, when you first arrived in the Ancestral Domain, any number of experts would have devoured you until not even your bones were left.

Then he thought again of what that young man had said today, and Han Qiancheng grew even more irritated. He had taken this woman for pure and untouched, only to discover she had long since married, and he fumed over having failed to taste the first soup.

"Junior Sister Su, I trust you have been well," Han Qiancheng said, smiling as he looked at Su Yan. Although her cultivation was a full realm higher than his, he was the Young Sect Master of Yunxia Sect. How could he call her Senior Sister like everyone else?

"Young Sect Master." Su Yan calmly looked ahead, slowly rose, gathered her robes, and saluted. "Greetings, two Elders."

The two Yunxia Sect Elders said nothing, standing behind Han Qiancheng like wooden stakes. The lava cave was dimly lit, and they seemed to be hiding in the shadows, their expressions impossible to make out.

"May I ask what brings Young Sect Master here?"

Han Qiancheng smiled faintly. "I missed you, of course, so I came to see you. It has been many years since we last met, hasn't it?"

Su Yan's expression did not change. "Eight years."

"Eight years already." Han Qiancheng sighed with feeling. "Time truly passes quickly. It makes me think of the days when Junior Sister Su first entered Yunxia Sect. Back then, you were not so cold to Senior Brother."

Su Yan said, "Su Yan has never deliberately targeted anyone. If I have made Senior Brother uncomfortable, please forgive me."

Han Qiancheng laughed loudly. "No matter. That is simply Junior Sister's temperament. How could Senior Brother blame you?" He leaned forward slightly, like a cheetah about to pounce, brimming with aggression. "Eight years have passed. I wonder whether Junior Sister has seriously considered my proposal from back then."

Su Yan said, "I do not know which matter Senior Brother means."

Han Qiancheng pointed at her and laughed. "Junior Sister, why play the fool? Do you still not know what this Young Master wants?"

"Su Yan has no interest in romance."

Han Qiancheng smiled meaningfully. "Is that so?" He lifted one hand before his eyes, studied it, then rubbed his fingers and said casually, "I think it is not that Junior Sister has no interest in romance, but that your heart already belongs to someone else."

Su Yan said, "Since Young Sect Master already knows, there is no need to waste any more words."

Slut! Han Qiancheng cursed viciously in his heart. I am the Young Sect Master of Yunxia Sect. How am I inferior to that bumpkin? Even after all these years, you still cannot stop thinking about him. Just wait. Soon I will make you understand the consequences of defying this Young Master. This Young Master will play with you until you are rotten meat even wild dogs would not bother sniffing, then throw you away.

"That man's name is Yang Kai?"

Su Yan's face, which had been calm as an ancient well, finally shifted slightly. "How does Young Sect Master know that?"

She had only ever mentioned this to Ruan Biting and had never told an outsider that Yang Kai existed. Her first thought was that Ruan Biting had let it slip, but she immediately felt that was impossible. Ruan Biting was her Master in Yunxia Sect. Though they were Master and Disciple, they were as close as sisters, and Ruan Biting treated her extremely well. Why would she bring this up for no reason?

"Naturally, I know." Han Qiancheng chuckled. For the first time, something had caught Su Yan's attention, yet Han Qiancheng felt no sense of accomplishment. Instead, he felt defeated, all because he had mentioned a bumpkin's name. Pretending to be carefree, he said, "And I might as well tell Junior Sister that Yang Kai is in Yunxia Sect right now."

"What?" Su Yan cried out. "What did you say?"

She could hardly believe her ears, but forced herself to stay calm. "Young Sect Master, please do not joke."

"If you want to know whether I am joking, ask the two Elders."

Su Yan immediately looked past him at the two Elders with hopeful eyes.

One of them nodded. "Young Sect Master speaks the truth. Today, a young man calling himself Yang Kai came to the Sect looking for you. He also said..."

"Said what?"

"That he is your husband."

Su Yan staggered, and no doubt remained in her heart. Yang Kai was truly in Yunxia Sect! Had he come to find her? Could it be that he had really been in the Ancestral Domain all these years? A smile she could not suppress bloomed across that ever-cold face, and the whole world seemed to lose its colour, leaving only the radiance of that faint smile.

Han Qiancheng stared at her, utterly transfixed, wishing he could pull this woman into his arms right now and take liberties with her as he pleased. Playing with such an ice-cold beauty would surely feel different from before, would it not?

"Looks like he really is your husband," Han Qiancheng snorted, jealousy churning in his heart.

Su Yan said, "Young Sect Master, I request permission to return to the Sect."

Han Qiancheng shook his head. "I am afraid that will not do. Unless my father gives the order, you cannot go back." After a pause, he grinned. "Besides, what good would going back do you?"

"What do you mean?" Su Yan frowned.

"It is like this." Han Qiancheng sighed softly. "That Yang Kai does not know what is good for him. Coming to my Yunxia Sect to look for you was one thing, but he actually spoke wildly and damaged the Guardian Mountain Array. My Yunxia Sect has stood for several thousand years. How could just any stray cat or dog be allowed to humiliate it? By now... hm, he has probably already been captured. Tsk tsk, he must have suffered quite a bit."

A chill suddenly arose inside the scorching cavern, freezing one to the very heart.

Han Qiancheng looked at Su Yan smugly. He only feared she would not get angry. The angrier she became, the better. In a moment, she could beg him properly, and perhaps he would grant her request and let her meet her lover once before he died.

But his smugness quickly turned to astonishment, because the icy aura came quickly and vanished just as quickly. In the blink of an eye, Su Yan's expression had returned to normal.

Han Qiancheng said, "Junior Sister, are you not worried?"

"Worried about what?" Su Yan asked.

"..." Han Qiancheng was actually left speechless. Su Yan's reaction had gone far beyond his expectations. Wasn't this a little too heartless and unjust? That was her man, after all. Now that he was imprisoned, she should at least show some proper concern. How could she look so calm and indifferent?

"Worried about Yang Kai, of course!"

"There is nothing to worry about." Su Yan brushed back the hair beside her ear.

Back when he left the Star Domain, he had already been in the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. After all these years, with his aptitude, he had likely already reached the very peak of the Ancestral Domain, hadn't he? Yunxia Sect was not weak, but dealing with him would probably still be difficult. Moreover, he was proficient in the Power of Space and was best at escaping.

Han Qiancheng stared in astonishment for quite a while before saying, "Yang Kai will likely meet an unpleasant end."

"Then he brought it on himself."

"Junior Sister, he is still your husband. How can you abandon him and do nothing? If no one pleads for him, I fear my father will punish him severely. If he is injured or dies..."

"Life and death are decreed by fate, wealth and honour by Heaven. Young Sect Master is worrying too much."

"Junior Sister, I am willing to ask my father to spare him. Junior Sister should know my father will listen to me."

"I appreciate Young Sect Master's kindness, but there is no need to trouble you with this. The Fire Cloud Ore's Fire Qi is too heavy. Young Sect Master, please return."

"You..."

"Mm, if Young Sect Master returns and sees my husband, please tell him that I am waiting for him here and ask him to come over."

"Slut!" Han Qiancheng finally could not hold back and cursed aloud.

Nothing had gone the way he had imagined. He had thought that after coming here to tell her this news, he could take advantage of her concern and confusion to make certain demands, and surely she would not refuse. As long as she still cared about that bumpkin, he would definitely be able to fulfill the wish he had held for so many years.

But everything was beyond his expectations. She seemed utterly unconcerned with that bumpkin's life or death.

Could a person truly be this heartless and unjust? Even Han Qiancheng felt chilled on Yang Kai's behalf. A fickle, heartless woman like this deserved to be taught a ruthless lesson and ravaged, so she would know what a man was.

Suddenly, he could not help laughing. It seemed Yang Kai was not anyone very important in Su Yan's heart either. But since he had come all this way, he could hardly return empty-handed. He lifted his gaze and stared hotly at Su Yan.

Su Yan said, "Does Young Sect Master have other business?"

Han Qiancheng said, "Although the conditions here are a little crude, I imagine there will be a special flavour to it."

Su Yan frowned.

"This Young Master has truly never played with a woman in a place like this before. I am suddenly looking forward to it. I wonder, when I strip Junior Sister naked in a moment, will Junior Sister cry out?"

Clang. As cold light burst forth, the Profound Frost Divine Sword left its sheath.

A point of sword light rapidly enlarged in Han Qiancheng's eyes, and the aura of death descended over him. Although he had a Void King Realm cultivation, it had all been piled up with Spiritual Pills, and he had no experience at all in life-or-death battles. He simply could not block Su Yan's sword. If no one rescued him, he would likely die on the spot.

A large hand suddenly landed on Han Qiancheng's shoulder and pulled him backward. At the same time, another hand reached past Han Qiancheng's side and met that point of cold light.

With a light clang, the cold light shattered. Su Yan withdrew her sword and stood still. A middle-aged man in grey-white robes had appeared before her. He was the one who had blocked Su Yan's strike at the critical moment just now. This man had a First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation, not much different from Su Yan's.

Han Qiancheng was rescued by another Elder. Though he was completely unharmed, he felt as if he had just taken a trip through the gates of hell. Only after he steadied himself did cold sweat begin streaming down his forehead, and he flew into a rage, “You lowly slut, you actually tried to kill me?”

That sword just now had not been a joke. If it had really pierced him, he absolutely would not have survived.

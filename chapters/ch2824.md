## Chapter 2824 — Domain of Thunder

It had only been a short time, and Che still did not know what Shaman Niu liked. These days, they had been gathering information about Yang Kai, but could not find anything useful. All they knew was that he had entered Frost Snow City with Yu a little over a month ago, then stayed in the Divine Tree’s tree hollow. If not for him concocting and selling that miraculous healing medicine, he would have remained so low-key that no one would have paid him any attention.

Someone like him should not remain obscure; he ought to stay in the Frost Snow Tribe and shine with astonishing brilliance.

“Lord Che…” Across from him, Yang Kai grinned mockingly. “Is this how your Frost Snow Tribe treats its guests?”

Che’s expression did not change as they spoke, “Since you appeared here, it seems you understand my intentions. I sincerely invite you to join the Frost Snow Tribe. What do you think?”

Yang Kai replied, “I can feel your sincerity, Lord Che. If there’s nothing else, I’ll be leaving.”

Che’s brow furrowed, clearly displeased by Yang Kai’s casual and unrestrained attitude. Although they were not from the same tribe, weaker shamans were supposed to show respect to stronger shamans, yet Che felt no respect at all from Shaman Niu.

Still, this matter was their fault to begin with, so Che could not criticize him. Arranging for people to secretly learn from him was not honorable, and the other party generously taught everything without reservation. The Frost Snow Tribe was deeply grateful. Now, wanting to keep him as well was a bit greedy.

“Not even a thousand pieces of gold could buy my willingness!” Yang Kai shook his head slightly, his tone turning cold. “So far, I have no complaints about the Frost Snow Tribe. But if you keep pestering me, my opinion of your tribe might just change.”

“Ah!” Che sighed heavily, raising a hand. “Shaman Niu. I don’t want things to come to this, but since words fail, I’ll have to keep you here for now. I believe that after you spend some time in the royal palace, you’ll change your view of our tribe. As you said to Yu, the Barbarian Tribe is one big family—serving anywhere is still serving, isn’t it?”

As soon as he finished speaking, a bolt of lightning shot from his palm, crackling as it struck toward Yang Kai. Halfway there, it suddenly burst apart, transforming into an electric net that caught Yang Kai off guard and wrapped him tightly.

Electric arcs danced, binding Yang Kai like a rice dumpling; his hair stood on end.

“Hmm?” Che frowned. Though he had not used his full strength and only intended to restrain, not harm, the fact that Yang Kai took the Shamanic Art head-on without changing expression surprised him greatly.

The other party was just an Upper-Level Shaman Warrior. By rights, he should have fainted immediately, but Yang Kai’s clear gaze showed no sign of losing consciousness.

“Lord Che…” At that moment, Yang Kai spoke slowly, “It’s not that I underestimate you. It’s just that if I want to leave… you probably can’t stop me!”

As he finished, the electric net covering him was suddenly shattered by a surge of power.

Che’s face changed, exclaiming, “Shaman Master!”

Yang Kai’s move revealed his true strength. This guy… how did he become a Shaman Master?

Just a few days ago, he was clearly only an Upper-Level Shaman Warrior. In such a short time, he had crossed a major boundary. Though it was only from Shaman Warrior to Shaman Master, the leap was significant. Countless Shaman Warriors were stuck at this threshold for life. Thinking of the information Yu had revealed earlier, Che’s eyes suddenly lit up.

He had struck gold!

This was not just a simple apothecary; Shaman Niu’s strength had grown at a terrifying rate. A month ago, he was a Lower-Level Shaman Warrior, a few days ago an Upper-Level Shaman Warrior, and now, a Shaman Master.

Such rapid advancement clearly showed he was favored and blessed by the Barbarian God. Given more time, Great Shaman Master, Shaman King, and Shaman Saint would not be out of reach.

The Frost Snow Tribe had no Shaman Saint overseeing it, only a few Shaman Kings, so it lagged behind the top tribes. But if another Shaman Saint appeared, the tribe’s status would rise dramatically.

No matter what, Shaman Niu could not be allowed to leave today. Even if it annoyed or angered him, he had to be kept at all costs, so that when he reached the peak of Shaman Saint Realm, the Frost Snow Tribe would shake the world.

With his mind racing, Che made up his mind. His aura surged, electric arcs danced across his body, and he smiled, “Shaman Niu, I’m more and more impressed by you. Stay and become one of our Frost Snow Tribe.”

Yang Kai snorted coldly, “I was born a South Barbarian, and I’ll die a South Barbarian. If you want to keep me, you’ll have to prove you have the ability.”

Che wanted to say more, but his expression suddenly changed. He raised a hand and formed a Shamanic Art Shield in front of him. As a Great Shaman Master, years of experience had made his use of this simple Shamanic Art perfect—neither too much nor too little, not a bit wasted.

Such a Shamanic Art Shield was not something a newly advanced Shaman Master could break.

He was full of confidence, but the next moment, his face changed drastically.

A clear cracking sound reached his ears, and the Shamanic Art Shield he had just set up shattered instantly. A not-so-imposing fist rapidly enlarged before his eyes.

The Shamanic Art Shield was broken!

Che could hardly imagine what kind of wild power was hidden in that small body. Such brute force was no less than the tribe’s strongest warriors empowered by the Bloodlust Art.

With a loud bang, Yang Kai punched Che’s face, breaking his nose and sending him flying with blood spraying everywhere.

Yang Kai felt no joy at his success; instead, he raised his brows in surprise.

In his sight, Che who had been sent flying suddenly exploded, spiritual power surging as he turned into a piece of dead wood.

At the same time, thunder roared behind him, as if a powerful force was descending upon him.

Scanning with Divine Sense, Yang Kai saw Che had somehow appeared behind him, floating in midair, holding a beast bone that radiated purple light and crackled with electricity.

Che pressed the beast bone down, and thick bolts of lightning crashed from the sky, carrying an unstoppable force.

Yang Kai shifted his steps and dodged, countless bolts of lightning falling and leaving huge craters in the ground.

Che raised his brows, secretly admiring the speed. Though they had just started fighting, he could already see Shaman Niu was not to be underestimated. Though he had only recently become a Shaman Master and seemed to rely on his powerful body, such strength was not something a Shaman Master could contend with.

Unfortunately, he was a Great Shaman Master! A whole realm above him.

With a casual wave, a lightning python shot from the beast bone, twisting as it bit at the void. As its mouth opened, Yang Kai’s figure suddenly appeared there.

Che seemed to have a prophetic ability, anticipating Yang Kai’s landing spot and catching him off guard.

“Give up. With your skills, you can’t compete with me. The Frost Snow Tribe eagerly awaits your joining!” Che spoke sincerely from above.

Yang Kai sneered, reached into the void, and summoned the Myriad Sword. He swept the sword, sending a ribbon-like sword light flying.

The sword light was sharp and unmatched, slicing through the lightning python and splitting it in two, then flying past Che and smashing a distant palace to pieces.

Che’s mouth twitched, eyes fixed on the Myriad Sword in Yang Kai’s hand.

Cangnan Village was remote, with no experts and no decent weapons. The villagers fought with stone spears and axes, but the Frost Snow Tribe was different. The tribe’s powerful warriors had their own weapons, forged from refined iron, and some even had Shamanic Artifacts bestowed by the great shamans!

The beast bone in Che’s hand was also a Shamanic Artifact.

He was not inexperienced, but seeing the Myriad Sword still shook him deeply.

That sword was definitely a Shamanic Artifact, and a very high-level one. Che had only seen such a high-level artifact once before, in the hands of the Frost Snow Tribe’s Shaman King.

How could a Shaman Master have such a powerful artifact? And to wield it so easily— even as a Great Shaman Master, he could not use it so freely.

What he saw before him was beyond his understanding.

Yang Kai pointed his sword at Che, his black hair flying in the wind, smiling, “Lord Che, I’ll say it again: if I want to leave, you can’t stop me. If you keep pestering me, I won’t hold back.”

That confident tone shook Che’s heart, but he gritted his teeth and said, “The Frost Snow Tribe needs you. Please stay!”

Stomping his foot in the air, a domain of thunder suddenly spread out from him, instantly enveloping a hundred zhang radius. Within the domain, lightning flashed and thunder roared, as if the end of the world had come.

“A domain!” Yang Kai grinned.

This domain of thunder was somewhat similar to the domains mastered by Void King Realm cultivators, but also quite different. After thousands of years, the Martial Dao had flourished and changed.

Che’s domain of thunder was stronger than any Void King Realm domain Yang Kai had seen. Even ordinary Dao Origin Realm cultivators would struggle within it.

Che was clearly fighting with passion, his hair wild, shouting, “Enter my thunder prison, submit to my control. Shaman Niu, surrender. You know I don’t want to hurt you.”

Yang Kai retorted disdainfully, “That depends on your ability.”

Che shook his head and sighed, “It seems I must show you my power before you’ll change your view of the Frost Snow Tribe.”

Raising his hand, he shouted, “Bind!”

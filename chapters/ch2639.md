## Chapter 2639 — The Fiancée Arrives

Half a day later, the other four hundred cultivators also thundered over and joined Yang Kai’s command to await his orders.

Yang Kai stepped out of the wooden hut, glanced at them, gave a brief reprimand, and then paid them no further mind, turning to re-enter the hut.

Once inside, Yang Kai couldn’t help but fall into deep thought.

His main purpose in coming here, under the guise of Xiao Baiyi, was to find Nanmen Dajun. If he found him, he would kill him if possible, or capture him if not. But he hadn’t expected to be assigned to guard a formation flag, tying him to this spot.

Since he had to guard the formation flag, he naturally couldn’t wander about. Besides, he didn’t even know where Nanmen Dajun was at the moment.

For a while, Yang Kai couldn’t help but grow anxious.

Judging by the Wenqing Sect’s preparations, the formation flags and plates were already set up. It wouldn’t be long before they broke through Ice Heart Valley’s Grand Guardian Array. If he acted too late, it might be impossible to turn things around.

At this thought, a trace of determination flashed across his face. He pushed open the door and walked out again.

The five hundred cultivators were now scattered around the formation flags, sitting cross-legged in meditation. When they saw Yang Kai appear, they all stood up, unsure what orders he might have.

But Yang Kai didn’t even glance at them, simply turning to walk away.

“Young Master Xiao…” a loud voice called out. It was Huo Han, cupping his fists and shouting.

“What is it!” Yang Kai frowned, looking rather displeased.

“May I ask where Young Master Xiao is going?” Huo Han asked in a deep voice.

Yang Kai snorted coldly, “Where I go is none of your concern.”

Huo Han’s face darkened, but he replied neither servile nor overbearing, “Young Master Xiao, you are mistaken. Since Elder Feng ordered you to guard the formation flag here, you should obey. If you leave your post without reason and Elder Feng finds out, he won’t be pleased. If anything happens here, who will take responsibility?”

“It’s calm and peaceful here. What could possibly happen?” Yang Kai shot him a cold look.

Huo Han smiled faintly, “Who knows? I’m just saying, just in case…”

Yang Kai said, “I’m going for a stroll to look around. I’ll be right back.”

Huo Han replied, “Even leaving for a moment is abandoning your post. If you insist, I’ll have to report this.”

Earlier, Yang Kai had made him suffer a loss. He bore a grudge, and now that he had the chance, he naturally wanted to make things difficult for Yang Kai. Besides, these Wenqing Sect disciples stationed here weren’t just assisting the Emperor Realm cultivators who had accepted the Recruitment Order to guard the formation flags—they were also tasked with monitoring those Emperor Realm cultivators.

After all, you can know a person’s face but not their heart. Who knew if these Emperor Realm cultivators were reliable? It was more reassuring to trust their own sect’s disciples.

So, every Emperor Realm cultivator who had accepted the Recruitment Order had Wenqing Sect disciples under their command. There weren’t many, but Elder Feng needed these disciples to keep the Emperor Realm cultivators in check.

Seeing Huo Han’s earnest yet smug expression, Yang Kai felt nothing but disgust.

He knew the other was taking revenge, but since Huo Han had spoken up, he couldn’t force the issue. If he really attracted Elder Feng’s attention, they’d only be watched more closely.

“Fine, I won’t go if you don’t want me to. What’s the big deal?” Yang Kai curled his lips into a faint smile, turned around, and went back into the wooden hut, closing the door behind him.

Huo Han stood where he was, a look of satisfaction on his face.

Soon, night fell.

As Yang Kai was pondering his next move, he suddenly heard a low murmur outside. Listening carefully for a while, his face darkened.

The five hundred cultivators outside were gossiping about him.

“Did you hear? Our Lord Xiao might have quite the background, but he’s completely henpecked by his wife.”

“Everyone’s talking about it. I heard his fiancée is hideous—so ugly you can’t bear to look, and she’s got a terrible temper. Lord Xiao was tormented by her at Qingyang Shrine and couldn’t take it, so he ran off to the Northern Territory.”

“Haha! I thought he was something special, but he’s just afraid of women.”

“Really? Is that true?”

“He said it himself at the Recruitment Hall. How could it be fake? There were plenty of witnesses.”

“I think Lord Xiao probably never saw a beauty in his life. That’s why he rushed to take the Recruitment Order, hoping to pick a few pretty girls after conquering Ice Heart Valley.”

“And even if he brings them back? With a fierce wife at home, he wouldn’t dare do anything. He could only look, not touch.”

“Pitiful, truly pitiful. To think he’s an Emperor Realm cultivator, yet he’s a disgrace to men.”

…

The gossip went on and on. Who knew which bastard started the rumor, but now it was spreading everywhere, the whole city abuzz.

Yang Kai had only made an offhand remark at the Recruitment Hall, never expecting it to be dug up and used to mock him.

No doubt, it was that bastard Huo Han’s doing.

Though it was all nonsense, being gossiped about behind his back—especially by his supposed subordinates—left Yang Kai furious and embarrassed. He couldn’t help but snort.

At the sound, the chatter outside abruptly stopped, but after exchanging glances, the crowd couldn’t help but curl their lips in sneers.

“Humiliated and angry!” Huo Han stared at the hut, sneering coldly.

For the next two days, Yang Kai stayed in the hut. Huo Han’s obstruction and surveillance forced him to reconsider his plans.

With his mastery of space divine abilities, if Yang Kai really wanted to leave, a mere Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator couldn’t possibly detect him. But since he was deep in enemy territory, Yang Kai had to be extra cautious.

On the third day, as Yang Kai was meditating in the hut, he suddenly heard footsteps outside. Then Cao Xu’s voice called, “Young Master Xiao, Cao Xu requests an audience.”

Yang Kai frowned, wondering what Cao Xu wanted. Since leading him here a few days ago, Cao Xu hadn’t shown up again—yet here he was today.

Suspicious, Yang Kai opened the door, fanning himself as he walked out.

He had barely steadied himself when a cold snort rang out from across the way, brimming with anger. Immediately, a wave of overwhelming pressure surged toward him.

Yang Kai’s face changed. He staggered back several steps, eyes wide as he looked ahead.

In front of him, Cao Xu stood with an awkward smile. Beside him was a woman dressed in white, her whole figure as pure as snow and frost. Judging by her figure, she was curvaceous and alluring.

But one look at her face was enough to make anyone lose their appetite.

Her face was covered in freckles, unevenly scattered. She had single eyelids, small eyes, a flat nose, and a big mouth. Though her eyes were bright, paired with such features, even the brightest eyes became as sharp as blades, sending chills down one’s spine.

Her ugliness was beyond words. Who knew what she had done to offend the heavens to be punished so?

“You… you… you…” Yang Kai’s eyes nearly popped out. He stared in horror, but after stammering for a while, couldn’t get a full sentence out.

“So, you really did run off here! Made this young lady search so hard!” The ugly woman gritted her teeth, her eyes fierce, as if she wanted to skin Yang Kai alive and eat his flesh.

Yang Kai broke out in a cold sweat, wiping his brow, “You… why are you here?”

He never expected this person to show up.

Though the woman’s face was ugly and unfamiliar, Yang Kai recognized her at a glance.

Ji Yao!

It wasn’t that Yang Kai had such sharp eyes, but the moment they met, Ji Yao deliberately released her aura. Having spent plenty of time with her, Yang Kai naturally recognized it.

Ji Yao’s current appearance was clearly the result of Shi Tianhe’s disguise. There was no trace of her real face.

He had left Ice Heart Valley alone a few days ago precisely to keep Ji Yao out of his plans, yet she had come looking for him, and with Cao Xu leading her.

What was going on!

Shocked, Yang Kai sneaked a glance at Cao Xu. The latter gave a dry laugh, “Young Master Xiao, this… young lady says she’s your fiancée and came looking for you, so… haha, please forgive me!”

Cao Xu’s voice was dry and helpless.

“Fiancée!” Yang Kai’s face turned black.

The five hundred cultivators meditating nearby all looked over curiously. When they saw Ji Yao’s current appearance, their faces changed. Some with weaker stomachs even turned away and vomited.

“So ugly, so ugly! No wonder Lord Xiao ran away from the Southern Territory. If I had a fiancée like that, I’d run too.”

“Pitiful, truly pitiful. He’s an Emperor Realm cultivator, yet ended up with such a woman.”

“A man can be killed but not humiliated. To marry a woman like that, better to die!”

The crowd’s discussion grew louder. Most of the cultivators genuinely sympathized with Yang Kai.

After all, he was an Emperor Realm master—what kind of beauty couldn’t he have if he wished? Yet he ended up with a fiancée like this; who could bear it? With looks like that, even if you turned off all the lights on the wedding night, you’d still be haunted by nightmares.

Huo Han and the other Wenqing Sect disciples couldn’t help but gloat, eyes wide, ready to watch the show.

“What’s wrong?” Ji Yao stepped forward, her aura pressing down. “By order of our parents and the matchmaker, you dare deny it? I may look a bit plain, but there are plenty who want to marry me. If you dare deny it today, I’ll break your legs!”

“Plain…” Cao Xu’s heart skipped a beat. He thought, this young lady really dares to say it. With looks like that, she’s ruining the word ‘plain’!

“You’ve got the wrong person. I don’t know you, go away!” Yang Kai covered his face, as if he couldn’t bear to look at Ji Yao, while secretly sending her a message, “Why did you come here? It’s dangerous, go back quickly.”

Ji Yao shouted hysterically, “Xiao, don’t be shameless! If not for the Sect Master’s order, do you think I’d agree to this engagement?”

At the same time, she sent a message, “How dare you run off and leave me behind! Back in the Ancient Land, you kept deceiving and taking advantage of me. Did you think I’d let it go?”

“How did you even know I’m using the surname Xiao now? Ah, you know how things were before—I was forced into it, I had no choice!” Yang Kai replied anxiously via voice transmission, “That engagement doesn’t count, it doesn’t count at all! From now on, let’s just go our separate ways, never crossing paths again, as if we were strangers for life!”

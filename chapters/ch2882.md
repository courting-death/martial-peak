## Chapter 2882 — The Time to Prove Oneself

Facing Yang Kai’s questioning, Fei Li replied hoarsely, “Mo Duo arrived very suddenly. None of us expected it. As soon as he came, he began making arrangements. I stayed by his side the whole time and never had a chance to send any messages out.”

Yang Kai remained silent, making Fei Li somewhat nervous. He did not know if Yang Kai would vent his anger over his tribe’s destruction on him. Fortunately, after a short while, Yang Kai asked again, “How much strength can Mo Duo exert right now?”

Fei Li let out a sigh of relief and answered, “He was injured while passing through the boundary. At present, he should only be able to use sixty or seventy percent of his strength. But if he’s given enough time to heal, he’ll grow stronger and eventually return to his peak.”

“Sixty or seventy percent…” Yang Kai murmured, seeming to confirm his own suspicions. Although Mo Duo was comparable to a Great Emperor, he truly could not display his full power. Yet even a Great Emperor at sixty or seventy percent strength was not someone he could contend with.

“Sir, I’ve already been exposed. I’m afraid I can’t go back anymore,” Fei Li said.

This time, he had come out together with four other Demon Kings. All four of them had died, and only he had returned alive. If there were no Demon Saint overseeing things, it might be fine, since his own strength was among the top of the Demon Kings. But if he went back to face Mo Duo, he might end up revealing some flaw.

“Mm.” Yang Kai nodded, clearly having anticipated this. “I want you to take me to Torrent Fortress.”

“Torrent Fortress!” Fei Li was startled when he heard this, instantly understanding Yang Kai’s intentions. “Sir, do you really intend to kill three hundred thousand demons?”

“Do you think I’m joking?” Yang Kai glanced at him indifferently.

Fei Li shivered and said worriedly, “But over at Torrent Fortress… there are more than a hundred thousand demons!”

Over the past month, more than a hundred thousand demons had died at Yang Kai’s hands. If the demons at Torrent Fortress were added, it would indeed be close to three hundred thousand.

But the largest stronghold Yang Kai had destroyed before only had ten thousand demons stationed there. Moreover, because of his constant hunting, the demons’ defensive lines had grown tighter, and all the scattered demons had been gathered in one place—Torrent Fortress, one of the major strongholds. According to Fei Li, there were as many as eight Demon Kings stationed there, and over a hundred thousand clansmen. Could Shaman Niu really be planning to attack such a terrifying place?

Yang Kai glanced at him and said, “I’m not alone!”

Fei Li was taken aback, but quickly understood Yang Kai’s meaning.

It seemed that the pieces Shaman Niu had planted among the demons were finally about to play their part.

Torrent Fortress had originally been the royal city of a major Barbarian Tribe. After the demons invaded, this tribe was wiped out. Naturally, the territory was seized, and this place became the demons’ heartland.

Over a hundred thousand demons had gathered here, and the Demonic Qi that rose up nearly turned the entire world black. Eight Demon Kings sat in command, with countless Demon Commanders under them, making this place as impregnable as iron.

Because it was still deep in demon territory, the demons here had not yet clashed with outsiders. Still, the bloodthirsty and belligerent demons longed every day to charge onto the battlefield and slaughter the weaker outsiders, conquering this land.

Fei Li’s uninvited arrival surprised the eight Demon Kings stationed here. However, Fei Li’s strength and reputation spoke for themselves, so although everyone was surprised, they did not show much wariness. Instead, they welcomed him warmly, probing for the reason behind his visit.

Fei Li kept his lips tightly sealed, acting extremely mysterious, which made everyone afraid to ask too much. Many suspected he was acting on Mo Duo’s orders.

Yura of the Might Demon Clan was also one of the Demon Kings stationed at Torrent Fortress. When his eyes met Fei Li’s, he understood much of what remained unspoken, which made him somewhat uneasy.

Torrent Fortress soon welcomed even more Demon Kings.

Shadow Demon Mo Ke Tuo. Flame Demon Lai Wen. Snow Demon A Shi…

In just three days, counting Fei Li, a total of seven Demon Kings gathered here from all directions, causing the number of Demon Kings at Torrent Fortress to suddenly swell to fifteen.

This situation was extremely rare.

After Mo Duo descended upon this foreign land, every Demon King under his command had their own responsibilities and territories to guard and would not leave easily. But the arrival of Fei Li and the others broke this pattern.

Even the dullest Demon King knew something was about to happen.

Everyone probed the new Demon Kings, and finally reached a conclusion: they were indeed sent by Mo Duo, but as for their true purpose, no one knew. Only Fei Li knew everything.

Fei Li shut himself away, leaving others with no way to approach.

This suspicion ended five days after Fei Li’s arrival. In the largest building of Torrent Fortress, all fifteen Demon Kings gathered together. Fei Li, now acting as host, sat at the head of the hall, the ghostly fire in his eyes as calm as a spring.

The other fourteen Demon Kings sat to his left and right, all silent, every gaze fixed on Fei Li. Fei Li looked up and suddenly declared, “There is a traitor among us!”

The great hall fell silent. After a long time, a Demon King finally called out, “Fei Li, are you joking?”

“Yeah, how could there be a traitor among us? What could those lowly outsiders offer that would make us Demon Kings betray our kind?”

“Fei Li, if this is a joke, it’s a terrible one.”

Several Demon Kings could not hold back and began shouting all at once.

Fei Li said, “This is Lord Mo Duo’s judgment!”

The noisy crowd instantly fell silent, their expressions turning serious. Their eyes darted around, carefully scrutinizing every companion present, suspicion in every gaze.

If Fei Li had said there was a traitor among them, they could have laughed it off and refused to believe it. But if this was Lord Mo Duo’s judgment, then the matter was grave.

Lord Mo Duo must have gathered enough intelligence, perhaps even already knew who the traitor was, which was why he sent Fei Li here. In other words, there really was a traitor among them.

“Who?” Mo Ke Tuo’s neutral voice rang out. She sat to Fei Li’s lower left, shrouded in pitch-black mist, her true appearance hidden from all.

This question echoed everyone’s thoughts. They all looked at Fei Li. As soon as he named a Demon King, that one would be beset by the others.

“Those who are guilty know in their hearts.” Fei Li put on an inscrutable air.

“Did Lord Mo Duo not give clear instructions?” a Demon King asked, frowning as he caught the implication.

Fei Li stood up and said, “Lord Mo Duo naturally has a solution.”

As he spoke, he pointed forward. Before everyone’s eyes, a vortex appeared in the void, deep and dark. Even the Demon Kings present could not see what lay within.

“What is this?” a Demon King asked curiously. It was the first time anyone had seen such a thing, and it felt very strange.

Fei Li said, “This is something Lord Mo Duo entrusted to me. Inside is a Sacred Artifact that reflects the heart. As long as you stand before it, the traitor will be revealed at a glance.”

Everyone’s expressions changed, all shocked by Lord Mo Duo’s methods.

“Please, everyone!” Fei Li said calmly.

The remaining fourteen Demon Kings looked at each other, all hesitant. No one wanted to be the first to try.

At the critical moment, Shadow Demon Mo Ke Tuo snorted coldly, her body wrapped in black mist. She stepped into the vortex and disappeared.

Fei Li spoke at the right moment, “Only those with a guilty conscience would hesitate!”

These words were rather severe, making the remaining Demon Kings tense up. Coupled with Mo Ke Tuo’s action, no one dared hesitate any longer, and they all walked toward the vortex.

One after another, the Demon Kings disappeared. In no time at all, every Demon King had entered the vortex.

Fei Li followed closely behind and stepped in as well.

It was still that same world as last time—no sun, moon, or stars, but the environment was quite pleasant.

When the others saw Fei Li appear, a Demon King immediately shouted, “Fei Li, where is the Sacred Artifact that reflects the heart? Why can’t I see it?”

He wanted to prove he was not the traitor, so he was a bit impatient. He never considered that this was a trap, and one set by eight Demon Kings working together.

Fei Li did not answer him, but instead turned to Mo Ke Tuo and the other Demon Kings and said, “Lord said, each of you gets one. The time to prove yourselves has come.”

The Demon Kings stationed at Torrent Fortress were baffled, but Mo Ke Tuo, Lai Wen, and the others became solemn, understanding Fei Li’s meaning.

In the next instant, Mo Ke Tuo’s eerie laughter rang out. She turned into a mass of black mist and enveloped the nearest Demon King. A scream immediately sounded, making everyone’s faces change.

Before they could react, Flame Demon Lai Wen, Might Demon Yura, Snow Demon A Shi, and the others all attacked, each picking a Demon King as their target, as if they bore deep-seated hatred, every move aiming to kill.

Excluding Fei Li, the fourteen Demon Kings split into seven pairs and began fighting. Demonic Qi surged and intertwined, and all sorts of innate divine abilities burst forth in dazzling displays.

The Demon Kings of Torrent Fortress were caught off guard by this sudden upheaval and could not recover in time. Still, faced with crisis, they naturally fought back with all their might.

But for some reason, a force in this world seemed to suppress their strength, leaving them unable to bring out even thirty percent of their power.

Facing the ferocious Mo Ke Tuo and the others, how could they possibly be a match?

One Demon King after another fell, their demon hearts shattered and their corpses left without a place of burial.

Only when the last Demon King was about to die did he suddenly realize, “You were the traitors?”

Lai Wen’s entire body was wreathed in flames, as if he could burn all things in the world. The strange fire engulfed that Demon King, and he said coldly, “It’s too late to realize now.”

Under the scorching flames, that Demon King lasted only three breaths before being reduced to ashes, even his demon heart turning to dust.

After personally killing a Demon King, Mo Ke Tuo and the others knew they had set foot on a path of no return. Before, though they had been enslaved by Yang Kai’s Soul Brand, it was forced upon them. Even if Lord Mo Duo discovered it, they could still offer an explanation. If Lord Mo Duo was in a good mood, perhaps they could escape punishment.

But now that they had personally killed their own kind, they could never return to the demons’ ranks. From now on, they could only walk this dark road to the end.

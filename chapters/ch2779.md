## Chapter 2779 — A Lesson Learned

"Senior Sister, what brings you here?" Yang Kai was startled. As he spoke, he furtively glanced outside. Only after confirming no one had noticed did he close the Spirit Sword Hall's doors.

Gao Xueting frowned and said, "Why are you acting so sneaky? What are you up to?"

Yang Kai glared, "What could I possibly be up to? It's you who should be careful. You were locked up by Senior Brother Qiu for three months, yet you still dare to wander around. If Senior Brother Qiu finds out... he'll dock your Elder Contribution Points until you die."

Gao Xueting let out a mocking laugh. "That old fossil only put me in confinement—he never said I had to stay on Purple Bamboo Peak. The Shrine is vast; I can go wherever I please."

"Is that even allowed?" Yang Kai was utterly astonished, but what shocked him most was how casually Gao Xueting called that old man an 'old fossil.'

Yang Kai felt he really needed to reevaluate his understanding of Gao Xueting.

Gao Xueting coldly laughed, "Sooner or later, I'll snatch his position as Discipline Hall Elder... let him taste what it's like to have his Elder Contribution docked and be put in confinement."

Yang Kai felt a subtle, complicated mix of emotions. He wasn't sure if agreeing to join Qingyang Shrine as a Guest Elder at Gao Xueting's invitation was the right decision.

As the saying goes, when the upper beam is not straight, the lower beam is crooked. He had thought Gao Xueting was a noble and pure woman, but now it seemed, raised by Wen Zishan, her childhood was full of trauma and she never grew up healthy in mind or body. How noble and pure could such a person really be?

His previous impression was just a facade. Now that he truly understood her, his worldview was shattered...

"By the way, Senior Sister, what did you come to see me about?" Yang Kai didn't want to discuss usurping power, so he quickly changed the subject.

"Mm, I do have something." Gao Xueting nodded solemnly. With a flip of her palm, a Spatial Ring appeared, which she handed to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai took it suspiciously and swept his Divine Sense inside. Raising an eyebrow, he said, "So many Imperial-Grade Spiritual Medicines? Is this a benefit for becoming a Elder? The Shrine is generous, but I already have plenty of Spiritual Medicines, I don't need these."

Gao Xueting looked at him and said blandly, "You misunderstand. This isn't your benefit. Spirit Sword Peak is your benefit. If you want, you can ask Junior Sister Chen Qian for some servant disciples to help manage the flowers and plants on Spirit Sword Peak. That's a kind of benefit too. As for these Spiritual Herbs..."

"What are they for?"

"I want you to refine them into Spiritual Pills," Gao Xueting replied.

"Refine them into Spiritual Pills?" Yang Kai's face darkened. "With so many Imperial-Grade Spiritual Medicines, do you want me to refine Imperial Pills?"

"That's right, to refine Imperial Pills." Gao Xueting nodded. "Our Qingyang Shrine doesn't have its own Imperial-Grade Pill Master, but we've accumulated quite a few Imperial-Grade Spiritual Medicines. Now that you're here, naturally, it's your job to handle them."

"Why me?" Yang Kai was speechless.

Gao Xueting chuckled, "Those who are capable do more. Junior Brother, you refined a Supreme Mystic Pill in the Four Seasons Land back then. Others may have forgotten, but as the leading Elder, I remember it clearly. I checked—if you could refine a Supreme Mystic Pill, you were already at the level of an Imperial-Grade Pill Master. Years have passed, and you must have improved even more. Who else could handle these Spiritual Medicines? Should we let the other Elders waste them? Oh, and a few days ago, you refined a batch of Imperial Pills on my Purple Bamboo Peak. In just half a day, you succeeded. Impressive! There are a lot of materials here, but with your skills, a year or so in seclusion should be enough to finish."

Yang Kai was silent for a while, then said seriously, "Can I refuse?"

"Of course." Gao Xueting nodded. "No one's forcing you."

"Then I refuse!" Yang Kai handed the ring back.

Gao Xueting accepted the ring, turned to leave, and sighed as she walked away, "Ah, trying to help out of kindness, yet you don't appreciate it. Fine, forget it. That Divine Roaming Mirror—you'll probably never set foot inside it in this lifetime."

Yang Kai immediately raised his hand, "Senior Sister, wait!"

Gao Xueting turned, smiling sweetly, "Junior Brother, what else do you need?"

Yang Kai coughed lightly into his fist, "Senior Sister, I almost forgot about the Divine Roaming Mirror. The other day, I said I wanted to enter, but you said someone was using it and couldn't be disturbed. When can I go in?"

"Do you have any Elder Contribution Points?" Gao Xueting countered.

"No."

Gao Xueting sneered, "Two thousand points per entry. You don't have any, yet you still dream of entering the Divine Roaming Mirror? Still drunk, are you?"

"But last time..."

"Last time, you weren't an Elder yet. Now that you are, you have to follow the rules. Otherwise, anyone could go in, and the Divine Roaming Mirror wouldn't be so precious."

"Senior Sister, how could you do this?" Yang Kai was heartbroken.

Gao Xueting turned around and said earnestly, "Junior Brother, with great power comes great responsibility. As our Qingyang Shrine's only Imperial-Grade Pill Master, you have to shoulder the responsibility of refining Imperial Pills for us."

Yang Kai gnashed his teeth, "Senior Sister, you tricked me into becoming an Elder just for this! I'm so young, and you still have the heart to deceive me!"

Gao Xueting smiled, "I had this plan ever since you refined the Supreme Mystic Pill back then."

"I must have been blind..." Yang Kai was devastated, as if something beautiful had been shattered, his heart wrung with pain, his face a picture of blood and tears.

Gao Xueting said, "Don't be angry. If you don't want to refine pills, there's another way."

Yang Kai gritted his teeth, "Please enlighten me, Senior Sister!"

"Didn't you say before that the fifth disciple under Great Emperor Miaodan is the Chief Alchemist of your Lingxiao Palace? Since you have people under you, you don't have to do everything yourself."

"Hmm... that's actually a good idea." Yang Kai stroked his chin in thought.

After a moment, Yang Kai made up his mind, "What's in it for me?"

"One Imperial Pill, ten Elder Contribution Points! If you can provide two hundred Imperial Pills, you can enter the Divine Roaming Mirror once."

"Ridiculous!" Yang Kai jumped. "Ten points per Imperial Pill is only ten thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals. What Imperial-Grade Pill Master would work for so little?"

"Damn! Those bastards ruined my plan and even told you the exchange rate." Gao Xueting's face darkened as she muttered angrily.

Yang Kai's face darkened. "I can hear every word you're saying, you know..."

Gao Xueting pouted, "So what do you want?"

"One Imperial Pill, one hundred Elder Contribution Points. That's barely acceptable."

One hundred points meant one hundred thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals. Normally, an Imperial-Grade Pill Master wouldn't just produce one pill per batch—usually at least four or five. And hiring one from outside would cost at least five hundred thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals per batch.

Yang Kai's asking price was about the same as what the Qingyang Shrine would pay an outside Imperial-Grade Pill Master.

"Twenty points!" Gao Xueting bargained without hesitation.

"One hundred!" Yang Kai refused to budge.

"Keep dreaming!"

"One hundred it is!"

"Thirty at most!"

...

After a tough tug-of-war, with both sides bargaining hard, Yang Kai finally gave in. They agreed on fifty Elder Contribution Points per Imperial Pill, with no upper limit.

"Come, sign this Soul Contract." Gao Xueting flipped her hand, producing a contract radiating strange waves. It was a Soul Contract from Soul Palace, common in the Eastern Territory but rare and valuable in the Southern Territory. Who knew where she got it?

"Senior Sister, do you take me for a child? Junior Brother may be young, but I don't go back on my word." Yang Kai looked insulted.

"Just sign it," Gao Xueting urged.

Unable to refuse, Yang Kai branded his Soul Brand onto the Soul Contract.

Once the contract was signed, Gao Xueting beamed, carefully putting away the Soul Contract. She patted Yang Kai's shoulder, "Junior Brother, Senior Sister's future happiness depends on you."

Yang Kai was a bit flattered, but after a moment's thought, his face darkened. He asked in a bitter voice, "Senior Sister, this was Senior Brother Qiu's task for you, wasn't it? How much Elder Contribution do you get for completing it?"

"Same as you," Gao Xueting replied without looking up.

"What do you mean, same as me?"

"That old bachelor offered me one hundred Elder Contribution Points per Imperial Pill if I could persuade you to refine pills for the Qingyang Shrine. I give you fifty, keep fifty for myself. So it's the same!"

"Heh heh heh..." Yang Kai's smile turned sinister. "Senior Sister, you really are something, telling me this so casually. You're truly a good person. I'll remember this kindness."

Gao Xueting said lightly, "Ah, youth—one's path to growth is always strewn with thorns. Suffer a setback, gain some wisdom. In time, things will get better."

"I really must thank Senior Sister for your guidance!" Yang Kai squeezed out the words through gritted teeth, feeling his Qi and blood churn and a sweet taste rise in his throat.

Gao Xueting smiled sweetly, "Don't forget, you signed the Soul Contract. Work hard—when you can enter the Divine Roaming Mirror depends on your progress." She turned, stretched lazily, opened the Spirit Sword Hall's doors, and looked up at the sky. "Nice weather today."

Her delicate figure flickered, turning into a stream of light and vanishing without a trace.

Yang Kai clutched his chest, his face full of pain.

Half a day later, in a secluded valley behind Spirit Sword Peak, Yang Kai held his Elder Jade Token. With a thought, he mobilized the Earth Vein and Spirit Sword Peak's protective array, concealing a corner of the valley.

With this, even a Third Stage Emperor wouldn't be able to detect the hidden spot in this valley.

Yang Kai sighed, then took out all sorts of materials from his Spatial Ring, preparing to set up a Space Array here.

He hadn't planned to hide it at all; he had intended to tell Wen Zishan, because if Qingyang Shrine and Lingxiao Palace could be connected, it would benefit both sects greatly.

Hua Qingsi was currently collecting specialty goods from the Northern Territory. Once transported to the Southern Territory, they would surely fetch a huge profit. Likewise, if Qingyang Shrine gathered Southern Territory goods and sent them north, they could earn a handsome margin.

Not to mention, with a Space Array linking the two places, the two sects could support each other.

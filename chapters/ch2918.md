## Chapter 2918 — The Secret Behind the Door

When Yang Kai unleashed the seventh Shamanic Art, the massive water giant exploded with a thunderous crash, and Yang Tai's miserable scream was abruptly cut off. The raging, roaring sea instantly returned to calm.

Yang Kai's expression was stern as he released his Divine Sense, searching all around for any trace of Yang Tai.

But he found nothing. Within this illusory formation, Yang Tai's aura had vanished without a trace. After a short while, Yang Kai sensed the presence of the Formation Core and suddenly understood—Yang Tai had escaped; he was no longer here.

Just as Yang Kai had observed, at that very moment, in the stone cavern, beside a patch of blue mist, a figure suddenly flashed into view. Yang Tai appeared in utter disarray, and as soon as he emerged, he bent over, coughing violently and spitting out large amounts of fresh blood.

“Brother Yang, you... what happened to you...” Not far away, Gong Yue, who was presiding over the Sole Sovereign Formation, was greatly shocked by the sight. His mind wavered, nearly causing a flaw in the formation. At this moment, the Sole Sovereign Formation was already set up. Hua Yulu, Fang Zhuo, Wu Kuangyi, and Shen Bingru floated in midair with pale faces, arranged in a special pattern. Yet, it was as if they had lost all power to resist. Each of them bore a shallow wound, from which crimson blood trickled, drawn by Gong Yue toward the blood-red door.

That blood-red door seemed to have become a bottomless pit, devouring all the blood sent its way without leaving a drop behind. As the blood flowed in, the door gradually emitted a strange glow.

Gong Yue possessed the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, his strength already formidable. With his preparations and the aid of the formation, Hua Yulu and the others were no match for him. While Yang Kai and Yang Tai were entangled, the group had been subdued in short order, utterly unable to fight back.

Gong Yue had thought Yang Tai would capture that Yang Jiu without much effort, but seeing Yang Tai appear in such a sorry state, he realized he had been mistaken.

That Yang Jiu was actually able to defeat Yang Tai within the illusory formation—how could he possess such an ability?

“How is it going?” Yang Tai ignored Gong Yue’s shock, his demeanor no longer amiable but stern and impatient. At this moment, he seemed like a lofty king questioning his subordinate.

Gong Yue frowned and said, “There’s no problem on my end, but with two people missing, breaking the formation will proceed much more slowly. It will likely take another hour.”

Hearing this, Hua Yulu and the others, who were trapped, all looked ashen.

Using Blood Sacrifice to break the formation was far from as easy as it appeared. With every drop of blood drawn, they felt heart-wrenching pain, but the formation’s suppression kept them from crying out.

Upon hearing it would take another hour, the four were filled with dread. If that were truly the case, they would rather die immediately than endure such torment any longer.

“An hour is too long!” Yang Tai barked, turning his head with some apprehension toward the pale blue mist. Yang Jiu was trapped within, but Yang Tai no longer dared to enter and fight him again.

Though Yang Jiu's previous performance had surprised Yang Tai, it hadn't truly alarmed him. Yet those few secret techniques that Yang Jiu had unleashed at the crucial moment filled him with profound dread, as if he had encountered a natural enemy. His courage to face Yang Jiu again had completely dissipated.

“Use my blood!” Yang Tai moved closer to Hua Yulu and the others, his figure flickering as he took up another position in the formation. Forming a blade with his fingers, he slashed his own wrist, and blood gushed forth as though it cost him nothing.

“Brother Yang...” Gong Yue looked at Yang Tai in astonishment, instinctively sensing that something was off about him.

This was not according to plan. In their original arrangement, Yang Tai was also to join the Sole Sovereign Formation, but only as support. The main source of the Blood Sacrifice was still the other five. As long as their blood was drained, it should have been enough to break the formation.

But things had not gone as expected. First, Yang Jiu had spotted some flaws, leading to unforeseen events. Now, they didn’t even have enough people for the formation, and Yang Tai was not hesitating to offer his own blood, determined to break the formation at any cost.

“What are you waiting for? Hurry up!” Seeing Gong Yue hesitate, Yang Tai couldn’t help but urge him, his tone extremely unfriendly.

Gong Yue frowned and asked, “Brother Yang, is what you said really behind this door?”

At this point, Gong Yue was having second thoughts, mainly because Yang Tai’s behavior unsettled him. He couldn’t help but wonder if all this was truly worth it.

Yang Tai snorted coldly, “You’ve glimpsed the profundity of that character and know its power. There are more such characters behind the door, containing the ultimate secrets of the Martial Dao. As long as we break the seal, you’ll ascend to the Third Stage with ease. By then, who would dare look down on your Gong family of Tianhe Valley? As for your family’s reputation, once everyone here is dead, who will know what happened? This old man is with you; I won’t expose today’s events.”

Gong Yue’s expression shifted several times before he finally made up his mind. “Fine!”

The main reason he had agreed to cooperate with Yang Tai and deceive the others was because he had witnessed the power of that ancient character. With only the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, Yang Tai had wielded strength comparable to the Second Stage, proving the character’s extraordinary origin.

Although the Gong family was not small, their status in the Southern Territory was awkward due to the lack of a Third Stage Emperor expert. When people thought of the Gong family, they thought of formations, not strength. Yet Gong Yue longed for power, and reviving the family was his lifelong goal. But with his cultivation stuck at the Second Stage, it was hard to achieve greatness.

Yang Tai had promised him that after breaking the formation, he would obtain the secret to advancing to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm.

Gong Yue was moved, and so he joined forces with him.

Just as Yang Tai had said, after the Blood Sacrifice, everyone present would die. By then, who would know that the former Family Head of the Gong family had committed such a despicable act? It wouldn’t affect the family’s reputation.

Urged on by Yang Tai, Gong Yue changed his hand seals. Yang Tai immediately grunted, and blood gushed from his wrist like a fountain.

To speed up the process of breaking the formation, Gong Yue also increased the extraction from the other four. In an instant, Hua Yulu and the others' faces contorted with unbearable pain.

As the blood continued to flow in, the glow of the blood-red door grew ever brighter.

Despite losing so much blood, Yang Tai not only showed no discomfort but instead looked exhilarated, his gaze fixed unwaveringly on the blood-red door.

At that moment, a soft crack sounded from the pale blue mist nearby, and Yang Kai’s figure burst forth from within.

Without Yang Tai’s control and with Gong Yue distracted, the illusory formation created by a Formation Talisman could not withstand Yang Kai’s fierce assault. After Yang Tai left, it took Yang Kai only a short time to break the formation and escape.

With a sweep of his Divine Sense, Yang Kai immediately grasped the situation.

Under Yang Tai’s anxious and wary gaze, he swung the Myriad Sword in his hand, slashing down at Gong Yue with a sky-shattering sword light.

Gong Yue was the one presiding over the formation, and to break the Sole Sovereign Formation, attacking him directly would be the most effective strategy.

Gong Yue remained calm—or perhaps he had already anticipated this. After Yang Tai escaped the illusory formation, Gong Yue had foreseen this moment. He flicked his fingers, sending a Formation Talisman flying toward Yang Kai.

As the former Family Head of the Gong family and a master of formations, Gong Yue had no shortage of Formation Talismans—killing, illusory, trapping, all kinds.

The space was cramped, and Yang Kai, having just escaped, could not dodge. He was once again dragged into a formation.

Seeing this, Yang Tai cheered loudly, laughing heartily, while Hua Yulu and the others turned even paler.

They had no other hope now. Bound by the Sole Sovereign Formation, their strength was completely suppressed, and even their freedom was under Gong Yue’s control. They had no way to escape by their own power.

All their hopes rested on Yang Kai, but he was like morning dew—appearing for a moment and then gone—leaving them utterly disappointed. The little hope they had was instantly shattered, and they felt certain they would die this time.

“How long can you trap him?” Yang Tai turned to Gong Yue and asked.

Gong Yue frowned and replied, “Thirty breaths...”

Yang Tai seemed dissatisfied, but he knew there was no helping it. If Gong Yue could personally control the formation, he could trap Yang Jiu for longer. But now, Gong Yue was occupied, and with the formation left unattended, thirty breaths was the limit.

After just ten breaths, the formation suddenly shattered, and Yang Kai appeared once more.

“What?” Gong Yue was shocked, his face blank.

He had just said the formation could trap Yang Kai for thirty breaths, and even that was based on a generous estimate of his strength. But in reality, it lasted only ten before being broken. Just how strong was this Yang Jiu?

“Again!” Yang Tai shouted, urging Gong Yue to use another Formation Talisman.

Having suffered a loss once, Yang Kai became far more cautious. He flickered backward, opening distance between himself and Gong Yue while shouting urgently, "Family Head Gong, regardless of whether you acted willingly or were deceived, I tell you this one thing—Yang Tai has entered a Demonic State! Helping him like this will do you and the Gong family no good. It’s not too late to stop now!”

“You brat, what nonsense are you spouting? When did this old man ever enter a Demonic State?” Yang Tai retorted, afraid Gong Yue would be swayed, and hurriedly urged, “Brother Gong, don’t listen to him! Capture him quickly—that’s what matters!”

Yang Kai’s figure flickered and vanished, his voice echoing from all directions, “Behind that blood-colored gate are sealed ancient demons. Yang Tai intends to release them. You may not understand how terrifying the demons are, but I’m certain you would not wish to see them. Gong Yue, are you sure you want to be his accomplice?”

“This ancient cave residence has stood here for at least several tens of thousands of years, if not over a hundred thousand. How could any demons still be sealed here? Boy, you’re spouting nonsense and lies. Even if demons had once been sealed here, they would have long since died. Brother Gong, beyond this gate lies the ultimate profundity of the Martial Dao. Don’t be misled by that brat—open the gate, and you and I will share the benefits within.”

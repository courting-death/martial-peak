## Chapter 2718 — A Grand Gift

"Sister Hua, from this day forward, you are the Chief Steward of my Lingxiao Palace. From the Great Elders down to every disciple, all will be under your jurisdiction and heed your commands!" Yang Kai patted Hua Qingsi on the shoulder, looking at her with utmost seriousness.

Hua Qingsi blinked her long lashes, looking a bit dazed.

"Here, here, this ring contains two hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals, three billion Middle-Level Source Crystals, ten billion Lower-Level Source Crystals, and all sorts of materials. Take it for now—these cultivation resources are yours to allocate."

A Spatial Ring was pressed into Hua Qingsi's hand.

"Wait!" Hua Qingsi frowned, looking at Yang Kai. "When did I become the Chief Steward of your Lingxiao Palace?"

She had only sensed some commotion over at Thousand Leaves Peak and wanted to check it out. On the way, she ran into Yang Kai, who stopped her, shoved a resource-laden Spatial Ring into her hand, and then spouted a bunch of baffling words.

"I just said so, starting today!" Yang Kai looked at her solemnly, as if entrusting her with the great responsibility of maintaining the Sect's peace and leading it to glory.

"Why should I be the Chief Steward?" Hua Qingsi said, displeased.

Yang Kai replied earnestly, "I've thought this through. Looking at the whole of Lingxiao Palace, no one is more suited for this position than you, Sister Hua. You’re the most capable, so you should take on more. This is an important post—don’t let your Palace Master down."

"What whole Lingxiao Palace?" Hua Qingsi curled her lip. "There aren’t even that many people."

Excluding the disciples from Thousand Leaves Sect who were just staying here, Lingxiao Palace now only had the three Yao Kings as Honored Elders, Chi Yue, Ai Ou, and a few others.

Yang Kai scratched his face. "Everything’s hard at the start. There will be plenty of people in the future. Just get some practice in for now..."

"Practice on whom?" Hua Qingsi looked at him with disdain.

Yang Kai pointed toward the Thousand Leaves Sect side. "Aren’t there a few hundred people over there?"

"They’re not part of Lingxiao Palace."

"Not now, but who’s to say they won’t be in the future?" Yang Kai chuckled.

Hua Qingsi gave him a once-over and said, "So you want to poach their people."

Yang Kai put on a stern face. "Nonsense! Your Palace Master would never stoop to such low tactics. But people always seek higher ground, as the water flows downward—it’s just the way of the world. If they insist on staying and becoming disciples of my Lingxiao Palace, I can’t refuse. Of course, Sister Hua, if you can get the entire Thousand Leaves Sect to stay... that would be even better."

Hearing this, Hua Qingsi nodded. "You have a point. The Thousand Leaves Sect’s puppet arts are indeed impressive. If I could make use of them, it would be a fine thing."

Yang Kai was overjoyed. "So you agree, right? Then I’ll leave this task to you."

"Fine, I’ve got nothing else to do lately, so I’ll give it a try. But where should I start?"

Yang Kai pursed his lips. "Didn’t I just give you all those Source Crystals? Use them! They’ve just suffered a sect-destroying calamity and desperately need cultivation resources. With Source Crystals in hand, how could they not yield?"

Hua Qingsi shot him a reproachful look. "Source Crystals don’t just fall from the sky, you know. How can you hand them out for nothing? Still, if they actually contribute something to Lingxiao Palace, perhaps I could reward them with a few Source Crystals."

"Good, good!" Yang Kai was delighted. Sister Hua had just taken up the Chief Steward post and was already getting into the role, thinking about Lingxiao Palace and himself. He really hadn’t chosen the wrong person. Plus, she came from Star God Palace and was far more familiar with managing a great Sect than anyone else. Having her oversee Lingxiao Palace’s early development was the best choice.

"Do as you see fit. Your Palace Master grants you full authority!" Yang Kai said with satisfaction.

Hua Qingsi snorted. "If I do a good job, I want a reward! If you want the horse to run, you have to let it eat grass."

"Fine, fine. Whatever reward you want, you’ll have it," Yang Kai agreed readily.

"You said it—I’ll remember that." Hua Qingsi smiled, then glanced toward Thousand Leaves Peak. "Is Ye Hen about to break through to the Emperor Realm?"

"Who else could it be!" Yang Kai also looked over.

At that moment, Heaven-and-Earth Energy was gathering above Thousand Leaves Peak, with wild dragon-like torrents crashing down. The momentum was astonishing.

Hua Qingsi asked in surprise, "I thought Ye Hen hadn’t been in seclusion for long. How did he suddenly gain insight?"

"Maybe he’s found his own path," Yang Kai smiled faintly.

"His own path..." Hua Qingsi mused, her eyes flashing. "I’ll go take a look. Are you coming?"

Yang Kai shook his head. He had just come from there and already knew Ye Hen’s situation, so there was no need to check again. But it was good for Hua Qingsi to go—at such a crucial moment for Ye Hen’s breakthrough to the Emperor Realm, it was the perfect time for the Chief Steward of Lingxiao Palace to win hearts.

Five Saints Peak. Now the main peak where Ghost Ancestor, Chi Yue, and the others resided.

The so-called Five Saints were, of course, the five sworn siblings. Although Chai Hu’s whereabouts were currently unknown, Ghost Ancestor and the others hadn’t forgotten his place among them.

When Yang Kai arrived, the four seemed to all be in secluded cultivation.

Back when they first met Yang Kai in Tianhe City, all four had only just reached the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. After several years of cultivation in Thousand Leaves Sect, they had barely reached the peak of the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.

After all, Thousand Leaves Sect didn’t have the best cultivation environment.

The environment atop Five Saints Peak was truly remarkable—surpassing even Thousand Leaves Peak in grandeur. The mountain was vast and lofty, its ridges bold and majestic. Yet only four people resided here, as if dragons returning to the sea, living in carefree ease and utter freedom.

Yang Kai released his Divine Sense, letting the four sense his arrival, then with a flicker appeared in a palace atop the peak.

Though no one had cleaned it for years, the palace was still spotless, thanks to the protection of an array.

In no time, several figures shot in from outside.

"Palace Master!" The four greeted him with cupped fists.

Although Chi Yue and Ai Ou were technically Yang Kai’s elders, now that they had joined Lingxiao Palace, they naturally had to follow its rules.

"Sit!" Yang Kai called out, sitting cross-legged on the floor of the hall.

The four glanced at each other, then sat down as well, forming a circle.

With a casual flick, Yang Kai sent four Spatial Rings flying toward them.

"Each ring contains twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals, one hundred million Middle-Level Source Crystals, one billion Lower-Level Source Crystals, and some Spiritual Pills I refined. Use them for your cultivation. If it’s not enough, go find Hua Qingsi—she has more Source Crystals." Yang Kai explained.

The four’s eyes lit up. Without hesitation, they each accepted a ring.

They had all come from the Star Domain with Yang Kai, so there was no need for courtesy. Still, they were shocked by Yang Kai’s generosity. For years, they had struggled for cultivation resources—even as guests in Thousand Leaves Sect, they hadn’t received much. But here, Yang Kai handed out such a massive sum without a second thought.

Compared to him, they really were as poor as beggars.

With so many Source Crystals, they wouldn’t run out even after decades or centuries of cultivation. Each of them was filled with excitement, feeling that their hardships had finally come to an end.

"You all came to the Star Realm with me and have suffered a lot over the years, falling behind in cultivation. Now you must catch up—don’t worry about anything else. Otherwise, when people from our homeland arrive, you won’t want to be surpassed by them."

"Impossible. Those brats—" Ai Ou curled his lip in disdain.

Back in the Star Domain, he had been a top expert. Even if people from home did arrive, there was no way they’d surpass him in cultivation.

Chi Yue laughed, "Second Brother, don’t be so sure. Yang Kai’s cultivation speed is much faster than ours."

Ai Ou’s expression turned awkward. "Who can compare to him?"

They had all come to the Star Realm together, but now Yang Kai was already at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, while they were still at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm—a whole realm behind. Was it really just a matter of resources?

"Cultivation can’t be rushed," Gu Cangyun said with a bitter smile.

Ghost Ancestor nodded in agreement.

Yang Kai said, "That’s why I’ve prepared a grand gift for you all!"

The four squeezed their Spatial Rings, asking suspiciously, "Isn’t this it?"

Yang Kai grinned, then reached into the void and pulled out several sinister-looking banners. Ghostly energy swirled within them, and the sound of wailing ghosts echoed out, making everyone uncomfortable the moment they appeared.

Only Ghost Ancestor’s eyes flickered like ghostly flames, and he exclaimed in delight, "These are..."

"Netherworld Infernal Banners from the Netherworld Sect of the Eastern Territory!" Yang Kai explained.

The banners in his hand were all spoils of war from killing members of the Netherworld Sect. One of them was even an Imperial Treasure, the relic of Elder Hua Feichen. The rest, while not Imperial Treasures, were all Dao Origin-Grade.

Disciples of the Netherworld Sect began refining their Netherworld Infernal Banners from the moment they joined. These were their life-bound Secret Treasures—if the banner was destroyed, so were they.

In anyone else’s hands, these banners would be useless, but for Ghost Ancestor, it was a different story.

Yang Kai didn’t know exactly what cultivation technique Ghost Ancestor practiced, but it was definitely similar to the Netherworld Sect’s. Ghost Ancestor’s own Secret Treasure was the Ten Thousand Souls Banner, not so different from the Netherworld Infernal Banner.

That’s why Yang Kai had kept these Netherworld Infernal Banners all this time—waiting for the day he could give them to Ghost Ancestor to help him grow stronger.

Trembling, Ghost Ancestor took the banners from Yang Kai. Instantly, the ghostly aura around him surged, and his eyes burned even brighter. Even though he had never practiced the Netherworld Sect’s secret arts, he could tell these banners would be of great use to him.

"And these as well." Yang Kai handed over several jade slips to Ghost Ancestor.

Ghost Ancestor accepted them curiously, swept them with his Divine Sense, and exclaimed in delight, "Excellent! With these, this Old Man will soon break through to the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm!"

Those jade slips contained none other than the Netherworld Sect’s cultivation techniques and secret arts. If used together with the Netherworld Infernal Banners, Ghost Ancestor’s strength would skyrocket in a short time.

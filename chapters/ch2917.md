## Chapter 2917 — Suppressing Demons

With the force of a single palm, Yang Tai was sent plunging beneath the sea, the blood he spat out dyeing the azure waters red.

He paid a heavy price for his carelessness and underestimation of his opponent.

The Power of Time eroded his aged body, and the little life force he had left drained away like a deflating ball. This left him in utter panic, not knowing what to do.

Just as he was at a complete loss, Yang Kai had already pursued him, moving nimbly even underwater. With a few steps, he arrived before Yang Tai, raised his hand, and struck. The seawater churned as if boiling. Though this palm was not the Years Like a Shuttle Seal and did not possess such great power, it was still not something Yang Tai in his current state could withstand head-on.

Yang Tai let out a strange cry as his entire body was smashed to pieces.

Yet Yang Kai felt no joy at his success. Instead, he turned his head to look to the side.

Beneath the sea over there, a giant catfish with thick whiskers was swiftly swimming away, escaping several li in the blink of an eye.

That was Yang Tai’s true form. At the critical moment, he had drawn upon the power of the illusion array, creating the illusion of being killed before Yang Kai, while his real body transformed into a catfish and fled.

Yang Tai himself certainly did not possess such an ability, but with the help of the illusion array, he could do as he pleased.

Ten li away, the catfish leapt out of the sea, once again revealing the old man’s figure. Now, Yang Tai was no longer as calm and composed as before, but was instead in a wretched state, a large patch of blood glaringly visible on his chest. He bent over, coughing violently several times, like a terminally ill patient with little time left.

The power of Years Like a Shuttle continued to erode every inch of his flesh and every bone, leaving him flustered and unable to resist or dispel it.

Yang Kai also leapt out of the water, panting lightly as he gazed at Yang Tai from a distance.

Yang Tai was wounded by his attack, but Yang Kai's own condition was far from ideal. Under normal circumstances, he could eliminate someone like Yang Tai with a casual gesture, yet in his current state, killing him would require considerably more effort—especially given that his opponent held the advantage of favorable timing and terrain.

“Damn you. You deserve to die!” Yang Tai cursed through gritted teeth, his eyes full of venom.

Yang Kai raised his hand and sent several Moon Blades slashing toward him, then quickly followed behind the Moon Blades to press the attack.

He was not sure what was happening outside, but he knew that the longer things dragged on here, the worse it would be. Xia Sheng had entrusted Hua Yulu to him, so he had to bring her back safely.

Yang Kai could ignore the life and death of the others, but nothing could be allowed to happen to Hua Yulu.

Having seized the initiative, he was unwilling to be passive any longer and wanted to take control of the situation.

The Moon Blades howled as they swept past. Yang Tai no longer cursed, but upon sensing the terrifying lethality contained within the Moon Blades, his expression changed, and his body melted away like water.

The Moon Blades slashed through his body, yet failed to inflict any harm.

Yang Tai clearly began to activate the power of the array. The sea began to roar, sending waves a hundred zhang high into the air. The massive waves formed a giant water figure, with Yang Tai’s facial features and limbs faintly visible.

The water giant strode atop the waves. Each breath stirred the tides and carried terrifying power.

In comparison, Yang Kai rushing forward was like an ant facing an elephant.

In mere moments, Yang Kai encountered the water giant. He unleashed the Myriad Swords, and ribbon-like sword gleams crisscrossed back and forth, carving countless fissures across the colossal aqueous form. Yet no matter how fiercely Yang Kai attacked, those cracks would seal themselves in the blink of an eye, as if nothing had occurred.

The water giant formed by Yang Tai roared across the sea. Within the Illusion Array, he had brought the Water Element Secret Techniques of the Five Elements to the peak, immediately forcing Yang Kai into a difficult position.

He wanted to use Dragon Transformation, or even summon the Mountain and River Bell to settle things in one blow. But his injuries would not allow it. Now, he could not even bring out half his peak strength.

Yang Tai gradually gained the upper hand, and this advantage slowly grew until Yang Kai was completely suppressed.

The massive water giant roared, “I will tear you to pieces!”

He seemed to have forgotten his original goal of using Yang Kai’s blood for a Blood Sacrifice. Now, all Yang Tai wanted was to kill Yang Kai.

Suddenly, wisps of black qi emerged from the transparent water giant’s body.

Sensing the appearance of that black qi, Yang Kai’s whole body trembled and he cried out, “Demonic Qi!”

He was momentarily dumbfounded.

He actually saw Demonic Qi on Yang Tai. Though it was neither pure nor dense, it was undoubtedly Demonic Qi. Yang Kai could not be mistaken—he had just emerged from the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape and had dealt with the Ancient Demon Race for two years, so he remembered this aura vividly.

How could Yang Tai possess Demonic Qi? Yang Kai was utterly baffled.

In ancient times, the Demon Race had invaded this land several times, but had always been repelled, and the passages between the two worlds should have been sealed. Ordinary people should not be able to encounter them.

But then he recalled that he himself had once obtained Demonic Qi; only his was purer and more ancient than Yang Tai’s.

Yang Tai must have acquired something as well, which allowed Demonic Qi to arise within him.

Countless thoughts flashed through his mind in an instant. Suddenly, Yang Kai’s eyes lit up and he gritted his teeth, “Is it that secret art?”

Hua Yulu had said that half a year ago, the three who explored the ancient cave residence all gained some benefits. She obtained a small flower drum, Wu Kuangyi made great progress in his cultivation, and Yang Tai learned a secret art.

Previously, while traveling, Yang Kai had witnessed Yang Tai use that secret art. It was a power condensed from an extremely complex ancient character. Even after spending two years in the ancient world, Yang Kai could not fathom the mysteries of that character—he had only seen it in the Shaman God Hall, and even the records there were vague.

Now that he thought about it, that ancient character was likely not from this land, but perhaps a Demon Race character.

The Shaman God Hall had records warning future generations to be wary of such characters.

Unknowingly, Yang Tai had grasped the essence of that character, giving rise to a trace of demonic nature within him. When he fought Yang Kai to the death, that demonic nature erupted and awakened, thus giving birth to Demonic Qi.

All of this was speculation, but Yang Kai could find no better explanation. If this was the case, then Yang Tai’s previous abnormal behavior made sense. He was, after all, an Emperor Realm cultivator with the temperament expected of his cultivation. Such wild emotional swings and obsession were not normal for him, but if it was the hidden demonic thought at work, it was perfectly reasonable.

As he thought about it, a chill ran through Yang Kai. Perhaps it was the demonic thought born from that Demon Race character that had allowed Yang Tai to perceive the secret behind the blood-red gate.

If so, then whatever was sealed behind the blood-red gate must be related to the Demon Race.

“Yang Tai, you have entered a Demonic State!” Yang Kai shouted.

Yang Tai ignored him. The water giant he had become slammed down with a palm, raising a monstrous wave as if to shatter the world.

“Tell me, is there a demon sealed behind that gate?” Yang Kai continued to roar, this time using the power of his Divine Soul to send his voice directly into Yang Tai’s ears, making it impossible to ignore.

Some ancient Demon Race members were extremely difficult to kill. For example, the giant demon with black pupils that Yang Kai had encountered had been sealed outside Fenglin City by ancient experts. If what was sealed behind the blood-red gate was also a demon, or even a part of one, releasing it would be disastrous.

Last time, Yang Kai had managed by chance to seal that ancient Demonic Qi in his Dantian. There would be no such miracle this time.

This time, Yang Tai heard Yang Kai’s shout. The water giant’s movements paused slightly, then he flew into a rage. “How do you know? How could you know?”

It was as if some shameful secret had been exposed. Yang Tai was both embarrassed and furious.

Yang Kai’s heart sank to the bottom.

The water giant once again raised its palm and struck fiercely at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai stood his ground, no longer dodging. He took a deep breath and closed his eyes. When he opened them again, his aura had changed, becoming ancient and profound.

He unleashed his shamanic power.

After leaving the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, Yang Kai had always wanted to see if he could still use shamanic abilities. After all, he had cultivated that power within it for over two years—it could not have been for nothing.

So, on the way here, he had conducted several experiments.

The results delighted him. He could freely switch between Emperor Yuan and Shamanic Power. The two did not conflict, but his Shamanic Power was weaker than his Emperor Yuan, and Shamanic Arts were more cumbersome to use than today’s secret arts.

Yang Kai also noticed that while he could use Shamanic Arts, they were not as powerful as in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. At full strength, they were only about eighty percent as effective.

Yang Kai believed this was due to the laws of heaven and earth.

In ancient times, the Barbarian Tribe could only cultivate Shamanic Arts because the laws of heaven and earth were different. As time passed, those laws changed subtly, and each era’s living beings had their own characteristics.

Forcing himself to use ancient Shamanic Arts in this era, Yang Kai naturally faced some suppression.

This was the trend of the times, and no one could change it.

Incantations rang out as Yang Kai’s hands formed seals in rapid succession. Lightning-like radiance burst from his hands and swept outward with a roar.

“Demon Suppression!”

As the low chant sounded, the light swept across the water giant’s body.

No damage appeared on the surface, yet the water giant shuddered as if struck by a heavy blow. The fierce palm it had been about to slam down froze mid-motion, its momentum completely dissipated. In the same instant, a piercing and miserable shriek erupted from the water giant's mouth—Yang Tai's anguished cry.

The wisps of black qi on the water giant’s surface melted away like snow under the blazing sun as the light passed over them.

During the two years of war in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, the Barbarian Tribe had developed many Shamanic Arts targeting the Demon Race. This Demon Suppression Art was created at that time. It did not have much killing power, but could suppress the Demon Race’s strength.

Yang Kai had mastered dozens of such Shamanic Arts, all more or less the same.

After casting the Demon Suppression Art, Yang Kai chanted again, and another beam of light spread forward.

Yang Tai’s screams grew even more miserable.

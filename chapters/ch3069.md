## Chapter 3069 — A Dragon's Fall Brings Rain

What had originally been nothing more than two small bumps protruding from either side of his head now broke through like vigorous saplings sprouting from the soil. Two Dragon horns rose high into the air, making his appearance hardly inferior to that of someone with a true Dragon Bloodline.

His Dragon Pressure spread through the void like something tangible, making it somewhat difficult for the three Dragons to breathe. Their chests felt as though they were being crushed beneath massive boulders, leaving them unbearably stifled, while the circulation of their Dragon Yuan inexplicably slowed by a fraction.

The three Dragons were horrified.

Their horror and fear grew even stronger when they discovered that one of their Dragon Scales had begun flashing with intense light.

Those glowing Dragon Scales were precisely where the foundations of the Dragon Temple's Protection Technique resided. Such an abnormality had clearly appeared because the Protection Technique was becoming unstable.

When Yang Kai's Dragon body had been eight zhang tall, they had been unaffected by his bloodline suppression. But now that he had transformed into a fifteen-zhang Dragon body, that terrifying pressure made them feel a trace of fear arising from their bloodlines despite the Dragon Temple's protection.

Zhu Lie had said before that the Dragon Temple's Protection Technique could offset bloodline suppression to a very great extent, but only to a great extent, not completely. Once that suppression exceeded a certain limit, even the Dragon Temple's protection could not allow them to ignore the disparity between their bloodlines.

Just as Li Jiao had felt when facing Fu Chi, the three Dragons now felt deep dread and terror as they faced Yang Kai.

They had to end this battle quickly, or they would likely meet with disaster today.

The three Dragons instantly came to a decision. After Yang Kai transformed into a fifteen-zhang Dragon body, the looks in their Dragon eyes became even more resolute as they pressed forward without hesitation.

Yang Kai suddenly threw a punch. His house-sized fist struck the head of the giant Dragon attacking from the left at an inconceivable angle. A mournful Dragon roar accompanied the thunderous impact as the punch knocked the Dragon's head sideways and sent several scales flying from it. His enormous body spun uncontrollably before crashing into the distant sea.

However, with this brief delay, the giant dragon attacking from the right was already close at hand. Its outstretched dragon claw gleamed coldly like an indestructible blade as it viciously dug toward Yang Kai’s chest.

Yang Kai had nowhere to evade. His body was restrained, and he could not even use his Space Divine Ability.

Nothing good would come of being struck by that claw.

At that critical moment, something behind him suddenly moved in an almost instinctive reaction.

A Dragon tail swept over like a divine whip descending from the Heavens, carrying terrifying power as it lashed down.

With a sharp crack, the Dragon tail struck the giant Dragon squarely on the spine. Accompanied by a resounding Dragon roar, the Dragon attacking from the right plummeted downward like an asteroid.

Yang Kai wore a strange expression as his Divine Sense swept behind him, and he could not help raising his brow.

At some unknown point, a Dragon tail had actually appeared behind his rear.

This discovery surprised him somewhat.

Although more than a month of bitter cultivation had let him know that he had made great progress in the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, even he had not known what he would look like after using Dragon Transformation.

Not until this moment!

A fifteen-zhang Dragon body, Dragon horns growing from his head, a Dragon tail behind him, and golden light gleaming from every scale. These changes could almost be called earth-shaking, making him look completely different from when his Dragon body had been only five zhang tall.

Towering majestically, Yang Kai looked down upon his surroundings and felt as though the entire world were prostrate beneath his feet. For a time, his heroic spirit soared ten thousand zhang.

The giant Dragon who had been restraining Yang Kai could not help widening his Dragon eyes. All these changes had occurred in the blink of an eye, and two of his clansmen had been easily sent flying by a single punch and a sweep of Yang Kai's tail. Now left alone to face Yang Kai after his second Dragon Transformation, his heart involuntarily tightened.

His eyes flickered. Moving his Dragon head, he opened his bloody maw and bit down at Yang Kai's head.

Yang Kai raised an arm across his body to block. The sharp teeth immediately sank into the flesh of his arm, causing blood to pour out, but Yang Kai merely sneered. Under the giant Dragon's terrified gaze, he reached out with his other hand, seized one of the horns on the Dragon's head, and wrenched it viciously.

"Roar!" A Dragon roar shook the Heavens as the horn snapped and blood sprayed wildly from one side of the Dragon's head.

Yang Kai put the Dragon horn away, then extended a Dragon claw and raked it across a certain spot on the giant Dragon's abdomen.

Chi chi!

In an instant, a bloody gash several zhang long was torn open across the giant Dragon's abdomen. His writhing internal organs were faintly visible through the wound, and more than a dozen Dragon Scales flew off, one of which was the foundation bearing the Dragon Temple's Protection Technique.

As light flashed, the giant Dragon could no longer maintain his True Dragon form. His body visibly transformed, and in the blink of an eye he had returned to human form. He rapidly fled from Yang Kai's side, clutching his abdomen as blood streamed between his fingers, his face incomparably pale.

Yang Kai's two crimson Dragon eyes glanced at him disdainfully, without any intention of pursuing him.

Having lost the Dragon Temple's protection, this member of the Dragon Clan could no longer stir up any waves before him.

With a low shout, Yang Kai's enormous body dropped straight into the sea, strangely failing to raise even the slightest splash.

A moment later, violent energy fluctuations came from beneath the sea. Immediately afterward, columns of water soared upward as Yang Kai burst from the sea with a Dragon held in each hand. Water sprayed everywhere, and a rainbow instantly appeared in the sky.

These two giant Dragons were the ones Yang Kai had just sent flying. His enormous palms clamped tightly around their necks as he dragged them into the sky.

Yang Kai’s hair flew wildly as he threw back his head and laughed. Exerting strength with both hands, he smashed the two Dragons into each other, and with a thunderous boom, both were left dizzy and disoriented.

Boom, boom, boom, boom!

Yang Kai seemed to have become addicted to the game, repeatedly smashing them into each other. Dragon Blood splattered everywhere, and the sound of bones breaking rang out without pause.

Only after slamming them together dozens of times and thoroughly venting his anger did Yang Kai toss them aside. Following the same method as before, he broke the Dragon Temple's Protection Techniques bestowed upon the two Dragons.

At an altitude of a thousand zhang, the two members of the Dragon Clan were forced to assume human form as well. They swayed as though drunk and found it extremely difficult even to stand steady. Fleeing for their lives, they retreated dozens of zhang, their gazes toward Yang Kai filled with terror and apprehension.

Yang Kai paid them no heed. His gaze sharpened and locked onto a certain place. In the next instant, the Laws of Space surged and his figure abruptly vanished.

When he reappeared, he had already charged into Zhu Qing's battlefield.

The two members of the Dragon Clan who had been intercepting Zhu Qing turned pale with fright the moment they saw him appear.

They had been entangled with Zhu Qing not far from Yang Kai's position, so they had naturally seen everything that had happened earlier. Three Dragons had joined forces, yet in the blink of an eye Yang Kai had utterly crippled them, leaving them powerless to fight back.

How could the two of them possibly have the courage to face Yang Kai's ferocity now? When his Dragon Pressure crashed over them, their bloodlines were instantly suppressed to a certain extent.

They exchanged a glance, suddenly transformed into streaks of light, and tried to flee. Today's events were already beyond their ability to resolve. In other words, this was not a problem that could be solved by relying on superior numbers. If they wanted to restore Dragon Isle's reputation, only the Great Elder and Second Elder could personally take action.

They had to hurry over and report this to the two Elders.

But how could they still have such an opportunity?

The moment they tried to flee, the surrounding space became incomparably viscous, as though sealed by some profound force.

The Laws of Space.

The two Dragons were shocked and furious, desperately pushing their own pressure to break free from the influence of the Laws of Space. Before they could do so, Yang Kai had already charged in, seized one of them by the tail, swung him around several times, and smashed him hard into the other Dragon.

With a thunderous crash, both Dragons cried out miserably and flew backward. Yang Kai seized the opportunity to rush forward and rip one Dragon Scale from each of them, forcing them to reveal their human forms.

What followed was simple. With his fifteen-zhang Dragon body, Yang Kai was invincible before this group of Dragon Clan members below the Eighth-Tier, and no one could stand against him.

After he dealt with Fu Ling's opponent, the scene instantly fell quiet.

Yang Kai turned his gaze toward the depths of the sea, where the Incarnation was locked in a fierce battle with another member of the Dragon Clan, the two seemingly evenly matched.

The Incarnation's sudden awakening had pleasantly surprised him. After all, it had remained unconscious ever since merging with Shi Huo's Origin in the Wild Ancient Land that day, yet it had unexpectedly awakened this time, taking Yang Kai completely by surprise.

He had been paying close attention to the Incarnation's condition recently. By his calculations, it should still have needed some time before awakening. He did not know whether it had sensed the situation on his side and therefore awakened ahead of schedule.

Although it had awakened early, there did not seem to be any problem. Yang Kai could sense that the Incarnation had completely merged with Shi Huo's Origin. It was now the Divine Spirit Shi Huo!

After giving Zhu Qing a brief instruction, Yang Kai was just about to go assist the Incarnation when a Dragon roar that pierced the clouds suddenly rang out from the depths of Dragon Isle.

This Dragon roar was completely different from all those Yang Kai had heard before. It was exceedingly mournful, giving one the impression of a bird crying sorrowfully on the verge of death.

The moment the Dragon roar sounded, Zhu Qing's and Fu Ling's expressions changed drastically, and they turned to look toward its source.

Not only them, but every member of the Dragon Clan on Dragon Isle did the same.

The Dragon roar continued for a dozen or so breaths before abruptly falling silent.

In the next instant, Yang Kai suddenly reached up and touched his cheek when several drops of something landed on his face. Looking up at the sky, he frowned and said, "Rain from a clear sky. Dragon Isle's weather truly is strange."

A round sun hung high in the sky, which stretched clear and blue for ten thousand li without a single cloud in sight.

Yet raindrops were inexplicably falling from above, and they came in a torrential downpour that lashed down with incomparable ferocity.

What surprised Yang Kai even more was that the rain seemed to be filled with spirituality.

"This isn't rain!" Fu Ling's face was pale, and she had been staring toward the source of that sound the entire time. Hearing Yang Kai speak, she subconsciously replied as her delicate body began to tremble.

Zhu Qing’s expression was also ashen as she said in a solemn voice, “When a Dragon falls, it turns to rain.”

"What?" Yang Kai turned to look at Zhu Qing and could not help frowning. He vaguely sensed that something serious had happened; otherwise, Zhu Qing's expression could not possibly have been so grave.

"A member of the Dragon Clan has died!" Zhu Qing turned to him, her face filled with horror.

Yang Kai froze for a moment, and his pupils could not help contracting. "How do you know? That Dragon roar just now?"

Even if that Dragon roar had been incomparably sorrowful, it did not necessarily mean that a member of the Dragon Clan had fallen, did it? Perhaps they had merely suffered a severe injury.

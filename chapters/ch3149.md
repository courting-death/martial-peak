## Chapter 3149 — Nine Heavens Sacred Land

A short while later, corpses covered the ground. Across that vast stretch of land, no one remained alive except Yang Kai and Liu Yan.

In the end, not everyone had an iron backbone. Although the surviving Void King Realm cultivators joined forces at the last moment, they were only ants trying to shake a tree. After seeing how ruthlessly Yang Kai slaughtered people, one of them finally buckled under the pressure and gave Yang Kai the answer he wanted. He still did not escape death, but at least his end was quick.

"Nine Heavens Sacred Land, is it?" Yang Kai turned his head and looked in one direction. That name truly brought back memories.

Speaking of which, back when he was on Tongxuan Continent, he had been the Saint Lord of Nine Heavens Sacred Land. Later, he had taken a group of people from Nine Heavens Sacred Land to Dark Star, yet this time on Dark Star, he had seen no trace of them.

Thinking about it now, they had probably returned here together with Xia Ningshang.

This was their homeland, after all. With foreign enemies invading, they naturally could not stand by and do nothing.

Xia Ningshang choosing Nine Heavens Sacred Land as her base might well have been a way of remembering him.

"Let's go!" Yang Kai beckoned to Liu Yan. She darted to his side, then flew shoulder to shoulder with him toward Nine Heavens Sacred Land.

Meanwhile, just after Xin Xuanming was killed and the Eight Wastelands Formation Core was broken, an old man at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm suddenly trembled in another "ink spot" on Tongxuan Continent. His eyes opened from meditation, filled with lingering fear.

In that instant just now, he had vaguely sensed something, as if some immense crisis had descended. Frowning, he took out a Formation Plate, urged his Saint Yuan, and probed it for a moment. His face changed dramatically.

This Formation Plate controlled the Eight Wastelands sub-array at this location, but because of the Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array's special nature, it could also resonate with the other seven Formation Plates across great distances, allowing them to sense one another.

Through the Formation Plate's sensing, the old man discovered to his shock that the other six locations were safe and sound. Only the Eight Wastelands Formation Core gave no response at all. His side seemed to have lost contact with it.

An unbelievable thought sprang into his mind. Not daring to delay, he rose at once and swiftly entered a secret room. The room was covered in runes and formation patterns, clearly a carefully arranged Spirit Array, though what effect it had was impossible to tell.

He activated the array, and the entire secret room immediately began flickering between light and dark.

A short while later, a blurry figure appeared before him. The figure had only a human outline, with no visible face, as though it had been cast from flowing water and was constantly shifting. As soon as it appeared, it said, "Third Elder, perfect timing. I had something to ask you as well. Just now, there was some movement from Vice Sect Master Xin's side. I tried contacting Vice Sect Master Xin to ask about the situation, but received no response at all. Now even the sensing between the Formation Plates has disappeared. Third Elder, do you know what happened over there?"

Hearing this, Third Elder felt even more uneasy. If only his Formation Plate had lost contact with the Formation Core, there might still have been some minor accident, something that would recover after a while. But now even this person was in the same situation.

And since this man was closest to the Formation Core and had sensed some movement, the problem was clearly serious.

Third Elder said, "This old man summoned you all because of the Formation Plate as well. I do not know exactly what happened, only that Vice Sect Master Xin has indeed given no response. Let us wait and see how the others are faring."

As the two spoke, several more faint, illusory figures appeared one after another inside the secret room. Although they were separated by vast distances, the array in this secret room still allowed them to gather in one place and speak as if face to face. It was extraordinarily convenient.

Including Third Elder, there were seven figures in the secret room altogether. They were the Great Wilderness Star Domain experts stationed at the seven Eight Wastelands sub-arrays.

After a brief exchange, they all discovered a horrifying fact.

They had completely lost contact with the Formation Core, and even Vice Sect Master Xin had disappeared without a trace.

The secret room fell silent for a moment before someone said in horror, "Could it be..."

"Impossible!" another person immediately retorted. "Vice Sect Master Xin has cultivation at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm. None of us could defeat him one-on-one, and he was presiding over the Eight Wastelands Formation Core on top of that. Who could possibly be his match? Nothing could have happened to Vice Sect Master Xin."

"Third Brother You, you were closest to the Formation Core. Tell us exactly what you saw just now."

Everyone turned toward the figure who had arrived first.

Third Brother You said in a low voice, "A streak of light fell from the sky, the earth shook, and then there was no more news from the Formation Core."

The secret room grew even quieter.

Third Brother You said, "It is hard to believe, but judging from the present situation, Vice Sect Master Xin may truly have..." He shivered before he could finish. If that was really the case, then Netherworld Sect had provoked a terrifying enemy this time. But... what kind of person could possess such heaven-shaking methods?

"Third Elder, what do we do now?"

Third Elder's expression was grave. After pondering for a moment, he let out a long breath, as if he had made a difficult decision. "We stake everything on this."

Everyone was startled, and someone immediately said, "Third Elder, that may not be wise. We have been operating here for several years, and in another half a year, our great undertaking would be complete. If we act now, all our previous efforts will be wasted."

"Exactly. We should first confirm the situation on Vice Sect Master Xin's side. It will not be too late to make plans afterward."

"Fools!" Third Elder snorted coldly. "This old man knows you are unwilling to believe it, but the facts are right in front of us. How can you still deny them? If Vice Sect Master Xin were safe and sound, why would he not reply to our messages? Why would the Eight Wastelands Formation Core give no response at all? I fear... disaster has already struck over there. No matter who made a move, that person's strength is likely beyond our ability to resist." Someone who could kill Vice Sect Master Xin under the Formation Core's protection could also break into these sub-arrays and take their lives.

"It takes courage to cut off one's own wrist, and besides, our efforts will not all be wasted."

"Third Elder means..."

Third Elder sneered. "We lure the snake from its hole. Moreover, it is time for that hidden chess piece to play its role. When the time comes, as long as we can subdue that Star Lord, it will not matter whether we have the Eight Wastelands Array or not. Once we command the Star Lord's power, we may yet be able to fight that unknown enemy."

Hearing this, everyone thought it over briefly and understood his plan. Third Brother You was the first to agree. "I support Third Elder's decision."

After all, remaining in the sub-arrays no longer gave them the slightest sense of safety. They might as well stake everything on one throw; at least then they still had a thread of hope.

Everyone present was a Void King Realm cultivator and had lived a long life. They quickly grasped the gains and losses involved, so naturally no one raised any further objections. One after another, they nodded in agreement.

"Since that is settled, all of you go make preparations. After one incense stick of time, we will act together and catch them off guard!"

Everyone accepted the order, and their figures dispersed again like flowing water. Very soon, Third Elder was the only one left in the secret room, standing in place with his brows tightly furrowed. The unease in his heart had not faded; after making that decision, it had only grown stronger.

Gritting his teeth, he cast aside the distracting thoughts in his heart, turned, and left the secret room. Things had reached this point, and he had no better option.

...

Yang Kai hurried all the way toward Nine Heavens Sacred Land, his brow furrowed as he looked down over the earth below, his expression gloomy.

Tongxuan Continent's current state was far from promising. Its Spiritual Qi was actually even thinner than when he had left all those years ago, and its so-called spiritual mountains and great rivers had lost their former charm. The entire star seemed as though it had fallen gravely ill.

If things continued like this, then even without Netherworld Sect and Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators ravaging the land, it would probably take less than a hundred years for this place to lose all its Spiritual Qi. At that point, no cultivator would ever be born here again, and the lives of mortals would grow increasingly difficult, until the world's Spiritual Qi dried up completely, its Origin collapsed, and it became a Dead Star with no living thing remaining.

Of the countless Dead Stars in the Star Domain, many had transformed step by step in just this way. Perhaps tens of thousands, hundreds of thousands, or even more years ago, those stars had been full of life and flourishing. But with the passage of time, even the most dazzling stars would die. People knew birth, aging, sickness, and death; stars were no exception.

The world of cultivators had been greatly affected, to say nothing of the mortal world. Natural and man-made disasters came without cease, countless people had been displaced, and human tragedies could be seen everywhere.

Even if Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators could be driven away this time, Tongxuan Continent would probably still need at least a hundred years to recover.

Although he had not returned to Tongxuan Continent for many years, as the Saint Lord of Nine Heavens Sacred Land, he was still extremely familiar with this land. At the thought that he would soon see Xia Ningshang and Xue Yue, his mood improved considerably.

He would see them first and ease the pain of longing, then come back to deal with these small fry from Great Wilderness Star Domain.

Within the Nine Peaks, birds sang and flowers bloomed, Spiritual Qi was abundant, and tens of thousands of cultivators had gathered. However, many of them wore worried expressions.

This was the last pure land in all of Tongxuan Continent. As the Star Lord, although Xia Ningshang no longer had the strength to stop Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators from invading, nor the strength to stop them from arranging the Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array to poison Tongxuan Continent's Spiritual Qi, she could still shelter Nine Heavens Sacred Land.

With her here, Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators could forget about taking even one step into the Nine Peaks.

At this moment, the main hall was crowded with figures.

Led by Meng Wuya and Di Mo, there were An Ling'er, Saintess of Nine Heavens Sacred Land; Elder Qian Ning; Li Rong and Han Fei of the Ancient Demon Clan; the sisters Hu Jiao'er and Hu Mei'er of Twin Pavilion; the Palace Master of Dragon-Phoenix Palace; Wu Jie of Netherworld Sect...

Everyone in the main hall was familiar to Yang Kai, about thirty people in all. Aside from the few he had known since Zhongdu, the rest were friends he had made on Tongxuan Continent.

Beyond them, there was another familiar figure.

Gu Jianxin, Young Alliance Master of Sword Alliance and son of Gu Cangyun. He possessed the special physique known as the Taixu Sword Physique and had been born with exceptional talent for the sword. After many years apart, he remained elegant and distinguished. His left and right Sword Servants, Lingyue and Anxing, attended him at his side. Years of tempering had made him steadier than before. The sharp Sword Intent he once carried had vanished, and now he seemed like a keen blade hidden in its sheath. His edge was not exposed, but once drawn, his sword would strike like thunder.

At this moment, he was speaking quietly with another person.

This person was none other than Zi Wuji, the current helmsman of Purple Star, one of the Star Domain's top forces.

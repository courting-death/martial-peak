## Chapter 2879 — The Road Beneath His Feet

Teleportation was useless here, for the laws of this world had been severely damaged by the Black Sun, leaving the void extremely unstable. As long as he could find a way to escape this massive pit spanning a hundred li, he would be able to freely use the techniques he excelled at.

This massive pit spanning a hundred li was the key.

He did his best to rush toward the edge of the battlefield without drawing attention.

Yet, he was still noticed.

One of the Demon Kings with a pair of wings seemed uninterested in besieging Shaman Dang, so he simply crossed his arms and watched coldly from midair.

Yang Kai’s unusual movements quickly caught his eye. At first, he watched Yang Kai’s attempt to escape with interest, but soon grew angry, for he realized this outsider was truly capable—none of his kin could stop his advance. As Yang Kai charged forward, hundreds of his people died at his hands.

Seeing that this outsider was about to kill even more, the winged Demon King could no longer hold back. With a shake of his wings, he dove down, blocking Yang Kai’s path. In his hands was a long spear gleaming with cold light, which he thrust fiercely at Yang Kai. At the tip, a fist-sized orb of pitch-black energy gathered.

Yang Kai twisted his body slightly, hurriedly lifting the Myriad Sword to parry, appearing extremely flustered.

This sorry display made the Demon King burst into loud laughter, his face full of delight, as if he could already see Yang Kai being blasted to pieces by his spear.

Yet the result left him stunned. With a single thrust, not only did the outsider not die instantly, he even used the force of the attack to drift far to the side, easily escaping the Demon King’s entanglement.

The Demon King frowned and muttered, “Lucky bastard!”

He chalked it all up to Yang Kai’s good luck, never suspecting it was all intentional.

As he spoke, he began his pursuit. Although he hadn’t managed to kill the outsider in one blow, he was still a Demon King—he couldn’t let this brat escape right under his nose. Lord Mo Duo was watching this slaughter, and all the clansmen were fighting hard. If he couldn’t even handle a single brat, he might be punished.

He no longer held back. With a beat of his wings, he instantly appeared above Yang Kai, spear dancing as he thrust down again. This time, however, the orb of black energy at the tip was as large as a washbasin.

The aura radiating from that orb was now terrifying—even another Demon King would not dare take this blow lightly.

The outsider, still fighting his way out, seemed completely unaware, keeping his focus on the surrounding demons and ignoring the Demon King above. Yet in the next instant, as if he had eyes on the top of his head, he raised his sword and struck precisely at the spear tip.

With a thunderous boom, the energy orb exploded.

Caught off guard, the Demon King staggered back several steps. When he steadied himself and looked again, the outsider had already escaped a hundred zhang away, moving faster and faster. Wherever he passed, not a single demon could stop him for even a moment. The broad sword in his hand was like the scythe of death—anyone it touched died, and anyone it grazed was wounded.

Twice in a row, his attacks had been inexplicably neutralized, and the outsider brat had used the force of his blows to escape even farther. This was no longer something that could be explained by luck alone.

The Demon King’s expression shifted, and he roared angrily, “Damn you, I’ll tear you to pieces!”

He felt as if he were being toyed with—mocked by an outsider weaker than himself. His pride couldn’t take it. With a fierce lunge, he broke through the sound barrier and caught up in an instant.

He struck a third time, this time going all out, holding nothing back. In a flash, Demonic Qi surged, and the demon spear’s might was overwhelming.

Yang Kai had no choice but to turn and face him. One hand wielded the Myriad Sword in a dazzling display while the other constantly formed seals, and he chanted incantations as powerful Shamanic Arts burst forth in brilliant light.

In an instant, the two were locked in fierce combat.

For Yang Kai, this battle was extremely arduous—not because his opponent was too strong. He was now an Upper-Level Great Shaman Master, just a step away from the Shaman King realm. With the foundation of the Emperor Realm, he possessed many divine abilities and techniques that no ordinary Great Shaman Master could have.

He was fully capable of killing this winged Demon King, who clung to him like a maggot to rotting flesh.

But killing this Demon King might draw the attention of a Demon Saint.

So he had to display just the right amount of strength—appearing to be fighting desperately, on the verge of collapse under his opponent’s attacks, as if he could be killed at any moment. Striking this balance was not easy.

Fortunately, he managed it well. The Demon Saint’s attention remained, but his interest did not deepen. Yang Kai fought as he retreated, purposefully moving toward the edge of the pit.

In contrast, his opponent grew more and more frustrated, a strange feeling gnawing at his heart.

This outsider brat was like an immortal cockroach—no matter how fiercely he attacked, he couldn’t bring him down. Though Yang Kai looked flustered and in danger, he always managed to turn peril into safety at the last moment.

The Demon King’s attacks grew ever fiercer, but he could never achieve his goal. Instead, the shockwaves from their clashes killed many of his own people.

He sensed that, beside the distant Demon Saint, his fellow Demon Kings were pointing and laughing at him, mocking his incompetence.

He could not tolerate this.

A vicious glint flashed in his eyes. He thrust his spear again—though Yang Kai barely managed to block it, the Demon King’s face twisted into a cold, sinister smile as he snarled, “Die!”

At the same time, a feather on his right wing suddenly turned crimson, detached, and transformed into an indestructible spear, shooting toward Yang Kai in an instant.

This was the Demon King’s innate divine ability. Unlike Shaya’s endlessly reusable charm light, he had to sacrifice one of his own painstakingly refined feathers to use it. Once used, the feather would disappear completely.

But its destructive power was immense.

Under normal circumstances, the Demon King would never use this move lightly. But this outsider brat had humiliated him too much—he was desperate to kill him and reclaim his dignity.

Yang Kai’s pores tightened all over, an unprecedented sense of crisis enveloping him, alarms blaring in his heart.

He hadn’t felt such danger in a long time. Even at his peak, he might not have been able to resolve it easily.

Instinctively, he reached out with one hand—this time, he did not use a Shamanic Art, but instead…

“Banish!”

A black hole suddenly formed ahead, as if it could devour all things in the world.

The winged Demon King’s face changed dramatically at the sight, his eyes wide as he stared at the black hole, nearly turning to flee.

This black hole looked strikingly similar to the Black Sun that had erupted before. The Demon Saint had used heaven-shaking means to shift the Black Sun onto this land—a scene he had witnessed with his own eyes. In an instant, countless among the outsider race’s leaders had been killed or wounded, with seemingly only one survivor. As merely a Demon King, he naturally had no hope of facing the terror of the Black Sun head-on.

But he quickly realized he was mistaken.

It was not the Black Sun, for his feather pierced straight through it, shattering it instantly. Though its power was greatly diminished, it still shot directly at the outsider brat.

Everything happened in a flash. After the feather shattered Yang Kai’s spatial technique, he had no choice but to roar, “Dragon Transformation!”

Behind him, a golden dragon phantom raised its head and roared, its voice piercingly high, drowning out the chaos of the battlefield and making the whole world tremble.

In the distance, Demon Saint Mo Duo, who had been watching the battle, finally changed his expression. His sharp gaze seemed to pierce the barriers of space, reaching straight into Yang Kai’s heart.

The Golden Sacred Dragon phantom flashed and disappeared, merging into Yang Kai’s body.

Crackle…

His relatively modest frame suddenly swelled to more than twice its size. Scales covered his exposed skin, both hands turned into sharp dragon claws capable of shattering gold and stone, and two short dragon horns sprouted from his forehead.

The once-broad Myriad Sword now looked like a child’s toy in Yang Kai’s hand.

With a hiss, the crimson feather struck Yang Kai’s chest, but failed to deliver a fatal blow as expected.

After Dragon Transformation, Yang Kai’s body was greatly strengthened, his skin covered in dragon scales. The winged Demon King’s innate ability was indeed powerful, but it only pierced three inches into his flesh before it could go no further.

The winged Demon King was stunned by this turn of events, never expecting the outsider to possess such an ability. Moreover, the aura now radiating from Yang Kai filled him with deep unease.

Instinctively, he felt he was no match for the man before him. With a strange cry, he thrust his spear forward, feinting as he prepared to flee.

But the spear was caught in Yang Kai’s dragon claw, which yanked it forcefully toward himself.

The winged Demon King was pulled in uncontrollably.

Yang Kai shifted his stance, put away the Myriad Sword, and with one hand stabbed straight at the Demon King’s chest.

With a squelch, as if piercing paper, his arm went through the Demon King’s chest, leaving a gaping hole. In his palm, he now gripped a still-beating, pitch-black demon heart.

The two were face to face, eyes meeting—terror on the Demon King’s face, cold indifference on Yang Kai’s.

With a squeeze, the demon heart was crushed to powder. The winged Demon King let out a shrill scream, and under the force of the terrifying dragon breath, his body was reduced to a mist of blood. Of his three demon hearts, two were destroyed in an instant, leaving only the last, which, as if sentient, fled. Within that demon heart, a wisp of black qi wrapped around a human-like form was dimly visible—clearly the winged Demon King reduced to countless times smaller.

Yang Kai did not pursue. He didn’t want to, nor did he have the time.

With two of his demon hearts destroyed, the Demon King—even if he could revive with the last—would be greatly weakened and not a threat for now.

Yang Kai turned and charged toward the edge of the pit.

He was already close to the rim. Ten breaths later, as Yang Kai reached the top, a figure blocked his way. Though not tall, the shadow it cast seemed to cover the entire sky.

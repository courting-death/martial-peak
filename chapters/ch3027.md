## Chapter 3027 — The Fu Lineage of the Dragon Clan

There were only a little over twenty people in the teahouse. After counting them, Yuan Wu flew out again and busied himself throughout Half-Dragon City.

Fu Qi was watching him, so he did not dare shirk or play tricks. He put exceptional effort into gathering the required people and no longer had time to continue feuding with Li Jiao.

"Palace Master Yang, what should we do?" Li Jiao secretly sent a transmission to Yang Kai.

He did not want to go to the Dragon Palace. Although he had never been there, the place filled him with dread from the bottom of his heart, and he instinctively feared it. At this point, he could only rely on Yang Kai to make the decision.

Yang Kai glanced at him indifferently and transmitted back, "Let nature take its course."

He had come to Dragon Isle to find Zhu Qing, yet he had not even seen her, much less learned where to look for her. Fu Qi's appearance was now an opportunity. Zhu Qing should be inside the Dragon Palace.

Being able to slip into the Dragon Palace alongside Fu Qi was exactly what he wanted, so he naturally had little reason to resist. As for what he should do after entering, whether to lay his cards on the table or act according to the circumstances, he truly could only let nature take its course.

Since he had put it that way, Li Jiao could not object. He simply nodded lightly, then turned to Lu Sanniang and her daughter. "Stay with me. I will not let anyone bully you."

Lu Sanniang's face had gone somewhat pale ever since Yuan Wu called her name. The Dragon Palace was a place out of nightmares. She had stayed there for two or three hundred years, longing to escape every moment of every day. Although the Dragon Clan had ultimately swept her out in disgrace, at least her wish had been fulfilled.

She had never expected that, more than ten years later, she would have to return to the Dragon Palace. She could not help feeling anxious and uneasy, and she gripped her daughter's hand with tremendous force. Lu Yuqin had clearly noticed the change in her mother's state of mind and silently pressed close against her.

Before long, all the preparations were complete.

Far more than two hundred Dragon Descendants had gathered, but Yuan Wu was clearly experienced in this matter. He selected two hundred whose cultivation and condition were reasonably good, then dismissed the rest.

The Dragon Descendants who were dismissed visibly breathed sighs of relief, while all those who remained looked dejected, as though their parents had just died.

The five hundred mortals had also gathered there.

Only then did Yuan Wu fly up to meet Fu Qi. Stopping ten zhang away, he cupped his fists and smiled ingratiatingly. "Sir, everything is ready."

"There is no time to lose. Follow me." With that, Fu Qi took the lead.

Yuan Wu turned back and shouted in a low voice, "All of you, keep up! If you delay Sir Fu Qi's important business, you will be killed without mercy!"

He did not know what Fu Qi needed five hundred mortals for, but on Dragon Isle, matters concerning the Dragon Clan were of the utmost importance and naturally could not be treated carelessly.

Hearing this, everyone grew solemn and hurried after him.

In an instant, seven hundred figures soared into the sky. Emperor Realm Masters were everywhere among them, making for a spectacular sight.

Lu Sanniang was at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, but Lu Yuqin was only in the Saint King Realm. With such a cultivation, she naturally could not keep pace with the main group. Fortunately, Li Jiao was there to assist her, so she did not fall behind.

Li Jiao's feelings toward Lu Sanniang and her daughter were now extremely complicated.

This was especially true of Lu Yuqin. She was the child Lu Sanniang had borne with a member of the Dragon Clan, so no matter how pretty and adorable she was, Li Jiao found it difficult to feel any affection for her. By extension, he also felt some resentment toward Lu Sanniang, believing he had suffered this enormous humiliation for no reason.

On the other hand, he also knew that Lu Sanniang could not truly be blamed for any of this.

Back then, she had only snuck away from Ruyi Sect because she missed him and wanted to find him at Li Dragon Palace. Who could have imagined that a member of the Dragon Clan would abduct her along the way and bring her to Dragon Isle?

No one could have foreseen it, and Lu Sanniang certainly had not wanted to suffer such a fate. This could be seen from how she had not dared acknowledge Li Jiao when they first met. In her heart, she felt deeply guilty toward him.

Li Jiao's emotions were in complete turmoil. It was as though a great mountain weighed upon his chest, making even his breathing unsteady.

Lu Sanniang clearly knew what was troubling him, so throughout the journey, she merely followed silently beside him without saying a word, lest he grow disgusted with her.

The group of several hundred was utterly silent, and everyone felt uneasy. The Dragon Descendants at least knew what they were going there to do, but the five hundred mortals were anxious beyond measure. The Dragon Palace had never before summoned mortals, so this had come far too suddenly. Some people who were clearly on good terms with Yuan Wu tried to sound him out indirectly.

Unfortunately, what could Yuan Wu possibly know? He had merely obeyed Fu Qi's command and gathered the mortals, with no idea what they would be doing next. Yet when faced with their questions, he gave vague answers and put on an inscrutable air.

This left those people angry but helpless.

Midway through the journey, Yang Kai found an opportunity to secretly slip several bottles of Spiritual Pills to Li Jiao.

On this trip, Li Jiao would inevitably have his blood drawn to cultivate Dragon Blood Flowers. Without making preparations in advance, his strength might be affected. With the help of these bottles of Spiritual Pills, he should be able to weather this calamity safely.

Li Jiao clearly knew that anything Yang Kai took out was bound to be good, so he did not refuse. He quietly accepted them and thanked Yang Kai in his heart.

After leaving Half-Dragon City, the group flew for a full half day before a warm, humid sea breeze finally brushed against their faces. The roar of the waves and the sound of water striking the reefs reached their ears.

Yang Kai looked up and saw a boundless sea ahead. At the limits of his vision, the water stretched endlessly onward, and no one knew how vast this sea truly was.

This Small World actually contained a sea, which was beyond Yang Kai's expectations. From the looks of it, this Small World seemed to be much larger than even the Revolving World.

The Spiritual Qi over the sea was particularly rich, and the farther they flew, the more pronounced this became.

Another half day passed. It was not until dusk that the silhouette of a small island suddenly appeared ahead.

Yang Kai and Li Jiao exchanged a glance, each seeing the surprise in the other's eyes.

Lu Sanniang spoke at just the right moment. "That is the Dragon Palace over there."

"On that island?" Yang Kai asked.

Lu Sanniang shook her head and glanced at Li Jiao with her beautiful eyes. Seeing that he was paying little attention to her, she sighed inwardly and said with a downcast expression, "No. The entire Dragon Palace is made up of many islands. That is only its outermost edge. Dragon Palace is merely a collective name, and every member of the Dragon Clan has an island of their own. They do not live together."

Yang Kai nodded lightly in understanding. Powerful Divine Spirits like the Dragon Clan were unlikely to be accustomed to living in groups. Every one of them was immensely powerful and required a tremendous amount of Spiritual Qi when cultivating, so they naturally could not crowd together. If they did, it would be detrimental to their growth.

Even in a great Sect like Qingyang Shrine, every Elder had a Spirit Peak of their own, let alone the Dragon Clan. Those Spirit Islands were their Dragon nests.

Someone seemed to be waiting over there.

When they drew closer, they saw that it was a young woman, a girl in fact. At first glance, she appeared to be only fifteen or sixteen years old, not much older than Lu Yuqin, but her true age was clearly far greater. The Dragon Clan matured extremely slowly, often taking hundreds or even thousands of years to truly reach adulthood, so her fifteen- or sixteen-year-old appearance was merely superficial.

She wore purple robes that left her fair, pinkish-white arms exposed. Her curves were full and rounded, and she was quite attractive, but impatience was written all over her brow. She had probably been waiting here for a long time.

As soon as she saw Fu Qi approaching, she called out, "Why did you take so long? I've been waiting forever."

Fu Qi frowned. "The journey naturally takes time."

Yuan Wu stepped forward, bowed his head respectfully, and greeted her. "Greetings, Sir Fu Ling!"

Another member of the Fu lineage of the Dragon Clan? Yang Kai's expression shifted. He studied the young girl for a few moments and secretly frowned, immediately forming a poor impression of Fu Ling. There was only one reason: a trace of sinister malice lurked between her brows.

Judging by Yuan Wu's behavior, however, he had clearly entered and left the Dragon Palace many times. He could even exchange words with Fu Ling, which showed that his arrogant and domineering conduct in Half-Dragon City was not entirely without backing.

Fu Ling casually glanced at Yuan Wu and said disdainfully, "So it's you."

Yuan Wu was thrilled that she remembered him and was just about to say something when Fu Ling denied him the chance. She waved impatiently and said, "Enough, enough. Since you're here, follow me. The flowers need you now."

At these words, all the Dragon Descendants' faces fell, including Yuan Wu's.

Although everyone knew this was what awaited them here, they still could not help feeling frightened now that the moment had truly arrived.

Fu Qi stood with his hands clasped behind his back and snorted. "All you mongrels, get over there."

"Dragon Descendant" was merely the name used by living beings with Dragon Clan bloodlines in Half-Dragon City to refer to themselves. Before the true Dragon Clan, these so-called Dragon Descendants were nothing but mongrels with impure bloodlines, lowly products born after members of the Dragon Clan vented their lust.

Even with some blood relation between them, no member of the Dragon Clan cared whether they lived or died, much less about their dignity.

Otherwise, Lu Sanniang and her daughter would never have been driven out of the Dragon Palace. After all, no matter how one looked at it, Lu Yuqin's biological father was a member of the Dragon Clan, and a pure-blooded one at that.

The two hundred Dragon Descendants were clearly accustomed to this form of address. Regardless of their cultivation, none dared show the slightest dissatisfaction. They separated from the crowd and gathered around Fu Ling.

Li Jiao immediately grew restless. As he slowly moved over, he looked toward Yang Kai.

He had never expected to be separated from Yang Kai as soon as they arrived at Dragon Isle. He had been counting on relying on Yang Kai, but once they were separated, how the hell was he supposed to do that?

Moreover, compared to himself, he was more worried about Lu Sanniang. Although he still harbored some resentment toward her, those events belonged to the distant past. Now that the truth had come to light, he hated the member of the Dragon Clan who had harmed Lu Sanniang even more.

Yang Kai gave him a meaningful look. Li Jiao understood and nodded imperceptibly.

He knew there was little need to worry about Lu Sanniang and her daughter's safety. With Yang Kai beside them to look after them, nothing should go wrong.

Yuan Wu hurriedly asked, "Sirs, what about these mortals?"

"They have another use," Fu Qi replied with a frown.

Yuan Wu's eyes darted about before he immediately said, "Would you like this lowly one to stay behind and monitor them? You know how these people are... Heh heh, some of them are rather disobedient. If this lowly one stays, perhaps I can be of some help."

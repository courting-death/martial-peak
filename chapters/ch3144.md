## Chapter 3144 — Courtesy Demands Reciprocity

Biluo even saw Su Yan, and froze on the spot.

Su Yan had disappeared twenty years ago and had not been heard from since. Who knew when she had returned to Lingxiao Sect? Biluo did not dare slight her and hurried forward to salute. "Biluo greets First Madam!"

Although there were quite a few women by Yang Kai’s side, if one had to rank one of them first, it would absolutely be Su Yan. This was not because their feelings were especially deep, nor because she was Yang Kai’s first woman, but because she possessed a bearing that inspired respect.

Xue Yue and Shan Qingluo would occasionally bicker, but both behaved themselves perfectly in front of Su Yan. As for this First Madam in both name and reality, Biluo naturally had to show her due respect.

Su Yan smiled in reply. "It has been a long time."

Biluo said, "Twenty years. Lady has always been thinking of Madam. If she learns Madam is safe and sound, she will surely be able to put one worry to rest."

Su Yan turned to Yang Kai. "You did not tell Luo'er?"

Yang Kai rubbed his face. "I forgot."

"You had no time, I suppose?" Su Yan teased. Even with Yang Kai's thick skin, he looked a little uncomfortable.

He had spent three days with Shan Qingluo, and they had done nothing but fool around. How could he have had time to talk about anything else? Now that Su Yan had exposed him, he naturally felt a bit embarrassed.

Fortunately, Su Yan was not the sort to dwell on such matters. She turned to Biluo and said, "We came this time to lend Ah Luo a hand. Where is she? Take us to her."

Although the experts of Myriad Beasts Mountain Manor had all been executed, the Manor had, after all, operated on Di Chen Star for more than a dozen years and occupied nearly ninety percent of Di Chen's territory. If Red Moon Domain alone tried to recover the lost lands, it would likely take a great deal of time. Yang Kai had returned to Lingxiao Sect partly to test the Space Array's effectiveness, and partly to bring people over from Dark Star to help.

Su Yan and the other experts were only the vanguard. More cultivators would come later to assist Shan Qingluo.

Recovering Di Chen was only a matter of time.

"Yes!" Biluo answered.

Su Yan looked at Yang Kai. "I will leave Sister Yue and Ningshang to you."

"I know. I am about to set out." Yang Kai nodded. What had happened to Dark Star and Di Chen Star made him understand even more deeply how dire the situation across the Star Domain had become, so he was deeply worried about Xia Ningshang and Xue Yue as well. He urgently wanted to see them and know whether they were safe.

They exchanged only a few brief words and did not say farewell, because everyone knew they would reunite before long. Su Yan led Lingxiao Sect's group away with easy confidence, while Yang Kai and Liu Yan stepped back onto the Space Array and set out once more.

...

Shuiyue Star, the main star of Hengluo Commerce Guild.

By the time Yang Kai arrived, a month had passed since he left Di Chen Star. Looking out from the Void, doubt filled his eyes. He had expected this place to be wreathed in smoke and flames, suffering through war like the other stars he had encountered. To his surprise, Shuiyue Star was peaceful, without the slightest trace of battle.

A faint unease welled up in his heart.

Outside the nebula, several Warships lay across the Void. Yang Kai quickly noticed Netherworld Sect's mark on them, and the unease in his heart grew stronger.

He flew straight between the Warships and plunged into the atmosphere.

Violent explosions rang out behind him. The Warships belonging to Netherworld Sect had not even seen their enemy's shadow before they were blown apart one after another, erupting in dazzling light. This abnormal commotion immediately alarmed the experts on Shuiyue Star, and they all looked up. Quite a few people seemed to realize what had happened, and their expressions changed.

Their attention was quickly drawn by a streak of light. It fell straight from the sky, completely unrestrained, piercing the clouds before crashing into the main city that had once belonged to Hengluo Commerce Guild.

A thunderous boom rang out. The city shook, the earth split, and ravines spread in a crisscross pattern across the ground. Fierce winds howled outward, toppling the surrounding houses in swathes.

Xiu xiu xiu...

Figure after figure sped over from every direction, hovering in the Void as they stared at this uninvited guest.

Yang Kai swept his cold eyes around, and his heart sank. His earlier worry had come true. Shuiyue Star... had already fallen! Not one of the cultivators around him looked familiar, and the clothing of several clearly bore traces of Netherworld Sect. As for the others, even if they were not from Netherworld Sect, they were probably from Great Wilderness Star Domain.

How could it have fallen? Yang Kai could not understand it at all.

He was not surprised Cuiwei Star had fallen. After all, Cuiwei Star had no especially powerful experts and no especially powerful force. But even Dark Star and Di Chen had not fallen, so how could Shuiyue Star have fallen? This was Hengluo Commerce Guild's main star. Hengluo Commerce Guild had once been one of the three strongest great forces in the entire Star Domain. Before Dark Star and Lingxiao Sect rose, Hengluo Commerce Guild's strength had even surpassed Purple Star and Sword Alliance by a notch.

Yet such a star had actually fallen completely, and its current state was even worse than Cuiwei Star's.

What exactly had happened here over the past dozen years? Where had Xue Yue gone? And where were the people of Hengluo Commerce Guild?

As his thoughts churned, Yang Kai's aura turned bone-chillingly cold, making every cultivator around him feel as if they had fallen into an ice cellar. This man had descended straight from the Starry Sky, clearly confident in his strength. Even though he had yet to attack, that icy aura alone was enough to make their hearts tremble.

"Someone who can speak, step forward." Yang Kai's gaze swept over the crowd like a fierce tiger barging into a flock of sheep, regarding no one there as worth his attention.

Silence. No one answered. The cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain all stared at him as if facing a great enemy.

"I said..." Yang Kai slowly raised a fist, drew his arm back, and suddenly swung. "Someone who can speak, step forward. Are you deaf?"

Something seemed to burst apart. Everyone turned to look and sucked in a cold breath. In the direction Yang Kai had punched, a cultivator had exploded into blood mist on the spot, leaving not even bones behind.

And that man... had been a First Stage Void King Realm cultivator!

He had blown apart a Void King Realm cultivator with one punch from a hundred zhang away. How tyrannical was that? The crowd, already extremely wary of Yang Kai, felt their hearts go cold. Against such a powerful enemy, who could possibly stand a chance? Almost in unison, everyone took several steps back.

Only one person stepped forward: a thin, tall middle-aged man wreathed in death Qi. A Formation Plate suddenly appeared in his hand, and he urged his Saint Yuan into it. In an instant, Heaven and Earth hummed as the grand array in the city activated.

Netherworld Sect had occupied Shuiyue Star for several years. Since those years had passed peacefully, with no one daring to make trouble for them, their daily lives had gradually grown more relaxed. They had never expected someone to barge straight into the city.

The others feared Yang Kai's imposing might, but this thin, tall man did not, because he controlled the array covering the entire city.

If Yang Kai had acted wantonly outside the city, then so be it, but he just had to court death by charging inside. Once the man activated the grand array, he would make Yang Kai suffer. While urging the array, he shouted, "What are you standing around for? Take this man down! This Lord will support you with the array."

As soon as he finished speaking, the array was already running, gathering the full power of the city's grand array and pressing it down upon Yang Kai alone.

Yang Kai's body visibly sank, as if two great mountains had settled on his shoulders.

Seeing this, everyone was overjoyed. What hesitation could they still have? They all urged their Secret Techniques and Secret Treasures and attacked Yang Kai.

The thin, tall man, however, went from shock to delight and shouted at exactly the wrong moment, "Leave him alive!"

It was not that he disliked slaughter. Yang Kai had killed a Void King Realm cultivator on their side the moment he arrived and had even spoken rudely, so how could the man possibly spare him lightly? But under the suppression of the array he had activated, Yang Kai had not been forced to the ground, and there was no sign that he had used Saint Yuan. This made the man realize that Yang Kai's physique was extraordinarily powerful, and that he had very likely cultivated some profound Body Tempering Secret Art.

Body Tempering Secret Arts were not especially rare, but people with physiques this powerful were.

As an Elder of Netherworld Sect, he was proficient in Corpse Refining Techniques, and cultivators with such powerful bodies were his favorite kind. If he could capture Yang Kai alive, then slowly temper him with medicines and Secret Techniques, he would surely be able to refine him into a Heavenly Corpse. This was an extraordinarily precious material, something one could only encounter by chance, and to him, it was even better than the materials he had obtained on Shuiyue Star in earlier years.

With that in mind, how could he bear to see Yang Kai killed? Though corpses could still be refined after death, refining a living person produced better results and greater power.

His interruption made the many cultivators attacking Yang Kai curse inwardly. If he wanted them to hold back, could he not have said so earlier? Their attacks had already formed; how could pulling them back be easy? But they had no choice but to obey his order. Helpless, they could only desperately withdraw some of their strength, and several even suffered backlash, vomiting blood.

Amid a rumbling series of impacts, countless Secret Techniques and Secret Treasures struck Yang Kai's body at once. In an instant, energy surged and light shot in all directions.

The thin, tall man's heart lurched at the sight. Bad. This time, there probably would not even be an intact corpse left, so what could he possibly refine? His expression turned as dark as water, and his heart ached.

But when the light faded, he was horrified to find Yang Kai standing where he had been, completely unharmed. Not only was he unharmed, but even the little girl beside him looked calm, as if she had barely been affected at all.

The thin, tall man's hair stood on end, and unease began to grow in his heart.

"Courtesy demands reciprocity. Since all of you are so enthusiastic, take a move from this Young Master as well!" Yang Kai roared. His fists flew, sending countless fist shadows sweeping out in every direction.

Hong hong hong...

Bodies burst apart one after another in midair. No one could withstand even a single exchange. In the blink of an eye, the dozens of people gathered around him had been cleared away.

Only the thin, tall man remained safe and sound, but by now he truly felt as if his whole body had fallen into an ice cellar, his hands and feet cold.

Yang Kai turned his head to look at him. "Looks like you are the one who can talk. Do not be afraid. I have a few things to ask you. Answer well, and I will give you a quick death!" His black hair hung over his forehead, and his eyes were gloomy. The thin, tall man immediately felt as if a giant beast from the Primordial Era had locked onto him, and even breathing became incredibly difficult.

Still, his will was not weak. Although he was terrified, he had not lost his courage. With a lift of his hand, he took out a copper ring and shook it rapidly, staring at Yang Kai through gritted teeth. "No matter who you are, if you dare offend my Netherworld Sect, you will undoubtedly die today!"

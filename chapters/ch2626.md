## Chapter 2626 — Don’t Be Afraid, Come Together

“Tianhe, stand behind your Ancestor.” Ji Yao said calmly.

“Yes!” Shi Tianhe didn’t say anything more and hurriedly hid behind Yang Kai, this fake Ancestor.

By now, the group chasing after them was clearly visible. Waves of Emperor Yuan could also be distinctly felt. It was a bit different from what Shi Tianhe had guessed—at the front was indeed a Second Stage Emperor, who should be the Thunderclap Pavilion’s Pavilion Master, Bai Yu. But behind him were not five First Stage Emperor Realms, but only four, one less than expected.

Yang Kai thought for a moment. Yesterday, there was a man named Yan Qing who had been following Thunderclap Pavilion’s Young Pavilion Master, Bai Lu, but Yang Kai had crippled one of his arms with a single palm. That Yan Qing should also be an elder of Thunderclap Pavilion, and was probably recuperating at this moment, unable to come.

Even so, Thunderclap Pavilion had mobilized nearly all their Emperor Realm masters this time, showing just how much they valued Yang Kai and Ji Yao.

Swish, swish, swish…

Accompanied by the sound of wind, five figures stopped several zhang away from Yang Kai and Ji Yao.

At the forefront stood a man with a pale, beardless face. His features bore a striking resemblance to Bai Lu, suggesting he was none other than Bai Yu, the Pavilion Master of Thunderclap Pavilion.

The four behind him—two middle-aged men and two elderly men—wore cold expressions, their faces stern and imposing, eyes glaring fiercely in this direction.

Shi Tianhe sent a voice transmission to Yang Kai: “The one standing on the far left is Taiping City’s City Lord, Yan Dong. The other three are Thunderclap Pavilion’s elders. Senior, you and Third Senior Aunt must be careful.”

Yang Kai nodded slightly.

“You are Bing Yun’s third disciple, Ji Yao?” Not far away, Bai Yu suddenly called out in a loud voice. He could recognize Ji Yao not because he knew her, but because earlier at Taiping City’s gate, Shi Tianhe had addressed her as Senior Aunt Ji Yao, and naturally, others had overheard.

“Insolence! Even my Master’s name is not for you to call!” Ji Yao’s beautiful eyes flashed with anger as she bit out her words.

“Hahahaha!” Bai Yu laughed loudly, mocking, “Bing Yun herself hides in Ice Heart Valley, not daring to come out, living with her tail between her legs, but her disciple has quite the temper. I wonder where you got the courage to act so brazenly in the Northern Territory!”

The four behind him also laughed with great amusement, clearly confident that they had Yang Kai and Ji Yao at their mercy.

It was no wonder they were so confident. After all, Bai Yu possessed the cultivation of a Second Stage Emperor Realm. In Ice Heart Valley, the eldest disciple An Ruoyun and the second disciple Sun Yunxiu were also Second Stage Emperors.

As the third disciple, Ji Yao’s cultivation was at best the same, on par with Bai Yu.

If it really came to a fight, Bai Yu would not fear her. What’s more, he had four First Stage Emperor Realm helpers. As long as they dealt with Yang Kai first, the rest could gang up on Ji Yao, and she wouldn’t be able to stir up any waves.

“Master, I want to tear out his tongue and cut him to pieces!” Ji Yao’s chest heaved with anger, truly infuriated.

“Mm. Go ahead. Leave the rest to me.” Yang Kai nodded.

Receiving the order, Ji Yao’s delicate figure flickered and she pounced directly above Bai Yu’s head. Her Snow-Cold Sword trembled, sending a flurry of sword flowers to envelop Bai Yu.

“Insolent wench!” Bai Yu shouted, grasping at the air. A long spear appeared in his hand, and with Emperor Yuan surging, he thrust it out. The sword flowers instantly shattered. Swinging the spear in a circle, he swept at Ji Yao, but her expression didn’t change. With a sweep of her sword, a sword light slashed toward Bai Yu.

In an instant, the two were locked in fierce combat, Emperor Yuan surging, Laws colliding, the battle raging intensely.

While fighting Ji Yao, Bai Yu shouted, “You all, go kill those two and then come help me!”

“Yes!” Taiping City’s City Lord Yan Dong and the other three Thunderclap Pavilion elders responded and immediately sprang into action, their feet tapping the ground as they spread out, surrounding Yang Kai and Shi Tianhe.

“Madam Dong…” Yan Dong looked coldly at Shi Tianhe and said, “I truly didn’t expect you to be a disciple of Ice Heart Valley. You really kept it from me well.”

Shi Tianhe gritted her teeth and said, “I was expelled from Ice Heart Valley a hundred years ago. I am no longer considered a disciple.”

Yan Dong chuckled and said, “No matter what, you were once a member of Ice Heart Valley. I heard you were also a disciple of An Ruoyun?”

“So what if I am?”

Yan Dong let out a sigh and said, “Back in the day, when I was traveling outside, I once became acquainted with a disciple of Ice Heart Valley. She was pure as ice and flawless as jade, both beautiful and wise—much like Madam Dong herself. I admired her deeply, but alas, my feelings were unrequited. She never looked at me twice, and it became a regret that lingers in my heart to this day.”

“City Lord Yan, you had such a romantic past?” A Thunderclap Pavilion elder beside him couldn’t help but laugh. “I’ve never heard of that before.”

Another Thunderclap Pavilion elder chuckled, “Indeed. If that woman had accepted City Lord Yan’s affections back then, she’d probably be caught up in this mess today as well. Fortunately, City Lord Yan is blessed by the heavens.”

Yan Dong waved his hand and said, “Ah, that’s all ancient history now—no need to laugh at me, gentlemen. Still, even after all these years, I haven’t forgotten her name. That woman was called Yu Dan. Madam Dong, do you happen to know her?”

“Senior Sister Yu Dan?” Shi Tianhe exclaimed in surprise, “You actually know Senior Sister Yu Dan!”

Judging by her expression, she clearly knew her.

Yan Dong laughed, “You are both disciples of An Ruoyun, so of course you know each other. Good, very good!” As he spoke, he kept nodding, and the light in his eyes grew increasingly dangerous.

The Thunderclap Pavilion elder who had been silent glanced at him thoughtfully and said, “City Lord Yan, you’re not thinking of making up for that old regret today, are you?”

Yan Dong smiled, “Why not?”

The others were stunned. The Thunderclap Pavilion elder said, “City Lord Yan, you certainly have refined tastes, but Madam Dong has been married for many years.”

“A beautiful, mature woman—just my type. Besides, she and Yu Dan are Senior and Junior Sisters. Since I couldn’t have the Senior Sister, playing with the Junior Sister isn’t bad either. When you all make your move later, be gentle and don’t hurt her.”

The Thunderclap Pavilion elders didn’t know what to say, but they all knew that the matter from back then had become a knot in Yan Dong’s heart. If he could capture Shi Tianhe today, it would resolve it, so they nodded, “Since City Lord Yan says so, of course we’ll give you face.”

Yan Dong laughed, “Then I’ll thank you all in advance.”

Shi Tianhe’s face flushed with shame and anger. “Yan Dong, I didn’t expect you to be so despicable and shameless! To think Dong Hai has followed your lead all these years!”

“Dong Hai?” Yan Dong snorted coldly, “Why mention a dead man! Don’t worry, if he didn’t cherish you, I will from now on.”

“De… dead…” Shi Tianhe’s pretty face turned pale as she cried out, “What did you do to him?”

Yan Dong sneered, “As the Dong Family’s Family Head, he married a disciple of Ice Heart Valley. What do you think his end would be? Now he’s been cut to pieces by me, and the Dong Family no longer exists.”

Shi Tianhe’s delicate body trembled, and she was completely stunned.

She had long known that after her identity was exposed, the Dong Family would not have a good end, but hearing the news for real was still hard to accept.

That was the man she had been married to for a hundred years, the one she had left her sect for. Now… he was just gone.

“I’ll kill you, you beast!” Shi Tianhe’s eyes reddened, sword in hand, her power surging as she charged at Yan Dong.

But before she could move, someone patted her shoulder, and she froze in place, unable to move. If not for Yang Kai’s Emperor Yuan enveloping her, she would have collapsed on the spot.

Yan Dong looked coldly at Yang Kai and snorted, “Boy, you’re about to die and still have time to meddle in others’ affairs.”

Yang Kai grinned, “Are you all so confident you can kill me?” As he spoke, he pulled Shi Tianhe behind him.

His fearless demeanor made Yan Dong and the others frown, unsure where his confidence came from.

They knew Ji Yao’s identity, but as for this man—who he was and what strength he possessed—they had no idea, and for a moment, they were wary.

“Who exactly are you? Right now, everyone in the Northern Territory wants to destroy Ice Heart Valley. If you’re smart, you shouldn’t get involved in this mess!” Yan Dong said coldly, staring at Yang Kai.

“That’s right. If you turn back now, we might even become friends!” a Thunderclap Pavilion elder added.

Yang Kai bared his teeth in a grin. “Become friends? This Young Master cannot afford such an honor. Let’s drop the talk and settle it with our hands.”

“What are you all waiting for? Take care of him quickly!”

At that moment, Bai Yu’s angry shout came from the other side.

The group quickly turned to look and their faces changed, for they saw that Bai Yu, who was also a Second Stage Emperor Realm, was actually being pushed back in the fight. The extremely cold profound intent filled the air, and Bai Yu was struggling to defend against Ji Yao’s attacks, his face turning pale.

Judging by his appearance, he wouldn’t be able to hold out much longer.

The group was startled, realizing they had underestimated Ji Yao’s strength.

Although she was also a Second Stage Emperor, her strength was far greater than Bai Yu’s.

“If you don’t help soon, your Pavilion Master will be finished.” Yang Kai smiled at them, then released a surge of Emperor Yuan, deliberately revealing his cultivation. “Don’t worry, I’m not that strong—just a First Stage Emperor. Don’t be afraid, come at me together!”

“First Stage Emperor!”

“He really is a First Stage Emperor!”

“Damn it, just a mere First Stage dares to play the pig to eat the tiger and scare this Lord!”

Yan Dong and the three Thunderclap Pavilion elders all shouted. Previously, they had been cautious, afraid Yang Kai’s cultivation was higher than theirs, so they didn’t dare act rashly. They had planned to test his strength first, but unexpectedly, he revealed it himself.

What was there to fear about a First Stage Emperor? All four of them were had the same cultivation. Even if one couldn’t beat him, could four together not be enough?

“Do it!” a Thunderclap Pavilion elder shouted. The next moment, the four of them attacked Yang Kai from all sides, Emperor Yuan surging as they unleashed their secret techniques, their momentum overwhelming.

## Chapter 2993 — Yun'er Has Grown Up

"Need any help?" Yang Kai asked with a smile.

"No need!" The young woman declined confidently. As soon as she finished speaking, she threw a punch at the skull where Xiong Kaishan was hiding. The force of her fist raged out, squeezing the surrounding space into layer upon layer of irregular ripples with terrifying might.

Fast as Xiong Kaishan was, he was not faster than the might of this punch.

A thunderous explosion rang out, and the skull rocked unsteadily, nearly toppling over. A crack even split open across its cranium, and Xiong Kaishan's muffled grunt came from within.

A strange light flashed through Yang Kai's eyes. It seemed this little girl had encountered no small opportunity after coming to the Star Realm. She was actually able to display such formidable strength with a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivation. He wondered just how Yang Yan had drawn out the potential within her body.

At the thought of Yang Yan, Yang Kai's heart stirred again. He had been searching for her whereabouts for the past few years, but had no way to find her. He only vaguely suspected that she belonged to a force called the Star Court. Now that he had unexpectedly encountered Lin Yun'er, perhaps he could ask her about Yang Yan's whereabouts.

As long as he found Yang Yan, he could think of a way to return to Hengluo Star Domain and bring his friends and family over.

He had spent quite some time in the Star Realm, and now had a foundation of his own here. The vast Lingxiao Palace was more than sufficient to provide those from Dark Star with a better cultivation environment and cultivation resources.

“Young lady, leave some room for others and we’ll have an easier time meeting again in the future. Why must you press so relentlessly?” Xiong Kaishan’s somewhat flustered voice rang out. During the brief moment Yang Kai’s thoughts had wandered, Lin Yun’er had already beaten him into a desperate situation, leaving him with no way to the heavens and no path into the earth.

Countless cracks now covered the enormous skull where he was hiding, while the ghost flames in its eye sockets had dimmed and were on the verge of going out.

He had also realized how powerful Lin Yun'er was and knew that he was no match for her, so he wanted to turn hostility into friendship.

But how could Lin Yun'er let him have his way? No one knew where the little girl had cultivated all these years. Although her thoughts were relatively simple, her temperament was extraordinarily stubborn. She paid no attention whatsoever to what he was saying, merely pursing her lips as she hammered punch after punch into the skull.

After more than a dozen punches, the skull exploded into dust with a deafening boom. Xiong Kaishan shot out from within in a sorry state, a trace of blood leaking from the corner of his mouth. He had clearly suffered an internal injury.

Cold sweat streamed down Yang Kai's forehead at the sight. Destroying someone else's Imperial Treasure with nothing but her flesh-and-blood body was no ordinary feat. This young woman, as fierce as a rampaging dragon, was utterly different from the little girl in his memories.

Girls really do change beyond recognition as they grow up…

With his giant skull destroyed, Xiong Kaishan was left covered in dust and looking utterly bedraggled. Humiliated and enraged, malice welled up in his heart as he gritted his teeth and roared, “If this Lord can’t have an easy time, then neither will you!”

He formed a hand seal, and the remaining intact skulls suddenly began flying about. All of them burst into flames at once, releasing eerie, ice-cold fire that swept toward Lin Yun'er from every direction.

Yang Kai's expression changed. He could vaguely sense how troublesome these flames were. They should be a type of exotic flame born from Heaven and Earth, and anyone careless enough to be touched by them would certainly meet a bad end.

He was about to warn Lin Yun'er, but saw her charge forward without fear, neither dodging nor evading.

In an instant, the young woman's body was enveloped in the sinister, cold flames and completely disappeared from sight.

"Hahahaha!" Xiong Kaishan laughed loudly. "Little girl, you truly are unbelievably stupid! Is this Lord's Netherworld Ghost Fire something you can casually touch? Let's see if you die this time... Hiss!"

His laughter came to an abrupt halt. Xiong Kaishan's eyes bulged as though an invisible hand had seized him by the throat, his gaze overflowing with shock and horror.

From within the so-called Netherworld Ghost Fire ahead, a graceful figure strolled leisurely out. The cold flames churned restlessly around her, yet could not harm her in the slightest.

At that moment, Lin Yun'er's entire body radiated a dazzling light. That light seemed to contain some wondrous power, and it was precisely this layer of radiance that blocked the Netherworld Ghost Fire from harming her, allowing her to remain perfectly safe.

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes slightly, inwardly shocked.

He could naturally tell that this radiance did not come from the power of a defensive Imperial Treasure. Rather, Lin Yun'er had activated some unknown Secret Technique.

What kind of Secret Technique could possess such formidable defensive power?

"Master said that anyone who tries to kill me must die!" Lin Yun'er said softly, her face filled with devotion, as though she regarded her Master's words as inviolable law. As soon as she finished speaking, she hurled herself straight at Xiong Kaishan.

Xiong Kaishan drew a sharp breath. His strongest Divine Ability had been broken so effortlessly, so how could he have any confidence left to fight the young woman? Seeing her charge at him with such ferocity, he bit the tip of his tongue, burned his Essence-Blood, turned around, and fled.

But before he had taken more than a few steps, he suddenly noticed that the surrounding space had become incomparably viscous, making every step feel arduous.

"You..." Xiong Kaishan turned to look at Yang Kai, his face filled with grief and indignation and his eyes brimming with an accusation written in blood and tears. He had clearly sensed Yang Kai interfering and cutting off his only hope of escape.

"Have a safe journey!" Yang Kai smiled and waved at him as though bidding farewell to an old friend.

Xiong Kaishan felt the Qi and blood in his chest churn, and a mouthful of blood burst uncontrollably from his lips.

The next instant, Lin Yun'er slammed into his back and drove both fists forward with explosive force.

Blood filled the sky, blooming like a rose in full flower as flesh and bone rained down. Yet the young woman was like a pure white lotus, emerging unstained from the mud.

Suddenly, the young woman withdrew her imposing aura, and the pitch-black gauntlets over her fists disappeared without a trace. She flew like lightning into Yang Kai's arms and cried sweetly, "Uncle Yang!"

The change was rather abrupt. Caught completely off guard, Yang Kai was wrapped in a full embrace and forced back more than a dozen steps before he managed to steady himself.

The young woman's natural fragrance lingered at the tip of his nose, while her hair gave off an even more intoxicating scent. The fullness of her chest pressed against Yang Kai's, and he immediately felt an astonishing elasticity.

Yang Kai looked thoroughly embarrassed, never having expected the young woman to be so enthusiastic.

Lin Yun’er buried her head in Yang Kai’s chest and took several deep breaths. Then she looked up, a blissful expression on her face. Narrowing her eyes slightly, she murmured to herself, “It’s the same familiar scent… I even dream about it.”

Looking at the young woman's sincere face and hearing the heartfelt emotion in her voice, Yang Kai felt warmth flow through his heart. He raised a hand and patted her head, smiling as he said, "Little Yun'er has grown up too."

Lin Yun'er giggled, revealing a row of pearly white teeth. She took the initiative to put a little distance between herself and Yang Kai, then looked him up and down and nodded repeatedly. “Uncle Yang, you haven’t changed much. If you had, I might not have recognized you.”

After saying that, she hugged Yang Kai again as though she never wanted to let go.

"This isn't a good place to talk. Let's speak while we travel," Yang Kai suggested.

"Wait a little longer." Lin Yun'er did not move, seemingly wanting to deeply engrave this familiar scent and feeling into her memory.

Since she would not move, Yang Kai could not bring himself to force her and could only stand there.

A moment later, Yang Kai suddenly frowned when he distinctly felt a trace of warmth against his chest. Looking down, he saw the young woman's long eyelashes trembling gently and the corners of her eyes glistening with moisture.

"Why are you crying?" Yang Kai asked with a helpless laugh.

"I missed all of you!" Lin Yun'er's voice was filled with grievance. "I missed Aunt Yang Yan, Aunt Huang, Aunt Ye, and you. I missed you all so much, but I couldn't see any of you. I didn't know where any of you were, and I couldn't find you."

Her words made Yang Kai's nose sting. Although the little girl's strength was astonishing, she was still young, so it was only natural for her to be sentimental. Moreover, she had been taken away at a very young age. Now that she had finally met someone from her homeland, all the emotions she had kept suppressed immediately poured out.

Yang Kai raised a hand and gently patted her back, sharing this rare moment of warmth with her.

Gurgle...

A strange sound broke the mood. Yang Kai was startled at first, but once he realized what it was, he nearly burst out laughing.

Lin Yun'er lowered her head as a blush climbed from her neck to her cheeks and spread all the way to her delicate earlobes.

"Hungry?" Yang Kai asked, looking down at her.

Lin Yun'er gave an almost imperceptible nod. To her, this seemed to be an exceedingly shameful thing.

Yang Kai could not understand it. Logically speaking, after cultivating to the First-Stage Emperor Realm, Lin Yun'er should have been fine even if she went decades without eating. At her level of strength, she could dine on rosy clouds and drink morning dew, while the Spiritual Qi of the world was enough to satisfy her body's needs. How could she possibly be hungry?

An Emperor Realm Master would eat either to satisfy a craving for food or for the sake of cultivation.

Yang Kai had not experienced the feeling of hunger for a very long time.

Without asking further, Yang Kai immersed his Divine Sense into the Small Xuan Realm and began searching.

Although he did not have any delicious food on hand, he possessed plenty of spirit fruits. With so many exotic flowers and rare herbs growing in the Small Xuan Realm's medicine garden, finding a few fruits could not have been easier.

Moreover, these were no ordinary spirit fruits, but extremely high-grade ones.

With a flip of his hand, three bright red fruits appeared in Yang Kai's palm, each the size of an infant's fist. "Would you like these?"

"Uncle Yang, you're so good to me." The young woman immediately perked up and forgot her embarrassment. She hurriedly took the fruits and began nibbling on one, narrowing her eyes in satisfaction.

She seemed to eat slowly and carefully, but was actually extremely fast without losing the slightest bit of elegance. The tear stains at the corners of her eyes had yet to fade, but her face was already covered in smiles, making her look rather carefree.

"Let's go!" Yang Kai called out.

"Wait a moment longer." After saying this, Lin Yun'er's tender body flickered and she darted a short distance away.

An Emperor Realm Master lay there, heavily wounded. He was the first person Yang Kai had injured earlier.

Just as Yang Kai wondered what the little girl was doing, he saw Lin Yun'er continue eating her spirit fruit while throwing a punch at the man, directly blasting the half-dead fellow apart until not even his bones remained.

Yang Kai's eyelid twitched at the sight.

Lin Yun'er turned around and went over to another Emperor Realm Master, repeating the same process. She showed no hesitation whatsoever from beginning to end. To her, it was as though she had not killed two Emperor Realm Masters, but two insignificant insects.

Innocent and guileless on one hand, yet killing as though it were nothing on the other, Lin Yun'er left Yang Kai feeling that he could no longer understand this little girl.

## Chapter 3017 — Is Dragon Isle Beneath the Sea?

"Whether it's useful or not, we'll know once we try!" Yang Kai took a deep breath and poured his Emperor Yuan into the Dragon Isle Token.

Li Jiao watched with wide eyes, afraid he might miss something.

The Dragon Isle Token had been in Yang Kai's possession for many years. When he first obtained it, he had tried the same thing, wanting to learn what purpose the token served. However, no matter how he urged his Emperor Yuan, the Dragon Isle Token had not reacted in the slightest.

This time was no exception.

The Dragon Isle Token swallowed his Emperor Yuan, but the simple, ancient token showed no reaction whatsoever.

The expectant looks in Yang Kai and Li Jiao's eyes quickly turned to disappointment.

"Why... why isn't it working?" Li Jiao felt somewhat guilty. Although his earlier words had only been speculation, they were not without basis. Who could have known that the experiment would produce no result at all? He could not help feeling embarrassed, as though he had offered a terrible idea.

Yang Kai sighed softly. Just as he was about to withdraw his Emperor Yuan, a sudden change occurred.

The dragon carving on the front of the Dragon Isle Token suddenly blinked.

Yang Kai raised his head and looked at Li Jiao, only to find Li Jiao staring back at him with equally wide eyes.

Their gazes met, and Yang Kai asked, "Did you see that?"

Li Jiao replied, "You saw it too?"

Their spirits lifted, and they turned their attention back to the Dragon Isle Token. This time, they finally saw it clearly. The dragon carving really was blinking. Not only that, but the entire dragon seemed to have been granted life, as though it might leap out of the token.

The lifelike dragon stretched its body as if it had truly come alive and began swimming within the palm-sized token.

Of course, no matter how it swam, it could not escape the confines of the Dragon Isle Token. Its movements merely gave the impression that it was swimming.

Yang Kai observed it carefully, then seemed to recall something. Holding the Dragon Isle Token flat in his palm, he slowly turned it in different directions.

The dragon did not turn with it. No matter how Yang Kai rotated the Dragon Isle Token, it continued facing a particular direction, its claws churning as though it were soaring through the clouds.

"The dragon's head points toward Dragon Isle!" Li Jiao called out in a low voice.

"Let's go!" Yang Kai was overjoyed and sped off in the direction the dragon's head faced.

Li Jiao also seemed to receive a surge of energy and hurriedly followed behind him.

They flew onward at full speed. There was no longer any need to pay attention to their surroundings; Yang Kai only needed to follow the direction indicated by the dragon's head.

After they had flown an unknown number of tens of thousands of li, the dragon on the token suddenly reversed direction. The dragon's head moved to where its tail had been, while its tail took the former place of its head.

Yang Kai came to an abrupt halt, and Li Jiao stopped sharply beside him. "What's wrong?"

Yang Kai did not answer and instead slowly retreated.

After he had withdrawn several dozen steps, the dragon on the token suddenly changed again. Its body bent at a right angle, and its head disappeared from view, leaving only its tail facing Yang Kai.

"What does this mean?" Li Jiao had also been watching every change in the Dragon Isle Token and could not help staring blankly at the sight.

Yang Kai frowned and observed it carefully for a moment before pointing toward the sea. "Dragon Isle is below us!"

“What?” Li Jiao exclaimed in shock. “Dragon Isle is below us? You’re not mistaken?”

Yang Kai replied, “If this token truly serves as a guide, then I’m not.”

Yang Kai held the Dragon Isle Token flat in his palm. At this moment, the dragon on the token was clearly aligned straight toward the sea below, its head pointing into the depths.

"How could that be? How could Dragon Isle be beneath the sea?" Li Jiao found this difficult to accept. Dragon Isle was an island, so it ought to be above the sea. No matter how he thought about it, there was no way it could be at the bottom of the ocean.

Yet he had no explanation for the Dragon Isle Token's guidance.

Yang Kai, however, said, "If that's the case, then it isn't surprising that Dragon Isle has always been so mysterious."

Everyone assumed Dragon Isle was above the sea. No one had ever considered that it might lie beneath it, which naturally made Dragon Isle even harder to find.

"Let's go take a look!" Yang Kai said before plunging headfirst toward the sea below.

With a thunderous splash, seawater sprayed in every direction and Yang Kai vanished from sight. As he urged his Emperor Yuan, a protective barrier formed around his body and pushed back the surrounding water.

At first, there was still plenty of light, but as Yang Kai descended, the depths grew darker and darker. Enormous sea beasts also swam past, their colossal shadows inspiring an intense sense of oppression and dread.

Sea beasts tended to be enormous, and their strength was equally astonishing.

Fortunately, Yang Kai and Li Jiao were no weaklings. When they released their Imperial Might, the nearby sea beasts did not dare approach, allowing them to proceed without obstruction.

After an unknown amount of time, the sea around them became utterly dark, so black that they could not see their hands before their faces. The pressure around them had also become terrifying. Even a Dao Origin Realm cultivator would be crushed apart under such immense pressure, and only those in the Emperor Realm could barely continue descending.

The Emperor Yuan within their bodies drained away as swiftly as floodwaters through an opened sluice gate.

Cold sweat had already appeared on Li Jiao's forehead, but when his Divine Sense revealed that Yang Kai had no intention of stopping, he could only grit his teeth and follow.

A powerful aura rapidly approached from ahead. It was a sea beast heedlessly charging straight toward them.

Although their Imperial Might could frighten off most sea beasts, there were also overlord-like existences that feared neither Heaven nor Earth.

A dark shadow swept past, and before either of them could react, they were swallowed into its belly. A foul, fishy stench immediately enveloped them.

Yang Kai's expression sank, and with a lift of his hand, he sent several Moon Blades slashing out.

Several dull thuds rang out. Yang Kai charged in the direction the Moon Blades had struck and pierced through the sea beast's belly.

Looking back, he saw the enormous shadow thrashing wildly as the pitch-black seawater seemed to turn red. Li Jiao also broke free through the wound without difficulty. Sea beasts possessed tremendous vitality, so even such injuries would not kill it. However, after suffering this loss, it immediately understood that it had provoked enemies it should not have. With a flick of its tail, it fled in a sorry state.

"Keep up!" Yang Kai transmitted to Li Jiao.

Li Jiao nodded, increased his speed, and followed close behind.

Another quarter-hour passed, yet the seabed was still nowhere in sight. Yang Kai, however, suddenly stopped.

They had descended an unknown number of li beneath the sea. Not a trace of light or the slightest sound reached this place. It was deathly still, as though the world had yet to be born and everything remained within primordial chaos.

"What's wrong?" Li Jiao stood beside Yang Kai. The protective layer of Emperor Yuan around his body had been squeezed slightly out of shape by the seawater, and he looked at Yang Kai in confusion.

Yang Kai replied, "We came to the right place."

Li Jiao was delighted. "Good, good. As long as this is the right place. But... is Dragon Isle really beneath the sea?"

Yang Kai shook his head. "It isn't beneath the sea."

Li Jiao was bewildered. "What do you mean?"

The two of them had already descended this far beneath the sea, and Yang Kai had not said they had gone the wrong way. If Dragon Isle was not here, where else could it be?

"The entrance to Dragon Isle is just ahead!" Yang Kai raised his hand and pointed in a certain direction.

Li Jiao hurriedly released his Divine Sense to investigate, but found nothing in the area it swept across.

"A Void Passage!" Yang Kai breathed out softly. "There's a Void Passage here, but it's extremely well hidden. Take a closer look."

With this reminder, Li Jiao finally noticed something unusual. There were indeed traces of a Void Passage in a certain spot. Although he had never cultivated the Power of Space, he was still a Third-Stage Emperor and possessed the necessary experience and discernment. He immediately exclaimed in surprise, "Dragon Isle is hidden on the other side of that Void Passage?"

"That's right!" Yang Kai nodded lightly.

With this, everything made sense.

Yang Kai had also been puzzled before, unable to understand why the Dragon Isle Token pointed beneath the sea. It was only after discovering the entrance to this Void Passage that realization dawned on him.

Dragon Isle's actual location could be set aside for now. This entrance clearly led to Dragon Isle, and the direction indicated by the Dragon Isle Token was precisely where the entrance lay.

Gulp...

Li Jiao swallowed hard and asked, "If we pass through here, we'll be able to enter Dragon Isle?"

His lifelong pursuit and obsession were now within reach, so Li Jiao could not help becoming nervous. Countless times, he had imagined what Dragon Isle looked like and how he would feel after setting foot upon it. Now that this goal had suddenly become attainable, he found himself beset by both hope and fear.

"Brother Li, if you want to go back, you can leave now. I can handle the rest alone," Yang Kai transmitted to him.

Hearing this, Li Jiao truly felt an impulse to leave this place and return to the Northern Territory, but it stirred in his heart for only a moment before vanishing without a trace.

"I'm going in!" Li Jiao declared solemnly.

"Then... are you ready?" Yang Kai turned to him and asked with a smile.

Li Jiao's expression became solemn. He took several deep breaths to calm the turbulent emotions in his heart, then nodded heavily. "Mm!"

"Let's go!" Yang Kai shouted. The Power of Space enveloped Li Jiao, and the two rushed into the Void Passage.

The surrounding space suddenly shifted, and a tearing force came from all directions. As the barrier of space was breached, it seemed the two of them would enter a new world in the very next instant.

Just then, however, a resounding dragon roar suddenly rang out from the void ahead. Immediately afterward, several enormous dragon phantoms charged toward them with overwhelming momentum and murderous intent.

The moment the dragon roar sounded, Li Jiao jumped in fright and every hair on his body stood on end. He felt as though the aura of death had enveloped him.

Yang Kai's expression also changed. Sensing the terrifying destructive power contained within those dragon phantoms, he did not even pause to think before shouting, "Dragon Transformation!"

Another resounding dragon roar rang out. A dazzling golden dragon phantom flashed behind Yang Kai, rushed into his body, and vanished.

His Dragon Clan bloodline was stimulated, and a series of cracking sounds rang out as Yang Kai's body abruptly expanded to a height of four and a half zhang. Short horns protruded from his forehead, his hands transformed into dragon claws, and dragon scales covered his entire body.

He had only just transformed into his half-dragon form when the dragon phantoms ahead slammed into him.

An irresistible force struck him from the front, sending Yang Kai stumbling back more than a dozen zhang. Something sweet rose in his throat, and he nearly spat out a mouthful of blood.

After that single charge, the dragon phantoms immediately turned back and hovered high above, looking down from on high.

Yang Kai broke out in a cold sweat. If his reaction had been even slightly slower just now, he would have been severely injured even if he survived. Fortunately, the Dragon Transformation had protected him; otherwise, he would have been in serious trouble.

He focused his attention on the dragon phantoms, examining them closely as his brow furrowed.

He had discovered that these dragon phantoms were not physical objects, nor did they resemble Spirit Bodies. Instead, they seemed more like some kind of Secret Technique.

A Dragon Clan Secret Technique!

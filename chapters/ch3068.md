## Chapter 3068 — Second Dragon Transformation

Li Jiao did not want to die. He had bared his neck and waited for death earlier only because he could see no hope of survival. Now that hope had appeared, he naturally had no intention of sitting still and awaiting his doom.

Reaching into his Spatial Ring, he pulled out a handful of Pills and stuffed them all into his mouth. At the same time, with a thought, a cracked bead shot over from the sea and was swallowed by Li Jiao in one gulp.

That bead was none other than his impure Dragon Pearl.

With the Dragon Pearl in his belly and the aid of the Pills, Li Jiao finally recovered a little strength.

He beckoned, calling back his Golden Lion Halberd, which had lost much of its spirituality. Forcing himself to gather a breath, he hurriedly fled into the distance.

He did not know who the person behind him really was, but he knew the Divine Soul who had told him to flee must know Yang Kai. Therefore, he had to hurry over and warn Yang Kai. Unfortunately, that Divine Soul had not explained himself clearly at the time, or rather, he had not had the chance to do so. Otherwise, Li Jiao should have been able to work out his true identity.

......

Dragon roars shook the heavens. High in the void, Yang Kai remained locked in battle with four Dragons. Even though Zhu Qing and Fu Ling had rushed over and relieved some of the pressure on him, he was still at a disadvantage.

The Dragon Clan were Divine Spirits, and all four Dragons present were fully grown. Although their bloodlines varied in rank, every member of the Dragon Clan could easily crush an ordinary Third-Stage Emperor. Even after transforming into an eight-zhang Dragon body of unmatched ferocity, Yang Kai was still no match for them.

He secretly rejoiced that Zhu Qing and Fu Ling had come to share the pressure. Otherwise, he would likely have already lost this battle.

Although the situation was unfavorable, he still had some chance of turning it around.

He had been waiting all along, waiting for the right opportunity to severely wound all four Dragons in one stroke. He still had a trump card he had yet to reveal. A trump card was called a trump card precisely because it could produce a tremendous effect when used unexpectedly.

Blood streamed from his wounds, and there was hardly an uninjured patch anywhere on his body. His aura was no longer at its peak either, and he looked as though he might collapse at any moment.

Seeing this, the four Dragons no longer hesitated and lunged at him from four directions.

Yang Kai shifted his body, evading a Dragon Claw that raked at him and dodging a blast of Dragon Breath. He forcibly endured the Dragon Clan Secret Technique unleashed by another Dragon, his body shuddering violently, but was caught off guard when the fourth Dragon tightly coiled around him.

That Dragon was the strongest of the four present, possessing a Seventh-Tier bloodline and a body nearly twenty zhang long. His Dragon body bound Yang Kai fast, preventing him from easily using his Space Divine Ability to escape, while cracking sounds rang out from every bone in Yang Kai's body.

The other three Dragons were overjoyed. Wheeling around, they charged at him again, an icy gleam flashing in every great Dragon's eyes. If Yang Kai could not break free before they reached him, he would likely meet with disaster today.

Yang Kai roared, a sharp light flashing through his eyes.

At this point, he could no longer hide anything and could only use his final means in a desperate fight to the death.

Just then, his expression suddenly changed, and immediately afterward, wild joy spread across his face.

Flipping his wrist, he suddenly summoned an object into his hand, then hurled it at one of the approaching Dragons and roared, "Get him!"

The other three Dragons saw what he had done, and all of them were startled. Before they knew what Yang Kai had thrown, they did not dare meet it rashly. After all, they had already experienced Yang Kai's ferocity and tenacity firsthand.

Anything Yang Kai brought out at a time like this could not possibly be weak.

Under everyone's watchful eyes, a round stone shot through the air. It was roughly as tall as a man, smooth all over, and looked entirely ordinary.

What was this? The members of the Dragon Clan were all somewhat puzzled at the sight.

The Dragon flying directly toward the stone grew even more impatient and swept his Dragon Tail at it, intending to knock the round stone out of his way.

But the instant the Dragon Tail struck the round stone, an unexpected change occurred.

An explosion rang out, stone chips flying everywhere as the entire round stone suddenly shattered into pieces.

This sudden change startled all the Dragons. Looking closely, they saw that amid the shattered stones, a stone man about the height of an ordinary person had strangely appeared. This stone man’s entire body was sharp and angular, with complete facial features and unusually long limbs. If that were all, it would have been one thing, but the key was that his whole body burned with crimson flames, and there was actually an aura within him unique to a Divine Spirit.

"Shi Huo!" one Dragon growled, his face filled with disbelief.

As fellow Divine Spirits, the Dragon Clan were naturally familiar with Shi Huo. Although Shi Huo did not rank particularly high among the Divine Spirits, his rank was by no means low.

What the Dragon Clan could not understand was how this Shi Huo had leapt out of a round stone, much less why Yang Kai had been the one to throw him.

How had Yang Kai managed that here, in the middle of this vast sea?

The moment Shi Huo appeared, he let out a furious roar. The sound wave bursting from his mouth was almost visible to the naked eye. Like a shockwave, it rippled through the void, forcing the oncoming Dragon to halt involuntarily.

He instinctively sensed that this Shi Huo was not so simple.

"Get lost!" A Dragon Claw suddenly lashed out, raking viciously at Shi Huo.

So what if he was Shi Huo? No Divine Spirit could hold its head high before the Dragon Clan. Besides, this Shi Huo seemed to have only just been born, so he should not be capable of much.

In the next instant, that Dragon realized just how terribly mistaken he was.

After roaring, the man-sized Shi Huo rapidly began to swell.

Three zhang, five zhang, ten zhang, twenty zhang....

In almost the span of a single breath, Shi Huo, who had seemed no more than an ant in the Dragon's eyes, swelled until he was nearly the same size as him. The flames pouring from his body grew even fiercer and more turbulent, as though they could burn everything beneath the heavens.

With a thunderous crash, the Dragon Claw raked across Shi Huo's chest, sending dazzling sparks flying and producing an ear-piercing screech. Shi Huo staggered slightly and retreated several steps as several claw marks instantly appeared on his chest.

The Dragon that had attacked froze, clearly not expecting such a development.

Was this truly Shi Huo? Since when had Shi Huo possessed such an ability?

After taking the blow, Shi Huo lowered his head and looked at his chest, then raised it again. His two red-glowing eyes flickered like flames, and a strange, hoarse chuckle rumbled from his throat.

Then he abruptly thrust out both arms, grabbed the lower half of the Dragon's body, and viciously dragged him closer.

A Dragon roar rang out. Caught off guard, the Dragon was yanked over, and their two bodies slammed together with a heaven-shaking crash. Immediately afterward, the smell of roasting meat drifted through the air, making him roar in pain. Entangled with each other, the pair plunged into the sea, sending up an enormous wave and a billowing cloud of steam.

Of the four Dragons working together, one was now tied down by Shi Huo's appearance, leaving only three members of the Dragon Clan to deal with Yang Kai.

"Heh heh heh heh!" Yang Kai suddenly grinned, his laughter rolling out like thunder.

"You still dare act so arrogantly with death at hand!" the Dragon binding Yang Kai bellowed. He truly could not understand what Yang Kai found so amusing. Although one unexpected change after another had occurred today, Yang Kai was still clearly at a disadvantage.

The three Dragons were still certain of victory against him.

"It's time!" Yang Kai's expression turned solemn, and his voice sank low.

As soon as he spoke, all three Dragons were seized by an extremely ominous feeling. No longer hesitating, they unleashed their killing moves together. The great Dragon that had been binding Yang Kai with his body used even the strength he had suckled from his mother's milk to restrain him, leaving Yang Kai no room to struggle.

The other two Dragons lunged in from the left and right. A sharp, cold gleam flashed across their Dragon Claws as they tore through the barrier of space and struck straight toward Yang Kai's vital points.

"Dragon... Transformation!"

When that low voice rang out, all three Dragons froze.

Dragon Transformation? Was his current form not already a Dragon Transformation? For a Human to transform into an eight-zhang Dragon body was already an inconceivable achievement, yet he intended to undergo another Dragon Transformation?

Could he possibly be trying to transform into a True Dragon?

As that thought arose, all three Dragons could not help but scoff. In their view, no matter how hard Yang Kai tried, he would never become a True Dragon in this lifetime. This was a limitation of race that no human effort could overcome.

But immediately afterward, their disdain turned to horror.

The moment the words "Dragon Transformation" left Yang Kai's mouth, his already formidable Dragon Pressure began climbing steadily, swiftly reaching a level that terrified all three Dragons.

At the same time, a succession of cracking sounds rang out as Yang Kai, who was already eight zhang tall, began growing explosively once more.

"This is..." The Dragon that had kept Yang Kai bound widened his eyes. He only felt an immense force pushing outward from every direction, involuntarily prying open his coiled body.

Eight zhang, ten zhang, twelve zhang, fifteen zhang!

With the blue sky above his head and the air beneath his feet, Yang Kai stood towering like a giant, his terrifying pressure spreading wantonly, making Heaven and Earth tremble as thousand-layered waves surged across the sea.

The three Dragons' eyes trembled violently.

The members of the Dragon Clan fighting Zhu Qing and Fu Ling also lost focus, all turning to look this way.

Zhu Qing was stunned for a moment as well, but a trace of joy soon flashed through her eyes.

Even she had not expected Yang Kai to have been concealing his strength all along, waiting for an opportunity to counterattack.

And now, that opportunity had arrived.

There was a world of difference between an eight-zhang Dragon body and a fifteen-zhang one. The Dragon Clan judged the strength and rank of a bloodline by body size, and Yang Kai had naturally inherited this trait through cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art. The larger his body, the purer his bloodline.

A little over a month ago, his Dragon body had only been five zhang tall, but a month later, it had reached fifteen zhang. A threefold difference in size did not bring a threefold increase in strength. His actual strength had not increased by all that much, but his Dragon Bloodline Pressure and bloodline suppression had grown by more than three times.

His ability to advance the Dragon Transformation Art to this degree was naturally thanks to those Dragon Blood Flowers.

In particular, the Dragon Blood Pills refined from those two Supreme-Level Dragon Blood Flowers were responsible for most of his growth over the past month; the remaining credit belonged to the Upper-Level Dragon Blood Flowers.

Had he been given a little more time, this fifteen-zhang Dragon body should have grown even taller.

His size was not the only thing that had changed. The Dragon Scales covering Yang Kai's body had originally been nothing special, but now every one of them shone with a faint golden light. His whole body looked as though it had been coated in molten gold, gleaming brilliantly beneath the sunlight.

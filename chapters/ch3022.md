## Chapter 3022 — Third-Tier Dragon Bloodline

As a mother, how could the woman be willing to let her daughter watch her being tortured?

The young girl, however, was stunned. Her eyes were wide, and tears streamed down her face, though it was unclear whether she was frightened or overwhelmed by something else.

“If you’d agreed sooner, none of this would have been necessary.” The sinister man let out a cold snort as he viciously ground his foot against her chest several times. The sound of bones slipping out of place immediately rang out, yet he acted as though he had heard nothing. Bending down, he said, “Had you agreed earlier, you wouldn’t have had to suffer like this.”

The woman's face twisted in pain, but she gritted her teeth and remained silent. She only looked at her daughter and forced out a smile, though it looked even more miserable than crying.

"Wretched woman! It is your good fortune that this Lord has taken a fancy to you, yet you actually dared to make excuse after excuse!" The sinister man seemed not to have had his fill of tormenting her and still wanted to vent the fury in his heart. He also seemed to take great pleasure in brutalizing the woman, his eyes filled with delight.

Inside the teahouse, Yang Kai's gaze turned slightly cold.

He and Li Jiao had watched everything unfold from beginning to end, so they naturally understood exactly what had happened.

Although they had only just arrived and had yet to grasp the situation in Half-Dragon City, Yang Kai could not stand by and watch a mother and daughter be abused like this right under his nose.

Just as he was about to act, Li Jiao abruptly rose to his feet and stared blankly at the woman in palace dress who was being trampled on the ground, disbelief filling his eyes. In the next instant, fury consumed him.

"It's too late to beg for mercy now." The sinister man continued tormenting the woman in palace dress. "Neither you nor your daughter will escape. Both of you will come back with this Lord."

Saying so, he held the young girl in one hand and reached for her mother with the other.

A figure suddenly appeared before him, and shocking Imperial Might descended from the sky, enveloping the sinister man directly.

The sinister man's expression changed drastically. Pushing off with both feet, he drifted backward while shouting, "Who are you?"

Li Jiao thrust out his hand and punched straight at the man's face.

The sinister man was horrified and hurriedly circulated his Emperor Yuan to defend himself, but in the next instant, that fist rapidly filled his vision and struck him squarely on the bridge of his nose.

With a crack, something seemed to break, and the sinister man was sent flying by the punch. At the same time, his hand suddenly felt light; the young girl he had been holding had actually slipped free of his control.

A series of crashes rang out as the sinister man flew backward into the shop behind him, smashing who knew how many things along the way. He struggled to his feet, his head spinning and the reek of blood filling his nose and mouth.

He wiped his face with one hand and found it covered in blood. The bridge of his nose also seemed to have been broken.

The sinister man flew into a rage and instantly rushed out of the shop. He glared coldly at Li Jiao, murderous intent surging like a tide, and snarled through gritted teeth, "Insolence!"

Li Jiao ignored him and instead looked at the young girl he had rescued, then at the woman in palace dress lying on the ground. In a low voice, he said, "Go help your mother up."

The young girl did not move. She had probably been frightened senseless.

The woman in palace dress stared at Li Jiao in utter shock, seemingly never expecting anyone to stand up for her at a time like this. After all, the sinister man was not someone who could be easily offended. Although his cultivation was not among the highest in Half-Dragon City and many people were stronger than him, he was still someone no one could afford to offend.

Anyone who offended him met a bad end, regardless of their cultivation.

After getting a clear look at Li Jiao's face, the woman could not help widening her eyes and letting out a soft cry.

Their eyes met. A trace of pity and nostalgia flashed through Li Jiao's eyes, while the woman immediately looked away, avoiding his gaze.

Seeing her reaction, Li Jiao sighed softly and gave the young girl another push. Only then did she come back to her senses. Wailing loudly, she stepped forward and helped her mother up.

Li Jiao's dismissive attitude undoubtedly enraged the sinister man. Strangely, although he could tell that Li Jiao's cultivation was one Stage above his own, he showed no fear. Instead, he glared viciously at Li Jiao and declared, "You dared to injure this Lord! No matter who you are, you're dead today."

"That's exactly what I wanted to say." Li Jiao turned to look at the sinister man. His expression was calm, but anyone could hear the suppressed fury in his voice.

Inside the teahouse, Yang Kai narrowed his eyes slightly and raised a cup of tea to take a sip. His gaze moved from the woman in palace dress to Li Jiao, and his expression shifted as he realized something.

The sinister man froze for a moment, then burst into laughter and snarled, "You've got some nerve. No one in Half-Dragon City has ever dared speak to me like that."

"Now there is!" Li Jiao drew a slow breath, trying to calm the fury in his heart. He had never been so angry before. The rage churning in his chest felt as though it would burst out of him, leaving him desperate to vent it by pulverizing the sinister man.

"Courting death!" The sinister man was likewise beside himself with fury. As he released his aura, the few dragon scales below his neck suddenly deepened in color, and a faint Dragon Pressure instantly spread outward.

Many of the Dragon Descendants among the surrounding onlookers immediately showed expressions of discomfort.

There was nothing they could do; this was suppression through bloodline. The sinister man's Dragon Clan bloodline was stronger than theirs, and he also possessed a cultivation at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. With his aura released at full strength, few Dragon Descendants in all of Half-Dragon City could withstand it.

Even the Humans and Yao who were not Dragon Descendants wore strained expressions at this moment and retreated one after another.

Li Jiao's expression remained unchanged. He stood as steady as a mountain and merely watched the sinister man with cold eyes.

Seeing his calm demeanor, the sinister man's expression could not help changing. Something was wrong. He had also sensed the Dragon Clan bloodline within Li Jiao, which was why he had acted so fearlessly, certain that Li Jiao's Dragon Clan bloodline could not be as pure as his own.

Yet when his Dragon Pressure spread out, Li Jiao's reaction astonished him.

The other man was completely unaffected!

How was that possible? Had bloodline suppression failed?

Bloodline suppression could not possibly be ineffective. There was likely only one reason the other man remained so calm: his bloodline was definitely no weaker than his own!

At this thought, the sinister man's expression changed drastically. His cultivation was already inferior, and if he also held no advantage in bloodline, then he was no match at all.

The sinister man carefully sized up Li Jiao, and a trace of suspicion flashed through his eyes. Li Jiao looked completely unfamiliar, and he had no impression of ever seeing him before.

Although Half-Dragon City had a great many Dragon Descendants, numbering in the tens of thousands, there were not many among them in the Emperor Realm.

There were only a few hundred Emperor Realm cultivators in all of Half-Dragon City, and Humans and Yao made up over eighty percent of them. At most, only a hundred Emperor Realm cultivators possessed the Dragon Clan's bloodline. He was familiar with nearly all of these hundred people; even if they were not close, he had at least seen most of them several times.

After all, they all made their living in Half-Dragon City. Though the city was large, they were bound to cross paths after living there year after year.

Li Jiao, however, was a face he had never seen before.

Where had this fellow come from? He was at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, and his bloodline was not the slightest bit weaker than his own... A man like this was simply unheard of.

At this realization, the sinister man was dumbfounded!

The woman in palace dress was also dumbfounded!

The surrounding cultivators were dumbfounded as well! Everyone stared at Li Jiao in astonishment as though flowers had bloomed on his face.

A moment later, cold sweat began to bead on the sinister man's forehead. He felt as though he had kicked an iron plate this time.

"What a disgrace!" Li Jiao coldly snorted and released his own might. In the next instant, a Dragon Pressure clearly a little stronger than the sinister man's enveloped the surroundings. The sinister man's Dragon Pressure burst apart like a bubble.

Muffled groans rang out all around, each one a reaction to the suppression.

"A Third-Tier Dragon Bloodline!"

"It's actually a Third-Tier Dragon Bloodline?"

"There's a Third-Tier Dragon Bloodline in Half-Dragon City?"

Cries of alarm rang out one after another, and Li Jiao frowned when he heard them. Although he knew his Dragon Clan bloodline was impure, he had no idea which Tier it belonged to. After all, he had neither anyone to compare himself with nor any standard by which to judge.

The classification of Dragon Clan bloodlines came from Dragon Isle and the Dragon Clan, and outsiders had no knowledge of it.

Yet from the cries around him, Li Jiao discovered that Half-Dragon City's Dragon Descendants seemed to understand the bloodline ranks quite well.

So he had a Third-Tier Dragon Bloodline? This was the first time even Li Jiao had learned of it.

"Does our Half-Dragon City have someone with such a Dragon Bloodline?"

"Who is this man? Why have I never seen him before?"

"Judging by his might, his bloodline should be at the Third-Tier Upper-Level, right?"

"It shouldn't be quite that high. It's probably only Middle-Level."

"Someone's in trouble now."

"Don't joke around. You don't know that man's background. He may not be the one who ends up in trouble."

...

The sinister man's expression shifted repeatedly as he cursed inwardly. What the hell was going on? Where had a Third-Tier Middle-Level Dragon Bloodline suddenly come from? His own was only Second-Tier Upper-Level, a full two Stages below the other man's.

His cultivation was inferior, and so was his bloodline. How was he supposed to fight?

As the Dragon Pressure bore down on him, the sinister man's expression became extremely strained. He felt his Emperor Yuan turn sluggish, while his meridians, flesh, and blood writhed in waves. The trembling that rose from the depths of his soul filled him with panic and unease.

This was also Li Jiao's first taste of the benefits of bloodline suppression. In the past, he had always been the one suppressed, cowering like a grandson before Zhu Lie and Zhu Qing. Now, at last, it was his turn to play the grandfather.

The fury in his heart had not subsided. Raising his hand, he slapped the sinister man across the face.

The sinister man clearly wanted to resist, but he lacked the strength to do so. When the slap came, he could not dodge it at all.

A crisp smack rang out. One side of the sinister man's face swelled up, and several teeth flew from his mouth. His entire body spun several times through the air before crashing heavily to the ground, showing just how powerful Li Jiao's slap had been.

Li Jiao turned his head, and his icy expression abruptly softened. Looking kindly at the young girl, he asked, "He slapped you once, so I slapped him once too. Did that help vent your anger?"

The young girl was stunned. She did not know Li Jiao, much less why he was standing up for her. She and her mother had just been bullied by the sinister man, filling her with grievance and fear. Now that she saw evil receive its due, she was naturally overjoyed.

She also felt a surge of goodwill toward Li Jiao, whom she had met only by chance, and nodded firmly.

Li Jiao smiled. "If that wasn't enough, I'll slap him a few more times."

The young girl thought for a moment, then shook her head.

Li Jiao said, "Very well. Then I'll help your mother vent her anger too."

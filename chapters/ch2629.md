## Chapter 2629 — Senior Sister and Junior Sister Reunite

"Dividing up Ice Heart Valley disciples?" Yang Kai's eyes widened in shock.

Fang Minghui grinned shamelessly and said, "Ice Heart Valley is full of women, each pure as jade, cold as frost, and exceptionally talented. If a man could win one or two in his lifetime, he'd have no regrets."

In the Northern Territory, Ice Heart Valley was renowned. To those lesser powers, the female disciples of Ice Heart Valley were like goddesses—admired from afar, never to be defiled.

Throughout history, countless men in the Northern Territory have considered marrying an Ice Heart Valley disciple a great honor.

But few have ever succeeded. Now, with Wenqing Sect's recruitment order, countless men in the Northern Territory were going mad. The rewards were one thing, but if someone earned merit and, after Ice Heart Valley was destroyed, got to take away one or two female disciples—even as slaves or servants—it would be a matter of pride. Even marrying one as a proper wife would only invite envy, not shame.

"You all came for Ice Heart Valley disciples too?" Yang Kai looked curiously at Fang Minghui. This old fellow still had such thoughts at his age.

Fang Minghui smiled bitterly, "I don't dare hope for much. If I can just get some rewards from Wenqing Sect, my Fang family could enjoy them for years. If we could join Wenqing Sect, that would be best."

Yang Kai chuckled, "How many has Wenqing Sect recruited so far?"

"At least a hundred thousand. Most are family forces like mine, but some second- and third-rate sects have come to serve as well."

"Aren't you afraid Ice Heart Valley will rise again and come after you?"

Fang Minghui shook his head, "Ice Heart Valley has no chance."

"How so?" Yang Kai raised his brows.

Fang Minghui said, "I don't know the details, only that Wenqing Sect now has two experts at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. Ice Heart Valley's founder, Bing Yun, is also at that level, but she's alone. How can she compete? After years of fighting, Ice Heart Valley has suffered heavy losses. If not, they wouldn't have retreated inside, relying on their protective array to barely survive. And..."

"And what?" Yang Kai asked.

Fang Minghui smiled slightly, "Ice Heart Valley can't hold out much longer. Half a month at most, a month at the longest. Ice Heart Valley will be defeated."

Yang Kai snorted, "You're just at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Where does your confidence come from?"

Fang Minghui glanced around cautiously, then whispered, "Sect Master Feng Xuan invited Grandmaster Nanmen over to study the array. It won't be long before they figure it out. Once Ice Heart Valley's protective array is broken, we'll strike with all our might. Ice Heart Valley won't be able to resist."

Ji Yao and Shi Tianhe, standing behind Yang Kai, turned pale as they listened. Now, their faces were even whiter.

Fang Minghui rambled on, then said, "Sir, would you like to join in? Wenqing Sect is recruiting talents. With your strength, I'm sure they'd welcome you."

Yang Kai raised his brows, "Me too?"

Fang Minghui nodded, "Of course. Just register at Binglun City, state your background, and you can serve Wenqing Sect. The benefits will be plentiful... By the way, where are you from? If you have any connection to Ice Heart Valley, Wenqing Sect won't accept you."

Before Yang Kai could speak, Ji Yao stepped forward and said coldly, "I am Ji Yao of Ice Heart Valley!"

At her words, Fang Minghui and the others' faces changed dramatically, and they all retreated.

The Fang family members never expected that after chatting so long, the other party was actually from Ice Heart Valley.

Wasn't it said that Ice Heart Valley's people were all hiding inside their sect, not daring to come out, with the protective array already activated? How did these people get out?

"Run!" Fang Minghui reacted instantly, knowing his words had surely angered them. He shouted and tried to escape.

The four Fang family martial artists behind him reacted quickly, all activating their Origin Qi.

But before they could leave, a chilling profound intent suddenly spread.

Crack...

With a soft sound, the ground turned to frozen earth, even the space seemed sealed. The five were frozen in place, unable to move.

"Spare his life!" Yang Kai shouted urgently.

Ji Yao frowned but withdrew some power, freezing only the four Fang family martial artists into ice statues, leaving Fang Minghui standing alone, lips blue, teeth chattering, face miserable. "Spare me, sir! Since my Fang family joined Wenqing Sect, we've never wronged Ice Heart Valley. We only patrolled the outskirts, never harmed a single disciple."

Yang Kai patted his shoulder, knocking him out, then grabbed him. With a sweep of his sleeve, he shattered the four frozen Fang family disciples into powder.

"Master, why spare him?" Ji Yao's beautiful eyes were cold as she looked at the unconscious Fang Minghui, puzzled.

"He needs to repeat what he told us to someone else. Maybe we can get more information from him." Yang Kai explained calmly, then called, "Back to the Valley!"

"Yes!" Ji Yao and Shi Tianhe responded together.

With the Laws of Space surging, Yang Kai wrapped the two women, and in a flash, they vanished.

When they reappeared, the three were outside Ice Heart Valley's protective array. The array was massive and sturdy, forming a semi-transparent dome over the valley's core.

From afar, it looked like a giant bowl covering the ground. Everything inside the light screen was hazy and unclear.

Such a protective array was not easily breached. Wenqing Sect was strong, but couldn't break it by force, so they had to invite an array master.

But maintaining such an array consumes astronomical amounts of Source Crystals. Usually, only when a sect faces destruction is the array fully activated.

Having observed the area from the snowy mountain for a long time, Yang Kai was familiar with the patrol patterns. Bringing Ji Yao and Shi Tianhe over now, he didn't alarm anyone.

"Yao'er, open the array!" Yang Kai sent out his Divine Sense, staying alert while instructing Ji Yao.

"Yes!" Ji Yao nodded, quickly taking out a token from her Spatial Ring, infusing it with Emperor Yuan, and waving it at the light screen.

A mysterious light shot out, and the screen split open. Before it closed, Yang Kai and the others slipped inside.

Others couldn't open the array, but as Bing Yun's third disciple, Ji Yao naturally knew how.

"Who goes there!"

Before the three could steady themselves, a sharp shout rang out. A sword light flashed, wrapping a delicate figure, like a meteor falling from the heavens, dazzling and fierce.

Emperor Intent instantly filled the air, the icy aura dropping sharply, making Shi Tianhe shiver.

"Seventh Junior Sister? Is that you, Seventh Junior Sister?"

As the attack approached, Ji Yao didn't even try to defend. She stared at the figure in the sword light and called out in a trembling voice.

"Hm?" A surprised cry came from the sword light. The glow faded, revealing a petite figure.

The figure landed, holding a long sword, eyes fixed ahead.

"Seventh Junior Sister!" Ji Yao's body trembled as she called out.

"Th... Third Senior Sister!" The petite figure was instantly excited, almost unable to believe her eyes. After calling out, she rubbed them hard, realizing the person before her was indeed Ji Yao, missing for years.

The two sisters stared at each other, overwhelmed with emotion, then rushed into each other's arms.

"Third Senior Sister, you're finally back! Where have you been all these years?" The petite figure clung to Ji Yao, choking up.

Ji Yao's eyes were red, but she held back her emotions, gently stroking the girl's hair. "Sorry for making you all worry."

At this moment, some Ice Heart Valley female disciples who heard the commotion ran over. Suddenly, over a hundred surrounded them, all holding swords, full of killing intent, thinking it was an enemy. But seeing their Seventh Senior Aunt hugging another woman so intimately, they were stunned.

After hearing the two's conversation, the group of Ice Heart Valley female disciples suddenly understood.

"That's the Third Senior Aunt!"

"What? She's the long-missing Ji Yao, our Senior Aunt?"

"Yes, it's her. I've seen her portrait in the forbidden area. No mistake."

"Senior Aunt Ji Yao has returned..."

"Our sect has faced such peril, yet now that Senior Aunt Ji Yao has returned, it is truly a blessing for us all. The Ancestor will surely be overjoyed."

"Finally, some good news for the valley."

The group of women chirped like a flock of larks, noisy but full of joy.

"Junior Sister?" Another delicate call rang out.

In the crowd, a tall, clear-eyed woman stared at Shi Tianhe behind Yang Kai, surprised. "Junior Sister Tianhe?"

Shi Tianhe looked over, her body trembling, holding back her excitement, and said in a trembling voice, "Senior Sister Yu!"

She seemed nervous and timid, as if afraid to face Senior Sister Yu, like a child who'd done something wrong.

Hearing this, Yang Kai turned to look, recalling what Yan Dong had said earlier, and immediately understood that this tall woman was the Yu Dan he'd mentioned.

Yu Dan stepped forward, looked Shi Tianhe up and down, and smiled, "It's been years, Junior Sister. You've grown thin. Did you suffer outside?"

Shi Tianhe's eyes reddened, she shook her head, and tears streamed down.

"Don't cry. Master has always missed you. It's good you're back." Yu Dan said, gently wiping Shi Tianhe's tears.

But the tears kept falling like pearls on a string, no matter how she wiped them.

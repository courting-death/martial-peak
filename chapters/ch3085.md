## Chapter 3085 — I Didn't Do It on Purpose

Several more days passed. Suddenly, Lu Huaishuang and Sheng Yao both felt something stir in their hearts and looked up toward the mountain summit.

The young man who had been sitting cross-legged in meditation all this time had finally risen. He lazily stretched, yawned twice, and seemed to have just awakened from a great dream. With his hands clasped behind his back, he stood there gazing into the distance, his eyes deep.

Lu Huaishuang and Sheng Yao exchanged a glance, then both flickered and sped toward the mountain summit.

They reached the hundred-zhang peak in the blink of an eye.

After landing, both of them were startled.

For at that moment, the young man before them gave them the impression of absolute nothingness. Standing there, he seemed like a wisp of smoke, ethereal and without a trace. Had they not been able to see him with their own eyes, even their Divine Sense would have been unable to detect his presence.

He had sat here cross-legged in retreat for half a month. Could it have been solely to restrain his aura to the utmost? What was the point of doing that?

Not daring to be disrespectful, Lu Huaishuang performed a graceful salute and said, “This humble one is Lu Huaishuang. Many thanks, Senior, for your righteous intervention, allowing my Lu Family to escape disaster. This humble one is deeply grateful.”

The young man said, "It was a small effort. Consider it compensation for damaging your Warship."

So that Warship really had been damaged by him. Although she had suspected as much before, when she heard him admit it with his own mouth, Lu Huaishuang's heart still trembled. Respectfully, she said, "I would not dare."

She could be considered a local overlord, but facing this young man, who looked not much older than herself, she no longer had any confidence as a Void King Realm expert. Standing before him, she felt like a child still learning to speak, while the other party could topple her with a casual finger.

She wanted to ask how he had fallen unconscious despite possessing such terrifying strength, but she feared that saying too much would lead to mistakes, so she kept silent.

Sheng Yao had keener eyes than she did and was more quick-witted. With a fawning smile piled on his face, he took over the conversation. "May I ask Sir's honored name?"

Lu Huaishuang also looked toward the young man with bright beautiful eyes.

"Yang Kai. My name is Yang Kai!" the young man replied.

A strange light flashed through Sheng Yao's eyes. He had never heard this name before. He could be certain there was absolutely no such expert in the Star Domain. His heart leapt. Could this person be from that place?

If so, then everything made sense, including why this man had suddenly been struck by heavenly lightning before. It was because the Heavenly Way would not tolerate him!

Excitement surged through his heart, and he felt more and more that enduring humiliation had been the right choice. If he paid a price now, there might be returns in the future!

Lu Huaishuang had clearly also thought of this, and the unusual light in her beautiful eyes grew even brighter.

"Where is this?" Yang Kai asked.

"A... Dead Star?" Sheng Yao answered cautiously. If someone else had asked such an absurd question, he would have slapped them long ago. There were countless Dead Stars in the Star Domain, existing since time immemorial. Who could possibly give names to all those innumerable Dead Stars? The question truly made no sense.

"I am asking, where is this place!" Yang Kai stretched out a hand, pointed at the vast Starry Sky, and drew a circle. The movement was small, yet it seemed capable of gathering all the stars in the sky into his embrace.

Lu Huaishuang suppressed the excitement in her heart and said softly, "Wuji Star Domain!"

"Wuji Star Domain..." Yang Kai's eyes dimmed, and he sighed inwardly.

As expected, he had not returned to Hengluo Star Domain. This disappointed him somewhat.

Although the Void Passage within the Dragon Temple connected to the lower Star Domains, Yang Kai knew there were countless such Star Domains. The chance of returning directly to the Hengluo Star Domain through that Void Passage was less than one in a hundred. He had no idea how else to find his way back, so after entering the passage, he could only follow the trail of Wu Kuang’s aura.

Unfortunately, as he followed, he lost the trail.

Now, he did not know whether Wu Kuang was in this Star Domain, or whether he had already fled without a trace and hidden himself somewhere, waiting to make a comeback.

"Could it be that Sir... is not from the Star Domain?" Sheng Yao gathered his courage and asked. His heart pounded violently in his chest, and Lu Huaishuang also stared at Yang Kai without blinking.

"I am not!" There was nothing to hide about this, and with these two's eyesight, he believed they had already seen as much. This question was merely confirmation.

Hearing this, Sheng Yao and Lu Huaishuang both turned solemn. Sheng Yao was so excited that his lips trembled, and he asked in a shaking voice, "May I ask where Sir comes from..."

Yang Kai cast him a cold glance.

Sheng Yao immediately fell as silent as a cicada in winter. He had gotten carried away with excitement and actually forgotten the other party's power. A wave of lingering fear passed through him.

Lu Huaishuang said, “This place is rather crude, Senior. Why not return with this humble one so we may continue our conversation there?” Since he was not from the Wuji Star Domain, he was bound to have many questions. There would be plenty of opportunities to build a closer relationship with him, and if they could host him well, why would the Lu Family not prosper?

Yang Kai nodded. "Very well."

Lu Huaishuang pursed her lips in a smile and gestured in invitation.

Yang Kai stepped toward the foot of the mountain.

He had only just reached Lu Huaishuang when, for some unknown reason, his left foot tripped over his right. He staggered and pitched forward with a blank look on his face. By instinct, he waved his hands to steady himself, and by sheer coincidence, grabbed two full, soft mounds. An astonishing elasticity immediately came through his palms.

The air seemed to freeze in that instant. Time abruptly stopped flowing, and the scene was completely fixed in place.

Lu Huaishuang's face rapidly turned red, as if it were burning. The sensation of those large hands gripping her chest left her at a loss, and she nearly failed to stop herself from drawing her sword and stabbing out.

With her ability and reactions, she could actually have avoided Yang Kai the instant he fell toward her, but intimidated by his formidable strength, she did not dare react excessively and merely stood there. As a result... this was what happened.

Her body, which had never been taken liberties with before, had actually been grabbed in such a sensitive place, and by a man she had met for the first time at that. She did not even know the other party's background. Lu Huaishuang's eyes could not help turning red.

Sheng Yao's eyes went wide as he looked at Yang Kai with a strange expression, thinking that even if Sir was impatient for women, surely there was no need to go this far, right? He could fall just by tripping his left foot over his right? What sort of performance was this? Where was that heaven-shaking cultivation of yours? Where was that ability to kill two Void King Realm masters with a flick of your finger?

Yang Kai also looked astonished. Unhurriedly, he withdrew his hands and straightened up, then opened his palms and looked at them, his brow furrowing tightly.

When Sheng Yao and Lu Huaishuang saw this, it was hard for their thoughts not to wander. Could this Sir be savoring something?

“I didn’t do it on purpose.” Yang Kai looked up at Lu Huaishuang with complete sincerity, but the explanation sounded rather unconvincing.

Lu Huaishuang pressed her red lips together, lowered her head, and said, "It's fine."

How could it be fine? When a woman's reputation was involved, it was a serious matter. Yet her strength was inferior to his, so what could she do? Was she supposed to demand that he take responsibility? Even if he were willing, Lu Huaishuang herself would have been reluctant.

Yang Kai sighed. "Let's go."

Explaining was useless. Only he himself understood exactly what had happened just now.

He walked down the mountain step by step.

Sheng Yao was somewhat stunned and felt that this man's temperament was truly strange. His cultivation was clearly heaven-reaching; could he not simply fly? Yet he actually insisted on walking.

But he could not fathom the thoughts of such a master, so he could only follow step for step behind Yang Kai.

Lu Huaishuang stood in place for a while, calming herself, before finally gritting her teeth and following.

"First Chief, you do not need to follow me. Go back where you came from," Yang Kai suddenly said without turning his head.

Half a month had passed, and Yang Kai had long since lost any intention of killing Sheng Yao. In fact, he had never intended to kill him in the first place. A mere Second Stage Void King Realm cultivator simply was not worth his attention.

Whether Gale committed evil or not, that was Wuji Star Domain's business and had nothing at all to do with him.

As soon as these words came out, Sheng Yao immediately flushed red as if he had been humiliated and said, "Sir, a real man stands by his word. Since I said I would follow at Sir's side and serve like a dog or horse, I naturally will not leave."

Yang Kai suddenly stopped, turned his head, looked sideways at him, and smiled. "Are you a man?"

Sheng Yao's cheek could not help twitching, and he did not know how to answer...

"Suit yourself." Yang Kai stepped forward again, and Sheng Yao hurriedly followed.

Lu Huaishuang could not help but frown, secretly looking down on the Great Leader of Gale. Even after being treated like that, he did not seem to take it to heart. At the same time, she could not help but admire the thickness of his skin.

If it were her, she definitely could not do such a thing.

Yang Kai clearly did not walk quickly, nor was there any trace of a movement skill or footwork. It was as if an ordinary person were merely traveling, but both Lu Huaishuang and Sheng Yao had to circulate their Saint Yuan to keep up with him. In less than half an hour, they had actually returned to the Warship.

Two Warships were moored on the barren sand, like twin behemoths.

Lu Bing was standing outside. Seeing Lu Huaishuang appear, he hurried forward to meet her. He, too, was a man of outstanding eyesight; as soon as he saw Lu Huaishuang and Sheng Yao both following behind Yang Kai, he immediately bowed and said, "Lu Bing of the Lu Family greets Sir."

Yang Kai nodded slightly, passed by him, and walked straight in through the open hatch.

Lu Huaishuang swept past Lu Bing and quietly transmitted, "We'll talk later." She also followed Yang Kai into the Warship. She was the Lu Family's person in charge, so she had to see Yang Kai properly settled before she could do anything else.

Sheng Yao also wanted to enter, but Lu Bing led some people to stop him, their faces hostile.

Sheng Yao said, "I am Sir's attendant and must accompany him at his side. Move aside!"

His Saint Yuan shook and forced Lu Bing and the others back. The enormous gap in cultivation allowed him to take full advantage. His words actually carried a sense of pride rather than shame in this, leaving Lu Bing and the others astonished.

A Second Stage Void King Realm expert, Gale's First Chief, was actually willing to be someone else's attendant? What had happened to the world...

The Warship's interior was extremely spacious. Lu Huaishuang led the way, moving through the corridors inside the Warship. Before long, she brought Yang Kai to a side room, pushed open the door, and said, "Sir, please!"

As soon as he stepped into the room, a faint, delicate fragrance greeted him. It was not the scent of burning incense, but a natural aroma—the subtle fragrance unique to a woman.

The room's decorations could also be considered luxurious. The walls were adorned with fist-sized luminous pearls that gave off soft light, the floor was covered with a thick and soft red carpet, and on the left side stood a large bed hung with pink curtains.

On the other side of the room stood a large bathing pool, its waters strewn with fresh flowers. Though he had not stepped into it, he could still tell that the water had been prepared at just the right temperature.

On the other side was a dressing table. Although there were no vulgar things like rouge or powder on it, it really was a dressing table.

This was clearly a woman's boudoir!

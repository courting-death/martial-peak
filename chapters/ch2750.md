## Chapter 2750 — Return to the Zhang Family

If Xia Sheng hadn’t suddenly appeared today and explained the truth, he would have been led around by the nose by Yao Li, and would even have felt grateful to him. Although that top cultivation ground wasn’t much better than a Heaven-Grade one—just a little bit better—for someone hoping to break through to the Emperor Realm, sometimes that little bit was the line between failure and success.

This kind of thing absolutely could not be handled carelessly.

“Junior Nephew Yang, this old man has been so busy today that I actually forgot your Qingyang Shrine still has one cultivation ground left. My fault, my fault.” Yao Li wore a self-reproachful expression.

But how could there be such a mistake? There were only nine top cultivation grounds—no Emperor Realm cultivator would ever forget that.

Yang Kai replied coldly, “It’s good that Steward Yao remembered.”

Yao Li said, “But these nine cultivation grounds can only be used by disciples of the three Sects. Those who aren’t disciples of the three Sects can’t enter. Steward Xia knows this too. Junior Nephew Yang, if you want your cousin to enter for closed-door cultivation, I’m afraid it’s not possible.”

“I’ll use it myself!” Yang Kai said, turning to Xia Sheng, “For this closed-door cultivation, I want to bring my cousin in as protector. Is that alright?”

Xia Sheng grinned, “Of course that’s no problem.”

He turned to Yao Li, “Steward Yao, you’ll need to arrange this.”

He directly ordered Yao Li, and Yao Li didn’t show the slightest displeasure, nodding, “This old man will arrange it right away.”

With that, he turned and walked out.

Yang Kai watched his departing figure with a slightly heavy gaze, feeling a bit fortunate. Luckily, he ran into Xia Sheng here today, or he really would have been tricked by Yao Li.

“Are you curious why that old fox listens to me?” Xia Sheng asked with a smile, as if he’d guessed Yang Kai’s doubts.

Yang Kai nodded.

Xia Sheng explained, “Normally, the nine top cultivation grounds aren’t full. Most disciples of the three Sects cultivate in their own Sects, so these grounds often sit empty. They’ll privately sell or reserve them for their juniors. The one left in our Shrine was occupied this way. I usually don’t bother with it, but since you need it, Junior Brother, I won’t let them have it.”

“I understand.” Yang Kai nodded slightly.

“Let’s go, I’ll take you over.” Xia Sheng called out, leading the way.

Halfway there, the three saw two people approaching. One was Yao Li, and the other, following behind, had the cultivation of the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. He looked furious and helpless. As they passed, this cultivator glared at Yang Kai and Bian Yuqing, then quickly looked away.

He had spent a lot of effort to buy the right to cultivate in the top ground from Yao Li, and had just entered deep cultivation. But after less than ten days, Yao Li suddenly interrupted him, saying someone needed the ground and he couldn’t continue. How could he not be angry?

But he didn’t dare to lash out. Neither Yao Li nor Yang Kai were people he could provoke, so he could only swallow his frustration.

Fortunately, Yao Li promised that if there was another available ground, he’d let him use it again. Otherwise, the loss would be great.

The two groups passed each other. Yao Li still smiled, nodding to Xia Sheng and the others as if nothing had happened.

Soon, the three arrived at a stone door. Xia Sheng held a token, activated his Emperor Yuan, and waved it lightly. The stone door opened in response.

A bit of light suddenly spilled from the space inside, spreading like wildfire. In the blink of an eye, the whole stone chamber was filled with gentle radiance.

Xia Sheng said, “Normally, everyone’s cultivation time in the ground is one month. Even disciples of the three Sects aren’t exceptions. But Junior Brother, you can use it for two months.”

Yang Kai asked, “Won’t that make things difficult for you?”

Xia Sheng smiled, “Their hands aren’t clean either. Senior Brother isn’t afraid of them.” He patted Yang Kai’s shoulder, speaking earnestly, “Cultivate well, don’t mess around!”

Bian Yuqing’s expression suddenly became a little awkward.

She knew exactly what Xia Sheng meant by those words.

“Go in.” Xia Sheng smiled.

Yang Kai clasped his fists and entered the stone chamber with Bian Yuqing. The door closed, sealing off this world.

“Such dense Spiritual Qi!” Bian Yuqing walked to the center of the chamber, took a deep breath, and her eyes were full of shock.

In her life, she had never felt such dense Spiritual Qi. Not only was it dense, but also extremely pure. In just a short while, the whole chamber was filled with visible Spiritual Qi, like being surrounded by mist. Every inch of her flesh radiated pleasure.

No wonder disciples of the big Sects cultivate so quickly. The environment is different, so naturally small Sect disciples can’t compare.

Cultivating here for two months might raise the fifty percent chance of advancement by another ten percent.

“These are for you.” Yang Kai waved his hand, and a huge pile of Source Crystals appeared on the ground.

Bian Yuqing stared, exclaiming, “Upper-Level Source Crystals!”

And judging by the pile, there were at least one million.

One million Upper-Level Source Crystals—that’s ten million Middle-Level Source Crystals, and one billion Lower-Level Source Crystals! Yang Kai’s generosity stunned Bian Yuqing. She had spent all her wealth with Kou Wu to buy a Qing Jade Command Token, worth twenty million Middle-Level Source Crystals, which was nothing compared to Yang Kai’s gift.

“It’s too much!” Bian Yuqing said hurriedly. “Besides, didn’t you help me sell that Qing Jade Command Token for forty million Middle-Level Source Crystals? That’s enough, I don’t need these.”

“Take them.” Yang Kai waved his hand, “Upper-Level Source Crystals are more effective for cultivation. I can afford it.”

Bian Yuqing opened her mouth, not knowing what to say. She had received so much from Yang Kai this time, and secretly resolved to advance to Emperor no matter what. Otherwise, she’d let down the deceased Kou Wu and Yang Kai. If she could advance, she could help at his Sect. She had experience managing a Sect, so it would be useful. The Sect being small didn’t matter; it could grow step by step.

Silently sitting cross-legged, Bian Yuqing said, “I’m making you stay here with me for two months…”

Yang Kai smiled, “This place can’t hold me. You focus on your cultivation. I’ll come back in two months. I’ll go ahead now.”

As he spoke, Yang Kai pointed forward. The power of the Laws of Space surged, and the air in front of him rippled gently, spreading outward.

Yang Kai stepped forward, merging into the ripples and vanishing mysteriously. Only his words echoed in Bian Yuqing’s ears: “Don’t doubt yourself. Advancing to the Emperor Realm isn’t as hard as you think.”

Bian Yuqing was stunned for a moment, then softly exclaimed, “Space Technique!”

With that step, he vanished mysteriously. Only those skilled in Space Techniques could do this—it was a legendary ability. Bian Yuqing never expected to witness it herself.

After a while, she calmed her shock, steadied her breath, and sat down to meditate.

…

Outside Linghu City, a patch of space suddenly twisted and became unstable. Then, a figure emerged from the void.

A passing cultivator happened to see this, his eyes nearly popping out in fright. He thought he was seeing things, rubbed his eyes, and looked again—there was nothing there, as if the person he saw was just an illusion.

The cultivator became suspicious, quickly left, and didn’t dare linger.

Three hundred li outside Fenglin City, in front of a quiet manor, Yang Kai’s figure appeared suddenly.

In front of the manor, a girl of about sixteen or seventeen was sweeping leaves, her movements gentle, hair flying. From inside came shouts, as if someone was practicing martial arts.

Yang Kai swept the manor with his Divine Sense, taking in the situation. A weight lifted from his heart.

This was the Zhang Family, Ruoxi’s family. After so many years away, Fenglin City and Linghu City had changed so much, with countless experts appearing and daily bloodshed. Yang Kai had worried the Zhang Family might have met with misfortune.

If that happened, Ruoxi would surely be heartbroken.

But it seemed the Zhang Family, though lacking an expert to guard it, could still live peacefully. These years had been as calm as ever.

“Who…who are you looking for?” The girl sweeping suddenly saw Yang Kai, startled. Her big, dark eyes stared at him, hands nervously gripping the broom.

Yang Kai had taken Ruoxi from the Zhang Family nearly ten years ago. This girl was only fifteen or sixteen now. Even if she vaguely remembered the past, she couldn’t recall it clearly and naturally didn’t recognize Yang Kai.

Yang Kai immediately put on a harmless, friendly smile and said gently, “I’m here to see Madam Zhang.”

“Looking for Great-Grandmother?” the girl tilted her head.

“Yes, please let her know Yang Kai is here to see her.” Yang Kai smiled.

Although the Zhang Family was small and he could enter at will, it was Ruoxi’s family, so Yang Kai observed proper etiquette.

“You’re Lord Yang?” The girl’s eyes suddenly lit up, looking at Yang Kai with surprise and joy.

“You remember me?” Yang Kai was a bit surprised.

The girl shook her head, then nodded, not sure what she meant, but her expression was very happy. She threw down the broom, walked over, grabbed Yang Kai’s arm, and led him into the manor.

“Great-Grandmother! Great-Grandmother!”

The manor’s peace was broken. The girl’s clear voice echoed, and instantly, everyone in the Zhang Family was alarmed. Figures rushed from all directions, clothes fluttering in the wind.

## Chapter 3006 — Simply Inexplicable

Inside Wind and Cloud Pavilion, the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation broke apart just like that, revealing all hundred people who had formed it. The disciples watching from the periphery cried out in alarm, especially when they saw Yang Kai's savage appearance and enormous body, which horrified them to no end.

Just what kind of monster was this?

"You take the left, I'll take the right!" Yang Kai swept a cold gaze around and called out an instruction to Lin Yun'er.

Without saying a word, Lin Yun'er charged out with the iron pot still perched atop her head. Three Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples gathered together had yet to realize what was happening when she blasted them apart with a single punch.

Yang Kai also sprang into action. He simply reached out and grasped at the void, and Third Elder, who stood several dozen zhang away, was strangely caught in his palm. No one knew how he had done it.

A chill ran through Third Elder from head to toe. Before he could say a word, a tremendous force crushed him into pulp, sending him down the same path as Great Elder.

Yang Kai swept out his enormous hand again, directly sending another Elder standing near Third Elder flying. The man coughed up blood incessantly in midair and was already dead by the time he struck the ground.

Under his Dragon Transformation, Yang Kai's Dragon Clan bloodline had been stimulated, granting him boundless strength. How could a handful of mere First-Stage Emperor Realm Masters possibly be his opponents?

Like chopping melons and vegetables, Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er sent people tumbling wherever they passed. Wind and Cloud Pavilion's disciples and Elders suffered disastrous casualties, and no one could withstand even half a move from them.

In just three breaths, more than half of the hundred people had died.

Only then did the survivors awaken as if from a dream and flee in every direction.

Faced with two monstrous opponents like these, and with their own Secret Treasures taken from them, they could not summon even the courage to fight. Naturally, preserving their lives came first.

Chi, chi, chi...

Moon Blades shot out in every direction.

Every last Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciple who had participated in forming the Array screamed and collapsed. As the Moon Blades swept past, severed limbs flew through the air.

Lin Yun'er made a full circuit and killed only a dozen or so people. When she turned to look, no one was left.

Blood rained down from the sky.

The Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples watching from afar stood silent as cicadas in winter, their faces ashen.

Just what kind of enemy had their Sect provoked? The entire elite force of the Sect had actually been wiped out just like that. From Great Elder down to their Senior Brothers and Senior Sisters in the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, every last one of them had died.

At the Emperor Realm, only the Pavilion Master remained.

Wind and Cloud Pavilion had been passed down for ten thousand years, yet never had it suffered such a devastating blow. After this battle, even if it could drive away the enemy, it would be difficult for Wind and Cloud Pavilion to recover its vitality.

More importantly, could these two monsters be driven away so easily? At this rate, the entire Sect was clearly going to be annihilated.

Just then, Hua Xing's grief-stricken roar resounded through the clouds, "Summon the Sacred Beast!"

As soon as he said this, the expressions of countless Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples changed drastically. They looked even more terrified than when they had watched Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er slaughter their fellow disciples.

"What did the Pavilion Master say?"

"He actually wants to release the Sacred Beast?"

"Run! What are you still standing there for?"

"If we don't run now, we're dead!"

The Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples watching from afar reacted as though facing a great enemy and fled in every direction, as if the Sacred Beast were some god of plague.

Some disciples who had joined only recently looked bewildered. Although they knew Wind and Cloud Pavilion had a mountain-guarding Sacred Beast, no one had ever seen it, nor did they know what the damned thing actually was. They only knew that it was extremely formidable, no weaker than Pavilion Master Hua Xing himself.

When they saw the Senior Brothers and Senior Sisters who had joined before them fleeing in every direction, they also panicked.

Whatever the Sacred Beast was, it was certainly no benevolent creature.

Right after Hua Xing's roar, a horrifying malevolent aura suddenly erupted from somewhere deep within Wind and Cloud Pavilion. It was as though a sleeping dragon had awakened, causing the world itself to tremble.

"Hm?" Yang Kai turned toward the source of the aura. His blood faintly began to burn, as though drawn by some power, and an eager fighting spirit rose within him for no apparent reason.

Yun'er also flew over to his side, the pitch-black iron pot still perched atop her head. She gazed in that direction with her beautiful eyes and sniffed lightly, her eyes immediately brightening.

Roar...

Another furious roar rang out. Immediately afterward, an enormous figure came racing through the air from that direction. It appeared to be some kind of fierce beast.

The fierce beast strode through the air, flames blazing all over its body and its fiery might towering to the Heavens.

Was this Wind and Cloud Pavilion's mountain-guarding Sacred Beast? Yang Kai raised his brow, his fighting spirit growing even stronger. There was no other reason; he could actually sense a trace of a Divine Spirit's aura from this Sacred Beast.

Wind and Cloud Pavilion's mountain-guarding Sacred Beast was actually a Divine Spirit!

This was truly difficult to believe.

Soon, however, a scene that astonished Yang Kai unfolded. The Sacred Beast did not fly straight toward them, but instead charged toward some of the fleeing Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples. Moving as swiftly as lightning, it plunged directly into the crowd.

In the next instant, screams rang out one after another. Clearly, the Sacred Beast was biting them to death.

Hua Xing's heart bled.

Wind and Cloud Pavilion had raised a mountain-guarding Sacred Beast for several thousand years. However, this Sacred Beast was a vicious beast captured by Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Tenth-Generation Ancestor. While that ancestor was alive, the Sacred Beast had been fairly obedient, but after his death, its vicious nature was exposed.

Although it did not leave Wind and Cloud Pavilion, it obeyed no one. Only each generation's Pavilion Master could communicate with it to some extent.

Every time they invited it to emerge, they had to pay a great price. If they had time to prepare, they would ready a vast amount of fresh meat and blood in advance, barely managing to persuade it to act once and repel a powerful enemy.

This time, however, they had made no preparations, and the price was the tragic deaths of their disciples.

Even a hundred years ago, when Wind and Cloud Pavilion had faced a crisis, the previous Pavilion Master had not summoned the Sacred Beast for precisely this reason. This time, however, disaster loomed before them, and Hua Xing could no longer concern himself with so much.

Screams rang out without end as one Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciple after another was pounced upon by the Sacred Beast and swallowed whole.

"What the hell is that thing?" Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and peered in that direction, but unfortunately, the distance was too great for him to see it clearly.

All he knew was that this so-called Sacred Beast had yet to do anything to them and had instead slaughtered many of their enemies.

"It looks so delicious!" Lin Yun'er's nose twitched even more vigorously.

Yang Kai broke out in a cold sweat. Why did this little girl think of nothing but eating?

After the time it took to drink a cup of tea, perhaps because the Sacred Beast had finally eaten its fill, it soared into the air and flew toward them. Its four feet trod upon flames, and its two beastly pupils burned like raging fire.

Only after it drew closer did Yang Kai finally see what the Sacred Beast looked like.

It had a dragon's head, a deer's antlers, a lion's eyes, a tiger's back, a bear's waist, a snake's scales, a horse's hooves, and an ox's tail. Its imposing might was truly ferocious, and it gazed down upon all before it.

"A Fire Qilin?" Yang Kai's eyelid twitched.

No wonder it carried the aura of a Divine Spirit. So it was a Fire Qilin. There were many kinds of Qilin, but every kind was an Ancient Divine Spirit.

His Dragon Clan bloodline was also the power of a Divine Spirit, and one of the finest among Divine Spirits at that, so it was only natural that the other party's aura had stirred him.

"It's not a Fire Qilin," Lin Yun'er said. She seemed quite knowledgeable about Divine Spirits, and her eyes shone as she continued, "It only has some Qilin bloodline. It isn't pure."

Yang Kai turned to look at her, and his face darkened. “Girl… you’re drooling.”

Lin Yun'er raised a hand and wiped the corner of her mouth, then said excitedly, "Leave this one to me!"

As soon as she finished speaking, she charged straight ahead.

"Don't be careless!" Yang Kai hurriedly warned her.

Although Lin Yun'er had performed remarkably well, Yang Kai could not help worrying as she faced such a vicious beast with a Divine Spirit bloodline, uncertain whether she was its match. However, since Lin Yun'er was being so enthusiastic, Yang Kai could hardly dampen her spirits. He could only resolve to stand ready to assist her at any moment.

The girl and beast rapidly closed in on each other.

The Fire Qilin descendant had clearly developed sentience. Seeing Lin Yun'er charge over so brazenly, it roared in fury, and the flames covering its body blazed even more fiercely.

Lin Yun'er paid it no heed and continued charging straight toward it.

The Fire Qilin opened its mouth, and an enormous ball of fire blasted toward her with terrifying might.

Lin Yun'er neither dodged nor evaded. She simply took the iron pot from atop her head and thrust it forward.

The iron pot was clearly not large, only the size of a washbasin and countless times smaller than the fireball. Yet with that simple thrust, the fireball vanished without a trace.

Yang Kai, who had just been about to act, steadied himself again. His burning gaze fixed on the pitch-black iron pot, and only one question remained in his mind.

What the hell was that thing?

By then, Lin Yun'er had already reached the Fire Qilin. Tapping her feet against the air, she shot above its head and slammed the iron pot down over it.

The Fire Qilin raised a claw and swiped at the iron pot. Wherever its claw passed, the surrounding space was compressed into irregular patterns.

Lin Yun'er forcibly twisted her body and barely avoided the blow, but her lower abdomen was still raked by the claw, and blood immediately poured from the wound.

She paid it no heed and directly clapped the iron pot over the Fire Qilin's forehead.

In the next instant, a horrifying scene unfolded.

The next moment, an astonishing scene unfolded. The Fire Qilin, a ferocious beast with Divine Spirit bloodline comparable to a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, let out a mournful cry as its massive body swayed unsteadily. The little iron pot atop its head seemed as though it had fused with its body, emanating an incomparably terrifying suction force.

The enormous body of the vicious beast was pulled by some inexplicable force, twisting and changing shape. Soon, it was sucked into the iron pot like a stream of water and disappeared.

Yang Kai was dumbfounded.

Li Jiao was dumbfounded.

Hua Xing was also dumbfounded!

Lin Yun'er also seemed stunned for a moment. She scratched her head foolishly, her face filled with confusion.

She knew perfectly well how much power her treasure could display. Although she was confident she could use it to capture the Fire Qilin, it could not possibly have been this easy. She had even been preparing herself for a great battle against it.

Unexpectedly, she had succeeded with her very first move.

The little girl was simple-minded and did not think too deeply about it. Excitedly catching the iron pot, she produced a lid from who knew where and closed it with a crisp snap. Smiling from ear to ear, she looked it over from side to side, growing more delighted the longer she stared.

Li Jiao's expression fluctuated. Even though he had weathered countless great storms, he could not help feeling his hair stand on end at this moment.

While Hua Xing was summoning Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Sacred Beast, Li Jiao had spared a moment to pay attention and sensed how powerful it was. He believed that even if he personally fought it, he would not necessarily be assured of victory. Yet such a terrifying existence had actually been captured by a pitch-black iron pot.

It had been captured in a single exchange.

This was simply inexplicable.

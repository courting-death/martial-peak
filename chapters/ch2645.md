## Chapter 2645 — Big Bird, Big Bird

The yao commander suddenly acted as if he had encountered a venomous snake or scorpion, utterly terrified, leaving the many yao soldiers and generals around him completely bewildered, not knowing what was going on.

“I did not know Your Excellency had arrived. This lowly yao was blind and offended you. Please, Your Excellency, forgive me!” the yao commander stammered, trembling, sweat the size of beans pouring down his forehead, his mind a complete blank.

“You’re not that stupid after all!” Yang Kai snorted, leading Old Ban and carrying little Ling’er, flying straight past the group of yao.

Seeing that Yang Kai had no intention of making things difficult for him, the yao commander finally let out a heavy sigh of relief.

After Yang Kai’s figure disappeared from sight, one of the Yao Generals curiously asked, “Sir, who was that person? Why were you so respectful to him?”

None of the yao could figure it out. The Wild Ancient Land was a paradise for the primordial remnants, and here, the yao were the most powerful force. Why would the yao commander be so afraid of that human?

The yao commander’s expression kept shifting, but he didn’t answer. Instead, he said in a deep voice, “You must never provoke that man. All of you remember this: if you see him again, get as far away as you can. If you anger him, not even the Yao King can save you.”

“What…” All the yao were shocked, growing even more curious about Yang Kai’s identity. What kind of ability did he have, that even the Yao King would be helpless against him?

“No, no, I have to report this to the Yao King at once.” The yao commander muttered for a moment, then his figure blurred into a cloud of black mist, speeding off in one direction.

Yang Kai’s sudden appearance here was a major event for the entire Wild Ancient Land. Since he had encountered him, he had to report it. If the Yao King found out and blamed him, he’d be in serious trouble.

In the jungle, Yang Kai continued flying forward.

Little Ling’er was ignorant and didn’t understand what had just happened, but Old Ban was utterly awed by Yang Kai.

A powerful yao commander, leading so many yao soldiers and generals, had shown such respect to Yang Kai. What was going on? Old Ban wanted to ask, but felt his relationship with Yang Kai wasn’t close enough to pry, so he could only keep his questions to himself.

After flying forward for a while, Yang Kai suddenly frowned, stopped, and looked back.

“What’s wrong?” Old Ban asked curiously.

“A formidable fellow is coming. I don’t know what he wants,” Yang Kai said, squinting toward the rear.

“A formidable fellow…” Old Ban’s heart skipped a beat. Even the yao commander had avoided Yang Kai, so the one he called formidable must be at the Yao King’s level, right?

Old Ban’s head spun, and he felt unsteady on his feet.

In just a couple of exchanges, a streak of azure light shot over from behind and landed not far away. As the light faded, a vigorous figure appeared.

It was a middle-aged man in a fitted azure outfit, with a hooked nose. His eyes were as sharp as blades, and his aura was as deep as the sea, making it hard to breathe.

It really was a Yao King!

Old Ban’s face turned pale. He had never seen a Yao King before, but he could tell that this man was definitely a Yao King of the Wild Ancient Land.

Rumor had it that only experts on par with the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm could become Yao Kings of the Ancient Land. In other words, this middle-aged man was a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert!

Someone like that could probably wipe out him and little Ling’er a hundred times over with a single breath.

“So it really is Young Master Yang!”

The middle-aged man showed no hostility upon seeing Yang Kai. Instead, he was overjoyed, as if reuniting with an old friend. He cupped his fists and said, “Yao King Yingfei under Lord Cang Gou greets Young Master Yang!”

“So it’s Yao King Yingfei!” Yang Kai nodded slightly, his expression indifferent. This Yao King looked familiar—he must have seen him near the Blood Gate last time. Now that he introduced himself, Yang Kai finally knew his name was Yingfei.

“Cang Gou…” Old Ban’s eyes widened, his face blank, nearly petrified.

Although most people didn’t know the power structure of the Wild Ancient Land, even Old Ban had heard of Cang Gou’s name.

That was an Ancient Divine Spirit!

So, there really were Divine Spirits in the Wild Ancient Land, and this Yao King before them was a subordinate of Cang Gou, the Divine Spirit.

“I just heard that my subordinates seemed to have seen Young Master Yang. I thought they were mistaken, but it really is you,” Yingfei said with a familiar smile.

Yang Kai said, “I wonder why the Yao King chased after me. Is there something you need?”

Yingfei hurriedly replied, “I wouldn’t dare presume to instruct you. I just wanted to ask where you’re headed, and if you need any help?”

Yang Kai shook his head. “No need. I’m just going to find Lady Luan Feng to discuss something.”

“Looking for Lady Luan Feng!” Yingfei smiled. “It’s quite a distance from here to Lady Luan Feng’s Fengluo Palace. Although most of the Yao Kings and commanders in the Ancient Land know your face and wouldn’t dare offend you, there are still many yao who might not recognize you. If you don’t mind, let me escort you. I can’t boast about much else, but when it comes to speed, I’m second to none.”

Ever since Yang Kai had caused a huge commotion in the Wild Ancient Land last time, the three Saint Venerables had instructed their Yao Kings and Saint Envoys that if they ever saw Yang Kai again, they must not be disrespectful.

The Thirty-Two Yao Kings and Eight Saint Envoys had all participated in that battle, so they knew Yang Kai’s identity and his connection to the descendant of Heaven’s Punisher. Even without the Saint Venerables’ instructions, they knew what to do.

After returning to their territories, they gathered their commanders and generals, showed them Yang Kai’s image, and gave strict orders.

That’s why the yao commander earlier had recognized Yang Kai and was so terrified. He didn’t know who Yang Kai was, but he knew this human was someone the Yao King had specifically warned about, so he didn’t dare offend him.

Yang Kai looked deeply at Yingfei, a meaningful smile appearing at the corner of his mouth.

Of course, Yang Kai knew exactly what Yingfei was after, seeing how eager he was. After all, in this world, only Yang Kai had a close relationship with Zhang Ruoxi, the descendant of Heaven’s Punisher and the one who now controlled the Blood Gate—a forbidden land said to contain countless Divine Spirit origins.

Zhang Ruoxi had already entered the Blood Gate and couldn’t be found, but Yang Kai was right here.

If Yingfei could get on good terms with Yang Kai, maybe he could get a word in for him. Perhaps one day, Yingfei could obtain a suitable Divine Spirit origin from the descendant of Heaven’s Punisher and awaken his ancestral bloodline, becoming a Divine Spirit himself.

“Very well, I’ll trouble Yao King Yingfei then,” Yang Kai nodded.

Yingfei was overjoyed. “Not at all, not at all! It’s my honor to serve Young Master Yang.”

As soon as he finished speaking, his body spun, azure light flared, and with a piercing cry, a giant eagle over ten zhang long appeared where he had stood. The giant eagle gently flapped its wings, stirring up a raging wind.

“Big bird, big bird!” Little Ling’er, riding on Yang Kai’s neck, cheered and clapped when she saw Yingfei’s true form, as if she’d discovered something new and wonderful.

Yang Kai chuckled. “If Ling’er likes it, then let’s ride the big bird.”

“Yes, yes!” Ling’er’s eyes sparkled with joy.

Yang Kai shifted his body and landed on the bird’s back.

Yingfei’s true form was enormous, his back broad and flat. Even with twenty or thirty people sitting on it, it wouldn’t feel crowded. The feathers around his neck were smooth and soft, making it as comfortable as sitting on a plush carpet.

“Grandpa, come up! This big bird is so comfy, it’s even better than our bed at home!” Little Ling’er climbed down from Yang Kai’s neck, stood on the bird’s back, and called to Old Ban.

Old Ban broke out in a cold sweat and scolded, “Don’t talk nonsense.”

The little girl was ignorant, but he knew how powerful the Yao King was. With a single breath, he could be utterly destroyed.

On a normal day, he wouldn’t even get to see such a being, let alone sit on its back.

Old Ban was anxious and hesitant, not knowing what to do, so he looked to Yang Kai for help.

Yang Kai smiled. “The Yao King is being gracious, Old Ban. Come up with us.”

The giant eagle spoke in human tongue, “Don’t worry, old man. I’ll protect you.”

Hearing this, Old Ban nervously cupped his fists. “Then… please excuse this old man.”

As he spoke, he carefully climbed onto the bird’s back, feeling a deep sense of unreality.

He had muddled through life, and now he actually had the fortune to sit on a Yao King’s back…

“Everyone, hold on tight,” Yingfei reminded them. With a flap of his wings, his massive body turned into a streak of azure light, speeding deep into the Ancient Land. The scenery flashed by, making little Ling’er shout with excitement.

Though the speed was great, a layer of yao qi formed a protective barrier around them, blocking all the headwinds so that Yang Kai and the others weren’t disturbed at all.

Perhaps to show off, Yingfei not only flew fast but also incredibly steady. The whole way, it felt as if they were standing on solid ground, with no tilting or bumpiness at all.

In less than an hour, Luan Feng’s Fengluo Palace appeared in their sights.

Yingfei landed outside Fengluo Palace with the group. After Yang Kai and the others got off, he turned back into human form, cupped his fists to Yang Kai, and said, “Young Master Yang, I have fulfilled my duty. I’ll take my leave now.”

He was a Yao King under Lord Cang Gou, and this was outside Fengluo Palace, so it wasn’t appropriate for him to stay long.

Moreover, from start to finish, he hadn’t brought up anything else with Yang Kai. It seemed he had come just to escort him.

That was his cleverness. He believed Yang Kai understood why he was trying to get close. By not mentioning it, maybe he could win a bit of Yang Kai’s favor. If he brought it up, it would seem too calculating.

Besides, that kind of thing couldn’t be rushed, and it wasn’t something Yang Kai could just agree to. Now that the Blood Gate had vanished without a trace, there was no movement at all. Even if he wanted to obtain his ancestor’s Divine Spirit origin, he’d have to wait for the Blood Gate to open again.

Yang Kai smiled and cupped his fists. “Thank you, Yao King.”

“You’re too polite, Young Master Yang!” Yingfei returned the gesture, then turned and left the way he had come.

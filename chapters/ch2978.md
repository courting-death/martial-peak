## Chapter 2978 — Zhu Lie's Persuasion

It took Yang Kai a full two hours to completely expel the cold Qi that had invaded his meridians and flesh. When he opened his eyes, he saw Zhu Lie still standing motionless nearby. Although Zhu Qing was already fine, she was helping the little black dog expel the cold, and there was no telling how long it would take.

Sensing Yang Kai's gaze, Zhu Qing looked up and said, "She's in no serious danger. It will just take some time."

Yang Kai nodded lightly, unsurprised.

Ever since Gong Yue activated the little black dog's Guixu bloodline, her vitality had become astonishingly tenacious. The group had been able to travel so far up Yin Wind Mountain almost entirely because of her. At that height, even Yang Kai and Zhu Qing could not have done any better.

That was why Yang Kai had immediately grabbed Zhu Qing and fled the moment he realized the little black dog could no longer hold on.

Strictly speaking, the little black dog was not very powerful, as she had only ever given off the aura of a Fifth- or Sixth-Tier Yao Beast. Yao Beasts of this rank could be found everywhere in the Star Realm and were nothing rare at all.

Yet this very Fifth- or Sixth-Tier Yao Beast had single-handedly contended with ten Emperors, swallowed a Demon King alive, and devoured the Yin Wind that even Yang Kai and Zhu Qing found extremely troublesome, allowing them to advance a great distance up Yin Wind Mountain.

Distinctions such as rank and strength seemed completely meaningless to the little black dog. Her strength fluctuated wildly; when weak, she was harmless to man and beast, but when strong, even gods and ghosts would retreat before her.

The little black dog's bizarre performance earlier made Yang Kai feel even more strongly that if they wanted to cross Yin Wind Mountain and leave the Revolving World, they might have to place their hopes in her. Yang Kai had personally experienced the terror of the frigid Demonic Qi and did not believe he could reach the summit of Yin Wind Mountain with his own abilities.

Zhu Qing and Zhu Lie probably could not do it either. That cold, which extinguished all vitality, was not something any of them could withstand.

If leaving the Revolving World were truly so easy, someone from Human Sovereign City would likely have done it long ago. It would not have remained impossible for anyone to leave this world for more than a hundred thousand years.

Thus, their only option now was to start with the little black dog.

Yang Kai turned to glance in the direction of Demon Fury City. After frowning in thought for a moment, he said, "Qing'er, I'll be back soon."

Without waiting for Zhu Qing to respond, he leapt up and flew in that direction.

Before he had travelled far, he suddenly sensed someone following behind him. Turning his head, he saw Zhu Lie rapidly catching up with a solemn expression.

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and slowed down. Once Zhu Lie caught up, he snorted, "You actually wanted to kill me? You've got some nerve."

Yang Kai had not just realized this now. Although Zhu Lie’s expression was grave at the moment, there was no killing intent in it. Rather, it was while Yang Kai had been meditating to heal his injuries earlier that he had suddenly sensed a fleeting trace of killing intent from Zhu Lie.

That murderous intent had been extremely well concealed, and aside from Zhu Lie, only Yang Kai, Zhu Qing, and Li Jiao had been present at the time.

Zhu Qing was his biological Elder Sister, so naturally, Zhu Lie could not have directed any murderous intent toward her. If Li Jiao had been his target, Zhu Lie would likely have attacked already without the slightest scruple.

What did it matter if a dignified member of the Dragon Clan killed someone? If he wanted to kill, he would simply kill. Who could do anything to him?

Therefore, there had been only one person capable of arousing Zhu Lie's murderous intent. Yang Kai understood this perfectly well. It had been inconvenient to expose him in front of Zhu Qing, but now there was naturally no need to keep it hidden.

Only an enemy concealed in the shadows was truly frightening. Once schemes and intrigues were laid out in the open, most of them could not withstand a single blow.

"Since you noticed, there's no point hiding it." Zhu Lie readily admitted it. After all, once one's cultivation reached their level, certain things could not be concealed after they were revealed. With a dark expression, he said, "That's right, I wanted to kill you. If it had been possible, I would have done so already."

Yang Kai's expression turned icy. Without slowing, he flew side by side with Zhu Lie, though a subtle distance remained between them. He said in a chilling voice, "You had better give me a reasonable explanation. Otherwise, even if you're Zhu Qing's younger brother, I won't spare you."

If Zhu Lie had wanted to kill him in the past, it would not have mattered much, as the two had not known each other well. Things were different now. With Zhu Qing connecting them, they had formed a certain relationship whether either of them acknowledged it or not. Yang Kai could not tolerate Zhu Lie suddenly acting this way for no apparent reason.

It did not matter if he and Zhu Lie engaged in minor squabbles, but if they truly fought to the death, Zhu Qing would inevitably be caught in the most difficult position. Yang Kai did not want to see that happen, so he had to get to the bottom of this.

Gritting his teeth, Zhu Lie said, "It's precisely because I'm her younger brother that I wanted to kill you."

"What kind of bullshit logic is that!?" Yang Kai glared at him.

"You don't understand. I followed you only to tell you that if we can leave this world, you'd better stay away from Zhu Qing and never reveal that there was ever anything between you. Of course... if we can't leave this place, then forget it. Pretend I said nothing."

His expression was solemn and serious, as though he would immediately attack if Yang Kai refused.

Yang Kai was so angry that he laughed. "Foolish boy, it's not your place to say such things. If Zhu Qing truly has something on her mind, she'll naturally tell me herself."

Zhu Lie roared, "It's precisely because Zhu Qing won't say it that I have to say it for her!"

Yang Kai looked him up and down, narrowed his eyes, and asked, "In what capacity are you saying this? As a member of Dragon Isle's Dragon Clan, or as Zhu Qing's younger brother?"

Zhu Lie replied in a deep voice, "Does it make a difference?"

"It does!" Yang Kai grinned ferociously and cracked his knuckles. "If it's the former, I'll beat you up. If it's the latter, I'll still beat you up."

Zhu Lie said furiously, "Are you toying with me?"

Yang Kai stared at him coldly. "There are different kinds of beatings. Beating you to death or crippling you is one kind, while giving you a light punishment as a warning is another. Which would you prefer?"

Zhu Lie seriously shook his head. "I won't engage in a war of words with you. I ask only one thing of you: if we can leave this world, part ways with Zhu Qing. It will be good for both you and her."

For a member of the Dragon Clan, going so far as to ask was already the limit. How could the proud leader of all living creatures lower himself to plead with another? If Zhu Lie had been any match for Yang Kai, he would have caught him off guard and killed him long ago, putting an end to everything. Why else would he have wasted so much time talking?

Yang Kai sighed leisurely. "Although I can sense how much you care about Zhu Qing, your concern is misplaced. I'm sorry, but I won't agree to what you've asked, nor will I even consider it. Zhu Qing is my woman. She is today, she will be tomorrow, and she always will be. That will only change on the day I die. Understand?"

Zhu Lie said expressionlessly, "Zhu Qing would certainly be very happy if she heard these sweet words of yours."

Yang Kai shrugged. "I simply say what I mean."

"Unfortunately, acting this way will get Zhu Qing killed, and it will get you killed as well."

Riding the wind through the void, Yang Kai smiled faintly. "Are you trying to tell me that I'm a human while Zhu Qing belongs to the Dragon Clan, so Dragon Isle will never allow her to be with me? And that if word of this gets out, it will only bring enormous trouble?"

"You know?" Zhu Lie frowned slightly.

"I call you foolish, but you refuse to admit it." Yang Kai sneered. "I can infer a thing or two about Dragon Isle's nature just from your foolish behavior. It would be strange if Dragon Isle allowed Zhu Qing to be with me. If news that Zhu Qing and I are together truly reaches Dragon Isle, they'll probably send even more members of the Dragon Clan to crusade against me at once, won't they? They may even forcibly take Zhu Qing back to Dragon Isle for punishment and confine her for the rest of her life."

"Did Zhu Qing tell you all this?" Zhu Lie was completely dumbfounded, because Yang Kai's prediction did not differ in the slightest from his own.

A dignified Ninth-Tier Red Dragon had willingly debased herself by giving her body to an inferior human. Once such news reached Dragon Isle, it would inevitably cause a tremendous uproar. Dragon Isle would certainly send Masters to punish Yang Kai and forcibly bring Zhu Qing back. As for whether she would be confined for the rest of her life, that would depend on her conduct afterward.

Yang Kai curled his lips and tapped his temple. “It’s not exactly hard to figure out. Anyone with half a brain could guess it. Your Dragon Isle has existed for countless years, and the older a legacy becomes, the more hidebound and inflexible it grows. With such a lofty reputation and such an inflated opinion of yourselves, how could you possibly accept one of your clansmen giving herself to a human? To your Dragon Clan, that’s an insult worse than someone shitting and pissing on your heads.”

"That's exactly the point." Zhu Lie nodded heavily. "Since you already know, why do you remain so stubborn?"

"You know you'll die one day, so why are you still alive?" Yang Kai looked at him disdainfully.

Zhu Lie froze for a moment. He felt that these words seemed to make some sense, yet at the same time, something about them did not seem quite right.

Yang Kai snorted. "I don't have many great virtues, but I am fiercely protective of my own. Zhu Qing is already my woman. If Dragon Isle truly tries to bully her, don't blame me for showing no consideration to her maternal family. So don't say anything like this again. You and I are both close to her, and if we come into conflict, it will only make things difficult for her. I doubt you want to see that either, do you?"

Zhu Lie's brow furrowed deeply. He nodded lightly, then slowly shook his head, leaving it unclear whether he had agreed.

Yang Kai continued, "That said, only you and Li Jiao know about Zhu Qing and me right now. If you don't tell Dragon Isle, how would they ever find out?"

Zhu Lie said solemnly, "This matter is of immense importance. How could I know about it and fail to report it?"

"People who run to tattle are the most annoying!" Yang Kai coldly snorted.

"Say whatever you like. This is a matter of principle. What happened between you and Zhu Qing has already violated Dragon Isle's rules. If I know and fail to report it, the consequences will only become more severe."

"You truly can't conceal it?" Yang Kai glanced sideways at him.

Zhu Lie firmly shook his head.

"I'll remember this," Yang Kai coldly snorted.

"You don't understand." Zhu Lie sighed. "Although Dragon Isle has a tremendous reputation and a long inheritance, the number of members in my Dragon Clan has always been exceedingly small in every era. Giant Dragons possessing a pure Dragon Clan bloodline are rarer still. To ensure the continuation of the Dragon Clan's bloodline, Dragon Isle must have its own rules, and anyone who violates those rules must pay an extremely heavy price."

Yang Kai smiled faintly without comment, then leisurely said, "Dragon Isle may have existed since ancient times, but it will not endure forever. The waves wash away all heroes, and even Dragon Isle is no exception."

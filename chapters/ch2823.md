## Chapter 2823 — Small Xuan Realm Perfected

Inside the palace, Shaman Che held a small jar of sticky healing ointment in his hands. After a long silence, he finally looked up and asked, "Did you all refine these?"

In front of him, the two elders—one with mottled hair and the other with white hair—exchanged glances. The former replied, "Strictly speaking, it was under the guidance of Lord Niu that we managed to refine them."

Shaman Che asked, "Have you learned it?"

Both elders nodded. The white-haired elder, somewhat embarrassed, said, "He... did not hold anything back. He was very willing to teach us how to prepare these healing medicines, so we learned quickly."

"Very good." Shaman Che showed a faint smile and continued, "What do you think of this Shaman Niu?"

The flower-haired elder said, "He is broad-minded!"

The white-haired elder said, "He is open-hearted!"

Che said, "I mean his abilities."

The two elders fell silent for a moment, then spoke in unison, "No one can compare."

Che's eyes suddenly flashed with brilliance, like a hungry beast spotting its prey. He said calmly, "Such an outstanding apothecary—it's a real pity if the Southern Barbarian Tribe doesn't make good use of him." After a pause, he instructed the person beside him, "Yu, treat him well. Do everything you can to meet his demands, but... do not let him leave the palace."

Yu's heart trembled as she understood Che's intent. Her expression was complicated as she replied, "Yes!"

The white-haired and flower-haired elders exchanged glances and sighed softly. They had wanted to say more, but when they looked up, Che had already vanished.

Elsewhere in the royal palace, in a luxurious room by ancient world standards, Yang Kai sat cross-legged. Divine Sense surged as he savored the regained power, his heart filled with joy and excitement.

Just as the village chief had said, after reaching the Shaman Master realm, he truly possessed his own Divine Sense. Once he had Divine Sense, all the restrictions that had hindered Yang Kai's progress shattered instantly.

The Spatial Ring could be opened again, and many Secret Treasures could be summoned. For a moment, Yang Kai felt that the world was vast and he could venture anywhere.

The first thing he did was check on the Small Xuan Realm.

Back when the Xuan Realm Pearl was in the Fourth Layer of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, it had devoured countless fragments of broken laws. Yang Kai had even deliberately gathered the power of the five elements, believing that when all five elements were present, a true world would be born. Yet, until now, he had never had the chance to examine the changes within the Xuan Realm Pearl—at least, not until this very moment.

His Spirit Body entered the Small Xuan Realm, and Yang Kai immediately sensed that this world was very different from before. Although there were no obvious changes on the surface, the Xuan Realm Pearl had followed him for years, and even the slightest change could not escape his eyes.

If the Small Xuan Realm was an incomplete world before, now it had been fully repaired.

Yang Kai could clearly feel that this world was filled with wondrous laws, rooted in the five elements, generating and restraining each other, evolving together, giving birth to all things, and gathering the Three Thousand Great Daos.

This was a true world, a world without any flaws!

Previously, the Small Xuan Realm's laws were incomplete. If one's strength exceeded a certain realm, it was impossible to break through to a higher realm within it, because the resonance of the world's laws could not be triggered, and Heaven-and-Earth Energy could not gather for Baptism.

But now, this restriction no longer existed.

Even if someone advanced to the Emperor Realm within it, there would be no obstacles.

Yang Kai was overjoyed, lingering and exploring the Small Xuan Realm, feeling the joy this reborn world conveyed to him. The perfection of the Small Xuan Realm seemed to make his medicine garden even more vibrant. The many rare flowers and herbs planted in the garden swayed with life. Centered around the Everlasting Tree, the astonishing vitality it brought made countless herbs flourish.

What delighted Yang Kai most was that he sensed a faint will within the Small Xuan Realm.

It was the will of the world, like a newborn baby—ignorant and not yet fully conscious. But Yang Kai knew that, given time, it would grow completely.

When that time came, he could use it to comprehend the Great Might of Heaven and Earth and break through the shackles of the Great Emperor Realm.

This was the original intention behind Yang Kai's painstaking efforts to repair the Small Xuan Realm. Now, it seemed he had made the right move.

He did not rush to leave the Small Xuan Realm. Instead, he immersed his mind within it, spreading his invisible power like a parent, communicating with the realm's ignorant will.

Failure after failure, rejection after rejection, Yang Kai tried again and again.

He did not know how much time had passed, but Yang Kai felt the world's will was slightly less resistant. It seemed to be gradually accepting him. Just as Yang Kai was about to seize the opportunity, his vision suddenly went dark, his Spirit Body collapsed, and the next moment he returned to his physical body.

Opening his eyes again, a wave of dizziness struck. Sitting cross-legged on the bed, Yang Kai collapsed to the ground with a thud.

His head throbbed painfully, his face darkened, and he knew it was due to overconsumption of his Divine Soul power.

Although he now possessed the Shaman Master realm and had awakened Divine Sense, he was still far from his peak. Such reckless use of his Divine Soul was a huge burden.

If he were at his peak, this would not be a problem. But now, it was a big issue.

The sound of him falling startled the guard outside. Yu rushed in immediately. Seeing Yang Kai's pale face and blood streaming from his seven orifices, her expression changed. She hurried forward to help him up, calling out, "Shaman Niu... what's wrong with you?"

Yang Kai opened his eyes and looked at her. After recognizing her, he waved his hand to indicate he was fine. With a flip of his palm, he took out a Spiritual Pill to replenish his Divine Sense and discreetly swallowed it, then sat cross-legged on the bed.

Although Yu was worried, seeing Yang Kai's calm demeanor, she was somewhat reassured and stood nearby, watching him closely.

What shocked Yu even more was that, in just a short while, Yang Kai seemed completely revitalized, his energy restored as if nothing had happened—she could hardly believe her eyes.

Yu could tell that something had gone wrong during Shaman Niu's cultivation. It was a life-threatening matter, so how could he recover so quickly?

While she was lost in thought, Yang Kai asked, "How long has it been?"

Yu was stunned for a moment but still replied, "Five days."

Five days had passed since Yang Kai entered this room. Yu had checked on him once before, saw him cultivating, and didn't dare disturb him, so she had been guarding the door.

"So long..." Yang Kai said as he jumped off the bed. After stretching, his body crackled, and he said, "Now that things are done, I should head back."

Yu was startled and quickly stopped him, saying, "You can't leave yet."

"Why?" Yang Kai tilted his head and looked at her.

Yu's mind raced, and she finally came up with a good excuse: "Lord Che still wants to see you."

Yang Kai frowned. Although he found it troublesome, he patiently said, "Then take me to see Lord Che now."

Yu's expression stiffened. "Lord Che is out and hasn't returned... I don't know when he'll be back."

Yang Kai looked at her and saw Yu's evasive gaze, clearly not daring to meet his eyes. He couldn't help but laugh. "The Barbarian Tribe is naturally not good at lying..." Suddenly, a thief's image flashed in his mind, and he added, "Most of them, anyway. Lord Che wants to keep me in the Frost Snow Tribe?"

Exposed by Yang Kai, Yu panicked and didn't know how to explain.

Yang Kai patted her shoulder and said, "I've already taught the method for preparing healing medicine. Tell Lord Che for me: I don't like this at all. I have no intention of being enemies with the Frost Snow Tribe, but if you want to restrict my freedom, the Frost Snow Tribe will have to pay a price."

Though his tone was calm, it sent chills through Yu. For a moment, she felt she was facing not an Upper-Level Shaman Warrior, but a Shaman King, a Shaman Saint...

Yang Kai walked straight past her. Yu felt her body stiffen, unable even to turn around. In her shock, she realized her body was out of her control.

What kind of Shamanic Art was this! How could it have such a miraculous effect?

Several muffled thuds sounded outside the door. It seemed the guards had been knocked down.

Yu sighed softly. Though she felt some regret, she was also relieved.

Lord Che wanted to keep Shaman Niu in the Frost Snow Tribe, and she could understand that. An apothecary capable of preparing such healing medicine was a huge asset. If he could serve the tribe, it would be greatly beneficial.

Unfortunately, Lord Che hadn't had the chance to explain things to him, and Shaman Niu had already forced his way out. Now that things had escalated, she didn't know how to resolve it.

In any case, she had no further role in this matter. In Luobai Mountain, she owed Shaman Niu a favor and didn't want to use such means to deceive him, so she felt much more at ease.

In the royal palace, wherever Yang Kai passed, Barbarian Tribe warriors fell silently.

Just as he was about to leave the palace, a tall Great Shaman Master appeared before him, his aura as fierce as thunder.

Who else could it be but Che?

As the Great Shaman Master of the Frost Snow Tribe and one of the palace commanders, if Yang Kai had knocked down so many people without him noticing, it would have been a dereliction of duty.

Looking at the seemingly frail tribesman ahead, Che's eyes could not hide his admiration and surprise.

Being a skilled apothecary was already rare, but Shaman Niu's strength was also formidable. No wonder he dared to venture alone into Luobai Mountain in the cold winter, relying solely on his physical strength to kill six Bone-Eating Wolves and a Wolf King. Che had doubted it when Yu reported it, but now it seemed it was true.

Why isn't someone like this part of the Frost Snow Tribe?

But it's not too late. As long as he can be persuaded to join the Frost Snow Tribe, that's enough. Powerful shamans joining other tribes is not unusual in this world.

And the best way to win someone over is to cater to their interests.

## Chapter 2683 — Chess Piece

What made Wu Ming even more terrified was that the Yao King who had always followed Yang Kai suddenly erupted at this moment, unleashing a mighty pressure comparable to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, crashing down on him like a mountain.

Wu Ming was instantly immobilized, unable to circulate his Emperor Yuan.

"Pa..."

With a crisp sound, Wu Ming's head snapped to the side, a burning pain flaring on his cheek, his mouth filled with the taste of blood.

All the Emperor Realm experts who had come to attend the auction widened their eyes in shock, watching in horror.

That Wu Ming was a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert, yet he was slapped in the face in front of everyone. Just how arrogant and wild was this young man called Yang Kai? Did he really think that having a Yao King at his back meant he could do as he pleased?

"You dare..." Wu Ming clenched his fists, gritting his teeth as he growled at Yang Kai, like a furious beast.

Before he could finish, a palm rapidly enlarged before his eyes and struck his other cheek hard. With a loud smack, several teeth flew out.

Wu Ming felt dizzy and dazed, both from anger and from the beating. He was, after all, a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert and a Deacon of the Star God Palace, always standing above others. Who would have thought he would suffer such humiliation in this little Flowing Shadow City?

Consumed by rage, he frantically pushed his Emperor Yuan, barely shaking off Yingfei's oppressive aura, and leapt back several steps, roaring, "That's enough!"

"Still barking! Get over here!" Yang Kai's expression turned cold as he spread his hand. Under the undulating Laws of Space, Wu Ming was horrified to find his body moving uncontrollably, drawn toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai swung his fist, smashing it into Wu Ming's face, covering him in blood. Not finished, Yang Kai leapt high, bent his elbow, and drove it fiercely into Wu Ming's back.

Violent force poured out, and Wu Ming spat a mouthful of blood, collapsing to the ground.

What greeted him next was a storm of punches and kicks.

The surrounding cultivators were all stunned, staring blankly.

That young man called Yang Kai was beating Wu Ming on the ground like a street thug. As he pummeled, he cursed non-stop, his arrogance reaching its peak. Wu Ming, on the other hand, tried to resist several times, but his strength scattered before he could gather it. After a few failed attempts, he seemed to realize he was like a fish on the chopping block, and, abandoning all dignity, covered his head with his hands and curled up, letting Yang Kai do as he pleased.

No one had ever seen Emperor Realm experts fight like this. Today was truly an eye-opener; everyone clicked their tongues in amazement, yet trembled in fear.

In no time, Wu Ming was beaten black and blue, covered in blood, barely recognizable as human. Fortunately, his cultivation was strong; otherwise, after Yang Kai's outburst, he would likely have lost his life.

Panting, Yang Kai kicked him a few more times before finally stopping. He turned to look at Ye Hen and the others, saying, "Want to collect some interest?"

Ye Hen gave a bitter smile and shook his head. "Young Master Yang, you decide."

He knew Yang Kai was venting Thousand Leaves Sect's anger. Now that Wu Ming was lying on the ground, powerless to fight back, he could go take some revenge. But to rely on someone else's strength to act tough—what would that make him?

Yang Kai nodded slightly, not pressing the matter. He bent down, grabbed Wu Ming by the hair, and hauled him up. Leaning in so close their faces nearly touched, he spoke in a chilling voice, "I know you have someone backing you. Go tell your master this: if even a single hair is harmed on any of my friends, I'll tear his bones out one by one and soak them in wine."

Wu Ming let out a chilling laugh, his face twisted with malice. "That depends on whether you know what's good for you or not," he sneered.

Yang Kai stared at him and said flatly, "So you want to die."

Wu Ming's body trembled, a chill shooting from his soles to the top of his head, making his limbs cold and his whole body shiver. He had thought he held leverage and could make Yang Kai hesitate, but he never expected this man to act so recklessly.

He really wanted to kill him. That killing intent, though hidden, was like a sharp sword stabbing into Wu Ming's heart, making him freeze in terror.

He had no doubt that if he tried to provoke him again, he would become a corpse the next moment.

"I can grant your wish," Yang Kai grinned. "I'm a soft-hearted person. Beg me, and I'll give you a quick death."

Wu Ming fell silent, not daring to speak.

Yang Kai snorted and casually tossed him aside like a piece of trash.

When Wu Ming landed, he saw Yang Kai already leading his group into the auction hall.

Little bastard, just you wait. Relying on a Yao King for protection, you dare act so brazenly toward this Lord? Today's humiliation will be repaid a hundredfold, a thousandfold!

He could already picture Yang Kai kneeling and begging for mercy. Wu Ming felt a surge of delight, sneering repeatedly, drawing the attention of the Emperor Realm experts attending the auction. They wondered if Wu Ming had been beaten silly.

The auction hall was designed like a stepwell, spiraling upward in layers, spacious enough to hold over a thousand people without feeling crowded. Looking up, the oval dome was engraved with images of ferocious beasts, making the whole hall look like a giant beast with its mouth wide open, ready to swallow all the cultivators inside.

Led by a beautiful maid, Yang Kai and his group entered a private room. This room seemed to be at the very top of the auction hall. Standing inside and looking down, they could see everything in the hall clearly, feeling as if they overlooked all beneath them.

The maid, having seen Yang Kai's violent display at the door, was now trembling, barely daring to breathe. After leading them in, her face pale, she said, "Please wait a moment, honored guests. The auction will begin soon."

Though she was just a lowly maid, she could sense that Yang Kai and his group had come to cause trouble. After speaking, she fled the room as if escaping.

One by one, the Emperor Realm experts were led by maids into different private rooms, while the main hall below gradually filled with people.

Although most attendees lacked the wealth to bid, the more people at an auction, the better.

Yang Kai's powerful Divine Sense swept unrestrained across the entire auction hall. Instantly, the noisy main hall fell silent. Sensing the strength of this Divine Sense, no one dared make a sound.

Yingfei was doing the same.

After a moment, the two withdrew their Divine Senses.

"Young Master Yang, did you find anything?" Yingfei asked.

Yang Kai shook his head, sitting down. "They're hiding very well. What about you, Yao King?"

Yingfei shrugged, indicating he found nothing either, and frowned. "But since we're sure this is a trap, you should be careful, Young Master Yang. The deeper something is hidden, the more dangerous it is."

Yang Kai nodded.

Ye Qinghan was brewing tea at the side, her movements skillful, but her brows furrowed with worry. Du Xian and Ye Hen were equally anxious.

...

"Master, everything is ready." Wu Ming, his face still bruised from Yang Kai's beating, appeared at some point, standing respectfully before an old man and reporting.

Who knows what kind of Spiritual Pill he had taken, but though there were still traces of bruising, he looked much better than before. Only some internal injuries remained, impossible to heal so quickly. Every time Wu Ming circulated his Emperor Yuan, his organs ached faintly.

That little bastard really hit hard...

The old man nodded slightly. "How many Emperor Realm experts came?"

Wu Ming replied, "Over thirty."

The old man was satisfied. "That's enough." After a pause, he waved his hand. "Since all the guests have arrived, let's begin."

Wu Ming replied respectfully, "Yes!"

Turning to the side, Wu Ming gestured. "Junior Sister, please."

Hua Qingsi slowly rose, giving the old man a complicated look. "Master, this disciple takes her leave."

"Go," the old man said, waving his hand.

Hua Qingsi walked a few steps, then suddenly turned back, knelt before the old man, and kowtowed three times with utmost respect.

The old man's eyes flickered, seeming to understand Hua Qingsi's intent. He did not stop her, but instead closed his eyes.

Life is your own, and the path you walk is yours to choose. If you insist on standing against me, then even the bond of master and disciple means nothing!

Wu Ming stood to the side, sneering coldly. "Ungrateful wretch!"

Hua Qingsi was unmoved. After kowtowing, she stood up, took a deep breath as if shedding a burden, and walked out. As she passed Wu Ming, she paused and said, "Wu Ming, you've provoked someone you shouldn't have. You'll die a miserable death."

"Treacherous bitch!" Wu Ming roared in fury, slapping Hua Qingsi across the face, leaving a red handprint on her fair cheek.

Hua Qingsi touched her cheek and smiled. "What, got beaten by someone else and now want to take it out on me? That's all you're good for."

Her words instantly enraged Wu Ming. The humiliation Yang Kai had inflicted replayed vividly in his mind. His Emperor Yuan surged as he raised his hand to strike Hua Qingsi's head.

Hua Qingsi not only didn't dodge, she thrust out her chest and met his hand head-on.

"Enough!" The old man, who had kept his eyes closed, suddenly barked.

Wu Ming froze, his raised hand stopping in midair. He looked Hua Qingsi up and down and grinned. "Junior Sister, are you trying to die on purpose? But Senior Brother won't let you have your way. You're a precious chess piece!"

Hua Qingsi gritted her teeth. "Despicable!"

Wu Ming laughed. "Say what you like. Anyway, Master has already promised that after this is over, you'll be my reward. Rest assured, Senior Brother will cherish you well."

He emphasized the word "cherish," his expression full of meaning.

Hua Qingsi's face changed dramatically. She looked at the old man in disbelief, but he showed no reaction. She knew Wu Ming wasn't lying, and her heart ached with sorrow.

She had never imagined that her own Master possessed such a hidden side. Perhaps it was because her strength had been too low before, unable to touch upon deeper secrets, and so she had never truly understood this so-called Master. Only after advancing to the Emperor Realm did everything become clear.

"Go on, don't keep the guests waiting," the old man waved.

"Yes!" Wu Ming replied respectfully, glaring at Hua Qingsi before leaving with her.

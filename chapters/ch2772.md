## Chapter 2772 — Exchange Gathering

"I never had the chance to thank you for saving my life last time. Allow me to offer you this toast." Hua Yulu raised her cup and drained it in one go.

Although the Palace Master of Hundred Flowers Palace appeared delicate and frail, as if she could be toppled by the wind, she actually possessed a spirit that rivaled any man. After downing her drink, her face grew even more flushed.

"Palace Master Hua, you are too polite. It was the matter with Tan Junhao that caused you to be questioned by Star God Palace." Yang Kai smiled faintly and also finished the wine in his cup.

Hua Yulu shook her head and said, "It was nothing troublesome, just a few questions. I answered truthfully. I hope I didn't make things difficult for Elder Yang?"

Yang Kai chuckled, "Palace Master Hua, your honesty was a great help to me. How could you have made things difficult?"

Hua Yulu let out a sigh of relief, glanced around, and asked in a low voice, "Why don't I see Xia Sheng anywhere?"

Last time in Flowing Shadow City, Hua Yulu had said she was Xia Sheng's friend. It seemed they truly were old acquaintances, otherwise she wouldn't be so concerned.

"Senior Brother Xia is on duty at Linghu Palace and likely won't return for a while," Yang Kai explained. "If Palace Master Hua wishes to find Senior Brother Xia, you might try looking for him at Linghu Palace."

"I see..." Hua Yulu showed a hint of disappointment.

As they spoke, Gao Xueting suddenly walked over with a cold expression.

Hua Yulu immediately said, "Elder Yang, I'll leave you to your business. I'll take a walk." With that, she turned and left.

"How is your injury?" Gao Xueting leaned in close to Yang Kai and asked softly, her gaze fixed on Hua Yulu's departing figure.

"It's alright," Yang Kai replied.

After taking a blow to the chest from Xue Zhengmao, any other First Stage Emperor would have been bedridden for days, if not dead. But Yang Kai's body was exceptionally strong, and now that he wore the Feihong Black Dragon Armor, he had only broken a few bones, with no internal injuries. Such wounds would heal in a day or two even without treatment.

"How do you know that woman?" Gao Xueting asked abruptly, her tone a bit disdainful.

Yang Kai replied, "I met her once in Flowing Shadow City. Why?"

"It's nothing. If you're injured, go rest. Otherwise, stay away from those dubious people."

Dubious people...

Gao Xueting seemed to have quite a strong opinion of Hua Yulu. Realizing this, Yang Kai dared not say anything more.

Gao Xueting snatched the wine cup from his hand and said, "There will be an exchange gathering among the Emperors later. There might be some good things. If you're interested, come and have a look."

"Exchange gathering?" Yang Kai's interest was piqued.

Gao Xueting nodded, "It's rare for so many Emperors to gather in one place, so it's a good opportunity to trade. This is also the Hall Master's idea. We're hosting it at Qingyang Shrine. If you have any treasures you don't need, you can bring them out to exchange."

"Alright, let me know when it's time," Yang Kai nodded.

"Go on," Gao Xueting waved her hand.

The conferral ceremony had ended, and Yang Kai had officially become an Elder of the Shrine. However, with little time to spare, he still had no residence of his own, so he could only return to Purple Bamboo Peak.

After half a day of meditation and healing, by evening his injuries had mostly healed.

The Transmission Compass at his side trembled slightly. Yang Kai picked it up, swept it with his Divine Sense, and stood up.

It was still the Ten Thousand Saints Peak, but this time the exchange gathering was held inside the grand hall. Most guests had already received word, so hardly anyone had left—only three to five at most, those with urgent matters. Otherwise, no one would willingly miss such an event.

Several disciples of Qingyang Shrine were stationed at the entrance as greeters. When they saw Yang Kai, they all showed respectful expressions and bowed, "Elder Yang."

Yang Kai nodded in acknowledgment and strode inside.

With a glance, he saw the hall was already full. Nearly all the guests from earlier were present—over a hundred Emperors and their disciples or juniors.

Despite the large crowd, the scene was orderly and quiet. Even when people conversed, it was done in hushed tones.

The hall was brightly lit, as if it were daytime.

"Over here!"

As he was looking for a seat, Gao Xueting's voice suddenly called out to him. Yang Kai followed the sound and saw her sitting on a chair on the left side of the hall, waving at him.

Yang Kai smiled and walked over.

This area seemed reserved for Qingyang Shrine's Elders. Besides Gao Xueting, other Elders like Chen Qian, Di Rong, and You Kun were all present. When Yang Kai arrived, they all greeted him with friendly smiles.

Yang Kai greeted everyone in turn before sitting down next to Gao Xueting.

"Junior Brother, have you ever attended such an exchange gathering?" Gao Xueting suddenly turned and asked.

Yang Kai was momentarily stunned. He glanced to his other side and, seeing no one there, realized she was talking to him. "Elder Gao, are you talking to me?" he asked, wide-eyed.

Gao Xueting snorted, "If I weren't talking to you, who else would I be talking to—a ghost?"

Chen Qian, sitting on Gao Xueting's other side, covered her mouth and laughed. She explained, "Yang Kai, now that you've advanced to the Emperor Realm and become an Elder of the Shrine, you are our peer. That's the Shrine's rule."

Yang Kai scratched his head awkwardly, "I just didn't react in time."

Until today, he had always regarded himself as a disciple before Gao Xueting. Suddenly becoming her Junior Brother, with his status rising so abruptly, was quite a jolt.

But thinking about it, Yang Kai's cultivation and status were now on par with Gao Xueting and the others. He truly was qualified to be their peer.

"Call me Senior Sister!" Gao Xueting said solemnly, looking at him.

"Senior Sister Gao!" Yang Kai grinned, adapting quickly.

"Good boy, but there's no reward," Gao Xueting smirked, then asked again, "Have you ever attended such an exchange gathering?"

"No," Yang Kai shook his head. He had attended many auctions, but only heard of such high-level exchange gatherings. "But I know the basic rules—it's barter, right?"

"That's right," Gao Xueting nodded. "Once you reach the Emperor Realm, Source Crystals lose much of their value. Many treasures can't be bought with Source Crystals, so people prefer to trade directly. Of course, some may still want Source Crystals—it all depends on what the owner wants in exchange."

Yang Kai listened intently, nodding as he absorbed Gao Xueting's explanation. His earnest attitude made her quite satisfied.

After a short wait, the doors of Ten Thousand Saints Hall creaked shut.

Yang Kai knew the exchange gathering was about to begin.

Sure enough, as soon as the doors closed, an elderly man flew onto the high platform. It was none other than Qiu Ran, Deputy Hall Master of Qingyang Shrine.

As the host, Qingyang Shrine naturally had to go first and set the mood.

Qiu Ran cleared his throat and said, "Thank you all for coming, making it possible for tonight's exchange gathering to proceed smoothly. I wish everyone great success and abundant gains. There's no need to go over the rules. The exchange gathering begins now. Our Shrine will start things off and hopes everyone will bring out their best treasures."

With a wave of his hand, a jade bottle appeared. Raising it, Qiu Ran announced, "Taiwei Soul Refining Pills—Imperial-Grade Lower-Level Spiritual Pills. Even Emperors can greatly strengthen their Divine Soul by refining and absorbing them. If used with Divine Soul secret arts, the effect is even better. There are three pills in the bottle. Only Source Crystals will be accepted. Please bid as you wish."

As soon as he finished speaking, more than half of the Emperors present showed interest.

No wonder—Imperial Pills!

Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills were not easy to come by. Only an Imperial-Grade Pill Master could refine them, and in the entire Star Realm, how many such masters were there? Even a great Sect like Qingyang Shrine didn't have its own Imperial-Grade Pill Master.

Ziyuan Commerce Guild did have one, Master Huangfu, but his schedule was always full. Even if you wanted to ask him for pills, you'd have to wait years for your turn.

In the Star Realm, obtaining Spiritual Pills below the Imperial-Grade was not too difficult. While Dao Origin-Grade Alchemists were not exactly common, there were still a fair number of them around. But as for Imperial Pills, such things were truly rare and could only be chanced upon by luck.

For Qingyang Shrine to bring out three Taiwei Soul Refining Pills as the host showed their sincerity.

And since only Source Crystals were accepted, every Emperor present had a chance to participate.

So as soon as Qiu Ran finished speaking, bids rang out one after another. Each new bid quickly replaced the last, and the hall became as lively as a marketplace. The Emperors shouted themselves hoarse, determined to win.

Yang Kai was surprised as well. He hadn't expected the Shrine to auction off Imperial Pills right at the start. Even as a mere opening act, this was a heavy offering.

But then he realized something.

The main use of Taiwei Soul Refining Pills was to temper the Divine Soul. If used with Divine Soul secret arts, they could further increase one's Divine Soul power.

But for the Emperors of Qingyang Shrine, this wasn't very useful.

Because Qingyang Shrine had the Divine Roaming Mirror!

When Yang Kai first arrived at Qingyang Shrine, Wen Zishan had told him that the Shrine's cultivators all had stronger Divine Souls than those from other forces, thanks to the Divine Roaming Mirror.

The Divine Roaming Mirror itself was a secret realm for enhancing the Divine Soul. Any Emperor from the Shrine who trained inside would gain far more than from taking Taiwei Soul Refining Pills.

In other words, to strengthen the Divine Soul, all they needed was the Divine Roaming Mirror—no need for Imperial-Grade Pills.

So, in the eyes of Qingyang Shrine’s Emperors, these Taiwei Soul Refining Pills were nothing particularly remarkable. The calm expressions on Chen Qian and the others made it obvious.

Realizing this, Yang Kai couldn't help but laugh to himself.

"Five million!" Suddenly, someone shouted, and the hall fell silent.

A bid of five million Upper-Level Source Crystals immediately made the remaining Emperors back down. That price was already the limit. Anyone who could reach the Emperor Realm wasn't a fool—they wouldn't waste Source Crystals recklessly. And this was just the beginning; who knew what other treasures would appear later?

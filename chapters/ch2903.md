## Chapter 2903 — Thousand Illusion Dreamscape

Yang Kai withdrew his gaze and let out a gentle breath.

He had already seen the outcome of this great battle between the two races. As he had expected, the Barbarian Tribe, after paying a tremendous price, had achieved victory. The invaders were utterly wiped out. As for the two Demon Saints who had fled, even with their heaven-defying abilities, there was likely nowhere in this vast world for them to hide.

In a certain sense, this trial had been quite successful. Yang Kai himself had gained greatly, especially in his understanding of Shamanic Arts from ancient times—something no written record could ever compare to.

The only flaw was that Shaman Niu had died. Yet, given the situation at the time, as Shaman Niu, Yang Kai could not think of a better way to resolve the Great Demon God's attack from afar.

Now, only one final problem remained…

Yang Kai turned his head, looked at the petite young woman beside him, and asked seriously, "Who are you? How should I address you, or should I call you as before… Die!"

Die smiled, as if sensing a trace of dissatisfaction in Yang Kai's heart, and softly said, "I am a witness to history, a recorder. What you have experienced is but a drop in the ocean of what I have recorded."

"Was this illusion created by you?" Yang Kai raised his brow.

Die shook her head and said, "I only witness and record. What kind of illusion one encounters here is entirely the trial-taker's own choice."

Yang Kai frowned and said, "I don't recall choosing anything."

After entering here, he had awakened as Ah Niu of Cangnan Village. When had he ever had a chance to choose?

Die smiled and said, "Certain things within your body helped you make the choice."

Yang Kai thought for a moment and said, "Ancient Demon Qi?" Only the Ancient Demon Qi seemed to have any connection to the illusion of the two races' great battle before him.

As soon as he spoke, he was suddenly startled. He remembered that he had unlocked the Two-Color Seal at his Dantian and had already entered a demonic state, yet now he felt little discomfort. He wanted to check his own condition, but at this moment, he didn't even have a physical body. He was appearing here in a form similar to a Divine Soul, conversing with Die, so he simply could not examine his state.

As if sensing his thoughts, Die spoke, “There is no need to worry. Everything here is suspended. Deal with your troubles after you leave this place.”

Yang Kai nodded, pondered for a moment, and said, "You say you are a witness and recorder of history. Can I take it to mean you are this illusion itself?"

Die looked at him with bright eyes and said, "How clever. That's right, I am the illusion itself."

Yang Kai said, "Everyone who comes here experiences a different illusion, right? What I went through was the demon invasion and the great battle between the two races. If someone else came, perhaps they would experience something else."

"Exactly so." Die smiled lightly, feeling that talking to a clever person was never tiring. "Moreover, this time you experienced a scene of a great battle between two races. Perhaps next time you enter, it will be something else. Everything is in flux, and all things have their causes and effects."

"Does this secret realm have a name?" Yang Kai asked curiously.

"Thousand Illusion Dreamscape!" Die replied, her petite body suddenly spinning and, with a pop, bursting apart.

Yang Kai was slightly startled, but quickly calmed his mind, his gaze growing dazed as he looked at a giant butterfly ahead.

Where Die had originally stood, there was now a butterfly as tall as a person, dazzlingly colorful. The butterfly's vivid hues gave an impression of breathtaking beauty. It gently flapped its wings, sending streams of light flowing through the air and leaving one utterly entranced.

Die's voice came from the butterfly: "I am the Thousand Illusion Dream Butterfly, the master of this secret realm!"

"So you are the key to opening this secret realm!" Yang Kai suddenly understood.

Previously, Princess Lan Xun had told Yang Kai that among the many secret realms on the Fifth Stage of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, some required specific keys to open. For example, the Grand Illusionary Realm she had mentioned needed the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome to unlock. Ever since Star God Palace Elder Tan Junhao was killed by Yang Kai in Flowing Shadow City, the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome had fallen into Yang Kai's hands, causing some disciples of Star God Palace who were training in the Grand Illusionary Realm to be trapped inside, unable to leave.

In order to retrieve the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, Star God Palace had no choice but to agree to Yang Kai's demand to train in the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda for a month.

The key to the Grand Illusionary Realm was the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, while the key to the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape that Yang Kai experienced was Die, who had now transformed into the Thousand Illusion Dream Butterfly!

Die chuckled softly, "A key? I am not just a key. As I just told you, I am this illusion itself."

Yang Kai did not know what the difference was, but instinctively felt that this so-called Thousand Illusion Dreamscape was somewhat more advanced than the Grand Illusionary Realm Lan Xun had mentioned. He nodded and said, "I roughly understand. But since you are the master of this secret realm, why did you appear at my side as Die? Were you watching me?"

Die said, "I can easily perceive everything in the secret realm. There's no need to watch anything. As for why... it was merely a whim. I have slumbered for many years, and this time, you awakened me."

"Oh?" Yang Kai raised his brows, somewhat intrigued. "Does that mean I am special to you? Can I take you with me?" He paused, then corrected himself, "Would you be willing to come with me?"

If he could take Die away, he would be able to control a secret realm. Moreover, it would be a secret realm of endless transformations, suitable for martial artists of all levels to train in. As the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace, he had to consider many things.

Every great sect has its own foundation. For example, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda of Star God Palace, the Divine Roaming Mirror of Qingyang Shrine, the Starry Gale Beyond the Heavens of Wuhua Hall, the Martial Intent Hall of Tianwu Sacred Land—each has its own irreplaceable function, contributing unimaginable strength to the development of its sect.

The strength of a sect is not just about having a few experts holding the fort; the future development and the growth of its disciples must also be considered.

The current Lingxiao Palace lacked no experts. Several Yao Kings served as Guest Elders and answered Yang Kai's summons. The palace also had an Imperial-Grade Formation Master, Imperial-Grade Pill Master, Imperial-Grade Artifact Master, and a Space Array connecting north and south, so cultivation resources were not an issue. Yang Kai was even plotting to help Tian Yan rebuild his body and escape the Divine Roaming Mirror. If he succeeded, Lingxiao Palace would have a Great Emperor-level expert as a backer. Even if it did not qualify as a Great Emperor sect, it would be close.

What Lingxiao Palace lacked was a foundation that could help generations of disciples grow.

The Thousand Illusion Dreamscape was undoubtedly an excellent choice.

That was why Yang Kai very much wanted to take it away. The Thousand Illusion Dreamscape was one of the many secret realms on the Fifth Stage of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, and Die was both the secret realm itself and the key. As long as he could take Die away, Lingxiao Palace would have its own secret realm in the future.

As for whether Star God Palace would discover or intervene, Yang Kai felt there was no need to worry about such things.

Many secret realms were sealed on the Fifth Stage of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda. Some could not even be opened or found by the people of Star God Palace. If he took one away, Star God Palace would surely not know.

Besides, even if they did know, what could they do? This was a benefit Yang Kai had gained through his own training. Star God Palace could not just bully the weak and forcibly keep it. That would only damage their reputation.

After asking, Yang Kai rapidly pondered how to persuade Die if she refused. He was not very confident in this proposal.

"You can!" Die said softly.

"Wha… what?" Yang Kai was stunned. He had not expected Die to agree so readily. The words of persuasion he had prepared were instantly rendered useless.

"I said you can." Die repeated.

Yang Kai was momentarily speechless. Die's easy agreement left him at a loss for how to respond.

Instead, Die seemed to see through his concerns and let out a light laugh, “I have seen the transformation and unfolding of history in you. I must record it, and remaining here would be inconvenient.”

Yang Kai grinned, "I don't know what the future holds, but I think my future will be quite exciting. You might record many things."

"Let us hope so."

Yang Kai reached out his hand. Die gently flapped her dazzling wings, and her beautiful form suddenly turned into a stream of light, shooting into Yang Kai's hand.

Yang Kai gently closed his hand. When he looked down, he found a small, exquisite object in his palm, like a butterfly hairpin, identical to the Thousand Illusion Dream Butterfly Die had transformed into, only countless times smaller.

Die had vanished, turning into a butterfly-shaped key that remained in Yang Kai's hand.

Suddenly, everything around him twisted and warped into a chaotic, grotesque display. Then Yang Kai felt an irresistible force pulling at him, dragging him toward an unknown direction, as if to cast him into the depths of hell itself.

He could not resist and could only guard his mind.

Amid a rumbling sound, Yang Kai felt his Divine Soul pass through an endless tunnel of time and space, arriving at a familiar residence in an instant.

He opened his eyes and immediately confirmed that his Divine Soul had returned to his body.

However, his physical condition was extremely dire.

Dense Demonic Qi enveloped him, and his flesh swelled as his body radiated an extremely dangerous aura. That aura assaulted his Divine Soul, making it restless, as if he could be demonized at any moment.

The demonic state triggered within the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape had produced a genuine response in his physical form. In other words, although the experiences within that dreamscape were merely illusions, they maintained intricate connections to the experiencer's actual body. Just as it was now—when Yang Kai had entered a demonic state within the illusion, his real self had truly fallen into one as well.

There was no time to think further. Yang Kai hurriedly activated the power of the Warm Spirit Lotus in his Sea of Consciousness to protect the clarity of his Divine Sense, then immersed himself in the Small Xuan Realm, drawing on the sealing power of the gold and silver tree in the medicine garden and channeling it into himself.

Gold and silver light quickly surged out, flowing through his meridians and all over his body.

Under the wondrous power of the gold and silver seal, the Demonic Qi surging through his limbs, bones, and flesh cells seemed to meet its nemesis, instantly recoiling and rushing back toward his Dantian, like rabbits fleeing from a hawk, revealing a sense of panic.

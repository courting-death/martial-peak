## Chapter 2795 — Shattered Worlds

If a cultivator who practiced Metal Attribute techniques or secret arts came here, they would surely be overjoyed by such a cultivation environment! Training in a place like this would absolutely increase efficiency by ten or even a hundred times.

“So this is the shattered space of the Fourth Layer?” Yang Kai mused, “It really is somewhat distorted.”

Previously, Lan Xun had also said that because the spaces inside the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda were all shattered and incomplete, even the laws of heaven and earth within them had become broken and tattered. In some spaces, after countless years, certain laws would become extremely twisted, and perhaps one or several laws would become especially strong, while others would be weak or even missing.

The shattered space before him was just like this. The Metal Law nearly dominated everything, leaving no room for any other laws. This distortion was truly severe.

He had entered this place by choosing an entrance radiating golden light. In other words, the colors of those clouds must represent the state of the laws within the shattered spaces.

And those clusters of clouds were the entrances to each shattered space! By passing through clouds of different colors, one could enter different shattered spaces.

Why was it like this?

Yang Kai was certain that the other disciples of Star God Palace had never encountered such a thing, because if it had happened before, Lan Xun would definitely have mentioned it. Since Lan Xun had never brought it up, Yang Kai’s experience was unique.

This was good news, because while in the buffer zone Yang Kai could choose which shattered space to enter—provided he could return to it.

Thinking this, Yang Kai immediately set to work.

The Laws of Space surged as he reached out and tore at the void ahead.

It went surprisingly smoothly, without much resistance. On second thought, it made sense—this world was already broken, its laws incomplete, so how much interference could it really give Yang Kai?

A rift in the void appeared, and Yang Kai’s figure flickered as he slipped inside.

Sure enough, when he reappeared, he found himself back in that mysterious buffer zone. Just like last time, a strange force bounced Yang Kai away, but he instinctively activated the Laws of Space, instantly steadying himself within that wondrous realm, surrounded on all sides by swirling clouds and mist.

“Ha, so that’s how it is!” Yang Kai laughed heartily.

The first time he entered this buffer zone, Yang Kai hadn’t understood what was happening. Now, coming here a second time with the intent to test things, he had finally figured it out.

The Laws of Space!

According to Lan Xun, when Emperor Realm cultivators passed through the pagoda’s main gate, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda would send them into a shattered space on the Fourth Layer. This process must have involved the Laws of Space.

But Yang Kai himself was proficient in the Laws of Space, so when the laws surged, he repelled that pulling force. As a result, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda couldn’t send him into any shattered space and could only leave him in the buffer zone.

“Now I can play however I want, can’t I?” After figuring out the truth, Yang Kai grinned.

When others entered the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, they could only rely on luck. If they accidentally entered a dead zone, they could only blame their bad fortune. But for Yang Kai, he had much more control.

With his mind clear, Yang Kai immediately began searching around.

Those pitch-black, deathly clouds could be ignored—entering them would surely mean death, as even the laws of heaven and earth there were likely almost gone.

What he sought were the brightly colored clouds, because the color of the clouds was directly connected to the hidden shattered space. The brighter the color, the better the shattered space, and the more useful it would be to him.

Before long, Yang Kai locked onto a cluster of five-colored clouds and charged straight in.

Sure enough, this time the shattered world he entered, though its laws were still broken, was not twisted. Unexpectedly, the spiritual qi here was also extremely rich, not inferior at all to a first-tier Sect in the Southern Territory.

However, when Yang Kai released his Divine Sense, he couldn’t help but be a little surprised.

“So small…”

He found that this shattered space was extremely tiny, covering only a few dozen mu. Its edges were all nothingness and chaos, giving an extremely uncomfortable feeling.

Such a small space was about the same as Emperor Heaven Valley of Thousand Leaves Sect. There was some vegetation, but no living creatures. When Yang Kai swept his Divine Sense, nothing in this world could escape his notice.

Small as it was, it was better than nothing. Yang Kai was also curious to see what changes the Xuan Realm Pearl would undergo after refining and absorbing the laws of this world.

He might as well treat it as an experiment.

With this in mind, Yang Kai immediately summoned the Xuan Realm Pearl, sat cross-legged, pressed his palms together, and placed the Xuan Realm Pearl in his hands.

With a thought, the entrance to the Xuan Realm Pearl opened. Yang Kai immersed his mind within, silently circulating his cultivation technique, and used his mind to control the Xuan Realm Pearl to devour the spiritual qi and broken laws of this world.

In an instant, winds rose and clouds surged. Spiritual qi from all directions poured endlessly into the Xuan Realm Pearl, like ten thousand streams flowing into the sea.

At the same time, mysterious and nearly unfathomable laws flowed through the entrance into the Xuan Realm Pearl, filling the world inside the Small Xuan Realm.

Suddenly, lightning flashed and thunder roared. Yang Kai’s eyes snapped open as he abruptly sensed a wave of hostility enveloping him from all directions.

Yang Kai was shocked, thinking there was some danger here he hadn’t noticed. But after careful investigation, he realized that wasn’t the case. There were no living things in this world, yet the hostility was clear and real, omnipresent, wrapping tightly around him.

In an instant, it was as if he had fallen into an ocean full of hostility.

“World Force!” Yang Kai’s expression grew serious as he immediately understood what this hostility was.

By using the Xuan Realm Pearl to refine and devour the laws of this world, he had naturally provoked the rejection and resentment of this world. The Heavenly Dao is constant, but fate is unpredictable—even a broken world is still a world.

Amidst the flashing lightning and rolling thunder, bolts of electricity crashed down on Yang Kai’s head, catching him completely off guard. His entire body convulsed from the strikes, and only by hastily circulating his Emperor Yuan to defend himself did he manage to ease the pain somewhat.

He was secretly alarmed—this was only the rejection force of a tiny world of just a few dozen mu, yet it was already so powerful. If it were a larger world, how much stronger would the rejection and hostility be?

Fortunately, he had chosen such a small shattered world for his experiment. Otherwise, if he had rashly tried to touch a larger world, he might have suffered a disastrous setback.

Secretly relieved, Yang Kai clearly felt a strong force of rejection pressing in from all directions, as if trying to squeeze him out of this world and prevent him from doing as he pleased.

But this rejection force wasn’t strong. Yang Kai easily resisted it and remained as steady as a mountain.

The lightning and thunder became even more violent.

Yang Kai sat cross-legged with a nonchalant expression, letting the lightning strike his head, completely unconcerned, like a local ruffian barging into the home of a defenseless old farmer, intent on robbing everything inside.

“World Force…” Yang Kai’s lips curled into a smile. Not only was he unafraid, he was even a little pleased.

A few years ago, in the Shattered Star Sea, he had personally witnessed the earth-shattering battle between two Great Emperors. That allowed Yang Kai to learn about a power called the Great Might of Heaven and Earth—a force even more profound than Law Power, one that only Great Emperors could wield.

Wasn’t this World Force precisely the Great Might of Heaven and Earth?

Although the World Force of this world wasn’t strong, it was still pure. Its rejection and hostility toward Yang Kai couldn’t harm him, but instead gave him a chance to experience and comprehend this power in advance.

Vaguely, he gained some insight.

However, the good times didn’t last. After just three hours, the lightning and thunder suddenly vanished, and the world’s hostility disappeared without a trace.

This didn’t mean the world had accepted him, but that the Xuan Realm Pearl had devoured all the broken laws of this world.

With even the remaining laws gone, this world could no longer show any hostility or rejection.

Yang Kai opened his eyes, smacked his lips, and looked unsatisfied.

Just then, the world suddenly began to collapse. The sky split open with massive cracks, as if a giant mirror had been shattered by a heavy blow. The earth trembled and hummed, deep ravines appearing out of nowhere, while rifts tore through the surrounding void, leaking an aura of primordial chaos and nothingness.

Yang Kai quickly got up, tore open space, and escaped from this world, returning to the buffer zone.

He had just returned to the buffer zone when a cluster of clouds before him suddenly exploded, turning to dust and vanishing.

That shattered world had disappeared just like that.

“Next!” Yang Kai muttered, and began searching among the clouds again.

Before long, he entered another shattered world.

There were many shattered worlds here, varying in size, but with careful observation, there were still patterns to be found. Yang Kai didn’t dare touch the larger worlds, only targeting the smaller ones.

Following the same method, in just three days, Yang Kai had the Xuan Realm Pearl devour the laws of over a dozen small shattered worlds, leaving them completely broken and destroyed.

Yang Kai could clearly feel that the world inside the Xuan Realm Pearl had changed—the laws of heaven and earth were undoubtedly more complete, though still not perfect.

This made him overjoyed.

Over these three days, Yang Kai was in a wonderful mood, but in Star God Palace, outside the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, the four Great Elders were all shrouded in gloom, their faces full of worry.

Because after the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda opened this time, some strange things had happened.

Things that had never happened before.

Three hours after the pagoda opened, a burst of light suddenly erupted on the Fourth Layer. At that time, the Four Elders were all meditating near the pagoda, so when the anomaly occurred, they saw it clearly.

Lei Hong was shocked and immediately went to investigate, but no matter how he searched, he couldn’t figure it out. The remaining three Great Elders were also at a loss.

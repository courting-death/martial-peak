## Chapter 3102 — There Seems to Be Some Misunderstanding

"But thinking about it, those Sect forces probably aren't too strong. How could any truly powerful Sect do such a thing?" This was obvious enough from the fact that those people were only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Cultivators would flock to any truly powerful Sect. Why would it lower itself like this?

He Yunxiang nodded thoughtfully. "Sir is right."

"Here will do." Yang Kai reached out and patted He Yunxiang on the shoulder.

He Yunxiang brought the Star Shuttle to a stop.

Mountains enclosed the place on all sides. Within the valley, the scenery was beautiful, with birdsong and the fragrance of flowers.

"You really picked yourself a fine graveyard. Good eye!" Yan Luo's voice came from behind them. When the first word rang out, he was still far away; by the time he spoke the last, he had already arrived nearby.

The small boat was put away, and the four people from Sea Hall stood suspended in the air.

"It is a good graveyard, but I didn't choose it for myself." Yang Kai grinned.

How could he not know that after refusing those Sects' invitations and leaving on his own, he would draw Sea Hall's pursuit? If the people from Sea Hall and the Yan Family had not pursued him, then so be it. Perhaps they would still have had a path to survival. After all, the Yan Family had helped him enter the Ancestral Domain, and letting their grudge end there would not have been a bad thing. Since they had pursued him, however, then he could only return the discourtesy.

True experts never deliberately targeted the weak; they only destroyed everything the weak possessed in passing. Those who spent all day bullying the weak with their strength were not powerful. They were sick in the head.

How could Yan Luo fail to hear the meaning behind his words? With a cold snort, he said, "Shameless boasting. Now that my Yan Family's Old Ancestor is here, do you really think you can still act however you please?"

After enduring for so many days and bowing and scraping all this time, Yan Luo's great hatred was finally about to be repaid, filling his heart with delight. Gritting his teeth, he said, "Old Ancestor, please seek justice for my Yan Family and uphold justice for the dead Second Elder!"

Yan Renhao nodded slightly, took a step forward, and his First Stage Dao Origin Realm aura immediately climbed to its peak, pressing straight toward Yang Kai.

Duanmu Qi said casually, "Do whatever you want with the man. Leave the woman to me."

Shen Cuiling immediately pouted, her face full of displeasure. Duanmu Qi smiled and said, "I'm finding you another sister to share your burdens. What is there to be unhappy about?"

Shen Cuiling also knew she could not go against him. Men were always fickle, and as long as she could cling to him, that was enough, so she said faintly, "Then I have to be the elder sister."

"As you wish, as you wish, all as you wish." Duanmu Qi laughed.

Only then did Shen Cuiling break into a smile, but the glance she inadvertently cast at He Yunxiang held some rejection and disgust. After today, another woman would be competing with her for favor. Just thinking about it made her unhappy.

"Old Ancestor, be careful. This person seems to have cultivated Space Divine Abilities."

"Space Divine Abilities." Yan Renhao's expression sharpened, then he said proudly, "So what?"

Before absolute strength, what could Space Divine Abilities accomplish? Without another word, he shouted, "Die!" His figure moved like lightning, and in the blink of an eye he pounced in front of Yang Kai. He did not use any Secret Treasure or Secret Technique. One hand formed an eagle claw and grabbed at Yang Kai's crown. Origin Qi surged over his fingertips with hissing sounds, and all five fingers seemed to become sharp weapons.

From the very first move, he meant to put Yang Kai to death and repay the humiliation the Yan Family had suffered that day.

He Yunxiang cried out in alarm. The moment a Dao Origin Realm cultivator made a move, his extraordinary nature was displayed. She had not even seen his movements clearly. If she were in Yang Kai's place, she feared she could not avoid this move. Her beautiful face instantly lost color, and she was about to drive the Star Shuttle away with Yang Kai.

She dodged away, but Yang Kai remained standing where he was, unmoving.

Dang...

The sound of metal striking metal rang out, clear and pleasant to the ear.

The viciousness on Yan Renhao's face instantly froze, then turned to shock. He stared ahead in disbelief.

His move had struck its target squarely, without the slightest deviation, but the imagined scene of a skull shattering and brain matter splashing out did not appear. The sensation from his hand did not feel like he had grabbed a person's head. Instead, it was as if he had grabbed some extremely sturdy metal, leaving only a few faint white marks on the other party's forehead.

What kind of head was this? How could it be so hard!

Not daring to be careless, he released Origin Qi from his palm, and violent power burst out with a roar.

Yang Kai's head snapped back from the blow, then bounced right back like a roly-poly toy. His eyes were filled with mockery as he stared straight at Yan Renhao, who was close at hand.

Yan Renhao's heart suddenly sank. Before he could react, Yang Kai had seized his hand.

In an instant, an enormous force came from his wrist. With a series of crisp cracks, the bones in his wrist were crushed into powder.

He was no match for him! Absolutely no match!

Had this person really come from the Wuji Star Domain? How could such a monster exist in a Star Domain? Yan Renhao was scared out of his wits. He opened his mouth and spat out a ray of light, which transformed into a small sword, but instead of attacking Yang Kai, it circled around his own wrist.

His hand separated from his arm at the wrist, fresh blood gushing out, but he used the chance to break free from Yang Kai's restraint. What kind of resolve did it take for a man to sever his own wrist? Yet he knew that if he did not do this, he was probably not far from death.

For him to have cultivated all the way to this point, he had relied not only on talent and luck, but also on his own keen intuition.

Seeing Yang Kai step through the air toward him, refusing to let him off and moving one-third faster than he was, Yan Renhao was immediately alarmed. Retreating as he hurriedly shouted, "Friend, stop! I feel there seems to be some misunderstanding about today's matter. It must be that my Yan Family descendants were blind and offended Your Excellency first. This Yan must thank Friend for giving them a light punishment and teaching them how to conduct themselves. I have yet to ask Friend's honored surname and great name."

He spoke urgently and quickly, yet with clear logic. If he did not speak quickly, he feared he would have no chance to speak at all.

"Die."

I gave you a chance. Since you did not know how to cherish it, go to the underworld and regret it there.

A punch blasted out, simple and unadorned, without even the slightest fluctuation of Spiritual Qi.

Yan Renhao, who bore the brunt of it, went ashen and shouted, "Senior Brother Duanmu, save me!"

The instant his words fell, the fist struck him squarely. Without making a sound, he burst into a cloud of blood that bloomed in midair, visible even from ten li away, exceedingly bewitching.

Yang Kai reached both hands back and smoothed down the hair Yan Renhao had just messed up, then turned his head to look at Yan Luo.

Yan Luo's mouth hung open, and he had yet to recover from the blow of watching his Old Ancestor be defeated and killed.

It was too fast. From when Old Ancestor attacked to when he burst into a blood cloud, only three breaths had passed from beginning to end. The scene he had expected, of Yang Kai dying and washing away his former shame, had not appeared. Instead, his own Old Ancestor had died and his Dao had vanished.

He simply found it impossible to accept. The shock to his mind was so great it was as if someone had taken a heavy hammer and struck him violently. His heart clenched, and he could barely breathe.

Sensing that gaze stab toward him, Yan Luo trembled all over. He felt as if his whole body had been wrapped in an aura of death, and instinctively flashed behind Duanmu Qi, searching for a thread of security.

"You actually dare kill someone from my Sea Hall!" Duanmu Qi was no longer in the mood to flirt with Shen Cuiling. Yan Renhao had been killed in the blink of an eye, and he had not even clearly seen what had happened. He immediately shouted in anger, "You deserve death!"

The facts were just as Yang Kai had previously expected. Although Sea Hall had some influence in the Ancestral Domain, it was not among the very top Sects. It did not have many Dao Origin Realm cultivators, and the death of any one of them was a massive loss. Duanmu Qi had thought this would be only a small matter, easily handled, but it had actually caused the fall of an expert from the Hall. If he returned like this, he would definitely be punished.

If he wanted to avoid punishment, it was simple: kill this murderer and make up for his mistake with merit.

"Is there something wrong with this fellow's head?" Yang Kai turned his head and looked at He Yunxiang.

He Yunxiang pursed her lips and smiled. "I fear the illness has reached the marrow. Even immortals would find him hard to save."

After personally seeing someone above the Void King Realm fail to last even one move in Yang Kai's hands, the bit of worry He Yunxiang had felt before instantly vanished like smoke. She would never be able to measure just how powerful this Sir was.

"Since that's the case, I'll send you on your way!" Yang Kai's figure flickered, and he suddenly appeared in front of Duanmu Qi. Though his figure was not especially tall, it seemed capable of covering the sky and blocking out the sun, instantly cutting off the light before Duanmu Qi's eyes.

Only then did Duanmu Qi come back to his senses. Instinctively sensing danger, how could he not know disaster had arrived?

He did not panic, though. He reached into the Void with one hand, and a long sword leisurely appeared in his grasp. It was impressively a Lower-Level Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure. Layers of cold light rippled over the long sword, and blossoming sword flowers transformed into a sword net that instantly enveloped Yang Kai.

Yang Kai raised his hand high, and a sword also appeared in it.

However, this sword was not quite the same as ordinary long swords. It was extremely broad, broad enough for an ordinary person to use with both hands. A single glance revealed that it was extremely heavy, yet Yang Kai held it lightly, without the slightest strain.

There was no gorgeous move. The Myriad Sword simply chopped down.

The sword net filling the sky instantly collapsed. With a crisp clang, Duanmu Qi seemed to be struck by lightning and spat out a mouthful of blood. The Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure long sword in his hand actually snapped in two from the middle.

The Myriad Sword's remaining momentum did not diminish as it cleaved down.

Duanmu Qi seemed to have been struck by a body-binding spell. He stood blankly in place, motionless, his eyes vacant as he looked at Yang Kai. Only after a long while did he open his mouth and say, "You..."

With a wet clatter...

His body split down the middle, left and right halves perfectly symmetrical, while blood and viscera scattered across the sky.

After a brief silence, sobbing came from the side. Shen Cuiling's face was covered in tears, her complexion pale, her whole body trembling as she looked at Yang Kai. Her pupils had dilated to their limit, as if the one standing before her were not a person, but a vicious fiend that chose people to devour.

Two First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivators had not lasted two moves in this young man's hands, and were crushed to death like ants.

Her heart was filled with endless regret. Had she known this would happen, she would never have come with Duanmu Qi.

When Duanmu Qi died, blood sprayed everywhere. She had been standing close by and was immediately transformed into a blood-soaked figure. Blood mixed with tears slid down her cheeks and flowed into her mouth, both fishy and salty, making her want to vomit.

Yang Kai, however, did not look at her. He merely watched Yan Luo, who had originally been hiding behind Duanmu Qi, with a beaming smile.

Yan Luo was also covered in blood at this moment. The panic and horror in his eyes almost overflowed, and deep in his heart he wailed without end.

How could this be? How could he be so powerful? Were there people this strong in this world? When Yang Kai made his move, Yan Luo had barely even sensed any fluctuation of Spiritual Qi. It had only been one punch and one sword, yet his own Old Ancestor and that Duanmu Qi had died just like that, for no clear reason.

He fled in sorry haste, relying on his attachment to life to overcome the fear in his heart.

Yang Kai smiled as he watched his back.

He Yunxiang said, "Sir..."

She was unwilling to let Yan Luo go. If not for Sir's world-shaking Divine Abilities today, they would probably already have been trapped by Yan Luo. Since the situation had now reversed, how could they show mercy?

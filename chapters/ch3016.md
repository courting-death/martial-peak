## Chapter 3016 — If Only We Had a Dragon Isle Token

"Just you wait. Sooner or later, I'll settle this score with you."

Yang Kai's expression was gloomy, while Li Jiao hid off to the side, silent as a cicada in winter.

For some unknown reason, a month after setting out from the Northern Territory, Yang Kai's temper suddenly took a turn for the worse. Every day, his face was so frosty it seemed one could scrape a layer of ice from it, and he kept muttering curses at someone under his breath.

Li Jiao was deeply uneasy. He thought to himself that he had neither provoked nor offended Yang Kai these past few days, so why had he suddenly become like this?

Fortunately, although Yang Kai was in a bad mood, he did not glare at Li Jiao or snap at him. Even so, Li Jiao remained cautious every day, afraid that he might set off the volcano smouldering inside Yang Kai.

Yang Kai was naturally in a foul mood, but it was not because of Li Jiao. It was because of Luan Feng!

That damned woman had destroyed the Space Array he had arranged in the Wild Ancient Land. Otherwise, he could have travelled straight from Lingxiao Palace into the Wild Ancient Land, then made his way to the Eastern Sea from there.

Although it was still not a short distance, that route would undoubtedly have been far better than the one they were taking now.

It was all because Luan Feng had destroyed his Space Array that he now had to fly to the Eastern Territory. Even if the entire journey went smoothly, it would still cost him a great deal of time.

This left him extremely displeased. He secretly resolved that the next time he saw Luan Feng, he would settle accounts with her properly. It was simply too infuriating.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle was extremely fast. Yang Kai and Li Jiao took turns controlling it, cooperating seamlessly.

Whenever he had nothing else to do, Yang Kai meditated and cultivated. Although his cultivation had encountered a bottleneck, this did not affect his cultivation of the Dragon Transformation Art. He now stood four and a half zhang tall after undergoing Dragon Transformation. Cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art would allow him to integrate the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power with his body to a greater degree, thereby increasing his strength after transforming.

From time to time, he also studied the Laws of Space.

The Space Divine Abilities he currently possessed had basically all been comprehended by himself, and every one of them had unfathomable power. If he could comprehend one or two more, they would be of great help to him.

Although this trip to Dragon Isle was partly the result of a hot-headed impulse, it was also a decision he had made after careful consideration.

Yang Kai knew that the road ahead was fraught with danger and that he might well come into conflict with Dragon Isle. With his current strength, entering Dragon Isle would mean a narrow escape from certain death, but Zhu Qing was there, so no matter what, he could not sit back and do nothing.

The words Zhu Lie had spoken in the Revolving World still echoed in his ears. Yang Kai did not know what Zhu Qing would face after returning to Dragon Isle, but he imagined nothing good awaited her.

For one who stood at the head of all living creatures, foremost among the Divine Spirits, to form an intimate relationship with a human... Yang Kai did not even need to think about it to know that Dragon Isle would never permit such a thing.

If Zhu Qing could not withstand the pressure and was willing to sever ties with him, then so be it. But if she was unwilling, she would certainly suffer.

Would Zhu Qing be willing? Yang Kai had never considered that question, because he already knew what choice she would make without needing to think about it.

Time passed, and the days went by without incident.

Yang Kai made little progress with the Dragon Transformation Art. After all, he had only been cultivating it for a short time. The fact that he could reach a height of four and a half zhang was largely thanks to the Dragon Blood Pills and Zhu Qing's Pure Yin Qi.

If he wanted to improve any further, it would take time, and an extremely long time at that.

They arrived at the Eastern Sea.

Above the sea stretched a boundless expanse, endless to the horizon. Not a single cloud marred the sky, whose azure blue was as clear and pure as if freshly washed.

Even with the aid of the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, it had taken Yang Kai and Li Jiao a full two months to reach this place from the Northern Territory, which showed just how vast the distances were between the Star Realm's great territories.

After entering the Eastern Sea, Li Jiao took control of the Flowing Cloud Shuttle because Yang Kai had no clue whatsoever where Dragon Isle was located, whereas Li Jiao knew its approximate direction.

He knew this precisely because of what Yang Kai had previously guessed. Li Jiao was a Dragon Descendant and possessed the Dragon Clan's bloodline, so he harboured an exceptionally deep attachment to Dragon Isle. Even though he had never entered Dragon Isle, that did nothing to prevent this attachment from growing.

He wanted to enter Dragon Isle, but he also knew that with his meagre bloodline, he was utterly unqualified to set foot there.

Five hundred years ago, he had spent a long time searching the Eastern Sea, trying to discover Dragon Isle's location.

It had taken him a full three years to find the slightest clue, yet even then, he had only learned its approximate direction.

According to him, he had once followed someone into a certain stretch of sea by chance, only for that person to disappear inexplicably after entering it.

Moreover, the person he had followed seemed to possess the Dragon Clan's bloodline.

This discovery had greatly excited Li Jiao. He searched the area for a long time afterward, but never found any other clues and eventually had no choice but to give up.

It was not until Yang Kai came looking for him this time and unearthed the wish he had kept suppressed in his heart that he followed without hesitation.

"This is the stretch of sea where I lost track of that fellow back then." The Flowing Cloud Shuttle had already been put away. Above the vast and endless sea, Li Jiao drew a circle in the air ahead of them and spoke to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai swept his gaze from side to side and frowned. "Are you sure this is the place? There are no landmarks here."

Li Jiao replied, "Although there are no landmarks, I couldn't possibly be mistaken about this. This stretch of sea has always been engraved in my heart."

Yang Kai nodded. Since Li Jiao was so certain, there should be no mistake. "This area is enormous, spanning several hundred thousand li, and we don't have any clues."

"Why don't we forget it?" Li Jiao looked timidly at Yang Kai. Throughout their journey, he had held firm to his resolve to enter Dragon Isle and see it for himself, but now that he had truly arrived at this place, he suddenly felt like retreating.

He was a little afraid, though not of death. What he feared was whether, if they really found Dragon Isle, he would have the courage to go ashore.

When he searched his heart, he found no answer.

"Brother Li, if you wish to return, then go ahead. I intend to search."

As he spoke, Yang Kai took to the air and flew off to one side.

Li Jiao stood where he was and hesitated for a moment before shouting, "Then let's split up and search!"

With that, he began searching in the opposite direction.

Yang Kai's Divine Sense spread out around him like a tide, carefully probing every inch of space. He did not fly quickly, afraid that he might overlook some detail.

In his view, the reason Dragon Isle was so mysterious and no one knew exactly where it was located was simply that some astonishing Grand Array had been arranged to conceal all traces of it.

Since an Array had been used to conceal it, there would be clues to follow. As long as he found the location of the Array, Dragon Isle would surely be there. He did not know much about Arrays, but every Array was bound to produce fluctuations in Spiritual Qi. As long as he caught hold of even the faintest fluctuation, he could follow the trail back to its source.

One day, two days, three days...

Yang Kai gradually expanded the scope of his search, combing through the entire stretch of sea within a radius of a hundred thousand li, yet he still found nothing.

During this time, he met up with Li Jiao several times, but Li Jiao had likewise discovered nothing.

Ten days later, the search area had doubled. After a month, it doubled once more.

Yang Kai's expression gradually darkened.

Could it be that after coming all this way, he would have to turn back without even finding where Dragon Isle was? That would truly be the greatest joke in the world.

When he met Li Jiao again, he frowned and asked, "Are you certain Dragon Isle is in this stretch of sea?"

"Of course I'm certain." Li Jiao nodded repeatedly, only realizing something after a moment. "Palace Master Yang, you aren't suspecting me, are you? You'd truly be wronging me. What benefit would I gain from deceiving you?"

"I'm not suspecting you. It's simply that so many years have passed, and the scenery across the sea all looks the same. It would be understandable if you remembered incorrectly."

"Impossible!" Li Jiao exclaimed with some agitation. "I couldn't possibly remember incorrectly. This is definitely the place."

Seeing him act this way, Yang Kai could say nothing more and merely nodded. Gazing into the distance, he wondered whether he should go to Spirit Beast Island. Although the Beast Great Emperor did not have a good relationship with Dragon Isle, surely there would be no problem if Yang Kai only asked him for directions?

"Alas, Dragon Isle is so mysterious and difficult to find. If only we had a Dragon Isle Token," Li Jiao sighed.

Yang Kai abruptly turned his head to stare at him.

"What's wrong?" Li Jiao asked in astonishment.

Yang Kai asked, "Can a Dragon Isle Token locate Dragon Isle?"

Li Jiao replied, "It should be able to. A Dragon Isle Token is something issued by Dragon Isle, and whoever holds one may make a request of Dragon Isle that lies within their power to fulfil. The Dragon Clan has long remained secluded from the world, so if someone obtained a Dragon Isle Token, they would naturally have to visit Dragon Isle to have their wish granted. How would they enter Dragon Isle? Naturally, they could only rely on the Dragon Isle Token." As he spoke, he let out another dejected sigh. "However, it seems only a scant few Dragon Isle Tokens have ever existed, and apparently, Dragon Isle has already taken them all back. Finding one now would be harder than ascending to the Heavens."

Yang Kai let out a long sigh, raised his hand, and gripped Li Jiao's shoulder, his expression utterly grief-stricken.

Li Jiao was startled. "Palace Master Yang, what's wrong?"

Yang Kai raised his head and forced out a smile uglier than a crying face, gritting his teeth as he said, "Why didn't you tell me such crucial information sooner?"

Li Jiao felt a burst of excruciating pain from his shoulder, as though his arm was about to be crushed. Not knowing what madness had seized Yang Kai, he replied, "You never asked."

He had merely mentioned it in passing and never imagined Yang Kai would react so strongly.

"A Dragon Isle Token, right?" Yang Kai released Li Jiao's arm and flipped his palm. "I have one!"

"What?" Li Jiao was dumbfounded and did not know whether to laugh or cry. "Why didn't you take it out sooner?"

"How was I supposed to know?"

The two stared at each other, both feeling like weeping without a word. They had painstakingly searched this vast sea for a month without finding a single clue, never imagining that the key had been in their hands all along.

"Is this really a Dragon Isle Token?" Li Jiao asked in disbelief. He had just been talking about how rare Dragon Isle Tokens were. As far as he knew, there truly were only a few of them, and most had already been reclaimed. He never imagined Yang Kai would produce one as if performing a magic trick.

Looking over, the Dragon Isle Token appeared plain and unadorned. It was impossible to tell what material it had been refined from, as it was neither metal nor wood. One side was carved with a lifelike giant dragon, while the other bore a single "Dragon" character in bold, flowing script.

Li Jiao stared blankly for a moment. This thing... really did seem to be a Dragon Isle Token! It was not much different from the records he had seen in ancient texts.

"Are you certain this thing is useful?" Yang Kai asked as he looked at him.

Li Jiao replied uncertainly, "Probably... probably."

He could not be certain either, since he had never used one before. However, just as he had said, Dragon Isle's location was mysterious and the Dragon Clan had long remained secluded from the world. If someone who obtained a Dragon Isle Token wanted to visit Dragon Isle, they certainly would not be escorted there by the Dragon Clan, so it was highly likely they could use the Dragon Isle Token to find its location.

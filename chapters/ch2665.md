## Chapter 2665 — Return to the Southern Territory

The palace doors opened, and Yang Kai strode out from within. Xi Lei and the others' eyes lit up at the sight, each of them greeting him with visible delight.

Seeing his energetic and spirited appearance, Xie Wuwei smiled and said, "Young Master Yang, it seems your cultivation has advanced quite a bit during this retreat."

Yang Kai shook his head and replied, "I've made some small gains, that's all, but I still don't know when I'll be able to break through."

After reaching the Emperor Realm, Yang Kai felt more and more that the path to advancement was endless. This retreat had lasted nearly a year, and while his strength had indeed improved, he still couldn't sense the barrier of the Second Stage.

Yingfei smiled and said, "The road of cultivation is long and full of obstacles. This is not something that can be rushed."

Xi Lei nodded in agreement from the side, "Look at all those Emperor Realm cultivators in the world—aren't they all old monsters who have cultivated for hundreds or even thousands of years? For someone as young as you to have achieved this much already, you are truly a peerless talent. Given time, how could you not advance?"

Yang Kai naturally understood this principle as well, and knew that haste would only make things worse in cultivation. So he said no more, only asking, "How is the formation setup going over here?"

Xi Lei hurriedly handed Yang Kai a simple, unadorned token and replied, "That Nanmen Dajun is truly remarkable. He spent two months improving the Grand Guardian Array, and now it's completely upgraded. This is the Restriction Token to control the formation."

Yang Kai took it, sent his Divine Sense inside for a quick inspection, and immediately felt himself connected to certain existences within this world.

With a single thought, the Grand Guardian Array enveloping the entire Lingxiao Palace hummed, and the Spiritual Qi of heaven and earth trembled.

Xi Lei added from the side, "Nanmen Dajun said that the formation he improved this time is even stronger than the one Wenqing Sect had before, so Young Master Yang can rest assured."

Yang Kai casually responded, not doubting it in the least, but looked curiously outside the mountain gate. "Why are so many people gathered out there?"

When he had just inspected the Grand Guardian Array, he noticed quite a few cultivators lingering outside the mountain gate. Their cultivation levels varied, but most were young. Some were in small groups, some alone, all brimming with spirit. Many stared longingly at the Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron standing outside the gate, gazing at the three characters of Lingxiao Palace with eyes full of yearning.

Yingfei explained, "They're all here to knock on the mountain gate and seek entry into Lingxiao Palace, Young Master Yang. The name of Lingxiao Palace has now spread throughout the entire Northern Territory, so for the past half year, many young people have come here hoping for a chance to join. But with the gate closed, they can't enter and can only wait outside. Some have even waited for more than half a year—truly sincere."

"So that's how it is." Yang Kai nodded slightly, not too surprised. After sweeping the area with his Divine Sense, he didn't pay it much mind.

The name of Lingxiao Palace had truly been established, and with three Yao Kings stationed here, it was a clear sign of strength. As a newly founded Sect, it was the perfect time to absorb fresh blood. How could those wandering cultivators without background or support not be tempted?

Just like Liu Qianyun, who had once been a dominant figure in the Great Wilderness Star Domain, but after coming to this Star Realm, couldn't even obtain cultivation resources. That was the drawback of having no Sect background. If she hadn't met Yang Kai again and been brought into Ice Heart Valley, she'd probably still be worrying about resources, unable to cultivate in peace.

Chi Yue, Ghost Ancestor, and the others were no different.

In this Star Realm, there were countless cultivators in such situations. Not everyone was as lucky as Yang Kai. For them, as long as they could join Lingxiao Palace, even if they never soared to great heights, at least they'd have something to rely on in the future.

The mountain gate of Lingxiao Palace hadn't even opened yet, and it was already having this effect. If it truly opened, the entire Northern Territory would be in an uproar, and the number of people coming would be as numerous as carps crossing the river.

Xi Lei asked, "Does Young Master Yang want to open the mountain gate and recruit disciples widely?"

Yang Kai shook his head, "No rush."

He had taken over this place mainly for the sake of his friends and family from Dark Star. Now, he didn't even know how to return to the Hengluo Star Domain, so how could he open the gate so easily?

Still, since Yang Yan and the Insect Emperor had managed to go to the Hengluo Star Domain back then, he could too—he just didn't know the method yet.

Perhaps, he should try to find news of Yang Yan and ask her about it.

With his thoughts stirring, Yang Kai turned and said, "I'm going to make a trip to the Southern Territory. Which of the three Yao Kings will accompany me?"

"Me!" As expected, the three Yao Kings replied in unison, then glared at each other.

Yingfei came to his senses and said, "Young Master Yang, why only bring one of us? We could all go with you."

Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei also nodded repeatedly, not understanding Yang Kai's reasoning.

Yang Kai explained, "You are Yao Kings. If all three of you travel together, you'll draw too much attention and might cause trouble. Besides, I need someone to stay behind and guard this place. Even though Lingxiao Palace has the Grand Guardian Array and the gate is closed, who knows if some fools will come looking for trouble? If you all leave, it won't be safe here. Since I founded Lingxiao Palace, I plan to settle down here in the future. There's no rush—what are you worried about?"

The three of them, hearing this, found Yang Kai’s reasoning quite sound. After all, they had already boarded his ship and become Honored Elder of Lingxiao Palace. If they pressed too hard, it would only make them seem overly eager for quick results, and might even provoke Yang Kai’s displeasure.

Exchanging glances, Xie Wuwei smiled and said, "Then Young Master Yang, just choose one of us. No matter who you pick, we have no objections."

Yingfei and Xi Lei nodded vigorously from the side.

If they had to decide among themselves, they'd never agree, so it was better to let Yang Kai choose—no one could complain then.

Yang Kai nodded, "Then it'll be Yingfei. Yao King Yingfei is the fastest, and we might need your speed for this trip to the Southern Territory."

Hearing this, Yingfei was overjoyed and cupped his fists, "Young Master Yang is wise."

Xi Lei said gloomily, "Then Brother Xie and I will stay behind to watch the house."

"I'll trouble you both," Yang Kai nodded slightly.

After their discussion, Yang Kai took Yingfei and used the Space Array to make a trip to Ice Heart Valley to bid farewell to Bing Yun.

After a year away, returning to Ice Heart Valley, he found the valley completely renewed. Having endured the previous tribulation, the entire sect was now more united than ever. Most disciples were in secluded cultivation, not daring to slack off in the slightest.

He didn't stay long in Ice Heart Valley—just half a day later, Yang Kai and Yingfei left again via the Space Array.

The distance from the Northern Territory to the Southern Territory was hundreds of millions of li, a long and perilous journey. Even ordinary Emperor Realm cultivators would find it hard to cross. The Space Array couldn't connect everywhere, so much of the way had to be traveled by flight.

Under the clear sky, atop the white clouds.

A giant eagle soared high, as fast as lightning, leaving only a faint trace in the sky—a string of afterimages.

This giant eagle was, of course, Yao King Yingfei's true form. As the overlord of the skies, speed was his forte. Among the Thirty-Two Yao Kings and Eight Saint Envoys of the Ancient Land, if he claimed second, no one would dare claim first.

This was precisely why Yang Kai had chosen to bring him along.

Seated cross-legged on Yingfei's back, Yang Kai found the broad, flat surface spacious enough to hold a house. The flight was fast and steady, with not even a hint of headwind.

In this world, there were probably very few who could make a Yao King willingly serve as a mount.

Yet now, Yao King Yingfei was happy to do so, without the slightest complaint.

The journey was monotonous, so Yang Kai didn't waste time and meditated in cultivation.

The entire way was peaceful. Ordinary cultivators couldn't even sense Yingfei's presence, and those who could didn't dare provoke him, choosing instead to avoid him from afar.

After two full months, Yingfei suddenly sent a voice transmission, "Young Master Yang, we should be in the Southern Territory now. Where are we headed?"

Yang Kai slowly opened his eyes, withdrew his cultivation, stood up, and looked around, "Let's find a city to ask for directions."

Yingfei received the order and began searching below.

Half a day later, a fairly prosperous city came into view. Yingfei immediately flapped his wings and descended.

In a moment, he withdrew his true form, took on human shape, concealed his Yao Qi, and landed at the city gate with Yang Kai.

The two had no intention of entering the city. Yang Kai simply stopped a passing cultivator and asked, "Excuse me, which direction is Thousand Leaves Sect?"

This trip to the Southern Territory was mainly to visit Thousand Leaves Sect. Chi Yue, Ghost Ancestor, and the others were still there. Now that he had founded Lingxiao Palace, he naturally wanted to bring them to the Northern Territory, where the cultivation environment was better and safer than Thousand Leaves Sect.

But the Southern Territory was vast. Even though he had returned, Yang Kai didn't know the exact location of Thousand Leaves Sect and could only ask for directions.

The cultivator was in a hurry to enter the city, but was suddenly stopped and became furious. Relying on his Dao Origin Realm cultivation, he was about to lash out, but then saw the dazzling Upper-Level Source Crystal in Yang Kai's hand. His eyes lit up, and he snatched it, examined it carefully, and, after confirming it was genuine, put it away. He looked at Yang Kai curiously, "Are you a disciple of Thousand Leaves Sect?"

Yang Kai shook his head, "No. Why do you ask?"

The man glanced around cautiously, then lowered his voice, "If you're not a disciple of Thousand Leaves Sect, don't ask too much. Thousand Leaves Sect has gotten into trouble."

"What do you mean?" Yang Kai's expression changed, instinctively sensing something was wrong.

Logically, Thousand Leaves Sect was just a minor, unremarkable sect in the Southern Territory, not widely known. He hadn't expected that the very first person he asked would know so much about it, and even say that Thousand Leaves Sect had suffered a disaster.

The man asked curiously, "Friend, did you just come out of seclusion? How have you not heard this news?"

Yang Kai said in a deep voice, "What exactly happened?"

The man whispered, "Thousand Leaves Sect was destroyed."

"What?" Yang Kai was shocked, hardly able to believe his ears.

The man shook his head and said, "Thousand Leaves Sect overestimated themselves, like an ant trying to shake a tree. They were wiped out over a year ago. Now that the sect has fallen, everyone who once clung to it has scattered. Friend, if you have any ties to Thousand Leaves Sect, you'd best distance yourself quickly, lest you bring trouble upon your own head."

## Chapter 3123 — God

Bian Hong's heart sank. A chill swept from the top of his head to the soles of his feet. What a scheming woman. He had fought her for five years, yet he had never known she was still hiding a trump card like this. Only at the final moment had she revealed it, catching him off guard.

He bitterly cursed himself for being careless, but there was no medicine for regret in this world.

With a quick shift of thought, Bian Hong turned and fled. His voice came drifting back. "Stinking bitch, I will spare you today. Another day, I will make you submit beneath this Lord's crotch!"

Qiu Yimeng lifted her sword and gave chase, but she simply could not close the distance. She had just used a Secret Technique to increase her speed, consuming a great deal of strength, and had exposed an opening to lure Bian Hong into taking the bait. After taking his palm strike, she had also suffered some injuries. With their cultivations so close, if Bian Hong was determined to flee, she truly had no good way to stop him.

In the blink of an eye, the two of them had already crossed above the clouds, and the scenery below was nowhere to be seen.

Qiu Yimeng sent out one last burst of Sword Qi, then stopped.

Bian Hong easily dodged the attack, and before he left, he still did not forget to turn back and throw her a mocking sneer. Fury surged in Qiu Yimeng's heart. Her beautiful eyes fixed hard on Bian Hong's back, as if to carve him into the depths of her memory. One day, she would cut him down beneath her sword.

A figure suddenly appeared a hundred zhang above Bian Hong. Qiu Yimeng did not notice at first, but when she caught sight of him by chance, she could not help frowning and narrowing her eyes for a closer look.

The man stood with his back to the round sun. The golden light was too dazzling, casting his face in shadow and making his features impossible to see, but that figure was so familiar.

Qiu Yimeng's heart leapt violently. That silhouette perfectly overlapped with the shadow lingering deep in her memories.

"Who are you!" Bian Hong was fleeing a hundred zhang up in the sky when he suddenly seemed to sense something. He looked up and was greatly startled. He had no idea when someone had appeared above his head. Taking a closer look, he saw that it was a youth he had never seen before. When he swept him with Divine Sense, he could not detect any energy fluctuations at all.

"Scram!" Bian Hong flew into a rage. Today, he had suffered a huge loss at Qiu Yimeng's hands, nearly capsized in a ditch, and been forced to flee in disgrace. At a time like this, someone actually dared block his path?

He raised a hand and slapped his palm toward the youth. Energy surged in his palm, its might unfathomable.

The youth also raised a palm and gently pressed it downward. His posture was casual, as if he were not facing a Void King Realm cultivator, but a fallen leaf drifting over on the wind. A faint, inscrutable smile appeared on his face. "Go down."

Bian Hong's eyes snapped wide, and his body began to tremble uncontrollably.

That palm looked plain and harmless, without the slightest threat, but when the palm print descended, it seemed able to blot out all Heaven and Earth. His vision instantly went dark, without even the faintest trace of light.

For no apparent reason, Bian Hong felt himself plummeting downward at tremendous speed.

What was happening? Bian Hong's mind went blank. Before he could make sense of it, a sharp force was already attacking him from below. He turned his head to look and was horrified.

How could Qiu Yimeng miss such a perfect opportunity? She immediately wrapped herself in sword light and transformed into a heaven-shocking strike, cutting toward Bian Hong.

Sword light flashed as the two figures crossed. Qiu Yimeng reappeared, her face slightly flushed as she gasped for breath. That sure-kill strike had clearly consumed an enormous amount of her strength.

Bian Hong, meanwhile, clutched his chest, staggering as though he might fall at any moment. He stared at Qiu Yimeng in disbelief and gritted out, "You..."

Blood sprayed wildly between his fingers, soaking his robes in an instant as the stench of blood rushed into the sky.

Qiu Yimeng raised her hand and waved at him.

The life in Bian Hong's eyes rapidly faded, and he plunged headfirst toward the ground, vanishing from sight in the blink of an eye.

The cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain immediately panicked. Their strongest expert, Bian Hong, had actually been cut down so easily. It was almost impossible to believe. They had meant to flee at once, but suddenly felt the space around them become incomparably viscous, giving them the sense that even taking a single step would be difficult.

Tu Feng and the others finally arrived.

Tang Yuxian came to Qiu Yimeng's side and asked with concern, "Miss Qiu, are you all right?" The distance had been too great just now, so they had not known exactly what had happened. They had only seen Qiu Yimeng miraculously cut down Bian Hong, and guessed she must have paid some price for it.

Qiu Yimeng shook her head and pointed a fair hand at the cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain. "Kill them all."

Tu Feng, Tang Yuxian, and the others nodded, then flew out one after another and plunged into battle with the cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain. The result was unexpectedly simple. Opponents who could usually fight them evenly could not display even half their strength today, as if all of them were under some kind of suppression. The moment the many disciples of Lingxiao Sect charged in, they were like wolves rushing into a flock of sheep. Those people from Great Wilderness Star Domain were beaten until they had no power to fight back.

Screams rose and fell in succession, and one blood-soaked figure after another dropped from the sky.

After five years of hostility, they finally had a chance to wipe out their old enemies today. It should have been a tremendous joy, but Qiu Yimeng heard none of it. The sounds of slaughter by her ears grew distant, then gradually faded away entirely. She stared blankly upward, desperate to know the answer hidden deep in her heart.

The figure paced toward her, and Qiu Yimeng's heart beat faster and faster. For some reason, she felt anxious, both hoping and afraid.

At last, he arrived before her. A familiar face entered her eyes, a familiar smile hanging at the corners of his mouth. Memories from several dozen years ago gradually came alive again. It was as if he had stepped straight out of them.

Qiu Yimeng's breath caught, and for a moment she hardly dared recognize him.

They had clearly drawn closer, yet she felt he was even farther away than before. He seemed to be that round sun hanging in the sky, forever beyond her reach. She could see him, but could only feel that warm halo of light, illuminating the space before her while casting an ineradicable shadow behind her.

"Do you not recognize me?" Yang Kai looked at her with a smile.

"Greetings, Sect Master!" Qiu Yimeng gathered her emotions and lowered her head, her eyelashes trembling fiercely, her voice somewhat choked.

She had thought that decades of effort would let her draw a little closer to him, only to discover upon meeting again that it was not so.

Yang Kai froze for a moment, then raised his hand, curled his middle finger, and held it before her forehead. "Raise your head!"

Qiu Yimeng took a deep breath and raised her head.

Dong...

Qiu Yimeng's head snapped back. She covered her forehead with both hands and said angrily, "What are you doing!"

He had actually flicked her forehead with his finger. This was simply too much! Being strong did not mean he could bully people like this. Her eyes could not help reddening. She had waited so many years, and this was what she had waited for?

Yang Kai laughed loudly, “You are still Miss Qiu after all. Just now, I thought some evil spirit had possessed you and I was preparing to harden my heart and slay demons and eradicate evil. It seems that will not be necessary now.”

"You are the one whose body was seized," Qiu Yimeng said indignantly. Seeing that he was still smiling, her anger flared and reckless courage rose with it. She lifted her foot and kicked at him.

Yang Kai flashed aside and dodged.

"You dare!" Qiu Yimeng glared at him and kicked out again.

Yang Kai honestly took the kick. Seeing that Qiu Yimeng’s anger had yet to fade and that she was still glaring at him, he finally cried out, clutched his calf, and hopped up in an exaggerated act, looking as if he was in unbearable pain.

Qiu Yimeng looked sideways at him with an amazed expression that said, "Are you an idiot?" Yet as she watched, her face suddenly flushed.

This... why did it feel like flirting? She no longer dared glare at him.

Warmth filled her heart. No matter how high his cultivation was, no matter how long he had been gone, it turned out that he had not walked too far away from her. She was the one who had insisted on taking a ruler to measure the distance between them.

"All right, you are the master of a Sect now too. If others see this, what will it look like?" Qiu Yimeng could not help smiling.

Yang Kai suddenly grabbed her wrist.

Qiu Yimeng panicked, her thoughts thrown into chaos. "You... what are you doing?"

As soon as the words left her mouth, she blushed bright red, because a pure force had come from Yang Kai's palm and poured into her body. The injuries she had suffered earlier immediately showed signs of improving. She was instantly so embarrassed that she wished she could crawl into a hole in the ground.

He was only treating her injuries. Disappointment rose in her heart.

What had she been expecting?

A moment later, Yang Kai withdrew his hand, took out a Spiritual Pill, and placed it in her palm.

Qiu Yimeng did not ask anything. She tipped her head back and swallowed it, then smoothed her beautiful hair and asked, "When did you come back?" Her expression and tone had already returned to normal. Since they could neither support each other as husband and wife nor forget each other in the rivers and lakes, then they could only get along properly.

"I just came back," Yang Kai said. "Quite a lot has happened here."

"Yes. Have you returned to the Sect?"

"I already have."

"Great Elder should have told you, right?"

"I heard some of it."

"Dark Star is your territory. How do you want to handle this?"

Yang Kai said, "Since they came, they naturally shouldn't think of leaving."

As they spoke, Yang Kai had already begun acting in secret. The Spiritual Qi of the entire world first gave a sudden ripple, and immediately after, the World Laws became incomparably clear.

Qiu Yimeng looked around in stunned silence, faintly sensing a wondrous connection in the unseen.

The star beneath her feet seemed to have come alive.

It had always been alive, and had never died, but ever since Yang Kai left Dark Star, this Cultivation Star had grown somewhat lonely and still, changing bit by bit. If it was a living thing, then during the several dozen years after Yang Kai left, it had been asleep the entire time.

And now, with Yang Kai's return, it had awakened from that long slumber.

The Spiritual Qi of the entire world was filled with an indescribable vitality. Qiu Yimeng could even feel the surrounding Spiritual Qi pulsing joyfully and weaving together.

Between Heaven and Earth, an invisible net seemed to suddenly appear, transforming into a cage that covered the entire star.

Qiu Yimeng seemed to realize something. She turned toward an extremely distant place, Saint Yuan gathering in her eyes, and vaguely glimpsed a sliver of the scene there.

Several thousand li away, high in the sky, the cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain were like fish caught in a net, struggling with all their might to break free from Dark Star's restraints, yet unable to escape no matter what they did.

They could not leave Dark Star, and under the suppression of those omnipresent World Laws, they could not even fully display their own strength.

Dark Star's cultivators, however, suffered no such suppression. They rose up in pursuit and cut down the invading enemies one by one.

This was the power of a Star Lord!

Upon this star, he ruled over all. He was God, and God was him!

## Chapter 2954 — Standing Out from the Crowd

“Make way, make way!”

Just as Yang Kai silently observed his surroundings, a rough shout suddenly erupted from behind him. He turned his head to see a large group of people—over a dozen—emerging from the massive Space Array, each one possessing impressive cultivation.

Among them, one man stood out most prominently. Appearing to be in his early twenties, he possessed striking features and an arrogant demeanor. His phoenix-shaped eyes held an air of lofty superiority, and he wore a flowing moon-white cloak draped across his shoulders.

The rest of the group spread out around him, encircling him like stars surrounding the moon.

As soon as this group appeared, they headed in a certain direction. The leading martial artist cleared the way ahead, rudely shoving aside anyone in their path.

Those who were pushed dared not resist in the slightest. Instead, they hurriedly scurried to the side, trembling with fear.

“It’s His Highness the Third Prince! The Third Prince has returned!”

“Quick, kneel and keep quiet. Don’t even look at him. If he notices you, you’re finished.”

……

With a great commotion, the crowd all dropped to their knees, regardless of age, gender, or cultivation. Within a hundred zhang radius centered on the teleportation array, everyone, no matter what they were doing, prostrated themselves on the ground in reverence.

Yang Kai was dumbfounded.

He curiously sized up this so-called Third Prince, listening to the whispered discussions about him, and wondered what exactly a prince was. Such titles seemed to exist only in the mundane kingdoms. He had rarely heard such forms of address among martial artists.

While everyone around them knelt, only he and Zhu Qing remained standing. Zhu Qing still held a little black dog in her arms, making them stand out even more.

He sighed softly, knowing trouble was brewing. Before he fully understood the situation in this Revolving World and Human Sovereign City, he was unwilling to cause any problems. Yet, life rarely goes as one wishes. Even if he avoided trouble, trouble would still find him.

He glanced helplessly at Zhu Qing, who met his gaze with a smile at the corner of her eyes.

“Hmm?”

The abnormality of Yang Kai and Zhu Qing immediately drew the group’s attention. The leading martial artist strode over to Yang Kai. This man was tall and imposing like an iron tower, exuding a powerful sense of oppression as he looked down at Yang Kai and barked, “Boy, why aren’t you kneeling before His Highness the Third Prince?”

As he spoke, an overwhelming pressure surged from his body, crashing down on Yang Kai.

The burly man possessed First Stage Emperor Realm cultivation and appeared to be a seasoned veteran of countless battles. His Imperial Might carried an overwhelming bloodlust that would normally cause ordinary people to lose their nerve instantly, their will to resist crumbling before they could even mount a defense.

Yet Yang Kai remained unmoved, standing there as if only a gentle breeze brushed past him, his hair fluttering. He looked at the burly man and said, “I’m just a country bumpkin who’s never seen much of the world and doesn’t understand the proper etiquette. Please don’t take offense, sir. We’ll be on our way.” With that, he prepared to leave with Zhu Qing.

Whether it was the Third Prince or any other prince, kneeling was absolutely impossible for him, and even more so for Zhu Qing. As a proud member of the Dragon Clan, she would rather die than kneel to mortals. It was best to leave quickly and avoid further trouble.

Unfortunately, things did not go as he wished. Just as Yang Kai was about to leave, the burly man’s eyes widened and he shouted, “Insolence!” Like a maddened bull, his large palm slammed down on Yang Kai’s shoulder.

With a thunderous boom, Yang Kai's form remained as steady as a mountain. Yet the wild force surged through his body and down into the earth beneath his feet, causing the stone tiles of the ground to shatter and crack in all directions.

Yang Kai rolled his eyes at the burly man and lazily said, “What do you mean by this, sir?”

Only then did the burly man show a look of surprise. He sneered, “No wonder you’re so arrogant. Turns out you’ve got some skill. Too bad you ran into me!” As he spoke, his grip grew even more forceful, veins bulging on his thick neck as he poured all his strength into pressing Yang Kai down.

Yang Kai glanced at the others. The remaining guards all stared at him coldly, as if he had committed some grave offense and needed to be punished.

As for the Third Prince, his expression remained icy, showing little interest in Yang Kai. Only when he saw Zhu Qing did a flash of amazement cross his eyes, but that was all—he paid no further attention.

His gaze pierced through the crowd and across half of Human Sovereign City, fixating on a certain location with a fiery gleam dancing in his eyes.

He remained in place, seemingly just waiting for his subordinates to teach Yang Kai a lesson, so the residents of Human Sovereign City would understand the price of offending him and learn their place.

The burly man continued to exert pressure on Yang Kai, initially confident because he sensed Yang Kai was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Against someone of equal cultivation, he feared no one, let alone this seemingly ordinary youth.

But as time passed and his Emperor Yuan surged, his expression grew uglier. No matter how much strength he used, he couldn’t budge the youth before him. It was as if Yang Kai had taken root in the ground, not even swaying.

After a moment, the burly man’s face turned red and his iron tower-like body began to tremble. He was clearly running out of strength but couldn’t back down, so he stubbornly held on.

He had greatly underestimated his opponent.

Yang Kai’s expression darkened slightly. He looked past the burly man and fixed his gaze on the Third Prince. “Knowing when to stop is a virtue.”

The Third Prince ignored him, his eyes still fixed on a certain spot in Human Sovereign City, his mind completely immersed, oblivious to everything else.

Yang Kai snorted, raised his palm, and struck the burly man.

He had already warned the Third Prince. Since the other party didn’t care, he saw no need to worry about saving face. Whatever consequences came from slapping someone, he’d deal with them later—satisfying himself came first.

Everything happened too suddenly—the burly man had never expected Yang Kai to strike back. By the time the palm came down, it was already too late to defend, and with his mind entirely focused on suppressing Yang Kai just moments before, he had no chance to gather any defensive strength.

The seemingly light slap made the burly man’s face change dramatically. His whole body flew up like a paper kite and crashed heavily to the ground.

The crowd was shocked. Those kneeling on the ground secretly glanced up, and when they saw this scene, they cried out in surprise, clearly not expecting it.

In Human Sovereign City, there was actually someone foolish enough to clash with the Third Prince's guards, and right in front of the Third Prince himself! Such a person must have had their brain rattled loose to commit such a stupid act. It was a pity that the beautiful woman beside him would also suffer the consequences of his idiocy, though what fate awaited them remained uncertain.

Though shocked by Yang Kai’s counterattack, the Third Prince’s guards reacted instantly. Without a word, they surged forward, instantly surrounding Yang Kai and Zhu Qing so tightly that not even a breeze could pass through.

Among these guards, one was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, two were at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and the rest were at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Each possessed deep foundations and could break through at any moment.

Although Yang Kai hadn’t been here long, the number and quality of Emperor Realm cultivators in Human Sovereign City still surprised him. He wondered how the martial artists here cultivated, as Emperor Realm experts seemed common in this place.

Such a lineup of guards was truly luxurious, especially considering that the Third Prince himself was also at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

The many experts surrounded them but did not attack, waiting for the Third Prince’s command. If he gave the order to kill, they would immediately pounce and tear Yang Kai and Zhu Qing to pieces. No matter how strong they were, offending a prince in Human Sovereign City would never end well.

Surrounded like this, Yang Kai remained calm, a cold smile on his lips. Zhu Qing bent down to set the little black dog on the ground and quietly stood by Yang Kai’s side.

“Did you just arrive from outside?” The Third Prince suddenly withdrew his gaze and looked at Yang Kai.

“Is it that obvious?” Yang Kai frowned, surprised that this man would ask such a question.

The Third Prince said indifferently, “If you stay here for a while, you’ll understand that causing trouble with a prince in Human Sovereign City is unwise.”

Yang Kai grinned. “So that’s how it is. The Third Prince truly has sharp eyes. Yes, my wife and I just arrived from outside.”

The Third Prince nodded slightly. “There are rules outside, and there are rules here. I’ll let it go this time, but next time, I hope you’ll adapt to local customs.” With that, he gave Yang Kai a deep look and walked past him.

The guards who had surrounded Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, hearing their master’s words, no longer troubled them. They immediately turned to follow, and even the burly man Yang Kai had sent flying walked past them without a sideways glance, as if nothing had happened.

“By the way.” After walking a dozen steps, the Third Prince suddenly stopped and said without turning his head, “You should know, letting you go isn’t because you showed enough strength. It’s just that this is your first offense, so I’m giving you a chance to correct your ways.”

Yang Kai frowned slightly and mouthed something to Zhu Qing.

Though he made no sound and didn’t transmit his voice, Zhu Qing still understood what Yang Kai meant: This guy is really full of himself.

Zhu Qing covered her mouth and giggled.

With a snap, the Third Prince casually flicked his fingers.

Yang Kai’s expression changed abruptly, and he suddenly let out a muffled groan.

Zhu Qing’s face paled as she stared nervously at Yang Kai. “You’re bleeding!”

As she spoke, she hurriedly took out a silk handkerchief from her Spatial Ring and pressed it under Yang Kai’s nose.

Yang Kai took the handkerchief and held it to his nose, staring in disbelief at the receding Third Prince.

“How are you bleeding?” Zhu Qing frowned in confusion. She had witnessed the entire conflict and hadn’t seen Yang Kai get injured, yet he was now bleeding from his nose. Clearly, something was wrong.

“Let’s talk somewhere else.” Yang Kai’s expression was grave as he pulled Zhu Qing away.

The residents of Human Sovereign City slowly got up and moved aside, avoiding them as if they were venomous snakes or scorpions.

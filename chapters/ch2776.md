## Chapter 2776 — Senior Sister, Don’t Be Impulsive

The Qingyang Shrine Elder’s name really carried weight. If it were anyone else trying to get the Taiyi Divine Water from Zhang Dashan with just a few words, it would be nothing but a pipe dream. Zhang Dashan wasn’t a fool—how could he just hand over such a rare treasure as the Taiyi Divine Water?

It was precisely because Yang Kai held the status of Qingyang Shrine Elder that he was able to earn Zhang Dashan’s trust.

Watching Yang Kai’s figure disappear, Zhang Dashan felt both excited and anxious.

He was excited because this deal might allow him to establish a connection with Master Ji Ying. In the future, if he wanted to request an Emperor Pill, he could get a head start. But he was anxious because he didn’t know if the Emperor Pill he needed to refine this time would succeed.

Alchemy was a technical skill. Even an Imperial-Grade Pill Master could fail, and if that happened, all the medicinal ingredients he’d painstakingly gathered would be ruined. It had taken him five years to collect them all.

Just as he was thinking this, Zhang Dashan suddenly slapped his thigh in frustration. He’d been so excited earlier that he’d forgotten to tell Yang Kai what kind of Spiritual Pill he needed to refine. But then he thought, with Master Ji Ying’s keen eye and alchemy skills, he should be able to tell what kind of Spiritual Pill was needed just by looking at the ingredients.

A single day of anxious waiting felt like a year to Zhang Dashan.

Suddenly, a streak of light shot over from the distance. Zhang Dashan’s spirits lifted as he looked up.

Yang Kai’s figure reappeared. He casually tossed over a jade bottle and smiled, “Mission accomplished!”

Zhang Dashan’s heart pounded with excitement. He caught the jade bottle, opened it, and saw six plump, perfectly round Imperial-Grade Pills lying quietly inside. The fragrance of the pills was intoxicating.

He laughed heartily, his face full of gratitude, and said sincerely, “Many thanks, Elder Yang.”

If Yang Kai hadn’t acted as a go-between, how could he have gotten these Spiritual Pills in just one day? According to his original plan, he would have gone to the Ziyuan Commerce Guild’s main branch to seek out Master Huangfu, offered the Taiyi Divine Water, and then waited several months before possibly getting the Spiritual Pills from Master Huangfu.

Even then, getting three or four pills would have been the limit.

He never expected that in just a single day, his wish would come true—and not only that, he got six pills, each of excellent quality.

No wonder he served under Great Emperor Miaodan. His alchemy skills and abilities left Master Huangfu far behind. Zhang Dashan couldn’t help but feel extremely fortunate. Luckily, Yang Kai had sent him a message yesterday, and luckily, he’d trusted Yang Kai this once. Otherwise, how could such a good thing have fallen into his lap?

For a moment, he was overwhelmed with gratitude toward Yang Kai.

“You’re too polite, Sect Master Zhang. We each got what we needed.” Yang Kai smiled and cupped his fists. “Farewell!”

“Elder Yang, wait!” Zhang Dashan suddenly raised his hand and called out.

Yang Kai turned back and said, “Is there something else, Sect Master Zhang?”

Zhang Dashan hesitated and said, “May I ask, Elder Yang, if I want to request pills again in the future...?”

Yang Kai smiled, “Just come find me at Qingyang Shrine anytime.”

After all, with Ji Ying as a helper, refining Emperor Pills was no trouble at all—and he could even collect a little commission on the side.

Zhang Dashan was overjoyed. “Then I’ll thank Elder Yang in advance.”

Yang Kai nodded, his figure flickering as he retraced his steps.

……

Qingyang Shrine, Lingyu Peak—the spiritual peak where Deputy Hall Master Qiu Ran resided.

“Senior Brother Qiu, I’m here!” Yang Kai called out as he barged in. He soon saw Qiu Ran standing there quietly, as if waiting for him.

“Eh? Senior Brother Qiu, you don’t look so good.” Yang Kai looked at Qiu Ran in surprise.

Qiu Ran’s face darkened. “Didn’t you tell this old man to wait a moment yesterday? That ‘moment’ turned out to be a whole day?”

Only then did Yang Kai realize why Qiu Ran was looking at him so resentfully, as if he’d been abandoned. He scratched his head and laughed awkwardly, “Something came up and delayed me.”

Qiu Ran shook his head helplessly, not bothering to argue. He waved his hand, “Come with me.”

Yang Kai obediently followed.

Following Qiu Ran into a large hall, Qiu Ran waved his hand and the hall immediately closed. Yang Kai looked around and noticed traces of an array set up inside, but couldn’t tell what kind of mysterious formation it was. In the center of the hall was a huge sand table. At first glance, it seemed to possess a hint of spiritual vitality, its surface flowing gently as if alive. But when he looked closer, it was clearly just an inanimate object—strange and fascinating all at once.

Getting closer, Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. “Is this the Qingyang Mountain Range?”

Qiu Ran nodded. “That’s right. This is the mountain wave map of our Qingyang Mountain Range.” As he spoke, he waved his hand, and with Emperor Yuan surging, the sand table came alive. Verdant trees, rolling mountains, jagged rocks—it was a miniature version of the Qingyang Mountain Range. Every peak and every ravine in the real range could be found here.

Yang Kai clicked his tongue in wonder, thinking that when he returned to Lingxiao Palace, he should have Hua Qingsi make a mountain wave map like this too.

Such things shouldn’t be rare in major sects; they just needed to be crafted with care. With Lingxiao Palace’s current resources, it wouldn’t be difficult.

“Our Qingyang Shrine sits within the Qingyang Mountain Range, which is named after the Shrine. There are a total of one thousand three hundred fifty-two spiritual peaks, large and small. Of these, twenty-seven major peaks are available for the elders to reside in. Most of them are currently vacant. Take a look and pick whichever one you like.” Qiu Ran pointed at the mountain wave map.

Yang Kai examined it carefully and asked, “Senior Brother Qiu, the green areas are occupied, right?”

Qiu Ran nodded. “That’s right. The green peaks are occupied, while the white ones are unclaimed.” He pointed to a spot on the map. “This is my Lingyu Peak.”

“Oh?” Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. With Lingyu Peak as a reference, the layout of the map became clearer. Of the twenty-seven main spiritual peaks, about ten were occupied by elders, leaving more than a dozen vacant. Yang Kai looked around, feeling a bit overwhelmed by the choices and unable to decide.

Qiu Ran pointed to a peak. “This one is pretty good. Junior Brother, why not consider it?”

Yang Kai followed his finger, then pointed to a nearby peak. “If I’m not mistaken, that’s Purple Bamboo Peak, right?”

“Yes. That’s Junior Sister Gao’s Purple Bamboo Peak!”

“Forget it, then.” Yang Kai grimaced slightly.

In the entire Qingyang Shrine, the person he feared most was Gao Xueting. Although with his current strength he could probably beat her, being neighbors with Gao Xueting was something he’d rather avoid if possible.

“Hmm… Then take another look yourself.” Qiu Ran didn’t comment further.

After a while, Yang Kai suddenly pointed. “I’ll take this peak.”

Qiu Ran glanced at it and nodded. “Spirit Sword Peak—not bad. It’s just a bit remote and not very large.”

Of the twenty-seven major peaks available to elders, there wasn’t much difference overall, whether in size or the richness of Spiritual Qi. After all, they were all major peaks. Spirit Sword Peak was just a bit farther from the core area of the Qingyang Mountain Range, but for someone at the Emperor Realm, that didn’t matter.

“Since you’ve chosen, Junior Brother, then Spirit Sword Peak it is.” Qiu Ran waved his hand, and the mountain wave map vanished, revealing the sand table again. He continued, “Your Elder Token can activate the various restrictions on Spirit Sword Peak. Once you get there, remember to fuse your Elder Token with the earth vein of the peak. From then on, it will be your territory. Without your permission, not even I can enter at will.”

“Thank you, Senior Brother Qiu.” Yang Kai cupped his fists.

“Go on.” Qiu Ran waved his hand.

Yang Kai immediately took his leave.

Leaving Lingyu Peak, he got his bearings and flew toward Spirit Sword Peak.

After just the time it takes to drink a cup of tea, a tall, steep mountain came into view. At first glance, it looked like a sharp sword thrust into the earth, exuding an imposing aura. That’s how Spirit Sword Peak got its name.

The mountain was towering and precipitous, with clouds and mist swirling halfway up. Many birds and beasts lived within, making it seem like a fairyland.

Yang Kai looked at it from afar and was very satisfied.

Although he was the master of Lingxiao Palace and occupied a vast mountain range, here at Qingyang Shrine he was just a Guest Elder. Still, he now had a place of his own in the Southern Territory.

It was a pleasing sight indeed.

Turning into a stream of light, he flew to the mountaintop. After landing, he looked around and saw several exquisite halls. Though they’d been uninhabited for years and never repaired, the protective array kept them spotless and free of any sign of decay.

Standing there, he released his Divine Sense like a tide, covering the entire Spirit Sword Peak. In an instant, he had a complete grasp of the layout.

Retracting his Divine Sense, Yang Kai strolled around, inspecting his new domain like a king surveying his land.

After wandering for most of the day, Yang Kai returned to the main hall at the mountaintop and was surprised. “Senior Brothers and Senior Sisters, what brings you here?”

Gao Xueting and the others were waiting for him. When Yang Kai returned, Chen Qian smiled and said, “We came to congratulate you.”

Yang Kai chuckled, “Didn’t you already congratulate me yesterday?”

“What? Not happy to see us?” Gao Xueting looked at Yang Kai coldly.

Yang Kai was startled and quickly said, “How could I not? I’m overjoyed! Please, everyone, come inside.”

As he spoke, he invited the elders of the Shrine into the hall.

Before Yang Kai could settle everyone in, Gao Xueting suddenly waved her hand, and over a hundred jars of fine wine appeared on the floor. She declared boldly, “With a new Elder in the Shrine, no one leaves sober today!”

At these words, everyone was shocked.

Chen Qian’s face turned pale. “Senior Sister, don’t be impulsive!”

Di Rong’s expression also changed. He instinctively backed away and said, “I just remembered—the Hall Master needs me for something. I’ll be going now.”

“Hm?” Gao Xueting narrowed her eyes, a note of warning in her voice.

Di Rong froze on the spot. He turned to look at You Kun, hoping for help, but saw that You Kun was sweating profusely, his face completely drained of color.

## Chapter 2893 — Mo Duo Makes His Move

The shamans of the Frost Snow Tribe dispersed, with a third of them sitting cross-legged in a circle around the roots of the Divine Tree, while the remaining two-thirds leapt onto the trunk, vanishing into the various tree hollows.

The only remaining Shaman King of the Frost Snow Tribe nodded at Yang Kai, "Give us a little time!"

As soon as he finished speaking, he soared upward and disappeared into one of the tree hollows.

The Divine Tree had protected the Frost Snow Tribe for tens of thousands of years. They supported and aided each other, so if anyone in this world understood the Divine Tree best and could draw out its power, it would be none other than the Frost Snow Tribe.

At this moment, all of Qing's energy was focused on growing, striving to reach the sky and seal the Boundary Passage, leaving no strength to deal with the nearby Demons.

Nevertheless, he was still an expert on par with a Shaman Saint. The shamans of the Frost Snow Tribe could borrow and even wield his power to resist the enemy, but this was a secret technique of the Frost Snow Tribe, and the price for using it was unimaginably great.

Yang Kai had received Qing's memories and inheritance, so he had some understanding of this secret technique.

Thus, seeing the actions of the Frost Snow Tribe's shamans, he immediately understood their intentions. Turning around, Yang Kai's expression became solemn as he shouted to the remaining members of the Shaman Niu's tribe and the supporting Frost Snow Tribe warriors, rallying their morale.

The Demons' assault grew fiercer, and even with the mysterious Soul-Devouring Worms, the Shaman Niu's tribe struggled to hold on, barely withstanding wave after wave of attacks, each defense coming at a tremendous cost.

Every shaman's face was deathly pale, and they staggered from severe shamanic power exhaustion. Yet they gritted their teeth and chanted incantations, forcing out the last of their shamanic power to unleash spells of varying might, hurling them into the dense ranks of the Demons.

Yang Kai also fought without reservation, constantly unleashing Moon Blades. Each Moon Blade he sent out would slay several hundred Demons at a time.

Suddenly, the massive Divine Tree trembled, and then the shaking grew violent. Leaves drifted down from the sky, and many branches withered at a speed visible to the naked eye.

"Shaman Niu, underground!" came the voice of the Frost Snow Tribe's Shaman King from somewhere on the trunk.

Sand Demons were destroying the Divine Tree's roots underground, hoping to bring down this towering giant by such means.

Yang Kai turned his head, searching through the crowd until his gaze fixed on a figure. He called out in a low voice, "Azak!"

The figure, who had been fighting alongside the Shaman Niu's tribe against the Demons, immediately feinted and withdrew from the battle, then transformed into a mass of loose sand and merged into the ground, vanishing from sight.

Azak, the Sand Demon, was one of the ten Demon Kings subdued by Yang Kai. He was not the strongest, nor the weakest, but he was the best at survival. As a Sand Demon, his innate ability was earth escape, and he was among the six Demon Kings who still lived.

Now, the Sand Demons wreaking havoc underground were his kin, so it was best for him to handle them.

Sure enough, after Azak entered the ground, muffled groans kept coming from below, clearly indicating that the Sand Demons were suffering heavy casualties at his hands, greatly alleviating the Divine Tree's crisis.

But soon, Yang Kai sensed that Azak had met his match—another Demon King from the Sand Demon clan. The two Demon Kings fought a deadly battle underground, and Azak was quickly put at a disadvantage.

Realizing this, Yang Kai immediately took a step back, held his breath, and focused his powerful Divine Sense into a single point, transforming it into an indomitable blade that stabbed fiercely into a spot underground.

Blocked by the thick earth, the power of this Divine Soul attack was greatly diminished, but it still created an opportunity for Azak to kill his enemy.

Yang Kai sensed that, with his help, Azak had slain his opponent.

Looking up again, Yang Kai's expression changed. The Shaman Niu's tribe's defensive circle had been breached once more, and this time it was even worse than before. If the previous situation was like a leaky roof, now the entire roof had collapsed—the defensive circle was in total ruin.

The Demons surged in like a tide, howling as they charged from all directions. No matter how hard the Shaman Niu's tribe fought, they could not stop their advance. Only the spot where Yang Kai stood remained impregnable.

But this was no longer enough. He could defend one spot, but not everything.

"Are you done yet?" Yang Kai shouted harshly, holding the Mountain and River Bell in his palm. If he did not get a definite answer, he would have to launch an all-out, indiscriminate attack. The Mountain and River Bell was too powerful for him to fully control, and if he unleashed its primordial might, even the Shaman Niu's tribe would be affected, possibly even killed by the shockwave.

Yang Kai did not want to stain his hands with the blood of the Barbarian Tribe, so even in the direst moments earlier, he had not considered using the Mountain and River Bell. But now, he could not afford to hesitate any longer.

There was no response. The fastest Demons were already about to reach the roots of the Divine Tree, raising their weapons to strike at the Frost Snow Tribe's shamans gathered there.

The Mountain and River Bell began to glow, its intricate patterns lighting up as an ancient aura spread out.

Just then, the Divine Tree suddenly shuddered, and a branch dropped down from the crown. The branch was of unknown length, as thick as a person, and looked like a giant dragon awakening from slumber, swaying as it descended.

The branch swept across, and every Demon it touched was sent flying. Some even exploded into a mist of blood in midair. Those who survived fared little better—the immense force shattered their bones and caved in their chests, leaving them coughing up blood and barely clinging to life.

In a single strike, over a thousand Demons were killed or wounded.

The Demons who had broken through the defensive circle were shocked, retreating several steps in unison, their eyes fixed in terror and dread on the sweeping branch.

Swish, swish, swish…

More branches dropped from the crown, like long whips wielded by the Divine Tree, able to sweep from sky to earth. The whips danced wildly, seemingly chaotic but in fact highly organized, complementing each other perfectly.

The whip shadows flashed, and with the Divine Tree at the center, everything within a fifty zhang radius was swept clean.

All the charging Demons were flung away, while the members of the Shaman Niu's tribe and Frost Snow Tribe standing guard nearby were completely unharmed.

Seeing this, all the Barbarians were greatly encouraged, and Yang Kai immediately put away the Mountain and River Bell. He knew that the Frost Snow Tribe's shamans had begun to control the Divine Tree's power.

With such overwhelming power, the Demons could not hope to approach again. The Divine Tree was, after all, an existence on par with Saints—its power was not something ordinary Demons could withstand, not even Demon Kings.

Unless…

Suddenly, a gigantic palm appeared in the sky, its imprint vast enough to block out the heavens and obscure the view. As it appeared, the wind and clouds changed, the world trembled, and it descended straight toward the crown of the Divine Tree, as if to destroy it here and now.

The whips that had been sweeping through the Demons clearly sensed something and all turned to meet the palm in the sky.

Yang Kai also looked up sharply, and in the next instant, his figure vanished from where he stood.

With a thunderous boom, the massive palm struck down, shattering dozens of whips that rose to meet it. Muffled groans echoed from within the trunk, indicating that the Frost Snow Tribe's shamans had suffered heavy injuries.

Blocked by the dozens of whips, the palm's descent slowed, but it did not disappear.

At the critical moment, Yang Kai's figure suddenly appeared between the palm and the crown, an ancient bell spinning before him, growing to enormous size and enveloping the entire crown.

Clang…

A piercing sound rang out as the Mountain and River Bell's phantom shattered. Yang Kai could not help but spit out a mouthful of blood, his whole body struck as if by lightning, plummeting to the ground like a falling star.

But after being repelled by the Mountain and River Bell's power, the giant palm finally vanished.

"Oh?" a surprised voice suddenly sounded, and then a man appeared out of nowhere in midair. He looked down at Yang Kai, then up at the Divine Tree, a playful, mocking smile curling at his lips.

Yang Kai steadied himself in the air, his gaze icy as he glared at the man, gritting his teeth and growling, "Mo Duo!"

His voice was not loud, but in the sudden silence of the battlefield, it reached everyone's ears. The name Mo Duo made all the Barbarians' faces change, while the battered Demons began to roar, their confidence restored.

"You… what was your name again?" Mo Duo stood with his hands behind his back, tilting his head as he studied Yang Kai, a look of serious thought on his face. After a moment, he said, "Right, you're Shaman Niu!"

Yang Kai grinned, his bloodstained mouth making the smile look ferocious and terrifying. "To have a Demon Saint remember my name is truly an honor."

Mo Duo chuckled, "I always remember those who stand out."

Yang Kai glanced at another distant battlefield and said with a curl of his lip, "Shouldn't the Demon Saint be fighting over there? What brings you here? Aren't you worried about your own companions?"

"Their lives and deaths are none of my concern. You, on the other hand, should worry about yourself."

Yang Kai cursed inwardly, wondering what Xu and the others were doing that had allowed a Demon Saint to slip over here. Originally, as long as the four Saints of the Barbarian Tribe could hold back the five Demon Saints, there was still hope for this battle. The Shaman Niu's tribe had escorted the Divine Tree here to take root, and over a thousand shamans from the Frost Snow Tribe had come to support them. As long as they had enough time, the previous plan could be fully carried out.

Yet Mo Duo had suddenly arrived here. It was like a group of children fighting in high spirits when a burly adult abruptly stepped in—one hand alone was enough to sweep across the entire field.

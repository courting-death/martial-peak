## Chapter 3108 — Su Yan in the Ancestral Domain

Of course, if one wanted to avoid damaging the Star Domain too badly, it was still best not to reveal too much strength. Only someone like Wu Kuang, whose aim was to devour, would act without the slightest restraint.

Outside the bamboo house, Liu Yan sat on the Star Shuttle, cupping her delicate cheeks in both hands as she waited. The solitary peak was reflected in her clear eyes, down to the finest detail.

"Sir really picked you up?" He Yunxiang reached out and rubbed her head. A little girl so exquisitely carved from powder and jade was always lovable, and her soft, smooth hair made one even more reluctant to let go. Even playing with it for a whole day and night would not grow tiresome.

"Yes." In a sense, saying she had been picked up was not wrong. If she had not met Yang Kai, she would probably still be an Artifact Spirit trapped inside a Refining Furnace. Now she had her own body, and had even received the inheritance of the True Phoenix Fire. Her opportunities could hardly be called small.

"Who is Sir meeting inside?"

Liu Yan shook her head. She did not know who the person inside was either, but she could sense that they were very strong, so strong that even if she and Yang Kai joined forces, they might not necessarily win. Suddenly, she raised her head, looked at He Yunxiang, and said, "Do you want to become Master's woman?"

"What?" He Yunxiang blushed. Was that something a child should be asking? She crouched down and said earnestly, "Who taught you these things?"

Liu Yan said, “Then I am afraid you have little chance. My Master already has several wives.” Turning her head, she looked ahead.

Yang Kai pushed the door open and walked out. He stood in place, stroking his chin and thinking for a long time before his figure flickered and landed on the Star Shuttle.

"Sir, shall we continue?" He Yunxiang asked.

"Let's leave first." Yang Kai waved his hand and sat down cross-legged on the Star Shuttle. Seeing Liu Yan staring at him so pitifully, he helplessly gathered her into his arms.

Only then did Liu Yan smile and sit obediently.

The Star Shuttle turned into a streak of light and soon vanished.

A voice came from inside the bamboo house.

"This old man has lived wisely all his life, yet I never imagined that in the end I would actually have to join you in the filth."

"Hmph. Those who accomplish great things do not fuss over trifles. When that day comes, you will know your choice was not wrong."

"I hope so."

Calm descended once more, but the bamboo house was already empty.

...

"This way!" One day later, Yang Kai suddenly opened his eyes and pointed in a direction, a hint of excitement in his gaze.

In the unseen depths, he had caught hold of a barely perceptible trace, like a thread that might snap at any moment, or a lamp swaying in wind and rain, giving off a faint, almost negligible light.

Yet it was precisely this extremely faint, almost imperceptible connection that allowed him to determine the way forward, the way back to his homeland!

The Star Chart in his Sea of Consciousness was undoubtedly the Star Domain Origin. One day was nowhere near enough to refine it, but Yang Kai had tried many times to communicate with it, and finally received some response.

As long as he followed this perception, he would definitely find the road home. It was like a kite released high into the sky, while the end of its string remained in the hand of the person flying it.

Face after face flashed through his mind: Su Yan, Xia Ningshang, Shan Qingluo, Xue Yue... even many faces whose names he had almost forgotten. At this moment, they all seemed ready to leap out of his mind, as if wanting to say something to him.

An extremely complicated feeling suddenly surged in his heart, as if a wave of heat rolled through his chest and coursed around his heart.

Liu Yan seemed to sense something. She settled into his arms, stretched out a small hand, and laid it over his chest, feeling the violent heartbeat within.

The perception coming from the Star Chart was still very faint, intermittent and broken. Yang Kai constantly directed their course, while He Yunxiang urged the Star Shuttle onward.

One month later, above a stretch of wilderness.

A splendid, multicolored Star Path exit stretched across the air more than ten zhang above the ground. Nearby, several thatched huts stood scattered about. Inside them, cultivators sat meditating, clearly disciples stationed here by various Sects, waiting only for someone to appear from the exit so they could go recruit them.

In the Ancestral Domain, almost every Star Path entrance had a scene like this. The Sects in the Ancestral Domain seemed to look very favorably on cultivators who came from Lower Plane Star Domains. This was especially true of cultivators who had originally come from Lower Plane Star Domains themselves; many of them even took on this task of their own accord. That way, if they encountered a cultivator from the same Star Domain, they would hold a great advantage in recruiting them.

However, among Star Path entrances, some were bustling with activity while others were so deserted one could snare birds at the door. The main reason for this was the number of Star Domains connected to each entrance. Some entrances connected to a dozen or more Star Domains, while others connected to only a few. Naturally, the number of cultivators who walked out from them was different, and the attention they received varied greatly as well.

The Star Path exit before them undoubtedly belonged to the latter kind. It likely connected to only a scant few Star Domains, and among the cultivators stationed here, Dao Origin Realm masters were rare; they were all Void King Realm cultivators without exception. From this, it was clear this place was not valued very highly.

But in Yang Kai's eyes, this Star Path exit was the most beautiful thing in the world.

He immersed his mind and communicated with the Star Chart once more, discovering that this connection did indeed point into the depths of the entrance.

This was it!

In the Ancestral Domain, there was actually a Star Path connected to Hengluo Star Domain. This truly delighted Yang Kai beyond expectation. After all, when he had left back then, he had never heard of any Ancestral Domain, nor had there been any rumors about such a thing in the entire Hengluo Star Domain.

The doors of several thatched huts opened, and some cultivators walked out, lifting their heads to look toward Yang Kai and the others. One of them cupped his fist and said, "Friend, where have you come from, and where are you going?"

Yang Kai was in a fairly good mood and waved his hand. "Go about your business. We're just passing by."

That person frowned, rather displeased with Yang Kai's attitude, but said nothing more.

The next moment, Yang Kai's Divine Sense surged and probed toward that Star Path entrance. Although this entrance was definitely connected to Hengluo Star Domain, he feared it would still take some effort to return to his homeland through it.

Others would probably need to use extreme power to break open the void, but Yang Kai possessed Space Divine Abilities. As long as he grasped a certain pattern, returning should not be difficult. Shuttling through the void was already an everyday matter to him.

A short while later, he had a plan in mind. He patted He Yunxiang's shoulder and said, "Now you have two choices. The first is to remain here. The Ancestral Domain is not bad. If you stay, you will also have a good future. I will leave you some Spiritual Pills and cultivation materials."

"I choose the second one," He Yunxiang said. If not for everything that had happened during this period, she might really have chosen to stay in the Ancestral Domain, but after seeing so much with Yang Kai, how could she not know that the Ancestral Domain also could not be counted as the very highest world?

The Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm was also nowhere near the peak of the Martial Dao.

She had followed Yang Kai through hardship from Wuji Star Domain to this place, and she did not want to stop here. Without needing Yang Kai to give the second choice, she had already made her decision.

"That is fine too." Yang Kai nodded. In any case, it was merely bringing along one more person; to him, it was not much of a burden.

He was just about to step forward when he suddenly paused. Lowering his head, he looked at the people below and said, "Have any of you ever encountered someone from Hengluo Star Domain?"

During this period, every time he reached a Star Path exit, he had asked this question. Yang Kai was merely acting out of habit and asking casually, without expecting any affirmative answer. In any case, he was about to return to his homeland, and he would soon see the people he wanted to see.

"What are you asking that for?" One person suddenly became vigilant and frowned up at Yang Kai.

It was precisely the person who had spoken with him earlier.

Yang Kai blinked, somewhat stunned. "You have?"

"I have not." That person shook his head.

How could Yang Kai believe him? With a stretch of his hand, that person only felt a sudden suction force, and his body flew forward against his will. Yang Kai seized him directly by the neck, and in an instant his face flushed red and his neck bulged. The Saint Yuan throughout his body had actually gone out of his control, frightening him out of his wits.

Yang Kai put on a fierce, vicious look. "What I hate most is people lying to me. If there is, then there is. If there is not, then there is not. I will give you one more chance. Think carefully before you answer."

Sometimes, acting overbearing was far more effective than being pleasant and amiable.

The moment his strength was displayed, the expressions of those cultivators all changed. Only then did they realize this young man had the strength to put them to death with a flick of his finger.

"There is!" That person struggled with all his might and squeezed a single word from his mouth.

There really was? Yang Kai simply could not believe his ears. He loosened his grip slightly. "When did you see them? What did they look like? Where did they go? Tell me everything in detail. If you hide even the slightest thing, this day next year will be the anniversary of your death!"

"I cannot say exactly when. It was probably twenty years ago. At the time, I was not the one responsible for receiving people either. I only heard later that that Senior Sister came from Hengluo Star Domain."

"Senior Sister?" Yang Kai's expression tightened. "What was her name?"

"Su Yan!"

Yang Kai's mind shook violently. The name that haunted his dreams had suddenly come from a stranger's mouth, and in a place like the Ancestral Domain no less. The impact on him was simply unimaginable, so much so that his mind went completely blank and he actually lost the ability to think.

If any expert launched a sneak attack at that moment, it would succeed without fail.

Liu Yan then asked, "Are you certain she came from Hengluo Star Domain?"

"Certain, because Senior Sister Su did not hide her origins. Instead, she even intentionally spread them around. The Senior Brothers and Junior Brothers all paid attention." What they paid attention to was not only her origin, but also that beauty capable of toppling all living beings.

Yang Kai was thoroughly stunned.

If there had still been some doubt before, then now it could almost be confirmed.

Su Yan was here! Su Yan was in the Ancestral Domain!

This name could not be considered especially unique; it could almost be called ordinary, and there should be plenty of people with the same name. But a Su Yan from Hengluo Star Domain, one who also had the strength to pass through a Star Path, was probably the only one under all heaven.

Twenty years...

Did that not mean Su Yan had arrived in the Ancestral Domain only a few years after he left the Star Domain? How had she found the entrance to the Ancestral Domain? And with her cold and aloof temperament, the reason she had spread word of her origins was probably that she wanted to find him.

She did not know where he was, or even whether he was in the Ancestral Domain. She could only use this method to slightly increase their chances of meeting. If Yang Kai really was in the Ancestral Domain, then after hearing news of her, he would definitely go find her.

Thinking of her wandering alone through a completely unfamiliar world, Yang Kai suddenly felt a pang of heartache and self-reproach.

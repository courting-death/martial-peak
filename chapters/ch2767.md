## Chapter 2767 — The Grand Ceremony

Wen Zishan said, "I wonder what else those twenty-some Emperor Realm cultivators pointed out?"

Xiao Yuyang frowned slightly and, after a moment of silence, said, "Tan Junhao used a Gu technique to control them and make them enemies of Yang Kai. Unfortunately, despite all his scheming, he was killed by Yang Kai. Wu Ming aided his evil and also died violently on the spot."

"Tan Junhao would stoop to such means, so despicable, disgracing the Palace's reputation—he truly deserved to die!" Wen Zishan's expression turned stern as he stared at Xiao Yuyang and said, "So, Yang Kai has helped the Star God Palace clean house. The Palace should be grateful to him, shouldn't it? If that's all, Brother Xiao, you needn't have come just to thank Yang Kai. Elder Yang's actions were for the good of the people, the duty of a hero. The Palace is being too polite."

Xiao Yuyang let out a light breath and said gravely, "Hall Master Wen, Tan Junhao was an Elder of the Palace and a Third Stage Emperor. His death is no small matter—it concerns the Palace's honor!"

Wen Zishan smiled faintly and said, "Of course it's a serious matter, but with the testimony of those twenty-some Emperor Realm cultivators, doesn't that make things clear enough?"

Xiao Yuyang shook his head and said, "I'm afraid Yang Kai will have to come back to the Palace with me."

Wen Zishan looked troubled and said, "That won't do. Yang Kai is now an Elder of our Shrine, and the grand ceremony will be held in a few days. If you take him away, wouldn't our Shrine become a laughingstock? The Palace's honor is important, but so is ours."

Xiao Yuyang said, "Hall Master Wen, you yourself said the grand ceremony is in a few days. Right now, Yang Kai isn't yet an Elder of Qingyang Shrine."

"Who says so?" Wen Zishan grinned, then turned and called, "Qianqian!"

Chen Qian said nothing, but raised her hand and sent out over a hundred talisman messages, which turned into streaks of light shooting off in all directions, vanishing in the blink of an eye. She then cupped her fists and said, "Hall Master, I have informed all the Sects and families of the Southern Territory about the grand ceremony to be held in five days!"

"Good." Wen Zishan nodded slightly.

Xiao Yuyang's face darkened as he said, "Wen Zishan, do you realize what consequences this will bring?"

Wen Zishan's expression also turned serious. He said calmly, "Yang Kai is one of ours. Even if the Palace wants to take him, it must be with our Shrine's consent."

Xiao Yuyang sighed softly, then also sent out a talisman message. He said indifferently, "I've already reported this to the Great Emperor. How to handle it will be for the Great Emperor to decide."

Wen Zishan was clearly determined to use the power of the Sect to protect Yang Kai. Even if Xiao Yuyang fell out with him, he probably couldn't take Yang Kai away. All he could do was report the matter and wait for word.

Wen Zishan's expression finally changed a little, but he still smiled and said, "The Great Emperor is wise and will surely give a fair solution. Since you're a guest, Brother Xiao, why not stay at our Shrine for a few days?"

"Very well, I'll trouble you then." Xiao Yuyang nodded slightly.

"Please!"

……

Inside a side hall on Purple Bamboo Peak, amid a crackle of sound, Yang Kai donned the pitch-black Dragon Armor, his presence instantly becoming more imposing and awe-inspiring.

After the Golden Sacred Dragon Source exerted its presence, Yang Kai found refining this Feihong Black Dragon Armor surprisingly easy. It was even smoother than refining an ordinary Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure. Now that the armor was worn, it fit perfectly and was extremely flexible—even if he used the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, he wouldn't have to worry about it being torn apart.

The only thing Yang Kai found unsatisfactory was that the armor was missing quite a bit around the waist. As an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level Secret Treasure, it had dropped a rank and could only display the power of an Imperial-Grade Lower-Level artifact.

But that was enough.

Yang Kai hadn't bought this armor for its defensive power. He just needed something to cover his body after his form expanded from using Dragon Transformation. Whether it was Imperial-Grade Middle-Level or Lower-Level made little difference to him.

Still, Yang Kai was very pleased with the armor's design. Once it was worn close to the body, it felt light as air. With another layer of clothing on top, nothing could be seen at all.

After inspecting it for a while, Yang Kai sat down cross-legged again and continued refining the Feihong Black Dragon Armor.

Although Purple Bamboo Peak was rich in Spiritual Qi, it was remote and rarely visited.

Gao Xueting did come back a few times, but seeing Yang Kai always in seclusion, she didn't disturb him.

In a flash, five days passed.

At dawn, Gao Xueting pushed open the door and startled Yang Kai.

Looking up, he saw that under Gao Xueting's direction, a group of four or five young women entered in single file. Judging by their attire, they were disciples of Qingyang Shrine.

"Get up," Gao Xueting called to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai stood up and asked suspiciously, "What's this about?"

While signaling to the female disciples, Gao Xueting explained, "We're here to tidy you up. Today is your big day."

Yang Kai snorted, "You make it sound like I'm getting married." Seeing Gao Xueting's cold face, his smile faded and he obediently followed her arrangements.

Yang Kai sat down on a nearby chair, and immediately a female disciple stepped behind him, gently combing his hair. Another girl knelt gracefully at his feet, carefully tidying the stubble on his chin, their movements soft and attentive.

Gao Xueting stood by, explaining the grand ceremony's process and all the things to pay attention to.

Yang Kai asked curiously, "Aren't I just being made a Guest Elder? Why all this fuss?"

He thought it would just be a simple formality, but seeing all this, something seemed off.

"Don't worry about it, just cooperate," Gao Xueting instructed.

Yang Kai responded and sat quietly, closing his eyes to rest and memorizing everything Gao Xueting said.

The female disciples served Yang Kai, occasionally sneaking glances at him in amazement. The Shrine suddenly appointing a new Elder surprised them, especially since this new Elder was about the same age as Senior Brother Xia Sheng. They were even more curious.

After all, Xia Sheng's reputation in the Shrine was extraordinary, and many female disciples admired him. Now, looking at Yang Kai, he seemed no less impressive—perhaps even more heroic. Serving him up close, the girls' hearts raced and their imaginations ran wild.

After a full hour of fussing, Gao Xueting dismissed the female disciples, then had Yang Kai stand and turn around a few times. After looking him over, she nodded slightly.

Wearing a purple robe, Yang Kai looked less youthful and more composed—finally resembling an Elder.

"Let's go," Gao Xueting said, leading the way forward, with Yang Kai following close behind.

She wanted to remind Yang Kai not to be too nervous, but a sweep of her Divine Sense showed him calm and unruffled, not nervous at all, so she didn't bother.

She knew Yang Kai had seen many grand occasions over the years. Though the ceremony was impressive, it wasn't enough to shake him.

Atop Ten Thousand Saints Peak, people flowed like a tide. Guests from all sides were seated on either side of the plaza according to their status and cultivation. There weren't many—just over two hundred—but these two hundred represented nearly all the Sects of the Southern Territory.

Disciples of Qingyang Shrine bustled about, running between seats, pouring wine, serving delicacies and immortal nectar. Emperor Realm experts toasted each other, making for a lively scene.

So many experts gathered in one place was a rare sight. Even when Xia Sheng was made an Elder, there wasn't such a grand occasion—back then, the Shrine just sent talisman messages to allied forces afterward. But for Yang Kai, the whole Southern Territory knew, and if there'd been more time, even more guests would have come.

At the head table sat Xiao Yuyang and Xiao Chen, both Elders of Star God Palace, with Xiao Yuyang himself a Third Stage Emperor. Naturally, many came over to toast them, but after Xiao Yuyang casually responded to a few, he simply closed his eyes, making the rest too embarrassed to approach any further.

No one dared complain.

Suddenly, three drumbeats sounded and the whole place fell silent. The guests quickly took their seats.

All eyes turned in one direction.

There, two teams of martial artists flew in—one male, one female. The men were all handsome and heroic, the women beautiful and refined, all dressed in white, each with Dao Origin Realm cultivation and pure, powerful auras.

With the two teams leading the way, a splendid Multi-deck Ship followed. On the deck, Yang Kai stood at the bow, his long hair flying in the wind, exuding an air of riding the wind itself. Gao Xueting stood not far behind him, her expression solemn.

Landing atop Ten Thousand Saints Peak, the two teams immediately split to either side. The Multi-deck Ship came to a steady stop, and Yang Kai and Gao Xueting flew down together.

Instantly, countless eyes turned to Yang Kai, all curiously sizing him up.

A guy named Yang Kai had killed a Star God Palace Elder and Deacon. This had been the talk of the Southern Territory for days. Even those who hadn't known now did, and he was the star of today's grand ceremony at Qingyang Shrine.

Those who didn't know Yang Kai were naturally curious—what kind of bold man would dare attack a Star God Palace Elder?

At a glance, they saw Yang Kai was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm and couldn't help but look disappointed, thinking the rumors must be wrong. How could someone of this level kill Tan Junhao?

He probably couldn't even take a single move from Tan Junhao.

Some even glanced cautiously at Xiao Yuyang, now also a Star God Palace Elder, curious how he'd react to seeing Yang Kai. But to their disappointment, Xiao Yuyang kept his eyes closed and said nothing.

What was Qingyang Shrine doing? Appointing someone who killed a Star God Palace Elder as an Elder—were they looking for trouble? Many couldn't understand.

The grand ceremony was actually not complicated. After all, everyone here was a martial artist—straightforward and decisive, without many formalities. Under Gao Xueting's guidance, Yang Kai walked forward step by step, ignoring the curious stares from both sides, until he stood before Wen Zishan.

Wen Zishan smiled, extended a finger, and pointed above Yang Kai's head, then began to recite, one by one, the rights and duties Yang Kai would have as an Elder of Qingyang Shrine.

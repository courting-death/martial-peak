## Chapter 3107 — Suddenly Seeing the Light

"Young Friend, please promise me."

"Good! Since Senior has spoken, this Junior will naturally comply."

Only then did Duan Hongchen nod. "Cultivate well. With your aptitude, perhaps you can catch up. When that future comes, you and I may yet have a day when we fight side by side."

Yang Kai's spirit shook. Fighting side by side with a Great Emperor, what heroic grandeur was that? But very quickly, he felt puzzled again. Who was the enemy? Could it be Wu Kuang? He kept feeling something was wrong, and Duan Hongchen's words also seemed extremely heavy.

He chatted idly with him for a while longer, telling him about Qingyang Shrine's recent situation and the later developments on Dragon Isle. Duan Hongchen gave a long sigh. "A Dragon Clan member died. That is truly a pity." Then his expression changed, and he cursed, "What is there to pity? The Dragons all deserve to die."

"Can you shut up?" Yang Kai looked at him with a face full of disdain. He had been having a perfectly good chat with Duan Hongchen, so what had Wu Kuang come out for?

Wu Kuang smiled, unexpectedly carrying a somewhat sinister charm. He lifted his eyes and glanced outside, then asked, "Where is that Stone Puppet Incarnation of yours?"

"What for?" Yang Kai looked at him warily. It was not strange for Wu Kuang to know about the Stone Puppet Incarnation. After all, they had met face to face before, and both cultivated the Heaven Devouring Battle Art, so they could sense each other.

"This Lord's Heaven Devouring Battle Art is actually not suited for humans to cultivate, but it complements that Stone Puppet perfectly. This Lord had thought that if you brought that Stone Puppet Incarnation with you, I would give it a gift. Since it is not nearby, forget it."

Before he could finish speaking, the Incarnation had already appeared.

Fortunately, after the Incarnation entered retreat and devoured Shi Huo's Source, it could already transform to the size of a human. Otherwise, this room definitely could not have contained it.

"Space Divine Ability, truly marvelous." Wu Kuang praised. Even he had not discovered exactly how Yang Kai had brought the Incarnation out. He only vaguely guessed that this boy probably had some kind of Space-type Secret Treasure on him.

As soon as the Incarnation appeared, it sensed Wu Kuang's aura and immediately seemed to be facing a great enemy. Scarlet flames flickered and danced in its eye sockets.

"He said he wants to give you something," Yang Kai said.

The Incarnation said in a buzzing voice, "What thing?"

Yang Kai shrugged.

Wu Kuang smiled faintly and reached out for the Incarnation. The Incarnation hesitated for a moment but did not resist. In the next instant, Wu Kuang's large hand pressed onto the Incarnation's sharply angular head, and a strange wave of energy rippled out. The Incarnation froze in place.

Yang Kai appeared calm on the surface, but in truth he was incomparably vigilant.

Who the hell knew what Wu Kuang was trying to do? Yang Kai was ready to act the moment he sensed anything wrong, but from beginning to end, there seemed to be nothing amiss with the Incarnation.

A short while later, Wu Kuang withdrew his hand. Yang Kai looked toward the Incarnation and saw that it actually wore a thoughtful expression.

Yang Kai's Divine Sense stirred, and he immediately understood what the Incarnation had obtained from Wu Kuang. After all, the Incarnation had been transformed from his Soul Incarnation, so there was not the slightest barrier in communication between them. If Yang Kai wished, he could even sense everything the Incarnation felt.

All kinds of essences and cultivation insights for the Heaven Devouring Battle Art!

Wu Kuang had actually poured his entire life's experience cultivating the Heaven Devouring Battle Art into the Incarnation. This gift could hardly be called small. The Heaven Devouring Battle Art had originally been created by Wu Kuang, and he held absolute authority over it. The experience and cultivation insights accumulated over tens of thousands of years were simply an unimaginable fortune.

Just like that, he had said he would teach it and then taught it, without any reservation at all!

Even though Yang Kai had always held extremely deep hostility toward Wu Kuang, at this moment he could not help being somewhat moved.

Could this be hush money? Only, was the price not a little too great?

It was easy to imagine that after obtaining these essences and cultivation insights, the Incarnation's future cultivation road would surely be smooth and unhindered. Perhaps... it could reach the height of the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor, or even surpass it. After all, it was itself a Stone Puppet and had devoured Shi Huo's Origin Power, so its starting point was much higher than Wu Kuang's.

Perhaps seeing Yang Kai's suspicion and shock, Wu Kuang chuckled and said, "Perhaps one day, we can all turn from enemies into friends."

"Do not even think about it!" Yang Kai shouted, though his tone could not help softening considerably.

There was nothing he could do. After accepting someone's generosity, it was hard to keep treating him coldly. Yang Kai was not shameless enough to take such a great benefit and immediately keep giving him a hostile face. Still, this so-called turning from enemies into friends was probably impossible. In the Star Realm, Wu Kuang was hated by all, and as long as he remained inside Duan Hongchen's body, Yang Kai could never truly call him a friend.

People said shared interests could make enemies temporary allies, but Wu Kuang's crimes had long since gone beyond anything profit could excuse. Befriend him? Yang Kai would have to be out of his mind.

"Nothing is absolute. We might as well wait a hundred years, a thousand years, and look again." Wu Kuang did not take it to heart either.

Yang Kai put away the Incarnation, grinned, and said, "That depends on whether you can live until then." His gaze roamed around Wu Kuang's neck with ill intent, as if he were considering where it would be best to start.

His expression changed, and he suddenly became extremely solemn again. "Wu Kuang, since you have some thought of mending your ways, this Young Master does not mind giving you a chance to set aside your crimes and redeem yourself."

Wu Kuang said with a smile, "If there is something, say it and let me hear."

Yang Kai said, "I want to return to my homeland, but I cannot find a way. You know this area well. Tell me, how can I return to my own Star Domain?" This was the real reason he had still barged in despite clearly knowing Wu Kuang was here.

Back then, this fellow had brought chaos to countless Star Domains, so he was clearly well versed in this path. A little guidance from him would definitely be better than searching aimlessly like this.

"Which Star Domain are you from?" Wu Kuang asked.

"Hengluo Star Domain!"

Hearing this, Wu Kuang shook his head. "Never heard of it." The Star Domains he had been to were too many to count, but he had never been idle enough to deliberately inquire into their names. To him, all Lower Plane Star Domains were merely sources to supplement his strength.

Yang Kai could not help looking disappointed. Thinking about it, that made sense. If Wu Kuang had truly been to Hengluo Star Domain, how could there still be any Hengluo Commerce Guild in that Star Domain, or any Purple Star and Sword Alliance? They would probably have become stores in his belly long ago.

"If you want to return from here to a Lower Plane Star Domain, one method is through those Star Paths. The prerequisite is that the Star Domain has a Star Path connected to the Ancestral Domain. However, judging from how you look, you probably have not found one yet."

"Then what is the other method?"

Wu Kuang smiled and said, "Borrow the strength of the Star Domain Origin."

"Star Domain Origin?" Yang Kai raised his brow, and a vague flash of inspiration passed through his mind. It was very important, but he failed to grasp it.

"Stars have Stellar Origins, and Star Domains have Star Domain Origins! It is just that Stellar Origins are hidden on Cultivation Stars. Anyone, as long as their strength is sufficient, has a chance to refine one and become a Star Lord. A Star Domain Origin, however, is much harder to find. A Star Domain is vast. A single flower or blade of grass, a tree or piece of wood, even an ordinary person you run into on the street, a rabbit running through the wilderness, or an asteroid wandering through the Starry Sky could all be transformed from a Star Domain Origin. Even if ordinary people encounter it, they may not necessarily discover it. But if one can refine it, then one can control the entire Star Domain in one's hand and become that Star Domain's master, its guardian."

Wu Kuang spoke slowly, and Yang Kai listened carefully. The light in his eyes grew brighter and brighter.

Star Domain guardian. This was not the first time he had heard this term. He had heard others mention it before.

Back when he left Hengluo Star Domain and headed to the Star Realm, he had carried one resolve: to become Hengluo Star Domain's guardian.

"If you can become the Star Domain's guardian, then no matter where you are, you can sense that Star Domain's location. If you want to go from the Ancestral Domain to that Star Domain, would you still need to search for it deliberately?"

When that time came, with just a thought, he would be able to grasp the faint and traceless connection between himself and the Star Domain.

Yang Kai said calmly, "So that is how it is. I have received your instruction."

In his heart, he cursed fiercely. He really was an idiot!

If he had not run into Wu Kuang today and heard him say this, he might have forgotten about something in his Sea of Consciousness.

Star Chart!

Within his Sea of Consciousness, he had the Star Chart of the entire Hengluo Star Domain. It was not a Star Chart drawn by an ordinary Star Chart Master for navigation. It was more like a mysterious inheritance.

He had obtained it from a Star Chart Master not long after he first entered the Star Domain from Tongxuan Continent. That time, the Warship met disaster and broke apart, and that Star Chart Master died not far from Yang Kai. Then a streak of flowing light entered Yang Kai's mind. From then on, the Star Chart of the entire Star Domain had appeared inside Yang Kai's Sea of Consciousness.

During those years in Hengluo Star Domain, the Star Chart in his mind had pointed out the way for him many times, allowing him to avoid losing his way in the Star Domain.

There had once been an expert who charged into Yang Kai's Sea of Consciousness with Divine Sense. At first sight of those stars filling the heavens, he cried out in shock as if he had obtained a priceless treasure. Unfortunately, Yang Kai killed him in the end.

If Yang Kai had not dared to be certain in the past what exactly the Star Chart in his Sea of Consciousness was, then now he could almost conclude that it was undoubtedly the Star Domain Origin!

Yang Kai still remembered that when he had just arrived in the Star Realm, by relying on this Star Chart, he still had a faint connection with his homeland. Only, later, as time passed, this connection also broke.

The Star Domain Origin that others found so hard to seek had settled down inside his Sea of Consciousness for so many years. With this, would finding the road home not be simple?

His heart was wild with joy, but not the slightest trace showed on his face. He said indifferently, "One more question."

“Speak.” Wu Kuang seemed to be in quite a good mood today, so why would he fuss over Yang Kai pushing his luck?

"How did you avoid the rejection of the World Laws?" Tens of thousands of years ago, many Star Domains had been destroyed at his hands. Yang Kai did not believe he had accomplished that with peak Void King Realm strength.

Since he could act willfully and run rampant through those Star Domains, he definitely had not displayed only the power of the Void King Realm level. It was very possible that he had used his full strength. But even if he was a Great Emperor, the rejection of the World Laws was no laughing matter. How had he not suffered heavenly punishment?

Wu Kuang said, "Every time this Lord went to a Star Domain, the first thing I did was find that Star Domain's Origin. I stole it or seized it. After refining it, within that Star Domain, you are Heaven and Earth, so how would you be rejected?"

Yang Kai suddenly understood!

It was just like when a Star Lord refined the Origin Power of a Cultivation Star. On his own Cultivation Star, the Star Lord was Heaven and Earth. By the same principle, after refining a Star Domain Origin, the entire Star Domain was under one's control, so how could one suffer rejection?

## Chapter 3096 — Frog at the Bottom of a Well

An expert who stood at the very peak of the Star Domain, a Third Stage Void King Realm master, the Yan Family's Second Elder, had died just like that! Died for no clear reason at the hands of his own people!

Such an outcome was hard for everyone to accept. If Yan Qing had been killed by Yang Kai, then so be it, but from beginning to end, Yang Kai had only gripped his neck and had not dealt him any killing blow. What took his life were Great Elder Yan An's two sword strikes!

Was it because the Great Elder was less skilled and had been used by someone else?

Absolutely not. It was because this young man was too terrifying. With a casual movement, he had toyed with a Third Stage Void King Realm expert in the palm of his hand, using him to kill another Third Stage Void King Realm expert. Such ability was simply unmatched under the heavens.

A favour as small as a sheng of rice must be repaid, and a grudge as slight as a glare must be avenged! Though it was somewhat embarrassing, since the Yan Family had already formed killing intent toward him, then he could only return the discourtesy.

The moment a cultivator picked up a sword, he also had to be prepared to be stabbed by someone else.

Casually tossing Yan Qing's corpse aside as if it were a tattered sack, Yang Kai turned a cold gaze toward Yan An.

Their eyes met, and Yan An abruptly came back to his senses, his heart trembling. Now was not the time for self-reproach or grief. Dealing with the crisis before him was the real priority. He had thought that with the Yan Family deploying such a powerful lineup, they would capture him with ease this time. Yet now, before they had even figured out this young man's background, their own Second Elder was already dead. Their losses could be called disastrous. If something happened to him as well, the Yan Family's strength would fall greatly.

He hurriedly retreated, only for Yang Kai to reach out and grab at him without warning.

That slow, unhurried movement was, in Yan An's eyes, impossible to counter. All the hair on his body stood on end, cold sweat soaked through him, and he shouted, "Family Head, save me!"

Yang Kai's hand closed on empty air. Yan An silently vanished before his eyes, then suddenly appeared more than ten zhang to the side, still looking shaken as he gasped for breath.

"Hm?" Yang Kai raised his brow. He did not look at Yan An, but turned his head toward another direction.

Over there, at some point, another person had appeared, quietly hovering in midair and looking down at Yan Qing's corpse with a face as gloomy as deep water.

Yan An had escaped disaster not by his own ability, but through this person's means. In that instant just now, Yang Kai had actually sensed traces of Space Force fluctuations. Yan An's disappearance and reappearance were quite similar to his own teleportation, only over a much shorter distance.

"Patriarch Yan?" Yang Kai asked.

Yan Luo's face was cold, and he paid Yang Kai no heed, as if this person did not exist. Only after looking at Yan Qing for quite some time did he speak. "It has been a very long time since anyone dared act so wantonly in my Yan Family."

Although his voice was calm, fury towered to the heavens inside him.

A moment of carelessness had actually cost him a Family Elder. This loss could not be called small!

"You deserve death!" Yan Luo abruptly turned his head, his expression turning savage in an instant as he glared angrily at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai grinned. "That depends on whether Patriarch Yan has the ability." Those without ability had all been slaughtered by him.

"Do not think body tempering is omnipotent. There are many things in this world beyond your imagination. A frog at the bottom of a well, how could it imagine the magnificence of the Starry Sky!"

Hearing this, Yang Kai seriously thought for a moment, then nodded and said, "What you say is right."

Yan Luo raised his hand, pointed at him, and said indifferently, "End yourself. You can have a quick death. If this Lord takes action, you will not be so fortunate."

Yang Kai said, “I came here this time only to ask how to reach the Ancestral Domain, and I was even willing to offer compensation. Patriarch Yan, there is no need to be so unreasonable, is there? Why resort to fighting and killing at the slightest disagreement?”

"You will not shed tears until you see the coffin!" Yan Luo coldly snorted. How could he have any interest in speaking with him? His raised finger suddenly slashed downward.

Heaven and earth instantly hummed. An indescribable pressure descended from the sky, as if the heavens themselves had collapsed. Inside the entire hall, every Void King Realm expert, regardless of cultivation, sank down and could not even lift their heads.

He Yunxiang was horrified, her pretty face filled with fear.

She had thought that after advancing to the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, she would be able to move unhindered through the Star Domain. Even if she encountered some existence she could not defeat, if she could not win, she could always flee. But now, when Yan Luo made his move, she realized she had been wrong, terribly wrong.

The might of a single finger was already this terrifying. If he truly set his sights on her, she feared she would not even have a chance to escape.

How could he be so powerful? The aura on Yan Luo's body was only at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm. Even if he had advanced longer ago than she had, he should not be so much stronger than her.

At this moment, the pressure Yan Luo gave her was actually even more terrifying than Yang Kai's. Standing there, he seemed to be the heavens, the earth itself. Between this heaven and earth, his word was law, and no one could defy his will.

“Star Force!” Yang Kai’s sword-like brows rose, a look of surprise appearing on his face, but he soon understood.

This Yan Luo was actually a Star Lord, and from the looks of it, he was precisely the Star Lord of Taiyi Star. Otherwise, he could not possibly mobilize Star Strength.

Taiyi Star was the Yan Family's headquarters. As Patriarch Yan, it made sense that he had refined Taiyi Star's Origin and become this star's Star Lord. No wonder his tone was loftier than the heavens. So this was the reason.

As a Star Lord, on this Taiyi Star, he was the ruler.

Yang Kai had once been a Star Lord as well, so he naturally knew how powerful the strength a Star Lord could use on his own Cultivation Star was.

The reason Yan An had been able to escape from his grasp was not some Space Divine Ability either, but merely Yan Luo's strength as a Star Lord.

His spine sank under the pressure, but Yang Kai did not bend at the waist. He merely sized up Yan Luo in the sky with great interest.

Even without using any power, relying solely on his physical body, a mere Star Lord could not possibly make him bow. How powerful was his physical body? Now, once he used Dragon Transformation, he assumed a colossal fifteen-zhang half-dragon form. How could he lower his head to a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator?

"Hm?" Yan Luo could not help being shocked.

Although he had not used his full strength, when a Star Lord was enraged, who could withstand it? This boy's body tempering was actually so formidable that he could endure the pressure. Could he have obtained some powerful inheritance? Once this thought arose, Yan Luo could not help growing excited. The more this was the case, the greater the benefits he would obtain after killing him, and he could not help laughing.

"What are you laughing at?" Yang Kai asked.

"This Lord laughs at what deserves to be laughed at."

Yang Kai nodded and said, "Then from the looks of it, I should laugh a little too."

"If you can still laugh..." Yan Luo straightened his other four fingers, forming a palm. With a flick of his wrist, he slapped downward at Yang Kai from the air.

Heaven and earth hummed once more. Everyone who had already been bent at the waist was forced directly to their knees this time, and no one was spared.

If even the residual might was like this, everyone found it hard to imagine what kind of end Yang Kai, who was being targeted, would meet. He Yunxiang looked anxiously toward Yang Kai, then immediately stared, tongue-tied.

Over there, Yang Kai stamped both feet on the floor. The ground beneath his feet suddenly caved in, a deep pit forming with a crash. The several thousand floor tiles of the entire hall all flew up in that instant as well, turning into dust in midair.

Borrowing the force of this stomp, Yang Kai shot upward like a shooting star.

When he reached ten zhang, his figure paused slightly, clearly blocked by the might of Yan Luo's palm. But it was only a pause. In the next instant, that obstruction, enough to shatter the body and scatter the soul of any Void King Realm cultivator, was pierced through like a sheet of paper.

Yang Kai appeared in front of Yan Luo in a flash and swung a fist toward him.

Yan Luo was startled, the calm and disdain on his face suddenly vanishing, replaced by solemnity and disbelief.

What had gotten into this guy? He was a Star Lord. On Taiyi Star, he dominated everything. How could anyone possibly accomplish something like this?

The void shook, the universe shattered, and as the fist tore through space, it left behind a string of pitch-black scorch marks.

Yan Luo distinctly sensed that even space had shattered.

In an instant, myriad thoughts spun through his mind, but in the end there was only one way out: dodge!

If he took such a punch, he would undoubtedly die. Even if he was a Star Lord, he could not block it.

A figure flashed, and Yan Luo had already vanished without a trace.

Yang Kai's punch struck empty space, and the force erupted. With nowhere to vent, it scattered in all directions.

Amid a rumbling crash, the great hall collapsed, dust billowed everywhere, and figures flew out one after another in sorry states, coughing without end.

When everyone lifted their eyes and looked over, they were all horrified. The Yan Family's main hall, which had stood on Twin Peaks for several thousand years without falling, had now actually turned into a field of ruins. Broken walls and rubble were everywhere, as if the end of the world had come. People kept flying out from within, cursing loudly.

Below Twin Peaks, in the bustling night city, countless people stopped and looked over, all full of suspicion, not knowing what exactly had happened on the Yan Family's side to cause such an enormous commotion.

Without the support of power, Yang Kai also fell back to the ground, standing on a wooden board in the ruins as he looked in one direction. "I wonder whether Patriarch Yan can still laugh now?"

How could Yan Luo still laugh? His complexion was ashen beyond compare, so gloomy it seemed a layer of frost could almost be scraped from it. His trembling gaze fixed on Yang Kai, filled entirely with disbelief.

On Taiyi Star, there was actually someone who could threaten him. This was something he had never imagined before.

In the Star Domain, he was not the only Star Lord, but everyone acknowledged that on a Star Lord's own territory, regardless of whether that Star Lord's cultivation was at the Second Stage or Third Stage, he was invincible.

Because a Star Lord could mobilize the power of a Cultivation Star's Origin. That was the power of heaven and earth. Who in this world could contend with heaven and earth?

Something he had not dared imagine before had now truly appeared, and appeared right before his eyes. Yan Luo almost thought he was dreaming.

Only after remaining silent for a long while did he say, "You do indeed have some ability. As expected of a body tempering cultivator. But if you think you can defeat this Lord, then you are gravely mistaken."

He stood in an invincible position. No matter how powerful that young man below was, what did it matter if he could not touch him?

"Will Patriarch Yan still persist in his delusion?" Yang Kai looked at him indifferently.

Yan Luo shouted fiercely, “You killed my Yan Family’s Elders and destroyed my Yan Family’s foundation. No matter who you are, today, my Yan Family will not rest until one of us is dead!” Having suffered such a great loss, how could they swallow their anger in silence? The higher one’s strength, the greater one’s temper. If the Yan Family did not regain its face today, how could it establish itself in the Wuji Star Domain in the future?

Yang Kai sighed deeply. "Patriarch Yan did say one thing correctly just now."

Yan Luo looked at him coldly.

Yang Kai continued, "A frog at the bottom of a well naturally cannot imagine the magnificence of the Starry Sky. Today, this Young Master happens to be in a rare refined mood, so I will let you trash broaden your horizons." As he spoke, an aura of power actually surged out from within his body.

## Chapter 2754 — All of It

Fenglin City, Ziyuan Commerce Guild branch.

Fenglin City was no longer what it once had been. It had risen swiftly, and as one of the two great Commerce Guilds of the Southern Territory, Ziyuan Commerce Guild had naturally established a branch here, even stationing an Emperor Realm expert to oversee it. The endless stream of cultivators flowing between Fenglin City and nearby Linghu City brought tremendous business and profits to the Commerce Guild, so Ziyuan Commerce Guild paid close attention to this place.

The branch managed fifty diverse shops and a sizable auction house. Over the past few years, Ziyuan Commerce Guild had made a fortune in Fenglin City, their coffers overflowing.

Entering the Ziyuan Commerce Guild branch, Yang Kai glanced around and found the hall filled with cultivators, most quietly discussing something, while others wandered about, browsing the goods on display.

The hall was spacious and built with grandeur, showcasing the impressive wealth of Ziyuan Commerce Guild.

In a corner, several beautiful young girls whispered and played. Upon seeing Yang Kai enter, one immediately stepped forward, stood before him, and gracefully saluted: "Greetings, honored sir. May I ask what you require?"

Yang Kai could tell at a glance she was a specially trained attendant, the kind many Commerce Guilds employed. Judging by her appearance, she was young, about sixteen, with delicate features and a sweet smile, speaking softly and gently, easily leaving a good impression.

Yang Kai smiled, "I have a business matter to discuss with your Commerce Guild."

The girl's eyes lit up. Attendants like her loved to hear such words, as they earned commissions from every deal. Though the commission was small, if it was a big transaction, she wouldn’t have to worry about cultivation resources for years.

Having already noticed Yang Kai’s extraordinary bearing, she was now even more certain. For a moment, she became even more enthusiastic, smiling sweetly: "May I ask what kind of business, honored sir? For deals under ten million Source Crystals, I can make the decision myself."

"Lower-Level Source Crystals?" Yang Kai asked.

"Yes!" The girl nodded.

Yang Kai smiled, "Then please invite the person in charge."

The girl was momentarily stunned, then quickly realized this business was likely beyond her authority. Ten million Lower-Level Source Crystals was a huge sum for her, but for some, especially Emperor Realm experts, it was nothing.

It seemed this honored sir wanted to discuss just such a deal.

With a solemn expression, the girl said, "Please follow me to a private room, honored sir, and wait a moment."

Yang Kai nodded slightly and followed the girl in a certain direction.

Soon, they arrived at a private room. The girl settled Yang Kai inside, served him tea, and said, "Please wait, honored sir. I will invite the steward."

Yang Kai sat unmoving as the girl was about to leave, suddenly asking, "By the way, have you heard of Kang Siran?"

Shopkeeper Kang was the first friend Yang Kai made in Fenglin City. Yang Kai was able to inherit Gongsun Mu’s legacy thanks to Kang Siran’s guidance. Back then, Fenglin City was too small, and after Kang Siran advanced to the Dao Origin Realm, he was transferred by Ziyuan Commerce Guild. Yang Kai had no idea where he was sent.

It had been more than ten years since they last met.

Now that Yang Kai had returned to his old haunts, he naturally thought of him. He was just asking casually, but the girl was surprised: "You know Steward Kang?"

"Hm?" Yang Kai was puzzled.

The girl smiled, "If you know Steward Kang, I can invite him to discuss business with you."

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows, "Kang Siran is here?"

"Yes, Steward Kang is in this branch."

This was an unexpected delight. Yang Kai hadn’t thought a casual inquiry would yield such news, so he nodded, "Good, please invite him over."

"Certainly!" the girl replied, bowed, and left, closing the door behind her.

In the private room, Yang Kai sipped tea and waited quietly.

Before long, there was a knock at the door. Yang Kai smiled, "Come in!"

The door opened, and Kang Siran appeared in Yang Kai's view. Their eyes met, and Kang Siran was utterly shocked: "Yang... Brother Yang!"

"Fate brings us together again, Brother Kang!" Yang Kai smiled and cupped his fists.

Kang Siran was both surprised and delighted, closing the door: "I was just wondering which old acquaintance was looking for me. Turns out it's Brother Yang. It's been more than ten years, hasn't it?"

"Indeed. Brother Kang, sit and let's talk."

Seeing Yang Kai after so long, Kang Siran was quite moved. Back then, he was able to advance to the Dao Origin Realm thanks to Yang Kai giving him a Dao Origin Pill. Without it, he might have remained stuck at the peak of the Void King Realm, unable to progress, and wouldn't have his current cultivation and status.

Over the past ten years, Kang Siran's cultivation had reached the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. It seemed he had worked hard and cultivated diligently during this time.

After sitting down, Kang Siran couldn't help but sigh. Back then, both he and Yang Kai were in the Void King Realm, and had faced life and death together. Now, seeing each other again felt like a lifetime ago.

"Weren't you transferred away, Brother Kang? How come you're back in Fenglin City?" Yang Kai asked curiously.

Kang Siran smiled, "Fenglin City isn't what it used to be. The headquarters pays much more attention to it now. Although my cultivation isn't very high, I've managed this place for half my life and know it inside out, so they sent me back."

Fenglin City had become a coveted place—everyone at headquarters wanted a chance to come here. If Kang Siran hadn’t possessed a certain natural advantage, with his cultivation, he never would have gotten a taste of this prized posting.

Still, in a while, he'll likely be transferred again, since the branch has established itself and no longer needed his guidance. Someone with higher cultivation and stronger connections will take his place.

"I see. That's a good thing." Yang Kai nodded gently, not understanding the internal intrigues of the Commerce Guild.

Kang Siran smiled warmly, offering no further explanation. Matters within the Commerce Guild were, after all, internal affairs. Though he and Yang Kai were on good terms, there was no need to bring up such things.

Knock knock knock...

Suddenly, there was a knock at the door.

The knocking had barely finished when, without waiting for a response from Yang Kai or Kang Siran, the door was opened. A young man in brocade robes walked in with a smile.

Upon seeing him, Kang Siran's face instantly darkened. He frowned, "Gu Hong, what are you doing? Can't you see I'm discussing business with a guest?"

Gu Hong was far too presumptuous, entering the private room without permission. Fortunately, it was Yang Kai sitting here. If it were someone else, Kang Siran wouldn't know how to explain. It might make people think Ziyuan Commerce Guild's confidentiality was lacking, ruining a deal.

Facing Kang Siran's reproach, Gu Hong ignored him, smiling at Yang Kai: "Greetings, friend. I am Gu Hong, Steward of the Ziyuan Commerce Guild branch. May I ask your name?"

Yang Kai glanced at Kang Siran, then at Gu Hong, his eyes flashing. He grinned but said nothing.

Not getting a response, Gu Hong's expression became awkward, but he continued: "Sorry to disturb you, friend. Please forgive me. It's just that Steward Kang's abilities are lacking, and I'm afraid a big deal might be mishandled, so I came uninvited. My apologies."

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes, "What makes you say Steward Kang's abilities are lacking?"

Gu Hong smiled, "He only closed three deals in a year. Isn't that lacking?"

Kang Siran's face was grim. He retorted coldly, "If no one interfered, I could close more deals."

Gu Hong said, "No need to be angry, Steward Kang. My interference is for your own good. Soon you'll be transferred, and handling all the procedures will be troublesome. Why not let me take over now?"

Kang Siran snorted, "Should I thank you, then?"

Gu Hong smiled, "We're all part of the same Commerce Guild. No need for thanks." He turned to Yang Kai, "Friend, if you really have business, I'm happy to help. To be honest, Steward Kang will soon be transferred. If you have any deals, why not discuss them with me? I guarantee you'll be satisfied."

Yang Kai turned to Kang Siran, "You're being transferred again."

Kang Siran sighed.

Back then, being transferred from Fenglin City was his own wish. The city was too small and poor, and staying offered no prospects. But now, it was a coveted place everyone wanted a piece of. Who would want to leave? But there's no fighting the powers that be.

Yang Kai nodded, "Steward Gu, I appreciate your offer, but I have a life-and-death bond with Steward Kang. I'd like to discuss this deal with him."

"I see..." Gu Hong was momentarily stunned, then smiled, "In that case, I won't play the villain. Farewell!"

Yang Kai's words made it clear Gu Hong wouldn't get this deal, so he didn't waste time and left briskly.

The private room was soon left with only Yang Kai and Kang Siran.

Yang Kai asked, "Brother Kang, you don't want to leave Fenglin City?"

Kang Siran nodded, "Though there are a few branches in the Southern Territory comparable to Fenglin City, they're not places I can enter. If I'm transferred this time, it'll be hard to return."

"Understood." Yang Kai nodded.

Kang Siran smiled, "Such is fate. Let's not talk about me. What business do you have with the Commerce Guild, Brother Yang?"

Yang Kai's expression became serious, "Ethereal Crystal and Ethereal Jade."

Kang Siran stroked his beard, "Those are the main materials for refining Spatial Rings. They're not hard to find. The branch has some in storage. How much do you need, Brother Yang?"

"How much does your branch have?" Yang Kai asked.

Kang Siran replied, "Let me check."

As he spoke, Kang Siran took out a jade slip and examined it. A moment later, he looked up and said, "We have roughly thirty million Middle-Level Source Crystals' worth in stock. If you need them, Brother Yang, I can authorize a ten percent discount for you—that's my limit."

“I’ll take all of them.”

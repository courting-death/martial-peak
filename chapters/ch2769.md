## Chapter 2769 — Courtesy Demands Reciprocity

“Someone, arrange seats!” Wen Zishan waved his hand grandly, feeling a weight lift from his heart. No matter what, as long as Xue Zhengmao could be appeased, that was enough.

“No rush!” The long-silent Yang Kai suddenly spoke up.

Wen Zishan looked at him in astonishment.

Wiping the blood from the corner of his mouth, Yang Kai said, “May I ask, Hall Master, is there a spare sacrificial beast?”

Wen Zishan shook his head with a bitter smile.

A Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast was not so easy to find. Otherwise, Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores wouldn’t be so expensive. Especially now, with time so tight, where could they find another one? The Golden-Back Heaven-Piercing Ape that appeared earlier had been personally captured by Wen Zishan from a desolate mountain tens of thousands of li away.

Unfortunately, before Yang Kai could act, Xue Zhengmao had already killed it, leaving the ceremony at an awkward impasse.

With his eyes lowered, Yang Kai said, “If there’s no spare, then I’ll have to offend you.”

Wen Zishan’s heart skipped a beat, and he instinctively asked, “What are you going to do?”

Before he could finish speaking, Yang Kai’s figure suddenly flickered and vanished on the spot. Wen Zishan’s face changed dramatically, faintly guessing Yang Kai’s intent. He turned to look at Xue Zhengmao, and sure enough, Yang Kai’s figure abruptly appeared before Xue Zhengmao, slashing at him with a sword. The terrifying sword light, thirty zhang long, seemed as if it would shatter the very void.

Xue Zhengmao was also greatly startled. He raised his hand and struck out with a palm. As Emperor Yuan surged, the wind and clouds stirred, the sword light shattered, and Xue Zhengmao’s figure swayed.

“You brat—how dare you launch a sneak attack on this old man!” Xue Zhengmao roared in fury. He was a Third-Stage Emperor, an Elder of the Star God Palace, yet this youth had struck without so much as a word?

Luckily, he reacted quickly, or he really would have suffered a loss. Still, being ambushed in front of so many people left Xue Zhengmao’s face burning with shame, and his anger surged.

“Just returning the courtesy!” Yang Kai sneered, discarding his sword and rapidly forming seals with both hands. The Power of Time spread out, and the Laws of Time surged. In that instant, everyone felt a sense of unreality, as if time had stopped flowing, even their thoughts pausing for a moment.

As the Years Like a Shuttle Seal struck, golden light burst from his right eye, and a budding lotus flashed and vanished.

In his fury, Xue Zhengmao’s body stiffened, a wave of pain surged in his mind, and before his eyes, a giant lotus slowly bloomed. Shocked, he realized he had fallen into Yang Kai’s trap.

Yet the power of this Divine Soul secret technique seemed astonishingly great. Was this really a Divine Soul secret technique that a First Stage Emperor could unleash?

Biting the tip of his tongue, the sharp pain cleared his mind for a moment. He mustered all his Divine Soul power to resist the Bloom, Lotus secret technique’s influence. His vision cleared, and the giant blooming lotus disappeared.

Looking up, drenched in cold sweat, he saw that in that brief delay, Yang Kai’s palm was about to strike his chest.

He didn’t know what was special about this palm, but it gave him an extremely uneasy feeling. He wanted to resist, but found his thoughts sluggish, his movements no longer fluid.

As he roared, the aura of a Third-Stage Emperor expert erupted in full. Before he could act further, that palm had already landed on his chest.

The body and mind he had just regained control of once again froze for a brief moment…

At the same time, Xue Zhengmao’s counterattack struck Yang Kai’s chest. Visibly, Yang Kai’s chest caved in, the sound of bones breaking rang out, and his face turned deathly pale.

Yet he gritted his teeth and made no sound, grabbing the floating Myriad Sword and slashing it at Xue Zhengmao’s chest.

Hiss…

Sword light flashed as Yang Kai’s figure flickered, returning to his original spot. His body swayed slightly, and his face was as pale as paper.

With a gulp, Yang Kai forcibly swallowed the surging qi and blood in his chest, a flush of unnatural color rising to his pale face.

The entire exchange happened in the blink of an eye—so swift and dazzling that no one could keep up. With the interference of the Laws of Time, it was only when Yang Kai hurriedly retreated to his original spot that everyone finally snapped back to their senses. Each face was filled with utter shock, staring at Yang Kai as if they had just seen a ghost.

Even Wen Zishan was stunned, while Xiao Yuyang had shot to his feet, eyes full of disbelief.

With a low shout, Yang Kai declared, “With the blood of my enemy, I offer sacrifice to the heavens!” As he spoke, the Myriad Sword swept out, and a spray of blood mist suddenly burst forth, enveloping the vast space above. “The ceremony is complete!”

Hiss…

At the same time, blood spurted from Xue Zhengmao’s chest. As everyone turned to look, they gasped in shock—there was now a wound over a foot long on Xue Zhengmao’s chest, flesh torn open, blood pouring out…

Xue Zhengmao himself seemed unable to believe it. He had actually been wounded by a First Stage Emperor? He stood there in a daze, staring blankly at the blood spurting from his chest.

After a moment, he shuddered, pressed his hand to his chest to stop the bleeding, and glared at Yang Kai with bloodshot eyes, gritting out, “You little brat court death!”

What a humiliation—to be injured by a First Stage Emperor in a flurry of attacks! It was truly a disgrace. While he had underestimated his opponent, he couldn’t deny that this Yang Kai was truly remarkable. If it had been any other First Stage Emperor, no matter how careless he was, he wouldn’t have been hurt.

But now, in front of so many experts from the Southern Territory, how could Xue Zhengmao save face?

Murderous intent surged like a tide, killing intent gleamed in his eyes—Xue Zhengmao was truly enraged.

Wen Zishan stepped in front of Xue Zhengmao at just the right moment.

Though he was shocked that Yang Kai had managed to injure Xue Zhengmao in an instant, he also knew Yang Kai had paid a heavy price. Xue Zhengmao’s counterattack had definitely injured Yang Kai’s internal organs, and Yang Kai had used several powerful secret techniques to catch his opponent off guard. If it came to a direct fight, Wen Zishan didn’t think Yang Kai had any hope of victory.

“Out of my way!” Xue Zhengmao roared, completely losing control.

Wen Zishan said, “Elder Xue, you struck first. Elder Yang merely returned the favor. As a senior, surely you’re not so petty?”

Xue Zhengmao’s face darkened as he recalled Yang Kai’s earlier words about returning the courtesy. It was true—he had attacked Yang Kai without a word, so how could he complain about being ambushed in return?

But he absolutely couldn’t let this go. He couldn’t swallow this anger.

Turning his head, Xue Zhengmao fixed his gaze on the side and said in a low voice, “Elder Xiao!”

Xiao Yuyang sighed, took a few steps, and instantly arrived at Xue Zhengmao’s side.

Things had reached a point where he could no longer stand by. This wasn’t just about Xue Zhengmao’s face, but also the reputation of the Star God Palace. He had to stand with Xue Zhengmao.

“Hold off Wen Zishan. I want that brat dead!” Xue Zhengmao transmitted.

Xiao Yuyang replied, “Holding off Wen Zishan is no problem, but… you won’t be able to kill that brat.”

“You underestimate this old man?” Xue Zhengmao’s chest heaved with anger, like a volcano about to erupt. “Just now, I was merely careless.”

“It’s not underestimating you. That brat is skilled in space divine abilities. Without a perfect setup, no one can catch him!”

“Space… divine abilities!” Xue Zhengmao’s eyes narrowed, and a sense of helplessness welled up inside.

As an elder of the Star God Palace, he was no stranger to those words. Years ago, a man named Li Wuyi offended the Flower Shadow Great Emperor and was hunted down, but managed to escape alive, relying precisely on space divine abilities!

Later, with the Beast Great Emperor interceding, the Flower Shadow Great Emperor let the matter drop. To this day, Li Wuyi still lived freely on Spirit Beast Island.

If Yang Kai truly mastered space divine abilities, Xue Zhengmao admitted he really had no way to deal with him.

Xue Zhengmao frowned deeply, caught in a dilemma.

The two Star God Palace elders stood side by side, and everyone’s expressions changed. Wen Zishan’s face was more solemn than ever, and the many Emperor Realm experts of Qingyang Shrine surrounded Yang Kai, wary and on guard.

No one had expected the conferment ceremony to turn out like this.

“Elder Xue, Elder Xiao, are you truly determined?” Wen Zishan’s voice was low. “Though my Shrine is not as great as the Star God Palace, we are not some soft persimmon to be squeezed at will.”

Xiao Yuyang said nothing, and Xue Zhengmao also remained silent, only glaring angrily at Yang Kai.

The other guests watched with bated breath, afraid the two sides would really come to blows. If the Star God Palace and Qingyang Shrine fell out, the entire Southern Territory would be thrown into turmoil, which would benefit no one.

The tense, oppressive atmosphere spread throughout the hall. Even the younger disciples brought by the Emperor Realm experts were drenched in sweat, faces pale.

“Stop!”

From afar, a clear, melodious voice rang out, carrying a mysterious power that instantly eased everyone’s taut nerves.

At the sound, Xiao Chen, who had been staring at Yang Kai with a complicated expression, suddenly snapped out of it and looked to the side, eyes brightening.

Over there, a colorful ribbon appeared out of thin air. Upon it stood a woman of peerless beauty, her features exquisite, a vermilion mark between her brows. Her black hair was styled in twin-moon buns, with a few long strands draped over her chest. Dressed in a pale yellow gown, she looked even more ethereal and refined. With a light step, she floated down onto Ten Thousand Saints Peak.

“Princess!” Xiao Chen called out in delight.

Hearing Xiao Chen’s cry, everyone immediately recognized the newcomer’s identity. Shocked, they all rose and cupped their fists, saying, “Greetings, Princess Lan Xun!”

The older Emperor Realm experts were better able to compose themselves, their cultivation and temperament allowing them to merely glance in amazement at Lan Xun’s beauty. But the younger men and women who had come with their elders were all left staring in a daze, especially the men, each showing admiration in their eyes. In that moment, there was nothing else in their gaze but Lan Xun’s dazzling brilliance, captivating their very souls.

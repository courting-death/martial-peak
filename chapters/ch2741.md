## Chapter 2741 — Ji Ying’s Request


"You recognized me, huh?" Yang Kai scratched his cheek, feeling a bit deflated that his prank had failed.

Hua Qingsi rolled her eyes. "It all happened so suddenly, I didn’t have time to think. But now that I look closely, I can still see traces of the old you."

Once Yang Kai spoke, Hua Qingsi immediately recognized him.

Returning the five-colored spear to her, Yang Kai placed his hands on his waist and declared proudly, "Handsome, right?"

Hua Qingsi replied, "A bit too fierce."

"Fierce is good!" Yang Kai laughed heartily.

"What kind of secret technique is this? I’ve never seen you use it before," Hua Qingsi asked curiously.

"I’ve been in seclusion lately, focusing on cultivation, and made some gains," Yang Kai said, looking pleased.

Hua Qingsi pursed her lips. "It’s obvious Zhu Qing taught you."

"So, what did you need from me?" Yang Kai dispelled the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique and returned to his original appearance, though his clothes were now extremely tattered. His body had suddenly doubled in size, so naturally his clothes were ruined.

Hua Qingsi handed over two Spatial Rings. "The Source Crystals from Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect have been delivered."

Yang Kai waved his hand. "Keep them for yourself. If the Sect needs anything in the future, just draw from these as needed."

Hua Qingsi was now the Chief Steward of Lingxiao Palace. Although Yang Kai had given her quite a few Source Crystals last time, it hadn’t been a huge amount. With these two billion Upper-Level Source Crystals as a foundation, Lingxiao Palace would have no worries about cultivation resources for at least the next twenty years.

"Alright." Hua Qingsi nodded and put the rings away again. "Also, Master Ji is here and wants to see you."

"Oh? Brother Ji came back so quickly?" Yang Kai’s eyes lit up. He thought for a moment and said, "Let me change clothes. Have him wait a bit."

"Okay." Hua Qingsi accepted the task and left. Yang Kai returned to his room to change clothes, wondering if he should buy a set of armor. It didn’t need to be too good, just something that wouldn’t break when his body expanded.

Armor usually had strong flexibility. Its toughness far surpassed ordinary clothing.

In the main hall of Lingxiao Palace, Ji Ying sat upright in a chair, occasionally glancing outside, looking a bit anxious.

Hua Qingsi accompanied him.

After a short while, a figure darted in from outside—it was Yang Kai.

"Brother Yang!" Ji Ying quickly stood up.

Yang Kai laughed, "Brother Ji, you came so fast. Are you ready to stay in my Lingxiao Palace to refine pills?"

Ji Ying gave a wry smile. "I’m ashamed to say, I’ll have to postpone that for now."

Yang Kai’s heart sank. "Did the Great Emperor…?"

Ji Ying shook his head. "My master didn’t say much about it, but he’s quite interested in you. He asked me to tell you that if you have time, he hopes you’ll visit Medicine Pill Valley."

"The Great Emperor wants to see me?" Yang Kai’s expression became serious.

Ji Ying chuckled. "No need to be nervous. My master simply heard that your alchemy skills surpassed mine, and he’s quite impressed. He wishes to exchange some insights with you about the art of pill refinement, that’s all."

That was putting it far too modestly. Great Emperor Miaodan was an existence who had long surpassed the level of an Imperial-Grade Pill Master. No matter how skilled Yang Kai was in alchemy, what could he possibly discuss with someone of such stature? It was obvious that Great Emperor Miaodan simply wished to offer Yang Kai some guidance, but chose to phrase it so tactfully.

Yang Kai couldn’t help but feel a deep respect. Though he had never met Great Emperor Miaodan, just from this alone, he could tell the Great Emperor was approachable and without airs.

Of course, it was only because Yang Kai had the ability. Otherwise, not just anyone could meet the Great Emperor.

"Such kindness from the Great Emperor—I am truly grateful!" Yang Kai cupped his fists in thanks.

Ji Ying smiled. "As for the Purple Void Cauldron, my master said since you were fated to obtain it, it’s yours."

"Please thank the Great Emperor for me." Yang Kai nodded. This outcome was expected. Although the Purple Void Cauldron was an Imperial-Grade pill furnace, with Great Emperor Miaodan’s status, he would never try to seize it.

Ji Ying’s expression became solemn. "I came here on my master’s orders, with an unreasonable request."

Yang Kai said, "Since it’s the Great Emperor’s command, Brother Ji, speak freely."

Ji Ying said, "Could I trouble you to accompany me to retrieve my Third Senior Brother’s remains and bury them in Medicine Pill Valley?"

Yang Kai raised his brows and nodded. "Of course. Senior Gongsun Mu’s last wish was to return to Medicine Pill Valley. Now that it can be fulfilled, he’ll surely rest in peace."

"Thank you, Brother Yang. Earlier, you said my Third Senior Brother died in the Southern Territory?"

"That’s right, Southern Territory, Primordial Cauldron Mountain. I remember the place."

“The journey to the Southern Territory is long, so we shouldn’t delay. Shall we set off now?” Ji Ying asked, “Or do you have other matters to handle?”

Yang Kai laughed, “My Lingxiao Palace barely has any disciples now, so there’s nothing to handle. If you’re eager, let’s go.”

It just so happened that he planned to return to the Southern Territory anyway, so it was on the way. As for Zhu Qing’s instructions, he didn’t pay them any mind.

He didn’t notify the three Yao Kings either. Now that he could kill Third Stage Emperor Realm experts, there weren’t many who could threaten his life, so there was no need for the Yao Kings to follow.

Leading Ji Ying to the Space Array, the two Thousand Leaves Sect disciples guarding it immediately saluted, “Greetings, Palace Master!”

“Did Steward Hua have you guard here?” Yang Kai asked the two.

The older Thousand Leaves Sect disciple replied, “Yes, Steward Hua takes good care of us. She posted many tasks at Thousand Leaves Peak. My junior brother and I were lucky to get this guarding job, and we’re paid daily.”

“Good.” Yang Kai nodded, “If anything happens, notify Steward Hua immediately.”

“Yes!”

Yang Kai was quite satisfied with Hua Qingsi’s arrangements. This was an important place for the Sect. Last time he came, there wasn’t even a guard, so he mentioned it to Hua Qingsi, and now she had assigned people.

The two Thousand Leaves Sect disciples didn’t recognize Ji Ying. If they knew he was a disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, they’d be shocked.

“Brother Yang, where does this array lead?” Ji Ying stood on the array, watching Yang Kai embed Source Crystals, and asked curiously.

“You’ll see in a moment, Brother Ji.” Yang Kai smiled mysteriously, making Ji Ying even more curious.

“Brother Ji, hold on tight!” Yang Kai suddenly reminded Ji Ying.

Ji Ying chuckled. Though he was a pill master, he had Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivation. A simple teleport wouldn’t affect him—unless it was a long-distance cross-territory transfer.

At that thought, Ji Ying was startled.

Before he could react, his vision was flooded with white light, and a wave of dizziness washed over him…

When Ji Ying came to, he found Yang Kai standing nearby, smiling at him.

Ji Ying shook his head, still dizzy, but his face was full of shock, “Cross-territory teleportation?”

Ordinary Space Arrays couldn’t cause such side effects. He’d used them countless times, and no matter how far he traveled, he never felt anything. But this time, the dizziness wouldn’t stop, showing just how far they’d gone.

“Exactly!” Yang Kai nodded with a smile.

Ji Ying was shocked, “How does Lingxiao Palace have a cross-territory Space Array?”

Lingxiao Palace used to be Wenqing Sect. He’d been there many times. If there was a cross-territory Space Array, he would’ve known. Such arrays were extremely rare, and in the entire Star Realm, there were only a few, all controlled by Great Emperor sects. Even those were ancient relics.

Nowadays, no one in the Star Realm could set up a cross-territory Space Array.

Naturally, Ji Ying was shocked.

Yang Kai didn’t answer, just smiled and said, “Let’s go.”

Ji Ying followed Yang Kai, flying side by side. He looked around and was surprised again, “A small world?”

Yang Kai smiled, “Brother Ji, you have sharp eyes. This is indeed a small world, originally the foundation of Thousand Leaves Sect, now taken over by me.”

Ji Ying sighed, “What a pity.”

He wasn’t lamenting that Yang Kai took over the small world, but that its quality wasn’t high enough. The Spiritual Qi here was decent, but not at the level of a blessed land.

If the Spiritual Qi were richer, rare Spiritual Medicine could be grown here, turning it into a medicine garden.

Cultivating Spiritual Medicine in such a small world was extremely safe. As an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, Ji Ying always thought from a pill master’s perspective.

In no time, the two arrived at another Space Array, which was the exit of the small world.

Activating the array, Yang Kai and Ji Ying appeared in the heart of Thousand Leaves Sect.

“This was once the foundation of Thousand Leaves Sect, but over a year ago, it was wiped out.” Yang Kai explained, glancing at the ruined surroundings.

Ji Ying nodded slightly, “This is the Southern Territory, right?”

Since Yang Kai used the cross-territory Space Array, he naturally brought Ji Ying directly to the Southern Territory.

“That’s right.” Yang Kai nodded, “About the Space Array, Brother Ji, please keep it secret.”

Ji Ying was taken aback, then said seriously, “Don’t worry, Brother Yang. I’m not one to gossip.”

Yang Kai smiled, “It’s not that serious. If the Great Emperor asks, you can tell him.”

Ji Ying was grateful, “Thank you, Brother Yang.”

He had estimated that a round trip from the Northern Territory to the Southern Territory would take at least a year. If he suddenly returned in a few days, his master might ask, and he wouldn’t know how to explain. Yang Kai was clearly thinking of him.

“Let’s go. I’ll take you to Primordial Cauldron Mountain.” Yang Kai called out, flying ahead.

Primordial Cauldron Mountain wasn’t close to Thousand Leaves Sect. If they just flew, it would take at least three to five days. But both were Emperor Realm cultivators, so they didn’t need to rest.

Yang Kai remembered Primordial Cauldron Mountain was near Fenglin City, right next to Blazing Fire Hall.

Thinking back, Blazing Fire Hall used to be untouchable for Yang Kai, since he was only in the Void King Realm, while Blazing Fire Hall had Dao Origin Realm experts.

But now, Blazing Fire Hall was nothing compared to him. In just over ten years, those he once had to look up to now bowed at his feet.

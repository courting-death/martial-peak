## Chapter 2648 — Smash

With the strength of those unruly Yao Kings and Saint Envoys, there was no way they would have come out on top in the end.

For a moment, whether it was Xie Wuwei or Yingfei, both couldn’t help but feel a bit relieved. Especially Yingfei, who felt more and more that his decision a few days ago to personally see Yang Kai off had been the right one.

At this moment, Xi Lei also noticed something was off. He stared wide-eyed at Yingfei and Xie Wuwei and asked, “You two aren’t hurt?”

Yingfei grinned and said, “Why would we be hurt?”

Xi Lei shook his head and said, “Don’t tell me those guys would just hand over such a cushy job for nothing. Old Bull had to put in a lot of effort to get this opportunity.”

Yingfei replied smugly, “Lord Cang Gou gave the order. How could those bastards dare object? As for Brother Xie... I have no idea.”

Xie Wuwei snorted, looking inscrutable, and didn’t bother to explain.

Xi Lei said angrily, “Unbelievable, unbelievable, this is just too unbelievable!”

When the news came out a few days ago, all thirty-two Yao Kings and eight great Saint Envoys of the Ancient Land were in an uproar, everyone eager for the job, eyes red with envy. Xi Lei had fought them all and won by force, but when it came to Yingfei, Lord Cang Gou personally appointed him, saving him a lot of trouble.

Comparing the two, it would have been strange if Xi Lei felt at ease.

But thinking about it, no matter the process, he’d gotten the job in the end, so the result was good.

As the three were chatting, the palace doors swung open and a woman in palace attire stepped out.

The three Yao Kings immediately cupped their fists and said, “Miss Tianlong!”

Although Tianlong’s cultivation wasn’t as high as theirs, she was Luan Feng’s trusted aide. Naturally, the three didn’t dare be disrespectful. If Tianlong whispered a few words in Luan Feng’s ear, their days would be miserable.

“Lady asks you to come in,” Tianlong said, then led the way ahead.

The three hurried to follow.

Entering the grand hall, they saw Luan Feng seated above, with Yang Kai sitting below, and an old man and a young girl standing behind him.

The three kept their eyes straight ahead and cupped their fists in unison, “Subordinates greet Lord Luan Feng!”

“Mm.” Luan Feng nodded gently, exuding grace, and parted her red lips, “You should all know the situation. I’ve summoned you here to accompany Young Master Yang to the Northern Territory and resolve the crisis at Ice Heart Valley. I hope you will obey Young Master Yang’s orders. If you dare to disobey, you will be severely punished.”

The three immediately shouted, “We will not fail in our duty!”

How could they dare show even a hint of disobedience? Landing such a cushy job was like a blessing from their ancestors—without Luan Feng’s warning, they would still serve with all their might. After all, forging a good relationship with Yang Kai was a rare opportunity.

Luan Feng nodded in satisfaction, “Young Master Yang is no outsider. Go ahead and greet him.”

Only then did the three turn around and cup their fists, “Greetings, Young Master Yang.”

Yang Kai smiled, glancing at the three Yao Kings before him, seeing through them clearly.

Of these three Yao Kings, he knew the backgrounds of two: Xie Wuwei was under Fan Wu, Yingfei was under Cang Gou, so the remaining one must be under Luan Feng. He’d come to ask Luan Feng for help, but didn’t expect her to borrow a Yao King each from the other two Saint Venerables. Clearly, she wanted to share the risk.

He didn’t point it out, just said, “No need to stand on ceremony, I’ll be relying on you three from now on.”

Three Yao Kings were already within Yang Kai’s expectations, so he was not disappointed. Had Luan Feng assigned him seven or eight to accompany him, that would have been nothing short of absurd.

Xie Wuwei and the others naturally didn’t dare act out, their attitude extremely respectful.

Yang Kai stood up and said, “Lady Feng, everyone’s here. I’ll take my leave now.”

Luan Feng couldn’t wait for him to leave, so she stood up as well and said, “I’ll see you out.”

Seeing him off was just an excuse. She mainly wanted to see Yang Kai make a fool of himself.

Last time, this brat had swept away a huge fortune from the Ancient Land and dragged her into trouble at the Netherworld Sect. Now he shamelessly came to ask for help again. Luan Feng couldn’t do anything to Yang Kai, but watching him make a fool of himself was at least some consolation.

She had personally tested that so-called cross-territory space array—it was utterly useless, nothing but worthless junk.

She was quite looking forward to Yang Kai’s reaction when he discovered this.

The group headed straight for the space array. Once there, Yang Kai instructed them to stand in position, then took out Upper-Level Source Crystals and embedded them into the formation foundation’s grooves.

“Lady Feng, farewell. If I have free time in the future, I’ll come visit again.”

“Go on, go on, don’t waste time.” Luan Feng smiled and waved.

Yang Kai frowned slightly, feeling that Luan Feng’s expression was odd, but couldn’t pinpoint why. He glanced at Tianlong, who was also holding back a laugh.

What the hell?

Full of doubts, Yang Kai didn’t ask further. Standing in the array, he activated the Laws of Space. In the next instant, a burst of light suddenly flashed, enveloping everyone.

When the light faded, everyone had disappeared.

Luan Feng stared in shock at the now-empty space array. But she wasn’t stupid—after a moment, she gritted her teeth and said, “That brat actually left a trick behind!”

She couldn’t activate the space array at all, but Yang Kai could use it with ease. Clearly, he’d set up some kind of restriction. In other words, the array was fine, but no one except Yang Kai could use it.

This made Luan Feng grit her teeth in anger. She instinctively suspected Yang Kai knew she’d go to Ice Heart Valley to check things out, so he’d played these tricks.

“It really works!” Tianlong was so shocked she could barely close her mouth.

A space array set up in less than a day could actually be activated—this was nothing short of a miracle.

“So what if it works?” Luan Feng snorted coldly. “Smash it.”

“S-smash it?” Tianlong jumped in fright.

“Smash it!” Luan Feng nodded decisively. “If we leave this thing here, that brat will be able to come and go from the Ancient Land as he pleases. There’s no way I’m letting him have it that easy.”

She’d been tormented by Yang Kai enough. To prevent him from coming to bother her again, it was best to destroy the space array. Otherwise, who knew when he might suddenly show up again.

Tianlong received the order and didn’t hesitate. She quickly smashed the space array Yang Kai had set up to pieces.

……

At Ice Heart Valley, the air buzzed and the ground shook from time to time. The spiritual qi was in chaos, as if the end of days was approaching.

The entire Ice Heart Valley was now in grave danger. All the disciples were on edge, but each had a look of determination in their eyes—a resolve to live and die with Ice Heart Valley.

The protective array’s light screen over Ice Heart Valley had grown extremely dim, occasionally flickering, as if it could collapse at any moment.

“Figure something out! If you don’t stabilize the array, once it breaks, it’ll be your death!” Somewhere in Ice Heart Valley, Ji Yao pressed her longsword to a man’s neck. The sharp blade had already pierced his skin, drawing blood.

Even with a sword at his throat and his life in danger, the man wasn’t too flustered. He just sighed, “At this point, this Lord is powerless. Even though you captured me, the foundation for breaking the array is almost complete. Even without me, Wenqing Sect can still unleash half its power. If I hadn’t helped you stabilize the array these days, do you think you could have lasted this long? The array would have been broken half a month ago.”

This man was none other than Nanmen Dajun, whom Yang Kai had captured alive that day.

After Yang Kai brought him back, Bing Yun hadn’t made things difficult for him, only telling him he’d be released after the battle.

Nanmen Dajun was grateful and repaid the favor. Staying at Ice Heart Valley these days, he’d focused on helping stabilize the protective array.

He wasn’t wrong. Although Yang Kai had captured him over a month ago, by then, the many array flags he’d set up were already resonating, and the foundation for breaking the array was nearly complete.

Even without him, Wenqing Sect could still unleash half its power.

If Nanmen Dajun hadn’t voluntarily helped stabilize the array these days, how could they have lasted until now?

He was the one breaking the array and the one stabilizing it. It wasn’t that he was fickle—he’d just taken money to do a job before, and now he was repaying a debt of gratitude.

But this was the limit. Nanmen Dajun, deeply skilled in arrays, could see at a glance that the protective array of Ice Heart Valley would be broken within an hour.

“Enough talk! If you say you can’t do anything again, I’ll kill you right now!” Ji Yao shouted angrily.

Nanmen Dajun glanced at her and said, “If I were you, I’d hurry and evacuate the disciples before the array breaks. Otherwise, it’ll be too late.”

“You’re seeking death!” Ji Yao shouted, raising her sword to stab.

“Yao’er, don’t be rude!” At that moment, Bing Yun arrived from somewhere and shouted.

Over the past few days, Bing Yun had repeatedly left Ice Heart Valley to harass the enemy forces—not hoping to kill many, but simply to disrupt their progress in breaking the array. Unfortunately, every time she ventured out, Feng Xuan would intercept her, entangling her in battle and preventing her from accomplishing much.

If it weren’t for her quick wits these past few times, she might not have escaped unscathed. If it really came down to a fight, she was evenly matched with Feng Xuan—perhaps even a bit stronger—but with so many enemies on his side, sheer numbers would overwhelm her.

“Valley Lord Bing Yun!” Nanmen Dajun, who held Bing Yun in high regard, clasped his fists respectfully as she arrived and greeted her.

“No need for formalities, Grandmaster Nanmen.” Bing Yun looked worried and said, “Is there really nothing you can do?”

Nanmen Dajun shook his head, “The array will be broken, whether in half an hour or an hour. Valley Lord Bing Yun, you’d best make preparations.”

Bing Yun was silent for a while. Since even Nanmen Dajun said so, there was no hope of saving Ice Heart Valley. It was a pity Yang Kai hadn’t returned. If he had, maybe there’d be some hope.

But when Yang Kai left, he’d said it would take a month or two to return. It looked like he wouldn’t make it in time.

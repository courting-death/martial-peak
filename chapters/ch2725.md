## Chapter 2725 — Boyfriend and Girlfriend

Although Lingxiao Palace had risen abruptly, even leading to the destruction of Wenqing Sect, forcing Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace to huddle together for warmth and form a temporary alliance, who among them did not harbor private motives? Mi Qi naturally had no wish to see Li Jiao’s strength grow too formidable.

He secretly prayed in his heart, hoping that Ji Ying would fail in refining the Dragon Blood Pill.

But he also knew this was unlikely. Ji Ying was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, his skill in alchemy was extraordinary. Since he said he could produce three pills, it wasn’t just empty boasting—he truly had the confidence to do so.

Li Jiao burst out laughing, looking completely pleased with himself.

Just then, another strange sound rang out. The three of them were startled and all turned their attention to the Dragon Blood Flower.

The Dragon Blood Flower, which had been about to bloom, suddenly opened a little more. Crimson light refracted from within, dazzling their eyes.

All three Great Emperors held their breath, watching without blinking.

The red light grew stronger, the flower blossomed wider, as if a new life was being nurtured inside, struggling to break free. A faint dragon aura began to spread in all directions.

Thump, thump, thump, thump…

Li Jiao’s heartbeat was clearly audible. This matter concerned the improvement of his cultivation, so he was naturally more nervous than the other two.

Suddenly, the life being nurtured within the flower seemed to encounter some obstacle. The light abruptly dimmed, and even the bud that was about to open drew back considerably.

A withering feeling enveloped the entire Dragon Blood Flower.

Li Jiao’s expression changed dramatically as he cried out, “What…?”

Ji Ying shouted urgently, “Palace Master Li, quickly use your Essence-Blood to feed it! If you’re late, it’ll be in danger!”

The Dragon Blood Flower could only be nourished by Dragon Clan Essence-Blood. At this moment, only Li Jiao could save this rare flower.

Hearing Ji Ying’s words, Li Jiao didn’t hesitate. He flicked his finger, creating a wound at the tip, and with a secret art, a drop of rich Essence-Blood appeared on his fingertip.

“Go!” he commanded in a low voice. The drop of Essence-Blood traced an arc through the air, flying toward the Dragon Blood Flower and landing steadily in its center.

Nourished by the Essence-Blood, the Dragon Blood Flower immediately regained vitality. The bud bloomed even faster than before, and a strange fragrance spread in all directions.

But Li Jiao, who had forced out a drop of Essence-Blood, looked a bit pale, as if greatly weakened. That single drop had cost him at least ten years of hard cultivation. Still, thinking of the wonders of the Dragon Blood Pill, he felt it was all worth it.

Suddenly, a high-pitched dragon roar rang out. The Dragon Blood Flower’s blossom opened wide, and a streak of red light shot out, soaring into the sky like a living creature.

Ji Ying was prepared. With sharp eyes and quick hands, he reached out and grabbed the red light, freezing it in midair as it struggled fiercely.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao stared intently, both showing looks of amazement.

Because they saw that the red light had taken the shape of a miniature dragon…

“Is this the Dragon Blood Flower?” Li Jiao and Mi Qi exchanged glances, both finding it hard to believe. This Dragon Blood Flower didn’t look like a flower at all, but more like the Spirit Essence of the Dragon Clan. Was this really the Dragon Blood Flower?

Ji Ying, excited, took out a sandalwood box, placed the Dragon Blood Flower inside, set a sealing restriction, and finally breathed a sigh of relief. “Luckily its grade isn’t high, or I really wouldn’t have been able to suppress it!”

The Dragon Blood Flower could only be found on Dragon Isle, and only the Dragon Clan could subdue it.

“Now that the spirit flower has bloomed, I’ll leave the pill refining to you, Master Ji.” Li Jiao suppressed his excitement and spoke respectfully.

“Mm, leave everything to me. Palace Master Li, please prepare a quiet place for me.” Ji Ying nodded.

“No problem at all!” Li Jiao was overjoyed. He had thought Ji Ying would take the Dragon Blood Flower back to Medicine Pill Valley to refine it, but from his words, it seemed he intended to start right away. How could he not be delighted?

Mi Qi forced a smile and said, “Brother Li, go make the arrangements. I’ll keep Master Ji company.”

Li Jiao nodded, “Alright, you two wait here!”

With that, he strode off.

Just then, Ji Ying suddenly took out a Transmission Compass from his robe. After sensing it briefly, his joyful expression turned to anger and he shouted, “Outrageous!”

Li Jiao was startled and quickly turned back. “What’s wrong?”

He thought he had somehow offended Ji Ying without realizing it.

Mi Qi also asked curiously, “Master Ji, what’s happened?”

Ji Ying’s face was grim. “Li Xuan has been beaten!”

“Li Xuan…” Mi Qi and Li Jiao muttered, then quickly realized—wasn’t that Master Ji’s disciple? That kid wasn’t very old, but whether in cultivation or alchemy talent, he was outstanding. Ji Ying valued him highly, and he’d come to Li Dragon Palace with Ji Ying recently, but had been sent out to collect Spiritual Medicine.

“Ice Heart Valley? Or Lingxiao Palace?” Mi Qi’s heart pounded as he asked excitedly.

If it was someone from Lingxiao Palace who beat Li Xuan, there would be a good show to watch. He and Li Jiao had been worrying about how to curb Lingxiao Palace’s arrogance, hoping to suppress them. They hadn’t come up with a plan yet, but could it be that the other side was delivering an opportunity right to their door?

“It was Lingxiao Palace!” Ji Ying was furious. “They beat my disciple and even stole the Source Crystals I gave him to buy Spiritual Medicine. Lingxiao Palace is simply lawless!”

“What?” Mi Qi was shocked. “They not only beat him, but also stole Source Crystals? This…”

He could hardly keep from laughing out loud. Lingxiao Palace, you’re courting your own destruction. People from Medicine Pill Valley aren’t to be trifled with!

“This is outrageous! Lingxiao Palace dares to disregard Medicine Pill Valley? I, Li Jiao, am irreconcilable with them!” Li Jiao also realized this was an opportunity and spoke forcefully.

“Palace Master Li, I’m afraid the pill refining will have to be delayed. This concerns the reputation of Medicine Pill Valley—I must go personally to see what Lingxiao Palace is up to!” Ji Ying said angrily. Although he’d promised Li Jiao first, this was a serious matter and couldn’t be delayed. If word got out, it would damage the reputation of Medicine Pill Valley and Great Emperor Miaodan.

“This… can’t be helped.” Li Jiao was filled with hatred. He’d been about to get the Dragon Blood Pill, but now something had gone wrong. He already didn’t have a good impression of Lingxiao Palace, and now he hated them even more.

“But Master Ji, it’s best not to go alone,” Mi Qi said with a frown.

Ji Ying snorted, “What? Do you think they’d dare act wildly even if I go in person?”

Mi Qi said seriously, “If it were Northern Territory cultivators, of course they wouldn’t dare offend you, Master Ji. But I’ve heard that the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace isn’t from the Northern Territory, but from the Southern Territory. He’s said to be domineering and ruthless—otherwise, he wouldn’t have wiped out Wenqing Sect. It’s best to be cautious with such a bloodthirsty person. Your status is too important to risk!”

“Brother Mi Qi is right,” Li Jiao agreed. “I’ve heard that kid Yang Kai is not only arrogant and unruly, but also colludes with the yao. We can’t be too careful.”

Ji Ying said, “Even if it’s a dragon’s lair or a tiger’s den, I have to go. My disciple was beaten, my Source Crystals were stolen—if I keep silent, how can I face my master back at Medicine Pill Valley?”

“Of course,” Mi Qi said, exchanging a glance with Li Jiao. “But Master Ji, you mustn’t go alone.”

“Exactly,” Li Jiao nodded. “How about this: Brother Mi Qi and I have nothing else to do, so we’ll go with you, Master Ji, to back you up and see if Lingxiao Palace really wants to rebel!”

“Brother Li’s suggestion is excellent, I agree. If Lingxiao Palace dares disrespect you, Master Ji, Brother Li and I will protect you with our lives.”

“In that case, I’ll trouble you both,” Ji Ying said, not refusing their offer. Truthfully, the way these two spoke had left him uneasy—now he was genuinely worried Lingxiao Palace might not give him any face and would make things difficult for him.

“There’s no time to lose, let’s go!”

…

On a mountaintop at Lingxiao Palace, Yang Kai lay on the ground, legs crossed, lazily basking in the sun.

The sun was bright, the sky was clear, the weather was beautiful, and he had a lovely companion by his side.

Zhu Qing stood nearby, gritting her teeth as she poured him wine, handed it to him, and spat out, “Drink!”

In order to stay by his side and not be left behind again, Zhu Qing had endured humiliation, putting up with all of Yang Kai’s nitpicking and orders. Now, she served him almost like a maid.

Her dignity had been trampled to pieces—never in her life had Zhu Qing found anyone more infuriating than the man before her.

“Ah…” Yang Kai opened his mouth, not moving at all.

Zhu Qing glared at him, placed the cup above his mouth, and poured the wine in.

He gulped it down.

“Ah…” Yang Kai opened his mouth again, eyes half-closed, looking completely content.

Flames of anger churned in Zhu Qing’s chest. She wanted nothing more than to burn him to ashes with dragon fire, but still picked up a grape-like berry and tossed it into Yang Kai’s mouth.

After chewing and swallowing, Yang Kai propped himself up on his elbow, looked at Zhu Qing, and asked, “Qing’er, what is your Dragon Isle like?”

Zhu Qing replied coolly, “If you want to know, just come with me.”

“You’re bringing this up again?” Yang Kai frowned. “Are we still friends or not?”

“You’re the one who asked.” A trace of panic flashed across Zhu Qing’s face. It had taken so much effort to become ‘friends’ with Yang Kai—if he cut her off now, it would be a disaster.

Yang Kai chuckled, looking at her with interest. “Don’t you have any other friends?”

Zhu Qing shook her head.

How could a member of the Dragon Clan have friends? Ridiculous.

Yang Kai said seriously, “Let me tell you, there are several kinds of friends. Right now, we’re friends, but only the most ordinary kind.”

“There are others?” Zhu Qing raised her brows, curious.

Yang Kai nodded solemnly. “Of course. Above ordinary friends, there are close friends, and above that… there are boyfriend and girlfriend! If we were boyfriend and girlfriend, our relationship would be on a whole different level. In that case, going to Dragon Isle with you might not be out of the question.”

Zhu Qing’s eyes lit up. “Then let’s be boyfriend and girlfriend!”

“Do you really want that?” Yang Kai looked at her seriously.

“Of course! Let’s be boyfriend and girlfriend!” Zhu Qing nodded firmly.

## Chapter 3128 — Paying Respects

Dressing turned into another lingering, intimate affair.

Yang Kai's heart ached as he looked at the finger marks scattered across that snow-white, delicate body, and he felt more and more like an utter bastard. What had possessed him to use so much force? He swept a large hand over her, pure energy surging from his palm and pouring into Su Yan's body, finally fading those red marks by quite a bit.

After they finally got dressed, the two flew side by side toward Jade Tree Peak. Su Yan's cheeks stayed flushed the entire way. Yang Kai kept stealing glances at her, feeling as if he had discovered a hidden side of Senior Sister, and a faint smugness spread through his heart.

That side... bloomed for him alone!

When they reached the house on Jade Tree Peak, they saw his old father tending the flowers and plants out front.

Yang Kai called out a greeting. Father and son exchanged a look, and Fourth Master Yang quietly made a gesture telling him everything was fine.

Father, you truly worked hard! Yang Kai was endlessly grateful.

Fourth Master Yang turned and called, "Suzhu, our son is here."

"Tell him to get lost!"

Yang Kai sniffed and looked at Fourth Master Yang with a wounded expression. This was what you called fine? Father, I trusted the wrong man. Give me back my Dragon Tendon Tiger Bone Pills!

"Mother!" Su Yan called sweetly.

"Su Yan is here?" Dong Suzhu froze for a moment, then hurriedly said, "Come in, quickly. Come talk with me."

Su Yan gave Yang Kai a look, and the two walked in together.

The furnishings inside were very simple. It was clear that only his father and mother lived on Jade Tree Peak, without even a servant to attend them. They passed through the main hall and entered a side room, where Dong Suzhu sat before a dressing mirror combing her hair, her black hair falling like a waterfall, smooth and glossy.

Su Yan saw this and immediately stepped forward, saying softly, "Mother, let me help."

Dong Suzhu smiled and handed her the comb. "Then I'll trouble you."

Su Yan said, “It is no trouble. It is only proper.”

Dong Suzhu said, "Daughters really are better. Little Su Yan, tell me, how wonderful would it have been if you were my daughter? Mother has wanted a daughter so badly she could go mad."

Su Yan pursed her lips and smiled. "I am your daughter. And Mother, I am not your only daughter. Although the other sisters are not at your side, they must all be thinking of you."

"That is true. Little Qingluo, Little Ningshang, and Little Xue Yue have people bring me letters every few days, all asking after me." The smile on her face suddenly vanished. Through the bronze mirror before her, she fixed her gaze on Yang Kai's back and snorted. "Unlike certain people. I carried him for ten months and gave birth to him, but once his wings hardened, he flapped off and flew away. How could he still have this mother in his heart?"

Yang Kai stammered, "Mother..."

"Oh..." Dong Suzhu looked astonished and spoke in a strange, sarcastic tone. "Who is this? How did he wander into my room? Boy, you have no manners at all. Did I allow you in? Su Yan, throw him out for me."

Su Yan lowered her head and said, "I cannot beat him."

Dong Suzhu immediately glared and slapped the chair. "That boy dares hit you? He must be tired of living!"

"No, no." Su Yan's face instantly flushed crimson. She could not help remembering last night's madness, and her body suddenly went a little weak.

Had she not been hit last night? And for quite a long time too. Her bottom still burned even now.

Dong Suzhu said, "Do not be afraid of him. So what if his cultivation is high? So what if he is a Star Lord? If he bullies you, tell me, and I will beat him."

As they spoke, Yang Kai walked over, crouched beside Dong Suzhu, and offered up his head.

"What are you doing!" Dong Suzhu lowered her head and glared at him.

Yang Kai chuckled. "Mother wants to hit someone, so your son brought himself over. Otherwise, Mother would have to walk those extra steps."

"Move your head away, you shameless thing." Dong Suzhu's attitude turned cold one moment and warm the next, cold toward Yang Kai and warm toward Su Yan, until even the side room seemed to swing between chilly and warm.

Yang Kai took Dong Suzhu's hand and placed it on top of his head.

Dong Suzhu said coldly, "Do not think this old lady cannot bear to hit you."

"As long as Mother can calm down, you can hit me however you like."

"You said it yourself!" Dong Suzhu gritted her teeth, raised her palm, and slapped down hard.

Yang Kai did not even blink.

Her palm stopped above his head, stirring up a gust of wind. Dong Suzhu glared at him. "Sooner or later, you will anger me to death!"

How could she bear to hit him? From the moment she carried him for ten months and gave birth to him, all the hopes of her life had rested on this boy. Even a scrape on his skin would hurt her to death, so how could she bear to strike him herself?

"You only dare because you know I dote on you." As she spoke, she actually began wiping at her eyes.

Yang Kai stood and wrapped an arm around her shoulders, then said to the woman in the mirror, "Mother, you must not cry, and you must not get angry either. If you get angry and cry too much, you might grow wrinkles."

"Wrinkles?" Dong Suzhu was startled, and the tears in her eyes were forced right back. She studied herself in the mirror. "Where are there wrinkles? Where? Do you see any?"

"Even if there are wrinkles, there is no need to be afraid." Yang Kai flipped his hand, and as if he had prepared it long ago, a jade box appeared before Dong Suzhu.

"What is this?" Dong Suzhu asked, taking the jade box.

Yang Kai said solemnly, "Take it when you find a chance. I guarantee Mother will keep your youth forever and remain forever sixteen."

Dong Suzhu immediately beamed. "It is that good?" She hastily put the jade box into her Spatial Ring, then said without meaning a word of it, "Coming back is enough. Why bring gifts too?"

Yang Kai said, "It is nothing precious. As long as Mother likes it."

"I like it, I like it." How could Dong Suzhu not like it? She held Yang Kai's hand with one hand and stroked his cheek with the other. "You must have suffered quite a bit outside all these years, haven't you?"

"Not at all." Yang Kai quickly shook his head. "Your son has always been the one throwing his weight around. Who would dare walk all over me?" Anyone who dared had been slaughtered.

"Listen to yourself." Dong Suzhu knocked him on the head, knowing Yang Kai did not want her to worry and was unwilling to tell her much about such things.

Although he had returned safely now, the outside world, for all its wonders, was full of danger as well. He had been gone for several dozen years and had grown tremendously. How could he not have suffered? She feared he had brushed against life and death more than once.

"Now that you have come back this time, are you leaving again?" Dong Suzhu asked.

"I am." Yang Kai nodded.

Dong Suzhu's face immediately darkened. She let go of his hand and said, "Go then, go then. Go right now. If you leave again this time, never come back. Just let your mother die here."

Yang Kai said helplessly, "I am leaving, but I will take all of you with me."

Only then did Dong Suzhu understand. She looked at him reproachfully. "Stinking boy, why didn't you say that clearly?" After a pause, she asked, "Where are you taking us?"

"The Star Realm."

"Is that where you have been staying these past several dozen years?"

"Mm. I founded a Lingxiao Palace over there. The palace has experts as numerous as clouds. Once you and Father arrive, you can simply cultivate in peace. There will be no disputes or danger."

"You have a Sect over there too?" Dong Suzhu was endlessly amazed. Although she did not know what sort of place the Star Realm was, it was certainly far more formidable than Dark Star. Yet in only several dozen years, Yang Kai had actually founded a Sect and established his own faction there. How could an ordinary person be capable of such a thing?

Pride surged in her heart. This was her son!

Dong Suzhu was very curious about the Star Realm and Lingxiao Palace, and she held Yang Kai's hand, asking questions without end.

Outside the room, Fourth Master Yang tilted his ear to listen and smiled faintly, thinking that last night, this old man had displayed everything he had learned in his lifetime and even unlocked eighteen positions, aided by the Dragon Tendon Tiger Bone Pill. How could there be no effect?

This sacrifice had been worth it after all.

The sun set and the moon rose. Their conversation lasted all the way into evening.

Most of the time, it was Yang Kai who spoke, recounting all kinds of things he had seen and heard in the Star Realm, while Dong Suzhu and Su Yan listened quietly, both filled with yearning. In truth, as long as this man was there, it did not matter where they were. This family, whose members were not all bound by blood, had been gathered together by him.

Fourth Master Yang came in several times, busy serving tea and pouring water. He was quite indignant, but under Dong Suzhu’s tyranny, he did not dare act up.

"Right, Mother wants to ask you something. Answer honestly."

Dong Suzhu's expression suddenly turned serious.

Yang Kai said, "What is it? Mother, just ask."

Dong Suzhu glanced at Su Yan. Su Yan misunderstood her meaning and hurriedly said, "I will go back first."

Dong Suzhu took her hand and said, "Do not leave. This matter concerns you too. Sit there and listen."

"Yes." Although Su Yan did not know what Dong Suzhu wanted to ask, she could only sit quietly after hearing this.

Yang Kai inexplicably grew a little nervous. He had no idea what his mother wanted to ask that required such solemnity.

“Mother is asking you…” Dong Suzhu looked at Yang Kai with a serious expression, “After spending several dozen years outside, did you go around attracting women and flirting everywhere?”

Su Yan's expression turned serious, and she lifted her eyes to look at him.

Cold sweat streamed down Yang Kai's forehead.

"Very good!" Seeing this, how could Dong Suzhu not know the answer? She immediately flew into a rage and jabbed Yang Kai in the forehead. "You really went and did it!"

Yang Kai lowered his head and said nothing.

Dong Suzhu said, "You have Su Yan, Ningshang, Qingluo, and Xue Yue. Are you still not satisfied? How can you men all be like this? Tell me, what exactly were you thinking?"

Yang Kai muttered, "Accidents. They were accidents."

"Accidents?" Dong Suzhu sneered. "There are so many beauties under heaven. Do you plan to have an accident with every one of them?" She pointed at Fourth Master Yang, who stood nearby. "Why can't you learn from your father? Look at him. Has he ever fooled around with other women?"

Fourth Master Yang muttered, "There is nothing wrong with having three wives and four concubines."

"What did you say?" Dong Suzhu glared.

Fourth Master Yang glared at Yang Kai. "Boy, you have gone too far this time."

Only then did Dong Suzhu's expression ease slightly. She said in a low voice, "I do not care what accidents you had. When you return to the Star Realm this time, drive all those women away. Having Su Yan and the other three is enough. I only acknowledge the four of them."

“Mother.” Su Yan linked arms with Dong Suzhu and smiled, “Junior Brother is not a fickle or heartless person, nor is he someone who likes to attract women everywhere. Surely many things must have happened between him and those girls for matters to reach this point. Moreover, he has been alone outside for so many years, so it was only right that he had someone by his side to look after him. Ningshang and I should even thank those sisters, so please do not make things difficult for Junior Brother.”

Dong Suzhu said, "Having room in your heart for others is a good thing, but not everything can be tolerated. Especially not this. In matters like these, you must make him understand exactly where the line is."

Su Yan smiled faintly. "Junior Brother knows his limits. He is not a child anymore. It really is all right."

Dong Suzhu looked at her and sighed. "My Yang Family has wronged all of you."

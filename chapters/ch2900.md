## Chapter 2900 — The Black Pupil Reappears

Within the Demon Domain, the ancient demons came in all shapes and sizes, but without exception, each one was an expert.

Mo Duo knew of a kind of ancient demon whose body was unimaginably massive, like a towering mountain, and who possessed a single eye. That eye could unleash an inconceivable divine ability.

So when he saw the abnormality at Yang Kai’s right eye, countless memories about this kind of ancient demon involuntarily surfaced in his mind.

His heart skipped a beat, and he secretly prayed that his guess was wrong, because if it was right, then this outsider might truly be able to match him.

“Black-Pupil Purgatory—Boundless Darkness!”

The low chant from the outsider’s lips made Mo Duo feel as if he had fallen into an ice cellar. As the words fell, the entire world was suddenly plunged into darkness. The dark clouds overhead vanished, the colorful surroundings disappeared, and everything was shrouded in pitch-black.

In this world of darkness, a gigantic single eye hung high in the sky. In this pitch-black world, only it stood out, clear to all. Or rather, it was not seen, but felt. In this utterly dark environment, every living being could distinctly sense that enormous eye. It was like a black moon, suspended high above, overlooking all things below.

All the demons unconsciously stopped what they were doing and looked in the direction of the eye, panic appearing on their faces. Immediately after, they prostrated themselves in worship—even the Demon Kings were no exception. They suddenly became dazed and blank, completely forgetting what they were doing. Like the other ordinary demons, they revealed expressions of reverence toward that enormous eye.

The single eye hanging in the sky seemed to turn this world into a prison, and all the demons trapped within lost themselves.

Mo Duo’s expression was one of shock, for he realized things were developing in the worst possible direction.

That single eye was indeed the one possessed by the ancient demon he knew of, and this so-called Black-Pupil Purgatory was also the innate divine ability of that one-eyed ancient demon.

Had this outsider actually obtained the ancient demon’s black pupil? How did he get it? And how could he fuse with it? Since he had fused with it, why hadn’t he been assimilated?

Countless questions churned in Mo Duo’s mind, but he could not find a reasonable explanation.

At that moment, Yang Kai’s attack arrived.

The gigantic eye hanging in the sky suddenly blinked, and then a mysterious light shot down, catching Mo Duo off guard and striking him squarely on the head.

Mo Duo’s expression went blank for a moment. Even though he was a Demon Saint, after being struck by such an attack, he involuntarily experienced hallucinations. It was as if he had returned to the Demon Domain, sitting in his own bedchamber, enjoying a comfortable life, far from conflict and slaughter. Such a life calmed his heart and gradually eroded his fighting spirit.

The large hand holding Yang Kai loosened its grip, and he seized the opportunity to leap out.

However, Mo Duo was, after all, a Demon Saint. Although affected by the black pupil, he managed to dispel the hallucinations in his mind in just an instant. Without any hesitation, his fan-like hand swept toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai reached out and grabbed in the void, and a warhammer appeared in his palm.

He ignored Mo Duo’s attack, raised the warhammer, and smashed it fiercely toward Mo Duo’s forehead.

With a thunderous boom, both attacks landed at the same time. Yang Kai’s three-zhang-tall body was swatted away by Mo Duo like a ragged sack, but Mo Duo’s forehead was also struck hard by the Demonic Warhammer, instantly splitting open and bleeding. The tremendous force pierced through his body and crashed downward, making it impossible for him to maintain his climbing posture. His massive body plummeted toward the ground like a falling meteorite.

With a rumble, Mo Duo’s body crushed and rolled over the demons below, who had already lost their wits and were still worshipping the black pupil in the sky, causing enormous casualties in an instant.

Mo Duo shook his head and stood up, blood from his forehead blurring his vision. Yet his expression became even more ferocious. He closed his eyes, resisting the various illusory powers from the black pupil above, while searching for Yang Kai’s whereabouts in the darkness.

Yang Kai had no intention of hiding. After entering the Demonic State, his temperament had changed drastically. Even with the Dragon Transformation body and the Warm Spirit Lotus protecting his Divine Soul, the release of Demonic Qi and the use of innate divine abilities still had a huge impact on him.

The desire to kill deep within him was infinitely magnified. In his eyes, there was only one target—to kill that thirty-zhang-tall Demon Saint! For this, he was willing to pay any price.

After being swatted away by Mo Duo, Yang Kai quickly stabilized himself, raised the Demonic Warhammer, and charged at Mo Duo with fierce momentum. The Laws of Space were instinctively activated, and he appeared directly in front of Mo Duo.

With such an obvious attack trajectory, Mo Duo naturally had no trouble grasping it. He raised his fist and struck forward to meet the attack.

The Demonic Warhammer collided with the giant fist, and wild energy swept out, turning into blades that reaped lives. In an instant, countless demons around them were killed or injured.

Mo Duo’s fist bones cracked, and Yang Kai was also sent flying, spitting blood in midair, battered and miserable.

Yet he seemed completely unaware. After stabilizing himself, he charged at Mo Duo again.

In an instant, the two became entangled in a fierce battle, attacking and counterattacking relentlessly.

At this moment, Mo Duo’s massive body gave him an advantage. Although Yang Kai inflicted some injuries with each attack, the Demon Saint’s physique was so formidable that these wounds had little effect. On the contrary, every time Yang Kai lunged, he was met with a storm-like counterattack, sending him flying again and again.

But right now, Yang Kai was like an undying cockroach. Relying on the innate divine ability of Black-Pupil Purgatory, even though he was at a great disadvantage, he still fought Mo Duo to the death, refusing to yield an inch.

The aftermath of their battle brought disaster to the demons.

Beside the Divine Tree, hundreds of thousands or even over a million demons were gathered. When Yang Kai unleashed Black-Pupil Purgatory, he enveloped them all. Facing the ancient demon’s black pupil, they prostrated themselves in worship and fell into various hallucinations, unable to dodge or retreat.

The aftermath of the battle felled them in swathes. In less than the time it takes an incense stick to burn, half of that vast demon horde had died or been injured. The ground was awash with blood and piled with corpses, resembling a true hell.

Mo Duo was already furious, forced to watch helplessly as the giant tree continued to grow. His mood had never been worse.

If he had a choice, he would not have become entangled with Yang Kai at all. His only goal was to destroy the Divine Tree and prevent it from sealing the Boundary Passage. Before Yang Kai entered the Demonic State, he hadn’t thought this task difficult—it was just a tree that only knew how to grow, and he could destroy it with a wave of his hand. But after Yang Kai entered the Demonic State, he found it was nearly impossible.

Unless he could kill that outsider called Shaman Niu first. But if he could have done so, he would have already—why would he wait until now?

After exchanging more than a dozen moves, over a dozen wounds of varying sizes had appeared on Mo Duo’s body. Taking advantage of the moment after sending Yang Kai flying, he lifted his head to look upward, his face turning ashen.

He realized that after all this delay, the giant tree was about to reach the area beneath the Boundary Passage. Its massive canopy was nearly sealing off the passage, and at this rate, in just half an incense stick’s time, it would truly block the Boundary Passage.

With a furious roar, Mo Duo ignored Yang Kai’s entanglement, strode toward the Divine Tree, and leapt up when he was a hundred zhang away.

His thirty-zhang-tall body displayed unimaginable agility, shooting up several hundred zhang in an instant and grabbing a branch tightly, then climbing upward with both hands and feet.

Before he could get far, Yang Kai appeared above his head like a ghost.

The Demonic Warhammer, wrapped in dense Demonic Qi and radiating a destructive aura, smashed down fiercely at his head.

Mo Duo’s expression changed slightly, and he raised his hand to block.

The warhammer struck his arm, breaking the bone. Mo Duo’s massive body suddenly dropped, nearly falling off, but he still refused to let go, desperately maintaining his balance.

The second hammer blow fell, followed by the third, the fourth…

Boom, boom, boom, boom…

Mo Duo was like a punching bag, completely stripped of the Demon Saint’s dignity. The arm he used to block was already twisted and deformed, like a noodle, the bones shattered.

At last, he could no longer hold on and was knocked off the tree by Yang Kai.

Yet as he fell, he grinned ferociously.

Because in the sky, another Mo Duo had appeared at some point. Though not thirty zhang tall, his body was still over ten zhang—clearly a clone.

No one knew when Mo Duo had created this clone. Even Yang Kai was completely unaware—or perhaps simply could not sense it. After entering the Demonic State, Yang Kai’s perception was greatly diminished, his eyes fixed on a single target and his awareness of everything else severely dulled.

Mo Duo had taken advantage of this, easily sending his clone out.

At this moment, the clone stood before the black pupil hanging in the sky, his hands constantly forming seals, chanting incantations, and emanating an extremely mysterious aura, looking utterly bizarre.

Judging by the clone’s posture, he had clearly maintained this state for some time and was only now preparing to complete the technique.

Suddenly, the clone stopped moving, raised his hand, and pointed at the black pupil.

A point of light abruptly appeared on the black pupil. This light seemed to possess a powerful infectious force, and as soon as it appeared, it rapidly spread outward. In the blink of an eye, it occupied half of the black pupil, and it showed no sign of stopping.

Yang Kai finally sensed something was wrong. He halted in midair and turned to look at the black pupil.

Then he roared in fury and lunged at the black pupil, as if to stop the spread of the light.

But it was too late. When the light filled the black pupil, brightness returned to the world, the illusory world of darkness was shattered, and Yang Kai, who was rushing over, let out a miserable cry as if struck by lightning. Blood gushed from his right eye, and his whole body plummeted to the ground, smashing a pit into the earth.

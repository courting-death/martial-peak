## Chapter 2746 — The Highest Bid Wins

"Only Earth-Grade, huh?" Yang Kai murmured, pondering, "You want to enter and see if you can break through to the Emperor Realm?"

Bian Yuqing smiled slightly, "One must at least try, even if the chances are slim."

She didn’t have much confidence in herself. Yang Kai figured that even if she really entered the Earth-Grade secret cultivation ground, she likely still wouldn’t break through to the Emperor Realm. For martial artists, mindset was a crucial factor in cultivation.

After a brief pause, Yang Kai said, "Let’s go."

"Where to?" Bian Yuqing asked.

"To Linghu Palace. I haven’t been here in years. You seem quite familiar with Linghu Palace, so be my guide."

Bian Yuqing nodded, "Alright."

Yang Kai urged his Emperor Yuan, wrapped her up, and sped toward Linghu Palace, not returning the Qing Jade Command Token.

Bian Yuqing hesitated to speak, but ultimately didn’t ask for it back. If Yang Kai truly intended to seize her Qing Jade Command Token, she wouldn’t be able to keep it.

A thousand li passed in no time, and they arrived quickly.

Hovering in midair, Yang Kai gazed at Linghu Palace, marveling at the sight. In just a few years, this place had grown into a city rivaling Fenglin City in scale, and it seemed to be expanding still. Perhaps in a few more years, Linghu Palace would swallow Fenglin City whole, merging the two.

Seeing Yang Kai about to stride right in, Bian Yuqing hurriedly said, "Entering Linghu City requires paying Source Crystals—ten Middle-Level Source Crystals per person."

"That’s a steep toll," Yang Kai clicked his tongue. He’d visited many cities, but most charged tolls in Lower-Level Source Crystals, usually ten Lower-Level Source Crystals. Yet Linghu City demanded ten Middle-Level Source Crystals, a hundred times more than others.

"It’s worth it. The Spiritual Qi in Linghu City isn’t as rich as the cultivation grounds in Linghu Palace, but it’s still quite dense. Many who can’t obtain Command Tokens settle here and live year-round."

Yang Kai nodded. He too had climbed up from the bottom, so he understood the hardships of low-level martial artists. For them, the Spiritual Qi in Linghu City was a blessing. Ten Middle-Level Source Crystals was well worth it.

As they spoke, the two reached the city gate. Fortunately, it was spacious and not crowded. When it was Yang Kai’s turn, he tossed out an Upper-Level Source Crystal. The gate guard took it and let him pass without hesitation.

It wasn’t that Yang Kai was showing off his wealth; he’d simply run out of Middle-Level and Lower-Level Source Crystals, and all the Source Crystals in his ring were Upper-Level.

As they stepped into Linghu City, a wave of bustling energy washed over them. The streets teemed with martial artists, flowing like a river, countless as stars, coming and going in an endless stream. The lively atmosphere was palpable—shops lined both sides of the road, packed tightly together, while many simply set up stalls in open spaces, displaying items they no longer needed for sale. Most stalls attracted crowds, with the sounds of bargaining and haggling filling the air without pause.

Yang Kai clicked his tongue in wonder. Dao Origin Realm cultivators were everywhere. Some Emperor Realm experts even hid their cultivation and mingled in the crowd. Ten years ago, this was impossible—back then, the strongest near Fenglin City were only Second Stage Dao Origin Realm. Dao Origin Realm cultivators could be counted on two hands, but now, they were everywhere.

All these changes were thanks to the rise of Linghu Palace.

Yang Kai strolled through the city, soaking in the lively atmosphere, feeling a sense of unreality.

Bian Yuqing occasionally told Yang Kai about Linghu City. He nodded repeatedly. After Biyu Sect disbanded, she and Kou Wu came here to make a living, so she knew the place better than Yang Kai.

About an hour later, the two reached the very center of Linghu City—Linghu Palace.

The massive palace was like a city within a city, and at its gates, many people were lined up.

Yang Kai saw clearly that there were three lines. The leftmost had about five hundred people, the middle about half that number, and the rightmost fewer than seventy. The three lines were clearly divided according to strength: those in the leftmost line were mostly at the Void King Realm or below, with a few down-and-out Dao Origin Realm cultivators mixed in.

The middle line was made up primarily of First- and Second-Stage Dao Origin Realm experts, with a few at the Void King Realm among them.

The rightmost line consisted of at least Second-Stage Dao Origin Realm masters, many at the Third Stage, and even two Emperor Realm powerhouses.

Yang Kai pondered, "These three lines are for different Command Tokens, right?"

Bian Yuqing nodded, "That's right. The line on the left is for those holding White Jade Command Tokens, the middle is for Qing Jade Command Tokens, and the right is for Red Jade Command Tokens. In Linghu Palace, the Human-Grade cultivation grounds are the most numerous, Earth-Grade are fewer, and Heaven-Grade are the rarest. So, even though the line for White Jade Command Tokens looks the longest, it actually moves along quite quickly."

Yang Kai nodded, walked to a more open spot, looked around, and showed a hint of satisfaction.

Bian Yuqing didn’t know what he was up to, but didn’t ask, just watched curiously.

Suddenly, Yang Kai called out loudly, "I have Qing Jade Command Token number 3912 for sale—first come, first served!"

His voice wasn’t loud, but with power behind it, it probably echoed throughout Linghu City.

Bian Yuqing’s face changed, exclaiming, "What are you doing!"

She had spent all her assets to get that Qing Jade Command Token, and only needed to wait two more months for her turn. It was crucial for her to advance to Emperor, yet Yang Kai was selling it without her consent. If she really wanted to sell, she would have taken it to the auction house long ago, not kept it until now.

"Trust me," Yang Kai glanced at her.

Bian Yuqing fell silent, frowned, and nodded, saying no more.

Yang Kai was already an Emperor. If he meant her harm, she couldn’t resist.

After Yang Kai’s shout, nearby martial artists stopped in their tracks, staring in surprise. The next moment, the crowd surged, bodies flickering as they rushed toward him.

In the blink of an eye, Yang Kai and Bian Yuqing were surrounded layer upon layer. The sudden situation made Bian Yuqing nervous—many of the approaching martial artists radiated powerful auras, far beyond her ability to handle.

Unconsciously, she moved closer to Yang Kai, seeking a bit of safety.

"Number 3912? Is it real?"

"Show it to us!"

"Kid, if you dare trick us, you’re dead!"

Many martial artists in Linghu City were after Command Tokens, so they were very familiar with the numbers. Number 3912 Qing Jade Command Token was extremely close to the front—just two months away from its turn.

Usually, such low-numbered Command Tokens only appeared at auctions, not even in the black market. Now, one was suddenly being offered in public, so everyone was excited.

The crowd erupted in noise.

Yang Kai ignored the threats, continuing to call out.

More people flew in from afar, and soon, over five hundred martial artists had gathered around Yang Kai and Bian Yuqing, even filling the air above.

If Linghu City didn’t forbid fighting, someone would have tried to snatch it already.

Seeing enough people had gathered, Yang Kai finally took out the Qing Jade Command Token, first showing the front, then flipping it and holding it high for all to see.

The numbers on the back shone brightly in the sunlight, clearly visible.

"It really is number 3912!"

"Such a low-numbered Command Token hasn’t appeared in ages. With this number, you only need to wait two months!"

"Kid, I was first! Sell me the Command Token, I guarantee you’ll be satisfied with the price!" shouted a middle-aged elder, his hair white, but his cultivation only at the Second Stage Dao Origin Realm. His body was filled with deathly energy, his blood weak—he seemed near the end of his life.

If he could break through further in Martial Dao, he might live a few more years, so he cared deeply about the Command Token.

Another Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator, hovering in midair, said calmly, "Kid, how much for the Command Token? Name your price!"

Yang Kai smiled, looked around, and said, "Looks like everyone’s interested in the Command Token. Good! In that case, let’s auction it right here—the highest bid wins!"

The middle-aged elder’s face changed, growling, "You just said first come, first served! How can you go back on your word?"

Yang Kai grinned and said, “Elder, did you really take my words at face value? For something as precious as this Command Token, of course it has to be auctioned off.”

“You—!” The elder’s beard trembled. “You’re going too far!”

"If it’s an auction, what’s the starting price?" asked the Dao Origin Realm cultivator in the air.

Yang Kai replied, "Bid as you wish—no starting price."

With so many people competing, there was no need for a starting price. The price would naturally rise in the scramble.

As soon as he finished, someone shouted, "Twenty million!"

"Twenty million is the price for buying a Command Token from Linghu Palace. You dare offer that? I bid thirty million!"

"Thirty-one million!"

"Thirty-two million!"

The bids rose one after another, and in the blink of an eye, the Qing Jade Command Token reached thirty-six million.

Yang Kai quietly asked Bian Yuqing, "Is this in Lower-Level or Middle-Level Source Crystals?"

Bian Yuqing replied, "Middle-Level Source Crystals. White Jade Command Tokens are calculated in Lower-Level Source Crystals, while Red Jade Command Tokens use Upper-Level Source Crystals."

"How much did you pay for this?" Yang Kai asked again.

"Twenty million Middle-Level Source Crystals!"

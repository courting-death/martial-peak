## Chapter 2960 — I Also Know Sacred Art

The small fire snake tumbled nimbly through the air, yet its movements brought unfathomable shock to everyone watching.

Fu Shu had already gone dumbstruck. He stared blankly at the fire snake, completely forgetting that he was still in the middle of a fight.

The goateed old man shouted explosively, "First Prince, this is not a Sacred Art! You must not let his methods blind your eyes."

"This really isn't a Sacred Art?" Fu Shu turned his head and asked.

The goateed old man was so angry that his beard bristled wildly. He repeated once more, "This old man guarantees it with his life. This is absolutely not some Sacred Art. It only... looks somewhat similar to a Sacred Art, that is all. It is nothing to fear!"

Fu Shu's spirits shook when he heard this, and he shouted angrily, "I knew it! How could someone as insignificant as you possibly display a Sacred Art? So it turned out you were only deceiving me. You are dead for sure!"

He wanted to draw his bow and nock another arrow, but Yang Kai had already flicked his finger. The fire snake suddenly snapped straight and shot toward him like an arrow loosed from the string.

Fu Shu paid no heed to the incoming fire snake attack whatsoever. After all, he knew that the Sacred Tree would grant him powerful protection, and there was no way Yang Kai's attacks could harm him—just as had been the case before.

The fire snake did not seem very powerful. It was only an extremely ordinary Fire Snake Technique, a Shamanic Art that even a Shaman Warrior could perform in ancient times.

With a rumble, the fire snake struck Fu Shu's body, yet it produced an unexpected effect. The protective green light that had emerged around Fu Shu's body was actually smashed apart by the fire snake in a single blow. Although the fire snake also vanished at the same time, the scattered sparks splashed all over Fu Shu, scalding him until he cried out loudly.

The goateed old man's eyes narrowed, and he faintly felt that something was wrong.

Logically speaking, as the First Prince, Fu Shu had the protection of the Sacred Tree within Human Sovereign City, and only existences with Eight-Leaf Bodies or above were qualified to injure him. Yet this inexplicable outsider had actually done it. This simply exceeded the limits of his understanding, and no matter how he thought about it, he could not understand why things had turned out this way.

And there was also that Secret Technique. Why did it look exactly like the Sacred Tree's power? Apart from lacking the additional strength of the Sacred Tree, it was practically a Sacred Art cast from another mold.

"I also have the Sacred Tree's protection, you know," Yang Kai said, smiling as he looked at the First Prince.

"Nonsense!" Fu Shu brushed the sparks from his body and cursed angrily, "Who do you think you are?"

"Whether it is nonsense or not, First Prince, you can take a look for yourself." As Yang Kai spoke, he suddenly raised his hand. Another fire snake appeared in front of him, followed closely by another, then a third and a fourth...

"You, you, you..." Fu Shu was shocked beyond measure. "Why do you not need to chant an incantation?"

"Why would I need to chant?" Yang Kai continued to cast Shamanic Arts, and they were still simple Fire Snake Techniques.

"Naturally, one has to chant an incantation to display a Sacred Art!" Fu Shu felt as though common sense had been broken, and found it somewhat hard to accept.

Yang Kai scoffed coldly, "How could trash like you even be mentioned in the same breath as this Young Master? This Young Master can conjure Sacred Arts with a mere flick of the wrist."

As he spoke, dense fire snakes materialized before him, as if an entire serpent's lair had been overturned. Yet as time passed, the newly formed fire snakes began to undergo a peculiar transformation.

Threads of green light unexpectedly appeared atop their fiery red color. Although they were not very eye-catching, they were undoubtedly green lights. The color of the green light became stronger and stronger, until the last few fire snakes that appeared were actually almost entirely emerald green.

Yang Kai finally ceased his display and smiled brightly at Fu Shu. "See? I told you I also have the Sacred Tree's protection."

"Hiss..." The goateed old man sucked in a cold breath, sweat pouring down his forehead. The development of the situation had exceeded his expectations. This outsider had truly obtained the Sacred Tree's protection and displayed a Sacred Art that carried the power of the Sacred Tree. It was absolutely impossible for the Third Prince to make him submit now. It could even be said that this person's arrival was enough to raise a storm in Human Sovereign City that would not be seen in ten thousand years, and if things went poorly, it might change Human Sovereign City's existing structure.

He could no longer bring forth any thought of recruiting Yang Kai. All he thought about was how he should withdraw safely.

Over there, Fu Shu was also so shocked that he could not speak a single word. The huge emotional shift triggered a series of chain reactions. First, the Bloodlust Art reached its limit and was dispelled. Immediately afterward, the backlash from his unrestrained use of Sacred Arts erupted. Fu Shu screamed miserably and fell to the ground, covering his nose with his hands, yet no matter what he did, he could not stop the blood from flowing. His Qi and blood power weakened to the extreme in an instant, as though he might die at any moment. He wailed loudly and cried out to those around him for help, but who could lend a hand at such a time?

He turned his head and looked at the goateed old man with a smile.

Cold sweat slid down the latter's forehead as he said hoarsely, "Could it be... you are a member of the Imperial Family lost outside?"

Yang Kai said with an indifferent expression, "Your imagination is somewhat rich."

"Then why... can you mobilize the Sacred Tree's power?" The goateed old man could not understand this point no matter what.

"Who told you only the Imperial Family could receive the Sacred Tree's protection?" Yang Kai sneered.

The goateed old man was startled. Thinking carefully, this deeply rooted belief had completely come from the common sense he had accepted since childhood. It seemed that no one had ever truly said only the Imperial Family could receive the Sacred Tree's protection and mobilize the Sacred Tree's power, yet no one had ever doubted this claim either.

The goateed old man suddenly felt his mouth go dry, and faintly sensed that an enormous secret was about to be unveiled before him, revealing the truth.

He asked in a trembling voice, "What kind of person can receive the Sacred Tree's protection?"

Yang Kai said coldly, "Old thing, you have rebellious bones. Why are you asking this? Are you harboring some idea?"

The goateed old man was both shocked and furious, "Do not speak such nonsense." As he spoke, he looked left and right, as if afraid someone had heard those words.

Yang Kai coldly snorted, "Go back and tell that master of yours not to come bother me again in the future. This Young Master has no intention of provoking anyone, nor any intention of becoming enemies with anyone. I merely ended up stranded here by accident with my wife, and once we find a way out, we will naturally leave. If anyone dares come looking for trouble... hmph, hmph."

The goateed old man felt as if he had received a great pardon from a dream and nodded repeatedly, "This old man will certainly convey Your Excellency's words. As for what decision the Third Prince makes, that is not something this old man can interfere with."

"I also know you do not have the ability to interfere. Scram." Yang Kai waved his hand as if shooing away a fly.

The goateed old man immediately fled. How would he dare remain here any longer?

The enemy that had been fighting fiercely against him not long ago had unexpectedly fallen first. Yang Kai pouted dismissively, too lazy to kick a man while he was down. Fu Shu had never been worthy of his attention to begin with—he could only stand on equal footing with Yang Kai by relying on the Sacred Tree's power. Now that Yang Kai also stood on the same starting line, Fu Shu was no longer worth considering.

In the sky not far away, Zhu Qing was engaged in a bloody battle with those thirty-plus cultivators. Although those thirty-plus people had been enhanced by Fu Shu's Bloodlust Art and Chain of Life and were able to fight together, how could they be a match for a member of the Dragon Clan?

The Bloodlust Art had only stimulated their Qi and blood power, making their flesh stronger and making them braver and fearless. Although it had the ability to improve their strength, there was no way for it to improve them too much all at once, while Chain of Life could only link their lives together and improve their ability to withstand attacks.

Their offensive ability still remained around the level of their own strength.

Zhu Qing found dealing with them simply far too easy. The situation had always been completely one-sided. However, because of the existence of Chain of Life, Zhu Qing still found it rather troublesome to finish them off.

With a raise of Yang Kai's hand, the fire snakes that had been hovering in front of him all rushed over there with a roar and struck into the crowd at once.

The Chain of Life connecting their Qi and blood power and vitality instantly became like a balloon punctured full of holes by countless fire snakes, and its strength leaked away completely.

The Chain of Life technique was dispelled, and the connection was severed. The thirty-plus people were suddenly swept into Zhu Qing's violent aura, were heavily injured in an instant, spat blood one after another, and fell from the sky like dumplings dropping into a pot.

An ordinary Chain of Life could not possibly be dispelled so easily, but the Shamanic Art Fu Shu displayed had been strengthened by the Sacred Tree's power. Now that Yang Kai interfered with it using power from the same source, Chain of Life collapsed on its own.

Since the enemies had suddenly become so unable to withstand a single blow, Zhu Qing was also too lazy to pursue and kill them. Bullying such weaklings was a disgrace to the Dragon Clan. With a flicker of her body, she flew to Yang Kai's side, glanced at Fu Shu lying in a pool of blood, and said in disgust, "How is this man still alive?"

Yang Kai said in great shock, "You want to slaughter him? Truly, a woman’s heart is most cruel.”

Zhu Qing coldly snorted, "What is the point of letting this kind of trash live?"

"What you said is also right." Yang Kai suddenly raised his head and looked toward a certain place in the crowd, "Your Excellency has watched the show for so long, shouldn't you come out now? You heard it too. If you still do not come out, my wife is going to kill this First Prince, so do not cry when the time comes!"

As soon as Yang Kai's voice fell, a figure suddenly flashed by and appeared directly in front of the First Prince. This person was very tall, but he was nothing but skin and bones, as if malnourished. Standing there, he looked like a bamboo pole and was especially eye-catching.

The agonized Fu Shu saw this person, and his expression shook. Pointing at him, he said, "You, you, you... you are..."

The thin bamboo pole said nothing. He bent down and stuffed a Spiritual Pill into Fu Shu's mouth, then knocked him unconscious with a palm strike. Reaching out, he lifted Fu Shu up, turned around, and coldly looked at Yang Kai.

"Oh? Third-Stage Emperor!" Yang Kai's eyes narrowed slightly.

Although he had long known that Human Sovereign City's foundation was not ordinary, seeing a Third-Stage Emperor so quickly still surprised Yang Kai somewhat. The skinny man’s aura was exceptionally profound, no weaker than that of Qingyang Shrine’s Hall Master Wen Zishan or Xiao Yuyang of Star God Palace.

In other words, he was an expert of the same caliber as Hall Master Wen, Elder Xiao, and the others—not some empty showpiece. If they truly came to blows, Yang Kai was not at all certain he could defeat him.

Of course, in Human Sovereign City, with the Sacred Tree's protection, crushing him should not be much of a problem. Yang Kai was now also one who enjoyed the Sacred Tree's protection. The reason he had continuously displayed the Fire Snake Technique earlier was precisely to establish a connection with the Sacred Tree.

The Sacred Tree did not protect some Imperial Family. It only protected Shamans. When Yang Kai had seen the First Prince display Shamanic Arts earlier, he had already formed some guesses. To confirm his guesses, he had displayed the power of a Shaman.

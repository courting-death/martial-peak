## Chapter 3034 — Dumbstruck

Lu Sanniang thought about it and realized he was right. Things were different now. During the years she had lived here, anyone who was not of Dragon descent, whether human or Yao, had not been permitted to stray too far from the island where they resided.

Now that the palace was being constructed, even if Yang Kai encountered a member of the Dragon Clan while he was out, he would have a reasonable explanation.

Even so, she still could not put her mind at ease. "But..."

Yang Kai raised a hand. "If you wish to come with me, we will set out now."

He had no intention of remaining on this island. No one was paying attention to him at the moment, making this the perfect opportunity to seek news of Zhu Qing.

Lu Sanniang seemed to see his determination. After pondering briefly, she said, "My daughter and I won't go with you. You must be extremely careful on your own."

Although her cultivation was decent, Lu Yuqin was too weak. If they truly accompanied Yang Kai, she would inevitably become a burden. It was better for them to remain here; that way, even if Yang Kai encountered danger, he could escape more easily.

Yang Kai frowned and looked in a certain direction. "But that Yuan Wu..."

If he left, Yuan Wu might continue making trouble for Lu Sanniang and her daughter. This was Yang Kai's only concern at present. Since Li Jiao had entrusted the mother and daughter to him, he naturally had to take good care of them. It would be irresponsible to simply leave them behind.

He could also send Lu Sanniang and her daughter into the Small Xuan Realm, but he was unwilling to do so unless absolutely necessary.

Lu Sanniang smiled faintly. "Brother Yang, there is no need to worry. I lived here for several hundred years. If I truly wish to hide, he won't be able to find me."

As soon as she said this, Yang Kai knew his earlier guess had been correct. This Spirit Island was indeed the lair of the Dragon Clan member who had harmed Lu Sanniang. If so, he had nothing to worry about.

Looking toward the other side of the island, Yang Kai asked, "What is the master of this place called?"

Lu Sanniang fell silent for a moment before softly answering, "Fu Chi..."

Her body even trembled as she spoke, clearly because she had recalled something.

"What tier is his Dragon Bloodline?"

"He is an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon…"

Yang Kai nodded. "I understand."

An Eighth-Tier Dragon Bloodline was quite high. Even across all of Dragon Isle, it would likely rank quite highly. After all, Zhu Lie was only Eighth-Tier, and Zhu Qing had previously been Eighth-Tier as well. Now, however, she was Ninth-Tier, only one tier below the Dragon Clan's Great Elder.

"Brother Yang, if you wish to leave, you may do so without worry. There is no need to be overly concerned about me. I will take good care of myself."

Yang Kai nodded. "Then you must also be careful in all things. I will return in a few days."

With that, he soared into the sky and flew off in a certain direction.

The commotion on this side clearly had not alarmed many people. Yuan Wu and the others were still bustling busily at the center of the island and had no chance to notice anything elsewhere.

By now, the sky was bright. Yang Kai sped along like lightning, but he had no idea which direction to search and could only take things one step at a time. His Divine Sense quietly spread out as he probed the surroundings for movement.

Across the vast sea of the Dragon Palace, Spirit Islands were scattered like stars across a chessboard. Every one of them abounded with Spiritual Qi and possessed an excellent environment. Exotic flowers competed in splendor upon them, while heaven-and-earth treasures could be found everywhere. Even Yang Kai, who was accustomed to grand sights, could not help clicking his tongue in wonder.

He also knew that the Dragon Clan was wealthy, but wealth was a vague concept; without seeing it with one’s own eyes, it was difficult to imagine just what extent that wealth had reached.

Many Spirit Islands showed no sign of living creatures. In other words, the heaven-and-earth treasures on those islands were completely unguarded. Had Yang Kai not been so worried about Zhu Qing and eager to find her as soon as possible, he would certainly have swept away every treasure on those Spirit Islands.

For now, however, finding Zhu Qing was the most urgent matter. No matter how tempted he was, he had no interest in gathering those things.

Beyond the countless exotic flowers and rare herbs, there were also innumerable precious mineral veins, including Source Crystal lodes tempting enough to make anyone's eyes turn red. They lay bare in plain sight, yet no one paid them any attention.

Dragon Isle's wealth was simply far beyond that of the so-called top Sects of the Star Realm. Even the Sects of the Great Emperors could not be mentioned in the same breath. No wonder the Dragon Clan could expend such vast resources to construct a palace. Across the entire Star Realm, probably no one else possessed such boldness.

Yang Kai investigated one Spirit Island after another but gained little from his search.

Only after half a day did he finally sense signs of life on one of the Spirit Islands.

This appeared to be the residence of a member of the Dragon Clan, though he did not know whether they belonged to the Fu lineage or the Zhu lineage. No particularly powerful aura emanated from the island, so Yang Kai secretly slipped inside to investigate.

Every living soul on the island was a woman. A dazzling array of beauties filled the place, and a rough count put their number at no fewer than a hundred. Every one of them was striking, though their cultivations varied greatly.

Yang Kai knew these women were likely the exclusive playthings of a certain member of the Dragon Clan. Much like what Lu Sanniang had suffered, they had all been confined to this Spirit Island and belonged to a Dragon Clan member as his toys.

Perhaps out of rivalry and jealousy, many of the women had dressed themselves in dazzling finery and looked exceptionally seductive. Some even wore nothing but transparent gauze, revealing great expanses of snow-white skin that left Yang Kai dizzy.

The Dragon Clan member who lived here was nowhere to be seen, and Yang Kai had no idea where he had gone.

However, Yang Kai knew this was certainly not where Zhu Qing lived. She was a female dragon, so there was no possibility she would have brought so many women here.

For the first time, Yang Kai gained a true understanding of the Dragon Clan's extravagance and debauchery.

He hurriedly withdrew from the island and continued flying onward.

One day passed.

Two days passed.

For several days in succession, Yang Kai wandered throughout the Dragon Palace. His luck was fairly good, as he never encountered any members of the Dragon Clan. He investigated quite a few Spirit Islands as well. Apart from one or two that radiated powerful auras even he found somewhat daunting, he secretly slipped onto all the others to take a look.

There was no Zhu Qing.

Nor was there any sign of Zhu Lie.

Yang Kai had no idea where the siblings lived, and the fruitless search left him increasingly agitated.

On this day, another island appeared ahead. Yang Kai's figure flickered as he flew straight toward it. While still several dozen li away, he sensed signs of life on the island.

Most islands within the Dragon Palace were utterly deserted, so whenever Yang Kai came across one inhabited by living creatures, he naturally would not pass it by.

Concealing his aura as much as possible, Yang Kai's figure flickered as he rushed toward the island.

Over the past few days, Yang Kai had discovered that the places where members of the Dragon Clan resided were not heavily guarded. They did not even have a single barrier or Spirit Array, so infiltrating them was not difficult. He merely needed to conceal himself carefully and avoid discovery.

This was closely related to the Dragon Clan's arrogance. This was the Dragon Palace; who would be so blind as to run around as they pleased? Anyone who dared do so was courting death.

Thus, he landed on the island with ease and arrived inside a palace before long.

The palace appeared quite old, and its interior was decorated with extreme luxury. An ancient air permeated every corner. People occasionally came and went through its halls, but none had particularly high cultivation.

What Yang Kai found strange, however, was that there were actually men here.

In fact, they were all men, without a single woman in sight.

This inevitably made him suspicious. After all, the Spirit Islands he had investigated before were basically filled with women. Why was the situation on this island completely reversed?

The more suspicious Yang Kai became, the more determined he was to get to the bottom of it.

Carefully avoiding the people inside, Yang Kai made his way deeper into the palace.

When he reached a certain place, a burst of seductive sounds that could melt one's bones suddenly reached his ears, causing his expression to turn strange.

He listened closely and determined the direction the sound came from. With several flickers, Yang Kai arrived outside a great hall.

Holding his breath and making no sound, he quietly peered inside. What Yang Kai saw left him dumbstruck.

Over ten naked bodies were intertwined inside the great hall. The air was thick with a lewd and wanton scent, so strong that it drifted outside through the windows.

One blush-inducing cry after another came continuously from within, like invisible hands plucking at the heartstrings and stirring the blood until one could barely restrain oneself. Muffled groans and rapid sounds of impact were interspersed among them.

Yang Kai had seen many such scenes over the past few days. Life on most of the Spirit Islands inhabited by the Dragon Clan was similarly debauched beyond measure.

If that were all, however, Yang Kai would not have been so dumbfounded.

What shocked him was that almost everyone in the great hall was a man. Aside from them, there was only one woman!

The woman had her back to Yang Kai. The smooth curves of her bare back were graceful and exceedingly alluring, her skin gleaming like porcelain. Perhaps because she had been aroused, a rosy flush spread across her fair skin, stirring the imagination.

Behind her, a powerfully built man worked away vigorously, while two more men knelt before her, one on either side, seemingly sucking at something.

Two other men flanked her, each holding one of her small hands to his mouth and sucking her fingers one by one. Fawning expressions hung on their faces as they stared fixedly at the woman's face.

Many other naked men lay scattered across the great hall, looking so weak they seemed half-dead. There was no telling what sort of torment they had suffered.

Among those men were humans, Yao, and Half-Yao, while the woman radiated a pure Dragon aura.

This woman was actually a member of the Dragon Clan!

Yang Kai was stunned, simply unable to believe his eyes.

Over the past few days, he had learned that the Dragon Clan led dissolute lives, but such behavior was generally confined to the male members. This was the first time he had seen a female dragon act so wantonly.

The scene before him was extraordinarily shocking. Even Yang Kai, who had weathered countless storms, was struck senseless and simply stood frozen in place.

Suddenly, the woman waved her hand and pushed away the male favorites gathered around her. She then knocked down the powerfully built man who had been standing behind her, turned around, and straddled him.

The next moment, she tilted her head back slightly, her hair flying as her exquisite, graceful body rose and fell. Her chest surged in great waves without pause.

An extremely suppressed moan rang out as the woman's body began trembling violently. At the same time, her tightly shut eyes opened into narrow slits, revealing a look of utter ecstasy.

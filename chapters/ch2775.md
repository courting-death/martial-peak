## Chapter 2775 — One Day

"Good, good, good." Elder Lu's face lit up with joy. If things were truly as Yang Kai said, then there was no need to rush for two sets of Inner Cores. He could just get one set to cultivate first and take his time with the rest.

"The items can be exchanged, but this old man hopes Elder Yang can make up another ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals for me. After all, these things are far below my asking price."

The old fellow looked honest, but his mind was devious. Yang Kai grumbled inwardly and replied without hesitation, "Five million."

Elder Lu was silent for a moment, as if weighing his options, but finally nodded, "Deal."

Yang Kai immediately handed over another five million Upper-Level Source Crystals and exchanged them for the Heavenly Sound Treasure Box from Elder Lu, then tucked it into his Spatial Ring.

"Junior Brother, you've just become an elder of the Shrine and are already spending so much for us. Senior Sister must thank you on behalf of all the disciples. When they succeed in their cultivation one day, they will surely remember your kindness." Gao Xueting's voice suddenly drifted into Yang Kai's ears.

Yang Kai's face darkened. He turned to look, only to see Gao Xueting staring straight at the stage, as if she wasn't speaking to him at all.

"Um... Senior Sister Gao, this treasure box is for me..."

Gao Xueting tilted her head, her gaze sharp, "Hm?"

Yang Kai immediately changed his tune, "If the Shrine's disciples need it, of course there's no problem."

Only then did Gao Xueting nod, "Junior Brother is thoughtful."

Yang Kai let out an awkward chuckle, feeling as if he’d just swallowed something unbearably bitter. He silently wondered how he might find a way to please everyone in this situation…

The exchange meeting continued, with many rare treasures appearing. Numerous Emperor Realm cultivators took the stage one after another, presenting their treasured items, each time causing a scramble. But since this was Qingyang Shrine's territory, everyone still followed the rules. Even if a deal wasn't made, there was still courtesy—no one dared stir up trouble.

Yang Kai also made a few trades, obtaining some rare materials and even an ancient pill recipe.

It wasn't until dawn the next day that the exchange finally ended and everyone rose to their feet. As the host, Qiu Ran, leading the Qingyang Shrine's upper echelon, naturally extended polite invitations for everyone to stay, but no one actually did. They all took their leave.

Soon, over a hundred Emperor Realm cultivators and their disciples had all departed.

"Junior Brother Yang, come to my Lingyu Peak later. It's time to choose your residence," Qiu Ran suddenly called out.

Yang Kai nodded, "Alright, Senior Brother Qiu, wait a moment. I have something to do."

As he spoke, he darted out of the main hall, turning into a stream of light and flying away.

Qiu Ran frowned slightly and sighed, "Young people, always so hasty."

He had no idea what Yang Kai was running off to do at this moment. Even something as important as choosing a spiritual peak was postponed, so it seemed he had urgent business to handle.

Moments later, three hundred li from Qingyang Shrine, Yang Kai suddenly appeared. He glanced around and fixed his gaze in a certain direction. He cupped his fists and said, "Sect Master Zhang, thank you for waiting."

The void rippled, and at its center, an unremarkable middle-aged man suddenly emerged, stepping out from the emptiness with a hint of surprise on his face. "Elder Yang, you have sharp eyes."

He had been hiding there out of caution, but hadn’t expected Yang Kai to see through him at a glance. To be appointed as an elder of Qingyang Shrine, Yang Kai truly had some skill. What struck Zhang Dashan even more was Yang Kai’s youth—compared to him, Zhang Dashan suddenly felt a sense of his own age and decline.

"Elder Yang, you sent me a message just now to wait here. What urgent matter do you have?"

When the exchange ended, he was about to leave but heard Yang Kai's voice transmission, so he waited here. Otherwise, he would have returned to his own Sect long ago.

If it were anyone else sending such a message, he wouldn't have bothered, but since Yang Kai was a Qingyang Shrine elder, he had to take it seriously and wait.

Yang Kai smiled, "Forgive me, Sect Master Zhang. I want to ask you to transfer something to me."

Zhang Dashan frowned, immediately understanding, "That Taiyi Divine Water?"

When the previous owner of the Taiyi Divine Water took it out for exchange, Yang Kai had also made an offer, but failed. In the end, the Taiyi Divine Water landed in Zhang Dashan's hands. He and Yang Kai had never interacted before, so if Yang Kai was seeking him out, it could only be for the Taiyi Divine Water.

"That's right," Yang Kai nodded.

Zhang Dashan instantly became wary, his Divine Sense sweeping the surroundings, as if expecting an ambush at any moment.

Yang Kai chuckled, "No need for that, Sect Master Zhang. I came alone and sincerely wish to exchange for the Taiyi Divine Water."

Zhang Dashan frowned, "If that's the case, I'm sorry. The Taiyi Divine Water is also very useful to me, so I must disappoint you."

Yang Kai raised his hand, "Don't be so quick to refuse, Sect Master Zhang. Let me guess why you want the Taiyi Divine Water."

Zhang Dashan's frown deepened, clearly annoyed by Yang Kai's persistence. He had already refused, so what more was there to say?

Yang Kai ignored him and continued, "The only use for Taiyi Divine Water is to nurture Spiritual Medicine. It's a rare treasure for alchemists. Sect Master Zhang, you must want it for this reason, right?"

"That's right," Zhang Dashan nodded. It wasn't hard to guess, so it wasn't impressive.

Yang Kai smiled again, "But I see your hands are delicate, without any traces of smoke or Spiritual Medicine, so you must not be skilled in alchemy."

Zhang Dashan shook his head, "The Martial Dao is a long and arduous path. I have neither the leisure nor the ability to study alchemy."

Yang Kai looked confident and smiled, "So, you're giving the Taiyi Divine Water as a gift? And to an alchemy master, no less. For you to offer such a precious gift, you must be seeking a pill, right?" He paused, "Master Huangfu of Ziyuan Commerce Guild?"

Zhang Dashan finally showed surprise, "Elder Yang, you could even guess that?"

Yang Kai sneered, "There aren't many famous alchemists in the Southern Territory. For you to give away such a treasure, it could only be one of the few Imperial-Grade Pill Masters. The others all have their own affiliations and are hard to meet, let alone ask for pills. Only Ziyuan Commerce Guild keeps its doors open for business, and Master Huangfu often refines pills for others. Usually, if someone wants a pill, they go to him."

At this point, Zhang Dashan didn't hide anything and nodded, "That's right. The Taiyi Divine Water is indeed for Master Huangfu."

Yang Kai said, "You want to use this treasure to have Master Huangfu prioritize refining your Spiritual Pill?"

"Why not?"

"Of course you can, but Master Huangfu is famous, and countless people ask him to refine pills. Who wouldn't offer a generous gift? Your Taiyi Divine Water is precious, but it might not be enough to stand out. Even if Master Huangfu accepts your gift, you might still have to wait a while."

Zhang Dashan frowned, "I'm aware of that, but as long as Master Huangfu accepts the gift, I should get results within three months. That's still better than those who have to wait years."

"Three months..." Yang Kai sneered, raising a finger, "I can get you the pill you want in a single day."

"What?" Zhang Dashan was shocked. "One day? Impossible!"

"Whether it's possible or not depends on whether you're willing to trust me."

Zhang Dashan hesitated. If Yang Kai had said this yesterday, he would have scoffed, but today was different. Now that Yang Kai was a Qingyang Shrine elder, his words carried some weight.

After hesitating for a while, Zhang Dashan asked, “Elder Yang, which Imperial-Grade Pill Master do you know?”

"Have you ever heard of Ji Ying?" Yang Kai countered.

"Ji Ying..." Zhang Dashan muttered, then his eyes lit up, "Master Ji Ying? The one under Great Emperor Miaodan?"

"Who else could it be?"

"No way!" Zhang Dashan was stunned. "That master is in the Northern Territory. You said within a day..."

Yang Kai interrupted, "Ji Ying is currently at Qingyang Shrine. I have some friendship with him, so asking him to refine a pill is no problem."

Zhang Dashan was dumbfounded.

To have an Imperial-Grade Pill Master refine a pill, especially one under Great Emperor Miaodan, was not something a mere acquaintance could accomplish. The most unbelievable part was that Master Ji Ying was actually at Qingyang Shrine?

While Zhang Dashan was lost in thought, Yang Kai's voice sounded again, "So, will you wait three months or more for Master Huangfu to refine your pill after giving him a lavish gift, or will you try to get your pill within a day? Besides, even if you manage to get Master Huangfu to do it in three months, the fee will be sky-high. But if I handle it, there will be no fee at all."

"No fee?" Zhang Dashan's eyes lit up.

To have an Imperial-Grade Pill Master refine a pill, not only did you have to provide the materials, but the fee was also exorbitant. No wonder alchemists were all filthy rich.

"Just this once!" Yang Kai said seriously.

Zhang Dashan immediately caught the implication and asked excitedly, "Then next time, I can..."

"Next time, of course, there will be a fee." Yang Kai laughed.

Zhang Dashan was overjoyed. If he could establish a connection with Master Ji Ying this time, he wouldn't have to wait on others or suffer long delays in the future. As for the fee, that was only natural.

Sensing the moment, Yang Kai pressed on, "To be honest, I want the Taiyi Divine Water to give to Master Ji Ying as well. Name your price, and I'll buy it."

"No need." Zhang Dashan was decisive. Having made up his mind, he didn't hesitate. He tidied himself up and tossed a Spatial Ring to Yang Kai. "The Taiyi Divine Water and all the materials needed for alchemy are inside. I'll trouble Elder Yang with this."

Yang Kai reached out and took it, swept it with his Divine Sense, and smiled, "Sect Master Zhang, wait here for a day. I'll come find you tomorrow."

"Much obliged!"

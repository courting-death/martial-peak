## Chapter 2641 — Fierce Battle with Feng Xuan

In a single exchange, an Emperor Realm Elder of the Wenqing Sect had his head smashed to pieces and died on the spot. This brutal scene made the surrounding onlookers cry out in shock, their faces full of disbelief.

That was an Emperor Realm expert! How could he be killed with a single slap? How could this guy called Xiao Baiyi be so powerful?

Having succeeded with one move, Yang Kai unleashed another Moon Blade at another First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator, while at the same time, he reached out to grab Nanmen Dajun without hesitation.

"So it’s you, you little bastard!"

The First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator, seeing his companion die, did not show fear. Instead, his eyes flashed as if he had discovered some incredible secret. He reached out to grab Yang Kai, his cultivation surging higher and higher, quickly breaking through its limits to reach a despair-inducing height.

"Third Stage Emperor Realm?" Yang Kai’s eyes widened in shock as he sensed the man’s cultivation. He cried out, "Feng Xuan! You were hiding here all along!"

He had previously received word that Feng Xuan was stationed in Binglun City, but now he realized that Feng Xuan was never in Binglun City at all. Instead, he had always been by Nanmen Dajun’s side, even disguising his appearance and suppressing his cultivation, pretending to be an ordinary First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator.

As the Sect Master of the Wenqing Sect, he surely had secret techniques to accomplish this.

He must have done this to guard against Bing Yun, but unexpectedly, it wasn’t Bing Yun who came, but Yang Kai. Truly, it was a case of unintended consequences.

A few years ago, he had used Soul Descent in the Shattered Star Sea, possessing his own son Feng Xi’s body to fight Yang Kai. Although he was ultimately defeated and even lost his son, after that battle, he remembered all of Yang Kai’s various divine abilities.

Now, as soon as the Moon Blade appeared, Feng Xuan recognized Yang Kai’s identity.

"It’s too late for you to realize now. I knew you had close ties with Ice Heart Valley, but I didn’t expect you to walk right into my trap. Truly, I searched high and low, only to find you with no effort at all. Die, you little bastard!" Feng Xuan sneered. As he activated his Emperor Yuan, the laws of a Great Dao manifested around him, forming chains of Emperor Yuan that shot toward Yang Kai to bind him.

Yang Kai’s expression changed slightly. He had no choice but to withdraw the hand reaching for Nanmen Dajun, his face turning solemn as he quickly formed seals with both hands, chanting in a low voice, "The years wither and flourish, like a shuttle, like a dream!"

The seal formed, and he pressed it down toward Feng Xuan.

"Years Like a Shuttle Seal!" Feng Xuan was greatly alarmed, sensing the immense power contained within this divine ability. Even as a Third Stage Emperor, he couldn’t help but feel a chill in his heart. In the face of that technique, he suddenly felt a strange sensation.

Everything around seemed to come to a standstill.

"Laws of Time!" Feng Xuan’s face was ashen.

Back in the Shattered Star Sea, Yang Kai had used the Years Like a Shuttle Seal. It was this very divine ability that caused his Soul Descent body to be defeated and his son to be killed. So, over the past few years, Feng Xuan had been investigating the origins and mysteries of this technique.

His efforts were not in vain; he had finally uncovered the truth.

The Years Like a Shuttle Seal was the unique divine ability of the Great Emperor of Time. Once unleashed, it not only withered and flourished the years, but also involved the Laws of Time. It was a powerful secret technique coveted by all Emperor Realm cultivators.

Now, as Yang Kai used the Years Like a Shuttle Seal again, he was undoubtedly much more adept than he had been a few years ago.

Enveloped by the Laws of Time, Feng Xuan was momentarily stunned.

In that instant, Yang Kai flashed past him like a tiger descending the mountain, pouncing straight at Nanmen Dajun.

Nanmen Dajun, terrified, hurriedly retreated, his Emperor Yuan surging as he tried to resist.

But he, like Yang Kai, was only a First Stage Emperor. How could he possibly resist? He didn’t even have time to summon his Imperial Treasure before Yang Kai grabbed him by the throat. The next moment, a torrent of power surged into his body, rampaging through his meridians and flesh. Nanmen Dajun grunted, his face turning deathly pale, sweat pouring down his forehead.

"Don’t force me to kill you. You’re a formation master, and it wasn’t easy to reach such attainments!" Yang Kai snorted coldly as Nanmen Dajun continued to resist.

Nanmen Dajun let out a despondent sigh, dispersing his Emperor Yuan and allowing Yang Kai to lift him up.

At that moment, a faint cracking sound rang out.

Yang Kai looked back to see that Feng Xuan, who had been affected by the Laws of Time, had already broken free. The power of the Years Like a Shuttle Seal hadn’t even had time to fully unfold before he expelled it from his body.

"Worthy of being a Sect Master—truly impressive!" Yang Kai couldn’t help but praise.

Although the Years Like a Shuttle Seal was formidable, the gap in cultivation between him and Feng Xuan was simply too great. Being able to restrain him even for a moment was already lucky.

Whoosh, whoosh, whoosh, whoosh…

From all directions, figures soared into the sky, waves of Emperor Yuan fluctuations spreading out. The Emperor Realm cultivators of the Wenqing Sect, as well as those who had temporarily joined them, sensed something was wrong and rushed over to help.

Judging by their numbers, there were at least thirty. Thirty Emperor Realm cultivators—an incredibly terrifying force.

Although not as fearsome as the Thirty-Two Yao Kings and Eight Saint Envoys of the Wild Ancient Land, for a single sect to gather so many Emperor Realm cultivators was still astonishing.

"Yao’er, it’s done! Retreat, quickly!" Yang Kai shouted, calling out to Ji Yao, who was entangled with a Second Stage Emperor Realm opponent.

He had no desire to linger. Since he had captured Nanmen Dajun, it was best to leave at once.

Ji Yao gave a slight nod, her Ice Snow Pear Blossom Sword sweeping out in a graceful arc. Petals of pear blossoms bloomed in the air, dazzling the Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivator from the Wenqing Sect and throwing him into disarray. Seizing the opportunity, Ji Yao broke free from the entanglement and flew swiftly toward Yang Kai.

"Trying to leave? Since you’ve come, don’t even think about leaving!" Feng Xuan erupted like a volcano, his Supreme Sentiment Art pushed to the limit. In an instant, a profound intent enveloped the heavens and earth.

Within this intent, everyone’s emotions and desires seemed to be infinitely magnified, their minds shaken, making it impossible to maintain composure.

This was the terrifying aspect of the Supreme Sentiment Art. Once unleashed, it could stir up the hidden emotions and desires in an enemy’s heart. In a life-and-death battle, one would be completely suppressed, and the slightest mistake would mean destruction.

Activating his divine art, Feng Xuan spread his hand, grasping a spear out of thin air. As his qi and blood surged and Emperor Yuan poured in, the spear immediately emitted the sound of howling wind and thunder.

"Go!" Feng Xuan shouted, hurling the spear fiercely at Yang Kai.

Crack—

The spear transformed into a bolt of purple lightning, as if it would destroy everything, soaring through the sky like a dragon.

"The relic of Great Emperor Wenqing—the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear!"

Many Emperor Realm cultivators who saw this scene trembled, and those with broader knowledge recognized the origin of the spear.

It was said that the Wenqing Sect was founded by a Great Emperor, and the Supreme Sentiment Art was the very technique that Great Emperor had cultivated. It was by relying on this supreme art that he attained the title of Great Emperor.

After Great Emperor Wenqing passed away, he left not only the Supreme Sentiment Art to the Wenqing Sect, but also other relics.

The Wind and Thunder Divine Spear was the Great Emperor’s own Imperial Treasure! Its power was limitless, its destructive force immense.

To deal with a mere First Stage Emperor like Yang Kai, Feng Xuan even brought out the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear. It showed just how determined he was to kill Yang Kai.

Locked onto by the spear’s aura, Yang Kai’s face changed. He felt an unprecedented sense of crisis, as if he would die in the next instant.

Ji Yao’s face also turned slightly pale.

At the critical moment, Yang Kai reached out, and a simple, ancient little bell suddenly appeared before him.

The little bell looked old, its surface engraved with mysterious patterns. It grew with the wind, instantly becoming as large as a house, shielding Yang Kai and Ji Yao behind it.

The Mountain and River Bell!

As soon as this Primordial Era treasure appeared, a suppressive aura swept out, causing all the Emperor Realm cultivators rushing over to feel as if a great mountain was pressing down on them, making it hard to breathe.

The power of the Primordial Era burst forth in an instant.

But that wasn’t all. Yang Kai struck the Mountain and River Bell with force.

Boom—

The bell’s sound soared to the heavens, and the suppressive force multiplied several times. At that moment, countless cultivators in the sky fell like dumplings, unable to maintain their footing. Only the Emperor Realm cultivators could barely resist.

"What the hell is that thing!" Feng Xuan was shocked. As powerful as he was, even he felt his qi and blood roil after hearing the bell, nearly staggering. It was clear how formidable this Secret Treasure was.

Boom—

Another loud crash rang out as the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear struck the Mountain and River Bell.

When the two collided, the spear’s true form was revealed, its brilliance suddenly dimming as it was sent flying. The Mountain and River Bell, however, remained steady in the void, unmoved.

But Yang Kai spat out a mouthful of blood.

This time, Yang Kai was truly injured—not like before, when he had only bitten his tongue to spit blood while acting with Ji Yao.

Although the Mountain and River Bell was a Primordial Era treasure and now recognized Yang Kai as its master, the force of the collision was partly transferred to him. The bell itself was unafraid of the spear’s power, but some of the impact was borne by Yang Kai.

The relic of Great Emperor Wenqing was indeed extraordinary.

"Time to go!" Yang Kai shouted, quickly putting away the Mountain and River Bell. The Laws of Space swirled around him, enveloping Ji Yao and Nanmen Dajun, and in an instant, they vanished from the scene.

The wilderness fell silent, so quiet that one could hear a pin drop.

Everyone was dazed. The Emperor Realm cultivators who had intended to rush to the rescue all froze, making no further moves.

Although the battle had been brief, the earth-shattering fluctuations showed just how fierce it was. No one could have imagined that a mere First Stage Emperor cultivator could exchange blows with a powerhouse like Feng Xuan and even escape unscathed.

"Where did they go?"

"Where’s that kid?"

"Who is he, really?"

One question after another arose in the hearts of the Emperor Realm experts, but no one could answer. Perhaps the Wenqing Sect knew the man’s identity, but at this moment, who would dare ask? Feng Xuan’s face was ashen, as if he had lost his parents. Anyone who provoked him now would be asking for trouble.

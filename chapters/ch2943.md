## Chapter 2943 — Complaints

“It’s not that we have no savings, Palace Master, but compared to the gap in the Sect’s finances… it’s like a cup of water on a burning cart.” Elder Xu glanced timidly at Li Jiao with an embarrassed expression.

“That’s right, Palace Master. Even if we old fellows handed over everything we have, it wouldn’t last long.”

“We should last as long as we can.” Li Jiao earnestly persuaded, “If we can get through this difficult time, once the Sect’s situation improves, you will all be repaid in full.”

Since Li Jiao had said as much, the elders could not refuse any further. After all, what he said was true. They had all been in the Emperor Realm for many years and had accumulated some savings. If they brought out their assets, it would indeed help ease the Sect’s crisis.

The elders looked at each other, but Elder Xu was the first to speak: “Palace Master is right. In that case, this old man will set an example and contribute five million…”

Everyone’s eyes lit up at his words, and Li Jiao’s gaze became especially intense.

Elder Xu gave an awkward laugh. “Middle-Level Source Crystals! That’s almost all I have.”

Li Jiao’s face instantly turned pitch black.

Five million Middle-Level Source Crystals, when converted, was only fifty thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals. What could that amount accomplish? Moreover, an Emperor Realm elder who had managed the Alchemy Room for over a hundred years could not possibly have so little to his name.

“That can’t be right, Elder Xu.” A burly, dark-faced man jumped out and shouted, “Just the other day, you served a pot of Myriad Flower Brew to your guests, and that alone cost thirty thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals! That’s a top-quality wine from Medicine Pill Valley. If you only had that much to your name, how could you afford it?”

Exposed in public, Elder Xu’s face flushed red as he defended himself, “That… that wasn’t bought by this old man. My friend brought it and invited me to taste it. How could it be mine? My Honored Elder stipend is barely enough for my own cultivation, let alone buying Myriad Flower Brew. Don’t slander me.”

The dark-faced man snorted, “No Source Crystals, Elder Xu? Who would believe that? You control the entire Alchemy Room of Li Dragon Palace. Every year, you take countless Source Crystals from the Palace Master. Just two years ago, you took six million Upper-Level Source Crystals, and the year before that, over seven million, and before that, eight million. The Sect’s annual income is only fifty million, and the Alchemy Room alone consumes ten to twenty percent.”

Elder Xu flicked his sleeve and said, “I’ve said it before, the Alchemy Room is the lifeblood of Li Dragon Palace. Which of our tens of thousands of disciples can cultivate without pills? High consumption is only natural.”

The dark-faced man sneered, “If all those Source Crystals were really used to buy herbs, that would be fine. But can you guarantee that every Source Crystal you took from the Palace Master was spent on the Alchemy Room’s operations?”

Elder Xu was instantly both angry and embarrassed. He shouted, “What are you implying?”

The dark-faced man snorted, “You know exactly what I mean. When the Sect was wealthy, you skimmed off the top for yourself. Now that the Sect is in trouble, shouldn’t you show some sincerity?”

“Skimming off the top, lining my own pockets!” Elder Xu jumped up like a cat whose tail had been stepped on, his Emperor Yuan surging, looking ready to fight the dark-faced man to prove his innocence. He turned to Li Jiao in grief and anger, “Palace Master, someone is slandering me! Please uphold justice!”

Li Jiao stood there with a numb expression, as if he hadn’t heard Elder Xu’s words at all.

Elder Xu turned and sneered at the dark-faced man, “The Alchemy Room’s accounts are all open for inspection. I won’t argue with you here. Whether I’ve skimmed off the top or not, the Palace Master will decide. But as for your Artifact Room, who knows if your own hands are clean!”

“You old thing, how dare you bite back!” The dark-faced man flew into a rage.

“You dare call me an old thing? Bastard!”

“So what if I do? Old thing, old thing, old thing!”

“…”

The two immediately started quarreling, with some elders joining in to help and others trying to calm things down. The hall was thrown into chaos, and if Li Jiao hadn’t been present, they might have started fighting already.

Li Jiao watched coldly from above, wishing he could throw all these troublesome people out and enjoy some peace. But as the Palace Master, every decision he made affected the lives of tens of thousands of disciples. No matter how angry he was, he could only suppress the feeling and focus on solving the crisis at hand.

Just then, with a creak, the main hall doors opened and a disciple walked in.

The noise instantly stopped, and everyone turned to look at the disciple.

The disciple clearly had no idea what dramatic events had just unfolded in the hall. All he saw were those usually dignified elders, their faces now flushed red and necks bulging with anger, while the Palace Master stood at the highest position with a cold sneer on his face. Terrified, the disciple didn't even dare to take a breath.

“What is it?” Li Jiao asked impatiently.

“Reporting to the Palace Master, someone requests an audience!” the disciple stammered.

“Not seeing anyone!” Li Jiao was in a foul mood and refused without even thinking.

The disciple, as if granted amnesty, hurried to leave.

But Li Jiao called out again, “Who is it?”

The disciple thought for a moment and said, “He calls himself Yang Kai!”

“What?” Li Jiao was shocked, and all the elders were stunned as well.

Elder Xu said, “Yang Kai? Could it be the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace?”

“If it’s really him, this will be interesting!” The dark-faced man who had just been arguing with Elder Xu had a dangerous glint in his eyes. He looked up and said, “Palace Master, why not let Yang Kai in? Isn’t he our Li Dragon Palace’s creditor? We can have a good discussion about those debts.”

“Indeed! His timing is perfect. Let him in, Palace Master—what do you think?”

Everyone turned to look at Li Jiao, whose face was as black as the bottom of a pot. Clearly, he didn’t think it was a good idea.

Li Jiao sighed, “Lingxiao Palace is not to be provoked, and neither is Yang Kai.”

The elders were unconvinced. They had heard that the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Last time, on his own turf, their Palace Master and the Sect Master of Mitian Sect had suffered a huge loss. Now that he dared to come here, they couldn’t understand what their Palace Master was afraid of. As long as they let him into the hall and closed the doors, all those debts would be settled in an instant.

“Tell him the Palace Master is out traveling and not in the palace.” Li Jiao waved his hand.

The disciple agreed, but just as he was about to turn, a cold laugh came from outside the door: “Brother Li, you really put on airs. I came in person and you won’t even see me?”

As the words fell, two figures appeared in the hall.

Before they appeared, none of the Emperor Realm experts in the hall had noticed a thing, as if the two had always been there. Both were young men—one with a playful expression, sword-like brows and starry eyes, a faint smile on his lips; the other with fiery red hair, a cold and proud demeanor, and an aloofness that made it seem as if he stood above the clouds, not in the same world as everyone else present.

The elders were all startled, their faces changing color.

Li Jiao nearly staggered, almost turning to flee. He barely managed to suppress his panic and looked at Yang Kai with a speechless expression.

Yang Kai looked up at him with a grin. “Brother Li, are you planning to head out and travel?”

Li Jiao forced a dry laugh. “I was thinking about it, but it’s nothing important—just a whim. But since Palace Master Yang is here, of course I can’t leave.”

Yang Kai stroked his chin. “You still have the mood to travel? Looks like you’re living quite well, Palace Master Li.”

Li Jiao’s face immediately turned bitter. “Palace Master Yang, please don’t joke. My days now… are worse than death.”

The elders all lowered their heads in embarrassment at his words.

Yang Kai laughed loudly. “Brother Li, you’re really something. Li Dragon Palace is a grand and powerful sect, and you wield immense authority and strength. How could your days be worse than death?”

Li Jiao thought to himself that it was all Yang Kai’s fault, but forced a smile and said, “May I ask what brings Palace Master Yang here today?”

Yang Kai didn’t answer, just smiled at him.

Li Jiao wiped the cold sweat from his forehead. “Could it be… Palace Master Yang is here to collect the debt? Such a small matter shouldn’t trouble you personally. You could have sent someone. Li Dragon Palace hasn’t forgotten this year’s payment, but we need some time to gather the funds. Once it’s ready, we’ll send it to Lingxiao Palace right away—there won’t be any delay. Besides… the deadline hasn’t even arrived yet!”

“When is the deadline? I don’t know. I think today is the deadline.”

The cold sweat on Li Jiao’s forehead grew heavier. “So Palace Master Yang really is here to collect the debt?”

He could hardly believe it. Li Dragon Palace did owe Lingxiao Palace a huge sum of Source Crystals, but for Yang Kai to come in person and enter unannounced was going too far.

“Don’t get too arrogant, Yang!” The dark-faced burly man leapt out from the crowd, pointing at Yang Kai. “I don’t know what trick you used to deceive our Palace Master and leave my Li Dragon Palace in such straits, but since you are at least a Palace Master and a fellow cultivator of the Northern Territory, we will give you some face. As long as you sign a written agreement here and now, all debts between our two Sects will be wiped clean, and we can let you off this once. Otherwise… hmph hmph!”

Many Emperor Realm experts looked at Yang Kai with ill intent.

After seeing Yang Kai in person and confirming that he was indeed only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, the crowd naturally had nothing left to fear. No matter how deep Lingxiao Palace's foundation was, as long as they could restrain Yang Kai here, it would be equivalent to controlling Lingxiao Palace itself. Their so-called debts would become nothing but a joke.

Li Jiao broke out in a cold sweat, nearly fainting.

Yang Kai glanced at the dark-faced man, then at Li Jiao. “Is this your elder?”

“No!” Li Jiao denied flatly.

## Chapter 2736 — Trying to Dodge the Debt?

Li Jiao and Mi Qi felt a jolt in their hearts. The outcome of a draw left them somewhat dissatisfied, but it was still acceptable since they hadn’t lost anything. Yet now, Yang Kai was actively suggesting they judge the quality to determine the winner, and that gave them a bad feeling.

Could it be... he’s actually confident he’ll win? Otherwise, why propose this?

As soon as this thought arose, the two became unsettled. Mi Qi hurriedly said, “Let’s just call it a draw. No need to scrutinize the quality further.”

Yang Kai snorted, “Since it’s a wager, we must determine a winner. What’s Sect Master Mi afraid of?”

Mi Qi nervously replied, “What’s there to be afraid of? Palace Master Yang, you must be joking.”

Li Jiao’s forehead broke out in a fine sweat, the bad feeling in his heart growing fiercer.

“I’ll have to trouble Brother Ji to appraise them once more!” Yang Kai turned to Ji Ying.

“It’s a trivial matter,” Ji Ying nodded.

At this point, the outcome between himself and Yang Kai was no longer important. Besides, Yang Kai clearly grasped the true essence of alchemy; given time, he might even rival his master. Losing to him wasn’t shameful.

This round of pill refining had been fruitful for him. After returning, he planned to enter seclusion for a while, and he was sure to make progress in alchemy. By then, his pill refining skills would be on par with his Senior Brothers.

He calmed his mind and began the appraisal, holding the Emperor Yuan Pills refined by himself and Yang Kai in each hand. He examined their color, size, purity, and the aura they emitted, even scraping a bit of powder from each Spiritual Pill to taste.

The hall was silent, everyone tense, especially Mi Qi and Li Jiao. This wager concerned fifty years of their sects’ profits. Their eyes locked onto Ji Ying like leeches, watching his every move, dreading any unfavorable verdict.

Only Yang Kai looked completely at ease, even returning to his seat to sip some tea.

“Phew...” After a long while, Ji Ying finally exhaled. His expression showed he’d reached a conclusion.

“M... Master Ji, what’s the result?” Mi Qi nervously leaned in, his voice trembling.

Li Jiao also stared at Ji Ying, a bead of sweat sliding down his cheek unnoticed.

“I... I’m not skilled enough. Brother Yang’s technique is superior. I admit defeat!” Ji Ying didn’t answer them, but turned to Yang Kai, cupping his fists.

“Brother Ji, you flatter me. I only won by luck and some fortunate encounters. I’ll need your guidance in the future.” Yang Kai smiled and returned the salute.

Ji Ying gave a bitter smile and waved his hand.

As the two conversed, Li Jiao and Mi Qi stood there dumbfounded, faces ashen, their blood nearly frozen.

Ji Ying hadn’t stated outright who won, but the meaning was clear.

“Master Ji, are you sure? Are his Spiritual Pills really superior?” Mi Qi asked, voice quivering.

Li Jiao anxiously added, “Shouldn’t you check again?”

Ji Ying’s expression turned angry. “Do you two distrust my character so much?”

These two had questioned his integrity before, and he’d let it slide for old times’ sake. But now they were at it again, and Ji Ying was furious.

Once was forgivable, but twice? Was his reputation worth so little?

Seeing Ji Ying’s anger, Mi Qi and Li Jiao realized their rudeness.

Mi Qi wiped the sweat from his forehead, “No, no, Master Ji, you misunderstand. Brother Li and I never meant that. Please don’t be angry.”

Offending Ji Ying was the last thing they wanted.

Ji Ying snorted, “Let me tell you—true alchemists never joke about the pills they refine. If I lost, then I lost. There’s no need to exaggerate Yang Kai’s skill just to tarnish my own reputation.” He paused, then extended both hands. “Since you two don’t trust me, try the pills yourselves. With your Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, you should easily tell which pill is superior after refining them.”

Li Jiao and Mi Qi exchanged glances, both tempted. It was a good idea.

The stakes were high, so they had to be cautious. But doing so would completely offend Ji Ying, since refining the pills themselves meant they didn’t trust his word.

The choice left Li Jiao and Mi Qi in a difficult spot.

Yang Kai smiled, “You two should try for yourselves. It’ll also clear Brother Ji’s name!”

Ji Ying stood there with a cold face, hands outstretched.

Gritting his teeth, Li Jiao stepped forward, ashamed. “Forgive me.”

He took Emperor Yuan Pills from Ji Ying’s left and right hands, choosing two at random.

Mi Qi, seeing this, could only take two Emperor Yuan Pills as well.

Ji Ying said indifferently, “Since you don’t trust me, our cooperation ends here. In the future, don’t come to Medicine Pill Valley for pill refining.”

Li Jiao and Mi Qi trembled, faces pale, hurriedly called out, “Master Ji...”

“No need to say more!” Ji Ying snorted coldly, not even bothering to respond.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi let out a heavy sigh, gazing at the two Spiritual Pills in their hands. In that moment, the tiny pills felt as heavy as ten thousand catties, as if the little Spiritual Pills weighed down their hearts with unbearable pressure.

With things as they were, the two could only grit their teeth and swallow an Emperor Yuan Pill to refine.

Since they’d already offended Ji Ying, they couldn’t afford to lose the five billion as well. As long as they could prove Ji Ying’s Emperor Yuan Pill was better than Yang Kai’s, they’d at least gain something.

There weren’t many Imperial-Grade Pill Masters, but the Northern Territory had one or two. If needed, they could avoid Medicine Pill Valley in the future.

They activated their Emperor Yuan to refine the Emperor Yuan Pill. In no time, the effects were fully absorbed. Then they took the other pill and continued refining.

After a while, Li Jiao and Mi Qi opened their eyes, looking utterly defeated.

Their own test proved the first Emperor Yuan Pill’s effects were slightly inferior to the second. The first Spiritual Pill was the one refined by Ji Ying.

The difference wasn’t huge, but the first pill was indeed a bit weaker.

Ji Ying said, “I lost to Brother Yang in the process of condensing the medicinal liquid. Mine wasn’t as pure, so the final pill was slightly inferior. Now that you’ve tested them yourselves, you should know the result. Did I make things up or favor Brother Yang?”

Mi Qi replied bitterly, “We never meant that.”

They could have lied and claimed Ji Ying’s pill was better, but that would be shameless. The three Yao Kings watching them weren’t fools, and there was also a terrifying woman named Qing’er. If they tried to cheat, they’d be in serious trouble.

“Now that the result is clear, you two... should honor the bet.” Yang Kai walked down with his hands behind his back.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao’s faces twitched, their hearts aching.

“Master Ji...” Mi Qi looked to Ji Ying for help, hoping he’d speak up for them.

Ji Ying returned to his seat, ignoring them, clearly annoyed.

Seeing this, Mi Qi and Li Jiao felt utterly hopeless. The situation was against them; they couldn’t win, couldn’t escape, and if they tried to break things off, they’d definitely be kept here.

Why did they come to Lingxiao Palace with Ji Ying? This dragon’s lair and tiger’s den was easy to enter, hard to leave. They regretted it deeply.

“I’m talking to you. You two are prominent figures in the Northern Territory. Are you trying to dodge the debt?” Yang Kai’s expression turned fierce as he glared at them.

“Hmm?” The three Yao Kings let out threatening sounds, Yao Yuan surging, ready to act at Yang Kai’s command.

“How could we? How could we?” Li Jiao quickly wiped his sweat, his mouth bitter as if he’d swallowed a bitter herb, forcing a smile. “We’ll honor the bet. Brother Mi and I would never dodge the debt.”

“Yes, yes. We’d never dodge the debt,” Mi Qi nodded repeatedly.

How many years had it been since they’d been forced to swallow their pride like this? Even in front of Ji Ying, they’d never been so humble. Yet today, the two of them finally remembered what it felt like to be utterly humiliated. The frustration in their hearts was so overwhelming, not even the waters of the Three Rivers and Five Lakes could wash it away.

“Since you don’t want to dodge the debt, hurry up!” Yang Kai stretched out his hand, looking every bit the debt collector.

Mi Qi replied bitterly, “Palace Master Yang, five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals isn’t something you can just produce on a whim.”

“That’s right, it’s too much,” Li Jiao added nervously.

“Two and a half billion then. If you can’t come up with five billion, don’t tell me you can’t even produce two and a half billion?” Yang Kai shot the two of them a sidelong glance.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao shook their heads like rattles.

Yang Kai glared, “Your sects have stood in the Northern Territory for so many years, how can you be so short of funds? Aren’t you afraid of being laughed at if word gets out?”

Li Jiao said, “Palace Master Yang, you don’t understand. Our sects earn a lot each year, but our expenses are enormous. There’s not much left over.”

“Exactly,” Mi Qi nodded. “You’ve inherited Wenqing Sect’s foundation and accumulated wealth. You should know how many Source Crystals you got. Our sects aren’t much different from Wenqing Sect.”

“So, you two can’t pay the wager?” Yang Kai’s face turned cold.

“For now... we can’t,” Mi Qi and Li Jiao replied guiltily.

“How bold!” Yang Kai shouted. “You dared to make such a big bet without the funds? Are you here to make a fool of me? Yao Kings, kill them!”

“Yes!” The three Yao Kings shot forward instantly.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi were terrified, thinking Yang Kai must have planned this all along, intending to kill them and seize the foundations of Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace. If they died here, their sects would be leaderless and unable to resist Lingxiao Palace’s power, likely to be wiped out soon.

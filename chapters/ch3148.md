## Chapter 3148 — The Might of One Palm

The crisis came without warning, dropping from the sky with an indescribably terrifying pressure, as if a mountain had smashed down on Xin Xuanming's chest. His face changed, and blood sprayed from his mouth.

He was stationed at the Formation Core of the Eight Wastelands Array. Countless disciples from Netherworld Sect and the other Sects of Great Wilderness Star Domain had gathered here, and its defenses were airtight, so he had never imagined anyone would dare launch a sneak attack in this place.

And until now, that had been true. Over the past few years, aside from those rats harassing them for a while when the Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array was first being built, they had never shown themselves again.

He had been cultivating here in secluded retreat with complete peace of mind, waiting only for the day the great array took shape so he could emerge and slaughter until rivers of blood flowed.

During secluded retreat, one's mind had to remain utterly focused and could not tolerate even the slightest disturbance. Yet that tremendous pressure from above had torn him straight out of that mysterious state. If that had been all, it would have been fine; he would not have suffered any injury. But the pressure was so horrifying that even he could not take it lightly. His body buckled, and he was injured on the spot.

Shock filled his eyes. He frantically urged his Saint Yuan, straightened up, and took a step forward under that immense pressure, appearing outside the great hall.

Everywhere he looked, miserable screams rang out. Countless disciples from Great Wilderness Star Domain had been crushed flat against the ground. Dark red blood stained the earth in glaring patches, clearly vomited up by these disciples, and some of the weaker ones had exploded into blood mist on the spot.

In the blink of an eye, over a hundred cultivators had died miserably.

He looked up, and his pupils shrank.

A ray of light was falling from the sky. An enormous palm print came pressing down, covering the entire Eight Wastelands Formation Core with room to spare.

What Divine Ability was this? And who was attacking him?

Even though Xin Xuanming had weathered many great storms, his hair stood on end at this moment. Such overwhelming might was simply beyond an ordinary Void King Realm cultivator. Across all of Tongxuan Continent, only one person should have been capable of this: the Star Lord who had spent the last several years cowering in hiding!

But that Star Lord was a woman, while the palm print before his eyes clearly belonged to a man. How could that be explained?

In that split second, there was no time to think. Xin Xuanming knew that if he did not resist, the Eight Wastelands Formation Core would likely be finished today. Once the might of this palm struck down, never mind how many people here would die; if the Eight Wastelands Formation Core was broken, then all their years of effort and persistence would go to waste.

As Netherworld Sect's Vice Sect Master, how could he sit still and wait for death?

With an angry roar, his thin, small-looking body swelled as if inflated. His robes snapped in the air, and a Formation Plate suddenly appeared in his hand. He formed one seal after another, urging his Saint Yuan and pouring it madly inside.

The Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array was a heaven-shaking great array, composed of eight sub-arrays. Each sub-array could operate independently, and the place Xin Xuanming guarded was the Formation Core, stronger than the other seven sub-arrays.

The Formation Plate in his hand was what controlled this Formation Core.

The moment he activated the Eight Wastelands Formation Core, Yang Kai, who was plunging down from high above, gave a soft sound of surprise. The enormous "ink spot" below suddenly twisted and squirmed. A moment later, thick black Qi gathered together, transforming into a ghostly hand. Its fingers bent into claws and stabbed upward.

From high in the sky, that ghostly hand did not seem especially large, but in truth it was enormous beyond compare. Spread open, it could cover an area a hundred li in every direction. Its might was formidable, and it was clearly not easy to deal with.

Of course, that was only true for an ordinary Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator.

This array had been arranged by Void King Realm cultivators. However powerful it might be, it still could not exceed its limits.

Yang Kai, however, was an existence who had surpassed those limits and broken the rules.

"Troublesome!" Yang Kai muttered softly, then urged a little more strength.

The palm print that already blotted out the sky and covered the earth suddenly expanded by half, its might rising sharply.

Xin Xuanming had been wearing a cold, sinister look. With the Eight Wastelands Formation Core under his control, he was confident the enemy's sneak attack would fail. He was just about to control the ghostly hand and pit it against the palm print when he saw the palm print expand, and terror seized him.

The enemy still had strength to spare!

How was that possible? He almost thought there was something wrong with his eyes, but the pressure bearing down on his body was unmistakably real.

Under his dumbstruck gaze, the pitch-black ghostly hand smashed into the enormous palm print. The palm print paused for only a moment before rumbling downward again. Although its color had visibly dimmed, the ghostly hand fared even worse. Like a piece of firewood, it cracked apart inch by inch. Even after Xin Xuanming exhausted all his strength, he still could not stop the palm print from falling.

Heaven and Earth suddenly went dark, even the light blotted out.

The palm print struck the "ink spot," and violent pressure burst outward. The earth caved in, and an enormous pit appeared.

The "ink spot" broke apart. Viewed from high above, a clear five-fingered imprint now marked the ground, covering every place the original ink spot had enveloped.

Bang, bang, bang...

Dull thuds rang out one after another as body after body burst into powder. Inside the ink spot, none of the cultivators below the Void King Realm survived. Some could not withstand the pressure before the palm print even arrived; others were flattened into meat patties beneath it.

Building after building also crumbled to dust.

Only a small number of Void King Realm cultivators were lucky enough to escape the disaster, but every one of them was badly wounded. Many had not even realized what had happened. They had only felt danger descend, then pain throughout their bodies. Now they looked around blankly, their mouths hanging open.

The Eight Wastelands Formation Core had been broken!

Everything Great Wilderness Star Domain had spent years building here vanished in an instant. Countless disciples were dead or injured. This loss was nothing short of disastrous.

Xin Xuanming coughed up mouthfuls of blood. Both of his legs had shattered from the knees down, exposing pale bone as blood streamed everywhere. He sat collapsed on the ground, looking around in horror.

His cultivation was the highest here, and his strength the greatest, but he was also the one presiding over the Formation Core. He had thought that with the Eight Wastelands Formation Core, he could contend with Yang Kai, only to discover he had underestimated the other party's methods. After the Eight Wastelands Formation Core was broken, the pressure he bore was far greater than anyone else's, and his end was the most miserable. Even the Formation Plate in his hand had exploded outright, blasting him until he was covered in dirt and ash. The other Void King Realm cultivators were badly injured, but none had fallen into such a wretched state.

Who? Who exactly was it?

How could this world hold someone with such heaven-shaking methods? To break the Eight Wastelands Formation Core with a single palm was simply too unbelievable.

As if sensing something, he raised his head and looked up. A handsome, elegant young man was slowly drifting down from the sky, standing in midair as he looked down at them.

Was it him? Xin Xuanming released his Divine Sense, only to discover that he could not sense the depth of the young man's cultivation at all. Then a little girl of seven or eight flew down as well. She was delicate and fair, yet to his shock, she also gave him an unfathomable feeling.

"Your Excellency... who are you?" Xin Xuanming endured the heart-piercing pain of his wounds and asked hoarsely. Under this man's gaze, he could not even muster the desire to flee. He knew today was likely to end badly, but even if he died, he wanted to die knowing why. At the very least, he had to know who had attacked them.

Perhaps... he could still send the news out before he died.

"Looks like I didn't kill the wrong people." Yang Kai ignored him and glanced around. They were all cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain.

With a shift of his feet, he arrived before Xin Xuanming and planted one foot on his chest. Resting an arm on his thigh, he leaned down and stared coldly at him. “Old thing, I want to ask you about someone’s whereabouts. Where is this place’s Star Lord now?”

"Pah!" Xin Xuanming opened his mouth and spat a mouthful of blood at Yang Kai.

The newcomer had struck ruthlessly and without mercy, clearly targeting them. Xin Xuanming knew there was no way he would survive today, and begging for mercy would probably be useless, so he did not waste the effort. Instead, he acted with a certain reckless defiance.

Yang Kai waved his hand, and the bloody spittle flew back into Xin Xuanming's mouth, turning incomparably hard as it went and smashing a mouthful of teeth. Yang Kai grinned fiercely. "You still dare spit at me? Old dog, are you tired of living?"

Xin Xuanming sneered. "If you want to kill me, kill me. If you want to flay me, flay me. Spare me the nonsense."

Yang Kai sneered, “You tell me to kill you, so I should kill you? Who do you think you are? This Young Master just happens not to want to kill you."

“If you do not kill this old man, then one day, when the dragon turns over, I will make you repay this a hundredfold!” Xin Xuanming shouted fiercely.

"Then this Young Master will wait." Yang Kai grinned at him, took his foot off his chest, and turned toward another Void King Realm cultivator.

Behind him, however, a smug light flashed through Xin Xuanming’s eyes. This provocation method was indeed useful. Although it was somewhat clichéd, it often produced miraculous results, especially when used against powerful fellows who were young and hot-blooded.

Yang Kai suddenly turned back and teased, "You don't actually think I'd spare your life, do you?"

Xin Xuanming's heart lurched, and his Saint Yuan erupted. But before he could do anything, a force shot toward him head-on and struck his forehead. His eyes widened, and he died with everlasting regret.

“Sure enough, old bastards who refuse to die are all crafty thieves!” Yang Kai coldly snorted, then looked again at the injured Void King Realm cultivators and said loudly, “Good. Now, who will answer my question?”

His voice had not been loud when he asked earlier, but everyone who had survived was a Void King Realm cultivator. How could they not have heard?

At that, one of them asked cautiously, "If I answer you, will you let me live?"

The moment the words left his mouth, his body shuddered, and both of his arms flew away. Pain hit him, and he cried out in alarm before screaming. As he staggered back, he lost his footing and fell to the ground. Then he felt a chill at his legs. When he lowered his head, he saw that both legs had been severed at the knees.

All four limbs had been cut off! His screams rang through the clouds.

Another stabbing pain came from his Dantian, and all the strength in his body poured away.

His cultivation had been crippled!

Everyone else felt their hair stand on end.

Yang Kai said coldly, "I only asked casually. Whether you answer or not is your choice."

"Fight him!" someone shouted.

Yang Kai was so cruel and tyrannical that even if they answered him, they probably had no way to live. Everyone here had cultivated to the Void King Realm, so none of them were fools. Even if Yang Kai promised to spare them, probably no one would dare believe him.

If they wanted to survive, joining forces was their only sliver of hope.

"Fight him!" someone immediately echoed, throwing himself at Yang Kai.

In an instant, seven or eight figures surrounded him from every side, each one brimming with murderous intent and charging forward without hesitation.

## Chapter 2666 — To Possess a Treasure Is to Invite Trouble

This person seemed to be offering Yang Kai a kind reminder, likely on account of that Upper-Level Source Crystal.

Yet Yang Kai was left utterly dazed.

A year ago, he had led the three Yao Kings to wipe out the entire Wenqing Sect, but he never imagined that, at the very same time, the Thousand Leaves Sect would also be exterminated.

Back then, he had received an invitation from Ye Qinghan, the daughter of the Thousand Leaves Sect Master, in Fenglin City, to go to the Thousand Leaves Sect and repair their Space Array, reopening that sealed pocket world and retrieving their ancestral inheritance. By chance, he had also encountered Chi Yue, Ghost Ancestor, Ai Ou, and the others, who had long been separated from him.

The Thousand Leaves Sect didn’t mean much to him—just a few ties—but Chi Yue and the others had stayed behind at the Thousand Leaves Sect.

Now that the Thousand Leaves Sect had been wiped out, what fate had befallen Chi Yue and the others?

Those who had come with him from the Hengluo Star Domain to the Star Realm were all deeply connected to him. Chi Yue was Shan Qingluo’s adoptive mother, making her his mother-in-law. Ai Ou was Xue Yue’s father, making him his father-in-law. Ghost Ancestor was the Grand Elder of his Lingxiao Sect, someone who had shared life and death with him. Only the Sword Alliance’s Alliance Master, Gu Cangyun, was not particularly familiar to him…

Upon hearing such terrible news, Yang Kai’s face twisted, a chilling murderous intent surging forth as he glared at the man and demanded, “Are you lying to me?”

The man hadn’t paid much attention to Yang Kai at first, but now, sensing Yang Kai’s aura, his expression changed dramatically as he exclaimed in shock, “Emperor Realm!”

He hadn’t expected that the young man who had casually stopped him for questions would actually be a genuine Emperor Realm master. Had he known, he would never have dared to be so careless.

That chilling murderous intent coiled around him like something tangible, making him feel as if he might die at any moment. Drenched in cold sweat, he stammered, “M-My lord, I’m not lying! The Thousand Leaves Sect really was wiped out—everyone in the Southern Territory knows about it!”

“Nonsense!” Yang Kai grabbed him by the throat and lifted him off the ground.

In the Star Realm, sects of all sizes were as numerous as cattle. For a minor sect like the Thousand Leaves Sect to be destroyed, how could it possibly become common knowledge? Only the destruction of a behemoth like the Wenqing Sect would cause such a stir throughout the Northern Territory.

To put it bluntly, even if the Thousand Leaves Sect really was wiped out, it would be like tossing a stone into the vast sea of the Southern Territory—at most, it would cause a few ripples before quickly fading away.

So Yang Kai instinctively felt this fellow was lying.

With Yang Kai’s hand around his throat, the man could barely breathe and struggled desperately, but it was useless.

“Young Master Yang!” Yingfei called out softly from the side, also noticing that Yang Kai’s emotions were a bit off and realizing that the Thousand Leaves Sect must hold great importance in Yang Kai’s heart.

Yang Kai frowned, realizing his own loss of composure. He loosened his grip and set the man down, saying in a deep voice, “Tell me everything you know. If it’s true, I won’t make things difficult for you.”

The man coughed for quite a while, even tearing up, but dared not show the slightest dissatisfaction. Only after catching his breath did he say, “My lord, w-what do you want to know?”

The Emperor Realm master before him was clearly unpredictable, so he dared not guess at his intentions and could only probe carefully.

“Who destroyed the Thousand Leaves Sect?” Yang Kai asked in a low voice.

“They say it was because they provoked the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect.”

“Flowing Shadow Sword Sect!” Yang Kai frowned, vaguely recalling having heard of this sect somewhere before.

Thinking carefully, he remembered: back in Four Seasons Land, he had dealt with disciples of the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect. As he recalled, this sect wasn’t among the top sects, but their strength was decent.

“Why did the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect destroy the Thousand Leaves Sect?” Yang Kai pressed. “Did the two sects have some deep-seated grudge?”

The man hurriedly replied, “There was no deep-seated grudge between them—only that the Thousand Leaves Sect was like an innocent man harboring a jade; to possess a treasure is to invite trouble!”

A flash of insight struck Yang Kai, and he blurted out, “Heavenly Puppets?”

These so-called Heavenly Puppets referred to the Heaven-Grade puppets. The Thousand Leaves Sect had risen to prominence through puppet techniques, and their founder was a master of the art, leaving behind ten Heavenly Puppets for the sect, each one comparable to an Emperor Realm expert.

At its peak, the Thousand Leaves Sect was one of the top sects in the Southern Territory, renowned throughout the Star Realm.

But later generations were mediocre, unable to carry on the puppet arts. As the inheritance declined, the Thousand Leaves Sect eventually became a minor sect, even losing much of their puppet-controlling techniques.

Fortunately, last time Yang Kai had helped them repair the Space Array, reopening that sealed pocket world and retrieving many lost secret arts, as well as reactivating those Heavenly Puppets.

Liu Yan’s puppet body had also been obtained from that pocket world. If not for that, Liu Yan might never have gained a physical form.

Hearing Yang Kai mention the Heavenly Puppets, the man nodded repeatedly. “Exactly, exactly! So my lord also knows about the Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppets. A few years ago, the Thousand Leaves Sect somehow regained control over those Heavenly Puppets that had been sealed for so many years. The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect seemed very interested in this, so they found an excuse to wipe out the Thousand Leaves Sect.”

“That simple?” Yang Kai’s face darkened.

The man gave a bitter smile. “The world bustles for profit, and all strive for gain. The Thousand Leaves Sect Master was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, yet he possessed Heavenly Puppets as strong as Emperor Realm experts. How could that not attract covetous eyes?”

He wasn’t wrong. If the Thousand Leaves Sect truly had Emperor Realm experts overseeing things, then having Heavenly Puppets of similar strength wouldn’t be an issue. The problem was that even the Thousand Leaves Sect Master, Ye Hen, was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. With such treasures in hand, of course others would covet them.

After pondering for a moment, Yang Kai spoke coldly, “The Thousand Leaves Sect possessed ten Heavenly Puppets, each with strength rivaling the Emperor Realm. How could the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect so easily wipe them out?”

He knew the Heavenly Puppets’ power well, since Liu Yan had a White Tiger Heavenly Puppet that could fight on par with a First Stage Emperor Realm expert. The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect was strong, but not a top sect—how could they swallow such a fat piece of meat in one bite?

The man gave a bitter smile. “I don’t know, sir. Everyone finds it strange. Maybe the Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppets weren’t as formidable as their reputation suggested.”

That wasn’t wrong either. Heavenly Puppets were powerful, but they still needed someone to control them. Without any Emperor Realm masters in the Thousand Leaves Sect, perhaps they really couldn’t unleash the puppets’ full strength. Still, Yang Kai felt things weren’t so simple.

“The Thousand Leaves Sect was destroyed—what about its disciples?” Yang Kai asked nervously.

The man replied, “Some died, some escaped—probably not many survived. But I heard a few were captured, and among those, there was supposedly a Star Lord from a lower Star Domain!”

“Is that true?” Yang Kai’s eyes flashed with a sharp light.

The man gave a bitter smile. “How would I dare deceive you, sir? There are many in the Star Realm who came from lower Star Domains, but not many who are Star Lords. It’s said that to become a Star Lord, one must refine a Stellar Origin. I don’t know what kind of luck that person had to obtain a complete Stellar Origin. Though his cultivation isn’t high, his future is bright. This news was released by the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect. Otherwise, how could it have become so widely known in the Southern Territory?”

“Why would they release this news?” Yang Kai asked, his expression odd, though his anxiety eased somewhat.

The Star Lord mentioned was, barring any surprises, probably Ai Ou.

If Ai Ou was unharmed, then perhaps Chi Yue and the others were also safe. Of course, that was the best-case scenario—some of them might have met with misfortune.

“What else? Naturally, it’s to spread the word for an auction. So you know, the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect is planning to hold an auction in Flowing Shadow City in a month, where the items up for bid will be the Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppets and that Star Lord.”

“They’re auctioning off the Star Lord too?” Yang Kai was stunned.

The man gave a bitter smile. “You jest, sir. That Star Lord has a complete Stellar Origin within him. If it could be extracted and transferred to a talented disciple, that disciple’s future would be limitless. Naturally, everyone is eager for it.”

Hearing this, Yang Kai suddenly recalled that, when he first entered the Starlight Passage and encountered Yin Lesheng, Yin Lesheng had shown great interest in the Stellar Origin within him and had even mentioned a secret technique to extract and transfer it.

Now it seemed Yin Lesheng hadn’t been speaking nonsense after all.

A complete Stellar Origin was extremely important for a cultivator’s advancement. If that was the case, then Ai Ou’s life was likely not in danger. What Yang Kai worried about now was Chi Yue and the others.

“A month from now, in Flowing Shadow City…” Yang Kai muttered to himself, already making plans in his heart.

Now that the Thousand Leaves Sect had been destroyed, and a year had already passed, there was no use in being anxious. At this point, he could only act according to circumstances.

“One last question: where is the Thousand Leaves Sect, and where is Flowing Shadow City?” Yang Kai asked the man in a deep voice.

The man didn’t dare refuse and obediently pointed in two directions.

After a long while without a response, he looked up to find that the young man had already vanished. A sense of having escaped death welled up in his heart, and only now did he realize his clothes were soaked with sweat, as if he’d just been fished out of a lake.

In the void, Yang Kai and Yingfei sped side by side, moving as fast as lightning.

Sensing Yang Kai’s foul mood, Yingfei didn’t dare disturb him and only thought to himself that someone was about to suffer.

Yang Kai had dared to destroy a behemoth like the Wenqing Sect in the Northern Territory. Now that this Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had provoked him, they likely wouldn’t end well either.

After a long silence, Yingfei finally said, “Young Master Yang, I don’t know if it’s just my imagination, but I feel like something about this whole affair is off.”

Yang Kai nodded slightly. “I feel it too. Yao King, what do you think is wrong?”

As the saying goes, those involved are confused, while bystanders see clearly. Earlier, he had been focused on learning about the Thousand Leaves Sect and Chi Yue’s fate, so he hadn’t thought much. Now, flying through the sky with the cold wind clearing his mind, he noticed many flaws.

With Yingfei’s reminder, he immediately realized he wasn’t just being paranoid—something really was off.

Yingfei replied, “If the Thousand Leaves Sect was wiped out a year ago, why is this auction only being held now? If they really intended to do it, it should have started long ago. Not only that, but the whole affair seems to have been deliberately made into a huge spectacle, as if someone wanted to make sure the news reached a certain person. And this whole year was just time for the news to ferment, as if someone was afraid it wouldn’t spread.”

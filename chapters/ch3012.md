## Chapter 3012 — Entering Ice Heart Valley

At her words, the three Yao Kings finally noticed Yang Kai's presence. They hurriedly cupped their fists in greeting, their expressions incomparably awkward.

Quick-eyed and deft-handed, Yingfei swept up the dice cup and dice from the stone table.

Three Yao Kings and an Imperial-Grade Artifact Refiner had neglected their proper duties to gather here and gamble, only to be caught red-handed by Yang Kai. It truly was rather disgraceful.

"Mm. I sensed that you were here, so I came over to have a look," Yang Kai casually replied.

Xi Lei hurriedly asked, "Does Young Master Yang have any instructions for us?"

"Nothing in particular!" Yang Kai did not mention their gambling. Reaching out, he seized Hou Yu, who had been casually edging away in an attempt to retreat, and dragged her back.

"Palace Master... I won't dare do it again." Held aloft in Yang Kai's grasp, Hou Yu squeezed out a placating smile. "Spare me this once. I'll go refine artifacts for you right away. Didn't you bring back a lot of things a few days ago? Don't worry, just leave them all to me."

Yang Kai released her and asked, "When you encounter a bottleneck in your cultivation and can't break through, what do you do?"

"What?"

"A bottleneck?"

"What bottleneck?"

The three Yao Kings stared at him in astonishment, never expecting him to suddenly ask such an unrelated question.

Yingfei replied, "We've never encountered any bottlenecks."

Yang Kai stared at him wide-eyed. "Not even once?"

Xi Lei chuckled. "Young Master Yang, we Yao rely on bloodline and origins, which is somewhat different from you humans. Our bloodline and origins basically determine the greatest heights we can reach in this life. Of course, there are some exceptions and fortuitous opportunities, but generally speaking, there are no bottlenecks in the cultivation of the Yao."

"Right, right." Xie Wuwei also nodded. "Cultivation is an innate gift for us Yao, unlike the myriad cultivation techniques of the Human Race, so the concept of a bottleneck has no meaning to us."

"I forgot, you're Yao!" Yang Kai nodded, then looked at Hou Yu. "What about you?"

"Me?" Hou Yu had yet to recover from her fright. Seeing Yang Kai look at her, she smiled awkwardly. "Palace Master, I'm an artificer. I don't pay much attention to cultivation, and somehow I just stumbled my way into the Emperor Realm. This question you're asking me... is a little too profound."

"You've never encountered a bottleneck?" Yang Kai looked at her doubtfully.

"I think I have." Hou Yu thought back for a moment, then nodded. "Yes, I have."

"Then how did you break through?"

"I waited. Refined some artifacts, gambled a little... When the time came, I naturally broke through."

Yang Kai pondered for a moment, then nodded lightly.

"Young Master Yang, you..." Yingfei looked at him, hesitating to speak.

"It's nothing. Carry on." Yang Kai smiled faintly. With a flicker, he vanished without a trace, departing as mysteriously as he had arrived.

"Has Young Master Yang encountered a bottleneck in his cultivation?" Xi Lei asked thoughtfully.

"He probably has." Xie Wuwei nodded. "I heard that he's been cultivating in retreat all this time, but judging by his cultivation today, it hasn't increased. He may truly have encountered a bottleneck."

Yingfei said, "Humans have cultivation techniques and Secret Arts to assist them, so their cultivation may generally improve faster than that of the Yao, and they have greater potential for growth. But if they encounter a bottleneck they cannot overcome, they may never advance another step. I hope Young Master Yang won't remain stuck in the First-Stage Emperor Realm."

Xi Lei replied, "He's still young. Even if he has encountered a bottleneck, it's nothing serious. Everyone suffers a setback or two. Young Master Yang isn't the sort to be easily discouraged, so don't worry. He'll overcome this hurdle."

"Let's hope so!"

"Are you three still gambling?" Hou Yu gazed eagerly at the three Yao Kings, her hands itching unbearably.

Xi Lei glanced sideways at her. "What do you have left to lose?"

Hou Yu thought for a moment and found herself speechless.

...

Spirit Pill Peak was one of Lingxiao Palace's main peaks. Whether in scenery or Spiritual Qi, it was in no way inferior to Lingxiao Peak. It was also the residence of Ji Ying, Great Emperor Miaodan's fifth disciple and Lingxiao Palace's Chief Alchemist.

When Yang Kai arrived, he heard a gentle voice ringing out, as though someone were explaining the Alchemic Dao.

Looking over, he saw a man sitting cross-legged atop the peak, speaking eloquently and without pause.

Below him, more than a dozen cultivators of varying cultivation listened with rapt attention, thoughtful expressions on their faces.

These cultivators were all disciples of Thousand Leaves Sect. Presumably, they wished to study the Alchemic Dao and had come here to learn from a teacher. Given Ji Ying's status as one of the few Imperial-Grade Pill Masters in the entire Star Realm, however, he naturally could not personally lecture them. Even if Ji Ying were willing, they might not necessarily understand him.

The one giving the lecture was one of Ji Ying's disciples, whom Yang Kai had also met before. Yang Kai knew he was a Dao Origin-Grade Alchemist with a solid foundation.

Ji Ying's voice suddenly sounded in his ear through a transmission. "Brother Yang, you've come. Please, come in!"

Yang Kai smiled faintly, then entered a grand hall with a flicker of his figure.

Inside the grand hall, heat waves rolled and Alchemic Flames surged. A Pill Furnace stood at the centre of the hall, while Ji Ying waited beside it with a smile. When he saw Yang Kai arrive, he said joyfully, "Brother Yang, you've come at just the right time. I wanted to visit you earlier, but Chief Steward Hua told me you were in retreat, so it wasn't convenient to disturb you."

"Is something the matter, Brother Ji?" Yang Kai asked curiously.

"It's like this. This Ji is preparing to refine a furnace of Nine Heavens Fortune Pills, but there are several obscure points concerning the condensation of the medicinal liquid and the inscription of the Pill Array. I hoped to seek Brother Yang's guidance."

"Nine Heavens Fortune Pills!" Yang Kai was startled. He naturally knew of this Emperor Pill. It was an extremely difficult Imperial-Grade Spirit Pill to refine, and even an Imperial-Grade Pill Master might fail despite going all out. Although Ji Ying was Great Emperor Miaodan's fifth disciple, his skill had not reached Great Emperor Miaodan's level, so he did not have much confidence in successfully refining such a Spirit Pill.

In all of Lingxiao Palace, only Yang Kai was qualified to discuss the matter with him.

"I wouldn't dare call it guidance. I happen to have nothing else to do, so let's study it together," Yang Kai replied modestly.

"That was precisely my intention. With Brother Yang's help, there should be no problem with this furnace of Spirit Pills." Ji Ying was overjoyed.

Three days later, Yang Kai emerged from Spirit Pill Peak. His expression was weary, but his eyes were exceptionally bright.

As he walked, his expression suddenly changed and he smiled wryly. He had come here to ask Ji Ying about bottlenecks in cultivation, but Ji Ying had drawn him into studying the Nine Heavens Fortune Pill, causing him to completely forget.

Ji Ying had already begun refining the pills, so it was naturally inconvenient to disturb him. Besides, even if Yang Kai asked him, Ji Ying might not be able to offer any useful insight. After all, Ji Ying's situation was similar to Hou Yu's. Neither of them focused primarily on cultivation; one was obsessed with the Alchemic Dao, while the other was devoted to the Artifact Refining Dao. Even if they encountered bottlenecks in their cultivation, they might not take them too seriously, so how could they have much experience in such matters?

With a sigh, he looked around. Despite the vastness of Lingxiao Palace, he unexpectedly could not find anyone he could talk to.

At that moment, one beautiful figure after another flashed through his mind, and longing churned within his chest.

Only after a long while did he take a step and disappear from the spot.

When he reappeared, he had already arrived at the Space Array.

"Greetings, Palace Master!" The disciples guarding the Array hurriedly saluted.

Yang Kai nodded and stepped onto the Array.

One disciple hurriedly asked, "Is Palace Master going out? When will you return? The Chief Steward may ask about it."

"I'm going to Ice Heart Valley," Yang Kai replied. He activated the Laws of Space, and light instantly enveloped him.

Ice Heart Valley, Ice Lake Forbidden Ground.

As Yang Kai's figure appeared, a feminine shout immediately rang out. "A friend from Lingxiao Palace?"

"Yes!" Yang Kai replied.

This Array connected only Ice Heart Valley and Lingxiao Palace to facilitate travel between them. Therefore, only people from Lingxiao Palace could teleport here through it. When the Array activated, the Ice Heart Valley disciple guarding it naturally had to ask.

After confirming that he was from Lingxiao Palace, the disciple walked forward. Once she saw his face clearly, she exclaimed, "Are... are you Palace Master Yang?"

Yang Kai looked at her and found that he did not recognize her. She was a baby-faced woman who appeared to be only eighteen or nineteen, yet she had a First-Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation, so her true age was clearly greater than her appearance suggested.

At that moment, the woman looked overjoyed. A strange light flickered in her beautiful eyes, as though she had seen a family member.

"You recognize me?" Yang Kai smiled faintly.

The woman excitedly replied, "All the sisters in Ice Heart Valley recognize you."

Yang Kai felt a little embarrassed, but when he thought about it, it made sense. The last time Ice Heart Valley was besieged by Wenqing Sect, he had brought a Yao King with him and turned the tide. Many Ice Heart Valley disciples had witnessed that battle, and even those who had not could have heard about him from their Senior and Junior Sisters.

"Palace Master Yang, have you come to see the Founder, or... Senior Aunt Ji Yao?" Everyone in Ice Heart Valley knew that Yang Kai and Ji Yao were on good terms. It was Yang Kai who had brought back this Third Senior Aunt after her many years of disappearance, and she was said to have suffered many hardships outside over the years.

"I've come to request an audience with Senior Bing Yun," Yang Kai replied politely.

"Then I'll send word for you!" As the disciple spoke, she hurriedly took out her Transmission Compass and poured her Divine Sense into it. A moment later, she said, "Palace Master Yang, please follow me."

"Many thanks!"

Yang Kai followed the disciple deeper into the valley. Along the way, they encountered many Ice Heart Valley disciples, a dazzling profusion of beautiful women.

Just as she had said, all the disciples of Ice Heart Valley recognized Yang Kai. Most immediately saluted when they saw him, while those farther away gathered together and whispered, pointing at him. He had no idea what they were saying, but judging by their expressions, it was nothing bad.

Ice Heart Valley was a women's Sect, and every person in the valley was female. Men were generally forbidden from setting foot here.

Only Yang Kai would receive such treatment. If any other man had come here, he would have long since been torn to pieces.

The valley's scenery was beautiful and its Spiritual Qi rich. Combined with the countless beauties of every shape and figure, any man who came here would probably be reluctant to leave.

The female disciple leading the way seemed quite proud of herself. She chatted with Yang Kai from time to time along the way, provoking boundless envy from many of her Senior and Junior Sisters.

Before long, they arrived at a bedchamber.

The female disciple stopped and said, "Please enter, Palace Master Yang. The Founder is inside."

"Many thanks!"

The woman hurriedly waved her hands. "Palace Master Yang is too polite. This is merely this disciple's duty."

## Chapter 3115 — Who Can Oppose Her

"That was only a warning!" Su Yan looked calmly at Han Qiancheng. Since he had spoken so rudely, he naturally deserved one.

"Very good!" Han Qiancheng had escaped death by a hair, and his expression shifted again and again. He glared venomously at Su Yan as he slowly backed away, then stopped outside the lava cave and waved his hand. "Take her down!"

Today, no matter what, he would have a taste of her beauty. His patience had long since been worn away, and he was done waiting. If she refused the offered toast, then she could drink the punishment wine!

The two Elders who had followed him stood shoulder to shoulder, blocking the cave entrance. Their faces bore some resemblance, so they were probably brothers. Both were broad-backed and powerfully built. When they urged their Origin Qi, a faint red glow surfaced over their bodies, like burning flames, scorching hot.

Since Han Qiancheng had brought people to the Fire Cloud Mine to trouble Su Yan, of course he had come prepared. The two Elders he had chosen both cultivated Fire Attribute Secret Arts. In a place like the Fire Cloud Mine, where Fire Qi was abundant, they could display strength beyond their usual limits.

Once those two stood there, the only exit was sealed. If Su Yan wanted to leave, she would have to carve out a bloody path.

The Elder on the left said in a deep voice, "Su Yan, my Yunxia Sect has shown you a kindness almost equal to giving you a new life. Back when you first came to the Ancestral Domain, if my Yunxia Sect had not taken you in, how could you have reached this point? Since you received such kindness, you should know how to repay it. Is this how you repay the Young Sect Master?"

Su Yan said, "The one who showed me kindness is Master. What does that have to do with Yunxia Sect?"

The Elder on the right snorted coldly. "The Young Sect Master is a dragon among men. One day, he will surely inherit the position of Yunxia Sect's Sect Master. In what way is he unworthy of you? If you know what is good for you, agree to the Young Sect Master's request at once. Perhaps you can spare yourself some pain."

Su Yan slowly shook her head. "Impossible!"

"Stubborn fool!"

"You're courting death!"

The two Elders shouted from left and right. Their Origin Qi shook the cave, and loose stones rattled down.

Han Qiancheng cursed even more furiously, "Why waste words? Hurry up and take her down for me. I want her to regret this for the rest of her life! Mm, don't ruin her face or body, lest you spoil this Young Master's pleasure."

"Young Sect Master, rest assured. She is only a little girl. The two of us can capture her easily." The Elder on the left snorted coldly, and the Origin Qi inside him suddenly surged. With a shift of his feet, he pounced toward Su Yan. His large hand opened and grabbed down with a crash. The surrounding Fire Qi seemed drawn by him, swiftly gathering in his palm and adding to his might.

The Profound Frost Divine Sword swept out, and a semicircular curtain of sword light suddenly appeared one zhang in front of Su Yan, radiating a deep blue glow as it shielded her. The sword curtain looked impossibly thin, as if a breeze could break it, but it had been forged from densely packed sword lights.

The left Elder's large hand had only just touched the sword curtain when pain stabbed through it. Origin Qi burst from his palm and slammed into the sword curtain.

Ripples spread across it, but the sword curtain remained unharmed. Instead, the left Elder's charging figure was stopped cold. His brow rose, and surprise flashed across his face. Although everyone here had First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation, he had advanced many years ago, while Su Yan had been stationed inside the Fire Cloud Mine all these years and could not possibly have grown stronger. With time and terrain also on his side, the left Elder had thought he could defeat her casually.

Only after one exchange did he realize how wrong he had been. Su Yan's sword curtain was actually as solid as an impregnable fortress. As expected of a cultivator from a Lower Plane Star Domain, the purity of her Origin Qi was simply beyond imagination.

But so what?

When the left Elder's strike failed, he immediately drifted three steps away. Angling to the side, he circled behind Su Yan.

At the same time, the Elder on the right stepped forward, and a bronze hammer appeared in his hand. The bronze hammer was a full three chi in diameter, clearly a weapon of tremendous force and weight. Holding this Secret Treasure, the right Elder reached the sword curtain in a few steps and smashed down fiercely.

Su Yan's fine brows furrowed slightly. She also sensed the power of this move. If she were outside, she could still rely on her nimble movement to dodge it, but here inside this lava cave, with her movements restricted, how could she avoid it?

The sword curtain suddenly scattered. The Profound Frost Divine Sword burst with countless rays of cold light, and in the next instant, those countless cold rays gathered into a single point and stabbed straight toward the right Elder.

Sword Intent soared to the sky, and Sword Qi turned bitterly cold. Su Yan's face was indifferent, her eyes filled with ruthless, resolute chill, as if she did not even see the bronze hammer smashing down over her head. She looked ready to perish together with the right Elder.

The right Elder was startled. How could he dare continue his attack? If his hammer fell, it could certainly make Su Yan perish like fading fragrance and scattered jade, but he feared he would die even faster. Feeling the power carried by that longsword, his heart shook in horror. How was this woman so strong?

And this was after she had been suppressed for ten years. If she had been cultivating peacefully in Yunxia Sect all along, enjoying Yunxia Sect's supply of resources, then how far would she have grown by today?

Such aptitude was simply terrifying. No wonder the Sect Master had planned ahead and exiled her to the Fire Cloud Mine to stand guard for ten years. Otherwise, in today's Yunxia Sect, who could possibly be her opponent?

In that flash of time, the bronze hammer swept downward and sideways, blocking his vital points.

The Profound Frost Divine Sword nimbly turned aside, brushed past the right Elder, and shot straight toward Han Qiancheng behind him.

Such a fast change! The right Elder's face changed drastically. How could he fail to understand that Su Yan's apparent willingness to die together with him had only been a feint? Her real aim was to capture the ringleader first. With the two Elders working together, there was no chance Su Yan could leave this place today, but if she captured Han Qiancheng, she would seize absolute initiative.

At this point, it was already a little too late to turn back and save Han Qiancheng. At the critical moment, the right Elder lifted one foot and stomped heavily on the ground. Earth and stone cracked, and broken rocks shot into the air, filling Su Yan's vision. Even that sharp Sword Intent seemed to slow for a moment.

"Get back here!" Seizing this chance, the right Elder finally swung his bronze hammer out, blocking Su Yan's only path forward.

The Profound Frost Divine Sword struck the bronze hammer, and Su Yan used the force to rebound, springing back to her original position. A trace of regret appeared on her face. If she had been just a little faster, everything would have been different. But as a First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator who had been suppressed for ten years, accomplishing this much under the two Elders' pincer attack was already beyond expectation. Whether Han Qiancheng or the two Yunxia Sect Elders, all of them broke out in a cold sweat.

The exchange unfolded with the speed of a startled hare and a diving hawk, so swift and dazzling that the eye could barely follow.

The left Elder, who had long since circled behind Su Yan, finally found his chance. A fiery red soft whip came whistling over, like a fire snake flicking its tongue as it coiled toward Su Yan.

Su Yan did not even turn her head, simply bringing her sword behind her to block. With a movement exquisite to the extreme, she stopped the fire snake’s strike, but unexpectedly, the fire snake twisted and climbed up along the blade. In an instant, fire and ice clashed fiercely, and thick mist filled the cavern until one could not see even their own fingers.

Su Yan turned back and tapped one finger on the fiery red soft whip that had become a fire snake. In that instant, her slender jade finger suddenly became crystalline and translucent, as if carved from ice and jade rather than made of flesh and blood.

The left Elder cried out in alarm. In that instant, he actually felt the spirituality of his Secret Treasure suffer damage, and that damage was deepening at an astonishing speed. Not daring to delay, he quickly withdrew it. When he fixed his eyes on it, his expression changed.

The tip of his soft whip had actually been covered in a layer of ice crystals, which were rapidly spreading outward.

The left Elder did not dare be careless. He urged his Origin Qi, and the soft whip snapped open, only then shattering the ice crystals. Horror filled his face.

So strong. So strong!

Although they had only exchanged blows for a few breaths, both the left Elder and the right Elder had already seen that in a one-on-one fight, neither of them was Su Yan's opponent at all! This had already exceeded the limits of their understanding.

Within the dense fog, the Divine Senses of all three surged, probing for one another's positions. After that exchange, no one dared attack lightly. Su Yan was beset from front and back, and although she had displayed powerful strength, if she could not think of a perfect plan today, she definitely would not be able to leave this place safely.

The left and right Elders also did not dare advance rashly. They only wanted to find a chance to capture their enemy in one strike.

The scene suddenly fell quiet, strange to the extreme.

Han Qiancheng shouted angrily in dissatisfaction, “What the hell are you doing? Usually, when it comes to collecting your stipends, each of you is more eager than the last, but now that I have asked you to capture one person, you are actually dragging your feet like this. You are simply useless!”

The left and right Elders burned with anger at being cursed and thought to themselves that if this piece of trash were not the Young Sect Master, they would have killed him long ago. He knew nothing, yet he stood there ordering them around. He was simply ridiculous.

But no matter what, the two of them had joined hands and still failed to gain any advantage, so both the left Elder and the right Elder had lost face. Their Divine Senses communicated secretly for a moment, as if they had reached some agreement. In the next instant, Divine Sense surged inside the lava cave, and two invisible forces rushed toward where Su Yan stood.

Divine Soul attacks!

This woman's Origin Qi was extremely pure, not inferior to theirs in the slightest. If they clashed head-on, she might very well drag one of them down with her. But Divine Soul attacks were different. She had only advanced a dozen years or so ago. How powerful could her Divine Sense be?

Using one's own strength to attack the enemy's weakness was the usual method of clever people.

Sure enough, when the two Divine Soul attacks blasted toward her, Su Yan gave a muffled groan, clearly finding it somewhat hard to bear. Her Divine Soul defense held for only a short while before it was broken open.

The left and right Elders were overjoyed. An opportunity missed would not come again. Without any more hesitation, the right Elder swung his bronze hammer and pounced toward Su Yan.

In the dimness, a point of cold light bloomed ahead. Sweeping over it with Divine Sense, he saw Su Yan holding her sword and stabbing toward him with a resolute expression, completely giving up her own defense.

Again?

The right Elder sneered inwardly. Earlier, Su Yan had deceived him with this posture of mutual destruction, making him lose face. How could the same move work again?

His heart hardened, and he also abandoned his own defense, sending the bronze hammer straight toward the Profound Frost Divine Sword.

The bronze hammer was a Lower-Level Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure, while the Profound Frost Divine Sword was only an Upper-Level Void King-Grade artifact that Su Yan had brought with her from the Star Domain. The two were not of the same grade. If they truly collided head-on, Su Yan would be the only one to suffer.

A smug smile appeared on the right Elder's face.

That smile suddenly froze.

In the instant before the two Secret Treasures touched, Su Yan's long sword nimbly avoided the bronze hammer and wound its way up along it as if it were alive. Cold Qi lingered around the sword's edge, making one shudder.

The sound of a sharp blade piercing flesh rang out, and the right Elder gave a muffled groan.

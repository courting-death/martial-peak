## Chapter 2855 — Kill

The two, being twins, had cultivated Shamanic Art together for many years and had long since reached a state of perfect mutual understanding. So, even with just a simple exchange of glances, they could see what the other was thinking.

The Green Demon’s poison was incurable, yet Shaman Niu claimed he would not die. That left only one choice for him…

“How much do you know about the demons?” Yang Kai asked again.

Yu collected her thoughts and replied, “Not much. There’s very little information in the records. Besides the Green Demon, there’s also a Red Demon, which is its counterpart. In the last war, these two types of demons caused countless deaths among the people of this land.”

“Red Demon?” Yang Kai raised his eyebrows, and some ridiculous figures involuntarily appeared in his mind. “Are they the short, round ones with red skin and hair?”

“Maybe…” Yu wasn’t too sure either.

“What special abilities do the Red Demons have?”

Yu said, “The Red Demons are also called Explosive Demons. Their only value and purpose is to explode!”

“There are such demons?” Yang Kai was greatly surprised. Back in the demon cave, Yang Kai had indeed killed a few demons with red skin. Those demons exuded an extremely dangerous aura, were short and fat, like wooden barrels. He hadn’t thought much of it when killing them, but now, after Yu’s explanation, he realized these creatures were born to explode.

Just imagine if they hadn't learned this information beforehand. If they were to encounter a massive army of Red Demons on the battlefield, only to have them explode one after another during combat, the Barbarian Tribe would surely suffer devastating casualties.

This was another extremely important piece of intelligence.

However, regarding the demons, the sisters Yu and Lu only knew about the Red and Green Demons. They hadn’t even heard of the Sand Demon before.

So, they couldn’t provide Yang Kai with much useful information.

On the contrary, it was Yang Kai who, after a trip to the demon cave, had gathered a lot of intelligence. He shared what he had seen with the other shamans, so they could pass it on, ensuring the tribesmen would be cautious in future battles.

Afterwards, Yang Kai found an open space, raised his hand, and a huge black shadow suddenly appeared.

It was the demon beast Yang Kai had captured from the demon cave earlier.

Black energy swirled around the demon beast’s body, constantly seeping out. As soon as it appeared, it let out a furious roar, startling more than a dozen shamans, who looked at the beast and then at Yang Kai in shock, completely clueless as to how he had conjured such a creature out of thin air.

However, Yang Kai had placed several restrictions on the demon beast, sealing all its strength. So, despite its fierce appearance, it couldn’t do anything at all.

“It seems the demons have cavalry. This kind of demon beast is their mount. Study it a bit and see if you can find any weaknesses. If we can use it, all the better. If not, just kill it.”

Having said that, Yang Kai walked into a newly built wooden house nearby.

A group of shamans immediately surrounded the demon beast, enthusiastically studying it.

In a flash, two days passed. Yang Kai hid in the wooden house for two days without appearing, and no one knew what he was doing. The three-thousand-strong army was brimming with anger and fighting spirit, but had nowhere to vent it.

In the demon cave, Shaya sat on her throne, her tight and revealing clothes outlining her graceful figure in exquisite detail. The breast Yang Kai had blasted apart a few days ago had now returned to its former glory. Who knew what secret art she had used to completely heal her injuries.

At this moment, she rested her chin on her hand, seemingly dozing.

Below, the short demon stood there silently, completely devoid of any aura, like a corpse.

After a long while, Shaya finally opened her eyes and said indifferently, “What’s the situation over there? Has he not come yet?”

The short demon immediately replied, “According to the intelligence gathered by the Sandfolk, the tribesmen over there seem somewhat unsettled. As for that person, he has been keeping himself hidden the entire time, without making any notable moves.”

Shaya sneered, “Looks like he’s trying to find a way to neutralize the Green Demon’s poison. Ridiculous. If the Green Demon’s poison could be easily neutralized, it wouldn’t be the most sinister poison of my demon race. Still, for him to last two days without giving in is quite impressive.”

As she spoke, she stood up, her beautiful eyes sharp. “Pass down the order for the tribesmen to prepare.”

The short demon’s expression brightened. “My Lady, are you going to start the battle?”

Shaya sneered, “If he won’t come to me, I’ll go to him. There aren’t many slaves as interesting as him. I don’t want him to just die like that.”

Hearing this, the short demon couldn’t help but feel sorry for the Barbarian Tribe, but still turned to pass on the order.

In an instant, Demonic Qi surged within the demon cave as countless demons gathered.

Half an hour later, the tribesmen of Shaman Niu’s tribe, a hundred li away, suddenly felt a strange tremor from the earth. At the same time, the Sky Cavalry on patrol in the sky sounded their horns.

The deep sound echoed across the land. All three thousand tribesmen’s expressions turned grim as they picked up their weapons and formed ranks.

Yang Kai, still shrouded in green light, quickly walked out of the wooden house and looked toward the demon cave. The sky over there was pitch black, as if a storm was about to break. The darkness was rapidly surging toward them, threatening to swallow the light.

Yang Kai curled his lips into a cold sneer.

More than a dozen shamans stood at the front of the three thousand, all looking at him with worried expressions.

Yang Kai had been poisoned by the Green Demon for two days. Although he seemed fine now, who knew how long he could hold out? With the great battle about to begin, what if he succumbed to the poison and died in the fight?

In such a war, if the commander died, it would be a huge blow to everyone, possibly turning victory into defeat in an instant.

But at this critical moment, no matter how worried everyone was, they couldn’t voice it. They could only pray silently. Yu and Lu, in particular, had mentally prepared themselves for the worst.

Yang Kai’s gaze swept past the dozen or so Shamans before settling on the three thousand tribesmen. Drawing a deep breath, he shouted, “Those standing here are the finest warriors of my Barbarian Tribe, brothers of Shaman Niu’s tribe! The demons have already come—we have only one path left: fight them to the death! Our enemies are the very ones who ambushed us in that village days ago! We lost over a hundred people then, but that was only the beginning. If we cannot win this battle, thousands will die—perhaps even more! They will slaughter our men, seize our women, enslave our children, and take the land that belongs to us!”

The three thousand tribesmen’s breathing grew heavy, their fighting spirit soaring to the sky, as if to tear apart the clouds.

“This is our first true battle! So we have only one goal—victory! Not just victory, but a glorious one! Only then can we wash away the humiliation Shaman Niu’s tribe suffered days ago! My warriors… are you ready!?”

“Roar! Roar! Roar!”

The three thousand tribesmen, like three thousand maddened beasts, had red glints in their eyes even without the blessing of the Bloodlust Art.

Yang Kai smiled faintly, raised his hand, and pointed forward, facing the darkest part of the sky. “If you’re ready, then make them pay the price!”

The cry of an eagle rang out as the Sky Cavalry soared into the sky.

Ten regular squads, led by their captains, marched swiftly and silently toward the battlefield. The synchronized, urgent, and dense footsteps made the earth tremble.

The guard squad surrounded the shamans, defending them closely. As they marched, obscure incantations began to ring out, and rays of Shamanic Art light burst from the shamans’ hands, spreading in circles over the ten squads.

Bloodlust Art, Lightness Art, Stone Armor Art, Great Strength Art, Chain of Life…

Every warrior’s body glowed with brilliant colors. Each barbarian warrior grew even taller, their muscles bulging, faces fierce, and their already red eyes turned even redder.

Without any elaborate tactics, the demons and barbarians charged at each other as if by agreement.

From above, two armies of several thousand each converged from different directions, with the wide plain in the center as the battlefield.

In the space of a single incense stick, the hundred-li distance was closed.

The dozens of li sprint pushed the barbarians’ fighting spirit and killing intent to the limit.

As the black clouds began to cover the land, the two armies finally clashed.

The barbarians at the front were momentarily stunned.

Because they saw familiar faces—faces of their own people, their fellow tribesmen.

The vanguard in the demon ranks was actually those tribesmen who had been demonized.

But now, these tribesmen had lost all clarity of mind, each one crazed and irrational. Standing before them were not their former kin, but mortal enemies.

Without hesitation, they raised their weapons and struck down hard.

“Kill!” roared the voices.

The people of Shaman Niu’s tribe only hesitated for a moment before quickly regaining their composure.

Thanks to the intelligence Yang Kai had gathered earlier, everyone was mentally prepared for this situation in the battle against the demons.

Every barbarian had asked themselves: if they really encountered demonized tribesmen, could they bring themselves to strike? No matter their current state, they had once been barbarians, with their own brothers, sisters, and clans. Maybe there was still a chance to save them.

But soon, everyone realized such thoughts were meaningless.

If you met such tribesmen on the battlefield, letting them die swiftly was the best release.

Showing mercy to them would be irresponsible to your companions.

At the front, Ah Hu leapt high, jumping ten zhang into the air, and brought his giant axe down hard.

Even before the attack landed, the sharp aura from the axe split the skin of the demonized tribesman. When Ah Hu landed, the man was already cleaved in two.

Ah Hu raised his giant axe and roared ferociously, “Kill!”

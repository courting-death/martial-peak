## Chapter 3003 — This Cannot Be Tolerated

Hong, hong, hong...

Swords rained down, bombarding Yang Kai's body and sending dazzling sparks flying. Even his enormous four-and-a-half-zhang-tall body was knocked this way and that by the attacks coming from every direction, as though he might collapse at any moment.

Chi, chi, chi...

As blood sprayed through the air, Yang Kai grimaced in pain, making his already fierce face appear even more terrifying.

He remained rooted to the spot, curling his entire body into a ball and exposing his back as he shielded Lin Yun'er from the slightest harm.

"Uncle Yang!" Yun'er cried out as she raised her head, shock and wonder flashing through her eyes. She clearly had not expected Yang Kai to suddenly undergo such an enormous transformation, but after sensing the attacks he was enduring, her eyes instantly reddened.

Yang Kai grinned. “Avoid their edge for now. We’ll deal with them later.”

Lin Yun'er nodded, a ruthless light flashing across her face.

The unending rain of swords continued for quite some time before gradually subsiding.

"This..." Great Elder's voice rang out, clearly astonished to the extreme. He had truly never imagined that Yang Kai could survive inside the Sword Formation. This was the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation formed by a hundred people, with a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master like himself serving as its Formation Core, five or six First-Stage Emperor Realm Masters assisting him, and the rest all Third-Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivators.

Even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master would surely have been annihilated by an attack like that, would they not?

Yet not only was this fellow still alive, but his appearance had also changed.

Two bumps had risen from his head, his hands had transformed into sharp claws, and the exposed parts of his skin were densely covered in scales.

What the fuck is that thing?

Great Elder was so horrified that he was nearly speechless. His thoughts raced as he once again urged the power of the Sword Formation.

After that rain of swords, there had not been many longswords left within the Sword Formation, but in the next instant, sharp swords of varying shapes and auras unexpectedly began appearing from every direction, one after another, as though they were inexhaustible.

Those sharp swords wavered between illusion and reality, making it impossible to tell whether they were real or false, but every one of them radiated soaring sword Qi.

Yang Kai's expression became solemn as his Divine Sense swept in all directions, anxiety inevitably rising in his heart.

He and Lin Yun'er were trapped inside this Sword Formation. Unless they found a way to break it or escape, their strength would be worn away sooner or later. After all, their enemies were numerous and could use the power of the Array to bring out their strength to the greatest extent, while he and Lin Yun'er stood alone. They might be able to hold out for a while, but not forever. Yet even after sweeping the area with his Divine Sense, he could not find a single opening.

His study of the Dao of Spirit Arrays was not profound, and he thought that if Nanmen Dajun were here, he might be able to offer some advice.

Just as Yang Kai was frowning deeply, Lin Yun'er spoke in a huff, "Uncle Yang, buy me some time."

She had killed many people before, but had always done so calmly, as though she were not killing people but casually kicking aside small stones by the roadside. Even after being repeatedly targeted and ambushed some time ago, she had never become angry. This time, however, she was truly furious.

There was no other reason than that Uncle Yang had suffered considerable injuries protecting her.

Yang Kai was taken aback. "You can break the Array?"

Lin Yun'er replied, "I have a trump card. It should be able to break it!"

"Good!" Yang Kai was overjoyed. "Focus on your technique. Uncle Yang definitely won't let them disturb you."

“Mm!” Lin Yun’er responded and immediately sat down cross-legged. She closed her beautiful eyes, placed one hand flat before her lower abdomen while the other formed a strange hand seal, and the aura around her body began to waver unpredictably.

Yang Kai did not know what she intended to do, but since Lin Yun'er had said so, he could only place his utmost trust in her. He probably would not be able to break this Array anytime soon, so if Lin Yun'er had a way, he naturally had to let her try.

Hopefully, she would give him a pleasant surprise. Even if she failed, he could simply think of another way when the time came.

"A mere junior dares speak of breaking this Array? What shameless boasting." Great Elder's voice rang out again. He had clearly overheard their conversation, and his tone was filled with disdain.

If the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation were truly so easy to break, it could not have helped Wind and Cloud Pavilion avert disaster several times. Even if these two juniors had some attainments in the Dao of Spirit Arrays, they were still incapable of seeing through its flaws.

As he spoke, Great Elder roared again, "Slash!"

An enormous sword light thundered down, aimed directly at Lin Yun'er.

The sword light was more than ten zhang long and resembled a pillar supporting the Heavens. As it cleaved downward, even the Void split apart.

The power of this strike was comparable to the full-force attack of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm expert, so its might was naturally extraordinary.

Yang Kai let out a low shout and placed his enormous body between Lin Yun'er and the sword light. He thrust forward a fist the size of a window, striking the sword light head-on.

With a thunderous crash, the sword light shattered. Yang Kai retreated two steps, a gash appearing across his knuckles as blood poured out.

"Impressive!" Great Elder was moved. Although he did not know what Secret Technique Yang Kai had used to make his body so enormous, receiving a strike from the Great Change Profound Sword Formation with his flesh alone was still an incredible feat. Great Elder coldly snorted, "This old man would like to see how long you can last."

"Slash!"

"Slash!"

"Slash!"

One enormous sword light after another descended, intent on exterminating Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er.

Unlike the unending rain of swords from before, every one of these sword lights possessed boundless power. Although they were far fewer in number, their strength had increased a hundredfold, even a thousandfold.

Yang Kai strode forward to meet them, his enormous body displaying an indescribable agility and grace as he swung his fists and received one sword after another.

With every sword he received, he was forced back several steps, and another wound appeared on his fists. His blood fell to the ground with a steady dripping sound.

By the time the final sword arrived, both of his fists had been reduced to a bloody mess, and even the Dragon Scales covering them had shattered. Gritting his teeth, he rammed it with his head.

With a thunderous crash, the sword light struck his head and sank more than an inch into it. Yang Kai's entire body was forced downward, and blood ran along his cheek.

"Hiss..." Great Elder sucked in a sharp breath, utterly shocked.

He actually could not kill him!

Just what was this fellow's background, and how could his defenses be so formidable? Even after taking so many of Great Elder's slashes, he had not fallen.

No Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master could possibly still be standing after that, could they? This brat was going to overturn the Heavens!

A ruthless light flashed through Great Elder's eyes. Another enormous sword light cleaved toward Yang Kai, while a separate streak of sword light silently flew toward Lin Yun'er.

If he could not kill Yang Kai, surely he could kill that little girl!

The moment the sword light appeared, Great Elder suddenly shuddered. For no apparent reason, he felt as though a powerful Divine Sense had locked onto him, and beneath that terrifying Divine Sense, it seemed the other party could kill him and leave him without a burial place with a single thought.

"Who is it?" Great Elder cried out in alarm.

No one answered.

Strangely enough, the feeling from just now also vanished without a trace, as though it had only been an illusion.

Great Elder frowned, unable to understand what had happened. He secretly wondered whether he had simply become too tense. He was now the Formation Core of the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation, joined as one with more than a hundred disciples and integrated into the Sword Formation. If anyone truly wished to harm him, they would first need to break the Sword Formation before they could find his true body.

That inexplicable murderous intent could not possibly have locked directly onto him. It must have been an illusion.

The sword light had already arrived before Lin Yun'er, but she remained motionless, as though she had not noticed the approaching danger. She simply continued casting her technique in silence, preparing to display her trump card.

It was unclear what was going on with this trump card of hers, but it actually required such a long time to prepare.

Yang Kai could not help secretly criticizing Yun'er's Master for being somewhat unreliable. With such a long preparation time, what did it matter how powerful this trump card was? In a true life-or-death battle, who would give her time to prepare?

Although he was occupied dealing with Great Elder and the others, Yang Kai had never relaxed his attention on Lin Yun'er, so he noticed the sword light the moment it appeared.

Left with no other choice, he could only summon the Mountain and River Bell. The tiny bell looked like a child's toy in his enormous hand.

As it spun rapidly, the Mountain and River Bell enveloped Lin Yun'er.

The sword light arrived as expected, but struck the bell instead.

"This is..." Great Elder was shocked yet again. His gaze pierced through the void and latched onto the Mountain and River Bell like a leech, a strange light flickering in his eyes.

With his eyesight, how could he fail to see how profound this Secret Treasure was? The aura of the Primordial Era radiating from it seemed to have crossed through eternity, inexplicably giving one the illusion of entering an ancient world.

"A Primordial Era Exotic Treasure!" Great Elder exclaimed, his gaze instantly becoming greedy.

He could not help wanting to laugh.

He had never imagined that the young man would possess such a precious treasure. Great Elder was a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master and had his own Imperial Treasures, but Imperial Treasures and Primordial Era Exotic Treasures were two entirely different concepts. The latter were few in number, yet possessed unfathomable power. If he could obtain one, it would greatly enhance his strength.

Looking at this bell again, it was clearly extraordinary. Even among Primordial Era Treasures, it was undoubtedly a high-grade existence that ordinary Primordial Era Treasures could not compare with.

Greed immediately arose in his heart, and he secretly resolved to seize this Primordial Era Treasure. How could a mere First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivator be worthy of owning such a treasure? Leaving it in Yang Kai's hands was simply a reckless waste of Heaven's gifts.

If he could obtain this Primordial Era Treasure, Great Elder was even confident he could exchange moves with a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master. Its attraction to him was simply too great.

While countless distracting thoughts surged through Great Elder's mind, Yang Kai raised a hand to wipe the blood from his face and gently drew a breath, a cold and stern light flashing through his eyes.

Unless absolutely necessary, he was unwilling to use the Mountain and River Bell.

The suppressive force of this Primordial Era Treasure was simply too powerful. Although it could provide Yun'er with formidable protection, she was currently preparing her trump card, and that suppressive force would inevitably interfere with her to some extent.

But with matters having reached this point, he had no other choice.

He could only hope Yun'er would not be affected too severely. To that end, he had weakened the power of the Mountain and River Bell as much as possible.

"Now I can finally fight without holding back. Old dog, you'd better hide well and pray this Young Master doesn't find you. Once I do, I'll skin you alive!" Yang Kai swept a cold gaze around and sneered menacingly.

"Junior, you still dare act rampant!" Great Elder roared furiously, thoroughly exasperated.

He could hardly save face anymore. After all, they had even deployed the Array and joined forces with more than a hundred people, so they should have dealt with Yang Kai quickly. Yet not only was this fellow still alive and kicking, but he even dared threaten him.

This simply could not be tolerated!

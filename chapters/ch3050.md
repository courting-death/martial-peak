## Chapter 3050 — Stealing Dragon Blood Flowers

Fu Ling shook her head. "It isn't that our Dragon Clan is arrogant. It's just that no one on Dragon Isle would dare trespass upon the place where the Dragon Blood Flowers are cultivated. Moreover, every Dragon Blood Flower there is accounted for. Only the Elders are qualified to harvest them; ordinary members of the Dragon Clan are not allowed to touch them at all. That's why the place doesn't need to be guarded." She paused, then continued, "Besides, the Dragon Clan is small in number to begin with, so we can't possibly station several guards there. We generally take turns standing watch. It happens to be my turn this year; otherwise, I wouldn't have gone to receive those mongrels a few days ago."

Yang Kai nodded upon hearing this.

When he had come to Dragon Isle with Li Jiao and the other Dragon Descendants some time ago, Fu Ling had indeed been the one to take them away. It seemed that this was also one of her duties as the guard.

Moreover, the defenses everywhere on Dragon Isle were extremely lax, something Yang Kai had experienced firsthand over the past few days.

He had visited so many Spirit Islands, yet Fu Ling was the only one who had discovered his presence. That alone showed just how lax Dragon Isle's defenses were.

In his view, this was precisely a manifestation of the Dragon Clan's arrogance. Because they were arrogant, they saw no need to guard against anything.

The Dragon Clan did have the strength to justify such arrogance.

They likely never imagined that a human would actually be bold enough to set his sights on the Dragon Blood Flowers.

As they traveled, Yang Kai suddenly thought of a question. "When does your Dragon Clan usually harvest the Dragon Blood Flowers?"

Fu Ling replied, "It's still a long way off, at least another two months."

"Good." Yang Kai relaxed. In two months, he would likely have left Dragon Isle long ago, so it would not matter even if the matter came to light by then.

He would definitely disrupt Zhu Qing's wedding and steal the bride. That alone was enough to offend the Dragon Clan completely. Having already prepared himself for that outcome, Yang Kai felt no guilt whatsoever about plundering Dragon Isle's Dragon Blood Flowers.

Since he was destined to offend them anyway, he might as well go all the way and obtain some Dragon Blood Flowers to strengthen his position.

The Spirit Island where the Dragon Blood Flowers were cultivated was not very large, but its Spiritual Qi was extraordinarily rich, seemingly due to the presence of an excellent Earth Vein. The entire island was also a vivid crimson, as though it had been cast from fresh blood. Even from the shore, the scent of blood was clearly discernible.

Yang Kai and Fu Ling landed on the island together. The moment he swept it with his Divine Sense, he detected the auras of living beings.

These living beings felt extremely weak, their life forces like candle flames flickering in the wind and rain, liable to go out at any moment. There were quite a few of them, a full two hundred.

Dragon Descendants!

These living beings were all Dragon Descendants who had been brought to Dragon Isle. The Dragon Clan was using their blood to water the Dragon Blood Flowers.

Yang Kai frowned. His Divine Sense locked onto a familiar aura, and his figure flickered before disappearing from the spot.

Li Jiao had been living miserably these past few days. After parting with Yang Kai, he had been brought to this island and, under Fu Ling's arrangements, made to sit inside a Spirit Array. The instant the Spirit Array began operating, Li Jiao felt his Essence-Blood being extracted and transformed into a blood mist that permeated the island.

The entire Spirit Island was shrouded in a layer of deep crimson mist, all of it formed from the Essence-Blood of Dragon Descendants.

He had wanted to resist, but he could not escape the Spirit Array at all. As the array continued to operate, his Essence-Blood was extracted without pause. Although he had prepared himself for this and knew what to expect, experiencing it for the first time still filled him with horror.

The Spirit Array did not extract his Essence-Blood very quickly, but if this continued for too long, his cultivation would inevitably fall, and he might not even be able to preserve his Third-Tier Lower-Level Dragon Bloodline.

This was utterly unacceptable to him.

He finally understood why the Dragon Descendants in Half-Dragon City generally had lower cultivations. They were being used as nourishment for the Dragon Blood Flowers, so how could their cultivations possibly rise too high?

The Dragon Clan's ruthlessness and heartlessness sent a chill through him.

To keep his cultivation from falling, Li Jiao could only sit cross-legged inside the Spirit Array and circulate his Secret Art. Even so, he could not replenish what he lost as quickly as the array drained him. He felt that he could not endure much longer. In ten days at most, his cultivation would fall to the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm.

If that truly happened, restoring his cultivation afterward would be as difficult as ascending to Heaven.

He constantly swallowed Spiritual Pills and precious medicines and had even used up a great many Upper-Level Source Crystals. One could easily imagine how deeply he resented the Dragon Clan.

He had always yearned for Dragon Isle, wanting to see the place where his bloodline had originated. It had practically become the greatest wish of his life.

Now that his wish had been fulfilled, the outcome was unbearably cruel.

If he could turn back time, he would rather have remained carefree and content in Li Dragon Palace and would never have followed Yang Kai into these muddy waters. He did not even know whether he would leave Dragon Isle alive. Whenever he thought of this, Li Jiao was overwhelmed with grief.

Suddenly, he seemed to notice something and furrowed his brow. When he opened his eyes, a smiling face appeared before him.

"Brother Yang!" Li Jiao was overjoyed. Rising excitedly, he asked, "You've come to save me?"

All the resentment he had harbored toward Yang Kai vanished in that instant, replaced by boundless gratitude and warmth.

Li Jiao suddenly felt that he truly had no self-respect. Yang Kai was clearly the reason he had landed in such a miserable situation, so it was only right that Yang Kai save him. What was there for him to be so grateful about?

"Brother Li, you seem to be doing quite well," Yang Kai said with a chuckle.

Li Jiao's expression turned bitter. "Brother Yang, please stop joking. Hurry and get me out of here before we talk. I don't want to remain in this damned place for even another moment."

A rustling sound came from nearby, and a graceful figure entered Li Jiao's sight. Turning his head, Li Jiao's expression changed drastically, and he shouted in a low voice, "Not good! A member of the Dragon Clan is here. Be careful, Brother Yang!"

Fu Ling walked over with her hands clasped behind her back and curled her lips when she heard him.

"Release him," Yang Kai ordered, turning his head toward her.

Li Jiao could not help staring blankly. He looked at Fu Ling in astonishment, then at Yang Kai, clearly unable to understand what was happening. This member of the Dragon Clan did not appear hostile toward Yang Kai, and the casual way Yang Kai had ordered her around was even more astonishing.

Just what was going on?

Then something even more unimaginable happened. After hearing Yang Kai's words, the Dragon Clan woman actually formed a hand seal and sent a palm strike toward Li Jiao.

In the next instant, the Spirit Array that had been operating all this time abruptly stopped. Li Jiao immediately felt his whole body lighten, and he no longer had to endure the agony of having his Essence-Blood extracted.

"Brother-in-law, you know him?" Fu Ling asked, glancing curiously at Li Jiao.

Yang Kai nodded.

"Brother... Brother-in-law?" Li Jiao's eyes bulged. Leaning close to Yang Kai, he asked in a low voice, "Is this esteemed lady Young Lady Qing's younger sister?"

"It's a long story." Yang Kai did not explain further, and Li Jiao naturally did not press him. He merely said with deep gratitude, "Brother Yang, this Li will remember that you came to save me."

"Saving you was merely incidental. I came here for another important matter."

"What matter? This Li can help as well," Li Jiao volunteered.

"Stealing the Dragon Blood Flowers!"

Li Jiao froze, then began sweating like rain.

"You're this timid?" Fu Ling curled her lips and looked at Li Jiao with disdain.

"Are you really a member of the Dragon Clan?" Li Jiao asked as he wiped his sweat and stared at Fu Ling.

This was far too strange. Even if she was Young Lady Qing's younger sister, surely she would not help Yang Kai steal the Dragon Blood Flowers. If the Dragon Clan learned of such a thing, how could she possibly escape punishment? Li Jiao did not even dare imagine doing it, yet Yang Kai acted as though it were perfectly natural. Stranger still was this Dragon woman's attitude.

Fu Ling sneered. “If I’m not a member of the Dragon Clan, then who is? You? Although I don’t want to steal Dragon Blood Flowers either, since Brother-in-law has given the order, I naturally cannot shirk this duty.”

"Enough nonsense. Hurry and get to work." As Yang Kai spoke, he walked off to one side. A vivid crimson spiritual flower grew there. It was unmistakably a Dragon Blood Flower, and judging by its size and color, it was clearly a Middle-Level one.

He carefully reached out, plucked it, and placed it in his Spatial Ring, his heart surging with excitement. A single Dragon Blood Flower could be refined into at least four Dragon Blood Pills, which would greatly aid his cultivation of the Dragon Transformation Art.

Seeing this, Fu Ling gave an awkward smile. "Brother-in-law, can I just watch?"

Although she had already made a grave mistake and knew no good outcome awaited her, she was still somewhat afraid to personally steal the Dragon Blood Flowers.

Stealing what she was supposed to guard and being forced into helpless compliance were two entirely different things. If the Elders investigated afterward, she would at least have an excuse.

Yang Kai said nothing, so Fu Ling took his silence as agreement. She followed closely beside him but never lifted a hand.

One flower, two flowers, three flowers...

The Dragon Blood Flowers grew far apart from one another. Yang Kai continued deeper into the island, plucking every flower he encountered. In less than the time it took to burn an incense stick, he had collected ten. Most were Lower-Level Dragon Blood Flowers, while only two were Middle-Level. It seemed that even on Dragon Isle, Dragon Blood Flowers of Middle-Level or above were not abundant.

Li Jiao did not dare lift a hand either and simply followed behind Yang Kai. Watching him harvest the flowers with such delight, Li Jiao could not help turning red-eyed with envy.

Back then, he had spent several hundred years cultivating a single Dragon Blood Flower and had treasured it dearly, only for Yang Kai to benefit from it in the end. His heart ached terribly whenever he remembered this.

A single flower had already been enough to affect him so deeply, while this was an entire island devoted to cultivating Dragon Blood Flowers. How could he possibly not be envious?

He was sorely tempted. Plucking even one flower would be enough to improve his bloodline, but no matter what, he could not muster courage as great as Yang Kai's.

Fu Ling felt the same. Although these Dragon Blood Flowers were considered the wealth of the entire Dragon Clan, she had a share in them too. By plundering them like this, Yang Kai was also robbing her of benefits that rightfully belonged to her.

Fortunately, she discovered that Yang Kai had principles. He did not touch any immature Dragon Blood Flowers, harvesting only those that had already matured.

There were certainly more than a few tens of thousands of Dragon Blood Flowers across the entire island.

However, roughly ninety percent of them had yet to mature.

Thus, even if Yang Kai truly swept away every mature Dragon Blood Flower, he would not destroy their foundation. After another dozen to twenty years, many more Dragon Blood Flowers would mature.

Ten flowers, twenty flowers, thirty flowers...

In just one short hour, Yang Kai harvested over a hundred Dragon Blood Flowers.

His original goal had been twenty, but he had long since cast that goal beyond the highest heavens.

Stealing twenty flowers was theft, and stealing a hundred was theft as well. Since he had already begun, there was naturally no need to hold back.

Of the hundred-plus Dragon Blood Flowers, fully eighty percent were Lower-Level, while only a dozen or so were Middle-Level. What thrilled Yang Kai was that he had actually harvested several Upper-Level Dragon Blood Flowers.

The Upper-Level Dragon Blood Flowers were visibly quite different from the Middle-Level and Lower-Level ones, and the medicinal efficacy contained within them was astonishing. If they were refined into Dragon Blood Pills, the pills would certainly be far different from those he had refined before.

## Chapter 3007 — Boiled It

Wait...

An iron pot?

Li Jiao's eyes narrowed as he suddenly recalled something. A long-sealed memory surfaced, and a thought that made his heart tremble with fear sprang into his mind.

"This treasure..." Hua Xing had clearly also recognized the iron pot's origins, and his face filled with horror. "Could it be..."

Before he could finish speaking, a sudden pain shot through his arm. Taking advantage of his distraction, Li Jiao had launched a sneak attack and directly blasted one of his arms into dust.

"Old dog Li, you tricked me!" Hua Xing roared in grief and fury as he hurriedly retreated, putting some distance between himself and Li Jiao. Blood gushed like a fountain from the wound on his arm.

Li Jiao pressed forward in pursuit, showing no mercy with his attacks as he said, "You can eat whatever you like, but you can't say whatever you like. When did this Li ever trick you?"

"Who was it who told this Hua that these two had nothing to do with a Great Emperor?" Hua Xing's face twisted beyond recognition, partly from rage and partly from panic. If he had known that little girl was connected to a Great Emperor, he never would have dared make an enemy of her.

Li Jiao wiped the sweat from his brow and said, “If I told you I had no idea things would turn out like this, would you believe me?”

This was the honest truth. He knew Yang Kai's background thoroughly and knew that Yang Kai had no connection to any Great Emperor. He had assumed the little girl would not have any great background either, but after seeing that pitch-black iron pot instantly collect even a Divine Spirit descendant, he realized he had been wrong.

This little girl was not merely connected to a Great Emperor; her connection ran extremely deep.

Moreover, that Great Emperor was the most belligerent and ruthless of the Ten Great Emperors, an existence even other Great Emperors were unwilling to provoke lightly.

Since the little girl was connected to that great figure, how could anyone dare offend her so lightly?

"Believe you, my ass!" Hua Xing roared, erupting like a volcano. With his Qi and blood assailing his heart, his defenses became riddled with openings, and Li Jiao beat him until he was utterly unable to fight back.

He had originally been able to contend with Li Jiao for a while, but after suffering one upheaval after another, he could no longer maintain his composure. Coupled with the loss of an arm, the gap between them immediately became apparent.

After taking another blow, Hua Xing was sent flying backward, spilling blood all the way.

Before he could steady himself, a colossal figure suddenly descended from the sky, its enormous shadow enveloping him.

Hua Xing was horrified. Looking up, he saw Yang Kai wearing a savage expression as he smashed down with his fist.

He did not dare be careless and hurriedly defended himself, but all his defenses were as fragile as paper before this punch.

The violent force smashed him into the ground, creating a deep crater. Yang Kai pursued him down and planted a foot on his body, rendering Hua Xing completely unable to move. His eyes filled with fear and unease as he cried, "Spare my life!"

Yang Kai looked down at him from above and said lightly, "Whether your life is spared isn't up to me." He raised his head toward Lin Yun'er and called out, "What should we do with this man?"

Lin Yun'er replied, "Master said, if you cut the weeds but don't pull out the roots..."

Before she could finish speaking, power surged from beneath Yang Kai's foot.

Hua Xing fell!

Li Jiao's forehead was covered in cold sweat.

This was a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, the leader of a top Sect, yet Yang Kai had killed him just like that, leaving no room for negotiation. Palace Master Yang was far too fierce. On second thought, however, this was not the first time Yang Kai had done such a thing. Back in the Northern Territory, he had uprooted the entire Wenqing Sect. Compared to that, what did killing a mere Hua Xing amount to?

He turned to look at the chaotic Wind and Cloud Pavilion and sighed inwardly, knowing it was now utterly finished. This great Sect, whose inheritance had endured for ten thousand years, had collapsed overnight, its members scattering like monkeys from a fallen tree. From its Pavilion Master down to the Sect's elite disciples, not one remained; every last one of them had died.

He truly knew how to stir up trouble. Palace Master Yang had turned the Northern Territory upside down, and now that he had arrived in the Western Territory, he showed no restraint whatsoever.

However, after looking at the young girl nearby, Li Jiao knew that even if Yang Kai poked a hole through the Heavens, nothing would happen to him. This young girl... had an extraordinary background.

A series of crackling sounds rang out as Yang Kai returned to his original form.

The Feihong Black Dragon Armor was extremely elastic. Without this precious armor protecting him, he would probably have ended up stark naked again.

"Woof, woof, woof!" The little black dog leisurely trotted over.

No one knew where it had hidden while the fighting turned the world upside down just now, but the moment the dust settled, it immediately appeared. Its ability to seek fortune and avoid disaster was truly first-rate.

Yang Kai shot it an irritated glance but paid it no further attention.

His body was covered in countless wounds, all of them left by the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation. The largest were so deep that bone could be seen, but he ignored them and simply took out a fresh set of clothes and put them on. Glancing sideways at Li Jiao, he said, "Look after that girl and clean up the battlefield while you're at it. I'll be back shortly."

Li Jiao hurriedly replied, "Palace Master Yang can rest assured. This Li will protect that young lady with his life."

Yang Kai nodded, then vanished with a flicker of his figure.

Wind and Cloud Pavilion was in complete chaos as countless disciples scrambled to flee.

The Pavilion Master was dead!

Great Elder was dead!

All the Elders were dead!

Even the elite disciples in the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm had been completely wiped out!

Wind and Cloud Pavilion was doomed. No one was a fool. Several gods of plague had descended upon the Sect, so if they did not flee now, were they supposed to wait around to be killed?

This enormous Sect had several thousand disciples, yet in less than a quarter-hour, seventy to eighty percent of them had fled. Every moment, more people flew away without once looking back.

Yang Kai searched for quite some time before finally finding Wind and Cloud Pavilion's storehouse.

A Sect this large was bound to have good things in its storehouse, so Yang Kai naturally could not pass it up. Though he was now wealthier than a nation, even his fortune could not sustain the expenses of an entire Sect forever. Besides, he wanted to stockpile as many cultivation resources as possible so that when his family and friends from Dark Star arrived, they could spend them to their hearts’ content.

They were not a small number of people.

After working busily for most of the day, he finally returned with a full harvest.

The reserves in the storehouse of a top Sect like Wind and Cloud Pavilion were astonishing, hardly inferior to what Wenqing Sect had possessed. There were nearly one billion Upper-Level Source Crystals alone, to say nothing of the countless other cultivation resources.

These were all indispensable treasures for Lingxiao Palace.

He made another circuit through Wind and Cloud Pavilion and collected anything of value he came across.

He did not return until evening.

Lin Yun'er and Li Jiao had moved to a small mountain peak nearby.

As Yang Kai approached, the first thing he noticed was an enticing aroma. It seemed to be the scent of meat and was brimming with Spiritual Qi. The moment it entered his nose, his appetite surged and he suddenly felt hungry. Could little Yun'er, that glutton, be cooking?

"What are you doing?" Yang Kai walked over and asked curiously, but after taking one look, he was instantly dumbfounded.

Atop the peak, Lin Yun'er had already started a fire and set the pitch-black iron pot over it. Bubbling sounds came from inside, as though something were being boiled.

That enticing aroma was actually drifting out of the iron pot.

Cold sweat covered Yang Kai's brow. She really was cooking? And she was using that iron pot to do it? This, this, this...

Yang Kai felt a little dizzy.

"Palace Master Yang, you're back." Li Jiao eagerly trotted over and handed him a bag of Spatial Rings. "These are the spoils this Li collected while cleaning up the battlefield. Palace Master Yang, please count them."

Whether it was Hua Xing or the Elders of Wind and Cloud Pavilion, they were certain to have stockpiles in their Spatial Rings. This was especially true of Hua Xing. As the Pavilion Master of Wind and Cloud Pavilion, how could his Spatial Ring not contain some good things? To say nothing of the hundred or so elite disciples in the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.

This was an enormous fortune.

Li Jiao had gathered everything without daring to keep anything for himself, and now handed it all to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai casually accepted them without counting the rings or examining their contents. He simply stared at Lin Yun'er, who was crouching in front of the iron pot, and asked with a twitching mouth, "What's going on?"

Lin Yun'er was staring at her iron pot with shining eyes. The corners of her mouth glistened with moisture, and she was clearly on the verge of drooling. After noticing Yang Kai's return, she merely greeted him casually and smacked her lips before focusing all her attention on the iron pot again.

"This young lady probably wanted to make something to eat," Li Jiao replied, bracing himself.

"Make something to eat? With what?" Yang Kai asked casually. His face soon darkened, however, and he turned to Li Jiao. "It couldn't be..."

Li Jiao nodded grimly.

Yang Kai paled in shock. "You can do that?"

Li Jiao hesitated. "Palace Master Yang doesn't know the origins of this rare treasure?"

"You do?"

Li Jiao replied, “I have heard of it before. If I am not mistaken, this pot is called the Unity Cauldron.”

"Unity Cauldron..." Yang Kai frowned. He had truly never heard that name before, but there was only one thing he cared about now. He walked over and called softly, "Little Yun'er."

Without raising her head, Lin Yun'er replied, "It's almost ready. Just wait a little longer, Uncle Yang."

Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his brow. "You really boiled that Fire Qilin?"

"Of course. It's a Divine Spirit descendant, and I've never eaten one before. It must be delicious."

Li Jiao shuddered from head to toe when he heard this.

He was also a Divine Spirit descendant, and a Dragon descendant at that. What if this girl put him into her pot and boiled him one day? In an instant, the look he gave Lin Yun'er became filled with alarm and dread, as though he were looking at his natural enemy.

Sure enough, nothing good ever came from staying with Palace Master Yang.

The Dragon Clan was one thing; at worst, they would merely suppress his bloodline. This little girl, however, was far too terrifying. Wielding the Unity Cauldron, she had captured a Divine Spirit descendant and immediately started boiling it... Li Jiao felt his heart could not take much more of this. If he stayed with Yang Kai any longer, he would sooner or later be frightened to death. Cherish life and stay far away from Yang Kai!

Yang Kai's face turned green. "Little Yun'er, you need to think this through. You can't just eat anything you want. That thing swallowed quite a few Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples earlier." At this point, his face could not help turning pale.

If it had only been a Divine Spirit descendant, then so be it; boiling it would have been fine. It smelled so fragrant that it certainly could not taste bad. The problem was that Yang Kai had personally watched the Fire Qilin eat quite a few Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples earlier.

They were all in the pot now. At the thought of that, how could he possibly have any appetite? It was already good enough that he did not vomit.

Lin Yun'er waved her hand with a grin. "Don't worry, Uncle Yang. I took care of all that."

Yang Kai was stunned and turned to look at Li Jiao.

Li Jiao nodded. "She did indeed take care of it. I also put all the longsword Secret Treasures this young lady collected earlier into a ring."

Evidently, while Yang Kai was away, Lin Yun'er had cleaned and prepared the Fire Qilin.

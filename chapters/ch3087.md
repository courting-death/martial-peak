## Chapter 3087 — I Heard You Can Bed a Hundred Women in One Night?

Lu An was the clan uncle who had met her eyes, then looked away.

The situation was obvious. Lu Huaishuang was not blind; she had already seen through the truth without needing to ask. Earlier, she had not had time to deal with this traitor, but now that she was free, she naturally could not allow this black sheep to remain.

The Lu Family had no need for anyone with divided loyalties.

Lu Bing was startled. "Him? That can't be, can it?"

By clan reckoning, he and Lu An were cousins, and just a month ago they had been drinking and chatting together. No matter what, he could not believe Lu An would actually betray the Lu Family. But he knew Lu Huaishuang would not make such a claim without reason. Since she had said this, she must have conclusive proof. His expression sharpened. "I'll go take a look."

He turned and went out the door.

A short while later, he returned with an ugly expression. "Lu An ran!"

He had searched the entire Warship and found no trace of Lu An. After asking the Lu Family cultivators, he learned that no one had seen him for quite some time either.

"As expected." Lu Huaishuang was not surprised in the least. If she had been Lu An, she certainly would have run too. Traitors were always more hateful than enemies. If he had stayed, his end would have been miserable beyond measure.

Inwardly, she let out a faint breath of relief. If Lu An had not run, she truly would not have known how to handle him. Kill the chicken to warn the monkeys? No matter what, that man was still her elder, her clan uncle. If she really took action, would it chill some people's hearts and make them think she was ruthless and heartless?

Since he had run, it saved her a troublesome matter. Just like those few fish from the He Family who had slipped through the net, on this Dead Star without the slightest bit of Spiritual Qi, even if they managed to drag out an ignoble existence, they probably would not live very comfortably. So Lu Huaishuang had no intention of pursuing him. Leaving him to fend for himself was the greatest punishment for him.

"How could he betray the Lu Family?" Lu Bing was bitterly grieved.

"Either for profit, or because someone had leverage over him," Lu Huaishuang said lightly, waving her hand. "Enough. Don't spread this matter outside, lest it cause unnecessary suspicion."

"Yes." Lu Bing nodded. "But Lu An still has family on Morning Sun Star. After we return, if those people ask about him, how should we explain it?"

"Third Uncle, you'll have to think of a story." Lu Huaishuang looked at him.

Lu Bing nodded. "Good. Leave this matter to me." After a pause, he added, "Right, about that Warship from the He Family..."

Lu Huaishuang abruptly raised her head. "Before receiving that person's permission, absolutely do not get any ideas about that Warship." Although the He Family members had been wiped out by their hands, and that Void King-Grade Warship should, by rights, be the Lu Family's spoils of war, in the final accounting, most of the credit belonged to Yang Kai.

That Void King-Grade Warship had been invisibly marked with the surname Yang.

Lu Bing said, "Naturally, I know that. These past days, I've also restrained the clansmen from boarding that Warship. Still, from what I can see, that person may not necessarily care about a mere Warship. Could you perhaps go... ask for his opinion?"

Although the Lu Family was a local hegemon on Morning Sun Star, they only had one Void King-Grade Warship. If they could obtain another one out of thin air, their strength would surely rise by a level. A Void King-Grade Warship was the greatest weapon for attacking cities and seizing territory. If they wanted to move against the He Family remnants after returning to Morning Sun Star, the deterrence of a Warship would be indispensable.

Lu Huaishuang rubbed her temples. Thinking of that person made her remember how he had molested her on the way down the mountain, and she could still recall the sensation on her chest with perfect clarity. Her expression turned slightly unnatural.

He had said he did not do it on purpose. Heh...

"Have you found out that person's origins?" Lu Bing asked again.

Lu Huaishuang shook her head. "I only know he isn't from the Wuji Star Domain."

Lu Bing's whole body shook. "He isn't from the Wuji Star Domain?" As something suddenly occurred to him, he cried out softly, "Could it be..."

Lu Huaishuang nodded. "I now suspect he comes from that place too."

"No wonder." Lu Bing looked thoughtful. "Even Gale's First Chief is willing to serve as his follower. If he is from that place, then it would not be beneath Sheng Yao either. Sheng Yao must have seen it as well, right?"

Lu Huaishuang smiled faintly. "A person like him is best at seeking fortune and avoiding disaster. If there were no benefits, how could he willingly trail along at someone else's heels?"

Lu Bing said solemnly, "Then our Lu Family must build a good relationship with him. We can't let Sheng Yao seize the initiative. Mm, it would be best to cater to his preferences. We just don't know what this person likes. Still, what men pursue is usually wealth, women, authority, and influence. Perhaps we can start from there. Among the women who came out with our Lu Family this time, there are a few who are quite outstanding."

"I understand. Let me think about it." Lu Huaishuang nodded.

Her words clearly meant to send him away. Lu Bing said, "Then I'll go out and busy myself first. Notify me if there is any news."

Sitting in her chair, Lu Huaishuang's expression shifted again and again. After thinking for a long time without finding any clue, she finally decided to meet that person first.

Regardless of whether she could build a good relationship with him, making herself a familiar face was the main thing. With that fellow Sheng Yao clinging to his side, if Sheng Yao really seized the initiative, the Lu Family would probably have no part in this at all.

This was a Heaven-sent opportunity, and Lu Huaishuang did not want to miss it.

...

Light footsteps came from the passageway, and Lu Huaishuang's figure slowly appeared.

When she reached the front of her own boudoir, Lu Huaishuang was startled. She stared ahead and shouted in a low voice, "Who's there?"

At the doorway to the room, there was actually a woman standing there. Her figure was astonishingly voluptuous, graceful and curvaceous, her full chest swelling high as if about to burst from her clothes. A long, pale-purple dress fit her perfectly, and an equally purple silk sash was tied around her waist, outlining that slender waist to perfection. Behind her, her lower back curved inward in a clear arc before her hips swept sharply upward, a sight shocking enough to steal the breath.

Her black hair was lustrous and smooth, draped over her shoulders, and her peach blossom eyes were bright and moist, as if they could speak.

Even though Lu Huaishuang was also a woman, looking at her made Lu Huaishuang's heart stir a little.

This woman was simply an enchantress!

Of course, it would have been even better if she had not been giving off the aura of the Second Stage of the Void King Realm.

Lu Huaishuang was in no mood to admire her at the moment. No matter who it was, suddenly discovering such a completely unfamiliar expert inside one's own Warship would make anyone feel as if they faced a great enemy.

A flash of azure light bloomed. Lu Huaishuang pointed her sword at the other party, her face icy. Although her cultivation was weaker, this was her Warship. If they truly fought, it was still uncertain who would prove stronger.

To her surprise, faced with such open hostility, that woman remained completely unmoved. In one hand, she held a rouge box and was carefully applying makeup in its little mirror, her expression meticulous.

Suddenly, her hand paused, and she said irritably, "It's been so many years since I've done this that I'm not used to it anymore. Little Sister Shuang, can you help me draw on some eye shadow?"

Little... Sister Shuang?

Lu Huaishuang frowned. "Do we know each other?"

She was certain she had never seen this mature, charming woman who looked plump and succulent at first glance. The way the other party addressed her naturally left her suspicious.

The woman pursed her lips and smiled. "We only parted not long ago. How can you not recognize me?"

"What are you talking..." Lu Huaishuang was somewhat impatient, but in the next instant, she stared at the woman in utter shock as a possibility occurred to her.

The woman suddenly changed her voice. "How about now?"

"You're Sheng Yao?" Lu Huaishuang could hardly believe her eyes. Her small mouth fell open as if someone had stuffed two invisible fingers into it.

The famous First Chief of Gale, known throughout the entire Wuji Star Domain, had transformed with a shake of the body into a fair-skinned beauty as lovely as peach blossoms. In that instant, Lu Huaishuang felt as if her entire view of the world had been shattered, and her thoughts came to a halt.

"Ge ge ge..." The First Chief let out a string of delicate laughter, her chest trembling like flowers in the wind until her clothes nearly burst open.

Lu Huaishuang still could not quite believe it, but at this time and in this place, who else but Gale's First Chief could appear here and possess the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Void King Realm?

"How did you..." Lu Huaishuang simply did not know what to say. At this moment, every possible feeling tangled together inside her.

The First Chief's smile faded, and a somewhat sorrowful expression appeared on her face. "We're both women, and both of us stand in high positions. I imagine you can understand the reason, can't you?"

Lu Huaishuang froze, then slowly understood.

When she took over the Lu Family, doubts arose from every corner. It was not because her cultivation was lacking—she was in the Void King Realm, and no one in the entire Lu Family surpassed her in strength—but simply because she was a woman.

Only after fighting several hard battles, personally destroying countless enemies, and stabilizing the Lu Family's wavering, uncertain situation had she established herself as the Lu Family's helmsman.

But the ease and freedom she once had were gone. At every moment, she had to watch her words and actions.

It was as if, in this world, men always had one more advantage than women.

On what basis? All men under Heaven were born from women's bodies, so on what basis did they look down on women? Men and women were both people, so why should one be low and the other noble?

If even the Lu Family was like this, how much more so for a Star Pirate gang like Gale, which gathered every kind of vicious, savage, and ruthless scoundrel in the Star Domain?

The First Chief said, "If those brats learned that I am a woman, and such a beautiful woman at that, do you think I could still rule them? Just those sworn brothers of mine would probably want to eat me alive."

Lu Huaishuang nodded in deep agreement.

Ma Chaoqun and Ge Ming could drink wine, eat meat, and play with women together with Sheng Yao, but only if Sheng Yao was a thickset man just like them. If Sheng Yao returned to a woman's body, it would be hard to say Ma Chaoqun and Ge Ming would not have crooked thoughts.

For no clear reason, Lu Huaishuang actually felt a sense of kinship with her, as one who understood the other's suffering.

Their situations were, to a certain extent, very similar.

Both were extremely beautiful women, both held high positions, and both carried all kinds of restraints.

"Sheng Yao isn't your name!" Lu Huaishuang said. No woman would use such a masculine name.

The First Chief pursed her lips in a charming smile. "Just an alias I chose at random."

"What should I call you from now on?"

"He Yunxiang." The First Chief pursed her lips and smiled. "I was born to a poor family, so the name isn't very pleasant to hear."

Lu Huaishuang nodded lightly, then frowned again, her expression strange. "I heard you can bed a hundred women in a single night?"

Since she was a woman, how could she do such a thing? But no wind came from an empty cave. Since such rumors existed, it must truly have happened.

He Yunxiang covered her mouth and laughed delicately. "Little Sister Shuang, you really love gossip." Her eyes flashed. "If you want to know, you can come find me and try it sometime when you're free."

Lu Huaishuang's expression immediately turned solemn, and she said somewhat unnaturally, "No need."

She secretly regretted it. How had she thought to ask such a question? Her head must have been full of water.

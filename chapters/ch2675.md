## Chapter 2675 — Nothing More Than This

In the air, two figures sped past one after the other, like thunderbolts.

Yang Kai led the way ahead, his fists gradually healing under his formidable recovery abilities, his heart secretly shocked by the girl's sheer brute strength. He had no idea where this lass had come from, and when he swept his Divine Sense across, he found her casually walking with hands clasped behind her back, following unhurriedly in his footsteps, never falling an inch behind, her expression utterly serene.

Her expression seemed as if she was out for a stroll, not here to fight him.

Yang Kai was so amused he almost laughed out loud. He took a gentle breath, his irritation and anger gradually subsiding, his expression becoming as calm as still water. Although the other party looked young and was quite unreasonable, she was indeed formidable.

Though they had no prior grievance and this was their first meeting, since the other party harbored killing intent, Yang Kai would not show weakness either.

If you want to kill me, then I'll kill you. Let's see who dies first.

Leaping tens of thousands of li, they left Flowing Shadow City far behind and rushed into a stretch of continuous mountains.

Yang Kai suddenly stopped, turning around.

The girl's figure froze thirty zhang ahead of him, glanced around, and parted her pink lips: "Is this the burial ground you've chosen for yourself? Your taste isn't very impressive."

Yang Kai laughed loudly, "Ha! Little brat, just because you have some brute strength, you think you're so impressive? You really don't know how vast the heavens are or how deep the earth is!"

Usually, others spoke to him like this, but it was the first time he'd mocked someone else. Yang Kai felt a strange sense of being the villain, and couldn't help but feel awkward. Generally, those who spoke to him this way never ended well.

The girl sneered, "Well, it doesn't matter. You won't have the chance to use them anyway. Since you dared to refine those things, I'll dismantle you bone by bone, leaving nothing for burial!"

As she spoke, she actually stuck out her crimson little tongue and licked her red lips, looking as if she had spotted a delicious treat, her fingers itching. Her pure appearance suddenly gained a seductive charm. Yang Kai was momentarily stunned, unable to help but imagine what it would feel like if that little tongue licked him, his mind wavering.

Damn it, with a deadly enemy before him, how could he be distracted by beauty? Was it because he'd kept himself pure all these years in the Star Realm without release? Yang Kai urged his cultivation, and his mind instantly cleared.

"Are you ready?" The girl looked up, smiling innocently.

Yang Kai laughed, beckoning: "Come! Today I'll teach you a lesson for your elders... pu..."

Before he finished, Yang Kai felt a huge force slam into his abdomen. Thirty zhang away, the girl was nowhere to be seen; only a faint afterimage remained. Her small body sank, and she punched forward.

It was as if an Emperor Realm expert self-destructed right in front of him. Yang Kai's stomach cramped, and he spat out a mouthful of blood, splattering the girl from head to toe. He himself flew backward like a cannonball, smashing a mountain peak into collapse.

Rumble...

Rocks tumbled and rolled as the hundred zhang high mountain slowly collapsed in a cascading torrent. Only a massive cavern appeared at the mountainside, while Yang Kai's figure vanished without a trace.

The girl stood with her fist withdrawn, sneering at the hole: "Overestimating yourself."

With her strength, even a Third Stage Emperor would suffer from such a blow. Yet a mere First Stage Emperor dared to act up in front of her—he really didn't know the meaning of death.

On this trip, she had come to the Southern Territory under the orders of the clan elders to investigate the whereabouts of a clan treasure. She had traveled to many places without finding any clues, but when passing through Flowing Shadow City, she suddenly discovered something odd about Yang Kai.

“If it were just some ordinary Emperor Realm cultivator, she might have overlooked it. But the key was that Yang Kai had actually refined things that should never be touched. As a disciple of her clan, she naturally had both the duty and the obligation to wipe him out.”

It was laughable how arrogant this human seemed to be, daring to face her in single combat without even understanding her true identity. One could admire his courage, but his intellect was clearly lacking.

She stretched out her pink little hand and wiped her face. Seeing a bit of blood, she frowned: "Golden..."

It was the first time she'd encountered a human with golden blood, and the Qi and blood power contained within even moved her.

She stuck out her pink tongue and licked the Golden Blood, her expression shifting subtly: "Demonic nature… yet also Spiritual. How strange."

From this golden blood, she tasted aura that she both hated and felt close to.

Yang Kai's golden blood originated from the Demon God's Secret Codex he obtained years ago. By refining his blood with demonic arts, it became pure golden blood, naturally full of demonic nature. But he also refined the Origin Power of the Golden Sacred Dragon, giving his blood strong recovery abilities and a pure spiritual nature.

The two combined perfectly, blending together.

By all accounts, the demonic nature refined from the Demon God's Secret Codex should have been unable to withstand the spirituality of the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin Power. However, Yang Kai's body now harbored a seal containing pure ancient Demonic Qi. Though sealed away, it had subtly influenced his physique through several unsealings, allowing it to achieve perfect balance with the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin Power.

While the girl was puzzled, a muffled thunderous explosion came from the hole in the mountain's belly. A stream of light shot out, forming Yang Kai's figure, who stood before her once more.

"You..." The girl was startled, looking ahead in surprise. She had thought that after taking her punch, Yang Kai would be either dead or seriously injured, but he still had the ability to move. Not only that, he seemed even more spirited, his fighting will soaring, his Emperor Yuan roaring, and his eyes shining with terrifying brilliance.

"That hurts!" Yang Kai said, but his face showed no pain, only fierce determination. He gritted his teeth: "Little brat, I underestimated you. It's only fair to return the favor—take this old man's punch!"

As he spoke, his aura sank. He was like a volcano about to erupt, gathering all his strength into his fist. Under the surging Laws of Space, he flashed to the girl's side and punched fiercely at her chest.

If this punch landed, her full, tender chest would burst instantly. It wasn't that Yang Kai wanted to take advantage, but the girl's petite figure meant his punch naturally reached her chest, just as she had chosen his abdomen as her target earlier. It wasn't intentional, just natural.

With a deadly enemy before him, who cared about beauty? Even if a stunning beauty stripped naked and danced seductively in front of him, Yang Kai would still punch without hesitation.

Multicolored radiance bloomed, the Undying Five Elements Sword Qi wrapped around his fist, further enhancing the power of this strike.

For some reason, Yang Kai felt utterly exhilarated, an indescribable joy in his heart. It seemed that this simple, brutal battle best suited a man's savage instincts. Secret techniques and treasures were simply too weak.

A vortex of power appeared, the void branded with marks. What he threw out was no longer just a fist, but the culmination of his lifelong Martial Dao insights, the condensation of his essence, energy, and spirit.

The girl's eyes narrowed, clearly surprised. She hadn't expected that a mere First Stage of the Emperor Realm human could possess such wild power. Combined with the vigorous Qi and blood of his golden blood, she secretly thought Yang Kai must have cultivated some body-refining art, or else he couldn't have reached this level.

She sneered inwardly. So young, yet daring to call himself an old man in front of her. Though she looked young, her growth cycle was long—she had actually lived for hundreds of years.

"You'll regret not dying easily just now." The girl's red lips parted as she stood unmoving, only raising her delicate palm to block Yang Kai's all-out punch. That exquisite hand seemed like a work of art standing against a raging storm, about to shatter in the next moment.

Pa...

The fist and palm collided, yet the girl remained motionless, her clothes merely fluttering in response. Yang Kai's confident strike, against all reason, was simply blocked, as if she were swatting away a mindless fly rather than withstanding a devastating blow.

A wild wind rose, the girl's black hair flew, her beautiful eyes mocking.

"Damn it!" Even Yang Kai, who had weathered countless storms, couldn't help but widen his eyes in this critical moment. The conflict between his ideals and reality was far too jarring, leaving him somewhat unable to accept it.

He knew the girl's strength was immense, but never imagined it was to such a degree—an unattainable height.

"My turn!" The girl smiled, her silver-bell laughter scattering everywhere. Yet in Yang Kai’s ears it was nothing but chilling. The hand that blocked his fist suddenly gripped, soft and warm as jade, and Yang Kai instantly felt his fist locked tight, unable to break free.

Next, the girl's other arm bent back like a drawn bow, her figure leaning back, her full chest rising even higher, the pale blue dress almost unable to contain it.

Boom...

The bow was drawn, the arrow shot, her pink fist struck, wrapped in a destructive aura.

Yang Kai's eyes widened, his free fist also punched out like a dragon, forming countless fist shadows.

Explosions sounded one after another, accompanied by Yang Kai's muffled groans.

Hiss...

Yang Kai stepped back, the Undying Five Elements Sword Qi shattered, his attacking fist nearly twisted, flesh mangled, while the other fist bore deep five-finger marks, flesh torn away to reveal white bone.

His Qi and blood churned violently in his chest, and Yang Kai couldn't help but spray out another mist of blood. Yet, unwilling to lose face, he tilted his head back with a sneer: “Is that all?”

"How amusing!" A cold smirk played across the girl's lips. Humans truly were pathetic creatures—facing death itself, yet still refusing to repent, their words contradicting their hearts. Surely this one must be regretting everything to death right now, trembling in fear? Who knows, perhaps he'd soon be on his knees begging for mercy.

Maybe he'd turn and run any moment. After all, knowing he couldn't win but still resisting—no one could be that foolish.

"Again!" Yang Kai roared, gathering his strength once more. Without waiting for the girl's reply, he dashed at her.

"He actually... charged over!" The girl's heart skipped a beat. Things weren't going as she'd expected. Had this guy been beaten stupid?

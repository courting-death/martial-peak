## Chapter 2762 — Sister Hua, You’re So Devious

Gu Hong chased all the way, not stopping for a moment, exhausted and panting heavily.

He only possessed the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm cultivation, which was far inferior to Le Dongzheng. Naturally, his speed was lacking. If he hadn’t already known the secret path Yang Kai took, he wouldn’t even know which direction to pursue.

Upon arrival, Gu Hong immediately hid himself in a thicket, suppressing his aura and scanning the surroundings.

No sounds of fighting or energy fluctuations could be heard. Could it be that Le Dongzheng dealt with that brat so quickly?

That shouldn’t be. He was right behind them. Even if Le Dongzheng was strong, that brat was an Emperor Realm cultivator and should be able to hold out for a while.

Maybe the battlefield shifted.

Gu Hong thought this was likely. Perhaps the two were evenly matched, locked in a fierce struggle, and now fighting somewhere else. Ideally, they’d both be wounded, letting him profit as the fisherman. If the opportunity arose, he wouldn’t mind double-crossing them and taking out both Yang Kai and Le Dongzheng.

As Gu Hong fantasized, a faint scent of blood drifted to his nose on the wind.

He sniffed lightly, his expression changing.

Raising his head to look again, he saw that under the moonlight, about a hundred zhang away, someone was lying sprawled on the ground.

“Is that—” Gu Hong’s face tightened, focusing his gaze.

The person lying there had their head turned just enough to face his direction, letting him see their face clearly.

“Le Dongzheng!” Gu Hong was startled, a chill running down his spine.

The Sect Master of Duanyue Sect, who had left the Ziyuan Commerce Guild auction just ahead of him, was now lying there covered in blood, motionless, his fate unknown. Most likely dead, since Gu Hong couldn’t sense any aura.

His blood nearly froze in his veins. He stood there, rigid and unmoving for a long while, not daring to make the slightest move.

Just a moment ago, he’d been full of life, vowing to take revenge on that brat from Room Three. How had he ended up like a dead fish in the blink of an eye?

Gu Hong didn’t dare move for ages, afraid a powerful enemy was still lurking nearby. Any movement might bring disaster.

After waiting a full hour with no sign of trouble, Gu Hong finally summoned his courage, cautiously approaching Le Dongzheng.

Soon, standing before Le Dongzheng’s corpse, Gu Hong looked at his tragic state, his face grim.

One strike!

Le Dongzheng had been killed in a single blow. There was a clear five-finger imprint on his chest, which had completely caved in. Blood still lingered in his mouth and nose, and his wide-open eyes were filled with disbelief, as if he’d seen something unimaginable before dying.

Gulp.

Gu Hong swallowed hard. To him, Le Dongzheng was a lofty expert. To be killed in one move—just how strong was the killer?

Whether or not it was that brat from Room Three, it was related to him. Fenglin City was no longer safe. If that brat found out he’d leaked his whereabouts, his own fate would be grim.

Shivering, Gu Hong glanced at Le Dongzheng’s hand. No sign of a Spatial Ring—likely taken by the killer.

Not daring to linger, he activated his Origin Qi, turning into a stream of light and quickly fleeing the scene.

The speed of over a hundred people was slower than Yang Kai expected. With frequent stops, it took ten days to cover half the distance.

Fortunately, they encountered no danger. Normally, such a group in the Star Realm would easily attract malicious cultivators, but with Yang Kai present, any would-be predators ended up dead.

Suddenly leaving the land where generations had lived, both the Zhang Family and Qin Family felt a sense of loss, especially the elders.

The younger ones, however, were full of curiosity and excitement about the outside world, especially Ruoyu, who constantly pestered Yang Kai with questions, finding joy in every strange tale.

This girl’s personality was completely different from Ruoxi—lively and energetic, always full of questions.

Yang Kai showed no impatience. He needed to introduce the Northern Territory and his Sect to the Zhang and Qin families, so he answered Ruoyu’s questions, gradually inspiring everyone’s longing for the Northern Territory and Lingxiao Palace.

After more than twenty days, the group finally arrived at the old site of Thousand Leaves Sect.

He led the two families into Emperor Heaven Valley’s small world, activating the Space Array to teleport everyone in batches to Lingxiao Palace.

Yang Kai brought up the rear. When he and the last group arrived, Hua Qingsi immediately came forward: “Palace Master!”

When the first batch of cultivators arrived, two Thousand Leaves Sect disciples guarding the place immediately notified Hua Qingsi, the chief manager. She hurried over to investigate.

After some questioning, she learned that Yang Kai had arranged their arrival, so she treated them with proper respect.

Yang Kai nodded, glanced at the two families’ cultivators, and smiled.

At this moment, all hundred-plus people wore expressions of disbelief, their eyes full of shock and joy. Ruoyu spread her arms wide, her face radiant, as if she wanted to embrace the whole world.

Lingxiao Palace was originally the foundation of Wenqing Sect. With its mountains and rivers, and rich Spiritual Qi, it was far superior to Fenglin City. Arriving in such a paradise, everyone felt reborn. If not for restraint, they would have shouted in excitement.

“Zhu Qing didn’t come, did she?” Yang Kai asked Hua Qingsi cautiously, scanning the area.

“No,” Hua Qingsi shook her head.

“Good.” Yang Kai breathed a sigh of relief. He knew Zhu Qing couldn’t arrive so soon—she was headed to the Frozen Earth to investigate her clansmen’s deaths. Still, better safe than sorry. If Zhu Qing did show up, she’d cling to him like glue again.

“Palace Master, how should these two families be arranged?” Hua Qingsi asked.

Yang Kai replied, “You’re the chief manager, so handle it as you see fit. No need for special treatment. Pick some talented people to become the first batch of Lingxiao Palace disciples and focus on training them.”

“Understood.” Hua Qingsi nodded.

With Yang Kai’s instructions, she felt confident.

“Go make arrangements. I have something else to discuss with you,” Yang Kai waved.

“Yes!” Hua Qingsi responded, then flew into the air, calling out, “Since you’ve entered Lingxiao Palace, you are now its people. Follow me.”

As an Emperor Realm cultivator, her words immediately commanded respect, and the two families followed her.

In the blink of an eye, the hundred-plus people were gone.

Yang Kai flickered and arrived at his own residence.

Half a day later, Hua Qingsi came to report. The two families had been settled on a mountain with excellent Spiritual Qi and existing buildings, enough for them to live comfortably. She also left them cultivation resources and told them she’d check their progress in a year, granting different ranks based on their advancement.

The arrangement was flawless. The two families wouldn’t complain. In Fenglin City, they’d always lived in fear, but now Lingxiao Palace provided a safe home and a great cultivation environment, so they were naturally excited.

“Palace Master, you said you had something to discuss. What is it?” Hua Qingsi asked after reporting.

“I went to the Southern Territory and made a deal with Ziyuan Commerce Guild,” Yang Kai explained briefly.

Hua Qingsi asked, “And then?”

Yang Kai said, “Then I thought, shouldn’t Lingxiao Palace establish a long-term partnership with Ziyuan Commerce Guild? We're in the Northern Territory, they’re in the Southern Territory. The two regions have different products—some things are abundant and cheap in the north but rare and expensive in the south, and vice versa. If we could exchange—”

“Arbitrage!” Hua Qingsi immediately understood.

In the Star Realm, many merchants did this, but they had to cross countless dangers between territories, risking everything for profit.

Lingxiao Palace was different. With a cross-territory Space Array, they could travel between territories easily, enjoying a natural advantage. This business would be pure profit.

Hua Qingsi smiled, “Even if you hadn’t mentioned it, I’d thought about it too.”

“Oh? What ideas do you have?” Yang Kai asked.

Hua Qingsi replied seriously, “It’s a great idea and definitely feasible, but there’s one difficulty. Have you considered it?”

“Please, enlighten me,” Yang Kai said humbly.

“Whether we have enough manpower is another matter. Palace Master, you know the situation in Lingxiao Palace—there aren’t many true disciples. If we really want to do this, we’ll need a lot of people. But compared to that, the real problem is sourcing the goods—unless…”

“Unless what?”

Hua Qingsi laughed, “Unless Palace Master asks others to help collect materials.”

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and said with a sly smile, “For example, Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect! Sister Hua, you’re so devious.”

Hua Qingsi pouted, “Anyway, those two Sects owe us so many Source Crystals. Letting them repay with materials is already generous.”

“Exactly.” Yang Kai nodded with a smile. “By the way, we should involve Ice Heart Valley. Unlike us, Ice Heart Valley is well-established and powerful. It should be easy for them to gather materials from the Northern Territory.”

“Alright.”

## Chapter 2747 — Forced to Buy and Sell

Twenty million Middle-Level Source Crystals—equivalent to just two hundred thousand Upper-Level Source Crystals—was a trivial sum for Yang Kai, barely a drop in the bucket. But for Bian Yuqing, it represented her entire fortune.

It was all thanks to her managing the Biyu Sect over the years, holding the cultivation resources of a whole Sect in her hands. Otherwise, she wouldn’t have had so much wealth.

The item bought for twenty million soared to thirty-six million in the blink of an eye, and that wasn’t even the limit. It seemed Bian Yuqing’s claim that it could be resold for double was no exaggeration. The demand for this Command Token among the martial artists of Linghu City was obvious.

“Forty million!” In midair, a martial artist at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm shouted the price, his face flushed. He looked as if he was in agony, but with this Qing Jade Command Token, he could attempt to break through to the Emperor Realm in two months. If he succeeded, what would forty million Middle-Level Source Crystals matter?

The appearance of Linghu Palace had already allowed many martial artists to break through their shackles. In just these years since the Emperor ascensions began, dozens had succeeded, nearly ten each year. If others could do it, why couldn’t he?

“If anyone can offer a higher price, I’ll give up the Command Token.” The martial artist at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm gritted his teeth, his gaze anxious as he looked around.

To his delight, after he called out that price, no one else bid. It made sense—forty million was twice the price of buying a Command Token from Linghu Palace. That was basically the limit. No one was foolish; even though this token’s serial number was very high, everyone’s Source Crystals didn’t grow on trees. How could they squander them so easily?

“Forty million and ten thousand!” an aged voice suddenly rang out from not far away.

Everyone showed strange expressions. Raising the price by just ten thousand—was he trying to get a bargain or did he have a grudge against the martial artist who bid forty million, deliberately trying to mess with him?

“Who? Who dares?” The martial artist at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm was furious, his anger boiling like a volcano about to erupt.

Although Yang Kai hadn’t specified the minimum increment, everyone had been following the rules during the auction, raising by at least five hundred thousand each time, usually a million. Now someone suddenly raised by just ten thousand—wasn’t that just causing trouble?

“It’s this old man. What, you have a problem?”

A figure flashed, and an old man suddenly appeared, flying above everyone’s heads and looking down. As he spoke, he surged his power, and a wave of Emperor Intent swept out.

“Emperor Realm!” The martial artist, who had been scanning the crowd for troublemakers, suddenly froze, his expression changing as he fell silent.

An Emperor was actually competing for the Qing Jade Command Token. He hadn’t expected that. Although the token could grant access to an Earth-Grade cultivation ground and was useful for Emperors, they usually went to Heaven-Grade grounds. Of course, it was possible this old man was bidding for his disciples or descendants.

“It’s Duanyue Sect’s Sect Master, Le Dongzheng!”

Someone in the crowd suddenly called out, seemingly recognizing the old man.

The others all showed wary expressions upon hearing this.

Bian Yuqing trembled slightly, instinctively feeling Le Dongzheng was here to cause trouble. But thinking carefully, no one had seen her and Yang Kai kill those three Duanyue Sect disciples, so she relaxed.

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes, looking at Le Dongzheng, his face thoughtful.

Not only did this old man suddenly pop up and raise the price by ten thousand, which seemed like trouble, but Yang Kai had just killed Duanyue Sect disciples, and now the Sect Master appeared. It was too much of a coincidence.

Could the matter have been exposed? No, when he killed them, his Divine Sense was always scanning the surroundings. No one was spying, and the three didn’t send any messages before dying. How could Le Dongzheng know?

“So it’s Lord Le.” The martial artist at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm recognized Le Dongzheng and smiled bitterly. “Since Lord Le wants the Qing Jade Command Token, I dare not object.”

“No objections is best!” Le Dongzheng snorted, then turned to stare at Yang Kai, reaching out. “Hand over the Command Token.”

Yang Kai stared at him, sneering. “The auction isn’t over yet.”

Le Dongzheng said, “What, is there anyone who can outbid this old man?”

His imposing gaze swept the crowd. Anyone he looked at felt a chill in their heart. Even those who wanted to bid didn’t dare speak, and besides, forty million was already near the limit.

“See, this old man’s price is the highest!” Le Dongzheng reached out again. “Hand over the Command Token.”

Yang Kai rubbed his chin and shook his head. “Even if your price is highest, I won’t sell you the Command Token.”

Did he really think he could snatch the Command Token by just raising the bid by ten thousand? If Yang Kai handed it over, it would leave a bitter taste for a long time. Besides, this Command Token was the very thing those three Duanyue Sect martial artists had tried to seize—Kou Wu had even been killed for it. How could Yang Kai let it fall into Duanyue Sect’s hands?

Le Dongzheng’s face darkened, and he shouted, “Boy, you dare break the rules?”

Yang Kai sneered, “My item, my rules. Who do you think you are?”

At these words, everyone stared at Yang Kai in shock, secretly admiring his courage. Le Dongzheng was an Emperor, yet this young man dared provoke him.

“Courting death!” Le Dongzheng was furious, his aura surging as he pressed down on Yang Kai.

Many nearby groaned and retreated together.

Yang Kai stood firm, unmoved, his lips curling into a cold smile as he gazed at Le Dongzheng. He exhaled softly, and the aura of an Emperor Realm cultivator—like a slumbering beast—began to awaken, slowly rising and spreading.

Le Dongzheng’s expression changed. “You’re actually an Emperor!”

The crowd was also shocked. No one expected Yang Kai to have Emperor Realm cultivation. They were all relieved they’d followed the rules during the auction and hadn’t offended him, or they’d be in trouble now.

“Looks like this old man misjudged.” Le Dongzheng’s expression turned cold.

Yang Kai ignored him and pointed ahead. “You there, come here!”

Where he pointed, everyone quickly moved aside, as if his finger was a venomous snake.

The martial artist who had bid forty million tried to dodge, but Yang Kai’s finger kept pointing at him.

His face turned bitter, and he asked, “Sir, what do you need?”

Though older than Yang Kai, his cultivation was lower, so he could only address him as ‘Sir’.

“Come here!” Yang Kai repeated.

The man had no choice but to walk over, anxious, not knowing what Yang Kai wanted.

As he approached, before he could speak, Yang Kai shoved the Qing Jade Command Token into his hand. “I’m selling you the Command Token.”

Instantly, Le Dongzheng’s face turned ashen in midair.

He had First Stage Emperor Realm cultivation and had bid higher, yet Yang Kai refused to sell to him and gave the token to a Dao Origin Realm martial artist instead. It was a public slap in the face.

The Dao Origin Realm martial artist was shocked and terrified, not happy at all. The Qing Jade Command Token in his hand felt like a burning iron, unbearably hot.

If Le Dongzheng hadn’t bid, he would have gladly taken the token. But now, with Le Dongzheng involved, he didn’t dare accept. Taking it meant making enemies, and he could be retaliated against at any time.

“Sir, I don’t want the Command Token.” He struggled to return it.

“Dare to refuse?” Yang Kai seized his collar, eyes wide and fierce. “Believe it or not, I’ll thrash you right here!”

The man was terrified, his face pale. “No fighting is allowed inside Linghu City.”

Yang Kai said darkly, “Can you hide in Linghu City forever? When you leave, I’ll beat you up.”

The man was nearly in tears. “Sir, why make things difficult for me? I’m just a Dao Origin Realm martial artist.”

“Selling you the Command Token is making things difficult?” Yang Kai sneered, still holding his collar. “Will you take it? If not, you’re dead.”

The man avoided Yang Kai’s gaze, not daring to look him in the eye.

Yang Kai whispered, “If you take it, you’ll offend Le Dongzheng, but you’ll get a Command Token and a chance to become an Emperor. If you refuse, you’ll offend me and get nothing. Think carefully about the gains and losses.”

Hearing this, the man’s eyes lit up.

Yes, either way he’d offend an Emperor, but if he took the token, he’d have a chance to ascend. How had he not thought of this before?

In an instant, he made up his mind.

Looking at Le Dongzheng, he cupped his fists. “Lord Le, I’m not competing with you. I was forced to buy and sell, please understand!”

“Hmph!” Le Dongzheng snorted, making the man’s face pale again. But at this point, he had no choice. He quickly transferred forty million Middle-Level Source Crystals from his Spatial Ring to another, handing it to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai tossed it to Bian Yuqing, finally letting go of the martial artist. He said seriously, “You can use this token yourself, but absolutely must not sell it, especially to that old man!” He pointed at Le Dongzheng, threatening, “If I find out you sold it to him, hmph!”

“Understood, understood, I’ll use it myself, won’t sell it.” The man nodded repeatedly.

“Go on!” Yang Kai waved his hand.

The man, as if pardoned, quickly ran into the crowd and disappeared.

The others, seeing the Command Token sold, had no reason to stay and scattered like birds and beasts.

Only Le Dongzheng remained, eyes narrowed at Yang Kai, a cold light flickering in the slits. He said, “Young friend, I wonder what I did to offend you, for you to target me like this.”

Yang Kai sneered, “Sect Master Le, no need to play dumb. Since you’ve come, let’s speak plainly.”

Le Dongzheng said, “I don’t know what you’re talking about.”

“If you don’t know, then there’s nothing more to say.” Yang Kai turned to call Bian Yuqing, “Let’s go.”

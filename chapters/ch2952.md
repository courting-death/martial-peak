## Chapter 2952 — Why Are You So Arrogant

The laws of heaven and earth were the supreme power, the very foundation that maintained all things in existence. Such a foundation could never change in a short period of time, unless the end of days arrived. Yet the other party spoke with such certainty, and he didn’t seem to be lying. It was simply unacceptable.

“Hurry up, hurry up! What are you dawdling for over there?”

A low shout suddenly came from inside the cave. Yang Kai raised his eyebrows—there was actually someone else in this cave.

Upon hearing the urging, the pair of man and woman hurried deeper into the cave. Yang Kai exchanged a glance with Zhu Qing and quickly followed.

A faint glimmer of light blossomed. In a not-so-spacious stone chamber, a middle-aged man stood with a gloomy face, as if waiting for something.

“Steward Liao.” The man who had brought Yang Kai over stepped forward, cupped his fists, and greeted the man respectfully.

Yet Steward Liao didn’t even spare him a glance. He simply stared curiously at Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, who had followed in, and exclaimed, “Why are there two more people? Where did they come from? They look unfamiliar.”

The man replied, “They seemed to have wandered in from outside. We happened to run into them, so we brought them along.”

“From outside?” Steward Liao clicked his tongue in wonder, sizing up the two unfamiliar faces. He didn’t react much when looking at Yang Kai, but when his gaze landed on Zhu Qing, his eyes lit up, showing clear interest.

Zhu Qing was a natural beauty, and after a few days of tender care, she was at her most radiant. She looked especially alluring at this moment.

Zhu Qing’s expression was icy. Though she disliked being stared at like this, she didn’t get angry. Yang Kai’s figure flickered, and he stepped in front of her, blocking the man’s gaze.

Steward Liao snorted coldly, his expression clearly displeased. “Outsiders really don’t know the rules.” He looked up at the man and said, “You don’t happen to be ignorant of them as well, do you?”

The man quickly replied, “We’ll trouble you, Steward Liao.”

Only then did Steward Liao’s expression ease a little. He beckoned to the couple.

The two immediately stepped forward. Steward Liao began muttering incantations, channeling his own power. With his fingers shaped like a sword, he pointed in the air before the man, tracing patterns.

Yang Kai was taken aback, for he actually saw traces of a Shamanic Art in the process. This piqued his curiosity, and he watched even more intently.

Before long, a complex seal suddenly appeared in the void. Steward Liao slapped it, and the seal shot straight into the man’s body. The seal seemed to have some other use with no offensive power. The man only swayed slightly before returning to normal.

When it was the woman’s turn, Steward Liao’s cold face actually showed a rare smile. The woman, on the other hand, looked extremely nervous and glanced pleadingly at her husband.

The man slowly shook his head, his gaze dropping.

Steward Liao repeated the same method, but this time his movements were far less proper. As his finger-sword traced about, it would from time to time brush against the woman’s body, occasionally sweeping across her full chest, causing her delicate figure to tremble slightly.

The man pretended not to notice, standing aside with his eyes lowered.

Steward Liao grew more pleased, his hands becoming even more brazen, making the woman’s face look worse and worse. Yet she could only endure it.

After a short while, the seal formed again. Steward Liao reluctantly slapped it into the woman’s body and withdrew his hand. Only then did the woman’s stiff body relax, and she looked at her husband with grievance.

Steward Liao’s expression immediately turned cold as he looked at Yang Kai and said, “Since you’ve come from outside, I’ll explain the rules here to you.”

Yang Kai smiled at him and said, “Please enlighten us.”

Steward Liao said, “In a little while, the laws of heaven and earth will change. In the entire Revolving World, aside from the Human Sovereign City, there will be no safe place. So if you want to survive, you must go to the Human Sovereign City. Understand?”

“Understood, we’ll go to the Human Sovereign City!” Yang Kai looked like he was being taught a lesson.

Steward Liao continued, “And if you want to go to the Human Sovereign City, you must have this Steward bestow upon you the Human Sovereign Seal. Only those with the seal can use the array to teleport to the city. So there’s only one rule here… Heaven and Earth may be vast, but here, I am the greatest!”

Yang Kai let out an “oh,” cupped his fists, and said, “Thank you for the lesson!”

Steward Liao swept his sleeve and barked, “Let me ask you—do you want to live or die?”

Yang Kai smiled, “If I can live, of course I want to live.”

Steward Liao nodded, “A teachable one. Come, let me bestow the Human Sovereign Seal upon you.”

Yang Kai stepped forward, but Steward Liao waved him aside with a look of disdain. “Women first. You wait over there.”

Yang Kai frowned but didn’t insist. As he stepped aside, he said, “Steward Liao, please be careful. My wife has a bit of a temper.”

Steward Liao snorted, “If you want to live, you’d better behave!”

As he spoke, he began chanting again, fingers like a sword, tracing in the air before Zhu Qing. At first, he behaved, but soon his movements grew bolder, and his fingers swept directly toward Zhu Qing’s chest. His expression became feverish, clearly anticipating something.

Zhu Qing suddenly looked up, staring at him coldly.

Steward Liao was startled, but his hands didn’t stop, clearly intent on taking advantage.

“Woof!”

“Ah!”

A dog’s bark and a scream rang out almost simultaneously. The little black dog that Zhu Qing had been holding suddenly opened its mouth and bit down on Steward Liao’s hand. Its mouth wasn’t even that big, yet it swallowed his entire hand, its sharp teeth clamping down on his wrist, and blood instantly gushed out.

Steward Liao howled in pain, but the little black dog refused to let go, as if it meant to bite his hand off. Its head shook back and forth, and it even let out a low, threatening growl.

“Let go! Let go of me! You little beast, you’re courting death!” Steward Liao cursed, but couldn’t break free. In a rage, he struck at the little black dog, energy surging as thunder and lightning filled the cave.

Yang Kai flicked his finger, sending a burst of force through the air.

Steward Liao let out another miserable scream. The arm he swung went limp and hung down uselessly.

“You… you dare attack me!” Steward Liao turned, glaring at Yang Kai with venom and hatred, gritting his teeth and roaring. He hadn’t suffered such humiliation in ages—he was always the one barking orders, taking whatever he pleased—he'd never expected to kick against an iron plate.

The sudden turn of events happened in a flash. The couple who had brought Yang Kai and Zhu Qing were startled. Seeing Steward Liao suffer, the man hurriedly said, “Stop! Don’t hurt him!”

The woman finally spoke, “Don’t hit him, you’ll regret it.” It was her first time speaking, her voice filled with indescribable anxiety.

Steward Liao also shouted at them, “Quick, quick, kill these two! Otherwise, I’ll have you punished as well!”

The couple were stunned, their faces turning pale as if they’d committed a grave crime. For a moment, they were at a loss.

“Why are you so arrogant!” Yang Kai walked up to Steward Liao, staring at him curiously. “Tell me, what makes you so arrogant? You’re just at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, and you dare act up in front of me?”

Steward Liao’s forehead was covered in cold sweat. He struggled a few times but couldn’t break free from the little black dog. He sneered, “Why? Because I’m the Steward! Because I control the Human Sovereign Seal!”

Yang Kai turned to the couple and asked, “Just because of this Human Sovereign Seal? So even if you’re bullied, you have to swallow your anger? This guy isn’t even stronger than you, is he?”

The man lowered his head, “Without the Human Sovereign Seal, we can’t enter the Human Sovereign City. Without entering, we’ll die!”

Yang Kai let out a cold laugh. "What is there to celebrate in living, or to fear in dying? You're a man, yet you don't even dare resist when your own wife is humiliated right before your eyes. You might as well be dead." He turned to Zhu Qing and winked playfully. "Don't worry, my dear. Even if I have to die, I won't let a single hair on your head be harmed."

Zhu Qing pouted, “Don’t be so glib. I didn’t see you do anything just now.”

Yang Kai grinned, “He’s just trash. You could finish him off with a single breath—why would I need to step in?”

Zhu Qing snorted and said no more.

The man was left ashamed and angry by Yang Kai’s words. The woman stepped forward, took his hand, and looked at Yang Kai, “You’re new here and don’t understand how things work. You’d better let Steward Liao go, or you won’t be able to survive in the Human Sovereign City.”

Steward Liao also grew emboldened, shouting, “Newcomers always have to suffer a little to learn the rules. Let go now, or I’ll make sure you die with no grave!”

Yang Kai asked with a smile, “If I let you go, will you just let this go?”

Steward Liao said, “No way! I’m a man of the Human Sovereign. If you dare hurt me, the Human Sovereign City won’t accept you. But as for your woman… if she’s willing to follow me, that might be a way out.”

“Your brains are your biggest weakness.” Yang Kai looked at him speechlessly. Even at death’s door, he was still making threats. Who knew what was going on in his head?

Zhu Qing shouted coldly, “Ah Wang, bite him!”

The little black dog obeyed the command as if possessed. Its mouth suddenly grew enormous and swallowed Steward Liao whole. Afterwards, it even stuck out its tongue and licked its lips, looking unsatisfied.

It was the first time Zhu Qing had seen the little black dog use such a power. She was momentarily stunned. She’d only meant for it to bite harder, never expecting it to swallow a person whole.

What kind of power was this?

The couple’s faces changed dramatically. “Demonic Beast! It’s really a Demonic Beast!”

The reason they’d chased the little black dog before was because they sensed a Demonic Beast’s aura from it, but in the end, they’d thought it was just a mistake. Now, it was clear it wasn’t. The dog’s demonic qi had been hidden too well, but when it transformed just now, the demonic qi surged out, making it obvious.

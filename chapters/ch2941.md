## Chapter 2941 — Siblings

Zhu Lie snorted coldly, "It means probably, not certain. Are you stupid?"

Yang Kai opened his mouth, but found himself at a loss for words, his mood instantly turning utterly foul.

Zhu Lie glanced at him and let out a light chuckle, "You care about her quite a bit, don't you? Eager to hear news about her?"

Yang Kai sneered, "I'm not even familiar with her, why would I care? Whether she lives or dies has nothing to do with me. That's your Dragon Isle's business, so stop spouting nonsense."

Zhu Lie said, "The last time she contacted Dragon Isle was two months ago. Precisely because it was confirmed she was in grave danger and likely to perish there, I came out from Dragon Isle to find you."

"Why are you looking for me?" Yang Kai asked with a sullen face.

Zhu Lie said, "Her mission wasn't completed, so naturally someone needs to finish it for her!" He shook his head and sighed, "But it seems I can't bring you back to Dragon Isle either. But remember, Dragon Isle will not let this go. Next time, you won't be so lucky."

Yang Kai snorted coldly, "Go back and tell the Dragon Clan: if anyone comes to disturb me again, I'll kill them one by one. If two come, I'll slay both. I'm just curious how many dragons Dragon Isle has for me to slaughter."

Zhu Lie's face darkened, and he gritted his teeth, growling, "Such arrogant boasting."

"Take care, I won't see you off!"

With a flick of his sleeve, Yang Kai turned and entered Spirit Sword Peak. Zhu Lie stood in place, coldly watching for a long while before his figure flickered and he turned into a stream of light, flying away.

No one stopped him. Now, everyone in the Shrine was busy with the Demon Purging Order and searching for Wu Kuang. Most of the experts in the Sect had already set out, so naturally, no one would stop a Dragon Clan member.

Zhu Lie was extremely fast. In just a short while, he had left Qingyang Shrine, and as soon as he exited the Qingyang Mountain Range, he suddenly paused, his gaze coldly fixed ahead at a figure—it was Yang Kai.

Zhu Lie had no idea when Yang Kai had gotten ahead of him. At this moment, Yang Kai stood there with his hands behind his back. Annoyed, Zhu Lie shouted, "What do you want now? Are you going back on your word? This dragon doesn't intend to trouble you anymore. If you dare break your promise..."

"Don't be nervous!" Yang Kai interrupted him with a faint sneer, "I just want to ask, how does your Dragon Isle plan to handle Zhu Qing's matter?"

"Zhu Qing is already dead, what else is there to handle?" Zhu Lie frowned.

Yang Kai said, "Didn't you just say probably, not certain? How can you now be sure she's already dead?"

Zhu Lie said, "Even if she's not dead, she won't live much longer. She went to the Frozen Earth. You should know how dangerous that place is."

"Of course I know. If it weren't dangerous, a Dragon wouldn't have fallen there. I just want to ask, isn't Dragon Isle going to save her?"

Zhu Lie let out a cold laugh, "She's an Eighth-Tier Red Dragon. If even she fell into that place, there aren't many dragons on the island who can guarantee they'd escape unscathed. To risk the lives of other clansmen just to save one who may already be dead? Dragon Isle doesn't do such things!"

Yang Kai shouted, "Even if she's already dead, you should at least retrieve her body. She's your clansman, after all!"

Zhu Lie sneered repeatedly, "She overestimated herself and barged into the Frozen Earth. Why should others risk themselves for her? If she's dead, then so be it. No one will go save her."

"Is that your opinion or Dragon Isle's?" Yang Kai's face darkened.

"It's my opinion, and Dragon Isle's as well!" Zhu Lie tilted his head, avoiding Yang Kai's gaze.

Yang Kai was silent for a while before saying softly, "I really want to beat you up."

Zhu Lie sneered, "Haven't you beaten me enough these days? The humiliation you've given me, I will repay a hundredfold in the future!"

"Say that again when you can actually beat me." Yang Kai glanced at him disdainfully, then turned and flew back toward Qingyang Shrine.

He was extremely fast, and in just a few breaths, he returned to Spirit Sword Peak, heading straight for the back mountain. Although Spirit Sword Peak had suffered from the Demonic Intent breaking the seal, the back mountain hadn't been much damaged, so the Space Array he had set up here was still intact.

Only when he stood before the Space Array did Yang Kai suddenly come to his senses. Just now, his mind had been preoccupied with other matters, and he hadn't even noticed his own movements. By the time he realized, he was already here.

"Following my heart, huh..." Yang Kai muttered softly, helplessly saying, "What a hassle."

Though he said so, the hesitation on his face vanished without a trace, replaced by a look of firm resolve.

A sound of something tearing through the air rapidly approached. Yang Kai turned his head and saw a fiery red light flying over. Sensing Yang Kai's presence, it came straight to the back mountain, landing not far from him.

"What do you want?" Yang Kai looked at the returning Zhu Lie with annoyance, as if ready to fight him at any moment.

Zhu Lie asked, "Are you planning to go to the Frozen Earth? To find Zhu Qing?"

"None of your damn business!" Yang Kai sneered.

Zhu Lie looked a bit awkward, turning his head aside, "If you're going, count me in."

"Oh?" Yang Kai raised an eyebrow, looking Zhu Lie up and down with interest, "Give me a reason. And don't say your conscience was pricked—I'm not going to believe such a childish excuse."

Zhu Lie rubbed his nose and said, "Zhu Qing is my elder sister!"

Yang Kai was slightly surprised, but quickly understood.

He had vaguely suspected there was some blood relation between Zhu Lie and Zhu Qing before, since they looked somewhat alike. But after asking once and not getting an answer, he hadn't pursued it further.

Now he finally knew his earlier guess was correct—these two Red Dragons were actually siblings.

"Is that reason enough?" Zhu Lie looked at Yang Kai seriously.

"It's enough, more than enough." Yang Kai nodded, stepped onto the Space Array, and called out, "Come on."

Zhu Lie frowned, not knowing what Yang Kai was up to, but sensing no malice, and being confident in his own strength, he leapt onto the array.

"Don't resist!" Yang Kai shouted as the Laws of Space surged. The void seemed to collapse in that instant. If not for Yang Kai's warning, Zhu Lie would surely have resisted, and might have ended up lost in the endless void.

A flash of light, and the two disappeared together.

At the same time, in the Northern Territory's Lingxiao Palace.

At the Space Array, Yang Kai and Zhu Lie appeared together. The two disciples who had been standing guard hurried forward to salute, "Greetings, Palace Master!"

Yang Kai nodded, took out a Transmission Compass, poured a message into it, and then stood waiting.

Zhu Lie had recovered from the aftereffects of the cross-domain teleportation. He glanced around, frowning, "Where is this place?"

Without turning his head, Yang Kai replied, "Northern Territory, Lingxiao Palace!"

Zhu Lie was stunned, then turned to look at the Space Array, dazed, "Zhu Qing said you had a cross-domain Space Array here. So it was true."

Yang Kai's face suddenly darkened with rage, "That damned woman! Why does she report everything back to Dragon Isle? Do I have no privacy at all?"

Zhu Lie's face darkened, "Watch your words, human. That's my elder sister!"

Yang Kai let out a cold laugh, "Elder sister? Yet I don't see you making any effort to save her. If I had a younger brother like you, I would've strangled him long ago. What use would such a brother be?"

"What do you know!" Who knows what Yang Kai said to hit Zhu Lie's sore spot, but he suddenly became furious, gritting his teeth, "You think I don't want to save her? You think I want to watch her die there?"

"Then why don't you go? When the heart moves, the body follows. As cultivators, we should always follow our hearts, forge ahead bravely, and strive forward. If you hesitate and waver, that will become a heart demon. If she's really your elder sister, you shouldn't have come to Qingyang Shrine to find me, but gone to the Frozen Earth to find her. Even if you die, you should die with her. Now, time has been wasted, and perhaps the last bit of hope she had was cut off by you."

Zhu Lie's lips moved, his anger reaching its peak, his eyes almost spitting fire.

But for some reason, he suddenly deflated, looking dejected, and sighed, "When the heart moves, the body follows. Is that why you want to save her?"

Yang Kai curled his lip, "What else? I'm not close enough to her to risk my life for her. I'd be glad if she died sooner... But since the thought of saving her has arisen, if I don't act on it, that thought will linger in my heart and become a heart demon."

Zhu Lie said, "Whatever the case, I thank you on Zhu Qing's behalf."

"No need for thanks. Just don't bother me in the future." Yang Kai snorted coldly.

A graceful figure flew over and landed nearby. It was Lingxiao Palace's Chief Steward, Hua Qingsi, who cupped her fists, "Palace Master, you've returned!" As she spoke, she glanced curiously at Zhu Lie standing beside Yang Kai, but didn't ask further.

Zhu Lie naturally had no interest in saying anything. The Dragons had always been arrogant; if not for the losses he had suffered at Yang Kai’s hands before, he would not be regarding him in a different light now.

"I'm about to leave again," Yang Kai said, looking impatient.

"Where are you going?" Hua Qingsi asked.

"I need to make a trip to Li Dragon Palace. Is our Space Array connected to theirs?"

Hua Qingsi replied, "I did suggest it before, but Li Jiao wasn't too willing, so I didn't push it."

"Of course he wasn't..." Yang Kai smiled. Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect were both old top sects in the Northern Territory, but both had suffered heavy losses at his hands and owed Lingxiao Palace a huge debt that would take decades to repay. Who would want to connect their Space Array to their creditor? That would just make it easier for the debt collector to show up! "Never mind, I'll just fly over. Point me in the right direction."

Hua Qingsi quickly pointed out a direction, "Li Dragon Palace is this way. At your speed, Palace Master, you should arrive in about three days. Should I accompany you? I've been to Li Dragon Palace once."

"No need." Yang Kai waved his hand, then switched to a voice transmission, "How's the matter with the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape?"

Hua Qingsi straightened her expression, "It's all been handled. Chi Yue and the others went in once and gained a lot. But the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape is so vast, it'll take some time to figure out its patterns. I'm planning to select some disciples from Thousand Leaves Sect to go in next."

## Chapter 3001 — Too Shameless

They had no connection to any Great Emperor, yet still dared act so arrogant and overbearing? Hua Xing's expression instantly turned gloomy.

While the two were speaking, Yang Kai was lecturing Lin Yun'er. "Didn't we agree to launch a sneak attack together? Why did you act so quickly?"

Lin Yun'er scratched her head. "I forgot."

Yang Kai sighed. "Well, now we've alerted the enemy. I'm afraid taking him down won't be so easy anymore."

"Then what should we do now?" Lin Yun'er looked at Yang Kai somewhat innocently. After exchanging several moves with Hua Xing just now, she had also realized how troublesome this fellow was. Ever since arriving in the Star Realm, she had remained by her Master's side, cultivating assiduously in retreat, and had never truly fought anyone before.

It was only after running away from home this time that she discovered the people outside seemed unable to withstand a beating, often dying after just a few punches and kicks from her. She had thought everyone was like that.

Hua Xing's performance broadened her horizons and showed her that there were still experts in this world.

"What else can we do? We'll just have to take him head-on." Yang Kai shrugged.

Lin Yun'er immediately understood and nodded her little head. "I see. Then we'll beat him to death together."

She spoke as though it were only natural, as if victory were already assured.

Hua Xing was beside himself with rage. “Insolent juniors! This Lord had no intention of stooping to your level, yet you insisted on delivering yourselves into my hands. Good, good! Today, this place shall be your burial ground!”

Since these two had nothing to do with a Great Emperor, his courage immediately returned, and he felt that he had been far too cautious. Young people were always hot-blooded. Had he not been the same in his youth? A newborn calf did not fear a tiger; no matter how many thorns lay ahead, it would press forward with vigour and determination. Yet now that he was old, he considered every possible consequence before acting. Not every young person had a profound background.

He secretly hardened his heart, resolving to teach these two juniors a harsh lesson today.

Gritting his teeth as he looked at Li Jiao, Hua Xing said, "Brother Li, if you leave now, we can still remain good friends."

He did not fear the two juniors. The one he dreaded was Li Jiao. This fellow possessed a Dragon Descendant's bloodline, after all, and was somewhat stronger than he was.

"What kind of friend sets someone up like this?" Li Jiao shot back bluntly, his voice filled with resentment.

Hua Xing sighed. "Since that is the case, this Hua has nothing more to say." Raising both hands, he roared, "Kill!"

The instant he finished speaking, he made the first move. The azure ruler once again transformed into ruler shadows that filled the sky and smashed down toward Yang Kai and the other two. At the same time, the surrounding Wind and Cloud Pavilion cultivators all attacked in unison. Secret Treasures flew through the air and the radiance of Secret Techniques blossomed, instantly engulfing the three.

"Palace Master Yang, this Li will keep this old bastard occupied. Once you deal with this trash, come help me!" Li Jiao roared. Reaching into the air with one hand, he immediately summoned an ancient-looking scroll. The scroll swiftly unfurled, releasing streams of mysterious light that guarded the surrounding area with an impenetrable defence.

A thunderous series of explosions rang out as Li Jiao blocked every attack. His figure flickered, and he pounced toward Hua Xing.

Hua Xing roared in fury, “Li Jiao, you shameless old dog!”

He was nearly driven mad with rage. When Li Jiao had visited Wind and Cloud Pavilion a few days ago, Hua Xing had treated him with the utmost courtesy, serving him fine food and wine and even gifting him many beautiful women for his pleasure. Never had he imagined that Li Jiao would turn hostile and cast aside all friendship after merely going out once.

Hatred surged in Hua Xing's heart, and he immediately began battling Li Jiao. Two Third-Stage Emperor Realm Masters fought with their lives on the line, and in an instant, their battle shook Heaven and Earth, making ghosts and gods weep. The air cracked and Spiritual Qi churned violently.

Once the two began fighting, the Wind and Cloud Pavilion cultivators naturally did not remain idle. Relying on their superior numbers, they unleashed an overwhelming barrage of attacks toward Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er.

It had little effect.

Most of these cultivators were only in the Dao Origin Realm, so how could they possibly be opponents for Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er?

As the attacks arrived, the two swayed aside, one to the left and the other to the right, and instantly vanished from where they stood, causing every attack to strike empty air.

Immediately afterward, miserable screams rang out one after another as Wind and Cloud Pavilion cultivators burst into clouds of blood.

In the span of just ten breaths, more than forty people died miserably on the spot. The remaining cultivators paled in alarm, their eyes brimming with horror and panic.

They had assumed that even if Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er were in the Emperor Realm, they could not be too strong, given the auras they displayed. Only after exchanging blows did they realize how terribly wrong they had been. These two were simply human meat grinders. Wherever they passed, no one could withstand even a single move, and their fellow Senior Brothers and Junior Brothers fell like stalks of rice being reaped.

How could they possibly continue fighting? The surviving cultivators had no courage to battle the pair and only wanted to flee as far away as possible.

Just then, a series of whooshing sounds rapidly approached from the distance.

When the Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples looked over, they were immediately overjoyed. "Great Elder is here!"

"The other Elders are here too!"

"And all our Senior Brothers and Senior Sisters!"

"Elders, save us!"

Disorderly shouts rang out one after another. As they gazed at Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Great Elder flying toward them, they looked like drowning people who had finally grasped a life-saving straw.

Wind and Cloud Pavilion was one of the Western Territory's top Sects. Besides Hua Xing, a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, it also had two Second-Stage Emperor Realm Masters and nearly ten First-Stage Emperor Realm Masters. Such a foundation could not be called weak.

The two Second-Stage Emperor Realm Masters held the positions of Great Elder and Second Elder respectively. Unfortunately, Second Elder had previously been killed in Changyun City, costing Wind and Cloud Pavilion a capable master.

Great Elder and the others had clearly received Hua Xing's message and were now leading a force here.

More than a hundred people followed behind him in a great mass, and every one of them had at least reached the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. They were clearly the elite among Wind and Cloud Pavilion's elite.

It had been a long time since Wind and Cloud Pavilion had mobilized the full strength of the entire Sect to deal with an enemy, but the situation was critical. Unless they did so, they had no hope at all.

Great Elder flew through the air with his broad sleeves billowing. From afar, he saw the scene of his Sect's disciples being slaughtered and immediately flew into a rage, roaring, "Juniors, stop at once!"

As he spoke, an ancient-looking longsword appeared in his hand. Surging his Emperor Yuan, he slashed his sword down toward Yang Kai.

The sword shot skyward, and a breathtaking Sword Intent filled the air, causing everyone’s skin to tighten involuntarily as a chill ran through them.

The sword light swept forward and cut through Yang Kai's body with incomparable precision, cleaving him in two.

The many Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples cheered endlessly at the sight.

"Great Elder is mighty!"

"Great Elder is so powerful!"

"How could a mere thief withstand the might of Great Elder's sword? In terms of attainments in the Sword Dao, Great Elder ranks among the top ten in the entire Western Territory."

"Hahahaha! That's what you get for being so arrogant! Now you know how powerful we are!"

...

The morale of the Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples, who had just been beaten into a panic and sent fleeing in every direction, soared, and their hearts filled with delight.

Lin Yun'er's pretty face instantly went pale as she screamed, "Uncle Yang!"

"Mm?" Great Elder, however, felt none of the joy of succeeding. He knew his own swordsmanship well, and he had not felt his blade cut through anything solid at all. When he focused his gaze again, a chill swept over him from head to toe, leaving his whole body cold.

How could the figure he had cut apart have been a real person? Not even a single drop of blood had flowed from it. It was clearly nothing more than an afterimage.

It was only because the afterimage had been so lifelike that it deceived so many pairs of eyes.

Where was he? Since that was an afterimage, the boy himself had to be unharmed. Where had he gone? How had even Great Elder failed to detect his movements?

Just as this thought crossed his mind, a strange figure suddenly appeared before him.

"Hiss..." Great Elder sucked in a sharp breath and thrust his longsword forward without thinking.

This longsword was undoubtedly a high-grade Imperial Treasure, radiating waves of Imperial Might. Moreover, Great Elder had refined and nurtured it for many years and had long since reached the state of becoming one with his sword. This thrust was exquisitely executed, its angle and timing both grasped with flawless precision. Its sword intent remained perfectly condensed while its momentum was hidden and unreleased, making the strike completely silent.

"Oh?" Yang Kai was also somewhat surprised. He had encountered many Second-Stage Emperor Realm Masters and had even killed several of them, but those people were far inferior to Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Great Elder.

This sword thrust appeared simple, but in truth, it sealed off every direction around him. Yang Kai discovered that there was nowhere he could evade.

Since he could not dodge, he could only meet it head-on.

The Myriad Sword appeared in his hand, erupting with a heaven-shaking sword light as Yang Kai swung it forward.

A rapid series of metallic clangs rang out, and in the blink of an eye, the two had exchanged an unknown number of moves.

Suddenly, Yang Kai drifted backward and looked at Great Elder in amazement.

Not far away, Great Elder's expression was livid, and the hand gripping his sword trembled uncontrollably. Gritting his teeth, he shouted, "What is this supposed to be?"

In terms of exquisite swordsmanship, the young man opposite him was not even worthy of carrying his shoes, yet with only a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, he had somehow withstood an entire round of rapid attacks. What he relied on was not his attainments in the Sword Dao, but his ability to adapt in the moment and his terrifying strength, especially the latter. It left Great Elder badly shaken. Every collision sent a tremendous force surging from the other party's sword, causing his Qi and blood to roil. Had his cultivation not been so profound, he feared he would not even have been able to keep hold of his sword.

Too shameless!

“Overwhelming strength overcomes all techniques!” Yang Kai raised a brow and smiled. “Great Elder, your swordsmanship is truly remarkable!”

As soon as he finished speaking, jets of blood suddenly shot from numerous places on his arms and thighs, and even the clothes he wore fell to tatters.

In this exchange, Yang Kai had been the one to suffer a loss. Although Great Elder's aura had been thrown into turmoil by his strength, Yang Kai had still sustained some injuries. Had he not been wearing the Feihong Black Dragon Armor, he would likely have suffered even more.

“Uncle Yang, you’re alright!” Lin Yun’er’s voice came from the side. Wherever her two fists swept through, the disciples of Wind and Cloud Pavilion fell in droves, leaving the dead and wounded strewn across the battlefield.

When she saw Yang Kai cleaved in two just now, she had thought he was dead and had nearly burst into tears from grief. Fortunately, Yang Kai immediately reappeared, allowing her to understand that her eyes had deceived her. Only then did she relax.

"Of course I'm all right. Little Yun'er, be careful yourself," Yang Kai instructed.

"Mm, mm." Lin Yun'er nodded repeatedly. "They can't beat me."

Her casual, contemptuous attitude enraged Great Elder and the other Elders. They had also realized how extraordinary Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er were and believed that if they continued fighting like a loose pile of sand, they might truly suffer an unexpected defeat.

Great Elder turned to glance at the other battlefield. Li Jiao and Hua Xing were locked in a fierce struggle, and it was unlikely that either would emerge victorious anytime soon, so they naturally could not count on the Pavilion Master coming to help.

A gloomy light flashed through Great Elder's eyes as he made his decision and shouted, "Form the Array!"

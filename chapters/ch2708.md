## Chapter 2708 — The Dragon Clan Has Three Passions

With a heart full of excitement and anticipation, Yang Kai began to examine the contents carefully.

But soon, his brows furrowed and his expression turned odd. As time passed, that oddness only deepened, veins bulging on his forehead. Finally, unable to bear it, he looked up at Zhu Qing and said, "What the hell are all these things!"

"What, nothing you like?" Zhu Qing stared at him in surprise.

That couldn't be. Everything in this ring had been painstakingly collected by her, each item precious in her eyes. How could he not like a single one?

"Like hell I do!" Yang Kai couldn't take it anymore. He casually pulled out a jade hairpin from the Spatial Ring. The hairpin shone brilliantly, dazzling with color and looking quite impressive, but the key point was that it was just a Dao Origin-Grade Lower-Level Secret Treasure, with barely any power fluctuations. It seemed to only have a passable defensive technique sealed within. He glared at Zhu Qing and growled, "This is what you call a good item?"

Yang Kai felt utterly deceived, his heart deeply wounded.

Zhu Qing looked at him nervously and said, "Be gentle, don't break it."

As if that jade hairpin was some extraordinary treasure.

Yang Kai was so angry he could cough up blood.

Zhu Qing hurriedly said, "If you don't like this one, look at the others. There are plenty more."

Yang Kai reached into the Spatial Ring and grabbed a handful of odd trinkets. Some were Secret Treasures, some were things he couldn't even name, and there were even a few strange gems.

Each and every one of them sparkled brilliantly, dazzling the eyes with their radiant luster. Just by appearance alone, they looked quite impressive. Gathered together, their combined brilliance was blinding, filling the room with shimmering light.

But the key was, to someone like Yang Kai at the Emperor Realm, these things were utterly worthless. They couldn't be used for cultivation or to enhance strength, so naturally, they had no value. Yet Zhu Qing treated them like treasures, storing them carefully in her Spatial Ring.

Yang Kai could hardly believe this was a Dragon's collection. He almost wanted to gouge out his own eyes.

"Is there anything you like or want? I can trade any of them with you," Zhu Qing said, completely oblivious, her beautiful eyes shining as if he was about to make a huge profit.

"Are you really this clueless, or just pretending?" Yang Kai sneered.

"What do you mean!" Zhu Qing was a bit angry now, hearing the mockery in Yang Kai's tone.

Yang Kai was taken aback for a moment before suddenly understanding. “I’ve heard the Dragon Clan has three great passions. From the looks of it, that rumor seems to be true?”

"What three great passions?" Zhu Qing asked curiously. She had lived on Dragon Isle since childhood and this was her first time leaving to travel the Star Realm, so she had never heard of such a thing.

Yang Kai sighed, "Sleeping, shiny things... uh, and that other thing!"

"What other thing?" Zhu Qing glared at him, refusing to let it go. "Make yourself clear."

Yang Kai shot her an annoyed look. "Kids shouldn't ask so many questions."

"You're the kid! Your whole family are kids!" Zhu Qing fumed, nearly exploding with anger.

Yang Kai couldn't be bothered to argue. He laughed it off and said, "Anyway, all this stuff is trash to me. No need to trade." With that, he tossed the Spatial Ring back.

The most important thing was, this girl's Spatial Ring didn't even have a single Source Crystal. He had no idea what she used for cultivation. Maybe the Dragon Clan didn't need Source Crystals to cultivate. After all, the Dragon Clan were also considered yao, and Yang Kai had never seen any yao use Source Crystals for cultivation. Yao always cultivated by directly absorbing Spiritual Qi and the essence of sun and moon, or by consuming heaven-and-earth treasures and the Inner Cores of other Demonic Beasts.

"Impossible!" Zhu Qing huffed, looking as if he could insult her, but not her treasures.

But then, remembering she still needed his help, her momentum deflated. She hesitated, then said, "If you don't like these, what about these two?"

As she spoke, she suddenly flipped her hand, and a scale the size of a basket appeared in her snow-white palm, along with a jade bottle.

"Dragon scale!" Yang Kai sensed the dragon aura emanating from the scale and his eyes lit up.

He immediately took the dragon scale and jade bottle, gently stroking the hard scale.

Years ago, back in the Hengluo Star Domain, he had obtained a dragon scale and later refined it into his chest, right over his heart. Now, whenever he activated the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, that dragon scale would amplify its power.

If he could refine another one, the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique would surely become even stronger.

As he stroked it, Yang Kai sensed a familiar aura within the dragon scale and immediately realized it must have come from Zhu Qing herself, probably shed during a previous transformation. He glanced at her thoughtfully.

He found Zhu Qing's face flushed red, looking extremely uncomfortable. He couldn't help but wonder what was going on with her.

In fact, just as Yang Kai guessed, this dragon scale was indeed Zhu Qing's, shed during a transformation. Even so, it had once been part of her body, and now, with Yang Kai caressing it so gently, Zhu Qing couldn't help but feel as if he were touching her directly.

Scenes of Yang Kai's wanton teasing and tasting flashed through her mind, making her feel awkward.

Fortunately, Yang Kai didn't continue for long. After a moment, he set down the dragon scale and opened the jade bottle.

Faintly, a dragon's roar echoed from the bottle, and a cloud of crimson light burst forth, scorching hot.

Yang Kai looked closely and saw that inside the jade bottle, a drop of bright red blood had taken the shape of a dragon, like a burning flame about to leap from the bottle.

"True Dragon Blood!" Yang Kai exclaimed, quickly capping the bottle.

"How about it? These two things in exchange for your Dragon Isle Token!" Zhu Qing looked at Yang Kai anxiously.

Yang Kai pondered for a moment before nodding. “They are indeed fine items, but… in your view, is the Dragon Isle Token only worth these two things?”

Zhu Qing bit her red lips and whispered, "It's definitely a loss."

Yang Kai found her honesty refreshing, and his impression of Zhu Qing improved quite a bit. He didn’t realize that the Dragon Clan, proud by nature, would never stoop to lying.

"But haven't you already refined Dragon Secret Treasures? If you refine these two as well, your Dragon Transformation Secret Technique will become even stronger," Zhu Qing urged.

Yang Kai smiled slightly. "The things you mentioned when we first met, about me refining things that didn't belong to me, you meant those Dragon Secret Treasures, right?"

He had only figured this out after learning Zhu Qing's identity.

Dragon Secret Treasures were originally parts of the Dragon Clan's bodies. As a dragon woman, Zhu Qing would naturally want to punish anyone who refined them, to uphold the Dragon Clan's dignity.

But she never imagined that, in the end, she would be the one to suffer defeat.

Otherwise, having never met Yang Kai before and bearing him neither grievance nor enmity, why would she have come looking for trouble with him the moment they crossed paths?

"Since you know I've already refined several Dragon Secret Treasures, how much stronger could I get with two more?" Yang Kai grinned, tossing the dragon scale and the bottle of True Dragon Blood back to Zhu Qing, then flipped the Dragon Isle Token in his hand. "But this thing is truly one of a kind."

"But..." Zhu Qing frowned, "Aren't we friends?"

"Friends are friends, business is business!" Yang Kai said seriously. "Alright, I need to rest. You should go."

Zhu Qing refused to leave, simply staring at him with her wide, clear eyes.

Yang Kai's face darkened. "If you don't go, I'll activate my Dragon Clan Origin. Alone in a room with a man, if anything happens, don't blame me for not treating you as a friend!"

Startled, Zhu Qing stomped her foot and left.

The Ancestral Dragon Origin had absolute suppressive power over her. Even if her bloodline improved, she couldn't resist. If Yang Kai really activated the Ancestral Dragon Origin, she might suffer again.

After sending Zhu Qing away, Yang Kai chuckled to himself.

After sorting through his spoils, Yang Kai found his gains were quite impressive. He had collected over a hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals alone, not to mention all the other treasures.

But those were secondary, as none could increase his strength. The real prize was the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome he had taken from Tan Junhao. If he could refine it, it would greatly boost his power.

Yang Kai took out the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, intending to examine it and see how many abilities it contained. But he found that without refining it, he couldn't open the tome at all. In the end, he could only erase Tan Junhao's Soul Brand, then slowly pour his own Emperor Yuan and Divine Sense into it to refine this Imperial Treasure.

Back in her room, Zhu Qing immediately took out her Dragon Talisman and performed a spell. An old voice came from the Dragon Talisman: "Is it done?"

Zhu Qing replied respectfully, "Not yet."

"If it's not done, why are you contacting me?"

Zhu Qing said, "Qing'er has found the last Dragon Isle Token."

The old voice said, "You're quite lucky. Of the four clansmen sent out this time, you found it first. Can you retrieve the Dragon Isle Token?"

Zhu Qing answered in shame, "Not yet..."

The old voice said, "Retrieve the Dragon Isle Token at once. This thing cannot be left outside. Use any means necessary—even if you have to snatch it. With your strength, there shouldn't be many in this world who can stop you."

Zhu Qing replied, "That's exactly the problem. The owner of the Dragon Isle Token is the one I mentioned to you before, so... it's not convenient to use force."

"He has the Dragon Isle Token too?" The Dragon Clan elder was shocked. Was this luck, or fate's will?

If it were anyone else, Zhu Qing could have taken it by force, but that man possessed the Ancestral Dragon Origin. Even if Zhu Qing was stronger, the suppression of Origin Power left her helpless.

"Qing'er invited him to Dragon Isle, but he didn't agree. Now I don't know what to do. Please advise me, Elder."

The Dragon Talisman fell silent for a long time, as if it too was at a loss for a solution. After quite a while, it finally asked, "Has he done anything noteworthy lately?"

"Yes."

"Tell me in detail!"

Zhu Qing immediately recounted how Yang Kai had fought many Emperor Realm masters a few days ago.

The Dragon Talisman chuckled and said, “Is this person truly so remarkable? With just a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, he managed to slay Second and Third Stage Emperors? And one of them was even an elder from Star God Palace?”

Zhu Qing said, "I didn't see it with my own eyes, but that's how it happened."

"Then that's easy." The voice in the Dragon Talisman was full of confidence. "Tell him this..."

Zhu Qing listened carefully, nodding repeatedly.

## Chapter 2981 — Who Do You Want to Kill?

"Call your elder sister over." Yang Kai turned to Zhu Lie and instructed him.

Zhu Lie snorted lightly. Although he was displeased with Yang Kai's casual tone, he did not argue with him. Upon hearing this, he urged his Dragon Essence, formed a hand seal, and moved his lips softly a few times.

Yang Kai turned back to the Succubus and asked with a smile, "If I asked you to kill a Demon King, whom would you choose?"

The Succubus's expression changed as she exclaimed, "What does Sir mean by that?"

"Let me put it another way. Who do you find most displeasing?"

The Succubus forced a smile. "Sir must be joking. We all belong to the Demon Race and have been stranded here, relying on one another for survival. Everyone in Demon Fury City is like a brother or sister, so how could I find anyone displeasing?"

When the other Demon Kings heard this, they all straightened their backs and tried their best to look as though they shared both hardship and life and death. Although no one knew what Yang Kai meant by his question, everyone sensed that something was amiss.

This human seemed to harbor ill intentions. He was definitely not as amiable as he appeared. He was like a needle hidden inside a ball of cotton; the slightest carelessness would leave one bloodied and battered.

The corner of Yang Kai's mouth curled upward. "The opportunity is in your hands. Once it slips away, there won't be another."

The Succubus remained silent. After hesitating for a moment, her Divine Sense quietly surged.

Yang Kai glanced to the side and met the gaze of a gloomy-faced Demon King. Blood Qi churned around this Demon King's body, giving off a faint red glow. He appeared to be from the Blood Demon Clan. His expression was uneasy, as though he were deeply worried about something.

The instant Yang Kai's gaze swept toward him, he reacted. Gritting his teeth, he roared furiously at the Succubus, "You bitch! How dare you betray me!"

The Succubus pursed her red lips, her pretty face turning slightly pale, but she remained silent in the face of the Blood Demon's accusation.

The Blood Demon grew even angrier. "You heartless wench! When you were writhing beneath my crotch, you were full of charm and seductive wiles, but now that you've climbed onto a higher branch, you want to kill me? The saying is true: whores are heartless, and actors are faithless!"

His words left the Succubus ashamed and furious. Gritting her teeth, she said, "I may be a Succubus, but I am not a woman who will sleep with just anyone. If you hadn't forced yourself on me back then, how could I have..."

"Hahahaha!" The Blood Demon laughed as the Blood Qi around him churned violently. "Enough of your excuses! The Succubus Clan is nothing but a pack of cheap whores. If this King had not supported you in so many ways over the years, how could someone of your aptitude have advanced to become a Demon King? Do you think this King doesn't know that you've been secretly seducing other men all these years? You actually have the face to claim you don't sleep with just anyone? Now you're nothing but a heap of rotting, stinking meat that even wild dogs would disdain to sniff. Even if you knelt and begged me, this King would never touch you again."

"Stop spouting nonsense!" the Succubus shrieked. Turning to Yang Kai with a pleading expression, she said, "Sir, although this humble one does not know what you intend to do, if you wish to kill someone, please kill him now. This humble one will obey your every command from now on."

"Impudent! You want to kill me? I'll kill you first!" The Blood Demon flew into a rage. With a raise of his hand, a streak of blood light shot toward the Succubus. It exploded in midair and transformed into a shower of blood that covered a vast area.

The blood rain seemed extremely corrosive, producing a series of sizzling sounds as it flew through the air.

The Succubus screamed. Her cultivation was already far below the Blood Demon's, so once he attacked, she was utterly unable to resist. Her body rapidly retreated as her Demonic Voice poured into everyone's ears. She waved both hands repeatedly, releasing streams of pure Demonic Qi, but still could not stop the blood rain from closing in.

Sizzle, sizzle, sizzle, sizzle... Droplets of blood rain broke through the Succubus's defenses and struck her body, corroding her clothes and exposing large patches of snow-white skin. The places touched by the blood rain quickly began to rot, turning into pus that ran down her body. Some areas even exposed ghastly white bone, leaving her looking incomparably miserable in an instant.

"Die, bitch!" The Blood Demon refused to relent. His figure flickered as he lunged toward the Succubus, clearly intending to exterminate her completely.

The Succubus's face turned ashen with terror. Her Demonic Voice continued without pause, but she still could not stop the Blood Demon's advance. Feeling the aura of death rushing toward her, she thought that there was likely no way she would survive this time.

However, the Blood Demon suddenly changed direction in midair. His entire body burst apart, transforming into an endless torrent of blood that surged fiercely toward Yang Kai with astonishing might, like a raging tide in a sea of blood. Terrifying destructive power filled the blood wave, as though it intended to sweep across the surrounding land. At the very front, the Blood Demon's face emerged. He seemed to ride atop the wave as he lunged ferociously toward Yang Kai.

The Succubus had only been a smokescreen. His target had been Yang Kai from beginning to end.

Although he had not wanted to clash with Yang Kai, the Succubus's interference had pushed him into the eye of the storm. If he wanted to survive this crisis safely, his only choice was to seize the initiative.

That red-haired young man's strength was far too terrifying. He had instantly killed the First Demon King in a single move, so the Blood Demon did not dare target him under any circumstances.

Although Yang Kai had also killed several Demon Kings earlier, he had relied on the power of an artifact, so his own strength might not be particularly formidable. Before he could summon that artifact, there might still be a chance to capture him.

As long as he captured this human, he would hold the absolute initiative. The moment this thought occurred to the Blood Demon, he acted, believing he had a very good chance of success.

Just then, he suddenly saw a meaningful smile appear on Yang Kai's face. That smile made his heart sink, and an inexplicable feeling of a sheep walking into a tiger's den arose within him, as though he had made the wrong decision.

The hesitation in his heart caused the momentum of the blood wave he had transformed into to falter.

Yang Kai raised his hand and flicked his fingers repeatedly.

Chi chi chi chi...

Pitch-black crescent-shaped slashes whistled forth, sweeping unstoppably through everything in their path as they cut across the blood wave.

Every Moon Blade made the Blood Demon grunt and caused the blood-red light to dim and the volume of blood to diminish.

After seven or eight Moon Blades, the blood wave rolled back, and the Blood Demon's figure reappeared, staggering into view not far in front of Yang Kai.

Yang Kai rapidly formed a series of hand seals, his expression solemn as a faint power of the Laws of Time flowed around him.

"The years wither and flourish, like a shuttle, like a dream!"

He lightly struck out with his palm, instantly hitting the dazed Blood Demon. The Blood Demon spat out a mouthful of blood and flew backward like a paper kite. Forcing himself to focus, he shook his body again and transformed into streaks of blood light that shot in every direction.

Being severely wounded in an instant made this Demon King of the Blood Demon Clan realize that he had underestimated his opponent and had no chance of winning.

All he wanted now was to escape, as far away as possible.

“Blood Shadow Escape!” Yang Kai snorted and sneered. “If this were the Shadow Demon Clan’s Shadow Kill Divine Escape, this Young Master might have to spend a bit of effort to break it. But you dare show off a mere Blood Shadow Escape before me? Watch this Young Master break it at once.” As he spoke, his gaze swept swiftly across the countless streaks of blood-light before locking onto one of them. He then raised a hand and pointed at it from afar.

The Laws of Space surged, and the place occupied by that streak of blood light instantly transformed into an invisible cage.

The speeding blood light crashed into the cage wall and recoiled, revealing the Blood Demon's pale face.

"How is that possible?!" he cried out.

Blood Shadow Escape was an innate Divine Ability of the Blood Demon Clan and had no flaws to speak of. When ordinary people encountered this Divine Ability, they had no way to distinguish which streak was the true body and which were illusions.

Yet this human youth, who was only a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivator, had seen through it at a glance.

Moreover, what the young man had just said frightened him even more. The Blood Demon Clan's Blood Shadow Escape and the Shadow Demon Clan's Shadow-Killing Divine Escape were both innate Divine Abilities, supreme techniques for preserving one's life and escaping. Even Demon Kings of the Demon Race found them extremely difficult to break, yet judging by this human's tone, he seemed to possess extensive knowledge of the Demon Race.

The Blood Demon was horrified beyond measure, and all the blood in his body ran cold.

He tried to use his escape technique again, but a figure suddenly flashed before his eyes, and Yang Kai was already blocking his path.

The Blood Demon immediately sucked in a cold breath and hurriedly tried to retreat.

"Look at me!" Yang Kai's voice was low, carrying an irresistible magic. Hearing this, the Blood Demon instinctively looked toward him. At some point, Yang Kai's left eye had transformed into a golden vertical pupil. Its majestic golden light seemed capable of seizing one's soul, causing the Blood Demon's Divine Sense to tremble and leaving him unable to summon the slightest thought of resistance. Yang Kai's vast Divine Sense surged like a tide, and a budding lotus flashed at his left eye before plunging into the Blood Demon's mind.

In an instant, the Blood Demon's thoughts froze completely, and he stood there in a daze.

His Sea of Consciousness had collapsed, reducing the Blood Demon to a walking corpse.

A figure suddenly charged in from the side, eyes flashing with deep-seated hatred. In her hand was a ghastly white bone spike. The spike did not appear to have been made from the bone of a Monster Beast, but rather from the remains of a Human or Demon Race expert, refined into a Demon Artifact by some unknown method.

The bone spike gave off a remarkable aura as it stabbed viciously toward the motionless Blood Demon's head, clearly intending to kill him outright.

Yang Kai coldly snorted and struck out to the side with his palm. The Succubus was sent flying. After falling to the ground, she climbed back up, wiped the blood from the corner of her mouth, and looked at Yang Kai in confusion. "Sir?"

At this moment, the Succubus looked incomparably wretched. Her clothes were in tatters, and her originally beautiful, alluring body was covered in enormous abscesses. Many of them were still oozing bloody pus and giving off a foul stench.

The sight perfectly matched the Blood Demon's earlier insult that she was a heap of rotten meat even wild dogs would disdain.

Her strength was far inferior to the Blood Demon's. She had nearly been beaten to death by his fierce assault and then suffered a torrent of humiliation, so naturally, she wanted to avenge herself and wash away the shame.

She simply could not understand why Yang Kai had stopped her when he was clearly the one who had asked whom she wanted to kill.

"What are you doing?" Yang Kai looked at her coldly, the golden light in his left eye yet to fade. Under that eye's gaze, the Succubus felt a chill run through her whole body and hurriedly explained, "Wasn't Sir going to kill him? I came to help!"

"If I want to kill someone, I can do it myself. Why would I need your help?" Yang Kai snorted lightly.

The Succubus trembled as she said, "This humble one was wrong. Please calm your anger, Sir!"

She knew that if she wanted to survive, she had to appear obedient, so she did not dare show the slightest grievance or dissatisfaction.

Yang Kai ignored her and looked up toward Zhu Lie.

## Chapter 2721 — Couldn't Hold Back

"You're finally back. Where are the Spiritual Medicines I asked for?"

Outside the mountain gate, seeing Yang Kai return, the young man gritted his teeth and demanded.

Yang Kai chuckled and said, "There are so many types on that jade slip's list, I can't sort them all out at once. Young Brother, please come in and rest for a while. I'll find the Spiritual Medicines you need right away."

As he spoke, he opened the Grand Guardian Array and exchanged a glance with Hua Qingsi.

Hua Qingsi understood and gestured, "This way, Young Brother!"

"Hmph!" The young man snorted, swaggering into Lingxiao Palace aboard his Multi-deck Ship. Looking down at Yang Kai, he warned, "I'm telling you, don't try any tricks. The Spiritual Medicine on the list—no matter the age, type, or quantity—there can't be a single mistake, or you'll pay for it!"

"Yeah, yeah, I know!" Yang Kai nodded impatiently.

Although this matter had little to do with Lingxiao Palace, the Wenqing Sect's things were indeed in his hands now. Since the boy wanted to buy, he might as well sell them, and it would also be a good way to build rapport with Medicine Pill Valley.

"You'd better know..." The young man was about to say more when his eyes suddenly widened, staring straight at the two beautiful figures who had appeared behind Yang Kai. His eyes lit up, and he instantly put away his Multi-deck Ship. With a few steps in the air, he arrived behind Yang Kai, beaming at Ji Yao and Zhu Qing. His gaze lingered, finally settling on Zhu Qing, and he greeted her politely, "I am Li Xuan. May I ask this elder sister's name?"

He was overjoyed inside. His master hadn't let him go to Ice Heart Valley, instead sending him to this Lingxiao Palace. Who would have thought there would be so many beauties in this backwater place, one after another, dazzling to the eyes.

Especially this elder sister. That chest, that waist... tsk tsk...

The other elder sister wasn't bad either, though a bit cold.

He had originally resented his master's decision, sending his Senior Brother to Ice Heart Valley to enjoy the company of so many beauties. But now, he had no complaints at all. There might be many women in Ice Heart Valley, but not necessarily any as outstanding as these.

Yang Kai shuddered, goosebumps rising all over as he looked the boy up and down. He hadn't realized this kid was such a lecher! So young, yet already like this—what would he be like when he grew up?

Zhu Qing said nothing, her beautiful eyes fixed on Yang Kai, as if afraid he might disappear again.

The boy wasn't angry at all. Instead, he shamelessly said, "Elder sister, I'm talking to you."

Only then did Zhu Qing slowly glance at him and spit out, "Get lost!"

With those words, the boy's face instantly turned bright red.

Yang Kai thought this was bad. This Li Xuan clearly wasn't a decent person, but he was a Medicine Pill Valley disciple. Zhu Qing's words might anger him. Just as he was wondering how to smooth things over, Li Xuan took a deep breath, showing a dazed expression, and said, "Nice, nice... Elder sister, say a few more words."

Cold sweat poured down Yang Kai's back. Ji Yao and Hua Qingsi also looked at him strangely.

"Didn't you hear me tell you to get lost? Believe it or not, I'll beat you up!" Zhu Qing's eyes turned cold.

The boy clutched his chest, looking utterly satisfied, his breathing ragged. He staggered back a few steps, his already red face growing even redder.

"This kid likes that sort of thing?" Yang Kai asked Hua Qingsi in a low voice.

"How should I know?" Hua Qingsi rolled her eyes at him.

Yang Kai clicked his tongue in amazement. "The world really is full of all kinds..."

Regaining his composure, he instructed Hua Qingsi, "Sister Hua, take this Young Brother to rest for a while. I'll go sort out the medicinal herbs."

"Yes." Hua Qingsi nodded, once again gesturing to Li Xuan, "This way, Young Brother!"

"No, no, no!" Li Xuan raised a hand to Hua Qingsi, his eyes glued to Zhu Qing. "I don't want you, I want this elder sister to look after me!"

Hua Qingsi's face turned cold as she cursed inwardly. Just now this brat wanted her to help make his bed, and now he was already turning his nose up at her. What's so great about her anyway... it's just her chest... damn, it really is big!

"Are you looking for death?" Zhu Qing stared at him icily.

Li Xuan grinned brightly. "If elder sister is willing to let me die, then this Junior Brother will die for you."

The boy was young, but his glib tongue was impressive. He didn't realize that Zhu Qing truly wanted to kill him. Even if he were a direct disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, Zhu Qing would still dare to kill him.

"As you wish!" Zhu Qing snorted coldly, really about to make a move.

Yang Kai was startled and quickly shouted, "What are you doing!"

He stepped in front of Zhu Qing.

"Move aside," Zhu Qing said angrily, glaring at him.

"Oh, you dare get mad at me now!" Yang Kai glared back.

Zhu Qing's momentum instantly faltered, and she frowned. "Just move!"

Yang Kai whispered, "You can't kill this person!"

Zhu Qing scoffed, "Who can't be killed?"

"Anyway, you can't kill him. He's from Medicine Pill Valley. If you do, I'll be in trouble. Just brush him off, or ignore him if you have to."

"He has bad intentions toward me, and you want me to humor him!" Zhu Qing looked at Yang Kai, heartbroken.

"He's just got a filthy mouth, it's not like you lost anything. Last time I even... ahem..."

He thought, I did worse to you and you didn't try to kill me. Am I really that charming?

Zhu Qing blushed and turned away. "I'm not talking to him, no matter what."

"Fine, fine, just stand here and help me out. I'll sort the herbs and send him away soon."

"Hurry up," Zhu Qing grumbled.

"Qing'er is the best," Yang Kai flattered, slipping away at once.

Zhu Qing blushed again, making Li Xuan's heart race. He wished he could hold her hand and grow old together.

In a secluded spot, Yang Kai took out the jade slip Li Xuan had given him and began searching his Spatial Ring for the corresponding items.

After destroying Wenqing Sect, Yang Kai had packed up all their belongings. If they really had collected herbs for Medicine Pill Valley, he would definitely find them.

In fact, Yang Kai did find them.

Each and every item matched—not just in age and type, but even with a little extra in quantity.

There were over a thousand kinds of herbs, including some Imperial-Grade Spiritual Medicine. Yang Kai felt a bit reluctant to hand them over, since as an alchemist, he could use them himself.

But his wealth was abundant now, and he didn't care much for these Spiritual Medicines. Giving them to Medicine Pill Valley would at least save him some trouble.

Ji Yao had also said that Medicine Pill Valley wouldn't shortchange him and would pay market price.

That made sense. Medicine Pill Valley's reputation was huge, and it was tied to Great Emperor Miaodan's honor. They would never bully others or cheat him out of Source Crystals.

In less than a quarter of an hour, Yang Kai had sorted all the herbs.

He was about to hand them over and send Li Xuan away when his Transmission Compass suddenly vibrated.

Yang Kai frowned, muttered to himself, took out the Transmission Compass, and sent his Divine Sense into it. The next moment, his expression changed.

The Laws of Space surged, and he vanished on the spot. When he reappeared, he was at the mountain gate.

Looking around, he saw Li Xuan sitting on the ground, his face full of shock, clutching his cheek. A clear five-finger mark stood out on his clean face, and his mouth was full of blood.

Zhu Qing stood not far away, her icy killing intent enveloping him, making him tremble with fear.

At the brink of life and death, Li Xuan finally realized that his Medicine Pill Valley disciple's protective clothing was useless. The aura of death washed over him, leaving him cold and numb.

Ji Yao stood there coldly, looking at Li Xuan without a trace of sympathy.

Hua Qingsi held the Transmission Compass, at a loss for what to do.

Yang Kai appeared suddenly, instantly quelling Zhu Qing's anger. Only then did the Dragon girl remember Yang Kai's earlier warning, and she became uneasy.

"What happened?" Yang Kai shot Li Xuan a cold look, then turned to Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing's lips moved, but she couldn't say a word.

"Sister Hua, you tell me," Yang Kai said, turning to Hua Qingsi.

Hua Qingsi sighed. "This brat got handsy with Miss Qing. After she dodged, he pushed his luck, so she lost her temper and taught him a lesson."

As a woman, Hua Qingsi wanted to praise Zhu Qing and thought she hadn't hit him hard enough. Even if she didn't kill him, she should have crippled his cultivation. But as the Chief Steward of Lingxiao Sect, Hua Qingsi knew how much trouble this slap could bring to the sect, and she couldn't help but worry.

"Handsy?" Yang Kai raised an eyebrow.

"Nonsense! That never happened!" Li Xuan shouted, suddenly emboldened. He got up and said, "This is slander! She's an Emperor, and I'm only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. How could I possibly lay a hand on her!"

"So you know you're only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm," Yang Kai said, surprised.

This brat had acted so arrogant, as if he were an Emperor and they were at the Dao Origin Realm.

Li Xuan wiped the blood from his mouth, glared at Yang Kai, and shouted, "This woman hit me for no reason! I'm a Medicine Pill Valley disciple—hitting me is the same as hitting Medicine Pill Valley! You're the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace, I want an explanation, or I won't let this go!"

"You want an explanation?" Yang Kai narrowed his eyes at him. "What kind of explanation do you want?"

Li Xuan’s expression grew savage as he looked at Zhu Qing. “Cripple this wench’s cultivation and hand her over to me!”

The gap in strength was too great, but if her cultivation was crippled, he wouldn't have to fear her. He swore to make her pay for defying him.

Yang Kai turned to Zhu Qing. "Did you really hit him?"

Zhu Qing looked downcast and whispered, "I couldn't hold back... Sorry!"

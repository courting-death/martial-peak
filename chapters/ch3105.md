## Chapter 3105 — Who Are You?

The pressure on He Yunxiang was as heavy as a mountain, but since Yang Kai said nothing, she could only grit her teeth and keep following. She felt like a moth flying into the flame.

They sped along all the way, and only after a full half day did they stop before a solitary peak.

The peak was a hundred zhang high. A bamboo house stood at the summit, with bamboo groves covering the ground. They billowed in waves with the wind, picturesque and poetic.

The arrival of several hundred cultivators added a solemn, murderous air to this place out of nowhere. Those cultivators were clearly all from the same Sect; their movements were highly disciplined, their advance and retreat well ordered. After arriving here, they immediately spread out and surrounded the lone peak.

He Yunxiang took one look and could not help trembling in her heart, only feeling that the arrangement of these people seemed to form the beginnings of a great array. She truly did not know whom they had come out in full force to deal with, or what expert in this world was worth deploying such a massive lineup against. She could only sigh with emotion that there were indeed heavens beyond the heavens and men above men. Glancing at Yang Kai, however, she saw that he was still reclining lazily on the Star Shuttle with an indifferent look, as if the great commotion below was not worth his attention at all.

She had nearly forgotten that this Sir she followed was also unfathomable. He probably truly did not take those people below seriously.

Her heart settled slightly, and she began watching the spectacle with interest.

"It is only an ordinary matter of seeking revenge. There is nothing much to see." Yang Kai tugged Liu Yan's ear.

Liu Yan let him do as he liked and peered down with keen interest, softly saying, "The Phoenix Race has Secret Techniques that can prophesy and divine. Just now, for no reason at all, I had a sudden stirring in my heart. I feel that Master's matter may find its answer today."

Yang Kai could not help sitting upright and looking at her in surprise. "Really?"

Liu Yan said, "Either way, there is nothing to lose. Let's watch."

Yang Kai nodded slightly. His Divine Sense spread out silently and swept toward the solitary peak, only to meet an obstruction before the bamboo house, unable to advance another inch. His expression immediately turned somewhat strange.

An old man with crane-white hair and a youthful complexion stepped out through the air. His figure was thin and withered, yet his spirit was hale. A few age spots dotted his aged face, making him look as if he were nearing the end of his life, but his eyes shone with a frightening brilliance.

He stared at the bamboo hut and shouted, “Audacious madman! You actually dared to break into my Mad Dragon Pavilion and steal our Ancestral Treasure. Today, this place shall be your grave!”

"Mad Dragon Pavilion..." He Yunxiang was astonished.

It had already been half a year since she came to the Ancestral Domain. Although she had been constantly traveling under Yang Kai's instructions, she was no longer as ignorant of the Ancestral Domain as she had been at first, and she had managed to gather quite a bit of news from all sides. She had also heard the great name of Mad Dragon Pavilion. It was one of the few major forces in the entire Ancestral Domain.

No wonder there was a Third Stage Dao Origin Realm expert holding the fort, and no wonder they could dispatch so many elites. So it was Mad Dragon Pavilion!

In the Ancestral Domain, it was not as if no forces could firmly suppress Mad Dragon Pavilion, but there were absolutely not many. Just who was so audacious as to go to Mad Dragon Pavilion and steal its Ancestral Treasure? What made He Yunxiang feel even more incredulous was that this person had actually succeeded and escaped.

How much ability and courage would that require?

For a moment, He Yunxiang also became curious about the person hiding inside the bamboo house.

"You rat who hides your head and shows your tail, why have you not rolled out before this old man!" Gongyang Xi was furious. Mad Dragon Pavilion's Ancestral Treasure had been stolen without the slightest warning. If the thief had not deliberately left traces behind, they likely would not have been able to track him here.

This was simply looking down on him and the Mad Dragon Pavilion far too much. He wanted to see just who in this Ancestral Domain had the sheer nerve to be so audacious as to come and touch a tiger’s backside.

"Roll out!" The heroes of Mad Dragon Pavilion were filled with righteous anger and shouted.

A voice drifted lazily out of the bamboo house. "This Lord merely took back his own thing. How can that be called stealing? You trash had best be careful when speaking. This Lord is in a good mood today and does not have much killing intent, so all of you should hurry and flee for your lives."

Gongyang Xi laughed in his fury. "Shameless boasting!"

Who was this fellow? His tone was higher than the Heavens. Did he truly take himself for something? Mad Dragon Pavilion had stood in the Ancestral Domain for several tens of thousands of years without falling. Its inheritance was powerful, and its disciples were numerous and flourishing. He himself even possessed a peak cultivation at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. When had he ever feared anyone?

Moreover, what this person said was complete nonsense. That Ancestral Treasure was a treasure passed down by the founding Ancestor, and by his generation, more than thirty generations had already gone by over several tens of thousands of years. How could it possibly belong to someone else?

"Sir?" He Yunxiang had also been astonished by that person's arrogant tone, which was simply far too contemptuous of everyone else, but then she suddenly saw Yang Kai stand up from the Star Shuttle. His expression, which had always been calm and composed, unchanged even if a great mountain collapsed before him, had for the first time become incomparably solemn. He stared fixedly at the bamboo house, as if facing a great enemy.

What was going on? He Yunxiang was full of doubts.

Just how great Yang Kai's ability was, she could not fathom, because ever since she had started following him, she had never seen him use his true strength. Everyone he had killed, whether Void King Realm or Dao Origin Realm, had basically died in one move. When facing enemies who could be killed in a single move, how would he ever need to use his full strength?

He Yunxiang had guessed at Yang Kai's cultivation in her heart. The greatest possibility was the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm! Otherwise, he could not have killed those First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivators as easily as slaughtering chickens and dogs.

Such cultivation, looking across the entire Ancestral Domain, was also among the very top. No matter where he went or whom he faced, he would have ample confidence.

Such an expert would actually show an expression as if facing a great enemy? Just who was the person inside that bamboo house...

"Master?" Liu Yan also sensed that something was wrong and raised her head to look up at him.

Yang Kai swallowed, reached out to stroke her head, and said with difficulty, “It seems you were right. The thing I have been searching for may truly appear today.”

Liu Yan's sudden stirring of the heart had indeed lit a guiding lamp for him, but this lamp was far too dazzling, and he did not know whether it might burn his eyes blind.

"Young Friend has also come here. Truly, life is full of unexpected meetings!" The voice inside the bamboo house rang out once more.

Yang Kai sniffed. If he had originally been three parts suspicious and seven parts astonished, then the moment these words came out, he could confirm the guess in his heart. The fellow inside the bamboo house was precisely the person he had thought of.

He had actually come here!

Before such an expert, there was no need to conceal their traces. When the three of them arrived here, the other party had probably already discovered them.

He simply revealed himself and shouted, “Hey, you thief! I do not care if you have three heads and six arms, but to actually steal another Sect’s Ancestral Treasure is simply disgraceful and utterly lacking the bearing of an expert. Had this Young Master not run into this matter, then so be it, but since I have seen it, I naturally must draw my blade to aid the wronged.” Turning his head, he cupped his fists to the old man from the Mad Dragon Pavilion and said, “Old Sir, although this matter has nothing to do with me, meeting is fate. If you have any instructions, please say them freely. This one has no other hobbies, but has always loved standing up against injustice.” His face was full of righteous awe, and every word rang with conviction.

Old... Old Sir! Gongyang Xi could not help feeling somewhat thrown off. Ever since he took over Mad Dragon Pavilion, when he walked the Ancestral Domain, who would not politely call him Pavilion Master Gongyang upon seeing him? Now someone had actually called him Old Sir, and his expression immediately became strange. It was not appropriate for him to flare up, but his face still turned somewhat ugly.

The person inside the bamboo house fell silent for a moment, then his voice came out again. "Does Young Friend intend to pretend not to know this Lord?"

"Who are you?"

And who are you! Gongyang Xi was left somewhat speechless by this young man. Although he had long since discovered him following along the whole way, he had important matters on hand and had not paid much attention. He had not expected Yang Kai to actually meddle in his affairs.

Standing up against injustice, my ass. Gongyang Xi was no fool, so how could he believe such nonsense? Moreover, judging from the look of things, this young man and the person inside the bamboo hut seemed to be old acquaintances, only their relationship was not very good.

Wishing to avoid unnecessary complications, Gongyang Xi still returned him a half salute. "Many thanks for Young Friend's good intentions, but this matter is my Mad Dragon Pavilion's family affair. It is inconvenient for outsiders to interfere, so please withdraw to the side, Young Friend. Otherwise, blades and swords have no eyes, and it would not be good if you were wounded in the chaos." He was also wary that Yang Kai and the person inside the bamboo house were accomplices and did not want to let him interfere.

"Mm!" Yang Kai nodded deeply. "Since Old Sir says so, then I will stand to the side for now." This was just right. He could use Mad Dragon Pavilion to probe that person's depths.

Since he had come to this lower plane, he had to suffer the suppression and rejection of the World Laws, so that fellow was presumably the same, right? When they fought in a moment, he should be able to make out some clues. Afraid that Gongyang Xi would underestimate the enemy, he solemnly said, "Old Sir, I am not being alarmist, but that fellow gives off a very dangerous feeling. You had better be careful, and do not capsize in a gutter."

Gongyang Xi frowned slightly and said indifferently, "Many thanks for Young Friend's warning."

But his expression showed he did not take it very seriously. He was at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm and already stood at the very peak of the Ancestral Domain. Even if there were people in this world stronger than him, they could not possibly be stronger by much. Now that the Mad Dragon Pavilion’s elites had come out in full force, who in this world could be their opponent? Even if it was a stone, today they would beat the shit out of it.

Turning his head, he said with a heavy expression, "It seems Your Excellency refuses a toast only to drink a forfeit. This old man will ask one more time. Are you returning the thing or not?"

"This Lord has said that this thing was mine to begin with. Stop making such a racket. It is tiresome."

“Good, good, good. Since that is the case, do not blame this old man for bullying the young. Come, hand over your life!” Gongyang Xi’s Origin Qi suddenly surged violently, his hair flying wildly. With a leap, he pounced toward the wooden hut like an enraged ram, his might blazing and his arrogance soaring to the heavens.

There was not the slightest reaction from inside.

Gongyang Xi’s caution proved useless. He charged straight to the front of the wooden hut, raised his hand, and struck toward the bamboo hut. “Hiding like this is disgraceful. Get out here!”

Creak...

The bamboo house's door suddenly opened wide, and a gale swept out from within. Gongyang Xi's eyes narrowed, and a trace of horror that was difficult to conceal abruptly surfaced on his face. Under the dumbstruck gazes of the several hundred Mad Dragon Pavilion elites, he plunged headfirst into the bamboo house.

Hong...

The door closed, but no movement came from inside. There were not even any traces of a fight. It was as quiet as if Heaven and Earth had just opened and primal chaos had only just begun.

Pair after pair of eyes widened. Everyone held their breath and pricked up their ears, but the scene was strangely, incomparably silent. This silence made a thick horror rise involuntarily in their hearts.

In that instant, that bamboo house seemed like an ancient vicious beast, swallowing Gongyang Xi into its belly.

## Chapter 2716 — Chief Formation Master

Ji Yao ignored him, turning away and showing Yang Kai the back of her head.

Yang Kai set down his teacup, at a loss for words, feeling awkward. He turned to Bing Yun to change the subject, "Senior, did you need something from me?"

Bing Yun nodded and said, “Are you familiar with the two great sects, Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace?”

"I think I've heard of them." Yang Kai frowned in thought, then clapped his hands. "Aren't they sects from the Northern Territory?"

He hadn’t spent much time in the Northern Territory, nor had he traveled widely there, so he wasn’t as familiar with it as he was with the Southern Territory. The only places he really knew were Ice Heart Valley and Wenqing Sect.

At first, the names Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace didn’t ring a bell, but after thinking for a moment, he remembered that these two sects were just as powerful and deep-rooted as Wenqing Sect. They were also top sects in the Northern Territory, each with Emperor Realm experts overseeing them.

Yang Kai frowned, "Senior, why bring up these two sects all of a sudden? Is there something I should be aware of?"

Now that he had established his own sect in the Northern Territory, and since Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace were also top sects here, it was inevitable that they would interact in the future—maybe even compete or clash.

They were different from Ice Heart Valley. Ice Heart Valley had deep ties with Yang Kai, so there was no chance of conflict.

Bing Yun said, "Looking at the entire Star Realm, the Eastern Territory has the Soul Palace of Great Emperor Youhun, the Southern Territory has the Star God Palace of Radiant Moon Great Emperor, and even the Western Territory has the Shadow Slaughter Palace of the Night Shadow Great Emperor. All the top sects in these regions bow to the Great Emperor sects, obeying their orders and authority. Only our Northern Territory has no Great Emperor sect."

"Why is that?" Yang Kai asked curiously. He had never thought about it before, but now that Bing Yun mentioned it, he realized it was true.

Bing Yun shook his head. “I’m not sure either. Perhaps it’s because much of the Northern Territory is simply too cold, not as rich in resources as other regions, and not really suited for founding great sects.” He paused, then continued, “Still, while our Northern Territory has no Great Emperor sect, we do have a Great Emperor—Great Emperor Miaodan.”

Yang Kai nodded slightly. He was aware of this.

Great Emperor Miaodan had lived in the Medicine Pill Valley of the Northern Territory for many years. In fact, Yang Kai had once inherited the legacy left behind by Gongsun Mu, the third disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan. Without that, he wouldn’t have such a deep understanding of the Star Realm’s herbs. It was thanks to the jade slips and pill recipes left by Gongsun Mu that Yang Kai was able to excel in the Dao of Alchemy.

However, when he received that inheritance, Gongsun Mu had also placed a restriction in his body. To remove it, Yang Kai would have to reach the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm within a hundred years, or seek out Great Emperor Miaodan to have it lifted.

Gongsun Mu had done this simply to have Yang Kai deliver a message to Great Emperor Miaodan, letting him know that he had realized his mistakes and asking the Great Emperor to take him back as a disciple.

But since the restriction hadn’t caused Yang Kai any trouble over the years, he hadn’t bothered with it. There was still plenty of time before the hundred-year deadline, and he was confident he could reach the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm by then.

There was no need to trouble Great Emperor Miaodan over it.

Bing Yun said, "For many years, the four top sects of the Northern Territory have maintained a balance. I was missing for years, and though Ice Heart Valley suffered some suppression, we managed to hold on. But now, that balance has been broken."

The broken balance was, without a doubt, the destruction of Wenqing Sect.

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. "So what?"

Bing Yun chuckled. "For Ice Heart Valley, it doesn’t matter. Wenqing Sect tried to destroy us and was destroyed in turn—they brought it on themselves. But for Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace, it’s a problem."

Yang Kai’s expression turned cold. "Are they looking for trouble now?"

Bing Yun said, "If you could destroy Wenqing Sect, there’s no reason you couldn’t destroy Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace. It’s only natural for them to be worried."

"So what do they want? Join forces against me?"

Bing Yun smiled. "When the lips are gone, the teeth feel the cold. Banding together for warmth is only natural. Lately, those two sects have grown very close—there may be some action soon."

Yang Kai sneered, "Are they really not afraid I’ll wipe them out too?"

Bing Yun’s expression grew serious. "People die for wealth, birds for food. You destroyed Wenqing Sect and took over their vast foundation and stores of tens of thousands of years. Anyone would be envious. But maybe they’re just joining forces to make you wary, to keep you from getting too ambitious and trying to unite the Northern Territory."

"I have no such intention." Yang Kai shook his head. He only kept Wenqing Sect’s foundation for the sake of his homeland’s people. He had no thoughts of uniting the Northern Territory—without the prestige of a Great Emperor, it was impossible anyway.

Bing Yun had only called Yang Kai over to give him a heads-up. Seeing that he had his own plans, she said nothing more.

Suddenly, Yang Kai seemed to remember something. "When Wenqing Sect made things difficult for Ice Heart Valley last time, did those two sects help them?"

Bing Yun looked at him and asked, "What do you think?"

Yang Kai understood. Even if those two sects hadn’t helped openly, they had probably fanned the flames in secret. Otherwise, how could Wenqing Sect have gathered so many helpers at once? Some of them were likely disciples from those two sects.

Truly despicable. If he’d known, he wouldn’t have let them off so easily—he should have investigated more thoroughly.

Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace were both top sects in the Northern Territory, so they must have plenty of stores.

Open and hidden struggles between sects were normal. They had helped Wenqing Sect try to destroy Ice Heart Valley, so they clearly didn’t care about balance. Now that he had taken over Wenqing Sect’s territory and shown his strength, they were the ones getting nervous.

If they left him alone, fine. But if they provoked him, he’d make them pay—Yang Kai vowed to himself.

After lingering in Ice Heart Valley for a while longer, Yang Kai finally took his leave.

When he tried to say goodbye to Ji Yao, she didn’t respond at all, leaving Yang Kai rather frustrated. He had no idea how he’d offended her.

Stepping out of the main hall, he found snowflakes drifting down outside. Zhu Qing stood there, wrapped in silver, not using her Emperor Yuan to block the cold. At her feet was a snowman as tall as a person—hideous beyond words.

"Have you been standing here this whole time?" Yang Kai asked curiously. He had told her to wait, but hadn’t meant for her to stand there without moving.

Zhu Qing didn’t answer. She pointed at the ugly snowman. "That’s you!"

"Screw you!" Yang Kai rolled his eyes at her, kicked the snowman to pieces, and walked off with his hands behind his back.

Zhu Qing turned and followed him.

Back at the Space Array on the small island in the ice lake, Nanmen Dajun was still waiting, looking hesitant and indecisive.

Yang Kai said impatiently, "If you’re a man, be decisive! You’ll never amount to anything if you keep dithering!"

Nanmen Dajun snapped, "I’m a grandmaster of formations! How can you insult me like this!"

Yang Kai sneered, pointing at the Space Array. "Set one up, and I’ll admit you’re a formation grandmaster!"

Nanmen Dajun instantly fell silent, his face turning bright red.

Yang Kai said irritably, "Forget it. My Lingxiao Palace doesn’t need a half-baked formation master like you." He turned and stepped onto the Space Array.

Suddenly, Nanmen Dajun grabbed his clothes.

Yang Kai turned to look at him. Nanmen Dajun, face flushed, raised a finger. "Ten years! I’ll leave Lingxiao Palace in ten years!"

Yang Kai snorted. "Still trying to bargain with me?"

"Just ten years! Not a day more." Nanmen Dajun was resolute.

"Fine, ten years it is! Get on!" Yang Kai beckoned.

Nanmen Dajun let out a heavy sigh and stepped onto the Space Array, looking utterly dejected, as if he’d just been sold off.

Returning to Lingxiao Palace, Yang Kai found no sign of the three Yao Kings—they had vanished, off somewhere seeking their own amusements.

There wasn’t even a guard by the Space Array.

This made Yang Kai frown. While the Space Array he’d set up was generally safe and couldn’t be invaded, there should at least be someone watching over it.

There needed to be a steward. Several faces flashed through Yang Kai’s mind, and he made a decision.

"Go find yourself a place to stay," Yang Kai instructed Nanmen Dajun.

Nanmen Dajun nodded. "By the way, Palace Master, what’s my position in Lingxiao Palace?"

"Chief Formation Master, didn’t I tell you?" Yang Kai waved his hand. "When you’re free, walk around and see which formations need reinforcing or rebuilding—do as you see fit."

"Alright, alright!" Nanmen Dajun nodded enthusiastically.

Yang Kai turned and pointed at Zhu Qing. "You, stop following me."

The Laws of Space surged, and he vanished in a flash.

Zhu Qing stomped her foot and swept the area with her Divine Sense, but couldn’t sense where Yang Kai had gone.

Arriving at the palace on the main peak, Yang Kai slipped inside and sat down cross-legged.

Although Lingxiao Palace was still sparsely populated, it finally had some life. Most importantly, the arts of puppetry and formations were now secured. When the disciples from his homeland arrived, they could learn these two skills. In the long run, this would greatly benefit both the palace’s development and the disciples’ growth.

As for Nanmen Dajun’s ten-year term, Yang Kai didn’t take it seriously at all.

Who knew what might happen in ten years? By then, Nanmen Dajun might not even be able to leave if he wanted to.

Feeling refreshed, Yang Kai took out a large pile of Upper-Level Source Crystals from his Spatial Ring. Instantly, the room was filled with rolling Spiritual Qi. Now that he was in the Emperor Realm, Lower-Level and Middle-Level Source Crystals were too inefficient for cultivation—only Upper-Level Source Crystals were worth using.

At present, Yang Kai had more than a billion Upper-Level Source Crystals on hand—he was practically swimming in wealth. The cost of cultivation was nothing to him now.

His entire body crackled as his meridians and flesh pulsed, and a torrent of Spiritual Qi poured into him for refining and absorption.

Not only that, he also took out the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome and began refining it with divided attention.

The Golden Armored Heavenly Tome was quite powerful. Refining it sooner would help him improve his strength and give him more means in future battles, so Yang Kai wasn’t about to neglect it.

But refining this thing was no easy task—it couldn’t be completed in a short time.

Time slipped by. With every breath, Spiritual Qi poured into Yang Kai’s body. His Emperor Yuan and Divine Sense constantly invaded the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, trying to leave his own Soul Brand on it.

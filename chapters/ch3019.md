## Chapter 3019 — Someone Has Broken into Dragon Isle

The existence of a maze within a Void Gap was already inconceivable enough.

Yet at this moment, there was actually a humanoid projection inside the maze, which was even more unbelievable.

Who was this person? Or rather, who had left behind this projection?

Yang Kai and Li Jiao both stared wide-eyed in that direction, but to their disappointment, no matter how hard they looked, they could not make out the projection's true appearance.

This projected image had evidently existed for many years. Though the Secret Technique itself was immensely powerful, after enduring the influence of the Void Gap for so long, it had become somewhat unstable. The figure flickered and wavered, stretching tall one moment and shrinking into a squat shape the next, as though it might collapse at any instant.

For some reason, however, Yang Kai felt that the figure looked familiar, as though he had seen it somewhere before.

"Brother Li, have you seen this person before?" Yang Kai asked softly.

Li Jiao shook his head. "I can't tell, but this person is definitely not weak."

Although the projection was unstable, the aura it occasionally revealed in fleeting flashes was extremely powerful. This person was clearly no ordinary Emperor Realm cultivator.

Yang Kai nodded. Images of the experts he had met constantly flashed through his mind as he tried to compare them with the figure before him.

Just then, however, the projection suddenly expanded and contracted before transforming into a fist-sized, luminous white orb that slowly drifted in a certain direction.

Yang Kai had no time to think it over. He tugged Li Jiao and said, "Follow it!"

Li Jiao was taken aback. "Follow him?"

"Mm," Yang Kai replied, feeling that the appearance of this figure might be a turning point. He had searched here for a month without finding a way out of the Void Maze, so he could only treat a dead horse as though it were still alive and see what this orb of light was up to.

Without any further hesitation, he followed closely behind the orb.

The route taken by the orb was even more erratic and unpredictable than Yang Kai's earlier path. It seemed aimless, but careful observation revealed a discernible pattern.

Yang Kai secretly grew excited, feeling that his guess had indeed been correct.

At this point, all he wanted to know was who had left behind that projected image, so he kept observing the sphere of light. Yet he discovered very little. The sphere had been created by a projection Secret Technique, but after so many years under the influence of the Power of Space within the Void Gap, it had long since become distorted beyond recognition. Trying to trace it back to its origin was therefore exceedingly difficult for Yang Kai.

They continued walking for several days, and Li Jiao had already fallen into despair.

Yang Kai's heart was also filled with uncertainty, and several times he considered giving up. However, it would have been a pity to stop halfway, so he could only grit his teeth and persevere.

At a certain moment, the orb suddenly accelerated and shot straight in one direction.

Seeing this, Yang Kai hurriedly chased after it.

When it reached a certain point in the void, the orb suddenly burst apart without warning, transforming into motes of light that vanished.

"Uh..." Li Jiao stared blankly.

They had followed this orb for several days, never expecting such an outcome. Li Jiao almost could not stop himself from cursing aloud. Had they not simply wasted all that time? With this, he suddenly lost confidence in Yang Kai as well, feeling that the two of them were destined to remain trapped here for the rest of their lives and would probably never leave this maze.

However, when he turned to look at Yang Kai, he saw that Yang Kai was actually beaming with delight, as though he had discovered something extraordinary.

"Palace Master Yang?" Li Jiao called out.

Yang Kai turned to him and said, "I found it!"

"You found it?" Li Jiao frowned and instinctively asked, "Found what?"

"What do you think?"

Li Jiao was startled, then immediately became excited. "You mean... you found the exit?"

"Mm." Yang Kai nodded heavily, then thought for a moment and said, "When I open the exit, follow me inside immediately. If we delay, the people on Dragon Isle may notice something."

"Good!" Li Jiao could not help swallowing hard.

They had found it just like that? After following the orb for several days, they had actually found the exit? What the hell was that orb, and why had it guided them to the exit?

None of that mattered anymore!

What mattered was that he was about to set foot on Dragon Isle and fulfil his lifelong wish! Li Jiao felt excited, apprehensive, expectant, and uneasy...

While his emotions churned, Yang Kai's expression had already become solemn, and the Laws of Space surged violently.

He thrust both hands into the void ahead and then tore outward with all his strength.

A strange sight appeared. As though something had been ripped apart, a huge opening suddenly appeared in the void ahead.

"Go!" Yang Kai shouted.

Li Jiao plunged headfirst into the opening, with Yang Kai following close behind. The two vanished from the endless void in an instant.

In the Eastern Sea of the Eastern Territory, on an island rich with Spiritual Qi and blessed with natural beauty, like an Immortal paradise...

A man dressed in white stood in the void with his hands clasped behind his back.

Beside him stood a beautiful woman in a black palace dress, her arms folded beneath her ample breasts and pressing them slightly out of shape. Her palace dress was slit up to just below her thigh, leaving most of one shapely leg exposed. Her fair, smooth skin gleamed invitingly like fine white jade.

If the men of the world saw this sight, they would surely be utterly captivated.

The man was handsome and distinguished, while the woman was voluptuous and graceful. Simply standing there, they formed an eye-catching sight.

Both of them gazed into the distance. Atop a surging volcanic crater, a tiny figure sat cross-legged. The scorching heat waves did not harm her in the slightest, but instead continuously surged toward her childlike body and were completely absorbed.

"Feng'er, did this girl really fuse with the True Phoenix Fire?" the white-robed man suddenly asked.

The beautiful woman in the palace dress whom he had called Feng'er replied, "Yes, and the fusion was perfect."

The white-robed man said, "Isn't she an Artifact Spirit? Although she is a fire-type Artifact Spirit and her attributes do not conflict with the True Phoenix Fire, her body is not made of flesh and blood. How could she fuse with the True Phoenix Fire?"

The beautiful woman in the palace dress replied, "Don't underestimate her body. Although it is not made of flesh and blood, its malleability and potential for growth are both extraordinary. That is a Spirit Puppet body, and a second one cannot be found anywhere in the world."

"You think highly of her?" The white-robed man turned his head to look at the woman beside him.

The beautiful woman in the palace dress smiled faintly. "Her future achievements will definitely be no lower than mine."

The white-robed man nodded. "In any case, congratulations are in order. Your Phoenix Race has gained another member."

The beautiful woman pursed her lips in an enchanting smile.

Just then, the white-robed man suddenly frowned and revealed a surprised expression.

"What is it?" The beautiful woman looked at him in astonishment. At their level of cultivation, very few things could surprise them, yet the white-robed man's expression clearly indicated that something unexpected and significant had occurred.

“You may not believe this,” the man in white said, turning to look at her with an expression of utter disbelief, “but someone has broken into Dragon Isle!”

The beautiful woman froze for a moment, then giggled. "Which fellow is so tired of living? What terrible luck."

She did not ask how the white-robed man knew, because she knew he had investigated Dragon Isle many years ago and left a strand of his Soul in that Void Maze.

That maze was not easy to unravel. Even the white-robed man had been unable to find an exit back then, so in the end, he could only use a Secret Technique to leave behind a strand of his Soul, mainly intending to rely on its power to slowly unravel the maze.

More than a dozen years had passed, so it should have found some clues by now.

The fact that the white-robed man had received this information clearly meant it had come from that strand of his Soul. In other words, the Soul left in the maze had already dissipated completely.

"If it really is the person I think it is, you know him too," the white-robed man said with a strange expression.

He could not be certain whether the person who had broken into Dragon Isle was truly the one he had in mind. After all, the information transmitted back when the strand of his Soul shattered was incomplete, so he was only sixty to seventy percent certain.

"I know him too?" The beautiful woman in the palace dress was startled. "Who?"

"He has something to do with Young Lady."

The beautiful woman in the palace dress frowned.

"And with her!" The white-robed man pointed toward the tiny figure at the volcanic crater.

"It's him?" The beautiful woman in the palace dress was dumbfounded. The white-robed man's hints were so obvious that she would have been an idiot if she still could not guess who it was.

She had simply never imagined that the person breaking into Dragon Isle would be that fellow.

"How could it be him?" The beautiful woman found it difficult to believe. "What madness possessed him to force his way into Dragon Isle?"

The white-robed man shrugged, looking as though he had no idea either.

"You led him inside?" the beautiful woman asked.

The man replied, "It wasn't me. It was that strand of my Soul."

The beautiful woman said, "What nonsense! What kind of place do you think Dragon Isle is? With his meagre strength, how could entering possibly end well for him? He'll probably be beaten to a pulp immediately."

The man replied, "I left that Soul strand behind more than a dozen years ago. I didn't know him at the time, so naturally, the Soul strand I left behind didn't know him either. It led him inside only to muddy the waters on Dragon Isle and see whether it could create an opportunity for Honored Master. It would naturally be good if he could cause some trouble, but it doesn't matter if he can't."

"If something happens to him, Xiaoqi definitely won't let you off. You know how important he is to her. She has been clamouring to go to the Southern Territory and find him for the past few years. If Honored Master hadn't confined her, she probably would have run off long ago."

The man broke out in a cold sweat. "Don't tell her."

The beautiful woman replied, "There are no secrets that remain hidden forever."

The man asked, "Then what should we do now? He's already inside, and if he was going to die, he may already be dead."

The beautiful woman pondered for a moment before saying, "Still, this really is an opportunity. If we can use it to infiltrate Dragon Isle, that may not be a bad thing... I'll report to Honored Master and see what he decides."

"Mm." The man nodded.

The beautiful woman's figure flickered, transforming into a streak of fire that shot into the distance as swiftly as thunder.

"Were you talking about Master just now?" a childish voice suddenly asked.

The corner of the white-robed man's mouth twitched. Lowering his head, he saw that at some point, a little girl of seven or eight had appeared before him. Her bright, sparkling eyes stared fixedly at him without blinking.

The little girl was exquisitely beautiful, and anyone could tell at a glance that she would grow into a devastating beauty.

However, the white-robed man knew that she was no little girl at all. He looked up at the sky with a world-weary expression and said, "The wind is rather noisy today."

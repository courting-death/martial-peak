## Chapter 2904 — Twisted Time

After some time, Yang Kai finally managed to reseal his own Demonic Qi and return to normal.

But this action once again exhausted all the sealing power that the Firmament Tree had painstakingly accumulated. In the medicine garden of the Small Xuan Realm, the Firmament Tree no longer showed any gold or silver hues, and instead looked somewhat withered. Clearly, Yang Kai had drawn too much power from it earlier.

With a slight sigh, Yang Kai reached out and summoned a jade bottle. Inside, a drop of liquid, like mercury, slowly rolled around.

Taiyi Divine Water! This was a treasure Yang Kai had recently exchanged from an Emperor Realm expert who had attended the grand ceremony at Qingyang Shrine. He hadn’t expected it to come in handy so soon.

Taiyi Divine Water could stimulate Spirit Plants, greatly shortening the growth period of Spirit Objects. However, this thing was extremely rare and valuable, and few people could ever obtain it. With the Firmament Tree’s sealing power depleted, Yang Kai had to plan ahead and use this Taiyi Divine Water to replenish it.

Otherwise, if he was ever forced to enter Demonic State again, he would have no extra sealing power left to resolve his own crisis.

After several bouts of entering Demonic State, Yang Kai found that the amount of sealing power required was increasing each time.

By his estimation, he could probably only Enter Demonic State two or three more times at most before he would no longer be able to seal the ancient demonic aura within his body. If that happened, he might permanently become a demon and lose his mind completely.

Perhaps… the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique was a way to delay that outcome. This time, while entering Demonic State, he maintained the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, and his mind was much clearer than the previous two times. It showed that the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique had some restraining effect on the Demonic State—at the very least, it allowed him to maintain his sanity.

Should he find Zhu Qing and have her take him to Dragon Isle? There were plenty of Dragon Blood Flowers on Dragon Isle, and if he collected them, he could refine them into Dragon Blood Pills to help him perfect the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique.

However, Zhu Qing had gone to the Frozen Earth in the north and hadn’t been heard from since. He didn’t know whether she was alive or dead.

Shaking his head, Yang Kai stopped thinking about it and checked his own condition again.

His physical body wasn’t in too bad a state. After resolving the Demonic Qi crisis, he was just a bit weak, and a few days of rest would be enough to recover.

It was the wounds on his Divine Soul that left Yang Kai feeling rather alarmed.

In that Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, although everything he experienced was an illusion, his Divine Soul had participated in it. In such a vivid environment, Yang Kai had completely forgotten his true identity and regarded himself as Shaman Niu.

Any harm suffered by Shaman Niu would be directly reflected on his Divine Soul.

The earth-shattering battle with Mo Duo had already left him badly injured, and at the last moment, being struck across space by the Great Demon God and having his soul scattered was an unimaginable ordeal. Yang Kai suspected that if not for the protection of the Warm Spirit Lotus, he would have died in the illusion.

It was the Warm Spirit Lotus, this supreme treasure of heaven and earth, that protected his Divine Soul and allowed him to return to reality. Anyone else would surely not have awakened from such an ordeal.

All illusions have the power to pass for reality, let alone a top-tier secret realm like the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape.

Only then did Yang Kai feel a belated sense of dread wash over him. At the same time, a wave of indescribable exhaustion swept through his entire being, making him wish he could collapse and fall asleep on the spot.

Still, he remembered that this was someone else’s territory, and he had spent more than two years inside the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, completely breaking his agreement with the Star God Palace. He didn’t know what kind of questioning he would face after coming out.

Spreading his palm, he saw that the butterfly-shaped key was still there. The cold touch told Yang Kai that everything he had experienced was not a dream, but had truly happened.

Die had transformed into a butterfly before his eyes, and then into this key.

He called out softly, but received no response.

The vividly lifelike Die in the illusion was now a cold, lifeless object, devoid of any spirit. Yang Kai couldn’t see anything special about it. At this moment, the butterfly key looked like a trinket from a street stall—aside from its novel shape, there was nothing remarkable about it.

Still, this was a secret realm! With it, Lingxiao Palace’s future development would be even better.

Yang Kai gripped it and then stored it in the Small Xuan Realm.

Rising slowly and surveying his surroundings, Yang Kai realized he seemed to be in a grand hall that looked somewhat familiar. The hall was empty except for a portal emitting a faint light not far away. After thinking carefully, Yang Kai couldn’t recall ever coming to this place before.

However, that glowing portal should be the exit.

He took a deep breath, then, with unsteady steps, walked forward.

As he passed through the doorway, his vision swam for a moment, and he arrived in another place.

Fierce waves of power surged all around, startling Yang Kai. He instinctively thought he was about to be attacked, but the next moment, a familiar voice reached his ears: “Senior Brother Yang, you’re out?”

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes slightly and looked in the direction of the voice. A beautiful, refined, and fair face came into view.

He couldn’t help but feel dazed, as if he hadn’t yet shaken off the identity of Shaman Niu. Two years was neither long nor short, but in that ancient world, Yang Kai had experienced the cycle from weakness to strength, from life to death. It was an extraordinary life experience that dulled his memories of other things. At this moment, the boundary between reality and illusion was blurred.

“Princess… Lan Xun?” When Yang Kai saw the person’s face clearly, a name popped into his mind. That name gradually overlapped with the figure before him, finally confirming her identity.

The smile on Lan Xun’s face slowly faded. She clearly sensed Yang Kai’s abnormality and asked with concern, “Senior Brother Yang, are you hurt?”

At the same time, Lan Xun also noticed Yang Kai’s sense of distance. It wasn’t deliberate, but unconsciously revealed, as if they were meeting for the first time and were not familiar with each other.

This struck Lan Xun as odd. Although she and Yang Kai had never been particularly close, they had interacted several times. Last time, when the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome was reclaimed, she had handled it herself. By rights, Yang Kai shouldn’t be acting this way toward her.

All around, dignified gazes swept over Yang Kai, scrutinizing him up and down. They found nothing unusual, only that he seemed excessively weak, likely badly injured.

That was only natural. The Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was the foundation of the Star God Palace. While disciples could gain great benefits from training inside, there was always a certain degree of danger. Every few years, some disciples would perish within.

Yang Kai’s injuries and weakness were nothing out of the ordinary.

“Just a minor injury, nothing serious.” Yang Kai forced a smile. As his memories gradually returned, he shook off Shaman Niu’s influence and looked at Lan Xun, saying, “Junior Sister Lan, have you been waiting here for me?”

Hearing him call her Junior Sister again, Lan Xun smiled and replied, “I only just came out myself. I wasn’t waiting for you on purpose.”

Yang Kai was taken aback. “Junior Sister Lan, you just came out?”

He couldn’t help but feel confused. He had spent over two years in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, so what had happened to Lan Xun? She had only just come out. Suddenly, Yang Kai seemed to realize something and turned to ask, “Where are Brother Lei and Brother Xiao?”

He remembered that many people had entered the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda for training. Originally, the pagoda wasn’t supposed to open yet, but because of his agreement, it had opened early, allowing many Star God Palace disciples to benefit and enter ahead of time.

By now, quite a few people had come out, gathering in small groups and chatting quietly. Some looked happy, clearly having gained much, while others looked disappointed, likely having gained nothing and wasted the opportunity. Seeing Yang Kai and Lan Xun talking, many Star God Palace disciples glared at him, as if they wanted to beat him up.

Yang Kai didn’t see Lei Ting or Xiao Chen.

Lan Xun said, “They’re still inside. They should be coming out soon. The pagoda is about to close, so they won’t be able to stay even if they want to.”

Yang Kai was slightly surprised and asked casually, “How long has it been since we entered the pagoda?”

Lan Xun looked at him curiously, not knowing why he asked, but still replied, “Today makes exactly one month.”

One month…

Yang Kai could hardly believe his ears.

He had clearly spent over two years in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, but in reality… it seemed only five days had passed.

He remembered that when he entered the Fifth Stage secret realm, there were only five days left before the deadline. One month referred to the time from the opening to the closing of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda.

He subconsciously clenched his palm, but it was already empty.

The Thousand Illusion Dreamscape actually had the miraculous effect of stretching time. Five short days were magnified into two years—this was simply unbelievable. Even if it was just an illusion, it was not something ordinary means could achieve.

He had already thought the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape was extraordinary, but now he realized he had underestimated this secret realm. Just the involvement of the Laws of Time made its value immeasurable.

Yang Kai’s silence made Lan Xun a bit worried. “Senior Brother Yang, are you alright?”

“I’m fine, just a little tired.” Yang Kai forced a smile, but it was extremely strained. He truly was exhausted—his Divine Soul wounded, his body fatigued—neither could recover in a short time.

He suspected this exhaustion was because he had done two years’ worth of things in just five days.

To keep Lan Xun from noticing anything, Yang Kai changed the subject: “What are Elder Xiao and the others doing?”

When Yang Kai first left the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, he noticed that several Third Stage Emperor Realm elders from the Star God Palace seemed to be working together to cast a spell, and their target was the pagoda itself. Judging by the looks on Xiao Yuyang and the others’ faces, this had been going on for some time. Even with their deep reserves of strength, they were struggling to hold on.

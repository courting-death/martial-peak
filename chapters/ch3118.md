## Chapter 3118 — Returning Home

"Congratulations." Ruan Biting regained her composure and looked at Su Yan.

The disciple she had taken in now possessed cultivation on par with her own. Ruan Biting felt only delight, for this proved her judgment back then had been right.

Su Yan said, "It is all thanks to Master's protection over these years." If Ruan Biting had not helped her from time to time during the past decade, secretly sending her Spiritual Pills and wondrous medicines, Su Yan's cultivation might have declined. She certainly could not have kept her realm steady in this place so rich in Fire Qi.

"Many thanks, Elder Ruan!" Yang Kai bowed respectfully, his expression sincere. Ruan Biting had been nothing but good to Su Yan. Over the past few days, Yang Kai had also learned a little about her from Su Yan, so his gratitude was only natural.

Ruan Biting waved a hand. "There is no need for that. Su Yan is my disciple; of course I should look after her. I only regret being so indecisive back then and failing to take her away from Yunxia Sect, wasting ten years of her life for nothing. Now, at least, her suffering has finally come to an end. Otherwise, my guilt would have been great." She turned to Yang Kai. "Treat her well. My cultivation may be inferior to yours, but if you ever wrong her, I will never let it rest."

Yang Kai grinned and drew a hand across his own neck. "If that day truly comes, this boy will offer up his head. Senior will not need to act." Although his cultivation was higher than hers, she was Su Yan's Master after all, so calling her Senior was only proper.

Ruan Biting smiled. "I will remember those words."

Su Yan seemed to sense something. "Master, where are you going?"

Ruan Biting took her hand and sighed. "Yunxia Sect is gone, and before this, Han Zhengyuan expelled me from the Sect. Now that the world is so vast, I should go out and see some of it. I have stayed in one place for a hundred years and am almost growing mold. Besides, you little girl have already caught up to me in cultivation. If I do not work a little harder, how can I still be your Master?"

Su Yan said, "Master might as well come with me."

Ruan Biting smiled. "You young couple have reunited after a long separation. What would I be doing tagging along beside you? Let us leave it at that. If fate allows, we will naturally meet again."

Yang Kai shook his head. "If Senior leaves, I am afraid we will have no chance to meet again in this life."

Ruan Biting said, "The Ancestral Domain may be vast, but if we wish to meet, naturally we can."

"We will soon leave the Ancestral Domain."

Ruan Biting was startled. Looking at Su Yan, she asked, "You are leaving the Ancestral Domain? Going where?"

Su Yan said, "The Star Realm!"

"The Star Realm?" Ruan Biting froze, then astonishment flashed in her eyes. "You have a way to reach the Star Realm?" Her reaction made it clear she knew the Star Realm existed.

That was not surprising. The Ancestral Domain connected to countless Star Domains, and in those Star Domains there were always some rumors about the Star Realm. Ordinary cultivators in the Ancestral Domain might not know much, but once their strength and cultivation reached a certain level, they would naturally come into contact with deeper knowledge.

The Star Realm was a legendary existence. It was rumored that there was a wider world there, richer Spiritual Qi, more complete World Laws, and a deeper Martial Dao!

That place was the end point of the Martial Dao. Even Third Stage Dao Origin Realm experts did not count for much there.

"We do!" Yang Kai nodded solemnly.

"Master, come with me," Su Yan pleaded. She had received many favors from Ruan Biting. If they parted here, she feared she would have no chance to repay her in this life.

Ruan Biting's expression shifted. She had originally planned to wander the world, but who could have known a great door would open before her, and that the scenery beyond it would hold such endless temptation?

Taking a light breath, Ruan Biting looked at Yang Kai and asked, “What Realm are you in now?”

"First Stage Emperor Realm!" Yang Kai smiled faintly.

"Emperor Realm!" Yearning appeared on Ruan Biting's face. "Above Dao Origin is Emperor?" She reached up to smooth her hair, then smiled faintly. "If I go with you, I will not disturb you, will I?"

"No, no. I have already eaten and drunk my fill... uh..."

Su Yan jabbed Yang Kai in the stomach with her elbow.

Ruan Biting smiled at her. "Then Master will have to rely on you to support me in my old age."

...

Yunxia Sect's scenery remained unchanged, but the usual bustle was gone.

Across the Sect's vast grounds, all was silent. Not a single figure could be seen in the squares or palaces. The place seemed to have become a domain of death. Even the birds and beasts in the mountains had retreated into their caves and no longer showed themselves, as if they had sensed some danger.

On the steps before the main hall, Liu Yan sat with both hands propping up her cheeks, staring ahead with blank, hollow eyes. She seemed to see no one and nothing, yet also seemed capable of holding the entire world within her gaze.

He Yunxiang strolled over, glanced at her, and felt a faint chill run through her.

Who could have imagined that such an adorable little girl would kill with such ruthless decisiveness? Everything that had happened in the main hall ten days ago remained vivid in He Yunxiang's mind. Liu Yan had merely unleashed an attack like a fire serpent, yet it burned Yunxia Sect's Sect Master and many Elders to ash in no more than ten breaths. Those Dao Origin Realm cultivators, so lofty and unreachable in He Yunxiang's eyes, had not been able to fight back at all.

"Did you find anything good?" Liu Yan suddenly raised her head and looked at her. She beckoned, and the palm-sized Fire Bird that had been following behind He Yunxiang, burning like a living flame, flew into her palm. It cried once, then sank into her skin and vanished.

He Yunxiang was startled and said, "I emptied their Treasure Vault."

As a respectable force in the Ancestral Domain, Yunxia Sect naturally had its own Treasure Vault. Normally, only the Sect Master and a few Elders were qualified to open it, but Yunxia Sect's Sect Master, Han Zhengyuan, and the Elders had died suddenly, while the disciples scattered just as quickly. The Treasure Vault was left behind, forgotten and unattended.

The Treasure Vault was protected by layer upon layer of restrictions. He Yunxiang was only at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and had advanced only recently at that, so naturally she had no way to open it herself. Fortunately, Liu Yan had given her that Fire Bird. With a gentle touch, it melted through the Treasure Vault's doors, leaving her free to go in and take whatever she liked.

He Yunxiang had never imagined there would come a day when she gathered treasures until her hands went weak from the effort. This was Yunxia Sect's accumulation over several thousand years: Spiritual Pills and wondrous medicines, rare flowers and exotic grasses, cultivation methods and Secret Treasures, too many to count.

As Gale's First Chief, she had roamed and ravaged the Wuji Star Domain for so many years, but only now did He Yunxiang realize what a country bumpkin she had been. The wealth Gale had plundered simply could not compare with Yunxia Sect's Treasure Vault.

The haul filled more than a dozen Spatial Rings, each one packed to the brim.

She was rich. She had struck it hugely rich!

She offered the dozen-plus Spatial Rings in her hand to Liu Yan.

Liu Yan raised her head and looked at her, her black-and-white eyes perfectly clear.

"I will keep one. The rest are for you and Sir," He Yunxiang explained. With so many good things, naturally she could not keep them all for herself. She knew her limits. One share was already enough to last her for many years.

"Keep them." Liu Yan waved her hand.

Liu Yan would not even glance at this sort of trash, and Yang Kai definitely would not either.

"But..."

Liu Yan suddenly turned to look in one direction. She rose to her feet as well, her expression turning strange, mixed with a trace of excitement and reverence.

Astonished, He Yunxiang followed her gaze and saw several figures flying swiftly over from that direction, with Yang Kai in the lead.

Once they drew near, the three landed.

He Yunxiang's eyes were instantly drawn to that unfamiliar face, and shock rippled through her. What a pure person! Was this Su Yan, the woman whose name had sent Sir into a thunderous rage the moment he heard it?

He Yunxiang's first impression of Su Yan was purity, purity without the slightest flaw. The Creator seemed to have bestowed all the most beautiful things in the world upon her, making anyone who looked at her feel ashamed of their own inadequacy.

Only then did He Yunxiang notice her looks. Calling her a beauty capable of toppling cities and nations would not be an exaggeration.

All at once, she understood somewhat why Yang Kai had not shown the slightest interest in her.

With such a woman at his side, perhaps no other woman in the world could stir the slightest ripple in his heart.

"Sir!" He Yunxiang hurriedly saluted, suddenly uneasy. She had no idea whether this Madam would take issue with her. After all, she had been following Yang Kai all this time; it would be bad if that stirred up jealousy. From now on, it seemed she would have to be more restrained. At the very least, she could not allow herself any improper thoughts about Yang Kai.

"What is it?" Yang Kai looked at Liu Yan in confusion, stepped forward, rubbed her head, and grinned. "Do you not recognize her?"

Liu Yan and Su Yan naturally knew each other. Back in the Hengluo Star Domain, they had interacted quite a few times. What Yang Kai found strange was why Liu Yan was looking at Su Yan that way, as if seeing her for the first time.

Stranger still, Su Yan was also looking at Liu Yan, her beautiful eyes full of interest.

Yang Kai looked from one to the other, completely bewildered. "What are you doing?"

Liu Yan stepped forward until she stood before Su Yan. Her small nose twitched gently, as though she were sniffing something like a little dog. A moment later, she raised her little face, looked up at Su Yan, and smiled faintly.

Su Yan smiled as well, as if she had found someone dear to her. She reached out dotingly to rub Liu Yan's head and asked softly, "What is your name?"

Yang Kai broke out in a cold sweat. "She is Liu Yan."

Su Yan's hand paused, and she stared in astonishment. "Liu Yan?"

How had she become like this? In Su Yan's memory, Liu Yan was a beautiful woman dressed rather revealingly. She never would have imagined that, when they met again, Liu Yan would have become a child.

Su Yan turned to Yang Kai, her eyes full of questions.

"It is a long story. All sorts of things happened, and she ended up like this." After a pause, he said, "But what about you two? What riddles are you playing at?"

He truly could not understand what strange trick these two women, one large and one small, were up to. He had never seen them this close before.

Su Yan and Liu Yan exchanged a glance and shared knowing smiles, but neither seemed inclined to explain.

Just you wait! Yang Kai secretly gritted his teeth, but did not dwell on it. Looking around, he said, "Let us set out."

With a wave of her fair hand, Ruan Biting immediately summoned a Multi-deck Ship. It was about ten zhang long and four decks high, exquisitely carved. One could tell at a glance that it had been made by a master craftsman.

The group boarded the Multi-deck Ship. Ruan Biting gave a delicate shout, runes flickered across the vessel, and it shot away like lightning.

Their target: home!

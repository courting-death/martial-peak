## Chapter 2815 — Frost Snow City

Half a day later, the dust settled.

Yu was panting, her face slightly pale, staring in disbelief at the corpse of the Bone-Eating Wolf King lying on the ground.

In this battle, not a single person was lost.

Only a few of her tribesmen suffered minor injuries, and it was all thanks to the slight-built Shaman Niu.

Relying on his own strength, he entangled the Bone-Eating Wolf King, leaving the Wolf King no chance to retaliate. Yu led her tribesmen to continuously wound it, and after a fierce fight lasting half a day, they finally killed it!

The Wolf King was essentially worn down to death.

Watching Shaman Niu rummaging through the Wolf King's corpse, Yu felt a mix of emotions, unable to believe this victory had come so miraculously. That was a Wolf King on par with a Shaman Master—even if it was already gravely wounded, it was not something her group could have handled.

Yet under Shaman Niu's many miraculous methods, the Wolf King had no strength to resist at all.

How did he do it? From start to finish, he hadn't used any particularly powerful Shamanic Art. Most of the arts he used were ones she could perform, but never with such remarkable effect.

"Ha, found it!" Yang Kai laughed loudly, pulling a still-bloody Inner Core from the Wolf King's corpse. He carefully wiped it clean in the snow and put it away like a treasure.

Then, without looking back, he walked to the other six dead Bone-Eating Wolves and did the same, extracting six more Inner Cores.

Yu composed herself and signaled to her tribesmen. One immediately went to collect the Condensed Snow Flower, while Yu walked straight to Yang Kai and said, "Lord Niu, do you need Inner Cores that much?"

Yang Kai played with his bulging cloth bag and replied without looking up, "Absolutely!"

If he wanted to quickly accumulate strength, he had to rely on the Inner Cores of Demonic Beasts.

Yu said, "I don't know why you need so many Inner Cores, but there is a place you might be interested in."

"What place?" Yang Kai looked up at her.

"Frost Snow City!"

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. "Your Frost Snow Tribe's city?"

"That's right."

"Are there many Inner Cores there?"

Yu smiled faintly. "Frost Snow City is the only city of my Frost Snow Tribe. Nearly all of our tribe's Shamans gather there, and even Shamans from other tribes often visit—especially in winter. Only our Frost Snow City is untouched by the harsh cold and always remains open. Every day, countless trades take place within its walls. Inner Cores from Demonic Beasts are nothing rare there; you'll even find ones far superior to the Bone-Eating Wolf King's. As long as you can offer something of equal value, Lord Niu, I'm sure the other Shamans will be eager to trade with you."

"Oh?" Yang Kai stroked his chin, clearly tempted.

If Frost Snow City was really as Yu described, he could indeed go take a look. At the very least, it would be much faster than hunting wild beasts for Inner Cores in Luobai Mountain.

Of course, he didn't have much on hand to trade.

"So? Want to go take a look?" Yu pressed, immediately seeing that Yang Kai was interested.

"With your kind invitation, Lady Yu, I would be honored." Yang Kai grinned.

Yu nodded. "Then let's set out together in a bit."

At this moment, the barbarian warrior who had gone to collect the Condensed Snow Flower returned. He whispered something to Yu, who then looked at Yang Kai and said, "Lord Niu, these wild beast corpses..."

"Handle them as you like."

"Thank you very much!" Yu expressed her gratitude, then instructed several tribesmen to deal with the Bone-Eating Wolf corpses.

If a Shaman could regularly eat wild beast meat, it would help improve their strength. So even though Yu was an Upper-Level Shaman Warrior, she couldn't bear to leave so many wild beast corpses here to go to waste—especially the Bone-Eating Wolf King's corpse, which was a great tonic even for a Great Shaman Master.

As for Shaman Niu, he only wanted the Inner Cores and not the meat. Yu had no idea what he was planning.

Although Inner Cores were quite valuable, the most common use was to soak them in wine. Some apothecaries could also use wild beast Inner Cores to make potions, but Shaman Niu didn't look like an apothecary at all.

The barbarian warriors were clearly not new to handling Demonic Beast corpses—their methods were extremely skilled. In less than the time it took for an incense stick to burn, the six Bone-Eating Wolves and the Wolf King's corpses were all dealt with.

Seven wolf pelts, plus the most tender and delicious parts of the wild beasts, were carried or hauled by everyone, all returning fully loaded.

Once everything was packed up, the group set out immediately.

Frost Snow City was not close to Luobai Mountain. Even at full speed, it would take at least three days to get there—and that was with Yu blessing everyone with the Divine Movement Art. Without it, they would be even slower.

On the way, Yang Kai asked Yu about the Divine Movement Art, and, to her astonishment, he mastered it almost instantly. Yu was left marveling at his extraordinary comprehension.

Three days later, a massive shadow suddenly appeared in everyone's sight.

Several barbarian warriors, including Yu, couldn't help but reveal genuine smiles.

"Lord Niu, we've arrived at Frost Snow City!" Yu pointed ahead.

Yang Kai focused his gaze and was stunned.

This was his first time coming to a city of the Ancient Barbarian Tribe, but what he saw was nothing like he had imagined.

He had thought that since it was called a city, it would at least look like one. But there was no sign of a city at all—only a gigantic, towering tree standing on the horizon. Even from a great distance, Yang Kai could feel its majestic presence. The tree's canopy blotted out the sky and reached into the clouds. It was hard to imagine just how tall it was.

Beneath the tree's sky-like canopy, buildings of all shapes and sizes were scattered haphazardly across the ground, like a chessboard thrown into disarray.

He could vaguely see many people moving about among them.

"This is Frost Snow City?" Yang Kai turned to look at Yu.

Yu smiled slightly. "That's right, this is Frost Snow City! It's also the foundation of our Frost Snow Tribe. Do you see that Divine Tree? That's Lord Qing, the guardian deity who protects our tribe."

"Lord Qing..." Yang Kai's mouth twitched. "You mean that tree... is alive?"

If it were alive, wouldn’t that make it a tree yao? Though a tree yao of such size would indeed be a rare sight.

Yu's expression turned solemn. "Lord Qing has lived for ten thousand years, undying and indestructible. Lord Niu, if you enter Frost Snow City, you must never disrespect Lord Qing!"

Undying and indestructible—wasn't that an Everlasting Tree? But compared to his own Everlasting Tree, this Divine Tree was like an ancestor compared to a grandchild.

However, seeing how seriously Yu warned him, Yang Kai realized that the Divine Tree must hold the highest status in the hearts of the Frost Snow Tribe. He nodded. "I understand."

Yu smiled again. "You don't need to worry too much, Lord Niu. Lord Qing is tolerant of all things. As long as you don't deliberately disrespect him, my tribesmen won't make things difficult for you."

As they spoke, the group gradually approached Frost Snow City. Up close, the Divine Tree's impact was even more overwhelming. The city had no walls or defensive formations like those of later generations, but the massive canopy covered an area of several hundred li, sheltering the Frost Snow Tribe's people for generations.

Though it was winter, there was not a trace of snow inside Frost Snow City. Instead, it looked full of spring vitality.

Half an hour later, as Yang Kai followed Yu to the edge of Frost Snow City, he suddenly felt a powerful Divine Sense sweep over him. Yang Kai's hair stood on end, and he looked around warily, but found nothing.

Yu said, "No need to be nervous, Lord Niu. Everyone who enters Frost Snow City is inspected by Lord Qing. If anyone harbors hostility toward the Frost Snow Tribe, they will be expelled immediately."

Yang Kai sighed in awe. "Lord Qing is truly formidable."

This was no flattery, but the truth. That Divine Sense just now was even stronger than his own at his peak. Clearly, this tree yao was an extraordinary expert!

Yu said, "Of course. Lord Qing is our tribe's guardian deity and has protected us for ten thousand years. It's just a pity..."

"A pity?"

Yu said sadly, "I heard that Lord Qing fell into a deep sleep thousands of years ago, and now no one can awaken him."

"Then just now..." Yang Kai asked in confusion. If he was asleep, how did that Divine Sense appear?

Yu smiled slightly. "Even in slumber, Lord Qing can still distinguish friend from foe."

Isn't that nonsense... Yang Kai thought. If he were in seclusion, unless his life was in danger, he wouldn't sense anything else. No matter how powerful the Divine Tree was, it couldn't possibly do that.

But since that was what they said, Yang Kai didn't argue.

As they spoke, the group entered Frost Snow City. After such a long journey, the barbarian warriors were finally home and looked quite spirited, their faces glowing with pride.

Standing inside Frost Snow City, Yang Kai felt a long-lost sense of belonging.

The shouts and haggling in his ears made him, for a moment, feel as if he had returned to the Star Realm.

"Lord Niu, thank you for your help in Luobai Mountain. We'll part ways here. I need to report to the Shaman Master and hand over the mission items." Yu turned and said to Yang Kai.

"Go ahead, don't worry about me," Yang Kai nodded.

Yu reached out to a barbarian warrior beside her, who immediately handed her something like a water pouch. Yu took it and handed it to Yang Kai. "Take this. Pour it at the roots of Lord Qing, and you'll be able to find a place to stay."

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. He recognized this water pouch. Back in Luobai Mountain, the barbarian warriors had used it to collect Demonic Beast blood when handling the corpses.

At the time, he hadn't known what Yu and the others wanted the Demonic Beast blood for, but now it seemed there was another use.

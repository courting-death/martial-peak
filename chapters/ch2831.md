## Chapter 2831 — Clouds Part, Sun Shines Bright

The sea of blood still lingered in the void as the three stood silently.

Yang Kai's gaze was as sharp as lightning, his Divine Sense surging as he searched for You's whereabouts. Chi and Zhu dared not relax in the slightest; the two stood back to back in a defensive formation, guarding against any sneak attack from behind.

The sea of blood no longer churned, appearing tranquil and silent.

Yet everyone knew that You had not escaped—he was still here.

It was not that he did not want to flee, but that he could not. The moment he emerged from the sea of blood, that strange and powerful Shaman Niu would never let him go. Having witnessed Shaman Niu's methods firsthand, Chi and Zhu could no longer treat him as just a Shaman Master. This was someone who, in just a few moves, had left You utterly powerless and severed several of his limbs.

“You think hiding will leave me powerless against you?” Yang Kai’s voice suddenly rang out, thick with mockery and disdain. “Today, this shaman will make you repay blood with blood!”

As his words fell, he suddenly reached out and pinched a bead the size of a longan between his fingers.

The bead looked utterly ordinary, but as Yang Kai urged it, a vast suction force suddenly emanated from it. In front of the bead, a pitch-black vortex appeared, as if leading to the Nine Nether Purgatory.

The boundless sea of blood, drawn by that suction, surged toward the vortex.

Chi and Zhu's eyes lit up at the sight, excitement quietly rising in their hearts.

In the current situation, with You hiding and refusing to show himself, using the sea of blood's wonders to conceal his presence, the two of them had no good way to find him. If this stalemate continued, You might very well escape alive.

After all, they could not stand guard over this sea of blood forever.

But now, seeing the power of the shamanic artifact Shaman Niu had produced, it seemed there was hope for a breakthrough.

As long as the sea of blood was completely absorbed, You would have nowhere left to hide. The only question now was whether that unremarkable bead could truly absorb such a vast sea of blood.

The answer, of course, was yes!

The Xuan Realm Pearl contained a world of its own. Though You's sea of blood was not small, to the Xuan Realm Pearl, it was nothing. Swallowing it clean was only a matter of time.

Under the tense gazes of Chi and Zhu, the blood sea that shrouded the sky shrank at a speed visible to the naked eye. The suction from the bead did not weaken in the slightest; the massive vortex remained, its surging pull drawing the blood into itself.

In less than the time it takes to burn a stick of incense, the sea of blood had already shrunk to half its original size.

At this point, You, who had been hiding in the sea of blood to heal and seek an opportunity, could no longer sit still. Eight-tenths of his power depended on this sea of blood. If it was destroyed, he would suffer backlash.

Suddenly, the calm sea of blood began to churn. Beneath the rolling surface, a figure darted like a fish, surging toward Chi and Zhu. Silent and secretive, no matter how vigilant the two were, they failed to notice a thing.

With a splash, You appeared before Zhu like a divine weapon descending from the heavens, a savage grin on his face. The arms and right leg Yang Kai had severed were somehow whole again, though the new skin was pale and tender, lacking the previous strength.

Zhu was shocked, hastily forming a seal with one hand as the light of a Shamanic Art shield flared up.

At the same time, a Wind Blade slashed toward You, aiming to drive him back.

As the sea of blood surged, a wall of blood rose before You, blocking the Wind Blade. When the wall collapsed, You had vanished.

Immediately after, Zhu sensed her Shamanic Art shield shatter. In an instant, she was terrified, her whole body turning cold.

Just then, a figure suddenly appeared before her. He was not tall—in fact, he seemed rather slight and frail—yet for some reason, Zhu felt an overwhelming sense of insignificance, as if she could never hope to measure up to him.

Shaman Niu!

Yang Kai reached out and grabbed at the void. Amidst the blood mist, it was as if he had seized something. With a muffled grunt, You's figure reappeared, now held by the neck in Yang Kai's grasp, his strong body dangling like a chick.

Zhu gasped for breath, her face full of lingering fear.

The sudden change just now had happened in a flash, so fast she had no time to react. If Shaman Niu had not appeared at the critical moment and precisely caught You hidden in the blood mist, she would have been finished.

How did he do it? Zhu was utterly confused, but knew this was not the time to ponder. Now that You was captured, with all the means and strength Shaman Niu had shown, You would not be able to stir up any more trouble. The battle was as good as won.

Just as she relaxed, she saw You suddenly grin strangely and say unhurriedly, “Do you think you've caught me?”

Zhu was dazed, instinctively sensing something was wrong, but could not say what.

“You think you've succeeded?” Yang Kai retorted with a cold laugh.

The look of confidence on You's face instantly turned to shock. Before he could say more, Yang Kai twisted hard, snapping his neck.

Splash...

You's body suddenly dissolved, turning into a pool of blood that merged back into the sea.

“Blood clone!” Chi and Zhu both cried out in shock.

If the You that Shaman Niu caught was a blood clone, then where was the real You? The two instinctively turned to look. There, above the sea of blood, in front of the bead devouring endless blood, You had appeared at some point, now grabbing the bead, clearly trying to seize the shamanic artifact.

This was undoubtedly the wisest move.

All of You's power was built upon the sea of blood. Now that Shaman Niu's artifact could destroy the sea and was even more powerful than expected, anyone in You's position would also try to snatch the artifact first.

Without the artifact to break the sea of blood, You would remain invincible.

The earlier blood clone that attacked Zhu was just a decoy, meant to draw Shaman Niu's attention away from the artifact.

And as it turned out, You's tactic was quite effective. Shaman Niu was indeed drawn away, leaving the artifact unattended for You to grab.

At this moment, You should have been overjoyed, but... why did he look so strained and terrified?

The hand clutching the bead trembled violently, as if struck by lightning.

“Foolish!” Yang Kai sneered, walking atop the sea of blood as he approached You step by step.

Sensing Yang Kai's approach, You's face was filled with terror. His shamanic power surged wildly, but he still could not escape the suction from the Xuan Realm Pearl, remaining firmly rooted in place.

He thought that as long as he seized the Xuan Realm Pearl, he would be safe, not knowing that Yang Kai and the Xuan Realm Pearl were already united in mind and spirit, moving as one.

Shamanic artifacts of ancient times were vastly different from the secret treasures of later generations. If the Xuan Realm Pearl were truly just a shamanic artifact, perhaps You could have taken it. But the Xuan Realm Pearl contained a world of its own. Without refining it, how could someone of You's strength hope to lift a world?

Even a Shaman King or Shaman Saint could not lift it.

Instead, the suction from within the Xuan Realm Pearl locked You in place.

Watching Yang Kai draw closer, a trace of panic finally flashed across You's face. Even when his arms and leg had been severed earlier, his expression had barely changed, but now, he truly felt despair.

He did not beg for mercy or show fear. You only glared coldly at Yang Kai, grinning savagely: “The Bone-Eater Tribe will never let you go!”

As he finished, a look of resolve appeared on his face, and his shamanic power suddenly surged.

Yang Kai frowned, halting his steps.

In the next instant, a loud explosion rang out as You's body burst apart. Blood rained down from the sky, dyeing the earth red.

In the face of certain death, You chose self-destruction, showing just how fierce his nature was.

But most barbarians were like this. In a hopeless situation, few would choose to cling to life.

The sea of blood vanished, the sky cleared. The sun had risen, shining brightly, yet it could not dispel the chill lingering over the land.

Below, the slaughter continued. The Bone-Eater Tribe's stone fortress held seven or eight hundred tribesmen, while the Raging Flame Tribe and Gale Tribe had only three hundred each. But the prisoners Yang Kai had released made up for the numbers, giving them a slight advantage.

The only drawback was that they had fewer shamans than the enemy—only seven or eight on their side, while the Bone-Eater Tribe had nearly twenty.

Thus, the battle was earth-shattering, both sides evenly matched, with barbarian warriors falling constantly.

This balance was broken the moment You died.

When the blood sea that had covered the sky vanished, everyone looked up to see the outcome of the high-level battle. In a sense, the result of the high-level fight was the final outcome.

Three figures stood in the void. The three tribes' warriors let out wild cheers, while the Bone-Eater Tribe's people were ashen-faced.

“Kill!” With a furious roar, the three barbarian tribes launched their final charge. Without You, the Bone-Eater Tribe could no longer compete. Morale collapsed, and the stalemate was instantly broken.

Zhu and Chi said nothing, each flying down. Their powerful Shamanic Arts swept the battlefield, and with a wave of their hands, enemies fell. The more than twenty Bone-Eater Tribe shamans lasted less than ten breaths before being wiped out.

Without their shamans' support, the Bone-Eater Tribe's warriors lost much of their strength and were soon slaughtered.

In the final stage, it was a one-sided massacre. No one showed mercy, for the enemy was the Bone-Eater Tribe—a tribe of outcasts that should have long been erased from the barbarian race.

The battle lasted for half a day. When the last Bone-Eater Tribe tribesman died, the stone fortress was awash in blood, corpses piled like mountains.

The warriors of the three tribes roamed the battlefield, making sure none of their enemies survived—whether the Bone-Eater Tribe members were dead or alive, each received another thrust of a blade to ensure their death.

Afterwards, everyone began gathering the bodies of their own tribesmen.

At nightfall, a simple ceremony was held outside the stone fortress. The bodies of all three tribes' warriors were piled together and burned under Chi's supervision.

The barbarian tribe believed that blazing flames would allow their people to return to the embrace of the Barbarian God after death.

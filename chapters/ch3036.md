## Chapter 3036 — Madam Hua

Sensing something unusual, Yang Kai turned his head and glanced over.

There were not many people, only four in total: one man and three women.

The man was none other than Yuan Wu. He accompanied the three women, bowing and scraping with an exceedingly cautious expression.

All three women were quite beautiful, especially the one in the lead. She appeared to be twenty-four or twenty-five and was a stunning, kingdom-toppling beauty with a graceful, voluptuous figure. She wore a pale green palace dress, and as she flew through the air, her lustrous hair streamed behind her and her long skirt fluttered, making her an exceptionally enchanting sight.

However, no one knew what this woman had encountered to make her so furious. Her expression was icy, and her beautiful eyes gleamed with unrestrained cold light.

Compared with her, the two women behind her, though also fairly attractive, seemed to be lacking something.

The four flew straight toward them. Yang Kai could not help frowning inwardly, wondering what they intended to do.

Lu Sanniang had clearly noticed them as well. Her face suddenly paled, and her delicate body began trembling.

Lu Yuqin hurriedly moved closer to her and whispered, "Mother... what's wrong?"

Lu Sanniang shook her head without saying a word, but her expression grew increasingly uneasy.

Noticing that something was amiss, Yang Kai glanced at Lu Sanniang, then at the women. A thought occurred to him, and he asked softly, "You know them?"

Lu Sanniang gave an almost imperceptible nod.

Yang Kai immediately understood.

According to what Lu Sanniang had told him earlier, this Spirit Island was the residence of a Dragon Clan member named Fu Chi. It was Fu Chi who had captured her, and she had lived on this Spirit Island for several hundred years. Clearly, Lu Sanniang was not the only woman here; there were many more playthings belonging to Fu Chi.

The three women flying toward them were obviously such people. It was no surprise that Lu Sanniang knew them. After living together for several hundred years, how could they possibly be strangers?

Judging by the current situation, however, Lu Sanniang was clearly somewhat afraid of these women. Perhaps there were even old grievances between them.

As these thoughts flashed through his mind, Yang Kai pieced together most of the situation. With a subtle shift of his feet, he unobtrusively blocked Lu Sanniang from view.

He did not know why the three women had come, but the look on the lead woman's face made it obvious that they had not come for anything good.

In the blink of an eye, Yuan Wu arrived with the three women. The four did not land, instead hovering in midair and gazing down at Yang Kai and the others.

"Who did it?" The woman in the palace dress sized up Yang Kai with a frosty expression and asked coldly.

Yuan Wu pointed at them and hurriedly replied, "Madam, it was them!"

The woman in the palace dress narrowed her eyes and ground out, "You've got some nerve. So you were the ones who injured Xiao Hong?"

Yang Kai's expression remained unchanged. He merely stared coldly at Yuan Wu, who immediately felt uncomfortable beneath his gaze. The humiliation he had suffered a few days ago flashed through his mind, and his expression shifted several times as he involuntarily shrank back.

He was genuinely afraid of Yang Kai. Even now, he could not understand how a mere First Stage Emperor Realm human could be so much stronger than him, nor what method Yang Kai had used to suppress his Dragon Descendant bloodline.

However, he quickly remembered that he had not come alone. A person of great status stood beside him, so why should he fear Yang Kai with such powerful backing? At this thought, Yuan Wu immediately straightened his back, pointed to the woman in the palace dress, and barked, "This is Madam Hua, the mistress of this place! Madam Hua is asking you a question. Are you deaf?"

Hearing Yuan Wu introduce her this way, Madam Hua raised her chin slightly, looking utterly conceited.

In truth, there was no such thing as a mistress of this Spirit Island. Fu Chi had brought these women here and kept them as playthings at most, summoning and dismissing them as he pleased. He had never treated them as equals.

However, Madam Hua knew how to ingratiate herself and possessed kingdom-toppling beauty, so she was quite highly favored by Fu Chi. Her status ranked among the highest on the entire Spirit Island, below only Fu Chi.

Yuan Wu's introduction had undoubtedly bolstered her prestige, so Madam Hua was quite satisfied.

Madam Hua narrowed her long, slender phoenix eyes and looked down upon Yang Kai as though she were gazing at an ant.

Yang Kai did not even look at her. He simply stared at Yuan Wu, a cold sneer curling at the corner of his mouth.

Yuan Wu immediately felt as though thorns were pricking his back, and anger surged within him. He had already made such an introduction, so how could this fellow still dare act as though no one else existed? Did he still not understand what kind of person Madam Hua was?

Yet the more Yang Kai acted this way, the more delighted Yuan Wu became. He could not defeat Yang Kai himself, but it would be just as good if he could use Madam Hua to teach Yang Kai an unforgettable lesson. Thus, seeing Yang Kai remain so unruly, Yuan Wu immediately began gloating inwardly, wishing Yang Kai would act even more arrogantly. He called out, "Madam Hua, look at this fellow! He is far too arrogant. He clearly doesn't take you seriously at all!"

Madam Hua was also furious. She had assumed that once she appeared, these ants would immediately prostrate themselves before her. Though she was neither a member of the Dragon Clan nor even a Dragon Descendant, she was greatly favored by Fu Chi. How could this riffraff possibly compare with her?

Further incited by Yuan Wu, she immediately snorted. "You truly are audacious. No wonder you dared injure Xiao Hong!"

Only then did Yang Kai slowly turn his gaze toward her and ask with a frown, "What's Xiao Hong?"

He genuinely had no idea what Xiao Hong was and instinctively suspected that Yuan Wu had resorted to some scheme to frame him.

Madam Hua sneered. "Don't you know what you've done?"

"I don't know what you're talking about."

"Insolence!" Yuan Wu shouted, then yelled at Yang Kai, "Madam Hua keeps a Golden-Backed Red Carp that she is extremely fond of, but someone injured it for no reason today. Who could it be if not you? You're the only person on this island who would dare act so wantonly!"

Yang Kai shook his head. "What Golden-Backed Red Carp? I've never seen it."

He had only just returned from outside, so how could he have seen this Golden-Backed Red Carp? Judging by its name, it was probably some kind of fish and likely a pet kept by Madam Hua. That made sense. These women were trapped on this Spirit Island with nothing to do each day except satisfy Fu Chi's lust, so keeping a pet to lift their spirits was perfectly normal.

However, this matter truly had nothing to do with him. Yang Kai sneered inwardly. Yuan Wu, oh Yuan Wu. I had no intention of doing anything more to you, but I never expected you to resort to such a despicable scheme. It seems the previous lesson truly wasn't enough.

Killing intent toward Yuan Wu rose in his heart, and he looked at him as though he were already a dead man.

"Show him!" Madam Hua suddenly waved her hand.

One of the women who had been following beside her immediately stepped forward, holding something resembling a fish tank in both hands. Inside it was a small red fish about the length of an index finger. Its back gleamed brilliantly with golden light, while the rest of its body was vivid red, making it look quite extraordinary.

This was evidently the Golden-Backed Red Carp Yuan Wu had mentioned. Judging by the aura it radiated, however, it was only a Fifth-Tier Yao Beast. It was not very powerful and was merely pleasing to the eye.

Moreover, its true body was probably much larger than this. It could be kept inside the fish tank only because of the tank's wondrous properties.

The Golden-Backed Red Carp was indeed injured. One or two scales were missing from a spot on its back, marring its overall beauty, but the injury was nothing serious. It swam energetically around the tank, clearly still brimming with vitality. Such an injury should heal after a few days of care, yet judging by Madam Hua's expression, one would think it had already died.

"This is Xiao Hong!" The woman holding the fish tank wore an icy expression, her pretty face so cold one could almost scrape off a layer of frost. She snapped at Yang Kai, "Do you see clearly now?"

Yang Kai replied indifferently, "I've never seen this red carp, nor did I injure it. You've found the wrong person."

"You still dare quibble at this point!" Madam Hua lost her temper, feeling that this fellow truly did not know what was good for him.

Yang Kai said coldly, "What makes you think I injured it? If this Young Master had acted, it wouldn't have ended with a mere injury."

Madam Hua froze at his words, then trembled with rage. "You dare speak to this Madam that way?"

"How this Young Master speaks to you is my business. Who the hell do you think you are?" Yang Kai scoffed.

Madam Hua was incensed. On this Spirit Island, only Fu Chi stood above her. Aside from having to defer to him, everyone else treated her with the utmost respect, fawning over and flattering her. It had been the same when she went to Yuan Wu's group just now. As soon as she announced her identity, everyone immediately became meek and deferential.

Who could have known Yang Kai would rebuff her so completely? Madam Hua simply could not accept it.

Yang Kai paid her no mind and said coldly, “The matter of the red carp has nothing to do with this Young Master. Go back where you came from, and stop making a disgrace of yourselves here!”

At these words, Madam Hua's face turned livid.

The red carp was not particularly valuable, but it was a pet she had raised for more than ten years. Whenever her loneliness became unbearable, she would tease and play with it, and it had accompanied her through many tedious days. Now that someone had injured it for no reason, Madam Hua naturally intended to settle the score. Yet the man before her had repeatedly contradicted her and showed her no respect whatsoever.

This was no longer about the red carp. If she did not teach this young man a lesson today, she would never be able to save face.

How could a mere human dare behave so presumptuously here? He was simply courting death.

Just as Madam Hua was preparing to teach Yang Kai a lesson at any cost, Yuan Wu's eyes darted about and he suddenly said, "If it wasn't you, then it must have been them! That's right, it was them! Those Luminous Pearls were gathered by that mother and daughter. They must have encountered Madam Hua's pet in the sea and injured it!"

"Them?" Madam Hua frowned and looked behind Yang Kai.

She had not paid any attention to the people behind him. Xiao Hong's injury had left her burning with rage and wholly intent on revenge, so how could she have been in the mood to notice anyone else?

Only after Yuan Wu's reminder did she notice that two people had been hiding behind Yang Kai all along.

One of them was blocked from view by the young man before her, making it impossible to see her face. The other was a young girl who was watching Madam Hua timidly.

"Hm?" Madam Hua gazed at the young girl and could not help furrowing her delicate brows. The girl looked vaguely familiar, as though she had seen her somewhere before.

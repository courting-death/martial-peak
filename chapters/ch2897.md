## Chapter 2897 — Colossal Being

Mo Duo glared, gritting his teeth at Yang Kai before him, only to be met with a heavy punch.

The tremendous force struck his face, twisting his features and sending him tumbling through the air. The feeling of being suppressed finally vanished, and Mo Duo hurriedly steadied himself. But just as he regained his footing, that strange bell rang out by his ear once more.

The recently vanished suppressive force enveloped him again, causing his power to stall for a moment. In that instant, he was unable to dodge, and another punch landed on his chest. The sound of bones breaking rang out as his chest caved in and he was sent flying.

A dignified Demon Saint was treated like a mere sandbag, sent flying back and forth through the air by a Shaman King without the slightest ability to fight back. This was simply an unbearable humiliation. In Mo Duo’s chest, fury churned like a brewing volcano, his rage ready to erupt at any moment.

Though the opponent’s attacks were not fatal, to lose face before millions of Demons was something Mo Duo could not tolerate.

The root of it all was that strange little bell. If Shaman Niu had only his own strength to rely on, Mo Duo would not have feared him, but that bell could even suppress a Demon Saint’s power. He lost the initiative at the start, and every step since had gone wrong, leaving him no chance to turn the situation around.

Each time the bell rang, the momentum and power Mo Duo had just gathered would be suppressed, followed by Yang Kai’s onslaught like a raging storm.

In just the time it takes for a stick of incense to burn, Yang Kai had attacked Mo Duo over a thousand times, each blow delivered with full force, enough to instantly kill an ordinary Emperor.

Yet such a barrage, when unleashed on Mo Duo, only left him looking battered. He didn’t even show signs of serious injury. No matter how badly his chest caved in or how many bones broke, he seemed able to recover in an instant.

Demon Saint Mo Duo possessed an indomitable vitality!

Yang Kai was exhausted, gasping for breath. After Dragon Transformation, his strength had greatly increased, but so had his energy consumption. What’s more, every attack required him to activate the Mountain and River Bell in advance, which was no small burden. If not for his deep reserves, he would have collapsed long ago.

Still, he remained calm and patient, waiting for an opportunity—a chance to win in a single blow.

The opportunity soon arrived.

Mo Duo had been tormented into a frenzy, his face twisted and savage, his teeth grinding audibly, eyes bloodshot as if he had lost all reason.

The Mountain and River Bell rang out again, and a visible wave of sound crashed toward Mo Duo.

But this time, Yang Kai’s timing seemed off. Before the sound wave struck, Mo Duo dodged aside. Without the bell’s suppression, he could finally go all out against Yang Kai. He grinned fiercely, demonic qi surging as he lunged at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai’s face showed just the right hint of panic as he hurriedly retreated, but he couldn’t widen the distance between himself and Mo Duo.

Mo Duo swooped overhead and struck down with his palm, a destructive aura surging from his hand and enveloping Yang Kai.

At that moment, Yang Kai suddenly raised his hand, sending the Xuan Realm Pearl flying. Under his control, the Xuan Realm Pearl vanished into the void, and a vortex-like passage appeared between him and Mo Duo.

Divine Sense surged like a tide, wrapping around Mo Duo and forming an invisible net, with Mo Duo as the great fish caught within.

Yang Kai grunted, his feet rooted to the ground, as he mobilized his Divine Soul to close the net and catch his prey.

As long as he could trap Mo Duo within the Small Xuan Realm, he would hold the demon's life and death in his hands. Even a genuine Demon Saint operating at only seventy percent of his power would be no match for Yang Kai within that realm.

The most crucial thing was to get Mo Duo into the Small Xuan Realm.

When the vortex appeared, Mo Duo’s expression changed slightly. Clearly, he realized something and was alarmed. He hurriedly tried to retreat, but the surrounding space became viscous, as if he had fallen into a quagmire, making it impossible to break free.

That stickiness was the combined effect of Yang Kai’s Divine Soul and his Space Divine Ability.

Mo Duo reacted quickly, unleashing Demon Saint-level Divine Sense, which turned into countless sharp awls stabbing madly in all directions.

In an instant, the net formed by Yang Kai’s Divine Soul was riddled with holes. Not only that, but his Divine Soul also suffered heavy damage, with blood streaming from his seven orifices, making for a terrifying sight.

“How foolish, to compete with me in Divine Soul strength?” Mo Duo wore a look of utter confidence, his face mocking. He was in no hurry to escape, having realized that Shaman Niu had trapped himself by trying to restrain him with Divine Soul power.

The invisible clash continued without pause. Yang Kai’s Divine Soul was being wounded more and more severely, and the net had long since been torn apart, yet he still refused to let go.

Mo Duo’s mockery turned to shock, for Yang Kai’s persistence exceeded his expectations.

The Divine Soul is the source of every living being’s intelligence. If it suffers too much damage, the being becomes a mindless shell.

His attacks were wild and relentless. By rights, this Shaman Niu should have lost all Divine Sense and become an idiot by now, but he found that was not the case. Shaman Niu’s face was twisted in pain, clearly suffering, yet he remained lucid.

Something was protecting his Divine Soul!

Mo Duo was, after all, a Demon Saint. Though he didn’t know about the Warm Spirit Lotus, he quickly guessed the truth.

It was precisely because of the Warm Spirit Lotus that Yang Kai dared to fight Mo Duo to the death in this way.

Mo Duo was both shocked and excited by his own deduction. He suddenly pushed his Divine Soul power, staring into Yang Kai’s eyes, his deep gaze seeming to pierce all barriers, seeking the secrets within Yang Kai’s Sea of Consciousness.

A treasure that could protect one’s Divine Soul from destruction was certainly worth investigating for himself.

At that moment, a strange smile appeared on Yang Kai’s pained face.

Suddenly, his left eye turned golden, a majestic vertical pupil appearing. In a flash, a budding lotus shot forth, plunging into Mo Duo’s Sea of Consciousness.

Mo Duo grunted in anger and was about to resist the Blooming Lotus secret technique, but was shocked to find that the net he had just destroyed had suddenly contracted with such force that it nearly caught him off guard, dragging him rapidly toward the vortex.

Mo Duo was horrified, his body involuntarily drawn toward the entrance of the Xuan Realm Pearl, while the Blooming Lotus secret technique exploded in his mind, causing him excruciating pain.

If he allowed himself to be caught this way, that would be true humiliation. Though Mo Duo didn't know where this vortex led, seeing how desperately Yang Kai was pulling him inside, he understood it was certainly no place of safety. Perhaps once he entered, he would never escape.

Enduring the pain in his mind, he roared, “Enough!”

His furious shout shook the world.

The force pulling him suddenly paused. At the same time, a jet-black beam shot down from the battlefield high above, plunging into Mo Duo’s body and vanishing.

Mo Duo’s already terrifying aura surged explosively.

Yang Kai’s expression changed drastically, his mouth filled with bitterness, worse than if he had swallowed bitter herbs.

He had used every means at his disposal and was about to drag Mo Duo into his Small Xuan Realm, only for Mo Duo to recall the power of his Incarnation at the last moment. Now, Mo Duo was a true Demon Saint, at his peak.

The stalemate was instantly broken.

Mo Duo, having regained his full strength, didn’t even need to act. His surging aura alone shattered all of Yang Kai’s Divine Soul power and space abilities.

On the battlefield, millions of Demons and Barbarians turned to look, witnessing a scene they would never forget.

A black shadow suddenly stood in midair, then, like an inflating balloon, rapidly grew larger.

Three zhang, five zhang, ten zhang, twenty zhang…

Only when it reached thirty zhang tall did the black shadow stop growing.

Mo Duo had transformed into a colossal being. Two horns sprouted from his head, slightly curved and razor-sharp, as if they could pierce the sky. His feet became two massive hooves, planted firmly on the earth. All things in the world seemed tiny before him. His body looked as if it were cast from bronze and iron, gleaming with a metallic luster. With a light snort from his nostrils, a visible tornado instantly formed in midair.

Demon Saint Mo Duo, having recovered his full strength, transformed into a monstrous being that was half man, half ox. Thick Demonic Qi coiled around his mountain-like body, churning and writhing as if it were alive.

Yang Kai was nowhere to be seen, the Xuan Realm Pearl gone as well.

The moment Mo Duo regained his strength, Yang Kai had already concealed his presence.

Mo Duo’s window-sized eyes swept casually, piercing the void and locking onto Yang Kai’s fleeing trace.

He plucked a single bristle from his body, holding it as if it were an embroidery needle.

Then, with utter nonchalance, he tossed the bristle forward.

With a whoosh, the bristle pierced the void and vanished.

Ten li away, a muffled grunt sounded. Yang Kai’s figure stumbled into view, crashing to the ground. His abdomen had been pierced by a jet-black energy, pinning him in place. That energy, clearly transformed from Mo Duo’s bristle, was filled with a most sinister power. Pinned by it, Yang Kai found his body immobile, even his thoughts sluggish.

Dong dong dong…

Mo Duo strode forward. Though he seemed to walk slowly, his speed was astonishing.

In just three breaths, he crossed the ten li, looming over Yang Kai and looking down as if at an ant. “To force me to this point, you truly are capable.”

When he first appeared, he used half his strength against Yang Kai, and they were evenly matched. Then, using seventy percent, he found himself at a disadvantage. But after returning to his peak, he could finally crush his opponent.

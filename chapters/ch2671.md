## Chapter 2671 — How High Is the Mountain

Sword light flickered and danced, sword qi crisscrossed the heavens, and the sword formation's power erupted in full force. The dazzling radiance seemed capable of illuminating the ages past and future, completely engulfing the vast area around the city gate.

Du Xian, who bore the brunt of the attack, had nowhere to run. Countless rampaging energies transformed into strangling force, wrapping around him.

In an instant, Du Xian was torn limb from limb.

“Hahaha, overestimating yourself!” The leading Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple, seeing Du Xian charge in so fiercely, had thought he had some trump card. Who would have guessed it was all thunder and no rain? With a single round of the sword formation, the enemy was already decapitated, and he burst out laughing.

Yet as soon as he started laughing, he instinctively sensed something was wrong. His expression changed, and he stared wide-eyed at the dismembered Du Xian, as if witnessing something unbelievable, exclaiming in horror, “A puppet!”

The Du Xian who had charged at them so resolutely, ready to die, was not a living person at all, but a puppet!

Only, this puppet was crafted with such exquisite skill that it was completely indistinguishable from a real person. In the heat of battle, he had no time to examine it closely. Only after dismembering the opponent did he notice something was off.

"Explode!" A low shout rang out, like a voice from the Underworld, echoing in everyone’s ears.

On the corpse fragments of the puppet Du Xian, threads of scorching light suddenly flashed, becoming incomparably bright in an instant.

An extremely dangerous aura spread out like a tsunami.

The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple’s face changed drastically, and he screamed hoarsely, “Run!”

As he spoke, the sword in his hand spun, stirring up layer upon layer of impregnable sword intent to guard himself, and he pushed off with his foot, retreating rapidly.

Boom…

A deafening explosion rang out. Outside the city gate, a blinding white sphere burst open, covering a full hundred zhang in every direction. The violent energy shockwave whipped up a sudden gale, sweeping through the void.

Muffled grunts echoed out, then suddenly ceased. Life forces vanished one after another, each one marking the fall of a cultivator. The sight left the onlookers trembling with fear, their faces drained of color.

After a short while, the dust settled.

At the city gate of Flowing Shadow City, a pit more than ten zhang deep and a hundred zhang across had appeared. The area around the pit was a scene of devastation; a large section of the city wall had collapsed, and the ground was littered with charred corpses and severed limbs.

Crackle…

The puppet python that had been coiled in place, guarding Du Xian and Ye Qinghan, now raised its head slightly, its scarlet tongue flickering in and out. In its coiled belly, Ye Qinghan and Du Xian still stood there, completely unharmed.

Amidst the devastated ground, a figure staggered to his feet. It was the leading Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple from before. Among the many disciples, he was the strongest and the quickest to react, so although he was injured in the puppet’s self-destruction, he managed to survive by sheer luck.

He had just gotten up when the light before him dimmed—someone was blocking his way.

Looking up, it was Du Xian.

Their eyes met, and the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple’s eyes nearly split with rage as he shouted, “You tricked me!”

He swung his sword, wanting to strike at Du Xian, but as soon as he moved, he felt unbearable pain all over his body. His strength would not circulate, and his face grew even paler.

The puppet Du Xian’s self-destruction was extremely powerful, almost equal to the self-destruction of a peak Dao Origin Realm cultivator. The Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples had been very close at the time, and almost all perished in the blast. Even though he had survived by luck, he was in a miserable state.

Du Xian's eyes turned blood-red as he gritted his teeth and said, "Compared to what Flowing Shadow Sword Sect did to Thousand Leaves Sect, this is nothing—merely interest on the debt!"

The hatred of extermination is irreconcilable. Whether or not Flowing Shadow Sword Sect was acting on someone else’s orders, they were still murderers. Du Xian naturally felt no mercy for him.

As he spoke, he ruthlessly struck the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple on the head, intent on killing him then and there.

“Insolent brat, this is Flowing Shadow City! How dare you commit murder here!”

As the angry shout rang out, an elderly figure suddenly appeared in the sky. With a casual wave, he reached out to grab Du Xian.

A wave of Emperor Intent spread out, and Du Xian suddenly felt his whole body stiffen. No matter how hard he struggled, he could not move.

“Emperor Realm!” Du Xian’s expression changed. He realized his actions had drawn out an expert, but this person didn’t seem to be the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect’s Sect Master. He had no idea why this person had intervened or what his relationship to the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect was.

“Such a bloodthirsty nature at such a young age—if you grow up, who knows what you’ll become? This old man shall help you resolve this killing karma!"

The old man seemed to be explaining his actions. As he finished speaking, his large hand was already reaching for Du Xian’s head.

In the void, several other figures flickered, rushing toward Ye Qinghan, who was still standing in place.

Thousand Leaves Sect, a mere minor sect, would have been ignored in the past. The recent commotion was only because Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had been making a big fuss about it.

However, puppet crafting was ultimately an obscure and unconventional technique. Even if it had some uses, it wasn't something that would normally attract attention.

But after witnessing the puppet techniques displayed by Du Xian and Ye Qinghan today, these hidden Emperor Realm experts no longer dared to look down on them. Two Thousand Leaves Sect survivors, relying only on a few puppets, had slaughtered so many in Flowing Shadow City. If they could obtain such things, it would definitely strengthen their own sects.

So in an instant, Ye Qinghan and Du Xian became hot commodities. Many people decided to take them back and uncover the secrets of Thousand Leaves Sect’s puppet refinement and control.

“Where did this shameless old thing come from?!”

A cold snort rang out, and a sharp burst of energy shot straight at the old man, like a Jiao surging out to strike him.

The old man’s heart skipped a beat. Instinctively sensing danger, he no longer cared about grabbing Du Xian and hurriedly struck at the incoming energy, instantly unleashing seventy or eighty percent of his strength.

Boom…

With a muffled sound, the old man staggered back several steps, his face suddenly pale. His qi and blood churned in his chest, and he nearly coughed up blood. Shocked, he glared in the direction of the attack and shouted, “Who are you?!”

Yang Kai sneered without answering, instead striking several palms through the air toward where Ye Qinghan was standing.

With a series of crisp sounds, several sneaky figures approaching Ye Qinghan were suddenly revealed, each with an ugly expression.

With the old man’s suppression gone, Du Xian’s Origin Qi circulated again. He slapped the surviving Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciple into a pulp, feeling immense satisfaction, and let out a long howl.

Yang Kai’s gaze swept over the Emperor Realm experts, then he asked, “Which one of you is the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect’s Sect Master?”

Silence. No one answered.

Ye Qinghan walked over and whispered, “He’s not here.”

When Flowing Shadow Sword Sect invaded Thousand Leaves Sect that day, Ye Qinghan had seen Li Qingyun once and recognized his appearance.

Yang Kai snorted, “So he’s hiding like a turtle?”

He was also a bit curious. So many from Flowing Shadow Sword Sect had been killed, yet the Sect Master still hadn’t shown up. This was different from what he expected, and he couldn’t help but feel annoyed.

“Who exactly are you, boy?!” The old man, having suffered a setback at Yang Kai’s hands and getting no answer, felt his dignity slipping away.

Yang Kai, in a foul mood, glanced at him coldly and snorted, “At your age, you should be enjoying retirement. Jumping around like this, be careful you don’t break your back.”

“Insolence!” the old man roared. He was a Sect Master, yet now he was being humiliated by a youth. It was intolerable. If not for Yang Kai’s formidable strength, he would have taught him a lesson to show that the old are still strong.

“Whose disciple are you, to be so arrogant? Didn’t your elders teach you how to behave?” a burly middle-aged man frowned, displeased with Yang Kai’s attitude.

Yang Kai grinned and said, “You want to teach me how to behave?”

The middle-aged man snorted, “Young man, being too sharp isn’t a good thing. There’s always a higher mountain!”

Yang Kai swung his Myriad Sword, pointed at him, and said, “Then come fight me. Let me see how high your mountain is.”

The middle-aged man was choked with anger and gritted his teeth. “Unreasonable.”

The other Emperor Realm experts all shook their heads, thinking this youth was like a mad dog, biting whoever he saw. They didn’t know Yang Kai had come here to cause trouble and was afraid things wouldn’t get lively enough. Now that someone had stepped up, he certainly wouldn’t back down.

But after all this commotion, the main target still hadn’t appeared, leaving Yang Kai somewhat disappointed. With a long laugh, he put away his Myriad Sword and said, “It seems you all are just talk. In that case, get out of my way and don’t waste my time.”

He called out to Ye Qinghan and Du Xian, swept his Divine Sense, locked onto the direction of the City Lord’s mansion, and said loudly, “Since Sect Master Li refuses to show himself, I’ll pay him a visit myself.”

With that, he leapt forward, leading Ye Qinghan and Du Xian toward the City Lord’s mansion.

The Emperor Realm experts exchanged glances, all guessing his intentions. They were puzzled, not knowing where the two Thousand Leaves Sect survivors had found such a powerful Emperor Realm expert. It seemed Flowing Shadow Sword Sect was in big trouble this time, and they wondered if the auction could proceed as planned.

After witnessing the puppet techniques of Du Xian and Ye Qinghan, these Emperor Realm experts were extremely interested in those Heavenly Puppets. They all felt that buying one or two would make the trip worthwhile.

It was difficult to advance to the Emperor Realm, but obtaining a puppet comparable to an Emperor Realm expert was different—it was something that could be used immediately, requiring only the consumption of some Source Crystals.

For a while, many people were torn, afraid Yang Kai would ruin the auction.

Still, this was someone else’s business. At most, they were just here for the spectacle. They had no intention of getting involved.

In front of the City Lord’s mansion, a group of Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples stood as if facing a great enemy, all trembling and pale.

Dozens of Senior Brother disciples had died at the city gate. They had received the news and thought the Sect Master would surely take action against the two Thousand Leaves Sect survivors, but to their surprise, the Sect Master had not responded at all.

As Yang Kai led his group forward step by step, the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples’ palms grew sweaty as they watched, wary and afraid.

A gentle breeze blew, carrying waves of murderous intent that chilled everyone to the bone.

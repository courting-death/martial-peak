## Chapter 2822 — Stealing Techniques and Learning Skills

Following Yu all the way deep into the palace, it was only after an hour that they arrived before a tall, imposing Great Shaman Master. The Great Shaman Master's aura was thunderous, sharp eyes piercing Yang Kai, making him feel extremely uncomfortable.

"Lord Che, this is Shaman Niu!" Yu respectfully performed a barbarian shaman salute, not daring to lift her eyes, introducing Yang Kai to Lord Che.

Yang Kai followed local customs, placed his hand on his chest, bent at the waist, and said, "Shaman Niu greets Lord Che!"

Lord Che nodded slightly, his voice rumbling like muffled thunder: "You are the shaman who prepares healing medicine?"

"Indeed!" Yang Kai raised his head, meeting Lord Che's gaze.

Lord Che asked with interest, "It's rare to see a barbarian like you. Are you from the Drifting Tribe?"

Yang Kai shook his head, "Southern Barbarian Tribe. I'm just born a bit weaker, that's all."

No shaman or barbarian would ever deny their origin; that would be self-contempt. So, although Yang Kai looked frail and seemed like someone from the Drifting Tribe, since he said so, Lord Che did not question it.

The corner of Lord Che's mouth twitched in a wry smile: "An apothecary doesn't need a strong physique. The healing medicine you prepared is sought after by my tribe, so don't belittle yourself."

"Thank you for your praise, Lord!"

Yang Kai's calm and composed demeanor made Lord Che look at him differently. There weren't many Upper-Level Shaman Warriors who could face him so freely.

Wait... Didn't Yu say he was just a Lower-Level Shaman Warrior? How could he be Upper-Level?

There was a two-grade difference here. Lord Che believed Yu wouldn't make a mistake about this information.

Though suspicious, Lord Che didn't ask further, only saying, "I believe Yu has already told you the purpose of inviting you here."

Yang Kai replied, "Shaman Yu did say so, and I, Shaman Niu, am willing to contribute to the Frost Snow Tribe."

Lord Che's eyes flashed. He smiled, "Very good. In that case, Yu... take Shaman Niu inside. If you need any medicinal ingredients, just ask."

"Yes!" Yu responded, then led Yang Kai inside.

Soon, the two entered a side room. To Yang Kai's surprise, there were already two people inside, both elderly, their aura much stronger than his, clearly Shaman Master level.

One elder had white hair, the other mottled. Seeing Yu and Yang Kai enter, both looked to Yu for confirmation.

Yu nodded slightly, and the two elders stepped forward, saying in unison, "Greetings, Lord Niu!"

Yang Kai was stunned, "These two are...?"

Yu's expression grew a bit uneasy as she explained, "These are assistants arranged for you by Lord Che. If you need anything, just let them know."

"Is that so..." Yang Kai smiled slightly. Though he had some guesses, he didn't point them out. It wasn't a big deal to him. Seeing Yang Kai's meaningful smile, Yu's expression became even more uneasy, with a hint of guilt.

Fortunately, Yang Kai didn't question further, only saying, "I need some medicinal ingredients. Please help me fetch them."

"Oh, alright. What do you need?" Yu quickly asked.

Yang Kai immediately listed the names of the ingredients. Yu memorized them, and the two elders perked up their ears, listening intently, not missing a single detail.

After a moment, Yang Kai finished listing the ingredients and quantities. Yu turned and went out to prepare them.

The room was left with Yang Kai and the two elders. Yang Kai turned and asked, "How should I address you, Seniors?"

The two looked at each other, embarrassed, shaking their heads and waving their hands, clearly unwilling to reveal their names.

Yang Kai found it amusing, thinking that the Ancient Barbarian Tribe truly was simple and honest. Though they came to steal techniques, their conscience was troubled, so they didn't want to reveal their names.

Yang Kai didn't press them. Instead, he found a spot, took out a handful of Inner Cores from his pouch, and swallowed them all at once.

These Inner Cores, each like a grain of rice, were small and low in energy. Yang Kai could eat dozens at a time, his stomach rumbling, leaving the two elders dumbfounded. They didn't know what he was eating, only that he was cultivating, so though surprised, they kept silent and didn't disturb him.

In less than the time it takes to burn a stick of incense, Yang Kai finished refining the Inner Cores, his aura suddenly becoming denser.

When he repeated the process, the two elders finally saw what he was eating.

The white-haired elder was shocked and quickly stopped him, "Lord Niu, these are Barbaric Beast Inner Cores! How can you swallow them?"

The mottled-haired elder also stopped him, "You can't eat those!"

No one had ever dared to swallow Barbaric Beast Inner Cores like this. Wasn't he afraid of bursting?

"I've always eaten them like this." Yang Kai said as he continued, tossing Inner Cores into his mouth, the crunching sound making the two elders shudder, looking at him like a monster.

"Would you two like some? They taste like insects, nice and crunchy!" Yang Kai smiled, opening his palm.

The two elders shook their heads like rattles, terrified.

Fortunately, at that moment, a large group of Frost Snow Tribe barbarians rushed in, each bringing a pile of medicinal ingredients. Yu led them, directing the tribe members to sort the ingredients.

Once everything was settled, Yu said, "Shaman Niu, everything you asked for is here. I'll wait outside. If you need anything else, just call me."

"Thank you!" Yang Kai nodded gently to her.

Yu turned and left, closing the door.

Yang Kai closed his eyes to regulate his breath, refined the Inner Cores, then opened his eyes and said to the two elders, "Let's begin."

The two elders straightened up, rising together.

"Two flower rain branches, one earth skin fruit, three goufei herbs..." Yang Kai continuously listed the names and quantities of ingredients, and the two elders, after a brief pause, immediately got to work, accurately finding everything Yang Kai needed, not missing a single type or amount.

Soon, a pile of ingredients was stacked before Yang Kai.

Yang Kai smiled, "Watch closely..."

As he spoke, he picked up several ingredients, and a ball of fire suddenly appeared in his other hand, flickering, its temperature rising and falling.

The two elders' expressions became serious, staring intently at Yang Kai's hands, not daring to look away for a moment.

They were the Frost Snow Tribe's best apothecaries, highly respected. This time, arranged by Shaman Che to steal techniques, they felt deeply ashamed, but knew they couldn't make the miraculous healing medicine themselves. Though they knew the ingredients, they lacked the skill.

An apothecary's recipes are often tribal secrets, never easily shared. They wanted to learn the healing medicine's method, so they cooperated as assistants to Shaman Niu, hoping to learn by watching.

Unexpectedly, Yang Kai saw through their intentions and didn't hide anything, openly preparing the medicine, even deliberately teaching them.

This generosity and spirit, compared to their own petty actions, was like heaven and earth, making the two esteemed apothecaries feel even more guilty.

Guilty as they were, the two remained extremely focused on the art of alchemy, watching intently, preparing to thank and apologize to Shaman Niu later.

With an Imperial-Grade Pill Master like Yang Kai personally demonstrating, the secret of the healing medicine was quickly understood by the two elders.

They were already skilled apothecaries; Yang Kai's healing medicine wasn't difficult to prepare, just some unusual techniques. The two elders quickly grasped it, given their deep foundation in alchemy.

In less than a day, the two apothecaries basically mastered the method, and under Yang Kai's encouragement, began to try it themselves.

Yang Kai ignored them, refining Inner Cores to improve his cultivation, waiting until they finished to check their work and point out mistakes.

The healing medicine made by the two apothecaries became increasingly perfect.

Time quietly passed. In the palace, the two elders and the young man almost forgot the passage of time.

Though the Frost Snow Tribe's apothecaries were old, they were energized every day. The more they interacted with Shaman Niu, the more they realized how terrifying his knowledge was. Even a casual pointer from him could give them sudden insight, benefiting them immensely.

The healing medicine they made was now indistinguishable from Yang Kai's own. In other words, it would become the Frost Snow Tribe's standard medicine, and ordinary injuries would no longer be a concern.

This was a blessing for the whole tribe, making the two elders deeply grateful to Yang Kai.

In ten days, all the vast medicinal ingredients Yu had delivered were turned into healing medicine by the two elders. With so much medicine, the tribe wouldn't have to worry about injuries for at least twenty years, and could even sell it to other tribes for precious cultivation and living supplies.

While the two elders focused on alchemy, they didn't notice that at a certain moment, Yang Kai's aura suddenly surged, and at the same time, all the insect Inner Cores he had collected were completely used up.

Feeling delighted, Yang Kai opened his eyes slightly, his gaze flashing like lightning, dazzling.

The hardships of the early stage were over. The cultivation ahead would be smooth, and what awaited him would be a period of rapid progress.

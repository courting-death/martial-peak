## Chapter 2622 — Lady Dong

The old woman coughed violently again, her frail body wracked with such force it seemed as if her very heart and lungs might be expelled. Only after a long while did she finally catch her breath. Turning her head weakly, she said, "It's nothing, it's nothing. Just old age catching up with me. Thank you, young lady."

Ji Yao shook her head. Before she could let go of her hand, the old woman's legs suddenly gave out again.

Startled, Ji Yao quickly supported her, frowning as she said, "Granny, let me help you."

The old woman replied, "Thank you, thank you. Such a kind-hearted young lady. You will surely be rewarded in the future. Cough, cough..."

Ji Yao smiled slightly and said, "Our Honored Master always teaches us disciples to respect the elderly and care for the young when traveling. It's just a small effort."

The old woman chuckled softly and said, "Such a fine young lady, and your master has taught you well. May I ask, which Sect do you hail from?"

Ji Yao shook her head and smiled without answering.

Yang Kai's eyes flickered as he smiled and said, "Respecting the old and caring for the young is indeed a virtue, but when traveling, you must also keep your eyes open and not be easily deceived. The world is full of trickery and malice. Sometimes, a good deed can be used by someone with ill intent, and then you become an accomplice to evil. Wouldn't you agree, Granny?"

The old woman's body trembled imperceptibly, forcing a smile as she said, "This young man speaks the truth."

Ji Yao nodded seriously and said, "This disciple will remember."

"Disciple?" The old woman was startled when she heard this, faintly realizing that the Honored Master Ji Yao mentioned earlier was actually the man standing behind her. This made her feel somewhat incredulous.

"Granny, your earrings are quite exquisite," Yang Kai suddenly remarked out of nowhere.

The old woman was startled, subconsciously touching her earlobe, but found it empty. She turned her head and forced a smile, "Young man, your eyes must be playing tricks on you. At my age, how could I still wear earrings?"

Yang Kai smiled and said, "You may not be wearing them today, but I’d wager you wore them yesterday. The earring holes are still fresh—certainly not the look of someone who’s gone years without jewelry."

Hearing the veiled barbs in Yang Kai’s words, even Ji Yao—no fool herself—sensed something was amiss. Her heart stirred, and she quietly channeled her Emperor Yuan into the old woman’s body to probe her condition.

In the next moment, Ji Yao's expression turned cold. She grabbed the old woman's arm and said icily, "Who are you, and why did you deceive me?"

She hadn't paid much attention before, but now that she probed, she discovered that this seemingly gravely ill old woman was actually brimming with vitality inside. The appearance of frailty was clearly a disguise; everything was an act.

This made Ji Yao furious. She had just kindly helped this person, only to find out the old woman had ulterior motives. It truly infuriated her.

The old woman's cultivation wasn't high, only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. With Ji Yao's grip, she couldn't move at all. All her Origin Qi was suppressed and couldn't circulate. Now she was truly panicked, looking at Ji Yao pleadingly and whispering, "Senior, I mean no harm. I was forced to take this desperate measure. Please, Senior, be magnanimous and don't expose me."

Ji Yao's expression shifted. She glanced at the guards at the city gate and asked, "Are they here for you?"

The old woman nodded silently.

"Why did you think to use me?" Ji Yao asked coldly.

The old woman replied, "Earlier, when Young Master Bai and Miss Yan entered the city, they seemed very wary of you. So..."

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows and said, "Lady Dong, you have sharp eyes. Even such a small matter didn't escape you."

Hearing this, the old woman's body trembled violently. She looked at Yang Kai in horror and asked, "You know me?"

"Lady Dong?" Ji Yao also frowned, unable to understand how her Honored Master had so easily seen through the old woman's identity. As far as she knew, this was their first time in Taiping City, and they had no acquaintances here.

Yang Kai said, "I don't know you, but I've heard some things about you."

"What... what did they say about me?" the old woman asked in a trembling voice, as if desperate to know.

Yang Kai replied calmly, "They say you are the Dong Family Head’s wife. That you stole the clan’s treasured heirloom, enraging the Family Head. He even requested experts from the City Lord’s Mansion to seal off the area and search for you. If anyone discovers your whereabouts, they are to quietly apprehend you."

Lady Dong let out a bitter laugh and said, "Stealing the clan’s treasured heirloom... Such a crime is far too great for me to bear."

Ji Yao frowned, "Is what my Honored Master said not true?"

Lady Dong shook her head, "What treasured heirloom? I've never seen such a thing. The Dong Family doesn't have anything like that. I've been the Family Head's wife for so many years—if there were such a thing, how could I not know?"

"Then why are they slandering you?" Ji Yao asked curiously.

Lady Dong replied, "Naturally, because they want to catch me."

Ji Yao seemed to want to ask more, but Yang Kai waved his hand and said, "Lady Dong, we have no interest in your affairs. You tried to use us to leave the city, but I won't hold it against you. Take care of yourself!"

"Sir..." Lady Dong looked at Yang Kai pleadingly. "What happened before was my fault. I only ask that you help me leave Taiping City. I will repay your great kindness one day."

Yang Kai sneered, "What could you possibly repay me with? I have no interest in meddling in other people's messes!"

"Sir..."

"If you say one more word, I'll seize you right now and throw you out!" Yang Kai said coldly, his eyes fixed on her.

Lady Dong shivered. She had no doubt Yang Kai had the ability. Even Ji Yao, his disciple, could subdue her instantly—how could her Honored Master be any weaker?

She had hoped to persuade Yang Kai to help, but it was just wishful thinking.

Turning around in despair, Lady Dong stood in the middle of the group, not daring to say another word.

"Honored Master, this person's disguise technique is truly impressive!" Ji Yao secretly sent a message to Yang Kai. If not for his earlier suspicion, she would never have guessed that this old woman was in disguise.

This was not some secret art, but a pure disguise technique—not something just anyone could master.

Yang Kai nodded slightly and said, "Indeed, it's impressive. If I hadn't been on guard, I might not have noticed her either."

It was only because he had overheard a conversation between Commander Yu and the Dong Family Head, and noticed the fresh earring holes on the old woman's ears, that he became suspicious.

"Honored Master, are we really just going to stand by? She seems to have some unspeakable difficulties," Ji Yao said, feeling a bit of pity as she watched the old woman look so lost.

Yang Kai replied indifferently, "The world is full of deceit. We don't know who's right or wrong in this matter. If we intervene rashly, we might just be helping the wicked."

"Yes!" Ji Yao nodded.

As they spoke, the group had already reached the old woman.

A guard at the city gate beckoned to the old woman. She trembled as she walked over, and the guard opened a cloth bag in front of her to let her see inside.

With just one glance, the old woman's face changed dramatically. Then, a squeaking sound came from the bag, as if some demonic beast was calling out.

The old woman's demeanor changed completely. No longer feeble, she moved with astonishing agility, darting straight for the city gate.

"Catch her!" the guard shouted.

"Since you've come out, you might as well stay." Another voice rang out, and a blade flashed through the air, slashing toward the old woman's path.

The old woman's expression changed again. She knew that if she forced her way forward, she would definitely be injured. With no other choice, she retreated a few steps.

Swish, swish, swish...

Figures suddenly shot out from all directions, all rushing toward the old woman.

Delayed for just a moment, by the time the old woman came to her senses, she was already surrounded.

Ji Yao couldn't help but sigh as she watched. She knew the old woman had no hope of escaping now.

The old woman only had the cultivation of the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, but those surrounding her were five or six of the same stage, none weaker than her. With so many people, if she still managed to escape, it would be a joke.

"Those are the Dong Family's Elders!"

"That's right, they're definitely the Dong Family's Elders."

"Strange, what are they doing here? Who is that old woman, and how did she provoke the Dong Family?"

The nearby cultivators murmured among themselves, all curious about the scene before them.

However, although the Dong Family's Elders had surrounded the old woman, they didn't make a move. Clearly, they all knew her true identity, and for a moment, their gazes were extremely complicated.

After all, she was the Family Head's wife—her status was no lower than theirs.

Two more people walked over from the side and arrived at the city gate. The guards all cupped their fists in greeting.

"Greetings, Commander Yu."

"Greetings, Dong Family Head!"

Yang Kai glanced over and saw that one wore black armor, looking imposing, while the other wore a blue robe, exuding a natural authority.

Both were at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.

The one in black armor should be Commander Yu, and the other was naturally the Dong Family Head.

The old woman turned around, her gaze fixed on the Dong Family Head. She gritted her teeth and said, "Let me go!"

The Dong Family Head slowly shook his head and said, "You know that's impossible."

"I'm begging you, let me go. If not today, then tomorrow I'll still leave. You can't stop me!"

"If I break your legs and cripple your cultivation, how will you leave?" the Dong Family Head said coldly.

The old woman trembled all over at his words, unable to believe it. "We've been husband and wife for a hundred years. Are you really going to be so heartless?"

"Husband and wife?"

The old woman's words shocked the onlookers. Wasn't the Dong Family Head's wife supposed to be a great beauty? When did she become an old woman?

For a moment, the crowd whispered among themselves, making the Dong Family Head's face look extremely unpleasant.

The old woman standing in midair wiped her face, removing her disguise and revealing a beautiful countenance.

"Hiss... It's really Lady Dong! How could this be?"

"Heh, what kind of drama is the Dong Family putting on now?"

"This is going to be a good show. The Family Head's wife actually wants to escape the Dong Family, and the Dong Family has mobilized everyone to stop her. Who knows what secrets are behind this?"

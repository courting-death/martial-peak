## Chapter 2863 — Luring the Enemy

When Yang Kai returned from the Small Xuan Realm, his expression was grave.

After a candid conversation with Shaya, he had learned a great deal about the demons, making it clear that they were not an easy race to deal with. The demon race was made up of a hundred clans, each with its own innate abilities. For example, Shaya was a Succubus, and her innate ability was the Bewitching Light—a power she was born with. Once unleashed, it could easily charm those weaker than herself; at best, they would be compelled to serve her, at worst, they would lose their minds and become walking corpses.

The short, shadowy figures encountered in the previous battle were Shadow Demons, one of the hundred clans, most skilled at stealth and assassination, moving through darkness and shadow.

There were also Sand Demons, Red Demons, Green Demons…

The largest clan was called the Might Demons. They were born demon warriors. Of the demons encountered on the battlefield before, eighty percent belonged to this clan, but now they were all dead.

Yang Kai led Shaya out of his stone cave, causing a commotion.

Over a dozen shamans and other members of the Barbarian Tribe stared in shock at Demon King Shaya. The twin sisters, Yu and Lu, immediately began gathering Shamanic power in their hands, ready to fight at any moment.

This was a Demon King, the commander of the previous demon army. Naturally, everyone was on guard.

Only Die looked calm, as if she had expected this, showing no sign of surprise.

“There’s no need to be nervous, everyone. I am now the slave of Lord Shaman Niu and bear you no ill will.” Facing the crowd’s hostility, Shaya smiled faintly.

“A slave!?” The crowd cried out in shock—everyone understood what that word implied.

Yu and Lu looked to Yang Kai for confirmation. Seeing him nod, they still felt it was unreal. Although Yang Kai had always been extremely powerful, to subdue a Demon King as a slave was simply unbelievable.

Still, none of the Barbarian Tribe fully trusted Shaya. Even with Yang Kai’s assurance, they remained wary of this Demon King.

Yu and Lu stepped forward to report.

After several days of treatment by the shamans, many of the captured demonized tribesmen had been freed from the grip of Demonic Qi and regained their senses. However, perhaps because they had been demonized for too long, more than half died from their heads exploding during the process, which was truly regrettable.

This was unavoidable. Once demonized, their former kin became demonic beings—devoid of reason, recognizing neither kin nor friend. To save them, a price had to be paid.

Saving even a small number was better than letting everyone remain demonized.

In the battle a few days earlier, Shaman Niu’s tribe had lost over two hundred people, but now their numbers had been replenished. However, those who had been saved were still weak and would need time to recover.

Yu and Lu were also researching better ways to heal, using the Bloodlust Art to stimulate the tribe’s Qi and blood, supplemented by two cleansing arts. This was a method Yang Kai had casually devised before. While it had some miraculous effects, there was likely an even better way.

Yu and Lu came from the Shaman God Hall and were well-versed in such matters, making them the most suitable for this research. If they could truly succeed, the casualty rate in future rescues would surely drop dramatically.

In addition, most of the captured Demonic Beasts had been tamed by the shamans using beast-taming arts. The twins wanted to select the strongest warriors from the Shaman Niu’s tribe to form a cavalry unit, but without Yang Kai’s approval, they dared not decide, so they came to ask for his opinion.

Yang Kai naturally had no objections.

Although the demons had suffered a crushing defeat in the previous war, with countless casualties, the might of that cavalry had still impressed Yang Kai. Now that the Demonic Beasts had been tamed, it was no bad thing for the Shaman Niu’s tribe to have a true cavalry.

If such a cavalry existed, they would surely have an advantage in future battles like the last one.

“Do you need my help?” Shaya took the initiative to ask from the side.

Having cast aside her pride to submit to Yang Kai and become his slave, she was naturally eager to prove herself.

Seeing Yang Kai look at her, Shaya smiled slightly. “After all, I am a Demon King. If I am present, those beasts won’t dare act up, and I know how to handle them better.”

“Very well, go help them,” Yang Kai nodded.

Yu and Lu did not trust Shaya, but they could not refute Yang Kai’s decision. They could only leave with Shaya to select suitable cavalry members from the ranks.

The next few days passed uneventfully. The Shaman Niu’s tribe continued to recuperate, preparing for the next possible war. Their resounding victory had greatly boosted morale and confidence. They suddenly realized that the demons who had destroyed four Barbarian Tribe divisions were not as invincible as imagined. They too could bleed and die, and even fear strong enemies.

The sense of mystery and dread toward the demons vanished completely.

The unknown was a frightening force, but once you understood your opponent, you could respond appropriately and bring your abilities fully to bear.

The first large-scale battle had dispelled the Shaman Niu’s tribe’s unfamiliarity with the demons. The so-called demons were like a maiden about to be wed, with her veil slowly lifted by the Shaman Niu’s tribe.

Shaya truly lived up to her title as a Demon King—her methods were impressive.

In just a few days, she had blended into the Shaman Niu’s tribe like a fish in water. Even the dozen or so shamans, including the twins Yu and Lu, no longer regarded her with suspicion. Although Yang Kai had not deliberately observed her actions, he could sense some things through their shared Soul Brand.

Shaya had not used the Bewitching Light at all. She had simply won over the shamans with sincerity and patience, truly integrating herself into the tribe and gaining their acceptance.

With her help, the dozens of captured Demonic Beasts had smoothly become the tribe’s mounts, and a skilled cavalry was successfully formed.

Of course, Demonic Beasts still carried Demonic Qi, so the tribesmen dared not approach them casually. Only after stimulating their Qi and blood to gain some resistance to Demonic Qi would they gradually learn to ride them.

Five days later, in the stone cave, Yang Kai summoned Shaya.

“What are your orders, my lord?” When not acting wantonly, Shaya was quite dignified and beautiful. Ever since Yang Kai had warned her in the Small Xuan Realm a few days ago, she no longer dared to act flirtatious before her master. Every time she met Yang Kai, she was as proper as a maiden. Of course, her revealing clothes had not changed.

The reason she had integrated so quickly into the Shaman Niu’s tribe was partly due to her beauty. Most of the dozen shamans were men, and men liked beautiful women.

“You said before that there are other demon strongholds nearby, right?” Yang Kai looked up at her.

Shaya nodded. “That’s right. Now that the Demon Saints are recuperating, our demon race can only remain inactive. Everyone is scattered, guarding the front lines to prevent your Barbarian Tribe from advancing.”

“Where is the nearest one?”

“About three hundred li away.” Shaya vaguely understood Yang Kai’s intention and smiled. “My lord wants me to lead the way and attack them? That’s not a problem. There’s no Demon King at that stronghold, only a Demon Commander with about a thousand men. With our current strength, we could easily wipe them out.”

As a Demon King, she used the word "we" without the slightest hesitation, as if she had truly become one of the Barbarian Tribe.

“We’re not going to attack them.”

Shaya was taken aback, looking at Yang Kai in surprise.

Yang Kai grinned. “Let them come to us!”

Shaya suddenly understood Yang Kai’s plan.

“You can contact them, can’t you?” Yang Kai asked.

Shaya nodded. “We demons do have ways to communicate with each other.”

"Then go prepare. You know what needs to be done, right?"

Shaya pursed her lips into a smile. "I have reached a critical juncture in my cultivation and have no time to spare for other matters. A foreign army is attacking my base—dispatch reinforcements at once!”

“Very good.” Yang Kai nodded in satisfaction and waved. “Go!”

Shaya bowed and withdrew.

Not long after, outside the stone cave, Shaya floated in the air. A stream of pitch-black Demonic Qi suddenly emerged from her body, transforming into a Black Crow that flapped its wings and flew off into the distance, vanishing in the blink of an eye.

As she turned around, she suddenly noticed a young girl standing there, quietly watching her.

Although she had done nothing wrong, Shaya inexplicably felt guilty, as if she had just sent information detrimental to the Barbarian Tribe.

Shaya remembered this girl well. In the battle a few days ago, she had unleashed a deadly technique that even Shaya’s strongest Shadow Demons could not match.

She seemed to be called Die…

Shaya had interacted with all the other shamans and was familiar with them, but this Die remained mysterious. Shaya had only seen her a few times.

Since she rarely encountered Die, Shaya naturally did not wish to miss this opportunity to draw closer. She smiled softly and said, "Hello there."

Die continued to watch her quietly, not responding, like a delicate puppet.

For some reason, Shaya suddenly felt a little afraid and gave up on getting closer. She explained, “Just now, my lord ordered me to send a message to the nearby demons, luring them into our trap.”

Die still gazed at her with that calm look.

Shaya felt uncomfortable all over and forced a smile. “I’ll go report back.”

With that, she brushed past Die.

After walking a dozen steps, Shaya looked back and was stunned to find no trace of anyone behind her. That girl had appeared suddenly and left just as mysteriously. Even as a Demon King, Shaya had not sensed a thing.

## Chapter 2692 — The Eight Directions Element-Splitting Array

"By the way, where is Chai Hu?" Yang Kai suddenly remembered someone.

Chai Hu wasn't from the Hengluo Star Domain; he was a native of the Star Realm.

However, he was extremely close with Ghost Ancestor, Chi Yue, Ai Ou, and the others—even sworn brothers with them.

Back then, five people came with Yang Kai from the Hengluo Star Domain: Wu Dao, Ghost Ancestor, Ai Ou, Chi Yue, and Gu Cangyun.

Wu Dao had once been the number one expert of his Star Domain, but fate is ever fickle. In the end, he perished during the Heaven-and-Earth Energy Baptism while attempting to break through to the Dao Origin Realm. The remaining four—Ghost Ancestor, Ai Ou, Chi Yue, and Gu Cangyun—struggled to survive in Tianhe City, enduring hardship at every turn.

It was during that time that the four met Chai Hu, who helped them a lot. As their friendship deepened, they became sworn siblings: Ghost Ancestor as the eldest, Ai Ou second, Gu Cangyun third, Chai Hu fourth, and Chi Yue as their fifth younger sister.

When Yang Kai passed through Tianhe City back then, he happened to encounter the City Lord, Luo Jin, trying to force Chi Yue into marriage. At that time, Chai Hu kidnapped Luo Jin's daughter, Luo Bing, as a hostage in an attempt to rescue Chi Yue, but ultimately failed. If Yang Kai hadn't been there, Chai Hu might not have made it out alive.

Later, Yang Kai caused a huge commotion at the City Lord’s mansion in Tianhe City, killed many people, and took a group out, settling them in the Thousand Leaves Sect.

Now, he suddenly remembered Chai Hu again.

Hearing Yang Kai's question, Ghost Ancestor cackled, "Fourth Brother's got a love tribulation!"

"With whom?" Yang Kai was stunned.

Ai Ou also wore a strange expression. "That Luo Bing—do you remember her?"

"Her?" Yang Kai was taken aback, hardly able to believe it.

Chai Hu once kidnapped Luo Bing. By rights, even if they weren't enemies, they shouldn't be flirting with each other. He really couldn't figure out how those two ended up falling for each other.

Ai Ou said, "When Fourth Brother kidnapped that girl, she happened to be in some danger. So, although he took her hostage, he also saved her. That girl is clear about gratitude and grudges, and she developed some feelings for Fourth Brother. Unfortunately, her father died at your hands, Yang boy. And since Fourth Brother is our brother, she could never get past that. Even if she had feelings for him, she kept them hidden. Two years ago, she left, and Fourth Brother, worried, followed her. There's been no news since, and no one knows where they've gone."

Gu Cangyun said, "Blessing or misfortune, who can say? Because of this, Fourth Brother avoided this calamity."

Yang Kai nodded slightly. Since Chai Hu hadn't been in the Thousand Leaves Sect all this time, there was nothing to worry about. He only asked for Ghost Ancestor and the others' sake; he himself had no real connection with Chai Hu.

"Fifth Sister is still in that old bastard's hands," Ai Ou said grimly. "Who knows if she'll be brought out for auction."

"Let's hope so," Gu Cangyun said, turning to look at the high platform. Since the rest of them had been brought out, logically, Chi Yue should be as well.

Yang Kai said calmly, "If I were him, I'd definitely keep one hostage."

The others were startled by his words.

Just then, Hua Qingsi on the high platform spoke up: "Everyone, this auction is now concluded, but please don't be impatient. Since you've all come from afar, my Master has prepared a generous gift for you. I hope you won't find it lacking."

"They really are holding her back," Ai Ou's face darkened, looking extremely grim.

The auction was over, yet Chi Yue was still nowhere to be seen. Clearly, just as Yang Kai suspected, she was being kept as a hostage.

Although they'd all been detained by that old man, with their cultivation sealed, they never understood what he was after.

"Yao King, protect them!" Yang Kai ordered, his figure flickering as he suddenly shot out from Room One, hovering in midair above the auction hall. He looked toward the backstage area.

It was empty there—Wu Ming was long gone.

He shot Hua Qingsi a look and sent a message: "Sister Hua, go to Room One."

Yingfei was there. Even if something happened, he could at least help out.

Hua Qingsi nodded, stomped her foot, and dashed toward Room One, vanishing in the blink of an eye.

In the hall and in the private rooms, countless eyes turned to Yang Kai, not knowing what this arrogant young man was up to.

Yang Kai unleashed his Divine Sense like a tidal wave and called out loudly, "Old dog, you used this auction to lure me out. Well, here I am! You used my friends to buy time and set up your arrangements—I've played along. So what now? Are you still going to hide, too scared to show yourself?"

His words rang out, but there was no response at all.

Instead, the many cultivators in the hall looked at each other, whispering and gossiping, casting looks at Yang Kai as if he were crazy.

"Sneaky and shameful! Old dog, you're at least an elder of the Star God Palace, yet you're this cowardly?" Yang Kai sneered, unleashing a torrent of mockery.

"What? An elder of the Star God Palace?"

"Where did this Star God Palace elder come from?"

"What does he mean?"

Everyone was shocked. Yang Kai's words were like a stone thrown into a pond, stirring up waves.

In the Southern Territory, Star God Palace wasn't just a name—it was a symbol, an invincible force. After all, it was the sect of a Great Emperor, the giant that guarded the Southern Territory.

And the elders of Star God Palace were figures rarely seen by ordinary people, their status no less than the sect masters of top sects.

Judging by Yang Kai's words, he seemed to have a grudge with a Star God Palace elder…

Wasn't this courting death?

In the Southern Territory, of all people to offend, he had to offend a Star God Palace elder—and dared to shout and curse at him, calling him "old dog" so casually. He really didn't know the immensity of heaven and earth.

For a moment, many people looked at Yang Kai as if he were already dead.

To offend a Star God Palace elder like this—how could he possibly survive?

"Hahahaha!" Suddenly, a wild laugh rang out, the voice drifting unpredictably, impossible to pin down. All the Emperor Realm experts in the private rooms turned pale, feeling their auras stirred by the laughter. Their Emperor Yuan churned uncontrollably, and they were horrified, realizing the one laughing was a truly terrifying existence.

Could this be the Star God Palace elder? There weren't many elders in Star God Palace, so which one was this?

"I've long heard of Young friend Yang's reputation. Meeting you today, you truly live up to it."

Yang Kai snorted coldly. "I have no interest in speaking with cowards who skulk in the shadows. Show yourself!"

The voice fell silent for a moment, then spoke: "Since you insist, Young friend Yang, as you wish."

As the words fell, the world suddenly darkened. The entire auction venue lit up with countless array patterns, which flared like sparks and spread in all directions.

In the next instant, everyone felt as if the world had turned upside down and space itself was splitting apart.

An invisible force tugged at them, as if to throw them into endless void.

"Hmm? The Eight Directions Element-Splitting Array!" In Room One, the Gong family head's face changed. He quickly took out a set of array flags, poured Emperor Yuan into them, and with a flurry of hand seals, tossed the flags into the air.

The flags quickly vanished, and the chaotic space in Room One immediately stabilized.

"Gong Tai, are you courting death!" came an angry shout—the same mysterious voice as before. Gong Tai's actions had clearly enraged him, and his words carried a violent power that shook the mind.

That force crashed into Gong Tai's Sea of Consciousness, making his head throb with pain. He spat out a mouthful of blood and collapsed to the ground.

It wasn't over yet. Suddenly, a five-colored radiance burst out in Room One, condensing into a sharp blade that slashed straight at Gong Tai.

This five-colored blade was the power of a formidable array that had previously killed an Emperor Realm expert.

Seeing this, Gong Tai's face turned ashen. He cried out in his heart that his life was over.

Just as the five-colored blade was about to strike, an eagle's cry rang out. An eagle's claw suddenly reached out, grabbed the blade, and with a crisp snap, shattered it.

Gong Tai gasped for breath, his face pale. He knew someone had just saved his life at the critical moment.

With lingering fear, he looked to the side and saw Yingfei nod at him. "Relax. With this king here, no one can kill you."

Gong Tai stood up and bowed respectfully. "Many thanks for your protection, Yao King."

An elder of Star God Palace wanted him dead, but a Yao King saved his life. The irony was almost laughable.

Yingfei waved it off. "No need. By the way, that Eight Directions Element-Splitting Array you mentioned—what does it do?"

When Yang Kai brought Gong Tai over, he'd chatted with him a bit. Of course Yingfei knew Gong Tai was a master of arrays; otherwise, he wouldn't have recognized the array at a glance or had the means to counter it.

Ghost Ancestor and the others also looked at Gong Tai curiously.

Gong Tai sighed. "It's an array that separates heaven and earth and splits space. I noticed there was an array within an array here, but I didn't expect it to be the Eight Directions Element-Splitting Array."

"An array within an array? What else is there?" Yingfei frowned.

"The Grand Square Five Elements Array!" Gong Tai replied. "These two arrays complement each other, each relying on the other. The one who set them up is truly skilled."

After a pause, he looked at Yingfei and asked, "May I ask, Yao King, what grudge does Junior Brother Yang have with this Star God Palace elder, that he would go so far as to set up two grand arrays to trap him?"

Yingfei's face darkened. "Are you saying this array can trap people?"

Gong Tai nodded. "That's right. The Grand Square Five Elements Array seals heaven and earth, locking down space. The auction hall is now sealed off. Unless the array is broken, no one can leave. The Eight Directions Element-Splitting Array isolates everyone here, putting them in separate spaces. If I hadn't acted quickly to counter it, we would have been separated too."

Hearing this, Yingfei's face changed dramatically. "Not good!"

He turned to look, but Yang Kai was nowhere to be seen. Outside Room One was pitch black, as if the whole room had sunk into the void. Even probing with Divine Sense, he couldn't sense anyone.

The thousand or so people at the auction had all suddenly vanished without a trace.

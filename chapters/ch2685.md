## Chapter 2685 — Greed Without Limit

Yang Kai reached out and drew the tray into his hand. Lifting the red cloth covering it, he glanced inside and saw a puppet about a foot tall, shaped like a wolf.

“Is the item correct?”

Yang Kai turned to look at Ye Hen and asked.

Ye Hen responded excitedly, “It is indeed my Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppet, Tianlang!”

Having recovered the lost item, Ye Hen was nearly moved to tears.

Yang Kai nodded slightly and casually tossed the Heavenly Puppet to Ye Hen, saying, “Put it away.”

He couldn’t help but marvel inwardly. Back in the day, he had seen the original forms of these Heavenly Puppets—each one towering like a mountain, guarding the vital points of the Thousand Leaves Sect. Who would have thought they could be shrunk down to such a size?

The puppet arts of the Thousand Leaves Sect truly were extraordinary.

Ye Hen was stunned and said, “But this is your auction item, Young Master Yang. I… feel unworthy to accept it!”

This was, after all, something worth a million Upper-Level Source Crystals—a tremendous value. Ye Hen felt embarrassed to take it for free.

Yang Kai smiled, “This item can only be used to its fullest in your hands. Take it. There may be a fierce battle soon.”

The puppets of the Thousand Leaves Sect are refined and controlled by secret arts, passed down through generations. Even if others obtain them, they cannot unleash their full power. Only those who have cultivated the corresponding secret arts can unlock their greatest value.

Hearing Yang Kai’s words, Ye Hen’s heart tightened, and he silently put away the Heavenly Puppet.

The maid’s heart fluttered with unease for no apparent reason. She forced a smile and looked at Yang Kai, saying, “Sir, the auction item has been delivered. Please pay the Source Crystals!”

Yang Kai waved his hand, “No rush. I still have items to bid on. I’ll pay everything together when the auction ends to avoid trouble.”

“Ah.” The maid frowned deeply, pleading as she looked at Yang Kai, “But… but that’s not our rule, and I can’t make the decision.”

Yang Kai glanced at her indifferently, “If you can’t decide, go ask someone who can.”

His gaze sent a chill through her. The maid shivered, dared not say more, apologized, and hurried off to report.

Just then, another maid ascended the platform carrying a tray. Hua Qingsi announced coldly, “The second auction item: Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppet, Hundred-Legged Silver Spider. Starting price: one million Upper-Level Source Crystals!”

After speaking, she stood quietly.

Yang Kai immediately said, “One million, I want it. I hope everyone will give me face again.”

His words caused an uproar. The Emperor Realm cultivators gritted their teeth in anger.

When the first item, Tianlang, appeared, Yang Kai had said the same thing, and the Emperor Realm cultivators had indeed given him face, not competing. But now, with the second item, he repeated the phrase, making him seem greedy and ignorant of his limits.

Still, recalling the Yao King behind Yang Kai and his lawless behavior, most Emperor Realm cultivators chose to remain silent, not wanting to stand out. Only those unaware of his background mocked him quietly in their private rooms, but none raised the price.

Though their voices were low, there were many Emperor Realm cultivators present, all sharp-eared, so nothing was missed.

Some worried Yang Kai might lash out, but his room remained calm, with no special reaction.

Meanwhile, the maid who had delivered Tianlang to Yang Kai found Wu Ming and carefully recounted what had happened.

Wu Ming listened and cursed angrily, “Damn it, he actually wants a free meal!”

One million Upper-Level Source Crystals was a considerable fortune for him, but judging by Yang Kai’s attitude, he clearly didn’t intend to pay, using any excuse to dismiss the maid.

Wu Ming believed that even after the auction ended, Yang Kai wouldn’t pay a single Source Crystal.

Such behavior was utterly disgraceful—a petty act, unworthy of respect.

“Sir, what should we do now?” The maid panicked. If word spread that someone got items for free at their auction house, the Qiyao Commerce Guild’s reputation would be ruined. But as a maid, she had no authority to decide.

Wu Ming’s eyes flickered, and he snorted coldly, “No matter. Ignore him.”

In any case, Yang Kai was doomed today. No matter how many treasures he had hidden in that Spatial Ring, they would all become spoils of war soon enough. He wanted to freeload? Let him. Once the trap was sprung, everything would be swept up in one net—then we’ll see who truly has the upper hand.

The maid was startled, “But he’s bidding again. If he wins more, what should we do?”

Wu Ming waved his hand, “Send everything to him.”

“Ah!” The maid was shocked, not expecting Wu Ming to give such an answer. But before the auction began, all maids had been instructed to follow Wu Ming’s orders, so she dared not object, though she found it strange—what was the point of today’s auction?

Wu Ming sneered, glancing at Yang Kai’s room, thinking, Let him be smug for now. Once Master’s preparations are complete, he’ll die with no grave.

The entire auction was both a lure for Yang Kai and a tactic to buy time. While the auction proceeded, Master was secretly preparing. Once the net was cast, no one would escape.

The second item received no bids, and soon another maid delivered it to Yang Kai’s room.

Apparently instructed, this maid wisely didn’t ask Yang Kai for Source Crystals, simply delivered the item and withdrew respectfully.

Yang Kai casually tossed it to Ye Hen again.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian watched from the side, secretly excited, feeling that they might recover all the Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppets this trip. If so, the sect could rise again. Many secret manuals had been taken into Emperor Heaven Valley; if they reclaimed the puppets and recruited new disciples, the Thousand Leaves Sect would surely shine once more.

“The third auction item…” On the platform, Hua Qingsi continued her duties as auctioneer, expression still indifferent.

But before she finished, Yang Kai called out, “One million, I want it.”

No need to guess—it was surely another Heavenly Puppet.

The Thousand Leaves Sect originally had ten Heavenly Puppets, but after Liu Yan took the White Tiger, only nine remained. Auctioning them one by one would take quite some time.

From Room Seven came an elderly voice, full of dissatisfaction and indignation: “Friend, do you expect us to give you face again?”

Yang Kai grinned, “If you would, that would be best.”

The old man in Room Seven snorted, “Face is earned, not given.”

“Indeed, things can happen once or twice, but not thrice.” Someone immediately chimed in, apparently from Room Thirteen. “Since it’s an auction, the one with wealth wins. Your approach is rather disrespectful to us.”

“Boy, you’ve gotten two Heavenly Puppets. You should be satisfied. Don’t bid for the rest.”

“Exactly. We traveled thousands of li for these Heavenly Puppets. If you want to take them all, be careful not to bite off more than you can chew.”

With someone speaking up, others followed. Earlier, not knowing Yang Kai’s background, everyone held back, letting him snatch two bargains. But seeing his lack of reaction to their private criticisms, they felt he wasn’t so formidable, and began to mock and advise him.

Everyone wanted good things. Seeing Yang Kai’s posture, they realized if they didn’t speak up, he might monopolize the auction. Giving him the base price for two Heavenly Puppets was already a huge favor; he should be content.

Many secretly regretted not bidding earlier. Usually, the first few auction items are cheaper, with prices rising later.

Only those who knew Yang Kai had a Yao King at his side and had seen him beat Wu Ming kept silent, inwardly sneering, thinking these Emperor Realm cultivators were courting disaster.

Yang Kai dared to beat Wu Ming into a pulp at the auction entrance, showing he was reckless and bold. Provoking him now was asking for trouble; few Emperor Realm cultivators present were stronger than Wu Ming.

Most simply watched from the sidelines, eager to see how the drama would unfold.

To their surprise, Yang Kai didn’t intend to fight anyone. After a moment’s silence, he said, “If you won’t give face, then let’s bid. No need for so many words.”

This stunned many, wondering what was wrong with him. Had he changed? Or was it because of the crowd that he hesitated?

After all, it’s hard to go against the anger of the masses. Beating Wu Ming was one thing, but if he kept demanding face, he’d offend all the Emperor Realm cultivators present.

If that happened, even he couldn’t fight everyone alone.

Thinking this, those who had kept silent grew excited, feeling it was time to join the bidding.

With Yang Kai no longer the biggest obstacle, they had nothing to fear. It was all about who had the deepest pockets to win the Heavenly Puppets.

With this realization, the Emperor Realm cultivators straightened up, ready to compete fiercely.

The old man in Room Seven smiled and said, “You’re teachable. This is the atmosphere an auction should have. In that case, I’ll start—one million one hundred thousand!”

“One million two hundred thousand!”

“One million five hundred thousand!”

“Two million!”

Shouts rose from all directions. In less than ten breaths, the price for the Heavenly Puppet, starting at one million Upper-Level Source Crystals, soared to three million—and it was far from over.

## Chapter 3045 — Capturing Fu Ling

Zhu Lie left.

Yang Kai was the only one left on the island.

His expression was gloomy, his face twisting into a savage snarl. The news he had just received from Zhu Lie sent his emotions churning, and he could not calm himself for a long time.

Zhu Qing was getting married in one month? Yang Kai suddenly threw back his head and let out a baleful laugh. His Imperial Might erupted, and the Emperor Yuan spilling from his body became a circular shockwave that swept outward in every direction!

Trees swayed as sand and stones flew through the air. In an instant, the island that had resembled an immortal paradise was reduced to a complete mess.

As his laughter gradually died away, Yang Kai turned and glanced in a certain direction. In the waters at the edge of the island stood a beautiful purple coral reef, clear and lustrous as crystal.

Pushing off lightly, Yang Kai soared into the air and vanished into the night sky in the blink of an eye.

Tranquility returned to the island.

After an unknown amount of time, the purple coral reef suddenly warped and shifted, transforming into a graceful figure. Clad in purple robes, she had an alluring and shapely form.

She stared in the direction Yang Kai had departed, blinked her peach-blossom eyes, and suddenly curled her lips into a smile. "It seems I've discovered a great secret. Zhu Lie, oh Zhu Lie, let's see how you escape from my grasp now. You dare look down on me? This time, I'll teach you a lesson."

She chuckled for a while, looking delighted beyond measure. Her eyes darted about as she hatched a devious scheme, then she turned to leave.

But at that instant, her body suddenly stiffened. A chill swept over her from head to toe, and all the blood in her body seemed to freeze.

Yang Kai, who had left long ago, had somehow returned and now stood silently behind her. His eyes, crimson with rage, stared at her coldly as though he were looking at a dead woman.

"You..." Fu Ling paled in fright and instinctively shot backward, trying to put some distance between herself and Yang Kai.

She had heard every word of the earlier conversation between Zhu Lie and Yang Kai, so she naturally knew just how capable this seemingly ordinary human was. Her first reaction was therefore to flee; the thought of fighting Yang Kai never even crossed her mind.

It was not because she was cowardly. There was no such thing as cowardice among the Dragon Clan. They stood above all living creatures and at the head of all Divine Spirits. The only ones who could make them afraid were stronger members of their own clan.

She had retreated because she knew she was no match for Yang Kai.

She possessed only a Sixth-Tier Dragon Bloodline. Even Zhu Lie and his elder sister, Zhu Qing, who both possessed Eighth-Tier Dragon Bloodlines, had suffered grievously at Yang Kai's hands. How could she possibly be his opponent?

It had to be said that this was an extremely wise choice. As long as she could put some distance between herself and Yang Kai at the first opportunity and escape into the sea, her myriad transformation abilities might truly allow her to survive this calamity.

Fu Ling pushed her speed to the limit, her entire body transforming into a purple dragon that tore through the void.

Yang Kai did not move and continued to stare at her coldly, yet he clung to her like a maggot to bone, always remaining within three steps of her no matter how she tried to shake him off.

Fu Ling's face went pale. Circulating her Dragon Yuan, she prepared to call for help.

It might not work, but perhaps it would make the human before her hesitate.

"Look at me!" Yang Kai shouted. His voice boomed like thunder and condensed into a thread that pierced Fu Ling's eardrums.

Fu Ling instinctively looked up and saw that Yang Kai had already assumed his four-zhang Dragon form. His enormous figure loomed like a great mountain pressing down on her heart, leaving her unable to breathe. Even more terrifying was the pressure radiating from him, which froze her blood and prevented her from using even half her strength.

What terrified Fu Ling most was the brilliant golden light blazing from Yang Kai's left eye. Her mind instantly grew unstable and her Divine Soul trembled. Immediately afterward, an enormous palm resembling a broad fan slapped toward her.

Fu Ling was horrified, her heart consumed by boundless terror. She wanted to open her mouth and beg for mercy, but no matter how she tried, she could not make a sound, as though she had been dragged into a nightmare.

In the next instant, a tremendous suction force appeared. The boundless sea suddenly vanished, as did the nearby island. Solid ground appeared beneath her feet, and the surrounding scenery transformed in a bizarre fashion.

"Cough... cough, cough..." Fu Ling knelt on one knee and gasped heavily, her face as pale as paper. In that brief instant, sweat had soaked through her clothes. The thin fabric clung tightly to her exquisite, shapely body, outlining a breathtaking sight.

A pair of huge feet suddenly appeared before her.

Fu Ling raised her head with difficulty and looked up at the figure blocking her entire field of vision, her delicate body trembling uncontrollably. Her nightmarish experience just now had left saliva trickling from the corner of her mouth, lending her a peculiar allure.

Although she knew Yang Kai was highly capable, she had never imagined that even with all her abilities, she would be utterly powerless to resist him.

Just what was going on with this man? How was he even more terrifying than Zhu Lie had reported?

When Zhu Lie and his elder sister returned to Dragon Isle some time ago, they had gone to meet several Elders. Zhu Qing had subsequently been imprisoned, and news suddenly spread that she was to marry Fu Chi. All of Dragon Isle was shaken, and everyone marveled at the news.

After all, the whole affair was strange, and everyone wanted to know the reason behind it.

Fu Ling was no exception. Women were naturally fond of gossip, and members of the Dragon Clan were no different. After going to some trouble, she had finally managed to uncover a few details.

It was said that during this trip away from Dragon Isle, Zhu Qing had actually fallen in love and carried on an illicit relationship with a human. That alone was bad enough, but Zhu Qing even intended to give herself to him. She had returned to Dragon Isle solely to ask the Elders to approve their union.

That human's name was Yang Kai, and it was said that he had obtained the Ancestral Dragon Origin and enjoyed extraordinary opportunities.

But a human was still a human, so how could the Elders possibly agree to Zhu Qing's request? This was not the first time such a thing had happened. The one from more than ten years ago had been exiled to the Dragon Tomb and would never emerge again in this lifetime. Her fate had been countless times more tragic than Zhu Qing's.

Zhu Qing had been confined to the Second Elder's Spirit Island and would marry Fu Chi in one month. As for how the human would be dealt with, Fu Ling did not know, but she could roughly guess that his future would not be pleasant.

Once Fu Chi and Zhu Qing were married, the Dragon Clan would certainly move against him.

How could the Ancestral Dragon Origin be allowed to remain outside Dragon Isle, much less under the control of a human?

Fu Ling knew a few things about Yang Kai, but she had never expected him to be audacious enough to secretly infiltrate Dragon Isle. Had she not unexpectedly spotted Zhu Lie today and followed him here, she would never have discovered this astonishing secret.

Now, she was beginning to regret it. Her presence had been exposed, and she was no match for Yang Kai. She had no idea what fate awaited her.

After a brief inner struggle, she suddenly sprang up and leapt several hundred zhang away.

Looking back, she was overjoyed to see that Yang Kai was still standing in the same spot, staring at her coldly with no intention of pursuing her.

Fu Ling no longer hesitated. With a roar, a surge of purple Qi shot into the sky and rose straight into the clouds, transforming into the shape of a dragon that lingered for a long time.

"You're dead! My clansmen will be here any moment. Kneel obediently and beg for mercy, and perhaps I'll spare your life!" Fu Ling stopped several hundred zhang away and smiled at Yang Kai.

The Secret Technique she had just used was a distress signal. She believed that every member of the Dragon Clan on Dragon Isle was now rushing toward this place, so she had nothing to fear and no longer needed to be afraid of Yang Kai.

With her courage restored, her earlier unease vanished completely.

Yang Kai remained silent, merely staring at Fu Ling with a mocking expression. He did not look nervous in the slightest.

Fu Ling clicked her tongue in wonder and looked him up and down before asking in surprise, "You're not afraid?"

"Why should I be?" Yang Kai grinned.

Fu Ling nodded. "You certainly have courage." She tossed her hair, and once her fear disappeared, her whole person seemed to glow. Her misty eyes appeared capable of speech and were deeply seductive. "But courage alone couldn't have been enough to catch Zhu Qing's eye, could it? Just what does she see in you? She was even willing to leave Dragon Isle to be with you."

She was happy to chat with Yang Kai and stall for time. Once her clansmen arrived, no matter how great his abilities were, he would not be able to stir up any trouble. Suddenly, she covered her mouth and giggled. "Could it be... that you're especially skilled in that regard? Zhu Qing had a taste and couldn't get enough, so she... hehehe... What an inexperienced woman."

Yang Kai was not at all surprised to hear such shameless words from her. He had, after all, witnessed Fu Ling's debauched lifestyle a few days ago.

"Who knows?" Yang Kai shrugged, still standing exactly where he was.

Fu Ling licked her red lips. They were full and lustrous, as tempting as rubies. Breathing slightly faster, she asked, "I heard that Zhu Qing was only able to advance to a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline because of you?"

This was no secret, at least not on Dragon Isle.

When Zhu Qing returned some time ago, her Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline had shaken all of Dragon Isle.

Advancing one's Dragon Bloodline was extremely difficult. Even with the constant consumption of Dragon Blood Flowers, a member of the Dragon Clan might not advance a single Tier in ten thousand years. This was especially true for someone like Zhu Qing, who had possessed an Eighth-Tier Dragon Bloodline; advancing any further was even more difficult.

Yet Zhu Qing had done it.

Although she had not explained the reason, how could the knowledgeable and experienced Elders fail to guess the truth?

The human she had joined with had obtained the Ancestral Dragon Origin. Through their union, Zhu Qing must have received tremendous benefits from him, allowing her bloodline to advance.

Fu Ling possessed only a Sixth-Tier Dragon Bloodline. If she too could receive some of his rain and dew, advancing easily to the Seventh-Tier would be no problem at all, and she might even be able to set her sights on the Eighth-Tier.

At the thought, her entire body grew hot and restless desire stirred deep within her. Her cheeks flushed, and a rosy glow spread across her long, fair neck.

"I want to try it too," Fu Ling said in a soft, seductive voice that seemed capable of stealing one's very soul. Panting lightly, she displayed her charms with seemingly casual movements.

She made no attempt to conceal her promiscuity. After all, Yang Kai had caught her indulging in carnal pleasure inside her Dragon Palace a few days ago, so there was no need to hide anything now.

Yang Kai had been peeping through the window at the time, and at the final moment, she had noticed him and memorized his appearance.

"Sorry, I'm not interested in used-up sluts!" Yang Kai sneered contemptuously.

The heat in Fu Ling's body instantly turned cold. Her previously misty, beautiful eyes became chillingly icy as she gritted her teeth and shouted, "Human, you will pay for your insolence! Once the Elders capture you, I will make sure they hand you over to me so I can take very good care of you. I hope you'll be able to endure it when the time comes. I am very difficult to satisfy."

"We can talk about that when you get the chance." Yang Kai remained unmoved, then changed the subject. "I am rather curious, though. How did you hide there without Zhu Lie or me noticing?"

## Chapter 3055 — The Dragon Isle Token Is Here

This was an intolerable humiliation! It was not only an insult to him, but an insult to the entire Dragon Clan! His eyes turned bitterly cold, and he looked at Yang Kai as though he were already dead.

"What blind fool dares act so presumptuously here?!" His hair bristled with fury as he roared.

His perfectly good wedding had been ruined, and the Dragon woman who should have belonged to him was nestled in another man's arms. No matter how much self-restraint Fu Chi possessed, he could not endure this.

Zhu Qing stared at him coldly and said in an icy voice, "Fu Chi, watch your mouth. This is my man."

"Your... man..." Fu Chi's eyes bulged, and he staggered. Then realization abruptly dawned, and he glared at Yang Kai with boundless resentment. "It's you!"

He knew that Zhu Qing had gone out and had an illicit affair with a human. If not for that, he would never have had the chance to receive the Elders' blessing for this marriage. He had planned to subdue Zhu Qing after the wedding, then leave Dragon Isle and deal with that human. Who could have known the man would come to Dragon Isle on his own and behave so outrageously on Fu Chi's wedding day?

Fu Chi's face instantly turned livid, his eyes overflowing with hatred and rage. He had marked Yang Kai for death long ago, and now that he stood face-to-face with his enemy, his fury naturally burned all the hotter.

"That's right. I'm her man." Yang Kai smiled faintly, wrapped an arm around Zhu Qing, and kissed her forehead with complete abandon, ignoring everyone around them.

Fu Chi's expression grew even uglier. His bride had been kissed by another man in front of so many people, yet not only did she show no resistance, she even looked shy and happy. The rage in his chest was like a long-suppressed volcano on the verge of erupting, and even his breaths became scorching and ragged.

Among the three Elders, Zhu Yan gazed at Yang Kai in amazement. He, too, had only just worked out Yang Kai's identity. He felt little anger and was instead rather astonished.

He had never particularly approved of Zhu Qing's marriage to Fu Chi, but Zhu Qing had indeed made a mistake, and the Dragon Clan truly did need to continue its bloodline. With the Second Elder determined to have her way, he could not oppose her too forcefully and could only allow matters to proceed.

He had assumed that after Zhu Qing married Fu Chi, she might forget that human in a few dozen or perhaps a hundred years. They could reconsider the matter then, but who could have expected such a scene today?

Fu Zhun cast Zhu Yan a chilly sideways glance and snorted. "Did you know something about this?"

Dragon Isle was not a place one could enter and leave at will. Its defenses had been strengthened more than ten years ago. Even if a Great Emperor came in person, the Dragon Clan would sense it. The whole clan would mobilize, and even a Great Emperor would not be able to force his way inside. She strongly suspected that Yang Kai had only managed to enter Dragon Isle with the Great Elder's help.

Zhu Yan shook his head. "What could I possibly know?"

Fu Zhun snorted coldly, her heart filled with doubt. If this had nothing to do with the Great Elder, how had this man appeared on Dragon Isle without anyone noticing? Could the arrangements made more than ten years ago have failed?

A sudden unease welled up within Fu Zhun, and her brow furrowed.

"This fellow certainly has courage." Fourth Elder Zhu Kong watched Yang Kai with great interest and smiled. "No wonder Qing'er is so devoted to him. I only wonder whether his luck will hold."

If today's affair was not handled properly, both Yang Kai and Zhu Qing would face disaster. The impartial and unyielding Second Elder had not the slightest trace of sentimentality.

Hearing this, the Great Elder's expression also grew solemn.

"May I ask which of you is the Second Elder?" Yang Kai turned toward the three Elders.

Although he asked the question, his gaze remained fixed on Fu Zhun's face. Among the three Elders, the Great Elder and Second Elder both possessed Tenth-Tier Dragon Bloodlines, making them easy to distinguish. The somewhat elderly man looked older than this cold-faced woman, and since the elder of the two would naturally rank first, he had to be the Great Elder. Thus, although this was Yang Kai's first time meeting the Second Elder, he recognized her at a glance.

Inwardly, he grumbled without end. This woman's cold face resembled ice that had remained frozen for ten thousand years. Why did she have to look so forbidding? He had no favorable impression of the Second Elder whatsoever and even felt rather resentful toward her. She clearly had nothing better to do. What had possessed her to single-handedly arrange Zhu Qing's marriage to Fu Chi?

Displeased though he was, Yang Kai did not want to fall out with Dragon Isle the moment he arrived. It would be best if today's affair could be settled peacefully. If that proved impossible, they would simply have to let their strength decide the outcome. He had already resolved that he would rather be shattered jade than an intact tile. No matter what happened today, he would take Zhu Qing away, even if he had to turn this place upside down.

"Release her!" the Second Elder said coldly. Her tone brooked no dispute, and she made no attempt to answer Yang Kai's question.

Yang Kai gazed at her calmly. "This Junior is Yang Kai. Greetings, Second Elder. I had no choice but to intrude so abruptly today, and I ask Second Elder to forgive my discourtesy."

"I know who you are." Fu Zhun stared at him indifferently. "If you wish to live, release Zhu Qing."

Yang Kai shook his head. "Qing'er and I love each other dearly, and Second Elder should know this perfectly well. I came today specifically to rescue her from this ordeal. I am not holding her hostage, so how can I release her? Has Second Elder perhaps misunderstood something?"

"I said, release her!" the Second Elder barked. The surrounding temperature plummeted, and the World Laws fell into disarray. All of the several hundred cultivators began trembling, regardless of their cultivation.

Few people present could withstand the wrath of an expert comparable to a Great Emperor. They stared at Yang Kai as though he were a monster, all thinking the same thing: The Dragon Clan's Second Elder is already this furious, yet you still dare contradict her so brazenly. You truly must have no idea what death is!

Yang Kai's anger surged, and his expression darkened. He had not tried to avoid antagonizing the Dragon Clan out of fear. Zhu Qing was also a member of the Dragon Clan, and if his relationship with them became too hostile, she would only be caught in the middle. That was why he had repeatedly humbled himself and spoken with complete sincerity. Yet the Second Elder refused to listen to reason, ordering him about and rebuking him without end.

He took a deep breath, suppressed his anger, and said solemnly, "Please give Qing'er and me a chance, Second Elder. My wife and I would be endlessly grateful!"

"Husband and wife?" Fu Zhun's shapely brow twitched as she said in a chilling voice, "Zhu Qing is marrying Fu Chi today. Fu Chi alone is her husband. Who do you think you are, to dare make such outrageous claims and tarnish my Dragon Clan's reputation?"

Zhu Qing immediately retorted, "Second Elder is mistaken. He is Qing'er's one and only husband. Whether the Dragon Clan acknowledges it or permits it, Qing'er belongs to him for this entire lifetime."

"Insolence!" Fu Zhun shouted. Her Dragon Pressure nearly took tangible form as it instantly enveloped Yang Kai and Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing's face turned pale, and she collapsed into Yang Kai's arms. Her Dragon Bloodline had been sealed and her cultivation suppressed. How could she possibly withstand the full brunt of the Second Elder's fury? Had her body not been so powerful, this alone would have been enough to take her life.

Yang Kai was also suddenly overcome by dizziness, his head ringing as alarm surged through him.

The wrath of a Great Emperor. So this was the wrath of a Great Emperor. With his current strength, he was still somewhat outmatched if he confronted such power head-on.

Zhu Qing's defiance had undoubtedly enraged Fu Zhun completely. She stared coldly at Yang Kai and said, "Today is a joyous occasion for the Dragon Clan, so I had no desire to shed blood. Since you remain so stubborn and refuse to see reason, however, do not blame me for showing no mercy."

As she spoke, killing intent surged like a tide.

Everyone's expression changed drastically.

Even though they were not being deliberately targeted, the several hundred cultivators found it difficult to breathe. It felt as though a mountain were pressing down on their chests, nearly making them cough up blood, and they seemed liable to die at any moment. If even they were suffering so badly, what must Yang Kai, the true target, be enduring?

Yang Kai was naturally in dire straits. The instant that killing intent enveloped him, he felt death more clearly than ever before. His entire body turned ice-cold, and the blood in his veins nearly froze.

Fu Chi stood to one side and watched coldly, inwardly delighted by the sight. Yang Kai's earlier words had filled him with rage and thoroughly humiliated him. If the Elders had not been present, he would have attacked Yang Kai long ago, but he did not dare behave too presumptuously before them. Now that the Second Elder was genuinely enraged, he immediately began gloating over Yang Kai's misfortune.

Everyone on Dragon Isle knew the Second Elder's temper. Her word had always been law, and no one dared defy her. Since Yang Kai remained so obstinate, he was doomed to meet a miserable end. As this thought crossed Fu Chi's mind, he glared resentfully at Zhu Qing, who was nestled in Yang Kai's arms, and secretly resolved to thoroughly "cherish" this slut once they were married.

The only hateful thing was that this slut's virginal Yin essence had already been taken by that human. A Dragon woman's virginal Yin essence was an extraordinary tonic for both Dragons and humans. Fu Chi was an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon, and with the help of Zhu Qing's virginal Yin essence, he might have had a chance to break through to the Ninth Tier sooner. Unfortunately...

At the thought, Fu Chi could not help feeling frustrated, and he cast Yang Kai a hostile glance.

"Wait!" Yang Kai suddenly shouted through gritted teeth, veins bulging across his forehead.

The Second Elder stared at him coldly, utterly unmoved. She raised one fair hand slightly as World Laws churned in her pale palm and a freezing aura spread outward, plainly intent on exterminating him without mercy.

Yang Kai flipped his wrist, and a token abruptly appeared in his hand. He shouted, "The Dragon Isle Token is here!"

Fu Zhun froze mid-motion.

Great Elder Zhu Yan, who had remained silent all this time, now carefully examined the token in Yang Kai's hand and nodded. "Indeed. It truly is a Dragon Isle Token."

Zhu Kong said, "Oh? The last Dragon Isle Token was actually in your hands? Now this is interesting."

The Dragon Isle Tokens had been created by the Dragon Clan, and only ten existed in total. Over countless years, the Dragon Clan had gone to great lengths to recover nine of them. The last remained somewhere in the outside world, and they had never managed to uncover any news of it.

When Zhu Qing last left Dragon Isle, her primary mission had been to find this final Dragon Isle Token. Unfortunately, although she discovered clues that it was in Yang Kai's possession, she failed to retrieve it. Through a succession of unexpected twists, she instead developed an extremely intimate relationship with him.

Now that Yang Kai had produced the Dragon Isle Token for all to see, his purpose could not have been more obvious.

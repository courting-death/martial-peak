## Chapter 2621 — Big but Brainless

"Taiping City..." Yang Kai walked out of the inn, thought for a moment, and suddenly realized, "Isn't this the city where that woman named Xue Man lives?"

Earlier, when those three people blocked the way to buy Ji Yao's multi-deck ship, the old man introduced the man and woman, saying the man was the Young Pavilion Master of the Thunderclap Pavilion, and the woman was the City Lord's daughter of Taiping City.

When entering the city yesterday, Yang Kai had been preoccupied with Ji Yao's condition and hadn't paid attention to the city's name. Only now did he realize this was actually the Taiping City.

That was quite a coincidence.

However, Yang Kai wasn't afraid. Such a small city wasn't enough to make him wary.

With Ji Yao in tow, Yang Kai made his way toward the city gate. In just a short while, they arrived at the entrance.

Many people were queuing to leave the city, and even more martial artists were standing guard, strictly enforcing security. Everyone leaving had to be inspected by those guards.

"Hmm?" Yang Kai frowned as he saw this scene, unsure if this was a regular rule of Taiping City or if something had happened.

"Master, could it be because of those three people from yesterday?" Ji Yao suddenly asked in a low voice. "Are they looking for us?"

"How could it be such a coincidence?" Yang Kai chuckled. "We didn't alarm anyone when we entered yesterday. Maybe something else happened in the city."

Ji Yao nodded lightly.

The two of them didn't try to force their way through the gate with their high cultivation. Since they were on someone else's turf, it was fine to follow their rules. So, they lined up at the back of the crowd, waiting to leave the city.

The guards at the gate were checking very carefully, and there were even hidden Divine Senses probing those preparing to leave.

One by one, martial artists were cleared and allowed to pass, and the line gradually moved forward.

"Patriarch Dong!" Suddenly, a voice came from a building beside the city gate.

"Still haven't found her?" another voice asked—it seemed to be none other than Patriarch Dong himself.

"No sign yet," the first speaker replied. "But please rest assured, Patriarch Dong. The other three city gates have already been closed. If Madam wants to leave, she must use this gate. As soon as she appears, we'll find her."

"Thank you, Commander. Ah, what a misfortune for my family," Patriarch Dong sighed deeply.

Commander Yu suddenly chuckled, "Patriarch Dong, as far as I know, you and your wife have always been loving, always together in Taiping City, making others envious. How did things get so out of hand overnight? Why did Madam run away from home? You even had to ask the City Lord’s mansion for help to find her."

With this question, the room fell silent, as if it was hard to speak about.

Commander Yu added, "If it's inconvenient, just pretend I didn't ask."

"Ah!" Patriarch Dong sighed heavily and said, "As the saying goes, family scandals shouldn't be made public. I wanted to keep things quiet, but that foolish woman forced my hand! It's nothing hard to say—she got greedy for a moment and stole my Dong Family's ancestral treasure."

"Stole it!" Commander Yu was shocked. "That can't be! Madam holds a position second only to you in the Dong Family. Besides, I heard she comes from a great sect. What could the Dong Family have that she'd want to steal?"

"I don't know if she ate too much lard and muddled her mind, but she actually did such a shameful thing," Patriarch Dong said bitterly, then solemnly added, "Commander Yu, for the sake of our past friendship, if you find that foolish woman, quietly capture her. Don't alert anyone else, especially not City Lord Yan!"

Commander Yu replied, "I understand. Please rest assured, Patriarch Dong."

......

The two spoke in low voices inside the room, but Yang Kai heard every word clearly.

He hadn't meant to eavesdrop, but just happened to overhear. Still, now he understood why Taiping City was under such strict guard.

It turned out the matriarch of a family had stolen the clan's ancestral treasure, prompting the family head to pursue her and enlist the City Lord’s mansion's help. This was a real family scandal. The helplessness and pain in Patriarch Dong's words made Yang Kai secretly amused. He was probably at his wits' end, wanting to recover the stolen item but also to keep the scandal quiet.

No one knew exactly what the stolen item was, but for it to tempt the matriarch of a family into such greed, it must have been extraordinary.

The line kept moving forward. Soon, Yang Kai and Ji Yao were almost at the gate.

Just then, a streak of light flew in from outside the city, heading straight for the gate.

"Who goes there? No unruliness at Taiping City's gate!" a guard shouted, flying up to intercept.

The streak of light paused, revealing a man and a woman.

"Are you blind? Do you not know who I am, daring to shout at me!" the man snorted coldly.

The guard took a closer look and immediately trembled, hurriedly saying, "So it's Young Master Bai and Miss Yan."

This man and woman were none other than Bai Lu and Xue Man, whom Yang Kai had met just yesterday. Who knew where they'd come from, but the old man Yan who had been with them was nowhere to be seen.

"Get lost!" Bai Lu, clearly in a foul mood, struck the guard with his palm, sending him flying and spitting blood in midair, utterly miserable.

Under the gaze of many eyes, Bai Lu and Xue Man rushed straight through the city gate, landed, and looked around arrogantly, as if they were the most important people here.

But soon, Bai Lu's eyes twitched, and his body shuddered violently as he stared in horror in a certain direction.

In that direction, Yang Kai was looking at him calmly.

After losing face in front of Yang Kai yesterday and nearly losing his life, how could Bai Lu forget Yang Kai's face? In just one day, he hadn't expected to meet him again at Taiping City's gate.

The scene of Old Yan, an Emperor Realm expert, having his arm shattered by a single blow was still vivid in his mind. Bai Lu was truly afraid of Yang Kai, and couldn't help but shrink back, his aura completely gone.

"What's wrong?" Xue Man, beside him, noticed his odd behavior and asked softly, following his gaze. The next moment, her face turned pale and she looked away.

She had clearly seen Yang Kai as well.

"Let's go!" Bai Lu whispered, withdrawing his gaze and dragging Xue Man away as if fleeing, quickly disappearing into the city. The City Lord’s mansion guards were left bewildered, exchanging glances, not knowing what was wrong with Young Master Bai and Miss Yan today.

At a street corner, Bai Lu and Xue Man peeked out, secretly watching the city gate.

Xue Man trembled, "Why are those two here?"

Bai Lu's face darkened, "How should I know?"

After yesterday's huge loss, the pair was so frightened that seeing Yang Kai was like a mouse seeing a cat—they wanted nothing more than to find a hole to hide in.

Suddenly, Xue Man seemed to remember something, frowning, "Wait, this is Taiping City. Why should I be afraid of them? No, I have to tell my father and have him teach them a lesson."

As she spoke, she was about to go to the City Lord’s mansion to find her father.

Bai Lu grabbed her, face dark, "How can you be so big but so brainless?"

"Who... who's big but brainless!" Xue Man retorted angrily.

Bai Lu said, "Old Yan and your father are about equal in strength, but even Old Yan couldn't withstand a single move from that man. Do you think your father could do anything to him? If your father comes, he might end up dead. Don't come crying to me then."

Hearing this, Xue Man's face turned pale. She thought to herself, that's true—if even Old Yan lost an arm in one move, how could her father be a match? If she really called her father, he'd probably be the one to suffer.

"Then what should we do!" Xue Man panicked. "Why don't you call Uncle Bai? He's at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. He should be able to handle that man."

Bai Lu shook his head, "I don't even know how to explain Old Yan's injury to my father. There aren't many Emperor Realm experts in the Thunderclap Pavilion. With Old Yan so badly hurt, if my father finds out, he'll skin me alive. This is all your fault—why did you insist on buying that multi-deck ship? Now Old Yan's arm is gone because of you."

Blamed like this, Xue Man's eyes immediately reddened. She trembled, "Bai, when you were whispering sweet things to me, you never said this. Now you blame me—do you have any conscience at all!"

Seeing her cry, Bai Lu felt irritated but had to comfort her, "Alright, alright, stop crying. Yesterday was my fault, okay? It's not your fault."

"You're bad, you're bad, you're bad..." Xue Man shouted, pounding him with her fists.

"Stop it!" Bai Lu had no patience for her antics, quickly grabbing her hands. "The important thing is to get rid of those two troublemakers. Looks like they're leaving the city..."

As he spoke, Bai Lu pulled Xue Man back into hiding.

Because he saw Yang Kai turn his head and look at them with a smile, that glance nearly scared him out of his wits.

At the city gate, Yang Kai, waiting in line to leave, turned his head and sneered.

He hadn't expected Young Master Bai and Xue Man to react like that upon seeing him today. But that made things easier—if they were really foolish enough to call for an expert to take revenge, Yang Kai wouldn't mind causing a scene here.

"Cough, cough, cough..." A fit of violent coughing came from ahead in the line. An old woman nearly collapsed backward.

Ji Yao reacted quickly, catching her and asking with concern, "Granny, are you alright?"

## Chapter 2825 — Senior, Don’t Joke

As Che finished speaking, the power of thunder all around transformed into a force of compression, enveloping Yang Kai and directly suppressing him on the spot.

Che raised his head proudly and asked, “Well? Do you want to reconsider?”

Within the Domain of Thunder, he had control over everything. The Shaman Niu before him would never again cause the slightest ripple.

Yang Kai struggled a few times, but his body was firmly locked in place, unable to move at all. He couldn’t help but praise, “Not bad!”

Che’s brows furrowed, revealing a strange expression. The faint praise from Shaman Niu sounded extremely absurd in his ears, as if he was facing not a newly promoted Shaman Master, but a Shaman King or Shaman Saint, standing in a position he could only look up to, critiquing the methods he took pride in.

Why did he have such a strange feeling? The other party was just a Shaman Master.

Yet in the next moment, Yang Kai took a deep breath, his chest swelling, and the Myriad Sword in his hand gently swayed. From the tip of the sword, a wondrous and extreme fluctuation suddenly emerged, stirring up visible, bizarre ripples.

Wherever those ripples passed, the Domain of Thunder loosened abruptly, showing signs of collapse.

Che’s expression changed dramatically, no longer daring to hold anything back. His body trembled as he intensified his control over the Domain of Thunder.

When he first deployed the Domain, he hadn’t used his full strength. After all, the other’s cultivation was a whole realm lower. Who would have thought this Shaman Niu actually had the ability to shake his domain?

Yet it was still futile. The ripples emanating from that sword seemed to possess incredible power. No matter how much he increased his control, he still couldn’t stop the domain from collapsing.

Cold sweat suddenly broke out on Che’s forehead. He looked at Yang Kai as if seeing a ghost, his eyes filled with terror.

The Myriad Sword danced lightly, the power of the Laws of Space swirling around. Yang Kai stepped forward, breaking through the Domain of Thunder, walking step by step toward Che.

In the martial world of later generations, Origin Returning Realm warriors had to master the Shi Field, Void King Realm warriors mastered the Domain Field, and upon reaching Dao Origin Realm, one could communicate with the Laws of heaven and earth. As for the Emperors, they could control the Laws of heaven and earth.

Che’s Domain of Thunder was more advanced than a Domain Field mastered by Void King Realm, as it also communicated a trace of the laws of heaven and earth.

But the power Yang Kai mastered was even more profound.

If Che hadn’t tried to show off by using the Domain of Thunder to bind Yang Kai, but instead fought him head-on, Yang Kai might have had a harder time dealing with him. After all, his current cultivation limited the strength he could unleash. Yet Che, not understanding the situation, insisted on relying on his overwhelming advantage in realm to crush Yang Kai, unwittingly handing him an easy opportunity to break free.

The power of the Laws of Space was already extremely mysterious. Now, as it filled the Domain of Thunder, it instantly made the domain totter on the verge of collapse. Wherever Yang Kai passed, the thunderous light turned into emptiness, all Domain Fields wiped away.

The difference in their realms was clear at a glance.

As the other’s steps fell, Che could even hear the sound of his domain collapsing.

The distance between the two was rapidly closing.

Yang Kai’s lips curled into a mocking smile, while Che’s expression was no longer calm and aloof.

When Yang Kai took the final step, it was as if he stepped not on the void, but on Che’s heart.

The mighty Great Shaman Master Che—one of the Frost Snow Tribe’s chief Commanders—shuddered violently. A mouthful of blood mist burst from his lips as the Domain of Thunder shattered in an instant. Staggering backward, he clutched his chest, the brilliance in his eyes dimming all at once.

With the domain broken, his foundation was damaged, and he was seriously injured. Earlier, when Shaman Niu said he couldn’t be stopped, he thought it was just empty boasting, but now… it turned out to be the truth.

How could there be such a Shaman Master in this world?

Yang Kai felt no joy at breaking free. Instead, he turned his head, glanced around, clicked his tongue, and muttered gloomily, “What a hassle.”

As he spoke, he took out the Green Leaf, communicated with it through his mind, and as a light flashed, his figure disappeared.

Deep within the palace, a terrifying aura awakened like a sleeping dragon. Its emergence seemed to trigger a remarkable chain reaction, and soon, equally powerful auras spread throughout the royal palace.

Those were the Shaman Kings of the Frost Snow Tribe, startled into action.

Within the entire royal palace, all members of the Frost Snow Tribe turned pale, trembling, bowing their heads in fear.

Several powerful Divine Senses swept over. Che, enduring his injuries, held his chest and bent over, saluting the void: “Greetings, honored lords.”

“What happened?” The first aura to appear suddenly spoke, its voice drifting, origin unclear.

Che pondered for a moment, then briefly recounted what had happened, focusing on Yang Kai’s incredible abilities and his skill in concocting medicines.

The Shaman Kings were silent for a while. It was unclear if they communicated among themselves. The one who spoke earlier said, “Since he is useful to the tribe, invite him over again. However, Lord Qing seems to be protecting him, so don’t be too rude.”

“Lord Qing…” Che’s expression changed.

To be addressed as “Lord Qing” by the Shaman Kings, there was only one such existence in the entire Barbarian Tribe—the Divine Evergreen Tree that protected all of Frost Snow City!

He actually received Lord Qing’s protection?

Hesitating, Che suddenly understood. No wonder Shaman Niu had vanished so suddenly—it was Lord Qing’s doing. He had just been wondering what happened.

Now that he thought about it, the other party seemed to have taken out a Green Leaf before disappearing…

The Shaman Kings’ auras came quickly and left just as fast. In the blink of an eye, they receded like a tide, leaving Che frowning, his face troubled.

The Shaman Kings wanted him to invite Shaman Niu again, but Shaman Niu was now under Lord Qing’s protection. If the other party was unwilling, what could he do? Before, he had confidence in suppressing Shaman Niu by force, but after their battle, he realized he had no such ability.

That Shaman Niu couldn’t be treated as an ordinary Shaman Master. His strength was at least at the Great Shaman Master level, perhaps even at its peak.

…

After communicating with the Divine Tree via the Green Leaf and returning to his tree hollow, Yang Kai knocked on the trunk and called out unceremoniously, “Senior Qing!”

A figure flashed in the tree hollow. An elderly man appeared leisurely, smiling as he looked at him.

Yang Kai didn’t bother with pleasantries and said directly, “Thank you, Senior, for looking after me these past days. The green hills remain, the rivers flow on—we’ll meet again someday.”

The Frost Snow Tribe had their eyes on him. Though they didn’t want his life, they wanted him to serve them. But Yang Kai didn’t want to lose his freedom, so it was best to leave first.

With his current strength, Yang Kai had a good chance against the Great Shaman Master Che, but if he had to face the entire Frost Snow Tribe, his odds were slim. He had already alarmed the Shaman Kings—if he didn’t leave now, it might soon be too late.

Besides, he had already achieved his goal in Frost Snow City. Now that he had advanced to the Shaman Master realm, it was best to return to a remote place like Cangnan Village. With the help of the million Inner Cores in his ring, as long as he had time, he could quickly advance. Once he was strong enough, even if the Frost Snow Tribe wanted to keep him, they wouldn’t be able to.

As he spoke, Yang Kai peeked outside the tree hollow. The outside was calm, people coming and going under the tree, peaceful. Clearly, the events in the royal palace hadn’t reached here yet.

It made sense. He had teleported here instantly with the Green Leaf, and no matter how fast the Frost Snow Tribe reacted, there would be some delay.

With this buffer, he had enough time to leave the Frost Snow Tribe’s territory and escape far away. By then, the sky and sea would be vast, and the tribe wouldn’t be able to do anything to him.

“Don’t rush off yet!” Qing raised his hand.

“Is there something else, Senior?” Yang Kai asked curiously.

Qing stroked his beard and smiled, “I noticed the cultivation technique you’ve been using lately is extremely poor. Would you like to change it?”

Yang Kai was stunned, then overjoyed, “Of course!”

He had thought about getting a new cultivation technique from the Frost Snow Tribe, since his current one was taught by the village chief and wasn’t anything special. But most of the tribe’s techniques focused on ice and cold, which might not suit him, and he didn’t want to get too entangled with them. Anyway, his cultivation was already different from others, so a poor technique was fine for now. He could always find a chance to change it later.

Now that Qing offered, it was like someone bringing him a pillow when he was sleepy.

After a pause, Yang Kai asked, “Is there anything you need me to help with, Senior?”

Though they hadn’t interacted much, Yang Kai could tell this elder wasn’t one to give favors for no reason. He was very clear-cut in his actions. If you wanted to live in the tree hollow, you had to water it with Demonic Beast blood. If you scratched his itch, he rewarded you with a Green Leaf. Maybe it was related to his role as the tree of protection.

He protected the entire Frost Snow Tribe. If everyone asked for something, he’d have been exhausted long ago.

So, though he was happy, Yang Kai still asked cautiously.

Qing smiled, “There is indeed a small favor I need from you.”

“Let’s hear it!” Yang Kai replied solemnly.

Qing said, “If I told you my time was almost up, what would you think?”

Yang Kai was startled, thinking the topic had shifted a bit too quickly. He glanced up and down at Qing, twitching his mouth, “Senior, don’t joke!”

Qing laughed heartily, not continuing on that topic, but said, “You’re a very unique person. In all my years, I’ve only seen one like you. So I want to entrust you with something.”

“What is it?”

Qing didn’t say more, but reached out and pointed at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai was startled, but knowing Qing meant no harm, he didn’t defend himself. When the finger touched his forehead, it felt like a mountain crashed into him. He was instantly dizzy, and mysterious words appeared in his mind.

But in his current state, he couldn’t see what those words were. As his vision went black, he fainted to the ground.

Just before losing consciousness, he vaguely heard Qing’s voice by his ear.

## Chapter 2980 — I Raised a Guixu

Zhu Lie said with a look of contempt, "What bullshit Great Demon God? How come this Dragon has never heard of him? Who knows what paltry ability he possesses, yet he actually dared to proclaim himself a god? Truly shameless."

The moment these words left his mouth, it was as though he had stirred up a hornet's nest. The many Demon Kings all glared furiously at Zhu Lie, clearly enraged by his disrespect toward the Great Demon God.

Even the Succubus, who had been speaking with the utmost caution all this time, rarely put on a solemn expression and gritted her teeth. "Sir, please mind your words!"

Zhu Lie leisurely glanced at her and said indifferently, "This Dragon says whatever he wishes. Why should I mind my words?"

That indifferent gaze immediately brought the Succubus back to her senses. Her face abruptly went pale, and she became somewhat flustered, regretting her earlier impulse. Lightly pursing her red lips, she no longer dared to speak carelessly.

Another Demon King stepped forward and said in a deep voice, "Your Excellency's cultivation is profound and your methods formidable. We may indeed be no match for you, but if Your Excellency has deliberately come to humiliate us, we would rather fight you to mutual destruction. Our deaths are not worth regretting, but the name of the Great Demon God will not be defiled!"

As soon as he finished speaking, all the Demon Kings stepped forward in unison, each wearing a cold and stern expression, looking ready to wage a great battle against Zhu Lie at any moment.

The Succubus hesitated for a moment before stepping forward as well, her face growing even paler.

"Oh?" A cold smile curled at the corner of Zhu Lie's mouth. "Trash like you actually dares to contend with this Dragon?"

Threads of murderous intent swirled through the air as the Dragon Essence within Zhu Lie secretly churned. Although killing these Demon Kings might require some effort, he was fully capable of doing so. The fury of an Eighth-Tier Red Dragon was not something just anyone could endure. Even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master would gain no advantage before an enraged Giant Dragon.

The fate of Demon Fury City's First Demon King was the best example.

Zhu Lie did not take these Demon Kings seriously, nor did he need to speak to them as equals. To him, Demon Fury City was merely a slightly larger anthill. If he was in a good mood while passing by, he would step over it; if not, he would kick it aside. Now that these ants had actually taken the initiative to provoke him, only blood and death could quell the anger in his heart.

His murderous intent surged like a tide as the atmosphere grew taut and the situation became ready to erupt.

"The Great Demon God... is very strong," Yang Kai said softly in a low voice.

Zhu Lie turned to look at him, his brow furrowing. He did not know why Yang Kai had suddenly said such a thing. That said, although he found Yang Kai extremely displeasing and believed he had only lost to him because of bloodline suppression, he could not deny that this fellow truly had some ability.

Despite possessing only a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, Yang Kai could display strength comparable to a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master. Even across the entire Star Realm, few possessed such a foundation.

Thus, Zhu Lie placed at least some weight on his words.

The many Demon Kings were also somewhat astonished. Their expressions softened considerably as they looked at Yang Kai, and every one of them felt that this human was more than a hundred times more pleasing to the eye than the red-haired young man.

Yang Kai smiled faintly at Zhu Lie. "The vast world is filled with extraordinary people and gifted experts. Although Divine Spirits can each dominate a region, some existences will always lie beyond your imagination and mine. All we can do is strive to catch up, until one day we overtake them, surpass them, and crush them beneath our feet."

Zhu Lie stared blankly for a moment. "Are you lecturing me?"

Although he inwardly admitted that Yang Kai's words made sense, the pride deep in his bones prevented him from agreeing. In the eyes of every member of the Dragon Clan, the Dragon Clan was the most powerful existence, the leader of all living creatures standing above the clouds, while every other being could only look up to them. Yang Kai speaking to him in such a matter-of-fact tone only made him more displeased.

"Young man, you're rather prideful!" Yang Kai chuckled, then turned away before Zhu Lie could lose his temper and looked at the Succubus. "I called all of you here for just one thing."

"May I ask what Sir wishes to know?" the Succubus asked gently.

Yang Kai had just defended the Great Demon God's reputation, greatly improving her impression of him. She was no longer as afraid of him as before, and most of the other Demon Kings felt the same.

"Tell me about Guixu," Yang Kai said lightly.

"What?"

"Gui... xu!"

"The Domain-Destroying Beast!"

"How do you know the name Guixu?"

The expressions of the Demon Kings changed one after another as they began shouting over each other, as though they had heard something extraordinary. Yang Kai coldly snorted, finally making them shut their mouths, but judging from the glances they exchanged, they still could not conceal the shock in their hearts.

The Succubus swallowed and cautiously asked, "Sir, may I ask why you are inquiring about this?"

"It's nothing much. I'm raising a Guixu, so I want to learn more about it. Isn't Guixu an exotic beast from the Demon Domain? Naturally, I came to ask all of you."

"You're... raising a Guixu?" The Succubus's beautiful face paled in shock. Forcing a smile, she said, "Sir truly knows how to jest. How could an exotic beast like that possibly be raised?"

Some of the Demon Kings also wore strange expressions, looking as though they wanted to laugh but did not dare. None of them believed Yang Kai's words, feeling that this fellow truly knew how to boast. After all, it was simply too unbelievable. How could Guixu, an exotic beast born to destroy all things, be kept at a human's side? If that were true, this man would have been swallowed long ago. How could he still be standing here speaking?

"Do I look like I'm joking?" Yang Kai stared at the Succubus with a solemn and serious expression.

The Succubus put away her smile and slowly shook her head.

"Then don't ask such childish questions."

The Succubus coughed lightly, secretly wondering whether this fellow had mistaken some Yao Beast for Guixu. However, seeing his serious expression, she did not dare say anything more and could only follow his lead. "Then what does Sir wish to ask?"

"Tell me everything you know!" Yang Kai tapped one hand on the stone table while pointing at the many Demon Kings with the other. He said to the Succubus, "Starting with you, each person will tell me one thing about Guixu. Anyone who cannot answer or fails to satisfy me will bear the consequences."

The Succubus jumped in fright. "What consequences?"

Yang Kai said, "I haven't decided yet, but they certainly won't be pleasant." He gave a soft, cold laugh as a perfectly measured trace of murderous intent flashed and vanished, making the expressions of the many Demon Kings turn solemn. They realized this fellow was serious and truly wanted information about Guixu.

None of them could understand why. Although Guixu's name was famous, it had long since gone extinct. Moreover, Guixu was an exotic beast of the Demon Domain, so why would a human inquire about it? No one dared ask, and they all turned their attention to the Succubus.

The Succubus inwardly complained bitterly, but she could only comply with Yang Kai's wishes. After thinking for a moment, she said, "Guixu is known as the Domain-Destroying Beast and is infamous throughout our Demon Domain. It bears this title because it represents destruction. Wherever it goes, all is reduced to shattered nothingness."

After speaking, she cautiously looked at Yang Kai. Yang Kai frowned slightly. Although he was somewhat dissatisfied with such vague information, he did not make things difficult for her and merely waved his hand to let her pass.

The Succubus breathed a sigh of relief and turned to look at the next Demon King.

This Demon King had clearly prepared his answer long ago and immediately said, "Rumor has it that Guixu was born from the primordial chaos. Although it possesses a body of flesh and blood, it has neither root nor origin. No one knows how it came into being. It seems to have existed since the Demon Domain first took shape."

Yang Kai nodded lightly.

The Demon King who had spoken understood and turned to look at the next person.

The third Demon King said, "I only know that there seems to be an incredibly strange space inside Guixu's body, one capable of devouring all things."

"Because Guixu is an exotic beast of the Demon Domain, it is extremely interested in Demonic Qi. It seems able to grow stronger by devouring Demonic Qi."

"Indeed. Rumor has it that Guixu was not powerful when it was first born. Although it displayed strange abilities, our Demon Race did not pay it much attention. It was only after it gradually matured that the experts of the Demon Domain took notice, but by then it was already too late."

"Back then, Guixu ran rampant throughout the Demon Domain, devouring countless members of the Demon Race and destroying innumerable cities and towns. It even swallowed several Demon Saints. To resist it, our Demon Race united as one and paid an extremely grievous price before finally killing it."

"Guixu's vitality is extraordinarily tenacious. Some say it did not actually die but merely feigned death and concealed itself within the primordial chaos, waiting for the day it could rise again."

"..."

One piece of information after another about Guixu came from the mouths of these Demon Kings. Although they had been trapped in the Revolving World for more than a hundred thousand years, certain information about the Demon Domain had been passed down orally from generation to generation. This was especially true of such an iconic beast of destruction as Guixu. Although most of what they said was prefaced with phrases like "it is said" and "rumor has it," it was still extremely valuable information.

Occasionally, a few Demon Kings knew little about Guixu, or someone else had already mentioned the information they possessed. When that happened, other Demon Kings would secretly send them a Divine Sense transmission with different information, allowing them to pass Yang Kai's test all the same.

After two or three rounds, no matter how hard the Demon Kings racked their brains and searched their memories, they could think of nothing more. Yet seeing that Yang Kai still had no intention of stopping, they could not help feeling bitter, wondering whether this fellow was deliberately making things difficult for them. Otherwise, why ask about Guixu of all things?

After another Demon King shared what he knew, the next one wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and thought for a long time with a troubled expression, but still could not come up with anything.

Yang Kai raised his head. "Nothing?"

The Demon King smiled bitterly and shook his head.

The Succubus hurriedly said, "Sir, we have already told you everything we know. We truly aren't concealing anything else."

"Mm, I know." Yang Kai nodded. "I believe everything you said just now was true, but there is one point that greatly interests me."

"May I ask which point Sir is referring to?"

“Guixu can grow stronger by devouring Demonic Qi. Could it grow stronger by devouring you as well?”

“U-Us?” The Succubus swallowed. “It… it should.”

"I don't think it's merely 'should'..." Yang Kai smilingly sized her up. "If Guixu can devour Demon Kings like you, I imagine it would grow very quickly."

"Heh heh, probably." The Succubus forced a laugh. Although she did not believe some Guixu would truly appear and devour them, discussing such a topic still made her hair stand on end, as though a bloody maw might lunge at her at any moment, bite down, and swallow her whole.

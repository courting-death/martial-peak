## Chapter 3086 — Heavenly Way's Rejection

Judging by the look of it, this was Lu Huaishuang's own room, and the finest room on the entire Warship.

Women generally took pains to avoid suspicion. Under ordinary circumstances, no matter how much one respected a guest, one would never offer up one's own boudoir for him to stay in. That would inevitably invite idle speculation and gossip. But Yang Kai's weight had long since surpassed the category of a mere guest. All Lu Huaishuang could think was that only by giving Yang Kai the very best could she avoid slighting him.

Even though he had previously taken liberties with her, Lu Huaishuang did not dare feel the slightest resentment.

Sheng Yao gave Lu Huaishuang a meaningful glance, a smile on his face.

A blush rose over Lu Huaishuang's cheeks. Although she had no other intentions, giving her own boudoir to a man, and one who looked about her own age at that, still left her somewhat embarrassed. She said softly, "Does this place satisfy Sir? If not, we can change to another room."

"You were thoughtful." Yang Kai nodded, showing no particular scruples. To him, it was only a place to rest; whether it was a boudoir or not made no difference.

"As long as Sir is satisfied." For some inexplicable reason, Lu Huaishuang actually breathed a sigh of relief. The faint expectation in her heart had become reality, startling even herself, and she hurriedly said, "Sir, please rest for now. This humble one will go handle the matters outside, then return to speak with Sir."

"Go on." Yang Kai waved his hand.

Only then did Lu Huaishuang withdraw. Before leaving, she glanced at Sheng Yao. She did not like this uninvited fellow very much, but Sheng Yao was acting under Yang Kai's banner, so she could not very well drive him away. She only glared at him as a warning.

The door closed. Yang Kai wandered around the room, casually picking one thing up for a look before setting it down, then picking up another to examine it, as if everything struck him as novel.

Sheng Yao followed behind Yang Kai like a tail.

Yang Kai suddenly turned around.

Sheng Yao could not dodge in time and almost crashed into him. He hurriedly stepped aside, looking over in panic, only to see Yang Kai smiling at him as he said, "Bathing. Coming along?"

Sheng Yao's eye twitched. Lowering his brow in deference, he said, "I would not dare, I would not dare. This lowly one will withdraw for now. If Sir has any instructions, please call at any time. This lowly one will wait outside the door."

As he spoke, he fled the room as if escaping, closing the door behind him and standing outside respectfully.

All at once, a bitter smile appeared on his face.

He believed his disguise was flawless. Even the sworn brothers in Gale who had drunk wine, eaten meat, and played with women alongside him all these years had never seen through his true face, but he knew that this little disguise of his was probably not worth mentioning in Sir Yang's eyes.

The moment that man first saw him, he had already pierced through the disguise; he had simply been too lazy to expose it.

Thinking of what that Sir had done to Lu Huaishuang earlier, then of what he himself sought, a trace of resolve flashed through Sheng Yao's eyes. He nodded to himself, suddenly formed a Spirit Art with his hands, and in the next instant, his figure underwent an earth-shaking change.

In the bath, Yang Kai stripped off his clothes and leaned against the pool wall, both arms spread wide, his expression utterly comfortable. The lean, powerful muscles across his body were extremely well proportioned; if those lovesick maidens and neglected wives saw him, they would likely shriek without end.

Now that he had reached a Lower Star Domain, even if it was the completely unfamiliar Wuji Star Domain, he could still be considered to have barely accomplished his goal.

When he founded Lingxiao Palace, he had once thought that one day he would bring over his friends and family from Dark Star. But for so many years, he had been working hard to raise his cultivation and strength, leaving him neither the chance to return to the Star Domain nor any means to do so.

Now, however, there was an opportunity.

Although he had come to the Wuji Star Domain, that did not necessarily mean there was no chance of returning to the Hengluo Star Domain. He knew that Star Domains differed greatly from one another, and that some were connected. Perhaps, in this Wuji Star Domain, he could find a path and method leading to other Star Domains.

It was decided, then. His primary goal was to find a way back to the Hengluo Star Domain, while his secondary goal was to inquire in passing about Wu Kuang's whereabouts. He had a feeling Wu Kuang might also be in this Wuji Star Domain. If so, he could look for traces of him along the way. Wu Kuang was widely experienced and knowledgeable; he would surely know how to travel back and forth between two Star Domains.

After taking a thoroughly refreshing bath, Yang Kai dressed himself again, combed his hair, summoned the Xuan Realm Pearl, gritted his teeth, and carefully urged his strength before flashing into the Small Xuan Realm.

In the loft area of the Small Xuan Realm, Liu Yan, who had been meditating with her eyes closed, suddenly opened them. In a flicker, she arrived outside the loft. The Incarnation appeared with her, and when they exchanged a glance, each saw the thought in the other's eyes.

Li Jiao and Lu Sanniang, mother and daughter, were enjoying a warm family moment, though no one knew what they were chatting about. Seeing the two appear, Li Jiao could not help asking, "Young Lady Liu Yan, what happened?"

"Master is here." Liu Yan's beautiful eyes shone as she looked off in that direction.

Li Jiao was startled at these words, then suddenly understood and looked over in pleasant surprise.

Over there, Yang Kai's figure flickered in and out of sight, and very soon he arrived before the loft. He grinned. "Everyone is here."

Liu Yan stood there and opened her arms toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai stared. "What are you doing?"

"Hug!"

Yang Kai said, "Are you teasing me? The older you get, the more childish you become." Even as he spoke, he walked up to her and reached out to lift her into his arms, as if holding an infant.

Liu Yan sat on his arm, her soft black hair hanging down, her face filled with satisfaction. She had cultivated on Spirit Beast Island with the Fire Phoenix for a long time, and although she had finally managed to see Yang Kai on Dragon Isle, there had been no time to talk or be affectionate. Now she had at last gotten her wish. With both hands, she tugged at Yang Kai's ears and played with them as if she had obtained some fascinating new toy, acting utterly unrestrained.

Yang Kai shook his head, but she refused to relent, so he could only leave her be.

“Brother Yang.” Li Jiao bowed deeply. “I will never forget this great kindness. If you ever have need of me in the future, Brother Yang, just give the word. I, Li, will never refuse.”

Lu Sanniang also stepped forward, gave a graceful bow, and said, "Thank you, Young Brother Yang, for avenging Sanniang's hatred." As she spoke, her eyes reddened.

Lu Yuqin followed, saying, "Thank you, Uncle Yang."

She had not the slightest good impression of her Dragon father, and these days she had also heard many things about the past from her mother. In her eyes, Li Jiao was her father, while that fellow called Fu Chi was the greatest villain under the Heavens.

Yang Kai raised his hand and lifted them all up, then said, "The one who took revenge was not me, but Brother Li himself. There is no need to thank me."

Li Jiao said in shame, "This Li's skills were lacking. If not for Brother Yang's great assistance, how could I ever have had the chance to take revenge?"

Although Fu Chi had not died by his hand, Fu Chi's death was closely tied to him, and if Yang Kai had not seized a portion of the Dragon Temple's blessing for him, he would never have had the chance to battle Fu Chi at all.

For this alone, he was endlessly grateful to Yang Kai. The resentment he had accumulated on Dragon Isle was swept clean away, and he now looked forward to the future. Secretly making up his mind, he decided that after returning to Li Dragon Palace, he would treat Lu Sanniang and her daughter well, as well as those women of his.

After chatting idly for a while, the Incarnation suddenly cut in, "Did you run into some trouble?"

It had been formed from one of Yang Kai's Divine Souls inhabiting a Stone Puppet body, so in certain respects, its mind was connected to Yang Kai's. Naturally, it had noticed a few clues.

"Mm, there is some trouble." Yang Kai nodded and said, "Let's sit down and talk."

With a beckoning motion of his hand, several chairs and a wooden table immediately flew out from the loft. These had all been made by people from Tongxuan Continent in earlier years and left here.

Everyone took their seats. Yang Kai held Liu Yan by the arms and tried to place her on a chair beside him, but Liu Yan refused. In the end, he could only set her on his own lap and wrap one arm around her.

In a few words, Yang Kai recounted what had happened afterward in the Dragon Temple. When they heard that Wu Kuang had escaped, shattered the Void, and slipped into a Lower Star Domain, everyone was startled.

When they further heard that Yang Kai had also chased him to an unfamiliar Wuji Star Domain, their astonishment only grew.

"The current situation is this." Yang Kai's face turned solemn. "A Lower Star Domain has its own World Laws, and they reject the existence of cultivators above the Void King Realm. So among you, aside from Yuqin, none of you can move around safely outside. Once you go out, you will definitely suffer the rejection of the Heavenly Way."

Liu Yan asked, "Then what about you, Master?"

Yang Kai was in the Emperor Realm. This Lower Star Domain rejected even Dao Origin Realm cultivators, let alone an even more powerful existence.

"I am rejected all the same." Yang Kai rubbed his nose. "Although before entering this Star Domain, I did everything I could to suppress and seal my cultivation, after arriving here, I was still struck by lightning."

That lightning punishment was the most obvious sign that the World Laws were rejecting Yang Kai.

Fortunately, he had long since sealed away most of his cultivation. Otherwise, the lightning punishment would probably have been even worse. Although this was only a Lower Star Domain, how mighty were the World Laws? If he truly provoked the Heavens and Earth into targeting him, even Yang Kai would be unable to survive safely in this Star Domain.

“And then… I tripped and fell.”

"Pff..." Liu Yan burst out laughing. It was hard to imagine what it looked like for a First-Stage Emperor Realm Master to take a fall, and the others could not help smiling either.

Yang Kai reached out and knocked her on the head. Liu Yan immediately covered her head with both hands and stuck out her lips in a pout.

"So Brother Li and Sanniang, you can only stay here for the time being, and there is no way for you to return to the Star Realm right now. But don't worry. Once I finish handling my own affairs, I will find the way back."

Li Jiao nodded. "That is nothing. Since Brother Yang could come here, you can certainly leave. But do you need our help? Although with Brother Yang's cultivation, it should be impossible for you to have any opponents in this kind of Lower Star Domain, even a hero needs three helpers. We can still lend some strength, more or less."

Yang Kai smiled. "If Brother Li wants to help, then first seal your own cultivation."

"Good." Li Jiao nodded.

"I want to help too," Liu Yan said.

"Granted."

Liu Yan smiled sweetly.

He could not shake the feeling that after this reunion, her personality had changed a great deal.

...

"Where is Lu An?" In another side room, after Lu Huaishuang finished handling some miscellaneous matters, she asked Lu Bing.

Hearing the coldness in her words, Lu Bing's heart clenched, and he asked doubtfully, "What did he do?"

Lu Huaishuang said, "He was the one who sold out our location and drew the people from Gale and the He Family here."

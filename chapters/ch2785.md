## Chapter 2785 — Are You Master Hou?

"Well?" Yang Kai smiled as he looked at the woman.

"Aiya..." The woman scratched her head, glanced aside, and chuckled dryly, "This warship... is indeed... not bad, hahaha..."

Nanmen Dajun smiled at the side and said, "Little Hou, do you still remember what you said earlier?"

The woman glared at him fiercely, then put on a tough act and looked at Yang Kai, saying, "Alright, although I have a few minor flaws in my character..."

"Minor flaws?" Nanmen Dajun's forehead twitched.

"But I also have strengths. My greatest strength is that I always honor my bets!" The woman looked at Yang Kai with a carefree air and said, "Since that's the case, when are we getting married?"

"There's no need for marriage." Yang Kai raised his hand to refuse. "Master Hou is a renowned artificer of this generation. I mainly came to invite you to Lingxiao Palace to take up the position of Chief Artificer. If I offended you earlier, I hope Master Hou won't take it to heart."

"Hehehehe..." The woman covered her mouth and laughed, her body shaking with mirth. "Junior Brother, you really know how to talk." Her expression suddenly turned serious as she said, "Let me make this clear: it's you who doesn't need me to fulfill the agreement, not me reneging on it. My reputation must not be tarnished."

"Dajun can testify." Yang Kai smiled slightly and added, "Provided that Master Hou is willing to return to Lingxiao Palace with me."

Upon hearing this, the woman's expression became solemn. She glanced at Nanmen Dajun, pondered for a moment, and said, "If not for Dajun, no matter how many benefits your Lingxiao Palace offered, I wouldn't consider it. But since Dajun has already joined... I'll go join the fun too."

"I guarantee Master Hou won't be disappointed!" Yang Kai nodded with a smile.

"Wait!" The woman raised a hand. "Before that, I have one condition."

Yang Kai frowned and nodded, "Go ahead."

The woman turned around, quickly moved behind a rock, lifted her skirt to her waist, and placed one foot on the stone, exposing half her leg without a care. At some point, a dice cup appeared in her hand. She shook it dazzlingly, and a rattling sound came from inside. After a moment, she slammed it down on the rock and shouted, "Let's gamble! The stake is this warship!"

"That's your condition?" Yang Kai asked in surprise.

"That's right!" The woman nodded.

Yang Kai was speechless. He'd heard from Nanmen Dajun that this Master Hou was addicted to gambling, but hadn't had the chance to witness it until now.

The Origin-Grade warship refined by Yang Yan must be extremely attractive to her. That's why she wanted Yang Kai to use it as a stake.

"Alright!" Yang Kai naturally had no problem with it. It was just a gamble. "If I win, what then? What's your stake?"

"I've got something to wager myself!" the woman snorted, then called out in a low voice, "So, will you bet big or small?"

Yang Kai glanced at Nanmen Dajun, who said, "Palace Master, bet as you like. Don't worry about her cheating—she's extremely serious about gambling and would never play tricks."

"Then big." Yang Kai said casually. Winning or losing didn't matter. At worst, he'd lose an Origin-Grade warship.

Nanmen Dajun silently took out a Spatial Ring and tossed it onto the rock. "I'll choose big too."

The woman sneered at Nanmen Dajun, "Careful you don't lose everything."

"Relax, I could win against you with my eyes closed."

"Come on, place your bets, no more changes! Open!"

......

A moment later, the woman grabbed her right wrist with her left hand and shook it hard, her expression as if she’d just lost her dearest kin. With a look of utter despair, she wailed, “I should just cut off my hand! How could it be big?”

Nanmen Dajun calmly retrieved the Spatial Ring and said, "Counting it up, Little Hou, you owe me fifty million Source Crystals."

"Put it on the tab!" The woman glared at him, then looked at Yang Kai and sighed, "Since I lost, I'll give you something."

As she spoke, she reached into her clothes, pulled out an item, and tossed it to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai took it without thinking, but when he looked down and saw what it was, he nearly had a nosebleed on the spot.

The woman chuckled, "My personal undergarment. Still warm..."

Yang Kai's face darkened, "What do I want this for!"

The woman said meaningfully, "Who knows..."

Yang Kai sighed, just about to return the delicate undergarment, when he suddenly frowned, shook it open, and examined it carefully in his palm.

The woman had intended to make fun of Yang Kai, but seeing his reaction, her heart skipped a beat. She quietly sent a voice transmission to Nanmen Dajun, "Dajun, could our Palace Master be a virgin? Will he eat me up if I tease him like this?"

"You?" Nanmen Dajun sneered.

"What's wrong with me?" The woman was immediately displeased.

Meanwhile, Yang Kai noticed something, looked up at the woman in surprise, and asked, "You're Master Hou?"

The woman blinked and said, "Yes, my surname is Hou, given name Yu!"

"So you really are Master Hou!" Yang Kai laughed, pointing at the monkey-head emblem on the undergarment.

Only then did Hou Yu understand, and she smiled, "So that's what you meant. That's right, I'm the elusive artificer—Master Hou!"

"It's really you!" Yang Kai's eyes widened, but then he nodded, "I should have guessed."

Previously, on Nanmen Dajun's flying boat, Yang Kai had seen a monkey-head sculpture and had a vague sense of familiarity, but was interrupted before he could think further.

Now, seeing the monkey-head emblem on the undergarment, Yang Kai immediately understood.

A few days ago, when he took Qin Yu to an auction in Fenglin City, he bought her a Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin, spending quite a few Upper-Level Source Crystals. That hairpin also had a monkey-head emblem.

According to Qin Yu, Master Hou was an extremely mysterious artificer—no one even knew if they were male or female. But every Secret Treasure crafted by Master Hou was a masterpiece and fetched a high price.

The name Master Hou was renowned even in the Southern Territory. But no one expected that Master Hou was actually a woman.

And a bizarre woman at that—fond of drinking, addicted to gambling, and always losing!

What a pleasant surprise. Hou Yu's skill in artifact crafting was already outstanding, and with the reputation of Master Hou, Lingxiao Palace had truly struck gold this time.

Now, Lingxiao Palace had gathered an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, an Imperial-Grade Formation Master, and now an Imperial-Grade Artificer. They even dabbled in the Dao of Puppetry, and with the three great Yao Kings overseeing the sect, plus cross-domain Space Arrays connecting every corner, it was clear that nothing could stand in the way of the sect’s rise.

Yang Kai was in high spirits and waved his hand, "This Origin-Grade warship is yours. To me, it's just a keepsake."

"Really?" Hou Yu was overjoyed, beaming as she gave Yang Kai a graceful salute, "Then I thank the Palace Master for the reward."

Yang Kai found himself a bit unaccustomed to her sudden change in demeanor.

In the blink of an eye, Hou Yu reverted to her usual self, darted over to the Origin-Grade warship, and hugged it as if it were a priceless treasure.

Yang Kai removed his Soul Brand, and Hou Yu quickly refined it and put it away with great care.

"Palace Master, if there's a chance in the future, I hope you can introduce me to the person who crafted this warship." Hou Yu returned, her face solemn.

"There will be a chance." Yang Kai nodded slightly. "Do you have anything else to pack? If not, let's go."

Hou Yu chuckled, "I own nothing."

Years of gambling had nearly cost her everything. Despite being an Imperial-Grade Artificer, she was actually penniless.

"Then let's go." Yang Kai said, "You two, please don't resist."

As he spoke, Yang Kai summoned the Laws of Space and Emperor Yuan, enveloping the three of them. With a single thought, they vanished from the cave in an instant.

In a flash, the three of them reappeared above the island.

Nanmen Dajun was prepared, so he felt nothing unusual, but Hou Yu was shocked, exclaiming, "Teleportation?"

In an instant, they had gone from the cave to here, bypassing countless barriers and formations. Hou Yu hadn't sensed Yang Kai using any Secret Treasure, so there was only one explanation.

"Palace Master, you are proficient in the Laws of Space?"

"Our Palace Master is incredibly capable. You'll be amazed in the future." Nanmen Dajun grinned.

Hou Yu's heart trembled, secretly glad she hadn't clashed with Yang Kai. With his strength and mastery of the Laws of Space, how terrifying would such an opponent be, even if they were both at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm?

"Not good." Hou Yu's face suddenly turned pale as she urged Yang Kai, "Hurry, or it'll be too late."

Yang Kai naturally knew what she was worried about and said, "One day, my Lingxiao Palace will be famous throughout the world. As the Chief Artificer, you won't be able to keep your identity hidden. Rather than have people come to collect debts and ruin our reputation, it's better to pay them off now."

"Palace Master, you..." Hou Yu looked moved.

But Yang Kai's next words shattered her feelings completely.

"All today's repayments will be deducted from your future monthly salary."

“Wretched woman, you finally show yourself! Today, even if you sprout wings, you won’t escape!” A furious shout echoed across the island as a streak of light shot toward them from the distance. At the same time, a warning howl rang out, reverberating in all directions.

Moments later, a figure appeared from the light—it was the middle-aged man Yang Kai and Nanmen Dajun had encountered two days ago when they first arrived on the island. His aura was that of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, vast and unstoppable. His Divine Sense locked onto Hou Yu, making it clear he wouldn't let her escape.

As he approached, he reached out to grab Hou Yu.

The terrifying pressure descended like a mountain. Hou Yu's face turned pale, and even Nanmen Dajun staggered.

Both of them were only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Facing this furious Second Stage Emperor, it was hard to withstand his might—even breathing became difficult.

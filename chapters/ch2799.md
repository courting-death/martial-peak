## Chapter 2799 — Ah Niu

For ordinary people, seeking out secret realms was as difficult as ascending to the heavens. Many cultivators would go their entire lives without ever getting the chance to enter a secret realm to temper themselves or search for treasures. Yet here in the Star God Palace, there were countless secret realms waiting to be explored.

Did a Great Emperor Sect truly possess such terrifying foundations?

Lan Xun smiled faintly and said, "You remember the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, Senior Brother Yang, don't you?"

Yang Kai chuckled, "I'm not that old yet."

It had only been a few days since Lan Xun took away the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, so how could Yang Kai have forgotten? It was precisely because Lan Xun had taken the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome that Yang Kai had the opportunity to train for a month inside the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda. This had happened not long ago, so the memory was still fresh in his mind.

However, for her to bring up the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome at this moment, it was clearly not just a casual remark, but rather...

Yang Kai's expression shifted as he asked, "Could it be that the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome is related to the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda?"

Lan Xun nodded, "That's right. That Golden Armored Heavenly Tome was brought out from a secret realm on the Fifth Layer of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda by Senior Uncle Tan... Tan Junhao. Not only is it a powerful Imperial Treasure, but it is also the key to opening that secret realm!"

Yang Kai was startled, "So, that secret realm was originally inside the Fifth Layer of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, but because Tan Junhao took out the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, the disciples of Star God Palace no longer need to pass through the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda to enter and temper themselves in that secret realm?"

"Exactly!" Lan Xun nodded. "That secret realm originally existed within the Fifth Layer of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda. Normally, to enter it, one would have to pass through the Fourth Layer and also be lucky enough to find it. But ever since Tan Junhao brought out the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, the disciples have been able to enter and exit at will."

"Tan Junhao's luck is truly something!" Yang Kai clicked his tongue in amazement.

Lan Xun smiled gently and said, “According to my father’s speculation, there should be many secret realms within the Fifth Layer just like that one, each with its own key. As long as you can find the key, you can open the entrance to that secret realm at any time, allowing disciples to enter and temper themselves whenever they wish.”

Yang Kai's spirits lifted and he exclaimed in shock, "There’s actually such a thing!"

Xiao Chen's expression suddenly changed. He quietly whispered to Lan Xun, "Your Highness, you’ve said too much."

This matter was a secret of Star God Palace. Although it wasn’t an absolute secret, it wasn’t something that could be casually revealed to outsiders. However, Lan Xun’s status was different, so no one could really blame her for saying it.

Lan Xun shook her head, "It’s fine. In any case, those keys can't be found easily. My Palace has been searching for so many years, but after all this time, we’ve only found a key to one secret realm."

Yang Kai pondered for a moment before cupping his fists, "Thank you for the guidance."

Lan Xun pursed her lips and smiled, "We’ve talked so much, it’s delayed us quite a bit. Senior Brother Yang, let’s head into the Fifth Layer first.”

Yang Kai nodded, "Very well, Junior Sister Lan, after you."

Lan Xun’s expression became solemn as she cautioned, "There’s one thing Senior Brother Yang should know. In those many secret realms within the Fifth Layer, anything can happen. You must hold fast to your heart and not be swayed by external things!"

"I’ll remember." Yang Kai nodded.

As they spoke, the three of them had already reached the end of the long corridor. Looking ahead, there was a swirling nebula at the corridor’s end, mysterious and unfathomable. A strange aura emanated from within, making one feel extremely uncomfortable.

"Senior Brother Yang, I’ll go first. Take care, let’s meet again outside." After saying this to Yang Kai, Lan Xun leapt into the nebula and disappeared.

Xiao Chen sighed where he stood, looking somewhat depressed. He had finally managed to find Lan Xun on the Fourth Layer during this training, and had hoped to accompany her until the end. Who would have thought Lan Xun insisted on coming to the Fifth Layer, so now they had to part ways again.

Xiao Chen cast a faint glance at Yang Kai, but had no intention of speaking further. With a light leap, he too disappeared into the swirling nebula.

Soon, only Yang Kai was left standing there.

Time was short, so Yang Kai didn’t hesitate. Since Lan Xun and Xiao Chen had both gone in, he naturally had to follow.

The moment he stepped into the nebula, Yang Kai immediately activated the Laws of Space, wanting to see if he could, like on the Fourth Layer, remain in a buffer zone.

If that were the case, he would have a lot of room to maneuver and could choose a secret realm suitable for his own training.

But this time, he was disappointed. Even after activating the Laws of Space, there was no reaction at all. When his vision cleared, a frantic shout rang in his ears: "Ah Niu, wake up, wake up! The beast tide is coming, hurry and wake up! What are you spacing out for?"

As the voice sounded, it seemed that a large hand grabbed his shoulder and shook him vigorously.

Yang Kai was startled, completely at a loss as to what was happening. His expression darkened as he reached out to grab the other person’s hand.

Unexpectedly, the other party reacted with incredible speed. As soon as Yang Kai moved, his wrist was seized, and an unimaginable, wild strength surged from the other’s hand. All of Yang Kai’s brute force was instantly suppressed within his body, leaving him unable to move at all.

Yang Kai’s expression changed again. Shocked by the other’s strength, he immediately tried to circulate his Emperor Yuan, and then...

Nothing happened at all!

His body was completely empty—there wasn’t a trace of Emperor Yuan left!

In an instant, Yang Kai’s face turned pale with shock, and his Divine Soul nearly left his body.

Before entering the secret realm of the Fifth Layer, Yang Kai had been quite confident. After all, his current strength was extraordinary. He had killed more than one Third-Stage Emperor with his own hands. Even Lan Xun, Xiao Chen, and Lei Ting dared to enter the Fifth Layer’s secret realm, so he had no reason to fear.

He thought that even if he encountered danger, his life wouldn’t be at risk. With the Laws of Space, if he couldn’t win, he could at least escape, right?

But this sudden change left him completely at a loss.

How could his Emperor Yuan be gone? Without Emperor Yuan, was he still in the Emperor Realm? He wasn’t even a beginner cultivator anymore.

What on earth was going on?

In his shock, Lan Xun’s earlier words echoed in his ears: "In those many secret realms within the Fifth Layer, anything can happen. Senior Brother, you must hold fast to your heart and not be swayed by external things!"

Yang Kai’s mind steadied, and his expression instantly calmed down.

Don’t panic. Figure out what’s going on first. Maybe this secret realm has a law that suppresses one’s strength, which is why he couldn’t sense any Emperor Yuan.

And although the one grabbing his wrist was incredibly strong, there was no hostility or malice, so he probably wasn’t a threat.

Looking up, he saw a pair of bloodshot, crimson eyes close at hand, veins bulging. As his vision widened, a face covered in a thick beard came into view.

This was a young man, about the same age as himself, but with extremely rough features and a burly build that didn’t seem human. Moreover, he was somewhat different from the people Yang Kai usually saw, as if he had a different bloodline. His clothing was extremely simple, just animal skins wrapped around his body to cover the vital parts, with the rest exposed. His muscles were like iron, bulging high, exuding a sense of explosive power at a glance.

Glancing down at himself, Yang Kai saw his own slender arms and legs—compared to this burly youth, the difference was like heaven and earth!

To be fair, Yang Kai’s build wasn’t exactly small, but he was still considered sturdy. It was just that the other was simply too robust.

But... why was there a living person here?

Was this a dream, or an illusion of the secret realm?

It couldn’t be a dream, but if it was an illusion, it was far too realistic. Yang Kai couldn’t sense a single trace of falsehood from this bearded young man, as if he were a living, breathing person!

The environment here was also very simple. It seemed to be just a shabby wooden hut, with almost no furnishings inside. There was a simple bench made from a tree root, a table cobbled together from a few planks, some pots and pans nearby, a few pieces of unknown beast meat hanging on the wall, blackened by smoke, a basket with some wild vegetables, and a stone bowl with some leftover food...

What kind of place was this?

As Yang Kai’s thoughts raced, the bearded young man shouted at him, "Are you stupid? I told you the beast tide is coming, did you hear me?"

His voice was full of urgency, deafening to the ears.

Yang Kai nodded blankly, "I heard you!"

"If you heard, then hurry up and help! The village is short on people, the defenses are about to be breached." As he spoke, the bearded young man dragged Yang Kai out, dashing forward with incredible speed.

Yang Kai took the opportunity to ask, "May I ask..."

The bearded man abruptly halted, turning to look at Yang Kai. Those crimson, beast-like eyes made Yang Kai’s heart skip a beat. After a long moment, the young man sighed and said, “Did you get confused again? It’s me, Ah Hu!”

Yang Kai’s mouth twitched as he asked, “You’re saying my name is Ah Niu?”

Ah Niu... damn it, I’m actually called Ah Niu? What kind of ridiculous secret realm is this? And the other guy is called Ah Hu—these names are really simple.

"No time to explain, hurry up and help. Once the beast tide is over, I’ll talk to you!" Ah Hu hurriedly dragged Yang Kai along as they ran.

Not far from the wooden hut, shouts and cries rang out, and the roars of beasts echoed through the sky. Even the earth seemed to tremble and howl.

Yang Kai looked around and was immediately shocked.

The men running about all had burly builds like Ah Hu, their bodies like iron towers, each one looking immensely strong. Even the women were broad-shouldered and thick-waisted, unbelievably robust.

A sturdy woman was carrying a massive stone two zhang in diameter, running as if it weighed nothing. Yang Kai couldn’t sense any fluctuation of power from her, meaning she was lifting it with pure physical strength alone.

There were also some clearly underage children, looking to be in their teens, but they too had picked up weapons. Their still-immature faces were filled with a spirit of unity and a readiness to die.

Yang Kai’s eyes nearly popped out of his head.

Everyone was in a hurry, converging in one direction. Each person was wrapped in a few pieces of animal skin; there wasn’t a single simple garment to be seen here.

Looking down, Yang Kai found that he was dressed the same! His exposed skin was as white as snow, forming a stark contrast with Ah Hu’s dark complexion.

Well, whatever, that was just a minor detail...

Yang Kai comforted himself.

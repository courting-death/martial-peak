## Chapter 3097 — Good Man, Spare My Life

From beginning to end, Yang Kai had shown not the slightest trace of cultivation. All he seemed to rely on was the strength of his fleshly body, so even He Yunxiang had taken him for a body tempering cultivator.

Yet at this very moment, the aura of Saint Yuan was actually spreading out from within Yang Kai.

No, it was not Saint Yuan. That aura was far purer and far deeper than Saint Yuan.

What kind of power was this?

His aura climbed step by step, crossing one barrier after another in succession, and soon reached the First Stage of the Void King Realm before stabilizing.

Yang Kai shook his head helplessly. This was about the limit. In this damned place, the most power he could use was at the Void King Realm. Any more, and he would very likely draw the rejection of the World Laws.

Yan Luo's expression turned more solemn than ever. He had thought Yang Kai was nothing more than a musclebound brute, hardly worth worrying about, but he had not expected him to have been hiding his strength all along. Powerful body tempering, together with cultivation at the First Stage of the Void King Realm... this was somewhat troublesome.

Very quickly, however, the worry in his eyes vanished. It was only the First Stage of the Void King Realm, after all, hardly some overwhelming power. As the Star Lord of Taiyi Star, could he possibly be afraid of him?

"Yan Family Head, are you ready?" Yang Kai raised his head and looked at Yan Luo.

As those pitch-black eyes looked over, Yan Luo's heart lurched, and an unimaginable sense of crisis enveloped him. His vision suddenly blurred, and another figure appeared before him. Who else could it be but that young man?

Hiss. Yan Luo sucked in a cold breath, not even noticing how the other party had rushed right before his eyes. By instinct, he urged the power of the Stellar Origin and vanished from where he stood in an instant.

In the next moment, he appeared thirty zhang away. Before he could even catch his breath, that young man followed like a shadow and appeared before him once more. The two were separated by no more than one chi, and that smiling expression made Yan Luo's hair stand on end.

Shua shua shua.

Yan Luo kept shifting from place to place, cold sweat gradually seeping from his forehead until his clothes were soaked through.

Relying on the various advantages of being a Star Lord, he could change position at will, yet that young man clung to him like a maggot on bone. No matter what he did, he could not shake him off. Often, Yan Luo had only just steadied himself when the young man had already followed close behind, always no more than one chi away.

His greatest reliance became useless in an instant.

Down below, the many Yan Family experts watching were already dumbstruck. The two figures in the sky changed positions so quickly that they could hardly follow them. In the short span of a dozen or so breaths, the pair flashed and shifted several dozen times, yet they always moved in the same rhythm.

"Laws of Space!" Yan Luo cried out in alarm.

As a Star Lord, he could sense changes in the power of heaven and earth more keenly than others. A very clear fluctuation of Space Force spread around the surface of that young man's body. This was clearly because he had grasped the Laws of Space.

Unlike Yan Luo, who relied on his status as Star Lord, Yang Kai's ability to teleport depended entirely on his own skill.

This time, Yan Luo was truly panicking. He had thought he stood in an invincible position, but who would have thought the other party still had such a way to counter him?

A fist rapidly enlarged before his eyes. Yan Luo wanted to dodge, only to discover that the surrounding space had turned viscous. The fist pressed toward him like a great mountain, blotting out the light before his eyes. No matter how he tried to evade, there was no escaping it, and his chest immediately felt unbearably stifled.

With a boom, blood splashed. Yan Luo fell like a meteor, smashing into the ruins of the Yan Family's great hall and sending up a cloud of dust.

Golden stars swam wildly before his eyes, and it felt as if the bridge of his nose had collapsed. When he opened his eyes, the shadow of a tall figure loomed over him.

"Spare my life!" Yan Luo shouted, his voice wretched, all trace of his previous ease gone. The person before him truly had the power and ability to kill him, and from the look of things, he had not yet used his full strength. Yan Luo could hardly imagine what it would look like if this man unleashed everything he had.

Where had this person popped out from? Was he sick in the head?

With strength this great, would it not have been over if you had shown it earlier? Why go to such great lengths? Today, he had left the Yan Family covered in disgrace. Not only had Yan Luo, the Family Head, been beaten until he had no strength to fight back, even an Elder at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm had died an unnatural death.

It was not only Yan Luo who thought this. The other Yan Family experts all had the same thought, especially Yan An, who felt as though he had swallowed bitter gall. Yan Qing had died miserably at his hands, and he had pinned all his hopes on the Family Head avenging him and washing away this hatred. Who could have imagined that the Family Head, who could be called invincible, would beg for mercy before even the time it took to drink a cup of tea had passed?

Was the Family Head too cowardly? No. A wise man knew when to submit to circumstances!

In any case, the Yan Family had completely lost face today. Fortunately, there were no outsiders here, so word would not spread. Otherwise, it would absolutely be a tremendous blow to the Yan Family's reputation and prestige.

"I think we can have a proper talk now." Yang Kai looked down at Yan Luo.

"Whatever questions Sir has, this Yan will certainly tell all he knows and hold nothing back," Yan Luo said, forcing out a faint smile.

Higher up the mountain, inside another great hall, excellent fragrant tea was served. He Yunxiang stood obediently behind Yang Kai, like a maidservant.

Yan Luo had changed his clothes and tidied himself up. He strode out from the rear hall and bowed. "Greetings, Sir." After speaking, he gave Yan An a meaningful look.

Yan An accompanied him with a smile, holding a box in both hands as he respectfully offered it over. "Sir, this is your Spiritual Pill." The box opened, and the Origin Condensing Pill lay quietly inside.

He Yunxiang reached out and accepted it, then sneered. "Did I not hear Second Elder say it had already been lost?"

A trace of discomfort flashed across Yan An's face, and he explained, "That was pure nonsense. Yan Qing acted on his own initiative and had not the slightest relation to my Yan Family. Sir, please forgive us."

"Second Elder also said this Spiritual Pill was fake."

"Impossible. This Spiritual Pill is translucent, full and rounded, with a pleasant pill fragrance. How could it possibly be fake? Although we do not recognize what kind of Spiritual Pill it is, we do know this pill is certainly out of the ordinary. I am afraid Yan Qing saw profit and grew greedy, fabricating nonsense because he wanted to pocket it."

He Yunxiang said solemnly, "For such a black sheep, it is fortunate Elder Yan An took action to clean house. Otherwise, he would certainly have brought calamity to the Yan Family one day."

Yan An kept nodding. "Exactly, exactly. It was also thanks to Sir's assistance; otherwise, how could this Yan have had such ability? The Family Head and I were also deceived by that scoundrel, causing many misunderstandings earlier. Sir must not take it to heart."

He Yunxiang smiled. "Sir will not care about these things."

"Many thanks, Sir."

Yan Luo said with a pained expression, "Today's matter truly came from this Yan's failure to judge people clearly. If Sir has any punishment, this Yan is willing to accept it."

He Yunxiang glanced at Yang Kai and, seeing he gave no indication, pursed her lips into a smile. "The chief culprit has already been executed, so this matter is over. Yan Family Head and Great Elder are both people who understand the larger picture and were merely deceived by someone else. How could you be blamed?"

Hearing this, Yan Luo and Yan An finally relaxed a little.

He Yunxiang's expression turned serious. "Sir has come from thousands upon thousands of li away this time for only one matter."

Yan Luo said solemnly, "Sir, rest assured. The matter of going to the Ancestral Domain will be handled by my Yan Family. When the time comes, this Yan will personally lead Sir there."

He Yunxiang was speechless. Before absolute power, something as difficult as ascending to the heavens had actually become this easy? She had not even said it yet, and Yan Luo had agreed at once. Moreover, the Yan Family Head would personally lead the way. Looking across the entire Star Domain, who could receive such courtesy?

Yan Luo rubbed his hands and said cautiously, "However, this Yan has an unreasonable request. I ask Sir to graciously allow it."

He Yunxiang's pretty face sank. "Are you negotiating conditions with Sir?"

Yan Luo shivered and said in panic, "I would not dare, I would not dare, absolutely not. It is definitely not negotiating conditions, merely a small request."

"If Sir does not agree, then is your Yan Family not prepared to tell Sir the method for going to the Ancestral Domain?"

"That is absolutely impossible. Whether Sir agrees or not, this Yan will fulfill the agreement."

He Yunxiang glanced at Yang Kai, and only then said, "What request? Speak and let us hear it."

Yan Luo gave a laugh. "To Sir, this matter is no more than lifting a hand. It is like this: this Yan also wishes to go to the Ancestral Domain, so I would like to ask Sir to take me along."

"You also want to go to the Ancestral Domain?" He Yunxiang asked curiously. "If you want to go, then just go. It is not as if you do not know the method."

Yan Luo's expression turned solemn. "Sir does not know this. My Yan Family does indeed know the method to go to the Ancestral Domain, but the road is extremely dangerous. Even for someone like this Yan, at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, the slightest carelessness would end with my body smashed to pieces. If not for this, this Yan would have gone to the Ancestral Domain long ago. Why would I need to wait until today? Sir's strength reaches the heavens, so the few thorns along the way certainly will not be worth your notice. This Yan merely wants to hitch a ride."

"The road into the Ancestral Domain is very dangerous?" This was also the first time He Yunxiang had heard of such a thing, so she could not help feeling somewhat doubtful.

"It depends on luck," Yan Luo said solemnly.

Luck was something that could not be clearly explained. With good luck, the road would be smooth all the way. With bad luck, every step would be dangerous and obstructed. Sometimes, luck was also an important measure of a cultivator's strength. Do not lament that the Heavenly Way is unfair, because this is simply how the Heavenly Way is.

He Yunxiang could not decide this matter, so naturally she could only ask Yang Kai's opinion.

Yang Kai did not mind and nodded. "Fine."

Hearing this, Yan Luo was overjoyed and bowed all the way to the ground. "Many thanks, Sir!"

Yan An also cupped his fists at the side. "Congratulations, Family Head, on obtaining your wish."

Yan Luo smiled. "After I leave, you will be the Lord of the Yan Family, so you must manage it well when the time comes."

Yan An bowed. "I will certainly not disappoint the Family Head's trust."

Yan Luo turned around and said, “Sir, we will set out in ten days. What do you think? Of course, if Sir is in a hurry, three days will do as well. It is just that Yan still has some matters here that need to be arranged.”

Stepping down from the position of Family Head naturally meant there were many matters to handle.

"Then ten days it is." Yang Kai stood up. In any case, a few more days would not make much difference.

Yan Luo thanked him profusely and immediately led Yang Kai away to rest.

The guest room was extravagantly luxurious, with luminous pearls adorning the surroundings.

He Yunxiang said, "Sir, the Yan Family will not play tricks, will they?"

Yan Luo's deferential behavior made her somewhat suspicious. Even if he had submitted to overwhelming strength, there was no need for him to act so humble and servile. At the very least, he was still a Star Lord.

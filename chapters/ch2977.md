## Chapter 2977 — Divine Beast Ah Wang

In the brief moment he was distracted, Hao Fei's body trembled and went completely rigid, while the sound of something shattering rang out from within him.

Zhu Lie's figure appeared behind Hao Fei. He gently exhaled, suddenly feeling much better.

He coldly snorted and, paying no heed to the other dumbstruck Demons, flew straight ahead in pursuit.

No one stopped him, nor did anyone dare to.

Only long after Zhu Lie had left did a Demon softly call Hao Fei's name, but there was no response. Hao Fei remained frozen in midair as though he had been subjected to a Binding Technique, without the slightest change in his expression or gaze, like a lifelike statue.

"Sir? Sir Hao Fei!" The Succubus boldly stepped forward and reached out to touch Hao Fei.

With a loud bang, Hao Fei's entire body suddenly exploded into a cloud of blood mist, leaving no corpse or bones behind.

Cries of shock and sharp gasps rang out one after another as every demon stared at the cloud of blood hanging in midair, their eyes trembling, almost convinced they were dreaming.

Demon Fury City's number one expert, the illustrious Sir Hao Fei, was actually dead!

He had been killed in a single exchange by a red-haired young man who had appeared out of nowhere.

Had they not witnessed such a thing with their own eyes, none of the Demons would have been willing to believe it. Yet the truth was right before them, so they had no choice but to believe.

Compared with the earlier scene of that human using an artifact to kill several Demon Kings at once, Hao Fei being slain in a single move shocked the Demons even more.

After the shock came confusion.

Just who was that red-haired young man? And who was that human? They had never heard of such powerful experts in Human Sovereign City. If these two fellows truly came from Human Sovereign City, how could the Demon Race possibly have a place in the Revolving World? They would probably have been exterminated long ago.

Demon Fury City had suffered one upheaval after another. First, several Demon Kings had met violent deaths, and then the number one expert of their race had been obliterated without even leaving a corpse behind. A shadow suddenly fell over the whole of Demon Fury City, and every Demon became anxious and fearful that those two fellows might turn around and attack again. If that truly happened, no one in Demon Fury City would be able to resist them.

"They won't live much longer," a Demon King shouted. "They went to Yin Wind Mountain, a place of certain death. They won't be coming back, so there's nothing to worry about."

Hearing this, the many Demons' spirits lifted as they recalled Yang Kai saying that he intended to go to Yin Wind Mountain to search for a way out.

It was as though a ray of light had appeared amid their despair, and the Demons finally became less fearful. Nevertheless, Demon Fury City had suffered grievous losses this time. Although not many of their clansmen had died, all of them were Demon Kings, including their number one expert and another ranked among the top ten. If this news reached Human Sovereign City, the Human Race there would likely grow restless again.

For a time, many Demons were once more consumed by worry.

A thousand zhang up the lower slopes of Yin Wind Mountain, Yang Kai and the others came to a halt, all wearing solemn expressions.

Yang Kai had piloted the Flowing Cloud Shuttle all the way here, but it had become unable to advance a thousand zhang from the foot of the mountain. They had been forced to leave the Flowing Cloud Shuttle and press onward against the Yin Wind blowing down from the summit.

He led the way, Zhu Qing followed in the middle holding the little black dog, and Li Jiao brought up the rear.

After advancing another thousand zhang in this manner, even Yang Kai felt that he could not move another inch.

Yin Wind Mountain truly lived up to its name. The records in Human Sovereign City's ancient books revealed only a tiny fraction of its true nature. Yang Kai had already tried to overestimate the dangers of Yin Wind Mountain as much as possible, yet he had still underestimated them.

There were no other obstacles on this mountain, only the unceasing frigid Demonic Qi blowing down from its summit. This frigid Demonic Qi contained not only the bitter cold unique to the Frozen Earth that extinguished all vitality, but also the sinister Demonic Qi of the Demon Domain. Mixed together, the two complemented each other and multiplied their power.

Even with Yang Kai's powerful cultivation, he found it somewhat difficult to withstand the ravages of the frigid Demonic Qi. His Emperor Yuan poured out like floodwaters through an opened sluice gate, and after advancing only a thousand zhang, he was already close to exhaustion.

Looking up, he saw only layer upon layer of mist, with no idea where the summit might be.

"Let me!" Zhu Qing said.

Yang Kai nodded and did not force himself to continue. He flashed behind Zhu Qing and let her take the lead while he seized the opportunity to swallow a Spiritual Pill and recover. In such an environment, the person at the front faced the greatest hardship, as they blocked more than half of the frigid Demonic Qi blowing down from the summit. Those behind them needed to expend only a small amount of strength to keep up.

At the very back of the group, Li Jiao's face was pale as he gave Yang Kai a flattering smile, his expression full of ingratiation.

In terms of resistance to this frigid Demonic Qi, he was undoubtedly the weakest of the group. Although Yang Kai's cultivation was lower than his, both his Dragon Clan bloodline and the pure Demonic Qi sealed within his body gave him unique advantages in resisting the frigid Demonic Qi. The same was true of Zhu Qing. The bloodline of a Ninth-Tier Red Dragon was no empty boast, and the Dragon Clan was naturally highly resistant to all forms of power, with this resistance directly proportional to the strength of their bloodline.

Therefore, if Li Jiao wanted to cross Yin Wind Mountain and leave this world, he would have to rely on Yang Kai and Zhu Qing. He was deeply afraid they might abandon him, leaving him anxious and uneasy.

Yang Kai could not be bothered with him. As he circulated his Secret Art to refine the medicinal efficacy of the Spiritual Pill, he silently observed the changes in the frigid Demonic Qi to guard against any unforeseen danger.

"Huh..." Zhu Qing suddenly exclaimed softly, as though she had discovered something extremely unexpected.

Yang Kai asked nervously, "What is it?"

"Ah Wang, she..."

Yang Kai hurriedly leaned forward to look and immediately revealed a surprised expression. The little black dog in Zhu Qing's arms had actually opened its mouth wide toward the oncoming frigid Demonic Qi. Great quantities of it were being swallowed into her belly, greatly reducing the resistance the group faced.

As though sensing Yang Kai and Zhu Qing's attention, she even began bobbing her head smugly and opened her mouth wider. The resistance weakened further, and Zhu Qing, who was walking at the very front, could now barely feel the interference of the frigid Demonic Qi. Her speed immediately increased considerably.

"This beast is truly extraordinary!" Li Jiao hurriedly flattered them, saying with an ingratiating expression, "I wonder where Palace Master Yang found such a Divine Beast. Your fortune is truly profound."

Yang Kai turned back and gave him a long look. "Brother Li seems full of vigor. If you have the energy to chat, why don't you take the lead later?"

"Of course, of course," Li Jiao meekly agreed, wishing he could slap himself across the mouth.

Thanks to the little black dog's miraculous display, the trio's speed suddenly increased considerably. In the time it took an incense stick to burn, they advanced more than a hundred zhang without showing the slightest strain.

The little black dog had a particular fondness for Demonic Qi and Demonic Beasts. Although the frigid Demonic Qi on Yin Wind Mountain had undergone some changes, its fundamental nature remained the same, making it exactly the sort of thing she loved.

If this could continue, they might truly be able to reach the summit with ease.

After another hundred zhang, Zhu Qing, who had been silently observing the little black dog's condition, suddenly said, "Yang Kai, I'm afraid Ah Wang can't hold on much longer."

Hearing this, Yang Kai looked at the little black dog and saw that something was indeed wrong. Her earlier ease had vanished, and she now seemed to be struggling desperately. Her enormous mouth fluctuated in size as though it might return to normal at any moment, while a visible layer of frost had formed over her pitch-black body. Curled up in Zhu Qing's arms, she shivered uncontrollably.

The places where Zhu Qing's body touched hers were likewise covered in frost. Though the layer was thin, it radiated an unimaginable chill, as though it could freeze all things.

Without warning, the little black dog reached her limit, and her gaping mouth abruptly returned to normal.

With no outlet left, the overwhelming frigid Demonic Qi swept down from above.

The instant Zhu Qing came into contact with it, she cried out in alarm and erupted with the full force of her Dragon Clan Origin Power. An enormous phantom of a Red Dragon coiled between Heaven and Earth, enveloping both her and Yang Kai.

Kacha...

The Red Dragon phantom lasted only an instant before cracks spread across it, like a fractured mirror on the verge of shattering.

"Not good, retreat!" Yang Kai's pupils contracted. As he shouted, he wrapped an arm around Zhu Qing's waist, lightly tapped his foot, and rapidly retreated down the mountain.

Li Jiao's reaction was also extraordinarily swift. Seeing even Yang Kai so alarmed, he immediately realized that a crisis beyond his ability to withstand had arrived, so he turned and fled.

Descending the mountain was much easier than climbing it. The trio had exhausted every means and endured countless hardships to advance a hundred zhang, yet they retreated the same distance in the blink of an eye.

They encountered Zhu Lie coming the other way, tenaciously pushing forward against the frigid Demonic Qi, and swept past him beneath his dumbfounded gaze.

A short while later, at the foot of Yin Wind Mountain...

The trio looked up with lingering fear. A layer of cold Qi clung to each of their bodies like maggots burrowing into their bones and could not be easily expelled. It froze them until the power within their bodies circulated sluggishly and their limbs became stiff.

The little black dog's condition was especially severe. She lay motionless in Zhu Qing's arms, her entire body encased in ice as though she were already dead. No matter how Zhu Qing called to her, she gave no response.

"Don't worry. Her life is incredibly tenacious, so she won't die so easily," Yang Kai comforted her, though he was not certain himself.

Last time, the little black dog had been attacked jointly by ten Emperor Realm Masters and had likewise been left on the verge of death, yet she had ultimately survived. Although her condition did not look promising this time, it was still somewhat better than before.

Zhu Qing nodded and sat down cross-legged without a word, holding the little black dog tightly in her arms. As she circulated her Dragon Yuan to dissolve the frigid Demonic Qi that had invaded her body, she also nourished the little black dog's body, hoping for a miracle.

Li Jiao did the same, sitting down while shivering and silently circulating his Secret Art.

The brief erosion of the cold just now had nearly allowed him to smell the scent of death, and even recalling it now left him deeply afraid.

Zhu Lie also returned. He glanced at their condition, frowned slightly, and stood silently to the side.

He had not ventured as deep as the others, and the path he had travelled remained within the limits of what he could endure. Thus, he was uninjured and had merely consumed a great deal of strength.

He still did not understand how Yang Kai and the others had managed to travel so much farther than him. The frigid Qi blowing down from Yin Wind Mountain was on a completely different level from what they had experienced in the Frozen Earth, as different as clouds and mud.

Truly, only the cold within Yin Wind Mountain lived up to the Frozen Earth's fearsome reputation as a forbidden land of life.

Even as an Eighth-Tier Dragon, he had reached his limit just now. If he had gone any farther, he estimated that he would inevitably have suffered frostbite.

Yang Kai glanced at him, forced a smile, and sat down cross-legged to adjust his breathing, his body trembling from the cold.

With Zhu Lie standing guard here, Yang Kai naturally had nothing to worry about. Although he and this Little Brother-in-Law did not get along very well, no matter what, this foolish boy was still a member of the Dragon Clan and had his trustworthy qualities.

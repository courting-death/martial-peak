## Chapter 3138 — Shan Qingluo's Cold Shoulder

She had always been an enchanting woman. Back in Zhongdu, she had been known as the Enchantress Queen, and now that her cultivation had risen, that charm had grown even more devastating. That pretty face alone was enough to turn any man's Divine Soul upside down.

Red was a vulgar color by nature, but on her, it looked perfectly suited, making her beauty seem all the more incomparable.

At that moment, she held a wine jar in one hand, her beautiful eyes half-lidded as she looked up at the stars. She seemed to be admiring the moonlit sky, but a closer look would show that her eyes were unfocused, as if nothing in the world was worth her attention.

She tilted her head back and poured the wine into her mouth. Clear liquor slipped from the corner of her lips, ran down her long, fair neck, and disappeared into the high swell of her chest, soaking her clothes. If a man were present, his blood would likely surge on the spot, leaving him unable to restrain himself.

She lifted one hand toward the Starry Sky, as if trying to seize a star, clutch it tight, and never let go.

But when she opened her hand, it was empty.

She flung the wine jar away, raked both hands through her hair until it was a mess, and gnashed her teeth. "Stinking man!"

The crash of the wine jar startled someone outside. The door opened, and a graceful, exquisite figure walked in. She looked at Shan Qingluo, then at the shattered porcelain on the floor, crouched down, and quietly began cleaning it up.

"Biluo, come here!" Shan Qingluo beckoned to her, her voice so soft and seductive it seemed to melt into the bones. Biluo, despite being a woman herself, felt her heart tremble at the sound, as if something were stirring inside her delicate body and setting her restlessly aflame.

Biluo lifted a hand to her forehead. "My Lady, what are you trying to do this time?"

"Come here already!" Shan Qingluo was clearly drunk and making a scene.

Biluo had no choice but to step forward. Before she could even steady herself, Shan Qingluo pulled her into her arms and laid half her body across her lap.

Looking up, Biluo found that face capable of bewitching all living beings just inches away. Those red lips were as clear and tempting as gemstones, and a dizzying fragrance lingered around her. Shan Qingluo lowered her head and smiled down at her, the loose strands of hair over her forehead brushing Biluo's cheek, tickling her.

Gulp. Biluo swallowed with difficulty, her cheeks flushing red. "M-My Lady..."

Shan Qingluo only smiled, saying nothing, which made Biluo even more nervous. Was the wish she had cherished for so many years finally about to come true? I am not mentally prepared yet. What do I do? What do I do? Should I steel myself and refuse her? But a heaven-sent chance like this is hard to come by. Once she sobers up, I may never have another opportunity.

As the maidservant who had followed Shan Qingluo all the way here from Zhongdu, Biluo had long since given her both body and heart. Her own cultivation was not low now either, having reached the First Stage of the Void King Realm. With her mediocre aptitude, such an achievement was due entirely to Shan Qingluo's generous support and careful guidance over the years.

She would do anything for Shan Qingluo. Even if it meant dying, she would not so much as furrow her brow.

Her breathing quickened and her blood surged. Biluo felt as if she had been struck by lightning, her whole body stiff as a board.

"You like me?" Shan Qingluo suddenly asked with a soft laugh, her eyes hazy. As she spoke, she extended a jade finger and brushed it over Biluo's red lips.

Biluo almost could not stop herself from licking it, but fortunately, she had not lost all reason. Not knowing whether to laugh or cry, she said, "My Lady, you are drunk."

"Nonsense." Shan Qingluo pressed her soft finger against Biluo's red lips, her voice lazy. "It was only a few jars of wine. How could I be drunk? Mm, you still have not answered my question."

Biluo pursed her lips, nodded lightly, and said, "Biluo belongs to My Lady."

Shan Qingluo giggled. "Little Biluo is still the best." She leaned down toward her, her breath warm against those red lips, fragrant and bewitching.

Biluo's eyes went wide. Just as she thought something was about to happen, she felt Shan Qingluo give her a light kiss on the forehead, and disappointment immediately flooded her.

Then Shan Qingluo said, "But we are both women! If I were a man, I would eat you up right now!" She reached out and flicked Biluo's nose.

Biluo looked away and said faintly, "I would not mind..."

"Mind what!" Shan Qingluo sighed. "Men really do have it easy. They leave whenever they please, without a single care, never knowing they have taken someone's heart with them. Who knows what romantic pleasures he is enjoying outside? I am afraid he already has a whole crowd of wives and concubines, even children, and has forgotten this old lady completely."

Biluo immediately said angrily, "My Lady, a man like that is not worth entrusting yourself to. You would be better off not thinking about him."

Shan Qingluo took Biluo's hand, pressed it to her own heart, and smiled. "But this place refuses."

Biluo's heart ached so badly that she almost cried. Sadly, she said, "My Lady, no matter how the world changes or how Heaven and Earth transform, Biluo will always stay with My Lady. If My Lady does not drive me away, I will never leave."

"How could I ever drive you away?" Shan Qingluo pursed her lips and laughed sweetly.

Biluo laughed happily too, then suddenly grew bashful. "If My Lady truly cannot endure it... this servant can think of a way to help My Lady... relieve your worries. Perhaps it could ease My Lady's longing."

Shan Qingluo asked curiously, "What way?"

Biluo said, "In recent years, I learned some transformation techniques."

"Oh?" Shan Qingluo sounded surprised.

"I can transform into that person's appearance."

Shan Qingluo fell silent, seemingly tempted.

Seeing this, Biluo hurriedly pressed the advantage. "Perhaps I cannot reproduce his spirit and bearing, but imitating seventy or eighty percent of him should not be a problem."

"Imitating who?"

A voice suddenly came from outside the window, startling both women.

Biluo immediately jumped up and shouted, "Who goes there!"

This was the very heart of Half Moon Mountain, the Lady's bedchamber. How could someone have broken in without a sound?

Biluo looked up, her mouth falling open at once. She stared blankly. "You, you, you..."

She had actually seen a face she never would have imagined.

Shan Qingluo froze as well, turning her head to look at the face so close at hand, the face she had missed for several dozen years, her expression lost.

"Luo’er!" Yang Kai smiled and raised a hand in greeting, then turned to Biluo. "Little girl, taking advantage of someone's weakness to steal another man's woman is immoral!" He had long known how Biluo felt about Shan Qingluo. She had been like this back in Zhongdu, and he had not expected that after so many years, this woman's tastes still had not changed. She truly was rotten wood that could not be carved.

What a waste. The harmony of Yin and Yang was the Great Dao of Heaven and Earth, yet she insisted on going against it.

Then again, back in the Zhongdu world, something rather subtle had happened between him and Biluo. Seeing her again now, Yang Kai could not help glancing at her red lips.

That sort of thing left one craving more, the memory lingering endlessly.

As he spoke, Yang Kai moved to step in through the window.

With a bang, Shan Qingluo slammed it shut. The hard wooden frame struck Yang Kai on the nose and stopped him in his tracks.

"Hmph!" Liu Yan snorted lightly from the side, her voice carrying an indescribable note of mockery.

Inside the room, Shan Qingluo reached up and rubbed her temples.

Biluo stared at her in a daze. "My Lady, what are you doing?" The man she had missed day and night for so many years had appeared before her eyes, so why had she shut him out? Shouldn't she have thrown herself into his arms at once and poured out all the pain of her longing?

Shan Qingluo frowned. "I drank too much. I seem to be hallucinating."

As she spoke, she opened the window again and looked outside. Their eyes met, and with another bang, she shut the window once more.

"This wine is far too strong. Biluo, where did you find it?"

Biluo did not know whether to laugh or cry.

"Foolish woman!" Outside the window, Yang Kai could bear it no longer. He shoved it open and strode in, looking around with a heroic bearing. "Your husband has returned, yet you dare refuse to see me? You are simply rebelling against Heaven!"

Shan Qingluo turned to look at him and sized him up and down several times. Only then did she cry out in great surprise, "Husband?"

As if she had only just noticed him.

Yang Kai stepped forward, pinched her cheek, and pulled it to one side, distorting that beautiful face at once. Fiercely, he said, "Speak. What are you trying to do?"

"Nuffing!" Shan Qingluo mumbled unclearly. "I thought I was drunk. You really came back? I am so happy, simply overjoyed."

Yet where was the slightest hint of joy on her face?

Yang Kai's eye twitched. Why was this scene so different from what he had imagined? He snorted lightly and let go.

Shan Qingluo rubbed her cheek and stole a timid glance at him, looking guilty as a thief. Carefully, she asked, "Husband, how long have you been here? Did you hear anything... you should not have?"

Yang Kai snorted. "I heard everything, both what I should and what I should not."

Shan Qingluo sighed, and a trace of resolve flashed through her eyes, as if she had made some extremely important decision. She reached out, wrapped an arm around Biluo's shoulders, and pulled her into her embrace, adopting a shameless, do-what-you-will attitude. "Since that is the case, there is no point hiding it. To tell you the truth, Biluo and I are now of one heart and one mind, paired together night and day. Our days are so carefree and unfettered that I forgot you completely long ago. Please forgive me for being so lonely and unable to endure all these years, and for giving my heart to someone else." As she spoke, she bent at the waist and bowed deeply. "I am sorry."

Yang Kai's eye twitched even harder.

Biluo was still staring blankly. Shan Qingluo pressed a hand against her back, and she immediately bent over too, hurriedly saying, "I tried to steal her from you. I am truly so... so sorry."

Shan Qingluo straightened, holding Biluo's small hand. She tilted her head and looked at her with a face full of tender affection. "Husband, no, Little Brother Yang, as long as you do not separate us, anything can be discussed."

"Little. Brother. Yang?" Yang Kai gritted out, word by word.

Shan Qingluo smiled. "You are younger than me, so there is nothing wrong with calling you that. If you feel cheated, I can call you Big Brother Yang instead. It is not as if I will lose a piece of flesh."

Before she could finish, Yang Kai reached out with both hands and grabbed her cheeks, pulling left and right until her pretty face was distorted once more. "Are you done making trouble?"

"Stop!" Shan Qingluo cried coquettishly. "The bond between us as husband and wife is over, so why are you still pawing at me? You are acting like a rogue!"

"Enough. Let's talk seriously." After kneading her face for a while and satisfying his hands, Yang Kai looked at her with feigned dignity, still somewhat unable to understand what exactly she was fussing about. They had not seen each other in several dozen years. He had rushed over eagerly, only for her to perform this grand drama for him. What was this supposed to mean?

"Hmph, I am being serious." Shan Qingluo looked unconvinced. "What, you are allowed to go around flirting outside, but I am not allowed to fall for someone else?"

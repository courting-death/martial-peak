## Chapter 3104 — There Is Excitement to Watch

He Yunxiang suppressed her displeasure and said, "If anyone can give me any clues about Hengluo Star Domain, I will certainly reward them handsomely."

The burly man laughed. "And what will you use as a reward?"

She was only at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and judging by the look of her, she had only advanced not long ago. What good things could she possibly take out? If she was willing to pay with her body, she would certainly attract quite a few people willing to inquire into that Hengluo Star Domain for her.

At the very least, the burly man himself would be more than willing. Otherwise, he would not have deliberately teased her just now.

He Yunxiang smiled faintly, casually took out an object, and said, "This will be the reward!"

It was a round mirror. At first glance, it looked entirely unremarkable, like something a young woman might use at her dressing table. It was old and plain, clearly worn by time, but when He Yunxiang gently urged her Saint Yuan and poured it into the mirror, a ray of light immediately flashed. At the same time, an astonishing energy fluctuation surged out from the mirror.

Every pair of eyes was instantly drawn to that mirror, filled with greed and covetous desire.

Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure! And a Middle-Level Dao Origin-Grade... no, an Upper-Level Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure!

Although no one knew exactly what divine might this round mirror possessed, its grade alone was impossible to ignore. Among everyone present, the Dao Origin Realm cultivators were all only First Stage. Even if they had Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasures in hand, they were only Lower-Level ones; they did not have so much as a single Middle-Level one. When had they ever seen an Upper-Level Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure?

A treasure like this was beyond them; even the Sect Masters and Sect Heads of the various forces did not have one. Usually, only the most powerful Sects in the Ancestral Domain possessed a few such Secret Treasures, and every one of them existed like an Ancestral Treasure guarding the Sect.

Gulp...

The sound of people swallowing rang out.

Since ancient times, wealth had stirred men's hearts. To those who walked the Martial Dao, excellent Pills and high-grade Secret Treasures held an indescribable allure. Obtaining even one at random could greatly increase their strength and let them leap through the dragon gate.

"I know news of that Hengluo Star Domain. Hand me this precious mirror first and let me have a look. If it is not bad, I will tell you the information you need." It was still that burly man speaking. As he spoke, he had already leapt upward, his Dao Origin Realm pressure bearing down on He Yunxiang from above. His five fingers spread wide as he grabbed toward the precious mirror.

He was clearly preparing to make a move and seize it by force.

The remaining Dao Origin Realm cultivators were all startled and inwardly cried out that this was bad. They had all been around here for a long time and understood one another's temperaments quite well. This burly man acted without restraint and had never been reasonable. If he really snatched the thing away, they feared it would be very difficult to make him spit it back out.

In an instant, figure after figure shot into the sky, all pouncing toward He Yunxiang, every one of them aiming straight for the precious mirror.

He Yunxiang sighed softly, her face full of pity as she looked at the crowd pouncing toward her.

A Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator, facing so many Dao Origin Realm cultivators, was not alarmed at all and even showed such an expression. Quite a few people who saw it clicked their tongues in wonder, while an inexplicable thread of unease rose in their hearts.

A fist suddenly appeared and shot out with lightning speed. Wherever it passed, it actually left a spiral-shaped hollow in the air.

Before the fist had even arrived, the burly man's expression changed drastically and his eyes bulged round. He opened his mouth, but before he could utter a single word, the fist struck his face.

As if a watermelon had been smashed open, red and white splashed out. The headless corpse flew a short distance on its momentum before drifting down and smashing into the swamp, sending water spraying in all directions.

A chorus of sharp inhalations rang out. The seven or eight figures pouncing toward He Yunxiang all stopped one after another, standing stiffly in midair. The blood in their bodies nearly froze, and they swallowed with difficulty.

Everyone looked toward the youth lying at ease on the Star Shuttle, their eyes filled with horror.

In that instant just now, they had clearly seen this youth move, and then... and then that burly man had died.

They knew the man's strength all too well. In a one-on-one fight, not one of them was confident of certain victory, yet now he had been blown apart by a single punch before so many eyes, without even the strength to resist, dying as if he were an ant.

Who was this person!

Amid their terror, they even suppressed their breathing, afraid of angering this person.

He Yunxiang still wore a faint smile as her beautiful eyes swept the surroundings. "Has no one heard of Hengluo Star Domain?"

No one answered, fearing that opening their mouths would summon death.

He Yunxiang said, "My lord keeps his word. If anyone can provide information about Hengluo Star Domain, this precious mirror will be his. Of course, if you can point out the entrance to the Star Path leading to Hengluo Star Domain, that would be even better. My lord has said there will certainly be another reward!"

One elderly man among them gathered his courage and said, "Madam, we truly do not know Hengluo Star Domain, nor have we ever heard of it."

They only wanted to send this man and woman away as quickly as possible. With the two of them staying here, the pressure they gave people was too great.

"That is rather unfortunate." He Yunxiang had not expected to uncover any useful news the moment they arrived. Perhaps even after running through every Star Path entrance, there would still be no clues, but there had to be a process of effort and trial.

"We absolutely have not deceived you," the elderly man said sincerely.

He Yunxiang pursed her lips in a smile. "I believe you. However, since all of you are guarding this place, please be sure to keep watch on my lord's behalf. If anyone else comes through this entrance, ask him where he is from. If he is from Hengluo Star Domain, please have him go to Azure Dragon City three years from now, and tell him Yang Kai is waiting for him!"

Azure Dragon City was one of the Ancestral Domain's top great cities, known to everyone throughout the entire Ancestral Domain. It could not be more suitable as a meeting place. As for the three-year period, that was a time Yang Kai had calculated. To run through the Star Path entrances of the entire Ancestral Domain, three years would be just about right. At that time, if there were gains, all well and good; if there were no gains, then he could only go to Azure Dragon City and wait.

"Certainly, certainly!" the elderly man answered again and again.

He Yunxiang said, "When the time comes, if possible, please escort him along the way. Once you reach Azure Dragon City, everyone will receive a reward." She raised the precious mirror in her hand and smiled, saying, "My lord has many things like this."

Everyone's expressions shook, and they all grew excited.

This was a tremendous opportunity. Merely escorting one person to Azure Dragon City could earn them an Upper-Level Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasure. Where under Heaven was there such an easy bargain? Of course, the premise was that a cultivator from Hengluo Star Domain had to come through this entrance.

Yang Kai. Everyone silently remembered this name, because this might very well be their own opportunity.

At the same time, they secretly guessed that this Yang Kai must have no small background. Otherwise, after killing someone, he absolutely would not have deliberately left his name behind. This clearly meant he did not fear the forces behind that burly man coming to seek revenge.

And judging from the strength he had displayed just now, his cultivation was also unfathomable. If someone really went to seek revenge, they feared it would be like throwing a meat bun to a dog.

The Star Shuttle turned into a streak of flowing light and soon vanished.

...

They searched one Star Path entrance after another, leaving behind the same instructions each time.

Quite a few fellows whose greed was stirred at the sight of wealth were killed, and even more people remembered the name Yang Kai, as well as the three-year agreement and Azure Dragon City.

In the blink of an eye, half a year had passed. During this period, Yang Kai and He Yunxiang constantly rushed about between the various Star Path entrances without rest. He Yunxiang knew this matter was of the utmost importance to Yang Kai, so she handled it with great care. On ordinary days, aside from hurrying on their way, she cultivated by herself and had no complaints at all.

Although she had been in the Ancestral Domain for half a year, she had not even gone to experience its many wonders. Her entire life here had been spent traveling and searching for people.

"My lord, the next entrance is almost here." The Star Path Chart in her hand had played a crucial role. Without it, searching through this vast Ancestral Domain would probably have been like fishing for a needle in the sea.

As she spoke, she looked with a somewhat strange expression at a little girl around seven or eight years old.

One day half a month ago, He Yunxiang had awakened from meditation, turned her head, and received a fright.

Another person had actually appeared on the Star Shuttle, and it was a little girl of seven or eight, delicate as carved jade and powdered rouge, extremely adorable. Her eyes were clear and flawless, like a pool of spring water.

From the way Yang Kai addressed her, this little girl seemed to be called Liu Yan.

She simply had no idea how Liu Yan had appeared on her Star Shuttle. Moreover, this little girl was as strange as Yang Kai. From head to toe, she did not give off the slightest fluctuation of Spiritual Qi, as if she had never cultivated.

Yet when facing her, He Yunxiang actually felt an enormous pressure, and this pressure was not much less than when she faced Yang Kai.

Could she possess strength on par with my lord? He Yunxiang simply did not dare believe such a thought. Although cultivators retained their youth after cultivating and age became extremely difficult to judge, she was still only a little girl after all. How strong could she be?

She had asked Yang Kai where Liu Yan had come from.

Yang Kai casually said he had picked her up, leaving He Yunxiang unsure whether to laugh or cry.

Moreover, Liu Yan's way of addressing Yang Kai also left her somewhat puzzled. She called him Master again and again with complete ease, making He Yunxiang unable to help suspecting whether Yang Kai had some special preference in that regard. Otherwise, how could he be completely unmoved by her?

At this moment, Yang Kai was sitting cross-legged on the Star Shuttle, while Liu Yan leaned in his arms, playing with her long, soft hair and looking bored out of her mind. Yang Kai yawned and said, "Mm."

Suddenly, he turned his head, looked off into the distance, and grinned. "How lively."

Liu Yan immediately said, "Go take a look, go take a look."

"What is there to look at? A bunch of ants fighting. What is so interesting about that? Proper business matters."

Liu Yan pouted.

"Fine, fine, fine, we'll go take a look." Yang Kai relented helplessly and pinched her cheek.

Only then did Liu Yan smile. Her secluded cultivation on Spirit Beast Island had greatly changed her temperament, making her seem as if she truly were a child. A phoenix's nirvana... perhaps this was also because of her inheritance.

Without needing instructions, He Yunxiang changed direction and sped to one side, at the same time using a Secret Technique to conceal their trail and aura.

Below, it was indeed very lively. Several hundred people in a mighty procession each used their movement skills, flying forward as swiftly as wind and lightning. The few people in the lead had broad sleeves billowing in the wind and steady auras. They were impressively all Dao Origin Realm experts, and the strongest among them was actually at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. Besides him, there were no fewer than five Second Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivators.

He Yunxiang felt extremely powerful pressure from these people.

Her minor concealment methods could not hide from these people's perception at all. Gazes swept over one after another, but they paid no further attention, clearly having important matters at hand.

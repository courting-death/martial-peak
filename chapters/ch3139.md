## Chapter 3139 — If You Are Wrong, You Must Be Punished

"When have I ever fooled around outside?" Yang Kai felt guilty, and his protest came out with very little force. No, wait. Even if he really had fooled around, and even if he had confessed it to Dong Suzhu and Su Yan, how could Shan Qingluo know? She had to be bluffing! No matter what she said, he would deny it to his dying breath!

Shan Qingluo sneered, then lowered her gaze to Liu Yan. "You even have a child now, and you still dare say you have not been fooling around?" Her expression suddenly softened, and she crouched down. "Little Sister, what is your mother's name?"

Yang Kai burst out laughing and reached over to pat Liu Yan's head. "You're asking her?"

He finally understood why Shan Qingluo had been so prickly from the moment they met. She thought he had brought a daughter home, and her jealousy was thick enough to smell. Then again, if Yang Kai had not been present back when Liu Yan broke out of her shell and took human form, even he would not have dared recognize her, let alone Shan Qingluo, who had not seen Liu Yan in several dozen years. The misunderstanding was inevitable. The change was simply too great.

"Tell her who you are yourself!" Yang Kai said, smiling broadly at Liu Yan.

Liu Yan's eyelashes fluttered. Looking timid, she caught the corner of Yang Kai's robe and slowly hid behind him. "Father, I don't want to stay here. This auntie is strange."

Yang Kai's smile froze. He stared wide-eyed at Liu Yan, suddenly feeling as if one of his own had stabbed him in the back.

"Hehehehe..." Shan Qingluo laughed coldly a few times and slowly stood, her beautiful eyes pressing down on Yang Kai. "What else do you have to say?"

"What the hell am I supposed to say?" Yang Kai was furious. He grabbed Liu Yan and turned her to face him, staring at her sternly. "How could you do this? I've treated you well all these years, so why would you... set me up like this?"

Liu Yan looked close to tears. "Father, you're hurting me!"

"Enough!" Shan Qingluo snapped, pulling Liu Yan behind her as she glared at Yang Kai. "You even bully children? Is this how you act as a father?"

Yang Kai immediately felt as if he could not explain himself no matter how hard he tried. He stepped forward and took her into his arms. "Luo'er, this is a misunderstanding. Let me explain."

"What is there to explain?" Shan Qingluo pushed him away, her face cold. "I truly misjudged you. From this day onward, our bond as husband and wife is severed. Do not come looking for me again."

Yang Kai looked as if he had been struck by lightning. He swayed, all color draining from his face. Beside Shan Qingluo, Liu Yan stuck out her tongue. This was bad. The joke seemed to have gone too far. Biluo, however, looked utterly thrilled. If things truly turned out this way, she might not be without a chance. Taking advantage of the situation was somewhat immoral, but this was also for the sake of helping her Lady escape the sea of suffering as soon as possible. If I do not enter hell, who will?

"Luo'er? Is this truly what you want?" Yang Kai asked, looking at her solemnly.

Mischief flashed through Shan Qingluo's eyes, but she nodded firmly. "My mind is made up."

Yang Kai turned his head, his face full of worldly sorrow. After pondering for a moment, he sighed and said, “So be it. I once acted recklessly in drunken folly; now I only fear that too much affection may wrong a beauty. Luo’er, take good care of yourself.”

Shan Qingluo's heart gave a jump, and she quickly asked, "Where are you going?"

Yang Kai leapt onto the windowsill and gave a casual wave. His figure flickered, then he rushed into the night and vanished in the blink of an eye.

"Hey, come back!" Shan Qingluo was shocked. She had never imagined Yang Kai would actually leave. How could he just leave? She had only been angry that he had stayed away for so many years and had played a little joke on him. This wretched man was actually so decisive, and he did not even know to coax her. She rushed to the window and shouted, "Come back! Come back!"

The night was empty and still. No answer came.

Shan Qingluo went limp and collapsed to the floor, her eyes red as she kept muttering, "Come back, you heartless thing!"

A broad chest pressed against her from behind, and a pair of large hands circled her waist, gently drawing her close. A deep, powerful breath brushed her ear. "I lied."

Shan Qingluo turned around, tears falling like rain. Her small pink fists beat against Yang Kai's chest, but they were soft and weak. Rather than venting, it felt more like spoiled affection.

Yang Kai simply grinned at her without moving.

After pounding him for a while, Shan Qingluo suddenly reached up, wrapped her arms around Yang Kai's neck, lifted her face, and pressed her red lips to his.

“Mm…” Yang Kai’s breathing instantly grew uneven. The body in his arms burned with passion, and a tender pink tongue slipped into his mouth like a spirit snake, acting wantonly and with utter abandon. How could Yang Kai show weakness? He caught that tongue and teased it for a while, causing Shan Qingluo’s already soft body to lose even more strength as she melted limply into his arms.

After a long while, their lips parted, a silvery thread still clinging between them.

Shan Qingluo's eyes were soft and seductive, and she seemed like a flower in full bloom, glowing with an extraordinary radiance. Only then did she remember something and blush. "Your daughter..."

In that rush of passion, she had actually forgotten there was still a little girl beside them. Even though she was bold and reckless, and extremely open when it came to matters between men and women, she still found the situation embarrassing.

"She left," Yang Kai said with a faint smile.

Biluo and Liu Yan had long since sensed trouble and slipped away. Now only the two of them remained in the wing room, one man and one woman, like dry kindling beside open flame.

Shan Qingluo leaned out for a look and found that it was true. The little girl from before had long since disappeared, and even Biluo was nowhere to be found. Her last shred of worry vanished. She cupped Yang Kai's cheeks in both hands and studied him closely, her eyes full of tenderness even as she said, "You heartless thing. I was only teasing you, yet you were actually willing to hurt me like that."

Yang Kai reached out and smoothed the hair by her ear. His eyes were just as tender, as if he wanted to drink in the sight of her and make up for the longing of these several dozen years. Warmly, he said, "I was wrong."

Shan Qingluo said, "If you were wrong, then you have to be punished."

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "Then I wonder how Luo'er intends to punish her husband."

Shan Qingluo raised a hand and pressed it against Yang Kai's chest, then looked up at him with a giggling smile. "I hope husband can endure it. Do not die too soon!"

As soon as the words left her mouth, Saint Yuan burst from her palm and sent Yang Kai flying. She now had Third Stage Void King Realm cultivation and could control her strength perfectly, so the palm would not harm Yang Kai at all.

Yang Kai folded both hands behind his head as he flew straight toward the wall, seemingly unconcerned. He only watched Shan Qingluo with a smile, curious what trick she planned to play.

Shan Qingluo slowly rose. Her red lips parted, and she breathed out a stream of Saint Yuan. Though released later, it arrived first, rushing behind Yang Kai and transforming into a great spider web that hung diagonally in midair.

Yang Kai crashed into the web and immediately felt a powerful hold seize him from behind. He struggled slightly, but not only did he fail to break free, he sank even deeper, until even his hands were stuck fast.

She actually... wanted to play this wildly!

Yang Kai swallowed. Without warning, his blood began to boil, and he stared at Shan Qingluo with eyes like fire, as if he would burn every stitch of clothing from her body.

"Hehehehe..." Shan Qingluo covered her mouth and laughed, her voice so sweet and sultry that Yang Kai's bones went weak before she even touched him.

She lifted a delicate hand, and the doors and windows rattled shut all at once. Then she moved toward Yang Kai with light, unhurried steps. As she walked, her clothes slipped from her body, gradually baring her fair, voluptuous form to the air. Her skin was like mutton-fat white jade, so delicate it seemed a breath might mark it. Her full chest trembled with each step, and the two cherry-like points of deep red were like whirlpools that could swallow Yang Kai's gaze. Her smooth, flat lower abdomen showed not the slightest excess flesh, and her long, fair legs were even more soul-stirring. Her small bare feet were exquisitely shaped; that pair of feet alone could keep a man occupied for an entire day and night.

Yang Kai’s mouth went dry, and he said hoarsely, “You seductress, what are you planning to do?”

Shan Qingluo lifted a foot and stepped onto the great spider web. The limitless scenery between her legs flickered in and out of sight as she looked down at Yang Kai from above, like a god surveying her own territory. "What do you think?" she said lightly. She slid out her scarlet tongue and licked her lips, adding another layer of temptation.

He really could not stand seeing her like this! Fortunately, he had fooled around with Su Yan a few days earlier; otherwise, Yang Kai could not guarantee that he would not break free right now, throw her to the floor, and pounce on her. Unlike Su Yan's reserve, Shan Qingluo had always been this uninhibited in bed, and she could bring Yang Kai a kind of pleasure few others could match. Even so, this was the first time they had played so wildly. It seemed that several dozen years of separation had not been without benefit. At the very least, it had unlocked some of the restraints deep in their hearts.

Shan Qingluo lifted one foot and lightly dragged it down from Yang Kai's collar.

Yang Kai's clothes split apart, revealing his well-built body.

Shan Qingluo licked her red lips, her gaze cold and noble. "Who knows who has enjoyed this body over all these years!"

Yang Kai said with absolute conviction, "Absolutely no one." Even if there had been, he could not admit it. At the very least, he could not admit it now.

Shan Qingluo abruptly waved her hand, shattering what remained of Yang Kai's clothes. "You still dare argue? If I do not make you suffer a little today, you will have forgotten how formidable I am."

Yang Kai grinned. "Then please hurry up and make me suffer, Luo'er. Your husband can hardly wait."

Shan Qingluo straddled him, bent down, and lightly licked Yang Kai's earlobe, her breath as fragrant as orchids. "Aren't you afraid I will eat you?"

Yang Kai said, "I hear there is a kind of spider called a Poison Widow that eats the male after mating. Luo'er, do you want to be that Poison Widow too?"

"Perhaps I do!"

Yang Kai raised his body and suddenly thrust upward without warning. Shan Qingluo immediately straightened, her hair flying as she let out a sharp cry, though her face was filled with pleasure. "Wretched thing, you did not even warn me, ah..."

Yang Kai said, "You are already planning to eat me, so why would I warn you? Let us see who subdues whom today. Luo'er, you had better not lose too quickly!"

Shan Qingluo gritted her teeth. Her body rose and fell with Yang Kai's movements as she gasped between words, "You... are dead... for sure! I absolutely... will not let you... have an easy time."

Her full chest rose and fell without cease until Yang Kai seized it and kneaded fiercely. Shan Qingluo's brows drew together, and her cries rose higher and higher. The enormous spider web swayed unsteadily as boundless spring scenery filled the wing room. Before long, a strange aura spread through the air.

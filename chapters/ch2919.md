## Chapter 2919 — Yang Tai Goes Mad

The bickering between Yang Kai and Yang Tai buzzed incessantly in Gong Yue's ears like flies, leaving him profoundly irritated.

With a wave of his hand, he summoned several Formation Talismans, arranging multiple layers of formations to guard himself. This way, no matter from which direction Yang Kai tried to launch a sneak attack, he would inevitably fall into the formation.

“That’s how it should be!” Yang Tai laughed heartily, taunting the void, “Boy, this old man admits you have some skill, but that’s all there is to it. If you want to live, you’d better run now while you still can, or it’ll soon be too late.”

He was truly afraid of Yang Kai’s various secret techniques, always feeling as if they were designed to counter him. He wished Yang Kai would just leave as soon as possible. As for whether Yang Kai would expose the secret of this place after escaping, that was not his concern.

All he needed to do was open that great door.

Including himself, there were still five people left here, which was enough for the Blood Sacrifice. It would just take a bit more time.

Gong Yue’s face darkened at these words. Although he had joined forces with Yang Tai to set this trap, it was for the sake of the entire Gong family, so he had never intended to leave any survivors. Even Yang Tai was someone he planned to kill. How could he allow Yang Kai to escape alone?

Turning this over in his mind, he spoke, “Young Brother, you say there are demons sealed behind that door—do you have any proof? Yang Tai told me there are ultimate Martial Dao mysteries there.”

Yang Kai’s cold snort echoed from the void, “Do you really believe that? If you truly do, then you’re a fool.”

Gong Yue barked, “I’ve probed the secret of that character myself. It told me Yang Tai wasn’t lying.”

“That is the Demon Script! You’ve been deceived.”

“What?” Gong Yue’s face changed dramatically, and he cried out, “Demon Script?”

Shocked to his core, a flaw appeared in the layers of formations he had set up.

At that moment, Yang Kai suddenly appeared. No one saw how he did it, but when he revealed himself, he was already standing three feet in front of Gong Yue.

The panic on Gong Yue’s face vanished instantly, replaced by a shrewd glint in his eyes. Clearly, everything just now had been an act to lure Yang Kai out.

Yang Kai had really shown himself. With a cold sneer, Gong Yue’s mind stirred as he poured all his strength into activating the surrounding formations, determined to drag Yang Kai into them. This time, he would not be careless. He silently resolved that even if he had to divert his attention from everything else, he would dispose of Yang Kai before proceeding.

But in Yang Kai’s hand appeared a simple, ancient little bell, spinning rapidly as a massive bell shadow suddenly expanded with a roar.

An ancient, desolate aura spread out. Yang Kai’s whole body trembled, as if on the verge of collapse, but the hand holding the little bell remained as steady as a rock.

Clang—

A muffled boom rang out. Everyone felt as if their eardrums had been shattered, their heads spinning. The layers of formations Gong Yue had set up hadn’t even had time to activate before the bell’s sound swept them to pieces, shattering them instantly.

Not only that, but Gong Yue was caught off guard by the Mountain and River Bell striking at such close range. His chest caved in, his back arched high, and his whole body flew out like a ragged sack, crashing into the cave wall and sliding down.

He possessed the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. By rights, he shouldn’t have been so easily defeated, but thinking victory was in his grasp, he had let down his guard and taken the Mountain and River Bell’s fierce blow head-on, suffering a grievous injury.

The bell’s sound rampaged through his flesh and Meridians, leaving him unable to breathe.

With his control lost, the Sole Sovereign Formation finally broke. Hua Yulu and the others, who had been suspended in midair, fell like dumplings, each one crying out in shock.

They were all Emperor Realm cultivators, yet now they tumbled to the ground like infants who hadn’t learned to walk, unable to get up, utterly wretched.

Although most of the Mountain and River Bell’s power had been borne by Gong Yue, the others were still affected. Coupled with being controlled by Gong Yue and losing too much blood, they had long since reached the limits of exhaustion.

Now, they probably couldn’t muster even the strength of ordinary people.

The only one in slightly better shape was Yang Tai. He had entered the formation later, and though he had lost a lot of blood, he was still better off than the others, managing to steady himself after stumbling.

But this sudden blow left his mind reeling. Everything had been going so smoothly, yet in the blink of an eye, the situation had turned upside down. He stood there in a daze for a long while before finally reacting, his eyes filled with venom as he glared at Yang Kai, gritting his teeth, “Why, why, why...?”

A murderous intent surged like a tide, enveloping Yang Kai.

Yang Kai staggered as well. The Mountain and River Bell’s strike had drained all his strength. Now, he was utterly spent, even his Emperor Yuan sluggish. Seeing Yang Tai’s Divine Sense lock onto him, clearly about to strike, he quickly took a deep breath, squeezing out what little power remained in his Dantian, and began chanting an incantation.

Yang Tai was startled, his rage doused as if with cold water, instantly replaced by fear. He shrank back from Yang Kai.

He had suffered at Yang Kai’s hands in the illusion formation before, knowing how strange and deadly these secret techniques were—almost as if they were his nemesis. So, hearing that obscure incantation, he instinctively felt terror.

He retreated several steps before regaining a sense of safety.

Yang Kai also stopped chanting at the right moment. Now, at the end of his strength, he could no longer wield his power at will. Since Yang Tai didn’t move, he couldn’t be bothered to make the first move either.

The current situation gave him a headache. All seven Emperor Realm cultivators were severely weakened, and Yang Tai was in the best condition. If he truly went on a killing spree, Yang Kai could only take Hua Yulu and retreat into the Xuan Realm Pearl. As for the others, he could no longer care about their fate.

Yet Yang Tai was truly terrified of Yang Kai, not daring to attack him again no matter what.

The gradually dimming light caught Yang Tai's attention. He turned toward the blood-red door and let out a wail of anguish: "No, this can't be happening!"

Earlier, under Gong Yue’s control, the Blood Sacrifice had made the door’s glow brighter and brighter, the ancient restriction nearly opening. But after Yang Kai’s interference, the Blood Sacrifice was interrupted, the light faded, and the ancient restriction stabilized once more.

Yang Tai howled a few times, and, as if struck by a sudden thought, turned and glared viciously at one of those present, then reached out to grab him.

“Yang Tai, how dare you!” Wu Kuangyi roared, wanting to resist but lacking the strength. Yang Tai lifted him like a chicken.

Before everyone’s eyes, Yang Tai struck Wu Kuangyi several times, then hurled him toward the blood-red door.

Wu Kuangyi let out a pained scream, which was abruptly cut off.

He crashed into the blood-red door. Whether from the impact or the force of Yang Tai’s earlier strikes, his body suddenly exploded into a cloud of blood, leaving no bones behind. Thick blood and flesh splattered across the door.

The dimming door regained a trace of brightness.

Everyone who witnessed this scene was shaken. Wu Kuangyi’s death left the survivors with a chilling sense of dread, each wondering if they would be Yang Tai’s next target.

“Not enough, not enough, far from enough!” Yang Tai seemed utterly deranged. After killing Wu Kuangyi with his own hands, he looked deeply frustrated, muttering to himself as his gaze swept over the remaining people.

At the same time, a faint black aura reappeared around him—the Demonic Qi Yang Kai knew all too well.

Everyone he looked at instinctively averted their eyes, afraid to provoke him. Only Yang Kai met his gaze coldly, sneering, “Give it up, Yang Tai. Your plan is finished. This restriction can never be opened again. You’ve entered the Demonic State—your death is well deserved!”

On the other side, Gong Yue, who had barely recovered from Yang Kai’s blow, stared blankly at the Demonic Qi swirling around Yang Tai, pointing at him with a trembling finger, “You, you...”

He finally realized something was wrong. The traces of Demonic Qi on Yang Tai were so obvious—how could Gong Yue not notice?

Only now did he begin to believe what Yang Kai had said earlier.

Yang Tai had truly entered the Demonic State. If that was the case, then Yang Tai’s promises were worthless...

At this thought, Gong Yue’s heart turned to ashes. He had risked everything to collude with Yang Tai, only to find it was all a scam. He didn’t know what Yang Tai was after, but he was certain there were no ultimate Martial Dao mysteries behind that door.

“Give up?” Yang Tai ignored Gong Yue, screaming hysterically, “My mission isn’t complete—how could I give up?”

Yang Kai caught onto something and frowned. “Mission? Is opening this restriction your mission? Did the thing behind the door give you this mission? Have you communicated with it?”

Yang Tai fell silent, apparently realizing he had said too much. His gaze swept over the crowd as he sneered, “A bunch of trash. Even if I blood sacrifice all of you, it’s still far from enough!”

Actually, had Yang Kai not interrupted the Blood Sacrifice, it would have been sufficient. But now that it was disrupted, all their efforts had come to naught.

Yang Tai had completely lost his mind, spreading his arms wide as if to embrace the entire world. The Demonic Qi around him surged with unprecedented intensity, churning in pitch-black waves that sent shivers down everyone's spine. He opened his mouth and roared, "Open your dog eyes wide and take a good look! A new era is about to dawn, and all of you in the future will be nothing but ants!"

He shouted something incomprehensible, then burst into manic laughter and charged toward the blood-red door.

Yang Kai’s eyelids twitched at the sight, and the others were dumbfounded. No one had expected such a dramatic turn of events.

From the look of it, Yang Tai intended to sacrifice himself to break the ancient restriction.

No one tried to stop him. First, Yang Tai’s treachery had left everyone in such a miserable state that his death was well deserved. Second, after seeing Wu Kuangyi killed for the Blood Sacrifice with no effect, it was unlikely that another would make any difference now.

So everyone just watched coldly.

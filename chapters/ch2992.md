## Chapter 2992 — Uncle Yang

Enormous skulls flew through the air, spewing streams of extremely cold Yin Qi from their mouths. That Yin Qi seemed capable of freezing a person's Emperor Yuan, and each stream clearly possessed tremendous power.

In an instant, the young woman was surrounded so tightly that not even water could pass through, and her figure disappeared from sight.

Xiong Kaishan snorted coldly. “There are always ignorant juniors who don’t know the immensity of Heaven and Earth. Today, this Lord will teach you what it means that there is always a Heaven beyond Heaven and people greater than yourself!”

He continued forming hand seals, and the might of the flying skulls grew increasingly astonishing.

A thunderous explosion rang out, and Xiong Kaishan's expression changed, his shock plain for all to see. Looking up, he saw the skulls tumbling through the air as an enormous force blasted them all away.

The young woman's figure was revealed once more. Standing proudly in the void, she was as unstoppable as a God of War.

"Wretched slut, how dare you damage my Imperial Treasure!" Xiong Kaishan's eyes instantly turned red. Those skulls were his greatest reliance, yet the light in one skull's eye sockets had now dimmed. It drifted unsteadily and was somewhat beyond his control, clearly having suffered a severe loss of spirituality.

He had never expected that the young woman could blast aside even his Imperial Treasure with her flesh-and-blood body.

At this moment, a pair of gauntlets had appeared over the young woman's fists. The gauntlets were pitch-black throughout and gave off an unfathomably profound air. Judging by the aura radiating from them, they were clearly an extraordinary Imperial Treasure as well.

It was precisely by relying on the might of this Imperial Treasure that the young woman had managed to blast away the skulls surrounding her.

Shouting furiously, Xiong Kaishan once again drove the skulls to attack the young woman from both sides. At the same time, he roared, "Are you all here just to watch the show?"

After this exchange, he no longer had any confidence that he could defeat the young woman and could only pin his hopes on the others. As long as everyone present joined forces, then no matter how strong she was, two fists could not contend with four hands.

The wine-red-nosed old man immediately echoed, "What are you all waiting for? If you don't act now, then when will you?"

As soon as he finished speaking, he was the first to act. With a flip of his hand, he summoned an Imperial Treasure resembling a wine gourd. It swelled against the wind, its mouth pointing directly toward the young woman before spewing out monstrous flames that seemed capable of incinerating all things.

Once he acted, the remaining Emperor Realm Masters who had still been watching could no longer restrain themselves. They summoned their Imperial Treasures and attacked the young woman one after another. In an instant, Imperial Treasures and Secret Techniques flew through the air together, creating an extraordinarily lively scene.

Although the young woman was quite strong, she found it difficult to withstand the combined attacks of so many experts. She immediately became flustered, constantly dodging and weaving through the various attacks. She wanted to break through the encirclement and defeat them one by one, but she had already lost the initiative and remained trapped in place.

From this alone, it was clear that she truly lacked combat experience. She had thought herself strong enough and had lured these people here, but who could have known that she would instead leave herself unable to act freely?

Even so, she was formidable enough. Her fists swept out with commanding force, unleashing tremendous power with every blow, and she was not at the slightest disadvantage even when contending with Imperial Treasures.

Her pair of delicate fists could almost be considered the equal of an Imperial Treasure. The fact that she could remain standing against the combined assault of nearly ten Emperor Realm Masters was more than enough to demonstrate her strength. Until now, the Dragon Clan was the only one Yang Kai knew capable of accomplishing such a feat with a flesh-and-blood body.

"Friend, why haven't you acted yet?" someone suddenly asked curiously, turning to look at Yang Kai.

All the Emperor Realm Masters present had already taken action, either charging forward to attack or summoning Secret Treasures and unleashing Secret Techniques. Yang Kai alone remained motionless, so he naturally stood out.

Yang Kai nodded lightly. "Don't rush, don't rush. I'll act right now."

As he spoke, he pointed a finger to the side.

"You..." The man paled in shock, never having expected Yang Kai to suddenly attack him without warning. As far as he knew, he had not provoked this young man, nor had they ever met before, so there was no reason for him to be targeted all of a sudden.

What was going on?

Suppressing his confusion and shock, he hurriedly circulated his Emperor Yuan to protect himself. At the same time, his feet tapped rapidly through the air as he tried to put some distance between himself and Yang Kai.

Unfortunately, it was all futile. No matter how far he retreated, he was unable to escape the power of Yang Kai's finger.

The fingertip struck the man, and surging power poured out. The protective Emperor Yuan he had hastily summoned was pierced as easily as a bubble. Blood sprayed wildly from his mouth, and his aura abruptly weakened.

Yang Kai brought down another palm. An enormous palm print descended and plunged the man's vision into darkness, knocking him unconscious on the spot.

This sudden turn of events happened in the blink of an eye, shocking everyone else.

The wine-red-nosed old man roared angrily, "This man is in league with that slut!"

Yang Kai chuckled. "Say whatever you like."

The only reason he had followed them here was to see whether the young woman was someone he knew. By now, he could more or less conclude that he had mistaken her for someone else, but he was still unwilling to stand by and do nothing.

These fellows were clearly not good people, so they deserved to be taught a lesson.

As he spoke, the wine-red-nosed old man beckoned with his hand, and the enormous gourd immediately flew back. Its mouth pointed at Yang Kai, and the surrounding Spiritual Qi instantly fell into disorder. Just as flames were about to erupt from the gourd's mouth, however, the old man's vision blurred and a person suddenly appeared three cun in front of him.

"Hiss..." The old man paled in fright and focused his gaze, only then discovering that the young man had somehow arrived right before him.

What kind of Divine Ability was this? It had not left the slightest trace!

Panic crossed his face. He opened his mouth and bit the tip of his tongue, directly spraying out an arrow of blood as a cold smile appeared on his face.

He thought this attack was certain to succeed, but contrary to his expectations, the blood arrow somehow missed. It seemed to be drawn aside by an invisible force and flew past the young man's ear.

"How could this be?" the old man cried out.

At a distance of only three cun, and with the attack launched by surprise, even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master would have been struck. How could he possibly have missed?

"You old bastard, you're quite insidious!" Yang Kai nodded, slowly raised a hand, and pressed it against the old man's chest.

The old man was instantly scared out of his wits, but soon noticed that nothing seemed wrong with him. Just as he was secretly rejoicing, an irresistible force erupted from his chest. His entire body convulsed as though struck by lightning, and he flew backward, his fate unknown.

Without anyone controlling it, the gourd-shaped Imperial Treasure immediately returned to its original size and fell from the sky.

As soon as Yang Kai acted, he crippled two Emperor Realm Masters, and less than three breaths had passed from beginning to end.

Such terrifying combat strength frightened the others out of their wits. They had thought the young woman was terrifying enough, but another person had appeared who was even more terrifying.

How could anyone still have the confidence to continue fighting now? Their expressions changed, and they scattered in every direction, hoping to flee this place. Only Xiong Kaishan remained entangled with the young woman. It was not that he did not want to escape, but with no one cooperating with him, he had immediately lost the initiative and was unable to break free of her.

“This Young Master rarely goes out of his way to uphold justice. If I let the lot of you escape, where would my face be?” Yang Kai stood in place without moving, but the surrounding Laws of Space began to surge.

With him at the centre, the area within a radius of one hundred zhang instantly transformed into a cage.

Every Emperor Realm Master who reached the edge of the cage was blocked by an invisible barrier. They crashed into it and were left dizzy and disoriented.

"Huh?" A surprised cry rang out. The young woman, who had been suppressing Xiong Kaishan with a relentless assault, looked up toward Yang Kai, tilted her head, and wore a puzzled expression.

She vaguely felt that this person's voice sounded familiar, and his back also looked extremely familiar.

Immediately afterward, a figure leapt into her mind, and she cried out in a sweet voice, "Uncle Yang?"

This form of address made Yang Kai tremble. The hope he had already abandoned flared to life again. Turning to look at the delighted young woman, he exclaimed in surprise, "Yun'er?"

"It's me, Uncle Yang! I'm Yun'er!" Lin Yun'er cheered, her excitement plain for all to see. Perhaps because she had encountered an old acquaintance in a foreign land, the young woman's eyes misted over with emotion.

"It really is you." Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and looked Lin Yun'er up and down. "I almost didn't recognize you."

Although he had suspected it before, Yang Kai truly had not dared to believe that this young woman was Lin Yun'er.

After all, many years had passed. Back on Dark Star, when Yang Kai took Lin Yun'er into Lingxiao Sect, she had only been a little girl of five or six. Later, when Yang Yan left the Hengluo Star Domain to return to the Star Realm, she had taken no one with her except Lin Yun'er. Many years had passed since then without the slightest news from either of them.

In the blink of an eye, the little girl from back then had grown into a young woman. If that were all, Yang Kai would not have failed to recognize her. Although a girl might change greatly as she grew up, some traces of her former appearance would always remain.

The current Lin Yun'er still bore many similarities to how she had looked back then.

What had made Yang Kai dismiss his own guess was Lin Yun'er's current cultivation.

First-Stage Emperor Realm! Moreover, she could fight opponents above her realm, while cultivators at the same level were as fragile as paper before her and could not withstand a single blow.

If not for this, Yang Kai would not have hesitated to recognize her.

When Yang Yan took her away back then, Lin Yun'er had been very young and her cultivation pitifully low. Even if Yang Yan had brought her to the Star Realm and she had encountered opportunities of her own, she should not have grown this quickly, should she?

Even Yang Kai himself was only at the First-Stage Emperor Realm!

Lin Yun'er's strength was already almost on par with his own. What was going on?

However, no matter how shocked Yang Kai was, there was no changing the fact that the young woman was Lin Yun'er. Her cry of "Uncle Yang" was the best proof.

Back when they were in Lingxiao Sect on Dark Star, the little girl had always addressed him this way.

While the two of them spoke, Yang Kai's manipulation of the Laws of Space was interrupted. The imprisoned Emperor Realm Masters finally regained their freedom and hurriedly used their Movement Skills to flee, covering a great distance in the blink of an eye.

Yang Kai turned his head to glance at them, frowned, and raised his hand to send out more than ten Moon Blades.

Space was sliced apart, and several miserable screams rang out in the distance. Someone had clearly been struck, but half of them still managed to escape.

Xiong Kaishan also wanted to flee. His battle with Lin Yun'er had made him feel the threat of death, and only now did he understand how terrifying this little girl was. Taking advantage of Lin Yun'er's distraction, he hurriedly recalled his skulls, then flickered and concealed himself inside one of them before attempting to escape.

Lin Yun'er came back to her senses at this moment and wiped the corner of her eye. "Uncle Yang, I'll deal with him first, then come talk to you."

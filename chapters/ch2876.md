## Chapter 2876 — Mo Duo Has Arrived

In the blink of an eye, a month had passed.

During this month, some changes had taken place within Shaman Niu's tribe's encampment.

The most obvious change was the explosive increase in numbers.

After three months of replenishment through battle, the three-thousand-strong force had grown to five thousand. After capturing this city, their numbers increased even more, reaching as many as seven thousand.

And now, that number had more than doubled, with the total approaching the twenty-thousand mark.

This was an extremely terrifying figure, one that was completely beyond what a Great Shaman Master could control. Only Shaman Kings could command so many tribesmen.

Naturally, the extra tribesmen had all been sent over by the Demon Kings led by Fei Li. With Yu, Lu, and the other Shaman Masters working hard to heal them, many tribesmen had regained their senses.

The Shaman Warriors did not know where these demonized tribesmen had come from. They simply followed Yang Kai's orders, going outside every few days to receive them. Bao Qi and Shaya had also made significant contributions to the process. If it were not for these two Demon Kings taking the lead, the handover would not have gone so smoothly.

Every Shaman Master was so busy their feet barely touched the ground, yet each wore a look of joy.

This was not the only good news, for among the tribesmen who had been sent over, there were even some with shamanic abilities.

Originally, Shaman Niu's tribe had only a dozen or so shamans, but after a month, this number had expanded to forty or fifty. Most were Shaman Apprentices and Shaman Warriors, with only a handful at the Shaman Master level, and not a single Great Shaman Master to be seen.

That was to be expected. Great Shaman Masters had shamanic arts to protect themselves, so under normal circumstances, it was unlikely for them to be demonized. If they really encountered a powerful expert of the Demon Race, they would have been killed. How could any be left for Yang Kai?

Besides that, the Demonic Beasts under the various Demon Kings were also sent over one after another.

Originally, Shaman Niu's tribe's cavalry squad had only a few dozen members, but now it had expanded to a thousand. Once a force of a thousand riders charged, the momentum they could unleash would be formidable—sure to play a decisive role in the battles to come.

With more people, the various squads naturally had to be reorganized. However, Yang Kai did not interfere in this matter, leaving it to Yu and Lu to handle, trusting that they could manage everything well.

The Sky Cavalry went out to scout every day, and the range of their reconnaissance grew ever wider.

According to the information received, the Barbarian Tribe's counterattack had been quite effective. They had already reclaimed vast swathes of lost territory. Wherever the armies of the various tribes passed, they often won more than they lost, step by step pushing the Demon Race northward.

As the vast front shrank, the Demon Race's forces began to concentrate, and the resistance the Barbarian Tribe encountered grew stronger and fiercer. Several major tribes had already gathered all their strength to fight great battles with the Demon Race, with victories and defeats on both sides.

Anyone with a bit of foresight knew that such scattered skirmishes would soon become rare. Large-scale battles between the two races were about to begin.

This was indeed the case. One day, a month later, a sharp horn suddenly sounded in the encampment, summoning everyone.

At the same time, there was a knock at the door of Yang Kai's room, and Yu's voice came from outside.

Yang Kai got up and opened the door. The twins stood outside, their expressions solemn. As soon as he appeared, Yu immediately said, "Sir, the Shaman Kings have summoned all the tribesmen of the Southern Barbarian Tribe to gather at the Jiaohe region!"

Yang Kai's expression turned serious. "How much time do we have?"

"Ten days."

"Are we about to fight a decisive battle with someone?" Yang Kai frowned.

Yu said, "The message says that more than a dozen Demon Kings have gathered over there, so all the strength of the Southern Barbarian Tribe needs to be assembled."

"More than a dozen Demon Kings..." Yang Kai raised his eyebrows. The first thought that came to mind was whether any of those Demon Kings were his people. If so, things would be much easier.

The sound of the horn still echoed in the city. Since the order came from the Shaman Kings, Yang Kai naturally could not disobey. He nodded and said, "Have the tribesmen prepare. We set out in two hours."

"Yes!" The twins nodded and turned to leave.

Two hours later, nearly twenty thousand people gathered outside the city. Yang Kai did not ride the giant eagle but instead sat upright on the back of a tall Demonic Beast. Looking over his army, he said nothing more, simply waved his hand and called out in a low voice, "Move out!"

The sound of orderly footsteps rang out, the rhythm of their march beating against the earth like war drums. Nearly twenty thousand people, divided into more than twenty squads, set out in a mighty procession toward the Jiaohe region.

A moment later, Yang Kai turned to look at Bao Qi and said in a low voice, "Ask Fei Li about the situation on the Demon Race's side."

Bao Qi nodded slightly. A Black Crow flew from his body, flapping its wings and disappearing from everyone's sight. The many barbarians were not surprised by this.

Marching with twenty thousand people was no simple matter. Coordinating the squads and maintaining their cooperation and vigilance all required considerable skill. Although it was Yang Kai's first time commanding so many people, he at least had the foundation of the Emperor Realm and a powerful Divine Sense. So after a few days of initial discomfort, he was soon able to issue all kinds of orders in an orderly fashion, commanding with the air of a true, battle-hardened commander.

Although the army was large, their speed was not slow at all.

Along the way, they occasionally encountered scattered Demon Race stragglers, but they were all easily wiped out. Fighting their way forward, Shaman Niu's tribe's morale soared ever higher.

However, a trace of unease welled up in Yang Kai's heart.

The reason was that Fei Li had not sent any reply, which made him sense something was wrong. Fei Li had been branded with his Soul Brand and had become his slave. Although the distance was too great to sense his thoughts, Yang Kai knew Fei Li could never betray him again.

Yet after several days, there was still no reply from him.

From what he could sense, Fei Li was not dead either.

It was not until the seventh day that a small black dot appeared on the horizon. Yang Kai looked up and confirmed it was a Black Crow formed from Demonic Qi, the most common means of communication among Demon Kings.

The Black Crow flew straight toward them, circled above the army for a while, then landed on Bao Qi's shoulder.

Bao Qi grabbed the Black Crow, and the lifelike bird immediately turned into a wisp of black energy, which he absorbed into his body.

Immediately after, Bao Qi's whole body trembled, and a trace of terror appeared on his face. He turned to Yang Kai and said, "Mo Duo... has arrived!"

"What?" Shaya's face also changed dramatically. She looked at Bao Qi in shock. "How could he possibly come?"

Yang Kai frowned. "Who is that?"

From the expressions of the two Demon Kings, he guessed that this Mo Duo was definitely an extraordinary figure. Otherwise, Bao Qi and Shaya would not be so afraid. For a name alone to have such an effect, it could only be...

"Mo Duo is a Demon Saint!"

Yang Kai grew even more confused. "Aren't the Demon Saints all recovering from their injuries? Then what about Mo Duo?" He glanced at Shaya. Most of his information about the Demon Race came from her, and she had indeed said before that the Demon Saints were all recuperating. The intelligence the Barbarian Tribe had received also confirmed this.

For experts like the Demon Saints, recovering from injuries was not something that could be done in just a few months.

Shaya's face turned pale. "Lord Mo Duo... should be a newcomer."

"A newcomer?"

Bao Qi said, "He only arrived recently."

Yang Kai was shocked. "How is that possible? Can your Demon Domain send Demon Saints over at any time?"

If that were really the case, then the Barbarian Tribe had no hope at all. In the last great battle, the Demon Saints had suffered, and so had the Shaman Saints. Both sides had been racing to recover, which was why there had been no sign of the Saints in recent months.

But if the Demon Domain could casually send more Demon Saints over, the Barbarian Tribe would have no power to resist.

Bao Qi shook his head. "Normally, that's impossible. The last time, the Great Demon God opened the boundary and let enough demons through. If more came, the boundary would become unstable, and we wouldn't be able to return. Lord Mo Duo... must have paid some price to descend upon this land."

Shaya also said, "The means of the Demon Saints are beyond imagination. Perhaps there are things we don't understand."

Bao Qi took a deep breath. "In any case, the Demon Saint Mo Duo has already arrived, and his target is the Southern Barbarian Tribe."

Yang Kai's expression changed.

"Sir, we can't go any further. The reason Fei Li took so long to reply was because Mo Duo the Demon Saint was by his side, and he didn't dare send a message. But according to the information he obtained, Mo Duo intends to wipe out one of your tribes this time, to let you barbarians know the consequences of killing demons. The reason he hasn't acted yet is that he's waiting for you to gather. Once the time is right, he'll use his divine abilities to annihilate your entire army."

Shaya said, "Yes, sir, let's go back."

Yang Kai glanced at them and said in a deep voice, "If it were you, would you press forward or shrink back in fear?"

The two did not answer. Shaya's choice aside, Bao Qi would definitely press forward. Twenty thousand people was no small number. Once they joined the Southern Barbarian Tribe, they might have an unexpected effect. Even if they could not turn defeat into victory, they would definitely greatly strengthen the Southern Barbarian Tribe.

Both already knew Yang Kai's choice.

"Accelerate!" Yang Kai shouted in a low voice, urging the Demonic Beast beneath him to speed up.

The Shaman Masters began chanting, and circles of Lightness Art enveloped the army, immediately increasing their marching speed.

In a single day, they covered two days' worth of distance, and the assembly point was already in sight.

Yet at that very moment, an overwhelmingly ominous aura suddenly descended from all directions, as though heaven and earth themselves had sensed something amiss and were issuing a warning to all living things.

The Demonic Beasts beneath the cavalry clearly showed signs of unease, becoming uncontrollable and neighing wildly. Dozens of Demonic Beasts even charged into the crowd, throwing the orderly ranks into chaos.

The army's marching rhythm was instantly disrupted.

Yang Kai frowned, sensing something, and looked up ahead, narrowing his eyes. He raised his hand to halt the army's advance.

The Demonic Beasts' agitation grew stronger and stronger. The cavalry tried hard to restrain them, but to no avail.

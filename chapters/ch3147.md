## Chapter 3147 — Falling From the Sky

Tongxuan Continent was the second stop on Yang Kai's Martial Dao path, and an important springboard on his journey toward a higher world. Without the many opportunities and trials he had encountered there, he never would have achieved what he had today.

His thoughts drifted, the past passing like smoke. From the Void, Yang Kai gazed at the star he had been parted from for so long, his face calm as still water.

All his earlier worries had come true.

Viewed from the Starry Sky, Tongxuan Continent's condition was actually even worse than Cuiwei Star's. The star looked as if it had suffered a grave illness, showing a sickly decline, like an old man in his twilight years walking toward his own grave.

Pitch-black blotches spread across Tongxuan Continent one after another, as if someone had casually splashed a few huge drops of ink onto it. Even from thousands upon tens of thousands of li away in the Starry Sky, they were clearly visible.

A faint black Qi also hung over the surface of the star.

Yang Kai studied those "ink spots" and vaguely felt that their positions formed a kind of array. They were connected to one another, echoing from afar, weaving an invisible great net that wrapped around Tongxuan Continent and sealed it off.

This great array had not fully taken shape yet, but it was already slowly revealing extraordinary might. Even from out in the Starry Sky, Yang Kai could feel an extremely oppressive aura from it, to say nothing of the people living on Tongxuan Continent. Everyone trapped inside would be affected by this formation. This great array was utterly inhuman. If it were truly allowed to activate completely, Tongxuan Continent would surely suffer disaster.

Fortunately, he had not arrived too late.

Although he did not know when this array would fully take shape and activate, it clearly was not something that could be completed in just a few days.

And with his current ability, a few days was more than enough for him to sweep back and forth across Tongxuan Continent and slaughter every last invading enemy.

By rights, with a Star Lord presiding over Tongxuan Continent, the cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain should not have been able to invade so easily. A Star Lord's might was extraordinary. Once a Star Lord stood upon their own Cultivation Star, strengthened by its Stellar Origin, they were practically invincible.

But Tongxuan Continent's situation was different. Just as he had told Liu Yan earlier, Tongxuan Continent had already suffered Spiritual Qi exhaustion several dozen years ago. Even though Xia Ningshang had later refined its Stellar Origin to help it recover, how much could it truly have regained in a few short decades?

Compared to other Cultivation Stars, Tongxuan Continent's World Energy and Laws were much weaker. Otherwise, the Saint Realm would not have reigned supreme on Tongxuan Continent back then. Precisely because of its Laws and Spiritual Qi, Tongxuan Continent could not give birth to stronger cultivators, while other stars in the same Star Domain could produce Saint King Realm, Origin Returning Realm, and even Void King Realm cultivators.

A few dozen years was enough at most to let Tongxuan Continent recover a little vitality, and even that slender thread of life had probably been exhausted by the arrival of Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators. If Xia Ningshang wanted to resist their invasion, she had to borrow the power of the entire star. Use that power too many times, and it would inevitably place an unbearable burden on Tongxuan Continent.

So even as a Star Lord, she could not keep the cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain blocked outside the star forever. Letting them enter Tongxuan Continent was probably a helpless move. In a flash, Yang Kai had thought through most of Xia Ningshang's current predicament.

Suppressing the longing in his heart, he admitted to himself that he did not know where to look for Xia Ningshang, Xue Yue, and the others. Tongxuan Continent was neither especially large nor especially small. If they were hiding somewhere, he definitely would not find them in a short while.

It would be better to spend that time killing some cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain and let them know the fate of those who offended someone they should never have provoked.

After watching for a moment, Yang Kai launched himself forward and plunged toward Tongxuan Continent.

He fixed his sights on the largest "ink spot" on the star ahead and allowed the pull of the earth to drag him down. Liu Yan followed close behind, unhurried in her movements yet not falling behind him by even the slightest bit.

In only a short while, the two of them rushed into the atmosphere together. The aura around them made Yang Kai frown. It was foul, pungent, and deeply uncomfortable, but his physique was powerful enough to ignore its influence. If an ordinary cultivator were wrapped in this aura, though, it would probably not be long before their mind was affected and they turned into a walking corpse.

Earlier, while observing from the Starry Sky, he had only felt that Tongxuan Continent's situation was grim. Now that he had entered the region covered by this "ink spot," he discovered it was even worse than he had imagined.

He had no idea what the hell this "ink spot" was, but being inside it felt like stepping into purgatory.

Fierce winds roared past his ears, Yang Kai's robes snapping loudly as the gale whipped his black hair behind him. At this moment, he was falling headfirst, like a meteor dropping from the heavens, piercing the air as he descended toward the earth.

His astonishing speed dragged his body against the air, igniting a blaze of firelight. To anyone looking up from the ground, it would look as if a real meteor were falling, an exceedingly magnificent sight.

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes slightly, raised one hand, spread his fingers into a palm, bent his arm, then slowly pushed downward.

An incomparably huge palm print suddenly formed, stretching horizontally in front of him and pressing toward the ground along with him. As the distance closed, the palm print grew larger and larger, as if it could cover the sky and blot out the earth.

Inside the "ink spot" below, in the largest building, an old man with a face steeped in death Qi was meditating and cultivating. Judging by his robes, he was clearly from Netherworld Sect, and his cultivation was extremely high, reaching the Third Stage of the Void King Realm. With such cultivation, even in Netherworld Sect, he would at least hold an Elder-level position.

Netherworld Sect originally did not have so many Third Stage Void King Realm cultivators. Its number of masters had once been almost the same as Lingxiao Sect's at its peak, only three or four in all. But invading Hengluo Star Domain had brought enormous benefits to the whole of Netherworld Sect. Whether they were refining Blood Sea Banners, Ten Thousand Souls Banners, or corpses, they needed living people as materials, and the stronger those living people were, the greater the benefits they brought.

Netherworld Sect had unified Great Wilderness Star Domain, and once it gave an order, none dared disobey. Fundamentally, no force there was its match. Even so, they could not be too wanton in their cultivation. At most, they would find some pretext to destroy a few Sect forces and bring those Sects' cultivators back as cultivation materials. Even then, they could not go too far. Everything had its limits, and it was precisely because Netherworld Sect could grasp those limits that it could unify Great Wilderness Star Domain without rousing the wrath of the entire Star Domain.

But after coming to Hengluo Star Domain, everything changed. Their suppressed cruelty was unleashed. To them, every cultivator who belonged to Hengluo Star Domain could be slaughtered!

With countless cultivators from Hengluo Star Domain as cultivation materials, how could the people of Netherworld Sect not improve their cultivation quickly?

It had only been a little more than ten years since the invasion, but every member of Netherworld Sect had advanced by at least one Realm. The Elders, Protectors, Deacons, and the like were even more astonishing. Origin Returning Realm cultivators had advanced to the Void King Realm, First Stage Void King Realm cultivators to the Second Stage, and Second Stage cultivators to the Third Stage!

If it were only individual improvement, that would be one thing. The real issue was the overall rise in strength among all Netherworld Sect disciples. This current state was extremely terrifying. In terms of overall strength, Netherworld Sect had long since left Lingxiao Sect far behind. It now had more than a dozen Third Stage Void King Realm cultivators alone, and more than half of them had gained their chance to advance only after invading Hengluo Star Domain.

As for Second Stage and First Stage Void King Realm cultivators, they were emerging one after another without end.

It was precisely because they had tasted all these benefits that Netherworld Sect was so eager in its invasion of Hengluo Star Domain. Wherever they passed, no one in Hengluo Star Domain could stop them. Even Hengluo Commerce Guild had fallen, to say nothing of other forces. Only those Cultivation Stars with a Star Lord presiding over them made them feel even slightly troubled.

And Tongxuan Continent was one such troublesome star, but... only slightly troublesome.

This star's World Energy was thin, and the level of its Principles was low. Presumably, it could not give birth to anything good. Under ordinary circumstances, Netherworld Sect might not have been willing to spend the effort to deal with Tongxuan Continent.

But many powerful cultivators had gathered here! This was exactly what Netherworld Sect wanted. If they could turn all these cultivators into cultivation materials, then at the very least, the Sect could produce one more Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator.

Whether to kill the chicken to warn the monkeys or for the sake of cultivation, Tongxuan Continent had to be taken, and anyone who dared resist had to be killed to the last! Only then could Netherworld Sect declare who ruled this Star Domain.

For this reason, a full five Third Stage Void King Realm cultivators from Netherworld Sect were stationed across Tongxuan Continent, and one of them was even Netherworld Sect's Vice Sect Master, who had chased Xue Yue all the way here that day.

This old man meditating in the great hall was Netherworld Sect's Vice Sect Master, Xin Xuanming.

He knew Tongxuan Continent had a Star Lord. He even knew that the Star Lord was a woman, and that her cultivation had also reached the Third Stage of the Void King Realm.

But he did not fear her in the slightest.

If this were a higher-level star, then so be it; dealing with it might take him some effort. But at a glance, this star was clearly low-level. As long as he ruined its World Energy, he would effectively destroy Tongxuan Continent's source at the root. When that time came, he would not need to target that Star Lord himself; she would meet no good end on her own, and without a Star Lord's protection, how could those hiding rats be anything but clay in his hands?

The Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array had been arranged precisely for this purpose. Once the great array took shape, all of Tongxuan Continent would be sealed off by the array. This Cultivation Star would no longer receive any nourishment from the essence of the Starry Sky, becoming water without a source, a tree without roots. Not only that, the Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array would also summon the Underworld Spring Source. When the time came, the power of the Yellow Springs would spread across the land, and this place would become Netherworld Sect's paradise and every other cultivator's hell.

Thinking back to a few years ago, when he had led a group of people chasing all the way here, that Star Lord had intercepted them outside the star. Laughably, Netherworld Sect's dozen or so Warships had opened fire with all their Crystal Cannons at once, and after only a dozen or so volleys, they blasted through that Star Lord's blockade and drove straight in.

Over these past few years, the cultivators of Netherworld Sect and the other Sects from Great Wilderness Star Domain had already set foot across the whole of Tongxuan Continent. That Star Lord had no strength to resist at all. She could only protect a group of people within a small area and turtle up, refusing to come out.

Since they had refused the offered toast and chosen the punishment wine instead, they could only blame their own lack of strength.

It would not be long now. In another half a year at most, the Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array would completely take shape. When that time came, that Star Lord could forget about resisting!

## Chapter 2694 — I Don’t Understand a Word You’re Saying

“If you know how formidable I am, you’d better let her go right now, or I’ll twist your head off and use it as a chamber pot!” Yang Kai suppressed the surging qi and blood within him, gritting his teeth as he shouted.

Tan Junhao shook his head slightly.

With his master shielding him, Wu Ming’s fear diminished greatly. He sneered coldly, “You little bastard, weren’t you acting wild just now? Go on, show me how wild you can be.”

As he spoke, he grabbed Chi Yue’s long hair and yanked her backward. Chi Yue cried out in surprise, her body arching, her ample chest thrusting forward.

Wu Ming grinned viciously, channeling Emperor Yuan as light flared between his fingers. He slowly reached toward Chi Yue’s chest, glaring at Yang Kai. “This is your mother-in-law, isn’t it? Let’s all take a good look at your mother-in-law’s figure, shall we?”

As soon as he finished speaking, his finger suddenly slashed downward.

With a ripping sound, Chi Yue’s clothes split open, revealing a swath of snowy skin more than three fingers wide. Whether from anger or a hint of shame, a faint flush appeared on her pale skin.

It was one thing for Wu Ming to act so shamelessly, but what truly stung was that Yang Kai was Shan Qingluo’s man, and she herself was Shan Qingluo’s sworn mother. To be humiliated like this right in front of Yang Kai, even having part of her body exposed to his gaze—though Chi Yue was a yao, even she found it hard to bear.

Yang Kai’s eyes narrowed sharply.

Wu Ming, however, brazenly enjoyed the view. Unfortunately, though her clothes were torn, they still covered the most important parts, leaving him unable to see everything.

Excited, he reached out as if to pull her clothes further aside and get a better look at her most exquisite parts.

“That’s enough!” Yang Kai shouted.

Wu Ming ignored him.

Yang Kai said angrily, “If you dare lay another finger on her, you can forget about getting anything you want today.”

Tan Junhao, who had been watching coldly, frowned and raised his hand.

Wu Ming’s movements immediately halted, a trace of regret appearing on his face.

Chi Yue couldn’t help but breathe a sigh of relief. If Wu Ming had really pulled her torn clothes aside, she’d have no face left to see Yang Kai or Shan Qingluo again. Though her current situation was still embarrassing, at least it was only a bit of exposed skin. For a yao, that wasn’t such a big deal.

“Young friend Yang, you should know my sincerity by now, yes?” Tan Junhao looked at Yang Kai with a leisurely air.

Yang Kai sneered, “To think that an esteemed elder of the Star God Palace would stoop to such despicable means—truly an eye-opener.”

Tan Junhao said, “No need for sarcasm, young friend. Those who accomplish great things don’t fuss over trifles. You should understand that.”

“All I see are two petty villains bullying a defenseless woman, shamelessly talking about not fussing over trifles. It’s laughable.”

Tan Junhao chuckled, “No matter. As long as you hand over the Dragon Isle Token, everything can be discussed.”

As he spoke, he reached a hand toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai grunted through clenched teeth, pouring all his Emperor Yuan into the Dragon Isle Token and hurling it fiercely at Tan Junhao.

At this point, he had no other options. If he didn’t compromise, Chi Yue would probably be stripped bare by Wu Ming right in front of him. How would he explain that to Shan Qingluo later?

The Dragon Isle Token was nothing he cared much about. If Hua Qingsi hadn’t reminded him earlier, he’d have nearly forgotten it existed. The only reason he’d refused to hand it over was to get Chi Yue back first.

The Dragon Isle Token turned into a streak of light, brimming with unstoppable power. Tan Junhao caught it with a single hand, examined it carefully, and nodded. “Yes, this is the token.”

Yang Kai called out, “I’ve returned the Dragon Isle Token. Now let her go!”

Tan Junhao smiled, “No rush.”

Yang Kai’s eyes widened. “You old dog, you’re going back on your word!”

Wu Ming burst out laughing. “Master never said he’d let her go just because you returned the Dragon Isle Token. He said everything could be discussed. We haven’t even discussed anything yet, so how is he going back on his word?”

Seeing Yang Kai’s frustrated expression, Wu Ming was overjoyed, as if he’d finally gotten his revenge.

Yang Kai’s face turned extremely ugly at those words. His expression shifted back and forth, and he slapped his thigh in annoyance, looking like he’d been thoroughly duped.

“Hahahahaha!” Wu Ming finally couldn’t hold back his laughter.

“What more do you want, old dog? The Dragon Isle Token is already yours. Don’t push me, or I’ll fight you to the bitter end!” Yang Kai gritted his teeth.

Tan Junhao said, “There’s just one more thing I want from you, young friend. Don’t worry—if you satisfy my wish, I’ll return this lady to you immediately.”

“What is it? Let’s hear it.” Yang Kai looked uneasy, as if a bit flustered.

Tan Junhao smiled faintly, then moved his lips and sent a message directly to Yang Kai’s mind: “A pearl. A pearl said to contain a world of its own!”

So Sister Hua really had revealed the existence of the Xuan Realm Pearl. Judging by Tan Junhao’s secretive manner, he clearly didn’t want Wu Ming to know about it.

That made sense. The Xuan Realm Pearl was an extremely precious Secret Treasure, and it even contained the Everlasting Tree, a supreme treasure of heaven and earth. Even as master and disciple, Tan Junhao had no intention of letting Wu Ming know about the Everlasting Tree, lest he become covetous.

“I don’t understand a word you’re saying!” Yang Kai replied with a straight face, utterly serious.

Tan Junhao continued sending messages: “My rebellious disciple has already told me all your secrets. There’s no need to hide anything. This whole scheme was aimed at you—the Dragon Isle Token was just a pretext. What I truly want is that pearl. If you can give it to me, I’ll be endlessly grateful. In the future, if you need anything, I’ll do my best to help. After all, I am an elder of the Star God Palace. In the Southern Territory, there’s nothing I can’t handle.”

Yang Kai shook his head like a rattle drum. “I don’t have anything like that.”

Tan Junhao’s face darkened. “So you’d refuse a toast only to drink a forfeit, is that it?”

He had always acted polite, but after Yang Kai repeatedly refused to hand over the Xuan Realm Pearl, he suddenly showed signs of turning hostile. That alone showed how much he coveted the pearl.

Off to the side, Wu Ming saw Tan Junhao and Yang Kai exchanging Divine Sense messages and grew suspicious. He couldn’t understand why his master would keep something from him, and he felt a bit resentful.

But he didn’t dare show it, so he kept it bottled up, planning to question his master after dealing with Yang Kai.

Yang Kai said helplessly, “If I don’t have what you want, how can I give it to you?”

Tan Junhao snorted and signaled Wu Ming with his eyes. “Since Young friend Yang refuses to satisfy my wish, you may do as you please with this yao woman.”

Wu Ming’s eyes lit up with joy. “Thank you, Master!”

As he spoke, he reached for Chi Yue’s chest.

“Wait, wait, wait…” Yang Kai hurriedly called out.

Tan Junhao raised his hand, knocking Wu Ming’s hand aside. Wu Ming looked frustrated but didn’t dare resist.

“Have you changed your mind, young friend?” Tan Junhao stared intently at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai scratched his cheek and sniffed. “I think I remember now. There is such a thing.”

Tan Junhao’s eyes flashed eagerly. “If you remember, then hand it over. If I can get the Xuan Realm Pearl and the Everlasting Tree inside, I won’t care about the Dragon Isle Token at all.”

It was said that refining the Everlasting Tree could grant an immortal, indestructible body, and with that, one could surely ascend to Great Emperor.

Yang Kai snorted, “But as for your trustworthiness, I have my doubts.”

Tan Junhao replied immediately, “Rest assured. If you give me that item, you can take this demon woman away at once.”

“Are you serious?” Yang Kai asked, frowning.

Tan Junhao raised his hand. “If I break my word, may the heavens strike me down.”

Yang Kai curled his lip. “If oaths worked, who would bother cultivating? Still, you are an elder of the Star God Palace, so I’ll trust you one more time!”

Tan Junhao smiled. “Young friend Yang, you’re a straightforward man. I won’t let you down.”

Yang Kai sighed, flipped his palm, and a pearl the size of a dragon’s eye appeared in his hand. At first glance, it looked ordinary, but it radiated a strange spatial power.

Tan Junhao’s eyes widened as he stared unblinkingly at the Xuan Realm Pearl, sweeping it with his Divine Sense to examine it closely.

With his cultivation and experience, he could instantly tell how extraordinary the pearl was. He knew at once that this must be the Xuan Realm Pearl. Even as a spatial Secret Treasure, it was already remarkable, but what mattered most was what was inside.

Not everyone could refine a spatial Secret Treasure, but Tan Junhao was confident that with his strength, he could open it just enough to retrieve the Everlasting Tree inside.

Wu Ming was watching too, but he frowned, unable to understand why such a pearl was so important to his master—more important even than the Dragon Isle Token.

He vaguely sensed that his master’s true goal this time wasn’t the Dragon Isle Token, but this strange pearl.

“I got this thing a long time ago, as a gift from a friend. Now I’m handing it over to Elder Tan, and I hope you’ll treat it with care.” Yang Kai looked at the Xuan Realm Pearl in his hand with reluctant resignation, even changing how he addressed Tan Junhao.

Tan Junhao said, “Don’t worry. I’ll treasure such a precious item. From now on, you are my friend.”

Yang Kai closed his hand and said, “Before that, I have one small request.”

Tan Junhao frowned. “What is it?”

Yang Kai said, “I hope Hua Qingsi can leave Elder Tan’s tutelage and become a free person.”

Wu Ming snorted, “Wishful thinking. That wretch betrayed Master. Not killing her is already mercy, and you want her to be free?”

Yang Kai looked at him coldly. “Can you make decisions for Elder Tan?”

Wu Ming’s face changed. He quickly cupped his fists to Tan Junhao. “Master, that’s not what I meant.”

Tan Junhao waved his hand. “I understand.”

He was relieved. He wasn’t afraid of Yang Kai making demands. After several encounters, he could tell Yang Kai was a man of deep feelings and loyalty. If not, he wouldn’t have been able to force Yang Kai to surrender the Dragon Isle Token and Xuan Realm Pearl just by threatening Chi Yue.

In Tan Junhao’s view, this request was reasonable. Given Yang Kai’s character, it made sense he’d ask for this.

He nodded. “Very well. From this day forward, Hua Qingsi is no longer my disciple and has nothing more to do with me.”

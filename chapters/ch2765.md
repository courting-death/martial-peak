## Chapter 2765 — Dare Not Ask Again

Yang Kai stood rigid, not daring to move. A bead of cold sweat slid down his forehead as he replied solemnly, "Disciple means... I accept."

"A teachable child!" Gao Xueting's lips curled as she sheathed her longsword.

Only then did Deputy Hall Master Qiu Ran and the many elders step forward, smiling as they cupped their fists and addressed Yang Kai as Elder.

Wen Zishan beamed, "Wonderful, wonderful! Our Shrine welcomes a new Elder—such a joyous occasion, truly worth celebrating. As Hall Master, I've decided: in five days, we'll hold a grand ceremony and announce it to the world."

Yang Kai's spirits lifted and he hurriedly said, "Hall Master, that really isn't necessary. Disciple now..."

Before he could finish, Wen Zishan patted his shoulder with a smile, "No need to say more, I understand."

Yang Kai opened his mouth, but all his words turned into a sigh. He had killed Tan Junhao and Wu Ming—though there were reasons, he had still clashed with a giant like Star God Palace. Once he became an elder of Qingyang Shrine, he would surely bring trouble to the Shrine.

If this matter were kept secret, it would be fine, but Wen Zishan insisted on holding a grand ceremony and announcing it to the world. Wasn't this just inviting disaster?

Yang Kai felt moved, but also uneasy. If he truly brought calamity to the Shrine, it would be terrible. Yet Wen Zishan, fully aware, still insisted—clearly intending to use the Shrine's power to protect him.

Gao Xueting spoke from the side, "Among Shrine disciples, once one advances to the Emperor Realm, they can become a sect elder and enjoy elder privileges. Xia Sheng did so, so you needn't feel burdened."

Yang Kai scratched his head, "There's something I should let you all know. Not long ago, I founded a sect..."

"Oh?" Wen Zishan and Gao Xueting exchanged glances, surprised. The elders were also utterly astonished.

“Your disciple is now a Palace Master. If I were to become an Elder of the Shrine as well, I fear there may be some conflict.”

Wen Zishan nodded, while Gao Xueting looked skeptical, "Are you trying to fool us? Want to shirk the Elder position?"

Yang Kai quickly replied, "I wouldn't dare. It's really true."

Gao Xueting frowned, "Though you are an Emperor and qualified to found a sect, you are still young. Getting involved in these matters too early won't benefit your future. You should focus on improving your cultivation."

"Thank you for your concern, Elder Gao. I know, but I have reasons for founding a sect." Yang Kai nodded.

Gao Xueting sighed, "Since you've already founded a sect, you can't just disband it. How about this: your sect will form an alliance with the Shrine. If you need help, the Shrine will assist—even send people to help you, so you needn't worry about mundane affairs."

Yang Kai smiled, "There are people managing the sect's affairs—no need to worry about it."

"That's best. You're a smart man—you know your own strength is the foundation of everything." Gao Xueting pondered, "But since you're a Palace Master, becoming a Shrine elder is indeed a bit inappropriate. How about you become a Guest Elder? Hall Master, what do you think?"

Wen Zishan chuckled, "Whatever Little Xueting says, so it shall be."

Guest Elders were also elders, but much freer—coming and going as they please. It's similar to Yang Kai's previous Registered Disciple status, only far more prestigious.

"By the way, what's your sect called, and where is it located?" Gao Xueting asked.

Qiu Ran, Chen Qian, and the others all looked at Yang Kai, curiosity in their eyes.

"Lingxiao Palace, in the Northern Territory," Yang Kai grinned.

"The Northern Territory is so far away..." Gao Xueting frowned. Qingyang Shrine was in the Southern Territory, while Lingxiao Palace was in the Northern Territory—separated by countless li. Even if they formed an alliance, it would hardly matter; if Lingxiao Palace ever faced trouble, Qingyang Shrine would be too distant to offer timely help.

"How strong is your sect? How many disciples?"

Yang Kai looked at Gao Xueting, cautiously asking, "Does Elder Gao want the truth... or a lie?"

Gao Xueting's beautiful eyes flashed with a sharp light. "Just try telling a single lie!" she warned.

Yang Kai's mouth twitched, "Not many disciples—just over a hundred."

"Over a hundred..." Gao Xueting nodded, not looking down on him. Yang Kai had only recently advanced to Emperor, so his sect must be small. Recruiting over a hundred disciples was already impressive. A sect grows like a snowball—slowly getting bigger.

"As for strength... it's a mixed bag. In the Emperor Realm—besides myself, there are six others."

"What?" Several elders were shocked.

"Six others? So there are seven Emperors?"

"Are you sure your sect only has a hundred or so people?"

The Shrine elders couldn't stay calm, even Wen Zishan's eyes widened.

A sect of just over a hundred, with seven Emperors? What kind of sect was this? That ratio was terrifying! Qingyang Shrine had tens of thousands of disciples, but only a dozen Emperors.

Gao Xueting was even more astonished. She had thought Yang Kai's sect was small, but now it seemed extraordinary.

"Those six—what are their cultivations?"

Yang Kai scratched his face, "One is a First Stage Emperor Realm Chief Steward, handling sect affairs for me. Another is a First Stage Emperor Imperial-Grade Formation Master, and another is a Second Stage Emperor Imperial-Grade Pill Master..."

Hiss...

A chorus of gasps sounded. On Ten Thousand Saints Peak, everyone looked at Yang Kai as if he'd grown horns.

A sect of just over a hundred, with seven Emperors was shocking enough, but among them were an Imperial-Grade Formation Master and an Imperial-Grade Pill Master...

How could this be? Even Qingyang Shrine didn't have its own Imperial-Grade Formation Master or Pill Master. Such a foundation was only found in Great Emperor sects.

"And the other three?" Gao Xueting pressed, her eyes full of shock.

Yang Kai tilted his head, embarrassed, "Those three... are Yao Kings."

Everyone was stunned.

Yao Kings... and three of them!

Only yao with strength equal to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm could be called Yao Kings. In other words, any Yao King was no weaker than Qingyang Shrine's Hall Master Wen Zishan.

The Shrine was a top sect in the Southern Territory because Wen Zishan sat in charge. Wuhua Hall and Tianwu Sacred Land were the same, each with a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert.

But in Yang Kai's newly founded Lingxiao Palace, there were three such powerful beings.

Such a foundation—except for lacking a Great Emperor and having fewer Emperors—what did it lack compared to Great Emperor sects? If it grows, it might overshadow all forces in the Star Realm except Great Emperor sects.

Shocked, Gao Xueting suddenly grew serious, "Those not of our race have different hearts. Yang Kai, how can you control those three Yao Kings? Why would they obey you?"

Wen Zishan nodded gravely, "Indeed, you must be careful about this."

Yang Kai nodded, "Disciple understands."

"You're not reckless. Since you know, we won't say more." Wen Zishan nodded, then grinned, "But it seems your Lingxiao Palace really is qualified to ally with the Shrine. It's just that the distance is a pity. By the way, since your Palace has such a foundation, where did you choose for its base? A sect's growth is closely tied to its location—don't be careless."

Yang Kai chuckled, "I just seized someone else's territory, so I didn’t trouble myself too much over that matter."

"Oh?" Wen Zishan raised his brows, "Whose sect did you take? I don't know much about Northern Territory sects, but I've heard of a few."

"Wenqing Sect!"

Wen Zishan's expression became wonderfully complex, staring at Yang Kai in a daze. The elders' mouths dropped open.

Wenqing Sect—they all knew it. One of the top sects in the Northern Territory, its status rivaled Qingyang Shrine in the Southern Territory.

Yang Kai actually seized Wenqing Sect's territory and founded his own sect there. In other words, Wenqing Sect must have been destroyed.

Thinking further, with Lingxiao Palace's powerful foundation, destroying Wenqing Sect wasn't impossible.

"Indeed, Wenqing Sect's base must be excellent..." Wen Zishan wiped cold sweat, realizing he'd underestimated Yang Kai. He used to think Yang Kai had great potential, so he tried hard to recruit him to Qingyang Shrine, finally making him a Registered Disciple.

But after just a few years, this Registered Disciple had accomplished such earth-shaking feats. If the two territories weren't so far apart, this news would have already shaken the entire Star Domain.

"Enough idle talk. The grand ceremony will be held in five days—there's much to prepare," Gao Xueting changed the subject.

She dared not ask any further, fearing that if she pressed on, she would only hear even more astonishing revelations.

Just then, Gao Xueting frowned, took out a Transmission Compass, checked it, and said, "Hall Master, Xiao Yuyang has arrived."

Wen Zishan's eyes narrowed, "He came quickly!"

Yang Kai said, "I ran into him on the road, but shook him off. He must be chasing after me."

Wen Zishan nodded, "I see. Little Xueting, take him aside."

"Mm." Gao Xueting responded, then said to Yang Kai, "Come with me."

Yang Kai cupped his fists to the elders, then sped away with Gao Xueting.

Though he wasn't afraid of Xiao Yuyang, meeting him now would bring nothing good. Better to avoid him, though it dragged Qingyang Shrine into the mess, making Yang Kai feel a bit guilty.

Soon, Gao Xueting led Yang Kai to her residence on Purple Bamboo Peak. On the peak, swathes of purple bamboo formed shady groves, swaying in the wind.

Atop the vast Purple Bamboo Peak stood a solitary building, lending the place a quiet, almost desolate air—no doubt a reflection of Gao Xueting’s reserved nature.

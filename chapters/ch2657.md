## Chapter 2657 — Treasure Vault

The Wenqing Sect's foundation spanned a vast territory. Yang Kai, accompanied by Ji Yao and the others, wandered here for two full days before they finally managed to traverse most of this enormous estate.

The more he observed, the more satisfied Yang Kai became with this place.

Previously, he had said that this might be the place in the Northern Territory with the densest Spiritual Qi, and that was only because of the Great Emperor Wenqing's illustrious reputation. But now, it seemed his words might have hit the mark.

The cultivation environment here was far superior even to that of the Qingyang Shrine.

The Qingyang Shrine was also a top Sect, and in terms of status and strength, it was on par with the Wenqing Sect. Yet, when it came to the environment, it still fell a bit short.

Perhaps only those Sects founded by Great Emperors could truly compare with the Wenqing Sect.

With such a foundation, so many ready-made buildings, and the Wenqing Sect's heritage accumulated over tens of thousands of years, Yang Kai became even more reluctant to hand it over to others, secretly making plans in his heart.

Although they had passed through many places, they hadn't found anything of real value.

In the end, it was under Ji Yao's guidance that the group arrived atop a towering mountain peak.

Ji Yao had visited the Wenqing Sect several times in her early years with Bing Yun, so she knew this mountain was the most secretive place in the Wenqing Sect. If there was a vault storing the Sect's wealth, it would surely be hidden atop this peak.

When Yang Kai and the others arrived, they saw a layer of light enveloping the mountain, appearing extremely sturdy—clearly the result of an activated barrier formation.

This made Yang Kai's eyes light up with delight rather than surprise.

The barrier was completely intact, which meant that since it was activated, no one had broken through it. If there really were treasures inside, they would definitely still be there, not taken away by the fleeing Wenqing Sect disciples.

Stopping the Yao Kings from trying to break the formation by force, Yang Kai rummaged through his Spatial Ring, pulling out several extraordinary-looking tokens and began infusing them one by one with Emperor Yuan to test them.

In just a short while, one of the tokens suddenly emitted a beam of light that shot into the barrier, which instantly split open.

All these tokens had been found by Yang Kai in the Spatial Rings of the Wenqing Sect's high-ranking cultivators after their deaths. Especially the one that opened the barrier—it should be the Sect Master Feng Xuan's command token.

"Let's go in and see what treasures are inside," Yang Kai said, his spirits high as he took the lead inside.

Atop the mountain stood a grand hall, built with imposing grandeur and extraordinary style.

Entering the hall, they saw that the furnishings and decorations exuded an ancient simplicity. It seemed that even the furniture had many years behind it, with the marks of time clearly visible.

Sweeping his Divine Sense around, Yang Kai found nothing unusual. After a moment's thought, he said, "Let's split up and search. If you find anything suspicious, call out."

"Yes!" The three Yao Kings immediately scattered to search.

Ji Yao did not leave, instead following Yang Kai as she wandered about.

"Do you really intend to establish a Sect here?"

After a while, Ji Yao suddenly asked.

The three Yao Kings were all straightforward and bold, so they hadn't noticed the look of interest on Yang Kai's face these past few days. But Ji Yao had been quietly observing and realized that Yang Kai was truly considering founding a Sect here.

"I have a bit of an idea," Yang Kai replied, glancing back at Ji Yao with a grin. "Why?"

Ji Yao gently shook her head. "It's nothing. If you really want to establish a Sect here, it's actually a good choice."

"You think so too?"

Ji Yao said, "The Wenqing Sect and my Ice Heart Valley are both in the Northern Territory. If you can take root here, we can support each other, and you certainly have the qualifications now."

Although Yang Kai was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, Ji Yao knew that his strength was far beyond the norm. Even if she fought him one-on-one, she would not be his match. In this world, only those at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm could truly contend with him.

If even he wasn't qualified to found a Sect, then most Sects in this world had no right to exist.

Yang Kai smiled and said, "That's what I think too. I just don't have a complete plan yet, and if I really go through with it, there will surely be many troubles."

Ji Yao smiled faintly and said, "That's nothing. When the time comes, just talk to Master. She'll help you, and Ice Heart Valley will lend you a hand as well."

Yang Kai nodded. "There's no rush. It's just a passing thought for now. If that day really comes, I'll definitely invite Senior Bing Yun to give guidance."

Ji Yao nodded, a trace of anticipation rising in her heart.

Yang Kai was closely connected to Ice Heart Valley, and even more so to her Master, Bing Yun. If he truly took root on the Wenqing Sect's ancestral grounds, then Ice Heart Valley and he could support each other, and with two great powers here, who would dare provoke them? The disaster that befell Ice Heart Valley this time would never happen again.

The main reason Ice Heart Valley suffered this calamity was that they had always been too closed off, with too little contact with the outside world and too shallow ties to other Sects and powers.

"But you'll have to think carefully about the name of the Sect. You can't keep using the Wenqing Sect's name," Ji Yao reminded.

Yang Kai smiled and nodded. "I have my own plans."

He had already thought of a name for the Sect.

Just then, the cry of an eagle suddenly came from one direction.

Yang Kai turned to look that way and said, "Yingfei seems to have found something. Let's go take a look."

Ji Yao nodded and followed.

In no time, the two arrived before a sheer cliff atop the mountain. The cliff looked unremarkable, but Yingfei was examining it with great interest, occasionally patting and feeling around.

Seeing Yang Kai arrive, Yingfei quickly said, "Young Master Yang, there's something strange here."

Yang Kai swept his gaze over the cliff and released his Divine Sense to investigate, nodding. "There really is something odd."

At first glance, the cliff seemed ordinary, but in fact, it was covered by a highly sophisticated barrier, making it nearly impossible to notice without careful inspection.

If Yang Kai had come alone, he might have overlooked it, but Yingfei's true form was a giant eagle, renowned for speed and keen eyesight, so he immediately spotted something amiss.

"I wonder if those tokens can open this barrier," Yingfei said, a bit anxiously.

"We'll know if we try," Yang Kai replied with a smile, once again taking out the tokens he had collected from the Wenqing Sect's high-ranking members and testing them one by one.

While he was busy, Xi Lei and Xie Wuwei also arrived after hearing the commotion. After learning the situation, they too became excited, secretly thinking this must be the Wenqing Sect's vault or treasure trove.

A top Sect with a heritage spanning tens of thousands of years—its stored wealth was naturally enough to make anyone's heart race.

As it turned out, the tokens Yang Kai had collected were indeed useful. Who knew which one did the trick, but just like when they opened the mountain's barrier, a mysterious light flashed, and the cliff before them rumbled open, revealing a pitch-black passage leading into the mountain.

Immediately after, a point of light suddenly appeared in the corridor, like a spark that quickly spread, illuminating the entire passage.

"Let's go!" Yang Kai called out, cautiously scanning his surroundings as he led the way inside.

The others naturally hurried to follow.

Once inside, the cliff closed up again, but with the lights all around, the passage remained brightly lit.

They continued downward for who knew how long before the path suddenly opened up, and a massive underground stone cavern appeared before their eyes.

Upon seeing the cavern's contents, Xie Wuwei gasped and exclaimed in excitement, "It really is a treasure vault!"

Not only him, but the other two Yao Kings and even Yang Kai were a bit unsettled. Only Ji Yao remained calm, treating wealth as dirt. After all, she cultivated ice techniques—her heart was as clear as ice, unshaken by anything.

Looking around the cavern, what blocked their way were piles upon piles of Source Crystals, stacked like small mountains. Lower-Level, Middle-Level, Upper-Level Source Crystals—everything was here, in uncountable quantities.

Yang Kai’s eyes were dazzled by the brilliance of the Source Crystals, their light reflecting in every direction and nearly blinding him at first glance.

Although he had previously extorted a huge sum in the Wild Ancient Land, with his haul of Source Crystals counted in the hundreds of millions, the amount before him was by no means small—perhaps even more than what he had gained in the Wild Ancient Land.

After all, this was the Wenqing Sect's accumulation over tens of thousands of years, far beyond what ordinary Sects could compare to.

Since Yang Kai was now planning to establish a Sect here, the more resources he had, the better. When the day came that he could bring his friends and family from Dark Star, he would have enough cultivation resources for them.

Beyond the Source Crystals were rows of wooden shelves, densely packed with countless boxes—wooden, jade, gold, and more.

Yang Kai led the group to the shelves and casually checked a few boxes, finding that they contained exactly what he expected: rare medicinal herbs, each one precious and aged.

The Yao Kings weren't interested in the herbs, since the Wild Ancient Land was rich in Spiritual Herbs and Medicines. If there was anywhere in the world to find the best spiritual medicines, the Wild Ancient Land was certainly one of them.

So they only glanced at the herbs and quickly lost interest.

On the other side were more shelves, but these held jade bottles instead of boxes.

After checking, Yang Kai found that the bottles contained refined Spiritual Pills of various grades, all essential for cultivators. There were tens of thousands of bottles in total.

Further in were all kinds of secret manuals—the Wenqing Sect's many esoteric inheritances.

These made Yang Kai's eyes light up, for they were the treasures the Wenqing Sect had preserved over tens of thousands of years. Once he established his Sect, his disciples would be able to use them.

Deeper in the cavern were large numbers of Secret Treasures, all at least Dao Origin-Grade, and even more than a hundred dazzling Imperial Treasures.

With so many Secret Treasures gathered in one place, the energy fluctuations they emitted were truly astonishing.

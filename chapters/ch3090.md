## Chapter 3090 — A Pawn to Send Ahead

"If Sir doubts my loyalty, I can offer up my Soul Brand. From now on, I will be your slave and servant without a single complaint."

Yang Kai chuckled, not in mockery, but in admiration of this woman's courage and spirit. As the First Chief who commanded Gale, she did indeed have some ability. If she were merely a pretty vase, others would probably have devoured her until not even bones remained.

Since she was not a vase, perhaps there were places where she could prove useful. He was completely unfamiliar with Wuji Star Domain, and having a local guide should spare him a great deal of trouble. Furthermore, he had descended upon this Lower Plane Star Domain with overwhelming cultivation. Though he had the ability to overturn Heaven and Earth, he did not dare display it at will, lest he again suffer the Heavenly Way's rejection. Having a pawn to send ahead... now that he thought about it, that was not a bad thing.

Especially when this pawn was so pleasing to the eye. It was much better than having some grown man following at his side.

Hearing him laugh, He Yunxiang lifted her head to look up at him, her delicate and alluring face full of expectation.

"There is no need to be a slave or servant, and no need for a Soul Brand either."

"Sir..." He Yunxiang grew anxious. She had already gone this far, yet still could not move him? Could it be that she really had to take the initiative, loosen her clothes and belt, and serve him once? Although she was mentally prepared to give herself to him, she still could not bring herself to do such a thing.

"Since you want to follow me, you need to have some value I can use. And if you want to work for me, your cultivation is still a little low."

He Yunxiang was startled. Then a joyful light burst from her beautiful eyes. "Sir, you... you mean..."

Had he agreed? This should mean he had agreed, right? Never before had she been so anxious over gains and losses. It was as if a beautiful scroll were slowly unfurling before her, revealing the scenery of a new world.

"Then let us first raise your cultivation."

He Yunxiang said excitedly, "Yes, I will go cultivate at once. I certainly will not disappoint Sir."

Though she said this, with her strength and cultivation at this level, how difficult was it to advance another step? She had already been stuck at the Second Stage of the Void King Realm for several hundred years. If there had been a chance to break through, she would have broken through long ago. How could ordinary meditation and cultivation accomplish it? But since Yang Kai said her cultivation was insufficient, she naturally had to hurry off and cultivate. Whether or not she could break through, she had to show the proper attitude.

"Open your mouth!" Yang Kai barked.

He Yunxiang froze for a moment, but immediately opened her small mouth. Before she could react, she felt something enter her mouth and slide straight down her throat into her lower abdomen.

She did not even taste it.

In the next instant, He Yunxiang's expression changed drastically. A scorching sensation suddenly came from her lower abdomen, as if flames were burning there, threatening to incinerate her from the inside out.

What was this? A Pill?

What kind of Pill could have such terrifying medicinal efficacy?

She knew that Yang Kai must have given her some extraordinary Spiritual Pill or miraculous medicine. At once, she could no longer concern herself with shock or horror. She quickly shifted posture, sat cross-legged on the ground, closed her eyes, formed several hand seals, and circulated the mysterious art she cultivated.

Yang Kai sat down off to the side, ignoring her, and lifted the spirit tea on the table to take a sip.

Fierce winds surged through the wing room. All kinds of ornaments rattled noisily as they were blown about, and the water in the bathing pool rose in layer after layer of waves, spilling over the edge.

Streams of uncontrolled force rampaged through the wing room, reducing the entire room to chaos, as if peerless experts had fought a life-and-death battle here. Yang Kai sat upright without moving and made no effort to resist those forces, allowing them to lash against his body.

He Yunxiang's face flushed crimson. One strained yet soul-stirring moan after another escaped her throat. Her whole body was soaked in sweat, her clothes clinging to her skin and outlining her enticing figure.

Her Second Stage Void King Realm aura climbed higher and higher, and very soon reached its peak.

It did not take long, merely the time it took to drink a cup of tea.

The aura that had been holding at a certain level suddenly surged, climbing straight to new heights. At the same time, an invisible field erupted outward from He Yunxiang, stirring the already messy wing room into even greater disorder.

Fortunately, this wing room was Lu Huaishuang's boudoir and was protected by all sorts of powerful arrays, so the commotion here did not spread out. Otherwise, it would certainly have drawn the attention of the Lu Family.

A long moan rang out. He Yunxiang's entire face was flushed as if painted with rouge, her delicate body pulled taut, and the insides of her thighs trembled without stop. Through the narrow slits of her half-open eyes shone a hazy radiance, like the lingering aftertaste of having climbed into the clouds.

Her body remained stiff for a long, long time before she suddenly drew a sharp breath. Her ample chest heaved violently, tracing a breathtaking rise and fall.

Yang Kai broke out in a cold sweat. What should have been a perfectly normal breakthrough had somehow been turned by her into something with an unmistakably sensual undertone. He simply did not know what to say.

Drenched in sweat, He Yunxiang finally recovered, and inexplicably felt that her body had become even lighter, as if she had thrown off some burden. Her thoughts were clear, her body light, and her vision bright.

Third Stage of the Void King Realm!

She had actually reached the Third Stage of the Void King Realm just like that!

She almost thought she was dreaming, but when she turned her head and met a pair of teasing eyes, she finally understood that this had truly happened.

She was at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm now, and she too stood at the very top of the Star Domain's food chain! The bottleneck that had blocked her for several hundred years had been blasted open before she even knew what was happening.

Just what kind of Spiritual Pill was that? It actually had such a miraculous effect.

When she looked at Yang Kai, there was even more gratitude and respect in her gaze. Leaving aside whether she might encounter danger by following him in the future, or whether her delicate body might perish like shattered jade, this present reward alone proved that her earlier choice had not been wrong.

If she had not insisted on following him, how could such a good thing have fallen to her? If she truly had to cultivate by herself, who knew whether she would have any hope of advancing to the Third Stage of the Void King Realm in this lifetime?

Since the man before her could use a single Spiritual Pill to push her cultivation up to the Third Stage, it might not be impossible for him to bring her to even greater heights!

She rose gracefully and was just about to offer thanks when a fishy stench suddenly rushed into her nose. He Yunxiang frowned and examined herself, immediately becoming unbearably embarrassed.

Her body's surface was sticky all over, and her exposed arms were covered in an extra layer of black mud-like substance. She looked as if she had rolled through a mud pit and then run back out.

After the embarrassment came shock.

She naturally knew what this situation meant, because she had encountered it before and had also seen many people encounter it before.

This was something one encountered only soon after beginning cultivation, when one's strength was still low.

Washing the marrow and cutting the meridians, shedding the mortal body and exchanging the bones!

The black mud covering her body's surface was clearly impurities expelled from within her body!

She, a Second Stage of the Void King Realm cultivator advancing to the Third Stage, could actually wash the marrow and cut the meridians, shed the mortal body and exchange the bones? This surprise was far greater than the joy brought by her advancement. Advancement was merely an increase in strength and realm, but this washing of the marrow and cutting of the meridians had improved her cultivation aptitude from the roots.

She realized that she had greatly underestimated the efficacy of that unknown Spiritual Pill.

No wonder she felt so light now, as if her constitution had become purer. How could she not feel light?

Pleasant surprise filled her heart to the brim, and she felt there was no one in the world luckier than herself.

"Sir, excuse my discourtesy." After saying this, He Yunxiang swayed and fell toward the bathing pool to the side. While she was still in midair, her clothes were already swiftly removed, revealing her shapely figure. She dropped into the water as lightly as a feather, without splashing up the slightest wave.

She submerged herself in the water for a moment, then suddenly shot out. The filth on her body had already vanished, and her skin was fairer than frost and snow, tender enough to break with a breath.

Crystal-clear water streamed down her cheeks, as though flowing over the finest porcelain in the world. It slipped across her full, uplifted chest, slowed briefly, then accelerated again as it flowed down over her smooth, flat abdomen.

He Yunxiang raised a hand to wipe the water from her face, then climbed to the edge of the pool, crossed her hands beneath her chin, and looked up at Yang Kai.

She did not cover herself in the slightest. Although she was somewhat embarrassed, she remained poised and graceful.

Since she had already thrown in her lot with this Sir, she believed she would be eaten by him sooner or later. What harm was there in letting him take a look?

Moreover, after what had just happened, she instead looked forward to being eaten sooner. If she could become his woman, could he still fail to treat her attentively? She had things she wanted, but she had absolutely no malice or scheme.

Yang Kai did not avert his gaze either. Since a bargain had delivered itself to his door, why should he refuse it? As a grown man, could he really be afraid of her?

He paced over to the poolside, sat down, and said, "Recite the cultivation method you practice for me."

He Yunxiang's expression stirred, and she vaguely realized something. Suppressing the excitement in her heart, she explained the essentials and methods of her cultivation method in great detail.

The cultivation method she practiced was quite good even across the entire Wuji Star Domain. Even if it was not the very top, it was still first-class. Others might covet her cultivation method, but how could the person before her do so?

She spoke in detail, and Yang Kai listened seriously, occasionally interrupting to ask a few questions.

Afterward, Yang Kai had her take out her Secret Treasure for him to look over, and soon had a plan in mind.

Half an hour later, He Yunxiang was properly dressed and once again stood before Yang Kai.

After cleansing her marrow and refining her meridians, her entire person seemed far more radiant than before. Standing there, she was an exceedingly alluring sight. Inevitably, a faint disappointment rose in her heart. Even that charming atmosphere from before had failed to stir this man…

But the more he proved to be such a man, the more worthy he was of entrusting herself to, was he not? She felt more and more that she had made the right gamble this time.

"For people of our generation, pursuing the Martial Dao tirelessly, clashes are unavoidable. When facing an enemy, the level of cultivation can basically determine victory or defeat, but Secret Treasures and cultivation methods are also indispensable. Your cultivation method is not bad, but it is still somewhat low-level. I have a cultivation method here that should suit you, so take it and study it." Yang Kai flipped his hand and took out a jade slip, handing it to her.

He Yunxiang suppressed the excitement in her heart, smoothed her robes, and bowed. "Thank you, Sir, for the reward."

"I will give you this Secret Treasure as well. Refine it carefully. On Taiyi Star, there may be places where I will need you to lend a hand."

Another Secret Treasure shaped like a colored ribbon was handed to He Yunxiang.

The ribbon was seven-colored, flowing with light and overflowing with brilliance, gorgeous beyond compare. He Yunxiang liked it at first sight. She had always pretended to be a man before, so the Secret Treasures she used had all been masculine in style, and she had never used a feminine Secret Treasure. This ribbon undoubtedly satisfied an expectation she had held for a very long time.

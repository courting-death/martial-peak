## Chapter 2974 — The Dragon Clan's Disgrace

After asking, Yang Kai suddenly recalled that Zhu Qing had revealed her true form in the palace of Human Sovereign City. She had been an enormous Red Dragon thirty zhang long, while the form Zhu Lie had revealed at Qingyang Shrine seemed somewhat smaller, measuring only a little over twenty zhang.

His situation was much the same. For the Dragon Clan, the purity of one’s bloodline could be judged simply by the size of one’s true form.

From this, it seemed that Zhu Qing's bloodline truly was stronger than Zhu Lie's. Zhu Lie had not been speaking carelessly when he said she possessed a Ninth-Tier body.

However, there was one thing Yang Kai found strange. "Ninth-Tier is Ninth-Tier. That's a good thing, isn't it? Hasn't your Dragon Clan always pursued greater bloodline purity? Qing'er, why are you blushing?"

"Impossible, impossible! How could this be?" Zhu Lie muttered, his brow tightly furrowed. No matter how he thought about it, he could not understand how Zhu Qing had advanced to the Ninth-Tier in such a short time. Feeling aggrieved, he lamented the unfairness of the Heavenly Way. Why had such a wonderful opportunity not fallen to him?

He suddenly turned his head and stared coldly at Yang Kai. "What did you just call her?"

He had been so shocked by the improvement in Zhu Qing's bloodline that he had not paid much attention to how Yang Kai addressed his Elder Sister. Now that he had recovered from his shock, he realized something was amiss. How could such an intimate form of address come from a human? This was nothing less than blasphemy against the Dragon Clan, an insult! Yet Zhu Qing had not refuted him.

He suddenly realized something, and his tone grew even colder as murderous intent swirled around him. "And what did you just call me?"

Yang Kai stepped forward to stand beside Zhu Qing. Then, under Zhu Lie's astonished gaze, he reached out and wrapped an arm around Zhu Qing's delicate shoulder, grinning at him. "You're Qing'er's younger brother, so of course I'll call you Little Brother-in-Law."

Zhu Qing twisted her body but failed to escape Yang Kai's clutches, so she could only let him hold her like this.

Zhu Lie's pupils abruptly contracted. He was no fool. Seeing that Zhu Qing did not reject such an intimate gesture from Yang Kai and instead seemed half willing despite her show of resistance, coupled with how Yang Kai addressed the two siblings, an unimaginable thought finally surfaced in his mind.

His face instantly turned cold, as though covered in a layer of frost.

Nearby, Li Jiao's mouth hung wide enough to fit an egg. His heart surged as he gazed at Yang Kai, feeling as though he were looking up at an insurmountable mountain, and a profound sense of admiration welled up within him. Had this man actually ridden a Dragon? He was not dreaming, was he? If that were true, would that not make Palace Master Yang the son-in-law of Dragon Isle? How incredible was that?

His admiration for Yang Kai was complete and utter. He had still harbored some resentment earlier, but now he could no longer muster any. Compared to his own affairs, Yang Kai had even dared commit the great taboo of Dragon Isle by riding a Dragon. What in this world would he not dare to do?

"You two... aren't joking, are you?" Zhu Lie's eyes burned like flames as he looked from Zhu Qing to Yang Kai and questioned them in a deep voice.

Yang Kai frowned. Sensing Zhu Lie's murderous intent, he said unhappily, "Do I look like I'm joking with you?"

"I want to hear your answer!" Zhu Lie ignored him and stared only at Zhu Qing.

"Is that any way to speak to your Elder Sister?" Yang Kai's face sank, and he said fiercely, "Don't think I won't beat you just because you're my Little Brother-in-Law. If you keep showing no respect for your elders, I'll beat you until even your Elder Sister won't recognize you."

Zhu Lie stiffened his neck, murderous intent erupting as he glared at Yang Kai and growled, "I couldn't ask for more!"

“Oh? Give you a little color and you dare open a dyehouse? Today, as your brother-in-law, I’ll teach you how to conduct yourself!” Yang Kai rolled up his sleeves and was about to step forward when Zhu Qing reached out and pulled him back.

"Why are you stopping me?" Yang Kai looked displeased. "This brother of yours is completely lawless and needs a good lesson. Don't worry, I won't kill him. I'll just break a few bones so he'll remember how he's supposed to speak to you."

Zhu Qing gently shook her head, looked at Zhu Lie, and said, "You've seen everything and heard everything. I have nothing to say."

Zhu Lie shuddered and stumbled back several steps. Although Yang Kai's words and actions had provided ample clues and he had already formed a suspicion, he still found it difficult to accept when Zhu Qing confirmed it.

A dignified member of the Dragon Clan, a Ninth-Tier Red Dragon, the leader of all living creatures, had actually given herself to a human!

This was nothing short of a disgrace, a disgrace to Dragon Isle! A disgrace to the entire Dragon Clan!

Zhu Lie slowly shook his head, his expression dark with dismay. “Zhu Qing, you’ve truly disappointed me. How could you be so foolish?”

Yang Kai coldly snorted. "You make it sound as though this Young Master is somehow unworthy. Zhu Lie, I find you more unpleasant by the moment."

"The feeling is mutual!" Zhu Lie shot Yang Kai a cold glance. Their gazes collided, sparks flying for quite some time, as though they might come to blows at any moment.

Zhu Qing, however, smiled faintly. "I don't think I've been foolish at all. On the contrary, I believe this is the greatest happiness of my life."

The moment she said this, Yang Kai's displeasure with Zhu Lie vanished like smoke. An indescribable sense of satisfaction filled his entire being, and he began chuckling foolishly.

Zhu Lie said with bitter anguish, "Women really are fools, no matter how powerful they may be. You should know what consequences this will bring."

Zhu Qing's face paled slightly. She pursed her red lips and nodded.

"Then why did you still do it!?" Zhu Lie roared hysterically, looking utterly exasperated by her failure to live up to his expectations.

Zhu Qing did not answer. Lowering her head, she said, "Even if I were given another chance to choose, I would still make the same choice."

"Madness. This is truly madness." Zhu Lie repeatedly shook his head as he looked at Zhu Qing and then Yang Kai. His expression shifted constantly, becoming extraordinarily complicated.

Only after a long while did he softly say, "It was because of him that you advanced to become a Ninth-Tier Red Dragon, wasn't it? So that's how it is. I thought this was your own opportunity, but I never expected... Hah."

Zhu Qing's face reddened slightly, clearly confirming Zhu Lie's guess through her silence.

Yang Kai stared at Zhu Qing in surprise. "Your advancement to the Ninth-Tier had something to do with me? How could it have anything to do with me?" He had no idea when Zhu Qing had advanced to the Ninth-Tier.

"Don't ask." Zhu Qing looked thoroughly bashful.

Seeing her like this, Yang Kai suddenly recalled the two wild days they had spent in the Small Xuan Realm. At the time, he had vaguely sensed some change in Zhu Qing's aura, but he had not investigated it closely. Thinking back now, her bloodline had probably improved at that time.

If that was the case...

Yang Kai switched to Divine Sense transmission and quietly asked, "Qing'er, could it be that my Primordial Yang Essence also had some... you know..."

Zhu Qing reached out and pinched his arm, replying through Divine Sense in embarrassment and annoyance, "What Primordial Yang Essence could you possibly have?"

Yang Kai chuckled awkwardly and waggled his eyebrows at her before continuing through Divine Sense, "You know, that..."

Zhu Qing gently nodded. "You possess the Ancestral Dragon bloodline. The nourishment of your bloodline... essence is extremely beneficial to a member of the Dragon Clan like me."

She had never discussed such an explicit subject with a man before, so she could not help feeling her cheeks burn.

"So your advancement to the Ninth-Tier really was because of me?" Yang Kai continued pestering her with questions.

"Mm." Zhu Qing's cheeks burned even hotter.

"Then doesn't that mean that as long as the two of us cultivate diligently in the future..." Yang Kai's eyes darted about as sudden enlightenment filled his heart. "There'll be a chance for your bloodline to advance to an even higher tier?"

"Stop talking about this..." Zhu Qing bit her red lip, far too embarrassed to continue discussing it with Yang Kai.

"I understand, I understand!" Yang Kai chuckled.

Zhu Lie watched coldly from the side. Seeing the two exchange affectionate glances like a loving couple, he wished he could gouge out his own eyes. He truly could not understand what Zhu Qing was thinking or how she could have made such a foolish choice.

With a cold laugh, he said, "Enjoy yourselves while you still can. Sooner or later, you'll regret this."

Yang Kai shot Zhu Lie a cold glance. “I’ve discovered that you’re truly insufferable. If you weren’t my brother-in-law, I’d have already taught you a lesson.”

Zhu Lie gritted his teeth. "Do you think I enjoy being your Little Brother-in-Law? Stop flattering yourself."

Yang Kai said furiously, "If I don't teach you a lesson today, my surname isn't Yang! Qing'er, step aside."

As he spoke, he pushed Zhu Qing away, let out a low shout, and directly displayed the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique.

His body expanded with a thunderous burst, transforming into a four-and-a-half-zhang-tall colossus. He immediately brought a fist crashing down on Zhu Lie's head.

Under the suppression of Yang Kai's bloodline, Zhu Lie was completely unable to transform into a giant Dragon, and his cultivation was suppressed by more than forty percent. He was secretly shocked. The last time he fought Yang Kai, this man had not seemed so tall. Yang Kai had only managed to suppress him after making a breakthrough in the middle of their battle. This time, however, Yang Kai had actually fused with even more of his Ancestral Dragon Origin. Faced with the might of that fist, Zhu Lie could only defend with all his strength.

With a thunderous boom, Zhu Lie was sent flying by the enormous fist, light flashing wildly around his body.

Yang Kai pursued him closely. Before Zhu Lie could steady himself, a storm of attacks came crashing toward him. Amid a series of cracking and banging sounds, Zhu Lie was knocked back and forth like a sandbag.

"Stop fighting!" Zhu Qing anxiously stamped her feet. One was her man and the other was her younger brother, so why could they not get along? They acted like mortal enemies every time they met, leaving her caught in the middle and making things extremely difficult for her.

"Don't worry, I'm just teaching him a lesson. I won't beat him to death!" Yang Kai called back from afar, but his hands showed no mercy. In any case, an Eighth-Tier Red Dragon had thick skin, tough flesh, and could take quite a beating.

The battle was completely one-sided. Watching from nearby, Li Jiao trembled with fear, and his admiration for Yang Kai grew even deeper.

Riding a Dragon was one thing, but he had even given his own Little Brother-in-Law such a savage beating. Looking across all of history, no one but Palace Master Yang had likely ever done such a thing. Then again, Palace Master Yang had probably stirred up a heaven-shaking disaster without even realizing it.

"If you have the ability, then don't rely on bloodline suppression!" Zhu Lie endured Yang Kai's fists. Although he would not suffer any severe injuries, he had completely lost face. With nowhere to vent his fury, he could only attack with words.

"You said that last time. Have you forgotten how that ended, foolish boy?" Yang Kai snorted and continued punching and kicking him.

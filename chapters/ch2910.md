## Chapter 2910 — Poison Barrier

The result shocked everyone, and even Fang Zhuo’s always indifferent expression changed dramatically.

Less than ten breaths after he finished setting up the formation, that patch of void twisted again, then returned to its original state. Gong Yue’s figure appeared from within, holding several formation flags in his hands—clearly the very ones Fang Zhuo had previously inserted into the void.

He had spent only ten breaths to break Fang Zhuo’s formation. One could see just how profound his attainments in the Dao of Formations were.

Gong Yue stood in the void with a gentle smile, giving off an unfathomable feeling. He spoke, “Small Universe Inversion Array—this friend’s formation method is not bad, but these formation flags are a bit roughly refined and can’t bring out their full power. If they could be refined a bit more, their might would increase considerably.”

As he spoke, he tossed those formation flags back to Fang Zhuo.

Fang Zhuo received them, lowered his head and pondered seriously for a while, then cupped his fists solemnly to thank Gong Yue for his guidance.

Yang Tai laughed heartily, “Brother Gong’s attainments in the Dao of Formations are truly extraordinary, opening our eyes! Having Brother Gong’s help this time is truly our good fortune.” He turned to Yang Kai and said, “Young Brother Yang Jiu, do you have any other questions? If so, ask them all at once.”

Yang Kai shook his head, “No more.”

Yang Tai said, “If that’s the case, then no one should have any objections to Brother Gong joining us, right?”

After what just happened, naturally no one had any objections. In fact, everyone was quite happy to have someone like Gong Yue assisting them. It was just that Yang Kai’s earlier question had made people cautious, so they had tried to verify Gong Yue’s identity. Now that Gong Yue’s reputation as a formation master was well-deserved, and with the prestigious name of the Gong family from Tianhe Valley, everyone felt reassured and no longer harbored any doubts about him.

Wu Kuangyi even glared fiercely at Yang Kai, probably thinking he was making a fuss over nothing and had caused him to misjudge the situation as well.

Yang Kai’s tardiness had already left a bad impression on him, and now it was even worse. If not for their prior agreement, he might have driven Yang Kai away already.

Since no one had any objections, the matter was settled. As the one who had invited such a powerful ally as Gong Yue, Yang Tai seemed to have risen to become the leader of this small group, enthusiastically calling everyone to set out.

The Southern Marsh was vast, and the agreed meeting point was quite far from the location of the ancient cave residence. It would take about three or four days to reach it.

The seven Emperor Realm cultivators traveled together, flying swiftly, their auras spreading far and wide. No fierce beasts dared to show themselves. Gong Yue and Yang Tai led the way, chatting warmly as if they were old friends, often bursting into hearty laughter.

The woman named Shen Bingru stayed in the middle with Wu Kuangyi and Fang Zhuo, while Yang Kai and Hua Yulu brought up the rear. The others intentionally or unintentionally excluded them, which made Hua Yulu somewhat anxious.

Among the group, aside from the leading Yang Tai and Gong Yue’s lively conversation, everyone else remained silent.

Yang Kai was multitasking—traveling with the group while also digesting the gains from his experiences in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. Everything he had gone through there was a precious asset, a cornerstone for his growth, especially the knowledge inheritance from Qing and what he had seen and heard in the Shaman God Hall.

Hua Yulu had tried several times to strike up a conversation with Yang Kai, but seeing his distant demeanor that seemed to keep everyone at arm's length, she found herself too embarrassed to bother him further. After all, they weren't particularly familiar with each other—they'd only met a few times and had never developed any real rapport.

Of the seven present, only she felt extremely uneasy about this treasure-seeking trip. The others were paired up, and Yang Kai ignored her completely. If a conflict broke out over the treasure, she didn’t know how much help Yang Kai could be to her.

She even faintly regretted coming. Had she known, she would rather have given up this opportunity than take such a risk.

Time passed as she wavered between hope and fear.

The seven Emperor Realm cultivators traveled through the poison barriers of the Southern Marsh. After four full days, Yang Tai, who was leading, suddenly stopped. Wu Kuangyi’s expression brightened with anticipation.

“Are we here?” Gong Yue asked curiously, looking around but not seeing anything special.

Yang Kai also collected his thoughts and looked around. Like Gong Yue, he didn’t notice anything unusual, but the large poison barrier ahead caught his attention.

The Southern Marsh was filled with poison barriers. The seven had been traveling through them for days, but their strong cultivation gave them considerable resistance, so it hadn’t been a problem.

But the poison barrier ahead was clearly different. Yang Kai sensed a trace of danger from it, as if charging in would mean certain death.

Just as he was about to send out his Divine Sense to investigate, Hua Yulu hurried over and whispered, “Be careful, that poison barrier is extremely toxic.”

A fragrant breeze swirled around Yang Kai, tickling his nose. He nodded lightly, and Hua Yulu, realizing what she’d done, blushed and stepped back two paces.

Yang Tai spoke at the right moment, “We’ve arrived. The ancient cave residence is hidden within this poison barrier, but it’s so strong that even we Emperor Realm cultivators can’t withstand it. So don’t act rashly.”

To prove his point, he suddenly raised his hand and sent a ribbon-like attack toward the poison barrier ahead.

As the light flashed, the poison barrier was split open, creating a gap more than thirty zhang long. In the middle of that gap, a pitch-black skeleton lay on the marshy grass, clearly dead for a long time.

“Speaking of which, when we discovered that cave residence last time, there were four of us, including a fellow at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. But look, he’s lying there now.” Yang Tai pointed at the skeleton and smiled.

Shen Bingru’s pretty face changed color at these words, and even the always impassive Fang Zhuo’s face twitched.

Judging by the state of the skeleton, everyone thought this person had been dead for a long time. But after hearing Yang Tai, they realized he’d only died about half a year ago. The poison in the barrier was so strong that it had caused unexpected changes to the corpse.

As for the cause of death, there was no need for Yang Tai to say more—everyone could guess.

Even a Second Stage Emperor Realm couldn’t withstand the poison’s corrosion, showing just how dangerous it was. The world is full of wonders, but before the might of heaven and earth, even the strongest individuals can seem insignificant.

As soon as Yang Tai finished speaking, the poison barrier that had been split apart surged back together, closing in an instant. It was as if some mysterious force kept the barrier in place, blocking everyone from advancing.

Gong Yue frowned slightly in amazement, “Such potent poison. With this barrier blocking the way, Brother Yang, how did you get into the cave residence last time?”

Yang Tai smiled, “It was a coincidence. Everything has its counter. Though this poison barrier is strong, there happens to be something here that restrains it—just like where there are venomous snakes, there’s always an antidote. You’ll see for yourselves soon.”

He kept them in suspense and didn’t explain further. Gong Yue just smiled and didn’t mind.

“Our timing is perfect. We’ll be able to enter tonight, so let’s take the chance to rest. Who knows what dangers await in the ancient cave residence? It’s best to be well-rested.”

No one objected, so everyone found a place to sit and rest.

Hua Yulu naturally stayed with Yang Kai, took out two Spirit Pills, and swallowed them. Though she was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, traveling at full speed for days wasn’t a problem. But being in this poison-filled environment had seriously depleted her Emperor Yuan, so she needed to recover.

The others did the same.

Suddenly, Yang Kai looked up and said, “Family Head Gong, you are well-traveled and knowledgeable. I have a question.”

Gong Yue was observing the surroundings. Hearing this, he turned to Yang Kai with a smile, “I wouldn’t say well-traveled, just lived a bit longer. What would you like to ask, Young Brother? If I know, I’ll tell you.”

Yang Kai said, “It’s like this. A friend of mine obtained a Fruit of Time. How should it be taken to maximize its medicinal effect?”

“Fruit of Time?” Yang Tai’s eyes lit up, staring at Yang Kai, “That Fruit of Time that can extend one’s lifespan?”

“Exactly!” Yang Kai nodded.

Yang Tai’s eyes grew even brighter. He was at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm and had a long lifespan, but even he couldn’t resist the erosion of time. In other words, he had already grown old. But he was not ready to accept his fate. For nearly a century, he had abandoned his obsession with cultivation, single-mindedly searching for ways to extend his life. A hundred years of effort had indeed borne some fruit, granting him additional years, but the common methods and Spirit Pills he had tried no longer worked for him.

The reason he’d risked coming to the Southern Marsh was to find some heaven-and-earth treasure that could extend his life. That was how he and Hua Yulu had accidentally discovered the ancient cave residence.

Now, hearing Yang Kai mention the Fruit of Time, he was naturally excited.

He had naturally heard of the Fruit of Time before, and it would be immensely useful to him, so he couldn’t help but take notice.

“The Fruit of Time is a wonderful thing,” Gong Yue stroked his beard and smiled. “But Young Brother, you may be asking the wrong person. I specialize in formations, not in Spirit Pills or medicines. So aside from suggesting you have it refined into a pill, I have no better advice.”

Yang Kai agreed, “It seems that’s the only way. But to refine the Fruit of Time into a pill, you’d need an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, and asking one to do so…”

Everyone knew how difficult it was to ask an Imperial-Grade Pill Master to refine a pill. Even with enough materials and payment, without the right connections, it was normal to wait years or even decades for your turn.

Gong Yue smiled, “Let’s hope your friend isn’t in a hurry.”

“He’s still young, no rush.” Yang Kai smiled as well.

“That’s good.” Gong Yue said no more, turning back to observe the poison barrier ahead, his expression unchanged.

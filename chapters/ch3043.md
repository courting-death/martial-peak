## Chapter 3043 — Night Meeting with Zhu Lie

Yang Kai stood on the beach for a long time.

As the sea breeze blew past, the smell of blood faded considerably.

Lu Sanniang and her daughter dug several pits nearby and buried the four dead one by one, allowing them to rest in peace. Such was her nature. Even though Madam Hua had targeted and humiliated her earlier, and even though Madam Hua had harbored ill intentions toward both mother and daughter, Lu Sanniang still could not simply leave their bodies unattended after death.

Her conscience would not allow it.

After all, they had lived together for two or three hundred years. Although there had been no friendship between them, they had still been acquaintances. How could she stand by and watch their corpses lie exposed in the wilderness?

She even properly handled the final arrangements for Yuan Wu, who had been reduced to a lump of charcoal.

This kept them busy until evening. Only after the sun had set did they finish laying everyone to rest. Lu Sanniang returned with an arm around the pale-faced Lu Yuqin, but for a time, she felt lost and did not know where they should go next.

Li Jiao had been taken away to cultivate Dragon Blood Flowers, so the only person she could rely on now was Yang Kai. However, she was not very familiar with him, and after spending the day with him, she had discovered that he was an enormous mystery she could not fathom at all.

He had refused to yield before Madam Hua and had even hurled abuse at a member of the Dragon Clan. Everything he did had seemed perilous in the extreme, yet in the end, he had still emerged completely unscathed.

She stood ten zhang away from Yang Kai, considering what to say. The day's events had shown her that there seemed to be a vast gulf between them, and she could no longer treat him as casually as she had before.

With a great splash, a plume of mist erupted from the sea, as though some sea beast had exhaled. Through the haze, a mountain-like black shadow surfaced somewhere out at sea.

Without looking back, Yang Kai said, "Elder Sister Lu, I am going out for a while. I will be back shortly, so do not wander off."

"Oh, all right." Lu Sanniang answered instinctively, warmth filling her heart. She knew Yang Kai was looking after her like this because of Li Jiao, and she secretly rejoiced that Li Jiao had made such a friend.

At the thought of Li Jiao, Lu Sanniang's expression dimmed again. She feared she would never again be able to return to how things had once been with Big Brother Li.

This love could only be cherished in memory, though even then she had already been lost and bewildered.

By the time she came back to her senses, Yang Kai had already flown away and was descending toward a certain spot in the sea.

Another plume of mist rose into the air. The unknown sea beast turned and swam toward the depths of the ocean, now carrying an unexpected guest on its back.

An hour later, a small Spirit Island appeared ahead. The mountain-like sea beast swam up to it, then stopped.

Understanding its meaning, Yang Kai stepped forward and appeared on the Spirit Island. Only then did the sea beast turn around and disappear into the ocean.

Sweeping the island with his Divine Sense, Yang Kai immediately locked onto a certain location. The Laws of Space surged, and he instantly appeared atop a small mountain peak.

Zhu Lie was already waiting there. He must have seen Yang Kai arrive, so he showed no surprise. He merely glanced back at him and asked with a frown, "How did you end up on Dragon Isle?"

Yang Kai's anger flared. He rushed forward, grabbed Zhu Lie by the collar, and glared at him ferociously. "What do you think, Little Brother-in-Law?"

"Let go!" Zhu Lie glanced down at his collar, his brow tightly furrowed.

Although no outsiders were present, as a member of the Dragon Clan, he still found it difficult to accept being grabbed by the clothes and spoken to in this manner.

"This Young Master has put up with you for a long time!" Yang Kai refused to let go, his expression turning even more savage. "Do not think I cannot guess what happened. Zhu Qing did not return to the Northern Territory and came straight back to Dragon Isle with you instead. That was all because of you, was it not? If you had not been stirring up trouble from the sidelines, Qing'er would definitely have waited for me at Lingxiao Palace."

Zhu Lie said coldly, "Dragon Isle is the ancestral land of my Dragon Clan. If she did not return to Dragon Isle, where else would she go?"

"Still acting stubborn? Do you really think I would not dare teach you a lesson?"

"Do not go too far! This is not your Lingxiao Palace. This is Dragon Isle!"

"Why are you shouting? Since I came here, of course I know this is Dragon Isle! But... it is perfectly natural for a brother-in-law to discipline his wife's younger brother. What does it matter whether this is Dragon Isle or not? Even if Zhu Qing were here, I would still discipline you whenever I pleased. Just see whose side she takes."

Gritting his teeth, Zhu Lie said, "You are utterly unreasonable!"

Yang Kai sneered. "That is still better than resorting to underhanded schemes when someone is caught off guard."

Zhu Lie said angrily, "What schemes have I used? Do not slander me!"

Yang Kai said, "Then let us talk about why Qing'er returned to Dragon Isle without a word! Why did she not even tell me? Do you dare claim you had nothing to do with it?"

"Who knows? Perhaps she grew tired of you. Do not think too highly of yourself. You are merely a human. In the eyes of my Dragon Clan, you are nothing but an ant."

Yang Kai laughed in fury. "Good! Then today, this ant will trample on the pride of your Dragon Clan and see just how much you are truly capable of."

"You are beyond reason!"

"You have no respect for your elders!"

After arguing noisily for a while, the two suddenly fell silent and glared at each other. Neither was willing to back down, and the air bristled with tension.

After a long while, Yang Kai finally released Zhu Lie's collar and snorted.

Looking displeased, Zhu Lie straightened his clothes and brushed off the spot Yang Kai had grabbed, his face filled with disgust.

"Where is Zhu Qing?" Yang Kai asked, gazing out over the star-strewn sea.

Instead of answering, Zhu Lie asked, "Just how did you get in?"

Yang Kai curled his lip. "This Young Master is proficient in the Laws of Space. Entering a Small World is naturally a simple matter."

Zhu Lie shook his head. "Perhaps that would have been possible before, but several Elders have now joined forces to place restrictions over Dragon Isle's entrance. If they are disturbed, they will automatically form a Void Maze. There is no way your methods could have forced a path inside."

Yang Kai nodded. "There was indeed a Void Maze, but it could not stop me."

He was anxious to learn Zhu Qing's whereabouts and could not be bothered to tell Zhu Lie what he had experienced inside the Void Maze, lest it cause further complications.

"You were quite lucky." Zhu Lie sighed. He had heard the Elders say that the Void Maze could trap even a Great Emperor for a short while. If someone tried to break through it by force, it would inevitably alert the Elders of the Dragon Temple. That was the very reason such a method had been left in place: to prevent anyone from secretly infiltrating Dragon Isle. It was not truly intended to hold anyone inside, only to serve as a warning at a critical moment.

He had not expected Yang Kai to be so fortunate as to actually find a way out of the Void Maze.

"Go back," Zhu Lie said with a sigh. "You should never have come."

Yang Kai glanced sideways at him. "Since I am already here, how could I turn around and leave so easily? I want to see your Elder Sister."

Zhu Lie shook his head. "You will not be able to see her."

Yang Kai's expression turned cold. "Would it kill you to help me? Do not forget who brought you out of the Revolving World. I am not asking you to repay my kindness, but surely you can do me a small favor?"

Zhu Lie still shook his head.

Yang Kai's patience began to wear thin. "I truly want to beat you up."

Zhu Lie said, "It is not that I am unwilling to help, but now... even if I helped you, it would be useless."

"What do you mean?" Yang Kai frowned.

Zhu Lie pondered for a long time, seemingly weighing his words. Only after a long while did he suddenly turn and stare intently at Yang Kai. "For my Elder Sister's sake, how far are you willing to go?"

This was the first time Yang Kai had ever heard Zhu Lie call Zhu Qing his Elder Sister. Although he was not addressing Zhu Qing directly, it was still an expression of his true feelings.

Yang Kai vaguely realized something and asked in a grim voice, "Is Zhu Qing in some kind of danger?"

"Answer my question first."

Yang Kai shook his head. "What happens between your Elder Sister and me is none of your concern, so I do not need to explain it to you. But if anyone dares stand in our way, then they are my enemy. Even your Dragon Clan is no exception."

"Even if it means death?"

Yang Kai replied sternly, "I have always been hard to kill."

Zhu Lie nodded, and his perpetually cold gaze finally softened somewhat. "Zhu Qing chose well. I hope you remember what you just said."

Yang Kai asked impatiently, "Is Zhu Qing in danger or not?"

Zhu Lie replied, "She is not exactly in danger, but... she has been imprisoned."

Yang Kai's expression turned icy. "Because of me?"

Zhu Lie nodded. "Correct. You said Zhu Qing did not wait for you in the Northern Territory and returned directly to Dragon Isle because I urged her to do so. I will not deny that I had such intentions at the time and tried to persuade her, but ultimately, the final decision was hers. It was just... I never expected that she wanted to return to Dragon Isle because she intended to tell the Elders about her relationship with you." He turned his head and enunciated each word. "She intends to become your woman!"

Yang Kai froze, then flew into a rage. "Your Dragon Clan imprisoned her just because of that?"

Zhu Lie shouted, "Union with a human is a grave taboo for the Dragon Clan!"

Yang Kai snorted. "Bullshit! Do not think I do not know what your Dragon Clan is like. Over the past few days, I have seen playthings kept by members of the Dragon Clan on many islands. It is not only the male Dragons who do this; some female Dragons do the same. Why have the Elders of your Dragon Clan not imprisoned them?"

Zhu Lie replied, "You said it yourself: those are playthings. How can they compare to what is happening between you and Zhu Qing? Zhu Qing intends to leave Dragon Isle! How could the Elders tolerate that?"

"Leave Dragon Isle?" Yang Kai was completely stunned. He had never expected Zhu Qing to make such a choice. Even though he did not know much about the Dragon Clan, he could still imagine the furious expressions of its Elders when they heard this news.

It might have been different if Zhu Qing were an ordinary member of the Dragon Clan, but she was already a Ninth-Tier Great Dragon. How could Dragon Isle allow such a precedent?

Zhu Lie sighed. "Had I known this would happen, it would have been better not to let her return. But now... alas."

Yang Kai frowned. "Has Zhu Qing not thought about escaping?"

Zhu Lie looked at him as though he were an idiot. "Her Dragon Bloodline has been sealed and her cultivation suppressed. She cannot draw on even the slightest bit of strength now. I would like to see you try escaping like that."

"Then where is she? Hurry and take me to her." Yang Kai was beside himself with anxiety.

"Going there would be useless. You cannot break into the place where she is imprisoned, and even if you managed to enter, you would only be walking to your death."

"How will we know without trying?" Yang Kai coldly snorted.

"I know you are proficient in the Laws of Space, but even that will be useless. The Second Elder is keeping watch over her on his own Spirit Island. No disturbance, however slight, can escape his perception."

"Is the Second Elder strong?"

"Comparable to a Great Emperor!"

Yang Kai sucked in a sharp breath. If that was true, then there really was no point in trying. He might be discovered before he even drew near.

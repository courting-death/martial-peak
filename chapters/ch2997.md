## Chapter 2997 — Righteous Li Jiao

As an Emperor Realm Master and the Lord of a great palace, this was the kind of life he ought to be living, not following behind others like a fawning dog and spending each day unsure whether he would live to see the next.

He secretly resolved that, even if a blade were held to his neck in the future, he would never again undertake such thankless work.

The two beauties in his arms had soft, supple figures, and their exposed skin gleamed like the finest porcelain. His hands roamed freely over them until he had thoroughly satisfied himself, making the two women pant and chide him coquettishly without pause. Their reactions only made him throw back his head and laugh, immensely pleased with himself.

Comparing the misery he had suffered some time ago with his present comfort, he could not help sighing with emotion and secretly feeling sorry for himself.

Suddenly, he raised his head and looked outside, a smile appearing on his face. Rising to his feet, he laughed loudly. "Brother Hua has arrived! Forgive me for failing to welcome you."

Outside the doors, the neatly dressed Hua Xing strode in. The young women performing graceful songs and dances in the hall all kowtowed in welcome when they saw him, calling out, "Greetings, Pavilion Master."

Hua Xing raised a hand slightly, indicating that there was no need for such ceremony. Only then did the young women dare continue their performance. Perhaps because the Pavilion Master had arrived, they danced with even greater effort, becoming all the more seductive and alluring. Their eyes shifted about, clearly anticipating something.

"Brother Li, forgive the intrusion!" Hua Xing cupped his fists and smiled.

Li Jiao smiled upon hearing this. "What are you saying, Brother Hua? I am the one imposing on you. This is your Wind and Cloud Pavilion, so you are the host. Come, come, sit over here!"

As he spoke, he invited Hua Xing to take the seat of honour.

Li Jiao and Hua Xing had known each other not for one or two years, but for over a century. Back when Hua Xing was wholeheartedly pursuing the Martial Dao, he had travelled through the Star Realm's Four Great Territories. When he reached the Northern Territory, he had some friction with Li Jiao. After a bout of fighting, however, their enmity did not deepen; instead, each came to appreciate the other. It was then that their friendship began.

Later, they even explored a Secret Realm together, so their relationship could be considered fairly good. However, after Hua Xing returned to the Western Territory to inherit Wind and Cloud Pavilion, the two had not contacted each other again.

After escaping from the Revolving World this time, Li Jiao had also arrived in the Western Territory. He had landed in a different place from Yang Kai, Zhu Qing, and her younger brother, one relatively close to Wind and Cloud Pavilion, so he decided to visit his old friend.

Although they had not seen each other for a hundred years, Hua Xing had not forgotten Li Jiao. Naturally, he received him warmly, arranging for him to live alone on a mountain peak and sending beauties to serve him. Li Jiao spent every day seeking pleasure, indulging himself, and living freely and happily.

Li Jiao had been stifled for far too long. Ever since losing his wager with Yang Kai at Lingxiao Palace, he had not had a single good day.

First, his Sect fell into a financial crisis, filling the many elders of Li Dragon Palace with resentment. They were furious with him but did not dare speak out. Before that matter could be resolved, Yang Kai used his status as a creditor to drag him off to the Frozen Earth. What happened afterward was simply unbearable to recall, and every time Li Jiao thought about it, he felt the urge to shed tears.

He was the Palace Master of Li Dragon Palace! He was a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master! Even across the entire Star Realm, he ranked among the strongest of experts. As long as the Great Emperors did not appear, the Third-Stage Emperor Realm was synonymous with supreme strength. Who would not treat him with the utmost respect and courtesy upon seeing him?

Yet when he stayed with those monsters around Yang Kai, he could not feel the slightest sense of superiority.

There were two members of the Dragon Clan, both with pure bloodlines and both Great Dragons of at least the Eighth Tier. Either one could scare him witless merely by releasing a little Dragon Pressure, forcing him to wait upon them with the utmost care. Even Yang Kai possessed a Great Dragon bloodline. Though he only cultivated at the First-Stage Emperor Realm, if they truly fought, Li Jiao estimated he would not last three moves against him.

Those three… were practically his grandfathers. In their presence, Li Jiao felt no different from a grandson. Any one of them could order him about and command him at will, yet he did not dare utter a single complaint or show the slightest hint of defiance. The bearing and confidence of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master had been almost completely ground away.

It was far too humiliating! Merely thinking about it brought bitter tears to his eyes.

Therefore, as soon as he broke away from Yang Kai and the others, Li Jiao had no intention of seeking them out. He only felt that the sky was now high enough for birds to fly and the sea vast enough for fish to leap. If possible, it would be best never to see them again for the rest of his life.

Only after coming to Wind and Cloud Pavilion as a guest and receiving Hua Xing's lavish hospitality did Li Jiao regain his confidence.

He was a descendant of the Dragon Clan, a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, a mighty expert, a formidable overlord of his generation!

After indulging himself these past few days, all the resentment accumulated in his body had dispersed. He now felt that staying at Wind and Cloud Pavilion was even more comfortable than living in his own Li Dragon Palace. Though Li Dragon Palace was his Sect, returning meant facing a mountain of debt and a repayment period stretching across several dozen years. Just thinking about the elders' evasive expressions made Li Jiao's heart ache as though cut by a knife.

How could Li Dragon Palace compare with the freedom and ease of Wind and Cloud Pavilion? If possible, he would not mind staying here for several dozen years before returning. Presumably, Hua Xing would not object. After all, with an expert like him residing in Wind and Cloud Pavilion, Hua Xing was probably delighted to have him.

Thus, when he saw Hua Xing arrive, Li Jiao was overjoyed and began considering how to tell him that he wished to remain here for a while longer.

"Brother Li, have you grown accustomed to living here?" Hua Xing asked with a concerned expression.

Li Jiao laughed loudly and pointed to the graceful, shapely young women below. "With Brother Hua treating me so generously, how could I not be comfortable? Wind and Cloud Pavilion truly is a land blessed by the Heavens, far better than this Lord's Li Dragon Palace."

Hua Xing hurriedly waved his hand. "Brother Li praises us too highly. The Western Territory is already the most barren and desolate place in the Star Realm. No matter how fine Wind and Cloud Pavilion may be, how could it compare with your Li Dragon Palace? Brother Li must not forget that this Hua visited Li Dragon Palace back then, so I understand what it is like there."

These words were pleasing to the ear and even carried a hint of flattery. Li Jiao had not heard such things in a long time, so he immediately smiled, feeling thoroughly gratified. Most people in this world were still normal, he thought. Existences like those three monsters were a rare few, after all.

"Brother Hua need not belittle yourself. For Wind and Cloud Pavilion to possess such grandeur and such rich Spiritual Qi while situated in the Western Territory is already quite extraordinary. It is clear that Brother Hua manages it well. In that regard, this Li should seek your guidance."

"I would not dare, I would not dare. This Hua no longer has any heart to manage Wind and Cloud Pavilion. If I visit the Northern Territory again someday, I hope Brother Li will receive me. This Hua would be endlessly grateful." As he spoke, Hua Xing revealed an expression of pain and despondency.

Li Jiao asked in surprise, "Receiving you would naturally be no problem. Brother Hua and I have been old friends for many years, so if you went to the Northern Territory, how could this Li possibly fail to entertain you? But... what do you mean by that? Wind and Cloud Pavilion appears to be on the rise. If you continue managing it for another three to five hundred years, it will surely reach even greater heights. Why is Brother Hua sighing so heavily? Could you have encountered some difficulty?"

Hearing this, Hua Xing smiled bitterly. With a forlorn expression, he picked up the wine cup before him and drained it in one gulp, then said listlessly, "Family shame should not be aired in public. Best not to mention it, best not to mention it!"

Li Jiao's face sank, and he said angrily, "Brother Hua is wrong to say that. You and this Li have been old friends for a hundred years, so what family shame could there be that cannot be shared? If you have truly encountered some difficulty, you may as well tell me. If this Li can help, I will never stand idly by."

A gleam flashed through Hua Xing's eyes, though he concealed it well. Smiling bitterly, he said, "This Hua appreciates Brother Li's kindness, but... this is Wind and Cloud Pavilion's affair, after all."

Li Jiao rose to his feet with a dark expression. "So Brother Hua regards me as an outsider? Very well. Since Brother Hua insists on treating me so distantly, this Li will trouble you no longer. The green hills will not change and the flowing waters will endure. Brother Hua, this Li bids you farewell!"

Saying so, he strode toward the exit as though he truly intended to leave.

Hua Xing hurriedly stood and grabbed Li Jiao's sleeve. "Brother Li, please calm your anger. It was this Hua's mistake, all right?"

Li Jiao turned back to look at him. He had only been pretending to leave in the first place, so he naturally seized the opportunity to stay. He said earnestly, "Brother Hua, back then, you and I went through life and death together. Though we have not met in a hundred years, our friendship has not diminished. If you face some difficulty, this Li is willing to help."

Hua Xing looked moved and said gratefully, "Brother Li is truly righteous. It was this Hua who was narrow-minded!" After struggling for a moment, he said decisively, "Very well. Brother Li is no outsider, so there is no shame in telling you. Please, let us sit and talk."

Li Jiao sat down again, personally poured Hua Xing a cup of wine, and then quietly watched him.

Hua Xing opened his mouth, only to sigh once more. After draining another cup of wine, he finally said with bitter anguish, "Brother Li, to tell you the truth, someone has walked all over Wind and Cloud Pavilion... We have utterly lost face during this time."

"What happened?" Li Jiao frowned upon hearing this. Although he had been staying here as a guest these past few days, he did nothing but indulge in wine and women from morning to night and had paid no attention whatsoever to outside affairs. Naturally, the young women sent to serve him would not discuss such matters with him, leaving him somewhat cut off from the news.

"Who could be so bold!" Li Jiao flew into a rage, slapping the table and shouting in a low voice, though inwardly he began to mutter uneasily. Hua Xing's cultivation was equal to his own. Although Li Jiao was somewhat stronger than an ordinary Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master because he possessed a Dragon Clan bloodline, even if he was stronger than Hua Xing, the difference was limited.

Hua Xing looked as though he had encountered a powerful enemy. Li Jiao had no idea whom Wind and Cloud Pavilion had provoked to leave even Hua Xing appearing completely helpless.

Li Jiao secretly regretted acting so forthrightly just now. He should have taken the opportunity to leave. Now that he had gotten himself entangled in this trouble, he feared it would not be easy to escape.

As he worried anxiously, he heard Hua Xing say, "It is only a little girl who knows neither the immensity of Heaven nor Earth. She killed my son!"

As he spoke, the corners of his eyes grew moist at just the right moment. Grabbing the wine pot from the table, he gulped from it wildly, as though he wished to remain drunk forever and escape his pain.

"A little girl..." A drop of cold sweat seeped from Li Jiao's forehead. A face that filled him with dread and terror involuntarily surfaced in his mind, and he asked in alarm, "What did that little girl look like? Did she... have red hair?"

He was truly afraid. If the woman who had killed Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Young Pavilion Master was Zhu Qing, then even if he had to fall out with Hua Xing, he would flee this place at once.

Although he shared some friendship with Hua Xing, only the two of them knew how reliable that friendship truly was. Offending a member of the Dragon Clan, and a Ninth-Tier Great Dragon at that, for the sake of such an unreliable friendship? Li Jiao had not lost his mind.

## Chapter 2791 — Nine Stars Assault the Moon

There were at most three Great Emperor Sects in the Star Realm. In the Eastern Territory stood the Soul Palace of Great Emperor Youhun; in the Southern Territory, the Star God Palace of the Radiant Moon Great Emperor; and in the Western Territory, the Shadow Slaughter Palace of the Night Shadow Great Emperor…

Of course, Medicine Pill Valley and Spirit Beast Island also counted as Great Emperor forces, but they were not considered Sects, since they did not widely accept disciples. Whether it was Medicine Pill Valley or Spirit Beast Island, both were simply the secluded abodes of two Great Emperors, places ordinary people had no chance to enter.

And any Great Emperor Sect was like an unshakable pillar for its territory—a divine guardian, awe-inspiring and inviolable.

As the overlord Sect of the Southern Territory, the Star God Palace had played this role for tens of thousands of years, earning the reverence and worship of countless cultivators in the Southern Territory.

Over the course of tens of thousands of years, the Origin Qi of all living beings has gathered here, so much so that the very existence of Star God Palace has become inseparably entwined with the fate of the entire Southern Territory. It was often said that if Star God Palace were ever to fall, the Southern Territory itself would be plunged into chaos and misery, its people suffering untold calamities from which there would be no return.

However, with Radiant Moon Great Emperor overseeing things, destroying the Star God Palace was certainly no easy feat—unless another era of chaos like that of the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor arrived.

When Yang Kai arrived at the Star-Moon Mountain, seven days had already passed.

The peaks of Star-Moon Mountain soared high and proud, towering steeply as if they brushed against the heavens themselves. It was said that here, one could reach out and pluck the sun and moon, and with a wave of the hand, shroud the stars.

A heavy, ancient aura rushed toward him. What stood before him was not just a mountain range, but a living giant that had towered over the Southern Territory for tens of thousands of years, overlooking the rise and fall of all beings.

Yang Kai was well-traveled, having seen the wonders of countless sects. Even top sects like Qingyang Shrine, Ice Heart Valley, or Wenqing Sect could not compare to the Star God Palace.

Standing here, it felt as if his entire being was inexplicably elevated, filling him with a subtle sense of excitement and joy.

In the wilds, rare birds and beasts frolicked and wandered. Amidst the clouds and mist, pavilions and towers appeared and disappeared, presenting a scene of immortal paradise, breathtakingly beautiful and impossible to tire of.

“Is this what a Great Emperor Sect is like?” Yang Kai murmured.

“Who goes there!”

Yang Kai had barely stood for a moment when an angry shout rang out. Two streaks of light sped over from afar, transforming into the figures of two young men as they approached. Both exuded vigorous and pure Dao Origin Realm auras, clearly possessing solid foundations and boundless potential. Given time, they might well break through the shackles of the Emperor Realm and become experts of their generation.

Such outstanding cultivators would be groomed as successors in any Sect.

Yet, judging by their appearance, these two seemed to be mere gatekeepers. Yang Kai could not help but marvel that the Star God Palace truly lived up to its name. Only Soul Palace and Shadow Slaughter Palace, as fellow Great Emperor Sects, could match such depth.

“This is Star-Moon Mountain. Outsiders are not allowed to trespass. Leave the way you came, or you will not be let off lightly!” Of the two Dao Origin Realm cultivators, the one on the left, who seemed a bit steadier, shouted sternly at Yang Kai.

Although their cultivation was far below Yang Kai’s, neither showed the slightest fear. Instead, they shouted at him boldly.

This was the confidence of Star God Palace disciples!

In the eyes of Star God Palace disciples, all cultivators in the Southern Territory fell into two categories: those who were fellow disciples, and those who were not.

Yang Kai was obviously the latter, so regardless of his strength, they had no need to be overly concerned.

Glancing at the two, Yang Kai cupped his fists and said, “I am here by invitation. There is no need for alarm.”

“By invitation?” The young man who spoke first frowned and scrutinized Yang Kai suspiciously. “Who invited you? Can you give a name? My Senior Brother can pass on the message for you.”

Since this person was here by invitation, he must have some connection to their Sect, so it would not do to offend him lightly. Their tone softened accordingly.

Yang Kai was about to explain when his expression suddenly changed. He smiled and said, “No need. Someone is coming to receive me.”

The two gatekeepers turned to look.

A streak of multicolored light sped over from the distance and stopped nearby in the blink of an eye. As the light faded, a beautiful and moving figure appeared.

“Senior Brother Yang!” Lan Xun greeted with a smile. In that instant, it was as if all color vanished from the world.

“P-Princess?” The Senior and Junior Brothers were instantly dumbfounded, staring blankly at the suddenly appearing Lan Xun, their eyes nearly frozen in place.

When Yang Kai said he was here by invitation, they had not cared much, thinking he was just an outsider with some connection to their Sect. But now, it turned out the one who invited him was the Princess of Star God Palace!

How could it be the Princess? The Princess was outstanding in every way—not only inheriting the Radiant Moon Physique of the Great Emperor, but also possessing peerless talent in the Martial Dao, and being stunningly beautiful, gentle, and approachable. She was extremely popular throughout Star God Palace and the dream of countless young talents.

Even the proud disciples of Star God Palace considered it an honor to exchange a single word with the Princess.

Very few ever received such an honor. Only Xiao Chen, who guarded the Princess year-round, could see her so often.

How could someone worshipped as a goddess by countless Star God Palace disciples invite a man here?

In their shock, the two could barely think.

“Junior Sister Lan, you came so quickly!” Yang Kai replied. When he arrived at Star-Moon Mountain, he had contacted Lan Xun, who told him to wait a moment. He had not expected her to arrive so soon.

He actually called her… Junior Sister Lan!

The two Star God Palace disciples’ eyes widened even further as they watched Yang Kai and Lan Xun interact so intimately. Their hearts twisted with inexplicable pain, and the Princess’s reaction made them feel even more hopeless, as if life had lost all meaning.

“Senior Brother Yang sent word, so of course I hurried over!” Lan Xun laughed, gesturing with her hand. “Please, Senior Brother.”

“Thank you!” Yang Kai nodded and strode forward. When he reached Lan Xun’s side, she took the lead and guided the way.

Only after their figures disappeared did the two Star God Palace disciples exchange a dazed glance. From the moment Lan Xun appeared, she had not spoken a single word to them, nor even glanced their way. All her attention had been on the outsider.

What did this mean? It meant the Princess valued that man greatly. Otherwise, the usually kind Princess would not have withheld even a smile from them.

“Senior Brother, Senior Brother Xiao is in trouble now,” the Junior Brother said gloomily.

“Indeed… I wonder if Senior Brother Xiao knows about this. If he finds out…”

The Senior Brother Xiao they spoke of was naturally Xiao Chen. Among all disciples of the same generation in Star God Palace, no one interacted with the Princess more than Xiao Chen. Xiao Chen was fine in every other respect, but when it came to Lan Xun, he was extremely petty.

If any Senior Brother or Junior Brother spoke to the Princess, he would hold a grudge and look for a chance to teach them a lesson. Yet, the Princess herself only ever treated him as an elder brother—never showing the slightest hint of romantic affection.

But now, it seemed this outsider was about to steal Senior Brother Xiao’s place. If Xiao Chen found out, who knew what would happen?

“Senior Brother Xiao aside, the real problem is Senior Brother Lei…”

At the mention of Senior Brother Lei, both their faces showed a trace of fear, as if greatly frightened. They quickly glanced around to make sure no one was nearby before fleeing in haste.

Meanwhile, in the core hall of Star God Palace—the Star Hall—a youth sitting cross-legged in meditation suddenly opened his eyes.

Those eyes were filled with countless stars, blazing with heat. As he looked up, his gaze was as sharp as a hawk’s, seeming to pierce the heavens.

Suddenly, the youth’s brow furrowed as he let out a soft exclamation, “Nine Stars Assault the Moon? How could such a sign appear? This Lord is called Radiant Moon—are they aimed at me? Ill fortune… ill fortune indeed…”

Few in the world knew that the famed Radiant Moon Great Emperor, the guardian deity of the Southern Territory, appeared outwardly as a young, inexperienced boy.

As he spoke, he lowered his head and formed a strange hand seal with one hand. A mysterious aura spread out, while the fingers of his other hand tapped in succession.

After a moment, the youth curled his lips, released the hand seal, and muttered, “That old man Tianji’s secret art is utter nonsense. Can’t divine a thing, yet still dares to call himself the Arbiter of Fate. Next time I see him, I’ll pluck every hair from his beard.”

Propping his cheek with his hand, the youth pondered for a while, then lost interest. “Forget it. At most, it’s a minor disaster—not worth worrying about.”

With that, he closed his eyes and resumed meditating.

…

“Senior Brother Yang, please settle here for the next few days.” On Nandou Peak, after arranging Yang Kai’s accommodations, Lan Xun spoke.

Yang Kai asked, “Junior Sister Lan, when can I enter the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda?”

If not for his prior agreement with Hua Yulu, Yang Kai would not have been in a hurry. After all, this was a Great Emperor Sect, and staying here for a while would do him no harm—he might even gain some unexpected benefits. But with the agreement in place, he was afraid of delaying things.

After all, even after entering the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, he would need to cultivate inside for a month.

Traveling back and forth would take time; he could not afford too much delay.

Lan Xun smiled and said, “Rest assured, Senior Brother Yang. Since my father has promised you, he will not go back on his word. Normally, the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda would only open in half a year, but since there is a promise to you, Star God Palace will make preparations. At most, it will be five days.”

“That’s good.” Yang Kai breathed a sigh of relief.

Lan Xun smiled, “Seeing Senior Brother Yang so anxious, could it be you have a rendezvous with a beauty?”

She joked casually, but seeing Yang Kai freeze, she was surprised. “So you really do have a date with a beauty?”

Yang Kai’s face darkened. “Don’t talk nonsense. But yes, I do have an agreement.”

Lan Xun covered her mouth and giggled. “No need to explain, Senior Brother Yang. Well, I’ll be off. I’ll come notify you when the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda opens.”

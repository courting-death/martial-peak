## Chapter 2975 — Demon Fury City

After the time it took to drink a cup of tea, Zhu Lie lay on the ground with a bruised and swollen face, his clothes disheveled and his breath coming in heavy gasps. He looked like a helpless young maiden who had been ravaged by a hundred burly men.

His eyes were swollen to mere slits, barely visible at all, while his once handsome face was so battered that it was almost unrecognizable.

"If you dare disrespect me or Qing'er again, this is what will happen to you." Yang Kai looked down at him from above.

Zhu Lie turned his head aside and ignored him, secretly resolving that as long as the green hills remained, there would be no shortage of firewood. Did the Human Race not have a saying that a wise man knows better than to fight when the odds were against him? His momentary forbearance was only so he could erupt with greater force later. One day, he would make Yang Kai understand this Dragon's true might.

Yang Kai withdrew the Dragon Transformation Art and returned to his original form.

"Palace... Palace Master Yang!" Li Jiao's timid voice came from nearby. "Save me!"

Yang Kai turned his head to look and suddenly felt like laughing.

At some point, the little black dog had run over to Li Jiao and begun sniffing him all over. Confusion and curiosity flickered in its eyes, and from time to time, it even stuck out its tongue to lick Li Jiao's exposed skin.

Li Jiao had been corroded by Demonic Qi and gave off a faint scent similar to that of the Demon Race. This was probably what had attracted the little black dog's attention.

The scene of it swallowing a Demon King from the Blood Demon Clan in a single bite was still fresh in Li Jiao's mind, so he was extremely wary of this little beast. Moreover, it was Yang Kai's beast companion, making him even more afraid to act rashly. He could only ask Yang Kai for help.

"Ah Wang, that's not food!" Yang Kai called out.

Only then did the little black dog cast one last reluctant glance at Li Jiao and obediently walk back. With a leap, it sprang into Zhu Qing's arms.

"Lecherous dog!" Yang Kai looked at it with disdain. Come to think of it, the little black dog used to be close only to him, but ever since meeting Zhu Qing, it had clung to her side and refused to leave.

Zhu Qing said, "What nonsense are you spouting? She's female!"

Yang Kai stared at her in astonishment. "Really?"

He truly had never paid attention to the little black dog's sex. Walking over, he grabbed its tail and was about to lift it for a look, but Zhu Qing slapped his hand away.

"What's wrong with taking a look..." Yang Kai muttered. He did not dwell on the matter, instead glancing at Li Jiao and Zhu Lie before saying, "Now that we've found everyone, let's set out for Yin Wind Mountain."

Li Jiao asked, "What kind of place is Yin Wind Mountain?"

Yang Kai replied, "It's the only exit if we want to leave this world."

Li Jiao immediately perked up and sprang to his feet. "Then what are we waiting for? Let's hurry."

Among everyone present, his cultivation was not the lowest, as Yang Kai was still beneath him, but in terms of actual combat strength, he was undoubtedly the weakest. Coupled with the Demonic Qi corroding his body and making him uncomfortable all over, he could not wait to leave this damned place.

"Let's go." As Yang Kai spoke, he summoned his Flowing Cloud Shuttle.

Zhu Qing entered while holding the little black dog, and Li Jiao followed her inside with an ingratiating smile.

Yang Kai turned to look nearby. "Do you want to come in?"

"Hmph!" Zhu Lie coldly snorted, his face filled with dislike. Anyone who had just been beaten would be in a foul mood, let alone a member of the Dragon Clan.

"Suit yourself!" Yang Kai curled his lips and did not insist. He had invited Zhu Lie, after all, so no one could blame him if the other man refused to enter. Since Zhu Lie did not acknowledge him as his Brother-in-Law, there was no need for him to grovel for the man's approval.

He went straight inside the shuttle, determined their direction, and circulated his Emperor Yuan, piloting the Flowing Cloud Shuttle toward Yin Wind Mountain.

The flight-type Imperial Treasure was astonishingly fast. The Flowing Cloud Shuttle transformed into a streak of light and vanished in an instant.

Li Jiao knew his place. He found a corner, sat down cross-legged, and circulated his strength to resist the corrosion of the Demonic Qi, while a restless thought in his heart steadily grew stronger. Yang Kai might have casually suggested that he obtain some Dragon Blood from Zhu Lie to suppress and expel the Demonic Qi within him, and although the idea sounded rather unrealistic, Li Jiao had taken it seriously.

The more he thought about it, the better the idea seemed. Dragon Blood possessed divinity and would definitely be able to suppress and expel Demonic Qi. For a Jiao like him, Dragon Blood had another, even more important use: purifying his bloodline.

His cultivation had already reached its limit, and he had no hope of making any further progress in this lifetime. If he wanted to increase his strength, his only option was to start with his bloodline.

Back then, he had narrowly escaped death to bring a Dragon Blood Flower back from the Frozen Earth. He had spent three hundred years continuously watering and nurturing it with his own blood, and just when it had finally ripened, Yang Kai had reaped the benefits. His heart ached whenever he remembered it. Worse still, the one who had taken the Dragon Blood Flower from him had been Zhu Qing, a member of the Dragon Clan, leaving him unable to even entertain the thought of resisting or taking revenge.

However, compared with genuine, pure Dragon Blood, a Dragon Blood Flower was simply nothing.

If he could obtain even a single drop of Dragon Blood, it would benefit him more than consuming over a hundred Dragon Blood Flowers.

The more he thought about it, the harder it became to control himself. He definitely could not defeat Zhu Lie in a fight, so he wondered whether he should simply speak to him about it. Perhaps Zhu Lie might actually agree to give him a drop of Dragon Blood.

As wild thoughts filled his head, the Demonic Qi around him churned erratically. The little black dog raised its head from Zhu Qing's arms and fixed its burning gaze on Li Jiao.

On the other side, Yang Kai chuckled and consoled Zhu Qing, "Don't worry. He's following us."

Zhu Qing sighed. "Don't bully him like that in the future. Most members of the Dragon Clan have this kind of temper. He didn't mean any harm."

Yang Kai smiled. "Your temper doesn't seem to be like that."

Although Zhu Qing had indeed been supremely arrogant when they first met, as though no one beneath the Heavens was worthy of her notice, she had changed after that.

"Only toward you. Who told you to do that the first time we met..." Zhu Qing glared fiercely at him. "I should have realized long ago that you were no good."

"Yet you still refuse to leave my side." Yang Kai shamelessly pressed closer.

Zhu Qing placed a hand against his chest to hold him back, then bashfully gestured to the side.

Yang Kai glanced at Li Jiao, who was meditating nearby, and cursed resentfully.

If only he had known better than to let this fellow aboard.

...

The Revolving World truly was not very large. With the Flowing Cloud Shuttle's speed, Yang Kai and the others arrived near Yin Wind Mountain after just one day.

In the distance, a massive ancient fortress stood several hundred li from a mountain peak. It had been built in an extremely rough and sinister style, and from afar, it resembled a vicious beast crouching there, ready to choose someone to devour at any moment.

All kinds of buildings were scattered around the fortress, clustered here and there without the slightest planning. Despite their chaotic arrangement, they formed a peculiar city in this place.

Demon Fury City!

The gathering place of the Demon Race in the Revolving World.

Demon Fury City covered a much larger area than Human Sovereign City. After all, Demon Fury City did not need any Barrier Array to resist the changes in the World Laws. Unlike the Human Race, the Demon Race was not greatly affected regardless of how the Revolving World's Laws changed. One could even say that when the Laws favored the Demon Domain, they were like fish in water.

By the time they reached this place, the Demonic Qi outside had become extremely dense. The Demonic Qi here also possessed an icy characteristic and could almost be called a kind of frigid Demonic Qi.

This was solely because Demon Fury City stood near the foot of Yin Wind Mountain. The Yin Wind blowing down from the mountain possessed this very characteristic: it was bone-chillingly cold and caused Demonic Intent to arise within those it touched.

The Demon Race had clearly built Demon Fury City here to cultivate more effectively. The closer one drew to Yin Wind Mountain, the denser the Demonic Qi became and the more suitable it was for the Demon Race to increase their strength. After more than a hundred thousand years of study, they had learned to minimize the cold's effects while drawing the Demonic Qi into their bodies and turning it into nourishment for their growth.

When the Flowing Cloud Shuttle came piercing through the air, the many experts in Demon Fury City naturally sensed it. Figures flew up from within the city one after another and stood aloft in the void.

In the blink of an eye, the sky above Demon Fury City was packed with figures. There were at least several thousand of them, including no shortage of Demon King-level existences.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle stopped outside Demon Fury City, and Yang Kai leisurely stepped out.

Countless gazes converged on him as powerful Divine Senses swept over, compressing and distorting the surrounding void.

Yang Kai remained perfectly calm, a smile on his face.

"Audacious Human! You actually dare trespass into my Demon Fury City? You truly have no regard for your own life!"

"Who are you? State your name!"

"Kill him! Kill!"

"Leave him to me. I'll drain every last drop of his Yang Essence and make him die from depletion!"

The Demon Kings began shouting over one another. Never before had any Human dared come to Demon Fury City so openly. It was simply beyond reason. The many Demon Kings all felt that the dignity of the Demon Race had been violated, so naturally, they could not let the matter pass.

Pitch-black Demonic Qi soared into the sky and blotted out the heavens, transforming into an enormous black cloud that slowly advanced toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai chuckled and called out, "This Young Master wishes to travel to Yin Wind Mountain and is merely passing through. I have no intention of entering into conflict with any of you, so if it is convenient, I ask that you make an exception and let me pass."

"Hahaha! What did this boy just say? He wants to go to Yin Wind Mountain? Is he trying to get himself killed?"

"They say the Human Race doesn't know the immensity of Heaven and Earth, and now I see it's true. With such meager strength, he still wants to brave Yin Wind Mountain? This King is dying of laughter!"

"Little Brother, why seek your own death? Even Demon Kings like us do not dare approach Yin Wind Mountain lightly. If you go there, you will almost certainly die. Why not stay and chat with Big Sister instead? Big Sister is so lonely here."

Yang Kai cupped his fists. "Many thanks for this Big Sister's concern, but I truly must make a trip to Yin Wind Mountain, so I can only appreciate your kind intentions. Could I trouble Big Sister to speak to the other Demon Kings and ask them to let me pass?"

The Succubus who had spoken smiled sweetly, her expression so innocent that others could not help developing a favorable impression of her. Hearing Yang Kai's words, she asked guilelessly, "Little Brother, what are you going to Yin Wind Mountain for?"

"Rumor has it that there is a way to leave this world at Yin Wind Mountain, so I want to search for it."

The Succubus nodded. "There is indeed such a rumor, but no one can guarantee whether it's true."

"That's fine. I'm only going to give it a try. If I really can't find a way out, I'll come back and keep Big Sister company. This Little Brother is well-endowed and highly skilled, so I certainly won't disappoint Big Sister."

The Succubus covered her mouth and giggled. "Oh my, Little Brother, what are you saying? You're making me blush to death. Ha... Very well. Since you seem so sincere, Big Sister will ask them for you, but whether they're willing to let you pass is beyond my control."

## Chapter 2881 — Ghostly and Elusive

The city, once belonging to the Barbarian Tribe, had long since been occupied by the demons, with Demonic Qi lingering throughout, refusing to dissipate. Yet at this moment, the vast city was eerily silent, not a single soul to be seen on the streets, as if it had become a city of the dead.

Clusters of black mist drifted in from afar at incredible speed, arriving above the city in the blink of an eye, whereupon several Demon Kings revealed themselves.

There were quite a few of them, no less than five. The leading Demon King had not a trace of flesh or blood on his body, only a tall, pitch-black skeleton. Two eerie ghostly flames danced incessantly in his eye sockets.

Bone Demon Fei Li.

“We’re late!” A Demon King standing behind Fei Li swept his Divine Sense across the entire city, his expression suddenly darkening. The other Demon Kings didn’t look any better, except for Fei Li, who, lacking flesh, showed no expression. However, the ghostly flames in his eyes flickered even more violently, as if displaying his anger.

“This is the nineteenth place! Damn that outsider!” The speaking Demon King gnashed his teeth, as if he bore a deep grudge against someone.

Another Demon King stepped forward, frowning, “I remember that when that outsider was fleeing from under Lord Mo Duo’s hand, he once said that since the Southern Barbarian Tribe was exterminated with three hundred thousand dead, my demon race must pay the same price. Does he really intend to kill three hundred thousand before he’s satisfied? Where does he get such energy?”

Three hundred thousand lives—those were not three hundred thousand insects. Even if they were, killing that many would still take some effort.

Yet in reality, ever since that outsider escaped that day, he had been roaming the demon territory, penetrating deep behind enemy lines. Wherever he went, the stationed demons would be utterly wiped out, and even the Demon Kings could not escape.

A whole month—an entire month—he had traversed nineteen places, turning each demon stronghold into a ghostly lair, leaving no demon alive wherever he passed.

Mo Duo was furious about this. He even personally tried to track down the outsider’s whereabouts, but to his frustration, the outsider was extremely cunning, always managing to evade pursuit. The closest they ever got was a hundred li, but even then, the outsider vanished without a trace.

He never fought the Demon Saints, only picked off the easy targets.

Moreover, the demons he killed died in bizarre ways. Ninety-nine percent bore no visible wounds, yet every dead demon’s face was twisted in terror, as if they had encountered something that frightened them to death.

The outsider’s relentless harassment had tied up a great deal of the demon race’s strength. In a month, over a hundred thousand demons had died by his hand, including five Demon Kings. Such losses were disastrous for the demons. Though there were over a hundred Demon Kings in this invasion, no number could withstand such attrition.

Most Demon Saints were recuperating from injuries. Although Lord Mo Duo had come to assist, for special reasons he could not exert his full strength. In the battles against the outsiders, the Demon Kings were the backbone. If too many were lost, it would bode ill for the future.

The demons’ lines rapidly contracted, and the Demon Kings no longer dared to act alone, instead moving in groups of three to five. Some resisted the outsider’s army, while others tracked the whereabouts of that foreign shaman.

Gazing down at the city, Fei Li spoke, “I remember this is Bahan’s territory, but I can’t sense his presence. Who will go check on him?”

“I’ll take a look.” The Demon King who spoke first hesitated, then volunteered, descending straight toward the largest building in the city.

The remaining four Demon Kings released their Divine Sense, staying alert. Though they hadn’t encountered the outsider recently, and every place they arrived was filled with corpses, none dared guarantee the foreign shaman wasn’t lying in ambush.

Every Demon King felt fear toward that foreign shaman, for he had the power to kill Demon Kings!

The surroundings were deathly quiet, the air inexplicably cold. Except for Bone Demon Fei Li, the other three Demon Kings were visibly tense.

At that moment, a violent surge of demonic power suddenly erupted and vanished in a flash. Immediately after, a building below collapsed into dust.

The Demon Kings’ expressions changed, their Divine Sense surging over like a tide, but their search left them shocked—there was nothing there, not even a trace of the companion who had gone to investigate.

Someone began calling that Demon King’s name, but received no response.

This sent chills down their spines, making them realize their companion had likely met with disaster.

“He’s here!” Fei Li shouted in a low voice, his skull swiveling as the ghostly flames in his eyes flickered, searching for the enemy’s trace.

The remaining three Demon Kings braced themselves as if facing a great enemy, each drawing their demonic weapons, Demonic Qi surging within, ready to strike at any moment.

Even the air seemed to freeze. The tense, murderous atmosphere made every Demon King’s hair stand on end. Yet no matter how they searched, they could not find the foreign shaman’s trace.

He was always this ghostly and elusive, keeping everyone on edge.

But every Demon King knew he hadn’t left, still lurking in the shadows, ready to strike a careless Demon King with a fatal blow at any moment.

If only Mo Ke Tuo were here! When it came to stealth and assassination, she was a master. With her skills, she could surely find the foreign shaman’s trail.

“Behind!” Fei Li suddenly shouted, making the already tense Demon Kings react instinctively. As he spoke, Fei Li raised his hand, sending a ghostly flame attack in that direction.

The other three Demon Kings simultaneously unleashed their killing moves, striking at the void.

They hadn’t sensed the foreign shaman’s approach, but Fei Li was the strongest among them, so they trusted his judgment.

All attacks landed on the same spot, but to no effect. Just as the three Demon Kings were still bewildered, a muffled groan suddenly sounded.

One Demon King looked down at his chest, eyes wide, seeing a skeletal hand piercing through his chest, clutching his two demon hearts.

That skeletal hand was so familiar, it was hard to believe.

“Fei Li?”

The other two Demon Kings were terrified by the sudden turn of events, unable to understand why Fei Li, who had always fought alongside them, would suddenly turn on his own companions. Their momentary hesitation cost them their vigilance.

A figure appeared like a ghost from the side, darting forward like lightning. Brushing past another Demon King, it came to a stop ten zhang away.

Pfft…

The sound of blood spraying rang out. A towering figure over twenty zhang tall appeared, covered in scales and with short horns on his head. Who else could it be but the foreign shaman?

At this moment, the foreign shaman held half a corpse in each hand, having torn a Demon King in two, blood and viscera gushing everywhere.

“Shaman Niu!” The last remaining Demon King felt his blood freeze, crying out in shock.

The five had come together. One vanished while investigating, likely dead. One was ambushed by Fei Li, his demon hearts taken—surely dead. Another was torn in half by Shaman Niu.

Only he was left alone!

His face changed rapidly. He glanced at Yang Kai, then at Fei Li, and cried out in anguish, “Why?”

Fei Li had clearly betrayed the demons, or he wouldn’t have ambushed his own companion and misled the others. Without his interference, even if the foreign shaman was strong, several Demon Kings together could have put up a fight. But now?

He just couldn’t understand why Fei Li would betray the demons. He was a Demon King, one of the best among them. What could the outsider offer to make him abandon his race’s dignity and position?

“Since you’ve guessed, why ask?” Fei Li replied coldly. With a squeeze, the demon hearts in his hand burst, and the Demon King he’d pierced through quickly lost all light in his eyes.

Fei Li shook his hand as if to fling off something filthy, and the Demon King’s corpse fell straight down.

“You are a disgrace to the demons!” The surviving Demon King’s eyes blazed with unprecedented fury, as if he wanted to burn Fei Li to ashes.

Between enemy and traitor, the latter is always more detestable.

The ghostly flames in Fei Li’s eyes flickered. Without a word, he lunged at him.

The two Demon Kings immediately clashed, Demonic Qi surging as they fought fiercely.

But Fei Li was much stronger. By Yang Kai’s assessment of Demon King strength, Fei Li was an Upper-Level Demon King, likely among the top even at that level, while his opponent was only a Middle-Level Demon King.

The gap of a single Stage was an unbridgeable chasm.

Just like the difference in strength between the Second and the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm.

The battle didn’t last long. Under Yang Kai’s cold gaze, Fei Li took only the time it takes to burn an incense stick to shatter his opponent’s body and demon hearts, leaving him beyond redemption.

Five Demon Kings had come together, but in the end, only Fei Li survived.

“My lord!” After the fierce battle, Fei Li showed no sign of fatigue, bowing his head to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai had already released the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, returning to his original appearance. Earlier, to ensure a fatal blow, he had to use some tricks—Demon Kings were not so easy to kill.

Casting a cold glance at him, Yang Kai said, “You should have told me about Mo Duo’s movements sooner!”

A month ago, if Yang Kai had received news of Mo Duo a day or two earlier, the Southern Barbarian Tribe might not have been wiped out, or at least wouldn’t have suffered such a devastating blow. But just that brief delay led to the destruction of all three hundred thousand of the Southern Barbarian Tribe.

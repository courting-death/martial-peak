## Chapter 2961 — Human Sovereign

Facts proved that his guess had been correct. When he cast the first Fire Snake Technique, he had indeed formed a trace of resonance with the Sacred Tree, producing some subtle connection, and afterward, his repeated use of it made this connection much more solid.

That was why he had obtained the Sacred Tree's shelter, and why he could display the "Sacred Art" known by Fu Shu and that goateed man.

The so-called Sacred Art was merely a Shamanic Art augmented by another kind of power.

Everyone in Human Sovereign City believed that the Sacred Tree sheltered the Imperial Family, so the Imperial Family was majestic and inviolable here, possessed many privileges, and could not be opposed by anyone. But in reality, it was probably because in Human Sovereign City, only the Imperial Family members were Shamans, so only they could establish resonance and connection with the Sacred Tree, and only they could mobilize the Sacred Tree's power.

Apart from the Imperial Family, others simply could not cultivate Shamanic Arts, or rather, they had been prevented from cultivating Shamanic Arts.

This should have been Human Sovereign City's means of control.

The only thing Yang Kai could not understand was what exactly that Sacred Tree was, and why it sheltered Shamans and could be used by Shamans.

All kinds of thoughts flashed through his mind, but Yang Kai could not find an answer for the time being unless he personally went to see that so-called Sacred Tree. But how could such a thing be easy? Since the Sacred Tree was so special and important, it was certainly under heavy protection by Human Sovereign City. For an outsider like him to see it would probably be harder than ascending to Heaven.

He focused his thoughts on the skinny man before him and smiled, "Let me guess. The First Prince and Third Prince have both appeared, so could Your Excellency be someone under the Second Prince? No, no, you should not be someone under the Second Prince, though I do not even know whether there is such a person as the Second Prince."

The skinny man's expression was indifferent as he replied faintly, "What makes you say that?"

His voice was just like his thin build, sounding very dry, as if he had not drunk a mouthful of water in dozens of years.

Yang Kai snorted, "Since ancient times, Imperial Families have had no kinship. There must be fierce open and hidden struggles between the princes. If you were truly someone under the Second Prince, you would probably wish the First Prince would die early and reincarnate sooner, so how could you be kind enough to save him? The Spiritual Pill you gave him just now was quite good."

“I see. You’re rather perceptive.” The skinny man gave a slight nod. “You’re right—I do not serve the Second Prince.”

"Then whose man are you?" Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and asked.

The skinny man said, "By order of the Human Sovereign, I invite you two into the palace!"

"Human Sovereign!" Yang Kai raised his brow, then turned his head toward Zhu Qing and said, "Madam, do you think we should go or not?"

Zhu Qing said, "You can decide."

Yang Kai nodded slightly and chuckled softly, "Let me ask first—the Human Sovereign won't hold it against me for striking the First Prince, will he?"

The skinny man replied, "As a subordinate, I dare not speculate on the Human Sovereign's thoughts. I merely follow orders. Please, both of you."

As he spoke, he turned sideways and made an inviting gesture.

Yang Kai said to Zhu Qing, "It seems we have to make this trip. It just so happens that I also have some questions I want to ask him."

"Then let us go," Zhu Qing nodded.

"Lead the way!" Yang Kai jerked his chin at that skinny man.

The skinny man said nothing. With a flicker of his figure, he flew off in a certain direction, and Yang Kai stepped forward to follow.

The little black dog ran out from some unknown place, barked for a while, then darted into Zhu Qing's arms.

During the fight just now, this little fellow had hidden until no trace of it could be found, but now that the battle had ended, it immediately appeared.

Human Sovereign City was extremely large, and that skinny man was clearly also a person of power and status, because Yang Kai had previously noticed that the clothes on his body bore a five-leaf mark.

A five-leaf mark was far inferior to those of the First Prince and Third Prince, but it was also a symbol of power and status. According to the goateed man's earlier explanation, only those with six leaves were qualified to mobilize the Sacred Tree's power and display the Sacred Art that crushed everything.

Five leaves and six leaves were only separated by a single step.

Yang Kai estimated that in Human Sovereign City, all true members of the Imperial Family should at least have six leaves. As for someone like the skinny man, who had profound cultivation but did not possess Imperial Family blood, five leaves was his peak, and there could be no more room for advancement.

He was a Third-Stage Emperor Realm master. Outside, he would absolutely have been the Sect Master of a top Sect or a powerful hegemon of some region, but in Human Sovereign City, he was only a servant driven by others.

In Human Sovereign City, whether one had leaves or not was used to distinguish commoners from the powerful, and the number of leaves was used to distinguish true nobles from the experts under their command. This was a means of control. Yang Kai did not agree with it, but he also had no desire to criticize it.

He only felt that the Human Sovereign appointed people according to kinship, and truly was petty enough that Yang Kai's first impression of him had worsened greatly.

The reason the Human Sovereign had summoned him was probably because he could communicate with the Sacred Tree and urge its power. His appearance and actions had shaken the foundation of the Human Sovereign's rule.

He was like a stone cast into a calm lake, splashing layer upon layer of ripples across the surface and attracting the attention of the overlord within the lake.

Yang Kai estimated that if he refused the Human Sovereign's summons, he would immediately face the rejection and pursuit of all Human Sovereign City, so no matter what, he was not allowed to refuse this summons. Since he could not refuse, he could only go along with the current.

However, this trip would certainly not be peaceful, so he had to be even more vigilant.

In Human Sovereign City, only those with power and status could fly through the air. Those without leaves, no matter how high their cultivation was, had to obediently walk on the ground.

The skinny man possessed five leaves, and moreover seemed to be an expert at the Human Sovereign's side, so naturally, he had the qualifications to fly. As he sped along the entire way, the people approaching from the front all retreated one after another.

Those people's status was inferior to the skinny man's.

Yang Kai soon noticed that those with six leaves were very rare. At the very least, he had not seen a single one while following along this way. Most of those powerful people had three leaves or fewer, and people above three leaves were extremely scarce.

With this comparison, the skinny man's five-leaf status appeared lofty and superior. The only difference between him and the First Prince and Third Prince was that he could not mobilize the Sacred Tree's power.

Because he had not been born into the Imperial Family, he could not cultivate Shamanic Arts and could not obtain the Sacred Tree's shelter.

The Imperial Palace was located at the very center of Human Sovereign City. Palaces stood in great numbers, occupying an extremely vast area. It was precisely the direction from which Yang Kai had previously sensed the source of the resonance, and also the direction from which the Sacred Tree provided power.

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and looked toward the direction where the Sacred Tree was located, only to see that the area was hazy, as if shrouded by some powerful Spirit Arrays, preventing anyone from spying on the situation within. The things that had previously blocked the probing of his Divine Sense should have been these Spirit Arrays.

Not long after, the skinny man descended and landed before a magnificent palace. The structure was extraordinarily grand, its ancient foundations bearing the weight of countless years. As they stood outside its gates, a desolate and primordial aura swept forth, as if the very passage of time itself whispered through its stones.

Yang Kai's expression became solemn.

The skinny man handed over the First Prince, whom he had been holding all this while, to a guard standing outside the hall, then turned back and said, "Please enter, you two. The Human Sovereign is waiting inside."

Yang Kai chuckled softly, "There shouldn't be any traps inside, right? You haven't hidden a few hundred assassins in there, just waiting for us to enter before launching a frenzied assault?"

The skinny man said expressionlessly, "Is this a joke from the outside world? It does not sound very funny."

Yang Kai shrugged, "That is because you have no sense of humor."

The skinny man replied, "The Human Sovereign is supreme and unparalleled. If he truly wished to deal with you, there would be no need for such trickery."

"We are all in the Emperor Realm, so what bullshit are you bragging about?" Yang Kai curled his lips and strode into the main hall.

The skinny man pointed at the little black dog in Zhu Qing's arms and said, "The beast stays behind."

"Woof!" The little black dog barked at the skinny man, clearly somewhat annoyed.

Zhu Qing shook her head and said, "Ah Wang will not stay behind."

"The Human Sovereign Hall is not a place just anyone can enter, let alone a beast. Please, you two, do not make things difficult for me." The skinny man took a step and blocked the way in front of the two of them.

Yang Kai's expression turned cold as he said, "You were the one who said the Human Sovereign summoned me and my wife. Now you block our way here—so give us a clear answer: are we entering or not?"

The skinny man opened his mouth, seemingly about to say something, when he suddenly tilted his ear and listened. Clearly, someone was transmitting a message to him.

A moment later, his figure flashed and he moved aside, clearing the path.

"Now that is the way to treat guests." Yang Kai smiled slightly and walked inward shoulder to shoulder with Zhu Qing.

From the outside, the interior of the Human Sovereign Hall had looked dim, as if nothing could be seen, but once they entered, the light inside was bright. A dozen or so enormous pillars stood all around, as if connecting Heaven and Earth, and Luminous Pearls adorned the hall one after another, their gentle light enveloping the entire main hall.

Pair after pair of eyes suddenly swept over from all around.

Yang Kai raised his brow, not having expected that there would actually be so many people in this Human Sovereign Hall. His gaze swept over them, and he soon discovered a familiar figure among the crowd. He smiled and greeted him, "Hello, Third Prince. We meet again so soon. I really must thank the Third Prince for taking care of me before."

The Third Prince coldly snorted, his eyes filled with disdain.

The people inside the main hall were all existences with six leaves or more. In other words, they were all true members of the Imperial Family, and they were all Great Shamans with considerable strength. Their ages varied, and there were both men and women.

There were not many with nine leaves, only two. Apart from the Third Prince, there was also a beautiful woman in her early thirties. The two of them stood at the very front of the main hall, only beneath the dragon throne, while the others were arranged in order to either side of them.

What slightly surprised Yang Kai was that this beautiful woman actually had the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm.

More than twenty Shamans had uneven cultivations, but among all the people in the hall, there was not a single Third-Stage Emperor Realm master.

Above them was a huge dragon throne, large enough that even if someone lay on it, it would not seem cramped. On the dragon throne, a somewhat plump half-old man sat upright. He did not look especially tall, and seated on the dragon throne, his round body seemed somewhat ridiculous.

However, the aura radiating from his body startled Yang Kai.

Before coming here, he had secretly speculated about the Human Sovereign's cultivation.

The special circumstances within the city had made him understand that here, as long as one could communicate with the Sacred Tree, mobilizing the Sacred Tree's power was one's greatest reliance, while one's own cultivation was instead secondary.

Therefore, he had vaguely guessed that the Human Sovereign's cultivation should not be too high, and should not have reached the level of a Great Emperor.

It was precisely because of this that he had dared to come boldly to this Human Sovereign Hall to meet the Human Sovereign.

If the other party had truly been a Great Emperor, he probably would not even have had a chance to resist.

Only after meeting the Human Sovereign did Yang Kai realize that he had greatly overestimated him. This Human Sovereign was not some Great Emperor, and was not even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm expert.

The aura radiating from his body was actually only at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, and moreover, it was extremely superficial.

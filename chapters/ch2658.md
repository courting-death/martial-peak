## Chapter 2658 — Stripped Bare

As he investigated along the way, even someone as experienced as Yang Kai was stunned by the immense wealth stored within Wenqing Sect.

And from today onward, this vast fortune would belong to him. It truly was a windfall from the heavens.

"There's more over there!" Ji Yao suddenly pointed with her hand.

Everyone looked in the direction she indicated and saw, at the deepest part of the stone cavern, three stone platforms. Each platform was shrouded in a barrier of light, with a box placed atop each one.

Preserved with such caution, it was clear that the items on these three stone platforms were anything but ordinary.

Yang Kai walked over with great interest, took out the token that had opened the vault earlier, and casually waved it over one of the stone platforms. A flash of mysterious light appeared, and the barrier of light opened in response.

Yang Kai directly picked up a long box placed on the stone platform.

"Let's see what treasure it is." The three Yao Kings crowded over, all examining it with great interest.

Curiosity was common to all; the Yao Kings were no exception.

Yang Kai weighed the long box in his hand with a strange expression and said, "It seems to be empty."

"No way?" Yingfei looked stunned, and Xie Wuwei and Xi Lei also wore expressions of disbelief. But they knew Yang Kai had no reason to lie to them.

Under everyone's gaze, Yang Kai opened the long box in his hand. Sure enough, it was completely empty inside.

"Why would an empty box be placed here so grandly!" Yingfei said angrily, clearly disappointed.

Yang Kai glanced at the length of the empty box and suddenly had a thought. He took something out from his Spatial Ring and placed it inside the box—it fit perfectly.

"Is this... the Imperial Treasure that Feng Xuan used before?" Xie Wuwei raised his eyebrows, recognizing the Imperial Treasure Yang Kai had taken out.

Yang Kai nodded. "The Wind and Thunder Divine Spear. It's said to be a relic of the Great Emperor!"

"So that's how it is. That means the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear was originally kept here, and Feng Xuan would only take it out when he needed it." Xie Wuwei looked suddenly enlightened.

"That should be it. After all, it's an ancestral relic. Even though Feng Xuan is the Sect Master of Wenqing Sect, he couldn't carry the Imperial Treasure with him at all times." Yang Kai nodded and casually put the box away.

Now it could be confirmed that the item originally kept in this empty box was the Wind and Thunder Divine Spear. As for the remaining two, since they were placed here with equal importance, their value surely wouldn't be much less.

Yang Kai was also curious about what was inside the other two boxes. Without hesitation, he opened the barriers on the other two stone platforms and picked up a box to take a look.

Yingfei was quick and picked up the other box to inspect it as well.

"Oh..." Yang Kai raised his eyebrows, his eyes lighting up. "This is..."

As soon as he opened the box in his hand, an extremely pure wave of Spiritual Qi rippled out, even causing the Emperor Yuan within his body to resonate. It seemed this pure Spiritual Qi would be greatly beneficial for cultivation.

"Source Crystals!" Xie Wuwei interjected. But his expression quickly changed. "No, these are Supreme-Grade Source Crystals!"

"Supreme-Grade Source Crystals?" Xi Lei's eyes widened as he looked at the box in Yang Kai's hand. Even the always-calm Ji Yao couldn't help but glance over.

"They really are Supreme-Grade Source Crystals!" Yang Kai grinned, reaching into the box and taking out a piece about the size of a thumb, crystal clear and sparkling like a gemstone.

These Source Crystals were smaller than Upper-Level Source Crystals, but the Spiritual Qi stored within was far beyond what Upper-Level Source Crystals could compare to.

Throughout the entire Star Realm, once a cultivator reached the Emperor Realm, Lower-Level and Middle-Level Source Crystals became almost useless. Most Emperor Realm cultivators relied on Upper-Level Source Crystals for cultivation, and even for trading precious items, Upper-Level Source Crystals were the standard currency.

But above Upper-Level Source Crystals, there existed an even better kind called Supreme-Grade Source Crystals.

Compared to Upper-Level Source Crystals, Supreme-Grade Source Crystals were as rare as phoenix feathers and unicorn horns—extremely hard to find.

Even among a hundred Source Crystal mines, one might not find even one or two. Every Supreme-Grade Source Crystal was a creation of heaven and earth.

According to legend, Supreme-Grade Source Crystals had many uses, but the most important for cultivators was breaking through bottlenecks. If a cultivator encountered a bottleneck in their cultivation, a Supreme-Grade Source Crystal was the best external aid, and unlike taking special pills, it carried no hidden dangers.

In the Star Realm, countless Emperor Realm cultivators spent centuries stuck at their bottlenecks, unable to make progress. Throughout history, who knew how many sought these legendary Supreme-Grade Source Crystals, hoping to take that final step forward.

But how could Supreme-Grade Source Crystals be so easily found? Those who searched painstakingly were destined to labor in vain.

Yet here, in Wenqing Sect's vault, there was actually a box of Supreme-Grade Source Crystals, and not just a few—there were seven, neatly arranged inside the box.

"What a treasure, what a treasure. It's just a pity..." Xi Lei sighed and shook his head.

The other two Yao Kings were also moved, nodding in agreement. "It really is a pity."

Although Supreme-Grade Source Crystals are invaluable for breaking through cultivation bottlenecks, the three of them were already Yao Kings—existences on par with the Third Stage Emperor Realm. For them, these Supreme-Grade Source Crystals were like tasteless chicken ribs: good, but ultimately of little use.

If they wished to reach even greater heights, their only hope lay in the Divine Spirit Origin and the bloodline of their ancient ancestors.

If not for this reason, with their strength and status, how could the three of them be so willing to follow Yang Kai’s lead?

Yang Kai understood what they regretted, but didn't point it out. He casually put the box away and looked at Yingfei. "What's in your box?"

Yingfei looked frustrated. "A cultivation technique!"

As he spoke, he handed the box to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai took it, glanced over it, and his expression instantly grew serious. He said in a low voice, "Supreme Sentiment Art!"

This was the very foundation of Wenqing Sect, an unparalleled technique created by the Great Emperor. The deeper one's feelings, the faster the cultivation. But for those who had the seed of emotion planted in them, it was a disaster.

Once affected by the Supreme Sentiment Art, those with the seed of emotion would never be able to free themselves. Not only would their cultivation stagnate, but they would also become utterly obedient to the one who practiced the art.

Back then, Feng Xi took a liking to Zi Yu of Ice Heart Valley. Though he truly loved her, his main goal was to use Zi Yu to enter the path of emotion. If the two really did unite by marriage, Zi Yu would likely end up as nothing more than Feng Xi's plaything, never able to touch the mysteries of the Emperor Realm.

The Supreme Sentiment Art was both righteous and evil. Though it was an unparalleled technique, Yang Kai held it in contempt.

He always felt this technique relied too much on external forces and couldn't lead one to glimpse the true Dao. He wondered how the Great Emperor had managed to reach the pinnacle with it.

Casually putting the technique away, Yang Kai turned and said, "See if there's anything you need. Take whatever you want."

The three Yao Kings looked at each other and laughed, cupping their fists. "Then we won't be polite."

Entering a treasure mountain, it would be unreasonable to leave empty-handed. Many of the treasures here even tempted the three Yao Kings.

The three immediately dispersed, clearly having already chosen their targets.

Yang Kai looked at Ji Yao. "You should look for something too."

Ji Yao shook her head. "No need. This time, Ice Heart Valley was saved entirely thanks to you. How could I take your things as well?"

"As you wish!" Yang Kai knew her temperament. Since she said she wouldn't take anything, no amount of persuasion would work, so he didn't waste his breath. He wandered around the vault, waving his hand and sweeping all of Wenqing Sect's tens of thousands of years of accumulation into the Small Xuan Realm.

In no time, more than half the vault's stores had disappeared.

At this moment, the three Yao Kings returned.

Yang Kai glanced at them and saw they hadn't taken much—each had a jade bottle for storing pills and an Imperial Treasure.

The yao couldn't refine pills, so although the Wild Ancient Land was rich in spiritual herbs and wondrous medicines, the yao there couldn't make full use of them. They usually just swallowed them whole, greatly wasting their medicinal effects.

Now, with so many pills here, the three Yao Kings naturally took some—better to have them just in case.

As for the Imperial Treasures, of course each took one, holding them with beaming smiles.

"Is this enough?" Yang Kai asked with a smile. "There are plenty of good things here."

"It's enough, it's enough!" Yingfei nodded repeatedly. "There are a lot of good things, but not many that are useful to us. What we've taken is plenty."

Xie Wuwei and Xi Lei nodded in agreement.

Yang Kai said no more and continued collecting things.

After a full incense stick of time, the vast underground cavern was left empty. All the wealth had been moved into the Small Xuan Realm by Yang Kai.

With these resources as a foundation, if Yang Kai ever wanted to establish his own sect in the future, he would have no worries about resources.

"I just thought of something," Ji Yao said, her brows slightly furrowed. "Yao Zhuo might have been able to advance to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm thanks to those Supreme-Grade Source Crystals."

Yang Kai nodded. "Makes sense!"

Yao Zhuo had previously been at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. It was too much of a coincidence for him to suddenly advance. If there was a reason, the Supreme-Grade Source Crystals were the most suspicious.

Ji Yao smiled slightly. "There's no point in investigating now. He's dead anyway."

"Let's go." Yang Kai called out, leading the way back along the original path.

After leaving the vault, the cliff face closed up again. Yang Kai took out the token and reopened the barrier.

Looking around, Yang Kai said, "I'm going to set up a Space Array here. Do as you please."

The three Yao Kings nodded and dispersed.

Ji Yao asked, "Are you going to connect Ice Heart Valley and Wenqing Sect?"

Yang Kai nodded. "Yes, if they're connected, it'll be much more convenient to travel between them in the future."

Since he planned to put down roots here, he naturally wanted to have more dealings with Ice Heart Valley. Now that there was a Space Array in Ice Heart Valley, setting up another one here would allow direct travel between the two.

In the past, Yang Kai had no fixed abode and never thought about these things. But now that he had plans, this arrangement was essential.

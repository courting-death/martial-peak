## Chapter 2688 — No Need to Thank Me

Crackle, crackle...

The faint sound sent chills down everyone’s spines. The old man hiding in Room Seven turned pale, his eyes wide with disbelief that Yang Kai possessed such overwhelming strength.

With a single punch, he had cracked the barrier array. If he threw a few more punches...

Damn that Qiyao Commerce Guild—what kind of garbage array did they set up? It was absolute trash! The old man cursed furiously in his heart.

Just as he reeled in shock, Yang Kai suddenly flipped his hand and summoned the Myriad Sword. The blade trembled, sword light soaring to the heavens, as he slashed forward with ruthless force.

Boom...

The might of that single sword seemed capable of shattering the very sky, turning the world upside down. All the Emperor Realm cultivators in the private rooms watched with grave expressions, realizing just how powerful Yang Kai truly was.

The barrier, already split by a crack, shattered completely under that sword, dissolving into countless motes of light and vanishing without a trace.

In an instant, Room Seven was left completely exposed and defenseless.

Yang Kai swept his sword again, breaking open the room. Inside, an old man with a pale face and terrified eyes stared back at him in shock.

Yang Kai’s figure flickered as he charged straight into Room Seven, hefting the broad, blade-like Myriad Sword onto his shoulder. Pointing forward, his aura surging, he barked, “Old bastard, hand over your head!”

Thud, thud, thud, thud...

The old man recoiled in terror, gritting his teeth as he shouted, “Friend, if you don’t act now, when will you?!”

As soon as he finished, he surged his Emperor Yuan and lunged at Yang Kai. Both hands formed claws, and behind him, the phantom of a great eagle flashed and vanished, unleashing a storm of claw shadows. The ferocious assault seemed intent on tearing Yang Kai open.

Though the old man could tell Yang Kai was not to be trifled with, he was confident in his own strength as a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator, and with the support of Room Thirteen, he wasn’t truly afraid. He attacked without mercy, clearly aiming to kill Yang Kai.

But just as he neared Yang Kai, a large foot suddenly kicked out at him. Emperor Yuan surged around that foot—though the move seemed simple, it bizarrely dispersed all his claw shadows and landed squarely on his face with uncanny precision.

Instinctively, the old man tried to dodge, only to find in horror that the space around him had solidified, leaving him unable to move at all.

With a thud, the old man was sent flying, crashing into the wall behind him before sliding limply to the floor. Stars danced before his eyes, his head spinning, and he felt as if his nose had been smashed flat.

When he opened his eyes again, he saw the broad blade of a longsword pressed against his neck, its touch icy cold. Yang Kai stood before him, a mocking sneer on his face, radiating utter disdain.

The old man was aghast. He hadn’t expected that, despite being a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator himself, he couldn’t even withstand a single exchange and was beaten down in a daze.

Was... was this guy really just a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator?

All around, the other private rooms echoed with the sound of people sucking in cold breaths, unable to comprehend what they had just witnessed.

They had seen the whole fight clearly: Yang Kai broke through the barrier array, then clashed head-on with the Cang Kong Sect Master. Yet somehow, the Cang Kong Sect Master was kicked to the ground and now had his life in Yang Kai’s hands.

It was all too unbelievable. If they hadn’t seen it with their own eyes, they would never have believed such a thing was possible.

The Cang Kong Sect Master was at least a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator. How could he be so utterly helpless against an opponent of the same realm?

“Got more helpers?” Yang Kai sneered, glancing meaningfully toward Room Thirteen.

At the door of Room Thirteen, the door stood wide open. A middle-aged man inside was poised as if ready to rush out, but his posture was frozen, as if he’d been turned to stone.

In front of him, Yingfei lounged against the doorframe, arms crossed, his hawk-like eyes watching the man with a faint smile.

There was no hostility, no aura, not even a hint of cultivation to be sensed. Yet this man made the middle-aged cultivator in Room Thirteen break out in a cold sweat, frozen in place as large beads of sweat rolled down his forehead.

For some reason, he felt that this person could kill him at any moment, so even though he heard the Cang Kong Sect Master’s call, he couldn’t take a single step forward.

“Go back. Young Master Yang will visit you soon,” Yingfei said, waving his hand lightly at the middle-aged man.

The man swallowed hard, quietly closed the door, and retreated, collapsing into a chair, his whole body trembling like a leaf.

Back in Room Seven, Yang Kai grinned at the Cang Kong Sect Master and said, “Looks like you’ve been abandoned.”

Of course, he knew exactly what had happened to the Emperor Realm cultivator in Room Thirteen. With Yingfei guarding the door, that man couldn’t escape, let alone come to help.

But when the Cang Kong Sect Master heard this, his face instantly turned ashen.

He had thought that, since he and Yang Kai were of the same realm and he had Room Thirteen’s support, he didn’t need to fear Yang Kai. But after just one exchange, he realized how wrong he’d been.

This First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator was completely unlike anyone he’d ever faced before.

What infuriated him even more was that Room Thirteen had gone completely silent.

Feeling the icy chill of the Myriad Sword at his throat, the Cang Kong Sect Master trembled and pleaded, “Eldest Young Master, spare me! This old man is willing to apologize and make amends. Please, Eldest Young Master, let me go!”

At the brink of life and death, the Cang Kong Sect Master no longer cared about pride or shame—he just wanted to survive. If he lost his life, nothing else mattered.

Yang Kai slowly shook his head and said, “A man is nothing without his word.”

As soon as he finished speaking, the Myriad Sword flashed a rainbow arc, slicing across the old man’s neck.

The old man’s eyes bulged in terror as he stared at Yang Kai, clutching his neck and gritting his teeth. “You...”

Before he could finish, blood spurted from his neck, the force so great that it sent his head flying.

“Ah...”

Cries of shock erupted from all around the room.

The Cang Kong Sect Master had brought several young disciples to the auction. When Yang Kai broke in earlier, they’d been scared stiff. Now, seeing their revered Sect Master beheaded in an instant, they screamed in terror, faces pale as paper. Two of the prettier female disciples even collapsed to the floor, their eyes unfocused.

Yang Kai ignored them. He casually grabbed the Cang Kong Sect Master’s head by the hair and gave it a look. As expected of an Emperor Realm cultivator—even after being beheaded, the Sect Master’s eyes glared at Yang Kai with undying rage, refusing to close. Only after a long moment did those eyes finally dim.

In the main hall, over a thousand cultivators were silent with fear.

Earlier, Yang Kai had taken out billions of Upper-Level Source Crystals, creating a cultivation environment they’d never experienced before. They’d been moved to tears, thinking he was a rare good man. But now, seeing him behead the Cang Kong Sect Master without hesitation, they realized just how terrifying Yang Kai truly was.

The Emperor Realm cultivators in the private rooms wore complicated expressions. Those who knew a bit about Yang Kai’s background shook their heads, feeling sorry for the old man.

After all, this was someone who had even beaten Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivator—right at the auction house entrance, no less. Others had run for their lives, yet this old man dared to provoke him. Wasn’t that just asking for death?

As for those Emperor Realm cultivators who didn’t know Yang Kai’s background, they couldn’t help but feel a chill. Most of them, like the Cang Kong Sect Master, were only First Stage Emperors. Seeing him killed so easily, they shuddered at the thought and silently vowed never to provoke this fiend—lest they be next.

“Since you wanted to offer your head for me to kick around, I’ll reluctantly accept,” Yang Kai said. With that, he tossed the old man’s head into the air and spun, sending it flying with a powerful kick.

With a whoosh, the Cang Kong Sect Master’s head shot toward the auction stage, soaring over Hua Qingsi’s head and smashing into the backstage area, exploding like a watermelon.

Backstage, Wu Ming was watching the commotion, only for Yang Kai to kick the head his way. He barely dodged, but was splattered with blood, his face turning dark as he fumed in silence.

After all this, Yang Kai hefted the Cang Kong Sect Master’s corpse, glanced at Room Thirteen, and grinned. “Where’s the friend who said he’d eat three catties of shit? Get ready, I’ve got something fresh for you.”

With that, he carried the corpse toward Room Thirteen.

Swish, swish, swish...

Everyone’s eyes turned to Room Thirteen, full of sympathy.

The Cang Kong Sect Master was dead, but at least it was quick. Yang Kai hadn’t tortured him. But the Emperor Realm cultivator in Room Thirteen was likely to suffer a fate worse than death.

Imagining the torment awaiting Room Thirteen, many people shivered, goosebumps rising all over.

No one doubted Yang Kai’s resolve or strength. The Cang Kong Sect Master had said he’d let Yang Kai use his head as a ball, and Yang Kai had done exactly that. Room Thirteen had said he’d eat three catties of shit—he’d probably have to follow through.

No one had ever seen an auction like this, or a person like this. He was utterly lawless.

The hall was silent. Divine Senses from all the private rooms swept toward Room Thirteen. Curiosity was universal—though they knew what was about to happen, they couldn’t help but want to see it for themselves.

Bang...

The door to Room Thirteen seemed to be kicked open, and a middle-aged man’s furious voice rang out: “What do you want?!”

“I’m just here to fulfill your wish. No need to thank me!”

“No, no, no, I didn’t say that! Don’t come looking for me!”

“A real man stands by his word. Why shrink back now?”

“Young Master, please, let me go! I was wrong, all right? It was my fault before. Please, be magnanimous and don’t hold it against me.”

“That won’t do. I’ve never heard such bold words in my life, so I have to see you follow through. Yao King, make sure he doesn’t resist!”

“Yes!”

“You... You can kill me, but you can’t humiliate me! I’ll fight you to the end!”

“You still want to fight in front of this King? Young Master Yang, three catties is too little—let’s make it thirty!”

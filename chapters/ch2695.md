## Chapter 2695 — A Single Misstep

Yang Kai shook his head and said, “Telling me this is pointless. Elder Tan, please tell Hua Qingsi directly. Since you’re using this formation, you should be able to contact her, right?”

Tan Junhao frowned at these words, looking a bit impatient as he replied, “Young friend Yang, I’m afraid this old man can't grant your request.”

“Why not?” Yang Kai’s brow furrowed.

Tan Junhao said, “That room is now isolated outside of my formation and is beyond my control. My voice can’t reach over there.”

If it had been just a moment ago, he could have done it, but with Gong Tai’s methods and formation now in place, that Room One was completely out of his control.

The abilities of a formation grandmaster can be glimpsed from this.

“Is that so?” Yang Kai said in surprise.

Yet inwardly, he was delighted. If that was the case, there was no need to worry about Hua Qingsi and the others’ safety. He had been concerned that, with the Heart-Controlling Gu in them, their lives would be at Tan Junhao’s mercy, leaving him constantly restrained. Now, hearing Tan Junhao say this, he was overjoyed.

Since the Room One was out of his control and even his voice couldn’t reach, it was likely impossible to use the Heart-Controlling Gu to threaten their lives.

It seemed that saving the Gong family head just now had earned him some good karma after all.

“Alright, I’ve fulfilled all your requests, young friend. Now it’s your turn to fulfill this old man’s wish.” Tan Junhao looked at Yang Kai as he spoke.

Yang Kai let out a heavy sigh and said helplessly, “I hope I haven’t misjudged you, Elder Tan.”

As he spoke, he seemed to resign himself, flicking his finger and sending the Xuan Realm Pearl toward Tan Junhao.

Tan Junhao’s eyes lit up and he hurriedly reached out to grab the Xuan Realm Pearl. He didn’t see Yang Kai do anything to it, which meant Yang Kai truly seemed willing to hand it over to redeem his mother-in-law.

Yet for some reason, just as he was about to grab the Xuan Realm Pearl, Tan Junhao suddenly felt a sense of unease.

At his level, he relied heavily on his instincts. Sensing something was wrong, he quickly activated his Emperor Yuan to protect himself, staring intently at the Xuan Realm Pearl to guard against any mishap.

At that moment, a black vortex suddenly appeared in front of the Xuan Realm Pearl, like a Void Passage connecting two worlds.

Immediately after, a massive fist slowly emerged from the black vortex. The fist was covered in bone-like spikes, fierce and terrifying, like sharp spears, and as it smashed forward, it came straight at him.

Tan Junhao’s face changed drastically, his eyes widening as he hurriedly raised both palms to meet the attack.

“Hahahaha!” A loud laugh rang out as Yang Kai followed right behind the Xuan Realm Pearl, closing in. “No matter how cunning you are, you’ll still have to drink my foot-washing water! Die, Old Dog Tan!”

In this life-and-death moment, a flash of insight struck Tan Junhao, and he suddenly recognized what that giant fist was. He cried out, “Stone Puppet!”

From the secrets revealed by Hua Qingsi, Tan Junhao knew that Yang Kai always kept a Stone Puppet hidden inside the Xuan Realm Pearl. It was said to be enormous, like a small mountain.

However, Hua Qingsi had also said that while the Stone Puppet was incredibly strong, its true strength was only comparable to a First Stage of the Emperor Realm cultivator. As long as one used speed and movement techniques, it wasn’t hard to deal with.

So Tan Junhao had never really taken the so-called Stone Puppet seriously. After all, he was a Third Stage Emperor. No matter how strong the Stone Puppet was, if it couldn’t hit him, it was useless.

When he first saw that huge, spiked fist, he hadn’t recognized it.

Now that he remembered, it was already too late.

What shocked him even more was that the power of this punch was far beyond what a First Stage of the Emperor Realm cultivator could unleash. The wild force on it was something even he, at the Third Stage, dared not take head-on. Not only that, but there seemed to be another kind of power mixed in with the Stone Puppet’s fist, a force that made his heart tremble.

Tan Junhao didn’t know that the Stone Puppet was refining Shi Huo’s source. Once it finished, it would become the Divine Spirit Shi Huo.

Although it hadn’t fully refined it yet, this strike already contained a trace of Divine Spirit power—enough to crush ordinary Emperor Yuan.

The Stone Puppet itself was still in deep sleep, refining Shi Huo’s source, and it would take at least a year or two to finish. But as Yang Kai’s Incarnation, it was connected to his mind, so Yang Kai could control it through the Xuan Realm Pearl to ambush Tan Junhao without issue.

This punch from the Xuan Realm Pearl was entirely under Yang Kai’s control.

The price paid was not small. Divine Sense poured out of Yang Kai’s Sea of Consciousness like a flood, making his head ache as if it would split open.

Already shocked by the power of the fist, Tan Junhao’s mind was thrown into chaos by Yang Kai’s shout. With a wave of his hands, countless five-colored blades gathered before him to meet the Incarnation’s fist, while he rapidly retreated.

Boom…

The five-colored blades shattered all at once, but the massive fist pressed forward, its sharp spikes piercing through Tan Junhao’s protective Emperor Yuan and stabbing into his body. The wild force poured in, sending him flying and spraying blood in midair, his fate unknown.

Meanwhile, Yang Kai charged straight toward Wu Ming.

Though he shouted for Old Dog Tan to die, Yang Kai had no intention of dealing with Tan Junhao. His top priority was to rescue Chi Yue; everything else could wait.

So when Yang Kai rushed up to Wu Ming, he hadn’t even reacted yet.

Their eyes met, and Wu Ming cried out, his face turning pale as he shouted, “Don’t come any closer! If you do, I’ll kill her!”

As he spoke, he flipped his wrist, producing an Emperor Artifact blade and slashing fiercely at Chi Yue.

“Condense!” Yang Kai roared, and the Laws of Space surged. The space around Wu Ming and Chi Yue instantly solidified.

The blade aimed at Chi Yue paused for a moment, but in that instant, Yang Kai had already snatched Chi Yue from Wu Ming’s grasp and kicked Wu Ming hard in the head.

With a miserable scream, Wu Ming was sent flying, tumbling through the air before crashing heavily to the ground, losing who knows how many teeth.

Strictly speaking, Wu Ming was a Second Stage Emperor Realm master. Even if he wasn’t a match for Yang Kai in a life-and-death struggle, he shouldn’t have been sent flying with a single blow. However, after being thoroughly beaten by Yang Kai earlier, he had long since developed a deep fear and wariness toward him. Now, seeing even his own master suffer such a heavy loss, how could he possibly have the will to fight Yang Kai? With his heart filled with dread, he naturally had no strength to resist.

“Senior, are you alright?” Yang Kai swept Chi Yue with his Divine Sense to check her condition.

Chi Yue’s face flushed slightly as she tightened her tattered clothes and shook her head. “I’m fine, just my cultivation is sealed.”

Yang Kai was about to say more when his expression suddenly changed. He looked up in a certain direction and gritted his teeth. “You really are hard to kill—worthy of being a Third Stage Emperor!”

Over there, a powerful aura suddenly spread out. In the darkness, Tan Junhao stepped forward, no longer looking like the immortal sage he had before. His face was twisted with hatred and rage, his eyes full of venom.

Several large holes had been punched through his body, his chest sunken in—clearly the result of the Incarnation’s punch.

Not just anyone could take a punch from the Incarnation.

Somehow, he had managed to stop the bleeding, but his black robe was soaked with blood.

“You little brat dare to humiliate me—you deserve to die!” Tan Junhao growled, his voice as cold as a wind from the Nine Nether Purgatory, sending chills down the spine.

As he spoke, his hands began to form a spell.

Suddenly, Chi Yue, standing beside Yang Kai, let out a muffled groan and a heart-wrenching scream.

Yang Kai was shocked and turned to look, only to see a bulge moving rapidly under the skin of Chi Yue’s neck, as if something was crawling inside.

“Heart-Controlling Gu!” Yang Kai exclaimed. Chi Yue’s current state was just like Hua Qingsi’s before—she had clearly been implanted with the Heart-Controlling Gu.

Now that Tan Junhao was enraged, he clearly intended to kill Chi Yue in revenge.

“Despicable!” Yang Kai cursed, reaching out to summon the Xuan Realm Pearl. With a thought, Chi Yue instantly vanished, sent into the Small Xuan Realm.

In the midst of casting the Heart-Controlling Gu, Tan Junhao frowned as he felt his connection to it suddenly break. He gritted his teeth and cursed, “Damn it!”

The Small Xuan Realm was a world of its own. Yang Kai had repeatedly gathered Origin Power to perfect its laws. The Heart-Controlling Gu was formidable, but with them in two separate worlds, Tan Junhao couldn’t control it unless he could break through the Small Xuan Realm’s laws and send his will inside.

Even a Great Emperor might not be able to do such a thing, let alone Tan Junhao.

“Haha!” Yang Kai laughed, his Divine Sense connecting to the Small Xuan Realm. He found that after being sent inside, Chi Yue looked much calmer, and the Heart-Controlling Gu in her neck quickly settled down. Knowing he’d done the right thing, he no longer had any worries.

Tan Junhao’s face was ashen, as if he’d lost his parents.

He had planned everything so well, but when actually facing Yang Kai, he suffered setback after setback, leaving him deeply frustrated.

“Damn Gong Tai!” he cursed inwardly. If he’d known Gong Tai was so capable, he would have taken him down earlier. Now, with Gong Tai’s formation stabilizing the Room One’s space, he was powerless to do anything to those inside.

Now, that room was like the Small Xuan Realm, an independent space beyond his control.

If not for that, there were still several hostages over there he could use to threaten Yang Kai at any time.

That’s also why he hadn’t interfered when Hua Qingsi sent the hostages over during the auction. He could always get them back, so it didn’t matter.

A single misstep, and the whole game was lost—life truly was like chess!

“Old Dog Tan, if you have any tricks left, bring them out now, so you won’t die with regrets!” Yang Kai taunted, feeling elated.

## Chapter 2679 — Dragon Clan

Zhu Qing took a deep breath and replied, "I saw the Ancestral Dragon Origin."

"Is that true?" That aged voice suddenly became incomparably agitated.

"Yes!" Zhu Qing nodded. "I saw it with my own eyes. There is absolutely no mistake."

"Where did you see it? When did you see it... Quickly, tell me everything you know."

Zhu Qing immediately recounted everything that happened after she encountered Yang Kai today, but she left out the part where Yang Kai had thoroughly taken advantage of her. After all, she was a woman, and saying such a thing would be too shameful to bear.

Recalling Yang Kai's rough treatment, Zhu Qing couldn't help but gnash her teeth in hatred. She had never imagined that her first contact with a man would happen under such circumstances.

After listening, the dragon eyes on the Dragon Talisman flickered uncertainly for a long while before saying, "To so easily suppress your Origin Qi, and for it to appear golden—this is indeed the Ancestral Dragon Origin!"

That aged voice was extremely excited, as if encountering something that thrilled him greatly. After a pause, he said, "Are you certain that person is a human?"

"I'm certain," Zhu Qing replied. "But he seems to have refined several Dragon Secret Treasures and knows a bit of crude Dragon Transformation Secret Techniques."

The aged voice said, "Since he has obtained the Ancestral Dragon Origin, it's not surprising that he could refine a few Dragon Secret Treasures."

"Elder, the Ancestral Dragon Origin is of great importance. Should I... bring him back to the island?" Zhu Qing asked.

The aged voice said, "Do you have that ability?"

Zhu Qing was at a loss for words.

Although Yang Kai was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, under normal circumstances Zhu Qing could have easily crushed him. However, the suppression from the Ancestral Dragon Origin within him was simply too overwhelming. Their earlier entanglement had already proven this—she hadn’t even managed to exert half her strength before Yang Kai subdued her. Against someone like him, unless an Elder personally intervened, no one could withstand the might of the Ancestral Dragon Origin.

"Then what should I do?" Zhu Qing's brows furrowed. The Ancestral Dragon Origin was unlike anything else; her clan had searched for it for tens of thousands of years, and now that it had finally been found, how could they ignore it?

The Dragon Talisman was silent for a long time before finally saying, "Follow him. Do your best to build a good relationship with him. When the opportunity is right, invite him to the island as a guest."

"Build a good relationship with him?" Zhu Qing was stunned and frowned. "He's just a mere human. What qualifications does he have..."

"The Ancestral Dragon Origin is his qualification." The aged voice interrupted Zhu Qing before she could finish, his tone brooking no argument and tinged with anger. "Are you questioning me?"

Zhu Qing's body trembled. She lowered her head and said, "Zhu Qing would not dare."

"Good," the aged voice said. "No matter the cost, you must bring him to the island."

"Then... my task..." Zhu Qing asked hesitantly.

"Continue investigating, but don't force it," the aged voice replied. Compared to the appearance of the Ancestral Dragon Origin, Zhu Qing's original task was no longer important. It was just a token, after all.

"Yes!" Zhu Qing nodded respectfully.

The light in the dragon eyes on the Dragon Talisman gradually dimmed, and that profound aura slowly faded away. Zhu Qing put it away again, frowning as she sat on the bedside, her mind in turmoil.

Having suffered such a huge loss at Yang Kai's hands this time, Zhu Qing felt she must find an opportunity to tear him to pieces and vent her hatred. But the Elder's order was to do her best to build a good relationship with him...

For a mere lowly human, must this young lady lower herself? What an utter humiliation!

The Yuelai Inn, the largest inn in Flowing Shadow City, was an industry of the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect.

Yang Kai soon returned here. After booking a few upper rooms, he went to the main hall downstairs to eat.

With his cultivation at this level, he no longer needed to eat. His body was a furnace, creation was the fuel, and Spiritual Qi of heaven and earth was the best food. Still, indulging occasionally was no harm.

Mainly, Ye Qinghan and Du Xian had been gloomy and worried all along. Yang Kai didn't know how to comfort them, so he brought them here to drown their sorrows in wine.

Jar after jar of strong liquor was brought up. Ye Qinghan accepted every cup, and before long, she was dead drunk, fast asleep in Du Xian's arms.

Du Xian cast an apologetic glance at Yang Kai, who waved his hand lightly, letting him take Ye Qinghan away to rest.

Only Yang Kai and Yingfei remained, drinking together.

After a few rounds, Yang Kai suddenly spoke: "Yao King, that woman said her name is Zhu Qing. Does that ring any bells?"

He suddenly remembered that when Zhu Qing introduced herself, she had said something strange. At the time, Yang Kai hadn't thought much of it, but now that he recalled it, that sentence seemed to have a deeper meaning.

Yet, no matter how much Yang Kai pondered, he couldn't figure it out. Yingfei, though he had lived in the Wild Ancient Land for a long time, had cultivated for many years and had far more experience than Yang Kai. Perhaps asking him would yield some clues.

At the very least, he needed to figure out Zhu Qing's background.

He didn't think that woman would let things go so easily. She had left with harsh words earlier.

Yingfei, who had just raised his wine cup, paused mid-motion. His eagle-like eyes widened as he stared at Yang Kai and asked, "You said... her name is Zhu Qing?"

"What is it?" Yang Kai looked at him suspiciously. Yingfei's reaction seemed a bit much.

Yingfei's expression shifted several times before a look of realization dawned on his face. He drained his cup in one gulp, then said solemnly, "On Dragon Isle in the Eastern Sea, the Great Elder of the Dragon Clan is named Zhu Yan."

Yang Kai's eyes narrowed, and he said sternly, "Yao King, are you saying... she's a member of the Dragon Clan?"

Yingfei said, "It's just a guess, I can't be sure. But earlier, Zhu Qing said that if I uttered that word again, she'd kill me. I thought about it afterward and couldn't figure out which word offended her, but now it seems there really was a word that angered her."

"Which word?" Yang Kai asked.

Yingfei said, "Does Young Master Yang remember what I said at the time?"

Yang Kai thought for a moment, then grinned and said, "You said I was handsome and outstanding, a man among..." His words suddenly stopped, and he exclaimed, "So that's it."

Yingfei also nodded and said, "If she really is of the Dragon Clan, then it all makes sense. The Dragons have always been proud and unruly, barely giving even other Divine Spirits a second glance—let alone a mere yao like me. My words praising Young Master Yang would certainly have angered her."

At this point, Yingfei's face turned pale, and he felt a bit of lingering fear. Although he was a Yao King, the other party was a Divine Spirit.

No wonder, no wonder her strength was so great. The power of the Dragon Clan could never be measured by common sense, and no wonder he couldn't see through her cultivation.

So Zhu Qing was actually a member of the Dragon Clan!

Thinking of this, he admired Yang Kai even more.

That was a member of the Dragon Clan! Just what method did Young Master Yang use to subdue her in such a short time, and even take such liberties? On the other hand, she didn't seem to resist much. If he hadn't brought Ye Qinghan and Du Xian over and interrupted, perhaps a little dragon would have been born by now.

How could such a thing happen?

The Dragon Clan has always been proud, considering themselves the rulers of all living beings. No other race was worthy of their regard—let alone the lowly Human Race.

To be so defiled by a human, how could Zhu Qing, a Dragon Clan member, endure it?

"Young Master Yang, could it be... you are also of the Dragon Clan?" Yingfei suddenly blurted out a ridiculous thought. Recalling all the unbelievable things about Yang Kai, it seemed only being of the Dragon Clan could explain them.

Only a stronger dragon can subdue a dragon!

Yang Kai laughed heartily, patting his chest. "A genuine human, no mistake!"

Yingfei gave a wry smile, not knowing whether to believe it or not.

"Excuse me... are you... Yang Kai... Lord Yang?"

At that moment, a middle-aged man suddenly approached the table, his head lowered, body trembling as he asked.

Yingfei glanced at him and saw that this fellow was only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, so he didn't pay much attention. Unexpectedly, under Yingfei's gaze, the man suddenly cried out, fell to his knees with a thud, and wailed, "Spare me, Lord, spare me! I'm just here to deliver a message!"

Although Yang Kai had been quite unrestrained in Flowing Shadow City earlier, he hadn't drawn much attention. But after Yingfei revealed his identity and cultivation, everyone was shocked to learn he was a genuine Yao King.

At this moment, the middle-aged man seemed far less afraid of Yang Kai than he was of Yingfei—his terror of Yingfei was palpable.

"No one wants your life!" Yang Kai glanced at him, lifted his chin slightly, and said, "What message are you delivering?"

The middle-aged man glanced at Yingfei, saw that he wasn't looking at him, and finally breathed a sigh of relief. He reached into his robe and took out a gilded invitation, handing it to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai took a look and grinned. "An invitation?"

The middle-aged man nodded repeatedly. "Exactly."

"Who sent you?" Yang Kai asked.

"It was..." The middle-aged man was about to answer, but his eyes suddenly became blank. He thought for a long time but couldn't remember, and instead his head throbbed painfully.

"Young Master Yang, someone has used a secret technique to erase that part of his memory," Yingfei said, his eyes flashing as he saw through the trick.

Yang Kai nodded slightly, waved his sleeve, and sent the middle-aged man out the door. Since someone had tampered with the messenger, there was no point in asking further—he probably wouldn't get anything useful.

Picking up the invitation and examining it carefully, Yang Kai couldn't help but raise his eyebrows.

"Young Master Yang, what kind of invitation is that?" Yingfei asked.

Yang Kai casually handed the invitation over, took a sip of wine, and said, "Looks like someone can't wait any longer."

"An invitation to an auction?" Yingfei saw the contents and frowned slightly.

They had come to Flowing Shadow City for this auction, but before they could make any moves, someone had already sent them an invitation, as if afraid they wouldn't attend.

"Young Master Yang, this is a trap, and a dangerous one."

Today, he had already revealed his identity and cultivation, yet the person hiding in the shadows still dared to send an invitation. Clearly, they had a way to deal with him, which was why they were so fearless.

"I'm not an idiot. How could I not see something so obvious?" Yang Kai smiled faintly.

"Should we call Old Bull and Wuwei as well?" Yingfei quietly transmitted his voice.

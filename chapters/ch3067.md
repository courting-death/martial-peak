## Chapter 3067 — I Only Came to Eat a Dragon

Fu Chi walked toward Li Jiao step by step, a vicious light flashing in his eyes. Although his figure swayed unsteadily, his condition was still far better than Li Jiao's.

He could already tell that Li Jiao was a spent force, someone he could flatten or knead into any shape with a single thought. There was no possibility of him putting up any resistance.

Having been wounded so severely by a Dragon Descendant, Fu Chi's fury had reached its peak. He decided to torture Li Jiao by the cruelest means possible and make him understand the consequences of offending him.

He did not care why Li Jiao had fought him so desperately. He could not be bothered to understand the thoughts of a mongrel.

Though the distance was only a dozen or so zhang, Fu Chi took fully half a cup of tea's time to cross it, stumbling with every step. It was clear that he was also in an extremely poor state.

Standing before Li Jiao, he assumed the posture of a victor and looked down at him as he knelt on the ground, gasping for breath. A chilling smile curled across Fu Chi's lips.

Li Jiao sighed, slowly closed his eyes, and bared his neck for the killing blow.

If he had possessed even the slightest strength to resist, he would not have sat there waiting for death. First, the Golden Lion Halberd, which was bound to his life, had suffered a tremendous loss of spirituality; then his own Dragon Pearl had cracked. Li Jiao's very foundation had been damaged, and he now lacked even the strength to move a finger.

Resigned to your fate? Fu Chi watched coldly, unable to stop sneering inwardly. So what if he had? Anyone who offended him had to pay the corresponding price.

Just as Li Jiao's eyelids were about to close, they suddenly snapped open again. He stared behind Fu Chi in astonishment as though he had seen something unbelievable, his eyes bulging wide.

Seeing this, Fu Chi could not help snorting coldly. "What trick are you trying to play when you're already at death's door?"

He thought Li Jiao was trying to distract him.

Yet the moment the words left his mouth, he sensed that something was wrong, because behind him... there truly seemed to be the aura of a living being.

"Who goes there?!" Every hair on Fu Chi's body stood on end as he whipped his head around. There, he saw a rat-faced, shifty-eyed fellow with a pointed mouth and monkey-like cheeks smiling at him with narrowed eyes. That smile made him feel extremely uncomfortable.

"It's you!" Fu Chi frowned, recognizing the newcomer.

He remembered that this fellow was the person in charge of constructing the palace. When he and Zhu Lie had gone to inspect it a few days ago, this fellow had accompanied them. Since little time had passed, Fu Chi naturally had not forgotten that ugly face or the groveling, servile manner he had found so contemptible at the time.

Before the Dragon Clan, inferior humans would forever be no more than ants.

"What are you doing here?" Fu Chi asked with a frown.

Everything about today's events seemed strange. First, a mongrel had followed him here and fought him, and now a human had appeared. Once bitten, twice shy, Fu Chi was naturally somewhat wary of this man.

Ordinarily, such a person would not even be qualified to receive a direct glance from him, but Fu Chi had suffered one heavy injury after another today, so he was naturally more cautious than usual.

He kept sensing something loaded with hidden meaning in this fellow's smile, which inevitably irritated him.

Faced with Fu Chi's question, Wu Chen did not answer. He merely smiled as he looked Li Jiao over, then examined Fu Chi from head to toe. Nodding repeatedly, he said, "Good, good, good!"

With each of those three words, his smile grew stranger. He even stuck out his tongue and licked his lips, like a vicious wolf hunting in winter that had spotted a delicious meal.

"Have you also come to make an enemy of my Dragon Clan?" Fu Chi stared at him coldly, a sneer curling across his lips.

He was no fool and could naturally tell that this sharp-featured fellow harbored ill intentions. However... this human was only a Third-Stage Emperor Realm cultivator. Even though Fu Chi was in poor condition and severely wounded, this man still could not possibly be his opponent.

Hearing this, Li Jiao's spirits lifted, and he looked toward Wu Chen with immense anticipation.

He had already fallen into despair. He had exhausted every means at his disposal but had still failed to bring Fu Chi down, and now his lamp had run dry. Yet if this Wu Chen had also come to deal with the Dragon Clan, perhaps Li Jiao could use him to avenge himself.

"Make an enemy of your Dragon Clan?" Wu Chen smiled and shook his head. "No, no, no... I didn't come to make an enemy of your Dragon Clan."

Fu Chi said disdainfully, "You wouldn't have the courage anyway."

Wu Chen nodded. "I only came to eat a dragon."

"Eat a dragon?" Fu Chi froze, clearly failing to understand what he meant, but he had no chance to think it over. The instant Wu Chen finished speaking, a brilliant light suddenly burst from his eyes, and a vortex abruptly formed within them.

When their gazes met, Fu Chi jolted. He felt an extremely strange devouring force emanating from those eyes, as if they intended to swallow his Divine Soul.

His expression changed drastically, and he hurriedly tried to look away, but it was already too late.

No matter how hard he tried, he could not move his eyes in the slightest. The peculiar power emanating from the other man's eyes grew stronger and more terrifying, forcing him to continue staring into them against his will. After only a few breaths, Fu Chi felt his Divine Soul begin to waver.

His back was instantly drenched in cold sweat. Then his vision went black and he lost consciousness, standing there blankly without moving.

Wu Chen stepped forward, and under Li Jiao's dumbfounded gaze, his entire person underwent a heaven-shaking transformation.

His small, thin body suddenly swelled, while his shifty-eyed, rat-faced, pointed-mouthed, monkey-cheeked appearance twisted and warped.

In the blink of an eye, Wu Chen transformed into a dignified-looking man with a haughty gaze and an unrivalled presence. He appeared to be middle-aged, and not a single trace of Wu Chen remained anywhere from head to toe.

What kind of Secret Art was this? Li Jiao was horrified, his eyes brimming with fear and unease.

With his eyesight, he had not noticed the slightest flaw before. He believed Yang Kai had not seen through it either, and the several hundred people who had constructed the palace alongside Wu Chen had probably all been fooled as well.

Could a Secret Art that completely changed one's appearance truly be so miraculous?

When the man's gaze casually swept over him, Li Jiao involuntarily felt a chill of terror.

He could not resist. There was no way to resist. If this man who had disguised himself as Wu Chen wanted to take his life, it would probably require no more than a snap of his fingers. This realization left Li Jiao trembling with fear, all the blood in his body nearly freezing.

After all, when facing Fu Chi, an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon, he had still been able to summon the courage and determination to risk his life in battle. Yet a single casual glance from this man had shaken his mind, which made it easy to imagine how terrifying the other party was.

"Hahahaha!" The man circled Fu Chi once and could not help laughing aloud. "After wearing out iron shoes in a fruitless search, I found it without any effort at all." As he finished speaking, he licked the corner of his mouth again, his gaze flickering as he stared at Fu Chi.

"May I ask... who exactly is Your Excellency?" Li Jiao asked, swallowing hard.

By now, he had realized that this fellow disguised as Wu Chen was definitely an extremely terrifying existence. He had likely assumed Wu Chen's identity and infiltrated Dragon Isle because he was plotting something, and judging from the present situation, his scheme was probably far from simple. It might very well send shockwaves through the entirety of Dragon Isle.

Wu Chen turned a deaf ear to him and continued staring at Fu Chi. "This Lord really must thank you for what happened here. Had you not severely wounded him, even this Lord would have needed to expend some effort to capture him. Tch, being weak is troublesome indeed."

Weak? Li Jiao looked horrified.

The strength Wu Chen currently displayed was anything but weak. With a mere thought, he had immobilized an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon. If such strength was considered weak, then what did Li Jiao's strength amount to?

"Stay there obediently. Once this Lord has dealt with this little dragon, I'll come back and give you a quick death!" Wu Chen reached out and pointed at him.

Li Jiao's entire body went cold.

When the other man thanked him earlier, he had hoped that the man might spare him. But after hearing those words, how could he not realize that he had no chance of surviving this ordeal either? So be it. Fu Chi would not meet a good end either, which could at least be considered vengeance for Lu Sanniang. He had not lived his life as a man in vain, and he could now die without regrets.

It was not that he had never considered fleeing, but before this man, he knew he had no possible chance of escape. Acting rashly would likely only make him die faster.

He gently closed his eyes, his expression perfectly calm.

But just then, an unexpected change occurred.

A strange aura suddenly surged from the man. The moment it appeared, the man's expression changed, and he shouted in a low voice, "Old Ghost, what are you trying to do?!"

He staggered several steps backward, his expression becoming incomparably solemn as the look on his face shifted without pause. At the same time, the two auras within him unexpectedly began clashing and entangling with each other, making his entire person seem exceedingly bizarre.

Li Jiao stared at him in astonishment, unable to understand what madness had seized him.

"Do not act presumptuously! If you dare ruin this Lord's plans, this Lord will never let you off! Don't think this Lord cannot devour you!" The man roared again as the conflict between the auras within him grew even more violent. The unrestrained energy spilling out made Li Jiao's skin ache.

Li Jiao grew even more confused. From his perspective, the man was plainly talking to himself, as if he had a split personality.

He had lived for many years and witnessed plenty of strange things, but this was the first time he had ever seen anything like this.

As the man spoke, his hands suddenly began to move. Even more strangely, each hand formed different seals with incredible speed, leaving Li Jiao dazzled and unable to follow them all.

Even Li Jiao was astonished by the complexity and profundity of those seals. They were simply unheard of and unseen before.

A moment later, the man suddenly whipped his head around. His face twisted hideously as he glared viciously at Li Jiao and shouted through gritted teeth, "Get out of here, now! Do you want to die?"

Li Jiao jumped in fright. Vaguely realizing something, he pointed at the man and said, "You..."

The man roared again, "Go find Yang Kai at once! Tell him..."

At this point, the man abruptly fell silent, then bellowed, "It won't help no matter whom you find! No one can stop this Lord today!"

Two Souls Sharing One Body! This man actually had two souls sharing one body!

Li Jiao finally understood why the man before him was behaving so strangely. There were actually two Divine Souls inside his body, which was why he talked to himself and contradicted himself, threatening to kill Li Jiao one moment and urging him to flee the next.

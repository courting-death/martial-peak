## Chapter 2963 — Mutual Questions

"Then it should be my turn now, yes?" Yang Kai said. His expression suddenly darkened. "Where are the husband and wife who returned to Human Sovereign City with me? Are they dead or alive?"

The Human Sovereign smiled. "Those are two questions."

Yang Kai shrugged. "The first one does not count."

"They are still alive. How did you enter Human Sovereign City through the Space Array without a Human Sovereign Seal?"

Yang Kai raised his hand and struck the empty space in front of him. That void suddenly collapsed inward, as if a piece had been moved from a complete puzzle. Although it quickly recovered, it could already be called a divine skill.

The Human Sovereign's expression suddenly brightened with understanding. "Ah, so that is how it is!"

Yang Kai said, "May I ask how Sir Human Sovereign intends to deal with that husband and wife?"

"Kill them!"

Yang Kai froze. "What kind of reasoning is that? Steward Liao died by my hand, and that husband and wife merely happened to be present. They did not even help me. Why kill them? Does Sir really hold human life in such little regard?"

The Human Sovereign looked at him with a beaming smile.

Yang Kai coughed lightly and said, "You ask first, you ask first."

The Human Sovereign's gaze suddenly turned to the little black dog in Zhu Qing's arms, and he said, "Is this beast a demon creature?"

"It is!" Yang Kai nodded and admitted it. "Although its bloodline is impure, it is indeed a demon creature without a doubt!"

As soon as these words came out, the expressions of the Imperial Family members in the hall changed drastically again. They all looked toward the little black dog in utter horror, and the gazes they turned toward Yang Kai and Zhu Qing also became extremely wary.

Yang Kai said, "Now you can answer my earlier question, yes?"

The Human Sovereign said, "Knowing the truth and not reporting it was precisely the reason they must die."

Yang Kai sneered repeatedly. "Sir truly has such awe-inspiring authority."

"It seems you care greatly for those two, yet as far as I know, you have not even bothered to ask what their names are, much less do you share any deep connection with them."

"I may not know their names, but I cannot slay the innocent and have them perish because of me. Such a thing would weigh heavily upon my conscience."

"You are rather kind-hearted." The Human Sovereign nodded lightly, his gaze like lightning as he suddenly looked toward Yang Kai. "Are you a member of the Demon Race?"

Yang Kai sneered, "If I were a member of the Demon Race, I would have overturned this Human Sovereign Temple of yours long ago. Would I still be standing here discussing nonsense with you?"

The Human Sovereign said, "I also do not think it likely, but in all things, one must account for unlikely possibilities. I simply needed to ask for clarification."

"Rest assured, my wife and I merely wandered into this place by accident. We have no designs on your Human Sovereign City." Yang Kai snorted with laughter. "What must be done for you to spare those two?"

The Human Sovereign said indifferently, "You may immediately leave Human Sovereign City and never take even half a step inside the city for all eternity. Then this Sovereign will release them."

Yang Kai curled his lip and said, "As long as I can find the way back, this Young Master will leave at once. Who would care to stay long in this Human Sovereign City of yours? If you want us to leave, that is also fine. Sir, please point out a clear path."

The Human Sovereign shook his head and said, "The Revolving World is easy to enter but difficult to leave. This world has existed for more than a hundred thousand years, and no one has ever been able to leave this place."

“If one can enter, one can naturally leave as well. It is only a matter of finding the right method. If Sir truly wishes for us to depart as soon as possible, then don’t keep your knowledge to yourself.”

The Human Sovereign slowly shook his head. "I truly do not know."

Yang Kai frowned. He felt that the Human Sovereign did not seem to be lying either, and continuing to press him would probably be meaningless. He could only sigh dejectedly and say, "I can roughly understand why Sir wants my wife and me to leave, but if there is no way out, where should my wife and I go after leaving?"

"Although the Revolving World is not large, it is not small either. With the strength you possess, even if you truly encounter the Demon Race, it is not certain that you would be unable to fight back. Aside from Human Sovereign City, you may go anywhere you wish."

Yang Kai curled his lip and said, "Sir, your words are far too irresponsible. If that is the case, then my wife and I would have to linger in this Human Sovereign City for quite some time."

The Human Sovereign narrowed his eyes and said, "If you do so, then this Sovereign can only exhaust every effort to go to war with you. I hope the two of you have already prepared yourselves to go together to the Yellow Springs."

As his words fell, an extremely dazzling ring of emerald light suddenly erupted from the surface of his body, and his entire person seemed to grow more than a hundred years younger in an instant. An aggressive pressure came crashing over.

Yang Kai frowned. Instinctively, he felt that this old fellow was somewhat difficult to provoke. At this moment, the Human Sovereign seemed to have established an unbreakable connection with the Sacred Tree, mobilizing unimaginable power and giving off an unmatched feeling.

Not only was he like this, but the princes and princesses around him were all the same. Every one of them eyed Yang Kai and Zhu Qing like prey.

Drawn by their auras, Zhu Qing's Dragon Yuan churned inside her body, and her expression turned icy.

Yang Kai patted her shoulder, signaling her to remain calm, and smiled. "Sir, do not be so nervous. It is not impossible for us to leave Human Sovereign City. As long as Sir agrees to two conditions, that will do."

The Human Sovereign lifted his chin and said, "Speak."

Yang Kai raised a finger and said, "Release those two unrelated people. They have no intention of defying you."

The Human Sovereign said, "Whether they die or not is not important. What matters is your decision."

Yang Kai smiled and said, "Then I will take that as your agreement. The second condition... I want to go take a look at the Sacred Tree!"

"Impudent!" the Third Prince and the Second Princess shouted in low voices at the same time, as if they had suffered some insult.

Yang Kai glanced at them coldly. "Is this request so difficult to accomplish? What is impudent about it?"

The Second Princess spoke up, "The Sacred Tree is the foundation of Human Sovereign City. Only the Imperial Family has ever been permitted to approach it. Who are you to make such an audacious demand?" She turned to face the Human Sovereign. "Royal Father, your daughter requests permission to execute this suspicious and treacherous person."

She had suffered a great loss at Yang Kai's hands earlier and had lost all face, so she had been looking for a chance to recover it. Now that the chance had come, she naturally wished she could tear away all pretenses with Yang Kai. Although this husband and wife appeared very strong, in Human Sovereign City, the Imperial Family was the strongest. As long as the Human Sovereign acted, no matter how capable they were, they would be unable to stir up any waves.

The Human Sovereign seemed not to hear Fu Yu's words. He merely looked at Yang Kai with a deep gaze. "What do you intend?"

Yang Kai shrugged. "Pure curiosity, nothing more. I only want to know what exactly the Sacred Tree is, and why it can provide such powerful strength."

The Human Sovereign said, "What if this Sovereign does not agree?"

Yang Kai chuckled coldly, his smile suddenly fading as his expression grew icy. "Then we shall tear off this facade and settle the matter with force. Let us see whether your Imperial Family is superior, or whether my wife and I are more capable! Either way, I have made up my mind—I will see the Sacred Tree."

"Shameless boasting!" Fu Yu flew into a rage. "Royal Father, your daughter is willing to take the vanguard. I will definitely let this boy know how powerful my Imperial Family is."

Yang Kai looked sideways at her. "Disgracing yourself in public. Does this Young Master need to teach you another lesson in manners?”

Fu Yu was so angry smoke seemed to pour from all seven orifices. Her chest heaved violently, and she wished she could attack Yang Kai right now and fight him to recover her face. Even losing would be better than being mocked by him like this, but since the Human Sovereign had not given the order, she truly did not dare act rashly.

"You must see it?" the Human Sovereign suddenly asked, looking at Yang Kai with a solemn expression.

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "I ask Sir to satisfy this small wish of mine."

The Human Sovereign closed his eyes and pondered. He seemed to find it very difficult to make a decision. Only after quite a while did he suddenly open his eyes and say, "Answer this Sovereign one more question."

Yang Kai nodded and said, "Please ask!"

The Human Sovereign narrowed his eyes slightly. "How are you able to use Sacred Arts? How can you mobilize the power of the Sacred Tree?"

Yang Kai chuckled and said, "Sir should already have guessed it, so why ask unnecessarily?"

"Although I have some guesses, I dare not believe them. As far as I know, the path of ancient Shamanic Arts in the Star Realm should have gone extinct long ago. How are you able to use them?"

"I learned one or two moves by chance, that is all. I have merely shown off my meager skills before an expert."

The Human Sovereign slowly shook his head. Clearly, he was not satisfied with this answer, but apart from this explanation, he could not think of a better one.

Just as Yang Kai had guessed earlier, the Sacred Tree was not protecting the Imperial Family, but rather protecting shamans. In the whole of Human Sovereign City, only the Imperial Family could borrow the Sacred Tree's power to cultivate Shamanic Arts and become Great Shamans, which was why the Imperial Family appeared so special in Human Sovereign City.

Over the past hundred thousand years or more, it was not as if no one from the Star Realm had entered Human Sovereign City. Every outsider who had entered Human Sovereign City had been recorded in detail, and the records of those outsiders who arrived one after another were practically the entire history of the Star Realm's development.

The changes in the World Laws had caused Shamanic Arts to gradually be replaced by the Martial Dao, and the path of ancient Shamanic Arts had indeed gone extinct long ago. Yet Yang Kai, this living example, had appeared right before his eyes, so the Human Sovereign had no choice but to take the matter seriously.

"This Sovereign can satisfy your request. I hope you can also keep your promise." In the end, the Human Sovereign still agreed.

He was not very willing to go to war with Yang Kai. Yang Kai could likewise use Sacred Arts and could likewise mobilize the power of the Sacred Tree. If they truly fought, the result would definitely be mutual destruction. Moreover, that woman was no ordinary person either. She seemed to be a legendary Divine Spirit, a Dragon Clan member!

Sacred Arts were enough to suppress ordinary Third-Stage Emperors, but they were still somewhat insufficient against a true Dragon.

The Second Princess seemed to want to say something more, but when the words reached her lips, she swallowed them again.

She knew the Human Sovereign had already made his decision, so saying anything now would be useless.

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "Sir, please rest assured. I have always kept my word and never gone back on what I say. Of course, I also hope Sir will not make things difficult for that husband and wife again, and will release them as soon as possible."

The Human Sovereign nodded slightly and said, "Follow me."

As his words fell, he had already drifted outside.

Yang Kai and Zhu Qing stepped forward and followed. The other princes and princesses also closely followed after them. The Second Princess looked at Yang Kai's back with an extremely venomous gaze, as if she wished she could hack him into ten thousand pieces, yet there was nothing she could do.

The Third Prince walked over to her side and transmitted his voice quietly, "Father has already made his decision, so why are you anxious?"

Fu Yu was shocked when she heard this. She stared at the Third Prince in surprise and replied, "Third Brother, you mean..."

The Third Prince passed by her, and his voice came over. "I did not say anything."

A strange light appeared in the Second Princess's eyes. Her heart grew excited, and she began to faintly look forward to what would happen.

The Sacred Tree was located in the deepest part of the palace, with experts guarding it on all sides. Even the guards in plain sight numbered more than ten, each of them in the Emperor Realm, to say nothing of those powerful auras hidden in the dark.

The Human Sovereign led the group over, and those guards all stepped forward to salute. After seeing Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, they revealed puzzled expressions, not knowing what backgrounds these two had.

## Chapter 3130 — You Should Just Die

Light flared in the darkness, and five figures lunged in.

Yang Kai suddenly raised his head and looked forward, meeting the eyes of a burly man.

As their eyes met, the burly man was startled. Yang Kai’s black-and-white eyes were perfectly clear, without the slightest sign of discomfort. With no time to think further, the great hammer in his hand had already smashed down toward Yang Kai’s head, stirring up a surge of wind and cloud.

A large hand that did not seem especially powerful suddenly caught the falling hammerhead, and that crushing momentum actually halted. The rebound even numbed the man's arms and split the webbing between his thumb and forefinger.

The burly man's eyes narrowed sharply. How was that possible? This fellow had just transmitted over from Dark Star. It was already absurd that he had woken so quickly, but he had actually caught a blow from his Secret Treasure with one hand. How could such a thing happen?

Before he could think further, the other four attacks had already slammed into the young man's body. Energy surged, the mine tunnel groaned, and broken stones came rattling down.

The four of them landed their blows, then instantly sprang back.

Among them, a scantily dressed woman looked at the burly man and sneered. "Barbarian, did this old woman squeeze you too hard a few days ago? That strike of yours had no strength at all. You're getting useless."

A short, skinny old man coughed a few times and said weakly, "Lust is a blade over the head. Take warning."

Another fair-faced man laughed. "Madam Silkworm, Barbarian is already no good. Care to try something different today?"

The beautiful woman called Madam Silkworm glanced at him and curled her lip. "Get lost. This old woman can't stand pretty boys like you."

A bone-jade folding fan suddenly appeared in the fair-faced man's hand. He snapped it open and gave it a leisurely wave, smiling. "I may be a little fair, but I'm not some pretty thing with no substance. How would Madam Silkworm know without trying?"

Madam Silkworm said coldly, "Even if you were a diamond drill, this old woman still wouldn't want you. This old woman likes burly, sturdy men like Barbarian." She suddenly turned her head in surprise. "Hey, Barbarian, what are you doing? Have you gone stupid?"

The burly man called Barbarian stood utterly rigid, cold sweat sliding down his forehead. He stared silently at Yang Kai, his eyes filled with terror and shock, as though he had seen a ghost.

He wasn't dead, you bastards! The other four had retreated after a single strike, so they had not paid close attention to Yang Kai at all. After all, with the five of them joining hands in an ambush, who could block them? They had simply assumed Yang Kai was dead for certain. Only Barbarian, who was standing right beside him, saw it clearly: the thunderous strike from his four companions had not harmed even half a hair on the man's head. It was as if they had only scratched the young man's itch. He had not even furrowed his brow, and now he was staring at Barbarian with eyes full of mockery.

Barbarian tried to pull back his Secret Treasure, only to find that it would not budge. The strength in the other man's hand actually made him feel powerless. When a subtle curve rose at the corner of Yang Kai's mouth, every hair on Barbarian's body stood on end. He immediately released the Secret Treasure and tried to retreat.

A hand reached out unhurriedly and clamped around his neck.

Barbarian instantly froze. The iron-tower-like man was lifted off the ground just like that, his feet dangling as he struggled.

Madam Silkworm and the other four cried out in alarm. Their faces changed, and they could hardly believe their eyes. Terror rose in their hearts and spread through their bodies, leaving them cold from head to toe.

They knew exactly what Barbarian's cultivation was like. His strength was no worse than theirs, yet now someone had grabbed him with one hand? Who was this man?

"You all seemed to be having such a good time chatting." Yang Kai's sinister voice echoed through the mine tunnel. Though his voice was deep and resonant, it chilled Madam Silkworm and the others to the bone.

The group exchanged glances. Almost without a word, they charged forward again at the same time. This time, they drove their strength to the limit, light blazing from their bodies and illuminating the mine tunnel until it was bright as day.

"Ha ha, good!" Yang Kai laughed. If these people fled, he would still have to chase them down. He had not expected them to rush at him on their own, saving him the trouble.

The large hand gripping Barbarian suddenly tightened, and Yang Kai swung him toward one side. He had actually turned Barbarian into a convenient human weapon.

There had been nothing in that direction, but when Barbarian's body came smashing down, the short, skinny old man somehow appeared in that exact spot at the worst possible moment, as if he had offered himself up.

A fierce wind howled. Horror flashed in the skinny old man's seemingly cloudy eyes, and he could only watch as Barbarian's sturdy body blotted out the space above his head.

Flesh tore and bones snapped. Barbarian and the skinny old man were smashed together into meat paste, blood spraying everywhere as the stench of blood surged through the air.

Yang Kai held the hammer Secret Treasure Barbarian had abandoned in his right hand. Without using any energy, he simply swept it out, a crude, straightforward blow fit to mow down an army. A force like overturned rivers and seas swept forth.

The fair-faced man was smashed in two on the spot. The defensive Secret Treasure on his body and his protective Saint Yuan were as fragile as paper, unable to withstand a single blow. His two halves exploded into a cloud of blood mist in midair, killing him instantly.

Madam Silkworm and the remaining man survived, but they coughed blood as they were thrown back by that violent vortex of force. They were heavily injured in an instant, and every protective Secret Treasure on them was ruined.

By the time they steadied themselves, fear had swallowed them body and soul.

Who? Who was he?

It was already impossible enough that he had transmitted over from Dark Star without the slightest discomfort, but with a casual movement, he had killed three First Stage Void Kings one after another as easily as slaughtering chickens and dogs. Was he even human? How could such a powerful existence possibly come from Dark Star?

Madam Silkworm was still dazed, but the other First Stage Void King had already shifted his footing. His figure turned into a streak of light, and he fled outside without looking back.

He was no match. If he did not run, he would die! He could not even see how high Yang Kai's cultivation was, so how could he dare remain?

The mine tunnel twisted and turned. After rounding several bends, he soon vanished from Yang Kai's sight.

Yang Kai slowly shook his head, disdain showing on his face. Before absolute power, any resistance was futile. He had understood this deeply after returning from the Star Realm.

The great hammer Secret Treasure spun in his hand. Yang Kai casually tossed it, and the great hammer flew out, smashing through several layers of rock wall. A scream rang out immediately after, then stopped short.

Madam Silkworm's face went bloodlessly pale. She clearly felt the aura of her last companion vanish from her senses. After hearing that scream, how could she not know what had happened to him?

Of the five First Stage Void Kings lying in ambush here, only she remained in the blink of an eye.

Yang Kai looked over, and Madam Silkworm dropped to her knees with a thud, trembling all over as she said hoarsely, "Spare me!"

Yang Kai frowned, walked forward, and pinched her smooth chin, making her raise her head to look at him. "You're called Madam Silkworm?"

Madam Silkworm forced out a smile, though it looked more like a grimace. "That is what they all call me."

Yang Kai nodded and examined her shapely figure. Fair-skinned and full-figured, she did rather suit the title Madam Silkworm. His fingers tightened slightly. "You're making this very difficult for me."

If Madam Silkworm had turned and fled, that would have been one thing. He could have killed her directly, just as he had dealt with the man before. But this woman had not run. Instead, she had knelt on the ground and begged him. Killing her now would feel rather unrighteous.

Pain showed on Madam Silkworm's face.

"I'm not very good at killing women," Yang Kai said, shaking his head.

Hearing this, Madam Silkworm's eyes lit up. "I beg Sir to show mercy and spare my life. I am willing to serve you like an ox or a horse in repayment."

Yang Kai shook his head. "Not interested."

Madam Silkworm immediately panicked. She looked up at Yang Kai and, seeing his gaze drift toward the fullness beneath her collar, hurriedly said, “I am willing to be a dog at Sir’s side. I only beg to live.”

As she spoke, she actually crawled down, lifted her plump hips high, stuck out her scarlet tongue, and put on a fawning look. She even barked twice. If she had a tail, she really would have been a bitch.

Yang Kai immediately found this somewhat hard to endure, and scenes from his wild night with Su Yan a few days ago involuntarily surfaced in his mind.

The madness of that night had left him the most excited he had been in years. Unfortunately, after that night, Su Yan had not let him touch her again, and he did not know whether she was angry or shy.

Now that Madam Silkworm had stirred up those memories, he suddenly felt his blood heat like a beast's, and his breathing grew heavy.

Su Yan was unwilling to play this game with him, but the woman before him would certainly give him whatever he wanted. Tsk, tsk. The thought was truly tempting.

Madam Silkworm watched his face carefully. Seeing Yang Kai's interest stirred, she was delighted and put even more effort into posing seductively. The barking from her mouth gradually turned into whimpers, like moans, like teasing. Faced with such a scene, most men would probably lose control.

Yang Kai took a deep breath and placed his hand on her shoulder. "I thought it over carefully. You should just die."

Power surged from his palm and poured into Madam Silkworm's body. It rampaged wildly through her, churning her internal organs, flesh, blood, and meridians into a complete mess. Madam Silkworm collapsed limply to the ground, fragments of organs and blood spilling from her mouth and nose, her eyes filled with disbelief.

Even at the moment of her death, she had never expected this man to have no tenderness toward women at all. He killed the moment he said he would kill. Her eyes remained wide as she fell, her vitality rapidly fading.

Yang Kai swept his gaze around and, after confirming that he had not missed any enemies, nodded slightly.

The Space Array he had spent effort, energy, and wealth arranging in his early years had now actually been reduced to a convenient tool for the cultivators of the Great Wilderness Star Domain. This was something he had never expected. Since these five had been guarding this place, they had certainly killed cultivators from Dark Star, perhaps even from Lingxiao Sect. How could Yang Kai leave them a path to live? That woman could wag her head and tail to beg for pity, or even strip herself bare, but she still would not escape death.

He truly was not good at killing women, but when it was time to kill, he would not go soft.

He collected the Spatial Rings from the dead and searched through them. Sure enough, he found a Teleportation Token. The things inside the Spatial Rings meant nothing to him, but this Teleportation Token had to be recovered.

He stepped back onto the Space Array and stood still. After sensing for a while, he detected several transmission directions. He sorted them out, confirmed the right one, activated the Laws, and vanished.

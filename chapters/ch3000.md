## Chapter 3000 — What Exactly Is Going On?

"Do not ask what you should not ask!" Yang Kai glanced at him coldly.

Li Jiao immediately fell silent as a cicada in winter.

"I need you to do me a favour!"

Li Jiao hurriedly said, "Palace Master Yang, there is no need to speak so formally. You saved this Li's life, so whatever your instructions may be, just say the word. This Li will certainly not refuse."

He put on an extremely forthright appearance while secretly feeling bitter. In truth, he had already guessed what Yang Kai wanted his help with. It was undoubtedly related to the grudge with Wind and Cloud Pavilion. Since they had already reached Changyun City, they clearly intended to fight their way into Wind and Cloud Pavilion's headquarters.

Inwardly, he thought, Hua Xing, oh Hua Xing, it is not that this Lord disregards our old friendship, but that I truly have no other choice.

"Take us into Wind and Cloud Pavilion."

Li Jiao puffed out his chest. "No problem. Leave this matter to this Li."

A cup of tea's time later, outside Wind and Cloud Pavilion, Li Jiao came flying through the air. He circulated his Emperor Yuan, wrapping it around Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er, and even the little black dog was not spared. The two people and one dog appeared to have been placed under some kind of restriction and obediently followed behind him.

"Is that Lord Li?"

An Elder of Wind and Cloud Pavilion stood behind the Grand Guardian Array and cupped his fists as he called out. He had clearly received word from Hua Xing and had therefore come here specifically to wait. At this moment, the Grand Guardian Array was active. Hua Xing had plainly not placed all his hopes on Li Jiao and had made other preparations as well.

"It is this Li!" Li Jiao replied in a deep voice.

"Those two are..." the Elder asked suspiciously.

"Open your damned eyes and look carefully. Is this woman the murderer who killed your Young Pavilion Master? This Lord has captured them and brought them back."

The Elder looked closely and was overjoyed. Nodding, he said, "Indeed, it is that wretched slut! Lord Li captured them with ease, as expected of your great ability. The Pavilion Master will certainly be delighted when he learns of this."

"Then what are you wasting words for? Hurry up and open the Array. This Lord will hand these two over to your Pavilion Master for punishment!"

"Yes, yes, yes." The Elder repeatedly assented, took out a token, circulated his Emperor Yuan, and formed several hand seals. A stream of light immediately shot from the token and struck the Grand Guardian Array.

A gap opened in the Grand Array, and Li Jiao darted inside.

"Brother Li has returned victorious, yet this Hua failed to welcome you from afar. Please forgive me." Hua Xing suddenly appeared in a strange manner and flew over from nearby. It was impossible to tell whether he had been hiding in the vicinity all along, waiting for Li Jiao's return.

"It was a trivial matter, hardly worth mentioning!" Li Jiao chuckled.

"Are you the father of that annoying fellow?" At that moment, Lin Yun'er suddenly frowned at Hua Xing and asked. Without waiting for him to answer, she continued to herself, "It must be you. The two of you look alike."

"You wretched slut!" Hua Xing turned to glare at Lin Yun'er, the fury in his heart blazing fiercely as murderous intent surged like a tide. He roared, "You dared to murder my son! Today, this Lord will... Hm?"

As he spoke, he suddenly sensed something was amiss. The young woman’s power was surging wildly—she showed no signs whatsoever of being restrained.

While he was still suspicious, Lin Yun'er suddenly shot forward like lightning. A pair of pitch-black gloves appeared over her delicate fists out of thin air, and she swung them in a wide arc, ruthlessly pounding toward him.

"Hiss..." Hua Xing was startled. Although the turn of events was sudden, it was not enough by itself to shock him so greatly. What astonished him was the strength the young girl erupted with at that instant.

That tiny fist seemed to contain might capable of destroying Heaven and Earth. If he were truly struck by such a fist, even as a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, he would definitely not have an easy time.

“Courting death!” Enraged, Hua Xing struck without hesitation. He had originally intended to torture the young woman, making her suffer every agony imaginable before killing her, but in this split-second exchange there was no time for such considerations. Emperor Yuan surged as he thrust out a palm toward Lin Yun’er.

A gigantic palm suddenly appeared in mid-air, condensed entirely from Emperor Yuan.

The palm even formed a hand seal in mid-air, raising its middle finger before pressing down toward Lin Yun'er. It drew in the surrounding Spiritual Qi, throwing it into turmoil as World Laws gathered densely around it, as though it could erase everything in existence.

This was undoubtedly an immensely powerful Secret Technique. Every Wind and Cloud Pavilion cultivator trembled beneath this palm and could not help feeling an impulse to kneel in worship. Even the nearby Grand Guardian Array rippled without end, as though it were about to be broken open.

The full-powered strike of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master naturally possessed extraordinary might.

Lin Yun'er's fist smashed into the palm print, and she involuntarily let out a muffled grunt. A tremendous force sent her plummeting downward like a kite with its string cut.

Although she was strong, her cultivation was ultimately somewhat lower. She was at a considerable disadvantage when facing a veteran expert like Hua Xing and was forced onto the back foot after a single exchange.

Pa...

A soft sound rang out as Yang Kai brought his hands together, then pulled them apart. As the power of the Laws of Space surged, an enormous Moon Blade shot upward.

The Moon Blade slashed out, collapsing the space in its wake.

A gash was instantly cut through the palm that was forming the hand seal, greatly diminishing its might.

The pressure bearing down on Lin Yun'er abruptly vanished. She tapped her feet against the empty air and shot through the opening like a bolt of lightning, arriving before Hua Xing in an instant. Her fists surged forward like twin Jiao emerging from the sea.

Hua Xing's expression changed. He opened his mouth and spat out an azure ruler. The ruler slowly spun, instantly producing countless ruler shadows that transformed into a barrier before him.

Hong, hong, hong...

Lin Yun'er hammered down with punch after punch, putting all her strength into every blow. The resulting explosions were deafening, and the violent force made everyone's expression change drastically.

Yet the ruler shadows were endless, firmly guarding Hua Xing and rendering all of Lin Yun'er's attacks fruitless.

"Slash!" Yang Kai repeatedly flicked his fingers, sending Moon Blades toward Hua Xing one after another.

Hua Xing frowned slightly and formed a series of hand seals. The ruler shadows before him suddenly flew out one after another to meet the Moon Blades, neutralizing each one with incomparable precision. Not only that, but an enormous ruler shadow also descended from the sky and ruthlessly slammed down toward Yang Kai.

"Hmph!" Li Jiao coldly snorted and raised a hand. The phantom of a Jiao surged upward and smashed the enormous ruler shadow to pieces.

Lin Yun'er gracefully retreated and returned to Yang Kai and Li Jiao's side, breathing slightly heavily.

That fierce barrage had consumed a great deal of her strength, yet she had actually failed to cause Hua Xing any real trouble. It was clear that the ruler-shaped Imperial Treasure was no ordinary artifact.

This exchange between them had occurred in the blink of an eye, so swiftly that the onlookers could scarcely follow it. The surrounding Wind and Cloud Pavilion cultivators had not even understood what was happening before both sides had already ceased fighting.

A fierce light flashed through Hua Xing's eyes as he gritted his teeth and growled, "Li Jiao! This Hua treated you sincerely, yet you colluded with outsiders to bully me?"

This man and woman appeared to have been captured by Li Jiao, but in truth, they had not been restrained at all. Otherwise, they could not possibly have exchanged moves with him. Coupled with Li Jiao's intervention just now, Hua Xing would truly have been an idiot if he still could not see that something was wrong.

No matter how he thought about it, he could not understand. Li Jiao had only been gone for a short while, so how had he changed from a helper into an enemy? This man and woman should not have possessed enough influence to make Li Jiao switch allegiance to them, should they?

What the hell was going on?

Although he did not understand, Hua Xing already bore a deep grudge against Li Jiao. If he had not trusted him so readily, he would never have allowed the enemy inside the Grand Guardian Array. This was a major matter concerning Wind and Cloud Pavilion's face.

It would not have mattered much if they had merely fought their way into Changyun City, but forcing their way into Wind and Cloud Pavilion's headquarters and entering the Grand Guardian Array was a blatant slap in the face. Wind and Cloud Pavilion had suffered no small number of such slaps recently, but the Grand Guardian Array had at least been its final fig leaf.

Now even that final fig leaf had been torn away. In the future, the people of Wind and Cloud Pavilion would likely be too ashamed to show their faces.

Hua Xing had originally hated Lin Yun'er to the bone because his son had died at her hands, but compared to what Li Jiao had done, he now hated Li Jiao's faithlessness and duplicity even more.

Faced with Hua Xing's accusation, Li Jiao's expression remained unchanged. He merely coldly snorted and said, "Brother Hua's aura is vigorous. It seems you have recovered quite well. Impressive, truly impressive. It has only been a short while, yet Brother Hua's hidden injuries have actually healed completely. This Li cannot help admiring you. I wonder what miraculous Spirit Pill or wonder medicine Brother Hua took to achieve such wondrous effects. If it is convenient, could you spare a few pills for this Li as well?"

He avoided the important matter and dwelled on the trivial. Too embarrassed to explain his own circumstances, he directly exposed how Hua Xing had deceived him about being injured in order to make him take action.

He could now also see that this old bastard Hua Xing had definitely harboured no good intentions when asking him to act. Thinking of it this way, much of the guilt in his heart immediately faded.

Hua Xing turned to look at Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er, then stared at Li Jiao and asked in a deep voice, "Brother Li, during your stay at my Wind and Cloud Pavilion, has this Hua ever failed to treat you properly?"

"No. Brother Hua made this Li feel completely at home," Li Jiao replied lightly.

Hua Xing said with a heartbroken expression, "Since that is so, why did you betray this Hua and even... even join forces with them?"

Li Jiao replied, "I had no other choice!"

Hua Xing stared at him in disbelief. "You had no other choice?"

This was what he understood least of all. Why would Li Jiao rather join forces with this man and woman than stand with him? Gritting his teeth, he demanded, "Can our hundred years of friendship truly be outweighed by the words 'I had no other choice'?"

Li Jiao scratched his cheek. "Friendship cannot be more important than my old life, can it?"

Hua Xing could not help staring blankly. "Just who exactly are they?"

He understood to some extent what kind of person Li Jiao was. Li Jiao was a descendant of the Dragon Clan, a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, and the Palace Master of a great force in the Northern Territory. Yet he had actually said such words, proving that Hua Xing's earlier guess had been correct. This man and woman truly possessed astonishing backgrounds, perhaps even enough to threaten Li Jiao's life!

Otherwise, Li Jiao would never have said such a thing.

Hua Xing's expression immediately became uncertain. Li Jiao's defection had already left him flustered. Wind and Cloud Pavilion was indeed extraordinary, but that depended on whom it was compared with. If this man and woman, together with Li Jiao, truly ran amok here, half of Wind and Cloud Pavilion would likely be destroyed.

Even if Wind and Cloud Pavilion ultimately won, it would certainly be a bitter victory.

At this thought, the desire to retreat arose in his heart. Silently, he sent Li Jiao a Divine Sense transmission. "Brother Li, tell me honestly. Are these two... connected to a Great Emperor?"

If that were truly the case, then even if he had to suffer a loss of face today, he could not allow the conflict to worsen. Wind and Cloud Pavilion could not afford to provoke anyone connected to a Great Emperor.

Li Jiao glanced at him, then looked at Yang Kai. Seeing that Yang Kai had no intention of stopping him, he casually replied, "No."

"No?" Hua Xing could not help staring in astonishment.

## Chapter 3094 — Seizing by Force

"By taking this Spiritual Pill, one can aspire to reach above the Void King Realm?" Yan Qing's throat went a little dry. How many years had it been since he had encountered such a thing?

He Yunxiang said, "It only gives one an opportunity. A Spiritual Pill is merely an external object; the foundation still lies in oneself."

There was one more thing she did not say. Taking this Spiritual Pill did indeed provide a chance to reach above the Void King Realm, but only if one took great quantities of them over a long period of time. A single Origin Condensing Pill could not possibly have much effect.

Transforming the Saint Yuan inside a Void King Realm cultivator's body was an extremely long process.

"How am I to know whether Madam's words are true or false?" Yan Qing frowned. He had lived to such an old age, after all, and had seen his share of storms. Once the initial shock passed, his mind settled, and suspicion naturally arose. If this so-called Origin Condensing Pill truly had such miraculous effects, why did this woman not take it herself? Why take it out here of all places to tempt him? Was she not afraid he would devour her until not even her bones were left?

"I have something to ask of the Yan Family. What benefit would there be in deceiving you?" He Yunxiang said solemnly.

Yan Qing shook his head. "Words alone are no proof. If that is truly the case, this old man would like to personally inspect this Spiritual Pill."

He Yunxiang smiled faintly. "Very well."

White light flashed before his eyes, and Yan Qing found himself holding that Origin Condensing Pill, still somewhat unable to believe it. Was there something wrong with this woman? Since such a Spiritual Pill was so precious, why had she handed it over to him so easily?

"Is Madam not afraid this old man will seize this Spiritual Pill?" Yan Qing asked.

"Would Elder do that?" He Yunxiang asked in surprise, like a little girl ignorant of worldly affairs. If he wanted to seize it, he first had to have the ability!

"I would not!" Yan Qing shook his head. The people of the world bustled to and fro, coming and going for profit. In the face of enormous benefits, what would people not do? He simply could not understand where this woman's confidence came from. No matter how he looked at it, there seemed to be something wrong with her head.

He Yunxiang pursed her lips and smiled. "If it is this Spiritual Pill, I wonder whether it can be exchanged for information on the way to the Ancestral Domain?"

Yan Qing let out a light breath. "It is enough, but only if this Spiritual Pill truly has the effect you described. Madam, please wait a moment. This old man needs to go find someone to examine it."

"Elder Yan Qing, please do as you wish." He Yunxiang gestured with her hand.

Since the matter was of great importance, Yan Qing did not dare be negligent. He quickly turned and entered the inner hall, soon disappearing from sight.

He Yunxiang turned her head to look at Yang Kai and secretly transmitted, "The fish has taken the bait."

Yang Kai nodded without a change in expression.

He Yunxiang said, "Actually, with Sir's strength, you could make the Yan Family hand over the method of reaching the Ancestral Domain without the slightest effort. Why go to all this trouble?"

The Origin Condensing Pill was nothing more than bait, and this place was the Yan Family's headquarters. For an outsider with no background to take out such a Spiritual Pill here was practically inviting the Yan Family to rob her. In the end, they would still be suppressing others with power, so would it not be better to make a direct move from the start and spare themselves all this trouble?

Gale's style was precisely that. If they took a fancy to something, they robbed it. As Gale's First Chief, He Yunxiang had carried out this principle to the fullest.

Yang Kai said, "I have my own plans."

In truth, he was somewhat embarrassed. Bullying the weak with strength was never pretty. With his Emperor Realm cultivation, bullying a group of Void King Realm and Origin Returning Realm cultivators always felt like an adult stealing things from children. But if the child was an unruly brat, then he needed to be properly disciplined.

At the summit of the Twin Peaks, inside a secret room.

Yan Luo, the Yan Family Head, pinched that Origin Condensing Pill between his fingers, his expression shifting again and again. Yan Qing stood to the side, licking his dry lips from time to time.

Only after a long while did he ask, "Family Head, does this Spiritual Pill truly have such miraculous effects?"

Yan Luo pondered for a while, then shook his head. "I cannot tell. Wait for the Masters to come and have a look."

Even the Family Head could not tell! Yan Qing was not startled but delighted, feeling more and more that this Origin Condensing Pill was extraordinary.

Before long, several people filed in one after another. Leaving aside their cultivations, each of them carried a rich pill fragrance, clearly people who dealt with herbs and Spiritual Pills year-round. Such people were naturally Alchemists.

These people were the Yan Family's most outstanding Alchemists, each one at the level of a Void King-Grade Alchemist.

After entering the secret room and greeting Yan Luo and Yan Qing, the black-robed old man in the lead said, "Family Head, you summoned us here in such haste. What is the matter?"

"Take a look at this Spiritual Pill." Yan Luo handed over the Spiritual Pill in his hand.

The black-robed old man accepted it, and the others also looked over. Their initial casualness vanished at once, and each of them widened his eyes and cried out in shock, "This..."

"What Spiritual Pill is this?"

"Quickly, let me see."

"Do not snatch it!"

A group of highly respected Alchemy Grandmasters instantly lost all bearing, like country bumpkins who had never seen the world. The shock in their eyes nearly spilled out of their sockets.

After quite some time, the black-robed old man in the lead looked at Yan Luo in excitement. "Family Head, may I ask where this Spiritual Pill came from?"

"Can you tell what grade it is?" Yan Luo answered with a question.

"We cannot tell, but with our methods, we absolutely could not refine such a Spiritual Pill."

The other Alchemists all nodded deeply in agreement. This Spiritual Pill had already exceeded their knowledge and experience. Forget refining it; they could not even identify its components.

The answer was already obvious. This suddenly appearing Origin Condensing Pill could not be identified by an expert at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm or by Void King-Grade Alchemists. The only possibility was that this Spiritual Pill belonged to the level above the Void King Realm!

The black-robed old man still wanted to ask something, but Yan Luo had already waved his hand and said, "Withdraw."

Although the Alchemy Grandmasters were reluctant, they did not dare show the slightest disobedience. They bowed and withdrew, their gazes full of reluctance as they looked at that Spiritual Pill before leaving.

After they left, Yan Luo said indifferently, "You said just now that the woman called He Yunxiang casually handed this Spiritual Pill to you?"

"Yes."

"It seems she has more than just this one Spiritual Pill in her hands." Yan Luo suddenly laughed.

Seeing this, Yan Qing immediately understood what idea he was entertaining. Had he himself not had the same thought? Their ideas simply coincided without prior discussion. He hesitated and said, "But someone who can casually take out such a thing cannot be easy to deal with. What if behind her..."

"The possibility is not great. Besides, do not forget what place this is." Yan Luo glanced at him indifferently.

Yan Qing's expression changed, and he nodded. "I understand."

He turned and walked out of the secret room.

A woman with no background at all, even if she were killed or captured on Twin Peaks, probably no one would know, right?

"It has begun, Sir." In the main hall, He Yunxiang looked at Yang Kai with a beaming smile. Bursts of sound from people cutting through the air reached her ears, and powerful auras awakened like sleeping dragons, surrounding the main hall layer upon layer.

A person with a grave expression strode out from the inner hall. It was Yan Qing, who had left not long ago.

He Yunxiang said, "Elder Yan Qing, what was the result of the appraisal?"

Yan Qing shouted sternly, "Audacious woman! You actually dared to use a fake Spiritual Pill to deceive my Yan Family. You truly have eaten a bear's heart and a leopard's gall."

He Yunxiang turned pale with fright. "Elder Yan Qing, what are you saying? What fake Spiritual Pill?"

Yan Qing coldly snorted. "Although this old man does not know where you found that thing, that Spiritual Pill has been verified by several of my Yan Family's Void King-Grade Alchemists and is nothing but a useless object. Yet you actually wanted to use it in exchange for my Yan Family's secrets? Do you truly think my Yan Family is so easy to bully?"

He Yunxiang looked utterly terrified, her face pale as she said, "How could it possibly be a fake Spiritual Pill? That really is a Spiritual Pill useful for advancing above the Void King Realm. How would I dare deceive the Yan Family?" Inwardly, she found it amusing and understood somewhat why Yang Kai had gone to all this trouble earlier.

Even when the Yan Family moved against her, they first had to find a pretext. This was probably what people meant by marching out with a righteous cause.

How could that compare to Gale's carefree way of killing when they wanted to kill and robbing when they wanted to rob? It seemed she would still need some time to adapt to the outside world.

"Could the eyesight of several Void King-Grade Alchemists of my Yan Family be inferior to yours?" Yan Qing coldly snorted.

He Yunxiang sighed and said, "Since that is the case, then please, Elder Yan Qing, return that Spiritual Pill to me. We can treat it as though I never made that proposal."

Yan Qing sneered again and again. "You still want that Spiritual Pill? This old man threw it away long ago."

He Yunxiang's eyes reddened. "How can your Yan Family do this? That was a very precious Spiritual Pill. It is one thing if you failed to recognize its worth, but why did you throw it away?" She looked so wronged and helpless that anyone who saw her would feel a surge of pity.

Was this woman truly stupid or pretending to be stupid? Even Yan Qing could not quite understand. Could it be that she had always been hiding somewhere cultivating and had only recently come out of retreat? Otherwise, with such cultivation, how could she be so ignorant of worldly affairs?

Right, that had to be it! Otherwise, how could he possibly never have heard the name of a cultivator at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm? Perhaps she had obtained some incredible inheritance and had been in secluded cultivation somewhere all this time.

Once this thought occurred to him, Yan Qing was deeply shaken, and the one remaining trace of apprehension vanished without a trace.

"Forget it, forget it. It was only one Spiritual Pill. If it was thrown away, then so be it. Since the Yan Family is so unreasonable, I will take my leave." He Yunxiang stood up, wiped the corners of her eyes, and looked heartbroken and dejected.

"Is my Yan Family a place you can come to and leave from as you please?" Yan Qing shouted sternly.

He Yunxiang said in panic, "Then what do you want?"

"You deceived my Yan Family and vainly attempted to steal the Yan Family's secrets. If we let you escape, where would my Yan Family’s face remain? Madam He, obediently stay behind.”

He Yunxiang stumbled back a few steps and said in shock, "You want to detain me?"

Since the pretense had already been torn away, Yan Qing no longer hid anything. He said in a deep voice, "Madam He, if I were you, I would surrender obediently. Madam is delicate and weak, and you seem to have only advanced recently. If you were to fight this old man, it would not be good if you were injured somewhere." He had absolute confidence in taking down He Yunxiang. By comparison, he was an established master at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, so how could He Yunxiang compare? All the arrangements were merely to guard against the unexpected.

He Yunxiang cursed angrily, "You are despicable and shameless! How can the Yan Family have someone like you? I demand to see Old Family Head Yan and ask him to uphold justice for me."

Yan Qing sneered. "The Family Head will not see you."

He Yunxiang opened her mouth and was about to say something more when Yang Kai suddenly stood up.

The moment he moved, He Yunxiang immediately closed her mouth and said no more. Yan Qing glanced at him coldly and frowned slightly, then raised his hand and swept it toward Yang Kai. The movement was relaxed and effortless, as if he were driving away a mosquito.

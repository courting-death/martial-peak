## Chapter 2906 — The Weather Is Nice

Yang Kai let out a muffled groan as blood streamed from his seven orifices. His already weak face turned as pale as paper, and his body swayed unsteadily, as if he might collapse at any moment.

The young man was startled for a moment and hurriedly withdrew his divine abilities.

He bore no ill will toward Yang Kai; there were simply matters he had to investigate. Yet, given his status, it was inconvenient to look into them directly, so he had come here to intercept him.

He had thought it would be a simple matter to handle with his means, but he hadn’t expected to force Yang Kai into such a desperate counterattack—and with considerable effect.

This left him rather embarrassed.

Having failed with one move, he could not strike again. His figure flickered, retreating a hundred zhang, as if he had never moved at all.

A hundred zhang away, Yang Kai glared furiously. With no regard for the consequences, he frantically urged the power of the Warm Spirit Lotus, mobilizing his Divine Soul to guard his Sea of Consciousness. He would never hand over any secrets from his Sea of Consciousness unless the other party defeated him.

The other party absolutely had the strength to do so, but Yang Kai would not yield easily.

The scene was like a rabbit facing a hawk swooping down from the sky. Even if it bared its teeth, it would be of little use—the hawk would still snatch the rabbit into the air, soar high, and then drop it to its death.

The young man gazed at Yang Kai for a while, then suddenly sighed softly, lifted his head to the sky, and seemed to speak to the air: “The weather is nice.”

After saying this, he took a step forward and suddenly vanished from Yang Kai’s sight, leaving as imperceptibly as he had arrived.

Yang Kai maintained his vigilant stance for quite some time, his Divine Sense sweeping back and forth across a thousand zhang radius. Only after a long while did he confirm the man had truly left. Only then did he finally let out a heavy breath and lower his guard.

An unimaginable wave of exhaustion swept over him like a tide, causing him to stagger in midair, nearly plummeting headfirst.

Forcing himself to steady his body, he wiped the blood from his face and frowned.

That person had suddenly rushed over to intercept him, even using a Divine Ability to probe his secrets, yet in the end, it amounted to much thunder but little rain, leaving Yang Kai both puzzled and surprised.

Did he really come all this way just to say, “The weather is nice”?

That was clearly impossible.

After pondering for a while, the gloom on Yang Kai’s face gradually faded, and he began to smile. The more he smiled, the more carefree he felt, until the last layer of worry and burden in his heart was completely lifted.

He more or less understood what that man meant now.

He had taken the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape from the Fifth Stage of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda. As the master of the Star God Palace and the one who controlled the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, the Great Emperor must have noticed something, but could not see everything clearly. That was why he needed to investigate.

However, Yang Kai was not a disciple of the Star God Palace. He had only entered the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda because of a previous agreement, so neither the Great Emperor nor the elders could demand that Yang Kai voluntarily explain his experiences inside.

If Yang Kai were just an ordinary Emperor Realm cultivator, the Star God Palace would likely have many secret techniques to make him unwittingly reveal the existence of the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape. But now, Yang Kai still held the status of an elder of the Qingyang Shrine. As the overlord sect and guardian of the Southern Territory, the Star God Palace naturally could not bully the weak or abuse its power.

That was why the Great Emperor acted personally, and not within the Star God Palace, but outside it, to show that this had nothing to do with the Star God Palace.

He had not revealed his identity for the same reason. Yang Kai had guessed as much but did not expose it. In a way, the two shared a tacit understanding, turning a big issue into a small one, and a small one into nothing.

The matter was settled. Even though the Great Emperor had acted personally, he still failed to obtain any useful information—or rather, he did not force Yang Kai. After this, the Star God Palace would not pursue what Yang Kai had experienced or gained inside the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda.

In other words, no matter what Yang Kai took from the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda, the Star God Palace and Radiant Moon Great Emperor could only accept it, whether they liked it or not.

It was precisely because he figured this out that Yang Kai’s mood suddenly became incomparably bright.

Previously, he had always been somewhat worried. Taking something without permission was theft, so even if he brought the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape back to Lingxiao Palace, he could only use it in secret, never openly. But after what just happened, the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape could now be revealed to the world. Even if the Star God Palace found out later, they could say nothing.

Thinking it over, Yang Kai felt he should actually thank the Great Emperor. Moreover, the Great Emperor seemed to be a decent person. With his means and ability, he could have forcibly broken through Yang Kai’s Sea of Consciousness defenses, but he had only probed lightly and then withdrew, fully displaying the bearing of an expert.

This left Yang Kai with a good impression of him.

That line about the weather being nice was probably the Great Emperor’s helpless and awkward remark.

As he laughed, Yang Kai suddenly couldn’t laugh anymore. He wiped under his nose, and blood flowed profusely, seemingly unstoppable. The consumption of his Divine Soul during this period had far exceeded his limits. If not for the protection of the Warm Spirit Lotus, he would have become an idiot by now.

…

What should have been a journey of only a few days took Yang Kai more than a dozen days of unsteady flight before he finally arrived.

The ruins of the Thousand Leaves Sect were still full of devastation, with broken walls and rubble everywhere. Yang Kai, familiar with the place, made his way to the valley, activated the Laws of Space, and entered the Emperor Heaven Valley of the Thousand Leaves Sect.

The Emperor Heaven Valley was an independent little world. At present, aside from Yang Kai, no one else in the world could freely enter or leave it.

Yet at this very moment, there was a figure near the entrance to the Emperor Heaven Valley.

That person possessed Emperor Realm cultivation and was drawing something on a patch of open ground—sometimes frowning, sometimes widening his eyes, sometimes breaking into a smile—his expressions ever-changing.

He seemed completely absorbed in his own world, not even noticing when Yang Kai entered.

It was only when Yang Kai gave a light cough that he snapped out of his reverie. First, he frowned in displeasure, his face showing clear anger at having his thoughts interrupted. But when he saw Yang Kai, all displeasure vanished, and he greeted him with a cheerful smile: “Palace Master!”

“Mm.” Yang Kai nodded lightly and casually glanced at what he had drawn on the ground, discovering that it was related to a Space Array. Someone who could immerse himself in the world of formations at all times and also appear here—within the entire Lingxiao Palace, there was no one else but the Chief Formation Master, Nanmen Dajun.

When Yang Kai had previously sent Bian Yuqing back to the Northern Territory’s Lingxiao Palace, he had asked her to tell Nanmen Dajun to come over. It seemed the Imperial-Grade Formation Master had been here for some time, just waiting for Yang Kai, and had been passing the time with his own research.

“Did you bring the item?” Yang Kai asked.

Nanmen Dajun was momentarily stunned, then quickly reacted and handed over a jade slip to Yang Kai. “Palace Master, I’ve sealed all the information about that formation inside. You can look it over on the way. But let me remind you, this formation is quite sinister and vicious. Whoever set it up is definitely not a good person—be careful.”

“I’ll keep that in mind.” Yang Kai nodded.

Nanmen Dajun then took out another item. It was a smooth spindle-shaped object, radiating a brilliant light and looking extraordinary. He handed it to Yang Kai.

“What’s this?” Yang Kai asked curiously.

Nanmen Dajun chuckled, “Didn’t you ask Hou Yu to forge a flying Secret Treasure for you? This is it.”

“So fast?” Yang Kai exclaimed in surprise as he received the flying Secret Treasure, sensing it briefly before showing a shocked expression. “An Imperial-Grade Secret Treasure?”

He hadn’t brought Hou Yu back to Lingxiao Palace that long ago. Although he had assigned her the task of forging a flying Secret Treasure, he thought it would take at least several years to make a good one. But it had only been three or four months, not even half a year!

Nanmen Dajun smiled, “Little Hou’s character isn’t great, but her artifact forging skills are solid. And now that our Lingxiao Palace isn’t lacking in materials, with so many resources thrown at her, how could she not produce something? She said there’s still room for improvement. When you have time, let her upgrade it for you.”

“She’s not thinking of running away, is she?” Yang Kai smiled faintly, his mind slowly immersing into the flying Secret Treasure, a trace of fondness appearing on his face.

Nanmen Dajun let out a chuckle and said with schadenfreude, “The three Yao Kings are taking turns guarding her. If she dares to run, they’ll break her legs. The Yao Kings aren’t the easygoing type.” The Yao Kings’ respect was reserved only for Yang Kai, or rather, for Heaven’s Punisher and the Divine Spirit Origin. Toward others, they showed no courtesy at all. Whether Imperial-Grade Formation Masters, Imperial-Grade Pill Masters, or Imperial-Grade Artifact Masters, in the eyes of the Yao Kings they were no more than dogshit. Among the yao, there were no such distinctions—whoever possessed greater strength ruled over all.

With the three Yao Kings taking turns guarding Hou Yu, she could forget about ever escaping from Lingxiao Palace.

The image of Hou Yu’s miserable expression flashed through Yang Kai’s mind, making him want to laugh. Still, Hou Yu truly lived up to her reputation as Master Hou. Even before refining the Secret Treasure, Yang Kai could tell it was extraordinary. If he could fully refine it, it should have excellent capabilities.

But for Yang Kai, the other functions of this Secret Treasure didn’t matter. The most important thing was flight—as long as it was fast enough, nothing else was important.

“Did Hou Yu give it a name?” Yang Kai asked.

Nanmen Dajun thought for a moment before replying, “Flowing Cloud Shuttle. Mm, she said that if Palace Master doesn’t like it, you can name it whatever you wish—it’s just a name, after all.”

“Flowing Cloud Shuttle it is,” Yang Kai said with a slight smile.

As they spoke, Yang Kai suddenly took out an item and handed it to Nanmen Dajun. “Take this back.”

“What’s this?” Nanmen Dajun asked curiously as he received it. He found that it looked like a rouge box, but it was clearly not an ordinary one. An ancient aura flowed from the box, showing it had a long history—it was a fine Secret Treasure.

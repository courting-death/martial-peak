## Chapter 2678 — Thank You for Coming, Please Visit Again

“Young Master Yang won’t be in any danger, will he? That woman seems like trouble.” Ye Qinghan asked worriedly, recalling the scene in front of the City Lord’s mansion just now, still feeling a lingering fear.

“Since Brother Yang took the initiative to lure her out, he must be confident.” Although Du Xian said this, he wasn’t certain either, and could only look to Yingfei for confirmation.

Yingfei said nothing, but a trace of hidden worry appeared on his cold, stern face.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, so they couldn’t sense how terrifying that woman was. But as a Yao King, Yingfei could clearly feel the strength of that young woman.

What she showed in front of the City Lord’s mansion was just the tip of the iceberg. If she went all out, even Yingfei wasn’t sure he could defeat her.

Yang Kai was impressive, but in the end, he was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.

So even though Yang Kai told them not to follow, Yingfei still chased after him, just in case.

Following the trail of Yang Kai’s lingering aura, they didn’t lose their way. After entering the mountains, all they saw along the way were traces left by a fierce battle—mountains split, trees toppled, the air thick with the scent of blood. It was clear how dangerous the fight had been.

Yingfei couldn’t help but furrow his brows, silently praying that nothing had happened to Yang Kai.

Sniffing the air to confirm the direction, Yingfei’s Yao Yuan surged, wrapping Ye Qinghan and Du Xian as he sped off.

After a short while, Du Xian suddenly pointed ahead and said, “Over there.”

Even though it was dozens of li away, Du Xian still keenly spotted two figures on the ground. He didn’t know what the situation was, but there were no sounds of fighting—rather, the two seemed tightly entangled, as if locked in a struggle.

Yingfei’s eyes narrowed and he immediately flew in that direction.

Dozens of li passed in an instant.

“Young Master Yang!” Yingfei called out softly, but as soon as he spoke, his mouth fell open in shock, staring blankly at the ground ahead.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were also stunned, their expressions growing increasingly odd.

Along the way, they’d seen traces of fierce battle everywhere. Though they hadn’t witnessed it themselves, they could imagine how tough it had been. They’d thought Yang Kai was in dire straits, but the scene before them left them speechless.

On the ground, Yang Kai and the young woman were locked in a tight embrace. Yang Kai’s upper body was nearly bare, pressing the woman beneath him, his hands roaming freely. The woman, in turn, clung to Yang Kai, her ten fingers raking bloody lines down his back. The scene was violent to the extreme, and the ambiguous moans drifting over made one’s mouth go dry.

“This…” Yingfei was speechless, only wanting to ask, what the hell is going on here!

“See no evil, see no evil!” Du Xian hurriedly covered Ye Qinghan’s eyes, but he himself watched with relish, eyes glued to the scene.

Ye Qinghan’s face flushed red with embarrassment. She angrily slapped Du Xian’s hand away, twisted his ear, and yanked his head to the side.

Du Xian could only give a dry, awkward laugh.

“Let’s go!” As expected of a Yao King, Yingfei quickly regained his composure and whispered to Du Xian and Ye Qinghan, then immediately turned and led them back the way they came.

Although he didn’t know exactly what had happened, Yang Kai was clearly in no danger. In fact, it looked like he’d conquered that unruly young woman. Yingfei was too embarrassed to stick around and interrupt Yang Kai’s good fortune.

Unfortunately, the arrival of the three still caught the attention of Yang Kai and Zhu Qing.

Immersed in the eruption of her instincts and the suppression of her bloodline, Zhu Qing suddenly regained a sliver of clarity and bit down hard.

“Hiss…” Yang Kai sucked in a cold breath. That bite nearly took off his tongue, and he quickly lifted his head.

Zhu Qing pushed him away with both hands, putting distance between them. She gasped for breath, her face flushed, still lost in the afterglow. In a cold voice, she said, “That’s enough!”

Yang Kai found her reaction amusing and smiled, “Not enough.”

Zhu Qing snapped, “Don’t push your luck!” She glared at his hand, still kneading her chest, and barked, “Take your filthy hand away!”

Yang Kai shrugged, then gave a final hard squeeze, making Zhu Qing frown, before rolling off her.

Having been interrupted by Yingfei and the others, he couldn’t keep going even if he wanted to. He wasn’t that shameless. Besides, after all that, he’d already vented much of his pent-up desire. Though he still felt uncomfortable, seeing Zhu Qing’s clear reluctance, he couldn’t bring himself to force her.

Freed from Yang Kai’s grasp, Zhu Qing immediately leapt away, putting thirty zhang between them. She glared at him, her beautiful eyes full of complicated emotions.

She’d only come to trouble Yang Kai because she sensed he’d refined something he shouldn't have. She never expected to end up suffering such a loss herself.

The feeling of being wantonly taken advantage of still lingered, making her whole body uncomfortable.

“First time?” Yang Kai tore off his tattered clothes, took out a fresh set, and asked without looking up.

Yang Kai couldn’t help but feel puzzled. Zhu Qing had clearly cultivated some kind of charm technique, and she’d even gone so far as to throw herself into the tiger’s den—so how could this possibly be her first time? Yet that awkward, inexperienced feeling was unmistakably genuine.

Vaguely, Yang Kai felt Zhu Qing hadn’t actually cultivated any charm technique. Thinking back on his own loss of control, it didn’t seem to be the result of any technique, but rather a bodily instinct.

Was he really so desperate for a woman? That couldn’t be it.

“What?” Zhu Qing was momentarily stunned, but quickly understood Yang Kai’s meaning. Her cheeks, still flushed, turned even redder as she snapped, “None of your damn business.”

“Should I take responsibility?” Yang Kai waggled his eyebrows at her, quietly circulating his Emperor Yuan to calm himself down.

“Get lost!” Zhu Qing shouted angrily. “This isn’t over. Just you wait!” With that, she turned and flew off.

Yang Kai chuckled and said, “Thank you for coming. Please visit again next time.”

Zhu Qing stumbled mid-flight, nearly falling out of the sky. Behind her, Yang Kai’s unrestrained laughter rang out, making her feel even more mortified.

After flying for a while, she saw the Yao King standing with two humans. When he saw her, the Yao King even smiled and nodded in greeting, looking very friendly.

In the past, she would have punched him without hesitation. But after being caught in such an embarrassing situation, Zhu Qing had no face to meet Yingfei, and with a flick of her body, she turned into a stream of light and vanished.

“Young Master Yang is quite the lucky man,” Yingfei said, raising his brows as he watched Zhu Qing fly away.

That woman was indeed overbearing, but also truly powerful. Who knew what shocking means Young Master Yang had used to subdue her in such a short time? As a fellow man, Yingfei couldn’t help but admire Yang Kai to the utmost.

“Indeed!” Du Xian agreed wholeheartedly.

Ye Qinghan shot him a sidelong glance and snorted, “Why do I smell a hint of jealousy?”

Du Xian was startled and quickly flattered her, “No way! A woman that rough isn’t even fit to carry your shoes, Junior Sister. In Senior Brother’s eyes, only you are truly unique in all the world.”

Ye Qinghan shivered, rubbing her arms. “Enough, enough, you’re giving me goosebumps.”

Du Xian’s mouth twitched as he shook his head and sighed.

“Didn’t I tell you not to come over?” Yang Kai’s figure suddenly appeared in front of Yingfei.

Yingfei gave an embarrassed laugh. “We only came to back you up, Young Master Yang. If I’d known, I wouldn’t have come no matter what. Please forgive us.”

If he hadn’t brought Ye Qinghan and Du Xian and interrupted, the two might not have separated so soon. Maybe things would have gone all the way, and that woman would have become Yang Kai’s.

Yingfei felt quite guilty.

“It’s fine.” Yang Kai waved his hand, gazing into the void ahead.

Today was truly bizarre. Zhu Qing had suddenly appeared out of nowhere, and somehow, he’d ended up tangled with her in a whirlwind of passion—like a fleeting spring dream, leaving no trace behind.

He licked his lips, as if a faint fragrance still lingered.

“Young Master Yang, that woman… who is she?” Yingfei asked cautiously.

Such a powerful woman must have quite the background. If she could be won over, she’d be a great asset.

“No idea.” Yang Kai shook his head. In truth, even after getting so intimate with Zhu Qing, he only knew her name—nothing about her origins.

“Young Master Yang, you’re truly remarkable!” Yingfei said admiringly. To conquer someone without even knowing her background—he was a true paragon among men.

“Haha, can’t help it, I’m just too charming,” Yang Kai laughed.

Ye Qinghan immediately shot him a look of disdain.

……

In Flowing Shadow City, inside an inn.

Zhu Qing hurried back to her room in the inn at Flowing Shadow City, activated the protective wards, and collapsed onto the bed in a daze. Not only had her plan failed, she’d ended up suffering a humiliating loss—her greatest disgrace, a stain she’d carry for life. Yet, when she remembered that the other party possessed the Ancestral Dragon Origin, she couldn’t help but feel a bit relieved.

That innate suppression was something she simply couldn’t resist.

Fortunately, she hadn’t completely lost herself in that Origin aura and had managed to escape.

But this matter was too serious for her to handle on her own.

After a moment’s thought, Zhu Qing took a deep breath to calm herself, then took out a jade pendant from her Spatial Ring. The pendant was shaped like a dragon, lifelike, as if a giant dragon was leaping out.

Zhu Qing put the pendant to her mouth, bit her fingertip, and a drop of blood instantly welled up. Strangely, the blood burned like a flame.

The blood seeped into the jade pendant and quickly disappeared, making the whole pendant glow red.

Suddenly, the dragon eyes on the pendant moved, and a deep, ocean-like aura descended.

“Elder!” Zhu Qing’s expression turned solemn as she greeted respectfully.

“News?” An aged voice came from the pendant, full of authority. Even someone as strong as Zhu Qing stiffened at the sound.

“No.” Zhu Qing quickly replied.

“If not, why use the Dragon Talisman?”

Zhu Qing said, “I saw the Ancestral Dragon Origin.”

At these words, the pendant’s light flashed, and the aged voice asked urgently, “What did you say?”

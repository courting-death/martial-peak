## Chapter 2931 — Entering the Divine Roaming Mirror Again

Gulp...

The sound of many Emperor Realm cultivators swallowing echoed, each of them taking up a defensive posture. For a moment, sorrow welled up in their hearts, and they simply had no idea what to do.

Gong Yue had just broken open the sealed land for suppressing demons hidden somewhere on Spirit Sword Peak, releasing countless strands of Demonic Intent. Suddenly, another fellow who called himself the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor appeared. The upheaval in Qingyang Shrine on this day was greater than all the changes of the past several thousand years combined.

"Where is Wen Zishan? What did you do to him?" Gao Xueting asked in a trembling voice.

Wen Zishan should be assisting the Mortal Great Emperor in separating his Divine Soul. At this moment, Wu Kuang had taken over Duan Hongchen's body and emerged, but Wen Zishan was nowhere to be seen, leaving Gao Xueting unable to help but worry.

"Hmph, who is Wen Zishan? Just now, this Lord crushed quite a few little bugs. I don't know if he was among them."

Gao Xueting's pretty face turned pale, and she nearly collapsed to the ground.

Wu Kuang swept his blazing gaze over the Emperor Realm cultivators present, pointing his finger repeatedly as he counted, "One, two, three..."

Yang Kai said coldly, "Wu Kuang, what are you counting?"

Wu Kuang burst out laughing, "Not bad, not bad. There are actually nine little Emperor Realm bugs. This Lord has just escaped and is still a bit weak. You all are just right to replenish my strength. Although your abilities are lacking, at least you are plump enough."

As soon as these words were spoken, everyone's expression changed drastically.

Everyone present had heard of the Heaven Devouring Battle Art's fearsome reputation and knew exactly what this evil technique was. It was a technique that devoured everything. It was said that the number of Star Domains destroyed by the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor was countless. Wherever he went in those years, all the Cultivation Stars in the lower Star Realms became barren dead stars, all vitality devoured clean, leaving nothing but desolation and extinction.

So, upon hearing Wu Kuang say this, everyone immediately understood his intentions.

No one cared about the Demonic Intent that had rushed out anymore. All the Emperor Realm cultivators were on high alert, secretly circulating their Emperor Yuan, ready to strike at any moment.

Wu Kuang frowned, then suddenly grinned, "Just joking, don't be so nervous. This Lord has just escaped and is in a good mood, not planning to start a massacre. So I'll let you off this time."

No one responded to him. No one knew what this guy really meant, and no one dared to speak lightly, afraid of angering him.

Wu Kuang sneered, apparently finding it rather boring. He turned to glance into the distance, reached out and grabbed at the void. His movements were casual, but when he opened his hand, a trace of black light appeared in his palm.

No one knew where he had grabbed that black light from.

"An interesting thing." The Demonic Intent that all the Emperor Realm cultivators avoided was now in Wu Kuang's palm, but he examined it with great interest. That black light obediently stayed in place, showing no sign of corroding him, as if it were a dead thing. "I was able to escape thanks to your help. Hm, let me lend you a hand in return."

As he finished speaking, he raised his hand and pointed somewhere.

The void trembled, as if something had been pierced through.

That was the sound of the Grand Guardian Array being broken. Qiu Ran had secretly ordered the array to be activated just now, but in no time at all, it had been breached.

In an instant, Qingyang Shrine was like a balloon with a hole in it. All the Demonic Intent trapped in the array found an outlet and rushed out through the breach, disappearing without a trace.

Yang Kai and the others could only watch helplessly, powerless to do anything.

Wu Kuang swept his gaze over the crowd again and smiled faintly, "Next time we meet, this Lord will not show mercy again. Be careful, the outside world is very dangerous."

He laughed loudly, his figure transforming into a stream of light, and shot out through the gap in the array.

Only after he disappeared from everyone's sight did the Emperor Realm cultivators let out a heavy sigh. For a moment just now, they had clearly sensed Wu Kuang's killing intent. Although they didn't know why he changed his mind, the feeling of surviving was one of immense relief.

"Quick, to the forbidden area!" Gao Xueting called out in a low voice, forcing herself to stay strong as she led the way.

Qiu Ran and the others, concerned for Wen Zishan's safety, hurriedly followed.

Yang Kai turned to Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming and said, "There is a sealed land for suppressing demons on Spirit Sword Peak. Please investigate it carefully."

Chen Wenhao nodded, "Alright, leave this place to me and Brother Feng. You all... be careful as well."

Yang Kai nodded and quickly chased after Gao Xueting and the others.

There were several forbidden areas in Qingyang Shrine, but the most important one was the mountain cavern where the Divine Roaming Mirror was kept. The Divine Roaming Mirror was Qingyang Shrine's greatest secret; ordinary disciples didn't even know of its existence.

The last time Yang Kai was able to enter for training was because he had rendered meritorious service in the Four Seasons Land, and Wen Zishan had rewarded him exceptionally. He remembered the location clearly.

After chasing all the way, he finally caught up with the others in front of the mountain wall.

Gao Xueting opened the restriction on the mountain wall and led the way inside.

The group followed the passage down into the mountain and soon arrived at their destination.

As they looked around, everyone's hearts sank. In the stone cave, a middle-aged woman was leaning against the wall, blood trickling from her mouth, her face as pale as paper. She was clearly badly injured.

On a stone platform not far away sat the Divine Roaming Mirror. But this time, it looked different from when Yang Kai first saw it. The mirror was no longer smooth and clear; instead, wisps of black energy were spreading across its surface.

"Granny..." Gao Xueting rushed forward, squatting in front of the middle-aged woman and anxiously asked, "How are you?"

The middle-aged woman gave a miserable smile, one uglier than crying. "I won't die. Go check on the Hall Master... cough, cough..."

She appeared to be severely injured, clearly beaten by someone. After exchanging a few words with Gao Xueting, she coughed up a large mouthful of blood, her complexion looking extremely haggard and dispirited.

Gao Xueting couldn't care about anything else. Worried for Wen Zishan's safety, she immediately rushed inside to look for his body.

Qiu Ran stepped forward and took out a few Spiritual Pills for the middle-aged woman to take.

Yang Kai looked at the woman's face, feeling a vague sense of familiarity, and hesitantly asked, "Are you Granny You?"

The woman raised her eyes and looked at him, "Heh, the brat from back then has become an Elder. You all have grown so fast."

"You really are Granny You," Yang Kai exclaimed in surprise. "You've changed a lot."

He didn't know Granny You's real name, only that she was the guardian of the Divine Roaming Mirror. He remembered that the last time he came here with Xia Sheng and the others, Granny You had appeared as an old crone, her voice hoarse and unpleasant. Later, during the Divine Roaming Mirror trial, she even sent people to hunt him down.

In the world of the Divine Roaming Mirror, Granny You controlled a force called the Divine Roaming Nation, commanding countless experts. Yang Kai had narrowly escaped death several times back then.

After leaving the Divine Roaming Mirror world, he heard that Granny You had been imprisoned by Wen Zishan, and there had been no news of her since. Now, it seemed Wen Zishan had completely subdued her, and she was still entrusted with guarding the Divine Roaming Mirror.

This time, when Wu Kuang escaped, Granny You was clearly injured by him.

In the distance, Gao Xueting's voice called out.

Everyone was startled and hurried over. In a separate stone chamber, Wen Zishan's body was intact, but for some reason, he was drenched in sweat as if he had suffered some kind of attack.

"Granny, the Hall Master isn't dead, but it seems he's trapped inside the Divine Roaming Mirror and can't get out. Do you have any way to help?" Gao Xueting asked.

Yang Kai supported Granny You as they walked over. Both of them looked unwell, one injured, the other greatly exhausted.

Granny You first checked Wen Zishan's condition, then glanced at the changes in the Divine Roaming Mirror, and sighed, "He isn't trapped. He just won't come out. Something must have happened in the Divine Roaming world. What happened outside?"

There was no time to explain in detail, so Yang Kai simply used his Divine Sense to transmit all the information he knew into Granny You's mind.

She was silent for a moment as she reviewed the information, then her expression turned grim. "A sealed land for suppressing demons? Demonic Intent breaking the seal? In that case, the thing that rushed into the Divine Roaming Mirror earlier must have been Demonic Intent..."

Gao Xueting was startled, "Demonic Intent rushed in?"

Granny You nodded, "Yes, it entered the Divine Roaming Mirror. Then that lord... suddenly awoke, injured me, and left."

Yang Kai said in a deep voice, "That wasn't Lord Hongchen, that was the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor!"

Granny You's eyes narrowed as she softly said, "So that's how it is. I wondered why that lord would attack me for no reason."

"Demonic Intent can actually corrode the Divine Roaming Mirror?" Qiu Ran's face was extremely grave, his gaze fixed on the mirror. In just a short time, the black light on the mirror had grown, and it was clearly radiating Demonic Intent.

Yang Kai said, "The Divine Roaming Mirror is a world of Divine Souls. Demonic Intent targets Divine Souls for corruption. The Divine Roaming world is its nourishment."

"What you say makes sense. We must find a way to stop it. I wonder how the Hall Master is doing inside."

"I'll go in and take a look," Yang Kai volunteered.

"I'll go too!" Gao Xueting quickly said. Although Wen Zishan's body was intact, she couldn't rest easy until she saw his Divine Soul.

Yang Kai nodded and turned to the others, saying, "I'll trouble you all to stand guard as our protectors."

Chen Qian said, "Junior Brother Yang, you don't look well. How about I accompany Senior Sister Gao inside?"

Yang Kai shook his head, "I can withstand the corruption of Demonic Intent. I'll be fine."

Since he insisted, Chen Qian couldn't say more and could only remind him to be careful.

Time was short. Yang Kai and Gao Xueting each found a stone chamber, entered, and sat cross-legged. Once the chambers were sealed, Granny You forced herself to cast a spell, opening the passage to the Divine Roaming Mirror world.

Two mysterious lights shot out from the Divine Roaming Mirror and poured into the sealed stone chambers. Yang Kai and Gao Xueting both trembled as an inexplicable feeling of their Divine Souls separating arose.

It wasn't their first time entering the Divine Roaming Mirror world, so they didn't resist, letting the pulling force drag their Divine Souls toward some unknown direction.

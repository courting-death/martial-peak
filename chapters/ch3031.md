## Chapter 3031 — Not a Soft Persimmon

Coupled with his earlier clash with Yang Kai, both new grudges and old hatreds surged up at once. Yuan Wu's face abruptly sank, and he pointed at Yang Kai. "You, go mine Ice Soul Snow Jade! If you fail to mine five hundred jin each day, this Lord will make you regret it."

Swish, swish, swish. Everyone turned to look at Yang Kai, their gazes filled with sympathy.

Not many people had paid attention to the minor clash between Yang Kai and Yuan Wu earlier. They only knew that this young man was with the Dragon Descendant who had beaten Yuan Wu. Now that Yuan Wu had singled him out first, he had clearly been dragged into the affair.

This was naturally an undeserved calamity.

If it had been any other task, it would not have mattered much. It might have been arduous, but that was all.

The problem was that mining Ice Soul Snow Jade was extremely dangerous.

There was no other reason. The place where Ice Soul Snow Jade formed was incomparably cold. Even an Emperor Realm Master who stayed there too long would suffer greatly, and might even be injured by the cold or frozen to death. Moreover, Ice Soul Snow Jade was extraordinarily hard. Mine five hundred jin every day? That was simply an impossible task.

Yuan Wu was clearly making trouble for him on purpose.

Everyone understood this perfectly well, and Yuan Wu made no attempt to hide it. He sneered inwardly. Weren't you arrogant? Weren't you presumptuous? You dared disregard this Lord and make an enemy of this Lord. This is the consequence.

He was still thinking about Lu Sanniang and her daughter, believing that once Yang Kai was sent away, he could do whatever he pleased.

Thinking about it this way, accepting this assignment today did not seem so miserable after all. At the very least, he could command the five hundred people present. His mood immediately soared, and his vanity swelled. In any case, for the next month, he was the supreme authority here, and everyone had to obediently follow his orders.

As for whether Yang Kai could mine five hundred jin of Ice Soul Snow Jade each day, that was not something Yuan Wu needed to consider. It would naturally be good if Yang Kai brought enough back, but if he failed, Yuan Wu would have an excuse to deal with him properly and teach him the consequences of offending him.

Yuan Wu did not believe Yang Kai could possibly complete this task. Inwardly, he was already plotting how to torture and humiliate him to vent the hatred in his heart.

The others had clearly seen through Yuan Wu's intentions as well, and their hearts clenched as their expressions shifted.

Earlier, they had been preoccupied with their shock at the Dragon Clan's extravagance and the difficulty of constructing the palace. Only now, after hearing the task Yuan Wu had assigned Yang Kai, did they suddenly come to their senses.

Mining Ice Soul Snow Jade was no easy job, and no one was willing to do it. A moment of carelessness could cost them their lives. Now that Yuan Wu held great authority, anyone who wanted to avoid such a dangerous task would have to establish a good relationship with him.

Mining the Ice Soul Snow Jade was certainly not something one person could accomplish. At least twenty or thirty people would have to go.

As soon as this thought occurred to them, someone spoke up. "Brother Yuan, the cultivation technique this Zhou cultivates happens to be well suited to mining Qing Gold Purple Copper. I think you should leave that task to me."

Both were mining jobs, but the difficulty and danger of mining Qing Gold Purple Copper could not compare to those of mining Ice Soul Snow Jade, so that man naturally rushed to claim the task.

"That's right. I also think I'm well suited to mining Qing Gold Purple Copper."

"Heh heh, this Liu thinks mining Heavenly Crystal Meteor Sand would suit me better. Brother Yuan, what do you think..."

More than a dozen Emperor Realm Masters crowded around him, each volunteering to take on a mining task. Their overlapping voices made the scene extremely lively.

A smug look appeared on Yuan Wu's face. He increasingly felt that he had truly shown great foresight by accepting such a fine assignment, and his earlier complaints had long since vanished. Although he had always acted arrogantly and done whatever he pleased in Half-Dragon City, he had never experienced the glory of wielding such authority. Now, he had finally gotten a taste of it and immediately felt that this trip had been worthwhile.

However, he paid no attention to their requests. A man in power had to carry himself like one. If he agreed whenever anyone made a suggestion, how could he display his wisdom? They could make suggestions, but the final decision had to rest in his hands. He alone would decide who mined Ice Soul Snow Jade, who mined Qing Gold Purple Copper, and who mined Heavenly Crystal Meteor Sand.

He merely stared coldly at Yang Kai, the corner of his mouth curling upward as he snorted. "Brat, I'm talking to you. Are you deaf?"

Yang Kai glanced at him, then withdrew his gaze and looked out toward the depths of the sea.

In which direction was Zhu Qing? He had seen several Spirit Islands on the way here, and Lu Sanniang had also said that the Dragon Clan lived scattered throughout the area. Zhu Qing must also live on one of the Spirit Islands in this vast sea.

The problem was that he did not know which Spirit Island Zhu Qing lived on. He first had to investigate the matter clearly.

"Impudent!" Seeing Yang Kai show him no respect whatsoever, Yuan Wu immediately flew into a rage.

It was one thing for that Dragon Descendant to show him no respect. His cultivation was higher, his Dragon Bloodline was stronger, and Yuan Wu truly was no match for him. But what the hell did Yang Kai think he was? A mere Human at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm actually dared show him such an attitude?

He simply did not know his place!

Yuan Wu was currently basking in glory, so how could he allow Yang Kai to undermine his authority? With a low shout, he raised his hand and sent a palm strike toward Yang Kai. A gigantic cyan palm print immediately appeared in the sky and slammed down viciously.

Yuan Wu did not use his full strength in this strike, as he did not want to kill Yang Kai. Setting aside how arduous their task was and how losing even one person meant losing a share of their manpower, the one-month deadline alone already weighed on him so heavily that he could barely breathe. More importantly, he did not intend to let Yang Kai off so easily. He had already transferred the hatred he felt toward Li Jiao for humiliating him onto Yang Kai, so how could he possibly stop without thoroughly disgracing him?

He could not defeat Li Jiao. Whether in cultivation or Dragon Bloodline, he was no match for him, so Yuan Wu could only settle for the next best thing.

Aren't you close to that Dragon Descendant? Don't you want to protect that beautiful mother and daughter? This Lord would like to see whether you have the ability.

Thus, Yuan Wu used only half his strength in this attack.

However, that was already enough.

He was a Dragon Descendant at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, while his opponent was merely a Human at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Even half his strength was more than Yang Kai could withstand. Once this palm landed, the brat's bones and tendons would surely break.

"Be careful!" Lu Sanniang cried out, her beautiful face paling. Ever since arriving on this island, she had been in a strange state, looking nervous and pale. She had not had the presence of mind to pay attention to the conflict between Yang Kai and Yuan Wu, and only cried out when Yuan Wu suddenly attacked.

As she spoke, Lu Sanniang moved. Her Emperor Yuan surged, and she clearly intended to block the attack for Yang Kai. Although she was no match for Yuan Wu either, if she joined forces with Yang Kai, they might still be able to contend with him for a time. At the very least, they had to stop this palm strike.

At worst, she could take the initiative to apologize to Yuan Wu afterward, turning a major matter into a minor one and a minor matter into nothing.

Her thoughts were simple and naive, but to her surprise, Yang Kai suddenly grabbed her. She did not even feel him exert any strength, yet all the Emperor Yuan in her body was abruptly suppressed, leaving her unable to move.

Lu Sanniang's beautiful eyes instantly widened, and she could not understand what had just happened.

The experts gathered around them slowly shook their heads, secretly feeling that Yang Kai was in serious trouble now. This fellow was truly unlucky. He had offended Yuan Wu simply because he was associated with that Dragon Descendant, so his days ahead would likely be difficult.

They had no ties to Yang Kai and had never even met him before. Although some sympathized with him, no one would naturally step forward to help him now. Everyone could see that Yuan Wu was the one they needed to curry favor with.

Who would be foolish enough to offend Yuan Wu for a complete stranger?

Everyone could almost foresee Yang Kai's miserable state shortly, and they became even more determined not to offend Yuan Wu. Otherwise, they might not live to return a month later.

In the blink of an eye, the gigantic cyan palm print had already descended above Yang Kai's head.

Yet Yang Kai remained perfectly still, and even his expression did not change, as though he had been frightened senseless.

Only when the palm print fell did he gently raise one hand. Emperor Yuan surged in his palm as a profound Law Power rippled outward.

He lifted his palm slightly and pressed it against the cyan palm print.

Kacha...

The sound of something shattering rang out.

The gigantic cyan palm print suddenly shattered into pieces, transforming into specks of light that faded away.

Yuan Wu froze.

Lu Sanniang froze.

Everyone froze.

No one knew how Yang Kai had broken this attack. He seemed only to have raised his hand lightly, yet the strike Yuan Wu had been certain would succeed had fallen apart.

Was that real? Was this some kind of joke? Had Yuan Wu underestimated his enemy too much?

That was what most people thought. After all, Yuan Wu's strength was plain to see, while Yang Kai's cultivation was one Stage below his. Even if Yuan Wu had not used his full strength, Yang Kai still should not have been able to contend with him.

The only explanation was that Yuan Wu had underestimated his enemy, allowing Yang Kai to break his move.

However, the eyes of a few people narrowed slightly as they studied Yang Kai in astonishment, incredulous light flashing through their gazes.

They had paid no attention to Yang Kai before because there had been nothing about him worth noticing, but things were different now. That calm and effortless display alone was enough for Yang Kai to draw the attention of the handful of experts present.

This young man was no soft persimmon! The fact that he could break Yuan Wu's attack was not merely because Yuan Wu had underestimated him.

On the other side, Yuan Wu stared blankly for a moment before his face abruptly fell. He had intended to establish his authority, but who could have known that not only would his attack fail to achieve that effect, it would instead feel as though he had been slapped in the face? He immediately shouted in fury, "Courting death!"

Rage burned within him. Several dragon scales beneath his neck suddenly deepened in color, and a faint Dragon Pressure spread outward as he pushed the aura of his Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivation to its peak.

Yang Kai's resistance had undoubtedly enraged him. Originally, he had only intended to teach Yang Kai a small lesson so the latter would recognize the situation and obediently follow orders from then on, but now he planned to use his full strength to beat him thoroughly.

He would first beat him half to death!

Yuan Wu secretly made up his mind, but before he could make another move, his vision suddenly blurred and a figure abruptly appeared before him.

Yuan Wu jumped in fright. When he looked over, he was horrified to discover that Yang Kai, who had been some distance away moments ago, had somehow appeared directly in front of him, only three steps away.

What was going on?

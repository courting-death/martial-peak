## Chapter 2924 — Refusing a Toast Only to Drink a Forfeit

The sky was bright and clear, not a single cloud in sight. Beneath the azure dome, a faint glimmer flashed by and disappeared in an instant. It was the light of a Secret Treasure in flight, but it moved so quickly that ordinary people could not possibly notice it.

The Secret Treasure was Yang Kai's Flowing Cloud Shuttle, a flying Imperial Treasure crafted by Hou Yu. Its performance was exceptional; though the internal space was not large, it could still accommodate four people without issue.

Yang Kai released the control restriction, allowing the four of them to take turns piloting at full speed. Relying on Yang Kai's faint sense of that mark, they tracked Gong Yue's whereabouts.

Gong Yue was much harder to deal with than he had imagined. The four of them had left that ancient cave residence a month ago, yet they still had not caught sight of Gong Yue. The more time passed, the weaker the sense of that mark became. If this dragged on, they might truly lose the trail.

Yang Kai was extremely anxious about this. At a certain moment, he suddenly opened his eyes.

Gao Xueting, who was currently controlling the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, seemed to sense something and looked over at him, asking, "Did it break again?"

Yang Kai nodded slightly, revealing a trace of helplessness. In the first half month, his connection to the mark, though faint, had remained constant. But in the past half month, for some unknown reason, the connection would occasionally break, causing him to lose the direction of the pursuit.

At that time, although Gong Yue's mind was in chaos and he could not detect Yang Kai's actions, Yang Kai did not dare to be careless. The mark he left was not too strong, and at such a distance, it was impossible to sense it. Only thanks to the wonders of his Space Divine Abilities could they track him at all.

"Do you think Gong Yue has noticed something? After all, he is at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm," Gao Xueting asked with a frown.

Yang Kai said, "Whether he noticed or not, we should be glad he hasn't taken the opportunity to cause trouble."

Although Gong Yue had been on the run for two months, he had not done anything to harm the masses. He just kept circling around, as if searching for something, or perhaps hiding from something.

No one knew whether that was his instinctive reaction, whether he had regained consciousness, or whether he had been possessed by Demonic Intent.

No one knew his current state. Only by meeting him face to face could they see clearly.

Feng Ming snorted coldly, "Now that he has entered a Demonic State, no matter how vast the world, there is nowhere for him to hide. We will find him sooner or later."

Chen Wenhao suddenly said, "Do you two still remember the incident in Fenglin City back then?"

Gao Xueting and Feng Ming both nodded, "Of course we remember."

Chen Wenhao sighed, "After that incident, I specifically returned to the Sacred Land to consult many ancient records. I found that such situations were extremely rare in history, but in recent years, they seem to be happening more and more often."

"So what?" Feng Ming did not understand why he brought this up.

Chen Wenhao said gravely, "A Demonic Calamity is about to unfold. Though such occurrences were rare in ancient times, and the records of them are fragmented and incomplete, whenever such incidents do occur, they serve as a harbinger—an ominous one at that."

The other three were slightly stunned by his words.

Feng Ming laughed, "Even if that's true, it's not for us to worry about. If the sky falls, there are those above us to hold it up."

Gao Xueting and Chen Wenhao neither agreed nor disagreed with his carefree attitude.

Changing the subject, Feng Ming stroked his chin and said, "That aside, this old man is quite curious. What is Gong Yue like after entering a Demonic State? Is he similar to the one we encountered outside Fenglin City back then?"

"There were many suspicious points about that incident back then," Chen Wenhao said, frowning.

Gao Xueting nodded in agreement. Back then, the three of them had fought a demon outside Fenglin City, but were inexplicably dragged into a pitch-black environment. When they regained their senses, the enemy had vanished, and they were unharmed, as if the demon had deliberately let them go.

No one could say what had really happened. After returning to their sects, all three had carefully checked themselves and found nothing wrong. Even so, after all these years, the memory of that day remained vivid.

Hearing them mention the past, Yang Kai silently closed his eyes, piloting the Flowing Cloud Shuttle while doing his best to track Gong Yue's whereabouts.

Another day passed like this. Suddenly, Yang Kai opened his eyes and shouted, "Found him!"

"Where?" Gao Xueting and the others opened their eyes at the same time and asked.

Yang Kai pointed, "Three thousand li ahead."

Feng Ming's eyes lit up, "So close! Let's see where he runs now!"

For ordinary people, three thousand li was a distance they could never cross in a lifetime, but for a few Emperor Realm experts, it was a matter of moments.

After tracking Gong Yue for a month, they had never been this close. So, upon hearing the news, everyone was excited.

In just a moment, the Flowing Cloud Shuttle arrived at the location.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle stopped, and four figures appeared in the sky. They did not bother to hide their auras. The vast, ocean-like Imperial Might filled the void, making the entire area tremble.

It was a small mountain peak, no more than a thousand zhang high. Pavilions and towers dotted the summit, and though the scenery was decent, the Spiritual Qi was not abundant.

With a glance, Yang Kai understood that the Earth Vein here was poor—scattered and mixed. Such a place would never attract a Sect or family with any foundation. Only those with no background or support would choose it as a place to settle.

Yet, judging by the situation on the mountain, there was indeed a small Sect here.

There were about fifty or sixty people, the strongest of whom only exuded the aura of the Void King Realm. Such a small Sect was insignificant, not just in the entire Southern Territory, but even within a single city.

This place was remote and rarely visited by experts.

The strongest person these disciples had ever seen was only a First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm cultivator, and even then, it was by chance. They were amazed by him.

Today, four Emperor Realm experts had suddenly descended, each a true powerhouse from a top Sect in the Southern Territory. It was as if the sky had collapsed, stirring up waves of shock in everyone's hearts.

Their perception was not keen enough to recognize that the four in the sky were legendary Emperor Realm experts, but the suffocating pressure made it clear that any one of them could kill them at will.

On the mountain, in the houses, along the forest paths, fifty or sixty cultivators of varying strength trembled, faces pale and eyes filled with terror as they looked up.

They were like a group of ants before an elephant. The elephant had already raised its foot, and all they could do was silently pray it would not step on them. There was nothing else they could do.

Yang Kai and the others took in the situation at a glance. As soon as they appeared, the four of them spread out, forming an encirclement that covered the entire mountain.

Feng Ming called out loudly, "This Lord is Elder Feng Ming of Wuhua Hall. Together with my companions, I have come to pursue a vicious demon. We mean you no harm. Stand where you are and do not move, and your lives will be spared. If you disobey, you are responsible for your own fate!"

With his status, it was already kind of him to give a warning to these cultivators, most of whom had not even reached the Origin Returning Realm. After speaking, he said no more.

Naturally, those cultivators dared not move. In fact, even if Feng Ming had not spoken, they could not move. Under the Imperial Might, it was as if an invisible shackle bound them. Their bodies were stiff, their minds blank, filled only with terror and dread.

The four Emperor Realm experts looked at each other from a distance and each summoned their Imperial Treasure.

Chen Wenhao had entered the Dao with the sword, his swordsmanship reaching perfection. With his sword in hand, his aura was awe-inspiring. The sword was named Waterflow, its brilliance flowing. It had accompanied Chen Wenhao for over a thousand years; man and sword were as one.

Gao Xueting summoned her own Imperial Treasure, the Scorching Sun Mirror. The mirror was ancient and unadorned, revealing little, but it was her life-bound Imperial Treasure.

Feng Ming wielded the Yanlei Spear. Though his body appeared old, it suddenly straightened, exuding a sharp, unstoppable aura, even more imposing than many young people.

Yang Kai held the Myriad Sword, a broad longsword that, compared to Chen Wenhao's Waterflow Sword, seemed rather unremarkable.

Yet Chen Wenhao still glanced at the Myriad Sword. As an Emperor Realm expert who had entered the Dao with the sword, he instinctively sensed its power.

The four Emperor Realm experts set up their formation. Feng Ming shouted down at the largest building on the mountain, "Gong Yue, show yourself! You have nowhere left to run."

After arriving, he did not need Yang Kai to remind him. He had already sensed a clear energy fluctuation, closely linked to Yang Kai's aura. It was obviously the mark Yang Kai had placed on Gong Yue.

With the mark here, Gong Yue must also be here.

A loud shout rang out, but no one replied.

Feng Ming shook his spear and sneered, "Refusing a toast only to drink a forfeit. I intended to leave you a whole corpse, but since you are so stubborn, don't blame this old man for showing no mercy."

As he finished speaking, a cold gleam flashed, and the spear shot out like a dragon.

There was no dazzling light. Feng Ming's spear was as simple as could be. The disciples of the small Sect could not sense much, but the other three Emperor Realm experts could appreciate Feng Ming's skill.

He concentrated all his power into a single point, not letting a bit leak out.

The spear's momentum broke through the barriers of space. The mountain shook violently, as if the earth were collapsing. The building was instantly reduced to dust.

"Hm?" At that moment, Feng Ming frowned, as if he had discovered something.

Yang Kai hurriedly shouted, "Elder Feng, show mercy!"

Feng Ming's hand trembled, and eighty percent of the force he had unleashed instantly dissipated. The remaining twenty percent was diverted to the side.

With a rumble, the building collapsed, revealing a middle-aged man trembling like a sieve, dumbfounded. He stood alone in the ruins, covered in dust, his face as pale as paper.

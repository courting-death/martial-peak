## Chapter 2673 — The Girl Blocking the Road

The middle-aged man, hearing this, turned his head to look at the beautiful woman kneeling to the side and said, "Junior Sister Hua, tell me, if I were to act personally, could I capture that boy?"

This beautiful woman was none other than Hua Qingsi, who had parted ways with Yang Kai in the Shattered Star Sea. Back then, she was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, but after gaining some opportunities in the Shattered Star Sea, she had now advanced to the Emperor Realm.

Yet at this moment, she bore none of the dignity an Emperor Realm cultivator should possess, instead resembling a prisoner under strict watch.

Hearing the middle-aged man's question, Hua Qingsi pressed her lips together and did not answer, turning her head aside.

"Junior Sister Hua, you're being disobedient again." The middle-aged man sneered, a cold smile curling at his lips, and suddenly formed a strange hand seal with both hands.

In the next instant, a miserable scream burst from Hua Qingsi's mouth. It was as if she were suffering the most unbearable torture in the world—her beautiful face twisted in pain, sweat pouring down her forehead in large drops, and her clothes were instantly soaked through.

"Master!" She forced herself to crawl to the old man's feet, kneeling and calling out.

The old man remained unmoved, as steady as a mountain, not lifting his brows or eyes, as if he had heard and seen nothing.

The middle-aged man sneered, "As a disciple under your Master, you actually shield an outsider like this. If not for Master's compassion, your life would have been forfeit long ago. Now, sparing your life is to let you atone for your mistakes, but you remain stubborn. As your Senior Brother, I truly can't bear to watch. I suppose I must punish you as well."

"Master..." Hua Qingsi's voice trembled, her face deathly pale as she gritted her teeth and said, "If you want that thing back, I'll go and speak to him. Please, Master, stop... spare his life. He is not... an unreasonable person. I do have some friendship with him. I can... I can surely persuade him. Please, Master... give me a chance."

"Insolent!" The middle-aged man, seeing her endure such torment without begging for mercy and even pleading for someone else, was instantly enraged. His hand seal changed again, and Hua Qingsi screamed sharply, curling up on the ground.

The old man sighed lightly, a look of helplessness appearing on his face. "Truly, a grown woman can't be kept at home—always siding with outsiders."

With a wave of his hand, the middle-aged man finally withdrew his hand seal.

The old man lifted his hand, supporting Hua Qingsi up with a gentle force, and said, "I know that your advancement to the Emperor Realm is entirely thanks to him. But do not forget who taught and raised you."

"It was... it was Master. I will never forget Master's kindness in raising me," Hua Qingsi replied weakly, opening her tired eyes.

The old man nodded. "As long as you know. If it were anything else, I might have agreed. After all, you are now in the Emperor Realm, and as my disciple, you bring me honor. But... this matter is of great importance. The fewer who know, the better. I sent you to gather information back then because I trusted you. But you have disappointed me."

Hearing this, guilt flashed in Hua Qingsi's eyes.

At that time, she had no choice—Yang Kai had planted a soul imprint on her and taken her into the Small Xuan Realm. Although Yang Kai later set her free, she no longer wished to report anything. Since Yang Kai had shown her kindness, she could not repay it with betrayal. She only wanted to hide her identity and never return to the Star God Palace, as if the old Hua Qingsi had died.

Yet she never expected that her Master would send Senior Brother Wu Ming to find her in the Thousand Leaves Sect. Not only did he capture her, but he even brought calamity upon the entire sect, leading to its complete destruction.

She had been subjected to a secret art and could not remember if she had ever revealed anything to her Master, but she knew the Thousand Leaves Sect's destruction was her fault, and it was also a means for her Master to lure Yang Kai out.

Because there were several people in the Thousand Leaves Sect whom Yang Kai valued greatly. As long as they were captured, Yang Kai would never let the matter rest.

Facts proved this move was brilliant—Yang Kai had indeed been lured to Flowing Shadow City and was now in the trap.

"That boy dares to steal my treasure—how could I let him go? Qingsi, don't worry about it. Once this matter is over, return with me to the Star God Palace to cultivate in seclusion." The old man waved his hand. Though his tone was calm, a glint of excitement flashed in his eyes.

A year ago, when Wu Ming brought Hua Qingsi back to the Star God Palace, he used a secret art to force many secrets from her. Not only did he learn who had his lost treasure, but also that this person possessed a vast spatial Secret Treasure.

If it were only that, it would be nothing. Though spatial Secret Treasures were rare and precious, they were still just Secret Treasures. Without proficiency in space techniques, one could hardly use them freely.

The key was that within that spatial Secret Treasure, there were many rare items. What caught his attention most were two Divine Trees—one full of vitality, the other gold and silver in color.

As an elder of the Star God Palace and a cultivator at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, the old man had seen countless treasures in his long life. But after carefully inquiring about the characteristics of those two Divine Trees, he was almost certain that one was the legendary Everlasting Tree.

The Everlasting Tree! It was said that refining it could grant an immortal, indestructible body—a tremendous temptation for any cultivator. He was old and had thought he would never break through again, but now, fate had suddenly smiled upon him.

If he could obtain and refine the Everlasting Tree, he might well become a Great Emperor, standing equal to the Radiant Moon Great Emperor!

As for the gold and silver Divine Tree, he had some guesses but dared not be certain. Regardless, both Divine Trees were supreme treasures of heaven and earth. Anyone who obtained even one would be incredibly fortunate, yet now both had ended up in one person's hands.

As long as he could kill that person, such treasures would be his. After refining the Everlasting Tree, the world would be his to roam!

The old man had revealed this secret matter to no one, not even Wu Ming, whom he relied upon most. Wu Ming merely thought his master was scheming over a precious treasure lost years ago, completely unaware of his greater ambitions. Meanwhile, the old man had quietly made up his mind—after this matter was concluded, Hua Qingsi would have to be silenced as well.

Hua Qingsi's expression dimmed. She knew that nothing she said now would make a difference.

Wu Ming, standing to the side, glanced at Hua Qingsi and sneered, "Junior Sister Hua, could it be you've taken a liking to that boy?"

Hua Qingsi glared at him, gritting her teeth. "Watch your mouth."

"You wretch, still dare to talk back?" Wu Ming's face twisted, and he raised his palm, about to form a hand seal to teach Hua Qingsi a lesson.

Hua Qingsi had a Heart-Controlling Gu planted in her body—her life and death were at his whim. How could he allow her to be so insolent before him?

"Enough." The old man raised his hand slightly, stopping Wu Ming. "Go to the auction venue and make careful preparations. Nothing can go wrong this time."

Dealing with a cultivator skilled in space powers, even someone like the old man dared not be careless. Back when Yin Lesheng wanted to deal with Yang Kai, he had to set up a grand array to isolate heaven and earth. How could the old man be negligent? One misstep and Yang Kai could escape. Though he held people Yang Kai cared about, he could not guarantee Yang Kai would submit.

Wu Ming's expression darkened as he withdrew his hand, shooting Hua Qingsi a cold glare. He thought to himself that he would let her rest easy for now, but once this matter was concluded, he would ask his master to hand her over to him so he could shower her with proper affection.

After Wu Ming left, the old man said, "Your Senior Brother has always been arrogant. Don't blame him."

Hua Qingsi replied that she dared not, but her heart was full of bitterness. In her current state, with this treatment, there was no longer any bond of master and disciple, or of fellow disciples. Her Master guarded against her like a thief, and her Senior Brother looked at her with disdain. The world was vast, but where could she go? She had broken through to the Emperor Realm, yet her path in life had suddenly become lost.

…

"Boy, what's your name?"

Outside the City Lord’s mansion, Yang Kai and his group had just stepped out when someone blocked their way.

Standing in front of them was a young girl, looking no more than seventeen or eighteen, petite and delicate, dressed in a clean, light blue long dress. Though not tall, her figure was extremely well-developed—her chest full and straining against her clothes, her waist slender, and her hips round and shapely.

The girl's features were beautiful, but she exuded a vibrant heroic spirit.

Standing before Yang Kai, she looked up at him with an air of exaggerated maturity.

Yang Kai was taken aback. He looked around, then pointed at his own nose. "Are you talking to me?"

He was sure he didn't know this girl. He hadn't seen her when entering the city, and had no idea why she was suddenly blocking their way.

The girl's brows knitted, and she said angrily, "Who else could I be talking to?"

"Do we know each other?" Yang Kai looked at her with a strange expression.

The girl replied impatiently, "Just answer when you're asked. Why so much nonsense?"

Yang Kai arched a brow and said, “You expect me to answer just because you asked? Where would that leave my dignity?”

This little girl was truly baffling—blocking their way for no reason and acting so high and mighty. Yang Kai couldn't bring himself to like her at all.

Yingfei, standing to the side, broke into a grin and said, "Young Master Yang possesses both talent and grace—a true dragon among men. I suspect someone has fallen for you at first sight..."

Hiss—

Before Yingfei could finish, a pink fist suddenly swept past his cheek. If he hadn't dodged quickly, that punch would have landed squarely on his face.

The one who threw the punch was, of course, the girl. After this strike, both Yang Kai and Yingfei couldn't help but change their expressions.

The force behind that punch was truly terrifying. Even the void seemed to tremble, yet the girl acted as if it was nothing, not even using her full strength.

Yingfei's face darkened, and he felt a chill in his heart.

Although he was a Yao King and quite powerful, if he had taken that punch, he would have been seriously injured if not killed.

The girl was so young and petite, yet possessed such astonishing strength.

"Just a mere Yao King. If you dare mention that word again, I'll take your dog life on the spot!" the girl warned Yingfei, slowly withdrawing her fist.

This time, Yingfei was truly terrified. His eagle-like eyes flashed with a frightening light, enveloping the girl as if to see into her very soul.

He had always concealed his aura while following Yang Kai, never revealing the slightest flaw. Even most Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivators couldn't see through him, yet this girl not only saw through him at a glance, but even knew he was a Yao King!

Such perception was truly terrifying.

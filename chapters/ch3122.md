## Chapter 3122 — You Schemed Against Me

Dark Star, Qiyun Mountain. Spiritual Qi flourished here, and energy was abundant. This was one of Dark Star's most famous Crystal Stone veins, as well as one of Lingxiao Sect's most important holdings on Dark Star. The Crystal Stones mined here each year accounted for more than one-tenth of Lingxiao Sect's total income.

An industry this important naturally had an expert stationed to guard it.

Her fitted dress traced her graceful curves, and her beautiful hair was tied neatly behind her head, giving her a valiant, capable air. The former Eldest Young Lady of Zhongdu's Qiu Family, and now the Patriarch of the Qiu Family, Qiu Yimeng, was the one guarding this place. After several dozen years of hardship and striving, the aura radiating from her had astonishingly reached the First Stage of the Void King Realm.

Among the younger generation that had left Zhongdu together back then, it could be said that aside from Yang Kai, her cultivation was the highest. Even many of the older generation could not compare to her in this regard. Her outstanding aptitude was certainly part of it, but there was a greater reason as well.

The stars in the sky were too far from her. If she wanted to pluck the stars and invite the moon, how could she dare slack off? Even if she could not reach them, being a little closer would be enough.

As one of the main battlefields in the war between the two Star Domains, Dark Star naturally received plenty of attention from the Great Wilderness Star Domain. There were several dozen battlefields across Dark Star, and the fighting over various strategic resource sites was even more frequent.

Qiyun Mountain had naturally drawn the Great Wilderness Star Domain's eye as well. The other side also had a Void King Realm master and several hundred cultivators under him, with no shortage of Origin Returning Realm cultivators. They had been locked in conflict here with the Lingxiao Sect disciples led by Qiu Yimeng for five full years. After several hundred battles large and small, both sides had won and lost, but neither could do anything decisive to the other. The Great Wilderness Star Domain side had never managed to seize this Crystal Stone vein, while Qiu Yimeng was equally powerless to wipe them out. Even asking Lingxiao Sect for reinforcements would not work, because if her side called for help, the other side would do the same. Their reinforcements came quickly too. If Lingxiao Sect sent more Void King Realm masters, the enemy would also strengthen their forces.

After five years, the two sides had instead reached a tacit understanding: they would fight only with the manpower they already had, and neither would ask the backer behind them for anything more.

A ruthless drive simmered in Qiu Yimeng's heart. Several dozen years had passed. He had probably already gone much higher and farther, hadn't he? If she could not even deal with the enemies before her, how could she speak of getting a little closer to him?

When there was no fighting, she cultivated hard and did not dare waste a moment.

A rapid knock suddenly came from outside the door.

"Come in!" Qiu Yimeng opened her eyes.

The door opened, and a burly man with broad shoulders and a thick waist strode in, his killing intent so fierce it had almost taken substance. The man was none other than Tu Feng, the Blood Servant who had followed Yang Kai in the Succession War and rendered outstanding service.

Every one of the Yang Family's Blood Servants was an outstanding talent, and each was utterly loyal to the Yang Family. After coming to Dark Star with Yang Kai, these Blood Servants had seen their strength grow explosively. As one of the best among them, Tu Feng now possessed cultivation at the Third Stage of the Origin Returning Realm and was only one step away from advancing to the Void King Realm.

As for Qiu Yimeng's own forces, almost all of them were also from Zhongdu's Eight Great Families, so it was only natural for the Yang Family Blood Servants to serve under her command.

"Elder Qiu!" Tu Feng cupped his fists.

Having advanced to the Void King Realm, Qiu Yimeng naturally qualified for a seat among Lingxiao Sect's Elders.

"What is it?"

"Those dog-bred bastards are making a move."

"Again?" Qiu Yimeng's brow tightened. Something felt wrong. Wasn't this pace a little too quick? Their last battle had been only five days ago. Logically, both sides should have been able to beat the gongs and call a halt for at least a month. Why had they come back after only a few days this time?

Tu Feng's expression turned strange. "They're not coming. They want to run."

"They want to run?" Qiu Yimeng frowned even more deeply. They had fought here for five years. Who did not understand whom by now? Why would they suddenly flee? Vaguely sensing something, she asked, "What were those noises just now?"

She had been cultivating earlier. Although she had heard some strange sounds, she had not thought much of them. Thinking back now, those noises seemed to have come from the Starry Sky, and from that direction...

Could it be...

Tu Feng said excitedly, "I was just about to mention that. The Great Wilderness Star Domain's Warships seem to have been destroyed."

"Did Great Elder act?" Qiu Yimeng's expression turned serious.

Those dozen or so Warships were moored outside Dark Star, watching the entire Star like tigers eyeing their prey. They were not only the reason the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators dared run rampant on Dark Star, but also what restrained Lingxiao Sect's movements as a whole.

Lingxiao Sect was not weak now, with Void King Realm experts emerging one after another, but at present, Great Elder Ye Xijun was the only Third Stage Void King Realm master stationed in the Sect. The others were not on Dark Star, and distant water could not quench a nearby thirst.

Unless Ye Xijun acted personally, no one could do anything to those Warships.

No, even if Ye Xijun acted, she could not have achieved something like this unless those few people had returned to Lingxiao Sect as well and joined hands with her. Had they come back? Qiu Yimeng could not help thinking along those lines. If that was true, then it would make sense.

Tu Feng said, "I don't know the details, but this is exactly the right time for us to strike."

Qiu Yimeng rose to her feet and sneered, "It's time to settle accounts."

With the backer behind them gone, it would be strange if the people from the Great Wilderness Star Domain did not panic. Naturally, Qiu Yimeng would not let this excellent chance to beat a drowning dog slip by.

When she stepped outside, every pair of eyes turned toward her, each person exuding a powerful aura. Looking over those familiar faces one by one, Qiu Yimeng nodded slightly.

She turned her head and saw streaks of light flashing through the sky several hundred li away. They shone like meteors, but instead of falling, they raced upward. They were the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators trying to flee Dark Star.

She turned to look the other way, and the same sight met her eyes from even farther away.

It seemed the destruction of the Warships had terrified the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators. How could they still have the nerve to remain on Dark Star? One after another, they had all decided to escape.

"Don't let a single one go!" Qiu Yimeng shouted. Did they think Dark Star was a place they could enter and leave as they pleased?

"Yes!"

Saint Yuan surged, and figures shot out one after another, racing after their enemies of five years.

Lights flashed brilliantly. Dark Star was extraordinarily lively today, with similar scenes playing out everywhere.

Through the chase and flight, the distance between them did not shrink. After all, the people from the Great Wilderness Star Domain were no pushovers. If they dared invade another Star Domain, how could they possibly lack strength?

Seeing that the group would escape Dark Star if she did not push harder, Qiu Yimeng gritted her teeth and formed seals with both hands. A layer of pale red mist suddenly spread across her body.

Her speed shot up, swift as lightning and thunder.

The group of fleeing Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators seemed to sense something and looked back, only to see a scarlet streak charging toward them at incredible speed. The leader's face immediately darkened. "Stinking woman!"

He was the Void King Realm expert from the Great Wilderness Star Domain who had fought Qiu Yimeng for several years, a lean man of about forty. Although he coveted Qiu Yimeng's beauty, he had never been able to suppress her. Now, seeing her charge ahead alone, his mind immediately began to work.

Before leaving, snatching a wife to take back with him would not be bad.

His Divine Sense surged as he transmitted a few words, and the fleeing formation abruptly scattered.

Qiu Yimeng naturally saw this, but her speed did not slow in the slightest.

"Come then. I'll make sure you come and never return," the lean man sneered.

"Bian Hong, die!" Qiu Yimeng's shout rang out before she even arrived.

The man called Bian Hong burst into laughter. Pretending to flee, he looked back and teased, "Little shrew, if you can't bear to part with your Uncle Bian, why not come with me? Uncle Bian will take good care of you. I'll make sure you're fed nice and full."

His filthy words drew loud laughter from the group.

Qiu Yimeng answered with a Divine Ability.

She raised her fair hand high. A long sword trembled ceaselessly, its radiance flashing as the power of wind gathered and coiled around it.

When the long sword swung down, sword light surged, stretching to several dozen zhang as it chopped straight into the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators. Wind power coiled around the blade, actually letting out the roar of a tornado.

At the sight, all the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators turned solemn. No matter what, the one attacking was a Void King Realm master. How could they afford to take her lightly?

The scattered formation suddenly closed ranks. Waves of Saint Yuan surged out, and in the blink of an eye, the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators actually formed a battle formation. Their Saint Yuan linked together and turned into a light shield above them.

With a thunderous boom, the giant sword struck the light shield. Light scattered in all directions, and energy shook the air.

Qiu Yimeng gritted her teeth and drove her strength downward, intending to break through the light shield and cut down a few of them first.

Cracks immediately split across the light shield with a series of sharp snaps, as if it would collapse at any moment.

Qiu Yimeng froze. Instead of joy, alarm rose in her heart. Her strike was certainly powerful, but it should not have been able to break through the enemy's defense so easily. After all, there was also a Void King Realm master in the enemy camp. Unless...

She focused her gaze. Among the crowd, there was no longer any sign of Bian Hong. She had no idea when he had vanished.

Bad! Qiu Yimeng's heart jolted, and she realized that in her eagerness to pursue the enemy, she had carelessly fallen for a trick.

A fierce gust came from behind her head, and from far off came the alarmed cries of Tu Feng and the others.

Though startled, Qiu Yimeng did not panic. So many years of battle and slaughter had given her flexible combat instincts. Saint Yuan surged behind her, condensing into a defense.

Saint Yuan clashed violently. The hastily formed protective Saint Yuan broke at the first touch, and Bian Hong's palm slammed into her back. He grinned savagely. "Die!"

Qiu Yimeng staggered and spat blood, using the momentum to pull away from Bian Hong.

Bian Hong's face turned ashen. He lowered his head to look at his palm and saw that it had turned pitch-black, with numbness spreading through it. He desperately urged his strength to suppress it, yet even that proved somewhat difficult. Gritting his teeth, he roared, "What did you do?"

Qiu Yimeng chuckled, wiped the blood from the corner of her mouth, and tilted her head at him. "Do you like this gift? I prepared it specially for you."

Seeing her expression, Bian Hong's heart lurched. "You schemed against me!"

How could he fail to understand? Qiu Yimeng had deliberately unleashed an extremely powerful Divine Ability and exposed an opening at her back to lure him into a sneak attack.

She had to be wearing some kind of precious armor, and poisonous armor at that! His palm had struck her, but instead of killing her, he had poisoned himself. This poison might not be fatal, but he still had to spend energy suppressing it. In front of her, how could he spare the strength?

Qiu Yimeng reached up and smoothed the hair by her ear, her eyes soft as water. "Yes. So what?"

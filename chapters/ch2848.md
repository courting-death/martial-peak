## Chapter 2848 — The Storm Approaches

Shaman Niu did not know what relationship Yu and Lu had with the Shaman King, but the captain of the guards was well aware of it.

With a great battle about to erupt, entrusting Yu and Lu to a Great Shaman Master could never be as safe as keeping them by the Shaman King's side.

Shaman Dang said calmly, "Only by following him is there hope of survival."

The captain of the guards frowned slightly, clearly disagreeing with this view, but in front of the Shaman King, he could not show it openly and could only remain silent.

Outside the royal city, Yang Kai led a pair of twins toward his own quarters. After a while, he suddenly turned and asked, "Which of you is Yu, and which is Lu?"

The two women halted their steps. The one on the left spoke, "I am Yu."

The one on the right said, "I am Lu."

Yang Kai grinned, baring his teeth, and nodded, "I’ll remember that. Mm, the Shaman King said you two have cultivated in the Shaman God Hall since childhood. The Shaman God Hall... is it fun?"

The twins both frowned. Yu replied in a deep voice, "The Shaman God Hall is the most important place for the entire Barbarian Tribe. Only the most outstanding shamans are qualified to enter and serve the Shaman God. It is not a place for games."

Lu added, "Please mind your words, sir."

Yang Kai shrugged, having vaguely grasped the personalities of these two Shaman Masters, and said no more, leading the way ahead.

Soon, they returned to his quarters. A group of Barbarian Tribe warriors and shamans who were training saw him return with a pair of beautiful twins and could not help but be surprised.

Yang Kai clapped his hands to draw everyone’s attention and announced loudly, "These two Shaman Masters are Yu and Lu, specially assigned to Shaman Niu's tribe by the Shaman King. Now that you all know them, I hope you can get along and work together to resist the demons!"

Everyone responded in unison. The group of rough men looked at the twins with eyes full of amazement. If not for the high status of Yu and Lu as Shaman Masters, someone would surely have tried to strike up a conversation.

Several Shaman Apprentices and Shaman Warriors stepped forward respectfully to greet the two Shaman Masters.

Yu and Lu only nodded slightly, their gazes sweeping over the members of Shaman Niu's tribe with a hint of scrutiny.

"Continue your training," Yang Kai waved his hand, then said to the twins, "You two join them as well. The Shaman God Hall only taught you how to cultivate shamanic arts, but probably not how to use your abilities in war, right? Take these few days to get familiar, so you don’t panic on the battlefield."

The twins nodded slightly.

Yang Kai then whistled to the side. The next moment, the Eagle King, who was crouched on the ground, seemed to receive a command, raised its head, and let out a sharp cry.

The giant eagles of the Sky Cavalry circling in the sky responded one after another, soon carrying many archers back from the air and landing on the ground.

Many archers looked pale as they dismounted, running off to vomit, and some even staggered and fell to the ground.

"Sir! What are your orders?" Ah Hua, carrying a longbow and looking valiant, walked over. Though her face was also a bit pale, she was clearly in much better shape than the others, at least her steps were steady.

Yang Kai said solemnly, "I just received orders from the Shaman King to send twenty-five giant eagles to the royal city."

Ah Hua was startled, "Send twenty-five giant eagles? Doesn’t that mean..."

Originally, each of the seventy-five giant eagles had a corresponding archer. If twenty-five were sent away, that meant twenty-five people would lose their qualification as Sky Cavalry, something no archer could accept.

These archers were all elite members of Shaman Niu's tribe, carefully selected experts. In terms of archery, none was much inferior to another.

"So you have one day. After one day, the twenty-five with the worst performance will return to their original teams. Understood?"

Ah Hua pondered for a moment and nodded, "Understood."

Yang Kai waved his hand, "Go on, I’ll leave this to you."

"Yes!" Ah Hua turned around with mixed feelings. As she looked up, seventy-four pairs of eyes full of fighting spirit stared at her. There was no need for more words; everyone had heard Yang Kai’s instructions clearly. If they wanted to keep their giant eagle, they would have to outperform the others in this final day.

Soon, seventy-five giant eagles soared into the sky together, making for a spectacular sight.

Yang Kai returned to his wooden hut and swallowed a Yao Beast Inner Core, sitting down to cultivate.

A day later, Ah Hua came to report on the Sky Cavalry. With Yang Kai’s decision, the twenty-five archers who performed the worst were removed, and the spare giant eagles were sent to the royal city.

The following days passed uneventfully. The twelve teams of Shaman Niu's tribe continued their intensive training in an orderly fashion, and as time went on, their coordination became increasingly smooth.

Especially after Yu and Lu joined, the shamanic arts cast by the shamans of Shaman Niu's tribe could now cover all three thousand people, something they had not achieved before.

Previously, to reach this level, Yang Kai or Die had to help, but now, with Yu and Lu, Yang Kai and Die no longer needed to intervene.

The more the shamans interacted with the twins Yu and Lu, the more they realized how strong they were. As expected of Shaman Masters who came from the Shaman God Hall, their mastery and understanding of shamanic arts, as well as their timing when casting them, far surpassed those of ordinary shamans.

Several Shaman Warriors and Shaman Apprentices made rapid progress under their guidance.

Although the twins were reserved, they were not cold. In fact, if one took the initiative, they were easy to get along with. Over these days, several Shaman Warriors and Shaman Apprentices had become familiar with them. If anyone had questions, the twins would patiently teach them, never holding anything back.

After a few days, the two Shaman Masters had gained considerable prestige within Shaman Niu's tribe.

Yang Kai was happy to be free of those duties and spent his days hidden in the wooden hut, cultivating diligently.

The Ninth-Tier Inner Core could no longer provide him with much improvement, so Yang Kai began to consume Tenth-Tier Inner Cores.

In just three days, he reached the Middle-Level Great Shaman Master rank.

On this day, the calm was finally broken.

When the dull sound of the horn echoed in the royal city, all the busy members of the Barbarian Tribe fell silent. Hundreds of thousands of eyes turned toward the royal city, and everyone sensed the coming storm in that heavy horn call.

One figure after another soared into the sky, rushing toward the royal city.

Soon, many Great Shaman Masters arrived at the palace where they had gathered last time.

This time, there were half again as many Great Shaman Masters as before. Last time, many were still on the road and had not yet reached the royal city, but today, all the Great Shaman Masters of the Southern Barbarian Tribe had assembled here.

This was essentially the gathering of the elite of a great tribe.

Shaman Dang was nowhere to be seen in the main hall. After the Great Shaman Masters arrived, they naturally formed small groups, discussing the progress of the war. However, since everyone had been training their subordinates outside the royal city these days, information was scarce, and no one knew the true situation outside. Their discussions led nowhere, and they could only wait for Shaman Dang to come and explain.

After a while, Shaman Dang appeared with a grave expression.

The noisy hall instantly fell silent, and countless eyes turned to him. At the sight of Shaman Dang’s expression, many felt a jolt in their hearts, sensing something was wrong.

Sure enough, the moment Shaman Dang spoke, his words were as shocking as a thunderbolt: “A few days ago, the Shaman Saints ventured deep into the demon territory and fought a bloody battle against the demon experts. After that clash, three of them were gravely wounded and fell into a deep slumber. Only Lord Xiong managed to escape by sheer luck, though he too has lost the strength to fight again for the time being.”

Everyone in the hall was shocked. All the Great Shaman Masters gaped as if an invisible fist had struck them, unable to close their mouths, utterly stunned.

No wonder everyone reacted this way. In the era without the Shaman God, the Shaman Saints were synonymous with the strongest experts.

There were only four Shaman Saints in the entire Barbarian Tribe. A few days ago, when Shaman Dang said all four had taken action, many still held out hope that the four Shaman Saints could wipe out the demons.

But after hearing this shocking news today, everyone realized that the demons were not so easily crushed.

All four Shaman Saints had acted, three were gravely wounded and fell into slumber, and only one escaped, unable to fight again for now. For the demons to inflict such damage, it was clear they had beings on par with the Shaman Saints.

If even the Shaman Saints were not their match, what could the rest of them do, no matter how many they were? For a moment, many faces turned pale.

Shaman Dang looked around and raised his voice, "But the sacrifices of the Shaman Saints were not in vain. According to the information from Xiong, the strongest among the demons also suffered heavy losses, so this battle was not our defeat."

Hearing this, everyone finally breathed a sigh of relief. The four Shaman Saints had acted personally—this was an acceptable result.

"The Shaman Saints need to recuperate, and the demon experts do as well. At this critical moment, the outcome of the war will be decided by us. If we can take this opportunity to wipe out the remaining demons, we can preserve our Barbarian Tribe! But if we fail, this land will be utterly lost. We have only one choice—fight to the death!"

"Fight to the death!"

"Fight to the death!"

In the main hall, all the Great Shaman Masters shouted in unison, their blood boiling.

Shaman Dang looked on with satisfaction at their response, then pressed his hand down to quiet them. "The sacrifices of the Shaman Saints have slowed the demons’ advance. Now, it seems they are building a defensive line based on the four territories they seized. Our task is to take back those four territories and leave them no foothold."

"Sir, what exactly should we do?" someone asked.

Shaman Dang replied, "See for yourselves."

As he spoke, he waved his hand, and with a surge of shamanic power, a massive map appeared in the air above the hall. On the map, a large swath of blood-red territory made everyone’s expression turn grave.

Everyone could see clearly—those were the four territories occupied by the demons.

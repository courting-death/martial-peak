## Chapter 3113 — Kill Them

When the old Sect Master left all those years ago, he had entrusted Yunxia Sect to her care, so she remained here for a hundred years. At the time, she was at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, while Han Zhengyuan had only just entered the Dao Origin Realm. Without her protection, Yunxia Sect would certainly have had enemies knocking at its door for revenge. How could it have preserved its ancestral foundation? A hundred years later, Han Zhengyuan had finally advanced somewhat, yet his attitude toward her had grown worse and worse.

If he could not even tolerate her, a meritorious elder who had sheltered Yunxia Sect for a hundred years, how could he possibly tolerate Su Yan, a rising star? If Su Yan were truly allowed to advance to the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm as well, then once Master and Disciple joined hands, Yunxia Sect might no longer answer to the Han name.

Sending Su Yan out to guard the Fire Cloud Mine was both a warning and a way to pressure her. If she knew what was good for her and married into the Han family as soon as possible, her future would surely be smooth. But ten years had passed, and Su Yan had not returned to Yunxia Sect even once. She had set her heart on staying there.

"How this Sect Master conducts himself is not for you to criticize!" Han Zhengyuan snorted coldly. "As an Honored Elder of Yunxia Sect, you draw my Yunxia Sect's stipend, yet not only do you fail to share this Sect Master's burdens, you even collude with outside enemies. Ruan Biting, today this Sect Master strips you of your title as Elder. From now on, you and my Yunxia Sect have nothing to do with one another." He felt utterly delighted. This was an unexpected joy. He had long wanted to drive Ruan Biting out of the Sect, but unfortunately he had never found a suitable reason. After all, in earlier years this woman had protected Yunxia Sect all along and had done Yunxia Sect some kindness. If he acted without proper cause, it would inevitably unsettle people's hearts.

Now he had that reason!

Ruan Biting reached up and smoothed the hair beside her ear, her expression calm. "Very well. I suppose I have not failed the old Sect Master's trust. By now, Yunxia Sect should be fine even without me. Then, as you wish, from this day forth, I, Ruan Biting, have nothing more to do with Yunxia Sect."

"See the guest out!" Han Zhengyuan barked.

Ruan Biting said, "He leaves with me!" Her slender jade finger pointed at Yang Kai. Since he was Su Yan's husband, there was no way she could let him remain here alone.

"Wishful thinking!" Han Zhengyuan shouted angrily.

Ruan Biting said quietly, "It was not easy for Yunxia Sect to preserve this ancestral foundation. Do not let it be ruined in your hands." Although she was bitterly disappointed in Han Zhengyuan, this was still a Sect she had protected for a hundred years, and she had some feelings for it. How could she bear to see it fall to ruin in a single day?

"I am the Sect Master of Yunxia Sect!" Han Zhengyuan raged.

"Han Zhengyuan, you must think this through!" Ruan Biting shouted through gritted teeth.

Han Zhengyuan cast her a cold glance. “If you refuse to leave, then you can stay here as well.”

The last trace of expectation in her heart shattered. With such a Sect Master, how could Yunxia Sect endure? Protecting it for a hundred years was already the limit of what she could do.

"Are you finished?" Yang Kai suddenly spoke. A faint red light glimmered in his eyes as he looked from Han Zhengyuan to the Yunxia Sect Elders and back again, chilling everyone who saw it.

A trace of unease welled up in Han Zhengyuan's heart, but it was quickly replaced by anger. He waved his hand and said, "Take him down!"

Two figures pounced over from left and right, their forms like lightning.

In the blink of an eye, however, they flew back even faster than they had come. Still in midair, both exploded into clouds of blood mist, leaving no bones or corpses behind.

The main hall fell silent. Everyone stared in stunned disbelief, their mouths hanging open.

Ruan Biting closed her eyes. Han Zhengyuan, oh Han Zhengyuan, what you did today was the stupidest decision you have ever made! Did you think I wanted to take him away for his sake? I only wanted to help Yunxia Sect one last time.

Even after being struck by a deadly poison, Yang Kai remained perfectly calm, and Ruan Biting had already seen that he was unfathomable. Add in that strange little girl, and who in Yunxia Sect could possibly match them? Laughable that Han Zhengyuan still thought everything was under his control, unaware that the roles of hunter and prey had quietly reversed before anyone noticed.

"Activate the array!" After a moment of stunned silence, Han Zhengyuan shouted, his hands swiftly forming seal after seal.

Runes flashed inside the main hall, and a Spirit Array suddenly enveloped it. Only then did the many Yunxia Sect Elders recover the slightest sense of security, though they still looked at Yang Kai as if he were the most terrifying demon imaginable.

In that instant just now, he seemed to have merely swung his fist casually, yet the two First Stage Dao Origin Realm Elders who had pounced at him were smashed to pieces. What kind of power was that? Fortunately, the Sect Master had possessed the foresight not to clash with him at the mountain gate, but had led him here instead.

Now, with the Spirit Array assisting them and their own strength added on top, they would surely be invincible.

"Kill them!" Yang Kai stretched out one finger and lightly tapped forward.

Liu Yan opened her mouth, and a stream of flame poured out as if alive. In an instant, firelight surged through the entire main hall, and scorching heat spread outward, making the runes of the hall's great array flicker unsteadily.

Everyone was horrified and hurriedly used their own methods to resist.

He Yunxiang was stunned as well.

She had spent some time with Liu Yan, but she had never imagined that this delicate little girl, lovely as carved jade and powder, could unleash such terrifying power. A Yunxia Sect Elder used every ounce of strength he had to resist that flame, but it was useless. The fire climbed over his whole body and burned him into a pile of ash in the blink of an eye.

That was a Dao Origin Realm cultivator! She was not surprised that Yang Kai could easily kill someone in the Dao Origin Realm. Along this journey, she had already witnessed Yang Kai's strength. But what exactly was going on with this little girl called Liu Yan?

Could her strength be nearly equal to Yang Kai's as well?

"Go!" Yang Kai grabbed Ruan Biting's wrist.

Ruan Biting asked blankly, "Where?" She had been stunned too. Although she had previously seen Liu Yan heavily injure that red-faced Elder Chen in a single move and knew her strength was extraordinary, she still had not realized just how much she had underestimated Liu Yan.

With such strength, who in Yunxia Sect could stop her?

"Take me to find Su Yan!"

Without another word, he pulled Ruan Biting toward the exit. When they reached the door, he punched out, and the main hall's Spirit Array shattered with a crash.

Behind them came miserable screams and Han Zhengyuan's cries. "Elder Ruan, Elder Ruan, save me!"

Ruan Biting looked back, a trace of reluctance showing on her face.

"If you ask, I will spare his life!" Yang Kai seemed to sense something and looked at her as he spoke. Su Yan's Master deserved at least this much face.

Ruan Biting opened her mouth, but in the end it turned into a sigh. "Let's go."

All debts and grudges had been settled. Yunxia Sect no longer had the slightest connection to her, yet she had failed the old Sect Master's entrustment from back then. Moreover, although this was her first time meeting Yang Kai, she had gained a slight understanding of his nature. This young man seemed extremely decisive in his actions. Even if she pleaded for mercy and he spared Han Zhengyuan's life, he would certainly cripple him.

For a cultivator, being crippled was worse than dying outright. She wondered whether Han Zhengyuan was regretting now that he had not listened to her advice.

He probably was...

In front of the Space Array, several Yunxia Sect disciples looked at one another, then cupped their fists together and said, "Greetings, Elder Ruan."

The leader asked, "Where is Elder Ruan going?"

Ruan Biting was still somewhat dazed as she said, "To Fire Cloud Mountain." The Fire Cloud Mine was located inside the belly of Fire Cloud Mountain.

That disciple was astonished. "Elder Ruan is going to Fire Cloud Mountain too?"

Ruan Biting nodded, but Yang Kai caught the implication in the disciple's words. "What, has someone else gone to Fire Cloud Mountain besides Elder Ruan?"

"You are..." Seeing that Yang Kai was unfamiliar, the disciple could not help asking.

Ruan Biting also came back to her senses. "Who went to Fire Cloud Mountain?"

When an Elder asked a question, that disciple naturally did not dare refuse to answer. He hurriedly said respectfully, "A quarter hour ago, the Young Sect Master took two Elders to Fire Cloud Mountain."

"What?" Ruan Biting's expression changed, and she cried out softly, "Not good." She reached out and pushed aside the disciple blocking the way, then stepped onto the Space Array with Yang Kai and urged her strength. The array's light flared, wrapping around the two of them, and when the light faded, they had already vanished.

Within the belly of Fire Cloud Mountain, mine tunnels stretched in all directions, interlacing like jagged teeth. This place produced Fire Cloud Ore and was one of Yunxia Sect's extremely important industries, with a Dao Origin Realm Elder stationed here year-round.

However, the environment here was harsh. Under ordinary circumstances, Yunxia Sect's Elders were unwilling to take this assignment, and even the two Elders who cultivated Fire Attribute Secret Arts would not stay here permanently.

As a result, they basically took turns guarding it. Each Elder would keep watch for six months to a year before being replaced.

But ever since ten years ago, only one person had been stationed here, and no other Elders had come.

Her white robes were whiter than snow; pure as ice and clean as jade, she stood alone, aloof from the world.

Such a beauty would stir goodwill in anyone who saw her, yet she had been sent to guard this filthy, chaotic mine deep in the mountain. Her clean white figure formed an extremely sharp contrast with the disorder around her. Wherever she stood became pure land.

The disciples all knew that she had offended the Young Sect Master, which was why she had been given such a bitter assignment. In their hearts, they were deeply dissatisfied with the Young Sect Master, but they did not dare say so aloud. They could only silently ache for her. Such a beauty should be cherished properly; how could anyone bear to make her do this sort of thing? The Young Sect Master was truly too heartless.

Ordinary disciples who mined Fire Cloud Ore would return to the Sect every ten days or half a month to rest for a few days, then come back after restoring their strength. Yet she had stayed here for ten full years.

She was the most talented disciple in Yunxia Sect's history, and after entering the Sect, she had advanced from the Void King Realm to the Dao Origin Realm in only five years. But during these ten years, her cultivation had not increased in the slightest. She could only barely keep herself at the Dao Origin Realm without letting her realm fall.

It was all thanks to Ruan Biting supplementing her resources every few days; otherwise, whether she could preserve her Dao Origin Realm at all would have been a question.

Inside the stone cave, a steward-like disciple lowered his head as he reported the recent mining situation to the white-robed woman before him. After spending so long in this place, the steward was also disheveled and filthy. He kept his head down and did not look at the woman before him, not because he did not dare, but because he was unwilling, as if even his own existence profaned that beauty, that purity.

After he hurriedly finished his report, the woman gave a slight nod. "I know. You have worked hard."

Her voice was soft and pleasing to the ear, warm and kind. The steward looked completely satisfied. The report every ten days was what he looked forward to most, for no other reason than to hear her say a single sentence.

"Please rest." The steward respectfully backed away, then suddenly bumped into someone.

Annoyed, he turned to look. Who was so lacking in sense that he had actually entered this place without being announced? Did he not know this was Senior Sister Su's place of quiet cultivation?

When he saw who it was, he was startled and hurriedly stepped aside, bowing. "Young Sect Master!"

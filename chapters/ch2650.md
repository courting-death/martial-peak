## Chapter 2650 — No Need to Stand on Ceremony

Over a month ago, when Yang Kai left, he entrusted the method for arranging the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation to Ji Yao. For more than a month now, every disciple of Ice Heart Valley had been diligently practicing this formation, and naturally, Bing Yun and the others had also trained in it for a while.

This was originally the Black Tortoise Sect’s core formation, a secret never passed on, with extremely formidable defensive power. Now, with seven Emperor Realm masters joining forces to form it, and with someone like Bing Yun, a Third Stage Emperor Realm, leading, the resulting formation could truly be called impregnable.

Although Bing Yun and the others did not doubt the power of the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation, it was their first time using it, so they did not know its true might. Seeing how it easily forced back the combined assault of Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo, they were all overjoyed and deeply moved, knowing that if Yang Kai had not left behind this peerless formation, Ice Heart Valley would have been doomed today.

Although the Grand Guardian Array had been broken, with the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation in place, perhaps the seven thousand disciples could avoid heavy casualties—some might even escape, preserving the Sect’s legacy for the future.

“Damn it!” Compared to the astonishment of Ice Heart Valley’s people, Feng Xuan was furious beyond measure.

Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley were both top sects of the Northern Territory, and knew each other’s strengths and secrets well. Yet, Feng Xuan had never even heard of such a wondrous formation before, nor did he know where Bing Yun had acquired it. His confidence in breaking through was instantly shattered, leaving him in a foul mood.

“All disciples, heed my command! Follow me and carve a bloody path out!” Bing Yun shouted coldly.

“Yes!” everyone responded in unison.

“What are you all standing around for? Attack! Kill them!” Feng Xuan roared angrily. Seeing that even joining forces with Yao Zhuo yielded no advantage, he could only resort to a strategy of overwhelming numbers.

This formation was indeed wondrous and unparalleled, but Feng Xuan could also see that Bing Yun and the others had not practiced it for long and were not yet proficient. In other words, they could not bring out the formation’s full power.

So even though the formation was established, its amplification was limited. On their side, more than twenty Emperor Realm masters were working together. It might not be impossible to break through.

At Feng Xuan’s command, the Emperor Realm masters all steeled themselves, surging their Emperor Yuan and summoning their Imperial Treasures, launching attacks at Bing Yun and the others.

In an instant, Heaven-and-Earth Energy was thrown into chaos, the void was torn asunder, the world turned upside down, and the earth trembled incessantly.

Nearly thirty Emperor Realm masters were fighting fiercely here. Such a battle was extremely rare, something ordinary people would never witness in their lifetimes.

All kinds of uniquely shaped Imperial Treasures unleashed tremendous power, transforming into waves of attacks that struck the Black Tortoise phantom. Yet, the Black Tortoise phantom was truly extraordinary—no matter how it was struck, it remained unscathed.

Off to the side, Nanmen Dajun watched with bright, piercing eyes. After a long while, he suddenly realized and exclaimed, “Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation!”

As expected of a formation grandmaster, his insight was truly remarkable. Although it was his first time seeing this formation, he still recognized its origin based on his experience and knowledge.

“So it’s the Black Tortoise Sect’s secret art!” Nanmen Dajun was overwhelmed with excitement.

As the saying goes, every field has its master. His attainments in formations were profound, and his love for rare and strange formations was like a lecher’s passion for beauties. He had once risked his life to study an ancient formation, spending three years to unravel it, nearly dying in the process.

He had seen records of the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation in ancient texts. He knew it was the Black Tortoise Sect’s secret art from the Eastern Territory, but when the Black Tortoise Sect was destroyed, the formation was lost.

The ancient texts said this formation was created by the Black Tortoise Sect after observing the Divine Spirit Black Tortoise. The Divine Spirit Black Tortoise was one of the most supreme beings—how could a formation inspired by its majestic form be anything less?

Nanmen Dajun, his curiosity piqued, had naturally studied it for some time, but unfortunately, he could never find the method to arrange it.

Who would have thought that today, in Ice Heart Valley, he would witness this lost formation?

“This trip was worth it! Truly worth it!” Nanmen Dajun’s eyes shone, completely forgetting this was a life-and-death struggle, instead observing with great interest. Judging by his eager posture, he seemed ready to rush forward and study it up close.

However, though he was feverish with excitement, he knew that if he really charged in, with his cultivation, he would likely be caught in the crossfire and die without even knowing how.

Meanwhile, the battle between Ice Heart Valley and Wenqing Sect was raging fiercely.

Although Wenqing Sect had more than twenty Emperor Realm masters working together, outnumbering and outmatching Ice Heart Valley in both numbers and strength, they still could not break through the Black Tortoise Seven-Section Formation’s defense.

All attacks were blocked by Bing Yun and the others, and the occasional deadly counterattack from Bing Yun’s side left the Wenqing Sect members drenched in cold sweat, scrambling to cope.

Especially the profound intent and the pervasive laws of ice in the surroundings made it difficult for one’s power to circulate smoothly.

As time passed, the Wenqing Sect group grew more and more alarmed. If this continued, they feared they would exhaust themselves without ever breaking through, and would eventually be at the mercy of others.

After all, Bing Yun and the others were defending rather than attacking, and with the aid of the formation, they expended far less energy than their opponents.

Many began to panic, and their attacks lost the ferocity they had at the start.

Sensing this, Feng Xuan, who was like a volcano erupting, suddenly shouted, “Bing Yun, you may be able to resist for now, but I’d like to see how your seven thousand disciples withstand this! Our sect has another ten Emperor Realm masters who have entered the heart of Ice Heart Valley. By now, the valley must be awash in blood!”

The Emperor Realm masters on Wenqing Sect’s side were not just the twenty or so present, but more than thirty in total. Only about a third were actual Wenqing Sect members; the rest were helpers recruited by the sect.

Before coming here, ten Emperor Realm masters had been ordered to lead people to slaughter the disciples of Ice Heart Valley. With the strength Ice Heart Valley had left, they could not possibly resist.

Sure enough, as soon as Feng Xuan said this, the faces of everyone from Ice Heart Valley changed.

Bing Yun gritted her teeth and said in a low voice, “Go!”

As she spoke, she led her disciples in an attempt to break out.

Feng Xuan’s face twisted with malice. With a thunderous crash, his Wind and Thunder Divine Spear struck down as he roared, “Foolish dreams! Bing Yun, just watch as I flatten Ice Heart Valley and leave you trembling in despair! When my ten Emperor Realm masters return, it will be your day of death!”

All this time, Yao Zhuo had remained silent, but now he sneered arrogantly, “This is the fate of those who oppose my Wenqing Sect!”

His words carried a deeper meaning, making the Emperor Realm masters who were not from Wenqing Sect fall silent with fear. They resolved that after this battle, they must pledge allegiance to Wenqing Sect, or else, even in the vast Northern Territory, they would have nowhere to stand.

“Ten Emperor Realm masters?”

Just then, a lazy voice suddenly rang out. The sound reached the ears of everyone from Ice Heart Valley, filling them with joy as they all looked toward its source.

Ji Yao called out in delight, “Senior Brother Yang!”

Feng Xuan’s eyes nearly popped out of his head as he roared, “You little beast!”

He recognized the voice as Yang Kai’s—the one who had killed his son. Just over a month ago, he had encountered Yang Kai outside Ice Heart Valley; how could he forget?

Suddenly, Feng Xuan turned, glaring murderously, his killing intent surging, oblivious to everything else.

“Are these the ten Emperor Realm masters you mentioned? I brought them here for you. No need to thank me—it was no trouble at all.”

In the air, Yang Kai strolled forward, showing not the slightest hint of tension. Instead, he walked at a leisurely pace, and as he appeared, he waved his hand.

Clatter...

Corpses suddenly fell from the sky, landing on the ground. Each corpse was dressed in fine clothes, clearly people of high status, but now, they were lifeless, each with a fatal wound and blood still flowing.

“Ah, that’s Feng Ziping, the Great Elder of Cang Le Valley!”

“Liu Rui of Hundred Refinements Palace!”

“The Sect Master of Star Gate!”

...

Exclamations rang out as the Emperor Realm masters attacking Bing Yun and the others swept their eyes over the corpses, crying out in shock.

They recognized every one of them. Just a short while ago, these people had parted ways with them, with some following Feng Xuan to block Bing Yun, while the rest led a hundred thousand troops into Ice Heart Valley.

But now, all of them had become corpses.

The crowd counted—ten Emperor Realm masters, not one more, not one less.

In other words, those who had separated from them earlier were all dead.

In that moment, more than twenty Emperor Realm masters all gasped, feeling chills run through their bodies, their hands and feet turning cold, as a wave of icy dread shot from their tailbones to the tops of their heads.

All attacks ceased as everyone stared in disbelief at the ten corpses.

How long had it been? How could they all be dead already? How could Ice Heart Valley still possess such overwhelming strength?

Compared to the horror of Wenqing Sect’s people, those from Ice Heart Valley were filled with joy.

Especially Bing Yun and Ji Yao, who knew Yang Kai’s plan. Now, seeing the ten corpses, they immediately understood that Yang Kai must have brought some incredible helpers; otherwise, it would have been impossible to accomplish this so quietly.

“Yang Kai...” Bing Yun’s lips trembled as she looked at Yang Kai standing there, not knowing what to say.

Yang Kai nodded to Bing Yun and said, “I came back late.”

“Not late. It’s good that you’re back.” Bing Yun slowly shook her head.

She knew that now that Yang Kai had returned, Ice Heart Valley was saved.

“Sect Master Feng, it’s been a while.” Yang Kai turned to look at the ashen-faced Feng Xuan and grinned.

“You little beast!” Feng Xuan nearly ground his teeth to dust as he shouted, “With your abilities, how could you possibly kill them?!”

“Well...” Yang Kai smiled mysteriously. “Why should I tell you?”

“Wretch, give me your life!” At that moment, Yao Zhuo suddenly shouted, surging his Emperor Yuan as his long blade tore through the air, slashing at Yang Kai with terrifying force.

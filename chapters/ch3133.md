## Chapter 3133 — A Single Spark

"Brother Peng, why don't we just run?" Near the blood pool, one of the several dozen cultivators who had been prepared to die suddenly spoke up.

He no longer wanted to throw his life away for nothing and urged, "As long as we live, there will still be another chance."

Peng Shizong smiled bitterly when he heard this and was just about to say something when he suddenly turned to look to the side. Crimson light was surging there, and the Blood Sea Banner hanging in midair billowed without stop, sending bloody light roiling as if something were about to burst out from within.

"It's too late." Peng Shizong slowly shook his head.

Had that young man been killed so quickly? He laughed at himself. He had been far too naive to place any hope in him.

Seeing this, the others were speechless as well. Earlier, when Yang Kai had spoken as if even the Heavens were beneath him, they had thought he must have some extraordinary ability. Who would have known that after entering the Blood Sea Banner, he would be dealt with in less than ten breaths? It was laughable enough to make one's teeth fall out.

No one was in the mood to laugh, because they were the ones who would die next.

The next moment, everyone froze, their eyes slowly widening as they stared ahead in disbelief.

Someone leisurely leapt out from the Blood Sea Banner, but it was not the Netherworld Sect old woman or the old man they had imagined. It was actually that reckless, muddleheaded young man.

The young man looked as if he had merely gone for a stroll, calm and unruffled. After escaping from the Blood Sea Banner, he beckoned and put away that Secret Treasure built from countless human lives, then looked around and frowned. "Where are the others?"

No one answered. Everyone stood rigidly in place as if struck by lightning, their minds a muddled blank, unable to recover.

"I'm asking you." Yang Kai frowned. What were these fellows doing? Faced with their life-saving benefactor, should they not be falling to their knees and crying out that they would surely repay him, even in the next life? Why did they all look like fools? Had their brains broken?

"They ran... ran away." Peng Shizong swallowed and answered somewhat awkwardly.

Yang Kai swept out his Divine Sense and found that many people were indeed scattering in all directions a hundred zhang away. Their cultivations were sealed, however, so they were not moving very quickly.

"Let them run, then." Yang Kai waved his hand, and a gentle force swept out, brushing over Peng Shizong and the others like a light breeze.

Peng Shizong and the others' eyes widened even further, because with that single sweep from Yang Kai, all the restrictions inside their bodies had been undone. Long-lost strength flowed freely through their meridians, filling them with the urge to throw back their heads and howl.

"Sir, those two..." Peng Shizong looked at Yang Kai hesitantly. Although he had a guess, he did not dare be certain.

"The culprits have been executed. That should count as justice for the living beings who died in this city, though unfortunately, I came too late." Yang Kai sighed. "You should all flee for your lives too. Don't get caught again."

This time, they had been lucky. Next time, that might not be the case.

His figure flickered, and he sped out of the city, vanishing without a trace in the blink of an eye.

Peng Shizong was dumbfounded. Although he had guessed the outcome, hearing Yang Kai confirm it still felt inconceivable.

Those two Netherworld Sect experts were both Void King Realm masters, and they had the Ten Thousand Souls Banner and Blood Sea Banner in hand. That young man had even gone deep into the Blood Sea Banner, yet he had killed them in less than ten breaths?

What astonishing means. What terrifying strength. He could hardly believe it, yet he had no choice but to believe. After all, the Blood Sea Banner itself had been taken away. If the Netherworld Sect old woman were still alive, how could she possibly allow that to happen?

Who exactly was that young man? What cultivation did he have? How could he possibly do such a thing?

Peng Shizong slapped his forehead in vexation. He had been so shocked just now that he had forgotten to ask the other's name. Now he did not even know the great name of his life-saving benefactor. It was simply discourteous.

Just as he was thinking this, a tiny speck suddenly appeared in his field of vision. He blinked, and the speck became the size of a fist. In another instant, it grew to the size of an infant. By the time he came back to his senses, his benefactor was already before him.

Peng Shizong stared blankly at Yang Kai. "Sir, you..."

"Aih, I might as well see this good deed through to the end." Yang Kai reached out and drew Peng Shizong toward him, and Peng Shizong floated over against his will, stopping directly in front of him.

"Sir!" Peng Shizong was somewhat flustered and did not know what Yang Kai intended, but he sensed no malice from him, so he was not too worried.

"Open your mouth!" Yang Kai barked.

Peng Shizong obediently opened his mouth.

Yang Kai flicked his finger, and a Spiritual Pill flew into Peng Shizong's mouth and rolled down into his stomach.

The force binding him quietly vanished, and Peng Shizong dropped straight down. After landing steadily, his eyes suddenly widened, and he immediately sat cross-legged. The other cultivators watched, at first unsure what had happened, but after observing for a moment, they were all shocked.

An extremely powerful aura was surging out from Peng Shizong's body. He was like a trapped beast, that aura crashing endlessly inside him as if trying to break free of some shackle.

Strange vibrations came from between Heaven and Earth. A moment later, dark clouds gathered in the sky, and streaks of lightning roamed and shuttled through them.

This was... he was about to break through?

Peng Shizong was already at the Third Stage of the Origin Returning Realm. If he broke through, would that not make him a Void King?

Many of the people present were familiar with Peng Shizong, so they naturally knew his circumstances. Although Peng Shizong had some talent in cultivation, he was still a little short of advancing to the Void King Realm, and closing that gap would require at least several dozen to a hundred years of bitter cultivation.

Now, without the slightest warning, he was about to break through on the spot. This was obviously all that young man's doing, all thanks to that one Spiritual Pill!

In an instant, everyone looked at Peng Shizong with envy. This was a tremendous opportunity, and Yang Kai's image in their hearts became even more unfathomable. Easily killing two Void King Realm masters had already been shocking enough, but now, with a casual move, he had produced such a generous gift. Just what was this person's background?

Hong long long...

In just a few breaths, the dark clouds in the sky grew thicker and heavier, like a mass of pitch-black cotton wadding spread across the heavens.

Yang Kai raised his head, displeasure on his face. Too slow. He was in a hurry to go to Di Chen Star. Although he was willing to lend Peng Shizong a hand and do what he could, he did not want to waste too much time on this.

At this rate, the Baptism of Heaven and Earth probably would not descend for half a day.

An idea struck him, and Yang Kai flashed to Peng Shizong's side. Urging his Emperor Yuan, he unsealed a small portion of his cultivation.

Hong long long...

The tumbling dark clouds in the sky indeed grew increasingly restless, and the lightning seemed to become far more violent as well.

Yang Kai chuckled. It had only been a sudden idea, but he had not expected it to actually work. His existence was a burden to this Star Domain, and these days he had been constantly suppressing his cultivation to avoid rejection from the World Laws. Now that he had taken the initiative to unseal it, he had indeed drawn the hostility of Heaven and Earth. Combined with the Baptism of Heaven and Earth caused by Peng Shizong's breakthrough to a great realm, it sped up the gathering of Heaven-and-Earth Energy.

After silently estimating the timing, Yang Kai quickly sealed his cultivation again once he felt it was about right.

Heavenly might had already formed. The arrow was on the string; it had to be loosed.

And Peng Shizong had become the best target.

From beginning to end, only a dozen or so breaths had passed before this Heavenly might took shape.

Although Peng Shizong was meditating and refining the medicinal effects, he was not completely unaware of the outside world. At this moment, his hair stood on end, and he felt certain he was going to die. After all, breaking through a great realm required a long period of preparation. Today's events had happened so suddenly, so where would he have found time to prepare anything? He was not even wearing a protective Secret Treasure.

What in the world was this Sir doing? He had given him a Spiritual Pill and helped him break through, yet now he was pushing him down a road to death. The ups and downs of life were simply too thrilling.

Ka-cha...

A bolt of lightning as thick as an infant's arm struck down from the sky, blasting straight toward Peng Shizong.

My life is over! Peng Shizong fell into despair. At this moment, the enormous medicinal effect was crashing through his body, and he could not even flee. He could only sit on the ground and endure that fatal blow.

The lightning struck him, and Peng Shizong trembled. His whole body went numb, and then pain followed. Yet instead of being frightened, he was delighted. He was not dead? The power of this Heaven-and-Earth Energy Baptism was weaker than he had expected. If that was the case... he might actually be able to endure it.

If he endured it, he would become a Void King, and a whole new world would open before him. In this age of endless war, he would not dare say he could roam freely without fear, but he would at least have some power to protect himself and would not be casually captured as he had been this time.

"Why are you letting your thoughts wander? Guard your mind, comprehend the power of Heaven and Earth, and condense your own Domain Field!" A voice like a morning bell and evening drum suddenly rang in Peng Shizong's ear, jolting his spirit. He hurriedly kept his Sea of Consciousness clear.

Another streak of lightning fell.

Only then did Peng Shizong sense that when the Heaven-and-Earth Energy Baptism descended, the young man standing beside him suddenly waved his hand and actually drew away the energy falling from the sky, splitting it apart.

More than half shot toward the horizon, and only a smaller half landed on him.

He could actually interfere with the might of Heaven and Earth? Peng Shizong no longer knew how to express what he was feeling.

The cultivators watching from all around had long since been dumbstruck as well. Such a method was simply unheard of and unseen. This was already a power beyond the limits of this world.

The might of Heaven and Earth continued to descend, and Yang Kai kept waving his hand, guiding it away and splitting it apart. With him acting as Protector, Peng Shizong would find it difficult to die even if he wanted to.

One hour later, Peng Shizong rose to his feet. Although he looked somewhat battered, with ragged clothes and a weary expression, a radiance he had never possessed before shone in his eyes. He bowed all the way to the ground. "Peng Shizong thanks Sir for helping me advance to the Void King Realm. For this great kindness, I have no way to repay you..."

"Enough, enough." Yang Kai raised his hand. "If I wanted repayment, I wouldn't have gone to all this trouble."

The value of that one Spiritual Pill alone was more than a mere Peng Shizong could ever repay.

He had helped only because he did not want them to fall into the hands of the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators again after he saved them. With one more Void King Realm master, they would at least have a little more strength to resist.

Cuiwei Star was a Cultivation Star of the Hengluo Star Domain. How could they tolerate outsiders throwing their weight around here? Sooner or later, they would have to drive all these people out. Leaving behind a single spark might one day set the entire star ablaze.

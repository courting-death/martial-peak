## Chapter 2773 — Taiyi Divine Water

In the end, this bottle containing three Taiwei Soul Refining Pills was taken by a red-robed elder for the price of five million Upper-Level Source Crystals.

This red-robed elder seemed to be the Sect Master of a third-class sect, surnamed Yu, though Yang Kai couldn’t recall his full name. Earlier today, when Wen Zishan led him over to offer a toast, he had met this red-robed elder in passing. But even with Yang Kai’s excellent memory, it was impossible to remember the names of over a hundred Emperor Realm experts in a single day.

Or perhaps, he simply couldn’t be bothered to remember.

Next, Qiu Ran took out two more treasures, both auctioned off for Source Crystals just like the Taiwei Soul Refining Pills, sparking a fierce scramble among the Emperor Realm experts present. In the end, a man and a woman each won one at a high price.

After these three treasures, the atmosphere of the exchange gathering was instantly enlivened.

Once Qiu Ran stepped down, the red-robed elder who had won the Taiwei Soul Refining Pills was the first to leap onto the high platform. After making a cupped-fist salute, he smiled and said, “This old man will make a humble offering.”

As he spoke, he lifted his hand, revealing a crimson berry in his palm. He announced, “A three-thousand-year-old Scarlet Blood Red Dragon Fruit, to be exchanged for a water-attribute Spiritual Medicine or an Imperial-Grade Pill of equal value.”

As soon as the Scarlet Blood Red Dragon Fruit appeared, a peculiar fragrance wafted through the hall, refreshing everyone’s mind and invigorating their spirits.

The Emperor Realm experts present were all sharp-eyed and immediately recognized the Scarlet Blood Red Dragon Fruit as a rare treasure. Quite a few were tempted on the spot, especially those who cultivated fire-attribute techniques or secret arts, whose faces lit up with eagerness.

Four or five rings were silently tossed up from below.

The red-robed elder accepted them, inspecting each one in turn, his face showing a look of consideration. In the end, he kept one ring, returned the rest, and stepped down from the platform, handing the Scarlet Blood Red Dragon Fruit to the person who had made the exchange.

With the red-robed elder setting a good example, the exchanges that followed grew even livelier and much more frequent.

As soon as one Emperor Realm expert stepped down, another would immediately leap onto the platform, take out their item for exchange, and state their terms.

Except for a few who asked exorbitant prices or set overly harsh conditions, almost everyone managed to trade for what they wanted.

Yang Kai sat below the stage, with no intention of participating for the moment, but he watched with great interest. Though he was now quite wealthy and unimpressed by ordinary treasures, every item brought to this exchange was a prized possession of an Emperor Realm expert—strange and marvelous treasures that greatly broadened Yang Kai’s horizons. In the future, he would be able to recognize similar items at a glance.

Even Chen Qian and Di Rong, the two Elders of Qingyang Shrine, each made a trade, exchanging for something they needed from the Emperor Realm experts nearby.

In such exchanges, some might suffer a slight loss, but it hardly mattered. If the value gap between the items was too great, Source Crystals could make up the difference. Everyone present had keen judgment, so no one would let another take too much advantage. Hoping to pick up a bargain here was impossible.

“Didn’t see anything you want?” Gao Xueting suddenly sent a voice transmission.

Yang Kai shook his head and replied, “Let’s just watch for now.”

“If there’s anything you want, just tell your Senior Sister Chen. She manages the Shrine’s treasury and can help you exchange for one or two items. Consider it a small benefit for your first time as a Shrine Elder.”

As the two were speaking, a strange fluctuation suddenly came from the high platform.

Everyone looked over and saw a middle-aged man on the platform, holding a transparent porcelain bottle. Inside, a small amount of liquid swayed gently, and the peculiar energy fluctuation was emanating from this droplet-like substance.

“This is…”

Yang Kai’s eyes lit up. He vaguely felt he had seen this thing somewhere before. As he was recalling, a startled cry sounded by his ear: “Taiyi Divine Water!”

The Emperor Realm experts were in an uproar, each one’s gaze brightening as they stared fixedly at the liquid in the porcelain bottle.

It really was Taiyi Divine Water—Yang Kai remembered now.

He had indeed seen it before. Back in the Time Temple of Four Seasons Land, a drop of Taiyi Divine Water had appeared. But that Taiyi Divine Water was ultimately obtained by Murong Xiaoxiao. Who knew if it had been used by now?

In the Star Realm, there were Three Divine Waters: Soul Cleansing Divine Water, Life Jade Nectar, and Immortal Source Liquid. Each was a creation of heaven and earth, with tremendous benefits for a cultivator’s growth.

Yang Kai had obtained all Three Divine Waters before, but there was another Divine Water, not counted among the three, whose value and effects surpassed even them.

That was the Taiyi Divine Water!

Because its only use was to accelerate the growth of Spiritual Medicines and had little effect on cultivators themselves, it wasn’t included among the Three Divine Waters. Still, no one could deny its rarity.

If a Spiritual Medicine could be nourished by a single drop of Taiyi Divine Water, its growth period could be drastically shortened—a year might equal centuries of age, with no side effects, as if it had grown naturally.

Taiyi Divine Water was also the most coveted Divine Water among alchemists. Imagine: a precious Spiritual Medicine that would otherwise take centuries to mature could bloom and bear fruit the very next year with just one drop, ready for pill refining.

In Yang Kai’s Small Xuan Realm’s medicine garden, there was an abundance of Heavy Earth and quite a few Earth Vein Beads, which also promoted the growth of Spiritual Medicines. Many Spiritual Medicines in the Small Xuan Realm’s medicine garden could grow a hundred years in a single year, similar in effect to Taiyi Divine Water. Still, Yang Kai was deeply tempted by the Taiyi Divine Water.

After all, the Firmament Tree might need Taiyi Divine Water to stimulate its sealing power.

The Firmament Tree, with its gold and silver hues, contained sealing power that could suppress the ancient Demonic Qi in Yang Kai’s body. But if Yang Kai used his Enter Demonic State technique, the seal would be broken, and he would need the Firmament Tree’s power to reseal the Demonic Qi, or else he would surely become a demon.

The Taiyi Divine Water in the middle-aged man’s hand on stage was not much—at most three to five drops. In the hands of others, it could only accelerate the growth of a few precious Spiritual Medicines, but in Yang Kai’s hands, it might save his life at a critical moment.

With this in mind, Yang Kai had already made up his mind.

The middle-aged man on stage looked around and smiled slightly. “I’m sure there’s no need for me to explain the reputation of Taiyi Divine Water. Please make your offers as you see fit. However, I don’t need Source Crystals—if you have something suitable, we’ll trade.”

These words sounded rather sly. He hadn’t set any terms, giving the impression of asking for the moon, which annoyed many Emperor Realm experts below. But the item was his, so whether or not to trade, and how to trade, was up to him. Even if they were displeased, they could only accept it.

After a moment of silence, one Spatial Ring after another flew onto the platform, each containing items for exchange.

Yang Kai also placed a few treasures into his own Spatial Ring—each one a token of genuine sincerity. With so many rare items in his possession, he hardly cared about suffering a small loss. As long as he could obtain the Taiyi Divine Water, any price would be worth it.

The middle-aged man examined the contents of each Spatial Ring, but returned them all to their owners, leaving many Emperor Realm experts disappointed and secretly grinding their teeth, thinking the man was insatiable.

They believed the items in their rings were more than enough to exchange for the Taiyi Divine Water, yet they were all rejected.

When it was Yang Kai’s turn, the middle-aged man glanced at his ring but didn’t return it immediately. Instead, he pondered for a while, holding it in his hand as he continued to inspect the others.

This detail made Yang Kai’s heart skip a beat—he knew his ring had met the man’s requirements, but the man hadn’t made up his mind yet.

Soon, when the middle-aged man checked another ring, his eyes lit up, and without hesitation, he returned Yang Kai’s Spatial Ring.

Yang Kai’s face darkened as he clutched his ring, feeling frustrated.

He felt he had offered enough, and no one else could possibly outbid him. But the owner of the Taiyi Divine Water still didn’t choose him.

That left only two possibilities: either someone else had offered more—which was unlikely, since if Yang Kai claimed to be the second richest here, no one would dare claim first—or the other person had offered exactly what the middle-aged man needed, hitting the mark perfectly.

“Thank you all for your generosity. I’ve decided to trade with this friend.” The middle-aged man finished inspecting all the rings, then smiled and tossed the porcelain bottle containing the Taiyi Divine Water down from the stage.

Someone reached out and caught it, stowing it away in their own Spatial Ring.

Yang Kai glanced at that person, silently memorizing their appearance.

The exchange gathering continued, with good items appearing from time to time, causing a stir among the crowd. But since everyone present was an Emperor Realm expert, order was never lost, and no matter how lively things got, everything proceeded in an orderly fashion.

Almost everyone managed to exchange for something satisfactory, with only a few leaving disappointed.

An hour later, a short, elderly man stepped onto the stage, wearing a kindly smile that immediately put everyone at ease. He said, “I don’t have anything particularly valuable, but since I’m here, I can’t leave empty-handed. I have one item to share with everyone.”

As he spoke, he took out something resembling a lady’s cosmetic case.

The box wasn’t large—about the size of a palm—but it was exquisitely crafted, with a surface dotted with glowing specks, dazzlingly beautiful. Many of the women present couldn’t help but brighten at the sight.

But everyone knew this box couldn’t possibly be an ordinary cosmetic case. Anything an Emperor Realm expert brought out couldn’t be mundane.

One after another, threads of Divine Sense swept over the box, probing its contents. After a short while, many of the Emperor Realm experts wore strange expressions.

“Please take a look, everyone!” The old man smiled and tapped the lid of the box.

In the next moment, a gentle, melodious chime sounded from within, playing a simple yet magical tune. The sound seemed to contain a peculiar power, transforming into an invisible jade hand that soothed everyone’s impatience and anxiety, instantly calming their hearts.

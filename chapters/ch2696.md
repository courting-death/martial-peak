## Chapter 2696 — Please Show Some Respect

He mainly wanted to buy some time. Just now, controlling the Incarnation to unleash that punch from the Xuan Realm Pearl had drained his Divine Sense too much, leaving him with a lingering headache. Fortunately, the Warm Spirit Lotus was working its magic—a cool, soothing sensation spread through his mind, and his depleted Divine Sense was rapidly recovering.

Tan Junhao was already gravely wounded. That punch from the Incarnation was not easy to endure. Even if Tan Junhao were at his peak, Yang Kai would still have the confidence to fight him, let alone now when he was far from his best.

They had never met, yet the man schemed against him from the shadows. Yang Kai was determined to kill him here!

“You little brat, courting death!” Tan Junhao roared, raising both hands and gathering the power of heaven and earth. He gritted his teeth and said, “How dare you defy this old man? Today, I’ll make sure you die knowing why.”

As soon as he finished speaking, figures suddenly appeared all around, gathering at his side.

Yang Kai frowned and looked over at those figures, quickly realizing that they were all Emperor Realm cultivators.

Most were only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, but there were a few at the Second Stage as well. Many of them looked familiar—sect masters and elders from the various great Sects who had attended the auction earlier.

There were quite a few of them, at least twenty or thirty.

These Emperor Realm cultivators had appeared mysteriously, each one looking bewildered. After Tan Junhao activated the formation, they had all been separated and plunged into endless darkness, searching for a way out but never finding one.

Suddenly, a strange force had dragged them all here. Turning around, they immediately spotted Yang Kai and Tan Junhao.

Seeing Tan Junhao’s injuries, all the Emperor Realm cultivators felt a chill in their hearts, secretly shocked.

At this moment, Tan Junhao was radiating the powerful aura of the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, yet he was still injured—and quite seriously at that. Besides him, only Yang Kai stood there.

Could it be that the wounds were inflicted by that young man called Yang Kai? How could someone at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm possibly injure someone at the Third Stage?

“Ah? Elder Tan!” A middle-aged man scrutinized Tan Junhao, then suddenly cried out and stepped forward with cupped fists. “Greetings, Elder Tan. What brings you here?”

Tan Junhao glanced at him and nodded, “You recognize this old man?”

The middle-aged man smiled obsequiously. “More than ten years ago, I was fortunate enough to receive a reward from the Star God Palace and cultivated for a time in the Heaven-Grade secret land. Back then, I caught a distant glimpse of Elder Tan. I doubt you remember me.”

Although this middle-aged man was a sect master in his own right, he was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and his Sect was merely second-rate in the entire Southern Territory. Facing an elder of the Star God Palace, how could he dare act arrogantly? It was precisely because of that reward from the Star God Palace and his cultivation in the Heaven-Grade secret land that he had managed to break through to the Emperor Realm.

He was deeply grateful to the Star God Palace. Now, seeing an elder of the Star God Palace, he was naturally full of respect.

Tan Junhao nodded slightly, saying nothing more.

But the middle-aged man’s words left the others quite surprised.

“Elder Tan, Star God Palace… could it be…?”

Although most of the Emperor Realm cultivators present didn’t recognize Tan Junhao and had never seen him before, they now had some suspicions.

The Star God Palace was a Sect under a Great Emperor. To become an elder, one had to be at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm, and even within the Star God Palace, there were only a handful at that level.

They might not have seen Tan Junhao before, but his name was well known.

Everyone’s hearts tightened, and they all stepped forward, cupping their fists. “Greetings, Elder Tan!”

“No need for such formalities!” Tan Junhao waved his hand, fully displaying the bearing of a master.

The middle-aged man who had spoken earlier wore a grim expression. “Elder Tan, who would dare to attack you? And even injure you?”

Tan Junhao’s face turned cold. He looked at Yang Kai and said, “It was this young man. This old man was caught off guard and let him succeed with a sneak attack!”

Yang Kai couldn’t help but curl his lip. While that wasn’t wrong—he had indeed succeeded with a sneak attack—if Tan Junhao hadn’t provoked him first, why would he have attacked a Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivator out of nowhere?

The middle-aged man was furious when he heard this. He shouted at Yang Kai, “So it was you, you little beast! Elder Tan is an elder of the Star God Palace, with a noble status and high standing. The Star God Palace is the overlord Sect of our Southern Territory, sheltering countless cultivators. Meeting Elder Tan is your good fortune, yet instead of being grateful, you sneak-attacked him! Get over here and apologize at once. Elder Tan is magnanimous and might spare your life. If you remain stubborn, we’ll kill you ourselves!”

His words rang out forcefully and decisively. Tan Junhao was quite pleased, a smile appearing on his face.

The other Emperor Realm cultivators, however, wore various expressions. Unlike the middle-aged man, they hadn’t received much favor from the Star God Palace. Though they respected the Star God Palace, they weren’t about to grovel before Tan Junhao.

Still, it wouldn’t do to contradict him, so they simply stood quietly in place, looking at Yang Kai with sympathy.

Back at the auction, Yang Kai had mentioned something about a Star God Palace elder. Now it seemed that this elder was indeed Tan Junhao.

No one knew how he had managed to make an enemy of Tan Junhao. Offending him meant there was no place left for him in the Southern Territory. Young people truly were hot-blooded and reckless.

“Oh… So it’s you, the friend from Room Thirteen who ate three catties of shit. No wonder it suddenly stinks so much here.” Yang Kai glanced sideways at the middle-aged man and waved his hand in front of his nose, feigning disgust.

“Ah…”

“It’s really him!”

“He actually ate it?”

“This… this…”

All the Emperor Realm cultivators stared wide-eyed at the middle-aged man. Some even jumped away from him, putting distance between themselves and him, their faces full of disgust as if they really smelled something foul.

In an instant, a three-zhang radius around the middle-aged man was completely empty, as if an invisible hand had isolated him. Even Tan Junhao frowned, looking at the man with a strange expression.

“You…” The middle-aged man pointed angrily at Yang Kai, his face flushed red with shame and rage.

What had happened in Room Thirteen was the greatest humiliation of his life. At the time, he’d have preferred to be killed by Yang Kai than suffer such disgrace, but his burning hatred had kept him alive, plotting revenge.

When the formation had just teleported him here, he’d immediately seen Yang Kai and guessed that Tan Junhao’s injuries were related to him. That was why he’d been so eager to jump out and pressure Yang Kai in Tan Junhao’s name.

Who could have guessed he’d be humiliated by Yang Kai all over again?

The countless scornful and disdainful gazes around him made his skin crawl. He wished he could find a hole to crawl into. Flustered, he said, “Don’t listen to this brat’s nonsense, everyone. That wasn’t me.”

No one believed him. He’d spoken at the auction, and everyone had heard his voice clearly. Now that they thought about it, it was obviously the same person.

“You little bastard, I’ll never forgive you!” The middle-aged man, seeing no one respond, knew his reputation was ruined. From now on, whenever anyone mentioned him, the first thing they’d think of would be eating three catties of shit…

Yang Kai tilted his head and said, “Sorry, but I don’t talk to people who eat shit. Please show some respect!”

“Pfft…” The middle-aged man’s qi and blood surged, his anger having nowhere to go. He was so furious that he coughed up a mouthful of blood.

His mind in chaos, he just wanted to kill Yang Kai to wash away his shame. Gritting his teeth, he shouted, “I’ll fight you to the death!”

With a flash of light, a sharp sword appeared in his hand. Emperor Yuan surged as he wrapped himself in sword light and slashed at Yang Kai.

This furious strike was almost the full power of a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator. Even the other Emperor Realm cultivators present couldn’t help but tense up, not daring to underestimate it.

Yang Kai, on the other hand, looked disdainful and sneered coldly. Suddenly, a broad, heavy sword appeared in his hand. He lifted it onto his shoulder and said coldly, “Since you’re so eager to die, I’ll grant your wish!”

“Die!” The middle-aged man roared, unleashing his full strength as he charged at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai stepped forward, raising the Myriad Sword high. Sword light burst forth as he swung it down. There was no technique to the strike, but its power seemed enough to shatter the sky, making everyone’s faces change.

Ssshhh—

It was as if silk was being torn. The dazzling sword light that had rushed at Yang Kai suddenly split in two, passing by on either side of him. The brilliant light instantly faded, crashing to the ground and rolling far away before finally coming to a stop.

“Hiss…” A chorus of shocked gasps rang out. Every pair of eyes was filled with disbelief and horror.

Behind Yang Kai, two long trails of blood stretched out on either side. Scattered across the ground were shattered fragments of internal organs, and farther back lay the corpse, split cleanly in two, sprawled grotesquely on the spot.

That single strike had actually cleaved a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator in half.

And it looked as if Yang Kai had done it casually, without even trying.

Everyone had already known Yang Kai was formidable and doubted that the middle-aged man could be his match, but none of them had expected him to be defeated so easily.

No, it wasn’t that the man was weak—it was that Yang Kai’s strength defied reason!

At the auction, he had killed the old man from Room Seven with a punch and a single sword strike, shocking everyone with his terrifying power. But only now did they realize they had still underestimated him.

Yang Kai flicked the blood from the Myriad Sword, grinned, and said, “This place is nothing but trouble. Just leave if you can, no need to stay and watch the show.”

Tan Junhao had suddenly brought so many Emperor Realm cultivators here. It was obviously not just to let them watch. He was probably plotting something, maybe even planning to use them against Yang Kai.

Yang Kai had no desire to make enemies of so many people. His only target now was Tan Junhao.

Sensing the threat in Yang Kai’s words and recalling what had just happened, the Emperor Realm cultivators couldn’t help but shiver. They thought this young man was arrogant and overbearing, and at the auction he had shown no regard for anyone, but he truly had the strength to back it up. Making an enemy of him would be unwise.

Besides, they had no personal grudge against Yang Kai. Why would they want to provoke him for no reason?

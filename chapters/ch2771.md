## Chapter 2771 — The Third One

Two hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals—such a massive sum was not something just anyone could possess. This amount was equivalent to several years’ total income for the entire Qingyang Shrine.

Even Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao, upon hearing this, couldn’t help but feel their hearts stir.

In contrast, Yang Kai’s expression remained calm, as if what he’d heard wasn’t two hundred million, but merely twenty million, or even two million…

This inevitably left the few people present clicking their tongues in wonder. After all, Yang Kai had only recently broken through to the Emperor Realm. Even if he had some assets, he couldn’t possibly be that wealthy. So how could he remain so unmoved in the face of such an enormous fortune?

Little did they know, Yang Kai’s current wealth was so abundant that two hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals truly meant little to him.

“And the second condition?” Yang Kai asked indifferently.

The strange light in Lan Xun’s eyes faded as she softly replied, “The second condition… is that you may ask my father three questions—any questions at all.”

As soon as she spoke, Xue Zhengmao and Xiao Yuyang’s breathing grew rapid, their faces filled with fervor.

Although they were Elders of the Star God Palace, holding high positions—second only to one and above all others—it was not easy to meet the Great Emperor. The Great Emperor was in seclusion year-round, as elusive as a dragon, and it could be years or even decades before one might see him, let alone have the chance to ask him questions.

If they could obtain such an opportunity, the two would be overjoyed, for no matter a cultivator’s realm, there were always many doubts on the Martial Dao. For experts of their level, perhaps only the Great Emperor could resolve their confusion. Even a single casual pointer from the Great Emperor might allow one’s strength to soar.

It could be said that, although this second condition was not as straightforward as two hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals, its value far surpassed the first. Source Crystals could be earned in the future, but the chance to ask the Great Emperor three questions was not something one could obtain at will.

Even Wen Zishan, upon hearing this, looked tempted, turning his head to glance at Yang Kai, clearly hoping he would agree at once.

Yet Yang Kai’s expression remained indifferent. After pondering for a moment, he said, “And the third condition?”

Lan Xun replied, “The third condition is that you may enter the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda of my Star God Palace and cultivate there for one month.”

Yang Kai nodded. The three conditions had now been laid out. To be honest, the Star God Palace’s offer was indeed sincere. Any one of these conditions would be enough for Yang Kai to return the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome.

“Must this Imperial Treasure be returned?” Yang Kai asked, holding the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome as he looked up at Lan Xun.

Lan Xun wore an apologetic expression. “Senior Brother Yang, I hope you can consider the dozens of disciples from my Star God Palace who are trapped in the secret realm.”

Without the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, it would be impossible to open the Grand Illusionary Realm, and the dozens of disciples trapped inside would never hope to escape.

Yang Kai sighed and said, “Then I’ll choose the third one.”

Lan Xun was first delighted, then stunned. “You don’t want to reconsider?”

Wen Zishan also advised, “That’s right, Yang Kai. You should think this over carefully. Since the Great Emperor has granted you these three conditions, he certainly won’t force you to decide immediately. It won’t be too late to choose after you’ve thought it through.”

Yang Kai shook his head. “No need. I’ll choose the third one.”

Wen Zishan opened his mouth, but in the end said nothing more.

Putting himself in Yang Kai’s place, if it were him, he would definitely choose the second condition. Although the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was extraordinary, cultivating inside depended on one’s fortune. Without luck, even a year or ten years might yield nothing, let alone just a month. It was far less practical than asking the Great Emperor three questions face-to-face. He didn’t know what Yang Kai was thinking, but in the end, it was Yang Kai’s own business. Since he had made his choice, Wen Zishan naturally couldn’t say more.

Lan Xun was clearly surprised as well. In her estimation, there was a ninety percent chance Yang Kai would choose the second condition, but things had not gone as she expected. In the end, she simply nodded and said, “Then the Star God Palace will await Senior Brother Yang’s arrival at any time.”

Yang Kai nodded, activated his Divine Sense, and erased the Soul Brand from the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome in his hand. He returned the Imperial Treasure to Lan Xun, who stored it in her Spatial Ring.

With this matter settled, Wen Zishan warmly invited Lan Xun and the others to stay at Qingyang Shrine for a few more days. However, Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao had no such intention and immediately took their leave.

Having completed her task, Lan Xun naturally had to depart as well.

Just before leaving, Yang Kai suddenly called out, “Junior Sister Lan, please wait. I have a private matter to ask you about.”

Lan Xun turned back, motioning for Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao to wait outside. The two had no objections, but Xiao Chen shamelessly stayed behind, looking very uneasy about leaving Yang Kai and Lan Xun alone, his hostility obvious.

“Senior Brother Xiao, you should go out too,” Lan Xun said, glancing at Xiao Chen.

Xiao Chen opened his mouth, about to say something, but when he met Lan Xun’s beautiful eyes, his momentum faltered. The words stuck in his throat, and he glared resentfully at Yang Kai before turning to leave.

In no time, only Yang Kai and Lan Xun remained in the grand hall.

Lan Xun smiled slightly. “There’s no one else here now, Senior Brother Yang. What would you like to ask?”

Yang Kai said, “Junior Sister Lan, did you say you recently visited Spirit Beast Island?”

Lan Xun nodded. “That’s right, about three months ago. My father was invited by Senior Beast Great Emperor, so he took me to the Eastern Territory.”

“Did you see Senior Jiu Feng on Spirit Beast Island?” Yang Kai asked, his expression shifting.

“Aunt Jiu Feng?” Lan Xun was a bit surprised. She had thought Yang Kai wanted to ask about Mo Xiaoqi, but instead, his first question was about Jiu Feng. As far as she knew, Yang Kai and Jiu Feng had little connection. Shaking her head, she replied, “I didn’t see her.”

After a pause, she added, “But I heard from Sister Xiaoqi that Aunt Jiu Feng has been in seclusion recently and shouldn’t be disturbed lightly.”

Yang Kai raised his eyebrows, deep in thought.

If Jiu Feng was in seclusion, it was definitely related to Liu Yan. After leaving the Shattered Star Sea, Liu Yan had fused with the True Phoenix Fire and transformed into a phoenix egg, which Jiu Feng took back to Spirit Beast Island. There had been no news since. At the time, Jiu Feng had said that it would take as little as a year, or as long as three to five years, for the outcome to become clear.

Although Jiu Feng was acting as Protector, Liu Yan was still taking some risks in accepting the Phoenix inheritance this time, which had left Yang Kai uneasy.

He had hoped to get some news from Lan Xun, but it turned out she knew nothing either.

Seeing his disappointed expression, Lan Xun looked puzzled, not knowing how Yang Kai and Jiu Feng were connected.

After a moment’s thought, Yang Kai said, “There’s one more thing I’d like to ask Junior Sister Lan for help with.”

Lan Xun smiled. “If Senior Brother Yang needs anything, just say so.”

Yang Kai said, “Junior Sister Lan, do you know someone named Hua Qingsi? She’s also a disciple of the Star God Palace, and her Master is Tan Junhao…”

…

Half an hour later, Lan Xun finally left the grand hall. Xiao Chen, who had been waiting anxiously outside, immediately rushed over, nervously examining Lan Xun as if afraid she had suffered some loss. Seeing that she was unharmed and that Yang Kai, who followed her out, looked a bit stifled, he was greatly relieved.

“Senior Brother Yang, whenever you wish to visit the Star God Palace, you may come at any time. Junior Sister will be waiting for you!” Lan Xun called back to Yang Kai.

“Take care, Junior Sister Lan,” Yang Kai replied absentmindedly with a nod.

Lan Xun gestured to Xiao Yuyang and Xue Zhengmao, and the group turned into streaks of light, shooting toward the horizon.

Atop Ten Thousand Saints Peak, the many guests watched quietly.

The grand ceremony was now complete, and Yang Kai had also received the Elder Jade Token of Qingyang Shrine. From this day forward, he was an Elder of Qingyang Shrine. If it were just any Elder, it wouldn’t matter, but the key was that Yang Kai had previously shown his strength before everyone, daring to clash with Xue Zhengmao and even injuring him. Although he himself seemed to have suffered some injuries, both his courage and strength were enough to command respect.

The arrival of Lan Xun and the calm departure of the Star God Palace’s people also made the guests sense a different atmosphere.

So at this moment, many people looked at Yang Kai with new eyes—not as elders looking at a junior, but as equals.

After a brief silence, under the arrangements of Qingyang Shrine’s high-level members, the atmosphere became lively again.

Although the grand ceremony was over, so many Emperor Realm experts gathering together was a rare event. Since it was being held at Qingyang Shrine, they naturally couldn’t neglect their guests.

Wen Zishan also took Yang Kai around the hall, toasting each group and introducing him to the many Emperor Realm experts from the Southern Territory. In the future, there might be more interactions.

As they made their rounds, Yang Kai saw many familiar faces.

Wuchang of Tianwu Sacred Land, Zhuang Bufan of Wuhua Hall, and Luo Yuan of Eight Directions Sect—Yang Kai had dealt with all of them before.

These rising stars, who had already shone brilliantly in the Dao Origin Realm, had now all advanced to the Emperor Realm.

Wuchang and Zhuang Bufan were understandable, as they were both rising stars of the Southern Territory, holding positions in their respective Sects similar to Xia Sheng’s in Qingyang Shrine, enjoying resources and treatment beyond imagination.

But as for Luo Yuan, his Eight Directions Sect was just a small force, even inferior to the former Biyu Sect. Yet, relying on his extraordinary talent and perseverance, he had reached the Emperor Realm and now led the Eight Directions Sect as its Sect Master!

As he stared at Yang Kai, his eyes burned with fighting spirit, as if he wanted to challenge Yang Kai to a duel at any moment.

If today weren’t Yang Kai’s big day, he probably would have already issued a challenge.

After several rounds of wine and dishes, people mingled, toasting and chatting in groups large and small, exchanging pleasantries.

Most of the Emperor Realm experts who came had brought their disciples and juniors, giving them a chance to broaden their horizons.

“Young Master Yang, we meet again.” A faint fragrance drifted over as a voluptuous figure pressed close to Yang Kai’s side, smiling before she spoke. “No, I should call you Elder Yang now.”

Yang Kai turned to look and saw a beautiful, mature woman standing gracefully with a wine cup in hand. Perhaps because she had been drinking, her pretty face was flushed, and even her fair neck was tinged with red, making her extremely alluring.

“Palace Master Hua!” Yang Kai recognized her and smiled, raising his cup in greeting.

## Chapter 3013 — Following One's Heart

As Yang Kai stepped forward, a beautiful figure suddenly blocked his path.

Looking up, Yang Kai saw a cold yet stunningly beautiful face. The woman was tall and graceful, her white dress fluttering as though she were a goddess descended to the mortal world. With her appearance came a refreshing fragrance. It was entirely natural, clearly the woman's own scent, and breathing it in left one feeling relaxed and refreshed.

"Junior Sister Yao, it has been many days since we last met. I trust you've been well!" Yang Kai smiled lightly at her.

Ji Yao nodded gently and looked him up and down. Her red lips parted, and out came a sentence that nearly made Yang Kai cough up blood. "Senior Brother is still alive, then."

"Ha ha..." The corner of Yang Kai's mouth twitched. "Many thanks for Junior Sister's concern. I've been quite well."

"Where have you been all this time?" Ji Yao asked.

"Travelling around," Yang Kai casually replied, feeling inexplicably guilty.

Speaking of which, he had barely seen Ji Yao since Wenqing Sect was destroyed. He had been constantly running about outside, after all. On the rare occasions he returned to Lingxiao Palace, Hua Qingsi would sometimes mention that Ji Yao had come looking for him and stayed at Lingxiao Palace for a while, only to return after failing to see him.

Hua Qingsi had mentioned it again when he returned this time.

There had been many misunderstandings between him and Ji Yao.

When she had been stranded in the Wild Ancient Land of the Eastern Territory, her mind had been unclear. For some reason, she had mistaken him for Bing Yun, resulting in several awkward incidents. Yang Kai could still remember the breathtaking sight he had witnessed in Luan Feng's bedchamber.

Now that he saw Ji Yao again, that nosebleed-inducing scene involuntarily sprang to mind.

Fortunately, Ji Yao later met the real Bing Yun and finally returned to normal after the shock to her mind.

Regardless, Yang Kai still felt somewhat guilty whenever he faced her. He believed Ji Yao remembered what had happened before, but since she had never mentioned it, he naturally would not be foolish enough to bring it up either.

Ji Yao gazed at him indifferently, the corner of her mouth curving upward. "How many more flowers have you plucked? How many more grasses have you trifled with?" As she spoke, she even sniffed lightly before nodding. "Mm, very fragrant."

Yang Kai felt even guiltier. Wiping the cold sweat from his forehead, he forced a smile. "Junior Sister, the way you put it..."

As he spoke, he secretly sniffed himself and wondered how Zhu Qing's scent could possibly still linger after so long.

"Yang Kai is here? Since you've arrived, come inside. What are you doing out there?" Bing Yun's voice suddenly came from inside the room.

Hearing this, Yang Kai felt as though he had been granted a great pardon. He hurried inside while calling out a response.

Ji Yao shot him a vicious glare. Her gaze was like a blade piercing his flesh, leaving his back prickling as though covered in thorns.

After entering the room and seeing Bing Yun, Yang Kai hurriedly saluted.

Bing Yun looked at him with a warm smile, then glanced at Ji Yao, who had followed him inside, and said softly, "Neither of you is an outsider. Sit."

Yang Kai thanked her and sat down calmly across from Bing Yun.

Ji Yao half-knelt to the side with a detached expression, took out a tea set, and silently began brewing tea. Every step was performed with practised ease, clearly showing her mastery of the art.

"When did you return?" Bing Yun asked, gazing at him kindly.

"I returned two months ago," Yang Kai replied.

Ji Yao's hands paused slightly. She looked up at Yang Kai just as he happened to be looking at her. When their eyes met, Yang Kai clearly saw a trace of reproach in Ji Yao's gaze.

She had thought Yang Kai had only returned recently, never imagining he had already been back for two months.

Yet she had not received the slightest word.

Yang Kai hurriedly looked away and sat up straight.

"Has Senior been well lately?" Yang Kai asked, simply to make conversation.

Bing Yun smiled faintly. "Thanks to you, everything has been well."

These were not merely polite words, but the honest truth. Ever since Yang Kai helped Ice Heart Valley resolve its crisis, the Sect had developed by leaps and bounds. Now that it had close ties with Lingxiao Palace, who in the entire Northern Territory would dare not give it face? Although Ice Heart Valley had once been considered one of the Northern Territory's top Sects, Bing Yun had been missing for many years, leaving only a group of disciples to manage it. Moreover, because they were all women, others inevitably looked down on them, so their title as a top Sect had been somewhat undeserved.

Otherwise, Wenqing Sect would never have dared mobilize its entire Sect to invade.

Things were different now. Apart from Medicine Pill Valley, Lingxiao Palace's prestige was at its zenith in the Northern Territory, and Ice Heart Valley had benefited greatly from its association with it. No one dared provoke its disciples when they travelled outside anymore.

Bing Yun said, "I heard Great Emperor Miaodan invited you to Medicine Pill Valley. Have you gone yet?"

Yang Kai shook his head. "Not yet. I've been tied up with trivial matters lately and haven't had the time."

Bing Yun said, "A Great Emperor's invitation cannot be taken lightly. You must make the trip when you have time. If you can receive even a little guidance from the Great Emperor, you will benefit greatly for the rest of your life."

"Mm. Senior is right." Yang Kai nodded lightly.

The two chatted for a while longer. Before long, the tea was ready. Ji Yao poured each of them a cup, then sat there quietly.

Yang Kai took a sip and praised, "Good tea. Junior Sister's skill is quite impressive."

His flattery had no effect whatsoever. Ji Yao did not react, so Yang Kai coughed dryly and continued drinking his tea.

Bing Yun chuckled. "Have you encountered some trouble?"

Yang Kai was startled, then smiled bitterly. "Senior's insight is truly keen."

Bing Yun smiled. "You're distracted and burdened with worries. If I couldn't even see that much, I would truly be blind."

Yang Kai nodded. "I have indeed encountered something."

Ji Yao raised her head and stared fixedly at him. Bing Yun said, "Tell me about it if it is convenient. I may not be able to help you, but I can at least listen."

Yang Kai said solemnly, "Junior's cultivation seems to have encountered a bottleneck."

"Oh?" Bing Yun looked at him in surprise.

"I've been cultivating in retreat all this time. I clearly feel that I'm only a little short of breaking through, but I've never been able to grasp that elusive opportunity."

"And then?" Bing Yun asked.

"There is no then." Yang Kai spread his hands.

Bing Yun could not help laughing. "How is that even a problem?"

Yang Kai was speechless, thinking that this was a serious problem!

Bing Yun smiled and said, "Although I don't know your exact age, you should be less than a hundred years old in my estimation."

Yang Kai nodded. "About that."

Even he could no longer remember exactly how old he was.

Bing Yun continued, "For ordinary people, that age would indeed place them in the twilight of their lives, and some might already be nothing more than a mound of yellow earth. But for cultivators like us, it is merely the beginning. At such a young age, you have already reached the First-Stage Emperor Realm, and you cultivated all the way up from a Star Domain in a lower plane. Your aptitude can already be considered outstanding."

"Senior praises me too highly."

Bing Yun shook her head. "I'm not praising you; I'm simply speaking the truth. Do you know how long it took me to reach the First-Stage Emperor Realm?" Without waiting for Yang Kai to answer, she raised one finger. "One thousand years, a full one thousand years! Compared with your achievements, my meager aptitude is hardly worth mentioning."

Yang Kai said with embarrassment, "How could this Junior dare compare himself with Senior?"

Bing Yun pointed at Ji Yao. "Then do you know how long it took your Junior Sister Yao to advance to the Emperor Realm?"

Yang Kai looked curiously at Ji Yao.

Ji Yao raised her hand, picked up the teapot, and poured Bing Yun another cup. "Master, please have some tea!"

Bing Yun smiled faintly and did not continue the subject. Instead, she said, "To have achieved so much at your age, I imagine you've grown accustomed to smooth sailing along your journey."

Has my journey really been smooth sailing? Yang Kai thought about it, but did not feel that anything had gone particularly smoothly. On the contrary, his path had seemed filled with hardships and setbacks.

"That is why you take this bottleneck so seriously," Bing Yun said with a faint smile. "But in my eyes, it is nothing at all. What cultivator has never encountered a bottleneck? Who does not suffer setbacks along the road of growth? A blade grows sharper the more it is honed, and cultivators are no different. Bottlenecks and setbacks are merely tools that temper you..."

"Senior is right." Yang Kai nodded.

Bing Yun studied him for a moment, then suddenly said, "No, what troubles you isn't your bottleneck."

Yang Kai stared at her in astonishment.

Bing Yun smiled. "You understood everything I just said long ago, and you also know that you will overcome this bottleneck sooner or later. What troubles you is something else."

"What makes Senior say that?" Yang Kai looked at her in puzzlement.

Bing Yun smiled inscrutably and stared intently at Yang Kai until he began to feel uncomfortable.

Suddenly, Bing Yun said, "Follow your heart."

After saying that, she simply closed her eyes, clearly dismissing her guest.

Yang Kai's eyes flickered. He pondered for a long time, then picked up the teacup before him and drained it in one gulp. Rising, he said, "Many thanks, Senior."

Bing Yun said nothing more. Yang Kai turned to Ji Yao. "Junior Sister Yao, farewell."

With that, he strode outside.

Ji Yao stared at his departing back but did not follow him. Only after he had left did she ask, "Master, what exactly has Senior Brother Yang encountered?"

"I don't know." Bing Yun slowly shook her head. "But he is definitely hesitating over something. The bottleneck is merely an excuse for him to avoid it."

"Then Senior Brother Yang hasn't encountered a bottleneck?"

"The bottleneck is real, but for him, it is merely that the right opportunity has yet to arrive. This other matter, however, is not a question of opportunity."

Ji Yao looked outside, intensely curious about what Yang Kai was hesitating over.

Within Ice Heart Valley, Yang Kai summoned the Flowing Cloud Shuttle and disappeared in a streak of light.

He did not return to Lingxiao Palace through the Space Array because his destination this time was not in the Northern Territory.

His destination was the Eastern Territory, Dragon Isle!

Zhu Qing still had not returned.

At first, Yang Kai had assumed she had been delayed along the way, but two months had already passed since he returned to Lingxiao Palace. No matter what delayed Zhu Qing on the road, it could not possibly have held her up for so long.

In other words, Zhu Qing had definitely returned to Dragon Isle.

He had been hesitating over whether to go to Dragon Isle and look for her, yet in his heart he had also hoped Zhu Qing would come to Lingxiao Palace. After all, they had already become husband and wife in truth, and their relationship was inseparable.

Unfortunately, no matter how long he waited, there was still no news, leaving Yang Kai rather disappointed.

What was that supposed to mean? That she wanted to share nothing more than a one-night tryst with him? There was no such cheap bargain in this world. Just you wait, you little wench. Once I find you, I’m going to spank you until your backside is sore.

Yang Kai secretly swore to himself.

He also recalled what Zhu Lie had told him before and sneered inwardly.

He knew Zhu Qing had made this choice for his own good, but he still refused to accept it. He was a grown man; why would he need her protection?

He had been wavering between waiting for Zhu Qing to return on her own and going to Dragon Isle to find her. Now, he had finally made his decision.

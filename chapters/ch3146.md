## Chapter 3146 — Tongxuan

Liu Yan's conversation with the thin, tall man reached Yang Kai without a single word missing. At the same time, he was struggling to communicate with Long Tianshang.

Although his Divine Ability had restored Long Tianshang's consciousness, too much of the man's memory was gone. Many things had slipped beyond recall. He remembered only that Hengluo Commerce Guild, as a towering tree, had drawn the wind and attracted the covetous eyes of many experts from Great Wilderness Star Domain. Netherworld Sect had joined several powerful forces of Great Wilderness Star Domain to campaign against and suppress Hengluo Commerce Guild. More than that, the infamous Star Pirates of Spine of the Star River and Abandoned Nest had also thrown in with the cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain.

Spine of the Star River and Abandoned Nest were the two largest Star Pirate factions in the entire Hengluo Star Domain. The Star Pirates gathered there were all vicious criminals who stopped at nothing. Compared with these two forces, He Yunxiang's Wild Wind Star Pirates were simply small fry before true giants.

Before Great Wilderness Star Domain invaded, those two forces had battled openly and covertly with Hengluo Commerce Guild, Purple Star, and Sword Alliance for hundreds, even a thousand years. Every Warship the great forces sent sailing through the Star Domain had been visited by them, while Hengluo Commerce Guild, Purple Star, and Sword Alliance had also joined forces to encircle and suppress them. Each side had blood from the other on its hands, and their feud had long since become irreconcilable.

Now that the cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain had invaded, the forces led by Netherworld Sect displayed extraordinary strength. The evildoers of Spine of the Star River and Abandoned Nest naturally defected at once and became their accomplices.

Yang Kai finally understood why Shuiyue Star had fallen.

Targeted from so many sides, Hengluo Commerce Guild could not guarantee its own safety no matter how strong it was. Several Cultivation Stars had fallen one after another, so Shuiyue Star, as the main star, naturally could not remain untouched.

If this was what had happened to Hengluo Commerce Guild, then Purple Star and Sword Alliance were probably not faring much better.

It was not that the cultivators of Hengluo Star Domain were weaker than those of Great Wilderness Star Domain. Both were Lower Plane Star Domains, and even if there was some difference in overall strength, it could not be too large. If both sides drew up their forces and fought head-on, they would only leave each other badly wounded.

The problem was that in Great Wilderness Star Domain, Netherworld Sect alone stood supreme. It could command the heroes and issue orders, while Hengluo Star Domain lacked such a leading Sect. Lingxiao Sect in its prime might have filled that role, but after Great Wilderness Star Domain invaded, the Sect's several Third Stage Void King Realm cultivators had all returned to their own stars and failed to coordinate their battles.

"Be careful!" Long Tianshang warned solemnly.

"Careful of what?" Yang Kai frowned at him.

Long Tianshang shook his head. He did not know what he wanted Yang Kai to beware of either. The warning had come by instinct. He only vaguely felt that there was someone Yang Kai had to guard against, and that this person had been the key to Hengluo Commerce Guild's defeat and retreat.

Yang Kai asked again, but Long Tianshang could no longer answer. Although Yang Kai had helped him regain a brief moment of clarity, Long Tianshang himself had already been refined into a Heavenly Corpse. Both his Divine Sense and his flesh had suffered irreversible damage. His brief lucidity was something like the final flare of a dying flame.

Low growls rumbled from Long Tianshang's throat, and his eyes began trembling violently. His consciousness was fading away again. Soon, he would once more become a refined corpse that knew no pain, only slaughter.

Yang Kai sighed softly.

To think that the Grand Elder of Hengluo Commerce Guild had fallen to such a state. It was enough to make anyone sigh with regret.

Liu Yan came to Yang Kai's side and caught the corner of his robe. The thin, tall man, whose cultivation had already been crippled, lay slumped nearby, his face deathly pale.

Liu Yan had asked him a few questions, then had not troubled him much further.

At this moment, however, he looked as if he had seen the most terrifying thing in the world. He stared fixedly at Long Tianshang, his eyes overflowing with horror, then shouted, "Kill me! Hurry, kill me!"

Yang Kai stared at him coldly without moving.

The thin, tall man begged in a shrill voice, "Please, hurry and kill me!"

If a person could live, who would choose death? But his cultivation had been crippled, and he no longer had the strength to flee. If he was not killed, then he truly would have to experience something even more painful than death.

Perhaps the obsession with revenge was too deeply rooted, or perhaps the thin, tall man's voice drew his attention. Long Tianshang suddenly turned to look at him, and with a flicker, appeared before the thin, tall man. His sharp fingers stabbed straight into the man's chest and lifted him high.

Blood poured down, and screams rang through the clouds.

Long Tianshang's face was savage and terrifying. Meaningless growls came from his mouth. The consciousness he had recovered earlier had been completely buried beneath his corpse nature. He opened his mouth, bared his fangs, and bit into the thin, tall man.

Another scream of unbearable misery rang out.

Long Tianshang tore a chunk of flesh from the thin, tall man's body and chewed with his fang-filled mouth. Blood streamed from the corners of his lips.

People often spoke of eating others, but that was usually only a figure of speech. At this moment, however, it was truly happening before their eyes.

"Kill me... I beg you... wah..."

The thin, tall man went on begging. Yang Kai's expression remained cold and cruel as he quietly watched Long Tianshang tear mouthful after mouthful of flesh from him and swallow it down whole. In less than half a cup of tea's time, the thin, tall man stopped moving, his vitality cut off.

Yang Kai stepped forward and came to Long Tianshang's side.

He was still eating flesh and blood. Sensing the aura of a living person drawing near, he whipped around and roared in fury.

Yang Kai brought his palm down on him. Wind and clouds surged, and not even bones remained.

Yang Kai waved his hand again, and several figures appeared. They were Li Jiao and the others, whom he had kept settled inside the Small Xuan Realm all this time. This time, he even released Lu Yuqin. Although Lu Yuqin's cultivation was not high, Lu Sanniang was there to look after her, so there was no real danger in a place like this.

Seeing the miserable state of their surroundings at first glance, Li Jiao and the others all froze. When they saw Yang Kai's face dark as water, their hearts gave a jump. They had no idea who had angered him, but from the look of things, he was in a terrible mood.

"Brother Li, there is something I need your help with."

Li Jiao's expression turned solemn. "Brother Yang, whatever it is, just give the order."

"Help me take back this Cultivation Star." Yang Kai pointed at the ground beneath his feet.

Shuiyue Star was Hengluo Commerce Guild's main star, and also the place where Xue Yue had grown up. No matter what, it could not be allowed to remain in Great Wilderness Star Domain's hands. With his ability, he could certainly take it back himself, but he was unwilling to spend any more time here.

This was also a good chance to have Li Jiao and the others lend a hand, lest they grow stale from staying too long in the Small Xuan Realm.

"No problem." Li Jiao agreed at once.

He already understood what sort of people inhabited this Star Domain. With his Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, handling this matter would be laughably easy, especially with Lu Sanniang, He Yunxiang, Ruan Biting, the Incarnation, and the others helping him.

A Cultivation Star certainly was not small, but if they split up, they could probably settle everything in less than half a month.

"If there is anything you do not understand, ask the Incarnation. He will tell you who is friend and who is foe."

"Good!"

Leaving them behind to discuss the recovery, Yang Kai and Liu Yan went straight into the great hall at the center of the city. There had originally been some cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain inside the hall, but during the earlier battle they had watched from afar as their own experts were slaughtered as easily as melons being sliced and vegetables chopped. How could they not know that a fearsome butcher had arrived? They had long since fled without a trace.

Yang Kai swept straight in without meeting the slightest resistance.

Before long, he arrived at a plaza and found the Space Array he had arranged here back then.

The array had not been destroyed, but its connecting passages to many places had all been severed, making travel impossible. Moreover, for Yang Kai's current needs, the array he had arranged back then was already somewhat inadequate.

He spent half a day working with Liu Yan to modify and clear the array, connecting it to Di Chen and Dark Star. Only then did he leave Shuiyue Star and rush off in a certain direction.

As if sensing Yang Kai's anxiety, Liu Yan took the initiative to hold his hand. "Madam Yue will definitely be safe."

Yang Kai forced a smile. "If Xue Yue really fled to that place, then there is no need to worry about her safety."

"Master knows where Madam Xue Yue went?"

Yang Kai nodded. "Almost certainly."

In this direction, among the Cultivation Stars with a Star Lord overseeing them, there was no other possibility besides Tongxuan Continent. The Star Lord of Tongxuan Continent was Xia Ningshang, and Xue Yue knew that as well.

After Shuiyue Star was breached, there were only three places she could go: Dark Star, Di Chen Star, and Tongxuan Continent.

Dark Star was too far away and could be ruled out. Who knew what dangers she might encounter sailing through the Starry Sky? Di Chen Star was somewhat closer, but it was a star of the yao race after all, and without a Star Lord presiding over it, its safety was not assured.

With Xue Yue's intelligence, she had only one choice.

Go to Tongxuan Continent and find Xia Ningshang! First, to seek refuge with her, and second, to add strength to Tongxuan Continent.

Liu Yan pondered briefly, seeming to understand as well, and said, "Since that is the case, why is Master so anxious?"

Yang Kai said, "Tongxuan Continent is not like other Cultivation Stars. Back then, it already showed signs of Spiritual Qi exhaustion. If Little Senior Sister had not risked refining the Stellar Origin and bringing the world back to life, Tongxuan Continent would probably be filled with deathly Qi by now. I am not anxious about Xue Yue. I am anxious about Little Senior Sister."

If the cultivators of Great Wilderness Star Domain went all out against Tongxuan Continent, then even with Xia Ningshang presiding over it, defending it would probably be difficult. After all, Tongxuan Continent had not completely recovered its vitality yet. If it now suffered through the flames of war, that would only make matters worse. If its Spiritual Qi dried up again, Xia Ningshang, as its Star Lord, would absolutely be dragged down with it. Once her foundation was damaged, even the heavens would be powerless to save her.

A Star Lord and a Cultivation Star shared both loss and glory. That was not an idle saying.

Moreover, judging by the time, it had been several years since Xue Yue fled to Tongxuan Continent. During all those years, could the people of Great Wilderness Star Domain have been sitting idle? They had undoubtedly been committing outrages on Tongxuan Continent. Xia Ningshang had been supporting it bitterly on her own. How long could she last?

This was the true root of Yang Kai's anxiety.

Liu Yan understood and said no more. In her calm eyes, two balls of flame suddenly began to burn, murderous intent surging.

She had gained new life because of Yang Kai, and with his help, she had grown step by step to where she was today. If not for Yang Kai, she would probably still be nothing more than an Artifact Spirit, sinking and drifting in the Earth's Lung Fire. Perhaps one day she could have shed her old form and transformed, but that would have been thousands, perhaps tens of thousands of years later. To her, Yang Kai was heaven and earth. If someone dared make Yang Kai anxious and unhappy, hehe, then they could wait to taste the wrath of a phoenix...

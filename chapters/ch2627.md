## Chapter 2627 — Who Are You, Really

Four Emperor Realm cultivators suddenly lunged forward, creating a truly terrifying scene.

Shi Tianhe was pinned in place by Yang Kai, feeling her body abruptly sink, as if a mountain had pressed down upon her chest, leaving her unable to breathe.

In her shock, the four attackers' strikes arrived right on cue.

Boom, boom, boom...

After a thunderous crash, Shi Tianhe nearly had her heart leap out of her chest, thinking she was doomed. But when she looked again, she bizarrely found herself in a different spot, unharmed by the four attackers.

Yang Kai stood in front, slapping his hands together and then pulling them apart.

A massive, pitch-black Moon Blade attack slowly took shape, its energy fluctuations so intense they seemed like the scythe of death, waiting to reap lives.

With a sharp sound, the Moon Blade shot out, slashing straight toward the four Emperor Realm cultivators.

"Not good!" Yan Dong's expression changed as he shouted loudly.

After their failed attack, he realized they had underestimated Yang Kai's strength. Though this young man was indeed at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, his speed was uncanny. With such speed, he could stand undefeated in any battle.

Turning his head, he saw the giant Moon Blade whistling toward them, frightening Yan Dong into retreating rapidly as he shouted, "Elders, dodge quickly!"

He reacted quickly and dodged nimbly, but the three Thunderclap Pavilion elders weren't so agile. They were still trying to figure out what was happening, never seeing Yang Kai move—how had he vanished?

By the time they heard Yan Dong's warning, the Moon Blade was already upon them.

The three Thunderclap Pavilion elders shouted in unison, each surging their Emperor Yuan and channeling Law Power to defend.

Sss, sss, sss, sss...

A shocking scene unfolded—the Moon Blade tore through their Emperor Yuan and Laws, cutting through everything like a hot knife through butter.

Pfft...

With a soft sound, the pitch-black Moon Blade sliced through the body of the Thunderclap Pavilion elder in front, then continued toward the second elder.

The second elder wasn't spared either, also cut by the Moon Blade.

When it reached the third middle-aged elder, he finally managed to shift his body slightly, letting the Moon Blade slice through his arm instead.

Yan Dong's eyes widened instantly, staring in horror at the two Thunderclap Pavilion elders who had been cut by the Moon Blade, not daring to blink.

With a crash, the two elders suddenly split in half, blood and organs raining down from midair. At the same time, a miserable scream rang out, startling Yan Dong, who quickly turned to look.

He saw the third Thunderclap Pavilion middle-aged elder's arm severed at the root, blood spraying like a fountain from the wound, the beating heart faintly visible in the chest cavity—a grievous injury.

"Hiss..." Yan Dong sucked in a cold breath, his clothes instantly soaked with sweat. He turned in terror to stare at Yang Kai, trembling, "You... who are you, really?"

With a single move, just one attack, he had instantly killed two First Stage Emperor Realm Thunderclap Pavilion elders and severely wounded another.

How the hell could a First Stage Emperor do this? Was he even human?

Yan Dong was truly terrified. If Yang Kai hadn't just revealed his Emperor Realm cultivation, Yan Dong might have thought he was a Third Stage Emperor pretending to be weak.

Standing behind Yang Kai, Shi Tianhe was also utterly shocked, unable to help swallowing hard.

At that moment, she realized the force binding her had vanished, and she was free again.

Yang Kai grinned and said, "You came looking for trouble without even knowing who I am. Looks like City Lord Yan has never tasted death."

"Death..." Yan Dong's eyes narrowed as he glanced at the two dead Thunderclap Pavilion elders, then at Bai Yu, who was barely holding on. Gritting his teeth, he turned and fled as a streak of rainbow light.

He dared not stay any longer. The opponent could kill two cultivators equal to him in an instant—taking his life would be just as easy. If he stayed, he'd surely die here.

But facing such ghostly speed, Yan Dong had no confidence he could escape.

So, as he ran, he nervously glanced back, only to see Yang Kai still standing in place, not pursuing him.

Emperor Realm cultivators moved incredibly fast. In just a blink, Yan Dong had vanished from sight.

Only then did Yan Dong start gasping for breath, his face showing lingering fear and a sense of relief at surviving.

He didn't know why the opponent hadn't chased him, but since he was allowed to escape, he wouldn't miss the chance.

After flying dozens more breaths forward, Yan Dong finally felt a bit safer. No matter how fast that young man was, since he hadn't chased him, Yan Dong figured he was safe.

But before the thought finished, Yan Dong suddenly stopped, his expression turning serious as he looked ahead.

In the void ahead, ripples spread like a stone dropped in a calm lake, and from the center, a strange energy fluctuation emerged.

His vision blurred, and a figure appeared like a ghost, nearly scaring his soul out of his body.

Step, step, step, step...

Yan Dong retreated in fright, his face pale as if he'd seen a ghost in broad daylight, staring at Yang Kai, "How can you..."

Suddenly, he seemed to remember something and said in a deep voice, "Spatial Divine Ability!"

He'd heard that cultivators who mastered Spatial Divine Abilities and comprehended the Laws of Space to the extreme could perform miraculous spatial movement. But that was only rumor—Spatial Power was extremely rare, and those without fate could never touch it, no matter how hard they tried.

In the entire Star Realm, there was one person with profound mastery of Spatial Power, known as the greatest below the Great Emperors.

That person was the famous Li Wuyi of Spirit Beast Island in the Eastern Territory.

Yan Dong never expected to encounter an enemy skilled in Spatial Power, whose methods seemed not much inferior to Li Wuyi's.

So, the opponent hadn't let him escape, but was confident he could intercept him. With spatial movement, no matter how far Yan Dong ran, he couldn't escape his grasp.

"City Lord Yan has sharp eyes," Yang Kai said with a mocking smile.

Yan Dong's expression changed again, gritting his teeth as he shouted, "I'll fight you to the death!"

He knew he couldn't escape, and judging by the opponent's previous methods, begging for mercy wouldn't help. Now, his only hope was to fight for his life.

Yan Dong was decisive. As soon as he finished speaking, his power erupted, a long blade suddenly appearing in his hand. He moved with the blade, its light soaring as he slashed at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai snorted coldly, raising his hand and striking out with his palm.

Emperor Yuan surged, and the dazzling blade light instantly shattered. His palm landed squarely on Yan Dong's chest.

Pfft...

Yan Dong spat blood, stumbling and crashing to the ground, creating a deep pit.

Struggling out of the pit, Yan Dong stared trembling at Yang Kai, who was slowly descending from the sky, and shouted hoarsely, "You're not a First Stage Emperor! Who are you, really?"

How could a First Stage Emperor so easily injure him with a single palm? How could a First Stage Emperor instantly kill two and wound one Thunderclap Pavilion elder?

Yan Dong was almost certain Yang Kai had hidden his cultivation and was deliberately toying with them.

How ridiculous that they all thought Ji Yao was the strongest, only to run into a real monster.

"Guess," Yang Kai winked at him. In a flash, another pitch-black Moon Blade appeared in his hand, though this one wasn't as large as before.

Even so, sensing the power within made Yan Dong's face turn ashen. This was a technique condensed from Spatial Power, and with the Laws of Space, he had no way to resist.

"Wait..." Yan Dong shouted desperately. Since he couldn't win, he could only beg for his life.

But Yang Kai had no intention of letting him speak. With a flick of his finger, the Moon Blade shot toward Yan Dong.

With a soft sound, the Moon Blade sliced through Yan Dong's body. The City Lord of Taiping City stared wide-eyed as he fell, his body splitting in two upon hitting the ground.

Yang Kai reached out, taking Yan Dong's Spatial Ring, then vanished like a ghost.

When he reappeared, he was back at Shi Tianhe's side.

The sounds of battle echoed. Yang Kai glanced over and saw Ji Yao and Bai Yu locked in a life-or-death struggle. Bai Yu was in a sorry state, hair disheveled, covered in wounds and blood. Under Ji Yao's profound intent, his blood froze as soon as it flowed.

Bai Yu's hair was frosted, his movements sluggish.

Clearly, the Laws and intent he comprehended couldn't compare to Ji Yao's. His defeat and death were only a matter of time.

"Senior, here are the three Spatial Rings from those men," Shi Tianhe said respectfully, handing over the rings to Yang Kai.

The previously wounded Thunderclap Pavilion elder was already dead; Yang Kai had killed him before chasing Yan Dong.

At this moment, the air was thick with the stench of blood, the ground littered with colorful intestines and crimson blood—a scene like hell on earth.

"Mm." Yang Kai nodded, unceremoniously taking the three Spatial Rings and placing them with the one from Yan Dong. After a quick inspection, he found these men were quite wealthy, especially Yan Dong, whose ring contained many Source Crystals and plenty of heaven-and-earth treasures.

"Senior..." Shi Tianhe called softly. Ever since Yang Kai had shown such terrifying strength, her gaze toward him was different—no longer casual, but filled with awe, "Who... who are you, really?"

"Me? I'm called Yang Kai," Yang Kai grinned at her.

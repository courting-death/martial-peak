## Chapter 3083 — Bowing at First Sight

Lu Huaishuang looked away, her face full of pity. No matter how utterly wicked this young man might be, such a death was far too miserable. More importantly, he had just helped her; if he had not suddenly acted, she would likely have been injured already.

Ma Chaoqun and He Wuzui, however, were overjoyed. They nearly burst into laughter, for it seemed that the heavens truly spared no one, and retribution had finally come for the young man.

The two exchanged a glance and understood each other's thoughts without needing to discuss a thing.

Only after quite some time did the lightning pouring down from the sky finally subside, and the vortex of black clouds overhead vanished as well.

The whole field fell silent.

The young man still stood there holding Ge Ming in his hand, but both he and Gale's Third Chief now had their hair standing on end, their faces scorched black, and their auras almost nonexistent.

Whatever had happened to the young man, Gale’s Third Chief was unquestionably dead!

Everyone had seen the power of that lightning punishment just now. Even if a Third Stage Void King Realm master had been standing here, he could not possibly have survived, much less Ge Ming, who was only at the First Stage. This was simply an undeserved disaster. Although the young man had the ability to take his life, from beginning to end, he had never truly struck to kill him.

Instead, it was a lightning punishment from the Heavens that had taken Ge Ming's life.

Two figures suddenly shot over.

Ma Chaoqun and He Wuzui attacked from left and right, pouncing over like racing lightning and rumbling thunder.

When cutting weeds, one had to remove the roots; if one failed to kill a snake, it would climb up the stick. How could they not understand such a simple principle? Their earlier caution had only been because this strange young man had displayed strength far beyond ordinary men.

Now that this young man had been struck by lightning punishment, dead or not, they would dismember him first. Only by making sure this young man was thoroughly dead could they have any path to survival; otherwise, they could only place their lives and deaths in someone else's hands.

Although Ma Chaoqun was fat, his movements were extremely agile, his pale, round body indescribably nimble and swift. The two golden spheres in his hands emitted dazzling light. Before he even arrived, the two golden spheres flew out first, carrying an irresistible force as they smashed toward the young man's head and chest with the weight of a thousand jun.

He Wuzui formed a Spirit Seal with his hands and released an astonishingly powerful Divine Ability Secret Technique.

A giant python's silhouette suddenly appeared in midair. The giant python raised its head as it approached, opening its bloody maw to bite down at the young man.

Everything happened in the blink of an eye. Lu Huaishuang had no time to react before her heart lurched. With a wave of her hand, countless Sword Qi slashed toward He Wuzui and Ma Chaoqun, hoping to force them to abandon their attack and turn back to defend themselves.

But she had realized too late and was clearly a step slow. This was only doing what she could and leaving the rest to fate; it could not help much at all.

Hissing sounds rang out. As the sword Qi flew, Ma Chaoqun and He Wuzui, who were lunging forward, seemed to slam into an invisible wall. Their figures abruptly froze, and they stared ahead in extreme horror as their hearts suddenly sank to the bottom of a valley.

What made the two of them halt was not Lu Huaishuang's sword Qi, but the young man's reaction.

Up ahead, the young man who had been bombarded by lightning punishment for a dozen breaths suddenly moved. He raised a hand and wiped at his messy hair. His originally calm, deep eyes were now filled with resentment, as though he were extremely dissatisfied with something.

Just this simple movement caused Ma Chaoqun and He Wuzui to lose the courage to attack, forcing them to stop.

The scene seemed bound by an invisible great hand, and time itself seemed to stop flowing. Everyone stood rooted in place, their eyes blank.

He was not dead? After being bombarded by that kind of lightning punishment for a dozen breaths, he actually was not dead? Lu Huaishuang could not help widening her beautiful eyes as a strange light flickered in them.

Only Ma Chaoqun's two golden spheres were still hurtling toward the young man, smashing right in front of him in the blink of an eye.

The young man lowered the hand touching his hair and casually flicked it.

Ma Chaoqun’s heart skipped a beat. In that instant, he completely lost his spiritual connection to his two golden pellets. When he looked closely, his body began to tremble violently.

Those two golden spheres had somehow already reached the young man's hand. The young man's five fingers moved nimbly, and the two golden spheres darted back and forth over his palm, turning into a blur of afterimages.

He had stolen his life-bound Secret Treasure with a single casual movement! What kind of Divine Ability was this? What in the world was this guy?

He Wuzui's giant python Divine Ability also lunged over, but with He Wuzui's mind shaken, he no longer had the strength to maintain it. The giant python merely swayed once before dissolving into nothing.

Gulp. He swallowed a mouthful of saliva, and his face began twitching uncontrollably.

"Um..." Ma Chaoqun pursed his dry lips, trying hard to find something to say, only to discover that his courage had long since vanished like smoke. Even his voice shook terribly.

"I wasn't the one who killed him." The young man casually tossed away Ge Ming's corpse, which he had been holding the entire time.

Ge Ming's death was entirely due to the lightning punishment. From beginning to end, he had never struck Ge Ming with any deadly intent.

“Sir… this is probably a misunderstanding.” Ma Chaoqun forced a smile, but it looked even worse than crying.

"What kind of misunderstanding? Tell me." The young man looked at him indifferently.

How could Ma Chaoqun explain anything? Normally, he always relied on strength to suppress others and attacked at the slightest disagreement. His skill with words had always been ordinary, and making flowers bloom from his tongue truly was not his specialty. Sweat seeped from his forehead, and he kept reaching up to wipe it away. If he could not explain himself, he feared he would die today.

Gritting his teeth, he said nothing more, turned around, and fled from this place as fast as he could.

If they had still had a sliver of a path to survival before the lightning punishment, then now, other than fleeing, they had no other way out.

Who told him and He Wuzui to court death by attacking this man? He could only hate himself for not bringing a sharper pair of eyes when leaving home this time.

Once he fled, how could He Wuzui dare remain? He immediately made a decision and fled in the completely opposite direction. This was an instinctive reaction. He only hoped the young man would chase Ma Chaoqun and give him a little space to escape.

But the result was destined to disappoint him.

The young man did not even look at them. He simply tossed the golden spheres in his hand, then took a step back, leaned backward, and fiercely swung his arm. One golden sphere tore through the air.

"Wah..." A miserable cry rang out, followed immediately by the sound of something collapsing to the ground.

He Wuzui did not dare look back. He fled with every ounce of strength he possessed, only wishing his parents had given him two more legs when he was born.

He could tell that voice belonged to Fatty Ma.

A sudden pain struck his back. His whole body was carried forward by a great force, and he involuntarily fell to the ground. He Wuzui hurriedly scrambled up, only for his expression to freeze. A puddle of crimson blood had somehow appeared on the ground, and... for some reason, his chest felt strangely hollow, as though something was missing.

Looking down, he saw the final sight of his life.

A huge hole had been torn open in his chest, and through that wound, he could clearly see his internal organs writhing.

His vision went black, and he collapsed weakly to the ground as his vitality rapidly drained away.

Not a sound could be heard. On this nameless Dead Star, it was so quiet that one could hear a needle drop.

Whether it was the Lu Family's several hundred disciples, Gale's Star Pirates, or the He Family's elites, everyone was deeply shaken. Pair after pair of eyes focused on the young man standing there without having moved at all, filled with awe as though they were looking at a heavenly god.

There was no other reason. Just now, this young man had merely thrown out two golden spheres at random, without the slightest fluctuation of aura. In other words, what he used was purely the strength of his physical body.

Then Ma Chaoqun and He Wuzui fell in response, without even a chance to resist.

Ma Chaoqun was dead.

He Wuzui was also dead.

Two First Stage Void King Realm masters were as fragile as three-year-old children in front of this young man.

Before the two of them plotted today's scheme, they probably never would have imagined they would come to such an end.

Shock, terror, dread, and all manner of emotions spread among the cultivators of the three groups. With the terrifying power this young man had displayed, if he wished, he could likely slaughter the thousand people here with a casual wave of his hand.

A complicated look filled Lu Huaishuang's beautiful eyes.

In the gaze she directed at the young man, there was hidden worry as well as gratitude.

The young man had indeed acted to help her, but Lu Huaishuang had only just met this person and did not know whether he was good or evil. She was not a little girl ignorant of the ways of the world, and with several hundred Lu Family elites under her command, she had to consider more than just her own likes and dislikes.

She wanted to step forward and probe this young man's character, but she did not have the courage.

Just as she was hesitating, she suddenly saw the young man turn his head and look toward her, a faint smile playing at the corner of his mouth.

Lu Huaishuang's heart jumped. She kept feeling that this smile harbored ill intent, and when those eyes fixed on her, they actually gave her the illusion that her clothes had been stripped away, leaving her completely naked. She could not help shrinking in on herself.

"If I were you, I would obediently roll over here."

At those words, Lu Huaishuang's expression changed slightly.

What did he mean? She did not seem to have offended him in any way. When the sudden change occurred just now, she had even wanted to help him, and when the lightning punishment descended, he had also thrown her out of the way. He did not seem like a bad person, so why had his attitude toward her suddenly changed?

Could it be that she had just escaped the wolf's den only to enter the tiger's lair?

But his fist was bigger, so Lu Huaishuang did not dare resist.

Her cultivation was not much stronger than Ma Chaoqun's or He Wuzui's. Both of them had been casually killed by the young man, so if he wanted to kill her, she feared she would be unable to resist.

With an inward sigh, she could only accept her fate.

Before she could lift her foot and step out, she suddenly detected a trace of movement behind her. She turned her head in astonishment and saw that another figure had appeared there at some unknown point, radiating the powerful cultivation of the Second Stage Void King Realm.

Lu Huaishuang's beautiful eyes could not help narrowing.

Sheng Yao!

Gale's First Chief.

Although this was her first time seeing this man, Lu Huaishuang recognized him at a glance.

Who else but Sheng Yao could be a Second Stage Void King Realm master appearing here at this moment?

This First Chief of Gale, who could still live extremely comfortably even after the great forces of the Star Domain had joined hands to encircle and suppress him, was now as humble as a brothel doorman. He came jogging over with his back bent and head bowed, his face wearing a cautious, ingratiating smile.

Passing Lu Huaishuang, he went straight to the young man, half knelt on the ground, and said in a clear voice, "Sheng Yao greets Sir. Sir's divine skill is peerless, and Sheng Yao is convinced. I am willing to follow at Sir's side and serve you like a dog or horse. Please grant me this favor!"

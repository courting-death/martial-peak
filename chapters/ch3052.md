## Chapter 3052 — Setting Out

Yet what awaited her was an unimaginable storm. Her cultivation was sealed, her Dragon Bloodline suppressed, and she was betrothed to Fu Chi, with the wedding set for one month later. Everything had happened so suddenly.

She would rather be exiled to the Dragon Tomb and left to fend for herself than marry another member of the Dragon Clan. There were not many members of the Dragon Clan, and she knew Fu Chi's character all too well. That man had set his sights on her long ago, repeatedly asking the Elders to grant him her hand in marriage and constantly pestering her.

Zhu Qing had never paid him any attention, yet she had not expected him to finally get his way this time.

She secretly made up her mind that if she were truly driven into a corner, she would have no choice but to fight to the death.

The soft rustle of footsteps approached.

Zhu Qing opened her eyes and saw a beautiful woman in white palace robes standing before her.

The woman appeared to be under thirty, with a full, voluptuous figure and peerless beauty. Her silver hair, like snow-white silk, was gathered into a married woman's bun behind her head, yet her lovely face resembled ice that had not thawed in ten thousand years. A single glance at her made one feel chilled and instinctively fearful.

"Second Elder." Although Zhu Qing resented her for acting so arbitrarily, she did not neglect the proper courtesy.

Fu Zhun asked indifferently, "Have you come around?"

Zhu Qing replied calmly, "Second Elder already knows what Qing'er thinks, so why ask an unnecessary question?"

Fu Zhun said, "It does not matter. The wedding is still more than ten days away, so you have time to consider it slowly. The Great Elder and I will only undo your seal once you have come around."

Zhu Qing frowned. "A melon forcibly plucked is not sweet. Why must Second Elder pressure Qing'er like this? You clearly know that Qing'er is unwilling."

Fu Zhun said, "The continuation of the Dragon Clan is what truly matters. What significance could a little romantic attachment possibly have? Worse, you actually dared to choose a human as your lover. You have brought shame upon the Dragon Clan!"

Zhu Qing said, "That is Qing'er's private affair. What does it have to do with the Dragon Clan?"

Fu Zhun's expression grew even colder. "Since the blood of the Dragon Clan flows through your veins, your every word and deed concerns the Dragon Clan's reputation. I cannot allow you to act however you please."

Zhu Qing asked sorrowfully, "In Second Elder's eyes, is there truly nothing else besides the continuation of the Dragon Clan?"

Fu Zhun swept her sleeve. "The Dragon Clan is already small in number, and the balance between men and women is severely uneven. If everyone refused to heed instruction as you do, the Dragon Clan would likely perish before long. As a member of the Dragon Clan, you must give more thought to the clan."

Zhu Qing smiled self-mockingly. "I never imagined Qing'er bore the great responsibility of perpetuating the Dragon Clan. Qing'er is truly overwhelmed by the honor."

"I know you resent me, but that does not matter. So long as you can give birth to a Dragon heir, what harm is there in enduring a few curses from you?" Fu Zhun looked at her and continued, "You should also abandon any unrealistic notions. Until you and Fu Chi have produced a Dragon child, I will not give you any opportunity to take your own life. I have said all there is to say. Think it over carefully. Rather than suffering unbearably in the future, it would be better for you to come around sooner."

With that, she turned and walked away.

Zhu Qing's body trembled slightly. Suddenly, she gritted her teeth and shouted, "Second Elder, do you truly feel no remorse over what happened more than ten years ago? She is still suffering in the Dragon Tomb. There is still time to release her now! If you wait any longer, it will be too late even if you regret it!"

The Second Elder paused for a moment but did not look back. "The Dragon Tomb can only be entered, never left. She brought it upon herself!"

The Second Elder disappeared from view, and a bitter smile appeared on Zhu Qing's face. Only now did she truly understand how hard-hearted the Second Elder was. Deliberately bringing up the events from more than ten years ago had not affected her in the slightest. Zhu Qing knew that no matter what she did, she would never persuade the Second Elder.

Several li away, a sturdy figure came forward to greet her with a fawning smile and cupped his fists. "Greetings, Second Elder."

Fu Zhun frowned at Fu Chi. "What are you doing here?"

Fu Chi asked cautiously, "I came to see how Qing'er is doing. You just met with her. What did she say?"

Fu Zhun said, "What does it matter what she said? In a little over ten days, she will be yours."

Fu Chi chuckled. "That is true. Second Elder, may I go in and see Qing'er? If I speak with her more, perhaps she will change her mind."

"She is in a foul mood right now. Seeing her would only have the opposite effect." Fu Zhun passed him, her voice drifting back. "Rather than wasting effort on her, you should prepare yourself for the wedding."

"Yes!" Fu Chi answered respectfully.

Since the Second Elder had refused him, he did not dare insist. He had come today hoping he might see Zhu Qing and learn whether her attitude had changed at all, but after hearing the Second Elder's words, he immediately understood that Zhu Qing was still as unyielding as ever.

He could not help feeling annoyed.

He was a member of the Dragon Clan, an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon. In what way was he inferior to a mere human? Not only had that slut Zhu Qing rejected the marriage arranged by the Elders, but she still could not forget that human.

That man's name seemed to be Yang Kai.

Fu Chi silently committed the name to memory, planning to seek trouble with Yang Kai when the opportunity arose. He bitterly hated Yang Kai for taking Zhu Qing's virginity. Zhu Qing was different from Fu Ling and had an excellent reputation on Dragon Isle, which was precisely why he liked her so much.

He had pursued her many times in the past but had never won her affection. Who could have imagined that after a single trip outside, she would actually become involved with a human?

This filled Fu Chi with bitter hatred.

But none of that mattered now. The wedding would take place in a little over ten days, and Zhu Qing would then become his woman. Moreover, the Second Elder had apparently already declared that until he and Zhu Qing produced a Dragon child, she would not undo Zhu Qing's seal.

Thus, even though Zhu Qing now possessed a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline, he did not fear her in the slightest. Once they were married, would he not be free to do whatever he pleased with her? There were plenty of playthings on the island, but how could those ants possibly compare with Zhu Qing?

Fu Chi was eagerly looking forward to the wedding in a little over ten days. After lingering on the ice island for a while, he immediately flew back to his own Spirit Island. To ensure the wedding proceeded as scheduled, he had to keep the palace's construction on track.

...

Inside the Small Xuan Realm, Yang Kai opened his eyes. A brilliant light gleamed within them, and Dragon Pressure radiated from his body.

He had consumed all of the more than one hundred Dragon Blood Pills, and had even eaten every last Middle-Level Dragon Blood Flower. Only Lower-Level Dragon Blood Flowers remained in his Spatial Ring now.

Counting the days, it was almost time.

Yang Kai stopped circulating the Dragon Transformation Art and returned to his human form.

Sweeping the Small Xuan Realm with his Divine Sense, he soon found Li Jiao and Lu Sanniang.

The two seemed ready to put their past grievances behind them and were standing together, talking. Lu Yuqin hid some distance away, watching them timidly.

When Yang Kai suddenly appeared, Li Jiao and Lu Sanniang were both startled, and their expressions became somewhat unnatural.

After all, they had been caught sharing a romantic moment, leaving both of them rather embarrassed. Lu Sanniang's face immediately flushed red.

Li Jiao was even more awkward and hurriedly made conversation. "Brother Yang, what place is this?"

He had spent several days here with Lu Sanniang and her daughter and had keenly realized that this place was a Small World, leaving him secretly shocked.

It was only now that he understood how he had escaped the corrosion of the Demonic Qi back in the Revolving World. Yang Kai had knocked him unconscious several times there, and every time had coincided with a change in the World Laws.

Yang Kai must have thrown him into this Small World on those occasions as well, allowing him to avoid the miserable fate of being tainted by Demonic Qi.

Thinking back, Yang Kai probably had not trusted him much at the time, which was why he had repeatedly knocked him unconscious. Now, however, he had clearly earned Yang Kai's trust.

Yang Kai ignored his question and declared, "I'm going to teach Fu Chi a lesson!"

Li Jiao stared blankly for a moment, then gritted his teeth. "I will lend Brother Yang a hand."

He had not known who Fu Chi was before, but after speaking with Lu Sanniang many times over the past few days, he had learned that Fu Chi was the man responsible for what she had suffered all those years ago. He had long since come to hate Fu Chi bitterly, so upon hearing Yang Kai's declaration, how could he possibly restrain himself?

His grudge against Fu Chi was tantamount to the hatred of having his wife stolen. How could he leave it unavenged?

Fu Chi was an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon, so Li Jiao truly had no chance of victory against him. Bloodline suppression alone would leave him powerless.

But with Yang Kai supporting him from the side, the situation would be entirely different.

He did not expect to be Fu Chi's opponent. He only hoped to risk his life and bite a chunk of flesh from Fu Chi's body to vent the hatred in his heart.

Lu Sanniang's expression changed drastically when she heard this, and she cried out, "You cannot!"

Li Jiao looked at her, his expression gradually softening, and smiled. "Wait for me. I will avenge you!"

"You cannot go!" Lu Sanniang paled in alarm. She stepped forward and seized Li Jiao's arm, looking as though she would not release him even if she died.

Li Jiao could not bear to act against her, so he could only turn to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai understood and raised his hand, pointing toward Lu Sanniang.

With a single finger strike, Lu Sanniang collapsed limply.

"Mother!" Lu Yuqin ran over from a distance with a cry of alarm and caught Lu Sanniang in her arms.

"Your mother is fine." Li Jiao reached out and stroked her head, then sighed. "Take good care of your mother. If I return, all will be well. If I do not..." He paused for a long time before continuing, "Have your mother take you back to Ruyi Sect."

"Uncle Li..." Lu Yuqin had clearly sensed something as well and looked at Li Jiao with reddened eyes.

Although she did not know exactly what relationship Li Jiao had with her mother, she had seen everything that had happened over the past few days. She had never seen her mother so happy before, so she knew Uncle Li must be very important to her.

This scene of parting, perhaps forever, nearly brought her to tears.

Li Jiao smiled faintly. "The next time we meet, remember to call me Father!"

With life or death uncertain and the road ahead fraught with danger, only at this moment did Li Jiao truly untie the knot in his heart. His heart became open and unburdened, his thoughts clear, and his entire being felt refreshed.

Her vision blurred, and Li Jiao and Yang Kai vanished without a trace.

Lu Yuqin sat there in a slight daze.

She had a father now? She sat blankly on the ground, holding her mother in her arms. Only after a long time did she begin laughing and crying at once. "Mother, I have a father too! Hurry and wake up! Qin'er has a father too!"

Had anyone else heard these words, they would surely have found them laughable. Who in this world did not have a father? Yet this had been a young girl's most heartfelt wish for more than ten years.

## Chapter 2753 — You're Dead

Qin Chaoyang's eyes widened, staring blankly at the figure blocking the doorway. He never expected Yang Kai to appear at such a critical moment, and with a single finger, injure Luo Ping! Qin Chaoyang's expression changed, panic rising within him. Yang Kai didn't know Luo Ping's identity, but Qin Chaoyang did—he was not someone to provoke lightly. If not for that, Qin Chaoyang would never have been able to keep the Qin Family safe.

"Family Head Qin, it's been a while!" Yang Kai smiled faintly.

Just then, the servant in a round cap slipped in from behind, looking miserable. “Family Head, this man insisted on seeing you. I came to report it, but when I heard the commotion inside, I didn’t dare interrupt. I didn’t expect—”

Qin Chaoyang waved his hand. "You may go."

The servant hurried out as though he had just been granted a pardon.

"Who are you, and why are you so ruthless!" Luo Ping gritted his teeth and growled. Just now, Yang Kai's finger had pierced through his palm, leaving a hole that bled profusely.

"You call that ruthless?" Yang Kai grinned. "Compared to what you planned, this is nothing." He turned to Qin Chaoyang. "Who is this guy?"

He'd listened for a while but still didn't know this man's identity. Qin Chaoyang seemed quite afraid of him.

Qin Chaoyang's face turned bitter, but before he could speak, Luo Ping shouted, "I am a disciple of Star God Palace! You dare injure me? You're dead!"

"Star God Palace?" Yang Kai's brows furrowed.

Luo Ping sneered. "What, scared? If you are, kneel and kowtow a few times. Maybe I'll spare your life!"

"Is he really a Star God Palace disciple?" Yang Kai looked solemnly at Qin Chaoyang.

Qin Chaoyang forced a smile. "Absolutely."

Yang Kai nodded. Star God Palace disciples weren't ones to impersonate lightly. In the Southern Territory, impersonating disciples of the other three major sects might not be a big deal as long as you weren't caught, but pretending to be a Star God Palace disciple would bring disaster.

The man before him had cultivation at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm with a steady aura. He was young, likely from a major sect, and since Qin Chaoyang confirmed his identity, it must be true.

In other words, Luo Ping really was a Star God Palace disciple.

No wonder he was so arrogant, showing no fear even before an Emperor Realm cultivator like Yang Kai. It was all because of his Star God Palace status.

"Kneel!" Luo Ping, clutching his bleeding hand, snarled at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai slowly raised his palm, Emperor Yuan surging within. He said calmly, "You have three breaths to get out of here. If you're still here after that, I'll clean house for Star God Palace!"

Luo Ping froze, dumbfounded.

He thought revealing his identity would terrify the other man, but Yang Kai didn't care at all, even boasting he'd clean house for Star God Palace. Was he serious? Was there something wrong with his head? Luo Ping was a Star God Palace disciple!

"Yang Kai, don't be reckless!" Qin Chaoyang cried out, his face pale. If Luo Ping really died in the Qin Family, the Qin Family would be finished.

"Yang Kai?" Hearing Qin Chaoyang's shout, Luo Ping's eyes widened as he scrutinized Yang Kai. His pupils shrank, and fear appeared on his face.

Suddenly, he remembered a sect message he'd received recently. The description of the person matched Yang Kai perfectly.

Was this really Yang Kai? If so, his Star God Palace disciple status wouldn't intimidate him. Seeing the power in Yang Kai's palm grow stronger, Luo Ping dared not linger. He darted out, fleeing the Qin Family like the wind, not even leaving a threat behind.

Qin Chaoyang was stunned, staring in the direction Luo Ping disappeared, his mind blank.

What just happened?

A dignified Star God Palace disciple fled like a stray dog. Did Qin Chaoyang see that right? Even if Yang Kai showed Emperor Realm cultivation, it shouldn't have scared him so much. Was he really a Star God Palace disciple? Qin Chaoyang even doubted Luo Ping's identity.

In a small alley in Fenglin City, Luo Ping stopped, face pale. He released his Divine Sense, and, sensing no trace of Yang Kai, finally breathed a sigh of relief, collapsing to the ground without a shred of dignity, his major sect disciple bearing gone.

Only now did he feel a surge of fear and lingering dread, his hands trembling uncontrollably.

His Star God Palace disciple status was genuine, not an act, and he was even an inner disciple with some standing in Star God Palace.

With such status, he could ignore most Emperor Realm cultivators in the Southern Territory, but Yang Kai was different—different from all Emperor Realm cultivators.

Recently, the sect sent a message telling disciples outside to watch for a certain person—Yang Kai. Luo Ping hadn't known why, but after asking a Senior Brother in Linghu City, he understood.

Yang Kai was involved in the deaths of Tan Junhao, Elder Tan, and Wu Ming, Deacon Wu. All signs pointed to Elder Tan and Deacon Wu dying at his hands.

This was confirmed by more than twenty Emperor Realm experts in the Southern Territory, with not a shred of doubt.

Elder Tan was one of Star God Palace's only four Third Stage Emperor elders, his cultivation unfathomable, yet Yang Kai killed him. When Luo Ping heard this, he couldn't believe it.

Even a Third Stage Emperor could die? By comparison, Wu Ming's death wasn't as shocking.

A Star God Palace elder and a deacon killed—this was earth-shattering. The entire Star God Palace took it seriously, countless people searching everywhere for Yang Kai.

But since Flowing Shadow City, Yang Kai had vanished, as if evaporated. Now, he appeared in Fenglin City.

"You dared hurt me, now you're dead." Luo Ping gritted his teeth, took out a Transmission Compass, furtively checked his surroundings, then immersed his Divine Sense and sent a message.

After sending the message, Luo Ping smiled sinisterly.

Sect experts would soon arrive in Fenglin City. Let's see how you die!

Back at the Qin Family, Yang Kai, having learned the truth, was silent for a long time.

According to Qin Chaoyang, Luo Ping, the Star God Palace disciple, had been invited by the Qin Family to sit in residence. Fenglin City wasn't stable lately; its rise had attracted many experts. The old families had the advantage of timing and location, but lacked strength, surviving only in the cracks. Two families had already been wiped out.

The Qin Family survived thanks to Luo Ping's protection, but Luo Ping, though not demanding cultivation resources, was extremely interested in women, making it hard for Qin Chaoyang to satisfy him. That's how today's events happened.

"Yang Kai, you'd better leave. Luo Ping will definitely send a message to Star God Palace. Staying here isn't wise," Qin Chaoyang advised worriedly.

Yang Kai waved his hand. "It's fine. If Star God Palace doesn't come, so be it. If they do, I have a score to settle with them."

He'd killed Tan Junhao and Wu Ming. Star God Palace wouldn't let it go; they'd demand an explanation, or their supreme prestige would be challenged.

Yang Kai never planned to hide from Star God Palace. In the past, he could only look up to such a giant, but now, he vaguely had the capital to negotiate with them.

And Radiant Moon Great Emperor, being a Great Emperor, surely wasn't unreasonable. The twenty-plus pawns he arranged in Flowing Shadow City should have played their part by now.

"You—" Qin Chaoyang was exasperated. This was Star God Palace, not some random sect. How could Yang Kai remain so calm?

Yang Kai smiled. "Enough about me. What will the Qin Family do?"

Qin Chaoyang was stunned, then sighed heavily. Now, not only had the Qin Family lost Luo Ping's protection, they'd offended him. Their days would be hard, and Fenglin City might no longer have a place for the Qin Family.

"I don't know, but we'll definitely have to leave Fenglin City." Qin Chaoyang suddenly seemed much older.

"Leave Fenglin City, but where can you go? The entire Southern Territory belongs to Star God Palace."

Qin Chaoyang fell silent. Even if they left Fenglin City, revenge would still come, unless they left the Southern Territory and escaped Star God Palace's sight—but that was easier said than done.

Yang Kai said, "How about I arrange a place for you?"

Qin Chaoyang looked up. "Where?"

"Northern Territory!" Yang Kai replied.

Qin Chaoyang opened his mouth, then forced a smile. "It's too far. The Qin Family's disciples have uneven cultivation; we can't possibly travel that far."

"You don't need to walk. If the Family Head agrees, I'll send you to the Northern Territory within ten days!"

"Ten days!" Qin Chaoyang was shocked. His first thought was that it was impossible, but then he realized it might be, though it would require something legendary.

"You can really get us to the Northern Territory in ten days?" Qin Chaoyang asked, trembling. If so, the Qin Family could escape disaster. The Northern Territory was worlds apart from the Southern Territory; Star God Palace couldn't reach there.

"Absolutely!" Yang Kai nodded seriously. "If the Family Head trusts me, arrangements can be made immediately. We'll leave in two days."

Qin Chaoyang struggled for a moment. He wanted to trust Yang Kai, but the family's future was at stake, so he had to be cautious.

After a while, he made up his mind. "Then I'll trouble Brother Yang."

Yang Kai smiled. "I won't let the Family Head down."

He hadn't planned to bring the Qin Family to Lingxiao Palace, just came to check in. But since the Qin Family was desperate, taking them along was no trouble.

Whether it's one sheep or a flock, it's all the same to Yang Kai.

Besides, this way, he could gain Qin Yu! With Qin Yu's current cultivation talent, she might one day become an Emperor, adding strength to Lingxiao Palace. For the sect, that's nothing but good.

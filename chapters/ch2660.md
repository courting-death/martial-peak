## Chapter 2660 — Lingxiao Palace

Yang Kai asked in surprise, "You all came so openly to offer Wenqing Sect help in their time of need. Aren't you afraid Ice Heart Valley will cause you trouble?"

The conflict between Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley had long spread throughout the Northern Territory. Now that Wenqing Sect was in trouble, these people actually came under the pretense of offering aid. If word got out, would Ice Heart Valley really let them off?

Li Qingyuan smiled and said, "Ice Heart Valley won't. Lord Bing Yun is a person of great benevolence. Since we haven't directly opposed Ice Heart Valley, she naturally has no reason to take her anger out on us."

"Exactly. The women of Ice Heart Valley may be proud and cold, but they're all reasonable people. Why would they fuss over this?"

"So that's how it is!" Yang Kai's expression showed understanding as he nodded slightly.

Those who abide by principles can be persuaded—or even taken advantage of—through proper means. Though it may not be entirely appropriate to describe Ice Heart Valley that way, it was more or less the case.

If the situation were reversed and Ice Heart Valley suffered disaster, and these people came under the guise of offering help, Wenqing Sect would never let them off.

It was precisely because they understood Ice Heart Valley's way of handling things that these people acted so fearlessly.

"Young Brother, you still haven't told us what's really going on inside Wenqing Sect." Li Qingyuan, seeing the topic had drifted, hurriedly brought it back.

Yang Kai replied indifferently, "The Wenqing Sect leadership has been completely wiped out, and countless disciples are dead or wounded. What else could it be? It's just a case of the tree falling and the monkeys scattering."

Li Qingyuan's eyes lit up with delight. "So, Wenqing Sect is now completely empty?"

Yang Kai looked at him mockingly and said, "This lord seems quite happy. Weren't you all here to offer comfort and aid?"

Li Qingyuan immediately put on a sorrowful face and said, "Happy? Not at all. This Lord is heartbroken. Wenqing Sect, a top sect, has now been wiped out. Tens of thousands of years of heritage cut off—it's a great loss for our Northern Territory."

"What a pity, truly a pity."

"Such a great force, how did it suddenly get destroyed?"

The others all sighed in lament, faces full of grief. To outsiders, it would really seem as if they had a close relationship with Wenqing Sect.

Changing the subject, Li Qingyuan's eyes burned as he asked, "Since you came out of Wenqing Sect, did you... discover anything inside?"

At this point, he was in no rush to go in and scavenge, because since Yang Kai had entered Wenqing Sect before them, if there were any benefits, Yang Kai would surely have taken them.

Right now, keeping an eye on Yang Kai was the safest bet.

"I found the Treasure Vault!" Yang Kai declared, dropping a bombshell.

With those words, countless greedy and covetous eyes fixed on Yang Kai, as sharp as blades.

"What's in the Treasure Vault...?" Li Qingyuan's voice trembled uncontrollably.

Yang Kai chuckled and said, "Source Crystals, hundreds of millions of them—Lower-Level, Middle-Level, Upper-Level, everything you can imagine. All kinds of Spiritual Pills and wondrous medicines. Secret Treasures, Imperial Treasures, cultivation manuals—anything you can think of, it's there."

"Hiss..."

The sound of people sucking in cold breaths rang out. Everyone was excited, and the greed in their eyes grew even stronger.

Yang Kai looked at the crowd with a smile and said, "You all seem quite tempted."

Li Qingyuan forcibly suppressed the urge to swallow and began plotting how to take down this ignorant brat and enjoy the spoils alone. If he really got Wenqing Sect's tens of thousands of years of accumulation, Feiyuan Pavilion's strength would soar, and becoming a second Wenqing Sect would no longer be a dream.

Many others had the same idea. For a moment, everyone saw those around them as enemies, and their eyes grew wary.

"This brat is full of nonsense!" someone suddenly shouted. "It's true Wenqing Sect has a Treasure Vault, but with your strength, how could you possibly open it? So young and already so scheming, trying to sow discord here—he deserves to be punished. Don't let him fool you!"

The crowd was startled, thinking this person made a lot of sense.

Wenqing Sect was such a large sect, and it did have a Treasure Vault, but how could it be so easily opened? The method to open it was surely only known to people like Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo.

Li Qingyuan came to his senses and said unhappily, "Young Brother, that's a bit dishonest."

Yang Kai sneered, "Believe it or not, it's up to you!"

As he spoke, he suddenly took out a strange stone about three zhang tall from his Spatial Ring. As soon as it appeared, the temperature around them plummeted, and an intense chill spread in all directions.

Even the dozen or so Emperor Realm experts felt their power stagnate under the influence of this chill.

"This is..."

"Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron!"

"What? It's actually Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron! Such a treasure exists in this world, and such a huge piece!"

"My heavens, where did this come from? I've heard that even a palm-sized piece of Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron can be used to forge an Imperial Treasure. How many Imperial Treasures could be made from a piece three zhang tall?"

"If this were sold, it would fetch at least over a hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals!"

"This brat wasn't lying—he really did open Wenqing Sect's Treasure Vault. This is Wenqing Sect's property!"

...

A cacophony of voices erupted as everyone stared wide-eyed at the three zhang tall Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron, utterly shocked. Just this one item alone was priceless. If Yang Kai really opened Wenqing Sect's Treasure Vault, how many treasures did he get?

All previous doubts vanished. Everyone now believed Yang Kai had struck it rich in Wenqing Sect, and the Emperor Realm experts grew restless, eyeing the Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron like tigers, ready to pounce at any moment.

But with so many rivals, no one dared to act rashly, and the situation reached a stalemate.

More people, however, were simply curious—why did Yang Kai suddenly take out such a peerless treasure? To show off? That made no sense. Didn't he know it would bring him trouble?

While everyone was puzzled, Yang Kai flicked his wrist, and the Myriad Sword appeared in his hand. He poured Emperor Yuan into the blade, Laws of Space swirling around him, and began to carve on the Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron, stroke by stroke.

"What is this brat up to?"

"Looks like he's carving something."

"What a waste! How could he carve on such a treasure? It's simply outrageous—an affront to both men and gods!"

Sss sss sss sss...

No matter how angry or scornful the others were, Yang Kai continued to carve, focused and meticulous.

After a full incense stick's time, Yang Kai finally took a deep breath, put away the Myriad Sword, and his face was a bit pale, as if the effort had taken a toll on him.

After all, this was the finest artifact material. Even carving a few words was no easy task.

Yet after giving his handiwork a careful inspection, Yang Kai found himself rather satisfied with the result.

At that moment, a strong wind blew, scattering some debris from the Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron and revealing three large, flamboyant characters. The calligraphy wasn't exactly beautiful, but it gave off a strange feeling, as if one could get lost in it just by looking, as if it could devour one's mind.

"Lingxiao Palace!"

Someone read out the three characters carved on the Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron, leaving the others confused.

"What is this Lingxiao Palace?"

"Sounds like the name of a sect, but is there such a sect in the Northern Territory?"

"Never heard of it."

At this moment, Li Qingyuan looked thoughtful and asked Yang Kai, "Young Brother, what is the meaning of this?"

Yang Kai patted the Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron beside him and said with a grin, "Nothing much. I just wanted to let everyone know that from today on, this land is the foundation of my Lingxiao Palace. Since so many guests have come, I don't have to notify everyone one by one. I hope you'll all spread the word when you return. If any fools trespass on Lingxiao Palace's territory, don't blame me for being ruthless."

"What! What does this brat mean?"

"Is he planning to found a sect?"

"What a joke! Just a brat—what right does he have to establish a sect here?"

"Exactly. This is Wenqing Sect's legacy. Even if someone were to take it, it wouldn't be his turn."

"His ambitions are sky-high, but I fear his life is as fragile as paper!"

...

The voices of discussion reached Yang Kai's ears, but he remained unmoved, as steady as a mountain. When the noise died down, he sneered, "Anyone have a problem? If you do, keep it to yourself—no need to ruin everyone's mood!"

"This brat is too arrogant! Does he really think there's no one in the Northern Territory?"

Li Qingyuan's face darkened as he said, "Brat, you're going too far. Since we've all gathered here today, this matter isn't for you alone to decide. Wenqing Sect is a sect of the Northern Territory, and its legacy and Treasure Vault should be decided by all the heroes of the Northern Territory, not just you."

With a single sentence, Li Qingyuan elevated the issue of Wenqing Sect's legacy to the level of the entire Northern Territory. He truly was a cunning old fox, and the crowd cheered him on.

Yang Kai laughed, "On my turf, everything goes my way. If anyone's not convinced, feel free to step up."

"Sect Master Li, this brat is too arrogant. Today, we must teach him a lesson."

"That's right. We've already given him enough face, but he keeps pushing. No need to waste words with him."

"Let's do it. Now that Wenqing Sect is empty, there's nothing to worry about."

They hadn't dared to rush in before, fearing some experts wer still in Wenqing Sect. Now that it was confirmed empty, and Yang Kai had taken the Treasure Vault and wanted to claim all this land, no one could hold back any longer.

A trace of determination flashed in Li Qingyuan's eyes as he nodded, "Fine. Since he's so stubborn, let's teach him a lesson."

As soon as he finished speaking, he charged forward.

The others were unwilling to fall behind and all rushed at Yang Kai, each hoping to snatch his Spatial Ring.

Yang Kai casually took out a three zhang tall piece of Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron. Who knew how many more treasures he had in his ring?

In an instant, more than a dozen Emperor Realm experts charged at him, making for a lively scene.

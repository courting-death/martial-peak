## Chapter 2968 — Grew Taller

The palace doors were pushed open, and several women filed in one after another. Each one looked quite beautiful, and with Fu Yu at their head, they walked in arranged in a line.

Each of them held a large pile of ancient-looking texts in her hands.

"Sir, everything you requested is here." Fu Yu lowered her head as she spoke, her expression looking reluctant and unwilling, as if she had been forced to do something she did not want to do.

That was indeed the case. If the Human Sovereign had not given the order, how could she possibly have come to do this sort of servant's work? She also had no idea what the Human Sovereign was thinking. Was there any need to curry favor with this outsider to such an extent?

The dissatisfaction in her heart showed directly on her face.

"Put them down." Yang Kai swept his gaze over this group of people and discovered that most of these women looked somewhat familiar. They were actually all princesses without exception. Fu Yu appeared to be the oldest, while the youngest was only fourteen or fifteen years old. He had not seen her in the main hall earlier, but her Six-Leaf Body could not be false. This youngest princess was clearly extremely curious. Standing at the end of the line, she kept sizing Yang Kai up with a pair of large eyes.

Influenced by Fu Yu, she had a preconceived idea that Yang Kai was some heinous villain beyond pardon, but looking at him now, he did not seem quite like one. He did not have three heads and six arms either, so she did not know why Second Sister hated him so much.

Yang Kai's gaze swept over, and he winked at her.

The little princess was startled and hurriedly lowered her head, setting down the things in her hands.

"Do you have any other instructions? If not, we are leaving." Fu Yu looked coldly at Yang Kai, her tone stiff and her face filled with defiance.

Yang Kai glanced at her with a hint of mischief in his eyes and waved his hand dismissively. "Wait outside for now. I'll call you if there's anything I need. Don't disturb me otherwise."

"W-Wait?" Fu Yu's eyes widened, and she almost suspected she had heard wrong.

She was the dignified Second Princess of Human Sovereign City, beneath one person and above tens of thousands. Coming here to deliver texts to him was already humiliating, yet he actually dared tell her to wait outside? Did he really think she was a servant he could order around at will? This was simply intolerable.

Her anger nearly erupted, but when she recalled the Human Sovereign's earlier instructions, it was as if a basin of cold water had been poured over her. Her towering flames of rage were instantly extinguished, and she could only grit her teeth and say, "Yes!"

She did not dare disobey the Human Sovereign's orders no matter what.

The princesses slowly withdrew from the palace and waited outside the doors, each with her own thoughts. Aside from the little princess, the others clearly all had resentful looks on their faces, feeling that this sort of thing should not have been done by them.

Inside the palace, Yang Kai and Zhu Qing looked at the texts piled on the table. Each of them picked up one volume and began reading.

These texts were basically all records concerning the Revolving World. Half of them covered the history of the Revolving World, while the other half introduced this world's customs, terrain, and geography. The human race had stood here for more than a hundred thousand years, and this world was not especially large, so aside from the ten-thousand-li region around Demon Fury City, which was forbidden ground to the human race, the humans had already explored basically every other place.

These were all secret collections of the royal family, impossible for ordinary people to see.

For several days in succession, Yang Kai and Zhu Qing both remained inside the palace behind closed doors, carefully reading through these texts and collecting all kinds of information about this world.

Only several days later did Yang Kai rub his forehead, put down the final text in his hands, and fall into thought with his brows lightly furrowed.

Over there, Zhu Qing finished reading at almost the same time and said thoughtfully, "So this world really is located in the gap between two worlds, within a spatial crack."

Yang Kai chuckled softly and said, "If not, there would not be something like the changing of World Laws."

"It was truly not easy for them to survive and reproduce in a place like this. The Sacred Tree helped them greatly."

"The Sacred Tree..." Yang Kai nodded slightly. "It did indeed help them greatly. Not only did it shelter the whole of Human Sovereign City, it also left behind the inheritance of Shamanic Arts."

Yang Kai had originally thought the Shamanic Arts of the Imperial Family had been passed down from generation to generation by their ancestors, keeping the flame of Shamanic Arts alive, but now it seemed that this was not entirely the case.

More than a hundred thousand years had passed, and World Laws had changed. In the Star Realm, Shamanic Arts had gradually been replaced by Martial Dao. The Revolving World occasionally connected with the Star Realm's Laws, so naturally, it would not be suitable for cultivating Shamanic Arts either.

But Qing's existence had allowed Shamanic Arts to be passed down generation after generation.

Those members of the Imperial Family could truly cultivate Shamanic Arts only by entering secluded cultivation near Qing, comprehending Qing's power, and resonating with Qing. Without Qing's help, even if they possessed the cultivation methods of Shamanic Arts, the World Laws would not allow them to cultivate the power of a Shaman.

Qing was a meritorious cultivator of an entire era, the very transmitter of the primordial world's power. Without him, Shamanic Arts would have long since perished in the Revolving World.

Unfortunately, after that decisive battle between humans and demons back then, his thoughts had already sunk into oblivion. The Qing of today had only a chaotic mass of will left, strictly guarding the wish he had held before his death: to shelter the human race and shelter Shamanic Arts.

Yang Kai did not know whether he would ever have a chance to awaken again, but he estimated that the chance was very slim. After all, more than a hundred thousand years had already passed. If Qing could truly awaken, he feared he would have done so long ago, and would not have waited until now.

Moreover, Yang Kai estimated that even if Qing could truly awaken, he probably would no longer remember the past. He would obtain a new life.

He deserved that new beginning as well.

Information about Yin Wind Mountain was also recorded in the texts, but it was extremely scarce. Only a rough location had been recorded, while nothing was known about what the situation inside was like, because the human race had never set foot in that ghostly place for more than a hundred thousand years.

On the other hand, demons often lingered nearby, borrowing the pure Demonic Qi that surged out from Yin Wind Mountain to cultivate.

So if they truly wanted to go to Yin Wind Mountain, they would inevitably come into conflict with the demons.

Yang Kai did not care about this. At any rate, he had once subdued more than ten Demon Kings and driven them at will, and he also knew the demons' various traits like the back of his hand. If he really ran into demons, the ones who would suffer would be them.

There was still no news of Zhu Lie from the Human Sovereign.

The World Laws outside had already changed, and the experts of the whole of Human Sovereign City had been dispatched frequently, searching everywhere for information about Zhu Lie, but so far, they had found nothing.

Yang Kai and Zhu Qing were not too worried about Zhu Lie. At any rate, he was an Eighth-Tier red dragon. If something happened to him so easily, he would not be a member of the Dragon Clan.

So the two of them lived leisurely and contentedly in the Imperial Palace.

One day, Yang Kai suddenly woke from meditation. His eyes shone faintly, and he seemed somewhat excited.

"What is it?" Zhu Qing did not need to cultivate and was holding a book at the side, reading through it. After noticing Yang Kai's movement, she looked up and asked.

"Qing'er, I seem to have grown stronger," Yang Kai said, somewhat exhilarated.

"Growing stronger is a good thing, so why are you acting like this?"

"Because I do not know why I grew stronger."

"In what aspect?"

"Bloodline!" Yang Kai looked at Zhu Qing curiously. "I do not seem to have done anything during this time, so why can I feel that my bloodline has grown much stronger?"

The strengthening of his bloodline was very obvious, because Yang Kai could feel that the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin Power had fused with him a little more deeply. During this time, he had not cultivated the Dragon Transformation Art, nor had he encountered any life-or-death battle. There was no reason for him to have grown stronger for no reason.

The enhancement of power had always required a certain price.

"Let's try it!" To avoid the possibility that it was some misconception, Yang Kai immediately let out a low roar and activated the Dragon Transformation Art.

When a high-pitched dragon roar rang out, a golden giant dragon flashed and vanished.

A series of crackling sounds followed as the bones throughout his body gave off explosive pops like beans being fried. His figure suddenly rose taller, and his neatly tied hair bun burst apart. The instant dragon pressure spread out, Yang Kai felt an itch on his forehead, as if something were about to break out of his body.

His arms lengthened, his hands transformed into dragon claws, his clothes were stretched until they tore apart, and a layer of sturdy dragon scales covered the skin exposed outside.

He had long since grown accustomed to all of this and also knew what sort of changes would happen to him, so he focused intently on experiencing the changes in his own body.

By the time all the changes stopped, Yang Kai had already transformed into a colossal creature. He gently breathed out, and two streams of air like white smoke instantly sprayed from his nostrils.

Zhu Qing sat on the chair, closed the book, and raised her head to look up at him.

Yang Kai strode forward and walked once around the palace, saying in surprise, "Heh, I seem to have grown a little taller."

During his previous battle with Zhu Lie in Qingyang Shrine, he had made a breakthrough in the Dragon Transformation Art, and his three-zhang body had suddenly become four zhang.

But now, his height had actually increased again. By a conservative estimate, it had already reached four and a half zhang. This increase was not a misconception, but something real.

Since cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art, Yang Kai had also vaguely grasped some key points. The deeper he cultivated this Secret Art, the more thoroughly his body fused with the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin Power, and the purer and higher-grade his Dragon Clan bloodline became. The most intuitive manifestation of this was his height. As his cultivation deepened, his half-dragon body had also grown taller and larger.

Yang Kai strongly suspected that when the day came that he could truly refine the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin Power completely, he would be able to transform into a Golden Dragon and roam the myriad worlds.

"How tall were you before?" Zhu Qing asked.

"About this tall." Yang Kai gestured with his hand. Fortunately, this palace had been built extremely grandly; otherwise, he truly would not have been able to straighten his back now.

"Then you really did grow stronger." Zhu Qing pursed her lips and smiled.

"But why?" Yang Kai wanted to scratch his face, but his dragon claw scraped against the scales on his cheek, sending sparks flying everywhere.

Growing stronger was a good thing, but knowing the result without knowing the reason was not good. He had to understand the cause before he could continue working in this direction in the future.

He carefully recalled what he had experienced recently and found that there was nothing especially worth paying attention to. Moreover, it had been less than two months since his last breakthrough in the Dragon Transformation Art, and Yang Kai could clearly remember every detail in between, but he could not recall any special experience he had undergone.

Zhu Qing lowered her head, a trace of crimson flying across her cheeks. She opened the book and continued reading, but her thoughts were not on it at all.

"Do you know something?" Yang Kai noticed her strange behavior and turned his head to look.

Zhu Qing shook her head like a rattle-drum, her face turning even redder.

"Hurry up and tell me." Yang Kai walked up to her and crouched down in front of her. Even like this, he was still several times taller than Zhu Qing. Looking down at that little person, he felt extremely strange.

He estimated that after Zhu Qing transformed into a giant dragon, her field of vision was probably like this as well.

## Chapter 2638 — Abusing Power for Personal Gain

Feng Chi let out a cold laugh for a while before finally raising his head to glance at Yang Kai, then asked Cao Xu, “Another Emperor has arrived?”

If Cao Xu personally brought him over, then he must be an Emperor Realm cultivator without a doubt.

“Yes!” Cao Xu replied respectfully.

“Good, very good!” Feng Chi laughed heartily, nodded lightly at Yang Kai, cupped his fists, and said, “May I ask how to address you, friend?”

Although he looked much older than Yang Kai, on the path of the Martial Dao, the accomplished come first. Since Yang Kai was an Emperor, he was qualified to interact as an equal.

“Xiao Baiyi greets Elder Feng.” Yang Kai also smiled slightly, his manner polite and proper.

“So it’s Brother Xiao!” Feng Chi raised his brows and asked, “May I ask where Brother Xiao hails from?”

Yang Kai replied, “Southern Territory, Qingyang Shrine.”

“Someone from the Southern Territory?” Feng Chi couldn’t help but frown. Recently, many Emperor Realm cultivators had responded to Wenqing Sect’s Recruitment Order, but never had there been one from another territory. Yang Kai was the first.

He cast an inquiring look at Cao Xu, who quickly said, “Elder Feng, rest assured. Young Master Xiao’s identity has already been verified by Elder Sun. There’s no problem.”

Feng Chi nodded, “Elder Sun Ping is meticulous and cautious. If he says there’s no problem, then there’s definitely no problem. Brother Xiao, you’ve come at just the right time. I happen to be short on manpower here and need your help.”

Yang Kai flicked his folding fan and said, “Elder Feng, just give your orders. Since I’ve accepted the Recruitment Order, I will naturally obey.”

“Come over and take a look!” Feng Chi beckoned, pointing at the Formation Plate to signal Yang Kai.

Yang Kai quickly stepped forward a few paces, tucking his folding fan behind his neck. Focusing his attention, he gazed intently at the Formation Plate before him.

On that Formation Plate, the terrain of Ice Heart Valley was clearly outlined. Outside this outline, countless points of light flickered, forming an ironclad encirclement around Ice Heart Valley.

Although he didn’t fully understand, Yang Kai could still sense the perilous situation Ice Heart Valley was in.

“This Hooking Heaven Formation Plate was personally set up by Grandmaster Nanmen, just to make it convenient for me to oversee things here and strategize. It’s saved me a lot of trouble,” Feng Chi said, full of admiration for Grandmaster Nanmen.

Yang Kai also praised, “Grandmaster Nanmen truly is a master of formations. This is an impressive feat.”

Feng Chi smiled slightly, pointed to a spot, and said, “Young Master Xiao, I hope you can oversee this place and guard the formation flag set by Grandmaster Nanmen, in case Ice Heart Valley gets desperate and tries to cause trouble.”

“No problem, but is my only task to guard that formation flag?” Yang Kai looked up and asked.

Feng Chi said solemnly, “This matter is of great importance. If the formation flag is broken, the entire formation will be disrupted, giving Ice Heart Valley a chance to catch their breath. That’s why an Emperor must personally oversee it. But under normal circumstances, nothing should happen. Ice Heart Valley has been trapped until now without making a move. Still, Young Master Xiao, you must not be careless.”

“Understood.” Yang Kai nodded seriously.

Feng Chi nodded, “In addition, I will assign five hundred men to serve under you. When Grandmaster Nanmen is ready, you’ll need to lead them to activate the formation flag and coordinate with the other flags. We’ll be able to break through Ice Heart Valley’s Grand Guardian Array in one fell swoop. Then we’ll charge straight in and flatten Ice Heart Valley.”

Yang Kai cupped his fists, “I will not fail Elder Feng’s trust.”

“Good, good, good.” Feng Chi was very satisfied with Yang Kai’s attitude, nodding repeatedly. “Cao Xu, you’re familiar with the terrain nearby, so take Young Master Xiao over. The five hundred men will arrive shortly.”

“Yes!” Cao Xu accepted the order and immediately led Yang Kai out.

Flying all the way, the two soon arrived at the location designated by Feng Chi.

Looking around, there were fewer than a hundred people guarding a massive formation flag. The flag was set up in the void and concealed, but when Yang Kai swept his Divine Sense over, he could still detect some clues.

As soon as Cao Xu and Yang Kai arrived, the hundred people below were immediately alerted and all looked up.

Cao Xu glanced around and shouted, “Who’s in charge here?”

A middle-aged man stepped out and cupped his fists, “Senior Brother Cao, it’s me!”

Cao Xu looked at him and nodded, “So it’s Junior Brother Huo Han.”

He clearly recognized this Wenqing Sect disciple.

“Senior Brother Cao, this is…?” Huo Han looked curiously at Yang Kai, seeking confirmation.

Cao Xu announced loudly, “This is the core disciple of Qingyang Shrine in the Southern Territory, Xiao Baiyi. Young Master Xiao has accepted our Wenqing Sect’s Recruitment Order and, by Elder Feng’s command, will be stationed here from today. I hope you all obey his orders.”

Hearing that Yang Kai was from Qingyang Shrine in the Southern Territory, everyone below showed great curiosity, but no one dared to speak out of turn. They all cupped their fists and agreed.

“Additionally, Elder Feng will send another four hundred men. They will also be under Young Master Xiao’s command. Once Ice Heart Valley is destroyed, rewards will be distributed according to merit!”

At the mention of rewards, many of those below became excited, but the Wenqing Sect disciples like Huo Han remained calm. They knew that if Ice Heart Valley was really taken, most of the benefits would go to their sect, and only scraps would be given to outsiders.

“Young Master Xiao, I’ll head back now. I’ll leave this place to you.” Cao Xu cupped his fists to Yang Kai.

“Mm.” Yang Kai nodded indifferently, displaying the demeanor of an Emperor Realm expert.

Cao Xu didn’t say anything more and turned to leave.

After he left, Yang Kai swept his gaze over the hundred people below and said calmly, “You all know my background and my task. In the days to come, I’ll need your full cooperation. If anything goes wrong here, none of us can bear the responsibility.”

Huo Han cupped his fists, “Young Master Xiao is absolutely right. Since it’s Elder Feng’s order, we will naturally comply.”

Yang Kai nodded, “Very good. Now, listen to my command!”

At these words, everyone’s expression became serious.

Yang Kai said unhurriedly, “Go find some trees and build a wooden house for me here!” He pointed to a flat spot.

The hundred people below were all stunned. Seeing Yang Kai’s serious demeanor, they hadn’t known what to expect, but now that he wanted a wooden house built, their expressions turned odd, especially the Wenqing Sect disciples, who looked particularly displeased.

They were disciples of Wenqing Sect—when had they ever been ordered around like this? Besides, Yang Kai was just an outsider Emperor. What right did he have to be so arrogant?

“What? Did I not make myself clear, or are you all deaf?” Yang Kai snorted coldly, releasing his Imperial Might. Instantly, the hundred people’s faces became strained.

Someone immediately cupped his fists, “We will obey Young Master Xiao’s order!”

As soon as he finished speaking, he rushed out. This person wasn’t a Wenqing Sect disciple, but someone who had come to serve after accepting the Recruitment Order. He didn’t have the guts to challenge Yang Kai.

There were quite a few like him. Over ninety people left at once, leaving only Huo Han and five or six others behind.

These five or six were clearly Wenqing Sect disciples, relying on their status and unwilling to submit to Yang Kai’s authority.

Yang Kai snorted again, Emperor Yuan surging as he increased the pressure, saying coldly, “Those who don’t obey will not be shown mercy. Let’s see how long you can last!”

Huo Han’s face was strained and flushed, “You’re abusing your power for personal gain. I’m going to report this to Elder Feng!”

“Report?” Yang Kai sneered, “Since Elder Feng entrusted me with this place, everything here is under my command. The very first order I give, you dare to defy? Even if I kill you as a warning, Elder Feng won’t blame me!”

“You wouldn’t dare!” Huo Han shouted angrily.

“Try me!” Yang Kai raised his brows, increasing the pressure even more.

Pu pu pu pu…

The remaining Wenqing Sect disciples couldn’t withstand it. Blood spurted from their mouths, and their bones creaked as if they might snap at any moment.

This startled Huo Han and the others. They had thought their status as Wenqing Sect disciples would earn them some face, but who knew Yang Kai would be so ruthless, making an example of them right away.

But as Yang Kai said, even if this matter reached Elder Feng, he wouldn’t openly side with them. There were many Emperor Realm cultivators who had accepted the Recruitment Order. If the Wenqing Sect disciples under their command were all this unruly, how could they guard so many formation flags?

Wenqing Sect needed to rely on the strength of these Emperor Realm cultivators and wouldn’t make things difficult for them.

Not only would Elder Feng not help them, he might even punish them to appease the others.

At most, after the battle was over, they might settle accounts with Yang Kai.

“Young Master Xiao, please stop!” Huo Han’s body trembled, unable to withstand the Emperor Realm pressure, his body sinking lower as if he might kneel at any moment. At this point, he no longer dared to resist, and a trace of panic finally appeared on his face.

If he really was forced to kneel, he’d lose all face.

“So, will you obey orders?” Yang Kai asked coldly.

“Yes, yes, yes, we will obey!” Huo Han and the others answered repeatedly.

“What a bunch of wretches! You only behave after suffering a little!” Yang Kai snorted and withdrew his pressure.

Huo Han and the others immediately collapsed to the ground, drenched in sweat.

“Get moving! If the wooden house isn’t finished within an hour, you’re all dead.” Yang Kai shouted.

Huo Han and the others scrambled up and rushed out.

The efficiency of over a hundred cultivators was no joke. In less than an hour—just over half an hour—a fine little wooden house was built on the flat ground.

Yang Kai was very satisfied after inspecting it. He praised the crowd a few times, then strolled inside, closed the door, and began meditating.

However, after today’s events, most of the hundred or so people didn’t have a good impression of Yang Kai, especially Huo Han and the others, who glared at the wooden house with resentment, plotting to have a Sect Elder avenge them once Yang Kai was no longer useful.

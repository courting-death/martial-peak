## Chapter 2640 — Nanmen Dajun

“The matter of your fierce fiancée has been spreading like wildfire these past few days. The whole of Binglun City knows about it. Just a little inquiry and anyone would know. If not for that, I wouldn’t have known the name you’re using is Xiao Baiyi. Don’t worry, my background is fabricated perfectly as well—Wenqing Sect won’t be able to see through it.” Ji Yao quickly replied, then shouted furiously, “Xiao Baiyi! Do you have any conscience left? If I hadn’t taken care of you back then, you would’ve been fed to wild beasts in the wilderness long ago! And now, after taking all the advantage, you want to abandon me!”

“Taking advantage!” The ears of all five hundred cultivators perked up at once, each one regarding Yang Kai as a man among men.

You really had to admire him—this woman was so ugly no one would take her even if she threw herself at them, yet Yang Kai actually had the nerve to take advantage of her. His tastes… were something else.

The commotion here not only entertained the five hundred cultivators nearby, but even drew the attention of those guarding other formation flags in the distance. For a moment, many people looked over in this direction.

With just a little inquiry, everyone knew that the fierce fiancée of someone named Xiao Baiyi had come looking for him.

The story of Xiao Baiyi’s fierce and hideous fiancée had already spread these past few days, so everyone watched the spectacle with glee, wanting to see how Yang Kai would handle it.

“Taking advantage…” Yang Kai broke out in a cold sweat, looking extremely guilty and uneasy.

Seeing his expression, everyone believed it even more, and their admiration grew.

“You came to Wenqing Sect to accept the Recruitment Order—don’t think I don’t know. Isn’t it just because you saw the Ice Heart Valley disciples are fair-skinned and beautiful and wanted to take a few back with you? Let me tell you, you’re dreaming! If you dare take one, I’ll kill one. If you dare take two, I’ll kill both! I’ll even cut off your manhood!”

The five hundred cultivators burst into laughter. Although Ji Yao’s current appearance was truly hard to praise, her words were explosive enough. She was indeed fierce—what ordinary woman would dare say such things?

Especially Huo Han, who was laughing so hard at the side he could barely stand straight, wishing for Yang Kai to be as unlucky as possible.

"Enough, enough…" Yang Kai quickly sent a voice transmission. Others didn’t know what Ji Yao looked like, but he did. For such a beautiful and aloof woman to speak like this, it felt out of character. He sighed inwardly and continued, "Fine, since we’ve made a scene, just cooperate with me. Find a chance to fight me in a bit!"

Ji Yao’s heart stirred. She quickly replied, “Alright!”

“So vulgar!” Yang Kai cried out in anguish, pointing at Ji Yao and repeatedly jabbing his finger, looking utterly miserable. “I, Xiao Baiyi, have always been elegant—how did I end up with such a vulgar woman! Heaven is blind, truly blind!”

Ji Yao shouted in fury, “You still dare to despise this Young Lady!”

As she spoke, her figure flickered and she rushed over, waving her hands and scratching at Yang Kai while cursing non-stop. The crowd watched, all looking terrified.

Yang Kai tried to fend her off left and right, but was clearly outmatched. In no time, his clothes were torn to shreds by Ji Yao, leaving him in a sorry state.

“Hiss… This woman is actually an Emperor?”

“Lord Xiao is an Emperor, but he’s completely outmatched. Clearly, this woman’s cultivation is even higher than his.”

“I heard Lord Xiao is from Qingyang Shrine in the Southern Territory. Where is this woman from?”

Earlier, everyone had just been watching the fun and hadn’t paid much attention to Ji Yao, but now that she made a move, her Emperor Realm cultivation was exposed, leaving everyone in awe!

A fierce fiancée isn’t scary—what’s scary is a fierce fiancée with real strength.

“Enough!” Yang Kai shouted angrily, surging his Emperor Yuan and shaking Ji Yao off. His eyes flashed with menace as he said, “Stop laying your hands on me, or don’t blame me for being rude!”

Ji Yao was stunned for a moment, not expecting Yang Kai to actually fight back, but soon she screamed hysterically, “Xiao Baiyi, you dare strike me? This Young Lady will fight you to the end!”

As she spoke, she flipped her hand, summoning a long sword with a clang, and swept it at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai couldn’t dodge in time, and his clothes were slashed open with a loud rip—he nearly had his belly cut open right then and there.

“You wretch, courting death!” Yang Kai roared in fury, clapping his hands together and pushing out. Emperor Yuan surged, and a massive palm print struck toward Ji Yao.

Boom, boom, boom…

Emperor Realm cultivators clashed, their momentum extraordinary, causing the faces of many cultivators to change. Yet Ji Yao, at the center of it all, lightly broke Yang Kai’s killing move with a single sword, then pressed forward, sword flowers blooming and enveloping Yang Kai.

“They’re really fighting, really fighting…”

“Is this a battle to the death?”

“How did an engaged couple end up fighting like this? Lord Xiao’s temper isn’t exactly mild, but that woman is even more unreasonable.”

Earlier, the five hundred cultivators had been enjoying the show, but now that Yang Kai and Ji Yao were truly fighting, they all grew uneasy, unsure if they’d get caught up in it.

Huo Han, however, was watching with delight, secretly cheering Ji Yao on, wishing she’d stab Yang Kai to death with a single sword.

Cao Xu, on the other hand, was drenched in cold sweat, hesitated for a moment, then hurried toward where Feng Chi was. Now that two Emperor Realm cultivators were fighting, it was beyond his ability to intervene. He could only report to Feng Chi and let him decide how to handle it.

In the sky, Yang Kai and Ji Yao fought fiercely, as if they had a deep-seated grudge. As they fought, they hurled insults at each other.

Thunder rumbled as the two clashed fiercely, their battle raging from one place to another, each exchange more intense than the last.

Many Emperor Realm cultivators below looked up, but since they had nothing to do with Yang Kai or Ji Yao, they naturally didn’t interfere. A little inquiry told them what was going on, and they couldn’t help but laugh.

After fighting for a while, the battlefield had already shifted. Just then, a voice called out from below, “Young Master Xiao, what are you doing up there?”

Amidst the chaos, Yang Kai glanced down and saw Feng Chi hurrying over, his face full of anger.

Yang Kai ignored him, continuing to fight Ji Yao while moving in a certain direction.

“Nanmen Dajun is over there?” Ji Yao caught on, asking while unleashing her attacks.

“Yes. I’ve snuck out a few times these past days. I didn’t dare investigate too deeply, but Nanmen Dajun is definitely over there. I’m not sure of the exact spot, so let’s keep an eye out!”

“What does he look like?” Ji Yao asked.

“Never seen him, don’t know him. But he’s a formation grandmaster—since he’s in charge of breaking the formation, there must be experts protecting him. Just look for a spot where several Emperors are gathered.”

“Alright!” Ji Yao replied, releasing her Divine Sense to search the surroundings.

Receiving no response from Yang Kai, a frosty chill crept across Feng Chi’s face.

“Elder Feng, that’s where Grandmaster Nanmen is now. We can’t let them get any closer, or they’ll interfere with Grandmaster Nanmen.” Cao Xu, seeing the direction Yang Kai and Ji Yao were moving, quickly reminded him.

“You don’t need to tell me. But now that those two are fighting for real, they won’t stop until one of them is dead. If I’d known it would come to this, I never would’ve let you bring that woman over.” Feng Chi said gloomily, annoyed.

Cao Xu smiled bitterly, “Who knew that woman would be so unreasonable.”

“There’s no time to lose—stop them at once!” Feng Chi said, then immediately flew toward Yang Kai and Ji Yao.

“Over there!” At that moment, Yang Kai’s expression changed. He secretly signaled Ji Yao, “There’s one Second Stage Emperor Realm and two First Stage Emperor Realm cultivators over there. They must be the ones protecting Nanmen Dajun!”

The two exchanged a glance and rushed over, still attacking each other fiercely.

In no time, they neared the spot they’d investigated earlier.

With a sweep of his eyes, Yang Kai saw a burly middle-aged man setting up formation flags, surrounded by three Emperor Realm cultivators—one Second Stage and two First Stage.

“Nanmen Dajun!” Yang Kai’s eyes lit up, fixing on the burly man arranging the formation.

“Who dares act so wildly here? Leave at once!” The Second Stage Emperor among the three looked up and shouted coldly.

“Do it!” Yang Kai growled to Ji Yao.

Ji Yao seized the moment, striking Yang Kai’s chest with a fierce palm. The force changed the very wind and clouds, landing squarely on his chest.

With a cry, Yang Kai spat out a mouthful of blood, his body plummeting like a meteor.

The direction he fell was straight toward where Nanmen Dajun was.

At the same time, a fierce light flashed in Ji Yao’s eyes as she shouted, “Xiao Baiyi, today will be your grave!”

Shouting and making a scene, she flew down as if determined to finish him off.

Ji Yao landed just a hair’s breadth after Yang Kai, but as they neared the others, the two suddenly turned and lunged forward together.

Ji Yao no longer held back, sweeping out with her sword as the world hummed.

“Second Stage Emperor Realm!” The three guarding Nanmen Dajun changed expressions. As they shouted, the Second Stage Emperor Realm expert moved to intercept Ji Yao.

Yang Kai, meanwhile, ignored everything else and charged straight at Nanmen Dajun, his expression cold and his qi and blood surging—showing no sign of injury at all.

Although Nanmen Dajun was a formation grandmaster, his own cultivation wasn’t weak—he was also a First Stage Emperor Realm expert.

Seeing Yang Kai charging at him so fiercely, Nanmen Dajun was startled but not flustered. He simply retreated a few steps and watched coldly.

“How dare you, little thief, threaten Grandmaster Nanmen!” One of the First Stage Emperor Realm cultivators shouted, stepping forward to meet him.

“Out of my way!” Yang Kai roared, flicking his finger as a Moon Blade slashed forward.

The First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator snorted coldly as the Moon Blade approached, surging his Emperor Yuan to defend. But he didn’t know the Moon Blade’s strangeness and lethality—his Emperor Yuan collapsed the moment it touched the Moon Blade, scaring him out of his wits. He hurriedly summoned a Secret Treasure to block in front of him.

But he’d missed his chance. Yang Kai was already upon him, slamming a palm down on his head.

With a bang, the First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator didn’t even have time to react before his head was smashed, brains splattering everywhere.

## Chapter 2788 — Mouse Droppings

"Brother Yang!" In the Ziyuan Commerce Guild branch, within a fine private room, Kang Siran entered with a glowing face, warmly cupping his fists in greeting.

Yang Kai rose and returned the gesture. "Brother Kang!"

"Sit and talk, we're not outsiders." Kang Siran motioned, then glanced back. Immediately, a maid brought in excellent fragrant tea.

After closing the door, Kang Siran said, "Brother Yang, are you here to pick up the goods?"

"That's right. The two-month period is nearly up. How are preparations on your end?"

Kang Siran smiled. "Brother Yang's business is a big deal. President Lou has been working hard, and both the Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade are ready." As he spoke, he took out a Spatial Ring and placed it on the table, pushing it toward Yang Kai.

"This is goods worth forty million Upper-Level Source Crystals. Please check it, Brother Yang."

"No need." Yang Kai shook his head. If he were doing business with someone else, he might inspect the goods to see if everything was in order, but since it was Kang Siran, there was no need.

Kang Siran would never cheat him.

In fact, gathering Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade worth forty million Upper-Level Source Crystals within two months from the various branches in the Southern Territory took quite some effort. It consumed a lot of manpower and resources. Even with Lou Chi's full support, Kang Siran's supervision and urging were indispensable. In the end, they managed to gather everything, with a bit extra, which Kang Siran decided to gift to Yang Kai.

Compared to this huge transaction, the extra goods were hardly worth mentioning.

"How much should I pay?" Yang Kai asked.

"Brother Yang previously paid a deposit of ten million, plus two Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores worth thirteen million, so you still need to pay seventeen million Upper-Level Source Crystals," Kang Siran quoted. "Also, Vice President Lou said that if you don't want to pay in Source Crystals, you can use Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores instead."

Yang Kai grinned. "President Lou is quite the schemer. I'll pay in Source Crystals."

Although he had plenty of Inner Cores, he couldn't just hand them out. If too many Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores circulated in the market, it would affect their price. Yang Kai certainly wouldn't dig his own grave.

As for Source Crystals, he had paid off Hou Yu's seventy million gambling debt, so seventeen million was nothing.

Soon, the payment was settled. Kang Siran produced the paperwork, and the two placed their Soul Brands, marking the successful completion of the transaction.

Yang Kai said, "Brother Kang, please continue to help me acquire Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade. If I don't come to you, just send them to Qingyang Shrine."

"No problem." Kang Siran nodded. "By the way, congratulations to Brother Yang for being promoted to Elder of Qingyang Shrine."

"Just a Guest Elder," Yang Kai shook his head.

Kang Siran said, "The Commerce Guild received an invitation last time, but I was busy with the Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade, so I couldn't attend to offer congratulations. I'm ashamed!"

"Brother Kang, you're being too formal. Let's not mention it." Yang Kai smiled slightly.

With the transaction complete, the two chatted casually for a while.

When Yang Kai asked about Kang Siran’s recent situation, he learned that Kang Siran was thriving in the Fenglin City branch, like a fish in water. Originally, with only a First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation, he would never have been able to secure the manager’s position here.

But because he was very familiar with Fenglin City, he was specially valued this time. However, before Yang Kai's business, his tenure as manager was nearly over, and he would soon be transferred from this wealthy place, possibly to some remote area.

Yet, Yang Kai's huge transaction turned his fortunes around, earning Lou Chi's appreciation.

A single transaction worth forty million—who else could pull that off?

Not to mention, Yang Kai promised to bring the Ziyuan Commerce Guild two Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast Inner Cores every half year.

This was a long-term business deal. With this alone, Kang Siran's position as manager was irreplaceable, since Yang Kai made the agreement for Kang Siran's sake.

Now, among the managers, Lou Chi regarded Kang Siran differently. Many major clients specifically requested him, especially after Gu Hong left Fenglin City. All of Gu Hong's clients were handed over to Kang Siran.

Kang Siran was now clearly the top manager of the Ziyuan Commerce Guild branch.

"Gu Hong left Fenglin City?" Yang Kai was surprised, realizing why he hadn't seen him on this trip.

Kang Siran grinned from ear to ear. "That's right. After your last visit, Brother Yang, Gu Hong himself requested a transfer out of Fenglin City. President Lou had no reason to refuse, so he waved his hand and approved it immediately." Kang Siran's smile was radiant—he and Gu Hong had fiercely competed for the manager's position, and victory once seemed out of reach. Yet, in the end, it was Kang Siran who had the last laugh, while Gu Hong, who once held the upper hand, chose to leave of his own accord.

"Probably felt there was no point in staying. Thanks to you, Brother Yang." Kang Siran kept thanking him, feeling it was all Yang Kai's doing.

Yang Kai listened intently, feeling as though he had grasped something important. He pressed further, “How was Gu Hong’s condition when he left?”

Kang Siran wasn’t sure why Yang Kai was asking, but he thought for a moment and replied, “He left in a hurry… And he seemed restless, like a disaster was about to strike at any moment.”

"That scoundrel!" Yang Kai slapped his thigh and snorted coldly.

"What's wrong?" Kang Siran was startled.

Yang Kai sneered, "I understand why he suddenly requested a transfer from Fenglin City... He's afraid I'll settle accounts with him."

Kang Siran asked suspiciously, "Did Gu Hong offend you, Brother Yang?"

Yang Kai said, "What kind of offense is it to sell client information in the Commerce Guild?"

Kang Siran was shocked and grew solemn. "That's a serious crime. If proven, immediate execution! What do you mean, Brother Yang?"

Yang Kai said, "After the last auction in Fenglin City, I was intercepted outside the city by Le Dongzheng, the Sect Master of Duanyue Sect. I wondered how he knew my movements so precisely. Now it seems someone was playing tricks!"

"What? Le Dongzheng was killed by you, Brother Yang?" Kang Siran was startled.

Kang Siran had heard about Le Dongzheng’s death. After all, Le Dongzheng was the Sect Master of Duanyue Sect and a major client of the Ziyuan Commerce Guild, so Kang Siran naturally paid attention—especially since he had taken over Gu Hong’s clients.

After checking the timing, Kang Siran learned Le Dongzheng died after that auction.

Few knew about this. Kang Siran didn't know who killed Le Dongzheng, but now realized it was Yang Kai.

Though his cultivation wasn't high, as a manager of the Ziyuan Commerce Guild, Kang Siran was shrewd. From Yang Kai's words, he quickly deduced the truth.

Clearly, after the auction, Gu Hong leaked Yang Kai's information to Le Dongzheng, who then tried to ambush him, but was killed instead.

Kang Siran knew Yang Kai had a grudge with Le Dongzheng.

Gu Hong's motive against Yang Kai was even simpler. He competed with Kang Siran for the manager position, and seeing Kang Siran's position secured thanks to Yang Kai, he grew desperate.

Realizing this, Kang Siran's face darkened. "This is serious, Brother Yang. Please wait a moment, I need to report to President Lou."

Yang Kai said, "Forget it. Le Dongzheng is dead, Gu Hong transferred out. Let's leave it at that. No need to trouble President Lou."

A mere Gu Hong meant nothing to him, so there was no need to make a fuss.

Kang Siran said, "You don't understand, Brother Yang. The Commerce Guild values reputation above all. Without it, the Guild is finished. Gu Hong dared to leak client information—this can't be tolerated. If he's let off, who knows what trouble he'll cause next time."

He brought up the Guild's reputation, so Yang Kai couldn't object further. "But there's no solid evidence..."

Kang Siran said, "No evidence needed. It's obvious. President Lou will see it."

With that, he walked out.

It was clear Kang Siran wasn't seeking revenge. Though his competition with Gu Hong had been unpleasant, Kang Siran wasn't petty. His actions were genuinely for the Ziyuan Commerce Guild's sake.

A single mouse dropping can spoil a whole pot of porridge.

In no time, Kang Siran returned. As for Gu Hong's fate and Lou Chi's decision, Kang Siran didn't say, and Yang Kai didn't ask. Kang Siran simply conveyed Lou Chi's apology and said that in the future, anything Yang Kai wanted to buy from the Commerce Guild would be at a ten percent discount.

Clearly, this was Lou Chi's hush money.

Gu Hong's actions were a disgrace to the Commerce Guild and couldn't be spread. The ten percent discount was generous hush money, especially given the scale of Yang Kai's business with the Ziyuan Commerce Guild.

After chatting a bit more, Yang Kai took his leave.

He went straight to Linghu City.

On the way, he took out his Elder Jade Token and sent a message to Xia Sheng. When Yang Kai arrived at Linghu Palace, Xia Sheng was already waiting with a smile, waving enthusiastically.

Yang Kai walked over, and Xia Sheng immediately put his arm around his shoulder, laughing, "Congratulations, Junior Brother Yang, on becoming an Elder of our Shrine."

Yang Kai said, "Senior Brother, don't tease me."

"How is it teasing? It's a great event for you and the Shrine." Xia Sheng patted his shoulder and whispered, "I heard you've witnessed two of the Shrine's three great taboos?"

If Xia Sheng hadn't brought it up, it would have been fine, but as soon as he did, Yang Kai couldn't help but recall Gao Xueting's heroic figure battling the crowd in a drunken state, and his mouth twitched.

Xia Sheng laughed heartily. "You'll get used to it, you'll get used to it."

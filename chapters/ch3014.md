## Chapter 3014 — Are You Sick?

At the rear mountain of Li Dragon Palace, the scenery was beautiful, with birdsong and fragrant flowers filling the air.

Li Jiao strolled leisurely through a flower garden, completely at ease. Beside him was a young married woman in palace dress. Their fingers were intertwined, and they appeared exceedingly intimate. The young woman leaned gently against Li Jiao, a contented smile on her face.

The young woman was dressed in red. Her face was like a peach blossom, and her fair, tender skin seemed delicate enough to break with a puff of breath. An irrepressible allure lingered between her brows, while her limpid, beautiful eyes seemed almost to brim with water.

Dragons were lustful by nature. Although Li Jiao was not a pure-blooded member of the Dragon Clan, he possessed the Dragon Clan's bloodline, so his appetite for relations between men and women was extremely strong. As a result, he had quite a few wives and concubines in Li Dragon Palace.

Despite having so many wives and concubines, the one Li Jiao doted on most was this Madam Hong beside him.

Whenever he had free time, he would engage in the most intimate of acts with Madam Hong. Madam Hong also knew that her lifelong happiness depended on Li Jiao, so she naturally treated him with boundless tenderness and served him with all her heart.

It could be said that among Li Jiao's many wives and concubines, no one could compete with Madam Hong for his favor.

It had been so in the past, and was even more so now.

Ever since Li Jiao returned to Li Dragon Palace two months ago, Madam Hong had felt that he doted on her even more. Although Li Jiao had treated her well before, the change this time was particularly obvious.

For two whole months, Li Jiao had not left her side. He spent every day with her, clearly showing how important she was in his heart.

Madam Hong had also keenly sensed the change in Li Jiao. Clever as she was, she did not ask him about it and simply did everything she could to display her gentleness and charm, hoping to bind both his heart and body to her.

"Palace Master, that flower is so beautiful." Madam Hong suddenly pointed to a large crimson flower, her face filled with delight.

"Do you like it?" Li Jiao's tone was also extremely gentle as he gazed at her with a smile.

"Mm, I do." Madam Hong nodded gently, with the meek charm of a little bird nestling close.

Li Jiao smiled faintly and beckoned with his hand, plucking the flower into his grasp. He then gestured to Madam Hong.

Madam Hong glanced at him joyfully and lowered her head slightly, allowing Li Jiao to place the flower in her hair. When she raised her head again, she tilted it from side to side and asked with pursed lips and a smile, "Does it look good?"

Li Jiao reached out and pinched her tender cheek, nodding repeatedly. "Beautiful, beautiful. You're even more beautiful than the flower."

Hearing him say this, Madam Hong felt as though honey had been poured into her heart, and her whole body seemed to float. She pouted coyly, “Palace Master, you’re always teasing me.”

Palace Master truly had changed greatly. She did not know what he had experienced outside, but in any case, he had treated her even better and more gently since returning. In the past, he would still enter retreat to cultivate, but over the past two months, she had not seen the slightest sign that Li Jiao intended to cultivate. He spent every day taking her sightseeing, making all her other sisters both envious and jealous. They all said she had stolen all of Palace Master's favor for herself.

"Hong'er, would you like this Lord to stay by your side like this from now on?" Li Jiao asked softly. His eyes gazed at her, filled entirely with her reflection and nothing else.

Madam Hong's tender body trembled. As their eyes met, her own reddened slightly and she said with deep emotion, "Hong'er cannot hold Palace Master back like this. Palace Master still needs to cultivate."

"What is there to cultivate?" Li Jiao scoffed. "This Lord's cultivation has already reached its limit. I won't cultivate anymore. From now on, I'll accompany you every day to admire the flowers and gaze upon the sea, until the end of time."

These words pierced Madam Hong's heart like a sharp sword, moving her so deeply that she could no longer hold back her tears.

"Why are you crying?" Li Jiao raised his hand and wiped away the strings of pearl-like tears, chuckling. "They say women are made of water. It seems that isn't false in the slightest."

"Palace Master treats Hong'er too well. Hong'er is so happy." Madam Hong nestled gently against Li Jiao's chest and soon soaked his robes with her tears.

Although Li Jiao had treated her well in the past, it had never been to this extent. She immediately felt that she had entrusted herself to the right man and would have no regrets in this life.

"This is nothing. We still have a long future ahead of us." Li Jiao smiled faintly and gently patted Madam Hong's back in a steady rhythm.

Madam Hong nestled deeper into Li Jiao's embrace, speaking in a soft and delicate voice, "Palace Master, I... I want..."

Li Jiao immediately perked up upon hearing this and raised his brow. "What do you want?"

Madam Hong pouted and struck him with her dainty fist, acting spoiled as she said, "Palace Master is so wicked."

Li Jiao burst into laughter, but as he laughed, his mirth gradually faded. His face sank, and he turned his head and barked in a low voice, "Who goes there?"

Following his gaze, he saw that at some unknown point, a person had appeared atop the hill nearby. That person was quietly squatting there, watching the tender affection between him and Madam Hong with great interest.

After seeing the man's face clearly, Li Jiao nearly died of fright and cried out, "You..."

"Yo!" Yang Kai greeted them with a cheerful laugh, raising his hand in salute. "Brother Li, it's been two months since we last met, and you haven't lost any of your refinement, I see."

"Ah!" Madam Hong jumped in fright and hurriedly sprang out of Li Jiao's embrace. She looked toward the source of the voice, her face turning bright red.

That man must have heard what she had just said. Thinking of how wantonly she had behaved, Madam Hong wished she could crawl into a hole. Stamping her foot in embarrassed fury, she demanded, "Who are you? How can you be so lacking in manners?"

The moment she finished speaking, Li Jiao pulled her behind him. Madam Hong had never seen Yang Kai and did not recognize him, but Li Jiao certainly did.

"Palace Master Yang..." Li Jiao was on the verge of tears. "When did you arrive? You could have at least said something."

If there was anyone Li Jiao least wanted to see in this life, it was Yang Kai. Even the Dragon Clan had to stand aside.

This man was simply the nightmare in Li Jiao's heart, his natural nemesis!

Whenever he became involved with Yang Kai in even the slightest way, nothing good ever happened to him. His experiences some time ago were the best proof of that. It was simply a memory too painful to recall.

Yang Kai chuckled. “I’ve been here for a little while already, but I was too embarrassed to interrupt. I hope Brother Li won’t take offense!”

Too embarrassed to interrupt!

Yet you had no qualms about watching the show!

Li Jiao grumbled endlessly in his heart, but did not dare show the slightest trace of it on his face. He merely watched Yang Kai vigilantly and asked, “Palace Master Yang… to what do I owe the honor of your visit?”

"You two carry on. I'm in no hurry. We can talk after you're finished."

Li Jiao's face went black. You have completely ruined the mood, so how are we supposed to carry on? Filled with resentment, he said, "Palace Master Yang may as well speak plainly. Mm, but let us agree on one thing first. If you've come to collect a debt, then forget it."

"I'm not here to collect a debt. How could it be about collecting a debt?" Yang Kai waved his hand and rose from the hill. His expression turned solemn as he said in a deep voice, "I've only come to ask Brother Li for a favor!"

Gulp! Li Jiao could not help swallowing hard.

These words... Why did they sound so familiar?

When Yang Kai last came to Li Dragon Palace, he seemed to have said the same thing. Then Yang Kai had taken Li Jiao to the Frozen Earth, where he entered the Revolving World and finally ended up stranded in the Western Territory. He had narrowly escaped death and almost failed to return. That experience could truly be described as rich and eventful.

Li Jiao had lived for many years and weathered countless great storms, but how could all his experiences over those innumerable years compare to how colourful and thrilling that last journey had been?

And now Yang Kai had come again? Would this ever end? Wasn't it just that he owed Yang Kai some Source Crystals? Did Yang Kai have to bully him like this? Li Jiao wished he could fight Yang Kai to the death right now. Even dying would be better than suffering such torment at his hands.

However, the thought merely flashed through his mind. He knew he was definitely no match for Yang Kai.

There was no overcoming the suppression of his bloodline.

Li Jiao had not even thought of asking for details before directly declining. "This Li is but a man of humble station and modest cultivation. I'm afraid I have little to offer Palace Master Yang."

He absolutely, positively did not want to have even the tiniest connection with Yang Kai ever again.

He wanted to live his life in peace, passing his days without concern for the upheavals of the outside world or the level of his own cultivation, slowly growing old and dying together with the woman he doted upon.

Yang Kai said solemnly, "I need to go somewhere, so I require Brother Li's assistance."

Damn it, are you ignoring me? Did my words go in one ear and out the other?

Li Jiao was filled with grief and indignation. Gritting his teeth, he said, "Palace Master Yang, this Li has made himself very clear. I'm afraid I cannot help you, so I must ask Palace Master Yang to..."

"I want to make a trip to Dragon Isle!"

"Enough already!" Li Jiao finally could not bear it anymore and roared, glaring viciously at Yang Kai as though he wanted to devour him. However, his expression soon changed drastically, and he cried out, "You, you, you... What did you say? Where are you going?"

"Dragon Isle!"

"Hiss!" Li Jiao sucked in a sharp breath, looking as though he had seen a ghost in broad daylight.

After a long silence, Li Jiao finally asked cautiously, “Palace Master Yang… are you ill?”

“Do you have the cure?”

Li Jiao: ...

Taking a deep breath, Li Jiao said, "Palace Master Yang, don't you know what kind of place Dragon Isle is? Why would you want to go there?"

Yang Kai replied, "Zhu Qing didn't come back!"

Li Jiao immediately grinned when he heard this and blurted out, “So… you’ve been abandoned?”

"Bullshit!" Yang Kai flew into an embarrassed rage. "She has her own considerations."

Li Jiao had clearly understood something as well. Nodding, he said, "Young Lady Qing is a good person. Since she made this choice, she naturally did it for your sake. Why must Palace Master Yang go to Dragon Isle and seek trouble for himself?"

Yang Kai snorted. "It remains to be seen whose trouble it will be."

Li Jiao looked utterly speechless. "If you want to go to Dragon Isle, then just go on your own. Why have you come looking for me? How could this Li possibly help you?"

Yang Kai said, "You're a descendant of the Dragon Clan. Don't you know where Dragon Isle is?"

Li Jiao blinked. "How would I know where Dragon Isle is? Palace Master Yang's words are rather baffling."

"You truly don't know?" Yang Kai stared at him.

Li Jiao shook his head. "I truly don't."

Yang Kai nodded lightly. "In that case, I won't trouble you further. Madam, I beg your pardon for the intrusion."

As he spoke, he cupped his fists slightly toward Madam Hong, lightly pushed off with his feet, and shot straight into the sky.

He left? Just like that?

Li Jiao stared blankly at Yang Kai's departing figure, scarcely able to believe his eyes. He had thought Yang Kai would pester him unreasonably as he had last time, but who could have known he would be so easy to talk to on this visit?

"Was that... the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace?" The flush had yet to fade from Madam Hong's face as she gazed in the direction Yang Kai had departed. She had now realized who he was and could not help feeling a little frightened. Rumour had it that the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace was a demon who killed without batting an eye, and that he was the one who destroyed Wenqing Sect.

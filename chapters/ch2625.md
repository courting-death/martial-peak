## Chapter 2625 — Ice Heart Valley in Peril

In the sky, a group of three flew forward.

Perhaps it was out of disappointment at Shi Tianhe’s lack of resolve, but Ji Yao’s gaze toward her was filled with frustration. Having just suffered a great upheaval, Shi Tianhe had managed to preserve her life, but she was left lost and despondent, her expression clouded with sorrow. If not for a lingering wish that kept her going, she might have already lost the will to live.

"Master, you should have killed that man who played with her feelings and then abandoned her. Why did you spare his life?"

After flying for half a day, Ji Yao finally couldn't hold back and spoke, her words brimming with murderous intent, clearly still resentful that Dong Hai was wounded but not killed.

Yang Kai shook his head and said nothing.

"Master?" Shi Tianhe was startled by this, looking at Yang Kai in astonishment before cupping her fists and saying, "Tianhe greets this Senior. May I ask how to address you?"

She had been lost in her own thoughts until now, only just remembering to ask Yang Kai's identity. Moreover, hearing her Third Senior Aunt address him so, Shi Tianhe found it odd.

The Third Senior Aunt was a disciple of the Ice Heart Valley. Her master should be the founder of the Ice Heart Valley, so why was she calling a man 'Master'?

"What Senior? This is the Ancestor! You really are blind," Ji Yao said through gritted teeth, seething with anger. Years ago, she was expelled from the Sect by Senior Sister for a man, and now, with the Ancestor right before her, Shi Tianhe still couldn't recognize him.

If that's not blindness, what is?

How could Senior Sister have accepted such a disciple?

"Ancestor..." Shi Tianhe was shocked, staring wide-eyed at Yang Kai, not knowing what kind of Ancestor he was supposed to be.

Yang Kai quietly sent a voice transmission: "Tianhe, your Third Senior Aunt has been wandering outside all these years and seems to have suffered some trauma—her mind isn’t quite right, and she’s mistaken me for Senior Bing Yun. I can’t bring myself to correct her, lest she has another episode. This time, I’m escorting her back to Ice Heart Valley. Once she sees Senior Bing Yun, she’ll understand. For now, just play along and don’t let anything slip."

Shi Tianhe was stunned by this. She never expected her seemingly normal Third Senior Aunt to have such a problem—mistaking a man for the Ancestor was no small matter...

Still, she was clever and quickly composed herself. She respectfully saluted, "Disciple was blind. Ancestor, please do not blame me."

"Hmph!" Ji Yao snorted coldly, clearly displeased with her.

Shi Tianhe gave a bitter smile, but her expression soon changed. "Ancestor, did you and Third Senior Aunt just return to the Northern Territory?"

Yang Kai asked in surprise, "How did you know?"

Shi Tianhe replied, "Then you don't know what's happened to the Sect?"

Ji Yao frowned, "What happened to the Sect?"

Shi Tianhe's face turned pale. "The Sect... has been surrounded. In the Northern Territory, many Senior Sisters who married out have been brutally killed, and all the Sect's external holdings have been destroyed. Now, Ice Heart Valley itself is besieged and in grave danger."

"What?"

Both Yang Kai and Ji Yao's faces changed dramatically at her words.

Ji Yao demanded angrily, "Who did this?"

"Wenqing Sect!" A flash of hatred appeared in Shi Tianhe's eyes.

"Wenqing Sect?" Ji Yao's pretty face darkened as she shouted, "How dare they! Why would they do this to my Ice Heart Valley?"

Yang Kai frowned, suddenly realizing this might have something to do with him.

With that thought, he said, "Tianhe, tell us everything that happened. We really don't know about this change in the Sect."

"Yes!" Shi Tianhe immediately gave a brief account of what had happened in the Northern Territory over the past few years.

A few years ago, when the Shattered Star Sea event in the Star Realm began, Wenqing Sect's Sect Master Feng Xuan suddenly led people to Ice Heart Valley. That day, Wenqing Sect's experts gathered in force, pressuring the Ice Heart Valley. No one knew what conflict broke out, but the two sides fought fiercely outside the Valley, both suffering heavy losses.

From then on, the Ice Heart Valley and Wenqing Sect became mortal enemies.

As time passed, the grudge between the two Sects deepened. Many disciples of Ice Heart Valley who had married out were hunted down and cruelly killed by Wenqing Sect, and the Valley's external holdings were also destroyed. Even forces associated with Ice Heart Valley were suppressed and persecuted.

Half a year ago, countless Wenqing Sect experts, led by Sect Master Feng Xuan, seized Binglun City and blockaded the area around the Ice Heart Valley, clearly intending to destroy the Valley in one fell swoop.

Binglun City was the closest city to Ice Heart Valley and had been its base since the Sect's founding, serving as a vital link to the outside world. Now that even Binglun City had fallen, Ice Heart Valley's situation was dire.

To this day, half a year has already passed.

Fortunately, Ice Heart Valley had a Grand Guardian Array, allowing it to hold out until now.

"Wenqing Sect and Ice Heart Valley are both first-tier Sects in the Northern Territory, with similar strength. If there wasn't an irreconcilable grudge, they wouldn't go to war. Tianhe, do you know the reason?" Yang Kai asked with a serious face.

Shi Tianhe replied, "No one knew at first, but later rumors spread that Wenqing Sect acted so ruthlessly because Feng Xuan's son, Feng Xi, was killed in the Shattered Star Sea."

Ji Yao sneered, "If he wasn't as skilled, then he deserved to die. Was it an Ice Heart Valley disciple who killed Feng Xi?"

Shi Tianhe shook her head. "No, it's said the killer was a man named Yang Kai! And that Yang Kai is closely connected to the Ancestor. Feng Xuan wanted to find Yang Kai's whereabouts, so he targeted the Ancestor. But the Ancestor wouldn't say, so Feng Xuan, enraged, started a war between the two Sects to avenge his son."

"Yang Kai..." Ji Yao frowned, feeling the name was somehow familiar.

Seeing her reaction, Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and quickly changed the subject: "Wenqing Sect is strong, but Feng Xuan is only at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. How could he force Ice Heart Valley into such a predicament?"

Shi Tianhe shook her head. "I don't know the details. I only know the Sect is in grave danger."

Yang Kai's eyes flashed as he suddenly asked, "You wanted to leave the Dong Family and Taiping City to return to the Sect and do your part?"

Shi Tianhe replied bitterly, "Yes! Though I was expelled by my master, I'm still a disciple of Ice Heart Valley. Now that the Sect is in trouble, how could I stand by? But the Dong Family wouldn't let me leave, because if my identity as an Ice Heart Valley disciple was exposed, it would implicate them. Right now, no one in the Northern Territory dares to be associated with Ice Heart Valley."

Ji Yao stared at her in a daze, then nodded. "It's admirable that you have this resolve."

Before, she had been disappointed in her Junior Nephew, thinking Shi Tianhe was hopeless—expelled from the Sect for a man, then cast out of her family by that same man a hundred years later, all for nothing.

But now, it seemed her Junior Nephew still had some redeeming qualities. At least, when the Sect was in trouble, she wanted to return and do her part. Even knowing the danger, she was willing to risk her life. Not everyone could do that.

Shi Tianhe shook her head. "The Sect raised and taught me. Tianhe was born an Ice Heart Valley disciple and will die as one!"

Ji Yao nodded. "Good. With such resolve, Senior Sister will surely take you back as her disciple!"

"Really?" Shi Tianhe looked at Ji Yao in surprise.

Ji Yao rarely smiled. "Don't worry. If she doesn't, I won't acknowledge her as Senior Sister."

Shi Tianhe was startled and quickly said, "No, no, Third Senior Aunt, please don't joke."

"Master, now that the Sect is in trouble, we must hurry back. Senior Sister can't hold on alone," Ji Yao said, turning to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai nodded. "Yes, but we need to deal with the tail behind us first. It seems someone is following us."

"Hm?" Ji Yao immediately turned to look behind, only to see a small black dot speeding toward them from the horizon, clearly coming for them.

Shi Tianhe's face changed. "This is bad. We must have exposed our identities in Taiping City and attracted attention."

"Tianhe, do you know who it is?" Yang Kai turned and asked calmly.

Shi Tianhe frowned. "If I'm not mistaken, it should be people from Thunderclap Pavilion."

"Thunderclap Pavilion..." Yang Kai's lips curled into a playful smile.

Shi Tianhe said, "Taiping City and Thunderclap Pavilion are closely connected, with a Space Array linking them. It's easy to travel between the two. Thunderclap Pavilion is now subordinate to Wenqing Sect. Since we've been exposed, Thunderclap Pavilion won't just stand by—they must want to capture us and take us to Wenqing Sect for a reward."

"We'll see if they have the ability," Yang Kai sneered. "What's the Pavilion Master's cultivation?"

"Thunderclap Pavilion's Pavilion Master, Bai Yu, is at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm!" Shi Tianhe said gravely. "They also have four First Stage Emperor Realm Elders, and if you add Taiping City's City Lord Yan Dong, that's five First Stage Emperor Realm cultivators. Third Senior Aunt, we should leave now."

"Leave? They're coming right to our door," Ji Yao glared at her.

Shi Tianhe was stunned, not knowing where her Third Senior Aunt's confidence came from. Remembering her aunt's mental state, she quickly turned to Yang Kai for his opinion.

Yang Kai smiled. "Relax! They're just a few clowns."

"Clowns..." Shi Tianhe was speechless. A Second Stage Emperor and five First Stage Emperors—how could they be clowns?

Maybe this man was as crazy as her Third Senior Aunt?

She worried anxiously, but seeing both Yang Kai and Ji Yao calm, she gradually settled down as well.

From the moment she decided to leave the Dong Family and return to the Sect, she never expected to survive.

Maybe... she wouldn't even make it back to the Sect before being killed. After all, Ice Heart Valley was completely surrounded, and as soon as she showed herself, she'd be attacked.

Having already put life and death aside, what was there left to fear in this world?

Her only wish was to return to her master's side before dying, to receive her master's understanding and forgiveness.

## Chapter 3121 — The Fish in the Moat Suffer

Great Wilderness Star Domain. What a familiar name.

Back when he, Chi Yue, and the others left Hengluo Star Domain and passed through that Starlight Corridor, they had crossed hands with several Void King Realm cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain. The fight caused the Starlight Corridor to collapse, scattering everyone in every direction. Even Liu Yan and Xiaoxiao had been swept away to parts unknown, and Yang Kai had spent many years searching before he finally found them.

The leader of that group was named Yin Lesheng. He was the Sect Master of Netherworld Sect in Great Wilderness Star Domain, and the overlord of Great Wilderness Star Domain as well!

Liu Qianyun, who had once shared life-and-death hardship with Yang Kai, was also from Great Wilderness Star Domain. However, these two had treated Yang Kai differently, and their final fates were different too.

Yin Lesheng had been lucky enough to escape disaster in the Shattered Star Sea, only to be killed by Yang Kai in the Wild Ancient Land of the Eastern Territory. Liu Qianyun, on the other hand, had entered Ice Heart Valley through Yang Kai's recommendation. The last time Yang Kai visited Ice Heart Valley, he had taken time to meet her and exchange a few words. He learned that she was doing quite well and was already preparing to break through to the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. With her aptitude and Ice Heart Valley's support, breaking through should not be difficult. Given time, she would surely become another Emperor Realm expert.

Hearing the words Great Wilderness Star Domain suddenly come from Ye Xijun's mouth, Yang Kai could not help feeling a little surprised.

On second thought, however, when he and Yin Lesheng's group entered the Star Realm back then, they had encountered each other on that Starlight Corridor. Presumably, the two Star Domains were right next to each other, separated by only a single World Barrier. Now that the World Barrier had weakened and collapsed, connecting the two Star Domains, cultivators from Great Wilderness Star Domain could naturally come to Hengluo Star Domain.

“Both sides are from Star Domains, so how are we no match for them?” Yang Kai asked.

Ye Xijun said, "They are led by a Sect called Netherworld Sect, and their side is united as one. Our side, meanwhile, fights separately, so we are indeed somewhat inferior. On top of that, as invaders, they can move with greater flexibility, which naturally gives them some advantages."

It really was Netherworld Sect. It seemed that even without Yin Lesheng, Netherworld Sect's position as overlord remained unshakable. They had actually managed to unite the strength of an entire Star Domain.

"In that case, the Warships outside Dark Star belong to Great Wilderness Star Domain?"

Ye Xijun nodded. "Correct. Dark Star is one of the most important battlefields, and Great Wilderness Star Domain has committed a large portion of its forces here specifically to deal with this Sect."

"That old fellow dared lie to me!" Yang Kai snorted coldly.

The others had no idea who had deceived him, and all of them looked somewhat surprised.

Ye Xijun continued, "What they were arguing over just now was whether to attack those Warships directly and destroy their high-end combat strength, or to divide and conquer by eliminating the enemies scattered across Dark Star. But since you have returned, there is probably nothing left to argue about."

"Mm." Yang Kai nodded. "Wait a moment."

He suddenly closed his eyes and slowly raised one hand. His wrist shook, then he struck and seized toward the void in a motion that seemed fast, yet was strangely slow.

Everyone in the hall felt their hearts clench, as if Yang Kai's gesture had caught hold of their own hearts, leaving them almost unable to breathe.

Yang Kai's outspread fingers abruptly closed into a fist.

"Done!" he said, opening his eyes. Only when he lowered his raised hand did that terrifying pressure vanish.

Everyone looked at one another, their faces full of doubt and confusion, not understanding what he meant.

Before long, however, violent booms and droning explosions rolled in from an extremely distant place. Everyone could not help turning their ears to listen, and several people who seemed to realize something hurried out of the hall and looked up toward the Starry Sky.

Somewhere in the Starry Sky, enormous flowers of flame were blooming one after another. Even in broad daylight, even from several thousand li away, they could be seen clearly. As each fiery blossom appeared, a fresh explosion rang out and reached their ears.

The place where those flowers of flame bloomed was exactly where Great Wilderness Star Domain's Warships had been moored.

When the people who had gone out returned to the hall and reported what they had seen, everyone was struck dumb.

They knew Yang Kai was Dark Star's Star Lord and could rule over everything on Dark Star, but Great Wilderness Star Domain's Warships had been moored in the Starry Sky. They had not landed on Dark Star's surface. Yet across such a vast distance, one casual movement from him had crushed those Warships to pieces. What kind of power was this?

Many Void King Realm experts had been stationed aboard those Warships as well. Most likely, not a single one had managed to escape, and all had died violent deaths!

For a time, everyone was greatly encouraged.

Ye Xijun's expression turned solemn, and she said, "Now that the disagreement is gone, there is no need for everyone to wait any longer, is there? If we do not strike now, when will we?"

With a rustling clatter, everyone rose and headed outside, leaving Lingxiao Sect through different Space Arrays to travel to every part of Dark Star. They would take advantage of their momentum to pursue the routed foe and wipe out the remaining evildoers of Great Wilderness Star Domain on Dark Star.

Yang Kai said, "I will go stretch my limbs as well."

Dark Star was his territory. How could he tolerate a few petty clowns committing evil here? Those people who had invaded from Great Wilderness Star Domain were like fleas jumping around on his body. Naturally, he had to catch them, yank out their intestines, loop them around their necks, and pull hard, giving himself some peace and quiet again.

Turning his head, he said, "Senior Sister, rest for a while."

Before Su Yan could object, his figure flickered and vanished.

Su Yan shook her head helplessly.

A figure suddenly rushed in from outside the hall and called out excitedly, "Where is my son?"

It was Dong Suzhu. Shocked by the news of Yang Kai's return, she had immediately rushed over, only to arrive one step too late after all.

"Slow down. What if you trip and fall? You are already old, so why are you still so reckless?" Fourth Master Yang's unhurried voice came from behind her, though he too was moving extremely quickly. In the blink of an eye, he stood shoulder to shoulder with Dong Suzhu, which showed that his current mood was not nearly as calm as he made it seem.

Husband and wife swept their eyes around. They did not see Yang Kai, but both of their gazes landed on Su Yan.

“Su Yan…” Dong Suzhu’s eyes reddened at once. Covering her lips with one hand as tears fell like rain, she asked in a trembling voice, “Is it really you, Su Yan?”

Su Yan stepped forward and gave a graceful bow. "Mother!"

Her clear, pleasant voice entered Fourth Master Yang and Dong Suzhu's ears like heavenly music. They felt there could be no sound in the world more beautiful than this.

"It really is Su Yan!" Fourth Master Yang gave the dazed Dong Suzhu a push. Sigh, women were women after all. The slightest matter left them flustered. He truly had worried himself sick for her all his life.

Only then did Dong Suzhu hurriedly reach out, take Su Yan's hand, and pull her into her arms.

Dong Suzhu was petite and delicate, while Su Yan was tall and slender, half a head taller than her. Once they embraced, it looked more as if Dong Suzhu had thrown herself into Su Yan's arms.

Su Yan smiled faintly, her gaze passing over Dong Suzhu's shoulder to Fourth Master Yang. "Father!"

Fourth Master Yang suppressed the excitement in his heart and nodded lightly. He felt as if a great stone that had weighed on his heart for twenty years had finally been set down.

Back then, after Su Yan went to investigate that Void Passage, she had vanished without explanation. Everyone had been terribly worried, and Dong Suzhu and Fourth Master Yang in particular had lost all appetite and sleep. They had always felt they had wronged Yang Kai, unable to even look after their own daughter-in-law properly, and secretly hated that their cultivations were too low. Otherwise, they never would have let Su Yan take such a risk.

Over the years, husband and wife had also worked hard in retreat, thinking only of advancing to the Void King Realm one day so they could go to that Void Passage and search for Su Yan. Otherwise, how would they have the face to meet Yang Kai?

They had never imagined that this surprise would come so suddenly.

Yang Kai had returned, and Su Yan had returned with him.

No one was happier than the two of them.

"All right, all right. You are soaking Su Yan's clothes. What sort of scene is this?" Seeing Dong Suzhu cry without end, Fourth Master Yang could no longer stand it.

Only after hearing him say this did Dong Suzhu raise her head. She pulled Su Yan back and looked her up and down, just as Su Yan had looked over Yang Kai earlier, her face full of concern.

"Come, sit down and talk." Dong Suzhu took Su Yan's hand and led her aside to sit, impatient to know where she had been all these years and whether she had suffered any hardship.

After the two sat and chatted for a while, Su Yan said, "Mother, Yang Kai..."

"Do not mention that stinking brat!" Dong Suzhu's pretty face stiffened. "Let him die outside for all I care."

Fourth Master Yang mumbled, "Then why did you rush over in such a hurry just now?"

"What business is it of yours? I felt like it!" Dong Suzhu glared at him. "I came to look for Su Yan."

"Look at that. I only said one thing, so why are you losing your temper?" Fourth Master Yang was helpless too, raising a hand to his forehead. He wondered who it was who had shouted, "Where is my son?" the moment she came in.

"If you cannot stand me, then divorce me. In any case, the dazzling world outside is so alluring. I think you have wanted to for a long time, haven't you? Should I prepare a divorce letter for you? All you would need to do is sign your name."

Fourth Master Yang spread his hands, then reached up and mimed zipping his mouth shut.

Su Yan burst out laughing.

Dong Suzhu patted her hand and earnestly instructed her, "This is how men should be dealt with. Do not be too good to that stinking brat. The better you treat him, the less seriously he will take you. You have to be a little fiercer and a little harsher, and then he will keep you in mind wherever he goes."

"Mother is right. I will remember," Su Yan said with a light nod.

"Mother is saying this for your own good. Sigh, that temperament of yours... Ningshang is the same. Only little Qingluo has a little mind of her own. Another day, I will go speak with her. You girls all indulge him too much." She paused, then lowered her voice. "When he came back this time, did he bring any..."

"Any what?" Su Yan looked at her doubtfully.

"He did not bring back any improper women, did he?"

Su Yan thought for a moment and said, "That one does not count, right?" She had also met He Yunxiang and knew why He Yunxiang was following Yang Kai, so naturally she would not consider her one of Yang Kai's women.

Dong Suzhu, however, was like a cat whose tail had been stepped on, instantly bristling. Her face turned ashen as she said, “He really brought one?” She was truly furious. If someone had such a wonderful wife, they would not even have enough time to cherish her properly, so how could he leave for several dozen years without a word and still go around flirting outside?

"She really is not."

"He has rebelled!" Dong Suzhu slapped the armrest of her chair, then turned and glared angrily at Fourth Master Yang, who had been treating silence as gold. "This is all because of your good son!"

Fourth Master Yang lowered his head and said nothing, feeling terribly wronged. He had not even said anything more, yet he had still been dragged into the mess.

Brat, you are on your own. When there is time, if your mother does not beat your ass until it blooms, this old master will do it himself!

"She really is not," Su Yan said, caught between laughter and tears, though warmth filled her heart.

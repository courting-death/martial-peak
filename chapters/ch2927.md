## Chapter 2927 — Rushing to the Scene

“But Senior Brother Qiu and the others are there too!” Yang Kai said.

Gao Xueting said, “Senior Brother Qiu and the others are indeed strong, but I’m worried they don’t know how insidious Demonic Intent is. If they’re careless, they might fall for it. We can’t reach them right now, and we don’t know what’s happening at the Shrine. You said you saw Gong Yue on Spirit Sword Peak—why was he there?”

“I’m curious about that too.” Yang Kai shook his head. There are many spirit peaks in Qingyang Shrine, yet Gong Yue specifically went to Spirit Sword Peak. Yang Kai even suspected he was deliberately seeking revenge, since he had always blocked that Demonic Intent’s return in the ancient cave residence.

But that didn’t seem right either. If it was just for revenge, Gong Yue wouldn’t take such a huge risk.

“Senior Sister, what did you mean earlier when you said the Hall Master couldn’t spare any attention?” Yang Kai pressed.

Gao Xueting pondered for a moment, as if weighing her words, then replied, “You’re already an Elder of the Shrine, so there’s nothing I can’t tell you. Do you remember last time when you wanted to use the Divine Roaming Mirror and I stopped you?”

“Of course I remember. But what does that have to do with this?” Yang Kai asked, puzzled.

“The Hall Master is connected to the Divine Roaming Mirror,” Gao Xueting explained casually. “Back then, I told you someone was using the Divine Roaming Mirror, so it couldn’t be disturbed. Do you know who that person was?”

That was what Yang Kai found strange at the time. The Divine Roaming Mirror is the Ancestral Treasure of Qingyang Shrine, the foundation of the sect. Because of it, the Divine Soul cultivation of the Shrine’s experts is generally higher than average. But entering the Divine Roaming Mirror to cultivate isn’t easy. Ordinary disciples must render great merit to earn a rare chance, and even Elders have to pay three thousand Elder Contribution points each time—no exceptions.

Elder Contribution is hard to come by, and three thousand is no small sum. As a newly appointed Elder, Yang Kai naturally didn’t have any.

Gao Xueting didn’t stop him because he lacked Elder Contribution, but because someone was already using the Divine Roaming Mirror at the time.

Yang Kai had been curious about who it was, but since it wasn’t urgent, he hadn’t pursued it.

He hadn’t expected Gao Xueting to bring it up now.

“Who?” Yang Kai frowned, faintly sensing that this person must be extraordinary.

“This person is closely related to the Hall Master. The Hall Master personally raised and taught him,” Gao Xueting glanced at Yang Kai, not naming the person directly.

Yang Kai’s body trembled, and a figure immediately surfaced in his mind. He suddenly understood, “It’s him!”

Gao Xueting looked at him curiously. “You’re much calmer than I expected.”

Yang Kai vaguely realized something and asked, “That lord entered the Divine Roaming world to separate his Divine Soul?”

Gao Xueting’s delicate body trembled. She stared at Yang Kai in shock. “How did you know about this?”

Even she didn’t know the details of such a secret. She only guessed because Wen Zishan had casually mentioned a few things in front of her. She hadn’t expected Yang Kai to see through the truth in one go. No one could deduce so much unless they already knew something.

Yang Kai gave a wry smile and said, “That Senior’s current state is, in a way, related to me.”

“Related to you?” Gao Xueting grew even more puzzled, her beautiful eyes filled with confusion as she stared at Yang Kai like an eager student seeking knowledge in an unfamiliar field.

Yang Kai briefly recounted what happened in the Shattered Star Sea. “I only encountered that by chance and survived by luck. But it seems that lord’s Divine Soul became entangled with another’s, forcing them to share one body. I didn’t expect him to return to the Shrine. Right, the Divine Roaming Mirror is a world of Divine Souls. Tian Yan is also inside. Maybe there’s really a way to separate the two souls in there.”

“So that’s how it is!” Gao Xueting suddenly understood. Many things she hadn’t figured out now made sense after Yang Kai’s explanation. She never imagined this matter was related to Yang Kai, and that he had even participated in the struggle between two Great Emperors. Although neither was at their peak then, such an opportunity was rare.

Most importantly, Yang Kai had played a role in it. If he hadn’t helped, that lord might have perished with his enemy. The current situation wasn’t great, but at least he was alive. As long as he lived, there was hope to separate the two souls.

Because that person’s identity was too sensitive, Yang Kai couldn’t use the Divine Roaming Mirror at the time.

Gao Xueting continued, “Things aren’t going smoothly over there, so the Hall Master went to help. Now, the Hall Master’s Divine Soul is inside the Divine Roaming Mirror and knows nothing of the outside world. If Gong Yue is causing trouble in the Shrine, the Hall Master won’t notice at all.”

“I see!” Yang Kai nodded, understanding Gao Xueting’s concern.

Yang Kai ended his Divine Sense transmission with Gao Xueting, opened his eyes, and said, "I'm about to deploy my space divine ability. If any of you feel any discomfort, please don't resist."

Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming both nodded lightly, watching Yang Kai with curiosity.

They had long heard that Yang Kai, like the one from Spirit Beast Island, was proficient in the Laws of Space, but had never seen it with their own eyes. Such an esoteric and mysterious power wasn’t something one could witness easily, especially at Yang Kai’s level of mastery.

So they watched with great interest, waiting quietly.

The power of the Laws of Space surged, enveloping the entire Flowing Cloud Shuttle. This was Yang Kai’s power. Without his prior warning, Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming would have instinctively resisted. But with the warning, they knew what Yang Kai was doing and relaxed their minds, not interfering with the flow of the laws.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle seemed to tremble slightly. For a moment, both Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming felt extremely uncomfortable, as if they had suddenly entered a world of nothingness—no sound, no light, just emptiness.

But the feeling was fleeting, vanishing in the blink of an eye.

At the same time, the Flowing Cloud Shuttle had already appeared a thousand li away.

Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming exchanged glances, both seeing the shock in each other’s eyes.

It’s said that those proficient in space powers are best at escaping. Now it seemed the rumors were true. Both were at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, but even they couldn’t move a thousand li in an instant. Maybe with a special Secret Treasure they could barely manage, but never as effortlessly as Yang Kai.

With such a divine ability, as long as the surrounding space wasn’t sealed, who in this world could stop him?

No wonder back then, when Flower Shadow Great Emperor chased the one from Spirit Beast Island, even with overwhelming strength, she couldn’t get her way. Those proficient in the Power of Space were as slippery as eels—if a single strike failed, it would be impossible to track their whereabouts again.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle kept trembling, and with each tremor, it appeared another thousand li away.

The people inside grew more and more aware of the power of the Laws of Space.

At first, Yang Kai’s breath was steady and his expression calm, but as time passed, he gradually became exhausted. Teleporting three Emperor Realm cultivators and an Imperial Treasure at once consumed him greatly.

But the situation was urgent, so he couldn’t worry about that—he just wanted to get back to the Shrine as soon as possible.

After half a day, he stuffed a handful of Spiritual Pills into his mouth, signaled Gao Xueting to take over control of the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, and sat down to meditate and recover.

Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming saw clearly that the Spiritual Pills Yang Kai stuffed into his mouth were all Emperor Pills, each one priceless, yet he chewed and swallowed them like candy.

Even though both were used to high status, this scene made their faces twitch, cursing his wastefulness in their hearts.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle they were currently riding was an Imperial Treasure–grade flying Secret Treasure, its value beyond estimation—something even they did not possess.

No matter how hard they thought, they couldn’t figure out how this young man, with so few years of cultivation, had amassed such a fortune. Compared to him, they were like country bumpkins, with no advantage as Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivators at all.

The three took turns controlling the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, so the consumption wasn’t great, and they could always stay at their peak to deal with any danger.

Once Yang Kai recovered, he used his space divine ability again, and their speed increased.

After three days, the four finally arrived at the Qingyang Mountain Range.

Sweeping his Divine Sense across the area, he found nothing unusual in the mountain range; it appeared just as it normally did. Among the scattered peaks, the disciples of the Shrine each carried out their duties, with no signs of having been attacked.

This made Yang Kai and Gao Xueting, who had rushed back in such a hurry, both breathe a sigh of relief.

The Flowing Cloud Shuttle stopped before the Grand Guardian Array, and the four stepped out. Gao Xueting was the first to notice something amiss.

There was an inexplicable new restriction outside the Grand Guardian Array. It wasn’t very strong, but extremely well hidden. If Gao Xueting weren’t so familiar with the array, she might not have noticed it at all.

Judging by the traces, it had clearly been set up in the last few days.

Yang Kai pondered, then took out his Elder Jade Token, poured Divine Sense into it, and after probing, said in a deep voice, “It was Gong Yue who did this. This restriction is what’s blocking our connection with the Shrine.”

“Despicable!” Gao Xueting gritted her teeth, raised the Scorching Sun Mirror, and a blazing ray of sunlight blasted down at the restriction.

With a rumble, the ground shook, and the weak restriction was instantly destroyed.

“Who dares… Uh, Elder Gao, Elder Yang!”

A disciple flew out from inside, full of momentum, but wilted at the sight of Gao Xueting, hurriedly bowing in greeting.

“Has anything unusual happened at the Shrine these past few days?” Gao Xueting asked the disciple.

The disciple didn’t dare look at her, confused by the question, and replied, “Everything’s normal.”

Gao Xueting didn’t ask further, realizing the disciple knew nothing. She said urgently, “To Spirit Sword Peak!”

As she rushed off, she poured a message into her Elder Jade Token, sending word to the others.

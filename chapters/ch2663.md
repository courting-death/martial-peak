## Chapter 2663 — Useless

Yingfei and Xi Lei’s mouths dropped open, staring blankly.

In this situation, how could they not know what was really going on?

This Xie Wuwei was truly Yang Kai’s soul slave, branded with a Soul Brand.

But... when did this happen? The three Yao Kings had been together all this time, and never noticed anything amiss.

Xie Wuwei withdrew the golden light, chuckling, “Back when Young Master Yang last entered the Ancient Land, I had already submitted to him. My life and death are entirely at his whim. If anything happens to Young Master Yang, I’ll surely die as well. That’s why my loyalty is unwavering, far beyond what you two could ever compare to.”

As a Yao King, now reduced to someone’s soul slave, Xie Wuwei showed no shame—instead, he seemed proud. If others unaware of the truth saw this, their eyes would surely pop out in shock.

But for Yingfei and Xi Lei, all they could do was curse him inwardly for being utterly despicable.

With this relationship between Xie Wuwei and Yang Kai, if someone had to stay behind to protect Yang Kai, the choice was obvious.

For a moment, Xi Lei and Yingfei were full of unwillingness, glancing at Yang Kai with stammering reluctance.

Yang Kai wore a wry smile. Last time in the Tomb of All Spirits in the Ancient Land, he had no choice but to make Xie Wuwei his soul slave, hoping to borrow his strength. At that time, Xie Wuwei was extremely unwilling, and after leaving the Tomb of All Spirits, he kept his distance, afraid others would discover his connection to Yang Kai and look down on him.

But now, he was actually revealing this information himself, just to gain a slight advantage.

This change in attitude was quite comical. Still, Yang Kai couldn’t call him opportunistic—after all, these Yao Kings had reached the peak of their cultivation, and their only hope in life was the secrets within the Blood Gate.

Now that the opportunity was right before them, who wouldn’t want to seize it?

Just as Xie Wuwei was feeling smug, Yang Kai suddenly waved his hand, and a strand of golden light shot from Xie Wuwei’s forehead, returning to Yang Kai’s body.

Xie Wuwei’s proud expression froze, feeling as if a shackle had been lifted, but instead of joy, he looked miserable. “Young Master Yang, what are you...?”

With the Soul Brand withdrawn, the connection between Yang Kai and his Divine Soul was severed. He was no longer Yang Kai’s soul slave, and regained his freedom.

Xie Wuwei never expected Yang Kai to do this, and so decisively.

Most people would never let go of such a powerful soul slave, keeping them close for orders. But Yang Kai simply discarded him without hesitation. Such magnanimity and mindset were beyond ordinary people.

“Hahaha!” Yingfei and Xi Lei burst out laughing, utterly delighted. Their eyes shone, and they knew exactly what had happened. In their hearts, they cheered—serves you right, Xie Wuwei, for showing off. Retribution came fast!

“Young Master Yang, you can’t do this...” Xie Wuwei was utterly aggrieved, looking at Yang Kai with a resentful face, as if Yang Kai had abandoned him.

Yang Kai impatiently waved his hand, “Go on, go on, all of you.”

“We're really leaving?” Xi Lei’s face was bitter, clearly reluctant.

Yang Kai said, “Don’t blame me for sending you away after using you. You’re Yao Kings of the Ancient Land, and there are still the three Saint Venerables above. If I just take you away without informing them, I can’t explain myself to the Saint Venerables. Anyway, now that I can come and go from the Ancient Land easily, if there’s anything good next time, I’ll call you over.”

Hearing this, the three Yao Kings looked much better, nodding, “Young Master Yang, you must keep your word. Only call us three, don’t bring in anyone else.”

In the Ancient Land, there were Thirty-Two Yao Kings and Eight Saint Envoys, all top experts. The competition was fierce. They didn’t want other Yao Kings or Saint Envoys to get too close to Yang Kai, so they kept lingering, unwilling to leave.

“Got it!” Yang Kai agreed readily, waving his hand repeatedly as if shooing flies.

The three Yao Kings finally walked slowly onto the Space Array.

Yang Kai casually embedded Source Crystals nearby, then glanced at the three, “Ready?”

They nodded, cupping their fists, “Take care, Young Master Yang.”

Their words were full of reluctance, as if parting for life and death.

Yang Kai responded, then shouted, “Go!”

As he spoke, he poured Emperor Yuan into the Space Array.

Strangely, the Space Array didn’t react. Yang Kai and the three Yao Kings stared at each other for a long while, all with odd expressions.

“Huh?” Yang Kai was stunned, scratching his head. He tried again, but still couldn’t activate the Space Array.

The three Yao Kings exchanged glances, their thoughts stirring. Yingfei laughed, “Looks like even Heavens don’t want us to leave.”

Xie Wuwei immediately echoed, “Exactly, Young Master Yang. Since Heavens will it, don’t force it.”

“What’s going on?” Yang Kai was confused, circling the Space Array to inspect it. Though he’d just set it up, there shouldn’t be any mistakes.

After checking, he confirmed the Space Array was perfectly fine.

Xi Lei reminded, “Young Master Yang, last time we transmitted directly from the Ancient Land to Ice Heart Valley. Maybe this Space Array can’t connect to the Ancient Land?”

“Impossible!” Yang Kai shook his head. The Space Arrays he set up could connect with each other. In other words, the one in Lingxiao Palace could link to Ice Heart Valley and also to the Wild Ancient Land.

As Yang Kai was puzzled, the Space Array suddenly burst with light, and two figures appeared from within.

Everyone looked, and it was Ji Yao and Nanmen Dajun.

After the transmission, both seemed a bit dizzy. Ji Yao quickly recovered, while Nanmen Dajun took a moment longer—his cultivation was lower than Ji Yao’s.

“Senior Brother Yang, I’ve brought the person you wanted.” Ji Yao glanced at Yang Kai and spoke softly.

Yang Kai nodded, looking at Nanmen Dajun, who also looked back. Seeing Yang Kai, his eyes lit up and he said excitedly, “Master Yang, I heard you needed me?”

Ever since witnessing Yang Kai’s Space Array, Nanmen Dajun regarded him as a grandmaster of arrays—one even more skilled than himself.

“There’s something I need.” Yang Kai nodded, puzzled, “But didn’t you say you’d come after repairing Ice Heart Valley’s protective array? How did you arrive so quickly?”

Nanmen Dajun replied, “It’s already fixed. Hearing Master Yang summoned me, I worked day and night, and just finished. So I rushed right over.”

As he spoke, his face was full of flattery.

“You didn’t just do a rush job, did you?” Yang Kai looked at him skeptically.

Ice Heart Valley’s protective array was a massive project—how could it be fully repaired in just a few days?

Nanmen Dajun said anxiously, “How could I dare? Master Yang is deeply skilled in arrays. I wouldn’t show off in front of you. The quick repair was mainly because I’d studied Ice Heart Valley’s array for months, and the method to break it was provided by me. By reversing the process, it was easy to fix.”

“I see.” Yang Kai believed him, but still warned, “I’ll check Ice Heart Valley later. If you cut corners, watch your head.”

Nanmen Dajun replied confidently, “Master Yang, rest assured.”

After a pause, he asked, “By the way, what did you need me for?”

Yang Kai waved, “Take a look here.”

Nanmen Dajun turned to look, uttered a soft ‘huh’, then flew up to investigate, and landed again, “Isn’t this Wenqing Sect?”

He’d visited Wenqing Sect before, so he recognized the land. Last time, it was full of talented people, but now, though the scenery remained, the people were gone.

The Great Emperor’s legacy of tens of thousands of years was wasted here, making him sigh.

“This used to be Wenqing Sect, but now it’s Lingxiao Palace!” Yang Kai smiled.

“Lingxiao Palace?” Nanmen Dajun frowned, not understanding Yang Kai’s meaning.

Ji Yao’s beautiful eyes flashed, her face thoughtful, as if she’d guessed something.

Yang Kai continued, “I want you to reopen and repair the protective array here, and use all your skills to set up an array on this land that rivals any sect in the world.”

Hearing this, Nanmen Dajun’s spirit lifted, as if he’d heard a thrilling challenge.

Yang Kai observed him, nodding in satisfaction at his reaction.

“Master Yang, you mean to take over this land?” Nanmen Dajun quickly guessed Yang Kai’s intention.

“Exactly.” Yang Kai nodded. “Such a huge project—how long will it take?”

Nanmen Dajun didn’t answer immediately, but thought carefully, “Wenqing Sect’s protective array wasn’t destroyed, just shut down. I need to study the layout, then figure out improvements. Two months at the shortest, three at the latest, and I’ll give you a satisfactory result.”

“Good, you have three months.” Yang Kai agreed readily.

Nanmen Dajun said, “But for such a big array, the materials...”

“Just make a list of what you need, I’ll provide everything.” Yang Kai replied without concern.

He’d gathered so much wealth, even emptied Wenqing Sect’s treasury, so he had all the materials needed for a protective array.

Nanmen Dajun grinned, “With materials, it’s easy. By the way, Master Yang, do you want to join me...?”

Yang Kai said seriously, “I’m too busy to help. You handle the array yourself. If you need manpower...” He glanced at Ji Yao.

Ji Yao understood, “Just ask me. I’ll call some sisters from my sect.”

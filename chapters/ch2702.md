## Chapter 2702 — The Crafty Little Thief

“So this is what you’re relying on?” Although Yang Kai was wary inside, he appeared utterly unconcerned on the surface. He was eager to see what kind of power this so-called Golden Armored Heavenly Tome possessed. As he spoke, he charged at Tan Junhao with his sword, and in midair, he flicked the Myriad Sword, unleashing countless sword lights that swept toward Tan Junhao like a swarm of locusts. He sneered, “You treat a worthless book like some kind of treasure? Old dog, you’re past your prime. This world belongs to the young now—just accept your death.”

“A worthless book…” Tan Junhao’s brow twitched, his face full of anger as if Yang Kai had gravely insulted him. Gritting his teeth, he said, “Let this old man show you the might of this worthless book!”

As he spoke, he suddenly waved his hand.

The Golden Armored Heavenly Tome floating above his head suddenly began to flip through its pages with a rustling sound, then abruptly stopped on a particular page.

Yang Kai saw clearly that on that page was engraved an oval-shaped pattern, winding and twisting like a child’s doodle, completely unrecognizable.

But as soon as the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome settled on that page, it suddenly radiated golden light, forming an oval barrier of light that enveloped Tan Junhao.

Boom…

The might of the Myriad Sword struck the golden barrier, but it didn’t budge in the slightest.

Yang Kai’s eyes narrowed in shock.

Although this strike was mostly a probe, it wasn’t something just anyone could withstand. Tan Junhao hadn’t even used his Emperor Yuan to protect himself—he relied solely on the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome for perfect defense. Clearly, this tome’s power was extraordinary.

An Imperial Treasure for defense? Yang Kai’s heart stirred, but he quickly realized something was off. At the very start, several golden beams had shot out—something a defensive Imperial Treasure couldn’t do.

Tan Junhao stood there smiling, not even frowning when the sword lights came at him. That expression showed his absolute confidence in the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, certain that Yang Kai couldn’t harm him.

And indeed, that was the case.

“Didn’t even tickle!” Tan Junhao mocked with a sneer. “Is that all you’ve got? If so, you’d best accept your death.”

With that, he pointed at Yang Kai, and the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome above his head began flipping again, stopping on another page.

Yang Kai looked over and saw that this page was engraved with many fierce birds, all as crude as a child’s doodles, but with a savage edge.

A sudden sense of foreboding welled up in Yang Kai’s heart.

A flurry of flapping sounds rang out as beams of golden light shot from the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, twisting and morphing in midair into all kinds of fierce birds, all swooping toward Yang Kai.

There were at least a hundred of these birds, large and small—some as long as twenty or thirty zhang, others as tiny as sparrows.

Each one radiated an aura as powerful as the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and one especially massive bird even had the strength of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm.

“Both offense and defense!” Yang Kai exclaimed in shock. Now he understood—the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome wasn’t just a defensive Imperial Treasure, but a rare composite treasure that could attack and defend.

Such Imperial Treasures were extremely rare and difficult to forge. Even ordinary treasures were worth a fortune, let alone this Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, which was clearly of the highest grade.

“How can a junior like you compete with this old man?” Tan Junhao stood with his hands behind his back, laughing heartily. “You think just because you used the Mountain and River Bell to trap those fools, this old man has no helpers? With the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, I don’t even need to lift a finger to make sure you die with no grave.”

As he spoke, the fierce birds and beasts were already swooping at Yang Kai from all directions, each one vicious to the extreme. Some spat out mysterious light, some flapped their wings to unleash fireballs or wind blades, while others simply lunged with razor-sharp claws.

In an instant, Yang Kai was overwhelmed, barely able to cope as the beasts drove him into a perilous situation.

Tan Junhao kept laughing wildly. To Yang Kai’s ears, it was unbearably infuriating—he wanted nothing more than to rush over and tear his mouth apart.

After a while, Yang Kai finally steadied himself, but his situation hadn’t improved. He was still surrounded on all sides by the fierce birds and beasts summoned from the tome, all eyeing him hungrily.

“Break!” Yang Kai suddenly punched in one direction. From his fist, a Moon Blade shot out, slicing several beasts in half before they could dodge.

Yang Kai merged with his sword and charged through the opening.

There were simply too many of these beasts. If he really got surrounded, even with his overwhelming strength, he’d be hard-pressed to fight back. Fortunately, while these things could unleash Emperor Realm power, their defenses weren’t as strong—he could take out several with a single punch. Killing them all shouldn’t be too difficult.

“Naive!” Tan Junhao saw Yang Kai trying to break out but didn’t stop him, just stood there looking supremely confident.

“Not good!” Yang Kai’s heart suddenly lurched—something was wrong.

The beasts he’d just killed didn’t vanish. Instead, they twisted and morphed, returning to their original forms.

These things couldn’t be killed!

Yang Kai cursed inwardly. With savage auras closing in from all sides, it was too late to dodge. One of the fierce birds swooped down, its golden claws gleaming—sharp enough to tear through even the toughest steel.

In this critical moment, Yang Kai could only unleash his Undying Five Elements Sword Qi again, five-colored light swirling around him. At the same time, he pushed his Dragon Transformation Secret Technique to the limit, his hands flying as countless Moon Blades shot out in all directions.

Shhh shhh shhh shhh…

One after another, Moon Blades tore through the air, slicing the fierce birds and beasts in half, their forms twisting and changing in midair.

Rip…

A sound rang out as the Undying Five Elements Sword Qi instantly collapsed. Yang Kai was struck several times in the chest and back, and one bird’s claws ripped open his chest, leaving a deep, bloody wound.

And this was after using his Dragon Transformation—if not for the dragon scales absorbing some of the impact, that single blow might have gutted him on the spot.

“It’s useless. No matter how many times you kill them, they’ll never die.” Tan Junhao sneered, standing off to the side, spouting cold mockery.

Compared to those Emperor Realm cultivators he’d tricked at the auction, the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome was his true trump card. As long as he held the tome, the summoned creatures would never perish—no matter how many times they were killed, they’d always return.

Of course Yang Kai understood this.

He didn’t know the tome’s secrets, but he’d guessed a few things by now.

Taking advantage of the moment before the slain beasts recovered, Yang Kai’s figure flickered as the Laws of Space surged, preparing to teleport away.

“Ha! Out of tricks, boy?” Tan Junhao laughed. He’d set up so many traps here precisely to counter Yang Kai’s space abilities.

And it had worked. Without those measures, with Yang Kai’s mastery of space, he’d have been helpless against him.

Yet now, Yang Kai was trying the same trick again, attempting to teleport away—how foolish.

As he spoke, Tan Junhao flipped his palm, and a formation flag appeared in his hand.

This was the flag controlling the Grand Square Five Elements Array. With it, he’d previously been able to perfectly counter Yang Kai’s space techniques. Now that Yang Kai was out of options, he didn’t mind using it again.

Being forcibly shaken out from the void was never a pleasant experience. Once that happened, with just a single command from him, all those fierce birds and beasts would pounce at once—Yang Kai would have no chance of survival.

Emperor Yuan surged as Tan Junhao poured power into the flag, then waved it toward Yang Kai’s position.

In the next instant, Yang Kai’s form shifted from ethereal to solid, reappearing in place.

Tan Junhao snarled, “Die!”

But before the words had even faded, Yang Kai suddenly vanished.

Tan Junhao’s laughter cut off abruptly. In a flash, Yang Kai appeared right in front of him, almost nose to nose, his eyes full of mocking amusement. “Old dog, your timing’s a bit off.”

“You…” Tan Junhao was shocked, but in a split second, he realized what had happened.

Yang Kai’s earlier attempt at teleportation had been a feint—he hadn’t actually used the technique. His form becoming solid was just him revealing himself, not being forced out.

Thinking he’d succeeded, Tan Junhao had let his guard down, and only then did Yang Kai truly use his teleportation technique.

Damn it, he’d fallen for the brat’s trick. Tan Junhao nearly ground his teeth to dust. He never imagined his trump card would become a weakness, used against him—what a humiliation.

Mind racing, Tan Junhao tried to retreat.

The real battle was just beginning. The Golden Armored Heavenly Tome hadn’t even shown a tenth of its power. Even if Yang Kai had tricked him once, there was no way he could win.

Tan Junhao was fully confident he could kill Yang Kai here.

“Look into my eyes!”

At that moment, Yang Kai pressed his tongue to the roof of his mouth and shouted.

Tan Junhao instinctively looked into his eyes.

“Good boy!” Yang Kai grinned savagely. Suddenly, his left eye turned solemn, filled with intense golden light. His pupil became a vertical golden slit, like a heavenly eye, radiating boundless majesty.

World-Ending Demon Eye!

“No, tricked again! Crafty little thief!” Tan Junhao was horrified, cursing Yang Kai to the heavens in his heart.

He tried to look away, but found he couldn’t. That golden vertical pupil emitted a strange power that shook his mind, a mysterious force that seemed to seize his very soul.

Eye technique!

Tan Junhao bit through his tongue, desperately rousing his Divine Soul. The soul-seizing power loosened for a moment.

But between masters, victory or defeat is decided in an instant.

Before Tan Junhao could look away, Yang Kai had already struck.

“Soul Slash, Heaven-Shattering Strike!”

A beam of golden light shot from his left eye, slashing toward Tan Junhao in a flash.

Yang Kai grunted, his face pale and sweat pouring from his brow.

Tan Junhao, on the other hand, let out a shrill scream, blood spurting from his eyes as his face twisted in agony.

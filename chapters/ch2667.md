## Chapter 2667 — Reopening Emperor Heaven Valley

When Yang Kai last came to the Thousand Leaves Sect, this place was still a towering mountain, full of the world’s spiritual essence. But today, as he returned, everything he saw was in utter ruin, the scenery bleak and desolate.

Amid the wilds, all that remained were broken walls and shattered ruins. The pavilions and towers that once stood tall had mostly collapsed, everywhere bearing the scars of a great battle. The blood on the ground had long since dried, birds flew overhead, and the earth was barren.

Yang Kai flew slowly through the air, his expression extremely dark.

Sweeping his Divine Sense across the vast Thousand Leaves Sect, he found not a single living soul. Only the occasional beast or bird could be seen in the mountains, and the air still seemed to carry a lingering stench of decay.

The Thousand Leaves Sect had truly been annihilated.

Though the Thousand Leaves Sect was not considered great or powerful, it had still endured for tens of thousands of years. At its peak, it dominated the Southern Territory, boasting countless disciples and experts as numerous as clouds. But now, the Thousand Leaves Sect no longer existed in this world.

A surge of inexplicable fury churned in his chest, leaving Yang Kai unbearably stifled. Though it was only an instinct, he could not shake the feeling that this matter had something to do with him.

Yet no matter how he pondered, he couldn’t figure out what link he might have to the Thousand Leaves Sect’s destruction.

Perhaps, only by finding the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect and getting answers could he understand the reason behind all this.

Suddenly, Yang Kai lifted his head, his gaze turning toward the main hall of the Thousand Leaves Sect, where he sensed the presence of several living people.

His eyes grew cold, and with a flicker, he sped in that direction.

Moments later, he landed before the main hall. There had once stood a statue here, the image of the Thousand Leaves Sect’s founder, for later generations to revere. But now, the statue had long since toppled, as if someone had cut it down with a single sword. The break was clean, revealing the extraordinary strength of the one who did it. Yang Kai reached out to touch the break, feeling a trace of the wind’s lively aura within.

From inside the hall drifted the aroma of meat. Faint voices could also be heard within.

Yang Kai pushed open the door and entered, bringing a gust of cold wind with him.

Inside, several people had built a fire and were boiling an unknown meat stew. They sat on the ground, drinking wine from large bowls and eating meat in big chunks, looking thoroughly pleased with themselves.

Hearing the commotion, they all turned at once. An older man stood up, glaring at Yang Kai. “Who are you?!”

Yang Kai glanced at them and saw they were not disciples of the Thousand Leaves Sect, leaving him somewhat disappointed.

It made sense—if any Thousand Leaves Sect disciples had survived, they would have long since changed their names and fled. Who would dare remain in their own sect’s main hall doing such things?

The strongest among them was only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. The rest were merely at the Saint King Realm, seemingly all rootless wandering martial artists.

With the Thousand Leaves Sect destroyed and such a vast mountain gate left deserted, it was naturally a good place for people like them to settle.

Yang Kai had no interest in lingering with them. He simply asked, "There are no survivors left in the Thousand Leaves Sect?"

Lest their eyesight be lacking, he released a bit of pressure as he spoke.

The few of them instantly tensed as if facing a great enemy. The man who had been putting on airs to display his authority suddenly paled, his body shrinking half a foot in an instant as he stared at Yang Kai with trepidation and said, "We haven't seen any survivors."

As expected. Yang Kai nodded, glanced around, and said nothing more.

Cold sweat drenched the man’s forehead as he stood frozen, not daring to move. Only after a long while did Yang Kai finally turn and walk out.

Only then did the martial artists inside the hall feel as if they escaped death penalty. Their bodies went limp. A strong expert like Yang Kai could take their lives with a flick of his hand, and such experts were notoriously unpredictable—no one knew when they might kill.

That’s why, when traveling outside, it was best to avoid dealing with anyone stronger than oneself.

Having narrowly escaped death, the group exchanged hurried glances. No longer caring about the nearly finished stew, they fled the hall in panic, running as fast as their legs could carry them.

In the nameless valley, Yang Kai looked around for a while before his eyes lit up as he stood in a certain spot.

Yang Kai was very familiar with this valley. The last time he came to the Thousand Leaves Sect, he had visited this place, for it was the sect’s forbidden ground. Throughout the generations, only the Sect Master of the Thousand Leaves Sect could enter here.

On the surface, it looked like an ordinary valley, but in truth, it was shrouded by a powerful illusion array, concealing the space array that led to the Thousand Leaves Sect’s independent small world.

Emperor Heaven Valley!

Yang Kai finally remembered the name of the Thousand Leaves Sect’s independent small world. It was in Emperor Heaven Valley that he had encountered a powerful Corpse Puppet, and Liu Yan had obtained her own spirit puppet body.

There must have been a great battle in this valley that day, for the surroundings were full of traces left by fighting—dried blood everywhere, white bones scattered about. It seemed that many people had died here when the Thousand Leaves Sect was destroyed.

In the center of the valley, where the space array was located, Yang Kai carefully examined the damaged formation. Before long, his eyes brightened with realization.

He could tell that the space array had been destroyed by brute force, and the person who did it had acted in great haste, as if something urgent had happened at the time.

Yang Kai couldn’t help but wonder if the one who destroyed the array was the Thousand Leaves Sect Master, Ye Hen.

If it was him, then the news wasn’t all bad, because on the other side of the space array, in Emperor Heaven Valley, there was a good chance that some Thousand Leaves Sect disciples had survived.

If he were Ye Hen, knowing the sect was doomed, he would surely have transferred his disciples and close ones into Emperor Heaven Valley, then destroyed the space array to ensure their safety.

Emperor Heaven Valley was an independent small world. Though not very large, as long as the space array was destroyed, it would be nearly impossible for anyone else to enter.

The hurriedly destroyed array was proof of this.

Thoughts flashed through his mind like lightning. Yang Kai stood up and said, “Yao King, stand guard.”

He was going to restore the space array and enter Emperor Heaven Valley to see for himself. Then, everything would become clear.

Yingfei nodded, his figure flickering as he vanished, hiding who knows where.

With a sweep of his sleeve, Yang Kai sent a fierce wind across the broken space array, blowing away the debris. He reached out to feel the array before him.

Yang Kai had helped repair this space array for the Thousand Leaves Sect last time. Though it had been destroyed again, restoring it was no difficult task for him—he was more than familiar with the process.

The power of the Laws of Space surged forth. Ripples and hums of space spread out, and the area around Yang Kai became illusory, as if space itself was collapsing.

In the midst of it all, a mysterious force resonated with Yang Kai, reconnecting the broken ends with this power as the source.

Whoosh…

Suddenly, a pitch-black opening appeared, its interior a chaotic nothingness, giving off a sense of devouring all things and making one’s heart tremble with unease.

Void Passage!

Yang Kai maintained the Void Passage’s stability with his power, then called out, “Yao King!”

Yingfei reappeared at his side, his gaze stern as he looked at the Void Passage.

Even someone as strong as him dared not underestimate this strange existence, for once trapped inside, one could easily become lost in the void forever, never finding a way out.

But Yang Kai boldly waved his hand, Emperor Yuan wrapping around him, and charged straight into the passage.

In no time, both figures vanished, along with the formed Void Passage.

In Emperor Heaven Valley, above the space array, Yang Kai and Yingfei appeared together.

The surroundings were empty, not a soul in sight, but Yang Kai’s sharp gaze soon found something on the ground.

He saw traces of blood and quite a few footprints.

Yingfei sniffed the air and said, “There are a lot of people here.”

As a Yao King, his sense of smell was naturally exceptional.

Yang Kai nodded, locking his gaze in one direction. “Over there.”

He remembered there was a valley and many caves in that direction. The last time, he had encountered the Corpse Puppet there. If any Thousand Leaves Sect disciples had survived and escaped here, that was where they would be hiding.

The two of them flew toward the valley, one after the other.

Emperor Heaven Valley was not large, but it was a self-contained world with excellent Spiritual Qi, which was why it had become the foundation of the Thousand Leaves Sect.

In less than the time it takes for an incense stick to burn, the two arrived above the valley. Yang Kai did not conceal his presence; instead, he deliberately released his aura as he descended.

Instantly, a flurry of noise erupted around the valley. Figures began to emerge from the woods, martial artists of varying strength, all looking pale as they stared at Yang Kai.

They had been hiding here for over a year, never seeing outsiders. They believed this was the safest place in the world, since the array outside had been destroyed and no one could enter.

But today, two outsiders had broken in.

This left the surviving Thousand Leaves Sect disciples anxious, unsure what fate awaited them. The day their sect was destroyed was still fresh in their minds, the sorrow of that day of blood and tears still fermenting in their hearts. Would even those who had managed to survive now meet their end?

From the largest cave, two figures emerged—one tall and straight, the other graceful and slender.

“Senior Sister Ye, Senior Brother Du!”

The many Thousand Leaves Sect disciples all saluted.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian arrived together. Unlike the other disciples, whose faces were filled with alarm, their eyes shone instead with unconcealed delight.

Because when Ye Hen sent them into the Emperor Heaven Valley that day, he told them that if anyone could reopen the valley, it would surely be Yang Kai!

So the moment they sensed outsiders, their first thought was that Yang Kai had arrived.

Now that they looked closely, it was indeed so. That familiar figure came into view—without question, the best news they had received in the past year.

“Young Master Yang…” As soon as Ye Qinghan spoke, tears began streaming down her face.

The last time Yang Kai returned, the Thousand Leaves Sect was in high spirits, having regained their ancestral legacy and recovered long-lost secret arts. But in just a few years, everything had changed, people and things alike. Thinking of her father, whose fate was unknown, Ye Qinghan couldn’t help but be overcome with emotion, unable to hold back her tears.

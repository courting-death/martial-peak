## Chapter 2763 — Few Words Are Too Many When There’s No Common Ground

"As for what to buy and sell, we must consider it carefully. Otherwise, not only might we fail to profit, we could even lose out."

Hua Qingsi said, "I will personally go to the Southern Territory to investigate this matter."

If this succeeded, it could shape the future of Lingxiao Palace. Though Yang Kai was now wealthy, and both Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect owed Lingxiao Palace enormous sums, a sect that wishes to endure must have its own source of income.

Yang Kai did not stay long at Lingxiao Palace. The next day, he returned to the Southern Territory.

A few days later, Yang Kai gazed at the endless mountain range ahead, his figure streaking through the sky like lightning.

That mountain range was the Qingyang Mountain Range—he was heading to the Qingyang Shrine. Although he was only registered in name at the Qingyang Shrine, he did possess the Qingyang Gold Token, which both Wen Zishan and Gao Xueting valued highly. Now that he had returned to the Southern Territory, it was only proper to visit the Shrine.

Besides, he also needed to enter the Divine Roaming Mirror to inform Tian Yan of the good news that he had found the Life Origin Fruit.

Originally, he had planned to refine the Life Origin Pill himself, but now that he had Ji Ying’s help, he naturally entrusted the task to him. Before leaving, Yang Kai had already left all the medicinal ingredients at Lingxiao Palace. He believed that next time he returned, he would be able to obtain the Life Origin Pill.

As long as he had the Life Origin Pill, Tian Yan could reconstruct his physical body, break free from the shackles of the Divine Roaming Mirror, descend upon the Star Realm, and become a solid pillar of support for Lingxiao Palace.

With a Great Emperor-level expert as their backing, even if Lingxiao Palace was not a Great Emperor Sect, it would become one, and its status would be on par with the Star God Palace and Soul Palace.

Suddenly, Yang Kai halted in midair. He looked coldly to the side and said in a deep voice, "Which friend is skulking about? Why not show yourself?"

As soon as he finished speaking, a burst of dazzling light exploded in the void over there, so bright that Yang Kai had to squint. When the light faded, two imposing figures appeared.

One was a middle-aged man, pale-faced and beardless, exuding a natural air of authority. The other was a young man about Yang Kai’s age, handsome and spirited, with sword-like brows and starry eyes. The two looked somewhat alike, seemingly father and son.

The middle-aged man’s gaze was calm, but the young man’s eyes brimmed with fighting spirit as he looked at Yang Kai.

"Lord Xiao!" Yang Kai raised his brows.

Xiao Yuyang said, "You are Yang Kai?"

"Indeed." Yang Kai nodded, glancing at the young man with a smile. "Brother Xiao, it’s been a while since we parted at the Shattered Star Sea. How have you been?"

These two were clearly from the Star God Palace, and Yang Kai recognized them both.

The young man, Xiao Chen, needed no introduction—Yang Kai had dealt with him many times, always seeing him at Princess Lan Xun’s side as her escort. As for his father, Xiao Yuyang, he was one of the first Emperor Realm experts Yang Kai had met upon arriving in the Star Realm. Back then, Xiao Yuyang was the Star Envoy of the Star God Palace, his cultivation at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, a figure Yang Kai could only look up to, someone who could kill him with a single breath.

So many years had passed, and Xiao Yuyang’s aura seemed even more powerful, now at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm! As for Xiao Chen, his cultivation was also impressive—First Stage of the Emperor Realm. It seemed he had gained much from the Shattered Star Sea.

For these two to suddenly appear here, blocking Yang Kai’s path, their intentions were obvious.

Elder Tan Junhao and Deacon Wu Ming of the Star God Palace had been killed. The Star God Palace had been investigating this matter for some time, and naturally, their search had led to Yang Kai. However, Yang Kai’s recent movements had been elusive, and even with the Star God Palace’s extensive network, they had trouble tracking him.

Some days ago, they received a message from a disciple named Luo Ping, saying he had seen Yang Kai in Fenglin City.

Xiao Yuyang had hurried over without pause. Now, as one of the Star God Palace’s Elders and with his extraordinary cultivation, he had come in person not to give Yang Kai face, but because, according to the information from the Star God Palace, both Tan Junhao and Wu Ming had died at Yang Kai’s hands. With such a fierce reputation, sending anyone weaker would be pointless; only a Third Stage Emperor could hope to handle this matter.

Because of Tan Junhao’s death, all the Third Stage Emperor Elders of the Star God Palace had been dispatched, searching everywhere for Yang Kai.

Xiao Yuyang knew Yang Kai was a disciple of the Qingyang Shrine and guessed that, since Yang Kai had appeared in Fenglin City, he might return to the Shrine. So he lay in ambush here, and after waiting half a month, finally caught Yang Kai.

Hearing Yang Kai’s words, Xiao Chen sneered, "Who are you? Do I know you?"

He looked down his nose at everyone, as if no one was worth his attention, carrying an air of superiority no matter who he faced.

After several encounters, Yang Kai naturally understood his temperament, so he just smiled and didn’t get angry. Instead, he looked at Xiao Yuyang and said, "May I ask why Lord Xiao is blocking my way?"

"Still pretending?" Xiao Chen shouted angrily, pointing his longsword at Yang Kai. "Don’t you know what you’ve done? Stop putting on an act!"

Yang Kai smiled, "I have no idea what Brother Xiao is talking about."

Xiao Chen’s face darkened. "Are you really clueless, or just acting like it?"

Yang Kai ignored him, only looking at Xiao Yuyang, which made Xiao Chen fume.

Xiao Yuyang stood with his hands behind his back, his expression calm, though inwardly he was quite amazed. He had been unfamiliar with Yang Kai before, only vaguely remembering that years ago, someone in the Dao Origin Realm had refined a batch of Supreme Mystic Pills and made a big name for himself—that person was Yang Kai.

He didn’t know much else, but over the past few days, he had asked Xiao Chen about Yang Kai and realized this young man was truly remarkable. Even in his eyes, the things Yang Kai had accomplished were extremely difficult. His own son was outstanding, but compared to Yang Kai, he seemed a bit lacking.

Now, meeting face to face, Xiao Yuyang’s opinion of Yang Kai rose even higher.

He was an Elder of the Star God Palace, with the cultivation of a Third Stage Emperor, yet this young First Stage Emperor showed not the slightest fear, speaking calmly and at ease.

Was it really Yang Kai who killed Tan Junhao and Wu Ming? Xiao Yuyang was deeply skeptical. Yang Kai’s strength was clear, and Tan Junhao and Wu Ming were no pushovers—how could they have died by his hand?

But from the Star God Palace’s investigations and the testimony of more than twenty Emperor Realm experts, it seemed that, even if Yang Kai hadn’t killed them himself, he was definitely involved.

"Tan Junhao, Wu Ming!" Xiao Yuyang said little, just naming the two, his eyes fixed on Yang Kai as if trying to see into his soul and catch the slightest reaction.

Yang Kai raised his brows. "So it’s about that... Well, if Lord Xiao is here to ask about those two, I can tell you—I killed them."

He admitted it readily, without the slightest attempt to hide it.

"Nonsense!" Xiao Chen sneered with contempt, clearly not believing him.

"I hear you have a powerful Yao King at your side?" Xiao Yuyang hinted. "Where is that Yao King now?"

Yang Kai shook his head. "The Yao King is not here."

Xiao Yuyang said, "Yang Kai, you are a talent worth nurturing. This Lord does not wish to make things difficult for you. Tell me where the Yao King is, then come with me to the Star God Palace."

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes. "Does Lord Xiao think Tan Junhao and Wu Ming were killed by the Yao King?"

"Is there any other possibility?"

Yang Kai laughed, shaking his head. He hadn’t expected that telling the truth would still be met with disbelief. After a moment’s thought, his expression turned serious. "Tan Junhao, as an Elder of the Star God Palace, represented the Palace and the Great Emperor. He should have protected the cultivators of the Southern Territory, but in Flowing Shadow City, he acted against all reason, using despicable means to control many Emperor Realm experts and ordered them to oppose me, trying to seize my treasures. I killed him in anger, which was cleaning house for the Star God Palace. I don’t expect gratitude from the Great Emperor or the Palace—just don’t cause me trouble. But if Lord Xiao is blocking my way here today, and this is the Palace’s attitude, then I am... very disappointed in the Star God Palace!"

"The Star God Palace’s affairs are not for you to criticize!" Xiao Chen snorted coldly.

Xiao Yuyang nodded. "We have figured out what happened. Tan Junhao was cruel and unrighteous, bullying the weak and disgracing the Palace’s name—he deserved to die. If you really killed him, the Palace has nothing more to say. But if he died at the hands of a Yao King... that’s a different matter. Do you understand?"

Yang Kai replied impatiently, "The Yao King didn’t act. Didn’t those Emperor Realm experts tell you all this?"

Xiao Yuyang said, "They did, but it’s hard to believe. So you need to come with me to the Star God Palace. I promise, no one will harm you. Just tell me where the Yao King is."

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes. "I cannot agree to Lord Xiao’s request."

"You don’t trust me..." Xiao Yuyang frowned slightly.

Yang Kai said, "Lord Xiao and I are neither kin nor friends—how could I trust you?"

"Few words are too many when there’s no common ground. Father, there’s no need to waste words with him. Let me take him down." Xiao Chen, who had been itching for a fight, could no longer hold back. With a swing of his longsword, a clear ring sounded as sword light burst forth. He charged at Yang Kai, snorting, "Just because you’ve become an Emperor, you think you’re something special? Today, I’ll show you the difference between us."

Emperor Intent filled the air. Sword Intent soared to the heavens, and the sound of slicing wind was endless. Xiao Chen’s expression was solemn as he chanted, "Plum blossoms bring fragrance in the snow, the slanting moon rides the mirror across the sky!"

From the very first move, he unleashed a powerful killing strike. The sword’s momentum was beautiful, dazzling to behold.

Yang Kai laughed heartily. "I return your words to you!"

With one hand, he formed a strange gesture and struck at the heart of the overwhelming Sword Intent.

At first, Xiao Yuyang had been secretly praising his son’s sword technique, but when Yang Kai made his move, his expression changed.

## Chapter 3038 — An Eye for an Eye

The sea wind howled. Madam Hua's tone was icy, and Lu Yuqin immediately shrank back, her gaze darting about guiltily.

"Do you think keeping quiet will make this go away?" Madam Hua snorted softly. "Do you think nothing will happen if you don't speak? There are only so many people on this island. Whoever injured Xiao Hong knows what they did. If you step forward on your own, this Madam may deal with you leniently, but if I have to drag you out..."

Yuan Wu immediately said, "Madam, it was definitely that mother and daughter who injured it."

Madam Hua snorted. "What makes you say that?"

Yuan Wu replied, "As this Yuan said earlier, the two of them were responsible for entering the sea to gather luminous pearls. Madam's red carp was likely injured by someone while it was playing in the sea. No one else on the entire island has entered the water besides that mother and daughter. This Yuan can guarantee it."

Madam Hua's gaze chilled slightly as she stared at Lu Sanniang. "Sanniang, what else do you have to say?"

Lu Sanniang lowered her head. "Please quell your anger, Elder Sister. I did not know that red carp was a pet you raised. Had I known, this Younger Sister would never have struck it."

"Good! So you really were the one who injured Xiao Hong. Younger Sister, you have truly disappointed me!" Madam Hua ground her teeth, her face filled with anger.

Lu Yuqin suddenly stepped forward and cried, "My mother didn't injure it. I did! It suddenly darted toward me, so I casually struck it once. I hardly even hurt it, did I?"

At this sudden confession, Lu Sanniang hurriedly pulled her back and rebuked her in a low voice, "Don't speak."

Madam Hua watched the scene coldly and chuckled. "What a touching display of affection between mother and daughter. Your elder sister is truly moved." Despite her words, there was not the slightest trace of being moved on her face. Her expression was so cold that she looked ready to devour someone.

The woman holding the fish tank also shouted, "You hardly hurt Xiao Hong? Open your damned eyes and look! Even the scales on Xiao Hong's back were knocked off. Do you have any idea how much Madam adores Xiao Hong? Can you bear the responsibility now that it has been reduced to this state?"

Lu Sanniang knew there would be no peaceful end to today's matter. She understood Madam Hua's temperament all too well; whenever Madam Hua gained the upper hand, she would press it relentlessly. Moreover, Madam Hua had always disliked her. Now that she had found such an opportunity, how could she possibly let it pass? Lu Sanniang hurriedly pleaded, "Elder Sister, I was the one who injured this red carp. Whether Elder Sister wishes to beat or punish me, this Younger Sister will submit."

"Sanniang, do you take your elder sister for a fool?" Madam Hua stared coldly at Lu Sanniang and parted her red lips. "You are an Emperor Realm Master, while Xiao Hong is only a Fifth-Tier Yao Beast. If you had truly struck it, how could Xiao Hong still be alive?"

"It really was me!"

"Enough. This Madam knows perfectly well who injured it!" Madam Hua's gaze passed over Lu Sanniang and fixed on Lu Yuqin's face. "Sanniang, you have failed to discipline your daughter, so as your elder sister, I will properly discipline her for you. Consider it a token of my goodwill. There is no need to thank me!"

"Elder Sister!" Lu Sanniang paled in alarm and hurriedly shielded Lu Yuqin. Lu Yuqin's beautiful face also went white. How could she not realize that she had caused a great disaster?

Yet she had not done it deliberately. While she and her mother were gathering Luminous Pearls beneath the sea, a huge red carp had suddenly swum toward them. Startled, she had casually struck it with her palm. Who could have known that one strike would cause such trouble? Had she known, she would never have injured the red carp no matter what.

"Don't bully my mother!" Although Lu Yuqin was afraid, she still shouted back.

"I'm bullying your mother?" Madam Hua sneered. "A child spouting shameless lies. Slap her mouth!"

"Yes, Madam!" The woman who had remained silent until now stepped forward. In just a few strides, she arrived before Lu Sanniang, stared at her coldly, and shouted, "Get lost!"

Lu Sanniang refused to move. She merely gazed pleadingly at Madam Hua and shook her head again and again.

Madam Hua said, "Sanniang, you know my temper. If you do not interfere, I will merely teach her a little lesson. But if you dare obstruct me, it will not end with a mere lesson."

Lu Sanniang's expression changed slightly at these words.

"Get lost already!" The woman reached out and dragged Lu Sanniang aside. Her cultivation was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, far below Lu Sanniang's, but Lu Sanniang clearly did not dare resist. The pull sent her staggering to one side, and she looked toward Lu Yuqin with tears in her eyes.

She knew her daughter could not avoid this vicious beating. She could only hope Madam Hua would know when to stop.

"Mother!" Lu Yuqin cried softly.

The woman raised her hand high, a sinister smile hanging from her lips, then viciously swung it toward the face that bore a five- or six-tenths resemblance to Lu Sanniang's.

Origin Qi surged through her palm, concealing vicious force within it. Lu Yuqin's cultivation was low, so how could she withstand such a blow? If this slap landed, it would undoubtedly leave her skin split and flesh torn.

Lu Sanniang clenched her delicate fists so tightly that her nails dug into her palms without her noticing. Her eyes reddened as she forced herself to remain where she was instead of rushing forward.

She knew that if she rushed over, both mother and daughter would likely die here today. Madam Hua would never spare either of them. If she did not intervene, perhaps her daughter might still have a chance to survive.

Unable to bear watching, she turned her head away as tears slid down her cheeks.

Pa...

A crisp sound rang out.

A scream followed immediately afterward.

The entire world suddenly fell silent.

Lu Sanniang's heart bled, pain wracking her in spasms. Although she had never wanted her daughter to be born, the girl was still the child she had carried for ten months. They had depended on each other all these years, and her daughter was her very life.

She had never had the heart to hit or scold her, and could not even bear to let anyone touch a single hair on her head. Yet today, someone had slapped her, right in front of her own mother.

She hated her own helplessness. She hated Madam Hua's tyranny and utter unreasonableness. She felt that all of this was her fault.

Her tears fell to the ground like pearls from a broken string.

Lu Sanniang turned back and looked anxiously toward Lu Yuqin, terrified that she would see a sight she could not bear.

The next moment, she froze.

Lu Yuqin was completely unharmed. Although her face was somewhat frightened, she showed no sign of injury. Instead, the woman who had tried to strike her had a clear five-fingered handprint across her cheek. The cheek was bright red, and a trace of blood even leaked from the corner of her mouth.

That slap had clearly carried considerable force, and the woman's cheek quickly swelled.

Her daughter was fine? Someone else had been struck instead?

Lu Sanniang's mind went blank. She had no idea what had just happened.

The woman who had been struck was clearly dumbfounded as well. She stood rooted to the spot with a blank expression. She could not understand how she had clearly been the one striking someone else, and her palm had even come down, yet she was somehow the one who had been hit.

Yuan Wu stared blankly.

Madam Hua and the woman holding the fish tank also stared blankly.

Everyone was dumbfounded.

Madam Hua's expression soon darkened, however. A venomous light flashed in her long, narrow phoenix eyes as she stared coldly at the young man standing before Lu Yuqin and asked in a low voice, "Was that your doing?"

Apart from Madam Hua's group, only this young man, Lu Sanniang, and her daughter were present. Since the mother and daughter did not possess such ability, the only explanation was that this young man had acted.

Ever since arriving here, Madam Hua had not spared Yang Kai a single glance. A mere First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator was not worthy of her attention. Although her own cultivation was at the same stage, cultivation was not what mattered most on Dragon Isle.

She had an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon backing her. Apart from the Dragon Clan, who would dare provoke her?

She had never expected this unremarkable young man to dare slight her so openly. Striking one of her people was no different from slapping her own face.

He was simply lawless! Rage blazed in Madam Hua's chest, and she no longer showed Yang Kai the slightest trace of civility.

"It was me," Yang Kai readily admitted.

Hearing this, Lu Sanniang felt grateful, but she also began worrying for Yang Kai. She knew he was highly capable and had even taught someone like Yuan Wu a lesson, but teaching Yuan Wu a lesson was entirely different from what he had just done.

Teaching Yuan Wu a lesson was no great matter, but who could possibly survive after offending Madam Hua?

Growing terribly anxious, she wiped away her tears and hurriedly signaled to Yang Kai with her eyes.

Yang Kai acted as though he had not noticed. He shook out his hand and said with a look of disgust, "I didn't want to hit a woman. There is no satisfaction in it. But vicious women like you deserve to be hit. The only pity is that it dirtied this Young Master’s hand.”

"What did you say?" Madam Hua narrowed her eyes, cold light flashing within them.

Yang Kai cocked his head defiantly. "Are you deaf? Can't you hear what this Young Master said?"

Madam Hua froze, nearly exploding with rage.

He dared speak to her like this? How could he dare speak to her like this? Who had given him the courage? On this Spirit Island, she was practically its mistress. Apart from the Dragon Clan, no one was allowed to speak to her this way.

Madam Hua trembled all over with rage and shouted through gritted teeth, "Brat, you are courting death!"

Yang Kai snorted. “That depends on whether you have the ability. You are nothing but a plaything of the Dragon Clan, yet who knows where you got this sense of superiority from? You made a mountain out of a trivial matter and prattled on for half the day, and you still dare act arrogant before this Young Master? If you dare keep blathering, I’ll hit you too! This Young Master does not like hitting women, but that does not mean I won’t.”

A Dragon Clan plaything? A trivial matter?

Madam Hua's beautiful eyes widened, and she laughed in her fury. "Xiao Hong was injured, and this Madam came to demand justice. Yet you injured one of my people and even dared contradict this Madam? Have you eaten a bear's heart and a leopard's gall?"

"So what if a plaything's pet was injured? Elder Sister Lu already apologized to you, yet you insist on using your position to bully others?"

"You think an apology is enough?" Madam Hua's face was as dark as water. "Whatever Xiao Hong suffered, this Madam will make that little bastard suffer the same!"

Lu Yuqin could not help trembling at these words.

The red carp's injuries were not serious; it had merely lost two scales. But if the same injury were inflicted upon Lu Yuqin, a layer of flesh would have to be scraped from her back.

She was only in her teens, so how could she endure such pain? Her face instantly went pale.

"You certainly know how to settle accounts," Yang Kai scoffed.

"An eye for an eye. It is only right!" Madam Hua declared coldly. Her ample chest heaved violently, clearly showing how thoroughly Yang Kai had enraged her.

Yang Kai frowned and pondered for a moment before nodding. "Fine. What you say does make sense. An eye for an eye is indeed fair!"

Lu Sanniang's beautiful face paled at these words. She thought Yang Kai could no longer withstand Madam Hua's pressure and was preparing to compromise. She did not blame him. She was already endlessly grateful that Yang Kai had kept Lu Yuqin from being hurt, so how could she possibly resent him? Compromising now was also the wise choice. She only feared Madam Hua would refuse to spare even him, causing him to be dragged into their troubles.

Lu Sanniang secretly resolved that no matter how Madam Hua intended to punish her daughter, she would take the punishment in her place. She absolutely could not allow her daughter to suffer the slightest grievance.

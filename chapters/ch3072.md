## Chapter 3072 — Mo Xiaoqi?

The two Great Elders of the Dragon Clan wore rather ugly expressions. The moment Fu Zhun appeared, her gaze swept over Yang Kai, Jiu Feng, and the others before she demanded coldly, "Who was it?"

Fu Qi stepped forward, cupped his fists, and replied, "Second Elder, we are unharmed."

Fu Ling also hurriedly said, "Nothing happened on our side either."

Fu Zhun's slender brows furrowed.

Zhu Yan swept his gaze over the gathered Dragons and said in a deep voice, "Fourth Elder and Fu Chi aren't here."

There were only so many members of the Dragon Clan, and every one of them could be accounted for, so it was easy to notice who was missing.

Zhu Kong was absent because Li Wuyi had used a Divine Ability to pull him into a separate void. Given Zhu Kong's ability, he should not suffer a loss against Li Wuyi.

That left only one possibility: something had happened to Fu Chi. At this thought, Zhu Yan's expression also sank.

Fu Chi was an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon whose strength ranked in the upper-middle reaches of the Dragon Clan. If something truly had happened to him, Dragon Isle's losses today would be far too great. Fu Chi had previously been severely wounded by Yang Kai, so his strength had certainly fallen, but on Dragon Isle, aside from the people before them, who else could kill an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon?

This was what Zhu Yan found most incomprehensible.

Yang Kai pondered for a moment, but still cupped his fists and said, "Great Elder, I have something to report."

Zhu Yan had no favorable impression of him. Strictly speaking, everything that had happened today had been instigated by Yang Kai. Although Mo Huang's appearance had little to do with him, Yang Kai was still the one who had stirred up this pool of muddy water that was Dragon Isle. If Yang Kai had not been here drawing their attention, they would not have failed to notice Mo Huang infiltrating Dragon Isle, allowing him to trespass into the Dragon Tomb and violate the Dragon Clan's rules.

However, the Great Elder had a fairly mild temperament, so he still nodded and asked, "What is it?"

Yang Kai said solemnly, "Wu Kuang may be on Dragon Isle!"

"Wu Kuang?" Zhu Yan frowned. "Which Wu Kuang?"

Yang Kai said, "Could there be anyone else in this world named Wu Kuang?"

Zhu Yan's expression finally changed. "You mean... the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor?"

At these words, everyone could not help gulping. Pair after pair of profoundly shocked eyes turned towards Yang Kai as though they wanted to see if he had gone mad. Few people knew the name Wu Kuang, but the title of Heaven Devourer Great Emperor was simply too renowned.

Even Zhu Qing stared at Yang Kai in disbelief.

Fu Zhun rebuked him even more angrily, "Cease your wild nonsense! Wu Kuang perished many years ago, so how could he possibly appear on my Dragon Isle? Boy, don't spout lies with your eyes wide open."

She had never thought highly of Yang Kai to begin with, and now, with a member of the Dragon Clan having fallen, her mood was even worse. Naturally, she had no kindness to spare for him.

Yang Kai calmly said, "Wu Kuang isn't dead."

Zhu Yan and Fu Zhun were both startled by these words. The former repeatedly shook his head, while the latter looked openly contemptuous, clearly not believing Yang Kai.

Their attitude also made Yang Kai somewhat angry. He was merely providing information he knew and had no ulterior motive. He did not expect the Dragon Clan's gratitude, but at the very least, they did not have to look at him with such disdain.

Displeased by the attitude of the Dragon Clan's two Great Elders, Yang Kai stopped trying to explain. The one who had died was a member of the Dragon Clan anyway, so it had nothing to do with him. The only thing he needed to consider now was how to slip away with Zhu Qing and the others right under Zhu Yan and Fu Zhun's noses.

"Heh heh, he isn't speaking nonsense."

As a resounding shout rang out, a burly figure suddenly landed nearby, cradling a pale-faced woman in his arms.

"Third Elder!" Zhu Qing looked at the woman and cried out in delight.

The many members of the Dragon Clan also looked at Fu Xuan with complicated expressions, their greetings rising one after another.

Fu Xuan turned to look at Zhu Qing, weakly nodding with a smile before fixing her gaze on Yang Kai and examining him from head to toe.

Yang Kai happened to be looking at her as well, and when their eyes met, he could not help freezing in astonishment.

Mo Xiaoqi? Why was she here?

However, in the next instant, Yang Kai realized he had mistaken her for someone else. Although this woman looked seventy to eighty percent similar to Mo Xiaoqi, their temperaments were entirely different. This woman appeared much more mature than Mo Xiaoqi. If Mo Xiaoqi was a flower bud waiting to bloom, then this woman was a flower in full bloom, possessing a mature charm that Mo Xiaoqi lacked.

Most importantly, this woman did not have the butterfly-shaped mark on her face.

The butterfly mark on Mo Xiaoqi's face was a seal, not the birthmark Yang Kai had initially believed it to be. It was a life-saving measure the Beast Great Emperor had placed on her. Back in the Shattered Star Sea, when that seal broke, Mo Xiaoqi had nearly gone berserk, and it was only thanks to Zhang Ruoxi that she had been suppressed.

Thus, this woman was not Mo Xiaoqi, but instead seemed to be one of Mo Xiaoqi's elders or perhaps her elder sister.

However, the way Zhu Qing and the others addressed this woman left him somewhat confused, and he could not work out the relationship between her and Mo Xiaoqi.

What Yang Kai found strangest was the way this woman looked at him. He kept feeling as though she was scrutinizing him.

"Is this the Third Elder of your Dragon Clan?" Too embarrassed to meet her gaze, Yang Kai looked away and sent Zhu Qing a message with his Divine Sense.

"Yes."

"Then the man beside her is..."

"The Beast Great Emperor, Mo Huang!"

Yang Kai's whole body shook. When this man arrived just now, Yang Kai had sensed that he was anything but simple, but he had never imagined that he was the Beast Great Emperor. Yang Kai had never met the Beast Great Emperor and did not know what he looked like. He had only imagined the Beast Great Emperor's appearance based on Mo Xiaoqi's features, but the man before him differed far too much from what he had pictured.

So this was the Beast Great Emperor! One of the supreme Great Emperors who commanded all the beasts beneath Heaven—whose every order was obeyed by the myriad beasts of the world.

As these thoughts flashed through his mind, Yang Kai suddenly seemed to understand something. His expression becoming strange, he continued transmitting, "Did Senior Beast Great Emperor come to Dragon Isle for your Third Elder?"

Zhu Qing replied, "Indeed. Their circumstances are... somewhat similar to ours."

Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead and asked, "Was this why Third Elder was exiled to the Dragon Tomb?"

Zhu Qing nodded lightly, then said, "The main reason, however, was that Third Elder had joined with Senior Beast Great Emperor and already given birth to a child. Without that child, Second Elder would not have exiled her to the Dragon Tomb; after all, she possesses a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline. Second Elder originally thought there was still room to turn the situation around and kept urging her to separate from Senior Beast Great Emperor, but once that child was born, everything changed."

Yang Kai said in a daze, "So Xiaoqi's mother is actually a member of the Dragon Clan."

Yang Kai did not need to ask to know who the child born from the union of this Third Elder of the Dragon Clan and Mo Huang was. There was only one little princess on Spirit Beast Island, and Mo Xiaoqi was the Beast Great Emperor's only descendant. No wonder the two looked seventy to eighty percent alike; as mother and daughter, they naturally shared certain features.

"What did you say?" Zhu Qing turned to look at him.

Yang Kai's expression turned solemn. "Don't worry. Even if we have a child, I won't let you be exiled to the Dragon Tomb."

Zhu Qing's face reddened, and she stopped discussing the matter with him.

How difficult was it for a human and a member of the Dragon Clan to produce a child? The Beast Great Emperor and Fu Xuan had loved each other for a thousand years, yet Fu Xuan had given birth to Mo Xiaoqi only a little over ten years ago. One could easily imagine the degree of difficulty involved.

Throughout those thousand years, Dragon Isle had always opposed the relationship, but because the Beast Great Emperor was involved, the Dragon Clan could not use its power to bully them. It was not until Mo Xiaoqi was born that the Dragon Clan finally made its decision.

On that occasion, the Dragon Clan mobilized in full force and descended upon Spirit Beast Island. Although Mo Huang wished to resist, Fu Xuan voluntarily returned to Dragon Isle with the Dragon Clan. Mo Huang naturally followed her, but in the end, he was outnumbered and driven off Dragon Isle.

This wait lasted more than ten years.

While the two of them quietly conversed, Zhu Yan and Fu Zhun stared at Mo Huang in shock, almost believing they had misheard him.

Mo Huang sneered, "Your Dragon Clan shuts itself away, remains blindly arrogant, and pays no attention to major events in the outside world, so naturally, you think everyone else is deceiving you."

"What do you mean?" Fu Zhun stared at him coldly.

Zhu Yan also frowned. "Are you saying Wu Kuang truly isn't dead?"

Mo Huang declared in a deep voice, "Wu Kuang has been resurrected!"

"What?" This time, Zhu Yan and Fu Zhun's expressions changed in unison, and the many members of the Dragon Clan were also horrified.

"Is this information reliable?" Zhu Yan stared solemnly at Mo Huang. If this were true, then the entire world would likely undergo a tremendous upheaval. The Dragon Clan had also participated in the war among the Great Emperors back then, and Zhu Yan had been one of those involved. Only those who had directly faced Wu Kuang knew just how terrifying the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor truly was.

Mo Huang said, "This Lord had an exchange with him just one year ago. Do you think this information is reliable?"

"An exchange..." Zhu Yan furrowed his brow deeply, unable to understand exactly what he meant. The Heaven Devourer Great Emperor was not someone with whom one could easily have an exchange, and the Beast Great Emperor had grievances with him. If they met, would they not immediately start fighting? How could they possibly sit down and talk?

Mo Huang said impatiently, "Wu Kuang's situation is somewhat unusual. He had been hiding in the Origin Sea of the Shattered Star Sea all along, intending to gather Essence-Blood, reconstruct his body, and resurrect himself, and he had nearly succeeded. At the critical moment, however, Duan Hongchen barged in and fought a great battle with him. In the end, both sides suffered. Wu Kuang failed to reconstruct his body and resurrect himself, but he took possession of half of Duan Hongchen's body."

"Duan Hongchen is also involved in this matter?"

Mo Huang said, "After the Shattered Star Sea, Old Duan began trying to seal Wu Kuang's Divine Soul. He made a trip to Soul Palace, but even that person from Soul Palace had no solution. Before leaving, Old Duan stayed on my Spirit Beast Island for a time, which is why I know about his situation."

Spirit Beast Island and Soul Palace were both in the Eastern Territory, so stopping at Spirit Beast Island after departing Soul Palace was conveniently along the way.

"Even that person from Soul Palace had no solution?" Zhu Yan looked astonished.

If there was anyone in this world whose research into the Divine Soul was deepest and whose comprehension of it was most thorough, it could only be that person from Soul Palace. Yet even he was unable to deal with Duan Hongchen's condition, so there was likely no one else in the world capable of doing so.

"So, Wu Kuang truly isn't dead." Mo Huang looked disdainfully at the Dragon Clan's two Great Elders. "Your Dragon Clan should go out more often, look around, and learn about major events in the world."

That said, only a handful of people in the entire world knew about the matter between Wu Kuang and Duan Hongchen, so even if the Dragon Clan really did venture outside, they would not necessarily be able to discover any news.

## Chapter 3077 — Elders of Past Generations Appear Together

In the blink of an eye, the figures of more than ten Great Dragons gathered around Yang Kai. None of these Great Dragons had a physical body; they were formed from extremely pure Origin Power. Even more terrifying, however, was their size.

Even the smallest among them was forty zhang long!

The largest was nearly a hundred zhang long. Its enormous body coiled and swam through the air, filling the great hall and placing everyone under indescribably terrifying pressure.

These Great Dragons seemed to have leapt out of an ancient painting and inexplicably descended upon this place.

Gulp...

The sounds of people swallowing rang out one after another. The expressions of the many Dragon Clan members finally changed. Under that supremely terrifying Dragon Pressure, even remaining on their feet seemed extremely difficult. Every one of them trembled like chaff in a sieve, their faces pale.

The Dragon Tomb was where members of the Dragon Clan went after death, but not every Dragon Clan member was laid to rest there. After some members of the Dragon Clan fell, they would instead be enshrined in the Dragon Temple as Honored Elders, leaving behind their grace and protection for later generations.

Every member of the Dragon Clan qualified to enter the Dragon Temple after death had been the finest of their generation and a leader of the Dragon Clan.

In other words, only the Great Elder of each generation was qualified to be enshrined in the Dragon Temple as an Honored Elder after death! Among the current generation of the Dragon Clan, only Zhu Yan possessed such qualifications. Even Fu Zhun did not.

The Great Dragon figures that had suddenly surged in from every direction within the Dragon Temple were none other than the Dragon Clan leaders of past generations who had been enshrined here as Honored Elders.

Zhu Yan even saw a familiar figure among them. It was a Yellow Dragon some forty or fifty zhang long. His thoughts instantly drifted back tens of thousands of years as he recalled events from his youth.

Back then, he had grown strong under this lord's guidance. After this lord fell, Zhu Yan had succeeded him as the Dragon Clan's Great Elder.

He stared blankly at the Yellow Dragon, his lips trembling involuntarily. Deep in his heart, he yearned for this lord, who had fallen tens of thousands of years ago, to raise his head and look at him, to praise him for doing well. To his disappointment, however, this lord had lowered the head he had never bowed in life and now floated quietly behind Yang Kai.

"The First-Generation Elder..." Fu Zhun stared vacantly at the only Great Dragon present whose body reached a full hundred zhang. Though they had lived in eras separated by endless years, she recognized the Great Dragon's identity at a glance.

Only a Dragon of that era could have possessed such imposing might!

As time passed, however, the Dragon Clan declined further and further. By their generation, their true Great Dragon forms were only forty or fifty zhang long, barely half the size of the First Generation.

The one slightly smaller than the First-Generation Elder was probably the Second-Generation Elder. The smaller ones should be the Third-Generation Elder, the Fourth, the Fifth...

The Dragon Clan's Great Elders from more than ten generations seemed to have heard a summons from time immemorial and gathered together in one hall. The sight left everyone shaken to their very souls.

"How dare you do this!" After standing stunned for a long time, Fu Zhun suddenly clenched her teeth and shouted. Her fair face twisted, and her eyes stabbed at Yang Kai with such hatred that they seemed intent on riddling him with holes. "How dare you disturb the ancestral spirits of my Dragon Clan!"

Though her tone was fierce, suppressed by the might of the entire Dragon Temple and the Dragon Pressure of more than ten generations of Dragon Clan Great Elders, Fu Zhun no longer possessed her former overbearing arrogance. Now, she looked like an enraged kitten waving its claws, an utterly laughable sight.

Her fury was understandable. The Dragon Temple was where the Dragon Clan's Great Elders of past generations were enshrined as Honored Elders, the foundation that protected the Dragon Clan and allowed it to endure. These predecessors had fallen long ago and possessed no will or thought of their own. Ordinarily, even if she and the Great Elder used a Secret Technique, they could not summon them.

Yet at Yang Kai's command, Elders from more than ten generations had emerged from their slumber together.

Every member of the Dragon Clan felt deeply insulted.

Mo Huang also gazed at Yang Kai in amazement, clicking his tongue in wonder. He had never imagined Yang Kai could accomplish such a feat. His gaze swept over the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin, and he knew that the reason for all of this had to lie with this strange Dragon Clan Origin.

"This Junior had no other choice." Yang Kai looked at Fu Zhun with an unruly expression, but he did not gloat excessively or take the opportunity to do anything to her.

Had he not been forced into a corner, he would not have wanted to cause such a commotion either.

The moment he stepped into the Dragon Temple, he had suddenly felt the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin within him become active, as though it had formed a close connection with something in his surroundings. Commanding and summoning the ancestral spirits of the Dragon Clan's Great Elders from past generations had been nothing more than an instinctive action.

"What a fine claim of having no other choice." Fu Zhun sneered repeatedly. "What do you plan to do now? Slaughter my Dragon Clan?"

"That's not a bad suggestion. Since we've already become enemies, killing all of you first would spare me any future trouble!" As Yang Kai spoke, his cold eyes swept around the hall as if he were seriously considering whether it was feasible.

The expression of every Dragon Clan member his gaze landed on changed. The blood in their bodies seemed to freeze as the aura of death descended over their heads.

Fu Zhun pursed her red lips, her face filled with stubborn defiance. Zhu Yan's expression also became solemn.

If Yang Kai borrowed the power of the entire Dragon Temple to deal with them now, then even if Zhu Yan and Fu Zhun joined forces, they would likely have no choice but to flee at the mere sight of him. Their only hope of victory would be to kill Yang Kai in a single strike.

Yet under the watch of more than ten generations of Dragon Clan Great Elders, even Zhu Yan had no opportunity to attack Yang Kai. Only they knew just how powerful the Dragon Temple was.

This special place and its special power had created an existence that could be called invincible.

Mo Huang opened his mouth as though he wanted to offer some advice.

Although he also found this group of Dragon Clan members displeasing, he had never thought of killing any of them. There was not actually any great hatred between them. He did not know what Yang Kai's temperament was like, but if Fu Zhun's words proved true, the consequences would be unimaginable.

No matter what, he did not wish to see any member of the Dragon Clan come to harm here. This was the Dragon Temple, one of the Dragon Clan's two great foundations. If the Dragon Clan were massacred here, that would truly be a colossal joke.

Before Mo Huang could speak, Yang Kai's murderous air suddenly receded, and he became harmless-looking in an instant. “Everyone here is Qing’er’s family, so let’s put aside all this talk of fighting and killing. Whatever the matter, we can sit down and discuss it properly. What do you think, Great Elder?”

Zhu Yan’s cheek twitched as he said in a deep voice, “The strong prey upon the weak, and the victor becomes king while the loser is branded a bandit. Right now, your fist is the biggest—so what you say goes.”

Yang Kai said, "Great Elder seems to have some objections to this Junior, but that does not matter. This Junior only wishes to make a trip to the lower plane Star Domain and asks for nothing else. I hope Great Elder will grant this request... Mm, since you aren't saying anything, I'll take that as your agreement. Great Elder truly understands the greater good. This Junior offers his thanks."

The corner of Zhu Yan's mouth twitched even more violently. He lowered his eyes and said nothing. Yang Kai had been talking to himself from beginning to end, yet he still had the nerve to claim Zhu Yan understood the greater good.

There was no time to lose. The Void Passage Wu Kuang had torn open would close completely before long, so Yang Kai had no time to dally here. If he wanted to use this Void Passage to reach the lower plane Star Domain, he had to leave as soon as possible.

He turned to look at Zhu Qing. "You stay here."

Zhu Qing opened her mouth, but Yang Kai stepped forward, wrapped an arm around her, and kissed her in full view of everyone.

The expressions of the crowd became exceedingly strange. They felt awkward whether they watched or looked away, so they all turned their gazes aside. This brat was simply too shameless, actually committing such a filthy act in a place as sacred as the Dragon Temple.

Had it been anyone else, they would have been killed even if they had a hundred lives.

Zhu Qing's mouth was sealed, leaving her unable to speak. When their lips parted, her face was bright red, and Yang Kai's instruction sounded beside her ear: "Wait for me."

It was not that he did not want to take Zhu Qing with him, but the road ahead on this journey was uncertain. How could he bear to make her suffer hardship with him? Fu Chi was dead, so there would be no problem with Zhu Qing remaining on Dragon Isle.

He then turned to Fu Zhun and said, “Second Elder, I have offended you greatly today. I hope a person of your stature will not stoop to holding the mistakes of a junior against him. I am young and hot-blooded, so I ask for your forbearance. Another day, I will personally come to offer my apologies.”

Since Zhu Qing was staying behind, he had no choice but to soften his stance, lest this woman make things difficult for her.

His words could also be understood as a threat. If he could break into Dragon Isle once, he could do so a second time. If Zhu Qing truly suffered any grievance after he left, then Yang Kai would likely return to Dragon Isle with a very different attitude. At that time, he would not come to apologize, but to demand an accounting.

Fu Zhun had clearly understood his meaning and coldly snorted.

Yang Kai then looked at Fu Xuan, who had remained nestled in Mo Huang's arms, and smiled faintly. "I must also thank Third Elder for what happened today. Since this is our first meeting, I didn't prepare anything, so please accept this as a gift of thanks!"

As he spoke, he formed a hand seal with one hand, and a Dragon Roar immediately rang out.

At the same time, a streak of light suddenly appeared from the void and shot toward Fu Xuan like lightning.

Mo Huang frowned. Although he could have intercepted it, he sensed no malice from Yang Kai and allowed him to act.

The streak of light darted into Fu Xuan's body, and a sound rang out as though something had shattered.

Zhu Yan's gaze toward Yang Kai softened somewhat, but Fu Zhun's expression grew even uglier.

Fu Xuan's face, which had remained pale all this time, finally regained a trace of color. Her beautiful eyes shone with delight as she looked at Yang Kai with immense gratitude.

Yang Kai nodded faintly to her, turned around without further hesitation, and stepped into the Void Passage. In the blink of an eye, he vanished from sight.

Only after he left did the figures of the Dragon Clan Elders from more than ten generations who had been summoned slowly fade away, as though they had never appeared.

The terrifying pressure vanished without a trace, and faint sounds of exhalation rang out one after another. The many Dragon Clan members looked at one another, unexpectedly feeling as though they had survived a great calamity.

Mo Huang, however, now gazed at Fu Xuan with tremendous delight and asked in a low voice, "Could that just now have been..."

Fu Xuan nodded gently. "The Dragon Binding Seal has been lifted."

The Dragon Binding Seal was one of the Dragon Clan's Secret Techniques and had always been used only on criminals of the Dragon Clan. Once the Dragon Binding Seal was placed on someone, the Dragon Clan Origin within their body would be completely suppressed.

When Fu Xuan was exiled to the Dragon Tomb more than a dozen years ago, the Dragon Binding Seal had been placed on her.

This Secret Technique was not only a punishment for members of the Dragon Clan who had committed wrongdoing, but also the mark of a Dragon Clan criminal. The Secret Technique shackling Fu Xuan was something even Mo Huang had been unable to remove.

Though he could forcibly bring Fu Xuan out of the Dragon Tomb, as long as the Dragon Binding Seal remained, she would continue to weaken with each passing day until she died.

He had never found a good way to deal with the Dragon Binding Seal.

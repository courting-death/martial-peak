## Chapter 3106 — Have You Switched to Vegetarian Food?

A moment later, the bamboo house's door opened again. Under everyone's watchful eyes, Gongyang Xi stepped out, his face slightly pale.

He turned around, respectfully closed the door behind him, then bowed. "I have disturbed Sir. Please rest, Sir."

The eyes of everyone present nearly popped out, and the several hundred elites of the Mad Dragon Pavilion simply could not believe what they were seeing.

"Go!" Gongyang Xi barked. His figure flickered, transformed into a streak of light, and shot into the distance.

Although the people of Mad Dragon Pavilion were full of doubt and confusion, none of them dared ask questions. One after another, they summoned their flying Secret Treasures and used their Divine Abilities to leave. In the blink of an eye, not one of the several hundred people who had arrived in such grand fashion remained, and peace returned to the solitary peak.

Only after they were a hundred li away did Gongyang Xi, flying at the very front, sway slightly. He opened his mouth, and a cloud of blood mist sprayed out. His aura instantly withered to an extreme, and his already aged face became even more decrepit, as if he might die at any moment.

"Pavilion Master!" Several Elders turned pale with fright and hurriedly gathered around him.

What exactly had happened inside that bamboo house? What sort of exchange had the Pavilion Master had with that thief? Why had he left without a word after coming out, and why did he now look so badly injured?

All these mysteries threw their thoughts into disorder.

Gongyang Xi barely steadied himself, then waved a hand to indicate that he was in no serious danger.

"Pavilion Master, that person..." One Elder truly could not suppress his curiosity and could not help asking.

"Do not ask." A trace of terror flashed across Gongyang Xi's face.

"But our Sect's Ancestral Treasure..."

"We do not want it anymore!" A flash of painful resolve crossed Gongyang Xi's eyes. "Just treat it as... something we never had."

"Ah..." A burst of startled cries rang out.

Just who was that person, to frighten their own Pavilion Master to such an extent that he was no longer prepared to demand back the Ancestral Treasure passed down for several tens of thousands of years? The Elders no longer dared think further. They feared the thoughts that surfaced in their minds would make them lose their Martial Dao heart.

...

Outside the bamboo house, He Yunxiang's red lips were slightly parted, as if someone had stuffed two invisible fingers into her mouth. She stared blankly as the people of Mad Dragon Pavilion left, feeling as if her mind could no longer keep up.

Was this not one of the top Sects in the Ancestral Domain? How could they be so timid and fearful when trouble came? If not for their obvious strength, He Yunxiang would almost have suspected that there was another force called Mad Dragon Pavilion somewhere in the Ancestral Domain.

Yang Kai's eyelids had also been twitching all this time. If he could, he truly wanted to turn around and leave.

But the matter he sought was very likely tied to the person inside the hut. He could not bring himself to leave just like that. After hesitating for a long while, he finally clenched his teeth and made up his mind. For the sake of returning to his homeland, even if there was a pile of shit inside, he still had to step in it!

"Stay here. I will be back in a moment." Yang Kai patted Liu Yan on the head.

Liu Yan grabbed his sleeve and refused to let go.

"Be good!" Yang Kai glared at her.

"No!" Liu Yan refused to back down, glaring back with eyes even wider than his.

Yang Kai said, “One must not put all one’s eggs in one basket. Later, listen for me smashing a cup as the signal. If anything seems wrong, immediately rush in and roast that person into a suckling pig.”

Liu Yan nodded and let him go. "Fine then. Be careful."

"Mm." Before that person, what use was being careful?

He stepped forward and came to the bamboo house. Yang Kai coughed lightly, reached out, knocked on the door, and called loudly, "Is anyone home?"

The originally solemn atmosphere suddenly became somewhat comical, and He Yunxiang could not help laughing. She covered her mouth just in time to keep herself from making a sound.

No one inside the house responded. Yang Kai straightened his clothes and said apologetically, "Excuse me!"

He pushed the door open and entered. As before, the door closed by itself.

The light shifted from bright to dim. A person sat cross-legged on a prayer mat ahead, smiling at Yang Kai.

Their eyes met, and Yang Kai raised his vigilance to its limit, though his expression remained deliberately calm. He casually cupped his fist. "Brother Wu, I trust you have been well since we last met." If you are willing to use the alias Wu Chen, then I will call you Brother Wu. This Young Master will never call you Senior Wu, you greatest tumor in the Star Domain for tens of thousands of years!

Wu Kuang did not mind in the slightest and extended a hand. "Sit."

Yang Kai said, "I am fine standing. Quite fine." The strength throughout his body was gathered but unreleased, ready at any moment for a fierce fight with the person before him.

Wu Kuang said, "But this Lord has to tilt his head up to speak with you."

The corner of Yang Kai's mouth lifted. After thinking it over, he decided he was not willing to take advantage of such a thing, lest he provoke the man into shame and anger, so he sat down as told.

The two sat facing each other. Wu Kuang took out tea ware and slowly brewed tea.

Yang Kai immediately felt somewhat overwhelmed by the honor. Regardless of whether this person was the greatest tumor in the Star Domain for tens of thousands of years, and regardless of how many Star Domains he had once plunged into chaos or how many living beings he had slaughtered, he was, after all, the unprecedented number one figure of the Star Domain. He had even slaughtered several Great Emperors, yet now he was actually brewing tea for Yang Kai. That could certainly be counted as an honor.

His hostility could not help easing considerably. Such bearing truly had the style of an expert. In the future, telling others he had once drunk tea with Wu Kuang would also be a great matter of face.

"How has Young Friend come to appear here?" Wu Kuang asked casually.

To encounter Yang Kai in a place like this was truly beyond his expectations.

Yang Kai said with a solemn face, "It is a long story, but originally, I came here to hunt you down."

Wu Kuang smiled faintly. "And now?"

"Will you let me kill you?" Yang Kai asked.

"That would depend on whether Young Friend has the ability."

Yang Kai pondered seriously for a moment, then shook his head. "Even if I had the ability, I am afraid I still could not kill you." Killing Wu Kuang would be the same as killing Duan Hongchen, and the price was too great. He immediately said with righteous indignation, "Just you wait. When this Young Master finds a way one day to force you out of Senior Hongchen's body, that will be the day you die!"

"Then you will have to cultivate diligently." Wu Kuang smiled faintly.

Yang Kai felt deeply uncomfortable. This meeting was completely different from what he had imagined. Are you not someone who kills people like scything hemp? Why put on the tolerance of a wise old man? It made him feel as if he had not even thrown a punch, and the other party had already set cotton in front of him to block it.

It would be far more satisfying if they simply tore off all pretense and fought fiercely, no matter who lived or died.

"Have you switched to vegetarian food?" Yang Kai suddenly asked.

"What?" Wu Kuang looked puzzled.

“Those people from the Mad Dragon Pavilion, why did you not devour them?” To be honest, he had also been utterly astonished just now. That group from the Mad Dragon Pavilion had actually been able to leave safely, which was simply inconceivable. Calling it ancestral luck would be putting it mildly; their ancestral graves were practically shooting flames.

Wu Kuang absolutely could not have turned vegetarian. The Heaven Devouring Battle Art was his core cultivation art, and just half a year ago, he had devoured a dragon on Dragon Isle.

"Why eat something that can only get stuck between my teeth?"

Yang Kai nodded in deep agreement, then said, "Then why did you come to the Ancestral Domain? When you went to Dragon Isle, was your original plan to come here?"

Wu Kuang said, "To retrieve some things I left here and make some preparations for the future."

"The future?" Yang Kai frowned.

"Future matters can wait until the future." The tea was ready. Wu Kuang took a cup for himself and sipped it slowly.

Yang Kai took one look and saw there was not even a teacup for him, so he simply picked up the teapot and took a fierce drink. Only then did he say, "You did not eat those people not because you did not want to, but because you could not, right?"

Wu Kuang's hand paused. A sharp light flickered in his eyes as he stared at Yang Kai. "What do you mean by that?" In this world, was there still anything he could not do?

"Even the smallest mosquito is still meat, and besides, you are still at a critical point in your recovery. You hid in this lower plane precisely because you wanted to devour more strength, did you not?"

"And then?" Wu Kuang looked at him with a smile, neither confirming nor denying it.

Yang Kai said, "Senior Duan would never tolerate such a thing. He must have acted to stop you..." As he spoke, Yang Kai suddenly frowned. He leaned back slightly and studied Wu Kuang carefully.

In that instant, an inconceivable thought leapt into his mind.

Wu Kuang's smile did not fade.

Yang Kai shook all over and said hesitantly, "Senior... Duan?"

Wu Kuang's smile turned into a bitter smile.

Yang Kai said in disbelief, "It really is Senior Duan?"

Wu Kuang suddenly laughed loudly, “Old Duan, I told you, you cannot learn this Lord’s carefree style. Sure enough, you tried to paint a tiger and ended up with a dog instead.” Then his voice turned deep, “Shut up.”

Cold sweat streamed down Yang Kai's head. If he had only been somewhat suspicious just now, then now he could be certain. The one who had been speaking with him ever since he entered the house was not Wu Kuang, but Duan Hongchen.

His face abruptly darkened, and he demanded, "Senior Duan, what is going on?"

He had always thought Duan Hongchen had been suppressed by Wu Kuang. Otherwise, how could he have run to Dragon Isle to slaughter dragons? And how could he have hidden in this lower plane Star Domain? But now it seemed that was not the case. Duan Hongchen had not been completely suppressed; he still had some freedom.

Judging even from the exchange between Wu Kuang and Duan Hongchen, the relationship between the two did not seem so terrible anymore.

No matter what evil deeds Wu Kuang committed, Yang Kai would not find them excessive. But if those deeds involved Duan Hongchen, then the situation was completely different.

Duan Hongchen clearly knew what Yang Kai was angry about and sighed, “I only broke free after he arrived here. As for what happened before, I did not deliberately conceal it.”

Yang Kai let out a breath. That was more like it. If Duan Hongchen had truly colluded with Wu Kuang and joined him in villainy, then the trouble would be great.

Seeing Yang Kai’s expression shift, now fierce, now ill-intentioned, Duan Hongchen said, “Stop getting crooked ideas. Even if you and I joined forces now, we still could not kill him. I was able to come out for a breath of air only because he allowed it. If he truly suppressed me with all his strength, I could not possibly break free."

Yang Kai felt stifled, but only then did he set aside the thought in his heart. Helplessly, he said, "Then what do we do?"

Duan Hongchen said, "Let nature take its course. However, since we have the fate to meet today, this old man does have one matter to entrust to you."

Yang Kai said solemnly, "Senior, if you have any instructions, please just say them."

Duan Hongchen said, "If you return to the Star Realm, someone will certainly come ask you about Wu Kuang's whereabouts. Please do not tell anyone."

Yang Kai asked curiously, "Why is that?" He kept feeling that Duan Hongchen's attitude toward Wu Kuang had changed considerably. It was no longer as sharply opposed as before, and even carried a hint of compromise.

Why? What was happening? Just what kind of exchange had the two Great Emperors had? And what agreement had they reached? It had actually made Duan Hongchen deliberately help Wu Kuang conceal his whereabouts.

## Chapter 3035 — Radiant Night Pearl

Inside the great hall, the sounds gradually subsided.

Outside the great hall, Yang Kai stared in dumbfounded silence.

Time seemed to freeze at that instant.

The woman's slightly narrowed eyes met Yang Kai's gaze as he peered inside, catching him in the act.

Yang Kai's heart lurched, and he inwardly cried out in alarm.

He had weathered countless storms, but what he had witnessed today was still difficult to believe, leaving his mind shaken. Only now did he see clearly that the Dragon Clan member inside the great hall was actually Fu Ling, whom he had met just a few days ago.

Fu Ling's narrowed eyes slowly became sharp and piercing. She had clearly spotted Yang Kai.

How could Yang Kai dare remain? The Laws of Space surged, and with a flicker, he vanished from the spot.

Fu Ling frowned and slowly rose to her feet. A glistening trail of moisture slid down her thigh, but she paid it no mind. Without making any attempt to cover her body, she walked straight outside.

She glanced at the spot where Yang Kai had been standing, but found nothing. Fu Ling's brow furrowed even more deeply. Turning to gaze into the distance, she let out a soft snort.

...

Above the sea, Yang Kai flew through the air with a somewhat awkward expression.

He had never expected to encounter such a thing. He had not meant to spy on anyone, but the scene had been so intense that he could not help watching a little longer, accidentally exposing himself in the process. He had no idea whether Fu Ling had noticed him.

She probably had; otherwise, the change in her gaze would not have been so obvious.

Would she come looking for trouble? Given the Dragon Clan's temperament, that was almost certain. However, even if Fu Ling wanted to settle accounts with him, she probably did not know who he was. When they met a few days ago, she had paid him no particular attention and likely did not remember an insignificant nobody like him.

So there was no need to worry too much.

Still, although that female dragon led a rather debauched life, her figure and appearance were truly exceptional. She was a Divine Spirit of the Dragon Clan, after all; naturally, she could hardly be ordinary. Recalling what he had just seen, Yang Kai's heart could not help pounding.

Lost in wild thoughts along the way, he investigated several more islands but still found nothing. Yang Kai could only return for the time being.

He had already been gone for several days and did not know how Lu Sanniang was faring. He was still somewhat worried that Yuan Wu might cause trouble for her and her daughter.

Half a day later, Yang Kai returned to the island and landed. He looked around but saw no sign of Lu Sanniang or her daughter and had no idea whether they had gone into hiding or somewhere else.

Just as Yang Kai was about to search for them, the sound of splashing suddenly came from the nearby sea, followed by clear, melodious laughter.

Yang Kai turned his head and saw two graceful figures slowly rising from the sea.

They were Lu Sanniang and her daughter. He had no idea what they had gone into the sea to do, but they were now returning.

Both mother and daughter were soaking wet, their clothes clinging tightly to their bodies and outlining every inch of their graceful curves. One was full and mature, while the other was youthful and alluring.

Yang Kai's eyesight was exceptionally sharp. Though they were still some distance away, a single glance allowed him to take in the entire beautiful scene.

He unobtrusively shifted his gaze elsewhere. Seeing that they were unharmed, Yang Kai also put his worries to rest.

The laughter continued. It was Lu Yuqin's voice. Whatever had made the little girl so happy, she could not stop laughing.

Suddenly, the laughter ceased. They had probably noticed Yang Kai. The mother and daughter exchanged a glance, hurriedly circulated their strength to dry the seawater from their bodies, then flew over hand in hand.

"Brother Yang!" Lu Sanniang called out as she and her daughter landed not far from Yang Kai.

Perhaps embarrassed by what had just happened, Lu Yuqin hid behind her mother, unwilling to show her face, her cheeks burning.

Only then did Yang Kai look toward them. He nodded lightly and smiled. "Elder Sister Lu seems to be in fine spirits."

He had assumed the mother and daughter had gone into the sea to play.

Lu Sanniang pursed her lips in a smile and asked, "Did you gain anything from your trip, Brother Yang?"

Although she did not know why Yang Kai had gone out, she knew he must have had some purpose. Since he had not brought it up himself, however, it was not appropriate for her to question him too closely.

"I was merely looking around," Yang Kai replied.

Lu Sanniang said, "The scenery in the Dragon Palace is quite lovely. Please wait a moment, Brother Yang. I need to deliver something, then we can talk when I return."

Yang Kai asked in puzzlement, "What are you delivering?"

"This!" As Lu Sanniang spoke, she flipped her wrist, and a luminous pearl suddenly appeared in her palm. It was quite large, as big as a man's fist. More remarkably, its surface was smooth and its shape perfectly round, clearly marking it as a superior specimen. Its gentle glow also imparted an exceptionally warm feeling.

Yang Kai's eyes lit up at the sight.

This kind of luminous pearl had no value for Martial Dao cultivation, but many women were extremely fond of them. There was no other reason: luminous pearls were ideal for illumination, far surpassing the strange stones commonly used as light sources.

"This pearl is quite good," Yang Kai praised with a nod.

Lu Sanniang said, "This is a specialty of the Dragon Palace, a Radiant Night Pearl. Such things are rarely seen in the outside world."

"Who are you giving it to?"

Lu Sanniang's expression became somewhat unnatural. After hesitating for a moment, however, she still answered, "To Yuan Wu..."

Yang Kai's face abruptly sank. "Did he assign you a task?"

When Yang Kai left earlier, all five hundred people had been given tasks except Yang Kai, Lu Sanniang, and her daughter. It was not that Yuan Wu had shown Yang Kai any respect; he simply did not dare order the three of them around after suffering such a heavy loss at Yang Kai's hands.

Yang Kai had never expected Yuan Wu to pull something like this after he left, and he could not help feeling angry.

It seemed his earlier lesson had not been enough! Yang Kai immediately turned toward the center of the island.

Seeing his expression, how could Lu Sanniang not know what he was thinking? She hurriedly said, "It isn't like that. He didn't force me to do anything."

"Then why are you..." Yang Kai frowned at her.

Lu Sanniang said, "I volunteered for the task myself."

She volunteered? Why?

Yang Kai said, "There was really no need for you to do this. He wouldn't dare do anything to you."

"I know." Lu Sanniang smiled bitterly. "But I want to leave this place as soon as possible. The Dragon Clan gave us a one-month deadline. If we can finish that palace sooner, we can also leave sooner."

With this explanation, Yang Kai immediately understood what she intended.

This was the place of her nightmares. Every extra moment she remained here subjected her to the torment of those painful memories. Naturally, she was unwilling to stay long and only wanted to leave as soon as possible.

Once he understood this, Yang Kai's anger subsided. Since she had volunteered for the work, it was not his place to say anything more.

"What do they need these luminous pearls for? Illumination?"

Lu Sanniang nodded. "That's right. A palace that large will certainly need these pearls as decoration. Besides, I'm very familiar with the surrounding waters and know where such pearls can be found easily, so it isn't a difficult task. I might as well contribute what I can."

Lu Yuqin suddenly poked her head out and said, "Mother found several hundred of them at once."

Lu Sanniang had lived here for two or three hundred years. Even if she had not been able to venture far from this island, she must have explored the nearby area before and was naturally very familiar with these waters. Having her search for luminous pearls was clearly far more efficient than sending anyone else.

"Yuan Wu hasn't made things difficult for you again, has he?" Yang Kai asked, looking at her.

"No." Lu Sanniang shook her head. "His attitude wasn't pleasant, but he didn't cause us any more trouble."

"Good." Yang Kai nodded.

"Please wait a moment, Brother Yang. I'll go deliver the pearls to him." After speaking, Lu Sanniang gave Lu Yuqin a few instructions before flying away.

Yang Kai and Lu Yuqin were left alone. Yang Kai smiled at her, but Lu Yuqin was clearly shy around strangers. She stood there awkwardly without saying a word, her face slightly red.

Yang Kai searched for something to say and chatted with her for a while, but the little girl merely gave a few hesitant, short replies, not daring to speak much with him.

Before long, Lu Sanniang returned with a smile. Looking at Yang Kai, she said, "Yuan Wu probably saw that you had returned, so this time, he didn't even dare give me a sour look."

"Someone like him only bullies the weak and fears the strong," Yang Kai snorted coldly.

He held people like Yuan Wu in utter contempt. The only reason Yuan Wu had escaped with a mere lesson was that he had not truly crossed Yang Kai's line. If he had, the consequences would have been far more severe.

Lu Sanniang's expression suddenly dimmed again, and she said softly, "I heard someone over there say that several of those mining Ice Soul Snow Jade have died. There was even an Emperor Realm Master among them." Lingering fear filled her face. She was clearly grateful that Yang Kai had protected her and her daughter; otherwise, Yuan Wu might well have assigned them to mine Ice Soul Snow Jade.

"Even an Emperor Realm Master died?" Yang Kai asked in astonishment.

He knew that mining Ice Soul Snow Jade was no easy task and certainly involved some danger, but he had never expected people to die. It had only been a few days.

"Mm. I didn't hear many details. I only know that a burst of extreme cold apparently erupted from the Ice Soul Snow Jade lode. Those people couldn't avoid it in time and were simply frozen to death."

Yang Kai asked, "What about Wu Chen?"

Yang Kai could not quite understand that sharp-faced, monkey-cheeked fellow. He had previously helped Yang Kai out of a difficult situation, then later volunteered to mine Ice Soul Snow Jade, helping Yuan Wu recover some of his dignity. He seemed like a selfless do-gooder who was always thinking of others.

Yet Yang Kai always felt there was more to him than met the eye, so he could not help paying closer attention to him.

Lu Sanniang said, "I'm not sure. I didn't see him, so he should still be mining Ice Soul Snow Jade. After all, it is a task assigned by the Dragon Clan, and no one dares neglect it. Dangerous or not, they can only steel themselves and press on."

"That's true." Yang Kai nodded lightly. Of the five hundred people, he alone did not take this matter seriously. Who among the others was not pushing themselves to the limit to complete the Dragon Clan's assignment within a month?

Their lives were tied to the outcome, after all. If they did not work as though their lives depended on it, they could only wait to die.

While he was speaking with Lu Sanniang, a noisy commotion suddenly erupted in the distance. It seemed to be coming from the planned site of the palace, though neither of them knew what had happened.

Yang Kai and Lu Sanniang did not pay much attention at first, but to their surprise, several people soon flew over from that direction, rushing straight toward them.

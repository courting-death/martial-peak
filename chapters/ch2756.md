## Chapter 2756 — Long-Term Cooperation

“Twelfth-Tier Inner Core!” Lou Chi’s eyes widened in shock, blazing with excitement. He snatched the Inner Core from the table, holding it in his hand and examining it closely, nodding repeatedly. “It truly is a Twelfth-Tier Inner Core.”

A Twelfth-Tier Inner Core could only be produced inside a Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast, and a Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast was equivalent to an Emperor Realm existence.

Lou Chi himself was only a First Stage Emperor Realm cultivator. If he truly encountered a Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast, there was no telling who would come out on top—he might very well end up as food in the beast’s bloody maw.

It could be said that Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores were rare treasures throughout the entire Star Realm. The weak could never hope to obtain one, while the strong—even if they managed to acquire such a prize—would never easily part with it. After all, Yao Beast Inner Cores had countless uses; just in alchemy alone, they were coveted by all, drawing countless people to pursue them relentlessly.

“What a pity…” Lou Chi sighed again.

Kang Siran asked, “What’s a pity?”

He was also shocked. Meeting Yang Kai again, he found himself not quite recognizing him. Yang Kai came to discuss such a big deal, and now casually produced a Twelfth-Tier Inner Core. Fortunately, Yang Kai’s personality was still the same, not much changed.

He still remembered when Yang Kai first arrived in Fenglin City. Even if not destitute, he was rather shabby. How did he suddenly become so wealthy? Compared to him, Kang Siran felt his own ten years had been wasted.

“It’s a pity this is just a Twelfth-Tier Lower-Level Yao Beast’s Inner Core, equivalent to the First Stage of the Emperor Realm.” Lou Chi stroked his beard, shaking his head.

Even among Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores, quality and value differed greatly, just as the Emperor Realm had First, Second, and Third Stages. Each increase in quality multiplied the value.

“How much is this Inner Core worth?” Yang Kai asked.

Lou Chi replied, “If my Commerce Guild were to purchase it, two million Upper-Level Source Crystals!”

“That’s a fair price!” Yang Kai nodded slightly. For a Twelfth-Tier Lower-Level Inner Core, that price was about right.

“Of course, if it were auctioned at my Commerce Guild’s auction house, three million Upper-Level Source Crystals is possible.” Lou Chi smiled. “Is this the business you mentioned earlier?”

It truly was a good deal. Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast Inner Cores were always in demand, but a Lower-Level Inner Core wasn’t much. Though uncommon, the auction house occasionally saw them.

“Add this one, too.” Yang Kai casually tossed another.

Lou Chi instinctively caught it, staring wide-eyed, his eyes going round as he exclaimed, “This… this this…”

“Twelfth-Tier Upper-Level?” Kang Siran’s eyes were also glued to it.

Without the previous Lower-Level Inner Core for comparison, Kang Siran might not have noticed, but now, this Inner Core’s aura and feel were far superior.

This was definitely a Twelfth-Tier Upper-Level Inner Core!

That could only be produced by a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert.

“Where did you get this?” Lou Chi was completely unsettled, suddenly standing up.

Yang Kai looked at him with a meaningful smile.

Lou Chi lips twitched. “This old man misspoke.”

Such matters weren’t for him to inquire about. The Commerce Guild had strict rules—if violated, even a vice president could be punished or expelled. He was simply too shocked; in his life, he’d never seen a Twelfth-Tier Upper-Level Inner Core.

For a moment, he gripped the Inner Core tightly, reluctant to let it go.

“How much is this Inner Core worth?” Yang Kai asked.

“Ten million!” Lou Chi said solemnly. “At least ten million. If auctioned, fifteen million is possible!”

Yang Kai raised his brows. For Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores, the price gap between Upper-Level and Lower-Level was indeed huge.

“How’s this business?” Yang Kai asked with a smile.

“This is indeed an excellent deal!” Lou Chi nodded seriously. Yang Kai had come with two transactions, each worth over ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals—enough to warrant his personal attention. Such major deals were rare opportunities. With these two as a foundation, Kang Siran’s performance would surely soar, leaving the other stewards in the branch far behind.

“But how can this business be long-term?” Lou Chi frowned.

He certainly wished to establish a long-term partnership with Yang Kai, but Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast Inner Cores were not so easily obtained. Who knew how Yang Kai had managed to bring out two of them this time? There was no guarantee he’d be so lucky again in the future.

“From now on, I’ll trade two Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores with Brother Kang each year, any quality, until Brother Kang leaves Fenglin City.”

Lou Chi smiled bitterly. “So, you want to keep Steward Kang in Fenglin City. It’s not that this old man doesn’t want to help, nor that I don’t trust you, but how can you get Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores every year…?”

As he spoke, Lou Chi’s eyes suddenly widened in disbelief. He stared dumbfounded at Yang Kai’s right hand, which held several Inner Cores between his fingers. Each one, in size, color, and aura, was no less than the first.

Looking at his left hand, it was the same.

“Gulp…” Even Lou Chi, who had weathered countless storms, couldn’t help but swallow hard at the sight.

Just as he tried to look closer, Yang Kai flipped his hands, and all the Inner Cores vanished.

“All Twelfth-Tier?” Lou Chi asked, dizzy.

Yang Kai smiled and said, “Hearing is one thing, seeing is another. Didn’t you see them with your own eyes, President Lou?”

Lou Chi scratched his head anxiously and said, “Young friend, would you be willing to sell all those Inner Cores…?”

“No way!” Yang Kai refused flatly. Even though he had more Inner Cores than he could ever use, he wasn’t about to sell them off so casually.

Lou Chi was frustrated, but the items belonged to Yang Kai. Whether to sell depended on his mood; Lou Chi couldn’t just take them by force.

Sighing deeply, Lou Chi made a last effort. “If you sell all those Inner Cores to my Commerce Guild, this old man can give you an extremely favorable price.”

“Not selling!” Yang Kai waved his hand.

Lou Chi slumped in resignation, but then straightened up and said solemnly, “Very well, young friend—you truly have the qualifications to establish a long-term partnership with my Commerce Guild.” Just the Inner Cores he glimpsed earlier numbered eight, enough for four years of cooperation.

“I’ll report this matter to the main guild. As for how they decide, this old man has no say in it.”

Yang Kai smiled. “You’re too modest, President Lou. You’re a vice president, you have some say. How could you not interfere? Hmm, if I can cooperate long-term with Brother Kang, how about selling you two Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores every half year?”

“Two every half year!” Lou Chi’s eyes lit up, smiling. “That works, that works. Don’t worry, this old man will speak well for you with the main guild.”

Yang Kai cupped his fists. “Thank you, President Lou.”

“No problem, no problem. The rest is up to you two. I will go gather those Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade for you, so you don’t miss your big event.” With that, he stood up, placed the two Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores in Kang Siran’s hand, sent a Divine Sense transmission, gave a few instructions, and then left.

The door closed again. Kang Siran held the two Inner Cores, his face full of gratitude. “Brother Yang, any thanks I give would feel too small. If you ever need me, just say the word.”

Yang Kai waved his hand. “Brother Kang, you’re too serious. I’m not just doing this for you, but also for myself.”

Kang Siran didn’t believe it, thinking Yang Kai was comforting him.

Yang Kai said, “I’ve recently established a Sect—it’s still in its early days, but as it grows, its demand for all kinds of resources will only increase. The Ziyuan Commerce Guild is an excellent partner, and with Brother Kang here, I know I’ll never get the short end of any deal.”

Kang Siran was shocked. “Brother Yang, you’ve actually founded a Sect?”

He was truly stunned. Though Yang Kai was an Emperor, not every Emperor could found a Sect. First, you needed a good location, broad connections, strong power, and disciples. All sorts of trouble.

“Just a small venture!” Yang Kai smiled.

Kang Siran said seriously, “Rest assured, Brother Yang. If your Sect and the Commerce Guild ever trade, Kang will do his utmost.”

Yang Kai smiled. “I trust you, Brother Kang. If you’re ever unhappy at the Commerce Guild, you’re always welcome at my Sect.”

Kang Siran laughed. “If you didn’t say your Sect and the Commerce Guild would cooperate, I’d go right now.”

But if Yang Kai’s Sect and Ziyuan Commerce Guild established a partnership, staying would be even better for Kang Siran.

After chatting for a while, Kang Siran said, “When President Lou left, he told me that if you’re willing to sell these two Inner Cores to the Commerce Guild, the Commerce Guild can offer thirteen million Source Crystals.”

Yang Kai chuckled, “Of course I brought out these Inner Cores to sell—what else would I do with them?”

Kang Siran said, “For this first cooperation, President Lou said you can sell directly to the Commerce Guild or let the Commerce Guild auction them. In the future, there won’t be the auction option.”

Yang Kai didn’t hesitate. “I'll just sell to the Commerce Guild.”

Though he’d lose a bit, the auction price wasn’t much higher, and the Commerce Guild would charge a fee. After deductions, it would be about the same.

Since he wanted to help Kang Siran, Yang Kai wouldn’t be greedy for small gains.

“Alright!” Kang Siran nodded. “There’s an auction at the Commerce Guild tomorrow night. These two Inner Cores should fetch a good price. As for the Source Crystals, Brother Yang, what do you want to do?”

Yang Kai said, “Just count them toward the purchase of Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jade.”

“Good!” Kang Siran nodded. Yang Kai’s straightforwardness made him realize Yang Kai’s wealth was even more terrifying than he thought. Otherwise, he wouldn’t ignore one or two million Upper-Level Source Crystals.

“By the way, Brother Kang, let me ask you something.”

Kang Siran said, “Please speak, Brother Yang!”

“Will there be armor for sale at tomorrow night’s auction?”

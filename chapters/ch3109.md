## Chapter 3109 — News of Su Yan

Chi Yue, Ai Ou, and the others' experiences wandering the Star Realm were still vivid in his mind. Yang Kai knew full well how much hardship they had endured along the way. Even Wu Dao, once known as the number one person in the Star Domain, had fallen, so what of Su Yan, a woman all on her own?

Deep in his heart, a voice roared and howled without end.

He had to see her!

Immediately! Right now!

Longing surged like a flood tide, almost drowning Yang Kai's body and mind completely.

"Master!" Liu Yan caught hold of Yang Kai's arm. That delicate touch snapped Yang Kai back to himself, and only then did he see the fellow whose neck he was gripping rolling his eyes wildly, his breathing ragged and his face pale, as if he might die at any moment.

Yang Kai hurriedly released him and dropped him back onto the Star Shuttle.

A violent fit of coughing followed. The man half knelt with a hand pressed to his throat, coughing so hard that tears nearly came out. Below, the eyelids of several cultivators twitched as they watched.

"I'm truly sorry." Yang Kai reached out a large hand toward him, though it trembled slightly despite his efforts to steady it.

With his current mental state and cultivation, very few things in this world could shake him to such a degree, but finding news of Su Yan in the Ancestral Domain was absolutely one of them. The fellow before him had called Su Yan Senior Sister, which meant that after Su Yan arrived in the Ancestral Domain, she had joined the Sect force behind him. No matter how one looked at it, bullying her Junior Brother was hardly glorious, especially since Yang Kai still needed to ask something of him.

The man took a while to recover, then followed the gesture and took Yang Kai's hand to stand. Lingering fear still covered his face, and when he looked at Yang Kai, he seemed wary and timid.

He was also a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator, yet in this young man's hands, he had been as helpless as an ant, without the slightest ability to resist. The other party was clearly not someone he could provoke. The speed of Yang Kai's shift in attitude also made him feel that this fellow was somewhat moody, which only made him more apprehensive. Such strength, such a temper, strange to the extreme.

"How should I address this Junior Brother?" Yang Kai made his expression as calm as he could.

The man swallowed and said, "Xiang Fei."

"So it is Junior Brother Xiang." Yang Kai smiled and patted his shoulder, pressing him down a little with the force. "I offended you greatly just now, so please don't take it to heart, Junior Brother Xiang. Mm, earlier you called Su Yan Senior Sister. Has she entered your noble Sect as a disciple?"

Through a few simple questions and answers, Yang Kai picked up several clues.

It was indeed as he had guessed. After Su Yan came to the Ancestral Domain, she had joined the force behind Xiang Fei, Yunxia Sect, and she had entered the Ancestral Domain through this very Star Path entrance.

However, Xiang Fei had not been the one receiving people here at the time. It had been another person from Yunxia Sect, so Xiang Fei did not know the specifics either. He had only learned a little about Su Yan after she joined Yunxia Sect.

With peerless beauty, a cold temperament, and extraordinary aptitude, Su Yan had gained a large following within just a few years of joining Yunxia Sect. Senior Brothers and Junior Brothers trying to curry favor with her were too numerous to count, and she had practically become a famous figure in Yunxia Sect.

Every Sect had a few such people, more or less, but as a disciple who had come from a Lower Plane Star Domain, Su Yan naturally drew even more attention. That was how the news that she came from Hengluo Star Domain had spread.

Xiang Fei had seen Su Yan several times himself, but he had never had the courage to go up and speak to her. He only felt that her cold expression carried a chill that kept others a thousand li away. If he got too close, he might really be frostbitten.

"Senior Sister Su has extraordinary aptitude. She advanced to the Dao Origin Realm only five years after entering our Sect. Such talent truly makes one ashamed." Xiang Fei sighed with feeling. He had likely realized that Yang Kai had some connection to Su Yan, so he picked only pleasant things to say.

Sure enough, Yang Kai's heart blossomed with joy as soon as he heard it. "She advanced to the Dao Origin Realm?"

He had always worried that she might suffer unfair treatment or exclusion in this world. From the sound of it, she had been doing fairly well.

"How is she now?" Yang Kai pressed.

Xiang Fei said, "I don't know either."

Yang Kai's brows drew together. "How can you not know? Aren't you a disciple of Yunxia Sect too?"

Xiang Fei was startled and hurriedly said, "Junior Brother is indeed a Yunxia Sect disciple, but more than ten years ago, I was sent here to garrison this place. I rarely return to the Sect, so I don't know much about what happens inside."

Yang Kai looked at him sympathetically. Generally speaking, those sent to garrison Star Path entrances were people who were unpopular in their Sect and had no backers, which was how they ended up with this kind of time-consuming assignment that offered few benefits.

Xiang Fei was clearly one such person in Yunxia Sect.

"Do you want to go back?"

"Of course." How could these lonely, miserable days outside compare with the ease and comfort of life in the Sect?

Yang Kai patted his shoulder. "Congratulations. You're about to get your wish!"

"Wh... what!" Xiang Fei still had not caught on, but instinctively sensed that something was wrong.

"To Yunxia Sect!" Yang Kai ordered He Yunxiang.

He Yunxiang immediately urged the Star Shuttle onward.

"Point the way!" Yang Kai turned back to Xiang Fei.

Xiang Fei said anxiously, "No, no, you can't! Hurry and let me down. Without a summons from the Sect Master and the Elders, I... uh, wrong direction. This way!" Faced with Yang Kai's fist, as large as a clay pot, he had no choice but to submit. The moody young man before him was not someone he could afford to offend. Under another's roof, one had no choice but to bow one's head. Once they returned to the Sect, if someone asked, he would at least have an explanation. He just did not know what punishment he would receive. This month's salary was probably gone. Thinking of those few crucial Origin Condensing Pills, he could not help feeling dejected.

Yang Kai punched him in the chest, frightening him so badly that his soul nearly flew out. Only after realizing the punch had not injured him did he say with a mournful face, "Senior Brother, don't scare me like that."

"Tell me more about Su Yan. If you speak well, there will definitely be benefits for you."

I would thank the heavens if you just didn't drag me into this. Xiang Fei did not know whether bringing this fellow to Yunxia Sect was a blessing or a disaster. At a glance, this person clearly had a deep relationship with Senior Sister Su Yan and was very likely one of her pursuers. Otherwise, why would he ask so much about Senior Sister Su Yan? And with strength this formidable, if he went to the Sect, wouldn't he inevitably clash with certain people?

But after thinking about it, this matter seemed to have little to do with him. Everyone wanted a taste of swan meat, but most of them were just toads crawling on the ground, drooling as they gazed up at the swan flying in the sky. If this man really caused trouble in the Sect, he would be the only one to suffer.

After steadying himself, Xiang Fei chose a few things he knew and began recounting them in detail.

He did not know much about Su Yan. Although Yunxia Sect could not be considered one of the top Sects in the Ancestral Domain, it was barely second-rate. There were more than a thousand disciples in the Sect, and people who were not familiar with one another rarely had chances to interact. Most of what he knew about Su Yan was hearsay.

Yet whether the news was true or false, Yang Kai listened with a smile, as if he had personally seen Su Yan's life during those years.

“By the way, not long after Senior Sister Su Yan joined our Sect, she was taken in as a disciple by Elder Ruan. I heard that Elder Ruan had been extremely adamant at the time and raised a huge fuss. It’s because of this incident that Senior Sister Su became famous in our Sect.”

"Is this Elder Ruan... a man or a woman?"

"A wo... woman. Elder Ruan is the oldest and most senior Elder in the Sect." Xiang Fei had the feeling that when Yang Kai asked that question just now, a cold glint had flashed through his eyes. Looking again now, however, there was no trace of it at all, and he did not know whether it had been his imagination.

Yang Kai laughed loudly and patted Xiang Fei's shoulder. "Your Elder Ruan has good eyes."

"Heh heh..." Xiang Fei tugged the corners of his mouth into a smile. "Elder Ruan's temper is quite strange. None of us dare casually approach the Spirit Peak where she lives. Although she is an Elder of my Yunxia Sect, she doesn't seem to take much part in the Sect's affairs, and she had never accepted a disciple before. So when word spread that year that she wanted to take Senior Sister Su as her disciple, it caused a great stir throughout the Sect."

From Xiang Fei's words, Yang Kai formed an image of a wrinkled old woman with an eccentric temper and a volatile nature.

Yang Kai was extremely grateful to this Elder Ruan, whom he had never met.

Su Yan had met a benefactor after leaving home. Otherwise, with her looks, even if she had entered Yunxia Sect, trouble would likely have followed her endlessly. There were plenty of people with outstanding aptitude, so why had she been able to advance to the Dao Origin Realm within five years? Elder Ruan's full support had likely played no small part. Just the Origin Condensing Pills needed to advance to the Dao Origin Realm cost an enormous amount. Su Yan had only just arrived; how could she possibly have gathered so many Pills?

They talked as they traveled, and Xiang Fei racked his brains, telling Yang Kai everything he knew about Su Yan. Yang Kai, however, still was not satisfied and kept pressing him, like a starving man forcing someone to draw flatbread in front of him.

Xiang Fei had nothing left to say, yet did not dare offend him. Sorrow welled up in his heart, and he grumbled inwardly, What kind of person is this!

Hardening his heart and gritting his teeth, he said, "Senior Sister Su has countless pursuers in the Sect."

He had figured it out. This young man definitely knew Senior Sister Su Yan and absolutely had feelings for Senior Sister Su. When they had spoken about Su Yan earlier, he had deliberately avoided the subject of romance, afraid that it might touch some nerve.

Now that he truly had nothing else to say, he could only bring this up.

You asked for this trouble yourself, so don't blame me!

To his surprise, Yang Kai merely smiled, looking completely unconcerned.

Even if Xiang Fei had not said it, Yang Kai could have guessed as much. If Su Yan did not have a few pursuers, that would be strange. But under all heaven, how many people could catch her eye? In Yang Kai's eyes, those pursuers did not even qualify as rivals.

Seeing that he had no reaction, Xiang Fei continued, "The others are one thing. Senior Sister Su is Elder Ruan's only disciple, after all, so they can't pester her too shamelessly. Once rejected, they usually know enough to withdraw. There is only one person..."

"What? Someone is really that shameless?"

"If I tell you, you can't say it came from me." Xiang Fei looked left and right. Seeing no one around, he lowered his voice.

“If you have something to say, say it. If you have a fart to let out, let it out!”

"Our Young Sect Master is extremely fond of Senior Sister Su. He once said in front of many people that he wanted to take Senior Sister Su as a concubine."

"Like hell he will!" Yang Kai flew into a thunderous rage, his expression turning savage. His previous calm vanished completely.

Fuck, do not let this Old Master see you, otherwise I will beat you so badly even your mother will not recognize you! Secretly admiring her would have been one thing, but you actually dared to boast so shamelessly!

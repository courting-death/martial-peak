## Chapter 2710 — I Have No Idea What You're Talking About

"Young Master Yang!"

"Lord Yang!"

As light returned, the many Emperor Realm cultivators were both shocked and delighted. Turning to see Yang Kai, they all cupped their fists in salute. Though Yang Kai was much younger than them and his cultivation wasn't particularly high, his actions in recent days had earned him universal respect—none dared show him the slightest disrespect.

The beautiful woman in palace attire, who knew Xia Sheng, gracefully flew to Yang Kai and offered a gentle salute. "Hua Yulu greets Young Master Yang."

Yang Kai nodded and asked, "How did those few get injured?"

Hua Yulu smiled, her charm overflowing, and replied softly, "Those friends were probably impatient to get out, but lacked the strength. So..."

Yang Kai let out an "Oh," understanding instantly.

He had suppressed these people for days; anyone would feel uneasy, unsure how Yang Kai would deal with them, so naturally they'd try to escape. But the Mountain and River Bell was a Primordial Era artifact—how could they break it? After exerting themselves, they were likely harmed by the bell's power rebounding on them.

Hua Yulu glanced around, seeing no one else nearby, only traces of battle everywhere. Observing Yang Kai, whose aura was steady and unscathed, she was surprised and whispered, "Young Master Yang, where did Elder Tan go?"

The others looked over curiously, eager for answers. When they were suppressed by the Mountain and River Bell, Yang Kai and Tan Junhao had already fallen out, sure to fight. After the bell's suppression, they had no idea what happened outside. Now, seeing Yang Kai unharmed, a thought they could hardly believe arose: Could Elder Tan have been driven off?

That shouldn’t have been possible. Tan Junhao was an Elder of Star God Palace, and his cultivation had reached the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. No matter how capable Yang Kai was, he shouldn’t have been a match for him. Not to mention, Tan Junhao had even set up a formation here beforehand—he should have had the advantage.

Yang Kai laughed heartily, clapping his hands. "Tan, that old dog, acted against the natural order and was cruel by nature. Hiding behind the name of Star God Palace, he only brought shame to its reputation. Though he called himself an Elder, he was nothing but a cancer to Star God Palace and a disgrace to the Southern Territory. I have already executed him on the spot! Truly, this is something worth celebrating—a joyous occasion!"

Everyone was stunned, eyes wide, staring at Yang Kai in disbelief. No one responded.

Yang Kai's face darkened. "What, you don't believe me?"

Seeing he was about to get angry, everyone was startled and hurriedly insisted they dared not doubt him. It wasn't that they didn't believe, but the matter was simply too unbelievable.

If Tan Junhao were standing before them, claiming to have killed Yang Kai, that would be a reasonable outcome. But it was Yang Kai alive and well, while Tan Junhao had vanished.

But... what was there to celebrate about this? No matter what, Tan Junhao was still an Elder of Star God Palace—how many such Elders did the entire Star God Palace even have? Now, one had died here; this was no small matter, it was as if the sky itself had been pierced. If Star God Palace decided to investigate, none of those present would be able to escape involvement.

Though they were Emperors, facing a giant like Star God Palace still made them nervous.

Hua Yulu forced a smile, her charm gone, and cautiously asked, "Young Master Yang, did you really... kill Elder Tan?"

"What's there to hide? I killed him, plain and simple," Yang Kai replied with an easy air, not the slightest hesitation in his voice. "Not just him—Wu Ming is dead too."

Only then did everyone believe him.

After all, there was no reason to lie. Killing a Star God Palace Elder wasn't a good thing—it could bring disaster. Yang Kai didn't seem foolish; why invite trouble?

For a moment, their respect for him grew even deeper.

If he could kill a Star God Palace Elder, just how strong was this young man? In this world, perhaps only a Great Emperor could suppress him.

"Young Master Yang, killing Elder Tan is going to cause a lot of trouble." Hua Yulu's delicate brows knitted in worry. No matter what Tan Junhao had done before, his status alone meant that Star God Palace would never let this matter rest—they would surely investigate his death.

"Lord Yang, this has nothing to do with us. We have no idea what happened afterward."

"That's right, Lord Yang, we never intended to oppose Star God Palace."

"We were just here for the auction, nothing else."

The group of Emperor Realm cultivators, as if caught in trouble, all began to protest loudly.

Yang Kai knew exactly what they were worried about. He sneered inwardly—if he hadn't killed Tan Junhao, none of them would have survived. Tan, as a Star God Palace elder, had used the Heart-Controlling Gu to control so many Emperor Realm cultivators. If exposed, Star God Palace would never tolerate him, so he would have killed everyone to cover it up, then blamed Yang Kai for the murders.

At that point, he could have claimed to be slaying a demon and avenging the Southern Territory's Emperor Realm cultivators, earning great merit.

How laughable that these people were now eager to distance themselves, oblivious to the fact that Yang Kai had saved their lives.

Yang Kai didn't need their gratitude. He knew the world was cold and never expected thanks.

"Young Master Yang, you should leave—go as far as you can. The Southern Territory isn't safe anymore." Hua Yulu quietly sent Yang Kai a message. "Go to the Northern Territory. I heard there's a forbidden land called the Frozen Wastes, even Great Emperors don't dare enter lightly. If there's anywhere you can hide, it's there."

Yang Kai looked at Hua Yulu in surprise. She was different from the others—at least she cared about him.

"No worries!" Yang Kai smiled, then shouted, "Enough! Quiet down, all of you!"

The Emperor Realm cultivators immediately fell silent.

Yang Kai said seriously, "Rest assured, I alone take responsibility. Tan Junhao was killed by me. If Star God Palace investigates, just tell the truth. You won't be implicated."

At these words, everyone was overjoyed.

"Lord Yang truly has noble character. I am deeply impressed."

"Thank you, Lord Yang. If that day comes, we won't hide anything."

"Tan Junhao was nothing but a wretch—his death is no loss. Lord Yang, by killing him, you've done Star God Palace a great service and cleansed their ranks."

Hearing Yang Kai was willing to take the blame, everyone relaxed, smiling in relief. Their previous worries vanished.

"Enough flattery." Yang Kai snorted. "I saved your lives. I don't need your gratitude, but I have one request!"

"Please speak, Lord Yang. Whatever you ask, we will obey!" an elder said, cupping his fists.

Yang Kai wasn't exaggerating about saving them; everyone knew it. But his blunt words made some feel awkward, as if they'd been ungrateful.

"Tan Junhao is dead. Star God Palace will definitely investigate. If they come to you, I hope you'll recount everything that happened, especially about the Heart-Controlling Gu!"

Everyone nodded. "Of course."

Hua Yulu added, "Rest assured, Young Master Yang. Even without your instructions, we won't hide anything. Tan Junhao, as a Star God Palace elder, harbored such evil intentions. If not for your intervention, we would have died. We owe you our lives. If anyone distorts the truth or slanders you, we won't forgive them."

"We won't forgive them!" everyone echoed, united in resolve.

"Excellent." Yang Kai nodded in satisfaction, cupping his fists. "Thank you, everyone."

"Not at all."

With things settled, everyone understood. If they all told the truth, even if Star God Palace investigated, Yang Kai and the rest would likely be fine.

Ultimately, it was Tan Junhao who angered everyone. As a Star God Palace Elder, he didn't protect its reputation, but instead enslaved so many Emperor Realm cultivators with evil methods. He brought about his own destruction.

Maybe Star God Palace would even thank Yang Kai.

Of course, that wouldn't be public. How it ended was Star God Palace's business, not theirs.

"In that case, everyone may go," Yang Kai said with a wave of his hand.

Hearing this, everyone was overjoyed. They cupped their fists in thanks and quickly turned to leave. Although Yang Kai had no intention of making things difficult for them, this young man was simply too fierce—he killed without batting an eye. Staying by his side never felt safe. It was better to keep their distance; who knew when disaster might strike if they lingered any longer?

"Sister Hua, wait!" Yang Kai suddenly called out to Hua Yulu.

Hua Yulu smiled at his amusing address. "Is there something else, Young Master Yang?"

Yang Kai said, "Since you're friends with Senior Brother Xia Sheng, you're my friend too. I'll remove the Heart-Controlling Gu for you, so you won't have any worries."

Hearing this, Hua Yulu's mouth opened in surprise, her hand covering her lips, her face full of joy and astonishment.

She had just been thinking that, though she escaped disaster, the Heart-Controlling Gu remained in her body. She'd have to find a way to remove it, but she'd never heard of it and had no idea how.

She hadn't expected Yang Kai to offer exactly what she needed.

What a thoughtful person...

Swish, swish, swish...

The Emperor Realm cultivators about to leave all stopped, turned their heads, ears perked up, and some even flew back, landing before Yang Kai with flattering smiles. "Lord Yang, did you say... you can remove the Heart-Controlling Gu?"

"I have no idea what you're talking about." Yang Kai replied seriously, shaking his head like a rattle drum.

One of the cultivators grew anxious and blurted out, "Lord Yang, th-this... I heard it clearly! Just now, you said you’d help Palace Master Hua remove the Heart-Controlling Gu!"

Yang Kai turned to Hua Yulu, curious. "Did I say that?"

Hua Yulu didn't know what Yang Kai was planning, so she just smiled sweetly, saying nothing, keeping everyone in suspense.

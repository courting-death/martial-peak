## Chapter 3002 — Everchanging Sword

The Dao of Spirit Arrays had a long and storied history, often capable of turning the mundane into the miraculous. Looking across the world, which great Sect did not have its own Arrays? Setting aside large-scale Arrays like Grand Guardian Arrays and Guardian Mountain Arrays, there were also many Arrays formed by people.

Since the participants were all fellow disciples, they usually had plenty of opportunities to practice and develop their coordination. When such an Array was deployed against a powerful enemy, it often produced miraculous results.

Wind and Cloud Pavilion naturally possessed Arrays as well, including a formidable Sword Array that excelled above all in slaughter.

This Sword Array was called the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation. It had been passed down from the ancestors of Wind and Cloud Pavilion for ten thousand years. Throughout the Pavilion's history, it had relied on this Sword Array many times to turn danger into safety and preserve its legacy.

The Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation required at least five people to form, but there was no upper limit. The more people who joined it, the greater its power.

Ordinarily, just ten Third-Stage Dao Origin Realm disciples forming the Array could contend with an average Emperor Realm Master. With twenty people, they could kill a First-Stage Emperor Realm Master.

Every Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciple had to cultivate this Sword Formation after reaching the Dao Origin Realm, and a large-scale joint drill was held once a month. Thus, every cultivator in Wind and Cloud Pavilion at or above the Dao Origin Realm had long since become thoroughly familiar with this Array.

This was all to guard against the possibility that Wind and Cloud Pavilion might one day provoke some powerful enemy.

The last time Wind and Cloud Pavilion had deployed the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation was over a hundred years ago. At the time, Hua Xing had yet to return from his travels, while the previous Pavilion Master was old and nearing the end of his life, leaving Wind and Cloud Pavilion showing signs of decline. As a result, several great forces in the Western Territory coveted it and joined forces to attack.

A Third-Stage Emperor Realm expert had even broken into the Grand Guardian Array.

It was precisely by relying on this great Array that Wind and Cloud Pavilion's disciples repelled their powerful enemies. That battle spread the Pavilion's fame far and wide, and the situation only gradually stabilized after Hua Xing returned and took control of Wind and Cloud Pavilion.

A hundred years later, the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation rose once more.

Yet this time, its opponents were merely two First-Stage Emperor Realm Masters.

It was not that Great Elder was making a fuss over nothing; he knew how terrifying these two were. During this period, Wind and Cloud Pavilion had sent many experts to intercept and kill them. More than one Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master had also been drawn in by the twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals, but what had been the result?

These two juniors remained alive and well, and had even fought their way into Wind and Cloud Pavilion, while those so-called experts had all met with defeat.

Great Elder himself was only a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master. He had learned from the failures of those before him, and after personally crossing hands with Yang Kai and experiencing his strength, he did not hesitate to bring out his trump card.

The expressions of the many Wind and Cloud Pavilion Elders and disciples all became solemn at his words.

"Form the Array!" Great Elder roared, raising his longsword high toward the sky as soaring Sword Intent burst forth.

Five or six First-Stage Emperor Realm Elders and more than a hundred Third-Stage Dao Origin Realm disciples of Wind and Cloud Pavilion also summoned their Secret Treasure longswords in unison. Each formed a different hand seal, and as their swords trembled, they unleashed dazzling streams of Sword Intent while shouting, "Form the Array!"

The voices of more than a hundred people converged into one, and even their auras seemed to be linked together by some mysterious force. The world hummed and the Void trembled.

Yang Kai's eyes narrowed. Although he did not know exactly what kind of Array this was, he instinctively sensed that something was wrong. Turning his gaze toward the densest part of the crowd, he murmured, "Myriad Sword radiance, chase the moon and swallow the wolf!"

The Myriad Sword swept down, producing a flood of sword lights that blotted out the sky and surged toward the Dao Origin Realm cultivators like a swarm of locusts.

Under normal circumstances, even a First-Stage Emperor Realm Master would not be able to receive such a strike easily, while a Dao Origin Realm cultivator could only wait to be slaughtered.

Yet as the sword lights approached, the Dao Origin Realm cultivators showed not the slightest panic. They merely watched coldly while maintaining the circulation of their Sword Intent and urging their sword intent onward.

"Receive!" Great Elder flicked his longsword, drawing a sword flower in the air as he roared.

An Array Diagram strangely appeared in mid-air. It slowly rotated and transformed into a visible barrier that stood before the Dao Origin Realm cultivators.

Hong, hong, hong, hong...

All the sword lights struck the Array Diagram. Unexpectedly, they failed to produce any effect and were instead completely absorbed by it.

Yang Kai raised his brow, sensing that this would be troublesome. This great Array seemed truly extraordinary.

Before he could finish that thought, Great Elder shouted again, "Go!"

The Array Diagram suddenly began rotating in reverse, and streams of sword light shot out from it, arriving before Yang Kai in the blink of an eye.

"Uncle Yang, be careful!" Lin Yun'er cried out.

Yang Kai had long been on guard. Wielding the Myriad Sword, he blocked the sword lights and raised his brow. "Reflection?"

He discovered that the sword lights flying toward him were the very ones he had just launched. Thinking back to the mysteries of the Array Diagram, he immediately understood.

The Array Diagram that had appeared out of thin air could not only absorb his attacks, but also release them back at him. This Array was simply heaven-defying.

"Turn!" Great Elder circulated another sequence of Sword Intent and shouted.

In the next instant, the world changed.

Everyone suddenly disappeared, and the entire world seemed to transform. Looking around, Yang Kai saw longswords with chilling auras standing throughout the void. Some were solid and others illusory, but they were as numerous as the stars in the sky, stretching endlessly beyond sight.

This was simply a world of swords.

Terrifying Sword Intent pressed in from every direction, while invisible sword Qi sliced through everything in this world, making even Yang Kai's skin ache all over.

A drop of sweat slid down Yang Kai's forehead. He felt that the situation had taken a grave turn. Although he had yet to experience the true power of this great Array, he still possessed enough discernment to recognize how strange it was.

"Once you enter the Sword Formation, there is no path to life. Junior, die!" Great Elder's voice came from the Void, ethereal and untraceable, making it impossible to determine his position.

"Once you enter the Sword Formation, there is no path to life!" More than a hundred people shouted in unison. Their words seemed to contain shocking Sword Intent that transformed into invisible sharp blades and pierced toward the minds of Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er.

This was clearly a type of Divine Soul attack, one that also contained Sword Intent, and its power was truly unfathomable.

Yang Kai felt a sudden pain in his head as his Sea of Consciousness churned. Its defenses were broken through in an instant, and an enormous sword light appeared within it, slashing toward his Divine Soul.

Yang Kai was greatly alarmed and hurriedly drew upon the power of the Warm Spirit Lotus, transforming it into a shield before him.

With a thunderous crash, the sword light slashed down but was blocked by the shield, and seven-coloured radiance burst forth.

The two sides remained deadlocked for quite some time before the sword light finally shattered.

Yang Kai was horrified and turned to look at Lin Yun'er, only to see the little girl staring with her big eyes, her fists tightly clenched as she searched all around for the others. She had not been affected in the slightest.

This little girl was also heaven-defying!

That Divine Soul attack just now clearly could not have targeted him alone. Lin Yun'er must also have been among its targets, yet the little girl was perfectly fine and had not even reacted. This was simply inconceivable.

Although Lin Yun'er's cultivation was the same as his, Yang Kai did not believe her Divine Soul power could be stronger than his.

After all, his Divine Soul had been nourished by the Warm Spirit Lotus for so many years and was comparable to that of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master. It would be unreasonable if Lin Yun'er was even stronger than him.

There were only two possible reasons she remained unharmed: either it was due to her physique, or she carried some powerful Secret Treasure.

Yang Kai was more inclined toward the latter. The special characteristics of the Force Tyrant Body were mainly reflected in the physical body and should have nothing to do with the Divine Soul.

With no time to think further, Yang Kai flickered to Lin Yun'er's side and wrapped an arm around her. Urging his Laws of Space, he instantly vanished from the spot.

This Array was strange and unfathomable. If they remained here, they might suffer an unexpected defeat. Their most pressing task was to escape the Array as quickly as possible.

“Trying to escape?” the Great Elder snorted. One of the longswords suspended in midair suddenly quivered before vanishing like a bolt of lightning, plunging directly into space.

In the next instant, Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er stumbled out of the air nearby. Lin Yun'er was clutching a longsword in one hand, her face filled with anger.

"This is bad," Yang Kai said, his expression changing slightly.

It was extremely rare for his Space Secret Techniques to be broken, but whenever it happened, it meant the enemy's strength had exceeded Yang Kai's expectations.

"Power of Space?" Great Elder was also somewhat surprised. Under ordinary circumstances, with his cultivation, it would have been absolutely impossible for him to break Yang Kai's Laws of Space. Within this Sword Formation, however, he was the Formation Core and controlled everything inside, allowing him to grasp Yang Kai's movements and traces.

It was precisely by relying on this that he had knocked Yang Kai out of the void and thwarted his plan at the final step.

"Yun'er, are you afraid?" Yang Kai swept a cold gaze around. Although he pushed his Divine Sense to its limit, he still could not discover where the people of Wind and Cloud Pavilion were. There were only swords, sword will, and Sword Intent here; nothing else existed.

"I'm not afraid!" Lin Yun'er replied. Her small hand exerted force, and the longsword she held shattered into motes of light before disappearing.

It had only been an illusory sword, yet it had seemed utterly lifelike within this Sword Formation.

"Good." Yang Kai laughed loudly. "Then follow Uncle Yang and break this bullshit Sword Formation!"

"What shameless boasting!" Great Elder shouted. "No matter how many methods you possess, do not even think of escaping this Array!"

As soon as his words fell, the countless longswords surrounding them began trembling in unison. They turned one after another, their points aimed directly at Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er.

Yang Kai’s expression darkened. Enveloped by that overwhelming Sword Intent, even the flow of Emperor Yuan within his body slowed ever so slightly.

"Die!"

Following that furious roar, endless whooshing sounds tore through the air.

As far as the eye could see, countless longswords flew toward Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er from every direction. Every sword blossomed with astonishing sword light that seemed capable of severing everything before it.

The entire world within the Sword Formation was filled with these attacks, leaving Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er nowhere to hide.

"Yun'er, crouch down!" Yang Kai shouted.

Without even thinking, Lin Yun'er covered her head and crouched down.

"Dragon Transformation!" Yang Kai roared again.

As a resounding dragon roar rang out, the Sword Formation trembled slightly, as though struck by some force.

A dense series of cracking sounds rang out as Yang Kai's body rapidly expanded, transforming in an instant into a four-and-a-half-zhang-tall colossus. Dragon horns sprouted from his head, his hands became Dragon Claws, and scales covered his entire body. His aura towered to the Heavens, and his might was overwhelming.

He crouched down and curled up his enormous body, shielding Lin Yun'er in his embrace.

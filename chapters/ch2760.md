## Chapter 2760 — Feihong Black Dragon Armor

In the auction hall, many people’s breathing quickened, countless threads of Divine Sense probing toward the high platform from all directions, eager to catch a glimpse first.

An Imperial Treasure was already an item of immense value, let alone a defensive Imperial Treasure, and moreover, an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor.

Yet, to everyone’s disappointment, the box, seemingly forged from refined iron, completely blocked Divine Sense from probing inside. No matter how hard they tried, their efforts were in vain.

On the high platform, the beautiful woman smiled as she looked around, her voice soft and gentle: "This armor was not crafted by any contemporary artifact master, but comes from an ancient cave residence. It is a relic from a distant era, discovered by a friend by chance and sent to our Commerce Guild for auction."

At her words, many faces showed expressions of envy and jealousy.

Ancient cave residences were not uncommon in the Star Realm, but not everyone could encounter one. Even if they did, they might become trapped by restrictive formations within, unable to escape, let alone obtain treasures.

To find an ancient cave residence, retreat unscathed, and even obtain such a treasure—one could imagine how great that person’s luck must be.

Why couldn’t such a stroke of fortune fall to me? Everyone asked themselves this in their hearts, grinding their teeth with envy. If one were lucky enough to encounter such a chance, one wouldn’t have to worry about cultivation resources for several lifetimes.

"Because of its age, although this armor is Middle-Level, it has some flaws!"

As the young woman finished speaking, the auction hall suddenly fell silent.

After a while, a voice came from a Heaven-class private room: "May I ask, what kind of flaws?"

Many Emperor Realm experts were here for this armor. Previously, Ziyuan Commerce Guild's promotion had said an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor would be auctioned, but now the young woman was telling them it had flaws.

Yang Kai was also taken aback; Kang Siran had never mentioned this to him.

However, given his relationship with Kang Siran, if Kang Siran had known beforehand, he certainly wouldn't have kept it secret. In other words, even Kang Siran didn't know this detail.

Thinking it over, Yang Kai felt at ease. Though flawed, it was still an Imperial-Grade Middle-Level armor. There was no need to be too clear in the promotion—just attracting people was enough.

At this moment, the young woman smiled and said, "Everyone, please wait a moment and you'll see. Now, let President Lou test the armor's power."

She shifted slightly to the side and gestured toward the rear. From that direction, a figure strode forward—not particularly tall, but carrying an air of authority. It was Lou Chi, the overseer of this place.

Arriving beside the young woman, Lou Chi cupped his fists to the clients in the private rooms, said nothing, then suddenly activated Emperor Yuan, pointing at the refined iron box. Accompanied by a series of sharp whistling sounds, thumb-sized armor plates flew out from the box, forming a black dragon that enveloped Lou Chi.

As the armor settled onto Lou Chi, his aura suddenly transformed—he seemed to radiate the might of a war god, his presence fierce and commanding. Even the power of his First Stage Emperor Realm cultivation appeared to surge, leaving the audience utterly astonished.

Looking closely, the armor was not bulky at all; instead, it appeared exceptionally light and agile. Lou Chi moved nimbly atop the platform, his motions swift and fluid, with not the slightest hindrance at the joints. Though the armor covered nearly his entire body, everyone could see that around his waist, there was a gap of about a foot, the edges uneven and jagged.

That gap was not part of the armor’s original design. Rather, it gave the impression that something was missing—as though the armor had once suffered a devastating blow, and the plates around the waist had been torn away.

"This Feihong Black Dragon Armor was originally complete, but unfortunately, for some reason, a portion is missing," the young woman explained.

"With such a serious loss, is this armor still useful?" someone asked.

The young woman smiled: "The methods of ancient artifact masters are beyond our imagination. Rest assured, though this armor is missing quite a bit, most of its power remains. Of course, while it is still Imperial-Grade Middle-Level, it can now only display the defensive power of Imperial-Grade Lower-Level."

As the young woman spoke, Lou Chi suddenly let out a light shout. The black armor on his body made a series of clattering sounds, then flew off him, each plate floating in midair and reassembling into the shape of the armor.

Lou Chi raised his hand, and a thick, yellowed tome appeared in his palm.

"Two Forms Record!" someone exclaimed.

This book-like item was Lou Chi's Imperial Treasure, the Two Forms Record, with immense power.

Activating Emperor Yuan, two mysterious lights shot out from the Two Forms Record, one yin and one yang, intertwining and spiraling toward the floating armor. The surging power fluctuations made everyone change color. No one doubted that if these two lights struck an unguarded Emperor Realm expert, even they would be instantly killed.

With a rumbling sound, the mysterious lights struck the black armor, only causing sparks to fly. Black light flashed across the armor's surface, but it remained intact.

The young woman's voice rose sharply: "This armor has been appraised by several masters from our Commerce Guild. It was forged from the scales of an adult Ink-Scaled Jiao. In the Star Realm, there are many jiao beasts, but Ink-Scaled Jiao are extremely rare, renowned for their incredible defense. The Feihong Black Dragon Armor, made from their scales, has defensive power far beyond ordinary Imperial Treasures. This piece can save your life at a critical moment!"

"It was actually forged from Ink-Scaled Jiao scales!"

"Jiao creatures all have Dragon Clan bloodlines. The scales of an adult Ink-Scaled Jiao must carry a trace of dragon nature. What a treasure, truly a treasure!"

"The item is good, but the price..."

The crowd below buzzed with excitement. The young woman's smile grew even brighter. After a while, she announced: "Feihong Black Dragon Armor, starting price ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals. Bid increments are up to you—please, everyone!"

At her words, a chorus of gasps sounded.

Previously, the highest auction price was only a bottle of Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills, worth six million Upper-Level Source Crystals.

Now, the Feihong Black Dragon Armor's starting price was ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals. One could imagine how terrifying the final price would be. Just the starting price made ninety-nine percent of people give up on bidding.

Only those Emperor Realm experts who had come prepared were eager to act. Ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals was a staggering sum, but after witnessing Lou Chi’s demonstration, everyone could see that this armor was utterly impervious to attacks from a First Stage Emperor Realm expert. Against such an opponent, wearing this treasure was almost the same as being invincible. Even when facing a Second Stage Emperor Realm master, one could still hold one’s ground for a time.

No one wanted to miss such a good item. The competition would surely be fierce.

No one wanted to be the first to stand out; everyone preferred to wait and see how things unfolded. For a moment, the entire auction hall fell into a hush.

The young woman stood on the auction platform, smiling, not urging anyone. Having hosted many auctions, she understood these people's mentality. Even with some flaws, the Feihong Black Dragon Armor was a rare treasure, not likely to go unsold.

After a quiet ten breaths, a voice suddenly rang out: "Twenty million!"

The Emperor Realm experts who had been watching were all startled, turning toward the source of the voice.

Heaven Room Three—it was the person who had previously won the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin.

In an instant, many faces darkened, and some cursed the person in Room Three in their hearts.

An item with a starting price of ten million, raised to twenty million in one go—what was that supposed to mean? Even if you have money, you can't just throw it around. Was this a shill arranged by Ziyuan Commerce Guild?

Many auctions had such people. Their job was to drive up the price, stir up the bidders' emotions, and make others buy at higher prices.

Thinking this way, some immediately believed the person in Room Three was indeed a shill.

In Room Three, Qin Yu covered her red lips, shocked as she looked at Yang Kai, her voice trembling: "Eldest Brother Yang, that's Upper-Level Source Crystals..."

She was afraid Yang Kai hadn't understood the starting price, mistaking it for Middle-Level or Lower-Level, so she hurried to remind him, lest things get out of hand.

"I know," Yang Kai nodded, grinning.

Qin Yu was stunned. Thinking back to what he said when he gave her the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin, she realized... two million Upper-Level Source Crystals really meant nothing to him.

"Damn brat!"

"Brat, go eat shit!"

"Brat, do you dare to give your name? This old man wants to see which sect you belong to."

The Emperor Realm experts in the private rooms were now unsettled, cursing and shouting.

In Room Nine, Le Dongzheng's eyes nearly popped out. Earlier, Yang Kai had spent two million Upper-Level Source Crystals to win the Liu Yun Butterfly Hairpin, making Le Dongzheng think Yang Kai was wealthy. If he could capture Yang Kai, he'd surely profit. But he never expected Yang Kai's wealth to be this vast.

Twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals, just like that. What was his limit—thirty million, forty million?

Whatever it was, he definitely had a huge amount of Upper-Level Source Crystals.

"Good, good, truly heaven favors my Duanyue Sect!" Le Dongzheng suddenly laughed. The richer Yang Kai was, the happier he became. As long as he could kill Yang Kai, all that wealth would be his.

As for Gu Hong, he could just give him a small share. Fifty percent? Ridiculous.

Yang Kai’s face darkened from the curses, and he shouted toward the beautiful woman on the high platform, “A bunch of decrepit old fools—no strength, yet they dare to attend the auction! Madam, I suggest you revoke their bidding qualifications and throw them out, lest they tarnish the reputation of Ziyuan Commerce Guild.”

The young woman heard it all, frowned, and said, "Are you here to attend the auction or to quarrel? If you're here to quarrel, please step aside and don't disrupt the proceedings."

As soon as she spoke, the Emperor Realm experts quieted down, not daring to cause any more trouble.

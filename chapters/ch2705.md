## Chapter 2705 — Did I Hurt You?

"Yao King..." Yang Kai called out weakly, pounding his fist against the ground with a dull thud.

Things were looking grim. If he were at his peak, he wouldn't fear this demoness Zhu Qing, but after that fierce battle, he was utterly drained. Being alone with her gave him no sense of security at all.

He figured it would be safer to call Yingfei over to guard him.

“What are you shouting for? He won’t find this place anytime soon.” Zhu Qing, seeing Yang Kai looking half-dead, felt a surge of satisfaction. With a faint smile, she squatted down right in front of his head, carefully tucking her skirt beneath her knees to avoid any accidental exposure. Resting her chin in her hand, she studied him with great interest.

Yang Kai eyed her warily, as if facing a great enemy. "Then how did you find me so quickly?"

“Dragon aura, of course! I’m a Dragon—when I sense your dragon aura, finding you is as easy as following a beacon.” Zhu Qing reached out and poked the dragon scale on Yang Kai’s chest.

"Hiss..." Yang Kai sucked in a sharp breath, his face twisting in pain. There was already a deep, bone-revealing wound there, and Zhu Qing's careless poke nearly made him jump from the pain.

"Oh, sorry! Did I hurt you?" Zhu Qing covered her red lips with her hand, looking at Yang Kai with a face full of guilt, like a child who'd done something wrong. But the sly glint in her eyes made it clear she knew exactly what she was doing.

"It's just a scratch, nothing worth mentioning!" Yang Kai snorted coldly, refusing to show weakness. Even if it hurt, he wouldn't admit it—he couldn't let his spirit falter.

“Oh, really?” Zhu Qing sneered mischievously, her intentions anything but pure. With a delicate jade finger, she reached out and pressed directly onto the wound on Yang Kai’s shoulder.

"What are you doing!" Yang Kai roared in anger, thinking that if he weren't so weak right now, he'd make sure to give her a good beating.

Zhu Qing just smiled silently, poking at the wound again and again with her finger.

She even feigned concern, asking, "Does it hurt? That's a pretty big wound—must hurt a lot, right?"

Yang Kai's expression became a colorful spectacle, but he gritted his teeth and stayed silent. Inwardly, he swore that if he survived her torment today, he'd pay her back a hundredfold in the future.

"Does it hurt?" Zhu Qing asked again, her face full of concern as her finger pressed even harder, digging right into the wound and stirring it around.

A tear slid from the corner of Yang Kai's eye. He couldn't help but feel a tragic sense of a dragon stranded in shallow waters being toyed with by shrimp, a tiger fallen from grace being bullied by dogs.

Suddenly, Yang Kai turned his head and stared intently between Zhu Qing's legs. "Hey, your underwear is showing."

Zhu Qing froze, then quickly looked down.

At that moment, Yang Kai abruptly activated the power of the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin, golden light bursting forth to envelop both him and Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing let out an involuntary groan, as if struck by something overwhelming. Her face changed color.

Yang Kai reached out, his arm sweeping forward in a powerful grab, seizing her by the neck and viciously dragging her down. Zhu Qing was forced straight to her knees before him, utterly unable to resist.

With a loud thud, their foreheads slammed together.

Yang Kai felt dizzy, nearly passing out, but he still managed to catch her red lips precisely, biting down and then prying her teeth open with a move like a dragon emerging from its cave, savoring her sweetness without restraint.

"Mmm..." Zhu Qing groaned, her eyes widening in shock.

Even though it wasn't the first time, she still couldn't handle such intimacy. It felt as if an unstoppable spear was sweeping through her mouth, radiating a strange power that battered her heart. Her whole body tingled, and the strength that could rival a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert completely deserted her. Soon, she felt as if she were burning up, unbearably hot.

Amidst the blood and corpses littering the square, the two of them seemed frozen in time. Even the flow of time itself seemed to stop.

The golden light gradually faded, and the power of the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin slowly receded.

Without the suppression of the Golden Sacred Dragon's Origin, Zhu Qing suddenly raised her head, breaking free from Yang Kai's wantonness. Her beautiful eyes blazed with fury, like a volcano about to erupt. Her chest heaved as she gritted her teeth and spat, "You... how dare you!"

For a mere human to take such liberties with her again and again—she was absolutely livid.

"Heh heh heh heh..." Yang Kai sprawled shamelessly on the ground, arms and legs spread wide in the shape of a cross. He looked utterly disheveled, yet couldn't stop grinning slyly. Like a rascal, he taunted, "Go ahead, do your worst! If I die beneath a peony, I'll die a happy man. Kill me, and you'll be a widow!"

He spoke as if Zhu Qing were already his woman.

Zhu Qing’s face turned icy cold. Biting her lip, she raised a delicate fist and, without hesitation, landed a solid punch right on Yang Kai’s face.

"You... damn it!" Yang Kai cursed loudly, his vision going black as he completely lost consciousness.

...

Groggy and aching all over, Yang Kai felt as weak as he had in his early days. It had been a long time since he'd felt like this.

His mind drifted in and out of consciousness. Familiar voices called to him, and blurry faces appeared in his hazy vision. He tried to see them clearly, but they remained indistinct, just out of reach like the moon reflected in water.

Suddenly, Yang Kai's eyes snapped open, his muddled mind instantly clearing.

"Young Master Yang!"

"Yang Kai!"

"Sect Master!"

Voices rang out by his ear. Looking around, Yang Kai saw Yingfei at the head, with Ye Hen, Ye Jinghan, Chi Yue, and others gathered nearby. Their eyes were full of concern, and seeing him finally awake, their faces showed obvious relief.

"I'm not dead..." Yang Kai muttered softly.

Yingfei said, "Young Master, you fought a fierce battle and suffered heavy injuries. You later fell unconscious, but fortunately... Miss Zhu Qing was the first to find you and stayed by your side. If some villain had found you instead, the consequences would have been unimaginable."

Just thinking about it made Yang Kai break out in a cold sweat. If someone with ill intent had found him while he was so deeply unconscious, his life would have been in grave danger.

Although Yang Kai had dispelled the Grand Square Five Elements Array, the Eight Directions Element-Splitting Array was still active. Yingfei and the others had been waiting for Gong Tai to break the formation and merge the separated spaces before they could leave their rooms.

"Zhu Qing..." Yang Kai murmured in surprise.

Yingfei smiled, "That's right, it was Miss Zhu Qing. We owe her a debt of gratitude."

As he spoke, he glanced meaningfully to the side, a knowing smile on his face. He clearly thought there was something between Yang Kai and Zhu Qing—after all, last time in the wilderness, the two had been quite affectionate...

Following his gaze, Yang Kai saw Zhu Qing standing there, looking a bit awkward.

Yang Kai grinned wickedly, thinking that if this demoness hadn't given him that final blow, he probably wouldn't have passed out.

Still, she hadn't taken advantage of him while he was out, which surprised Yang Kai. Maybe... she really didn't mean him any harm? Had he misunderstood her?

"Huh, who washed me and changed my clothes?" Yang Kai suddenly realized his bloodstains were gone and he was wearing a fresh set of clothes.

Could it have been Zhu Qing? Tsk, just thinking about it made his heart race...

"It was me!" Ai Ou snorted coldly, clearly seeing through Yang Kai's wild imaginings. "What, do you have any complaints?"

Yang Kai's face darkened. "Thank you, Father-in-law. How could I possibly complain?"

"Hmph. Out there on your own, you don't even know how to protect yourself. What if you made Yue'er and the others widows?" Ai Ou scolded.

"Yes, yes, yes!" Yang Kai nodded repeatedly, feeling extremely guilty—after all, he was the one who had ruined his daughter's innocence.

Gu Cangyun chimed in, "If anyone's to blame, it's us for being too weak. Sect Master Yang was only standing up for us."

Ai Ou sighed, "Just don't be so reckless next time." His tone was full of concern. After a pause, he added, "And about Fifth Sister..."

Yang Kai froze, then quickly replied, "She's fine. I'll let her out now."

With a wave, he opened the Small Xuan Realm and released Chi Yue.

This move left Zhu Qing momentarily stunned.

As everyone gathered, they naturally began asking questions, and upon learning how seriously Yang Kai was injured, Chi Yue’s face filled with guilt as well.

"Everyone, let Young Master Yang rest for a while," Yingfei said, worried that the commotion would disturb Yang Kai's recovery.

"Yes, yes," Ghost Ancestor nodded. "Whatever it is, let's wait until Sect Master recovers."

With that, everyone took their leave.

Zhu Qing said nothing, blending into the crowd as she left.

"Hey, Qing'er, wait a moment. I have something to ask you," Yang Kai called out to her.

Zhu Qing paused, frowning slightly at the intimate way he addressed her. She didn't seem pleased, but still nodded and stopped.

She wanted to find a chance to talk to Yang Kai alone as well.

Chi Yue and Ai Ou both turned back suddenly, giving Yang Kai a meaningful look before turning away again.

Soon, everyone was gone.

Yang Kai climbed out of bed and sat cross-legged, circulating his Divine Sense to examine himself.

What he saw surprised him greatly.

He realized his strength had increased. After double-checking in disbelief, he confirmed it—he hadn't reached the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm yet, but he was definitely at the peak of the First Stage, much stronger than before.

In the past few days, he'd fought desperately—first against nearly thirty Emperor Realm experts, then against a hundred Emperor Realm Yao Beasts summoned from the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, and finally a life-or-death struggle with Tan Junhao. He'd barely scraped out a win.

All his strength, both Divine Sense and Emperor Yuan, had been nearly exhausted. But after surviving such peril, he'd actually gained something from it.

Although he hadn't fully recovered yet, once he did, his strength would be far greater than before. Even if he faced another battle like the last, he wouldn't end up half-dead again.

Truly, a blessing from the heavens.

He checked his injuries and found they were almost healed. He must have been unconscious for several days, during which his powerful physique and Golden Blood had been working to restore him. The wounds on his chest and shoulder were nearly closed, and his depleted Divine Sense had been completely replenished by the Warm Spirit Lotus.

With another day or two of meditation, he'd be back to full strength.

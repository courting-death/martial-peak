## Chapter 2982 — A Chance to Leave

Zhu Lie remained seated in place without moving in the slightest, but his Dragon Pressure had already been pushed to its limit. The many Demon Kings standing before him were all drenched in sweat. Those with slightly weaker cultivations were even trembling, their faces pale and their chests feeling as though a great mountain were pressing down on them, making it difficult to breathe.

Although the battle between Yang Kai and the Blood Demon had ended in an extremely short time, without Zhu Lie's deterrence, the other Demon Kings could not possibly have stood by and watched. No matter what, they were all members of the Demon Race and could not bring themselves to leave one of their own to die.

In fact, the instant the Blood Demon attacked, several Demon Kings had wanted to lend him a hand, but before they could act, Zhu Lie suppressed them where they stood.

The pressure this red-haired young man placed on the Demon Kings was far greater than that human's. No one was confident they could survive against him, so under duress, they could only wait obediently and secretly pray that they would not be dragged into the calamity.

Now that the dust had settled, the Blood Demon had been severely wounded. Though he still clung to life, he was little more than a walking corpse. This made them even more terrified, chills filling their hearts as they wondered what fate awaited them.

"What exactly are you trying to do?" Zhu Lie frowned and looked at Yang Kai impatiently.

First, Yang Kai had come to Demon Fury City to question a group of Demon Kings. Then, after a few disagreeable words, he had suddenly started fighting. It would have been one thing if he had killed the Blood Demon, but instead, he had deliberately left the man half-dead. It was simply baffling.

"You'll know soon enough," Yang Kai replied lightly. In the distance, Zhu Qing was already flying toward them with Li Jiao following half a step behind her. No one knew what sort of flattery he was spouting as he bowed and scraped, an ingratiating smile plastered across his face, yet Zhu Qing did not respond to him at all.

Once the two arrived, Li Jiao hurried over to pay his respects to Yang Kai, acting exceptionally attentive.

"What happened here?" Zhu Qing looked around in confusion, curious about what had taken place. There seemed to be traces left behind by a battle, but it had clearly lasted only a short while.

"Nothing much." Yang Kai casually brushed off the question, then looked at the little black dog in her arms. "How is it doing?"

"A little better, but it still seems somewhat weak." Zhu Qing reached out and stroked the little black dog's head.

Its condition had indeed improved. When Yang Kai left earlier, the little black dog had been frozen into a block of ice. Although frost still covered its body, it was clearly much better than before.

Moreover, at this moment, the little fellow seemed to have caught the scent of some exceptionally delicious food. It raised its head and stared at the Demon Kings, drool running from the corner of its mouth, looking utterly disgusting.

Under its gaze, the many Demon Kings all reacted as though facing a great enemy. Their hands and feet turned cold, and their eyes darted about evasively.

"Want to eat?" Yang Kai asked with a smile.

"Woof!" The little black dog immediately perked up and struggled to climb out of Zhu Qing's arms.

"You can't just eat anything you please." Zhu Qing frowned and scolded it. She had wanted to say something when she saw Ah Wang swallow two members of the Demon Race earlier, but the subject had been changed. Now that it was reverting to its old ways, she immediately knocked it on the head.

The little black dog whimpered aggrievedly.

Yang Kai reached out, lifted the little black dog from Zhu Qing's arms, and said, "Qing'er, if we want to leave this place, we may really need to let it eat more."

"What?" Zhu Qing frowned, not understanding what Yang Kai meant.

When she looked up, she saw Yang Kai toss the little black dog directly toward the dazed Blood Demon.

While still in midair, Ah Wang became vigorous and energetic, as though infused with new life. A terrifying light burst from its two glossy black eyes as its mouth suddenly split open, expanding to the size of a door. With a single bite, it swallowed the Blood Demon whole.

"Hiss..."

A chorus of sharp gasps rang out. The many Demon Kings stared dumbfoundedly at the little black dog, which had suddenly transformed, their eyes nearly popping from their sockets. The terrifying information Yang Kai had only just stirred up in their memories churned through their minds, gradually aligning with what they saw before them. A conjecture frightening enough to shatter their courage filled their thoughts.

"Guixu!"

The Succubus's shriek seemed capable of piercing the sky. The trace of color that had only just returned to her face vanished once more, leaving her deathly pale. As though all strength had left her body, she collapsed onto the ground.

The other Demon Kings did not fare quite so badly, but they too had clearly been frightened out of their wits. Panic spread through their ranks, leaving every Demon King shaken to the core and their minds in utter turmoil.

A legendary exotic beast they had believed extinct had suddenly appeared before their eyes, something they simply could not accept. After all, Guixu occupied a prominent place in the history of the Demon Race. It was an existence that represented destruction and devastation. Nearly one-third of the entire Demon Domain had been destroyed by its maw, and the Demon Domain's present harsh environment was also largely related to it.

No matter how they thought about it, they could not understand how Guixu could still be alive. Had it not been killed by the Great Demon God? Could the rumor mentioned earlier actually be true? Had Guixu merely feigned death and escaped, awaiting the day it could rise again? If that day truly came, it would certainly seek revenge against the Great Demon God, and the already shattered Demon Domain could no longer withstand another rampage from it.

With so many Demon Kings present, there was no possibility they had mistaken Guixu's identity. Although Guixu appeared perfectly ordinary at first glance, its characteristics were unmistakable once it transformed.

In other words, this was undoubtedly Guixu, and it was extremely likely to be the same terrifying existence that had ravaged the Demon Domain and fought the Great Demon God in a life-or-death battle all those years ago.

No wonder. No wonder this human had previously asked them for all kinds of information about Guixu.

It turned out his claim that he was raising a Guixu was actually true. How laughable that they had all thought he was boasting.

As this realization struck them, the many Demon Kings' faces turned as black as the bottom of a pot.

They seemed to have revealed far too much information that should never have been disclosed, especially the part about how Guixu could grow stronger. That piece of information was simply fatal.

For a time, the Demon Kings were overwhelmed with regret. The Demon King who had revealed that information in particular wished he could tear his own mouth apart.

While the many Demon Kings stood in a daze, the little black dog swallowed the Blood Demon in one bite. Its enormous mouth returned to normal, and a satisfied look appeared on its face. Its belly rose and fell several times, and the frost covering its body began melting at a speed visible to the naked eye. Wisps of cold Qi seeped from its body and merged into the surrounding void, causing the nearby temperature to plummet.

Moreover, under Yang Kai's careful perception, the little black dog's aura seemed to surge dramatically the instant it transformed, rising far beyond the level of a Fifth or Sixth-Tier Yao Beast before returning to normal once the transformation ended.

In just a dozen breaths, the little black dog had fully recovered and no longer appeared weak. It was once again lively and vigorous, its steps nimble. It gazed longingly at the Demon Kings, making all of them tremble with fear.

"Hm?" Zhu Qing stared at the little black dog in surprise, seemingly not expecting that swallowing a Demon King would benefit it so greatly.

Yang Kai explained, "This little thing is an exotic beast from the Demon Domain and seems to be quite famous. There is an unknown space inside its belly capable of devouring all things, while things belonging to the Demon Race seem able to make it stronger."

Zhu Qing said thoughtfully, "In that case, was the Yin Wind it devoured earlier swallowed into that unknown space?"

Yang Kai replied, "That should be the case. I also suspect the size of that space is related to its strength. It isn't strong enough right now, so the space in its belly is too small. Once it devours a certain amount, it can no longer continue."

"It really is formidable." Zhu Qing looked astonished and carefully examined the little black dog as though seeing it anew. The frigid Demonic Qi they had encountered on Yin Wind Mountain had been terrifying, growing denser the farther they advanced. Even she and Yang Kai had been unable to resist it, yet the little black dog could swallow it into another space and had forcibly led them another thousand zhang onward. Such an ability was truly astonishing.

"You want to make it stronger, strong enough to bring us sufficiently deep into Yin Wind Mountain?" Zhu Lie asked with a frown. Yang Kai's intentions were already obvious. Anyone who still could not guess them would truly be a fool.

"Exactly." Yang Kai nodded. "Ah Wang is our only chance right now. As long as it becomes strong enough and the space in its belly grows large enough, it will definitely be able to lead us out."

Zhu Lie considered this for a moment, then nodded in agreement. "This is indeed an opportunity, but... before that, it will need a great deal of food." As he spoke, he glanced at the Demon Kings.

The many Demon Kings' faces turned bitter. They wanted to resist but were powerless to do so, leaving them feeling indescribably aggrieved.

The Succubus sat on the ground in a daze, seemingly never having expected such a fate to await her. She racked her brains for a way to survive. Without the Blood Demon, she might have been able to try using her charms, but the previous battle had left her in an incomparably wretched state. It would take at least a month or two for her injuries to heal, so how could she possibly have enough time to recuperate now?

Yang Kai said, "Of course, this is only my conjecture. I don't know whether it will work. To confirm it, we'll need to conduct an experiment."

"How do you plan to test it?" Zhu Qing asked.

Yang Kai smiled slightly and reached toward the little black dog, picking it up directly. The little black dog looked utterly confused, completely unaware of what sort of treatment it was about to endure.

"Keep a close watch on these fellows. Don't let a single one leave." After issuing this instruction, Yang Kai's figure flickered and disappeared from the spot.

Zhu Lie thoughtfully glanced in the direction of Yin Wind Mountain.

A moment later, he withdrew his gaze and sat as steady as a mountain.

The Succubus remained dazed for a while. She wanted to flee but lacked the courage. After thinking it over, she could only stand and walk toward the other Demon Kings.

She cautiously glanced at Zhu Lie and secretly breathed a sigh of relief when she saw that he had no intention of stopping her.

The many Demon Kings gathered together, their Divine Senses surging as they communicated, clearly discussing ways to preserve their lives. From time to time, faint murderous intent leaked out, making it obvious they wanted to risk everything in one desperate struggle.

After all, none of them were weak. If they truly fought with no regard for the consequences, they might still have a chance to survive. That was better than remaining here as food for Guixu's growth.

Demon Fury City had stood in the Revolving World for more than a hundred thousand years and clashed constantly with Human Sovereign City, but never before had it faced such a crisis.

The experts who had appeared so suddenly were simply beyond their ability to resist.

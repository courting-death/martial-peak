## Chapter 2748 — Don’t Know How to Repay

“Wait a moment!” Seeing that Yang Kai was truly about to leave, Le Dongzheng hurriedly called out.

“Is there something else, Sect Master Le?” Yang Kai tilted his head and looked over.

Le Dongzheng narrowed his eyes, coldly staring at Yang Kai for a long while before finally speaking: “Those three disciples of mine, where are they now?”

“Ha!” Yang Kai’s expression brightened, looking at Le Dongzheng with mockery. “I thought Sect Master Le really knew nothing, but it turns out those three acted under your orders.”

Bian Yuqing’s face turned pale, her gaze blazing as she stared at Le Dongzheng. If that was true, then Le Dongzheng was the true culprit behind Kou Wu’s death. Without his instructions, she and Kou Wu wouldn’t have been hunted by those three.

Le Dongzheng acted as if he hadn’t heard, simply saying, “You still haven’t answered this old man’s question.”

“Want to know?” Yang Kai grinned, “Figure it out yourself!”

He turned and walked away.

Le Dongzheng was so furious smoke seemed to pour from his seven orifices. If not for Linghu City’s prohibition on fighting, he would have already rushed up to battle Yang Kai to the death. One of those three disciples was his own descendant, whom he had high hopes for. Hearing Yang Kai’s tone, those three were likely doomed, which made Le Dongzheng’s heart ache.

Gritting his teeth, he growled, “Boy, don’t let this old man see you outside the city, or I’ll kill you!”

Yang Kai snorted, not even bothering to turn his head, leaving Le Dongzheng with a feeling of powerlessness, as if he’d punched cotton. He nearly spat blood from rage.

Staring venomously at Yang Kai’s back, he secretly resolved to make that brat suffer, or else his three disciples would have died in vain.

……

“What are you doing? Go back and wait in line!”

In front of Linghu Palace, a Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator wearing Tianwu Sacred Land robes reached out to stop Yang Kai, his face full of arrogance.

As a disciple of a top sect in the Southern Territory, he naturally had reason to be proud. What’s more, he was still young and already possessed Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation. His chances of advancing to the Emperor Realm in the future were quite high.

Linghu Palace was built by Star God Palace, together with the three top sects of the Southern Territory, and was managed jointly by them.

Yang Kai took out a token and waved it in front of the man.

The Tianwu Sacred Land disciple instantly dropped his arrogance, cupping his fists. “So you’re a Senior Brother from Qingyang Shrine. May I ask your name, Senior Brother?”

Yang Kai held the Qingyang Gold Token, which only core disciples were qualified to possess. Though this man was from Tianwu Sacred Land, he was merely an inner disciple, far below a core disciple. Confirming Yang Kai’s identity, he dared not act presumptuously.

Bian Yuqing’s eyes widened. She had seen firsthand how arrogant and dismissive those guarding Linghu Palace’s entrance could be—even Emperor Realm cultivators sometimes got attitude. Yet, seeing Yang Kai, the guard cupped his fists in respect. Hearing his words, Bian Yuqing suddenly recalled that when they went to Four Seasons Land years ago, Yang Kai had accompanied Qingyang Shrine.

So Yang Kai was now a disciple of Qingyang Shrine, and judging by the scene, his status wasn’t low. Otherwise, the gatekeeper wouldn’t have dropped his posture.

“Yang Kai!”

The gatekeeper frowned, thinking carefully, but found the name unfamiliar—he’d never heard of it.

He knew most of Qingyang Shrine’s core disciples, but Yang Kai wasn’t among them. However, the Qingyang Gold Token couldn’t be faked; possessing it meant Yang Kai was truly a core disciple.

Years ago, Yang Kai had refined the Supreme Mystic Pill in Four Seasons Land, making his name famous throughout the Southern Territory. But so many years had passed, things had quieted down. Many cultivators spent years in seclusion, so it was normal not to know Yang Kai’s name. The gatekeeper was one of them.

“So you’re Senior Brother Yang!” Though he didn’t recognize Yang Kai, the guard still showed proper respect. “May I ask, Senior Brother Yang, are you here to use the secret cultivation grounds?”

“That’s right!” Yang Kai nodded.

When Gao Xueting handed him the Qingyang Gold Token, she’d said that Linghu Palace, aside from the three main levels of secret cultivation grounds, had even better ones reserved for disciples of the three top sects. With the Gold Token, one could enter for free.

Otherwise, he wouldn’t have sold Bian Yuqing’s Qing Jade Command Token. Since he had a better option, the Qing Jade Command Token was useless.

“Please follow me, Senior Brother Yang. I’ll take you to see the steward.” The man said, turning to lead the way.

Yang Kai beckoned to Bian Yuqing and followed.

At this moment, Bian Yuqing understood Yang Kai’s intentions. Gratitude filled her eyes, though she couldn’t help but smile bitterly. She had spent all her savings to obtain a Qing Jade Command Token, only to wait eight months for her turn. Yet Yang Kai, simply by arriving, could enter the secret cultivation grounds immediately. Comparing their situations, Bian Yuqing realized the privileges enjoyed by disciples of great sects were beyond anything she could imagine. A wave of helplessness and discouragement washed over her.

Without a strong background, even if one became an Emperor, they couldn’t show off in front of these top sect disciples.

“What are you doing?” Yang Kai suddenly turned to look at Bian Yuqing, frowning. He noticed her state was off.

Bian Yuqing was startled, suddenly realizing how she’d fallen into such despair. Once that thought took hold, her already slim hope of breaking through to Emperor became even slimmer.

She immediately calmed her mind, her gaze growing resolute!

Yes, Kou Wu had died to protect the Qing Jade Command Token, just to leave her a chance to break through. If she didn’t give a satisfactory account, how could she face Kou Wu in the Nine Nether Hell someday?

Kou Wu’s blood must not be shed in vain.

“It’s nothing!” Bian Yuqing smiled, her whole body radiating confidence and determination. She felt that if she could find a good place to go into seclusion right now, her chances of breaking through to Emperor would rise to thirty percent!

She originally only had ten percent, so thirty percent was already very high.

Yang Kai stared at Bian Yuqing for a while before turning away.

He didn’t understand the inner struggle Bian Yuqing had just gone through, but seeing her in good spirits, he was pleased.

The Tianwu Sacred Land disciple led Yang Kai and Bian Yuqing to a private room, then said, “Senior Brother Yang, please wait here. I’ll go invite the steward.”

“Thank you!” Yang Kai nodded.

The man turned and left.

“Thank you!” Bian Yuqing suddenly spoke. This was the second time she’d thanked Yang Kai since meeting him, and each time was heartfelt. In all her years, no one had ever treated her so well. She was deeply moved.

Yang Kai smiled, “It’s nothing!”

As he spoke, he casually tossed a few jade bottles over.

Bian Yuqing caught them, looking at Yang Kai suspiciously. “These are…?”

“You can use them when you go into seclusion to cultivate,” Yang Kai explained.

Curious, Bian Yuqing opened one jade bottle and was instantly shocked, her eyes wide. “These are Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills?”

She checked another bottle—also Imperial-Grade Spiritual Pills. The first bottle’s pills looked and smelled like the legendary Purifying Heart Pill, which could help cultivators clear distracting thoughts and reduce the risk of inner demons during cultivation.

They were available on the market, but never at Imperial-Grade. The best were only Middle-Level Dao Origin-Grade, worlds apart from the pills in the jade bottle.

The other bottle seemed to contain Heavenly Dao Pills, which were even more incredible. Taking them could help cultivators comprehend the power of the Heavenly Dao and the laws of heaven and earth…

With these two bottles of Spiritual Pills, her original thirty percent chance would rise to at least fifty percent! That was half. If she failed to break through this time, she might never get such a good opportunity again.

Clutching the two jade bottles tightly, Bian Yuqing was overwhelmed, her gaze complicated as she looked at Yang Kai. “You’re giving me such a great gift, I don’t know how to repay you.”

“I’m not looking for repayment,” Yang Kai stroked his chin, pondering. “But if you really want to repay me… join my sect.”

“Qingyang Shrine?” Bian Yuqing was stunned.

That was another huge gift—how could it count as repayment? There were tens of thousands cultivators like her who wanted to join Qingyang Shrine. As one of the top sects in the Southern Territory, it wasn’t something you could just enter.

If she could really join Qingyang Shrine—even as the lowest-ranked disciple—it would be far better than wandering the world alone. For a woman, especially one as beautiful and alluring as her, traveling by herself was bound to attract trouble. Sometimes, even when she kept to herself, trouble would still find her.

“No, no, not Qingyang Shrine,” Yang Kai shook his head.

“Not Qingyang Shrine?” Bian Yuqing was confused.

Yang Kai smiled, “The sect I founded myself. Right now, it has… well, less than ten people. It’s just starting out and needs manpower. Protector Bian, are you interested?”

“Alright!” Bian Yuqing didn’t hesitate, immediately agreeing. Yang Kai had helped her so much—she wouldn’t hesitate even if he asked for her hand in marriage, let alone just joining a sect.

Bian Yuqing didn’t think Yang Kai’s sect was very powerful. Though he was an Emperor and strong, every sect had to develop. With less than ten people, it was clearly a small sect, perhaps not even as good as Biyu Sect back then.

But so what? As long as everyone worked together, the sect would grow strong one day. If she could break through to the Emperor Realm, she’d surely be a great help to Yang Kai and could repay today’s kindness.

“You agreed so quickly!” Yang Kai grinned.

Bian Yuqing tucked a strand of hair behind her ear and smiled, “I’m someone who knows how to repay kindness. Don’t worry, if I make it out of the secret grounds safely, I’ll follow you to your sect. If I die in there… that’s just my fate.”

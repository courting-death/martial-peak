## Chapter 3063 — Fight! Fight! Fight!

In the span of a finger snap, an Eighth-Tier Dragon had been crippled.

Strictly speaking, "crippled" was not quite accurate. Yang Kai had merely torn off one of his Dragon Scales. Such an injury was practically nothing, and anywhere else, that Eighth-Tier Dragon would still possess his full combat strength, almost completely unaffected.

But without the Dragon Temple's protection, he could not even maintain his True Dragon form beneath the pressure of Yang Kai's Dragon aura. How could he possibly continue fighting?

Although he had suffered no serious harm, he had already lost the right to take part in the battle.

The lightning speed and direct, ruthless manner in which Yang Kai had acted made the eyelids of every Dragon present twitch.

At the same time Yang Kai attacked the Eighth-Tier Dragon, the several hundred mortals and Fu Chi's women finally came to their senses. Screaming in terror, they fled in every direction.

A great battle was about to erupt here, so how could they dare remain? In the blink of an eye, all bystanders had vanished from before the palace. Only those connected to today's affair remained.

Zhu Qing and her brother, Jiu Feng, Liu Yan, and Yang Kai himself comprised the entirety of Yang Kai's five-person fighting force.

The Dragon Clan, by contrast, still had more than a dozen members. The disparity in numbers was enormous.

Yet in terms of sheer presence, Yang Kai stood head and shoulders above everyone else. Carrying the unstoppable momentum of one leading the charge, the weight of his presence actually left the many Dragons struggling to breathe.

"Hahahaha!" Jiu Feng laughed loudly. "Well done, brat! These arrogant fellows deserve a thorough beating."

Because of what had happened more than ten years ago, relations between Spirit Beast Island and Dragon Isle were rather strained. Jiu Feng was naturally delighted to see the Dragon Clan suffer, and she found Yang Kai much more pleasing to the eye as well. No wonder Young Lady could not stop thinking about this brat. He truly was no pushover.

Secretly, she rejoiced that she had not brought Young Lady along today. If that little girl had witnessed Yang Kai's unrivalled valour just now, would she not have been utterly captivated? Young Lady was at precisely the age when a maiden's heart first stirred and was especially susceptible to a man's heroic bearing.

Yang Kai swept a disdainful gaze around him and shouted in a deep voice, "I will ask one last time. Will you move aside or not?"

The Dragon Clan gave no answer. Only pairs of hostile, furious eyes stared back at him.

"Then let us fight!" Yang Kai growled. Scorching breath burst from his nostrils as he stamped both feet against the ground.

With a thunderous boom, the entire Spirit Island seemed to tremble. His enormous body shot into the sky like a cannonball and hurtled straight toward one of the Dragons.

An earth-shaking battle erupted in an instant.

After what had happened earlier, how could the gathered Dragons fail to understand what he intended? The moment they saw Yang Kai pounce, they all released resounding Dragon roars and revealed their True Dragon forms.

In an instant, red, white, green, and blue Dragons filled the sky.

The smallest was more than ten zhang long, while the largest exceeded twenty zhang. One enormous Dragon after another coiled across the void, their Dragon Pressure spreading outward and making the four corners of the world tremble as Heaven and Earth seemed to turn upside down.

Before Yang Kai could reach them, the dozen or so Dragons opened their mouths together and unleashed streams of incomparably pure Dragon Breath from above.

Scorching Dragon Flames, freezing Dragon Breath, emerald-green poison, water as heavy as mountains, and wind blades that seemed capable of slicing apart Heaven and Earth came crashing down toward Yang Kai from every direction, their terrifying might seemingly enough to destroy the world.

Yang Kai growled as abstruse syllables in the Dragon Language rang from his mouth. He opened his jaws and spat out a dazzling golden radiance that spread above his head and transformed into a protective barrier.

Amid a series of deafening booms, every attack struck the golden barrier. In a single instant, the barrier was blasted to pieces, and the remaining force of the attacks continued unabated, crashing into Yang Kai's body one after another.

Yang Kai had charged out quickly, and he came back just as quickly.

His enormous body plummeted onto the island and smashed a deep crater into the ground, sending dust billowing into the air. A dense web of wounds covered his exposed skin.

The Dragons were overjoyed at the sight and were just about to press their advantage and finish him while he was weakened when, to their horror, Yang Kai clambered out of the crater, shaking his head. His already terrifying eyes shone with a ravenous red light. Like an enraged beast, he glanced up at them, flickered, and instantly vanished from the spot.

The Dragons' expressions changed as every instinct screamed of danger.

Their vision blurred, and Yang Kai appeared nearby. Reaching out with one hand, he grabbed at a Dragon more than ten zhang long.

That Dragon had long been on guard. The moment Yang Kai appeared, he immediately tried to flee into the distance, while the surrounding Dragons also spread out warily.

"Condense!" Yang Kai roared. As the Laws of Space surged, they transformed into an invisible cage that enveloped the Dragon more than ten zhang long. The surrounding space became a quagmire with an immensely powerful restraining force.

For that brief instant, the Dragon could not break free. Recalling Yang Kai's vicious methods from earlier, he released a terrified roar, turned his head, and snapped at Yang Kai. His jaws opened wide, revealing teeth as sharp as guillotine blades, a sight that made one's blood run cold.

Yang Kai's grasping hand did not slow. He clenched his other hand into a fist and smashed it viciously into the Dragon's head.

The punch carried such tremendous force that it knocked the Dragon's head to one side and sent a patch of scales flying from his skull, leaving him dizzy and disoriented.

Seizing the opportunity, Yang Kai grabbed hold of his tail.

Before Yang Kai could repeat his earlier method, another Dragon had already wrapped itself around him. Its enormous body spiralled upward from Yang Kai's feet like a python constricting its prey, binding him in place in an instant and squeezing his body with all its might.

Seeing this, the surrounding Dragons immediately rushed to the rescue.

It was bad enough that Yang Kai had caught them off guard and rendered one of their clansmen unable to fight. If it happened again, the Dragon Clan would truly lose all face today. Almost every Dragon on Dragon Isle had turned out in force, so how could they allow a mere human to run rampant here?

Zhu Qing and the others finally charged forward as well.

The Dragon Clan had clearly prepared for this, however. No one knew who secretly gave the order, but the dozen or so Dragons each took up a clearly defined role. They moved to intercept Zhu Qing and the others, stopping them in their tracks.

"Elder Sister Qing, please do not make things difficult for me. I do not want to be your enemy either, but I have no choice." A Purple Dragon barred Zhu Qing's path. Her jaws opened and closed as she spoke, and her Dragon eyes were filled with conflict.

This Purple Dragon was undoubtedly Fu Ling.

She possessed only a Sixth-Tier Dragon Bloodline, so she had little confidence in facing Zhu Qing's Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline head-on. Without the Dragon Temple's protection, she could never have dared stand in Zhu Qing's way. The suppression of Zhu Qing's bloodline alone would have left her unable to act freely.

Beside Fu Ling was a Blue Dragon slightly longer than her. Hearing this, the Blue Dragon echoed, "Elder Sister Qing, please stop here!"

This Blue Dragon was the Dragon woman who had carried a flower basket with Fu Ling earlier and scattered a rain of blossoms along the way. Her bloodline was slightly higher than Fu Ling's, but it was still only Seventh-Tier.

With the Dragon Temple's protection, however, she and Fu Ling could contend with Zhu Qing if they joined forces and might even gain a slight advantage.

Zhu Qing's expression turned fierce as she shouted, "Get out of my way!"

The Seventh-Tier Blue Dragon shook her head. "Please forgive me, but I cannot comply."

Zhu Qing wasted no more words. As a Dragon roar rang out, she transformed into a thirty-zhang-long Red Dragon. Monstrous waves of heat rolled from her body as though flames were burning across it. She was fully twice Fu Ling's size, causing both Fu Ling and the Blue Dragon to swallow involuntarily.

Although they had long heard that Zhu Qing's Dragon Bloodline had advanced to the Ninth Tier, those had only been rumours. Now that they saw her with their own eyes, they finally knew the rumours were true.

Before Zhu Qing's advancement, only two Ninth-Tier Dragons existed on all of Dragon Isle: the Third Elder and Fourth Elder.

The Third Elder had been exiled to the Dragon Tomb more than ten years ago, with no word of whether she was alive or dead. Fourth Elder, meanwhile, had a relatively amiable temperament, so the many members of the Dragon Clan had never felt much pressure in his presence.

Only now did the two Dragon women understand just how vast the gap was between them and a Ninth-Tier Dragon. Even with the Dragon Temple's protection, they still felt deeply apprehensive when faced with Zhu Qing's True Dragon form.

The moment Zhu Qing revealed her True Dragon form, she transformed into a streak of red light and rammed toward Fu Ling and the Blue Dragon. Like a blazing inferno, the heat radiating from her distorted the surrounding space.

Fu Ling and the Blue Dragon hurriedly moved to intercept her.

Zhu Qing was stopped, and Zhu Lie met the same fate.

He had already assumed his True Dragon form and become locked in fierce combat with another Eighth-Tier Dragon. They clawed, bit, and lashed out with their tails, unleashing Dragon Clan Secret Arts from time to time. They fought as though they shared some irreconcilable blood feud and battled their way high into the sky in the blink of an eye, their clash incomparably fierce.

Elsewhere, Jiu Feng single-handedly held her own against two Dragons. Her movements were graceful and elusive as she darted back and forth, weaving and turning through the air, infuriating the two enormous Dragons trying to surround and intercept her.

It was an awful feeling. They possessed tremendous strength, yet Jiu Feng stubbornly refused to confront them head-on and seemed to be toying with them instead. The longer they fought, the angrier they became.

"Little girl, get out of the way if you do not want to die!"

Fu Qi faced Liu Yan alone. The contrast between his body, more than ten zhang long, and her tiny figure was striking. His house-sized Dragon head drew close to Liu Yan as a booming voice rumbled from his mouth. It was not that Fu Qi was merciful; the Dragon Clan simply had its pride. What would it prove to attack a little girl who appeared only seven or eight years old?

But he soon learned that he was wrong, terribly wrong.

This delicate, adorable little girl, who looked as though she had been carved from jade, actually contained a power in no way inferior to his own.

As a clear and resonant Phoenix cry rang out, two brilliant streams of flame suddenly spread from Liu Yan's back. The flowing fire transformed into a pair of wings behind her, blazing with a loud whoosh.

At the same time, Liu Yan's petite body rapidly grew. Amid a wave of twisting transformations, a Fire Bird no smaller than Fu Chi emerged in a blaze of splendour.

The Fire Bird possessed a beautiful form, but her eyes held an arrogance even greater than that of the Dragon Clan, along with the majesty of a sovereign.

"The Phoenix Race!" Fu Qi exclaimed, his Dragon eyes narrowing involuntarily.

Although he knew this little girl could not possibly be as harmless as she appeared, he had never imagined that she was actually a member of the Phoenix Race.

Dragon and Phoenix were always spoken of together. Though the Dragon came first, the Phoenix Race was no weaker.

Among the Divine Spirits, it was truly difficult to say whether Dragons or Phoenixes were stronger. If one had to compare them, however, the Phoenix Race was even rarer than the Dragon Clan. At the very least, the Dragon Clan still had Dragon Isle and its own inheritance, while no news of the Phoenix Race seemed to have surfaced for countless years.

Luan Feng of the Eastern Territory's Wild Ancient Land was not a true member of the Phoenix Race; she belonged only to one of its branches. Even Jiu Feng, who had come here today, did not seem to count as a true member of the Phoenix Race.

But the one before him was unquestionably a Phoenix of the purest lineage!

Fu Qi's heart tightened, and he no longer dared underestimate her.

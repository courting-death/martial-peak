## Chapter 2996 — Old Yet Vigorous

The pair had only just left when a strange figure suddenly appeared outside the restaurant. He materialized out of thin air as though he had been standing there all along, yet strangely enough, none of the passers-by noticed him.

This man had a burly build and an imposing appearance. He wore a dark robe, and thick stubble covered his chin, giving him an extremely bold and rugged air. At that moment, he was staring in the direction the Flowing Cloud Shuttle had departed, his face filled with bitter anguish. Veins throbbed wildly on his forehead, twisting his entire expression into something rather fierce and terrifying.

Only after a long while did his figure sway. Clutching his chest, he let out a heavy sigh.

After sighing, he suddenly vanished again.

This startled the people nearby, who rubbed their eyes one after another, wondering whether they had seen things.

...

Wind and Cloud Pavilion was considered a top Sect in the Western Territory, its status equivalent to that of Qingyang Shrine in the Southern Territory. Its old Pavilion Master, Hua Xing, possessed a formidable Third-Stage Emperor Realm cultivation and was the pillar that held Wind and Cloud Pavilion steady. Even throughout the entire Western Territory, he was a renowned figure.

The great Sect was prosperous and flourishing, with a legacy stretching back more than ten thousand years. Whenever its disciples travelled through the Western Territory, everyone would show them a measure of respect.

The disciples of Wind and Cloud Pavilion also took great pride in their Sect.

Yet someone had actually dared to slap such a powerful Sect in the face.

A month ago, the Young Pavilion Master, who had gone out for pleasure, was killed in a city three hundred thousand li from Wind and Cloud Pavilion's headquarters! When the news returned, the entire Sect was shaken. The old Pavilion Master coughed up blood on the spot and, overcome by rage, fainted outright.

It was not that the old Pavilion Master's mental fortitude was lacking. He was, after all, a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master who had weathered countless storms. Ordinarily, even if the sky collapsed, he would not so much as furrow his brow. This matter, however, was simply more than he could bear.

In his youth, the old Pavilion Master, Hua Xing, had devoted himself wholeheartedly to pursuing the Martial Dao. He cultivated arduously and travelled across the Star Realm's Four Great Territories, enduring countless trials and hardships and narrowly escaping death innumerable times. After spending two thousand years, he finally cultivated to the Third-Stage Emperor Realm. Only when he realized that his cultivation had reached its limit and that he had no hope of advancing another step in this lifetime did Hua Xing return to the Western Territory and inherit the position of Wind and Cloud Pavilion's Pavilion Master.

His own Martial Dao had reached its end and all his hopes had been severed, so Hua Xing could only place them upon the next generation, hoping that one of his descendants would possess aptitude far surpassing his own and have a chance to reach the level of a Great Emperor.

He understood better than anyone that although the Third-Stage Emperor Realm appeared infinitely glorious, before a figure like a Great Emperor, he was still no different from an ant.

A single thought from any Great Emperor would be enough to sever Wind and Cloud Pavilion's ten-thousand-year legacy.

After returning to Wind and Cloud Pavilion, he developed the Sect while taking wives and concubines far and wide. In just ten short years, his harem grew to over a thousand beauties, with women of every figure and each possessing her own charms.

Yet the Heavenly Dao took from those who had an excess to make up for those who lacked. The higher his cultivation grew, the slimmer his chances of siring offspring became. He had guarded his Yang Essence all his life and kept away from women, only beginning to toil day and night after reaching the Third-Stage Emperor Realm. Yet despite sowing seed far and wide, none of it showed the slightest sign of taking root.

It was not until thirty years later that one of his wives and concubines finally gave birth to a baby boy.

Hua Xing treasured the child as though he had obtained a priceless treasure and began cultivating him with painstaking care.

He had thought this baby boy was his hope, but as the child grew, Hua Xing discovered that his aptitude was far inferior to his own. Even with all of Wind and Cloud Pavilion supporting him and Hua Xing personally teaching him, his cultivation progressed only through constant stumbles and setbacks.

With such aptitude, reaching the Emperor Realm would already be a miracle, let alone attaining the level of a Great Emperor.

After realizing this, Hua Xing immediately changed his approach. He decided not to pin his hopes on this child and, as soon as the boy came of age, arranged for him to marry numerous beautiful wives and concubines while vigorously encouraging him to go out and seek pleasure among other women.

The Young Pavilion Master had never been suited to cultivation in the first place. The old Pavilion Master's heavy pressure during his youth had left him instinctively resistant to cultivating, and his aptitude was also insufficient. His entire cultivation had been piled up using precious resources and Imperial Treasures, so once the old Pavilion Master gave his permission, what scruples could he possibly have left?

He spent most of his days rolling around in bed with women. In time, however, he grew tired of his own delicate wives and beautiful concubines, so it was only natural that he began seeking amusement elsewhere.

The old Pavilion Master did not object and even dispatched a Second-Stage Emperor Realm expert to protect him personally.

Yet trouble struck on his very first trip outside.

Trouble itself would have been one thing. The old Pavilion Master was not actually very fond of this son. If the boy had left behind an heir, then he could die and it would have been no great matter. The crucial point was that he had not left behind a single descendant!

This was equivalent to severing the Hua Family's bloodline.

Hua Xing could not accept it. On the one hand, he cursed his unfilial son for failing to recognize whom he should not provoke; on the other, he hated the person who had killed the Young Pavilion Master and swore to tear them into ten thousand pieces to vent the hatred in his heart.

He immediately issued a public bounty. The reward of twenty million Source Crystals truly tempted many experts, but every piece of news that returned from outside left Hua Xing badly shaken.

The woman who had killed his son was actually extraordinarily powerful. In recent days, many experts seeking the bounty had died at her hands.

Moreover, judging by the direction the woman was travelling, she was actually heading straight for Wind and Cloud Pavilion.

Hua Xing flew into a rage. If that woman were truly allowed to fight her way into Wind and Cloud Pavilion, the Sect would lose all face. He increased the bounty to attract more helpers while also dispatching experts from the Pavilion to intercept and kill her, but the results were far from satisfactory. Not only had the helpers drawn by the bounty proved useless, but several Emperor Realm Masters from his own Pavilion had also died one after another.

Reports even claimed that the woman had found a companion from somewhere. The two now travelled together and swept through everything in their path, utterly unstoppable.

Inside the rear hall of Wind and Cloud Pavilion...

A pink haze filled the vast hall. Beautiful bodies lay strewn all over the floor, their eyes glazed and their breaths coming in soft gasps. The constant sounds of pleasure were enough to make anyone who heard them blush and grow parched.

Although the old Pavilion Master was advanced in age, he was still extremely robust. At this moment, he was drenched in sweat as he ploughed away atop one of his beautiful concubines. Every thrust made the woman pinned beneath him cry out, though whether from pain or pleasure was impossible to tell.

Several other completely naked beauties coiled around Hua Xing like snakes, moaning softly as they continuously begged for more.

It was a scene of thunder and fire, wind rising and clouds surging. The entire rear hall was in complete disarray, and the air was thick with lust.

No one knew how long had passed. Only when all the women lay on the floor, panting weakly, did Hua Xing let out a low growl and release his seed.

"Knock, knock, knock!"

A knock suddenly came from outside.

Hua Xing swept a cold gaze around him, not the slightest trace of pity on his face. With nothing more than a soft snort, he walked outside just as he was.

When he opened the door, he found a beautiful mature woman standing outside with her head lowered submissively, though her expression was somewhat flustered.

"What is it?" Hua Xing glanced at her coldly, faintly sensing that something had happened.

The beautiful woman replied in fear and trepidation, "Reporting to Pavilion Master, that wretched pair has already fought their way to Changyun City."

A fierce light flashed through Hua Xing's eyes when he heard this, and he gritted his teeth. "So they really have come. They truly don’t take this Lord seriously at all. These Juniors are going too far!"

As he released the aura of a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master, the beautiful woman's whole body instantly went limp, and she fell to her knees with a thud.

Hua Xing looked at her, then reached out and pressed a hand atop her head.

The beautiful woman's face went deathly pale as she called out in a trembling voice, "Pavilion Master..."

Hua Xing said coldly, "What are you afraid of? This Lord is not going to kill you."

As he spoke, he exerted a little force with his hand and guided the beautiful woman toward his lower body.

The beautiful woman relaxed, knowing that her life was not in danger. She hurriedly opened her small mouth and began swallowing and spitting, cleaning away the filth. Judging by her manner and practiced movements, this was clearly not her first time doing such a thing.

Hua Xing narrowed his eyes slightly, and the coldness on his face deepened.

Although he did not know the origins of that wretched pair, judging from the intelligence he had received over the past few days, neither of them seemed ordinary.

The two were only at the First-Stage Emperor Realm, yet as they fought their way here, they had actually killed many Second-Stage experts. This was clearly not something ordinary people could accomplish.

Wind and Cloud Pavilion had lost many people trying to intercept and kill them over the past few days. Several Emperor Realm Masters alone had died, causing the entire Wind and Cloud Pavilion to become deeply wary of the man and woman.

Hua Xing was also somewhat apprehensive. The more he learned, the more he felt that this man and woman had extraordinary backgrounds.

Although he was at the Third-Stage Emperor Realm, there were still people in this world he could not afford to provoke. There was no need even to mention the elusive Great Emperors; among others at the Third-Stage Emperor Realm, some were also stronger than him. If he truly provoked such people, Wind and Cloud Pavilion's days would become difficult.

But how could he leave the grudge of having his bloodline severed unavenged?

Perhaps... he could have that person test the waters. If this allowed him to glimpse the origins of that wretched pair, so much the better. Even if it did not, that person was still more than capable of capturing them, and when the time came, Hua Xing would not be the one who had offended their backers.

Compared to Hua Xing, that person was only stronger, not weaker.

Coincidentally, that person had arrived at Wind and Cloud Pavilion as a guest some time ago, almost as though the Heavens had sent him here to help. Would Hua Xing not be a fool if he failed to make use of him?

Having eaten his food and accepted his gifts, that person could hardly refuse him. He had been a guest at Wind and Cloud Pavilion for some time, during which Hua Xing had entertained him with fine food and wine and gifted him countless beauties. Surely he could help with such a small matter.

Once this thought occurred to him, Hua Xing made his decision. He waved a hand and pushed aside the beautiful woman before his lower body, who was still swallowing and spitting as she gasped softly, then strode outside. Within a few steps, a set of clothes had already appeared on his body.

A palace stood atop a mountain peak, and at this moment, it was bustling with activity.

More than twenty young women dressed in thin gauze danced gracefully through the hall. The gauze was nearly transparent, and as they moved, the lovely parts of their exquisite bodies appeared and disappeared from view. Whether intentionally or otherwise, they continually displayed themselves to a great figure seated above.

Above them, a middle-aged man narrowed his eyes in complete pleasure and comfort. Reclining against a large chair with a beauty in each arm, he admired the singing and dancing girls below while enjoying the service of the women in his embrace. He felt all the frustration he had suffered some days ago drain away, and his entire body regained its vitality.

This world was still beautiful after all.

He was a dignified Third-Stage Emperor Realm expert, so why should he have to act according to another's mood? Was it not far better to enjoy himself like this?

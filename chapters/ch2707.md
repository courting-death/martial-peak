## Chapter 2707 — Trade for the Token

"Absolutely genuine!" Yang Kai grinned slyly, putting the Dragon Isle Token away again. After a pause, he asked, "By the way, there's a rumor that holding the Dragon Isle Token lets you go to Dragon Isle and make a request that your Dragon Clan will fulfill. Is that true?"

Zhu Qing replied, "Of course it's true."

"Any kind of request?" Yang Kai's eyes lit up, realizing just how valuable the Dragon Isle Token was.

"For the most part, yes. As long as we can do it, and it doesn't go against the Dragon Clan's principles," Zhu Qing nodded.

Yang Kai's heart stirred. He wondered if he could use the Dragon Isle Token to go to Dragon Isle and ask for Zhu Qing herself—would the Dragon Clan agree? Probably not. He might even offend them.

Shaking his head, Yang Kai stopped letting his thoughts run wild and went back to checking through Tan Junhao's other treasures.

"Are you... planning to go to Dragon Isle?" Zhu Qing suddenly grew interested and asked softly.

If she could persuade Yang Kai to bring the Dragon Isle Token to Dragon Isle, her mission would be as good as complete. No matter what, the final Dragon Isle Token had been found, and the one who bore the Ancestral Dragon Source would be brought to Dragon Isle itself. Such an achievement would be more than enough to earn her entry into the forbidden grounds to strengthen her bloodline.

"What would I do there?" Yang Kai shook his head as he sorted through the many treasures.

Zhu Qing pressed, "Don't you have any wishes you want fulfilled? With this Dragon Isle Token, our Dragon Clan will make it happen for you."

"Actually, there is one thing!" Yang Kai suddenly looked up, as if something had just come to mind.

"What is it? Tell me," Zhu Qing said excitedly.

"I came from a lower Star Domain called Hengluo Star Domain. I want to go back for a visit. Can your Dragon Isle send me back?" Yang Kai looked at Zhu Qing as he spoke. Now that he had a foothold in the Star Realm, with his own territory and a much better cultivation environment than Dark Star, he naturally wanted to bring his family and friends over.

But he had never known how to return to Hengluo Star Domain. He had planned to wait until he found Yang Yan before figuring it out, but now that he had the Dragon Isle Token, maybe the Dragon Clan could help.

The Dragon Clan had passed down their knowledge for countless years and knew everything about the world. They surely knew how to return to Hengluo Star Domain.

"Return to a lower Star Domain?" Zhu Qing frowned at his words.

"Is it difficult?" Yang Kai could tell from her expression that things weren't as simple as he'd hoped.

Zhu Qing said, "It really is difficult. You should know that ever since the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor ravaged so many Star Domains tens of thousands of years ago, all the passages between the Star Realm and those Star Domains were sealed. Many of the connections between the Star Realm and the lower Star Domains were cut off."

Yang Kai had heard about this before. Tens of thousands of years ago, it wasn't too hard for people from the Star Realm to go to lower Star Domains, though there was a price. But after the Heaven Devouring Great Emperor's rampage, all the passages to the lower Star Domains were sealed, to prevent powerful experts from entering and doing as they pleased. After all, the cultivators in those Star Domains were much weaker—Void King Realm was the peak, and some Star Domains didn't even have Void King Realm cultivators.

Even a Dao Origin Realm cultivator from the Star Realm could sweep through a lower Star Domain. The Heaven Devouring Great Emperor was the best example—he devastated countless Star Domains, devouring all the Stellar Origins wherever he went, causing Cultivation Stars to collapse and billions of cultivators to perish.

"Now, the places where those sealed passages are have the Star Court guarding them. There are always Great Emperor-level experts stationed there, so no one can force their way through."

"Star Court?" Yang Kai frowned.

Zhu Qing explained, "The Star Court is a very powerful organization in the Star Realm. It's not a Sect, but was founded by the current Ten Great Emperors working together. They guard the sealed passageways, taking turns. As far as I know, the one currently stationed at the Star Court is the Iron Blood Great Emperor—the most skilled at warfare among the Ten Great Emperors."

"I've never heard of it," Yang Kai said in surprise, feeling like his horizons had just expanded.

Zhu Qing smiled, "The Star Court is very mysterious. Ordinary people wouldn't have heard of it. Maybe your elders know—ask them when you get the chance."

Yang Kai suddenly understood.

If he were from a family with a legacy in the Emperor Realm, maybe he could have heard about the Star Court from his elders. But he was all alone, and hadn't been in the Star Realm long. Who would have told him?

"The Star Court's authority is unshakable. Not only was it founded by the Ten Great Emperors, but there are rumors that several unknown Great Emperor-level experts are there as well. Even our Dragon Clan can't afford to provoke them lightly."

"Unknown Great Emperors!" Yang Kai was shocked. "Aren't there only ten Great Emperors?"

Zhu Qing laughed, "Who told you there are only ten?"

Yang Kai was speechless.

Right, no one had ever told him there were only ten. He'd just always heard people talk about the Ten Great Emperors and assumed that was all there were.

"Great Emperors live extremely long lives. Maybe not as long as heaven and earth, but they don't die easily. Over the years, even though it's hard to become a Great Emperor, there are always some extraordinary people who succeed and join their ranks—just that no one knows about them. For example, our Dragon Clan Elders are comparable to Great Emperors, but they're not counted among the Ten Great Emperors."

"What about you?" Yang Kai asked.

Zhu Qing smiled and said, "I'm still far from that. A Great Emperor... is a special kind of existence, not something just anyone can become."

"Then I have to work hard," Yang Kai said seriously.

Zhu Qing looked at him in surprise, realizing he wasn't joking. He was truly determined, as if he really wanted to reach the realm of Great Emperor. Her first reaction was to find it funny—if becoming a Great Emperor was just a matter of hard work, there would be countless Great Emperors in the world by now.

But then she thought, if it was this man in front of her, maybe... it wasn't impossible.

With only First Stage of the Emperor Realm cultivation, he had defeated so many stronger enemies. That alone was incredible. How many of the Great Emperors could have done that when they were young?

It was truly astonishing. When the Great Emperors were young, how many could compare to him?

One's vision and ambition determine their goals and drive. For a moment, Zhu Qing was deeply moved.

"So, if I want to go to a lower Star Domain, I have to get past the Star Court first?" Yang Kai asked for advice.

"Of course. But that's not a place to mess around. Don't do anything reckless."

Yang Kai replied absentmindedly, feeling a headache coming on. He'd hoped to find a way back to the lower Star Domain, since he had space abilities. If he could just locate Hengluo Star Domain, maybe he could break through the spatial barrier.

But now he knew there was something called the Star Court guarding the sealed passageways. If he wanted to go back, he'd have to get past that heavy barrier.

"So, do you want to go to Dragon Isle?" Zhu Qing looked at Yang Kai with hopeful eyes.

Yang Kai looked up and asked, "Does Dragon Isle have a way to get the Star Court to open the passage and send me back?"

Zhu Qing shook her head. "There's no need to even think about it. While Dragon Isle is impressive, the Star Court is responsible for the entire Star Realm. They wouldn't open the passage just because Dragon Isle asked. If they did, the seal would be meaningless—open today, open tomorrow, what would be the point of having it at all?"

"Then why would I go?" Yang Kai waved his hand dismissively.

"But you could ask for something else!" Zhu Qing said anxiously, her mind racing. "That's right, you're only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. If you went to Dragon Isle, you could cultivate there. It would be great for your progress. In less than a hundred years, you could reach the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm."

"A hundred years!" Yang Kai couldn't help but roll his eyes. He thought to himself, he hadn't even spent a hundred years cultivating to reach the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and he was already at the peak. With a bit of luck, he could break through to the Second Stage at any time. If it took a hundred years to reach the Third Stage, he might as well be dead.

"A hundred years is already very fast. In the whole Star Realm, there are few places better for cultivation than Dragon Isle. If you go and hand over the Dragon Isle Token, I guarantee the Elders will arrange the best place for you to train," Zhu Qing pleaded earnestly.

Yang Kai replied casually, "We'll see. If I ever think of something I need help with, I'll pay a visit to your Dragon Isle."

"Then... how long will that take?"

Yang Kai shook his head. "No idea. Maybe I'll never need your help, and just won't go."

Zhu Qing's face fell. After hesitating a while, she asked, "Then... could you give me the Dragon Isle Token?"

"Give it to you?" Yang Kai laughed. He thought, we're not that close, are we? This girl really doesn't know her boundaries—she dares to say anything.

Zhu Qing said, "Of course not for free. I'll trade you something for it. To be honest, I left the isle this time just to track down this Dragon Isle Token. Now that I've found it, I can finally report back. Please... help me, won't you?"

For a Dragon Clan member to beg someone was truly difficult. Her cheeks burned with embarrassment—she felt utterly humiliated.

Yang Kai frowned and thought seriously for a while. "Since you saved me last time, I'll give you a chance."

Zhu Qing's face lit up, but before she could speak, Yang Kai raised his hand and said, "But it's only one chance. If you don't have what I want, then forget it."

Zhu Qing nodded repeatedly, took off her Spatial Ring, and tossed it to Yang Kai. "I've got lots of good stuff. Take a look and pick whatever you want. I just need the Dragon Isle Token!"

"So generous!" Yang Kai was stunned. A Spatial Ring was usually a warrior's private possession, never shown to others. For Zhu Qing to toss it over without a word showed just how much she valued the Dragon Isle Token—she was ready to pay any price for it.

This girl had no idea how to bargain or play hard to get—she'd just revealed her bottom line. Since that was the case... he couldn't be blamed for being ruthless.

Yang Kai was also curious to see what treasures a Dragon Clan member might have.

He swept his Divine Sense into the Spatial Ring, and was immediately dazzled by a misty glow.

Goodness, there really were a lot of treasures! Yang Kai was delighted, and fully immersed himself in examining them.

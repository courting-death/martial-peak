## Chapter 3073 — Wu Chen Is Wu Kuang

The news that Wu Kuang had returned to life struck every member of the Dragon Clan like a blow to the head, leaving them speechless for a long time.

Suddenly, a figure came stumbling through the air from afar. The man looked exceedingly weak, like a drunken bee, bobbing unsteadily as he flew as though he might fall into the sea at any moment.

Everyone turned to look at him, suspicion in their eyes.

Once Yang Kai saw the man's face clearly, his expression changed slightly.

Zhu Qing also said in surprise, "It's Li Jiao."

Li Jiao coughed up mouthfuls of blood, his face deathly pale. From afar, it was as though he had spotted a long-lost relative, and he cried out urgently, “Palace Master Yang…”

Yang Kai hurried forward and supported him. After examining him and confirming that his life was not in danger, Yang Kai sent him a Divine Sense transmission, "Is Fu Chi dead?"

"Yes!"

Yang Kai's expression changed again. "You killed him?"

Li Jiao shook his head.

He had certainly wanted to kill Fu Chi, but unfortunately, his skills had ultimately been inferior. If an unexpected guest had not appeared at the final moment, he would likely have met with disaster today. Although he knew what consequences would follow from slaughtering a member of the Dragon Clan, the circumstances at the time had left him no room to consider them. When he thought of everything Lu Sanniang had suffered over the years, only Fu Chi's blood could wash away the humiliation they had each endured.

"It was Wu Chen! Wu Chen killed Fu Chi," Li Jiao said solemnly.

"Wu Chen?" Yang Kai's expression turned strange when he heard this. How could it have been him?

Yang Kai relaxed slightly upon learning that Fu Chi had not died at Li Jiao's hands, but Li Jiao's answer was still somewhat unbelievable. When Yang Kai confirmed that Fu Chi was dead, he had thought of two possibilities. One was that Li Jiao had erupted with strength and bravely slain the Dragon. If that were the case, Li Jiao could forget about leaving Dragon Isle today, and perhaps even his Li Dragon Palace would be dragged into the calamity. This possibility was unlikely, however. Yang Kai had let Li Jiao go after Fu Chi precisely because he knew that, with Li Jiao's cultivation, he could not do anything to Fu Chi. At most, he could fight Fu Chi to vent his anger. With Li Jiao's abilities, killing Fu Chi was simply unrealistic.

The other possibility was that Wu Kuang had killed Fu Chi. This was the most likely possibility, and the only one.

Yet Li Jiao's answer surpassed all expectations.

Wu Chen had actually killed Fu Chi?

Yang Kai had always had an extremely strange feeling about Wu Chen and had constantly remained on guard against him, but over the past month, Wu Chen had not shown him the slightest hostility. On the contrary, Wu Chen had even done him a small favor at the beginning.

What concerned Yang Kai even more was the peculiar way Wu Chen's cultivation had risen.

When Yang Kai first met him, he had only been at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, yet a month later, he had actually reached the Third Stage. Although this was only an increase of one Stage, it was still as difficult as ascending to the Heavens for any cultivator.

Yang Kai did not know what sort of opportunity Wu Chen had obtained, but he simply felt that this fellow was anything but ordinary.

"Who is Wu Chen?" Fu Zhun suddenly demanded.

Li Jiao had not delivered his final words through Divine Sense transmission but had spoken them aloud. Everyone present possessed Heaven-reaching cultivation, so they naturally heard him clearly. Now that they had confirmed the dead Dragon was Fu Chi and learned the name of the Dragon slayer, the Dragon Clan naturally wanted to determine Wu Chen's origins.

As she unleashed a trace of her pressure, Li Jiao spat out another mouthful of blood, looking as though he were on the verge of death.

Yang Kai flashed in front of him and replied, "He's a human from Half-Dragon City."

Fu Zhun said furiously, "A mere human could slaughter a member of my Dragon Clan?" Her gaze turned fierce as she stared at Li Jiao. "You carry the scent of Fu Chi's blood. You are lying. Was it you who killed Fu Chi?"

Li Jiao hurriedly shook his head. "It wasn't me! It really was Wu Chen. Although I fought a great battle with Fu Chi, I could do nothing to him and nearly died at his hands instead. That Wu Chen suddenly appeared, and I don't know what Secret Art he used, but he subdued Fu Chi in an instant."

"Wu Chen possesses such ability?" Zhu Yan was also shocked. Subduing an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon in an instant was no simple feat. How could such an expert have been hiding in Half-Dragon City? After pondering for a moment, he said, "Tell us exactly what happened. If even half a word is false, you will die without a grave."

Li Jiao looked miserable, but his expression was steady and did not seem like that of someone who was lying.

Li Jiao nodded lightly and considered his wording.

Yang Kai placed a hand on Li Jiao's wrist and poured Emperor Yuan into him. Only then did Li Jiao's complexion improve somewhat, and he cast Yang Kai a grateful look.

The next moment, Li Jiao slowly recounted everything.

He held nothing back, recounting how he had followed Fu Chi to that Spirit Island, how he had unleashed three desperate moves against him, and how Wu Chen had suddenly appeared in such a bizarre manner. He spoke of every detail, withholding nothing—including the drastic changes in Wu Chen's appearance and the extreme shifts in his personality.

"After I escaped from there, I heard Fu Chi's miserable screams. Immediately afterward, rain fell from a clear sky. Although I only possess a Dragon descendant's body, I know that a fallen Dragon transforms into rain, so in my view, Fu Chi is most likely already dead, and the one who killed him was that Wu Chen."

Fu Chi's death naturally delighted him. Although that fellow had not died at his hands, the three desperate moves he had used earlier had inflicted lasting injuries on Fu Chi, so Li Jiao had still vented his anger thoroughly.

Li Jiao even thought that without his three desperate moves, Wu Chen would likely have needed to expend more effort to kill Fu Chi. From that perspective, he had also contributed to Fu Chi's death, so it could be considered revenge taken with his own hands.

Wu Chen gave him an extremely dangerous feeling, yet Li Jiao was somewhat grateful to him.

When he finished speaking, everyone's expressions shifted.

Yang Kai's expression grew even more solemn as he murmured, "A changed appearance, a changed personality... Wu Chen, Wu Chen... Wu Kuang's Wu, Duan Hongchen's Chen!"

Wu Chen was merely an alias, formed from a homophone of Wu Kuang's surname and one character from Duan Hongchen's name. It fit perfectly. After all, the two Great Emperors already shared one body with two Souls, so combining their names to create an alias was entirely appropriate.

A bitter smile appeared at the corner of Yang Kai's mouth.

No matter how vivid his imagination, he could never have connected Wu Chen with the two Great Emperors. After all, Wu Chen's previous behavior had lacked even the slightest bearing of a Great Emperor. His appearance went without saying; with his deer-like head, rat-like eyes, pointed mouth, and monkey cheeks, he had absolutely nothing in common with a Great Emperor.

From the looks of things, Wu Kuang was still the one primarily controlling that body, while Duan Hongchen had likely been suppressed. Yang Kai had no idea how Wu Kuang had managed it.

As he continued thinking, Yang Kai was suddenly horrified again.

He abruptly remembered that of the five hundred people who had been constructing the palace, only two or three hundred remained after a month, and most of the Emperor Realm cultivators had disappeared. Yang Kai had not previously considered what had happened to those people because their lives and deaths had little to do with him. Now that he knew Wu Chen's true identity, however, their fate was obvious!

Wu Kuang had devoured them.

There was now a reasonable explanation for Wu Chen's explosive increase in strength over that short month. He was the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor, the legendary figure who had created the Heaven Devouring Battle Art. Judging from the Incarnation's cultivation of the Heaven Devouring Battle Art, those cultivators had likely all become nourishment for Wu Kuang's growth.

Yang Kai could now confirm Wu Chen's true identity.

Mo Huang, Zhu Yan, and the others naturally could as well.

Mo Huang had just said that Wu Kuang and Duan Hongchen now shared one body with two Souls. Did that not perfectly corroborate what Li Jiao had just said about the changes in Wu Chen's personality?

Everyone realized the gravity of the problem.

Fu Zhun reached out to grab Li Jiao. "Come with me."

Wu Kuang had slain a Dragon, shaking the entire Dragon Clan. The most urgent task now was to determine his whereabouts. He had secretly infiltrated Dragon Isle and had not hesitated to lower his status and endure humiliation, so he was likely after more than simply slaughtering one member of the Dragon Clan.

To track down Wu Kuang, Li Jiao was indispensable.

Only he knew exactly where Fu Chi had died, and only he knew where Wu Kuang had made his move.

As Fu Zhun's jade-white hand reached toward him, Li Jiao's entire body froze in place. The Emperor Yuan within him instantly stagnated, and even his breathing involuntarily stopped.

Like a rabbit with no experience evading predators, faced with the talons of a hawk swooping down from the sky, he stood there completely stupefied.

Only at this moment did he understand how vast the disparity was between his proudly held Third Stage Emperor Realm cultivation and a Great Emperor. His already bloodless face grew even paler.

The sound of cracking bones rang out as one of Yang Kai's hands transformed into a Dragon Claw. Ignoring the barrier of space, he reached for Fu Zhun's jade-white hand.

The moment he moved, his Incarnation moved with him, driving a punch at Fu Zhun’s flank. The strike erupted with overwhelming might, descending like a thunderbolt.

A blur of afterimages flashed past. Yang Kai only felt a tremendous blow strike his chest, and his entire body was forced back more than a hundred zhang as he spat out a mouthful of blood. The Incarnation also seemed to have been struck by lightning. Its body stiffened, and it plummeted straight down, crashing into the sea and disappearing.

Zhu Qing was shocked and hurriedly flew toward Yang Kai.

Clutching his chest, Yang Kai looked up and saw that Fu Zhun had already lifted Li Jiao like a chick. She then flew off in a certain direction, and the many members of the Dragon Clan hurriedly followed.

Yang Kai gritted his teeth and cursed.

He knew he was no match for a Great Emperor, but when Fu Zhun moved against Li Jiao, he could not simply stand by and watch. No matter how great the disparity, he still had to show that he would not submit.

It was also fortunate that Fu Zhun had no intention of wasting time with him, so she had merely knocked him back. Otherwise, he likely would have suffered far more than a minor injury.

"You've got some nerve." Mo Huang glanced at Yang Kai, though it was unclear whether he was praising or mocking him.

Mo Huang had not looked at Yang Kai even once since his arrival until now. For some inexplicable reason, he found Yang Kai rather displeasing. Or rather, he knew the reason perfectly well, but was unwilling to admit it.

If not for Mo Xiaoqi being unable to stop thinking about him, the young man before him would have counted as an outstanding talent in Mo Huang's eyes, one of the finest of the younger generation. After all, who else at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm would dare trespass on Dragon Isle and even turn the place upside down?

Zhu Yan remained behind. His gaze crossed a hundred zhang of empty space and settled on Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, who stood side by side. "You two come along as well."

He could not allow Yang Kai and Zhu Qing to remain here. If anything went wrong, Yang Kai might seize the opportunity to escape Dragon Isle, an outcome the Dragon Clan could not accept. Enough had already happened on Dragon Isle today. If Yang Kai were also allowed to abduct a Dragon woman, especially one capable of becoming an Elder, how could Dragon Isle preserve its dignity?

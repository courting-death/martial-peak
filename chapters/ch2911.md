## Chapter 2911 — One Thing Overcomes Another

Yang Tai seemed very interested in the Fruit of Time. At this moment, he left Gong Yue and stayed by Yang Kai’s side, probing obliquely about the identity of Yang Kai’s “friend.”

Naturally, Yang Kai would not tell the truth, so he skillfully diverted the topic. Yang Tai also could not appear too eager, so in the end, he could only give up and walk away in frustration.

The scene instantly quieted down. All seven people sat in meditation, resting and waiting for nightfall.

Suddenly, a subtle wisp of Divine Sense extended toward Yang Kai. Immediately after, Yang Kai heard Hua Yulu’s secret voice transmission: “Is there a problem?”

Though she was a woman, as the Palace Master of the Hundred Flowers Palace she was by no means some naive young girl. From Yang Kai’s earlier actions, she had already sensed something amiss, which was why she quietly inquired.

Yang Kai responded calmly, “Once we enter that ancient cave residence, do not stray more than three zhang from me.”

Hua Yulu was slightly surprised. “Is there really a problem?”

If there were no problem, Yang Kai would not have warned her like this. Yet, no matter how she looked, she could not see anything amiss, so she could not help but feel suspicious.

Yang Kai said, “You should have heard about the time when the Four Seasons Land opened many years ago, right?”

Hua Yulu replied, “I’ve heard of it. I also heard that Senior Brother Yang, you refined the Supreme Mystic Pill inside.”

The Four Seasons Land was not a small secret realm. It was a palace left behind by the Great Emperor of Time. Each time it opened, only the most outstanding Dao Origin Realm cultivators of the Southern Territory could enter to temper themselves, and each time, many benefits could be brought out.

The most recent opening of the Four Seasons Land caused quite a stir. First, because Yang Kai refined the heaven-defying Supreme Mystic Pill inside, which made his reputation soar. Second, because an Ancient Divine Spirit—Qiong Qi—emerged from the Four Seasons Land!

The appearance of a Divine Spirit was something no one could ignore, especially since it was a ferocious beast famous for its brutality even in ancient times. It was said that Qiong Qi was once the mount of the Great Emperor of Time. After the Great Emperor fell, Qiong Qi had slumbered in the Time Temple until the most recent opening of the Four Seasons Land, when it finally emerged.

In the years that followed, the experts of the Southern Territory were all on edge, fearing that Qiong Qi would cause trouble. But to everyone’s surprise, Qiong Qi vanished without a trace after that and was never seen again. As time passed, people’s attention to Qiong Qi gradually faded.

Hua Yulu had witnessed Yang Kai’s strength several times, and since she was to explore the ancient cave residence with him this time, she had deliberately inquired about him and learned some information about the Four Seasons Land from back then.

Thinking back, at that time she was already in the Emperor Realm, while Yang Kai had just entered the Dao Origin Realm. Yet now, Yang Kai could stand as her equal, and she even had to rely on him for her own safety.

With such a comparison, Hua Yulu’s feelings became inexplicably complicated.

“Back in the Four Seasons Land, I once encountered a descendant of the Gong family. There, the two of us each obtained a Fruit of Time,” Yang Kai replied.

Hua Yulu said in surprise, “You two were really lucky.”

Yang Kai chuckled, “The world only knows that the Fruit of Time can prolong life, but they don’t know there are two kinds. One indeed extends life, but the other is poisonous. If eaten by mistake, it will cause time to pass rapidly and one to age quickly. The two types of Fruit of Time look identical on the surface, but their effects are completely opposite.”

“Is there really such a thing?” Hua Yulu was slightly surprised. Although she was in the Emperor Realm, she did not understand much about herbs and their effects. But then she thought of how Yang Kai had been able to refine even the Supreme Mystic Pill—his attainments in Alchemy must be extraordinary, so he would naturally be well acquainted with the Fruit of Time.

He said that he and the Gong family descendant had each obtained a Fruit of Time. With his knowledge of medicinal arts, he would surely have chosen the one that prolonged life. In other words, the Gong family descendant had taken the poisonous one back.

Perhaps someone in the Gong family suffered greatly because of this…

However, this was unlikely. Even if the Gong family didn't know the potency of the Fruit of Time, they would surely have sought out a Pill Master to refine it. And any Imperial-Grade Pill Master would certainly be familiar with the Fruit of Time. If that were the case, the Gong family should have possessed complete knowledge of the fruit's properties.

But what Gong Yue said just now…

At this thought, Hua Yulu was startled and immediately understood where the problem lay.

“Of course, this is just my suspicion. That old Family Head Gong seems to have been in seclusion for a long time, so perhaps he doesn’t know what happened in the clan. But it’s always better to be cautious.”

Hua Yulu nodded in deep agreement and no longer spoke with Yang Kai, lest others notice something.

After ending his exchange with Hua Yulu, Yang Kai's eyes flickered subtly as he glanced toward another figure in the group. If his suspicions were correct, then there was something wrong with this person as well.

It was a moonless night, and the sky was full of brilliant stars. But in the Southern Marsh, this scene could not be enjoyed, as the poisonous miasma shrouding the land blocked everything from view.

When it was around midnight, everyone suddenly sensed something and opened their eyes in unison.

“Shh…” Yang Tai raised a finger to his lips, signaling everyone to stay calm. Then he restrained his aura and used a concealment technique. If not seen with the naked eye, no one would be able to detect his presence.

Hua Yulu and Wu Kuangyi clearly also knew what was about to happen, and they too concealed themselves like Yang Tai.

The others followed suit, withdrawing their auras.

Bubbles appeared in the puddles of the marsh, as if something was about to burst out from below. In everyone’s perception, there was indeed a living presence rising from deep underground.

Soon, a figure several feet long suddenly appeared before everyone’s eyes.

It was a bizarre creature, somewhat like a crocodile and somewhat like a lizard, with eight claws, a long tail, and two large eyes on top of its head bulging like a toad’s. As it crawled, its long tongue flicked in and out like a venomous snake searching for prey. Moreover, it emitted a foul stench that made one want to vomit.

This thing was extremely ugly. Even though the two women were highly accomplished, they could not help but show looks of disgust. Yang Tai, on the other hand, watched with relish, as if they were some kind of delicacy.

More and more of these monsters crawled out of the puddles, an endless stream, starting with dozens or hundreds and soon covering the ground.

None of the Emperor Realm cultivators present recognized what these things were. They seemed to be a kind of yao beast, as they all bore traces of yao qi, but their strength was not great—at most at the Sixth Tier, and some might not even have condensed an Inner Core.

Such yao beasts could be wiped out in droves by any one of those present. No one knew how they survived in such a harsh environment. Though the group had not encountered danger on their way here, they had sensed that many ferocious monsters lurked beneath the marsh.

Their only advantage was their sheer numbers.

These monsters crawled out from beneath the marsh, all heading toward the poisonous miasma ahead. As they climbed, a rustling sound rang out, making everyone’s skin crawl.

Soon, some of the leading monsters reached the miasma. Under the close watch of Yang Kai and the others, these beasts flicked out their long tongues, and in a shocking scene, the miasma that could kill even those at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm was swallowed into their bellies. Afterward, they remained unharmed.

They actually fed on the miasma, and the stronger the poison, the more delicious it was to them.

Each monster could only consume a tiny bit of the miasma at a time, and the surrounding miasma would quickly replenish what was lost.

But what if there were thousands, tens of thousands, or even more…

As tongues flicked in and out, the miasma blocking the way visibly thinned. Even with the rapid replenishment from the surroundings, it could not withstand the monsters’ devouring.

More and more monsters moved into the miasma, gradually opening up a safe path for the group to advance.

Those seeing this for the first time suddenly understood what Yang Tai had meant during the day.

Heaven truly creates all things, and one thing overcomes another. If not witnessed personally, who would have thought that these yao beasts of only Sixth-Tier strength could devour a miasma that even Emperor Realm cultivators could not withstand?

Moreover, from what Yang Tai said earlier, these yao beasts did not come out every night. There seemed to be a specific time for this, otherwise Yang Tai would not have said they had arrived at just the right moment.

The discovery of the ancient cave residence was nothing short of a miracle; every coincidence had been indispensable.

But if not for this, the ancient cave residence would not have remained until now. If it were easy to find, it would have been found long ago.

Although the night was deep and there was no light at all, the group was strong, so the darkness did not hinder their observation.

The poisonous miasma that had blocked their way during the day was now gradually thinning before the countless yao beasts, opening a path deeper inside.

However, Yang Tai did not move, and neither did the others. All waited quietly.

After waiting for about two hours, with dawn approaching, Yang Tai suddenly gave a low shout, “Go!”

As soon as he spoke, he was the first to activate his Emperor Yuan and sped forward, following the path opened by the yao beasts.

Gong Yue and Shen Bingru, who were already prepared, immediately followed. Wu Kuangyi and Fang Zhuo, who were better positioned, charged in next, only about a dozen zhang behind Yang Tai.

Yang Kai and Hua Yulu were at the rear. Yang Kai gave Hua Yulu a push, letting her go ahead. This gesture warmed Hua Yulu’s heart and greatly increased her sense of security.

The moment the seven of them made a move, the Yao Beasts that had been devouring the poisonous miasma were immediately alerted. They cast aside their previous stillness and disguise, letting out a series of sharp cries that pierced the ears like metal scraping against metal, stirring unease and throwing the mind into turmoil.

Not only that, but some yao beasts leapt high from the ground, flicking their tongues toward the group as if to drag them down.

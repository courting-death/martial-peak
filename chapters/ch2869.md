## Chapter 2869 — Daring Beyond Measure

Back then, the territory controlled by Shaya spanned ten thousand li in every direction. It took Shaman Niu's tribe three months to completely purge all the demons from that land.

The territory under Bao Qi's control was even larger, several times that of Shaya's, so naturally, he commanded even more demons. Besides the six or seven thousand stationed in the city, there were about the same number scattered outside.

In just half a month, with the city as the center, all the demons within a fifty-thousand-li radius were slaughtered. All the demons stationed outside were summoned back by Bao Qi under the same pretext, then surrounded by Shaman Niu's tribe, leaving them utterly powerless to resist. The efficiency was simply incomparable to the previous three months.

Both Bao Qi and Shaya speculated about which Demon King would be the next unlucky one. The two kept recalling the Demon Kings who had offended them, preparing to submit their names to Lord Shaman Niu so those Demon Kings would meet the same fate as themselves.

Just as the two were mentally marking one Demon King after another for enslavement, Yang Kai suddenly emerged from seclusion, summoned them, and issued an order that left both Demon Kings shocked and uneasy.

Without giving them a chance to question him, Yang Kai turned and left. Bao Qi and Shaya stared at each other in bewilderment, then both suddenly shivered.

"This method... is a bit too extreme, isn't it? What if it fails..." Shaya said, torn between hope and fear.

Bao Qi no longer had the heart to argue with her. Although he had found Shaya disagreeable these days, on this matter, he shared her opinion. He kept thinking about persuading Lord Shaman Niu not to act so rashly.

But after much thought, he still didn't have the courage. In the end, he could only sigh, "Since our lord has given the order, we can only obey. Shaya, you have to help me with this."

Shaya immediately jumped away. "Lord wants you to do this. I'm not getting involved."

Bao Qi said, "We're all in the same boat now. None of us can escape responsibility."

Shaya chuckled lightly. "Since Lord only asked you to do it, he must have his reasons. If he wanted my help, he would have said so earlier. If I help you, I might just ruin Lord's plans."

Bao Qi frowned. Although he felt this was just Shaya's excuse, it wasn't entirely unreasonable. He sighed and could only go make preparations.

Unlike Shaya, Bao Qi had little contact with Shaman Niu. After returning to the city half a month ago, Shaman Niu had remained in seclusion, seemingly indifferent to outside affairs, but Bao Qi knew that nothing happening in the city escaped his notice.

The more Shaman Niu kept his distance, maintaining that veil of mystery, the more Bao Qi feared him.

Before long, atop the city wall, Bao Qi's Demonic Qi surged. Strands of Demonic Qi separated from his body, twisting and transforming midair into one black crow after another, circling and cawing noisily.

With a wave of his hand, Bao Qi sent the black crows flying in all directions, more than twenty in total.

Watching the crows depart, Bao Qi suddenly shivered. If this plan succeeded, the demons would suffer heavy losses. Though it wouldn't shake their foundation, it would certainly deal a blow to their morale.

The next few days passed uneventfully. With no more battles, Shaman Niu's tribe recuperated in the city, while Yang Kai remained behind closed doors, striving to break through to the Shaman King Realm.

The laws of heaven and earth in the ancient world were different, giving rise to the unique shamans. But no matter how many paths led to the peak, the final destination was the same.

Yang Kai possessed the foundation of the Emperor Realm. Cultivating shamanic arts in this ancient world allowed him to draw many parallels. The more he cultivated, the more he realized his previous weaknesses. He believed that if he had the chance to leave this secret realm, even if his cultivation didn't increase, his strength would still improve greatly.

Five days later, Yang Kai emerged from seclusion.

Bao Qi and Shaya waited respectfully outside the door.

"Let's go," Yang Kai signaled, taking the lead. The two Demon Kings followed respectfully behind.

After a day, Yang Kai stopped in a desolate wilderness. As they looked around, there was not a soul in sight, only emptiness and silence.

"Is this the place you chose?" Yang Kai asked.

"Yes!" Bao Qi replied respectfully, then hesitated, "If Lord thinks there's a problem, we can choose another."

"No need, this place will do." Yang Kai nodded, took a deep breath, and opened his mouth. A perfectly round bead rolled out.

Bao Qi and Shaya watched curiously, not knowing what was special about this bead. Demonic Beasts and the Barbaric Beasts of this land both had Inner Cores, but they'd never heard of shamans having Inner Cores.

Moreover, this round bead emitted a very peculiar power. It didn't seem like an Inner Core at all.

As they wondered, the bead suddenly began to spin, faster and faster, until it vanished, replaced by a visible vortex. The vortex was a chaotic nothingness, devouring all light, as if it led to another mysterious world.

Bao Qi and Shaya were both stunned, but soon understood.

The world this vortex led to was clearly the place they'd visited before. In that world, Lord Shaman Niu was omnipotent, like a true god!

So, that world was actually created by a bead!

What kind of bead could possess such a wondrous ability? What kind of treasure was this? Even the Demon Saints probably didn't have such a treasure!

Shocked as he was, Bao Qi still said, "Lord, with all due respect, this entrance may look mysterious, but it might not fool those Demon Kings. They're always wary. If I say this leads to the place where an ancient Demon Saint fell, they might not believe it."

According to Yang Kai's plan, Bao Qi was to spread the word that he'd discovered the site where an ancient Demon Saint had fallen. During the last demon invasion, a Demon Saint had indeed perished, so the story was plausible and should attract the interest of many Demon Kings.

The place where a Demon Saint fell might hold the secret to becoming a Saint. Any Demon King still seeking power would surely be unable to resist coming to investigate.

As long as they could lure the Demon Kings through this entrance and into that world, no matter how many came, Yang Kai could crush them and turn them into slaves.

Bao Qi had always thought this plan was too risky and unlikely to succeed, which was why he had been reluctant to spread the word and wanted to drag Shaya into it as well.

Now, seeing it with his own eyes, he felt the same.

He had already sent the message, and he believed many Demon Kings would come, but this vortex... it didn't look like the site of a Demon Saint's fall at all. It was obviously a trap. What Demon King would foolishly walk into it?

Yang Kai smiled slightly. "You have a point, but what about this..."

As he spoke, Yang Kai's expression suddenly turned solemn. Instantly, a surge of pure and powerful Demonic Qi burst from his body. The moment Bao Qi and Shaya sensed it, they were both shocked and deeply unsettled.

This aura was far beyond their own Demonic Qi. It was the kind only Demon Saints possessed.

"Lord... are you a demon?" Bao Qi suddenly shouted excitedly, his face full of anticipation.

In all the days since Yang Kai had subdued him, Bao Qi had resigned himself to his fate, but deep down, he was still resentful, though he dared not show it. After all, he was a Demon King, yet had been subdued by an outsider—an utter humiliation. Only that shameless wretch Shaya seemed unfazed.

Demon Kings had their own pride and dignity.

Now, suddenly sensing the pure Demonic Qi within this young man, Bao Qi couldn't help but get excited.

That was the aura of a true ancient Demon Saint! Could it be that this young man was descended from an ancient Demon Saint? It made sense—during the last demon invasion of these lands, some scattered bloodlines had indeed been left behind.

It was said that among the outsiders, there was a tribe called the Bone-Eater Tribe that had demon blood.

If Lord Shaman Niu truly had the blood of an ancient Demon Saint, then being subdued by him wasn't shameful at all. The more he thought about it, the more likely it seemed. Bao Qi's eyes shone, and for the first time, he found Yang Kai so approachable.

Shaya's mouth hung open in shock. Suddenly, she understood why her Demon Heart Blood had no effect on Lord Shaman Niu a few months ago.

Since he harbored such pure Demonic Qi, how could her Demon Heart Blood have any effect? Was he really a demon? But in all this time, he'd led the outsiders to slaughter countless demons without mercy. Why? If he was a demon, why oppose the demons?

No matter what, the pure Demonic Qi now flowing from Yang Kai was real.

Faced with the Demon Kings' shock and questions, Yang Kai only smiled mysteriously, offering no explanation, making Shaya and Bao Qi even more curious.

Of course, he wasn't a demon. The Demonic Qi he released was just the aura of an Ancient Giant Demon sealed in his dantian.

Before cultivating shamanic arts, Yang Kai hadn't dared to use this power for fear of breaking the seal and falling into a demonic state.

But the shamanic arts seemed to suppress demonic power, so now, as long as he only drew out a little Demonic Qi, there was no problem. As long as he didn't fully break the seal, he wouldn't risk entering a demonic state.

The pure aura of the Ancient Giant Demon flew from Yang Kai's hand, slowly enveloping the vortex.

In an instant, the vortex leading to the Small Xuan Realm took on a sinister, demonic air, as if it hid some great secret, luring any demon to investigate.

Even Bao Qi and Shaya felt that if they hadn't seen Yang Kai's handiwork with their own eyes, they would have tried to enter and take a look.

Bao Qi's expression grew solemn as he said admiringly, "With this, we can fool those Demon Kings."

## Chapter 3062 — The First

If Yang Kai truly unleashed his full might, the entire Dragon Clan on Dragon Isle would likely be no match for him.

Therefore, before the battle began, Fourth Elder had to neutralize the suppression of their bloodlines, and the Protection Technique was his only means of doing so.

As the two voices chanting in the Dragon Language rang out, waves of Dragon roars suddenly came from the distant and secluded Dragon Temple. Immediately afterward, a dozen or so streaks of light soared into the sky. Like a host of Dragons emerging from the sea, they twisted and turned across the heavens, arriving above the Spirit Island in the blink of an eye before plunging downward.

The many members of the Dragon Clan were naturally overjoyed at the sight and rushed forward to meet them.

Chi chi chi chi!

The streaks of light from the Dragon Temple entered the bodies of the gathered Dragons one after another. Although they did not increase their strength, they filled them with newfound confidence, as though they had been reborn. All their dread and fear of Yang Kai vanished.

"Come!" Yang Kai shouted. One of the streaks of light abruptly changed direction, swerved around the Dragon rushing to meet it, and descended beside Yang Kai before plunging straight into Li Jiao's body.

Li Jiao's body shook. Before he could understand what had happened, he clearly sensed the changes within himself after the light entered his body. The most obvious and tangible change was that the Dragon Pressure radiating from the surrounding Dragons no longer seemed to affect him much. His bloodline, which had been suppressed all this time, regained its freedom, and his Emperor Yuan flowed joyfully through his body without the slightest obstruction.

"This is..." Li Jiao stared blankly as he spread his palm and examined it, scarcely able to believe his eyes.

"The Dragon Temple's protection," Yang Kai answered casually, shaking his head with obvious dissatisfaction.

His original plan had been to intercept all the protection sent from the Dragon Temple. If he had truly managed that, he could have won today's battle without even fighting. The suppressive power of his Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power alone would have been enough to leave every Dragon present constrained, likely unable to display even half their strength.

In the end, however, he had still underestimated Zhu Kong's ability.

In their struggle over the Dragon Temple's protection, he had fallen one step short of Zhu Kong.

Zhu Kong was, after all, the genuine Fourth Elder of the Dragon Clan, so he was naturally well versed in communicating with the Dragon Temple.

Hearing Yang Kai's answer, a sharp light flashed across Li Jiao's eyes.

Ever since these Dragons appeared, he had remained suppressed. The only reason he had been able to step out from the crowd earlier and resolutely stand at Yang Kai's side was because he could not stop thinking about Lu Sanniang.

If he wanted to avenge Lu Sanniang, he could only rely on Yang Kai's strength.

He had never imagined that adding one more person would be of much help to Yang Kai. With his Third-Tier Lower-Level Dragon Bloodline pitted against the pure-blooded Dragon Clan, he had no hope of victory.

But now, that suppression had been greatly weakened. It seemed that everyone once again stood at the same starting line in terms of bloodline, with no one able to suppress anyone else.

Li Jiao's expression turned solemn. Staring in the direction Fu Chi had left earlier, his eyes blazed with a terrifying light as he said in a low voice, "Brother Yang, words cannot express my gratitude for this great favour. This Li wants to..."

"Go!" Yang Kai knew what he wanted to do and agreed before he could finish.

"I'll return as soon as possible!" After saying this, Li Jiao flickered and drifted backward. Under everyone's puzzled gazes, he disappeared from sight.

During the brief exchange between Yang Kai and Li Jiao, the space around Fourth Elder suddenly became severely distorted. The void took on a bizarre and dazzling appearance, as though it might collapse at any moment. Fourth Elder struggled to resist for a while, but he was still unable to break this Space Divine Ability. He could only sigh and shout, "Since you insist, I will grant your wish."

Li Wuyi laughed. "Please forgive my offence, Fourth Elder!"

As he spoke, the enormous hand that had remained suspended in midair suddenly slapped downward.

Without the slightest sound, Fourth Elder vanished as though he had never appeared. At the same time, Li Wuyi also disappeared without a trace, leaving only ripples of spatial fluctuation spreading in every direction.

Yang Kai raised his brow, keenly realizing that this was a Space Divine Ability. It seemed Li Wuyi had dragged Fourth Elder into some unknown space.

He truly deserved his reputation as the strongest person beneath the Great Emperors. Yang Kai had never experienced Fourth Elder's strength firsthand, but he was undoubtedly far stronger than an ordinary Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master. Even so, he was unable to break Li Wuyi's Divine Ability.

Li Wuyi clearly intended to fight Fourth Elder one-on-one, which was exactly what Yang Kai wanted to see. With the formidable Fourth Elder gone, Yang Kai feared none of the Dragons present.

Besides, he was not fighting alone.

"Today, I will settle the score for the loss I suffered at the hands of you crawling worms more than ten years ago." Jiu Feng's beautiful eyes swept across the gathered Dragons, a cold smile hanging from the corner of her lips.

Her words thoroughly enraged the Dragon Clan.

The Dragon Clan stood at the head of all Divine Spirits and foremost among all living creatures. When had anyone ever called them crawling worms?

Even Zhu Lie frowned. Jiu Feng's insult against their entire race had undoubtedly included both him and Zhu Qing. Then again, the siblings had indeed fought Jiu Feng and Li Wuyi over ten years ago, so they knew just how difficult this woman was to deal with.

The faces of the gathered Dragons sank. Although no one spoke, their expressions said everything.

Yang Kai swept his gaze across them and said solemnly, "Let Qing'er and me leave, and I will not fight you today."

He did not want to completely fall out with the Dragon Clan, so it would naturally be best if these Dragons could look the other way and allow him and Zhu Qing to leave.

"Hey, brat, aren't you being a little disloyal?" Jiu Feng glared at him, never having expected Yang Kai to make such a proposal at a time like this.

If Yang Kai left, would she not have to fight alone? No matter how strong she was, how could she come to a good end while besieged by a dozen or so Dragons?

"Senior can leave with us!" Yang Kai looked down at her.

Jiu Feng said, "Spirit Beast Island came to Dragon Isle today on important business. If I leave, what will happen to the others?"

Yang Kai replied, "The Great Emperor's methods are unfathomable, so you need not worry about him. Senior Li's mastery of the Laws of Space surpasses even mine. If he truly wants to escape, how could Dragon Isle possibly stop him?"

Jiu Feng sneered. "What you say does make some sense, but it looks like these fellows have no intention of letting you leave."

Yang Kai turned his head and saw that the many Dragons wore gloomy expressions and remained silent.

Nothing more needed to be said. Yang Kai already knew it was impossible to resolve today's affair peacefully.

He nodded lightly. "Blades have no eyes. If anyone is injured or killed, do not blame Heaven or anyone else!"

As soon as he finished speaking, his eight-zhang-tall body blurred and instantly vanished from the spot.

The Laws of Space surged, and he pounced into the midst of the Dragons like a fierce tiger descending a mountain. Rampaging about without restraint or concern, he reached out with an enormous hand and grabbed at one of them, his expression cold and ruthless.

Despite the sudden attack, the gathered Dragons did not panic, clearly having remained on guard. The instant they saw him charge over, they scattered in every direction.

The Dragon Yang Kai had targeted, however, suddenly felt the surrounding space become thick and viscous like a swamp, causing his movements to stall despite himself.

In that single instant of delay, Yang Kai seized him in his palm.

No one knew whether this Dragon belonged to Zhu's line or Fu's line, but judging by the purity of his Dragon aura, he should possess an Eighth-Tier bloodline.

Eighth-Tier Dragon Bloodlines were rare on Dragon Isle. The entire Dragon Clan numbered only a dozen or so, and just five of them were Eighth-Tier. Apart from Zhu Lie and Fu Chi, only three others possessed Eighth-Tier bloodlines.

This Dragon was one of those three.

Caught in Yang Kai's grip, the Dragon was greatly alarmed.

The sight of Yang Kai tearing off both of Fu Chi's arms in the blink of an eye remained fresh in everyone's memory. This Dragon knew that if he truly fell into Yang Kai's hands, his fate would certainly be no better than Fu Chi's.

In a flash, a Dragon roar exploded and resounded through the clouds.

His true form, more than twenty zhang long, abruptly appeared. Boundless Dragon Pressure spread out, placing everyone under tremendous pressure.

By revealing his True Dragon form, he had unleashed his full strength. No matter how formidable Yang Kai was, he could not possibly crush him with one hand; his sheer size made that impossible.

Sure enough, once his True Dragon form appeared, Yang Kai's enormous hand was forced open, and the tremendous force he was exerting could no longer be maintained. The Dragon twisted his body, about to break free from Yang Kai's grasp.

Yang Kai let out a thunderous shout and reached out with both hands, grabbing the Dragon by the tail. The muscles across his body bulged high, and both arms seemed to swell noticeably.

He gave a savage heave and swung the Dragon down by the tail.

Under everyone's dumbfounded gazes, the giant Dragon that had been about to soar into the sky abruptly halted and was smashed heavily into the ground like a dead snake.

A deafening boom rang out as the ground before the palace split apart, crisscrossing gullies spreading in a jagged pattern.

A mournful Dragon roar rang out.

How tremendous was Yang Kai's strength after transforming into an eight-zhang Dragon body? Even a giant Dragon could not bear being yanked down and smashed like this. As he raised his head and roared, he sprayed a mouthful of Dragon Blood, clearly having suffered an internal injury.

Yang Kai stepped onto the Dragon's body and strode along it, his eyes wide as they swept across the Dragon Scales one by one.

In a single instant, he identified his target.

It was a Dragon Scale about the size of a washbasin. At first glance, it looked no different from the others, but careful observation revealed a flash of light passing over it from time to time.

The Dragon Temple's Protection Technique!

From his previous experience dealing with Zhu Lie, Yang Kai knew that the Dragon Temple's protection was generally attached to a particular Dragon Scale. As long as he tore away that scale, the Dragon Temple's protection would lose its effect.

He wedged his hand beneath this special Dragon Scale. Veins bulged across his forehead as he used nearly all his strength and tore it upward.

"Roar!" The giant Dragon roared in pain and fury, turning his head to spew a blast of Dragon Breath at Yang Kai. The Dragon Breath transformed into boundless cold that seemed intent on freezing him solid.

Yang Kai paid it no heed. Frost instantly covered his enormous body, and even his hair turned a frightening snow-white.

With a tearing sound, Dragon Blood splashed as Yang Kai ripped out the gleaming Dragon Scale.

Having lost the Dragon Temple's protection, the Eighth-Tier Dragon's aura immediately weakened. The suppression from Yang Kai's bloodline made it difficult for him to circulate his strength, and his body, originally more than twenty zhang long, began shrinking at a speed visible to the naked eye. In an instant, it had dwindled to a little over ten zhang, as though he would soon be unable to maintain his True Dragon form.

Yang Kai strode down from the giant Dragon's body and swept out with his foot, sending him flying into the sea and raising an enormous splash.

He shook his body, and all the frost covering him instantly melted away. Holding the bloodstained Dragon Scale in one hand, he swept his gaze across the remaining Dragons. His voice was chillingly cold, striking fear into every one of them. “That’s one! Who’s next?”

## Chapter 3060 — What If You Add Us?

It was shocking enough that the Second Elder had inexplicably gone to the Dragon Tomb, but now even the Great Elder had rushed over.

What in the world had happened at the Dragon Tomb that it required two experts comparable to Great Emperors to handle it together?

Yang Kai also wore a thoughtful expression as he reached up and scratched his chin.

Suddenly, the corner of his mouth twitched and he said bitterly, "I fell for it."

"What?" Zhu Qing looked up at him in confusion.

Yang Kai gritted his teeth. "That old fox truly is crafty!"

Judging from what had just happened, something earth-shattering had indeed occurred at the Dragon Tomb. Even the Second Elder could not handle it alone, so no matter what, the Great Elder had to rush over to help.

In other words, whether Yang Kai released Fu Chi or not, the Great Elder could not remain here for long.

If he had stalled a little longer just now, he might not have needed to negotiate any terms with the Great Elder. With Fu Chi in his hands, would it not have been easy for him and Zhu Qing to leave Dragon Isle safely? Without the Great Elder and Second Elder holding down the fort, the Fourth Elder alone certainly could not have stopped them.

Instead, he had carelessly fallen into the Great Elder's trap. His hostage was gone, and he had lost the initiative in an instant.

On second thought, however, this might have been the best possible outcome.

Had he refused the Great Elder's proposal, he might have forced the Great Elder to act personally and bring the battle to a swift end. Fu Chi would not have met a good end, but neither would Yang Kai. Thinking about it this way, Yang Kai calmed down again. Although the Great Elder had played him, he could still accept this outcome.

With the two Elders gone, only Fourth Elder Zhu Kong and the many members of the Dragon Clan remained before the palace.

Yang Kai grinned and looked toward Zhu Kong. "What does Fourth Elder say? Will you let my wife and me leave, or shall we fight it out and settle this with our strength?"

The situation today had taken one unexpected turn after another, but everything was moving in his favour. Facing a group of Dragons who had lost the support of the Great Elder and Second Elder, Yang Kai was brimming with confidence.

Zhu Kong let out a long sigh. "Dragon Isle is beset by troubles today. If possible, could I ask you to remain here for a few days and let everyone decide what to do afterward?"

Yang Kai chuckled. "Does Fourth Elder take me for a naive youth? If I do not seize this chance to leave Dragon Isle today, what opportunity will I have in the future? I cannot agree to Fourth Elder's proposal."

"Fair enough." Zhu Kong nodded lightly. "This Elder was asking too much of you."

Zhu Qing stepped forward and looked earnestly at Zhu Kong. "Fourth Elder, Yang Kai and I do not wish to become enemies of the Dragon Clan. We only want to leave this place. Please grant us our wish."

Zhu Kong shook his head solemnly. "You heard what the Great Elder said earlier. Today's affair is destined not to end peacefully unless you surrender without resistance."

"Then let us fight!" Yang Kai took a thunderous step forward. His enormous body towered like an Iron Tower as monstrous Dragon Pressure surged around him.

The Fourth Elder narrowed his eyes. "There are only two of you. I fear you have little chance of victory, so I advise you not to resist."

Just as he finished speaking, someone suddenly flashed out and stood beside Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing turned to look at him and frowned. She opened her mouth as though to say something, but ultimately remained silent.

Zhu Lie cupped his fists. "Fourth Elder, forgive my offence!"

The one who had stepped forward was none other than Zhu Lie.

The expressions of the gathered Dragons changed. Although Zhu Lie and Zhu Qing were siblings, his actions still meant opposing Dragon Isle and the Dragon Clan. Making such a decision required more than the pull of familial affection; it also demanded tremendous courage.

Zhu Kong's expression did not change, as though he had expected this. He nodded. "It is fine. Every choice comes with a corresponding price. I only hope you will not regret your decision today."

Zhu Lie replied solemnly, "I will not regret it."

Yang Kai laughed loudly. "Brothers fight side by side, and fathers and sons go into battle together. Well done, little brother-in-law!"

The corner of Zhu Lie's mouth twitched. Every time Yang Kai called him his little brother-in-law, he felt an urge to hit him.

Just then, another person stepped out from the crowd and resolutely took his place beside Zhu Lie. His composure, however, was far inferior to Zhu Lie's. His expression was strained and his entire body trembled, plainly terrified of something.

"You came too?" Zhu Lie looked at Li Jiao in surprise.

The corner of Li Jiao's mouth twitched. "Young Master Zhu, it has been a long time."

Zhu Lie said nothing and merely reached out to pat him on the shoulder.

Li Jiao immediately looked flattered beyond measure. Although he had dealt with Zhu Lie before and had even shared life-and-death trials with him for a time in the Revolving World, Zhu Lie had never once looked him squarely in the eye.

Li Jiao could clearly sense Zhu Lie's contempt and disdain for him. He knew his own circumstances well enough to understand that a Dragon Descendant like him could never be taken seriously by the Dragon Clan.

Until today!

Although it was only a simple gesture, it represented acknowledgment and a sense of closeness.

Li Jiao did not know whether he should be pleased or saddened. By stepping forward, he had gained Zhu Lie's acknowledgment, and if he was fortunate enough to survive, he would undoubtedly reap many benefits in the future. But in the present situation, he was likely facing nine deaths and only one chance at life. He did not even know whether he would have another opportunity to return to Li Dragon Palace.

"A Dragon Descendant?" The Fourth Elder frowned at Li Jiao and saw through his identity at a glance. Dragon Descendants were far too easy to recognize because the Dragon Blood in their bodies was impure. All members of the Dragon Clan disliked Dragon Descendants because they found their aura unpleasant.

No Dragon Descendant could hold their head high before a true member of the Dragon Clan. Their sense of inferiority was obvious; they did not even have the courage to meet a Dragon's eyes, much less oppose one.

Yet this Dragon Descendant's conduct today was rather unexpected. Zhu Kong cast Yang Kai a meaningful glance, knowing that the only reason Li Jiao had stepped forward was because of Yang Kai.

Did this brat truly possess such charisma? He had actually inspired a Dragon Descendant to risk his life for him. Even so, it would make little difference. Although this Dragon Descendant's cultivation was not weak, his bloodline was far too low, and he was bound to suffer greatly against a true member of the Dragon Clan.

"Fourth Elder, there are four of us now!" Yang Kai raised his brow at Zhu Kong.

Although such a number was still insignificant compared with the Dragon Clan, they had at least doubled their original number.

Zhu Kong smiled faintly. "Four is still not many."

"Four truly is not many, but what if you add us?"

A clear voice suddenly rang directly in everyone's ears. Everyone became startled and suspicious, for no figure could be seen anywhere, yet the voice had reached them in such an uncanny fashion.

"Oh?" Yang Kai raised his brow and stared at a certain point in the void in astonishment.

Zhu Kong had clearly noticed something as well. His gaze abruptly sharpened. "This voice..."

A vortex appeared strangely at that point in the void. Within it lay an expanse of chaotic nothingness, like the maw of a great beast capable of devouring everything.

"A Void Passage!" someone exclaimed in amazement.

A Void Passage could not appear out of thin air. Together with the voice just now, everyone immediately realized that an expert had shattered the void to come here. But who could be so audacious as to provoke the Dragon Clan before even showing his face?

He was simply courting death. Who could easily withstand the wrath of the Dragon Clan?

Under everyone's watchful eyes, a tiny figure suddenly emerged from the Void Passage, leaving them all stunned.

This person was simply too small. She was clearly a child, and a little girl at that.

She appeared to be only seven or eight years old and wore a slightly oversized dress whose long sleeves almost reached her feet. Her hair was simply tied into two ponytails behind her head, and her delicate little face looked as though it had been carved from jade, making her extremely adorable.

Setting aside the several hundred mortals, even the many Dragons could not help taking a few extra looks at her.

The moment the little girl appeared, her round eyes darted about as she swept her gaze across the crowd, seemingly searching for something. Obvious excitement and anticipation shone on her face.

"Oh?" Yang Kai exclaimed again in surprise, then broke into a broad grin.

The little girl's gaze finally landed on him. After only a brief pause, she flew toward him like a bolt of lightning and stopped directly before him.

Everyone involuntarily began to fear for her safety.

The contrast between Yang Kai's enormous form and the little girl's petite body was simply too striking. Even if she stood perfectly straight, her entire body was no larger than the bridge of Yang Kai's nose.

Especially now that Yang Kai was grinning, his cavernous maw gaped wide as though he might swallow the little girl whole at any moment.

Just as everyone was holding their breath in alarm, the little girl's clear voice rang out. "Master, why did you get so big?"

Mas... Master?

Everyone stared in stunned silence, almost believing they had misheard.

Zhu Kong's expression also became exceedingly strange.

He could sense that this harmless-looking, jade-like little girl contained an enormous power. Moreover, that power seemed to be the strength of a Divine Spirit, one that could even be said to be no weaker than that of the Dragon Clan.

He had never even heard of such a terrifying expert before.

What puzzled him even more was that this little girl had actually called Yang Kai her Master!

Were the two of them in a master-servant relationship? How could that possibly be?

For the first time that day, a trace of unease welled up in Zhu Kong's heart. It seemed that Dragon Isle would undergo a tremendous upheaval today, and this foreboding weighed heavily on him.

Moreover, the voice that had inexplicably sounded earlier definitely did not belong to this little girl. It had sounded more like...

“How dare you touch me there!”

While everyone was still distracted, a woman's furious shout rang out. Immediately afterward, a beautiful woman in palace robes shot out of the Void Passage. She turned and glared back toward it, her face filled with indignant anger.

In the midst of such a tense and charged atmosphere, this sudden outburst made everyone's expressions turn strange.

With this interruption, the tense and murderous atmosphere abruptly dissipated.

"Why were you blocking the way? I did not do it on purpose." A third person's voice came from within. Everyone could clearly hear that it belonged to the man who had spoken first.

The Void Passage drew inward, revealing a handsome and elegant man of distinguished bearing. He wore a wry smile, and his expression was somewhat awkward.

Only after he appeared did the Void Passage suddenly vanish.

## Chapter 3039 — Let's See You Pay Me Back

"You think it's fair too!" Madam Hua narrowed her eyes at Yang Kai, cold light flashing wildly within them.

"It is quite fair." Yang Kai nodded.

"Since you think it's fair, get the hell out of the way. This Madam will settle accounts with you later." Madam Hua swept her sleeve and shouted impatiently.

Yang Kai grinned and suddenly extended one hand, slowly grasping toward the space before him.

The woman he had slapped flinched at the sight, thinking Yang Kai meant to lash out at someone again, and hurriedly retreated to the side.

But Yang Kai had no intention of paying her any attention at all, which only made her look as if she had overreacted. She covered her cheek, feeling a burning sting, though she did not know whether it came from embarrassment or from the slap.

Splash...

Water sprayed through the air.

When Yang Kai withdrew his hand, a carp with a gleaming golden back and a vivid red body had somehow appeared in his grasp.

A Golden-Backed Red Carp. It was none other than the Golden-Backed Red Carp Madam Hua had raised for more than ten years!

No one saw how he had caught the red carp. He seemed merely to reach out, and the fish Madam Hua called Xiao Hong appeared in his palm.

The woman holding the fish tank visibly froze. She looked at the red carp in Yang Kai's hand, then at the fish tank in her own arms. Her beautiful face instantly lost all color as she shrieked, "Ah!"

Madam Hua also realized what had happened. Her expression turned exceedingly ugly as she shouted, "What are you doing? Release Xiao Hong at once!"

"It's only a red carp, yet you treat it like a treasure. In my eyes, it is nothing at all." Yang Kai sneered softly.

An ominous premonition immediately rose in Madam Hua's heart. Her expression changed as she threatened, "I'll count to three. If you haven't let go by then, you can prepare to die." Without giving him any chance to respond, she began counting. "One!"

Pu!

A muffled sound rang out at the same moment, and Madam Hua's pupils abruptly contracted.

Before her very eyes, Yang Kai suddenly exerted force with the hand holding the red carp and crushed Xiao Hong to death. Although Xiao Hong was a Yao Beast, it was only Fifth-Tier, so how could it possibly withstand a single blow from Yang Kai?

Under the force of his grip, its body burst in two. Its head and tail fell to the ground, revealing its three-zhang-long true form. By the time it landed, it was dead beyond any doubt.

Yuan Wu sucked in a sharp breath and stared at Yang Kai as though he were an idiot, never having imagined this fellow could be so bold.

A single red carp was nothing, but that depended on whose red carp it was!

Madam Hua had raised this one, and it had been her pet for more than ten years. Earlier, merely because someone had injured it and knocked off two scales, Madam Hua had come in person with such fanfare to demand an accounting. Now that it had been crushed to death, how could she possibly let the matter rest?

This brat was dead for certain. There was absolutely no way he would survive.

Yuan Wu was overjoyed, and the look he gave Yang Kai became openly gleeful at his misfortune. He still remembered the humiliation Yang Kai had inflicted upon him several days ago. He lacked the ability to avenge himself, but if he could use Madam Hua's hand to kill Yang Kai, that would be some consolation.

Lu Sanniang and her daughter were also dumbfounded. They had not interacted much with Yang Kai and knew only that he was Li Jiao's friend. Never could they have imagined he would be so recklessly bold. He clearly did not take Madam Hua seriously in the slightest.

What was she supposed to do now? There could be no peaceful resolution anymore. Before this, at worst, she and her daughter would have suffered some punishment, and the matter would probably have passed once Madam Hua had vented her anger. But now...

Lu Sanniang was beside herself with anxiety. She blamed Yang Kai for acting so impulsively, but also hated that the trouble she and her daughter had caused had dragged him into it. How was she supposed to explain this to Li Jiao afterward?

"You... You actually killed Xiao Hong!" The woman holding the fish tank turned deathly pale. She stared at Yang Kai in disbelief, wailing inwardly.

Where had this madman come from? Did he not know what a miserable fate awaited him for doing such a thing?

The other woman also stood frozen in place. With one hand still covering her cheek, she could no longer feel the pain, for her entire being had been shaken by the sight before her.

Everyone was horrified, their expressions varying, yet the culprit showed no awareness that he had caused a great calamity. He merely looked at Madam Hua mockingly and sneered, "You wanted an eye for an eye. So how are you going to pay this one back? Let me see you do it."

Madam Hua's chest suddenly began heaving violently, and her pretty face twisted hideously as she screamed hysterically, "Kill him! Kill him! Kill him for me!"

She could endure no more. When she first arrived, she had only intended to demand justice for Xiao Hong and teach whoever had injured it a lesson. After seeing Lu Sanniang and her daughter, however, she had changed her mind and decided to thoroughly humiliate this wretched woman who had competed with her for favor for several hundred years in the past. But now, Yang Kai was the only person in her eyes. She wanted nothing but his death!

Xiao Hong had been killed before her very eyes. Only this man's life could quell the fury in her heart.

Yet when she gave the order, no one moved.

Although the two women Madam Hua had brought were also Fu Chi's playthings, they were only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. How could they dare confront Yang Kai, who was at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm? Moreover, judging by his behavior just now, Yang Kai was clearly a madman who could not be judged by common sense. Stepping forward now would only invite humiliation, so the two women hesitated and remained where they were, their expressions strained.

Seeing this, Madam Hua grew even angrier and shouted through gritted teeth, "Why haven't you attacked? Must this Madam do it herself?"

If Madam Hua were truly forced to act herself, the two of them would probably have no way to survive either. Helpless, the women exchanged a glance before pouncing at Yang Kai together. Whether or not they were a match for this young man, they at least had to put on a proper show, lest Madam Hua settle accounts with them later.

In the blink of an eye, the two women closed in on Yang Kai from the left and right.

Unfortunately, although both possessed Third Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation, they had spent many years on Dragon Isle doing nothing but scheming against one another and had long since forgotten how to fight. They could not even bring out seventy percent of their strength.

Besides, even if they managed to exceed their usual limits, they would still be no match for Yang Kai, an Emperor Realm Master.

Yang Kai did not appear to move at all, yet the instant the two women reached him, they screamed in unison and flew backward like ragged sacks. Blood sprayed endlessly from their mouths in midair, and when they struck the ground, their Origin Qi scattered, leaving them without even the strength to stand.

"You have some damned nerve!" Madam Hua shrieked, her voice so piercing it seemed capable of tearing through one's eardrums.

Yang Kai had crushed Xiao Hong to death and even dared injure the women on this Spirit Island. His actions had already surpassed anything Madam Hua could have imagined. She shook all over with rage, her fury erupting like a volcano and consuming her. Yet after so many years of pampered living, no matter how angry she became, she still had no intention of acting personally. Instead, she turned and glared fiercely at Yuan Wu. "What are you still standing there watching for? Hurry up and kill him for this Madam!"

Yuan Wu immediately grew restless and uneasy, his expression turning ugly.

If he had been a match for Yang Kai, there would have been no need for Madam Hua to order him. He would have rushed forward long ago to demonstrate his loyalty. Establishing a connection with Madam Hua would certainly benefit him, and the situation before him was the perfect opportunity.

But an opportunity was only useful if one had the ability to seize it.

He had clashed with Yang Kai only a few days ago. Despite facing a human whose cultivation was one Stage lower than his own, he had been completely powerless to fight back. Had Yang Kai not chosen to let the matter drop, Yuan Wu would probably have died then. How could he still be alive and standing here now?

This time, he had brought Madam Hua here intending to throw his weight around under her protection. Who could have imagined Yang Kai would not even show Madam Hua any respect? He had killed Xiao Hong and injured the two women she had brought. This was sheer madness.

"I..." Yuan Wu did not have the courage to fight Yang Kai and stammered out a single word.

Madam Hua immediately said coldly, "You even dare disobey this Madam's command? Good. This Madam will go tell Lord Fu Chi that all of you joined forces to bully me!"

"Please quell your anger, Madam!" Yuan Wu paled in fright, cursing Madam Hua viciously in his heart. He had only come to watch the excitement, so how had he become entangled in this? This was truly an undeserved calamity. His face was filled with panic and unease. If Madam Hua reported what had happened here to Fu Chi, his life would be over.

He did not believe a member of the Dragon Clan would show him any mercy. In the eyes of the Dragon Clan, Dragon Descendants like him were merely ant-like mongrels who could be killed on a whim.

What kind of damned mess was this?

Had he known this would happen, nothing could have persuaded him to come here. Now he was trapped between advancing and retreating, and Yuan Wu wished he could slap himself hard across the face.

He could not afford to offend Madam Hua, so he could only look uneasily toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai's cold gaze swept over him, and Yuan Wu immediately shuddered. Recalling how Yang Kai had effortlessly suppressed his bloodline several days ago, his eyes darted about before he put on a vicious expression and shouted, "Rest assured, Madam Hua. Leave this matter to this Yuan."

At the same time, he secretly transmitted his voice to Yang Kai. "This Yuan has no other choice. Please don't take offense, Young Brother."

As soon as he finished speaking, he circulated his Emperor Yuan and pounced toward Yang Kai. His movements were identical to those of the two women before him, as though they had been cast from the same mold.

Only then did Madam Hua reveal a satisfied expression, staring venomously at Yang Kai.

She had lost a great deal of face today, but that did not matter. As long as this brat was captured, she would have ample opportunity to vent the fury in her chest.

She could sense Yang Kai's cultivation. Like her, he was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, while Yuan Wu was a Dragon Descendant at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. The difference in their strength was enormous. Now that Yuan Wu had attacked, there was no possibility of failure.

Madam Hua was already considering how she should torment Yang Kai later to vent her rage.

You dared kill Xiao Hong? This Madam will make you taste every torture in this world!

A miserable scream rang out. Madam Hua froze in place, and her wishful calculations shattered with a crash.

Yuan Wu, who had charged ferociously at Yang Kai, was somehow struck by a palm and sent flying backward, face-up. He spat out a mouthful of blood in midair, the crimson spray dazzling beneath the sunlight.

What happened? What was going on?

Madam Hua could hardly accept the scene before her eyes.

Wasn't Yuan Wu supposed to subdue that young man with ease? Why had Yuan Wu been knocked back instead? What in the world had happened?

With a thunderous crash, Yuan Wu struck the ground. His whole body twitched several times before he fainted outright.

Madam Hua's face fell instantly.

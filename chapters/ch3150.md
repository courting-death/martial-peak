## Chapter 3150 — He Has Come

Back then, Purple Star's Master, Zi Long, had made an enemy of Yang Kai over his son's affairs and was slain in the Starry Sky by Yang Kai and his Incarnation together. Purple Star was left leaderless for many years afterward, plagued by constant internal strife, but in the end, Zi Wuji took control and became the new Master of Purple Star.

Unfortunately, before Zi Wuji could enjoy the pleasure of becoming an overlord, Great Wilderness Star Domain invaded.

Years of internal strife had left Purple Star far weaker than it had been in the past. Against the experts of Great Wilderness Star Domain, it was simply powerless to resist. In less than half a year, Great Wilderness Star Domain uprooted it completely and seized several Cultivation Stars.

Seeing the situation turn bad, Zi Wuji brought some of his subordinates to seek refuge with Hengluo Commerce Guild, where they managed to enjoy a stretch of peace.

But once Hengluo Commerce Guild could no longer hold out, he had no choice but to hurry to Tongxuan Continent with Xue Yue and the others.

Sword Alliance's fate was much the same. Over the years, Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators had run rampant throughout Hengluo Star Domain. Even Hengluo Commerce Guild could not withstand them, so Sword Alliance naturally had no strength to resist. One after another, the great forces that had once stood at the top fell.

That was why everyone had gathered here.

Nine Heavens Sacred Land was their final pure land and their last hope. If even this place could not be defended, then in the vast Star Domain, there would likely be nowhere left for them to stand.

They had not spent these years waiting helplessly for death. After discovering that Netherworld Sect was arranging the Eight Wastelands Array, they had gone out several times to harass them and force a battle. Unfortunately, the Eight Wastelands Array was immensely powerful, and they had no way to break through its defenses. Worse, if they were tainted by that black Qi for too long, they would grow dizzy and dull-headed, and their Saint Yuan would falter. Although they had killed some people, all of them were insignificant small fry, and their deaths had little effect on Netherworld Sect's grand plan.

It could be said that this group of people, along with Nine Heavens Sacred Land itself, had reached the end of the road. They did not know exactly what the Eight Wastelands Array did, but they could sense this much: once the great array was activated, even Xia Ningshang would probably no longer have the strength to protect them.

Tongxuan Continent's foundation was simply too shallow. If it were a star like Dark Star or Shuiyue Star, with a Star Lord presiding over it, how could Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators be allowed to act so wantonly?

"How is Lady Xia's condition now?" Zi Wuji asked quietly.

Gu Jianxin held an ancient, plain longsword in his arms and slowly shook his head. "I haven't seen her for two months. The last time I saw her... ah, best not to speak of it."

For someone like her to suffer such torment because of Great Wilderness Star Domain's poisoning was more than even Gu Jianxin could bear to see.

"Is Lady Xue Yue looking after her?"

"Mm." Gu Jianxin nodded. "They are as close as sisters, so it is only right that she look after her."

"That Brother Yang Kai truly has good fortune." Zi Wuji chuckled, then added, "But he is rather lacking as a man. He ran off on his own and left his women here to endure suffering."

Gu Jianxin frowned when he heard this. He was a sword cultivator, upright and resolute by nature, and did not like hearing others speak ill of someone behind their back. Still, that was Zi Wuji's freedom, so although he was displeased, he did not intend to refute him.

Speaking of it, he also felt Yang Kai had gone a little too far.

Once Yang Kai left, several dozen years passed without a word from him, leaving several women to struggle on in the Star Domain. No one knew whether he was unable to return or had long since forgotten them. If it was the latter... then that would be far too sad.

Zi Wuji said sourly, "That Lady Xia is beautiful, intelligent, gentle, and serene. If this Zi could win her favor..."

"Brother Zi!" Gu Jianxin suddenly raised his head and glanced at him, a clear warning in his eyes.

Others might not know, but Gu Jianxin understood all too well. The first time Zi Wuji saw Xia Ningshang, he had been stunned as if he had beheld a heavenly immortal, and his words had carried hints of admiration more than once. Gu Jianxin had simply pretended not to notice. Their situation was poor, and Zi Wuji was also a Second Stage Void King Realm expert, so it was not worth creating unpleasantness over such a matter.

Today's words, however, were far too direct.

Then again, although Lady Xia always wore a veil and no one could see her true face clearly, anyone who looked at her would feel their heart stir. Xue Yue was certainly outstanding as well, but because she had occupied a high position for so long, she inevitably carried a somewhat imperious air that made people feel distant from her. Xia Ningshang, by contrast, was honored as a Star Lord and was also a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator, yet she had never put on airs, and her temperament was gentleness itself. What man would not have thoughts about such a woman? She was the very model of a virtuous wife and loving mother. Whoever could take her as his wife would likely have cultivated good fortune for ten lifetimes, so it was only natural for Zi Wuji to admire her.

Perhaps Zi Wuji also meant to use her status to rise higher. Xia Ningshang was a Star Lord, after all. If he truly could form a bond with her, then advancing to the Third Stage Void King Realm in the future would certainly not be a foolish fantasy.

Gu Jianxin's friendship with Zi Wuji was not especially deep. Before coming to Tongxuan Continent, they had hardly dealt with each other, and he did not understand Zi Wuji's character very well. But seeing him speak so improperly now, he had no choice but to offer a warning.

If nothing else, should Zi Wuji reveal even the slightest hint of such intentions in front of others, he would surely be rejected by Xue Yue, Meng Wuya, and the rest.

Zi Wuji also knew he had misspoken and laughed awkwardly. "Good fortune, good fortune indeed." It was impossible to tell whom he meant.

At the same time.

In a side room of the inner hall, Xia Ningshang lay weakly on the bed, a faint black Qi shrouding her body. She looked as though she were gravely ill and beyond cure.

Tongxuan Continent had been poisoned by Netherworld Sect, and as its Star Lord, she suffered the most direct effects. The Eight Wastelands Spirit-Locking Array was like a shackle locked around her, while the eight arrays were like poisonous sores growing on her body, gnawing away at her vitality every moment.

Her veil had been removed, revealing a face capable of toppling all living beings. Her complexion was pale enough to make one's heart ache, and her breaths came soft and shallow.

Xue Yue sat beside the bed, holding her hand and refusing to let go, her heart filled with self-reproach.

When Shuiyue Star had fallen, she had nowhere left to go and could only come to Tongxuan Continent to seek Xia Ningshang's protection. Looking back now, that had been a terrible mistake.

Tongxuan Continent lay near the edge of the Star Domain and had not originally drawn much attention from Great Wilderness Star Domain's side. It was only because she had brought a group of people here that the pursuers had followed them. If not for her, Tongxuan Continent would not have been poisoned, and how would Xia Ningshang have come to suffer such torment?

The more Xue Yue thought about it, the more guilt and self-blame weighed on her, until she wished she could suffer in Xia Ningshang's place.

A man’s figure appeared before her eyes. Clenching her silver teeth, she cried out in her heart, Where are you now!

Suddenly, Xia Ningshang groaned from the bed. Her delicate brows drew together, and her aura grew unstable.

Xue Yue was startled and quickly raised a hand to press gently against Xia Ningshang's forehead. At the same time, she urged her Saint Yuan and poured it into Xia Ningshang's body. The knitted brows slowly relaxed, and the wavering aura steadied once more.

Xue Yue, however, did not stop, thinking something must still be wrong with Xia Ningshang's body.

Shua...

Xia Ningshang suddenly opened her eyes, dazzling light blooming in her pupils.

"Ningshang!" Xue Yue cried softly, startled rather than pleased. It had been a long time since she had seen Xia Ningshang show such an expression. These past few years, Xia Ningshang had always been sickly, and even her figure had grown much thinner.

What was happening? Had Great Wilderness Star Domain's side finally made a move?

"He..." Xia Ningshang lifted one hand and clutched Xue Yue's arm tightly. Her grip was strong enough that even Xue Yue felt a faint pain. Although Xia Ningshang was still weak, her face was filled with delighted surprise.

"Take your time. I'm here," Xue Yue quickly comforted her.

"He... he has come!" Xia Ningshang said.

"Who has come?" Xue Yue asked doubtfully, but the moment the words left her mouth, she froze, and an unbelievable thought leapt into her mind.

Xia Ningshang broke into a smile. "He has come!"

It was the same sentence, yet this time Xue Yue clearly understood what Xia Ningshang meant. Her heart suddenly gave a violent beat, and she pursed her lips before saying, "He... truly is here?"

Xia Ningshang nodded slightly. "I can feel him. He is coming this way. Big Sister Yue, go meet him, but do not let him come here to me."

After giving that instruction, Xia Ningshang closed her eyes and seemed to fall asleep all at once, though a faint ripple of energy spread from her body.

At the same time, several tens of thousands of li away, Yang Kai suddenly let out a soft sound of surprise and stopped in place.

The Spiritual Qi around him reacted strangely, flickering with specks of light like countless fireflies dancing at his side.

Those points of light quickly gathered together and transformed into an exquisitely beautiful, brilliantly colored butterfly that danced gracefully around Yang Kai.

Yang Kai grinned and held out a finger.

The butterfly immediately landed on it.

"Little Senior Sister!" Yang Kai gazed at the multicolored butterfly and called softly.

This butterfly could not have appeared for no reason. Someone must have used some kind of Secret Technique. But besides Liu Yan, there was no one at Yang Kai's side, and apart from Xia Ningshang, there was no other possibility.

It seemed Xia Ningshang had also sensed his arrival, so she had deliberately sent him this guidance, afraid he would not be able to find her and the others. As a Star Lord, it was not strange for Xia Ningshang to sense his arrival; she had that ability.

But... why had Xia Ningshang not appeared in person? Before Yang Kai could think too deeply, the butterfly spread its wings and flew ahead to lead the way, heading precisely toward Nine Heavens Sacred Land. Its movements were small, but its speed was astonishingly fast, as if displaying Xia Ningshang's urgent mood at this moment.

Yang Kai stopped thinking about it. In any case, he would see Xia Ningshang in just a short while. When the time came, everything would naturally become clear.

In the main hall of Nine Heavens Sacred Land, the hall that had been buzzing with noise suddenly fell silent when one person walked out.

Meng Wuya hurriedly stood up and looked anxiously at Xue Yue. "Madam Yue, how is Ningshang now?"

Xia Ningshang had been raised by his own hand and was like his own daughter. Now that calamity had befallen her, he naturally could neither sit nor stand at ease.

But Xia Ningshang's current condition was no ordinary injury or illness. It was closely tied to the vitality of the entire Tongxuan Continent, and no medicine could cure her. Unless all of Great Wilderness Star Domain's cultivators were driven away and the entire star carefully restored, perhaps after several dozen to a hundred years, once the star's vitality recovered, Xia Ningshang would heal on her own.

Xue Yue gently shook her head.

Meng Wuya immediately knew the situation was still grim. In vexation, he slapped his palm down, and the chair beneath him instantly turned to dust.

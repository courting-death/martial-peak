## Chapter 2886 — Bear the Consequences

The bald Shaman King couldn't figure out one thing: how could the sacred relic of the Southern Barbarian Tribe end up in the hands of this man called Shaman Niu?

When the Black Sun descended, hundreds of thousands of Southern Barbarian Tribe members perished, including all the Shaman Kings alive at the time. By all rights, the sacred relic should have been lost or destroyed along with those Shaman Kings, yet now it had truly appeared before his eyes.

Could it be that this Shaman Niu had been to that battlefield?

As soon as this thought surfaced, the bald Shaman King immediately dismissed it. From what he knew, there had been a Demon Saint on that battlefield, a being as powerful as a Shaman Saint. If this Shaman Niu had really gone there, there was no way he could have come back alive.

There was still a Shaman King in the Southern Barbarian Tribe, and the sacred relic still existed. This was somewhat different from what the Bone-Eater Tribe had expected.

Led by the bald Shaman King, several Bone-Eater Tribe Shaman Kings exchanged glances, seemingly communicating in secret. After a moment, they reached a consensus.

The bald Shaman King said, "Shaman Niu, hand over the sacred relic. You have sided with the demons and are no longer a child of the Barbarian God."

Yang Kai snorted coldly, “What makes you think we’ve sided with the demons?”

Shaman Chi shouted sternly, "Still trying to argue? If you haven't sided with the demons, why did you save this Demon King?"

Yang Kai replied, "I told you, he's my man. Of course I had to save him!"

Shaman Chi sneered, "So, by your words, this Demon King has submitted to you?"

"Exactly!"

Shaman Chi burst out laughing, clearly not believing it. The other Shaman Kings also shook their heads slightly, thinking Yang Kai was boasting too much. Just before, Yang Kai had only been a Great Shaman Master—how could he possibly subdue a Demon King? That was a being on par with these Shaman Kings, equally proud and arrogant. If it were them, they would rather die than submit to a Demon Commander, let alone a Great Shaman Master.

"You've already been tainted by Demonic Qi. You've entered the demonic path!" The bald Shaman King sighed. "Fortunately, the demonic thoughts haven't taken deep root yet. Hand over the sacred relic, and your Southern Barbarian Tribe may still have a chance to endure."

"Whether I've entered the demonic path or not, I know in my own heart. No need for you to worry, sir. I've come today only to take my Shaman Niu tribe's people away. I hope you all can make things easy!"

Shaman Chi shouted harshly, "Enough talk! This guy's already lost his mind, only thinking of saving this Demon King. Let me kill him and take back the sacred relic!"

Shaman Chi was hot-tempered and acted quickly. As he spoke, he activated his shamanic power, and a giant skull suddenly shot out, growing to the size of a house, its jaws snapping toward Yang Kai as if to swallow him whole.

The bald Shaman King and the others watched coldly, neither stopping nor helping, clearly tacitly approving of Shaman Chi's actions.

Yang Kai had seen that skull before. The last time he fought Shaman Chi, Shaman Chi had used this shamanic artifact—vicious and sinister to the extreme. But today, as Shaman Chi activated it, its power seemed much greater than before.

A chilling aura rushed at him. Yang Kai narrowed his eyes and watched closely.

The skull descended and swallowed him in one bite.

Shaman Chi was stunned, clearly not expecting to succeed so easily. The joy on his face had just appeared when it turned to shock. The next moment, dazzling sword light shot out from the skull, streaming from its seven orifices, and the whole skull flew about like a headless fly, tumbling through the air, refusing to obey Shaman Chi's commands no matter how he tried.

Shaman Chi's expression changed, and he quickly formed a new hand seal.

The skull's jaws opened wide, and Yang Kai, together with his sword, burst out in a flash of sword light. The skull then shrank rapidly, its spirit greatly diminished.

Shaman Chi's eyes nearly split with pain, deeply distressed. He was secretly shocked—how could this Shaman Niu's strength be so terrifying? Last time, when he fought him as a Lower-Level Shaman King against a Lower-Level Great Shaman Master, he had suffered a small loss. He thought that now, having advanced to Middle-Level Shaman King, he could regain some face, but who knew the other had grown even more fearsome.

He realized he was completely no match. Even though they had only clashed for an instant, Shaman Chi already had a clear judgment.

Fortunately, he wasn't alone... Thinking this, Shaman Chi looked up.

Over there, in front of Yang Kai, the bald Shaman King seemed to have anticipated everything, blocking Yang Kai's path in advance. With a wave of his hand, a pitch-black pillar of energy blasted toward Yang Kai, its might so great it seemed it could overturn heaven and earth, shatter the universe.

A full-powered strike from an Upper-Level Shaman King was naturally terrifying.

He shouted in a low voice, "Shaman Niu, you were a rare genius of my barbarian tribe. If nothing unexpected happened, you would have achieved great things in the future. It's a pity you didn't cherish yourself and fell into the demonic path. The barbarian tribe cannot tolerate you. Die here!"

Yang Kai laughed loudly, "Many have wanted me dead, and none have ended well. Sir, you will be no exception."

As he spoke, the Myriad Sword pointed forward, a flash of cold light appearing, then the sword shot out like a dragon.

The pitch-black energy pillar was shattered by countless sword flowers, melting away like snow under the blazing sun.

The bald Shaman King remained calm. With a thought, his hand seal changed, and the shattered black energy suddenly contracted inward, forming a cage, as if to trap Yang Kai inside.

At the same time, the other three Shaman Kings, excluding Shaman Chi, launched their attacks as well—each one ruthless and merciless, holding nothing back.

They were determined to kill Yang Kai here. Not just because of his old grudge with the Bone-Eater Tribe, but also because the Southern Barbarian Tribe's sacred relic alone was enough to make them covetous.

Moreover, these people from the Shaman Niu tribe were mingling with the demons. Their deaths would not be regretted. Even if they were all killed, no one from the other tribes would say a word.

More than twenty thousand people of the Shaman Niu tribe cried out in alarm. Yu, Lu, and the others looked tense, wanting to rush forward to help Yang Kai, but in a battle at this level, they knew they could do nothing. They could only resolve to resist on the spot if Lord Shaman Niu truly met with misfortune, preferring to be slaughtered by the Bone-Eater Tribe than be kept here as food.

Atop the high platform, facing the combined assault of four Shaman Kings, Yang Kai still appeared calm and unhurried. The Myriad Sword in his hand struck east, west, up, and down—seemingly chaotic and without pattern, yet with just a few strikes, he destroyed all the Shamanic Arts of the Shaman Kings, except for the bald Shaman King's cage, which still held him fast.

Yang Kai's body shook, and a surging power erupted from within. The invisible cage was suddenly forced open several times over, faint cracking sounds echoing as if it might shatter at any moment.

The bald Shaman King's expression changed slightly. No longer daring to hesitate, he began chanting an obscure incantation.

The other four Shaman Kings also grew solemn, chanting incantations as well.

The voices of the five quickly merged, as if coming from a single person, indistinguishable from one another. As the incantation changed, a gigantic skeletal hand appeared out of thin air, dozens of zhang long, stretching across the sky. With a speed both slow and fast, it reached down, aiming straight for Yang Kai's position.

With one grasp, it seemed even the laws of heaven and earth could be seized in its palm.

Yang Kai suddenly felt his breath stop, his body sink, as if a great mountain pressed down on his neck, making it impossible to straighten up.

He clicked his tongue in wonder. Though he didn't know what kind of Shamanic Art this was, anything that required five Shaman Kings to cast together could not be taken lightly. He kicked Bao Qi aside, then his figure flickered as he dodged to the side.

With a thunderous crash, the hastily built high platform was reduced to powder as the skeletal hand struck down.

Before Yang Kai could steady himself, the giant skeletal hand reached for him again. His face darkened, and just as he was about to test its power, he saw a blazing beam of light shoot from its palm, piercing the void and aiming straight for his chest.

Yang Kai dodged again.

This happened several times in a row.

Yang Kai grew furious and shouted, "Enough!"

Shaman Chi laughed, "Run! Keep running! I want to see how long you can keep it up."

"Shaman Chi, you're courting death!" Yang Kai glared coldly at him.

"You can't even protect yourself and still have the mind to threaten me?" Shaman Chi curled his lip disdainfully. "Worry about yourself first."

"Do you know why I'm running?" Yang Kai asked coldly as he dodged the skeletal hand's attacks.

Shaman Chi replied, "If your strength isn't enough, of course you run. Everyone has the instinct to survive."

"You're wrong." Yang Kai shook his head slightly and said calmly, "I just don't want Lord Shaman Saint to hunt me down."

"Why would Lord Shaman Saint want to hunt you down?" Shaman Chi frowned.

Yang Kai bared his teeth in a fierce grin, his fangs glinting coldly as he spoke in a chilling voice, "If I were to slaughter all of you here, do you not think Lord Shaman Saint would come after me?"

Shaman Chi was stunned for a moment, then burst out laughing as if he'd heard a great joke. "Just you? You think you can kill us? Shaman Niu, did you not get enough sleep?"

Yang Kai stopped talking to him and turned to the bald Shaman King. "You shouldn't die by my hand. The demons are still rampaging across this land. You should save your strength to deal with those Demon Kings!"

"The Demon Kings are none of your concern. We'll handle them ourselves. You'd best surrender quietly." The bald Shaman King snorted coldly.

Yang Kai sighed and made one last effort. "It's not too late to stop now. Otherwise... bear all the consequences yourselves!"

The bald Shaman King seemed to lose his temper as well, snorting, "I've never met anyone with such a big mouth. I hope your skills are even greater than your boasting!" As he spoke, not only did he refuse to stop, but he also pushed his shamanic power even more fiercely, and the giant skeletal hand's might increased again.

"Whether my skills are great or not, see for yourself!" Yang Kai suddenly stopped running, standing tall in midair, the Myriad Sword slightly raised.

The five Bone-Eater Tribe Shaman Kings all grew solemn, knowing Yang Kai was truly going all out. Without hesitation, they drove the giant skeletal hand at him.

The tiny figure and the enormous skeletal hand formed an extremely striking visual contrast. Every barbarian who saw this scene—whether from the Bone-Eater Tribe or the Shaman Niu tribe—couldn't help but hold their breath, as if witnessing a moment destined to be recorded in history.

## Chapter 3005 — The Miraculous Iron Pot

"Bastard!"

"Junior, you dare!"

"Release Great Elder at once, or you will die without a grave!"

...

Shouts rang out from all directions. Yang Kai held Great Elder in one hand, his sharp eyes sweeping across the surrounding space as he sneered softly.

Ever since this Array had been deployed, he had been trapped in a passive position, taking blows without even being able to find his enemies' shadows. His belly had long been full of pent-up rage with nowhere to vent it. Now that he had finally caught hold of an opening and captured one of the enemy's leaders, how could he possibly let him go so easily?

More than a hundred people had formed an Array to deal with him and Lin Yun'er, including several Emperor Realm Masters and even a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master, relentlessly pursuing them with the intent to exterminate them. Yet now that Yang Kai had seized the initiative, they had flown into a rage and begun making threats? They even wanted him to release their man. There was no such cheap bargain in this world.

He chuckled darkly, shifted his gaze to Great Elder, and spat out a single icy word, "Die!"

"No!" Great Elder's expression changed drastically as he cried out in alarm.

Yet no matter how desperately he struggled, he could not break free from the grip of that enormous hand.

Violent force pressed in from every direction, and a series of cracking sounds rang out as Great Elder's bones broke. His eyes bulged wide and blood began flowing from all seven orifices. He could clearly feel his bones snapping and his internal organs being crushed out of shape before bursting apart.

Bang!

Blood splattered and flesh flew.

"Ah!"

"Great Elder is dead!"

The disciples of Wind and Cloud Pavilion cried out in alarm, simply unable to accept what they had witnessed. What kind of figure was Great Elder? In all of Wind and Cloud Pavilion, only Pavilion Master Hua Xing was stronger than him. Yet such a powerful Master had actually been crushed to pieces by that monster with one hand.

"Insolent junior!"

"Avenge Great Elder!"

After a brief silence born of shock, the clamour grew even fiercer.

The Sword Formation began circulating once more. Sharp swords appeared in every direction as its murderous aura suddenly soared.

The Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation was founded upon swords and rooted in cultivators of the Sword Dao. It was an Array intended for slaughter to begin with, and Great Elder's death caused everyone's murderous intent to surge, greatly bolstering the Array's might.

Unfortunately, even so, the overall power of the Array was weakening. There was no other reason than that Great Elder, who had originally served as the Formation Core, was dead. Third Elder, who had taken his place, was only a First-Stage Emperor Realm Master. With the strength of the Formation Core falling by an entire Stage, the power of the whole Sword Formation weakened along with it, and the increase in momentum could not make up for this loss.

Yang Kai laughed wildly. "If this Young Master can kill one of you, he can kill all of you! Wash your necks clean and wait. I'll drag you out one by one, then kill or cripple every last one of you!"

"You still dare boast so shamelessly!" Third Elder, who now presided over the Array, roared. Without any further hesitation, he willed the overwhelming mass of sword lights to attack Yang Kai once more.

Before Yang Kai could make a move, Lin Yun'er shouted clearly again, "Collect!"

As soon as she spoke, the countless sword lights seemed to answer some kind of summons. They all changed direction in unison and raced toward a certain place, pouring into it before disappearing.

Yang Kai turned to look, raising his brow in surprise. "Huh?"

At that moment, Lin Yun'er was holding both hands high, supporting a basin-sized object above her head. It was pitch-black and looked entirely ordinary, just like an iron pot used for cooking in a common household.

Yet this very iron pot radiated an unimaginable devouring force. All the sword Qi produced by the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation was sucked into the pot and vanished.

The iron pot was like a bottomless pit, accepting every sword light that came its way without showing the slightest sign of reaching its limit.

What surprised Yang Kai was not the power it displayed, but the aura it radiated.

It was an extremely pure, ancient, and desolate aura, identical to that of his Mountain and River Bell, as though it had traveled through time from the Primordial Era.

"A Primordial Era Treasure!" Yang Kai's eyes lit up.

He had not expected Lin Yun'er to possess a Primordial Era Treasure as well.

Primordial Era Treasures had been passed down from ancient times and were exceedingly rare. Apart from his own Mountain and River Bell, the only one Yang Kai knew of was Hua Yulu's little flower drum, and he did not know much about what that drum looked like or what powers it possessed.

This iron pot, however, was extraordinary.

Its devouring power alone could not be matched by any ordinary treasure.

It was just that its appearance was... a little shabby. At first glance, anyone would think it was meant for cooking rice.

"Is this your trump card?" Yang Kai asked in a booming voice.

Holding the iron pot above her head, Yun'er giggled and darted over to Yang Kai. With a light tap of her feet, she landed on his shoulder and ignored the question entirely. "Uncle, you look so handsome like this!"

Yang Kai threw his head back and laughed. “I think so too! I’m handsome enough to leave everyone in the dust. Little Yun’er really has good taste.”

Hearing this, Third Elder nearly coughed up blood.

This was a battle of life and death, a fight in which they were wagering everything, yet the enemy had actually started chatting over there. They were clearly not taking Third Elder and the others seriously at all!

Although he did not know what the iron pot was or how it could display such miraculous power, Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er's casual attitude still infuriated him.

Just as he was about to urge the power of the Array again, cries of alarm suddenly rang out from every direction.

"Ah!"

"What's happening?"

"My sword!"

"Senior Brother, help me! My sword is being sucked away!"

"Help you, my ass... Mine is too! I can barely protect myself right now."

Hearing these voices, Third Elder's expression changed drastically. Recalling the strange scene Great Elder had encountered earlier, he suddenly understood what was happening, and his face filled with horror.

Just then, he also felt the longsword in his hand being pulled by some force. It jerked uncontrollably in a certain direction, as though it wanted to fly over, and that direction was precisely where the little girl holding the iron pot stood.

Xiu, xiu, xiu...

Streams of light tore through the air.

This time, they were not those illusory sword lights, but genuine Secret Treasure longswords.

Everyone who had formed the Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation was a Third-Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator or higher, so the grades of their Secret Treasures were naturally not low. At minimum, they were Middle-Level Dao Origin-Grade treasures, and there were also many Imperial Treasures among them.

At this moment, however, whether they were Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasures or Imperial Treasure longswords, they all came flying over one after another, plunging into the iron pot and disappearing.

In the blink of an eye, the iron pot had collected dozens of longswords, and even more were still streaking toward it.

"What the hell is that thing?" Third Elder was horrified beyond measure. This iron pot could actually collect other people's Secret Treasures. How were they supposed to fight now? He had never heard of such a bizarre Secret Treasure existing in this world.

The devouring force acting on the longsword in his hand grew stronger and stronger until even he found it difficult to maintain his grip. Third Elder did not dare be careless and hurriedly focused his mind on controlling his Imperial Treasure.

Shua, shua, shua...

In less than ten breaths, more than ninety longswords had been sucked into the iron pot.

A moment later, even Third Elder and the others could no longer hold on to the swords in their hands. With a whoosh, the swords broke free of their control and flew away.

"Ah!" Third Elder cried out in alarm, frantically urging his Secret Technique in an attempt to summon back his Imperial Treasure, but how could that possibly work? Even Great Elder had been unable to do so, so naturally, he could not either.

When the final longsword fell into the iron pot, a shattering sound seemed to ring out through the air.

Figures abruptly appeared one after another, surrounding Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er, every face filled with shock and horror.

These people were all the disciples and Elders of Wind and Cloud Pavilion who had participated in forming the Everchanging Sword Formation.

The Everchanging Sword Formation was founded upon swords and rooted in people. Now that all the longswords had been taken away, the Array collapsed on its own even though its cultivators remained, naturally leaving them unable to conceal themselves.

"Hiss... How is that possible?"

In the sky off to one side, Hua Xing drew a sharp breath, staring at the scene in disbelief.

The Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation had been broken!

The Yin-Yang Five Elements Everchanging Sword Formation, which had saved Wind and Cloud Pavilion from disaster several times and kept the flame of its inheritance alive, had actually been broken!

Hua Xing almost thought he was dreaming!

This was impossible. The Array Diagram had been passed down from ten thousand years ago and required only five people to form, with its power increasing as more people joined it.

This time, more than a hundred people had formed it, an extremely large-scale deployment, and the person presiding over it had been their own Great Elder, a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master.

Even Hua Xing himself would have had no chance of surviving such a lineup, so how could two unknown juniors possibly have broken it?

What about Great Elder? Where had he gone?

He swept his gaze across the crowd but did not see Great Elder among them. His heart clenched, and he knew that something terrible had happened.

Great Elder had clearly been the Formation Core presiding over the Array, yet he was nowhere to be seen now. There was only one possibility: Great Elder was dead!

How had he died? Who had killed him?

Hua Xing could not understand it no matter how hard he tried. He had no idea what had happened inside the Sword Formation, so naturally, his mind was filled with questions.

Just then, Hua Xing's skin suddenly tightened as he instinctively sensed a trace of danger.

Not good!

Only then did he remember that he was still in the middle of a battle, and his opponent's strength was no weaker than his own.

Under normal circumstances, he never would have made such a basic mistake, but the scene just now had shocked him so deeply that he had inevitably become distracted.

That moment of distraction gave Li Jiao an opportunity to strike.

Ferocious Emperor Yuan pressed in from the front and exploded against his chest.

Hua Xing's whole body shook, and he spat out a mouthful of blood.

"Old dog Hua, you dared make an enemy of Palace Master Yang! Today, this Li will fight you to the death!"

At those words, Hua Xing coughed up another mouthful of blood. Grief and rage filling his voice, he cursed, "Li Jiao, fuck your mother!"

A moment ago, the two of them had been fighting as though they were merely playing, exchanging moves that looked lively but did not use even ten percent of their strength. Now Li Jiao had suddenly begun fighting for his life, catching Hua Xing completely off guard.

Hua Xing also knew why Li Jiao had changed like this. He was clearly adjusting his sails according to the wind, but Hua Xing still could not restrain his fury. The little boat of their century-old friendship had capsized just like that. Gritting his teeth, Hua Xing growled, "You think you've already won? Every single one of you here today can forget about leaving." After a pause, he roared, "Summon the Saint Beast!"

## Chapter 2751 — Take You Somewhere

The young girl led Yang Kai, and as soon as they arrived at the central courtyard, they were immediately surrounded by ten members of the Zhang Family.

“Who goes there!” someone shouted loudly, their face full of vigilance.

“It’s Lord Yang, it’s Lord Yang!” The girl was beside herself with excitement, clutching Yang Kai’s arm tightly and repeatedly announcing it to everyone around.

“How improper for a little girl to shout like that.” A familiar voice came, and soon a voluptuous, beautiful woman appeared from the other side, her features vaguely resembling Ruoxi.

“Grandmother, Lord Yang is here!” the girl hurriedly called out.

“Lord Yang?” The beautiful woman frowned slightly, stepped forward to take a closer look, then suddenly covered her red lips, both surprised and delighted: “Lord Yang!”

“Madam Zhang, I hope you’ve been well!” Yang Kai smiled gently. This woman was none other than Ruoxi’s grandmother, Ning Suwan.

Ning Suwan was dazed for a long time before she finally smiled and said, “Lord Yang, you’re as impressive as ever!” She paused, then suddenly came to her senses and said to the girl holding Yang Kai’s arm, “Ruoyu, go invite your great-grandmother out.”

“Alright.” The girl called Ruoyu rushed inside.

Ning Suwan peeked behind Yang Kai, but didn’t see the figure she hoped for. Her pretty face changed slightly, and she asked in a trembling voice, “Lord Yang… Ruoxi, she…?”

Yang Kai smiled, “Madam Zhang, don’t worry. Ruoxi is perfectly fine, she just couldn’t come this time due to some matters.”

Hearing Yang Kai’s reassurance, Ning Suwan finally let out a sigh of relief, pressing a hand to her chest. She had truly feared hearing bad news from him. Years ago, when the Zhang Family was bullied and oppressed, it was Yang Kai who had stepped forward to save them from disaster. Afterwards, Zhang Ruoxi followed Yang Kai and disappeared for ten years, with no word from her. Naturally, Ning Suwan had always worried. The world outside was far less peaceful than the Zhang Family’s quiet years. Although Yang Kai had shown remarkable ability back then, as the Zhang Family’s horizons broadened over the years, they realized that Yang Kai’s strength at the time was not as extraordinary as they once believed.

If he had provoked a formidable enemy outside, Ruoxi might have been implicated.

Fortunately, Yang Kai had come and assured her that Ruoxi was safe.

“Lord Yang, please come inside!” After calming down, Ning Suwan gestured for him to enter. Though she had a bellyful of questions, years of experience had made her temperament much steadier, and she knew now was not the time to ask.

At this moment, many members of the Zhang Family who had gathered realized who Yang Kai was. Many had seen him in the past, and even those who hadn’t had heard of him, knowing Ruoxi was taken away by him.

Hostility and vigilance faded, replaced by warmth and smiles.

Ning Suwan led Yang Kai into the Zhang Family’s reception hall, where they took their seats as host and guest, and someone immediately served tea.

They had barely exchanged a few words when an elderly voice suddenly called from outside, “Lord Yang has arrived?”

Yang Kai hurriedly stood up, only to see an old woman being supported by Zhang Ruoyu as she walked slowly toward them. Her cloudy eyes suddenly shone with a dazzling light as she looked at Yang Kai, exclaiming with delight, “It really is Lord Yang! This old woman greets Lord Yang!”

Yang Kai was startled, quickly reaching out to support her, flustered, “Madam, you’ll overwhelm me, please don’t.”

The old Madam Zhang said, “It’s only right. If not for you back then, the Zhang Family would have long ceased to exist.”

“I was entrusted, so I fulfilled my duty. There’s no need to bring up the past.” Yang Kai gently shook his head, personally helping the old Madam Zhang to the seat of honor, settling her in before returning to his own seat.

Ning Suwan quietly watched this scene, the corners of her mouth lifting in a gratified smile.

Although the Zhang Family had agreed to let Yang Kai take Ruoxi away back then, everyone knew that a talented young man like Yang Kai traveling with a beautiful girl was bound to lead to something. Ruoxi becoming Yang Kai’s concubine was only a matter of time.

In other words, Ning Suwan had long regarded Yang Kai as the Zhang Family’s son-in-law.

Yet, this son-in-law was far stronger than the Zhang Family itself. Although Ning Suwan had already accepted Yang Kai as family in her heart, she dared not show it openly, lest it cause resentment and bring hardship upon Ruoxi.

Looking at Yang Kai now, he showed not a hint of arrogance—he respected his elders, cared for the young, and possessed a truly admirable temperament.

“Lord Yang…” The old Madam Zhang had just sat down and started to speak, but Yang Kai waved his hand, “Old Madam, just call me by my name. There’s no need for the ‘Lord’ title.”

The old Madam Zhang said, “How could I…”

Ning Suwan smiled, “Mother, since Yang Kai insists, why make things so formal?”

The old Madam Zhang seemed to understand, smiling, “Then I’ll rely on my age!”

“That’s only right.” Yang Kai nodded slightly.

“How is Ruoxi these days?”

Ning Suwan said, “Yang Kai just mentioned it, Ruoxi is perfectly fine, Mother, you needn’t worry.”

“Really?” The old Madam Zhang looked at Yang Kai earnestly.

“Really.” Yang Kai smiled, “Ruoxi has been in seclusion recently, so she couldn’t come back with me this time.”

“As long as she’s well, that’s all that matters!” The old Madam Zhang’s face was full of relief. “By the way, what’s Ruoxi’s cultivation now?”

The Zhang Family suffered because they lacked an expert. Over the years, they had been conserving their strength and produced a few talented disciples, but with the family in decline, there weren’t enough resources for cultivation, so progress was slow and their cultivation levels were low. Ning Suwan, however, had advanced quite a bit after the upheaval years ago, but was only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.

In the entire Zhang Family, aside from her First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, there were no other Dao Origin Realm cultivators. The old Madam Zhang had Void King Realm cultivation, but was too old, her vitality waning, and could no longer fight.

In other words, all these years, it was Ning Suwan who had been supporting the Zhang Family.

“Ruoxi… she’s at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm now!” Yang Kai thought for a moment, but didn’t dare tell the truth.

Ruoxi’s current cultivation was truly hard to judge. Before the incident, she had indeed been at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm—but at that level, she was likely already unmatched even by ordinary Emperor Realm masters. Now that she had inherited the mysterious power of Heaven’s Punisher within the Blood Gate, once she emerged from seclusion, who knew how far she would have advanced?

As soon as Yang Kai said this, the entire Zhang Family’s reception hall fell silent. Everyone stared at Yang Kai, not with joy or excitement, but with disbelief.

It wasn’t that they didn’t trust Yang Kai, but his words were simply… too astonishing.

When Yang Kai took Zhang Ruoxi away, she was only at the Origin Returning Realm, then came the Void King Realm, and only after that the Dao Origin Realm…

The gap between the Void King Realm and the Dao Origin Realm was enormous, not everyone could cross it. Otherwise, the Zhang Family wouldn’t have so few Dao Origin Realm cultivators. Even if Ruoxi had good talent and really broke through, it was impossible to reach the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm in just ten years.

Ten years, to go from the Origin Returning Realm to the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm—how could such a thing happen?

Subconsciously, Ning Suwan and the old Madam Zhang felt Yang Kai was just comforting them.

“Ruoxi really is at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.” Yang Kai saw their doubt and said solemnly.

Ning Suwan and the old Madam Zhang exchanged a glance before asking, “Yang Kai, what is your cultivation?”

Yang Kai didn’t answer, but instead activated his Emperor Yuan, and the clear Emperor Intent instantly filled the room.

“Emperor Realm!” Ning Suwan cried out in shock, and the other younger members of the Zhang Family instantly widened their eyes.

Emperor Realm! A living Emperor! Although there were quite a few Emperor Realm experts in Fenglin City and Linghu City these days, such masters weren’t people they could just meet. Now, there was an Emperor Realm cultivator sitting in the Zhang Family’s reception hall.

“So, do you believe me now?” Yang Kai was a bit speechless. Luckily, he hadn’t told the whole truth, or the Zhang Family might have thought he’d lost his mind.

“Ruoxi… she really is at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm?” Ning Suwan asked, her voice trembling.

If Yang Kai was at the Emperor Realm, then Zhang Ruoxi being at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm wasn’t impossible.

“Absolutely!” Yang Kai nodded seriously.

“Good, good, Heaven bless the Zhang Family! The Zhang Family finally has someone at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.” Ning Suwan’s excitement was obvious, and even the white-haired old Madam Zhang showed joy. The younger members standing in the hall were full of admiration.

The Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm was, in their eyes, an unattainable existence. The Zhang Family had produced one, and she was their peer.

Especially the girl named Zhang Ruoyu, whose beautiful eyes shone with a heat that could melt ice.

“I came to the Zhang Family this time mainly to discuss something with you.” Yang Kai spoke once their emotions had calmed a bit.

“Go ahead, as long as it’s not too difficult, our Zhang Family will agree.” Ning Suwan said quickly. Refusing wasn’t an option; the requests of an Emperor Realm cultivator couldn’t be denied.

Yang Kai said, “I want to take you all somewhere.”

“Where?” Ning Suwan was puzzled.

“My Sect!” Yang Kai replied. “It’s completely safe there, and the cultivation environment is just as good as Linghu Palace. If you go, you’ll never have to worry about your cultivation or daily life again. There will be people to guide the Zhang Family disciples in cultivation, and you won’t have to worry about resources anymore.”

With every word Yang Kai spoke, the young Zhang Family disciples’ eyes grew brighter, each of them filled with anticipation. His words painted a picture before their eyes, a picture full of a beautiful future.

“Such a place…” Ning Suwan was a little lost in thought.

“Is Ruoxi there too?” the old Madam Zhang asked.

“Not right now, but she will be in the future,” Yang Kai replied.

“Alright, I agree. Yang Kai, how could our Zhang Family not accept your kindness?”

Ning Suwan was silent for a moment, then nodded slightly. If things were really as Yang Kai described, there would be many benefits to going. The Zhang Family had lived quietly in a corner for years, but recently, too many experts had been coming and going in Fenglin City and Linghu City. Any one of them could wipe out the Zhang Family. If they stayed here without the protection of an expert, who knows when disaster might strike.

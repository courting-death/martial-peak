## Chapter 2985 — Three Months

Yang Kai changed his incantation once more and struck out with another palm. "Demon Exorcism."

As the light flashed, a stream of black Qi howled out from behind Li Jiao, as though a vengeful spirit possessing him had been driven away. The sound of ghosts wailing and wolves howling even rang out.

The black Qi continued pouring out for more than a dozen breaths. When the Demonic Qi had finally been purged completely, a trace of joy flashed across Li Jiao's eyes.

He knew his own condition best. Demonic Qi had plagued him all these days, clinging to him like maggots to bone and even continuously multiplying. Despite his cultivation at the Third-Stage Emperor Realm, he had been unable to expel it.

Yet after Yang Kai's treatment, he could clearly feel that the problem troubling him had been completely resolved. Not a trace of Demonic Qi remained in his body. With the Demonic Qi expelled and the Bloodlust Art restraining him, not the slightest Demonic Intent could stir in his heart, and his Sea of Consciousness had become perfectly clear.

"Many thanks, Palace Master Yang!" Li Jiao was so grateful that tears filled his eyes.

The moment he finished speaking, however, he saw Yang Kai grin at him before fiercely striking him with another palm. This was definitely not a Demon Exorcism technique, because its power was quite formidable. Most importantly, it carried the suppressive power of the Dragon Clan's bloodline.

Poor Li Jiao, a Third-Stage Emperor Realm expert, did not even have time to react before the palm strike knocked him unconscious.

Yang Kai beckoned toward him, and Li Jiao immediately vanished without a trace.

Zhu Lie, who had been watching coldly from the side, could not help narrowing his eyes, unable to understand where Yang Kai had sent Li Jiao.

Only Zhu Qing knew that the little Jiao had likely been thrown into that strange space. The World Laws were about to change. Although the Demonic Qi in Li Jiao's body had been purged, it would inevitably corrode him again if he did not find a way to hide. Human Sovereign City was too far away, while Demon Fury City had nowhere to shelter, so that strange space was the best place for him.

Recalling what had happened in that strange space, Zhu Qing could not help feeling her cheeks grow hot.

At this moment, the transformation of the World Laws had already become extremely obvious. The surrounding Spiritual Qi seemed to have been tainted by something strange and was rapidly transforming into Demonic Qi.

The many Demon Kings and Demon Commanders waited excitedly. This was the aura of their ancestral land. Whenever the Revolving World's Laws leaned toward the Demon Domain, their cultivation and recovery would both accelerate considerably.

The little black dog also became extremely excited, clearly fond of these World Laws. Its enormous mouth split wide open as it continuously devoured the surrounding Demonic Qi.

In a short while, the entire world had undergone a dramatic transformation. Dense Demonic Qi filled the surroundings, shrouding the world in darkness and spreading in every direction, giving one the illusion of having entered the Demon Domain.

Although Yang Kai did not know what the Demon Domain actually looked like, he guessed it should not be much different from the Revolving World at this moment.

This transformation of the World Laws lasted only three days before ending. Over those three days, the experts of Demon Fury City recovered considerably, but their recovery was still unable to satisfy the little black dog's appetite.

Three days later, Yang Kai took the little black dog to Yin Wind Mountain once more. When he returned, his brow was tightly furrowed. Under the disappointed gazes of the many Demon Kings and Demon Commanders, he set the little black dog down and had it continue devouring Demon Yuan.

"How was it?" Zhu Lie looked up and asked.

"I couldn't see the exit." Yang Kai shook his head.

Although they had managed to travel farther this time, he still could not determine where the exit was. In other words, the little black dog was not yet strong enough to take them away, so they could only let it continue growing stronger.

Zhu Qing stepped forward, gently took Yang Kai's hand, and said, "Don't worry. We'll find a way out eventually; it's only a matter of time."

Yang Kai chuckled. "I'm not particularly worried. This damned place is simply rather oppressive. The sooner we leave, the sooner I can put my mind at ease."

Zhu Qing hesitated for a moment before softly asking, "What if we truly can't leave?"

Yang Kai tightened his grip on her hand and said with deep affection, "Then we'll stay here and grow old together."

Zhu Qing lowered her head, her face turning red.

"You two, do you think I'm blind?" Zhu Lie coldly snorted, his expression ugly.

He had already sternly warned Yang Kai before, yet this fellow had completely disregarded his words. That alone would have been bad enough, but Yang Kai was even openly exchanging tender looks and sweet words with Zhu Qing right beneath his nose. It was simply intolerable.

Yang Kai raised his arm and wrapped it around Zhu Qing's shoulders, then looked at Zhu Lie provocatively. "Keep an eye on things here. I'm taking your elder sister out for a walk."

After saying so, he directly embraced Zhu Qing and flew away.

Li Jiao became anxious and called loudly after them, "Palace Master Yang, come back soon!"

Without Yang Kai, he definitely could not withstand the corrosion of Demonic Qi when the World Laws changed. Although he still did not know what method Yang Kai had used earlier, he had indeed shown no sign of being corroded by even the slightest trace of Demonic Qi while unconscious.

If he wanted to survive in this place, he could only rely on Yang Kai.

"Where are we going?" Zhu Qing had thought Yang Kai brought her out because he had some business to attend to, so she asked with an innocent expression.

"Anywhere is fine, as long as no one else is around."

His words were far too explicit. Zhu Qing immediately understood, and her heart began leaping like a frightened rabbit as her face burned.

...

The days passed one after another, and in the blink of an eye, three months had passed since Yang Kai and the others arrived in Demon Fury City.

During these three months, the Demon Kings and Demon Commanders of Demon Fury City had lived a life worse than death, endlessly alternating between recovering and being drained. Often, they were forced back into action after recovering only twenty or thirty percent of their strength.

After three months, the cultivation of every Demon King and Demon Commander had fallen by one or two Stages.

It was like having their flesh carved away with a blunt knife, even more agonizing than being killed outright. At times, the Demon Kings wanted to disregard everything and fight Yang Kai and the others to the death, but after thinking it over, they decided that a miserable life was still better than a good death. Their ancestors had built their foundations here over more than a hundred thousand years, so Demon Fury City's inheritance could not be severed in their generation. With this thought in mind, they could only endure bitterly and hope these harbingers of calamity would leave as soon as possible.

The little black dog still looked the same and gave off only the aura of a Fifth- or Sixth-Tier beast, but Yang Kai's repeated tests had shown that it had grown much stronger.

The unknown space inside its belly had expanded to more than a hundred times its original size, allowing it to take Yang Kai much deeper than before.

Over these three months, Yang Kai and Zhu Qing occasionally went out to "tour the mountains and rivers," spending a day or two outside before returning to Demon Fury City. Zhu Lie had many complaints about this and repeatedly mocked them with cutting remarks, but to no effect. Zhu Qing was thin-skinned, so every time he mentioned it, she would feel guilty and blush for quite a while. Yang Kai, however, remained utterly shameless and treated everything Zhu Lie said as nothing more than a fart.

Li Jiao's days were not particularly pleasant either, because every time the World Laws leaned toward the Demon Domain, Yang Kai would knock him unconscious and only awaken him once the transformation ended.

Even now, he still did not know how Yang Kai had spared him the calamity of being corroded by Demonic Qi. Nevertheless, his gratitude toward Yang Kai grew with each passing day, and he increasingly seemed inclined to follow Yang Kai's every command.

By this point, Yang Kai felt somewhat fortunate that he had not let the little black dog devour the Demon Kings here. Had he truly done so, it could never have grown to its current level in just three months.

Keeping these egg-laying chickens alive was indeed better than devouring them outright.

The Demon Race's days of suffering were finally nearing their end.

The last time Yang Kai returned from Yin Wind Mountain with the little black dog, he told Zhu Lie that he had vaguely grasped the exit's location. As long as the little black dog grew a little stronger, they should have a chance to leave this place.

This news greatly encouraged every member of the Demon Race. They had endured bitterly for three months precisely for this day, and now that they could see hope, they naturally worked even harder to recover.

They brought out their treasured Demon Fruits and Demon Artifacts they could not ordinarily bear to use, sparing no effort to restore their strength.

After yet another transformation of the World Laws, Yang Kai brought Li Jiao out of the Small Xuan Realm and woke him.

In the square, all the Demon Kings and Demon Commanders sat cross-legged on the ground, their faces weak and their bodies gaunt as they stared eagerly at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai grinned and declared in a clear voice, "Since ancient times, Humans and Demons have been irreconcilable. Our two races bear a blood feud that cannot be resolved. Logically speaking, I should kill all of you and settle matters once and for all."

As soon as he said this, the expressions of the many members of the Demon Race changed slightly, but they did not panic too much. From the meaning behind Yang Kai's words, they could tell he had no intention of doing so.

"However, thanks to your help over these past few months, we now have hope of leaving, so I am willing to spare your lives."

"Many thanks, Sir!" the Succubus shouted, but none of the other members of the Demon Race responded.

Yang Kai smiled. "I hope we never have the chance to meet again."

Who would ever want to see you again? The many Demon Kings and Demon Commanders cursed inwardly.

"Farewell!" Yang Kai smiled faintly, beckoned the little black dog over, and took the lead in flying toward Yin Wind Mountain.

Zhu Qing, Zhu Lie, and Li Jiao hurriedly followed.

"May Sir enjoy triumphant martial fortune and immediate success!" The Succubus vigorously waved her hand, her eyes growing slightly moist.

It had not been easy, but they had finally sent these harbingers of calamity away. As the many Demon Kings and Demon Commanders watched Yang Kai and the others depart, they sincerely prayed that the group would successfully leave this world.

Yang Kai had visited Yin Wind Mountain no fewer than a dozen times, bringing the little black dog here to test it each time, and on every attempt, they had managed to travel much farther than before.

As a result, he was already quite familiar with the entire process.

Frigid Demonic Qi swept over endlessly from some unknown place, growing more terrifying the deeper they ventured.

For the first thousand zhang, none of them encountered any problems, as their cultivations were enough to resist the frigid Demonic Qi's effects. Beyond a thousand zhang, however, the journey began to become strenuous. Without the little black dog's help, none of them could have advanced even another hundred zhang.

The little black dog was clearly well acquainted with this as well, so once they passed a thousand zhang, it immediately opened its enormous mouth and swallowed all the frigid Demonic Qi rushing toward them.

In an instant, the pressure on the group of four diminished dramatically. The little black dog devoured roughly ninety percent of the frigid Demonic Qi blowing toward them, while the remaining ten percent dispersed, making it exceedingly easy for Yang Kai and the others to handle.

They continued advancing without pause. Although the frigid Demonic Qi grew increasingly powerful, the little black dog remained perfectly unharmed and swallowed all of it without exception.

Only after they passed ten thousand zhang did a faint chill begin to appear across the surface of its body, clearly indicating that the frigid Demonic Qi was finally beginning to affect it.

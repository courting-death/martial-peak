## Chapter 2732 — Truly an Imperial-Grade Pill Master

He actually took it out?

Mi Qi and Li Jiao exchanged glances, a bit shocked, but mostly distrustful.

Although Yang Kai had destroyed Wenqing Sect, seized its foundation, and gained its accumulated wealth over the years, they didn’t believe there were really five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals in that ring.

Wenqing Sect, Li Dragon Palace, and Mitian Sect were roughly comparable. Their annual income was impressive, but with such large operations, their expenses were terrifying. Most of the income was spent each year, and only a small portion was ever saved.

Even Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace only had about one billion Upper-Level Source Crystals in storage. Wenqing Sect should be similar.

In other words, even if Yang Kai got all of Wenqing Sect’s wealth, it would only be about one billion Upper-Level Source Crystals. Adding in other treasures converted to Source Crystals, the total would be two or three billion at most—how could there possibly be five billion?

“It’s not that we don’t trust Palace Master Yang, but we need to check the ring,” Mi Qi said in a deep voice.

“Of course it needs to be checked,” Yang Kai nodded. “But… I’ll trouble Master Ji to inspect it. You two trust Master Ji’s character, don’t you?”

“Naturally,” Mi Qi quickly nodded.

Li Jiao had no objections either.

“Then I’ll trouble Master Ji,” Yang Kai smiled slightly, tossing the ring to Ji Ying.

Ji Ying caught it, swept his Divine Sense inside carelessly, and the next moment his eyes widened, his face full of shock as he exclaimed, “Th-this…!”

He was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master and never lacked for wealth. Across the entire Northern Territory, those seeking his alchemy were beyond count; each refinement brought him immense returns. Not to mention five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals—even ten billion would not have been enough to leave Ji Ying so astonished.

Yet now he was so excited he couldn’t even speak, his expression clearly seeing something unbelievable.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao were both startled. They wondered what was in the ring to make Master Ji lose composure like this. Their curiosity was killing them, but they were too embarrassed to ask for a look.

They didn’t think Yang Kai would let them see it. Otherwise, he wouldn’t have asked Ji Ying to inspect the ring.

“Where did you get so much?” Ji Ying turned, staring at Yang Kai in shock.

Yang Kai chuckled, took the Spatial Ring back from Ji Ying, and shook his head, saying, “Can’t say, can’t say!”

Ji Ying swallowed, reluctantly watching as Yang Kai put the ring away. He knew it must be Yang Kai’s secret and didn’t press further.

After hesitating, he said, “Palace Master Yang, if you lose this pill refining contest, I won’t take your head. How about you give me half of what’s in there?”

He never intended to take Yang Kai’s life—just to scare him. Now that he saw something better, he naturally wanted to change the wager.

The ring didn’t hold anything else—it was filled with the million Inner Cores Yang Kai had brought out from the Wild Ancient Land.

A hundred billion Source Crystals wouldn’t move Ji Ying, but a million Inner Cores was different. So many piled together, including Twelfth-Tier Inner Cores, not even the ten Great Emperors had seen such a thing.

Moreover, Inner Cores had endless uses. Pill Masters often needed Demonic Beast Inner Cores as ingredients. A top-quality Inner Core was a perfect catalyst, able to produce an exceptional Spiritual Pill.

These were far more attractive to Ji Ying than Source Crystals, no wonder he was so shocked.

“Master Ji wants them?” Yang Kai smiled at him.

Ji Ying blushed, coughed, and said, “What Pill Master wouldn’t want such treasures?”

“If you want, I can give them all to you,” Yang Kai’s smile didn’t fade.

“Really?” Ji Ying was startled, but quickly realized, his face turning serious. “You want me to refine pills for Lingxiao Palace, don’t you?”

Yang Kai laughed loudly, “Master Ji sees right through me.”

Ji Ying snorted, “Palace Master Yang, nice try, but I won’t agree.”

Yang Kai had expected this, so he wasn’t disappointed. He nodded, “In that case, if Master Ji wins, you get half of them.”

“Good, good, you said it! Hope Palace Master Yang keeps his word.” Ji Ying was overjoyed. To him, it was as if Yang Kai was gifting him Inner Cores.

He never imagined he’d lose to Yang Kai in pill refining.

“Master Ji, are the things in the ring worth five billion?” Li Jiao and Mi Qi had been listening for a while, still unsure of the ring’s value, so Li Jiao couldn’t help but ask.

“Five billion?” Ji Ying looked at them disdainfully. “The things in that ring could buy both your Sects!”

“What!” Mi Qi and Li Jiao were utterly shocked.

Enough to buy two Sects—what treasures were in that ring to earn such praise from Master Ji?

Their curiosity grew even stronger. They wished they could knock Yang Kai out and snatch the ring for a look.

“My wager is set. Are you two still betting?” Yang Kai turned to Mi Qi and Li Jiao.

Mi Qi frowned, then said firmly, “Of course, why not? Who would refuse a free benefit?”

“Indeed,” Li Jiao said seriously. “This Lord trusts Master Ji.”

Ji Ying frowned, “Your bet is your own business. Win or lose, it has nothing to do with me.”

Pill refining isn’t for gambling. He only agreed to bet with Yang Kai because Yang Kai provoked him. He didn’t want Li Jiao and Mi Qi tying their outcome to his, as if it was all his responsibility.

“Yes, yes, Master Ji is right,” Li Jiao quickly corrected. “This is just a bet between Mi Qi, myself, and Palace Master Yang.”

“In that case, let’s begin,” Yang Kai smiled.

Ji Ying turned to him, “You first.”

Yang Kai said seriously, “Master Ji, is it really wise to underestimate your opponent?”

Though he said this, his hands were already moving. He raised both hands and brushed them across his temples, striking a posture as if ready to give it his all. His fingers flickered like threads of silk; the lid of the Purple Void Cauldron flew open at once, and a Spiritual Medicine he had long prepared shot straight into the cauldron.

With a sudden whoosh, it was as if flames had ignited—the air grew noticeably hotter. Strangely, there was no sign that Yang Kai had activated his Emperor Yuan at all.

“Divine Sense Fire!” Ji Ying’s expression changed, exclaiming in a low voice.

He hadn’t expected Yang Kai to possess Divine Sense Fire. That alone was enough to make him take this seriously.

Divine Sense Fire, a variant of Divine Sense, gave a unique advantage in pill and artifact refinement. During pill refining, Divine Sense played a major role—controlling heat and timing the addition of Spiritual Medicine required careful inspection by Divine Sense.

With Divine Sense Fire, one could directly adjust the heat and precisely time the addition of Spiritual Medicine.

Even when inscribing spirit arrays, no carrier was needed—one could inscribe them in midair.

No other Pill Master could compare.

“So that’s it—no wonder he’s so confident. He actually possesses Divine Sense Fire,” Ji Ying suddenly understood where Yang Kai’s confidence came from. His expression grew solemn, and without further hesitation, his hands moved swiftly. As he activated his Emperor Yuan, a gentle flame rose within his Three-Legged Twin-Dragon Cauldron.

Ji Ying put the same Spiritual Medicines into his pill furnace. A sizzling sound came out as his hands formed seals, inscribing spiritual formation inside to help condense the medicinal liquid.

For a moment, the hall was silent. Only Yang Kai and Ji Ying’s powers surged, their pill furnaces radiating intense heat.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao wanted to take a look, but the three Yao Kings blocked them, glaring fiercely.

When Pill Masters refine pills, interruptions are taboo. The three Yao Kings guarded against these two, afraid they’d interfere with Yang Kai. Mi Qi and Li Jiao could only glare helplessly.

“Can Palace Master win?” Hua Qingsi and Zhu Qing stood side by side, quietly watching.

Yang Kai was competing with Ji Ying in pill refining. Hua Qingsi felt uneasy, so she secretly sent a message to Zhu Qing.

“I don’t understand pill refining,” Zhu Qing replied calmly.

Hua Qingsi thought so too. She’d seen pills refined, but knew nothing of the art, so she couldn’t tell who had the advantage.

“But look at his demeanor—he seems very confident,” Zhu Qing added.

Hearing this, Hua Qingsi quickly observed Yang Kai and found, just as Zhu Qing said, his expression was full of confidence and fighting spirit. If he had no hope of winning, he wouldn’t look like that.

This discovery eased Hua Qingsi’s heart, and she silently cheered for Yang Kai.

Sss sss sss sss…

The sound of the furnace fire never stopped. The two competitors’ hands moved constantly, seals changing, intricate spirit arrays appearing and vanishing inside the pill furnaces. Spiritual Medicine after Spiritual Medicine was added, and the fragrance of the condensed medicinal liquid soon filled the air.

Ji Ying kept a close eye on Yang Kai’s actions. Though Yang Kai had Divine Sense Fire and couldn’t be underestimated, Ji Ying still felt he couldn’t lose.

Yet after observing, Ji Ying confirmed one thing: Yang Kai really was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, and his fundamentals were extremely solid. His movements in condensing medicinal liquid were meticulous, with no mistakes, and the result was exceptionally pure.

Without being an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, it would be impossible to reach this level.

He was secretly delighted. Another Imperial-Grade Pill Master had appeared in the world. If he told his master, he would be pleased. The Pill Dao was ancient and profound, harder to advance than the Martial Dao, which meant Pill Masters were rare.

Others only saw the glory of Pill Masters, but who could see the hardship behind it?

Medicine Pill Valley was devoted to spreading the Pill Dao. Great Emperor Miaodan’s disciples all took this as their mission, hoping for more Pill Masters. Only with more Pill Masters could the Star Realm’s cultivators get the pills they needed and advance quickly.

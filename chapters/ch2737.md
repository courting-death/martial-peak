## Chapter 2737 — You All Think It's Good Too

"Wait, wait!" Seeing the three Yao Kings rush toward him, Mi Qi hurriedly raised his hand and shouted.

"Hold on!" Yang Kai also barked out.

The three Yao Kings immediately halted, but surrounded Li Jiao and Mi Qi, their Yao Yuan enveloping them, leaving no room to escape.

Cold sweat slid down their cheeks, dripping onto the ground with a clear sound.

Yang Kai stared at the two and said, "Sect Master Mi, any last words? This Palace Master is merciful—I'll let you linger for a few more breaths!"

So it's already time for last words... Mi Qi's face darkened. He suppressed his nerves and said anxiously, "Palace Master Yang, Brother Li and I aren't trying to renege, nor did we intend to break the bet. It's true, we don't have that much to wager, and shouldn't have made such a big bet with you. But now that things have come to this, even if you kill us, it won't do you any good."

"That's right, that's right. If you kill us, those five billion Source Crystals will really be gone, and you'll also make mortal enemies of Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect."

"I wiped out Wenqing Sect, you think I'm afraid of Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect?" Yang Kai sneered, clearly disdainful. Still, he stroked his chin, looking as if he was seriously considering it. After a while, he nodded, "But you make a fair point. Go on—whether you live depends on your tongues."

Mi Qi let out a huge sigh of relief, knowing his life was safe for now. But he also understood that if he couldn't convince Yang Kai to spare them, their lives would end here today.

After pondering for a moment and organizing his words, he said, "Brother Li and I can't come up with that many Source Crystals right now, but we can scrape together a portion."

"How much?" Yang Kai asked in a deep voice.

"Five... five hundred million?" Mi Qi asked tentatively.

Li Jiao nodded, "Five hundred million is doable."

"Are you trying to fob me off like a beggar?" Yang Kai snorted coldly. "Even the Source Crystals I found in Wenqing Sect's vaults were more than five hundred million! If you dare mention five hundred million again, I'll kill you right now. I don't care about those five billion Source Crystals anymore!"

"One billion, one billion!" Mi Qi quickly corrected himself, then added, "Can't go any higher, really can't."

That was already Mitian Sect's limit. To gather one billion, they'd probably have to sell off some assets.

"Is that what Palace Master Li thinks too?" Yang Kai looked coldly at Li Jiao.

Li Jiao replied uneasily, "Yes, yes, one billion is the most we can manage."

"One billion, huh..." Yang Kai paced back and forth, thought for a while, then nodded, "One billion will do."

Li Jiao and Mi Qi were overjoyed at his words.

Before they could respond, Yang Kai continued, “You’re still short one and a half billion. How do you plan to settle the rest?”

"Uh..." Li Jiao and Mi Qi were momentarily speechless, not knowing how to answer.

Yang Kai said, "I actually have an idea. Would you like to hear it?"

"Please enlighten us, Palace Master Yang!" Li Jiao and Mi Qi both looked at Yang Kai.

"If you don't have enough Source Crystals, use treasures instead. I'm sure your sects' vaults have plenty of rare treasures. My Lingxiao Palace was just founded and needs a lot of cultivation resources. I'll count one Imperial Treasure as ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals. One and a half billion means one hundred fifty items."

"One hundred fifty Imperial Treasures!"

"An Imperial Treasure is only worth ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals?"

Mi Qi and Li Jiao exclaimed together, cursing in their hearts. They knew Yang Kai would take the chance to fleece them, but didn't expect it to be so ruthless—this was outright robbery.

What Imperial Treasure is only worth ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals? Even the worst Imperial Treasure is worth more than that. And one hundred fifty Imperial Treasures? That's an absurd amount. Even if both sects pooled all their resources, they couldn't find that many.

"If you don't have Imperial Treasures, Dao Origin-Grade Secret Treasures, Void King-Grade Secret Treasures will do. Also, all kinds of spirit flowers, rare herbs, precious minerals—any cultivation resources, I'll gladly accept." Yang Kai waved his hand, looking very accommodating.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi's faces were as black as the bottom of a pot. If they really did as Yang Kai said, wouldn't their sects be emptied out? How would their disciples cultivate? How could they face their elders and disciples?

"This won't do, won't do..." Li Jiao was nearly in tears. "Palace Master Yang, please show mercy."

Mi Qi added, "Palace Master Yang, you're cutting the ground from under us, destroying our sects!"

Yang Kai's face turned cold. "I've already given you a chance, but you don't appreciate it. Now you want to blame me?"

Mi Qi and Li Jiao were left speechless.

Yang Kai let out a sigh and said, “Just as Brother Ji mentioned earlier, we’re all cultivators of the Northern Territory. We’ll be seeing each other often in the future—if I go too far, even I would feel a bit embarrassed.”

You? Embarrassed? Li Jiao and Mi Qi grumbled inwardly.

"Alright, I have another proposal. If that doesn't work, you two will stay here and let your sects ransom you. When they've gathered enough Source Crystals, I'll let you go."

"Please go on, Palace Master Yang."

Though they knew Yang Kai's idea wouldn't be good, it was better than him using force. Listening couldn't hurt.

"First, gather one billion Source Crystals. The remaining one and a half billion... will be paid in installments." Yang Kai waved his hand.

"Installments?" Li Jiao and Mi Qi exchanged glances, cautiously asking, "How would that work?"

Yang Kai said, "Your sects are wealthy, with plenty of income each year. From now on, give a portion of that income to my Lingxiao Palace. When the debt is paid off, we're done. What do you think?"

Mi Qi was delighted, "That's a good idea, Palace Master Yang. Let's pay in installments."

Li Jiao nodded, "Yes, yes, Palace Master Yang is truly clever to come up with such a plan."

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "So you all think it's a good idea. Since that's the case, let's settle it this way. Of course, we'll need to discuss the details carefully."

"Of course, of course." Mi Qi kept nodding.

Yang Kai said, "Your sects' annual income—I estimate fifty million Upper-Level Source Crystals. That's about right, isn't it?"

"That's roughly correct." Mi Qi and Li Jiao nodded. For smaller sects, that number was terrifying. At the auction in Flowing Shadow City, Emperor Realm cultivators emptied their sects' savings to gather one or two million Upper-Level Source Crystals. But for Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect, annual income was fifty million.

The difference was enormous.

"I don't need fifty million. Just give my Lingxiao Palace forty million each year." Yang Kai looked at them.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao's faces twitched. With forty million a year, only ten million would be left for each sect. Their income would shrink drastically, and life would be tough.

But to pay off the debt, they had no choice.

They nodded in resignation, agreeing.

"Pay for fifty years, and we're even." Yang Kai said solemnly.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao frowned. Mi Qi said, "Palace Master Yang, the years... don't add up."

Li Jiao added, “Indeed, Palace Master Yang, have you miscalculated? Brother Mi and I each wagered two and a half billion. After first making up one billion for you, there would still be one and a half billion remaining. If we pay you forty million each year, no matter how you calculate it, it wouldn’t take fifty years.”


If calculated according to what Yang Kai had said, the total each of the two great Sects would end up paying Lingxiao Palace would reach three billion Upper-Level Source Crystals—fully five hundred million more than the original wager.

Yang Kai sneered, "You aren't counting interest?"

"Interest!" Mi Qi and Li Jiao were dumbfounded.

What kind of interest is that? Five hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals—that's ten years' income for each sect!

Only now did they realize Yang Kai's cunning. Installments sounded like mercy, but it was a trap, luring them in.

"Two options, your choice," Yang Kai said impatiently. "Either gather two and a half billion in Source Crystals and deliver them to Lingxiao Palace within a month, or pay one billion now and the rest over fifty years!"

"Palace Master Yang..."

"If you keep haggling, do you really think I can't wipe out your sects?" Yang Kai's expression turned fierce.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao immediately fell silent, fear on their faces.

They could see Yang Kai's patience was nearly gone. If they kept arguing, they'd only suffer more.

Mi Qi sighed helplessly, "Let's pay in installments."

Though they'd have to pay five hundred million more and it would hurt, at least their foundations wouldn't be destroyed. If they had to gather two and a half billion in a month, their sects would be crippled for a century.

But this way, for fifty years, Li Dragon Palace and Mitian Sect would have to follow Lingxiao Palace's lead. Their disciples wouldn't be able to hold their heads up in front of Lingxiao Palace's disciples.

Li Jiao said gloomily, "I'll pay in installments too."

"Good!" Yang Kai clapped and laughed. "You two are straightforward. In that case, go back first. In a month, I want to see your one billion Source Crystals. The rest I'll collect each year. No time to waste, I won't keep you—please go!"

Mi Qi and Li Jiao weakly cupped their fists and turned to leave.

Unlike their earlier excitement, now they were utterly dejected.

Anyone who went through this would be unhappy. And explaining it to their sects' leaders would be troublesome. A lifetime of reputation ruined in a day! Both were filled with regret, wondering if they should just walk out and refuse to pay...

"Palace Master Li, wait."

Before they could leave the hall, Ji Ying suddenly spoke.

Li Jiao stopped and turned to Ji Ying, "Master Ji, what instructions do you have?"

"I wouldn't dare call it instructions," Ji Ying replied calmly. He tossed a wooden box to Li Jiao. "My skills are lacking, and I fear I've let you down, Palace Master Li. I'll return the Dragon Blood Flower to you. Please find someone more capable."

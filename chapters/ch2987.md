## Chapter 2987 — The Desert

In the boundless Void, the group travelled for an unknown length of time, avoiding countless Void Turbulences before finally arriving at a peculiar place.

This place seemed divided into upper and lower levels, each containing an enormous vortex. The interiors of these vortices were pitch-black, as though they could devour all things, making one's blood run cold. Both vortices appeared to be in a mysterious state of stillness, yet anyone could sense the astonishing suction coming from within them.

Yang Kai and the others stood directly between these two vortices.

After observing them intently for a long time, Yang Kai said solemnly, "There are two exits here. If my guess is correct, one should lead to the Star Realm, while the other should lead to the Demon Domain."

Li Jiao asked, "Which one should we take?"

Yang Kai pointed and said, "The one above. Although they look almost identical, some Demonic Qi is leaking from the one below, so it definitely leads to the Demon Domain."

"Since you can determine the way, then let's go," Zhu Lie urged.

Yang Kai nodded and warned, “I will take you out of this place and back to the Star Realm, but I cannot guarantee your safety along the way. After all, too much depends on chance.”

"Matters have already reached this point. There's no use saying more."

Yang Kai grinned. "Then let's go. Make sure you stay close."

As soon as he finished speaking, he shot toward the vortex above. The Laws of Space surged wildly around him, transforming into an invisible impact that poured into the vortex. As he probed the situation within, he broke through layer after layer of barriers, opening a safe passage between the two worlds.

The silent vortex immediately underwent an unexpected change. It began rotating in layer upon layer, while a violent crushing force came from every direction, seemingly intent on squeezing them until they burst.

At first glance, Yang Kai seemed to be riding the wind and breaking through the waves. The waves parted to either side of him, only to close and surge together again behind him.

Zhu Lie and the others had to follow closely behind Yang Kai to avoid the danger of being swallowed by those waves.

Fortunately, everyone present was an expert among experts, and they were connected by the colored ribbon Imperial Treasure, so no one made a mistake at this critical moment.

Yang Kai charged forward, pushing the Laws of Space to their limit and breaking through every obstacle in his path.

After travelling through the Void vortex for an unknown length of time, Yang Kai suddenly felt an enormous resistance ahead. It was as though he had slammed into an iron plate, causing his Qi and blood to roil as his head spun and his vision blurred.

A world barrier!

Great Worlds could not connect freely with one another precisely because of the existence of world barriers. These barriers separated two worlds and were generally so sturdy that even a Great Emperor could not tear through them.

As far as Yang Kai knew, only the Great Demon God of the Demon Domain had ever pierced a world barrier, allowing experts from the Demon Domain to descend upon the Star Realm in ancient times.

If this world barrier had been in its normal state, Yang Kai could not have hoped to break through it even with his mastery of the Laws of Space. His cultivation was still too low, after all. Compared with those supreme experts, he was only a First-Stage Emperor Realm cultivator.

However, the world barrier here was not complete. The Revolving World drifted through the gap between the Star Realm and the Demon Domain, so flaws had always existed in its barrier.

Although the collision left Yang Kai dazed and disoriented, it also allowed him to perceive an opportunity to break through.

The Seven-Colored Warm Spirit Lotus in his Sea of Consciousness suddenly blossomed with seven-colored radiance, stabilizing his mind. At the same time, he urged the power of the Laws of Space and condensed Moon Blades one after another, bombarding the space ahead.

Countless Moon Blades shot forward as though chasing the stars and pursuing the moon, then exploded.

A crack truly appeared in the sturdy world barrier.

Yang Kai was overjoyed and led everyone through the crack.

Just then, however, an unexpected change occurred. Perhaps because of Yang Kai's actions, the moment the crack appeared in the world barrier, streams of Void Turbulence suddenly erupted and instantly engulfed the group.

Yang Kai's expression changed. By the time he recovered, his hand was empty and Zhu Qing had vanished.

His face paled in alarm as he spread out his Divine Sense, his heart sinking to the bottom of a valley.

Not only had Zhu Qing disappeared, but Zhu Lie and Li Jiao, who had been following directly behind him, had also vanished without a trace. Even the little black dog was nowhere to be found.

Cold sweat immediately poured down Yang Kai's body. He turned and tried to rush back, but the world barrier had already become impossible to locate.

His vision blurred, and he appeared in another world.

Sensing the familiar World Laws around him, Yang Kai understood that he had returned to the Star Realm. However, contrary to his expectations, this was not the Frozen Earth, for there was no bitter cold or vast expanse of snow in the surroundings.

This was not even the Northern Territory. Although the Star Realm's World Laws were broadly the same everywhere, there were still slight differences between its various territories.

Yang Kai had not spent very long in the Northern Territory, but if this were the Northern Territory, he would definitely have been able to sense it.

What damned place was this?

Could Zhu Qing and the others have failed to keep up and become lost in the Void? At this thought, Yang Kai's expression became extremely ugly. He had never expected the accident that occurred when he broke through the world barrier. Had he known this would happen, he would certainly have been even more careful.

If Zhu Qing and the others had truly become lost in the Void, they might never find a way out. In that case, he would have to return to the Revolving World, but now that the little black dog was also missing, even if he could enter the Revolving World again, he would be unable to venture deep into Yin Wind Mountain.

Yang Kai's mood became extremely heavy, and all the joy he had felt upon returning to the Star Realm vanished.

Looking down at his waist, he saw that Zhu Lie's colored ribbon Secret Treasure had snapped. Its radiance was dim, and all its spirituality had been lost. This was an Imperial Treasure of considerable quality, yet the Void Turbulence had severed it. If that same force had struck Zhu Qing and the others, even their Dragon bodies would likely have been injured.

Taking a deep breath, Yang Kai forced himself to calm down and carefully recalled everything that had just happened.

Only after a short while did his tense expression gradually ease.

Although everything had happened in an extremely brief instant, he had still sensed something. Looking back over those events, he could find a few clues. He now felt that Zhu Qing and the others were unlikely to have become lost in the Void. Rather, the Void Turbulence had affected them while they were passing through the world barrier, causing them to become separated from him.

If so, that meant they had also returned to the Star Realm and had merely landed in different places.

As long as they had returned to the Star Realm, Zhu Qing and Zhu Lie would face no danger given their strength. Perhaps they were also searching for him right now.

This thought allowed Yang Kai to relax considerably, though whether his guess was correct still remained to be verified.

He turned and surveyed his surroundings. Yellow sand covered the ground, and fierce winds howled through what appeared to be a desert. The Spiritual Qi here was rather poor, and the environment was harsh. Not a single blade of grass could be seen.

No matter what, finding Zhu Qing and the others was the most urgent task.

With this thought, Yang Kai immediately summoned his Flowing Cloud Shuttle, chose a direction at random, and began searching.

This desert seemed to be enormous. Yang Kai searched it for several days without seeing its edge. Of course, he had been circling continuously around the place where he first appeared rather than travelling in a straight line. Had he flown straight ahead, he might already have left the desert.

Naturally, he kept circling because he was searching for traces of Zhu Qing and the others.

Five days later, while monitoring the surroundings from inside the Flowing Cloud Shuttle, Yang Kai finally sensed a familiar aura. His spirits rose, and he immediately sped in that direction.

It did not take long for him to arrive. Ahead, clouds of dust billowed as though an army of thousands were galloping past, creating an astonishing commotion.

In front of the dust cloud, a tiny black shadow was fleeing at full speed, as though being pursued by a powerful expert. It appeared quite panicked and constantly changed direction.

The black shadow was astonishingly fast. The creature chasing it was undoubtedly an extremely powerful Yao Beast. Although it travelled atop the sand, it was still unable to catch its target. It repeatedly let out furious roars and launched attack after attack, blasting enormous craters into the surrounding desert.

Yang Kai put away the Flowing Cloud Shuttle and flew directly toward the fleeing black shadow.

Seemingly sensing Yang Kai's aura, the little black shadow looked up at him and immediately rushed over in delighted surprise. Its four short legs flew beneath it as its speed suddenly increased, instantly widening the distance between itself and its pursuer.

A moment later, the little black shadow reached Yang Kai and leapt into his arms, whimpering several times as its body trembled.

"Don't be afraid, Ah Wang!" Yang Kai's anxious heart finally relaxed as he gently stroked the little black dog's back.

Only after seeing the little black dog could Yang Kai confirm that Zhu Qing and the others should be safe. At the very least, Zhu Qing was definitely fine, because she had always carried the little black dog in her arms. Since the little black dog had returned to the Star Realm, Zhu Qing must have returned as well. He simply did not know where she was now.

The little black dog had clearly been pursued for a long time. It was now panting heavily, its tiny body heaving without pause. Only after Yang Kai held it in his arms did it finally regain some sense of security.

"Roar!"

A thunderous roar erupted, making their eardrums tremble. The next moment, a cloud of dust swept toward them as a gigantic Yao Beast, standing as tall as a two-story building, suddenly appeared not far ahead. Its appearance was savage and terrifying, resembling an enraged bull, with a pair of tusks jutting from its mouth and blood-red eyes blazing with ferocity. Yao Yuan surged around its body as it radiated the aura of an Eleventh-Tier peak beast.

Yang Kai burst out laughing. "You're far too useless. You let this thing chase you all over the place? Don't you have a devouring Divine Ability?"

An Eleventh-Tier Yao Beast was only equivalent to the Dao Origin Realm. This Yao Beast might look imposing, but the little black dog had even swallowed a Demon King before, so how could it be helpless against a mere Eleventh-Tier Yao Beast?

No matter how Yang Kai thought about it, he could not understand what was happening.

"Stop fooling around and swallow it." Yang Kai prodded the little black dog, but to his surprise, it shrank even deeper into his embrace, leaving only its rear exposed. Its hind legs clamped its short tail between them, and it seemed determined not to come out no matter what.

"Roar!" Another furious roar rang out. The Eleventh-Tier Yao Beast seemed enraged by Yang Kai's contempt. It raised an enormous paw and slammed it down toward his head, seemingly intent on flattening him into a meat patty.

Yang Kai frowned and pointed a finger forward, striking the centre of the Yao Beast's paw.

Without a sound, the enormous paw suddenly exploded. The force of the blast sent the Yao Beast staggering backward. The savage ferocity in its scarlet eyes instantly vanished, replaced by intense horror.

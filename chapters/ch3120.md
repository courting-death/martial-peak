## Chapter 3120 — What a Coincidence

They were arguing over only one question: how should they fight?

Should they divide the enemy and defeat them one by one, or strike straight at the heart? The people in the hall had split into two factions, each airing their own views, and even after arguing for a long time, they still had no conclusion.

Suddenly, Ye Xijun, who had been sitting motionless, raised her head. Her deep eyes looked outward, as if they could pierce the barriers of space.

The argument in the hall came to an abrupt halt, and everyone turned to look at her.

Chu Lingxiao asked, "What happened?"

"Someone has broken into Lingxiao Sect," Ye Xijun replied. As the acting Sect Master, she held the Formation Plate that controlled Lingxiao Sect's Guardian Mountain Array, so if anyone trespassed, she would naturally sense it.

"What?" Everyone was shocked, and all of them found it inconceivable. The Lingxiao Sect of today was no longer the fledgling force it had been back then. In the Star Domain, the Sect was a colossal existence. Who could be so outrageously bold as to trespass here? And who would even have the ability to break in?

In an instant, everyone's expression turned extremely solemn.

"This aura..." Ye Xijun's expression changed again. Her pretty face, always as calm as an ancient well, filled first with disbelief, then immediately with excitement. In a flicker, she left the hall.

Everyone exchanged puzzled looks, unable to understand what could have happened to make Ye Xijun lose her composure like this. They knew her temperament better than anyone. Even if the sky collapsed, she would not so much as furrow her brow, so her reaction now was truly unexpected.

One after another, they rose and walked out of the hall, gathering outside to gaze into the distance.

Over there, two figures were approaching gracefully, strolling along as if through a quiet courtyard, neither hurried nor slow. Pair after pair of eyes fixed on them, then suddenly widened all at once, as if everyone thought they were dreaming.

Yang Kai greeted every Lingxiao Sect disciple he met along the way with easy warmth, leaving many of them staring openmouthed at him or Su Yan and looking utterly foolish.

Most of Lingxiao Sect's disciples were people he had brought over from Tongxuan Continent and Zhongdu, so nearly all of them recognized him. Even those who did not should at least recognize Su Yan. Now that these two people, who had vanished for several dozen years, had appeared together, none of them could quite believe it.

Wherever the two passed, every disciple froze in a daze, their eyes following their backs without shifting for even an instant, unable to recover for a long time.

All of Lingxiao Sect seemed to have been struck by a body-binding spell cast by some mighty master. Everything froze in place, and only Yang Kai's greetings continued to ring out.

"Yang Kai! It's Yang Kai!" Dong Qinghan suddenly cried out, rubbing his eyes hard for fear he had seen wrong. He grabbed Lan Chudie and said, "Do you see him too? Is Yang Kai back?"

Lan Chudie nodded somewhat blankly. "You saw right. He's back."

Dong Qinghan slapped his palms together hard.

The two of them had become husband and wife back in Zhongdu. Now, after sharing hardships for several dozen years, they had always treated each other with mutual respect and could be considered a fine match made by Heaven.

A dozen or so old acquaintances of the younger generation all showed excitement. Ye Xijun, Chu Lingxiao, and the others had far stronger composure and cultivation of the mind, but even they found it difficult to keep their emotions from showing at this moment.

Before long, Yang Kai arrived in front of everyone. Sweeping his eyes over the group, he grinned and said, "I'm back!"

The enthusiastic welcome he had imagined did not appear. The group only stared at him in a daze, making his eagerness feel rather awkward. He reached up, rubbed his nose, and said carefully, "I'm back..."

As soon as he finished speaking, Dong Qinghan pounced over and gave Yang Kai a bear hug, his large hands thumping hard against Yang Kai's back.

Yang Kai did not know whether to laugh or cry. He had not expected the first person to answer him to be this fatty. Only after letting Dong Qinghan slap him for a while did he say, "Cousin, you should lose some weight."

"You're really back?" Only then did Dong Qinghan let go, pull back a little, and carefully size Yang Kai up.

"I'm really back!"

Dong Qinghan turned, pointed at Yang Kai, and shouted to everyone, "Yang Kai is back!"

As if he had been the first one to notice.

Everyone's expressions turned solemn, and they saluted together. "Greetings, Sect Master!" Their voices trembled slightly, betraying the turmoil in their hearts.

Yang Kai raised a hand, and an invisible force kept everyone from bowing. Stranger still, no one sensed the slightest trace of him using any power. This ability far exceeded everyone's imagination, and they suddenly felt he was truly unfathomable.

Yang Kai's expression turned solemn. Cupping his fists, he saluted and said, "Greetings, Ancestor. Greetings, Grandmaster!"

Chu Lingxiao was the Founding Ancestor of Lingxiao Pavilion, while Ling Taixu was also the honored teacher who had instructed his father. These two could be considered Yang Kai's true elders in every sense. Even though his cultivation now reached the heavens, Yang Kai had never dared place himself above them, much less let them salute him.

If he really did such a thing, would Fourth Master Yang not break both his legs?

Chu Lingxiao smiled and said, "You are Lingxiao Sect's Sect Master now. There is no need to be so ceremonious anymore."

Ling Taixu also said, "Indeed. All of us owe our achievements today to your protection. The master of a Sect should carry himself like the master of a Sect."

Yang Kai said, "Even the master of a Sect cannot meet his elders without showing respect. Both of you are this Junior's elders, so this is only proper."

Chu Lingxiao and Ling Taixu looked at each other, and both saw the gratification in the other's eyes.

"Big Brother Yang..." Wu Yi walked forward, lightly pressing her red lips together.

Yang Kai looked at her and smiled. "It has been many years. Have you been well?" When he had first established himself on Dark Star, he had relied heavily on Wu Yi's help. It could be said that Wu Yi and those family disciples had been his first helpers on Dark Star. Without their assistance, there would be no Lingxiao Sect today.

"Everything is well!" Wu Yi answered with a smile.

She had only missed him somewhat. Reunited after such a long separation, her long-still heart rippled once more, but when she glanced at the person beside him, that faint ripple soon calmed. She could no longer keep pace with him. He stood too high, and his light was too dazzling.

After greeting everyone one by one, Yang Kai finally turned to Ye Xijun and grinned. "Great Elder, you have worked hard!"

Ye Xijun said, "You came back at the perfect time. Lingxiao Sect is yours again. I am going to retire and enjoy my old age."

Yang Kai said in shock, "Great Elder is in the prime of youth, radiant and brilliant. How could you speak so dispiritedly?"

Ye Xijun snorted. "Flattery is useless. My mind is made up, so say no more." Whatever his cultivation was now, she did not want to do this anymore. Managing a Sect was far too exhausting and nerve-racking. How could it compare to wandering free and unfettered on her own?

Yang Kai sighed. "Since Great Elder says so, I will not force you. In any case, after some time, I will be taking all of you to another place. Once we arrive, Great Elder can rest as long as you like."

"Another place?"

"What place?"

"The place you came back from?"

Everyone grew curious.

Yang Kai only smiled without answering. Instead, he said, "Just now, I heard you arguing about something. What is it? Has some blind fool dared to offend my Lingxiao Sect?"

As soon as he said this, everyone suddenly fell silent, and the excitement from before vanished.

Yang Kai read their expressions and immediately knew something must have happened on Lingxiao Sect's side.

Ye Xijun said, "Let us talk inside."

Everyone entered the hall again one after another. After they took their seats, Ye Xijun explained the current situation in the Star Domain to Yang Kai.

Only then did Yang Kai learn that the problem was not Lingxiao Sect, but the entire Hengluo Star Domain!

Twenty years ago, when Su Yan left the Star Domain and entered the Ancestral Domain, the Star Domain had still been safe and sound. In the years that followed, however, strange Void Passages suddenly began appearing throughout the Star Domain. One Warship after another soon sailed out from those Void Passages, packed with powerful cultivators who invaded Hengluo Star Domain, rampaging and plundering across its various Cultivation Stars.

At first, no one knew what had happened. They only knew that this group of foreign invaders was extremely powerful. Hengluo Star Domain had been sealed off for countless years. When had it ever encountered such a scene? Caught unprepared, several Cultivation Stars fell.

For a time, panic spread throughout the Star Domain, and everyone feared for themselves. After the initial period of adjustment, the great forces of the Star Domain also began to resist. Led by the four top forces of Lingxiao Sect, Hengluo Commerce Guild, Purple Star, and Sword Alliance, they gathered experts from all sides and fought the foreign enemies constantly in the Starry Sky. Countless people fell because of it, countless experts were reduced to ashes, and even Lingxiao Sect lost many people. Their losses could only be called disastrous.

Hearing this, Yang Kai's heart gave a thump.

He had already noticed that although there were many familiar faces in the hall, the women closest to him were not here, and he had not even seen his parents.

As if she had guessed his worry, Ye Xijun said, "The Madams are all safe and sound for now. It is only that each of them has returned to her own star to gather forces and strengthen the defenses."

Only then did Yang Kai relax.

Shan Qingluo should have returned to Yao Star Di Chen, Little Senior Sister had probably gone to Tongxuan Continent, and as for Xue Yue, needless to say, she had to be on Hengluo Commerce Guild's main star, Shuiyue Star. With such a major incident in the Star Domain, they certainly could not remain in Lingxiao Sect the whole time and ignore everything.

Moreover, when he left back then, he had left many Space Arrays in the Star Domain. Whether it was Yao Star Di Chen, Tongxuan Continent, or Shuiyue Star, all of them could connect to Lingxiao Sect, requiring at most a few transfers. If they truly encountered danger, they would also have a way out, so there was no need to worry too much about their safety.

"Where do those people come from?" Yang Kai asked in a deep voice.

When the tiger was away, the monkeys had actually dared to rebel.

Ye Xijun said, "It is said they come from a place called Great Wilderness Star Domain."

Yang Kai immediately looked strange. "Great Wilderness Star Domain? Are you certain they come from Great Wilderness Star Domain?"

Ye Xijun nodded. "Absolutely certain." After more than ten years of warfare, if they did not even know where their opponents came from, that would simply be an enormous joke. When they first found out, they had also been greatly shocked. They had never imagined that another Star Domain existed in this world, much less that it could actually connect to Hengluo Star Domain.

If not for the sudden invasion by the experts of Great Wilderness Star Domain, stirring up such turmoil, the people of Hengluo Star Domain would probably still be in the dark.

"Sect Master knows Great Wilderness Star Domain?" Ye Xijun looked at Yang Kai suspiciously.

Yang Kai chuckled. "I do. Of course I do."

This really was a coincidence.

## Chapter 2684 — I Want It

With three beats of the gong and drum, the auction officially began.

From backstage, a beautiful woman in palace attire slowly walked out and stood atop the round platform. Her enchanting gaze swept over the crowd, causing many men’s eyes to light up.

Normally, auctions were hosted by men, most of whom were elderly. It was rare for a woman to appear in such a setting, but this beautiful woman was certainly a feast for the eyes, making many secretly look forward to this auction even more.

"Sister Hua?"

In the topmost private room, Yang Kai’s eyes widened in astonishment as he stared at the beautiful woman who had just appeared, calling out softly.

"Lady Hua..." Ye Hen and the others also wore complicated expressions.

Naturally, they all knew Hua Qingsi. They had lived in the Thousand Leaves Sect for a time, and it was only after Wu Ming came looking for Hua Qingsi that the Thousand Leaves Sect suffered its calamity. Previously, Ye Hen and the others had suspected that this matter might be related to Hua Qingsi.

Now, with Hua Qingsi suddenly appearing before them as the auction host, it was hard not to let their thoughts run wild.

A breeze stirred beside her, and another figure appeared on the auction stage. When everyone looked closely, they realized Yang Kai had somehow made his way up there.

"Sister Hua!" Yang Kai greeted her with a beaming smile as he stood before her.

"Yang Kai..." Hua Qingsi was momentarily stunned, but then delight flashed in her beautiful eyes. Although she had known Yang Kai was here, seeing him appear before her like this was still unexpected.

"Long time no see. How have you been?" Yang Kai grinned, baring his teeth.

Hua Qingsi gave a bitter smile and did not answer, her expression suddenly turning solemn. She said, "You shouldn’t have come here."

Yang Kai replied, "If I didn’t come, how could I take you all away?"

Hua Qingsi said, "But even if you came, you won’t be able to leave."

"So this really is all aimed at me?" Yang Kai’s face darkened.

Hua Qingsi sighed and said, "Do you still remember the Dragon Isle Token?"

Yang Kai frowned in thought, then suddenly realized, "Of course I remember."

In truth, he had long forgotten about it. If not for Hua Qingsi’s reminder, he would never have recalled that this thing was still in his Spatial Ring.

Hua Qingsi gave a bitter smile. "Back then, I left the Star God Palace on my master’s orders to go to Fenglin City and investigate the whereabouts of the Dragon Isle Token. You know about this. The Dragon Isle Token is extremely important. With it, one can go to Dragon Isle and make a single request that Dragon Isle must fulfill."

Yang Kai nodded slightly, and distant memories gradually became clear.

That Dragon Isle Token was something he had found in the Spatial Ring of a man named Han Leng, whom he had killed.

It was said that Han Leng was a traitor of the Star God Palace and, like Hua Qingsi, was a fellow disciple. Back then, Han Leng had somehow stolen the Dragon Isle Token and fled the Star God Palace. After he died at Yang Kai’s hands, the Dragon Isle Token naturally changed owners.

Later, when Hua Qingsi investigated the Dragon Isle Token, she traced it to Yang Kai and ended up being captured herself.

The function of the Dragon Isle Token was also something Hua Qingsi had told Yang Kai at that time.

"The Dragon Isle Token belonged to my master. A year ago, my Senior Brother Wu Ming found me and brought me back to the Star God Palace. Although I didn’t want to reveal the truth, my master planted a secret technique on me, leaving me no choice. You know what happened after that. I don’t know exactly what I told my master, but he definitely knows the Dragon Isle Token is with you. That’s why he targeted the Thousand Leaves Sect and captured Chi Yue and the others—to force you to show yourself."

Yang Kai frowned and said, "So your master might know my other secrets as well?"

Hua Qingsi’s expression dimmed. "I’m sorry."

Yang Kai smiled. "It wasn’t your intention, so it’s fine."

Although he had never doubted Hua Qingsi’s character, if he had truly been betrayed by her, Yang Kai would have found it hard to let go. Now, after hearing her explanation, he felt inexplicably relieved.

"Are my friends still safe?" Yang Kai asked in a low voice.

Hua Qingsi replied, “My master hasn’t harmed them. After all, his true target is you. Until he has you in his grasp, those people are nothing more than pawns on the board.”

Wasn’t she the same? So many years of master-disciple affection were nothing in the face of absolute interests.

Yang Kai glanced at the spot where Hua Qingsi had suddenly appeared earlier and said, "That Space Array..."

"Don’t be impulsive. They’ve all been implanted with Heart-Controlling Gu. Even if you go to them, you won’t be able to save them." Hua Qingsi slowly shook her head. "With just a thought, my master can make them wish they were dead."

"Heart-Controlling Gu!" Yang Kai’s eyes narrowed. With the Strange Insects Manual in hand, he naturally knew what the Heart-Controlling Gu was. It was a rare and deadly insect, ranked among the strange insects, and although not as famous as the Soul-Devouring Worms, it was still a lethal tool for taking lives.

As they spoke, Hua Qingsi suddenly let out a muffled groan, her beautiful face contorted in pain. Beneath her fair skin, something seemed to be writhing, moving under the surface.

"Sister Hua, you too..." Yang Kai’s expression changed slightly.

Hua Qingsi endured the pain and said, "You should go. My master is always cautious. He wouldn’t act against you unless he was absolutely certain."

Yang Kai’s gaze turned cold as he looked in one direction, only to see Wu Ming grinning at him while forming a mysterious hand seal.

It seemed that Hua Qingsi’s pain was precisely because he was activating the Heart-Controlling Gu.

"Sister Hua, I’ll save you!" Yang Kai said urgently, then made a throat-slitting gesture at Wu Ming before suddenly vanishing.

If he stayed any longer, Hua Qingsi would only suffer more. Retreating now was the best choice.

The intimate exchange between the two atop the high platform had long been noticed by the many cultivators present. Each of them clicked their tongues in wonder, unsure of what relationship Yang Kai and Hua Qingsi really had.

After Yang Kai left, Wu Ming indeed stopped tormenting Hua Qingsi. After all, she was still needed to host the auction, and it would be unseemly to go too far.

The Heart-Controlling Gu wriggling beneath Hua Qingsi’s skin gradually disappeared, and the unbearable pain slowly subsided. However, Hua Qingsi was already drenched in fragrant sweat. Forcing herself to stay composed, she lifted her head and said expressionlessly, "The auction will now begin. The first item is the Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppet, Tianlang. The starting price is one million Upper-Level Source Crystals!"

As soon as she spoke, the crowd erupted in commotion.

Although everyone had known that many Heavenly Puppets from the Thousand Leaves Sect would appear in this auction, they hadn’t expected the very first item to be one.

This was unusual. Normally, the value of auction items increased from low to high to stir up competition and get people caught up in the bidding frenzy, unconsciously raising the price.

A Heavenly Puppet was valuable enough to be the grand finale, yet it was brought out first—this was truly against convention.

But remembering that the Thousand Leaves Sect had more than one Heavenly Puppet, many cultivators felt reassured, thinking this first one was just to whet appetites.

One million Upper-Level Source Crystals was no small sum. That was equivalent to one hundred million Middle-Level Source Crystals or ten billion Lower-Level Source Crystals. Ordinary cultivators simply couldn’t afford to participate.

But compared to a Heavenly Puppet with strength rivaling the Emperor Realm, one million Upper-Level Source Crystals was a bargain. Buying it was like buying an Emperor Realm expert who would obey your every command.

Many Emperor Realm experts had come to this auction, so they were already prepared.

Upon hearing the starting price, everyone was eager to fight for it.

"One million, I want it! I hope everyone will give me some face!"

A loud voice suddenly rang out, almost as soon as Hua Qingsi finished speaking.

Everyone who heard this just curled their lips, feeling a bit disdainful.

Who did this guy think he was, that he could claim a Heavenly Puppet at the starting price with just a single sentence? If his reputation really carried that much weight, what was the point of holding an auction at all?

Looking toward the source of the voice, many people’s expressions changed.

Because they saw that the speaker was none other than the arrogant, domineering, and utterly unruly Yang Kai.

The words of those about to bid instantly died in their throats.

When Yang Kai first arrived in Flowing Shadow City, he had killed quite a few disciples of the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect. Yet, even now, the sect master, Li Qingyun, hadn’t sought revenge. In fact, not long ago, he had even taken the initiative to return the Thousand Leaves Sect’s sect master.

That alone was impressive enough, but earlier, Yang Kai had beaten up Wu Ming right at the auction entrance. That kind of courage and audacity was enough to make people think twice, not to mention the rumors that he was accompanied by a Yao King.

That kind of reputation... really couldn’t be ignored.

The entire auction hall fell into a strange silence.

Those who knew how fierce Yang Kai was didn’t want to make an enemy of him lightly, and even those who didn’t know the details sensed something was off and didn’t dare to stand out.

So, after Yang Kai spoke, no one else placed a bid.

Hua Qingsi glanced around and simply announced, "Since that’s the case, the first Heavenly Puppet goes to the friend in Room One."

Upon hearing this, everyone was dumbfounded. They thought this auctioneer was truly odd. Normally, the auctioneer would try to drive up the price, ask if anyone else wanted to bid, and even count down three times before finalizing the sale. But this young woman didn’t even ask—she just made the deal with a single word.

But recalling the earlier intimacy between Yang Kai and her, everyone secretly thought they must know each other, which explained her decisiveness.

What was this?

Was this even an auction anymore?

The Emperor Realm experts present had all attended auctions before, but this was the first time they’d seen one like this.

From the moment Hua Qingsi spoke to the end of the bidding, only five breaths had passed. Only then did a maid appear from the back, carrying a tray. She hadn’t even had time to display the Heavenly Puppet to the crowd before the auction was over.

The maid seemed a bit dazed as well, but still carried the tray straight toward Room One.

When she reached the door, it opened by itself. The maid stood at the entrance and said, "Honored guest, here is your auction item. Please pay one million Upper-Level Source Crystals."

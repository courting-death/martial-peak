## Chapter 3024 — Big Brother Li

"Lu Qiu agreed to the matter, and this Li did not intend to delay, so we planned to hold the wedding one month later. Unfortunately, the plans of men are no match for the plans of Heaven. Half a month later, Lu Qiu sent word that Sanniang had disappeared. This Li was naturally furious and went to Ruyi Sect to throw his weight around, injuring quite a few people. Later, however, it was confirmed that Sanniang really had vanished for no apparent reason and that Ruyi Sect was not responsible. For the next several years, Li Dragon Palace and Ruyi Sect searched for Sanniang, but we never found any clues."

Li Jiao let out a bitter laugh. "There was no trace of her alive and no corpse if she was dead. The whole affair was strange from beginning to end. Back then, everyone in the Northern Territory knew about it, and this Li lost a great deal of face."

He was the dignified Palace Master of Li Dragon Palace, a Third Stage Emperor Realm Master, yet even taking a concubine had gone awry. Naturally, others had laughed at him. Yang Kai could almost imagine how Li Jiao had felt at the time, so it was no wonder he had stormed into Ruyi Sect in a rage to throw his weight around.

Had Yang Kai encountered such a thing himself, he certainly would not have been able to hold back either.

"Until today..." Li Jiao finished speaking and turned to look at Lu Sanniang. After a moment of silence, he sighed again.

The woman with whom he had once shared mutual affection now actually had a daughter. Li Jiao could not help feeling stifled. Although more than three hundred years had passed, some things could not be erased by time.

He did not know how Lu Sanniang had disappeared back then.

Nor did he know how Lu Sanniang had come to be in this Small World.

Still less did he know whose child the young girl was.

Earlier, when he heard Lu Sanniang's voice in the teahouse, he had merely thought it sounded somewhat familiar. It was only when the sinister man kicked Lu Sanniang to the ground that Li Jiao saw her face clearly, whereupon he furiously intervened.

At the time, he had thought only of standing up for Lu Sanniang, but now his emotions were extremely complicated. It would not be an exaggeration to say that all five flavors mingled in his heart.

The woman who had once been betrothed to him had disappeared for three hundred years, only to have a child with another man. No man could preserve his dignity in such a situation.

He would only speak of this to Yang Kai. Had anyone else been present, Li Jiao certainly would not have revealed any of it.

In any case, this was not the first time he had lost face in front of Yang Kai. He was already used to it.

"Big Brother Li..."

A soft call came from nearby. Lu Sanniang had finally stopped sobbing and called out while wiping the corners of her eyes.

Yang Kai let out a muffled grunt and nearly burst out laughing.

Big Brother Li...

He simply could not believe that there was actually someone in this world who addressed Li Jiao this way, and that she was such a peerless beauty at that.

Li Jiao was truly blessed in love! However, this was clearly no time to laugh, so Yang Kai hurriedly raised his teacup to his lips and took a sip.

Li Jiao's spirits lifted, however. Turning to look at Lu Sanniang, his emotions surged, but he still sneered, "Done pretending?"

Lu Sanniang did not raise her head and softly said, "Sanniang was too ashamed to face you, so she did not dare acknowledge you."

"So you know you should be ashamed to face me!" Li Jiao coldly snorted, feeling terribly aggrieved. He had wanted to mock her and vent the anger accumulated in his heart over all these years, but when he saw her miserable appearance, he swallowed the words on the tip of his tongue, and his expression could not help becoming somewhat twisted.

Lu Sanniang vigorously wiped the corners of her eyes again, raised her head, and forced a smile. "Sanniang truly never imagined I would be able to see Big Brother Li again in this life."

Seeing her like this, Li Jiao's heart softened, and he became even less willing to reproach her.

"The person Sanniang has wronged most in this life is Big Brother Li. If you wish to hit me, curse me, or even kill me, Sanniang will have no complaints. Only..." As she spoke, she turned to look at her daughter, her eyes filled with love and reluctance to part.

Hearing this, the young girl paled in alarm. All the affection and gratitude she had felt toward Li Jiao vanished in an instant. Spreading her arms in front of Lu Sanniang, she cried out, "Don't bully my mother!"

Li Jiao was so angry he laughed. Slapping the table, he demanded fiercely, "When did I bully her?"

The little girl jumped in fright and blinked her eyes. Bean-sized tears suddenly rolled down her cheeks, and her body trembled, but she still kept her arms spread wide with all her strength.

"Why is this girl exactly like you?" Li Jiao was somewhat speechless. He knew his tone had been too harsh just now, but he was too embarrassed to apologize, leaving him looking extremely uncomfortable.

Lu Sanniang reached out and pulled her daughter's arms back, saying gently, "Qin'er, don't make trouble. Uncle Li won't bully Mother."

The young girl remained doubtful, but after Li Jiao had roared at her just now, she no longer dared challenge him.

Lu Sanniang said, "Big Brother Li, you should hurry and leave. After you beat that man, he will not let the matter rest. If you do not go, trouble will certainly come looking for you."

Li Jiao sneered. "That piece of trash? If he dares continue pestering us, this Lord will certainly not spare him."

He was merely putting on a brave front to preserve his dignity; in truth, he still felt somewhat uneasy. Half-Dragon City was far too strange. Had he not unexpectedly encountered Lu Sanniang, nothing could have persuaded him to step into the limelight like this.

Lu Sanniang shook her head and said, "Big Brother Li, I know your cultivation is profound, but in this place, no matter how strong you are, it will be of no avail. The opponent you face is no ordinary foe."

Li Jiao had already been somewhat nervous, but the moment Lu Sanniang said this, he immediately snorted. "Then this Lord would like to see what sort of helper he can bring."

Lu Sanniang was about to say something more, but Yang Kai suddenly smiled and said, “Sanniang, you don’t need to worry about our affairs. What I’m really curious about is why you suddenly disappeared all those years ago.”

He knew this was what Li Jiao most wanted to understand. Putting himself in Li Jiao's place, Yang Kai could see that this matter was plainly a knot in his heart, one that had troubled him for more than three hundred years.

It was just that Li Jiao seemed unwilling to ask, or perhaps did not dare to ask. He was probably worrying about what he might gain or lose, so Yang Kai simply asked for him.

Hearing this, Li Jiao's expression shifted. He raised his teacup and took a sip. Although he forced himself to appear calm, the hand holding the cup trembled slightly.

Lu Sanniang clearly had not expected Yang Kai to suddenly ask this question. A trace of shame appeared on her face as she lowered her head. "It happened so many years ago that I can no longer remember it clearly."

This answer was clearly not enough to satisfy Li Jiao. No longer caring to keep up appearances, he immediately darkened his face. "So your feelings for this Lord back then were nothing but false affection? Good, very good!"

His face was filled with grief and indignation. No man could remain calm after encountering such a thing. Recalling the tenderness they had shared back then, Li Jiao felt unbearably aggrieved and wished he could slap this shameless slut to death right now!

"How could that be?" Lu Sanniang abruptly raised her head and shook it vigorously as tears streamed down her face again. "Sanniang's feelings for Big Brother Li were not false. Sanniang wanted to marry Big Brother Li."

Her appearance softened Li Jiao's heart once more. The ferocity on his face receded somewhat as he said in a deep voice, "This Lord needs an explanation for what happened back then."

Lu Sanniang continued shaking her head. The anger Li Jiao had only just suppressed flared up again, and he stood up abruptly. "Fine. If you will not speak, this Lord will not force you. We can simply pretend we never knew each other. Palace Master Yang, let us go."

Although what happened back then had been a regret, Li Jiao had endured it for so many years. At this point, he could naturally afford to let go. His cultivation was considerable, after all, so he possessed at least this much mental fortitude.

He had only just turned around when Lu Sanniang grabbed his sleeve.

"What else do you want?" Li Jiao looked down at her, his face cold.

Lu Sanniang sobbed for a while. Only after a long moment did she softly say, "Back then, Sanniang... was captured."

Li Jiao's face twitched, but he said nothing. He merely stood there without moving and made no further mention of leaving.

"After our wedding date was set, Sanniang waited for you in Ruyi Sect. Later, I missed you so much that I secretly slipped out alone, intending to go to Li Dragon Palace to find you. But unexpectedly... unexpectedly..."

At this point, she could no longer continue. She must have recalled what happened back then, for she choked so badly that she could not even speak.

"Mother..." Seeing this, the young girl began crying as well. She raised a hand to wipe her mother's tears, and for a time, grief overwhelmed both mother and daughter as they wept inconsolably.

Li Jiao sighed. "Enough, stop crying. There is no need to mention what happened back then. I went too far."

Lu Sanniang shook her head, barely brought her emotions under control, and continued, "Back then, Sanniang was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, while that man's cultivation was profound. Sanniang was no match for him and was captured. Later..."

There was no need to say what happened afterward. When a beauty capable of toppling kingdoms like Lu Sanniang was captured, how could any good fate await her?

Li Jiao's expression turned bitterly cold as he asked in a deep voice, "Who was it?"

Lu Sanniang did not answer and merely continued, "Later, he brought me to Dragon Isle."

"Dragon Isle?"

Li Jiao and Yang Kai were both startled. Looking at her together, they asked, "This place really is Dragon Isle?"

Lu Sanniang looked up at them, somewhat curious as to how they could already be here without even knowing where they were. She nodded. "Yes, this is Dragon Isle."

Yang Kai and Li Jiao exchanged a glance, and both their expressions sank.

Although Lu Sanniang had not said who captured her, the answer was already obvious. Other than a member of the Dragon Clan, who could freely enter and leave Dragon Isle?

Li Jiao clenched his fists, his hands trembling violently.

No wonder Lu Sanniang had been unwilling to speak. So it was the Dragon Clan's doing. Had he not repeatedly forced her, Lu Sanniang certainly would not have revealed the truth!

Lu Sanniang's disappearance back then was actually connected to the Dragon Clan. Yang Kai frowned, secretly feeling that the matter was rather difficult to handle.

Li Jiao felt the same. Had it been anyone else, even another Third Stage Emperor Realm Master, he would have risked his life to avenge Lu Sanniang and seek justice for himself. But the Dragon Clan... He smiled bitterly to himself. The Dragon Clan was not something he could afford to provoke.

The suppression from their bloodline alone left him unable to summon any thought of resistance.

He dropped heavily back into his seat, his face ashen.

His woman had been stolen and subjected to endless humiliation, while his own dignity had suffered, yet in the end, he did not even have the ability to avenge her or wash away his shame. A profound sense of powerlessness rose in Li Jiao's heart, leaving his chest unbearably stifled.

"Then why are you here?" Yang Kai asked, frowning at her.

Dragons were lustful by nature, and Lu Sanniang was so beautiful that it made sense for a member of the Dragon Clan to take a fancy to her and abduct her. But why had she appeared in Half-Dragon City? That was what Yang Kai could not understand. He sympathized with Li Jiao and felt that the Dragon Clan member who had stolen Lu Sanniang was truly a despicable bastard.

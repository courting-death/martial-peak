## Chapter 2964 — Sacred Tree

These guards had stood watch over this place all year round, and they themselves were not permitted to approach within one hundred zhang of the Sacred Tree. Over all these years, apart from the Imperial Family, they had never seen anyone come here.

Yet today, they had seen two at once, and the Human Sovereign himself had brought them over. Naturally, it was impossible for them not to care.

Ahead lay a dense mass of mist, seemingly able to isolate the probing of Divine Sense and obstruct vision, preventing anyone from seeing what the situation inside was like.

The Human Sovereign stopped and pointed ahead. "The Sacred Tree is over there. If my guests wish to see it, you may enter and take a look."

Yang Kai stared at the churning mist, smiled faintly, then turned to Zhu Qing and said, "Wait for me a moment. I will be out soon."

"Be careful!" Zhu Qing cautioned solemnly. After all, this was someone else's domain. Although neither she nor Yang Kai were soft targets, it was always better to be on guard. It was easy to dodge an open spear, but hard to guard against a hidden arrow.

"Do not worry!" Yang Kai smiled faintly and stepped inside. He had only taken two steps when his figure suddenly disappeared into the mist, vanishing from sight.

Zhu Qing's dark brows furrowed slightly. Although she knew this was the effect of a Spirit Array, she could not help becoming somewhat vigilant.

"Your Excellency is a Divine Spirit of the Dragon Clan?" the Human Sovereign suddenly asked.

Zhu Qing's beautiful eyes stared at the mist ahead, as if she had not heard him speak at all.

The Human Sovereign did not mind and simply smiled faintly. "There are no Divine Spirits in the Revolving World, but there are many records of them in ancient texts. Rumor has it that the Dragon Clan is the head of all living beings and is extremely proud. How could Your Excellency mingle with an ordinary mortal? That only throws away the face of the Dragon Clan for nothing. Moreover, from what this Sovereign has observed, Your Excellency's relationship with that man... seems rather unusual."

Zhu Qing suddenly turned to the Human Sovereign and looked at him solemnly. “Outside this place, someone like you would not even be qualified to speak to me.”

A trace of gloom flashed through the Human Sovereign's eyes, while the many princes and princesses were also filled with anger. No one had ever dared speak to the Human Sovereign like this. It was simply the height of disrespect.

"So you had best watch your words and actions. Since you know of the Dragon Clan, you should understand the Dragon Clan's temper." After Zhu Qing finished speaking, she turned her head again and gazed into the depths of the mist.

The Human Sovereign remained silent for a long while before chuckling. "The Dragon Clan is proud indeed. Its reputation is not undeserved."

He suddenly curled his hand slightly, and a burst of emerald light shot out from the mist ahead, transforming into a rope that directly bound Zhu Qing.

"Although the Dragon Clan is strong, before this Sovereign, it is still somewhat lacking."

Zhu Qing's expression became extremely cold. She lowered her head to examine the bindings wrapped around her body, then raised her head and said, "I hope you know what you are doing."

The Human Sovereign sneered. "This Sovereign naturally knows what he is doing. Human Sovereign City has stood for more than a hundred thousand years. It is my Imperial Family that has protected the human race's survival and reproduction, allowing the human race to gain a foothold in this world. This Sovereign will not allow any accident to occur. That friend of yours... is the greatest accident, and accidents must be erased."

"So you brought us here to make your move easier?" Zhu Qing asked coldly.

The Human Sovereign sighed. "The method is indeed somewhat despicable, but this Sovereign has no choice but to consider the human race. I hope Your Excellency can understand. Only here can my Imperial Family display all of its strength. Still, you need not worry. With someone accompanying you on the Yellow Springs Road, I imagine you will not be too lonely."

Zhu Qing said nothing more. Instead, a dragon's cry erupted from her, and with a spin of her body, she suddenly transformed into a scarlet dragon thirty zhang in length. Dragon’s might filled the air, and the eight directions trembled.

The emerald rope binding her was not broken. It seemed extraordinarily resilient and could freely change its size. Even though Zhu Qing had revealed her Divine Spirit true form, it still held her tightly bound, its emerald radiance standing out vividly against her crimson dragon body.

The many princes and princesses all changed countenance, then each displayed Sacred Arts and bombarded the giant dragon. Their attacks struck those hard scales, sending sparks flying.

The guards in the open and in hiding also all moved out together. In total, there were almost sixteen Emperor Realm experts, and the two leading them were even at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. The moment they acted, they had the momentum to overturn rivers and seas.

Fighting against so many foes at once, even with Zhu Qing's formidable Divine Arts, she was instantly engulfed in a barrage of attacks, her dragon cries shaking the heavens.

Among the princes and princesses, the Second Princess attacked especially ruthlessly, as if she wished to repay Yang Kai twice over for the humiliation she had suffered earlier.

The little black dog charged out from the side without fear of death, taking advantage of Fu Yu's lack of preparation to bite down on her thigh. It bared its teeth, shook its head, and looked very much as if it intended to gnaw a mouthful of flesh from her leg.

"Scram!" Fu Yu flew into a rage and slammed down a palm fiercely. With her powerful cultivation as a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert, coupled with the augmentation of the Sacred Tree's power, the might of this strike was extremely terrifying.

The little black dog, however, was extremely nimble. It actually dodged this blow with a flash of its body, biting off the clothes on Fu Yu's leg and exposing a large expanse of snow-white skin.

Fu Yu was beside herself with rage. No longer bothering with Zhu Qing, she turned her full attention to killing the little black dog.

The Human Sovereign stood there with an icy expression and had not acted again after his initial move. In his view, although the Dragon Clan was strong, gathering the strength of all the Imperial Family members and the guards here was already enough to kill that red dragon.

What he needed to deal with now was that fellow who could display Sacred Arts. As he turned his head, the Human Sovereign's gaze became cold enough to pierce bone.

He had never truly considered allowing Yang Kai to observe the Sacred Tree. Leading him here had merely been to make dealing with him easier, because the closer he was to the Sacred Tree, the stronger the power he could display.

Within the mist, east, south, west, and north could not be distinguished.

Yet Yang Kai could follow that omnipresent sensation in the unseen and walk forward. He could sense the location of the Sacred Tree, a feeling that was both familiar and intimate, as though he had experienced something similar before.

The closer he drew, the more obvious this feeling became.

Finally, after crossing layer upon layer of obstruction, Yang Kai arrived before a towering tree. The dense mist seemed to suddenly disperse, exposing everything around him.

This was an extremely majestic and enormous ancient tree. Its branches and leaves were dense, spreading out like a canopy. When he raised his head to look up, its branches and leaves covered the entire sky, and its enormous crown seemed like an infinitely broad chest, able to provide strong and powerful protection and shield everything from wind and rain.

Yang Kai's pupils suddenly shrank to the size of needlepoints. He stared blankly at this towering ancient tree as countless thoughts flew through his mind. The sounds of war and battle rang beside his ears, and scenes of blood and flesh flying appeared before his eyes.

His lips trembled lightly as he reached out and touched the tree trunk.

An incomparably familiar feeling immediately wrapped around him.

Yang Kai laughed despite himself. "How could it be you? Senior Qing..."

In that moment of dazed realization, he felt as if he had returned once more to that ancient world, to the first time he had arrived at Frost Snow City and seen Qing. The shock to his soul was still vivid before his eyes, as clear as if it had happened yesterday.

Back then, in Frost Snow City, the Royal City of the Frost Snow Tribe, the Divine Tree had stood upon the earth just like this, protecting the entire city from natural disasters and man-made calamities.

On the final battlefield of the war between humans and demons, it had relied on its strength alone to support the human race's final hope.

This familiar aura and the countless tree holes in the trunk were exactly the same as the Divine Tree from back then. One could say they were identical.

This was definitely the Divine Tree. Yang Kai believed he could not be mistaken.

There might well be other trees in this world just as grand and immense, but this familiar aura could never be mistaken. After all, he had lived within this tree’s hollow for a very long time.

Yang Kai had never imagined that the so-called Sacred Tree in Human Sovereign City was actually Qing!

Should Qing not have already died? In order to seal the passage between the two worlds, Qing had sacrificed himself for righteousness, erupting with all the essence he had accumulated throughout his life. He had grown vigorously from the earth, reaching straight into the Demon Domain, and with his own sacrifice had blocked the entrance from the Demon Domain to the Star Realm.

In that battle, millions of soldiers had fought to the death on the wilderness, sacrificing themselves without regard for their own lives. In that battle, countless experts had died body and soul, and corpses had piled up like mountains. That battle was enough to be recorded in the annals of history and leave its name for all eternity.

Die had once said that everything experienced in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape bore traces of reality.

Therefore, the two years Yang Kai had spent in the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape had been an illusion based on some period of ancient history. In other words, during some era of antiquity, there truly had been such an earth-shaking war. It was just that in the real world, that battle had not had Yang Kai, but there had been countless existences similar to Yang Kai, and only then had the Barbarian Tribe obtained the final victory.

Qing undoubtedly existed. He was the greatest contributor to the Barbarian Tribe's victory and the key to turning the tide of battle. This point would not change, whether in illusion or in reality.

History from more than a hundred thousand years ago and the present day had an astonishing point of intersection in their paths, leaving Yang Kai somewhat stunned.

But he soon discovered that something was wrong, because within this Sacred Tree there was only a hazy, chaotic will, like a newborn infant, without thoughts of its own.

When he came into contact with the Sacred Tree, this will clearly also perceived Yang Kai's existence and, without the slightest defense, opened its heart and accepted him.

In an instant, an indescribable resonance formed between Yang Kai and the Sacred Tree before him, as if they had become connected to each other.

Yang Kai's expression changed slightly in surprise, because from within the Sacred Tree, he sensed energy as vast as the sea. The immensity of that energy was enough to compare with any Great Emperor, and perhaps even surpass one.

The Imperial Family of Human Sovereign City could display Sacred Arts and form killing moves far beyond their true level precisely by mobilizing this energy.

And if he was willing at this moment, he could also release this surging power. The Sacred Tree did not resist him in the slightest; instead, it accepted his existence with great intimacy.

Yang Kai's brow suddenly furrowed. He sensed that something disharmonious had appeared within the Sacred Tree—a factor that felt like a festering sore growing upon one's body, deeply uncomfortable and unsettling.

"Get out here!" Yang Kai shouted fiercely. One of his hands suddenly reached into the Sacred Tree's interior and violently grabbed hold of something.

With another exertion of strength, a figure stumbled as it was dragged out from within the Sacred Tree.

This person was astonishingly the Human Sovereign, from whom he had separated not long ago. His face was filled with horror, as if he had never expected to encounter such a thing, and he was somewhat dazed. Only after he met Yang Kai's icy, cold, and ruthless gaze did he abruptly come back to his senses. He formed a hand seal, chanted an incantation, and pointed toward Yang Kai.

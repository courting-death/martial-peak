## Chapter 3098 — Old Ancestor

What puzzled He Yunxiang, however, was Yan Luo's claim that he wanted to travel to the Ancestral Domain with Yang Kai. If the Yan Family truly had some scheme in mind, why would Yan Luo keep himself at Yang Kai's side?

“We’ll see how things go.” Even if the Yan Family had plotted against him, how could Yang Kai possibly fear them?

More importantly, since he had unsealed a little of his cultivation today, he needed to seal it again as soon as possible. He had already sensed a faint, inexplicable hostility from the world around him.

Ten days passed in the blink of an eye.

Early in the morning, crowds gathered below Twin Peaks. Almost every notable figure in the Yan Family had assembled here. The square was packed shoulder to shoulder, and a pitch-black Warship lay moored there.

News that the Family Head was going to the Ancestral Domain had already spread on its own. To the Yan Family, this was no small matter. The last time someone from the family had gone to the Ancestral Domain was five hundred years ago, and no one even knew whether that person had succeeded. This time, the Family Head had suddenly announced that he would step down from his position, follow in the ancestor's footsteps, and seek a deeper Martial Dao and a broader world. When the news spread, the entire Yan Family was shaken.

Several thousand people had gathered, all of them here to see him off.

As for what had happened ten days ago, only a small number of Yan Family members knew the truth. They knew that the Family Head and Great Elder had utterly lost face that day, and that even the Second Elder had died an unnatural death. Those people were all pillars of the Yan Family, so naturally they would not spread the matter around. As a result, the Yan Family members who came here knew almost nothing about the conflict from ten days ago, and when Yan Luo walked out, they all looked toward him with reverence.

If they knew that this Family Head of theirs had been beaten until he begged for mercy just a few days earlier, who knew what they would think.

As for Yang Kai and He Yunxiang, who walked at the very front, many Yan Family disciples were suspicious and confused, but none knew the two's origins or identities. They only felt that the attitude the Family Head and Elders showed toward them was rather intriguing.

Before long, everyone boarded the Warship. Amid a great hum and intense energy fluctuations, the Warship slowly set sail, soon rushing into the vast Starry Sky.

Only after the Warship disappeared from view did the Yan Family members reluctantly disperse.

The former Great Elder, now the Lord of the Yan Family, Yan An, gave the crowd a few instructions, then hurried back.

Striding into the secret room, he immediately saw a conspicuous lantern. The lantern clearly looked old, and no one knew when it had been placed here, yet it was spotless.

There was nothing else in the entire secret room, only this lantern and a table. An incense burner sat on the table.

Yan An's expression was extremely solemn, filled with a pilgrimage-like piety. From his Spatial Ring, he took out a stick of incense. The incense also seemed to have been specially made. It was as thick as a thumb and about one chi long. No one knew what material had been refined to make it, but even before it was lit, it gave off a strange fragrance.

Yan An held the incense and recalled Yan Luo's instructions from a few days ago. A trace of determination flashed through his eyes. Bringing two fingers together, he drew them across his wrist, and a wound immediately opened there, blood trickling out.

He placed the incense against the wound, and the blood immediately seeped into it, gradually staining it crimson.

Only after a full half an hour did the stick of incense completely change color. It was like a thick leech that had drunk its fill of blood, blood-red from end to end and unspeakably bewitching.

Yan An's face had gone somewhat pale, but his spirit grew even more excited. He formed spirit seals with his hands, Saint Yuan surging around him, then pointed two fingers at the incense and shouted in a low voice, "Ignite!"

A point of light bloomed from the incense stick, as if it had begun to burn, but the fragrance drifting out from it was crimson.

Yan An did not dare be negligent. Holding the incense respectfully, he bowed several times toward the lantern before inserting it into the incense burner on the table before him.

For a time, all was still. Even Yan An's breathing was almost imperceptible. The incense burned, and the red smoke it released seemed to have received a summons, surging toward the lantern without leaving the slightest trace behind. As time passed, the ancient lantern also seemed to show a subtle reaction.

When half the incense had burned, a horrifying pressure suddenly descended from above. At the same time, two points of light lit up within the lantern, like a person's eyes.

Yan An trembled violently, knelt on the ground, and said respectfully, "Yan An greets Old Ancestor!"

The two points of light inside the lantern flickered a few more times before slowly stabilizing. An aged voice suddenly sounded from within the lantern. "Yan An? It has been several hundred years since we last met. Is the Yan Family now under your charge?"

From the meaning of those words, the owner of the aged voice clearly knew Yan An.

"Replying to Old Ancestor, I am indeed the Family Head of the Yan Family now."

"Where is Yan Luo? This old man remembers that when I left back then, Yan Luo was the one who presided over the Yan Family, was he not? It has only been a few hundred years. Do not tell me he is dead."

Yan An hurriedly said, "He is not dead, not dead. He has already stepped down as Family Head and set out for the Ancestral Domain."

"He has come to the Ancestral Domain? Rare for him to be willing to take this step. Truly worthy of celebration." The aged voice carried a note of joy, but then his tone changed, turning majestic. "However, did you summon this old man here merely to tell me this?"

If it was only this, then this was rather like making a mountain out of a molehill. When he left the Wuji Star Domain back then, he had instructed them that unless it was an important matter, they absolutely must not disturb him lightly.

Yan An bowed again and said in grief, "Old Ancestor, the Yan Family has suffered extraordinary shame and humiliation. We beg Old Ancestor to uphold justice for us."

The aura inside the lantern changed, suddenly brimming with killing intent. The aged voice said coldly, "Tell me in detail!"

...

Time slipped by, and in the blink of an eye, a month had passed.

The Yan Family's Warship had traveled this route with extreme ease. The huge Yan character on the Warship was the best pass one could have. No one dared set their sights on the Yan Family's Warship, and the few star pirates who saw it from afar only fled at the mere sight of it.

During this month, Yang Kai still lived leisurely and at ease, reading through many ancient records and historical texts and greatly broadening his knowledge.

Yan Luo came every day to pay his respects, offering immortal delicacies, fine food, jade nectar, and precious wine. Yang Kai refused none of it, though he did send back the several women arranged to serve him.

One month later, He Yunxiang knocked on the door and entered.

Yang Kai saw that her aura was steady and her complexion radiant, and knew her realm had already stabilized.

"We have arrived?"

He Yunxiang replied, "Yan Luo says we are almost there."

"Let us go take a look." Yang Kai was also quite curious about the method and route for entering the Ancestral Domain. Although the Hengluo Star Domain did not necessarily have a similar place, stones from other hills could polish jade. Observing this might perhaps yield some gains.

The two soon arrived in the Warship's control cabin. A group of Yan Family disciples guarded the operating Spirit Arrays, their expressions solemn but excited.

They all knew the destination of this trip, and secretly felt grateful that Yan Luo had chosen them to accompany him. This way, they would have the chance to get one step ahead of others and witness the Ancestral Domain's splendor.

Seeing Yang Kai appear, the group hurriedly saluted.

Yan Luo came over and said, "Sir, that star up ahead contains the passage into the Ancestral Domain."

Yang Kai looked in the indicated direction and immediately said in surprise, "A Dead Star?"

It was a dim, lightless sphere. Even viewed from the Starry Sky, it looked utterly desolate. Such Dead Stars were countless within the Star Domain, and probably no one could imagine that this one was actually the key to reaching the Ancestral Domain.

Yan Luo smiled slightly and said, "If not, how could the method of reaching the Ancestral Domain be so rarely known?"

There was no Spiritual Qi of heaven and earth on a Dead Star, so ordinary people would never investigate one at all. Even if they passed by, they would quickly leave. No one would imagine that a path into the Ancestral Domain was hidden within.

"Your Yan Family's luck is rather good, to have discovered this."

"It was indeed luck." Yan Luo chuckled and did not explain further. The Yan Family's discovery of this place had indeed been a coincidence, and it had to be traced back many generations. Even he was not clear how it had first been discovered and confirmed. By the time it was passed down to his generation, a great deal of information had already been lost.

"Sir, please sit for a moment. If this trip goes smoothly, we will soon reach the Ancestral Domain." Yan Luo gestured with his hand.

Yang Kai nodded and calmly took a seat to the side.

The Warship slowly sped toward the Dead Star, chose a direction, and descended. Soon, it entered a huge canyon. The canyon was deep and secluded, like a bottomless pit. When Divine Sense probed downward, it was so deep the bottom could not be seen.

Under Yan Luo's command, the Warship continued descending, and soon daylight could no longer be seen.

It was as if they were sailing through cracks in the underworld. A cold and gloomy aura actually seeped in through the Warship, making everyone shudder.

In such a harsh environment, even a Void King Realm expert going in alone would probably be unable to endure for long. Only by relying on the Warship's resistance could they reduce the drain on their strength.

After an unknown length of time, Yang Kai's expression suddenly stirred, and his Divine Sense spread out like a tide.

In the deepest reaches of this canyon, he had actually sensed intense fluctuations of Space Force.

With a brief probe, he immediately knew his guess was correct. Below them, there was actually a huge spatial crack. Within it, streams of light overflowed with color, dazzling to the extreme and completely different from the Void Passages he had encountered before.

Yan Luo's expression also turned extremely solemn in an instant. He personally operated several Spirit Arrays and activated the Warship's defenses.

Without a sound, the enormous Warship sailed into the crack. It seemed to be swallowed, yet also seemed to melt, and soon vanished from sight.

A series of cracking sounds came from all directions, and the expression of everyone inside the Warship changed.

He Yunxiang looked at Yan Luo in astonishment. Seeing his expressionless face, she could not help feeling a chill in her heart.

As expected, those who accomplished great things did not fuss over minor sacrifices.

The other members of the Yan Family were probably unaware, but now that she had reached the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, how could she fail to notice that this Warship simply could not hold out for long? Under the pressure of the terrifying forces all around them, she feared that before the time it took for a stick of incense to burn, this Warship would fall apart.

When that time came, other than the three Void King Realm cultivators among them, who inside this Warship could escape disaster? Even He Yunxiang herself had no confidence she could retreat unscathed. Now, she could only place her hopes on Yang Kai.

Yan Luo had definitely known in advance that the Yan Family members would die. Yet he had still brought two or three hundred Yan Family members with him and driven this Warship in here. What he sought was probably nothing more than to use this Warship to buy that one incense stick's worth of safety.

It was laughable that this group from the Yan Family had spent the previous month full of expectation, wanting to witness the scenery of the Ancestral Domain, unaware that they had long since become chess pieces in Yan Luo's hand, and abandoned pieces that could be discarded at will.

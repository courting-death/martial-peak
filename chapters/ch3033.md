## Chapter 3033 — In Full Swing

Yuan Wu's face was livid. After the middle-aged man helped him to his feet, he coughed violently for a while before saying hoarsely, "I'm fine!"

He cast a venomous glance toward Yang Kai, only to meet Yang Kai's gaze. He hurriedly looked away. After what had just happened, he no longer dared challenge Yang Kai and could only bury this hatred in his heart, waiting for a chance to settle the score in the future.

Only then did the middle-aged man breathe a sigh of relief and say with a fawning smile, "It's good that you're all right, Sir, truly good. We still need you to take charge of constructing the palace. If anything were to happen to you, what would become of us? We wouldn't have anyone to lead us."

His relentless flattery made many people glance sideways at him, all wondering how this fellow could be so shameless.

Even so, Yuan Wu's expression improved considerably, and he felt that he had regained some face.

The middle-aged man continued, "Sir, if there truly is no suitable candidate, why not leave the mining of Ice Soul Snow Jade to me? What do you think?"

"Hm?" Yuan Wu was taken aback. Turning to look at him, he seriously sized the man up for the first time.

Everyone else also looked astonished, their expressions strange.

They all knew perfectly well how dangerous mining Ice Soul Snow Jade was. That was why no one had volunteered to take on the job just now. Instead, they had all rushed to propose taking other tasks, afraid that Yuan Wu would assign this one to them.

The middle-aged man was, after all, at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, so everyone believed he understood this as well.

Then what was he doing? He knew it was dangerous, yet he had actually volunteered? Was there something wrong with his head? Even if he wanted to flatter Yuan Wu, there was no need to gamble with his own life. For a time, everyone looked at the middle-aged man as though he were a fool.

The middle-aged man seemed not to notice and smiled faintly. "Of course, this is merely my suggestion. The final decision is still yours to make, Sir."

As soon as he said this, Yuan Wu's opinion of the middle-aged man improved greatly. Yang Kai had made him lose face, rejected the very first task he assigned, and ruthlessly humiliated him. This was also an enormous blow to his prestige and could very well affect his standing among these five hundred people.

Yet at this very moment, someone had actually stepped forward to support him. How could Yuan Wu not feel grateful? Moreover, this fellow spoke with great tact and showed him ample respect.

Yuan Wu gave him a deep look and asked, "What is your name?"

He did not recognize the man and had no recollection of seeing such a fellow in Half-Dragon City. He could not help finding this strange, since such a peculiar appearance should have left a deep impression on anyone.

The middle-aged man chuckled. "This lowly one is Wu Chen!"

"Good, very good!" Yuan Wu reached out and patted Wu Chen on the shoulder. It was easy to add flowers to brocade, but difficult to deliver charcoal in the snow. Wu Chen had undoubtedly offered him help when he needed it most. Although the two had never met and were complete strangers, from this moment onward, Wu Chen's standing in Yuan Wu's heart far surpassed everyone else's. Yuan Wu nodded. "This Lord will leave the mining of Ice Soul Snow Jade in your charge. Will thirty men be enough?"

Wu Chen was startled and looked surprised, but then became overjoyed. "Many thanks, Sir."

When the others heard this, they looked at Wu Chen with immense envy and secretly regretted their inaction. Had they known this would happen, why would they have stood by and watched the show? They would have rushed forward to express their concern long ago. Yet Wu Chen had earned Yuan Wu's favor with only a few words and could now be considered a minor leader among the five hundred. Yuan Wu had even appointed him to oversee the mining of Ice Soul Snow Jade. It was simply a pie falling from the heavens.

Yuan Wu narrowed his eyes. "Since this matter has been entrusted to you, choose whoever you need yourself. Tell this Lord once you have made your selections." At this point, his gaze slowly swept over the crowd and he said indifferently, "If anyone dares make excuses or refuse, tell this Lord as well. This Lord will deal with them."

"Yes, yes! Many thanks for your favor, Sir!" Wu Chen was beaming with delight, clearly beside himself with joy.

With Yuan Wu backing him, he could naturally act without restraint. After all, not everyone possessed Yang Kai's courage and strength or dared challenge Yuan Wu as he had. Even the few cultivators present who were at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm lacked such confidence.

With Wu Chen setting the precedent, how could the other Emperor Realm Masters hold back? They all surged forward and surrounded Yuan Wu, showering him with compliments and fawning smiles.

They had realized that constructing the palace was far too vast and complicated a task for Yuan Wu to handle every matter personally. If they were to finish it within a month, he would inevitably appoint a few minor leaders to oversee other matters. Naturally, this was the time to put on a good performance.

In the blink of an eye, Yuan Wu once again savored the feeling of being the moon surrounded by stars. His earlier humiliation and resentment immediately vanished. He cast a cold glance toward Yang Kai, wondering whether he should order these people to kill him first.

However, the thought merely passed through Yuan Wu's mind before he abandoned it.

It was not that he feared Yang Kai. He simply felt that raising the matter now would make him seem too impatient for quick results.

Go ahead and feel pleased with yourself for now. There is still a whole month ahead. Once I find an opportunity, do you think I won't be able to kill you?

Yuan Wu sneered inwardly and stopped thinking about Yang Kai, instead beginning to assign tasks directly.

After his earlier inspection, he had gained a basic understanding of what needed to be done over the next month. The most urgent matter was naturally to send people out to mine the materials needed to construct the palace. Otherwise, if they waited until the materials in the ring were exhausted before mining more, it would likely be too late.

He called out more than two hundred people in succession and divided them into seven or eight teams, assigning each team to mine a different material.

Yuan Wu was careful, however. Apart from Wu Chen, he did not appoint anyone else to take charge. He was grateful that Wu Chen had helped him recover his dignity, so he returned the favor. The others had done nothing for him just now.

You want this Lord to value you? First show this Lord where you stand!

Each type of material could be found on the islands of the Dragon Palace, scattered across different Spirit Islands. Only after Yuan Wu finished assigning the tasks and pointed out the directions did those two hundred-odd people reluctantly fly off into the night.

Time was pressing, and there was no opportunity to rest. Thus, although everyone had some complaints, there was nothing they could do.

The number of people present immediately fell by nearly half.

Over the next stretch of time, Yuan Wu began assigning different tasks to those who remained. Although the Dragon Clan had designated a site on the island, they had merely marked out a general area. These five hundred people still had to handle everything else.

Constructing a palace was no simple matter and involved all manner of work. Fortunately, the remaining three hundred people were not useless, and many had some experience in this regard. Yuan Wu gathered them for a brief discussion, then issued a variety of orders.

Wave after wave of people flew out, each receiving a different task and beginning preparations across the island.

In an instant, the entire island bustled with activity. Even though it was night, the sounds of labor rang out without pause.

By the time dawn approached, basically everyone had been assigned a task. Only Yang Kai, Lu Sanniang, and her daughter remained standing where they were.

Yuan Wu clearly had no intention of provoking Yang Kai again, and even Lu Sanniang and her daughter were ignored along with him.

He stared coldly at Yang Kai's back, snorted, then turned and left.

As soon as he was gone, Lu Sanniang looked at Yang Kai and asked helplessly, "Brother Yang, what should we do now?"

This turn of events was somewhat unexpected. She had thought that she and her daughter would likely be unable to escape Yuan Wu’s vicious hands and had already mentally prepared herself to resist, only to find that when the moment came, there was simply nothing for her to do.

That they could remain so idle was all thanks to Yang Kai.

Only now did she understand why Li Jiao had left with complete peace of mind, without even giving her any instructions before his departure.

Li Jiao clearly knew what Yang Kai was capable of, so there had been no need for him to say anything.

"Elder Sister Lu, let me ask you something." Yang Kai turned to look at her.

"Ask me anything you wish." Lu Sanniang looked at Yang Kai somewhat reservedly. She no longer dared regard him as an equal. Earlier, she had been able to act relaxed and natural in front of him because they were both at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. After witnessing Yang Kai's strength, however, she immediately understood that his First Stage of the Emperor Realm cultivation was on a completely different level from hers.

The strong were respected; this was an eternal truth. Lu Sanniang naturally felt some awe toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai seemed not to notice. After pondering for a moment, he asked, "You have lived in the Dragon Palace for a long time. Have you ever heard of a member of the Dragon Clan named Zhu Qing?"

"A member of the Zhu lineage of the Dragon Clan?" Lu Sanniang was startled.

"That's right!" Yang Kai nodded lightly.

"I haven't heard of her." Lu Sanniang shook her head. "You may laugh at me for saying this, but although I lived here for two or three hundred years, I never once left the place where I resided. As a result, I don't know much about affairs within the Dragon Palace."

"I see..." Yang Kai was somewhat disappointed.

He had thought that since Lu Sanniang had lived here for so long, she would know at least some of the Dragon Clan's inner affairs and could, at the very least, point him in the right direction. Who could have expected that she had effectively been under house arrest throughout her two or three hundred years here? It seemed he could not count on her help now.

Lu Sanniang looked uncertain and surprised. "Brother Yang, do you know someone from the Dragon Clan?"

Yang Kai thought to himself that he did far more than simply know them; his connection to them was quite deep. Naturally, he could not say that aloud, so he said, “I need to go out for a while. Big Sister Lu, will you and your daughter come with me?”

Lu Sanniang jumped in fright and asked in alarm, "Go out? Where?"

"Just to look around!"

"Absolutely not!" Lu Sanniang's expression changed at once. She looked around and, after confirming no one was nearby, lowered her voice. "Brother Yang, I know your strength is extraordinary, but this is the Dragon Palace, the home of the Dragon Clan. If you wander around and the Dragon Clan discovers you..."

Yang Kai smiled. "Many people have already gone out. Even if the Dragon Clan does discover us, it won't matter."

Just now, seven or eight teams comprising more than two hundred people had flown off in different directions, all heading to separate Spirit Islands to mine the materials needed to construct the palace. They would provide Yang Kai with cover.

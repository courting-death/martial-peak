## Chapter 2723 — Imperial-Grade Pill Master Ji Ying

As soon as Hua Qingsi spoke, she suddenly realized what she was saying. She looked at Yang Kai in horror and said, "Palace Master, you can't possibly be thinking..."

To suddenly want to kill someone—combined with what had just happened—made it obvious who the target was.

She couldn't help but be shocked by Yang Kai's audacity.

That person was a disciple of Medicine Pill Valley! How could he even consider silencing him? If he really did such a thing, Medicine Pill Valley would never let it go.

"What nonsense are you talking about..." Yang Kai flatly denied it. "Nothing of the sort. Yao'er, your imagination is really something."

"Then why don't you dare look me in the eye when you say it!" Ji Yao snorted coldly.

"Look at you? Fine, I'll look at you! Am I supposed to be afraid of you?" Yang Kai turned his head, his eyes wide like eggs, glaring fiercely at Ji Yao.

The beautiful eyes before him were clear and flawless, as pure as a spring, his own reflection mirrored within. Her long lashes fluttered like dancing sprites, making her seem even more pure and ethereal.

“Hmph!” Ji Yao sneered. “You think staring at me like that makes you look innocent? Don’t think I can’t tell what’s on your mind.”

What’s on my mind is how you looked in Fengluo Palace—stripped bare, slick all over!

"That kid is clearly no pushover. On the surface, he acted all submissive after you beat him, but deep down, he already hates you. He's young and inexperienced, thinking he can fool us with a little act. But you and I both know he's full of schemes, ready to run back to Medicine Pill Valley and report you. You want to silence him so there are no witnesses, right?"

Yang Kai's face darkened. "Given the situation, do you have a better solution?"

This time, he didn't deny it. He just found it strange—was he really that obvious? How did Ji Yao see through him at a glance?

"You can't just kill him!" Ji Yao frowned. "If you really do, that's when the real trouble starts. Honestly, you shouldn't have beaten him in the first place. Now that the mess is made, it's too late to ignore it. As the Palace Master, how can you act so recklessly!"

"I shouldn't have beaten him?" Yang Kai jumped up like a cat whose tail had been stepped on. "Where did that little brat come from, acting so brazen in my Lingxiao Palace, laying hands on my people and mouthing off? You say I shouldn't have beaten him? I wanted to kill him right then! Letting him leave Lingxiao Palace alive was already a mercy!"

“Who are you calling your people?” Zhu Qing flushed.

“Anyway, that’s the end of it. Why make such a fuss?” Yang Kai waved his hand impatiently. “If he’d known his place and let the matter drop, that would’ve been one thing. But instead he’s plotting revenge behind my back—how could I possibly tolerate that?”

"Flying into a rage for the sake of a beauty!" Ji Yao teased, her words sounding like praise but her eyes full of mockery. "Is that just how you men are?"

"This has nothing to do with any so-called beauty!" Yang Kai said seriously, his voice steady. "It's a matter of principle!"

"I don't blame you for hitting him... Even I wanted to hit him just now." Ji Yao pursed her lips, her tone softening a bit. "But you shouldn't try to silence him."

"I haven't done anything yet. Didn't you stop me?" Yang Kai pouted.

"If you had, it would be over. Even if you were in the right, you'd end up in the wrong." Ji Yao sighed. "I think you've let your anger cloud your judgment. That's why you want to do something so foolish."

"Whatever you say, so be it," Yang Kai replied, putting on a look of utter indifference, like a dead pig unafraid of boiling water. He stiffened his neck and let Ji Yao lecture him as much as she pleased.

Ji Yao was both angry and amused. "You've been roaming the Star Realm for years now. Don't you understand the mindset of these young, ambitious types? He doesn't represent Medicine Pill Valley—he's just a second-generation disciple."

"Second-generation disciple?" Yang Kai raised an eyebrow.

"Yes." Ji Yao nodded, explaining, "Medicine Pill Valley is the place where Great Emperor Miaodan cultivates in seclusion. Only his personal disciples are first-generation, and they're all Imperial-Grade Pill Masters, deeply skilled in alchemy. Great Emperor Miaodan hasn't taken new disciples in ages. The disciples his personal disciples accept are the second generation. That Li Xuan is one of them."

"So... it's really not that big a deal?" Yang Kai stroked his chin, deep in thought. What he feared was only Great Emperor Miaodan. If things were as Ji Yao said, then it really wasn't a big deal.

That Li Xuan was a whole generation removed from Great Emperor Miaodan. The only backing he had was one of the Great Emperor's personal disciples.

Imperial-Grade Pill Master, huh... Yang Kai was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master too. Who was afraid of whom?

Besides, no matter how you looked at it, Li Xuan had brought this humiliation on himself, relying too much on his Medicine Pill Valley status and acting arrogantly. If his master had any sense, he wouldn't blame Yang Kai or Lingxiao Palace.

Of course, it was too much to expect gratitude. If his master was petty, he might still come looking for trouble.

"So, are you still planning to silence him?" Ji Yao asked.

Yang Kai laughed and waved his hand. "What are you saying? Silencing someone—how could you even say something so scary? As Palace Master, I wouldn't harm even an ant, let alone a moth drawn to a lamp. How could I do such a thing?"

The three women all rolled their eyes in unison.

"Even if there's no need to worry too much, we still can't be careless. I'll head back to Ice Heart Valley and help you gather some information. Li Xuan's Senior Brother is at my Ice Heart Valley right now." Ji Yao thought for a moment.

"That works." Yang Kai nodded.

Ji Yao turned and left.

Yang Kai tilted his head to the side and said, "You've been hiding for so long—what are you up to? Get out here already."

The air over there suddenly rippled, like a stone tossed into a calm lake. As the ripples spread, Nanmen Dajun's figure appeared.

"Palace Master, your might is unmatched! Nanmen bows in admiration!" Nanmen Dajun walked over with a fawning smile.

"Enough with the flattery. What is it?" Yang Kai glanced at him sideways.

This guy had been hiding here the whole time—who knew what he was up to? But Yang Kai didn't care. Now that Nanmen Dajun was Lingxiao Palace's Сhief Formation Master, there was no way he'd be a threat.

Nanmen Dajun immediately said, "This subordinate has prepared some good things for the Palace Master."

"What good things?"

Nanmen Dajun chuckled mysteriously. "Please take a look, Palace Master!"

...

"Kill them, kill them, kill them..."

The multi-deck ship sped through the sky. On the deck, Li Xuan's face was twisted with hatred as he muttered curses under his breath.

What happened today was a humiliation beyond compare. As a disciple of Medicine Pill Valley, he held a noble status and was destined to become an Imperial-Grade Pill Master. How could he endure such disgrace? Just as Ji Yao had said, he acted submissive on the surface after being beaten, but inside he was plotting revenge.

He didn't stop until he reached a deserted mountain valley, where he brought the multi-deck ship down.

Putting away his Secret Treasure, his face twisted with malice. Feeling the burning pain on his cheek, he slammed his palm into a rock, sending shards flying. Amid the swirling dust, Li Xuan vented all his anger and frustration, unleashing his power recklessly.

Only after a long while did he collapse to the ground, panting. He took out some Healing Pills from his Spatial Ring and swallowed them, his eyes turning with sinister intent. He muttered coldly, "You're all going to die!"

With that, he took out a Transmission Compass, poured his Divine Sense into it, and sent a message.

Billions of li away from this valley, at the main headquarters of Li Dragon Palace, one of the top sects in the Northern Territory, there was a place overflowing with Spiritual Qi.

The area covered only a dozen or so mu, but even within Li Dragon Palace, it was a forbidden ground. Aside from Palace Master Li Jiao, only a few elders were allowed to enter.

This was because it was a precious Spiritual Medicine garden.

There weren't many Spiritual Medicines cultivated in the garden—about a hundred or so—but every single one was Imperial-Grade, extremely rare and precious. Outside, such things were almost impossible to find. The entire garden was filled with Spiritual Qi and the fragrance of medicine. Any ordinary cultivator who came here would find their cultivation soaring with just a single breath.

At this moment, in a pavilion beside the medicine garden, three people sat around a table.

On the left was a man in a purple robe, dignified and noble, with a broad mouth and nose and wild, bristling hair. He was none other than Li Jiao, Palace Master of Li Dragon Palace.

On the right was another man, equally imposing. He looked about forty, though his true age was unknown. His expression was naturally authoritative. He was Mi Qi, Sect Master of Mitian Sect, another top sect in the Northern Territory!

It wasn't strange for the two of them to be together. Ever since Yang Kai destroyed Wenqing Sect, these two great sects had grown close. As Bing Yun had said, they were wary of Yang Kai's Lingxiao Palace and naturally wanted to band together for support.

Seated in the center was a middle-aged man. His cultivation was only at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, a level lower than Li Jiao and Mi Qi. Yet from their attitudes and words, it was clear that both Li Jiao and Mi Qi treated him with great respect—even a hint of flattery.

There were few in this world who could make two Third Stage Emperors show such deference, but a personal disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan had that qualification.

This man was the last disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, the fifth-ranked among his disciples—Imperial-Grade Pill Master Ji Ying.

Every ten years, Medicine Pill Valley collected medicines, and it was impossible to entrust such a task to a disciple like Li Xuan. This time, Ji Ying was supposed to lead the team and visit each great sect. But after arriving at Li Dragon Palace to collect their decade's worth of Spiritual Medicine, something came up and delayed him. Helpless, he had to send his two disciples to Ice Heart Valley and Lingxiao Palace instead.

He also knew that his disciple Li Xuan had a bit of a roving eye, which was precisely why he sent his other disciple to Ice Heart Valley—lest this rascal get distracted by beauties and let it affect his cultivation.

Still, being a womanizer wasn't a big problem. Young men were full of energy, and after years of studying alchemy in Medicine Pill Valley, it was normal to be curious about women.

In his eyes, both of his disciples were excellent prospects. With a little guidance, even if they couldn't match him in the future, they wouldn't fall far behind. They usually behaved well, so he was fairly confident about this trip.

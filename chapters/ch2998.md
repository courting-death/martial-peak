## Chapter 2998 — Leave It to Me

"Red hair?" Hua Xing was somewhat bewildered by his question. He looked at him doubtfully and shook his head. "No, not red hair. That little girl has black hair."

Although he did not know why Li Jiao had asked this, he could vaguely guess that Li Jiao was wary of something.

There was actually someone he feared? Hua Xing found this strange.

"Black hair..." Li Jiao immediately breathed a sigh of relief. If she had black hair, then she was definitely not Zhu Qing. Still, to guard against any chance of error, he carefully asked for confirmation, "What is that little girl's cultivation?"

"First-Stage Emperor Realm!"

Li Jiao was startled. "Only the First-Stage Emperor Realm?"

Then it was definitely not Zhu Qing. Li Jiao finally relaxed completely.

"Exactly, which is why this Hua feels so humiliated!" Hua Xing fetched another pot of wine and drank from it in great gulps. Wiping his mouth, he complained, "Brother Li, you know my circumstances. After striving for so many years, I had only one son. I had hoped he would inherit my position as Pavilion Master, but who could have expected... who could have expected..." At this point, his voice actually choked with emotion.

Li Jiao consoled him, "For a white-haired parent to bury his black-haired child, Brother Hua must be suffering terribly. Please restrain your grief and accept this loss."

Hua Xing slammed the table, murderous intent surging from him. "If I do not avenge this hatred, this Hua swears he is not a man!"

Li Jiao said, "Such a blood feud naturally must be avenged." After hesitating for a moment, he asked, "There is just one thing this Li does not quite understand. Wind and Cloud Pavilion is an extraordinary force even in the Western Territory, and Brother Hua possesses cultivation at the Third-Stage Emperor Realm. Could it be that you truly have no way to deal with that little girl?"

Hua Xing shook his head with a bitter smile. "What you do not know is that although the little girl's cultivation is not high, her strength is astonishing. Some days ago, this Hua underestimated her and dispatched several experts from the Pavilion to capture her, but unexpectedly, she killed them all. One of them was even at the Second-Stage Emperor Realm."

"Oh? She can actually fight above her Stage?" Li Jiao raised his brow. "It seems this little girl is indeed a troublesome character."

He thought of Yang Kai again and said solemnly, "There are not many people capable of fighting above their Stage. If she does not die prematurely, she is bound to accomplish great things in the future."

Hua Xing said, "My Wind and Cloud Pavilion has lost all face because of this matter recently. Brother Li, please assist me!"

Li Jiao said, "Brother Hua, there is no need for such formality. Although that little girl is indeed powerful enough to kill a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master, if Brother Hua personally took action, surely she would be unable to stir up any waves. I know Brother Hua's strength. Although this Li would like to help, it would be far more satisfying for you to personally avenge the murder of your son."

Hearing this, Hua Xing smiled bitterly. "How could this Hua not understand that? Ever since my son was killed, I have neither eaten nor slept in peace, wishing I could tear out that wretched slut's tendons and flay her skin. If I could avenge him personally, I naturally would not show mercy. It is just..."

"Just what?" Li Jiao looked at him curiously, unable to understand why Hua Xing had come to ask him for help.

"Brother Li will understand once you take a look." As Hua Xing spoke, he held out his wrist.

With a puzzled look, Li Jiao extended two fingers and placed them on Hua Xing's wrist. A moment later, his expression changed, and he exclaimed, "Brother Hua is injured? Who did this?"

Hua Xing said dejectedly, "When news of my son's death arrived, this Hua was cultivating in retreat. The shock nearly caused me to suffer Qi Deviation. Although I managed to restore the chaotic power in my body to order, I inevitably suffered hidden injuries. Otherwise, this Hua would have taken action personally long ago. How could I have allowed that wretched slut to continue running wild?"

"So that is how it is!" Li Jiao nodded slightly, a trace of sympathy appearing on his face.

Hua Xing truly did not have it easy. He had only one son, so it was indeed difficult to accept his sudden death.

"That wretched slut was able to kill a Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master, so her strength cannot be weak. This Hua is currently in poor condition. If I rashly take action and succeed, all will be well, but if I fail, the situation will become truly embarrassing."

"Brother Hua is absolutely right." Li Jiao pondered for a moment, then said, "I understand Brother Hua's meaning. Rest assured, leave this matter to this Lord. I will teach that little girl what is good for her."

Hua Xing was overjoyed when he heard this. Rising to his feet, he bowed deeply. "Many thanks, Brother Li. With Brother Li personally taking action, no matter how capable that wretched slut may be, she will not be able to cause any trouble."

Li Jiao raised a hand to stop his bow and smiled. "This Li has imposed upon you for some time now. Since Brother Hua is in difficulty, this Li naturally cannot stand idly by. Mm, but where is that little girl now?"

Hua Xing said solemnly, "To tell Brother Li the truth, that wretched slut is currently in Changyun City, fifty li outside Wind and Cloud Pavilion."

"She actually dares to fight her way to your doorstep?" Li Jiao's expression turned fierce, and he snorted. "She certainly has no shortage of courage."

Hua Xing gritted his teeth. "Exactly. Relying on what little strength she has, that wretched slut simply does not take my Wind and Cloud Pavilion seriously. I will have to trouble Brother Li this time."

Li Jiao said, "Since she is nearby, why not let her enter Wind and Cloud Pavilion before this Lord takes action? I will make certain she comes but never leaves."

Hua Xing smiled bitterly. "If she truly succeeds in fighting her way into Wind and Cloud Pavilion and charges into the Grand Guardian Array, then..."

Li Jiao's thoughts turned, and he immediately understood Hua Xing's concern. Nodding, he said, "Indeed, that would be inappropriate. This Li did not consider the matter thoroughly. How about this? This Lord will make a trip to Changyun City and simply capture that girl."

"That is precisely what this Hua hoped for. I will have to trouble Brother Li."

"There is no time to lose. This Lord will set out now." As he spoke, Li Jiao rose and walked outside.

"I will await Brother Li's good news!" Hua Xing cupped his fists and saw him off.

Atop the mountain peak, Li Jiao soared into the sky and flew into the distance. Only after his figure disappeared did the smile Hua Xing had maintained gradually fade. He stood in place with a frown and thought for a while, then coldly snorted and strode away.

As far as he was concerned, persuading Li Jiao to act was enough.

He had never managed to discover the origins of that man and woman, but since they were so openly fighting their way toward Wind and Cloud Pavilion, they clearly had something to rely on. If those two truly possessed powerful backgrounds, then Li Jiao would be the one who offended them, and the matter would have nothing to do with Wind and Cloud Pavilion.

He only needed to wait quietly for news.

In Changyun City, the air was thick with the stench of blood. Broken walls and ruined buildings lay everywhere, while crimson bloodstains, chunks of flesh, and severed limbs covered the ground.

Changyun City was only fifty li from Wind and Cloud Pavilion. It was also an important stronghold through which Wind and Cloud Pavilion interacted with the outside world. In other words, the city was one of Wind and Cloud Pavilion's properties.

Yang Kai and Lin Yun'er had travelled here with a clear objective, plainly intending to fight their way straight into Wind and Cloud Pavilion. How could Wind and Cloud Pavilion have made no arrangements in Changyun City?

A Second-Stage Emperor Realm expert led several hundred disciples from the Pavilion, accompanied by numerous Emperor Realm experts drawn by the bounty. They had long since arranged a formidable Grand Array and quietly waited for the pair to walk into their trap.

Thus, the moment the two stepped into Changyun City, they were recognized, and a great battle erupted instantly.

In just one short hour, nearly half of Changyun City was destroyed, and Wind and Cloud Pavilion's several hundred disciples suffered disastrous casualties. Even the Second-Stage Emperor Realm Master leading them was blown apart by a single punch from Lin Yun'er.

Once the Grand Array was broken, the survivors scattered like frightened birds and beasts. The surviving disciples of Wind and Cloud Pavilion hurriedly fled back to their headquarters, while the Emperor Realm Masters drawn by the bounty were either dead or fleeing. The whole of Changyun City was gripped by panic.

On the road, Li Jiao came roaring through the air and blocked the path of a group of bloodstained Wind and Cloud Pavilion disciples.

"What happened?" Li Jiao released his Imperial Might, leaving the group trembling with fear and too terrified to make any reckless movements.

"Are you... Lord Li?" The First-Stage Emperor Realm Master at their head recognized Li Jiao and looked at him with great joy.

"Who are you?" Li Jiao glanced sideways at the man.

The Emperor Realm Master hurriedly cupped his fists. "This one is Cai Shi, Ninth Elder of Wind and Cloud Pavilion. Greetings, Lord Li."

"You recognize this Lord?"

Cai Shi hurriedly replied, "When Sir visited our Pavilion some days ago, this Cai had the honour of admiring Sir's bearing from afar."

"Oh, so it is you." Li Jiao had some impression of him. He did seem to have seen this man when he visited Wind and Cloud Pavilion some days ago. Frowning, he asked in a deep voice, "Why are all of you in such a wretched state?"

Cai Shi’s face reddened with shame. “I am ashamed to say that the enemy was too powerful. We were no match, and many of the Pavilion’s experts were killed or wounded.”

"That little girl is so formidable?" Li Jiao raised his brow.

Cai Shi said, "Even Second Elder died miserably at her hands, and the Nine Nether Chain Array we arranged was broken in less than an hour."

Li Jiao chuckled. "It seems Wind and Cloud Pavilion has provoked a truly extraordinary character this time."

Cai Shi said, "I fear only the Pavilion Master can subdue her."

Li Jiao raised his hand. "There is no need to ask your Pavilion Master to act. He has already entrusted this matter to me. This Lord will deal with that girl."

Cai Shi's eyes lit up when he heard this, and he exclaimed in delight, "Lord Li is going to act?"

"Why else would this Lord have come here?" Li Jiao replied proudly.

“That is wonderful! With Lord Li taking action, this matter will soon be settled.”

Li Jiao said impatiently, "Come with me. The sooner we settle this, the sooner we can return and report."

"Yes, yes, yes! This way, Lord Li!" Cai Shi responded repeatedly, the fear on his face disappearing. He had previously heard Hua Xing say that Li Jiao possessed a trace of Dragon Clan blood, so although both men were at the Third-Stage Emperor Realm, Hua Xing admitted that he was not Li Jiao's opponent.

With such an expert taking action, matters would be much easier. They had suffered disastrous losses this time, and even Second Elder had been killed. Cai Shi had been worrying about how he would explain himself after returning, but unexpectedly, a saviour like Li Jiao had appeared. How could he not be delighted?

In his opinion, no matter how powerful the man and woman in Changyun City were, they could not possibly defeat Li Jiao. As long as the two were captured and brought back, Cai Shi and the others could presumably make up for their failures and restore some of Wind and Cloud Pavilion's face.

Thus, Cai Shi was exceptionally attentive and took the initiative to lead the way.

Before long, the group returned to Changyun City.

Li Jiao swept the city with his Divine Sense and saw devastation everywhere. He could not help frowning, secretly thinking that these fellows from Wind and Cloud Pavilion were truly useless. A mere little girl had actually left them completely helpless. He really did not know how Hua Xing managed his subordinates.

If this had happened at Li Dragon Palace, that girl would have died countless times over by now.

"Where are they?" Li Jiao could not find any trace of the enemies' auras and asked with a frown.

Cai Shi replied, "They should be in the City Lord's Mansion. Earlier, after killing many people from the Pavilion, they directly broke into the City Lord's Mansion, presumably intending to plunder its wealth."

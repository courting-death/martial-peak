## Chapter 2875 — A Forced Melon

"One last time—do you wish to die, or do you wish to live?" Yang Kai's expression suddenly turned impatient as he issued his ultimatum. "If none of you are willing to submit, I have no qualms about slaughtering you all. There is no shortage of Demon Kings in this world. Besides you, I have far more options to choose from. Moreover, there is no shame in this. Those who refuse me will die; those who submit to me will live. No one will remember the choice you make today, save for yourselves." He held up three fingers, his face cold and stern as he curled one down: "Three!"

After a pause, he continued, "Two!"

All the Demon Kings' expressions tightened, knowing exactly what would happen when this Shaman Niu finished his countdown.

Before he could call out the last number, a Demon King with a terrifying scar on his face stepped forward and said in a low voice, "I am willing... to pledge allegiance to you, my lord!"

He gritted his teeth as he spoke, his expression shifting repeatedly as he wrestled with the decision.

The ghostly flames in Bone Demon Fei Li's eye sockets flickered. When Shaya was the first to surrender to this outsider, he had already foreseen this outcome, so now he didn't bother to blame anyone else.

To die standing or live kneeling—it was all a matter of choice. If some among them were willing to discard their dignity and pride and reveal their submissive side before an outsider, that was none of his concern.

But he knew that most of the Demon Kings present wouldn't be so spineless.

After all, they were Demon Kings!

Just as Fei Li expected, under his and most Demon Kings' cold gazes, only a handful of Demon Kings, faces full of shame and unease, chose to submit, half-kneeling on the ground, not daring to look at their former companions, like a group of children who had done something wrong, awaiting Yang Kai's judgment.

"Five..." Yang Kai smiled slightly, looking at the row of kneeling Demon Kings, then glanced at the eight behind them, smiling again: "It seems you've all made your choices."

Fei Li snorted coldly, his gaze full of contempt as it swept over the five Demon Kings in front, then met Yang Kai's eyes, the ghostly flames in his sockets dancing with mockery.

"The number is just right." Yang Kai grinned.

This left all the Demon Kings a bit confused.

Yang Kai slowly raised his hand, his expression cold: "Then now, please go die!"

"As long as demonic fire burns, the demon race will never perish!" Fei Li shouted.

The other Demon Kings also shouted, wearing expressions of fearless resolve.

With several explosive sounds, the bodies of the Demon Kings burst into blood mist, leaving no bones behind, even the demon hearts hidden within vanished without a trace.

Shaya's smiling face froze, Bao Qi's eyelids twitched violently, and the ghostly flames in Fei Li's eyes burned even brighter, revealing his shock.

The other Demon Kings were all dumbfounded.

Because the ones Yang Kai killed were not the group of unyielding Demon Kings, but rather the five cowards who had shown him their loyalty.

Was it a mistake?

The same thought involuntarily arose in every Demon King’s mind—could it be a mistake? Yet upon further reflection, how could such a thing be possible? If it wasn't an error, then only one explanation remained.

This was a deliberate act by the outsider. The ones he truly wanted to keep were not those cowards, but the remaining unyielding Demon Kings. No wonder he said the number was just right earlier—excluding Bao Qi and Shaya, who had already submitted, the remaining eight were indeed the perfect number!

"What do you mean?" Fei Li asked coldly, staring at Yang Kai.

"Can't you understand what I mean?" Yang Kai chuckled softly.

Fei Li said, "We will never submit. You might as well kill us."

Yang Kai nodded, "I have no doubt about that, and you've proven it with your actions. But as I said, even if you refuse to submit, I have ways to turn you into slaves. What I need are the strong among you—those who bow their heads willingly are of little use!"

These words made Shaya blush a little. Back then, she had surrendered without much resistance. Thinking about it now, she was lucky to be the first to submit to Yang Kai. If she had waited until just now, she probably would have been killed too.

"So just because of this, you humiliated them, mocked them, and then killed them?" Fei Li's emotions suddenly flared. He didn't care about the death of those cowardly Demon Kings, but Yang Kai's actions still made him angry.

"That's right, just because of this." Yang Kai's face was cold and emotionless. "Only in this way could I weed out that trash."

Fei Li wanted to say more, but Yang Kai waved his hand, not giving him the chance, impatiently saying, "Enough, now it's your turn."

As he spoke, he reached out and drew a Demon King in front of him. Suddenly, his left eye shone with golden light, his gaze majestic as he looked at the Demon King.

The Demon King struggled desperately but couldn't break free. Suppressed by the power of this world as Yang Kai's Divine Sense poured into him, the Demon King gradually showed a look of pain, but it soon faded, replaced by an expression of comfort.

When Yang Kai let him go, the Demon King shook his head, still a bit dazed. When he looked up at Yang Kai again, his gaze was complicated but no longer hostile. He bowed respectfully and silently stood beside Bao Qi and Shaya.

"Welcome, Might Demon Yura!" Bao Qi smiled.

The Might Demon called Yura glanced at him but said nothing, leaving Bao Qi a bit embarrassed.

"Hey, hey, what is this? Isn't this a bit much?" Shadow Demon Mo Ke Tuo swallowed hard, no longer bothering to hide herself with secret arts. Seeing a Demon King who wouldn't bow even in the face of death be subdued in an instant, Mo Ke Tuo was shaken to the core, feeling completely ill at ease.

The other Demon Kings who witnessed this scene felt the same.

They were not afraid of death; if they truly feared it, they would not have stood their ground refusing to submit just moments ago. What they feared was becoming someone else's slaves!

Even the Demon Saints had never enslaved them.

If not for the suppression of this world, they would rather self-destruct than live in disgrace. Sadly, they no longer even had the ability to self-destruct.

Another Demon King was pulled in front of the outsider. Everyone could guess what would happen next, leaving the remaining Demon Kings in utter despair.

"My lord, didn't you just say a forced melon isn't sweet? Let us die, why go to all this trouble?" Mo Ke Tuo called out to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai glanced at her and grinned. “A forced melon may not be sweet… but it still quenches thirst.”

Mo Ke Tuo's expression froze, her face twitching slightly as she thought to herself: what kind of nonsense is this?

Just as she was fuming, her body was pulled over by Yang Kai. She sighed softly, knowing she couldn't escape no matter what, and could only stand there helplessly.

One by one, the Demon Kings were branded with a Soul Brand, until finally it was Fei Li's turn.

As the unspoken leader among the Demon Kings, Fei Li certainly had reason to be proud. Even as Yang Kai branded him, he tried every way to resist, but in the Small Xuan Realm, his resistance was utterly useless.

In less than the time it takes to burn an incense stick, all eight Demon Kings led by Fei Li were dealt with.

Standing together, they exchanged strange looks.

Shaya enthusiastically mingled among the Demon Kings, praising Yang Kai's strength at every opportunity to ease their dissatisfaction.

After all this, Yang Kai was severely drained, but with the Warm Spirit Lotus, he could bear it. He only needed a short rest to recover.

In the Small Xuan Realm, the ten Demon Kings stood solemnly, all looking at Yang Kai.

After glancing around, Yang Kai said, "I need you to do a few things for me."

"Please speak, my lord!" Fei Li said awkwardly.

"First, bring me all the converted demons under your command! I know each of you has some demonized Barbarians under you. I need those people."

Fei Li nodded, "Yes."

Converted demons held low status among the demon race; no one cared about them.

"Second, I want your Demonic Beasts!" Yang Kai continued.

This request was a bit harder than the last, but not impossible. Now that the Demon Saints were all healing, almost no one could restrain the Demon Kings. In their own territories, their word was law. If they wanted to send away their Demonic Beasts, their subordinates wouldn't dare question it.

"Third, gather the subordinates of the Demon Kings I killed today, and make sure not to leak news of their deaths."

The Demon Kings all nodded in agreement.

Fei Li asked, "My lord, after we finish these things, should we regroup with you?"

"No need!" Yang Kai shook his head. "After you're done, just do as you please. When I need you, I'll summon you."

Mo Ke Tuo asked, "What if outsiders attack us? You know your people are fighting back everywhere now."

Yang Kai looked at her and said, "Let your subordinates die. That shouldn't be hard, right?"

At these words, the eight Demon Kings' faces changed slightly. They had indeed been branded with Soul Brands and become his slaves, but sending their own people to die was still hard to accept, even if it was Yang Kai's order.

Yang Kai didn't care what they thought. After saying what he needed to, he sent them all out of the Small Xuan Realm.

Out in the wilderness, the eight Demon Kings scattered in all directions.

Watching the Demon Kings leave, Shaya smiled and said, "I thought you would do as before, using them and then devouring their subordinates."

Yang Kai replied indifferently, "Drawing too much attention isn't a good thing. Besides... I still need a bit more time."

Shaman Niu's tribe originally had only three thousand people and had already devoured the groups led by two Demon Kings. If he kept doing as before, he would be unstoppable in the short term, but it would definitely attract the attention of the demon race.

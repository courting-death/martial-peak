## Chapter 2847 — Yu and Lu

As expected, Shaman Dang had summoned him over the giant eagles. It was clear that the Royal City also wanted a share of them.

If the other Great Shaman Masters wanted to get some giant eagles from him, Yang Kai could refuse without hesitation, or even give them a good beating. After all, they were equals, so it didn’t matter if he roughed them up. But if the Royal City made the request, he probably couldn’t refuse.

This was simply too despicable!

“My lord, I saw that the Royal City also has Sky Cavalry, but my giant eagles are all Barbaric Beasts from the mountains, wild and untamed. I’m afraid they’re not presentable.”

Shaman Dang smiled, “The Royal City does have some Sky Cavalry, but not many. Flying Barbaric Beasts are hard to tame. Honestly, I’m quite surprised you managed to tame so many at once.”

Yang Kai immediately said, “I put in a lot of effort, coughed up several liters of blood, and nearly died there.”

Shaman Dang said, “So the Royal City won’t take your giant eagles for nothing!”

Yang Kai’s interest was piqued, his eyes lighting up, “Then how many giant eagles does the Royal City want, and what price are they willing to pay?”

Since he couldn’t resist, he could only try to minimize his losses. If the Royal City wanted giant eagles, they had to show some sincerity. Yang Kai’s giant eagles didn’t just fall from the sky.

Shaman Dang’s expression turned serious, “Half. You name the terms!”

At these words, Yang Kai jumped up from his chair like a cat whose tail had been stepped on, angrily shouting, “Half? My lord, you really dare to ask! Why don’t I just give you all the giant eagles?”

This sudden outburst startled the captain of the guards, who immediately drew his weapon and stared at Yang Kai warily.

Shaman Dang patted his shoulder and said with a smile, “It’s rare for you to have such generosity. In that case, I’ll gladly accept.”

Yang Kai stared at him dumbfounded, his eyelid twitching, “My lord, I have a question.”

“Ask!” Shaman Dang raised his brows.

“Do you have any shame?”

Shaman Dang chuckled, “That’s why I said you should name your own terms!”

He didn’t mind Yang Kai’s rudeness at all, which surprised Yang Kai. He immediately thought this Shaman King was quite a character.

Yang Kai was silent for a moment before saying, “I’ll give you twenty-five. I’ll keep fifty for myself. At least with that number, they can still be effective in small-scale battles. Any fewer and they’d be worthless. The Royal City probably just wants the giant eagles for scouting, right?”

Shaman Dang didn’t deny it. He nodded, “That’s right!”

“Then it doesn’t matter much. Twenty-five giant eagles, plus the Sky Cavalry the Royal City already has, should be enough for reconnaissance.”

Shaman Dang was about to speak, but Yang Kai cut him off, “Twenty-five. That’s my limit.”

“Fine! Twenty-five it is. I won’t make things difficult for you.” Shaman Dang agreed readily, clearly prepared for this, then looked up, “So what are your terms?”

Yang Kai immediately said, “Equip each of my subordinates with a set of armor!”

Shaman Dang looked at him with a strange expression.

Yang Kai scratched his face, “Alright, alright, a thousand sets of armor at least?”

Shaman Dang shook his head, “Not to mention a thousand, the Royal City can’t even produce a hundred sets right now.”

“The Royal City is that poor?” Yang Kai was shocked.

“It’s not that the Royal City is poor,” Shaman Dang smiled wryly, “It’s just that all the supplies in the city have been swept up by the Great Shaman Masters. To be honest, you started this. You equipped three thousand people with weapons at once. How could the other Great Shaman Masters not show some sincerity? Even if they don’t have your wealth, if each buys a little, how much is left in the city to go around?”

“That’s true,” Yang Kai nodded, knowing he wasn’t being lied to. In the past few days outside the city, he’d seen many Barbarian Tribe members equipped with weapons and armor, which must have been bought by the Great Shaman Masters for their own people.

But they couldn’t possibly equip every tribesman, only a select few.

“Let’s not talk about supplies anymore. There’s nothing I can do.”

Yang Kai thought for a moment. “If not supplies, then give me people.”

Shaman Dang narrowed his eyes, “What kind of people do you want?”

Yang Kai grinned and said, “As it happens, I have no Shaman Masters under my command. My lord, why not assign a few to serve under me?”

“How many?”

“Five!” Yang Kai smiled, “Five giant eagles for one Shaman Master. That’s a good deal, my lord!”

“Five is too many. I can only give you one!”

“Are Shaman Masters really that precious?” Yang Kai was shocked, “My lord, show some sincerity.”

Shaman Dang snorted, “And you, asking for five Shaman Masters, is that sincerity?”

Yang Kai replied calmly, “That’s just how bargaining works. You start high and settle low. That’s business.”

“At most two. That’s my limit. If that’s not enough, just forget it. Pretend I never mentioned it.”

“How could I refuse?” Yang Kai quickly said, “Two is fine. After all, you’re the Shaman King, my lord.”

Shaman Dang pointed at him, looking as though he wanted to scold him but didn’t quite know how to begin. Then he turned to the guard captain and said, “Call Yu and Lu over.”

The captain was clearly surprised, but left without a word.

After waiting in the hall for a while, footsteps sounded again outside the door.

Soon, the captain led two women inside. Yang Kai looked up and couldn’t help but be amazed.

He had vaguely guessed that Yu and Lu were women, but he hadn’t expected them to be so outstanding. Compared to other Barbarian Tribe women, they were exceptionally beautiful, and their tall figures were quite impressive.

Most importantly, the two women looked exactly alike—they were clearly twins.

This surprised Yang Kai even more.

Among the Barbarian Tribe, Shamans were always rare, as only those favored by the Barbarian God could cultivate Shamanic Arts. In a village, having even one Shaman Apprentice was remarkable, but these twins were both Shamans, and even more impressively, both had reached the Shaman Master realm.

Judging by their youthful appearance, they didn’t seem much older than Die. Given time, their future would be limitless.

Faced with Yang Kai’s gaze, the two women kept their eyes straight ahead, their expressions cold. They stepped forward and spoke in unison, “My lord, did you summon us?”

Their voices were almost identical, their diction nearly indistinguishable. Anyone unaware might think it was just one person speaking.

“A shared mind?” Yang Kai raised his brows, his gaze burning as he looked at them.

Although twins often had a special connection, a shared mind was a higher level. Twins who reached this state could accomplish twice the result with half the effort in anything they did. If they cast spells together, their power would far exceed their current realm.

In other words, although Yu and Lu were only Shaman Masters, if they worked together, even Great Shaman Masters might not be their match. What a find! Yang Kai was overjoyed. He had only wanted two Shaman Masters to fill the gap in his ranks, but Shaman Dang had sent him such a pair.

Shaman Dang nodded, looking at Yu and Lu with a trace of gentleness and affection in his eyes.

Yang Kai felt something was off. He wondered if these two girls were related to Shaman Dang. If so, taking them might cause him trouble in the future.

Just as he was feeling uneasy, Shaman Dang said, “From today on, you will follow Shaman Niu and obey his orders without question!”

Yu and Lu turned to look at Yang Kai, placed their hands on their chests, and said in unison, “Greetings, Shaman Niu!”

Their tone was flat, lacking the respect Shaman Masters usually showed to Great Shaman Masters.

Yang Kai put on the air of an expert and nodded, “No need for formalities.”

Shaman Dang smiled, “Yu and Lu grew up and cultivated in the Shaman God Hall. They only arrived at the Royal City a few days ago, so they’re not very worldly. From now on, you can discipline them as you see fit. If they disobey, you may punish them as you wish.”

“Shaman God Hall?” Yang Kai’s expression turned serious.

The Shaman God Hall was the spiritual pillar of the entire Barbarian Tribe, a symbol of their people. Within its sacred halls stood the statue of the Shaman God, revered by all. Only the most gifted shamans from each tribe were qualified to enter the Shaman God Hall to cultivate and serve the Shaman God.

Normally, any shaman who came out of the Shaman God Hall would have mastered exquisite Shamanic Arts and be unrivaled among their peers.

Yang Kai, having inherited Qing’s knowledge, also knew the status of the Shaman God Hall in the hearts of the Barbarian Tribe.

He had once thought that if he had the chance, he would visit the Shaman God Hall to see if he could learn something.

But the demon invasion had ruined his plans.

He hadn’t expected that now, two Shaman Masters from the Shaman God Hall would be standing before him.

No wonder they had reached such a realm at such a young age. Yu and Lu’s talent was beyond doubt if they had been able to enter the Shaman God Hall—they were definitely among the most gifted shamans in the Southern Barbarian Tribe.

Shaman Dang smiled, “With your talent, if not for this unexpected event, you could have entered the Shaman God Hall as well. So live well, Shaman Niu. Only in the Shaman God Hall is there true Shamanic Art inheritance. If you want to experience its wonders, become stronger and survive this war!”

Yang Kai replied solemnly, “Yes, I will heed your teachings, my lord!”

“Go.” Shaman Dang waved his hand, saying no more.

Yang Kai placed his hand on his chest and withdrew. Before leaving, Yu and Lu looked at Shaman Dang with some reluctance and said in unison, “My lord, take care!”

Shaman Dang nodded gently, and only then did Yu and Lu turn and leave the hall.

Only Shaman Dang and the captain of the guards remained in the hall.

“My lord, he’s just a Great Shaman Master. Why give Yu and Lu to him?” The captain looked at Shaman Dang in confusion, clearly troubled by his decision. There were other Shaman Masters in the Royal City, so why give him Yu and Lu?

## Chapter 2821 — Summons

As soon as Die made her move, she displayed remarkable cultivation and strength. Three streaks of force sliced through the air, accompanied by three soft sounds, instantly splitting three fat demonic insect beasts in two. Pale yellow fluid splattered everywhere, and a nauseating stench quickly filled the air.

More demonic insects rushed forward, spewing highly corrosive venom from their mouthparts. Die nimbly dodged, avoiding any contact with the venom, and whenever she found an opening, she unleashed powerful shamanic arts, slaughtering countless insects.

On the other side, Yang Kai also charged into the swarm, his fists swinging wide and fierce like a true barbarian warrior. His fighting style was the complete opposite of Die’s elegance, but his speed in killing insects was no less. Not a speck of dust touched him; all venom and fluids splashed toward him were blocked by a shield of shamanic arts.

In less than half an hour, the entire nest of demonic insects was cleared out.

Without a word, the two began searching the corpses for inner cores.

But after obtaining the inner cores, Yang Kai noticed they were somewhat shrunken. Unlike the round and full inner cores of demonic beasts outside, these were misshapen and large, with pitifully little energy inside.

This was likely due to the environment here.

These demonic insects survived by parasitizing the Divine Tree, drawing on its energy to grow rapidly. However, compared to demonic beasts outside, their foundations were much less solid, and even their inner cores were lacking in quality.

It didn’t matter. There were plenty of insects; if the quality was lacking, quantity would make up for it. Since arriving in this ancient world, Yang Kai’s appetite had been excellent. He had refined and swallowed at least eight hundred or a thousand demonic beast inner cores. As long as they could help him quickly advance to the Shaman Master realm, he wouldn’t miss a single one.

After collecting all the inner cores, Die finally looked up and said, “Grandpa Qing, the next place.”

As soon as she finished speaking, a crack suddenly opened up where they stood, leading straight to the next insect nest.

After traveling through several more nests, Yang Kai and Die worked seamlessly together, leaving the demonic insects utterly powerless to resist—their foes were slaughtered to the last. Suddenly, a question occurred to Yang Kai: “Senior Qing, since you can sense these demonic insects, why don’t you drive them out yourself? Why rely on others to do it for you?”

Although he hadn’t experienced Qing’s strength firsthand, Yang Kai had some guesses. The insects parasitizing him weren’t anything formidable—just minor ailments. If Qing really wanted, he could easily deal with them himself. Yet, he always had Die help.

Die replied, “Grandpa Qing never kills.”

Yang Kai’s mouth twitched, and he was momentarily at a loss for words.

Although Qing’s philosophy was hard for him to accept, considering this was a tree of protection, Yang Kai could somewhat understand his thinking.

He said nothing more and, together with Die, focused on clearing the insects inside the tree.

Three days passed. Yang Kai and Die had no idea how many insect nests they’d cleared. The inner cores they harvested were still poor in quality, but their quantity was endless.

After three days of continuous battle, Die was slightly out of breath, but Yang Kai remained vigorous, leaving Die amazed.

“That’s enough. You two, come back,” Qing’s voice suddenly sounded in their ears.

At the same time, a passage appeared before them, with a faint light at its end.

Yang Kai and Die exchanged glances and walked into the passage.

Soon, they returned to Yang Kai’s tree cave. Qing was still there, smiling at Yang Kai. “Thank you, young man.”

“Helping others is its own reward. No need to be so formal, Senior.” Yang Kai grinned, holding his bundle of inner cores, beaming with joy.

With these inner cores, advancing to Shaman Master shouldn’t be a problem.

On the other side, Die apologized, “Grandpa Qing, when I’m stronger, I’ll definitely help you drive out the insect king!”

Yang Kai was startled and looked up. “There’s an insect king?”

Die said, “Otherwise, why do you think the insects never run out? Every time Grandpa Qing wakes up, I have to kill a batch, but when I return again, there are still plenty.”

“Senior, you should have said so earlier.” Yang Kai immediately put down his bundle, eager. “Open a passage, I’ll go meet the insect king!”

Die looked at him as if he were crazy.

Qing smiled, “You’re not strong enough yet. It’s not your opponent.”

Die added, “That’s a being comparable to a Shaman King. Be careful it doesn’t swallow you whole.”

Yang Kai had never experienced the strength of a Shaman King directly. The hierarchy of power in this world was very different from later generations, but in this ancient world, a Shaman King was a mighty figure ruling a region—equivalent to the Emperor Realm in later times!

Someone like Die was likely around the Dao Origin Realm.

After being teased by Die, Yang Kai was about to retort when Qing said, “You have a guest. She’s been waiting for you for two days. You should see her first.”

Yang Kai frowned and curiously poked his head out of the cave, looking down. He saw a familiar figure pacing anxiously below.

Yu!

Suddenly, Qing spoke again, “Young man, since you helped me scratch that itch, take this green leaf as a token of my thanks.”

As he spoke, he pinched a bright green leaf between his fingers and flicked it toward Yang Kai.

Yang Kai caught the leaf but didn’t sense any power from it. Curious, he asked, “Senior, what’s the use of this leaf?”

Qing smiled, "It's nothing special. Within my protection, you can return to your residence with a thought."

"I see!" Yang Kai raised his brows and carefully put the leaf away. Although it only allowed him to return to his tree cave, it was a gift from Qing, so he accepted it.

Qing said no more and vanished.

After three days of battle, Die was also tired and took her leave to rest in her own tree cave.

After they left, Yang Kai poked his head out and called down, “You were looking for me?”

Hearing this, Yu looked up in surprise. She chanted, and a green light enveloped her, slowly lifting her into Yang Kai’s tree cave.

“What is it?” Yang Kai asked.

Yu was about to speak but suddenly looked Yang Kai up and down, then exclaimed in shock, “You’re an Upper-Level Shaman Warrior now?”

How long had it been? Barely more than a month.

Last time she brought him back from Luobai Mountain, he was just a Lower-Level Shaman Warrior. How had he advanced two ranks in such a short time? If she hadn’t seen it herself, Yu wouldn’t have believed such nonsense.

She herself hadn’t made any progress in the past month.

Yang Kai shrugged, not bothering to explain.

With the foundation of the Emperor Realm, he had no bottlenecks cultivating ancient shaman arts. Once his strength accumulated, breakthroughs came naturally.

This was still slow due to his poor cultivation method. The one he used was taught by the village chief of Cangnan Village—how could it be any good? If he could get a set of cultivation methods used by the Shaman Saints, his progress would be even faster.

After standing there in shock for quite a while, Yu finally came back to her senses. Looking somewhat shaken, she said softly, “Shaman Niu, Lord Che wishes to see you!”

“Lord Che? Who is he?” Yang Kai frowned.

A trace of reverence appeared on Yu’s face as she replied, “Lord Che is one of the Great Shaman Masters of our Frost Snow Tribe.”

“Why does your tribe’s Great Shaman Master want to see me?” Yang Kai was puzzled.

Great Shaman Masters weren’t unfamiliar to him—Die was one, but her background was sensitive, so she never revealed herself. Even so, she was a powerful figure. So, although Yu spoke seriously, Yang Kai wasn’t nervous.

Yu hesitated, then asked, “That healing medicine… did you really make it?”

“Yes!” Yang Kai nodded.

Yu became respectful. “I didn’t expect you to be an apothecary. Many of our tribe have bought your healing medicine recently and found it very effective. So, my Frost Snow Tribe wants you to make more for backup. Of course, we’ll provide all the materials—you just need to prepare it.”

“I see!” Yang Kai nodded in understanding. If he’d heard this news three days ago, he would have been delighted—back then, his healing medicine wasn’t selling, and he was worrying about how to earn enough inner cores. If the Frost Snow Tribe had made this request then, he wouldn’t have had to fret about not having enough Qing Coins to buy inner cores.

But now, after a big harvest, he was planning to refine those insect inner cores and quickly advance to Shaman Master Realm.

The tribe’s sudden request left him a bit helpless.

After thinking for a moment, Yang Kai said, “Alright, lead the way.”

Making healing medicine wasn’t much trouble. He could refine medicine and cultivate at the same time.

Seeing him agree, Yu was overjoyed. “Thank you so much!”

“Barbarian Tribe is one big family.” Yang Kai laughed heartily.

Leaving the tree cave, led by Yu, the two headed toward the most magnificent palace. In this ancient world, such palaces were rare. The places where the barbarian tribe lived were usually simple, and only those with high status could reside in palaces like these.

The palace complex in Frost Snow City was the royal palace of the Frost Snow Tribe, where the Shaman Kings lived.

The Great Shaman Master named Che also resided in this palace.

With Yang Kai’s current status, being able to meet a Great Shaman Master was already a great honor. It was impossible for him to meet the tribe’s Shaman Kings.

## Chapter 2689 — The Grand Square Five Elements Array

The restriction array in Private Room Thirteen was still perfectly intact, so everyone’s Divine Sense was blocked outside, making it impossible to probe what was happening within. Yet every word of the conversation inside was transmitted out with crystal clarity.

All they heard was the Emperor Realm cultivator in Room Thirteen, failing to beg for mercy and seemingly preparing for a desperate struggle. But before any sounds of fighting could be heard, he was subdued. Soon after, muffled whimpers rang out, followed by a few strange, gurgling swallowing noises…

Even though nothing could be seen, everyone could easily imagine what that Emperor Realm cultivator in Room Thirteen must have suffered.

“Urgh…”

In the main hall, a woman was the first to lose control and vomited on the spot.

That sound seemed to be the fuse that set everything off. Those martial artists who had been barely holding on could no longer endure; one after another, they turned away and vomited everywhere. In an instant, the entire hall was filled with a foul stench, making the rest frown deeply.

The other Emperor Realm cultivators in the private rooms were all equally baffled. The Qiyao Commerce Guild was, after all, one of the two top Commerce Guilds in the Southern Territory. For something like this to happen, and for no one to intervene, was truly strange. Many began to sense that this auction was anything but simple, as if they had unwittingly stepped into a quagmire.

After quite a while, Yang Kai finally strode out of Room Thirteen, clapping his hands as he said, “In all my years, this is the first time I’ve encountered such a bizarre request. Truly eye-opening.”

Behind him, the sounds of retching continued to echo, making it feel as if life itself was worse than death.

“Continue!” Yang Kai looked up at Hua Qingsi on the high platform.

Hua Qingsi nodded slightly, knowing that Yang Kai had likely been angered by her Master this time. He was brimming with fury and had nowhere to vent it. Yet Rooms Seven and Thirteen had thrown themselves into his path—how could he possibly let them off lightly?

For Room Thirteen to escape with his life was already a stroke of luck, but given what he’d just endured, that fate might have been worse than death.

Glancing around, Hua Qingsi announced, “Room One has bid one hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals. Any further bids?”

The crowd was silent, thinking, "He’s already bid a hundred million. Who could possibly top that?"

Hua Qingsi nodded, “Since there are no more bids, this third auction item goes to Room One.”

She gestured, and the maid immediately carried the tray toward Room One, her face pale and her legs trembling, as if she were heading not to Room One, but to some ghostly purgatory.

Yang Kai returned to his room and carefully put away the third Heavenly Puppet.

On the high platform, Hua Qingsi spoke again: “The fourth auction item—Thousand Leaves Sect’s Heavenly Puppet…”

“One hundred million!” Yang Kai called out loudly.

Hua Qingsi smiled faintly and looked around the hall. “No one else wants to bid?”

After speaking, she signaled to the maid who had just come out from backstage. This time, she didn’t even bother with pleasantries.

The rest of the auction was essentially just an exchange between Yang Kai and Hua Qingsi. Every time an item was presented, Yang Kai would immediately bid one hundred million, and Hua Qingsi would simply have the Heavenly Puppet delivered to Room One.

In the various private rooms, the Emperor Realm cultivators looked both helpless and grim. They had traveled from afar to participate in this auction, only to become mere bystanders. Not only did they fail to win any items, they didn’t even have the right to compete. With Yang Kai opening every bid at one hundred million, who could possibly challenge him? And anyone with eyes could see that Yang Kai wasn’t here for the auction—he was here to cause trouble.

Never mind that he’d just killed the people in Room Seven and humiliated those in Room Thirteen; even his bidding was suspicious.

He certainly had deep pockets, with several hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals on hand, but even so, there was no reason to bid like this. Those Source Crystals didn’t just fall from the sky. The only explanation was that Yang Kai never intended to pay at all.

The Emperor Realm cultivators present weren’t fools. After watching for so long, they finally sensed something was off. A vague unease welled up in their hearts, as if invisible shackles were binding them, leaving them restless and anxious.

Yet the auctioneer on stage was cooperating with him to an extreme degree, making the whole situation feel even stranger.

In less than a quarter of an hour, all nine Heavenly Puppets from the Thousand Leaves Sect had been auctioned off. As expected, every single one ended up in Yang Kai’s hands, and he handed them all over to Ye Hen.

Ye Hen was so overwhelmed with emotion that he could hardly contain himself. He never imagined these Heavenly Puppets could be recovered. He had thought that with the destruction of the Thousand Leaves Sect, the sect’s legacy would end with him. For days, he’d been wracked with guilt, feeling he’d failed the ancestors. Even when Li Qingyun returned him safe and sound, Ye Hen couldn’t lift his spirits.

But now, with all nine Heavenly Puppets reclaimed, a surge of fighting spirit suddenly welled up within Ye Hen.

Though the Thousand Leaves Sect had been destroyed in his hands, he was determined to rebuild it and restore its former glory. The five hundred disciples he’d placed in Emperor Heaven Valley were his hope, as were the secret techniques and manuals that had survived. These nine Heavenly Puppets were hope as well.

In an instant, Ye Hen’s spirit was completely renewed, as if he had become a different person.

Ye Qinghan and Du Xian took all this in and were secretly inspired, their gratitude toward Yang Kai deepening even further. After all, Heavenly Puppets were extraordinary treasures—each one rivaled an Emperor Realm cultivator. If anyone else had won them, they would surely have kept them for themselves. But Yang Kai returned them all to Ye Hen. Such magnanimity was truly admirable; it was impossible not to feel grateful.

“Young Master Yang, it seems they’re stalling for time,” Yingfei suddenly said.

Yang Kai nodded. “I know.”

He had already acted so domineeringly, sweeping up every auction item, yet Wu Ming still hadn’t stepped in to stop him and instead remained silent. If that wasn’t stalling for time, what was?

They were willing to give up all nine Heavenly Puppets just to buy time. Clearly, there was a deeper scheme at play behind the scenes.

“Should we have Madam Hua speed up the auction?” Yingfei asked, frowning.

Yang Kai shook his head. “Sister Hua has been afflicted with a Heart-Controlling Gu. She’s not acting of her own will.”

“Heart-Controlling Gu!” Yingfei’s expression changed; he had clearly heard of such a thing before.

“Since we’re here, let’s just see what tricks they want to play.” Yang Kai took a calm sip of tea.

On the high platform, Hua Qingsi suddenly frowned, glanced backstage, hesitated for a moment, then announced, “The first half of the auction is now over. Please take a short break. There will be even better items coming up, and I promise you won’t be disappointed.”

Hearing this, Yang Kai’s eyes narrowed slightly.

In the private rooms, the Emperor Realm cultivators all pursed their lips. If not for the presence of this monster Yang Kai, this would have been a fine auction. But with him here, it was like a rat dropping in a pot of thick soup.

He was the very definition of a troublemaker. With him around, who else had any right to bid?

All nine Heavenly Puppets from the Thousand Leaves Sect had been swept up by him. No matter how good the next items were, no one else would have a chance. As long as he opened his mouth, they would inevitably end up in his hands.

Realizing this, many people lost all interest. They knew that no matter how good the remaining items were, they would have nothing to do with them. One after another, they got up from their rooms, intending to leave.

In no time, more than half of the thirty-odd private rooms had people heading out. The rest stayed behind, likely just to watch the spectacle. As for the auction items, they no longer held out any hope.

But when they reached the entrance of the auction hall, they discovered that the place had already been sealed off. A powerful array seemed to be covering the entire Qiyao Commerce Guild branch, making it impossible to leave.

“What’s going on? Why won’t they let us leave?”

“The Qiyao Commerce Guild is really bold. Are they planning to catch us all in one net?”

“I knew something was off about this auction. Looks like there really is a plot.”

“Don’t panic, everyone. If we work together, a mere array can be broken.”

Over a dozen Emperor Realm cultivators at the First and Second Stages joined forces. After a brief discussion, they all unleashed their techniques against the array.

After the time it took for an incense stick to burn, the dozen or so people stopped, their faces grim as they stared at the unchanging barrier. Their hearts sank to rock bottom.

No one had expected the array to be so formidable. Even after withstanding the combined assault of so many Emperor Realm cultivators for so long, it hadn’t reacted at all.

“Brother Gong, your Gong family from Tianhe Valley is famous for arrays. Can you tell what kind of array this is?” someone suddenly asked, turning to an elderly man in a dark robe. He blended in with the crowd, but he was actually the current Family Head of the Gong family from Tianhe Valley.

Throughout the Southern Territory, the Gong family of Tianhe Valley was well known. Every member was skilled in arrays, and many sects’ Grand Guardian Arrays had been set up by this family.

Back when Yang Kai was in the Four Seasons Land, he’d met Gong Wenshan, a disciple of the Gong family. Even though Gong Wenshan was only at the Dao Origin Realm, his mastery of arrays was already extraordinary—let alone the Family Head himself.

In terms of array mastery, this Family Head of the Gong family was on par with Nanmen Dajun of the Northern Territory.

“What, you’re the head of the Gong family? I’m honored!” someone exclaimed, clearly having heard of the old man’s reputation. It was their first time meeting, so they cupped their fists in greeting.

Most people did the same, and for a while, everyone exchanged pleasantries with the old man surnamed Gong.

Thinking back, it now seemed that when everyone had joined forces to attack the array earlier, the Gong Family Head had never truly exerted himself. It wasn’t that he was shirking his duty, but rather that he had been carefully observing the array all along.

The Gong Family Head frowned in thought for a while before saying, “If this Old Man isn’t mistaken, this is the Grand Square Five Elements Array.”

“What’s special about this array?” someone asked.

The Gong Family Head replied, “This Grand Square Five Elements Array gathers the power of the five elements, generating and restraining each other in an endless cycle. It cannot be broken by brute force. Once set up, it can guard heaven and earth.”

“Guard heaven and earth!” someone exclaimed.

“It’s an extremely sophisticated array,” the Gong Family Head said gravely. “And moreover…”

“Moreover what?”

## Chapter 2664 — Abandoned

"Well... alright, I'll have to trouble Elder Ji Yao then." Nanmen Dajun was rather disappointed. He had wanted to take this opportunity to exchange some insights and experiences on the Dao of Arrays with Yang Kai, but Yang Kai simply gave him no such chance, leaving him a bit dejected.

Still, being able to replan and perfect the Grand Guardian Array for such a vast territory was a lifelong wish for him as a grandmaster of arrays.

He quickly collected his thoughts, excused himself, and hurried out to inspect the layout of the Grand Guardian Array that originally belonged to Wenqing Sect.

After he left, Yang Kai stroked his chin and looked at the Space Array beside him, saying, "As I thought, it's not broken."

If the Space Array had been set up incorrectly, Ji Yao and Nanmen Dajun wouldn't have been able to teleport here from Ice Heart Valley.

"What's not broken?" Ji Yao asked curiously.

Xi Lei immediately grinned and recounted what had just happened, unable to hide his joy.

Ji Yao pondered for a moment and said, "Since the Space Array connects two places, and neither here nor Ice Heart Valley's array is damaged, perhaps the problem lies with the array in Ancient Land."

Her words were a wake-up call. Yang Kai's eyes lit up and he clapped his hands, "That's definitely it."

After speaking, he suddenly remembered something and gritted his teeth, "Lady Feng, oh Lady Feng, this move of cutting off the retreat is truly ruthless."

The Space Array in Ancient Land couldn’t have broken down for no reason; the only explanation was that after Yang Kai left, Luan Feng deliberately destroyed it.

Luan Feng did this simply to prevent him from coming to bother them from time to time. This was understandable, but it still made Yang Kai gnash his teeth in hatred.

Setting up a Space Array wasn't much trouble, but the key was that Ancient Land was far away. Traveling there would take quite some time. He had thought that he would have to deal with Ancient Land often in the future, so leaving an array there would make things convenient. Who could have guessed that just after he left, Luan Feng would smash the array to pieces? Truly, the most venomous is a woman's heart. Usually, Luan Feng appeared dignified and gentle, but who knew she could be so ruthless?

It seems that next time he went to Ancient Land, he'd have to set up the array within the Stone Spirit Race's territory, lest Luan Feng destroys it again.

Having made up his mind, Yang Kai turned to glance at Xi Lei and the others, saying, "Looks like you all have been abandoned by Ancient Land."

Xi Lei and the others had also figured it out by now. They understood why they hadn't been teleported away. Though a bit disappointed, they were even more delighted. They all grinned, "It's fine, it's fine. Lady Luan Feng must think Young Master Yang is important and wants us to stay by your side to protect you. Since that's her intention, you can't refuse us anymore."

Yang Kai said, "Now that things are like this, how could I drive you away?"

He hadn't been willing to keep the three Yao Kings before, mainly out of consideration for the three Saint Venerables of Ancient Land. After all, these three Yao Kings served under those three Saint Venerables, and just taking them away like this seemed a bit improper.

But now that Luan Feng had made the first move, he could only follow through. In every sense, Ancient Land couldn't fault him for it.

Losing a Space Array but gaining the three Yao Kings, Yang Kai had made a huge profit and had a clear conscience.

Xie Wuwei grinned from ear to ear at the side, rubbing his hands, "Young Master Yang, since things have come to this, can we join Lingxiao Palace now? I don't ask for much, just to be a Protector."

Yingfei and Xi Lei both looked eager as well.

Yang Kai smiled, "With the abilities of you three, being Protectors would be a waste of your talents. Lingxiao Palace has only just established its mountain gate. It will still take some time before we can open our doors wide and recruit disciples. But when that day comes, you three can stay in my Lingxiao Palace as Honored Elders."

Xi Lei and the others exchanged glances, all unable to contain their joy.

With Yang Kai's words, it meant they would be considered insiders in the future. How could he forget them when benefits came around?

Compared to the other Yao Kings and Saint Envoys in Ancient Land, they were already ahead of the curve.

A sect not yet officially established already had three Honored Elders on par with the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. If this news got out, who knew how many people in the world would be envious?

Ji Yao pursed her lips and smiled, "Congratulations, Senior Brother Yang. When Lingxiao Palace opens its doors in the future, be sure to notify Ice Heart Valley. I'll come to drink a cup of celebratory wine."

Yang Kai being able to take root here and establish a sect made her genuinely happy. Since Ice Heart Valley had a close relationship with Yang Kai, the two sects would support each other in the future. In the entire Northern Territory, who could shake the status of these two giants?

Yang Kai smiled, "Of course. When the time comes, I'll have to trouble all the Junior Sisters of Ice Heart Valley." After a pause, he asked, "By the way, what happened to the tens of thousands of surrendered enemies captured by Ice Heart Valley?"

Ji Yao smiled bitterly, "Master is also troubled by this. Although they acted as accomplices this time and made things difficult for Ice Heart Valley, in the end, they have no real enmity with us. If we kill them all now, it will ruin Ice Heart Valley's reputation. But if we let them go, it will make us seem weak and easy to bully."

Yang Kai laughed, "If that's the case, why not have them come help with labor? Nanmen Dajun needs manpower to rearrange Wenqing Sect's Grand Guardian Array. Of course, those who have the blood of Ice Heart Valley disciples on their hands cannot be spared."

Ji Yao nodded, "Those people have already been killed. The ones still alive are just those who cheered from the sidelines. But your suggestion is good. When Grandmaster Nanmen returns, we'll see how many people he needs."

After discussing, everyone waited.

Fortunately, the earth vein here was excellent and the Spiritual Qi was abundant. While waiting, they could meditate and cultivate. There were fine palaces nearby to provide places for cultivation.

Two days later, Nanmen Dajun hurried back, handed Yang Kai a long list, and requested all sorts of precious materials. Yang Kai glanced at it and, without being stingy, filled several Spatial Rings with items for him.

Not only that, Ji Yao also returned to Ice Heart Valley and continuously brought over the surrendered enemies, instructing them to work for Nanmen Dajun.

In no time, Lingxiao Palace became lively, bustling with people everywhere, everyone busy inside and out.

As time passed, everyone came to know that a man named Yang Kai had seized the territory that originally belonged to Wenqing Sect and established a sect here called Lingxiao Palace.

Many people from Ice Heart Valley also came to help, all women, including Zi Yu and others who were familiar with Yang Kai.

With them overseeing things, the tens of thousands of surrendered enemies didn't dare slack off. After all, Ice Heart Valley had made it clear that as long as they could atone for their crimes and help set up the arrays for Lingxiao Palace, Ice Heart Valley would let bygones be bygones and let them go.

With such a promise, who would dare not to work hard?

Quite a few wandering martial artists were also interested in Lingxiao Palace, subtly probing the female disciples of Ice Heart Valley about how to join and become a member.

After all, this place originally belonged to Wenqing Sect. Who would dare occupy it without some ability? Moreover, recently, it was rumored that Lingxiao Palace had three Honored Elders at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm.

Such a lineup was enough to show the bright future of Lingxiao Palace. Once the mountain gate truly opened, it would surely become a top sect.

If one could become a member, it would be a lifetime of endless benefits.

Unfortunately, even the female disciples of Ice Heart Valley didn't know how to join Lingxiao Palace, so they couldn't give any answers, leaving the surrendered enemies disappointed.

Atop the finest peak of Lingxiao Palace, within a grand palace hall.

This should have originally been Feng Xuan's cultivation place.

Yang Kai sat cross-legged, surrounded by Upper-Level Source Crystals. As he circulated his cultivation technique, the Source Crystals turned to dust one by one, their energy converging and pouring into his body, flowing through his limbs and bones, and settling in his Dantian.

Ever since his battle with Shi Huo in Ancient Land, Yang Kai had felt an urgent need to improve his strength.

Although he was now an Emperor, he was still far from being invincible in the world. Moreover, as his strength increased, the enemies he encountered grew stronger. If he didn't want to feel powerless again, he could only improve his cultivation as quickly as possible.

But after Ancient Land, he encountered the events at Ice Heart Valley, leaving him with little time to cultivate. He had refined a pile of Spiritual Pills, but hadn't even had time to take them.

Now that he finally had some free time, he naturally went into seclusion, hungry for progress.

One after another, he swallowed auxiliary Spiritual Pills, exhausted piles of Source Crystals, and the power in his body slowly but steadily increased.

Xi Lei and the others knew Yang Kai was cultivating, so no one dared disturb him.

Two months later, the Grand Guardian Array of Lingxiao Palace was complete. All the surrendered enemies who had come to help were dismissed, and the female disciples of Ice Heart Valley returned to their sect through the Space Array.

The vast foundation of Lingxiao Palace returned to tranquility.

Nanmen Dajun came to see Yang Kai in high spirits, wanting to report his hard-earned results, but was stopped by Yingfei, who was guarding outside. After an unpleasant exchange, Nanmen Dajun couldn't persuade Yingfei to call Yang Kai out of seclusion and could only go off to study the cross-domain Space Array in frustration.

During this time, Ji Yao also came to visit several times, but never managed to see Yang Kai.

Since going into seclusion, Yang Kai seemed to have cut off all contact with the outside world. Even the three Yao Kings didn't know when he would emerge.

But the three Yao Kings knew that at the Emperor Realm, it was normal to go into seclusion for three, five, or even ten or twenty years, so they weren't worried about Yang Kai having any accidents.

Each day, one of them would guard outside the palace, while the other two wandered around.

Time flew by. After a full year of silence, a sudden crackling explosion sounded from within the palace, as if someone was stretching their muscles and bones.

Yingfei, who was guarding outside, immediately opened his eyes and looked toward the palace. His eagle-like gaze seemed able to pierce through the many barriers and see what was happening inside.

Soon, his expression brightened and he stood up, letting out a long cry.

After a moment, two figures quickly flew over from afar and landed, revealing Xie Wuwei and Xi Lei.

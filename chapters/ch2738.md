## Chapter 2738 — Bloodline Suppression

“Dragon Blood Flower!” Zhu Qing, who had been quietly standing in the hall, suddenly seemed as if a nerve had been struck. Her expression changed, and she reached out toward the wooden box. A powerful suction force surged forth, instantly pulling the box into her hand.

Li Jiao was still reeling from Ji Ying’s earlier words when he suddenly realized his precious item had been snatched away. His face changed dramatically as he cried out, “Miss, what are you doing?!”

Misfortune never comes alone; when it rains, it pours.

Not only had he completely offended Ji Ying on this trip, and lost his chance to have the Dragon Blood Pill refined, but now even the raw material had been taken from him.

That was a spirit flower he had spent years cultivating with his own Essence-Blood, the key to his own advancement. How could Li Jiao just watch it fall into someone else’s hands? Yet he didn’t dare act rashly, only staring at Zhu Qing with a face full of grief and indignation, hoping she would return the Dragon Blood Flower to him.

With a flick of her finger, Zhu Qing opened the wooden box. Instantly, a brilliant red flower lying quietly inside came into view.

Yang Kai looked over curiously, his eyes lighting up at the sight.

The Dragon Blood Flower was crystal clear, as if forged from fresh blood. Most astonishing of all, faint traces of dragon aura emanated from the blossom.

Even though he had never heard of a Dragon Blood Flower before, and this was his first time seeing one, Yang Kai could tell at a glance that it was something extraordinary.

“It really is a Dragon Blood Flower!” Zhu Qing’s brows knitted as she closed the box again, but she didn’t return it to Li Jiao. Instead, she glared at him and demanded coldly, “Where did you get this Dragon Blood Flower?”

Li Jiao said, “Miss, please return the flower to me first!”

Zhu Qing ignored him. Without any visible movement, she suddenly appeared right in front of Li Jiao, her expression more severe than ever, pressing him, “I’ll ask one more time—where exactly did you get this Dragon Blood Flower?”

An incredibly oppressive aura swept over him. Li Jiao felt his breath catch, his blood nearly freezing in his veins, and he couldn’t help but take two steps back, his face pale. “I got it by chance. Why do you ask?”

He was baffled. He really had obtained this Dragon Blood Flower by accident, though it was indeed precious. But why was this woman so agitated? What shocked him even more was that, as a Third-Stage Emperor, he couldn’t muster the slightest resistance before her. That bone-deep fear left his hands and feet icy cold.

How could this be? He was a Third-Stage Emperor—only a Great Emperor could suppress him with sheer presence alone. But this woman didn’t look like a Great Emperor at all.

“Lies!” Zhu Qing snapped. “Dragon Blood Flowers only grow on Dragon Isle. There’s no way you could get one anywhere else!”

She seemed truly angered. As she spoke, a high, piercing dragon roar suddenly echoed through the hall. A massive dragon head apparition appeared above her, its majestic eyes glaring down at Li Jiao. In an instant, all the strength drained from his body and he collapsed to the floor, eyes wide. Cold sweat poured down his forehead as he stammered, “D-Dragon Clan!”

“What?” Mi Qi was also startled, stumbling back to put distance between himself and Zhu Qing, his face turning ghostly pale.

Staring blankly at the dragon head apparition above Zhu Qing, Mi Qi couldn’t help but swallow hard.

No wonder—no wonder this woman looked so delicate yet possessed such terrifying power. No wonder Li Jiao, a Third-Stage Emperor, couldn’t resist her at all. She was actually a Dragon?

If that was the case, then everything made sense.

The Dragons are the leaders of all living things, the highest among Divine Spirits. For a grown Dragon Clan member, sending someone like him flying with a punch was nothing. Li Jiao was even worse off—because he had a trace of Dragon Clan blood in his veins, he was now being completely suppressed by this dragon woman’s bloodline, unable to exert even half his strength.

This was the terror of bloodline suppression by the Dragon Clan.

How could there be a Dragon Clan member here? And judging by Yang Kai’s earlier attitude toward her, their relationship seemed anything but ordinary.

Could it be that Lingxiao Palace was backed by Dragon Isle?

Mi Qi had been considering whether to renege on his promise as soon as he left, but now? With the Dragon Clan involved, he wouldn’t dare go back on his word even if he had a hundred lives to spare.

On the other side, Ji Ying, seated in his chair, was also dumbfounded.

As the fifth disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, Ji Ying was well-traveled and knowledgeable, but he had never seen a real Dragon Clan member before. He never expected to witness one here in Lingxiao Palace.

Thinking back to how Yang Kai had summoned this dragon woman at will, even having her serve tea—

Ji Ying broke out in a cold sweat. His admiration for Yang Kai grew, but so did his fear.

The Dragon Clan was an extremely proud race. When had one ever been ordered around like a maid? If she lost her temper, who in this hall could hope to leave alive?

Just what kind of ability did Yang Kai possess to make a dragon woman obey his every word? Could he be a Dragon himself?

At this thought, Ji Ying’s gaze toward Yang Kai changed. Before, he had respected Yang Kai as an Imperial-Grade Pill Master who had bested him in alchemy, but now, all he could think of was looking up to him as if to a towering mountain.

Making a dragon woman serve tea—probably not even his own master, Great Emperor Miaodan, could enjoy such treatment.

Ji Ying couldn’t help but let his imagination run wild.

“Speak! Where did you get the Dragon Blood Flower, or die!” Zhu Qing looked down at Li Jiao, her voice icy and full of murderous intent.

The Dragon Blood Flower was unique to Dragon Isle, yet one had appeared outside. Even if its quality wasn’t great, it was still a genuine Dragon Blood Flower. Could someone on Dragon Isle be colluding with outsiders to sell them? This was no small matter, which was why Zhu Qing was so serious.

“Frozen Earth! Frozen Earth! I got it from the Frozen Earth!” At this moment, how would Li Jiao dare conceal anything? Standing before him was a living Dragon woman, and the suppression emanating from her bloodline left him trembling with unease. If she wished to kill him, it would likely take no more than a single move. He hurriedly blurted out his secret without the slightest hesitation.

“So it was the Frozen Earth!” Mi Qi exclaimed. He had tried many times to find out where Li Jiao had gotten the Dragon Blood Flower, but Li Jiao always dodged the question. Only now did he learn it came from the Frozen Earth!

That place was a forbidden zone. Rumor had it that even Great Emperors dared not venture deep within, and a Third-Stage Emperor who entered might never return.

“Frozen Earth?” Zhu Qing’s brows furrowed, clearly believing him a bit more. She didn’t think Li Jiao could lie under her bloodline suppression, but curiosity still burned in her voice as she demanded, “How could a Dragon Blood Flower possibly exist in the Frozen Earth?”

That place was unimaginably cold, with not a single living thing. How could a Dragon Blood Flower exist there?

Li Jiao said anxiously, “It seems a Dragon Clan member fell there. The Dragon Blood Flower only survived because it grew on their burial site.”

Hearing this, Zhu Qing’s face changed and she exclaimed, “A Dragon Clan burial site? Could it be—”

She seemed to recall something. After a moment’s silence, she said, “You’d better be telling the truth, or I’ll kill you.”

“Every word is true!” Li Jiao said repeatedly.

“Where exactly is that place? What distinguishing features does it have?” Zhu Qing suddenly transmitted her voice in secret.

Li Jiao immediately understood that Zhu Qing wanted to investigate the site herself. After all, a Dragon Clan member had fallen there, and with so few Dragons in existence, every one of them was precious. If a clansman had perished in the Frozen Earth, Zhu Qing would naturally want to look into it, at the very least to gather the remains and return them to the Dragon Tomb.

Li Jiao also replied by voice transmission, “Miss, if you want to collect your clansman’s remains, there’s no need. I checked before—the Dragon Clan bones had already turned to dust, nothing left.”

Zhu Qing cut him off, “Just tell me the location. The rest is none of your concern.”

Seeing her insist, Li Jiao could only give Zhu Qing the location. In the end, he added, “The Frozen Earth is extremely dangerous. If anything happens to you in there, it’s not my responsibility.”

Zhu Qing sneered, “If you could come out alive, why couldn’t I?”

“Yes, yes.” Li Jiao wiped cold sweat from his brow, then looked longingly at the wooden box in Zhu Qing’s hand. “Then, about the Dragon Blood Flower—”

“Do you want to die?”

“I’ll just give it to you, then. It belongs to Dragon Isle anyway!” Li Jiao quickly changed his tune, his heart aching as if it were bleeding.

He’d lost both the bride and the army—what a disaster! Losing so many Source Crystals to Yang Kai had already left him bitter, and now, even the Dragon Blood Flower was gone.

He never should have come. He really never should have come to Lingxiao Palace.

“Get lost!” Zhu Qing waved her hand as if shooing a fly.

Only then did Li Jiao scramble to his feet. He cast one last, reluctant glance at the wooden box before turning and flying out.

With him gone, Mi Qi naturally didn’t dare linger. He cupped his fists in apology and hurried after him.

“Qing’er, let me see what this is.” In the hall, Yang Kai suddenly beckoned to Zhu Qing with a smile.

Zhu Qing shot him a look of disdain, then casually tossed the wooden box to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai caught it, opened it, and immediately clicked his tongue in amazement.

"The Dragon Blood Flower is one of the world’s most extraordinary spirit flowers. Only by watering it with the fresh blood of the Dragon Clan can it be cultivated. It can only be found on Dragon Isle," Ji Ying said from the side.

“Watered with dragon blood?” Yang Kai’s eyes lit up at the words.

Dragon blood itself was a rare treasure, almost impossible for ordinary people to see. Anything cultivated with dragon blood couldn’t possibly be ordinary. No wonder the Dragon Blood Flower was so full of Spiritual Qi and even carried a trace of dragon aura.

He didn’t know exactly what kind of Spiritual Pill could be refined from the Dragon Blood Flower, but he instinctively sensed it would be extremely useful.

“Can it be used for alchemy?” Yang Kai looked up at Ji Ying.

Ji Ying smiled, “Of course. Pills refined with the Dragon Blood Flower as the main ingredient are called Dragon Blood Pills. If the Dragon Clan or those with Dragon Clan bloodline take them, it can strengthen and temper their bloodline. Even ordinary people can benefit greatly, especially in tempering the body.”

## Chapter 2686 — Random Bidding

"Ten million!" A sudden voice rang out.

The raucous auction hall instantly fell silent. Countless people turned to look at Room One, their eyes twitching uncontrollably.

Of course, they all recognized the voice that had called out ten million as Yang Kai, and their hearts were instantly filled with hatred.

This price jump was terrifying—straight from three million to ten million, more than tripling it. Even these Emperor Realm experts were left shocked by such a leap.

Still, to be fair, a Heavenly Puppet really was worth that much. Ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals was no small sum, but if you could bring a Heavenly Puppet home, it was worth it. You could only say that the kid in Room One was decisive, scaring off most of the opportunistic competitors with a single high bid.

After all, an auction is a battle of wits and courage. If you can't handle the new price, you can only give up.

"Damn it!" Wu Ming cursed bitterly from behind the high platform.

Others thought Yang Kai was being extravagant and determined to win the Heavenly Puppet, but he alone knew Yang Kai's real plan. For Yang Kai, the bidding price was just a number—he never intended to pay any Source Crystals. Ten million, a hundred million, he could call out any figure he wanted.

Thinking of this, Wu Ming's heart skipped a beat, and cold sweat broke out on his forehead.

This brat... could he really call out a hundred million next?

"What a bold move, boy!" The old man in Room Seven shouted, his voice tinged with anger. He secretly resented Yang Kai for not understanding the rules—who drives up the price like this at an auction? When bidders fight each other, only the host profits. If Yang Kai hadn't already bid on two Heavenly Puppets, he would have suspected him of being a shill.

"Ten million Upper-Level Source Crystals to buy a Heavenly Puppet? Boy, your calculations are clever. Too bad you ran into me." The old man in Room Seven sneered and raised the bid: "Eleven million."

This price was nearly his limit, but Yang Kai's actions were so outrageous that he couldn't swallow his anger. He secretly resolved to fight him to the end, to show him that age brings cunning.

"One hundred million!"

Yang Kai sat in his chair, resting his cheek on his hand, and took a leisurely sip of tea.

"Pfft..." Ye Qinghan couldn't help herself and nearly burst out laughing.

By now, she could clearly see Yang Kai's intentions. This auction was a complete joke to him. Ten million, a hundred million, a billion, even ten billion—it made no difference. He was just tossing out numbers, never intending to pay.

Ye Hen and Du Xian also wore strange expressions, quietly wiping the sweat from their foreheads.

Backstage, Wu Ming's face was pitch black. He thought, "Of course, what you fear most always happens." The brat really did call out a hundred million.

Meanwhile, in the other private rooms, there was a constant sound of people sucking in cold breaths. In the main hall, countless eyes focused on Room One. Everyone was dumbfounded, their faces full of shock.

"Did I hear that right? A hundred million? Was it Lower-Level Source Crystals or Upper-Level?"

"I must be dreaming. This has to be a dream."

"That kid's crazy. Does he have so many Source Crystals he can't spend them all? If he can't, give me some! Why be such a sucker?"

"Young Master, this humble girl is willing to serve you... My chest is big, soft, and fair—you won't be disappointed..."

...

The hall was in an uproar. Everyone was stunned by Yang Kai's hundred-million bid. That number was like a thunderclap, leaving them dizzy and dazed.

"You, you, you..." In Room Seven, the old man was momentarily stunned, then flew into a rage. He slammed his chair so hard it rattled. "Boy, you're going too far!"

Yang Kai curled his lip and glanced indifferently at Room Seven. "It's an auction. The one with the money wins. How am I going too far?"

"You're just making random bids—this is outrageous." He didn't believe for a second that Yang Kai would really spend a hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals on a Heavenly Puppet. At most, a Heavenly Puppet was worth ten or twenty million. A hundred million? Even first-tier Sects might not be able to come up with that many Source Crystals.

Yang Kai snorted, "If you can't play, then don't. At your age, you should find a place to retire instead of making a fool of yourself."

"Infuriating!" The old man in Room Seven was livid. He turned to Hua Qingsi on the stage and shouted, "Madam, does this auction allow people to mess around and make random bids? Isn't there anyone to keep order?"

This was the Qiyao Commerce Guild's branch, and their reputation was well known. The old man assumed Hua Qingsi was with the Guild, so he asked her directly.

Hua Qingsi glanced coolly at Room Seven. "His bidding is his business. I haven't seen him causing any trouble. It's you who won't stop complaining."

The old man was taken aback, then remembered that the kid in Room One seemed to know the auctioneer well. Before the auction started, the two had been whispering on stage. Of course she'd take his side and ignore an outsider like him.

"A nest of snakes and rats, all in it together!" The old man flicked his sleeve, deeply disappointed in the Qiyao Commerce Guild, but still said, "I don't believe he can produce a hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals. He's just making random bids."

If anyone could produce a hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals, they wouldn't be called Upper-Level anymore. The old man had nearly emptied his entire Sect to bring less than twenty million. Yang Kai was just a First Stage of the Emperor Realm, how could he have such wealth?

"Old Zhou is right! That kid's just here to cause trouble—throw him out!" someone shouted from Room Thirteen, another Emperor Realm expert.

"Throw him out! He shouldn't even be allowed to participate. He already snatched up two bargains. What's the Qiyao Commerce Guild doing?"

For a moment, the crowd was in an uproar, all demanding Yang Kai be thrown out of the auction.

The main reason was that hundred-million bid was just too frightening. This was only the third auction item, and there were more Heavenly Puppets to come, even a rumored Star Lord. If he kept driving up the price like this, who could compete?

Even for their own interests, they couldn't let him stay any longer.

Two or three dozen Emperor Realm experts shouted together, their Emperor Intent pressing from every private room, as if a fight could break out at any moment. The martial artists in the hall turned pale with fear.

Hua Qingsi stood on the high platform, frowning deeply. If it were just one or two people, she could ignore them—after all, the auction was just a front. But with so many Emperor Realm experts causing trouble, she didn't know what to do. If she handled it poorly, it could bring big trouble for Yang Kai.

Wu Ming stood backstage, grinning viciously, secretly delighted. He had no intention of stopping it. The more hatred Yang Kai attracted, the better for his plans.

"All of you, shut up!" Yang Kai shouted from Room One, digging at his ear.

The noise from all directions was giving him a headache.

"You still dare to shout, boy!" The old man in Room Seven seemed to have it out for Yang Kai. Now that he had the upper hand, he looked down on Yang Kai even more. "If you know what's good for you, get out. If not, these fellow cultivators will teach you a lesson."

As soon as he finished speaking, there was a blur, and suddenly a figure appeared in the air above the auction hall—it was Yang Kai.

"You... what are you doing!" Even hiding in Room Seven, the old man was startled. He hadn't noticed at all how Yang Kai had appeared.

Those eyes looked over, and the walls and formations seemed to vanish, piercing straight into his heart.

Calmly, Yang Kai glanced at Room Seven, then swept his eyes around. “You bunch of trash,” he said unhurriedly. “You call this Young Master’s bid reckless because you think I can’t produce one hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals. Fine—if I can, what then?”

"Outrageous!"

"Arrogant!"

"I'm so angry I could die! Insolent brat!"

The Emperor Realm experts present might not be the most powerful, but each was a pillar of their Sect, with high status. Now, being called trash by Yang Kai, the crowd was instantly enraged.

"If you can produce it, I'll eat three catties of shit!"

A voice drifted through the hall, its source impossible to pinpoint—clearly someone shouting recklessly, not daring to reveal their identity.

But Yang Kai locked onto Room Thirteen and exclaimed, "Friend, you have quite the taste. Impressive!"

In Room Thirteen, a middle-aged man turned pale. He hadn't expected that even after using a secret technique, Yang Kai would still find him. He regretted speaking up—if he'd known, he would've kept quiet.

Mostly because Yang Kai rubbed him the wrong way, and on instinct he wanted to needle him a bit.

The others all looked toward Room Thirteen, their expressions varied.

"Very well! Since you have such ambition, I'll make sure to satisfy you," Yang Kai clapped his hands, then turned to Room Seven. "And you, old man?"

“You, of all people?” the old man sneered, clearly not believing Yang Kai could really produce that many Source Crystals.

Yang Kai snorted, "Yes, me. Enough with the empty talk—if you have the guts, show me something real!"

He'd come here for two reasons: to rescue Chi Yue and the others, and to recover the Thousand Leaves Sect's Heavenly Puppets. If anyone dared to stand in his way, he wouldn't be polite.

The old man said, "If you can do it, I'll let you use my head as a ball."

The kid was just a First Stage Emperor. Even if he could really produce a hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals, the old man wasn't afraid. At worst, he'd just fight him and show him there are always higher mountains and stronger people.

"Excellent." Yang Kai grinned viciously. "I'll be taking your head, then."

As he spoke, he turned and flew toward the high platform where Hua Qingsi stood. Landing, he looked around and shouted, "Open your eyes and see for yourselves—am I just making random bids or not!"

As soon as he finished, he waved his hand, and a small mountain of Source Crystals appeared on the platform. Each crystal glowed with a milky white light, and the surging Spiritual Qi instantly filled the entire auction hall, making the air thick with energy.

## Chapter 2727 — Maidservant

A flash of red light suddenly shot out from within Lingxiao Palace, streaking straight through the Grand Guardian Array.

When it reached the front of the Multi-deck Ship, the red light paused, revealing a petite and delicate figure—it was Zhu Qing, who had stormed off in a huff.

In truth, she was now regretting her actions terribly. After falling out with Yang Kai just now, she had stormed off in anger, completely forgetting the task the Elder had entrusted to her. In her heart, she cursed Yang Kai for not stopping or trying to keep her, leaving her in this awkward situation.

Suddenly noticing a Multi-deck Ship stopped outside the mountain gate, she naturally glanced over curiously.

Her gaze swept past Mi Qi and Ji Ying, but lingered for a moment on Li Jiao. The corner of her mouth curled slightly, as if in disdain.

For some inexplicable reason, Li Jiao’s heart tightened, and he felt as if he were facing a formidable enemy. It was as though this petite woman could end his life with a casual wave of her hand.

He was shocked, wondering how he could feel this way. He was already a Third Stage Emperor, and in this world, only a Great Emperor could kill him so easily.

Could this woman before him be a Great Emperor?

That couldn’t be. He’d never heard of a Great Emperor like this. Even though the Flower Shadow Great Emperor was a woman, she was nothing like this one.

What was going on? For a moment, Li Jiao couldn’t help but feel suspicious, wondering if he was just too nervous and imagining things.

“It’s you!” Li Xuan cried out, pointing at Zhu Qing, his expression turning red with hatred at the sight of an enemy.

“Who is she?” Ji Ying asked with a frown.

This woman had flown out from Lingxiao Palace, so she should be one of their people. Could she be the Palace Master? But that couldn’t be right—wasn’t the Palace Master supposed to be a man named Yang Kai?

“Master, it’s her! She and the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace beat me up. Your disciple has suffered so much…” Li Xuan began to cry.

“She attacked you too?” Ji Ying’s face darkened.

“That’s right, she was the first to hit me! She’s in the Emperor Realm, and I’m only at the First Stage of the Dao Origin Realm. I had no way to fight back at all.” Li Xuan sobbed, tears and snot running down his face, looking utterly miserable.

“Shameless!” Ji Ying cursed angrily. At first, he’d found this woman’s beauty rather striking and had a bit of goodwill toward her. But after hearing Li Xuan’s words, that goodwill vanished instantly, and he now found her utterly detestable.

“To think that someone in the Emperor Realm would bully the weak—what a disgrace to us Emperors!” Mi Qi shouted angrily, surging his Emperor Yuan. “Let this Lord teach you a lesson and seek justice for Junior Nephew Li!”

As soon as he finished speaking, he spread his arms like a great roc and lunged at Zhu Qing.

Ji Ying’s expression shifted, as if he wanted to stop Mi Qi’s rashness, but after a moment’s thought, he gave up and stood there with a gloomy face.

A little lesson wouldn’t hurt—Lingxiao Palace was getting too arrogant. He wasn’t worried that Mi Qi would lose; after all, Mi Qi was a Third Stage Emperor. No matter how strong this woman was, she couldn’t possibly defeat him.

Zhu Qing’s delicate brows knitted together, and her pent-up anger erupted in an instant.

She had already been wronged by Yang Kai, and now that Mi Qi was coming at her, it gave her the perfect outlet. She raised her hand, clenched her fist, and punched fiercely at Mi Qi’s chest.

Mi Qi’s hands danced as he formed a great seal, sneering, “A mere speck of light dares to compete with the sun!”

He thrust his hands forward, Emperor Intent surging. His move was fierce, as if he intended to suppress Zhu Qing in one blow.

But her pink fist shot forward, overwhelming everything in its path, scattering Mi Qi’s Emperor Intent into chaos. A vortex of force appeared, like a black hole devouring all, and the great seal at his chest shattered instantly.

“What?” Mi Qi’s face changed dramatically, a wave of terror rising from deep within. Only now did he realize he had underestimated his opponent. Even though this woman hadn’t used her Emperor Yuan, her strength was simply unimaginable. By the time he tried to change his move, it was too late—he could only desperately surge his Emperor Yuan to defend himself.

A thunderous boom resounded…

The petite pink fist smashed into Mi Qi’s chest. Visibly, his chest caved in, and those standing behind him, like Ji Ying, could clearly see his back bulge outward as if he’d grown a hump. At that spot, a blast of air exploded, tearing a huge hole in his clothes.

Mi Qi, Sect Master of the Mitian Sect, a top sect in the Northern Territory and a Third Stage Emperor expert, was sent flying, crashing into the Multi-deck Ship’s barrier and sticking to it in midair, arms and legs spread wide.

“Hiss…” A chorus of sharp intakes of breath rang out.

Everyone’s eyes were filled with shock.

Although Mi Qi had been careless, the fact that she could do this to him with a single punch left no doubt about her terrifying strength.

Li Xuan, who had been excitedly watching, hoping Mi Qi would avenge him, now felt as if an invisible hand was gripping his throat. His eyes bulged, his whole body trembled, and he was filled with dread.

A few days ago, Zhu Qing had only slapped him, and he’d been fine.

If she’d used this kind of strength back then, he’d be dead by now…

Cold sweat broke out all over his body.

After a long daze, Ji Ying finally remembered to withdraw the Multi-deck Ship’s barrier and let Mi Qi back in.

“I really underestimated you… ugh…” Mi Qi landed on the deck, trying to say something to save face, but halfway through, he coughed up a mouthful of blood, his face pale as paper.

Ji Ying was shocked and quickly gave him a Spiritual Pill.

After punching Mi Qi, Zhu Qing felt much calmer. She looked coldly ahead and asked, “Who are you people?”

Ji Ying’s face darkened, thinking she was deliberately trying to humiliate him. After all, he’d already introduced himself to the Chief Steward. Since she’d come from Lingxiao Palace, how could she not know who he was?

This was clearly feigning ignorance.

Had Lingxiao Palace really become this arrogant?

“Qing’er, don’t be rude!” A loud voice called from afar, and as it fell, Yang Kai and Hua Qingsi arrived together.

Zhu Qing glared at him, then turned her gaze away.

Don’t think I’ll forgive you just because you called me Qing’er!

“Master Ji, you’ve come such a long way, and I failed to greet you properly at the gate—please forgive my discourtesy!” Yang Kai said with a cheerful smile, cupping his fists in greeting. His tone was calm and composed, placing himself on equal footing with Ji Ying.

On the way over, he’d already learned of their identities from Hua Qingsi.

Ji Ying was the fifth disciple under Great Emperor Miaodan, an Imperial-Grade Pill Master.

Ji Ying replied calmly, “Palace Master Yang is too polite. I came uninvited and disturbed your Palace.”

Though he was annoyed at how long it had taken Yang Kai to arrive, he couldn’t lash out at someone who greeted him with a smile.

Yang Kai laughed, “I’ve long heard of Master Ji’s great name. Your visit brings honor to Lingxiao Palace—how could it be a disturbance?”

Li Jiao, standing to the side, said darkly, “Palace Master Yang, Master Ji has come all this way, yet you keep him outside the Grand Guardian Array. Is this how Lingxiao Palace treats its guests?”

“And you are…?” Yang Kai glanced at Li Jiao.

Ji Ying said, “This is Li Jiao, Palace Master of Li Dragon Palace!”

“So you’re Palace Master Li… and who’s the one over there coughing up blood?” Yang Kai looked at Mi Qi.

Mi Qi, having just taken a Spiritual Pill to suppress his churning qi and blood, nearly spat out another mouthful of blood at those words.

Ji Ying said, “That is Mi Qi, Sect Master of the Mitian Sect.”

Yang Kai narrowed his eyes. “Sect Master Mi is here as well.”

Li Jiao snorted coldly, “What, Palace Master Yang, are we not welcome here?”

Yang Kai stroked his chin, thought for a moment, and said seriously, “Not really, no.”

This was ridiculous. The two of them had clearly come looking for trouble, yet they were asking if they were welcome. Were they out of their minds?

Li Jiao nearly choked, his face turning ugly. With their status and strength, even if they disliked someone, they’d still put on a show of politeness. If Yang Kai visited Li Dragon Palace, even if he wasn’t welcome, they’d at least pretend. That was basic courtesy. But Yang Kai was so blunt.

Yang Kai chuckled, “But since you’re already here, come on in.”

As he spoke, he opened the Grand Guardian Array.

He looked as if he was doing it very reluctantly.

Ji Ying put away the Multi-deck Ship and led everyone into Lingxiao Palace.

“Everyone, follow me,” Yang Kai said, turning to lead the way. After flying a short distance, he called back, “Qing’er, aren’t you coming in?”

Zhu Qing had been standing there, full of resentment, waiting for Yang Kai to apologize. She’d decided that as long as he said a soft word, she’d forgive his earlier rudeness. But no matter how long she waited, he didn’t even glance at her.

She couldn’t help but feel a little sad.

Was she really just going to leave like this? What about the Elder’s instructions? What about the Ancestral Dragon Origin?

At that moment, hearing Yang Kai call out, she quickly turned and darted into Lingxiao Palace, trailing behind him, pouting and muttering under her breath as she stared at his back.

Judging by her lips, she seemed to be cursing him.

“Palace Master Yang, who is this woman?” Li Jiao asked in a low voice.

For some reason, having Zhu Qing following behind made him feel extremely uneasy, as if the beautiful woman behind him was actually a ferocious beast ready to devour him. The sensation sent chills down his spine.

“Oh, just a maidservant. Palace Master Li, you’re overthinking it,” Yang Kai replied offhandedly.

“A maidservant…” Li Jiao was speechless.

“Palace Master Yang, are you trying to humiliate me?” Mi Qi nearly choked, furious. With such strength, how could she possibly be a maidservant? She could start her own sect!

If she was a maidservant, then what did that make him?

“Why would I humiliate you? We bear no enmity!” Yang Kai bared his teeth in a grin. “If there really were a grudge, I wouldn’t waste time humiliating you—I’d just kill you. Wouldn’t that be much more gratifying?”

Mi Qi had no idea what he meant and quickly shut his mouth. Yang Kai was clearly not someone to mess with. He instinctively felt that coming here with Ji Ying had been a mistake—his reputation might be ruined here.

“Qing’er, go serve our guests some tea,” Yang Kai called back to Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing’s face darkened, and she glared at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai glared right back.

“Yes!” Zhu Qing had no choice but to comply, turning and flying off in another direction.

“I’ll help you!” Hua Qingsi hurried after her.

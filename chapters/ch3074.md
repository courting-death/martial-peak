## Chapter 3074 — Luring the Tiger Away from the Mountain

Yang Kai's expression was ashen as he looked at Zhu Yan. "What if I refuse?"

There were only two paths before him now. One was to accept Zhu Yan's proposal and go with him, which amounted to being kept under watch. The other was to fight Zhu Yan here and now and settle the problem with force.

However, he reckoned that would not end well for him.

He was no match even for Fu Zhun, much less Zhu Yan, who ranked above her.

This was the Great Elder of the Dragon Clan. Even if Yang Kai used every means at his disposal, he likely was not qualified to contend with him.

Zhu Yan slowly shook his head. "I advise you to agree. This old man has no wish to bully the young."

Yang Kai felt stifled, his mouth full of bitterness.

That said, Li Jiao was now in their hands, so Yang Kai could not simply abandon him. Whether out of sentiment or reason, he had to go with them and see how matters turned out.

"Come with us, young man. Mo Huang will look after you."

Fu Xuan suddenly spoke, making the corner of Mo Huang's eye twitch involuntarily.

With matters having come this far, Yang Kai naturally made his decision. He nodded lightly and said, "Many thanks, Third Elder. Then let us go and take a look together."

Fu Xuan smiled faintly.

Zhu Yan turned to Mo Huang and said, “Shouldn’t we have them stop now?”

Mo Huang said nothing. He merely threw out a casual punch. Without a sound, a layer of ripples spread through the void like waves upon water.

The next instant, the sound of something shattering rang out, and two figures abruptly appeared. They were none other than Li Wuyi and Zhu Kong. Neither seemed to have suffered any serious harm, and no one knew what kind of battle they had fought before. At the very least, it was impossible to tell from their appearances who was stronger and who was weaker.

With the Space Secret Technique broken, Li Wuyi and Zhu Kong also drew apart. Both looked suspiciously toward Mo Huang and Zhu Yan, not knowing what had happened outside.

"We'll talk on the way." Zhu Yan turned and flew off. Zhu Kong frowned, then hurried after him.

"Let's go." Mo Huang called out as well.

The large group flew toward the Spirit Island in a grand procession.

Midway there, Zhu Kong and Li Wuyi were evidently told what had happened. Zhu Kong's expression immediately turned ugly. He had never imagined that in less than two hours, a member of the Dragon Clan would actually perish on Dragon Isle, much less that the matter would involve the infamous Heaven Devourer Great Emperor.

Li Wuyi also looked shocked. He and Jiu Feng huddled together, whispering incessantly.

The Spirit Island was brimming with Spiritual Qi.

Some traces of a great battle remained, but they had all been left behind by Li Jiao's fight with Fu Chi.

When the group arrived, many members of the Dragon Clan were already investigating the scene. Li Jiao stood trembling in the middle of several Dragon Clan members, a bitter expression on his face.

"What did you find?" Zhu Yan flew over and landed beside Fu Zhun.

"Fu Chi did indeed die here."

"Where is Wu Kuang?"

"Nowhere to be seen."

Zhu Yan nodded. Having slaughtered a member of the Dragon Clan, Wu Kuang naturally could not have remained here. He had surely fled to some unknown place long ago.

"Find him." Zhu Yan coldly snorted, thoroughly enraged. He had not actually been too angry when Yang Kai disrupted the wedding or when Mo Huang forced his way into the Dragon Tomb and brought Fu Xuan out, but Wu Kuang's actions had clearly touched the reverse scale of the entire Dragon Clan.

The crime of slaying a Dragon had to be repaid in blood!

Fu Zhun nodded. As her red lips opened and closed, the abstruse sounds of Dragon Language rang out, while her hands rapidly changed seals. She was clearly using some kind of Secret Technique to follow the traces left here and track down Wu Kuang.

Fu Chi had only just died. No matter how capable Wu Kuang was, he could not have escaped Dragon Isle. In other words, he was definitely still here, merely hidden away.

While Fu Zhun used her Secret Technique to search for Wu Kuang, Mo Huang did not remain idle either.

Although many grudges entangled him with Dragon Isle, to the point that he would have been delighted to see every member of the Dragon Clan dead, he could not stand aside when the matter involved the Heaven Devourer Great Emperor.

If Heaven Devourer escaped, the entire Star Realm would likely know no peace from then on. Even if his Spirit Beast Island kept to its own secluded corner, it could not remain untouched.

No one knew better than these Great Emperors how terrifying Wu Kuang was. His very existence was a blight upon the entire world.

A small, snow-white beast darted out of Mo Huang's sleeve. Resembling both a marten and a fox, it was of some unknown species. It was about one chi long, its fur gleaming pure white, while its two eyes rolled about with human-like intelligence.

After landing on the ground, it began sniffing continuously.

Seeing this, Zhu Yan made no move to stop it. Instead, he began talking with Mo Huang. "Why does Brother Mo think Wu Kuang infiltrated Dragon Isle and even went so far as to expose his identity to slaughter one of my Dragon Clan?"

The two of them had still been locked in fierce battle moments ago, yet in the blink of an eye, they seemed like old friends of many years. There was nothing strange about it; on the contrary, it seemed perfectly natural.

Mo Huang said indifferently, "I am not Wu Kuang, so how could I know what scheme he is plotting? Great Elder has asked the wrong person."

He truly could not make sense of it. Logically speaking, Wu Kuang was infamous and hunted by all throughout the Star Realm. Having finally escaped death and gained control of Duan Hongchen's body, his best course should naturally have been to hide. Given the wonders of his Heaven Devouring Battle Art, it would likely take him no more than a few hundred years to recover to his former peak.

Once he emerged again after those few hundred years, he would once more be the strongest person in the Star Realm. Unless another War of the Great Emperors occurred, no one in the world would be able to restrain him.

Zhu Yan nodded faintly and said no more.

As they spoke, Yang Kai and the others also arrived. When the members of the Dragon Clan saw him, they all glared at him furiously.

This was especially true of those who had fought Yang Kai earlier.

Seven Dragons against one should have been a certain victory, allowing them to suppress Yang Kai so thoroughly that he could not even raise his head. In the end, however, Yang Kai had broken the Dragon Temple's protective technique, leaving every one of them battered and humiliated, their dignity utterly lost. Seeing him again now, none of them looked upon him kindly.

Yang Kai glared back at them. Defeated generals dared to flaunt their courage?

After their gazes clashed for a while, the many members of the Dragon Clan sheepishly looked away.

Yang Kai immediately became smug, looking around with a heroic bearing, only to happen upon Fu Xuan watching him with a beaming smile. He instantly grew embarrassed, lightly coughed, and said, “I have a feeling Wu Kuang is desperately trying to recover his strength.”

He had overheard a few words of Mo Huang and Zhu Yan's conversation just now, so his remark did not come entirely out of nowhere.

"Naturally, he wants to increase his strength." Zhu Yan nodded. Wu Kuang had once stood at the very summit of the Star Realm, looking down upon all living beings. Even the many Great Emperors had been so suppressed by him that they could not raise their heads. Then his cultivation suddenly plummeted a thousand zhang. How could he possibly resign himself to obscurity and patient concealment?

It was only natural that he wanted to increase his strength.

Yang Kai said, "When Wu Kuang and Senior Duan emerged from the Shattered Star Sea, his cultivation was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. When I saw him a month ago, he had already reached the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, and a month later, he was already at the Third Stage. The speed of his cultivation is simply inconceivable. He did not hesitate to expose his identity to slaughter a member of the Dragon Clan. Perhaps there is something on Dragon Isle that can help him rapidly increase his strength, such as... Fu Chi's Dragon Clan Origin Power."

A Dragon Clan Origin was an extraordinarily powerful force, so its attraction to Wu Kuang could hardly be small.

As soon as these words were spoken, Zhu Yan's expression suddenly changed, and a horrifying thought involuntarily surfaced in his mind.

He abruptly raised his head and shouted in a low voice, "The Dragon Tomb!"

Mo Huang's expression also sank. "Luring the tiger away from the mountain."

If Wu Kuang's true target was the Dragon Tomb, then everything made sense. The Dragon Tomb contained the Origin Power of many deceased Dragon Clan members. If he could completely devour all the Origin Power within it, Wu Kuang's cultivation would certainly increase tremendously. He already possessed the foundation of a Great Emperor and occupied Duan Hongchen's body, so there was no such thing as a bottleneck for him. Given the wonders of the Heaven Devouring Battle Art, as long as he had enough resources, he could easily raise his strength to a terrifying degree.

Even if he remained inferior to a Great Emperor, no ordinary Third Stage Emperor could compare with him.

Dragon Clan Origins were exceedingly rare, and the energy they contained surpassed imagination. If Wu Kuang's true target was the Dragon Tomb, then they were in grave trouble.

The reason he had exposed his identity and slaughtered Fu Chi should have been to lure the tiger away from the mountain, drawing Zhu Yan, Mo Huang, and the others over here. After all, those supreme experts had all been near the Dragon Tomb at the time. No matter how foolish Wu Kuang was, he could not possibly have walked into their trap.

Everything made sense now.

At that moment, Fu Zhun also stopped casting her technique and looked toward the Dragon Tomb. The strange little beast Mo Huang had released likewise growled in that direction.

Seeing this, everyone hesitated no longer and rushed toward the Dragon Tomb. For a time, streak after streak of light crossed the sky, each radiating a vast and astonishing momentum.

If anyone else saw such a lineup, they would likely be horrified.

The Dragon Tomb was extremely far from this place. Even after everyone flew at full speed for an hour, they still had not arrived.

Yang Kai’s expression remained calm, but inwardly he was deeply worried—not for himself, but for Duan Hongchen.

He was now an Elder of the Qingyang Shrine, and Duan Hongchen had innumerable ties to the Qingyang Shrine, so there was also some connection between them. Furthermore, Yang Kai had once received Duan Hongchen's kindness and deeply admired his resolve to sacrifice himself for the greater good, going so far as to cripple his own cultivation to venture deep into the Shattered Star Sea.

No matter how he looked at it, he did not want anything to happen to Duan Hongchen.

But as things stood, Duan Hongchen had already been completely suppressed by Wu Kuang and could not even easily regain control of his own body. His situation could hardly have been worse.

Wu Kuang could already accomplish this much before restoring his strength. If he were allowed to grow any stronger, how could Duan Hongchen possibly survive? Yang Kai feared that, just as Wu Kuang had said, even the Mortal Great Emperor would truly be devoured.

At that point, the fall of a Great Emperor would be a loss to the entire Star Realm.

After another short while, the Dragon Tomb came into view in the distance. It was a dim place that seemed cut off from this world, and merely looking at it made one feel extremely oppressed.

Zhu Yan and the others suddenly narrowed their eyes, staring blankly at a patch of void before the Dragon Tomb.

"Wu Kuang!"

A figure stood tall in the void. He had a burly build, and his long robes fluttered without wind. A faint smile hung on his face as he looked toward them, his expression utterly calm.

Who else could it be but Wu Kuang?

Every member of the Dragon Clan stared wide-eyed in that direction, wanting to see whether this overlord of the universe, whose mere mention still made people blanch even after he had vanished for countless years, truly had three heads and six arms.

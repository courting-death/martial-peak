## Chapter 2934 — Yet Another Wave

In fact, it could be said that after Guixu was injured and hid in the back mountain, he might have been waiting for his arrival.

The reason Guixu became so ferocious should be due to the secret technique Gong Yue used. Yang Kai still remembered that when he tracked Gong Yue through that fragment of Demonic Intent, on Spirit Sword Peak, Guixu even bit Gong Yue once.

Beneath Spirit Sword Peak lay the sealed demonic land, and the emergence of Guixu's bloodline was likely connected to this place.

Yang Kai possessed the Ancient Demon Black Eye and had Ancient Demonic Qi sealed within his body; Guixu had likely sensed this, which was why it had sought to draw close to him.

These thoughts flashed through his mind, and Yang Kai soon arrived before the Space Array. With the laws surging, he directly returned to the Northern Territory's Lingxiao Palace.

A moment later, light flashed atop the array, and two figures appeared.

"Palace Master, is this Qingyang Shrine?" Nanmen Dajun looked around curiously, clicking his tongue. "It’s not all that impressive, not as grand as our Lingxiao Palace. Hm, even the Spiritual Qi here seems inferior."

"Do more, talk less. Don’t you understand such a simple principle?" Yang Kai led the way ahead.

Nanmen Dajun chuckled, "Yes, yes, yes. Oh, right, Xiao Hou asked me to check with you—how’s that Secret Treasure for flight working? Any need for repairs?"

"No need, it’s working just fine."

"Also, Palace Master, that girl from Ice Heart Valley—what’s her name, Yao—she’s come by several times. Every time she came and didn’t see you, she just left. Is she your Palace Mistress?"

"You sure talk a lot, don’t you?"

"Alright, alright, I’ll stop." Nanmen Dajun covered his mouth, holding back for a while before suddenly saying, "But that girl really is quite nice."

"We’re here!" Yang Kai said as he descended.

The group below clearly noticed as well. When they looked up, they were all curious why Yang Kai had brought a stranger along.

Nanmen Dajun, however, was like a cat that had smelled fish, his expression excited as he stared at the entrance to the sealed land, eyes shining, pacing back and forth before the cave, growing more and more agitated.

After a while, he suddenly turned and said, "Palace Master, I’m going down. Wait for my good news."

He ignored everyone else and jumped straight down, quickly vanishing from sight.

The group looked at Yang Kai with curiosity.

Yang Kai said, "That’s the Northern Territory’s Imperial-Grade Formation Master, Nanmen Dajun. He happened to be nearby, so I asked him to help."

Wen Zishan looked at him with a smile, as if he had discovered something, but didn’t say it aloud.

As time passed, news kept coming from below. Nanmen Dajun truly lived up to his title as an Imperial-Grade Formation Master. After entering the sealed land, he quickly broke through many remaining formation restrictions, using his own strength to take control, earning the admiration of the Shrine’s Formation Masters.

The formation breaking lasted a full two days. During this time, people from various Sects of the Southern Territory kept arriving at Qingyang Shrine to inquire about the situation. The Shrine let them all in, and several elders took turns receiving them, explaining the matter of the sealed land and the breaking of the Demonic Intent’s restriction.

Many turned pale as they listened, deeply worried.

Two days later, over a hundred people had gathered beneath Spirit Sword Peak, almost all Emperor Realm experts, all waiting for news from inside the sealed cave.

Now that the Demonic Intent had escaped, tracking it was a huge problem. This was the first time in the Southern Territory’s history that such a thing had happened, so there was no experience to draw from. Everything depended on whether any clues were left inside the sealed cave; otherwise, it would be like searching for a needle in the sea.

There were no clues inside the sealed cave.

Nanmen Dajun and the shrine’s Formation Masters had broken all the restrictions inside, but still couldn’t find any information about the Demonic Intent. However, the formations they broke did yield some gains.

Perhaps in the future, if they needed to seal the Demonic Intent again, these would come in handy.

For the moment, everyone was filled with anxiety.

An emissary from the Star God Palace had arrived, with Great Elder Lei Hong personally leading the delegation. They brought the Great Emperor's demon-purging order, commanding all Sects and clan forces throughout the Southern Territory to thoroughly investigate their own disciples and members, lest anyone fall under the corruption of the Demonic Intent.

Lei Hong announced the Great Emperor’s order beneath Spirit Sword Peak, and naturally, everyone agreed without hesitation.

It had to be said, the Great Emperor’s demon-purging order gave the entire Southern Territory a backbone. The hearts of many experts were no longer as restless as before. After all, no matter what, the Southern Territory still had the Star God Palace and the Radiant Moon Great Emperor. No matter how sinister and fierce the Demonic Intent was, as long as the Great Emperor was present, it would never be able to stir up any real trouble.

After the order was given, the many Emperor Realm experts prepared to leave Qingyang Shrine and return to their own Sects to handle the matter.

Just then, a streak of red light suddenly shot in from outside, its aura fierce and overbearing, completely disregarding everyone. In the blink of an eye, it crossed over a hundred li and arrived before Spirit Sword Peak.

Having just experienced the Demonic Intent’s escape, the emotions of the many experts were still sensitive. Seeing someone barge into Qingyang Shrine so brazenly without any notice, they thought something had happened.

Startled, many instinctively launched attacks to intercept.

A few days ago, the scene of the shrine’s elders, along with Chen Wenhao and Feng Ming, joining forces to fight Gong Yue was already intense, but compared to what was happening now, it was nothing.

With over a hundred Emperor Realm experts gathered here, even if only a third of them attacked, the scene was still spectacular.

What’s more, far more than a third actually attacked.

Imperial Might spread out, beams of secret techniques flashed, and the power of one Imperial Treasure after another surged, all striking at the red light.

The red light charging in was clearly stunned, never expecting such a thing. It paused slightly in midair, but its surroundings were already enveloped by countless attacks.

Suddenly, a dragon’s roar exploded, the high-pitched sound making everyone’s eardrums tremble. All faces changed, for the sound was a pure dragon’s roar, not the cry of some Yao Beast.

The moment the dragon’s roar rang out, Yang Kai’s bloodline suddenly surged, as if drawn by an uncontrollable force. He hadn’t attacked just now, but now his eyes widened as he looked ahead.

The red light blazed brilliantly, and a colossal figure suddenly revealed itself in mid-air. Its body, stretching over twenty zhang in length, towered like a mountain. Dragon horns crowned its head, and four claws adorned its belly. Its entire form was draped in crimson dragon scales that shimmered like dancing flames. As its serpentine tail swept through the air, clouds and wind swirled in its wake.

"Hiss..."

Everyone gasped, staring blankly at the colossal figure in the sky, as if looking at a god high above. For a moment, many felt their limbs go weak.

Dragon Clan! The newcomer was actually a pure-blooded member of the Dragon Clan! No one had expected this.

Dragon Might spread out, clashing with the gathered Imperial Might of over a hundred Emperor Realm experts, shattering it to pieces.

With a crackling sound, the combined attacks of dozens of Emperor Realm experts struck the dragon’s body, making the giant dragon roar at the sky. Sparks flew from its blazing scales. The dragon’s body was tough—such attacks couldn’t take its life, but they still caused it great pain and fury.

The dragon raised its head high and spat out a breath of dragon fire.

Like a volcanic eruption, a scorching flame shot from the dragon’s mouth toward the Spirit Sword Peak. Even before the attack arrived, everyone felt their skin burning. That dragon flame seemed able to incinerate all under heaven—even Emperor Realm experts could sense mortal danger from it.

"Not good!" Yang Kai’s expression changed as he stepped forward, Elder Jade Token in hand. As Emperor Yuan surged, he rapidly formed seals.

The formation of the Spirit Sword Peak roared to life, instantly condensing a visible barrier in front of them.

This was a defense set up using the power of the entire spirit peak, but since it was activated in a rush, it couldn’t display its full strength.

The dragon flame struck the barrier, and the intense heat immediately warped it. Sizzling sounds rang out, and ripples spread across the transparent shield, as if it might melt at any moment.

Everyone was shocked, nearly scattering in panic. No one had the confidence to face the terrifying power of the dragon flame head-on.

Lei Hong stepped forward, throwing a punch that seemed ordinary and unremarkable.

Yet outside the barrier, a thunderous crack rang out. A bolt of lightning as thick as a bucket crashed into the flames, rampaging within and easing Yang Kai’s burden. This strike was the essence of Lei Hong’s cultivation—the strength of a veteran Third-Stage Emperor Realm expert was clear to see.

The giant dragon twisted its head, and the dragon flame grew even fiercer, as if it could melt the lightning.

"Enough!" Wen Zishan suddenly shouted, one hand behind his back holding a jade token, the other waving lightly. A beam of light like a silk ribbon slashed out, directly severing the dragon flame.

The giant dragon raised its head, circling in the sky, looking down on everyone. Its huge dragon eyes were full of furious flames, its mouth opening and closing as if it might spit out another breath of dragon fire at any moment.

Yang Kai withdrew the Spirit Sword Peak’s barrier and wiped the cold sweat from his forehead.

"State your identity and purpose, Dragon!" Wen Zishan looked coldly at the red dragon in the sky, sighing inwardly that Qingyang Shrine truly was beset by troubles—Demonic Intent breaking free, Hongchen trapped, Heaven Devourer escaping, and now a Dragon Clan member arriving. He could not understand why this year had been so ill-fated, one wave following another, leaving him almost overwhelmed.

However, before a pure-blooded member of the Dragon Clan, even Wen Zishan did not dare show the slightest carelessness. In this world, there was only one place where the Dragon Clan existed, and behind the red dragon before him stood a force that even Great Emperors could not ignore.

"Who hit me? Who hit me just now?" The red dragon didn’t answer Wen Zishan’s question, but instead roared, as if deeply humiliated and furious beyond measure. Its deafening roar made the entire Qingyang Shrine tremble.

Those Emperor Realm experts who had accidentally attacked the red dragon turned pale, shrinking into the crowd, afraid of being recognized.

"You, you, you!" The red dragon’s gaze swept over several Emperor Realm experts. "And you, don’t try to hide. I saw you all attack just now!"

"I... I didn’t, sir, you must be mistaken." The speaker shrank back even further, drenched in cold sweat.

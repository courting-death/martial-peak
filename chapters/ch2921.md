## Chapter 2921 — Two Matters

Within the Thousand Illusion Dreamscape, Yang Kai had witnessed far too many similar occurrences. On the battlefield where the two races slaughtered each other, even if a demon who had achieved mastery in cultivation was killed, a single strand of demonic intent could still demonize a member of the Barbarian Tribe, turning them against their own. The aura and remnant soul of the demons possessed an extremely powerful corrosive force.

"What do we do now?" Fang Zhuo also asked.

Before Yang Kai could respond, the howling Gong Yue suddenly fell silent. He jerked his head up, revealing a pair of pitch-black eyes, devoid of any whites, his appearance utterly terrifying.

"Family Head... Gong!" Shen Bingru called out in a trembling voice, hoping to confirm the identity of the person before her.

Gong Yue, however, paid her no heed. He only cocked his head, sizing up Yang Kai with a look of confusion and curiosity, as if waking from a long dream, still unable to make sense of his surroundings.

Yang Kai held his breath, quietly focusing his mind and remaining on guard.

Yet Gong Yue did not attack him. Instead, he let out a series of extremely bizarre sounds from his throat, then staggered unsteadily toward the exit.

Shen Bingru, Fang Zhuo, Hua Yulu, and the others hurriedly moved aside. None of them had the strength to fight, and they dared not block his path.

It was obvious that Gong Yue had been corrupted by demonic intent, for his current behavior was completely different from before. It was as if a new soul had taken up residence in his body, leaving him unable to fully control his movements—even his gait was abnormal.

In the utter silence, only Gong Yue’s footsteps rang out, piercingly loud as they faded into the distance.

Only after those footsteps could no longer be heard did Hua Yulu and the others exhale as if surviving a great calamity. Having witnessed Gong Yue’s transformation firsthand, they no longer doubted Yang Kai’s earlier explanation about demonic intent.

"Has the Family Head been possessed?" Fang Zhuo asked, looking at Yang Kai.

If that was the case, then things were troublesome. After all, Gong Yue was the former Family Head of the Gong family, and he possessed the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm. If such a person escaped, who knew what kind of disaster he might bring to the Southern Territory.

"I don’t know, but it might be even more serious than possession!" Yang Kai’s expression was grave. The only thing he was certain of was that Gong Yue had already entered a demonic state. There was one thing he couldn’t understand, though—even if Gong Yue’s mind was unsettled, he shouldn’t have been so easily corrupted by that demonic intent. Why was he unable to resist?

Yet what they had witnessed with their own eyes defied any reasonable explanation.

As he pondered, Yang Kai suddenly shuddered and shouted, "The Demon Script!"

"What?" Hua Yulu turned to look at him.

"That Demon Script character!" Yang Kai’s face was solemn as he turned to the others. "How much did you see?"

Hua Yulu said, "That secret art Yang Tai used before? I didn’t see it clearly."

Yang Kai gazed at the three of them and said gravely, "Regardless of how much you saw, it's best if you find a way to forget about it. Both Yang Tai's descent into a demonic state and Gong Yue's corruption may be connected to it."

"What do you mean by that?" Fang Zhuo asked curiously. When Yang Tai had performed that secret art earlier, he had paid special attention, so he saw more than Hua Yulu. Hearing Yang Kai speak so gravely, he dared not take it lightly.

Shen Bingru also cast a curious glance over.

Yang Kai said, "Yang Tai never had a chance to come into contact with that demonic intent, yet he was already in a demonic state when he arrived here. His transformation is very likely related to the ancient Demon Script character he learned. In order to persuade Gong Yue, Yang Tai once demonstrated the power of that single character to him. Gong Yue may have studied it a little. So... some demonic nature must have arisen within him as well. That’s why he dared to commit such a grave offense and join forces with Yang Tai to frame us. Yes, it must be so. Otherwise, as the former Family Head of the Gong family, how could Gong Yue have made such a muddle-headed choice? He was already influenced by the demonic nature lurking within him and could no longer maintain his true self. The reason the demonic intent was able to corrupt him so easily was not because his defenses were ineffective, but because the demonic nature within him was attracting that strand of demonic intent, unconsciously abandoning resistance!"

As he spoke, Yang Kai was half speculating, half explaining, but the more he said, the more convinced he became that his guess was correct.

The root of everything lay in that ancient Demon Script character.

Half a year ago, Hua Yulu, Yang Tai, and Wu Kuangyi discovered this ancient cave residence. Each of the three obtained different benefits, but the ancient Demon Script character Yang Tai learned was nothing but a trap—a lure to make him break the seal and release the demonic intent behind the door.

Anyone who learned that ancient Demon Script character would make the same choices as Yang Tai.

It was by relying on the true meaning of that Demon Script character that Yang Tai was able to establish a mysterious connection with the demonic intent behind the door. Only then did he know what was hidden there, and only then did he claim that opening the seal was his mission!

Hua Yulu listened, her face turning pale as she trembled uncontrollably.

She felt she was lucky. Last time, she had only obtained a small flower drum. If she had comprehended the true meaning of that ancient Demon Script like Yang Tai, she feared she would have shared his fate.

She was a woman who cherished her beauty. To die so miserably, with not even her bones remaining, and her mangled corpse used as a secret art to break the seal—she simply could not accept it.

Shen Bingru asked uneasily, "Will I also become like Yang Tai and enter a demonic state?"

She, too, had seen that ancient Demon Script.

Yang Kai shook his head. "The true meaning of that script is not so easily grasped. Yang Tai may have received some special help. Just seeing it once is of no consequence, but I still advise you to erase it from your memory completely. No one knows what mysterious power that script might hold."

The others all nodded. After exchanging glances, they sent their spirit bodies into their Seas of Consciousness, found the memory of that script, and destroyed it themselves.

In this way, they would forget the appearance of that character, remembering only that such a character existed. Destroying such memories would cause some damage to their divine souls, but compared to the risk of entering a demonic state, such damage was acceptable.

Yang Kai did not do this. While the others were erasing their memories, he remained deep in thought.

When the others finally opened their eyes, weak and exhausted, Yang Kai said, "There is likely more than one strand of demonic intent behind this door. We need to do two things now!"

Hearing that there might be more demonic intent behind the door, the others were startled. But thinking it over, it did seem possible.

After all, the restriction on the blood-red door had not been completely broken. Previously, it had only been opened a crack by Yang Tai’s blood sacrifice, allowing a single strand of demonic intent to escape. This restriction was set by an ancient master. Since it had closed again, it meant there were still hidden dangers inside.

"What two things?" Fang Zhuo asked. After all that had happened, Yang Kai had become the de facto leader of the group, and no one objected to his decisions.

"The first is to find someone to reinforce the restriction array here, to prevent anyone from accidentally breaking it again." Yang Kai raised a finger. "This concerns the entire Southern Territory, even the whole Star Realm, so we must notify several major sects in the Southern Territory and have them work together to find a solution. The Gong family needs to atone for their mistakes—they’re a good choice."

Gong Yue had made a mistake, and the destruction of the restriction was his doing. The task of repairing it fell squarely on the Gong family of Tianhe Valley. Even if Gong Yue had not caused trouble, the Gong family would still be the best choice. In the Southern Territory, aside from the array masters of the major sects, only they had the deepest knowledge of arrays.

Fang Zhuo and the others nodded slightly at Yang Kai’s words, feeling he was right. Although the exploration of the ancient cave residence had been their affair, the consequences were far beyond what they could bear.

Even Gong Yue, with his Second Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, could not resist the corruption of demonic intent. If the restriction here were truly broken, the Southern Territory would surely fall into chaos, and they would become eternal sinners.

"So I need you to go notify the Gong family of Tianhe Valley. First, to inform them of what happened here, and second, to have them guard against Gong Yue. If Gong Yue returns to Tianhe Valley... he probably won’t be so foolish, but it’s best to be prepared."

"I’ll go," Hua Yulu volunteered.

Although her cultivation was not high, she was still the Palace Master of Hundred Flowers Palace. Her words carried weight, and if she went to notify the Gong family, they would take it seriously.

"The three of you go together!" Yang Kai said solemnly.

The three thought it over and nodded.

They didn’t know where Gong Yue was now—he might be lying in ambush outside, waiting for them to walk into his trap. The three of them together would have a better chance of protecting themselves.

"Who will notify the other major sects?" Fang Zhuo asked in a deep voice. Without the leadership of those sects, nothing could be accomplished. Even if they brought the Gong family, the Gong family might seal off the news and not let it spread, so the strong experts of those sects had to be involved.

Yang Kai said, "Let me introduce myself again!"

As he spoke, he wiped his face, revealing his true appearance—pale as paper, without a trace of color.

"My name is Yang Kai!"

"Yang Kai!" Shen Bingru frowned in surprise. "I heard that Qingyang Shrine recently appointed a new Guest Elder..."

"That’s me," Yang Kai nodded.

Shen Bingru and Fang Zhuo straightened up respectfully and cupped their fists in unison. "So it’s Elder Yang. We were truly disrespectful."

Only at this moment did they no longer dare to underestimate Yang Kai. To become an Elder of a top Sect in the Southern Territory—even if only a Guest Elder—was by no means something an ordinary person could achieve. Moreover, it was said that at the investiture ceremony, this Yang Kai had even exchanged a move with an Elder of Star God Palace.

The elders of Star God Palace were at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. To cross hands with such a person was no easy feat.

Only now did the two realize why Yang Kai’s aura was not strong, yet he performed so brilliantly at every turn. It turned out he was an elder of Qingyang Shrine.

"I’ll notify the major sects. I have my own way to contact the Shrine. Once the news is out, they’ll naturally inform the other sects."

The three had no objections to this.

"And the second matter?" Hua Yulu asked.

"The second matter... let’s wait until this is settled. Before that, I’ll stay here to guard the restriction, in case Gong Yue tries to return."

"Brother Yang, your arrangements are thorough," Fang Zhuo said approvingly.

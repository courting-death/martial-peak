## Chapter 3136 — Refining the Origin

Ten li outside Yanghe City, Yang Kai sat in a pavilion and looked up at the clouds drifting and unfurling overhead.

A streak of white came flying over and landed before him, revealing Xu Binbai's vigorous figure.

Yanghe City had been retaken, and the Netherworld Sect's foundation on Cuiwei Star had been uprooted. A few fish had slipped through the net, but they no longer mattered. The gloom that had hung over Cuiwei Star for more than a dozen years had been swept away, and even the sky seemed much brighter.

Xu Binbai looked at the figure in the pavilion with a touch of awe, his thoughts surging as if he had returned to several dozen years ago.

Back then, he and Yang Kai had both taken part in the Blood Prison Trial. Back then, everyone had been in the Origin Returning Realm, and the gap between them had not been great. Xu Binbai had even thought himself a cut above the rest, for one simple reason: his Master was Wu Dao!

Wu Dao had been publicly acknowledged as the strongest expert in the Star Domain, of course, aside from that Starry Sky Emperor.

With such a Master sheltering him, Xu Binbai's future had seemed limitless, and the facts had borne that out. Several dozen years had passed, and he had already advanced to the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. His potential remained, and given enough time, reaching the Third Stage of the Void King Realm would be no problem.

But compared with the man before him, that small achievement was no more than a firefly's glow, too insignificant to mention.

In today's battle, Xu Binbai had not seen Yang Kai act, but every reinforcement Yang Kai had brought was unfathomable. Even that harmless-looking little girl could kill a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator in the snap of a finger.

Xu Binbai was certain now that Yang Kai's strength had long since passed beyond his ability to measure.

"Brother Yang!" Xu Binbai cupped his fists in salute. "Many thanks to Brother Yang for lending aid. That we were able to slay the Yellow Springs villains today is entirely thanks to Brother Yang. Please accept this bow from Xu."

He did not manage to bow. An invisible force held him up, and Yang Kai smiled. "With this, the two of us are even."

Xu Binbai looked at him in puzzlement.

Yang Kai said, "Back then, after I came out of the Blood Prison, if not for the word you spoke on my behalf, I fear I would not have left Cuiwei Star safely."

During the Blood Prison Trial, Yang Kai had already drawn the attention of Cuiwei Star's Star Lord, Luo Hai. When he emerged, he was stopped and questioned. It was precisely because Xu Binbai spoke up for Yang Kai that Luo Hai let the matter rest.

Xu Binbai's cultivation had not been high at the time, but the one standing behind him was Wu Dao. Luo Hai had no choice but to give him some face.

Though Luo Hai still chased him all the way afterward, even pursuing him to Chi Lan Star, without Xu Binbai's few words, Yang Kai would not have had time to flee. He might well have been captured by Luo Hai on Cuiwei Star. On Cuiwei Star, the might of a Star Lord was not something Yang Kai could resist back then.

Only then did Xu Binbai remember, and he laughed despite himself. "An old matter from years ago, not worth mentioning."

Who could have imagined back then that their positions would be reversed today?

"Sit." Yang Kai gestured with his hand.

Xu Binbai hesitated, then sat down with composure. When he raised his head and met Liu Yan's eyes, he still found it hard to believe. How had such a tiny little girl killed a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator? And from beginning to end, she had shown no emotion at all, as if she had merely crushed an ant in passing.

He shook his head and turned back to Yang Kai.

Before he could ask the question weighing most heavily on him, Yang Kai spoke first. "Senior Wu Dao has fallen."

Xu Binbai shuddered and swallowed hard.

Yang Kai said, "Senior fell beneath the might of Heaven and Earth. He did not die in battle against another person."

Of the few people who had gone to the Star Realm, only Wu Dao had died in a foreign land. According to Chi Yue and the others, when Wu Dao was breaking through from the Void King Realm to the Dao Origin Realm, he failed to withstand the Baptism of Heaven-and-Earth Energy and ultimately fell beneath Heavenly Might.

Yang Kai had not witnessed it himself, so he did not understand the details well enough to tell Xu Binbai much more. Still, Wu Dao's death was truly lamentable and made one sigh at the impermanence of the Heavenly Way. He had been the strongest expert in the Star Domain back then. Several people had gone to the Star Realm together, yet only he had fallen, while Chi Yue and the others had all advanced safely. Time and fate; who could explain such things clearly?

A heavy sorrow immediately settled over Xu Binbai, and the joy of returning from a great victory vanished without a trace. That old man had been both teacher and father to him, the most important person in his life. Without him, Xu Binbai could never have achieved what he had today.

But Xu Binbai had weathered great storms over the years, especially during the past dozen years, when he had grown accustomed to life and death and had even set aside concern for his own safety. He soon steadied himself, his eyes faintly red, and asked, "Can you tell me about it in detail?"

Yang Kai shook his head and explained why he could not.

Xu Binbai understood. "That Brother Yang could tell me this news at all is already enough for Xu to be endlessly grateful."

"Meeting you today was a matter of chance. Brother Xu, please restrain your grief and accept the change." After a pause, Yang Kai changed the subject. "Is the White Robe Sect yours?"

Xu Binbai took a deep breath and said, “That is right.” He had never intended to establish the White Robe Sect. Over the years, he had simply seen too much life and death, too many partings, and had acted out of righteousness several times, thereby earning himself some reputation. Some people had gathered around him, and under their efforts, using his name as a rallying banner, the White Robe Sect had come into being. In chaotic times, heroes emerged. Rather than saying that Xu Binbai and his companions had founded the White Robe Sect, it would be better to say that the current Cuiwei Star needed the White Robe Sect. Once the White Robe Sect appeared, it developed extremely rapidly and had now become the strongest force on Cuiwei Star resisting the Netherworld Sect. After today’s battle, the White Robe Sect would surely rise like the midday sun and truly take charge of everything on Cuiwei Star.

"Brother Yang, can you stay and relieve the suffering of Cuiwei Star's countless living beings?" Xu Binbai looked earnestly at Yang Kai. With him here, the damned Netherworld Sect would not be worth mentioning at all.

Yang Kai shook his head. "I am only passing through and cannot stay long."

Disappointment immediately showed on Xu Binbai's face.

Although today’s battle had damaged the Netherworld Sect’s vitality somewhat and uprooted its foundation on Cuiwei Star, no one could guarantee that the Netherworld Sect would not send even stronger cultivators here. If another Third Stage Void King Realm master really came, Xu Binbai, as he was now, would still be no match, and all their previous efforts would likely turn to foam.

But since matters had reached this point, he could not very well press Yang Kai any further. A real man relied on himself, not on pleading for another’s help. Although he was grateful to Yang Kai, he did not consider himself beneath him.

"However, I can linger here for two days," Yang Kai added.

Xu Binbai was speechless. Two days? What could that accomplish? Besides, if the Netherworld Sect or the cultivators of the Great Wilderness Star Domain truly wanted to make a comeback, they would not arrive so quickly.

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "Cuiwei Star's Stellar Origin has never been refined by anyone, has it?"

As soon as these words came out, Xu Binbai trembled violently. He stared at Yang Kai in disbelief and said in shock, "Brother Yang means..."

No way, right? That should be impossible, right? Even if Yang Kai's methods were extraordinary, he should not be able to do something like that.

"Come with me!" Yang Kai suddenly reached out and grabbed Xu Binbai by the shoulder. Before Xu Binbai could react, his body flickered, and he vanished.

Xu Binbai only felt the scenery before his eyes flash wildly. With his cultivation, he still could not see how Yang Kai was moving. He only knew that the smell of earth rushed at him from all around, and his vision had gone pitch-black. Instinct told him they had descended deep underground.

After barely a dozen breaths, they suddenly stopped.

Brilliant, multicolored light appeared before him.

Xu Binbai turned toward the source of the light, and his eyes narrowed.

He saw a mass of radiance slowly rippling there, dazzling with many colors, enough to make one dizzy and entranced. The moment he swept it with his Divine Sense, he sensed an extremely terrifying energy from within that radiance, and it also seemed to contain some matchlessly mysterious power.

"This is..." Xu Binbai had a guess, but he did not dare be certain.

"Cuiwei Star's Stellar Origin!" Yang Kai said.

As expected! Xu Binbai sucked in a cold breath and looked at Yang Kai uncertainly. "Brother Yang, you can't mean for me to refine it right now."

Yang Kai smiled faintly. "If you can refine it, you will become Cuiwei Star's master. With Cuiwei Star's aid, who would dare invade?"

"But I..."

"Calm your breathing, focus your mind, and comprehend it with your heart. This is the key to whether you can refine it!" Yang Kai suddenly reached out and tapped Xu Binbai's forehead. A vast amount of information immediately rushed into his mind, and passage after passage of text surfaced in his thoughts. Xu Binbai did not dare hesitate and quickly immersed himself in comprehension.

A short while later, Yang Kai withdrew his hand and stood quietly.

Xu Binbai, however, remained standing there without moving.

The Star Refining Art had been passed down. With this Secret Technique to assist him, Yang Kai believed it should not be difficult for Xu Binbai to refine Cuiwei Star's Stellar Origin. Of course, Yang Kai still had to watch over him from the side.

This was a Secret Technique obtained from Yang Yan's Imperial Garden. Yang Kai had relied on this Secret Art to refine Dark Star, and Xia Ningshang had relied on it to refine Tongxuan Continent. Its effects were powerful.

Moreover, Yang Kai had not only passed down the mnemonic chant of the Star Refining Art, but also his own comprehension and all its essentials, saving Xu Binbai a great deal of time.

Only after a full two hours did Xu Binbai open his eyes and look at Yang Kai with incomparable excitement.

"Begin," Yang Kai urged.

Xu Binbai nodded, then immediately sat cross-legged facing the Stellar Origin and circulated the Star Refining Art to communicate with it.

Points of light flew out like fireflies and drifted around Xu Binbai's body.

Yang Kai was left speechless. He had not expected Xu Binbai to succeed so easily.

After a moment's thought, however, it was not strange. Over the years, Cuiwei Star had suffered terribly under the cruelty of the Netherworld Sect's cultivators, and even its Spiritual Qi had been polluted. Xu Binbai, meanwhile, had always taken saving the common people as his duty, leading the White Robe Sect against the Netherworld Sect and acting in accord with the Heavenly Way.

A Stellar Origin was the will of an entire Cultivation Star. Although it was not a living thing, it could still sense certain things in the world.

By acting for the common people, Xu Binbai was acting for Cuiwei Star. It made sense that the Stellar Origin would be so close to him. The Heavenly Way was impermanent, yet Heaven's movement followed its own constancy.

Once he realized this, Yang Kai knew his earlier worries had been unnecessary. If anyone else had come to refine Cuiwei Star's Origin, it could never have gone this smoothly. Only Xu Binbai could do it.

Perhaps... before long, Cuiwei Star would give birth to another Star Lord. At that time, Cuiwei Star would have true power to protect itself and would no longer need to fear invasion and plunder by the Netherworld Sect.

Yang Kai had originally intended to leave, but after thinking it over, he walked to the side, took out all kinds of materials, and began making arrangements.

Setting up a Space Array here was a good choice. That way, the next time he wanted to come over, he would have a direct teleportation route.

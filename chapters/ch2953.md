## Chapter 2953 — Human Sovereign City

Yang Kai looked at them curiously. “You recognize Demonic Beasts?”

The man immediately shielded his wife behind him, looking at Yang Kai warily. “Are you a member of the Demon Race?”

“Hm?” Yang Kai was truly surprised this time. This guy could actually say the words ‘Demonic Beast’ and even knew about the Demon Race. He was clearly quite unusual.

The man became even more vigilant, gritting his teeth. “Did you come from Demon Fury City?”

Yang Kai shook his head. “I don’t know anything about Demon Fury City, and I’m not a member of the Demon Race. You must be mistaken…”

As he spoke, he suddenly frowned, because in that instant, he felt the world tremble. The laws of heaven and earth that filled the surroundings seemed to start twisting and changing. He glanced at Zhu Qing, who also showed a look of surprise.

The laws of heaven and earth really were beginning to change, and it had to be a tremendous transformation for such obvious signs to appear.

“Just kill us.” The man’s face was ashen, all desire to live gone. Steward Liao had been devoured by a Demonic Beast, and now two suspected members of the Demon Race had suddenly appeared before them, both powerful. They had no way out. He hugged his wife tightly, only wanting to enjoy a moment of warmth before death.

“If you want to die, it’s simple. I don’t need to do it.” Yang Kai stepped forward to stand before a certain array. There was nothing else in this cave, only this array standing there, making it especially conspicuous. He pointed at the array. “Can this send you to Human Sovereign City?”

The man looked over with dim eyes and nodded. “Yes.”

Yang Kai released his Divine Sense to investigate. “A Space Array, but it seems a bit different.”

The man said, “It’s useless. To activate this array, you need a Human Sovereign Seal.”

“Don’t you have a Human Sovereign Seal? Why don't you leave?” Yang Kai turned to look at them.

The man said, “It has to be the main seal from Steward Liao. Only the main seal can activate the array. The ones we have are just auxiliary seals, so we can’t activate it.”

“So that’s how it is!” Yang Kai nodded in understanding.

Seeing that he wasn’t making a move and was instead seriously studying the array, the man hesitated. “You really aren’t from the Demon Race?”

While examining the array, Yang Kai replied, “There’s a Demon Race in this world?”

The man said, "Yes, my Human Race and the Demon Race have stood in opposition, coexisting in this world for countless years. You don't seem to be of the Demon Race, but why do you have a Demonic Beast by your side?"

Yang Kai didn't answer his question. The little black dog's origins were somewhat peculiar and troublesome to explain, so he simply asked instead, "It must be difficult for humans and demons to coexist and survive."

The man said, “Human Sovereign City provides shelter. Without it, we wouldn’t be able to survive at all.”

Yang Kai mused, “Is it related to the changes in the laws of heaven and earth?”

“That’s right. In a little while, the laws of heaven and earth will change. At that time, my Human Race won’t be able to move about outside, while the Demon Race will thrive. During this period, no one dares to leave Human Sovereign City, or it’s certain death.”

“So the previous laws were suitable for the Human Race, and the Demon Race had to lie low?”

“That’s right.”

“What a wondrous world.” Yang Kai looked amazed.

The man smiled bitterly. “We’ve been here since birth. No one knows why this world is like this. Maybe only the Human Sovereign can see through it.”

“The Human Sovereign…” Yang Kai had heard the man mention these words several times and couldn’t help but ask, “Is that the master of Human Sovereign City?”

The man replied solemnly, “He is the lord of the Human Race! He protects Human Sovereign City and all of us!” His words were full of respect, clearly holding the Human Sovereign in the highest esteem.

Yang Kai smiled faintly and continued to study the array.

The changes in the laws of heaven and earth became more and more obvious. In the surrounding void, a great amount of Demonic Qi and other strange things began to emerge. Although the couple used their power to form a protective layer around themselves, it was clear they wouldn’t last long. Once their protection broke, they would be invaded by Demonic Qi and become demonic creatures.

Zhu Qing was of the Dragon Clan, so she didn’t feel much, but she still needed to use her dragon essence to dissolve the invasion of Demonic Qi.

Only the little black dog was excited, eyes shining, nostrils twitching as it sniffed around energetically.

It had the bloodline of Guixu, a descendant of a strange beast from the Demon Domain. When Demonic Qi surged, it naturally felt delighted and at home.

“Alright, come over here.” Yang Kai suddenly stood up and called out.

Zhu Qing, holding the little black dog, walked over to stand beside him.

The couple hesitated. The man asked, “What are you going to do?”

“Take you to Human Sovereign City.”

The man was shocked. “You don’t have a Human Sovereign Seal. How will you get to Human Sovereign City?”

Yang Kai sneered. “Who told you that you can’t go to Human Sovereign City without the seal? If you keep dawdling, it’ll be too late. Do you want to live or die?”

The man was still hesitant, but the woman tugged at him.

The man finally made up his mind and said in a low voice, “Fine, I’ll trust you this once.”

Yang Kai grinned. "I hope you'll keep quiet about what happened to Steward Liao."

The man smiled bitterly. “It wouldn’t do us any good to talk about it. Besides… Human Sovereign City has its own ways to find out the truth. Honestly, I think you’d be better off not going there. Even if you’re strong, it won’t help once you’re inside.”

“That’s not certain!” As Yang Kai finished speaking, his expression suddenly turned solemn, and the Laws of Space surged forth.

The place where the four stood was a Space Array connected to Human Sovereign City. Normally, someone like Steward Liao would brand the Human Sovereign Seal on those to be sent, then activate the array for transmission.

But these complicated steps were just layers of disguise, a means for Human Sovereign City to control the Human Race. In the face of Yang Kai, who was proficient in the Laws of Space, these disguises were easily stripped away.

He directly used his mind and the Laws of Space to connect to the other end of the Space Array, not needing any Human Sovereign Seal at all.

Light flared, and the couple’s eyes widened in disbelief as the Space Array really activated. For a moment, they were overjoyed, feeling as if they had escaped death.

The changes in the laws of heaven and earth were now extremely obvious. The world’s Spiritual Qi had all turned into Demonic Qi. In such an environment, the Human Race truly couldn’t survive. Even if one was strong enough to temporarily resist the invasion of Demonic Qi, staying in this environment for long would eventually exhaust one’s strength. At that point, they would be invaded by Demonic Qi, lose their reason, and become demonized.

A moment later, the light in the cave faded, and the four people on the array disappeared without a trace.

Suddenly, a cacophony of noise assaulted them from all directions, making it hard to adapt.

Yang Kai looked around and found that they had arrived in a bustling city. Underfoot was a massive Space Array, and people kept stepping off it, their cultivation levels varying, their expressions different—some overjoyed, some gloomy, some badly injured and hissing in pain.

It was obvious these people, like Yang Kai and the others, had just returned from outside to avoid the changes in the laws of heaven and earth.

Yang Kai saw quite a few Emperor Realm cultivators among them.

The four of them standing on the huge array weren’t particularly conspicuous.

The whole city seemed especially prosperous. The streets were packed with people, shops lined both sides, selling all kinds of local specialties. The cries of vendors rose and fell, giving the feeling of having accidentally wandered into the mortal world.

It was Zhu Qing’s first time in such a lively place, so she couldn’t help but look around in wonder.

Uniformed guards marched down the street, their steps neat and attire matching. It was clear they were the city’s protectors.

However, these guards weren’t very strong. The leader was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and the rest were all at the Dao Origin Realm. Yet, all the martial artists on the street, regardless of strength, avoided them as if they were poisonous snakes, clearly very afraid.

Yang Kai clearly saw a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert give way.

In this world, status didn’t seem to be determined by cultivation. That couple’s strength was obviously lower than his, but they didn’t show any of the deference weaklings usually had toward the strong. Steward Liao was even more obvious—his strength was inferior to anyone’s, yet he could order the couple around without them daring to object.

There were people flying through the sky, not many, and their cultivation levels varied.

“Let’s part ways here. Take care.” The man seemed eager to separate from Yang Kai and Zhu Qing, afraid of being implicated by them. As soon as he recovered, he immediately wanted to leave.

Yang Kai sensed his thoughts and casually agreed.

The woman suddenly said, “A word of advice: never provoke those with a leaf emblem on their chest. The more leaves, the higher their status. This isn’t your world, and it’s not survival of the fittest. Also, Steward Liao’s death won’t stay hidden for long. You’d best leave Human Sovereign City during the next change in the laws of heaven and earth.”

“Thank you.” Yang Kai smiled faintly.

The woman wanted to say more, but the man had already pulled her into the crowd, and they quickly disappeared.

“What an interesting world.” Yang Kai looked around, watching with great interest.

The entire Human Sovereign City seemed to be shrouded in a mysterious barrier, vast and grand. Inside the city, one couldn’t feel the changes in the laws of heaven and earth at all. The laws here were almost identical to those in the Star Realm. As he looked up, he saw that the sky was chaotic, with countless vortexes that seemed able to swallow one’s gaze. Staring too long made one dizzy.

“Is Zhu Lie here?” Yang Kai asked Zhu Qing.

Zhu Qing replied, “I’ll need to find a place to use a secret technique to be sure. Hopefully, he’s here too.”

Yang Kai smiled. “Don’t worry. He’s quite strong. Even if he’s outside, nothing will happen to him for a while.”

“Mm.” Zhu Qing nodded gently, subconsciously stroking the little black dog in her arms. Ah Wang was very content, squinting its eyes in comfort. No one knew whether Steward Liao, whom it had swallowed, was dead or alive.

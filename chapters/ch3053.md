## Chapter 3053 — Four Dragons Soar Together

The Spirit Island was bustling with activity.

A majestic and imposing palace stood tall. Although they had been given only one month, the five hundred mortals had relied on astonishing perseverance and resolve to complete it before the deadline.

Countless rare materials had gone into its construction, leaving the entire palace resplendent with richly carved beams and painted rafters. Neither Yang Kai nor Li Jiao had ever seen a palace on such a grand scale.

Wu Chen was directing the mortals as they decorated every corner of the palace. Today was the final day of the deadline, and tomorrow was the Dragon Clan's grand wedding. No one dared to be careless.

Hundreds of people streamed in and out, continuously carrying small furnishings into the palace and filling the spacious halls with color and life.

"Brother Yang, it has been quite some time."

When he saw Yang Kai approach, Wu Chen greeted him with a broad smile.

He did not ask where Yang Kai had been these past few days. After all, the two of them were not particularly close, and speaking too intimately with a mere acquaintance was a grave taboo. Li Jiao's appearance, however, puzzled him. He could not understand why a Dragon Descendant had suddenly appeared here.

If he remembered correctly, this Dragon Descendant should have been taken away to cultivate Dragon Blood Flowers.

"The wedding is tomorrow?" Yang Kai asked calmly.

Wu Chen's expression also turned solemn as he nodded. "That's right, tomorrow. Fortunately, we finished in time. Otherwise, I'm afraid every last one of us would have died."

"Brother Wu has worked hard and accomplished much." Yang Kai nodded.

Wu Chen smiled. "There was no helping it. The Dragon Clan assigned us this task, and this Wu doesn't want to die either. By the way, since Brother Yang is here, don't leave."

Yang Kai looked at him suspiciously.

Wu Chen explained, "A Dragon Clan wedding is an enormous occasion, so naturally it should be festive. But as you know, the Dragon Clan has very few members. For that reason, they have made a special exception and allowed us to remain here to observe the ceremony and add to the festivities."

A sharp light flashed through Yang Kai's eyes, and he nodded. "That was precisely my intention."

He had been wondering how he could remain here. If the Dragon Clan truly drove everyone away at this juncture, his only option would have been to hide and wait for tomorrow. Unexpectedly, however, the Dragon Clan had actually allowed the several hundred mortals to stay and observe the ceremony, saving him a good deal of trouble.

With the Dragon Clan's permission, he could openly wait here for Zhu Qing to arrive.

"There should be quite a show tomorrow," Wu Chen said with a chuckle.

"Indeed." Yang Kai nodded, then suddenly furrowed his brow and turned to Wu Chen in surprise. "Brother Wu, you actually broke through?"

Wu Chen had previously only been at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, but now that Yang Kai saw him again, he had actually reached the Third Stage. Yang Kai had not paid much attention earlier, but once he noticed, he could not help feeling surprised.

Breaking through from the Second Stage to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm was no simple feat. It was more than ten times as difficult as breaking through from the Dao Origin Realm to the Emperor Realm. Yang Kai had no idea how Wu Chen had managed it.

Wu Chen smiled bitterly. "Brother Yang, you have no idea what this Wu has endured over the past month. I narrowly escaped death several times and only managed to break through by a stroke of fortune. The slightest mishap along the way, and this Wu would not be standing here now. This Wu can still be considered fortunate, but many of our other brothers... haah, better not to speak of it."

He shook his head repeatedly, his face still showing lingering fear.

Yang Kai stared blankly for a moment before spreading his Divine Sense outward like a tide. A moment later, his expression changed slightly.

He discovered that only around three hundred people remained here.

One month ago, Fu Qi had brought a full five hundred people here, yet now nearly forty percent of them were missing. The palace had already been completed, so there was no longer any need for them to go out and mine materials. In other words, those missing people had most likely all died.

Could building a palace truly be so dangerous? Yang Kai knew that mining Ice Soul Snow Jade could be fatal, and Lu Sanniang had previously mentioned that several people had frozen to death from the cold aura, but surely so many people could not all have died that way.

Immediately afterward, he noticed something else strange.

Among the cultivators moving in and out, there were very few in the Emperor Realm and not a single one at the Third Stage. Moreover, regardless of age, gender, or cultivation, all of them seemed extremely afraid of Wu Chen. They went far out of their way to avoid him and did not dare approach.

Just what had happened over the past month for Wu Chen to inspire such fear in these people?

"Brother Yang, why not rest for now? You can return to watch the show tomorrow." Wu Chen thumped his chest. "Leave everything here to me. You can rest assured that I will have everything perfectly prepared."

Yang Kai nodded. "Very well."

He had no intention of involving himself in the palace preparations. Just standing here made him want to tear the entire palace down, so how could he possibly offer to help?

He simply found a place with Li Jiao where they could rest and conserve their strength.

"That Wu Chen is not simple," Li Jiao said with a frown.

"What did you notice?" Yang Kai asked, looking at him.

Li Jiao shook his head. "I didn't notice anything in particular. I simply feel that this man is... somewhat strange. Brother Yang, you must be on your guard against him."

"I know," Yang Kai replied indifferently. "As long as he doesn't stand in my way, all will be well. If he dares make an enemy of me, I won't show him any mercy."

"By the way, Brother Yang, what grievance do you have with Fu Chi?" Li Jiao asked in confusion.

Earlier, Yang Kai had said he was going to teach Fu Chi a lesson, but Li Jiao had not given much thought to why. Only now that he had calmed down did the question occur to him. Judging by appearances, Yang Kai's grudge against Fu Chi seemed no smaller than his own.

"Tomorrow's Dragon Clan wedding is between Fu Chi and Zhu Qing."

Li Jiao froze when he heard this, then nodded after a long while. "I understand."

Was that not exactly the same as his own situation? Both of them bore the hatred of having their wives stolen. The only difference was that one had already become reality, while the other was imminent. In an instant, Li Jiao felt a strong sense of solidarity with Yang Kai against their common enemy.

The night passed without incident.

The next day, the water and sky merged into one, and the azure heavens looked freshly washed.

The entire Dragon Palace seemed suffused with a festive and lively atmosphere.

A bevy of charming women arrived at the palace early in the morning, bringing a captivating vitality to the enormous structure.

These women were all Fu Chi's exclusive playthings, so their beauty naturally went without saying. Although they could not compare with Lu Sanniang or the deceased Madam Hua, every one of them was still a first-class beauty. There were over a hundred of them, moving throughout the palace as they inspected everything. Wu Chen accompanied them, bowing and scraping as he showed them the way.

Their main responsibility today was to welcome the bride, so naturally they could not afford the slightest carelessness. They all knew that the woman about to marry Fu Chi would become their future mistress, and that their lives and deaths would rest almost entirely on a single thought from her. Thus, each of them was exceptionally conscientious. If they found even a speck of dust anywhere in the palace, they would berate Wu Chen and order him to have it cleaned immediately.

This round of work lasted two full hours. Wu Chen was kept so busy that his head spun, and the many cultivators all suffered endlessly, yet under the Dragon Clan’s despotic might, not one of them dared utter a word of complaint.

Two hours later, everything was ready.

More than a hundred women walked out of the palace and formed orderly ranks, waiting in silence. The air immediately filled with all manner of fragrances.

The three hundred cultivators also split into neat formations on either side. The scene became somewhat solemn, and all fell silent.

The three hundred cultivators were mainly here to add to the festivities and had no other duties.

Yang Kai and Li Jiao stood motionless among the crowd. Perhaps because he was somewhat nervous, Li Jiao looked quite anxious. He wanted to exchange a few words with Yang Kai to ease the pressure, but seeing Yang Kai resting with his eyes closed, he did not feel comfortable disturbing him.

A moment later, a flash of lightning suddenly appeared, and unparalleled Dragon Pressure spread through the air.

A figure sped over from the other side of the island and landed before the palace, coming clearly into view.

Fu Chi wore a bright red robe today that looked exceptionally festive. Paired with his handsome face and uninhibited bearing, the vivid red did not appear vulgar in the slightest and instead made him even more charming.

The more than one hundred women were all left spellbound by the sight, while the three hundred cultivators immediately grew solemn.

The groom had appeared, so naturally they had to be at their most attentive.

"Sir!" A woman in palace robes standing at the very front hurried forward to greet him with a gentle smile. Her expression, however, appeared somewhat restrained. She was not as favored as Madam Hua had been and could not fully relax in Fu Chi's presence.

Fu Chi swept his gaze around and nodded slightly. "How are the preparations?"

The woman replied, "Everything is ready. We are only waiting for the bride to arrive."

"Very good." After saying this, Fu Chi paid her no further attention.

The woman opened her mouth to speak several times, but in the end, she silently withdrew.

Li Jiao lowered his eyes while Yang Kai kept his eyes tightly closed. Neither of them looked at Fu Chi, not because they were afraid, but because the hostility that might inadvertently show in their eyes could expose them and put others on guard.

The group waited in front of the palace.

Soon, members of the Dragon Clan began arriving one after another, offering their congratulations, and Fu Chi received them warmly.

It was not long before six or seven members of the Dragon Clan had gathered in front of the palace, talking animatedly and laughing heartily.

About half an hour later, several resounding Dragon roars suddenly rang out from the distance. Clouds churned at the edge of the sky as an enormous bank of clouds rapidly advanced toward them.

"They're here!" A member of the Dragon Clan pointed at the approaching clouds and said with a smile.

Everyone turned to look in that direction. Fu Chi adjusted his clothes and put a gentle smile on his face.

The bank of clouds drew closer and closer, while the Dragon roars grew ever louder and more resonant.

Suddenly, the clouds billowed, and a colossal creature emerged from within.

It was an enormous crimson Dragon more than twenty zhang long, its entire body covered in scales. Its fiery red hue resembled blazing flames as it tossed its head, lashed its tail, and came hurtling toward them.

Cries of alarm rang out.

No matter who they were, experiencing the pressure of a giant Dragon's true form at such close range was overwhelming.

That was not all. After the red Dragon appeared, a green Dragon no smaller than it also emerged from the side and flew alongside it.

A third Dragon appeared as well.

Then a fourth...

Four Dragons soared together in a truly magnificent spectacle, and everyone held their breath.

Behind the four Dragons was a luxuriously decorated bridal sedan chair. It too was entirely red and exceptionally festive. A graceful figure could vaguely be seen sitting perfectly still within.

Gauze curtains obscured her features, but everyone knew that the person inside the bridal sedan chair had to be today's bride.

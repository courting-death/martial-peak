## Chapter 2789 — Hua Yulu’s Invitation

Xia Sheng warmly welcomed Yang Kai into his room. After the two took their seats as host and guest, Yang Kai finally asked about Bian Yuqing’s situation.

“That woman successfully broke through to Emperor Realm half a month ago. The commotion that day was no small matter.” Xia Sheng’s expression became solemn.

“She broke through to the Emperor Realm?” Yang Kai’s brows rose. This news was both expected and unexpected.

Based on Bian Yuqing’s aptitude, even if she entered Linghu Palace’s Heaven-Grade secret cultivation ground for two months of closed-door training, her chances of breaking through would have been slim. Her talent in the Martial Dao was never outstanding, and after lingering at the Dao Origin Realm for so many years, her sharpness had long been worn away. With her ambition dulled and no drive to advance, how could she possibly achieve a breakthrough?

If nothing unexpected happened, the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm would be the peak she could reach in her lifetime.

But Kou Wu’s death greatly stimulated her, and with Yang Kai’s various forms of help, including the large amount of Source Crystals and Emperor Pills left before he departed, her breakthrough and further progress were only natural.

In any case, this was a joyful event, meaning Lingxiao Palace had gained another Emperor expert.

Linghu Palace had been established for many years, with countless martial artists breaking through their shackles here and seeking deeper mysteries of cultivation. Yet, those who advanced to the Emperor Realm here had always been few. Bian Yuqing’s breakthrough that day shook the entire Linghu City, especially those martial artists waiting in line to enter Linghu Palace for closed-door cultivation. Each of them seemed to see new hope, filled with energy, just waiting for their turn to enter the secret ground and cultivate well.

“She’s been stabilizing her cultivation these past half month. She should be able to leave seclusion in a few days.” Xia Sheng smiled slightly. As one of Linghu Palace’s three Deacons, he naturally knew Bian Yuqing’s situation well, not to mention that she was brought by Yang Kai, so he would help take care of her as much as possible.

“That’s excellent.” Yang Kai nodded with pleasure.

“Junior Brother, have you had anything going on lately?” Xia Sheng suddenly changed the topic and asked.

Yang Kai replied, “I need to make a trip to Star God Palace. Is there anything Senior Brother needs from me?”

“To Star God Palace, huh…” Xia Sheng was momentarily stunned, but didn’t ask what Yang Kai was going there for. After pondering for a moment, he said, “How long will it take?”

“Just a month or two.”

“In that case, the timing works out.” He grinned and said, “I want to ask Junior Brother for a favor. Are you free?”

“Senior Brother, please speak!” Yang Kai’s expression became serious.

Xia Sheng did not say what kind of help he needed. Instead, he tilted his head toward the room behind and called out, “Hua’er, come out. Junior Brother Yang knows you as well—stop hiding.”

As he finished speaking, a palace-dressed matron strolled out from the room. Her fitted long dress wrapped around her graceful figure, and her face wore a smile that stirred the heart. Her limpid eyes shimmered, as if she wanted to speak but hesitated.

“Palace Master Hua!” Yang Kai looked in surprise at the beautiful woman who suddenly appeared.

This woman was actually Hua Yulu, the Palace Master of Hundred Flowers Palace. He had just met her a few days ago in Qingyang Shrine, and didn’t expect to encounter her here again.

That day, Hua Yulu had inquired about Xia Sheng’s whereabouts. It seemed she had come directly to Linghu Palace after leaving Qingyang Shrine.

Although Yang Kai had sensed another person’s presence in the room earlier, since they were hiding, he hadn’t investigated. Now seeing her in person, he was quite surprised.

This man and woman seemed to have some illicit relationship!

Yang Kai’s expression immediately became meaningful.

Hua Yulu seemed a bit embarrassed, her cheeks slightly red. She approached and gave a graceful salute, softly saying, “I greet Elder Yang!”

“Palace Master Hua, you’re too polite.” Yang Kai hurriedly raised his hand in a gesture.

Not to mention that Hua Yulu’s cultivation was equal to his, and she was a Palace Master, just her unclear relationship with Xia Sheng made it impossible for Yang Kai to accept such courtesy.

Yang Kai turned to Xia Sheng and said, “Senior Brother, the favor you mentioned, does it have something to do with Palace Master Hua?”

Xia Sheng smiled slightly. “Yes, it’s indeed related to Hua’er.” He looked at Hua Yulu and said, “Hua’er, you explain.”

Hua Yulu nodded gently, her red lips parted as she slowly spoke, “Three months ago, when I went to the Southern Marsh to search for a medicinal herb, I accidentally discovered an ancient cave mansion…”

“An ancient cave mansion?” Yang Kai was startled by her words.

Hua Yulu nodded, “Yes, it truly was an ancient cave mansion. It’s been there for countless years, exuding an aura of great antiquity and desolation—no telling who left it behind. I ventured inside with two others, and though we exhausted every means to break through several old and weakened restrictions and gained some benefits, in the end, we were stopped by a much stronger barrier and could go no further.”

“Why were there two others?” Yang Kai frowned slightly.

Hua Yulu said, “The Southern Marsh is dangerous everywhere, but also full of opportunities. It’s said to be the birthplace and gathering place of ancient martial artists, so many good things are hidden there. The special environment can also produce some peerless spiritual medicines. Many experts explore the Southern Marsh, and when the ancient cave mansion appeared, it caused a bit of a stir. If not for that, I wouldn’t have discovered it, and the other two were also attracted by it.”

After listening, Yang Kai nodded, vaguely understanding the situation at the time.

Hua Yulu continued, “Of the other two, one understood a bit about restriction arrays. According to his estimation, even with the combined strength of the three of us, we couldn’t break through the restriction blocking our way. We could only seek help from others, so we agreed to each find a helper and meet again in the Southern Marsh in half a year.”

“And you found Senior Brother Xia!” Yang Kai asked.

Hua Yulu smiled bitterly. “I know quite a few in the Emperor Realm, but there are very few I can truly trust.”

Greed and betrayal, killing for treasure, are common on the martial path. When seeking partners for such adventures, one must find trustworthy people. Otherwise, you might end up dead.

Xia Sheng said, “Junior Brother Yang, Senior Brother really can’t get away. The Shrine sent me here for three years, and I can’t leave until the time is up. Otherwise, I wouldn’t trouble you with this.”

“It’s no big deal, just exploring an ancient cave mansion.” Yang Kai waved his hand and looked at Hua Yulu. “But… are you familiar with the other two? Can you trust them?”

Hua Yulu replied, “Not really. That was the first time we met. They’re only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. As for trust… since it was our first meeting, how could there be trust? But since I don’t trust them, they surely don’t trust me either. So when we parted, we each swore a heart demon oath, promising not to leak news of the ancient cave mansion, only to seek helpers at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm or below, and each could only bring one person.”

“I see…” Yang Kai stroked his chin in thought.

Heart demon oaths may be just words, but at the Emperor Realm, such vows aren’t made lightly. If broken, a heart demon may arise, affecting one’s future.

With the heart demon oath in place, those two can be trusted not to leak the news for now. This matter might really be worth pursuing.

“If I’m not mistaken, those two will definitely find helpers at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm.” Hua Yulu looked intently at Yang Kai and said, “If Elder Yang has concerns, then forget it. I’ll think of another way.”

It’s not that she couldn’t find helpers at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, but those people really couldn’t be trusted. Otherwise, she wouldn’t have come to Xia Sheng. Before reaching Emperor Realm, Xia Sheng was the chief disciple of Qingyang Shrine. Though he had only recently advanced to the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, if he really fought, it wouldn’t be easy for someone at the Second Stage to kill him.

As for Yang Kai’s strength, Hua Yulu had seen it herself at the conferral ceremony. He could even injure elders of Star God Palace. Although he was also seriously wounded, his strength couldn’t be simply measured as First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and should be comparable to Xia Sheng.

Xia Sheng said from the side, “Junior Brother, consider it yourself. Don’t worry about me, I’m just mentioning it. Whether you’re willing or not is up to you.”

He was clearly afraid Yang Kai would agree out of concern for his feelings.

Yang Kai smiled, “I’m also very interested in ancient cave mansions.”

Hua Yulu’s beautiful eyes lit up. “Elder Yang, you’re agreeing?”

Yang Kai didn’t answer, but said, “For an ancient cave mansion to make the three of you so persistent, searching for helpers to explore it, you must have gained something inside?”

“There were indeed benefits!” Hua Yulu nodded frankly. “Even if Elder Yang didn’t ask, I was going to explain. We didn’t explore deeply, and though we broke through several restrictions, we only obtained a few things. This is one of the things I gained from the cave mansion.”

As she spoke, Hua Yulu flipped her hand and took out a small flower drum.

The little flower drum didn’t look special, only about the size of a palm. No one knew what it was made from, but as soon as it appeared, a unique aura spread out.

Yang Kai’s eyes narrowed: “Primordial Era treasure!”

This little flower drum, like his Mountain and River Bell, was a genuine Primordial Era treasure, radiating pure primordial energy.

In ancient times, the tides of Spiritual Qi were very different from now, so the secret techniques, divine abilities, and even Secret Treasures used by ancient martial artists were quite distinct from those of this era.

But it’s undeniable that treasures from the Primordial Era are not to be underestimated.

However, treasures from the Primordial Era are exceedingly rare. In the past ten thousand years, only a handful have ever been recorded—perhaps no more than ten in the entire Star Realm, with the Mountain and River Bell being one of the few known examples. There may be others hidden from the world, but such artifacts are truly scarce.

Now, Hua Yulu actually had one in her hand!

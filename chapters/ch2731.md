## Chapter 2731 — However Big, However Much

Nine is the ultimate number; at most, a single furnace of Spiritual Pills can yield only nine pills.

Ji Ying’s suggestion to switch to Dao Origin-Grade Spiritual Pills wasn’t because he lacked confidence in winning, but rather because he didn’t want an unfair victory. He had been an Imperial-Grade Pill Master for many years, refining countless Emperor Yuan Pills. Even though Yang Kai was also an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, he couldn’t possibly match Ji Ying’s depth of experience. By choosing to refine Emperor Yuan Pills, Ji Ying would gain a significant advantage without anyone realizing it.

But if they switched to Dao Origin-Grade Spiritual Pills, Ji Ying would still have an edge, but at least the starting point wouldn’t be so far apart.

Yang Kai shook his head and said, “No need. Thank you for your kindness, Master Ji. Let’s refine Emperor Yuan Pills!”

He naturally understood why Ji Ying made this suggestion, and his impression of Ji Ying improved.

Not everyone can become the Chief Alchemist of Lingxiao Palace. Even if Ji Ying was the personal disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan, if his character wasn’t up to par, Yang Kai wouldn’t consider him for the position.

The way Ji Ying had previously admitted his mistake regarding Li Xuan, even apologizing and expelling Li Xuan from Medicine Pill Valley right in front of Yang Kai, had left Yang Kai with a good impression of Ji Ying’s character—honest and upright. Now, seeing his actions again, Yang Kai felt his judgment was indeed correct.

Thinking from another angle, Ji Ying was the disciple of Great Emperor Miaodan and a member of Medicine Pill Valley. Even if he didn’t apologize to Yang Kai, Yang Kai couldn’t do much to him unless he was willing to risk offending Medicine Pill Valley.

One honest action is enough. Ji Ying, despite his noble background and high status, didn’t have the fake arrogance most pill masters possess. With his Imperial-Grade Pill Master status, if he could become the Chief Alchemist of Lingxiao Palace, Yang Kai wouldn’t have to worry about Spiritual Pills for the sect anymore.

Ji Ying could even train a batch of alchemists for Lingxiao Sect!

A sect’s development can’t rely on just one person. Only by covering all aspects can it move forward.

“Since Palace Master Yang insists, Ji has no objections.” Ji Ying said solemnly, feeling a bit like his good intentions were misunderstood.

As he spoke, he flipped his hand and took out a pile of medicinal ingredients, dividing them into two portions. He looked at Yang Kai and said, “Both sets are for refining Emperor Yuan Pills. There’s no difference in quality or age. Please inspect them, Palace Master Yang.”

Yang Kai smiled and said, “I trust Master Ji’s character.”

“Then pick one!” Ji Ying said.

Yang Kai casually chose a set. Ji Ying’s reputation and status were well known. Even if he wanted to defeat Yang Kai, he would do so openly with his pill refining skills, not through tricks.

Seeing Yang Kai’s relaxed demeanor, Ji couldn’t help but get a bit irritated, snorting coldly, “Since that’s the case, let’s begin. As an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, you should have your own pill furnace, right? Do you need to borrow one?”

“No need.” Yang Kai smiled and shook his head.

Since it was a contest between pill masters, it had to be held in public, not in secret. Without moving, they set up right in the main hall.

Ji Ying casually tossed out a pill furnace. A three-legged furnace flew out and landed steadily on the ground. As soon as it appeared, a unique fragrance filled the hall, like orchid and musk, invigorating everyone. The furnace was ancient, clearly aged, with dragon heads on both ends, lifelike and majestic. They seemed to be the key to controlling the fire, and the inside was covered with dense runes and patterns, dizzying to look at.

Emperor Intent spread throughout the hall!

“Excellent furnace!” Yang Kai’s eyes lit up, full of praise.

As fellow pill masters, they naturally had a special fondness for good pill furnaces. The moment Ji Ying took out his furnace, Yang Kai knew it wasn’t ordinary. Judging by the fragrance, Ji Ying must have refined countless Spiritual Pills with it, likely reaching a state of unity with the furnace.

Ji Ying said proudly, “This is the worst furnace I own.”

Taking out the worst one was simply to avoid gaining an unfair advantage, and also showed his confidence in his own abilities.

Yang Kai nodded slightly, then raised his hand and tossed out his own pill furnace.

His furnace glowed with a purple light. As soon as it appeared, Emperor Intent spread out, matching Ji Ying’s furnace in grade—both were Imperial-Grade pill furnaces. Ji Ying was surprised, not expecting Yang Kai to have such a foundation.

But thinking about it, if Yang Kai was truly an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, it was normal for him to have an Imperial-Grade furnace.

The only thing that puzzled Ji Ying was that this furnace looked somewhat familiar.

Ji Ying was certain he hadn’t seen this furnace before. If he had, he would definitely recognize it.

With the duel imminent, he didn’t dwell on it, quickly focusing his mind. Even though his opponent was a young man who didn’t know his limits, Ji Ying didn’t dare be careless. Even a lion uses its full strength to hunt a rabbit.

He would use his true skills to defeat Yang Kai in every aspect, showing respect for pill refining and honoring his master’s teachings.

“Shall we begin?” Ji Ying asked solemnly, turning to Yang Kai.

Yang Kai was about to nod when he suddenly looked to the side and smiled, “Sect Master Mi and Palace Master Li, would you like to place a bet as well?”

“We can bet too?” Mi Qi and Li Jiao exchanged glances, pleasantly surprised.

“You’re already here. Are you really willing to leave empty-handed?” Yang Kai smiled at them.

“Alright!” Mi Qi said in a low voice. “We’ll bet, but I wonder how big a bet Palace Master Yang can accept!”

“However big, however much. Even betting the foundation of a sect is fine.” Yang Kai said casually.

“What…” Mi Qi and Li Jiao’s faces changed, shocked by Yang Kai’s words. Betting the foundation of a sect was far too much. Although they wanted to take advantage of Yang Kai, betting the foundation was something they wouldn’t even consider.

Even if they had absolute confidence in Ji Ying, they wouldn’t dare to gamble so recklessly.

“You two don’t dare?” Yang Kai looked at them with contempt.

“It’s not that we don’t dare, just no need. We have no grudges, so why play so big?” Mi Qi retorted, though his heart was uneasy.

“Yes, yes, just for fun.”

“In that case, how do you think the bet should be set so it’s bearable?” Yang Kai asked.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao exchanged glances, their Divine Senses surging as if communicating.

Yang Kai wasn’t in any hurry. He simply stood to the side, smiling at Ji Ying and remarked, “The weather’s quite nice today, isn’t it?”

Ji Ying frowned, thinking to himself, "Isn’t that obvious?" The sky outside was clear and cloudless—any fool could see that.

But why was Yang Kai saying this? Was he trying to disturb Ji Ying’s state of mind… Truly devious!

He couldn’t help but glare at Yang Kai, focusing inward.

“Palace Master Yang, I’ve finished discussing with Brother Li.” Mi Qi suddenly spoke.

Yang Kai gestured for him to continue.

Mi Qi said, “Brother Li and I will bet the profits of our respective sects for ten years. Does Sect Master Yang dare accept?”

Even Ji Ying was moved by this bet.

The profits of each sect for ten years might sound simple, but if converted to Source Crystals, it was an astronomical sum. For top sects like Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace, with countless industries and their own Source Crystal mines, the annual profit was at least fifty million Upper-Level Source Crystals.

Ten years would be five hundred million Upper-Level Source Crystals!

For two sects, that’s a billion!

The number was simply terrifying.

It seemed Mi Qi and Li Jiao were determined to get some benefits from Lingxiao Palace. Ji Ying glanced at Yang Kai, wanting to see his expression.

Surely… his face wouldn’t look good?

But when Ji Ying looked, Yang Kai curled his lip in disdain and sneered, “You two are famous figures in the Northern Territory, and yet you’re so stingy?”

“Stingy?” Mi Qi’s face darkened. “You dare call us stingy!”

Li Jiao squinted, “Is Palace Master Yang just bluffing, not daring to accept the bet?”

He thought he saw through Yang Kai’s intentions, speaking sarcastically.

“Fifty years!” Yang Kai declared in a ringing voice, raising one hand. “I’ll wager fifty years with you—fifty years of profits from each Sect!”

“Hiss…” Mi Qi and Li Jiao both gasped.

Ji Ying’s eyes widened, looking at Yang Kai like he was crazy.

“You, you, you…” Mi Qi stammered, clearly shaken. “Do you know how much Mitian Sect’s profits for fifty years are? How can you make such a boast?”

Yang Kai remained calm, “Let’s say fifty million Upper-Level Source Crystals a year, ten years is five hundred million, fifty years is two and a half billion! Two sects, fifty years, that’s just five billion!”

“Just… five billion!” Li Jiao was nearly choked by Yang Kai’s words. He really wanted to ask if Yang Kai understood what five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals meant.

Yang Kai sneered, looking at them with disdain, “What, you two don’t dare to bet? Or do you lack confidence in Master Ji?”

The first part was nothing, but the second was serious. If they backed out now, it would seem they lacked confidence in Ji Ying. That could cause trouble, possibly offending Ji Ying.

Truly cunning! Li Jiao and Mi Qi gritted their teeth in frustration.

At this point, they couldn’t retreat. Li Jiao snorted, “Brother Mi Qi and I have no problem with fifty years. The question is, can Palace Master Yang put up that much for the bet!”

“Exactly!” Mi Qi nodded in agreement. “Five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals isn’t a small sum. If Palace Master Yang defaults, what can Brother Li and I do?”

“Ridiculous!” Yang Kai pointed at his nose. “Do I look like someone who doesn’t keep his word?”

Mi Qi and Li Jiao sized him up. Li Jiao said, “We’re not familiar with you! Who knows what kind of person you are.”

“Fine, fine, fine.” Yang Kai acted exasperated, making Mi Qi and Li Jiao feel he was bluffing even more.

Mi Qi burst out laughing and said, “If Palace Master Yang can put up a matching stake, why shouldn’t Brother Li and I place our bets?”

“You said it, don’t regret it!” Yang Kai jumped up.

“Go ahead!” Li Jiao seemed to see through Yang Kai, urging him.

“I’ll take it out right now!” Yang Kai shouted forcefully.

“Do it!”

“Then... here it is!” Yang Kai flipped his hand, and a Spatial Ring appeared in his palm, instantly drawing everyone’s attention.

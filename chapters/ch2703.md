## Chapter 2703 — Mad

The Soul-Severing Blade was an Imperial Treasure, specializing in harming the Divine Soul, and its power was immense.

The Heaven-Shattering Strike was a secret technique Yang Kai had learned from Tian Yan in the world of the Divine Roaming Mirror. That Divine Roaming Mirror was the Ancestral Treasure of Qingyang Shrine, a world unto itself, where all living beings existed as Spirit Bodies without physical forms.

Tian Yan was the strongest expert in the Divine Roaming Mirror world, and the strength of his Spirit Body had even reached the level of a Great Emperor.

He had spent his entire life delving into the mysteries of Divine Soul secret arts, and the Heaven-Shattering Strike was his own creation—how could its might be anything less than extraordinary?

Supplemented by the Soul-Severing Blade, an Imperial Treasure, the power displayed was far more than a simple sum of its parts.

Moreover, to be safe, Yang Kai had pushed his Divine Soul power to the limit, to the point that even he could barely withstand it. Now, his face was pale, his whole body cold, and his head throbbed with pain.

As for Tan Junhao, who had taken the blow head-on, his fate was even more tragic.

The violent soul-severing force instantly tore through the defenses of his Sea of Consciousness, rampaging within and turning it upside down. The seawater surged, and his Divine Sense was thrown into chaos.

Tan Junhao let out a miserable scream, his entire body drenched in sweat as if he had just been fished out of a lake.

With his Sea of Consciousness so grievously wounded, there was almost no hope of recovery. Save for a handful of legendary Spiritual Pills long lost to the world, not even a Great Emperor could heal such an injury.

"Bloom, Lotus!" Yang Kai shouted again, ready to strike while his enemy was down. Forcing himself to focus, he prepared to unleash the Bloom, Lotus secret technique.

But just then, the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome above Tan Junhao's head suddenly flipped with a clatter and froze on a certain page.

In that instant, two beams of golden light shot out from the page.

At such close range, Yang Kai had no way to defend himself and was pierced straight through by the two golden beams.

"Puh..." Yang Kai spat out a mouthful of golden blood, and under a tremendous force, he was sent flying backward, rolling on the ground and unable to get up for quite a while.

A sharp pain came from his abdomen. When Yang Kai looked down, his face turned ashen and he cursed, "Old dog, I will never share the same sky with you!"

There were now two finger-sized holes in his abdomen, punched clean through his body. He had no idea what kind of attack those golden beams were, but even his Dragon Transformation Secret Technique couldn't defend against them.

He quickly circulated his Emperor Yuan, sending it to the wounds to staunch the bleeding.

At that moment, hundreds of ferocious avian beasts swarmed down again. Startled, Yang Kai could only use his teleportation secret technique to dodge.

Without Tan Junhao's restraint, it was much harder for these beasts to harm him. A single teleport was enough to shake them off.

"Ah!" A miserable scream rang out. Tan Junhao howled at the sky, his clothes fluttering without wind, and even his hair whipped wildly, making him look especially savage.

"Heh heh heh..." Yang Kai, clutching his abdomen and hiding to the side, felt much better seeing Tan Junhao in such a state.

Although he had been badly hurt by a moment's carelessness, Tan Junhao's injuries were even worse. Even if he couldn't kill him today, Tan Junhao likely wouldn't live much longer.

He took out a handful of Spiritual Pills for healing and recovery from his Spatial Ring and stuffed them into his mouth like candy.

A cornered beast will still fight back—let alone someone like Tan Junhao, a Third Stage Emperor. The next bout would be a true life-and-death struggle, so taking some pills as a precaution was never wrong.

"Clatter..."

The pages above Tan Junhao's head flipped again.

Yang Kai was startled, unsure what the old thing was up to now. He quietly gathered his strength, ready to dodge at any moment.

The pages froze. Golden light shot out, twisting and morphing midair into a golden longsword. With a piercing shriek, it slashed through the void, leaving cracks in space itself. Several nearby avian beasts, unable to dodge in time, were instantly cleaved into nothingness.

"Hm?" Yang Kai watched in astonishment.

Clatter...

The pages flipped again, and this time a massive seal flew out, smashing into the ground and leaving a huge pit.

Clatter...

The pages of the tome kept flipping, and with each pause, a different attack would shoot out—sometimes a dazzling Secret Treasure, sometimes a mighty secret technique, and at other times, even a vividly lifelike Demonic Beast would be summoned forth.

Yang Kai even saw a demonic beast comparable to a Third Stage Emperor. This beast was covered in eyes, too many to count, and its whole body looked like a ball of flesh—he had no idea what kind of monster it was.

However, the mysterious light shooting from the eyes on that round body seemed to have a petrifying effect.

As soon as the flesh-ball beast appeared, its eyes kept opening and closing, each time firing off a mysterious light—sometimes over a hundred beams, sometimes dozens.

The hundreds of avian beasts Tan Junhao had summoned earlier were all dealt with by it, each one frozen in place as if under a petrification spell.

In fact, those avian beasts seemed extremely afraid of the flesh-ball, only taking hits without fighting back, letting it rampage until they were all wiped out.

The scene was utter chaos, with all kinds of attacks flying everywhere. Yang Kai had to jump around frantically to dodge, looking utterly bedraggled.

Especially when facing the flesh-ball's attacks—if he got hit, his body's speed and agility dropped drastically, and he had to desperately circulate his Emperor Yuan to recover.

He was secretly alarmed; if Tan Junhao had attacked him just then, even with his space abilities, he might not have been able to escape.

"Ah..." Tan Junhao kept screaming, clutching his head, sometimes leaping into the air, sometimes rolling on the ground. There was no trace left of a Third Stage Emperor Realm expert or an elder of the Star God Palace.

"He's gone mad..." Yang Kai wiped the cold sweat from his forehead, his face dark.

He hadn’t expected things to take such a dramatic turn, leaving even him at a complete loss for what to do.

But thinking it over, using the Soul-Severing Blade as a base for the Heaven-Shattering Strike, if an ordinary person took it head-on, they'd probably die instantly.

After all, once the Sea of Consciousness was broken, the Divine Soul would be destroyed—how could anyone survive?

That Tan Junhao could survive such an attack was already impressive, but with his Sea of Consciousness so badly damaged, there was no way he could be unscathed.

At this moment, Tan Junhao’s mind was in chaos, his consciousness muddled—he had clearly gone mad.

Yang Kai couldn't help but feel relieved.

Earlier, Tan Junhao had only used three of the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome's divine abilities, and Yang Kai had already struggled to cope. Now, it seemed the tome had endless uses. If Tan Junhao had managed to unleash all its powers, just summoning that flesh-ball alone would have spelled disaster for him.

At this moment, Tan Junhao's Divine Sense was in chaos, and he was using the tome's powers at random, finally revealing its true terror.

Where did this Imperial Treasure come from? There was no way any living artificer could forge such a thing.

As Yang Kai pondered, the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome floating above Tan Junhao's head suddenly shone with golden light. All the summoned entities turned into golden streams and rushed back into the tome, vanishing in the blink of an eye.

Only after all the summoned entities had returned did the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome suddenly snap shut and tumble from above Tan Junhao’s head.

Yang Kai’s eyes widened, glinting with a thief’s cunning as his gaze locked onto the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome. In a flash, he teleported before it and snatched the tome up with a swift motion.

Having witnessed the tome's many powers, how could Yang Kai not be tempted? With such an Imperial Treasure in hand, he'd have a huge advantage in future battles.

Sssshhh—

Suddenly, as Yang Kai grabbed hold of the Golden Armored Heavenly Tome, a strange sound rang out. The tome erupted with dazzling golden light, violently trembling in his grasp and fiercely resisting his touch. Not only that, but the golden radiance seared his hand with excruciating pain, as if thousands of needles were stabbing into his flesh.

Suddenly, another hand grabbed the tome from the other side, holding it tightly.

Yang Kai looked up and saw Tan Junhao glaring at him with bloodshot eyes, blood trickling from the corner of his eye, his face twisted into a savage mask.

"Let go, that's mine!" Tan Junhao roared at Yang Kai.

Yang Kai replied, “You’ve lost your mind. I’ll keep this for you for the time being.”

“I’m not mad!” Tan Junhao shook his head, his expression incomparably serious.

“Oh, but you are.” Yang Kai fixed him with a deep gaze, his voice carrying a strange, bewitching force. “You truly have gone mad.”

“I’m… mad?” Tan Junhao stared at Yang Kai in a daze.

Yang Kai nodded lightly.

If he weren't mad, why would he fight Yang Kai for the tome? It was his Imperial Treasure—he only needed a thought to recall it.

"I'm mad!" Tan Junhao suddenly straightened, lost in thought. His grip on the tome loosened involuntarily.

He seemed not to realize what being crazy had to do with fighting over the tome.

Yang Kai seized the chance to snatch the tome and stuffed it into the Xuan Realm Pearl.

The tome bore Tan Junhao's Soul Brand, so it would have to be erased later.

But Yang Kai's action instantly caught Tan Junhao's attention. He snapped out of his daze, glared viciously at Yang Kai, and growled through gritted teeth, "Give it back!"

Yang Kai spread his hands. "It's gone."

Tan Junhao flew into a rage, his Emperor Yuan erupting as he lunged at Yang Kai.

Caught off guard, Yang Kai was rammed directly, his already wounded abdomen flaring with pain, his body curling up like a shrimp.

"Give it back!" Tan Junhao shouted, his fists like a storm, raining countless blows on Yang Kai's chest.

"Puh!" Yang Kai spat blood, splattering Tan Junhao's face. At the same time, he kicked Tan Junhao hard, finally breaking free.

Forming hand seals quickly, Yang Kai's face grew solemn as he chanted in a low voice, "Years wither and flourish, like a shuttle, like a dream!"

The seal formed, he gently pressed forward.

Tan Junhao had no thought of dodging. His blood-red eyes locked on Yang Kai as he kept attacking, repeating the same words over and over.

The Years Like a Shuttle seal struck Tan Junhao directly, sending him flying.

The power of time eroded him as he flew through the air. Already old, Tan Junhao suddenly became even older—his hair turned white and fell out, his skin wrinkled all over.

When he finally landed, he looked like a man on the verge of death.

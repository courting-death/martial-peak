## Chapter 3135 — Xu Binbai

With a rumble that shook the earth and mountains, Yanghe City's Grand Array finally closed.

The White Robe Sect cultivators went ashen. This time, they feared there would be no escaping calamity.

Under countless watching eyes, the Incarnation stepped forward and threw a light punch, one that seemed to carry no strength at all.

Yet the might of that single punch made Heaven and Earth change color. Along with them, the faces of the many Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators changed as well.

After refining Shi Huo's Source, the Incarnation had shrunk to the size of an ordinary man, but his true body was a Stone Giant who towered between Heaven and Earth, as tall as a mountain. How could the force of even a casual punch from him be described as a mere ten thousand jun?

When his fist landed, the Spirit Array, which had seemed as solid as an impregnable fortress, let out a deep hum. With the point of impact at the center, several enormous cracks thundered outward in every direction. Light flashed wildly across the array, and then it shattered with a crash.

Breaking the array with a single punch was an astonishing display of might. Those who had been watching the Incarnation with such interest a moment earlier nearly had their souls fly from their bodies.

Yang Kai did not need to give any further orders. Li Jiao, Lu Sanniang, Ruan Biting, He Yunxiang, and the Incarnation transformed into five streams of light, rushed in five different directions, and plunged into five separate battlefields. In an instant, enemies were thrown into chaos. Wherever they passed, no one could withstand even a single move.

Of the five, He Yunxiang had the lowest cultivation, having advanced to the Third Stage of the Void King Realm only recently. But in Yanghe City, the strongest enemies were only at the Third Stage of the Void King Realm, and there were not many other Void King Realm cultivators either, so how could they possibly be a match?

It was like a group of burly men barging into a children's game, overbearing and utterly unchecked.

In the blink of an eye, more than a hundred people had died at their hands, including quite a few Void King Realm cultivators.

These divine reinforcements from the heavens changed the situation in an instant. The White Robe Sect cultivators, who had already been resolved to die, were shaken awake. How could they not know that saviors had arrived? While their enemies were still dazed, they rose up and counterattacked, sweeping away their earlier disadvantage. Their momentum became unstoppable, driving the Netherworld Sect cultivators back step by step.

From high above, Li Jiao and the others looked like five giant dragons rolling through the city, dragging five twisting trails of dust and smoke behind them. Within several dozen zhang of them, enemies were injured at a touch and killed on contact.

They were no match at all. The gap between the two sides was no longer a chasm, but the distance between Heaven and Earth.

“Oh?” Yang Kai suddenly looked in a certain direction. In the sky over there, a white figure was darting and weaving within a cloud of black Qi, charging left and breaking right. His gaze pierced through the black Qi and saw the white figure’s face clearly, causing him to show some surprise.

He had not expected to run into a familiar face here.

After a moment's thought, the man's name sprang to mind.

Strictly speaking, he was not close to this person. They had only met once.

Still, this person had once done him a kindness, and in a certain sense, there was a connection between them.

After so many years apart, he had already advanced to the Second Stage of the Void King Realm. As expected of that man's disciple, his aptitude was extraordinary. His opponent, however, was a Third Stage Void King Realm master. The man's aura rose and fell unsteadily, much like He Yunxiang's, making it clear at a glance that he had only recently advanced to the Third Stage and had yet to stabilize his foundation.

He was a short old man, yet he wore a robe so wide it almost swallowed him whole. As his energy surged, a sinister, icy aura spread from him. In his left hand, he held a Ten Thousand Souls Banner, from which came the sounds of ghosts wailing and wolves howling, disturbing the white figure's mind. In his right hand, he held a Blood Sea Banner that kept shooting out streaks of bloody light and filthy energy.

Their cultivation differed by a Stage, and the old man's two great Secret Treasures were eerie and difficult to guard against. Even though the white figure was quite strong, he was being pressed hard and cut a sorry figure, able only to defend, with no strength to counterattack.

If the Incarnation's punch had not smashed apart Yanghe City's Grand Array and distracted the old man, the white figure would probably have already lost.

Yang Kai walked through the air toward them.

The old man's face darkened, and he could not help dividing even more of his attention, giving the white figure a chance to catch his breath.

In just two or three steps, Yang Kai had closed to within thirty zhang.

The old man immediately shouted in a low voice, "Who is Your Excellency, and why are you making trouble for my Netherworld Sect?" There was absolutely no group like this on Cuiwei Star. He had spent more than ten years here, dealing with this place's cultivators all the while. How could he not know who lived here? He had never heard of the group that had come to Yanghe City today, yet every one of them filled him with dread.

Watching Yang Kai close in, how could he hold back? Today, he feared he would not be able to wipe out the White Robe Sect, and even Yanghe City might be lost. Even so, he was a Third Stage Void King Realm master. Confident in his skill and courage, he was not in a hurry to flee. He only wanted to probe the situation and learn Yang Kai and the others' background, then invite the sect's experts to punish them later.

"Hey, old thief, take my hidden weapon!" Yang Kai was too lazy to waste words with him. With a loud shout, he reached above his head, grabbed something, and hurled it forward.

The old man and the white figure both stared dumbly, forgetting their fight for a moment.

Because the hidden weapon Yang Kai had thrown was actually the little girl who had been sitting on his neck all this time.

She was only seven or eight years old, with long hair reaching her waist and delicate, jade-carved features that would make anyone fond of her at a glance. Yet she had actually been thrown out as a hidden weapon? How could she survive that? The little girl seemed frightened senseless too, her face blank as her small body flew straight toward the old man, neither moving nor crying nor making a fuss.

The old man flew into a rage, feeling that he had been looked down on. He had meant to take the little girl's life, but the moment he met her clear black-and-white eyes, his heart inexplicably clenched.

In his panic, he swept out with the Blood Sea Banner. Bloody light flashed, and the little girl was drawn into the banner and vanished. No matter who these newcomers were, he would take a hostage first.

Seeing this, the white figure grew frantic and threw himself forward, exchanging several moves with the old man. Unfortunately, his cultivation was inferior, and he was forced back, holding a long spear at an angle as he panted heavily.

"Brother Xu, it has been many years. I trust you have been well!" Yang Kai stood in midair and greeted the white figure with a smile. A breeze swept over him, stirring his black hair and robes. If not for his earlier act of using a little girl as a hidden weapon, he truly would have looked otherworldly and elegant.

"You... you are..." Xu Binbai frowned at Yang Kai, suddenly finding his face somewhat familiar. After thinking carefully, a memory from several dozen years ago surfaced in his mind, and he cried out in shock, "It's you!"

He had recognized Yang Kai too.

Yang Kai grinned. "Who else?"

"How could you..." Xu Binbai could hardly believe Yang Kai had appeared here. After all, several dozen years ago, he had left the Star Domain. It was said that he had used those Great Emperor Tokens to forcibly tear open the Void and travel to another world.

Those who traveled with him back then were all outstanding figures of the Star Domain: Ai Ou, President of Hengluo Commerce Guild; Chi Yue, Star Master of Di Chen Star and Yao King; Gu Cangyun, Alliance Master of Sword Alliance; and also...

His own Honoured Master, Wu Dao!

For several dozen years after that departure, there had been no news at all. Wu Dao had also explained many things to him before leaving, so he understood the matter extremely well.

How could Yang Kai be here? Where was Honoured Master? Xu Binbai looked around, as if trying to find the figure who had raised him all his life, but he saw nothing.

"Who exactly are you!" The two were chatting idly, and the Netherworld Sect old man could no longer hold back, roaring the question.

Yang Kai turned to look at him. "Why does a dead man ask so many questions?"

The old man's face sank, and he gritted his teeth. "This old man will remember you. For today's enmity, my Netherworld Sect will surely repay you in the future!"

As he spoke, he shot away.

How could Xu Binbai allow him to leave just like that? He immediately moved to stop him.

Yang Kai, however, raised a hand. "Do not pursue a desperate foe!"

Xu Binbai looked at him in confusion. Although he could not see the depth of Yang Kai's cultivation, he knew the old man could not possibly be Yang Kai's opponent. That man was the Netherworld Sect's Sixth Elder. If they could kill him here, it would deal a blow to the Netherworld Sect.

Why let such a perfect opportunity slip away for nothing?

Turning back, he saw an inscrutable smile on Yang Kai's face as he watched the direction the old man had fled.

Xu Binbai seemed to sense something. He turned his head, and his eyes went wide.

The old man, who had fled several hundred zhang away, suddenly staggered as if struck by some fierce blow. Immediately afterward, specks of flame emerged from the Blood Sea Banner in his hand.

The flames were faint when they first appeared, but in the blink of an eye, they flared up with a whoosh, instantly turning the Blood Sea Banner into a ball of fire.

The old man spat blood and hastily threw the Blood Sea Banner away.

The Blood Sea Banner and the Ten Thousand Souls Banner were both Secret Treasures he had refined with his own mind and spirit. If either one was damaged, he would suffer the backlash. Now that the Blood Sea Banner was burning, there was no way he could remain unharmed.

From within the Blood Sea Banner, a tiny flame flew out, twisted, changed, and transformed into a miniature Fire Phoenix. This Fire Phoenix was only palm-sized, yet it possessed an indescribable nobility. It raised its head and cried, shaking Heaven and Earth.

The Fire Phoenix pounced. The old man's expression changed drastically, and he hurriedly swung the Ten Thousand Souls Banner. Thick black Qi wrapped around his entire body, forming a defense.

Wherever the palm-sized bird passed, the black Qi evaporated completely. The defense the old man had laid down did not work at all. Only when the flames reached him did he let out a miserable, ghostly wail, then turn into a ball of fire and fall from the sky.

Only then did a small, snow-white hand suddenly stretch out from the Blood Sea Banner, which had been burned almost to nothing, as if crossing over from another space. Immediately afterward, a small body appeared as well.

She patted her clothes, looked around, then flew back to Yang Kai. With a light leap, she climbed onto his neck again, clutching his ears with both hands to keep her balance, just as she had at the beginning.

Xu Binbai stared until his eyes went wide, his whole body left utterly disheveled in the wind.

He could be considered broadly experienced, yet what he had just witnessed made him feel as though he were dreaming.

A Third Stage Void King Realm expert had been killed so easily? What cultivation did this little girl have? And what kind of fire was that? How could it be so astonishingly effective?

As if sensing his gaze, Liu Yan turned to look at him. Her clear eyes were utterly pure, yet Xu Binbai's heart tightened, and he looked away.

"Brother Xu, let us end this fight first," Yang Kai said.

Only then did Xu Binbai come back to his senses. He nodded. "Good!"

His long spear danced, transforming into a meteor-like streak of light that fell toward the city. At the same time, he shouted, "When the White Robe Sect comes forth, evil demons retreat!"

The gathered heroes answered him. For a time, the earth shook and mountains trembled. With unmatched courage, and with Li Jiao and the others supporting them, the White Robe Sect cultivators drove the Great Wilderness Star Domain cultivators here into a rout...

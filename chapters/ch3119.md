## Chapter 3119 — The End Will Be Miserable

The vast Star Domain lay utterly silent.

In the Void, a Void Passage stood in quiet solitude, as if it had existed since time immemorial. It was no ordinary passage; it gave off an extremely unstable feeling, as though it might collapse at any moment, and radiated an aura of terrible danger.

The existence of this Void Passage was no secret in the Star Domain, but few dared to investigate it, for years ago, a Third Stage Void King Realm expert had vanished here and was never heard from again.

Because of this, although some had speculated over the years that the Void Passage led to some unknown world, no one had paid it any mind. Even a Third Stage Void King Realm expert had disappeared here, her fate unknown, so who would dare approach?

The place had been desolate for several dozen years.

But at this moment, the Void Passage suddenly changed. The mouth of the passage contracted violently, then expanded, repeating the cycle several times before a burst of dazzling multicolored light erupted from within and poured out.

Spat out along with the light was a figure. He shot from the mouth of the passage like an arrow loosed from a bow, hurtling a hundred li before he finally managed to steady himself.

This person was naturally Yang Kai.

At the moment, he was covered in dust and looked rather disheveled.

Returning to the Hengluo Star Domain from the Star Path entrance in the Ancestral Domain, passing through layer after layer of Void cracks, had taken no small amount of effort. He could not use too much strength, or he would very likely shatter the Void, but he could not use too little either, or he would never break through the barrier.

Only after many attempts had he found the road home.

Once he steadied himself, Yang Kai immediately sank his mind into his senses. A moment later, joy spread across his face, and he burst into laughter.

He had sensed Dark Star.

He spread both arms wide and embraced the universe!

Hengluo Star Domain, this Young Master has returned!

When he left his homeland back then, how could he have imagined he would return so soon? He had originally given himself a limit of one hundred years, yet now he had not even spent half that time. All the thorns and hardships along the way, all the life-and-death battles, had become profoundly worthwhile. The faces he had missed day and night grew clearer in his mind, and he wished he could sprout wings and fly to Dark Star this instant, appear before them, and see their surprised, delighted expressions.

Looking back, Yang Kai gazed at the Void Passage. He knew this was where Su Yan had entered the Ancestral Domain, and as he felt the aura coming from within it, a lingering fear rose in his heart. For Su Yan to have entered the Ancestral Domain safely, she had likely been extraordinarily lucky.

Taking a deep breath, Yang Kai raised his hand, and Su Yan appeared before him.

She seemed to have been meditating. Sensing the change, she opened her beautiful eyes, looked around, and smiled. "We're back?"

On the road back to his homeland, Yang Kai had taken everyone into the Small Xuan Realm. For one thing, carrying people between planes consumed an enormous amount of strength. For another, Su Yan was now at the Second Stage of the Dao Origin Realm, far beyond the limit the Hengluo Star Domain's world could bear, so she had to suppress and seal her cultivation.

Judging by her current state, she had done well, though she had suppressed herself a little too thoroughly. She now gave off only the aura of a Second Stage Void King Realm cultivator.

"We're back!" Yang Kai took a deep breath. Naturally there was no air in the Starry Sky, yet he could still smell that familiar scent.

He held out a hand, and Su Yan placed hers in it.

Yang Kai gripped her hand tightly, as if he never meant to let go again. Hand in hand with her, he was as excited as a child. "Let's go home!"

There was no need to determine a direction. His close connection with Dark Star was enough to guide him along the right path. Their figures flickered, and the two turned into a streak of light, racing toward Dark Star.

With a beauty at his side, the journey was no longer lonely.

Whenever the mood struck, he dragged Su Yan down onto a nearby Dead Star or asteroid for a bout of intimacy. After this happened several times, Su Yan had no choice but to activate her Ice-Crystal Yu Body and spoil Yang Kai's fun.

Several days later, a huge star appeared in the distance.

The Stellar Origin of Dark Star within him beat faster and faster, like a wandering son returning home.

Yang Kai once again grew inexplicably nervous, unexpectedly feeling a trace of timidity as he neared home. His hand tightened, only for Su Yan to grip his hand in return. When their eyes met, Yang Kai's heart settled into perfect clarity.

He wrapped an arm around her, kissed her forehead, and called softly, "Go!"

Another half day passed, and they were already very close to Dark Star.

Yang Kai and Su Yan suddenly turned to look toward a stretch of the Void. Over there, more than a dozen colossal Warships hung across the Starry Sky, and waves of powerful aura could be faintly sensed from within them.

"Which Sect do those belong to?" Yang Kai asked, pointing at a skull-like symbol on one of the Warships.

Perhaps he had been away too long, for he did not recognize which force the symbol represented.

To his surprise, Su Yan also shook her head. "I don't recognize it."

She, too, had been gone for many years, so she had no way of knowing what changes had taken place in the Hengluo Star Domain or on Dark Star.

A figure suddenly appeared on the deck of the largest Warship in the middle. His aura was deep and unfathomable, and he clearly possessed the bearing of a Third Stage Void King Realm cultivator. He was an old man, though not yet ancient. From several hundred li away, his sharp eyes looked in their direction, as if they could pierce the Void and see the essence of all things.

Yang Kai grinned at him.

The old man raised his hand. A Star Cannon on the Warship swung toward Yang Kai and Su Yan from afar. Dazzling light gathered at its mouth, and a beam of light thundered toward them.

"Hahahaha!" Yang Kai laughed. "I run into a madman the moment I come back. Let's go have a chat with him."

When the light faded, Yang Kai and Su Yan were nowhere to be seen. The old man stood on the deck and stared for a while, then snorted softly and turned to go back into the ship.

His steps suddenly froze. A chill enveloped his whole body, and he stared ahead in horror.

Behind him, at some unknown point, a man and a woman had appeared. They were precisely the two people he had just seen several hundred li away, the two who should have been reduced to ash by the Star Cannon.

Yet they had appeared behind him completely unharmed! He had not sensed the slightest trace of them.

"Old Sir, your killing intent is rather heavy." Yang Kai looked at him with a beaming smile. If this had been any other time, he would already have punched this old man into scraps, but today Yang Kai was in an excellent mood and had little desire to kill. Moreover, he did not know the old man's identity or background, so acting rashly would be unwise. If he killed someone he should not, that would be troublesome.

He had many ties in the Hengluo Star Domain, so he naturally could not act entirely as he pleased.

The old man did not answer. He only stared at Yang Kai with a grave expression, instinctively sensing that the young man before him was not someone he could afford to provoke. A drop of cold sweat slid down his forehead. His hands were tucked into his sleeves, secretly forming a hand seal, ready to counterattack at any moment.

"Old Sir, who are you? Whose Warships are these? What are they doing here?"

Yang Kai looked around and asked his questions casually, but received no answer.

He walked forward, circled the old man several times, then stopped in front of him, leaned down slightly, and stared him in the eye. "Old Sir, you seem very nervous."

More sweat ran down the old man's forehead.

Yang Kai reached out, patted his shoulder, and grinned. "Relax. As long as you're not an enemy, I won't do anything to you. Are you an enemy?"

"N-no!" the old man forced out with difficulty.

Yang Kai nodded. "Then that's best. Mm, I forgot to introduce myself. I am the master of this place. My name is Yang Kai!" He pointed at the huge star beneath their feet.

The old man's eyes narrowed in horror. He had never heard that this Cultivation Star had a Star Lord, but Yang Kai spoke with such seriousness that he truly did not know whether to believe him.

"I've been away from here for several dozen years, and today is my first time coming back to take a look. There are still several women waiting for me at home. Old Sir, what gift do you think I should bring them?"

Why are you asking me? the old man grumbled inwardly, but he did not dare refuse to answer. After giving it some serious thought, he said, "It is enough that you return. I imagine they won't care about gifts."

Yang Kai stroked his chin and nodded, deeply convinced. "Old Sir's words make some sense." Suddenly, he laughed. "I am the best gift! Right, Senior Sister?"

Su Yan said, "Right!"

"Many thanks for your guidance, Old Sir!" Yang Kai cupped his fist to the old man, took a deep breath, and said, "I should hurry back, or they'll start getting anxious."

Hurry up and leave, then. Where did this lunatic come from? And that flower-like, jade-like woman beside him, how pitiful that she would actually follow him so devotedly.

Fortunately, Yang Kai truly was eager to return, and once he said he was leaving, he left.

Suddenly, he turned back and looked at the old man. "Old Sir, you really aren't an enemy, are you?"

"I really am not!"

"Good." Yang Kai nodded. "Anyone who becomes my enemy meets a very miserable end."

The old man's heart lurched, and a chill shot straight from the soles of his feet to the top of his head. When he looked up again, there was no trace of the man and woman before him. Somehow, they had already vanished. Even when he swept the area with his Divine Sense, he found no aura at all, as if they had never appeared.

Swallowing with difficulty, the old man immediately took out a Transmission Compass and poured his Divine Sense into it. His withered hands trembled uncontrollably.

Lingxiao Sect.

As a newly risen force, Lingxiao Sect's rise could only be called a miracle. The Sect had been founded several dozen years ago, yet in a mere few decades, experts were now as numerous as clouds within the Sect, while Void King Realm cultivators emerged one after another. There were four or five Third Stage Void King Realm cultivators alone. It had already become the strongest Sect in the Star Domain; even established forces such as Hengluo Commerce Guild, Purple Star, and Sword Alliance could not compare.

At this moment, heroes gathered like clouds inside Lingxiao Sect's discussion hall.

At the head sat the acting Sect Master, who was also Lingxiao Sect's Great Elder, Ye Xijun. Elders Chu Lingxiao, Ling Taixu, Yang Xiuzhu, Chu Hanyi, Lin Yurao, Qing Ya, and the others sat on either side, every one of them possessing Void King Realm cultivation.

Further down, Wu Yi, Yang Wei, Dong Qinghan, and a dozen or so others of the younger generation also possessed Origin Returning Realm cultivation. Quite a few had already reached the peak of the Origin Returning Realm and could break through to the Void King Realm at any time.

The hall was filled with noisy discussion. Not only did the older generation have their disagreements, even the younger generation were arguing with one another.

Ye Xijun sat silently at the head, taking every word into her ears.

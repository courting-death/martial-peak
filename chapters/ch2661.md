## Chapter 2661 — Is There Any Justice?

Emperor Yuan surged, and as the crowd gathered, a great battle was about to erupt.

Facing the combined assault of more than a dozen Emperor Realm experts, Yang Kai didn’t dodge or retreat. Instead, he burst out laughing, drawing startled glances from the crowd—no one could fathom what had gone wrong with this young man.

Yet, with the arrow already nocked, even though everyone sensed something was off, they could not stop. Each one fixed their gaze on the Spatial Ring in Yang Kai’s hand.

In an instant, the dozen or so people rushed at Yang Kai, each unleashing their own techniques. Fierce attacks rained down in a wild barrage.

“Condense!” A cold snort rang out. Under the Laws of Space, the space within a hundred zhang radius instantly became thick and solidified.

This sudden change shocked the attackers. Their Emperor Yuan immediately became sluggish, as if trapped in a deep swamp. They felt as though it was difficult to move, their techniques lost much of their power, and even the Imperial Treasures they summoned dimmed.

“What!” The faces of the crowd changed dramatically. Sensing danger, they tried to retreat, but it was already too late.

Yang Kai raised both hands, transforming them into countless palm shadows that blasted forward.

A series of explosions rang out. Figures were sent flying in utter disgrace, each spitting blood in midair, their faces pale as paper.

Pa pa pa...

The dozen Emperor Realm experts fell to the ground like dumplings. When they finally stood up again, every face was ashen, their Divine Souls shaken.

In contrast, Yang Kai, having repelled so many Emperor Realm experts at once, seemed to have expended considerable effort. His figure swayed slightly, but he showed no sign of retreat. The color in his face faded for a moment, then quickly returned to normal.

Yet his silhouette remained upright before the mountain gate, like a divine pillar holding up the heavens. He stood firm, guarding this land.

“How is this possible!”

“He’s only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm?”

“How… how did he do that?”

…

A strange sense of unreality welled up in the hearts of the dozen Emperor Realm experts, as if they had suddenly found themselves trapped in a dream.

Previously, they didn’t know Yang Kai’s true strength, but seeing his young age, they assumed his cultivation was not high, so they didn’t take him seriously. As it turned out, their guess was correct. When Yang Kai made his move, his cultivation was exposed.

He was indeed at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, no stronger than any of them.

Yet, it was precisely this First Stage Emperor who, single-handedly, shattered their joint attack and injured every one of them.

If this hadn’t happened to them personally, no one would believe it. It was simply unbelievable.

Especially for someone like Li Qingyuan, a Second Stage of the Emperor Realm expert. He had dominated the Northern Territory for years and had never encountered such a bizarre event. He had fought many First Stage Emperor Realm cultivators, always crushing them. But who could match the ferocity of this young man before him?

The greedy, covetous looks in their eyes gradually faded, replaced by a heavy, oppressive solemnity.

Rumor had it that some Martial Dao geniuses in this world, though not highly cultivated, could fight across realms and even kill. Li Qingyuan had seen such people. But those so-called geniuses could at best fight him to a draw. Compared to the man before him, they were mere mediocrities.

This man not only fought across realms, but even faced more than a dozen opponents without falling behind.

Li Qingyuan even suspected this young man hadn’t used his full strength. If he did, perhaps half of them would die in an instant!

At this thought, cold sweat suddenly broke out on Li Qingyuan’s forehead…

If the First Stage of the Emperor Realm was already so formidable, what if he advanced to the Second or even Third Stage? Wouldn’t he be invincible? By then, only those who had ascended to the position of Great Emperor would be his match.

“Who are you, young man?!”

Someone cried out in anguish, unwilling to accept being defeated. They wondered if Yang Kai had used some trick, feeling very unconvinced.

This was the question everyone wanted answered. Such an outstanding youth couldn’t be an unknown newcomer; at the very least, they should have heard his name.

Turning to the questioner, Yang Kai grinned and declared loudly, “I am Yang Kai. Surely you all aren’t strangers to the name!”

“Hiss… He’s Yang Kai?”

“The one who killed Feng Xi in the Shattered Star Sea, enraging Feng Xuan—Yang Kai?”

“The battle between Ice Heart Valley and Wenqing Sect was all because of him.”

“I heard Wenqing Sect was wiped out thanks to his scheming. He somehow brought in powerful allies and killed Feng Xuan, Yao Zhuo, and the others!”

It must be said, in the Northern Territory, Yang Kai was quite well-known, thanks to Feng Xuan’s previous publicity. After all, the battle between the two sects was a major event. Wenqing Sect couldn’t act without reason, and Yang Kai’s killing of Feng Xi, with Bing Yun sheltering him, was the reason Wenqing Sect declared war on Ice Heart Valley.

Before this, Northern Territory cultivators were curious about Yang Kai, who dared to kill Feng Xi. They secretly thought this young man would not meet a good end.

But no one expected that the final outcome would be Wenqing Sect’s extermination, and the instigator would come to Wenqing Sect’s base to found a new sect, seizing the nest to establish Lingxiao Palace!

If Great Emperor Wenqing knew, he’d probably die of rage and come back to life just to be angry again.

For a moment, outside the mountain gate, silence reigned. Whether it was the dozen Emperor Realm experts or the thousands of cultivators outside, all stared blankly at Yang Kai.

Shua shua shua…

Several sounds of air being split rang out, and three figures suddenly appeared beside Yang Kai. As soon as they arrived, they unleashed overwhelming pressure. The intensity was such that the whole world seemed to dim, and everyone felt their breathing stall, as if a mountain pressed upon their chests.

All eyes turned to the three figures, each person’s gaze trembling.

Though their true forms were unclear, the powerful pressure told everyone present that any one of these three could slaughter everyone here without effort.

A chill surged up from the soles of everyone’s feet, racing straight to the crown of their heads. The faces of thousands turned ashen, as if they had just lost their parents. Though their hearts screamed to flee, not a single one dared to move recklessly—they could only stand rooted in place, enduring this torment.

“Young Master Yang, what happened here? Why do I sense the aftermath of a battle?” Xie Wuwei asked, his brows furrowing in concern.

“Hmph, it seems someone was causing trouble here.” Yingfei said, his eagle-like gaze sweeping over the dozen Emperor Realm experts, his expression extremely unfriendly.

“Good courage! Who dares cause trouble? Let this old bull meet them!” Xi Lei shouted, his Yao Yuan surging, stirring up a demonic wind as if to pierce the heavens.

“Yao!” Someone sensed Yao Qi and cried out in alarm.

“It’s a Yao King!” Li Qingyuan screamed, cold sweat pouring down his forehead, his face pale as paper.

Though he was a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert, facing a Yao King comparable to the Third Stage, he felt powerless and instinctively terrified. Moreover, if he was not mistaken, the other two were also yao, equally powerful Yao Kings.

Heavens! Yao Kings were rarer than Third Stage Emperor Realm experts, yet today he saw three at once. He didn’t know if it was luck or misfortune. Thinking back on what had happened, Li Qingyuan felt the latter was more likely.

After Li Qingyuan shouted “Yao King,” the dozen Emperor Realm experts and thousands of cultivators all stepped back in terror, staring at Xi Lei as if he were a monstrous beast, ready to devour them.

“Who was causing trouble just now? Step forward for this King!” Xi Lei glared, his voice booming like thunder.

Who dared answer? Fearing his attention, no one wanted to die without reason. The sound of swallowing echoed, but their feet seemed nailed in place.

The dozen Emperor Realm experts all looked to Li Qingyuan, hoping he would speak up and negotiate with the Yao King.

Li Qingyuan cursed the cowardly lot, but at this point, not speaking would be even worse. He could only say dryly, “Yao King, it’s a misunderstanding, a misunderstanding.”

“Misunderstanding?” Xi Lei glanced at Li Qingyuan and snorted, “How is it a misunderstanding?”

Li Qingyuan racked his brains, choosing his words carefully as he replied, “It’s like this. We are all Northern Territory cultivators. Hearing that Young Master Yang was founding a sect here, we came to offer congratulations. We failed to recognize Young Master Yang and were disrespectful. Please forgive us, Young Master Yang and esteemed lords.”

His words were eloquent and clear, and even Li Qingyuan admired his own cleverness.

“Founding a sect?” Xi Lei was stunned, looking to Yang Kai for confirmation. Though he’d mentioned it a few days ago, Yang Kai hadn’t given a clear answer. He wondered why Li Qingyuan brought it up now.

Yingfei, sharp-eyed, spotted the nearby Ten-Thousand-Year Coldheart Iron and clapped his hands. “Young Master Yang really is founding a sect here—Lingxiao Palace! Lofty ambitions, marvelous!”

Xie Wuwei laughed heartily, “Excellent, excellent! From now on, I’ll stay in Lingxiao Palace. Young Master Yang, just grant me a Deacon position or something.”

“Count this old bull in too!” Xi Lei dropped his fierce demeanor and instead put on a fawning smile, looking at Yang Kai. “Young Master Yang, don’t play favorites!”

Yingfei nodded, “I feel the same.”

The dozen Emperor Realm experts fainted, and the thousands of cultivators were dumbfounded. Each felt an unreal sensation, doubting their own hearing.

These were three mighty Yao Kings—experts whose strength rivaled that of Third Stage Emperor Realm masters. Yang Kai had only just carved the mark of his sect here, not even formally establishing Lingxiao Palace, yet these three couldn’t wait to pledge themselves to him.

And they only asked for a Deacon position…

Is there any justice left? Third Stage Emperor Realm experts are overlords of the Star Realm, grandmasters of their generation. With such strength, they could found their own sects. But now, they were begging to join this newly established Lingxiao Palace.

What kind of virtue or ability did Yang Kai have to make three Yao Kings flock to him?

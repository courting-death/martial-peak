## Chapter 3127 — Su Yan's Other Side

In the palace on Lingxiao Peak's main summit, everything remained spotless despite Yang Kai having been away for several dozen years.

Su Yan sat by the window, one hand propping up her smooth chin as she looked out at the view beyond. Her thoughts drifted through all she had experienced over the years, and a faint smile touched her lips.

She suddenly sensed something and turned her head, only to see Yang Kai leaning against the doorframe with his arms folded, quietly watching her.

"You're back?" Su Yan rose to greet him, but she had taken only two steps when his imposing body pressed aggressively close. He reached out, caught her around the waist, and with the slightest pull drew her helplessly against him. A full, springy softness immediately pressed into his chest.

"Senior Sister, you're so beautiful." Yang Kai lowered his head to gaze at the woman before him, his tongue as sweet as honey. His eyes lingered on her delicate nose and mouth before he bent to kiss those soft red lips.

Su Yan braced one hand against his chest and pressed a delicate finger to his lips. “You reek of wine. How much did you drink?”

Yang Kai grinned. "Not much. Roughly a cup with everyone."

"Sit." Su Yan took his hand, led him to the bedside, and gave the order.

Yang Kai obediently sat down.

Su Yan turned, brought over the clear water she had prepared long ago, wrung out a towel, and wiped his face. Yang Kai sat perfectly still and let her tend to him, warmth filling his heart.

A short while later, once he was clean, Su Yan turned to leave. Before she could react, Yang Kai pulled her back into his arms. The basin and washwater in her hands spilled out together, but Yang Kai waved a hand, and all the scattered items flew neatly back to their places.

He rolled over, pinned Su Yan beneath him, and stared at her with fiery eyes, as if he meant to burn her up.

Su Yan lightly pursed her red lips. Her long lashes trembled like tiny fans, and she turned her eyes away, as if she did not quite dare meet his gaze.

Yang Kai found it wonderfully amusing. They had been an old married couple for so many years, and who knew how many times they had dual cultivated, yet Senior Sister was still this shy. A woman was still a woman after all. Still, that bashful look only made it easier to stir certain desires in a man.

He reached out, tipped up Su Yan's chin, caught that smooth point between his fingers, and forced her to look at him.

Su Yan grew faintly annoyed, her expression full of reproach.

If Senior Sister suddenly showed the same seductive charm as Shan Qingluo, now that would be interesting. Just imagining this ice-clear, jade-pure, utterly unstained woman revealing every kind of charm and allure made Yang Kai feel as if a volcano inside him were about to erupt.

His gaze grew even more aggressive.

Su Yan finally could not bear it and said, "Have you gone to see Father and Mother? Mother has been talking about you constantly these past few days."

"I went." The hand Yang Kai had beneath her chin slowly climbed to her earlobe and kneaded it gently. As he spoke, he breathed warm air over that earlobe. This was her most sensitive spot. How could Yang Kai not know that?

Su Yan's breathing clearly grew unsteady. Biting her lower lip lightly with her pearly teeth, she asked, "Mother didn't scold you, did she?"

She had kept Fourth Master Yang and his wife company for a month, so she naturally knew what Dong Suzhu had been complaining about lately. She was only afraid Yang Kai might have suffered some grievance with Dong Suzhu.

"It would have been better if she had scolded me."

"What happened?"

Yang Kai said gloomily, "She won't even acknowledge me as her son anymore."

Su Yan pressed her lips together and smiled. "Serves you right!"

Yang Kai glared. "Even you are taking pleasure in my misfortune?"

Su Yan said, "Mother told me to give you a proper lesson when I came back."

"Give me a lesson?" Yang Kai's expression immediately turned strange. He looked Su Yan over with great interest. "Then may I ask how Senior Sister plans to deal with me? Tell me, so Junior Brother can prepare himself."

"I don't know..." Su Yan turned her head aside, a rosy flush already spreading over her slender neck.

Yang Kai covered that full place with one hand and kneaded hard. Su Yan's brows immediately drew together, but that only gave him a different sort of feeling.

"Since Senior Sister won't say, I'll just exhaust myself to death for you." How could Yang Kai hold back any longer? A fierce light suddenly bloomed in his eyes. He seized Su Yan's clothing with one hand and tore hard.

With a loud rip, her palace dress split open. Su Yan cried out and stared at Yang Kai in astonishment, her face filled with confusion.

For all these years, Yang Kai had always been incomparably gentle with her. This was the first time he had moved so roughly. She thought he might be drunk and acting wild from the wine, but when she looked closely, where was the slightest drunkenness in his eyes? Though they were filled with fierce light, they remained utterly clear. Yet in bed, he seemed to have become someone else entirely.

Su Yan's heart trembled for no reason. She was just about to speak when Yang Kai bent down and kissed her.

"Mm..." Su Yan let out a muffled sound. For the first time, she felt Yang Kai use such force, wanton and overbearing, and it actually left her feeling unable to withstand him.

At the same time, the torn garments on her body were pulled completely apart, revealing a figure like white mutton-fat jade, exposed to the air and gleaming with an unusual luster.

Two large hands like iron pincers roamed over and ravaged that flawless body, as if destroying a delicate work of art. There was not the slightest pity in them, and the force was great enough that Su Yan actually felt pain.

Yet mingled with the pain was an indescribable feeling.

It was a feeling she had never experienced before. Su Yan's body suddenly went rigid. A deep flush quickly rose over her snow-white skin, and in her heart came the sound of some barrier shattering as instincts suppressed and sealed away for many years were released.

Yang Kai was somewhat astonished.

Senior Sister had actually reached out of her own accord and wrapped both arms around his neck. She even sought out his kiss herself, looking dazed with passion and unable to control herself.

This shocked him greatly.

Although he had dual cultivated with Su Yan many times, this was the first time he had ever seen her so proactive. His spirits immediately rose, and the strength in his hands increased greatly, with not the slightest pity left. After kneading hard for a while, an extremely wild thought sprang into his mind.

It had to be like this. It had to be like this. Nothing else would do!

As if possessed, Yang Kai had only one voice clamoring in his mind, urging him to put the thought into action. He suddenly exerted his strength and turned Su Yan around, making her face away from him.

Su Yan looked back at him, her beautiful eyes hazy and confused.

Smack...

A crisp sound rang out, and a five-finger mark immediately appeared on that snow-white, rounded place.

Su Yan's head jerked up, her hair flying and her small mouth falling open like a fish out of water. A long inhalation sounded in her throat, yet not the slightest breath came back out.

Smack...

Another slap. The two five-finger marks were perfectly symmetrical on left and right, without the slightest deviation.

Su Yan's head lifted even higher. Her graceful waist sank downward as if it might snap, making that suddenly rising curve seem all the more soul-stealing and spirit-seizing. Her tender body actually began to tremble.

That doglike kneeling posture made her feel humiliated, yet she could not stop herself. Her mind went blank, and her whole body seemed to climb into the clouds, rising and falling.

A woman's nature told her she could not be like this, but her body's instincts could not refuse.

Smack smack smack...

Crisp sounds rang out one after another.

Su Yan could no longer suppress herself. Low moans and cries that made the blood surge spilled from her throat, like weeping and laughter, like madness and frenzy. She kept shaking her head, her waterfall-like black hair whipping back and forth as tears slid from the corners of her eyes. "No, no, Junior Brother, no more. Please..."

The demonic nature in Yang Kai's heart was released. How could he still heed her? He only felt he was doing the most amusing thing in the world. His large hand lifted high, fell, left marks on that snow-white skin, and then he admired Su Yan's expression and reactions, his breathing heavy, each exhale like flame.

Inside the great hall, crisp sounds and Su Yan's cries rang out without end.

No one knew how much time had passed before those cries gradually weakened. Her voice not only grew much quieter, but even turned hoarse. After a long while, Su Yan could no longer make a sound at all. Only the sounds of Yang Kai wantonly galloping and lashing the reins remained.

...

"Senior Sister, Senior Sister..." Yang Kai sat by the bedside and gently poked the person on the bed with his finger.

Su Yan had pulled the silk quilt over herself completely and lay on her side with her back to him, saying nothing.

Yang Kai scratched his head and inwardly cursed himself for being a brute. How could he have abused Su Yan like that? But in that situation, with that atmosphere, the thoughts in his heart had surged up and become impossible to suppress, and somehow he had done such a foolish thing.

Finished, finished, he was finished this time. Senior Sister was definitely angry. He did not know whether she would still pay attention to him, and along with guilt, a trace of unease rose in his heart.

"Senior Sister, if you're angry, just beat me up. My skin is thick and my flesh is tough anyway, so I won't break." Yang Kai lay down on his side beside her, propped himself up with one hand, and used the other to pull her into his arms.

There was still no movement.

Yang Kai sighed. "I was wrong. I was truly wrong. Don't be angry. What if you hurt yourself with anger?"

"No." Su Yan's voice came from inside the silk quilt.

"No what?" Yang Kai's spirits lifted. As long as she was willing to answer, that was good. What he feared was her refusing to say a word.

Su Yan turned over, seemingly to face him, though the silk quilt covered her and he could not see her expression. "I'm not angry with you."

"Then what's wrong?"

"I'm angry with myself."

Yang Kai was speechless. He reached out to lift the silk quilt, but Su Yan clutched it tightly and pleaded in a low voice, "Don't. I don't have the face to see you. Don't open it."

Yang Kai could not help laughing. So she was not angry with him, only embarrassed.

Thinking about it, that made sense. After all these years, this was the first time he and she had gone so wild. Never mind that Su Yan was a woman; even Yang Kai felt somewhat embarrassed now that he thought back on it.

He gently pried open her fingers and, with great difficulty, freed her head from under the silk quilt. Su Yan, however, kept her eyes tightly shut, her trembling lashes revealing the nervous unease in her heart.

"Look at me," Yang Kai said in a low voice.

Su Yan pursed her lips. Only after a long while did she slowly open her eyes. When she met Yang Kai's teasing gaze, she tried to cover her face with the silk quilt again.

"All right." Yang Kai reached out to stop her, smoothed the hair from her forehead, and gazed at her tenderly. "I like you no matter what you're like."

Or perhaps, he liked the way she had been yesterday even more.

Su Yan covered her face with her hand, her voice coming through the gaps between her fingers. "Don't be like that next time."

"All right, all right." Yang Kai nodded repeatedly, leaned down, and kissed her once, though it was only a brief taste. Smiling, he said, "Time to get up. Come with me to see Father and Mother."

If he went alone, he might very well be thrown out by his mother again. If he brought Su Yan with him, Mother probably would not refuse to give her at least some face.

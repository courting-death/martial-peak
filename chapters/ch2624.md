## Chapter 2624 — The Ice Heart Valley Outcast

"Ah!" Dong Hai's face turned pale, and he nearly collapsed to the ground.

This time, in order to capture Shi Tianhe, all the Third Stage Dao Origin Realm Elders of the Dong Family had been mobilized. After all, this was a matter of great importance and could not be allowed to fail. They thought it was a foolproof plan, but unexpectedly, they were wiped out in one fell swoop.

The Dong Family suddenly lost so many top experts; their family’s power was bound to plummet. By then, it wouldn’t be impossible for the other major families in the city to swallow them up.

Dong Hai could hardly accept this reality, his mind going blank.

"Who goes there!" Commander Yu was also shocked and horrified. As the Commander of Taiping City’s guards, he had no idea when an Emperor Realm expert had appeared in the city. Such negligence was a grave dereliction of duty.

What’s more, the pressure Ji Yao gave him was even greater than that of the City Lord himself.

In other words, this cold woman was even more formidable than the City Lord.

An expert above the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm? Commander Yu broke out in a cold sweat.

Around the corner, Bai Lu and Xue Man had witnessed everything that had just happened, both still shaken with lingering fear.

They had thought that, between Yang Kai and Ji Yao, only Yang Kai, as the Master, was an Emperor Realm expert. Only now did they realize that Ji Yao was also an Emperor Realm expert.

Just yesterday, Xue Man had even wanted to buy their Multi-deck Ship. Thinking back, it was clear they had shown mercy; otherwise, the two of them would have lost their lives on the spot.

"Commander Yu Zhen of Taiping City greets the honored one. May I ask your esteemed name?" Commander Yu, wiping his cold sweat, hurried out with small steps, cupping his fists respectfully to Ji Yao.

In front of a suspected Second Stage Emperor Realm expert, he dared not act presumptuously. After all, the City Lord was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. If he angered this expert and a massacre ensued, Taiping City would be doomed.

Ji Yao seemed not to hear Yu Zhen’s words, only turning her head to frown at Shi Tianhe.

Shi Tianhe was also stunned, gripping her longsword. She looked around and saw that all the Dong Family Elders who had troubled her were now dead, not a single one left.

"Senior..." Shi Tianhe’s face was full of gratitude as she looked at Ji Yao and said, "Thank you, Senior, for saving me!"

She had thought she was doomed. Who could have known that, at this critical moment, a benefactor would appear and turn misfortune into blessing? Shi Tianhe’s gratitude overflowed from her heart.

"Are you a disciple of the Ice Heart Valley?" Ji Yao asked with a frown.

As soon as these words were spoken, it was as if a giant stone had been thrown into a calm lake, stirring up a huge commotion.

"What? The Dong Family’s matron is a disciple of Ice Heart Valley? Is that true?"

"No way? If that’s really the case, then isn’t the Dong Family finished?"

"No wonder the Dong Family Head would rather expel his own wife than keep ties with her. So she’s a disciple of the Ice Heart Valley."

"The Dong Family Head is truly ruthless. If it were me, I couldn’t bear to drive such a beautiful wife out."

The crowd’s discussions spread, causing both Ji Yao and Yang Kai to frown repeatedly, not knowing what was going on.

Hearing Ji Yao’s question, Shi Tianhe gave a bitter smile. "I dare not claim to be a disciple of Ice Heart Valley. I was expelled from the Sect by my Master a hundred years ago."

"Expelled from the Sect? What mistake did you make?" Ji Yao asked in surprise.

Disciples of the Ice Heart Valley—unless they committed a grave offense—would never be expelled from the Sect.

Shi Tianhe’s face was bitter as she glanced at Dong Hai below.

Ji Yao understood and snorted coldly, "So it was because of a man! You really were blind, willing to be expelled from your Sect just to follow him. And now? He’s cast you out of the Dong Family as well."

At these words, Shi Tianhe’s eyes reddened, clearly struck at her sorest spot, and she was left speechless.

"It’s over, it’s all over..." Dong Hai heard the conversation between Ji Yao and Shi Tianhe, and his expression suddenly aged, his body swaying unsteadily.

He knew the Dong Family was truly finished this time. Not only had the Elders suffered heavy losses and the family’s power plummeted, but now Shi Tianhe’s connection to the Ice Heart Valley had been exposed. That alone was enough to doom the Dong Family forever!

After today, there would be no place for the Dong Family in Taiping City. Even if the other major families didn’t move against them, the City Lord would never tolerate them.

Even though Shi Tianhe had been expelled from Ice Heart Valley a hundred years ago, she had once been a disciple. In the entire Northern Territory, anyone with even the slightest connection to the Ice Heart Valley never met a good end.

"So she’s from the Ice Heart Valley..." Yang Kai also looked surprised.

Earlier, he had overheard Commander Yu and Dong Hai talking. Commander Yu had mentioned that Madam Dong came from some great Sect, but Yang Kai hadn’t paid it any mind. Only now did he realize that the great Sect was the Ice Heart Valley!

In the Northern Territory, the Ice Heart Valley was indeed a first-class power. For one of its disciples to marry into a small Dong Family in Taiping City was a tremendous honor for the Dong Family.

"Whose disciple are you?" Ji Yao asked again.

Shi Tianhe replied softly, "Senior, my Master’s surname is An—her full name is An Ruoyun."

"You’re a disciple under Senior Sister?" Ji Yao’s whole body trembled.

An Ruoyun, a Second Stage Emperor Realm expert, was the eldest among Bing Yun’s personal disciples, the Senior Sister to all the other sisters. During the years when Bing Yun was missing, it was An Ruoyun who managed the Ice Heart Valley and held it together.

It could be said that the name An Ruoyun was known to nearly every cultivator in the Northern Territory.

So when Shi Tianhe said her Master was An Ruoyun, the whole place was in an uproar. No one had expected the former matron of the Dong Family to have such a background!

When they looked at Dong Hai again, their gazes became much more complicated. An Ruoyun’s disciple had married him as his wife—what virtue or ability did Dong Hai possess? Unfortunately, what was once a source of glory had now become the Dong Family’s death sentence.

"Senior Sister?" Shi Tianhe was stunned, staring blankly at Ji Yao. "Senior, you are...?"

This person called her Master ‘Senior Sister’, so she was clearly from the Ice Heart Valley as well. Shi Tianhe’s mind flashed through the faces of the various Senior Aunts in Ice Heart Valley, but none matched the person before her, leaving her full of doubt.

"I am your Third Senior Aunt!" Ji Yao said coolly.

Shi Tianhe’s eyes lit up and she blurted out, "You’re... Senior Aunt Ji Yao!"

When she was in the Ice Heart Valley, Ji Yao had already disappeared, so it was normal that she had never met her. But during her days in the Valley, she had heard her Master mention this long-missing Senior Aunt. The other Senior Aunts in the Valley all worried about her safety, but no one knew where she was.

The Ice Heart Valley had sent people out to search many times, but there had never been any news.

Who would have thought that today, in Taiping City, she would run into her.

For a moment, Shi Tianhe was overjoyed, her sorrow eased by meeting Ji Yao. She hurriedly knelt before Ji Yao and said, "Shi Tianhe, the outcast of the Ice Heart Valley, greets Third Senior Aunt!"

"Get up!" Ji Yao raised her hand and lifted Shi Tianhe up, snorting coldly. "As a disciple of Senior Sister, you actually defied her for a man. If I were Senior Sister, I’d have killed you myself. How could I have let you live until today!"

Shi Tianhe gave a bitter smile and said nothing.

Ji Yao said, "But since Senior Sister spared your life, it’s not my place to overstep. Now that you’ve been expelled from the Dong Family, you are no longer one of them. Come back to the Ice Heart Valley with me and beg Senior Sister for forgiveness! Whether you live or die will be up to her alone."

Shi Tianhe wept, "Thank you, Third Senior Aunt!"

Ji Yao turned and looked coldly at the dazed Dong Hai below. "Though Tianhe is an outcast of the Ice Heart Valley, not just anyone can bully her. She abandoned her Sect to follow you, and if you had treated her well, that would have been fine. But now you’ve betrayed her as well. You deserve to die!"

As she spoke, she sent a gentle palm strike downward.

"No!" Shi Tianhe cried out in alarm, darting forward to kneel before Ji Yao and beg, "Third Senior Aunt, don’t kill him! Please, I beg you, don’t kill him!"

Emperor Yuan surged in Ji Yao’s palm, but she held back, glaring at Shi Tianhe in anger. "Even now, you still want to protect him?"

Shi Tianhe shook her head, weeping. "He saved my life once. Now, I only ask that you spare him, Senior Aunt. Consider it me repaying that debt. From now on, all ties between us are severed!"

Ji Yao was furious, wishing she could kill her foolish niece with a single palm. Emperor Yuan surged in her palm several times, but in the end, she couldn’t bring herself to do it. After all, Shi Tianhe’s fate had already been hard enough.

Helpless, Ji Yao could only turn to Yang Kai and ask, "Master, what do you think?"

Yang Kai said indifferently, "He can be spared from death, but not from punishment!"

As he spoke, he walked up to Dong Hai and struck his chest with a palm. Dong Hai didn’t even react before he was sent flying, crashing into a nearby building and passing out cold.

Since Yang Kai had acted, Ji Yao had no objections and withdrew her Emperor Yuan.

"Let’s go!" Yang Kai waved his hand and flew into the sky.

Ji Yao hurried to follow. Shi Tianhe glanced back at the unconscious Dong Hai, then turned away and quickly disappeared from everyone’s sight.

Only then did the many cultivators at the city gate quietly let out a breath. With an Emperor Realm expert present, they hadn’t even dared to breathe loudly, afraid of offending the expert.

"She’s actually from the Ice Heart Valley!"

Around the corner, Bai Lu looked as if he had discovered some earth-shattering secret, his face full of excitement. After thinking for a moment, he suddenly said, "Xue Man, go tell your father that an Emperor Realm expert from the Ice Heart Valley just left Taiping City. Have him gather his men and follow, but make sure not to alert them—just follow quietly."

"What about you?" Xue Man asked.

"I’m going back to the Thunderclap Pavilion to tell my father what happened here. I’m sure he’ll be very interested in that woman."

As he spoke, he dashed toward the location of Taiping City’s Space Array. With it, he could return directly to the Thunderclap Pavilion, so it wouldn’t take long to deliver the news to his father.

"So people from the Ice Heart Valley dare to show themselves now? Looks like revenge is finally within reach." Bai Lu sneered inwardly, imagining his father capturing that man and woman, feeling a secret delight.

## Chapter 2986 — The Void Gap

The group of four advanced in single file, with Yang Kai carrying the little black dog at the front, Zhu Qing and her younger brother in the middle, and Li Jiao bringing up the rear. No more than three steps separated each of them.

The surrounding scenery was completely obscured, and even their Divine Senses could not extend beyond twenty zhang. Yang Kai was not worried about heading in the wrong direction, however. He only needed to walk against the direction from which the frigid Demonic Qi blew.

After travelling like this for a day, they finally sensed something unusual ahead.

Powerful fluctuations of the Laws of Space undulated from that direction. Yang Kai's spirits rose, and he called out, "Stay alert. We're almost there."

Hearing this, the other three immediately became solemn. Li Jiao in particular pushed his Emperor Yuan with tremendous force. A voice echoed in his heart, telling him that in just a little while, he would be able to leave this bewildering damned place and return to his Li Dragon Palace.

At the thought, his blood surged and his heart filled with anticipation.

The chill around the little black dog grew even more severe. A thick layer of frost enveloped its body, and its perpetually open maw had shrunk considerably. It had clearly almost reached its limit.

A hundred zhang ahead of the group seemed to lie the source of the frigid Demonic Qi, from which Yin Wind howled. The frigid Demonic Qi anywhere else on Yin Wind Mountain was nowhere near as terrifying as it was here.

The last stretch of a journey was always the hardest. Yang Kai had discovered this place when he came here previously and knew it should be where the exit was located. The little black dog had also grown considerably in recent days, so by his estimation, it should be capable of bringing them close to the exit.

Success or failure would be decided in this one attempt.

Yang Kai continuously encouraged the little black dog beside its ear.

The little black dog clearly understood him. Perhaps it also knew that everyone's survival depended on it, for it suddenly seemed to receive divine assistance. A deep roar emerged from its throat, and its maw, which had shrunk considerably, expanded once again.

All the frigid Demonic Qi blowing toward them was devoured.

The pressure on everyone dropped dramatically, and Yang Kai quickened his pace.

Seventy zhang!

Fifty zhang!

Thirty zhang!

When they came within ten zhang, Yang Kai finally saw exactly what the exit looked like. At first glance, it resembled a fissure torn through the Void, yet it also looked like an open coin purse. Pure, dense Demonic Qi and a life-extinguishing frigid aura poured from the opening in rolling waves, merging together.

The fissure was several dozen zhang long and stretched across the summit of Yin Wind Mountain. It heaved and twisted, changing without pause, and appeared extremely unstable.

Logically speaking, such a fissure could not exist for long, because the World Laws would repair an imperfection like this.

However, the Revolving World was itself a peculiar Small World situated in the Void Gap between the Demon Domain and the Star Realm. It was hardly surprising that the opposing forces exerted by the two Great Worlds had torn a small fissure in the Revolving World.

If the pull exerted by one of those Great Worlds on the Revolving World suddenly became much stronger one day, it might even tear the entire Revolving World apart. When that happened, every living creature here would inevitably perish.

Such a fissure was not enough to allow people to pass through it freely, because its constant transformations stirred the power of the Laws of Space. If someone who did not comprehend this Dao tried to force their way through, they might be fine if they were lucky, but if not, they could die on the spot.

To pass through it and leave, Yang Kai first had to stabilize the fissure and create a safe entrance.

Many thoughts flashed through Yang Kai's mind before he settled on a plan and called out, "I'll cast a technique to stabilize the exit, but it won't last long, three breaths at most. You must stay close and make sure you don't get lost in the Void Fissure!"

"Mm!" Zhu Qing nodded heavily. Zhu Lie and Li Jiao also tensed, their minds taut as they prepared to move at any moment.

This concerned a Void Fissure and the Laws of Space. No matter how strong they were, their strength was useless here, so they could only follow Yang Kai's instructions.

Yang Kai's expression also became solemn as the Laws of Space surged around him. Although he stood motionless, he gave the strange impression of being both near and far away, a result of the space around him being affected.

"Open!"

With a thunderous shout, the invisible Laws of Space were mobilized and transformed into a shockwave visible to the naked eye, which slammed into the ever-changing Void Fissure.

In an instant, it was as though a transparent door had been installed over one section of the fissure, and the area it covered became perfectly stable.

"Qing'er, go!" Yang Kai called out.

Zhu Qing, who had long since prepared herself, flashed forward and rushed through the invisible doorway, vanishing in the blink of an eye.

Zhu Lie followed close behind without saying a word.

Li Jiao was the last to move. Given his strength, he should have been able to rush inside as long as nothing unexpected happened. Yet the instant he left the little black dog's protective range, his whole body stiffened and his Emperor Yuan stopped circulating properly. He could only stare helplessly at the entrance within arm's reach as he froze in place.

His eyes bulged, and even the blood in his body nearly froze. Terror engulfed him, followed by despair.

He knew he was likely finished. If he missed this perfect opportunity, he would never be able to leave this place, and his only possible fate would be to freeze to death.

In his desperation, he could only cast Yang Kai a pleading look.

"What a useless piece of trash!" Yang Kai cursed angrily. He had not expected this fellow to fail at such a critical moment and almost wanted to abandon him, but he still reached out, grabbed him, and pulled him over. Holding the little black dog with his other arm, Yang Kai swiftly charged toward the fissure.

Three breaths had already passed, and the stabilized region began to collapse as the fissure once again shifted unpredictably.

Yang Kai had no choice but to circulate his Power of Space again, pushing open a safe region ahead before flashing inside.

The howling wind beside his ears abruptly vanished. The surroundings were silent, without the slightest trace of light or any Spiritual Qi. This place seemed to be a realm of nothingness.

Anyone would find it difficult to adjust after suddenly leaving such a noisy environment and entering this strange, silent place.

In this place, only Divine Sense was still of some use.

"Qing'er!" Yang Kai called out the moment he arrived, but his voice could not travel. Fortunately, he had already sensed Zhu Qing's aura, so he immediately tossed Li Jiao aside, moved to Zhu Qing's side, and grabbed hold of her.

"Are you all right?" Yang Kai asked through Divine Sense.

"I'm fine," Zhu Qing replied. She had been the first to rush inside, so naturally nothing had happened to her, but Yang Kai's concern still filled her heart with warmth. "Is this the Void Gap?"

"That's right. This is the Void Gap!"

This was not Yang Kai's first time entering such a place. Void Gaps existed everywhere and could be entered from any location. Although they were largely similar, they were vast and boundless. To some people, such a place was extremely dangerous no matter how powerful they were. The slightest mishap could leave them lost within it, unable to find a way out for the rest of their lives.

Only those proficient in the Power of Space were qualified to travel through it, and they were merely qualified; they were still far from being able to stroll through it at leisure.

By this time, Zhu Lie and Li Jiao had also gathered around them. Zhu Lie was fine, but Li Jiao had been badly frozen just now. He was still shivering, his face filled with lingering fear.

Yang Kai's Divine Sense surged as he transmitted a warning to them. "There are many Void Turbulences within the Void Gap. If you encounter one, you'll either be swept away and vanish without a trace or be instantly transported to an unknown place. So if you sense anything unusual, don't investigate out of curiosity. Just stay as far away from it as possible."

"Palace Master Yang is right. I'll listen to you." Li Jiao nodded repeatedly.

In the current situation, if Yang Kai let out a fart, Li Jiao would probably cup it carefully in his hands. There was nothing he could do about it. Not only had Yang Kai helped him time and again, but he had also just saved his life. Now, if Li Jiao wanted to leave this place and return to the Star Realm, he could only place his hopes in Yang Kai. Who else could he rely on?

"It's also very easy to become separated here. Even if someone is right beside you, a moment's distraction might make them impossible to find. So I suggest that everyone hold on to one another. Hand in hand, like good friends!"

"That's a good suggestion." Li Jiao was eager for some sense of security, so Yang Kai's words spoke directly to his heart. As he spoke, he hurriedly looked toward Zhu Lie.

Zhu Lie said coldly, "If you dare come near me, you're dead."

"But Palace Master Yang said..." Li Jiao replied awkwardly.

Zhu Lie frowned and silently took a colored ribbon from his Spatial Ring.

Although this place was filled with chaotic Nothingness, a burst of radiant light still blossomed when the ribbon appeared. It was clearly a fine Imperial Treasure, and one intended for a woman.

"Use this," Zhu Lie said. Holding hands with Li Jiao would be even more unbearable than killing him.

"That works too!" Yang Kai nodded.

Zhu Lie immediately flicked the ribbon, sending it shooting straight toward Yang Kai. Yang Kai caught one end and tied it around his waist, while Zhu Lie tied the other end around himself.

Li Jiao watched eagerly and said with a shameless smile, "Leave some for me. Leave me a length too."

Zhu Lie snorted lightly. Although he was extremely unwilling, he did not take things too far and indeed left a section for Li Jiao, who happily tied it around his wrist.

In this way, the three men were connected. The ribbon was of considerable quality and would not be easily broken.

There was even less need to worry about Zhu Qing. Yang Kai kept one arm wrapped around her the whole time, making her the safest of them all. However, being so intimate with Yang Kai in front of others still made her blush with discomfort.

"Good. Now let's find the exit," Yang Kai called out.

"Do you know where it is?" Zhu Lie asked.

"I have a vague sense of it." Yang Kai turned to look in a certain direction, then sped off that way with a flicker of his figure. Zhu Lie and Li Jiao hurriedly followed.

Only after travelling for a while did Zhu Lie notice that something felt wrong.

Using the ribbon to connect them was indeed a good idea, as it avoided the awkwardness and discomfort of holding hands. But now that they were arranged like this, why did it feel as though Yang Kai were leading two heads of livestock...

As soon as this thought occurred to him, Zhu Lie's face turned pitch-black.

Humiliation! This was an enormous humiliation! He secretly resolved that if anyone ever mentioned today's events in front of him, he would fight them to the death!

Turning his head, he fiercely warned Li Jiao with his eyes.

Li Jiao was startled, and cold sweat slid down his forehead. For some inexplicable reason, he suddenly sensed murderous intent from Zhu Lie.

He had not even provoked Zhu Lie, so what was going on? He simply could not understand.

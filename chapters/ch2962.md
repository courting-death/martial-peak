## Chapter 2962 — You Are the One Who Should Kneel

The ruler of Human Sovereign City, the supreme existence above all others, actually only had the cultivation of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm!

Had they not seen it with their own eyes, they would not have believed it. Zhu Qing had clearly not expected this either and could not help but be taken aback. As someone whose strength rivaled a Third-Stage Emperor Realm master, she could tell at a glance the true depth of the Human Sovereign’s cultivation. A mere Second-Stage Emperor was almost laughable in her eyes. Moreover, the Human Sovereign was obviously old. Though she did not know his exact age, his qi and blood had already begun to decline. In other words, the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm was the peak he could ever reach. He had no room left for further advancement, and as time passed, his cultivation would only continue to deteriorate.

The unsteady nature of his aura was the best proof!

"Impudent! The Human Sovereign is before you, why have you not knelt!"

A delicate shout rang out. It was the beautiful woman standing at the very front on the right who took the initiative to make trouble. In the entire hall, only she and the Third Prince possessed a Nine-Leaf Body, but her cultivation was one Stage higher than the Third Prince's, giving her the strength of the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm.

It could be said that among all these royals, she was the strongest. Although the Human Sovereign's cultivation was the same as hers, his strength had already begun to decline. She was different; she was at the very peak of her life, with still a great deal of room to improve. If she encountered an opportunity, it was not impossible for her to advance to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm.

Yang Kai glanced over from the corner of his eye and snorted coldly, "The rules of your Human Sovereign City are truly strange. You want people to kneel whenever they meet someone, so why don't I see you kneeling to me?"

The beautiful woman stepped forward, her expression icy cold as a powerful aura pressed down upon Yang Kai. "I am the Second Princess of Human Sovereign City. Do you think yourself worthy of receiving my bow?"

Yang Kai remained utterly unmoved and declared proudly, “Princes and princesses mean less than drifting clouds to me. In my eyes, whoever has the bigger fist is the one who has the right to stand tall and speak. I already beat the First Prince into a miserable wreck. Why should I care that you’re the Second Princess?”

The Second Princess' phoenix eyes narrowed slightly, flickering with a dangerous light. "In that case, you think your fist is bigger?"

Yang Kai carefully sized her up and sneered, "At the very least, it's bigger than your chest."

The princes and princesses all burst into laughter. They felt that this outsider was truly vicious. The Second Princess relied on her profound cultivation and ordinarily looked down on every member of the imperial family. Even the highest-ranked princes failed to earn so much as a proper glance from her. Unfortunately, being born a woman was an insurmountable disadvantage. Had she been a man, she would likely have long since been chosen as the Human Sovereign’s heir.

Yet precisely because she was a woman, she lacked the qualities to take pride in. Her hips were not shapely, her chest was modest, and aside from a reasonably attractive face, there was little else about her that could be praised.

Yang Kai's words instantly struck the Second Princess' sore spot. Her face turned ashen with anger, and she gnashed her teeth. "Kneel down for me!"

As soon as her words fell, she suddenly raised her hand. A ball of emerald light shot toward Yang Kai like an arrow leaving the string, manifesting above his head in the shape of a palm before pressing down fiercely, as if it wanted to force Yang Kai to his knees with brute strength.

"I think you are the one who should kneel." Yang Kai snorted coldly. At the same time as he reached out and pulled Zhu Qing behind him, he formed a seal with one hand, and a similar mass of emerald light bloomed. This ray of light was not as large as the Second Princess', but it was undoubtedly much brighter and more condensed.

All the royals in the hall changed expression.

That light rushed upward and directly merged into the emerald palm. Under everyone's eyes, the palm actually reversed direction all at once and pressed toward the Second Princess. At first glance, it looked as if the Sacred Art the Second Princess had cast had turned back against herself.

The Third Prince's eyes narrowed, and an unprecedented sense of crisis lingered in his heart. He quietly glanced upward and saw that the Human Sovereign's expression had not changed. He sat there with his eyes half-closed, as if he had already fallen asleep.

Mountain-like pressure descended from the sky. The Second Princess' expression instantly became extremely ugly, and a series of cracking sounds came from within her body as her figure slowly sank downward.

She desperately resisted, constantly urging her strength as she tried to resonate with the Sacred Tree, but invisibly, there seemed to be another force interfering with her, preventing her from establishing a connection with the Sacred Tree no matter what. She could only rely on her own ability to stubbornly hold on.

She finally experienced what it felt like to be oppressed by a Sacred Art. The man opposite her was clearly lower in cultivation than she was, yet relying on the Sacred Art, he was able to toy with her in the palm of his hand. An unprecedented sense of humiliation surged into her heart, making her face flush red. She could only grit her teeth and shout in a low voice, "What are you all staring for? Hurry up and help!"

Only then did the many shocked royals suddenly awaken. Just as they were about to act, Zhu Qing suddenly said indifferently, "Let's see who dares move."

As the words left her lips, the enormous phantom of a red dragon suddenly emerged from within her body. Stretching more than thirty zhang in length, it coiled through the hall, its massive form filling nearly half of the Human Sovereign Hall. A dragon’s might, so dense it seemed almost tangible, flooded every corner of the space, chilling bodies and stifling breath. High above, upon its proudly raised head, a pair of enormous dragon eyes reflected all things beneath them, radiating an awe-inspiring majesty that brooked no violation.

Everyone stood dumbfounded, staring blankly at the towering dragon phantom. Sounds of nervous swallowing rippled through the hall.

The Human Sovereign, who had always been as steady as a mountain, finally opened his half-closed eyes. An astonishing light bloomed in his seemingly turbid gaze as he raised his head and met those dragon eyes. In an instant, Spiritual Energy surged violently through the hall.

The red dragon raised its head and roared, deafening everyone. The violent wind it stirred up blew several weaker royals off balance.

With the full force of the Dragon Clan's Origin Power coursing through her, Zhu Qing's strength transcended the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. Even with the Sacred Tree's protection, these royals could gain no advantage against her.

The Second Princess, who was bitterly holding on over there, became even more wretched. Her connection with the Sacred Tree had already been severed by Yang Kai, and now that she was struck by the Dragon Pressure, her body sank, and one knee had already touched the ground.

Her face turned incomparably pale in an instant. A tremendous sense of humiliation filled her heart, making her experience a feeling more unbearable than death.

Human Sovereign City had stood for more than a hundred thousand years, with the royal family alone reigning supreme. They had long ago developed a lofty character. It had always been commoners kneeling to the royals; this was the first time in history that a royal had knelt to a commoner.

Yang Kai did not press further. Turning to look at the Human Sovereign, he said, "Sir, what are your thoughts? If this continues, I fear your daughter truly will have to kneel to me. Not that I cannot accept her obeisance, but bullying a woman is hardly an honorable deed. As the saying goes, a true man does not contend with women."

The Human Sovereign slowly rose, his not particularly loud voice echoing through the Human Sovereign Palace. "This Sovereign failed to teach his daughter properly. Honored guest, please stop."

When the princes and princesses heard these words, all their expressions changed slightly.

They had never heard the Human Sovereign speak to anyone so politely. In ordinary times, even toward them, his children, he was extremely dignified. When had he ever been this gentle? A strange light flashed through the Third Prince's eyes, as if he had guessed something. When he looked at Yang Kai again, his gaze had completely changed.

He had not expected that after only half a day apart, the fellow he had dismissed at the Space Array would actually possess such powerful capital.

Yang Kai looked at the Human Sovereign with a grin. He did not withdraw his hand, nor did he reply, simply remaining deadlocked there.

The red dragon filling half the hall lifted its head slightly, and the Dragon Pressure became even stronger.

A trace of anger flashed through the Human Sovereign's eyes, but he did not erupt. Instead, he turned his head to look at the Second Princess and said, "Fu Yu, your words and actions were improper, and you offended the guest this Sovereign invited. Apologize and make amends at once."

"Royal Father!" The Second Princess called Fu Yu was shocked when she heard this and looked at the Human Sovereign with an expression of disbelief. After meeting the Human Sovereign's majestic gaze, which contained anger within it, her eyes trembled. She hurriedly lowered her head and gnashed her teeth. "Fu Yu had eyes but failed to see and offended... My Lord. Please, My Lord, do not blame me!"

Her tone was extremely stiff, especially the words "My Lord," which she bit out in an extremely muddled way. Clearly, she had never done such a thing before, much less addressed anyone else this way.

Yang Kai laughed heartily, "Since the Human Sovereign has spoken, it would be rude of me not to give face. Qing'er, stand down."

As his words fell, he was the first to withdraw his Shamanic Art. The emerald palm that had been pressing down on Fu Yu's head scattered with a boom and vanished in the blink of an eye. The red dragon that filled half the hall also collapsed into nothingness like a bubble. The Dragon Pressure dissipated, and only then did the many royals slightly let out a breath.

Fu Yu slowly rose, her head lowered. No one could see her expression or gaze, but her constantly trembling body clearly displayed the extreme unrest in her heart.

"Grant seats!" The Human Sovereign raised his hand.

Immediately, someone brought over two extremely luxurious chairs from the inner hall and placed them not far below the dragon throne.

Yang Kai smiled faintly. “There’s no need to sit. I imagine the Human Sovereign has many questions for me. As it happens, I have a few questions for you as well.”

The Human Sovereign did not force him and nodded. "Indeed, I do have some things I wish to ask you."

Yang Kai adopted a listening posture.

The Human Sovereign said, "Where did you come from?"

“The Star Realm. I wonder if Sir has ever heard of it.”

The Human Sovereign nodded. "Naturally, I have heard of it. Human Sovereign City has existed for more than a hundred thousand years, and outsiders have entered one after another. I do not know when it began, but those who entered called themselves people from the Star Realm. It seems you are the same."

Yang Kai smiled slightly. "You call this place the Revolving World. It has a spatial intersection with the Star Realm, so naturally, everyone who can enter comes from the Star Realm."

The Human Sovereign continued, “Then how did you enter Human Sovereign City? As far as I know, no one should have activated the Space Array for you, because the person responsible was killed by your hand.”

Yang Kai raised his brow. “Sir, your intelligence network is remarkably efficient. You uncovered all that in barely half a day?”

"When someone dies, it must naturally be investigated carefully."

Yang Kai suddenly shook his head. “I don’t think this is very fair. You’re the only one asking questions while I’m left answering them—that’s hardly a good bargain for me. How about we take turns instead? One question each. If the other party can answer, they answer; if not, they may ask a different question instead. What do you think?”

The many royals were dumbfounded as they listened. This was the first time they had ever seen someone bargain with the Human Sovereign, and he even spoke so confidently and self-righteously. Yet the Human Sovereign actually showed no sign of anger at all. He only pondered for a moment before nodding. "As you wish."

The strange light in the Third Prince's eyes became even deeper.

## Chapter 2635 — Miraculous Skill

Under the watchful eyes of everyone, Yang Kai's appearance underwent a tremendous transformation.

Originally, he was rather handsome and elegant, with striking features, but after Shi Tianhe's disguise technique, his looks became utterly ordinary. An Ruoyun and the others stared wide-eyed in amazement.

If this change hadn't happened right before their eyes, no one would have believed that such a simple disguise technique could achieve this level of transformation.

In less than half an hour, Shi Tianhe finally wiped Yang Kai's face one last time, examined him carefully, and nodded, "Done."

She stepped back a few paces, while An Ruoyun and Ji Yao immediately came closer to observe. To their shock, they couldn't spot a single flaw. The ordinary-looking man before them seemed to have been born with this face.

The only thing that couldn't be changed was Yang Kai's own aura.

However, in the Wenqing Sect, there weren't many who had met him face-to-face. Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo had dealt with him directly, but as long as he didn't reveal his aura in front of them, they likely wouldn't notice anything.

Bing Yun's beautiful eyes sparkled. "Miraculous skill, Tianhe, your disguise technique is truly remarkable."

Even she praised it so highly, which showed just how extraordinary Shi Tianhe's skill was.

An Ruoyun asked in surprise, "Tianhe, who did you learn this from? Does the Dong Family in Taiping City have such methods?"

Shi Tianhe was her disciple, but she had never taught her these things. Clearly, Shi Tianhe had learned it after going to Taiping City. But how could a mere Dong Family possess such a wondrous art?

Shi Tianhe shook her head. "This disguise technique didn't come from the Dong Family. I learned it from a strange man."

"A strange man?" An Ruoyun was taken aback.

Shi Tianhe's face showed a look of reminiscence. "That was about sixty years ago. A beggar came to Taiping City, disheveled and filthy, shunned by all. One day, he was beaten and injured, collapsing in front of the Dong Family's gate. I happened to be returning from an errand, so I had someone help him to the woodshed. Later, when he was about to leave, he sought me out and taught me this disguise technique as thanks. At the time, I didn't think much of it. Only later, when I practiced it out of boredom, did I realize how profound it was."

"A beggar..." An Ruoyun and Ji Yao exchanged glances, both finding it unbelievable.

"That was no ordinary beggar, but surely a master." Bing Yun's eyes flashed. "Tianhe, you were fortunate to receive such a chance. Good deeds are rewarded."

An Ruoyun and Ji Yao both nodded in agreement.

If that person had truly been just a beggar, how could he have possessed such a miraculous disguise technique? Clearly, he was a formidable expert toying with life. Some experts have such eccentric tempers, preferring to play the pig to eat the tiger rather than live comfortably.

"What a pity..." Bing Yun sighed again.

"Master, what are you regretting?" An Ruoyun asked curiously.

Bing Yun said, "That master may have been searching for a successor in this way. It's a pity Tianhe probably didn't meet his requirements, so he didn't pass down his true legacy, only taught the disguise technique as a token of thanks."

An Ruoyun nodded in realization. "It is indeed a pity."

If she had truly received that master's legacy, perhaps Shi Tianhe would now be like Zi Yu, already in the Emperor Realm. The Dong Family might not have suffered extermination.

"That beggar... did he have a rat-like face and yellow teeth?" Yang Kai, who had been listening, suddenly asked.

Shi Tianhe looked at him in surprise. "Senior Uncle Yang, do you know that master?"

Yang Kai nodded, a wry smile crossing his face. "I have a pretty good idea."

To elevate a simple disguise technique to such a level, and to be a rat-faced beggar—just who was this person? Yang Kai already had a guess in his heart.

Since this wondrous art came from that person, it made sense that it could have such miraculous effects.

Seeing that Yang Kai had no intention of explaining further, Bing Yun didn't ask. She simply nodded. "This disguise technique is truly impressive. Even I might be fooled if I didn't examine carefully."

Yang Kai smiled. "Then this plan should work."

Bing Yun still worried. "But there are powerful enemies everywhere outside. If you fail, how will you escape? Why not let Tianhe disguise me as well? If I act personally, the chances of success are greater."

Yang Kai quickly shook his head. "That won't do. Senior, you are at the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. If you go to receive the recruitment order, you'll immediately alert Feng Xuan and Yao Zhuo. If they come to investigate, you'll be exposed for sure. I'm different. I'm only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, not too high or too low. If I defect, I'll be valued but won't attract too much attention, making it easier to act. Please don't worry about my safety. If things become impossible, I'll escape at once."

Bing Yun sighed, knowing Yang Kai's mind was made up. She couldn't persuade him further and simply said gratefully, "Then you must be careful."

Yang Kai nodded. "There's one more thing I need your help with, Senior."

"Go ahead."

"I need a large amount of Ethereal Crystals. If there are Ethereal Jades, even better."

"Alright." Bing Yun nodded. "I'll have someone check the storeroom right away. If there are Ethereal Crystals or Ethereal Jades, they'll all be brought to you."

Whether Ethereal Crystals or Ethereal Jades, both were in high demand throughout the Star Realm, as they were the main materials for refining Secret Treasures for storage. Spatial Rings and spatial bracelets were all made from these materials.

Comparatively, Ethereal Jade was of a higher grade than Ethereal Crystal.

Although Bing Yun didn't know what Yang Kai needed Ethereal Crystals and Ethereal Jades for, she didn't ask and agreed readily.

"Tianhe, disguise your Senior Aunt Ji Yao as well." Yang Kai said to Shi Tianhe, then turned to Ji Yao. "Junior Sister Yao, I'll go prepare. Let's set out tonight."

Ji Yao glanced at him, not suspecting anything, and nodded slightly.

Yang Kai's figure flickered as he left the main hall.

After leaving, he didn't return to his own courtyard but headed straight for the outskirts of Ice Heart Valley.

Soon, he reached the edge of the Grand Guardian Array, the very place where he and Ji Yao had entered earlier.

"Who goes there!" Changsun Ying, who was guarding the area, immediately leapt out from hiding when she saw Yang Kai approach, staring at him coldly. The other disciples also rushed out, surrounding Yang Kai tightly.

"Junior Sister Changsun!" Yang Kai grinned at her. "It's me, no need to be nervous."

"You... you're Senior Brother Yang?" Changsun Ying's beautiful brows furrowed slightly in surprise. "How did you end up like this?"

She had just seen a strange man appear here and was startled. She thought the grand array of Ice Heart Valley had been breached without anyone noticing, otherwise how could such a mistake occur?

But now, hearing his voice, Changsun Ying knew it was Yang Kai, though his face had changed so much she could hardly recognize him.

"By Senior Bing Yun's order, I'm going out to handle something. Please open the array for a moment." Yang Kai pointed at the grand array.

"Alright, wait a moment!" Changsun Ying didn't refuse or check with Bing Yun, since Bing Yun had previously said that Yang Kai was a friend of Ice Heart Valley and any of his requests should be granted without reporting.

As she spoke, she took out a token, carefully checked the surroundings to make sure no one was nearby, then waved the token at the array's light curtain.

A mysterious light shot out, and the tightly closed array immediately opened a gap.

Yang Kai's figure flickered as he left the array, sending a message as he went: "I'll be back in two or three days at the shortest, five or six at the longest. When the time comes, I'll trouble you to open the array again, Junior Sister Changsun."

"No problem!" Changsun Ying replied quickly. After confirming Yang Kai had left, she closed the array again.

"Why is Senior Uncle Yang going out now, and even disguised like that?" Yu Dan came forward, asking curiously.

Changsun Ying shook her head. "I'll report to Master."

Earlier, when Yang Kai wanted to leave, she hadn't reported for verification because Bing Yun had already given instructions. Now that Yang Kai had left, she needed to inform Bing Yun.

She immediately took out her Transmission Compass and poured her Divine Sense into it.

Inside the main hall, Bing Yun quickly sensed it, took out her own Transmission Compass, and upon reading the message, couldn't help but smile wryly.

Below, Ji Yao was still sitting there, letting Shi Tianhe disguise her, waiting for nightfall to go to Binglun City with Yang Kai to receive the recruitment order. She hadn't expected Yang Kai to leave her behind and act alone.

Bing Yun didn't quite know how to explain this to Ji Yao. But she could tell Yang Kai had done it on purpose. She wondered what had happened between her third disciple and Yang Kai to make him wary of her.

...

Binglun City was originally the foundation of Ice Heart Valley, as well as its hub for external trade and intelligence gathering. Most of the city's industries belonged to Ice Heart Valley.

But since half a year ago, not a single disciple of Ice Heart Valley had been seen in Binglun City.

Wenqing Sect, wielding overwhelming force, descended upon the city, seized it, and used it as a base to recruit talent, eyeing Ice Heart Valley like a tiger. They were just waiting for the right moment to deliver a fatal blow and wipe it out completely.

Most of the industries in the city that belonged to Ice Heart Valley were now in ruins, their resources long since looted, and most shops reduced to rubble.

At this moment, in front of a grand hall in Binglun City, a plaque hung overhead, bearing three large characters.

Recruitment Hall!

This was where Wenqing Sect recruited talent.

Since Wenqing Sect issued the recruitment order and promised countless benefits, many cultivators had come to serve. Otherwise, even a top force like Wenqing Sect couldn't have gathered a hundred thousand people.

Among these people were not only those lacking cultivation resources but also Emperor Realm experts with their own motives, all enticed by Wenqing Sect's promises to stay and serve.

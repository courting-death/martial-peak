## Chapter 2734 — Victory Secured

Shock!

Ji Ying’s heart surged like a stormy sea—how could such a heaven-defying existence appear in this world? To think someone could grasp the very essence of alchemy, just as his master had.

No, not even his master could have achieved such mastery at this age.

Ji Ying was completely stunned, unable to describe the feeling. The person competing with him in alchemy no longer seemed to be the young man named Yang Kai, but rather the elusive Great Dao he had always pursued but could never capture.

How could he possibly win?

His mind grew chaotic, and suddenly a hissing sound came from within the Three-Legged Twin-Dragon Cauldron.

“Master Ji, you…” Li Jiao and Mi Qi exclaimed in unison.

Although they didn’t understand alchemy and couldn’t see much, Ji Ying’s current state was clearly off. His changing expression made it obvious that his mindset had faltered.

This was bad! If Ji Ying lost this competition, he wouldn’t suffer much, but the two of them had wagered fifty years of their sects’ profits. If they lost, their disciples would have to tighten their belts for the next fifty years.

Ji Ying was startled, suddenly realizing he was still in the midst of the contest. He immediately felt regret and shame.

This was already his second distraction, which would surely affect the entire alchemy process. Still, his skills were deep, and he quickly suppressed the furnace fire to salvage the situation.

Forcing himself to calm down and clear his mind, Ji Ying’s movements suddenly became much smoother under immense pressure.

He even began to faintly comprehend something.

The senior disciple continued to stare wide-eyed at Yang Kai’s actions, bloodshot eyes growing more intense, body trembling as if enduring great torment, yet his expression was extremely excited.

Time passed, and wisps of pill fragrance began to waft out. This was not simply the scent of spiritual medicine, but the aroma of spiritual pills.

The appearance of this fragrance meant the refining had reached its final stage. As long as nothing went wrong, the spiritual pills would soon be complete.

The closer to the end, the more nervous Mi Qi and Li Jiao became. They didn’t know if Ji Ying could surpass Yang Kai. Originally, their confidence in Ji Ying was absolute, but his unstable mindset earlier made them uneasy, unsure if it would affect the final quality of the spiritual pills.

Another hour passed. The medicinal aroma in the hall was now thick enough to be tangible. Yang Kai’s hands suddenly stopped moving, his expression solemn. He shook the Purple Void Cauldron and shouted, “Rise!”

“Collect the pills!”

Everyone’s faces grew serious, knowing Yang Kai’s batch of spiritual pills had reached the true final stage.

This process was also a crucial step in alchemy. A good pill collection technique could allow an alchemist to produce more pills and higher quality ones.

On the other side, Ji Ying also tapped his alchemy furnace, sending the lid flying. He too entered the pill collection stage.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao watched this scene and couldn’t help but breathe a sigh of relief.

They believed that at this point, Yang Kai could not surpass Ji Ying, because Ji Ying’s pill collection technique was personally taught by the Great Emperor. Even if Yang Kai had some fortune and became an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, he couldn’t possibly possess a better technique than Ji Ying.

The two didn’t dare pretend to understand, so they quickly looked to the senior disciple, hoping to glean something from his expression.

With one glance, their hearts sank.

Because the senior disciple was still watching Yang Kai’s actions, and his bloodshot eyes were shining with excitement, completely ignoring his own master.

Could it be? Was Yang Kai’s pill collection technique even superior? Otherwise, why wouldn’t the senior disciple take this chance to learn from his master, but instead focus on Yang Kai?

It must be because the senior disciple lacked skill and insight.

Mi Qi and Li Jiao comforted themselves. Even though Ji Ying had been distracted twice during the alchemy contest, they still held great hope for him.

The Nine Heavens Profound Pill Technique was unleashed. One seal after another struck the Purple Void Cauldron, making the final step of alchemy flawless.

This was the pill collection method Yang Kai had comprehended from the Great Dao Pill Tone. Even compared to the Great Emperor’s original technique, it was slightly superior.

Ding-ling-ling…

A pleasant, melodious sound suddenly rang out from within the pill furnace.

Pills were formed!

Yang Kai flicked the Purple Void Cauldron, and a string of round spiritual pills flew out. Mi Qi and Li Jiao stared, wanting to see how many pills Yang Kai had produced and their quality, but before they could get a clear look, Yang Kai had already stored them in a prepared jade bottle.

This made the two grind their teeth in frustration.

Meanwhile, Ji Ying also began collecting pills. After the final technique, several Emperor Yuan Pills flew from the furnace, which he casually caught and placed into a jade bottle in his left hand.

“Refining complete!” Yang Kai pressed his hands down, qi sinking into his Dantian, and let out a gentle breath, signaling the end of his alchemy.

Ji Ying stood motionless, brows tightly knit, lost deep in thought. His senior disciple, meanwhile, had closed his eyes the instant Yang Kai finished refining, half-kneeling like a statue, as if his spirit had wandered beyond the mortal realm.

This made Mi Qi and Li Jiao anxious. They were eager to know the outcome of the contest. Although they felt Ji Ying was unlikely to lose, they were still nervous at the critical moment.

After all, it concerned fifty years of their sects’ profits—five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals.

Ji Ying remained still, and Yang Kai didn’t disturb him, knowing he was reflecting on the gains and losses of the contest. For an alchemist, this was an important moment. Yang Kai glanced around, then winked at Zhu Qing, who was watching him.

Zhu Qing’s face was as cold as ice, as she completely ignored him and left him awkwardly standing there.

After a long while, Ji Ying let out a light breath. Turning around, he cupped his fists toward Yang Kai and said, “Brother Yang’s skill is superb—Ji concedes in admiration.”

After competing with Yang Kai, Ji Ying immediately realized he had underestimated him. Yang Kai was not only an Imperial-Grade Pill Master, but his skills were in no way inferior to Ji Ying’s own.

He had only caught a fleeting glimpse of that wondrous pill refining process, yet it left him utterly awestruck. If not for the immense stakes of this contest, he would have been sorely tempted to halt his own alchemy and observe Yang Kai’s technique in detail.

“Regardless of victory or defeat, I must thank Brother Yang for this time.” Ji Ying said sincerely, glancing at his senior disciple.

Knowing Yang Kai was an Imperial-Grade Pill Master no weaker than himself, Ji Ying’s address for him changed. His senior disciple had clearly gained much, so he naturally owed Yang Kai thanks.

He had previously called Yang Kai ‘Palace Master Yang’, but now his tone was much more familiar.

“No need for formality, Brother Ji.” Yang Kai smiled.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi were dumbfounded. Wasn’t Ji Ying supposed to give Yang Kai trouble? How had they suddenly become so friendly?

Even after knowing Ji Ying for years, neither of them dared, nor had the right, to address him so intimately.

They couldn’t help but feel regret. If they’d known, they wouldn’t have come along.

“Master Ji, let’s check the results of the contest first.” Mi Qi changed the subject, not wanting Yang Kai and Ji Ying to get any closer. As long as Ji Ying won, their trip would be worthwhile.

Just thinking about fifty years’ worth of sect profits was enough to make their hearts race.

The two were still curious about what was in Yang Kai’s ring, wanting to know what had earned Ji Ying’s high praise.

Ji Ying looked to Yang Kai for confirmation. Yang Kai smiled and gestured, “Guests first, Brother Ji!”

Ji Ying nodded, “Then I won’t stand on ceremony.”

As he spoke, he opened the jade bottle in his hand and poured out the Emperor Yuan Pills.

Everyone in the hall craned their necks to see what was in his palm.

“Nine!” Mi Qi glanced and exclaimed in excitement.

This time, Ji Ying produced nine pills—the maximum for a batch of spiritual pills. That alone was half the victory! The rest depended on the quality, but with Ji Ying’s skill, the pills were sure to be excellent. Just from their plump, translucent appearance, it was clear.

“Look, look…” Li Jiao, as if discovering a new world, pointed at one Emperor Yuan Pill, breathing rapidly, “Pill Patterns! It’s Pill Patterns!”

Among the nine spiritual pills, one was covered in lines, resembling the meridians of a human body. It was the legendary Pill Patterns!

If a spiritual pill developed Pill Patterns during refining, its efficacy would double, and its value would soar.

But Pill Patterns weren’t something everyone could produce. Even the most skilled alchemists could only hope for them. Yet now, Ji Ying had produced a spiritual pill with Pill Patterns.

And it was of Imperial-Grade quality!

This made it all the more precious and astonishing.

“Hahaha!” Mi Qi laughed heartily, finally relaxing. If producing nine pills was half the victory, then the one with Pill Patterns sealed it. Yang Kai had no chance to overturn the result.

There was no need to even check the quality of Yang Kai’s pills.

Mi Qi didn’t believe Yang Kai could also produce a pill with Pill Patterns.

If Pill Patterns were so easy to make, they wouldn’t be called Pill Patterns.

Li Jiao and Mi Qi exchanged glances, their eyes full of joy and satisfaction.

Five billion Upper-Level Source Crystals, fifty years of sect profits—soon to be in their hands. How could they not be delighted?

They secretly decided that once Yang Kai fulfilled the bet, they would buy the Emperor Yuan Pill with Pill Patterns from Ji Ying. It was the key to victory and worth preserving. With this pill, Lingxiao Palace and Yang Kai would never be able to hold their heads up before them.

Of course, they could also do Ji Ying a favor in the process.

Should they humiliate Yang Kai as well? Seeing the three Yao Kings standing nearby, they thought better of it. If Yang Kai got angry, they wouldn’t be able to handle him.

## Chapter 3066 — Li Jiao's Desperate Three Moves

In the next instant, Fu Chi's expression filled with astonishment.

The scene before him was somewhat beyond his comprehension. Under the suppression of his Dragon Pressure, that mongrel still showed almost no reaction, merely furrowing his brow. The scene he had imagined, in which the man was completely suppressed and knelt in terror, never appeared.

How was that possible?

The man opposite him was merely a mongrel with an impure bloodline, while he himself was an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon. The gulf between their bloodlines was as vast as the distance between Heaven and Earth, so how could this man remain unharmed? What had gone wrong?

Unlike Fu Chi's bewilderment, Li Jiao was immensely encouraged.

He had blocked it, truly blocked it! Although he still felt somewhat uncomfortable and suffered a little suppression, the gap between Third-Tier and Eighth-Tier was simply too vast after all. Even so, it finally allowed him to see hope of taking revenge.

The Dragon Temple's Protection Technique truly lived up to its reputation. As long as he possessed its protection, the gap between his and Fu Chi's bloodlines would no longer be so pronounced, and he would have the right to risk his life against him.

That right was all he asked for.

If Yang Kai had not intercepted a Protection Technique from the Dragon Temple earlier and bestowed it upon him, he would never have been able to avenge Lu Sanniang's blood feud in this lifetime.

Yang Kai had given him all of this, and Li Jiao was deeply grateful to him.

"You have the Dragon Temple's protection?" Fu Chi finally understood. Glaring at him, he roared, "How could you possibly have received the Dragon Temple's protection?"

Fu Chi had already left when the Fourth Elder communicated with the Dragon Temple and summoned its Protection Technique, so he naturally knew nothing about it. What he could not understand was why this Dragon Descendant had received the Dragon Temple's protection. Moreover... this man seemed to bear some deep grudge against him. Otherwise, he would not have followed Fu Chi all the way here, much less stared at him with such hatred.

"What do you want?" Fu Chi's expression darkened as he sneered inwardly. Could this fellow believe that merely because he was injured, he would be easy to bully? He was indeed wounded, but he was still an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon.

Li Jiao did not answer and merely said calmly, "My name is Li Jiao!"

As he spoke, golden light flashed in his hand and a long halberd appeared.

The Imperial Treasure, the Golden Lion Halberd.

This halberd had been refined from the skeleton of a Twelfth-Tier Yao Beast, the Imperial Apex Golden Lion, which Li Jiao had obtained years ago. The Imperial Apex Golden Lion was also an Ancient Variant that had long since gone extinct. In ancient times, it had commanded the wind and rain and could even contend with certain Divine Spirits. Li Jiao had obtained its skeleton through a fortuitous coincidence, then later sought out an Artifact Refining Grandmaster to forge it into a halberd. It had accompanied him for nearly five hundred years and had always been cultivated with his Essence-Blood. It could be said that this long halberd was Li Jiao's most powerful weapon.

With the long halberd in hand, Li Jiao's momentum rose steadily, reaching its peak in just three breaths.

Facing an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon, he did not dare show the slightest carelessness. Even though his opponent was injured, Li Jiao did not believe he could defeat him with certainty, so he adopted a life-or-death stance from the outset.

Fu Chi clearly noticed this. His brow furrowed, and he was somewhat startled. He had no idea what kind of deep-seated hatred Li Jiao bore him that he would go to such lengths.

Suddenly, the wind stirred, a figure flashed, the halberd thrust, and cold light blossomed.

Li Jiao had already lunged in front of Fu Chi. His halberd shot out like a Dragon, transforming into a sky full of halberd shadows that enveloped Fu Chi.

"Insolence!" Fu Chi flew into a rage. Yang Kai had humiliated him, Zhu Qing had humiliated him, and now even a mongrel wanted to humiliate him. How could the Dragon Clan's pride be trampled at will? As he shouted, a series of crackling explosions rang out and lightning raced across the sky. It transformed into bolts that crashed down toward Li Jiao as Fu Chi simultaneously opened his mouth and spat out an enormous ball of lightning at the Golden Lion Halberd.

Blood-red light emerged from Li Jiao's body as every pore opened and his Essence-Blood seeped out. That blood-red radiance was clearly the result of him stimulating his Essence-Blood.

He knew this battle had to be decided quickly. Only with the courage born of his fury did he have a chance to determine victory or defeat against Fu Chi. The longer the battle dragged on, the fainter his hope of revenge would become. Thus, he did not hesitate to stimulate his own Essence-Blood, hoping only to fight Fu Chi to the death within a few moves.

Arcs of lightning struck Li Jiao's body one after another, blasting the blood-red radiance until it flickered unsteadily, seemingly liable to collapse at any moment.

The ball of lightning Fu Chi had spat out also collided with the Golden Lion Halberd. A deafening boom erupted, and Li Jiao was sent tumbling through the air. Fu Chi, who had been sitting cross-legged on the ground, also lost his balance and was forced back more than a dozen zhang.

"Courting death!" Fu Chi roared. All the humiliation he had suffered today erupted like a volcano at that moment, but when he glared ahead, his expression changed slightly.

While tumbling through the air, Li Jiao spat a mouthful of Essence-Blood onto his Golden Lion Halberd, formed several complex hand seals, and thrust a finger fiercely toward Fu Chi.

"Roar!"

A beast's roar shook the heavens. The Golden Lion Halberd suddenly twisted and transformed into an enormous male lion. The might of a Beast King spread out as it became a streak of golden light that hurtled toward Fu Chi.

Sensing the terrifying power of this strike, Fu Chi did not dare be careless. As a Dragon roar rang out, he revealed his enormous True Dragon form and raked one claw viciously forward, striking the incoming golden light head-on.

The lion shattered apart, revealing the Golden Lion Halberd once more. Its light was dim, and it had clearly suffered severe damage that had even diminished its spirituality. The most immediate result was that Li Jiao spat out a mouthful of blood and his aura fell sharply.

"You dared provoke the Dragon Clan, so today you will certainly die!" Fu Chi's Dragon body coiled in place, his head held high above like a god in the heavens as he mercilessly pronounced Li Jiao's doom.

In the next moment, however, his expression changed drastically as an intense sense of unease suddenly rose within him. Looking closely, he saw a bead no larger than a longan shoot out from the mouthful of blood Li Jiao had spat.

The bead appeared perfectly ordinary, but it approached as swiftly as lightning.

Fu Chi clearly sensed a threat within it that filled him with trepidation.

"A Dragon Pearl! You actually cultivated a Dragon Pearl!" Fu Chi cried out in shock.

For a mongrel Dragon Descendant to have cultivated a Dragon Pearl was simply inconceivable. Although this Dragon Pearl was of rather poor quality, there was no doubt that it was genuine.

Every member of the Dragon Clan possessed a Dragon Pearl of their own. A Dragon Pearl was the foundation of a Dragon and was incomparably precious, equivalent to a Yao Beast's Inner Core.

When making a final counterattack before death, a Yao Beast could spit out its Inner Core to attack, and the Dragon Clan could naturally do the same. To this day, however, few members of the Dragon Clan had ever done so. There were not many beings capable of threatening a Dragon's life, and even those who could generally would not offend the Dragon Clan lightly, much less drive one of its members to death.

Spitting out one's Dragon Pearl required even greater courage. Although a Dragon Pearl possessed unmatched power, once expelled from the body and deprived of the flesh's protection, it could easily be damaged. If a Dragon Pearl was damaged, that Dragon's foundation would be ruined. At best, their strength would decline; at worst, they would die.

Unless driven into truly desperate straits, a Yao Beast would not spit out its Inner Core, and a member of the Dragon Clan was even less likely to spit out their Dragon Pearl. It was something one did only when risking everything in a fight to the death.

Yet at this very moment, a mongrel Dragon Descendant had spat out his Dragon Pearl right in front of Fu Chi, assuming a stance of victory or death.

What was wrong with this fellow? What grudge could he possibly bear against Fu Chi? He had actually spat out his Dragon Pearl to attack without even exchanging more than a few words. More than the fact that this fellow had managed to cultivate a Dragon Pearl, Fu Chi was shocked by his utter resolve.

Even now, he still did not understand why Li Jiao was acting so ferociously.

From beginning to end, Li Jiao had merely stated his name without another wasted word, then begun fighting to the death within three moves.

This rapid, three-pronged assault caught Fu Chi completely off guard.

This was already Li Jiao's strongest attack, a life-risking strike from a veteran Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master. Even as an Eighth-Tier Thunder Dragon, Fu Chi did not dare take it lightly.

His expression abruptly turned solemn. As words in the Dragon Language rang out, he exhaled a blast of Dragon Breath that transformed into a seemingly impregnable barrier before him.

Li Jiao's Dragon Pearl slammed into it.

Fu Chi's enormous body suddenly jolted, while Li Jiao violently sprayed a mouthful of blood that dyed the front of his robes red. The light around the Dragon Pearl flickered, but under Li Jiao's strenuous efforts to sustain it, the wavering radiance quickly stabilized.

Crack...

The sound of something shattering rang out.

Fu Chi's expression changed again. In the end, he had still underestimated Li Jiao's determination to risk his life. He had thought that once Li Jiao suffered a setback, he would recognize that he was outmatched and retreat, but this obstruction had instead further inflamed his ferocity.

That mongrel showed no concern whatsoever for his own Dragon Pearl and simply drove its power with all his might. He was utterly insane!

The defence Fu Chi had erected shattered in an instant, and the Dragon Pearl arrived before him in the blink of an eye.

By the time Fu Chi hesitated over whether to spit out his own Dragon Pearl to block it, the fleeting opportunity had already passed.

The tiny Dragon Pearl was like the sharpest weapon in the world. It broke through Fu Chi's Dragon Scales, blasted a hole in his chest, and pierced out through his back, spraying Dragon Blood like pouring rain.

Fu Chi wailed mournfully. His Dragon body writhed, smashing the island's rocks to pieces and sending dust billowing into the air.

Although the wound from this strike was not large compared with his enormous body, it had truly dealt him a severe injury. His internal organs had shifted out of place, and the Dragon Yuan within him had fallen into disorder and become difficult to control.

In his fury, he lashed out with his Dragon tail and struck the Dragon Pearl behind him.

Crack...

Cracks spread across the Dragon Pearl, and its light instantly dimmed.

"Wah..."

Li Jiao opened his mouth and coughed up blood, his complexion abruptly turning as pale as paper. His body swayed, and he dropped to his knees. He gulped in great breaths without exhaling at all, as though he might die at any moment.

With great effort, he raised his head just in time to see Fu Chi transform from his Dragon body back into human form.

His desperate strike had not been ineffective. Although Li Jiao himself was in terrible condition, Fu Chi had also suffered serious injuries. At that moment, Fu Chi's face was likewise pale. He covered his chest with one hand and glared at Li Jiao with a savage expression.

The flesh around his chest was a bloody ruin.

If they had fought head-on, Fu Chi was certain that even with the Dragon Temple's protection, the mongrel before him could never have been his opponent.

Yet this mongrel's decisiveness, courage, and ferocity were far beyond those of an ordinary person, and Fu Chi had been caught completely by surprise. Otherwise, how could he possibly have suffered such severe injuries?

This was simply a humiliation beyond compare!

He stared coldly at Li Jiao and enunciated each word, "You are dead!"

Li Jiao laboriously raised a hand and wiped the blood from the corner of his mouth, gazing calmly at Fu Chi.

He had failed! Even after staking his life, he truly could not kill a member of the Dragon Clan. Accomplishing this much was already his limit. He could only lament the injustice of the Heavens. Since the Heavens had created all living things, why did they divide them into ranks? Why did the Dragon Clan alone have to stand above all living creatures?

## Chapter 2630 — Completely Sober

The reunion of two pairs of Senior and Junior Sisters, separated by generation and time, was truly a moving sight.

Most of the female disciples from Ice Heart Valley were sentimental by nature. Looking here and there, their eyes soon grew moist, seemingly affected by the atmosphere. The swords in their hands were all put away, and they stood quietly in place.

"Ahem..." Yang Kai placed his fist to his mouth and coughed lightly, instantly drawing everyone's attention.

"Oh, right, Seventh Junior Sister, I actually came back together with Master," Ji Yao said with delight to the petite figure, her face lighting up with joy.

"Master?" The petite figure looked utterly confused and asked suspiciously, "Isn't Master still..."

"Miss Changsun, we meet again." Yang Kai smiled slightly, interrupting her.

This petite woman was none other than Changsun Ying, Bing Yun's seventh disciple, whom Yang Kai had met a few years ago!

Changsun Ying turned to look at Yang Kai in surprise and said, "Senior Brother Yang, why is it you!"

She was indeed surprised. Having just reunited with Ji Yao after so long, she had been immersed in the joy and hadn't paid much attention. Only now did she realize that her Third Senior Sister had actually come together with Yang Kai.

She called Yang Kai Senior Brother not because his cultivation was higher than hers—she was at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, and when she met Yang Kai a few years ago, he was only at the Third Stage of the Dao Origin Realm.

She addressed him this way purely out of respect.

After all, the reason Bing Yun was able to escape the Jixu Secret Realm and return to Ice Heart Valley was largely due to Yang Kai's great merit. After Bing Yun's disciples learned of this, they were all deeply grateful to Yang Kai, and calling him Senior Brother was a matter of courtesy.

"Miss Changsun doesn't welcome me?" Yang Kai smiled slightly.

Changsun Ying blushed and said, "How could that be? Senior Brother Yang has done a great favor for our Ice Heart Valley. How could we not welcome you? Master has long instructed us that Senior Brother Yang is to be treated as one of our own."

"Senior Bing Yun is most thoughtful." Yang Kai nodded gently.

Ji Yao, on the other side, frowned, looking at Yang Kai with a puzzled expression. Her beautiful eyes gradually grew confused.

In her understanding, Yang Kai was Master. He was Bing Yun. But now, hearing Yang Kai's words, she was completely bewildered and couldn't make sense of it.

Sensing her abnormality, Yang Kai's expression turned serious as he said, "Is Senior Bing Yun available now? I have something I need to discuss with her."

Changsun Ying quickly replied, "Master hasn't been in seclusion lately. If she learns you've come, she'll surely be delighted. And Third Senior Sister has also returned... Please, come with me!"

As she spoke, she took Ji Yao by the hand and led the way.

Yang Kai, carrying Fang Minghui, walked a few steps before turning back and calling, "Tianhe, you come too. See your Master."

Shi Tianhe's delicate body trembled, her face full of unease.

"Don't be afraid, Junior Sister. I'll go with you." Yu Dan patted Shi Tianhe's hand and spoke softly, which finally calmed Shi Tianhe a little. The two of them followed Yang Kai's pace.

On the way, Shi Tianhe asked anxiously, "Senior Sister, has Master still been angry with me all these years?"

Yu Dan nodded seriously, "Of course."

Shi Tianhe's face immediately turned pale, her legs feeling as heavy as lead, unable to move.

But Yu Dan gave a mysterious smile and said, "Back then, you defied Master just to be with that man named Dong. Of course Master would be angry. After all, she raised you herself. If you were so disobedient, how could she not be angry?"

Shi Tianhe said bitterly, "It was this Junior Sister who disappointed Master."

Yu Dan said, "But don't worry. Though Master is angry, she's actually been thinking about you all these years."

"Really?" Shi Tianhe asked in delight.

Yu Dan whispered, "For the past hundred years, every two years, I've been ordered to go to Taiping City to check on you and see if you've suffered any grievances."

Shi Tianhe's body trembled, and she asked excitedly, "Master sent you?"

Yu Dan smiled, "Would I dare go without Master's orders? Master said that if you suffered even the slightest grievance in the Dong Family, she would wipe them out completely. Don't think that just because she hasn't interfered in your affairs for a hundred years, she hasn't been thinking of you."

Hearing this, Shi Tianhe's eyes turned red again, nearly bursting into tears. Yu Dan comforted her for a while before she calmed down.

...

In the main hall of Ice Heart Valley, Yang Kai sat upright and solemn, with Ji Yao and Shi Tianhe standing behind him. As for Fang Minghui, he was still unconscious, tossed at Yang Kai's feet and ignored. Yu Dan stayed by Shi Tianhe's side, quietly communicating with her.

After serving tea, Changsun Ying said, "Senior Brother Yang, please wait a moment. Master has already received the message and will be here soon."

"She's already here!" Yang Kai's expression shifted as he looked toward the entrance of the hall.

A flash of light appeared outside the hall, and Bing Yun's figure emerged. Following closely behind her was a group of Emperor Realm experts from Ice Heart Valley, not many—fewer than ten.

The eldest disciple, An Ruoyun, the second disciple, Sun Yunxiu, and others were all present.

Yang Kai quickly got up, cupped his fists, and greeted, "Yang Kai greets Senior Bing Yun!"

Bing Yun smiled, "You've come?"

Although the two hadn't spent much time together, their shared origin from the Hengluo Star Domain made them feel much closer than with others. In this time of crisis, seeing Yang Kai truly made Bing Yun happy from the bottom of her heart.

"Greetings, Senior Brother Yang!" The disciples behind Bing Yun also hurriedly saluted.

Yang Kai nodded, his gaze sweeping over the crowd. He saw Liu Qianyun, Fan Xin, and Zi Yu. Now, Zi Yu had reached the Emperor Realm. Like Yang Kai, she had entered the Shattered Star Sea and, after gaining an opportunity there, was able to advance.

As their eyes met, Zi Yu nodded slightly at Yang Kai, her eyes full of gratitude.

Back in the Shattered Star Sea, she was on the verge of a breakthrough but attracted the attention of enemies. The disciples from Ice Heart Valley couldn't protect her. Only after she broke through to the Emperor Realm did she learn from those disciples that it was Yang Kai who had lured away the powerful foes, giving her the space to break through safely.

This favor alone was enough for Zi Yu to be deeply grateful, not to mention Yang Kai's countless ties to Ice Heart Valley and his close relationship with their founder, Bing Yun.

Liu Qianyun was also very excited. She hadn't seen Yang Kai in years and had much to say, but now was not the time.

"I heard Ji Yao has returned?" Bing Yun asked as she looked to the back, immediately spotting Ji Yao standing there in a daze.

"Third Junior Sister!"

"Third Senior Sister!"

An Ruoyun and the others also called out excitedly.

But Ji Yao just stood there, her beautiful eyes full of confusion, staring at Bing Yun. The confusion in her eyes grew clearer, as if a dark cloud had blocked her vision.

"Third Junior Sister, what are you dazing out for? Hurry and pay respects to Master!" An Ruoyun, not understanding, thought Ji Yao was just too emotional and quickly reminded her.

But Bing Yun noticed something was wrong and turned to Yang Kai, asking, "What's wrong with Yao'er?"

Yang Kai quickly sent a message, briefly explaining Ji Yao's current condition, not hiding the fact that he had been pretending to be her Master.

After hearing this, Bing Yun felt deep heartache. Ji Yao had suffered so much in the Wild Ancient Land that even her mind had become muddled. If not for her chance encounter with Yang Kai, she might still be wandering there in a daze.

With a gentle sigh and a kind expression, Bing Yun called out, "Yao'er, your Master is right here. Why don't you come over?"

"Master..." At the call, Ji Yao's delicate body trembled, her beautiful eyes suddenly quivering violently. In an instant, the confusion shattered, replaced by clarity.

"Pfft..." Ji Yao opened her lips and spat out a mouthful of black blood. Her aura weakened considerably, but her eyes became incomparably bright.

An Ruoyun and the others were all shocked, completely at a loss as to what had happened to Ji Yao.

"Master!" Ji Yao staggered forward, knelt before Bing Yun, and said in a trembling voice, "This unworthy disciple has made Master worry."

Bing Yun reached out and gently patted her head, saying, "It's good that you're back. Get up."

Ji Yao shook her head, sobbing uncontrollably, clutching Bing Yun's leg as if she would never let go again.

An Ruoyun and the others quietly wiped their eyes.

Bing Yun said helplessly, "We are Master and disciple, reunited after so long. This is a joyous occasion—no need to cry. Get up and speak."

She reached out and pulled Ji Yao up, personally wiping away her tears and handing her over to An Ruoyun to look after. Only then did she turn to Yang Kai and say, "You must think it funny. Yao'er has always been stubborn since she was a child."

Yang Kai shook his head, "Yao'er... ahem, Junior Sister Ji Yao is a person of deep feeling."

He had become used to calling her Yao'er and almost slipped up again.

Hearing this, Ji Yao glanced at Yang Kai, her gaze somewhat complicated. After seeing the real Bing Yun, she was undoubtedly completely sober. Though it was unclear if she would relapse in the future, at least for now she was safe and sound.

Precisely because she was sober, she realized just how bad her previous state had been.

To mistake a man for her Master—if it hadn't happened to her, Ji Yao would never have believed it possible.

Yang Kai felt a bit guilty and didn't dare meet her eyes, his gaze wandering.

"Let's all sit and talk. No need to stand." Bing Yun was in a great mood. Her long-lost third disciple had returned to the Sect, and Yang Kai was here as well. It was a day of great joy, and a rare smile appeared on her face.

The disciples, seeing this, were secretly delighted.

Bing Yun hadn't smiled in a long time. Today, the clouds finally parted, and the disciples all felt much more at ease.

At Bing Yun's word, no one dared disobey. Everyone took their seats according to their status.

An Ruoyun, having received Bing Yun's instructions, didn't dare let Ji Yao stray too far and naturally had her sit close by to look after her.

"Senior Brother Yang, did you come here because you heard about our Ice Heart Valley's situation?" After sitting down, Sun Yunxiu looked at Yang Kai curiously. If not, the timing was just too coincidental.

Yang Kai shook his head and said, "Not exactly. I just happened to run into Junior Sister Ji Yao in the Wild Ancient Land and thought to bring her back first. On the way through Taiping City, something happened that let us learn about Ice Heart Valley's current situation."

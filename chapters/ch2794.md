## Chapter 2794 — Buffer Zone

Yang Kai descended together with Lan Xun, and Xiao Chen and Lei Ting immediately came forward to greet them.

"Your Highness!" Xiao Chen greeted with a beaming smile, while Lei Ting, who had followed him over, looked rather uneasy, glancing around as if extremely uncomfortable.

"Senior Brother Xiao, Senior Brother Lei!" Lan Xun smiled, glancing at the two of them.

"Uh..." Lei Ting mumbled, his face suddenly flushing red, not even daring to meet Lan Xun's eyes.

A trace of surprise flashed in Yang Kai's eyes as he glanced at Lei Ting, then at Lan Xun, grinning with a knowing smile.

"Brother Yang!" Xiao Chen cupped his fists to Yang Kai, his manner warm and amiable, completely different from the overbearing attitude of their previous encounters, as if he were a different person.

"Brother Xiao, Brother Lei, we meet again." Yang Kai returned the gesture.

Lei Ting snorted softly and turned his head aside.

Yang Kai didn't mind and asked, "Are there only four of us Emperors entering the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda this time?"

Although many cultivators had gathered here, it seemed only the four of them were at the Emperor Realm. The four Third Stage Emperor Elders were also present, but they certainly wouldn't be entering.

Lan Xun nodded, "Yes, just the four of us. The price for Emperor Realm cultivators to enter the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda is not small, and it takes up more spots, so each time, there can't be too many Emperors entering."

Yang Kai let out a sound of acknowledgment.

Lan Xun continued, "Senior Brother Yang, although you've trained in the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda once before, the situation this time is different. The pagoda has five layers, each filled with many shattered spaces. Some of these spaces are interconnected and can be entered and exited freely, while others are completely sealed. The higher the layer, the more pronounced this becomes. The first layer is suitable for Origin Returning Realm cultivators, the second for Void King Realm, the third for Dao Origin Realm, and the fourth for Emperor Realm. As for the top fifth layer, only the strongest Emperor Realm experts can enter."

"Is there some kind of seal?" Yang Kai raised his brows.

Lan Xun nodded, "Yes, without sufficient strength, you can't enter the next layer. Of course, the higher the layer, the more dangerous it is. Over a decade ago, when the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda opened, an ancient Mu Xiao appeared in the second layer, causing many deaths in Fenglin City. Senior Brother Yang, you experienced this firsthand, so it should be fresh in your memory, right?"

Yang Kai replied gravely, "How could I forget?"

That Mu Xiao disaster in Fenglin City killed several thousand, even tens of thousands, and Yang Kai had witnessed it all, so he naturally remembered it clearly.

Lan Xun said solemnly, "Although training in the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda greatly benefits cultivators, there are also many dangers. Every time it opens, some of our Star God Palace disciples fall inside."

"There's no way to guarantee their safety?" Yang Kai frowned.

Lan Xun replied, "No way. It's said that the shattered spaces sealed within the pagoda are all ancient battlefields, fragments of worlds torn apart. Though broken, these worlds still retain many natural laws, and many tenacious ancient remnants have found ways to survive within. Later, a mighty figure used earth-shaking means to collect those shattered worlds and refined them into the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda. The pagoda has passed through many hands before coming to my father, but even he cannot fully control the countless shattered spaces."

Pausing, Lan Xun smiled, "Of course, even if he could, Father probably wouldn't interfere. If training had no danger, what would be the point?"

Yang Kai nodded in deep agreement, "Junior Sister Lan is right."

Lan Xun smiled, "So when you enter the pagoda later, Senior Brother Yang, you must be careful and not let your guard down."

"I'll remember." Yang Kai nodded solemnly.

Meanwhile, the Star God Palace disciples participating in the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda training had almost all arrived.

Great Elder Lei Hong gave a light cough, and the noisy crowd instantly fell silent, over a thousand pairs of eyes turning to him.

"The Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda opens today. You are all the elite of our Palace, the pillars of its future. The Elders and I hope you will gain something inside, but above all, keep yourselves alive. As fellow disciples, you should help and support each other inside..."

Taking advantage of the moment, Lan Xun quietly sent Yang Kai a few more details about the pagoda, which he committed to memory.

Lei Hong didn't say much more. After a few words, he waved his hand, "If you remember, then get ready to enter!"

As he finished, he and the other three Elders exchanged glances, then each moved to a corner of the pagoda and began channeling their power, forming hand seals.

As Emperor Yuan surged, the four of them pointed downward.

With a rumble, the tightly closed doors of the Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda suddenly swung open.

Though their movements were swift, the Four Elders were suddenly drenched in sweat, clearly showing that opening the pagoda was no small burden for them.

As the doors opened, a slowly spinning pitch-black vortex appeared within, like the gaping maw of a beast, ready to devour all.

At first, the vortex was unstable, twisting and shifting, sending out waves of unsettling power.

But as the Four Elders continued to pour in their strength, the vortex gradually stabilized, and the disturbing fluctuations faded away.

This process lasted about the time it takes to burn a stick of incense before the Four Elders finally withdrew their power, each pale and gasping for breath, their auras extremely weak.

At this moment, if even a Second Stage Emperor Realm master were to appear, the Four Elders would hardly be able to put up a fight.

At that moment, Great Elder Lei Hong suddenly shouted, "Go!"

The Star God Palace disciples, already eager to enter, didn't hesitate. One after another, they darted into the vortex and vanished.

The discipline of a great sect was clear. Over a thousand people moved like the wind, yet there was no crowding or jostling—everything remained orderly.

First, the Origin Returning Realm cultivators entered, then the Void King Realm, followed by the Dao Origin Realm...

In less than fifty breaths, nearly everyone had gone in, leaving only Yang Kai and the other three Emperor Realm cultivators.

"Let's go as well," Lan Xun called, taking the lead and flying forward.

Just before entering the vortex, Lan Xun reminded, "Take care, Senior Brothers."

"You too, Your Highness!" Xiao Chen smiled, showing timely concern.

"Xun... Xun..." Lei Ting stammered, but before he could finish, Lan Xun had already disappeared, leaving him looking frustrated and dejected.

"I'll go on ahead," Xiao Chen laughed, then dashed into the vortex.

Lei Ting turned and glared fiercely at Yang Kai.

"What?" Yang Kai looked back at him.

"Don't let me run into you in there, or you're dead," Lei Ting snorted coldly.

Yang Kai chuckled and was the first to rush into the vortex.

His vision blurred, but instead of falling into a shattered world as he'd expected, the moment he entered the pagoda, a strange force suddenly blocked his body, and he instinctively summoned his full strength in preparation.

As the Laws of Space surged, it felt as if he'd crashed into a mass of cotton and was bounced back.

What he saw next shocked Yang Kai.

He found himself floating in a void, surrounded on all sides by countless cloud-like masses. Some were brilliantly colored and pleasing to the eye, others were dull and lifeless, and some were pitch-black and unsettling.

These cloud-like things were densely packed all around him, too many to count.

What's going on? Yang Kai felt a bit dizzy.

This wasn't quite what Lan Xun had described earlier.

The Five-Colored Treasure Pagoda was divided into five layers, each containing many shattered spaces. Some were interconnected, some completely sealed. Cultivators entering the pagoda were automatically placed in the layer and space suited to their cultivation.

For example, Origin Returning Realm cultivators pass through the pagoda doors and enter a shattered space on the first layer.

Similarly, Void King Realm cultivators enter a shattered space on the second layer.

As a First Stage Emperor, Yang Kai would certainly be placed in the fourth layer.

The fifth layer couldn't be entered directly through the pagoda doors; you had to find an entrance from the fourth layer.

Yang Kai had assumed he'd appear in a shattered space on the fourth layer, and Lan Xun had said as much, but now it seemed something was off.

This definitely wasn't a shattered space of the fourth layer, because here Yang Kai couldn't sense any Law Power, and what were those cloud-like things?

Releasing his Divine Sense to investigate, Yang Kai was both shocked and alarmed.

To his astonishment, Yang Kai realized that these cloud-like masses resembled entrances to Void Passages. Passing through one of them might transport him to who knows where.

Frowning in thought for a moment, Yang Kai suddenly snapped to attention and looked back.

Behind him, a huge pitch-black vortex was slowly spinning.

Wasn't that the entrance to the pagoda? Why was it behind him?

If the entrance was behind, then the way forward must be ahead. Was he in some kind of buffer zone inside the pagoda?

Yang Kai's mind raced, and suddenly, inspiration struck—could these cloud-like Void Passage entrances be...

Looking around, Yang Kai fixed his gaze on a cloud radiating dazzling golden light, and with a flicker, he rushed into it.

His vision spun, the world turned upside down, and a strange Law Power swirled around him. When Yang Kai regained his senses, he found himself standing on a vast plain.

The plain was barren, covered in broken stones, utterly desolate. There were no sun, moon, or stars, yet it was as bright as day.

"Such dense Metal Attribute Spiritual Qi!" Yang Kai exclaimed, his face full of shock as he sensed the surroundings.

The Spiritual Qi here was strangely intense—so dense it was almost unnatural. Yet, despite its abundance, it possessed only a single attribute: pure Metal. This was not the natural Spiritual Qi of the world, but rather a force overwhelmingly slanted toward the Metal element.

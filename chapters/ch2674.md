## Chapter 2674 — Boundless Strength

After hesitating for a moment, Yingfei said, “Which word?”

The young woman’s words left him confused; he had no idea which word he’d just said that had angered her. But Yingfei wasn’t someone easily frightened—he was, after all, one of the Thirty-Two Yao Kings of the Ancient Land. Even if the girl’s cultivation was impressive, did she really think he was easy to deal with?

If it really came to a fight, Yingfei wouldn’t be afraid of her.

As he asked, he released his Divine Sense, sweeping it over the girl to gauge her cultivation.

Yang Kai was doing the same, but to both their shock, although the girl was standing right there, their Divine Senses found nothing at all. They couldn’t sense her cultivation—couldn’t even sense her presence.

There were only two possibilities for this. Either the girl’s cultivation was far higher than Yingfei’s and Yang Kai’s, or she possessed a powerful treasure that blocked others’ probing.

The first possibility was unlikely. Yingfei was a Yao King; anyone stronger would have to be a Divine Spirit or a Great Emperor. This girl was so young—her cultivation couldn’t possibly be that high. So, it must be that she was protected by some extraordinary treasure.

This girl’s background must be significant, Yang Kai thought to himself, wondering which sect or faction she hailed from.

“You’ll understand once you say that word.” The girl sneered, not answering Yingfei’s question directly. She looked at Yang Kai and asked, “What’s your name?”

She seemed fixated on Yang Kai’s name, determined to get a clear answer.

But before Yang Kai could reply, she suddenly waved her hand and said, “Forget it, forget it, there’s no need to answer.”

“Are you messing with me?” The veins on Yang Kai’s forehead bulged. He wondered if this girl was crazy—blocking his way for no reason and spouting such nonsense. A surge of anger flared up in his chest.

The girl snorted coldly. “You think you’re worth my time to toy with?”

Yang Kai's face darkened. "I don't even know who you are, miss."

He’d seen plenty of arrogant, overbearing people before, but never anyone like this. This wasn’t just arrogance—it was as if she was the center of the world, completely disregarding everyone else.

“The one who’s going to kill you,” the girl replied icily.

Yang Kai was stunned. “You want to kill me? This is our first meeting, isn’t it? What grudge do we have?”

The girl frowned seriously and nodded. “It is our first meeting, but… you possess things that don’t belong to you. You shouldn’t have refined those things. Since you did, you must die!”

“What things?” Yang Kai looked utterly confused.

“You’ll know once I break your bones.” With that, the girl suddenly swung her fist at Yang Kai. Her delicate, jade-like fist was small and translucent, looking almost like a lover’s playful gesture.

But the power behind that punch made Yang Kai’s expression change dramatically.

A pitch-black mark was carved into the void, space itself shattering. The wild force of her punch spun like a massive vortex, pulling Yang Kai toward its center, threatening to tear him to pieces.

When the girl had punched at Yingfei earlier, Yang Kai hadn’t felt much—he’d only thought this little girl was surprisingly strong. But now, facing her head-on, he realized he’d underestimated her.

This strength was simply not something a girl her age should possess. If it were Yao King Xi Lei in his true form, Yang Kai wouldn’t be surprised. But for such power to come from a girl of only seventeen or eighteen was shocking.

Yang Kai’s heart trembled. As expected, the world was full of hidden talents. Even a random young girl who appeared out of nowhere could be so formidable—how many more extraordinary people must there be in this vast world?

“Be careful, Young Master Yang!” Yingfei’s face changed as he reached out to grab the girl’s fist.

“You can’t block it!” Yang Kai’s face darkened. He summoned all his strength and threw a punch to meet hers.

Countless fist shadows exploded in the air. With a muffled grunt, Yingfei was sent flying back more than a dozen zhang, his eagle claw slightly twisted and trembling. Yang Kai’s chest churned with Qi and blood, and he heard the sound of bones cracking in his arm.

Even joining forces, the two of them couldn’t withstand this wild power.

After her first punch, the girl didn’t stop. Her waist twisted, and she launched another punch straight at Yang Kai’s forehead. The move was fierce, clearly meant to take his life in one blow, yet her expression remained calm, without the slightest killing intent—strangely detached.

But this didn’t mean she didn’t want to kill Yang Kai; it was just a casual display.

When a butcher slaughters cattle or sheep, does he need to look murderous?

The vortex of power swept out again. Ye Qinghan and Du Xian both cried out in alarm, their bodies drawn uncontrollably toward the girl’s fist, as if they were rushing to their deaths.

This was not their will. The girl’s cultivation was so high that she didn’t care about their lives at all. They were just collateral damage.

Their Origin Qi couldn’t be mobilized at all. In the torrent of power, they were like drowning children, unable to break free no matter how hard they struggled.

Just as they were about to crash into that delicate pink fist—certain to be crushed to pieces in the next instant—another fist suddenly met it head-on. Five-colored light erupted from that fist, and fierce sword qi swept out in all directions.

Yang Kai had pushed the Undying Five Elements Sword Qi to its limit, unleashing his Emperor Yuan in a frenzy.

Boom…

Amid the deafening crash, Yang Kai floated backward, his fist a bloody mess with white bone exposed. But using the rebound from the clash, he managed to pull Ye Qinghan and Du Xian back to safety.

Crash…

Behind Yang Kai, a surge of residual force exploded, shredding his clothes to dust and revealing his muscular back. Not only that, the towering City Lord’s mansion was leveled in an instant, and the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect disciples inside suffered heavy casualties.

“Maniac!” Yang Kai glared angrily at the girl.

The girl frowned but didn’t press further. She glanced at Yang Kai’s bloody fist, the corners of her mouth lifting in disdain.

“Young Master Yang!” Yingfei’s figure flickered as he moved to stand beside Yang Kai, killing intent flashing in his eagle eyes.

He had accompanied Yang Kai back to the Southern Territory mainly to protect his safety. Yang Kai’s affairs were too important—if anything happened to him, all the Yao Kings of the Wild Ancient Land would be implicated.

He hadn’t expected someone to injure Yang Kai right in front of him.

If word of this got out, Xie Wuwei and Xi Lei would never let it go. They’d probably curse him out thoroughly.

“Take care of them!” Yang Kai handed Ye Qinghan and Du Xian to Yingfei, then looked around.

The commotion had drawn the attention of many Emperor Realm cultivators. One after another, they flew over to watch from a distance. Seeing that Yang Kai was in a conflict and injured, they were all delighted, gloating inwardly.

After all, Yang Kai’s behavior at the city gate had been outrageously arrogant, angering everyone. Now that he was suffering, how could they not be pleased? Especially the old man and the burly middle-aged man whom Yang Kai had embarrassed earlier—they were overjoyed.

But what surprised everyone even more was the girl’s identity. She looked so young, yet she could injure Yang Kai—truly astonishing.

“If you want to fight, let’s do it outside.” Yang Kai called out, then flew toward the city outskirts.

This was Flowing Shadow City, after all. With the girl’s strength, a real fight here would harm many innocents. Besides, Yang Kai didn’t want to reveal too much of his power in front of so many people.

“Very well. I’ll let you die knowing why.” The girl nodded and, with a flicker, followed Yang Kai.

“Young Master Yang.” Yingfei’s face darkened as he prepared to give chase.

From afar, Yang Kai’s voice rang out: “No need to follow, and don’t let anyone else follow. It’s just a little girl—once I deal with her, I’ll be back!”

Yingfei paused, hearing the anger in Yang Kai’s words. Anyone would be upset to be troubled for no reason.

But Yang Kai’s order put him in a tough spot. If he didn’t follow, he worried something might happen to Yang Kai. If he did, he’d anger Yang Kai.

As he hesitated, quite a few Emperor Realm cultivators moved to follow and watch the excitement.

Yingfei snorted coldly, his Yao Qi surging and erupting in an instant.

“Hiss…”

“Yao King!”

The faces of the Emperor Realm cultivators changed dramatically. They froze in place as if struck by a binding spell.

Most of them were only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Now, with a Yao King-level expert appearing in the city, how could they not be terrified? A Yao King was comparable to the Third Stage of the Emperor Realm. If he went on a rampage, no one in Flowing Shadow City could stop him.

“Anyone who wants to die, go ahead and follow.” Yingfei swept his gaze coldly over the crowd. Every Emperor he looked at trembled in fear, their faces pale.

Those Emperor Realm cultivators who'd taken issue with Yang Kai and harbored grudges against him felt their hearts sink even further. When that Yao King had followed behind Yang Kai at the city gates, they hadn't thought much of it. But now they finally understood—Yang Kai's arrogance and overbearing attitude weren't baseless. He had the capital to back it up.

Fortunately, they hadn’t really clashed with Yang Kai back then—otherwise, they’d be dead by now.

“This is trouble…” In a hidden corner, Wu Ming frowned deeply, staring in Yingfei’s direction in confusion. “Why is there a Yao King here?”

This Flowing Shadow City auction was mainly targeting Yang Kai. If it were just him, an Emperor Realm cultivator, Wu Ming believed his master’s arrangements would be foolproof. But now, with a powerful Yao King suddenly appearing, things might change.

After a moment’s hesitation, Wu Ming quickly turned to report this to his master, to see what he planned to do.

A Yao King-level expert was not someone he could ignore.

“S-Sir!” Ye Qinghan and Du Xian were both stunned.

Although they’d spent a few days with Yingfei, they’d never imagined that the man who’d been following Yang Kai like a bodyguard was actually a Yao King.

Only now did they understand why Yang Kai had the confidence to enter the Flowing Shadow Sword Sect alone. It turned out his greatest support wasn’t his own strength, but this Yao King.

With a Yao King standing guard, what was there to worry about?

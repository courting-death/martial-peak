## Chapter 2939 — Starting Anew

The milky white radiance continued to flow through his Spirit Body, like countless stars scattered across the heavens, impossible to count. With each passing moment, his form grew a little more solid, and the vitality emanating from that body gradually became richer.

At a certain moment, Tian Yan’s gradually solidifying body suddenly emitted a powerful attraction. The Spiritual Qi of the entire Spirit Sword Peak rippled and surged toward him, and in the blink of an eye, it was completely devoured by him.

An even stronger pull spread out, and the winds howled atop Spirit Sword Peak. Spiritual Qi from afar rushed over, yet it still could not satisfy Tian Yan’s needs.

His fleshly body, formed from a Spiritual Pill, now seemed like a bottomless pit, able to swallow any amount of Spiritual Qi cleanly.

Wen Zishan noticed this immediately and acted at once. He used his mind to communicate with the Grand Guardian Array of Qingyang Shrine, gathering all the Spiritual Qi of the Shrine onto Spirit Sword Peak. Meanwhile, Yang Kai summoned his Elder Jade Token, directing the surging Spiritual Qi into Tian Yan’s body.

The two worked together seamlessly, each performing their part.

As the Spiritual Qi poured in, the vitality within Tian Yan’s body grew ever more exuberant.

If there had been anything unnatural about that vitality before, now even that last trace was slowly being erased. Tian Yan’s body was transforming into true flesh and blood.

The omnipresent Heavenly Dao seemed to sense something. Suddenly, an indescribable Heavenly Might began to condense atop Spirit Sword Peak. Though extremely faint and not yet fully formed, both Yang Kai and Wen Zishan were startled.

The Great Might of Heaven and Earth! Yang Kai clearly sensed a trace of that power.

The laws of Heaven and Earth differ in every world, but the one thing they share is the Great Might of Heaven and Earth. It is the world’s origin, the power of the entire world. Thus, those in the Emperor Realm control the laws of Heaven and Earth, while Great Emperors have glimpsed the Great Might of Heaven and Earth itself. This is the fundamental difference between the two.

Tian Yan’s existence was not tolerated by the Star Realm. Previously, he had used a divine ability to conceal himself and evade the Heavenly Dao, but as time passed, this trickery gradually revealed its flaws, allowing the Heavenly Dao to sense his presence.

There truly wasn’t much time left. If Tian Yan could not finish forging his fleshly body before the Heavenly Dao’s might descended, he would surely be expelled from this world and banished into the endless void. If that happened, even with the Warm Spirit Lotus, Yang Kai might not be able to save him.

Yang Kai and Wen Zishan’s expressions grew grave as they drew out the Spiritual Qi of the entire Qingyang Shrine even faster, pouring it into Tian Yan’s body. This was all they could do; the rest depended entirely on Tian Yan’s own efforts.

Time passed slowly. The Spiritual Qi within Qingyang Shrine surged without pause, and the sounds of wind and thunder echoed atop Spirit Sword Peak. Tian Yan’s body grew ever more solid, and his vitality ever more vigorous.

Meanwhile, the power of the Heavenly Dao gathering atop Spirit Sword Peak also grew stronger, like a sharp blade hanging over everyone’s heads, ready to fall at any moment, leaving all on edge and uneasy.

Suddenly, Tian Yan opened his eyes. His hands formed several mysterious seals, neither fast nor slow. His newly forged body seemed to swallow all the Spiritual Qi within dozens of li in an instant, and the milky white light that had been roaming within him vanished at that moment.

The moon and stars still hung in the sky, and at some point, peace had returned to Spirit Sword Peak. The Heavenly Dao’s might, which had been gathering, also disappeared without a trace.

Yang Kai’s eyes darted about, glancing at Tian Yan, then at Wen Zishan, then up at the sky, looking a bit lost.

“Congratulations, Senior!” Wen Zishan suddenly spoke, cupping his fists and smiling toward Tian Yan.

Tian Yan also smiled and said, “It’s all thanks to the help of you two. This old man is deeply grateful.”

Wen Zishan replied with a smile, “I only contributed some brute force. For Senior to forge a fleshly body, it was all due to Yang Kai finding the Life Origin Pill.”

Yang Kai asked, “Senior, you’ve succeeded?”

From their conversation, he could tell as much. If it hadn’t worked, Tian Yan wouldn’t be so at ease.

Tian Yan smiled, “It’s done.”

“That’s wonderful!” Yang Kai was overjoyed. Only now did he release his Divine Sense to scan Tian Yan, and what he saw left him dumbfounded.

At this moment, Tian Yan actually gave him an extremely weak feeling. But it wasn’t the weakness of someone at death’s door, rather a natural kind of frailty—one might even call it… feebleness.

Yang Kai couldn’t sense any power fluctuations from within him. Tian Yan was just like an ordinary person who had never cultivated.

“Senior, what’s going on…?” Yang Kai asked in surprise.

Tian Yan smiled faintly, “The Heavenly Dao is not so easily deceived. Even if you can fool it for a while, you can’t fool it forever. I can only go along with it. Right now, this old man is probably no different from a newborn infant.”

Yang Kai’s jaw dropped. This was not what he had expected at all…

He had thought that after forging a fleshly body, Tian Yan would still be a Great Emperor-level expert. Who could have guessed things would turn out like this? But thinking about it, it made sense. There was no such thing as a free lunch. In the Divine Roaming World, Tian Yan was indeed a Great Emperor, but that only applied to his Divine Soul. His fleshly body was created by the Life Origin Pill. No matter how much Spiritual Qi he devoured, it was impossible to instantly reach the level of a Great Emperor.

Realizing this, Yang Kai felt at ease. He had simply been too naive before.

Wen Zishan said, “With Senior’s foundation, even starting from scratch, it shouldn’t take long to return to your peak.”

“Let’s hope so.” Tian Yan chuckled. “But before that, this old man will need to stay in the Shrine for some time.”

Wen Zishan laughed, “As long as Senior doesn’t mind the humble conditions of my Shrine, you can stay as long as you like. Would you like me to arrange a Spirit Peak for you?”

Tian Yan shook his head with a slight smile. "There's no need. This place is quite suitable. I can see that boy Yang is a busy man, and this secluded peak is perfect for me to rest and recover. Besides..." He paused, glancing around thoughtfully. "This is, after all, the place where this old man was born anew. Cultivating here is like being nurtured in the womb; if I were to move elsewhere, I might not be accustomed to it."

“If that’s the case, then so be it. Tomorrow, I’ll have someone deliver some cultivation materials. If you need anything, just let me know.”

“Then send over some books—anything at all, the more the better.”

“Alright, I’ll take my leave for now.” Wen Zishan cupped his fists, glanced at Yang Kai, and departed.

Tian Yan turned to look at Yang Kai and smiled, “You seem a bit disappointed.”

Yang Kai chuckled softly, "I wouldn't say disappointed, exactly. It's just a bit different from what I imagined. Ah, take it slowly—let me help you up." As he spoke, he stepped forward and supported Tian Yan, who was attempting to rise.

Tian Yan was clearly not used to his new body. When he first stood, he staggered a few times. Fortunately, Yang Kai was right beside him, or he might have fallen.

Just as he said, at this moment he was like a newborn infant—everything had to be learned from scratch.

There was a palace atop Spirit Sword Peak. Though not large, it was spacious enough for two people.

Yang Kai and Tian Yan chatted as they walked into the palace together.

After only a few steps, Tian Yan suddenly stopped. Yang Kai didn’t know what was happening and turned to look, only to see Tian Yan’s expression go blank, his eyes vacant, as if he had lost consciousness.

Yang Kai was startled, thinking something had gone wrong. Just as he was about to check, Tian Yan’s eyes moved again, and his awareness returned in an instant.

“The fusion of body and soul wasn’t very successful. My Divine Soul just left my body for a moment,” Tian Yan explained, shaking his head in frustration and walking even more carefully.

“It won’t always be like this, will it?” Yang Kai asked worriedly.

“Of course not. In a few days, I’ll be fine. It’s just that these next few days will be a bit rough.”

Along the way, they had to stop three times. Each time, Tian Yan’s Divine Soul accidentally left his body, and Yang Kai had a hard time settling him in the side room. He became even more convinced that Tian Yan was right.

Right now, he really was just like a newborn infant!

The next day, Wen Zishan sent over a large number of jade slips, each containing all sorts of information. They were all delivered to Tian Yan’s room. Since Tian Yan had gone into seclusion the previous day and hadn’t come out, Yang Kai didn’t disturb him. Instead, he flew to the platform at the summit of Spirit Sword Peak.

With a single hand seal, the Mountain and River Bell flew up.

A dragon’s roar rang out as Zhu Lie, who had been suppressed within, suddenly transformed into a giant dragon twenty zhang long. He opened his bloody maw and lunged at Yang Kai.

“Dragon Transformation!” Yang Kai gazed at him with disdain, urging the Origin Power of his Golden Sacred Dragon bloodline and performing the Dragon Transformation Art.

His body shot up, and a series of crackling sounds like beans frying echoed from all over him. Short horns sprouted from his forehead, and his body was covered in scales.

The giant dragon that had just lunged at him let out a muffled groan. Its twenty zhang long body shattered like a bubble, and Zhu Lie, in a sorry state, reverted to human form. He happened to fly right in front of Yang Kai, meeting Yang Kai’s iron fist head-on, as if he had rushed up just to get hit.

With a bang, Zhu Lie was sent flying, crashing into the mountainside of a nearby peak and embedding himself several zhang deep.

He was dizzy for a while. When his vision cleared and he looked up, he found Yang Kai already standing before him. The four zhang tall half-dragon form radiated a powerful suppressive force, making Zhu Lie extremely uncomfortable.

“Looks like you still haven’t learned your lesson,” Yang Kai sneered, reaching out a dragon claw and lifting Zhu Lie from the rocks like a chick. No matter how hard Zhu Lie struggled, it was useless.

This scene had played out countless times over the past half month. Every time Yang Kai opened the Mountain and River Bell, Zhu Lie would launch a fierce sneak attack, but without that special dragon scale, he couldn’t resist Yang Kai’s suppression after the Dragon Transformation. Even though his true strength was two Stages higher than Yang Kai’s, he was still beaten without being able to fight back.

Almost every time, it ended with Zhu Lie getting a thorough beating.

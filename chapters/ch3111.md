## Chapter 3111 — Profound Frost Sword, Snow Like the Azure Sky

Yang Kai grinned at him. Han Qiancheng looked baffled, sensing only that there was some deeper meaning behind this fellow's smile. Too lazy to think about it, he said darkly, "Boy, you may eat whatever you like, but you can't say whatever you like. Otherwise, it is very easy to bring a fatal disaster down on yourself."

Throughout the entire Yunxia Sect, who did not know that Su Yan was his forbidden prize? Although many of the Senior Brothers and Junior Brothers had feelings for Su Yan, they only admired her in secret and did not dare show the slightest excessive closeness. There had once been a man who failed to understand his place; merely because he exchanged a few words with Su Yan, every bone in his body was broken the next day, his meridians were severed, and he was turned into a cripple.

From then on, there was not a single man in Yunxia Sect who dared speak to Su Yan.

Yet now someone actually dared claim to be Su Yan's husband? Han Qiancheng laughed in fury, sizing Yang Kai up from head to toe, his gaze sharp as knives, as if deciding where to begin so Yang Kai could properly taste pain.

He turned to Han Zhengyuan and said, "Father, this man barged through our mountain gate without permission, showed utter contempt for my Yunxia Sect, and even damaged our Guardian Mountain Array. He is simply unforgivable. Kill him. Only by doing so can we make an example of him. Otherwise, once this spreads, I fear others will laugh at my Yunxia Sect for being weak and afraid of trouble."

Han Zhengyuan's expression remained indifferent. Hearing this, he said, "Step back. I have my own considerations."

"Father!" Han Qiancheng cried unwillingly.

"Step back!" Han Zhengyuan's face hardened.

Only then did Han Qiancheng retreat, though his expression was not very good. He muttered a few words under his breath, seemingly cursing someone. Since childhood, he had been greatly doted on by Han Zhengyuan, developing an arrogant, domineering temperament that let him do whatever he pleased. If he found Yang Kai unpleasant to the eye and wanted to put him to death, then he was dead set on doing it, unable to understand why his father, who had always granted every request, would refuse him at a time like this.

This made him lose so much face.

If an ordinary person had been this arrogant and overbearing, Han Zhengyuan naturally could not have let it pass so easily. Even if only for Yunxia Sect's face, he would certainly have fought Yang Kai. But the young man before him, though there was not the slightest fluctuation of Spiritual Qi anywhere on his body, gave him an extremely strange feeling.

It was as if, should they make a move here, Yunxia Sect would be the one to suffer.

Han Zhengyuan had no choice but to be cautious.

"How should I address Young Brother?" Han Zhengyuan asked.

Yang Kai gave his name.

Han Zhengyuan frowned and thought for a moment. He had never heard it before; it was a completely unfamiliar name. Turning to look at the Elders, he found they were just as confused. Mm, since none of them had heard of him, he clearly had no background or backer, which made today's matter much easier to handle.

"You say you are Su Yan's husband. Do you have any proof?" Han Zhengyuan asked again, then added, "This is not to question you. It is only that Su Yan is now a disciple of my Yunxia Sect, and as Yunxia Sect's Sect Master, this Lord must ask a thing or two."

Yang Kai said, “Summon Su Yan here, and you will know once you ask her.”

Seeing how confident he was, Han Zhengyuan believed him somewhat. Han Qiancheng, however, looked more and more displeased. It should not be true, right? That woman actually had a husband? When had that happened? She had entered the Sect more than twenty years ago and had never interacted with outsiders. Where had this husband sprung from? It was simply the greatest joke under Heaven.

"Su Yan entered my Yunxia Sect twenty years ago, and during that time she has never interacted with outsiders. How could you have become her husband?"

"I come from Hengluo Star Domain!"

"What?" Han Zhengyuan was astonished to hear this, and in his heart he could not help mocking himself again. Had he lived too long and become too timid? He now hesitated over everything, long since losing the frank decisiveness of his youth.

It was no secret that Su Yan came from Hengluo Star Domain. Many people throughout Yunxia Sect knew this, and as Sect Master, Han Zhengyuan was naturally aware of it as well. Moreover, according to Su Yan, she could be considered the first person to leave Hengluo Star Domain and enter the Ancestral Domain.

In other words, had this boy not entered the Ancestral Domain after Su Yan? If so, he probably had not been in the Ancestral Domain for long. How high could such a fellow's cultivation be? How strong could he possibly be?

At most, he would only be a First Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivator. The reason Han Zhengyuan could not detect his cultivation was probably that he had cultivated some concealment Secret Art or was wearing a Secret Treasure.

Slowly shaking his head and laughing at himself in secret, Han Zhengyuan said, "So it turns out you and Su Yan come from the same Star Domain."

Han Qiancheng's face, however, turned ashen as he cursed endlessly in his heart. Bitch. On ordinary days she looked pure as ice and jade, lofty and untouchable, but who would have thought she was already a ruined flower, a broken willow? To think he had wasted so much effort on her. If he had known this earlier, why delay all these years? He should simply have taken her by force.

"Correct. Su Yan and I pledged ourselves to each other for life back in the Star Domain, and she and I are also Senior Sister and Junior Brother of the same Sect," Yang Kai said somewhat impatiently. "Sect Master Han, please call Su Yan out."

He had come here to find Su Yan. Where did he have the time to dawdle with these people?

Han Zhengyuan said, "Mm, very well." Then his tone changed. "However, the place where Su Yan resides is quite far from here. It will likely take some time for her to arrive."

"No matter. I will wait." He had already waited so many years; why would he fear waiting a little longer? No wonder he could not sense Su Yan's aura. So she was too far away.

"Elder Chen, lead this Young Brother to the side hall for tea while he waits. Then send a message to Su Yan and call her over," Han Zhengyuan said to the red-faced man.

The red-faced man froze for a moment, then cupped his fists and said, "Yes!" Turning around, he gestured to Yang Kai. "Please."

Originally, a cultivator from a Lower Plane Star Domain was not worth such courteous treatment, but the strength Yang Kai had displayed just now was somewhat extraordinary, enough to qualify him to be received in this way.

Yang Kai was neither servile nor overbearing. He nodded and followed Elder Chen away.

The Yunxia Sect disciples watched, all finding this somewhat inconceivable. Today's matter was over just like that? Someone had forced his way through the mountain gate and damaged the Guardian Mountain Array, yet the Elders and Sect Master actually had no intention of pursuing the matter? Moreover, they were even inviting him in for tea? Since when had their own Elders and Sect Master become so easy to talk to?

Besides... Senior Sister Su Yan was not even in the Sect!

Many people smelled a conspiracy and felt somewhat ashamed of their own Elders' and Sect Master's conduct, but they were powerless to change anything. They could only sigh and look with some sympathy at Yang Kai's departing back.

If this man really was Senior Sister Su Yan's man, then he would likely meet disaster today.

In the side hall, Yang Kai sat down casually, his bearing vigorous and imposing. He Yunxiang stood behind him, while Liu Yan unceremoniously sat at the seat below him.

Inside the hall, only the red-faced Elder Chen was receiving them, yet he did not say much to Yang Kai. He merely said, "Please wait a moment. A message has already been sent to Su Yan; she will arrive soon." After that, he closed his eyes and pretended to doze. From the look of it, he was still brooding somewhat over Yang Kai grabbing him by the collar earlier.

He would let this boy act arrogant for a while. Later, he would show him the consequences of offending this Lord. If he opened his eyes, he might reveal a trace of hostility and let Yang Kai notice something wrong.

Female disciples served fragrant tea, and the hall was immediately filled with its aroma.

Red-faced Elder Chen opened his eyes and smiled faintly. "This is Yunxia Tea, unique to my Yunxia Sect. Even in the Ancestral Domain, it enjoys a great reputation. Young Brother might as well have a taste. Ordinary people would never get to drink it, and the spirit teas of the Star Domain cannot compare." Arrogance seeped through his words, and the look he gave Yang Kai was like that of someone regarding a country bumpkin.

Yang Kai stared fixedly at him without saying a word.

Elder Chen's smile did not fade, but his heart was full of vigilance. Had he seen through it? Impossible. It was colorless and odorless; even Elder Chen himself could not distinguish it. How could a boy from a Star Domain possibly have such sharp eyes?

The Sect Master was making far too much of this. Even if this boy had displayed tyrannical strength earlier, with so many people in Yunxia Sect, could they really not take him down? Yet the Sect Master insisted on using this method to deal with him. It was simply unnecessary.

Though Elder Chen was dissatisfied, he could only carry out the order carefully. After all, this was something the Sect Master had instructed him to do in secret. Then again, the Sect Master was far too indulgent toward the Young Sect Master. This boy, like Su Yan, should be a talent. If they soothed him properly, it might not be impossible to recruit him into Yunxia Sect and add a fierce general to the Sect. What a pity, what a pity... He had actually deluded himself into competing with the Young Sect Master over a woman, and now he would no longer live to see tomorrow's sun.

As these thoughts turned in his mind, Elder Chen said, "Does Young Brother find this tea unpalatable?"

"Not at all."

"Since you do not, why not drink?" Elder Chen's face darkened, and paired with his red face, it actually gave him a somewhat imposing air. "Could it be that you refuse to give my Yunxia Sect face?"

Yang Kai smiled faintly, reached out, and lifted the teacup. "Since Elder Chen puts it that way, this Junior can only obey respectfully! I will use this tea in place of wine to thank Yunxia Sect for looking after and cultivating my Senior Sister Su all these years."

No matter what, Su Yan had been able to advance to the Dao Origin Realm and gain a foothold in the Ancestral Domain, so she could be considered to have received some benefits from Yunxia Sect.

After this tea, kindness would be kindness and grievance would be grievance. In any life-and-death struggle that followed, he hoped they would have no complaints.

"Boy, put that down!" A stern shout suddenly came from outside.

Elder Chen's expression changed at the sound. When he looked up, he saw a figure rush in from outside and arrive in front of Yang Kai in the blink of an eye, snatching at the teacup in his hand.

Elder Chen flew into a rage, slapped the table, rose, and shouted, "Elder Ruan, what are you doing!"

Yang Kai merely flicked his wrist and avoided that lightning-swift strike. Then he tilted his head back and drained the tea without leaving a single drop.

Elder Chen froze, then could not help bursting into loud laughter.

The person who had rushed into the hall was also stunned. With her Second Stage Dao Origin Realm cultivation, she had unexpectedly failed to seize the teacup from Yang Kai's hand just now. He had not even risen, yet he had dodged her with ease. What kind of reaction speed was this?

Looking closely, she found that he was indeed exactly as the information had described: there was not the slightest energy fluctuation anywhere on his body, as though he were an ordinary person who had never cultivated.

"Elder Ruan?" Only then did Yang Kai raise his head and look at the newcomer. He saw that she was, astonishingly, a beautiful middle-aged woman. Her coarse cloth robe could not hide her peerless charm, and her figure was full and graceful; she was actually a mature and alluring woman.

Eh, she was completely different from what he had imagined!

In the entire Yunxia Sect, someone with such cultivation and the surname Ruan could only be Su Yan's Master in Yunxia Sect.

"Boy, you say you are Su Yan's husband?" Ruan Biting asked with a frown.

Yang Kai nodded. "Genuine beyond doubt."

"How do you prove it?"

"Profound Frost Sword, Snow Like Clear Sky!"

Ruan Biting's expression sharpened. "It really is you!"

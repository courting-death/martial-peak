## Chapter 3029 — Land of Nightmares

But who could have known that he was actually so warm-hearted? Though he and Yang Kai were complete strangers, he was willing to take a risk to help him out of trouble. After all, doing so could easily offend Yuan Wu, and judging by the current situation, how could anyone who offended Yuan Wu possibly come to a good end?

This greatly improved Yang Kai's impression of him.

Strangely enough, although the sleazy man had interfered and no one knew what he said to Yuan Wu, Yuan Wu did not blame him. Instead, the two drew close and began communicating via transmission.

The sleazy man's face was filled with flattery and ingratiation, which Yuan Wu greatly enjoyed.

For the rest of the journey, Yuan Wu did not cause any more trouble for Yang Kai or Lu Sanniang and her daughter.

Gazing into the distance, Yang Kai narrowed his eyes. He had previously been wondering whether he should directly reveal his identity to make it easier to find Zhu Qing. After all, he and Zhu Qing were already husband and wife in truth, so it was only natural for him to come looking for her. Moreover, he also had the Dragon Isle Token in his possession.

However, after seeing Fu Qi and Fu Ling, he vaguely felt that acting so rashly would not be a good idea. If he really did so, not only might he fail to meet Zhu Qing, but he could also provoke some unforeseen consequences.

Therefore, he decided to act according to the circumstances and find Zhu Qing first.

Night fell, a cool breeze blew, and countless stars dotted the sky.

An island suddenly appeared in everyone's field of vision. Though they could not see it clearly, its area was certainly not small. Moreover, the Spiritual Qi on the island was both rich and incomparably pure, making it extremely suitable to cultivate there.

Yang Kai examined the island and secretly marveled at how exceptional the cultivation environment on Dragon Isle was. No wonder none of the Dragon Clan members he had encountered were below the Emperor Realm. Regardless of how pure Fu Qi's and Fu Ling's Dragon Bloodlines were, both had already reached the Emperor Realm.

This was not surprising. The Dragons were Divine Spirits to begin with, and they occupied such an excellent Small World. It would have been strange if they were not powerful.

"Mother..."

Lu Yuqin's soft cry suddenly came from nearby.

Hearing this, Yang Kai turned his head and frowned. He discovered that something was wrong with Lu Sanniang. Her entire body was trembling, her lips were tightly pressed together, and they were deathly pale and utterly bloodless.

Lu Yuqin had clearly noticed her mother's abnormal state, which was why she had called out in concern.

"Mother, what's wrong?" Lu Yuqin asked softly.

"N-Nothing." Lu Sanniang shook her head.

"Mother, are you feeling unwell? Why is your body so cold?"

"No, it must be the wind." Lu Sanniang reached out and held her daughter tightly.

Lu Yuqin looked at her mother in innocent confusion, only half convinced. She did not know what was wrong with her mother, who had been trembling violently just now, and it was becoming increasingly obvious.

Yang Kai had clearly noticed it too. Lu Sanniang's explanation was obviously only an excuse. She was a First Stage Emperor Realm Master, so how could a mere gust of wind reduce her to such a state? Such an explanation could only fool the naive Lu Yuqin.

After silently observing her for a moment, Yang Kai's eyes flashed, and he looked toward the island not far ahead.

He could roughly guess why Lu Sanniang was reacting this way.

She had been captured and brought to Dragon Isle by a member of the Dragon Clan, then imprisoned here for two or three hundred years. She had only been expelled when Lu Yuqin was born over a decade ago, so the place where the Dragon Clan member who had abducted her lived was undoubtedly the source of her nightmarish memories.

They had passed several islands along the way.

Lu Sanniang had not reacted much to any of them.

However, when the island ahead came into view, Lu Sanniang's condition suddenly changed. The reason was already obvious: that island was precisely where she had spent two or three hundred years!

Seeing this land of nightmares again after more than a decade inevitably stirred up Lu Sanniang's painful memories of the past, so such a reaction was only natural.

Her emotions churned, making even her flight unsteady. Her figure began to sway, frightening Lu Yuqin so badly that her face also paled.

Yang Kai circulated his Emperor Yuan and enveloped the mother and daughter, finally steadying them.

"Thank you!" Lu Sanniang nodded gratefully.

Yang Kai replied, "Since Li Jiao entrusted you to me, I will take care of you. You need not worry too much."

Lu Sanniang fell silent for a moment before asking, "May I ask Young Brother's name?"

"Yang Kai." Yang Kai smiled faintly.

Lu Sanniang said, "So it is Brother Yang. Are you very close to Elder Brother Li?"

Yang Kai nodded. "Very close. We are as close as true brothers, so his affairs are my affairs. Elder Sister Lu need not be so polite."

Yang Kai naturally knew what to say and when to say it. Lu Sanniang's love for Li Jiao ran deep, and she had never forgotten him after all these years, so Yang Kai naturally would not embarrass Li Jiao in front of her.

He could hardly tell her that Li Jiao had recently been bullied miserably by him, could he? That would be far too inconsiderate.

They were only a few nice words anyway, and saying them cost him nothing.

Hearing this, Lu Sanniang smiled. "Although Elder Brother Li has a Yao bloodline, he is a very good person. He especially likes to stand up against injustice, and he is also very kind to others."

As she spoke, she seemed to recall something, and a beaming smile appeared on her face.

With this distraction, her condition really did improve considerably, and she no longer trembled as badly as before.

Yang Kai was left speechless, wondering how someone like Li Jiao could receive such praise. Beauty truly was in the eye of the beholder. In Lu Sanniang's eyes, Li Jiao was probably perfect in every way; she would likely even think his farts smelled fragrant. Yet when he had followed Ji Ying to Lingxiao Palace to cause trouble some days ago, he had been jumping around with extraordinary vigor. How had he shown the slightest trace of kindness then?

At the time, Yang Kai had wanted nothing more than to slap him to death.

Naturally, Yang Kai would not say that in front of Lu Sanniang, so he could only follow along with her words. "Indeed, Elder Sister Lu is right. Although Brother Li and I have not known each other for long, adversity has revealed our true friendship. He has helped me a great deal."

It was true that Li Jiao had helped him. Whether it was searching for Zhu Qing or coming to Dragon Isle this time, Li Jiao had put in a great deal of effort, and Yang Kai would not deny that.

Lu Sanniang smiled. "That's just the kind of man he is. He loves to suppress the strong and help the weak. If he had not acted so righteously back then, I would probably have died long ago. How could I have survived until now?"

The corners of Yang Kai's mouth twitched. He knew that in Elder Sister Lu's eyes, he had probably become one of the weak whom Li Jiao was helping.

It was no wonder she thought so. He was only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm, just like her, while Li Jiao was a Third Stage Emperor Realm Master. If anything happened while they were together, any normal person would naturally assume that Li Jiao would be the one to step forward.

Her assumption was perfectly reasonable, and Yang Kai could not be bothered to explain.

As they chatted, Lu Sanniang's emotions stabilized further. She did not feel as much pressure when facing Yang Kai. Their cultivations were about the same, so she could naturally relax around him.

On the other hand, when facing Li Jiao, she felt guilty and inferior, causing her to act cautiously at every turn.

What a pitiful soul, Yang Kai sighed inwardly.

As they spoke, Lu Sanniang suddenly fell silent. Her body began trembling once more, and she stared ahead with quivering eyes.

Yang Kai took a look and was also somewhat speechless.

Fu Qi was actually flying straight toward the island ahead. From the looks of it, that island was his destination.

Was this not too much of a coincidence? There were countless Spirit Islands scattered across this vast sea, and the Dragons lived far apart from one another. No one had expected Fu Qi to bring everyone to the very island where Lu Sanniang had once lived.

Since Yang Kai had already guessed the truth, he naturally would not stand by and do nothing. He secretly sent her a transmission, "Elder Sister Lu, once we land on the island, stay close to me. Do not get separated from me."

Lu Sanniang gave no reaction, so it was unclear whether she had heard him. In any case, the closer they drew, the worse her complexion became.

Finally, the group landed on the island behind Fu Qi. Yang Kai's earlier observation had been correct; Fu Qi's destination was indeed this island.

A Dragon should have been living on the island, because there were many palaces on its far side. Although it was already late at night, lights still flickered inside those palaces, and no one knew what the people within were doing.

After arriving, Fu Qi beckoned, and Yuan Wu immediately scurried over, bowing and scraping.

Fu Qi wore a cold expression as he quietly gave Yuan Wu a few instructions, then took out a Spatial Ring and tossed it to him.

Yuan Wu’s face immediately turned bitter, and he was clearly placed in an extremely difficult position, but Fu Qi ignored him entirely. Soaring into the sky, his body flickered and transformed into a green dragon more than ten zhang long, which plunged headfirst into the sea and vanished from sight.

"Sir Fu Qi! Sir Fu Qi!" Yuan Wu shouted.

"You have one month. If you cannot complete it, all of you will die!"

A dragon roar thundered from the depths of the sea, causing everyone's expression to change.

No one knew what they had been brought here to do, so how could they possibly remain calm after suddenly hearing such words? A period of one month? What did that even mean? What were they supposed to accomplish within one month?

No one knew. After a brief silence, a noisy clamor broke out.

Fu Qi was gone, and everyone here was a resident of Half-Dragon City, so they no longer had so many reservations.

Several of the stronger Emperor Realm Masters gathered around Yuan Wu.

"Brother Yuan, why did the Dragon Clan summon us here?"

"That's right. What do they want us to do?"

"What is this one-month period about?"

"Brother Yuan, do you know something? If you do, hurry up and tell us what is going on."

"Brother Yuan, you cannot keep this to yourself. We are all grasshoppers tied to the same rope now, so we must work together."

"Indeed. If we cannot complete the task given to us by the Dragon Clan, the consequences will likely be unimaginable."

...

The crowd clamored without end, making Yuan Wu feel as though his head was about to explode.

There was no helping it. Their lives were at stake, so who would dare treat this matter lightly? Since Fu Qi had said they had one month, then one month was all they had. If they failed to satisfy his demands, forget five hundred people; even five thousand would be slaughtered to the last.

The Dragon Clan always kept their word, especially in matters like this.

Yuan Wu's face twisted to the extreme, and he was filled with regret. Had he known this would happen, he would have been better off following Fu Ling to cultivate the Dragon Blood Flowers. Now he had been saddled with a troublesome task even more life-threatening than cultivating Dragon Blood Flowers.

What a wretched fate! Why was his life so bitter?

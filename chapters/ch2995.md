## Chapter 2995 — Poison Venerable Bu Hong

"Exile!" Yang Kai's deep voice rang out as he spread his hand and slapped forward.

The space ahead instantly collapsed, swallowing all the poisonous creatures and exiling them into the endless void. In the blink of an eye, they vanished without a trace.

"A Space Divine Ability!" the black-robed man cried out. He had originally intended to exchange a few moves with Yang Kai, but after witnessing the might of this Divine Ability, he immediately abandoned that idea. His black robe fluttered as his Emperor Yuan surged, and his entire figure suddenly became ethereal and traceless, as though he were about to melt into the surrounding air.

This was clearly a supreme life-saving technique. Once he used it, no one could grasp his movements, and the slightest lapse would allow him to escape without a trace.

"Stay!" Yang Kai coldly snorted. The Laws of Space surged and enveloped the area around the man.

The fading figure became solid once more. The crushing force coming from the surrounding space instantly broke his Secret Technique and forced him to reveal himself again.

The black-robed man truly panicked now. He had long heard that experts proficient in the Laws of Space were difficult to provoke, but he had never imagined one could be powerful to such an extent. He and Yang Kai were both at the First-Stage Emperor Realm, yet before Yang Kai, he could not muster even the confidence to fight.

He desperately circulated his strength, trying to break free from the restraints of the Laws of Space, but in the next instant, a large hand suddenly reached out from ahead and seized him by the neck. A fierce, tsunami-like force surged from that hand, scattering his own strength into utter disarray.

The black-robed man instantly lost all power to resist. He was held aloft, unable to move.

Yang Kai flicked a finger, sending a burst of force through the air that tore apart the cloth covering the black-robed man's head. A pitted and uneven face, as though it had been boiled in scalding water, was revealed. It was hideous and terrifying, enough to make anyone who saw it shudder.

Although the battle between the two had been brief, it had inevitably caused some commotion. The display of their Divine Abilities drew the attention of many residents nearby.

The instant the black-robed man's true face was exposed, cries of alarm rose from the crowd below.

"Poison Venerable Bu Hong!"

"Why is he here?"

"Damn it! Why did Wind and Cloud Pavilion let this monster out?"

"Run! This place is too dangerous to linger in. Now that the Poison Venerable has arrived, countless lives will probably be lost here again."

"Why run? Can't you see Bu Hong has been captured? Tsk, tsk, who is that man? He's actually so formidable."

"Are you blind? Is the Poison Venerable someone who can be captured so casually? That brat is dead for sure. No, no, I have to leave at once. If I don't go now, I may die without a grave."

The crowd erupted into a clamour, and quite a few people truly fled desperately toward the city gates, as though the black-robed man were some devastating flood or savage beast and leaving even a moment too late would cost them their lives.

"It seems you're quite famous." Yang Kai stared coldly at the man before him.

Bu Hong chuckled sinisterly. "Brat, you're dead. This Venerable possesses a body of myriad poisons. If you hadn't come near me, this Venerable might not have been your opponent, but you just had to recklessly try to capture me. You are simply seeking your own death!"

As he spoke, an emerald-green aura spread from Yang Kai's wrist up his arm. Like a swimming green snake, it moved with astonishing speed and disappeared beneath his sleeve in the blink of an eye.

Yang Kai glanced down and said indifferently, "You think this trash poison of yours is enough?"

Bu Hong's expression turned fierce, but just as he was about to utter another threat, his eyes suddenly widened. He stared at Yang Kai's arm in disbelief as Yang Kai pulled up his sleeve, revealing that the emerald-green aura had become stuck at his elbow and could not advance another inch.

A drop of cold sweat rolled down Bu Hong's forehead as he cried out, "How could you possibly withstand it?"

Yang Kai replied coldly, "As long as one's physique is strong enough, naturally no poison can invade!"

"Impossible! That's absolutely impossible!" Bu Hong's eyes trembled violently, as though he had suffered an enormous blow, and his entire composure fell apart. Even a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master could not resist his poison, yet the First-Stage Emperor Realm Master before him remained perfectly unharmed. Although Yang Kai had been poisoned, the toxin was trapped below his elbow and could not spread through the rest of his body.

Bu Hong simply could not accept it. Just how powerful did a physique have to be to withstand his poison?

With his greatest reliance rendered useless, Bu Hong finally fell into a panic. Sensing Yang Kai's murderous intent and the hand around his neck gradually tightening, he hurriedly said, "Perhaps you have a way to neutralize this Venerable's poison, but what about that little girl? If you want her to live, obediently release this Venerable. Perhaps she may still have a chance to survive."

Yang Kai snorted. "You don't need to concern yourself with her. Have a safe journey on Yellow Springs Road!"

As soon as he finished speaking, Yang Kai's Emperor Yuan surged through his hand.

With a soft pop, Bu Hong's neck burst apart under Yang Kai's grip. His head flew into the air, his eyes wide and his face still filled with astonishment, as though he had not yet realized what had happened.

Even at the moment of his death, he could not understand why this young man did not care whether the little girl lived or died.

The mutilated corpse fell from the sky. Yang Kai reached out and beckoned, taking Bu Hong's Spatial Ring.

At the sight of this scene, the onlooking crowd below was frightened into complete silence.

Poison Venerable Bu Hong was dead! Bu Hong, the Poison Venerable who had once brought calamity to the Western Territory and devastated more than a dozen cities, had actually been killed with such ease! Moreover, his death had been incomparably miserable, his neck burst apart under a young man's single-handed grip.

After a long silence, cheers erupted from below. Pairs of worshipful and grateful eyes looked upward, as though Yang Kai had done some great deed that made everyone want to applaud and cheer.

It truly was a great deed. Back when Poison Venerable Bu Hong was cultivating his Poison Arts, he had often used the cultivators of the Western Territory as test subjects. Whenever he wanted to test the effects of a new poison, he would simply release it into a city, then hide nearby and observe the results.

More than a dozen cities had been wiped out by his hand, causing countless people to lose their spouses and children, their families ruined and loved ones dead.

Everyone in the Western Territory hated the Poison Venerable to the bone.

Unfortunately, although this man's cultivation was not particularly high, his Poison Arts were unpredictable and elusive. Even several Second-Stage Emperor Realm experts had fallen at his hands. Later, when a Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master pursued him, he hid inside Wind and Cloud Pavilion and had not shown himself for many years.

No one had expected him to meet his end here this time.

Cheers continued to ring out below, but Yang Kai silently circulated his Secret Art and forced the poison from his arm.

Bu Hong's poison was truly formidable. In the past, Yang Kai estimated that no matter how strong his physique was, he would certainly have fallen victim to it. However, since cultivating the Dragon Transformation Art and integrating the Golden Sacred Dragon Origin Power, he had gained a trace of Dragon Clan blood in his body, greatly increasing his resistance to poison.

True members of the Dragon Clan naturally possessed extremely strong resistance to all kinds of negative forces, and the experts among the Dragon Clan were even immune to all poisons.

Although Yang Kai could not compare to a true member of the Dragon Clan, he was still capable of suppressing Bu Hong's poison.

He had benefited from the advantage of his Dragon Clan bloodline.

A moment later, a drop of emerald-green venom was forced to his fingertip. Though there was very little of it, it seemed to contain enough power to destroy an entire city.

The Laws of Space surged, and Yang Kai exiled the drop of venom into the void.

His figure flickered, and he returned to the private room. The table was a complete mess, and every scrap of food had been eaten clean. Lin Yun'er had spread her ten fingers and was carefully sucking each of them in turn.

Cold sweat poured down Yang Kai's forehead. This girl's Master didn't have it easy either, he thought. Just how much did he have to spend every year to keep this glutton fed?

"Uncle Yang, you're back?" Lin Yun'er looked up at Yang Kai and smiled with narrowed eyes.

"Mm." Yang Kai nodded, then asked thoughtfully, "Were you planning to go to Wind and Cloud Pavilion before?"

"That's right." Lin Yun'er's pretty face sank, and her expression turned frosty. "Those people from Wind and Cloud Pavilion are too hateful. I heard that so-called Young Pavilion Master say that his family has imprisoned many girls. I want to rescue them." As she spoke, her expression suddenly changed and she slapped the table, rising to her feet.

"What's wrong?" Yang Kai asked in alarm.

"I don't know where Wind and Cloud Pavilion is." Lin Yun'er looked at Yang Kai with an innocent expression.

The corner of Yang Kai's mouth twitched. "Then how were you getting there before?"

Lin Yun'er giggled. "I was just wandering around at random."

Yang Kai immediately said with deep concern, "Forget it, I'll go with you."

He had nothing else to do at the moment anyway. He had originally intended to chase after Zhu Qing and her younger brother, but from the look of things, they had probably left long ago, so there was little point in continuing the pursuit. In any case, he could see them again once he returned to the Northern Territory. Having encountered Lin Yun'er by rare chance, her affairs naturally took priority.

The little girl might be strong, but there would always be existences in this world that she could not afford to provoke. With her innocent temperament, there was no telling what kind of trouble she might cause. It would be safer for him to accompany her.

Lin Yun'er's eyes lit up when she heard this. "Uncle Yang, you're so good to me."

As she spoke, she threw herself at him and hooked her arm around Yang Kai's, pressing half her body tightly against him.

Feeling the softness against his arm, Yang Kai said earnestly, "Little Yun'er, you've grown up. You're a young woman now."

Lin Yun'er nodded repeatedly. "Mm, mm."

Yang Kai continued, "Since you're a young woman now, there are some things you can't do the same way you did before."

"Mm, mm!"

Yang Kai opened his mouth, but swallowed the words that had reached his lips. Letting out a soft sigh, he realized that this little girl had probably been protected too well. She had never truly wandered the outside world all these years and had no understanding whatsoever of the proper distance between men and women.

Since she was still a blank sheet of paper, why should he use the brush of worldly convention to leave a mark upon it?

Abandoning the idea of educating her, Yang Kai was just about to say something else when he suddenly frowned and swept a vigilant gaze around the room. His Divine Sense spread outward like a tide.

"What's wrong?" Lin Yun'er noticed something unusual and hurriedly asked.

"Did you feel anything?" Yang Kai asked solemnly.

"I did." Lin Yun'er immediately nodded.

"You felt it too?" Yang Kai looked left and right, his nerves stretched to the limit. For some inexplicable reason, he had suddenly felt a trace of hostility envelop him just now, but it had vanished without a trace, leaving him uncertain whether it had merely been an illusion.

"That's right. I felt very comfortable."

"..."

Lin Yun'er giggled and ignored everything else, hugging Yang Kai's arm even more tightly.

The two left the restaurant and asked for the direction of Wind and Cloud Pavilion. Yang Kai immediately summoned his Flowing Cloud Shuttle and pulled Lin Yun'er inside. Soon, the Flowing Cloud Shuttle transformed into a streak of light and sped into the distance.

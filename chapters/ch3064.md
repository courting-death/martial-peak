## Chapter 3064 — Facing Seven Dragons

The heavens split and the earth cracked. The sun and moon lost their light, Laws ran rampant, and the Spiritual Qi fell into disarray. Great swathes of the void collapsed as thousand-chi-high waves rose from the sea.

Even the Small World containing Dragon Isle seemed to have become unstable.

Today's battle was destined to be recorded in the annals of history.

The Dragon Clan had turned out in full force, while Yang Kai and the others each fought their own battles. Although his companions had helped share the pressure, Yang Kai still faced seven enormous Dragons alone. Everything today had begun because of him, so the Dragon Clan naturally hated him to the bone and lavished him with special attention from all sides.

Looking across the entire battlefield, the only fight likely to produce a clear winner was the clash between Yang Kai and those seven Dragons.

Zhu Qing held her own against two opponents at once.

Jiu Feng was likewise fighting two opponents by herself, but she mainly relied on evasive tactics. She could do nothing to the two Dragons, but neither could they do anything to her. Just like Zhu Qing's battle, theirs could not possibly be decided in a short time.

Zhu Lie fought his opponent one-on-one. Both were Eighth-Tier Dragons and evenly matched, so how could deciding who was superior be a simple matter?

Although Fu Qi's bloodline was not particularly high, the Dragon Clan's profound heritage could not be underestimated. Liu Yan had inherited the legacy of the Phoenix Race, but she had not had long enough to mature and was still far from her prime. She was at a disadvantage as soon as she exchanged blows with Fu Qi. Dragon and Phoenix tangled as Dragon roars and Phoenix cries rose one after another, leaving her barely able to defend herself. She did not seek to defeat her enemy, only to relieve some of the pressure on Yang Kai. Worried for his safety, however, she became even more hard-pressed whenever her attention wandered.

The several battlefields were basically balanced. Only Yang Kai's side was at an obvious disadvantage.

After his surprise attack failed, the seven Dragons instantly closed in around him, giving him no room to display his abilities. They supported one another from every angle and attacked in well-ordered waves, unleashing Dragon Clan Secret Arts one after another. Yang Kai was battered into a thoroughly miserable state, his enormous body tumbling through the air from time to time. In less than half an incense stick of time, more than a hundred wounds covered his body. Bone showed through every one of them, and his blood-soaked Dragon Scales made him look as though he had been coated in a layer of gold dust.

Fortunately, Yang Kai's physique had improved significantly after his Dragon Transformation. Otherwise, he could never have endured such an intense battle and would have died countless times over by now.

It was also fortunate that he possessed his Space Secret Techniques, allowing him time and again to twist the void at critical moments and escape danger.

Drops of Golden Blood fell into the sea. Countless sea beasts rushed over at the scent, swallowing the bloodstained seawater and refining the Dragon nature within it for themselves. Dragon Blood was extraordinarily precious, and members of the Dragon Clan almost never bled under ordinary circumstances. Thus, despite living around Dragon Isle for so many years, these sea beasts had never before encountered an opportunity to consume Dragon Blood.

Today, however, drops of Dragon Blood were raining down as though they cost nothing, naturally drawing countless sea beasts into a frantic struggle for them.

In the sky, Yang Kai waged a fierce battle against seven Dragons. In the sea below, countless sea beasts bit and tore at one another. The entire world seemed to have gone mad.

The battlefield continued to shift, and after a while, Fu Chi's Spirit Island could no longer be seen.

Yang Kai panted heavily, his eight-zhang Dragon body standing tall in the air. His gaze was unruly and disdainful as the seven Dragons surrounding him watched like tigers eyeing their prey.

"For insulting my Dragon Clan, you deserve death!" one Dragon roared.

"You deserve death!" the other six Dragons echoed. Their voices, somewhere between Dragon roars and the howls of beasts, clearly carried traces of a Dragon Clan Secret Art. As the roars spread, they transformed into an invisible Divine Soul attack that blasted toward Yang Kai's Sea of Consciousness.

After fighting for so long, they were no longer as cautious as before. Yang Kai's earlier ferocity in crippling one of their clansmen in the blink of an eye had indeed frightened them, but now it seemed this man did not have three heads and six arms after all. He had only succeeded because his surprise attack had caught them unprepared.

To have seven Dragons arrayed against him was already an immense honour. He could die without regret.

Yang Kai remained completely unmoved. He raised a hand to dig at his ear and grinned. "It's still uncertain who will kill whom, so don't speak too soon. Careful your tongue doesn't trip you up and leave you no graceful way out."

"Stubborn fool!" that Dragon snarled impatiently. "Since you are so intent on seeking death, we will grant your wish."

As soon as those words fell, Dragon roars shook the heavens. Seven Dragons lunged at Yang Kai from every direction, their billowing Dragon Pressure filling the void. Even an ordinary Third-Stage Emperor Realm Master struck by this Dragon Pressure would likely lose much of his courage before the fight even began and find himself unable to resist.

Yang Kai was not so feeble, but his irreverent smile vanished at once, replaced by an extremely solemn expression.

Although he was reluctant to admit it, he truly... was no match for seven Dragons by himself. The Dragon Clan really was not so easy to provoke.

He had to call for reinforcements quickly. With that thought, Yang Kai struggled to evade the seven Dragons' killing moves while secretly sending out a surge of Divine Sense.

Elsewhere, Fu Ling and the Blue Dragon were joining forces to attack Zhu Qing from both sides, and the two sides were fairly evenly matched.

The three Dragons twisted and tangled through the air, exchanging attack after attack in an extremely lively battle.

Suddenly, Fu Ling's movements paused slightly. Her Divine Sense surged as she transmitted a message to the Blue Dragon.

The Blue Dragon understood. Twisting her body, she lunged straight toward Zhu Qing.

But at that moment, the Purple Dragon Fu Ling had transformed into suddenly pounced at the Blue Dragon's flank and raked out viciously with one claw.

With a tearing sound, the Blue Dragon's body shuddered. She instinctively avoided being struck in a vital spot, but an instant later, intense pain erupted from her waist and abdomen. At the same time, the Dragon Pressure of a Ninth-Tier Dragon descended upon her, causing the Dragon Yuan in her body to become obstructed and difficult to circulate.

A strained groan rang out as her nearly twenty-zhang-long Dragon body began shrinking at a speed visible to the naked eye.

Bloodline suppression!

The Blue Dragon's eyes overflowed with fear and confusion. She could not understand why this had happened.

When she turned her head, incredulity flashed through her eyes, and she roared furiously, "Fu Ling, you..."

At that moment, a pale-blue Dragon Scale was clearly clutched in Fu Ling's Dragon claw. It obviously belonged to the Blue Dragon, and if it had been an ordinary scale, it would not have mattered. Unfortunately, this particular scale was precisely the vessel carrying the Dragon Temple's Protection Technique.

Once the Dragon Scale was torn away, the Protection Technique lost its effect, and the suppression of bloodline immediately manifested.

No matter how she thought about it, she could not understand why Fu Ling had suddenly attacked her. Had she not been completely unguarded against Fu Ling, she would never have allowed Fu Ling's single strike to hit such a crucial point and break the Dragon Temple's Protection Technique.

Zhu Qing had clearly not expected this either. At the same time Fu Ling launched her surprise attack against the Blue Dragon, Zhu Qing had already flashed in front of her. Her enormous Dragon body coiled around Fu Ling and constricted her tightly, while her sharp Dragon claws dug into Fu Ling's body. Crimson Dragon Blood had already begun seeping from beneath their tips.

"Elder Sister Qing, don't!" Fu Ling cried out in terror.

Compared with Zhu Qing's Dragon body, hers was practically only half the size. The disparity had not felt so obvious while she and the Blue Dragon were fighting Zhu Qing together, but now that she faced Zhu Qing alone, she finally felt terrified.

It seemed as though Zhu Qing could take her life at any moment. Having her life and death controlled by another felt extremely unpleasant, yet she was powerless to do anything about it.

Zhu Qing let out a surprised sound but did not attack again. She turned to glance at the Blue Dragon, then looked down at Fu Ling and asked, "What is the meaning of this?"

Even she could not make sense of Fu Ling's surprise attack against the Blue Dragon. Her relationship with Fu Ling could hardly be called harmonious.

"I belong to Brother-in-law," Fu Ling hurriedly explained.

"Brother-in-law?"

"It's Yang Kai, Big Brother Yang."

"Hm?" Killing intent suddenly erupted in Zhu Qing's eyes.

Fu Ling jumped in fright and abruptly realized the problem. She immediately clarified, "Brother-in-law planted a brand in my Sea of Consciousness before. I obey his orders now."

She also understood why Zhu Qing had suddenly directed killing intent at her. By saying she was Yang Kai's, Zhu Qing would naturally assume at first that the two of them were involved. It would have been strange if Zhu Qing had not become angry.

"He actually planted a Soul Brand in you!" Zhu Qing cried softly in disbelief. Only then did she understand why Fu Ling had suddenly acted to help her.

Fu Ling nodded hastily. "Yes. He sent me an order just now and told me to act."

"I understand!" Zhu Qing withdrew her Dragon claws and glanced toward the Blue Dragon. By now, the Blue Dragon had already resumed her human form. Under the suppression of a Ninth-Tier Dragon Bloodline, there was no point in struggling to maintain her Dragon body; she was better off conserving her strength.

Blood streamed from a one-chi-long gash in her abdomen. She pressed one hand over the wound and glared furiously at Fu Ling.

No one would be in a good mood after suffering such a surprise attack. After hearing Fu Ling's explanation, however, she understood that Fu Ling had probably acted against her will. Still, for a member of the Dragon Clan to be branded with a Soul Brand by a human was an utter disgrace, and a trace of contempt involuntarily flashed through her eyes.

"Elder Sister Qing, Brother-in-law needs help," Fu Ling said, looking anxiously at Zhu Qing.

"Don't call him Brother-in-law. Zhu Lie won't marry you either!" After saying this, Zhu Qing lashed her Dragon tail and sped toward Yang Kai's position.

She clearly knew what kind of person Fu Ling was and naturally had no desire to see Zhu Lie marry her.

"Sorry," Fu Ling said, looking somewhat awkwardly at the Blue Dragon as she apologized.

"You can say that to Second Elder when the time comes and see whether she forgives you!" the Blue Dragon sneered.

Fear flashed through Fu Ling's eyes, but she could say nothing more. Turning around, she hurriedly chased after Zhu Qing.

Worried for Yang Kai's safety, Zhu Qing flew with astonishing speed. Her enormous body traced a beautiful arc through the air like a blazing flame, leaving scorch marks in the void wherever her Dragon body passed.

Although Fu Ling had set out only a moment later, she could not catch up no matter how hard she tried, which left her rather vexed.

The order Yang Kai had given her was not merely to go and help him. More importantly, they were to defeat their enemies one by one. That was the key to victory today.

Their greatest disadvantage was their lack of numbers.

But if they could first deal with the opponents fighting Zhu Lie, Jiu Feng, and Liu Yan, their side would have six people. Although they would still be outnumbered against seven Dragons, their side would definitely be far stronger overall.

Unfortunately, before she could explain Yang Kai's plan to Zhu Qing in detail, Zhu Qing had already flown away.

This made Fu Ling uneasy. She did not know whether Yang Kai would blame her later.

Yang Kai's battlefield was not far from Zhu Qing's, so it took her only a little over ten breaths to arrive.

The moment Zhu Qing saw what was happening, her eyes turned red.

Seven enormous Dragons were subjecting Yang Kai to a brutal gang beating with all the shamelessness of common thugs. Almost no part of his enormous body remained uninjured. Flesh had even been torn away from several places, exposing the stark white bones beneath.

## Chapter 2938 — Shaping the Flesh

Wen Zishan opened his mouth, but before he could say anything, Lei Hong’s figure flickered. With a crackling explosion, a bolt of lightning flashed in place, carrying the few from Star God Palace as they shot off into the distance like a long rainbow.

"You, Lei, get back here!" Zhu Lie suddenly bellowed, his voice carrying across the wilderness. "You Southern Territory cultivators dare treat the Dragon Clan in such a manner? Dragon Isle will never let this matter rest!"

Lei Hong ran even faster, vanishing in the blink of an eye.

He had originally wanted to ask Yang Kai about the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique and how it worked, but now was hardly the time for such questions. All he wanted was to put as much distance as possible between himself and this troublesome place.

Yang Kai stared in the direction Lei Hong had fled, chuckling for a while, but soon his laughter faded. Turning his head, he saw Gao Xueting, Wen Zishan, and the others all looking at him speechlessly.

“What do we do now?” Gao Xueting asked.

“No idea!” Yang Kai put on an innocent face, but paired with his current half-dragon form, the expression looked especially fierce.

“Maybe we should let him go,” Chen Qian suggested softly. She was still quite wary of Dragon Isle’s reputation, and seeing Zhu Lie beaten so miserably, she couldn’t help but feel a little sympathy.

Zhu Lie gritted his teeth and said, "That's right. If you know what's good for you, release me at once. Otherwise, you'll all come to a terrible end."

Yang Kai stomped down again and snorted, “Figure out your own situation before you speak, brat!”

“I am a Dragon, and you dare treat me like this!” Zhu Lie looked utterly humiliated.

“So what if you’re a Dragon? Aren’t you still lying at my feet?”

Zhu Lie’s face flushed red as he buried it in the ground, wishing he could find a hole to crawl into and never show his face again.

“Can’t let him go, can’t kill him…” Wen Zishan smiled bitterly. “Kid, you’ve really given me a huge headache.”

The Dragons were creatures of such pride that if Yang Kai truly released Zhu Lie after such humiliation, the dragon would never let the matter rest. He would certainly send word to Dragon Isle at once, and in a few days, a massive wave of dragons would descend upon them. Killing him was out of the question too—if Yang Kai truly slew Zhu Lie, they would incur Dragon Isle's eternal enmity. And Dragon Isle's wrath was something even a Great Emperor Sect could scarcely withstand, let alone Qingyang Shrine.

Yang Kai waved his hand and said, “Forget it, don’t worry about this. Whoever does the deed takes responsibility. I’ll handle it myself.”

Gao Xueting asked, “What are you planning to do?”

Yang Kai spread his hands. “Can’t kill, can’t let go, so I’ll just have to imprison him. What else can I do?”

Zhu Lie was shocked at these words, raising his head and shouting angrily, “You dare!”

Yang Kai sneered, “Open your dog eyes and see if I dare or not.”

As he spoke, he flicked his hand, and an ancient little bell appeared before him, spinning as it fell from the sky, rapidly growing larger.

Zhu Lie had clearly sensed the danger as well. That small bell looked utterly unremarkable, plain and ancient, yet it felt as if nothing could breach it. If he were truly trapped by the bell, he likely would have no way to escape.

He struggled desperately, only to be met with another round of Yang Kai’s beating, which made him grimace in pain.

The Mountain and River Bell fell, suppressing Zhu Lie within. Yang Kai flashed out, and immediately curses and the sounds of Zhu Lie’s attacks echoed from inside the bell. Yang Kai simply formed a seal with his hand, and all movement inside instantly ceased.

After all, the Mountain and River Bell was a primordial treasure, able to suppress all things. Even the True Phoenix Fire was suppressed inside for tens of thousands of years without escape. Though Zhu Lie was a Dragon, if he was truly sealed inside, he’d have no way out.

“This isn’t a long-term solution,” Wen Zishan sighed.

Yang Kai said, “Hall Master, you all focus on the Demonic Intent and Great Emperor matters. Leave him to me. I guarantee he’ll be obedient within a month.”

“If you’re confident, all the better. But don’t go overboard,” Wen Zishan said, then led the others away.

If not for all the recent upheavals, he would have tried to resolve the Zhu Lie matter first. But now, for the Shrine and the entire Southern Territory, the most urgent thing was to track down the Demonic Intent, and also Mortal Great Emperor. Wu Kuang had taken over Duan Hongchen’s body—no one knew what he would do next.

Tens of thousands of years ago, Heaven Devourer Great Emperor brought calamity to all living things, countless lives withered because of him. Compared to that, Dragon Isle’s wrath and pursuit were secondary.

In just a few days, the entire Southern Territory was moving in high gear. With the Great Emperor’s demon-slaying order, hundreds of millions of cultivators in the Southern Territory were mobilized.

Yang Kai did not participate. He spent these days on Spirit Sword Peak.

Although Spirit Sword Peak had become a sealed demon land, the Demonic Intent sealed inside had already escaped. The ruins left behind were neither dangerous nor special, so Spirit Sword Peak was still Yang Kai’s spirit peak.

The Mountain and River Bell had been moved to Spirit Sword Peak by Yang Kai, and Zhu Lie's curses and insults never ceased for a moment, disturbing the peace of the peak.

Every day, Yang Kai would go and teach him a lesson.

After losing that special dragon scale, Zhu Lie was no longer a match for Yang Kai. Before Yang Kai, who had unleashed the Dragon Transformation Secret Technique, Zhu Lie didn't even have the ability to assume his true dragon form.

Dragons were tough. No matter how badly Yang Kai beat him, Zhu Lie would recover completely by the next day.

Zhu Lie was indeed a stubborn one. Knowing full well that Yang Kai would never kill him regardless of the circumstances, no matter how battered and bruised he became, he never uttered a single word of submission. Most of the time, he simply glared at Yang Kai with those defiant, untamed eyes, as if to say: what can you possibly do to me?

Half a month passed like this, and gradually, good news began to arrive.

The Demonic Intent that had escaped from the sealed land at Spirit Sword Peak that day had mostly been found. After a series of battles, most of the Demonic Intent was destroyed. The Southern Territory suffered heavy losses, but as everyone learned more about the Demonic Intent, they also found many ways to restrain it, so it would no longer be able to corrupt people so easily.

Yet whether it was Yang Kai, Wen Zishan, or the other Emperor Realm cultivators of the Southern Territory, they all understood one thing: the Demonic Intent they had slain was far from all of it. The truly troublesome ones were those that remained hidden in the shadows.

No one knew who the remaining Demonic Intent was hiding in, or when they would reveal themselves.

There was still no news of Mortal Great Emperor or Wu Kuang.

Ever since Wu Kuang took over Duan Hongchen’s body and left Qingyang Shrine that day, it was as if he had vanished from the world. No one had seen any trace of him since.

He was just like those hidden strands of Demonic Intent—now a thorn in the flesh of the Southern Territory, always at risk of festering.

According to Gao Xueting, even Radiant Moon Great Emperor personally took action, but couldn’t find a single clue. For this, Radiant Moon Great Emperor even left Star God Palace to seek out Great Emperor Tianshu, hoping for help.

Yang Kai entered the Divine Roaming Mirror again, spending three thousand Elder Contribution Points to bring out Tian Yan’s Divine Soul.

It was time to fulfill his agreement with Tian Yan.

Ji Ying had long since finished refining the Life Origin Pill. Last time Yang Kai sent Nanmen Dajun back to Lingxiao Palace, he stopped by Ji Ying’s place and brought back all the Emperor Pills he had refined during this period to Qingyang Shrine. It was thanks to these pills that Yang Kai was able to quickly gather enough Elder Contribution Points to use the Divine Roaming Mirror again. Otherwise, he wouldn’t have been able to enter the Divine Roaming World a second time.

That night, the moon was bright and the stars sparse.

On the high platform atop Spirit Sword Peak, layers of restrictive formations were set up. Yang Kai had also activated the mountain-protecting grand array, just in case.

Tonight was the day Tian Yan would shape his fleshly body.

Wen Zishan, upon receiving word of this, came to serve as protector. His expression grave and solemn, he took his position at a formation core. This array had been laid down by Nanmen Dajun and possessed the ability to gather souls and stabilize spiritual essence. After all, Tian Yan was merely a Divine Soul without a fleshly body. This was his first time leaving the Divine Roaming World, brought by Yang Kai to the Star Realm. Without a vessel to inhabit, his soul would likely scatter and dissipate within moments.

The laws of heaven and earth in the Divine Roaming World were completely different from those in the Star Realm.

Yang Kai’s expression was solemn as he calmed his mind. After a long while, he opened his eyes and said slowly, “Senior, the time has come.”

Three quarters past midnight—the time of heaviest yin energy in a day.

As he spoke, he pointed forward.

A point of light blossomed, then spread out, condensing into Tian Yan’s form.

Yang Kai had used the Warm Spirit Lotus to bring Tian Yan’s Divine Soul out of the Divine Roaming World. Without such a supreme treasure, Tian Yan wouldn’t have been able to leave the Divine Roaming World at all.

His figure was illusory, half-transparent, like a Yin Soul that would scatter at the slightest breeze. This was just a spirit body.

As soon as he appeared, Tian Yan frowned, as if feeling some discomfort, a faint look of pain on his face.

The world buzzed, a thunderclap resounded, and an immensely powerful will and force seemed to descend from all directions, ignoring all the formations and the mountain-protecting grand array of the Spirit Sword Peak, pressing down on Tian Yan.

For such a being, comparable to a Great Emperor, to appear out of nowhere, the Heavenly Dao would not tolerate it—there would inevitably be rejection.

Tian Yan was clearly prepared for this. With a single-handed seal, a mysterious power spread out, forming a visible layer of protection around him, resisting the repulsive force of heaven and earth.

Thunder rumbled and lightning danced wildly atop the Spirit Sword Peak, as if the end of days had come—a terrifying sight.

The commotion didn’t last long. No one knew what marvelous method Tian Yan used, but the thunder gradually faded, the lightning vanished, and the omnipresent repulsive force disappeared completely.

“There’s not much time. The Heavenly Dao is not easily deceived.” Tian Yan suddenly opened his eyes and looked at Yang Kai. “Give me a Spiritual Pill and protect me.”

Yang Kai had been waiting for this. Hearing these words, he flicked his finger without hesitation, sending a milky-white Spiritual Pill flying straight toward Tian Yan.

Tian Yan didn’t open his mouth, but the pill still entered him.

Milky-white light began to shine, faint at first, but growing ever brighter as time passed, until all of Spirit Sword Peak seemed to be enveloped in its glow.

Yang Kai widened his eyes, focusing all his strength to observe carefully.

He saw the Life Origin Pill melt inside Tian Yan, turning into countless tiny white lights that merged into every part of his spirit body.

A faint trace of vitality suddenly appeared, like a candle flame in a storm, seemingly on the verge of being snuffed out.

Tian Yan sat cross-legged, eyes closed, completely still.

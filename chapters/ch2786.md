## Chapter 2786 — Sold for a Hundred Years

Facing the terrifying strike from the middle-aged man, Yang Kai took a step forward. The moment his foot landed, the overwhelming pressure that weighed on Hou Yu and Nanmen Dajun melted away like snow beneath the blazing sun.

He gently pointed a finger forward. It seemed ordinary, yet it secretly aligned with the profound truths of the Great Dao.

The middle-aged man rushing toward him changed expression. In his haste, he formed a palm, Emperor Yuan surging, and clashed with Yang Kai.

With a soft puff, the middle-aged man's aura instantly deflated like a punctured ball. He staggered back several steps before finally steadying himself.

In contrast, Yang Kai stood as steady as a mountain, his breath calm. The sea breeze fluttered his robes and tossed his black hair.

Hou Yu was stunned, staring blankly at Yang Kai's back. For a moment, she nearly regarded him as a being beyond mortal comprehension.

Did she see it right? Wasn’t Yang Kai only at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm? Both she and Nanmen Dajun were also at the First Stage, yet under the crushing pressure of the attack, even breathing had become difficult. But this Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace had simply raised a finger and sent the enemy retreating!

"You..."

The middle-aged man was also startled. In that instant, what he saw was not a finger, but a heaven-shaking blade slashing toward him. The aura of death enveloped him, leaving no room to dodge or block.

Just as he thought he would either die or be gravely injured, the other party effortlessly broke through all his defenses, merely forcing him back. He hadn't truly struck to kill, nor even injured him—just left his aura a bit disordered.

"Are you all together?" At this moment, the middle-aged man recognized Yang Kai and Nanmen Dajun. He had seen them two days ago when they first arrived on the island. Back then, the other person claimed to be here to collect a debt, and he had naively believed it. But now, it seemed they were clearly accomplices of that troublesome woman.

If it were an ordinary First Stage Emperor, he wouldn't care. But after suffering Yang Kai's finger and witnessing his skill, he was truly wary.

If things really turned ugly, he might not be a match.

His mind raced, but the middle-aged man quickly regained his composure and shouted, "No matter what, today this woman must either repay her debt or pay with her life!"

As soon as he finished, figures rushed in from all directions, gathering around the middle-aged man.

These people were all creditors who had lingered on the island, searching for Hou Yu to collect their debts.

There were more than twenty of them, including five at the Emperor Realm. Aside from the middle-aged man, who was at the Second Stage of the Emperor Realm, the other four were all at the First Stage.

With so many people at his side, the middle-aged man's apprehension eased a bit. He alone might not be a match for that young man, but could so many of them really not be?

A fierce tiger cannot fight a pack of wolves; two fists can't beat four hands. No matter how strong the other side was, he was still just one person.

As soon as these people appeared, they all glared at Hou Yu with hatred and anger, as if they had some irreconcilable grudge. Yet none spoke rashly, clearly taking their cues from the middle-aged man.

"Friend, this woman owes us a great deal of Source Crystals. If you insist on protecting her, don't blame us for being rude." The middle-aged man fixed his wary gaze on Yang Kai.

"Is everyone here?" Yang Kai smiled slightly, sweeping his gaze around before settling on the middle-aged man. Unhurriedly, he said, "Paying one's debts is only right. Whatever she owes you, I'll pay it for her."

"You'll pay for her?" The middle-aged man was shocked, staring at Yang Kai. "Do you even know how much she owes?"

"However much, I'll pay it all," Yang Kai replied calmly.

Hou Yu swallowed and quietly nudged Nanmen Dajun, asking, "Is our Palace Master very rich?"

Nanmen Dajun glanced at her and sneered, "What does that have to do with you? Anyway, the Palace Master will clear your debts this time, but it'll be deducted from your monthly salary. I reckon you'll have to stay in Lingxiao Palace for the rest of your life."

Hou Yu's pretty face changed instantly at his words. She clung to Nanmen Dajun's arm and said, "Dajun, you can't abandon me! I only joined Lingxiao Palace because of you. If you ever want to leave, you can't go alone—you must take me with you!"

Nanmen Dajun kept sneering and pulled his arm free.

On the other side, hearing Yang Kai's bold words, the crowd reacted differently—some with joy, others with suspicion.

After all, the woman's debt was no small sum. This young man actually said he'd pay it all—did he really have that kind of wealth? These weren't Lower-Level or Middle-Level Source Crystals, but Upper-Level Source Crystals counted in the tens of millions.

Even Mitian Sect and Li Dragon Palace would find such a debt hard to bear.

"Who are you?" The middle-aged man frowned at Yang Kai. "With such astonishing strength and such bold words, you can't be some nobody, can you?"

"It's just paying a debt. Why ask so much?" Yang Kai smiled faintly.

Nanmen Dajun interjected, "This is the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace, Yang Kai. You all shouldn't be strangers to the name."

"What?"

"The Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace?"

"The same Lingxiao Palace that destroyed Wenqing Sect and took over their territory?"

"I've heard the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace is a young man, his cultivation at the First Stage of the Emperor Realm. Looks like..."

"Hiss..."

Upon hearing Nanmen Dajun's introduction, the crowd was shocked. As they drew in cold breaths, they began to reassess Yang Kai. The middle-aged man's face darkened, and sweat beaded on his forehead.

So this was the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace, whose name had been spreading like wildfire across the Northern Territory!

The destruction of Wenqing Sect had caused a tremendous uproar throughout the Northern Territory. Though Lingxiao Palace had only just been established, its name now blazed like the midday sun. Among the countless cultivators of the Northern Territory, anyone with even the slightest access to news would have heard of it.

To be able to wipe out a behemoth like Wenqing Sect, Lingxiao Palace's strength was obvious. It was said that three Yao Kings resided there, and Wenqing Sect's Sect Master, Feng Xuan, was wiped out in an instant.

But now it seemed Lingxiao Palace didn't rely solely on the three Yao Kings. This young Palace Master was no ordinary figure either. With just First Stage Emperor Realm cultivation, he had easily suppressed him. Such skill was rare even in the entire Star Realm.

Fortunately, their conflict wasn't too deep. Otherwise, there would be no place for him in the Northern Territory.

With this thought, the middle-aged man's expression grew solemn. He quickly cupped his fists and said, "So it's Palace Master Yang. My apologies."

The others, seeing this, also hurriedly cupped their fists in greeting, not daring to show the slightest disrespect.

Yang Kai waved his hand and said, "Little Hou is a member of Lingxiao Palace. She's caused you all some trouble lately—my apologies. Now, tell me how much she owes each of you in Source Crystals. I'll settle all her debts on her behalf."

The crowd looked at each other in dismay.

Before knowing Yang Kai's identity, it was reasonable to doubt whether he could pay. But now that they knew he was the Palace Master of Lingxiao Palace, whether or not to accept the payment was the real headache.

After all, these debts were gambling debts, not legitimate transactions.

If they offended Lingxiao Palace over this, it might not be worth it.

Yet walking away from it entirely didn’t sit well—that was no small sum, after all.

So, although Yang Kai sincerely offered to pay Hou Yu's debts, no one responded.

Yang Kai read the room at once—how could he not see what they were wary of? A thought stirred in his mind as he looked toward the middle-aged man. “If I recall correctly, Little Hou owes you twenty million Upper-Level Source Crystals, does she not?”

"Uh... that's right!" The middle-aged man nodded.

He had told Yang Kai and Nanmen Dajun this two days ago.

"Then please check the amount." Yang Kai casually tossed over a Spatial Ring.

The middle-aged man caught it and swept it with his Divine Sense. The amount was correct. At this point, he could only say, "Indeed, twenty million."

With this precedent, the others no longer hesitated. One after another, they reported the amounts Hou Yu owed them or their factions.

Some were owed one or two million Upper-Level Source Crystals, others over ten million.

With more than twenty people, Yang Kai spent a total of seventy million Upper-Level Source Crystals to clear all the debts.

Having recovered their money, everyone was overjoyed.

Some of them had been here for a year, battered by the sea wind, unable to find Hou Yu, let alone recover their debts. Now, happiness had suddenly fallen from the sky, leaving them almost giddy with joy.

Getting their money back meant they no longer had to suffer here.

"Your money has been returned." Yang Kai clasped his hands behind his back, looking calmly at the crowd. "Now, I have a small favor to ask. I hope you all can help me out."

The leading middle-aged man grew solemn and cupped his fists. "Palace Master Yang, your integrity commands our respect. If you have any instructions, just say the word."

The others all echoed in agreement.

Yang Kai smiled. "After you return, please spread the word for me: from now on, if any sect or person dares to gamble with Little Hou again, Lingxiao Palace will not let it slide!"

The crowd was shocked, and Hou Yu cried out dramatically, "Palace Master, you might as well kill me!"

If this news spread, how could she ever gamble again? She immediately regretted joining this damned Lingxiao Palace—now even her greatest pleasure had been mercilessly stripped away. Hou Yu suddenly felt life was no longer worth living.

Truly hateful!

The middle-aged man clearly breathed a sigh of relief and smiled. "That's easy to arrange. Palace Master Yang, rest assured. We didn't know before that this lady was from Lingxiao Palace. If we had known earlier..."

"No need to mention the past." Yang Kai waved his hand.

He had no intention of chatting further. Turning to Hou Yu and Nanmen Dajun, he said, "Let's go."

Nanmen Dajun immediately summoned the Imperial Treasure flying boat. The three boarded in turn and soon turned into a stream of light, flying toward Lingxiao Palace.

"Want to gamble?" Inside the flying boat, Yang Kai looked at the dejected Hou Yu and asked.

Hou Yu looked up and nodded like a pecking chick.

"Pay back the seventy million Upper-Level Source Crystals first," Yang Kai grinned. "It won't take long—about a hundred years should do."

Hou Yu's eyes rolled back, and she fainted on the spot...
